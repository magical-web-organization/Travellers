Introduction to Juan de Nova Island

Juan de Nova Island is a French-administered atoll located about 600 kilometers east of Madagascar in the Mozambique Channel. With an area of just over six square kilometers, the island is uninhabited and serves as an important nesting site for several species of seabirds.
