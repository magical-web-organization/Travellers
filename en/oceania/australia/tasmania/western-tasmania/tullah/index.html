Introduction to Tullah

Tullah is a small town located in the western region of Tasmania in Australia. The town is situated near Lake Rosebery with scenic views of Mount Farrell. Tullah was established as a mining town in the early 1900s, but today it’s popular for outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, biking, and kayaking. Whether you are looking for an adrenaline rush or a peaceful escape, Tullah has something to offer everyone.

Local Culture and Customs of Tullah

The culture of Tullah is predominantly based on outdoor activities and the preservation of local heritage. The people of Tullah are friendly and welcoming to visitors.

Best Time to Visit Tullah

The best time to visit Tullah is during the summer season from December to February when the temperatures are mild and the weather is pleasant.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tullah

Tullah is a safe town for visitors. However, it’s important to take precautions when hiking in the wilderness, carry a map and compass, and be aware of changing weather conditions.

Health in Tullah

The health infrastructure in Tullah is limited, with no major hospital in the town. In case of any medical emergency, visitors should seek help from the local ambulance service.
