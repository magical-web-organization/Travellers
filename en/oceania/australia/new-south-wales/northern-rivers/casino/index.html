Comprehensive Tour Guide of Casino, New South Wales, Australia

Welcome to Casino, a charming town located in the heart of the Northern Rivers region of New South Wales, Australia. With its rich heritage and stunning natural beauty, Casino is a great destination for anyone looking for an authentic Australian experience. Whether you’re interested in exploring the local culture, indulging in some outdoor adventure, or simply relaxing and enjoying the scenery, Casino has something to offer everyone. In this travel guide, we’ll take a closer look at some of the best places to visit, things to do, and local highlights that make Casino such a unique and special destination.

List of Places to Visit in Casino

Whether you’re a history buff, an adventure seeker, or a foodie, there’s something for everyone in Casino. Here are some of the must-visit attractions and places in Casino:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Richmond River Historical Society Museum: Take a step back in time and learn about the rich history of the Northern Rivers region at this fascinating museum. From Aboriginal artifacts to early settler’s memorabilia, there’s plenty to discover here.

  • Casino Folk Museum: This community-run museum provides an insight into the life and times of the pioneers and early settlers of the region. There is an impressive collection of photographs, pictures, and artifacts of the area’s past.

Natural Attractions of Casino: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Flat Rock Lookout: Located in the Richmond Range National Park, this lookout provides stunning views over the Richmond Valley. You can take a leisurely walk from the picnic area or climb to the top to see the panoramic 360-degree views over the entire valley.

  • Bungabee State Forest: This vast forest provides campers and hikers endless opportunities to explore. Here you will find a variety of walking and biking tracks, including a full-day hike to Tooloom Falls.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Casino

The Northern Rivers region is known for its scenic beauty, and there are plenty of hiking and biking trails in and around Casino for those who love the great outdoors. Some of the best routes include:

  • Richmond River walk: This 4km track follows the scenic Richmond River, starting at the Casino CBD and heading west to the Jabiru genee habitat reserve.

  • Casino to Kyogle: This 35km route takes you through picturesque rural scenery, and the historic villages of Sextonville and Wadeville.

Best Things to Do in Casino: Activities and Tours

  • Lismore Regional Art Gallery: You can visit this eclectic regional gallery, which showcases works by local artists and traveling exhibitions.

  • Casino Miniature Railway and Museum: This miniature railway offers a fun day out for the whole family. It’s affordable, and you can ride on the train or use one of the many picnic facilities.

Week-Long Itinerary for Casino

If you’re planning a week-long visit to Casino, here’s a sample itinerary that you might want to consider:

  • Day 1: Visit Richmond River Historical Society Museum and stroll around the charming CBD.

  • Day 2: Hike to Flat Rock Lookout, check out Bungabee State Forest, and have a picnic at Jabiru genee habitat reserve.

  • Day 3: Take a day trip to Lismore Regional Art Gallery and Casino Miniature Railway and Museum.

  • Day 4: Bike to Kyogle and explore the historic villages.

  • Day 5: Visit Casino Folk Museum and take a leisurely hike to Tooloom Falls.

  • Day 6: Relax and enjoy a lazy day by the river or hit the town for some live music.

  • Day 7: Indulge in local delicacies and shop for souvenirs to bring home.

Food and Drink Scene of Casino

If you’re a foodie, you’ll love the dining scene in Casino. Here are some of the best restaurants and local dishes to try:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Meat pies: A traditional Australian dish, you can find savory meat pies sold at most bakeries, cafes, and restaurants in Casino.

  • Macadamias: This region is known for its macadamia nut production, and you can enjoy them in many forms, from oils to sweets.

Local Beverages

  • Bundjalung Wild Tea: Try this locally made tea blend, featuring a mix of native herbs and spices.

  • Little River Brewing Co.: Enjoy a range of artisan beers and ciders made right here in Casino.

Where to Stay in Casino: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options in Casino to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • Big 4 Casino Resort: Great for families, this resort has a playground, pool, and various types of cabins and powered sites.

  • River Park Motor Inn: This 4-star motel has spacious rooms, free Wi-Fi, and a restaurant on-site.

  • Casino Holiday Park: A fantastic place for camping, this holiday park has excellent facilities and is perfect for families.

What to Buy in Casino: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs or local products to bring home, here are some recommendations:

  • Macadamia nuts: This region is the largest macadamia nut producer in Australia, with plenty of varieties and products to choose from.

  • Aboriginal art and crafts: The region has a rich Aboriginal history, and you can find a range of authentic art and crafts from local artists.

Local Culture and Customs of Casino

The Northern Rivers region has a unique and rich cultural heritage, and it’s important to recognize and respect the local customs and traditions. Here are some guidelines to keep in mind:

  • It’s customary to acknowledge and show respect for the traditional custodians of the land, the Bundjalung people.

  • Be mindful of cultural sensitivities when visiting Aboriginal cultural sites or participating in cultural activities.

Best Time to Visit Casino

The best time to visit Casino is during the spring and autumn months, when the weather is mild and comfortable. Summer is excellent for swimming and water-based activities, while winter is perfect for exploring the national parks and taking peaceful bushwalks. Keep in mind, though, that regardless of the season, the Northern Rivers region is known for its abundance of rain.

Do’s and Don’ts of Casino

Here are some things to keep in mind during your visit to Casino:

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.

  • Take care of the environment and avoid leaving litter or disturbing wildlife.

Don’t:

  • Bring pets to national parks or protected areas.

  • Explore off-track or take shortcuts, as this could damage the ecosystem.

Safety and Health

Here are some tips for staying safe and healthy during your visit to Casino:

Safety in Casino

  • Wear sunscreen and appropriate clothing when spending time outdoors.

  • Take care when walking or hiking on uneven terrain.

Health in Casino

  • Bring medication if necessary, as pharmacies may have limited hours of operation.

  • Be aware of ticks and snakes, especially when hiking in national parks.

Getting to Casino: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here’s how to get to Casino:

Airports Near Casino

  • Lismore Regional Airport: Located 25km away from Casino, Lismore is the closest airport.

  • Gold Coast Airport: Located 148km away, the Gold Coast airport is a bit further but usually has more flights and airlines.

Bus Stations Near Casino

  • Casino Bus Terminal: Located in the town center, this is the main bus stop in Casino.

  • Greyhound Bus Station: The Greyhound bus stop is located on the outskirts of town, about 2km from the CBD.

Other Options

  • Car rental: Car rental options are available at Lismore Airport.

How to Get Around in Casino

The best way to get around Casino is by car, as public transportation options are limited. However, if you’re staying in town, walking, cycling or using taxis are reasonable options.

Where to Go Next from Casino: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions you might want to consider for your next trip:

  • Byron Bay: Visit this trendy coastal town known for its surfing, beaches, and vibrant nightlife. Takes an hour and a half by car.

  • Gold Coast: Spend some time exploring this bustling city and its iconic theme parks and beaches. Takes just over two hours by car.

  • Grafton: Explore this charming regional town with its beautiful historic architecture and beautiful surrounding countryside. Takes just over an hour by car.

Conclusion

Casino is a destination that offers endless possibilities for exploring, whether you’re looking for history, adventure, or relaxation. With its friendly locals, stunning scenery, and unique cultural heritage, Casino is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience the best of New South Wales, Australia.
