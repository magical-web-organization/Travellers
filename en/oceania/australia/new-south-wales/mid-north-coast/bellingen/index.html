Bellingen, situated in the Mid-North Coast of New South Wales, Australia, is a hidden gem that offers tourists an unforgettable experience like no other. Whether you’re a wildlife enthusiast, a culture lover, or simply looking for a relaxing getaway, Bellingen has something for everyone. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll give you all the information you need to plan a memorable trip to Bellingen.

Introduction to Bellingen

Bellingen is a small town that lies halfway between Brisbane and Sydney, making it a perfect stop for travelers making a road trip. The town is home to just over 3000 residents and has a rich history with Aboriginal heritage and European settlement in the 1840s.

Bellingen’s natural surroundings are simply spectacular, with the rolling hills of the Great Dividing Range on one side and the clear waters of the Bellinger River on the other. This stunning landscape, combined with the local arts and culture scene, delicious food and drink, and friendly locals, make Bellingen a must-visit destination for your next Australian adventure.

List of Places to Visit in Bellingen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Bellingen Museum: This museum showcases the historical past of Bellingen and the surrounding areas, with exhibits highlighting the area’s Aboriginal and settler heritage.
  • Hammond and Wheatley Emporium: Located in the town’s heritage-listed Hammond and Wheatley building, the emporium is a treasure trove of antiques, collectibles, and locally made crafts.
  • Urunga Boardwalk: This 1.2km boardwalk takes visitors on a walk through the mangroves, providing scenic views of the estuary along the way.

Natural Attractions of Bellingen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Dorrigo National Park: The park offers stunning rainforest scenery and cascading waterfalls, making it an excellent place for hiking, bird watching, and wildlife spotting.
  • Bellinger River National Park: Home to various native wildlife, including platypuses, wallabies, and possums.
  • The Promised Land: This picturesque spot has stunning waterfalls, crystal-clear swimming holes, and numerous picnic spots.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bellingen

  • The Never Never Creek Walk: Starting from the Northbank Road bridge over the never Never Creek, this trail meanders through pristine rainforest alongside the creek.
  • Waterfall Way: This scenic drive or bike route takes you past several waterfalls and stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
  • Bindarri National Park: Trail options through rainforests and along the eastern escarpment of the Great Dividing Range.

Best Things to Do in Bellingen: Activities and Tours

  • Rafting on the Nymboida River: The Nymboida River is known for its thrilling rapids, and rafting tours are available for all levels of adventurers.
  • Coffs Harbour Skydiving: For thrill-seekers, skydiving over the stunning NSW coastline is a must-do.
  • Horse riding tours: Tours are available to take visitors through the stunning countryside and forests of Bellingen.

Food and Drink Scene of Bellingen

Must-Try Dishes

  • The Purple Carrot: This vegetarian and vegan restaurant offers a range of tasty dishes, from burgers to salads, wraps, juices, and smoothies.
  • Cedar Bar and Kitchen: This cozy spot is known for its delicious range of pizzas and local craft beers.
  • Bellingen Gelato Bar: A perfect place to try fantastic ice cream flavors that are always the talk of the town!

Local Beverages

  • Bellingen Brewery and Co: A great local brewery that produces tasty craft beer offerings, including pale ales, IPAs, and seasonal beers.
  • The Happy Frog Kombucha: This local bottle shop offers a range of delicious probiotic kombucha drinks.

Where to Stay in Bellingen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Bellingen Valley Lodge: A charming boutique hotel situated in a peaceful rural setting.
  • Bellingen Backpackers: This budget-friendly hostel is located in the heart of the town and offers clean rooms and amenities.
  • The Diggers Tavern: A historic guesthouse, offering comfortable rooms and a pub-style restaurant serving delicious food and drinks.

What to Buy in Bellingen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Bellingen Market: Held every third Saturday of the month, this market is a great place to buy local produce, crafts, and souvenirs.
  • Bellingen Vintage: A great spot to buy vintage and second-hand clothing, accessories, and collectibles.
  • The Bellingen Brewery and Co: Bring home some locally brewed beers as a delicious souvenir of your trip.

Local Culture and Customs of Bellingen

Bellingen is a welcoming and friendly town, known for its artsy vibe and cultural events. The locals are proud of their heritage and are keen to share it with visitors.

Best Time to Visit Bellingen

The best time to visit Bellingen is during the autumn and spring seasons when the weather is mild and the town is not overrun with tourists.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bellingen

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Explore the great outdoors and take in the stunning natural scenery.
  • Support local businesses and artisans.

Don’t:

  • Litter in the natural environment.
  • Ignore local safety warnings and guidelines.
  • Be careless with your waste and conservation of natural resources.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bellingen

Bellingen is known for its friendly and safe environment. However, visitors are urged to be vigilant when enjoying outdoor activities and to take note of any safety warnings and guidelines given by local authorities.

Health in Bellingen

Bellingen has a medical center and a hospital that provide medical services to residents and visitors. It’s always wise to carry travel insurance that covers medical emergencies when visiting any destination.

Getting to Bellingen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bellingen

  • The nearest airport is Coffs Harbour Airport, situated about 28 kilometers away from Bellingen.
  • The second nearest airport is Port Macquarie Airport, situated approximately 142 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Bellingen

Greyhound Bus Services and Premier Motor Service provide bus transportation services to and from Bellingen. The closest bus stop is located in Coffs Harbour.

Other Options

Driving is also a great way to get to and explore Bellingen. Car rental services are available in various locations, including airports and central business districts across the region.

How to Get Around in Bellingen

Walking and cycling are great ways to get around Bellingen, with many attractions located within easy reach of the town’s center. Car rental services are also available for visitors who want to explore further afield.

Where to Go Next from Bellingen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Coffs Harbour: A nearby coastal town known for its stunning beaches and the iconic Big Banana landmark.
  • Port Macquarie: A small coastal town with beautiful beaches and plenty of outdoor adventure opportunities.
  • Kempsey: A historic town nearby with fascinating museums and heritage landmarks.

Conclusion

Bellingen is a unique and captivating destination that offers visitors a remarkable experience unlike any other. From natural attractions to cultural and historical highlights, outdoor adventures to delicious food and drink, Bellingen truly has something for everyone.
