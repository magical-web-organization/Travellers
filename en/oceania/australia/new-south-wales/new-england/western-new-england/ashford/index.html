Introduction to Ashford

Located in Western New England, Ashford is a quaint town that serves as the perfect destination for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. Home to stunning natural landscapes and historically significant landmarks, Ashford is a must-visit destination for every traveller.

  • The Ashford Heritage Trail
  • The Ashford War Memorial

Local Culture and Customs of Ashford

Ashford is known for its rich cultural heritage, with a range of cultural events and festivals taking place throughout the year. When in Ashford, take some time to explore the local culture and customs:

  • Attend the Ashford Arts and Crafts Festival
  • Learn about the local Aboriginal culture and history at the Ashford Cultural Centre

Best Time to Visit Ashford

The best time to visit Ashford is during the spring and autumn months (March-May, September-November) when the weather is mild, and the scenery is at its best.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ashford

Here are some of the do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Ashford:

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Take care of the environment and avoid littering
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local culture or engage in cultural appropriation
  • Speak loudly in public places

Safety and Health

Safety in Ashford

Ashford is a safe town, but basic safety precautions should still be taken, especially when hiking or engaging in outdoor activities. It is also important to always be aware of your surroundings and keep your valuables secure.
