Introduction to Bundarra

Bundarra is a small town located in Southern New England, New South Wales, Australia. The town is known for its idyllic scenery and natural beauty, attracting tourists from all over the world. Bundarra is situated on the eastern side of the Great Dividing Range, making it an ideal starting point for exploring the surrounding areas.

List of Places to Visit in Bundarra

Bundarra has a lot to offer to its visitors in terms of attractions and sightseeing options. Here are some top places you should consider visiting when in Bundarra:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Bundarra Historical Society Museum - This museum is a must-visit for history enthusiasts. It offers an insight into the town’s rich cultural history and showcases a variety of exhibits that date back to the settlement days.
  2. Bundarra Cemetery - A visit to the local cemetery is a great way to pay respects to the pioneers that helped shape the town. You can explore the grave markers and headstones and learn more about the town’s local history.

Natural Attractions of Bundarra: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Bundarra Nature Reserve - This reserve is located just outside of town and offers visitors a chance to explore the area’s flora and fauna. There are several walking trails to choose from, each offering a different experience. Bird watching and picnicking are also popular activities here.
  2. Tia Falls - The Tia Falls are a popular attraction for visitors to Bundarra. This impressive waterfall cascades over the granite rocks and offers stunning views of the valley below.
  3. Mt. Yarrowyck Nature Reserve - The Mt. Yarrowyck Nature Reserve is home to several walking trails that offer stunning panoramic views of the surrounding area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bundarra

Bundarra is a great place to explore on foot or by bike. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in the area:

  1. The Bundarra-Barraba Rail Trail - This is a 40 km long rail trail that takes visitors through the beautiful countryside. The complete trail is a two-day hike, but visitors can also choose to hike it in smaller sections.
  2. The Tia Falls Walk - This is an easy walk to the Tia Falls that offers stunning views of the waterfall and the surrounding area.
  3. Mt. Yarrowyck Trail - This is a moderate walk that takes visitors to the summit of Mt. Yarrowyck, offering breathtaking views of the town and surrounding area.

Best Things to Do in Bundarra: Activities and Tours

There are many activities and tours in Bundarra that cater to various interests. Here are some of the best things to do in Bundarra:

  1. Fishing - Bundarra is known for its excellent fishing opportunities. Grab a fishing rod and try your hand at catching Murray Cod, Yellowbelly, Redfin, and Trout in the Severn River and other nearby streams.
  2. Horse Riding - Horseback riding is a fantastic way to experience Bundarra’s natural scenery. Join a guided tour and explore the area on horseback.
  3. Sightseeing Tours - If you want to explore the area without getting your feet wet or getting too active, a sightseeing tour is a great way to see the sights and learn about the area’s rich history.

Food and Drink Scene of Bundarra

The food and drink scene in Bundarra is a reflection of the area’s local culture and history. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages in the area:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Murray Cod - A freshwater fish native to Australia, which is a popular dish in Bundarra.
  2. Lamb Roast - A traditional Australian dish that’s often served with roasted vegetables.
  3. Barramundi - Another freshwater fish local to the area and popular on menus in Bundarra.

Local Beverages

  1. Wines - The region is famous for its wines, and you can sample the various varieties at several local wineries.
  2. Craft beers - Local breweries produce unique craft beers that you can enjoy at the local pubs and restaurants.

Where to Stay in Bundarra: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bundarra has a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and tastes. Some of the top places to stay include:

  1. The Bundarra Motel - This motel is centrally located and offers comfortable and affordable accommodation.
  2. The Yarrowyck Inn - This historical inn offers guests a unique experience with its rustic decor and cosy rooms.
  3. The Paddock - This is a great option for travellers who want to experience life on a farm. Visitors can stay in their unique glamping tents and enjoy all the amenities that the farm has to offer.

What to Buy in Bundarra: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When visiting Bundarra, you won’t want to leave without taking some souvenirs or local products with you. Here are some great things you can buy in Bundarra:

  1. Local honey - The area around Bundarra is known for its honey production, and you can find jars of it at several local markets.
  2. Handmade crafts - You can find handmade crafts, such as pottery and jewellery, at several local markets and shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Bundarra

Bundarra’s local culture is a reflection of the town’s rich history and heritage. Visitors can learn about the indigenous people, settlers, and other groups that have shaped the area over the years. The local community is known for being friendly and welcoming to visitors, making it easy to immerse yourself in the local culture.

Best Time to Visit Bundarra

The best time to visit Bundarra is in the spring and summer months (September to February) when the weather is mild, and the countryside is in full bloom. Autumn and winter months can be chilly, but also offer unique experiences such as crackling fires and crisp morning walks.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bundarra

Bundarra is considered a very safe destination for tourists. Visitors are encouraged to pay attention to their surroundings, especially when exploring natural attractions or taking part in outdoor activities.

Health in Bundarra

Visitors should take precautions against sunburn and insect bites when exploring the natural environment. It’s also recommended to carry an adequate amount of water with you when taking part in outdoor activities.

Getting to Bundarra: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bundarra

The closest airport to Bundarra is the Armidale Regional Airport, located approximately 80 km away.

Bus Stations Near Bundarra

Interstate buses stop in Armidale, and there are several services that run from Armidale to Bundarra.

Other Options

You can drive to Bundarra, with the nearest major cities being Armidale (80 km away), Tamworth (120 km away), and Newcastle (400 km away).

How to Get Around in Bundarra

The best way to get around Bundarra is by car. Visitors can hire a car or take a taxi to explore the local attractions and scenery.

Where to Go Next from Bundarra: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  2. Tamworth - Known as the “Country Music Capital of Australia,” is a vibrant city with a rich cultural scene.
  3. The Hume Highway Route - A scenic drive that takes you through some of the most beautiful countryside in the region.
