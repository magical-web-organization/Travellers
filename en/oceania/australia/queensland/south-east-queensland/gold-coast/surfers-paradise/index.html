Introduction to Surfers Paradise

Surfers Paradise is a stunning coastal suburb of the Gold Coast city in South East Queensland, Australia. With its tall skyline buildings, breathtaking scenery, and endless activities, Surfers Paradise has earned the title of the tourist hotspot of the Gold Coast. This guide will take you through the best places to visit, things to do, what to eat and drink, where to stay, safety tips and much more.
