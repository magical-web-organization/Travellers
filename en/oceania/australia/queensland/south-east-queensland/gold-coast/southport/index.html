Introduction to Southport

Southport, located on the Gold Coast in Queensland, Australia, is a beautiful destination that has much to offer to its visitors. This vibrant suburb is well-known for its cultural landmarks, natural attractions, and diverse range of activities and tours. With its breathtaking scenery, warm climate, and friendly locals, Southport is the perfect place to unwind, relax, and explore.

List of Places to Visit in Southport

If you are planning a trip to Southport, you won’t want to miss these top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Southport Courthouse and Police Museum
  • St. Hilda’s School
  • Arts Centre Gold Coast

Natural Attractions of Southport: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Broadwater Parklands
  • Marina Mirage
  • Spit Beach

Hiking/Biking Routes of Southport

  • Broadwater Parklands Walk and Cycle Track
  • Seaworld Drive Recreational Trail

Best Things to Do in Southport: Activities and Tours

  • Water Activities: Kayaking, Stand-up Paddleboarding, and Fishing
  • Sightseeing Tours: Nature Walks, Cruises, and City Tours
  • Theme Parks: Warner Bros. Movie World, Dreamworld, and Seaworld

Week-Long Itinerary for Southport

Day 1: Visit the Southport Courthouse and Police Museum, followed by a picnic at Broadwater Parklands

Day 2: Spend the day at Marina Mirage, a luxury shopping and dining precinct

Day 3: Enjoy a scenic cruise around Gold Coast and its beaches

Day 4: Take a nature walk along Broadwater Parklands Walk and Cycle Track

Day 5: Visit Warner Bros. Movie World, an iconic theme park in Southport

Day 6: Relax at Spit Beach, a tranquil and secluded destination

Day 7: Explore the city on a panoramic city tour featuring popular landmarks and streets.
