Introduction to Paluma

Located in Central Queensland, Paluma is a charming mountain village that offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. This quiet town is surrounded by lush rainforest, making it a great destination for hiking, bird watching, and other outdoor activities. Despite being a small town, Paluma has a rich history and culture, making it a notable place to visit.

Safety and Health

Safety in Paluma

Paluma is generally a safe place, but visitors should be mindful of hiking and walking on trails alone, especially at night.

Health in Paluma

Visitors should carry insect repellent to protect against mosquito bites and avoid drinking unsafe tap water.
