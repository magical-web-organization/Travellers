Introduction to Murray Bridge

Murray Bridge is a charming town situated in the Murray and Mallee Riverland region of South Australia. With a rich history dating back to the 1800s, this once small community has grown into a thriving regional centre. It’s situated on the banks of the Murray River and is surrounded by gorgeous landscapes. Visitors can enjoy the mild climate, warm hospitality, and a range of outdoor activities.

Local Culture and Customs of Murray Bridge

The local culture and customs of Murray Bridge is a mix of European and Aboriginal influences. The town has a great appreciation for the Murray River and history of the area.
