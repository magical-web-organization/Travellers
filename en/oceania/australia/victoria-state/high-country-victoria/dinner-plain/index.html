Introduction to Dinner Plain

Located in the picturesque High Country region of Victoria, Dinner Plain is a charming alpine village that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures. With its stunning vistas, quaint streetscapes, and vibrant community, Dinner Plain is the ideal destination for travelers looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and experience the beauty and tranquility of the Australian Alps.

List of Places to Visit in Dinner Plain

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Onsen Retreat + Spa: This luxurious spa offers a range of treatments and therapies inspired by the traditional Japanese onsen experience. With its serene atmosphere and stunning views of the surrounding alpine landscape, Onsen Retreat + Spa is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking relaxation and rejuvenation.
  • Dinner Plain Museum: Explore the local history of the Dinner Plain region at this fascinating museum. From the indigenous tribes that populated the area for thousands of years to the early pioneers of the 19th century, the Dinner Plain Museum offers a rich and diverse glimpse into the cultural heritage of this unique alpine town.

Natural Attractions of Dinner Plain: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Hotham Alpine Resort: Just a short drive from Dinner Plain, Mount Hotham is Victoria’s highest alpine resort and the perfect destination for skiing, snowboarding, and other winter sports. With over 320 hectares of terrain and an average snowfall of 3 meters per season, Mount Hotham is a winter wonderland that offers endless opportunities for exploration, adventure, and fun.
  • The High Plains: The High Plains of the Alpine National Park offer some of the most stunning natural scenery in Australia. From the rolling meadows and wildflowers of the summer months to the crisp, snow-covered peaks of the winter season, the High Plains are a paradise for hikers, walkers, and nature lovers of all kinds.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dinner Plain

  • The Razorback Trail: This challenging trail offers a breathtaking trek along the ridge of the Mount Feathertop massif. With sweeping views of the surrounding alpine landscape and a variety of unique flora and fauna, the Razorback Trail is an unforgettable hike for experienced hikers and outdoor enthusiasts.
  • The Alpine Walking Track: The Alpine Walking Track is Australia’s premier long-distance hiking trail, spanning over 655 kilometers of stunning alpine terrain. With a variety of difficulty levels and scenic highlights along the way, this iconic trail is a must-do for any serious hiker or adventurer.

Best Things to Do in Dinner Plain: Activities and Tours

  • Horseback Riding: Explore the pristine beauty of the Alpine National Park on horseback with one of the many guided tours available in the area. With a range of options for riders of all levels, horseback riding is a unique and unforgettable way to experience the natural splendor of Dinner Plain.
  • Sled Dog Tours: Experience the thrill and excitement of sled dog racing with a guided tour through the alpine wilderness. Led by experienced mushers and their teams of highly-trained dogs, these tours offer a unique and exhilarating way to explore the beauty of the Australian Alps.

Food and Drink Scene of Dinner Plain

Must-Try Dishes

  • Alpine pizza: A local favorite, the Alpine pizza is a delicious combination of rich cheese, savory meats, and fresh local veggies. Served piping hot and perfect for sharing, this classic dish is a must-try for any foodie visiting Dinner Plain.
  • Mountain trout: Sourced from the pristine streams and rivers that flow through the alpine wilderness, mountain trout is a delicacy that captures the unique flavors and freshness of the Australian Alps. Served grilled or pan-fried, this delicious fish is a must-try for seafood lovers.

Local Beverages

  • Alpine beer: Made with pure alpine water and locally-sourced hops and malts, alpine beer is a refreshing and delicious way to experience the unique flavors of the Dinner Plain region. From light and crisp lagers to rich and robust stouts, there’s a beer for every taste and preference.
  • Mulled wine: Perfect for chilly winter evenings, mulled wine is a warm and comforting drink that combines rich red wine with a variety of spices and aromatics. Served hot and delicious, this classic alpine drink is a must-try for any winter visitor to Dinner Plain.

Where to Stay in Dinner Plain: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Alpenglow Alpine Resort: This luxurious resort offers elegant rooms and suites with stunning mountain views, a range of on-site dining options, and a host of amenities and services designed to make your stay in Dinner Plain unforgettable.
  • Snowgum Lodge: This cozy and welcoming guesthouse offers comfortable accommodations and a warm and friendly atmosphere that’s perfect for family vacations and romantic getaways alike.

What to Buy in Dinner Plain: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Local honey: Made from the nectar of alpine wildflowers and plants, local honey is a sweet and delicious souvenir that captures the unique flavors and scents of the Australian Alps.
  • Handmade candles: Made with natural wax and essential oils, handmade candles are a fragrant and beautiful way to remember your visit to Dinner Plain.

Local Culture and Customs of Dinner Plain

Dinner Plain is a small, tight-knit alpine community that prides itself on its rich cultural heritage and commitment to sustainable living. Visitors are encouraged to respect the natural environment and participate in community events and activities whenever possible.

Best Time to Visit Dinner Plain

The best time to visit Dinner Plain depends largely on your interests and preferences. The winter months (June-August) are ideal for skiing, snowboarding, and other winter sports, while the summer months (December-February) are perfect for hiking, camping, and outdoor adventures of all kinds.

Do’s and Don’ts of Dinner Plain

Do:

  • Respect the natural environment and local wildlife.
  • Participate in community events and cultural activities.
  • Embrace the unique alpine culture and way of life.

Don’t:

  • Engage in irresponsible or destructive behavior.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  • Ignore safety regulations and guidelines.

Safety and Health

Safety in Dinner Plain

Dinner Plain is a safe and welcoming community, but visitors should be aware of the risks associated with alpine environments and take appropriate precautions when engaging in outdoor activities.

Health in Dinner Plain

Dinner Plain offers a range of healthcare services, including a medical center and pharmacy, but visitors should also bring any necessary medication or medical supplies with them and be prepared for potential emergencies.

How to Get Around in Dinner Plain

Dinner Plain is a small village that can easily be explored on foot or by bicycle. There are also a number of shuttle services and taxis available for longer distances or for those who require additional assistance.

Where to Go Next from Dinner Plain: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Bright: Located just 10 kilometers from Dinner Plain, the charming town of Bright offers a range of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and shopping and dining options.
  • Mount Buffalo National Park: This stunning national park offers a range of outdoor adventures, including hiking, cycling, and rock climbing, as well as unparalleled views of the surrounding alpine landscape.
