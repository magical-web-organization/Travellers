Your Ultimate Travel Guide to Cann River, Australia

Introduction to Cann River

Cann River is a small town located in East Gippsland, Victoria, Australia. It is known for its natural beauty and outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, and camping. The town is home to around 200 residents and is a popular destination for those seeking a break from city life.

List of Places to Visit in Cann River

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Cann River Historical Museum: Take a trip back in time and explore the history of Cann River through a collection of artefacts, photos, and documents.
  • Wallagaraugh River Boat Ramp: Discover the significance of this historic boat ramp, which was used to carry supplies and produce along the river.

Natural Attractions of Cann River: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Croajingolong National Park: Explore the pristine wilderness of this national park, which boasts diverse ecosystems and abundant wildlife.
  • Point Hicks Lighthouse: Take a tour of this historic lighthouse and enjoy panoramic views of the rugged coastline.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Cann River

  • The Wilderness Coast Walk: This 100-kilometre trail follows the coastline from Sydenham Inlet to Wonboyn Lake and offers stunning views of the ocean and hinterland.
  • Cann River Rainforest Walk: This 2.2-kilometre walk takes you through an ancient temperate rainforest, where you can observe rare wildlife such as the yellow-bellied glider.

Best Things to Do in Cann River: Activities and Tours

  • Fishing: The Cann River is home to a variety of freshwater fish including Blackfish, Trout and Bream.
  • Camping: There are several campgrounds in the area that offer access to the wilderness and outdoor activities.
  • Guided Tours: Join a guided tour to explore the natural and cultural attractions of Cann River.

Food and Drink Scene of Cann River

Must-Try Dishes

  • Gippsland cheese: Sample some of the award-winning cheeses produced in the Gippsland region.
  • Oysters: Enjoy fresh oysters harvested from the nearby estuaries.

Local Beverages

  • Wine: The East Gippsland region is known for its cool-climate wines, particularly Chardonnay and Pinot Noir.
  • Craft Beer: Sample locally brewed beers that showcase unique flavours of the area.

Where to Stay in Cann River: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Swan Reach Hotel: A quaint country-style hotel that offers comfortable and affordable accommodation.
  • The Cann Valley Motel: A family-friendly motel that offers clean and spacious rooms, and a range of amenities.

What to Buy in Cann River: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Local honey: Indulge in some delicious honey that is produced by nearby apiaries.
  • Handmade crafts: Purchase unique handmade crafts created by local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Cann River

The people of Cann River pride themselves on their strong community spirit. Visitors are often welcomed warmly by the locals and encouraged to participate in community events.

Best Time to Visit Cann River

The best time to visit Cann River is during the months of September to November, when the weather is mild and the wildflowers are in bloom.

Do’s and Don’ts of Cann River

Do:

  • Respect the natural environment by disposing of rubbish responsibly.
  • Support local businesses by purchasing locally made products.

Don’t:

  • Harm wildlife by feeding or disturbing them.
  • Trespass on private property.

Safety and Health

Safety in Cann River

  • Be aware of the potential for bushfires during the summer months.
  • Take appropriate precautions when engaging in outdoor activities, such as hiking and fishing.

Health in Cann River

  • The nearest hospital is located in Orbost, which is approximately 50 kilometres away.

Getting to Cann River: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Cann River

  • Melbourne Airport: Approximately 350 kilometres away.

Bus Stations Near Cann River

  • Bairnsdale: Approximately 100 kilometres away.
  • Orbost: Approximately 50 kilometres away.

Other Options

  • Driving: Cann River is approximately a 4-hour drive from Melbourne.

How to Get Around in Cann River

The best way to get around Cann River is by car, as public transportation is limited.

Where to Go Next from Cann River: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Orbost: A town located along the Snowy River, which offers abundant outdoor activities and cultural attractions.
  • Mallacoota: A coastal town that offers stunning beaches and water activities.

Conclusion

Cann River is a hidden gem in the Gippsland region, offering visitors a chance to explore pristine wilderness, rich history, and warm locals. Whether you are seeking outdoor adventures or a peaceful retreat, Cann River has something to offer.
