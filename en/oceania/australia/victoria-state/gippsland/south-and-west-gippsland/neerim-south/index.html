Introduction to Neerim South

Located in the scenic region of South and West Gippsland, Neerim South is a charming town that offers visitors a glimpse into the natural beauty, rich history, and cultural heritage of Victoria. With its warm and friendly atmosphere, Neerim South is perfect for a weekend getaway or a week-long vacation.

List of Places to Visit in Neerim South

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Neerim South and District Museum

Housed in a heritage-listed building, the Neerim South and District Museum is dedicated to preserving the town’s history and showcasing its cultural heritage. The museum features a collection of photographs, artifacts, and exhibits that offer insights into the lives of early settlers and their contributions to the community.

Tarago Reservoir Park

Surrounded by rolling hills and towering trees, Tarago Reservoir Park is a popular destination for picnics, hiking, and nature walks. The park also has a rich history dating back to the 1930s when it was built as a water supply reservoir for Melbourne. Today, visitors can explore the park’s trails, enjoy the scenic views, and learn about the park’s origins through its historical exhibits.

Natural Attractions of Neerim South: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Mount Baw Baw

The majestic Mount Baw Baw is the perfect destination for adventure seekers. Visitors can hike, bike, ski, and snowboard their way through the mountain’s trails, slopes, and peaks. With its stunning vistas, diverse flora and fauna, and thrilling activities, Mount Baw Baw is a must-visit attraction for anyone who loves the great outdoors.

Toorongo Falls Reserve

Surrounded by lush rainforests and cascading waterfalls, Toorongo Falls Reserve is a natural wonderland that offers visitors a chance to reconnect with nature. The reserve features picturesque walking trails, picnic areas, and scenic lookout points that offer stunning views of the falls and the surrounding landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Neerim South

Mount Worth State Park

Featuring over 17km of hiking and biking trails, Mount Worth State Park is a paradise for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. The park’s trails wind through serene forests, serene valleys, and hilltops, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

Neerim South Rail Trail

The Neerim South Rail Trail is a scenic biking route that follows the old railway line from Neerim South to Warragul. The trail is relatively easy and suitable for all skill levels, making it a popular destination for families and casual bikers.

Best Things to Do in Neerim South: Activities and Tours

Horseback Riding at Jindivick

For visitors who want to experience the countryside in a unique way, horseback riding at Jindivick is a must-do activity. The tours take visitors through some of the region’s most scenic landscapes, including rolling hillsides, lush forests, and pristine lakes.

Wine Tasting at Brandy Creek Estate

Located in nearby Drouin East, Brandy Creek Estate is a family-owned winery that offers visitors a chance to sample some of the regions’ finest wines. The estate also features a restaurant, cellar door, and cheese room, where visitors can indulge in delicious food and drinks while enjoying the stunning views of the vineyards.

Food and Drink Scene of Neerim South

Must-Try Dishes

Gippsland Cheese

Gippsland is known for producing some of the best cheeses in the world, and Neerim South is no exception. Visitors can sample delicious artisanal cheeses at many local cheese makers, including Jindi Cheese and Prom Country Cheese.

Gourmet Pies

Made with fresh local ingredients, Neerim South’s gourmet pies are a must-try for foodies. Some of the best places to sample these delectable treats include Neerim South Bakery and the Neerim South Hotel.

Local Beverages

Gippsland Wine

Gippsland is home to some of Australia’s most celebrated wineries, and Neerim South is no exception. Visitors can taste a range of award-winning wines at Brandy Creek Estate and other nearby wineries.

Craft Beer

Neerim South’s local brewery, the Brindle Distillery, offers visitors a chance to taste a range of handcrafted beers. Visitors can also enjoy their beers in the brewery’s spacious outdoor beer garden.

Where to Stay in Neerim South: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Neerim Country Cottages

Nestled in rolling farmland, Neerim Country Cottages offers visitors a serene and peaceful retreat. The cottages are surrounded by lush gardens and have all the modern amenities, making it an ideal place to relax and unwind.

Neerim Park Family Accommodation

Located just a short walk from the town center, Neerim Park Family Accommodation is perfect for families or groups of friends. The property features spacious and well-appointed accommodation, as well as a range of on-site facilities, including a pool and barbecue area.

What to Buy in Neerim South: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Gippsland Cheese and Deli Products

Neerim South is home to some of the best cheese and deli products in the region. Visitors can take home an assortment of artisanal cheeses and local products, including jams, chutneys, and pickles.

Local Artwork and Crafts

Neerim South has a thriving arts and crafts community, and visitors can buy a range of unique and handmade artwork, jewelry, and textiles at local galleries and markets.

Local Culture and Customs of Neerim South

Neerim South has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its architecture, festivals, and local traditions. Visitors are encouraged to explore the town’s history and participate in local events and activities to get a deeper understanding of its culture and customs.

Best Time to Visit Neerim South

The best time to visit Neerim South is during the spring (September to November) and autumn (March to May) seasons. During these seasons, the weather is mild, and the countryside is at its most picturesque.

Do’s and Don’ts of Neerim South

Do:

  • Respect the natural environment and follow the leave-no-trace principles.
  • Try the local food and drinks, such as cheese and craft beers.
  • Learn about the history and culture of the town.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural environment.
  • Disrespect the locals or their customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Neerim South

Neerim South is a safe destination for travelers. However, visitors should take basic safety precautions, such as locking their vehicles and keeping their valuables safe.

Health in Neerim South

The region has several medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics, that offer a range of healthcare services. Visitors are advised to have travel insurance and to carry any necessary medication with them.

Getting to Neerim South: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Neerim South

The nearest major airport to Neerim South is Melbourne Airport, which is about 120km away. From the airport, visitors can take a car, taxi or bus to Neerim South.

Bus Stations Near Neerim South

There are several bus stations in nearby towns, including Warragul, which is about 25km away from Neerim South. From there, visitors can take a local bus or taxi to Neerim South.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Neerim South from Melbourne or other parts of Victoria.

How to Get Around in Neerim South

The best way to get around Neerim South is by car or bike. Visitors can also walk around the town center, which is relatively small and pedestrian-friendly.

Where to Go Next from Neerim South: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Melbourne

Located just 120km away, Melbourne is a vibrant and bustling city that offers visitors a range of cultural, culinary, and shopping experiences.

Wilsons Promontory National Park

Wilsons Promontory National Park is a stunning natural reserve that features some of the most beautiful beaches, forests, and wildlife in Australia. The park is located about 140km from Neerim South.

Conclusion

Neerim South is a hidden gem in the heart of Victoria. With its spectacular natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and friendly locals, the town offers visitors a unique and unforgettable travel experience. Whether you are looking for a relaxing retreat or an action-packed adventure, Neerim South has something for everyone.
