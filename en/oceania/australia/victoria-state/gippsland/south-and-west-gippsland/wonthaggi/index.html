Introduction to Wonthaggi

Wonthaggi is a charming town located in South and West Gippsland, in Gippsland, Victoria (state), Australia. It is situated at the foothills of the Strzelecki Ranges and offers a unique blend of natural beauty, history, and culture. The town is known for its rich coal mining history and its famous State Coal Mine. Wonthaggi is a popular destination for tourists who want to explore the great outdoors, learn about the town’s rich history, and immerse themselves in local culture.

List of Places to Visit in Wonthaggi

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Wonthaggi has a rich coal mining history and many museums and historical sites celebrate the town’s past. Some of the must-visit museums and historical places in Wonthaggi include:

  • State Coal Mine Wonthaggi: A former coal mine that is now a museum and home to an underground tour that takes you through the coal mine shafts.
  • Wonthaggi Railway Station Museum: A museum that showcases the history of Wonthaggi’s rail industry.
  • Wonthaggi Life Saving Museum: A museum that tells the history of lifesaving in the area and the technology that was developed to use the resources of the area.
  • Cape Paterson Lighthouse: A lighthouse that provides a stunning view of the Bass Strait and the sealing industry.

Natural Attractions of Wonthaggi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Wonthaggi has many natural attractions that offer the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Some of the must-visit outdoor attractions in Wonthaggi include:

  • The Bass Coast Rail Trail: A 16km walking, cycling, and horse-riding trail that runs from Wonthaggi to Anderson.
  • Bunurong Coastal Drive: A scenic drive that provides a stunning view of the rugged coastline and the Bass Strait.
  • Wonthaggi Wetlands: A hidden gem that offers a peaceful walk and the opportunity to see many bird species.
  • Inverloch Beach: A popular beach that is perfect for swimming, surfing, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Wonthaggi

Wonthaggi has many trails that are perfect for hiking and cycling. Some of the best hiking and biking routes in Wonthaggi include:

  • George Bass Coastal Walk: A 7km coastal walk that offers stunning views of the Southern Ocean and the Bass Strait.
  • Rail Trail Loop: A 28km loop that passes through the beautiful rural and coastal areas around Wonthaggi.
  • Lovers Walk Trail: A 3.5km walk that takes you through the hills near Wonthaggi and provides beautiful views of the town.

Best Things to Do in Wonthaggi: Activities and Tours

Wonthaggi has many activities and tours that are perfect for tourists who want to experience the best of the town. Some of the top things to do in Wonthaggi include:

  • Take a tour of the State Coal Mine Wonthaggi
  • Visit the Wonthaggi Farmers Market held every last Sunday of the month.
  • Go fishing at Inverloch Beach
  • Explore the rugged coastline on the Bunurong Coastal Drive
  • Take a scenic flight over the Bass Strait

Wonthaggi has a plethora of food options that cater to all tastes. Some of the must-try dishes in Wonthaggi include:

  • Fish and Chips: Wonthaggi is close to the ocean, so fresh fish and chips are a popular food option.
  • Gippsland Beef Burger: A juicy burger made using the best beef produced in the Gippsland region.
  • Gourmet Pies: Wonthaggi has a range of cafes and bakeries that serve delicious gourmet pies.

Local Beverages

Wonthaggi is home to a few breweries and wineries that are worth visiting for all wine and beer lovers. Some of the top breweries and wineries in the area include:
