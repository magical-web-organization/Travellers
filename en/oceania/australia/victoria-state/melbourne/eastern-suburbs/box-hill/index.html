Introduction to Box Hill

Box Hill is a beautiful suburb located in Melbourne’s eastern suburbs region, in the state of Victoria, Australia. With its lush green parks, stunning views, and rich cultural heritage, Box Hill is an ideal destination for nature lovers, history buffs, and adventure seekers alike. From hiking and biking in the Box Hill reserve to exploring the local food and drink scene, there’s something for everyone in this charming suburb.

List of Places to Visit in Box Hill

Whether you’re interested in historical sites, art and culture, or outdoor adventures, there’s no shortage of places to visit in Box Hill. Here are some must-see attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Box Hill Historical Society: This museum is dedicated to preserving the history of Box Hill and its surrounding areas. Visitors can learn about the first European settlers, the local indigenous people, and the development of the suburb through photographs, documents, and artifacts.

  2. Box Hill Town Hall: This iconic building was constructed in 1935 and is a fine example of art deco architecture. It served as a town hall until the local council moved to a new location in 1994 and is now used as a cultural center.

  3. Box Hill Central: This bustling shopping center is located in the heart of Box Hill and offers a wide range of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options.

Natural Attractions of Box Hill: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Box Hill reserve: This large park covers over 170 acres and offers a range of activities for visitors, including hiking, mountain biking, and bird watching. It’s also home to a diverse range of flora and fauna and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding areas.

  2. Surrey Dive: This popular swimming hole is located within the Box Hill reserve and is a great place for a refreshing dip on a hot day. There are also plenty of picnic areas and BBQ facilities for visitors to enjoy.

  3. Gardiners Creek Trail: This scenic trail follows the Gardiners Creek and offers stunning views of the surrounding areas. Visitors can walk, run, or cycle along the trail and take in the natural beauty of Box Hill.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Box Hill

Box Hill reserve is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and offers a range of hiking and biking trails that wind through the park. There are trails suitable for all levels of fitness and skill, from gentle strolls to challenging mountain bike routes. Some of the most popular trails include:

  • Box Hill Circuit Walk: This 6.1-kilometer trail takes visitors on a loop through the Box Hill reserve, passing by scenic lookouts, a tranquil lake, and dense forests.
  • Box Hill Mountain Bike Trail: This challenging trail is perfect for experienced mountain bikers and features steep climbs, technical descents, and challenging obstacles.
  • Gardiners Creek Trail: This 18-kilometer trail follows the Gardiners Creek and offers stunning views of the surrounding areas. Visitors can walk, run, or cycle along the trail and take in the natural beauty of Box Hill.
