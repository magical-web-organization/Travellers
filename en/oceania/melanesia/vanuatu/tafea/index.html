Introduction to Tafea

Located in the southernmost part of Vanuatu, Tafea is a province that comprises eight islands in Melanesia, Oceania. The islands feature stunning landscapes, an array of activities, and a rich and vibrant culture. Visitors can enjoy the beautiful beaches, lush rainforests, and amazing wildlife. Explore the traditional ways of life, local customs and cultures, and discover the unique history of this region. Plan your trip to Tafea and get ready for an unforgettable adventure.

List of Places to Visit in Tafea

Here are some of the top attractions that should be on your list of places to visit in Tafea:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Custom Village: An amazing opportunity to experience the traditions and customs of local people. Visitors to Tanna can explore various custom villages that offer a glimpse into the traditional Ni-Vanuatu way of life.

  • Chief Roi Mata’s Domain: This UNESCO World Heritage site is the location of Chief Roi Mata’s royal tomb. Visitors can take a guided tour through the forests and learn about the culture and history of the area.

  • Nakamal: The Nakamal is a sacred gathering place for men in Vanuatu. Visitors can appreciate the traditional architecture of the Nakamals and witness the solemn ceremonies that take place here.

Natural Attractions of Tafea: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Yasur: Tafea boasts a live volcano, Mount Yasur, which is one of the most popular attractions in Vanuatu. Visitors can take a tour to the top of the volcano and view the incredible fiery display put on by the natural wonder.

  • Port Resolution: A small coastal village on Tanna Island, Port Resolution is considered to be one of the most beautiful in Vanuatu. A trek to the top of the nearby volcano offers stunning views.

  • Lenakel Market: Located on the island of Tanna, Lenakel Market sells fresh produce and handicrafts. Visitors can interact with the locals and buy unique and authentic souvenirs.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tafea

Tafea is a great place for hiking, biking, and trekking. Visitors can take advantage of the numerous trails and paths that wind through the lush rainforests, across the mountains, and around the islands. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Mount Yasur: A hike to the summit of Mount Yasur is an unforgettable experience. Visitors can witness the fiery display of the volcano while enjoying incredible views of the surrounding countryside.

  • Louniel Surf Break: A great surfing spot located on the northeastern coast of Tanna Island. A day trip to the area is perfect for surfers of all levels.

Best Things to Do in Tafea: Activities and Tours

Tafea offers a wide variety of activities and tours, so there is something for everyone. Here are some of the top things to do:

  • Diving and Snorkeling: The waters around Tafea are home to a stunning variety of marine life, from vibrant coral reefs to schools of tropical fish. Visitors can take a snorkeling or diving tour to explore this underwater paradise.

  • Cultural Tours: To experience the traditional way of life on Tafea Islands, visitors can take a cultural tour that highlights the local customs, traditions, and culture.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tafea

Day 1: Arrive in Tafea and check in to your accommodation. Explore the surroundings and relax for the day.

Day 2: Take a guided tour of Mount Yasur.

Day 3: Take a diving or snorkeling tour of the amazing underwater wonders around Tafea Islands.

Day 4: Take a cultural tour of one of the traditional Tafea villages and experience the local way of life and customs.

Day 5: Take a hiking or biking tour of the lush rainforests and beautiful terrain surrounding Port Resolution.

Day 6: Visit one of the local markets to buy unique souvenirs and handicrafts made by the locals.

Day 7: Take a day trip to one of the nearby islands.

Food and Drink Scene of Tafea

Must-Try Dishes

Tafea offers an incredible variety of delicious food, from fresh seafood to tasty fruits and vegetables. Here are some of the must-try dishes:

  • Lap Lap: A traditional Ni-Vanuatu dish made from grated taro, yam, or sweet potato and coconut milk, then baked in banana leaves.

  • Kokoda: A popular dish made from raw fish marinated in lime juice, coconut cream, and a variety of herbs and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Kava: Tafea is known for its kava, which is a traditional drink made from the root of the kava plant. It is believed to have a calming effect and is often consumed in a social setting.

Where to Stay in Tafea: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Tafea offers a range of accommodation options, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are some of the top options:

  • Tanna Lodge: A relaxed lodge located on the beautiful island of Tanna, with breathtaking views of Yasur volcano.

  • Fatumaru Lodge: A stylish and luxurious oceanfront accommodation located on the island of Efate, offering an excellent place to stay while exploring Tafea.

What to Buy in Tafea: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to Tafea can buy a range of souvenirs and local products to take home, including:

  • Custom Carvings: Crafted from wood, these carvings depict the significant cultural symbols that are part of the local way of life.

  • Local Handicrafts: Unique woven baskets, mats, and other woven products made by the local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Tafea

Tafea is known for its rich and vibrant culture. Visitors can discover the unique customs and traditions of the locals, including:

  • Kava Ceremonies: Visitors can take part in a traditional kava ceremony, which is a unique cultural experience in the region.

  • Custom Dances: Tafea is known for its traditional custom dances, which are performed during important events and occasions.

Best Time to Visit Tafea

The best time to visit Tafea is between April and October when the climate is cooler and drier. However, visitors should be aware that the weather can still be unpredictable, so it’s best to come prepared with proper attire and equipment.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tafea

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions: Tafea is known for its unique and vibrant culture, and visitors should respect local customs and traditions.

  • Engage with the locals: Tafea is known for its friendly and welcoming people, so visitors should take advantage of the opportunity to engage with them.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing: Visitors should dress modestly and avoid wearing revealing clothing when visiting the islands.

  • Take photographs of locals without asking: Visitors should always ask permission before taking photos of the locals and their customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tafea

Tafea is generally a safe place to visit, but visitors should still exercise caution and take the necessary safety precautions. These include:

  • Be aware of your surroundings: Visitors should always be aware of their surroundings and avoid any areas that may be dangerous or unsafe.

  • Follow local laws and customs: Visitors should be aware of local laws and customs and abide by them at all times.

Health in Tafea

Visitors to Tafea should take appropriate measures to ensure their health and safety. This includes taking recommended vaccinations and being prepared for any potential health risks, such as mosquito-borne illnesses.

Getting to Tafea: Transportation Options and Logistics

Tafea is accessible by boat and plane.

Here are some transportation options to help you plan your trip:

Airports Near Tafea

  • Tanna Airport: Served by Air Vanuatu and Airports Vanuatu Limited, and offering direct flights from Port Vila.
  • Santo Airport: Served by Air Vanuatu, and offering direct flights from Port Vila.

Bus Stations Near Tafea

Tafea has local bus services that connect the various towns and villages around the islands.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Tafea by boat, with regular services and charter companies offering tours and trips.

How to Get Around in Tafea

There are several transportation options available to visitors in Tafea. These include:

  • Taxis: Taxis are readily available on the islands, and they can be used to get around the towns and villages.

  • Local buses: Local buses are available and provide cheap transportation services.

Where to Go Next from Tafea: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Tafea is located in a region of incredible natural beauty, and there are several nearby destinations and excursions that visitors can explore, including:

  • Port Vila: The capital city of Vanuatu is located on the island of Efate and features stunning beaches and a vibrant nightlife.

  • Espiritu Santo: The largest island in Vanuatu, Espiritu Santo boasts some of the best snorkeling and diving sites in the region.

Conclusion

Tafea is a destination that offers visitors some of the most beautiful and unique attractions in the world. Visitors can experience the natural beauty, rich culture, and customs of the local people, and enjoy an unforgettable adventure in this stunning region of Vanuatu. Plan your trip to Tafea today and immerse yourself in the beauty and wonder of this incredible destination.
