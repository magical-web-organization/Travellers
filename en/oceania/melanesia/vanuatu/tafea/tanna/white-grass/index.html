Introduction to White Grass

White Grass is a small village located in Tanna, in the province of Tafea, Vanuatu. It is situated in the heart of Melanesia, in the South Pacific Ocean. White Grass is famous for its pristine white sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and rich cultural heritage. With its vibrant local culture, stunning natural beauty, and warm hospitality, White Grass is a must-visit destination for travelers looking to experience the best that Vanuatu has to offer.

List of Places to Visit in White Grass

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Yakel Village: Yakel Village is a traditional village located in the heart of White Grass. It is home to the Yakel tribe, one of the oldest tribes in Vanuatu. Here you can witness authentic cultural practices and traditional Kastom dances.
  • Chief Roi Mata’s Domain: Chief Roi Mata’s Domain is a UNESCO World Heritage site located in Tafea Province. It is a complex of three islands connected by a network of paths and bridges. The site is of great historical and cultural importance and is a must-visit destination for history buffs and culture enthusiasts.

Natural Attractions of White Grass: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mt. Yasur: Mt. Yasur is an active volcano located on the island of Tanna. It is one of the most accessible active volcanoes in the world. Visitors can take a guided tour to the summit to witness the spectacular eruptions and lava flows.
  • Blue Cave: Blue Cave is a natural underwater cave located off the coast of Tanna Island. It is a popular destination for swimming, snorkeling, and diving. The cave is home to an abundance of marine life, including colorful fish, turtles, and sharks.

Hiking/Biking Routes of White Grass

  • Louniel Waterfall Trail: This scenic hiking trail leads to the magnificent Louniel Waterfall. It is a moderate hike that passes through lush rainforests and scenic views of the coastal region.
  • Tanna Biking Trail: For those looking for a more adventurous experience, the Tanna Biking Trail is a perfect option. This off-road trail leads through rugged terrain, dense forests, and steep hills.

Best Things to Do in White Grass: Activities and Tours

  • Kastom Tours: Kastom Tours is a popular local tour operator that offers guided tours and cultural experiences. Visitors can learn about traditional customs, beliefs, and practices of the local tribes.
  • Sailing and Cruising: The calm waters around White Grass are perfect for sailing and cruising. Visitors can charter a yacht or catamaran and explore the stunning coastline and nearby islands.

Food and Drink Scene of White Grass

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lap-lap: Lap-lap is a traditional Vanuatu dish made from grated yam, taro or cassava, coconut cream, and other local ingredients. It is usually wrapped in banana leaves and baked in an earth oven.
  • Coconut Crab: Coconut Crab is a local delicacy in Vanuatu. The crab is boiled or steamed and served with coconut cream or as a side dish with other local dishes.

Local Beverages

  • Kava: Kava is a traditional and popular drink in Vanuatu. Made from the root of the kava plant, it has a sedative effect and is often consumed in social settings.
  • Tusker Beer: Tusker Beer is a popular local beer in Vanuatu. It is brewed using local ingredients and has a unique, refreshing taste.

Where to Stay in White Grass: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • White Grass Ocean Resort: White Grass Ocean Resort is a luxurious beachfront resort in Tanna. It offers comfortable accommodation, excellent dining options, and a range of activities and tours.
  • Tanna Lodge: Tanna Lodge is a cozy and charming guesthouse located in the heart of White Grass. It offers affordable accommodation, local cuisine, and a friendly atmosphere.

What to Buy in White Grass: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Carved Wooden Masks: Carved wooden masks are a popular souvenir in Vanuatu. They are hand-carved and painted with traditional patterns and designs.
  • Tapa Cloth: Tapa Cloth is a traditional fabric made from the bark of the Paper Mulberry or Breadfruit tree. It is decorated with traditional patterns and is often used for clothing, wall hangings, and ceremonial purposes.

Local Culture and Customs of White Grass

White Grass is home to several traditional tribes, each with its unique customs and beliefs. The people of White Grass are known for their warm hospitality and welcoming nature. Visitors are advised to respect the local customs and traditions and ask for permission before taking photographs.

Best Time to Visit White Grass

The best time to visit White Grass is during the dry season, which runs from May to October. The weather is pleasant and dry, making it ideal for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of White Grass

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Dress modestly when visiting villages and other cultural sites
  • Ask for permission before taking photographs

Don’t:

  • Disturb the natural environment
  • Engage in disrespectful behavior or actions
  • Leave trash or litter behind

Safety and Health

Safety in White Grass

White Grass is a safe and peaceful destination. Visitors are advised to take usual safety precautions, such as locking valuables in safes and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in White Grass

Visitors are advised to take necessary health precautions before traveling to White Grass. It is recommended to get vaccinated against typhoid, hepatitis A, and polio. Water purification tablets are also recommended as tap water is not safe to drink.

Getting to White Grass: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near White Grass

The closest airport to White Grass is Tanna Airport, a small domestic airport located on the island of Tanna. Flights are available from Port Vila, the capital of Vanuatu.

Bus Stations Near White Grass

There are no bus stations in White Grass.

Other Options

Taxis and rental cars are available for transportation in and around White Grass.

How to Get Around in White Grass

The best way to get around White Grass is by hiring a car or hiring a tour operator.

Where to Go Next from White Grass: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Port Vila: Port Vila is the capital of Vanuatu and is located on the island of Efate. It is a bustling city with a range of dining and entertainment options. Visitors can also explore its many cultural and historical sites.
  • Mystery Island: Mystery Island is a small uninhabited island located off the coast of Tanna. It is known for its beautiful beach and crystal-clear waters, making it a popular destination for swimming and snorkeling.
