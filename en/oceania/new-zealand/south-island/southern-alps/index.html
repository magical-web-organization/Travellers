Introduction to Southern Alps

Southern Alps is a mountain range located in the South Island of New Zealand. Spanning over 500 km, this breathtakingly beautiful mountain range boasts snow-capped peaks, glaciers, crystal-clear lakes, meandering rivers, and verdant forests. Southern Alps is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, adventure seekers, nature lovers, and photographers.

Local Culture and Customs of Southern Alps

The Maori culture is an integral part of New Zealand’s identity and is prevalent in Southern Alps as well. Visitors are encouraged to respect the local customs and traditions, such as removing shoes before entering a marae (Maori meeting house) and treating the land with respect.

Best Time to Visit Southern Alps

The best time to visit Southern Alps is during the summer months (December to February) when the weather is warm and sunny. However, the winter months (June to August) offer an opportunity to experience skiing and snowboarding in some of the best ski resorts in the world.

Do’s and Don’ts of Southern Alps

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and traditions.
  • Stick to designated walking tracks and biking paths.
  • Dress appropriately for the weather and outdoor activities.
  • Take out travel insurance to cover any emergencies.

Don’t:

  • Leave any garbage or litter in the natural areas.
  • Enter any private property without permission.
  • Harass or disturb any wildlife.
  • Drink and drive.

Safety and Health

Safety in Southern Alps

Southern Alps is a safe destination, but visitors should take necessary precautions, such as:

  • Checking weather and trail conditions before heading out on a hike or bike ride.
  • Carrying a first aid kit.
  • Taking proper safety measures while participating in adventure activities.

Health in Southern Alps

Visitors to Southern Alps should be aware of the following health concerns:

  • Ultraviolet radiation is high in New Zealand, even on cloudy days. Using sunblock and wearing protective clothing is recommended.
  • Be prepared for changes in weather and temperature.
