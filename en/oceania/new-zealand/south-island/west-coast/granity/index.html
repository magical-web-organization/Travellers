Introduction to Granity

Granity is a charmingly remote town located in New Zealand’s West Coast region of the South Island. It’s a small but lively town with a closely knit community that welcomes visitors with open arms.

Granity is known for its breathtaking natural beauty, pristine beaches, rich history, and fascinating culture. The town is surrounded by mountains and dense forests, providing ample opportunities for outdoor adventurers. Whether you’re a nature lover, history buff, or simply looking for a relaxing getaway, Granity is the perfect destination.

List of Places to Visit in Granity

Here are some of the must-visit places in Granity that you won’t want to miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

1. Granity Museum

Located in the heart of the town, the Granity Museum is a small but fascinating museum that showcases the rich history and culture of Granity. The museum displays a range of artifacts and exhibits that highlight the town’s mining heritage, early settlers, and its Maori roots.

2. Pithead Baths

The Pithead Baths are a unique and fascinating piece of history that’s definitely worth a visit. The baths were built in the 1920s and were used to clean the miners after a long day’s work in the mines. The building has been lovingly restored and now houses a small museum that tells the story of the baths and the local mining industry.

Natural Attractions of Granity: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

1. Tauranga Bay

Tauranga Bay is a stunning beach that’s located just a short drive from Granity. The beach is known for its crystal-clear waters, golden sands, and stunning scenery. It’s the perfect place to soak up some sun, go for a swim, or take a relaxing stroll along the shore.

2. Kawatiri Coastal Trail

The Kawatiri Coastal Trail is a must-do for any nature lover. This stunning trail stretches for over 70 kilometers along the coast and offers breathtaking views of the ocean and the surrounding landscape. It’s a relatively easy trail that’s suitable for hikers of all skill levels.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Granity

If you’re an outdoor enthusiast, you’ll be glad to know that Granity has some of the best hiking and biking routes in New Zealand. Here are some trails you won’t want to miss:

1. Old Ghost Road

The Old Ghost Road is a world-renowned hiking and mountain biking trail that stretches for 85 kilometers through some of New Zealand’s most stunning landscapes. The trail is challenging but incredibly rewarding, with stunning views, diverse wildlife, and challenging terrain.

2. Charming Creek Walkway

The Charming Creek Walkway is a gentle and scenic walking trail that runs along the Charming Creek. The trail passes through lush forests, past scenic waterfalls, and over some stunning bridges.

Best Things to Do in Granity: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re looking to explore the town’s rich history or get your adrenaline pumping, Granity has something for everyone. Here are some of the best things to do in Granity:

1. Westport Walks

Westport Walks offer guided walking tours around the town that brings the history and culture of the area to life. The tours are led by knowledgeable and friendly guides who provide fascinating insights into the town’s rich history and culture.

2. Surfing

If you’re looking for an adrenaline rush, you’ll want to try your hand at surfing. The town’s beaches offer some of the best waves in New Zealand, and there are plenty of surf schools and rental stores in town to help you get started.

Food and Drink Scene of Granity

Granity might be a small town, but it has a thriving food and drink scene that’s sure to delight your taste buds. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

1. Seafood Platter

Granity is known for its fresh seafood, and you won’t want to miss the seafood platter at the Tauranga Bay Tavern. The platter features a selection of fresh fish, prawns, and scallops, along with a selection of sides.

2. Venison Burger

The venison burger at the Granity Tavern is a local favorite. The burger features a juicy and flavorful venison patty, along with a range of delicious toppings.

Local Beverages

1. Monteith’s Beer

Monteith’s Brewery is a local brewery that produces some of New Zealand’s finest beer. You won’t want to miss a visit to the brewery or trying their delicious beers.

2. Hawkes Bay Wines

New Zealand is known for its exceptional wines, and Hawkes Bay is one of the finest wine regions in the country. You’ll find a great selection of Hawkes Bay wines at local restaurants and bars in Granity.

Where to Stay in Granity: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Granity has a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best places to stay in Granity:

1. Tauranga Bay Holiday Park

The Tauranga Bay Holiday Park offers a range of accommodation options, including cottages, cabins, and campsites. The park is located just a short walk from the beach and is the perfect spot to relax and unwind.

2. Granity Motels

The Granity Motels offer comfortable and affordable accommodation that’s perfect for families and couples. The motel is located in the heart of the town and is within walking distance of all the major attractions.

What to Buy in Granity: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs and local products to bring home, here are some of the best places to shop in Granity:

1. Granity Gift Shop

The Granity Gift Shop offers a range of unique and locally made gifts, including jewelry, pottery, and paintings.

2. Tauranga Bay Trading Post

The Tauranga Bay Trading Post offers a range of souvenirs and mementos, including postcards, t-shirts, and local honey.

Local Culture and Customs of Granity

Granity has a rich and fascinating culture that’s influenced by its mining heritage and Maori roots. The town has a strong sense of community, and visitors are always welcomed with open arms.

Best Time to Visit Granity

The best time to visit Granity is during the summer months between December and February when the weather is warm and sunny. However, the town can be enjoyed all year round as each season has its own charm and attractions.

Safety and Health

Your safety and health are paramount during your visit to Granity. Here’s what you need to know:

Getting to Granity: Transportation Options and Logistics

Granity is located in the West Coast region of the South Island of New Zealand. Here are your transportation options for getting there:

Airports Near Granity

The closest airport to Granity is Westport Airport, located approximately 16 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Granity

The West Coast Shuttle offers daily bus services between Westport and Granity.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Granity using the well-maintained highways and roads that run through the region.

How to Get Around in Granity

Granity is a small town that can easily be explored on foot or on a bike. Visitors can also rent a car or a bike to explore further afield, or take guided tours to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Granity: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions you might want to consider after your visit to Granity:

The Nelson Lakes National Park is a stunning natural wonderland that’s perfect for outdoor adventurers. The park is located approximately a three-hour drive from Granity.
