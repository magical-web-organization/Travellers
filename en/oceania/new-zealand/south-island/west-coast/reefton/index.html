Introduction to Reefton

Located in the heart of the West Coast region of South Island, New Zealand, Reefton is a historic town surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty. Known for its gold mining past and stunning forests, Reefton is the ultimate destination for adventurers, history buffs, and nature enthusiasts.

List of Places to Visit in Reefton

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Reefton i-Site Visitor Centre: The perfect place to start your journey, the i-Site offers information on local attractions and history. You can also take a tour of the historic township with a knowledgeable guide.
  • Reefton Distilling Co.: Explore the history of whiskey-making in Reefton and sample some of the finest spirits in the region.
  • Goldsborough: An abandoned gold mining town, Goldsborough offers an eerie glimpse into the past.

Natural Attractions of Reefton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Victoria Forest Park: Spanning 206,000 hectares, Victoria Forest Park is a wilderness playground filled with hiking, biking, and four-wheel drive trails.
  • Inangahua River: Known for its stunning scenery and excellent fishing, the Inangahua River is a must-visit for outdoor enthusiasts.
  • Murray Creek: Follow this stunning trail through lush forests and glistening creeks for a close encounter with nature.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Reefton

  • The Paparoa Track: A challenging hiking trail spanning 55 km, with rugged terrain and breathtaking views of the coast.
  • The Murray Creek Walk: An easy 2-hour walk through the forest, with stunning views of cascading waterfalls.
  • The Kuramea-Totara Walk: A 4-hour hike through the forest, with spectacular views of the surrounding mountains.

Best Things to Do in Reefton: Activities and Tours

  • Horse Trekking: Explore the wilderness on horseback with a tour provider.
  • Gold Panning: Experience gold mining firsthand and try your hand at panning for gold.
  • Helicopter Scenic Tour: Get a bird’s eye view of the stunning natural beauty surrounding Reefton.

Week-Long Itinerary for Reefton

Food and Drink Scene of Reefton

Must-Try Dishes

  • Whitebait Fritters: A West Coast delicacy made with local whitebait.
  • West Coast Whitebait: Considered the world’s best whitebait, this dish is served with a sprinkle of lemon and a sprinkle of salt.
  • West Coast Scallops: Freshly caught and cooked to perfection.

Local Beverages

  • Reefton Distilling Co. Whiskey: Made with pure water from the surrounding mountains and locally sourced ingredients.
  • Monteith’s Handcrafted Beer: A popular beer brand made on the West Coast of New Zealand.

Where to Stay in Reefton: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Alfresco Historic Villas: A luxurious option for travelers who want to be immersed in Reefton’s rich history.
  • Reefton Auto Lodge: A budget-friendly option with clean and comfortable rooms.
  • Conservatory Accommodation: A charming guesthouse with beautiful gardens and warm hospitality.

What to Buy in Reefton: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Gold nuggets: A unique and valuable souvenir from Reefton’s gold mining past.
  • Reefton Distilling Co. Whiskey: A bottle or two of this delicious whiskey makes for a perfect souvenir or gift.
  • Local honey: Made from the abundant wildflowers surrounding Reefton.

Local Culture and Customs of Reefton

The West Coast region has a unique culture and customs that are deeply rooted in its gold mining past. Visitors to Reefton should always respect the region’s history and natural beauty.

Best Time to Visit Reefton

Summer (December-February) is the best time to visit Reefton, with warm and pleasant weather perfect for outdoor activities. However, Reefton is beautiful all year round, and each season has its unique charm.

Do’s and Don’ts of Reefton

Do:

  • Respect the local wildlife and natural environment.
  • Learn about Reefton’s gold mining history and culture.
  • Try the local food and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Damage or remove any natural resources.
  • Disrupt local wildlife or their habitats.
  • Litter or leave any garbage behind.

Safety and Health

Safety in Reefton

Reefton is a safe destination for travelers, but it’s always a good idea to take necessary precautions. Be mindful of weather conditions and always follow safety guidelines for outdoor activities.

Health in Reefton

Reefton has a small medical center that can cater to basic health needs, but serious medical issues require transport to larger centers in nearby towns or cities.

Getting to Reefton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Reefton

The closest airport to Reefton is Hokitika Airport, located approximately 90km away.

Bus Stations Near Reefton

Intercity and other regional bus services stop in Reefton. The town also has a local bus service.

Other Options

Car rental services are also available in Reefton, offering more independence for travelers who want to explore the surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Reefton

Reefton is a small town, and most attractions are within walking or biking distance. Taxis and rental cars are also available for travelers who prefer to explore at their convenience.

Where to Go Next from Reefton: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Punakaiki Pancake Rocks: A natural wonder with stunning rock formations and a blowhole.
  • Hokitika: A seaside town that is home to artisans and crafters, famous for its Jade and greenstone carving shops.
  • Nelson Lakes National Park: An area of immense natural beauty known for its high peaks and turquoise lakes.

Conclusion

With its stunning scenery, rich history, and endless outdoor activities, Reefton is a hidden gem in New Zealand’s South Island. Follow our comprehensive travel guide to make the most of your trip to this beautiful and unique destination.
