Introduction to Paekākāriki

Paekākāriki is a small coastal town located in Kapiti Coast, Greater Wellington, North Island, New Zealand. The town is popularly known for its scenic beauty and cultural significance. In this travel guide, we will take you on a tour of the best attractions, food scene, lodging options, and transportation options in Paekākāriki.

List of Places to Visit in Paekākāriki

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Paekākāriki has a rich history and numerous museums that showcase the town’s cultural heritage. Some of the must-visit cultural attractions in Paekākāriki are:

  1. Paekākāriki Station Museum - A museum that displays the rich history of Paekākāriki’s railway heritage.
  2. Beachcomber Museum - A museum that showcases the rich history of Paraparaumu beach and the Kapiti region.
  3. Ngā Manu Nature Reserve - A wildlife sanctuary and interactive educational facility, where visitors can interact with birds and learn more about the region’s wildlife.

Natural Attractions of Paekākāriki: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Paekākāriki’s natural beauty is breathtaking, with scenic views complemented by outdoor activities. Here are some of the must-visit natural attractions in Paekākāriki:

  1. Queen Elizabeth Park - A picturesque park that offers spectacular views of the Kapiti Island.
  2. Paekākāriki Escarpment Track - A trail that offers panoramic views of the region, and a unique experience of the Kapiti Coast.
  3. Whareroa Farm Track - A beautiful scenic reserve that features lush bush walks, where visitors can enjoy the clean fresh air.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Paekākāriki

Paekākāriki has a range of hiking and biking trails that offer beautiful views and a unique experience of the Kapiti Coast. Some of the must-visit hiking and biking routes are:

  1. Paekākāriki Escarpment Track - A 10.5 km track that offers panoramic views of the region.
  2. Whareroa Farm Track - A 5 km loop that features lush bush walks and stunning scenery.
  3. Queen Elizabeth Park - A beautiful park located on the western tip of the Kapiti Coast, which offers stunning views of the Kapiti Island.

Best Things to Do in Paekākāriki: Activities and Tours

Paekākāriki offers a range of activities and tours that cater to different interests. Here are some of the best things to do in Paekākāriki:

  1. Experience wildlife up close at Ngā Manu Nature Reserve.
  2. Explore the rich history of Paekākāriki’s railway heritage at Paekākāriki Station Museum.
  3. Take a hiking tour of the Paekākāriki Escarpment Track.

Week-Long Itinerary for Paekākāriki

If you have a week to explore Paekākāriki, the following itinerary will help you maximize your visit:

Day 1: Visit Ngā Manu Nature Reserve. Day 2: Explore the Paekākāriki Station Museum. Day 3: Take the Paekākāriki Escarpment Track. Day 4: Visit Queen Elizabeth Park for bird watching and enjoy a picnic. Day 5: Take a biking tour of Whareroa Farm Track. Day 6: Enjoy the beautiful sunset at Paekākāriki Beach. Day 7: Explore Paraparaumu Beach and the Beachcomber Museum.

Food and Drink Scene of Paekākāriki

Paekākāriki has a lively food and beverage scene, where visitors can try a variety of local dishes and beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Whitebait fritters - A popular dish made of whitebait, eggs, and flour, which is a specialty of the region.
  2. Manuka honey glazed lamb chops - A dish that features lamb chops glazed with Manuka honey, a local specialty.
  3. Seafood chowder - A delicious soup made of seafood, cream, and vegetables, perfect for cold winter days.

Local Beverages

  1. Kapiti craft beer - The region’s craft breweries offer unique beer flavors that are a must-try.
  2. Kapiti coffee - The region is renowned for its coffee, featuring a unique blend of robusta and arabica beans from local plantations.
  3. Kapiti Coast wine - The region’s coastal climate is ideal for wine production, resulting in unique and delicious wine flavors.

Where to Stay in Paekākāriki: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Paekākāriki offers various accommodation options that cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best lodging options in Paekākāriki:

  1. Kapiti Gateway Motel - A comfortable motel located near the beach, offering excellent amenities and service.
  2. Paekākāriki Beachfront Motel - Another excellent motel located near the beach, featuring sea-facing rooms and great service.
  3. Paekākāriki Holiday Park - A budget-friendly option that offers campsite and cabin lodging options.
  4. Mayuka Lodge - A cozy and stylish guesthouse that offers a personalized experience and beautiful sea views.

What to Buy in Paekākāriki: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Paekākāriki offers a range of local products and souvenirs that are perfect for taking back home as a memento of your trip to Kapiti Coast. Here are some must-buy souvenirs and local products:

  1. Kapiti Coast Honey - This honey is harvested from the Manuka trees that grow around the Kapiti Coast, and is renowned for its unique flavor and health benefits.
  2. Jewelry - Local artisans offer a range of jewelry pieces that are perfect for unique gifts.
  3. Organic preserves - The region’s fertile soil and abundant farms produce organic preserves, jams, and spreads that are perfect for taking back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Paekākāriki

The region’s culture and customs of the Maori people are an essential part of Paekākāriki’s social fabric. Visitors are encouraged to respect the customs and observe the local traditions.

Best Time to Visit Paekākāriki

The best time to visit Paekākāriki is from early November to late April when the weather is warm and suitable for outdoor activities. The peak season is from December to February when the town is bustling with tourists.

Safety and Health

Safety in Paekākāriki

Paekākāriki is a safe town, with low crime rates. However, visitors are always encouraged to stay vigilant and take necessary precautions.

Health in Paekākāriki

Paekākāriki has excellent healthcare facilities, including medical clinics and hospitals. Visitors are urged to have travel insurance and take necessary medical precautions before visiting the region.

Getting to Paekākāriki: Transportation Options and Logistics

Paekākāriki is easily accessible by various modes of transportation.

Airports Near Paekākāriki

  1. Wellington International Airport - 50 km away from Paekākāriki.
  2. Kapiti Coast Airport - 6 km away from Paekākāriki.

Bus Stations Near Paekākāriki

  1. Paekākāriki Train Station - Located in the center of town.
  2. Paraparaumu Bus Station - 8 km away from Paekākāriki.

Other Options

  1. Car rental - Visitors can rent cars from car rental companies.
  2. Taxi - Visitors can hire taxis to get around the town.

How to Get Around in Paekākāriki

Paekākāriki is a small town, and most of the attractions are located within walking distance of the town center. Visitors can also use bikes or hire taxis to get around the town.

Where to Go Next from Paekākāriki: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Paekākāriki is an excellent starting point for exploring the Kapiti Coast, with many exciting destinations and excursions nearby:

  1. Kapiti Island Nature Reserve - An island sanctuary located off the coast of Kapiti Coast, known for its wildlife and bird watching.
  2. Otaki Beach - A beautiful beach located 30 km north of Paekākāriki, known for its stunning scenery and friendly locals.
  3. Wellington City - The bustling capital of New Zealand, located 50 km south of Paekākāriki, known for its vibrant culture and attractions.
