Introduction to Paraparaumu

Paraparaumu is a coastal town located in Kapiti Coast, in Greater Wellington, in North Island, in New Zealand. It is famous for its stunning beaches, natural beauty, and outdoor activities. The town has a rich history and culture, which is evident in its well-preserved museums, galleries, and historical landmarks. Paraparaumu is an ideal destination for those who love nature, adventure, and culture.

List of Places to Visit in Paraparaumu

Here are some of the must-visit places in Paraparaumu:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Kapiti Coast Museum: Located in the center of Paraparaumu, Kapiti Coast Museum is a must-visit place for history lovers. The museum showcases the rich history and culture of the Kapiti Coast region through its impressive collections of artifacts, photographs, documents, and artwork.

  2. Southward Car Museum: Southward Car Museum is a paradise for automobile enthusiasts. The museum houses a vast collection of classic and vintage cars, motorcycles, and other vehicles. The museum also has a great collection of antique furniture, automobilia, and memorabilia.

  3. Paekakariki Station Museum: Paekakariki Station Museum is a restored railway station that provides a glimpse into the town’s railway history. The museum has an impressive collection of railway artifacts, including locomotives, carriages, and tools.

Natural Attractions of Paraparaumu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Kapiti Island: Kapiti Island is a protected wildlife reserve located off the coast of Paraparaumu. The island is home to a diverse range of native species, including rare birds, reptiles, and insects. Visitors can enjoy guided tours, hiking, and birdwatching on the island.

  2. Paraparaumu Beach: Paraparaumu Beach is a stunning sandy beach that stretches for miles. The beach is ideal for swimming, surfing, and sunbathing. Visitors can enjoy a range of beach activities, including picnicking, fishing, and beachcombing.

  3. Queen Elizabeth Park: Queen Elizabeth Park is a vast park located in Paraparaumu. The park has a range of recreational facilities, including picnic areas, playgrounds, and walking trails. Visitors can enjoy a range of outdoor activities, including cycling, hiking, and birdwatching.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Paraparaumu

  1. Te Araroa Trail: Te Araroa Trail is one of the longest hiking trails in New Zealand. The trail stretches for 3,000 kilometers, from Cape Reinga in North Island to Bluff in South Island. The route passes through Paraparaumu, providing hikers with stunning views of the town’s natural beauty.

  2. Waikanae River Trail: Waikanae River Trail is a 5-kilometer walking and cycling trail that follows the Waikanae River. The trail passes through lush native bush, open farmland, and beautiful streams and waterfalls.

Best Things to Do in Paraparaumu: Activities and Tours

  1. Kapiti Coast Wine Tour: Kapiti Coast Wine Tour is perfect for wine lovers. The tour takes visitors through some of the region’s best wineries, where they can taste a range of delicious wines and learn about the winemaking process.

  2. Kyudo Experience: Kyudo is a traditional Japanese archery form that focuses on meditation, relaxation, and harmony. The Kyudo Experience offers visitors the opportunity to learn this ancient art form and practice it in a beautiful natural setting.

Food and Drink Scene of Paraparaumu

Paraparaumu has a vibrant food and drink scene.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Fish and chips: Paraparaumu is known for its delicious fish and chips, made with fresh seafood from the Kapiti Coast.

  2. Venison pie: Venison is a popular meat in New Zealand, and the venison pie is a must-try dish for meat lovers.

Local Beverages

  1. Craft beer: Paraparaumu has a range of local craft breweries that offer a variety of delicious beers.

  2. Kapiti Coast wines: The Kapiti Coast region is known for its award-winning wines, including Sauvignon Blanc and Pinot Noir.

Where to Stay in Paraparaumu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Paraparaumu has a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses.

  1. Kapiti Gateway Motel: Kapiti Gateway Motel is a great option for those looking for affordable accommodation with all the modern amenities.

  2. Greenmantle Estate Lodge: Greenmantle Estate Lodge is a luxurious accommodation option located in a beautiful natural setting.

What to Buy in Paraparaumu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Kapiti Coast wines: Kapiti Coast wines make for a great souvenir or gift for wine lovers.

  2. Hand-crafted jewelry: Paraparaumu has a range of local jewelry stores that offer unique and beautiful pieces.

Local Culture and Customs of Paraparaumu

The Maori culture has a significant influence on the Kapiti Coast region and can be seen in its art, language, and traditions. Visitors should respect the local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Paraparaumu

The best time to visit Paraparaumu is during the summer months, from December to February, when the weather is warm and sunny. The town is busy during this time, so visitors should book their accommodation and tours in advance.
