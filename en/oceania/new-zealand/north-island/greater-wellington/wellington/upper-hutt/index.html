Upper Hutt is a charming city in Greater Wellington, located in the North Island of New Zealand. Known for its stunning natural beauty, outdoor adventures, fascinating culture, and warm hospitality, this city is a popular destination for tourists. Whether you’re looking to relax amidst beautiful surroundings or are an adventure seeker, Upper Hutt has something for everyone. In this travel guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit, activities to do, where to stay, and more.

Introduction to Upper Hutt

Located just half an hour from Wellington, Upper Hutt is a gateway to the Wairarapa region and is home to over 40,000 residents. Surrounded by mountains, rivers, and forests, Upper Hutt offers breathtaking landscapes, a rich cultural heritage, and a relaxed lifestyle. The city is known for its friendly locals, picturesque parks, and stunning outdoor spaces.

List of Places to Visit in Upper Hutt

Upper Hutt boasts a range of culturally important attractions that should be at the top of your list. Here are a few places you should visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Expressions Whirinaki Arts & Entertainment Centre: This arts and entertainment venue in Upper Hutt showcases exhibitions and performances of local and international artists.

  • Percy Scenic Reserve: This stunning nature reserve in Upper Hutt is home to over 300 hectares of native bush, wildlife, hiking, and mountain biking trails.

  • Upper Hutt Cosmopolitan Club: This social club is a great place to enjoy good food, drinks, and entertainment.

Natural Attractions of Upper Hutt: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Upper Hutt is famous for its natural beauty and offers some stunning locations for enjoying the great outdoors. Here are a few attractions that you shouldn’t miss:

  • Rimutaka Forest Park: Located in the eastern part of Upper Hutt, this park offers hiking and biking trails for all skill levels.

  • Maidstone Park: This park in Upper Hutt has a playground, sports fields, and a mini steam train that runs on the weekends.

  • Te Marua Golf Club: This golf club in Upper Hutt is an excellent place to take in scenic views while playing a round of golf.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Upper Hutt

Upper Hutt is a great destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. With miles of trails catering to all levels of experience, you’re sure to find something that suits your needs. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Upper Hutt:

  • Rimutaka Rail Trail: This trail in Upper Hutt is a popular destination for hikers and bikers, offering spectacular views of the mountains and bushland.

  • Akatarawa Forest: This forest in Upper Hutt has several hiking and biking trails, perfect for outdoor enthusiasts.

  • Harbour View: This track in Upper Hutt provides scenic views of the city and its nearby harbour.

Best Things to Do in Upper Hutt: Activities and Tours

Upper Hutt is a fantastic destination for adventure lovers. Here are some of the best activities and tours to try in Upper Hutt:

  • River Kayaking: Experience the beauty of the Hutt River while kayaking. You can also book a guided tour for an immersive experience.

  • Horseback Riding: Explore the stunning hills and challenging terrains of Upper Hutt on horseback. You can book a guided tour to experience the best of Upper Hutt’s natural beauty.

  • Quad Biking: Quad biking in Upper Hutt gives you an adrenaline-charged adventure. You can opt for guided tours or hire a quad bike and explore Upper Hutt’s vast outdoor spaces on your own.

Food and Drink Scene of Upper Hutt

Upper Hutt’s food and drink scene is not to be missed. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fish and Chips: Enjoy fresh fish and chips at one of Upper Hutt’s seaside restaurants.

  • Cheese and Wine: Enjoy delicious locally produced cheese and excellent wine in Upper Hutt.

  • Burgers: Upper Hutt is home to some great burger places, perfect for a quick lunch or dinner.

Local Beverages

What to Buy in Upper Hutt: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Local Culture and Customs of Upper Hutt

Upper Hutt is a multicultural city with a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. Locals are friendly, hospitable, and take pride in their city’s history and traditions. Visitors are advised to respect local customs and traditions when traveling to Upper Hutt.

Best Time to Visit Upper Hutt

The best time to visit Upper Hutt is from December to February, during the summer season. The weather is sunny and warm, making it the perfect time to enjoy outdoor adventures and scenic views.

  • Take care of the environment and natural surroundings.
  • Try the local food and drink.

Safety and Health

Safety in Upper Hutt

Upper Hutt is a relatively safe destination for travelers. Visitors are advised to exercise caution and take safety precautions, especially when traveling alone at night. Keep your valuables safe and secure.

Health in Upper Hutt

Upper Hutt has a good healthcare system and is home to several well-equipped hospitals and clinics. Visitors are advised to carry necessary medications and to also buy medical insurance before traveling to Upper Hutt.

Getting to Upper Hutt: Transportation Options and Logistics

Traveling to Upper Hutt is easy and convenient. Here are the transportation options and logistics to keep in mind:

Airports Near Upper Hutt

The closest airport to Upper Hutt is Wellington International Airport, which is located just 30 minutes from the city center.

Bus Stations Near Upper Hutt

Upper Hutt has several bus stations, including the Upper Hutt Bus Station and the Silverstream Bus Station, providing connections to other parts of the city.

Other Options

There are several other transportation options available, including taxis, car rentals, and Uber.

How to Get Around in Upper Hutt

Getting around Upper Hutt is easy and convenient. The city offers excellent public transportation, including buses and trains. Taxis and ride-sharing services like Uber are also readily available.

Where to Go Next from Upper Hutt: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Upper Hutt is surrounded by several other destinations and excursions that are worth visiting. Here are some of the best options to consider:

  • Wellington: Just 30 minutes from Upper Hutt is the vibrant city of Wellington, home to numerous cultural attractions, dining options, and shopping outlets.

  • Wairarapa: This stunning region is just an hour’s drive from Upper Hutt and offers beautiful scenery, vineyards, and cultural attractions.

Conclusion

Upper Hutt is an excellent destination for travelers looking to experience New Zealand’s natural beauty, culture, and adventure. From scenic hikes to outdoor activities and cultural attractions, Upper Hutt has something for everyone. Use this comprehensive travel guide to plan your perfect Upper Hutt getaway.
