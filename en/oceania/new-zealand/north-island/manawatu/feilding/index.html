Introduction to Feilding

Located in the heart of Manawatu, North Island of New Zealand, Feilding is a vibrant rural town that has something to offer every kind of traveler. The town is an agriculture hub and known for its annual Farmer’s Market, and has an abundance of natural and cultural attractions to explore. Here’s a comprehensive guide to help you plan your next trip to Feilding.

List of Places to Visit in Feilding

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Coach House Museum: An award-winning museum that showcases the history of transport in New Zealand, housing a collection of over 40 horse-drawn vehicles and carriages.
  • Feilding & District Art Society Gallery: An art gallery located in the heart of Feilding, showcasing local artists’ work in a range of mediums.
  • St. Johns Anglican Church: A 19th-century church in the center of the town that houses the historical heritage of the early settlers in the area.

Natural Attractions of Feilding: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Feilding Farmers’ Markets: A weekly market that takes place every Friday by the clock tower and sells fresh produce and artisan products.
  • Manawatu River Pathway: A scenic walking and biking track that extends over 10 kilometers, offering stunning views of the river, wetlands, and native flora and fauna.
  • Feilding Arboretum: A vibrant garden with a wide variety of trees, shrubs, and plants from all over the world, perfect for a leisurely afternoon walk.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Feilding

  • The Manawatu Gorge Track: A challenging track through the Manawatu Gorge, offering stunning views of the river valley and surrounding bush.
  • River Pathway: A 10-kilometer walking and biking track that follows the Manawatu River, passing wetlands and native flora and fauna.

Best Things to Do in Feilding: Activities and Tours

  • Feilding Farmers Market: A weekly market held in the town center, where you can buy fresh produce and artisan products.
  • Manawatu Gorge Track: A challenging hike that will take you through the Manawatu Gorge.
  • Coach House Museum: A museum that showcases the history of transport in New Zealand, with over 40 horse-drawn vehicles and carriages.

Food and Drink Scene of Feilding

Must-Try Dishes

  • Mince and Cheese Pie: A New Zealand classic, made with minced beef and cheddar cheese.
  • Pavlova: A sweet dessert made with a meringue crust and topped with whipped cream and fresh fruit.

Local Beverages

  • Beer: Feilding has a range of local breweries that produce a variety of beers, including Hop Federation Brewery and Rekorderlig Cider Brewery.
  • Wine: Manawatu produces a range of wines, including sauvignon blanc, chardonnay, and pinot noir.

Where to Stay in Feilding: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Kingsgate Hotel: A modern hotel located in the town center, offering comfortable rooms and excellent service.
  • Feilding Lodge: A bed and breakfast located in a historic building with beautiful period features and stunning gardens.
  • Hillcrest Bed & Breakfast: A charming and cozy guesthouse located in a peaceful location overlooking the town.

What to Buy in Feilding: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Artisan products from the Feilding Farmers Market. A range of fresh produce and handmade products, including jams, honey, cheese, and baked goods.
  • Manawatu Wine and Beer. A range of wines and beers produced by local wineries and breweries.

Local Culture and Customs of Feilding

  • Agriculture is the main industry. Feilding is known as the “rural heartland of New Zealand,” with a strong focus on agriculture.
  • Feilding Farmers’ Market is a must-visit. This weekly market takes place on Fridays and showcases local produce and artisan products from the region.

Best Time to Visit Feilding

The best time to visit Feilding is during the summer months (December-February), when the weather is warm and dry, and the landscape is in full bloom.

Do’s and Don’ts of Feilding

Do:

  • Make time for the Feilding Farmers Market. The weekly market is a must-visit for fresh produce and artisan products from the region.
  • Take a hike through the Manawatu Gorge. This challenging track offers stunning views of the river valley and surrounding bush.

Don’t:

  • Underestimate the sun. New Zealand has a harsh UV rating, so make sure you wear sunscreen and protective clothing.
  • Leave personal items in your car. Petty theft is a problem in New Zealand, so make sure you keep your valuables secured at all times.

Safety and Health

Safety in Feilding

Feilding is a relatively safe town, but it’s always best to take precautions. Lock your car and keep valuables secure, especially when parked in busy areas.

Health in Feilding

There are medical services available in Feilding, including a hospital and medical center. In case of a medical emergency, call 111 for assistance.

Getting to Feilding: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Feilding

The nearest domestic airport is Palmerston North Airport, located 17 kilometers from Feilding.

Bus Stations Near Feilding

Intercity and local bus services connect Feilding with nearby towns and cities, including Palmerston North, Wellington, and Auckland.

Other Options

Feilding is easily accessible by car, with State Highway 54 running through the town.

How to Get Around in Feilding

Feilding is a small town, and most of the attractions are within walking distance of each other. To get around town, you can hire a bike or a car.

Where to Go Next from Feilding: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Palmerston North: A vibrant city with a range of cultural and outdoor activities to explore, including the Victoria Esplanade gardens and the Manawatu River Pathway.
  • Foxton: A small town with a rich Maori history, located on the west coast of the North Island, offering scenic views of the river and the nearby Tararua ranges.

Conclusion

Feilding is a hidden gem in the rural heart of New Zealand. From its outdoor adventures to cultural highlights, this town has something to offer everyone. Plan your trip today and discover the beauty of Feilding.
