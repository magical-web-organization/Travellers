Introduction to Shebenik-Jabllanice National Park

Shebenik-Jabllanice National Park is located in Southeastern Albania, in the Balkans region of Europe. The park is named after its two highest peaks, Shebenik (2,254 m) and Jabllanice (1,831 m), and covers an area of 33,927 hectares. This pristine national park is known for its biodiversity, stunning landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. It is an ideal destination for nature enthusiasts, adventure seekers, and culture lovers alike, providing a unique travel experience in Albania.

Food and Drink Scene of Shebenik-Jabllanice National Park

Local Beverages

Safety and Health

Safety in Shebenik-Jabllanice National Park

The park is safe for visitors, but it’s always recommended to take necessary safety measures while participating in outdoor activities. Carry a map, water, and first-aid kit while hiking, and don’t go off-trail. Also, be aware of wildlife and their habitats.

Health in Shebenik-Jabllanice National Park

The park is mostly safe from any health risks, but it’s advisable to carry mosquito repellent and sunscreen, especially during summer.
