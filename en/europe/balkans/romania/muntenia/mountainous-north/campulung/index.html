Introduction to Câmpulung

Located in the Mountainous North region of Muntenia, Câmpulung is a picturesque town in Romania that can satisfy any traveler’s desire for adventure, history, and natural beauty. The town is situated in the heart of the Carpathian Mountains and surrounded by breathtaking peaks, dense forests, and crystal-clear streams. Câmpulung is also known for its rich cultural heritage and historical landmarks, dating back to the medieval times, which add a unique charm to this beautiful destination.

List of Places to Visit in Câmpulung

Here are the top places to visit in Câmpulung:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • (1) The Museum of Câmpulung - Explore the rich culture and history of the town through exhibits, historical artifacts, and art collections.
  • (2) The Episcopal Palace - A magnificent building built in the Baroque style, which offers a glimpse into the town’s religious history.
  • (3) The Church of St. John the Baptist - A beautiful Orthodox church dating back to the 15th century.

Natural Attractions of Câmpulung: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • (1) Zăganul Waterfall - A 30m high waterfall located near the town’s center, which can be accessed by hiking along a beautiful trail.
  • (2) The Cozia National Park - A natural reserve with a diverse flora and fauna, as well as numerous hiking trails, picnic spots, and camping areas.
  • (3) Vâlcea Gorges - A spectacular natural attraction with vertical cliffs, waterfalls, and caves, which can be explored by hiking or rock climbing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Câmpulung

Câmpulung is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, offering numerous hiking and biking routes that are suitable for all levels. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • (1) The Cozia Monastery trail - A 7km route that leads to the famous Cozia Monastery, offering breathtaking views of the Carpathian Mountains and the Olt River Valley.
  • (2) The Vâlcea Gorges circuit - A 12km circular route that takes you through the stunning Vâlcea Gorges, offering a unique opportunity to explore the region’s natural beauty.
  • (3) The Transfăgărășan Highway - A famous route that crosses the Carpathian Mountains, offering stunning views and challenging terrain for cycling enthusiasts.

Best Things to Do in Câmpulung: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of exciting things to do in Câmpulung, whether you want to explore the town’s culture and history or enjoy outdoor activities. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • (1) Join a guided tour of the town’s main attractions, led by knowledgeable guides who can provide a deeper insight into the region’s history and culture.
  • (2) Try Zip-lining or Tree Top Climbing at the Adventure Park Câmpulung, offering adrenaline pumping activities within a beautiful natural setting.
  • (3) Visit a local farm to learn about traditional Romanian agriculture and sample delicious local foods, such as cheese and honey.

Food and Drink Scene of Câmpulung

Câmpulung has a rich culinary heritage, known for its traditional dishes and local products. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • (1) Ciorbă de burtă - A hearty soup made from tripe, vegetables, and sour cream, seasoned with garlic and fresh dill.
  • (2) Mici - A type of grilled sausage made from minced meat, garlic, and spices, often served with mustard or bread.
  • (3) Papanași - A sweet dessert made from cottage cheese, flour, and sugar, served with sour cream and fruit preserve.

Local Beverages

  • (1) Țuică - A strong plum brandy that is considered the national drink of Romania.
  • (2) Bere Bănățeană - A local beer from the Banat region, known for its rich taste and high hop content.
  • (3) Vin de Cozia - A delicious wine from the Cozia region, made from local grapes and aged in oak barrels.

Where to Stay in Câmpulung: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Câmpulung offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • (1) Pensiunea Sebis - A charming guesthouse located near the town’s center, with beautifully designed rooms and excellent local cuisine.
  • (2) Hotel Roibu - A modern hotel with spacious rooms, a restaurant, and a sauna, located in a peaceful area near the Zăganul Waterfall.
  • (3) Hostel Cheia - A budget-friendly option for backpackers, offering clean and comfortable rooms and a great location, close to the town’s main attractions.

What to Buy in Câmpulung: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Câmpulung’s markets and shops are filled with unique souvenirs and local products that are perfect for bringing home. Here are some of the best items to buy:

  • (1) Traditional handcrafted pottery and ceramics, featuring intricate designs and colors.
  • (2) Local honey and honey-based products, such as candles and natural skincare.
  • (3) Woven rugs, blankets, and textiles featuring traditional Romanian patterns and designs.

Local Culture and Customs of Câmpulung

Câmpulung has a rich and traditional culture, heavily influenced by the town’s religious history and folklore. The locals are friendly and hospitable, and visitors are often greeted with warm smiles and a shot of Țuică. It is customary to dress conservatively when visiting religious sites and to remove shoes before entering private homes or guesthouses.

Best Time to Visit Câmpulung

The best time to visit Câmpulung is from May to September when the weather is warm and sunny, and the region is bursting with natural beauty. However, the autumn months are also a great time to visit, with colorful foliage and harvest festivals.

Do’s and Don’ts of Câmpulung

Do:

  • (1) Take advantage of the region’s outdoor activities and natural attractions, such as hiking, cycling, and camping.
  • (2) Try traditional Romanian cuisine and local beverages, and shop for authentic souvenirs.
  • (3) Respect the local customs and traditions, dress conservatively in religious sites, and remove shoes before entering homes.

Don’t:

  • (1) Litter or damage natural sites, and respect wildlife and flora in the region.
  • (2) Wear revealing clothing or act inappropriately in public places, especially in religious sites.
  • (3) Disrespect or ignore the town’s rich cultural heritage and history.

Safety and Health

Safety in Câmpulung

Câmpulung is a safe destination, and violent crime is rare. However, it is advisable to take normal safety precautions, such as avoiding deserted areas at night, keeping valuables safe, and being aware of pickpocketing in crowded places.

Health in Câmpulung

Câmpulung has a good standard of medical care and facilities, with a regional hospital and several private clinics. Tap water is safe, but it is recommended to drink bottled water to avoid stomach upsets.

Câmpulung is easily accessible by various transport options:

  • (2) Sibiu International Airport - 100km away from Câmpulung, with flights from several European destinations.

Bus Stations Near Câmpulung

  • (1) Câmpulung Bus Station - The town’s main bus station, with regular connections to major Romanian cities and towns.
  • (2) Curtea de Argeș Bus Station - A nearby town with a bus station, offering connections to other destinations.

Other Options

  • (1) Car rental - A convenient option for exploring the region’s natural beauty and cultural sites.
  • (2) Taxi - A viable option for short-distance trips within town.

How to Get Around in Câmpulung

Câmpulung is a small town, and most of the main attractions can be explored on foot. However, for those who prefer to use public transport, the town has a reliable bus service with regular connections to neighboring towns and cities.

Where to Go Next from Câmpulung: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Câmpulung is a great base for exploring some of the best attractions and destinations in Romania. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions:

  • (1) Sibiu - A beautiful medieval town with well-preserved architecture and a vibrant cultural scene, located 100km away from Câmpulung.
  • (2) Bran Castle - A stunning castle, famously known as Dracula’s Castle, located 200km away from Câmpulung.
  • (3) Brasov - A beautiful town with a charming old town, lively cultural scene, and access to the Carpathian Mountains, located 150km away from Câmpulung.
