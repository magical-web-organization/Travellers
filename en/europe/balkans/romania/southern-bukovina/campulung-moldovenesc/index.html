Introduction to Câmpulung Moldovenesc

Câmpulung Moldovenesc is a small town located in the Southern Bukovina region of Romania. This town is known for its rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and warm hospitality of its locals. Câmpulung Moldovenesc is an ideal destination for a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.

List of Places to Visit in Câmpulung Moldovenesc

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Muzeul Etnografic - A museum that exhibits the traditional clothing, crafts, and way of life of the inhabitants of Bukovina.
  • Muzeul „Bucovina Profunda” – This museum showcases the history of Câmpulung Moldovenesc and its surroundings, from the times of Dacians and ancient Roman administration, to its modern history.
  • Cetatea Şoimilor – A fortress built during the 14th century by Moldavian princes.

Natural Attractions of Câmpulung Moldovenesc: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Rarău Mountain - A picturesque mountain with superb hiking trails that offer the most spectacular views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Mănăstirea Voroneţ – A UNESCO heritage site that is famous for its religious paintings in a unique shade of blue.
  • Bucovina Village Museum – A museum that depicts the life and traditions of the local people through various exhibitions.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Câmpulung Moldovenesc

Food and Drink Scene of Câmpulung Moldovenesc

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sarmale - A traditional Romanian dish consisting of minced meat wrapped in cabbage leaves
  • Mici – Grilled minced meat rolls made out of a mixture of beef, lamb, and pork.
  • Tochitura – A stew made out of pork meat, onions, and garlic

Local Beverages

  • Tuica – A strong liquor made from plums or other fruits.
  • Vin fiert – Mulled wine served hot with cinnamon and cloves.
  • Palinca – A traditional liquor made out of plums, pears, or apples.

Local Culture and Customs of Câmpulung Moldovenesc

The culture of Câmpulung Moldovenesc is deeply rooted in tradition and folklore. The locals celebrate numerous festivals throughout the year that involve singing, dancing, and displaying traditional clothing.

Best Time to Visit Câmpulung Moldovenesc

The best time to visit Câmpulung Moldovenesc is from May to October when the weather is mild, and nature is at its best.

Safety and Health

The crime rate in Câmpulung Moldovenesc is low, and it is a safe destination for tourists. It is always advisable to take safety precautions while traveling, such as keeping your valuables safe and being aware of your surroundings.
