Introduction to Poiana Brașov

Located in the heart of Transylvania, Poiana Brașov is a popular winter sports destination in Romania. The scenic alpine resort offers a wide range of outdoor activities and cultural experiences. With its beautiful natural surroundings, Poiana Brașov is also a popular summertime retreat. Known for its ski slopes, this amazing location provides travelers with exciting adventures amid the Carpathian Mountains.

In this comprehensive tour guide, you will learn everything you need to know about this exciting destination, including the best attractions, food and drink scene, cultural experiences, and more.

Local Culture and Customs of Poiana Brașov

Romanian culture is rich in traditions and customs. The people in Poiana Brașov are warm and welcoming. A few cultural customs to keep in mind during your visit are:

  • Dress modestly when visiting churches or mosques.
  • Allow elders to enter and exit first in public places.
  • Always bring a small gift when invited to someone’s home.

Best Time to Visit Poiana Brașov

The winter months (December - February) are the peak season, but summer (June - August) is also a great time to visit to experience the sunny weather and enjoy the great outdoors.

Do’s and Don’ts of Poiana Brașov

Do:

  • Try the traditional Romanian cuisine.
  • Take photographs of the beautiful scenery.
  • Respect the local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Litter in public places.
  • Be disrespectful to elders or inhabitants of the area.
  • Make loud noises in residential areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Poiana Brașov

Poiana Brașov is a safe place to visit. However, it is always advisable to take necessary precautions while travelling. Always keep your valuables safe and lock the doors and windows of your hotel at night.

Health in Poiana Brașov

There are no major health concerns in Poiana Brașov. However, it is always advisable to carry a first aid kit, insect repellent, and sunscreen when participating in outdoor activities. It is also recommended to drink bottled water to stay hydrated.
