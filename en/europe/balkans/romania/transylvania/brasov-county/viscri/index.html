Introduction to Viscri

Viscri is a small and picturesque village situated in the Brașov County of Transylvania, in Romania. It is unnoticed by most of the tourists but is a dream destination for those who like to explore new places. This tiny village is surrounded by mountains and is full of traditional Transylvanian houses and fortified churches. It is a perfect escape from the bustling city, and the village’s calmness will surely relax you and rejuvenate your soul.

Viscri has various places to visit, each with its charm. From museums and historical places to natural attractions and outdoor expeditions, there’s something for everyone.

Local Culture and Customs of Viscri

Viscri is a traditional village, and its customs and traditions are still followed by the locals. Here are some of the customs you should keep in mind while visiting Viscri:

  • Dress modestly while visiting the churches.
  • Follow the traditional food customs while dining.
  • Respect the privacy of locals and avoid intruding in their personal lives.

Best Time to Visit Viscri

The best time to visit Viscri is in the summer months from June to September. The weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities. The village also hosts a traditional festival in the month of July, which is worth attending.

Safety and Health

Safety in Viscri

Viscri is a safe village, and petty crimes are uncommon. However, visitors are advised to keep their valuables safe and follow basic safety precautions.

Health in Viscri

Viscri has no hospitals within the village, and visitors are advised to carry basic medications and first aid kits along with them.
