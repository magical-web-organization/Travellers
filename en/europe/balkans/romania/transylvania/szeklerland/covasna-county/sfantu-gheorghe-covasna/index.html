Introduction to Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna)

Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna) is a town located in Covasna County, in Szeklerland, Transylvania. It is known for its thermal springs, beautiful nature, and rich cultural heritage. The town is home to a mixed population of Romanians, Hungarians, and Roma, which contributes to its diverse cultural and gastronomical offerings.

Local Culture and Customs of Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna)

The town is home to a mixed population of Romanians, Hungarians, and Roma, which contributes to its diverse cultural offerings. The region is known for its traditional Hungarian music and folk dancing. Visitors can experience local customs during festivals, such as the Sfântu Gheorghe Days Festival in April and the Harvest Festival in September.

Best Time to Visit Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna)

The best time to visit Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna) is from May to September, when the weather is mild and there are plenty of outdoor activities available. The town is also worth visiting during the winter months, when visitors can go skiing in the nearby resorts.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna)

Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna) is a safe town for tourists, with low levels of crime. Visitors should be cautious when using public transportation and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna)

Visitors should be aware of the effects of altitude on their health when visiting Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna). The town is located at an altitude of over 600 meters above sea level. Due to the high mineral content of the thermal waters, visitors with certain health conditions should consult with a physician before bathing in the hot springs.

Getting to Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna): Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna)

The nearest airport to Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna) is the Targu-Mures International Airport, which is located about 50 km away.

Bus Stations Near Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna)

Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna) is well-connected by bus to major cities in Romania and Hungary. The town has one main bus station, located in the city center.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna) by train or by renting a car.

How to Get Around in Sfântu Gheorghe (Covasna)
