Introduction to Craiova

Located in the Oltenia region of Romania, Craiova is a charming city that offers a perfect blend of rich history, natural beauty, and cosmopolitan living. With a population of over 300,000, it is the sixth-largest city in Romania and a major cultural and economic hub of the region. From ancient fortresses and towering cathedrals to bustling markets and trendy cafes, Craiova has something to offer for every type of traveler.

List of Places to Visit in Craiova

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Craiova boasts a long and fascinating history that is reflected in its numerous museums and historical places. Among the must-visit cultural attractions in the city are:

  • Craiova Art Museum: Located in a former royal palace, the museum boasts an impressive collection of Romanian and European art from the 15th to the 20th century.
  • Craiova Fortress: This 14th-century fortress is one of the most iconic landmarks in Craiova and offers breathtaking views of the city.
  • Memorial Constantin Poppescu: This museum is dedicated to the famous Romanian painter Constantin Poppescu and showcases some of his most famous works.

Natural Attractions of Craiova: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you love the great outdoors, Craiova has plenty to offer in terms of natural beauty and outdoor adventures. Here are some of the top natural attractions in the city:

  • Nicolae Romanescu Park: This huge park is the largest in Craiova and is perfect for a stroll or a picnic. It features several lakes, walking trails, and playgrounds.
  • Oltenia Plateau: This stunning plateau offers spectacular views of the surrounding countryside and is a great place for hiking and biking.
  • Jiu River Gorges: Located just outside Craiova, the Jiu River Gorges are home to a wide range of wildlife and offer plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities such as hiking, rock climbing, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Craiova

Craiova is a great destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts, with plenty of scenic routes that offer breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. Some of the most popular hiking and biking routes in the area include:

  • The Oltenia Plateau Trail: This 20 km trail takes hikers and bikers through some of the most picturesque landscapes in the area.
  • Jiu River Gorges Trail: This challenging 10 km trail takes hikers and bikers through rugged terrain and offers stunning views of the gorge.
  • Nicolae Romanescu Park Trail: This 5 km trail takes hikers and bikers through the heart of the park and offers plenty of opportunities to enjoy nature and wildlife.

Best Things to Do in Craiova: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re looking for an adventure-packed trip or a relaxing getaway, Craiova has plenty of fun and exciting things to do. Here are some of the best activities and tours to enjoy in the city:

  • Craiova City Tour: This walking tour takes you through the most iconic landmarks in the city, including the Craiova Art Museum and the Craiova Fortress.
  • Taste of Craiova Tour: This culinary tour takes you on a journey through the local food scene, with stops at some of the best restaurants and cafes in the city.
  • Adventure Tour: This tour takes you on an adrenaline-packed journey through the Jiu River Gorges, with activities such as rock climbing, hiking, and kayaking.

Food and Drink Scene of Craiova

Must-Try Dishes

Craiova’s food scene is a testament to the city’s rich culinary heritage. Here are some of the must-try dishes in the city:

  • Sarmale: Cabbage rolls stuffed with minced meat and rice, served with sour cream.
  • Mititei: Grilled sausages made from minced beef, pork, and lamb, served with mustard and bread.
  • Ghiveci: A traditional vegetable stew made with eggplant, zucchini, and tomatoes.

Local Beverages

Craiova is a great destination for wine lovers, with a variety of great local wines to choose from. Some of the top local beverages in the city include:

  • Vin de Cojocari: A unique red wine made from a blend of local grape varieties.
  • Vin de Craiova: A dry white wine with a notes of honey and citrus.
  • Tuica: A traditional Romanian fruit brandy made from plums or other fruits.

Local Culture and Customs of Craiova

Craiova is a city with a rich cultural heritage, and visitors can expect to encounter a variety of interesting customs and traditions during their stay. Some of the most notable cultural customs of Craiova include:

  • Traditional folk dancing and music
  • Orthodox Christianity
  • Regular community festivals and celebrations

Best Time to Visit Craiova

The best time to visit Craiova is during the summer months of June to August when the weather is warm and sunny. However, visitors who prefer cooler weather may want to consider visiting during the spring or fall months when the countryside is at its most beautiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of Craiova

Do:

  • Learn a few Romanian phrases before your trip
  • Try the local food and drink
  • Respect local customs and traditions

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing when visiting religious sites
  • Litter or disrespect nature
  • Be rude to locals

Safety and Health

Safety in Craiova

Craiova is generally a safe city, but visitors should exercise common sense and take appropriate precautions to avoid pickpockets and other petty crimes.

Health in Craiova

Visitors should make sure their routine vaccinations are up to date before traveling to Craiova. While tap water is generally safe to drink in the city, it is recommended to drink bottled water as a precaution.

Getting to Craiova: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Craiova

Craiova International Airport is located just 7 km from the city center and offers daily flights to and from major European cities.

Bus Stations Near Craiova

Craiova Bus Station is located in the city center and offers daily bus services to and from other major cities in Romania.

Other Options

Travelers can also reach Craiova by car or train, with excellent road and rail links connecting the city to other parts of Romania and Europe.

How to Get Around in Craiova

Craiova is a relatively small city that is easy to get around on foot or by bike. Visitors who prefer public transportation can use the city’s bus or tram networks, which are reliable and inexpensive.

Where to Go Next from Craiova: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Craiova is a great starting point for exploring the Oltenia region and beyond. Some of the top nearby destinations and excursions include:

  • The UNESCO World Heritage Site of Horezu Monastery
  • The scenic town of Sibiu
  • The Danube Delta Biosphere Reserve

Conclusion

Craiova is a hidden gem in Romania that offers a perfect combination of natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and modern amenities. With plenty of things to see and do, it is a great destination for anyone looking for an authentic Romanian experience.
