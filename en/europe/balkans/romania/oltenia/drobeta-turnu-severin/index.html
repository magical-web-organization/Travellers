A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Drobeta-Turnu Severin, Romania

Are you planning to travel to Oltenia, Romania, and exploring the Balkans and Europe? If so, you should consider adding Drobeta-Turnu Severin to your itinerary. This small, charming city is located on the banks of the Danube River and offers an excellent mix of history, culture, and nature. In this comprehensive travel guide to Drobeta-Turnu Severin, we will give you all the information you need to plan your perfect trip.

Introduction to Drobeta-Turnu Severin

Drobeta-Turnu Severin is a city located in the western part of Oltenia, Romania. It’s not a very big city, but it’s full of history, culture, and natural beauty. The city is known for its strategic location on the Danube River, which has made it an important transportation hub for centuries. The city is also known for its important role in the Roman Empire and the Dacian Wars. Today, Drobeta-Turnu Severin is a bustling city with a rich cultural heritage and a friendly local community.

List of Places to Visit in Drobeta-Turnu Severin

Drobeta-Turnu Severin has plenty of places to visit and explore. Here is a list of some of the most important ones you should consider:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Iron Gates Museum - This museum is located in the city center and houses a large collection of historical artifacts and exhibits about the city’s history and cultural heritage.
  • The Trajan’s Bridge - This ancient Roman bridge is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-see attraction for history and architecture enthusiasts.
  • The Tabula Traiana - This historical monument is located near the Trajan’s Bridge and features an inscription describing the construction of the bridge under Emperor Trajan.
  • The Turnu Severin Art Museum - This museum houses an impressive collection of art and artifacts from the local area and beyond.
  • The Medieval Fortress of Severin - This historical landmark is located on a hill overlooking the city and offers excellent views of the surrounding area.

Natural Attractions of Drobeta-Turnu Severin: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Danube River - The beautiful Danube River is the heart of Drobeta-Turnu Severin, and you can enjoy a boat ride or a walk along the riverbank to take in the stunning views.
  • The Iron Gates National Park - This national park is located near the city and offers hiking, biking, and wildlife watching opportunities.
  • The Decebalus Sculpture - This massive rock sculpture of the legendary Dacian king is located near the Iron Gates Dam and is best viewed from a boat ride on the Danube River.
  • The Bigar Waterfall - This stunning waterfall is located a short drive from the city and is considered one of the most beautiful waterfalls in Romania.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Drobeta-Turnu Severin

Drobeta-Turnu Severin is a great destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. The city and its surrounding areas offer plenty of trails and routes for all skill levels. Here are some of the best routes to consider:

  • The Iron Gates Trail - This is a popular hiking and biking trail that follows the Danube River and offers stunning views of the Iron Gates Dam and the surrounding area.
  • The Ciucaru Mare Trail - This challenging hiking trail takes you to the top of Ciucaru Mare, the highest peak in the area, and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
  • The Danube Gorges Trail - This scenic hiking and biking trail takes you through the beautiful Danube Gorges, offering stunning views of the river and its surroundings.

Best Things to Do in Drobeta-Turnu Severin: Activities and Tours

  • Boat tour of the Danube River - Take a relaxing boat ride on the Danube River and enjoy the stunning views of the Iron Gates Dam and the surrounding area.
  • Visit the Iron Gates Dam - Take a guided tour of the Iron Gates Dam and learn about its history and importance for the local area and Europe as a whole.
  • Visit the Drobeta-Turnu Severin Fortress - Explore this historical fortress and learn about its role in the Dacian Wars and the Roman Empire.
  • Taste the local cuisine - Try some traditional Romanian dishes and local specialties, such as sarmale (stuffed cabbage rolls) and mici (grilled meat rolls).
  • Visit the local markets - Explore the local markets of Drobeta-Turnu Severin and buy some souvenirs and traditional handicrafts, such as handmade woolen products and pottery.

Food and Drink Scene of Drobeta-Turnu Severin

The food and drink scene in Drobeta-Turnu Severin is a mix of traditional Romanian dishes and local specialties. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sarmale - Stuffed cabbage rolls with minced meat, rice, and vegetables.
  • Mici - Grilled meat rolls made with minced beef, lamb, or pork.
  • Ciorba - A sour soup usually made with meat, vegetables, and sour cream.
  • Zacusca - A vegetable spread made with eggplant, peppers, and tomatoes.

Local Beverages

  • Tuica - A Romanian brandy made from plums.
  • Berbecut - A local beer made with traditional Romanian ingredients.
  • Wine - The Oltenia region is known for its wine production, and you can find some excellent red and white wines in Drobeta-Turnu Severin.
