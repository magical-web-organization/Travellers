Introduction to Njivice

Located on the western coast of the island of Krk in the Kvarner region of Croatia, Njivice is a beautiful seaside town that attracts tourists with its crystal-clear waters, sandy beaches, and scenic views. Njivice is known for its preserved cultural heritage, well-preserved nature, and Mediterranean climate.

List of Places to Visit in Njivice

Njivice is rich in cultural attractions, nature spots, and outdoor adventures. Here are some places and activities worth checking out:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Franciscan monastery that dates back to the 14th century. It was built in the Gothic style and is located in the town center.
  • The Church of St. Mary, built-in 1882. It features a beautiful altar with an oil painting of the Virgin Mary.
  • The ethnographic museum, located in the Monastery, featuring a collection of artifacts that depict the living traditions and lifestyle of the locals.
  • Njivice also has several archaeological sites, including the remains of Roman villas and a necropolis in Soline.

Natural Attractions of Njivice: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Soline bay, known for its healing mud and curative properties, making it a popular destination for health and wellness enthusiasts.
  • The Rujnica Forest Park, a natural oasis offering fantastic hiking and hiking trails and stunning panoramic views.
  • The nearby beaches and coves like the Kijac cove and Jaz cove are perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Njivice

Njivice is a fantastic destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best hiking and biking trails in the area:

  • The Njivice loop, a 6.5-km trail that takes you through olive groves, pine forests, and offers panoramic views of the island and the sea.
  • The Rujnica Forest trail, a moderate to a difficult trail that takes you through a forested area and has some steep climbs and descents.
  • The Soline trail, a moderately easy trail that takes you along the Soline bay and is an excellent spot for bird-watching and nature photography.

Best Things to Do in Njivice: Activities and Tours

Njivice offers a variety of exciting activities and tours. Here are some of the best things to do in the area:

  • Explore the beaches and coves around Njivice by boat.
  • Take a wine tour and taste some of the best local wines.
  • Rent a bike or scooter and explore the island at your own pace.
  • Take a paddleboarding or kayaking tour and discover the hidden coves around Njivice.

Week-Long Itinerary for Njivice

Here is a sample itinerary for a week-long trip to Njivice:

Day 1: Arrive in Njivice, check-in at your accommodation, and explore the town center.

Day 2: Take a boat tour of the surrounding coves and beaches.

Day 3: Visit the Soline Bay and take a mud bath.

Day 4: Rent a bike or scooter and explore the island.

Day 5: Go wine tasting and visit local wineries.

Day 6: Take a hiking or biking tour in the Rujnica Forest Park.

Day 7: Spend the day relaxing on the beach.

Food and Drink Scene of Njivice

Njivice is a food lover’s paradise. Its cuisine is based on fresh seafood, olive oil, and local ingredients. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes:

  • Scampi Buzara (Scampi in a tomato-based sauce)
  • Njoki in Šmriki (Dumplings served with truffle sauce)
  • Peka (Mix of vegetables and meat, baked with potatoes in a covered pan)

Local Beverages:

  • Žlahtina (White wine made from a grape variety indigenous to the island of Krk)
  • Medica (Honey brandy)
  • Lozovača (Grape brandy)

Where to Stay in Njivice: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Njivice offers a wide range of accommodation options, from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels and guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay in Njivice:

  • Hotel Jadran is a luxury hotel with a spa, outdoor pool, and tennis court.
  • Hotel Miramare is a family-friendly hotel with a private beach and water sports facilities.
  • Apartments Junijana is a budget-friendly option that offers self-catering apartments.

What to Buy in Njivice: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some souvenirs and products you can bring back home from Njivice:

  • Olive oil
  • Local wine
  • Handmade lace
  • Souvenir t-shirts and magnets

Local Culture and Customs of Njivice

Njivice is a Catholic town, and customs and religious traditions are deeply ingrained in the local culture. Tipping is customary in restaurants and cafes, with 10% being the usual amount. The locals are hospitable and friendly, and tourists are always welcome.

Best Time to Visit Njivice

The best time to visit Njivice is from May to October when the weather is warm, and the sea is warm enough for swimming. July and August are the peak tourist season, and the town can get crowded.

Do’s and Don’ts of Njivice

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Tip servers in cafes and restaurants.
  • Use sunscreen and wear a hat in the sun.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing while visiting churches or religious sites.
  • Litter on the beaches or in nature.

Safety and Health

Safety in Njivice

Njivice is a safe town, with low crime rates. However, it is always advisable to take precautions. Always lock your accommodation and do not leave valuables in your car.

Health in Njivice

Njivice has a medical center and a pharmacy. Tourists can also seek medical assistance from the hospital in Krk town.

Getting to Njivice: Transportation Options and Logistics

Njivice is easily accessible by air, road, and sea. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Njivice

The nearest airport is Rijeka Airport, which is around 20km from Njivice. Zadar Airport is another option, which is around 170 km away.

Bus Stations Near Njivice

Njivice has several bus stops around town. The main bus station is in Krk town, from where you can take a bus to Njivice.

Other Options

Another option is to rent a car and drive to Njivice. Ferries connect Krk island with the mainland, and you can take your car on the ferry.

How to Get Around in Njivice

Njivice is a small town, and most places are easily accessible on foot. However, you can also rent a bike or scooter to explore the town and the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Njivice: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Njivice is an excellent starting point for exploring the Kvarner region and the island of Krk. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions:

  • Town of Krk (8km)
  • Biserujka Cave (12km)
  • The town of Omisalj (20km)
  • Vrbnik (20km)

Conclusion

Njivice is a fantastic destination that offers something for everyone. From cultural attractions to natural wonders, outdoor adventures to fantastic cuisine, tourists will never run out of things to see and to do. Plan your trip to Njivice today and experience the beauty of this Croatian gem.
