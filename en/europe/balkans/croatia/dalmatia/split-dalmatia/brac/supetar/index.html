Introduction to Supetar

Nestled in the Split-Dalmatia region of Croatia, Supetar is a hidden gem that offers a perfect mix of cultural, historical, and outdoor attractions. The town with its vibrant streets, charming old buildings, and picturesque surroundings depicts a peaceful, idyllic lifestyle that is perfect for travelers who want to escape the bustle of the city.

The town is located in Brač, one of the country’s largest islands in the Adriatic sea. Access to Supetar is by ferries from Split, and the trip will take you about 50 minutes. The city is small enough to walk around, and its friendly and accommodating locals make it a popular tourist destination.

List of Places to Visit in Supetar

Supetar offers a perfect mix of both natural and cultural attractions that visitors will fall in love with. Here are some of the top places to visit in Supetar:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Church of the Holy Mary of the Annunciation - This early Christian basilica is one of three UNESCO World Heritage Sites located on Brač island. It’s a must-visit for anyone interested in ancient architecture and religion.
  2. Vidova Gora - The highest peak on the island of Brač is a gorgeous artistry of nature, and the scenery from the top of this mountain will leave you breathless. It provides an incredible panoramic view of the island’s northern coastline and the surrounding area.
  3. Pučišća Stonemasonry School - This historic institution dates back to the 18th century, and it’s a symbol of Brač island’s rich cultural heritage. It’s a haven for artists and architects interested in ancient masonry techniques.

Natural Attractions of Supetar: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Brač Island Stone Quarries - The reputable stone quarries of Brač island are a must-see sight for tourists visiting Supetar. These storied quarries date back to an era that dates as far back as the ancient Greeks to the present era, where they are still functional.
  2. Bol Beach - Bol beach offers an abundance of beautiful sand and crystal-clear sapphire waters. Its location is perfect for activities such as wind-catching, scuba diving, and beach relaxation.
  3. Olive groves - Olive groves across Brač island have been around for centuries and serve as a common sight across Supetar. It’s a fantastic place for nature lovers, who can enjoy the scenery while walking around and maybe witness the olive harvest season and learn about the olive oil extraction process first-hand.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Supetar

Supetar provides visitors with beautiful hiking and biking opportunities across island landscapes and, of course, seaside trails with spectacular ocean views. Here are some of the top trails to try out:

  1. Brač Waterfalls Trail - This stunning route, located outside Skrip, leads to three waterfalls snuggled in a lush and beautiful forest setting. It’s an easy trail with long stretches of hikeable route sections.
  2. Vidova Gora - This scenic route is one of the most challenging mountain bike trails in Croatia, but the views of the Adriatic sea from the top make it completely worth doing.
  3. Škrip-Bol Trail - This trail stretches out fourteen kilometers through ancient villages, dense pine forests, and idyllic old olive groves. This exciting trail takes in beautiful sea views and hidden beaches amidst traditional cultural heritage.

Best Things to Do in Supetar: Activities and Tours

The town provides visitors with various activities and exciting tours to choose from. Here are some of the best things to do in Supetar:

  1. Wine tour - The Paklina vineyards on the slopes of Vidova Gora provide some of the island’s best wines and olive oil, and taking a tour to see the vineyards and learn about the production process is an unforgettable experience.
  2. Beach relaxation - The town has several beaches perfect for swimming and sunbathing, including the famous Banj beach in Supetar.
  3. Walking tour - Taking a stroll in Supetar and exploring the city’s streets, admiring old buildings in the town and the countryside is as enjoyable as it is educational.

Food and Drink Scene of Supetar

The food and drink scene of Supetar offers a glimpse into the town’s culinary heritage. Enjoy local dishes and excellent wines at the following places:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Peka - This hearty Croatian dish consists of meat and vegetables cooked under a bell-shaped lid fired by wood.
  2. Pašticada - This slow-cooked beef dish in thick, delicious sauce is a result of an intricate and time-consuming cooking process that has remained the same for generations.
  3. Fritule - This Croatian sweet and flavorful pastry, similar to a doughnut, is the perfect dessert.

Local Beverages

  1. Brač Island Wine - This wine provides visitors with a delightful taste of the island’s unique climate, terroir, and local flavors.
  2. Rakija - This Croatian brandy is usually made from fruits like grapes, apples, and plums.

Where to Stay in Supetar: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Supetar offers plenty of options for accommodation during your stay, whether you’re traveling solo or with a group. Here are some of the best places to stay in Supetar:

  1. Waterman Village - A four-star family-friendly hotel located on the island’s southern coast with plenty of recreational facilities, including a large sports complex.
  2. Villa Supetar - A luxurious guesthouse offering sea views, spacious rooms, and luxury baths, located in an old stone villa in Supetar’s center, close to the harbor.
  3. Hostel Villa Olimpica - A budget-friendly hostel that offers dormitory rooms set in an elegant villa with a shared kitchen and living room facilities.

What to Buy in Supetar: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Supetar provides tourists with various souvenirs and local products to take home, including:

  1. Brač Stone Carving - The island’s quarries and the skilled craftsmanship of its artisans have produced stunning hand-carved stone items ranging from wine racks to fireplace surrounds.
  2. Olive oil - The island is famous for its excellent quality olive oil, and you can purchase a bottle of cold-pressed or extra virgin oil from local markets.
  3. Local Wine - The island produces remarkable wines with a unique flavor thanks to the temperate climate and limestone soils.

Local Culture and Customs of Supetar

Supetar locals are friendly and welcoming to tourists, and it’s important for visitors to observe the following cultural customs and traditions:

  • Visitors are expected to dress appropriately while visiting cultural or religious sites.
  • Smoking is banned in public places like beaches, restaurants, and bars.
  • Sunday is considered a day of rest, and it’s advisable to avoid making loud noises and or wash clothes and refrain from working outside the house on this day.

Best Time to Visit Supetar

The best time to visit Supetar is between May and September. The weather during these months is warm and sunny, and it’s ideal for beach days and outdoor activities. The peak season in Supetar is July and August, which comes with larger crowds and higher prices.

Do’s and Don’ts of Supetar

Do:

  1. Tip in restaurants and cafes is customary in Croatia.
  2. Try the local dishes and wines.
  3. Learn about the local culture and customs through interactions with the locals.

Don’t:

  1. Don’t litter or disrespect the local environment.
  2. Don’t scream or whistle, it is considered to be rude.
  3. Don’t forget to wear appropriate clothing while visiting cultural and religious sites.

Safety and Health

Supetar is a safe travel destination, and visitors rarely experience safety concerns. You should take standard safety measures such as staying aware of your surroundings and keeping your valuables safe.

Safety in Supetar

  1. Be mindful of your surroundings and take reasonable precautions.
  2. Keep an eye on your belongings when you are on the beach or at beach bars.
  3. Always follow traffic rules and take special care on the narrow roads of the island.

Health in Supetar

There are no significant health risks or concerns in Supetar. However, tourists are required to get vaccinated against tick-borne encephalitis when visiting the island during the summer season.

Getting to Supetar: Transportation Options and Logistics

Access to Supetar is by taking ferries from Split. Jadrolinija runs a regular ferry service from mainland Croatia to the island of Brač. Sometimes during the peak season, additional ferries run to cater to the influx of tourists.

Airports Near Supetar

The nearest airports to Supetar are:

  1. Split airport (SPU) - Located approximately an hour’s drive from the town.
  2. Zadar airport (ZAD) - Located approximately two and a half hours’ drive from the town.

Bus Stations Near Supetar

Bus transportation in Croatia is a cheap and convenient way to travel around. There are several bus companies that run regular services to and from Split to Supetar.

Other Options

You may also opt to travel to Split from other countries in Europe by train, car, or bus, then take a ferry across to Supetar.

How to Get Around in Supetar

Getting around Supetar is easy with public transportation, which includes taxis, buses, and car rentals. However, many visitors opt to walk or cycle due to the town’s relatively small size.

Where to Go Next from Supetar: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Tourists will want to explore other destinations around Supetar. Here are some of the top nearby destinations and excursions:

  1. Bol - This small and charming town located on the southern coast of Brac Island, is famous for its winding beaches, crystal-clear water, and rich cultural heritage. Its historic sites and an array of water sports make it a great place to visit for a day trip.
  2. Zlatni Rat Beach - Zlatni Rat beach is located on Brač Island’s southern coast. This world-class beach has been voted the most beautiful beach in Europe numerous times by travelers and reviewers.
  3. Vis Island - This island located south of Brač Island is a perfect destination for a day trip. The island is famous for its untouched natural beauty, traditional architecture, and cultural and historical heritage.

Conclusion

Supetar is a perfect travel destination for anyone who wants to relax and enjoy the quieter, serene life while admiring cultural and historical sites and taking part in outdoor activities. Visitors should expect to indulge in great food and wine with luxurious sea views, take unforgettable tours, and learn about the fascinating local culture, traditions, and viewpoints. Supetar is a welcoming town that will leave you feeling refreshed and rejuvenated.
