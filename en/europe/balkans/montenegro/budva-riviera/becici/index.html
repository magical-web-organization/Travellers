Introduction to Bečići

Welcome to Bečići, a charming seaside resort nestled in the Budva Riviera in Montenegro. This beautiful town is located in the Balkans and offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural attractions. Bečići is known for its stunning sandy beaches, picturesque landscapes, and warm Mediterranean climate.

List of Places to Visit in Bečići

Here are some must-visit attractions in Bečići:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Budva City Walls - This magnificent fortress dates back to the Middle Ages and is a must-visit for history buffs.
  • The Maritime Museum - Learn about the rich maritime history of Montenegro and explore various exhibitions showcasing ancient maps, ship models, and more.
  • The 4th-century Roman mosaics - Discover the unique and stunning mosaics showcasing the daily lives of the Romans.

Natural Attractions of Bečići: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Jaz Beach - This stunning beach is an ideal spot for sunbathing, swimming, and water activities such as parasailing and jet skiing.
  • Sveti Stefan - Explore the breathtaking scenery of this beautiful island, once visited by the rich and famous.
  • Skadar Lake - Take a boat ride and discover the stunning natural beauty of Montenegro’s largest lake.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bečići

Bečići offers some of the most beautiful hiking and biking routes in the Balkan region. Here are some must-visit trails:

  • The Vrmac ridge - This is a challenging hike but offers stunning views of the bay and the Adriatic sea.
  • The Kanica trail - This is a classical medieval path connecting the coast to the hinterland of Montenegro.
  • The Crkvice canyon - This trail features stunning views of the Tara river and panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

Best Things to Do in Bečići: Activities and Tours

Here are some fun activities you can do in Bečići:

  • Sunbathing and swimming at the beach.
  • Water activities such as kayaking, parasailing, and jet-skiing.
  • Boat tours to nearby islands and bays.
  • Wine tasting tours to discover the rich wine culture of Montenegro.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bečići

Food and Drink Scene of Bečići

Bečići offers a unique blend of Mediterranean and Balkan cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cevapi - grilled minced meat served with flatbread and onions.
  • Burek - a savory pastry filled with meat, cheese, or vegetables.
  • Peka - a local dish of meat, vegetables, and potatoes cooked under a bell-shaped lid.

Local Beverages

  • Rakija - a strong fruit brandy, usually consumed as an aperitif.
  • Vranac - a full-bodied red wine, often paired with savory dishes.
  • Nikšićko Pivo - a local beer, perfect for a warm summer evening.

Bečići offers a wide range of accommodation options to suit every budget and preference. Here are some top picks:

  • Hotel Splendid - A luxury hotel with a private beach and spa.
  • Montebay Residence - A modern apartment with stunning sea views.
  • Hotel Kuc - A budget-friendly hotel with a great location.

What to Buy in Bečići: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some souvenirs and local products to bring home from Bečići:

  • Olive oil - Montenegro produces high-quality olive oil.
  • Wine - Try the famous Vranac red wine.
  • Handmade souvenirs - You can find handcrafted ceramics, textiles, and jewelry in the local markets.

Local Culture and Customs of Bečići

Montenegrins are known for their friendliness and hospitality. It is customary to greet people with a smile and a handshake, and it is considered polite to bring a small gift when visiting someone’s home.

Best Time to Visit Bečići

The best time to visit Bečići is from May to September when the weather is warm and sunny. The peak season is from July to August when the town is bustling with tourists. If you prefer a quieter and more relaxed atmosphere, consider visiting in May or September.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bečići

Here are some do’s and don’ts of Bečići:

Do:

  • Dress appropriately when visiting religious sites.
  • Bring sunscreen and plenty of water when visiting the beach.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter - Montenegro is a beautiful country, and it is important to keep it that way.
  • Don’t drink tap water - It is recommended to drink bottled water instead.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bečići

Bečići is generally a safe place to visit, with low crime rates. However, it is recommended to take precautions, such as:

  • Don’t leave your valuables unattended on the beach.
  • Take extra care when crossing the road.

Health in Bečići

Montenegro has a well-developed healthcare system, and most major towns have hospitals and clinics. It is recommended to have travel insurance and to check with your doctor before traveling.

Getting to Bečići: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bečići is easily accessible by air, bus, or car. Here are some options:

Airports Near Bečići

  • Tivat Airport - 27 km away, with regular flights to European destinations.
  • Podgorica Airport - 60 km away, with regular flights to European destinations.

Bus Stations Near Bečići

  • Budva Bus Station - 3 km away, with regular buses to major destinations in Montenegro.
  • Podgorica Bus Station - 60 km away, with regular buses to major destinations in Montenegro and neighboring countries.

Other Options

  • Car rental - Renting a car is a great option if you want to explore the surrounding areas.
  • Taxi - Taxis are readily available in Bečići, but be sure to agree on a price before getting in.

How to Get Around in Bečići

Bečići is a small town, and most attractions are within walking distance. You can also rent a bike or take a taxi if you prefer. Buses are also available to major destinations in Montenegro.

Where to Go Next from Bečići: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Kotor - A beautiful medieval town with stunning architecture and a UNESCO-listed Old Town.
  • Lovćen National Park - Discover the stunning landscapes and hiking trails in this beautiful national park.
  • Durmitor National Park - Explore the stunning natural beauty and ski slopes of this national park.

Conclusion

Bečići is a charming seaside town that offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural attractions. Whether you prefer relaxing on the beach, hiking in the mountains, or enjoying local cuisine, Bečići has something for everyone. We hope this comprehensive guide has helped you plan your trip to this beautiful town in Montenegro.
