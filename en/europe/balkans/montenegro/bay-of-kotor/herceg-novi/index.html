Introduction to Herceg Novi

Welcome to Herceg Novi, a beautiful coastal town situated in the Bay of Kotor, Montenegro. It is a picturesque destination that offers pristine beaches and stunning mountainscapes. Herceg Novi is brimming with historic landmarks, cultural institutions, and outdoor adventures. This guide will help you explore the best points of interest, itinerary ideas, and must-try dishes in the area. Let’s start!

List of Places to Visit in Herceg Novi

Get your itinerary ready, as the town is full of wonderful tourist spots for all kinds of travelers. We have compiled a list of the top places to visit in Herceg Novi to make the most of your trip.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Herceg Novi is rich in cultural and historical landmarks that show the charm of old architecture and preserved traditions. Explore these culturally significant places:

  • Forte Mare Castle
  • Kanli Kula Fortress
  • Clock Tower
  • Monastery Savina

Natural Attractions of Herceg Novi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Looking for some adventure in Herceg Novi? There are some breathtakingly beautiful natural attractions that will leave you in awe, such as:

  • Blue Cave
  • Mamula Island
  • Orjen Mountain
  • Kamenari Ferry

Hiking/Biking Routes of Herceg Novi

The town is full of fantastic hiking and biking routes that are perfect for adventure lovers. Some convenient and scenic routes are here for you:

  • Subra Circular Trail
  • Lukovici Waterfall Trail
  • Bijela-Boka Kotorska Bay Trail
  • Vrmac Ridge Trail

Best Things to Do in Herceg Novi: Activities and Tours

There is a lot of fun things to do in Herceg Novi for tourists of all ages and interests. Here are our top picks:

  • Kayaking, canoeing, and paddleboarding
  • Snorkelling and diving
  • Day trips to Budva or Sveti Stefan
  • Adventure parks and zip lining

Best Time to Visit Herceg Novi

The best time to visit Herceg Novi is during the months of March through June and September through November, when the crowds are fewer, and the weather is pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Herceg Novi

Do:

  • Try the local food and wines.
  • Explore the historical landmarks and natural wonders
  • Respect the customs and traditions of the locals

Don’t:

  • Litter on the streets or in the bay.
  • Be noisy in the late hours in the surrounding neighborhoods.
  • Disrespect religious and cultural traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Herceg Novi

Herceg Novi is a safe town, but it’s necessary to be cautious of pickpocketing in crowded areas.

Health in Herceg Novi

There are clinics and hospitals in Herceg Novi and the surrounding areas in case of unexpected instances. But always have travel insurance with health coverage before starting your trip.

Getting to Herceg Novi: Transportation Options and Logistics

Herceg Novi is easily accessible, with multiple transportation options available.

Airports Near Herceg Novi

The closest international airport is the Dubrovnik Airport, which is approximately 30 miles away from the town.

Bus Stations Near Herceg Novi

The town has its central station that connects to other areas in Montenegro via buses.

Other Options

You can also take taxis, charter a private car, or take the ferry to reach the town.

How to Get Around in Herceg Novi

One of the best ways to explore the town and the nearby attractions is by car or scooter. Public transport is reasonable and reliable.
