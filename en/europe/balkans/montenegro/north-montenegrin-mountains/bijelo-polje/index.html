Bijelo Polje is a quaint and charming town nestled in the North Montenegrin Mountains of Montenegro. With stunning natural landscapes, vibrant cultural heritage, and a range of exciting activities, Bijelo Polje is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking adventure and exploration. In this comprehensive travel guide to Bijelo Polje, we’ll take you through everything you need to know about this wonderful town, including top attractions, hiking trails, the best places to eat and stay, local customs, and much more.

Introduction to Bijelo Polje

Bijelo Polje, which means “white field” in Montenegrin language, is a small town located in the northwestern region of Montenegro. The town is surrounded by enchanting natural beauty, including misty mountains, lush green forests, and glistening rivers. Visitors flock to Bijelo Polje for its rustic charm, quiet ambiance, and the range of outdoor activities that are on offer.

The town is home to a rich cultural heritage, with many museums, galleries, and historical sites to explore. Bijelo Polje is also known for its delicious traditional cuisine, which is a fusion of Mediterranean and Balkan flavors, and is a delight for foodies. With its stunning scenery, friendly locals, and laid-back vibe, Bijelo Polje is a hidden gem that’s waiting to be discovered.

List of Places to Visit in Bijelo Polje

When it comes to attractions, Bijelo Polje has something for everyone. Whether you’re interested in history, culture, or nature, there are plenty of places to explore in this idyllic town. Here are some of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Bijelo Polje Museum - This museum is a must-visit for history buffs, with its vast collection of archaeological artifacts, traditional costumes, and historical documents.

  • Medieval Fortress - This ancient fortress is a testament to the town’s rich history and offers a stunning view of the surrounding area.

Natural Attractions of Bijelo Polje: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Biogradska Gora National Park - This beautiful national park boasts crystal-clear lakes, dense forests, and a diverse range of wildlife. Visitors can explore the park on foot, bike, or horseback.

  • Lim River Canyon - This scenic canyon is perfect for hiking and offers breathtaking views of the river and surrounding mountains.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bijelo Polje

One of the best ways to experience the natural beauty of Bijelo Polje is by hitting the hiking trails. The town is home to numerous trails that offer a mix of challenging and easy hikes, ranging from leisurely strolls through lush forests to more challenging climbs up rocky terrain. Here are some of the top hiking and biking routes in Bijelo Polje:

  • Prokletije National Park - This park offers some of the most stunning hiking trails in Montenegro, with routes suitable for both beginners and experienced hikers.

  • The Bijele Vode Trail - This scenic hiking trail offers stunning views of the town and the surrounding mountains.

Best Things to Do in Bijelo Polje: Activities and Tours

Bijelo Polje offers visitors a range of exciting activities, from outdoor adventures to cultural experiences. Here are some of the best things to do in the town:

  • Rafting on the Lim River - The Lim River is a popular destination for rafting, with stunning scenery and thrilling rapids.

  • Wine Tasting Tour - Montenegro is known for its delicious wine, and a wine tasting tour is the perfect way to sample some of the region’s best vintages.

Food and Drink Scene of Bijelo Polje

Montenegrin cuisine is a delightful mix of Mediterranean and Balkan flavors, and Bijelo Polje is no exception. From hearty stews to fresh seafood, the town offers a range of delicious dishes that will tantalize your taste buds. Here are some of the must-try dishes when visiting Bijelo Polje:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cevapi - This grilled dish consists of small sausage-like meatballs, served with ajvar and bread.

  • Kacamak - This hearty dish is made with cornmeal, cheese, and cream, and is a delicious comfort food.

Local Beverages

  • Rakija - This traditional fruit brandy is a must-try when visiting Bijelo Polje.

  • Vranac - This rich and full-bodied red wine is one of Montenegro’s most popular wines and is a must-try for wine enthusiasts.

Where to Stay in Bijelo Polje: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bijelo Polje offers visitors a range of accommodation options, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. Here are some of the best places to stay in Bijelo Polje:

  • The Guesthouse Skadarlija - This charming guesthouse is located in the heart of the Old Town and offers comfortable and affordable accommodations.

  • The Bianca Resort & Spa - This luxurious five-star hotel offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and boasts a range of amenities, including a spa and wellness center.

What to Buy in Bijelo Polje: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for a unique souvenir to bring back from Bijelo Polje, there are plenty of local products that make for the perfect gift. Here are some of the top souvenirs to buy:

  • Traditional Ceramics - Bijelo Polje is known for its beautiful handmade ceramics, which make for a beautiful and unique souvenir.

  • Honey - Montenegro is famous for its honey, and Bijelo Polje produces some of the best honey in the region.

Local Culture and Customs of Bijelo Polje

Bijelo Polje is a town with a rich cultural heritage, steeped in tradition and history. Montenegrins are known for their warm and welcoming nature, and visitors to Bijelo Polje can expect to be greeted with hospitality and kindness. It’s important to be respectful of the local customs and traditions, which include wearing modest clothing when visiting religious sites and removing your shoes when entering someone’s home.

Best Time to Visit Bijelo Polje

The best time to visit Bijelo Polje is between May and September when the weather is warm and sunny, and the outdoor activities are in full swing. The town is also beautiful during the winter months, with snow-covered mountains and the chance to enjoy skiing and winter sports.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bijelo Polje

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine and wine - Montenegrin food is a must-try, and Bijelo Polje offers some of the best traditional dishes in the region.

  • Explore the outdoor attractions - From hiking to rafting, Bijelo Polje offers a range of exciting outdoor activities to enjoy.

Don’t:

  • Be disrespectful of the local customs and traditions.

  • Overpack - You’ll need to travel light if you’re planning on hiking or biking in Bijelo Polje.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bijelo Polje

Bijelo Polje is a safe and friendly town, but visitors should take basic precautions, such as avoiding walking alone at night and keeping valuables in a secure place. It’s also important to be vigilant when hiking or exploring the natural areas surrounding the town, and to follow the advice of local guides or officials.

Health in Bijelo Polje

Montenegro offers a high standard of healthcare, and visitors can access medical services in Bijelo Polje if needed. It’s important to have travel insurance that covers medical care, in case of any emergencies.

Getting to Bijelo Polje: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bijelo Polje is well-connected to other parts of Montenegro, and visitors can easily reach the town by bus or car. Here are the main transportation options for getting to Bijelo Polje:

Airports Near Bijelo Polje

The nearest airport to Bijelo Polje is Podgorica Airport, which is around 87 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Bijelo Polje

Bijelo Polje has a well-connected bus station, with regular services to other parts of Montenegro and neighboring countries, such as Serbia and Bosnia.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Bijelo Polje by train, with the town’s train station offering connections to Podgorica and other destinations in Montenegro and the wider Balkans.

How to Get Around in Bijelo Polje

The best way to explore Bijelo Polje is on foot, with many of the town’s attractions located within easy walking distance of each other. Visitors can also rent bikes to explore the area, or arrange for transportation with local tour operators.

Where to Go Next from Bijelo Polje: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bijelo Polje is located in a picturesque region of Montenegro, with many other beautiful towns and natural attractions located nearby. Some of the top destinations to visit include:

  • Durmitor National Park - This stunning national park is home to some of the most beautiful natural attractions in Montenegro, including the Tara River Canyon and the Black Lake.

  • Podgorica - Montenegro’s capital city is a vibrant and exciting destination, with a rich history, beautiful architecture, and a range of cultural attractions.

Conclusion

Bijelo Polje is a hidden gem that offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and exciting activities. With stunning scenery, delicious food, and a warm and welcoming atmosphere, Bijelo Polje is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking adventure and exploration. This comprehensive travel guide provides everything you need to know to plan your perfect trip to this idyllic town, from top attractions to the best places to stay and eat. So pack your bags, grab your hiking boots, and get ready to experience all that Bijelo Polje has to offer!
