Food and Drink Scene of Kumanovo

Must-Try Dishes

  • Tavce gravce: A traditional Macedonian dish made with beans and served with roasted peppers and bread.
  • Pleskavica: A Balkan-style burger made from a mixture of beef and pork, served with onions and roasted peppers.
  • Mekici: A sweet pastry made with flour, eggs, and yogurt, commonly served with jam.

Local Beverages

  • Rakija: A traditional Macedonian fruit brandy commonly served as an aperitif.
  • Skopje Beer: A locally brewed beer that is well known for its crisp and refreshing taste.

Where to Stay in Kumanovo: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options to suit every budget in Kumanovo. Here are some of the best options:

  • Hotel Bella Donna: A centrally located hotel offering comfortable rooms and easy access to the city’s main attractions.
  • Hostel Plamski: A budget-friendly hostel located in the heart of the city, offering dorm-style accommodation and a shared kitchen.
  • Etno House Shancheva: A guesthouse located on the outskirts of Kumanovo, providing a traditional Macedonian stay experience.

What to Buy in Kumanovo: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Traditional Macedonian Ceramics: Handmade ceramic dishes, pots, and vases showcasing Macedonian art and culture.
  • Kumanovo Knives: Known for their sharpness and durability, the Kumanovo knife is a perfect souvenir for those interested in Macedonian craftsmanship.
  • Red and white tablecloths: A symbol of Macedonian tradition and hospitality, these tablecloths are an ideal gift for family and friends.

Local Culture and Customs of Kumanovo

Kumanovo has a rich cultural heritage that includes traditional songs and dances from the Ottoman era. Locals are warm and welcoming, but it’s advisable to dress modestly while visiting religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Kumanovo

The best time to visit Kumanovo is from April to June and from September to November. The weather is mild, and the crowds are thinner, allowing you to explore the city’s attractions comfortably.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kumanovo

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Respect the city’s cultural heritage and dress modestly while visiting religious sites.
  • Greet locals with a smile and engage in conversations.

Don’t:

  • Overindulge in alcohol, especially in public places.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  • Take pictures of military installations.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kumanovo

Kumanovo is a safe city, but it’s necessary to take precautions while traveling. Avoid dark alleys and deserted streets at night, and be cautious against pickpockets and thieves.

Health in Kumanovo

Kumanovo has a good healthcare system, and there are several hospitals and clinics located throughout the city. Make sure you have travel insurance before embarking on your trip.

Getting to Kumanovo: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Kumanovo

  • Skopje International Airport: Located 30 km south of Kumanovo, it offers domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Kumanovo

  • Kumanovo Bus Station: Located in the city center, it offers regular bus services to various cities in North Macedonia.

Other Options

  • Train: The main railway station is located in Skopje, offering domestic and international train services.
  • Taxi: Taxis are readily available in the city, but make sure to negotiate fares before embarking on a journey.

How to Get Around in Kumanovo

Kumanovo is a small city and easily navigable on foot. Alternatively, there are several taxi services available, or you can rent a car to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Kumanovo: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Skopje: The capital of North Macedonia, and home to the stunning Matka Canyon.
  • Tetovo: Known for its Ottoman-era architecture and Hasan Baba Tekke Shrine.
  • Kriva Palanka: A small town known for its virgin nature and the Smolare Waterfall.

Conclusion

Kumanovo is a hidden gem in North Macedonia, offering a perfect blend of history, culture, and adventure. From its magnificent monuments and beautiful natural surroundings to its delectable cuisine and welcoming locals, Kumanovo promises to be a fascinating destination for all types of travellers.
