Novo Brdo Travel Guide

Welcome to Novo Brdo, a hidden gem of Kosovo, located in the Balkans region of Europe. This small town is known for its historical significance, natural beauty, and hiking trails. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore all the must-visit places, things to do, where to stay, and how to get around in Novo Brdo.

Introduction to Novo Brdo

Novo Brdo is a town in southeastern Kosovo, located approximately 30 km east of Pristina, the capital city of Kosovo. The town has a rich history, with its origins dating back to the 14th century. Novo Brdo was once a thriving mining town, known for its abundance of silver and gold. However, the town was destroyed during the Ottoman invasion in the late 15th century. Today, Novo Brdo is a peaceful town that offers stunning natural landscapes and cultural attractions.

List of Places to Visit in Novo Brdo

Novo Brdo is a small town, but it is full of cultural and natural attractions that you shouldn’t miss. Here is a list of places to visit during your trip to Novo Brdo:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Novo Brdo Fortress: The Novo Brdo Fortress is the most famous attraction in the town. Built in the 14th century, the fortress offers panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
  • St. Nikola Church: This church is one of the oldest churches in the Balkans region, built in the 14th-15th century. The church features beautiful frescoes and architecture.
  • Mining Museum: The Mining Museum showcases the town’s history as a thriving mining town.

Natural Attractions of Novo Brdo: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Novo Brdo Hiking Trails: Novo Brdo offers several hiking trails that are suitable for all levels of hikers. The trails offer stunning views of the town and surrounding landscapes.
  • Novo Brdo Lake: The Novo Brdo Lake is a natural attraction that offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and forests.
  • Olympos Mountain: Olympos Mountain is the highest peak in the area, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Novo Brdo

Novo Brdo is a hiker’s paradise, with numerous hiking and biking trails that cater to all levels of hikers. Here are some of the best hiking/biking routes in Novo Brdo:

  • Novo Brdo - Kastriot: This route is a challenging hike that takes you through the town’s historical landmarks and the scenic countryside.
  • Novo Brdo - Kmetovci: This route offers a moderate hike through the town’s outskirts, with stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
  • Novo Brdo - Resorti: This route is a relatively easy hike that takes you through the town’s forests and offers scenic views of the surrounding landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Novo Brdo: Activities and Tours

There are several things to do in Novo Brdo, catering to all interests and age groups. Here are some of the best things to do during your trip to Novo Brdo:

  • Visit the Novo Brdo Fortress and enjoy panoramic views of the town and surroundings.
  • Hike or bike through the town’s scenic countryside and explore the natural beauty of the area.
  • Visit St. Nikola Church and admire the beautiful frescoes and architecture.
  • Take a guided tour of the Mining Museum and learn about the town’s history as a mining town.

Week-Long Itinerary for Novo Brdo

A week-long trip to Novo Brdo will allow you to explore all the must-visit places and popular activities. Here’s a sample itinerary for a week-long trip to Novo Brdo:

Day 1: Arrive in Novo Brdo and explore the town’s historical landmarks, including the Novo Brdo Fortress and St. Nikola Church.

Day 2: Hike the Novo Brdo - Kmetovci route and enjoy the scenic views of the countryside.

Day 3: Take a guided tour of the Mining Museum and learn about the town’s history as a mining town.

Day 4: Relax by the Novo Brdo Lake and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Day 5: Explore the Olympos Mountain and enjoy the panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.

Day 6: Take a day trip to Pristina, the capital city of Kosovo, and explore its cultural attractions and shopping scene.

Day 7: Depart from Novo Brdo and return home.

Food and Drink Scene of Novo Brdo

Novo Brdo offers a variety of local dishes and beverages that you should try during your trip. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kukes: A traditional Albanian dish made from goat meat and served with peppers, onions, and tomatoes.
  • Flia: A layered pastry made from cornmeal, flour, and water, served with meat or cheese.
  • Sarma: Cabbage rolls filled with minced meat and rice, cooked in a tomato-based sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Rakija: A strong alcoholic beverage made from distilled fruits, usually plums or grapes.
  • Boza: A sweet, fermented beverage made from wheat, corn, or barley.

Where to Stay in Novo Brdo: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Novo Brdo offers a variety of accommodations for different budgets and preferences. Here are some recommendations:

  • Guesthouse Hajra: This guesthouse offers comfortable rooms, a restaurant, and a terrace with panoramic views of the town and surrounding landscapes.
  • Hotel Fusha e Gjes: This hotel offers modern rooms, a restaurant, and free parking.
  • Hostel Hava: This hostel offers budget-friendly dormitory rooms and free Wi-Fi.

What to Buy in Novo Brdo: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Novo Brdo offers several local products and souvenirs that make for great gifts or tokens. Here are some recommendations:

  • Handmade traditional jewelry
  • Locally-made honey and jams
  • Handwoven carpets and rugs
  • Traditional Albanian clothing

Local Culture and Customs of Novo Brdo

Novo Brdo is a predominantly Albanian town, with a rich cultural heritage. It is polite to greet locals with a “Përshëndetje” or “Hello” and respect their customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Novo Brdo

The best time to visit Novo Brdo is during the summer months, from June to September. During this time, the weather is mild and conducive for outdoor activities. However, if you prefer cooler temperatures, spring and autumn are also good times to visit.

Do’s and Don’ts of Novo Brdo

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
  • Try the local delicacies and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural surroundings.
  • Don’t take photographs of locals without their permission.
  • Don’t use offensive language or gestures.

Safety and Health

Safety in Novo Brdo

Novo Brdo is a safe town, with a low crime rate. However, it is advisable to take precautions to ensure your safety, such as not travelling alone at night, avoiding secluded areas, and keeping an eye on your belongings.

Health in Novo Brdo

Novo Brdo has a basic healthcare system, but for serious medical issues, it is advisable to seek treatment in Pristina. Make sure you have adequate insurance that covers medical emergencies during your trip.

Getting to Novo Brdo: Transportation Options and Logistics

Novo Brdo is accessible by various transportation modes, including:

Airports Near Novo Brdo

The closest airport to Novo Brdo is Pristina International Airport, located approximately 50 km away.

Bus Stations Near Novo Brdo

There is a regular bus service that connects Novo Brdo to Pristina and other major towns in Kosovo.

Other Options

You can also hire a taxi or car rental from Pristina to Novo Brdo.

How to Get Around in Novo Brdo

Novo Brdo is a small town, and most of the attractions are within walking distance. However, hiring a car or taxi is recommended for exploring the nearby areas and hiking trails.

Where to Go Next from Novo Brdo: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Novo Brdo is a great base for exploring other nearby destinations, including:

  • Pristina: The capital city of Kosovo offers cultural attractions, shopping, and nightlife.
  • Gadime Cave: A natural attraction located approximately 20 km away, featuring stunning underground formations.

Conclusion

Novo Brdo is a hidden gem that offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has inspired you to plan a trip to this beautiful town and explore all it has to offer.
