A Comprehensive Guide to Abbeville, France

Are you looking for a vacation spot in Europe that is rich in history, culture, and nature? Look no further than Abbeville, France. Located within the Hauts-de-France region, this charming town is an ideal destination for travelers who want to experience the best of France.

Abbeville is full of cultural sites, museums, historic places, and natural beauty. From hiking and biking trails to unique local dishes, there is something for everyone in Abbeville.

List of Places to Visit in Abbeville

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Visiting historical sites is one of the main reasons why tourists flock to Abbeville. Some must-visit sites include:

  • Saint Wulfran’s Collegiate Church: This gothic-style church is one of the main attractions in Abbeville. It dates back to the 15th century and is located in the city center.

  • Boucher-de-Perthes Museum: This museum is dedicated to anthropology, prehistory, and arts. It holds a vast collection of artworks and cultural artifacts from around the world.

  • The Beaux-Arts Museum: This museum showcases an extensive collection of modern and contemporary art, fine arts, decorative arts, and archaeology. It is housed in a nostalgic 19th-century building.

Natural Attractions of Abbeville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Abbeville is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The town offers many parks, rivers, and natural areas. Here are some of the best outdoor attractions:

  • Jardins du Château de Bagatelle: These beautifully sculpted gardens are a perfect place for an afternoon stroll. They offer a vast range of beautiful landscapes and scenery, including a lake, a river, and a rose garden.

  • The Somme River Trail: This 74-kilometer-long trail follows the Somme River, an idyllic river that runs through the town. It is an excellent spot for hiking, biking, and bird-watching.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Abbeville

Abbeville is the perfect destination for those who enjoy outdoor activities. There are many hiking and biking routes to explore. Some popular routes include:

  • Somme Bay Nature Reserve: This nature reserve is one of the best places to explore in Abbeville. The reserve offers a range of scenic trails and paths for hikers, bikers, and nature enthusiasts. The reserve offers scenic views of the bay and the surrounding countryside.

  • The Green Trail: This trail offers a 25-kilometer-long route through the town’s rural countryside. The route passes through several quaint villages and offers beautiful views of the surrounding forests and fields.

Best Things to Do in Abbeville: Activities and Tours

Abbeville offers many activities and tours that are ideal for travelers looking for an authentic French experience. Some popular activities include:

  • Fishing: Abbeville is located near several rivers and ponds, making it a great destination for fishing enthusiasts.

  • Traditional Cooking Classes: Visitors interested in French cuisine can take traditional cooking classes in Abbeville. These classes teach visitors how to prepare authentic French dishes using local ingredients.

Week-Long Itinerary for Abbeville

If you’re looking for a week-long itinerary in Abbeville, consider the following:

  • Day 1: Visit the Saint Wulfran’s Collegiate Church and explore the town center.

  • Day 2: Visit the Beaux-Arts Museum and Boucher-de-Perthes Museum.

  • Day 3: Take a bike ride through the Green Trail.

  • Day 4: Visit the Somme Bay Nature Reserve and go hiking.

  • Day 5: Explore the town’s parks and go fishing.

  • Day 6: Take a cooking class and learn to prepare traditional French dishes.

  • Day 7: Visit the Jardins du Château de Bagatelle and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

Food and Drink Scene of Abbeville

Abbeville boasts a vibrant food and drink scene that centers around French cuisine. Some must-try dishes include:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Moules frites: A traditional dish of mussels and fries.

  • Tarte au Maroilles: A savory pie made with Maroilles cheese, a locally-made cheese with a strong flavor.

  • Flamiche: A pastry shell filled with cream, cheese, and vegetables.

Local Beverages

  • Local Beer: Abbeville is home to several breweries that produce delicious local beer.

  • Calvados: A popular apple brandy from Normandy.

  • Champagne: Abbeville is located near the Champagne region of France, making it an ideal destination to try some of France’s finest champagne.

Where to Stay in Abbeville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Abbeville offers several accommodation options for tourists, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Some popular places for tourists include:

  • Château de Noyelles: A 19th-century castle that has been converted into a hotel.

  • Logis Le Saint Louis: A charming hotel located in the town center.

  • La Source Bleue - Esprit de Picardie: A guesthouse located in the countryside, offering beautiful views of the surrounding nature.

What to Buy in Abbeville: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Abbeville is famous for several locally-made products, making it an ideal shopping destination for tourists. Some popular souvenirs include:

  • Maroilles Cheese: This pungent cheese is a must-try when visiting Abbeville.

  • Local Beer: Several breweries in Abbeville produce delicious local beer, making it an ideal destination for beer lovers.

Local Culture and Customs of Abbeville

Abbeville is proud of its rich cultural heritage, and visitors can experience many local customs and traditions during their stay. Visitors should respect the local customs, including:

  • Greeting: It is customary to shake hands when greeting someone in Abbeville.

  • Table Manners: Abbeville has strict table manners. Visitors should always wait for the host to propose a toast before drinking.

  • Language: Although French is the official language, many locals in Abbeville also speak English.

Best Time to Visit Abbeville

The best time to visit Abbeville is during the summer, from June to August. The weather is mild, and there are many outdoor activities to enjoy. The town also hosts several cultural festivals during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Abbeville

Do:

  • Explore the town center: The town center is full of beautiful architecture and history.

  • Try the local cuisine: Abbeville boasts some of the best traditional French cuisine.

  • Respect the local customs: Abbeville has several customs and traditions that visitors should respect.

Don’t:

  • Litter: Abbeville is proud of its clean streets, and visitors should not litter.

  • Be rude: Visitors should respect the locals, and any rude behavior is not tolerated.

  • Overdrink: Abbeville is known for its wine and beer culture. However, visitors should not overindulge.

Safety and Health

Safety in Abbeville

Abbeville is a very safe town with a low crime rate. Visitors should always be aware of their surroundings and take appropriate safety precautions.

Health in Abbeville

Abbeville has several medical facilities and pharmacies. Visitors should ensure they have adequate travel insurance and carry any necessary medications with them.

Getting to Abbeville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Abbeville is well-connected to nearby towns and cities. Some of the best transportation options include:

Airports Near Abbeville

  • Paris-Orly Airport: Located about 163 kilometers away from Abbeville.

  • Charles de Gaulle Airport: Located about 177 kilometers away from Abbeville.

Bus Stations Near Abbeville

  • Abbeville Bus Station: Located in the city center.

Other Options

  • Car rental: Visitors can rent cars at most airports and train stations.

How to Get Around in Abbeville

Abbeville is a small town, and most attractions are within walking distance. Visitors can also rent bikes, use public transportation, or take taxis.

Where to Go Next from Abbeville: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Abbeville is an ideal base for exploring nearby towns and cities. Some popular excursions include:

  • The Battlefields of the Somme: The Somme region is home to several significant World War I battlefields and war memorials.

  • Amiens: This charming town is a short train ride away from Abbeville and is home to the largest gothic cathedral in France.

Conclusion

Abbeville is an excellent vacation destination for travelers who love history, culture, and nature. With so much to see and do, visitors can create their ideal itinerary from the suggestions in this comprehensive guide. From hiking and biking to cultural tours and trying delicious cuisine, Abbeville has it all.
