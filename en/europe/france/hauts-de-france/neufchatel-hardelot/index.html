List of Places to Visit in Neufchâtel-Hardelot

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Neufchâtel-Hardelot is steeped in history and culture, making it a great destination for history buffs. Here are some must-visit museums and historical places in the area:

  • Château d’Hardelot – This stunning Renaissance-style castle was built in the 16th century and has been beautifully preserved. Visitors can take a tour of the castle and explore the surrounding gardens.
  • Musée Municipal – Located in the heart of Neufchâtel-Hardelot, this museum boasts a collection of local art and artifacts that reflect the history and culture of the town and its people.
  • Église Saint-Pierre – This beautiful church dates back to the 11th century and features a stunning neo-gothic interior that is definitely worth a visit.

Natural Attractions of Neufchâtel-Hardelot: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Neufchâtel-Hardelot is surrounded by breathtaking natural landscapes that are perfect for outdoor adventures and peaceful strolls. Here are some of the best natural attractions in the area:

  • Les Dunes de la Slack – This stunning nature reserve features towering dunes, meandering streams, and diverse bird species. Visitors can hike, bike, or even horseback ride through this beautiful landscape.
  • Le Parc des Jardins de Valloires – This lush, 8-hectare botanical garden is home to over 5,000 plant species from around the world. Visitors can explore themed gardens, a maze, and a beautiful water garden.
  • La Forêt Domaniale d’Hardelot – This beautiful forest boasts an extensive network of hiking and biking trails, making it the perfect place to explore the natural beauty of Neufchâtel-Hardelot.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Neufchâtel-Hardelot

If you’re looking for a more active way to explore the great outdoors of Neufchâtel-Hardelot, then hitting the hiking and biking trails is the perfect solution. Some of the best routes in the area include:

  • Sentier des Dunes – This easy 8-km coastal hiking trail takes you through the heart of the stunning dunes of Slack.
  • Les Garennes – This intermediate-level 17-km biking route takes you through the lush forests of Hardelot and offers beautiful views of the sea and countryside.
  • Grande Randonnée 120 – This difficult 137-km hiking trail takes you through some of the most stunning landscapes in the area, including forests, dunes, and cliffs.

Best Things to Do in Neufchâtel-Hardelot: Activities and Tours

There’s no shortage of exciting and enriching activities to partake in while in Neufchâtel-Hardelot. Here are some of the best tours and activities that you shouldn’t miss:

  • Watersports – From kayaking and paddleboarding to windsurfing and kitesurfing, Neufchâtel-Hardelot offers an array of aquatic adventures for all skill levels.
  • Golfing – The town has several excellent golf courses for visitors to choose from, each with its own unique charm and challenges.
  • Horse Racing at Hippodrome de la Canche – If you’re a fan of horse racing, then a visit to the Hippodrome de la Canche is a must. This top-class racetrack hosts several races and events throughout the year.

Food and Drink Scene of Neufchâtel-Hardelot

No trip to France is complete without indulging in its rich culinary culture. Neufchâtel-Hardelot offers a range of delicious food and drink options, including:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Carbonade Flamande – A hearty beef and beer stew that is a staple of northern French cuisine.
  • Andouillette – A savory sausage made with pork and chitterlings that is popular in the region.
  • Tarte à la Maroilles – A delectable cheese tart made with the local Maroilles cheese.

Local Beverages

  • Bières des 2 caps – A locally-brewed beer that is testimony to the region’s rich brewing heritage.
  • Benedictine – This warmly spiced liqueur is made from a secret recipe that dates back to the 16th century.
  • Cidre Hauts-de-France – A refreshing apple cider that is produced in local orchards.
