Introduction to Pleumeur-Bodou

Pleumeur-Bodou is a charming commune and popular tourist destination located in the department of Côtes-d’Armor in Brittany, France. The region is well-known for its rich history, culture, and breathtaking landscapes. In this travel guide, we will take you on an exciting journey through the best things to do, see, and eat in Pleumeur-Bodou.

List of Places to Visit in Pleumeur-Bodou

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Planetarium de Bretagne - Explore the wonders of space and science in this fascinating planetarium.
  • Centre de Découverte du Son - Discover the magic of sound in this interactive museum.
  • Château de Kergrist - Visit this 15th-century castle and immerse yourself in the rich history of the region.

Natural Attractions of Pleumeur-Bodou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Sentier des Douaniers - Take a long hike and witness the breathtaking views of the Brittany coastline.
  • Golf de Saint-Samson - Play a round of golf with a stunning view of the sea.
  • Côte de Granit Rose - The unique pink-granite coast is a must-see for any tourist visiting the region.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pleumeur-Bodou

For outdoor enthusiasts, Pleumeur-Bodou offers a range of hiking and biking trails that provide some of the most stunning views in the region. Whether it’s the breathtaking coastline or the lush green forests, these trails offer something for every type of adventurer.

Best Things to Do in Pleumeur-Bodou: Activities and Tours

  • Kayaking in the Bréhat Channel - Get up close and personal with the natural beauty of the Channel.
  • Horseback Riding - Explore the lush green forests while riding a horse.
  • Wine Tasting - Taste the local wine and learn about its history in this unique vineyard tour.

Food and Drink Scene of Pleumeur-Bodou

Must-Try Dishes

  • Crêpes - Thin, pancake-like, delicious delicacies that come in both sweet and savory forms.
  • Kouign-Amann - A sweet, buttery pastry that is highly popular in Brittany and is perfect for breakfast or a snack.
  • Seafood - Try the range of locally sourced seafood, including crab, lobster, and oysters.

Local Beverages

  • Cider - Cider is a popular beverage in Brittany, made from locally grown apples.
  • Chouchen - A mead-like beverage made from fermented honey.

Where to Stay in Pleumeur-Bodou: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There is no shortage of accommodation options in Pleumeur-Bodou, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Some popular places to stay include the Hotel Les Costans, the Camping Armor Loisirs, and the Château de Kergrist.

What to Buy in Pleumeur-Bodou: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handcrafted Glassware - The historic town of Pleumeur-Bodou is known to produce some beautiful handcrafted glassware.
  • Brittany Biscuits - Try the traditional buttery biscuits of the region.
  • Breton Striped Shirts - Take home an iconic Breton striped shirt as a souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Pleumeur-Bodou

The town of Pleumeur-Bodou is known for its rich traditional culture, including its historic churches, festive music, and sea-inspired customs. The locals take great pride in their traditions and are welcoming to visitors who embrace them.

Best Time to Visit Pleumeur-Bodou

The best time to visit Pleumeur-Bodou is from May to September when the weather is warm and ideal for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pleumeur-Bodou

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local foods and drinks.
  • Explore the natural beauty of the region.

Don’t:

  • Speak loudly or be rude in public places.
  • Over-tip in restaurants.

Safety and Health

Safety in Pleumeur-Bodou

The town is generally safe and crime is not a significant concern. Visitors should still take normal precautions, such as locking doors and keeping valuables secure.

Health in Pleumeur-Bodou

There are no significant health concerns in Pleumeur-Bodou beyond those typically associated with travel, such as sunburn and dehydration. Visitors should ensure they have travel insurance and be aware of the standard travel advisories.

Getting to Pleumeur-Bodou: Transportation Options and Logistics

Visitors can arrive at Pleumeur-Bodou via airports, buses, and trains.

Airports Near Pleumeur-Bodou

The nearest airports are Lannion Airport and Rennes Airport.

Bus Stations Near Pleumeur-Bodou

Pleumeur-Bodou is well-connected by bus, with several bus stations. The most commonly used bus station is Pleumeur-Bodou Gare.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel by train or drive to Pleumeur-Bodou. The town has a train station and is well connected to the rest of France.

How to Get Around in Pleumeur-Bodou

Public transportation is easily accessible in Pleumeur-Bodou, with local buses, taxis, and rental cars available for visitors.

Where to Go Next from Pleumeur-Bodou: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Several nearby destinations offer visitors an opportunity to explore more of Brittany, including:

  • Lannion - A historic town located just 15 minutes away from Pleumeur-Bodou.
  • Perros-Guirec - A beautiful coastal town that is a 30-minute drive from Pleumeur-Bodou.

Conclusion

Pleumeur-Bodou is one of the most appealing tourist destinations in Brittany, with abundant natural beauty and cultural attractions. With this guide, you can explore the town with the confidence and knowledge of a local, and enjoy all it has to offer.
