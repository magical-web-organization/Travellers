Introduction to Bidart

Bidart is a charming town in Pyrénées-Atlantiques, in the Nouvelle-Aquitaine region of France. Its strategic location between the Pyrenees mountains and the Atlantic Ocean makes it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The town is blessed with beautiful beaches, lush green hills, and a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its architecture, art, and cuisine. Bidart is also a great place for surfing, hiking, biking, golf, and several other outdoor activities. This guide will help you explore the best that Bidart has to offer, from its stunning natural beauty to its delightful cuisine, and everything in between.

List of Places to Visit in Bidart

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Château d’Ilbarritz: This elegant chateau dating back to the 19th century was designed by a famous French architect, Joseph-Armand Berthelin. It is located on a hill overlooking the sea and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding area. The chateau is surrounded by lush gardens and has been converted into a hotel, but visitors are welcome to explore the grounds.
  • Church of Saint Joseph: This beautiful church is located in the heart of Bidart and is known for its distinctive red and white bell tower. The church was built in the 16th century and has a rich history that has been preserved over the centuries.
  • Museum of Chocolate: This small museum located in the heart of Bidart is dedicated to the history and production of chocolate in France. Visitors can learn about the process of making chocolate and taste some of the finest chocolate in the world.

Natural Attractions of Bidart: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Parc d’Arradoy: This beautiful park covers over 21 hectares and is home to several species of native flora and fauna. The park also has several walking trails, picnic areas, and a playground for children.
  • Plage d’Ilbarritz: This stunning beach is located in an isolated cove and is surrounded by high cliffs on either side. The beach is popular among surfers and is considered one of the best surfing spots in Europe.
  • Sentier du Littoral: This coastal trail offers breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean and the rugged coastline of Bidart. The trail runs for several kilometers and is perfect for hiking enthusiasts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bidart

  • La Rhune: This hiking trail offers stunning views of the Pyrenees mountains and the sea. The trail is steep and challenging, but the views at the top are worth the effort.
  • Véloroute des Pyrénées: This biking route stretches over 800 kilometers through the Pyrenees mountains and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The route passes through several towns and villages, including Bidart.

Best Things to Do in Bidart: Activities and Tours

  • Surfing Lessons: Bidart is one of the best surfing spots in Europe, and several surf schools offer lessons and equipment rentals for beginners and experienced surfers alike.
  • Golfing: Bidart is home to several championship golf courses, including Golf de Biarritz le Phare and Golf d’Ilbarritz.
  • Wine Tasting: The Bordeaux region is just a short drive away from Bidart, and several wine tours are available for those who want to sample some of the best wines in the world.

Food and Drink Scene of Bidart

Must-Try Dishes

  • Piperade: This delicious dish is made with tomatoes, peppers, and onions and is typically served with eggs and ham.
  • Basque Cake: This sweet cake is made with almonds and has a caramelized crust.
  • Gateau Basque: This pastry is filled with custard or cherry jam and is a popular dessert in the region.

Local Beverages

  • Irouléguy Wine: This red wine is produced in the Basque region and is known for its rich flavor and aroma.
  • Patxaran: This liqueur is made from sloe berries and has a sweet and smooth taste.

Where to Stay in Bidart: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Château d’Ilbarritz: This elegant chateau has been converted into a hotel and offers luxurious accommodations with breathtaking views of the sea.
  • Domaine de Bassilour: This charming guesthouse is located in a quiet village just outside of Bidart and offers comfortable rooms and delicious breakfast.
  • Hotel Olatua: This boutique hotel is located in the heart of Bidart and offers stylish accommodations with modern amenities.

What to Buy in Bidart: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Espelette Pepper: This hot pepper is grown in the Basque region and is a staple in Basque cuisine.
  • Basque Beret: This traditional hat is a symbol of Basque culture and is popular among locals and tourists alike.
  • Basque Linens: These colorful tablecloths and napkins are made from high-quality fabrics and are a great souvenir to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Bidart

Bidart has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its architecture, art, and cuisine. The town is known for its traditional Basque architecture, which features white walls and red or green shutters. The Basque culture is also represented in the local cuisine, which includes dishes such as Piperade and Basque Cake. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and visitors are encouraged to engage with them and learn more about their culture and customs.

Best Time to Visit Bidart

The best time to visit Bidart is from June to September when the weather is warm and sunny. This is also the peak tourist season, so expect higher prices and larger crowds. Spring and fall are also good times to visit, as the weather is mild, and the crowds are smaller.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bidart

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine, including Piperade and Basque Cake.
  • Do engage with the locals and learn more about their culture and customs.
  • Do take surfing lessons and explore the beautiful beaches of Bidart.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural environment.
  • Don’t disrespect the locals or their culture.
  • Don’t leave valuables unattended on the beach or in public places.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bidart

Bidart is a safe town, and visitors are unlikely to encounter any major safety issues. However, it is always advisable to take precautions and avoid walking alone at night, especially in unlit areas. Visitors should also be careful when swimming in the sea and should be aware of rip currents and other dangers.

Health in Bidart

France has excellent healthcare facilities, and visitors to Bidart can be assured of high-quality medical care if needed. Visitors should ensure that they have comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies.

Getting to Bidart: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bidart is accessible by road, rail, and air.

Airports Near Bidart

The nearest airport to Bidart is Biarritz Pays Basque Airport, which is located just 10 kilometers away. The airport offers flights to several domestic and international destinations, including Paris and London.

Bus Stations Near Bidart

The bus station in Bidart is located in the town center and offers regular services to Biarritz, Bayonne, and other nearby towns.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Bidart by train, with services running to several nearby cities, including Bayonne and Biarritz.

How to Get Around in Bidart

The best way to get around Bidart is by car, as it offers the most flexibility and allows visitors to explore the surrounding area at their own pace. Car rental services are available at Biarritz Pays Basque Airport. Buses also offer a convenient and affordable way to get around Bidart and the surrounding towns, with services running regularly throughout the day.

Where to Go Next from Bidart: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Biarritz: This fashionable seaside town is located just a few kilometers from Bidart and is known for its stunning beaches, luxurious hotels, and excellent restaurants.
  • San Sebastian: This beautiful Spanish town is just a short drive from Bidart and is known for its beautiful architecture, vibrant nightlife, and delicious food.
  • Bayonne: This historic town is famous for its chocolate and its picturesque old town, which is filled with charming boutiques and cafes.

Conclusion

Bidart is a hidden gem on the French coast that offers something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, Bidart has it all. This comprehensive guide will help you plan your trip and make the most of your time in this stunning town. From its beautiful beaches to its rich cultural heritage, Bidart is a destination that you won’t want to miss.
