Introduction to Thouars

Thouars is a charming town located in Deux-Sèvres, Nouvelle-Aquitaine, in France, in Europe. This hidden gem is nestled in the heart of the Loire Valley, known for its picturesque landscapes, historic architecture, and world-renowned vineyards.

Thouars dates back to medieval times and has a rich cultural heritage that has been preserved throughout history. Visitors to this town can enjoy exploring the winding streets, quaint shops, stunning gardens, and museums.

List of Places to Visit in Thouars

Thouars offers numerous tourist attractions that cater to different interests and preferences. Below are some of the must-see places that should be added to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Château des Ducs de la Tremoille - This historical castle originally built in the 13th century is now a museum of art and history, featuring exhibitions that showcase the rich heritage of Thouars.
  • Thouarsais Museum - This museum offers a fascinating glimpse into the town’s past, with exhibitions on local archaeology, folk art, and ancient crafts.
  • Church of Saint Laon - This stunning Gothic church, located in the town’s center, is known for its beautiful stained-glass windows and unique architectural features.

Natural Attractions of Thouars: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Parc de la Garenne - This gorgeous park located next to the Thouaret River offers an ideal setting for picnicking, hiking, and biking. Visitors here can enjoy a peaceful walk through the park’s wooded paths and enhancing river views.
  • Les Jardins du Château de Saint-Loup-Lamaire - The castle’s gardens boast some of France’s most stunning water lilies, ponds, and exotic plants, making it an ideal place to unwind and reflect.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Thouars

Thouars offers a plethora of options for outdoor enthusiasts, ranging from riverside cycling to leisurely strolls through its idyllic countryside. Some of the most popular hiking and biking routes include:

  • The Thouaret River Trail - This gentle trail takes visitors through the heart of Thouars, past historic buildings and picturesque landscapes.
  • The Loire Valley Cycling Route - This scenic trail runs along the Loire River, offering breathtaking views of the region’s vineyards, chateaus, and forests.

Best Things to Do in Thouars: Activities and Tours

Whether you are a solo traveler, a family with children, or a couple looking for a romantic getaway, Thouars has something to offer everyone. Here are some of the best activities and tours to do while in Thouars:

  • Wine Tasting in the Loire Valley - The region is known worldwide for its vineyards and wineries.
  • Hot Air Ballooning Over the Countryside - Experience a unique perspective of the region with a hot air balloon tour over the green fields and hills.
  • Visit Local Markets and Art Galleries - Explore Thouars’s lively outdoor markets and artisan shops, such as la Halle, and take home handcrafted souvenirs.

Food and Drink Scene of Thouars

Thouars offers visitors a delectable array of local cuisines that reflect the region’s rich culinary heritage. Below are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Moules-frites - Mussels served with fries are a popular local dish.
  • Galette des Rois - This traditional cake made of puff pastry and almond cream is popular around the holiday season.
  • Rillons - Pork belly stewed in fat and served with bread or mashed potatoes.

Local Beverages

  • Loire Valley Wines - The region’s prime export, Loire Valley wines, boast of the highest quality and unique taste.
  • Apple Cider - A regional favorite, apple cider is a favorite among visitors who enjoy mildly alcoholic drinks.
  • Poire - A pear-flavored drink that’s typically served chilled.

Where to Stay in Thouars: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Thouars offers a wide range of accommodation options ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious private villas. Some of the recommended options include:

  • Château de la Mothe Chandeniers - This stunning castle converted into a luxury guest house is a must-visit for travelers looking for a unique, upscale stay.
  • Hôtel de la Gare - For a budget-friendly stay, this hotel offers a friendly atmosphere and comfortable rooms within walking distance of the train station.

What to Buy in Thouars: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Thouars’s artisan shops offer various souvenirs, ranging from handmade crafts and artwork to locally produced wine. Below are some of the must-buy souvenirs when in Thouars.

  • Tapestries and Embroidery - Thouars has a rich history of textile artisans, and visitors here can take home beautifully crafted tapestries and embroidery.
  • Nougat - This traditional French sweet is made with almonds, honey, and sugar and is a popular snack to take back home.
  • Loire Castles Wines - Loire Vineyards produce some of France’s finest wines that make an excellent gift or souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Thouars

Thouars has a rich cultural heritage that reflects in its architecture, cuisine, art, and lifestyle. Thouars residents are known for their warmth, hospitality, and pride in their town’s cultural history.

Best Time to Visit Thouars

Thouars offers a pleasant atmosphere throughout the year, with each season offering unique experiences. The best times to visit depend on personal preference, as spring brings flowers and ideal hiking temperatures, summers offer a host of outdoor activities, and autumn is the ideal time to enjoy the fall foliage colors. Winter is relatively colder but ideal for indoor activities such as wine tasting, art exhibits, etc.

  • Be mindful of your surroundings and stay safe while exploring Thouars.

Safety and Health

Safety in Thouars

Thouars is generally a safe city to visit, with low crime rates. Visitors should take standard precautions to ensure their safety, such as avoiding poorly lit areas at night and keeping an eye on their belongings at all times.

Health in Thouars

Thouars has numerous health facilities, such as hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies. Visitors from Europe carrying their EHIC card are eligible for emergency healthcare free of charge. It is advisable to carry travel insurance to cover any unforeseen circumstances.

Getting to Thouars: Transportation Options and Logistics

Thouars is well-connected by air, road, and rail to other parts of France and Europe.

Airports Near Thouars

The closest airports to Thouars are Nantes-Atlantique and Poitiers-Biard.

Bus Stations Near Thouars

There are several bus stations in Thouars, connecting the town to other nearby regions.

Other Options

Thouars can be easily accessed using a rental car or bike, making it an ideal way to explore the surrounding countryside.

How to Get Around in Thouars

Thouars is an easily walkable town. Alternatively, visitors can use the local bus or taxi services.

Where to Go Next from Thouars: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Thouars’s ideal location provides easy access to numerous nearby cities and towns that offer unique experiences and attractions. Some noteworthy nearby destinations are:

  • Saumur - Famous for its equestrian heritage and stunning chateau.
  • Chinon - World-renowned for its wine and picturesque setting

Conclusion

Thouars is a charming town that offers visitors an authentic French experience, with a range of cultural, historical and natural attractions to explore. From its remarkable cuisine to scenic landscapes, the town’s culture and hospitality make it an ideal destination for anyone looking to explore France’s rich heritage.
