Introduction to Sarlat-la-Canéda

Nestled in the heart of Dordogne, Sarlat-la-Canéda is a quaint and charming town that boasts of cobbled streets, medieval architecture, and an impressive food scene. This picturesque town is a popular destination for tourists seeking history, culture, and flavor.

In this travel guide, we’ll explore everything you need to know about Sarlat-la-Canéda, from the best places to visit, activities to do, and where to stay and eat.

List of Places to Visit in Sarlat-la-Canéda

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

1. Sarlat Cathedral

The Cathédrale Saint-Sacerdos is a Gothic cathedral built-in 1317 and is situated in the heart of the town.

2. La Roque-Gageac

A short drive from Sarlat, La Roque-Gageac is a picturesque village located along the Dordogne river. Head to its imposing 14th-century château, now owned by the Menthon family, or simply stroll the charming streets.

3. Chateau de Castelnaud

This medieval castle, located just outside of Sarlat, is a must-visit for history buffs. The castle displays a collection of medieval warfare and weaponry, and there are various exhibits on castles and their role in medieval society.

Natural Attractions of Sarlat-la-Canéda: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

1. Les Jardins de Marqueyssac

Sarlat-la-Canéda is surrounded by beautiful gardens, but none quite compare to Les Jardins de Marqueyssac. Designed in the 17th century, the gardens are famed for their hundreds of manicured boxwood trees, and visitors can enjoy a breathtaking view of the town’s roofs from the Belvedere overlooking the Dordogne Valley.

2. Dordogne River

The Dordogne River is a great place to kayak, canoe or paddleboard. Here you can explore caves, ruined castles, and forests, all from the safety of your boat.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sarlat-la-Canéda

There are several excellent hiking and biking tracks around Sarlat la Canéda. The Roque-Gageac bike route or the Four Castles hike are fantastic ways to see the town and surroundings. Explore the Dordogne Valley, following trails along the river, and taking in the picturesque countryside.

Best Things to Do in Sarlat-la-Canéda: Activities and Tours

1. Sarlat Food Walking Tour

An excellent place to start your journey is the Sarlat Food Walking Tour, where a local guide takes you through charming side streets to discover local cheeses, foie gras, paté, and wines.

2. Visit the Sarlat Market

Every Wednesday and Saturday, the town springs to life with a bustling market. Take in the sights, sounds, and smells of a traditional French market, and sample the local delicacies.

3. A Hot Air Balloon Ride

Sarlat-la-Canéda’s beautiful countryside can be seen from the sky. Take a hot air balloon ride and enjoy a bird’s eye view of the Dordogne Valley and the picturesque town.

Food and Drink Scene of Sarlat-la-Canéda

Must-Try Dishes

There are several local dishes to try while visiting Sarlat-la-Canéda. These include:

1. Truffles

The town is renowned for its Périgord truffles - the black variety and the summer truffle. They can be found in various restaurants and local delicatessens and markets.

2. Duck Confit

Duck confit is a staple in French cuisine and Sarlat-la-Canéda. The local restaurants serve it traditionally, with crispy skin and served with potatoes and girolles mushrooms.

3. Foie Gras

Foie gras is another local favorite and commonly eaten as an appetizer. It’s often served on brioche bread and sprinkled with salt and spices.

Local Beverages

Wine is the drink of choice in the Dordogne region. Two wines worth trying are:

1. Bergerac Wine

Bergerac is one of the most famous wines produced in the region. With 13 approved grape varieties, the quality and flavor differ depending on the chateau and terroir where it was produced.

2. Monbazillac Wine

Monbazillac is another local favorite and has been produced in the region since the 16th century. It’s characterized by its gold color and sweet flavor, often served as an aperitif but also pairs well with desserts.

Where to Stay in Sarlat-la-Canéda: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There is accommodation to suit any budget or preference in Sarlat-la-Canéda.

1. Luxury: Plaza Madeleine Hotel

This elegant hotel is located in the heart of the town. It is the perfect place to relax, unwind, and enjoy luxurious comfort.

2. Mid-Range: Hotel Bon Encontre

The Hotel Bon Encontre offers comfortable rooms and a swimming pool. It’s situated just outside of the town, offering serene surroundings and views of the luscious landscape.

3. Budget: Le Jardins du Barry

This hostel-style accommodation offers a friendly atmosphere and is strategically located right in Sarlat town. Rooms are small but cozy and clean, perfect for budget travelers who are comfortable sharing a room and bathroom.

What to Buy in Sarlat-la-Canéda: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Sarlat-la-Canéda offers several local products worth taking home with you. These include:

1. Foie Gras

Foie gras is a popular souvenir, and several local canneries or markets sell the product in jars, making it easier to transport.

2. Truffles

Sarlat-la-Canéda is renowned for truffles, and you can take home a small jar of truffle oil or truffle mustard to add to your pantry.

3. Handmade ceramics

In Sarlat-la-Canéda, you can find several artisan shops stocked with handmade ceramics, vases, and plates.

Local Culture and Customs of Sarlat-la-Canéda

Sarlat-la-Canéda is a part of the Dordogne region, which has a deep cultural history. The people are friendly and welcoming, and the local culture is deeply intertwined with the art of good food.

Best Time to Visit Sarlat-la-Canéda

The best time to visit Sarlat-la-Canéda is in the summer, between May and September, when it’s warm and sunny, and the town is buzzing with life. That said, visiting in the autumn can be colorful as the leaves of the trees change and the temperature cools, adding a different kind of charm to the town.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sarlat-la-Canéda

Sarlat-la-Canéda is a relatively safe town. Visitors should, however, always exercise caution by being aware of their surroundings and taking general safety measures like locking up valuables and keeping them out of sight, especially in crowded areas and public transport.

Health in Sarlat-la-Canéda

There are several hospitals in the region, including the Hospital Center of Sarlat. Visitors should take out comprehensive medical insurance before their travels and note that not all medical costs are free in France.

Getting to Sarlat-la-Canéda: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sarlat-la-Canéda

The closest international airport to Sarlat-la-Canéda is the Bergerac Dordogne Périgord Airport. It is 66 km to Sarlat.

The airport has car rental services. Alternatively, you can take a taxi from the terminal to the train station and continue your journey from there.

Bus Stations Near Sarlat-la-Canéda

Sarlat-la-Canéda has a bus station located in the town center. From here, you can catch a bus to neighboring towns, with direct routes to Bergerac, Cahors, and Perigueux.

Other Options

Train Station: The nearest station is Sarlat-la-Canéda Station, just a short walk from the town center.

Taxi Service: Taxi service is available throughout the town, with competitive pricing.

How to Get Around in Sarlat-la-Canéda

Sarlat-la-Canéda is a small town, and the best way to explore it is on foot. Most of the town’s streets are pedestrian-friendly, and the architecture of the buildings creates an idyllic environment for a leisurely stroll.

Where to Go Next from Sarlat-la-Canéda: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Dordogne is filled with other exciting towns worth visiting:

  • Rocamadour, one of the most famous pilgrimage destinations in France.
  • The medieval castle of Beynac-et-Cazenac, the nearby town of Domme.
  • The wine country of Bergerac, where you can enjoy wine tasting and vineyard visits.

Conclusion

Sarlat-la-Canéda offers visitors a chance to immerse themselves in French culture and history and provides an opportunity to taste various local specialties and excellent wine. With this ultimate travel guide, you now have all the information you need to plan an unforgettable trip to Sarlat-la-Canéda.
