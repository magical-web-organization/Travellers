Introduction to Divonne-les-Bains

Located in Greater Lyon, in Auvergne-Rhône-Alpes, in France, in Europe, Divonne-les-Bains is a charming town known for its thermal baths, scenic outdoor adventures, and culturally important landmarks. The town is located close to the Swiss border and is known for its stunning natural beauty. If you are looking for a place to relax and unwind, Divonne-les-Bains is the perfect destination for you.

List of Places to Visit in Divonne-les-Bains

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Divonne-les-Bains is home to several museums and cultural landmarks, including:

  • Château de Divonne: Built-in 1765, the Chateau de Divonne is a stunning historic landmark that served as the residence of many notable figures throughout its history. Today, it is open to the public and serves as a hub of cultural activities throughout the year.

  • Musée de la vigne et du vin: The Musée de la vigne et du vin is a museum dedicated to the art of winemaking and vineyards. Here, you can explore the history of winemaking in the region and learn about the techniques used to make some of the world’s finest wines.

Natural Attractions of Divonne-les-Bains: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Divonne-les-Bains is surrounded by natural beauty, and there are several outdoor adventures that are worth exploring, including:

  • Lac de Divonne: This beautiful lake is located at the heart of the town and offers a variety of outdoor activities, including swimming, boating, fishing, and more. The lake is surrounded by stunning scenery, making it the perfect spot for a picnic or a day of relaxation.

  • Jura Mountains: The Jura Mountains are a spectacular range that offers breathtaking views and outdoor adventure opportunities. You can hike, bike, or even go on a scenic car drive through the mountains, taking in the beautiful scenery all around.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Divonne-les-Bains

Divonne-les-Bains is known for its scenic hiking and biking trails, including:

  • GR59: The GR59 trail is a long-distance hiking trail that passes through Divonne-les-Bains. The trail offers stunning views of the Jura Mountains and the surrounding countryside.

  • VTT Jura Balcon: The VTT Jura Balcon is a mountain biking trail that offers spectacular views of the Jura Mountains and the surrounding region. The trail is suitable for both experienced and beginner mountain bikers.

Best Things to Do in Divonne-les-Bains: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of activities and tours to keep you busy in Divonne-les-Bains, including:

  • Thermal Baths: Divonne-les-Bains is famous for its thermal baths, which are said to have healing properties. There are several spas and thermal baths in the town where you can relax and soak in the therapeutic waters.

  • Wine Tasting Tours: The region surrounding Divonne-les-Bains is home to some of the world’s best vineyards and wineries. Taking a wine tasting tour is a great way to explore the area and learn about the history of winemaking in the region.

Week-Long Itinerary for Divonne-les-Bains

If you are planning a week-long trip to Divonne-les-Bains, here is a suggested itinerary:

Day 1: Visit the Château de Divonne and explore the town center.

Day 2: Visit the Musée de la vigne et du vin and take a wine tasting tour of the surrounding vineyards.

Day 3: Visit Lac de Divonne and spend the day relaxing by the lake.

Day 4: Take a scenic drive through the Jura Mountains and explore the small towns along the way.

Day 5: spend the day at the thermal baths and indulge in a spa treatment or two.

Day 6: Take a guided hiking or biking tour through the surrounding countryside.

Day 7: Explore the local food scene and try some of the region’s most famous dishes.

Food and Drink Scene of Divonne-les-Bains

Must-Try Dishes

Divonne-les-Bains is known for its delicious food, and there are several dishes that you simply must try, including:

  • Fondues: Fondue is a Swiss dish that is very popular in the region surrounding Divonne-les-Bains. The dish consists of melted cheese that is served with bread or potatoes.

  • Tartiflette: Tartiflette is a hearty dish made with potatoes, cheese, and bacon. It is perfect for a cold winter day.

Local Beverages

Divonne-les-Bains is home to some excellent local beverages, including:

  • Cider: The region surrounding Divonne-les-Bains is known for its delicious cider, which is made with the apples that are abundant in the area.
