Introduction to Disneyland Paris

Disneyland Paris, formerly known as Euro Disney Resort, is located in Marne-la-Vallée, east of Paris, France. It is one of Disney’s most popular theme parks outside of the United States, attracting approximately 15 million visitors each year. It is the perfect destination for families with children of all ages and for all those who love Disney magic. In this comprehensive guide, we will walk you through all the must-visit places, important cultural attractions, hiking trails, things to do, local food and drinks, where to stay, local culture, and customs, and much more.

Best Time to Visit Disneyland Paris

The best time to visit Disneyland Paris is from May to September, when the weather is pleasant, and there are fewer crowds. However, the park welcomes visitors all year round.

Do’s and Don’ts of Disneyland Paris

Do:

  • Respect French culture and customs
  • Wear comfortable shoes
  • Wear sunscreen
  • Use the FASTPASS System
  • Enjoy your time in the parks

Don’t:

  • Take flash photography on rides
  • Smoke inside the park
  • Cut in line
  • Bring outside food or drinks
  • Bring bulky backpacks or suitcases

Safety and Health

Safety in Disneyland Paris

Disneyland Paris is a safe place, and park management ensures visitors’ safety. Here are some safety guidelines to follow:

  • Follow the park’s rules and regulations
  • Keep an eye on your belongings
  • Do not run inside the park
  • Stay hydrated during hot weather

Health in Disneyland Paris

Disneyland Paris offers first aid stations and a pharmacy inside the park. Here are some health guidelines to follow:

  • Wear appropriate clothing for the weather
  • Avoid high-fat meals
  • Drink plenty of water
  • Take regular breaks
