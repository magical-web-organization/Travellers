Located in the heart of Île-de-France, France, Bobigny is a charming town that offers a perfect blend of history, culture, outdoor adventure, and delicious food. This guide will show you everything you need to know to make the most of your trip to Bobigny.

Introduction to Bobigny

Bobigny is a small town in the northeastern suburbs of Paris. It is known for its rich history, beautiful scenery, and delicious food. Despite being a lesser-known destination, Bobigny is a hidden gem that is worth a visit.

List of Places to Visit in Bobigny

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Bobigny has a rich history that is reflected in its many historical sites and museums. Here are some of the must-visit places in Bobigny:

  • Château de Bobigny: This stunning 18th-century castle is a must-see attraction in Bobigny. The castle was once owned by the de Montmorency family, one of the most important noble families in France.

  • Musée de l’Histoire de Bobigny: This museum is dedicated to the history of Bobigny. It showcases the town’s evolution from a small village to a thriving urban center.

  • Les Pavillons-sous-Bois: This picturesque town is located just a short distance from Bobigny. It is known for its beautiful architecture and charming atmosphere.

Natural Attractions of Bobigny: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Bobigny is surrounded by beautiful countryside and natural landscapes. Here are some of the best outdoor attractions to explore:

  • La Corniche des Forts: This scenic highway offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside and the city of Paris.

  • Le Parc de la Poudrerie: This expansive park is home to a wide range of flora and fauna. It is the perfect place to go for a picnic or a relaxing walk.

  • Canal de l’Ourcq: This beautiful canal stretches over 100 km and features scenic bike paths, walking trails, and picnic areas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bobigny

Bobigny is home to a variety of scenic hiking and biking routes. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • Le Canal de l’Ourcq: This scenic route follows the canal and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

  • Le Sentier des roses: This hiking trail takes you through a stunning rose garden and offers beautiful views of the countryside.

  • Le Parc Départemental de la Bergère: This park is home to a variety of hiking trails and is the perfect place to explore the natural beauty of Bobigny.

Best Things to Do in Bobigny: Activities and Tours

Bobigny offers a variety of activities and tours that are sure to keep you entertained during your visit. Here are some of the best things to do in Bobigny:

  • Take a historical walking tour: Learn about the rich history of Bobigny and explore the town’s most important historical landmarks.

  • Visit a local vineyard: Bobigny is home to several vineyards that offer tours and tastings.

  • Explore the local food scene: Bobigny is known for its delicious cuisine. Visit a local market or restaurant to try some of the town’s most famous dishes.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bobigny

Day 1: Explore the town center and visit the Château de Bobigny.

Day 2: Take a scenic bike ride along the Canal de l’Ourcq.

Day 3: Visit the Musée de l’Histoire de Bobigny and learn about the town’s history.

Day 4: Explore the natural beauty of Bobigny by hiking in Le Parc de la Poudrerie.

Day 5: Take a day trip to nearby Les Pavillons-sous-Bois.

Day 6: Visit a local vineyard and enjoy a wine tasting.

Day 7: Sample some of the delicious local cuisine at a local restaurant or market.

  • Boeuf bourguignon: This hearty beef stew is a classic French dish that is sure to warm you up on a chilly day.

  • Croissants: You can’t go to France without trying a croissant. Bobigny has many bakeries that offer fresh, delicious croissants.

Local Beverages
