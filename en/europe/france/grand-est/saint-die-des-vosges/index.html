Introduction

Saint-Dié-des-Vosges is a charming town located in the Grand Est region of France. Known for its stunning natural beauty and rich history, travelers can enjoy a wide range of outdoor activities, cultural experiences, and local cuisine. This guide will provide all the essential information you need to plan your trip to Saint-Dié-des-Vosges, including the best places to visit, where to stay, what to eat, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Saint-Dié-des-Vosges is home to several museums and historical landmarks that offer insight into the town’s cultural heritage. The Musée Pierre-Noël is a popular museum that showcases art and history of the region. The Chapelle des Cordeliers, a 14th-century Gothic chapel, is another must-visit site. You should also add the Saint-Dié Cathedral, a stunning 16th-century Gothic church, which is among the town’s most impressive landmarks.

Natural Attractions of Saint-Dié-des-Vosges: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Saint-Dié-des-Vosges is surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes, offering visitors plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures. One of the most popular activities is hiking or cycling the Vosges Mountains. Nature lovers can enjoy scenic views while exploring the Forêt Domaniale de la Petite-Pierre, a beautiful forest in the nearby area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

There are plenty of hiking and biking routes in and around Saint-Dié-des-Vosges. The Route des Crêtes is a scenic drive through the Vosges Mountains, offering stunning views of the surrounding area. Another popular route is the Col de Sainte-Marie, which is a challenging ride that rewards visitors with great views of the mountains and the town.

Best Things to Do in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of things to do in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges, from exploring the outdoors to experiencing the local culture. You can take a guided cultural tour of the town, visit a local winery, or take a cooking class to learn how to make local dishes. Adventure seekers can also try paragliding or rafting on the Vosges Mountains, or go skiing at the nearby ski resort.

Week-Long Itinerary for Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

If you’re planning to spend a week in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges, here is a suggested itinerary:

Day 1: Arrive in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges, take a tour of the town and visit the Musée Pierre-Noël. Day 2: Hike or cycle the Vosges Mountains. Day 3: Visit the Chapelle des Cordeliers, the Saint-Dié Cathedral, and other historical landmarks in the town. Day 4: Explore the Forêt Domaniale de la Petite-Pierre, and enjoy the region’s natural beauty. Day 5: Take a cultural tour of the town, or visit a local winery. Day 6: Try paragliding or rafting on the Vosges Mountains, or go skiing at a nearby ski resort. Day 7: Depart Saint-Dié-des-Vosges.

Food and Drink Scene of Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

Must-Try Dishes

Saint-Dié-des-Vosges is known for its delicious local cuisine, which features hearty dishes made with fresh, locally sourced ingredients. Some of the must-try dishes include the Baeckeoffe, a meat and vegetable casserole, and the Quiche Lorraine, a savory tart made with bacon and cheese. For dessert, try the Tarte aux Mirabelles, a tart made with local mirabelle plums.

Local Beverages

The region is also known for its wine and beer. One of the most popular wines in the area is the Alsace Pinot Gris, which pairs well with local dishes. Beer lovers can try the local lager, Kronenbourg 1664.

Where to Stay in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Saint-Dié-des-Vosges offers a wide range of accommodation options for travelers of all budgets. Popular choices include Hotel Beauséjour, Hotel Ibis Budget, and Le Clos Des Hirondelles, among others. There are also several hostels and guesthouses in the town, offering affordable options for backpackers and budget travelers.

What to Buy in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to Saint-Dié-des-Vosges can take home a variety of souvenirs and local products. Some top choices include local honey, pottery, and woodcarvings. You can also purchase some wine or cheese to bring home as a delicious souvenir from your trip.

Local Culture and Customs of Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

Saint-Dié-des-Vosges has a rich cultural heritage that visitors can experience through its museums, art galleries, and festivals. The town is also known for its love of cheese and wine, and local custom requires that you never cut the cheese - always break it!

Best Time to Visit Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

Saint-Dié-des-Vosges is a year-round destination, with something to offer visitors in every season. The summer months (June to August) are ideal for outdoor activities, while the winter months (December to February) are perfect for skiing and snowboarding.

Do’s and Don’ts of Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

  • Visit the museums and historical landmarks
  • Explore the natural beauty of the region

Don’t:

  • Fail to reserve a hotel room in advance during peak season (summer and winter)

Safety and Health

Safety in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

Saint-Dié-des-Vosges is a safe destination for travelers. However, visitors are reminded to take the usual precautions, such as being aware of pickpockets in crowded areas.

Health in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

There are no specific health risks in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges, but it’s always recommended to check with your doctor before traveling and to have adequate travel insurance.

Getting to Saint-Dié-des-Vosges: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

The nearest airport to Saint-Dié-des-Vosges is Strasbourg Airport, located about 65 miles away. Other nearby airports include EuroAirport Basel-Mulhouse-Freiburg and Lyon-Saint Exupéry Airport.

Bus Stations Near Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

The town has a train station that connects it to major cities in the region. Several bus companies also offer services to Saint-Dié-des-Vosges from nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

Driving to Saint-Dié-des-Vosges is also an option, as there are several car rental companies in the area. However, visitors should be aware that parking can be limited in the town center.

How to Get Around in Saint-Dié-des-Vosges

The best way to get around town is by foot, as most of the attractions are within walking distance. Cycling is another popular option, and the town has plenty of bike rental services.

Where to Go Next from Saint-Dié-des-Vosges: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Saint-Dié-des-Vosges is located in a stunning region of France, offering plenty of nearby destinations for day trips. You can visit the charming town of Colmar, explore the beautiful Alsace Wine Route, or take a trip to the historic city of Strasbourg.

Saint-Dié-des-Vosges offers visitors a perfect combination of natural beauty, cultural experiences, and local cuisine. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, historical landmarks, or a relaxing getaway, this charming town has something for everyone.
