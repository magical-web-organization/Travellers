Introduction to Vézelay

Vézelay is a charming medieval village situated in the Yonne department of the Bourgogne-Franche-Comté region in France. It is best known for its beautiful hilltop basilica, Sainte-Marie-Madeleine, which has been declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Vézelay also has a rich history, and throughout the centuries, it has been a hub for travelers and pilgrims traveling to Santiago de Compostela in Spain.

List of Places to Visit in Vézelay

Vézelay offers an array of attractions for visitors to explore. From culturally significant historical sites to natural wonders, this town has something for everyone. Here are some of the must-visit places in Vézelay.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Basilica of Sainte-Marie-Madeleine

The most famous attraction in Vézelay is the basilica of Sainte-Marie-Madeleine perched atop a hill overlooking the town. This beautiful Romanesque church dates back to the 11th century and is visited by thousands of pilgrims annually.

Maison Jules Roy

Maison Jules Roy is a museum that is dedicated to the life and work of the French writer, Jules Roy. The museum house now displays a collection of his letters, manuscripts, and documents.

Eglise Saint Etienne

Eglise Saint Etienne is an impressive Gothic church located on the northern end of the village that dates back to the 14th century.

Natural Attractions of Vézelay: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The Cure Valley

Take a stroll through the stunning Cure valley at the foot of Vézelay, which is home to many secluded paths that are perfect for hiking. Here you can enjoy the natural scenery and peaceful ambiance that surrounds Vézelay.

The Houssière Forest

The Houssière forest is located to the west of Vézelay and is a lovely area to hike or bike through. Many trails take you through the thick forest, and the top of Montaigu offers incredible panoramic views of the surroundings.

Best Things to Do in Vézelay: Activities and Tours

Vézelay has unique activities that range from indoor to outdoor, providing something fun and interesting to do for visitors of all ages. Below is a list of some of the best things to do in Vézelay.

Local Culture and Customs of Vézelay

Vézelay is a charming medieval village steeped in history and rich local culture. The locals celebrate many cultural festivals throughout the year, including religious processions and the famous “Fête de la Saint-Vincent Tournante,” which celebrates the local Burgundy wine.

Best Time to Visit Vézelay

The best time to visit Vézelay is during the spring and fall seasons when the weather is mild, and visitors can experience the incredible views of the countryside with fewer tourists. Nevertheless, it’s still a great place to visit year-round, and winter in Vézelay can still provide a lot of excitement.

Do:

  • Try local cuisine and drink

Don’t:

  • Be loud and disrespectful in public areas

Safety and Health

Safety in Vézelay

Vézelay is a safe village, but visitors should still take the usual precautions, such as keeping their belongings close and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Vézelay

Vézelay is a relatively healthy place to visit, and visitors need not take any special health precautions. However, visitors should still keep coronavirus and other diseases in mind.

Getting to Vézelay: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Vézelay

The nearest airports to Vézelay are the Paris airports of Orly and Charles de Gaulle. From there, visitors can take a train, a taxi, or rent a car.

Bus Stations Near Vézelay

Buses run as regular trains, but visitors can also take private bus tours that include Vézelay in the itinerary.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car and drive to Vézelay, which is roughly 200km from Paris.

How to Get Around in Vézelay

Vézelay is a small village, and the best way to get around is by foot or bike. Many of the sites are within walking distance, and the rolling hills provide visitors with scenic paths for biking tours. Taxis and rental cars are also available.

Where to Go Next from Vézelay: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Vézelay is located in Burgundy and is the perfect place for tourists interested in a wine tour of the region. Another nearby town worth visiting is Avallon, which is located approximately 15km from Vézelay and offers visitors exceptional medieval architecture.

Conclusion

Vézelay is a delightful French village that is rich in history and natural scenery. The town offers visitors a charming glimpse into rural French life and an opportunity to explore the beautiful countryside and experience authentic French cuisine and wine. Visit Vézelay for a unique and tranquil escape from the stress of everyday life.
