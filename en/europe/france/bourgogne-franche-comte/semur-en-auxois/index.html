Introduction to Semur-en-Auxois

Semur-en-Auxois is a charming medieval town located in the heart of Burgundy, France. Its historic old town is perched on a hill overlooking the Armançon river, surrounded by lush green hills and vineyards. The town is known for its well-preserved medieval architecture, beautiful gardens, and stunning views. It’s the perfect destination for a relaxing getaway or a romantic weekend trip.

List of Places to Visit in Semur-en-Auxois

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Tower of Semur-en-Auxois: This 13th-century tower is one of the most iconic landmarks in Semur-en-Auxois. Visitors can climb to the top for stunning views of the town and surrounding countryside.

  • Musée Municipal: This museum showcases the history of Semur-en-Auxois and its surrounding region through a collection of art, artifacts, and historical documents.

  • Eglise Notre-Dame: This beautiful church dates back to the 13th century and features stunning stained-glass windows.

Natural Attractions of Semur-en-Auxois: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Canal de Bourgogne: This beautiful canal runs through Semur-en-Auxois and is a popular spot for boat tours, cycling, and hiking.

  • Parc des Douves: This park features beautiful gardens, walking trails, and stunning views of the town and surrounding countryside.

  • Lac de Pont: A peaceful lake located just a short drive from Semur-en-Auxois, where visitors can enjoy fishing, picnicking, and boating.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Semur-en-Auxois

Semur-en-Auxois and the surrounding region offer plenty of hiking and biking trails for outdoor enthusiasts. A popular route is the Canal de Bourgogne Cycling Path, which follows the canal through beautiful countryside and towns.

Best Things to Do in Semur-en-Auxois: Activities and Tours

  • Wine Tasting: Burgundy is known for its exceptional wines, and Semur-en-Auxois is the perfect place to try them. Book a wine tour and taste the region’s best bottles.

  • Hot Air Balloon Ride: Experience the beauty of Semur-en-Auxois and the surrounding region from above with a hot air balloon ride.

  • Semur-en-Auxois Castle: Take a guided tour of the medieval castle and learn about its history and architecture.

Week-Long Itinerary for Semur-en-Auxois

Day 1: Explore the old town of Semur-en-Auxois, including the Tower and Church of Notre-Dame.

Day 2: Take a boat tour of the Canal de Bourgogne and enjoy lunch at one of the canal-side restaurants.

Day 3: Visit the Musée Municipal and the Eglise Notre-Dame.

Day 4: Take a hot air balloon ride over Semur-en-Auxois and the surrounding countryside.

Day 5: Visit a local vineyard for a wine tasting tour.

Day 6: Explore the Parc des Douves and take a hike around the lake.

Day 7: Relax and enjoy a picnic in the beautiful gardens of Semur-en-Auxois.

Food and Drink Scene of Semur-en-Auxois

Must-Try Dishes

  • Beef Bourguignon: One of the most famous dishes in Burgundy, made with beef cooked in red wine and served with vegetables and potatoes.

  • Escargots de Bourgogne: This traditional dish of Burgundy features snails cooked in butter, garlic, and parsley.

  • Coq au Vin: Another classic Burgundian dish, made with chicken cooked in red wine with bacon and mushrooms.

Local Beverages

  • Burgundy Wine: The region is known for its exceptional wines, including Pinot Noir and Chardonnay.

  • Kir: A popular local drink made with white wine and crème de cassis (blackcurrant liqueur).

Where to Stay in Semur-en-Auxois: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Semur-en-Auxois offers plenty of accommodation options to suit every budget and preference. Some popular options include:

  • Hotel de la Côte d’Or: This charming hotel is located in the heart of Semur-en-Auxois and features beautiful views of the town and the Armançon river.

  • La Ferme de la Lochere: This guesthouse is located just a short drive from Semur-en-Auxois and offers beautiful countryside views and a quiet, peaceful atmosphere.

  • Youth Hostel: Located in the heart of Semur-en-Auxois, this hostel offers budget-friendly accommodation for solo travelers and groups.

What to Buy in Semur-en-Auxois: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Wine: Burgundy is known for its exceptional wine, and Semur-en-Auxois offers plenty of opportunities to buy bottles from local vineyards.

  • Mustard: Dijon mustard is a famous local product, and visitors can buy jars of the authentic, high-quality stuff in Semur-en-Auxois.

  • Pottery: The town is home to several pottery shops, where visitors can buy handmade bowls, plates, and other ceramics.

Local Culture and Customs of Semur-en-Auxois

Semur-en-Auxois is a small, historic town with a strong sense of local pride and tradition. Visitors are encouraged to respect the town’s cultural heritage and to participate in local events and festivals.

Best Time to Visit Semur-en-Auxois

The best time to visit Semur-en-Auxois is during the spring and summer months, when the weather is mild and the countryside is in full bloom. Fall is also a beautiful time to visit, as the leaves change colors and the vineyards are busy with harvest season.

  • Participate in local events and festivals

Safety and Health

Safety in Semur-en-Auxois

Semur-en-Auxois is a safe town for visitors, with low levels of crime and a friendly, welcoming atmosphere. However, visitors should still take basic precautions like locking doors and windows and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Semur-en-Auxois

The town has several pharmacies and medical centers in case of emergency. Visitors should make sure they have adequate health insurance before traveling.

Getting to Semur-en-Auxois: Transportation Options and Logistics

Semur-en-Auxois is located in the heart of Burgundy and is easily accessible by car, train, or bus.

Airports Near Semur-en-Auxois

The nearest airports to Semur-en-Auxois are Dijon-Bourgogne Airport (about an hour’s drive) and Lyon Saint-Exupéry Airport (about two hours’ drive).

Bus Stations Near Semur-en-Auxois

Several bus companies operate in Burgundy, with connections to major cities like Dijon and Paris.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Semur-en-Auxois or take a train to the nearby town of Montbard and then take a bus or taxi to Semur-en-Auxois.

How to Get Around in Semur-en-Auxois

The town is small enough to explore on foot, but visitors can also rent bikes or take a taxi to explore the surrounding countryside.

Where to Go Next from Semur-en-Auxois: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Dijon: The capital of Burgundy is famous for its wine, mustard, and historic buildings.

  • Beaune: A charming town in the heart of the region’s vineyards, with a beautiful old town and plenty of wine tasting options.

  • Fontenay Abbey: A UNESCO World Heritage Site located just a short drive from Semur-en-Auxois and known for its beautiful gardens and architecture.

Conclusion

Semur-en-Auxois is a beautiful, historic town that offers visitors a glimpse into the heart of Burgundy. From its stunning medieval architecture to its beautiful countryside and world-class wine, Semur-en-Auxois has something to offer every traveler. Plan your trip today and prepare to be charmed by this hidden gem in the heart of France.
