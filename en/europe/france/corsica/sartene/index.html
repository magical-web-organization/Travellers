Introduction to Sartène

Nestled in the southern part of Corsica, Sartène is a charming hilltop town known for its medieval architecture and rich history. Dubbed as the “most Corsican of Corsican towns,” Sartène is a perfect destination for travellers looking for an authentic Corsican experience. This comprehensive tour guide will give you a rundown of the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more!

List of Places to Visit in Sartène

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Musée de la Corse

Located in the heart of Sartène, Musée de la Corse is a museum that showcases the history and culture of Corsica. The museum features fascinating exhibits such as the “Corsica, land of origins” exhibit, which takes visitors on a journey through the island’s history, culture, natural wonders, and traditions.

Église Santa Maria Assunta

Built in the 16th century, Église Santa Maria Assunta is a beautiful place of worship that boasts an impressive Baroque-style architecture. The church’s interior features intricate decorations and paintings that reflect Corsica’s rich religious traditions and history.

La Tour de Sartène

La Tour de Sartène is a historic tower that dates back to the 16th century. The tower was used as a lookout point to spot approaching invaders during Corsica’s tumultuous history. Today, the tower serves as a fascinating museum that chronicles Corsica’s military history.

Natural Attractions of Sartène: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Roccapina Beach

Located south of Sartène, Roccapina Beach is a stunning crescent-shaped beach that offers impressive views of the surrounding mountains and crystal-clear waters. The beach is an excellent spot for sunbathing, swimming, and snorkelling.

Montagne de Cagna

Rising to over 1,300 metres, Montagne de Cagna offers some of the most breathtaking views in Corsica. The mountain is a popular destination for hiking enthusiasts, with several trails ranging from easy to challenging. The mountain is also an excellent spot for paragliding, offering a bird’s eye view of Sartène and the surrounding area.

Les Aiguilles de Bavella

Les Aiguilles de Bavella is a granite mountain range that features jagged peaks, deep canyons, and lush forests. The range is a popular destination for rock climbing and hiking, offering stunning views of the Corsican landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sartène

Sartène is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, offering several hiking and biking routes that cater to all skill levels. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Mare a Mare Sud
  • Mare E Monti Sud
  • Sentier des Deux Seigneurs
  • Sentier de la Madonetta

Best Things to Do in Sartène: Activities and Tours

Wine Tasting

Corsica is known for its excellent wine, and Sartène is no exception. Visitors can take a tour of the local vineyards and sample some of the region’s finest wines.

Explore the Local Food Scene

Sartène boasts a rich culinary history, and visitors can sample some of the region’s traditional dishes at the local restaurants and cafes. Must-try dishes include the famous Corsican wild boar stew and the local charcuterie.

Take a Boat Tour

Explore the stunning coastline of Corsica on a boat tour. Visitors can take a guided tour or rent a boat and explore at their leisure.

Local Culture and Customs of Sartène

Corsican culture is unique and rich, with a distinct language, traditions, and way of life. Visitors are encouraged to respect the local customs and learn about the Corsican way of life, which includes a deep appreciation for family, food, and nature.

Best Time to Visit Sartène

The best time to visit Sartène is during the summer months of June to August when the weather is warm and sunny. However, visitors should be prepared for the crowds and higher prices during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sartène

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine
  • Respect the local customs and way of life
  • Explore the stunning Corsican landscape

Don’t:

  • Leave trash on the beaches or hiking trails
  • Disrespect the locals or their customs
  • Ignore the natural beauty of Corsica

Safety and Health

Safety in Sartène

Sartène is generally a safe destination for travellers. However, visitors should take the usual precautions and be aware of their surroundings, especially at night.

Health in Sartène

Corsica has a good healthcare system, and visitors can access medical care at the local clinics and hospitals. It’s recommended that visitors bring any necessary medications with them.

Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi to Sartène.

How to Get Around in Sartène

The best way to get around Sartène is on foot, as the town is small and easily walkable. However, visitors can also rent a car to explore the surrounding area.
