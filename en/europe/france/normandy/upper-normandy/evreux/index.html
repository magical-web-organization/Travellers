Introduction to Évreux

Évreux is a picturesque town located in Upper Normandy, in the northwestern region of France. Boasting a rich history, stunning architecture and beautiful natural scenery, Évreux is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Normandy or Europe. Here’s a comprehensive tour guide for Évreux, including everything you need to know to plan your trip.

List of Places to Visit in Évreux

Évreux offers a variety of attractions including museums, historical landmarks, natural attractions and outdoor adventures. Here’s a quick list of places you should visit while in town.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Évreux Cathedral: This stunning cathedral is a Gothic masterpiece and one of the town’s most significant landmarks.
  • Château de Trangis: A fortress originally built in the 11th century and converted into a palace in the 18th century that is now classified as a historical monument.
  • Musée d’Art, Histoire, et Archéologie: A museum that displays Norman art and cultural heritage.
  • The Hôtel de Ville: A stunning piece of architecture and now the town hall of Évreux, originally constructed in the early 16th century.

Natural Attractions of Évreux: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Forest of Évreux: Explore the beautiful and peaceful forests surrounding Évreux and enjoy a variety of outdoor activities including hiking, cycling and picnicking.
  • Lac de Damville: A picturesque lake about thirty minutes from Évreux, catch rentals are available for fishing and pleasure boating.
  • Gardens of Castel Béranger: See the beautiful gardens in front of the Hotel de Ville, take a romantic walk, and lose yourself amidst the natural elements.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Évreux

If you’re looking for an active way to explore the stunning natural scenery of Évreux, try one of the many hiking or biking routes in the area. From easy walks to challenging hikes, here are some route suggestions:

  • Les Bords de I’Iton: This popular hiking route runs along the river Iton and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
  • Le Haras National d’Évreux: An easy bike ride that takes cyclists around the outskirts of Évreux to the National Stud Farm.

Best Things to Do in Évreux: Activities and Tours

In addition to the attractions listed above, there are plenty of other things to do in Évreux. Here are some ideas:

  • Guided Tours: Take a guided tour of Évreux’s historic sites and learn about the town’s fascinating history and culture.
  • Parc animalier Biotropica: A fun day trip for the whole family, Biotropica is a large zoo-like park with a variety of animals and attractions.
  • Le Bowling d’Evreux: Visit the local bowling alley and challenge your group or family to a game.

Week-Long Itinerary for Évreux

If you have a whole week to spend in Évreux, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day Activity
Day 1 Explore the town center of Evreux and visit the famous Évreux Cathedral.
Day 2 Take a guided tour of the Musée d’Art, Histoire et Archéologie.
Day 3 Take a day trip to nearby Château de Trangis.
Day 4 Enjoy the natural beauty of Évreux and take a hike in the nearby Forest of Évreux.
Day 5 Visit the beautiful Gardens of Castel Béranger by the Hôtel de Ville.
Day 6 Take a day trip to Lac de Damville.
Day 7 Relax and unwind in Évreux’s many cafes, restaurants and shops.

Food and Drink Scene of Évreux

No visit to Évreux would be complete without sampling the town’s delicious local cuisine and beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pithiviers de pommes de terre: A traditional Norman dish made with mashed potatoes and meat, typically served with a salad.
  • Tarte Tatin: A popular French dessert, Tarte Tatin is an upside-down apple tart with caramelized sugar and butter.

Local Beverages

  • Cider: A traditional Norman beverage made from fermented apple juice.
  • Calvados: An apple brandy that originated in Normandy, France.

Where to Stay in Évreux: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Évreux offers a variety of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some suggestions:

  • Hôtel Normandy: A charming and cozy hotel located in the heart of Évreux’s historic center.
  • Le Beffroi: A modern hotel in the town center, convenient to shopping and restaurants.
  • Le Petit Verger: A bed and breakfast situated in the beautiful countryside just outside of Évreux.

What to Buy in Évreux: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs to bring home, Évreux has plenty of options. Here are some suggestions:

  • Calvados: A bottle of this delicious apple brandy makes for a great gift or memento.
  • Local jewelry and crafts: Évreux is home to several artisan shops, where visitors can find unique and locally made jewelry and crafts.
  • Products from Biotropica: Pick up a stuffed animal or toy from your visit to the animal park.

Local Culture and Customs of Évreux

French culture is famous all around the world, and Évreux is no exception. Some cultural highlights to note include:

  • Language: French is the official language of Évreux.
  • Manners: Good manners and politeness are highly valued in French culture.
  • Dress code: While there is no strict dress code, it’s always a good idea to dress appropriately for the occasion.

Best Time to Visit Évreux

The best time to visit Évreux is in the summer months from June to August when you can enjoy the sunshine and mild temperatures. Spring and autumn also offer comfortable temperatures but with fewer crowds.

Do’s and Don’ts of Évreux

Here are some helpful do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting Évreux:

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Take a guided tour of the town’s museums and historical landmarks.
  • Try some outdoor adventures.

Don’t:

  • Forget to bring comfortable walking shoes for exploring the town and natural attractions.
  • Eat with your hands, napkins are always served.
  • End your stay in Évreux without seeing the Cathedral.

Safety and Health

Évreux is generally a safe and healthy destination for travelers. Here are some tips to stay safe and healthy while in town:

Safety in Évreux

  • Always be aware of your surroundings and keep an eye on your belongings.
  • Take precautions to avoid pickpocketing in the busy tourist areas.

Health in Évreux

  • Ensure you have all the necessary travel vaccinations before departure.
  • If you require medication, ensure you have a sufficient amount for the duration of your trip.

Getting to Évreux: Transportation Options and Logistics

Évreux is well connected to major cities across France and Europe. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Évreux

  • Beauvais-Tillé Airport: This airport is located 110km away from Évreux, accessible through train services.

Bus Stations Near Évreux

  • Bus Line 390: Connects Évreux with Paris several times daily.

Other Options

  • Train: Évreux has its own railway station, connecting it to major cities in France and across Europe.

How to Get Around in Évreux

The best way to explore Évreux is on foot, as most of the town’s attractions are located within walking distance from each other. There is also a local bus service, should you require it.

Where to Go Next from Évreux: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Évreux is located in the heart of Normandy, making it an ideal base for exploring the region’s other attractions. Here are some destinations to consider visiting:

  • Giverny: The famous Monet’s Garden is located in this town, about an hour’s drive from Évreux.
  • Rouen: A major city in Normandy, approximately 80km from Évreux.
  • Honfleur: A scenic town on the coast of Normandy, approximately 100km from Évreux.

Conclusion

Évreux is a charming and historically rich town, offering plenty of attractions and activities for visitors to enjoy. From exploring the town’s historic landmarks and museums to enjoying outdoor adventures in the beautiful surrounding countryside, there’s something for everyone in Évreux. Plan your trip, follow our guide, and enjoy all that this wonderful destination has to offer.
