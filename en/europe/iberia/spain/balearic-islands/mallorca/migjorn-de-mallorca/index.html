Introduction to Migjorn de Mallorca

Migjorn de Mallorca is a stunning municipality located on the southern coast of Mallorca, the largest island in the Balearic Islands of Spain. Known for its pristine beaches, picturesque landscape, and rich culture, Migjorn de Mallorca attracts tourists from all over the world.

List of Places to Visit in Migjorn de Mallorca

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

One of the main attractions of Migjorn de Mallorca is its rich cultural heritage. There are many museums and historical places to visit in the area, including:

  • Santanyi Municipal Museum: This museum showcases the history and culture of Santanyi and Migjorn de Mallorca through a fascinating collection of art, archaeology, and ethnology.

  • Capocorb Vell: An ancient fortification that dates back to the 1400s and served as a great defence during the wars against the Moors.

  • Iglesia de Nuestra Señora del Rosario: Known for its beautiful architecture, this church is dedicated to the patron saint of Migjorn de Mallorca, the Virgin Mary.

Natural Attractions of Migjorn de Mallorca: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Migjorn de Mallorca is blessed with a perfect blend of natural beauty and adventure. Here are some of the natural attractions and activities to enjoy in Migjorn de Mallorca:

  • Beaches: The municipality has over 20 beaches that are known for their crystal clear waters and white sand. Popular options include Cala Santanyi, Cala Llombards and Calo des Moro.

  • Snorkeling and diving: There are numerous diving and snorkelling sites in Migjorn de Mallorca, including Cala Figuera and Cala Mitjana.

  • Hiking and biking: The area boasts a few fantastic hiking and mountain biking trails, including the ‘Ruta de Pedra en Sec’ nature trail through the Santanyi, into the Alqueria Blanca and Es Llombards in Migjorn de Mallorca.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Migjorn de Mallorca

The hiking and biking trails of Migjorn de Mallorca are a must-do for adventure seekers. Here are a few of the most popular routes:

  • S’Alquería Blanca: This 17.5-kilometre route is perfect for mountain bikers and hikers.

  • Calas de Mallorca: A 25-kilometre trail that takes you along some of the most beautiful beaches and stunning views in Migjorn de Mallorca.

Best Things to Do in Migjorn de Mallorca: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are many more things to do and experience in Migjorn de Mallorca:

  • Boat Tours: See the beautiful beaches and bays of Migjorn de Mallorca from a unique perspective on a boat tour.

  • Horse Riding: Take a horseback ride along the most picturesque areas of the municipality.

Week-Long Itinerary for Migjorn de Mallorca

To experience the best of Migjorn de Mallorca, we recommend the following itinerary:

Day 1: Explore the history and culture of the municipality by visiting the Santanyi Municipal Museum and the Capocorb Vell.

Day 2: Hit the beaches and indulge in water activities like snorkeling or diving.

Day 3: Go on a hiking or biking route through the scenic areas of the municipality.

Day 4: Take a boat tour to see the coastline and the nearby islands.

Day 5: Go horseback riding along the picturesque routes.

Day 6: Check out the local markets and delicacies in the area.

Day 7: Relax by the beach or indulge in any activity that you missed earlier in the week.

Food and Drink Scene of Migjorn de Mallorca

Migjorn de Mallorca is home to some fantastic cuisine and delicacies:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sopas Mallorquinas: A soup made with vegetables, meat, and bread that is common to the Balearic Islands.

  • Tumbet: A delicious and hearty vegetarian dish that includes eggplant, potato, red pepper, tomato, and onion.

Local Beverages

  • Herbes de Mallorca: A traditional liqueur made with anise and other herbs that is commonly enjoyed as a digestif after meals.

Where to Stay in Migjorn de Mallorca: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Migjorn de Mallorca offers plenty of accommodation options for all budgets:

  • Santanyi Hotel: A boutique hotel in the heart of Santanyi offering luxurious rooms and grand amenities.

  • Agroturismo Son Samà: A gorgeous country villa with swimming pool, wide terrace and stunning gardens.

What to Buy in Migjorn de Mallorca: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Some souvenirs and local products to bring home from Migjorn de Mallorca include:

  • Pimentón: Locally made and sweetly smoked paprika powder.

  • Gold and Silver: Mallorca is famous for its jewellery art and expertise, making this a perfect souvenir to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Migjorn de Mallorca

Migjorn de Mallorca is a proud and culturally rich area with a few customs and traditions:

  • Festivals: Migjorn de Mallorca is famous for its annual fiestas which include colourful parades, music and dance.

  • Languages: The native language of Migjorn de Mallorca is Catalan, but Spanish is widely spoken throughout the region.

Best Time to Visit Migjorn de Mallorca

The best time to visit Migjorn de Mallorca is between the months of May and October, when the weather is mild and sunny.

Do’s and Don’ts of Migjorn de Mallorca

Do:

  • Do try the local delicacies like ‘sopas mallorquinas’, ‘tumbet’ and ‘herbes de Mallorca’

  • Do respect the local culture and traditions during fiestas

Don’t:

  • Don’t leave litter on the beaches or in the natural parks

  • Don’t drink and drive or ride any motorized vehicles under the influence of alcohol.

Safety and Health

Safety in Migjorn de Mallorca

Migjorn de Mallorca is generally a safe place to visit, but tourists should exercise caution and keep an eye on their belongings while in crowded areas.

Health in Migjorn de Mallorca

No specific vaccinations are required for visiting Migjorn de Mallorca, but it’s always advisable to take care of individual health before taking any trip.

Getting to Migjorn de Mallorca: Transportation Options and Logistics

The following are various transportation options to help you get to Migjorn de Mallorca:

Airports Near Migjorn de Mallorca

Palma de Mallorca Airport is the closest airport to Migjorn de Mallorca, located only 40 minutes away. There are several flights from different parts of Spain and Europe landing at this airport.

Bus Stations Near Migjorn de Mallorca

There are several bus companies and local stations offering bus and coach services to Migjorn de Mallorca from different parts of Spain and Europe.

Other Options

For land-based travellers, taking a car or taxi from the Palma de Mallorca Airport is a great option, as it allows for easy and independent travel.

How to Get Around in Migjorn de Mallorca

The most common ways to get around Migjorn de Mallorca are by car, bike or on foot. The main roads in the municipality are in good condition and well-marked, and getting around is generally easy.

Where to Go Next from Migjorn de Mallorca: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are many more destinations in the Balearic Islands to explore from Migjorn de Mallorca, such as the island of Ibiza or the beautiful town of Calvia in nearby Mallorca.

Conclusion

If you’re looking for a destination that perfectly blends natural beauty, adventure, and culture, Migjorn de Mallorca is the place to be. This comprehensive travel guide offers all the information and inspiration you need to plan an unforgettable trip to this beautiful corner of Spain.
