Introduction to Novo Sancti Petri

Novo Sancti Petri is a beautiful and peaceful resort town that is located in the province of Cadiz, Andalusia, Spain. It is situated on the shore of the Atlantic Ocean, with a magnificent view of the ocean and the Golden Beach.

This town boasts of some of the best beaches in the region and is also home to 27-hole golf courses, nightclubs, bars, and shops. Novo Sancti Petri is perfect for tourists seeking a relaxed and tranquil holiday spot.

List of Places to Visit in Novo Sancti Petri

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Visitors who want to learn about the rich history of the region or love visiting museums will find plenty of options in Novo Sancti Petri. Below, we’ve outlined some of the most notable historical landmarks and museums worth a visit.

  • Torre del Reloj: Torre del Reloj is a clock tower located in the historic city center.
  • Chiclana Museum: This museum offers a great look at the history of Chiclana, the ancient ruins, and the life in Novo Sancti Petri since the Arab occupation.
  • Convento del Carmen: A Monastery with a Museum, the Convento del Carmen houses paintings, sculptures, textiles, and other religious artifacts.

Natural Attractions of Novo Sancti Petri: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Novo Sancti Petri is located on the coast of Andalusia and has an array of stunning natural attractions, including beaches, forests, and ocean views.

  • La Barrosa Beach: The most popular beach in Novo Sancti Petri, La Barrosa Beach, is stunning and perfect for a relaxing day out.
  • Playa Sancti Petri: Another fantastic beach that is quieter than La Barrosa Beach, providing a more private, tranquil experience.
  • Pinar de la Barrosa: Rare and peculiar pinewood that is home to beautiful birds, and the place where the fishermen of Novo Sancti Petri use to get their wood for the boats.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Novo Sancti Petri

Novo Sancti Petri is famous for its biking trails and hiking routes, specifically the route that lies within the pine woods next to La Barrosa Beach. There are many alternatives, though, that will take you to quiet and secret beaches and hidden spots.

Best Things to Do in Novo Sancti Petri: Activities and Tours

Novo Sancti Petri is home to various activities that are worth experiencing! Take a look at the top experiences:

  • Golfing: With two 18-hole and one 9-hole golf courses, Novo Sancti Petri is a golfer’s paradise.
  • Kitesurfing and Windsurfing: The windy conditions of Novo Sancti Petri beach make it an ideal spot for these water sports.
  • Horse Riding: Explore the area around the resorts on horseback and enjoy the scenery.
  • Wine Tours: Discover the vineyards and taste the best wines of the region.

Week-Long Itinerary for Novo Sancti Petri

When planning a week-long itinerary for Novo Sancti Petri, you should consider exploring the city and the nearby towns, like Jerez de la Frontera and Cadiz.

Day 1: Discover Chiclana Museum and Torre del Reloj.

Day 2: Enjoy La Barrosa Beach and Pinar de la Barossa.

Day 3: Visit Cadiz, a charming historic city that is older than Rome.

Day 4: Relax on the paradisiacal Playa Sancti Petri.

Day 5: Explore Jerez de la Frontera and its sherry wineries.

Day 6: Try horse riding in the resort.

Day 7: Go Golfing in one of the resort’s greens.

Food and Drink Scene of Novo Sancti Petri

Must-Try Dishes

The Andalusian cuisine has different dishes, some of which are unique to this region. When visiting Novo Sancti Petri, you must try:

  • Pescaíto frito: Fried fish that is popular in Andalusia, usually served with potatoes.
  • Paella: Rice dish traditionally prepared with rabbit and chicken.
  • Chipirones: Small squids usually roasted or fried, and served with lemon.

Local Beverages

The Andalusians are wild about their wines, and the region produces the finest Sherry wines worldwide. Only 30 minutes away by train is the famous visitable Jerez winery.

Where to Stay in Novo Sancti Petri: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Novo Sancti Petri provides a variety of choices for accommodation catering to all budgets and preferences.

  • Iberostar Royal Andalus: A high-end luxury resort that offers fantastic amenities.
  • Valentin Sancti Petri Chiclana: A more affordable family-friendly hotel.
  • Vincci Costa Golf Hotel: For those keen on golfing, this hotel is Iberostar’s competition and situated right in front of the golf-course.

What to Buy in Novo Sancti Petri: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When returning home from Novo Sancti Petri, bring back some souvenir items for your loved ones.

  • Sherry Wine: as mentioned previously, the region is world-known for its wines, so don’t forget to bring a bottle as a souvenir.
  • Fan: A simple, but a classic souvenir. Nowadays, they are always elaborately decorated.
  • Olive wood products: You can still find artisans producing unique olive wood handicrafts.

Local Culture and Customs of Novo Sancti Petri

In Novo Sancti Petri, people value traditions and enjoy their laid-back lifestyle. Most of their traditions revolve around religious traditions with week-long festivals and carnivals occurring during different seasons. Of particular note is the Carnaval de Cadiz, which is Spain’s most famous Carnival.

Best Time to Visit Novo Sancti Petri

The best times to visit Novo Sancti Petri are between April to May, and September to October. Both seasons provide moderate to good weather conditions with fewer tourists visiting, making it the perfect time to have a peaceful and enjoyable tourist experience.

Do’s and Don’ts of Novo Sancti Petri

Do:

  • Respect the traditions of the region.
  • Try the local food and drink.
  • Attend any traditional festivals happening during your visit.

Don’t:

  • Be too loud and disrespectful of cultural traditions.
  • Litter the beaches and outdoor areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Novo Sancti Petri

Novo Sancti Petri is generally considered safe with limited criminal activity. Visitors should take precautions when exploring the town past midnight.

Health in Novo Sancti Petri

There is both public and private medical assistance available in the town. Additionally, pharmacies are easily accessible in most areas.

Getting to Novo Sancti Petri: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Novo Sancti Petri

  • Jerez Airport: Only 50km away from Novo Sancti Petri, you can find flights from various airlines, including Ryanair and Iberia.

Bus Stations Near Novo Sancti Petri

  • Chiclana de la Frontera bus station: From here, visitors can reach all major towns and cities in Andalusia by bus.

Other Options

  • Train: The nearest train station is Bahia Sur train station, located 7km from Chiclana de la Frontera.

How to Get Around in Novo Sancti Petri

The best way to get around the region is via private car hire, but taxis and Uber are also available. For those who want to explore on foot, many sites are close enough either to walk or cycle.

Where to Go Next from Novo Sancti Petri: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Cadiz

Located just 22km away from Novo Sancti Petri, Cadiz is an ancient seaside town that is worth exploring for its rich, and cultural landmarks.

Jerez de la Frontera

Located less than 30km away from Novo Sancti Petri, Jerez is a traditional Andalusian town that offers tourist a chance to explore its vineyards and sherry wineries.

Conclusion

Novo Sancti Petri provides a perfect weekend getaway spot for those looking for tranquility away from the hustle and bustle of busy cities. With top-rated hotels, beautiful sceneries, and various activities to explore, Novo Sancti Petri is the perfect spot to unwind and relax. You can create unforgettable memories during your visit by taking time to explore the beautiful beaches and rich cultural history of the region.
