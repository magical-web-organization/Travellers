Introduction to Zarauz

Zarauz is a small and charming coastal town located in Basque Country, in the Northern part of Spain. It is known for its beautiful beaches, outdoor activities, local cuisine, and rich history. The town offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, and it is a great place to explore the natural beauty and traditions of the Basque culture.

List of Places to Visit in Zarauz

Zarauz has plenty of attractions for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the best places to visit in Zarauz:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Jesuit College of Zarauz: A beautiful building built in the early 20th century, which is now a private school. The college has a stunning chapel that visitors can explore.

  • Art and History Museum of Zarauz: This museum houses an impressive collection of local art and artifacts, which tell the story of Zarauz’s history and culture.

  • Church of Santa Maria: This beautiful church was built in the 16th century and has a stunning Gothic architecture. It is a must-visit for anyone interested in religious history and architecture.

Natural Attractions of Zarauz: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Zarauz Beach: The beach is a popular destination for both locals and tourists, and it offers stunning views of the Bay of Biscay. It is perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing.

  • Paseo Maritimo: This promenade is a great place to take a leisurely stroll, enjoy the sea breeze, and take in the beautiful views of the ocean.

  • Mount Talai Mendi: This mountain offers several hiking and biking trails, and it offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Zarauz

Zarauz offers plenty of hiking and biking trails for visitors to explore. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes of Zarauz:

  • Coastal Trail: This trail runs along the coastline from Zarauz to Getaria, and it offers stunning views of the Bay of Biscay.

  • Mount Ernio Trail: This trail offers a challenging hike to the summit of Mount Ernio, where visitors can enjoy the panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

  • Pasaia Bay Trail: This trail runs along the bay and offers stunning views of the ocean and surrounding areas.

Best Things to Do in Zarauz: Activities and Tours

Zarauz has plenty of activities and tours for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the best things to do in Zarauz:

  • Surfing Lessons: Zarauz is one of the best locations for surfing in Spain, and visitors can take surfing lessons from local instructors.

  • Kayaking Tours: Visitors can explore the Bay of Biscay on a kayaking tour, taking in the stunning views and enjoying the peace and tranquility of the sea.

  • Local Food and Wine Tours: Visitors can explore the local cuisine of Zarauz on a food and wine tour, tasting the traditional dishes and wines of the Basque region.

Food and Drink Scene of Zarauz

Zarauz has a vibrant food and drink scene, and there are plenty of local dishes and drinks to try. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pintxos: Pintxos are traditional Basque snacks, usually served with bread, and they come in many varieties, including meat, fish, and vegetarian options.

  • Marmitako: A traditional Basque dish made with tuna, potatoes, onions, and peppers.

  • Txakoli: A local white wine that is light, fruity, and refreshing.

Local Beverages

  • Sidra: A traditional Basque cider that is slightly sour and refreshing.

  • Patxaran: A sweet liqueur made from sloe berries, anise, and vanilla.

Where to Stay in Zarauz: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Zarauz has plenty of accommodation options for visitors, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay in Zarauz:

  • Hotel Zarauz: A luxurious hotel located just a few steps from the beach, offering stunning sea views, a spa, and a restaurant.

  • Camping Zarauz: A budget-friendly option for those who enjoy camping, offering pitches and apartments, located just a 5-minute walk from the beach.

  • Pension Txiki Polit: A cozy guest house located in the old town of Zarauz, offering comfortable rooms and a great breakfast.

What to Buy in Zarauz: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Zarauz has plenty of souvenirs and local products to bring home. Here are some of the best items to buy in Zarauz:

  • Basque beret: A traditional woolen cap that is a symbol of the Basque culture.

  • Txakoli wine: Local white wine that is light and refreshing, perfect for summer days.

  • Pintxos cookbook: A cookbook that features traditional Basque snacks and recipes.

Local Culture and Customs of Zarauz

Zarauz is a town deeply rooted in Basque culture and traditions. Visitors can experience the rich and unique culture of the Basque people, known for their love of music, dance, and local cuisine. The Basque language is still widely spoken in Zarauz, and visitors can hear it as a part of the local culture.

Best Time to Visit Zarauz

The best time to visit Zarauz is during the summer months, from June to September when the weather is warm, and the beaches are perfect for swimming and sunbathing. The town is vibrant and lively during the summer months, and there are plenty of festivals and cultural events to enjoy.

Getting to Zarauz: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Zarauz

The nearest airport to Zarauz is San Sebastian Airport, only 35 kilometers away from the town. Another option is Bilbao Airport located about 100 kilometers away from the town.

Bus Stations Near Zarauz

Zarauz has a bus station, and buses run regularly to San Sebastian and Bilbao.

Other Options

Another option to get to Zarauz is by the train. The Zarauz railway station is located in the town center, and it has regular trains to San Sebastian and Bilbao.

How to Get Around in Zarauz

Visitors can get around Zarauz on foot, bicycle, or city bus.

Where to Go Next from Zarauz: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
