The Ultimate Travel Guide to Roses, Spain

Located in the province of Girona, in Catalonia, in Eastern Spain, Roses is a charming coastal town set against the backdrop of the stunning Costa Brava. With a rich culture, natural beauty, and a host of exciting activities, Roses makes for a perfect destination for travelers of all ages and preferences. Plan your trip to Roses with this comprehensive travel guide featuring must-visit cultural and natural attractions, hiking and biking routes, food and drink scene, where to stay, local culture, dos and don’ts, safety, transportation options, and nearby destinations.

Best Time to Visit Roses

The best time to visit Roses is from May to October when the weather is warm and sunny.

Do’s and Don’ts of Roses

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages
  • Explore the natural beauty of the area
  • Learn about the local history and culture
  • Relax and enjoy the laid-back lifestyle
  • Respect the traditions and customs

Don’t:

  • Sunbathe topless on the public beach
  • Overlook the rich cultural and historical heritage of Roses
  • Ignore the siesta tradition
  • Forget to bring sunscreen and a hat
  • Be rude to locals or disregard their customs

Safety and Health

Safety in Roses

Roses is a very safe place for tourists; however, travelers should take precautions against petty crimes like pickpocketing or theft.

Health in Roses

Make sure to have comprehensive medical insurance when traveling to Spain. The tap water is safe to drink, and there are many pharmacies in and around Roses.
