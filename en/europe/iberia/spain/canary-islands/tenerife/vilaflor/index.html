Vilaflor, a small and picturesque town situated in the southern part of Tenerife, is a hidden gem in the Canary Islands that may not be on many tourists’ radars. However, it offers visitors a chance to discover the rich culture, stunning natural beauty, and exciting adventures that Tenerife has to offer. In this travel guide, we’ll take you on a journey to discover this charming town, its unique culture, and all the must-see attractions.

Introduction to Vilaflor

Vilaflor, the highest village in Spain, is known for its secluded location, historic architecture, and serene ambiance. This idyllic town, situated nearly 15 km from Costa Adeje and just over 20 km from Tenerife South Airport, boasts a population of approximately 1,800 inhabitants and sits at an altitude of 1,400 meters above sea level. It’s a fantastic place to escape from the hustle and bustle of urban life while immersing yourself in the natural beauty and slow-paced lifestyle of this charming town.

Week-Long Itinerary for Vilaflor

Day 1: Visit Vilaflor’s historic sights, including the Church of San Pedro Apostol and Casa del Patio. Day 2-3: Visit the magnificent Teide National Park and take in the stunning views of Mount Teide, Tenerife’s highest peak. Day 4: Explore the unique landscapes of Paisaje Lunar de Vilaflor and enjoy panoramic views across the island. Day 5-6: Discover Vilaflor’s outdoor adventures with hiking, biking, and stargazing tours. Day 7: Experience the local culinary scene through wine tastings and sampling traditional Canarian dishes, such as papas arrugadas and mojo sauce.

Local Culture and Customs of Vilaflor

The local culture of Vilaflor is rich and diverse, with a unique blend of Spanish and Canarian traditions. These include celebrations of Carnival and the Romeria, a traditional festival where locals dress up in stunning traditional Canarian costumes and dance to traditional folk music.

Best Time to Visit Vilaflor

The best time to visit Vilaflor is between April and November, when the weather is typically warm and sunny, and outdoor activities are at their best.

Do’s and Don’ts of Vilaflor

Do:

  • Respect local customs, traditions, and beliefs
  • Try the local cuisine and wines
  • Take advantage of outdoor activities and tours
  • Purchase locally made artisan goods

Don’t:

  • Leave trash behind in public areas or while hiking/biking
  • Drive rashly on winding roads
  • Ignore cultural and religious sensitivities

Safety and Health

Safety in Vilaflor

Vilaflor is a very safe town, with a low crime rate and friendly locals. However, it’s always advised to take necessary precautions while traveling and to keep an eye on your belongings.

Health in Vilaflor

Vilaflor has a well-equipped medical center, and emergency services are easily accessible. However, it’s recommended to take precautions to prevent sunburn, dehydration, and altitude sickness while exploring outdoor activities.

Getting to Vilaflor: Transportation Options and Logistics

Vilaflor is accessible through Tenerife South Airport, located approximately 20 km away. There are various transportation options available, including taxis, buses, and shuttle services.

Airports Near Vilaflor

  • Tenerife South Airport (TFS)

Bus Stations Near Vilaflor

  • Adeje Bus Station

Other Options

  • Taxis and shuttle services from the airport

How to Get Around in Vilaflor

The best way to get around Vilaflor is by car or bike rental services, as public transportation is limited.

Where to Go Next from Vilaflor: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Conclusion

Vilaflor is a truly unique destination that caters to a variety of interests and travel styles. Its stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and leisure activities make it an ideal vacation spot for adventure-seekers and those looking for relaxation alike. Experience the unparalleled beauty and charm of Vilaflor while discovering the unique traditions and customs of this remarkable destination in the heart of Tenerife, Canary Islands.
