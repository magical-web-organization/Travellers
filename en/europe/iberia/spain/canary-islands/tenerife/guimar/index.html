Introduction to Güímar

Nestled in Tenerife’s eastern coast, Güímar is a charming town known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural vistas, and outdoor adventures. This guide will take you on a virtual tour of Güímar’s top attractions and provide you with practical information to help you plan your visit.

List of Places to Visit in Güímar

Here’s a list of must-visit attractions in Güímar:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Pyramids of Güímar Ethnographic Park
  • Church of San Pedro
  • Casa de Los Zamorano

Natural Attractions of Güímar: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Malpais de Güímar Natural Monument
  • Playa del Puertito
  • Barranco de Badajoz

Hiking/Biking Routes of Güímar

Güímar offers a range of hiking and biking trails that are perfect for adventure seekers. Some of the popular routes include:

  • Ruta de los Canales
  • Barranco de Herques
  • Sendero del Agua

Best Things to Do in Güímar: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re traveling solo or with family and friends, Güímar has a variety of activities and tours that cater to all tastes. Here are some of the best things to do in Güímar:

  • Take a boat tour of the nearby islands
  • Go scuba diving in the crystal clear waters
  • Explore the town’s cultural heritage on a walking tour

Food and Drink Scene of Güímar

Canarian cuisine is a delightful fusion of Spanish, African, and Latin American flavors. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in Güímar:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Papas arrugadas: small, salty potatoes, cooked in their skins, served with mojo sauce
  • Sancocho: a fish stew made with salted fish, sweet potatoes, and onions
  • Ropa vieja: a meat stew with chickpeas, potatoes, and vegetables

Local Beverages

  • Ron miel: a smooth honey rum
  • Barraquito: a coffee drink with condensed milk, cinnamon, and liqueur
  • Malvasía: a sweet white wine made from the Malvasía grape

Where to Stay in Güímar: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Güímar offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets. Here are some of the best places to stay in Güímar:

  • Hotel Rural Finca Salamanca: a charming rural hotel with a pool and great views
  • Casa Los Geranios: a cozy guesthouse with a garden and terrace
  • Hostal Carel: a centrally located hostel with clean and comfortable rooms

  • Aloe vera skincare and beauty products

Local Culture and Customs of Güímar

Güímar is a town with a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its customs and traditions. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and visitors are encouraged to immerse themselves in the town’s laid-back way of life.

Best Time to Visit Güímar

Güímar is a great destination to visit year-round, thanks to its mild climate and sunny weather. However, the best time to visit Güímar is during the spring and fall seasons, when the weather is ideal for outdoor activities, and the crowds are less.

Do’s and Don’ts of Güímar

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Try some local dishes and beverages
  • Take care of the natural environment

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural environment
  • Disrespect the locals or their traditions
  • Ignore the safety precautions and guidelines

Safety and Health

Safety in Güímar

Güímar is a safe town, with low crime rates and friendly locals. However, visitors are advised to take common-sense precautions, such as keeping an eye on their belongings and avoiding dark and isolated areas at night.

Health in Güímar

Güímar has a modern healthcare system that provides high-quality medical services to locals and visitors. Travelers are advised to carry their medication and take necessary precautions to protect themselves from the sun and heat.

Getting to Güímar: Transportation Options and Logistics

Güímar is easily accessible by air, road, and sea. Here are some of the transportation options and logistics to consider:

Airports Near Güímar

The nearest airport to Güímar is Tenerife South Airport (Reina Sofia), which is approximately 28 km away. Other airports in the area include Tenerife North Airport (Los Rodeos) and Gran Canaria Airport.

Bus Stations Near Güímar

Güímar has a central bus station with regular services to nearby towns and cities. There are also plenty of taxis and car rental services available.

Other Options

Another popular way to get to Güímar is by ferry from the nearby islands of La Palma, El Hierro, and La Gomera.

How to Get Around in Güímar

Güímar is a small town that is best explored on foot or by bike. Alternatively, visitors can rent a car or take public transportation to reach nearby destinations.

Where to Go Next from Güímar: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Güímar is a great base to explore other nearby destinations and excursions, such as:

  • The Anaga Rural Park
  • Teide National Park
  • The capital city of Santa Cruz de Tenerife

Conclusion

Güímar is a charming and peaceful town that offers a perfect blend of culture, nature, and adventure. Whether you come for the history, the scenery, or the outdoor activities, Güímar is sure to leave you with unforgettable memories and experiences.
