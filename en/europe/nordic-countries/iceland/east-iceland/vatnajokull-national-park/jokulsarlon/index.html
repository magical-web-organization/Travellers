Introduction to Jökulsárlón

Jökulsárlón is a glacial lake located in Vatnajökull National Park on the southeastern coast of Iceland. The lake is fed by Breiðamerkurjökull glacier and is one of Iceland’s most popular tourist destinations.

Jökulsárlón is known for its stunning natural beauty, including crystal-clear blue water, floating icebergs, and breathtaking glacial landscapes. The area also boasts an array of cultural and historical attractions that are worth exploring.

Visitors to Jökulsárlón can enjoy a range of outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, and boat tours. The area is also known for its local cuisine, and visitors can try traditional dishes made from locally-sourced ingredients.

List of Places to Visit in Jökulsárlón

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Vatnajökull Glacier Visitor Center: Learn about Iceland’s glaciers and the effects of climate change on the environment.
  • Höfn Museum: Discover the history and culture of Höfn, a fishing village near Jökulsárlón.
  • Jökulsárlón Glacier Lagoon Exhibition: Explore the science and history of the glacier lagoon.
  • Skálafell Ski Area: Visit this historic ski resort and take in the stunning views of the surrounding glaciers.

Natural Attractions of Jökulsárlón: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Breiðamerkurjökull Glacier: Take a guided glacier hike to visit the impressive glacial landscapes.
  • Diamond Beach: Admire the sparkling black sand beach and stunning icebergs.
  • Kálffell Mountain: Hike up this 959-meter tall mountain for stunning views of the Vatnajökull glacier.
  • Ingólfshöfði Cape: Take a boat tour to visit this isolated cape, home to a variety of seabirds.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jökulsárlón

  • Skaftafell Glacier: Take a guided hike to explore the stunning glacial landscapes of this natural park.
  • Jökulsárgljúfur: Hike through the gorge to discover stunning waterfalls and geological wonders.
  • Kvíar Trail: Explore the beautiful terrain surrounding Jökulsárlón with this scenic bike trail.

Best Things to Do in Jökulsárlón: Activities and Tours

  • Boat Tours: Take a guided boat tour to experience the breathtaking beauty of the glacier lagoon and the surrounding landscapes.
  • Northern Lights Tours: Experience the natural phenomenon of the aurora borealis in the dark skies above Jökulsárlón.

Food and Drink Scene of Jökulsárlón

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lamb: Sample the delicious Icelandic lamb, which is known for its unique flavor and texture.
  • Fish: Try the locally-caught seafood, including smoked salmon and arctic char.
  • Skyr: Enjoy this traditional Icelandic yogurt, which is high in protein and low in fat.

Local Beverages

  • Brennivín: Try Iceland’s signature spirit, a potato and caraway-based schnapps that is often served with pickled herring.
  • Icelandic Beer: Sample locally-brewed beer, including Einstök and Viking beer.
