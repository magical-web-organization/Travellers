Introduction to Eyrarbakki

Nestled on the South Coast of Iceland, Eyrarbakki is a small town that transports you back in time. The town boasts of a rich cultural history, with its traditional Icelandic houses and museums. A perfect escape from the hustle-bustle of city life, Eyrarbakki offers scenic beauty and a plethora of outdoor activities. Our travel guide covers the best places to visit in Eyrarbakki, where to stay, what to eat, and other useful tips for your trip.

Local Culture and Customs of Eyrarbakki

Icelanders value nature and have great respect for their environment. It is also important to Icelanders to maintain their cultural heritage and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Eyrarbakki

The best time to visit Eyrarbakki is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is mild and outdoor activities are plenty. However, winter months from December to February offer opportunities for viewing the Northern lights and less crowds.

Do’s and Don’ts of Eyrarbakki

  • Try the local cuisine

Don’t:

Safety and Health

Safety in Eyrarbakki

Iceland is one of the safest countries in the world. However, it is important to follow basic safety guidelines while traveling, like wearing proper attire while exploring the outdoors.

Health in Eyrarbakki

Iceland has an efficient healthcare system with good facilities. The water is safe to drink and the air quality is excellent.

Getting to Eyrarbakki: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Eyrarbakki

The nearest airport to Eyrarbakki is Keflavik International Airport, which is located 100 km away.

Bus Stations Near Eyrarbakki

The town of Selfoss, 10 km away from Eyrarbakki, is the closest bus station to the town.

Other Options

Renting a car is a popular mode of transportation in Iceland. Alternatively, you can book a transfer service or take a taxi to reach Eyrarbakki.

How to Get Around in Eyrarbakki

Walking is the best way to explore the town of Eyrarbakki. Visitors can rent a car to explore the surrounding areas and book tours for outdoor activities.
