Introduction to Posio

Located in Finnish Lapland, Posio offers visitors a stunning natural landscape, rich cultural heritage, and outdoor adventure opportunities year-round. With its rugged wilderness, untouched forests, crystal-clear waters, and vivid Northern Lights, Posio is a destination that attracts nature lovers, hikers, photographers, and adventurous travelers from around the world. In this travel guide, we will explore the best places to visit in Posio, things to do, local customs, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in Posio

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Pentik Manor

Pentik Manor is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in Finnish history and culture. The manor dates back to the 19th century and is surrounded by beautiful gardens and grounds. The manor now functions as a museum and art exhibition space, showcasing local artistic and cultural heritage.

Motti Museum

The Motti Museum is another great place to learn about Finnish history, particularly the Lapland War of 1944-45. The museum features a variety of artifacts, photographs, and documents related to the war.

Natural Attractions of Posio: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Riisitunturi National Park

Riisitunturi National Park is a hiker’s paradise, with many well-marked trails of varying difficulty levels. The park is famous for its unique forests, with trees growing sideways because of the harsh Arctic wind. During the winter, visitors can also try snowshoeing or cross-country skiing in the park.

Korouoma Canyon

The Korouoma Canyon is a spectacular natural wonder, featuring steep canyons, frozen waterfalls, and stunning views. Visitors can hike around the canyon or try ice climbing during the winter months.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Posio

There are many hiking and biking routes in Posio, catering to different fitness levels and preferences. Some of the popular ones include the Posio Fell Lapland Nature trail, the Riisitunturi Fell trail, and the Korouoma nature trail.

Best Things to Do in Posio: Activities and Tours

Northern Lights Hunting

The Northern Lights are a major attraction in Posio, with visitors coming from all over the world to witness this natural wonder. There are many local tour operators that offer Northern Lights hunting expeditions, with knowledgeable guides who can help you maximize your chances of seeing this elusive phenomenon.

Husky Safari

Dog sledding is another popular activity in Posio, allowing visitors to experience the Arctic wilderness on a thrilling ride. You can opt for a short ride or a longer safari, with breaks to enjoy hot drinks and snacks by the fire.

Week-Long Itinerary for Posio

Day 1: Arrive in Posio and check in to your accommodation. Take a relaxing walk around the town, and try some local cuisine for dinner.

Day 2: Visit the Pentik Manor and learn about the rich culture and history of Posio. In the afternoon, hike the Posio Fell Lapland trail for breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

Day 3: Take a guided tour of the Korouoma Canyon and enjoy the stunning scenery. Afterwards, try ice climbing in the canyon.

Day 4: Try cross-country skiing or snowshoeing in the Riisitunturi National Park. In the evening, take a Northern Lights hunting expedition with a local guide.

Day 5: Visit the charming village of Näkkälä, located in the heart of Sami reindeer herding country. Learn about the Sami culture and enjoy a reindeer sled ride.

Day 6: Take a full-day husky safari, enjoying the thrill of dog sledding through the winter landscape. Have dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 7: Relax and unwind at your accommodation, or explore the town further. Depart Posio in the afternoon.

Food and Drink Scene of Posio

Must-Try Dishes

Sauteed Reindeer

Reindeer is a staple meat in Lapland, and sauteed reindeer is a delicious and traditional way to enjoy it. The meat is usually served with mashed potatoes and lingonberry jam.

Kalakukko

Kalakukko is a savory Finnish pastry, filled with fish and baked in rye crust. It’s a hearty and satisfying dish that’s perfect for a cold winter day.

Local Beverages

Sahti

Sahti is a traditional Finnish beer, brewed with malted barley, rye, and juniper berries. It has a distinctive flavor and aroma, and is a perfect accompaniment to hearty Finnish cuisine.

Cloudberry Wine

The cloudberry is a rare and exotic berry that grows in the Arctic regions of Lapland. It’s used to make a delicious, tangy wine that’s perfect for sipping on a chilly evening.

Where to Stay in Posio: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Posio offers a range of accommodation options, from cozy guesthouses to modern hotels. Some of the top options include the Lapland Hotel Bear’s Lodge, the Hotel Rinnetupa, and the Hostel Nisunen.

What to Buy in Posio: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Handmade Ceramics

Posio is known for its beautiful artisanal ceramics, especially those made by Pentik. You can buy handmade mugs, bowls, and plates that are both functional and beautiful.

Sami Handicrafts

The Sami people are an indigenous group in Lapland, known for their traditional crafts and textiles. You can buy Sami handicrafts, such as reindeer leather bags and bracelets, as souvenirs to take home.

Local Culture and Customs of Posio

The Sami culture is an integral part of life in Posio, as it is in much of Lapland. Visitors should be respectful of Sami traditions and customs, which include reindeer herding, storytelling, and traditional crafts. Remember to ask before taking photographs of Sami people or their belongings.

Best Time to Visit Posio

Posio is a beautiful destination year-round, but the best time to visit depends on your interests. Winter is perfect for activities like skiing, snowshoeing, and Northern Lights hunting. In summer, visitors can enjoy hiking, fishing, and boating on the lakes. Fall is a great time to see the autumn colors, while spring offers a chance to see the migrating birds and the emergence of new growth.

Do’s and Don’ts of Posio

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Dress warmly, regardless of the season
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages
  • Take a guided tour to learn about the natural and cultural history of Posio
  • Bring your camera and capture the stunning landscapes

Don’t:

  • Disturb the local wildlife
  • Litter or damage the natural environment
  • Ignore safety warnings or guidelines
  • Take photographs of Sami people or their belongings without permission
  • Wander off-trail in the wilderness areas

Safety and Health

Safety in Posio

Posio is a safe destination, but visitors should be aware of the potential risks associated with outdoor activities. Always follow safety instructions from guides and tour operators. In winter, be prepared for cold temperatures and pack appropriate clothing and equipment.

Health in Posio

The health care system in Finland is excellent, and there are medical facilities available in Posio. Visitors should make sure they have adequate travel insurance and be mindful of any pre-existing conditions.

Getting to Posio: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Posio

The nearest airport to Posio is the Rovaniemi Airport, located approximately 120 kilometers away. From Rovaniemi, visitors can travel to Posio by car, bus, or train. Other airports in the region include the Kuusamo airport and the Oulu airport.

Bus Stations Near Posio

There are several bus stations near Posio, including ones in Kuusamo, Rovaniemi, and Oulu. From these stations, visitors can take a local bus or taxi to Posio.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Posio, either by renting a car or by using their own vehicle. The roads in Lapland are well-maintained, but visitors should be aware of the potential hazards associated with driving in a remote region.

How to Get Around in Posio

The best way to get around Posio is by car or bicycle, especially if you want to explore the surrounding wilderness areas. There are also taxi services available, as well as local buses that connect the town with nearby destinations.

Where to Go Next from Posio: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Nearby destinations that are worth exploring include Rovaniemi, Oulu, and Kuusamo. Visitors can also try a variety of outdoor excursions, such as snowmobiling, ice fishing, and reindeer sledding.

Conclusion

Posio is a unique and beautiful travel destination that offers something for everyone, from outdoor adventures to cultural experiences. Whether you want to hike through the wilderness, witness the Northern Lights, or immerse yourself in Finnish culture and history, Posio is the perfect place to do it. We hope this travel guide has provided you with the information you need to plan your dream trip to Posio.
