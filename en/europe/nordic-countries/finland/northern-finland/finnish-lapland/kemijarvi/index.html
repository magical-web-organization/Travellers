Introduction to Kemijärvi

Kemijärvi is a small town located in northern Finland, in the Lapland region, known for its beautiful scenery, rich cultural heritage, and outdoor adventures. Kemijärvi is surrounded by forests, lakes, and hills, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. With its unique blend of Finnish and Sámi culture, Kemijärvi offers a one-of-a-kind vacation experience that will leave you with unforgettable memories.

List of Places to Visit in Kemijärvi

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kemijärvi has a rich cultural heritage, and there are several museums and historical places that you can visit to learn about the town’s history and traditions. Some of the must-visit attractions include:

  • Kemijärvi Church: This beautiful wooden church was built in the early 1950s and is considered one of the most important landmarks in the town. With its unique architecture and stunning interior, the Kemijärvi Church is a must-visit attraction for anyone visiting the town.

  • Särestöniemi Museum: This museum, located in the nearby village of Kaukonen, showcases the works of Reidar Särestöniemi, a famous Finnish painter. With its impressive collection of paintings and sculptures, the museum is a great place to learn about Finnish art and culture.

  • Kemijärvi Local History Museum: This museum, located in the center of Kemijärvi, features exhibits and artifacts that showcase the town’s history and development over the years. From traditional Sámi handicrafts to old photographs and documents, the museum is a treasure trove of information about Kemijärvi.

Natural Attractions of Kemijärvi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kemijärvi is a paradise for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts, with countless opportunities for hiking, biking, fishing, and skiing. Here are some of the best natural attractions that you shouldn’t miss:

  • Pyhä-Luosto National Park: This stunning national park, located about an hour’s drive from Kemijärvi, is a haven for hikers and nature lovers. With its beautiful forests, crystal-clear lakes, and breathtaking vistas, Pyhä-Luosto has something to offer for everyone.

  • Suomutunturi Ski Resort: This popular ski resort, located just a short drive from Kemijärvi, is a great place to hit the slopes and enjoy the winter wonderland. With its well-groomed trails, modern facilities, and spectacular views, Suomutunturi is a must-visit destination for anyone who loves skiing and snowboarding.

  • Lake Kemijärvi: This vast and picturesque lake is a great place to go fishing, boating, or just take a relaxing walk along the shore. With its pristine waters and scenic surroundings, Lake Kemijärvi is a popular spot for locals and tourists alike.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kemijärvi

Kemijärvi is home to some of the most beautiful hiking and biking trails in Finland. Whether you’re a seasoned hiker or a casual walker, there’s a route for you in Kemijärvi. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • Hiidenportti National Park: This national park, located about two hours’ drive from Kemijärvi, is a great place to hike and explore the rugged wilderness of northern Finland. With its rocky cliffs, deep gorges, and dense forests, Hiidenportti is a must-visit destination for any outdoor enthusiast.

  • Hetta-Pallas Trail: This iconic hiking trail, located in the nearby town of Enontekiö, stretches for 55 kilometers through some of the most spectacular landscapes in Lapland. With its stunning vistas, cozy cabins, and challenging terrain, the Hetta-Pallas Trail is an experience that you’ll never forget.

  • Kemijärven ympäri Pyöräilyreitti: This circular biking route, which takes you around the beautiful Lake Kemijärvi, is a great way to explore the town and its surroundings on two wheels. With its smooth roads, picturesque views, and friendly locals, Kemijärven ympäri Pyöräilyreitti is a must-try activity for any biking enthusiast.

Best Things to Do in Kemijärvi: Activities and Tours

Kemijärvi offers a wide range of activities and tours that cater to all tastes and interests. From reindeer safaris to Northern Lights tours, there’s something for everyone in Kemijärvi. Here are some of the best things to do in town:

  • Reindeer Safari: This traditional Sámi activity is a must-try experience in Kemijärvi. With its breathtaking views, cozy sleighs, and friendly reindeer, the reindeer safari is a unique way to explore the pristine Lapland wilderness.

  • Northern Lights Tour: Kemijärvi is one of the best places in the world to see the Northern Lights, and there are several tour operators in town that offer guided tours to find these elusive lights. With their expert guides, comfortable buses, and thermal clothing, these tours are a great way to experience the magic of the Aurora Borealis.

  • Ice Fishing: This traditional Nordic activity is a great way to experience the winter wonderland and catch some fresh fish at the same time. With its frozen lakes, cozy huts, and expert guides, ice fishing is a fun and relaxing activity for all ages.

Week-Long Itinerary for Kemijärvi

If you’re planning to spend a week in Kemijärvi, here’s a sample itinerary that will help you make the most of your time in town:

  • Day 1: Explore the town center, visit the Kemijärvi Church and the Local History Museum, and try some local cuisine in one of the town’s restaurants.

  • Day 2: Take a guided tour to see the Northern Lights, and experience the magic of the Aurora Borealis.

  • Day 3: Go hiking in Pyhä-Luosto National Park and enjoy the stunning scenery.

  • Day 4: Take a reindeer safari and learn about traditional Sámi culture and lifestyle.

  • Day 5: Visit the Särestöniemi Museum and learn about Finnish art and culture.

  • Day 6: Go skiing at Suomutunturi Ski Resort, and enjoy the winter wonderland scenery.

  • Day 7: Relax and enjoy the town’s beautiful surroundings, or take a day trip to another nearby destination.

Food and Drink Scene of Kemijärvi

Must-Try Dishes

Kemijärvi is famous for its delicious Scandinavian cuisine, and there are several must-try dishes that you should sample during your stay. Here are some of the culinary highlights of Kemijärvi:

  • Sautéed reindeer: This traditional dish, made with tender reindeer meat, lingonberries, and mashed potatoes, is a true delight for meat lovers.

  • Kemijärvi trout: This fresh and flavorful fish, caught from Lake Kemijärvi, is a must-try for seafood lovers.

  • Rössypottu: This hearty soup, made with meat, vegetables, and potatoes, is a perfect comfort food for a cold winter day.

Local Beverages

Kemijärvi is also known for its local beverages, including beer, wine, and spirits. Some of the best local drinks to try in Kemijärvi include:

  • Lapin Kulta beer: This popular Finnish beer, made with pure Lapland water and quality malt, is a refreshing treat after a day of outdoor activities.

  • Kilpisjärvi Polar Night Gin: This premium Finnish gin, made with handpicked berries from Kilpisjärvi, is a great way to warm up on a cold winter evening.

  • Cloudberry wine: This sweet and fruity wine, made from the golden berries that grow in the Lapland wilderness, is a perfect souvenir to bring home from your trip.

Where to Stay in Kemijärvi: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kemijärvi offers a wide range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels and cozy guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay in town:

  • Hotel Kemijärvi: This modern and stylish hotel, located in the heart of Kemijärvi, offers comfortable rooms, a relaxing sauna, and a popular restaurant.

  • Hostel Kemijärvi: This cozy and affordable hostel, located just a short walk from the town center, offers clean and comfortable dorm rooms and private rooms.

What to Buy in Kemijärvi: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kemijärvi is a great place to buy souvenirs and local products, including traditional Sámi handicrafts, handwoven woolen socks, and Lapland-themed gifts. Here are some of the best places to shop in town:

  • Paadar: This charming shop, located in the town center, offers a wide range of Sámi handicrafts, from traditional jewelry to leather bags and handwoven rugs.

  • Klaus Market: This grocery store, located in the heart of Kemijärvi, offers a great selection of local products and souvenirs, including Lapland-themed gifts and souvenirs.

  • Kultala: This small jewelry shop, located in the nearby village of Pyhä, specializes in beautiful gold and silver jewelry inspired by Lapland’s natural beauty.

Local Culture and Customs of Kemijärvi

Kemijärvi has a unique blend of Finnish and Sámi culture, and there are several customs and traditions that you should be aware of when visiting the town. Here are some of the most important cultural aspects of Kemijärvi:

  • Sámi culture: The Sámi people have a strong presence in Kemijärvi, and you can learn about their culture and traditions by visiting one of the local museums or participating in a reindeer safari.

  • Midsummer: The Finnish midsummer celebration, known as “juhannus,” is a major holiday in Kemijärvi, and locals celebrate it with bonfires, barbecues, and traditional songs and dances.

  • Respect for nature: Kemijärvi is surrounded by pristine wilderness, and locals have a deep respect for nature and the environment. When visiting the town, make sure to follow the leave-no-trace principles and tread lightly on the natural surroundings.

Best Time to Visit Kemijärvi

Kemijärvi has something to offer all year round, but the best time to visit depends on your preferences and interests. If you love winter activities like skiing and snowboarding, the winter months (December to March) are the best time to visit. If you’re more interested in hiking and exploring the national parks, the summer months (June to August) are the ideal time. For those who want to see the Northern Lights, the best time to visit is from September to March, when the nights are long and dark.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kemijärvi

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs, and learn about the traditions and history of the town.

  • Take care of the environment and follow the leave-no-trace principles when hiking and exploring the wilderness.

  • Dress in warm and comfortable clothing, especially in the winter months.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or disturb the wildlife, and avoid feeding or approaching the wild animals.

  • Don’t trespass on private property or disturb the locals.

  • Don’t ignore the safety guidelines for outdoor activities, and always follow the instructions of the guides and experts.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kemijärvi

Kemijärvi is a safe and secure town, with very low crime rates and friendly locals. However, when exploring the wilderness and participating in outdoor activities, it’s important to be aware of the potential hazards and risks. Make sure to follow the guidelines and instructions provided by the guides and experts, and always carry a map, a compass, and a first-aid kit with you.

Health in Kemijärvi

Kemijärvi has a good healthcare system and several medical facilities, including a health center and a hospital. However, if you’re planning to participate in outdoor activities, make sure to have a comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies and evacuation.

Getting to Kemijärvi: Transportation Options and Logistics

Kemijärvi is located in northern Finland, approximately 160 kilometers north of Rovaniemi, the largest city in Lapland. Here are some transportation options and logistics for getting to Kemijärvi:

Airports Near Kemijärvi

  • Rovaniemi Airport: This is the closest airport to Kemijärvi, located about 160 kilometers to the south. There are daily flights from Helsinki to Rovaniemi, operated by Finnair and Norwegian.

  • Kittilä Airport: This airport is located about 120 kilometers to the west of Kemijärvi, and there are daily flights from Helsinki to Kittilä, operated by Finnair and Norwegian.

Bus Stations Near Kemijärvi

  • Kemijärvi Bus Station: This is the main bus station in town, with daily connections to Rovaniemi, Kittilä, and other nearby destinations.

  • Rovaniemi Bus Station: This is the largest bus station in Lapland, with daily connections to Helsinki, Oulu, and other major cities in Finland.

Other Options

  • Rental car: If you’re a confident driver, renting a car is a great way to explore Kemijärvi and its surroundings at your own pace.

  • Train: There are daily train connections from Helsinki to Rovaniemi, and from Rovaniemi to Kemijärvi.

How to Get Around in Kemijärvi

Kemijärvi is a small town, and most of the attractions and activities are located within walking distance. However, if you want to explore the surroundings or participate in outdoor activities, here are some transportation options:

  • Bike rental: There are several places in town where you can rent a bike and explore the surroundings on two wheels.

  • Car rental: Renting a car is a great way to explore the national parks and nearby attractions, and there are several rental companies in town that offer affordable rates.

  • Taxi: There are several taxi companies in Kemijärvi that offer reliable and affordable transportation services.

Where to Go Next from Kemijärvi: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Kemijärvi is a great starting point for exploring the Lapland region and its unique attractions and destinations. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions that you should consider:

  • Rovaniemi: This is the largest city in Lapland, and it’s known for its Santa Claus Village, Arktikum Museum, and other tourist attractions.

  • Pyhä-Luosto National Park: This stunning national park, located about an hour’s drive from Kemijärvi, is a great place to explore the pristine Lapland wilderness and enjoy the scenic views.

  • Levi ski resort: This popular ski resort, located about two hours’ drive from Kemijärvi, is a great place to hit the slopes and enjoy the winter wonderland.

Conclusion

Kemijärvi is a magical destination that offers a unique blend of Finnish and Sámi culture, breathtaking scenery, and unlimited opportunities for outdoor adventure. Whether you’re a nature lover, a culture enthusiast, or simply looking for a relaxing getaway, Kemijärvi has something to offer for everyone. With its friendly locals, cozy accommodations, and wide range of activities and attractions, Kemijärvi is a destination that you’ll never forget.
