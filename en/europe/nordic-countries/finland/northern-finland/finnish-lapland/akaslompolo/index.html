Introduction to Äkäslompolo

Located in Finnish Lapland, Äkäslompolo is a peaceful and picturesque village that offers a unique cultural experience and stunning natural landscapes. The village is situated near Ylläs, the highest ski resort in Finland. Here, you can ski and snowboard on some of the best slopes in Northern Europe or hike and bike during summer months.

This comprehensive travel guide will provide you with everything you need to know to plan your visit to Äkäslompolo, including the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay.

List of Places to Visit in Äkäslompolo

Whether you are interested in cultural attractions or outdoor adventures, Äkäslompolo has something for everyone. Here are the best places to visit in Äkäslompolo:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Äkäslompolo Church: This historical church is a significant landmark in the area and is known for its impressive architecture.
  • Hannu Hautala Nature Photography Centre: If you appreciate nature photography, then you must visit this gallery, which features stunning images of Finland’s wildlife and landscapes.

Natural Attractions of Äkäslompolo: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ylläs National Park: This park covers over 1,500 square kilometers of untouched wilderness and offers some of the most breathtaking views in the region. Visitors can hike, bike, or ski in the park, depending on the season.
  • Kellokas Visitor Centre: This center provides visitors with information, maps, and guided tours of the area. The center is also home to a restaurant and a souvenir shop.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Äkäslompolo

If you are looking for some outdoor adventure, Äkäslompolo has plenty of hiking and biking routes to choose from. Here are some of the best routes in the area:

  • Ylläs-Levi Hiking Trail: This trail spans 50 kilometers from Ylläs to Levi and takes hikers through some of the most beautiful landscapes in Finland.
  • Pallastunturi National Park Hike: This hike is ideal for those who want to experience the breathtaking views of the Northern Lights and the stars.

Best Things to Do in Äkäslompolo: Activities and Tours

Äkäslompolo offers a wide range of activities and tours depending on the season.

  • Aurora Hunting Tour: If you visit during winter, you can take an Aurora Hunting Tour to catch a glimpse of the Northern Lights.
  • Horseback Riding Tour: Explore the stunning Finnish Lapland landscape while on horseback.
  • Snowmobile Safari: Enjoy a thrilling snowmobile ride through the snowy terrain of Äkäslompolo.

Week-Long Itinerary for Äkäslompolo

To help you plan your visit to Äkäslompolo, here is a recommended week-long itinerary:

Food and Drink Scene of Äkäslompolo

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lohikeitto (Salmon Soup): This traditional Finnish soup is made with fresh salmon, vegetables, and cream.
  • Poronkäristys (Reindeer Stew): Reindeer is a staple ingredient in Finnish cuisine and is often served as a stew.

Local Beverages

  • Sahti: This traditional Finnish beer is made with rye and juniper berries.
  • Glögi: A warm, spiced mulled wine that is the perfect drink to enjoy on a cold winter’s day.

Where to Stay in Äkäslompolo: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Ylläshumina Lodge: A cozy lodge located near the village center with complimentary breakfast and comfortable rooms.
  • Lapland Hotel Akashotelli Ylläs: A luxury hotel situated next to the Ylläs ski resort with spa, sauna, and exceptional dining options.
  • Äkäslompolo Cottages: If you prefer a more rustic stay, then Äkäslompolo Cottages offers a range of traditional Finnish log cabins.

What to Buy in Äkäslompolo: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Sámi Jewelry: Sámi is Finnish Lapland’s indigenous people, and their jewelry is often made with dyed reindeer hide, antlers, and other natural materials.
  • Kilpisjärvi Glass Igloos: These stunning glass igloos are a unique souvenir to bring home and can be used as a decoration or a lamp.

Local Culture and Customs of Äkäslompolo

The Sámi people have inhabited Finnish Lapland for thousands of years, and their influence can still be seen in the local culture, traditions, and cuisine. Visitors should be aware of the importance of reindeer herding to the Sámi community and respect their customs while in the area.

Best Time to Visit Äkäslompolo

The best time to visit Äkäslompolo depends on your interests. If you enjoy winter activities like skiing, snowboarding, and snowmobiling, visit between November to April. Alternatively, if you prefer hiking and biking, the summer months from June to August are ideal.

Do’s and Don’ts of Äkäslompolo

Do:

  • Dress in layers to protect yourself from the cold weather.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Respect the local Sámi traditions.

Don’t:

  • Ignore safety guidelines when participating in outdoor activities.
  • Capture photos of Sámi without their permission.
  • Litter in the natural landscapes of Äkäslompolo.

Safety and Health

Safety in Äkäslompolo

Visitors are advised to dress appropriately for the cold weather, especially during winter months. It is essential to follow safety guidelines given by the activity leaders when participating in outdoor activities to ensure your safety.

Health in Äkäslompolo

Visitors should have comprehensive travel insurance and basic medical knowledge in case of emergency. Information on COVID-19 regulations should be noted and observed while in Äkäslompolo.

Getting to Äkäslompolo: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Äkäslompolo

The nearest airports to Äkäslompolo are the Kittilä Airport and the Rovaniemi Airport, both of which have direct flights from Helsinki.

Bus Stations Near Äkäslompolo

Regular bus services operate from Rovaniemi, Kittilä, and Kolari to Äkäslompolo.

Other Options

Some tourists opt for hiring cars that provide flexible tour duration and privacy.

How to Get Around in Äkäslompolo

The easiest way to get around Äkäslompolo is by renting a car or using a taxi service. Äkäslompolo is also a great place to explore by bike or foot.

Where to Go Next from Äkäslompolo: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Levi: A popular winter destination, Levi is located a short distance from Ylläs and offers excellent skiing and snowboarding facilities.
  • Kilpisjärvi: For a unique experience, venture deeper into Lapland and explore Kilpisjärvi, which is home to the infamous Saana mountain.

Conclusion

We hope this comprehensive guide helped plan your visit to Äkäslompolo. This Finnish Lapland gem awaits with activities, attractions, and stunning landscapes.
