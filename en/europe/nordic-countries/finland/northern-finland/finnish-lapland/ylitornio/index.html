Introduction to Ylitornio

Ylitornio is a town in Finnish Lapland, Northern Finland, nestled in the heart of the Nordic countries. It is a popular destination for travelers looking for an authentic Lappish experience, featuring unique natural landscapes and arctic wonders. Ylitornio is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts and adventure-seekers. In this travel guide, you’ll find information on the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and safety tips to help you plan an unforgettable trip to Ylitornio.

List of Places to Visit in Ylitornio

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tornionlaakson Maakuntamuseo: The Tornionlaakson Maakuntamuseo is a museum that showcases the rich cultural heritage and history of the region. It features an extensive collection of historical artifacts, including household items used in the past, traditional clothing, and photographs that help visitors understand the way of life in Ylitornio and the surrounding areas.

  • Lapland War Museum: Located in Rovaniemi, the Lapland War Museum is a must-visit attraction for those interested in Finnish history. It documents Finland’s struggle against the Soviet Union in World War II, providing a comprehensive overview of Lapland’s war history.

Natural Attractions of Ylitornio: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Pooki Nature Trail: The Pooki Nature Trail is a scenic hiking trail that leads visitors through the rugged wilderness of Lapland. It offers stunning views of the surrounding forests, hills, and rivers. This trail is perfect for those who love nature and want to experience the true beauty of Ylitornio.

  • Olosjärvi Lake: Olosjärvi Lake is a popular spot for outdoor activities such as fishing and kayaking. The tranquil lake is surrounded by dense forests, offering a peaceful and relaxing environment to its visitors.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ylitornio

Ylitornio offers several hiking and biking routes that cater to various skill levels and preferences. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced hiker, you’ll find a trail that suits your needs. Some of the popular hiking and biking routes in Ylitornio include:

  • Taimenjärven kierros
  • Ahvenkosken kanavaraitti
  • Kukkolankoski

Best Things to Do in Ylitornio: Activities and Tours

  • Aurora Hunting: Ylitornio is one of the best places in Finland to witness the Northern Lights. Join a tour and witness the spectacular Aurora Borealis in all its glory.

  • Sauna Experience: Enjoy the Finnish tradition of sauna, a therapeutic experience that relaxes your mind and body. Many local accommodations offer sauna experiences to their guests.

  • Snowmobiling: Experience the thrill of racing over snow and ice with a snowmobile tour in Ylitornio’s beautiful landscapes.

Food and Drink Scene of Ylitornio

Must-Try Dishes

  • Reindeer Meat: One of the most popular delicacies in Lapland, reindeer meat is a must-try dish when in Ylitornio. It can be enjoyed in a variety of ways such as sautéed or smoked.

  • Kössi: Kössi is a delicious Finnish dish made from lamb meat and served with mashed potatoes and lingonberry jam. It is a perfect comfort food during the cold winter months.

Local Beverages

  • Lappish Berry Juice: This refreshing beverage is made from locally sourced berries and is a great way to cool off during hot summer days.

  • Lapin Kulta: Lapin Kulta is a popular Finnish beer that is synonymous with Lapland. Grab a cold one and toast to the good times!

Where to Stay in Ylitornio: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ylitornio has a variety of accommodations that cater to all budgets and preferences. From luxury hotels to cozy guesthouses, you’ll find something that suits your needs. Some of the most popular places to stay in Ylitornio include:

  • Lapland Hotel Pallas: Located in a picturesque location, Lapland Hotel Pallas offers guests a unique experience in traditional Lappish-style log cabins. It is a perfect retreat for nature lovers and those who want to escape from modern life.

  • Ylitornion Tunturihotelli: Ylitornion Tunturihotelli is a four-star hotel that offers guests a luxurious stay experience. The hotel features a sauna, fitness center, and an on-site restaurant serving traditional Finnish cuisine.

  • Lomakeskus Koivukankare: Lomakeskus Koivukankare is a budget-friendly option for those who prefer a simple but comfortable stay experience. The property features well-equipped cabins, a sauna, and an on-site restaurant.

What to Buy in Ylitornio: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Lappish Handicrafts: Ylitornio is known for its traditional handicrafts made from local materials such as reindeer skin, birch bark, and wood. You’ll find a variety of handmade souvenirs like jewelry, rugs, and textiles.

  • Lappish Food Products: Take the flavors of Lapland back home with locally made food products such as smoked salmon, traditional Finnish cheese, and of course, cloudberry jam.

Local Culture and Customs of Ylitornio

Lapland has a unique culture that is deeply rooted in nature and tradition. The Sami people, indigenous to the region, have their own language and customs that still have a significant influence on the area. Visitors to Ylitornio can expect a warm and welcoming environment that celebrates its culture and heritage.

Best Time to Visit Ylitornio

The best time to visit Ylitornio depends on what you’re looking for. The winter months offer the chance to experience the magical northern lights and snow-covered landscapes. Summer, on the other hand, is perfect for outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, and kayaking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ylitornio

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.

  • Try local food and drinks.

  • Dress appropriately for the weather, especially in winter.

Don’t:

  • Venture off marked trails while hiking.

  • Disturb the natural environment and wildlife.

  • Ignore the local rules and regulations.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ylitornio

Ylitornio is a very safe destination for travelers. However, visitors should be aware of the extreme weather conditions during the winter months. It’s important to dress warmly and follow the safety guidelines given by your tour guides or accommodation hosts.

Health in Ylitornio

Finland has excellent healthcare facilities, and visitors can take comfort knowing that medical treatment is readily available if needed.

Getting to Ylitornio: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ylitornio

The closest airport to Ylitornio is the Rovaniemi Airport, located approximately 120 km away. From the airport, visitors can take a taxi or rent a car to reach Ylitornio.

Bus Stations Near Ylitornio

Ylitornio has a bus station that connects the town to other cities in Finland. You can check the bus schedules and book your tickets through Matkahuolto.

Other Options

For those who prefer to travel by train, the nearest train station is located in Kemi, approximately 90 km away.

How to Get Around in Ylitornio

Ylitornio is a small town that can easily be explored on foot or by bike. Renting a car is also an option if you want to explore the surrounding areas independently.

Where to Go Next from Ylitornio: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Rovaniemi: Rovaniemi is a popular destination in Lapland and is famous for its Santa Claus Village. It’s located approximately 120 km away from Ylitornio.

  • Kemi: Kemi is located approximately 90 km away from Ylitornio and is known for its SnowCastle, which is a popular attraction during the winter months.

Conclusion

Ylitornio offers visitors a unique experience that showcases both the natural beauty and cultural heritage of Lapland. With its rich history, scenic trails, and delicious local cuisine, Ylitornio has something for everyone. We hope this travel guide has helped you plan your trip to Ylitornio and has provided you with all the information you need to make the most of your stay. Happy traveling!
