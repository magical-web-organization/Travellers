Introduction to Kevo Strict Nature Reserve

Kevo Strict Nature Reserve is a protected wilderness area covering 712 square kilometers. It is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna and is internationally recognized as an important area for ecological research. It is also known for its cultural significance as it has been inhabited by the indigenous Sami people for thousands of years. Kevo Strict Nature Reserve is a popular destination for hiking, birdwatching, and fishing.
