Introduction to Kitee

Kitee is a town located in the North Karelia region of Finland. It is a charming destination that boasts of natural beauty and cultural heritage. The town is located in the Finnish Lakeland, which comprises of over 18,000 islands and one of the largest lake systems in the world. Kitee is known for its scenic landscapes, pristine lakes, and rich cultural heritage. It is an ideal destination for travelers who want to experience the tranquility of nature, explore the Finnish culture, and indulge in outdoor adventures.

List of Places to Visit in Kitee

Kitee offers a plethora of attractions for tourists to explore. Here are some of the must-visit places in Kitee:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kitee’s rich cultural heritage is reflected in its museums and historical places.

  • Kitee Zoo: This zoo is located in the heart of Kitee, and it is an ideal place for families and animal lovers. The zoo is home to over 50 different species of animals, including the endangered Saimaa ringed seal, which is native to the region.

  • Kitee Railway Station: This historical building is located in the center of Kitee. It was built-in 1918 and was used as a railway station until 1999. Today, it serves as a cultural center, housing an art gallery and a restaurant.

  • Pajarinhovi Animal Park: This park is located on the outskirts of Kitee and houses exotic animals from all over the world. Visitors can interact with the animals and learn about their habits and habitats in a natural setting.

Natural Attractions of Kitee: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kitee is blessed with natural beauty, and it offers numerous opportunities for outdoor adventures and scenic views.

  • Lake Pyhäselkä: This lake is located in the heart of Kitee and provides plenty of opportunities for water activities such as swimming, fishing, and boating.

  • Puruvesi Lake: This is one of the most beautiful lakes in Finland, located near Kitee. It is an ideal destination for fishing, camping, and hiking.

  • Hiidenportti National Park: This park is located near Kitee and offers breathtaking natural landscapes, including deep ravines, waterfalls, and a river. It is an ideal destination for hiking and nature photography.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kitee

Kitee offers numerous hiking and biking routes for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best routes to explore in Kitee:

  • Kolvananuuro Nature Trail: This trail is located in Hiidenportti National Park and is a must-visit for hikers. It offers some of the most beautiful views of the national park, including waterfalls and ravines.

  • Kitee Hiking Trail: This trail is located in the southern part of Kitee and is ideal for both hiking and biking. It offers scenic views of the Finnish Lakeland and is a great way to explore the natural beauty of Kitee.

Best Things to Do in Kitee: Activities and Tours

Apart from exploring the natural beauty and cultural heritage of Kitee, visitors can indulge in various activities and tours. Here are some of the best things to do in Kitee:

  • Fishing: Kitee is an ideal destination for fishing, with numerous lakes and rivers offering a wide range of fish species.

  • Cycling Tours: Visitors can explore the natural beauty of Kitee on a cycling tour and enjoy the scenic views of the Finnish Lakeland.

  • Saimaa Seal Watching: Visitors can observe the endangered Saimaa ringed seals in their natural habitat on a seal watching tour.

Food and Drink Scene of Kitee

Kitee’s food and drink scene is a reflection of its Finnish heritage. Visitors can indulge in traditional Finnish cuisine and local beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages in Kitee:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kalakukko: This is a traditional Finnish dish that comprises of rye crust filled with fish and bacon.

  • Karjalanpaisti: This is a hearty stew made with beef, potatoes, and carrots, and is a specialty of the region.

  • Hernekeitto: This is a traditional Finnish pea soup served with pork and rye bread.

Local Beverages

  • Kitee Brewery Beer: This beer is brewed locally in Kitee and is a must-try for beer aficionados.

  • Salmiakki Koskenkorva: This is a Finnish vodka flavored with salmiakki, a salty licorice.

  • Karelian Balder: This is a Finnish cider made with local apples.

Where to Stay in Kitee: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kitee offers numerous options for accommodation, ranging from luxury hotels to budget-friendly guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay in Kitee:

  • Kruunupuisto Hotel: This is a luxury hotel located in the Finnish Lakeland. It offers scenic views of Lake Puruvesi and is an ideal destination for a romantic getaway.

  • Välituvan Lomamökit: This is a traditional Finnish cottage that offers a cozy and comfortable stay for families and groups.

  • Villa Pyyniemi: This is a guesthouse located near Lake Pyhäselkä and is an ideal destination for a peaceful and relaxing holiday.

What to Buy in Kitee: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors can buy a wide range of souvenirs and local products in Kitee. Here are some of the best products to bring home:

  • Karelian pies: These are traditional Finnish pastries filled with rice or potatoes and are a specialty of the region.

  • Finnish chocolates and candies: Visitors can stock up on Finnish chocolates and candies, which are famous for their unique flavors and quality.

  • Kalevala Jewelry: This is a beloved Finnish jewelry brand inspired by the national epic poem, Kalevala.

Local Culture and Customs of Kitee

Kitee is a small town that prides itself on its Finnish heritage. Visitors are expected to respect the local culture and customs, which revolve around family values and respect for nature.

Best Time to Visit Kitee

The best time to visit Kitee is during the summer months (June to August), when the weather is pleasant, and the natural beauty is at its peak. Visitors can enjoy water activities, hiking, and cycling during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kitee

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.

  • Explore the natural beauty of Kitee.

  • Indulge in Finnish cuisine and local beverages.

Don’t:

  • Litter or harm the natural beauty of Kitee.

  • Disrespect local traditions and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kitee

Kitee is a safe destination for tourists. However, visitors should take standard precautions such as locking their doors and not leaving valuables in their cars or hotel rooms.

Health in Kitee

Finland has a good healthcare system, and visitors can access medical services in Kitee if needed.

Getting to Kitee: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Kitee

The nearest airport to Kitee is Joensuu Airport, which is located approximately 50 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Kitee

Kitee has a bus station that connects it with other cities in Finland.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel to Kitee by car or train from Helsinki.

How to Get Around in Kitee

The best way to explore Kitee is by renting a car or bike. Visitors can also use local buses to navigate the town.

Where to Go Next from Kitee: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can travel to the nearby towns of Savonlinna or Joensuu to explore their cultural heritage. They can also take a trip to the Russian border, located approximately 100 kilometers away from Kitee.

Conclusion

Kitee is a charming destination that offers visitors a chance to experience Finnish culture and heritage. From exploring the natural beauty of the Finnish Lakeland to indulging in traditional Finnish cuisine, Kitee has something for everyone. This comprehensive travel guide provides visitors with all the information they need to plan their trip to Kitee and enjoy its natural beauty and cultural heritage.
