Welcome to Tohmajärvi, a hidden gem nestled in the Finnish Lakeland region in North Karelia. This town is known for its breathtaking natural landscapes, cultural heritage, and friendly locals. Whether you’re an adventure enthusiast, a culture buff, or a foodie, Tohmajärvi has something special in store for everyone. Read on for a comprehensive travel guide to help you plan your vacation to Tohmajärvi.

Introduction to Tohmajärvi

Located in the eastern part of Finland, Tohmajärvi is a town that offers visitors a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The town is surrounded by lush forests, sweeping lakes, and charming villages. Inhabited by friendly locals who welcome visitors with open arms, Tohmajärvi is an ideal destination to experience the Finnish way of living.

List of Places to Visit in Tohmajärvi

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Pielisjärvi Museum: This museum showcases the history of the Pielisjärvi region, highlighting the cultural heritage of Tohmajärvi and its neighboring towns.
  • Pyhäjärvi Church: This beautiful church was built in 1843 and is a fine example of Finnish Wooden Church architecture.
  • Hiekkaniemi Manor: This 200-year-old manor, located near Lake Orivesi, has been converted into a museum and is a must-visit for history buffs.

Natural Attractions of Tohmajärvi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hiidenportti National Park: This park is known for its rugged landscapes, gorges, and ravines, offering visitors an array of outdoor activities such as hiking, trekking, and bird-watching.
  • Orivesi Lake: This beautiful lake is known for its crystal clear waters and sandy shores. Visitors can swim, fish, or take a boat ride on the lake.
  • Kolinuuronjärvi Nature Reserve: This nature reserve is ideal for hiking and biking enthusiasts, with well-marked trails that take visitors through picturesque forests.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tohmajärvi

  • Hiidenportti National Park Trails: The park has several trails of varying difficulty levels, the most popular being the 8-kilometer Hiidenkierros trail that takes visitors through the heart of the park.
  • Orivesi Lake Trail: This 12-kilometer trail follows the shoreline of Lake Orivesi and offers scenic views of the lake and surrounding forests.
  • Koli-Uuro Trail: This 16-kilometer trail takes visitors on a journey through the Kolinuuronjärvi Nature Reserve, with stunning views of the lake and forested hills.

Best Things to Do in Tohmajärvi: Activities and Tours

  • Traditional Finnish Sauna Experience: A visit to Tohmajärvi is incomplete without experiencing an authentic Finnish sauna, a crucial part of the Finnish lifestyle. There are many saunas in the town that offer visitors a chance to unwind and enjoy this traditional experience.
  • Boat Cruise on Orivesi Lake: Enjoy a relaxing boat ride on the beautiful Orivesi Lake, surrounded by lush forests and sandy beaches.
  • Local Food and Drink Tour: Indulge in authentic Finnish cuisine and local beverages while exploring the town. Several tour operators offer guided tours that take visitors through a culinary journey of Tohmajärvi.

Food and Drink Scene of Tohmajärvi

Must-Try Dishes

  • Karelian Pies: These traditional Finnish pastries are made of rye flour and filled with rice pudding or mashed potato.
  • Kalakukko: This savory pie, popular in North Karelia, is filled with fish, pork, and bacon.
  • Karjalanpaisti: This slow-cooked beef and vegetable stew is a staple of Finnish cuisine.

Local Beverages

  • Karelian Beer: This locally brewed beer is made from pure Finnish water and is available in several flavors.
  • Lakka Liquor: Made from the wild berries of the Arctic region, this liquor is a popular Finnish drink.

Where to Stay in Tohmajärvi: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Pielisjärvi Hotel: This hotel, located near the Pielisjärvi Museum, offers comfortable accommodation and stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Hiidenportti Camping: This camping site is located in the Hiidenportti National Park, offering visitors a unique opportunity to stay amidst nature.
  • Penttilä Inn: This quaint guesthouse, located in the heart of Tohmajärvi, is known for its warm hospitality.

What to Buy in Tohmajärvi: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Karelian Pies and Breads: Several local bakeries in Tohmajärvi make traditional Karelian pies and bread, which make for an excellent souvenir.
  • Finnish Sauna Products: To experience a true Finnish sauna at home, take home some locally made sauna products such as fragrant candles, soaps, and oils.
  • Artisanal Handicrafts: Tohmajärvi is known for its art and handicrafts scene. Visitors can purchase locally made ceramics, glassware, and wooden handicrafts at several shops around the town.

Local Culture and Customs of Tohmajärvi

Tohmajärvi is known for its rich cultural heritage and traditions. Respect for nature, love for sauna, and cherishing family values are an integral part of the Finnish way of life and can be observed in Tohmajärvi as well. The town also has a vibrant arts and crafts scene with several local artists selling their wares in shops and galleries. Visitors are encouraged to immerse themselves in the local culture and interact with the friendly locals to make the most of their trip.

Best Time to Visit Tohmajärvi

The best time to visit Tohmajärvi is during the summer months (June-August) when the weather is pleasant, and the natural landscapes are in full bloom. The town is also a popular winter destination for its winter sports activities, such as snowmobiling, skiing, and ice fishing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tohmajärvi

Do:

  • Respect nature and the environment.
  • Experience a traditional Finnish sauna.
  • Take part in local cultural activities and interact with the locals.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural habitats.
  • Smoke in public places.
  • Behave inappropriately in public or toward others.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tohmajärvi

Tohmajärvi is a safe and friendly town with minimal crime. Visitors are advised to take necessary precautions such as keeping valuables safe and being aware of their surroundings, especially while exploring the natural trails.

Health in Tohmajärvi

The town has several medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics, that offer quality medical services.

Getting to Tohmajärvi: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tohmajärvi

The nearest airport to Tohmajärvi is Joensuu Airport, located about 35 kilometers from the town.

Bus Stations Near Tohmajärvi

Several bus companies provide bus services to Tohmajärvi from neighboring towns and cities, including Kuopio and Joensuu.

Other Options

Taxis and cars can also be rented from several agencies in the neighboring cities.

How to Get Around in Tohmajärvi

Tohmajärvi is a small town, and most of the attractions are easily accessible by foot or bike. Taxis and car rentals are also available for visitors who want to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Tohmajärvi: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Koli National Park: This national park, located about 70 kilometers from Tohmajärvi, is known for its stunning landscapes and is a must-visit for nature enthusiasts.
  • Joensuu: This city, located about 40 kilometers from Tohmajärvi, is known for its rich cultural heritage and lively atmosphere.
  • Savonlinna: This beautiful town, located about 130 kilometers from Tohmajärvi, is known for its stunning castle and vibrant music scene.

Conclusion

Tohmajärvi is a destination that perfectly combines natural beauty with cultural heritage, making it an ideal vacation spot for travelers who want to experience Finland’s serene lifestyle.
