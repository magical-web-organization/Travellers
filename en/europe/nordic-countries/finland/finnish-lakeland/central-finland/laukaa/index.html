Introduction to Laukaa

Located in the heart of Finnish Lakeland, Laukaa is a vibrant and charming destination in Central Finland. Known for its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and outdoor adventures, Laukaa offers something for every traveler.

With its calm lakes, pristine forests, and picturesque villages, Laukaa is an excellent destination for those looking to escape the crowds and immerse themselves in the peaceful Finnish countryside. Whether you’re into hiking, biking, fishing, or just relaxing, Laukaa has plenty to offer.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take a closer look at some of the top places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and what to eat in Laukaa. Let’s dive in!

List of Places to Visit in Laukaa

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Peasant Museum of Finland

The Peasant Museum of Finland is a unique open-air museum that showcases the life, culture, and traditions of Finnish peasants from the 18th to the 20th century. With over 70 buildings, including farms, barns, and workshops, the museum offers a fascinating glimpse into the past.

Laukaa Church

Laukaa Church is an iconic landmark in the region that dates back to the 15th century. The church features impressive wooden sculptures and paintings and is known for its scenic location on a hilltop overlooking Lake Leppävesi.

Laukaa Local History Museum

For those interested in the history of Laukaa, the Laukaa Local History Museum is a must-visit destination. The museum provides an insight into the local life and culture through its intriguing exhibits and displays.

Natural Attractions of Laukaa: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Peurunka

Peurunka is one of the most popular outdoor recreation centers in Laukaa, offering a wide range of activities for all seasons. From skiing, snowboarding, and ice skating in winter to golfing, swimming, and hiking in summer, Peurunka is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts.

Leivonmäki National Park

Leivonmäki National Park is a haven for nature lovers, offering breathtaking landscapes, rich biodiversity, and several hiking trails. The park features crystal-clear lakes, lush forests, and scenic viewpoints that provide a visual treat to visitors.

Konneveden Isojärvi National Park

Located just an hour’s drive from Laukaa, Konneveden Isojärvi National Park is another natural wonder that is worth a visit. The park’s centerpiece is the stunning Isojärvi Lake, surrounded by rugged cliffs, forests, and sandy beaches.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Laukaa

With its vast natural landscapes, Laukaa is a paradise for hikers and bikers. Some of the most popular trails include the Maaninganvuori Trail, the Keskisuomalainen Trail, and the Peurunka Trail. These routes offer stunning views of the surrounding forests, lakes, and villages and provide a great way to explore the area.

Best Things to Do in Laukaa: Activities and Tours

From cultural experiences to outdoor adventures, Laukaa offers a range of activities and tours that cater to every interest and budget. Some of the top things to do in Laukaa include:

  • Fishing in Lake Paijanne
  • Skiing and snowboarding at Peurunka
  • Visiting the Peasant Museum of Finland
  • Exploring the Leivonmäki National Park
  • Taking a cruise on Lake Keitele
  • Relaxing in the spa at Peurunka

Food and Drink Scene of Laukaa

Must-Try Dishes

When in Laukaa, you must try some of the local delicacies, such as:

  • Kalakukko, a fish-filled pasty
  • Karjalanpaisti, a hearty meat stew
  • Kropsu, thin pancakes topped with cream and lingonberries
  • Lihapulla, Finnish meatballs

Local Beverages

For drinks, sample the Finnish specialties, such as:

  • Sahti, a traditional beer brewed with juniper berries
  • Sima, a sweet mead made with honey
  • Glögi, a mulled wine flavored with spices

Laukaa has a range of accommodation options to suit every budget and style. From luxury resorts to cozy guesthouses, there’s something for everyone. Some of the top hotels in Laukaa include:

  • Peurunka Spa Hotel

  • Hotel Vuolake

What to Buy in Laukaa: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When visiting Laukaa, take home some unique souvenirs and local products to remember your trip. Some of the best items to buy include:

  • Handcrafted wooden items, such as bowls and spoons
  • Hand-knitted woolen socks and mittens
  • Finnish candy and chocolates
  • Finnish saunas and spa products

Local Culture and Customs of Laukaa

Finns are known for their love of nature and the outdoors, and Laukaa is no exception. The locals take great pride in their natural surroundings and have a deep respect for the environment. When visiting Laukaa, it is important to follow the local customs and respect the natural beauty of the area.

Best Time to Visit Laukaa

Laukaa is a year-round destination, with each season offering its unique charm and activities. Summer is the most popular time to visit, with long days, pleasant weather, and a host of outdoor activities. Winter, on the other hand, offers excellent skiing and snowboarding opportunities, as well as a chance to witness the Northern Lights.

Do’s and Don’ts of Laukaa

Do:

  • Respect the nature and wildlife of the region
  • Try some of the local cuisine and beverages
  • Take part in outdoor activities, such as hiking and fishing
  • Visit the local museums and cultural attractions

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the environment
  • Ignore the local customs and traditions
  • Disturb the animals or wildlife
  • Leave without trying the local saunas

Safety and Health

Safety in Laukaa

Laukaa is generally a safe destination, with low crime rates and excellent healthcare facilities. However, visitors should take precautions when hiking or engaging in outdoor activities, such as wearing appropriate clothing and footwear and following safety guidelines.

Health in Laukaa

Finland has a high standard of healthcare, and Laukaa is no exception. Visitors should ensure they have comprehensive health insurance and carry any necessary medications with them.

Getting to Laukaa: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Laukaa

The nearest international airport is Helsinki-Vantaa Airport, located approximately 260 km from Laukaa. From there, visitors can take a domestic flight to Jyvaskyla Airport or rent a car or take a train to Laukaa.

Bus Stations Near Laukaa

Laukaa is well-connected to the rest of Finland by bus, with regular services from Jyvaskyla and other major cities in the region.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel to Laukaa by train, with regular services from Helsinki and other major cities in Finland. Car rentals are also available for those who prefer to drive themselves.

How to Get Around in Laukaa

Laukaa is a small town, and most of the attractions are easily accessible on foot or by bike. Visitors can also rent a car or take a bus to explore the region.

Where to Go Next from Laukaa: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore more of Finland, some popular destinations near Laukaa include:

  • Jyvaskyla: A vibrant university town with a beautiful lakeside location
  • Tampere: A lively city known for its industrial heritage and vibrant food scene
  • Helsinki: The capital city of Finland, with stunning architecture, museums, and cultural attractions

Conclusion

Laukaa is a hidden gem in the heart of Finnish Lakeland, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and rich culture and heritage. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an action-packed adventure, Laukaa has something to offer. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has helped you plan your next trip to Laukaa. Happy travels!
