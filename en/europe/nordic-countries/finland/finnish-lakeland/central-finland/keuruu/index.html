Introducing Keuruu

Keuruu is a picturesque town located in Central Finland, part of the Finnish Lakeland region. Surrounded by beautiful forests and lakes, it offers stunning natural sceneries and a variety of outdoor activities for nature enthusiasts. The town also has a rich history and culture, with several museums and historical sites worth exploring. Whether you are looking for an active holiday or a relaxing getaway, Keuruu has something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will help you plan your trip and discover the best things to do, places to stay, and more in Keuruu.

List of Places to Visit in Keuruu

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The Old Church of Keuruu

Built-in 1756, The Old Church of Keuruu is a well-preserved wooden church that is a great example of Finnish church architecture. It is famous for its frescoes, painted by famous artist Mikael Toppelius.

Pihlajavesi Museum

Pihlajavesi Museum showcases the history of Keuruu and the surrounding region, from the Stone Age to modern times. Visitors can learn about the local customs and traditions, as well as see exhibits related to local agriculture and industry.

Jokilaaksojen Museo

The Jokilaaksojen Museum is an interactive museum that showcases the life and culture of Central Finland. It features several indoor and outdoor exhibits about local history, including a collection of historic buildings and farmsteads.

Natural Attractions of Keuruu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Keurusselka Lake

Keurusselka Lake is a popular spot for water activities, such as kayaking, boating, and fishing. The lake is also surrounded by beautiful forests and nature trails, making it a perfect spot for hiking and cycling.

Palsankallio Rock Park

Palsankallio Rock Park is a unique geological formation that offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. Visitors can hike through the park’s trails and appreciate the diverse flora and fauna.

Pihlajavesi Wilderness Area

The Pihlajavesi Wilderness Area is an unspoiled forest and lake landscape that is home to wolves and bears. It features several hiking trails, canoeing, and fishing spots, and it is a perfect place for nature enthusiasts to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Keuruu

Keuruu Hiking Trail

The Keuruu Hiking Trail is a 25-kilometer trail that runs around the Keuruu Lake. It offers stunning views of the lake and its surroundings, and it is a perfect place for a day hike or cycling.

Koskilinjail Walking Trail

The Koskilinjail Walking Trail is a 5-kilometer trail that takes you through the beautiful landscape of Pihlajavesi Lake. It features several picturesque sites, including the Kivitippu lookout point and the Ruokevaara nature trail.

Best Things to Do in Keuruu: Activities and Tours

Sauna Experience

The sauna is a quintessential Finnish experience, and Keuruu has several public and private saunas you can enjoy. Some of the best options in town include the Apaja Sauna and the Lomakyla Sieravuori.

Keuruu Music Festival

The Keuruu Music Festival takes place every summer and features a variety of music performances, from classical to contemporary. The festival is a great way to experience Finnish culture and meet locals.

Winter Activities

During winter months, Keuruu turns into a dreamland for those who enjoy winter activities. Snowshoeing, ice fishing, cross-country skiing, and ice skating are some of the popular activities you can enjoy in Keuruu.

Food and Drink Scene of Keuruu

Must-Try Dishes

Kalakukko

Kalakukko is a traditional Finnish dish that combines fish and pork in a rye bread crust. It is a unique and flavorful dish and is worth trying when in Keuruu.

Lohikeitto

Lohikeitto is a salmon soup and is a staple in the Finnish diet. It is a hearty and delicious soup that is perfect for cold winter days.

Local Beverages

Salmiakki Koskenkorva

Salmiakki Koskenkorva is a Finnish liquor that is flavored with salty licorice. It is a popular drink among locals and is a unique and flavorful drink.

Suomalainen Olut

Suomalainen Olut is a Finnish beer that is light and refreshing. It is a popular drink in Keuruu, and you can find it in most restaurants and bars.

Where to Stay in Keuruu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hotel Keurusselka

Hotel Keurusselka is a four-star hotel located by the Keurusselka Lake. It offers a variety of rooms and suites, and guests can enjoy several activities such as boating, fishing, and hiking.

Lomakyla Sieravuori

Lomakyla Sieravuori is a resort that offers a variety of accommodation options, from cottages to apartments. Guests can enjoy several activities, such as skiing, snowshoeing, and ice fishing.

Keuruu Hostel

Keuruu Hostel is a budget-friendly option that offers private rooms and dormitories. It is located in the heart of Keuruu and is a great option for travelers on a tight budget.

What to Buy in Keuruu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Handicrafts

Keuruu is known for its handicrafts, and you can find a variety of locally made products, such as wooden furniture, woolen blankets, and textiles.

Local Food Products

Local food products such as smoked fish, berry jams, and pickled vegetables are great souvenirs to bring home with you.

Local Culture and Customs of Keuruu

The people of Keuruu have a strong sense of community, and they enjoy celebrating their traditions and customs. Some of the cultural events you can experience in Keuruu include the St. Henry’s Feast, the Pihlaja Festival, and the Christmas Market.

Best Time to Visit Keuruu

The best time to visit Keuruu depends on what you are looking for. Summer months are perfect for outdoor activities, such as hiking, swimming, and boating. Winter months are great for winter sports and activities such as cross-country skiing and snowshoeing. Keuruu is also beautiful during the fall when the leaves turn into vibrant colors.

Do’s and Don’ts of Keuruu

Do:

  • Respect the Finnish culture and customs.
  • Try the local cuisine.
  • Enjoy the natural sceneries and outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Ignore the outdoor clothing rules in winter.
  • Overdo your drinking.

Safety and Health

Safety in Keuruu

Keuruu is generally safe and has a low crime rate. However, it is essential to take basic precautions, such as securing your valuables and avoiding poorly lit areas at night.

Health in Keuruu

Keuruu has several medical centers and pharmacies, and visitors can get medical care as needed. It is wise to have travel insurance that covers health issues of emergencies.

Getting to Keuruu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Keuruu

The nearest airport to Keuruu is Jyvaskyla Airport, which is about 60 kilometers away. You can also fly to Helsinki Airport and take a train or bus to Keuruu.

Bus Stations Near Keuruu

Keuruu has a bus station that connects the town with other major cities in Finland, such as Helsinki and Tampere. You can also take a long-distance bus to Keuruu from neighboring countries such as Estonia.

Other Options

You can also rent a car or a bike to explore Keuruu and its surroundings at your own pace.

How to Get Around in Keuruu

Keuruu is a small town, and it is easy to get around on foot or by bike. You can also use Local buses that operate in the city.

Where to Go Next from Keuruu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Jyvaskyla

Jyvaskyla is a vibrant city known for its festivals, outdoor activities, and beautiful architecture. You can take a day trip to Jyvaskyla and explore the city’s museums, shops, and restaurants.

Tampere

Tampere is a charming city located about 150 kilometers from Keuruu. It features several attractions, such as the Tampere Art Museum, the Pyynikki Observation Tower, and Särkänniemi Amusement Park.

Conclusion

Keuruu is a beautiful town that offers a variety of options for travelers. It combines stunning natural scenery, rich history, and culture, and it is a perfect destination for those seeking a peaceful getaway or a thrilling adventure. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has helped you plan your trip and discover what Keuruu has to offer.
