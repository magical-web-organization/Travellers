Introduction to Virrat

Virrat is situated in Pirkanmaa, in Finnish Lakeland, in Finland - a beautiful and serene destination with its unique nature and rich cultural heritage. If you are a lover of picturesque views, beautiful lakes, and well-preserved historical landmarks, then Virrat should be on top of your bucket list. This comprehensive travel guide will take you through some of the best things to do, sightseeing, food and drink scene, where to stay, local products and souvenirs to bring home, safety, and logistics of Virrat.

List of Places to Visit in Virrat

Virrat has many sightseeing destinations that you should explore during your visit. The following are some places you should not miss when you visit Virrat:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kohiseva Museum: Be immersed in the traditional Finnish way of living and farming practices and marvel at historical farming tools and equipment in this museum.
  • Kokko Museum: Kokko House is a traditional home dedicated to Marjatta and Matti Kokko, who are not only significant figures in the Finnish Evangelical Lutheran Church but also made a significant contribution in improving the spiritual life of Virrat and many other parts of the country as well.
  • Old Church Village: A well-preserved open-air museum in Virrat showcasing the local 18th century wooden architecture with its magnificent churches and chapels, exhibitions, and many ancient buildings.

Natural Attractions of Virrat: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Liedenpohja National Park: Go on a journey of adventure and exploration in a fantastic natural destination that features clean forests, scenic lake views, free-flowing rivers, and breathtaking scenery that will leave an impression on your mind.
  • Ähtävänjärvi Lake: This vast lake not only offers scenic views, but it also boasts quite a few villages like Ähtäri, which you could explore for a better experience in Finnish rural life.
  • Helvetinjärvi National Park: One of the must-visit national parks in Finland, with its deeply glaciated valleys, rugged outcrops, and tranquil pools that are perfect for swimming.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Virrat

Experiencing the lush green Finnish forests and scenic spectacular views by foot or bike is an incredible moment you won’t want to miss. If you’re looking for a challenging hike or a peaceful stroll, Virrat has a variety of trails for visitors of all abilities.

Some of the hiking and biking routes in Virrat include:

  • Helvetinjärvi National Park Hiking Trail: Enjoy the beautiful scenery of coves, hillsides, and the magnificent Pulkkilanharju-Ridge while hiking on this trail.
  • Isokyrö - Virrat Route: This is a cycling route that connects the two different municipalities of Virrat and Isokyrö.

Best Things to Do in Virrat: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of fun and enjoyable things to do in Virrat, making it a perfect destination for families, couples, and groups of friends. The following are some of the must-try activities and tours:

  • Camp Virmaila: Enjoy many outdoor activities such as boating, hiking, fishing and swimming in the camp Virmaila. With numerous campsites, camping services, and sauna facilities, you can make it an all-inclusive adventure.
  • Pirate Tour on Lake Keurusselkä: Experience an incredible adventure with the Pirate Tour on Lake Keurusselkä, where you’ll be transported by boat to a pirate island that includes treasure hunting and much more.

Food and Drink Scene of Virrat

Creating a travel itinerary is only half the fun; sampling the local cuisine and drinks is another fantastic experience and factor to consider before traveling. With Virrat, there are several must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Koskenlaskijasoppa: A traditional Finnish cheese-dumpling soup served with rye bread.
  • Kalakukko: A freshwater fish-filled bread that is a staple of the Finnish Lakeland cuisine.
  • Särä: A mouth-watering lamb dish cooked with potato and served hot with lingonberry jam.

Local Beverages

  • Ähtärin Vesi: A well-known bottled mineral water from the Ähtäri municipality in Virrat that contains natural minerals and salts for maximum refreshment.
  • Karjalan Kulta: A classic Finnish beer made with malted barley, barley, and hops with added spruce aroma for added flavor.

Where to Stay in Virrat: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Virrat offers a range of accommodation options that not only caters to almost every price point but styles as well. Here are some of the best accommodations options for your stay in Virrat:

  • Hotel Keurusharju: A stylish hotel located at the beautiful lake of Keurusselkä. The hotel features spacious rooms, indoor pools, gym facilities, and offers some stunning views of the lake and surrounding nature.
  • Aleksanderin Kappeli Hostels: A hostel situated in the Old Church Village of Virrat, surrounded by serene surroundings and nearby to abundant natural attractions.

What to Buy in Virrat: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

While in Virrat, you’ll find a lot of rewarding collectibles, souvenirs, and local products to bring back home. Here are some of our recommendations:

  • Virtain Siirappi Oy: A syrup factory where you can purchase unique flavors of siirappi, a traditional Finnish syrup.
  • Käsityöläismuseo Mylly: A Museum shop renowned for selling handmade traditional Finnish souvenirs, including wooden items, hand-knit socks, rugs, and gloves.

Local Culture and Customs of Virrat

Maintaining dense cultural ties is something the people of Virrat take pride in. They have unique customs and cultures that they follow to date, including traditional Finland farming practices and keeping heritage Finnish building structures.

Best Time to Visit Virrat

The best time to visit Virrat is during summer, which is between June and August. During this time, the weather is perfect for outdoor activities and exploration.

Do’s and Don’ts of Virrat

Do:

  • Respect Finnish customs and traditions.
  • Try out the local cuisine and drinks.
  • Take leisurely walks around the beautiful lakes, valleys and scenic destinations.

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave trash behind; adhere to the principles of responsible tourism.
  • Disturb wildlife and fauna while hiking.

Safety and Health

Virrat is generally safe for travelers, but some precautions should be taken when visiting the national parks or other outdoor areas.

Safety in Virrat

  • If you are hiking, always take a map, water, and appropriate gear suitable for the weather and terrain.
  • During the winter season, remember to dress appropriately and wear proper footwear to avoid slipping on ice.

Health in Virrat

Make sure that you have all the necessary vaccinations and health insurance coverage before traveling to Virrat. It is essential to carry your medications with you and enough supply to last for the entire trip.

Getting to Virrat: Transportation Options and Logistics

Virrat is well-connected with an efficient transportation system, making it easily accessible.

Airports Near Virrat

The nearest international airport to Virrat is Tampere Airport, located approximately 72km away.

Bus Stations Near Virrat

Virrat Bus Station is adjacent to City Hall, and it is served by Onnibus and Matkahuolto coaches, which travel in various directions.

Other Options

In Finland, travel by train and rent a car is also an available option for transportation.

How to Get Around in Virrat

Moving around in Virrat is relatively easy and not hectic for most travelers. You can rent bicycles and mopeds from Virrat as well as a taxi.

Where to Go Next from Virrat: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Apart from Virrat, there are some nearby destinations you can visit during your trip. Some of them include:

  • Ähtäri: Located 18km southeast of Virrat, Ähtäri is famous for the Ähtäri Zoo, which is the second-largest zoo in Finland, covering over 120ha.
  • Jyväskylä: A cultural hub located 100km east of Virrat, with historic museums, botanical gardens, and many other cultural and recreational activities.

Conclusion

Virrat is a charming and serene travel destination that is perfect for those seeking adventure, relaxation, and exploration. With its beautiful lakes, natural scenery, and rich cultural heritage, it is a destination worth exploring. Plan your visit today and experience all that Virrat has to offer.
