Introduction to Ruovesi

Ruovesi is a hidden gem located in Pirkanmaa, which is in the Finnish Lakeland region of Finland. It offers a perfect setting to enjoy Finland’s serene beauty. Away from the hustle-bustle of city life, this region offers an unparalleled opportunity to enjoy the pristine natural environment and explore the rich Finnish culture. It is a perfect destination for travelers looking for a peaceful vacation, surrounded by romantic landscapes, beautiful lakes, and forests.

List of Places to Visit in Ruovesi

When planning a trip to Ruovesi, knowing the top places to visit is a must. Here are some of the must-visit destinations in Ruovesi:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Ruovesi Old Church: This beautiful wooden church is over 200 years old and is considered to be one of the best-preserved churches of its time.

  • Herrala Estate: A historical manor house provides an exhibition of the medieval farming culture of Pirkanmaa and archaeological finds from different periods.

  • Eräjärvi Old Parsonage: The Old Parsonage of Eräjärvi is located near Lake Isojärvi National Park and provides visitors a glimpse into the historical life of the area.

Natural Attractions of Ruovesi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Rautavesi: This large lake is surrounded by stunning forests. It provides an opportunity for water activities such as boating, fishing, and swimming in summer. In winter, it is an excellent spot for ice fishing.

  • Pyhävuori: Pyhävuori is one of the highest points in Pirkanmaa and provides an amazing view of Lake Puulavesi and Pirkanmaa region. It is also an ideal hiking spot.

  • Lake Vihovuonteenjärvi: This beautiful lake is located in Isojärvi National Park and it provides visitors with an opportunity to enjoy swimming, fishing, and other water activities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ruovesi

Ruovesi is adorned with a plethora of beautiful hiking and biking routes. It is a heaven for outdoors activities. Here are some of the top ones:

  • The Pyhävuori Hiking Trail: This trail takes hikers to the top of Pyhävuori, through stunning forests and scenic lookouts.

  • The Pulkkilanharju Trail: This trail provides hikers with an opportunity to explore the unique geological formations of this region. The trail is also ideal for bird-watching and forest bathing.

Best Things to Do in Ruovesi: Activities and Tours

Ruovesi offers a lot of activities that cater to different tastes. Here are some of the best things to do in Ruovesi:

  • Canoeing in Isojärvi National Park: Isojärvi National Park is a perfect destination for those who love wildlife and canoeing. The park offers many different routes for canoeing, each highlighting different aspects of Finnish nature.

  • Horse Riding in Ruovesi: Ruovesi is a perfect spot for horse riding enthusiasts. There are several horse riding farms in the region that offer horse riding tours through various trails and beautiful sceneries.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ruovesi

If you are interested in spending an entire week in Ruovesi, here is an itinerary to consider:

Food and Drink Scene of Ruovesi

The Finnish cuisine has a unique flavor that you must try when visiting Ruovesi. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kalakukko: It is a traditional Finnish dish where fish is baked inside a rye dough. It is an excellent option for those who love fish.

  • Karjalanpaisti: It is a meat and vegetable stew that is made in cast iron pots. It is a perfect comfort food for cold weather.

Local Beverages

  • Lonkero (Long Drink): Lonkero is a popular Finnish alcoholic beverage that is made by combining gin and grapefruit soda. It is an excellent refreshing drink for summer.

  • Sahti: Sahti is a traditional Finnish beer that is made by fermenting rye and barley. It is a unique flavor that one must try when visiting Ruovesi.

Where to Stay in Ruovesi: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ruovesi has many accommodation options that suit different budgets:

  • Villa Pyhäniemi: This is a luxurious villa that overlooks Lake Pyhäjärvi and provides an excellent view of the surrounding forests.

  • Villa Haapsaari: It is another luxurious villa that is located on an island in Lake Längelmävesi. It provides an unparalleled private family vacation destination, with a large sauna and outdoor jacuzzi.

  • Jyväskylä Hostel: It is a budget-friendly hostel that provides clean and comfortable accommodation to budget-conscious travelers.

What to Buy in Ruovesi: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Ruovesi is known for its wood and other handicrafts:

  • Puutori: It is a local handicraft store that sells handmade wooden utensils, jewelry, knick-knacks, and other craft items.

  • Kokkomäen Puutarha: It is a local apple orchard that sells fresh apples, apple juice, local jams, and other apple-based products. They also provide apple cider tasting tours.

Local Culture and Customs of Ruovesi

Finland has a rich culture, and it is important to recognize the customs and norms of the region. Here are a few things to keep in mind when visiting Ruovesi:

  • Sauna Culture: Saunas are an integral part of Finnish culture, and Ruovesi has many public and private saunas.

  • Finnish Greetings: It is customary to greet people with a handshake or a nod. When departing, it is customary to say “hyvästi” (goodbye).

Best Time to Visit Ruovesi

The best time to visit Ruovesi is during the summer season (June to August) when the weather is mild, and you can enjoy various outdoor activities.

Don’t:

Safety and Health

The health system in Finland is top-notch, and it is safe to travel to Ruovesi. However, it is essential to keep the following in mind.

Safety in Ruovesi

  • Always carry a map and a phone when traveling.

  • It is essential to carry warm clothing as the temperature can drop suddenly.

Health in Ruovesi

  • Keep your required medications handy.

  • Use insect repellent to avoid mosquito bites.

How to Get Around in Ruovesi

The best way to get around in Ruovesi is by car or hired bike. There are also bus services available; however, they are limited to specific routes and times.

Where to Go Next from Ruovesi: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Ruovesi is an ideal starting point for exploring other destinations in Finland:

  • Tampere: It is an industrial city that offers an eclectic mix of culture, nature, and history.

  • Jyväskylä: It is a city that offers a beautiful blend of Finnish architecture, nature, and culture.

Conclusion

Ruovesi is an unexplored paradise in the Finnish Lakeland region that has a lot to offer to travelers. It is perfect for those who want to enjoy Finnish nature, culture, and cuisine in a serene and peaceful environment. A trip to Ruovesi is a must for anyone looking for a peaceful escape from city life.
