Introduction to Tavastia Proper

Tavastia Proper is situated in Southern Finland and is one of the country’s most beautiful regions. The area is known for its rich history, cultural landmarks, and beautiful scenery. Tavastia Proper boasts traditional towns, charming villages, and stunning natural beauty that is perfect for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers alike.

List of Places to Visit in Tavastia Proper

When visiting Tavastia Proper, there are several must-visit places that you need to add to your itinerary. These include:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Häme Castle: Built-in 1260, the Häme Castle is among the oldest buildings in Finland and an essential landmark of the country.
  • The Birthplace of Jean Sibelius: A National Museum of Finland, the birthplace of Jean Sibelius, is a charming museum dedicated to the life of the famous Finnish composer.
  • The Finnish Forest Museum Lusto: The Finnish Forest Museum Lusto showcases the history and traditions of Finland’s forestry and provides visitors with a fantastic insight into Finnish timber culture.

Natural Attractions of Tavastia Proper: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Torronsuo National Park: Torronsuo National Park is the perfect place for eco-tourists and adventurers. It is home to a variety of wildlife and a beautiful landscape filled with swamp forests, tranquil ponds, and meadows.
  • Evo Hiking Area: The Evo Hiking Area is one of Finland’s largest hiking areas, and it is open all year round, making it perfect for summer and winter activities.
  • Liesjärvi National Park: The Liesjärvi National Park is a beautiful destination for nature lovers. It has several trails that allow you to explore its rich flora and fauna.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tavastia Proper

Tavastia Proper is an excellent destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. The region is home to numerous hiking and biking routes, including:

  • The Velkua Trail: The Velkua Trail is a 7.2 km long circular trail that starts and ends in the village of Velkua. The trail takes you to the top of the scenic Keihäsmäki Hill and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding area.
  • The Tähtiniemi Nature Trail: The Tähtiniemi Nature Trail is 3.3 km long and takes you through the beautiful Tähtiniemi peninsula. The trail is famous for its birdlife and has several observation towers and bird watching stations.
  • The Evo Hiking Area Trails: The Evo hiking area has several trails that cater to hikers of all levels. There are short trails for beginners and longer routes for more advanced hikers.

Best Things to Do in Tavastia Proper: Activities and Tours

Tavastia Proper offers several activities and tours for visitors; some of the best include:

  • Rafting in Evo: Rafting in Evo is a thrilling experience that allows you to paddle down the beautiful River Evo. The adventure takes you through rapids and calm sections and provides an excellent opportunity to experience the region’s stunning landscapes.
  • Berry Picking Tours: Berry picking is a popular summer activity in Tavastia Proper. Several tour operators offer guided tours that allow visitors to pick berries and mushrooms while learning about Finnish folklore and traditions.
  • Boat Trips in Vanajavesi: Vanajavesi Lake is a popular destination for boat trips. The lake has several tour operators that offer guided tours and cruises that showcase the region’s beauty and history.

Food and Drink Scene of Tavastia Proper

Tavastia Proper has a rich and diverse food and drink scene. The region has something for everyone, including local specialties and international cuisine. Here’s a list of must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Karjalanpaisti: A traditional Finnish stew made from beef, pork, and root vegetables.
  • Riisipuuro: A Finnish rice pudding served with cinnamon and sugar.
  • Mämmi: A Finnish Easter dessert made from rye flour and sweetened with molasses.

Local Beverages

  • Sima: A traditional Finnish soft drink made from lemons, sugar, and yeast.
  • Lonkero: A Finnish cocktail made from gin and grapefruit soda.
  • Sahti: A traditional Finnish beer made from malted barley, rye, and oats.

Local Culture and Customs of Tavastia Proper

Tavastia Proper has a rich culture and history that spans many decades, and it is embodied in the region’s customs and traditions. Finnish Sauna is an important part of the local culture. Most Finnish people have a sauna in their homes, and they usually steam themselves before taking a dive in the icy waters of nearby lakes or rivers.
