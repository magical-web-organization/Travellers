Introduction to Iitti

Located in Päijänne Tavastia, in Southern Finland, Iitti is a charming town that has gained a reputation as a popular destination for nature lovers. This tranquil town is surrounded by beautiful landscapes that are perfect for outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, fishing, and skiing. Its rich cultural heritage, welcoming locals, and world-class facilities have made it an ideal spot for tourists looking to escape the chaos of city life.

List of Places to Visit in Iitti

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Iitti has a rich history and culture that is preserved in various museums and historical sites scattered throughout the town. The following are some of the key cultural attractions in Iitti:

Iitti Church

Built in the 15th century, Iitti Church is one of the oldest buildings in the town, and it is regarded as a masterpiece of medieval architecture. The church’s interior has been well-preserved and features intricate wood carvings and artwork.

Karkkila Ironworks

The Ironworks is a unique industrial complex that has been in operation since the 17th century. The complex features a blast furnace, a finery forge, and a bar iron rolling mill. It gives visitors an opportunity to learn about Finland’s industrial heritage.

Natural Attractions of Iitti: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The town of Iitti is blessed with some of the most stunning natural landscapes in the Nordic countries. Visitors can explore the following natural attractions:

Lapakisto Nature Reserve

Lapakisto Nature Reserve is a hiker’s paradise, with several trails that crisscross the park. The park is home to several plant and animal species and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.

Iitti Ski Centre

For skiing enthusiasts, Iitti Ski Centre is a must-visit destination. Located in the middle of a dense forest, the centre offers fantastic slopes for both beginners and experienced skiers.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Iitti

Iitti has an extensive network of biking and hiking trails that will take you through some of the most beautiful natural landscapes in the area. The most popular routes include:

  • Lapakisto Trail: a 7km circular trail that takes visitors through the stunning Lapakisto Nature Reserve.
  • Päijänne Valtatie Cycle Route: a 62 km cycle route that runs from Lahti to Heinola via Iitti.

Best Things to Do in Iitti: Activities and Tours

Iitti has several activities and tours that cater to all age groups. The following are some of the best things to do in Iitti:

Wildlife Safaris

If you’re looking to experience Finland’s wildlife up close, wildlife safaris are an ideal activity for you. The tours take visitors deep into the forest, and they will have a chance to see elk, lynx, and other wildlife species.

Fishing

Iitti is home to several lakes, and fishing is a popular activity in the area. Visitors can fish for various fish species such as pike, perch, and trout.

Food and Drink Scene of Iitti

Must-Try Dishes

Iitti has a rich food scene, with several local delicacies to try. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • Kalakukko: a traditional loaf of bread filled with fish and pork.
  • Lihamureke: a meatloaf made from ground pork and beef.
  • Marjapiirakka: a traditional Finnish berry tart.

Local Beverages

The locals of Iitti are proud of their local brews. Here are some of the popular local drinks:

  • Koskenkorva Vodka: a Finnish vodka made from barley.
  • Karjalan Olut: a Finnish beer brewed with traditional methods.
  • Laitilan Wirvoitusjuomatehdas: a craft brewery and distillery that makes various beers, ciders, and spirits.

Where to Stay in Iitti: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Iitti has several accommodation options that cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the popular places to stay:

  • Kumpeli Spa: a luxury spa hotel that offers various treatments and activities.
  • Lomakeskus Korpikoski: a rustic cottage resort located in a beautiful forest setting.
  • Kouvolan Kylpylä: a family-friendly spa hotel that features a water park and other recreational facilities.

What to Buy in Iitti: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Iitti has several local products and souvenirs that visitors can bring home as mementoes of their trip. Here are some of the popular products to buy:

  • Iittala Glass: a world-renowned Finnish glassware brand that has its roots in Iitti.
  • Lapuan Kankurit: a textile company that produces high-quality linen products.
  • Karjalan Kivi: a company that makes unique stone products, such as vases and candleholders.

Local Culture and Customs of Iitti

Iitti has a rich cultural heritage that involves several traditions and customs. The locals are known for their hospitality and friendliness towards visitors. When visiting Iitti, it is recommended to respect the locals’ customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Iitti

The best time to visit Iitti is during the summer months (June to August) when the weather is warm, and the nature is at its best. During this period, visitors can enjoy various outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, and cycling.

Do’s and Don’ts of Iitti

Do:

  • Respect the locals’ customs and traditions.
  • Explore the town on foot.
  • Try the local delicacies.

Don’t:

  • Litter in the natural environments.
  • Be loud and disruptive.

Safety and Health

Safety in Iitti

Iitti is a safe town to visit, but visitors should take the usual precautions to keep safe. It is advisable to keep an eye on personal belongings and avoid walking alone late at night.

Health in Iitti

Finland has an excellent public healthcare system, and visitors can access medical care in case of an emergency. It is advisable to have travel insurance that includes medical coverage.

Getting to Iitti: Transportation Options and Logistics

Iitti is located approximately 70 km from Helsinki, and visitors can access the town through the following transportation options:

Airports Near Iitti

The closest international airport to Iitti is Helsinki Airport, which is approximately 100 km away from the town.

Bus Stations Near Iitti

Visitors can take a bus to Iitti from Helsinki or other nearby towns. The buses are regular, and the trip takes approximately one hour.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a car and drive to Iitti. The drive takes approximately one hour from Helsinki.

How to Get Around in Iitti

Iitti is a small town, and visitors can easily explore it on foot. Biking is also a popular mode of transport, and visitors can rent bikes from several rental shops in town.

Where to Go Next from Iitti: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can explore several nearby destinations from Iitti. The following are some of the popular places to visit:

  • Lahti: a vibrant city located approximately 35 km south of Iitti.
  • Porvoo: a charming coastal town located approximately 70 km west of Iitti.
  • Kouvola: a town located approximately 50 km east of Iitti, known for its natural beauty and industrial heritage.

Conclusion

Iitti is a town that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and world-class facilities. Visitors can enjoy various outdoor activities, explore historical sites and museums, and sample the local delicacies. With its welcoming locals and laid-back atmosphere, Iitti is a perfect destination for anyone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.
