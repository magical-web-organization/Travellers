Introduction to Nurmijärvi

Nurmijärvi is a municipality located in the Uusimaa region of Southern Finland. The municipality is known for its pristine natural surroundings, cultural attractions, and outdoor activities. It is a perfect destination for those who are looking to escape the city life and immerse themselves in nature.

In this travel guide, we will provide you with a detailed overview of the best things to do in Nurmijärvi, including the top cultural and natural attractions, the best hiking and biking routes, and the local food and drink scene. We have also included information about the local culture, safety tips, and how to get around.

List of Places to Visit in Nurmijärvi

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Nurmijärvi Museum

The Nurmijärvi Museum takes visitors on a journey through the past of Nurmijärvi. The museum has several exhibitions that showcase the local history, including its agriculture, industry, and everyday life.

Pyhäkallio Stone Age Center

The Pyhäkallio Stone Age Center is an amazing place to learn about the Stone Age history of Finland. The center offers guided tours, where visitors can see how people lived and survived during that era. It is an excellent educational experience for both children and adults.

Natural Attractions of Nurmijärvi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Nuuksio National Park

Nurmijärvi is home to the beautiful Nuuksio National Park, which offers visitors an array of outdoor activities such as hiking, fishing, and skiing. The park’s scenic views of the lakes, forests, and wildlife make for an unforgettable experience.

Pirttimäki Frisbee Golf

Frisbee golf is a popular activity in Nurmijärvi, and Pirttimäki is a great place to play. It offers players a challenging course, beautiful scenery, and a great time.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Nurmijärvi

Paikkari-Pirttimäki Trail

This trail is perfect for hikers and bikers and offers beautiful views of the surrounding nature. It mostly passes through forests and around small lakes and is suitable for all skill levels.

Nuuksio National Park Trails

Nuuksio National Park has several scenic trails to choose from, ranging from easy walking paths to more challenging hikes. The park’s diverse terrain and wildlife make for an unforgettable hiking experience.

Best Things to Do in Nurmijärvi: Activities and Tours

Nurmijärvi Summer Theater

The Nurmijärvi Summer Theater is a must-see attraction for theater enthusiasts. The theater hosts a variety of shows, ranging from comedy to drama, and is a great way to spend an evening in Nurmijärvi.

Nurmijärvi Music Festival

The Nurmijärvi Music Festival is a popular event among locals and tourists alike. It offers a wide range of music genres, from classical to pop, and is a great way to experience the local cultural scene.

Food and Drink Scene of Nurmijärvi

Must-Try Dishes

Karjalanpaisti

Karjalanpaisti is a traditional Finnish dish, which consists of beef or pork stew, vegetables, and potatoes. It is a must-try dish for anyone visiting Nurmijärvi.

Salmiakki

Salmiakki is a popular Finnish candy, which has a unique salty taste. It is a must-try for those who love savory treats.

Local Beverages

Olvi

Olvi is a local Finnish beer, which is brewed in Lahti, not far from Nurmijärvi. It is a refreshing and delicious drink that pairs well with any Finnish meal.

Sima

Sima is a traditional Finnish drink, which is made with fermented honey. It has a light, sweet taste and is a great non-alcoholic option for those who want to sample local flavors.

Where to Stay in Nurmijärvi: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Nurmijärvi offers various accommodation options for visitors, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels.

Hotel Krapi

Hotel Krapi is a luxury hotel located in the heart of Nurmijärvi. It has comfortable rooms, a spa, and a restaurant, making it a perfect choice for those who want to experience the best that Nurmijärvi has to offer.

Kivilampi Guesthouse

Kivilampi Guesthouse is a budget-friendly option for travelers who want to experience the local culture. The guesthouse has comfortable rooms, a restaurant, and access to several outdoor activities.

What to Buy in Nurmijärvi: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Finnish Sauna Products

Finnish saunas are famous around the world, and Nurmijärvi offers excellent sauna products. Visitors can buy high-quality soaps, scrubs, and other sauna essentials to take a piece of Nurmijärvi home with them.

Finnish Clothing and Accessories

Visitors can shop for traditional Finnish clothing and accessories, such as the famous Sisu socks. These socks are designed to keep feet warm in extreme temperatures and are a great gift for family and friends.

Local Culture and Customs of Nurmijärvi

Nurmijärvi culture is heavily influenced by Finland’s rural history. Locals are friendly and welcoming, and visitors should respect local customs and traditions. It is advisable always to take off shoes when entering someone’s house and always thank the hosts for their hospitality.

Best Time to Visit Nurmijärvi

The best time to visit Nurmijärvi is during the summer months, from June to August. The weather is mild, and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy. Visitors who want to experience winter sports can visit between December and February when the weather is perfect for skiing and sledding.

Do’s and Don’ts of Nurmijärvi

Do:

  • Respect local culture and customs
  • Try the local food and drinks
  • Enjoy the outdoor activities

Don’t:

  • Ignore safety warnings in National Parks
  • Litter or damage the natural surroundings
  • Disrespect locals or their culture

Safety and Health

Safety in Nurmijärvi

Nurmijärvi is a safe place to visit. Visitors should take usual precautions, such as locking doors and windows when leaving their accommodation and not leaving valuables in their cars when parked.

Health in Nurmijärvi

Nurmijärvi has excellent medical facilities, including hospitals and pharmacies. Visitors should have travel insurance that covers medical emergencies.

Getting to Nurmijärvi: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Nurmijärvi

Helsinki-Vantaa Airport is the nearest international airport to Nurmijärvi, which is around 36km away. It has a good public transportation connection from Helsinki center to Nurmijärvi by bus.

Bus Stations Near Nurmijärvi

There is a bus station in Klaukkala which can be used to reach many nearby towns. There are regular buses from Helsinki center as well.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car at the airport or in Helsinki, which is a great way to explore Nurmijärvi and its surroundings at their own pace.

How to Get Around in Nurmijärvi

Nurmijärvi has an excellent public transportation network that includes buses and trains. Visitors can also rent a car or bike to explore the local area.

Where to Go Next from Nurmijärvi: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Helsinki

Helsinki, Finland’s capital, is only a short drive away from Nurmijärvi. It offers visitors a variety of historical and cultural attractions, as well as excellent shopping and dining options.

Espoo

Espoo is another nearby city, which is known for its natural beauty and outdoor activities. Visitors can enjoy hiking and biking trails, as well as cultural events throughout the year.

Conclusion

Nurmijärvi is an excellent destination for those who want to experience Finland’s beautiful natural surroundings and unique culture. With a multitude of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and excellent dining options, visitors will have a truly unforgettable experience.
