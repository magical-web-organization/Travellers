Introduction to Hanstholm

Hanstholm, located in the North Jutland region of Denmark, is a hidden gem that you wouldn’t want to miss. Renowned for its natural beauty and stunning sea views over the Atlantic, it presents a perfect example of an idyllic Danish coastal town. A place that, if you visit once, will forever be etched in your memory. From its dramatic beaches to its historical landmarks, Hanstholm is guaranteed to leave you surprised and enthralled.

List of Places to Visit in Hanstholm

A comprehensive travel guide for Hanstholm would be incomplete without a list of things to see and do in this beautiful coastal town. Let’s start with some of the most popular attractions in the town:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Hanstholm is home to a range of cultural attractions that reflect the town’s history and heritage. Here are some of the must-visit museums and historical places:

  • Hanstholm Lighthouse: It is one of the most popular landmarks in the town. This historical lighthouse was constructed in 1843 and is known for its amazing views of the sea and surrounding areas. Climb to the top for magnificent views of the North Sea and the Hanstholm harbor.

  • Hanstholm Bunker Museum: This is a fascinating museum that showcases the history of the German occupation of Denmark during WWII. It is situated in a WW2 bunker, which provides an unparalleled experience.

  • Hanstholm Fish Auction Hall: One of the largest in Europe, the Hanstholm Fish Auction Hall is an iconic landmark in the town. Visitors can observe the auction of fresh seafood and learn about the local fishing industry.

Natural Attractions of Hanstholm: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Hanstholm is endowed with natural beauty that will surely leave you breathless. Here are some of the must-visit natural attractions:

  • Hanstholm Nature Reserve, located next to the harbor. Explore the hiking and biking trails, the wildflower meadows, and the diverse fauna.

  • Hanstholm Beaches: Whether you are looking for a tranquil environment or some beachside fun, Hanstholm has it all. Hanstholm Beach, Lild Strand, and Klitmøller Beach are some of the most popular beaches.

  • Bulbjerg: This cliff standing a majestic 47 meters above sea level offers a sweeping view of the North Sea. The cliff tops are home to a wide range of flora and fauna worth exploring.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hanstholm

One of the best ways to explore Hanstholm is by hiking or biking. The town boasts of many scenic trails that offer an unobstructed view of the surroundings. Some of the popular hiking/biking routes in Hanstholm are:

  • Hanstholm-Stenbjerg Land route: A 30km route that stretches through coastal areas and nature reserves. It offers a scenic ride that passes through Lodbjerg Lighthouse, Bulbjerg, and Stenbjerg dune plantations.

  • Hanstholm Strandsti: This is a route that follows the coastline and offers breathtaking views of the North Sea. The route passes through Junget Plantage and is suitable for both hiking and biking.

Best Things to Do in Hanstholm: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, Hanstholm offers a plethora of activities and tours that will keep you engaged throughout your stay. Here are some of the must-do activities and tours in Hanstholm:

  • Surfing: Hanstholm is a popular surfing spot that attracts surfers from all over the world. Join a surfing class or rent a board and hit the waves.

  • Fishing: Hanstholm is known for its strong fishing culture. Join a fishing tour and try your hand at catching some of the local fish.

  • Whiskey Tasting: Visit NORDEX Whiskey Distillery and take a tour of their production facilities. You can sample some of their whiskeys and learn about the whiskey-making process.

Week-Long Itinerary for Hanstholm

If you have a week to spend in Hanstholm, there are many things that you can see and do. Here is a week-long itinerary that covers some of the top attractions in the town:

Day 1: Visit the Hanstholm Lighthouse, and the Bunker Museum. Day 2: Go for a biking/hiking trip at Hanstholm Nature Reserve. Day 3: Spend a day on one of Hanstholm’s beautiful beaches. Day 4: Visit the Nordex Whiskey Distillery and take a whiskey tasting tour. Day 5: Go on a fishing excursion and catch your dinner. Day 6: Visit the Hanstholm Fish Auction Hall and join an auction tour. Day 7: Explore the town’s market, enjoy local street food, and buy some souvenirs.

Food and Drink Scene of Hanstholm

Hanstholm prides itself on its culinary traditions, and there are many dishes and drinks worth exploring. Here are some of our favorites:

Must-Try Dishes

  • “Røgede Rødspætter” (Smoked Plaice): This is a classic local dish that features freshly caught plaice smoked in traditional smoking ovens for hours.

  • “Fiskefilet med remoulade”: This dish comprises freshly fried fish with a dollop of tangy remoulade sauce, served in a crispy bun.

Local Beverages

  • Nordex Whiskey: Visit the distillery and sample some of their locally-made whiskeys.

  • Mjød: It is a traditional Danish beverage made from fermented honey, water, and yeast. The town’s market is a great place to find some good ones.

Where to Stay in Hanstholm: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

From luxurious hotels to budget-friendly options, Hanstholm boasts a range of accommodation options that cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best options:

  • Hotel Hanstholm: A luxurious hotel located in close proximity to the Hanstholm Lighthouse.

  • Hanstholm Hostel: This is a budget-friendly option that offers clean and comfortable rooms and is located near the Hanstholm beach.

  • Hanstholm Guesthouse: This is a charming guesthouse that offers excellent views of the North Sea and is located close to many of Hanstholm’s attractions.

What to Buy in Hanstholm: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

There are many things that you can buy in Hanstholm, from traditional handicrafts to locally made food and drink products. Here are some of the best souvenirs to bring home:

  • Salted licorice: Denmark is famous for its salted licorice, and you can find some of the best ones in Hanstholm.

  • Handmade pottery: Visit some of the local shops and find some traditional and modern pottery.

Local Culture and Customs of Hanstholm

As a coastal town with a strong fishing culture, Hanstholm has unique customs and traditions. Tipping is neither expected nor necessary, and locals expect visitors to be friendly and respectful. The town is predominantly Lutheran, and visitors are welcome to join local services.

Best Time to Visit Hanstholm

The best time to visit Hanstholm is during the summer months, from June to August. This is when the weather is warm and perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hanstholm

Do:

  • Respect the town’s natural beauty and leave no trace.
  • Try the local cuisine and drinks.
  • Explore the hiking and biking trails.

Don’t:

  • Litter the town’s streets and beaches.
  • Swim in dangerous areas and obey safety signs.
  • Disturb the town’s fishing industry.

Safety and Health

Safety in Hanstholm

Hanstholm is a relatively safe town, and crime rates are low. However, it is advisable to keep your belongings safe when in public areas.

Health in Hanstholm

The town has various health facilities, including hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies. However, it is advisable to have comprehensive travel insurance before visiting Hanstholm.

Getting to Hanstholm: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Hanstholm

The closest airport is Aalborg Airport, which is about 76km from Hanstholm. From there, visitors can hire a car or take a bus to Hanstholm.

Bus Stations Near Hanstholm

Hanstholm has a bus station located in the town center, and visitors can take a bus from Aalborg, Copenhagen, and other major towns in Denmark.

Other Options

Visitors can also take a train to Thisted and then take a bus or taxi to Hanstholm.

How to Get Around in Hanstholm

The best way to get around Hanstholm is by foot, bike, or car. Visitors can rent bikes and cars from trusted vendors at reasonable prices.

Where to Go Next from Hanstholm: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you want to explore nearby destinations or go on an excursion, here are some of the best options:

  • Bulbjerg: Visit the majestic cliff tops and enjoy breathtaking views of the North Sea.

  • Thisted: Explore the historic town, with its charming old buildings, museums, and parks.

Conclusion

Hanstholm has a unique charm that will leave you wanting more. From its natural beauty to its rich culture, there is always something to explore and experience. This comprehensive travel guide should help you make the most of your stay in the town. So, pack your bags and get ready to fall in love with Hanstholm.
