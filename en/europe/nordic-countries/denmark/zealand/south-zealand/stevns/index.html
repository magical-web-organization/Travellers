Introduction to Stevns

Located in South Zealand in Denmark, Stevns is a region known for its beautiful coastline, natural attractions, and rich history. From the chalk cliffs to the sandy beaches, there’s something for everyone in Stevns. This travel guide will provide you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Stevns, from the best places to visit to the local culture and customs.

List of Places to Visit in Stevns

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Stevns Museum

Stevns Museum is a great place to start your visit to the area. The museum tells the story of Stevns from prehistoric times to the present day. You’ll learn about the geology of the area, the Viking Age, and the impact of World War II. The museum is housed in a 17th-century farmhouse and there are plenty of interactive exhibits for children.

Stevns Klint

Stevns Klint is a 17 km long stretch of chalk cliffs that has been designated a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Formed over 70 million years ago, the cliffs are home to a variety of wildlife, including rare birds, plants, and insects. The area is also home to some of the best-preserved fossils from the Cretaceous Period.

The Cold War Museum

The Cold War Museum is located in a former missile bunker and tells the story of Denmark’s role in the Cold War. You’ll learn about the history of NATO and the Warsaw Pact, as well as the impact of the Cold War on Denmark. The museum is unique and provides an interesting and informative perspective on recent history.

Natural Attractions of Stevns: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Stevns Fyr

Stevns Fyr is a lighthouse located on the eastern end of Stevns. The lighthouse was built in 1878 and provides a spectacular view of the coastline and the Baltic Sea. You can climb to the top of the lighthouse for a small fee and take in the views from the observation deck.

Hedeland

Hedeland is a large park located in the northwestern part of Stevns. The park covers over 15 km² and offers a variety of recreational activities, including hiking, biking, fishing, and more. There are also several lakes within the park that are great for picnics or just relaxing by the water.

Koldkrigsmuseum Stevnsfort

Koldkrigsmuseum Stevnsfort is a Cold War era fortress that has been turned into a museum. The museum tells the story of Denmark’s role in the Cold War and provides visitors with a unique opportunity to explore a former military installation. The fortress is surrounded by a moat and offers great views of the surrounding countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Stevns

Stevns Klint Stier

The Stevns Klint Stier is a network of hiking trails that run along the top of the chalk cliffs. The trails offer stunning views of the Baltic Sea and the surrounding countryside. The trails range in difficulty from easy to challenging and there are plenty of opportunities for picnics and rest stops along the way.

Møns Klint to Stevns Klint

The Møns Klint to Stevns Klint trail is a challenging hike that takes you from the chalk cliffs of Møn to the cliffs of Stevns. The trail covers a distance of over 40 km and takes two to three days to complete. Along the way, you’ll see some of Denmark’s most stunning landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Stevns: Activities and Tours

Canoeing on Stevnsån

Stevnsån is a river that runs through Stevns and is a great place for canoeing. You can rent canoes and kayaks from several places in the area and explore the river at your own pace. The river is calm and peaceful, making it a great activity for families and nature lovers.

Stevns Naturcenter

Stevns Naturcenter is a great place to learn about the natural history of the area. The center offers a variety of exhibits and interactive displays, as well as guided tours of the surrounding nature reserve. You’ll learn about the flora and fauna of the area and gain a deeper understanding of the importance of conservation.

Food and Drink Scene of Stevns

Must-Try Dishes

Stevns Pølsevogn

Stevns Pølsevogn is a hot dog stand that has been serving locals since 1967. The stand is located in the town of Strøby and is a must-visit for anyone looking to try traditional Danish hot dogs.

Stevnsbjerghus

Stevnsbjerghus is a restaurant located on the top of Stevns Klint. The restaurant offers spectacular views of the Baltic Sea and serves traditional Danish dishes made from locally sourced ingredients. The menu changes seasonally, so there’s always something new to try.

Local Beverages

Stevns Bryghus

Stevns Bryghus is a microbrewery located in the town of Hårlev. The brewery produces a variety of beers, including IPAs, stouts, and lagers. You can visit the brewery for a tour and tasting or find their beers at local bars and restaurants.

Stevns Æblemost

Stevns Æblemost is a local apple juice producer that uses only locally grown apples. The juice is available at local markets and can be found in many restaurants and cafes in the area.

Where to Stay in Stevns: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Stevns Klint Strandpension

Stevns Klint Strandpension is a guesthouse located just a few steps from the beach. The guesthouse offers comfortable rooms with ocean views and a restaurant that serves traditional Danish dishes.

Zleep Hotel Præstø

Zleep Hotel Præstø is a modern hotel located in the town of Præstø. The hotel offers comfortable rooms, free Wi-Fi, and a breakfast buffet. The hotel is a great base for exploring the area and is just a short drive from Stevns Klint.

What to Buy in Stevns: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Stevns Klint Souvenirs

There are several gift shops near Stevns Klint that sell souvenirs and local products. You can find everything from postcards and t-shirts to fossils and jewelry.

Stevns Honning

Stevns Honning is a local honey producer that has been in business since 1934. The honey is made from locally sourced flowers and is available at local markets and gift shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Stevns

Stevns is a region that is proud of its culture and heritage. The area has a rich history that is evident in the architecture, cuisine, and traditions of the locals. Visitors to Stevns are encouraged to learn about the local customs and to respect the traditions of the area.

Best Time to Visit Stevns

The best time to visit Stevns is from May to September when the weather is warm and sunny. This is also the peak tourist season, so be prepared for larger crowds and higher prices. If you’re looking to avoid the crowds, consider visiting in the spring or fall when the weather is still mild but the crowds have thinned out.

Safety and Health

Safety in Stevns

Stevns is a safe destination for travelers. However, it’s always a good idea to take normal precautions, such as locking your valuables in a safe and not leaving them unattended in public places. When hiking or exploring the natural areas of Stevns, be sure to stay on marked trails and obey any signage.

Health in Stevns

There are no specific health risks associated with traveling to Stevns. However, it’s always a good idea to carry a basic first aid kit and to be up to date on any necessary vaccinations.

Getting to Stevns: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Stevns

The closest airport to Stevns is Copenhagen Airport, which is located approximately 61 km northeast of the region. From the airport, you can take a train or bus to Stevns.

Bus Stations Near Stevns

There are several bus stations near Stevns, including in the towns of Store Heddinge and Hårlev. You can take a bus from Copenhagen and other major cities in Denmark to these stations.

Other Options

You can also reach Stevns by car or bike. There are several rental car companies in Copenhagen and other major cities in Denmark. Bikes can also be rented in Copenhagen and other towns along the coast.

How to Get Around in Stevns

The best way to get around Stevns is by car or bike. You can rent a car or bike from several places in the area or bring your own. Public transportation is also available, but the schedules may be limited, especially on weekends and holidays.

Where to Go Next from Stevns: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

The Island of Møn

The Island of Møn is located just across the water from Stevns and is known for its stunning landscapes and natural beauty. The island is home to Møns Klint, another UNESCO World Heritage Site, as well as several small villages and towns.

Copenhagen

Copenhagen is the capital city of Denmark and is located approximately 62 km northeast of Stevns. The city is known for its culture, history, and food scene. You can easily reach Copenhagen by car, bus, or train from Stevns.

Conclusion

Stevns is a region of Denmark that offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. From the chalk cliffs to the historic museums, there’s something for everyone in Stevns. If you’re looking for an off the beaten path destination in Denmark, Stevns should definitely be on your list.
