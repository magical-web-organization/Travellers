Introduction to Listed

Listed is a small town located on the Danish island of Bornholm in the Baltic Sea. Known for its quaint charm, Listed attracts visitors with its beautiful natural scenery, historic sites, and lively cultural scene. If you’re planning a trip to Denmark, Listed is definitely worth a visit.

List of Places to Visit in Listed

Here’s a list of some of the must-see places in Listed:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Oluf Høst Museum: This art museum is dedicated to the works of Danish painter Oluf Høst. It’s located in his former home in Listed and showcases his paintings, sketches, and drawings.

  • Listed Church: Built in the 14th century, Listed Church is a stunning example of Gothic architecture. It’s still in use today and worth a visit for its beautiful stained glass windows and other interior decorations.

  • The Old Merchants’ Courtyard: This historic courtyard dates back to the 17th century and is now home to several restaurants and shops. It’s a great place to grab a bite or do some shopping while admiring the old architecture.

Natural Attractions of Listed: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Helligdomsklipperne: Located just outside Listed, Helligdomsklipperne is a collection of cliff faces and rocky outcroppings. It’s a great place for a scenic hike or picnic and offers breathtaking views of the Baltic Sea.

  • Ekkodalen: This valley is located in the interior of Bornholm and is home to a series of natural springs and waterfalls. It’s a popular spot for hiking and birdwatching.

  • Dueodde Beach: This sandy beach stretches for several kilometers along the southern coast of Bornholm. It’s a great place for swimming, sunbathing, or just taking a relaxing walk along the shore.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Listed

Listed and the surrounding area offer a great variety of hiking and biking routes. Some popular options include:

  • The Bornholm Coast Path: This trail stretches for 120 kilometers along the coastline of Bornholm. It offers stunning views of the sea and the rocky cliffs that line the shore.

  • The Almindingen Forest: Located in the interior of Bornholm, the Almindingen Forest is a great place for a peaceful hike or bike ride. It’s home to several small lakes and is especially beautiful in the fall when the leaves change color.

  • The Hammershus Loop: This 15-kilometer loop takes you through the picturesque countryside of northern Bornholm. It passes through several small villages, and the highlight is a visit to the historic Hammershus Castle.

Best Things to Do in Listed: Activities and Tours

  • Take a guided tour of the Oluf Høst Museum: Learn about the life and works of Danish painter Oluf Høst on a guided tour of his former home and museum.

  • Visit the Bornholm Art Museum: Located in the town of Bornholm, this museum features a collection of Scandinavian art from the 19th and 20th centuries.

  • Attend a concert or cultural event in Listed: Throughout the year, Listed hosts a variety of festivals, concerts, and cultural events that showcase the local arts scene.

Food and Drink Scene of Listed

No trip to Listed would be complete without sampling some of the local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Smörrebröd: This classic Danish dish is a type of open-faced sandwich. There are many variations, but some popular toppings include pickled herring, egg salad, and roast beef.

  • Fried herring: This popular dish consists of breaded and fried herring served with a side of remoulade sauce.

  • Røget sild: This smoked herring is a staple of the Nordic diet and is often served cold with a side of rye bread.

Local Beverages

  • Aalborg Akvavit: This type of aquavit is a distilled spirit made from caraway seeds and other spices. It’s often served as a digestif and is a popular drink in Denmark.

  • Carlsberg Beer: This internationally renowned beer has been brewed in Denmark since 1847 and is a staple of Danish culture.

  • Bornholmer Bryg: This locally brewed beer is made on the island of Bornholm and is known for its crisp and refreshing taste.

Where to Stay in Listed: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Listed offers a variety of options for accommodations, from cozy guesthouses to luxury hotels. Some popular choices include:

  • Hotel Friheden: This hotel is located just outside Listed and offers comfortable rooms, a restaurant, and bike rental services.

  • Stammershalle Badehotel: This upscale hotel is located on the coast of Bornholm and offers stunning views of the sea. It’s also home to a popular restaurant that serves traditional Danish cuisine.

  • Kittebjergsskoven Hostel: This budget-friendly hostel is located in the interior of Bornholm and is a great choice for travelers who want to explore the island’s natural beauty.

Local Culture and Customs of Listed

As with any trip to a foreign country, it’s important to be aware of the local culture and customs. Here are a few things to keep in mind when visiting Listed:

  • Tipping: Tipping is not mandatory in Denmark, but it’s common to leave a small tip for good service.

  • Dress code: Danes tend to dress conservatively, so it’s a good idea to avoid revealing clothing when visiting historic sites or churches.

  • Language: Danish is the official language in Denmark, but most Danes speak English fluently.

Best Time to Visit Listed

Getting to Listed: Transportation Options and Logistics

Listed is located on the island of Bornholm, so getting there requires some planning. Here are a few transportation options:

Bus Stations Near Listed
