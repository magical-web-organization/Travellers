Nexø, a small town located on the island of Bornholm in Denmark, is a hidden gem that has plenty to offer travelers looking for unique outdoor experiences, natural beauty, and cultural attractions. Famous for its rich marine heritage, historic landmarks, and beautiful landscape, Nexø is a must-visit destination in Denmark.

Introduction to Nexø

Located on the eastern shore of Bornholm, Nexø is the second-largest town on the island with a local population of about 3,600 residents. The town’s picturesque harbor is its main attraction, and it is still one of the busiest fishing ports in Denmark. Nexø has an abundance of natural beauty with rolling hills, forests, and stunning sandy beaches. It is the perfect destination for travelers who love outdoor activities, including hiking, cycling, and kayaking.

List of Places to Visit in Nexø

Nexø has a lot of exciting places to visit that cater to all interests, from historical landmarks to beautiful outdoor spots. Here is a list of noteworthy attractions to check out when visiting Nexø:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Nexø Museum of Fisheries and Shipping: Discover the town’s rich marine heritage and learn about how it developed over the years.
  • Christiansø Island: Explore the historic fortress located on the small island in the Baltic Sea. It is a UNESCO World Heritage site since 2015.
  • Bornholm Art Museum: Experience the cultural diversity of Bornholm, one of Denmark’s most creative towns, with unique exhibits displaying the work of local artists.
  • Hammershus Castle: Located on the northern tip of Bornholm, this impressive castle ruins are a significant attraction on the island and offer beautiful views of the sea.

Natural Attractions of Nexø: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Snogebæk Beach: A beautiful section of the sandy beach where you can enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and other water activities.
  • Nexø woods: A forest in the town’s borders that is perfect for hiking and exploration of the local flora and fauna.
  • Hammerknuden: A nature reservation with spectacular scenery, hiking trails, and birdwatching opportunities.
  • Dueodde Beach: A popular beach for tourists located in the southern part of Bornholm, offering crystal clear waters and soft white sand.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Nexø

Nexø is the perfect destination for travelers who love outdoor activities such as hiking and cycling. With its rolling hills, stunning countryside and beautiful coastline, it offers an abundance of hiking and biking trails that suit all levels of ability.

  • Bornholm Rundt: A 120 km long trail around the island of Bornholm that leads in- and outside of Nexø.
  • Sømarken: A hiking trail that takes you through the forest and dunes to reach Sømarken, a beautiful beach in Nexø.
  • Nexø Woods: A forest hike popular with locals that offers a glimpse at the beautiful flora and fauna of the area.
  • Hammerknuden: Located at the northern part of Bornholm, it offers a diverse range of trails, including steep cliffs and beautiful rocky landscape.

Best Things to Do in Nexø: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re an adventure seeker or a culture enthusiast, Nexø has plenty of activities and tours for visitors. Here are some of the best things to do in Nexø:

  • Fishing: Spend a day fishing in Nexø’s beautiful shore and take in the ambiance of this traditional fishing village
  • Kayaking: Paddle through Nexø’s crystal clear waters, observe marine life and experience the beautiful coastline.
  • Bornholmsk Kulturuge: Experience the vibrant culture of Bornholm Island during the annual cultural week when visitors can enjoy music, art, and food.
  • Biking Tours: Experience the countryside with guided bike tours that will take you to beautiful, hidden spots around the town.

Week-Long Itinerary for Nexø

Here is a week-long itinerary when visiting Nexø:

  • Day 1: Explore the town’s rich marine heritage at Nexø Museum of Fisheries and Shipping and see local fishing boats in the harbor.
  • Day 2: Explore the region’s natural beauty with a hike through Nexø Woods.
  • Day 3: Take a day trip to Christansø Island and marvel at its historic fortress and picturesque views.
  • Day 4: Visit Hammerknuden for a day of outdoor adventures, including hiking and birdwatching.
  • Day 5: Experience Bornholm’s local cuisine with a food tour of the town.
  • Day 6: Take a guided biking tour through the countryside, visiting hidden spots around the town.
  • Day 7: Spend the day relaxing on the beautiful Snogebæk Beach and taking in the local atmosphere.

Food and Drink Scene of Nexø

Nexø has a diverse culinary scene, with plenty of restaurants, cafes, and bars that offer local and international food and drink. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sol over Gudhjem: A traditional open-faced sandwich made with smoked herring, raw egg yolk, radishes, and chives.
  • Røget Bornholmskulle: Smoked fish from the waters surrounding Bornholm often served with rye bread and potatoes.
  • Bornholmsk Bolle: A soft and flavorful bread with a salty and airy crust, usually served warm.

Local Beverages

  • Bornholmsk Brændevin: A local brandy that is often served before or after meals.
  • Mikkeler Grønlander: A locally brewed beer available in many bars and cafes.
  • Jette’s Onkel: A locally produced cider made from Bornholm apples.

Where to Stay in Nexø: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Nexø has plenty of accommodation options for all budgets. From luxury hotels to hostels and guesthouses, there are plenty of options for travelers. Here are some recommendations:

  • Hotel Klippen: A modern and luxurious hotel with sea views
  • Pension Slægtsgaarden: A smaller guesthouse just in the center of Nexø offering friendly service and cozy rooms.
  • Nexø Hostel: A budget-friendly option located close to the town center and harbor.

What to Buy in Nexø: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Nexø has plenty of stores and craft centers where visitors can purchase souvenirs or local products to bring home. Here are some recommendations:

  • Bornholmsk Keramik: Handmade Danish pottery with a modern design.
  • Nexø Gårdbutik: A farm store offering fresh, locally produced honey, jam, and vegetables.
  • Christinas Handicap Kunst: Art and decorative items created by local artists with disabilities.

Local Culture and Customs of Nexø

Nexø has a rich cultural tradition, and visitors will experience a warm and friendly atmosphere. Here are some customs and culture to keep in mind:

  • Respect for nature: Environmental consciousness is paramount in Nexø, and visitors are expected to respect the natural beauty of the town and its surroundings.
  • Tipping etiquette: Tipping is not an expectation in Denmark, although a 10% gratuity for exceptional service is appreciated and welcomed.
  • Dress code: Dress well, but there is no need to dress formally. Danish style tends to lean towards the casual with a sense of chic elegance.

Best Time to Visit Nexø

The best time to visit Nexø is in the summertime, from June to August, when temperatures are warm, and the sun is shining. During this time, tourists can enjoy outdoor activities such as swimming, hiking, and biking. Winters in Nexø are cold, gray, and wet, but it can be magical to experience the town in snowy conditions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Nexø

Here are some do’s and don’ts that visitors should keep in mind:

Do:

  • Take your bike: Biking enables visitors to explore Nexø’s gentle coastline, picturesque farmland, and stunning forests.
  • Try the local cuisine: Bornholm is a food lover’s paradise, and Nexø offers plenty of local foods that visitors should try.

Don’t:

  • Litter: Visitors should help keep Nexø clean by properly disposing of trash and litter.
  • Expect things to be open 24/7: Denmark has strict laws about when businesses can operate, and visitors should plan accordingly.

Safety and Health in Nexø

Nexø is generally safe, and tourists should not worry about their safety while exploring the town. Visitors should be aware of the weather, especially during icy or snowy conditions during the winter months. Visitors should also have health insurance that covers emergency medical treatment.

Getting to Nexø: Transportation Options and Logistics

Nexø is accessible by air, bus, and ferry. Some options include:

Airports Near Nexø

  • Bornholm Airport
  • Copenhagen Airport

Bus Stations Near Nexø

  • Nexø Bus Terminal

Other Options

  • Visitors can take the Bornholmslinjen ferry from Denmark to the island of Bornholm. Then, take a bus or taxi to Nexø.

How to Get Around Nexø

The town center is easily walkable, but visitors can rent bikes, take a taxi, or bus to surrounding areas or attractions.

Where to Go Next from Nexø: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Nexø is an excellent base for exploring other destinations on Bornholm Island. Here are some suggestions:

  • Rønne: Explore the island’s largest town with its bustling harbor and Old Town area.
  • Almindingen Forest: Visit Bornholm’s largest forest, where visitors can go horseback riding, hiking, or rent bikes to explore.
  • Dueodde Lighthouse: Visit the iconic lighthouse, the tallest in the Nordic countries, which is only a short distance from Nexø.

Conclusion

Nexø is a hidden gem of Denmark with stunning natural landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and delicious local cuisine. Whether you’re interested in outdoor adventures, history, or indulging in local cuisine, Nexø has something to offer. With this comprehensive guide, you can explore Nexø with confidence and experience everything this charming town has to offer.
