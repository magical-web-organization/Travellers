Introduction to Elverum

If you’re looking for a peaceful retreat to nature, Elverum is the perfect destination for you. This charming town in Hedmark, East Norway offers a variety of experiences from exploring natural wonders to learning about local history and culture. In this guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit, activities to do, food to try, and where to stay in Elverum.

List of Places to Visit in Elverum

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Glomdalsmuseet
  2. Elverum church
  3. The Elverum Region Museum
  4. Sagtjernet
  5. Terningen Arena

Elverum boasts of its rich cultural heritage, and no trip is complete without a visit to the local museums and historical places. Glomdalsmuseet is one of the top picks of cultural attraction; This museum provides an insight into the lifestyle, clothing, and handicrafts of locals since the 18th century. Elverum Church showcases the largest bells in Norway, with stunning architecture that dates back to the 17th century. Visit the Elverum Region Museum to learn more about its local history and how it was impacted by World War II.

Natural Attractions of Elverum: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Sorknes Golf Club
  2. Sølenstua Fjellstue
  3. Dørdalen tip hytte og aktivitet
  4. Skasenden Gård

With over 200 lakes and vast forests surrounding Elverum, there are plenty of opportunities to explore the great outdoors. Hike through the expansive landscape or cycle your way through the gravel roads and mountainous terrains. Sorknes Golf Club provides breathtaking views of the local vegetation, perfect for a calm and peaceful game of golf. Sølenstua Fjellstue, a mountain lodge and hotel, offers guided tours and activities such as skiing, hiking, and fishing. For an adventurous day trip with your family, visit Dørdalen tip hytte og aktivitet which offers various outdoor activities for all ages. Skasenden Gård, is a working farm, where you could learn about farming practices, and experience farm work activities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Elverum

  1. Øyungen for Elverum
  2. Foldalseter til Skasen
  3. Møystad til Terningen

Elverum is known for its scenic hikes and cycle routes. Øyungen for Elverum provides hikers with picturesque views of the lake, forest, and wildlife. Additionally, enjoy an exhilarating ride from Foldalseter til Skasen through the gravel roads of the mountains, or bike through Møystad til Terningen providing breathtaking panoramic views of the town.

Best Things to Do in Elverum: Activities and Tours

  1. Canoeing and Kayaking
  2. Fishing
  3. Skiing

Outdoor activities are the primary attraction in Elverum. Plan a day of canoeing, kayaking, or fishing to enjoy the serene lakes of the region. It’s an excellent opportunity to explore the beauty of nature while indulging in adventure sports. Additionally, experience skiing at Sølenstua for a more thrilling and breathtaking picturesque experience.

Food and Drink Scene of Elverum

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Rakfisk
  2. Fårikål
  3. Klippfisk
  4. Multekrem

Elverum offers a mix of traditional and trendy Norwegian cuisine. Rakfisk, a staple fermented fish dish, is served with flatbread and mustard, making it a unique tasting experience. Fårikål, a must-try lamb stew, is also a traditional Norwegian dish served with potatoes, carrots, and cabbage. Klippfisk, a dried and salted cod, is another classic dish served with potatoes, butter, and garlic. For dessert, try multekrem, a cloud-like mixture of whipped cream and cloudberries.

Local Beverages

  1. Aquavit
  2. Beer
  3. Cider

Elverum is known for its beer, Aquavit, and cider. Aquavit is a traditional Norwegian spirit consisting of cumin, dill, and caraway mainly consumed during special occasions. Local breweries like Ægir, Nøgne Ø, and Aass offer various beer options to choose from. Additionally, the local cider brand, Eplehagen, uses locally sourced apples to make authentic ciders.

Where to Stay in Elverum: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Scandic Elgstua
  2. First Hotel Central Elverum
  3. Østerdalen Hotel & Motell

Elverum offers a variety of staying options ranging from hostels, guesthouses to luxury hotels. Scandic Elgstua, located near Glomdalsmuseet is a popular four-star hotel in Elverum. First Hotel Central Elverum is another top-rated hotel known for its prime location and excellent amenities. Østerdalen Hotel & Motell located right at the foot of Norway’s longest river, Glomma, offers an exclusive countryside experience.

What to Buy in Elverum: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Handmade Knitwear
  2. Traditional Knife
  3. Norsk Flid Husfliden

Elverum is known for its traditional knitwear made from the wool of local sheep. Pick up warm woolen socks, mittens, and other warm outdoor essentials at Norsk Flid Husfliden. You will also find the famous Norwegian knives like Helle and Brusletto which are handcrafted locally.

Local Culture and Customs of Elverum

Elverum locals pride in their rich history and cultural heritage. They still wear traditional costumes during festivals and special occasions, showcasing the history and culture of the area. The town holds several festivals every year, including music festivals, food and drink festivals, and other cultural events, making it a must-visit place for culture enthusiasts.

Best Time to Visit Elverum

Summer is the best time to visit Elverum. With pleasant temperatures, clear skies, and extended daylight hours, you could make the best of your visit to explore the town and its surroundings fully. September is another ideal time to visit Elverum during autumn when the leaves turn into vibrant colors.

Do’s and Don’ts of Elverum

Do:

  • Respect local culture and traditions.
  • Take advantage of outdoor adventures and activities.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Litter or harm nature.
  • Ignore the wildlife and animals.
  • Disturb the peaceful atmosphere in local neighborhoods.

Safety and Health

Safety in Elverum

Elverum is a safe destination, and you can wander around without fear. The town’s crime rate is low, and the locals are friendly and welcoming.

Health in Elverum

Public healthcare services in Norway are top-notch. Elverum has a hospital providing excellent healthcare services for emergencies and day-to-day medical issues for residents and visitors.

Getting to Elverum: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Elverum

  1. Oslo Airport Gardermoen (OSL)
  2. Elverum Airport (ENC)

The nearest airport to Elverum is Oslo Airport Gardermoen (OSL), which connects international and domestic flights. The Elverum airport serves mainly regional flights.

Bus Stations Near Elverum

  1. Elverum Bus Terminal
  2. Åmot Sentrum

Elverum Bus Terminal provides several bus services to major cities in Norway. The Åmot Sentrum Bus Station is an excellent alternative for travelers outside the town.

Other Options

Elverum is accessible by car and train. It’s a five-hour drive from Oslo and can take an opportunity to enjoy the scenic drive. The train route from Oslo to Elverum is also a popular way to reach the town.

How to Get Around in Elverum

The town is small, and walking is the most convenient way to move around in Elverum. For more extended distances, buses and trains are available.

Where to Go Next from Elverum: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Trysil Ski Resort
  2. Lillehammer
  3. Rondane National Park

Elverum is surrounded by other popular destinations in Norway. Trysil Ski Resort, Norway’s largest ski resort, is only an hour’s drive away from Elverum. Lillehammer, famous for hosting the 1994 Winter Olympics, is just a two-hour drive away. Rondane National Park, with its majestic peaks and wildflowers, is less than a three-hour drive from Elverum.

Conclusion

Elverum is a hidden gem in East Norway. From cultural and historical attractions, scenic outdoor adventure, delicious cuisine and unique traditions- Elverum offers something for everyone. Plan a week-long trip to Elverum for an unforgettable experience immersed in nature and local culture.
