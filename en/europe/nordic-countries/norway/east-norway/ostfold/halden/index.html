Introduction to Halden

Nestled in the heart of Ostfold, Norway, lies the picturesque city of Halden. It is located in East Norway, one of the Nordic countries, in Europe. The city is surrounded by beautiful forests, expansive lakes, and rich cultural history, making it a perfect destination for nature and history lovers alike. Halden is easily accessible from Oslo, and provides a much-needed escape into nature, away from the hustle and bustle of the city.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you on a journey through the best places to visit in Halden, the cultural attractions that showcase the history of the region, and the beautiful natural sights that will leave you breathless. We’ll also share helpful tips on hiking and biking routes, the best things to do in Halden, and where to stay during your visit.

Day 2 Explore Tista River Park and hike to the Aspedammen waterfall

Food and Drink Scene of Halden

Halden has a thriving food and drink scene. Here are some dishes and local beverages you must try:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Rakfisk (fermented fish)
  • Mysost (brown cheese)
  • Kjøttkaker (meatballs)
  • Rømmegrøt (sour cream porridge)
  • Lefse (potato flatbread)

Local Beverages

  • Aass Brewery Beer
  • Hafslund’s locally produced wine
  • Grevlingen’s Cider

Local Culture and Customs of Halden

Halden locals take tremendous pride in their rich cultural history and traditions. They also take their environment very seriously, with pollution levels being significantly low in Halden. Visitors to Halden should respect local customs and take care of the environment.

Best Time to Visit Halden

Halden is a great destination to visit all year round. However, we recommend visiting during the summer months for more outdoor activities or in the fall for the harvest season.

Do’s and Don’ts of Halden

Do:

  • Respect the Norwegian customs and traditions
  • Follow hiking and biking rules
  • Use public transportation instead of driving
  • Enjoy local dishes and beverages

Don’t:

  • Litter or harm the environment
  • Smoke in public areas

Safety and Health

Safety in Halden

Halden is a safe place to travel, with low crime rates. However, visitors should be aware of the basic safety rules in public areas.

Health in Halden

Healthcare in Norway is universal, and access to healthcare is readily available. In case of any medical emergencies or issues, visitors may approach local hospitals or emergency services.
