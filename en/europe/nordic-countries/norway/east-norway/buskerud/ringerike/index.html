Introduction to Ringerike

Ringerike, located in Buskerud in East Norway, is known for its picturesque landscapes, rich cultural legacy, and stunning outdoor activities. This region is home to some of Norway’s best-known attractions, including the Hadeland Glassworks, Ringeriksbadet, and the Jevnaker Church. Additionally, it is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts who want to bike, hike, or ski, especially in the renowned Kjosterudjuvet Gorge. Whether you are interested in history, nature, or adventure, Ringerike is a must-visit for any traveler to Norway.

List of Places to Visit in Ringerike

Whether you are a cultural enthusiast or an adventurer, Ringerike has a lot to offer. Here is a list of must-visit attractions in Ringerike:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Hadeland Glasswork Museum: This museum showcases the history of the Hadeland Glasswork, one of Norway’s oldest glasswork sites. Visitors can learn about the art of glassblowing while also enjoying the beautiful pieces on display.

  • Ringerike Museum: This museum will take you through Ringerike’s rich cultural history and the region’s Viking past. The museum boasts a collection of Viking artifacts and provides cultural insights into the region.

  • Ringerikesbadet: This is a stunning example of contemporary architecture, designed by Thorbjørn Mann, which holds Norway’s largest indoor water park. The multi-level facility has everything from children’s pools to a thrill ride.

Natural Attractions of Ringerike: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Kjosterudjuvet Gorge: This is a breathtaking canyon that boasts crystal clear water streams and deep pools that are perfect for swimming during summer. Hiking along the gorge will reward you with stunning views and the opportunity to explore the natural beauty of the area.

  • Hønefossen Waterfall: This stunning waterfall is located in the center of Hønefoss and offers spectacular views of the surrounding landscape. The area surrounding the waterfall is also perfect for picnics and afternoon walks.

  • Randsfjorden Lake: This lake is the second-largest in Norway and is known for its crystal-clear water, making it a popular spot for fishing and boating. The surrounding area is perfect for hiking during the summer months, or to cross country ski and snowshoe in the winter.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ringerike

Ringerike offers some of the most beautiful hiking and biking routes, making it an excellent destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Ringerike:

  • Kjosterudjuvet Gorge Trail: This is a 3.2 km trail that takes hikers through the stunning canyon, offering views of crystal clear streams and deep pools, perfect for swimming during summer.

  • Holemsåsen National Park: This is a beautiful area that is perfect for an afternoon bike ride or hiking trip. Visitors will be rewarded with stunning views of the surrounding landscape and the opportunity to spot wildlife.

  • Randsfjorden Around: This is a beautiful trail that circles the Randsfjorden lake. It is perfect for biking or hiking during the summer months.

Best Things to Do in Ringerike: Activities and Tours

  • Hadeland Glass Factory Tour: Take a tour of the beautiful Hadeland Glass Factory and learn about the art of glassblowing while enjoying the stunning glass pieces on display.

  • Randsfjorden Round Trip Cruise: This is a beautiful cruise that circles the Randsfjorden lake, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

  • Kjosterudjuvet Canyon Tour: Take a guided tour of the breathtaking Kjosterudjuvet Canyon and explore the crystal clear streams and deep pools.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ringerike

Food and Drink Scene of Ringerike

Ringerike is known for its unique and delicious cuisine. From traditional Norwegian food to international dishes, there is something for everyone. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kjøttkaker: These are traditional Norwegian meatballs served with mashed potatoes, peas, and gravy.

  • Fårikål: This hearty dish consists of lamb, cabbage, and potatoes boiled together in a flavorful broth.

  • Rømmegrøt: This traditional Norwegian porridge is made with sour cream and served with a side of cured meats and cheese.

Local Beverages

  • Akevitt: This is a traditional Norwegian liquor made from potatoes and flavored with herbs like caraway and dill.

  • Coffee: Norway’s coffee culture is famous around the world. In Ringerike, you can find some of the best coffee shops, which use locally sourced beans.

  • Local Beer: Craft beer is a growing trend in Norway, and Ringerike has several microbreweries that offer unique and delicious beers to sample.

Where to Stay in Ringerike: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ringerike offers a wide range of accommodation options for all budgets. Whether you prefer luxurious hotels or cozy guesthouses, you will find something to suit your needs. Here are some top-rated options:

  • Grand Hotel Hønefoss: This is a luxurious hotel located in the center of Hønefoss. The rooms are spacious, and the hotel also has a restaurant, bar, and fitness center.

  • Hadeland Glassworks Thon Hotel: This is another luxury hotel located near the Hadeland Glassworks Museum. The rooms are modern and well-equipped, and the hotel also features a bar and restaurant.

  • Ringerike Vandrerhjem: This is a budget-friendly hostel that provides comfortable and clean rooms. It is located near several hiking and biking trails, making it an excellent choice for outdoor enthusiasts.

What to Buy in Ringerike: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Ringerike is famous for its unique and locally made products. Here are some top-rated souvenirs and local products to bring home:

  • Hadeland Glasswork: This is a beautiful souvenir that is handcrafted and unique. Each piece is different, making it a great gift or a treat for yourself.

  • Gjerdrumsbruket Cheese: This is a famous cheese made in Ringerike and is known for its rich and savory flavor.

  • Handmade Textiles: Ringerike is famous for its handmade textiles, including blankets, rugs, and clothing. They make excellent souvenirs and gifts.

Local Culture and Customs of Ringerike

Ringerike has a rich cultural heritage that dates back to the Viking era. The locals are friendly and proud of their heritage. Some customs and traditions that are unique to Ringerike include:

  • The Viking Market: This is an annual event that takes place in Hønefoss and celebrates Viking heritage. Visitors can experience traditional Viking crafts, music, and food.

  • Midsummer Celebrations: This is a traditional Norwegian celebration that takes place during the summer solstice. Locals gather around bonfires and sing traditional folk songs.

Best Time to Visit Ringerike

The best time to visit Ringerike is during the summer months (June-August) when the weather is mild and perfect for outdoor activities. The winter months are also a great time to visit, especially for skiing and other winter sports.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ringerike

Ringerike is a very safe destination for tourists, and the locals are friendly and approachable. However, it is always important to be cautious when traveling. Some safety tips to keep in mind while visiting Ringerike include:

  • Keep a close eye on your belongings, especially in tourist areas.

  • Follow safety guidelines when participating in outdoor activities.

  • Be careful when driving, especially in winter when roads can be icy.

Health in Ringerike

Tap water in Ringerike is safe to drink. However, it is still important to practice good hygiene and food safety. If you need medical assistance, there are several hospitals and medical clinics in the area.
