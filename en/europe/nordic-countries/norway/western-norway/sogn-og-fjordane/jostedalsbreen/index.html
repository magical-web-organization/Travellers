Introduction to Jostedalsbreen

Jostedalsbreen, the largest glacier in continental Europe, is an awe-inspiring natural wonder located in Sogn og Fjordane, Norway. Its ice cap covers nearly 487 square kilometers, and it towers over 2000 meters above sea level. Jostedalsbreen National Park is home to several glaciers, including Nigardsbreen, Briksdalsbreen, and Kjenndalsbreen. Here, visitors can indulge in various outdoor activities like hiking, glacier climbing, kayaking, and skiing. The park’s breathtaking natural beauty, enriched with cultural and historical sites, makes it a popular destination for travelers worldwide.

List of Places to Visit in Jostedalsbreen

From cultural attractions to natural wonders, Jostedalsbreen has an array of must-visit destinations to explore.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Norsk Bremuseum: This glacier museum has several exhibits that showcase the history and science of glaciers. You can learn about the myths and legends surrounding glaciers, their formation, and their impact on the environment and communities.
  • Melsvik farming museum: This museum depicts the lives and traditions of rural communities in Sogn og Fjordane.
  • Urnes Stave Church: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Urnes Stave Church is the oldest stave church in Norway, dating back to the 12th Century.

Natural Attractions of Jostedalsbreen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Nigardsbreen Glacier: This beautiful glacier boasts a 20 km long ice flow, and you can take a guided hike to the glacier’s front. You can also experience kayaking on the glacial lagoon.
  • Briksdalsbreen Glacier: Briksdalsbreen is one of the best-known glaciers in Norway. You can explore this glacier through a guided hike or ride on a shuttle.
  • Kjenndalsbreen Lake: This picturesque glacier lake is famous for its beautiful blue and green colors.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jostedalsbreen

Jostedalsbreen boasts several mountain and valley hiking trails that vary in difficulty levels. You can explore the glaciers from different angles, hike through lush forests, and cross rivers and waterfalls. For adventurous mountain bikers, there are biking trails in Jostedalsbreen, such as the Signalen loop that boasts breathtaking views of glaciers, mountains, and valleys.

Best Things to Do in Jostedalsbreen: Activities and Tours

Jostedalsbreen offers a wide range of activities and tours for travelers of all ages and tastes. Here are some of the best things to do in Jostedalsbreen:

  • Hiking and walking tours
  • Kayaking tours
  • Glacier walking and climbing tours
  • Fishing trips
  • Ice cave tours
  • Skiing and snowboarding
  • Boat tours

Week-Long Itinerary for Jostedalsbreen

Here’s an ideal seven-day itinerary to explore Jostedalsbreen fully:

  • Day 1: Visit Norsk Bremuseum and Melsvik farming museum
  • Day 2: Explore Nigardsbreen glacier in a guided hike or kayaking trip.
  • Day 3: Take a guided hike at Briksdalsbreen glacier.
  • Day 4: Visit Urnes Stave Church and take a day trip around the Lustrafjorden.
  • Day 5: Go fishing in Stryn or Lustrafjord.
  • Day 6: Go skiing or snowboarding at the Stryn Summer Ski Centre
  • Day 7: Go on a boat tour around the lakes and fjords to enjoy the natural beauty of the Jostedalsbreen region.

Food and Drink Scene of Jostedalsbreen

Jostedalsbreen offers visitors an excellent culinary experience with several local dishes and beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and drinks:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Laksfisk: This is a traditional fish dish made of salmon from the local rivers.
  • Brown cheese: This caramelized cheese made from goat’s milk is a popular Norwegian delicacy.

Local Beverages

  • Akevitt: This is the traditional Scandinavian liquor made from potatoes or grains and is often paired with fish dishes.
  • Raspberry cordial: This fruity summer drink is popular in the Jostedalsbreen region.

Where to Stay in Jostedalsbreen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Jostedalsbreen has a range of accommodation options to cater to various budgets and preferences. Here are some recommended places to stay:

  • Jostedal Hotell: This hotel offers a comfortable stay, with several hiking and skiing trails nearby.
  • Jostedal Camping: This campsite offers scenic views of the glaciers and is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts.
  • Jostedal Guesthouse: This guesthouse offers cozy rooms, a sauna, and a hot tub.

What to Buy in Jostedalsbreen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Jostedalsbreen has a vibrant shopping scene with several local products like knitwear, jewelry, and souvenirs. Gift shops at the museums and visitor centers sell local handicrafts and food items.

Local Culture and Customs of Jostedalsbreen

Jostedalsbreen is rich in culture and customs, dating back to the Viking era. Traditional folk music, dance, and cuisine are still prevalent in the region. One such cultural event is the Sogn og Fjordane Cultural Festival that celebrates the region’s music, dance, and traditional costumes. Respect for nature is an inherent part of Jostedalsbreen’s culture and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Jostedalsbreen

The best time to visit Jostedalsbreen is from May to September when the weather is pleasant, and the glaciers are accessible. Winter months offer visitors an opportunity to indulge in skiing and other winter sports.

Do’s and Don’ts of Jostedalsbreen

Do:

  • Respect nature and the environment.
  • Follow safety guidelines and instructions while hiking or exploring glaciers.
  • Try local cuisine and attend cultural events.

Don’t:

  • Leave trash in the environment.
  • Get too close to the glaciers or venture out without a guide.
  • Overlook the region’s cultural and historical sites.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jostedalsbreen

Adventurous activities like hiking, glacier climbing, and skiing can pose some risks. Visitors should carry appropriate gear like hiking boots, warm clothing, and helmets and follow safety guidelines provided by their guides. During winter months, visitors should be cautious of avalanches and ice conditions on the glaciers.

Health in Jostedalsbreen

Jostedalsbreen has several medical facilities and pharmacies to cater to visitors’ health needs. Visitors are encouraged to have travel insurance that covers emergency medical expenses.

How to Get Around in Jostedalsbreen

Where to Go Next from Jostedalsbreen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Jostedalsbreen is surrounded by several picturesque towns and villages. Here are some nearby places to explore:

  • Stryn: This charming town is known for its fjords, mountains, and lush valleys, and there are several hiking and skiing trails nearby.
  • Vik: This beautiful village is located on the shores of the Sognefjorden, and visitors can explore the Hove stone church.
  • Balestrand: Balestrand is the perfect destination to soak in the region’s stunning fjord scenery.
