Introduction to Møre og Romsdal

Møre og Romsdal is a scenic county located in Western Norway. It is known for its breathtaking mountain ranges, lush valleys, stunning fjords, and picturesque coastal towns. This region has something to offer every traveler, whether you’re looking for outdoor adventure, cultural landmarks, or simply breathtaking scenery.

List of Places to Visit in Møre og Romsdal

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kristiansund Opera House

Kristiansund Opera House is a contemporary architectural masterpiece that hosts opera, ballet, and other cultural events. It is located in the heart of Kristiansund and overlooks the fjord. The building’s distinctive glass facade is an iconic landmark in the region.

Molde Museum

Molde Museum is a cultural center that showcases the history and heritage of Møre og Romsdal. It features collections of art, historical artifacts, and natural history exhibits that are sure to fascinate visitors of all ages.

Natural Attractions of Møre og Romsdal: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Trollstigen

Trollstigen is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Møre og Romsdal. It is a serpentine mountain road that winds through steep cliffs and provides breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and fjords. Trollstigen is a must-see destination for anyone visiting the region.

Geirangerfjord

Geirangerfjord is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that offers some of the most spectacular scenery in Norway. It is a narrow and deep fjord that is flanked by towering cliffs and cascading waterfalls. Visitors can take a scenic ferry ride or go on a kayaking adventure to fully experience the beauty of this natural wonder.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Møre og Romsdal

Romsdalseggen Ridge Hike

Romsdalseggen Ridge Hike is a challenging hike that takes you through some of the most stunning scenery in Møre og Romsdal. It is a 10.3-kilometer long trail that takes around 7-9 hours to complete, with panoramic views of the mountains, fjords, and valleys along the way.

Tour de Trollstigen Bike Route

Tour de Trollstigen Bike Route is a popular biking route that takes you through some of the most scenic areas of Møre og Romsdal. The 80-kilometer route includes several steep climbs, challenging descents, and breathtaking views of the region’s natural beauty.

Best Things to Do in Møre og Romsdal: Activities and Tours

Fjord Fishing

Fjord Fishing is a popular activity in Møre og Romsdal, where visitors can rent a boat and try their luck catching salmon, trout, and other fish in the region’s fjords. This is a fantastic way to experience the beauty of the Norwegian countryside while enjoying a relaxing and rewarding outdoor activity.

Kayaking

Kayaking is a popular activity in Møre og Romsdal, where visitors can paddle through the fjords and get up close and personal with the region’s stunning natural beauty. There are many guided tours available, ranging from short half-day trips to multiday expeditions.

Food and Drink Scene of Møre og Romsdal

Must-Try Dishes

Fårikål

Fårikål is a traditional Norwegian dish that consists of lamb stewed with cabbage and potatoes. It is a hearty and flavorful dish that is perfect for cold autumn days.

Lutefisk

Lutefisk is a popular holiday dish in Norway that consists of dried cod soaked in lye and then boiled. It is served with an array of side dishes, including potatoes, bacon, and peas.

Local Beverages

Aquavit

Aquavit is a traditional Scandinavian spirit that is typically flavored with caraway or dill. It is often served cold and is a popular accompaniment to traditional Norwegian cuisine.

Craft Beer

Craft beer has become increasingly popular in Møre og Romsdal in recent years, with several local breweries producing a range of unique and delicious beers. Some of the most popular breweries include Aegir, Lervig, and Ægir.

Where to Stay in Møre og Romsdal: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Møre og Romsdal has a range of accommodation options for travelers of all budgets and preferences. Some of the most popular hotels in the region include Hotel Union Øye, Hotel Brosundet, and Hotel Molde Fjordstuer.

What to Buy in Møre og Romsdal: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Hand-knit Sweaters

Møre og Romsdal is known for its traditional hand-knit sweaters, which are made from high-quality wool and are both stylish and practical. They make for a fantastic souvenir or gift for friends and family back home.

Cheese

Norway is known for its delicious and high-quality cheese, and Møre og Romsdal is no exception. Some popular local varieties include brown cheese, gjetost, and kraftkar.

Local Culture and Customs of Møre og Romsdal

Møre og Romsdal has a rich and diverse cultural heritage, with a mix of traditional Norwegian customs and modern Western European influences. Visitors can explore the region’s cultural landmarks and learn more about its history at museums like Molde Museum and the Kristiansund Opera House.

Best Time to Visit Møre og Romsdal

The best time to visit Møre og Romsdal is during the summer months, from June to August. This is the peak tourist season, and the weather is warm and sunny, making it ideal for outdoor activities. However, visitors can also enjoy the region’s stunning natural beauty during the autumn months, when the leaves turn orange and red and the air is crisp and refreshing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Møre og Romsdal

Do:

  • Explore the region’s natural beauty through hiking, kayaking, or scenic drives.
  • Try the local cuisine, including traditional dishes like fårikål and lutefisk.
  • Embrace the local culture by visiting museums and cultural landmarks like the Kristiansund Opera House.

Don’t:

  • Be disrespectful towards local customs and traditions.
  • Litter or damage the region’s natural beauty.
  • Ignore safety precautions when enjoying outdoor activities like hiking and kayaking.

Safety and Health

Safety in Møre og Romsdal

Møre og Romsdal is a safe region with low crime rates. However, visitors should still take necessary precautions to stay safe, such as avoiding walking alone at night and keeping valuables out of sight.

Health in Møre og Romsdal

Møre og Romsdal has high-quality healthcare facilities, and visitors can access medical care if needed. However, visitors should have travel insurance and should take necessary precautions to stay healthy, such as getting vaccinated and practicing good hygiene.

Getting to Møre og Romsdal: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Møre og Romsdal

The closest major airport to Møre og Romsdal is Ålesund Airport, which is served by several major airlines and connects to destinations throughout Europe.

Bus Stations Near Møre og Romsdal

There are several bus stations throughout Møre og Romsdal that connect to destinations throughout Norway and the surrounding regions.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Møre og Romsdal by car, train, or ferry, depending on their location and preferences.

How to Get Around in Møre og Romsdal

The best way to get around Møre og Romsdal is by car, as this allows visitors to explore the region’s stunning natural beauty at their own pace. However, there are also several public transportation options available, including buses and ferries.

Where to Go Next from Møre og Romsdal: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can explore many other destinations in Norway and the surrounding regions from Møre og Romsdal. Some popular nearby destinations include Bergen, Trondheim, and the Lofoten Islands.

Conclusion

Møre og Romsdal is a stunning region with a rich cultural heritage and breathtaking natural beauty. Whether you’re interested in outdoor adventures, cultural landmarks, or simply enjoying the region’s stunning scenery, there is something for everyone in Møre og Romsdal. Plan your visit today to experience all that this beautiful region has to offer.
