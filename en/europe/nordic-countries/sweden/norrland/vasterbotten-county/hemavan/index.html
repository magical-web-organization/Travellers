Introduction to Hemavan

Located in the heart of Norrland, Hemavan is one of Sweden’s hidden gems, offering a unique blend of natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Renowned for its breathtaking mountain scenery and outdoor adventures, Hemavan is a haven for hikers, bikers, skiers, and nature enthusiasts. From its awe-inspiring landscapes to its hospitable people, Hemavan is a destination you don’t want to miss. In this travel guide, we’ve curated a list of must-see attractions, hiking and biking routes, local cuisine, and transportation options to help you plan the perfect holiday in Hemavan. Let’s dive in!

List of Places to Visit in Hemavan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Hemavans Fjällmuseum

If you’re a history buff, make sure to visit Hemavans Fjällmuseum, which showcases the rich cultural heritage of Hemavan. The museum displays artifacts and exhibits related to the traditional Sami way of life, highlighting the area’s indigenous people’s history. You’ll learn about their customs, language, and traditions.

Christer Aberg’s Museum

Another must-see museum is Christer Aberg’s Museum, where you can observe impressive wood carvings and sculptures and admire the exquisite craftsmanship. You’ll also find a range of other exhibits that celebrate the local culture and history.

Natural Attractions of Hemavan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Tärnaby Fjällby

Tärnaby Fjällby is an ideal base camp for exploring the stunning natural attractions of Hemavan. Hiking trails in the surrounding mountains offer breathtaking views of the unique landscape, and there are plenty of biking routes to explore as well. Additionally, you’ll find nearby fishing opportunities for all ages.

Kungsleden

Kungsleden, one of Sweden’s most famous hiking trails, passes through Hemavan. A fully guided trek is available on this 440km trail. The path offers an incredible view and traverses across forested valleys, rolling hills, and open tundra.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hemavan

The Blåhammaren Trek

The Blåhammaren Trek is a 30km trail that takes you through some of Northern Sweden’s most beautiful natural landscapes. This route offers some of the area’s most beautiful views for hikers who are willing to challenge themselves.

Lullengreisleden

Lullengreisleden is a beautiful 20km trail that is also family-friendly, making it ideal for bikers to take their family along. The trail leads bikers to Lullengreis, famous for its stunning panoramic views of the Barriärer mountain range.

Best Things to Do in Hemavan: Activities and Tours

Flyfishing in the Ume River

The Ume River, which flows through the heart of Hemavan, is a perfect location for fly fishing. The river is well-stocked with trout, which makes it an ideal spot for fishing enthusiasts. Several tour operators arrange fly fishing tours that take you to the best locations and guide you through the technique.

ATV Adventure in the Mountains

Rent an ATV, and explore the surrounding mountain terrain of Hemavan. It’s a perfect way to take the scenic route and explore your surroundings!

Food and Drink Scene of Hemavan

Must-Try Dishes

Janssons Frestelse

Janssons Frestelse is a traditional Swedish dish made of potatoes, onions, cream, anchovies, and breadcrumbs. It’s a staple in most Swedish households and is a must-try when in Hemavan.

Renskav

Renskav, a traditional Sami dish, is made using reindeer meat thinly sliced and fried until tender. It’s a unique delicacy that Hemavan is famous for.

Local Beverages

Arctic Beer

Arctic Beer is a locally brewed beer that uses traditional Swedish techniques and flavors. This beer is unique because it’s brewed using local water, giving it an authentic taste of Hemavan.

Where to Stay in Hemavan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hemavan has a wide range of accommodation options that cater to different budgets. Here are a few options that we recommend:

Hemavan Fjällcenter

Hemavan Fjällcenter is a lodge with a range of rooms and apartments at affordable prices, which makes it perfect for budget travelers. It’s located close to all the local attractions and offers excellent guest services.

Tärnaby Fjällby

Tärnaby Fjällby is a luxurious hotel with all the amenities you could ask for. As an additional plus, it’s situated near the outdoor activities of Hemavan and also offers some of its own activities, making it perfect for adventure-seeking travelers.

What to Buy in Hemavan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Reindeer Meat

Hemavan is known for its unique local delicacy, Renskav, which is thinly sliced and fried reindeer meat. It’s a perfect souvenir for meat-lovers and a delicious reminder of your stay in Hemavan.

Dala Knives

Dala Knives, uniquely crafted, have been a Swedish tradition for centuries and make for an excellent souvenir. They’re handmade in Sweden, which makes them an exceptional, high-quality purchase.

Local Culture and Customs of Hemavan

Hemavan has a rich cultural heritage that is celebrated throughout the year. The Sami culture has been a fundamental part of the locality, and their traditions continue to be alive today. Festivals like the Sami Week and the Hemavan Harvest Festival are perfect events for you to immerse yourself in their culture.

Best Time to Visit Hemavan

Hemavan is best visited during the summer and winter seasons. If you’re looking to enjoy the outdoors during summer, you’ll find various activities like hiking, fishing, and ATV riding. Winter brings an endless amount of activities like skiing, snowmobiling, and ice fishing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hemavan

Do:

  • Be respectful of the locals, their culture, and traditions
  • Bring comfortable clothes and shoes for outdoor activities
  • Try the local food, especially reindeer meat and Janssons Frestelse
  • Attend the Sami Week and the Hemavan Harvest Festival to immerse yourself in the local culture

Don’t:

  • Litter anywhere while exploring nature
  • Leave the outdoors without putting out fires
  • Ignore safety precautions while participating in adventure sports
  • Ignore locals requests such as dressing appropriately in certain situations

Safety and Health

Safety in Hemavan

Hemavan is known for its natural beauty, but it’s crucial to note that outdoor activities can sometimes be dangerous. Hire experienced guides when partaking in activities like skiing, snowmobiling, and other mountainous sports to avoid accidents. Also, make sure to check for any travel warnings with respect to weather conditions before embarking on outdoor adventures.

Health in Hemavan

There are medical facilities available in Hemavan, both private and public. It’s important to note that some activities can be physically demanding, so ensure that you’re physically fit before participating and seek medical help if necessary.

Getting to Hemavan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Hemavan is well-connected by air, road, and bus services. You can travel to Hemavan from Stockholm by air, and you’ll land at the Hemavan-Tärnaby Airport, which is only 5 km from Hemavan city. You can also take a bus or car from Stockholm, which can take anywhere between 12-15 hours, depending on the route you take.

Airports Near Hemavan

The Hemavan-Tärnaby Airport is only a 5 km distance from the city and offers both domestic and international flights to Hemavan. Here are the airport codes for reference - HEM / ESNH.

Bus Stations Near Hemavan

Several bus services offer transportation to Hemavan from Stockholm, and some of the most popular ones include CF Buss AB and Norrland buss.

Other Options

If you’re looking to travel to Hemavan by car, you can rent one in Stockholm and drive to Hemavan. The journey can be long, but it’s scenic, and you’ll get to view some beautiful Swedish landscapes.

How to Get Around in Hemavan

Hemavan is a small city, making it easy to navigate by foot. However, you can also rent a bike and explore the surrounding area. If you’re planning to go a little further, explore the buses and taxis in the city to help ease your journey.

Where to Go Next from Hemavan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations worth exploring:

  • Tärnaby is home to a ski resort and features an array of outdoor activities, including hiking, fishing, and mountaineering.
  • Kvikkjokk is a small village that’s perfect for outdoor adventurers and hikers. The Padjelanta trail, a famous hiking route, passes through the village.
  • Storuman is a nature reserve famous for its beautiful lake and diverse wildlife, including moose, lynx, and beavers.

Conclusion

Hemavan is a beautiful destination that offers an array of activities for nature enthusiasts. From its breathtaking mountain scenery to its rich cultural heritage, Hemavan has something to offer travelers of all kinds. Use this travel guide to plan your dream holiday in Hemavan, and make sure to experience all the natural and cultural attractions this incredible destination has to offer!
