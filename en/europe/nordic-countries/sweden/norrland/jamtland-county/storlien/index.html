Introduction to Storlien

Nestled in the heart of Jämtland County, in Norrland, Sweden, Storlien is a destination that offers stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and plenty of opportunities for adventure. Surrounded by the breathtaking vistas of the Nordic countries, Storlien has something to offer everyone - from hikers and bikers to history buffs and foodies. Discover the best things to see, do, and eat with our comprehensive travel guide to Storlien.

List of Places to Visit in Storlien

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Storliens Kulturstugan: Learn about the history and traditions of Storlien through exhibitions and displays depicting the life of local people.
  • Färdtjällen’s Chapel: This enchanting wooden chapel dates back to 1797 and is a perfect example of traditional Scandinavian architecture.
  • Kultursmedjan i Storlien: Discover the traditional crafts of Storlien with classes and workshops led by local artisans.

Natural Attractions of Storlien: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Tännådalen: Hikers and bikers can explore the scenic trails and incredible views of Tännådalen, located just two miles from Storlien.
  • Vålådalen: The stunning Vålådalen nature reserve is a must-see for nature enthusiasts. The area boasts crystal clear lakes, pristine forests, and abundant wildlife.
  • Storsnasen: For the ultimate adrenaline rush, try paragliding or hang gliding from the top of Storsnasen, one of the highest peaks in Storlien.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Storlien

  • Vålådalens Naturreservat: This stunning nature reserve offers hikers and bikers dozens of trails to choose from, ranging from easy hikes to more challenging treks.
  • Storulvån Fjällstation: Explore the majestic mountain ranges and fabulous views surrounding Storulvån Fjällstation with its many miles of hiking and biking trails.
  • Anarisfjällen: This pristine wilderness area boasts a network of hiking and biking trails that will take you through some of the most beautiful landscapes in Sweden.

Best Things to Do in Storlien: Activities and Tours

  • Dog Sledding: Experience the thrill of dog sledding through the snow-covered forests and mountains surrounding Storlien.
  • Fishing: The pristine lakes and rivers in and around Storlien are home to a variety of fish species, making it the perfect destination for anglers.
  • Snowmobiling: For an unforgettable adventure in the snow, try snowmobiling across the vast expanses of the Nordic forest near Storlien.

Food and Drink Scene of Storlien

Must-Try Dishes

  • Jämtlandic Cheese: This delicious cheese is made from unpasteurized milk and aged for up to a year, resulting in a unique and rich flavor.
  • Kroppkakor: These traditional Swedish potato dumplings are typically filled with pork or bacon and served with lingonberry sauce.
  • Renstek: This succulent reindeer dish is a must-try for meat lovers, with its rich and gamey flavor.

Local Beverages

  • Åre Whisky: Produced in nearby Åre, this premium whiskey is crafted using local ingredients and traditional methods.
  • Jämtlands Bryggeri Beer: This award-winning beer is brewed locally in Jämtland and is available in many flavors, including classic pilsner and IPA.

Where to Stay in Storlien: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Storlien Fjällgård: This family-friendly hotel is located just minutes from the slopes and offers cozy rooms and apartments.
  • Vålådalens Fjällstation: This rustic lodge is located in the heart of Vålådalen Nature Reserve and offers affordable accommodations and breathtaking views.
  • Jämtlands Stjärnlodge: For a truly unique experience, stay in a glass igloo and enjoy the Northern Lights from the comfort of your room.

What to Buy in Storlien: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Jämtlandic Cheese: Take home a wedge of this delicious and unique cheese to remember your trip by.
  • Reindeer Skins: Made from locally-sourced reindeer hides, these high-quality skins are a popular souvenir among visitors.
  • Åre Whisky: Bring home a bottle of this award-winning whiskey as a reminder of your time in Storlien.

Local Culture and Customs of Storlien

Storlien is a community that takes pride in its history and traditions. From the local cuisine to the traditional crafts, visitors will find many opportunities to learn about the local culture and customs. Many events, such as the traditional Midsummer celebration, provide a glimpse into the unique customs of Storlien.

Best Time to Visit Storlien

Visitors can enjoy Storlien’s natural beauty and culture year-round, with each season offering its own unique attractions. Summer visitors can enjoy hiking and biking, while winter visitors can enjoy skiing and snowmobiling. Visit in the fall to enjoy the colorful foliage, or in the spring to witness the region’s lively rebirth.

Safety and Health

Safety in Storlien

Storlien is a relatively safe destination, with low levels of crime and few accidents. Visitors should take standard precautions, such as securing valuables and watching their surroundings when out and about. When hiking or engaging in other outdoor activities, it is important to adhere to safety guidelines and to take proper precautions to avoid injury.

Storlien has high-quality medical facilities and services that can provide visitors with necessary medical care. However, visitors should take care to bring any medications or other medical supplies they need, as these may not be widely available.

Getting to Storlien: Transportation Options and Logistics

Getting to Storlien is relatively easy, with several transportation options available from nearby cities.

Airports Near Storlien

  • Trondheim Airport, Værnes: Located approximately 1.5 hours from Storlien, this major airport is served by several major airlines.
  • Åre Östersund Airport: Located approximately 1 hour from Storlien, this regional airport offers flights to several European destinations.

Bus Stations Near Storlien

  • Storlien Bus Station: Located in the heart of Storlien, this bus station offers daily service to nearby cities and destinations.
  • Åre Bus Station: Located approximately 1 hour from Storlien, this bus station offers service to several major cities in the region.

Other Options

  • Train: Storlien is located on the main train line connecting Trondheim and Östersund, making it easily accessible by rail.
  • Car: Visitors can also drive to Storlien, with several major roads connecting the area to nearby cities and destinations.

How to Get Around in Storlien

Storlien is a small community that is easily navigable on foot or by bike. However, visitors may also choose to rent a car or use local transportation options such as taxis or buses to get around more easily.

Where to Go Next from Storlien: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Åre: Located just 45 minutes from Storlien, this popular ski resort offers world-class skiing and snowboarding in the winter, and hiking and biking in the summer.
  • Trondheim: This charming city is located approximately 1.5 hours from Storlien and offers a wealth of cultural attractions and historic sites.
  • Östersund: Located approximately 2 hours from Storlien, this city offers a range of attractions including museums, parks, and outdoor adventures.
