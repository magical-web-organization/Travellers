Introduction to Norrbotten County

Norrbotten County, located in the far north of Sweden, is a hidden gem that boasts breathtaking natural scenery and a rich cultural heritage. This sparsely populated region is home to stunning national parks, idyllic coastal towns, and charming rural communities.

One of the highlights of Norrbotten County is the midnight sun experience, where the sun never sets during the summer months and provides an unforgettable sight. The winter season brings spectacular Northern Lights displays, offering a truly unique vacation experience.

Whether you want to go hiking, fishing, or explore the region’s fascinating history, Norrbotten County is the ideal destination for nature lovers, adrenaline junkies, and culture aficionados. In this guide, we’ll highlight some of the best places to visit and things to do in Norrbotten County.

List of Places to Visit in Norrbotten County

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Storforsen - This impressive waterfall in Älvsbyn is one of the most popular natural attractions in Norrbotten County. Visitors can take a walking trail to enjoy stunning views of the cascading waters and nearby forests.
  2. Kiruna Church - This historic church is a must-visit destination for those interested in the culture of Norrbotten County. Built in the early 20th century, Kiruna Church features stunning stained glass windows and beautiful frescoes that depict local life.
  3. Jokkmokk Market - A yearly market that has been celebrated for over 400 years and known for its celebration of Sámi culture.

Natural Attractions of Norrbotten County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Abisko National Park - One of the most photogenic places in Norrbotten County, Abisko National Park is a true paradise for hikers and nature enthusiasts. This park features over 75 miles of hiking trails, including the famous Kungsleden hiking trail that winds through the stunning landscapes of untamed wilderness.
  2. Padjelanta National Park - This serene national park spans over 1,500 square miles of untouched wilderness, complete with tranquil lakes and scenic hiking trails. Visitors can enjoy hiking, fishing, and camping amidst stunning glaciers and towering mountains.
  3. Luleå Archipelago - This stunning archipelago consists of over 1,300 islands and islets, making it the ideal destination for boating enthusiasts and lovers of water sports. Visitors can explore the many beaches, hiking trails, and quaint fishing villages that dot the area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Norrbotten County

  1. Kungsleden Trail - This world-renowned hiking trail stretches over 270 miles through the magnificent mountains and valleys of Norrbotten County, including Abisko and Padjelanta national parks.
  2. Lapland Arctic Trail - A 450-mile hiking trail that takes visitors on a journey through the most remote and breathtaking landscapes of Norrbotten County, with challenging terrains and stunning views.

Best Things to Do in Norrbotten County: Activities and Tours

  1. Aurora Sky Station - This unique observatory offers visitors a chance to witness the Northern Lights and learn more about the stars and constellations from knowledgeable guides.
  2. Icehotel - Norrbotten County is home to the world’s first ice hotel, which offers guests the chance to sleep in an igloo or enjoy ice-inspired cocktails at the bar.
  3. Wildlife safaris - Visitors can join a tour to observe wildlife such as moose, reindeer, and lynx in their natural habitat, with guided tours available in several areas.

Week-Long Itinerary for Norrbotten County

Food and Drink Scene of Norrbotten County

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Surströmming - A type of fermented herring that is typically served with onion, potatoes, and crispbread.
  2. Gravlax - A dish of cured salmon, typically served with dill and mustard sauce.
  3. Renkött - A dish of reindeer meat that is often served stewed or roasted.

Local Beverages

  1. Baltic Porters - A type of strong beer that is popular in Norrbotten County.
  2. Cloudberry liqueur - A local specialty that is made from cloudberries, which are a type of tart berry found in the region.

Where to Stay in Norrbotten County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Treehotel - A hotel that offers visitors the unique experience of staying in a treehouse. Guests can choose from a variety of different treehouses, each with its own theme and style.
  2. Arctic Bath Hotel and Spa - A hotel and spa that is situated on a deck-style structure in the middle of the Lule River. Guests can enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding wilderness while relaxing in the outdoor hot tub.

What to Buy in Norrbotten County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Sámi handicrafts - Handcrafted souvenirs such as silver jewelry, scarves, and mittens are popular among visitors, showcasing the rich history and culture of the Sámi people.
  2. Birch bark handicrafts - These handmade items range from baskets to dishes and trays and showcase traditional Scandinavian designs.

Local Culture and Customs of Norrbotten County

The Sámi people have inhabited Norrbotten County for thousands of years, and their unique customs and traditions are deeply ingrained in local culture. Visitors should be respectful of Sámi beliefs and traditions, such as the practice of reindeer herding and the use of ceremonial drums.

Best Time to Visit Norrbotten County

The best time to visit Norrbotten County is during the summer months (June to August) when the weather is mild and the days are long, providing ideal conditions for hiking, boating, and other outdoor activities. Winter months (November to February) see a unique display of Northern Lights and other winter activities like snowmobiling and dog sledding.

Do’s and Don’ts of Norrbotten County

Do:

  1. Respect local traditions and customs, especially those of the Sámi people.
  2. Take advantage of the opportunity to witness the Northern Lights.
  3. Embrace the great outdoors and explore the stunning natural beauty of the region.

Don’t:

  1. Litter or damage the environment.
  2. Disturb wildlife or stray off designated trails.
  3. Ignore safety guidelines when participating in outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Norrbotten County

Norrbotten County is a safe destination with low levels of crime. However, visitors should take usual precautions such as keeping valuables secured and being aware of their surroundings when in unfamiliar areas.

Health in Norrbotten County

Medical care facilities are widely available in Norrbotten County for emergencies. However, health insurance is highly recommended for all visitors as medical expenses can be high.

Getting to Norrbotten County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Norrbotten County

  1. Luleå Airport
  2. Kiruna Airport

Bus Stations Near Norrbotten County

  1. Luleå Bus Station
  2. Kiruna Bus Station

Other Options

  1. Train - Visitors can take a night train from Stockholm to Kiruna or Luleå.
  2. Car rental - Popular car rental companies operate in Norrbotten County, providing an opportunity for flexible exploration and making multiple stops.

How to Get Around in Norrbotten County

Public transport options beyond main cities might be limited, which makes renting a car a practical solution. For shorter distances, biking or walking are other options that allow travelers to appreciate the views.

Where to Go Next from Norrbotten County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Lofoten, Norway - The stunning Lofoten archipelago is a short ferry ride from Narvik, and offers visitors the chance to explore charming fishing villages and witness the Northern Lights in a unique setting.
  2. Kittilä, Finland - Located just over the Finnish border, Kittilä is home to stunning natural landscapes and charming rural communities that offer a unique glimpse into Finnish culture.

Conclusion

Norrbotten County is a true gem that offers visitors a chance to immerse themselves in stunning natural landscapes and rich cultural traditions. Whether you’re hiking the Kungsleden Trail, exploring Sámi culture, or simply relaxing by the water, Norrbotten County is a destination that should not be missed.
