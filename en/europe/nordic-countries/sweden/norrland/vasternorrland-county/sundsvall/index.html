A Comprehensive Tour Guide to Sundsvall, Sweden

Sundsvall is a charming town located in Västernorrland County, in Norrland, Sweden. It is a popular destination for its vibrant culture, stunning natural landscapes, and rich history. In this comprehensive tour guide, we have compiled all the essential information you need to plan a hassle-free trip to Sundsvall. From must-visit attractions to local culture and customs, this guide has got you covered.

Introduction to Sundsvall

Sundsvall is a bustling town that offers something for everyone. Its charming cobbled streets and historic buildings attract history buffs, while outdoor enthusiasts flock to its scenic forests and hiking trails. Sundsvall is also known for its vibrant cultural scene that celebrates both traditional and modern Swedish culture.

List of Places to Visit in Sundsvall

Here is a list of some of the top attractions in Sundsvall:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sundsvalls Museum This museum is a must-visit for history buffs as it showcases the history of Sundsvall and Norrland. The museum has a rich collection of artefacts, photographs, and documents that depict the region’s history.

  • Stenstan Visitor Center Stenstan Visitor Center is a historic 19th-century building that was restored after a devastating fire in 1888. It is an excellent place to learn about the town’s architecture and history.

  • Kulturmagasinet Kulturmagasinet is a popular cultural centre that hosts exhibitions, lectures, and workshops throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of Sundsvall: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Norra Stadsberget Norra Stadsberget is a scenic hiking trail that offers some of the best views of Sundsvall. The trail is suitable for all levels of hikers and offers stunning views of the town and surrounding forests.

  • Alnö Alnö is a scenic island located just outside Sundsvall. It is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna and offers several hiking and biking trails.

  • Naturum Naturum is an informative nature centre that showcases the biodiversity of the Norrland region. It is an excellent place to learn about the local flora and fauna and offers several interactive exhibits.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sundsvall

Sundsvall is an excellent destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some popular trails:

  • Sidsjöbacken Sidsjöbacken is a scenic hiking trail that offers stunning views of the town and surrounding forests. The trail is suitable for all levels of hikers and offers several rest areas along the way.

  • Norra Berget Norra Berget is a challenging hiking trail that offers some of the best views of Sundsvall. The trail is steep and takes around 4-5 hours to complete.

  • Alnön Alnön offers several scenic biking trails that offer stunning views of the surrounding forests and coastline.

Best Things to Do in Sundsvall: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the top things to do in Sundsvall:

  • Visit a local market Sundsvall has several vibrant markets that offer everything from fresh produce to handmade souvenirs. Some popular markets include the Stenstan market and the Möljen market.

  • Take a guided tour of Stenstan Stenstan is Sundsvall’s historic town centre and is home to several well-preserved 19th-century buildings. A guided tour of Stenstan is an excellent way to learn about the town’s architecture and history.

  • Visit a local brewery Sundsvall is known for its excellent beer, and several local breweries offer tours and tastings throughout the year.

Food and Drink Scene of Sundsvall

Sundsvall’s food and drink scene is a delightful surprise. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Palt Palt is a traditional Swedish dish that consists of boiled potato dumplings served with butter and lingonberry jam.

  • Surströmming Surströmming is a type of fermented herring that is a popular delicacy in Northern Sweden.

  • Kroppkaka Kroppkaka is a type of Swedish dumpling that is filled with meat, onions, and potatoes.

Local Beverages

  • Mead Mead is an ancient Nordic drink made from fermented honey. Several local breweries in Sundsvall offer traditional mead.

  • Cider Sundsvall is known for its excellent cider, and several local breweries offer tastings and tours throughout the year.

Where to Stay in Sundsvall: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Sundsvall offers several excellent options for accommodation, ranging from luxurious hotels to affordable hostels and guesthouses:

  • Elite Hotel Knaust Elite Hotel Knaust is a historic hotel located in the heart of Stenstan. It offers luxurious accommodations and excellent amenities.

  • Best Western Hotel Baltic Best Western Hotel Baltic is located in the city center and offers comfortable accommodation at an affordable price.

  • STF Vandrarhem Sundsvall STF Vandrarhem Sundsvall is a popular hostel located in the city center. It offers clean and comfortable accommodation at an affordable price.

What to Buy in Sundsvall: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Sundsvall is known for its high-quality handicrafts and traditional products. Here are some popular souvenirs and local products to bring home:

  • Handmade pottery Sundsvall is known for its excellent handmade pottery. Several local shops offer unique pieces that make great souvenirs.

  • Reindeer meat Reindeer meat is a popular delicacy in Norrland. You can find a wide range of reindeer meat products in local shops and markets.

Local Culture and Customs of Sundsvall

Sundsvall has a rich cultural heritage that celebrates both traditional and modern Swedish culture. Here are some local customs and traditions to keep in mind:

  • Fika Fika is a Swedish tradition that involves taking a break for coffee and a small snack. It is a great way to experience Swedish culture and meet new people.

  • Midsummer Midsummer is a popular Swedish holiday that celebrates the summer solstice. Sundsvall has several festivities throughout the week that include traditional dances and celebrations.

Best Time to Visit Sundsvall

The best time to visit Sundsvall is during the summer months between June and August when the weather is mild and sunny. However, the town’s unique charm and natural beauty make it a worth-visit destination in all seasons.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sundsvall

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Sundsvall:

Do:

  • Try traditional Swedish dishes Sundsvall is known for its excellent traditional Swedish cuisine. Be sure to try some of the local dishes during your visit.

  • Respect local customs Sundsvall has a rich cultural heritage, and it is essential to respect local customs and traditions while visiting the town.

Don’t:

  • Litter Sundsvall is known for its pristine natural landscapes, and it is important to keep them clean and litter-free.

  • Disrespect the outdoors Sundsvall’s stunning natural landscapes are a treasure. It is important to stay on designated trails and respect the flora and fauna.

Safety and Health

Sundsvall is a safe destination for travelers. Here are some health and safety tips to keep in mind:

Safety in Sundsvall

  • Stay aware of surroundings Sundsvall is a safe destination, but it is important to stay aware of your surroundings, especially when walking at night.

  • Avoid carrying large amounts of cash Sundsvall is a well-developed town with several ATM’s and credit card facilities.

Health in Sundsvall

  • Stay hydrated Sundsvall’s summer months can get warm, so it is important to stay hydrated.

  • Use sunscreen Sundsvall’s sunny days can be harmful. It is recommended to use sunscreen to avoid sunburn.

Getting to Sundsvall: Transportation Options and Logistics

Sundsvall is well-connected to all major cities in Sweden and Europe:

Airports Near Sundsvall

  • Sundsvall Timrå Airport Sundsvall Timrå Airport is the closest airport to Sundsvall and is located 25 kilometers from the town center. Several international and domestic flights are available.

  • Stockholm Arlanda Airport Stockholm Arlanda Airport is located 370 kilometers south of Sundsvall and is the largest airport in Sweden. Several international and domestic flights are available.

Bus Stations Near Sundsvall

  • Sundsvall Central Bus Station Sundsvall Central Bus Station is located in the town center and offers several daily bus services to various destinations in Sweden.

Other Options

  • Train Sundsvall has an excellent train connection with all major cities in Sweden and Europe.

How to Get Around in Sundsvall

Sundsvall is a compact town, and most attractions are within walking distance. There are also several public transport options:

  • Bus Sundsvall has a well-developed public bus system that covers the entire town.

  • Taxi Taxis are readily available in Sundsvall and are an excellent option for getting around the town.

Where to Go Next from Sundsvall: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Sundsvall is an excellent base to explore nearby towns and attractions:

  • Härnösand Härnösand is located 83 kilometers north of Sundsvall and is known for its charming old town and scenic coastline.

  • Örnsköldsvik Örnsköldsvik is located 120 kilometers north of Sundsvall and is a popular destination for its excellent hiking trails and scenic forests.

Conclusion

Sundsvall is a hidden gem in Northern Sweden that offers something for everyone, from scenic hiking trails to cultural attractions.
