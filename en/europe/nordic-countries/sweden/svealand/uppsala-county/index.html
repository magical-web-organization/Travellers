Introduction to Uppsala County

Located in Svealand, Sweden, Uppsala County is known for its rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty. It’s home to the historic city of Uppsala, which is known for its splendid architecture, fascinating museums, and vibrant student life. The county is also renowned for its picturesque countryside, which offers ample opportunities for outdoor adventures and relaxation.

In this travel guide, we’ll take you on a journey through Uppsala County, highlighting the best places to visit, things to do, and food to eat. Whether you’re a history buff, nature lover, or simply seeking a new adventure, Uppsala County is the perfect destination for you.

List of Places to Visit in Uppsala County

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Uppsala County is home to several museums and historical sites that are worth visiting. Here are some of the top cultural attractions:

Uppsala Cathedral

Uppsala Cathedral is a grand church that is over 500 years old. It’s known for its stunning architecture, beautiful stained-glass windows, and impressive organ. Visitors can take a guided tour of the cathedral or attend a Sunday service.

Gamla Uppsala Museum

Gamla Uppsala Museum is a fascinating museum that explores the history of the Vikings. It’s located in the ancient burial ground of Gamla Uppsala, where many prominent Viking leaders were laid to rest. The museum has a collection of artifacts, including weapons, jewelry, and everyday items.

Linnaeus Museum

Linnaeus Museum is dedicated to the life and work of Carl Linnaeus, a famous 18th-century botanist. The museum showcases his personal belongings and scientific instruments, as well as his contributions to modern taxonomy.

Natural Attractions of Uppsala County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Uppsala County is home to several nature reserves and parks that are perfect for hiking, biking, and nature-watching. Here are some of the top outdoor attractions:

Fjällnora

Fjällnora is a popular recreation area located just outside Uppsala. It’s known for its beautiful lakes, forests, and hiking trails. Visitors can rent bicycles, kayaks, and rowboats to explore the area, or simply relax on the beach.

Hågadalen-Nåsten Nature Reserve

Hågadalen-Nåsten Nature Reserve is a beautiful natural area located just outside Uppsala. It’s home to several lakes, forests, and meadows, as well as an abundance of wildlife. Visitors can hike, bike, or ride horses along the many trails in the reserve.

Upplandsleden

Upplandsleden is a network of hiking and biking trails that spans over 450 kilometers throughout Uppsala County. The trail takes visitors through several nature reserves and parks, allowing for an immersive outdoor experience.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Uppsala County

Uppsala County is an outdoor enthusiast’s paradise, with plenty of hiking and biking trails to explore. Here are some of the top routes:

Fjällnora Loop

The Fjällnora Loop is a scenic 12-kilometer trail that takes hikers and bikers through the beautiful Fjällnora recreation area. It’s a moderately difficult trail that offers stunning views of the surrounding lakes and forests.

Norra Lunsen

Norra Lunsen is a popular hiking and biking area located just outside Stockholm. It’s known for its beautiful forests and wetlands, as well as its abundant wildlife. There are several well-marked trails throughout the area, ranging from easy to difficult.

Svartådalen

Svartådalen is a beautiful natural area that’s perfect for hiking, biking, or just exploring. It’s located in central Sweden, and it’s known for its scenic lakes, forests, and meadows. There are several trails throughout the area, ranging from short and easy to long and challenging.

Best Things to Do in Uppsala County: Activities and Tours

Uppsala County offers a wide range of activities and tours for visitors of all ages and interests. Here are some of the top things to do:

Explore the University Town of Uppsala

Uppsala is a vibrant university town with plenty of historic sites, museums, and cultural attractions. Visitors can take a walking tour of the city, visit the local museums, or simply wander around the charming neighborhoods.

Go Kayaking on the Fyris River

The Fyris River is a scenic waterway that flows through Uppsala. Visitors can rent kayaks and explore the river at their leisure, taking in the scenic views and wildlife along the way.

Visit the Skokloster Castle

Skokloster Castle is a stunning 17th-century castle located on the banks of Lake Mälaren. It’s known for its beautiful architecture, impressive art collection, and stunning gardens. Visitors can take a guided tour of the castle or explore the grounds on their own.

Week-Long Itinerary for Uppsala County

Here’s a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to Uppsala County:

Day 1: Explore Uppsala

Explore the historic city of Uppsala, visiting the cathedral, museums, and other cultural attractions.

Day 2: Hike in Fjällnora

Spend the day hiking in the Fjällnora recreation area, enjoying the beautiful lakes and forests.

Day 3: Visit Gamla Uppsala

Visit the Gamla Uppsala Museum and explore the ancient burial ground of Gamla Uppsala.

Day 4: Hike in Norra Lunsen

Hike in the Norra Lunsen area, taking in the beautiful forests and wetlands.

Day 5: Visit Skokloster Castle

Visit the stunning Skokloster Castle and explore the gardens and art collection.

Day 6: Bike the Upplandsleden

Bike a section of the Upplandsleden trail, enjoying the beautiful natural scenery.

Day 7: Relax in Hågadalen-Nåsten

Spend the day relaxing in Hågadalen-Nåsten Nature Reserve, taking in the beautiful lakes and meadows.

Food and Drink Scene of Uppsala County

Uppsala County is known for its delicious cuisine, which includes traditional Swedish dishes and international flavors. Here are some of the top dishes and beverages to try:

Must-Try Dishes

Köttbullar

Köttbullar, or Swedish meatballs, are a classic dish that’s popular throughout Sweden. They’re typically served with mashed potatoes, lingonberry jam, and pickled cucumbers.

Raggmunk

Raggmunk, or Swedish potato pancakes, are a hearty and filling dish that’s perfect for cold weather. They’re typically served with lingonberry jam and a side of bacon or ham.

Local Beverages

Glögg

Glögg is a popular Swedish mulled wine that’s typically served during the winter months. It’s made with red wine, spices, and sugar, and it’s served hot with raisins and almonds.

Julmust

Julmust is a popular Swedish soft drink that’s typically consumed during the Christmas season. It’s made with malt extract, spices, and carbonated water, and it has a rich, sweet flavor.

Where to Stay in Uppsala County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Uppsala County offers plenty of accommodation options, ranging from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels. Here are some of the top places to stay:

Hotell Kvarntorget

Hotell Kvarntorget is a stylish and modern hotel located in the heart of Uppsala. It offers comfortable rooms with all the amenities and is within walking distance of several cultural attractions.

Vandrarhem Uppsala Kungsängstorg

Vandrarhem Uppsala Kungsängstorg is a budget-friendly hostel that’s perfect for travelers on a tight budget. It’s located near several bus stops and offers comfortable rooms and shared kitchen facilities.

Pensionat Slottsgården

Pensionat Slottsgården is a charming guesthouse located in the historic town of Sigtuna. It offers comfortable and beautifully decorated rooms with a delicious breakfast served every morning.

What to Buy in Uppsala County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Uppsala County offers plenty of souvenirs and local products that make great gifts or keepsakes. Here are some of the top things to buy:

Viking-themed souvenirs

Uppsala County is known for its Viking heritage, and you can find plenty of Viking-themed souvenirs, such as jewelry, clothing, and home decor.

Swedish candies

Swedish candies, such as salty licorice and fruit gummies, are a popular and delicious souvenir to bring home. You can find them at many local markets and candy shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Uppsala County

Uppsala County has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors are encouraged to respect local customs and traditions. Here are some things to keep in mind:

Respect for nature

Nature is highly valued in Uppsala County, and visitors are encouraged to respect the natural environment. This includes not littering, staying on marked trails, and not disturbing wildlife.

Quiet hours

In many residential areas of Uppsala, there are designated quiet hours from 10 pm to 6 am. Visitors should be mindful of noise levels during these times so as not to disturb local residents.

Best Time to Visit Uppsala County

The best time to visit Uppsala County is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and pleasant. Spring and fall can also be beautiful times to visit, with colorful foliage and fewer crowds. Winter can be very cold and dark, but it’s also a great time to experience traditional Swedish holiday celebrations.

Do’s and Don’ts of Uppsala County

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Be mindful of noise levels in residential areas
  • Enjoy the beautiful natural scenery

Don’t:

  • Litter or disturb wildlife
  • Ignore designated quiet hours
  • Disrespect the cultural heritage of Uppsala County

Safety and Health

Safety in Uppsala County

Uppsala County is a safe and friendly destination with low crime rates. However, visitors are encouraged to be cautious when traveling alone at night, especially in urban areas.

Health in Uppsala County

Uppsala County has excellent healthcare facilities and services, and visitors can expect to receive high-quality medical care if needed. It’s also important to pack any necessary medications or medical supplies when traveling to Uppsala County.

Getting to Uppsala County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Uppsala County

The closest airport to Uppsala County is Stockholm Arlanda Airport, which is located approximately 30 minutes away by car or public transportation. It’s a major international airport with direct flights to many destinations around the world.

Bus Stations Near Uppsala County

Uppsala Central Station is the main bus station in the city, and it offers connections to many major cities in Sweden and beyond. There are also several smaller bus stations throughout Uppsala County that offer local and regional transportation options.

Other Options

There are also several train stations throughout Uppsala County, offering convenient connections to other parts of Sweden and neighboring countries. Additionally, visitors can rent a car or bike to explore the area at their leisure.

How to Get Around in Uppsala County

Uppsala County offers many transportation options for visitors, including buses, trains, taxis, and bikes. The local bus and train systems are efficient and affordable, making it easy to explore the area without a car. Visitors can also rent bikes or take taxis or private cars for more flexibility.

Where to Go Next from Uppsala County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Uppsala County is located in an ideal location for exploring other parts of Sweden and neighboring countries. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions:

Stockholm

The vibrant and historic capital city of Sweden is located just over an hour away by train or car from Uppsala County. It offers plenty of cultural attractions, nightlife, and scenic beauty.

Sigtuna

The charming and historic town of Sigtuna is located just a short drive from Uppsala County. It’s known for its picturesque streets, colorful houses, and beautiful lakeside setting.

Åland Islands

The beautiful and remote Åland Islands are located just a short ferry ride from Uppsala County. They offer stunning natural scenery, outdoor adventures, and a rich cultural heritage.

Conclusion

Uppsala County is a beautiful and welcoming destination that offers something for everyone. With its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural scenery, and delicious cuisine, it’s the perfect place to explore on your next vacation. Use this comprehensive travel guide to plan your trip and make the most of your time in Uppsala County.
