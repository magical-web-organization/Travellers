Your Comprehensive Travel Guide to Norrköping

Norrköping is a charming city located in the province of Östergötland, in southern Sweden. With a rich history, beautiful green spaces, and plenty of cultural attractions, it’s a great destination for any traveler. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take you through the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay in Norrköping, so you can plan your trip with ease.

List of Places to Visit in Norrköping

Norrköping is packed with things to see and do, whether you’re interested in history, culture, or nature. Here are some of the must-visit attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Industrial Landscape: This unique exhibit showcases the city’s industrial heritage, including factories, machines, and tools.
  • The Norrköping Art Museum: This museum houses a collection of modern and contemporary art.
  • The Interactive Museum of Science and Technology: This museum is dedicated to science and technology and features hands-on exhibits and activities for both kids and adults.
  • The Himmelstalund Rock Carvings: These ancient carvings date back over 2,500 years and depict various animals and humans.
  • The City Museum: This museum covers the history and development of Norrköping, from the Viking era to present day.

Natural Attractions of Norrköping: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Folkparken: This park is a popular spot for a picnic or a leisurely walk. It features a playground, a stage, and a dance pavilion.
  • The Norrköping Wildlife Park: This park is home to a variety of animals, including bison, wolves, lynx, and bears.
  • The Kolmården Zoo: This zoo is the largest in Scandinavia and is known for its African savannah exhibit.
  • The Motala Ström River: This river runs through the heart of Norrköping and offers some stunning views of the city.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Norrköping

Norrköping is surrounded by beautiful countryside, and there are many hiking and biking trails to explore. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • The Söderköpingleden trail: This trail takes you through lush forests, past lakes, and along the coast.
  • The Göta Canal trail: This trail follows the famous canal and offers some beautiful views of the surrounding countryside.
  • The Tinnerö Eklandskap Nature Reserve: This reserve is home to a variety of wildlife, including deer, foxes, and eagles.

Best Things to Do in Norrköping: Activities and Tours

If you’re looking for some fun things to do in Norrköping, here are some suggestions:

  • Take a boat tour of the Motala Ström River: This is a great way to see the city from a different perspective.
  • Go on a guided walking tour of Norrköping: You’ll learn about the city’s history and see some of its most iconic landmarks.
  • Visit the local farmers’ market: It’s a great place to sample some local produce, such as pickled herring, smoked salmon, and lingonberries.

Week-Long Itinerary for Norrköping

Here’s a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to Norrköping:

  • Day 1: Explore the city center and visit some of the museums, such as the Industrial Landscape and the Norrköping Art Museum.
  • Day 2: Spend the day hiking in the nearby nature reserves, such as Tinnerö Eklandskap and Kolmården Wildlife Park.
  • Day 3: Take a boat tour of the Motala Ström River and visit some of the local attractions, such as the Himmelstalund Rock Carvings.
  • Day 4: Visit some of the nearby towns and villages, such as Söderköping and Skänninge.
  • Day 5: Go on a guided walking tour of Norrköping and visit the Folkparken.
  • Day 6: Spend the day at the Kolmården Zoo, or visit the local farmers’ market.
  • Day 7: Relax in one of the city’s many parks, such as the Louis De Geer Park.

Food and Drink Scene of Norrköping

Norrköping has a vibrant food and drink scene, with plenty of local specialties to try. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pickled herring: This is a classic Swedish dish, often served with boiled potatoes and sour cream.
  • Smörgåsbord: This is a buffet-style meal, featuring a variety of dishes, such as herring, meatballs, and salmon.
  • Laxpudding: This is a baked dish made with potatoes and salmon.
  • Prinsesstårta: This is a traditional Swedish cake, made with layers of sponge cake, custard, and whipped cream.

Local Beverages

  • Norrköping beer: There are several local breweries in Norrköping, producing a range of delicious beers.
  • Must: This is a non-alcoholic, carbonated drink made from fermented apple juice.
  • Gammeldags julmust: This is a traditional Christmas drink, made from spices and carbonated water.

Where to Stay in Norrköping: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Norrköping has plenty of accommodation options to suit all budgets. Some of the best places to stay include:

  • Elite Grand Hotel Norrköping: This historic hotel is located in the heart of the city and features luxurious rooms and an on-site restaurant.
  • Hotel President: This hotel is located just a short walk from the train station and offers comfortable rooms at an affordable price.
  • STF Vandrarhem: This hostel is located in a quiet area of the city and is a great option for budget travelers.
  • The Lamp Hotel Norrköping: This boutique hotel features stylish rooms and is located in a converted industrial building.

What to Buy in Norrköping: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for some unique souvenirs or local products to take home, here are some ideas:

  • Handmade glassware: Norrköping is home to several glass factories, producing beautiful hand-blown glassware.
  • Traditional Swedish textiles: Look for textiles featuring the traditional Swedish patterns, such as Dala horses or floral designs.
  • Local gourmet products: Some of the best local products to bring home include pickled herring, lingonberry jam, and smoked salmon.

Local Culture and Customs of Norrköping

Swedish culture is known for its emphasis on simplicity, fairness, and equality. When visiting Norrköping, it’s important to be respectful of these values. Here are some customs to keep in mind:

  • Practice “allemansrätten”: This is the Swedish principle of “everyman’s right,” which means that people are free to roam and enjoy the outdoors, as long as they do not disturb or damage the environment.
  • Remove your shoes indoors: It’s customary to remove your shoes when entering someone’s home, so be prepared.
  • Be punctual: Swedes value punctuality, so make sure to arrive on time for appointments and meetings.

Best Time to Visit Norrköping

The best time to visit Norrköping is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny, and many outdoor events and activities are taking place. However, the city is also beautiful in the winter, with plenty of opportunities for skiing and ice-skating.

Do’s and Don’ts of Norrköping

When visiting Norrköping, here are some things to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine, such as pickled herring, smoked salmon, and lingonberries.
  • Explore the city’s museums and cultural attractions.
  • Take a boat tour of the Motala Ström River.

Don’t:

  • Assume everyone speaks English: While many Swedes do speak English, it’s always polite to learn a few basic Swedish phrases.
  • Be loud or disruptive in public places.
  • Skip the opportunity to enjoy the outdoors and natural attractions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Norrköping

Norrköping is generally a safe city, with low levels of crime. However, it’s important to be aware of your surroundings and take basic precautions to avoid pickpocketing or other petty theft.

Health in Norrköping

Sweden has a high standard of healthcare, and Norrköping is no exception. There are several hospitals and clinics in the city, and visitors can receive medical treatment if needed. It’s also important to have adequate travel insurance when traveling to Sweden.

Getting to Norrköping: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Norrköping

Norrköping is served by the Norrköping Airport, which offers flights to several destinations in Sweden and Europe. Alternatively, visitors can fly into Stockholm Arlanda Airport, which is located about 2 hours away by train or car.

Bus Stations Near Norrköping

Norrköping is served by several bus companies, with connections to nearby towns and cities. The main bus station is located in the city center, next to the train station.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Norrköping by train, with connections to major cities in Sweden and neighboring countries. There are also several car rental companies located in the city, for those who prefer to drive.

How to Get Around in Norrköping

Norrköping is a compact city, and many of the attractions are within walking distance of each other. However, visitors can also use the local bus system, or rent a bike to explore the city at their own pace.

Where to Go Next from Norrköping: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Norrköping is located in the heart of southern Sweden, making it a great base for exploring the region. Some nearby destinations to consider include:

  • Stockholm: The capital city of Sweden is just a few hours away by train or car.
  • Söderköping: This quaint town is known for its historic buildings and picturesque canal.
  • Götaland: This region of southern Sweden is home to several national parks and nature reserves, perfect for outdoor enthusiasts.

Conclusion

Norrköping is a charming city with plenty to offer visitors. Whether you’re interested in history, culture, or nature, you’re sure to find something to enjoy here. This comprehensive travel guide should help you plan your trip and make the most of your time in Norrköping. Happy travels!
