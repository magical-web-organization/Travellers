Introduction to Eksjö

Located in the heart of Småland, Eksjö is a scenic town that boasts an abundance of cultural heritage and natural beauty. Known for its well-preserved wooden houses, cobblestone streets, and historical landmarks, Eksjö offers a glimpse into the country’s rich history and vibrant culture.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you through the top things to see and do in Eksjö, including must-visit attractions, hiking and biking routes, food and drink options, and more. Whether you’re a history buff, nature lover, or simply looking for a unique travel experience, Eksjö is the perfect destination for you.

List of Places to Visit in Eksjö

From museums and historical landmarks to scenic views and outdoor adventures, Eksjö has something for everyone. Below we’ve listed some of the top places to visit in Eksjö:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Eksjö Museum - featuring an extensive collection of historical artifacts, including traditional clothing, furnishings, and tools.
  2. Eksjö Old Town - a well-preserved area of old wooden houses and cobblestone streets dating back to the 17th century.
  3. Eksjö Church - a beautiful and historically significant church dating back to the 16th century.

Natural Attractions of Eksjö: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Skurugata Canyon - a dramatic 800-meter-long canyon with towering cliffs and a babbling creek.
  2. Vättern Lake - a large freshwater lake located just a short drive from Eksjö, offering opportunities for swimming, boating, and other water sports.
  3. Norra Kvill National Park - a stunning nature reserve featuring hiking trails, waterfalls, and an abundance of wildlife.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Eksjö

Eksjö is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, offering a range of hiking and biking routes to explore the beautiful natural landscapes. Some of the popular hiking and biking routes include:

  1. The Skurugata Trail - a challenging hiking trail that leads to the stunning Skurugata Canyon.
  2. The Gränna Trail - a scenic biking route that takes you through picturesque villages and orchards.
  3. Kalvsvik Trail - a stunning hiking trail that takes you through deep forests, secluded lakes, and scenic viewpoints.

Best Things to Do in Eksjö: Activities and Tours

Apart from exploring the historical landmarks and natural beauty, Eksjö offers plenty of activities and tours for visitors. Some of the best things to do in Eksjö include:

  1. Brewery Tour - visit the Eksjö Brewery, take a tour of the facility, and sample some of their delicious brews.
  2. Canoeing - rent a canoe and explore the scenic Klarälven River.
  3. Horseback Riding - take a leisurely horseback ride through the beautiful forests and meadows of Eksjö.

Week-Long Itinerary for Eksjö

If you’re planning on spending a week in Eksjö, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day 1-2: Explore Eksjö’s historic landmarks and museums, including the Eksjö Museum and the Eksjö Old Town.

Day 3: Hike the Skurugata Trail, visit the stunning Skurugata Canyon, and take in the scenic views.

Day 4: Take a guided tour of the Eksjö Brewery, sample some delicious brews, and learn about the brewing process.

Day 5: Rent a canoe and explore the beautiful Klarälven River.

Day 6: Take a horseback ride through the forests and meadows of Eksjö.

Day 7: Visit the Norra Kvill National Park, hike the Kalvsvik Trail, and enjoy the breathtaking views.

Food and Drink Scene of Eksjö

Eksjö offers a vibrant and diverse food and drink scene, with plenty of delicious options to satisfy any palate. Some of the must-try dishes and local beverages include:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Småland Meatballs - a traditional dish featuring juicy meatballs served with lingonberry sauce and mashed potatoes.
  2. Ostkaka - a sweet and creamy dessert made with fresh cheese, served with raspberry jam and whipped cream.
  3. Raggmunk - a savory potato pancake served with bacon and lingonberry jam.

Local Beverages

  1. Beer - try some of the local brews at the Eksjö Brewery or any of the town’s pubs and bars.
  2. Aquavit - a traditional Swedish distilled spirit that’s popular throughout the Nordic countries.
  3. Lingonberry Juice - a refreshing and delicious drink made from locally grown lingonberries.

Where to Stay in Eksjö: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Eksjö offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Some of the best hotels, hostels, and guesthouses in Eksjö include:

  1. Eksjö Stadshotell - a centrally located hotel with elegant rooms and top-notch amenities.
  2. Eksjö Camping - a budget-friendly option that offers cozy cabins and campsites in a scenic setting.
  3. Eksjö Vandrarhem - a comfortable hostel that offers affordable accommodations and a friendly atmosphere.

What to Buy in Eksjö: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Eksjö is known for its beautiful wooden handicrafts, traditional textiles, and locally produced delicacies. Some of the best souvenirs and local products to bring home include:

  1. Dala Horses - traditional handcrafted wooden horses, which are a popular souvenir throughout Sweden.
  2. Linens - beautiful and durable textiles that are woven from locally grown flax.
  3. Cheese - try some of the locally produced cheese, which is made from high-quality dairy from the area’s farms.

Local Culture and Customs of Eksjö

Eksjö has a rich cultural heritage and strong community values, which are reflected in the town’s traditions and customs. Some of the key aspects of local culture and customs in Eksjö include:

  1. Respect for nature - the people of Eksjö have a deep appreciation for the natural beauty of the area and take great care to preserve it.
  2. Festivals and traditions - throughout the year, Eksjö hosts a range of festivals and events that celebrate the town’s heritage and traditions.
  3. Community spirit - Eksjö is known for its strong sense of community and welcoming atmosphere, making visitors feel right at home.

Best Time to Visit Eksjö

The best time to visit Eksjö is during the summer months, from June to August. During this time, the weather is warm and sunny, and there are plenty of outdoor activities and festivals to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Eksjö

Do:

  1. Respect the local customs and traditions.
  2. Take care of the environment and wildlife by following the Leave No Trace principles.
  3. Try the local cuisine and beverages, and support small local businesses.

Don’t:

  1. Litter or damage the natural landscape.
  2. Disrespect the local culture or traditions.
  3. Engage in any illegal activities, such as drug use or theft.

Safety and Health

Safety in Eksjö

Eksjö is generally a safe destination, with low crime rates and a welcoming atmosphere. However, visitors should take standard precautions such as keeping their valuables safe and avoiding unlit or deserted areas at night.

Health in Eksjö

Eksjö has a high-quality healthcare system, with several hospitals and clinics in the area. Visitors should ensure they have adequate travel insurance, including coverage for emergency medical treatment.

Getting to Eksjö: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Eksjö

The nearest airport to Eksjö is Jönköping Airport (JKG), located approximately 74km away. Other nearby airports include Stockholm-Arlanda Airport (ARN), Gothenburg Landvetter Airport (GOT), and Copenhagen Airport (CPH).

Bus Stations Near Eksjö

Eksjö has a well-connected bus station, with regular services to nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Eksjö by train, with regular services running from major cities such as Stockholm and Gothenburg.

How to Get Around Eksjö

Eksjö is compact and easy to navigate on foot or by bike. Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Eksjö: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Eksjö is located in a prime position for exploring other nearby destinations and attractions. Some of the best nearby destinations and excursions include:

  1. Stockholm - the capital city of Sweden, located just a few hours’ drive from Eksjö.
  2. Öland Island - a scenic island with abundant wildlife and unspoiled natural beauty.
  3. Astrid Lindgren’s World - a popular theme park based on the works of the beloved Swedish children’s author.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking to explore the rich cultural heritage, stunning natural landscapes, or simply enjoy the welcoming atmosphere, Eksjö is the perfect destination. Get inspired by our comprehensive travel guide, and plan your next adventure to this charming and picturesque town.
