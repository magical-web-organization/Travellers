Introduction to Tanum

Tanum is a picturesque town located on the western coast of Sweden. It has a rich cultural heritage, dating back to the Bronze Age, and is home to some of the most stunning natural landscapes in the region. Visitors to Tanum can take in breathtaking ocean views, hike through dense forests, and explore ancient rock carvings. This comprehensive guide will give you all the information you need to plan your perfect trip to Tanum.

List of Places to Visit in Tanum

Here are some of the best places to visit in Tanum:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Vitlycke Museum - This museum is home to some of the most spectacular Bronze Age rock art in the world. It also showcases the history and culture of the people who inhabited the area more than 3,000 years ago.

  2. Tanum Church - This beautifully preserved 12th-century church has a rich history dating back to the Viking Age. Visitors can take in the stunning architecture and learn about the church’s role in local culture.

  3. Rock Carvings in Tanum - These ancient rock carvings, which date back to the Bronze Age, depict everything from animals to human activities. They are considered to be some of the best-preserved examples of prehistoric art in the world.

Natural Attractions of Tanum: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Kosterhavet National Park - This national park is home to an incredible range of marine life, including seals, lobsters, and sea eagles. Visitors can also take in some of the most stunning coastal views in the region.

  2. Fjallbacka Archipelago - This group of small islands offers visitors a chance to explore unspoiled natural beauty and picturesque fishing villages.

  3. Svinesund Bridge - The Svinesund Bridge spans the strait between Sweden and Norway. Visitors can take in breathtaking views of the fjord and enjoy a scenic drive across the bridge.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tanum

Tanum is home to some amazing hiking and biking routes. Here are some of the best:

  1. The Tanumsleden Trail - This 50km hiking trail takes visitors through some of the most spectacular landscapes in the region, including dense forests, rocky coastlines, and rolling hills.

  2. The Kuststigen Trail - This 50km trail runs along the coast of Bohuslän, offering hikers stunning views of the ocean and the surrounding islands.

Best Things to Do in Tanum: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best activities and tours to do in Tanum:

  1. Kayaking Tours - Visitors can rent kayaks and explore the stunning coastline of Bohuslän.

  2. Lobster Fishing Tours - The region is known for its lobster fishing industry, and visitors can take part in a tour that includes catching and preparing fresh lobster.

  3. Wildlife Safaris - Visitors can embark on a guided safari to see some of the region’s most amazing wildlife, including seals, sea eagles, and moose.

Food and Drink Scene of Tanum

Visitors to Tanum can enjoy an incredible range of local delicacies and beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and drinks:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Saffranspannkaka - This delicious Swedish saffron pancake is typically enjoyed during the holiday season.

  2. Kanelbullar - These cinnamon buns are a staple in Swedish bakeries and are a must-try for visitors.

Local Beverages

  1. Julmust - This non-alcoholic beverage is typically enjoyed during the Christmas season and has a unique, spicy flavor.

  2. Akvavit - This traditional Scandinavian spirit is made from potatoes and flavored with herbs and spices.

Where to Stay in Tanum: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Here are some great options for accommodation in Tanum:

  1. Salt & Sill - This unique hotel is located on a small island in the Fjallbacka archipelago and offers guests stunning ocean views and a chance to experience life on the coast.

  2. Tanum Gestgifveri - This beautifully restored guesthouse is located in the heart of Tanum and offers guests comfortable rooms and delicious food.

What to Buy in Tanum: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to Tanum can bring back a range of unique and locally produced products. Here are some suggestions:

  1. Handmade pottery - The region is known for its beautiful pottery, including plates, bowls, and vases.

  2. Lobster - Visitors can purchase fresh, locally caught lobster to take home.

Local Culture and Customs of Tanum

The people of Tanum have a rich cultural heritage, with a history that dates back thousands of years. Visitors should take care to respect local customs and traditions, including the importance of environmental conservation and sustainable living.

Best Time to Visit Tanum

The best time to visit Tanum is during the summer months, between June and August, when the weather is warm and visitors can take advantage of the many outdoor activities and attractions in the region.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tanum

Do:

  1. Take care to respect local customs and traditions.

  2. Be mindful of the environment and take care not to damage the natural landscape.

Don’t:

  1. Damage ancient rock carvings or other cultural sites.

  2. Litter or pollute the environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tanum

Tanum is a very safe destination for visitors. However, tourists should take care to avoid leaving valuables unattended and to be aware of their surroundings when exploring the area.

Health in Tanum

Tanum has excellent healthcare facilities, including hospitals and medical clinics. Visitors should take care to bring any necessary medications with them, and to consult a doctor before traveling if they have any medical concerns.

Where to Go Next from Tanum: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors to Tanum can also explore other nearby destinations and attractions. Some great options include:
