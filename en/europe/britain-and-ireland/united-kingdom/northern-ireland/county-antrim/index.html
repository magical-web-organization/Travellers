Introduction to County Antrim

Located in Northern Ireland, County Antrim offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and vibrant city life. Known for its rugged coastline, scenic views, and world-renowned attractions, County Antrim is a must-visit destination in Northern Ireland.

List of Places to Visit in County Antrim

From culturally significant attractions to natural wonders and breathtaking views, County Antrim has something for everyone. Here is a list of the must-visit places in County Antrim:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Giants Causeway
  • Old Bushmills Distillery
  • Dunluce Castle
  • Murlough Bay
  • Glenariff Forest Park

Natural Attractions of County Antrim: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Rathlin Island
  • Dark Hedges
  • Carrick-a-Rede Rope Bridge
  • Ballintoy Harbour
  • Slemish Mountain

Hiking/Biking Routes of County Antrim

Local Culture and Customs of County Antrim

County Antrim has a strong cultural identity, and visitors can experience the unique customs and traditions of the local people. Here are some aspects of local culture worth exploring:

  • Traditional Irish Music
  • GAA Sports
  • St. Patrick’s Day Celebrations

Best Time to Visit County Antrim

The best time to visit County Antrim is during the summer months from June to August when the weather is mild and sunny. However, if you want to avoid crowds and enjoy lower prices, consider visiting in the shoulder seasons of April to May and September to October.

Do’s and Don’ts of County Antrim

To make the most of your trip to County Antrim, keep these do’s and don’ts in mind:

Do:

  • Explore the natural wonders of County Antrim
  • Experience the local music and culture
  • Try Irish cuisine and beverages
  • Respect the local customs and traditions

Don’t:

  • Overlook the historic and cultural significance of the attractions
  • Take risks in natural or adventurous surroundings without proper guidance and equipment

County Antrim is a safe place to travel, but it’s still important to be aware of potential risks and take necessary precautions. Here’s what you need to know about safety and health in County Antrim:

Safety in County Antrim

Health in County Antrim

  • Tap water is safe to drink in County Antrim

Getting to County Antrim: Transportation Options and Logistics

County Antrim is easily accessible and well-connected to major cities in Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland. Here are some transportation options and logistics to consider:

  • Belfast International Airport and George Best Belfast City Airport are both located within easy reach of County Antrim.
  • There are frequent bus services from Belfast to County Antrim and other nearby destinations.
  • Renting a car or bike is a great option for exploring the rural areas and scenic landscapes of County Antrim.

How to Get Around in County Antrim

Getting around County Antrim is relatively easy, with several options available:

  • Public transport including buses and trains are available.
  • Taxis and car rentals are also available.
  • Walking and cycling are perfect ways to explore County Antrim’s scenic areas.
