Introduction to Unst

Unst is one of the stunning islands in the Shetland Islands archipelago, located in the northernmost region of Scotland and the United Kingdom. It is the northernmost inhabited island of the country, nestled in the heart of the North Sea.

List of Places to Visit in Unst

Unst offers a wide array of attractions and activities, ranging from culturally significant historical sites to awe-inspiring natural beauty. We’ve listed the top-rated places to visit in Unst so that you can experience the best of this beautiful island.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Unst Heritage Centre
  2. Muness Castle
  3. Skidbladnir

Natural Attractions of Unst: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Hermaness National Nature Reserve
  2. Keen of Hamar
  3. Muckle Flugga Lighthouse

Hiking/Biking Routes of Unst

Unst boasts of a number of hiking and biking routes that let you explore the stunning countryside landscapes and views.

Local Culture and Customs of Unst

Unst is famous for its rich Viking heritage and traditions, which can be seen in its distinct architecture, landscape, and celebrations

Best Time to Visit Unst

The best time to visit Unst is from May to September when the weather is mild, and you can enjoy outdoor activities and explore the island’s natural beauty.

Do’s and Don’ts of Unst

Do:

  1. Respect local traditions and customs
  2. Explore the island’s natural beauty
  3. Try local delicacies and products

Don’t:

  1. Damage the fragile natural ecosystem
  2. Touch or disturb the wildlife
  3. Disrespect local residents and their property

Safety and Health

Safety in Unst

Unst is a safe and welcoming community with low crime rates. However, visitors should exercise caution when hiking or exploring the rugged terrain of the island.

Health in Unst

Visitors to Unst should have adequate travel insurance and access to medical facilities on the neighboring island of Yell.

Getting to Unst: Transportation Options and Logistics

Unst can be accessed via ferry and flight connections from mainland Scotland and other nearby islands.

Airports Near Unst

  1. Sumburgh Airport (LSI)
  2. Kirkwall Airport (KOI)

Bus Stations Near Unst

  1. Yell Bus Station
  2. Brae Bus Station

Other Options

  1. Ferries from mainland Scotland
  2. Ferries from nearby islands like Yell and Fetlar

How to Get Around in Unst

Getting around Unst is relatively easy, with a good road network, bike rentals, and guided tour options.

Where to Go Next from Unst: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Visit the nearby island of Yell
  2. Explore mainland Scotland
  3. Take a ferry to the Orkney Islands

Conclusion

Unst is a magical island that offers visitors a unique cultural and natural experience like no other. By following this guide, you’re sure to have an unforgettable adventure in the stunning Shetland Islands.
