Introduction to Largs

Largs is a town located in Ayrshire, South West Scotland, UK. It is a traditional coastal town that is a favorite destination for people seeking a peaceful break from city life. The town’s history dates back to the Viking times and has a rich history that can be seen throughout the town.

This comprehensive tour guide will help you explore the beauty of Largs and make your trip an unforgettable one. From the best places to visit, activities, food, where to stay, and more, this guide has got you covered.

List of Places to Visit in Largs

Whether you are interested in exploring historical sites, witnessing natural beauty, or just relaxing by the beach, Largs has something for everyone. Check out the list of top places you should visit in Largs.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Kelburn Castle and Country Centre – This 13th-century castle is a popular tourist destination, and visitors can explore its stunning gardens, cafes, and shops.

  2. Vikingar – The exhibition center tells the story of the Vikings and their influence on Scotland.

Natural Attractions in Largs: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. The Pencil Monument – It is a popular hiking spot for visitors as it gives a panoramic view of the Hunterston power station and the Isle of Arran.

  2. Largs beach – A beautiful sandy beach, which is perfect for relaxing, picnicking, or just exploring the shorelines.

Hiking/Biking Routes in Largs

  1. The Largs Heritage Trail – The trail starts at Largs railway station and takes you through the town’s historic sites and beautiful landscapes.

  2. The Fairlie Moor Trail – It takes you to the world-renowned Fairlie Moors, where you can explore the stunning scenery and wildlife.

Best Things to Do in Largs: Activities and Tours

  1. Largs Viking Festival – Happening every August, it is a week-long festival celebrating the town’s Viking past with events and activities for all ages.

  2. Largs Food Fest – This food festival offers visitors a chance to sample the local cuisine, with a variety of dishes on offer.

Food and Drink Scene of Largs

Must-Try Dishes

  1. The Lorne sausage – A traditional Scottish recipe, this sausage is a must-try when in Largs.

  2. Fish and chips – Largs has many seafood restaurants that offer freshly caught fish and chips. The award-winning Nardini’s is highly recommended.

Local Beverages

  1. Scottish Whisky – Largs has a range of whisky bars that offer visitors a chance to sample some of Scotland’s best whiskies.

  2. Largs Ale – Locally brewed ale, which can be enjoyed in some of the town’s bars, is a must-try for beer lovers.

Where to Stay in Largs: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. The Woodhouse Hotel – A historic hotel in the town center that offers guests a comfortable stay.

  2. The Starfish Guest House – A cozy guest house located near the beach and promenade.

What to Buy in Largs: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Largs Tartan – Visitors can buy tartan products that are unique to the town, including scarves, hats, and jackets.

  2. Scottish Whisky – Largs has a range of whisky shops that offer visitors a chance to buy some of Scotland’s best whiskies.

Local Culture and Customs of Largs

Largs has a rich cultural history, and visitors can see evidence of it throughout the town. The area is known for its Viking past, and the town holds a week-long Viking festival every August. The Scottish kilt is also a symbol of traditional culture that visitors can see in the town.

Best Time to Visit Largs

The best time to visit Largs is from June to August. The summer months offer visitors the best weather, making it easy to explore the town’s outdoor attractions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Largs

Do:

  1. Visit the Largs Viking Festival.

  2. Explore the Largs Heritage Trail.

Don’t:

  1. Litter on the beach or in public areas.

  2. Be disrespectful to local culture and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Largs

Largs is a safe town for tourists. It is important to take the same precautions you would take in any town or city to ensure your safety.

Health in Largs

Largs has excellent healthcare facilities, including a local hospital and several clinics.

Getting to Largs: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Largs

  1. Prestwick Airport – 30 minutes by car.

  2. Glasgow Airport – 50 minutes by car.

Bus Stations Near Largs

  1. Largs Railway Station – easily accessible via public transport.

Other Options

  1. By Car – Largs is easily accessible via the M8 motorway, which is 25 minutes away by car.

How to Get Around in Largs

The best way to get around Largs is on foot. Most of the town’s attractions are located in the town center, making it easy to explore on foot. Public transport is also available for visitors who want to explore further afield.

Where to Go Next from Largs: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Glasgow – A vibrant city that is famous for its culture and city attractions. 50 minutes away by car.

  2. Isle of Arran – Known as ‘Scotland in miniature,’ the island is famous for its natural beauty. Easily accessible by boat from Largs harbor.

Conclusion

Largs is a beautiful town that has a rich history and culture. Whether you want to explore its Viking past, enjoy its stunning natural beauty, or simply relax by the seaside, Largs has something to offer everyone. Use this comprehensive tour guide to plan your perfect trip to Largs.
