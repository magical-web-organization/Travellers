Introduction to Ross and Cromarty

Ross and Cromarty is a historic county and a former local government district in the Highland unitary council area of Scotland. Situated in the Scottish Highlands, Ross and Cromarty has stunning natural beauty, rugged landscapes, and rich cultural heritage.

From the scenic views of the Scottish coastline to the magnificent mountains, this place has something to offer everyone. The region is home to several charming towns and villages, each with its unique character and culture.

Ross and Cromarty is beautiful all year-round, but the best time to visit is May to September when the weather is mild and ideal for outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ross and Cromarty

Ross and Cromarty is a safe place to visit, but it’s best to be cautious while exploring the natural areas:

  • Always wear sturdy boots while hiking
  • Check the weather forecast and bring appropriate clothing
  • Carry plenty of water and food when venturing into remote areas

Health in Ross and Cromarty

There are several medical facilities located throughout the region, and for emergency services, dial 999. It’s essential to carry a first-aid kit while hiking and be aware of common health hazards such as ticks and midges.

Getting to Ross and Cromarty: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ross and Cromarty

Inverness airport is the nearest airport, located approximately 70 miles from Ross and Cromarty. Other airports include Aberdeen and Glasgow.

Bus Stations Near Ross and Cromarty

Several bus companies offer regular services to Ross and Cromarty, including CityLink and Stagecoach.

Other Options

If you want to explore the region by car, there are several car rental companies at Inverness airport and in the nearby towns and cities.

How to Get Around Ross and Cromarty

Ross and Cromarty has a limited public transportation system, so the best way to explore the region is by car or bicycle. Several car rental companies operate in the region, and bicycles can be rented from some of the local shops and hotels.
