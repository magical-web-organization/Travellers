Introduction to Peebles

Located in the Scottish Borders, Peebles is a charming town known for its stunning natural surroundings and rich cultural heritage. It is a popular tourist destination that offers a perfect blend of historic landmarks, outdoor adventures, and warm hospitality. If you’re planning a trip to Peebles, this comprehensive guide will help you make the most of your visit.

List of Places to Visit in Peebles

There are numerous attractions in Peebles that cater to different interests and age groups. Here are some of the must-visit places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tweeddale Museum and Gallery: This museum showcases the rich cultural heritage of Peebles through its impressive collection of artwork, historic objects, and interactive exhibits.
  • Neidpath Castle: An impressive 14th-century castle that offers visitors a glimpse into Scotland’s turbulent history.
  • St. Andrews Church: A beautiful historic church that features stunning architecture and a serene atmosphere.

Natural Attractions of Peebles: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Glentress Forest: This picturesque forest offers numerous hiking, biking, and camping opportunities amidst nature’s beauty.
  • Peebles Hydro: A grand hotel that is perfect if you want to relax and indulge in some pampering amidst scenic surroundings.
  • The John Buchan Way: An incredible hiking route that takes you through rolling hills and picturesque landscapes.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Peebles

Peebles is known for its extensive network of hiking and biking trails that cater to different difficulty levels and fitness levels. Whether you’re an avid hiker or a beginner biker, Peebles has something to offer. Some of the popular trails in Peebles include:

  • The Tweed Valley Forest Park: This beautiful park offers visitors a chance to explore the region’s woodland trails and rugged countryside.
  • The Southern Uplands Way: This 212-mile hiking route stretches from the west to the east coast of Scotland, blending stunning coastal and countryside views.
  • The Border Loop: A mountain biking route that offers a challenging ride and scenic views of the surrounding countryside.

Best Things to Do in Peebles: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re looking for adventure or just want to relax and have a good time, Peebles offers several activities and tours that cater to different tastes and preferences. Some of the best things to do in Peebles include:

  • Go on a guided cycling or hiking tour and explore the region’s stunning landscapes and historic landmarks.
  • Visit the local distilleries and taste some of Scotland’s finest whiskey blends.
  • Take a stroll around Peebles and soak in the town’s charming atmosphere that is steeped in history and culture.

Food and Drink Scene of Peebles

Peebles is a foodie’s paradise with several excellent local eateries that offer a wide range of dishes and drinks to suit different tastes and budgets. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cullen Skink: Scotland’s signature dish that is made with smoked haddock, potatoes, and creamy milk.
  • Haggis: A traditional Scottish dish that is made with sheep’s heart, liver, and lungs, mixed with oats and spices, and served with neeps and tatties.
  • Cullenbeeck: A hearty soup that is made with vegetables, smoked haddock, and milk.

Local Beverages

  • Whiskey: Scotland’s famous drink that is made from fermented barley and aged in oak barrels.
  • Beer: Peebles is known for its excellent microbreweries that offer a wide range of ale blends.
  • Gin: Scotland’s latest craze, Peebles is home to several artisanal gins that are distilled with local botanicals and herbs.
