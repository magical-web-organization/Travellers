Craignure, Scotland Travel Guide

Welcome to Craignure, a charming village located on the Isle of Mull in the Inner Hebrides. This picturesque destination is known for its stunning natural scenery, outdoor adventures, and fascinating history. Whether you’re looking to hike to the top of a mountain, learn about local culture and customs, or simply relax on a secluded beach, Craignure has something to offer everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Craignure

Craignure is home to a variety of must-see attractions, including culturally important museums and historical sites, as well as natural wonders that offer scenic views and thrilling outdoor adventures. Here are some of the top places to visit in Craignure:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Duart Castle - This stunning 13th-century castle is one of the most popular attractions in the area. Take a tour of the castle and learn about its fascinating history.

  2. Mull Museum - Discover the rich history and culture of the region at this fascinating museum.

  3. An Tobar - An art center that showcases the work of local artists and musicians.

Natural Attractions of Craignure: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Ben More - A popular hiking destination, this peak offers breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

  2. Calgary Bay - Relax on a secluded beach with crystal clear waters and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

  3. Lochbuie Standing Stones - These ancient stones offer a glimpse into the rich history of the area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Craignure

Craignure is home to some of the best hiking and biking trails in Scotland. Here are some of our top picks for outdoor adventures:

  1. Ben More - This challenging hike offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

  2. Isle of Mull Cycle Way - Take a leisurely bike ride through scenic countryside and charming villages.

  3. Torosay Castle Gardens - Walk through beautifully landscaped gardens and explore the castle grounds.

Best Things to Do in Craignure: Activities and Tours

From wildlife tours to historical sightseeing, Craignure offers a wide range of activities and tours to suit all interests. Here are some of the best things to do in Craignure:

  1. Wildlife Tours - Take a boat tour and see whales, dolphins, and seals up close.

  2. Island Hopping - Explore the surrounding islands and take in their charming villages and natural beauty.

  3. Horse Riding - Explore the countryside on horseback and enjoy the fresh air and stunning views.

Food and Drink Scene of Craignure

No visit to Craignure would be complete without trying some of the delicious local cuisine and beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Cullen Skink - This creamy soup made with smoked haddock and potatoes is a traditional Scottish dish.

  2. Haggis - This savory meat dish made with sheep’s liver and oats is a staple of Scottish cuisine.

  3. Cranachan - A sweet dessert made with cream, toasted oats, and raspberries.

Local Beverages

  1. Scottish Whisky - Scotland is renowned for its whisky, and there are many distilleries in the area that offer tours and tastings.

  2. Real Ale - Sample some of the best locally brewed beers and ciders at the many pubs and bars in the area.
