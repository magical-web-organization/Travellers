Introduction to Bethesda

Bethesda is a small town situated in Gwynedd, in North Wales. The town is surrounded by beautiful mountainous landscapes, including the Carneddau and Glyderau mountain ranges. Bethesda is a great destination for those seeking adventure, with its wealth of outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and canoeing.

Local Culture and Customs of Bethesda

Bethesda has a rich history and culture, with a Welsh speaking population. The town is famous for its slate industry and notable people, including the poet William Williams. Visitors are advised to respect local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Bethesda

The best time to visit Bethesda is from May to September when the climate is warm and dry. Winter in Bethesda can be harsh, with snowfall accompanying the cold weather.

Safety in Bethesda
