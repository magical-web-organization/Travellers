Introduction to Broad Haven

Located in Pembrokeshire, South Wales, Broad Haven is a picturesque seaside village surrounded by the rugged beauty of the Pembrokeshire Coast National Park. It’s a hidden gem for visitors seeking unspoiled natural beauty, outdoor activities, and authentic Welsh culture. In this travel guide, we’ll take you through everything you need to know to plan your perfect trip to Broad Haven.

List of Places to Visit in Broad Haven

Here is a list of the top attractions in Broad Haven:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • St Davids Cathedral: Located 20 minutes from Broad Haven, this stunning 12th century cathedral is an architectural masterpiece and a site of Christian pilgrimage.
  • Pentre Ifan: A neolithic burial chamber known as the “giant’s tomb”, dating back to 3,500 BCE. It’s considered one of the most important prehistoric sites in Wales.
  • The Castle: A 12th century castle ruin standing on a rocky outcrop overlooking the sea, providing a breathtaking panorama of the coast.

Natural Attractions of Broad Haven: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Pembrokeshire Coast National Park: Covering over 240 square miles of coastline, cliffs, beaches, and hills, the national park offers endless opportunities for outdoor adventures, including hiking, cycling, surfing, kayaking, and wildlife watching.
  • The Coastal Path: This 186-mile trail takes you along the entire Pembrokeshire coastline, and the section from Broad Haven to St David’s is considered one of the most beautiful parts of the path.
  • Marloes Sands: One of the most beautiful and secluded beaches in Pembrokeshire, featuring a wide expanse of golden sand, crystal-clear waters, and towering cliffs.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Broad Haven

Broad Haven and the surrounding area offer a wide variety of hiking and biking trails, suitable for all levels of fitness and experience. Here are some of the best:

  • Broad Haven to St David’s Coastal Path: This 14-mile hike takes you along the stunning coastline, passing by picturesque villages, secluded coves, and dramatic cliffs.
  • Marloes Peninsula: A circular hike of 7 miles, taking you to the edge of the Pembrokeshire Coast and sweeping views of the Irish Sea. The route passes through ancient woodlands, wetlands, and sandy beaches.
  • Preseli Mountains: For more experienced hikers, this 8-mile circular route explores the rugged hills and moors of Preseli Mountains.

Best Things to Do in Broad Haven: Activities and Tours

Broad Haven offers a wide range of activities and tours, perfect for those looking for an adventure. Here are some highlights:

  • Surfing: Broad Haven is one of the best surfing beaches in Wales, with consistent waves and reliable swells. There are a variety of surf schools and equipment rentals available in the village.
  • Kayaking: Explore hidden coves and caves along the Pembrokeshire coastline by kayak, with guided tours available from several local operators.
  • Boat Tours: Take a boat tour to Ramsey Island, a wildlife paradise that’s home to seals, puffins, and other sea birds. Trips depart from St Justinians and St David’s.

Food and Drink Scene of Broad Haven

The culinary scene of Broad Haven is rooted in the rich agricultural and fishing traditions of Pembrokeshire, with a focus on locally sourced ingredients and fresh seafood. Some must-try dishes include:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fresh Lobster and Crab: Served simply with garlic, butter, and lemon, the seafood in Broad Haven is some of the best you’ll find in Wales.
  • Welsh Lamb: Raised on the mountains and hillsides around Pembrokeshire, Welsh lamb is known worldwide for its tenderness and flavor.
  • Bara Brith: A traditional Welsh tea bread made with dried fruit, brown sugar, and spices. Perfect with a cup of tea and a dollop of clotted cream.

Local Beverages

  • Felinfoel Brewery: A family-owned brewery in Llanelli, Felinfoel is known for its range of smooth and flavorful ales.
  • Celtic Country Wines: This family-run vineyard produces award-winning fruit wines from locally grown grapes, berries and flowers.
  • Penderyn Whisky: Produced in the Welsh valleys, Penderyn is a premium single malt whisky that’s gaining a reputation as one of the best in the world.

Where to Stay in Broad Haven: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several accommodation options in and around Broad Haven, ranging from cozy guesthouses to luxury hotels. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Broad Haven Youth Hostel: This budget-friendly hostel offers basic but comfortable rooms, a fully equipped kitchen, and a great location minutes from the beach.
  • St Brides Spa Hotel: A luxurious 5-star hotel with breathtaking ocean views, spa facilities, and fine dining restaurant.
  • Pebbles B&B: A charming bed and breakfast in the heart of the village, offering spacious rooms, a hearty breakfast, and friendly hosts.

What to Buy in Broad Haven: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Take home a piece of Broad Haven with you by picking up some unique souvenirs and locally sourced products. Here are some ideas:

  • Welsh Blankets: These handmade wool blankets are a staple of Welsh culture, and available at several shops and markets in Pembrokeshire.
  • Pembrokeshire Sea Salt: Produced from the sea water of Pembrokeshire’s coast, this salt is known for its pure taste and health benefits.
  • Ceramic Pottery: Pembrokeshire is known for its pottery traditions, and many local artists sell their creations at the weekly farmers’ markets.

Local Culture and Customs of Broad Haven

Welsh culture runs deep in Broad Haven, with a proud and unique heritage that’s celebrated throughout the community. Here are a few customs to keep in mind:

  • Welsh Language: While most people in Broad Haven speak English fluently, Welsh is also commonly spoken, especially among older generations.
  • Eisteddfod: This traditional Welsh music and poetry festival takes place annually in different parts of Wales, and includes competitions, concerts, and cultural events.
  • Pub Culture: Pubs are an integral part of Welsh social life, and a great place to enjoy a pint of local ale and meet friendly locals.

Best Time to Visit Broad Haven

Broad Haven is a year-round destination, with each season offering its own unique charm and experiences. However, the best time to visit depends on your personal preferences and interests.

  • Spring to Summer: From April to June, the weather is mild, the countryside is lush and green, and wildflowers bloom along the coast. The summer months of July and August are the busiest, with warm temperatures and long days.
  • Autumn to Winter: From September to November, the crowds dissipate and the coastline takes on a dramatic beauty, with waves crashing against cliffs and stormy skies. Winter is quiet but cozy, with festive markets, warming cuisine, and roaring fires in local pubs.

Do’s and Don’ts of Broad Haven

Here are some key do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting Broad Haven:

Do:

  • Do take a hike along the Coastal Path for incredible views of the coastline.
  • Do try the local seafood.
  • Do learn some Welsh phrases and use them with locals.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural environment.
  • Don’t underestimate the power of the sea. Be cautious when swimming or surfing.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask locals for directions or recommendations.

Safety and Health

Safety in Broad Haven

Broad Haven is a safe destination, with low levels of crime and a welcoming community. However, visitors should be mindful of natural hazards such as strong currents and rocky coastlines, and take appropriate precautions while hiking or engaging in water sports.

Health in Broad Haven

There are several medical centers and pharmacies in the area, and emergency services are available 24/7. It’s recommended to take out travel insurance and pack a basic first aid kit for minor injuries or illnesses.

Getting to Broad Haven: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here are some transportation options for getting to Broad Haven:

  • By Car: Broad Haven is easily accessible by car, with several major roads leading into Pembrokeshire. Parking is available throughout the village.
  • By Bus: Several bus operators run services to and from Broad Haven, connecting it to nearby cities and towns. The closest bus station is in Haverfordwest, around 20 minutes away.
  • By Train: The closest train station is in Haverfordwest, around 20 minutes away, with connecting services to major cities such as Cardiff and London.

Airports Near Broad Haven

The nearest airports to Broad Haven are Cardiff Airport and Bristol Airport, both around 2 hours away by car.

Bus Stations Near Broad Haven

The closest bus station is in Haverfordwest, around 20 minutes away by car, with connecting services to nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

Taxis, private transfers, and car rentals are also available in the area, providing added flexibility and convenience for travelers.

How to Get Around in Broad Haven

The village of Broad Haven is small and easily walkable, with most attractions located within a 10-minute walk from the beach. Cycling is also popular, with several rental shops in the area. For exploring further afield, bus services are available from the village, or car rentals can be arranged.

Where to Go Next from Broad Haven: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Broad Haven is conveniently located for exploring other parts of Pembrokeshire and South Wales. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions:

  • St David’s: A charming small city with a rich history and stunning cathedral, only 20 minutes from Broad Haven.
  • Tenby: A picturesque seaside town with colorful houses, narrow streets, and a medieval castle, around 40 minutes from Broad Haven.
  • Brecon Beacons National Park: A scenic landscape of hills, valleys, and waterfalls, perfect for hiking and exploring Wales’ natural beauty, around 2 hours from Broad Haven.

Conclusion

Broad Haven is a hidden gem on the Welsh coast, offering endless opportunities for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, and relaxation. Whether you’re a seasoned surfer, a history buff, or a foodie, there’s something for everyone in this enchanting village. We hope this travel guide helped you plan your perfect trip to Broad Haven, and that you fall in love with its beauty and charm as much as we have. Safe travels!
