Introduction to Poulton-le-Fylde

Poulton-le-Fylde is a charming market town in Lancashire, North West England. It is a popular tourist spot and is known for its rich history, beautiful natural scenery and friendly locals. The town is steeped in history, with many buildings and landmarks dating back to the 15th century, including the Old Hall, St Chad’s Church and the Market Cross.

The town has a relaxed atmosphere and offers something for everyone, from outdoor activities to cultural attractions. Its proximity to the coast makes it a great destination for those looking for a beach getaway as well as those interested in exploring the surrounding countryside. Poulton-le-Fylde is easily accessible from several major cities in the UK, including Manchester and Liverpool, making it an ideal destination for a day trip or weekend away.

List of Places to Visit in Poulton-le-Fylde

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Poulton-le-Fylde Old Hall - a 15th-century manor house that is steeped in history and has been restored to its original state.
  • St Chad’s Church - a historic church dating back to the 14th century, with a striking tower and beautiful interior
  • Poulton-le-Fylde Civic Centre - an iconic building designed by architect Sir Basil Spence in the 1960s
  • Poulton-le-Fylde Library - a historic building that has been renovated to accommodate a modern library

Natural Attractions of Poulton-le-Fylde: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Wyre Estuary Country Park - a beautiful nature reserve with stunning scenic views of the coast and wildlife
  • Millennium Green - a serene park with a pond and beautiful walking paths
  • Kneps Farm Holiday Park - an idyllic countryside park with stunning views of the Lancashire countryside

Hiking/Biking Routes of Poulton-le-Fylde

  • Wyre Way - this beautiful walking trail covers a stretch of the River Wyre and offers stunning views of nature
  • Pilling Sands - this scenic route takes you through the beautiful Lancashire countryside to a picturesque beach
  • Fleetwood to Cleveleys Promenade - a flat and easy route that takes you along the beautiful coast

Best Things to Do in Poulton-le-Fylde: Activities and Tours

  • Wyre Estuary Kayak Tours - explore the stunning coastline and wildlife on a guided kayak tour
  • Farmer Parrs Animal World - a family-friendly attraction with fun activities and cute animals to see
  • Market Days - visit Poulton-le-Fylde’s bustling market, held every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday in the town centre

Safety and Health

Safety in Poulton-le-Fylde

Poulton-le-Fylde is a safe town to visit, but it is always important to take precautions when traveling. Be aware of your surroundings and keep valuables out of sight at all times.

Health in Poulton-le-Fylde

Health services are easily accessible in Poulton-le-Fylde. The nearest hospital is Blackpool Victoria Hospital, which is around a 15-minute drive from the town centre.

Getting to Poulton-le-Fylde: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Poulton-le-Fylde

The nearest airport is Manchester Airport, which is approximately an hour’s drive from Poulton-le-Fylde. Liverpool John Lennon Airport is also within easy reach, around an hour and a half’s drive away.

Bus Stations Near Poulton-le-Fylde

Poulton-le-Fylde Bus Station is located in the town centre and is well-connected to several major towns and cities in the region, including Blackpool and Preston.

Other Options

There are several car rental services available at Manchester Airport and Liverpool John Lennon Airport, making it easy to explore the town and surrounding areas at your own pace.

How to Get Around in Poulton-le-Fylde

The town is small and easily walkable, but there are also several bus services available for those who prefer not to walk.
