Introduction to Lytham St Annes

Lytham St Annes is a charming seaside town located in the county of Lancashire, in the North West of England. The town comprises two coastal towns, Lytham and St Annes-on-Sea, that are adjoined to form a delightful venue that is perfect for a visit all year round. The town is rich in history, with charming Victorian and Edwardian architecture and winding cobbled streets that transport visitors to a bygone era.

List of Places to Visit in Lytham St Annes

Below are the must-visit tourist attractions in Lytham St Annes that you should not miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Lytham Hall: a historic house that dates back to the 18th Century, with over 80 acres of beautiful parkland and gardens.

  2. Lytham Windmill: a Grade II listed building that dates back to 1805. Visitors can take a guided tour of the Windmill and learn about its rich history.

  3. Lytham Heritage Trail: a self-guided tour that showcases the history and architecture of the town. The trail includes significant sites such as the Courthouse, Lowther Pavilion, and the Lifeboat Museum.

Natural Attractions of Lytham St Annes: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Fairhaven Lake: a picturesque lake that is excellent for boating, fishing, and walking.

  2. St Annes Beach: an award-winning sandy beach that is perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and windsurfing.

  3. Ashton Gardens: a beautiful park that features flower gardens, waterfall, a bowling green, and a playground for children.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lytham St Annes

Lytham St Annes boasts excellent routes perfect for hiking and biking.

  1. St Annes Sand Dunes: Take a hike in the St Annes sand dunes and enjoy the sea views from atop the dunes.

  2. Lytham Hall Parkland Trail: This easy trail is perfect for a family hike or cycle.

  3. Carr Bridge Park: A four-mile loop trail that offers breathtaking views of the River Ribble.

Best Things to Do in Lytham St Annes: Activities and Tours

  1. Lytham Festival: An annual music festival that features a variety of artists performing in a beautiful outdoor setting.

  2. Lytham St Annes Art Collection: A great collection of paintings and sculptures by talented local artists.

  3. Golf at St Annes Old Links: A wonderful experience of playing golf amid a stunning landscape.

Food and Drink Scene of Lytham St Annes

Lytham St Annes offers a great dining experience, with several excellent restaurants and pubs scattered throughout the town. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Fish and Chips: A traditional British dish that is widely popular in St Annes.

  2. Lancashire Hotpot: A classic hearty dish that is perfect for cold evenings.

  3. Cream Tea: A must-try dessert experience that includes scones, clotted cream, and jam.

Local Beverages

  1. Thwaites Beer: A local beer that is brewed in Blackburn and is very popular in Lytham St Annes.

  2. Mrs. Dowson’s Ice Cream: An award-winning ice cream made from locally sourced ingredients.

Where to Stay in Lytham St Annes: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Lytham St Annes has several affordable and luxury accommodation options, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Some popular options are:
