Introduction to Chorley

Chorley, located in Lancashire, North West England, is a small market town surrounded by green hills and picturesque countryside. With a rich history dating back to the Roman Era, Chorley boasts numerous museums, historical landmarks, and natural attractions. Known for its friendly locals, this charming town is the perfect destination for a peaceful countryside getaway.

Safety and Health

Safety in Chorley

Chorley is generally a safe town to visit. However, visitors are advised to take standard safety precautions, such as keeping an eye on their belongings and avoiding walking alone in isolated areas at night.

Health in Chorley

Visitors to Chorley should ensure they have adequate health insurance coverage before travelling. In case of any medical emergencies, the nearest hospital is Chorley Hospital.
