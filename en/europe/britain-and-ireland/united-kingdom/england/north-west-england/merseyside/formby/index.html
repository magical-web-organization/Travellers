Introduction to Formby

Formby is a small coastal town in the northern part of Merseyside, England. Known for its scenic views, unspoiled beaches, and nature reserves, Formby is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. With its charming atmosphere, rich history, and abundance of outdoor activities, Formby makes for the perfect destination whether you’re an adventure seeker, history buff, or just someone looking to explore a new place.

List of Places to Visit in Formby

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Formby Museum

Formby Museum is a small museum located near the town center. It features exhibits on the town’s history, including its prehistoric roots, Viking invasions, and important landmarks.

Sefton Women’s History Group

Sefton Women’s History Group is a community group dedicated to preserving the history and stories of women who lived in the Sefton area. The group hosts events, talks, and exhibitions about the area’s strong, independent women throughout history.

Natural Attractions of Formby: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Formby Beach

Formby Beach is a must-see destination for anyone visiting the area. Located on the Irish Sea, this expansive beach boasts sand dunes, wildlife, and breathtaking sunsets.

National Trust Formby

National Trust Formby is a nature reserve located on Formby Point. It’s home to a variety of wildlife, including red squirrels, natterjack toads, and sand lizards. The reserve also has a number of hiking trails and is a great place for bird-watching.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Formby

The Sefton Coastal Footpath

The Sefton Coastal Footpath is a 22-mile trail that follows the coastline from Crosby to Southport, passing through Formby. The trail offers stunning views of the coastline and is a popular route for hikers and bikers alike.

Formby Nature Reserve

Formby Nature Reserve has several hiking trails that will take visitors through the park’s stunning landscapes, including beach, woodland, and dune habitats.

Best Things to Do in Formby: Activities and Tours

Formby Golf Club

For golf lovers, Formby Golf Club is a must-visit destination. Set against the backdrop of the coast, the golf course offers some truly breathtaking views.

Formby Pool

Formby Pool is a community pool and fitness center that offers a variety of activities, including swimming lessons, water aerobics, and a sauna.

Week-Long Itinerary for Formby

Day Activity
1 Start your trip with a visit to Formby Beach and take in the stunning views of the Irish Sea.
2 Hike through the trails of National Trust Formby and keep an eye out for some of the local wildlife.
3 Spend the day at Formby Golf Club and play a round of golf while enjoying the beautiful coastal scenery.
4 Explore the town’s history by visiting the Sefton Women’s History Group and Formby Museum.
5 Take a walk along the Sefton Coastal Footpath and soak up the ocean views.

7 Depart Formby and head out to explore other destinations in the area, such as nearby Liverpool.

Food and Drink Scene of Formby

Must-Try Dishes

Formby has a selection of restaurants serving various cuisines. Some must-try dishes include:

Fish and Chips

A classic British dish, fish and chips is a must-try in Formby. There are several restaurants and cafes in the area serving up this traditional fare.

Lamb Scouse

Lamb Scouse is a hearty stew that originated in Liverpool, but is commonly found in the surrounding areas, including Formby. It’s made with lamb, potatoes, and vegetables and is a perfect meal to warm you up on a chilly day.

Local Beverages

There are several local breweries and pubs in the area where you can sample some of the local beverages, including ales, lagers, and stouts.

Where to Stay in Formby: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Formby has several accommodation options to suit every budget and taste.

Formby Hall Golf Resort & Spa

Formby Hall Golf Resort & Spa is a 4-star hotel that boasts a range of luxurious amenities, including an 18-hole golf course, spa, and indoor pool.

Horse & Jockey Hotel

The Horse & Jockey Hotel is a charming, family-run hotel located in the heart of Formby. The hotel provides comfortable, affordable accommodations and is a great option for travelers on a budget.

What to Buy in Formby: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Formby Point Gifts and Souvenirs

Located near the beach, Formby Point Gifts and Souvenirs offers a selection of locally made gifts and souvenirs, including art, jewelry, and other crafts.

Local Culture and Customs of Formby

Formby has a rich history and strong sense of community. The area is known for its friendly locals, independent spirit, and numerous community groups and events.

Best Time to Visit Formby

The best time to visit Formby depends on what you’re looking for. The summer months (June-August) are popular for beach-goers, while the spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) are ideal for hikers and nature lovers.

Do’s and Don’ts of Formby

Do:

  • Explore the town’s history by visiting museums and landmarks.
  • Try the fish and chips and other local dishes.
  • Take in the stunning natural scenery and wildlife.

Don’t:

  • Litter or disturb the local wildlife.
  • Ignore beach safety signs and warnings.
  • Be disrespectful to locals or their customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Formby

Formby is a safe place to visit and crime rates are relatively low. However, visitors should take usual safety precautions, such as keeping an eye on their belongings and staying aware of their surroundings.

Health in Formby

There are several medical facilities in the area, including hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies.

Getting to Formby: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Formby

The nearest airports to Formby are Liverpool John Lennon Airport and Manchester Airport, both of which are easily accessible by public transportation or taxi.

Bus Stations Near Formby

Formby has a local bus station that provides transportation to various destinations in and around the town.

Other Options

Other transportation options include trains and taxis.

How to Get Around in Formby

Formby is a small town and can be easily navigated on foot or by bike. There are also taxis and buses available for those who need them.

Where to Go Next from Formby: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Liverpool

Liverpool is a lively city located just a short distance from Formby. The city is known for its rich history, vibrant nightlife, and world-famous football team.
