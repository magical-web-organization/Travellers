Introduction to Kirkby Lonsdale

Kirkby Lonsdale is a picturesque town located in Cumbria, North West England. The town is situated near the Lake District and Yorkshire Dales National Parks, making it an ideal location for outdoor enthusiasts. With its charming streets, historic architecture, and beautiful scenery, Kirkby Lonsdale is a must-visit destination for travelers.

List of Places to Visit in Kirkby Lonsdale

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Ruskin’s View: A scenic viewpoint that overlooks the River Lune and the beautiful countryside of the Lune Valley. This view was famously admired by John Ruskin, a 19th-century art critic, who described it as “one of the loveliest views in England”.
  • The Devil’s Bridge: A historic stone bridge that is believed to have been built during the 12th century. According to legend, the bridge was built by the devil himself in exchange for the soul of the first creature to cross it.
  • St. Mary’s Church: A beautiful 13th-century parish church known for its impressive architecture and stained glass windows.

Natural Attractions of Kirkby Lonsdale: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Lune Valley: A scenic valley that offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside. It is a popular destination for walking, cycling, and picnicking.
  • Devil’s Bridge Trail: A popular hiking trail that takes you through some of the most scenic areas of Kirkby Lonsdale. The trail starts at the Devil’s Bridge and takes you up to the summit of Casterton Fell.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kirkby Lonsdale

  • The Ingleton Waterfalls Trail: A scenic trail that takes you through some of the most beautiful waterfalls in the Yorkshire Dales National Park.
  • The Howgill Fells: A narrow range of hills that offer a variety of hiking trails with stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

Best Things to Do in Kirkby Lonsdale: Activities and Tours

  • Walking tours: Kirkby Lonsdale offers a number of guided walking tours that take you through the town’s historic streets and landmarks.
  • Food and drink tours: Discover the town’s fantastic food and drink scene by taking a guided tour of local pubs and restaurants.
  • Cycling tours: Enjoy the stunning countryside around Kirkby Lonsdale by taking a guided cycling tour.
