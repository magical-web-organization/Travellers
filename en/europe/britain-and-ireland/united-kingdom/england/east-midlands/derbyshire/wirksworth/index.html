Introduction to Wirksworth

Wirksworth is a delightful small market town situated in Derbyshire’s beautiful Peak District National Park. The town is blessed with an abundance of cultural and natural attractions, including historical landmarks, scenic views, hiking, biking trails, and much more. The town has a rich history, with records dating back to the Anglo-Saxon period. Its fascinating history and picturesque surroundings have made Wirksworth an attractive destination for visitors from all over the world.

List of Places to Visit in Wirksworth

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Wirksworth Heritage Centre

Located at St Mary’s Church, Wirksworth Heritage Centre offers visitors a glimpse into the town’s rich heritage through its exhibits and interactive displays. The centre also hosts a variety of events, workshops, and talks throughout the year.

The National Stone Centre

The National Stone Centre is a fascinating attraction that offers insights into the history of stone quarrying, geology, and the use of stone in modern construction. This educational centre has something for everyone interested in the subject.

Derbyshire Eco Centre

Derbyshire Eco Centre is an environmental education centre located just a short distance from Wirksworth town centre. The centre offers visitors an opportunity to learn about environmental conservation, recycling, and sustainable living.

Natural Attractions of Wirksworth: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Ecclesbourne Valley Railway

Ecclesbourne Valley Railway is a beautiful heritage railway that takes visitors on a scenic journey through the stunning Ecclesbourne Valley. The railway travels through picturesque villages and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

The High Peak Trail

The High Peak Trail is a fantastic walking and biking trail that spans over 17 miles, passing through the beautiful Derbyshire countryside and the stunning High Peak District National Park. The trail also provides visitors with breathtaking views of the surrounding area.

The StarDisc

The StarDisc is a beautiful stone circle that is located just a short walk from the town centre. The attraction is perfect for stargazing, and visitors are encouraged to come at night to experience the breathtaking views of the stars.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Wirksworth

Wirksworth is an excellent destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts, with numerous trails that cater to all skill levels. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Wirksworth:

  • The High Peak Trail - This 17-mile trail passes through the scenic Peak District National Park, offering hikers and bikers breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

  • The Tissington Trail - This easy-to-navigate hiking and biking trail offers a scenic journey through some of Derbyshire’s most beautiful countryside.

  • The Limestone Way - This challenging hiking trail spans over 46 miles and offers incredible views of the Peak District National Park.

Best Things to Do in Wirksworth: Activities and Tours

Arts and Culture

Wirksworth is known for its thriving arts and culture scene, making it the perfect destination for art enthusiasts looking to explore the local art scene. The town boasts several art galleries and museums that showcase the works of both local and nationally-renowned artists.

Outdoor Adventures

Wirksworth is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, with an abundance of activities for visitors to enjoy. The town boasts several walking and biking trails that offer stunning views of the surrounding countryside. Additionally, visitors can enjoy horse riding, fishing, and rock climbing, among other activities.

Historical Landmarks

The town has a rich history, and visitors can explore several historical landmarks and buildings, such as St Mary’s Church, Wirksworth Heritage Centre, and Cromford Mill, to name a few.

Local Culture and Customs of Wirksworth

Safety and Health

Safety in Wirksworth

Wirksworth is a safe destination for visitors, with the crime rate being relatively low. Visitors should be cautious while exploring the town’s remote areas, and avoid any suspicious activities.

Health in Wirksworth

Visitors should take the necessary precautions to avoid getting sick during their stay in Wirksworth. This includes staying hydrated, using sunscreen, and wearing appropriate clothing while exploring the town’s outdoor attractions.

Getting to Wirksworth: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Wirksworth

The closest airports to Wirksworth are:

  • East Midlands Airport
  • Manchester Airport
  • Birmingham Airport

Bus Stations Near Wirksworth

The nearest bus station to Wirksworth is Matlock Bus Station, located just five miles from the town centre. Matlock Bus Station offers regular connections to other parts of Derbyshire and the UK.

Other Options

Visitors can also take a train to the nearby Derby or Matlock and take a bus or taxi to Wirksworth.

How to Get Around in Wirksworth

Visitors can explore Wirksworth on foot or by bicycle. Alternatively, visitors can hire a taxi or car to explore the surrounding areas.
