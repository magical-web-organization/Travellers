Welcome to Kettering, a picturesque town in the Northamptonshire county of East Midlands, England. Kettering is a hub of cultural heritage, scenic trails, and fun-filled activities, making it an ideal destination for travelers seeking an offbeat vacation. In this travel guide, you will discover the best places to visit, eat, stay, and explore in Kettering, along with useful tips and information.

Introduction to Kettering

Kettering is a historic market town that once served as the center of the thriving wool trade in the region during the 19th century. Today, the town is a vibrant hub of cultural attractions and leisure activities. Kettering is situated in the heart of England, making it easily accessible from most cities. The town is surrounded by lush green countryside and picturesque villages, offering a perfect mix of urban and rural attractions.

List of Places to Visit in Kettering

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kettering boasts a rich history and is home to several fascinating museums and historical places that are worth exploring. Here are the top cultural attractions in Kettering:

  • The Manor House Museum: This museum is located in the heart of Kettering and offers visitors a glimpse into the town’s rich history. The museum hosts regular exhibitions, talks, and events throughout the year.

  • Kettering Art Gallery and Museum: This museum showcases a vast collection of arts and artifacts, ranging from contemporary works to historical relics. The gallery also hosts regular exhibitions and workshops for visitors.

  • Rushton Triangular Lodge: This iconic landmark was built in the 16th century and is renowned for its unique triangular design. The lodge is located in Rushton and is open to the public during the summer months.

Natural Attractions of Kettering: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kettering is surrounded by stunning natural beauty and offers several opportunities for outdoor adventures and scenic views. Check out these must-visit natural attractions in Kettering:

  • Wicksteed Park: This is one of the most popular attractions in Kettering, offering visitors a range of exciting activities, including amusement rides, boating, and picnicking.

  • Brampton Valley Way: This trail is perfect for hiking and cycling enthusiasts, offering stunning views of the countryside. The trail runs for 14 miles and passes through several picturesque villages along the way.

  • Rockingham Forest: This is a vast forested area that offers visitors a chance to hike, bike, and explore the wilderness. The forest is home to several wildlife species, including deer, squirrels, and birds.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kettering

Kettering is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and offers several hiking and biking trails that are worth exploring. Here are some of the best routes for hiking and biking in Kettering:

  • Brampton Valley Way: This trail runs for 14 miles and follows the former Northampton to Market Harborough railway line. The trail passes through several picturesque villages and offers stunning views of the countryside.

  • National Cycle Network Route 6: This cycle route connects London and the Lake District and passes through Kettering along the way. The trail offers scenic views of the countryside and is perfect for experienced cyclists.
