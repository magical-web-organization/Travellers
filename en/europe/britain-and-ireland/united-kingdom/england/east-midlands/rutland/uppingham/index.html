Introduction to Uppingham

Uppingham is a picturesque market town that lies in the heart of rural Rutland in the East Midlands region of England. This charming town has a rich history and a beautiful architecture which attracts many visitors every year. The town is easily accessible by car and public transport, making it an ideal location for a day trip or a longer stay.

List of Places to Visit in Uppingham

Here are some of the top attractions that you should visit when in Uppingham:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Uppingham School Museum - This museum will take you back in time to discover the rich history of the Uppingham School. The museum has a range of exhibits, including school archives, photographs, and artifacts.
  • St. Peter and St. Paul’s Church - This historic church is located in the heart of the town and is a must-visit for its beautiful architecture and fascinating history.
  • Uppingham Town Hall - This building is an excellent example of Georgian architecture and is believed to have been built in the late 18th century.

Natural Attractions of Uppingham: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Rutland Water - This is a beautiful man-made lake and nature reserve which offers many activities such as sailing, fishing, walking, cycling and bird watching.
  • Fineshade Woods - This is one of the best natural areas in the region for walking, cycling, and horse riding, with beautiful scenery and varied wildlife.
  • Lyddington Bede House - This historic house is set in lovely gardens and is the perfect place to enjoy a peaceful afternoon stroll.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Uppingham

Uppingham and the surrounding area have some of the best hiking and biking trails in the region. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Rutland Water Circuit - This 23-mile-long cycle route takes you around Rutland Water, offering stunning views and plenty of opportunities to stop and take in the scenery.
  • Fineshade Woods Trail - This 3.5-mile-long trail is perfect for a leisurely walk or bike ride with stunning views of the forest and plenty of wildlife to spot.
  • Uppingham to Lyddington - This 4.3-mile-long trail is a circular route that takes you past some of Uppingham’s historic landmarks, including Lyddington Bede House and St. Peter and St. Paul’s Church.

Best Things to Do in Uppingham: Activities and Tours

If you’re looking for something to do in Uppingham, here are some of the best activities and tours to consider:

  • Uppingham Walking Tours - Take a guided tour of Uppingham’s historic sites and learn about the town’s rich history and culture.
  • Rutland Cycling - Rent a bike and explore the beautiful countryside surrounding Uppingham at your own pace.
  • Rutland Water Cruises - Take a leisurely cruise around Rutland Water and enjoy breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

Food and Drink Scene of Uppingham

Uppingham has a fantastic food and drink scene, with plenty of local dishes and drinks to try. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pork Pie - This classic English dish is made with hot water crust pastry and is filled with seasoned minced pork.
  • Rutland Bitter - A locally brewed beer that has a distinctive hoppy taste.
  • Melton Mowbray Pork Pie - This pie is made with uncured pork encased in a hot water crust pastry.

Local Beverages

  • Rutland Water - This locally sourced mineral water is delicious and refreshing.
  • Rutland Gin - This gin is distilled using botanicals from the local area and has a unique taste.

Where to Stay in Uppingham: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Uppingham has a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets, from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels. Here are some of the best places to stay in Uppingham:

  • The Falcon Hotel - This historic hotel is located in the heart of Uppingham and offers comfortable rooms and excellent dining options.
  • The Crown Inn - This charming inn is located in the nearby village of Oakham and offers comfortable rooms and a warm welcome.
  • The Lake Isle Hotel -Located close to Rutland Water, this hotel offers beautiful views of the surrounding countryside and comfortable rooms for a relaxing stay.

What to Buy in Uppingham: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Uppingham has plenty of local products and souvenirs that make for great gifts and keepsakes. Here are some of the best products to buy in Uppingham:

  • Melton Mowbray Pork Pies - These pies are a must-try when visiting Uppingham, and make for a delicious gift to take home.
  • Rutland Water Souvenirs - There are plenty of souvenirs available to purchase at Rutland Water, including t-shirts, postcards and mugs.
  • Local Gin - Rutland Gin is a unique and delicious gin that makes for an excellent gift or souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Uppingham

Uppingham has a rich culture and history, with many traditions and customs still observed today. The town is well-known for its excellent schools and literature, as well as its long-standing agricultural traditions.

Best Time to Visit Uppingham

The best time to visit Uppingham is during the summer months from May to September when the weather is warm and sunny, and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy. However, Uppingham is beautiful year-round, and visitors can enjoy its charm and character at any time of the year.

Do’s and Don’ts of Uppingham

Do:

  • Do visit Rutland Water and take part in some of the outdoor activities on offer, such as cycling, sailing, or fishing.
  • Do try some of the local food and drink, such as Melton Mowbray Pork Pies and Rutland Gin.
  • Do take a walking tour of Uppingham and discover some of its fascinating history.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter – Uppingham is a beautiful town, so please take care of the environment and dispose of waste responsibly.
  • Don’t wear shoes inside some historic buildings and houses - it is a custom to take off shoes in some locations.
  • Don’t forget to check out the etiquette here, before enjoying the surroundings.

Safety and Health

Safety in Uppingham

Uppingham is a safe town to visit, with low levels of crime. Tourists are advised to take the usual precautions when traveling, such as keeping valuables safe and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Uppingham

Uppingham has an excellent health care system, with several hospitals, health centers, and pharmacies located throughout the town and surrounding area.

Getting to Uppingham: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Uppingham

The nearest airports to Uppingham are East Midlands Airport, Birmingham Airport, and Stansted Airport. All have good transport links to the town.

Bus Stations Near Uppingham

Uppingham has its own bus station which connects to nearby towns and cities such as Leicester and Peterborough. National Express and other coach companies operate buses to Uppingham.

Other Options

For those who prefer to travel by car, Uppingham is easily accessible from the A1 and M1 motorways. The town also has a train station, with regular services connecting Uppingham to London and other nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Uppingham

Uppingham is a small town, and many of the main attractions are within easy walking distance of each other. However, there are also bus services and taxis available for those who prefer not to walk or who are visiting the surrounding area.

Where to Go Next from Uppingham: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Uppingham is located in a beautiful part of the country, with many nearby destinations and excursions to enjoy. Here are some of the best places to visit:

  • Leicester - A vibrant city with a rich history and plenty of shopping, dining and cultural attractions.
  • Oakham - A charming market town that is known for its beautiful architecture and quaint streets.
  • Stamford - A picturesque town that has been featured in many films and television shows for its stunning architecture and beautiful countryside.

Conclusion

Uppingham is a charming town that offers visitors a wide range of activities, attractions, and experiences. Whether you’re interested in history, culture, nature, or food and drink, Uppingham has something for everyone. So, come to Uppingham and enjoy a memorable experience in the heart of rural England.
