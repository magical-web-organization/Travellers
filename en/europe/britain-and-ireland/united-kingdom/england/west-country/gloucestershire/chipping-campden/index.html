Introduction to Chipping Campden

Chipping Campden is a small market town located in the Cotswold district of Gloucestershire, West Country, England. This picturesque town is situated on the edge of the North Cotswolds and is known for its honey-colored buildings and traditional English architecture. The town offers visitors a range of shopping, dining, cultural, and historical experiences.

Best Things to Do in Chipping Campden: Activities and Tours

  • Take a walking tour of the town and appreciate the honey-colored buildings and traditional English architecture.
  • Visit the Court Barn Museum and learn about the town’s heritage and arts.
  • Explore the outdoors - go for a country walk or cycle along the Cotswold Way.
  • Attend the annual Chipping Campden Music Festival.

Week-Long Itinerary for Chipping Campden

Day Activity
1 Explore Chipping Campden town, St. James’ Church, and the Grevel House.
2 Take a hiking or biking trip to Dover’s Hill, and then head to the Cotswold Falconry Centre.
3 Head to the Batsford Arboretum, then move on to the Cotswold Farm Park.
4 Explore the local produce markets and have a traditional English lunch at the Noel Arms Hotel.
5 Discover the local arts and crafts scene at the Court Barn Museum, and then walk along the Diamond Way.
6 Visit the nearby Bidford Grange Country Park or the Warwick Castle.
7 End your trip with a fun-filled evening at the annual Chipping Campden Music Festival.
