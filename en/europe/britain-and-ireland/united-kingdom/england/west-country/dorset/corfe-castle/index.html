Introduction to Corfe Castle

Corfe Castle is a charming village in the county of Dorset, situated in the West Country of England. It is a historic town that is best known for its magnificent castle ruins that overlook the village. The castle has a deep and fascinating history, having played significant roles in the English Civil War and the wars of the Roses. Today, Corfe Castle is a popular tourist destination, with plenty of things to see and do for visitors of all ages and interests.
