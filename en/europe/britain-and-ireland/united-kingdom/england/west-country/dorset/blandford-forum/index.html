Introduction to Blandford Forum

Nestled in the county of Dorset in West Country, England, lies the charming town of Blandford Forum. Known for its stunning Georgian architecture, lively market square, and beautiful countryside, Blandford Forum offers a unique experience for travelers seeking an off-the-beaten-path destination.

With plenty of cultural and historical attractions, outdoor activities, and a vibrant food and drink scene, Blandford Forum is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to the United Kingdom.

List of Places to Visit in Blandford Forum

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Blandford Forum is home to many culturally significant attractions, including a variety of museums and historical landmarks. Here are some of the most popular:

  • Blandford Fashion Museum: Located in the heart of the town, this museum showcases a collection of clothing and accessories from the Georgian era to the present day.
  • Blandford Town Museum: This museum provides an insight into the town’s history and showcases various artefacts from the Georgian era to the modern day.
  • Blandford Forum Parish Church: A stunning example of Georgian architecture, the parish church of Blandford Forum is a must-visit attraction.

Natural Attractions of Blandford Forum: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Blandford Forum is surrounded by stunning countryside, making it a perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the top natural attractions to visit:

  • Hod Hill: This Iron Age hill fort offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding countryside and is a perfect spot for a picnic or hike.
  • Trailway: A 14-mile walking and cycling trail that follows the old railway line from Sturminster Newton to Blandford Forum.
  • Riverside Park: Located on the banks of the River Stour, this park offers stunning views and is an excellent spot for a relaxing walk.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Blandford Forum

Blandford Forum is surrounded by beautiful countryside, making it a great location for hiking and biking. Here are some of the best routes to check out:

  • Hod Hill: The Iron Age hill fort offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding countryside and is a great spot for a hike or bike ride.
  • Trailway: A 14-mile walking and cycling trail that follows the old railway line from Sturminster Newton to Blandford Forum
  • The North Dorset Trailway: A gentle cycling trail that passes through picturesque villages and peaceful farmland.

Best Things to Do in Blandford Forum: Activities and Tours

In addition to the natural and cultural attractions, Blandford Forum offers a range of activities and tours to keep visitors entertained. Here are some top things to do in Blandford Forum:

  • Blandford Forum Market: A lively market held every Thursday and Saturday on the town’s market square.
  • The Corn Exchange: A venue that offers a variety of events, including concerts, plays, and exhibitions.
  • Blandford Brewery Tour: Take a tour of the local brewery and sample some of their award-winning ales.

Food and Drink Scene of Blandford Forum

Blandford Forum’s food and drink scene is diverse and offers something for everyone. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to sample when visiting:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Dorset Blue Vinney: A traditional blue cheese produced exclusively in Dorset, made from unpasteurized cows’ milk and has a unique taste.
  • Dorset Apple Cake: A scrumptious cake made with apples and cinnamon and is often served with custard or cream.

Local Beverages

  • Badger Ale: A locally brewed ale that offers a variety of flavours and strengths.
  • Dorset Cider: Produced with apples grown in the region, Dorset Cider is a refreshing drink with a distinctive taste.
