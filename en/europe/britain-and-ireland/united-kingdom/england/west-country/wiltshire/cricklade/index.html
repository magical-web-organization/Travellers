Introduction to Cricklade

Cricklade is a small town located in the Thames Valley, on the River Thames. It is nestled between the Cotswold Hills to the north and the Marlborough Downs to the south, providing visitors with breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside. The town combines a rich history, charming streets, and warm and friendly people, making it a popular destination for tourists visiting England.

Week-Long Itinerary for Cricklade

7 days itinerary plan for travelers exploring Cricklade is:

  • Day 1: Arrive and explore the town center.
  • Day 2: Visit Cricklade Museum and St. Sampson’s Church.
  • Day 3: Head to the Cotswold Water Park; enjoy kayaking, hiking, and water sports.
  • Day 4: Explore the North Meadow Reserve.
  • Day 5: Take a hot air balloon ride over the Thames Valley.
  • Day 6: Visit the Swindon and Cricklade Railway; take a ride on a steam train.
  • Day 7: Head to the North Wessex AONB; enjoy horseback riding and outdoor adventures.
