St Austell is a beautiful town located in the county of Cornwall, in West Country, England. With its vast landscapes and natural beauty, St Austell is known for being one of the most picturesque towns in Cornwall. Its warm climate, rugged coastline, and sandy beaches make it a popular vacation spot for tourists visiting the United Kingdom.

Introduction to St Austell

St Austell is situated on the River Austell and is a leading town for industrial activities with china clay in Cornwall. The town has a population of over 22,000 and has a lot of history and culture to explore. It is located just a few miles from the stunning Eden Project and the Lost Gardens of Heligan, making it an ideal base for exploring some of the best natural and cultural attractions in Cornwall.
