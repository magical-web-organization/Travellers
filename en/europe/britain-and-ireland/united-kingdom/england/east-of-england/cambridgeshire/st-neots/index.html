Introduction to St Neots

St Neots is a charming town located in Cambridgeshire, East of England, United Kingdom. It is known for its scenic views, diverse culture, and historical significance. The town has a vibrant community, making it an ideal destination for travelers who want to experience English life outside of the city. In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, what to buy, and local customs in St Neots.

List of Places to Visit in St Neots

If you’re wondering what to see in St Neots, our list of places to visit will guide you.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

St Neots has a rich historical significance, and some of the must-visit museums and historical places include:

  • St Neots Museum: This award-winning museum showcases the history and culture of St Neots and has several exhibitions on display.
  • The St Neots Picture Gallery: The picture gallery boasts some of the most spectacular paintings and portraits from local artists.
  • St Mary’s Church: This church dates back to the 12th century and is an excellent example of Norman architecture.

Natural Attractions of St Neots: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

St Neots is surrounded by natural beauty, making it the ideal destination for nature enthusiasts. Some of the must-visit natural attractions include:

  • Paxton Pits Nature Reserve: This reserve has several trails, and bird hides and boasts an array of wildlife and bird species.
  • The Riverside Park: This park is perfect for picnics and has a boating area.
  • Priory Country Park: This park has several trails and boasts beautiful lakes.

Hiking/Biking Routes of St Neots

Are you a fan of hiking or biking? St Neots has several hiking and biking routes that showcase the town’s natural beauty. Some of the best routes to consider include:

  • The St Neots Green Wheel: This route takes you along local green spaces, village lanes, and nature reserves.
  • The Ouse Valley Way: This route takes you along the River Great Ouse, and you’ll witness the natural beauty of St Neot’s riverside landscape.
  • Grafham Water Circular Walk: This walk takes you around the reservoir and has stunning views throughout.

Best Things to Do in St Neots: Activities and Tours

If you’re wondering what to do in St Neots, below are some of the must-try activities and tours:

  • St Neots River Cruise: Take a scenic river cruise and witness the town’s beauty from the water.
  • St Neots Ghost Walk: Take an eerie tour of some of the town’s most haunted places, accompanied by expert guides.
  • St Neots Beer Festival: This festival is held annually and features some of the best locally brewed beers and ciders.

Week-Long Itinerary for St Neots

If you have a week to spend in St Neots, below is an itinerary that showcases the town’s best features:

Day 1-2

  • Visit St Neots Museum and St Neots Picture Gallery
  • Take in the views of the Paxton Pits Nature Reserve
  • Take a St Neots river cruise in the evening

Day 3-4

  • Explore the scenic beauty of Grafham Water
  • Hike the Ouse Valley Way trail or the St Neots Green Wheel
  • Visit St Mary’s Church

Day 5-6

  • Take the St Neots Ghost Walk tour
  • Visit Priory Country Park
  • Enjoy drinks at the St Neots Beer Festival

Day 7

  • Buy souvenirs from the local markets and shops
  • Relax at the Riverside Park
  • Enjoy dinner and drinks at local restaurants and bars

Local Culture and Customs of St Neots

St Neots has a rich cultural heritage, and the locals are friendly and welcoming to visitors. Some of the local customs include:

  • Saying please and thank you
  • Queuing in an orderly manner
  • Tipping is not customary but appreciated

Best Time to Visit St Neots

The best time to visit St Neots is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is mild and sunny. However, the town is beautiful to explore all year round.
