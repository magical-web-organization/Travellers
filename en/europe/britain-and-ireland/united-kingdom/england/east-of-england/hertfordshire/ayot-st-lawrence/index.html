Introduction to Ayot St Lawrence

Ayot St Lawrence is a picturesque village located in Hertfordshire, East of England. The village is steeped in history and boasts an array of cultural and natural attractions that make it an ideal destination for travelers seeking a tranquil and charming escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take a closer look at the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more in and around Ayot St Lawrence.

List of Places to Visit in Ayot St Lawrence

Whether you are interested in exploring historical sites or enjoying outdoor activities, Ayot St Lawrence has something for everyone. Here are some of the top attractions to add to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Shaw’s Corner: This is the former home of renowned playwright George Bernard Shaw. Visitors can explore the home and gardens, learn about Shaw’s life and works, and enjoy special events and exhibitions throughout the year.
  • St Lawrence Church: Located in the heart of the village, this historic church dates back to the 12th century and features stunning architectural details and beautiful stained glass windows.
  • Ayot Greenway: This is a public footpath that runs through Ayot St Lawrence and surrounding areas. The trail passes through several historic sites, including the ruins of a Tudor palace and Roman baths.

Natural Attractions of Ayot St Lawrence: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mardley Heath Nature Reserve: This 33-acre nature reserve is home to an array of wildlife and plant species, making it a great destination for nature lovers. Visitors can enjoy hiking trails, birdwatching, and picnicking.
  • Ayot Park: This is a picturesque park that features lovely gardens and scenic walking paths that are perfect for a leisurely stroll.
  • Ayot St Lawrence Common: This is a beautiful natural area that is perfect for long walks and picnics. The area offers plenty of opportunities for wildlife spotting, including deer, pheasant, and rare bird species.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ayot St Lawrence

Ayot St Lawrence is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts, with plenty of scenic hiking and biking trails to explore. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Ayot Greenway: This 4.5-mile footpath is perfect for hikers and bikers alike, and passes through several historic sites and natural areas.
  • The Alban Way: This 6.5-mile trail runs from St Albans to Hatfield, passing through several villages along the way. The trail is perfect for cyclists and walkers who want to explore the local countryside.
  • Sherrardspark Wood: This is a woodland area that offers several walking trails through picturesque scenery. It is also a popular spot for mountain biking.

Best Things to Do in Ayot St Lawrence: Activities and Tours

Whether you want to explore the village’s history, enjoy the great outdoors, or sample local cuisine, Ayot St Lawrence offers plenty of activities and tours for visitors. Here are some of the top things to do:

  • George Bernard Shaw Walking Tour: This is a guided walking tour that takes visitors through the village and introduces them to the life and works of George Bernard Shaw.
  • Ayot St Lawrence Food Tour: This is a walking tour that takes visitors to some of the village’s top eateries, where they can sample delicious local cuisine and beverages.
  • Ayot St Lawrence Ghost Tour: This is a guided walking tour that takes visitors through the village’s historic sites and introduces them to some of its most famous ghostly residents.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ayot St Lawrence

If you have a week to spend in Ayot St Lawrence, here is a sample itinerary that will help you make the most of your time:

Day 1: Arrive in Ayot St Lawrence and explore the village’s historic sites, including St Lawrence Church and Shaw’s Corner.

Day 2: Embark on a guided walking tour of the village focusing on its literary heritage and visit the nearby Roman baths.

Day 3: Spend the day hiking or biking along the Alban Way or Ayot Greenway.

Day 4: Visit Mardley Heath Nature Reserve and enjoy a picnic lunch amid the scenic surroundings.

Day 5: Take a food tour of Ayot St Lawrence and sample local cuisine and beverages.

Day 6: Explore the nearby town of St Albans, which is home to a magnificent cathedral and plenty of shops and restaurants.

Day 7: Enjoy a leisurely day at Ayot Park or Ayot St Lawrence Common before departing Ayot St Lawrence.

  • Sticky Toffee Pudding: This is a sweet dessert that is made with dates and topped with a rich toffee sauce.
  • Beef and Ale Pie: This is a classic dish that is made with tender beef, vegetables, and local ale.
