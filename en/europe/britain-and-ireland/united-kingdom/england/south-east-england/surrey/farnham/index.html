Introduction to Farnham

Nestled in the heart of Surrey, Farnham is a charming town known for its rich history, stunning natural beauty, and welcoming community. From its medieval castle to its picturesque countryside, Farnham has something for every traveler.

Whether you’re seeking a relaxing retreat or an adventure-packed vacation, Farnham is the perfect destination. With its close proximity to London and easy access to transportation, this town is a must-visit for any traveler exploring the United Kingdom.

List of Places to Visit in Farnham

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Farnham Castle: Built in the 12th century, Farnham Castle is a historic gem located in the heart of the town. Guided tours are available for travelers to learn more about the castle’s rich history.

  • Museum of Farnham: Showcasing Farnham’s local history, the Museum of Farnham exhibits an impressive collection of objects and archives related to the town’s past and present.

  • Waverley Abbey: A peaceful ruin of a former monastery, Waverley Abbey is a must-see for history buffs and nature lovers alike. Located near the River Wey, Waverley Abbey is an ideal spot for a picnic or a scenic hike.

Natural Attractions of Farnham: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Frensham Ponds: With its crystal-clear waters and sandy beaches, Frensham Ponds is a popular spot for swimming, boating, and picnicking.

  • Hog’s Back: This stunning ridge offers breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside and is home to a variety of walking and hiking trails.

  • Alice Holt Forest: A large forested area with picnic spots and walking trails, Alice Holt Forest offers a range of outdoor activities for travelers.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Farnham

Farnham offers a multitude of hiking and biking trails for travelers to explore. Here are some of the best:

  • Surrey Hills: This Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty is home to some of the best hiking and biking trails in the region.

  • North Downs Way: One of England’s most popular long-distance trails, the North Downs Way crosses through Farnham and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

  • Birdworld to Alice Holt Loop: A 10-mile circular route that takes travelers through beautiful countryside, past the Birdworld aviary, and through Alice Holt Forest.

Best Things to Do in Farnham: Activities and Tours

  • The Rural Life Centre: A living museum that showcases rural life from the 1800s to the present day. The museum offers guided tours and interactive exhibits.

  • Hogs Back Brewery: A craft brewery that offers tours and tastings of their award-winning beers.

  • Farnham Park: A scenic park with walking trails, sports grounds, and a golf course.

Week-Long Itinerary for Farnham

Day 1: Visit Farnham Castle and explore the town center.

Day 2: Take a leisurely stroll through Alice Holt Forest.

Day 3: Visit the Rural Life Centre and learn about Farnham’s rural history.

Day 4: Bike or hike the North Downs Way.

Day 5: Explore the town’s museums, including the Museum of Farnham.

Day 6: Enjoy a picnic by Frensham Ponds.

Day 7: Take a day trip to nearby Guildford or London.

Food and Drink Scene of Farnham

Must-Try Dishes

  • Farnham Lamb: A local specialty that features succulent lamb from the nearby Surrey Hills.

  • Surrey Hills Beer: Farnham is home to several local craft breweries, including the award-winning Hogs Back Brewery.

  • Ploughman’s Lunch: This classic British dish features a selection of meats, cheeses, and breads, and is a popular dish in the town’s many pubs.

Local Beverages

  • Gin: The Gin Kitchen, a local distillery, produces a variety of award-winning gins.

  • Cider: A refreshing and locally produced drink, cider is a popular choice in Farnham’s many pubs and restaurants.

Where to Stay in Farnham: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Farnham House Hotel: A charming hotel located in a Victorian manor house, the Farnham House Hotel offers luxurious accommodations and stunning gardens.

  • The Hog’s Back Hotel & Spa: This elegant hotel features a spa, swimming pool, and a range of accommodations to suit any traveler’s needs.

  • Bel & The Dragon: A cozy gastropub with rooms, Bel & The Dragon offers comfortable accommodations and delicious meals.

What to Buy in Farnham: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Hogs Back Brewery Beer: Bring home a selection of award-winning beers from this local craft brewery.

  • Gin Kitchen Gin: The Gin Kitchen produces a range of unique and delicious gins that make for a great souvenir.

  • Surrey Hills Lamb: Take home a taste of the local cuisine by purchasing some Surrey Hills lamb to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Farnham

Farnham has a friendly and welcoming community that prides itself on its strong ties to history and tradition. Locals are happy to share their knowledge of the town’s rich cultural heritage with visitors, and travelers are encouraged to immerse themselves in local festivities and customs.

Best Time to Visit Farnham

Farnham is a year-round destination, with something to offer in every season. However, the best time to visit is during the summer months when the weather is warm and pleasant, and outdoor activities are at their peak.

Do’s and Don’ts of Farnham

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.

  • Take advantage of the town’s natural beauty and outdoor activities.

  • Explore the town’s history and cultural heritage.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local people or property.

  • Leave litter in public spaces.

  • Ignore safety warnings and guidelines.

Safety and Health

Safety in Farnham

Farnham is a safe and welcoming town, but travelers should still take the necessary precautions to ensure their safety. Be aware of your surroundings, lock up valuables, and take care when exploring the town’s natural areas.

Health in Farnham

Farnham has several medical clinics and hospitals in case of a medical emergency. It is also important to take precautions against tick bites, as Lyme disease is prevalent in the area.

Getting to Farnham: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Farnham

  • London Heathrow Airport (LHR)

  • London Gatwick Airport (LGW)

Bus Stations Near Farnham

  • Farnham Bus Station

  • Aldershot Bus Station

Other Options

  • By Train: Farnham is easily accessible by train from London and other major cities.

  • By Car: Farnham is located near several major motorways, making it easy to reach by car.

How to Get Around in Farnham

Farnham is a walkable town, and many of its attractions are within walking distance of each other. Taxis and buses are also available for those who prefer not to walk.

Where to Go Next from Farnham: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Guildford: A charming town with plenty of shopping, dining, and cultural attractions.

  • London: The bustling metropolis of London is just a short train ride away from Farnham.

Conclusion

Farnham is a hidden gem that has something for every traveler. Whether you’re seeking natural beauty or rich cultural heritage, Farnham is a must-visit destination. From its friendly locals to its stunning natural scenery, Farnham is sure to leave a lasting impression on any traveler.
