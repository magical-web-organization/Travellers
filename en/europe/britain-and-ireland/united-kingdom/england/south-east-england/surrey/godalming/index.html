Introduction to Godalming

Godalming is a historic market town in Surrey, South East England. With its idyllic setting, the town is a popular destination for tourists looking for a relaxed holiday in the countryside. Godalming is situated on the banks of the River Wey, and the town’s historic center, known for its well-preserved timber-framed buildings, is a designated conservation area.

List of Places to Visit in Godalming

Whether you are interested in history, culture, or outdoor activities, Godalming has plenty to offer. Here are some of the top attractions in Godalming:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Museum of Surrey: Located in nearby Woking, the Museum of Surrey houses a vast collection of artifacts that showcase Surrey’s social, cultural, and industrial history. It also has an impressive geological exhibit.
  • Godalming Museum: Housed in an old coaching inn, the Godalming Museum celebrates the town’s history and heritage through its collection of photographs, artifacts, and documents.
  • Loseley Park: This magnificent manor house dates back to the 16th century and boasts a beautiful garden and parkland. It is open to visitors throughout the year, and there are guided tours available.
  • Winkworth Arboretum: This stunning arboretum is located just outside Godalming and contains over 1,000 species of trees and shrubs from around the world. Its striking autumn colors and spring blooms make it one of the most picturesque places in the area.

Natural Attractions of Godalming: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The River Wey: The River Wey is a beautiful waterway that meanders through Godalming. Visitors can enjoy a leisurely walk along the riverbank, rent a boat, or try their hand at fishing.
  • Chantry Wood: This ancient forest is a great place for hiking and picnicking. Visitors can enjoy the scenic views and the fresh air as they explore the wood’s trails.
  • The Devil’s Punchbowl: Located just a short drive from Godalming, the Devil’s Punchbowl is a stunning natural landmark that boasts some of the most spectacular views in the Surrey Hills.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Godalming

Godalming and the surrounding countryside are a haven for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best hiking and biking trails in the area:

  • The Greensand Way: This long-distance trail runs from Surrey to Kent, passing through Godalming along the way. It offers stunning views of the countryside and is a great way to experience the area’s natural beauty.
  • The Wey South Path: This 19-mile trail follows the River Wey from Guildford to Godalming, passing through charming villages and picturesque countryside.
  • The Surrey Hills: The Surrey Hills offer a range of hiking and biking trails, from gentle strolls to challenging climbs. There are also several designated mountain biking trails for those looking for an adrenaline rush.

Best Things to Do in Godalming: Activities and Tours

In addition to its natural beauty and cultural attractions, there are plenty of activities and tours to enjoy in Godalming. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Godalming Ghost Tour: This spooky tour takes visitors on a journey through Godalming’s haunted past, visiting historic buildings and sites with a reputation for paranormal activity.
  • Godalming Food Tour: This delicious tour takes visitors on a culinary journey through the town, sampling some of the best food and drink that Godalming has to offer.
  • Paddleboarding on the River Wey: For a unique way to experience the river, try paddleboarding. There are several providers in the area that offer paddleboard rental and lessons.

Food and Drink Scene of Godalming

Godalming has a vibrant food and drink scene, with plenty of local specialties to try. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sussex Pond Pudding: This traditional dessert is made with suet pastry, lemon, and whole lemon encased in the pastry shell. It is steamed for several hours to create a soft and sticky pudding.
  • Local Cheese: Godalming is known for its award-winning cheeses, including the Sussex Slipcote and Munster-style cheeses.
  • Godalming Sausages: These tasty sausages are made with pork and chestnuts and are a popular local dish.

Local Beverages

  • Hogs Back Brewery: This local brewery produces a range of real ales, including its award-winning TEA (Traditional English Ale) and Hogstar Lager.
  • Silent Pool Distillery: Located just outside Godalming, Silent Pool Distillery produces a range of premium spirits, including gin, vodka, and liqueur.

Local Culture and Customs of Godalming

Godalming has a rich heritage, and its residents are proud of their town’s history and traditions. Here are some local customs and practices to be aware of:

  • Respect for History: Godalming has a well-preserved historic center, and locals take great pride in the town’s heritage. Visitors are encouraged to show respect for the town’s buildings and landmarks.
  • Community Spirit: Godalming has a strong sense of community, and locals are friendly and welcoming to visitors.
  • Environmental Awareness: Godalming is surrounded by beautiful countryside, and locals are passionate about protecting the natural environment. Visitors are encouraged to respect the area’s wildlife and landscapes.
