Introduction to Henley-on-Thames

Nestled in Oxfordshire, South East England, Henley-on-Thames is a charming town that attracts visitors with its rich history, cultural offerings, scenic beauty, and outdoor recreation opportunities. The town is situated on the banks of the River Thames, making it a popular spot for boating and river walks. Henley-on-Thames is also renowned for hosting the famous annual Royal Regatta, a five-day racing event that attracts participants and spectators from around the world. In this comprehensive travel guide, you’ll discover everything you need to know about exploring Henley-on-Thames, including places to visit, food and drink scene, accommodations, local culture, transportation, safety and health, and nearby destinations.

List of Places to Visit in Henley-on-Thames

From museums and historical landmarks to natural attractions and outdoor adventures, Henley-on-Thames has plenty of things to see and do. Here is a list of top places to visit in Henley-on-Thames:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Henley-on-Thames has a rich history that is reflected in its many museums and historical landmarks. Here are a few must-visit cultural attractions:

  • River & Rowing Museum: Learn about the history of the River Thames and the sport of rowing in this interactive museum.
  • The Henley Showroom: Explore the heritage of Henley’s industrial past by visiting this museum that showcases the evolution of brickmaking in the area.
  • The Chantry House: Take a tour of this 18th century house that houses a museum exhibiting life in Henley-on-Thames during the Georgian period.
  • Greys Court: Discover the grandeur and history of this Tudor country house and its beautifully landscaped gardens.

Natural Attractions of Henley-on-Thames: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Henley-on-Thames is surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty, including rolling hills and picturesque villages. Here are some of the best natural attractions that are worth checking out:

  • Thames Path: Take a stroll along the scenic Thames Path, a 184-mile walking path that follows the River Thames from its source to the sea.
  • Henley-on-Thames Riverside: Explore the riverside of Thames, offering scenic views, a variety of restaurants, cafes an bustling atmosphere.
  • Henley-on-Thames Kayaking: Enjoy kayaking down the River Thames with the many kayaking companies that operate in the town.
  • The Hambleden Valley: Relish in the natural beauty of this picturesque valley with its winding river, rolling hills, and scenic footpaths.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Henley-on-Thames

For outdoor enthusiasts, Henley-on-Thames offers a myriad of hiking and biking routes that offer stunning views along the way. Here are few routes to try:

  • The Chiltern Way: This 134-mile long route covers the Chiltern Hills, an Area of Outstanding Natural Beauty. It offers dramatic landscape views and is a particular hit with hikers.
  • Thames Path National Trail (Also Listed Above)
  • Henley Woodland Walk: Enjoy peaceful woodland environment with your loved ones in the heart of Henley.

Best Things to Do in Henley-on-Thames: Activities and Tours

When it comes to activities and tours, Henley-on-Thames offers something for everyone. Here are some must-try experiences:

  • Henley Royal Regatta: This internationally renowned rowing event takes place annually in Henley-on-Thames every July. Watch the races or even participate in them.
  • Henley Literary Festival: Participate in this fantastic literature festival featuring impressive talks, workshops, character parades and much more.
  • The Kenton Theatre: Head to this small, quaint town theatre, which provides show year-round for you to catch.

Food and Drink Scene of Henley-on-Thames

Henley-on-Thames has a diverse and exciting food and drink scene, offering a range of options from fine dining to hearty pub meals. Here’s what you should try:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Traditional Fish and Chips: Enjoy fresh, locally caught fish served with crispy chips wrapped in paper, at the numerous traditional fish and chip shops in the town.
  • Henley Fudge: Satisfy your sweet tooth cravings with this much-loved English sweet and treat yourself to a deliciously rich slice of Henley Fudge.
  • Cheeses: Taste the variety of English cheeses and sample styles you may not have tried before with the many options found in the town’s local markets.

Local Beverages

  • Henley Cider: Take a sip of locally produced cider, which exhibits exceptional quality and flavour.
  • Ale and Beer: Savor English ales and beers in many of the local breweries and pubs, who pride themselves on using only locally sourced ingredients.

Local Culture and Customs of Henley-on-Thames

Henley-on-Thames has a rich local culture and customs that are worth exploring. Here are some of the local traditions and customs:

  • Henley Royal Regatta: This popular event is a world-renowned sporting and social occasion, attracting thousands of rowers and visitors from around the world.

Best Time to Visit Henley-on-Thames

Henley-on-Thames is lovely year-round, but the best time to visit is between May and September when the weather is warm and sunny. The town gets particularly busy during the Royal Regatta, so plan accordingly.

Safety and Health

When travelling to Henley-on-Thames, it is important to take safety and health seriously.

Safety in Henley-on-Thames

  • Look out for other River traffic when using the River and be mindful of rules and regulation
  • Be aware of pick-pocketers when in crowded tourist areas.
  • In case of emergency, dial “999” for immediate assistance.

Health in Henley-on-Thames

  • Check in with local travel clinics and be up to date with the recommended vaccinations.
  • Carry sunscreen and use it regularly to protect yourself from harmful UV rays.
  • Bring medication for any known conditions and have it stored safely
