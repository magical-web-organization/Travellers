A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Ashford, Kent

Ashford, a beautiful town in Kent in South East England, offers an abundance of cultural and natural attractions for visitors. From picturesque hiking and biking routes to historical landmarks, this town has everything to suit every type of traveler. Plan your trip to Ashford with our comprehensive guide filled with useful information and insights.

Introduction to Ashford

Located in Kent, a county in South East England, Ashford is a town that’s steeped in history and natural beauty. Its close proximity to London, making it an easily accessible destination for travelers who wish to explore the town’s medieval architecture, explore the countryside, and enjoy several outdoor activities.

List of Places to Visit in Ashford

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Ashford has several historical landmarks and museums that exhibit the town’s rich history and culture. Here are some places to visit:

  • The Ashford Borough Museum
  • Willesborough Windmill
  • Godinton House and Gardens
  • St. Mary’s Church
  • Wye National Nature Reserve
