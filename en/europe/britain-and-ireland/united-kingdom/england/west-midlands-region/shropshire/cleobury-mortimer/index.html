Introduction to Cleobury Mortimer

Located in West Midlands, England, Cleobury Mortimer is a charming market town that is steeped in rich history and culture. From cultural attractions to natural wonders and outdoor activities, there is something for everyone in Cleobury Mortimer.

List of Places to Visit in Cleobury Mortimer

Whether you are interested in learning about the town’s past or exploring its beautiful natural surroundings, Cleobury Mortimer has plenty of attractions worth visiting. Here are some of the top places to add to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Cleobury Mortimer Museum: This museum explores the town’s rich history, showcasing unique artifacts and exhibits that provide insight into the area’s past.

  • St. Mary’s Church: This beautiful church boasts stunning architecture and dates back to the 12th century.

Natural Attractions of Cleobury Mortimer: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Cleobury Mortimer Country Walks: There are numerous walking routes throughout the surrounding countryside that offer stunning views of the town and its surroundings.

  • Brown Clee Hill: This nearby hill provides breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Cleobury Mortimer

Cleobury Mortimer’s beautiful countryside offers numerous opportunities for hiking and biking, making it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Some of the best routes to explore include:

  • The Shropshire Way: This 139-mile trail passes through Cleobury Mortimer and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside as well as the opportunity to explore other nearby towns and attractions.

  • The Clee Hills Challenge: This challenging route is perfect for experienced hikers and bikers and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Best Things to Do in Cleobury Mortimer: Activities and Tours

Aside from exploring the town’s cultural and natural attractions, there are plenty of other activities and tours to enjoy in Cleobury Mortimer. Here are some of the best:

Food and Drink Scene of Cleobury Mortimer

Cleobury Mortimer has a thriving food and drink scene that showcases the best of local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

  • Shropshire Fidget Pie: This savory pie is made with potatoes, apples, onions, and pork.
