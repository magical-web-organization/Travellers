Introduction to Stratford-upon-Avon

Stratford-upon-Avon, located in Warwickshire in the West Midlands region of England, is best known as the birthplace of the acclaimed playwright, William Shakespeare. This scenic town attracts millions of visitors each year who come to explore its cultural heritage, natural beauty, and rich history.
