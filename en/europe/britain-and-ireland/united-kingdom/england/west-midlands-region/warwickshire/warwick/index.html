Introduction to Warwick

Warwick is a charming county town located in Warwickshire, West Midlands, England. The town is brimming with history and is one of the most picturesque places to visit in the United Kingdom. From stunning gardens and castles to museums and natural reserves, there’s something for everyone in Warwick.
