Introduction to West Midlands (county)

West Midlands is a metropolitan county located in the West Midlands region of England in the United Kingdom. The county is famous for its industrial past, rich history and heritage, culture, and economy. It boasts of a diverse population and a wide range of attractions that make it a perfect destination for tourists.

Best Things to Do in West Midlands (county): Activities and Tours

Local Culture and Customs of West Midlands (county)
