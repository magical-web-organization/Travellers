Introduction to Clonmacnoise

Clonmacnoise is a charming ancient monastic site located in County Offaly, in the East Coast and Midlands region of Ireland. Founded in the 6th century by St. Ciarán, it is considered one of Ireland’s most important religious centers. The ruins of the site include three high crosses, a cathedral, seven churches, and two round towers.
