Introduction to Port St Mary

Nestled on the southern coast of the Isle of Man, Port St Mary is a delightful village known for its natural beauty, rich history, and cultural heritage. The village was named after the Virgin Mary and has been an important port since the 19th century. Port St Mary is a perfect holiday destination for travelers who love hiking, biking, and exploring museums and historical sites.
