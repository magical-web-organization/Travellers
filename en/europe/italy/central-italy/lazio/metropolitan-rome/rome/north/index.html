Introduction to North

North is a neighborhood in Metropolitan Rome that boasts a rich cultural heritage and beautiful natural landscapes. It is located in the Lazio region of Central Italy and is easily accessible by public transportation. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler to Rome, North is a must-visit destination.

List of Places to Visit in North

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

North is home to a variety of cultural attractions that offer a glimpse into Rome’s rich history. The must-visit museums and historical places in North include:

  1. Villa Borghese - a beautiful park and art-filled villa that houses a vast collection of paintings, sculptures, and antiques.
  2. MAXXI - National Museum of 21st-Century Arts - a modern museum that showcases contemporary works of art, architecture, and design.
  3. National Gallery of Modern and Contemporary Art - displays a vast collection of Italian and international art, dating back to the 19th century.
  4. Vatican Museums - the world-renowned museums that display an extensive collection of art and artifacts from ancient to modern times.
  5. Palazzo Barberini - a grand baroque palace that houses one of the most significant art collections in Rome.

Natural Attractions of North: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

North is also an excellent destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. The natural attractions of North include:

  1. Villa Borghese Gardens - one of the most beautiful parks in Rome, with stunning natural views and numerous fountains and sculptures.
  2. Bioparco di Roma - a zoological park that is home to more than 1200 animals, including endangered species from around the world.
  3. Monte Mario - the highest hill in Rome that offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city.
  4. Parco degli Acquedotti - an ancient park that is home to some of the most significant aqueducts of ancient Rome.
  5. The Tiber River - a beautiful river that runs through Rome and offers a tranquil escape from the city’s hustle and bustle.

Hiking/Biking Routes of North

North offers several excellent hiking and biking trails, including:

  1. Appia Antica Trail - a historic trail that runs along the ancient Roman road, the Appian Way.
  2. Monte Mario Park - a perfect spot for biking or hiking with beautiful views of the city.
  3. Villa Borghese Gardens - a great place to explore on foot or by bike while admiring the beautiful park’s natural beauty.

Best Things to Do in North: Activities and Tours

North offers many activities and tours to keep visitors entertained, including:

  1. Segway tours - A fun and unique way to explore the city that offers the freedom to explore while covering more ground than on foot.
  2. Food tours - Samples delicious local dishes from some of North’s best restaurants.
  3. Bike tours - A great way to explore the beautiful natural scenery of North.

Week-Long Itinerary for North

A week-long itinerary for North could include:

Day 1: Visit Villa Borghese Gardens and explore the neighborhoods surrounding the park. Day 2: Spend the day exploring the Vatican Museums and St. Peter’s Basilica. Day 3: Take a bike tour of North’s parks and natural scenery. Day 4: Experience North’s cultural attractions, including MAXXI and Palazzo Barberini. Day 5: Enjoy a Segway tour of Rome’s historic sites, including the Roman Forum and Colosseum. Day 6: Explore the AcquaPaola Fountain and Parco degli Acquedotti. Day 7: Experience Rome’s outdoor lifestyle at the Bioparco di Roma and enjoy dinner at one of North’s best restaurants.

Food and Drink Scene of North

Must-Try Dishes

North is famous for its delicious cuisine, and visitors should try the following dishes:

  1. Cacio e Pepe - a classic Roman pasta dish made with Pecorino Romano cheese and black pepper.
  2. Saltimbocca alla Romana - a dish of veal, prosciutto, and sage.
  3. Carciofi alla Romana - a traditional Roman dish of artichokes, garlic, and parsley.
  4. Suppli’ - a Roman street food made from rice, tomato sauce, and mozzarella cheese that is deep-fried.
  5. Gelato - a popular Italian ice cream that can be found in North’s many gelaterias.

Local Beverages

North is famous for its wine and beer, and visitors should try the following:

  1. Frascati - a dry white wine that is produced in the nearby town of Frascati.
  2. Birra Moretti - a classic Italian beer that pairs perfectly with North’s famous dishes.
  3. Limoncello - a lemon liqueur that is typically served as a digestif after dinner.

Where to Stay in North: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

North offers a variety of accommodation options for every type of traveler, from luxury hotels to budget hostels. Some of the best hotels in North include:

  1. Hotel Art by the Spanish Steps - a luxury hotel with a central location and beautiful views of the Spanish Steps.
  2. Portrait Roma - a stylish boutique hotel with only 14 rooms, each individually designed.
  3. Augusta Lucilla Palace - a modern hotel with a great location that offers comfortable rooms at an affordable price.

What to Buy in North: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to North should bring home some of the following souvenirs and local products:

  1. Limoncello - a popular lemon liqueur that is produced in the Lazio region and makes a great gift.
  2. Olive oil - Lazio is known for producing some of Italy’s best olive oil.
  3. Handmade leather goods - Rome is famous for its leather goods, and North has several shops that offer high-quality items.

Local Culture and Customs of North

North is a friendly and cosmopolitan neighborhood that welcomes visitors from all over the world. Visitors should be aware of the following cultural customs:

  1. Dress appropriately when visiting religious sites.
  2. Respect local customs and traditions.
  3. Be mindful of noise levels, especially at night.

Best Time to Visit North

The best time to visit North is from April to June and September to October when the weather is pleasant, and the crowds are smaller than during the high season in July and August.

Do’s and Don’ts of North

Do:

  1. Take the time to explore North’s parks and natural scenery.
  2. Try the local cuisine and wine.
  3. Check out North’s cultural attractions, including MAXXI and Palazzo Barberini.

Don’t:

  1. Expect small shops and business owners to speak English fluently.
  2. Wear revealing clothing when visiting religious sites.
  3. Drink too much and behave inappropriately.

Safety and Health

Safety in North

North is a safe neighborhood, and visitors should take the same precautions they would in any major city. Pickpocketing can be an issue, so be mindful of your belongings in crowded areas.

Health in North

Italy has a public healthcare system that provides free treatment to all EU citizens. Visitors from outside the EU should have travel insurance that covers medical treatment.

Getting to North: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near North

The nearest airports to North are Rome Fiumicino (FCO) and Rome Ciampino (CIA). There are several transportation options from both airports, including taxis, buses, and trains.

Bus Stations Near North

Rome’s main bus station is the Roma Tiburtina station, which offers connections to destinations throughout the city and beyond.

Other Options

North is well-connected by public transportation and is easily accessible by bus, metro, and tram.

How to Get Around in North

North is best explored on foot or by public transportation. Visitors can purchase a Roma Pass, which offers unlimited public transportation and discounts on museums and attractions.

Where to Go Next from North: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

North is the perfect base for exploring nearby destinations and excursions, including:

  1. Vatican City - the smallest country in the world, located within Rome’s city limits.
  2. Tivoli - a beautiful hilltop town with stunning gardens and historic villas.
  3. Ostia Antica - a well-preserved ancient Roman city that is located just outside Rome.

Conclusion

North is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Rome. With its cultural attractions, natural beauty, and delicious cuisine, North has something for everyone. Plan your trip to North today and discover all that this beautiful neighborhood has to offer.
