Introduction to Rome

Welcome to Rome - the eternal city that will leave you breathless! Rome is the capital of Italy and one of the world’s most popular tourist destinations, and for good reason. The city is brimming with ancient architecture, breathtaking art, mouth-watering cuisine, and a fascinating history. As you explore Rome, you will discover it’s a city filled with winding streets, beautiful piazzas, and majestic fountains. In this travel guide, we will explore the top places to visit, best things to do, where to stay, local culture and customs, safety, health, transportation, and nearby destinations to explore during your trip to Rome, Italy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rome

Like any other city, there are certain rules to follow and others to avoid while in Rome. Here are some of the things to do and don’t do in Rome:

Do:

  • Dress appropriately for cultural and religious sites
  • Be aware of the pickpockets in crowded areas
  • Learn a few Italian phrases

Don’t:

  • Do not bring your own bottled water into restaurants
  • Do not sit on public monuments
  • Do not underestimate the importance of the siesta time
