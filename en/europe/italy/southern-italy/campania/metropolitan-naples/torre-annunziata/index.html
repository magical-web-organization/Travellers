Introduction to Torre Annunziata

Torre Annunziata is a small town located in the Metropolitan Naples area of Campania in Southern Italy. It is situated between the Bay of Naples and the Mount Vesuvius. The town is rich in history and culture, boasting numerous museums and historical sites. Visitors can also enjoy the natural wonders of the area through hiking and biking.

In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the town of Torre Annunziata and provide you with essential information to make your trip to Southern Italy unforgettable.
