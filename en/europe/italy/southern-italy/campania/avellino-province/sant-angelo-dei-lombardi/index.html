Introduction to Sant’Angelo Dei Lombardi

Sant’Angelo Dei Lombardi is a beautiful hill-town located in Avellino, Campania in Southern Italy. It is a town rich in culture, history, natural beauty, and delicious food. Sant’Angelo is surrounded by the breathtaking Apennine Mountains and is a perfect destination for those who enjoy outdoor activities, hiking, and biking. The town is known for its carefully preserved medieval center, narrow alleys, and stunning architecture.

Sant’Angelo Dei Lombardi has a rich history which is reflected in its architecture and landmarks. The town has several museums and historical places to explore, including:

  • Museo Archeologico Nazionale

Local Culture and Customs of Sant’Angelo Dei Lombardi

Sant’Angelo Dei Lombardi is a small town with a rich cultural and artistic heritage. The locals are proud of their traditions and customs. When visiting the area, here are a few cultural and social practices to respect:

  • It is polite to greet locals with a “Buongiorno” (Good Morning) or “Buonasera” (Good Evening).
