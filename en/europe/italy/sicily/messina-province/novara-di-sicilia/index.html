Introduction to Novara di Sicilia

Novara di Sicilia is a charming town in the Messina province of Sicily, Italy. This historic town is located on a hilltop overlooking the Alcantara valley and boasts stunning views of the surrounding countryside. With its rich history, picturesque scenery, and warm hospitality, Novara di Sicilia is a must-visit destination for those looking to explore the authentic side of Italy.

List of Places to Visit in Novara di Sicilia

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Novara di Sicilia has a wealth of cultural attractions that are worth checking out. Some of the top museums and historical places include:

  • Museo dell’Alto Ionio: This museum showcases the history and culture of the Ionian coast, featuring exhibits on traditional crafts, farming, and fishing practices.
  • Chiesa Madre Santa Maria Assunta: This 17th-century church is the centerpiece of Novara di Sicilia’s historic district and features stunning baroque architecture and artwork.
  • Palazzo Nicolaci di Villadorata: This 18th-century palace is one of the most impressive buildings in Novara di Sicilia, with ornate façades, frescoed ceilings, and lavish interior decor.

Natural Attractions of Novara di Sicilia: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The natural beauty of Novara di Sicilia is simply breathtaking, offering plenty of opportunities to enjoy the great outdoors. Here are some of the top natural attractions:

  • Gole dell’Alcantara: This breathtaking natural park is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Sicily, thanks to its stunning rock formations, crystal-clear water, and lush vegetation.
  • Mount Etna: This iconic active volcano dominates the skyline of Novara di Sicilia, offering a unique opportunity to explore a natural wonder up close.
  • Lago di Gurrida: This beautiful lake is a hidden gem in the surrounding countryside, offering stunning views and a peaceful setting for swimming, fishing, and hiking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Novara di Sicilia

Novara di Sicilia is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, with plenty of hiking and biking trails that offer stunning views and unforgettable experiences. Some of the top routes include:

  • Monte Scuderi: This challenging hiking trail takes you up to the summit of one of the highest peaks in the area, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Via dei Noci: This scenic hiking trail takes you through the countryside and olive groves, offering breathtaking views of the Alcantara valley.
  • Nebrodi E-Bike Tour: This guided bike tour takes you through the stunning Nebrodi mountains, offering a unique perspective on the natural beauty of the area.

Best Things to Do in Novara di Sicilia: Activities and Tours

Novara di Sicilia offers a wide range of activities and tours that cater to all interests and budgets. Some of the top things to do in Novara di Sicilia include:

  • Wine Tasting Tour: Explore the wineries of the Alcantara valley and sample some of the best wines in Sicily.
  • Cooking Class: Learn how to prepare traditional Sicilian dishes with a local chef and savor the flavors of the island.
  • Alcantara River Rafting: Experience the thrill of rafting down the Alcantara river, surrounded by stunning natural scenery.

Week-Long Itinerary for Novara di Sicilia

If you have a week to spare, here’s a sample itinerary that will allow you to experience the best of Novara di Sicilia:

  • Day 1: Explore Novara di Sicilia’s historic center, visiting the Chiesa Madre Santa Maria Assunta and the Palazzo Nicolaci di Villadorata.
  • Day 2: Visit the Gole dell’Alcantara and go hiking in the surrounding park.
  • Day 3: Take a day trip to Taormina, one of the most beautiful towns in Sicily.
  • Day 4: Explore the Alcantara valley through a wine tasting tour and cooking class.
  • Day 5: Hike up Mount Etna and admire the stunning views from the summit.
  • Day 6: Go river rafting on the Alcantara river or take an e-bike tour through the Nebrodi mountains.
  • Day 7: Relax by Lago di Gurrida and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.

Food and Drink Scene of Novara di Sicilia

Must-Try Dishes

Sicilian cuisine is famous for its bold flavors and creative combinations, and Novara di Sicilia is no exception. Here are some of the top dishes to try:

  • Arancini: These deep-fried rice balls are filled with a variety of savory ingredients, such as meat, tomato sauce, and cheese.
  • Pasta alla Norma: This classic Sicilian dish features pasta, tomato sauce, fried eggplant, and grated ricotta cheese.
  • Cannoli: These sweet pastry shells are filled with ricotta cheese, chocolate chips, and candied fruit.

Local Beverages

Sicily is also known for its delicious wines and refreshing beverages. Here are some of the most popular drinks in the area:

  • Nero d’Avola: This bold red wine is one of the most famous in Sicily, known for its rich flavors and aromas.
  • Limoncello: This sweet lemon liqueur is the perfect way to end a meal in Sicily.
  • Granita: This refreshing frozen dessert is made with fruit juice, sugar, and ice, and is a great way to cool down on a hot day.

Novara di Sicilia offers a wide range of accommodation options, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are some of the top places to stay:

  • Ostello Artemis: This cozy hostel is perfect for budget travelers and backpackers, with a friendly atmosphere and a great location in the historic center.

What to Buy in Novara di Sicilia: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Novara di Sicilia is famous for its traditional crafts and local products. Here are some of the top souvenirs and gifts to bring back home:

Local Culture and Customs of Novara di Sicilia

Sicilian culture is deeply rooted in tradition and history, and Novara di Sicilia is no exception. Here are some of the key elements of the local culture:

  • Festivals and Celebrations: Novara di Sicilia hosts several festivals throughout the year, such as the Festa di San Francesco in October and the Festa dei Produttori in August.
  • Religious Traditions: Catholicism is the most widely practiced religion in Novara di Sicilia, and there are several churches and religious sites throughout the area.
  • Family and Community: Family and community are highly valued in Sicilian culture, and hospitality and generosity are an important part of everyday life.

Best Time to Visit Novara di Sicilia

The best time to visit Novara di Sicilia is from April to June and from September to November, when the weather is mild and pleasant and the crowds are smaller. July and August can be very hot and crowded, and many locals go on vacation during this time.
