Introduction to Fejér County

Fejér County, located in Central Transdanubia, Hungary, is a perfect blend of history, art, and natural beauty. The county is home to several charming towns and villages, each with its unique offerings. From ancient castles and museums to breathtaking nature reserves, Fejér County has it all. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will be taking you through the top attractions, best things to do, where to stay, and more in Fejér County.

List of Places to Visit in Fejér County

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Fejér County has a rich history and a wealth of cultural attractions. Some highlights include:

  • Bory Castle: Built by sculptor and architect Jenő Bory, the castle is truly a unique masterpiece and is regarded as one of Hungary’s national treasures.
  • The Archbishop’s Palace: Located in Esztergom, the palace offers a glimpse into the religious and political history of the county.
  • The Benedictine Abbey of Pannonhalma: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, this Benedictine abbey is Hungary’s oldest and most significant.
  • Esztergom Castle: Once the the royal castle of Hungary, it is now a museum that showcases medieval weaponry, art, and the Castle Chapel.

Natural Attractions of Fejér County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For nature lovers, Fejér County is a paradise with an abundance of natural attractions. Some must-visit places include:

  • Gerecse Hills: A beautiful range of hills with scenic views and valleys.
  • Fejér’s “Sea”: This lake of 84 hectares is a perfect spot for swimming and water sports.
  • Móricz Zsigmond Cave: The spectacular rock formations and chambers in the cave make it a popular destination for caving enthusiasts.
  • Csókakő Castle Hill Nature Reserve: This picturesque reserve features rare flora and fauna, and a stunning view of the Danube Valley.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fejér County

Fejér County is renowned for its beautiful countryside, and exploring on foot or by bike is a great way to experience it. Some of the best routes include:

  • Székesfehérvár PannonPath
  • The Water Circle in Sukoró
  • Rönk Road in Gánt
  • Velence Bike Ring

Best Things to Do in Fejér County: Activities and Tours

Fejér County has numerous activities and tours for tourists to explore. Some of these include:

  • Explore the “Sea”: Take a boat trip, swim or indulge in water sports at Fejér’s “Sea”.
  • Esztergom’s City Walk: Discover Esztergom’s rich history and stunning architecture by taking a city walk.
  • Wine tasting: Visit one of the wineries in the region and discover the taste of locally produced wines.
  • Hot Air Balloon ride: Get a bird’s eye view of the county by taking a hot air balloon ride.

Week-Long Itinerary for Fejér County

Day 1 - Visit Esztergom and view the grand Basilica and the Castle Hill, followed by a walking tour of the city. Day 2 - Discover Bory Castle and the CBA Terrarium. End the day with a relaxing evening in Velence Spa. Day 3 - Take a leisurely bike ride through the countryside following the Velence Bike Ring. Day 4 - Explore the “Sea” and take part in aquatic activities. Day 5 - Take a day trip to Pannonhalma, with its stunning Benedictine Abbey, Archaeological Park, and Arboretum. Day 6 - Visit the city of Székesfehérvár and the Bory Castle, take part in wine tasting or go shopping. Day 7 - Enjoy hiking trails and walks in the Móricz Zsigmond Cave, Csókakő Castle Hill Nature Reserve, and Gerecse Hills.

Food and Drink Scene of Fejér County

Must-Try Dishes

Hungarian cuisine is delicious, and Fejér County has its unique offerings. Don’t miss out on trying these local dishes:

  • Fisherman’s Soup: Made with freshwater fish and paprika, this soup is a perfect appetizer.
  • Lecsó: A tomato-based stew made with peppers and onions.
  • Dobos Torte: A famous Hungarian dessert made with several layers of sponge cake and chocolate cream.

Local Beverages

Fejér County has a strong tradition of producing locally made beverages. Don’t miss the chance to try these:

  • Wines: Known for producing some of the best wines in Hungary, Fejér County is the perfect spot to enjoy wine tasting.
  • Palinka: A Hungarian fruit brandy, made from various fruits like cherries, plums, and apricots.

Where to Stay in Fejér County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Fejér County has various accommodation options that cater to every traveler. Some popular options include:

  • Hotel Szárcsa* in Székesfehérvár, an affordable option that offers excellent services.
  • Chateau Visz** in Visz, an exclusive option for those who seek luxury.
  • Vinotel* in Pákozd, a charming boutique hotel with vineyard scenery.

What to Buy in Fejér County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Shopping for souvenirs is always fun, and Fejér County has an excellent variety of local products that make for perfect gifts, such as:

  • Porcelain: Zsolnay ceramics of Pécs and Kecskemét pottery are Hungarian national treasures, which you can also find in Fejér County.
  • Leather goods: Whether it’s a belt, a wallet, or a pair of boots, Fejér County is known for producing high-quality leather goods.

Local Culture and Customs of Fejér County

Hungarian culture is fascinating, and Fejér County has its unique customs and traditions. The county celebrates several festivals such as:

  • The Wine and Jazz Festival: Celebrated in Székesfehérvár, this festival features wine tasting, jazz performances, and art exhibitions.
  • Jézus Szíve Fesztivál: Celebrated in Esztergom, the festival features traditional Hungarian cuisine, folk dance performances, and a funfair.

Best Time to Visit Fejér County

The best time to visit Fejér County is during the spring and summer months (April to August) when the weather is pleasant, and outdoor activities can be enjoyed. The autumn season (September to November) is also a perfect time to visit when you can witness the vibrant autumn foliage.

Safety and Health

Safety in Fejér County

Overall, Fejér County is a safe destination to visit for tourists. However, like any other place, common sense should be applied. Stay alert in crowded areas and keep an eye on your belongings.

Health in Fejér County

Fejér County has excellent healthcare facilities. In case of any emergency, call the General Emergency Service at 112.
