Introduction to Sátoraljaújhely

Sátoraljaújhely is a town in Borsod-Abaúj-Zemplén County, in Northern Hungary. This charming town located in the foothills of the Zemplén Mountains is known for its beautiful architecture and rich history. From its beautiful Baroque-style buildings to its numerous cultural and natural attractions, Sátoraljaújhely has something to offer for every type of traveler. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll explore the best things to see, do, and experience in Sátoraljaújhely, Hungary.
