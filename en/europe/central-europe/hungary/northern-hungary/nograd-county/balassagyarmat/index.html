Nestled in Northern Hungary, Balassagyarmat is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. With a history spanning back to the medieval times, this small town is bursting with cultural and natural attractions. From museums to hiking trails, there is something for everyone in Balassagyarmat.

Safety and Health

Safety in Balassagyarmat

Balassagyarmat is generally a safe town, but it’s always best to be cautious and keep your belongings close. Make sure to lock your doors and windows at night for extra safety.
