Introduction to Békés County

Békés County is a hidden gem located in the Southern Great Plain region of Hungary. It is a perfect destination for those who want to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and immerse themselves in rural life. Known for its colorful landscapes, rich culture, and friendly people, Békés County is a destination that never fails to impress.

List of Places to Visit in Békés County

Békéscsaba, Gyula, and Szarvas are three of the most popular tourist destinations in Békés County. However, the county has so much more to offer than just these three cities.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Békés County Museum: This museum is the perfect place to learn about the history and culture of Békés County. It houses a collection of artifacts dating back to the Roman era.
  • Gyula Castle: Built in the 14th century, this castle is a beautiful example of Gothic architecture. Visitors can explore the castle on their own or take a guided tour.
  • Munkácsy Mihály Museum: This museum is dedicated to the life and works of Munkácsy Mihály, one of Hungary’s most famous painters.

Natural Attractions of Békés County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Körös-Maros National Park: This park is home to some of Hungary’s most beautiful natural scenery. Visitors can enjoy hiking, birdwatching, and even kayaking.
  • Ópusztaszer National Memorial Site: This outdoor museum commemorates the founding of Hungary. Visitors can take a stroll through history and learn about the country’s past.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Békés County

Békés County has many hiking and biking trails that offer stunning views of the countryside. The following are some of the most popular routes:

  • Körös-Maros Bike Trail: This 95km trail is perfect for those who love biking and want to explore the beauty of the Körös-Maros National Park.
  • Körösvölgyi Hiking Trail: This 45km trail takes visitors through some of Békés County’s most beautiful landscapes and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

Best Things to Do in Békés County: Activities and Tours

  • Thermal Baths: Békés County is known for its thermal baths, which are said to have healing properties. The Gyula Castle Bath is one of the most popular destinations.
  • Wine Tasting: Békés County is home to several wineries that offer tours and tastings. Visitors can sample some of Hungary’s best wines and learn about the winemaking process.

Week-Long Itinerary for Békés County

Day 1: Explore Békéscsaba, including the Békés County Museum and the Szanta House. Day 2: Visit the Körös-Maros National Park and go hiking or kayaking. Day 3-4: Relax at the Gyula Castle Bath and explore Gyula Castle. Day 5: Visit the Munkácsy Mihály Museum and learn about the famous Hungarian painter. Day 6: Take a cycling tour of the countryside and visit a local winery. Day 7: Visit the Ópusztaszer National Memorial Site and learn about Hungary’s history.

Food and Drink Scene of Békés County

Must-Try Dishes

  • Székelykáposzta: A traditional Hungarian dish made with sauerkraut and pork.
  • Gulyás: A hearty beef stew that is perfect for cold days.
  • Pörkölt: A Hungarian version of goulash made with paprika and beef.

Local Beverages

  • Wines: Békés County is home to several wineries that produce award-winning wines.
  • Pálinka: A Hungarian fruit brandy that is often used as a digestive.

Where to Stay in Békés County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Boutique Hotel Régi Posta: Located in the heart of Békéscsaba, this hotel offers cozy rooms and high-quality service.
  • Castle Hotel Gyula: This hotel is located inside Gyula Castle and offers a unique and luxurious experience.
  • Szarvasi Szálloda és Wellness: Located in Szarvas, this hotel is perfect for those looking for a relaxing and rejuvenating getaway.

What to Buy in Békés County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Paprika: Békés County is known for its high-quality paprika, which is used in many Hungarian dishes.
  • Pottery: Several villages in Békés County produce beautiful ceramics that make great souvenirs.
  • Embroidery: The delicate embroidery techniques used in Békés County are unique and can be found in many shops throughout the county.

Local Culture and Customs of Békés County

Békés County is known for its friendly and welcoming people. It is a place where traditions and customs are still alive and well. Visitors should be respectful of local customs, including dress codes in places of worship, and be mindful of their behavior in public places.

Best Time to Visit Békés County

The best time to visit Békés County is during the spring and summer months when the weather is warm and sunny. However, visitors can also enjoy the beauty of the county during the fall when the leaves change colors.

Do’s and Don’ts of Békés County

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Explore the natural beauty of the county.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect places of worship.
  • Litter in public places.
  • Ignore safety precautions while enjoying outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Békés County

Békés County is a safe and welcoming destination. However, visitors should take the usual precautions, such as not leaving valuables unattended and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Békés County

There are several hospitals and clinics throughout Békés County. Visitors should ensure that they have comprehensive travel insurance before visiting and be aware of any potential health risks associated with outdoor activities.

Getting to Békés County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Békés County

  • Debrecen International Airport: The closest airport to Békés County, located about 100km away.
  • Budapest Ferenc Liszt International Airport: Located about 230km away from Békés County.

Bus Stations Near Békés County

  • Békéscsaba Bus Station
  • Gyula Bus Station
  • Szarvas Bus Station

Other Options

  • Train: There are several train stations throughout Békés County, making it easy to travel to different destinations within the county.
  • Car rental: Visitors can rent a car at one of the airports or train stations to explore the county at their leisure.

How to Get Around in Békés County

Békés County has several affordable transportation options, including buses and trains. Visitors can also rent a car or bike to explore the countryside on their own.

Where to Go Next from Békés County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Debrecen: This city is known for its rich history and beautiful architecture.
  • Hortobágy National Park: This park is a UNESCO World Heritage site and is known for its unique landscapes and wildlife.
  • Szeged: Located in the Southern Great Plain, Szeged is home to several beautiful buildings and a thriving cultural scene.

Conclusion

Békés County is a destination that offers something for everyone. Whether you want to explore the history and culture of the county, enjoy outdoor activities, or simply relax and unwind, Békés County is the perfect destination. With its friendly people, beautiful landscapes, and delicious food and drink, you won’t want to leave this hidden gem in Hungary.
