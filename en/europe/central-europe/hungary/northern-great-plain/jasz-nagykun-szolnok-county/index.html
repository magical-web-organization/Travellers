Introduction to Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County

Located in Northern Great Plain, Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County is a picturesque region in Hungary that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural landmarks. The county is home to several important attractions, including museums, historical landmarks, outdoor adventures, and scenic views. Whether you’re an avid traveler or just looking for a relaxing getaway, Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County boasts of several museums and historical landmarks that offer a glimpse into Hungary’s rich culture and historic legacy. The following are a few must-visit attractions:

  • Jászberény Museum: located in Jászberény town, the museum showcases the history and culture of Jászság region through its exhibits and artifacts.
  • Tiszafüred Museum: dedicated to the history and culture of Tiszafüred town, the museum offers a fascinating insight into the town’s past.
  • Ferenczy Museum: located in Szentendre, the museum houses a vast collection of artworks by Hungarian artists of the 19th and 20th centuries.
  • Szolnok Gallery: located in the city center of Szolnok, the gallery showcases contemporary Hungarian art.

Natural Attractions of Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County is home to several natural attractions, making it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The following are a few must-visit natural attractions:

  • Hortobágy National Park: covering an area of 800 square kilometers, the park is the largest continuous natural grassland in Europe and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Lake Tisza: located near the Tisza River, the lake is a popular spot for boating, fishing, and bird-watching.
  • Kiskunság National Park: spread over 570 square kilometers, the park is home to several species of flora and fauna.
  • Bükk Hills: located near the town of Eger, the hills offer magnificent hiking trails and scenic views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County

Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County offers several hiking and biking routes that take visitors through the region’s picturesque landscapes. Some of the popular routes include the following:

  • Great Plain Blue Route: The 170 km hiking trail passes through several regions, including Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County.
  • Tisza Lake cycling route: The 83 km cycling route takes visitors around the picturesque Tisza Lake.
  • Great Plains Bicycle Route: The 600 km cycling route passes through several regions, including Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County.

Best Things to Do in Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County: Activities and Tours

Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County offers an array of activities and tours for visitors to explore the region. Here are some suggested activities:

  • Horseback riding in Hortobágy National Park: Take a guided horseback tour across the vast grasslands of the national park.
  • Water activities at Lake Tisza: Rent a boat or kayak to explore the lake’s pristine waters or go fishing for carp and pike.
  • Wine tasting in Eger: Visit the town of Eger and discover its wine cellars and vineyards.
  • Thermal spa visit: Hungary is famous for its thermal baths and Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County has several on offer including Kecskemét Thermal Bath and Aqua-Palace.
  • Visit to the Hortobágy Csárda: Enjoy Hungarian cuisine and experience traditional Hungarian culture at the Hortobágy Csárda.

Week-Long Itinerary for Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County

Here is a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County:

  • Day 1: Visit Jászberény Museum and experience traditional Hungarian culture.
  • Day 2: Spend the day at Hortobágy National Park and enjoy horseback riding across the vast grasslands.
  • Day 3: Explore the picturesque Tisza Lake and indulge in water activities.
  • Day 4: Visit Kiskunság National Park and hike through its several trails.
  • Day 5: Take a guided tour of the Ferenczy Museum and enjoy Hungarian art and culture.

  • Day 7: Explore the scenic Bükk Hills and hike through its trails.

Food and Drink Scene of Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County

Hungarian cuisine is a must-try when visiting Jász-Nagykun-Szolnok County. Here are a few must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Goulash: A Hungarian staple stew with beef, vegetables, and paprika.
  • Lángos: A deep-fried dough topped with garlic, sour cream, and cheese.
  • Chimney cake: A crispy pastry roll covered in cinnamon, sugar and walnut mix.
  • Szilvás Gombóc: Plum-filled dumplings coated with parsley.

Local Beverages

  • Tokaji wine: a sweet dessert wine produced in northeastern Hungary, it is one of the world’s oldest wine regions, with history dating back to the 16th century.
  • Pálinka: a spirit traditionally made from fruit that is commonly distilled across the region.
