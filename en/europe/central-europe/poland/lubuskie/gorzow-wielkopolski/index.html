Introduction to Gorzów Wielkopolski

Gorzów Wielkopolski is a city located in Lubuskie Voivodeship, in the western part of Poland. The city is known for its beautiful parks, museums, and historical sites. Gorzów Wielkopolski is also surrounded by natural beauty, with the area being home to stunning lakes and forests. The city has a population of around 120,000 people and is one of the most important cultural, educational, and economic centers of the region.

Local Culture and Customs of Gorzów Wielkopolski
