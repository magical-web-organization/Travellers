Introduction to Busko-Zdrój

Busko-Zdrój is a charming town located in Świętokrzyskie, in Poland, in Central Europe, in Europe. With a population of just over 10,000 people, Busko-Zdrój is known for its soothing hot springs and beautiful natural surroundings. Travelers who visit Busko-Zdrój are treated to breathtaking vistas, a rich cultural heritage, and plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.
