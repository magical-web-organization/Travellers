Introduction to Nowy Targ

Nowy Targ is a small town located in the Małopolskie region of Poland, situated in Central Europe, making it easily accessible through various means of transportation. It’s a place of rich history and culture with stunning natural landscapes. This charming town has something to offer each visitor, from its culinary delights to outdoor adventures. This travel guide will help you uncover the treasures of Nowy Targ.

List of Places to Visit in Nowy Targ

There are several culturally and historically significant attractions to visit in Nowy Targ, as well as thrilling outdoor adventures for those who love to take in scenic views while hiking or biking. Here is a list of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Nowy Targ Museum: showcasing cultural artifacts, paintings, and photographs that portray the history of Nowy Targ, from pre-war to present-day.
  • Gorce National Park: an ideal destination for nature lovers, hikers, and those who want to experience mountain tourism while learning about the life and customs of the local highlanders.
  • Ski Resort Kotelnica: a popular skiing spot located in Bukowina Tatrzańska, where skiers from all over Poland come to hit the slopes.

Natural Attractions of Nowy Targ: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Pieniny National Park: is a breathtaking place to explore, featuring diversified landscapes, enormous limestone mountains, and an array of plants and animals.
  • Dunajec River Gorge: take a rafting tour on the crystal-clear waters of this beautiful gorge that offers stunning views and unique flora and fauna.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Nowy Targ

Nowy Targ’s natural beauty and mountainous terrain offer several exciting hiking and biking trails, catering to varying skill levels. Here are the best hiking and biking routes to explore:

  • The Tatra Mountains: one of Europe’s most unique mountain ranges, offering scenic hiking and mountain biking trails suitable for all levels.
  • Bukowina- Białka Tatrzańska: where you can enjoy hiking trails that lead to beautiful mountain views.

Best Things to Do in Nowy Targ: Activities and Tours

There are several must-do activities and tours in Nowy Targ. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Nowy Targ Market: for a truly authentic Polish experience, visit the bustling Nowy Targ market, where you can buy local goods and taste delicious traditional cuisine.
  • Nowy Targ Wine and Fruit Market: offering locally produced wine and other liquors like Żubrówka and Wiśniówka.

Week-Long Itinerary for Nowy Targ

Here is a week-long itinerary that takes in all the top sights in Nowy Targ:

Day Activities
1 Visit the Nowy Targ Museum for an introduction to the town’s culture and history.
2 Explore Gorce National Park and take a hike through its beautiful trails.
3 Enjoy a day at the Ski Resort Kotelnica and hit the slopes.
4 Visit Pieniny National Park and experience the natural beauty of the Dunajec River Gorge.
5 Go hiking or mountain biking at Bukowina- Białka Tatrzańska.
6 Take a trip to the Nowy Targ Market and experience local Polish life.
7 Visit the Nowy Targ Wine and Fruit Market and try some local beverages.

Food and Drink Scene of Nowy Targ

Nowy Targ is a food lover’s paradise, with several traditional restaurants and local beverages to sample.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Oscypek: a type of cheese made from salted sheep’s milk. This dish is served in many local restaurants and is a huge delight for cheese enthusiasts.
  • Kotlet Schabowy: Polish style pork chop served with mashed potatoes and delicious pickles. This is a must-try dish for meat lovers.

Local Beverages

  • Tatra Tea: made with hot blackcurrant compote, vodka, and spices, this is a traditional beverage to ward off cold winters.
  • Żubrówka: a vodka made with bison grass giving it a unique flavor.

Where to Stay in Nowy Targ: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Nowy Targ offers several types of accommodation to suit every traveler’s budget and preference. Here are some options:

  • Hotel Sabala: a four-star hotel located in the town center, offering luxurious amenities and outstanding services.
  • Hostel Taternik: backpacker-friendly hostel, situated close to several tourist attractions and outdoor activities.
  • Guesthouse Krystyna: a beautiful guesthouse located in Gorce National Park, suitable for a laid-back vacation.

What to Buy in Nowy Targ: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Nowy Targ has a rich history and culture, and visitors can find several souvenirs and locally produced products to take home with them. Here are some to consider:

  • Hand-painted pottery: you can find beautiful, hand-painted pottery inspired by local designs in several shops in Nowy Targ.
  • Sheepskin slippers: locals are famous for their hand-made sheepskin slippers, which are extremely warm and comfortable during cold winters.
  • Polish chocolate: Nowy Targ is home to several fine chocolatiers offering a wide range of chocolates and other sweets.

Local Culture and Customs of Nowy Targ

Nowy Targ boasts a rich and unique local culture that draws visitors from all over the world. Some of the customs and cultures to explore include:

  • Folk dances and music: Nowy Targ is well-known for its traditional folk dances and music performed during special events and festivals.
  • Highland architecture: the distinctive wooden architecture found in Nowy Targ reflects the local highland lifestyle and is a must-see part of the town’s cultural heritage.

Best Time to Visit Nowy Targ

Nowy Targ is a year-round destination, with each season offering something unique. However, the best time to visit would be from May to September when the weather is the warmest, and outdoor activities are most enjoyable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Nowy Targ

Here are some essential do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Nowy Targ:

Do:

  • Respect local customs: Nowy Targ is home to a unique cultural heritage, and it’s important to respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try local cuisine: Nowy Targ is famous for its traditional dishes, and it’s essential to try some during your visit.

Don’t:

  • Overfish in the rivers: ensure that you don’t damage the local ecosystem by overfishing in the rivers.
  • Disturb the local wildlife: Nowy Targ is home to several unique species of flora and fauna, and it’s essential to respect their habitats and not disturb them.

Safety and Health

Here is some important safety and health information to keep in mind during your visit.

Safety in Nowy Targ

  • Avoid drinking tap water – ensure that you drink bottled water during your stay.
  • Be prepared for sudden weather changes – Nowy Targ has unpredictable weather, especially in the mountains, so ensure you are well-prepared for sudden changes.

Health in Nowy Targ

  • COVID-19: Nowy Targ is currently taking necessary measures to ensure visitors’ safety by enforcing social distancing and other protocols.

Getting to Nowy Targ: Transportation Options and Logistics

Nowy Targ can be reached conveniently through various means of transportation. Here’s what you need to know about getting to Nowy Targ:

Airports Near Nowy Targ

  • John Paul II International Airport: located in Krakow, 80 km from Nowy Targ, offers several international flights.

Bus Stations Near Nowy Targ

  • Nowy Targ Bus Station: there is a bus station in the town center that connects Nowy Targ to other cities in Poland.

Other Options

  • Train: there are several trains that go through Nowy Targ, connecting the town to other Polish cities.
  • Car: you can rent a car in Krakow or other major cities and drive to Nowy Targ.

How to Get Around in Nowy Targ

Nowy Targ is a small town, and several tourist attractions are within walking distance of the town center. However, if you want to explore the outskirts of the town, here are some transportation options:

  • Buses: there are buses that go to the nearby attractions and towns near Nowy Targ.
  • Taxis: taxis are available in the town center and can be hired for short trips.

Where to Go Next from Nowy Targ: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Nowy Targ is located in a region with several other beautiful destinations that are worth exploring. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions:

  • Zakopane: known for its beautiful Tatra Mountains, Zakopane is a popular destination for winter sports enthusiasts.
  • Krakow: Poland’s hotspot, rich in history and culture, with a vibrant nightlife and plenty of restaurants and shopping destinations.

Conclusion

Nowy Targ is a small town with a rich culture and history, featuring breathtaking natural scenery, exciting outdoor activities, and a thriving culinary scene. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can plan a perfect trip to explore Nowy Targ and discover all that it has to offer.
