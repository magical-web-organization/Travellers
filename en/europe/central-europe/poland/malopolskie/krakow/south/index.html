Introduction to South

South, located in the southern part of Kraków, is a region where you can enjoy the best of both worlds- the rich history and the natural beauty. From bike trails to skiing, you can find something to do year-round. The region is an ideal destination for couples, families, and solo travelers looking to explore something new, and soak in some of Poland’s vibrant culture. In this guide, we will take you on a tour of the best destinations and hidden gems you can visit in South.

Local Culture and Customs of South

South has a diverse mix of cultures due to its unique location. It has been home to Jews, Ukrainians, Tatars, and Lemkos, who have all left their mark on the region’s culture and customs. It is always good to educate yourself about local customs, such as greeting people, dining etiquette, and tipping customs.

Best Time to Visit South

The best time to visit South would be from May to September when the weather is pleasant, and the overall mood is relaxed. However, the region is worth visiting any time of the year as you can enjoy the winter wonderland in ski resorts in the colder months.

Do’s and Don’ts of South

Do:

  • Dress modestly when visiting historical landmarks or religious places.
  • Try the local cuisine; it has plenty of delicious options to try.
  • Carry cash; some smaller places might not accept cards.

Don’t:

  • Touch any historical relics or artifacts while visiting a museum or historical site.
  • Speak about politics, sports, or religion with strangers.

Safety and Health

Safety in South

Generally, South is safe for tourists. However, it is always good to take precautions like taking care of your belongings, avoiding empty streets, and being alert in public transport. In case of an emergency, the police helpline is reachable by dialing 112.

Health in South

Poland’s healthcare system is advanced. Travelers are advised to buy travel insurance and bring a local emergency contact number in case of any health emergency.

Getting to South: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near South

The closest airport to South is Kraków John Paul II International Airport, which is approximately 13 km away.

Bus Stations Near South

The main bus station in Krakow is the Kraków Główny Bus Station, which is the largest and most convenient for traveling to South.

Other Options

You can also hire a taxi or rent a car to travel to South. However, those are not exactly recommended options for budget travelers.

How to Get Around in South

The best way to get around South is by the local bus, which is fast, reliable, and affordable. You can purchase tickets online, at post offices, or from the ticket machines at the bus stops.

Where to Go Next from South: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Zakopane

Conclusion

South may be small, but it is also rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Plan your trip smartly, and you can explore all the hidden gems and must-see destinations. We hope our guide helped you in planning your perfect trip to South and discovering the charm of Krakow’s hidden gems.
