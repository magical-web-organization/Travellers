Introduction to Liptovský Hrádok

Liptovský Hrádok is a historic town in Central Slovakia that lies in the valley of the Váh river, surrounded by the Low and High Tatras mountain ranges. Its name derived from the ancient castle that stands majestically on a high rock above the town. The castle is a national cultural monument and one of the main tourist attractions in the area. Liptovský Hrádok is home to friendly locals, a rich history, and a variety of outdoor activities, making it an excellent choice for any traveler.

  • Jewish Cemetery
