Introduction to Ždiar

Located in Eastern Slovakia, Ždiar is a charming mountain village nestled in the picturesque Tatra Mountains. It’s known for its stunning natural scenery, numerous outdoor activities and traditions that have been preserved for hundreds of years. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an adventure-filled holiday, Ždiar has something for everyone.

Best Time to Visit Ždiar

The best time to visit Ždiar is during the summer months (June to August) for hiking, biking and other outdoor activities, and in the winter months (December to March) for skiing and snowboarding.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ždiar

Don’t:

Safety and Health

Safety in Ždiar

Crime rates are low in Ždiar, but visitors should take standard safety precautions, such as keeping an eye on their belongings and valuables.

Health in Ždiar

The standard of healthcare in Ždiar is good, and emergency services can be accessed by dialing 112 in case of an emergency. Visitors should bring any necessary prescription medication with them.

Getting to Ždiar: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ždiar

  • Poprad-Tatry Airport, Slovakia (35 km)

Bus Stations Near Ždiar

  • Ždiar and other villages are serviced by the regional bus network.

Other Options

How to Get Around in Ždiar

  • Walking or biking are great ways to explore the village, while a car is necessary for traveling outside of Ždiar.
