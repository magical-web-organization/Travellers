Introduction to Kelsterbach

Kelsterbach is a small town situated in the Rhine-Main, Hesse region of Germany. It has a rich history, with roots dating back to the Roman era. The town is located near the Frankfurt airport and is easily accessible from Munich, Cologne, and the rest of Europe. Kelsterbach is a perfect destination for travelers who want to explore Germany’s hidden gems.

List of Places to Visit in Kelsterbach

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kelsterbach has several historical landmarks and museums that will pique your interest in German culture and heritage. The following are some of the must-visit cultural attractions in Kelsterbach:

  • The St. Martinskirche: This Romanesque church was built in the 11th century and is one of the oldest structures in the town.
  • The Vesteburg: The ruins of the 13th-century castle are a testament to the town’s medieval past.
  • The Heimatmuseum: The local museum showcases the town’s rich history, with exhibits on Kelsterbach’s Roman and medieval pasts.

Natural Attractions of Kelsterbach: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kelsterbach’s scenic nature trails and gardens offer visitors a chance to explore the town’s natural offerings. Here are some of the best outdoor adventures and scenic views that you can experience in Kelsterbach:

  • The Kelsterbacher Terrassenweg: This 6 km long trail winds through scenic gardens, vineyards, and forests. The trail offers stunning views of the Rhine-Main region.
  • The Opel Zoo: Located near Kelsterbach, this family-friendly zoo is home to over 1500 animals and offers a fun-filled day trip.
  • The Kelsterbach River cruises: The tours run daily, offers a guided tour of the Rhine river, with breathtaking views of Germany’s stunning landscapes.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kelsterbach

Kelsterbach is well-known for its picturesque hiking and biking trails. The town features a network of scenic trails winding around its beautiful gardens, vineyards, and forests. Here are some not-to-miss hiking and biking routes:

  • The Kelsterbacher Terrassenweg: This 6 km trail is perfect for hiking and biking, with stunning views of the Rhine-Main valley.
  • The Mainuferweg: This hiking and biking route follows the Main river through the town of Kelsterbach to Frankfurt, offering breathtaking views of the city skyline.
  • The Nidda Radweg: This is a 90 km biking trail that runs through the Nidda river valley, offering picturesque views of the German countryside.

Best Things to Do in Kelsterbach: Activities and Tours

Kelsterbach offers a range of exciting activities and tours that will appease your inner adventurer. Here are some of the best things to do in Kelsterbach:

  • Visit the weekly farmer’s market: Every Saturday, a vibrant farmer’s market takes place in the town, with fresh produce, handmade crafts, and delicious local food.
  • Attend a wine-tasting session: Kelsterbach is known for its delicious wines, and several wineries around the town offer wine-tasting sessions.
  • Tour the Rhine-Main region: The local tour companies offer guided tours of the region’s attractions, including the stunning art city of Mainz, the historic city of Wiesbaden, and the cosmopolitan city of Frankfurt.

Food and Drink Scene of Kelsterbach

Kelsterbach is famous for its diverse food and drink scene. From traditional German cuisine to international flavors, the town offers something for every palate. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Schnitzel: Thin, crispy pork or chicken cutlets, served with potato salad, fries, or spaetzle.
  • Flammkuchen: A crispy pizza-like dish topped with cheese, onion, and bacon.
  • Spundekäs: A creamy cheese dip made with soft cheese like camembert or brie, served with bread.

Local Beverages

  • Riesling: A type of white wine native to the Rhine-Main region.
  • Apfelwein: A sharp-tasting, cider-like drink made from apples.
  • Kolsch: A light beer brewed in Cologne.

Local Culture and Customs of Kelsterbach

Kelsterbach is a charming town with rich cultural heritage. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and the town embodies the traditional German customs and values. It is important to be respectful of local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Kelsterbach

The best time to visit Kelsterbach is from mid-April to mid-October when the weather is pleasant, and the town’s gardens are in full bloom. The peak season for tourism is from June to August. During these peak seasons, it is advisable to book your accommodation in advance.

Getting to Kelsterbach: Transportation Options and Logistics

Kelsterbach is conveniently located near the Frankfurt airport and is easily accessible by public transportation. Here are some transportation options and logistics:

Airports Near Kelsterbach

  • Frankfurt Airport: The closest airport to the town is the Frankfurt airport, located 4 km away from the town center.

Bus Stations Near Kelsterbach

  • Kelsterbach Bahnhof: The town’s primary bus station offers frequent bus services to Frankfurt and other nearby towns.

Other Options

  • Train: The town’s train station offers regular train services to Frankfurt and other nearby towns.
  • Taxis: Taxis are readily available in the town and can be hired for transport to nearby areas.

How to Get Around in Kelsterbach

Kelsterbach is a small town, and most of its attractions are located within walking distance. The town’s public transportation system offers buses and trains that connect the town to nearby areas. Taxis are also readily available in the town.

Where to Go Next from Kelsterbach: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Kelsterbach is situated near several other exciting destinations that are worth a visit. Here are some nearby attractions:

  • Mainz: A historic city located just 30 km away from Kelsterbach, famous for its wine culture and historical landmarks.
  • Wiesbaden: A charming city located 40 km away from Kelsterbach, famous for its thermal baths, museums, and gardens.
