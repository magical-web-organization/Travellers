Introduction to Taunus

Located in Hesse, Germany, Taunus is a beautiful region that offers visitors a unique blend of nature, culture, and history. Taunus is home to scenic landscapes, charming towns, and historic landmarks that reflect the rich cultural heritage of the region. With so much to offer, Taunus is an ideal destination for those seeking an authentic German experience.

Safety and Health

Safety in Taunus

Taunus is generally a safe place to visit, with a low crime rate. However, it is advisable to take standard precautions when traveling, such as keeping your valuables safe and being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Taunus

Taunus has a good healthcare system, with hospitals and medical facilities located throughout the region. If you need emergency medical assistance, dial 112 or head to the nearest hospital.

Getting to Taunus: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Taunus

The nearest international airports to Taunus are Frankfurt Airport and Frankfurt-Hahn Airport.

Bus Stations Near Taunus

There are several bus stations in Taunus, including those in Bad Homburg, Kronberg, and Idstein.

Other Options

Other transportation options to Taunus include taxis, car rentals, and trains.

How to Get Around in Taunus

The best way to get around Taunus is by car, bike or walking. Buses and trains are also available but might not be as frequent.
