Introduction to South Holstein

Located in the northernmost part of Germany, South Holstein is a quaint and charming region known for its stunning nature, rich history, and traditional culture. The region covers an area of approximately 3,700 km² and is home to around 700,000 people. South Holstein borders the North Sea to the west and is located within the state of Schleswig-Holstein. In this guide, we will provide you with all the information you need to plan the perfect trip to South Holstein.

List of Places to Visit in South Holstein

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Eutin Castle: Located in the town of Eutin, Eutin Castle is a magnificent palace that dates back to the 18th century. The castle is famous for its beautiful gardens, musical events, and the Eutin Opera Summer Festival.
  • Mölln Old Town: Wander through the picturesque streets of the historic old town of Mölln. Admire the medieval half-timbered houses and visit the Mölln Museum to learn more about the town’s history.
  • Gut Wellenstein: Discover the history of the Wellenstein Estate in the town of Lensahn. This Baroque-style mansion dates back to 1760 and now serves as a museum, showcasing the history of the estate and the people who lived there.

Natural Attractions of South Holstein: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Ratzeburg: Take a boat ride on the crystal-clear waters of Lake Ratzeburg. Surrounded by picturesque hills and forests, Lake Ratzeburg is a popular spot for fishing, swimming, and hiking.
  • Höltigbaum Nature Reserve: This nature reserve is a haven for bird watchers and animal lovers. It provides a range of walking trails and picnicking areas for those looking for a peaceful getaway.
  • Lauenburg Nature Park: Located along the river Elbe, this natural park offers scenic views, hiking trails, and opportunities to spot local wildlife such as deer and wild boar.

Hiking/Biking Routes of South Holstein

Whether you’re a seasoned hiker or a casual walker, South Holstein has something for everyone. Some of the most popular hiking and biking trails in the region include:

  • Hohwachter Bay Trail: This 10 km trail along the Hohwachter Bay is perfect for hikers and bikers. Enjoy panoramic views of the sea and explore the surrounding nature.
  • Elbe-Lübeck Canal Trail: This charming trail follows the historic canal which connects the Elbe and Lübeck rivers. It’s a popular spot for cycling and walking.
  • Holstein Switzerland Trail: This trail in the heart of the Holstein Switzerland region offers a range of walking trails for all levels and offers stunning views of the surrounding hills and countryside.

Best Things to Do in South Holstein: Activities and Tours

There’s no shortage of activities to do in South Holstein, from exploring the region’s cultural heritage to enjoying its natural beauty. Here are some of the best things to do in South Holstein:

  • Booking a guided walk: Join a guided tour of one of South Holstein’s charming towns and learn about the region’s history and culture from an expert guide.
  • Visiting a local farm: Visit one of South Holstein’s many farms and learn about local agriculture. You can even pick your own fruits and vegetables!
  • Exploring the beaches: South Holstein boasts a range of stunning beaches along the North Sea, where you can relax, swim, and even do some surfing.

Food and Drink Scene of South Holstein

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pannfisch: A local specialty of fish served with potatoes and a cream sauce.
  • Labskaus: A traditional sailor’s dish made of corned beef, mashed potatoes, and beetroot.
  • Marzipan: Lübeck is famous for its marzipan, made from almonds and sugar.

Local Beverages

  • Beer: Try one of the many locally brewed beers, such as Holsten Pilsener.
  • Spirits: Traditional schnapps and Korn made from local ingredients.

Where to Stay in South Holstein: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

South Holstein offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. From luxury hotels to small guesthouses, you’ll find plenty of options for your stay. Some popular options include:

  • Hotel Waldhof auf Herrenland: A charming hotel set in the countryside near the town of Lensahn.
  • Mein Strandhaus - Friedrichskoog: A cozy guesthouse located just a stone’s throw from the beach.
  • Baltic Hotel Lübeck: A stylish hotel located in the heart of Lübeck’s old town.

What to Buy in South Holstein: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs and local products to bring home, South Holstein has plenty of options. Here are some ideas:

  • Marzipan: A Lübeck specialty, marzipan is a perfect gift for friends and family.
  • Jams and Chutneys: Local farms produce a range of delicious jams and chutneys made from local ingredients.
  • Amber Jewelry: South Holstein is known for its amber, and you’ll find plenty of beautiful amber jewelry to choose from.

Local Culture and Customs of South Holstein

South Holstein has a rich cultural heritage, with traditional crafts, music, and food still an important part of local life. The region is known for its friendly and welcoming residents, and you’ll often find community events and celebrations taking place.

Best Time to Visit South Holstein

The best time to visit South Holstein is during the summer months, from June to September. This is when the weather is at its best and you can enjoy the region’s beaches, outdoor activities, and local events.

Do’s and Don’ts of South Holstein

Do:

  • Try the local food and drink.
  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Take care of the environment and leave no trace when exploring nature.

Don’t:

  • Underestimate the weather, especially if you’re planning outdoor activities.
  • Touch or pick any plants or animals in nature reserves.
  • Ignore safety warnings at beaches and other tourist attractions.

Safety and Health

Safety in South Holstein

South Holstein is generally a safe place to visit, with low crime rates and a welcoming community. However, as with any travel destination, it’s important to be aware of your surroundings and take any necessary precautions when exploring the region.

Health in South Holstein

South Holstein has a good healthcare system, and visitors can access medical care at local hospitals and clinics if needed. It’s important to have adequate travel insurance that covers any medical expenses in case of illness or injury.

Getting to South Holstein: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near South Holstein

The nearest international airport to South Holstein is Hamburg Airport. There are also several regional airports nearby, including Lübeck Airport and Kiel Airport.

Bus Stations Near South Holstein

South Holstein is well connected by bus, with several bus stops in different towns across the region. The main bus station is situated in Lübeck.

Other Options

There are also several train stations across South Holstein, including Lübeck Central Station and Neumünster Station.

How to Get Around in South Holstein

The best way to get around South Holstein is by car, as this gives you the flexibility to explore the region at your own pace. However, there are also good public transportation links, including buses and trains, that connect the towns and villages across the region.

Where to Go Next from South Holstein: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

South Holstein is ideally located to explore the wider region of Schleswig-Holstein. Some nearby destinations and excursions include:

  • Kiel: The state capital is just a short drive away and offers a range of cultural attractions and events.
  • Sylt: This popular island resort is known for its long sandy beaches, dunes, and luxury spas.
  • Hamburg: This vibrant city is only an hour’s drive from South Holstein and offers a range of cultural and entertainment options.

Conclusion

South Holstein is a hidden gem in northern Germany, offering visitors a chance to explore stunning nature, rich history, and traditional culture. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or just a relaxing getaway, South Holstein has something for everyone. With this comprehensive guide, you’ll be able to plan the perfect trip to this beautiful region.
