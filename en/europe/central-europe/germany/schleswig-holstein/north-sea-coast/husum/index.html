Introduction to Husum

Located in the scenic North Sea Coast of Schleswig-Holstein, Germany, Husum is a charming coastal town that boasts a rich history, beautiful scenery, and exciting adventures. The town is known for its picturesque architecture, cultural landmarks, and outdoor activities. Whether you are looking for a relaxing getaway, a cultural adventure, or an exhilarating outdoor excursion, Husum has something special to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Husum

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Theodor Storm House

The Theodor Storm House is a must-visit attraction for literature enthusiasts who are fans of the German writer and poet Theodor Storm. The museum showcases his life and work and has a beautiful garden that was restored according to his writings.

Nordfriesisches Museum

The Nordfriesisches Museum is a great place to experience the local culture and history. It features an impressive collection of art, artifacts, and exhibits that tell the story of the region from prehistoric times to modern-day.

Natural Attractions of Husum: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Wadden Sea National Park

The Wadden Sea National Park is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that offers breathtaking natural beauty and diverse wildlife. The park is made up of mudflats, dunes, and marshes, and is an important habitat for seals, birds, and other animals.

Husum Harbour

Husum Harbour is a bustling waterfront area that is perfect for a relaxing stroll or romantic picnic. The picturesque fishing boats and yachts add to the charm of this lovely spot, and there are plenty of restaurants and cafes to enjoy a meal.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Husum

Husum has plenty of options for hikers and bikers who want to explore the outdoors. The region is home to several well-known trails, including the Nord-Ostsee-Kanal, which is a popular biking route along the coastline. Another great option is the North Friesian Islands Trail, which passes through several islands and offers stunning views of the sea.

Best Things to Do in Husum: Activities and Tours

Boat Tours

Boat tours are a popular activity in Husum, and there are plenty of options to choose from. Whether you want a quick jaunt or a longer excursion, you can find a tour that will fit your needs. Some boat tours even offer the chance to spot seals and other marine animals.

Explore Husum by Foot

Husum is a small town, and exploring it on foot is a great way to get to know the area. There are several walking tours available that highlight the town’s history, architecture, and culture.

Week-Long Itinerary for Husum

Husum is a perfect destination for those seeking a week-long vacation. Here is a sample itinerary:

Day 1: Explore Husum on foot.

Day 2: Visit the Nordfriesisches Museum and the Theodor Storm House.

Day 3: Take a bike ride along the Nord-Ostsee-Kanal.

Day 4: Take a boat tour and spot some seals.

Day 5: Visit the Wadden Sea National Park and nearby islands.

Day 6: Relax in the scenic Husum Harbour.

Day 7: Visit local shops and markets and purchase souvenirs and local products.

Food and Drink Scene of Husum

Must-Try Dishes

Finkenwerder Pannfisch

Finkenwerder Pannfisch is a classic fish dish that is native to the region. The dish usually features pan-fried fish, potatoes, and onions.

Rote Grütze

Rote Grütze is a berry pudding that is a sweet traditional dessert and a must-try for anyone with a sweet tooth.

Local Beverages

Husumer Brauhaus Beer

Husumer Brauhaus is a local brewery that produces excellent beer. The brewery’s beer garden is a great place to enjoy a cold brew on a warm summer day.

Where to Stay in Husum: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hotel Altes Gymnasium

The Hotel Altes Gymnasium is a historic hotel that boasts charming architecture and modern amenities. It is located within walking distance of the town center and offers a comfortable stay.

Nordsee Hotel Hinrichsen

The Nordsee Hotel Hinrichsen is a cozy hotel that offers beautiful views of the North Sea and is a great option for those on a budget.

What to Buy in Husum: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Frisian Tea

Frisian tea is a popular local product that makes a great gift for tea lovers. The tea is a blend of black tea and regional herbs and comes in several unique flavors.

Husum Salt

Husum Salt is a local product that comes from the salt marshes in the area. The salt has a unique flavor and makes an excellent addition to any dish.

Local Culture and Customs of Husum

The culture of Husum is rich in traditions, especially when it comes to local festivals and celebrations. One of the most famous is the annual Krokusbluete (Crocus Blossom), which celebrates the arrival of spring and the blooming of crocuses in the town’s gardens.

Best Time to Visit Husum

The best time to visit Husum is during the summer months of June to August when the weather is warm, and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy. However, if you are interested in cultural events, consider visiting during the winter months when there are many local festivals and celebrations.

Do’s and Don’ts of Husum

Do:

  • Take a walk by the scenic Husum Harbour.
  • Enjoy a cup of Frisian tea.
  • Visit the Nordfriesisches Museum to learn about the local culture and history.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter.
  • Respect the local culture and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Husum

Husum is a safe town, with low crime rates. However, it is still important to take some precautions and stay aware of your surroundings, especially when traveling alone at night.

Health in Husum

Husum has excellent healthcare services, and there are several hospitals and clinics in the area. It is advisable to have travel insurance when visiting, especially if you are planning on engaging in adventurous activities.

Getting to Husum: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Husum

The closest airports to Husum are Hamburg Airport and Bremen Airport. Both airports offer regular flights to several international and domestic destinations.

Bus Stations Near Husum

The Husum bus station is conveniently located next to the train station and offers regular bus services to several neighboring towns and cities.

Other Options

There are several car rental companies in the area, which is an excellent option if you want to explore the neighboring towns and countryside. Alternatively, you can also take a taxi or ride-sharing service like Uber to get around.

How to Get Around in Husum

Husum can easily be explored on foot, and many of the town’s attractions are within walking distance of the town center. However, if you want to explore the neighboring countryside, there are several transportation options available, including buses, rental cars, and taxis.

Where to Go Next from Husum: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Sylt

Sylt is an island that can be reached via a short ferry ride from Husum Harbour. The island is known for its beautiful beaches, picturesque towns, and upscale resorts.

Rendsburg

Rendsburg is a charming town that is famous for its impressive bridge over the Kiel Canal. The town is also home to several historical landmarks and museums.

Conclusion

Husum is a hidden gem in the North Sea Coast of Germany that offers a perfect blend of culture, history, and natural beauty. With its charming architecture, cultural landmarks, and outdoor adventures, Husum is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for a unique and unforgettable vacation experience. Plan your trip now and discover all that Husum has to offer.
