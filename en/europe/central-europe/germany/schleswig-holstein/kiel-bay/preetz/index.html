Introduction to Preetz

Nestled in Kiel Bay, Schleswig-Holstein, Preetz is a charming town in Germany that offers a lot of surprises to its visitors. The town boasts a rich history, picturesque landscapes, delicious food and drinks, welcoming locals, and a lot of exciting activities to do.

Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor enthusiast, a foodie, or a curious traveler, Preetz is a destination that you shouldn’t miss. In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ve listed the top attractions, outdoor adventures, food and drinks, accommodations, culture and customs, transportation options, and nearby excursions that you can experience while in Preetz.

List of Places to Visit in Preetz

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Preetz is a town steeped in history and culture, and it has several museums and historical places that are worth visiting. Some of these cultural attractions include:

  • Preetz Museum: housed in a historic building that used to be a local school, this museum features exhibits about the town’s history, artworks, and traditional costumes.
  • Gut Panker: a stately home located in a scenic countryside that houses a museum displaying the life and ancestry of the Flemish noble family Von Rumohr.
  • St. Nicholas Church: a beautiful church that dates back to the 13th century and features stunning paintings, sculptures, and stained-glass windows.

Natural Attractions of Preetz: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you’re an outdoor enthusiast, Preetz has a lot to offer. From scenic hikes to tranquil lakes, here are some of the natural attractions that you can explore:

  • Preetzer Tunneltal: a nature reserve that boasts stunning geological formations and hiking trails.
  • Lanker See: a serene lake where you can go fishing, swimming, or simply enjoy the beautiful scenery.
  • Klever Moor: a nature reserve that houses a variety of plant and animal species and many walking trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Preetz

Preetz offers numerous hiking and biking trails that provide visitors with an opportunity to explore the countryside and enjoy the fresh air. Some of the popular hiking and biking routes include:

  • Hiking route from Panker to Behrensdorf: a beautiful 13.5-kilometer hiking route that takes you through forests, hills, and the Baltic Sea coast.
  • Biking route from Preetz to Schwentinental: a 27-kilometer biking route that takes you through beautiful landscapes and historical sites.

Best Things to Do in Preetz: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, there are several other exciting things to do in Preetz. Here are some of the best activities and tours that you can experience:

  • Canoeing on the Schwentine: a popular activity where you can paddle down the Schwentine river and enjoy the beautiful scenery.
  • Preetz city tour: a guided tour of the town that takes you to the most important landmarks and historical sites.
  • Flea market: a weekend flea market that sells unique items and local products.

Week-Long Itinerary for Preetz

For visitors who are planning to stay in Preetz for a week, here’s a suggested itinerary that includes the top attractions and activities:

Day 1: Visit the Preetz Museum and St. Nicholas Church Day 2: Hike the Preetzer Tunneltal or bike the Preetz to Schwentinental route Day 3: Canoe on the Schwentine Day 4: Visit Gut Panker and Lanker See Day 5: Join the Preetz city tour and explore the town’s historic landmarks Day 6: Shop at the flea market or relax at one of the town’s cafes Day 7: Explore the Klever Moor nature reserve

Food and Drink Scene of Preetz

Preetz has a diverse food and drink scene, and there are plenty of options for visitors to choose from. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Holsteiner Katenschinken: a smoked ham that is a local delicacy in Preetz.
  • Preetzer Taler: a traditional biscuit that is a popular snack in the town.
  • Kohlroulade: a stuffed cabbage roll served with mashed potatoes.

Local Beverages

  • Beer: Preetz has several local breweries that produce excellent beers.
  • Birnenmost: a pear cider that is a refreshing drink during summer.
  • Korn: a type of schnapps that is a popular German drink.

Where to Stay in Preetz: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Preetz has several accommodations to choose from, ranging from hotels to guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay in Preetz:

Local Culture and Customs of Preetz

Preetz has a rich culture and customs that visitors should respect and be aware of. Here are some of the local customs and etiquette:

  • Greeting: Germans value formality and often greet each other using the formal “Sie” pronoun.
  • Punctuality: Germans are usually punctual and expect others to be on time.
  • Recycling: Germans have strict rules on recycling, and visitors should follow them.

Best Time to Visit Preetz

The best time to visit Preetz is during the summer months of June to August when the weather is warm and pleasant, and the outdoor activities are more enjoyable. However, Preetz offers different experiences throughout the year, such as Christmas markets during winter and the blooming of flowers in spring.

Do’s and Don’ts of Preetz

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and etiquette
  • Try the local food and drinks
  • Take your time to explore the town and countryside

Don’t:

  • Be loud and disruptive in public places
  • Smoke in public places or designated non-smoking areas
  • Forget to bring cash, as not all establishments accept credit cards.

Safety and Health

Safety in Preetz

Preetz is a safe town, and visitors can explore it without any concerns. It is still advisable to take necessary precautions such as keeping your valuables safe, avoiding unlit areas at night, and obeying traffic rules.

Health in Preetz

Preetz has several medical facilities, such as hospitals and clinics, that provide quality healthcare services to locals and visitors. It is still recommended to have travel insurance and bring medications if necessary.

Preetz is a small town that can easily be explored on foot or by bike. There’s also a local bus service that connects different parts of the town.

Where to Go Next from Preetz: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
