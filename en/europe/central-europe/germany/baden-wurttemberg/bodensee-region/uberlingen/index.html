Introduction to Überlingen

Nestled in the Bodensee Region of Baden-Württemberg, Germany, Überlingen is a charming lakeside town with a rich history, stunning natural surroundings, and a thriving cultural scene. As you stroll along its winding cobbled streets, you’ll be transported to a different era, with the town’s medieval architecture, picturesque alleys, and half-timbered houses holding a storybook charm.

But Überlingen isn’t just a pretty face. The town boasts a wealth of cultural treasures, including museums, galleries, and historical landmarks, as well as natural attractions like hiking paths, bike trails, and scenic viewpoints. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing retreat or an action-packed adventure, Überlingen has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Überlingen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

St. Nikolaus Cathedral

One of Überlingen’s most iconic landmarks, St. Nikolaus Cathedral stands tall in the heart of the old town. This stunning Gothic structure was built in the 15th century and features an ornate altarpiece, a beautiful pulpit, and a tower that offers panoramic views of the town and Lake Constance.

Heimatmuseum

Housed in a medieval half-timbered house, the Heimatmuseum is a must-visit for history buffs. Here, you’ll find artifacts and exhibits that showcase the town’s rich past, from Roman times to the present day. Don’t miss the impressive collection of folk art and crafts, which includes woodcarvings, ceramics, and textiles.

Kloster Birnau

Nestled on the shores of Lake Constance, the Kloster Birnau is a magnificent baroque church that dates back to the 18th century. With its ornate frescoes, elaborate ceiling paintings, and gilded altar, this stunning landmark is a testament to the region’s rich cultural heritage.

Natural Attractions of Überlingen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lake Constance Promenade

Stretching along the shores of Lake Constance, the promenade is the perfect place to take a leisurely stroll or bike ride. Here, you’ll enjoy sweeping views of the lake and the surrounding mountains, as well as access to a range of water sports and activities.

Scheffelblick Viewpoint

For a bird’s eye view of Überlingen, head to the Scheffelblick viewpoint. This scenic lookout offers panoramic vistas of the town and Lake Constance, as well as the nearby Alps.

Haldenhof Nature Reserve

Located just a short drive from Überlingen, the Haldenhof Nature Reserve is a must-visit for nature lovers. Here, you’ll find a network of hiking trails that wind through lush forests, past cascading waterfalls, and across verdant meadows.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Überlingen

Bodensee-Königssee Cycle Route

This scenic cycle route stretches over 450 kilometers and offers breathtaking views of Lake Constance and the surrounding mountains. The route passes through charming towns and villages, making it a great way to discover the region’s cultural and historical landmarks.

Überlinger See Panorama Path

This picturesque hiking trail winds around the shores of Lake Constance and offers stunning views of the water and the surrounding mountains. The route stretches for 13 kilometers, passing through idyllic landscapes and verdant forests.

Best Things to Do in Überlingen: Activities and Tours

Boat Tour of Lake Constance

A boat tour of Lake Constance is a great way to explore the region’s natural beauty and soak up the local culture. You’ll stop off at idyllic beaches and quaint lakeside villages, as well as enjoying panoramic views of the Alps and the surrounding countryside.

Überlingen Wine Tasting Tour

Baden-Württemberg is renowned for its delicious wines, and Überlingen is no exception. Take a wine tasting tour to sample the region’s best wines, as well as learning about the history and traditions of winemaking in the area.

Food and Drink Scene of Überlingen

Must-Try Dishes

Bodensee Fish

Fresh fish from the Bodensee is a must-try when in Überlingen. The local specialty is smoked trout, which is often served with potatoes and a range of local vegetables.

Käsespätzle

This hearty dish consists of soft egg noodles cooked with a range of local cheeses and topped with caramelized onions. It’s the perfect comfort food after a long day of hiking or cycling.

Local Beverages

Bodensee Wine

Baden-Württemberg is known for its high-quality wines, and the Bodensee region is no exception. Sample some of the local white and red wines, which are often grown on steep slopes overlooking the lake.

Überlinger Beer

For a refreshing taste of local brews, try the Überlinger beer. Brewed on-site at the town’s microbreweries, this crisp beer is the perfect way to quench your thirst after a long day of sightseeing.

Where to Stay in Überlingen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hotel Garni

This small but friendly family-run hotel is located just a short walk from the old town. The rooms are spacious and comfortable, with a range of amenities including free WiFi and flat-screen TVs.

Hostel Überlingen

For budget-friendly accommodation, check out the Hostel Überlingen. This centrally-located hostel offers clean and comfortable rooms, as well as a range of communal spaces where you can meet other travelers.

What to Buy in Überlingen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Bodensee Wine

Take home a bottle of the region’s best wines, which are made from grapes grown on steep hills overlooking the lake.

Handmade Crafts

Discover a range of handmade crafts at local markets and shops, including ceramics, woodwork, and textiles.

Local Culture and Customs of Überlingen

The people of Überlingen are known for their hospitality and friendliness. Traditional customs and festivals are an important part of the town’s cultural heritage, including the annual Fasnet carnival celebrations, which take place in February and March.

Best Time to Visit Überlingen

The best time to visit Überlingen is during the summer months, when the weather is warm and sunny, and the town’s lakeside beaches are perfect for swimming and sunbathing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Überlingen

Do:

  • Explore the town’s historic old town
  • Visit the Haldenhof Nature Reserve
  • Sample local foods and wines
  • Take in the panoramic views from the Scheffelblick viewpoint

Don’t:

  • Swim in the lake outside designated swimming areas
  • Leave valuables unattended
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions

Safety and Health

Safety in Überlingen

Überlingen is a safe town, but as with any destination, visitors should take precautions to protect their personal belongings, particularly in crowded areas. Swimming in the lake is only allowed in designated areas, so be sure to follow local regulations to stay safe.

Health in Überlingen

Überlingen has a range of medical facilities, including clinics and hospitals. EU citizens can access emergency medical treatment free of charge with their EHIC card.

Getting to Überlingen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Überlingen

The nearest international airport is located in Stuttgart, which is approximately two hours’ drive from Überlingen. Zurich Airport in Switzerland is also an option, located about an hour and a half’s drive away.

Bus Stations Near Überlingen

Überlingen is well-connected to other towns and cities in the region via bus services. The nearest bus station is located in the town center, with regular services to destinations throughout the Bodensee Region.

Other Options

If you’re traveling by car, Überlingen is easily accessible via the A81 motorway, which runs from Stuttgart to Singen. There are also regular train services from nearby towns and cities, including Friedrichshafen, Ravensburg, and Konstanz.

How to Get Around in Überlingen

The best way to explore Überlingen is on foot or by bike. The town is small and compact, with many of the main attractions located within walking distance of each other. Cycling is also a popular way to get around, with a range of bike rental services available throughout the town.

Where to Go Next from Überlingen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Konstanz

Located just 30 minutes’ drive from Überlingen, Konstanz is a charming university town with a rich history and a vibrant cultural scene. Explore the town’s medieval architecture, stroll along the scenic harbor, or take a boat tour of the lake to nearby destinations.

Meersburg

A short drive from Überlingen, Meersburg is a beautiful lakeside town with a rich history and stunning scenery. Visit the town’s impressive castle, stroll along the promenade, or explore the winding cobbled streets of the old town.

Conclusion

With its stunning natural scenery, rich cultural heritage, and range of activities and attractions, Überlingen is an ideal destination for a summer getaway. Explore the town’s historic landmarks, taste the best wines and local dishes, and immerse yourself in the region’s traditional culture and customs.
