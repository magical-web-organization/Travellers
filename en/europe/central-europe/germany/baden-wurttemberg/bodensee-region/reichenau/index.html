Ultimate Guide to Reichenau, Germany

Located in the picturesque Bodensee Region of Baden-Württemberg, Germany, Reichenau is a small island in the middle of Lake Constance. The island is home to three beautiful churches that have been recognized as UNESCO World Heritage Sites, as well as stunning natural landscapes that are perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. Whether you’re interested in history, culture, or the great outdoors, Reichenau is the perfect destination for your next trip to Germany. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll explore the best that Reichenau has to offer, including the top places to visit, hiking and biking routes, food and drink scene, where to stay, local culture, safety and health information, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Reichenau

Reichenau is home to an array of cultural and natural attractions. Here are some of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • St. George’s Church: Built in the 9th century, St. George’s Church is a stunning example of Carolingian architecture. The church has been recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • St. Peter and Paul’s Church: This 10th-century church features beautiful frescoes and intricate stonework. It too has been recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Abbey Museum: Located in the former Benedictine abbey, the Abbey Museum showcases artifacts from Reichenau’s rich history.

Natural Attractions of Reichenau: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Constance: The island is surrounded by the crystal clear waters of Lake Constance, perfect for swimming, boating, and fishing.
  • Herrenwiese Nature Reserve: This beautiful nature reserve is home to a variety of birds and other wildlife, as well as stunning views of the lake and surrounding mountains.
  • Bike Trail: Reichenau is home to a number of biking trails that allow visitors to explore the island’s scenery at their own pace.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Reichenau

Reichenau is a paradise for hikers and bikers. Here are some of the top routes to explore:

  • Bodensee-Radweg: This scenic bike trail runs along the shores of Lake Constance, offering stunning views of the water and surrounding mountains.
  • Reichenau Tour: This hiking trail takes visitors on a scenic tour of the island, offering breathtaking views of the lake and surrounding mountains.
  • Seerundweg: This 3.5-mile hiking trail around the island offers beautiful views of the lake and surrounding scenery.

Best Things to Do in Reichenau: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, Reichenau offers plenty of other activities for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the most popular:

  • Boat Tours: Take a boat tour of Lake Constance to discover the island’s stunning surroundings from a different perspective.
  • Wine Tasting: The Bodensee Region is known for its delicious wines, so be sure to sample some local vintages during your visit.
  • Water Sports: The crystal clear waters of Lake Constance make it the perfect spot for swimming, kayaking, and windsurfing.

Week-Long Itinerary for Reichenau

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Reichenau, here’s a sample itinerary to make the most of your time on the island:

Day Activity
1 Arrive and explore the island’s cultural attractions, including St. George’s Church and St. Peter and Paul’s Church.
2 Go on a bike tour of the island’s beautiful scenery, including the Herrenwiese Nature Reserve.
3 Take a boat tour of Lake Constance and explore nearby towns like Konstanz and Meersburg.
4 Spend the day hiking the island’s scenic trails, including the Seerundweg.
5 Explore Reichenau’s delicious food and drink scene, from local wines to fresh seafood.
6 Engage in water sports like swimming or kayaking on Lake Constance.
7 Shop for souvenirs and enjoy your last day on the island before departing.

Food and Drink Scene of Reichenau

German cuisine is famous for its hearty and delicious dishes, and Reichenau is no exception. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Spaetzle: A traditional German pasta dish that is hearty and filling.
  • Schnitzel: A thin, breaded pork or veal cutlet that is fried until crispy.
  • Black Forest Cake: A rich, delicious chocolate cake layered with whipped cream and cherries.

Local Beverages

  • Bodensee Wines: The Bodensee Region is known for its delicious white wines, including Müller-Thurgau and Grauburgunder.
  • Weizenbier: A popular German wheat beer that is often enjoyed during the summer months.
  • Apfelwein: A refreshing apple cider that is popular throughout Germany.

Where to Stay in Reichenau: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Reichenau offers a variety of accommodations for visitors, from cozy guesthouses to luxurious hotels. Here are some of our favorites:

  • Gästehaus St. Georgen: This charming guesthouse is located in the heart of Reichenau, just a short walk from St. George’s Church.
  • Hotel Gasthof Krone: This historic hotel has been welcoming guests since the 16th century and offers comfortable, traditional rooms.
  • Seehotel Litz: Located right on the shores of Lake Constance, this beautiful hotel offers stunning views of the water and surrounding mountains.

What to Buy in Reichenau: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking to bring home a souvenir from your trip to Reichenau, here are some of the best local products to consider:

  • Bodensee Wines: Pick up a bottle of Müller-Thurgau or Grauburgunder to enjoy a taste of the Bodensee Region back home.
  • Cuckoo Clocks: Germany is famous for its cuckoo clocks, and Reichenau is no exception. Pick up a traditional clock as a souvenir.
  • Handmade Pottery: Reichenau is home to a number of skilled potters, so consider picking up a beautiful handmade piece of pottery to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Reichenau

The people of Reichenau take pride in their rich cultural heritage and traditional way of life. Visitors should respect local customs and be mindful of the island’s historic churches and other cultural landmarks.

Best Time to Visit Reichenau

The best time to visit Reichenau is during the summer months, from June to September, when the weather is warm and the island’s outdoor attractions are in full swing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Reichenau

To make the most of your visit to Reichenau, here are some of the key do’s and don’ts:

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try local cuisine, including delicious wines and hearty German dishes.
  • Take advantage of the island’s outdoor attractions, including hiking, biking, and water sports.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs inside the island’s churches without permission.
  • Litter or disturb the island’s natural environment.
  • Ignore safety precautions when engaging in water sports or other outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Reichenau is a safe destination, but visitors should take appropriate safety precautions when swimming, hiking, and engaging in other outdoor activities. The island has a small medical clinic, but serious medical issues may require transport to a larger city.

Safety in Reichenau

  • Take appropriate safety precautions when engaging in water sports on Lake Constance.
  • Be mindful of weather conditions, particularly during storms or rough winds on the lake.
  • Avoid swimming alone or in unfamiliar areas.

Health in Reichenau

  • Be sure to bring all necessary medications or medical supplies with you, as the island’s medical facilities are limited.
  • Be aware of ticks, which are common in wooded areas throughout Germany.
  • Drink plenty of water and stay hydrated, particularly during the summer months.

Getting to Reichenau: Transportation Options and Logistics

Reichenau is located in the Bodensee Region of Germany and can be easily reached by a number of transportation options.

Airports Near Reichenau

  • Zurich Airport (ZRH): Located 59 miles away, Zurich Airport is the closest major airport to Reichenau.
  • Stuttgart Airport (STR): Located 130 miles away, Stuttgart Airport is another nearby option.

Bus Stations Near Reichenau

  • Konstanz Central Bus Station: The closest bus station, located just 6 miles from Reichenau.
  • Friedrichshafen Central Bus Station: Another nearby option, located 16 miles away.

Other Options

  • Train: The nearest train station is in Konstanz, which has connections to major cities throughout Europe.
  • Car: Reichenau is easily accessible by car, and there are several parking lots available on the island.

How to Get Around in Reichenau

Reichenau is a small island and can easily be explored on foot or by bike. Visitors can also take taxis or rent cars for longer trips around the Bodensee Region.

Where to Go Next from Reichenau: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Reichenau is a great base for exploring the Bodensee Region and nearby destinations. Here are some options for your next adventure:

  • Konstanz: Just a short ferry ride from Reichenau, the historic city of Konstanz is home to beautiful architecture and delicious cuisine.
  • Meersburg: Known for its beautiful castle and charming streets, Meersburg is a popular destination on the shores of Lake Constance.
  • Mainau Island: This stunning island is home to breathtaking gardens and stunning landscapes.

Conclusion

Reichenau is a beautiful destination that offers the perfect blend of history, culture, and outdoor adventure. From its historic churches to its stunning natural landscapes, there’s something for everyone to enjoy. By following the tips and information in this guide, you’ll be able to make the most of your visit to Reichenau and take home unforgettable memories.
