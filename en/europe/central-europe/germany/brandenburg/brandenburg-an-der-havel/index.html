A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Brandenburg an der Havel

Located in the state of Brandenburg in Germany, Brandenburg an der Havel is a picturesque town that offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. The city is situated on the banks of the River Havel, making it an ideal holiday destination for anyone who loves outdoor activities.

In this guide, we will give you a comprehensive tour of Brandenburg an der Havel, including all the best things to do, see, and eat in this lovely town.

Introduction to Brandenburg an der Havel

Brandenburg an der Havel is a small town with a population of just over 70,000 people. It has a long and rich history, dating back to the 12th century. The town was once an important trading center in medieval Germany, and its historic landmarks and impressive fortifications still bear the marks of this bygone era. Visitors to Brandenburg an der Havel can enjoy exploring the town’s charming narrow streets, visiting its museums, and taking in its stunning natural scenery.

List of Places to Visit in Brandenburg an der Havel

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Brandenburg Cathedral: Built in the Gothic style between the 12th and 14th centuries, this cathedral is the town’s most famous landmark. Visitors can admire its beautiful stained-glass windows, intricate details, and stunning vaulted ceilings.

  • Museum im Freyhaus: This museum is housed in a beautiful 18th-century building and showcases the history and culture of Brandenburg an der Havel. Visitors can view exhibits on local art, crafts, and the town’s industrial heritage.

  • Old Town Hall: Built in 1462, this historic hall once served as the seat of government for Brandenburg an der Havel’s city council. Today, it houses the town’s tourist information center, where visitors can pick up maps and guides to the town’s many attractions.

Natural Attractions of Brandenburg an der Havel: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Havel River Boat Tours: There are few better ways to experience Brandenburg an der Havel’s scenic beauty than on a boat tour along the Havel River. Along the trip, you will discover beautiful landscapes and stunning views of the town’s skyline

  • Beetzsee Lakes: Located just outside of the town, this beautiful lake is an ideal spot for swimming and nature watching. Visitors can enjoy kayaking, boating, and exploring the surrounding countryside.

  • Plauer See: This beautiful lake is a popular destination for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports. It is located just a short distance from the town and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Brandenburg an der Havel

Visitors to Brandenburg an der Havel can embark on several great hiking and biking routes that take in the town’s natural beauty. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Havel Cycle Route: This cycling route takes in 400km of stunning scenery along the Havel River. It passes through Brandenburg an der Havel and takes in several other charming towns and villages along the way.

  • Naturpark Westliches Brandenburg: This nature park offers several hiking trails that take visitors through lush forests, rolling hills and offer stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

Best Things to Do in Brandenburg an der Havel: Activities and Tours

Brandenburg an der Havel offers many different activities and tours that are perfect for visitors of all ages. Here are some of the best:

  • Hot-Air Balloon Rides: Take a hot-air balloon ride over the town’s beautiful countryside for a unique perspective of Brandenburg an der Havel.

  • Brandenburg walking tours: Join a walking tour of the town and discover its rich history, beautiful architecture and iconic landmarks.

Food and Drink Scene of Brandenburg an der Havel

Brandenburg an der Havel boasts an exciting culinary scene that is sure to satisfy even the most discerning foodies. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pörten: This is a delicious fried dough that is typically served with different dips

  • Brandenburger Teller: This is a local specialty that features a mouth-watering mix of meats, potatoes, and vegetables, sometimes served with a cream sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Spreewood Distillery: An award-winning distillery that offers several different types of liquors and spirits that are distilled using traditional methods.

  • Lusatian Lakeland’s winery: Refreshing and fruity wines are produced from quality grapes grown in the luscious growing area located by Lusatian Lakeland.

Where to Stay in Brandenburg an der Havel: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Brandenburg an der Havel offers a range of accommodation options, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. Here are some options:

  • Seehotel Brandenburg an der Havel: This four-star hotel is located on the banks of the Havel River, offering stunning views and a peaceful atmosphere.

  • Pension Havelfloß: This guesthouse is located in a peaceful setting and offers comfortable rooms and a welcoming atmosphere.

What to Buy in Brandenburg an der Havel: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Brandenburg an der Havel is known for its high-quality local products, which include:

  • Pottery: Buy handmade pottery from one of several local shops and artisanal workshops located in the town.

  • Linocut prints: Take home a unique souvenir that captures the town’s charm and artistic traditions.

Local Culture and Customs of Brandenburg an der Havel

Brandenburg an der Havel has a rich cultural heritage and unique customs that visitors can learn more about during their stay. One of the most popular festivals in Brandenburg is the “Rolandsfest,” a medieval festival that takes place in the town each year in June.

Best Time to Visit Brandenburg an der Havel

The best time to visit Brandenburg an der Havel is during the summer months, from June through August when the weather is warm, and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Brandenburg an der Havel

Do:

  • Take a bike tour: Biking is one of the best ways to explore the town’s beautiful countryside and charming streets.

  • Shop local: Support local artisans and businesses by shopping at one of the many local boutique stores and markets.

Don’t:

  • Be too loud or rowdy: The town has a peaceful atmosphere, so try to be respectful of the local customs and atmosphere.

  • Leave trash in the natural beauty spots: Make sure to leave any natural areas that you visit as pristine as when you arrived.

Safety and Health

Safety in Brandenburg an der Havel

Brandenburg is a safe and peaceful town, and visitors are unlikely to encounter any serious safety risks during their stay. However, be aware of the traffic around the town, and take appropriate safety measures such as wearing a bicycle helmet when cycling.

Health in Brandenburg an der Havel

Brandenburg an der Havel has several medical and health facilities, including pharmacies and a hospital, so visitors can be assured that medical help is never far away.

Getting to Brandenburg an der Havel: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Brandenburg an der Havel

Brandenburg an der Havel is located just 61 km from the Berlin Tegel Airport, which serves many international flights, making it convenient to get to the town from almost anywhere.

Bus Stations Near Brandenburg an der Havel

Several bus services operate around Brandenburg an der Havel that are ideal for getting to know the region.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Brandenburg an der Havel by train or car. The town is well-connected to the rest of Germany, making it easy to get to from anywhere in the country.

How to Get Around in Brandenburg an der Havel

The town is relatively small and can easily be explored by foot or bicycle. Visitors can also make use of the efficient public transport system that operates around the town.

Where to Go Next from Brandenburg an der Havel: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several exciting nearby destinations that visitors can explore after visiting Brandenburg an der Havel. Here are some options:

  • Potsdam: A nearby city that is home to several beautiful parks, palaces, and historic landmarks.

  • Berlin: The German capital is just 50 km from Brandenburg an der Havel and offers visitors a wealth of cultural, historical, and artistic attractions.

Conclusion

Brandenburg an der Havel is a charming town that offers visitors a perfect blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. Whether you are interested in hiking and biking, exploring historic landmarks, or sampling local cuisine, you are sure to find something that you love in this lovely German town. From its unique festivals to its picturesque countryside, Brandenburg an der Havel is a town that you won’t want to miss!
