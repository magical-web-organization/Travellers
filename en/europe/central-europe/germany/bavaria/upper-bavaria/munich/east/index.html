Getting to East: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near East

The closest airport to East is Munich Airport (MUC), which is about 40 minutes away by car or public transportation.

Bus Stations Near East

There are several bus stations in Munich that provide service to East, including the Munich Central Bus Station (ZOB) and the Munich-Riem Bus Station.

Other Options

The Munich S-Bahn commuter train system provides service to many areas of the city, including East. There are also many taxis and bike rental options available.

How to Get Around in East

The best way to get around East is on foot, by bike, or by public transportation. The Munich S-Bahn commuter train system provides service to many areas of the city, and there are also many bike rental options available. Taxis are also readily available in the city.
