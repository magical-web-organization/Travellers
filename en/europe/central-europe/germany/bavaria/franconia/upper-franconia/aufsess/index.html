Introduction to Aufseß

If you’re looking for a quaint town with rich history and a beautiful Bavarian landscape, look no further than Aufseß. Located in Upper Franconia, in Franconia, in Bavaria, in Germany, in Central Europe, in Europe, Aufseß is a hidden gem that boasts of natural beauty, scenic landscapes, and a rich cultural heritage. The town may not be as famous as other Bavarian destinations, but it more than makes up for it with its charm, hospitality, and picturesque backdrops.

List of Places to Visit in Aufseß

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The town’s history can be traced back to the early Middle Ages, and it has a rich cultural heritage that reflects in its architecture, landmarks, and museums. Some of the museums and historical places worth visiting in Aufseß include:

  • Castle Aufseß: One of the main landmarks of the town, the castle was built in the 12th century and has since been owned by the Aufseß family. The castle is now open to the public and is home to a museum showcasing the family’s history and the town’s past.
  • Brauerei Gasthof Rothenbach: A brewery-turned-hotel, Brauerei Gasthof Rothenbach offers a glimpse into the town’s brewing history. The brewery dates back to 1862 and is known for its traditional brewing techniques.
  • Pfarrkirche St. Michael auf dem Hügel: The town’s parish church, the Pfarrkirche St. Michael auf dem Hügel, was built in the 17th century and is known for its Baroque style and ornate altar.

Natural Attractions of Aufseß: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts, Aufseß has plenty of attractions to offer. From scenic hikes to refreshing dips in the creek, here are some of the town’s natural attractions to explore:

  • Aufseßer Thal: Also known as the Aufseß Valley, this picturesque valley offers scenic hiking trails through lush green forests and past gently flowing streams.
  • Schöllkrippener Weiher: A natural pond with crystal-clear waters, the Schöllkrippener Weiher is a popular spot for swimming and fishing.
  • Schlossberg Felsenkeller: One of the town’s hidden gems, the Schlossberg Felsenkeller is an underground cellar that was used to store beer in the old days. Visitors can explore the cellar and learn about the brewing traditions of the town.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Aufseß

Aufseß has several hiking and biking trails that offer stunning views of the town’s landscape. Some of the popular routes include:

  • Aufseß-Weißmaintal Cycle Path: This 40 km cycle path takes you through picturesque villages, past scenic landscapes, and along the banks of the Weißmain River.
  • Thal-Rundwanderweg: This circular hiking trail takes you through the Aufseß Valley and past enchanting waterfalls and towering rock formations.
  • Jura-Höhenweg: A long-distance hiking trail that stretches for 250 km, the Jura-Höhenweg is a challenging yet rewarding route that offers jaw-dropping views of the Bavarian countryside.

Best Things to Do in Aufseß: Activities and Tours

From cultural experiences to outdoor adventures, Aufseß has something for everyone. Here are some of the best things to do in Aufseß:

  • Visit a traditional brewery: Brewing is deeply interconnected with the town’s heritage, and a visit to one of its breweries is a must-do. Brauerei Gasthof Rothenbach and Brauerei Friedel are two of the most popular breweries in town.
  • Explore Aufseß Castle: The castle is open to visitors, and the museum inside showcases the history of the family and the town.
  • Hike through the Aufseß Valley: The valley offers some of the most scenic hiking trails in the town, with breathtaking waterfalls and serene landscapes.

Week-Long Itinerary for Aufseß

Here’s a sample itinerary for a week-long stay in Aufseß:

Day 1: Visit Aufseß Castle and its museum, followed by a traditional Bavarian lunch at Brauerei Friedel.

Day 2: Go on a scenic hike through the Aufseß Valley and stop at the Schlossberg Felsenkeller to learn about the town’s brewing traditions.

Day 3: Take a cycling tour along the Aufseß-Weißmaintal Cycle Path and stop at the Schöllkrippener Weiher for a refreshing swim.

Day 4: Visit the Pfarrkirche St. Michael auf dem Hügel and admire its ornate altar, followed by lunch at Brauerei Gasthof Rothenbach.

Day 5: Go on a day trip to nearby Bayreuth and explore its famous Margravial Opera House and its beautiful gardens.

Day 6: Enjoy a relaxing day exploring the town’s markets and shops, and take back souvenirs of the town’s famous ceramic products.

Day 7: Bid Aufseß adieu and take a scenic drive to the nearby town of Bamberg, known for its UNESCO-listed Old Town and its famous smoked beer.

Food and Drink Scene of Aufseß

Must-Try Dishes

Bavarian cuisine is all about hearty, meaty dishes that are perfect for a cold winter evening. Here are some of the must-try dishes in Aufseß:

  • Schäuferla: A pork shoulder roast that’s slow-cooked to perfection and served with potato dumplings and sauerkraut.
  • Leberknödelsuppe: A traditional Bavarian soup made with liver dumplings and beef broth.
  • Kaiserschmarrn: A sweet dish made from torn pancakes, served with fruit compote and whipped cream.

Local Beverages

Brewing has been an integral part of Aufseß’s culture for centuries, and it’s no wonder that the town has some of the best beers in Bavaria. Some of the local beers worth trying include:

  • Aufseßer Dunkel: An amber lager with a caramel-y flavor and a crisp finish.
  • Rothenbacher Kellerbier: A refreshing beer that’s brewed using traditional methods and served straight from the barrel.
  • Friedel Kellerbier: A classic German Helles beer that’s light and crisp, with a slightly sweet finish.

Where to Stay in Aufseß: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

From cozy guesthouses to luxurious hotels, Aufseß has plenty of options for every budget. Here are some of the best places to stay in town:

  • Schloss Thurnau Hotel: Located in the nearby town of Thurnau, this elegant hotel is housed in a castle and oozes luxury and sophistication.
  • Gasthof zur Post: A charming guesthouse in the heart of Aufseß town, Gasthof zur Post offers comfortable rooms and a cozy atmosphere.
  • Landgasthof Ploss: A family-run guesthouse that’s known for its warm hospitality and excellent Bavarian cuisine.

What to Buy in Aufseß: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Aufseß is known for its ceramic products, and visitors can take back unique souvenirs and gifts made by local artisans. Some of the popular items include:

  • Ceramic mugs: The town’s breweries are famous for their ceramic mugs, which make for excellent souvenirs.
  • Ceramic sculptures: The town has several ceramic workshops that produce intricate sculptures and figurines.
  • Ceramic Christmas ornaments: If you’re visiting the town during the festive season, don’t miss out on the beautiful ceramic Christmas ornaments that are sold at the Christmas market.

Local Culture and Customs of Aufseß

Bavarians are known for their love of tradition and culture, and Aufseß is no exception. Here are some of the town’s unique customs and cultural practices:

  • Krampuslauf: Every December, the town hosts a Krampuslauf, a traditional Bavarian event where locals dress up as Krampus, a mischievous creature from Alpine folklore.
  • Ceramics: The town’s ceramic industry dates back centuries, and it’s no wonder that ceramics play an important role in local culture and customs.
  • Brewing: Brewing has been a way of life in Aufseß for centuries, and it’s an art form that’s deeply intertwined with the town’s heritage.

Best Time to Visit Aufseß

The best time to visit Aufseß is during the summer months, from May to September, when the weather is mild and pleasant for outdoor activities. December is also a great time to visit if you want to experience the town’s Christmas markets and festivities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Aufseß

Do:

  • Try the local beer and Bavarian cuisine.
  • Visit the town’s landmarks and museums to learn about its history and culture.
  • Explore the town’s hiking and biking trails for stunning views of the Bavarian landscape.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget to carry cash, as not all places accept credit cards.
  • Don’t assume that everyone speaks English, as German is the primary language in town.
  • Avoid walking on the streets too late at night, as the town can be quiet and deserted after dark.

Safety and Health

Safety in Aufseß

Aufseß is generally a safe town, but visitors should be aware of petty crimes such as pickpocketing and thefts. It’s best to keep your valuables secure and carry only essential items when exploring the town.

Health in Aufseß

Aufseß has a good standard of healthcare, but visitors are advised to carry travel health insurance and ensure that their vaccinations are up-to-date before visiting.

Getting to Aufseß: Transportation Options and Logistics

Getting to Aufseß is easy, with several transportation options available.

Airports Near Aufseß

The nearest airports to Aufseß are Munich Airport (MUC) and Nuremberg Airport (NUE), both of which are about 2 hours away by car.

Bus Stations Near Aufseß

There are several bus stations near Aufseß, with the most popular options being Bayreuth Bus station and Forchheim Bus station.

Other Options

Driving to Aufseß is also a popular option, with several highways connecting it to nearby towns and cities.

How to Get Around in Aufseß

The best way to get around in Aufseß is on foot or by bike, as the town is relatively small and easy to navigate. Taxis and rental cars are also available for those who prefer them.

Where to Go Next from Aufseß: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Aufseß makes an excellent base for exploring the rest of Bavaria, with several destinations within easy reach. Some of the nearby destinations and excursions worth exploring include:

  • Bamberg: A UNESCO-listed town known for its medieval architecture and its famous smoked beer.
  • Bayreuth: A town known for its Baroque and Rococo architecture, its world-famous opera house, and its Richard Wagner Museum.
  • Nuremberg: A city known for its medieval architecture, its Christmas markets, and its famous gingerbread.

Conclusion

Aufseß is a town that’s steeped in history, culture, and natural beauty. From its picturesque hiking trails to its robust beer brewing culture, the town has something to offer every visitor. Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor enthusiast, or a beer lover, Aufseß is a destination that’s well worth exploring. So book your trip today, and experience the charm and hospitality of this Bavarian gem!
