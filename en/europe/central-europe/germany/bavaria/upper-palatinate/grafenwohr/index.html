Introduction to Grafenwöhr

Grafenwöhr is a small town situated in the Upper Palatinate region of Bavaria, Germany. The town is primarily known for the nearby US Army base that hosts various military training exercises throughout the year. However, Grafenwöhr is also a charming town that offers plenty of cultural and natural attractions for visitors to explore. With its picturesque countryside, rich history, and lively culture, Grafenwöhr is definitely worth a visit.

List of Places to Visit in Grafenwöhr

Here are some of the best places to visit in Grafenwöhr:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Grafenwöhr Tower Museum: Located in the heart of Grafenwöhr, this museum features exhibits about the town’s history and its relationship with the US Army.

  • Schloss Thumsenreuth: This elegant castle dates back to the 18th century and is surrounded by beautiful gardens. It’s a perfect spot for a leisurely stroll and offers stunning views of the town and the surrounding countryside.

  • St. Michael’s Church: This historic church dates back to the 15th century and features a beautiful baroque altar.

Natural Attractions of Grafenwöhr: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Felsenkeller Nature Reserve: This reserve is a paradise for hikers and nature lovers. It features a network of trails that wind through forests and meadows, and offers stunning views of rock formations and natural springs along the way.

  • Bayerischer Wald National Park: Located just a short drive from Grafenwöhr, this national park is a nature lover’s paradise. It features dense forests, rugged mountains, and crystal-clear lakes, and is home to a variety of wildlife, including lynx, wolves, and bears.

  • Steinwald Nature Park: This beautiful nature park is located near Grafenwöhr and offers an abundance of recreational opportunities, including hiking, biking, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Grafenwöhr

Grafenwöhr offers several hiking and biking trails that are perfect for exploring the region’s beautiful countryside. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • Fichtelgebirge Bike Trail: This 100-kilometer trail winds through the stunning Fichtelgebirge mountains and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

  • Goldsteig Trail: This long-distance hiking trail runs through the Upper Palatinate region and offers a variety of scenic landscapes and cultural highlights.

  • Fränkischer Gebirgsweg: This hiking trail runs through the Franconian Mountains and offers spectacular views of the region’s forests, meadows, and villages.

Best Things to Do in Grafenwöhr: Activities and Tours

  • US Army Training Area: This is one of the largest military training areas in Europe and features various military exercises and simulations. Visitors can observe the training from designated observation points.

  • Grafenwöhr Adventure Pool: This modern swimming pool complex features several indoor and outdoor pools, waterslides, and a sauna, making it a perfect spot for a family day out.

  • Grafenwöhr Golf Club: This beautiful golf course is located in the heart of Grafenwöhr and is known for its challenging design and picturesque setting.

Week-Long Itinerary for Grafenwöhr

Here is a suggested itinerary for a week-long stay in Grafenwöhr:

  • Day 1: Explore the town and visit the Grafenwöhr Tower Museum and St. Michael’s Church.

  • Day 2: Go hiking in the Felsenkeller Nature Reserve.

  • Day 3: Take a day trip to the Bayerischer Wald National Park.

  • Day 4: Visit the Schloss Thumsenreuth and enjoy a leisurely stroll in the castle gardens.

  • Day 5: Go biking on the Goldsteig Trail.

  • Day 6: Play a round of golf at the Grafenwöhr Golf Club.

  • Day 7: Relax at the Grafenwöhr Adventure Pool.

Food and Drink Scene of Grafenwöhr

Must-Try Dishes

  • Bratwurst: This is a classic German sausage that is especially popular in Bavaria. It’s usually served with sauerkraut and a side of potato salad.

  • Leberknödel: This is a traditional Bavarian dish made from liver dumplings. It’s usually served with a hearty soup or a side of potato salad.

  • Schweinshaxe: This is a roasted pork knuckle that is a popular dish in Bavarian cuisine. It’s served with sauerkraut and boiled potatoes.

Local Beverages

  • Bavarian Beer: Bavaria is known for its excellent beer, and Grafenwöhr is no exception. Some of the most popular local brews include Kölsch, Pilsner, and Hefeweizen.

  • Raspberry Lemonade: This sweet and refreshing drink is a popular summer beverage in Bavaria.

Local Culture and Customs of Grafenwöhr

Grafenwöhr is steeped in Bavarian culture and customs. Some of the most important traditions and customs of the town include:

  • Beer Gardens: Beer gardens are a quintessential part of Bavarian culture, and Grafenwöhr has several excellent beer gardens where visitors can enjoy a cold brew and some traditional Bavarian food.

  • Festivals: Grafenwöhr hosts several festivals throughout the year, including the annual Volksfest, which features live music, traditional food, and carnival rides.
