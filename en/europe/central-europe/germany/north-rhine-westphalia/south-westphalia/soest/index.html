Food and Drink Scene of Soest

Soest has a thriving food and drink scene. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to sample:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pfefferpotthast: A beef stew served with rye bread.
  • Spargel: White asparagus, a seasonal delicacy.
  • Reibekuchen: Potato pancakes served with apple sauce or sour cream.

Local Beverages

  • Soester Felsenkeller: A local beer made with natural spring water.
  • Steinhäger: A gin-like spirit made in nearby Steinhagen.
  • Korn: A traditional German liquor made from wheat or rye.

  • Youth Hostel Soest: An affordable option for backpackers and families.

What to Buy in Soest: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for some unique keepsakes to bring back home, here are some ideas:

  • Soester Puppenmanufaktur: Handmade dolls and puppets, a local tradition.
  • Soester Glas: Beautiful glassware made by local artisans.
  • Soester Originale: Local food products such as mustard, chocolates, and jams.

Local Culture and Customs of Soest

Soest has a rich cultural heritage. Here are some customs and traditions to be aware of:

  • Soester Fehde: A historical feud between two families that is still celebrated every year with a medieval festival.
  • Karneval: A festive season leading up to Lent, with parades and celebrations throughout the city.
  • Kirmes: An annual fair in the city center with rides and attractions.

Best Time to Visit Soest

The best time to visit Soest is in the summer months from May to September when the weather is mild and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy. However, the medieval festivals in August and the Christmas markets in December are also great times to visit.

Do’s and Don’ts of Soest

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your stay in Soest:

Do:

  • Try the local beer and cuisine.
  • Visit the city’s historic landmarks and museums.
  • Attend a medieval festival or other cultural event.

Don’t:

  • Smoke in public spaces or restaurants.
  • Take photographs of people without their permission.
  • Ignore traffic rules when biking or driving.

Safety and Health

Soest is a safe and healthy city for visitors. Here are some tips to stay safe and healthy:

Safety in Soest

  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially at night.
  • Keep your valuables secured or in a safe in your hotel room.
  • Follow traffic rules when biking or driving.

Health in Soest

  • Drink bottled water to avoid any potential health risks from tap water.
  • Bring any necessary medications or prescriptions with you.
  • Be aware of the potential risks of outdoor activities and take necessary precautions.
