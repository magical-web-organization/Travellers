Introduction to Kleve

Kleve is a quaint town located in the Lower Rhine region of North Rhine-Westphalia, in Germany. It is nestled in a picturesque landscape, surrounded by hills, forests, and meadows. The town boasts a rich history and cultural heritage that dates back to the Middle Ages. Its remarkable architecture and museums attract visitors from all over the world, and its natural beauty offers endless opportunities for exploration and adventure. This comprehensive guide will provide you with an overview of the best places to visit, food to try, and tips to keep in mind while exploring Kleve.

List of Places to Visit in Kleve

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kleve is home to several museums and historical landmarks that offer insight into the town’s cultural heritage. The Museum Kurhaus Kleve is a must-visit for art lovers, as it houses an extensive collection of contemporary art. The Moyland Castle, which is located on the outskirts of the town, is also worth a visit. It showcases numerous exhibitions of modern and contemporary art, and its beautiful garden is perfect for a leisurely stroll. Another cultural attraction worth mentioning is the Schwanenburg Castle, which was built in the 14th century and offers breathtaking views of the town and the surrounding landscape.

Natural Attractions of Kleve: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kleve is surrounded by stunning natural scenery that is perfect for outdoor activities and adventures. The Klever Reichswald forest is the largest continuous deciduous forest in the region, and it is ideal for hiking and biking. The Reichswald is also home to several lakes and ponds, which offer the perfect opportunity for fishing and swimming. The Rhenish hill country, which surrounds Kleve, is also worth exploring. Its rolling hills and panoramic views are sure to take your breath away.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kleve

Kleve offers a wide array of hiking and biking routes that are perfect for nature enthusiasts. The Klever Reichswald forest has several marked trails for hiking and cycling, ranging from easy to challenging. Another popular route is the RuhrtalRadweg, which is a scenic bike trail that follows the Ruhr River. It stretches for 230 kilometers and offers stunning views of the river and the surrounding countryside. There are also several guided tours and bike rental options available in Kleve.

Best Things to Do in Kleve: Activities and Tours

There is no shortage of things to do in Kleve. The town offers several activities and tours that cater to all ages and interests. For history buffs, there is the Kleve Town Museum, which showcases the history of Kleve and the region. The town also offers several guided tours, such as the wine tasting tour and the cultural heritage tour. For adventure seekers, there are several outdoor activities available, such as zip-lining, kayaking, and rock climbing.

Week-Long Itinerary for Kleve

If you plan on spending a week in Kleve, here is a suggested itinerary:

  • Day 1: Explore the town center and visit the Museum Kurhaus Kleve.
  • Day 2: Visit the Moyland Castle and have a picnic in the castle garden.
  • Day 3: Hike or bike through the Klever Reichswald forest.
  • Day 4: Take the cultural heritage tour of the town.
  • Day 5: Visit the Schwanenburg Castle and enjoy the panoramic views.
  • Day 6: Go on the wine tasting tour and sample the local wines.
  • Day 7: Indulge in outdoor activities such as kayaking or zip-lining.

Food and Drink Scene of Kleve

Must-Try Dishes

Kleve offers several traditional dishes that are worth trying. One such dish is Himmel un Ääd, which is a combination of mashed potatoes and apples, served with fried blood sausage or bacon. Another local favorite is Reibekuchen, which is a type of potato pancake served with apple sauce. For dessert, try the Apfelkuchen, which is a delicious apple cake made with fresh apples.

Local Beverages

Kleve is known for its exceptional wines and beers. The town offers several wine tasting tours, which allow visitors to sample the local wines. There are also several breweries in the area that offer tours and tastings. The most popular local beer is the König Pilsener, which is a refreshing, light beer brewed in the nearby town of Duisburg.

Where to Stay in Kleve: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kleve offers several accommodation options, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. The Hotel Schwanenburg is a popular choice for those looking for a luxurious experience. It is located in an 18th-century castle, and its rooms offer stunning views of the town and the surrounding landscape. For those on a budget, the Hostel Am Park is a great option. It is located in a scenic park and offers dorm rooms and private rooms at affordable rates.

What to Buy in Kleve: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kleve offers several souvenirs and local products that are perfect for bringing back home. The town is known for its delicious apple products, such as apple juice, apple cider, and apple brandy. You can also find handmade pottery, wooden carvings, and traditional clothing at the local markets.

Local Culture and Customs of Kleve

Kleve has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its architecture, museums, and traditions. The locals are known for their hospitality and friendliness, and visitors can expect to be welcomed with open arms. Traditional customs, such as the Klever Kirmes and the Rhineland Carnival, are still celebrated in the town.

Best Time to Visit Kleve

The best time to visit Kleve is during the summer months, from June to August. The weather is pleasant, and there are several outdoor activities and festivals to enjoy. The town is also beautiful during the fall, when the leaves change colors and the surrounding landscape is bathed in warm hues of red and gold.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kleve

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Dress appropriately, especially when visiting churches and museums.
  • Try the local food and drink.
  • Take the time to explore the natural beauty of the town.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage public property.
  • Make loud noise or disturb the peace.
  • Ignore traffic rules when cycling or driving.
  • Enter restricted areas without permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kleve

Kleve is generally a safe town, and there is little to no violent crime. However, visitors should still exercise caution when walking alone at night, especially in secluded areas. It is also important to keep an eye on personal belongings, such as wallets and purses, as pickpocketing can occur.

Health in Kleve

Kleve has a well-developed healthcare system, and there are several clinics and hospitals in the town. It is recommended that visitors have comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies.

Getting to Kleve: Transportation Options and Logistics

Kleve is easy to reach, thanks to its well-connected transportation system. The nearest airports are the Düsseldorf International Airport and the Weeze Airport, both of which are approximately an hour’s drive away. There are also several bus stations and train stations in the town, with services provided by Deutsche Bahn and local bus companies.

How to Get Around in Kleve

Kleve is a small town, and most attractions are within walking distance. To explore the surrounding countryside and forest, visitors can rent bikes or cars from local rental companies. Public transportation, such as buses and trains, is also available and is a convenient option for those traveling longer distances.

Where to Go Next from Kleve: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Kleve is located in a prime location for those looking to explore the surrounding region. The nearby towns of Kalkar and Emmerich offer their own unique charm and attractions. Visitors can also explore the nearby Rhenish hill country, which offers stunning views and endless opportunities for outdoor activities.

Conclusion

Kleve is a town that offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. Its museums, landmarks, and outdoor activities make it an ideal destination for travelers looking for an off-the-beaten-path experience. With this comprehensive guide, you will be able to make the most of your time in Kleve and discover all that this charming town has to offer.
