Upper Lusatia is a hidden gem located in Saxony, Central Europe. With its natural beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture, it is a must-visit destination in Germany. This comprehensive guide will help you plan your trip to Upper Lusatia, covering everything from the best things to do, places to visit, and local customs to follow.

Introduction to Upper Lusatia

Upper Lusatia is an area that covers the eastern part of the German state of Saxony, bordering Poland and the Czech Republic. It is known for its stunning landscapes, diverse cultural heritage, and exceptional outdoor activities.

List of Places to Visit in Upper Lusatia

Here are some must-visit places in Upper Lusatia:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Bautzen Memorial Museum
  • Zittau Town Hall Tower Museum
  • Grossschönau City Museum
  • Lusatian Museum in Görlitz

Natural Attractions of Upper Lusatia: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Saxon Switzerland National Park

Best Things to Do in Upper Lusatia: Activities and Tours

From cultural experiences to outdoor activities, here are some of the top things to do in Upper Lusatia:

  • Take a guided city tour of Görlitz
  • Hike through the Saxon Switzerland National Park
  • Go on a boat trip on Lake Olbersdorf
  • Explore the historic town of Bautzen

Local Culture and Customs of Upper Lusatia

The culture of Upper Lusatia is deeply rooted in its history and traditions. Here are some local customs and cultural practices to keep in mind:

  • Respect the local cuisine and try to taste as much as you can
  • Say “Guten Tag” (Good day) and greet people
  • Dress accordingly while visiting religious sites
  • Learn about the local customs and history

Best Time to Visit Upper Lusatia

The best time to visit Upper Lusatia is between May and September, when the weather is warm and pleasant. This is also the peak season for tourism in the area.

Do’s and Don’ts of Upper Lusatia

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Explore the great outdoors

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural surroundings
  • Don’t take pictures in religious places without permission
  • Don’t be loud and disruptive in public places

Safety and Health

Safety in Upper Lusatia

Upper Lusatia is a safe destination, but like any other place, it’s important to take appropriate precautions. Here are some safety tips:

  • Be aware of pickpockets
  • Leave valuables in your hotel’s safe
  • Avoid walking alone in poorly lit areas

Health in Upper Lusatia

Upper Lusatia has a good healthcare system, and hospitals and clinics are readily available. However, it’s always a good idea to have travel insurance and keep important documents with you.
