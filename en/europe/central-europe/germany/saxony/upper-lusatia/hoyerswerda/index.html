Planning a trip to Hoyerswerda? Located in Upper Lusatia, in Saxony, Germany, Hoyerswerda is a vibrant town that offers a mix of culture, history, nature, and adventure. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll explore the best things to do, see, eat and buy in Hoyerswerda.

Introduction to Hoyerswerda

Hoyerswerda lies in the heart of Central Europe and is one of the largest towns in Upper Lusatia. It has a long and rich history dating back to the 13th century. The town was devastated during World War II but went through a period of rapid growth during the GDR period. Today, Hoyerswerda is a bustling town with plenty of things to see and do.

List of Places to Visit in Hoyerswerda

Here are some of the top places to visit in Hoyerswerda:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

1. Saint Peter and Paul Church: This stunning 13th-century church is one of the oldest buildings in Hoyerswerda and features gorgeous Baroque architecture.

2. City Museum: If you want to learn more about the history of Hoyerswerda, then head over to the City Museum. Here you’ll find exhibits and artifacts that highlight the town’s rich cultural heritage.

3. Hoyerswerda Castle: This beautiful Renaissance-style castle dates back to the 16th century and is home to the town’s administrative offices.

Natural Attractions of Hoyerswerda: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

1. Knappensee Lake: This beautiful lake is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts who love hiking, swimming, fishing or boating. It’s also a great spot to take a picnic lunch and enjoy the stunning scenery.

2. Spreewald Biosphere Reserve: Located just a short drive from Hoyerswerda, the Spreewald Biosphere Reserve is a protected area that’s home to a unique ecosystem of plants and animals. It’s the perfect spot for nature lovers and hikers.

3. Lausitzer Seenland: This man-made lake district is one of the most popular tourist destinations in the region and features over 20 lakes that are perfect for swimming, boating, and sunbathing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hoyerswerda

Hoyerswerda and the surrounding area is a hiker’s and biker’s paradise. Some of the best routes include:

1. Black Elster Cycle Path: This popular bike path follows the Black Elster River and takes you through some of the most beautiful scenery in the region.

2. World Heritage Bike Path: If you want to explore the cultural heritage sites of Upper Lusatia, then the World Heritage Bike Path is the perfect way to do it.

3. Lausitzer Seenland Route: This Fits the nature lovers and takes you along the lakes in the Lausitzer Seenland.

Best Things to Do in Hoyerswerda: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of fun activities and tours to enjoy in Hoyerswerda, such as:

1. Hoyerswerda Zoo: This is a great place to take the kids and see over 500 animals from all over the world.

2. Hoyerswerda Castle Tour: Join a guided tour of the Hoyerswerda Castle to learn about the castle’s history and architecture.

3. Ballooning Adventure: Take a hot air balloon ride above the stunning landscape of Hoyerswerda for an unforgettable experience.

Food and Drink Scene of Hoyerswerda

Hoyerswerda has a vibrant food and drink scene, and there are plenty of local dishes and beverages to try.

Must-Try Dishes

1. Sour milk soup: This delicious soup is a local specialty and is made with kefir, sausage, and potatoes.

2. Quarkkeulchen: These delicious fried pancakes are made with quark (a type of cheese), flour, and eggs.

3. Lausitzer Sauerbraten: This hearty dish is made with beef that’s been marinated in vinegar and spices and is often served with red cabbage and potatoes.

Local Beverages

1. Radeberger Beer: This refreshing beer is a popular favorite in Hoyerswerda and is brewed just a short distance away in Radeberg.

2. Apfelwein: This sweet apple wine is a popular local drink and is often served during festivals and celebrations.

3. Saxonian Wine: The wine produced in the nearby Saxonian vineyards is some of the best in the region and is a must-try.

Where to Stay in Hoyerswerda: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of places to stay in Hoyerswerda, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Some of the best options include:

1. Hotel Zur Mühle: This charming hotel is located in the heart of Hoyerswerda’s Old Town and features comfortable rooms at affordable prices.

2. Hotel Schloss Hoyerswerda: This luxurious hotel is located in the stunning Hoyerswerda Castle and features elegant rooms, an on-site spa, and fine dining.

3. Gasthof Lückersdorf: This cozy guesthouse is located just a short drive from Hoyerswerda and features comfortable rooms and delicious local cuisine.

What to Buy in Hoyerswerda: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some popular souvenirs and local products to bring home:

1. Pottery: Hoyerswerda is known for its traditional pottery, which is handmade and painted by local artisans.

2. Lace: Upper Lusatia is famous for its fine lacework, and you can find a range of lace products in Hoyerswerda.

3. Wine: The wine produced in the Saxonian vineyards near Hoyerswerda makes an excellent gift for wine lovers.

Local Culture and Customs of Hoyerswerda

The local culture in Hoyerswerda is influenced by its long and rich history. The people are friendly and welcoming, and you’ll find a range of festivals and celebrations throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Hoyerswerda

The best time to visit Hoyerswerda is between the months of May and September when the weather is mild, and many of the outdoor attractions are open.

Safety and Health

Safety in Hoyerswerda

Hoyerswerda is a safe place to visit, and the crime rate is low. However, like in any other town, you should take normal precautions to keep yourself and your belongings safe.

Health in Hoyerswerda

The medical facilities in Hoyerswerda are good, and there are several hospitals and clinics in the town. The tap water is safe to drink, but bottled water is readily available if you prefer.

Getting to Hoyerswerda: Transportation Options and Logistics

Hoyerswerda is well-connected by road and rail, and there are several options for getting there.

Airports Near Hoyerswerda

The closest airport to Hoyerswerda is Dresden Airport, which is located about 50 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Hoyerswerda

There are several bus stations in and around Hoyerswerda, and you can find connections to most major cities in Germany.

Other Options

You can also reach Hoyerswerda by train or car. The town is located near several major highways, and the train station is well-connected to other major cities in Germany.

How to Get Around in Hoyerswerda

Hoyerswerda is a compact town, and most of the attractions are located within walking distance of each other. However, if you prefer to drive, there are plenty of rental car companies in town.
