Introduction to Aue

Nestled in the Saxon Ore Mountains of Saxony, Germany, Aue is a small but beautiful town with a rich history and culture. The town is known for its stunning natural beauty, scenic hiking trails, and charming architecture. Aue is ideal for a relaxing holiday, and it attracts a diverse range of visitors throughout the year, from couples seeking a romantic getaway to families looking for an adventure.

List of Places to Visit in Aue

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Aue is home to several cultural attractions that are worth visiting. The Auerhammer Folklore Museum is one of the most popular museums in the town. The museum showcases the history and culture of the region, with exhibits on traditional crafts, farming practices, and folk art.

Another must-visit attraction is the St. Nicholas Church, which dates back to the 16th century. The church is an architectural masterpiece, with stunning stained glass windows and intricate carvings. Visitors can also climb the church tower for panoramic views of the town and surrounding countryside.

Natural Attractions of Aue: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Aue is renowned for its stunning natural landscapes and scenic views. The Ore Mountains offer an abundance of outdoor activities, from hiking and biking to skiing and snowboarding. The Fichtelberg Cable Car takes visitors to the top of Fichtelberg Mountain, the highest peak in the range, with breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

For a more relaxed outdoor activity, visitors can stroll through the Kulturpark Deutzenwald, a beautiful park with walking trails, a lake, and a playground for children. The park is ideal for a family picnic or a quiet afternoon in nature.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Aue

Aue has some of the most beautiful and scenic hiking and biking trails in the Ore Mountains. The Kammweg trail runs along the ridge of the mountains, offering stunning views of the surrounding countryside. The trail is easy to navigate and is suitable for both experienced and novice hikers.

Another popular hiking trail is the Auer Hammerschmiede-Rundwanderweg, which is a circular route that starts and finishes at the Auerhammer Folklore Museum. The trail passes through beautiful forests, meadows, and quaint villages, offering visitors a glimpse of the stunning local landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Aue: Activities and Tours

Besides hiking and biking, Aue offers visitors a wide range of other activities and tours. For a unique experience, visitors can book a guided tour of the nickel mine at Friedenshutte. The mine is a fascinating glimpse into the region’s mining history, with tunnels, shafts, and passages that date back to the 17th century.

For a more relaxing activity, visitors can visit the Badegarten Eibenstock, a thermal spa with outdoor and indoor pools, saunas, and various wellness treatments. The spa is ideal for a romantic retreat or a weekend getaway with friends.

Week-Long Itinerary for Aue

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Aue, here’s an itinerary that will allow you to make the most of your stay:

  • Day 1: Explore Aue town and visit the St. Nicholas Church
  • Day 2: Take a scenic hike along the Kammweg trail and enjoy panoramic views of the mountains
  • Day 3: Book a guided tour of the Friedenshutte nickel mine
  • Day 4: Experience the thermal spa at Badegarten Eibenstock
  • Day 5: Visit the Auerhammer Folklore Museum and learn about local history and culture
  • Day 6: Take a cable car ride to the top of Fichtelberg Mountain and admire the stunning views
  • Day 7: Relax in the Kulturpark Deutzenwald and enjoy the beautiful natural surroundings

Food and Drink Scene of Aue

Must-Try Dishes

Aue is known for its hearty and delicious cuisine, with traditional dishes that are both filling and flavorful. One of the most popular dishes is the Erbswurstsuppe, a pea soup with smoked pork sausages, potatoes, and vegetables. Another must-try dish is the Sauerbraten, a marinated beef roast served with red cabbage and potato dumplings.

Local Beverages

Beer is the beverage of choice in Aue, with several local breweries producing their own signature brews. The Goldner Löwe brewery is one of the most popular in the region, with a wide range of beers that are famous for their rich flavor and quality.

Where to Stay in Aue: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Aue offers visitors a wide range of accommodation options, from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels and guesthouses. The Hotel am Fichtelberg is a popular choice for visitors, with comfortable rooms, a spa, and easy access to the Fichtelberg Cable Car.

Another excellent option is the Pension Haus Madonna, a cozy guesthouse with comfortable rooms, a garden, and a terrace with stunning views of the mountains.

What to Buy in Aue: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Aue is famous for its traditional crafts and local products, which make great souvenirs or gifts for loved ones back home. Visitors can buy handcrafted wooden toys, traditional Christmas ornaments, and locally produced honey and jams.

Local Culture and Customs of Aue

The culture and customs of Aue are deeply rooted in its mining history and rich traditions. Visitors should be sure to attend one of the many local festivals and celebrations that take place throughout the year, such as the Auer Schacht Fest or the Bergparade.

Best Time to Visit Aue

Aue is beautiful all year round, with each season offering its unique charm and attractions. The summer months are ideal for outdoor activities such as hiking and biking, while the winter months are perfect for skiing and snowboarding.

Safety and Health

Safety in Aue

Aue is a safe and welcoming town, with a low crime rate and friendly locals. Visitors should take standard safety precautions, such as keeping an eye on their belongings and avoiding unlit areas at night.

Health in Aue

Aue has excellent healthcare facilities, with several hospitals and clinics in the surrounding area. Visitors who require medical attention should contact their embassy or travel insurance provider for assistance.
