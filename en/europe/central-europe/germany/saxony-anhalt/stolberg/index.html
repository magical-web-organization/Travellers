Stolberg, located in Saxony-Anhalt, in Germany, is a hidden gem in Central Europe. This picturesque town is known for its rich history, charming architecture, scenic landscapes, and outdoor adventures. Whether you’re an avid hiker, history buff, or foodie, Stolberg has something to offer everyone. In this guide, we’ll take you on a journey through the must-see attractions, best things to do, and practical travel tips to make the most of your trip to Stolberg.

Introduction to Stolberg

Stolberg is a small and scenic town in the Harz Mountains, surrounded by lush forests, rugged mountains, and scenic valleys. The town dates back to the 10th century and has a rich history that is reflected in its charming architecture and cultural attractions. Despite its small size, Stolberg is home to a vibrant arts and culture scene, with a range of events and festivals throughout the year. From its historic town center to its stunning natural landscapes, Stolberg is a must-visit destination in Germany.

List of Places to Visit in Stolberg

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Stolberg is home to many culturally significant attractions that showcase the town’s rich history and heritage. These must-visit places include:

  • Stolberg Castle: Built in the 12th century, the Stolberg Castle is the town’s most iconic landmark. The castle has been well-preserved over the years and now houses a museum dedicated to the town’s history and culture.

  • Altstadt Stolberg: Located in the heart of the town, Altstadt Stolberg also known as the “Old Village” is one of the most preserved traditional villages in Germany. Its narrow cobbled streets, half-timbered buildings, and historical architecture take you back in time.

  • St. Martini Church: This historic church stands out for its Gothic architecture and stunning stained-glass windows. It is the perfect place to take a moment of peace and find inner serenity.

Natural Attractions of Stolberg: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Nature lovers will find plenty of things to do in Stolberg. With its scenic vistas and scenic trails, Stolberg is a paradise for hikers, cyclists, and outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the top attractions:

  • Harz National Park: One of Germany’s most stunning natural jewels, Harz National Park, is located just outside of Stolberg. The park boasts of diverse flora, fauna, and one of the best places for hiking.

  • Rappbode-Talsperre Dam: This dam offers some of the most breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside. You can catch your favorite outdoor sports such as mountain biking, hiking, and rock climbing from this spot.

  • Teufelsmauer: The Teufelsmauer, or the Devil’s Wall, is a series of striking rock formations stretching for miles across the Harz Mountains. It’s a popular hiking trail, and you can capture some incredible landscapes from its’ high altitude vantage point.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Stolberg

A travel guide to Stolberg would never be complete without mentioning the best hiking/biking trails in the region. Here are some of the most scenic and popular trails in and around Stolberg:

  • Harzer Hexenstieg: Spanning over 100 km, the Harzer Hexenstieg, or the Witch’s Trail, is the most famous hiking route in the Harz Mountains. It allows hikers to trek through different trails with diverse landscapes.

  • Stolberger Harzklubhütte: A charming hiking route that starts from the Stolberger Golf Club and takes you up through the mountains and down into the valley with a picturesque scenic view of the region.

  • Rappbode Dam Loop: This easy and circular trail is a favorite among walkers and mountain bikers alike. The Rappbode Loop begins at the eastern section of the dam and can give you a panoramic view of the town.

Best Things to Do in Stolberg: Activities and Tours

Stolberg may be small, but there’s still plenty to do. From cultural tours with a guide to exploring the region’s natural beauty, here are some of the best things to do in Stolberg:

  • Stolberg Segway Tour: Take a Segway tour through the narrow lanes of the town and learn more about the 950 year old rich history and culture of Stolberg.

  • Explore Rappbode Dam Tour: Get an exciting behind-the-scenes tour of Rappbode Dam, one of the most impressive engineering marvels of Germany.

  • Schwabstedter Lake Tour: Take a cruise on the beautiful Rappbode Reservoir and Schwabstedter Lake to appreciate the serene beauty of the region.

Food and Drink Scene of Stolberg

Stolberg has a rich culinary heritage that speaks to the town’s culture and history. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages of the region:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Plumpskloß: This traditional potato dumpling is a staple of the cuisine in Stolberg and is usually served with roast pork, red cabbage, and gravy.

  • Harzer Käse: A type of cheese that is unique to the Harz region, Harzer Käse is a delicious and healthy treat.

  • Rote Grütze: A sweet dessert, Rote Grütze, is a mix of red berries, raspberries, and currants cooked and served with whipped cream.

Local Beverages

  • Stolberger Mineralwasser: The town’s natural mineral water is among the best in Germany.

  • Harzer Schnaps: Locally produced schnaps, from a pure grain or fruit base, is a potent and delicious drink.

Where to Stay in Stolberg: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Stolberg has a range of accommodation options, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses, to cater to different types of travelers. Here are some of the best options:

  • Altenburgblick: This beautiful guesthouse offers comfortable and welcoming accommodation, and splendid views of the town.

  • Altes Forsthaus: Stay amidst nature and scenic beauty by checking into the Altes Forsthaus, located within the Harz National Park, away from the hustle-bustle of the town.

  • Ratshotel: Located in the heart of the town, Ratshotel is cosy and comfortable with delicious food and assistance with planning tours and activities in the region.

What to Buy in Stolberg: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Stolberg offers a range of souvenirs and local products that will remind you of your trip to the region. Here are some of the best things to buy in Stolberg:

  • Harzer-Baumkuchen: This traditional German cake, made of layers of cake batter, is a specialty of Stolberg. Buy this cake and give a taste of Germany to your loved ones back home.

  • Local honey: Stolberg is well-known for its delicious honey, which is produced by local bees. It comes in different varieties such as forest honey, acacia honey, and flower honey.

Local Culture and Customs of Stolberg

The residents of Stolberg are friendly and welcoming, and visitors are encouraged to show respect for local customs and traditions. It is customary to greet locals with a friendly “Guten Tag” or “Guten Morgen,” show reverence in churches and religious places, and admire the historic architecture with care.

Best Time to Visit Stolberg

The best time to visit Stolberg depends on your preference. The town is beautiful year-round, but outdoor activities are more enjoyable during spring and summer. However, the onset of Autumn brings in the spectacular fall colors, making it a perfect time for hikers and photographers.

Do’s and Don’ts of Stolberg

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine and beverages of the region.
  • Do explore the outdoors and go hiking or biking.
  • Do show respect for local traditions and customs.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or cause damage to the environment.
  • Don’t smoke in public places unless it’s a designated area.
  • Don’t forget to dress appropriately for cultural activities and places.

Safety and Health

Stolberg is an extremely safe town as compared to other cities of Germany. However, it is common sense to take precautions such as keeping a watchful eye on their belongings in crowded places, being mindful of traffic when cycling, and staying aware of their surroundings when hiking on the trails.

Health in Stolberg

Stolberg has all the basic medical facilities for minor medical emergencies. In case of serious medical issues, you can visit the nearest hospital or clinic.

Getting to Stolberg: Transportation Options and Logistics

Getting to Stolberg is relatively easy given its location. Here are some of the best transport options you can choose from:

Airports Near Stolberg

The nearest airports to Stolberg are Hannover Airport (136 km), Leipzig/Halle Airport (106 km), and Berlin Tegel International Airport ( 224km).

Bus Stations Near Stolberg

Stolberg has its bus stop, which is a part of the network of public buses running through Central Europe. Here are two of the closest bus stations:

  • Stolberg-Harzstraße Station: Located in the heart of the city, Stolberg-Harzstraße Station is the main bus depot in the town.

  • Stolberg Stadtmitte Station: This station is more suited for travelers looking for a way to travel directly to the city center.

Other Options

Train-travel is also another convenient mode of transportation to reach Stolberg. Wernigerode, located 30km away, is the nearest train station, served by regular, direct trains from many cities in Germany.

How to Get Around in Stolberg

Stolberg is a small town easily explored by foot, but it might be tiring for some. The town is well-connected with a network of public transportation that is efficient and reliable. Taxis and rental car services are also readily available if you want to explore the region on your terms.

Where to Go Next from Stolberg: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There is no shortage of nearby destinations to explore around Stolberg, and here are some of the best:

  • Quedlinburg: A charming medieval town filled with half-timbered buildings, Quedlinburg is located just an hour’s drive from Stolberg.

  • Goslar: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Goslar is a historic mining town set in the Harz Mountains, which offers a wealth of tourist attractions to explore.

Conclusion

Stolberg is a hidden gem in Germany that offers a perfect blend of culture, history, and outdoor adventures. From its historic landmarks to its natural beauty, there is always something to do and see in Stolberg. So pack your bags and get ready for an unforgettable trip to this incredible town!
