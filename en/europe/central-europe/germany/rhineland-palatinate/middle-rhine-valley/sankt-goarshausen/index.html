Introduction to Sankt Goarshausen

Located in the Middle Rhine Valley of Rhineland-Palatinate in Germany, Sankt Goarshausen is a picturesque town that offers visitors a blend of culture and natural beauty. It is situated on the banks of the Rhine River, with the towering Loreley rock formation overlooking the town. Sankt Goarshausen is known for its historic castle, Loreley-Plateau, outdoor activities, and charming streets. Whether you’re a history buff, nature lover, or just looking for a relaxing getaway, Sankt Goarshausen has something to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Sankt Goarshausen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Burg Katz (Cat Castle)

Burg Katz is a medieval castle located on a hill overlooking Sankt Goarshausen. Built in the late 14th century, it was destroyed and rebuilt several times over the centuries. Today, it is a popular tourist attraction with a museum that showcases the castle’s history. Visitors to Burg Katz can enjoy sweeping views of the Rhine River and the surrounding countryside.

Loreley-Plateau

The Loreley-Plateau offers stunning views of the Rhine River and the surrounding area. According to ancient legends, a beautiful siren named Loreley once sat on the plateau, luring sailors to their doom with her enchanting voice. Today, visitors can hike to the top of the plateau to take in the breathtaking views.

Museum der Stadt Sankt Goarshausen

The Museum der Stadt Sankt Goarshausen is housed in a 17th-century building and showcases the history of the town and its surroundings. The museum features exhibits that focus on the area’s geology, flora and fauna, and the history of the Rhine River. It also has a display of local art and artifacts from the Middle Ages.

Natural Attractions of Sankt Goarshausen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Rheinfels Castle

Rheinfels Castle is a majestic fortress that sits atop a hill overlooking Sankt Goarshausen and the Rhine River. Built in the late 13th century, it was once the largest fortress in the Middle Rhine Valley. Today, visitors can explore the castle’s ruins and learn about its history in the onsite museum.

Loreleyfelsen

The Loreleyfelsen is a cliff located on the eastern bank of the Rhine River. It is said to be the spot where the siren Loreley sat and sang, luring sailors to their death. Today, visitors can hike to the top of the cliff for stunning views of the river and the surrounding area.

Taunus Mountains

The Taunus Mountains are located just a short distance from Sankt Goarshausen and offer visitors a chance to experience the beauty of the German countryside. Visitors can hike or bike through the scenic trails, explore the charming towns in the area, or simply take in the natural beauty of the region.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sankt Goarshausen

Rheinsteig Trail

The Rheinsteig Trail is a long-distance hiking trail that runs along the Rhine River. It offers stunning views of the river and the surrounding countryside. The trail passes through Sankt Goarshausen, offering visitors an opportunity to explore the town and its attractions.

Bopparder Hamm Vineyards

The Bopparder Hamm Vineyards are located along the Rhine River and offer visitors a chance to hike through the beautiful vineyards. The trail runs for approximately 4.3 km and offers stunning views of the river and the surrounding countryside.

Hunsrück Mountains

The Hunsrück Mountains are located just a short distance from Sankt Goarshausen and offer visitors a chance to hike through the picturesque countryside. The trails pass through quaint villages, scenic valleys, and stunning forests.

Best Things to Do in Sankt Goarshausen: Activities and Tours

Boat Tour on the Rhine

A boat tour on the Rhine River is a must-do activity while in Sankt Goarshausen. Visitors can take a leisurely cruise down the river and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding scenery. The tours typically include a guide who provides information on the history and points of interest along the Rhine River.

Wine Tasting

The Middle Rhine Valley is known for its wine production, and visitors to Sankt Goarshausen can enjoy a wine tasting session in one of the local vineyards. Wine tastings typically include a selection of wines and are accompanied by local cheeses and other snacks.

Explore the Local Museums

Sankt Goarshausen has a number of local museums that showcase the history and culture of the town and its surroundings. Visitors can explore the Museum der Stadt Sankt Goarshausen, which is dedicated to the town’s history, or the Burg Katz, which offers views of the Rhine River and provides insights into medieval castle life.

Food and Drink Scene of Sankt Goarshausen

Must-Try Dishes

Riesling Spätlese

The Middle Rhine Valley is famous for its wine production, and the Riesling Spätlese is a popular local wine. It is a fruity and slightly sweet wine that pairs well with local seafood and other dishes.

Rheinischer Sauerbraten

Rheinischer Sauerbraten is a traditional dish that is made with marinated beef, spices, and vinegar, which is then served with a variety of sides such as bread dumplings, red cabbage, and potato dumplings.

Local Beverages

Kölsch Beer

Kölsch beer is a refreshing beer that hails from Cologne. It is a light beer that pairs well with local cuisine and is a popular drink in the Middle Rhine Valley.

Apfelwein

Apfelwein is a traditional German cider that is made from apples. It is a slightly tart and refreshing drink that is popular in the region.

Local Culture and Customs of Sankt Goarshausen

The local culture and customs of Sankt Goarshausen are deeply rooted in its history and traditions. The town celebrates a number of festivals throughout the year, including the Rhine in Flames Festival, which takes place in September and features fireworks and other events along the Rhine River.
