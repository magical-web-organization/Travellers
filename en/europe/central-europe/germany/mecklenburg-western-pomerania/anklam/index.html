Introduction to Anklam

Anklam is a charming small town located in the state of Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania in Germany, Europe. Known for its idyllic location on the stunning Peene River, Anklam offers visitors a unique blend of beautiful natural landscapes and rich cultural heritage. With its picturesque Old Town, historic architecture, and nearby outdoor adventures, Anklam is the perfect destination for those seeking a more authentic and off-the-beaten-track experience in Germany. In this guide, we will showcase all the must-visit places, things to do, where to stay, and information on how to get there.

List of Places to Visit in Anklam

Anklam is not only about its historic buildings but also the numerous sights worth seeing. Some places worth visiting include:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Otto Lilienthal Museum
  • Anklam Town Hall
  • the St. Mary’s Church
  • the Anklamer Stadtmauer
  • the Theater am Steg

Natural Attractions of Anklam: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Peene River
  • Lieper Eck Nature Reserve
  • Stolper Turm
  • Wenningsstedter Mountains
  • the Baltic Sea is just an hour away

Hiking/Biking Routes of Anklam

Anklam offers some fantastic hiking and biking routes for those who love to explore the outdoors. Some of the best routes to consider include:

  • Lieperpass-trail (5 km)
  • Peenestrom Cycle Route (28 km)
  • Insel-Usedom-Tour (100 km)

Best Things to Do in Anklam: Activities and Tours

Anklam has an array of activities to fulfill your adventurous spirit, some of which are:

  • Fishing Trip on the Peene River

  • Segway Tour

Food and Drink Scene of Anklam

Anklam offers a range of traditional German cuisine, with a few local specialities too. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fischsuppe: A fish soup with potatoes, onions, and carrots
  • Königsberger Klopse: meatballs in white sauce with capers, served with potatoes and beetroot
  • Sauerfleisch: marinated pork served with pickled cucumber, onions, and bread

Local Beverages

  • Usedomer Brauhaus beer
  • Swedish cider
  • Local German wines

Local Culture and Customs of Anklam

Anklam follows the German way of life and has rich cultural heritage. Locals are friendly and hospitable towards visitors, and the town is known for its community events and festivals.

Best Time to Visit Anklam

The best time to visit Anklam is during the summer months from June to September when the weather is mild, and outdoor activities are in full swing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Anklam

Here are some Do’s and Don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Anklam:

Do:

  • Carry cash, especially when shopping locally
  • Respect the customs and traditions of the locals
  • Try to speak some German, locals appreciate it

Don’t:

  • Litter the streets or natural attractions
  • Walk on the cycle paths or bike lanes

Safety and Health

Safety in Anklam

Anklam is a safe town to explore. The locals are hospitable and welcoming towards visitors. However, It is important to take standard safety precautions, such as locking your car and not leaving your belongings unattended.

Health in Anklam

Anklam offers standard health facilities, and in case of emergencies, visitors can seek medical attention at the nearby hospitals and clinics.

Getting to Anklam: Transportation Options and Logistics

Anklam can be reached by various transportation options, including:

Airports Near Anklam

  • Rostock-Laage Airport (86km)
  • Heringsdorf Airport (21km)

Bus Stations Near Anklam

  • Anklam Bahnhof (1 km)
  • Neubrandenburg Bahnhof (29km)

Other Options

  • Train from Berlin to Anklam Bahnhof through Frankfurt/Oder

How to Get Around in Anklam

Anklam can be easily explored by walking or cycling, but visitors can also use public transport, including buses and taxis, to get around the town and nearby areas.

Where to Go Next from Anklam: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Anklam provides an excellent base to explore some of the fantastic destinations and excursions nearby. Some recommended places to visit include:

  • The town of Ueckermünde on the Baltic coast, which is home to an animal park and a botanical garden.
  • The village of Rossin, which offers activities such as horse riding, quad biking etc.
  • The medieval town of Demmin, which has historic landmarks and scenic natural attractions.

Conclusion

Anklam is a small town packing a big punch, with a rich cultural heritage and stunning natural scenery making it a must-see destination.
