Putbus is a charming town located in the island of Rügen, Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, in Germany. Introduction to Putbus

Putbus is a small town located on the southeastern coast of Rügen, the largest island in Germany. The town was founded in the early 19th century by Prince Wilhelm Malte I of Putbus, who was inspired by the architecture of ancient Greece and Rome. As a result, Putbus is known for its classical-inspired buildings, beautiful parks, and lush gardens.

The town is located in a scenic area surrounded by forests, lakes, and the Baltic Sea, making it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. The town has a rich cultural heritage and a vibrant local culture, which is reflected in its museums, galleries, festivals, and traditional cuisine.

List of Places to Visit in Putbus

Putbus is full of exciting places to visit, from historic landmarks to natural wonders. Here are some of the best places to visit in Putbus:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Putbus Palace: This stunning neoclassical palace was built in the early 19th century and is now home to the Rügen Museum and the Theatre Putbus. The palace is surrounded by beautiful gardens and parks, making it a great place to explore on a sunny day.
  • Orangerie: This beautiful building was originally used to house plants during the winter months, but now serves as an exhibition space for art and cultural events.
  • Lighthouse Putbus: This lighthouse is located on the outskirts of the town and offers stunning views of the Baltic Sea. Visitors can climb to the top of the lighthouse for a panoramic view of the surrounding area.
  • Market Square: This is the heart of Putbus and is home to the town hall, a beautiful fountain, and several cafes and shops. There are also regular markets and festivals held here throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of Putbus: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Island of Vilm: This uninhabited island is located off the coast of Putbus and is home to a nature reserve. Visitors can explore the island on foot or by bike and enjoy the scenic views of the Baltic Sea and the surrounding islands.
  • Granitz Hunting Lodge: This historic lodge is located on the Granitz Forest and offers visitors a chance to explore the forest and enjoy stunning views of Rügen.
  • Jurassic Park Rügen: This outdoor attraction allows visitors to explore a prehistoric park with life-size models of dinosaurs that move and roar. There are also various activities for kids and families.
  • Jasmund National Park: This national park is located on the northern coast of Rügen and is famous for its stunning white chalk cliffs, dense beech forests, and lush meadows. There are several hiking trails and viewpoints in the park that offer breathtaking views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Putbus

Putbus is surrounded by beautiful forests, lakes, and beaches, making it a perfect destination for hikers and bikers. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • Rügen Coastal Trail: This trail covers 600km of the Rügen coastline and offers stunning views of the Baltic Sea and the surrounding islands. There are several access points to the trail, including Putbus.
  • Granitz Forest Trail: This trail takes visitors through the lush forests of the Granitz Forest, near the Granitz Hunting Lodge. The trail offers stunning views of Rügen and the surrounding area.
  • Jasmund National Park Trails: There are several hiking trails in Jasmund National Park, ranging from easy to difficult. The trails take visitors through dense beech forests, lush meadows, and to stunning cliff viewpoints.

Food and Drink Scene of Putbus

Putbus is known for its traditional cuisine, which includes fresh seafood, local meats, and hearty stews. There are several restaurants and cafes in Putbus that offer traditional dishes and local beverages. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages of Putbus:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Rügener Heringspufff: This is a traditional dish made with herring, onions, and dough. It is fried until golden brown and served with a side salad or potatoes.
  • Rügen Potato Soup: This hearty soup is made with potatoes, bacon, onions, and cream. It is a perfect dish to warm up on a cold day.
  • Rügen Zwiebackschnitte: This is a traditional dessert made with sliced bread, whipped cream, and fruits. It is a perfect dessert for any occasion.

Local Beverages

  • Rügener Insel-Brauerei Beer: This local beer is brewed on Rügen Island and is known for its unique flavor and quality.
  • Rügen Küstenwald Honey: This local honey is produced in the forests of Rügen and is known for its sweet and floral taste.
  • **Rügener Bodden: ** This is a local brand of vodka made with potatoes from Rügen. It is a perfect drink to enjoy on a night out in Putbus.
