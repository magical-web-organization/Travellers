Introduction to Altenkirchen, Mecklenburg-Vorpommern

Located in the northern part of Germany, Altenkirchen is a charming town in the district of Rügen, nestled in the heart of the stunning landscape of Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania. The town is famous for its historic landmarks, scenic natural surroundings and a range of cultural and recreational activities that attract tourists from across the globe. Whether you are a solo traveler, a family with kids, or a group of friends, this town offers something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll explore the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, what to eat, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in Altenkirchen, Mecklenburg-Vorpommern

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Ernst-Moritz-Arndt-Museum

One of the top cultural attractions in Altenkirchen is the Ernst-Moritz-Arndt-Museum. It’s a museum that displays the life and works of Ernst Moritz Arndt, a renowned writer, and scholar, who was born in this town in 1769. The museum has an impressive collection of manuscripts, books, and memorabilia related to Arndt’s life, and also showcases exhibits related to the region’s cultural and historical heritage.

Hiddenseer Kunsthaus

Another popular cultural attraction in Altenkirchen is the Hiddenseer Kunsthaus. It’s a museum that displays and sells the works of local artists, and also offers art-related workshops and events. Here, you will find high-quality paintings, sculptures, photographs, and other artworks depicting the natural beauty of the region.

Natural Attractions of Altenkirchen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

National Park Jasmund

The National Park Jasmund is one of the most popular natural attractions in the region, and it’s located just a short drive from Altenkirchen. It features a stunning natural landscape of cliffs, forests, and meadows, and is home to some of the rarest plants and animals in the world. Here, you can go for a hike, bike ride, or horseback ride on one of the many trails, or simply relax and enjoy the breathtaking views.

Cape Arkona

Another must-see natural attraction near Altenkirchen is Cape Arkona. It’s a scenic headland that overlooks the Baltic Sea, and features two historic lighthouses, a medieval castle, and several hiking trails. The area also has a range of dining options, souvenir shops, and cultural activities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Altenkirchen

Altenkirchen is a paradise for hiking and biking enthusiasts. The town is surrounded by beautiful countryside and hills, and there are several scenic routes for tourists to explore. Some popular trails include:

  • The Jasmund Circular Hike: This trail takes you through the stunning landscape of the National Park Jasmund, and offers panoramic views of the Baltic Sea.
  • Ostrügen-Hiking-Trail: This trail takes you through some of the most scenic areas of the region, including the charming villages of the island of Rügen.
  • Cycle Route Rügen: This trail is perfect for those who want to explore the island of Rügen by bike, and takes you through some of the most picturesque parts of the region.

Best Things to Do in Altenkirchen: Activities and Tours

Outdoor Adventures

For adrenaline junkies, Altenkirchen offers several exciting outdoor adventure activities, including:

  • Kayaking and Canoeing: Explore the region’s rivers and lakes by renting a kayak or canoe from a local service provider.
  • Fishing: The area is known for its rich marine biodiversity, and you can spend a day catching fish in the Baltic Sea or in one of the many nearby lakes.
  • Rock Climbing: The cliffs in Jasmund National Park provide a great opportunity for rock climbing enthusiasts.

Culture and Entertainment

For a taste of the local culture and entertainment scene in Altenkirchen, you can:

  • Attend a Traditional Festival: The town hosts several traditional festivals throughout the year, including the Rügener Spring Festival, the Rügener Islands Folk Festival, and the Rügener Knollenfest.
  • Visit a Local Micro-Brewery: The area is known for its traditional beer-making techniques, and you can visit one of the local micro-breweries to learn about the process and taste the beer.
  • Take a Guided Tour: There are several guided tours available, including walking tours, biking tours, and hiking tours, that offer a chance to learn more about the local culture and history.

Week-Long Itinerary for Altenkirchen

Here is a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to Altenkirchen:

Day 1: Explore the National Park Jasmund and visit the Ernst-Moritz-Arndt-Museum.

Day 2: Take a guided tour of the historic town center of Altenkirchen and visit the local micro-brewery.

Day 3: Enjoy a day at the beach, and indulge in some watersports or windsurfing.

Day 4: Drive to Cape Arkona and explore the lighthouses, castle, and hiking trails.

Day 5: Go for a bike ride along the Ostrügen-Hiking-Trail and visit the Hiddenseer Kunsthaus.

Day 6: Take a fishing trip and enjoy the rich marine biodiversity of the region.

Day 7: Participate in a traditional festival and enjoy the local food and beverages.

Food and Drink Scene of Altenkirchen

Altenkirchen has a vibrant food and drink scene that showcases the local produce and traditional culinary techniques of the region.

Must-Try Dishes

Some of the must-try dishes in Altenkirchen include:

  • Rügener Heringsfilet: A local variant of herring that is typically served with boiled potatoes and a creamy sauce.
  • Rügener Islandkartoffeln: Potatoes grown locally that are famous for their unique taste and flavor.
  • Rügener Inselbier: A traditional beer made with locally grown hops and yeast.

Local Beverages

Apart from beer, you can also try some of the local alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages in Altenkirchen, such as:

  • Rügener Heilkreide: A natural chalky mineral water that is believed to have health benefits.
  • Rügener Inselkaffee: A caffeine-free herbal coffee made from roasted figs, chicory, and barley.
  • Rügener Islander Bitter: A local digestive liqueur made from native herbs and spices.

Local Culture and Customs of Altenkirchen

Altenkirchen has a rich and diverse cultural heritage that is reflected in its art, architecture, festivals, and traditions. Some popular customs and traditions in the region include:

  • Traditional Fishing Techniques: The area is known for its traditional fishing techniques, and you can learn more about them by visiting one of the local fishing villages.
  • Folk Music: The region has a rich history of folk music, and you can attend one of the many folk music festivals or concerts to experience the local music scene.
  • Traditional Crafts: The region has a rich history of traditional crafts, such as pottery, wood carving, and basket weaving, and you can buy some beautiful handicrafts made by the local artisans.

Best Time to Visit Altenkirchen

The best time to visit Altenkirchen is during the summer months (June-August), when the weather is warm and sunny, and there are several outdoor activities and festivals to enjoy. Getting to Altenkirchen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Altenkirchen

The nearest international airport to Altenkirchen is Rostock-Laage Airport (RLG), located approximately 70 km away. There are regular flights to major European cities from this airport.

Bus Stations Near Altenkirchen

There are several bus stations near Altenkirchen, including:

  • Sassnitz Bus Station: Located approximately 10 km away from Altenkirchen, this bus station serves several regional and intercity routes.
  • Bergen auf Rügen Bus Station: Located approximately 30 km away from Altenkirchen, this bus station serves several regional and intercity routes.

Other Options

You can also reach Altenkirchen by train or by car. The nearest train station is located in Sassnitz, and there are several car rental services available throughout the region.
