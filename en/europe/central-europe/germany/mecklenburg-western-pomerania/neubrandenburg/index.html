Introduction to Neubrandenburg

Neubrandenburg is a charming city located in the northeast part of the state of Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania in Germany. Known for its picturesque brick buildings, stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage, there are plenty of things to see and do in this hidden gem.

List of Places to Visit in Neubrandenburg

Whether you’re interested in exploring the city’s rich history or taking in its natural beauty, Neubrandenburg has plenty of attractions to offer. Here are some of the best places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum im Steintor: This fascinating museum is housed in one of the town’s oldest buildings and showcases exhibits on the town’s history and culture.
  • St. Marien Church: Located in the heart of Neubrandenburg, this impressive brick church is the largest in the town and features beautiful Gothic architecture.
  • City Gates and Walls: The ancient walls and gates that surrounded the city in medieval times still stand today. You can explore them by walking around the city.

Natural Attractions of Neubrandenburg: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Tollense Valley Nature Park: This beautiful park features rolling hills, forests, and fields surrounding the Tollense river, making it perfect for hiking and biking.
  • Neubrandenburger See: This lake is a great place for boating, swimming, and fishing and is surrounded by beautiful scenery.
  • Müritz National Park: Just an hour’s drive from Neubrandenburg, this stunning park features lakes, forests, and untouched natural landscapes.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Neubrandenburg

If you’re looking for a way to explore the beautiful natural surroundings of Neubrandenburg and take in the fresh air, there are several hiking and biking routes to choose from. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • Tollense cycle path: This 32 km path follows the Tollense River from Neubrandenburg to Demmin and is perfect for anyone looking for a leisurely bike ride.
  • Lake Trail: A 5 km hike around the Neubrandenburger See.
  • Müritz National Park: With over 650km of hiking and biking trails, there are plenty of options for exploring the park on foot or by bike.

Best Things to Do in Neubrandenburg: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re looking for adventure or simply want to sit back and relax, Neubrandenburg has plenty of activities to keep you entertained. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Boat Trips: You can take a boat trip on Neubrandenburger See and discover the surrounding area from a new perspective.
  • Canoeing Tours: The Tollense River is perfect for canoeing tours, which will take you through the beautiful countryside surrounding Neubrandenburg.
  • City Walking Tour: Take a guided walking tour of the city and discover the fascinating history of its buildings and structures.

Food and Drink Scene of Neubrandenburg

Neubrandenburg offers a range of culinary delights, from traditional dishes to international cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • **Paling- a fish dish
  • Karbonade: A traditional pork dish with onions and gravy.
  • Neubrandenburger Bierbraten: A Bavarian dish made with pork

Local Beverages

  • Müritz Pils: A local beer made in the town of Waren.
  • Mecklenburger Landbier: A dark beer made in Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania.
  • Störtebeker-Cocktail: This refreshing cocktail is made with beer, lemonade, and a hint of vanilla syrup.

Where to Stay in Neubrandenburg: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Whether you’re looking for luxury accommodation or a budget-friendly option, there are plenty of great places to stay in Neubrandenburg. Here are some of the best:

  • Parkhotel Neubrandenburg: A four-star hotel located in the heart of the city.
  • City Hostel: A comfortable and affordable hostel for backpackers and budget travelers.
  • Pension Villa Neubrandenburg: A beautiful guesthouse located in a historic villa with charm.

What to Buy in Neubrandenburg: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Whether you’re looking for unique souvenirs or local products to bring home, Neubrandenburg has plenty to offer. Here are some great options:

  • Brick Souvenirs: You can buy replicas of the city’s famous brick buildings, which make for great souvenirs.
  • Mecklenburg-Vorpommern Wine: This wine is produced in the region and is a great way to enjoy the local produce.
  • Amber Jewelry: Amber jewelry is a popular souvenir in the area and is sold in many of the local shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Neubrandenburg

The people of Neubrandenburg are known for their friendly nature and welcoming attitude towards visitors. The city has a long history dating back to the 13th century, and its historic buildings and landmarks offer a glimpse into its rich past.

Best Time to Visit Neubrandenburg

The best time to visit Neubrandenburg is during the summer months when the weather is warm and sunny. However, if you’re looking to avoid the crowds, you can also visit during the spring or fall, when the weather is still pleasant but there are fewer tourists around.

Do’s and Don’ts of Neubrandenburg

Here are some do’s and don’ts to consider when visiting Neubrandenburg:

Do:

  • Enjoy the local food: Neubrandenburg offers a range of culinary delights, so be sure to try as much as you can.
  • Take your time: The city is best explored on foot, so take your time and enjoy the sights at your own pace.

Don’t:

  • Forget to bring comfortable shoes: The streets in Neubrandenburg are cobbled, so make sure to wear comfortable shoes when exploring the city.
  • Be afraid to interact with the locals: The people of Neubrandenburg are friendly and welcoming, so don’t be afraid to strike up a conversation.

Safety and Health

Safety in Neubrandenburg

Neubrandenburg is generally a safe city, with a low crime rate. However, like any other city, it’s important to take basic precautions such as keeping an eye on your belongings and staying aware of your surroundings when out and about.

Health in Neubrandenburg

Neubrandenburg has several hospitals and medical facilities, so in case of emergency you can be confident that you will receive adequate medical care.

Getting to Neubrandenburg: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Neubrandenburg

The closest airport to Neubrandenburg is Rostock-Laage Airport, which is located about 86 km away. However, most visitors choose to fly into Berlin Tegel International Airport, which is about 150 km away.

Bus Stations Near Neubrandenburg

There are several bus stations in Neubrandenburg, including the central bus station located in the city center.

Other Options

You can also travel to Neubrandenburg by train, with regular connections from Berlin, Rostock, and other nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Neubrandenburg

The best way to explore Neubrandenburg is on foot, as many of the city’s attractions are located within walking distance of each other. However, you can also rent a bike or take public transport, which consists of buses and trams.

Where to Go Next from Neubrandenburg: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore some of the other nearby destinations from Neubrandenburg, here are some great options:

  • Rostock: A beautiful city located on the Baltic Sea, known for its stunning architecture and cultural attractions.
  • Berlin: Germany’s capital city is just a two-hour drive from Neubrandenburg and offers a wealth of art, culture, and history to explore.
  • Usedom: A beautiful island located off the coast of Mecklenburg-Western Pomerania, home to some of Germany’s most stunning natural scenery.

Conclusion

Neubrandenburg is a hidden gem, offering a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. Whether you’re interested in exploring the city’s medieval past or enjoying its stunning landscapes, there’s plenty to see and do in this charming town. Plan your trip today and discover all that Neubrandenburg has to offer.
