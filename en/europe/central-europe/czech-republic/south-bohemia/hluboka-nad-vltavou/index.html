Introduction to Hluboká nad Vltavou

Hluboká nad Vltavou is a historic town located in South Bohemia, in the Czech Republic. It is known for its stunning architecture, beautiful natural surroundings, and rich cultural heritage. The main attraction of the town is the Hluboká Castle, one of the most beautiful castles in the country.

Apart from the castle, Hluboká nad Vltavou offers many other interesting places to visit, ranging from museums and historical landmarks to scenic natural attractions and outdoor activities. The town also has a vibrant food and drink scene, with many traditional Czech restaurants and bars serving local specialties.

List of Places to Visit in Hluboká nad Vltavou

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Hluboká Castle

Hluboká Castle is the main attraction of the town, and one of the most beautiful castles in the Czech Republic. The castle was originally built in the 13th century, and has been renovated many times over the centuries. Today, it is a museum that showcases the history of the town and the castle.

Church of St. John the Baptist

The Church of St. John the Baptist is a beautiful Baroque church located in the center of the town. It was built in the 18th century and has many interesting features, including a Gothic statue of the Madonna and Child.

Ohrada Hunting Lodge

The Ohrada Hunting Lodge is a historic building located in the Deer Park near Hluboká Castle. It was built in the 19th century as a hunting lodge for the nobility, and today it is a museum that showcases the lifestyle of the Czech aristocracy.

Natural Attractions of Hluboká nad Vltavou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Deer Park

The Deer Park is a beautiful natural area located near Hluboká Castle. It is home to many deer, as well as other wildlife, and offers scenic walking and hiking trails.

Lužnice River

The Lužnice River is a scenic river that flows through South Bohemia. It is a popular spot for fishing, boating, and swimming.

Hluboká Fishponds

The Hluboká Fishponds are a series of interconnected ponds located near the town. They are home to many species of fish and other aquatic life, and offer beautiful views of the surrounding countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hluboká nad Vltavou

Hluboká nad Vltavou and its surroundings offer many scenic hiking and biking trails. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • Hiking trail to Hluboká Castle
  • Biking trail along the Lužnice River
  • Hiking trail to the Ohrada Hunting Lodge

Best Things to Do in Hluboká nad Vltavou: Activities and Tours

Castle Tour

A tour of the Hluboká Castle is a must-do activity in the town. The tour takes visitors through the many rooms of the castle, showcasing the history and the art of the region.

Horseback Riding

Horseback riding is a popular activity in Hluboká nad Vltavou. There are many stables around the town that offer guided horseback riding tours through the surrounding countryside.

Wine Tasting

The region around Hluboká nad Vltavou is known for its wine production. There are many wineries and wine cellars in the area that offer wine tasting tours, allowing visitors to sample some of the best Czech wines.

Food and Drink Scene of Hluboká nad Vltavou

Must-Try Dishes

Svíčková

Svíčková is a traditional Czech dish made from beef sirloin that is simmered in a vegetable and cream sauce. It is served with dumplings and cranberries.

Trout

Trout is a popular fish in the Czech Republic. It is usually grilled or fried and served with boiled potatoes and a green salad.

Local Beverages

Pilsner Urquell

Pilsner Urquell is a world-famous Czech beer that has been brewed in the town of Pilsen since 1842.

Becherovka

Becherovka is a herbal liqueur that has been made in the Czech Republic since 1807. It is made from a secret blend of herbs and spices.
