Introduction to Říčany

Říčany is a town located in the Central Bohemian Region of the Czech Republic. It’s situated on the outskirts of Prague, the country’s capital, making it the perfect destination for those who want to explore both the Czech countryside and the city. Říčany is a small town known for its picturesque architecture, charming streets, and natural beauty.

List of Places to Visit in Říčany

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Říčany has a rich history that dates back to the 13th century. Here are some museums and historical places that you should visit:

  • Říčany Museum: The museum has several exhibits and collections that tell the story of the town’s history and culture.
  • St. Peter and Paul Church: This is the most prominent religious building in Říčany. The church is known for its unique baroque architecture and exquisite interior.
  • Zelený Vrch: Zelený Vrch is a hill located in Říčany. It’s a popular spot for tourists and locals alike because of the scenic views it offers. At the top of the hill, there is a church and a small restaurant.

Natural Attractions of Říčany: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you’re looking for some outdoor adventures and scenic views, then Říčany won’t disappoint. Here are some of the natural attractions that you should add to your list:

  • Říčanský Háj: Říčanský Háj is a forest located in Říčany. It’s an ideal destination for hiking, biking, or a peaceful picnic. The forest offers several trails for different difficulty levels that you can explore.
  • Sázava River: The Sázava River flows through Říčany, and you can take a canoe or kayak ride along the river to explore the stunning scenery along the way.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Říčany

Říčany has several hiking and biking routes that you can explore. Here are some of the trails that you should check out:

  • Voděradská Railway Trail: It’s a 14 km trail that follows the track of the former Voděradská Railway. The trail starts in Říčany and goes through forests, meadows, and picturesque villages.
  • Kuriánka Trail: Kuriánka Trail is a 7 km trail that takes you through the forests around Říčany. It’s a popular destination for hiking and mountain biking.

Best Things to Do in Říčany: Activities and Tours

Here are some activities and tours that you shouldn’t miss during your Říčany trip:

  • Visit the Říčany Museum: The museum is a must-visit if you want to learn more about the town’s history and culture.
  • Explore Říčanský Háj: A hike or bike ride through the Říčanský Háj forest is a beautiful escape from the city’s hustle and bustle.
  • Take a canoe or kayak ride along the Sázava River: It’s a peaceful and scenic adventure that will help you to connect with nature.

Week-Long Itinerary for Říčany

If you’re planning to stay in Říčany for a week, you should consider this itinerary:

  • Day 1: Explore the town center, St. Peter and Paul Church, and the Říčany Museum
  • Day 2: Hike or bike ride through Říčanský Háj forest
  • Day 3: Take a canoe or kayak ride along the Sázava River
  • Day 4: Visit the nearby village of Kamenice and explore its historical landmarks
  • Day 5: Take a day trip to Prague
  • Day 6: Visit the Zelený Vrch hill and enjoy the stunning views from the top
  • Day 7: Take a cultural tour of Říčany and learn more about its traditions and customs

Food and Drink Scene of Říčany

Must-Try Dishes

Here are some traditional Czech dishes that you shouldn’t miss during your visit to Říčany:

  • Svíčková: A beef sirloin dish served with dumplings and cream sauce.
  • Vepřová Pečeně: Roast pork served with dumplings and sauerkraut.
  • Bramborák: A Czech potato pancake served with sour cream and garlic.

Local Beverages

  • Beer: The Czech Republic is famous for its beer, and you should try some of the local brands during your visit to Říčany.
  • Slivovice: A fruit brandy made from plums. It’s a popular drink in the Czech Republic.

Where to Stay in Říčany: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Here are some of the accommodation options in Říčany:

  • Hotel Černý Orel: This is a four-star hotel located in the town center. It offers comfortable rooms and a restaurant that serves traditional Czech cuisine.
  • Penzion a Restaurace Na Staré Koleji: This is a guest house located in a renovated train station. It offers comfortable rooms and a restaurant that serves international cuisine.
  • Hostel Říčany: This is a budget-friendly hostel located in the town center. It offers private rooms and dormitories.

What to Buy in Říčany: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some of the souvenirs and local products that you should bring home:

  • Bohemian crystal: Czech Republic is famous for its crystal glassware, and you can find some beautiful pieces in Říčany.
  • Wooden toys: Wooden toys are a traditional Czech product that you can find in Říčany.
  • Local wines: Moravia, a region in the Czech Republic, produces excellent wines, and you can find some of them in Říčany.

Local Culture and Customs of Říčany

  • Czechs are known for their love for beer, and you can find several local breweries and pubs in Říčany.
  • Czechs also value their traditions and customs. You can see this in the town’s celebrations and festivals throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Říčany

The best time to visit Říčany is during spring and summer (April-September) when the weather is mild and pleasant.

Safety and Health

Safety in Říčany

Říčany is a safe town, and you don’t have to worry about your safety during your visit. However, you should take the usual precautions and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Říčany

The town has several medical facilities, and you can find pharmacies in the town center.

Getting to Říčany: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Říčany

The nearest airport to Říčany is the Vaclav Havel Airport in Prague. You can take a taxi or public transportation to reach the town.

Bus Stations Near Říčany

You can take a bus from Prague to Říčany. The journey takes approximately 30 minutes.

Other Options

You can also take a train from Prague to Říčany. The journey takes approximately 20 minutes.

How to Get Around in Říčany

You can explore Říčany on foot or by bike. Buses and taxis are also available.

Where to Go Next from Říčany: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations that you can explore from Říčany:
