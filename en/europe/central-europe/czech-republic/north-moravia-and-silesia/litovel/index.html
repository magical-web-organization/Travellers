Introduction to Litovel

Located in North Moravia and Silesia, Litovel is a charming town in Czech Republic that attracts tourists with its cultural and natural attractions, as well as its outdoor adventures, food and drink scenes, and rich history. With a population of just over 9,000 people, Litovel offers visitors a unique and authentic experience that is worth exploring.

List of Places to Visit in Litovel

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Litovel boasts several culturally important attractions that every traveler must visit.

Litovel Castle

Litovel Castle is a stunning Baroque palace that was built in the 17th century and is now a protected cultural monument. The castle is home to a museum that showcases the town’s history, art, and culture.

St. Wenceslas Church

St. Wenceslas Church is one of the oldest and largest churches in Litovel, dating back to the 13th century. The church boasts beautiful Gothic architecture and stained-glass windows.

Jewish Cemetery

The Jewish Cemetery in Litovel dates back to the mid-16th century and is one of the largest of its kind in the Czech Republic. It serves as a reminder of the Jewish community that once thrived in Litovel.

Natural Attractions of Litovel: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Litovel is surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes that offer visitors the opportunity for outdoor adventures and scenic views.

Litovelske Pomoravi Nature Park

Litovelske Pomoravi Nature Park is a protected area that covers over 350 square kilometers of wetlands, forests, and meadows. Visitors can explore the park on foot or by bike, and wildlife spotting is a common activity.

Svaty Kopecek

Svaty Kopecek is a hill that offers stunning panoramic views of Litovel and the surrounding area. Visitors can hike up the hill or take a cable car and enjoy the views from the top.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Litovel

For travelers who enjoy hiking and biking, Litovel offers several trails that showcase the town’s natural beauty.

Litovel-Bouzov Trail

The Litovel-Bouzov Trail is a 37-kilometer trail that starts in Litovel and takes visitors through the Litovelske Pomoravi Nature Park and ends at Bouzov Castle. The trail is suitable for both hiking and biking.

Litovelske Paseky Trail

The Litovelske Paseky Trail is a 10-kilometer trail that takes visitors through the forests and meadows of Litovelske Pomoravi Nature Park.

Best Things to Do in Litovel: Activities and Tours

In addition to its natural and cultural attractions, Litovel offers visitors a range of activities and tours.

Brewery Tour

Litovel is home to one of the oldest and most famous breweries in the Czech Republic. Visitors can take a tour of the brewery, learn about the brewing process, and sample some of the town’s famous beer.

Canoeing on the Morava River

The Morava River, which runs through Litovel, offers visitors the opportunity to go on a beautiful canoeing adventure. Several local tour operators offer guided canoeing tours.

Week-Long Itinerary for Litovel

For visitors who want to spend a week in Litovel, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day 1: Explore Litovel Castle and the town center

Day 2: Visit the Svaty Kopecek hill and enjoy the scenic views

Day 3: Take a tour of the Litovel Brewery and enjoy a beer tasting

Day 4: Go on a canoeing tour on the Morava River

Day 5: Visit the Litovelske Paseky Trail and explore the Litovelske Pomoravi Nature Park

Day 6: Take a day trip to Olomouc, a nearby city with stunning architecture and rich history

Day 7: Relax and enjoy the town’s local food and drink scene

Food and Drink Scene of Litovel

Litovel’s food and drink scene offers visitors a taste of traditional Czech cuisine that is sure to please any traveler.

Must-Try Dishes

Svickova

Svickova is a famous Czech dish that consists of beef sirloin in a cream sauce, served with dumplings and cranberry sauce.

Guláš

Guláš is a traditional Czech stew that is made with beef, onions, and paprika, and served with bread or dumplings.

Local Beverages

Litovel Beer

Litovel Beer is one of the town’s most famous products and is a must-try for any beer lover. The brewery has been producing beer since 1856 and is still using traditional brewing methods.

Slivovice

Slivovice is a traditional Czech brandy made from plums. It is a strong and potent drink that is often served as an aperitif.

Where to Stay in Litovel: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Litovel offers visitors a range of accommodations that cater to all budgets and preferences.

Hotel Zlatý Lev

Hotel Zlatý Lev is a stylish and comfortable hotel located in the heart of Litovel. It offers spacious rooms, a restaurant, and a bar.

Pension Privoz

Pension Privoz is a cozy guesthouse located near the Litovelske Pomoravi Nature Park. It offers comfortable rooms, a restaurant, and a garden.

What to Buy in Litovel: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to Litovel can bring home a range of souvenirs and local products that make for great gifts or reminders of their trip.

Litovel Beer

Litovel Beer makes for a great souvenir or gift for any beer lover.

Glass Products

Litovel is known for its glass products, which make for beautiful and unique souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Litovel

Litovel is a traditional Czech town with a rich culture and customs that visitors should be aware of.

Festivals

Litovel hosts several festivals throughout the year, including the Litovel Beer Festival and the Litovelske Pomoravi Festival.

Customs

Tipping is a common practice in Litovel, and visitors should tip around 10% at restaurants and cafes.

Best Time to Visit Litovel

The best time to visit Litovel is during the summer months, from May to September, when the weather is warm and sunny, and outdoor activities are at their best.

Do’s and Don’ts of Litovel

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Try the famous Litovel Beer.
  • Explore the Litovelske Pomoravi Nature Park.

Don’t:

  • Ignore the local rules and regulations.
  • Underestimate the strength of slivovice.
  • Leave trash in public areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Litovel

Litovel is a safe and peaceful town, and visitors should not face any safety concerns. However, it is always advisable to take the necessary precautions and keep valuables safe.

Health in Litovel

Litovel has a good healthcare system, and visitors can access medical facilities through their travel insurance or the Czech healthcare system.

Getting to Litovel: Transportation Options and Logistics

Visitors can get to Litovel through several means of transportation.

Airports Near Litovel

The nearest airport to Litovel is Brno-Turany Airport, which is around 85 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Litovel

Litovel has a bus station that offers regular connections to nearby cities and towns.

Other Options

Visitors can also get to Litovel by train or by car.

How to Get Around in Litovel

Litovel is a small town, and visitors can easily explore it on foot or by bike.

Where to Go Next from Litovel: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors to Litovel can take day trips to nearby destinations and explore the surrounding area.

Olomouc

Olomouc is a city located around 20 kilometers from Litovel and is known for its stunning architecture and rich history.

Bouzov Castle

Bouzov Castle is located around 25 kilometers from Litovel and is a beautiful castle that is worth a visit.

Conclusion

Litovel is a charming and authentic town in Czech Republic that offers visitors a range of cultural and natural attractions, outdoor adventures, food and drink scenes, and rich history and customs. A visit to Litovel is a must-try for anyone looking for an authentic Czech experience.
