Introduction to Ústí nad Orlicí District

Ústí nad Orlicí District is situated in East Bohemia, in the Czech Republic, in Central Europe. The district is known for its stunning natural landscapes, rich cultural history, and outdoor activities. With plenty of attractions to offer, Ústí nad Orlicí is the perfect destination for travelers who enjoy exploring less-touristy spots.

List of Places to Visit in Ústí nad Orlicí District

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The district is rich in history, and there are plenty of museums and historical sites to visit. Some of the most popular include:

  1. Kunětická Hora – A UNESCO World Heritage Site, Kunětická Hora is a 14th-century castle located just outside of the town of Pardubice.
  2. Chateau Litomyšl – A Renaissance chateau, built in the 16th century and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  3. Orlické Mountains Museum – A museum that showcases the history and culture of the Orlické Mountains.
  4. The Military History Museum at Žamberk – A museum dedicated to military history, with a wide range of exhibits and artifacts.

Natural Attractions of Ústí nad Orlicí District: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you love the outdoors, there’s plenty to explore in Ústí nad Orlicí District.

  1. Eagle Mountains – A mountain range that offers plenty of outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, and skiing.
  2. Tichá Orlice – A stunning river that runs through the district, perfect for fishing, kayaking, and boating.
  3. Žamberk – A small town that is home to some of the most beautiful and untouched natural landscapes in the region.
  4. Říčky v Orlických horách – A small village in the Orlické Mountains with breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ústí nad Orlicí District

There are plenty of hiking and biking routes winding through the district.

  1. Orlické Mountains – With over 250 km of marked trails, Orlické Mountains is one of the most popular hiking destinations in the region.
  2. Podorlicko – A cycling route that takes visitors through the picturesque countryside of the district.
  3. Eagle Mountains – The Eagle Mountains offer a variety of hiking trails, ranging from easy walks to more challenging routes.

Best Things to Do in Ústí nad Orlicí District: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re interested in history, culture, or the outdoors, Ústí nad Orlicí District has something for everyone.

  1. Kunětická Hora – Take a tour of the castle and learn about its fascinating history.
  2. Orlické Mountains Museum – Explore the history and culture of the Orlické Mountains.
  3. Tichá Orlice – Take a relaxing boat ride down the river and enjoy the beautiful scenery.
  4. Eagle Mountains – Rent a bike and explore the various trails and cycling routes throughout the mountains.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ústí nad Orlicí District

If you have a week to spare, here is a suggested itinerary for Ústí nad Orlicí District:

Day 1: Explore Kunětická Hora. Day 2: Visit Orlické Mountains Museum. Day 3: Take a boat tour of Tichá Orlice. Day 4: Hike or bike in Eagle Mountains. Day 5: Visit Chateau Litomyšl. Day 6: Explore Žamberk. Day 7: Relax in Říčky v Orlických horách.

Food and Drink Scene of Ústí nad Orlicí District

While visiting the district, be sure to try some of the local cuisine and drinks.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Sezamové řezy – A traditional Czech cake made with sesame seeds and honey.
  2. Svickova – A traditional Czech beef dish, served with dumplings and cream sauce.
  3. Trdlo – A pastry that’s rolled around a spit and roasted over an open fire.

Local Beverages

  1. Pivo – The Czech Republic is known for its beer, and there are plenty of local breweries to visit.
  2. Becherovka – A Czech herbal liquor that’s been produced for over 200 years.
  3. Slivovice – A popular fruit brandy that’s made from plums.

Where to Stay in Ústí nad Orlicí District: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of options for accommodation in the district, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels.

  1. Hotel U Zlatého lva – A charming hotel located in the heart of Pardubice.
  2. Hostel Žamberk – A budget-friendly hostel that’s perfect for backpackers and budget travelers.
  3. Podorlicko Hotel – A comfortable hotel that’s located in the town of Choceň.

What to Buy in Ústí nad Orlicí District: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When you visit the district, don’t forget to take some souvenirs and local products back home.

  2. Bohemian Glass – The Czech Republic is known for its beautiful glassware, and there are plenty of shops where you can buy it.
  3. Traditional Pottery – The district is known for its traditional pottery, which makes an excellent gift or souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Ústí nad Orlicí District

The district is home to a rich cultural heritage, and visitors can learn about various customs and traditions.

Festivals and Celebrations

  1. Easter Markets – Every year, the district celebrates Easter with traditional markets and events.
  2. St. Wenceslas Festival – Held annually in September, the festival celebrates the life of St. Wenceslas with music, food, and cultural events.
  3. Christmas Markets – During the holiday season, the district is home to traditional Christmas markets.

Best Time to Visit Ústí nad Orlicí District

The best time to visit the district is between May and September when the weather is warm and sunny.
