Introduction to Sankt Michael

Sankt Michael is a picturesque town located in Austria’s Salzburg state. This charming town sits amidst lush green meadows, snow-capped mountains and is a perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts. It is situated at an altitude of 1,075 meters and is also known as “Salzburg’s sun terrace” as it receives more than 2,000 hours of sunshine annually. The town is surrounded by stunning mountain ranges and is a popular destination for hiking, biking, and skiing.

In this guide, we’ll explore some of the best things to do in Sankt Michael, including the must-see attractions, natural wonders, hiking and biking routes, where to stay, what to eat, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in Sankt Michael

Here are the must-visit places in Sankt Michael:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Burg Mauterndorf: A centuries-old castle that is now turned into a museum showcasing medieval life.
  • Mariapfarr Parish Church: A beautiful church that dates back to the 14th-century with stunning frescos.
  • Pfarrkirche St. Michael im Lungau: A gothic-style church that has been well preserved over the centuries, with stunning interior decorations.

Natural Attractions of Sankt Michael: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Katschberg-Aineck Nature Reserve: Located just a short ride away from Sankt Michael, it is a beautiful nature reserve with stunning views and hiking trails.
  • Speiereck: A popular hiking destination located in the heart of the Austrian Alps, it offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Grosseck-Speiereck Ski Resort: A popular ski resort with slopes for beginners and experts alike.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sankt Michael

Sankt Michael is surrounded by stunning mountain ranges that offer a variety of hiking and biking trails suitable for all skill levels. Some popular trails include:

  • Schliereralm: A hiking trail that offers amazing views of the surrounding mountains and stunning alpine meadows.
  • Pöllatal cycle path: Suitable for all skill levels, the cycle path is beautiful and relaxing through the lush Pöllatal valley.

Best Things to Do in Sankt Michael: Activities and Tours

  • Treetop trail: A unique adventure park with treetop trails, zip lines, and obstacle courses.
  • Lungau farmers market: A weekly market where local farmers sell fresh produce like cheese, bread, and meat.
  • Total Krampus immersion exhibit: An interactive exhibit showcasing the traditional Krampusnacht festival.

Week-Long Itinerary for Sankt Michael

Here’s a suggested itinerary for spending a week in Sankt Michael:

Day 1-2: Explore the town and visit the cultural landmarks like Burg Mauterndorf and Mariapfarr Parish Church.

Day 3-4: Hike some of the beautiful mountain trails and enjoy the stunning scenery. Also, take a bike ride through the Pöllatal valley.

Day 5-6: Check out some of the unique activities like treetop trails and the Total Krampus Immersion exhibit.

Day 7: Relax and take in the beautiful scenery before heading off to the next destination.

Food and Drink Scene of Sankt Michael

Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to try in Sankt Michael.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lungauer ‘Makroni’: A pasta dish that is popular in the region, consisting of pasta, cheese, and onions.
  • Kasnocken: A traditional cheese dumpling dish served with crispy onions and salad.

Local Beverages

  • Murauer beer: A local beer that is popular in the region.
  • Schnapps: A distilled spirit made from locally grown fruit, common in Austria.

Where to Stay in Sankt Michael: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Sankt Michael has plenty of hotels, hostels, and guesthouses that cater to every budget:

  • Romantic Hotel Wastlwirt: A historic hotel with traditional decor, located in the heart of Sankt Michael.
  • Hotel Häuserl im Wald: A cozy hotel with modern amenities and a beautiful garden located just a short distance from the town center.

What to Buy in Sankt Michael: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some souvenirs and local products you can bring back home from Sankt Michael:

  • Lungau crafts: Unique crafts made by locals, including hand-woven textiles and pottery.
  • Local honey and cheese: Delicious honey and cheese made by local farms.

Local Culture and Customs of Sankt Michael

Sankt Michael is steeped in tradition and has a unique local culture that is worth experiencing. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Krampusnacht: A traditional winter festival that takes place every December, featuring performers in devilish costumes.
  • Tracht: Traditional clothing is still worn by locals in special events and is worth experiencing.

Best Time to Visit Sankt Michael

Sankt Michael is a year-round destination. However, the summer months from May to September are the best time to visit for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sankt Michael

Do:

  • Respect local traditions and customs.
  • Try local specialties like Kasnocken and Murauer beer.
  • Do plenty of hiking and biking in the surrounding mountains.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget warm clothing, even in summer, as the weather can be unpredictable.
  • Don’t miss out on the opportunity to take in the beautiful scenery.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sankt Michael

Sankt Michael is generally a safe destination for travelers. However, it’s always recommended to exercise caution while hiking or participating in any outdoor activities.

Health in Sankt Michael

Sankt Michael has excellent medical facilities and is served by the Tamsweg District Hospital.

Getting to Sankt Michael: Transportation Options and Logistics

Sankt Michael is located approximately 100 km from Salzburg and is easily accessible via public transport.

Airports Near Sankt Michael

  • Salzburg Airport: Serves many major international destinations and is approximately 100 km away from Sankt Michael.

Bus Stations Near Sankt Michael

  • Sankt Michael: There is a bus stop located in the town center with connections to nearby destinations.

Other Options

  • Train: There is a train station in Tamsweg, which is approximately 10 km away from Sankt Michael.

How to Get Around in Sankt Michael

Sankt Michael is a small town that can easily be explored on foot. However, if you want to visit other nearby destinations, it’s recommended to rent a car.

Where to Go Next from Sankt Michael: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations that are worth considering:

  • Salzburg: A beautiful city that is well-known for its historical buildings and music festivals.
  • Bad Gastein: A popular spa town with natural thermal springs and stunning alpine scenery.
  • Grossglockner High Alpine Road: A beautiful mountain road that provides stunning views of Austria’s highest mountain peak.

Conclusion

Sankt Michael is a picturesque town that is becoming increasingly popular among travelers who are looking for unique outdoor adventures and stunning scenery. This guide has highlighted some of the best things to do and see in the town, helping you make the most out of your trip to this beautiful part of Austria.
