Introduction to Krimml

Krimml is a picturesque town located in Salzburg, Austria, in Central Europe. It is best known for its magnificent Krimml Waterfalls, one of the tallest waterfalls in Europe. This charming town attracts tourists from all around the world with its natural beauty, outdoor activities, and cultural attractions.

In this comprehensive guide, we will take you through the must-visit places in Krimml, including the best cultural and natural attractions, hiking and biking routes, activities, tours, local food, and drink scene, places to stay, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in Krimml

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Krimmler Tauernhaus Museum: This museum showcases the history and culture of Krimml, including the importance of the Tauernhaus that served as a shelter for travelers in the past.
  • WasserWunderWelt Krimml: This museum is the perfect place to learn more about the natural wonders of Krimml, including the Krimml Waterfalls, glacier formations, and much more.

Natural Attractions of Krimml: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Krimml Waterfalls: This is the main attraction and a must-visit place in Krimml. These waterfalls are one of the tallest in Europe and offer spectacular views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Hochgebirgsstauseen Kaprun: These high-altitude reservoirs are located on the Kaprun glacier, and they offer breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains, glaciers, and lakes.
  • Zillertal Arena: This ski resort is located near the town of Krimml and offers fantastic slopes, trails, and lifts for skiers and snowboarders.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Krimml

Krimml offers a wide range of hiking and biking routes that cater to all levels of fitness and skill. Here are some of the most popular routes in Krimml:

  • The Krimmler Achental Valley: This valley is home to the Krimml Waterfalls and offers some beautiful hikes and walks along the river. It is also an ideal place for mountain biking.
  • The Zillertal Arena: This ski resort transforms into a hiking and biking paradise in the summer months. With over 200 km of trails, it offers some of the best hiking and biking routes in the region.

Best Things to Do in Krimml: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of fun activities and tours that you can do in Krimml, and here are some of the best:

  • Rafting on the Salzach River: This is an adrenaline-pumping experience that takes you along the Salzach River. It is the perfect activity for adventure lovers.
  • Paragliding: Soar high above the Krimmler Achental Valley and experience the stunning views of the mountains and the Krimml Waterfalls.
  • Salzburg Day Trip: Visit the historical city of Salzburg and explore its beautiful architecture, art, and culture.

Week-Long Itinerary for Krimml

Day 1: Arrive in Krimml and visit the Krimmler Tauernhaus Museum. Day 2: Explore the Krimml Waterfalls and go on a hike along the Krimmler Achental Valley. Day 3: Visit the WasserWunderWelt Krimml and relax in the nearby hot springs. Day 4: Go skiing or snowboarding in the Zillertal Arena. Day 5: Take a day trip to Salzburg and explore the city. Day 6: Go paragliding over the Krimmler Achental Valley and enjoy the stunning views. Day 7: Relax and enjoy the local food and drink scene of Krimml.

Food and Drink Scene of Krimml

Krimml has a vibrant food and drink scene, and here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Salzburger Nockerl: This is a signature dessert of Salzburg and is a must-try dish in Krimml.
  • Schnitzel: A classic Austrian dish, Schnitzel is a thin meat cutlet, breaded and deep-fried until crispy

Local Beverages

  • Stiegl beer: This is a local beer brand that is popular in Salzburg and is a must-try for beer lovers.
  • Schnapps: This is a traditional Austrian fruit brandy that is popular in Krimml and is available in different flavors.

Where to Stay in Krimml: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Krimml offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay in Krimml:

  • Hotel Gasthof zur Post: This is a cozy 3-star hotel located in the heart of Krimml and is perfect for budget travelers.
  • Wasserfallchalet Krimml: These are an excellent choice for families or groups of friends who want to enjoy a comfortable stay with stunning views of the Krimml Waterfalls.
  • Hotel Krimmlerfälle: This is a luxurious 4-star hotel that offers fantastic facilities, including a spa, sauna, and outdoor pool.

What to Buy in Krimml: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Some of the best souvenirs and local products to buy in Krimml include:

  • Krimml Waterfalls souvenirs: There are plenty of waterfalls-themed souvenirs available in Krimml, including postcards, magnets, and T-shirts.
  • Schnapps: Krimml is known for its Schnapps, and you can buy some local flavors to take home.
  • Traditional Austrian clothes: You will find a range of traditional Austrian clothes, including lederhosen, dirndls, and hats, in the local shops and boutiques.

Local Culture and Customs of Krimml

The locals in Krimml are warm and friendly, and they love to share their culture and customs with visitors. Here are some of the local customs and traditions that you should be aware of:

  • The wearing of traditional Austrian clothes: You will find many locals wearing traditional clothes, including lederhosen and dirndls, during festivals and celebrations.
  • Respect for nature: Krimml is a town that is famous for its natural beauty, and the locals have great respect for it. Visitors are expected to respect the environment and not litter or damage it in any way.

Best Time to Visit Krimml

The best time to visit Krimml is from May to September when the weather is warm, and the hiking and biking routes are open.

Do’s and Don’ts of Krimml

  • Experience the local food and drink scene.
  • Visit the Krimml Waterfalls.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the environment in any way.

  • Be disrespectful to the locals or other tourists.

Safety and Health

Safety in Krimml

Krimml is generally a safe town to visit, but it is always wise to take precautions. Here are some safety tips:

  • Keep your valuables in a safe place.
  • Don’t leave your belongings unattended.
  • Be aware of pickpockets in crowded areas.

Health in Krimml

Krimml has good medical facilities, and tourists can access emergency medical care if needed. It is also advisable to have travel medical insurance before you visit.

Getting to Krimml: Transportation Options and Logistics

Krimml is well-connected by various modes of transportation, including:

Airports Near Krimml

  • Salzburg Airport (SZG): This is the closest airport to Krimml, and it takes around 1 hour and 40 minutes by car.

Bus Stations Near Krimml

  • Krimml Bus Station: This bus station is located in the town center and is well-connected to other parts of Austria.

Other Options

  • Train: Zell am See is the nearest train station to Krimml, and it is well-connected to other parts of Austria.

How to Get Around in Krimml

Krimml is a small town and is easy to explore on foot. You can also hire bicycles from local shops if you want to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Krimml: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Krimml is located in Salzburg, Austria, and there are plenty of nearby destinations and excursions that you can explore, including:

  • Zell am See: This is a beautiful lakeside town that is located just 30 kilometers from Krimml.
  • Innsbruck: This is a historical city that is located around 120 kilometers from Krimml and makes for an excellent day trip.
