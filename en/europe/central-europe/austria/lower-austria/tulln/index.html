Introduction to Tulln

Located in the heart of Lower Austria, Tulln is a charming town known for its magnificent architecture, rich cultural heritage, and scenic natural beauty. The town’s history dates back to the Roman times, and over the centuries, it has been shaped by various cultures, rulers, and events. Today, Tulln is a popular tourist destination that offers visitors a plethora of attractions, activities, and experiences.

Best Things to Do in Tulln: Activities and Tours

From sightseeing and wine tasting to horse racing and water sports, Tulln has something for everyone. Here are some of the best things to do in Tulln:

  • Walking tours of the town
  • Wine tours and tastings
  • Horse racing at Magna Racino

  • Danube Boat Trips

Week-Long Itinerary for Tulln

Day 1: Arrive in Tulln and check into your hotel. Take a walking tour of the town and visit the Minoritenkirche and Die letzte Synagoge. Day 2: Visit the Egon Schiele Museum and Donauturm Tulln Observation Tower. Day 3: Explore the natural beauty of Tulln by taking a bike tour of the vineyards or visiting the Donaupark and Donau Insel. Day 4: Take a day trip to Vienna and explore the city’s historical and cultural landmarks. Day 5: Go horse racing at Magna Racino, or try your hand at water sports on the Danube River. Day 6: Visit the Tulln Museum and Rosenarcade, or take a wine tour and tasting. Day 7: Relax and enjoy your final day in Tulln at one of the town’s many spas or wellness centers.

Local Culture and Customs of Tulln

Tulln is a town with a rich cultural heritage, and its customs and traditions are firmly rooted in its history. Some of the most important cultural events and customs in Tulln include:

  • The annual Tulln Fair
  • The Perchtenlauf or Krampus Run
  • The Danube Festival
  • The Tulln Wine Festival

Best Time to Visit Tulln

The best time to visit Tulln is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny, and all the attractions and activities are in full swing. However, Tulln is a year-round destination, and each season has its unique charm and appeal.
