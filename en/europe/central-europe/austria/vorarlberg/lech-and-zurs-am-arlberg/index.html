Introduction to Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

Nestled in the heart of the Austrian Alps, Lech and Zürs am Arlberg are popular destinations for winter sports enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. The two villages are located in the western state of Vorarlberg, just a few kilometers from the border with Switzerland.

Lech is a traditional mountain village with a pedestrian-only center, while Zürs am Arlberg is known for its luxury hotels and lively après-ski scene. Both offer stunning mountain views, fresh alpine air, and a long list of outdoor activities.

List of Places to Visit in Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Lech and Zürs am Arlberg may be small villages, but they have a rich cultural heritage that is worth exploring.

The Huber Hus Museum in Lech is a traditional farmhouse that dates back to the 16th century. It has been preserved as a cultural monument and now houses a museum of local history. The Walser Museum in Lech also provides insights into the history and culture of the Walser people, who have inhabited the region for centuries.

If you are interested in religious art and architecture, don’t miss the St. Nicholas Church in Lech. This beautiful baroque church dates back to the 18th century and features impressive frescoes and a stunning high altar.

Natural Attractions of Lech and Zürs am Arlberg: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Though Lech and Zürs am Arlberg are popular winter destinations, they offer plenty of outdoor activities year-round.

Hiking is a popular summer activity. The Lechweg Trail is a 125 km hiking trail that runs from the source of the Lech River to its confluence with the Danube. The trail passes through stunning alpine scenery, quaint mountain villages, and offers plenty of opportunities for wildlife spotting.

In winter, the region becomes a winter wonderland. The Arlberg Ski Area is Austria’s largest interconnected skiing area with over 300km of slopes and 200km of deep-snow runs. It is known for its excellent snow conditions and variety of terrain suitable for everyone, from beginners to experienced freeriders.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

Apart from the Lechweg Trail, there are several other hiking and biking trails in the area which offer up-close and personal experiences of the beauty of the region. The Omeshorn tour is a difficult mountain trail that takes you through breathtaking alpine meadows. Alternatively, the Hochtannberg mountain tour is a perfect fit for intermediate level hikers and takes you through pine forests, wildflower meadows, and alpine pastures.

Best Things to Do in Lech and Zürs am Arlberg: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and skiing, there are several other exciting activities to enjoy in Lech and Zürs am Arlberg. A helicopter ride is an amazing way to explore the breathtaking scenery of the region, ice skating on the nearby lake, and an exhilarating toboggan ride through the snowy forests.

For those who want to learn about the region’s traditions, a cheese-making or schnapps distillery tour is a must-do activity. A guided tour of the local breweries is also a great way to sample the region’s brews.

Food and Drink Scene of Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

Must-Try Dishes

The culinary scene in Lech and Zürs am Arlberg is as rich as its culture. Käsespätzle, a traditional dish of soft egg noodles with melted cheese is a popular dish in the region. Other must-try dishes include Kaiserschmarrn, a shredded pancake with fruit compote and dumplings with Sauerkraut.

Local Beverages

Austria is known for its beer and wine. In Lech and Zürs am Arlberg, you can sample some of the local brews, such as Mohrenbräu, Huber Bräu, and Fohrenburger. For those who prefer wine, there are plenty of local wineries to explore.

Where to Stay in Lech and Zürs am Arlberg: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Lech and Zürs am Arlberg offer a range of accommodation options to suit every budget. From luxurious mountain retreats to cozy guesthouses, there is something for everyone.

Some of the most popular options include Hotel Brunnenhof, Hotel Arlberg, and Pension Grissemann.

What to Buy in Lech and Zürs am Arlberg: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

The region is known for its quality wool products, such as blankets, carpets, and hats. You can also find traditional wooden toys, ornaments, and glassware.

Local Culture and Customs of Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

The people of Lech and Zürs am Arlberg are proud of their cultural heritage and traditional way of life. Visitors are expected to show respect for local traditions, such as wearing appropriate clothing in churches and being quiet during church services.

Best Time to Visit Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

The best time to visit Lech and Zürs am Arlberg depends on your interests. Summer is ideal for hiking and exploring the region’s natural beauty, while winter is perfect for skiing and snowboarding.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

Lech and Zürs am Arlberg are safe places to visit. However, visitors are encouraged to follow safety guidelines when participating in outdoor activities, such as skiing and hiking.

Health in Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

The region has a modern healthcare system, and emergency services are available. However, visitors are advised to take out travel insurance before their trip.

Getting to Lech and Zürs am Arlberg: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

The closest airport to Lech and Zürs am Arlberg is Innsbruck Airport, situated about 120 km away. There are several other airports options including Friedrichshafen and Zurich which are approximately 100km and 200km, respectively.

Bus Stations Near Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

Buses from Innsbruck, Zurich, and other major cities in Europe regularly stop in Lech and Zürs am Arlberg.

Other Options

Driving is another option, and many visitors rent cars to explore the surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Lech and Zürs am Arlberg

Both villages have a well-developed public transportation system that connects all the main attractions, including ski lifts, hiking trails, and cultural landmarks.

Where to Go Next from Lech and Zürs am Arlberg: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Lech and Zürs am Arlberg are conveniently located near several other popular destinations in the region. Nearby attractions include the historic town of Innsbruck, the famous Neuschwanstein Castle, and the picturesque city of Salzburg.

Conclusion

Lech and Zürs am Arlberg offer a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and outdoor adventures. From hiking and skiing to exploring museums and enjoying local gastronomy, there is something for everyone in these charming mountain villages. Plan your trip well, and you are sure to have an unforgettable experience in one of Austria’s most beautiful regions.
