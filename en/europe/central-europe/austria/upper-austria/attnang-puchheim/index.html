Attnang-Puchheim, nestled in Upper Austria, Austria, is a charming little town that has a lot to offer visitors. From rich culture to striking outdoor views, there is no doubt visitors will fall in love with this town. This guide aims to provide tourists with enough information to enjoy a wholesome visit to Attnang-Puchheim.

Introduction to Attnang-Puchheim

Attnang-Puchheim is a town of about 10,000 people, located at an altitude of 365 meters. It is one of the oldest towns in Upper Austria, dating back to the 8th century. This makes it a highly historical and cultural town.

List of Places to Visit in Attnang-Puchheim

Visitors to Attnang-Puchheim will be spoilt for choice when it comes to the places they can explore. Here is a list of some of the top places to discover:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Attnang Museum - showcases the history of the town with artefacts and archives
  • Parish Church - an impressive church with neo-Gothic design and a 72-meter tower
  • Town Archive - located in the town hall and stores a collection of historical documents and records.

Natural Attractions of Attnang-Puchheim: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Salzkammergut Mountains - perfect for hiking, picnics, and catching serene views of the outdoors.
  • Attnang Wetlands - a serene wetland home to diverse bird species and a perfect place to catch a scenic view.
  • Kremstal - a picturesque valley that provides a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of life.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Attnang-Puchheim

Attnang-Puchheim is a haven for hikers and bikers. Many well-established trails offer opportunities to explore the natural scenery while staying physically active. Examples of hiking and biking trails include:

  • “Along the Attnang” - A hiking trail along the Attnang-Gries River.
  • “Attnang Green – Gaspoltshofen Blue” - A 17km bike trail that travels through green meadows, dramatic countryside views, and lush forests.

Best Things to Do in Attnang-Puchheim: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of activities to do in Attnang-Puchheim. Here are a few fun things to do in Attnang-Puchheim:

  • Explore the underground galleries of Salt Rooms Gmunden.
  • Take a guided tour of the beautiful castle of Schloss Neuwildenstein.

Week-Long Itinerary for Attnang-Puchheim

A week-long itinerary ensures that tourists can explore the town without missing anything. Here is a sample itinerary for visitors:

Day 1: Explore the town and its rich history Day 2: Hiking in the Salzkammergut Mountains Day 3: Check out Attnang Wetlands and Kremstal Day 4: A guided tour of Schloss Neuwildenstein Day 5: A bike tour along the scenic Gaspoltshofen Blue trail Day 6: Explore the underground galleries of Salt Rooms Gmunden Day 7: Relax with some local cuisine as you wind down your week

Food and Drink Scene of Attnang-Puchheim

Attnang-Puchheim has an array of different food options for every taste. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Wiener schnitzel - A famous Austrian dish consisting of a breaded and fried cutlet
  • Linzer torte - traditional Austrian cake with ground almonds, eggs, sugar, and jam.
  • A local cuisine, schlutzkrapfen, a stuffed pasta covered in breadcrumbs and melted butter

Local Beverages

  • Stiegl beer - one of the best-selling beers in Austria
  • Aperol Spritz - a refreshing cocktail made of Aperol, Prosecco, and soda water.

Where to Stay in Attnang-Puchheim: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Attnang-Puchheim has a range of accommodation options for travellers, from hotels to hostels to guesthouses. Here are a few top options:

  • Gasthaus Pension Baumkirchner - a friendly and cozy guesthouse with free Wi-Fi
  • Bahnhofhotel - a modern hotel near the station with excellent reviews
  • Haus Kati - a lovely guest house located next to the Attnang train station

What to Buy in Attnang-Puchheim: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to Attnang-Puchheim should check out local markets for souvenirs and local products. Here are some great souvenir options:

  • Krisang Garden - flower shop that sells fresh flowers, pots, and horticulture tools
  • Attnang-Puchheim market - great for buying handmade souvenirs and other local products.

Local Culture and Customs of Attnang-Puchheim

Attnang-Puchheim is a small town, and its residents are quite friendly and hospitable to visitors. They welcome visitors with open arms and are always willing to help.

Best Time to Visit Attnang-Puchheim

The ideal time to visit Attnang-Puchheim is during the summer months from June to August when there is sunshine, beautiful scenery, and warm weather. This is when outdoor activities like hiking, swimming, and cycling can be enjoyed the most.

Visitors do not need to be concerned about their safety and health in Attnang-Puchheim.

Safety in Attnang-Puchheim
