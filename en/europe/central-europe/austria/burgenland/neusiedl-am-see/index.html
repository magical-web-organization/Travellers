Introduction to Neusiedl am See

Neusiedl am See is a small town in Burgenland, located in eastern Austria. The town is situated on the shores of Europe’s largest steppe lake, Lake Neusiedl, which is a UNESCO World Heritage site. Neusiedl am See is a popular summer destination due to its warm and dry climate, as well as its close proximity to Vienna. In this article, we will provide you with a comprehensive tour guide for Neusiedl am See, featuring a list of places to visit, hiking/biking routes, food, and drink scene, and much more.
