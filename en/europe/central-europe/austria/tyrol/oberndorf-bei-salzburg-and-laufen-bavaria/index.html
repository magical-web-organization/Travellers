Introduction to Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria)

Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria) are located in the Tyrol region, in Austria, Central Europe. The region is famous for its picturesque landscapes, stunning natural beauty, and rich cultural heritage. Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria) are well-known for their historic, cultural, and natural attractions that bring tourists to the area.

This comprehensive tour guide will help you plan your adventure to Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria) with information on places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more!

List of Places to Visit in Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Silent Night Memorial Chapel

One of the most popular tourist destinations in the area is the Silent Night Memorial Chapel, located in Oberndorf bei Salzburg. The chapel was built in honor of the famous Christmas carol, Silent Night, which was first sung in the nearby village of Arnsdorf. The chapel features an exhibition that provides an insight into the history of the carol and its writer, Franz Xaver Gruber.

Laufen Castle

Another must-see attraction in the area is Laufen Castle, located in the Bavarian town of Laufen. The castle was built in the 11th century and has been refurbished, preserving its original architecture. The castle houses a museum that exhibits various artifacts, showcasing the history of the castle and the Bavarian region.

Natural Attractions of Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kaiser Mountains

The Kaiser Mountains, also known as Kaisergebirge, are a mountain range located near Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria). The area offers plenty of opportunities for hiking, biking, and other outdoor activities. The scenic views of the mountains and valleys are breathtaking and worth exploring.

Lake Chiemsee

Lake Chiemsee is another popular destination for tourists in the area. The lake is located near Laufen and is one of the largest lakes in Germany. The lake offers opportunities for swimming, boating, and fishing. The lake is surrounded by beautiful forests and hills, making it a perfect place for hiking and biking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria)

Hiking Trails

Geigelstein

The Geigelstein is a mountain located near Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria). There are multiple hiking trails in the area leading to the summit of the mountain. The trails offer scenic views of the surrounding landscapes and are perfect for a day hike.

Biking Trails

Chiemsee-Radweg

The Chiemsee-Radweg is a biking trail that runs along Lake Chiemsee. The trail is approximately 62 km long, and it offers scenic views of the lake and the surrounding areas. The trail is suitable for both beginners and experienced bikers.

Best Things to Do in Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria): Activities and Tours

Salzach River Tour

The Salzach River flows through both Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria). A tour of the river is an excellent opportunity to explore the scenic beauty of the area and learn about the history of the Salzach River.

Food and Drink Tour

A food and drink tour is a unique way to experience the culinary richness of the Tyrol region. The tour takes you through various eateries and breweries, providing an opportunity to sample delicious regional cuisine and beverages.

Food and Drink Scene of Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria)

Must-Try Dishes

Weißwurst

Weißwurst, also known as white sausage, is a traditional Bavarian dish that is a must-try when visiting the area. The sausage is made of veal and pork and is usually served with sweet mustard and a pretzel.

Kaiserschmarrn

Kaiserschmarrn is a popular dessert in the Tyrol region. The dish is made of shredded pancakes, raisins, and almonds, and is usually served with plum compote.

Local Beverages

Chiemsee Beer

Chiemsee Beer is a local beer that is brewed in the Bavarian town of Urfahrn. The beer is made of natural ingredients and has a unique taste that is worth trying.

Apfelkorn

Apfelkorn is a popular apple liqueur that is produced in Germany. The drink is made of apples and has a sweet and tart taste that is refreshing on a hot day.

Local Culture and Customs of Oberndorf bei Salzburg and Laufen (Bavaria)

Krampus

Krampus is a cultural tradition that is celebrated in the area during December. Krampus is a character that is depicted as a demonic creature with horns and fur. The tradition involves parades and performances that attract tourists from all over the world.
