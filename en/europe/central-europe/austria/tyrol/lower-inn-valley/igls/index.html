Introduction to Igls

Igls is a captivating village nestled in the Lower Inn Valley in Tyrol - the heart of the Austrian Alps in Central Europe. The area is home to the renowned Igls-Olympia Ski resort, which has hosted two Winter Olympics in the past as well as the annual Four Hills Tournament. The village is a popular summer retreat, known for its scenic hiking and biking trails, natural hot springs, and historic landmarks. Igls offers a perfect fusion of Austrian traditions and modern amenities, making it an ideal tourist destination.

List of Places to Visit in Igls

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Igls is packed with an array of museums and historical landmarks. Below are some prominent ones worth visiting:

1. Igls Parish Church

The Igls Parish Church is a remarkable piece of Baroque art that dates back to the 18th century. It features two towers and a beautiful interior adorned with chandeliers and frescoes.

2. FIS Igl-Silzum Ski Museum

The FIS Museum is dedicated to the history of winter sports and skiing in the region. Exhibitions showcase photographs, equipment, and other memorabilia dating back to the 1800s.

Natural Attractions of Igls: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Igls has plenty of natural attractions for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some popular ones:

1. Patscherkofel Mountain

Patscherkofel is one of the most beautiful mountains in Austria, towering at 2,247 meters above sea level. This landmark has a broad range of hiking and biking trails and panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

2. Lanser See

Lanser See is a crystalline blue lake surrounded by a dense forest. It’s renowned for its water sports facilities, including swimming, paddle boating, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Igls

Igls is a fantastic destination for active travelers, featuring scenic hiking and biking trails. Below are some hiking and biking routes in Igls:

1. Patscher Alm

This hiking trail is a gentler approach to Patscherkofel Mountain. The route provides hikers with a stunning view of the picturesque landscape.

2. Isar River Bike Path

The flat terrain makes the Isar River Bike Path an easy ride for those who enjoy leisurely cycling. It runs for approximately 480 km from Scharnitz to Munich throughout a stunning Alpine backdrop.

Best Things to Do in Igls: Activities and Tours

Igls is an outdoorsy resort town that offers a range of activities and tours. Below are some best things to do in Igls:

1. Olympia Bob run

The Olympia Bob run is a popular attraction in Igls. The ride offers a hair-raising experience, with speeds reaching up to 100 km/h.

2. Swarovski Crystal Worlds

The Swarovski Crystal World is a fun family day out. The exhibition showcases an extensive range of crystals incorporated into designs and artwork to form intricate masterpieces.

Food and Drink Scene of Igls

Must-Try Dishes

Austrian cuisine is a mix of local and international flavors with an abundance of cheese, meat, and vegetables. Below are some dishes worth trying in Igls:

1. Wiener Schnitzel

A schnitzel made from breaded veal and served with a side of potato salad.

2. Gulash

A hearty stew made from beef meat cooked slowly with potatoes and served with a side of bread.

Local Beverages

Austria is famous for its beer, wines, and schnapps. Below are a few local beverages which you must try:

1. Weißbier

Weißbier or weizenbier is a wheat beer delicacy crafted with barley malt and yeast. Austrian’s brewing tradition dates back 4,000 years, making it one of the oldest brewing cultures in the world.

2. Schnapps

Austria is known for its premium schnapps made from fruit distillates.

Where to Stay in Igls: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Igls offers a range of accommodation options. Here are some of the best:

Gasthof Hirschen

Gasthof Hirschen is a charming 3-star family-run hotel in the heart of Igls’s town center. Guests can enjoy a range of amenities, including a spa and wellness area.

Hotel Bon Alpina

A 4-star hotel located at the foot of the Patscherkofel mountain; the hotel features a restaurant, indoor pool, and sauna.

What to Buy in Igls: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Igls has a wide range of souvenirs and local products to buy. Below are some popular ones:

1. Swarovski Crystal

Swarovski crystals are renowned globally for their dazzling designs and high-quality crystals.

2. Handcrafted Wood Products

Woodcarving is common in Igls, and visitors can find intricate handcrafted clocks and sculptures.

Local Culture and Customs of Igls

Austrian culture places an emphasis on friendliness and respect. Visitors are generally expected to be polite and respectful towards locals.

Best Time to Visit Igls

The best time to visit Igls is between May -September, ideal for hiking and biking in the warm weather. If you prefer biking and skiing, the winter months between December-February are the best months to visit.

Do’s and Don’ts of Igls

Do:

  • Try and speak German
  • Carry cash with you
  • Respect the environment

Don’t:

  • Purchase goods sold by street vendors
  • Enter quiet zones of public transportation
  • Forget to dress respectfully in places of worship

Safety and Health

Safety in Igls

Igls is a safe place to visit and crime rates are low.

Health in Igls

It is always essential to travel with health insurance. The European Health Insurance Card provides access to state-financed healthcare in case of a medical emergency.

Getting to Igls: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Igls

Innsbruck Airport is the closest airport to Igls, located about a 15-minute drive away from the village center. Regular buses and taxis run from the airport to Igls.

Bus Stations Near Igls

Bus services are regular, and the bus station is located at few minutes walk from the village center in Igls.

Other Options

Train services from Innsbruck station also run regularly to the nearby towns. Cars can also be rented.

How to Get Around in Igls

The best way to get around is on foot or by hiring a bike.

Where to Go Next from Igls: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Salzburg

Salzburg is an hour and a half drive from Igls. The beautiful city is a UNESCO World Heritage Site, with its baroque architecture, cobbled streets and museums.

Vienna

Vienna is about a four-hour drive from Igls. The city is home to stunning architecture, art, music, and delicious cuisine.

Conclusion

Igls is an ideal destination for people who love outdoor activities, rich cultural experiences, and immersing themselves in history. The village is a year-round destination, with something to offer visitors regardless of the season. With its beautiful scenery, food, and unique character, it’s time to add Igls to your travel list.
