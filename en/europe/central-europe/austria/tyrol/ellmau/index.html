Introduction to Ellmau

Nestled in the heart of Tyrol, Austria, Ellmau is a charming tourist destination. Known for its towering mountains, lush greenery, and stunning scenery, Ellmau attracts visitors from around the world.

Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, culture buff, or foodie, there’s something for everyone in Ellmau. With its pleasant weather, welcoming locals, and serene ambiance, Ellmau is the perfect destination for a relaxing vacation.

In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ll highlight the top places to visit, best activities to do, where to stay, and local customs and traditions.

List of Places to Visit in Ellmau

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Ellmau Parish Church: Built over 200 years ago, the Ellmau Parish Church is a must-visit attraction. Admire the intricate frescoes and ornate altar.
  • Bergdoktorhaus: Home to the famous TV series “Bergdoktor,” this house allows visitors to explore the filming location and learn more about the show’s history.
  • KaiserBad Ellmau: A thermal bath complex which has a wide range of activities for adults and children.

Natural Attractions of Ellmau: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Wilder Kaiser Mountain: Known for its rugged terrain and stunning vistas, Wilder Kaiser Mountain is a sight to behold. Hike or bike its many trails or climb to the summit for breathtaking views.
  • Going-to-the-Sun Road: This scenic byway provides a picturesque drive through some of the most beautiful scenery in Austria.
  • Ellmauer Alm: This rustic mountain hut offers delicious food and stunning views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ellmau

  • The Adlerweg: This famous Austrian hiking trail passes through Ellmau and offers stunning views of the scenery along the way.
  • The Goinger Wand: This popular hiking and biking trail is known for its scenic views and challenging terrain.
  • The Kaiserkrone: A multi-day hiking route that circumnavigates the Wilder Kaiser Mountain.

Best Things to Do in Ellmau: Activities and Tours

  • Skiing/Snowboarding: Ellmau is home to SkiWelt, one of the largest interconnected ski areas in Austria.
  • Golfing: Gamble on the golf course at the Golf Club Wilder Kaiser.
  • Lake Adventure: Lake Going is a popular summer destination for swimming, boating, and beaches.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ellmau

Day Itinerary
Day 1 Explore the town and visit the Bergdoktorhaus.
Day 2 Take a scenic drive on the Going-to-the-Sun Road.
Day 3 Hike Wilder Kaiser Mountain and visit Ellmauer Alm.
Day 4 Enjoy skiing/snowboarding at SkiWelt.
Day 5 Take a break and enjoy the thermal spa at KaiserBad Ellmau.
Day 6 Go golfing at the Golf Club Wilder Kaiser.
Day 7 Relax and unwind at Lake Going.

Food and Drink Scene of Ellmau

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kaiserschmarrn: This Austrian classic is a sweet, fluffy pancake served with fruit compote.
  • Tiroler Speckknödel: A local dumpling filled with speck and served in a delicious broth.
  • Apfelstrudel: A sweet pastry filled with apples and cinnamon.

Local Beverages

  • Stiegl Beer: A popular Austrian beer that is brewed locally.
  • Schnapps: A traditional Austrian liqueur that comes in a variety of flavors.
  • Almdudler: A refreshing Austrian soft drink made from alpine herbs and fruit extracts.

Where to Stay in Ellmau: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Kaiserhof: A luxurious hotel with stunning views of the Wilder Kaiser Mountain.
  • Hotel Der Bär: A modern hotel with a spa and wellness center.
  • Bushauser: A traditional guesthouse with a rustic feel and homemade meals.

What to Buy in Ellmau: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Hand-carved wooden souvenirs
  • Tyrolean clothing and accessories
  • Local honey and cheese

Local Culture and Customs of Ellmau

  • Respect local traditions and customs, such as greeting others with a handshake or saying “Grüß Gott.”
  • Dress appropriately, especially when visiting churches or other religious sites.
  • Be mindful of noise levels and respect the local quiet hours.

Best Time to Visit Ellmau

The best time to visit Ellmau is during the summer months from May to September when the weather is warm and sunny, and outdoor activities like hiking, golfing, and swimming are in full swing. Winter is also an excellent time to visit for skiing and snowboarding.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ellmau

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Take care of the environment and leave no trace.
  • Try the local cuisine.

Don’t:

  • Wear inappropriate clothing in local churches or religious sites.
  • Make excessive noise during the nighttime quiet hours.
  • Litter or damage the natural environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ellmau

Ellmau is a safe destination, but it’s important to take precautions such as avoiding hiking or skiing alone, making sure to have appropriate gear and equipment, and paying attention to any posted signs or warnings.

Health in Ellmau

Healthcare facilities in Ellmau are excellent, with several hospitals and clinics available in the surrounding area. It’s always a good idea to have travel insurance in case of emergency.

Getting to Ellmau: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ellmau

The nearest airport to Ellmau is Salzburg (SZG), approximately 100 km away.

Bus Stations Near Ellmau

Buses run directly to Ellmau from major cities like Munich and Salzburg.

Other Options

Driving from major cities like Salzburg and Munich is also an option, as well as taking the train to nearby cities like Kufstein.

How to Get Around in Ellmau

The best way to get around Ellmau is by renting a car or using public transport. Buses run regularly between the major towns and cities, while taxi services are also available.

Where to Go Next from Ellmau: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Innsbruck: A charming Austrian city known for its stunning architecture and cultural attractions.
  • Salzburg: The picturesque birthplace of Mozart, known for its rich history and enchanting Eltz Castle.
  • Vienna: The stunning Austrian capital with breathtaking architecture, rich history, and excellent food and drink.

Conclusion

Ellmau offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures. With its welcoming locals, delicious food, and charming ambiance, it’s easy to see why this destination is popular with visitors from around the world.
