Introduction to Velden

Velden is a beautiful town located on the western shore of Lake Wörthersee, Carinthia, Austria. This picturesque lakeside town is known for its natural beauty, cultural significance, and luxurious lifestyle. Velden is an ideal destination for visitors looking for some relaxation, enjoying outdoor activities, exploring history and culture, indulging in fine cuisine, and spending their holidays in luxury accommodations. This comprehensive travel guide to Velden will give you all the necessary information about the town, offering valuable insights, recommendations, dos and don’ts, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Velden

Velden offers a plethora of attractions and activities that cater to various interests and preferences. In this section, we will explore the top places to visit in Velden.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Velden has a rich cultural heritage, and there are many museums and historical places to explore in the town.

  • Schloss Velden: It is a former palace that has been transformed into a five-star hotel. The palace was built in the 16th century and stands out for its architectural grandeur.
  • Schlosssternwarte: It is a small observatory that was built on the top of the Velden Castle. The observatory offers an excellent opportunity to get a glimpse of the stars and planets.
  • Adventurous Playground and Museum: It is an interactive museum that offers children and adults an opportunity to experience the natural and cultural history of the region in a fun and exciting way.

Natural Attractions of Velden: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Velden is surrounded by stunning natural beauty, and there are many ways to explore it. Here are some of the top natural attractions of Velden.

  • Lake Wörthersee: It is the largest lake in Carinthia and offers stunning views and opportunities to enjoy water activities like swimming, boating, and paddleboarding.
  • Pyramidenkogel: It is a tower that offers breathtaking views of Lake Wörthersee and the surrounding mountains.
  • Gerlitzen Alpe: It is a mountain range located near Velden and offers excellent hiking and biking opportunities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Velden

Velden offers excellent hiking and biking options for those looking to explore the area’s natural beauty and enjoy some outdoor adventure. Here are some of the popular hiking/biking routes in Velden.

  • The Wörthersee Rundwanderweg: A 55km trail around the Lake Wörthersee, which offers views of the lake, rolling hills, and forests.
  • The Keutschacher See: A 9km trail around the Keutschacher See, which takes you through the quaint villages and forests.

Best Things to Do in Velden: Activities and Tours

Velden offers numerous activities and tours for visitors to experience the town and its surroundings. Here are some of the best things to do in Velden.

  • Cruise on Lake Wörthersee: Taking a tour of the Lake Wörthersee offers visitors breathtaking views of the lake and the surrounding mountains.
  • Casino Velden: It is a luxurious casino that offers various games like poker, roulette, and slot machines.
  • Visit the Pink Villa: The Pink Villa is an iconic building that has been used as a backdrop for many movies and TV shows and offers stunning views of the Lake Wörthersee.

Food and Drink Scene of Velden

The food scene of Velden offers visitors an opportunity to indulge in the local cuisine and wine. Here are some of the top food and drink options in Velden.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kärntner Kasnudeln: It is a traditional Austrian dish made with pasta stuffed with potatoes and cheese.
  • Reindling: It is a sweet bread that is traditionally consumed during Christmas and Easter.
  • Carinthian Eiswein: It is a sweet wine made from frozen grapes.

Local Beverages

  • Weissburgunder: It is a local white wine that is dry with a fruity taste.
  • Rosé: It is a local pink wine that is refreshing and light.
  • Gortina: It is a local beer that is light and refreshing.

Where to Stay in Velden: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Velden offers many options for accommodations that cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the top options for accommodations in Velden.

  • Falkensteiner Schlosshotel Velden: It is a five-star hotel that offers luxurious amenities and breathtaking views of the lake.
  • Seehotel Europa: It is a four-star hotel that offers spacious accommodations and private beach access.
  • Villa Bulfon: It is a budget-friendly guest house that offers cozy rooms and a home-like atmosphere.

What to Buy in Velden: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to Velden can take home souvenirs and local products that offer a taste of the region’s culture and traditions. Here are some of the top souvenirs and local products to buy in Velden.

  • Carinthian Honey: It is a local honey that is produced in the region and is known for its unique taste.
  • Wörthersee Marmalade: It is a marmalade made from fruits grown in the region and offers a sweet and tangy flavor.
  • Handmade crafts: Visitors can buy handmade crafts like jewelry, pottery, and wooden items that are made by local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Velden

Velden is known for its luxurious lifestyle and bustling nightlife. Visitors to Velden should keep in mind the following customs and culture.

  • Dress code: Visitors should dress appropriately while visiting the town’s luxury hotels and restaurants.
  • Tipping: Tipping is customary in Austria, and visitors should tip their servers and tour guides.
  • Nightlife: Velden’s nightlife is vibrant and lively, with many bars and nightclubs that stay open till late.

Best Time to Visit Velden

The best time to visit Velden is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny, and the lake is an excellent place to cool off. Visitors can enjoy outdoor activities and explore the town’s natural beauty during this time.

Safety and Health

Visitors to Velden should keep in mind the following safety and health-related information while exploring the town.

Safety in Velden

Velden is a safe town, and visitors should take standard precautions like locking their valuables and belongings in their hotels and keeping an eye on their wallets and phones while in public areas.

Health in Velden

Visitors to Velden should carry their health insurance and carry necessary medication and precautions while traveling. The town has excellent medical facilities in case of an emergency.

Getting to Velden: Transportation Options and Logistics

Velden is well-connected to other parts of the country, and visitors can use various transportation options to reach the town.

Airports Near Velden

  • Klagenfurt Airport (KLU): It is the nearest airport to Velden, located just 32km away.
  • Ljubljana Jože Pučnik Airport (LJU): It is located 84km away, which is about an hour’s drive from Velden.

Bus Stations Near Velden

  • Velden am Wörther See, Zentrum: It is the central bus station in Velden.
  • Klagenfurt Hauptbahnhof: It is the main bus station in Klagenfurt, located 22km away from Velden.

Other Options

  • Train: Visitors can also reach Velden by train. The nearest train station is located in Villach, about 20km away from Velden.

How to Get Around in Velden

Velden is a small town, and visitors can easily explore it on foot. However, there are other options available for visitors who want to travel around the town.

  • Buses: Velden has a well-connected bus network that connects the town with nearby attractions and towns.
  • Taxis: Visitors can hire taxis to travel around Velden and nearby areas.
  • Bikes: Renting bicycles is an excellent option for visitors who want to explore the town’s surroundings and natural beauty.

Where to Go Next from Velden: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Velden’s central location makes it an ideal base camp for exploring the other nearby destinations. Here are some of the nearby destinations and excursions from Velden.

  • Klagenfurt: It is the capital city of Carinthia, located just 22km away from Velden.
  • Villach: It is a charming town located 20km away from Velden and is known for its thermal baths and natural beauty.
  • Border Triangle: This unique geographical point is where Austria, Slovenia, and Italy meet and is just a 40-minute drive from Velden.

Conclusion

Velden is a hidden gem in Austria and offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural significance, luxurious lifestyle, and vibrant nightlife. The town offers something for everyone and is an ideal destination for travelers who want to experience Austria’s unique culture and traditions.
