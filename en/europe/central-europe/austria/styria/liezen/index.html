Introduction to Liezen

Liezen is a picturesque town in the Styria region of Austria, located in Central Europe. It is located in a valley surrounded by mountains and is known for its beautiful landscapes, fresh air, and friendly locals. If you are looking for a relaxing vacation away from the hustle and bustle of the city, Liezen is the perfect destination for you. This travel guide will help you plan your trip to Liezen by providing you with all the information you need to make the most out of your visit.

List of Places to Visit in Liezen

Liezen has a lot to offer when it comes to places to visit. From museums and historical sites to natural attractions, there is something for everyone. Here are some of the must-visit places in Liezen:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Schloss Trautenfels - a beautiful castle located on a hill overlooking the Enns Valley. It houses the Landesmuseum Joanneum, which showcases the history of the region.
  2. Gothic Church - built in the 14th century, this church is an excellent example of Gothic architecture.
  3. Bürgeralm Museum - a museum that showcases the traditional lifestyle and culture of the local people.

Natural Attractions of Liezen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Dachstein Massif - a beautiful mountain range that offers spectacular views of the surrounding landscape. You can go hiking and take scenic cable car rides.
  2. Grimming Thermal Spa - a natural hot spring that offers relaxing thermal baths and saunas.
  3. Liezen Wildlife Park - a zoo that houses various animals like deer, bears, eagles, and wild cats.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Liezen

Liezen is an ideal destination for those who love outdoor activities like hiking and biking. Some of the best hiking/ biking trails in Liezen are:

  1. Enns Cycle Path - a 250 km-long trail that follows the Enns River from its source to the Danube.
  2. Gesäuse National Park - a protected area that offers hiking trails of different difficulty levels and amazing views of the surrounding landscape.
  3. Grüner See - a beautiful lake surrounded by mountains that offers hiking trails and beautiful views.

Best Things to Do in Liezen: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, there are plenty of other things to do in Liezen. Here are some of the best activities and tours you can take:

  1. Rafting on the Salza River - a thrilling adventure that takes you through the rapids of the Salza River.
  2. Winery tours - Liezen is known for its excellent wines, and visiting the vineyards is a must.
  3. Ski tour to Tote Gebirge - Liezen has some of the best ski slopes in Austria, and a ski tour to the Tote Gebirge is a must for every ski enthusiast.

Week-Long Itinerary for Liezen

If you are planning to spend a week in Liezen, here is an itinerary that will help you get the most out of your trip:

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit Schloss Trautenfels and go hiking in the Dachstein Massif.
Day 2 Take a winery tour and visit the Bürgeralm Museum.
Day 3 Go rafting on the Salza River and visit the Enns Cycle Path.
Day 4 Visit the Liezen Wildlife Park and take a ski tour to Tote Gebirge.
Day 5 Take a relaxing thermal bath in the Grimming Thermal Spa.
Day 6 Visit the Gothic Church and go hiking in the Gesäuse National Park.
Day 7 Visit Grüner See and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

Food and Drink Scene of Liezen

Liezen is known for its excellent cuisine and local wines. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Schilcher - a local wine made from the Blauer Wildbacher grape.
  2. Steirerkas - a traditional cheese made from the milk of mountain goats.
  3. Geselchtes - a smoked meat dish that is a local specialty.

Local Beverages

  1. Schilcher - a local wine made from the Blauer Wildbacher grape.
  2. Styrian Schnapps - a brandy made from locally grown fruit.
  3. Austrian Beer - Austria is known for its excellent beer, and Liezen is no exception.

Where to Stay in Liezen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Liezen has a range of accommodations to suit all budgets. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  1. Hotel Schloss Kassegg - a charming hotel located in a castle that dates back to the 13th century.
  2. Hotel Erzherzog Johann - a modern hotel that offers excellent views of the surrounding mountains.
  3. Jufa Hotel Liezen - a family-friendly hotel that is located in the town center.

What to Buy in Liezen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Liezen has a range of souvenirs and local products that you can bring home as a reminder of your trip. Here are some of the best things to buy:

  3. Handmade Crafts - the region of Styria is known for its handmade crafts like pottery and woodcraft.

Local Culture and Customs of Liezen

The people of Liezen are friendly and welcoming to tourists. However, it is important to respect their local customs and culture. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  1. Dress modestly when visiting churches and other religious places.
  2. Tipping is not mandatory but appreciated.
  3. Smoking is prohibited in most public places.

Best Time to Visit Liezen

The best time to visit Liezen is from May to September when the weather is pleasant and perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Liezen

Do:

  1. Try the local cuisine and wines.
  2. Respect the local customs and culture.
  3. Enjoy nature and outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  1. Smoke in public places.
  2. Wear revealing clothes when visiting religious places.
  3. Leave trash in public places.

Safety and Health

Safety in Liezen

Liezen is a safe destination for tourists. However, it is always better to be cautious and take necessary safety measures like not leaving valuables unattended.

Health in Liezen

Liezen has good medical facilities, and the water is safe to drink.

Getting to Liezen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Liezen is well-connected by road, rail, and air. Here are some of the ways to get to Liezen:

Airports Near Liezen

  1. Salzburg Airport - located 140 km from Liezen.
  2. Graz Airport - located 120 km from Liezen.

Bus Stations Near Liezen

Liezen has good bus connections with other cities and towns in Austria.

Other Options

You can also rent a car or hire a taxi to reach Liezen from the nearby airports or bus stations.

How to Get Around in Liezen

Liezen is a small town, and you can easily explore it on foot or by bike. There are also buses that run in and around the town.

Conclusion

Liezen is a charming town that offers something for everyone. It is an excellent destination for those who love nature, outdoor activities, and local culture. This comprehensive travel guide will help you plan your trip to Liezen and make the most out of your visit.
