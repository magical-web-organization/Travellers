A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Mürren, Switzerland

Nestled in the Bernese Highlands of Switzerland, Mürren exemplifies the breathtaking beauty that makes Switzerland famous. Situated in Central Europe, Mürren is a peaceful Alpine village that offers tourists an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. If you’re looking for picturesque landscapes, outdoor adventures, or cultural immersion, Mürren should be at the top of your travel list!

List of Places to Visit in Mürren

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Mürren has a rich history that has been displayed throughout the town’s architecture and design. If you want to immerse yourself in the culture of Mürren, you must visit these museums and historical places:

  • Allmendhubel Funicular: This iconic funicular was built-in 1907 and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains. You can also visit a Restaurant and an open-air cinema in summer.

  • Mürrenbachfall: These waterfalls descend 417 feet and offer visitors picturesque views. Experience the beauty and sounds of the natural scenery.

  • Piz Gloria: The revolving restaurant sits atop the 2,970-meter Schilthorn Peak. At here, you can have a meal with some of the most stunning views in Switzerland. As the restaurant revolves, you get a 360° view of Jungfrau Region view.

Natural Attractions of Mürren: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Mürren is surrounded by some of the most stunning scenery in the world. Whether you’re interested in hiking, biking, skiing, or simply enjoying the views, Mürren has something for everyone. Here are some of the must-see natural attractions of Mürren:

  • Stechelberg Valley: Take a stroll through this idyllic valley that offers views of the mountains and the Lauterbrunnen Valley. There are also hiking and biking trails for those who want a more active experience.

  • Winteregg-Kleine Scheidegg hiking trail: With the stunning view of mountains, take this scenic and popular hiking trail which is 30-60 minutes long. It is perfect for those who want to enjoy the Alps on a gentler route.

  • Schilthornbahn: Take a cable car and enjoy breathtaking views of the Jungfrau region. The cable car climbs to about 360-degree views where you’ll find Piz Gloria - a revolving restaurant.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mürren

Mürren offers some of the best hiking and biking routes in Switzerland. Here are some of the must-try routes:

  • Eiger Trail: This route offers a stunning view of the Eiger and Mönch mountains. During the walk, you can expect to see glaciers, waterfalls, and charming Swiss towns that will surely take your breath away.

  • Mürren-Schilthorn Via Ferrata: Considered one of the best Via Ferratas in the Alps, this route offers an adrenaline-pumping, adventurous experience. Here you can enjoy panoramic views, experience tight-rope walking above hidden lakes.

  • Mürren to Gimmelwald: This 1.5 hours hiking route offers cloud-scraping views of the Alps and starts from Mürren which eventually takes you to Gimmelwald.

Best Things to Do in Mürren: Activities and Tours

Mürren offers a range of activities and tours for all types of travelers. Here are some of the best things to do for a memorable holiday in Mürren:

  • Skiing: Mürren has numerous exceptional skiing trails that are perfect for anyone looking for a winter wonderland experience. You can get your ski pass from any of the local ski rental shops.

  • Paragliding: It is one of the best activities to have an aerial view of the Alps. The stunning aerial views of the Swiss Alps will surely a lifetime expericence.

  • The Mürrenbach Falls & Via Ferrata: Experience the beauty of nature at the Mürrenbach falls.

Week-Long Itinerary for Mürren

Mürren is a perfect scenic town for a relaxing holiday as well as adventurous ones. Below is a week-long itinerary for the best experience in Mürren:

Day 1: Arrival and visit to the Allmendhubel Funicular

Day 2: Hike to the Winteregg-Kleine Scheidegg trail

Day 3: Paragliding

Day 4: Visit Mürrenbachfall

Day 5: Visit the Eiger Trail

Day 6: Skiing

Day 7: Visit Piz Gloria

Food and Drink Scene of Mürren

Swiss cuisine is delicious and the same goes for Mürren’s dishes. Here are some of the food and drinks that you must try while visiting Mürren:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fondue: Most iconic dish of Swiss cuisine. It is made up of melted cheese and served with bread and vegetables.

  • Rösti: It is a Swiss-style hash brown that is crispy on the outside, soft on the inside. You can have it with any meat dish or as a vegetarian option.

Local Beverages

  • Heidi Beer: A Swiss beer made from barley and hops that you can enjoy with every meal.

  • Kirschwasser: A fruit brandy that is made from morello cherries.

Where to Stay in Mürren: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Mürren has several options for accommodation. Whether you’re looking for a luxurious stay or a budget stay, you’re sure to find something that suits you. Here are our top recommendations:

  • Hotel Bellevue: Offering breath-taking views of the Swiss Alps, Hotel Bellevue is one of the best accommodations in Mürren. The hotel is conveniently located 200 meters from the Mürren train station.

  • Eiger Guesthouse: This option offers budget accommodation and a friendly environment. Eiger Guesthouse is only 450 meters from Mürren railway station.

  • Alpenrose Hotel: A 4-star hotel that offers its guests a relaxing ambiance with high-quality services.

What to Buy in Mürren: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Switzerland is famous for its chocolates and cheese, but here are some exclusive souvenirs that you can bring back from Mürren:

  • Swiss Watches: Switzerland is known for its luxury watchmaker business. You can find exclusive luxury watches that take craftsmanship to a new level.

  • Bells on neck straps and key rings: Great gift for cow lovers. You can find the hand-crafted traditional cowbells from Mürren.

  • Swiss Clothing: You can buy Swiss clothing such as woolen blankets, hats, and scarfs.

Local Culture and Customs of Mürren

Switzerland has a rich cultural history, and Mürren has its own cultural customs. Mürren’s customs include yodeling, alpine horn blowing, and Swiss paper-cutting. Here are some things to know about local culture in Mürren:

  • Swiss are punctual, so always be on time.

  • When greeting someone, shake hands.

  • Residents respect silence, so keep the noise down, especially at night.

Best Time to Visit Mürren

Mürren is a year-round destination. During winters, you can enjoy skiing and during summers, you can indulge in hiking and paragliding. Below are the ideal times for different activities:

  • Summer: June to August are the best months for summer activities and tours.

  • Autumn: September to November, you can enjoy winter sports in the glorious Alps.

  • Winter: December to February is the best time for skiing.

  • Spring: From March to May, as the snow starts to melt, enjoy the town’s bright flora and fauna.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mürren

Do:

  • Respect the Swiss culture and customs.

  • Wear comfortable shoes when hiking.

  • Keep your surroundings clean.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter while enjoying the scenery.

  • Don’t wear loud clothing that disturbs the peaceful surroundings.

  • Don’t forget to carry cash as some shops in Mürren do not accept credit/debit cards.

Safety and Health

Safety in Mürren

Mürren is considered a safe town, but visitors should always take precautions, such as:

  • Not wandering off the trail while hiking.

  • Keep an eye on their belongings.

  • Follow the rules of the funicular, cable car, chairlift, and ski lift.

Health in Mürren

Visitors should follow standard health guidelines to avoid getting sick, such as:

  • Drink bottled mineral water only. Water from taps can be consumed, but it may taste bitter.

  • Wear sunscreen with high SPF when skiing.

  • Seek emergency medical services if required.

Getting to Mürren: Transportation Options and Logistics

Mürren is well-connected to major Swiss cities and towns. Here are some ideas for transportation options:

Airports Near Mürren

  • Zurich Airport: 187 km from Mürren.

  • Geneva Airport: 231 km from Mürren.

  • Bern-Belp Airport: Approximately 60 km from Mürren.

Bus Stations Near Mürren

  • Lauterbrunnen Bus Station: The station is a 20-minute drive from Mürren on public transport and connects you to the whole of the Lauterbrunnen valley by Yellow PostBus and local buses.

Other Options

  • Train station in Mürren: Mürren Railway Station is the closest to the town area. To reach here, you have to take a cogwheel railway from Lauterbrunnen or cable car from Stechelberg.

How to Get Around in Mürren

While Mürren is a walkable town, visitors can use public transport to get around when they don’t feel like trekking. The most common modes of transportation in Mürren are cable cars, the cogwheel trains, and buses.

Where to Go Next from Mürren: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Mürren offers easy access to some great destinations nearby. Here are some of the places you might want to visit:

  • Lauterbrunnen Valley: Take a bus to Lauterbrunnen, a gorgeous Swiss valley that will take you back in time.

  • Grindelwald: It is a famous Alpine village located near Mürren that offers skiing and hiking trails with panoramic views.

  • Gstaad: This posh village offers upscale shopping and dining as well as ski resorts and adventure activities.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventure, stunning views, or cultural immersion, Mürren is an excellent destination for any traveler. With so much to offer, you’re sure to have a lifetime experience here. So, pack your bags and book your holiday to Mürren now!
