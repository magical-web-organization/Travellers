Introduction to Vals

Located in the beautiful canton of Graubünden in Switzerland, Vals is a charming village that offers a perfect balance of natural beauty, adventure, culture, and history. Surrounded by stunning mountains and idyllic landscapes, Vals is a paradise for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. In this comprehensive travel guide to Vals, we will take a closer look at the best things to do, places to stay, and where to go in this beautiful village in Switzerland.

List of Places to Visit in Vals

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Vals has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in the various museums and historical places in the village. Here are some of the top cultural attractions in Vals:

  • Vals Thermal Baths: This beautiful thermal bath complex is not only a great place to relax but also a great example of modern Swiss architecture.
  • Talmuseum: A museum that showcases the rich cultural heritage of Vals and the surrounding region.
  • St. Martin’s Church: A beautiful 16th-century church that features stunning frescoes and Baroque architecture.
  • Gadenstatt: A historical site that offers a glimpse into the life of the Walser people who have lived in the region for over 700 years.

Natural Attractions of Vals: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Vals is well known for its natural beauty and stunning landscapes, making it a haven for outdoor activities and adventures. Here are some of the top natural attractions in Vals:

  • Valsertal: A scenic valley in the heart of Vals that offers hiking trails with breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Piz Mundaun: A popular ski resort in Vals that features 30 km of ski slopes and a stunning panoramic view of the Alps.
  • Aqua Viva: A breathtaking waterfall that cascades down the rocks, offering a perfect spot for hiking and photography.
  • Graubünden: The Land of 1000 Peaks: This mountain range offers an array of winter sports opportunities, including skiing, snowboarding, ice-skating, and more.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Vals

Vals offers several hiking and biking routes that cater to all levels of difficulty, from easy to advanced. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Vals:

  • Valserberg Panorama Trail: A scenic hiking trail that offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and a glimpse into the local flora and fauna.
  • Zervreilasee Lake Trail: A family-friendly hiking trail that offers stunning views of the Zervreilasee Lake and its beautiful surroundings.
  • Vals Bike Trail: A popular mountain biking trail that winds through the beautiful landscapes of Vals and offers several exciting challenges along the way.

Best Things to Do in Vals: Activities and Tours

Vals offers an array of activities and tours that cater to all interests and age groups. Here are some of the best things to do in Vals:

  • Vals Thermal Baths: A perfect place to relax and unwind after a long day of sightseeing or adventure sports.
  • Snowshoeing: A unique way to explore the scenic beauty of Vals during the winter months.
  • Valser Bergbahnen: A cable car that takes you to the top of Piz Mundaun, offering stunning views of the Alps in the distance.
  • Archery: A fun-filled activity that allows you to indulge in some proper archery and channel your inner Robin Hood.

Food and Drink Scene of Vals

Must-Try Dishes

Vals has an authentic Swiss cuisine that boasts of traditional dishes made with fresh local ingredients. Here are some of the must-try dishes in Vals:

  • Pizokel: A hearty dish made with buckwheat flour and served with bacon and cheese.
  • Raclette: A cheese dish that is served with boiled potatoes, pickled onions, and gherkins.
  • Bündner Nusstorte: A delicious walnut pie made with sugar, butter, and flour.

Local Beverages

Vals has a variety of local beverages that you should sip on during your visit. Here are some of the top local beverages in Vals:

  • Bündner Bergbier: A locally brewed beer that is available in many bars and restaurants in Vals.
  • Alois Lageder wines: A family-owned winery in Vals that produces some of the finest wines in Switzerland.

Where to Stay in Vals: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Vals offers a variety of accommodation options that cater to all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the top places to stay in Vals:

  • Valsana Hotel and Apartments: A luxurious hotel that offers modern amenities and breathtaking mountain views.
  • Gästehaus Balma: A comfortable guesthouse that offers excellent value for money and a central location in Vals.
  • Jugendherberge Vals: A budget-friendly hostel that offers basic amenities and easy access to the skiing and hiking trails.

What to Buy in Vals: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some of the souvenirs and local products that you can buy in Vals:

  • Bündner Nusstorte: A delicious walnut pie that makes for a great souvenir or gift.
  • Swiss Chocolate: Switzerland is known for its premium quality chocolates, and you can buy some of the finest Swiss chocolates in Vals.
  • Handwoven Woolen Scarves: The Walser people in Vals are known for their intricate weaving techniques, and you can buy handwoven woolen scarves and other handicrafts as souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Vals

Vals has a rich cultural heritage, and the locals are proud of their customs and traditions. Here are some of the local customs and traditions in Vals:

  • Walser Fasnacht: A traditional carnival that is celebrated in Vals in February or March.
  • Walser Sennenfest: A festival that is celebrated in Vals in May to honor the legacy of the Walser people.
  • Walserherbst: A cultural festival that is celebrated in Vals in September and October.

  • Hiking and Biking: The best time to visit Vals for hiking and biking is from May to October.

Safety and Health

Safety in Vals

Vals is a relatively safe village, but it is always advisable to be cautious, especially when participating in adventure sports. Here are some safety tips for your visit to Vals:

  • Wear appropriate gear and clothes when participating in adventure sports.
  • Follow the safety instructions provided by operators when indulging in adventure sports.
  • Be aware of the weather conditions and check the forecast before participating in outdoor activities.

Health in Vals

Vals has a good healthcare system, and medical facilities are easily accessible. However, it is recommended to have travel insurance, especially if you plan to participate in adventure sports. Here are some health tips for your visit to Vals:

  • Stay hydrated and drink plenty of fluids, especially during the summer months.
  • Wear sunscreen and protect yourself from the sun’s harmful rays.
  • Be aware of altitude sickness if you plan to indulge in adventure sports at higher altitudes.
