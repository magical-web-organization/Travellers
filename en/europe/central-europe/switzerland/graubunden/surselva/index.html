Introduction to Surselva

Surselva is a picturesque valley located in the Graubünden region of Switzerland. The region is known for its natural beauty, outdoor activities, and rich cultural heritage.

The valley is home to several small towns and villages, each with its unique charm and character. Visitors can explore medieval castles, hike through breathtaking mountain landscapes, taste authentic Swiss cuisine, and experience the traditional Swiss way of life.

In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit in Surselva, including natural and cultural attractions, hiking and biking trails, where to stay, what to eat and drink and much more.

Local Culture and Customs of Surselva

Surselva is heavily influenced by Swiss German culture and traditions. Visitors should familiarize themselves with the following customs:

  • Greeting locals using “Gruezi”
  • Removing shoes before entering homes
  • Tipping is not expected, as service charges are already included in the bill

Best Time to Visit Surselva

Surselva is a year-round destination, with each season offering its unique charm and appeal. Visitors can enjoy the stunning views of the snow-capped mountains in winter or hike through the lush valleys in the summer.
