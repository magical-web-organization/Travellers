Introduction to Safiental

Nestled in the heart of the Swiss Alps, Safiental is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. The valley is renowned for its pristine natural beauty and unspoiled landscapes, making it the perfect destination for those seeking an authentic alpine experience. In this travel guide, we’ll explore the best places to visit, things to do, and local tips for making the most of your stay in Safiental.

List of Places to Visit in Safiental

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Safiental is home to several museums and historical landmarks that provide a glimpse into the region’s rich cultural heritage. Some of the must-visit attractions include:

  • Safien Platz Museum: This museum chronicles the history and traditions of Safiental, showcasing the valley’s flora and fauna, ancient crafts, and traditional costumes.

  • Bergkirche St. Martin: This 17th-century church is a fine example of Baroque architecture and houses a stunning collection of frescoes.

Natural Attractions of Safiental: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The natural beauty of Safiental is second to none, offering a wide range of outdoor activities and breathtaking views. Some of the most popular natural attractions and activities in Safiental include:

  • Crestasee Lake: This pristine alpine lake is surrounded by towering mountains and dense forests, making it the perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, or picnicking.

  • Flimserstein: At 2,402 meters, Flimserstein is one of the highest peaks in Safiental and offers stunning panoramic views of the valley below.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Safiental

Safiental is a paradise for hikers and bikers, with numerous trails and routes to explore. Some of the best hiking and biking trails in the area include:

  • Murschetg Trail: This 7-kilometer trail winds through the forests and meadows surrounding the village of Murschetg, offering stunning views of the valley and surrounding mountains.

  • Rofla Trail: This 12-kilometer hiking trail leads to the Rofla Gorge, a spectacular natural wonder with towering cliffs and cascading waterfalls.

Best Things to Do in Safiental: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are plenty of other exciting things to see and do in Safiental, from adrenaline-pumping adventures to cultural experiences. Some of the best things to do in Safiental include:

  • Safien Platz Cheese Dairy: This family-owned dairy produces some of the finest artisanal cheeses in the region, using traditional methods passed down from generation to generation.

  • Rafting on the Vorderrhein: For thrill-seekers, rafting on the Vorderrhein River is a must-do experience, offering an exhilarating ride through the heart of the Swiss Alps.

Week-Long Itinerary for Safiental

If you’re planning a week-long stay in Safiental, here’s a sample itinerary to help you make the most of your trip:

Day Activities
1 Arrive in Chur, take the bus to Safien Platz, visit the Safien Platz Museum
2 Hike the Murschetg Trail, visit the Bergkirche St. Martin
3 Visit the Safien Platz Cheese Dairy, go rafting on the Vorderrhein
4 Explore the Flimserstein peak, take in the panoramic views
5 Relax by Crestasee Lake, go swimming or sunbathing
6 Hike the Rofla Trail to the Rofla Gorge and waterfalls
7 Depart Safiental via Chur

Food and Drink Scene of Safiental

Safiental has a rich culinary tradition that reflects the region’s alpine heritage and locally-sourced ingredients. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Capuns: A traditional Swiss dish made with Swiss chard and dough, rolled into a sausage shape and served in a creamy sauce.

  • Pizokel: A hearty noodle dish made with potatoes and flour, typically served with bacon and cheese.

Local Beverages

  • Braendli: A schnapps made from native fruits such as plums or apricots, distilled according to traditional methods.

  • Safientaler Bier: A locally brewed beer made with water from the Vorderrhein River, giving it a crisp, refreshing taste.

Where to Stay in Safiental: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Safiental offers a range of accommodation options to suit every budget and style, from cozy guesthouses to luxurious hotels. Here are some top options in the area:

  • Hotel Surselva: This elegant hotel combines traditional alpine architecture with modern amenities, offering comfortable rooms and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

  • Berggasthaus Piz Vizan: This peaceful guesthouse is nestled in the heart of the Safien Valley, offering rustic accommodations and traditional Swiss hospitality.

What to Buy in Safiental: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Safiental is famous for its artisanal products made with locally-sourced ingredients and traditional methods. Here are some top souvenirs and local products to bring home:

  • Safien Platz Cheese: This award-winning cheese is made from the milk of local cows and goats, and is renowned for its rich, nutty flavor.

  • Safientaler Bier: This locally brewed beer is the perfect souvenir for beer lovers, offering a taste of the region’s alpine heritage.

Local Culture and Customs of Safiental

Safiental has a unique cultural identity that is closely tied to its natural surroundings and alpine traditions. Some customs and traditions to be aware of include:

  • Alpabzug: This annual festival in the fall marks the return of the cattle from the alpine pastures to the valley, with parades and celebrations throughout the region.

  • Traditional Dress: Many locals in Safiental still wear traditional costumes, particularly during festivals and special occasions.

Best Time to Visit Safiental

The best time to visit Safiental depends on what type of experience you’re looking for. In the summer months (June to August), the weather is warm and pleasant, making it the perfect time for outdoor activities and adventures. In the winter months (December to February), the valley is transformed into a winter wonderland, with skiing, snowboarding, and other winter sports on offer.

Do’s and Don’ts of Safiental

Here are some important do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your stay in Safiental:

Do:

  • Respect Local Customs: Be mindful of local customs and traditions, and follow them respectfully.

  • Take Advantage of Outdoor Activities: Safiental is home to some of the most beautiful natural landscapes in Switzerland - be sure to take advantage of them!

Don’t:

  • Litter: Make sure to properly dispose of any trash or waste, and leave the area as you found it.

  • Disturb Wildlife: Safiental is home to a variety of wildlife, including protected species. Do not disturb or harm animals in any way.

Safety and Health

Safiental is a safe destination for travelers, with low crime rates and access to quality medical care. However, travelers should still take basic precautions such as locking doors and windows, not leaving valuables unattended, and carrying adequate health insurance.

Safety in Safiental

  • Weather: The weather in Safiental can change quickly, so be prepared for sudden changes in temperature and precipitation.

  • Nature: Safiental is home to a variety of wildlife, so be sure to take proper precautions when hiking or exploring the outdoors.

Health in Safiental

  • Altitude: Safiental is located at high altitude, so visitors may experience altitude sickness or other related health issues. Be sure to drink plenty of water and allow your body to acclimate to the altitude.

  • Medical Care: There is a medical center in nearby Falera, and emergency services are available throughout the region.

Getting to Safiental: Transportation Options and Logistics

Safiental is easily accessible by a variety of transportation options, including bus, train, and car. Here’s what you need to know about getting to and around Safiental:

Airports Near Safiental

  • Zurich Airport: Located approximately 2 hours 45 minutes away by car or public transport.

Bus Stations Near Safiental

  • Safien Platz Bus Station: Served by postbuses from Chur, Ilanz, and other nearby towns.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: If you prefer to drive, car rental is available at Zurich Airport and other major cities in Switzerland.

How to Get Around in Safiental

Public transport is the most convenient and eco-friendly way to get around Safiental, with regular bus services connecting Safien Platz with other towns and attractions in the region. There are also several taxi services available in the area, as well as bicycle rental options for those who prefer to explore on two wheels.

Where to Go Next from Safiental: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Safiental is located in the heart of Switzerland, making it an ideal base for exploring nearby attractions and destinations. Here are some top excursions and nearby destinations to consider:

  • Chur: The oldest city in Switzerland, with a charming historic center and plenty of cultural attractions.

  • Flims-Laax-Falera: A world-class ski and snowboard resort just a short drive from Safiental.

  • Rhine Gorge: The “Grand Canyon of Switzerland,” with stunning views and hiking trails along the Rhine River.

Conclusion

Safiental is a breathtakingly beautiful destination that offers visitors a chance to immerse themselves in alpine culture and natural beauty. From hiking and biking to cheese-making and beer-drinking, there’s something for everyone in Safiental. So pack your bags and get ready for an unforgettable adventure in the heart of the Swiss Alps!
