Introduction to Falera

Nestled in the Surselva region of the canton of Graubünden in Switzerland, Falera is a picturesque village surrounded by the majestic peaks of the Swiss Alps. Famous for its crystal-clear lakes, stunning mountain ranges, and cultural heritage, Falera is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, history buffs, and adventure seekers.

List of Places to Visit in Falera

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Falera has a rich cultural history that is apparent in its many museums and historical places. A few of the must-see attractions include:

  • Verbellin Tower: This well-preserved castle tower dates back to the 13th century and offers breathtaking views of the Rhine Valley.
  • Guler Kirchlein: A beautiful church built in the 12th century with stunning frescoes and an impressive Baroque altar.
  • Pfarrkirche St. Remigius: A church built in the 15th century that is still in use today, with a beautiful wooden altar and frescoes that date back to the 16th century.

Natural Attractions of Falera: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Falera is a hiker’s paradise, with some of the most stunning natural scenery in Switzerland. The top natural attractions in Falera include:

  • Laaxersee: A stunning alpine lake with crystal clear water surrounded by majestic mountains. The perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, and picnicking.
  • Piz Mundaun: Located at an altitude of 2067 meters, Piz Mundaun is an ideal spot for skiing, snowboarding, and snowshoeing in the wintertime. In the summer, the mountain offers breathtaking views and hiking trails.
  • Casa da Luzi: A traditional Swiss farmhouse turned restaurant, offering stunning views of the Rhine Valley and mouthwatering Swiss cuisine.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Falera

Falera has some of the best hiking and biking trails in Switzerland, with options for all skill levels. Some of the top routes include:

  • Crestasee to Conn: A beautiful hiking trail that takes you through stunning alpine scenery, with breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Laaxersee to Caumasee: A hiking trail that leads you through forests and alpine meadows, eventually reaching the stunning Caumasee lake.
  • Rhine Route: A cycling path that follows the banks of the sparkling Rhine river, with beautiful views and opportunities for picnicking and swimming.

Best Things to Do in Falera: Activities and Tours

There is no shortage of things to do in Falera, with something for everyone. Some of the best activities and tours include:

  • Skiing and Snowboarding: Falera is home to some of the best ski resorts in Switzerland, with slopes for all skill levels and breathtaking views.
  • Paragliding: A once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to take in the stunning Swiss Alps from above.
  • Cheese Tasting Tour: Sample some of Switzerland’s best cheeses with a tour of a traditional Swiss cheese factory.

Week-Long Itinerary for Falera

For a week-long stay in Falera, we recommend the following itinerary:

Food and Drink Scene of Falera

Must-Try Dishes

Falera has a rich culinary scene, offering traditional Swiss dishes that are a must-try for any visitor. Some of the top dishes include:

  • Fondue: A classic Swiss dish made from melted cheese and served with bread for dipping.
  • Rösti: A traditional Swiss dish made from grated potatoes, fried until crispy and served with a variety of toppings.
  • Bündnerfleisch: A type of air-dried beef that is a Swiss specialty, sliced thinly and served as a cold appetizer.

Local Beverages

Switzerland has a long history of beer-making, with some of the best breweries located in the Graubünden region. When in Falera, be sure to try some of the local beers, including the popular “Brauerei Flüeler” and “Bier Paul.”

Where to Stay in Falera: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Falera offers a variety of accommodations, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. Some of the best options include:

  • Rocksresort: A modern resort with stunning views and luxurious amenities, located in the heart of Laax.
  • Hotel Bellaval: A comfortable hotel with a cozy atmosphere, located in the heart of Falera.
  • Berghaus Nagens: A charming guesthouse with stunning views, located in the mountains above Falera.

What to Buy in Falera: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Falera is home to a variety of local products that make for great souvenirs. Be sure to check out:

  • Bündnerfleisch: A type of air-dried beef that is a Swiss specialty.
  • Handmade Swiss Chocolates: Switzerland is known for its high-quality chocolates, with some of the best made by local chocolatiers in Falera.
  • Traditional Swiss Woodcarvings: The Graubünden region of Switzerland is known for its beautiful wood carvings, with many local shops selling handmade items.

Local Culture and Customs of Falera

Switzerland has a rich cultural tradition that is evident in its music, art, and crafts. In Falera, the local culture is celebrated through traditional Swiss festivals and events throughout the year. Visitors can also explore the many local museums and historical places to learn more about the history and culture of this beautiful village.

Best Time to Visit Falera

The best time to visit Falera depends on your interests. For winter sports enthusiasts, December through March is the best time to visit, while those interested in hiking and outdoor activities may prefer to visit in the summer months. Fall and spring are also great times to visit, with fewer crowds and mild weather.

Safety and Health

Safety in Falera

Falera is a safe destination for travelers. However, visitors should still exercise common sense and take precautions to protect themselves and their belongings. Be sure to lock your valuables in a safe place and avoid leaving your belongings unattended in public areas.

Health in Falera

Switzerland has excellent healthcare facilities, with hospitals and clinics located in most major cities. Visitors should ensure they have adequate health insurance coverage before traveling and take necessary precautions to protect themselves from altitude sickness when hiking at high altitudes.

Getting to Falera: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Falera

The closest airport to Falera is Zurich Airport, located approximately 150 kilometers away. From the airport, travelers can take a train or rental car to reach Falera.

Bus Stations Near Falera

Falera has a bus station located in the village center, with regular connections to nearby towns and cities in the Graubünden region.

Other Options

Travelers can also reach Falera by train, with regular connections from major cities in Switzerland to the nearby town of Ilanz. From Ilanz, buses or taxis can be taken to reach Falera.

How to Get Around in Falera

Falera is a small village that is easily accessible on foot. However, for longer journeys or trips to nearby areas, visitors can take a bus or taxi.

Where to Go Next from Falera: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Falera is located in the heart of the Graubünden region, with a variety of nearby destinations and excursions to choose from. Some of the best options include:
