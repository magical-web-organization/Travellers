Introduction to Sarnen

Sarnen, a town located in the canton of Obwalden, is a charming place to visit. This small town has plenty of cultural and natural attractions to offer and is perfect for travelers who want to explore off-beat destinations in Switzerland. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll provide you with all the information you need to have an unforgettable experience in Sarnen.

List of Places to Visit in Sarnen

Here’s a list of places that you must explore when in Sarnen:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums, and Historical Places

  • Obwalden Museum: This museum, situated in the heart of Sarnen, is home to many historical artifacts and exhibits that will take you back in time.
  • St. Peter’s Chapel: Known for its unique baroque architecture, this chapel is a popular tourist destination.
  • Landenberg Castle: Located on a hilltop, this castle offers breathtaking views of the town and the surrounding mountains.

Natural Attractions of Sarnen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Sarnen: This stunning lake is perfect for water activities like swimming, boating, and fishing.
  • Mount Pilatus: The mountain offers stunning panoramic views of Sarnen and the surrounding areas. You can take a cable car or hike to the mountain top.
  • Schönbühl Wildlife Park: A great spot to explore the flora and fauna of the region.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sarnen

Sarnen is a perfect place for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Panorama Trail: This 12 km trail offers stunning views of the Alps and the surrounding landscape.
  • Aare Trail: The hiking and biking trail stretches over 307 km, taking you through scenic landscapes and historic towns.
  • Sarner Panoramaweg: This 53 km trail takes you through valleys, forests, and alpine meadows, offering stunning views along the way.

Best Things to Do in Sarnen: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Sarnen:

  • Take a Boat Tour: Take a relaxing boat tour on Lake Sarnen
  • Stroll Around the Town: Stroll around the town and explore its charming architecture and culture.
  • Visit a Cheese Factory: Visit a local cheese factory and taste the delicious Swiss cheese.
  • Go Skiing: Hit the slopes in winter at the nearby ski resort of Engelberg.

Week-Long Itinerary for Sarnen

Day 1: Explore the town of Sarnen and visit the Obwalden Museum.

Day 2: Go for a boat tour on Lake Sarnen, followed by a visit to the St. Peter’s Chapel.

Day 3: Take a cable car or hike up Mount Pilatus and enjoy the panoramic views of the surrounding area

Day 4: Visit the Schönbühl Wildlife Park to explore the local flora and fauna

Day 5: Take a day trip to Lucerne, a beautiful city 30 minutes away from Sarnen.

Day 6: Explore the hiking trails and go for a picnic at one of the scenic locations.

Day 7: Relax and unwind, while savoring local cuisine and drinks.

Food and Drink Scene of Sarnen

Sarnen has a fantastic food and drink scene. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Älplermagronen: A hearty dish made with potatoes, pasta, cheese, and onions.
  • Rösti: A Swiss classic dish made with potatoes, butter, and sometimes bacon.
  • Zuger Kirschtorte: A traditional Swiss cake made with the region’s cherry brandy.

Local Beverages

  • Swiss Wine: Enjoy a glass of local Swiss wine, which is known for its high quality and distinctive taste.
  • Beer: Switzerland is home to several great breweries, and Sarnen has a variety of local beers to try.

Where to Stay in Sarnen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Here are some of the best places to stay in Sarnen:

  • Hotel Krone: A cozy hotel located in the heart of Sarnen, featuring modern amenities and fantastic service.
  • Bauernhaus: A traditional Swiss farmhouse that has been converted into a guesthouse, offering an authentic Swiss experience.
  • Youth Hostel Sarnen: A great option for budget travelers, offering affordable accommodation in a stunning location.

What to Buy in Sarnen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some excellent souvenirs and local products to bring back home from Sarnen:

  • Swiss Cheese: A variety of Swiss cheeses are produced in the region and make a great gift.
  • Swiss Chocolate: Switzerland is famous for its rich and decadent chocolate, and Sarnen has several chocolate shops.
  • Traditional Swiss Clothing: Buy traditional Swiss clothing such as Dirndl and Lederhosen.

Local Culture and Customs of Sarnen

Switzerland has a rich culture and history, and Sarnen is no exception. Here are some tips to help you experience the local culture:

  • Respect the locals and their customs, especially during festivals and celebrations.
  • Try to learn a few basic words and phrases in German or Swiss German.
  • Dress appropriately for the weather and local customs.

Best Time to Visit Sarnen

The best time to visit Sarnen is during the summer months, between June and August. The weather is pleasant, and you can enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and water sports. Winter is also a great time to visit, especially for skiing and winter sports.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sarnen

Do:

  • Explore the town on foot as it’s easy to navigate and has many beautiful spots to discover.
  • Try local Swiss cuisine and drinks, including the Swiss Wine and beer.
  • Respect the natural environment and follow all the necessary etiquette while using hiking trails and exploring the natural attractions.

Don’t:

  • Litter anywhere and respect the environment.
  • Forget to carry cash, as many local establishments do not accept credit cards.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sarnen

Sarnen is a relatively safe town, and the crime rates are low. However, it is always important to take necessary precautions when traveling, like keeping your valuables secure.

Health in Sarnen

Switzerland has a high standard of healthcare facilities, and Sarnen is no exception. There are several hospitals and clinics in the town, and you can seek medical assistance if needed.

Getting to Sarnen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sarnen

The closest airport to Sarnen is Zurich Airport, which is approximately 75 km away. From Zurich, you can take a train to Sarnen.

Bus Stations Near Sarnen

The Sarnen train station is the central transportation hub, connecting Sarnen with other regions within Switzerland.

Other Options

You can also drive to Sarnen, and several car rental agencies are available at the airport.

How to Get Around in Sarnen

Sarnen is a small town, and most of the major attractions, as well as accommodations, are within walking distance. Biking is also a popular way to get around Sarnen, and you can rent bikes easily.

Where to Go Next from Sarnen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some great places to visit near Sarnen:

  • Lucerne: A beautiful city known for its picturesque scenery, great shopping, and nightlife.
  • Engelberg: A charming alpine village popular for its winter sports and natural beauty.
  • Interlaken: A scenic town located in the heart of the Swiss Alps, offering outdoor adventure activities like hiking, skiing, and water sports.

Conclusion

Sarnen is a hidden gem in Switzerland, offering a perfect blend of cultural attractions, natural beauty, and outdoor adventure activities. We hope this comprehensive guide has helped you plan your trip to Sarnen and get the most out of your visit.
