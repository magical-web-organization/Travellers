Introduction to La Chaux-de-Fonds

La Chaux-de-Fonds is a beautiful city located in Western Switzerland. Known for its watchmaking industry, La Chaux-de-Fonds has been part of the Swiss watchmaking industry since the 18th century. The city is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is home to some of the most important museums and cultural attractions in Europe. La Chaux-de-Fonds is also known for its beautiful natural landscapes, hiking and biking trails, and outdoor adventures.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fondue Neuchâteloise
  • Rösti
  • Cuchaule
  • Saffron Risotto
  • Cheese Fondue

La Chaux-de-Fonds has a rich culinary tradition that is known for its cheese and butter dishes. Fondue Neuchâteloise and Cheese Fondue are two popular dishes that every visitor should try. Rösti is a traditional Swiss potato dish that is also popular in La Chaux-de-Fonds. Cuchaule is a sweet bread that is made with saffron and served with butter, while Saffron Risotto is a popular Italian-inspired dish that is made with saffron, rice, and cheese.
