A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Crans Montana, Switzerland

Crans Montana, located in Valais, Switzerland, is a stunning alpine resort town that attracts visitors year-round with its beautiful nature and outdoor activities. Whether you’re looking to ski in the winter or hike in the summer, there’s something for everyone in Crans Montana. In this travel guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about planning your trip to this breathtaking destination.

List of Places to Visit in Crans Montana

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum of Art and History: This museum features a collection of artworks and artifacts from ancient times to the present day.
  • Fondation Suisse des Trains Miniatures: This museum displays an impressive collection of over 3,000 model train sets.
  • Edelweiss Lodge: Built in 1883, Edelweiss Lodge is a historical monument that offers guided tours and stunning views of the Rhone Valley.

Natural Attractions of Crans Montana: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lac de la Moubra: A peaceful lake surrounded by lush greenery, perfect for a picnic or a swim.
  • Glacier de la Plaine Morte: Take a cable car up to this glacier for stunning panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
  • Pointe de la Plaine Morte: Known for its challenging hiking trails and breathtaking views, this mountain peak is a must-visit for outdoor enthusiasts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Crans Montana

  • Bella Lui Loop: A picturesque 6km hike with stunning views of the Rhone Valley.
  • Montana Forest Trail: A 12km hike through the forest with plenty of opportunities for birdwatching and wildlife spotting.
  • Bisse du Ro: A 14km hiking trail that takes you along the historic irrigation channels of Valais.

Best Things to Do in Crans Montana: Activities and Tours

  • Skiing/Snowboarding: Crans Montana is a popular winter sports destination with over 140km of ski slopes.
  • Golfing: Crans Montana boasts some of the most beautiful and prestigious golf courses in Switzerland.
  • Paragliding: Take in the stunning mountain views from above with a paragliding tour.

Food and Drink Scene of Crans Montana

Must-Try Dishes

  • Raclette: A Swiss classic, raclette is a melted cheese dish typically served with potatoes and other toppings.
  • Fondue: Another Swiss favorite, fondue is a melted cheese dish that’s perfect for dipping bread, vegetables, and meats.
  • Valaisanne Pasty: A savory pastry filled with bacon, onions, and potatoes, this dish is a hearty and delicious meal.

Local Beverages

  • Vin du Glacier: Made from grapes grown on the slopes of Plaine Morte Glacier, this wine is a local specialty.
  • Abricotine: A fruit brandy made from Valais apricots, Abricotine is a popular after-dinner drink.
  • Swiss Craft Beer: Switzerland has a growing craft beer scene, and Crans Montana is no exception. Be sure to try some of the local brews on your visit.

Where to Stay in Crans Montana: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Crans Ambassador: A luxury hotel with stunning views of the mountains and easy access to the ski slopes.
  • Hotel Mont Blanc: A cozy and welcoming hotel in the heart of Crans Montana.
  • Pension Bella Lui: A charming guesthouse with comfortable rooms and a homey atmosphere.

What to Buy in Crans Montana: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Swiss Chocolate: Switzerland is famous for its chocolate, and Crans Montana has plenty of shops selling delicious treats.
  • Swiss Army Knife: A classic Swiss souvenir, the Swiss Army Knife is a useful and practical gift.
  • Swiss Cheese: Bring home some Swiss cheese to enjoy at home, or to give as a gift to friends and family.

Local Culture and Customs of Crans Montana

The local culture in Crans Montana is heavily influenced by its proximity to France and Italy. The official language spoken is French, but many locals also speak German and English. Switzerland is known for its punctuality and cleanliness, so be sure to respect these cultural norms during your visit.

Best Time to Visit Crans Montana

The best time to visit Crans Montana depends on your preferred activities. If you’re planning to ski, visit during the winter months from December to March. If you’re more interested in hiking and outdoor activities, visit in the summer or early fall (June to October).

Do’s and Don’ts of Crans Montana

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Take care of the environment and clean up after yourself.
  • Dress appropriately for the weather and activities you’ll be doing.

Don’t:

  • Be late, as punctuality is highly valued in Switzerland.
  • Ignore safety warnings or guidelines.
  • Leave trash or litter behind when you’re out in nature.

Safety and Health

Safety in Crans Montana

Overall, Crans Montana is a very safe destination for travelers. However, as with any mountainous region, it’s important to take precautions when participating in outdoor activities such as hiking, skiing, or paragliding. Be sure to follow safety guidelines and take appropriate precautions such as wearing helmets, bringing enough water and food, and being aware of changing weather conditions.

Health in Crans Montana

Switzerland has a high-quality healthcare system, so visitors can feel confident that they will receive the care they need if they fall ill or have an accident. However, it’s important to have adequate travel insurance before arriving in case of any unexpected medical expenses.

Getting to Crans Montana: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Crans Montana

The closest airport to Crans Montana is Geneva International Airport, which is approximately 2 hours away by car. Other nearby airports include Zurich International Airport and Milan Malpensa Airport.

Bus Stations Near Crans Montana

There are several bus stations in the area, including Sierre, Sion, and Martigny. From these stations, buses run regularly to Crans Montana.

Other Options

If you’re traveling from within Switzerland, you can also take the train to Sierre or Sion and then transfer to a bus or taxi to reach Crans Montana. Additionally, if you’re renting a car, there are several rental options available at Geneva International Airport.

How to Get Around in Crans Montana

Crans Montana is a relatively small town and is easy to navigate on foot. However, if you’re planning to explore the surrounding mountains or visit nearby towns, renting a car is recommended. Additionally, there are several local bus companies that run regular routes throughout the town.

Where to Go Next from Crans Montana: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Zermatt: Located approximately 2 hours away by car, Zermatt is a picturesque mountain town known for its stunning views of the Matterhorn.
  • Verbier: Famous for its ski resorts and lively nightlife, Verbier is approximately 1 hour away by car.
  • Lake Geneva: Approximately 2 hours away by car, Lake Geneva is the largest lake in Switzerland and offers many opportunities for water sports and outdoor activities.

Conclusion

Crans Montana is a beautiful and welcoming destination that offers something for everyone. Whether you’re looking to hit the ski slopes or explore the stunning natural beauty of the Swiss Alps, Crans Montana is a must-visit destination. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has helped you plan your trip and get the most out of your visit to this breathtaking destination.
