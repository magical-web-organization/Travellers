Introduction to Schaan

Nestled in the heart of Liechtenstein, a tiny country in Central Europe, Schaan is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. With its breathtaking natural scenery, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality, Schaan offers a unique and unforgettable travel experience. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, or a cozy small town atmosphere, Schaan has something to offer for every type of traveler.

List of Places to Visit in Schaan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The National Museum: Located in the capital city of Vaduz, just a short drive from Schaan, the National Museum features an impressive collection of artifacts and exhibitions showcasing Liechtenstein’s rich history and cultural heritage.
  • The Red House: Built in the early 20th century, this historic building now houses a museum dedicated to the history of Schaan and its people.
  • The Walser Museum: Located in the nearby town of Triesenberg, the Walser Museum offers a fascinating insight into the history and customs of the Walser people, an ethnic group with roots in the Alps.

Natural Attractions of Schaan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Alvierbad Schaan: This beautiful outdoor swimming pool is surrounded by lush greenery and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Drei Schwestern: Located just outside Schaan, this spectacular mountain peak offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Liechtenstein Alps.
  • Schaaner Berg: This picturesque mountain is a popular destination for hiking, biking, and skiing, with well-marked trails and stunning views at every turn.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Schaan

For outdoor enthusiasts, there are countless hiking and biking routes in and around Schaan, ranging from easy strolls to challenging mountain treks. Some recommended routes include:

  • The Rhine Valley Trail: This well-marked trail follows the course of the Rhine River through stunning scenery and charming towns, with plenty of opportunities to stop for a swim or a picnic along the way.
  • The Eschnerberg Trail: This challenging mountain trail offers panoramic views of the Rhine Valley and the surrounding Alps, with plenty of opportunities to spot wildlife and explore hidden valleys.
  • The Alpenpanorama Trail: This multi-day trek takes you through some of the most spectacular alpine scenery in Liechtenstein, with stunning views of glaciers, lakes, and towering peaks.

Best Things to Do in Schaan: Activities and Tours

  • Visit a local winery: Liechtenstein is renowned for its excellent wines, and a visit to a local winery is a must for any food and wine lover.
  • Take a guided tour of Schaan: Discover the history and hidden gems of Schaan with a knowledgeable local guide, who can show you all the best spots and share insider tips.
  • Try your hand at skiing or snowboarding: With its excellent ski resorts and pristine snow conditions, Liechtenstein is a top destination for winter sports enthusiasts.

Food and Drink Scene of Schaan

Must-Try Dishes

  • Käsknöpfle: A traditional Liechtenstein dish made with egg noodles, cheese, and caramelized onions.
  • Riebel: A hearty porridge-like dish made from cornmeal, typically served with butter and syrup.
  • Trüeblimusch: A sweet pastry made with apples, nuts, and cinnamon, perfect for a mid-day snack.

Local Beverages

  • Liechtenstein wines: From crisp whites to complex reds, Liechtenstein produces a variety of delicious wines that reflect the unique terroir of the region.
  • Alpenbitter: A traditional herbal liqueur made with over 30 different herbs, including mint, wormwood, and gentian. Perfect as a digestive after a hearty meal.

Where to Stay in Schaan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hotel Turna: A cozy family-run hotel located in the heart of Schaan, with comfortable rooms and a charming restaurant serving local specialties.
  • Youth Hostel Schaan: Budget-friendly accommodation with a friendly atmosphere and plenty of opportunities to meet other travelers.
  • Hotel Meierhof: A modern hotel located just outside Schaan, with spacious rooms, excellent amenities, and stunning views of the Alps.

What to Buy in Schaan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Liechtenstein wines: A bottle of locally-produced wine makes a great souvenir and a delicious reminder of your trip to Schaan.
  • Wooden toys: Handcrafted toys made from local wood are a timeless and unique souvenir to bring back home.
  • Chocolate: Switzerland is just a short drive away from Schaan, and as we know, Switzerland is famous for its chocolate. Be sure to grab a few bars on your way back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Schaan

  • Handshaking is the usual greeting in Liechtenstein.
  • Smoking is banned in public areas and all indoor spaces, including bars and cafes.
  • Tipping is not obligatory, but rounding up the bill to the nearest franc is appreciated.

Best Time to Visit Schaan

The best time to visit Schaan is from late spring to early fall, when the weather is warm and sunny, and the hiking and biking trails are at their best. However, for winter sports enthusiasts, the ski season runs from December to March, with excellent snow conditions and plenty of opportunities for skiing and snowboarding.

Do’s and Don’ts of Schaan

Do:

  • Try local specialties like Käsknöpfle and Alpenbitter.
  • Explore the many hiking and biking trails in and around Schaan.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Smoke in public areas or indoor spaces.
  • Take pictures of people without their permission.
  • Be loud or disrespectful in public places.

Safety and Health

Safety in Schaan

Liechtenstein is a safe country with low crime rates, but visitors should still take common-sense precautions, such as keeping valuables secure and staying aware of their surroundings.

Health in Schaan

Liechtenstein has a high standard of healthcare, and visitors can expect excellent medical care if needed. It’s always a good idea to have travel insurance with medical coverage, just in case.

Getting to Schaan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Schaan

The nearest airports to Schaan are Zurich Airport and Friedrichshafen Airport, with regular flights from major European cities.

Bus Stations Near Schaan

There are several bus stops and stations in Schaan and the surrounding area, with regular services to nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

For those coming by car, there are several highways leading to Schaan from Switzerland, Austria, and Germany.

How to Get Around in Schaan

The town center of Schaan is compact and easily walkable, but for those looking to explore further afield, there are several options for public transportation, including buses and trains.

Where to Go Next from Schaan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Vaduz

Located just a short drive from Schaan, the capital city of Vaduz offers a wide range of cultural attractions, shopping, and dining options, as well as stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Malbun

Situated in the heart of the Liechtenstein Alps, the ski resort of Malbun offers excellent skiing and snowboarding opportunities, as well as stunning natural scenery and hiking trails.

Conclusion

Schaan may be small, but it packs a big punch when it comes to offering a unique and unforgettable travel experience. From its stunning natural beauty to its rich cultural heritage and warm hospitality, Schaan is truly a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. So what are you waiting for?
