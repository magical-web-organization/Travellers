Introduction to Clervaux

Nestled in Éislek, in Luxembourg, Clervaux is a picturesque town that is popular among tourists. Whether you’re interested in exploring historical attractions, outdoor adventures, or simply soaking in the stunning views, Clervaux has something for everyone. This comprehensive guide will help you plan your trip by providing a detailed list of places to visit, hiking and biking routes to explore, and recommendations for food and drink. We’ll take a look at the best things to do in Clervaux, including activities and tours, and provide tips on where to stay and what to buy as souvenirs.

List of Places to Visit in Clervaux

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

One of the most significant cultural attractions in Clervaux is the Castle of Clervaux, a restored 12th-century castle that houses three museums. Historical and art exhibits are displayed here, including the famous “Family of Man” photo exhibition. The German-Luxembourg Nature Park is another popular tourist destination that attracts nature lovers.

The Saint Maurice and Saint Maur Abbey located in Clairvaux is an eye-catcher and must-visit attraction for history buffs. The abbey has a rich history dating back to the Middle Ages.

Natural Attractions of Clervaux: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Clervaux is also known for its stunning natural beauty, and visitors can explore its outdoor offerings through various scenic routes. In the summer, hiking and biking are highly recommended as Clervaux is filled with various forest trails. Spend an enjoyable day by the river banks amidst the lush green trees.

Those who love nature can climb to Ardennes forest near Wiltz. The forest is a popular attraction for both tourists and Luxembourg locals. Visitors can also enjoy the stunning views of Our valley and take a stroll through the town of Clervaux. There are several exciting activities to do in Clervaux, such as horseback riding, golfing, and fishing in the River Clerve.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Clervaux

Walking through the Ardennes forest, with its beautiful trees and flora, is a must-do. Sports enthusiasts have several options to explore in Clervaux. You can rent a bike and cycle your way around the town. Clervaux offers a diverse range of routes that are suitable for all levels of fitness. The most popular route is the Mullerthal Trail, which spans over 100km and takes visitors through some of the most beautiful parts of Luxembourg.

Best Things to Do in Clervaux: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are several exciting activities and tours that visitors can enjoy while in Clervaux. The Luxembourg Adventure Tours offers customizable trips that suit people of different ages. Discover the beauty and tranquility of the region by taking a hot-air balloon ride. You can also book a heritage railway ride with the Ardennes Lorraine Railway. Other highly recommended activities include visiting the Archery Club, trying bit and bar food in traditional luxurious restaurants, and parking yourself near a river in Clervaux and savour the serene environment.

Week-Long Itinerary for Clervaux

Here’s a week-long itinerary that allows you to experience the best of Clervaux:

Day 1: Start your trip by exploring the Castle of Clervaux. Day 2: Visit the St. Maurice and St. Maur Abbey in Clairevaux and Luxembourg German Nature Park. Day 3: Hike or bike along one of the many scenic routes in Clervaux. Day 4: Take a hot-air balloon ride over the countryside. Day 5: Go sightseeing or participate in adventure tours. Day 6: Spend a day fishing in the River Clerve or horseback riding in the surrounding countryside. Day 7: Shop for souvenirs and relax near the river in Clervaux.

Food and Drink Scene of Clervaux

No trip to Clervaux is complete without sampling some of the local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

Trout in Riesling sauce, Tarte flambée, and Luxembourgish Gromperekichelcher (potato fritters) are some of the local dishes worth trying.

Local Beverages

Try the locally brewed beer and wines that are popular in the region, such as the ‘Vin Moselle,’ and the ‘Claushaff,’ for a refreshing drink during your hike or bike ride.

Where to Stay in Clervaux: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of options available for those who are looking for a place to stay in Clervaux. Visitors can find hotels, hostels, and guesthouses that suit their budget and preferences. Hôtel International, BaseCamp Youth Hostel, and Le Clervaux Boutique come highly recommended.

What to Buy in Clervaux: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Clervaux offers several souvenirs that visitors can take back home. Handcrafted crystal glasses, pottery, and wooden handicrafts are some of the most popular souvenirs. Explore the local markets and boutiques that sell handmade crafts and goods, and take home some souvenirs for your loved ones.

Local Culture and Customs of Clervaux

Clervaux is a small town that is steeped in history and culture. Its people are friendly and welcoming to visitors. When visiting Clervaux, it’s essential to respect its cultural values and beliefs. The locals value peace and quiet and prefer if visitors maintain the same when around the town.

Best Time to Visit Clervaux

The best time to visit Clervaux is from June to September when the weather is warm and sunny, and the outdoor activities are in full swing. It is suggested that visitors plan their trip during summers and avoid the winter months between November and March. However, the winter months are better for those who prefer snowboarding and skiing during their trip.

Do’s and Don’ts of Clervaux

Here are some things to remember when visiting Clervaux:

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Participate in outdoor activities and tours.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the environment.
  • Take pictures without permission of people’s homes or specific area.

Safety and Health

Safety in Clervaux

Clervaux is generally a safe town with a low crime rate. However, it’s always essential to take precautions to ensure that you’re safe during your trip. Keep your belongings safe, do not flaunt your valuables, and be aware of your surroundings.

Health in Clervaux

Clervaux has an excellent healthcare system, but it’s always advisable to carry your travel insurance with you, which covers your health requirements. Do follow the COVID-19 guidelines and carry essential supplies along with sanitizer and masks.

Getting to Clervaux: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Clervaux

Luxembourg Airport, which is the nearest international airport to Clervaux, is approximately 100km away. Visitors can easily hire a taxi or take a rental car from the airport. For efficiency, it’s recommended to book a private cab or rental car for a hassle-free journey.

Bus Stations Near Clervaux

Clervaux has a well-developed bus system connecting it to the neighbouring towns and cities. Visitors can use the public bus system, which runs regularly throughout the day, to get around the town or travel to nearby attractions.

Other Options

Visitors can also take the train to nearby cities and then take a cab or bus to Clervaux.

How to Get Around Clervaux

The best way to get around Clervaux is by foot or bicycle. Visitors have the option to rent bikes or take part in organised bike tours. Taxis are also available for those who prefer not to travel by bus or bike.

Where to Go Next from Clervaux: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some nearby destinations worth exploring include the Mullerthal Trail, Ardennes forest near Wiltz, and the City Hall of Luxembourg. This magnificent location will surely leave tourists spellbound with its ancient architecture and ancient history.

Conclusion

Clervaux, Luxembourg, is the perfect destination for visitors who love the outdoors, history and nature, and want to sample local Luxembourgish culture and cuisine. From hiking and biking trails to cultural landmarks and museums, Clervaux has it all. Plan your trip now and experience all that Clervaux has to offer!
