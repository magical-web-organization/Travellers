Introduction to Hermalle-sous-Huy

Located in the beautiful Liège Province, Hermalle-sous-Huy is a charming Belgian village that boasts a rich cultural heritage, stunning natural landscapes, and a peaceful atmosphere. The village is nestled in the green hills of the Meuse Valley, offering a breathtaking view of the surrounding countryside. Hermalle-sous-Huy is an ideal destination for nature lovers, history buffs, adventurists, and anyone who needs a break from the hustle and bustle of the city.

List of Places to Visit in Hermalle-sous-Huy

Here is a list of the best places to visit in Hermalle-sous-Huy that will help you discover the best things to see and do in this charming Belgian village:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Musée de la Vie Rurale: This museum offers a glimpse into the daily life of Belgian farmers in the 19th and early 20th centuries. Visitors can see a collection of antique agricultural tools, furniture, clothing, and household items.
  • Château de Hermalle: This castle, built in the 17th century, is a fine example of Baroque architecture. It is surrounded by a moat and a park, and it houses a collection of Flemish tapestries and paintings.
  • Église Saint-Martin: This Romanesque-Gothic church, built between the 12th and 15th centuries, is famous for its stained-glass windows and its polychrome wooden altarpiece.

Natural Attractions of Hermalle-sous-Huy: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Vallée du Hoyoux Nature Reserve: This natural park offers hiking and biking trails that wind through forests, meadows, and streams. Birdwatchers can spot kingfishers, herons, and other waterfowl.
  • Rocher de Néviau: This rocky summit offers a panoramic view of the Meuse Valley and the surrounding countryside. The summit is accessible by a hiking trail that passes through a dense forest.
  • Grotte de Comblain: This limestone cave, located near the village of Comblain-au-Pont, is an impressive natural wonder. Visitors can see an underground river, stalactites, and stalagmites.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hermalle-sous-Huy

Hermalle-sous-Huy is a perfect destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in the area:

  • Sentier de la Vallée du Hoyoux: This hiking trail follows the picturesque Hoyoux River and offers beautiful views of the valley.
  • Ravel 5: This 15-km bike path connects Hermalle-sous-Huy to Huy and passes through several villages and scenic landscapes.
  • Randonnée de la Roche d’Al Pîtres: This challenging hiking trail leads to the rocky summit of Al Pîtres and offers panoramic views of the valley below.

Best Things to Do in Hermalle-sous-Huy: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking, biking, and sightseeing, there are many other things to do in Hermalle-sous-Huy. Here are some of the best activities and tours:

  • Kayaking on the Meuse River: Rent a kayak and explore the Meuse River, which is famous for its scenic beauty and abundant wildlife.
  • Visit a local brewery: Belgians are famous for their beer, and Hermalle-sous-Huy is no exception. Visit a local brewery, such as Brasserie Lefebvre, and sample some of the best beers in the area.
  • Go on a gastronomic tour: Belgian cuisine is famous for its waffles, chocolates, and fries. Take a gastronomic tour and taste some of the local delicacies.
