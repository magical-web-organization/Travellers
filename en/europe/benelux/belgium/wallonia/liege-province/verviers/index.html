Verviers is a charming city located in Liège Province, Wallonia, Belgium. It is a great destination for travelers looking for a relaxing break surrounded by natural beauty and historical landmarks. This travel guide will introduce you to everything you need to know about Verviers, including the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay.

Introduction to Verviers

Verviers is a vibrant city with a rich history and culture. It is known for its wool industry in the 19th century and is often referred to as the ‘Water capital of Belgium’ due to the Vesdre River which flows through the city. Verviers is surrounded by lush greenery and stunning hikes, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers, hikers and bikers.

List of Places to Visit in Verviers

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Centre Touristique de la Laine et de la Mode - This museum is dedicated to the history of wool and fashion. Visitors can learn about the production of wool and see an extensive collection of 18th and 19th century fashion.
  • Musée Communal des Beaux-Arts - This museum has a diverse collection of European paintings and sculptures from the 16th to the 21st century.
  • Eglise St-Remacle - Built in the 11th century, this church is a must-visit for architecture enthusiasts and history buffs.

Natural Attractions of Verviers: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Parc de la Haute-Sûre - A beautiful park with a lake and walking trails. It is located just outside Verviers but is worth the short trip for a day of relaxation.
  • Les Fagnes Nature Reserve - The plateau of Les Fagnes offers spectacular views that are perfect for hikers and nature lovers.
  • Waterfall of Coo - A 15-meter high waterfall located in the nearby town of Coo, perfect for a day trip.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Verviers

For hikers and bikers, Verviers offers numerous trails and routes to explore the natural beauty of the region:

  • The Ravel des trois vallées - A 125 km cycling route along the banks of the Vesdre River.
  • The Herve plateau - A scenic and tranquil hike that starts from the village of Battice and goes up to the Plateau de Herve.
  • Point de Vue de la Statte - A hiking trail that offers stunning views of the valley of the Vesdre.

Best Things to Do in Verviers: Activities and Tours

  • Kayaking on the Vesdre River - A fun and refreshing way to explore the region’s waterways.
  • Karting des Fagnes - A popular go-karting track with an outdoor terrace.
  • Plopsa Coo - A family-friendly theme park located near the waterfall of Coo.

Week-Long Itinerary for Verviers

Day 1 - Visit the Centre Touristique de la Laine et de la Mode and explore the city center. Day 2 - Take a hike in Les Fagnes nature reserve and enjoy a picnic lunch by the lake. Day 3 - Spend a day exploring the nearby towns of Spa and Francorchamps. Day 4 - Enjoy an exhilarating day at Kayaking on the Vesdre River. Day 5 - Visit the Musée Communal des Beaux-Arts followed by a visit to the Eglise St-Remacle. Day 6 - Take a cycling route along the banks of the Vesdre River. Day 7 - Relax at the Parc de la Haute-Sûre.

Food and Drink Scene in Verviers

Must-Try Dishes

  • Boulets à la liégeoise - Meatballs in Liège style sauce.
  • Waterzooi - A creamy Belgian stew made with fish or chicken.
  • Carbonnade flamande - Beef stewed in beer, onion and mustard.

Local Beverages

  • Pèkèt - A local liquor made from juniper berries.
  • Belgian Beer - Belgium is famous for its huge variety of tasty beers.

Where to Stay in Verviers: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hotel Verviers Van Der Valk - A luxurious hotel located on the outskirts of Verviers.
  • Le Palace - A stylish and comfortable hotel located in the heart of the city center.
  • Auberge de Jeunesse de Spa - A great budget option located in the nearby town of Spa.

What to Buy in Verviers: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Chocolate - Belgium is famous for its chocolate, and there are many chocolatiers in Verviers to choose from.
  • Crafts - The region is known for its craftsmanship, and you can find beautiful handmade items such as pottery and woodwork.
  • Wool products - Verviers has a rich textile history, and visitors can purchase woolen clothing and textiles.

Local Culture and Customs of Verviers

Verviers is a melting pot of cultures due to its location in Belgium’s French-speaking region. The city celebrates many festivals throughout the year, including Carnaval de Verviers, which is held in March.

Best Time to Visit Verviers

The best time to visit Verviers is during the spring and summer months (May - September) when the weather is mild and perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Verviers

Do:

  • Practice your French. While most locals speak English, they will appreciate the effort to speak their native language.
  • Respect local customs and traditions, especially during festivals.
  • Try local delicacies and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Forget to pack an umbrella and waterproof clothing as the region is known for its rainy weather.
  • Be disrespectful to local culture and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Verviers

Verviers is a relatively safe city, but visitors should still take normal travel precautions, such as not leaving valuables unattended and being aware of pickpocketing.

Health in Verviers

Belgium has a high-quality health care system, and Verviers has a hospital that offers medical emergency services.

Getting to Verviers: Transportation Options and Logistics

Verviers is easily accessible by public transportation.

Airports Near Verviers

  • Liège Airport - Located approximately 30 km from Verviers.
  • Brussels Airport - Located approximately 115 km from Verviers.

Bus Stations Near Verviers

  • Verviers Central Bus Station - Located in the city center, it offers connections to national and international destinations.

Other Options

  • Train - Verviers is on the train line Brussels-Liege-Germany.

How to Get Around in Verviers

Verviers is a walkable city, but visitors can also use public transportation, taxis or rent a bike.

Where to Go Next from Verviers: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Spa - Known for its natural springs and beautiful architecture, Spa is the perfect destination for a relaxing day trip.
  • Liege - One of Belgium’s historic cities, Liege offers visitors beautiful medieval architecture and great shopping.
  • Durbuy - A picturesque village known for its beautiful castle and great food.

Conclusion

Verviers is a beautiful, charming city with a rich history and culture. It is perfect for travelers who want to explore the natural beauty of Belgium and enjoy great food and drink. With this travel guide, you can now plan the perfect trip to Verviers and make the most of your visit.
