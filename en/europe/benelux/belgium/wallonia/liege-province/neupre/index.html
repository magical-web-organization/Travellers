Neupré Travel Guide - Discovering the Heart of Wallonia

Welcome to Neupré, a charming town in the province of Liège, Wallonia. This hidden gem of Belgium offers visitors a unique combination of history, culture, scenic landscapes, and tranquil village life. Our travel guide is here to help you explore Neupré and all of its wonders. From thrilling outdoor activities to culturally-rich experiences, there is something here for everyone.

Introduction to Neupré

Neupré is located in the heart of Wallonia, the southern, French-speaking region of Belgium. The town is situated on the banks of the river Ourthe and is surrounded by acres of verdant forests, rolling hills, and vibrant wildlife. Neupré is known for its tranquil atmosphere, lush greenery, and rich history.

The town is home to a population of just over 10,000 people and is known for its strong sense of community. You will find locals wandering along the cobblestone streets, cycling around the town, and enjoying the local cuisine in its picturesque cafes and restaurants.

List of Places to Visit in Neupré

Neupré is home to numerous cultural and natural attractions. Here are some of the most notable places to visit while in town:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Eglise Saint-Martin: a stunning 13th-century Romanesque church with intricate stone carvings and stained-glass windows
  • Chateau de Bonne Espérance: a magnificent castle built in the 17th century, now home to the International Cooking School
  • Fort de Boncelles: one of the most important Belgian forts during World War I, with a museum showcasing the history of the fortifications and the battlefields

Natural Attractions of Neupré: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Domaine Provincial de Wégimont: a sprawling park with walking trails, lake, swimming pool, and spa
  • La Roche aux Faucons: a famous cycling climb up the Ardennes, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys
  • Vallée du Ninglinspo: a scenic valley with a river, waterfalls, and ancient forests

Hiking/Biking Routes of Neupré

Neupré offers stunning walking and cycling trails that provide visitors with an opportunity to explore the natural beauty of the area. Here are some popular routes:

  • Ravel Ligne 126: a 30-km long cycling path that takes you from Liege to Taviers
  • Escapardenne Lee Trail: a 53-km walking trail that begins in Ettelbruck (Luxembourg) and ends in Neupré
  • La Roche aux Faucons: a famous cycling climb up the Ardennes, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys

Best Things to Do in Neupré: Activities and Tours

Visitors to Neupré have plenty to do, including:

  • International Cooking School: learn from the experts and enjoy a French cuisine course at the Chateau de Bonne Espérance
  • L’Imaginaire: an immersive theatre experience that takes audiences on an unforgettable journey through time, space and imagination
  • Vallée du Ninglinspo: a scenic valley with a river, waterfalls and ancient forests to explore

Week-Long Itinerary for Neupré

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Neupré, here are some activities to keep you busy:

Day Activity
1 Walk around the town center and see the Eglise Saint-Martin
2 Visit the Chateau de Bonne Espérance and take a French cuisine course
3 Explore the Domaine Provincial de Wégimont and enjoy a spa day
4 Cycle up La Roche aux Faucons and take in the stunning views

Food and Drink Scene of Neupré

Neupré offers visitors a rich food and drink scene that cannot be missed. Here are some must-try local dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Liège meatballs: meatballs made from a mix of beef and pork, served with a sweet and sour sauce
  • Carbonnade Flamande: beef and onion stew made with beer
  • Couque de Dinant: a biscuit-like pastry made with honey

Local Beverages

  • Belgian Triple Beer: a strong, light-colored ale with a distinctive hop aroma and flavor
  • Pèkèt: a potent drink made from juniper berries, similar to gin
  • Cherry beer: a sweet and sour beer made with cherries

Where to Stay in Neupré: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Neupré offers a range of accommodations from luxurious hotels to budget-friendly hostels and guesthouses. Here are some of the best options:

  • Hotel de la Tour: a charming hotel located in the heart of the town center
  • Domaine de la Carrière: a beautiful guesthouse surrounded by lush greenery
  • Hotel Aquarium: a budget-friendly option, located just a short drive from the town center

What to Buy in Neupré: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Shopping in Neupré is a great way to bring home a piece of local culture. Here are some unique souvenirs and local products to take home:

  • Couque de Dinant: the sweet honey biscuit-like pastry unique to the Ardennes region
  • Local beer and spirits: Belgian beer and Pèkèt are popular local spirits
  • Handmade crafts: pottery, lace, and other handmade crafts can be found in local shops and markets

Local Culture and Customs of Neupré

Neupré is known for its strong sense of community and its cultural heritage. Visitors to Neupré should respect local customs and traditions and be aware of their surroundings. Local events and festivals are a great way to experience the town’s culture and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Neupré

The best time to visit Neupré is in the spring and summer, from April to August, when the weather is mild and the landscapes are lush and vibrant. Fall is also a good time to visit, with cooler temperatures and colorful foliage.

Do’s and Don’ts of Neupré

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Explore the scenic countryside
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages
  • Attend local festivals and events

Don’t:

  • Be disrespectful to locals or their culture
  • Leave litter or damage natural landscapes

Safety and Health

Safety in Neupré

Neupré is generally a safe place to visit, but visitors should take normal safety precautions and be aware of their surroundings, particularly at night.

Health in Neupré

Belgium has a well-developed healthcare system, and visitors can access medical care if needed. Visitors should have comprehensive travel insurance before arriving and should obtain a European Health Insurance Card if they are travelling from another European country.

Getting to Neupré: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Neupré

The nearest airport to Neupré is Liège Airport (LGG), which is a 20-minute drive from the town center.

Bus Stations Near Neupré

The closest bus station is in Liège, which is a 20-minute drive from Neupré. You can take a bus from Liège to Neupré.

Other Options

Taxis are available from Liège airport and the bus terminal. Visitors can also opt to rent a car to explore the area.

How to Get Around in Neupré

Neupré is a small town, and visitors can easily explore it on foot or by bike. Taxis are also available to transport visitors around the town and to other areas of Wallonia.

Where to Go Next from Neupré: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some nearby destinations and excursions worth visiting include:

  • Liège: just a 20-minute drive from Neupré, Liège is a vibrant city known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and famous waffles
  • Spa: a 50-minute drive from Neupré, Spa is a popular spa town known for its healing hot springs and relaxing treatments
  • Brussels: the capital city of Belgium is only a 1-hour drive from Neupré and offers visitors a lively and diverse cultural experience

Conclusion

Neupré offers visitors a unique experience of Belgian culture and traditions. Its stunning landscapes, historical attractions, and friendly locals make it a must-visit destination. Whether you are seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural experiences, Neupré has something for everyone. We hope this guide has given you a taste of what this beautiful town has to offer and inspires you to plan your next journey to Neupré.
