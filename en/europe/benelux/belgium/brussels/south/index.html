Introduction to South

Located in Brussels, Belgium, South is a vibrant and culturally rich destination that attracts visitors from all over the world. Known for its historic sites and outdoor adventures, South is the ideal place for a unique travel experience. With its beautiful scenery, amazing architecture, delicious cuisine, and friendly locals, visiting South is a must-do for everyone. In this article, we will give you an in-depth guide to explore this charming city. Read on to discover the best places to visit, where to stay, what to eat, and more in South.

Best Things to Do in South: Activities and Tours

If you are looking for things to do in South, there are plenty of options available. Here are some of the best activities and tours to enjoy:

  • Take a chocolate-making workshop
  • Visit the Cantillon Brewery
  • Have a picnic in the Sonian Forest
  • Take a horse-drawn carriage ride through the city
  • Go on a guided walking tour of the historical landmarks

Week-Long Itinerary for South

If you are planning a trip to South, here is a seven-day itinerary that will allow you to experience the best the city has to offer:

Day 1 Explore the historic Grand Place and visit the iconic Manneken Pis statue
Day 2 Take a chocolate-making workshop and enjoy a walking tour of the city center
Day 3 Visit the Brussels Royal Palace, the Museum of Natural Sciences, and the Belvue Museum
Day 4 Take a trip to the Waterloo Battlefield and learn about the history of the battle
Day 5 Explore the beautiful natural surroundings of the Sonian Forest and Gaasbeek Castle
Day 6 Visit the Atomium and take a guided tour of the Horta Museum
Day 7 Have a picnic in the Bois de la Cambre and take a horse-drawn carriage ride through the city

Local Culture and Customs of South

South has a rich cultural history and unique customs that visitors should be aware of. Here are some of the most important things to know:

  • Belgian people are known for their hospitality and welcoming nature.
  • The official languages are French, Dutch, and German.
  • Tipping is customary in restaurants and cafes.

Best Time to Visit South

The best time to visit South is between April and October, when the weather is mild and the outdoor activities are most enjoyable. However, visitors can also enjoy the beauty of the city during the winter months, when the Christmas markets and decorations are in full swing.

Do’s and Don’ts of South

Here are some of the do’s and don’ts of traveling to South:

Do:

  • Visit the Grand Place and explore the local markets.
  • Try the local cuisine and drinks.
  • Be respectful of the local culture and customs.

Don’t:

  • Forget to tip in restaurants and cafes.
  • Be loud and disruptive in public places.
  • Disrespect historic landmarks and buildings.

Safety and Health

Safety is generally not a concern in South, as the city has a low crime rate and good healthcare infrastructure. However, it is always a good idea to take basic precautions when traveling. Here are some tips:

Safety in South

  • Be aware of pickpockets in tourist areas.
  • Keep your belongings close to you at all times.
  • Avoid walking alone at night in secluded areas.

Health in South

  • Ensure that your vaccinations are up to date.
  • Drink bottled water only.
  • Wear sunscreen and insect repellant when spending time outdoors.
