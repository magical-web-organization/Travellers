Introduction to Geraardsbergen

Nestled in the heart of East Flanders in Belgium, Geraardsbergen is a scenic town that’s rich in history, culture, and natural beauty. Known for its rustic charm and quaint architecture, Geraardsbergen is a delight to explore, with plenty of things to see, do, and experience. Whether you’re looking to hike in the hills, explore historical sites, or savor the local cuisine, Geraardsbergen is a destination that will leave you enchanted.

In this travel guide, we’ll tell you all about the best places to visit, things to do, and sights to see in Geraardsbergen. From outdoor adventures to cultural expeditions, we’ve got you covered.

List of Places to Visit in Geraardsbergen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Geraardsbergen is steeped in history, with lots of museums and historical sites that offer a glimpse into its rich past. These are some of the top cultural attractions in Geraardsbergen:

  • Manneken Pis: This statue of a little boy urinating is an iconic symbol of Geraardsbergen and a must-see for anyone visiting the town.
  • Geraardsbergen City Hall: Built in the 16th century, this Gothic-style town hall is a stunning example of medieval architecture and houses a museum showcasing historical artifacts from the town.
  • The Geraardsbergen Wall of Honor: This wall features the names of all the winners of the legendary Geraardsbergen cycling race, one of the oldest and most prestigious races in the world.
  • The Farmer’s Museum: This museum offers a glimpse into the rural life of Geraardsbergen’s past, with exhibits showcasing traditional farming tools and techniques.

Natural Attractions of Geraardsbergen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Geraardsbergen is surrounded by stunning natural beauty, with rolling hills, lush forests, and scenic vistas that are perfect for hiking, biking, and outdoor adventures. Here are some of the top natural attractions in Geraardsbergen:

  • The Muur: Also known as the Wall of Geraardsbergen, this steep cobbled climb is a favorite among cyclists and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.
  • The Dender River: This picturesque river flows through Geraardsbergen and is a popular spot for kayaking, canoeing, and fishing.
  • The Gavers: Located just outside of Geraardsbergen, this nature reserve features hiking trails, picnic areas, and a lake that’s perfect for swimming and water sports.
  • The Bosberg: This forested hill on the outskirts of Geraardsbergen is a popular spot for hiking, walking, and birdwatching, with stunning views of the town and surrounding region.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Geraardsbergen

Geraardsbergen is famous for its hills and is a magnet for cyclists and hikers looking to explore the beautiful countryside. Here are some of the top hiking and biking routes in Geraardsbergen:

  • The Muur and Bosberg Loop: This 25 km loop takes you up the famous Muur climb and the picturesque Bosberg hill, with stunning views and challenging climbs.
  • The Dender Valley Trail: This trail follows the Dender River from Geraardsbergen to Aalst, passing through picturesque villages and countryside along the way.
  • The Flemish Ardennes Trail: This challenging 75 km route takes you through the heart of the famed Flemish Ardennes, passing by steep hills, cobbled climbs, and stunning vistas.

Best Things to Do in Geraardsbergen: Activities and Tours

Besides hiking, biking, and exploring cultural attractions, Geraardsbergen also offers a range of other activities and tours. Here are some of the best things to do in Geraardsbergen:

  • The Geraardsbergen City Walking Tour: A guided walking tour of the town’s historical sites, including the Manneken Pis statue and the Geraardsbergen City Hall.
  • The Beer Tasting Tour: Geraardsbergen is famous for its beer, and this tour takes you through some of the town’s top breweries and pubs, with plenty of tasting along the way.
  • The River Cruise: Take a leisurely cruise down the Dender River and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding countryside.
  • The Cycling Tour: Join a guided cycling tour of the town and explore the hills and countryside around Geraardsbergen.

Week-Long Itinerary for Geraardsbergen

Food and Drink Scene of Geraardsbergen

Geraardsbergen boasts a rich food and drink scene, with a range of local dishes and specialty beers that you won’t find anywhere else.

Must-Try Dishes

Here are some of the top dishes that you should try in Geraardsbergen:

  • Mattentaart: A traditional pastry made with cheese and often filled with cream or fruit.
  • Witloof in den oven: A delicious dish of chicory baked with ham and cheese.
  • Waterzooi: A creamy soup made with fish or chicken and vegetables.
  • Stoemp: A hearty dish that’s similar to mashed potatoes, made with a mix of mashed potatoes and other vegetables.

Local Beverages

Geraardsbergen is famous for its beer, with a range of specialty brews that you can’t find anywhere else. Here are some of the top local beverages in Geraardsbergen:

  • Geraardsbergen Oudenberg Tripel: A strong, flavorful beer with a fruity finish.
  • Saison de Geraardsbergen: A refreshing beer with a spicy, hoppy flavor.
  • Kasseien: A dark, malty beer with chocolate and coffee undertones.
  • Mattetaartbier: A unique beer inspired by the town’s famous Mattentaart pastry.
