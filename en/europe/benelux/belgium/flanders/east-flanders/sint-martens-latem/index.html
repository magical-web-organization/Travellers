Introduction to Sint-Martens-Latem

Welcome to Sint-Martens-Latem - a charming and picturesque village located in East Flanders, Belgium. Known for its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage and warm hospitality, Sint-Martens-Latem is a perfect destination for travelers seeking a peaceful and rejuvenating getaway. It is famous for being home to a number of renowned artists who found inspiration in the village’s beauty.

With this comprehensive, ultimate guide, we will help you plan your itinerary, explore the best features of the village, understand the local lifestyle, and make the most of your visit. Get ready for an exciting adventure in Sint-Martens-Latem!

List of Places to Visit in Sint-Martens-Latem

Sint-Martens-Latem has a lot to offer. No matter what your interests are, you can find something exciting to explore. Here’s a list of top places to visit in Sint-Martens-Latem:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum Dhondt-Dhaenens: This art museum located in the heart of the village showcases contemporary art by both Belgian and international artists. It has both permanent and temporary exhibitions.

  • Castle Ooidonk: A 16th-century castle located at a short drive from Sint-Martens-Latem, Castle Ooidonk is an architectural marvel. It sits in the middle of a beautiful park with ponds, gardens, and ancient trees. You can take guided tours of the castle to explore its history and art collections.

Natural Attractions of Sint-Martens-Latem: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Latemse Meersen Nature Reserve: This vast nature reserve includes meadows, wetlands, and woods, and is home to a variety of flora and fauna. It is perfect for a peaceful walk or bike ride and offers stunning views of the village.

  • Scheldt River: Sint-Martens-Latem is situated on the banks of the Scheldt River. You can take a boat tour to explore the river and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sint-Martens-Latem

Sint-Martens-Latem is a paradise for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. There are several hiking and biking trails in the village that offer breathtaking views of the surroundings. Some popular routes include:

  • Latemse Meersen Nature Reserve Trail: A 10-kilometer circular trail that takes you through the nature reserve. It offers a chance to spot a variety of birds and other wildlife.

  • Scheldt River Trail: A scenic trail that runs along the riverbank and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscapes. It is ideal for both biking and walking.

Best Things to Do in Sint-Martens-Latem: Activities and Tours

Sint-Martens-Latem has a range of activities and tours that you can enjoy during your stay. Some popular ones include:

  • Chocolate Workshop: Learn how to make Belgian chocolate from scratch at “Chocoladehuisje Van op den Buiten” and taste some of the freshest and delicious chocolates.

  • Beer Tasting Tour: Discover the various kinds of Belgian beers at “Brouwerij Van Steenberge” and its brewing process. Sample some of their most popular beers such as Gulden Draak and Piraat.

Week-Long Itinerary for Sint-Martens-Latem

If you are planning to explore Sint-Martens-Latem for a week, here is a suggested itinerary to make the most of your trip:

Day 1: Arrive in Sint-Martens-Latem and take a guided tour of Castle Ooidonk.

Day 2: Visit Museum Dhondt-Dhaenens and enjoy a leisurely walk in the village.

Day 3: Take a bike ride through Latemse Meersen Nature Reserve and enjoy the serene beauty of the surroundings.

Day 4: Go on a boat tour of the Scheldt River and witness the glorious sunset over the river.

Day 5: Take a chocolate workshop and satisfy your sweet tooth.

Day 6: Visit Brouwerij Van Steenberge for a beer tasting tour and sample some of the best Belgian beers.

Day 7: Spend the day at leisure, enjoying the slow pace of the village and relishing local cuisines.

Food and Drink Scene of Sint-Martens-Latem

When it comes to food and drink, Sint-Martens-Latem has much to offer. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Waterzooi: Traditional Ghentian stew made with chicken or fish in cream-based broth, seasoned with herbs and vegetables.

  • Garnaalkroketten: Fried croquettes made with shrimp and served with white wine.

Local Beverages

  • Belgian Beers: Belgium is famous for its wide variety of beers, including Trappist beers made by monks. Some must-try options include Duvel, Westmalle, and Chimay.

  • Jenever or Belgian Gin: A traditional distilled spirit made from juniper berries, mixed with other botanicals.

Where to Stay in Sint-Martens-Latem: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Sint-Martens-Latem has several options for accommodations. Some popular ones include:

  • Charme Hotel Hancelot: A historic hotel with a charming garden and tastefully decorated rooms.

  • B&B Au Grenier: A cozy bed and breakfast located in the heart of the village, with comfortable rooms and a homemade breakfast.

What to Buy in Sint-Martens-Latem: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Sint-Martens-Latem has a range of unique and high-quality local products that make for great souvenirs. Some of the popular items to bring back home include:

  • Belgian Chocolates: Sint-Martens-Latem has several outstanding chocolate shops where you can purchase some of the finest Belgian chocolates.

  • Local Wines: The region produces some of the best wines in Belgium. Try and buy some excellent wines to share with your friends and family.

Local Culture and Customs of Sint-Martens-Latem

Sint-Martens-Latem is known for its rich cultural heritage and friendly locals. Here are a few customs and traditions to keep in mind:

  • Respect the local culture, customs, and traditions.

  • Greet the locals with “Bonjour” or “Goeden dag.”

  • Dress modestly and appropriately when visiting religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Sint-Martens-Latem

The best time to visit Sint-Martens-Latem is during the summer months, from June to August. The weather is pleasant, and the natural landscapes are at their most beautiful. However, the village can get crowded during this time, and prices may also be higher.

  • Follow all safety guidelines and instructions when engaging in outdoor activities.

Getting to Sint-Martens-Latem: Transportation Options and Logistics

Sint-Martens-Latem is well-connected to the major transport networks in Belgium. Here are some transportation options to reach Sint-Martens-Latem:

  • By Air: The nearest airport to Sint-Martens-Latem is Brussels International Airport. Once you land, you can take a train, bus, or taxi to the village.

  • By Train: Sint-Martens-Latem doesn’t have its own train station. The nearest train stations are in Deinze and Gent-Sint-Pieters, from which you can take a bus or taxi to reach the village.

  • By Bus: Sint-Martens-Latem is well-connected by bus to the nearby cities and towns. There are frequent bus services from Ghent and Deinze.

How to Get Around in Sint-Martens-Latem

The best way to explore Sint-Martens-Latem is on foot or by bike. It’s a small village and easy to navigate. Bike rentals are available throughout the village to explore the natural beauty. Taxis and buses are an excellent option for longer distances.

Where to Go Next from Sint-Martens-Latem: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Sint-Martens-Latem’s location is perfect for exploring other nearby cities and towns. Here are some popular destinations:

  • Ghent: A picturesque medieval city with stunning architecture and history. Perfect for a day trip.

  • Bruges: A charming city known for its quaint cobblestone streets and medieval buildings. It’s a delight to visit any day, any season.
