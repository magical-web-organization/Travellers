Introduction to Kraainem

Kraainem is a small, peaceful town located in Flemish Brabant, in the northern part of Belgium. It is famed for its beautiful nature, lush greenery, awe-inspiring landscapes, and vibrant cultural scene. Visitors to Kraainem will find plenty of things to see and do, from exploring its museums and historical landmarks to hiking and biking through its scenic trails.

  • Atomium
  • Museum of the Belgian Brewers

  • Autoworld Brussels

Best Things to Do in Kraainem: Activities and Tours
