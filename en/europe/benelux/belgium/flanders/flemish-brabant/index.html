Introduction to Flemish Brabant

Located in Flanders, Belgium, Flemish Brabant offers a perfect blend of nature, culture, and cuisine. This stunning region is renowned for its picturesque landscapes, historic monuments, and delicious dishes. Whether you’re a history buff or a foodie, Flemish Brabant has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Flemish Brabant

Here are some must-visit places in Flemish Brabant:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Grand Place, Brussels: This iconic square is surrounded by magnificent guildhalls and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Hergé Museum, Louvain-la-Neuve: This museum showcases the life and works of Hergé, the creator of the famous Tintin comics.
  • Africa Museum, Tervuren: Explore the art, culture, and history of Africa at this museum.
  • Stella Artois Brewery, Leuven: Take a tour and sample this famous Belgian beer.

Natural Attractions of Flemish Brabant: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Zoniënwoud Forest: This forest covers over 4,000 hectares and offers hiking, biking, and horse-riding trails.
  • Sonian Forest: Another beautiful forest with unique flora and fauna.
  • Kessel-Lo Lake: A beautiful lake perfect for a scenic walk or picnic.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Flemish Brabant

Flemish Brabant has a great number of hiking and biking routes, including:

  • The Hagelandse Valley: Explore the rolling hills and medieval castles.
  • The Dijle Valley: Follow the river and enjoy the beautiful scenery.
  • The Zoniënwoud Forest: Discover the flora and fauna of this beautiful forest.

Best Things to Do in Flemish Brabant: Activities and Tours

Here are some fun activities and tours to experience in Flemish Brabant:

  • Hot Air Balloon ride: Soar over the stunning landscapes of Flemish Brabant.
  • Beer tasting tour: Sample the best beers of Flemish Brabant.
  • Chocolate tour: Indulge in the best chocolates of Belgium.

Food and Drink Scene of Flemish Brabant

Flemish Brabant is famous for its mouth-watering cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Stoemp: A local dish made of mashed potatoes and vegetables.
  • Waterzooi: A creamy chicken or fish-based soup.
  • Bierbrood: A beer-based bread.

Local Beverages

  • Belgian beers: The region is famous for its beer, including Stella Artois, Leffe, and Duvel.
  • Lambic beer: A beer made from spontaneous fermentation in Bellegems and Pepingenlager.

Local Culture and Customs of Flemish Brabant

Flemish Brabant has a rich cultural heritage. The language spoken in the region is Dutch. The locals are friendly and welcoming. It’s customary to greet people with a handshake.

Best Time to Visit Flemish Brabant

The best time to visit Flemish Brabant is from May to September. The weather is mild, and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Flemish Brabant
