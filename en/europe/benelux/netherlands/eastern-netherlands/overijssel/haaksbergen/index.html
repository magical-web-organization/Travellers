Introduction to Haaksbergen

Located in the Overijssel province of Eastern Netherlands, Haaksbergen is a small town that’s known for its peaceful ambiance and scenic beauty. The town is located in the heart of the Twente region and is surrounded by lush green forests, rolling hills, and picturesque landscapes.

Haaksbergen is steeped in history, with a legacy that stretches back several centuries. Visitors can explore the town’s rich heritage through its well-preserved buildings, historical monuments and museums. But Haaksbergen is not just about the past. It’s a vibrant, thriving town that’s known for its welcoming locals, lively arts scene, and bustling food and drink scene.

List of Places to Visit in Haaksbergen

When you’re in Haaksbergen, there is no shortage of things to see and do. Here are some of the must-visit places in Haaksbergen:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Buurserzand Nature Reserve: This stunning nature reserve is located on the outskirts of Haaksbergen and is a paradise for nature enthusiasts. Wander through the sprawling expanses of heathland, woodlands, and marshlands and spot a variety of rare and endangered species of flora and fauna.

  • Het Lankheet Estate: This historical estate located on the outskirts of the town is the perfect place for a leisurely stroll. The park-like gardens, ponds, and lawns are a delightful spot to spend a relaxing afternoon, picnic, or explore the peaceful Dutch countryside.

  • The CactusOase: This unique attraction is a fascinating showcase of the world’s cacti and succulents. Visitors can wander through the unique indoor and outdoor gardens and marvel at the hundreds of exotic plants on display.

Natural Attractions of Haaksbergen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Haaksbergerveen Nature Reserve: This beautiful nature reserve is a must-visit for anyone who loves the great outdoors. Hike through the picturesque landscapes, explore the rich flora and fauna, and spot a variety of bird species.

  • The Twente Canal: Take a stroll along the banks of the Twente Canal and admire the beauty of the Dutch countryside.

  • De Lutte: This scenic village just outside of Haaksbergen is famous for its stunning landscapes and picturesque countryside. It’s the perfect place for a hike, bike ride, or leisurely stroll.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Haaksbergen

Haaksbergen is a paradise for hikers and bikers, with a host of scenic trails and routes to explore. Here are some of the best hikes and bike routes in Haaksbergen:

  • The Buurserzand Nature Reserve Trail: This circular hiking trail takes you through the heart of the beautiful Buurserzand Nature Reserve. The trail covers a distance of 6.5 km and takes around 2-3 hours to complete.

  • The Twente Canal Bike Route: This scenic bike route takes you along the banks of the beautiful Twente Canal. The route covers a distance of 45 km and takes around 3-4 hours to complete.

  • The Haaksbergerveen Nature Reserve Trail: This circular hiking trail takes you through the heart of the sprawling Haaksbergerveen Nature Reserve. The trail covers a distance of 5 km and takes around 2 hours to complete.

Best Things to Do in Haaksbergen: Activities and Tours

Haaksbergen offers a host of exciting activities and tours for visitors of all ages. Here are some things you should definitely not miss:

  • Wagon Riding Tour: Experience the country life around Haaksbergen by taking a wagon riding tour around the town. It’s the perfect way to soak up the scenery and experience the local culture.

  • Haaksbergen Farmers’ Market: Head to the weekly farmers’ market to sample some fresh produce and mingle with the locals.

  • Haaksbergen Street Art Tour: Take a walking tour of the town’s vibrant street art scene and admire the works of some of the region’s most talented artists.

Week-Long Itinerary for Haaksbergen

If you’ve got a week to spare, here’s an itinerary that will help you make the most of your time in Haaksbergen:

  • Day 1: Explore the town’s rich cultural heritage by taking a tour of the Historical Museum or the Buurserzand Nature Reserve.

  • Day 2: Take a scenic bike ride along the Twente Canal and explore the beautiful Dutch countryside.

  • Day 3: Spend a leisurely day at the Het Lankheet Estate, admiring the beautiful gardens and strolling along the scenic trails.

Food and Drink Scene of Haaksbergen

Haaksbergen is a food lover’s paradise, with a thriving food and drink scene that’s sure to tantalize your taste buds. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Poffertjes: These mini Dutch pancakes are a favorite local snack and can be found at most food stalls and cafes.

  • Stamppot: This traditional Dutch dish is a hearty stew made with potatoes, vegetables, and smoked sausage.

  • Krentenwegge: This sweet bread is a local delicacy made with currants and raisins and is perfect with a cup of hot tea.

Local Beverages

  • Twents Bier: This locally brewed beer is a must-try for beer lovers. It comes in a variety of flavors and is brewed using locally sourced ingredients.

  • Schrobbelèr: This sweet liqueur is a local favorite and is made with a mix of spices and herbs.

  • Twentse Krentenwegge Liqueur: This unique liqueur is made from the local sweet bread and is perfect for those with a sweet tooth.
