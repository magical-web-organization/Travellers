Introduction to Kampen

Kampen is a city located in the Overijssel province in the Eastern Netherlands. The city has a rich history that dates back to the 13th century and is known for its beautiful architecture and scenic views of the IJssel river. Kampen was once a prominent member of the Hanseatic League, which was a commercial and defensive confederation of merchants and their market towns in Northwestern and Central Europe during the medieval period. Today, Kampen is a popular destination for tourists who are looking for a relaxing and cultural experience.

List of Places to Visit in Kampen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kampen is a city with a rich history and has many cultural attractions that will capture the minds of tourists. Some of the notable places to visit include:

  • Stedelijk Museum Kampen - The museum is located in a 17th-century building and showcases the rich history of Kampen, including paintings, artifacts, and sculptures.
  • St. Nicolas Church - The church is one of the oldest buildings in Kampen and dates back to the 15th century. The church has a Gothic architecture, and visitors can explore the beautiful stained-glass windows and climb to the top of the tower for a panoramic view of the city.
  • Kamper Kogge - The Kamper Kogge is a replica of a medieval cargo ship that used to sail along the IJssel River. Visitors can explore the ship and learn about the trade routes of Kampen in the Middle Ages.

Natural Attractions of Kampen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kampen is known for its beautiful landscapes and natural attractions. Some of the best places to visit include:

  • Rivierduin Kampen - This is a nature reserve situated on the banks of the IJssel River. Visitors can take a walk along the trails and enjoy the scenic views of the river and its surroundings.
  • De Ossenwaard - It is a small island situated at the mouth of the River IJssel. It is an ideal spot for bird watchers and nature lovers. Visitors can also take a cycle ride around the island.
  • Kampen City Park - The City park is situated in the heart of Kampen, and visitors can take a stroll around the park and enjoy the beautiful flowers and foliage. The park also features several sculptures and water fountains.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kampen

Kampen has a vast network of cycling and hiking trails that cut across its scenic countryside. Some of the best trails include:

  • Ommetje Kampen - It is a walking trail that takes visitors around the city’s historic sites and scenic countryside.
  • IJsselvalleitocht - It is a cycling route that runs along the IJssel River. The trail is known for its scenic views and stops at several historic sites and landmarks.

Best Things to Do in Kampen: Activities and Tours

Kampen has numerous activities and tours that cater to every visitor’s taste. Some of the best activities and tours include:

  • Guided Walking Tour - Visitors can take a guided walking tour of the city and explore its rich history and landmarks.
  • Boat Trip - Visitors can take a boat trip along the IJssel River and explore the city from a different perspective.
  • Brewery Tour - Kampen is home to several breweries, and visitors can take a tour and learn about the brewing process and taste different beers.

Food and Drink Scene of Kampen

Kampen has a vibrant food and drink scene that showcases its rich cultural heritage and local produce. Some of the must-try dishes and beverages include:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kampen Black Pudding
  • Roggebrood met katenspek (rye bread with a spiced bacon topping)
  • Kampersteur (smoked eel)

Local Beverages

  • Bokbier - A dark seasonal beer that is popular in Kampen during the colder months
  • Jenever - A traditional Dutch gin that is made from juniper berries and other botanicals

Local Culture and Customs of Kampen

Kampen has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors should be aware of certain customs and traditions:

  • The people of Kampen are proud of their history and heritage, so visitors should show respect for their customs, monuments and landmarks.
  • Smoking is not allowed in public places, including bars, restaurants, and cafes.
  • Tipping is not compulsory, but it is customary to round up the bill or tip 10%.

Best Time to Visit Kampen

The best time to visit Kampen is in the spring (April to May) and autumn (September to October) when the weather is mild, and the tourists are fewer.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kampen

Kampen is a safe city, and visitors should follow these safety tips:

  • Be aware of pickpockets and keep your valuables in a safe place
  • Travel in well-lit areas and avoid walking alone at night
  • Follow the traffic rules when cycling or driving

Health in Kampen

Medical facilities in Kampen are of a high standard, and visitors should have health insurance that includes medical treatment in the Netherlands.

Getting to Kampen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Kampen is easily accessible by different modes of transportation. Some of the options include:

  • By Train - Kampen has a train station that connects it to other major Dutch cities.
  • By Bus - Several bus companies operate regular services to Kampen from nearby towns and cities.

Where to Go Next from Kampen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
