Introduction to Enschede

Enschede is a vibrant city located in the Eastern Netherlands and is the largest city in the region of Twente. A perfect blend of traditional and modern, Enschede is a popular hub for trade, culture, and education.

Some of the notable features of this city include beautiful parks, rich cultural history, and vibrant nightlife. Enschede is a popular destination for both business and leisure travelers. Whether you’re a history buff or an adventure seeker, Enschede has a lot to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Enschede

Here are some of the top places to visit in Enschede:

Culturally Important Attractions

Rijksmuseum Twenthe

This is a must-visit museum that showcases a vast collection of art and culture from the Middle Ages to the contemporary era. The museum boasts of an extensive collection of paintings, sculptures, and drawings.

Grote Kerk

The imposing stone church Grote Kerk is one of the landmarks of Enschede. Built-in the 14th century, it is famous for its magnificent Gothic architecture.

Natural Attractions of Enschede

De Lonneker Molen

This beautiful 500-year-old windmill has witnessed the expansion of Enschede into a bustling city. Visitors can climb up the narrow stone staircases to the top of the windmill and enjoy the scenic view of the surrounding countryside.

Volkspark

For nature lovers, Volkspark is an ideal spot for relaxation and picnics. This beautiful park is home to a variety of flora and fauna and has various hiking trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Enschede

Enschede is a city that can be best explored on foot and by bike.

Hiking routes

The most popular hiking routes include the Hulsbeek Walk and the Lonneker Steide Walk that offer a mix of unique natural, historical and cultural sites.

Biking routes

The Cycle Route network caters to all levels of fitness and offers various routes like the Oldenzaal Route and the Twente Route that will take you through beautiful countryside, pretty villages and fascinating cities.

Best Things to Do in Enschede: Activities and Tours

Outdoor Activities

Enschede has a vast choice of outdoor activities like canoeing, kayaking, and horse riding to name but a few.

Food Tours

A food tour in Enschede is the perfect way to sample traditional Dutch cuisine from farmer’s markets, food stalls, and local restaurants.

Week-Long Itinerary for Enschede

Here is an itinerary that covers the highlights of Enschede for a week-long trip:

Day Itinerary
1 Visit Rijksmuseum Twenthe and stroll through the city centre
2 Explore De Lonneker Molen and Volkspark
3 Hike the Hulsbeek Walk and discover the countryside
4 Cycle the Twente Route and visit local villages
5 Indulge in local food tours and experience Dutch cuisine
6 Explore the city’s nightlife and sample local drinks
7 Visit Grote Kerk and shop for souvenirs before departing

Food and Drink Scene of Enschede

Enschede has a vibrant food scene with numerous restaurants, cafes, and bars that offer a wide range of cuisine.

Must-Try Dishes

Poffertjes

This is a sweet Dutch delicacy made of buckwheat flour and served with powdered sugar and butter. They are usually eaten for breakfast or dessert.

Bitterballen

A popular Dutch snack, bitterballen are deep-fried croquettes filled with beef ragout and spices.

Local Beverages

Grolsch Beer

Grolsch is a locally brewed beer that has been around since 1615, and it has a distinct taste that is famous throughout the country. Take a tour of the Grolsch Brewery to experience the brewing process.

Where to Stay in Enschede: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Enschede has a varied range of accommodations, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels.

Budget-friendly Options

U Parkhotel

This affordable hotel is situated in the heart of Enschede, has a minimalist décor and offers exceptional value for money.

Mid-range Options

Hotel de Broeierd

This hotel is ideally located near the University of Twente and offers modern amenities with a touch of traditional Dutch style.

Luxury Options

Landgoedhotel de Wilmersberg

This luxurious hotel is located in a beautiful estate and offers its guests an authentic Dutch experience with gourmet cuisine and luxurious accommodations.

What to Buy in Enschede: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

For those who want to take some mementos of their trip home, here are some local products to look out for:

Grolsch Beer

Gift your loved ones the famous Grolsch beer that has been brewed in Enschede since the 17th century.

Twentsche Snoepjes

These traditional Dutch candies are unique to the Twente region and are available in many flavours like cinnamon and black licorice.

Local Culture and Customs of Enschede

The people of Enschede are warm, welcoming, and proud of their cultural heritage. Here are some unique customs that you can experience in Enschede:

Koningsdag

This is the most important national holiday in the Netherlands, celebrated every year on April 27th.

Carnavals

Carnavals that are celebrated throughout the Lent period are a popular tradition in Twente.

Best Time to Visit Enschede

The best time to visit Enschede is from May to September when the city enjoys a moderate climate with mild temperatures and the trees and flowers are in full bloom.

Do’s and Don’ts of Enschede

Do:

  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions.
  • Try local cuisine and beverages.
  • Keep an open mind and embrace new experiences.

Don’t:

  • Litter on the streets or parks.
  • Smoke in public areas.
  • Take pictures of people without obtaining their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Enschede

Enschede is a safe city with low crime rates; however, it is advisable to take usual precautions like securing personal belongings and avoiding dark alleys at night.

Health in Enschede

The Netherlands has excellent healthcare facilities, and you can find hospitals, pharmacies, and medical clinics throughout the city.

Getting to Enschede: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Enschede

The nearest airport to Enschede is the Amsterdam Schiphol Airport, which is approximately 135 kilometres away. Enschede Airport is a smaller airport that caters to domestic flight traffic.

Bus Stations Near Enschede

Enschede has a well-connected network of buses, and the central bus station is located in the heart of the city center.

Other Options

Enschede can be easily reached by train from other major cities in the Netherlands.

How to Get Around in Enschede

Enschede is a compact city that can easily be explored on foot or by bike. However, if you’re planning to travel outside the city center, the bus is a convenient option.

Where to Go Next from Enschede: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Giethoorn

Giethoorn, also known as the “Venice of the Netherlands,” is a charming town located only a few hours away from Enschede. Famous for its picturesque canals and bridges, it is a popular destination for nature lovers and adventurers alike.

Amsterdam

Amsterdam, the capital of the Netherlands, is located around two hours away from Enschede and is world-famous for its museums, art galleries, and nightlife.

Conclusion

Enschede has a lot to offer for travelers looking for a unique cultural experience and fun outdoor activities. With its beautiful parks, museums, and vibrant nightlife, this city is a perfect destination for a holiday. Whether you’re traveling with family, friends, or solo, Enschede will not disappoint you.
