Amersfoort, one of the beautiful cities in the Netherlands, is a hidden gem that has a lot to offer. Located in the Utrecht province, this city boasts of historic sightseeing, scenic landscapes, and numerous recreational activities. In this comprehensive Amersfoort travel guide, we provide you with all the essential information you need for your trip, including the best places to visit, local culture, dining spots, accommodations and transportation options.

Introduction to Amersfoort

Amersfoort is a medieval city, known for its historic character and cultural attractions. It is a perfect weekend destination for travelers seeking history and art in a position that is off the beaten path. With a population of 160,000, Amersfoort offers an intimate and cozy atmosphere, making it an excellent alternative to the bustling Dutch metropolises. The city is also a hub for dining, shopping, and events featuring Dutch history and culture.

List of Places to Visit in Amersfoort

Amersfoort has a vast array of attractions to visit, including charming architectural and elegant panoramic views of the city. Here are some of the recommended locations to explore:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Onze-Lieve-Vrouwetoren or Our Lady Tower
  2. Museum Flehite
  3. Mondriaanhuis
  4. The Zocher garden

Natural Attractions of Amersfoort: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Amersfoort Zoo
  2. De Nieuwe Stad (The New City)
  3. The Amersfoortse Bos, a vast wooded area with hiking and cycling tracks, public BBQs, children playground, petting zoo, and pancake house.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Amersfoort

Amersfoort has plenty of cycling and hiking tracks that offer incredible views of the scenic landscapes and serene nature of the city. The most famous routes for hiking and biking are:

  1. Around the Eem
  2. Hackfort Walk
  3. Walking the City Walls & Koppelpoort

Best Things to Do in Amersfoort: Activities and Tours

  1. Try the local cheeses at the Amersfoort cheese market
  2. Go on a boat tour through the medieval canal system.
  3. Take a bicycle tour, and explore the city’s scenic landscapes
  4. Attend a performance at the Theater de Lieve Vrouw

Week-Long Itinerary for Amersfoort

To help you explore Amersfoort, here’s a one-week itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival and check-in at your hotel Day 2: Visit the Onze-Lieve-Vrouwetoren and go on a city walking tour Day 3: Visit the Museum Flehite and Mondriaanhuis Day 4: Take a day trip to Utrecht and sample Dutch cuisine Day 5: Explore the Amersfoort Zoo and De Nieuwe Stad Day 6: Relax and enjoy scenic nature at the Amersfoortse Bos. Day 7: Shop at the local markets and enjoy the dynamic nightlife.
