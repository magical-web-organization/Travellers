Introduction to Oudewater

Oudewater is a quaint town located in the Utrecht province of the Netherlands. It is a popular destination for those who wish to experience the Dutch countryside while enjoying some historical sites. The town is famous for its Rumours Museum which is home to antique artifacts. Oudewater is also known for its handmade cheese, Gouda and other culinary delights. In this tour guide, we will explore the best things to do and see in Oudewater.

List of Places to Visit in Oudewater

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Rumours Museum

The Rumours Museum is a 17th century townhouse converted into an antique museum. The museum contains various historical artifacts such as gramophones, typewriters, and telephones. It is also famous for its witch trial memorial.

Heksenwaag (Witch Weighing House)

The Heksenwaag is a 16th century building that was formerly used to weigh witches accused of witchcraft. The building is now a museum and tourist attraction but the traditions still exist and witches can be weighed for a small fee.

St. Michael’s Church

St. Michael’s Church is a 14th century neo-gothic church known for its beautiful organ.

Natural Attractions of Oudewater: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Hollandse IJssel River

The river offers beautiful scenic views and is a great place to take a stroll.

Lange Linschoten River

It’s a great place for a bike ride or a picnic. It is surrounded by beautiful farms and meadows.

Delftse Blauwe Huis

This area is famous for its beautiful blue cottage that overlooks the river and looks like it has been taken out from a fairy tale book.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Oudewater

Oudewater has many hiking and biking trails.

Hiking Trails

The path of St. James is a popular hiking trail that passes through Oudewater. It is a medieval pilgrimage route connecting all the great pilgrimage destinations.

Biking Trails

The Oudewater - Woerden route is one of the most beautiful biking routes in the area, through the countryside and marked by idyllic canals and cozy villages.

Best Things to Do in Oudewater: Activities and Tours

Cheese Tasting at Kaasmuseum De Graaf

De Graaf Cheese Museum is a museum and a unique shop that offers a variety of Dutch cheeses. Visitors can taste and purchase different types of handmade cheese from the region.

Canal Tour

A canal tour is an excellent way to explore the historic canals and scenery of Oudewater.

Oudewater Summer Festival

The Oudewater Summer Festival is a traditional Dutch festival celebrated in August that features a wide range of activities including music, theater, and street performances.

Food and Drink Scene of Oudewater

Must-Try Dishes

Gouda Cheese

Gouda is an internationally recognized Dutch cheese and Oudewater makes some of the finest handmade Gouda cheese. It is a must-try dish when in Oudewater.

Stroopwafel

It is a traditional Dutch waffle cookie made from two thin layers of baked batter with a caramel-like syrup filling.

Local Beverages

Jenever

Jenever is a traditional Dutch gin and is the national drink of the Netherlands.

Where to Stay in Oudewater: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hotel Restaurant Abrona

Hotel Restaurant Abrona is a modern hotel with views of the scenic Hollandse IJssel River.

Het Oude Stadhuys

Het Oude Stadhuys is a historic guest house with a beautiful garden and restaurant.

What to Buy in Oudewater: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Handmade Gouda Cheese

Handmade Gouda Cheese is the most popular item to take back home from Oudewater.

Local Culture and Customs of Oudewater

Oudewater is a traditional Dutch town with a deep connection with the Dutch countryside. The town celebrates many traditional Dutch festivals throughout the year. Their customs and traditions can be observed in the Witch Weighing House and other historical sites of the town.

Best Time to Visit Oudewater

The best time to visit Oudewater is in the summer months (June-August) when the weather is pleasant and many festivals take place.

Do’s and Don’ts of Oudewater

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Try the local foods and drinks
  • Take a canal tour

Don’t:

  • Damage the historical sites
  • Disrespect the witch tradition

Safety and Health

Safety in Oudewater

Oudewater is a very safe town. However, visitors should take normal precautions for personal safety and the safety of their belongings.

Health in Oudewater

The Netherlands has a well-developed healthcare system. Visitors can access medical services by locating a local doctor or going to a hospital.

Getting to Oudewater: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Oudewater

The two closest airports to Oudewater are Amsterdam Airport Schiphol and Rotterdam The Hague Airport.

Bus Stations Near Oudewater

The Oudewater bus station is located in the center of the town and provides transportation to many nearby destinations.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel by car, and there are many rental car companies available.

How to Get Around in Oudewater

Walking or biking is the best way to explore the city.

Where to Go Next from Oudewater: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Utrecht

Utrecht is a vibrant city located only 15 kilometers from Oudewater.

Gouda

Gouda is known for its cheese and is only 20 kilometers away from Oudewater. It is one of the best places to take part in cheese tasting.

Conclusion

Oudewater is a lovely town in the Netherlands with a deep connection with the Dutch countryside. Visitors can enjoy cycling, hiking, canal tours, cheese tasting, and explore the cultural and historical sites in the town making it a perfect destination for a weekend getaway or even a week-long vacation.
