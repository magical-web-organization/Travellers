Introduction to Vlaardingen

Vlaardingen is a charming city located in the Rotterdam-The Hague Metropolitan Region in South Holland, Western Netherlands. Known for its rich history, Vlaardingen offers visitors a perfect blend of traditional Dutch architecture and contemporary modern design. The city offers a wide range of cultural attractions, outdoor adventures, and a fantastic food and drink scene.

Safety and Health

Safety in Vlaardingen

Vlaardingen is a relatively safe city; however, it is always best to take necessary precautions like not walking alone at night and keeping valuables safely hidden.

Health in Vlaardingen

Vlaardingen has a well-developed healthcare system, and emergency services are available 24/7. If you need medical attention, call 112 for emergency services.

Getting to Vlaardingen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Vlaardingen

The nearest international airport to Vlaardingen is Amsterdam Schiphol Airport, which is about 32 km away. From the airport, you can take either a train or a taxi to Vlaardingen.

Bus Stations Near Vlaardingen

There are several bus stations located throughout Vlaardingen where you can catch a bus to other parts of the city.

Other Options

You can also reach Vlaardingen by train, car, or ferry. The city is well-connected to other parts of the Netherlands.

How to Get Around in Vlaardingen

Vlaardingen is a bike-friendly city, and renting a bike is a great way to explore the city. You can also use the local bus service or call a taxi to get around. Walking is another option if you’re staying in the city center.
