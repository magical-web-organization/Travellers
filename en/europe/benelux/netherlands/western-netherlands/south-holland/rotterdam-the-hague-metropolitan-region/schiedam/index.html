Introduction to Schiedam

Located in the Rotterdam-The Hague Metropolitan Region, in South Holland, Western Netherlands, Schiedam is a hidden gem that often gets overlooked by tourists. Though small, Schiedam has a rich history and unique culture that is worth exploring.

Once known as the “Genever Capital of the World,” Schiedam was home to around 400 distilleries that produced the traditional Dutch gin known as jenever. Today, only five distilleries remain, but the town still has a strong connection to the spirit and is a great place to learn about its history.

Aside from jenever, Schiedam has much to offer visitors, including historic windmills, charming canals, and beautiful architecture. Whether you’re interested in culture, food, or outdoor activities, Schiedam has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Schiedam

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Jenever Museum: As Schiedam’s most famous drink, jenever is celebrated at the Jenever Museum. Learn about the production process, taste some varieties, and see old jenever bottles and advertisements. Be sure to take a tour of the nearby Nolet Distillery, home of the famous Ketel One vodka.

  • Stedelijk Museum Schiedam: This museum features contemporary and classic art exhibitions. It is also home to a gin distillery, where visitors can see how gin is made.

  • De Nieuwe Palmboom: This historic windmill was used to grind wheat and barley to produce jenever. It has now been restored and provides an insight into Schiedam’s past.

Natural Attractions of Schiedam: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Juliana Park: With a playground, petting zoo, and small lake, this park is ideal for a family day out. It’s a great place for a picnic or a relaxing stroll.

  • Beatrix Park: This large park offers walking and cycling paths, as well as several water features and a playground. Perfect for a picnic on a sunny day.

  • Ketelmeer Nature Reserve: This nature reserve has a wide range of flora and fauna, including several bird species. It’s a great place to hike or cycle.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Schiedam

  • Ketellapperpad walking route: This 12 km walking trail takes you through historic parts of Schiedam, including the Jenever Museum, windmills, and the old city center.

  • Midden-Delfland cycle route: This 25 km cycling route takes you through the surrounding countryside, passing through several villages and providing beautiful views of the local area.

Best Things to Do in Schiedam: Activities and Tours

  • Jenever tasting: Whether you’re a gin lover or not, sampling jenever, the traditional Dutch gin, is a must-do activity in Schiedam.

  • Canal boat tour: Take a boat tour around Schiedam’s canals to see the historic city center from a different perspective.

  • Biking tour: Rent a bike and explore Schiedam and its surrounding countryside at your own pace.

Week-Long Itinerary for Schiedam

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit the Jenever Museum and take a tour of the Nolet Distillery.
Day 2 Explore the historic windmills of Schiedam.

Food and Drink Scene of Schiedam

Must-Try Dishes

  • Stamppot: This dish is made of mashed potatoes with vegetables such as kale or sauerkraut, served with meat.

  • Hollandse Nieuwe: This is a traditional Dutch herring, served with onions, pickles, and bread.

  • Bitterballen: Meat-based croquette served with mustard.

Local Beverages

  • Jenever: As previously mentioned, Schiedam has a strong connection to jenever, its traditional gin.

  • Ketel One Vodka: The Nolet Distillery produces Ketel One Vodka, which has won several international awards.

Where to Stay in Schiedam: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hotel Stadsvilla Mout: This historic hotel is located in the heart of Schiedam’s city center, close to the windmills and the Jenever Museum.

  • Hotel Spinx: This affordable hotel is located near the train station, making it a convenient base for exploring Schiedam and its surroundings.

  • B&B Wevershuisje Schiedam: This cozy bed and breakfast is located in a traditional Dutch house and offers a unique experience for those looking for something different.

What to Buy in Schiedam: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Jenever: Bring home a bottle of the traditional Dutch gin, jenever, as a souvenir of your trip.

  • Delftware: Delftware is a type of blue and white pottery that is synonymous with the Netherlands. You can find it at several shops in Schiedam.

Local Culture and Customs of Schiedam

Schiedam is a friendly and welcoming town with a strong sense of community. Don’t be afraid to strike up a conversation with locals, who are often more than happy to share their stories and recommendations with visitors.

Best Time to Visit Schiedam

The best time to visit Schiedam is from May to September when the weather is warm and mild. The summer months are also the busiest, so it’s best to book accommodation in advance.

Do’s and Don’ts of Schiedam

Do:

  • Try jenever at the Jenever Museum or one of the local distilleries.

  • Rent a bike and explore the surrounding countryside.

Don’t:

  • Be disrespectful of local customs and traditions.

  • Litter or damage historic buildings.

Safety and Health

Safety in Schiedam

Schiedam is a safe town, and tourists are unlikely to encounter any problems. As with any destination, it’s important to keep an eye on your belongings and be aware of your surroundings.

Health in Schiedam

The Netherlands has an excellent healthcare system, and there are several hospitals and clinics in Rotterdam, just a short distance from Schiedam.

Getting to Schiedam: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Schiedam

  • Rotterdam The Hague Airport: The closest airport to Schiedam is Rotterdam The Hague Airport, which is approximately 7 km away.

  • Amsterdam Airport Schiphol: Amsterdam Airport Schiphol is located approximately 48 km from Schiedam.

Bus Stations Near Schiedam

  • Station Schiedam Centrum: This train station in the city center offers connections to Rotterdam and other Dutch destinations.

  • Station Schiedam Nieuwland: This train station is located on the outskirts of Schiedam, offering connections to nearby cities.

Other Options

  • Train: Schiedam is well-connected by train to other Dutch cities.

  • Car: Schiedam is easily accessible by car, with several major highways running through the area.

How to Get Around in Schiedam

Schiedam is a small town and can easily be explored on foot. Bicycles are also a popular mode of transportation, and there are several bike rental companies in the town.

Where to Go Next from Schiedam: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Rotterdam: Just a short train ride away from Schiedam, Rotterdam is a bustling city with plenty to see and do.

  • The Hague: The Hague is the political capital of the Netherlands and is home to several international organizations, including the International Court of Justice.

Conclusion

Schiedam is a beautiful and unique destination that is often overlooked by tourists. With its rich history and culture, charming canals, and beautiful windmills, it’s a must-see for anyone visiting the Netherlands. Whether you’re interested in history, culture, or outdoor activities, Schiedam has something for everyone.
