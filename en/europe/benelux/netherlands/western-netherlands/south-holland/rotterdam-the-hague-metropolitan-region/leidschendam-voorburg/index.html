Introduction to Leidschendam-Voorburg

Leidschendam-Voorburg, located in the Rotterdam-The Hague Metropolitan Region of South Holland, is a beautiful municipality with a lot to offer. It is home to picturesque canals, stunning parks, and historical attractions that will transport you back in time. Whether you’re interested in exploring the rich history of the area or just want to spend some time in nature, Leidschendam-Voorburg is the perfect destination.

List of Places to Visit in Leidschendam-Voorburg

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Leidschendam-Voorburg is home to a number of museums and historical attractions that will give you a deeper understanding of the area’s rich history. Some of the must-visit cultural attractions include:

  • Museum Swaensteyn: This museum is dedicated to the rich cultural and historical heritage of Voorburg and Leidschendam. It features a collection of objects, paintings, and photos that tell the story of the area’s past.

  • Huygens’ Hofwijck: This 17th-century estate was once home to Christiaan Huygens, a Dutch scientist and inventor who made significant contributions to fields like astronomy and mathematics. Today, visitors can explore the estate and learn more about his life and work.

  • Vlietlanden Windmill: This windmill was originally built in 1785 and was used to pump water out of the polder. It has since been restored and is now a popular tourist attraction.

Natural Attractions of Leidschendam-Voorburg: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Leidschendam-Voorburg is surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes and offers plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures. Some of the best natural attractions in the area include:

  • De Vlietlanden: This recreational area is perfect for hiking, biking, and birdwatching. It features a number of lakes and ponds, making it a great place to relax and enjoy nature.

  • Park Vreugd en Rust: This beautiful park is home to a number of historic buildings, including a 19th-century castle and a tea house. It’s a great place to spend a sunny afternoon and escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Leidschendam-Voorburg

Whether you’re an avid hiker or just looking for a leisurely bike ride, Leidschendam-Voorburg has plenty of options. Some of the best hiking and biking routes in the area include:

  • De Herenroute: This 21-kilometer trail runs through the lush countryside of South Holland and takes you past beautiful farms, historic estates, and charming villages.

  • Haaglandenroute: This 40-kilometer route takes you through the scenic landscapes of The Hague and Leidschendam-Voorburg. It features stunning views of the sea and beautiful parks.

Best Things to Do in Leidschendam-Voorburg: Activities and Tours

From cultural activities to outdoor adventures, there is no shortage of things to do in Leidschendam-Voorburg. Some of the best activities and tours in the area include:

  • Canal Tour: Explore the canals and waterways of Leidschendam-Voorburg on a guided canal tour. You’ll get a unique perspective of the area and learn more about its history and culture.

  • Gemeentemuseum Den Haag: This museum is located in The Hague and is home to an extensive collection of art and artifacts. It’s a great place to spend a rainy day and learn more about the region’s history.

Week-Long Itinerary for Leidschendam-Voorburg

If you’re planning on spending a week in Leidschendam-Voorburg, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day 1: Explore the historic city center of Leidschendam. Visit the Museum Swaensteyn and take a guided canal tour.

Day 2: Spend the day discovering the natural beauty of the area. Rent a bike and explore the Haaglandenroute.

Day 3: Take a day trip to The Hague and visit the Gemeentemuseum Den Haag.

Day 4: Visit Huygens’ Hofwijck and learn more about the life and work of Christiaan Huygens.

Day 5: Spend a relaxing day in Park Vreugd en Rust.

Day 6: Visit Vlietlanden Windmill and learn more about the area’s history.

Day 7: Spend the day exploring the surrounding countryside and visit some of the charming villages in the area.

Food and Drink Scene of Leidschendam-Voorburg

No trip to Leidschendam-Voorburg is complete without sampling some of the local cuisine and drinks. Here are some of the must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Haring: This local speciality is a type of Dutch raw herring that is typically served with chopped onions and pickles.

  • Poffertjes: These tiny pancakes are a beloved Dutch treat and are often served with powdered sugar and butter.

  • Stroopwafels: These thin waffles are filled with caramel and make for a delicious sweet treat.

Local Beverages

  • Jenever: This strong Dutch gin is traditionally served in a tulip-shaped glass and is typically enjoyed as a shot.

  • Bitterballen: These deep-fried meat-based snacks are a popular bar snack in the Netherlands and pair well with a cold beer.

Getting to Leidschendam-Voorburg: Transportation Options and Logistics

The closest airport to Leidschendam-Voorburg is Rotterdam The Hague Airport, which is located about 20 minutes away by car or taxi. Amsterdam Schiphol Airport is also nearby, with a travel time of around 45 minutes by car or train.
