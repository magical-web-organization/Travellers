Introduction to Uitgeest

Uitgeest is a charming little town in Kennemerland, North Holland, located in Western Netherlands’s Benelux region. It is best known for its natural beauty and scenic views, making it an ideal destination for those looking to enjoy a peaceful getaway.

Apart from its scenic beauty, the town also boasts of a rich cultural heritage, reflected in its museums and historical sites.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we have compiled a list of top tourist attractions, best things to do, where to stay, and more!

List of Places to Visit in Uitgeest

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Fort K’IJK - An impressive fortress constructed in 1906 that was used by the Dutch military until 2011. It’s now a popular museum, where visitors can explore the impressive structure and learn about its rich history.

  2. Museum De Oude Bakkerij - This museum offers a fascinating insight into the bakery industry’s development and history in Uitgeest.

Natural Attractions of Uitgeest: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Uitgeestermeer - A beautiful lake surrounded by peaceful hiking and biking trails.

  2. Recreatiepark De Buitenplaats - A stunning nature reserve, known for its scenic views, hiking trails, and bird watching opportunities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Uitgeest

  1. Uitgeestermeer Route - A quaint cycling route, offering some beautiful views of the lake and surrounding countryside.

  2. Kustwandelroute - This trail runs along the coast, starting from the NS station and finishing at Castricum station.

Best Things to Do in Uitgeest: Activities and Tours

  1. Sailing on the Uitgeestermeer - Visitors can rent boats and enjoy a day out on the scenic lake.

  2. Uitgeestermeer Beach - A quaint beach located on the banks of the Uitgeestermeer lake, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and picnicking.

Food and Drink Scene of Uitgeest

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Dutch Pancakes - A local favorite, these pancakes come in both sweet and savory flavors.

  2. Stamppot - A traditional Dutch dish made from mashed potatoes and kale, served with a choice of meats.

Local Beverages

  1. Jenever - A juniper-flavored drink that is the national spirit of the Netherlands.

  2. Beer - The Netherlands is famous for its beer, and Uitgeest is no exception. Local breweries offer a wide range of craft beers.

Where to Stay in Uitgeest: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Hotel Restaurant Posthoorn - A charming hotel located in the heart of the town offering picturesque views.

  2. Het Gouden Winckeltje - A cozy guesthouse located in a charming historic building.

What to Buy in Uitgeest: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Cheese - The Netherlands is known for its cheese, and Uitgeest produces some of the best varieties.

  2. Delftware - Hand-painted blue and white pottery is a quintessential Dutch souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Uitgeest

  1. Bicycling - Cycling is a popular mode of transportation in the Netherlands and is deeply ingrained in the culture.

  2. Tulips - The Netherlands is famous for its tulips, and visitors can enjoy the beautiful flower fields in and around Uitgeest.

Best Time to Visit Uitgeest

  1. The best time to visit Uitgeest is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is mild, and the town’s natural beauty is at its peak.

Do’s and Don’ts of Uitgeest

Do:

  1. Rent a bicycle and explore the town and its surroundings.

  2. Sample the local cuisine, including Dutch pancakes and Stamppot.

Don’t:

  1. Don’t forget to bring comfortable walking shoes as a lot of the town’s attractions require a lot of walking and hiking.

  2. Don’t forget to try out some of the local cheese varieties.

Safety and Health

Safety in Uitgeest

  1. Uitgeest is a safe town for tourists, but as with any other place, it’s advisable to keep your valuables safe and be aware of your surroundings while traveling.

Health in Uitgeest

  1. There are no specific health concerns in Uitgeest, and visitors can rest assured that the medical facilities in the town are advanced and equipped to handle any health emergency.

Getting to Uitgeest: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Uitgeest

  1. Schiphol Airport - The nearest international airport, located 37 km away from Uitgeest. Visitors can take a train or bus to reach Uitgeest.

Bus Stations Near Uitgeest

  1. Uitgeest Station - The primary bus station, with buses connecting to Amsterdam, Zaandam, and other neighboring towns and cities.

Other Options

  1. Train - The Netherlands’ advanced rail network makes it easy to travel to Uitgeest by train.

How to Get Around in Uitgeest

  1. Bicycles are the most popular mode of transportation in Uitgeest, with visitors renting bikes to explore the town and its surroundings.

Where to Go Next from Uitgeest: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Amsterdam - A vibrant city with stunning architecture, art museums, and world-class nightlife, only 20 km away from Uitgeest.

  2. Haarlem - A quaint, historic city known for its beautiful medieval architecture and picturesque cobblestone streets, only 15 km away from Uitgeest.

Conclusion

Uitgeest is a charming destination, offering something for every traveler. With its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and lively food scene, Uitgeest is the perfect place to unwind and escape the hustle and bustle of city life. Remember to check out the must-try dishes, take a cycling tour, and explore the quaint town’s bustling cultural scene.
