Introduction to Schagen

Schagen is a charming town located in the North Holland Peninsula of Western Netherlands. This beautiful place will leave you in awe with its eye-catching architecture, picturesque streets, and friendly people. This guide provides a comprehensive overview of Schagen, including top tourist attractions, where to stay, what to eat, cultural customs, and transportation options.

Local Culture and Customs of Schagen

Schagen is a diverse city that is steeped in rich culture and customs. Here are some things that you should know before visiting Schagen:

  • The Dutch are direct communicators
  • Tipping is not mandatory
  • Smoking is prohibited in many public places
  • Schagen is a bike-friendly city

Best Time to Visit Schagen

The best time to visit Schagen is during the summer season, from June to August. The weather is mild, and the town is lively as locals and tourists alike are out and about, making the most of the scenic environment. With longer hours of daylight and a range of outdoor activities to choose from, summer is the perfect time to visit Schagen.

Safety and Health

When it comes to safety and health measures, Schagen has a good record. However, it’s still important to stay careful and be prepared for any eventuality.

Safety in Schagen

Schagen is generally a safe place, but always take precautions against pickpockets and thieves. Do not leave your valuables unattended, and lock your doors and windows before leaving your accommodation.

Health in Schagen

Schagen has a well-established health care system, with numerous hospitals and clinics available for medical emergencies. Visitors from other countries should ensure that they have adequate travel insurance to cover any medical expenses that they may incur in the unlikely case of requiring medical attention during their stay.

Getting to Schagen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Schagen is easily accessible through multiple transportation modes, including air, train, and bus.

Airports Near Schagen

The nearest international airport to Schagen is Amsterdam Schiphol Airport, which is located around 60 km away from the city center.

Bus Stations Near Schagen

There are several bus stops in Schagen that provide easy access to neighboring areas. The town has a well-connected public transport network, which makes it easy to explore different places.

Other Options

Renting a car or hiring a taxi is an alternative way to reach Schagen, offering more flexibility than public transport.

How to Get Around in Schagen

Schagen is a small town, which makes it easy to get around by foot or bike. In-town buses and taxis are also available. However, if you’re planning to explore neighboring towns, renting a car is a good option.
