Introduction to Enkhuizen

Enkhuizen is a picturesque city located in the North Holland Peninsula of Netherlands. It’s known for its rich history, beautiful harbor, and well-preserved architecture from the Dutch Golden Age. Enkhuizen is a perfect destination for travelers who want to immerse themselves in Dutch culture and history.

List of Places to Visit in Enkhuizen

Whether you are interested in visiting museums and historical places, or you want to enjoy the natural beauty of the city, Enkhuizen has something for everyone. Here are the top places to visit in Enkhuizen:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Enkhuizen is known for its well-preserved architecture from the Dutch Golden Age. Some of the most popular cultural attractions in Enkhuizen are:

  • The Zuiderzee Museum
  • The Drommedaris Tower
  • The Westfries Museum
  • The Enkhuizen City Walls
  • The Stavoren Lighthouse

Natural Attractions of Enkhuizen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Enkhuizen is surrounded by water, which makes it an ideal destination for outdoor activities. Some of the top natural attractions in Enkhuizen are:

  • The Enkhuizen-Medemblik steam train ride
  • The Markermeer lake
  • The Enkhuizen Dijk beach

Hiking/Biking Routes of Enkhuizen

Enkhuizen offers numerous biking and hiking routes that you can explore to witness the stunning natural beauty of the region. Some of the top hiking and biking trails in Enkhuizen include:

  • The Enkhuizen to Medemblik cycling route
  • The Zuiderzeepad hiking trail
  • The Dijk route

Best Things to Do in Enkhuizen: Activities and Tours

Apart from the above-mentioned attractions, Enkhuizen has plenty of activities and tours that you can enjoy. Some of the best things to do in Enkhuizen include:

  • Visit the Enkhuizen harbor
  • Attend the Enkhuizen Jazz Festival
  • Explore the city on a bike tour
  • Take part in the Enkhuizen Beer Festival

Week-Long Itinerary for Enkhuizen

If you have a week to explore the city, we suggest the following itinerary:

Day Activity
Day 1 Explore the Zuiderzee Museum and the Enkhuizen City Walls
Day 2 Take a steam train ride from Enkhuizen to Medemblik
Day 3 Cycle from Enkhuizen to the Markermeer lake
Day 4 Visit the Westfries Museum and the Drommedaris Tower
Day 5 Enjoy the Enkhuizen beach and take a boat tour
Day 6 Go on a hiking trip on the Zuiderzeepad
Day 7 Attend the Enkhuizen Jazz Festival or the Enkhuizen Beer Festival

Food and Drink Scene of Enkhuizen

Enkhuizen has a vibrant food and drink culture with unique local dishes and beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

Here are the top dishes you must try while in Enkhuizen:

  • Haring (Dutch-style herring)
  • Kroket (Deep-fried Dutch croquette)
  • Stamppot (Mashed potatoes with vegetables)

Local Beverages

Don’t forget to try the following local drinks in Enkhuizen:

  • Beer from the Bierbrouwerij de Werf brewery
  • Jenever (Dutch gin)

Where to Stay in Enkhuizen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Whether you prefer a luxurious stay or a budget-friendly option, Enkhuizen offers a range of accommodations to fit your budget. Some of the top places to stay in Enkhuizen are:

  • Hotel De Koepoort
  • Herberg de Compagnie
  • Stayokay Hostel Enkhuizen
  • Bed & Breakfast De Vierhoeksluizen

What to Buy in Enkhuizen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Enkhuizen is known for its unique local products that reflect the city’s culture and history. Some of the top items to buy in Enkhuizen include:

  • Delftware pottery
  • Dutch cheese
  • Stroopwafels (Caramel-filled Dutch waffles)
  • Wooden clogs (Traditional Dutch shoes)

Local Culture and Customs of Enkhuizen

Enkhuizen has a rich cultural history, and the locals take pride in their heritage. Here are some local customs and traditions to keep in mind while visiting Enkhuizen:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Shake hands with everyone when entering a room
  • Remove your shoes when entering a house
  • Use proper table manners

Best Time to Visit Enkhuizen

The best time to visit Enkhuizen is from April to September when the weather is warm, and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Enkhuizen

Do:

  • Explore the Enkhuizen harbor
  • Attend the Enkhuizen Jazz Festival
  • Take part in the Enkhuizen Beer Festival

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local customs and traditions
  • Litter in public places
  • Ignore traffic rules

Safety and Health

Safety in Enkhuizen

Enkhuizen is generally a safe city, and visitors can explore the city without worry. However, it’s always a good idea to take precautions and stay alert in crowded areas.

Health in Enkhuizen

Enkhuizen has quality medical facilities, and visitors can find medical help if required. Make sure to have travel insurance before visiting Enkhuizen.

Getting to Enkhuizen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Enkhuizen is well-connected to major cities in the Netherlands and Europe. Here are the transportation options to reach Enkhuizen.

Airports Near Enkhuizen

The closest airport to Enkhuizen is the Amsterdam Schiphol Airport, located 73 km from the city center.

Bus Stations Near Enkhuizen

Enkhuizen has its own bus station with connections to other cities in the Netherlands.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Enkhuizen by train. The city has its own train station with connections to major cities in the Netherlands.

How to Get Around in Enkhuizen

Enkhuizen is a small city, and visitors can explore the city on foot or by bike. Bikes can be rented from various shops in the city.

Where to Go Next from Enkhuizen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Enkhuizen is located in a beautiful region of the Netherlands with plenty of nearby destinations and excursions worth exploring. Some of the top places to visit near Enkhuizen are:

  • Alkmaar
  • Amsterdam
  • Hoorn
  • Medemblik

Conclusion

Enkhuizen is a beautiful city that offers a mixture of natural and cultural attractions. From exploring the museums and historical sites to enjoying the scenic views, the city has something for everyone. Take advantage of our comprehensive Enkhuizen travel guide to make the most of your visit.
