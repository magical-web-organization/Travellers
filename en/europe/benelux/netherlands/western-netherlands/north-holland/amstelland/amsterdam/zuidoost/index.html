Zuidoost, a vibrant borough located on the southeastern edge of Amsterdam, offers visitors an array of rich cultural experiences, scenic landscapes, and outdoor activities. Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor enthusiast, a foodie, or simply looking to soak in local culture, Zuidoost has something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you on a tour of the best things to see, eat, and do while exploring this unique Amsterdam borough.

Introduction to Zuidoost

Zuidoost, which translates to “South East,” is situated in the Amstelland region of North Holland, in Western Netherlands. It is home to Amsterdam Arena, the largest stadium in the country, and boasts a colorful mosaic of cultures and communities, including a significant Afro-Surinamese population. Zuidoost offers a range of activities and attractions that attract both locals and tourists.

List of Places to Visit in Zuidoost

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Zuidoost is rich in cultural and historical significance. The borough is home to the Bijlmer Museum, which showcases the history of the Bijlmermeer district and its redevelopment after a devastating plane crash in 1992. The Museum of Bags and Purses houses an impressive collection of more than 5,000 bags dating from the Middle Ages to the present day. Meanwhile, the National Holocaust Museum focuses on Amsterdam’s Jewish history during World War II and the Holocaust.

Natural Attractions of Zuidoost: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Zuidoost is home to several parks, nature reserves, and scenic lakes, making it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The Gaasperplas recreation area boasts a beautiful lake and beaches, biking and hiking trails, and picnic areas. Visitors can also explore the Bijlmerweide, a stunning nature reserve offering a range of diverse flora and fauna. Finally, the Nelson Mandelapark offers jogging trails, a children’s playground, and a lake.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Zuidoost

With its vast natural reserves and parks, Zuidoost is ideal for explorers looking for hiking and biking trails. The Bijlmerweide offers a range of hiking trails that traverse the area’s scenic landscapes, while Gaasperplas provides visitors with biking trails that take them on a journey through a forested area and past historic castles.

Best Things to Do in Zuidoost: Activities and Tours

Zuidoost has a range of activities and tours that cater to all interests. For those looking for adventure, the Gaasperplas recreation area offers water sports, such as kayaking and windsurfing, while the Jaap Eden Ice Skating Rink provides ice-skating lessons and rentals. If you’re interested in history and culture, take a walking tour of the Bijlmermeer and learn about its rich history. Finally, if you’re looking for live entertainment, the Ziggo Dome hosts world-class concerts.

Week-Long Itinerary for Zuidoost

Day 1: Visit the Bijlmer Museum and explore the Bijlmermeer district. Day 2: Take a walking tour of Amsterdam Arena and surrounding neighborhoods. Day 3: Explore the natural beauty and diverse wildlife in the Bijlmerweide nature reserve. Day 4: Go on a biking tour around Gaasperplas recreation area. Day 5: Visit the Museum of Bags and Purses and the National Holocaust Museum. Day 6: Enjoy a day of water sports and outdoor activities at the Gaasperplas recreation area. Day 7: Take an evening concert at the Ziggo Dome.

Food and Drink Scene of Zuidoost

Zuidoost has a vibrant food and drink scene consisting of various cuisines: Surinamese, Indonesian, Turkish, and Caribbean. The Afro-Surinamese community offers mouth-watering dishes such as roti and bara, while the Turkish community provides traditional dishes such as kebab and baklava. For a unique culinary experience, try the Indonesian cuisine, characterized by its use of spices and rich flavors.

Must-Try Dishes

Roti, bara, kebab, baklava, nasi goreng, and sate.

Local Beverages

Try Surinamese and Indonesian beers, sodas, and teas.

Where to Stay in Zuidoost: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Zuidoost offers a variety of accommodations, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. The Hampton by Hilton Amsterdam Arena Boulevard is conveniently located near the Ziggo Dome and Amsterdam Arena and is an excellent choice for luxury accommodations. Alternatively, the Wow Amsterdam Hostel provides budget-friendly accommodations with a communal kitchen and spacious lounge.

What to Buy in Zuidoost: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Zuidoost provides visitors with a wide range of authentic products and souvenirs that reflect its rich culture and history. Popular souvenirs include Surinamese textiles, Indonesian spices, and Turkish silk rugs.

Local Culture and Customs of Zuidoost

Zuidoost is a diverse community with several cultural customs that visitors should be aware of. Its population comprises people from various cultural and ethnic backgrounds, and in most places, residents proudly display their cultural heritage, such as the vibrant colors of African clothing.

Best Time to Visit Zuidoost

The best time to visit Zuidoost is from May to September when the weather is warm and dry. This is ideal for exploring the borough’s numerous outdoor activities, such as the hiking and biking routes.

Do’s and Don’ts of Zuidoost

Do:

  • Respect the borough’s diverse cultural customs and heritage.
  • Try the diverse range of foods and drinks available in the area.
  • Take safety precautions when on hikes and outdoor adventures.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs without permission.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  • Wander into unknown neighborhoods late at night.

Safety and Health

Safety in Zuidoost

Zuidoost is considered a relatively safe community, but visitors should take necessary safety precautions. Lock valuables in hotel safes, be aware of your surroundings, and hold onto your bags and purses in crowded areas.

Health in Zuidoost

Zuidoost has several pharmacies and medical facilities, including the Academic Medical Center, which provides emergency care and medical services.

Getting to Zuidoost: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Zuidoost

Zuidoost is easily accessible via the Amsterdam Schiphol Airport, located just 15 minutes away by car or train.

Bus Stations Near Zuidoost

Several bus stations, including Amsterdam Bijlmer station and Amsterdam Arena station, are located throughout the borough.

Other Options

Visitors can easily travel throughout Zuidoost on foot, bike, or public transit, such as the Metro trains and buses.

How to Get Around in Zuidoost

Zuidoost has an excellent network of public transport, including buses and trains, that can take you to different parts of the borough. Biking is also popular, and several bike rental companies are available for those interested in exploring the stunning outdoors.

Where to Go Next from Zuidoost: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Zuidoost’s convenient location makes it easy to explore other nearby attractions, including Amsterdam city center, Utrecht, and Haarlem.

Conclusion

Zuidoost is more than a typical Amsterdam borough, offering a diverse range of cultural landmarks, outdoor activities, and culinary experiences. With its rich history and cultural heritage, and stunning outdoor landscapes, it is an ideal destination for travelers seeking unique and authentic experiences.
