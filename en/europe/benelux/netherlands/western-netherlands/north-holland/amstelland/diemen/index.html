Introduction to Diemen

Diemen is a quaint town situated in Amstelland, in North Holland, in Western Netherlands. It is a perfect getaway destination, close enough to Amsterdam to explore its world-renowned attractions, yet far enough to avoid the hustle and bustle of city life. Although Diemen may not be as popular as Amsterdam, it has some hidden gems you should not miss. Surrounded by beautiful landscapes, Diemen offers a unique mix of culture, history, and nature.

List of Places to Visit in Diemen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kasteel de Krijgsman: This magnificent castle dates back to the 17th century and was home to many royal families over the years. Visitors can explore and admire the elegant architecture and the surrounding gardens.
  • Diemen’s Church: This imposing church was built in the 19th century and boasts stunning stained-glass windows, impressive statues, and a serene atmosphere.

Natural Attractions of Diemen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Diemerbos: This vast forested area is perfect for hiking and biking. It also has a lake where visitors can enjoy a picnic or a swim.
  • Weespertrekvaart: This serene canal runs through Diemen and is an excellent spot for a boat ride or a leisurely walk.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Diemen

  • Diemerbos Route: This 6 km route will take you through the scenic Diemerbos forest and past Diemervijfhoek Lake.
  • Weespertrekvaart Route: This 9 km route follows the canal and is perfect for a relaxing bike ride.

Best Things to Do in Diemen: Activities and Tours

  • City Tour: A guided tour of Diemen’s historical sites and cultural attractions is a great way to get to know the town.
  • Canal Tour: There is nothing better than a relaxing boat ride down the picturesque Weespertrekvaart canal.

Week-Long Itinerary for Diemen

Day 1: Start your day with a visit to the Diemen’s Church. After that, head to the Kasteel de Krijgsman and spend time exploring the castle and its beautiful gardens.

Day 2: Take a guided tour of the town to learn about its rich history and culture. After that, enjoy a boat ride down the Weespertrekvaart canal.

Day 3: Spend the day hiking or biking through the lush Diemerbos forest and enjoy a picnic by the lake.

Day 4: Take a day trip to nearby Amsterdam, where you can explore the world-famous museums, parks, and attractions.

Day 5: Relax and unwind at one of Diemen’s cafes or restaurants, sampling delicious local cuisine.

Day 6: Take a boat tour to explore the surrounding areas of Diemen, including nearby towns and villages.

Day 7: Pack up and prepare to leave, taking with you beautiful memories of your time in Diemen.

Food and Drink Scene of Diemen

Must-Try Dishes

  • Stroopwafel: A traditional Dutch delicacy, this sweet treat consists of two thin waffles stuck together with syrup and dusted with cinnamon.
  • Bitterballen: These deep-fried meatballs are popular snacks in the Netherlands and are best enjoyed with beer.

Local Beverages

  • Jenever: This traditional Dutch liquor is distilled from malted barley, rye, and corn and is similar to gin.
  • Beer: The Netherlands is known for its rich brewing tradition, and Diemen has many local breweries where you can sample the local beer.

Where to Stay in Diemen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hotel Diemen: This cozy hotel is located in the heart of Diemen and offers comfortable rooms at affordable prices.
  • Hostel Stayokay: This affordable hostel is perfect for travelers on a budget and offers a range of dormitories and private rooms.

What to Buy in Diemen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Dutch Cheese: The Netherlands is famous for its cheese, and Diemen has many local shops where you can purchase Gouda, Edam, and other traditional cheeses.
  • Delftware: This hand-painted blue and white pottery is a classic souvenir to bring home from the Netherlands.

Local Culture and Customs of Diemen

  • Bicycles: The Netherlands is known as a bike-friendly country, and you’ll see bikes everywhere in Diemen. Be sure to rent a bike and explore the town on two wheels.
  • Coffee culture: Coffee is a big part of the Dutch culture, and Diemen has many cafes where you can enjoy a cup of coffee while people-watching.

Best Time to Visit Diemen

The best time to visit Diemen is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny, and the town comes alive with festivals and events.

Do’s and Don’ts of Diemen

Do:

  • Rent a bike: Biking is the best way to explore Diemen’s beautiful surroundings and get a feel for the town.
  • Sample the local cuisine: Diemen has many great restaurants and cafes serving traditional Dutch dishes, so be sure to try as many as you can.

Don’t:

  • Smoke in public places: The Netherlands has strict laws regarding smoking in public places, so be sure to adhere to them while in Diemen.
  • Be rude to locals: The people in Diemen are friendly and welcoming, so be sure to treat them with respect and kindness.

Safety and Health

Safety in Diemen

Diemen is a safe town, and crime rates are generally low. However, like any other place, visitors should be aware of their surroundings and take necessary precautions.

Health in Diemen

Visitors to Diemen should ensure they have comprehensive travel insurance before arriving. It is also advisable to bring any prescription medication with you, as it may not be readily available in Diemen.

Getting to Diemen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Diemen

  • Amsterdam Airport Schiphol: The nearest international airport to Diemen, located just a short drive away.

Bus Stations Near Diemen

  • Diemen NS Station: This is the primary bus and train station in Diemen and is well-connected to the rest of the country.

Other Options

  • Train: Diemen is well-connected by train to Amsterdam and other major towns and cities in the Netherlands.

How to Get Around in Diemen

The best way to get around Diemen is on foot or by bike. Bicycle rentals are affordable and widely available in town.

Where to Go Next from Diemen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Amsterdam: Just a short train ride away, Amsterdam offers a wide range of attractions, including museums, parks, and nightlife.
  • Keukenhof Gardens: Located just outside of Diemen, these beautiful gardens are a must-see for nature lovers.

Conclusion

A visit to Diemen is a perfect way to experience the beauty and charm of the Netherlands. With its rich culture, beautiful landscapes, and friendly people, there is something for everyone in Diemen. From exploring historic sites and indulging in local cuisine to hiking and biking through the forest, Diemen is a must-visit destination.
