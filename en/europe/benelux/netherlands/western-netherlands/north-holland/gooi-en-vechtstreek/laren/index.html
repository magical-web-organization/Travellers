Introduction to Laren

Welcome to Laren, a charming little village located in the heart of Gooi en Vechtstreek, in North Holland, Western Netherlands. With a population of around 11,000, Laren is known for its peaceful and idyllic surroundings, historic buildings, and rich cultural heritage. The village was founded in the 12th century and has managed to preserve much of its quaint architecture and traditional charm even today.

Whether you are an art enthusiast, history buff, nature lover, or simply looking for a quiet escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, Laren has something in store for you. In this travel guide, we take you on a tour of the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and where to eat in Laren. So come along and discover the secrets of this hidden gem!

List of Places to Visit in Laren

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Laren has a rich cultural heritage, evident in its many museums and historical places. Here are some must-visit cultural attractions in Laren:

Singer Museum

The Singer Museum is a must-visit for art lovers. The museum is housed in a beautifully restored villa and showcases an impressive collection of paintings by Dutch and international artists from the 19th and 20th centuries. The museum also organizes temporary exhibitions and cultural events throughout the year.

Het Brinkhuis

Het Brinkhuis is a historical building that has been transformed into a cultural center. It houses a library, a theater, a cinema, and several exhibition spaces. Check out their calendar of events to catch concerts, films, and other cultural events.

Old Church of Laren

The Old Church of Laren is a beautiful 14th-century church that has survived wars, fires, and floods throughout the centuries. The church has a stunning interior, with beautiful stained-glass windows, wooden pews, and a majestic organ. It is located in the village center and is open to visitors.

Natural Attractions of Laren: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Laren is situated in a beautiful natural setting, surrounded by green forests, heaths, and lakes. Here are some natural attractions in Laren that you should not miss:

Blaricumse Zwarte Berg

Blaricumse Zwarte Berg is a beautiful heathland that stretches between Laren and Blaricum. It is home to many rare plant and animal species and is a popular destination for hiking and cycling.

Larense Bos

Larense Bos is a beautiful forest that covers an area of 300 hectares. It features many walking and cycling trails, picnic areas, and a small pond. It is a perfect place for a relaxing day out in nature.

Singer Laren Sculpture Garden

The Singer Laren Sculpture Garden is a beautiful outdoor art museum that showcases sculptures by Dutch and international artists. The garden is located behind the Singer Museum and is open to visitors throughout the year.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Laren

Laren is surrounded by beautiful natural areas that offer endless opportunities for hiking and cycling. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in and around Laren:

Hiking Routes

Larense Bos Route

The Larense Bos Route is a 6.5 km circular walking route that takes you through the beautiful forest of Larense Bos. The trail is easy to navigate and offers stunning views of the forest and its wildlife.

Blaricumse Zwarte Berg Route

The Blaricumse Zwarte Berg Route is a 12 km circular walking route that takes you through the heathland of Blaricumse Zwarte Berg. The trail offers beautiful views of the heathland and its unique flora and fauna.

Biking Routes

Gooise Meren Route

The Gooise Meren Route is a 35 km circular biking route that takes you through the picturesque villages and scenic countryside of the Gooi en Vechtstreek region. The trail passes through Laren, Blaricum, Naarden, and other historic towns and villages.

Hilversumse Heide Route

The Hilversumse Heide Route is a 25 km circular biking route that takes you through the beautiful heathland and forests of Hilversumse Heide and the surrounding area. The trail offers stunning views of the countryside and is perfect for a leisurely day out.

Best Things to Do in Laren: Activities and Tours

Laren offers a wide range of activities and tours for visitors of all ages and interests. Here are some of the best things to do in Laren:

Singer Laren Events

Singer Laren organizes a variety of cultural events throughout the year, including concerts, theater performances, and films. Check their calendar of events to see what’s happening during your visit.

Het Gooi E-Bike Tour

Discover the beautiful countryside of Het Gooi in a fun and eco-friendly way with the Het Gooi E-Bike Tour. The tour takes you through the scenic landscape and historic villages of the region on electric bikes.

Larense Kermis

The Larense Kermis is an annual fair that takes place in late August. The fair features amusement rides, games, food, and drinks, and is a fun event for families and friends.

Week-Long Itinerary for Laren

If you’re planning a week-long visit to Laren, here’s an itinerary to help you make the most of your stay:

Day 1: Explore the village of Laren and its historical buildings and museums. Day 2: Visit the nearby towns of Blaricum and Huizen and discover their picturesque landscapes and historical attractions. Day 3: Take a bike tour of the surrounding villages and countryside, stopping for a picnic lunch in one of the beautiful parks or forests. Day 4: Visit the beautiful gardens of Muiderslot Castle and explore its rich history. Day 5: Discover the cultural scene of Amsterdam, just a 30-minute drive away from Laren. Day 6: Spend a day at the beach in nearby Zandvoort or Bloemendaal and soak up the sun and sea. Day 7: Take a boat tour of the beautiful lakes and canals of Gooi en Vechtstreek and enjoy the scenic surroundings.

Food and Drink Scene of Laren

Laren has a vibrant food and drink scene, with many excellent restaurants, cafes, and bars. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to explore:

Must-Try Dishes

Stroopwafels

Stroopwafels are a Dutch specialty and are a must-try when in Laren. These crispy, chewy waffles are filled with a caramel syrup and are a perfect snack to enjoy with your coffee or tea.

Bitterballen

Bitterballen are a popular Dutch snack, made with a mixture of beef or veal ragout and breadcrumbs. They are deep-fried until crispy and served with mustard or mayonnaise.

Local Beverages

Dutch Beer

The Netherlands has a rich tradition of brewing beer, and you can find a variety of excellent local beers in Laren. Try the Gooische Bierbrouwerij beers, brewed in nearby Hilversum, or the ‘t IJ brewery beers, brewed in Amsterdam.

Jenever

Jenever is a traditional Dutch spirit, similar to gin, that has been produced in the Netherlands for over 400 years. It is typically enjoyed as a shot or in a cocktail.

Where to Stay in Laren: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Laren has a variety of accommodations to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best options for your stay:

Hotels

Hotel-Restaurant Het Witte Huis

Hotel-Restaurant Het Witte Huis is a cozy family-run hotel with comfortable rooms and a restaurant that serves delicious local and international cuisine. The hotel is located in a quiet area of Laren, just a short walk from the village center.

Hotel Restaurant La Promenade

Hotel Restaurant La Promenade is a luxurious four-star hotel located in the heart of Laren. The hotel features beautiful rooms with modern amenities, a gourmet restaurant, and a fitness center.

Hostels

Stayokay Hostel Heemskerk

Located 20 km from Laren, Stayokay Hostel Heemskerk is a budget-friendly option for travelers who want to explore the region without breaking the bank. The hostel offers clean and comfortable dormitory rooms, a shared kitchen, and a bar.

Guesthouses

Het Rechthuis aan den Amstel

Het Rechthuis aan den Amstel is a beautiful guesthouse located in a restored 17th-century building. The guesthouse features elegant rooms with antique furnishings, a garden, and a terrace overlooking the Amstel River.

What to Buy in Laren: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Laren has a variety of local products and souvenirs that make for great gifts and mementos of your visit. Here are some of the best things to buy in Laren:

Dutch Cheese

The Netherlands is famous for its cheeses, and you can find a variety of delicious cheeses in Laren. Try the Gouda, Edam, or Leerdammer cheeses, which are all locally produced.

Delftware

Delftware is a type of blue and white pottery that has been produced in the Netherlands since the 17th century. You can find a variety of beautiful Delftware items, such as plates, vases, and figurines, in Laren.

Local Culture and Customs of Laren

Laren has a rich cultural heritage, with many traditions and customs that are still alive today. Here are some things to keep in mind when visiting Laren:

Language

The official language of the Netherlands is Dutch, but most people in Laren speak English as well.

Clothing

Dress comfortably for the weather conditions, especially if you are planning to spend time outdoors. Laren is a casual village, so you don’t need to dress up for most occasions.

Tipping

Tipping is not mandatory in the Netherlands, but it is customary to round up the bill or leave a small tip (10%) at restaurants, cafes, and bars.

Best Time to Visit Laren

The best time to visit Laren is during the spring and summer months (April to September), when the weather is mild and the nature is at its best. The fall (October to November) is also a beautiful time to visit, with colorful foliage and harvest festivals. The winter months (December to March) can be chilly, but the Christmas markets and winter activities make it a cozy and festive season to visit.

Do’s and Don’ts of Laren

Here are some things to keep in mind when visiting Laren:

Do:

  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local food and drink specialties.
  • Explore the many hiking and biking trails in and around Laren.

Don’t:

  • Leave litter in public spaces.
  • Smoke in public areas.
  • Be loud or disruptive in residential areas.

Safety and Health in Laren

Laren is a safe and peaceful village, with low crime rates and good healthcare facilities. However, it’s always a good idea to take usual precautions, such as keeping an eye on your belongings and avoiding poorly lit areas at night.

Getting to Laren: Transportation Options and Logistics

Laren is easily accessible by public transportation and car. Here are some transportation options to help you plan your trip:

Airports Near Laren

The nearest airport to Laren is Amsterdam Schiphol Airport, located 40 km away. From the airport, you can take public transportation or rent a car to get to Laren.

Bus Stations Near Laren

Laren has a bus station located in the village center, with regular connections to nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

You can also reach Laren by car, taxi, or bicycle. The village has good road connections with major highways, and bike rentals are available in the village center.

How to Get Around Laren

Laren is a small village that can easily be explored on foot or by bike. Taxis and buses are also available for longer distances or for those with limited mobility.

Where to Go Next from Laren: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Laren is located in the heart of the Gooi en Vechtstreek region, which offers a variety of beautiful towns, cities, and natural attractions to explore. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider after your visit to Laren:

Amsterdam

The vibrant city of Amsterdam is just a 30-minute drive from Laren. Explore its many museums, cultural attractions, and historic landmarks, or take a boat tour of its beautiful canals.

Utrecht

The historic city of Utrecht is located 40 km from Laren and is known for its beautiful Gothic architecture, canals, and lively cultural scene.

Naarden

The fortified town of Naarden is located 10 km from Laren and is a UNESCO World Heritage site. It features a stunning 17th-century fortress, beautiful churches, and museums.

Conclusion

Laren is a hidden gem in the heart of the Netherlands, offering a perfect blend of nature, culture, and history. Whether you’re a fan of art, food, outdoor adventures, or simply looking for a quiet escape, Laren has something in store for you. We hope that our travel guide has inspired you to explore this beautiful village and discover its many secrets!
