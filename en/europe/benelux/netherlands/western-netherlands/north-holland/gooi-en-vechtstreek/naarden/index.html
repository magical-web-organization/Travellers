Comprehensive Travel Guide to Naarden, Netherlands

Naarden is a charming fortified town situated in the heart of Gooi en Vechtstreek, North Holland, in Western Netherlands. Naarden is a well-preserved, picturesque town that reflects the authentic Dutch history and culture. This town is known for its historic monuments, beautiful nature, traditional architecture and peaceful surroundings. In this comprehensive travel guide to Naarden, you will find everything you need to know about this destination.

Introduction to Naarden

Naarden is a unique town that has managed to keep its essence intact, despite modernisation. Historically, Naarden was founded in the 14th century as a strategic defence town and is recognised as one of the best-preserved fortified towns in the Netherlands.

List of Places to Visit in Naarden

Naarden has plenty to offer to its visitors. Whether you’re interested in history, nature or culture, Naarden has something for everyone.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Great Church of Naarden - a historic church with beautiful stained-glass windows
  • The Netherlands Fortress Museum - displaying the military history of the Netherlands
  • The Comenius Museum - dedicated to the famous Czech philosopher and educator Jan Amos Comenius
  • The Vesting Museum - showcasing the history and defence mechanism of the fortress

Natural Attractions of Naarden: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Naarden-Vesting Wall – a circular walkway on top of the Naarden fortress walls
  • Naardermeer Lake - a beautiful lake surrounded by gorgeous greenery. Ideal for bird watching and a perfect location for hikers
  • Goois Natuurreservaat - a nature reserve with diverse flora and fauna, and ideal for nature lovers and hikers

Hiking/Biking Routes of Naarden

There are various hiking and biking trails that allow visitors to explore the beautiful scenery, view the historic monuments and enjoy the town’s peaceful ambiance. The Groene Wissel and Rondje Naardermeer routes are two of the most popular ones.

Best Things to Do in Naarden: Activities and Tours

  • Naarden Weekend Market - a famous outdoor market held every Saturday in Naarden
  • Boat Tour of Naarden - a guided tour around the fortress on a boat led by local tour guides
  • Cinema Bussum - a vintage cinema in the nearby town of Bussum that offers a unique movie experience

Week-Long Itinerary for Naarden

Day 1: Explore the Naarden fortress and visit The Netherlands Fortress Museum

Day 2: Take a stroll in Goois Natuurreservaat and explore Naardermeer Lake and its surroundings

Day 3: Visit The Great Church of Naarden and the Comenius Museum

Day 4: Go shopping at Naarden Weekend Market, followed by a relaxing boat tour of the fortress

Day 5: Take a day trip to the nearby town of Bussum and visit Cinema Bussum

Day 6: Explore the surrounding area and take a hike or bike ride along the beautiful route of Groene Wissel

Day 7: Relax and enjoy the slow-paced lifestyle of Naarden

Food and Drink Scene of Naarden

Naarden has a unique food and drink scene that reflects its cultural identity and history.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Stamppot - a traditional Dutch dish made of mashed potatoes and vegetables
  • Goudse Kaas - famous Dutch cheese made from cow’s milk

Local Beverages

  • Jenever - a traditional Dutch gin
  • Heineken - a widely recognised Dutch beer

Where to Stay in Naarden: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Naarden offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Some of the popular hotels are:

  • NH Naarden Hotel
  • Bastion Hotel
  • Hotel Amrath Gooiland

What to Buy in Naarden: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Traditional wooden shoes - a famous Dutch souvenir
  • Cheese - Goudse Kaas is a popular souvenir from the Netherlands

Local Culture and Customs of Naarden

Naarden has a strong culture and identity that is reflected in its festivals and traditional celebrations. Some of the popular festivals include the Naarden International Piano Festival and the Annual Naarden Summer Festival.

Best Time to Visit Naarden

The best time to visit Naarden is from April to October when the weather is mild, and the town is less crowded.

Do’s and Don’ts of Naarden

Do:

  • Respect the town’s cultural heritage and traditions
  • Try the local food and drinks
  • Take a boat tour around the Naarden fortress

Don’t:

  • Throw litter on the streets
  • Take photographs of people without their permission
  • Be disrespectful towards local customs and habits

Safety and Health

Safety in Naarden

Naarden is a safe town with low crime rates. However, tourists should stay vigilant and take necessary precautions to avoid petty theft and pickpocketing.

Health in Naarden

Naarden is a well-equipped town with well-established healthcare facilities. Tourists are advised to take their health insurance documents with them.

Getting to Naarden: Transportation Options and Logistics

Naarden is located within close proximity to Amsterdam, making it easy to access by various modes of transportation.

Airports Near Naarden

  • Amsterdam Airport Schiphol is the nearest international airport to Naarden.

Bus Stations Near Naarden

  • Naarden-Bussum railway station offers regular bus services to and from Naarden.

Other Options

  • Visitors can also hire a taxi or rent a car to reach Naarden from Amsterdam.

How to Get Around in Naarden

Naarden is a small town, and most of the important tourist spots are within walking distance. Taxis and bicycles are also available for rent if required.

Where to Go Next from Naarden: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Amsterdam - just 25 km southwest of Naarden and is easily accessible by train or bus
  • Utrecht - located just 45 km east from Naarden and offers a beautiful historic cityscape

Conclusion

Naarden is a hidden gem that has a lot to offer to its visitors. This fortified town boasts of cultural heritage, scenic views, and delicious food and drinks. With this comprehensive guide, you can plan your trip to Naarden and make the most out of your journey.
