Introduction to Zaandam

Nestled in the region of Zaanstreek-Waterland in North Holland, Zaandam is a hidden gem in the Netherlands that boasts of picturesque windmills, historic monuments and colorful buildings. The city is located just a few kilometers away from Amsterdam, making it a perfect day trip destination for tourists who want to experience a quieter side of the Netherlands. In this travel guide, we will reveal the best things to do in Zaandam, the must-try foods, ideal accommodations, and everything in between that will make your visit truly worthwhile.

List of Places to Visit in Zaandam

Zaandam is a small city but packed with wonderful attractions, combining both nature and culture. Here are some of the top tourist spots in Zaandam:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. The Zaanse Schans open-air museum
  2. The Czaar Peterhuisje Museum
  3. The Honig Breethuis Museum

Natural Attractions of Zaandam: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. The Zaandam windmills
  2. Zaanse Schans Nature Reserve
  3. Wormerveer Forest

Hiking/Biking Routes of Zaandam

Zaandam has a well-developed biking and hiking infrastructure, with safe routes through the city and prime paths through natural reserves and scenic countryside. The most popular hiking and biking routes are available at the Zaanse Schans open-air museum or at the tourist information center in town.

Best Things to Do in Zaandam: Activities and Tours

Zaandam caters to different kinds of travelers. Here are some of the best activities and tours you can do in and around Zaandam:

River Cruise

Explore the windmills and traditional industrial heritage sites on the river cruise along Zaan river.

Chocolate Workshop

Visit the CacaoLab and learn the art of making Dutch chocolate.

Boat Ride

Take a boat tour of the city to explore the picturesque architecture, canal houses and landmark buildings.

Week-Long Itinerary for Zaandam

Here’s a suggested week-long itinerary to maximize your stay in Zaandam:

Food and Drink Scene of Zaandam

Must-Try Dishes

One of the joys of traveling is trying out the local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes in Zaandam:

  1. Stroopwafel - delicious sweet, caramel-filled waffles
  2. Poffertjes - small puffy pancakes served with butter and powdered sugar
  3. Rijsttafel - an Indonesian transformed experience of lots of small dishes, best enjoyed in groups

Local Beverages

Complete your cultural immersion in Zaandam by trying out their local beverages:

  1. Jenever - traditional Dutch gin
  2. Local Beers - Zaansche Schans amber, honey and cherry beers

Where to Stay in Zaandam: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Zaandam offers a selection of accommodations, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. The town also has numerous Airbnb rentals that provide a more authentic Dutch experience. Some of the best accommodations in the city include:

  1. Inntel Hotels Amsterdam Zaandam
  2. Best Western Zaan Inn
  3. Heerlijck Slaapen Op De Zaanse Schans

What to Buy in Zaandam: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

In your visit to Zaandam, there are plenty of great souvenirs to bring home:

  1. Delftware Pottery, a typical Dutch ceramic that’s often painted blue and white.
  2. Real traditional Dutch cheese, such as Gouda or Edam.
  3. Dutch Clogs, the famous wooden shoes that are surprisingly comfy.

Local Culture and Customs of Zaandam

As is the case of any place, Zaandam has particular customs and traditions. The town has a rich history of producing chocolate, colourful buildings and waterways, and a distinctive architecture of the Zaanse Schans area.

Best Time to Visit Zaandam

The best time to visit Zaandam is between March to May when you will find optimal temperatures and beautiful tulips and flowers blooming around the region. Summer is also an excellent time to visit if you don’t mind long lines and crowds in tourist spots.

Do’s and Don’ts of Zaandam

Do:

  1. Say “dag” or “hallo” as a greeting.
  2. Respect the local culture and treat the clothing with the conservative style.
  3. Be punctual for appointments.

Don’t:

  1. Speak too loudly in public.
  2. Over-consume alcohol.
  3. Forget to be vigilant of bike riders while walking.

Safety and Health

Safety in Zaandam

Zaandam is a safe place to visit, but like any other major tourist center, visitors should take care of their belongings and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Zaandam

The Netherlands has an excellent health care system that offers a high level of treatment. Before traveling, make sure to obtain health travel insurance from your provider.

Getting to Zaandam: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Zaandam

The nearest major international airport is Amsterdam Schiphol Airport, which is about 20 minutes away.

Bus Stations Near Zaandam

Zaandam has several bus lines from Amsterdam Central Station, and surrounding cities

Other Options

Zaandam is also accessible by train and car from neighboring European countries. There are train stations in the city center that can take you to other popular destinations in the Netherlands, such as Rotterdam, The Hague, or Amsterdam.

How to Get Around in Zaandam

Zaandam is a relatively small town, easily navigable by bicycle or on foot. The city also has a reliable bus service and train network that can take you to other parts of the Netherlands easily.

Where to Go Next from Zaandam: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Amsterdam, the bustling capital city of the Netherlands
  2. Keukenhof Gardens, where you can find the most famous Tulip Garden in the world.
  3. Haarlem, a picturesque, medieval town with plenty of history and architecture.

Conclusion

Zaandam has plenty of offer for visitors who want to experience the local culture and natural beauty of the Netherlands. With its winding waterways, historic windmills, local delicacies, and unique architecture, it is indeed a hidden gem in the heart of the country. Plan your visit to Zaandam today and create lifelong memories of a Dutch adventure like no other.
