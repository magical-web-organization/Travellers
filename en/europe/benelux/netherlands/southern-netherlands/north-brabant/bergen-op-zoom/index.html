Introduction to Bergen op Zoom

Bergen op Zoom is one of the most charming cities in the Southern Netherlands, known for its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. Located in the North Brabant province, Bergen op Zoom is a perfect blend of contemporary glamour and ancient traditions. The city has a rich history that dates back to the 13th century, which is reflected in its well-preserved architecture, museums, and galleries.

Whether you’re a history buff looking for a glimpse into the past or an adventurer seeking outdoor thrills, Bergen op Zoom has something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit, activities to do, and local experiences to enjoy in Bergen op Zoom.

List of Places to Visit in Bergen op Zoom

Bergen op Zoom is home to several cultural and natural attractions that cater to all kinds of travelers. Here’s a list of places you must visit while in Bergen op Zoom.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Bergen op Zoom is steeped in history, and its museums and historical places are a testament to its rich cultural heritage. Here are some must-visit attractions for history buffs:

  • Markiezenhof Museum: This beautiful palace-turned-museum is one of the most iconic landmarks of Bergen op Zoom. It showcases the city’s rich cultural history through its impressive collection of art, antiquities, and artifacts.
  • Grote Kerk: This stunning Gothic church is one of the oldest buildings in Bergen op Zoom, and an important religious site. The church is known for its beautiful stained-glass windows and intricate architecture.
  • Het Markiezenhof: This 14th-century castle is now a museum that showcases the cultural history of Bergen op Zoom. Visitors can take a guided tour of the castle and explore its many exhibits.

Natural Attractions of Bergen op Zoom: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Bergen op Zoom is blessed with natural beauty that attracts adventurers and nature lovers. Here are some places you must add to your itinerary:

  • Kalmthoutse Heide: Located just outside Bergen op Zoom, this beautiful nature reserve is a haven for bird watchers and nature enthusiasts. It offers several hiking trails, cycle routes, and picturesque picnic spots.
  • Fort de Roovere: This historical fort is known for its stunning views of the city and its strategic location. Visitors can take a guided tour of the fort or explore its many hiking and cycling trails.
  • Binnenschelde: This large artificial lake is a popular spot for water sports and outdoor activities. It offers several amenities such as picnic areas, hiking trails, and recreational facilities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bergen op Zoom

Bergen op Zoom boasts several scenic hiking and bike trails that offer some of the most beautiful views of the countryside. Here are some popular options:

  • Brabantse Wal: This hiking trail is part of a larger network of trails that runs through the Brabantse Wal region. It offers panoramic views of the city and the surrounding countryside.
  • Blind Wall Route: This outdoor art gallery features several murals painted on the walls of buildings in Bergen op Zoom. Visitors can take a guided tour or explore it on their own with a map.
  • Mastbos: This stunning forest offers several hiking and cycling routes through its beautiful landscapes. It’s located just outside Bergen op Zoom, making it a perfect day trip.

Best Things to Do in Bergen op Zoom: Activities and Tours

Bergen op Zoom has something for everyone, whether you’re looking for a cultural experience or an adventure. Here are some of the best things to do while in Bergen op Zoom:

  • Food Tours: Take a food tour of Bergen op Zoom and sample the local delicacies such as the famous Brabantse worstenbroodje (sausage roll) and beers from the local breweries.
  • Cultural Tours: Take a guided tour of the city and explore its rich cultural history. Visit historical landmarks such as the Grote Markt and the Stadhuis, and learn about the city’s past.
  • Outdoor Adventures: Go kayaking on the Binnenschelde lake, try your hand at rock climbing in the De Zeeland quarry, or take a hot air balloon ride for a bird’s eye view of the city.

Food and Drink Scene of Bergen op Zoom

Bergen op Zoom has a vibrant food and drink scene that showcases the best of local cuisine and beer. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Brabantse worstenbroodje: This delicious sausage roll is a must-try when in Bergen op Zoom. It’s a simple dish, but the local bakeries have perfected it over the years.
  • Stoofvlees: This traditional beef stew is a staple in Brabantse cuisine. It’s typically served with fries or bread and is a hearty and delicious meal.
  • Asperges: Asparagus is a popular vegetable in Netherlands, and Bergen op Zoom is known for its delicious asparagus dishes. Try it in a soup or as a side dish.

Local Beverages

  • Bergs Beer: This local brewery produces delicious Belgian-inspired beers that are a must-try for beer lovers.
  • Rode Wijn: Red wine is a popular drink in the Netherlands, and Bergen op Zoom has several vineyards that produce delicious red wine.

What to Buy in Bergen op Zoom: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Bergen op Zoom has several unique souvenirs and local products that make great gifts for friends and family. Here are some popular options:

  • Antiques and Art: Bergen op Zoom is known for its art and antiques markets, where visitors can shop for unique pieces of art and artifacts.
  • Cheese and Dairy Products: Brabantse cuisine is known for its amazing cheese and dairy products such as cheese spreads and yogurts. These make great gifts to take back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Bergen op Zoom

Bergen op Zoom is a melting pot of cultures and traditions, with a vibrant local community that’s proud of its heritage. Here are some customs you should be aware of:

  • Bergen op Zoom Carnival: The city celebrates Carnival every year, which is a five-day festival full of parades, music, and food. It’s a great time to experience the local culture and traditions.
  • Cycling Culture: Bergen op Zoom has a strong cycling culture, and visitors are encouraged to explore the city on two wheels. Most streets have dedicated bike lanes, and there are several cycling routes that take you through the beautiful countryside.

Best Time to Visit Bergen op Zoom

The best time to visit Bergen op Zoom is during the summer months between June and August when the weather is pleasant and sunny. However, spring is a great time to visit as well, with the beautiful tulip fields in bloom and several outdoor activities available.

Getting to Bergen op Zoom: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bergen op Zoom is well-connected to the rest of Netherlands, making it easily accessible for travelers. Here are some transportation options:

The nearest major airport is Amsterdam’s Schiphol Airport, which is about two hours away from Bergen op Zoom. Other airports in the region include Rotterdam The Hague Airport and Eindhoven Airport.

Bus Stations Near Bergen op Zoom

Bergen op Zoom has several bus stations that connect it to other cities in the region. Some popular bus companies include Arriva, Connexxion, and NS.

Other Options

Visitors can also take the train to Bergen op Zoom, as it’s well-connected to other cities in the region. The nearest train station is Bergen op Zoom Station, which has several train connections to Rotterdam, Amsterdam, and other cities.
