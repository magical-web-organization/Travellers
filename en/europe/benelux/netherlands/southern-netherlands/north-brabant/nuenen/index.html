Introduction to Nuenen

Nuenen is a picturesque village located in the southern region of the Netherlands in the province of North Brabant. It’s renowned for its idyllic countryside, historical landmarks, and vibrant arts and culture scene. Home to around 23,000 inhabitants, Nuenen offers a charming respite from the bustling cities of Amsterdam and Rotterdam. The village is famously known as the former residence of Vincent van Gogh, one of the most celebrated artists in history. Follow Van Gogh’s footsteps and take a journey into Nuenen’s stunning landscapes by following its scenic biking and hiking routes.

List of Places to Visit in Nuenen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Vincentre - explores the works of Vincent van Gogh and his life in Nuenen.
  • Van Gogh-Roosegaarde Cycle Path - a unique path that lit with thousands of twinkling stones, inspired by Vincent Van Gogh's masterpiece "The Starry Night"
  • Museum Nuenen - located in the former residence of Vincent van Gogh, exhibits the village's rich history and traditions.
  • Park Gulbergen - an expansive park with multiple walking and biking trails, situated over sand dunes and pine forests

Natural Attractions of Nuenen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Eeneindse Plas - a pristine lake that offers various recreational activities such as swimming, fishing, and windsurfing.
  • Lunet - a beautiful walking trail that leads past a historic fortification with stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
  • Boslustweg - a scenic walking path that immerses you in the soothing sights and sounds of nature.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Nuenen

  • The Van Gogh Cycle Route - a 335-kilometer bike trail that takes you through the villages, countryside, and notable landmarks that inspired Van Gogh's paintings, originating from Nuenen.
  • The Butterfly Route - a 13-kilometer bike route that takes you through farms, fields, and meadows filled with colorful wildflowers and butterflies.
  • The Vincent's Walk - a walking tour that takes you through the locations where Van Gogh lived and worked in Nuenen, introducing you to the village's exciting cultural and natural dimensions.

Best Things to Do in Nuenen: Activities & Tours

  • Electric Tour - Nuenen in a New Light - an illuminating and informative e-bike tour that takes you through Nuenen's starry landscape, its art, and its people.
  • Wine and Art Walk - Nuenen Wines - This walking tour takes you through Nuenen's unique and unusual wine cellars, where you can taste wines and learn about the winemaking process in the village.
  • Museum Nuenen Tour and Workshop - a tour of the Nuenen Museum and a workshop where you can learn how to paint like Van Gogh and take home your own masterpiece.

Food and Drink Scene of Nuenen

Must-Try Dishes

  • Boerenkool met Worst - a Dutch dish made with mashed potatoes, kale, and sausage.
  • Stroopwafels - a national Dutch sweet treat that's made from two thin, waffle-like layers stuck together with syrup.
  • Hutspot - a mashed potato dish mixed with carrots and onions.

Local Beverages

  • Jenever - a traditional Dutch gin beautifully paired with snacks like pickled herring and ossenworst.
  • Bavaria Brewery - a family-owned brewery that makes a range of beers from lagers to IPA.
  • Nuenen Wines - a unique blend of seven grape varieties grown in Nuenen's vineyards.

Where to Stay in Nuenen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • De Collse Hoeve - a cozy hotel situated in a peaceful forest area with famous Dutch cuisine served on-site.
  • B&B De Beekhoeve - a comfortable bed and breakfast surrounded by Nuenen's farmland, complete with an outdoor pool and terrace.
  • Auberge Vincent - a lodging situated within walking distance of Van Gogh's Residence and features a restaurant with regional and international cuisine.

What to Buy in Nuenen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Van Gogh Reproductions - prints of Van Gogh's famous artwork and reproductions are available in the gift shops around Nuenen.
  • Cheese - regional cheeses sold locally made in the Brabant region.
  • Bier and Wijn - take home locally made wine and beer, available for purchase in most souvenir shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Nuenen

Nuenen is a village that celebrates its history, art, and nature, with many of its traditions embodied in textiles, gastronomy, and music. The people of Nuenen are renowned for their hospitality and love for Dutch culture and tradition. They celebrate various festivals such as the Van Gogh Festival—which comprises music, theater, and literature—a magical experience that brings together art enthusiasts, nature lovers, and cultural travelers. Nueners also celebrate cycling and hiking as a part of their history, and you can join any Fortenroute ravel adventure that takes visitors through Nuenen’s original medieval forts, including many nature preserves.

Best Time to Visit Nuenen

The best time to visit Nuenen is from mid-April to September, especially during the Van Gogh Festival held in late August, where art, music, culture, and nature combine to form a unique and immersive experience. Springtime in Nuenen is magical as flowers, trees, and meadows come to life, making it perfect for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. Throughout the year, the village celebrates its rich culture and history by hosting various festivals, markets, and exhibitions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Nuenen

Do:

  • Take a scenic bike ride through Nuenen's picturesque countryside.
  • Taste the local wines and cheeses in Nuenen's quaint local shops.
  • Visit Vincent's house to learn more about the artist's work and life.

Don’t:

  • Litter in Nuenen's stunning natural surroundings.
  • Disrespect people from Nuenen's culture and heritage.
  • Leave your bike unsecured.

Safety and Health

Safety in Nuenen

Nuenen is a safe village with low crime rates; however, it’s always best to take precautions and stick to well-lit streets at night. Be aware of bike theft, especially in areas of high pedestrian traffic.

Health in Nuenen

Nuenen has quality health care systems with reputable physicians and medical facilities. Ensure you have health insurance coverage before visiting, and bring all essential medication you may need throughout your trip.

Getting to Nuenen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Nuenen is accessible through multiple means of transportation, making it relatively easy to get to:

Airports Near Nuenen

  • Eindhoven Airport (EIN) - located only 10 kilometers from Nuenen, the airport has direct flights to and from numerous European destinations.
  • Amsterdam Airport Schiphol (AMS) - The largest airport in the Netherlands, is well connected globally with one-hour train service to Eindhoven.

Bus Stations Near Nuenen

  • The Eindhoven railway station and Transferium lie within easy reach of Nuenen, with buses departing frequently during the day.
  • Other options include the Helmond railway station and the Best railway station, located around 7km from Nuenen village.

Other Options

Since Nuenen is on the outskirts of Eindhoven, getting there by car or taxi is also feasible, with well-connected highways covering the area.

How to Get Around in Nuenen

Nuenen village is easy to navigate by bike, offering picturesque routes through the countryside that take in many of the landmarks and beautiful scenery. The village center is also small enough to explore on foot. Buses are also available for getting to areas further out of the village.

Where to Go Next from Nuenen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Eindhoven - A vibrant student city, filled with modern architecture, innovative technology, and a vibrant nightlife scene.
  • Den Bosch - A picturesque medieval town with beautiful scenery and friendly locals
  • Breda - A city known for its history, art, and culture, this charming city is a sight to behold.

Conclusion

Nuenen offers an ideal escape for anyone looking for a relaxing and culturally rich holiday. Offering an enchanting blend of art, history, culture, and nature, the village has something for everyone. From its beautiful landscapes to its rich cultural landmarks, Nuenen is sure to delight and inspire the senses of even the seasoned traveler.
