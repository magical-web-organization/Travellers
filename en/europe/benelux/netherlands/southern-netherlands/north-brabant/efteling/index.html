Introduction to Efteling

Located in North Brabant, in Southern Netherlands, Efteling is a magical theme park that has been enchanting visitors of all ages for over 68 years. Spread across 72 hectares of natural forest, the park is home to over 30 thrilling rides, live shows, and attractions that bring fairy tales to life. From enchanting castles and thrilling rollercoasters to mystical forests and impressive water shows, Efteling is the perfect destination for a fun-filled day out with the family.

List of Places to Visit in Efteling

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Fairytale Forest: A charming walk-through attraction that features over 30 classic fairy tales, brought to life with amazing animatronics and special effects.
  • Villa Volta: A twisted house that spins around and around while telling the story of the cursed Bokkenrijders.
  • The Flying Dutchman: A water coaster that takes visitors on a spine-chilling adventure through a dark and eerie landscape.
  • The Python: A thrilling rollercoaster that loops and corkscrews its way through the trees.
  • Droomvlucht: A beautiful, dreamlike ride that takes visitors through magical forests, enchanting gardens, and mystical caves, while accompanied by the sounds of an enchanting musical score.

Natural Attractions of Efteling: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Bob: A bobsled ride that features steep drops and thrilling twists and turns.
  • The Vliegende Hollander: A water ride with a dark and scary surprise ending.
  • Raveleijn: A live horse show with a medieval theme and amazing special effects.
  • Polka Marina: A gentle water ride that takes visitors around a picturesque lighthouse.
  • Aquanura: An enchanting water show that features fountains, music, and lights.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Efteling

Efteling offers several hiking and biking routes that enable visitors to explore the lush surrounding forest. The Boos, Brute Heuvel, and Boze Wolf routes traverse the wooded terrain, while the Brossel and Baron 1898 routes offer breathtaking views of the park’s attractions and surrounding landscape.

Best Things to Do in Efteling: Activities and Tours

  • Take a ride on the Fairytale Forest train that chugs through the heart of the park.
  • Meet your favorite fairy tale characters and get a photo with them to commemorate your visit.
  • Join a guided tour of the park to learn more about its history and attractions.
  • Watch the thrilling water show of Aquanura, after sunset when the lights are shining bright.
  • Enjoy a relaxing ride on the Grand Carrousel, an authentic 100-year-old carousel from 1895 Germany.

Week-Long Itinerary for Efteling

Day 1 - Visit the Fairytale Forest and Villa Volta, and enjoy a relaxing stroll in the park. Day 2 - Check out the Python and travel back in time with the Droomvlucht ride. Day 3 - Explore the Bob sled ride, and watch the Raveleijn live show. Day 4 - Take a scenic ride on the Vliegende Hollander water ride and go on a guided tour of the park. Day 5 - Take a spin on the Polka Marina and enjoy some downtime with a picnic in the park. Day 6 - Visit the Aquanura water show, and ride on the Grand Carrousel carousel. Day 7 - Make merry memories with the Merry-go-Round and the wooden rollercoaster.

Food and Drink Scene of Efteling

Must-Try Dishes

  • Poffertjes: Sweet Dutch mini pancakes that are a favorite with visitors.
  • Stroopwafels: Delicious syrup-filled waffles that are a popular Dutch treat.
  • Frikandel: A deep-fried sausage that is served with a variety of sauces.
  • Kibbeling: Fried pieces of cod that are a favorite Dutch snack.

Local Beverages

  • Heineken Beer: A popular Dutch beer that is served throughout the park.
  • Jenever: A traditional Dutch gin that is a specialty in the region.
  • Apple Juice: Freshly pressed apple juice that is a refreshing beverage option.

Where to Stay in Efteling: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Efteling Hotel: A charming and spacious hotel with a fairy-tale theme.
  • Hotel de Kroon: An elegant, four-star hotel with modern amenities.
  • Guesthouse de Jong: A cozy and comfortable guesthouse with a friendly atmosphere.

What to Buy in Efteling: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Fairytale-themed T-shirts, hoodies, and bags.
  • Traditional Dutch wooden shoes, also called clogs.
  • Handmade pottery from local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Efteling

The people of North Brabant, and the Netherlands in general, are known for their friendliness and welcoming nature. Additionally, tipping is not customary in this country, so it is not necessary to leave a tip after eating, drinking, or utilizing other services.

Best Time to Visit Efteling

Efteling is open year-round, but it is busiest during the summer months of July and August. For a less crowded experience, it is advised that visitors plan to visit during the off-season of October to March, or on weekdays during the peak season.

Do’s and Don’ts of Efteling

Do:

  • Follow the guidelines and instructions of the park staff to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.
  • Bring a refillable water bottle to stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Take advantage of the park’s FastPass or skip-the-line tickets to save time waiting in lines.

Don’t:

  • Smoke or vape in the park, as it is strictly prohibited.
  • Bring outside food and drinks into the park, as there are several food and drink options available inside the park.
  • Use flash photography on rides or in dark areas, as it can disrupt other guests and cause safety concerns.

Safety and Health

Safety in Efteling

Efteling takes safety seriously and has several protocols in place to ensure the safety of its visitors. The park’s staff regularly inspects the rides and attractions and conducts safety checks before they are opened to the public. Additionally, the park has trained medical personnel on standby in case of an emergency.

Health in Efteling

The park offers several first aid stations which are marked throughout the park. Visitors are encouraged to carry any necessary medication with them and be aware of any allergies or medical conditions they may have.

Getting to Efteling: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Efteling

The nearest international airport is Schiphol Airport in Amsterdam, which offers several transportation options to Efteling, including trains, buses, and rental cars.

Bus Stations Near Efteling

Visitors can take the bus to Efteling from several nearby cities, including Tilburg and Waalwijk.

Other Options

Efteling is also easily accessible by train. The nearest station is the Tilburg station, which is approximately 20 minutes from the park.

How to Get Around in Efteling

Efteling is easily navigable on foot, but visitors can also take advantage of the park’s train and boat rides to travel around the park. Additionally, Efteling has several wheelchair-accessible attractions and areas, making it universally accessible.

Where to Go Next from Efteling: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • The city of Tilburg: A vibrant university city that offers several cultural and entertainment options.
  • De Efteling Loonsche Land Hotel: An eco-hotel that provides guests with a unique and sustainable experience.
  • National Park De Loonse en Drunense Duinen: A stunning natural reserve with sand dunes and heaths that is perfect for hiking and cycling.

Conclusion

Efteling is a charming and magical theme park that offers a unique and enchanting experience for all visitors. With its thrilling rides, live shows, and attractions, and its lush surrounding forest, Efteling is the perfect destination for an enchanting day out. From souvenir shopping to exploring the local culture and customs, this comprehensive guide has covered everything you need to know to plan your next trip to Efteling, the Fairytale Theme Park of the Netherlands.
