Introduction to Valkenburg aan de Geul

Valkenburg aan de Geul is a picturesque town located in South Limburg, in Limburg, Netherlands. It is a small town with an outstanding historical importance and natural beauty. It was first inhabited by the Celts in the 5th century BC, later the Romans, who also contributed to its development. The town has many interesting historic sites, scenic routes and museums to visit. Valkenburg aan de Geul offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and rich history that every traveller will appreciate, and the town is easily accessible by car, train or bus.

List of Places to Visit in Valkenburg aan de Geul

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Valkenburg Castle - A medieval castle situated on a hilltop that provides scenic views of the surrounding Limburg hills. The castle now hosts several excursions and exhibitions throughout the year.
  • Gemeentegrot Cave - An elaborate cave system dating back to the Roman Period, which was later used as a shelter for the villagers in the Second World War.
  • Museum Land van Valkenburg - This museum has art from different time periods and displays the rich history of the region, including artefacts from the Bronze and Iron Age and various Roman finds.
  • Roman Catacombs Valkenburg aan de Geul - These catacombs are situated below the Church of Saint Paul and contain many fascinating details of the Church’s holy past.

Natural Attractions of Valkenburg aan de Geul: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Valkenburg Castle Ruins - These ruins of the once-mighty castle are beautifully maintained and offer stunning views of the surrounding areas.
  • Cauberg - A hill located towards the southern side of Valkenburg aan de Geul. It is a popular spot for hiking and cycling and also has a ski slope.
  • Velvet Cave - This is an extensive cave system that has some ancient artwork and stalagmites that are worth a visit.
  • Bicycle tour of the small town - Pedal through the town to explore the quiet streets, historic landmarks, shops, and restaurants at your own pace while enjoying the scenic beauty of the town.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Valkenburg aan de Geul

Valkenburg aan de Geul is a hiker’s paradise and a cyclist’s delight! Its winding hills, valleys and countryside are perfect for mountain biking enthusiasts. Some of the popular routes are:

  • Cauberg Hill - A steep and twisty ride that culminates in breathtaking views of the town.
  • Valkenburg Loop - It is a 20 km loop through small towns and scenic landscapes.
  • Geul Valley - A smooth and easy ride along the river Geul through beautiful landscapes.
  • Three Country Point Route - A 30 km route through three countries, Belgium, Germany, and Netherlands.

Best Things to Do in Valkenburg aan de Geul: Activities and Tours

  • Amstel Gold Race Experience - Visit the professional Amstel Gold Race event where you can experience the thrill of cycling on a professional circuit.
  • Laurensberg - A trip to Laurensberg, Germany which is just a few kilometers away from Valkenburg. The town will leave you agape with quaint architecture, local food and drinks.
  • Thermae 2000 Spa - After all the exploring indulge in some relaxation time at this thermal spa with its mineral baths and massages.
  • Valkenburg Christmas Market - Get into the holiday spirit by visiting the amazing Christmas market where you can get local products while enjoying the festive spirit of the town.

Week-Long Itinerary for Valkenburg aan de Geul

Day 1: Visit the Valkenburg Castle Ruins and the Velvet Caves. Day 2: Explore the scenic routes around Valkenburg aan de Geul on a bike tour. Day 3: Visit the town’s most popular attraction, the Gemeentegrot Cave. Day 4: Go skiing or snowboarding on the slopes of Cauberg Hill in the winter, or take a trip to the Countryside to nearby towns like Maastricht. Day 5: Tour the town on foot to explore every nook and cranny. End the day with a refreshing soak at Thermae 2000 Spa. Day 6: Visit the Roman Catacombs at Saint Paul’s Church and check out the Museum Land van Valkenburg. Day 7: Finish your trip by visiting the Laurensberg, Germany.

  • Pannenkoeken - Dutch pancakes that are a must-try in Valkenburg aan de Geul.

Local Culture and Customs of Valkenburg aan de Geul

The town has a rich cultural heritage that is visible through their historic landmarks and apparent in the local culinary traditions. Limburgish is a regional language spoken in the town, and the town has a vibrant tradition of music, theatre, and festivals.

Best Time to Visit Valkenburg aan de Geul

The best time to visit Valkenburg aan de Geul is in the summer months between May to September when the weather is perfect for outdoor activities, and the town is bustling with tourists.

Safety and Health

Safety in Valkenburg aan de Geul

Valkenburg aan de Geul is a safe town, but tourists should take care of their belongings and keep an eye on their surroundings, particularly during the busy tourist season.

Health in Valkenburg aan de Geul

The town has several pharmacies and hospitals that can assist with any health concerns or medical emergencies.

Getting to Valkenburg aan de Geul: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Valkenburg aan de Geul

The closest airport to Valkenburg aan de Geul is Maastricht Aachen Airport, which is approximately 10 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Valkenburg aan de Geul

The town has a bus station located just outside the town centre, which connects with multiple towns in the region.

Other Options

The town is easily accessible by train, with a station located within walking distance of the town centre.

How to Get Around in Valkenburg aan de Geul

The town is small enough to be explored on foot. Biking is also popular, and there are several rental shops for tourists who want to explore the town on two wheels.

Where to Go Next from Valkenburg aan de Geul: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Maastricht - One of the most beautiful cities in Netherlands, located just 15-km away from Valkenburg aan de Geul.
  • Aachen - Located just 18 kilometers from Valkenburg aan de Geul is Aachen, a historic German city with many sites worth visiting.

Conclusion

Valkenburg aan de Geul is a stunning town with much to offer for travellers. The rich cultural heritage and the scenic nature of the town make it a must-visit location in Netherlands. The activities, sights and sounds that the town offers will leave you with lasting memories and a desire to return.
