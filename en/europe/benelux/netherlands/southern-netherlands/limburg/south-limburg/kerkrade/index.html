Introduction to Kerkrade

Kerkrade is a small town in the South Limburg region of the Netherlands. It is a charming town with plenty of things to see and do. It is famous for its museums, hiking/biking routes, and natural wonders. Kerkrade is rich in history and culture and has a vibrant food and drink scene that will excite your taste buds.

List of Places to Visit in Kerkrade

Here are the top places to visit in Kerkrade:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Discovery Museum Continium - A science museum that showcases the past, present, and future of technology and science. It is perfect for young and old alike and promises to be a fun and educational experience.

  • Abdij Rolduc- The biggest abbey complex in the Netherlands, Rolduc has breathtaking architecture and a fascinating history. This place is perfect for history buffs or those who want to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

  • GaiaZOO - A unique zoo that has over 100 species of animals from all over the world. From tigers to giraffes, you will find all kinds of exotic animals at this zoo.

Natural Attractions of Kerkrade: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Parkstad Limburg - A beautiful parkland covering 100 square kilometers. It is perfect for hiking and biking and has several scenic routes to choose from.

  • Lauradorp neoliths - A prehistoric monument that dates back to 3000 BC. The Lauradorp neoliths are a must-see attraction for anyone fascinated by history.

  • Gaiazoo Backstage Tour - Take a unique tour of one of the best zoos in Europe. The backstage tour will give you a glimpse of what happens behind the scenes in a zoo.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kerkrade

Kerkrade is a great place for hiking and biking. It has several scenic routes that you can explore. Some of the best routes to try are:

  • Brunssummerheide - A beautiful heathland that’s perfect for hiking. The route has several scenic spots that offer stunning views of the beautiful Dutch countryside.

  • Route langs Oud Limburgs Kerken - A biking route that takes you to some of the oldest churches in the Netherlands. The journey is scenic and peaceful and a great way to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

  • Kerkrade-Rolduc - A walking route that takes you through the beautiful countryside of South Limburg. The route is easy to traverse and perfect for families with young children.

Best Things to Do in Kerkrade: Activities and Tours

There are several things to do in Kerkrade that will keep you entertained and engaged. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Brewery Schaloen - Take a brewery tour and taste some of the best beers in the region.

  • Escape Room Limburg - A fun and challenging escape room that will test your skills and wits. It is perfect for groups and families.

  • Trampoline Park Jump XL - A fun-filled trampoline park that’s perfect for children and adults alike. It has several trampolines and obstacle courses that will keep you entertained for hours.

Food and Drink Scene of Kerkrade

Kerkrade has a diverse food and drink scene that will tantalize your taste buds. Here are some of the must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Limburgse Vlaai - A famous pie made with fruits and pastry.

  • Zuurvlees - A delicious beef stew cooked with vinegar and onions.

  • Gehaktballen - A meatball dish that’s a staple in the Dutch cuisine.

Local Beverages

  • Dutch beer - The Netherlands has a rich beer culture, and Kerkrade is no exception. Some popular local breweries include Brand, Gulpener, and Hertog Jan.

  • Jenever - A Dutch gin that’s perfect for sipping or cocktails.

Local Culture and Customs of Kerkrade

Kerkrade is a small town that has a rich history and culture. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Languages spoken - Dutch is the official language, although many people also speak English, German, and Limburgish.

  • Timings - The shops and restaurants in Kerkrade close early, so make sure you plan accordingly.

  • Tipping - Tipping is not mandatory but is appreciated.

Best Time to Visit Kerkrade

The best time to visit Kerkrade is from April to October when the weather is warm and sunny. However, the town has its charm during the winter months when it’s covered in snow.
