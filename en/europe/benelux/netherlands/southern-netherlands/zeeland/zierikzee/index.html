Introduction to Zierikzee

Zierikzee is a small town located in Zeeland, Southern Netherlands. With its rich cultural heritage, beautiful historic architecture, and stunning natural surroundings, it is a hidden gem that is perfect for a relaxed vacation. Founded in the 10th century, Zierikzee is steeped in history and offers a wide variety of activities for visitors to enjoy.

List of Places to Visit in Zierikzee

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Stadhuis Museum: Housed in a 16th-century town hall, the Stadhuis Museum offers a glimpse into the history of Zierikzee and the surrounding region through its galleries of fine art, historic artifacts, and archaeological finds.
  • Maritime Museum: A must-visit for history buffs and maritime enthusiasts, the Maritime Museum showcases the proud seafaring heritage of Zierikzee. Exhibits include historic models of ships, navigation instruments, and sea charts.
  • Nieuwe Kerk: The Nieuwe Kerk is a stunning Gothic church dating back to the 15th century. With its impressive tower and ornate carvings, this historic landmark is a must-visit for architecture aficionados.

Natural Attractions of Zierikzee: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Oosterscheldekering: The Oosterscheldekering is a massive series of dams and barriers that protect the region from the North Sea. Visitors can enjoy stunning views of the coastline and observe the abundant marine life that thrives in the surrounding waters.
  • Deltapark Neeltje Jans: Located just a few miles from Zierikzee, Deltapark Neeltje Jans is a popular tourist destination that offers a variety of outdoor activities. Visitors can enjoy water sports, nature walks, and learn about the challenges and opportunities presented by the North Sea.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Zierikzee

  • Stadswandeling Zierikzee: The Stadswandeling Zierikzee is a self-guided walking tour that takes visitors through the historic streets of Zierikzee. Along the way, visitors can admire the beautiful architecture, visit notable landmarks, and learn about the town’s rich history.
  • Fietsroute Zierikzee: The Fietsroute Zierikzee is a scenic cycling route that takes visitors through the beautiful countryside surrounding the town. Along the way, visitors can enjoy picturesque views of farmland, waterways, and historic windmills.

Best Things to Do in Zierikzee: Activities and Tours

  • Boat Tour: A boat tour of the surrounding waters is a great way to experience the beauty of Zierikzee’s natural surroundings. Visitors can observe the diverse marine life, stop at secluded beaches, and learn about the history of the local fishing industry.
  • Cultural Events: Throughout the year, Zierikzee hosts a variety of cultural events that celebrate the town’s rich history and heritage. From music festivals to artisan markets, these events showcase the best of Zierikzee.

Food and Drink Scene of Zierikzee

Must-Try Dishes

  • Oosterschelde Lobster: Zierikzee is famous for its succulent Oosterschelde lobsters, which are a must-try for seafood lovers.
  • Zeeuwse bolus: A traditional Dutch pastry, the Zeeuwse bolus is a sweet, sticky delicacy that is popular throughout Zeeland.

Local Beverages

  • Zeeuwse Babbelaars: The Zeeuwse Babbelaars are a type of hard candy that are flavored with butter and brown sugar. They are a local specialty that make for a great souvenir or gift.
  • Zeeuwse Bolus Liquor: Made from distilled Zeeuwse Bolus, this liquor is a unique local specialty that is sure to delight.

Where to Stay in Zierikzee: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Campsite De Zuidhoek: With its peaceful location on the edge of a nature reserve, this campsite is a great option for visitors who want to enjoy the great outdoors.
  • Grand Hotel: This elegant hotel is located in the heart of Zierikzee’s historic town center and offers luxurious accommodations and top-notch amenities.

What to Buy in Zierikzee: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Zeeland Cheese: Made from the milk of Zeeland cows, this cheese is a delicious and authentic local product that is perfect for cheese lovers.
  • Zeeuwse Mosselen: Zeeuwse Mosselen or Zeeland mussels are a must-try dish in Zierikzee and make for a great souvenir to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Zierikzee

Zierikzee is a friendly and welcoming town with a strong sense of community. Visitors are encouraged to respect the local customs and traditions, including taking care to properly dispose of litter and being respectful of historic landmarks and sites.
