Noord-Beveland, a small island in the southern province of Zeeland, Netherlands, is a hidden gem that is often overlooked by tourists. But with its stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and charming towns, Noord-Beveland is a must-visit destination for travelers looking for an off-the-beaten-track experience. In this comprehensive guide to Noord-Beveland, we’ll explore the highlights of this beautiful island, including places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more!

Introduction to Noord-Beveland

Noord-Beveland is a small island in the Zeeland region of the Netherlands. It’s known for its beautiful beaches, lush greenery, and traditional Dutch architecture. Despite being a small island, Noord-Beveland has a rich history and culture, with numerous museums, historical landmarks, and cultural events. The island is also famous for its delicious local cuisine and traditional crafts. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing vacation surrounded by nature or a cultural immersion in Dutch history, Noord-Beveland has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Noord-Beveland

Noord-Beveland is full of interesting places to explore, from picturesque towns to beautiful nature reserves. Here are some of the top places to visit on the island:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Deltapark Neeltje Jans: This modern attraction is an interactive museum dedicated to the Dutch Delta Works, a system of dams and barriers built to protect the Netherlands from floods. The park offers educational and fun activities for all ages, including a virtual reality experience, a seal show, and a water park.

  • Stoomtrein Goes-Borsele: This heritage railway offers a unique way to experience the beauty of Zeeland’s countryside. The steam train runs on a scenic route through orchards, meadows, and small villages, passing through several historical landmarks along the way.

  • Museum Veere: Veere is a charming town that was an important trading port during the 16th century. Today, it’s a popular tourist destination thanks to its well-preserved historical center and interesting museums. The Museum Veere showcases the town’s rich history, including its role in the Dutch Golden Age and its connection to the sea.

Natural Attractions of Noord-Beveland: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • National Park Oosterschelde: This marine park, the largest in the Netherlands, is located just off the coast of Noord-Beveland. The park is home to a diverse range of marine life, including seals, porpoises, and hundreds of species of birds. Visitors can explore the park’s trails, beaches, and mudflats, or take a boat trip to discover the hidden coves and islands.

  • Loods Tien Beach: This tranquil beach is the perfect place to relax and soak up the sun. The beach is surrounded by lush dunes and offers stunning views of the sea. There are plenty of activities for families, including a playground, a volleyball court, and a beachside café.

  • Fruitteeltmuseum: This museum celebrates the history of fruit growing in Zeeland, with interactive exhibits, hands-on activities, and a collection of antique tools and equipment. Visitors can learn about the different types of fruit grown on Noord-Beveland, from apples and pears to cherries and strawberries.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Noord-Beveland

Noord-Beveland is an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts, with miles of hiking and biking trails that wind through the island’s picturesque countryside. The most popular routes include:

  • Oosterschelde Route: This 38-kilometer biking trail follows the coastline of the Oosterschelde National Park, offering stunning views of the sea and the surrounding islands. The route passes through several charming towns, including Wissenkerke and Kats.

  • Noord-Beveland Route: This 42-kilometer biking trail circles the island and passes through several nature reserves, scenic landscapes, and picturesque villages. Highlights include the Veerse Meer lake, the beautiful dunes of Slikken van de Heen, and the historic town of Kamperland.

Best Things to Do in Noord-Beveland: Activities and Tours

Noord-Beveland offers plenty of activities and tours for visitors, from water sports to cultural events. Here are some of the best things to do on the island:

  • Water sports: Visitors can try their hand at a variety of water sports, including windsurfing, kayaking, and sailing. The clear waters of the Oosterschelde National Park are ideal for swimming, scuba diving, and snorkeling.

  • Local markets: Noord-Beveland is known for its vibrant local markets, where visitors can sample traditional food, buy handmade crafts, and meet friendly locals. The farmers’ market in Kamperland is a must-visit for foodies, offering a wide range of fresh local produce.

  • Cultural events: Noord-Beveland hosts several cultural events throughout the year, including music festivals, art exhibitions, and dance performances. The popular Ringrijden tournament, where horse riders compete to spear a small ring with a long pole, is a unique cultural experience not to be missed.

Week-Long Itinerary for Noord-Beveland

Planning a week-long itinerary for Noord-Beveland is easy, as there are plenty of diverse activities to keep visitors busy. Here’s a sample itinerary for a week-long trip to Noord-Beveland:

Food and Drink Scene of Noord-Beveland

Noord-Beveland’s cuisine is a blend of traditional Dutch dishes and local specialties, including seafood, berries, and dairy products. Visitors can sample the island’s gastronomic delights at numerous restaurants, cafes, and markets. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Mosselen met Friet: This dish of mussels and French fries is a classic Dutch favorite and can be found at many seafood restaurants on Noord-Beveland.

  • Zeeuws Spek: This smoked bacon from Zeeland is a local specialty and is often served as an appetizer.

  • Stoofperen: These stewed pears are a traditional Dutch dessert and are often served with ice cream or whipped cream.

Local Beverages

  • Zeeuwse Babbelaars: These caramel candies are a popular local treat and are often paired with coffee or tea.

  • Witte Wieven: This local beer is brewed on Noord-Beveland and is a refreshing drink after a day of exploring.

  • Zeeuwse Schijf: This local liquor is made from berries and has a sweet and sour taste.

Where to Stay in Noord-Beveland: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Noord-Beveland offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets, from luxurious hotels to cozy guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay on the island:

  • Hotel de Kamperduinen: This upscale hotel offers modern rooms with sea views, wellness facilities, and a restaurant serving local cuisine.

  • B&B Binnen de Veste: This charming bed and breakfast is located in the historic center of Veere and offers comfortable rooms with traditional Dutch decor.

  • Camping De Witte Raaf: This campsite is located just a few minutes’ walk from Loods Tien Beach and offers spacious pitches and modern facilities.

What to Buy in Noord-Beveland: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Noord-Beveland is famous for its traditional crafts, local delicacies, and handmade souvenirs. Here are some of the best products to bring home as a memento of your trip:

  • Zeeuwse T-shirts: These colorful T-shirts with a Zeeuwse flag or other typical Zeeuwse symbols are a great souvenir.

  • Klompen: Traditional wooden clogs are still made on Noord-Beveland and are a unique and practical souvenir.

  • Zeeuwse Buttons: These small pins with the Zeeuwse flag or other typical Zeeuwse symbols are a fun and inexpensive souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Noord-Beveland

Noord-Beveland has a rich and unique culture that reflects its history and geography. The island’s traditional crafts, local festivals, and dialects are all part of its cultural heritage. Visitors should respect local customs and etiquette when visiting Noord-Beveland, including:

  • Dress: Noord-Beveland is a laid-back and casual destination, so comfortable clothing and shoes are recommended.

  • Language: Most locals speak Dutch and English, and many also speak the local dialect. Learning a few words of Dutch or the local dialect can be a fun way to connect with locals.

  • Tipping: Tipping in restaurants and cafes is not expected in the Netherlands, but rounding up the bill or leaving a small tip is appreciated.

Best Time to Visit Noord-Beveland

Noord-Beveland can be visited year-round, but the best time to visit depends on what activities and experiences you’re looking for. The peak tourist season is during the summer months (June to August), when the weather is warm and sunny, and outdoor activities are in full swing. However, the shoulder seasons of spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November) can offer milder weather, fewer crowds, and lower prices. Winter (December to February) can be cold and rainy, but also offers unique winter activities and festive events.

Do’s and Don’ts of Noord-Beveland

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Noord-Beveland:

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Hire a bike or go for a hike to explore the island’s beautiful scenery.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Visit the local markets to interact with friendly locals.
  • Learn some basic Dutch or the local dialect to connect with locals.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural environment.
  • Ignore the rules and regulations of the national parks and reserves.
  • Take photos of people without their permission.
  • Smoke in non-smoking areas.
  • Drink and drive.

Safety and Health

Noord-Beveland is a safe and healthy destination for travelers. However, visitors should be aware of the following:

Safety in Noord-Beveland

  • Observe traffic rules when cycling or walking.
  • Be aware of the risks of swimming in open water.
  • Watch out for strong currents and tides.
  • Keep valuables safe and secure in crowded areas.

Health in Noord-Beveland

  • Noord-Beveland has a good healthcare system, with several hospitals and clinics on the island.
  • Visitors should have comprehensive medical insurance that covers emergency treatment.
  • It’s recommended to get vaccinated against tick-borne diseases if visiting during the summer months.

Getting to Noord-Beveland: Transportation Options and Logistics

Noord-Beveland is easily accessible by various modes of transportation, including:

Airports Near Noord-Beveland

  • Amsterdam Airport Schiphol (AMS), located 140 km away from Noord-Beveland, is the nearest international airport.
  • Rotterdam The Hague Airport (RTM), located 100 km away from Noord-Beveland, offers domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Noord-Beveland

  • Bus stations in Goes and Middelburg connect Noord-Beveland to other parts of Zeeland and the Netherlands.

Other Options

  • Train services from major cities connect to Goes, which has a railway station.
  • Ferries and water taxis from neighboring islands and the mainland are also available.

How to Get Around in Noord-Beveland

Noord-Beveland is a small island that is easily explored on foot, by bike, or by car. There are several bike rental shops and car rental agencies on the island, and public transportation is available but limited. Taxis can also be hired from the main towns.

Where to Go Next from Noord-Beveland: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Noord-Beveland is a great base for exploring other parts of Zeeland and the Netherlands. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Delta Works: The impressive Delta Works, a system of dams and barriers designed to protect the Netherlands from floods, is just a short distance from Noord-Beveland.

  • Middelburg: The charming city of Middelburg, the capital of Zeeland, is home to numerous historical landmarks, museums, and restaurants.

  • Amsterdam: The famous Dutch capital is just a few hours’ drive or train ride from Noord-Beveland and offers endless attractions, including museums, art galleries, and nightlife.

Conclusion

Noord-Beveland may be a small island, but it’s a big destination for travelers seeking an authentic Dutch experience. With its stunning natural landscapes, rich cultural heritage, vibrant cuisine, and friendly locals, Noord-Beveland has something to offer everyone.
