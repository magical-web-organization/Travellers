Bolsward is a hidden gem located in the heart of Friesland, Northern Netherlands. It is a quaint yet picturesque town surrounded by canals and boasts of beautiful architecture, making it a popular destination for locals and tourists alike. If you’re planning to visit Bolsward, this comprehensive tour guide will take you on a journey through the town’s culturally important attractions, natural wonders, and the best things to do and see.

Introduction to Bolsward

Bolsward is situated in the Benelux area in Europe, and it has a rich history that dates back to the 13th century. In the past, the town was an important trade center for salt, and it has since then developed into a cultural and historical hub.

List of Places to Visit in Bolsward

Bolsward is home to several must-visit places that offer visitors an insight into the town’s history and culture. Here’s a list of some of the most popular attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Martinikerk Bolsward

The Martinikerk Bolsward is the town’s most iconic landmark and one of the oldest and most beautiful buildings in Friesland. It is a massive church that features impressive stained glass windows and a stunning organ.

Het Gysbert Japicxhûs

The Het Gysbert Japicxhûs pays homage to Gysbert Japicks, a prominent Frisian poet. It’s a museum that showcases books, documents, and other artifacts that highlight the poet’s life and works.

Museum Stadhuis Bolsward

The Museum Stadhuis Bolsward is located in a former town hall and features a range of exhibits that detail the town’s history, culture, and heritage. You can expect to find paintings, sculptures, and old documents that offer a glimpse into Bolsward’s past.

Natural Attractions of Bolsward: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Bolsward Canals

Bolsward is home to a network of canals that offer visitors a chance to take in the town’s beauty by boat. You can rent a boat and enjoy a leisurely ride for the entire day if you’d like.

Stadspark Bolsward

The Stadspark Bolsward is a beautiful park in the middle of the town that is perfect for a picnic or a pleasant stroll. It features a tranquil pond, and during the summer, you can enjoy outdoor concerts and exhibitions.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bolsward

Bolsward has an extensive network of biking trails that guarantees to take you on an exceptional cycling experience. One of the most picturesque trails is the Elfstedentocht, which is a 200-kilometer-long route that takes you through eleven Frisian towns, including Bolsward.

Best Things to Do in Bolsward: Activities and Tours

Walking Tour of Bolsward

One of the best things to do while in Bolsward is to go on a walking tour through the town. You’ll get an up-close view of the town’s historic buildings and learn about its culture and heritage.

Visit the Bolsward Distillery

The town’s distillery is famous for producing Beerenburg, a traditional Dutch liqueur that’s been around since the 17th century. Visit the distillery and take a tour to learn about the history and production process of Beerenburg.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bolsward

A week in Bolsward offers genuinely endless opportunities to explore the town, its history, and an excellent chance to experience the Dutch culture. You can check our website for a week-long itinerary that covers the must-do activities while you’re in Bolsward.

Food and Drink Scene of Bolsward

Must-Try Dishes

Bolsward is renowned for its hearty, traditional Dutch cuisine. A popular dish that you have to try while in the town is Stampot, a classic Dutch dish made of mashed potatoes and a vegetable, usually kale.

Local Beverages

In addition to Beerenburg liqueur, Bolsward is home to several breweries. Try out some of the beers on offer, or enjoy a glass of wine from one of the town’s wine merchants.

Where to Stay in Bolsward: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bolsward offers several accommodation options, ranging from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels. Some popular options include Hotel Restaurant De Wijnberg and Pension de Stadsherberg.

What to Buy in Bolsward: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When you’re in Bolsward, make sure to buy some souvenirs as mementos of your trip. Some popular souvenir options include the Bolsward beer glass or shot glass or the Bolsward flag.

Local Culture and Customs of Bolsward

Bolsward and Friesland are home to the Frisian people, who have their own distinct language and culture. You’ll notice this in the architecture and the cultural events that take place during the year. The town also has a healthy respect for the environment. You’ll find many local initiatives that aim to protect and improve Bolsward’s flora and fauna.

Best Time to Visit Bolsward

Bolsward is beautiful year-round, but the best time to visit is during the warmer months between May to September. The weather is pleasant, and there are several cultural events and festivals during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bolsward

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Do explore the town’s many outdoor destinations.
  • Do try to learn a few words in Frisian.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in the town or its natural areas.
  • Don’t park your car on the streets or in prohibited areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bolsward

Bolsward is a safe town to visit and explore, crime rates are incredibly low, and the locals are friendly and helpful.

Health in Bolsward

The town has a modern healthcare system that guarantees quality medical care in case of emergencies.

Getting to Bolsward: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bolsward

The nearest airports to Bolsward are Eindhoven Airport and Amsterdam Airport Schiphol. From both airports, you can travel to Bolsward by train or car.

Bus Stations Near Bolsward

The Bolsward bus station is within walking distance of the town center, and it serves multiple destinations around the Netherlands.

Other Options

You can also travel to Bolsward by train or car.

How to Get Around in Bolsward

Bolsward is a town best explored on foot or by bicycle. However, if you prefer not to walk or cycle, you can take a bus or taxi.

Where to Go Next from Bolsward: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

The Frisian cities of Sneek and Leeuwarden are just a short drive or train ride away from Bolsward. Both cities offer an array of activities, attractions, and things to do.

Conclusion

Bolsward is a fascinating town that offers visitors a perfect blend of culture, history, and stunning natural beauty. Whether you’re exploring the town’s museums and historical places, going on a biking tour, or enjoying local cuisine and beverages, Bolsward has something to offer every traveler. We hope this comprehensive tour guide has given you an insight into everything you need to see and do while in Bolsward.
