Introduction to Aa en Hunze

Aa en Hunze is a beautiful municipality located in the province of Drenthe, in Northern Netherlands. The municipality was formed by the merger of the former municipalities of Aa and Hunze in 1998. Aa en Hunze is known for its natural beauty, picturesque villages, and beautiful landscapes. It is a popular destination for tourists looking for tranquility and outdoor adventures.

List of Places to Visit in Aa en Hunze

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you are interested in the history and culture of Aa en Hunze, there are several museums and historical places that you can visit.

Drents Museum

The Drents Museum is a must-visit for anyone interested in the history of Drenthe. The museum has a vast collection of archaeological finds, historic artifacts, and modern art.

Giethoorn

Giethoorn is a picturesque village located in the municipality of Aa en Hunze. It is known for its beautiful waterways, rustic bridges, and thatched-roof houses.

Natural Attractions of Aa en Hunze: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Aa en Hunze is known for its natural beauty and offers several outdoor activities and scenic views.

Drentsche Aa National Landscape

The Drentsche Aa National Landscape is a beautiful area of natural beauty. It is known for its diverse landscapes, including forests, heaths, and meadows. You can explore the national landscape on foot, bike, or horseback.

Hunebed D27

Hunebed D27 is the largest and most impressive hunebed of Drenthe. It is a megalithic tomb that is approximately 5,000 years old. It is located in the village of Borger, which is a short drive from Aa en Hunze.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Aa en Hunze

Aa en Hunze is an excellent destination for hiking and cycling enthusiasts. There are several hiking and biking routes that you can explore.

Het Drenthepad

Het Drenthepad is a long-distance hiking trail that covers the entire province of Drenthe. It is a beautiful trail that takes you through forests, heaths, and meadows.

Drenthe Fietsvierdaagse

Drenthe Fietsvierdaagse is a four-day cycling event that takes place every year in July. It is a beautiful event that takes you through the most beautiful places in Drenthe, including Aa en Hunze.

Best Things to Do in Aa en Hunze: Activities and Tours

There are several exciting things to do and tours that you can take in Aa en Hunze.

Horse Riding

Aa en Hunze is an excellent destination for horse riding enthusiasts. There are several companies that offer guided horseback rides through the beautiful landscapes of the municipality.

Hot Air Balloon Rides

Hot air balloon rides are a popular activity in Aa en Hunze. You can take a hot air balloon ride and enjoy the stunning views of the national park from above.

Food and Drink Scene of Aa en Hunze

Must-Try Dishes

Aa en Hunze is known for its delicious local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes:

Stoofpeertjes

Stoofpeertjes is a traditional Dutch dish that is usually served as a side dish. It is made from slow-cooked pears, sugar, red wine, and cinnamon.

Peperkoek

Peperkoek is a spiced gingerbread cake that is popular in the Netherlands. It is usually served as a snack or a dessert.

Local Beverages

Aa en Hunze is known for its local beer and wine. Here are some local beverages that you should try:

Gueuze Beer

Gueuze beer is a type of lambic beer that is brewed in the region around Brussels. It is a tart and refreshing beer that is perfect for hot summer days.

Dutch Gin

Dutch gin, also known as jenever, is a traditional Dutch spirit that is made from malted barley, rye, and juniper berries. It has a smooth, herbaceous flavor and is perfect for sipping.

Hotels

What to Buy in Aa en Hunze: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you are looking for souvenirs and local products to bring home, there are several options in Aa en Hunze.

Drentse Aa Honey

Drentse Aa Honey is a delicious local honey that is made from the nectar of flowers in the Drentse Aa National Landscape.

Drentse Turf

Drentse Turf is a traditional Dutch sweet made from puff pastry, marzipan, and almond paste. It is named after the marshy landscapes of the province of Drenthe.

Local Culture and Customs of Aa en Hunze

Aa en Hunze has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its charming villages and traditional festivals.

Festivals

Some of the most popular festivals in Aa en Hunze include:

Bloemencorso Eelde

Bloemencorso Eelde is a beautiful flower parade that takes place in the village of Eelde every year in August. It features beautiful floats decorated with thousands of flowers.

TT Festival Assen

TT Festival Assen is a popular event that takes place every year in June. It is a celebration of motorcycle culture and features live music, street performances, and a motorcycle race.
