Introduction to Westerkwartier

Westerkwartier is a hidden gem in the Northern part of the Netherlands. This green and lush area boasts beautiful nature reserves, scenic views, and charming villages. The region is rich in history and culture, with plenty of museums and cultural attractions. If you’re a fan of outdoor activities, Westerkwartier also offers a wide range of hiking and biking routes.

In this travel guide, we’ll explore everything you need to know about visiting Westerkwartier. From where to stay to what to do, we’ve got you covered.

  • Lauwersmeer National Park
  • Abel Tasman Museum
  • Scheepstrakabinet Museum
  • De Theefabriek
  • Museum Nienoord
  • Zulthe Nature Reserve

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Westerkwartier’s history is fascinating, and there are plenty of museums and historical places to explore.

The Groninger Museum is a must-visit for art lovers. The museum has an extensive collection of modern and contemporary art, design, and fashion.

The Abel Tasman Museum is a homage to the famous explorer who discovered Tasmania. The museum has a collection of artifacts and information about Tasman’s life and voyages.

The Scheepstrakabinet Museum is dedicated to the children’s book author, Hindericus Scheepstra, and illustrator, Jan Ligthart. The museum showcases their work and offers an interactive experience for visitors.

Natural Attractions of Westerkwartier: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Westerkwartier is a paradise for nature lovers. With its scenic views, hiking and biking trails, there’s plenty to explore.

Lauwersmeer National Park is a stunning nature reserve that offers breathtaking views of the lake, forests, and wetlands. The park is perfect for hiking, biking, and bird-watching.

Zulthe Nature Reserve is another beautiful nature reserve in the area. The reserve has plenty of hiking trails that take you through forests and meadows.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Westerkwartier

Westerkwartier has a diverse range of hiking and biking routes that cater to every level of experience. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • The Nienoord Estate route
  • The Boerakker route
  • The De Onlanden route
  • The Pieterpad hiking trail

Best Things to Do in Westerkwartier: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, Westerkwartier offers plenty of activities and tours for visitors to enjoy. Some of the best things to do in Westerkwartier include:

  • Take a boat trip on the Leekstermeer
  • Visit a tea plantation at De Theefabriek
  • Go horseback riding at Nienoord Estate
  • Explore the area by hot air balloon
