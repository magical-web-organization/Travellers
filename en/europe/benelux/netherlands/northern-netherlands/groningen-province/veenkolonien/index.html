Introduction to Veenkoloniën

Located in the province of Groningen in the Netherlands, Veenkoloniën is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered by adventure seekers and travelers. Once known for its peat mining industry, this area now boasts a rich cultural history, picturesque landscapes, and outdoor adventures.

Whether you’re interested in hiking, biking, exploring museums and historical places, indulging in local cuisine or just relaxing in nature, Veenkoloniën has something special to offer visitors.

This comprehensive travel guide will provide you with all the essential information you need to plan your trip to Veenkoloniën.

List of Places to Visit in Veenkoloniën

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Veenkoloniaal Museum - Showcases the rich history of Veenkoloniën and its peat mining industry. Explore the museum’s exhibits and learn about the area’s cultural heritage.
  • De Oude Egyptenaar - A restored peat miner’s house that provides a glimpse into the everyday life of people who lived in the area during the 19th century.
  • Bourtange Fortress - A historical fortification built in the 16th century. Visit the museum inside the fortress and learn about its strategic importance and history.

Natural Attractions of Veenkoloniën: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Westerswold Forest - Known for its hiking and biking trails, Westerswold Forest is a stunning natural park that offers breathtaking views of the landscape.
  • Stadskanaal Canal - Take a boat ride along the canal and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding countryside.
  • Westerwolde Stream Valley - A beautiful valley with scenic hiking trails, quaint villages, and picturesque landscapes.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Veenkoloniën

Veenkoloniën is known for its extensive network of biking and hiking trails that take visitors through picturesque landscapes, quaint villages, and scenic countryside.

Some popular trails include:

  • Nordic Walking Route - This trail takes you through the Westerwolde nature reserve and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Biking Route - Follow the biking trail through the Stadskanaal Canal and enjoy scenic views of the waterways and countryside.

Best Things to Do in Veenkoloniën: Activities and Tours

  • Canal Boat Ride - Take a boat ride along the Stadskanaal Canal and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding countryside.
  • Visit a Local Brewery - Indulge in Veenkoloniën’s local beer scene by visiting a local brewery and sampling some of the delicious beers.
  • Museum Tour - Explore Veenkoloniaal Museum and learn about the area’s rich history and cultural heritage.

Week-Long Itinerary for Veenkoloniën

Day 1: Explore the city of Stadskanaal and take a boat tour of the Stadskanaal Canal

Day 2: Visit the Veenkoloniaal Museum and De Oude Egyptenaar, and explore the city of Veendam

Day 3: Hike through Westerswold Forest

Day 4: Take a biking tour of Veenkoloniën and visit local breweries

Day 5: Visit Bourtange Fortress and explore the nearby villages

Day 6: Enjoy the scenic views of the Westerwolde Stream Valley and go on a nature walk.

Day 7: Take a day trip to the nearby city of Groningen.

Food and Drink Scene of Veenkoloniën

Must-Try Dishes

  • Groninger Mustard Soup - A traditional soup made with Groninger mustard, onions, and vegetables.
  • Poffert - A traditional dish that consists of a dense, sweet bread pudding.
  • Stamppot - A classic Dutch dish made with mashed potatoes, vegetables, and sausage.

Local Beverages

  • Groninger Beer - Enjoy a cold pint of local beer, brewed using traditional methods and techniques.
  • Dutch Gin - Also known as Jenever, this traditional Dutch spirit is a popular beverage in Veenkoloniën.

Where to Stay in Veenkoloniën: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are a variety of different accommodations available in Veenkoloniën to suit all budgets and preferences.

Some popular options include:

  • Van der Valk Hotel - A luxury hotel located in the heart of Veenkoloniën
  • Landgoed Westerlee - A beautiful guesthouse located in the Westerwolde nature reserve
  • Hostel Van Gogh - A budget-friendly hostel located in the city of Stadskanaal

What to Buy in Veenkoloniën: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Groninger Mustard - A famous local product that is known for its unique flavor.
  • Traditional Dutch Clogs - A classic Dutch souvenir that is still worn in parts of Veenkoloniën.
  • Stroopwafels - A popular Dutch snack that consists of two thin waffles with a caramel filling.

Local Culture and Customs of Veenkoloniën

In Veenkoloniën, be sure to respect the locals and their customs. Some tips to keep in mind include:

  • Always greet locals with a friendly “hallo” or “goedendag.”
  • Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Do not litter or damage the environment.

Best Time to Visit Veenkoloniën

The best time to visit Veenkoloniën is during the summer months (June to August) when the weather is warm and sunny. This is also when many festivals and events take place in the area.

Do’s and Don’ts of Veenkoloniën

  • Try the local cuisine and beer.
  • Respect the local culture and customs.

Safety and Health

Veenkoloniën is a safe place to visit, with low levels of crime. However, it is important to take basic precautions including:

  • Keep your valuables safe and secure.
  • Be aware of your surroundings when exploring the area.

Veenkoloniën has excellent healthcare facilities, with quality medical care available throughout the area.
