Introduction to Appingedam

Appingedam, a small town situated in the province of Groningen in Northern Netherlands, is a perfect destination for travelers looking for a culturally rich and naturally beautiful escape. With its unique architecture, rich history, and scenic landscapes, Appingedam has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Appingedam

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum Stad Appingedam: This museum offers a glimpse into the history and culture of the town. It showcases historical artifacts, and interactive exhibits that give insights into the town’s rich history.

  • Nicolai Church: This beautiful and historic church was constructed in the 13th century and is now a must-visit attraction. It contains beautiful stained-glass windows and features interactive exhibits and audio guides.

  • De Oude Apotheek: One of the oldest buildings in the town, De Oude Apotheek is now a cultural center that offers a variety of workshops, film screenings, and exhibitions.

Natural Attractions of Appingedam: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Reitdiep River: The Reitdiep River is a calm and serene spot that offers a peaceful escape with scenic views of the town’s uniquely shaped bridges.

  • Wadden Sea: The Wadden Sea, on the northern coast of the Netherlands, is a UNESCO World Heritage site and an ecological treasure trove. It is home to a variety of birds, sea creatures, and stunning sand dunes.

  • Eems River Estuary: The Eems River Estuary, located just north of the town, provides a great spot for bird watching and relaxation with its tranquil mudflats and tidal flats.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Appingedam

The town of Appingedam is dotted with scenic hiking and biking routes that offer amazing views of the surrounding nature and the unique architecture.

  • Molenwandeling route (Mill Walk): A scenic route amidst the mills of the Eemspolder and Appingedam. A route filled with ancient mills, picturesque streets and cozy squares in both the village and the countryside.

  • Lopster Toren route (Lopster Tower route): A route through the beautiful and varied landscape of the Eemsdelta region. Gardens, crops, dikes, and on the horizon, various towers and mills. The notorious medieval Lopster Tower cannot be missed.

Best Things to Do in Appingedam: Activities and Tours

  • Canal Cruise: A canal cruise is one of the best ways to enjoy the scenic beauty of Appingedam. Cruise through the beautiful canals and get a glimpse of the town’s unique architecture.

  • Buitenwesten Appingedam: Experience Appingedam like a local; cycle through the beautiful Groningen countryside with locals. Also known as “Bicycle Tour of Appingedam,” the tour takes you through scenic locations and historical buildings while educating you about the locals’ way of life.

Food and Drink Scene of Appingedam

Must-Try Dishes

  • Groninger Koek: A traditional spicy cake containing cinnamon, aniseed, and cloves. It is often served with a dollop of whipped cream or with a slice of Groninger cheese.

  • Poffertjes: Miniature pancakes served with a variety of toppings such as maple syrup, icing sugar, and whipped cream.

  • Groninger Mosterdsoep: A hearty and flavorful mustard soup made from Dutch mustard, flour, cream, and butter.

Local Beverages

  • Groninger Jenever: A type of Dutch gin that is distilled locally and is a popular drink in the region.

  • Groninger Bier: Brewed by local micro-breweries, Groninger Bier is a traditional beer that has been enjoyed in the region for centuries.

Where to Stay in Appingedam: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Appingedam has a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences:

  • Hotel Aan de Singel: Located in the heart of Appingedam, Hotel Aan de Singel offers comfortable accommodation with a cozy, homely atmosphere. It has all the modern amenities and is great for families and groups.

  • Hotel Marenland: A beautiful hotel set in a picturesque countryside overlooking the Wadden Sea. Hotel Marenland offers comfortable accommodation with excellent facilities and great service.

What to Buy in Appingedam: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Grunneger Koaibonbon: Traditional Dutch candies that are made from honey and licorice and have a unique flavor that has been enjoyed by the locals for generations.

  • Groninger Paardje: A small, white porcelain horse that is a symbol of Groningen and a great souvenir to take home.

  • Groninger Kruidkoek: A traditional Dutch spiced bread that has been baked in the region for centuries.

Local Culture and Customs of Appingedam

Appingedam has a rich culture and customs, which are heavily influenced by its history and geography. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and visitors should respect their culture and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Appingedam

The best time to visit Appingedam is from April to September, as the weather is pleasant and mild during this time. This is also the time when most of the town’s festivals and events take place.

Where to Go Next from Appingedam: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Nearby destinations worth exploring include:

  • Groninger Museum: An art museum located in Groningen that is known for its stunning collection of modern art and design.

  • Bourtange: A fortified village near the German border that showcases the town’s history and culture.

Conclusion

From beautiful architecture and rich history to stunning natural landscapes and unique cuisine, Appingedam has something for everyone. Whether visitors are looking to relax and unwind or explore new destinations and cultures, Appingedam is a perfect destination that should not be missed.
