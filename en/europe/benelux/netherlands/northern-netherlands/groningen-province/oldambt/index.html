Introduction to Oldambt

Oldambt is a municipality and region located in the province of Groningen, Northern Netherlands. Known for its stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage, the area boasts breathtaking landscapes, historic sites, and thriving communities. Whether looking for outdoor adventures, cultural exploration, or a mix of both, Oldambt is the perfect destination for travelers looking to experience the best Northern Netherlands has to offer.

Food and Drink Scene of Oldambt

The food and drink scene in Oldambt is a perfect reflection of the region’s rich culinary heritage. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Stamppot (mashed potatoes mixed with vegetables)
  • Poffertjes (mini pancakes)
  • Rijsttafel (rice dish with various side dishes)

Local Beverages

  • Beer from the local breweries
  • Jenever (Dutch gin)
  • Boerenjongens (raisins soaked in brandy)
