A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Midden-Groningen, Netherlands

Midden-Groningen is a beautiful municipality located in the province of Groningen, in Northern Netherlands. It is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered by travelers who want to experience the authentic Dutch way of life. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take a deep dive into everything you need to know about Midden-Groningen, from its cultural and natural attractions to its food and drink scene, local customs, and safety tips. Let’s get started!

Introduction to Midden-Groningen

Midden-Groningen is a municipality that was formed in 2018 after a merger of three municipalities: Hoogezand-Sappemeer, Slochteren, and Menterwolde. It is home to over 60,000 people and covers an area of 186.2 km². The municipality is located in the province of Groningen and is surrounded by picturesque villages and stunning natural landscapes. Midden-Groningen has a rich history, and there are plenty of cultural and historical attractions to explore.

List of Places to Visit in Midden-Groningen

Midden-Groningen is a great place to explore if you’re interested in Dutch history, culture, and nature. Here are some of the must-visit places in Midden-Groningen:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum of War and Reconstruction: This museum is located in the village of Slochteren and provides an insight into the lives of the people of Groningen during World War II and the reconstruction period that followed.
  • Fraeylemaborg: A beautiful castle located in Slochteren, Fraeylemaborg was built in the 17th century and has now been converted into a museum that showcases the art and culture of the region.
  • Museum Hoogezand-Sappemeer: This museum located in the town of Hoogezand-Sappemeer showcases the industrial and cultural heritage of the region.

Natural Attractions of Midden-Groningen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Schildmeer: This lake located in the village of Steendam is a great spot for swimming and water sports. The lake is surrounded by beautiful forests and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Midden-Groningen Bike Trail: This bike trail spans over 40 kilometers and takes you through some of the most beautiful villages and natural landscapes in Midden-Groningen.
  • Zuidlaardermeer: This lake is located on the border of Groningen and Drenthe and is one of the largest lakes in the Netherlands. It is a popular spot for fishing, swimming, and sailing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Midden-Groningen

Midden-Groningen is a paradise for hikers and bikers, with plenty of scenic routes to choose from. Here are some of the best routes in Midden-Groningen:

  • Slochterbos Route: This hiking route takes you through the beautiful forests of Slochteren and offers stunning views of the Fraeylemaborg castle.
  • Foxham Route: This cycling route takes you through the Groninger landscape and the village of Foxham. The route is approximately 25 kilometers long and takes about 2 hours to complete.
  • Stuifzand Route: This hiking route takes you through the beautiful Stuifzand nature reserve, which is known for its sand dunes and heathland. The route is approximately 5 kilometers long and takes about 1 hour to complete.

Best Things to Do in Midden-Groningen: Activities and Tours

Midden-Groningen offers a range of activities and tours that cater to different interests. Here are some of the best things to do in Midden-Groningen:

  • Boat Tours: Take a boat tour of the canals in Hoogezand-Sappemeer and enjoy the beautiful scenery and architecture of the town.
  • Watersports: Schildmeer and Zuidlaardermeer are great spots for watersports like swimming, sailing, and fishing.
  • Groninger Kroon: Visit the Groninger Kroon brewery in the village of Noordbroek and learn about the process of brewing beer.

Food and Drink Scene of Midden-Groningen

Midden-Groningen offers a range of traditional Dutch dishes that are a must-try for every foodie. Here are some of the best dishes and local beverages to sample in Midden-Groningen:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Groninger Koek: A traditional Dutch cake made with honey, ginger, and cinnamon.
  • Borrelgarnituur: An assortment of snacks that are typically served with drinks.
  • Poffertjes: Miniature Dutch pancakes that are served with powdered sugar and butter.

Local Beverages

  • Bokbier: A dark beer that is typically brewed in the winter months.
  • Jenever: A traditional Dutch gin that is made with juniper berries and other herbs.

Local Culture and Customs of Midden-Groningen

Midden-Groningen has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its arts, crafts, and festivals. Here are some of the local customs and traditions that you should be aware of:

  • Sinterklaas: A traditional Dutch holiday that is celebrated in December.
  • Groninger Rundvee Show: A livestock show that is held annually in Hoogezand-Sappemeer.
  • Groninger Weekmarkt: A weekly market that is held on Wednesdays in Hoogezand.
