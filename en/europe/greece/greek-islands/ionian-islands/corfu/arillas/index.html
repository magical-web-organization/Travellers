Introduction to Arillas

Arillas is a beautiful coastal village located on the northwestern coast of Corfu in the Ionian Islands. It is a popular destination among tourists who are looking for a peaceful and relaxing holiday. The village has a laid-back vibe and offers visitors a chance to experience the traditional Greek way of life. In this guide, we will take you through the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more, to help you plan a memorable trip to Arillas.

List of Places to Visit in Arillas

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Arillas has a rich cultural history, and there are several museums and historical places that visitors can explore to learn more about its past.

1. Arillas Folklore Museum

The Arillas Folklore Museum is a must-visit attraction in the village. It showcases the daily life and customs of the people who lived in the area in the past. The museum houses traditional costumes, agricultural tools, musical instruments, and other artifacts.

2. Angelokastro Castle

The Angelokastro Castle is a historical monument located on a hilltop overlooking the village. It was built in the 13th century and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and sea. Visitors can explore the castle’s ruins and learn about its history.

Natural Attractions of Arillas: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Arillas is blessed with natural beauty, and visitors can indulge in a variety of outdoor activities to enjoy it fully.

1. Arillas Beach

Arillas Beach is a beautiful sandy beach that offers clear blue waters and stunning sunsets. The beach is ideal for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing. Visitors can also enjoy water sports like windsurfing and paddleboarding.

2. Cape Drastis

Cape Drastis is a rocky peninsula located on the northwest coast of the island. It offers breathtaking views of the sea and the surrounding cliffs. Visitors can explore the area on foot and take in the stunning scenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Arillas

Arillas has some fantastic hiking and biking routes that offer visitors a chance to explore the area’s natural beauty.

1. Arillas Trail

The Arillas Trail is a popular hiking trail that takes visitors through scenic routes around the village. The trail passes through olive groves, lush green hills, and offers stunning views of the Mediterranean sea.

2. Corfu Trail

The Corfu Trail is a long-distance hiking trail that goes through several villages on the island. It is a challenging route and is only recommended for experienced hikers. The trail offers stunning views of the island’s landscape and flora and fauna.

Best Things to Do in Arillas: Activities and Tours

There are several fun activities and tours that visitors can enjoy in Arillas. Here are some of the best ones:

1. Boat Tours

Boat tours are a great way to explore the island’s coastline and discover its hidden beaches and coves. Visitors can book a boat tour and enjoy swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing on remote beaches.

2. Olive Oil Tasting

Olive oil is an important product of the region, and visitors can book a tour to learn about the process of making olive oil. They can also taste different varieties of olive oil and purchase some to take back home.

Food and Drink Scene of Arillas

Arillas has a vibrant food and drink scene, and visitors can indulge in traditional Greek cuisine and local specialties.

Must-Try Dishes

1. Sofrito

Sofrito is an iconic Corfiot dish made with beef or veal cooked in a sauce of white wine, garlic, and parsley. It is served with mashed potatoes or rice.

2. Pastitsada

Pastitsada is a hearty stew made with beef or veal and pasta. It is cooked in a tomato-based sauce with cinnamon, cloves, and other spices, giving it a unique flavor.

Local Beverages

1. Koumquat Liqueur

Koumquat Liqueur is a popular local specialty made with koumquat fruits, sugar, and alcohol. It has a sweet and tangy flavor and is a great way to end a meal.

2. Corfiot Beer

Corfu has its own beer, called Corfiot beer. It is brewed using traditional methods and offers a unique flavor. Visitors can try it at local bars and restaurants.

Where to Stay in Arillas: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several options for accommodation in Arillas, ranging from hotels to hostels and guesthouses.

1. Hotel Arillas

Hotel Arillas is located just a few steps away from Arillas Beach and offers comfortable rooms with balconies and sea views. The hotel has a restaurant, bar, and outdoor pool.

2. Rainbow Apartments

Rainbow Apartments is a great option for budget travelers. It is located close to the beach and offers self-catering apartments with kitchenettes.

What to Buy in Arillas: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors can purchase souvenirs and local products to bring back home as a memory of their trip. Here are some of the best things to buy in Arillas:

1. Olive Oil

Corfu is famous for its olive oil, and visitors can purchase high-quality olive oil from the local markets and olive oil producers.

2. Ceramic Art

Corfu has a long tradition of ceramic art, and visitors can purchase beautiful handmade ceramics from local shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Arillas

Arillas has a rich cultural heritage, and its residents take pride in their traditions and customs. Visitors should respect the local culture and customs and dress modestly while visiting churches and historical sites.

Best Time to Visit Arillas

The best time to visit Arillas is between May and September when the weather is warm and sunny. However, visitors can also enjoy the beauty of the village during the winter months when it is less crowded.

Do’s and Don’ts of Arillas

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Dress modestly while visiting churches and historical sites
  • Try the local cuisine and drinks
  • Buy souvenirs from local shops

Don’t:

  • Litter on the beach or in public areas
  • Be disrespectful towards the locals
  • Sunbathe topless on the beach

Safety and Health

Safety in Arillas

Arillas is a safe village for tourists. However, visitors should be cautious while swimming, as the currents can be strong in some areas.

Health in Arillas

Visitors should carry a first-aid kit with them and take precautions against insect bites and sunburn.

Getting to Arillas: Transportation Options and Logistics

Arillas is easily accessible by air, sea, and road.

Airports Near Arillas

The closest airport to Arillas is the Corfu International Airport, located 40 km away. Visitors can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach the village.

Bus Stations Near Arillas

Visitors can take a bus from Corfu Town to Arillas. The journey takes around 1 hour.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or a scooter to explore the island on their own.

How to Get Around in Arillas

Arillas is a small village, and visitors can explore it on foot. However, visitors can also rent a bike or a scooter to visit nearby attractions.

Where to Go Next from Arillas: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can explore nearby villages like Peroulades and Agios Stefanos, or take a day trip to the nearby island of Paxos.

Conclusion

Arillas is a beautiful and peaceful village that offers visitors a chance to relax and explore the traditional Greek way of life. From the stunning beaches and natural beauty to the historical sites and cultural attractions, there is something for everyone in Arillas. We hope this travel guide has helped you plan a memorable trip to this beautiful Greek destination.
