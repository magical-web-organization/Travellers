A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Lefkada, Greece

Lefkada is a beautiful island in Greece, located in the Ionian Sea. It is a great destination for those who love nature, adventure, and relaxation. This travel guide to Lefkada includes everything you need to know to plan your trip.

Introduction to Lefkada

Lefkada, also known as Lefkas, is an island located in the Ionian Sea, on the west coast of Greece. It is connected to the mainland by a bridge, making it easily accessible. The island is known for its turquoise waters, sandy beaches, and beautiful landscapes. It is the perfect destination for those who love sun, sea, and sand.

List of Places to Visit in Lefkada

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Lefkada has a rich history and culture, and there are several museums and historical places that you can visit to learn more about the island’s past. Here are some of the must-visit attractions:

  • Archaeological Museum of Lefkada
  • Monastery of Faneromeni
  • Castle of Agia Mavra
  • Phonograph Museum

Natural Attractions of Lefkada: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lefkada is blessed with natural beauty, and there are plenty of outdoor adventures and scenic views that you can experience on the island. Here are some of the popular natural attractions:

  • Porto Katsiki Beach
  • Egremni Beach
  • Kathisma Beach
  • Nydri Waterfalls

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lefkada

Lefkada is also a great destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts, thanks to its stunning trails. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes on the island:

  • Melissa Gorge
  • Lefkada Monastery Trail
  • Skaros Mountain

Best Things to Do in Lefkada: Activities and Tours

There are several activities and tours that you can enjoy on the island. Here are some of the best things that you can do in Lefkada:

  • Windsurfing and kitesurfing at Vassiliki Beach
  • Scuba diving at Meganisi Island
  • Sailing tours to nearby islands
  • Paragliding over Kathisma Beach

Week-Long Itinerary for Lefkada

If you have a week to spend in Lefkada, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival and take a stroll around Lefkada Town. Day 2: Explore the west coast, including Egremni Beach and Porto Katsiki Beach. Day 3: Visit the villages of Karya and Exanthia. Day 4: Go hiking in the Melissa Gorge. Day 5: Take a boat trip to Meganisi Island. Day 6: Visit the Monastery of Faneromeni and enjoy the sunset at Agios Nikitas. Day 7: Relax at Kathisma Beach and enjoy the water sports.

Food and Drink Scene of Lefkada

Lefkada offers a delicious cuisine that is worth exploring. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Savoro (fried fish with rosemary and garlic)
  • Bourdeto (fish stew)
  • Stifado (beef stew)

Local Beverages

  • Wine (try the Robola wine)
  • Kafes Lefkada (local coffee)

Where to Stay in Lefkada: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Lefkada has several accommodation options, ranging from hotels to hostels and guesthouses. Here are some of the top-rated options:

  • Hotel Porto Ligia
  • Hotel Scorpios
  • Hotel Thalero
  • Ionian Blue

What to Buy in Lefkada: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Lefkada is known for its local products that are worth taking home as souvenirs. Here are some of the top souvenirs and local products that you can buy:

  • Olive oil
  • Honey
  • Leather Products

Local Culture and Customs of Lefkada

The locals of Lefkada are friendly and welcoming. It is important to respect their customs and culture. Here are some of the customs of Lefkada:

  • Do not enter churches wearing shorts or sleeveless shirts.
  • Avoid excessive noise during the afternoon siesta time.
  • Do not litter on the beaches.

Best Time to Visit Lefkada

The best time to visit Lefkada is between May and September when the weather is warm, and the sea is perfect for swimming and water sports.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lefkada

Do:

  • Respect the locals and their culture.
  • Try the local cuisine and wine.
  • Explore the natural beauty of the island.

Don’t:

  • Litter on the beaches or in public places.
  • Wear revealing clothes when visiting churches and other religious sites.
  • Overspend on souvenirs and local products.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lefkada

Lefkada is a safe destination, but you should take precautions to keep your belongings safe, especially on the beaches. Also, be careful when driving on the narrow and winding roads.

Health in Lefkada

Lefkada has a good healthcare system, with both public and private hospitals. However, it is recommended to have travel insurance that covers medical emergencies.

Getting to Lefkada: Transportation Options and Logistics

Lefkada is easily accessible by air, land, and sea. Here are the transportation options and logistics:

Airports Near Lefkada

The closest airport to Lefkada is Aktion airport, located 20 km away. There are several daily flights from Athens.

Bus Stations Near Lefkada

The intercity bus station in Lefkada is located in the town of Lefkada and provides daily connections to Athens and other major cities in Greece.

Other Options

You can also reach Lefkada by car or taxi from Athens or other nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Lefkada

The best way to explore Lefkada is by renting a car, scooter, or ATV. You can also use the local buses that connect the major towns and villages.

Where to Go Next from Lefkada: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several nearby destinations and excursions that you can explore after visiting Lefkada. Here are some of the options:

  • Corfu Island
  • Parga town
  • Acheron River

Conclusion

Lefkada is a beautiful island that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and cultural attractions. This travel guide will help you plan your trip to Lefkada and make the most of your stay on the island.
