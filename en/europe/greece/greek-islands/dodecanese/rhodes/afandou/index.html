Introduction to Afandou

Afandou is a charming little town situated on the eastern coast of the Greek island of Rhodes in the Dodecanese. The town is renowned for its fine sandy beach, crystal-clear waters, and rich cultural heritage which dates back to ancient times. Afandou is the perfect destination for travelers seeking a peaceful and picturesque place to unwind, explore ancient ruins, and indulge in some of the best Greek cuisine.

List of Places to Visit in Afandou

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Afandou is steeped in history and mythology. The town square is home to the stunningly beautiful Afandou Church, built in the 17th century in honor of the Virgin Mary. The church is a masterpiece of architecture, with intricate carvings and frescoes on the walls, and a bell-tower that affords a breathtaking panoramic view of the surrounding countryside.

Another must-visit landmark is the ancient city of Kallithea, located just a few kilometers south of Afandou. The city was built by the Roman Emperor Diocletian in the 3rd century BC and later became a popular spa center during the Ottoman era. Today, Kallithea is a popular tourist destination known for its thermal springs, imposing buildings, and beautiful gardens.

Natural Attractions of Afandou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Afandou is surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty. One of the most popular attractions is the Afandou Beach, which stretches for more than 2 kilometers along the crystal-clear waters of the Aegean Sea. The beach is perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

For a more exhilarating experience, take a hike to the top of the Tsambika Mountain, which towers 300 meters above the town. The trail is not for the faint-hearted, but the bird’s-eye view of the town and surrounding areas is worth the effort.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Afandou

Afandou is a paradise for adventure seekers. The town and its surrounding areas offer numerous hiking and biking trails for nature lovers. A popular route is the 13-kilometer trek from Afandou to the ancient city of Lindos, which offers breathtaking views of the sea and mountains.

Best Things to Do in Afandou: Activities and Tours

Apart from swimming, sunbathing, and water sports on Afandou Beach, there are many activities to enjoy in Afandou. The town is famous for its golf course, located in the stunning Afandou Bay. The Afandou Golf Club is considered one of the best in Greece and attracts golf enthusiasts from around the world.

Another popular activity is a boat tour to nearby beaches and coves. The boat tours offer an excellent opportunity to explore the stunning coastline of Rhodes, swim in secluded bays, and experience the breathtaking natural beauty of the island.

Week-Long Itinerary for Afandou

Day 1: Arrive in Afandou, check into your hotel, and explore the town square and the Afandou Church.

Day 2: Visit the ancient city of Kallithea and spend the day exploring the ruins, gardens, and thermal springs.

Day 3: Take a hike up the Tsambika Mountain and enjoy the breathtaking views from the top.

Day 4: Spend the day relaxing on the Afandou Beach and indulge in some water sports.

Day 5: Enjoy a round of golf at the Afandou Golf Club, or take a boat tour to nearby beaches and coves.

Day 6: Take a trip to the nearby town of Lindos and explore the ancient acropolis and beautiful sandy beaches.

Day 7: Spend the day indulging in local cuisine and shopping for souvenirs to bring back home.

Food and Drink Scene of Afandou

Must-Try Dishes

Afandou features some of the best restaurants and tavernas, serving delicious Greek cuisine. One must-try dish is the traditional Greek salad, consisting of tomatoes, cucumber, onion, feta cheese, and olives, drizzled with olive oil and herbs. Other popular dishes include grilled lamb chops, moussaka, and spanakopita.

Local Beverages

Rhodes is famous for its wines, and Afandou produces some of the best wines in Greece. Try the sweet red or white wine varieties, or indulge in a glass of ouzo, the traditional Greek drink.

Where to Stay in Afandou: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Afandou offers a wide range of accommodation options, to suit all tastes and budgets. Some popular hotels include the Afandou Bay Resort Suites, the Kladissos Hotel, and the Maravelias Apartments.

What to Buy in Afandou: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Afandou is famous for its ceramics, handmade pottery, and traditional Greek textiles. Other souvenirs include olive oil, honey, and local spices.

Local Culture and Customs of Afandou

The local culture of Afandou is influenced by ancient Greek traditions and customs. The residents of the town are warm and hospitable, and visitors are welcome to experience the local culture and cuisine.

Best Time to Visit Afandou

The best time to visit Afandou is between May and September, when the weather is warm and sunny, and the sea is perfect for swimming and water sports.

Do’s and Don’ts of Afandou

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local cuisine and wine.
  • Spend time on the Afandou Beach.
  • Explore the ancient ruins and monuments.
  • Engage with the locals and learn about their culture.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing in public places.
  • Litter or damage the natural beauty of the area.
  • Ignore the dress codes when visiting churches or religious sites.
  • Disrespect the locals or their customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Afandou

Afandou is a safe place to visit, with very low crime rates. However, visitors should take normal precautions to ensure their safety, such as securing valuables and staying alert in crowded areas.

Health in Afandou

Visitors to Afandou should be up-to-date on routine vaccinations and should take precautions to avoid mosquito bites to prevent the spread of mosquito-borne diseases like West Nile Virus and Zika.

Getting to Afandou: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Afandou

The nearest airport to Afandou is the Diagoras International Airport, located in Rhodes, approximately 15 kilometers from the town.

Bus Stations Near Afandou

There is a bus station in Afandou, offering regular services to nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi to reach Afandou from the airport or the nearby towns.

How to Get Around in Afandou

Afandou is a small town and can easily be explored on foot. Visitors can also rent a bike or a car to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Afandou: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Afandou is close to many other popular destinations on the island of Rhodes, such as Lindos, Faliraki, and Rhodes town. Visitors can take a day trip to these places to explore their ancient ruins, picturesque streets, and beautiful beaches.

Conclusion

Afandou is a charming little town that offers visitors an opportunity to unwind, explore ancient ruins, indulge in delicious cuisine, and experience the warm hospitality of the locals. Plan your trip to Afandou using this comprehensive guide and discover the best that this beautiful town has to offer.
