Introduction to Archangelos

Nestled in the southeastern coast of Rhodes Island in the Dodecanese region, Archangelos is a charming village surrounded by mountains. It boasts of rich history and natural beauty, enticing travelers with its quaint streets, traditional tavernas, and picturesque alleyways.

Despite its popularity, Archangelos remains untouched by mass tourism, preserving its authentic Greek charm and traditions. In this travel guide, we will explore the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay in Archangelos.

List of Places to Visit in Archangelos

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Archangelos Castle

Built in the 14th century by the Knights of Rhodes, Archangelos Castle sits on top of a hill with stunning views of the village and the sea. The castle boasts of towers, gates, and barracks still preserved to this day. A visit to the castle should definitely be on every traveler’s list.

Folklore Museum

The Archangelos Folklore Museum exhibits everyday life items and agricultural tools that were used in the past, creating an immersive experience for visitors who want to learn more about the village’s history.

Church of the Archangel Michael

The Church of the Archangel Michael is a beautiful Byzantine-style church built in the 19th century. Its architecture, frescoes, and religious artifacts are worth visiting. Every September, the church hosts a three-day festival in honor of the village’s patron saint, Archangel Michael.

Natural Attractions of Archangelos: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Stegna Beach

Stegna Beach is a peaceful haven located 3 km from Archangelos. It boasts of crystal-clear waters and a long stretch of sandy shore. The beach has several restaurants and bars that serve fresh seafood and traditional Greek cuisine, making it an ideal place for families and couples looking for a relaxing holiday.

Seven Springs

Seven Springs is a lush green paradise that boasts a natural spring with seven water outlets that flow into a lake. The springs are surrounded by dense forests of pine and oak trees, making it an ideal spot for nature enthusiasts.

Tsambika Mountain

Tsambika Mountain is a popular hiking spot that offers panoramic views of the Aegean Sea and the surrounding areas. The trail is easy to moderate and is suitable for walkers, hikers, and mountain bikers of all levels.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Archangelos

Archangelos has a plethora of hiking and biking trails that offer opportunities to explore the stunning landscape of the region. Here are some of the popular routes:

Archangelos-Kolimbia Trail

This is an easy 6 km trail that passes through the village of Stegna and ends in the seaside village of Kolimbia, overlooking the sea.

Tsambika Mountain Trail

The 3 km hike to the top of Tsambika Mountain is an exhilarating experience, offering stunning views of the island.

Best Things to Do in Archangelos: Activities and Tours

Archery

Archangelos is home to the award-winning Archery Club of Rhodes, offering visitors an opportunity to learn and practice this ancient sport.

Village Cooking Class

Get a taste of authentic Greek cuisine by taking a village cooking class. Learn how to make traditional dishes such as moussaka and tzatziki, using locally-sourced ingredients.

Island Tour

Explore the island of Rhodes by taking a guided tour that will take you to all the major tourist attractions, including the Palace of the Grand Master and the Acropolis of Lindos.

Week-Long Itinerary for Archangelos

Day 1: Arrival in Archangelos, check-in at your hotel and explore the village.

Day 2: Visit the Castle of Archangelos and the Church of the Archangel Michael.

Day 3: Take a hike to the top of Tsambika Mountain and enjoy the stunning views.

Day 4: Spend the day at Stegna Beach, sunbathe, and swim in the crystal-clear waters.

Day 5: Take an island tour and explore the must-visit tourist attractions.

Day 6: Go on a village cooking class and learn how to prepare traditional Greek dishes.

Day 7: Take a day trip to the nearby island of Symi and explore its charming harbor and colorful houses.

Where to Stay in Archangelos: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hotel Stegna Mare

Located just a kilometer from Stegna Beach, Hotel Stegna Mare offers comfortable accommodation with a pool and a bar.

Cannas Beach Hotel

Cannas Beach Hotel is located just a few steps from the beach and features an outdoor pool, restaurant, and bar.

Archangelos Village Hotel

Archangelos Village Hotel is a traditional-style hotel located in the village center, offering a peaceful and authentic Greek experience.

Local Culture and Customs of Archangelos

Archangelos is a village steeped in tradition and has several customs that are unique to the region. One such custom is the annual “Ciao” festivity, where young boys and girls dress up in traditional costumes and perform traditional dances and songs.

Best Time to Visit Archangelos

The best time to visit Archangelos is during the summer months from June to September when the weather is warm and sunny, and the sea is calm and inviting. However, the village can get crowded during peak season, so it’s recommended to visit during the shoulder season months of May and October.

Do’s and Don’ts of Archangelos

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local cuisine and wines.
  • Explore the hiking and biking trails around the village.

Don’t:

  • Take photos inside churches without permission.
  • Dress inappropriately when visiting religious sites.
  • Leave trash behind on trails or beaches.

Safety and Health

Safety in Archangelos

Archangelos is a safe village, but visitors should always be cautious and keep their belongings close by, especially in crowded areas.

Health in Archangelos

Archangelos has a primary healthcare center and a pharmacy for basic medical needs. In case of emergencies, visitors can visit the hospital in Rhodes town, which is a 30-minute drive from the village.

Getting to Archangelos: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Archangelos

The nearest airport to Archangelos is the Rhodes International Airport “Diagoras,” located 30 km away. Visitors can take a taxi or a rental car to reach the village.

Bus Stations Near Archangelos

There are several bus stations in Rhodes town that offer connections to Archangelos. Visitors can take a local bus or a private transfer to reach the village.

Other Options

Visitors can also opt for a taxi or a rental car to reach Archangelos. It’s recommended to book these in advance to avoid any hassle.

How to Get Around in Archangelos

Archangelos is a small village, and getting around on foot is the best option. However, visitors can also rent bicycles or motorbikes to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Archangelos: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Lindos

Visit the charming seaside village of Lindos, located just a 40-minute drive from Archangelos. Lindos boasts of an ancient acropolis, stunning beaches, and traditional tavernas.

Symi

Take a day trip to the stunning island of Symi, located just a short boat ride from Rhodes. Symi boasts of a picturesque harbor, colorful houses, and stunning beaches.

Conclusion

Archangelos is a hidden gem in Rhodes, offering travelers a glimpse of traditional Greek village life. With its stunning natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and rich cultural heritage, Archangelos is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Rhodes.
