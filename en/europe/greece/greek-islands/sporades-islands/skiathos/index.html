Introduction to Skiathos

Skiathos is a small, lush island located in the Sporades Islands, in the Greek Islands, in Greece, in Europe. It is well-known for its blue flag beaches, turquoise waters, vibrant nightlife, and historical landmarks, making it a popular travel destination for tourists.

With its crystal-clear waters, unspoiled natural beauty, and amazing seaside atmosphere, Skiathos offers a perfect getaway for adventure lovers and beach bums alike.

In this comprehensive guide, we will provide everything you need to know to plan your ultimate Skiathos vacation, including the places to visit, the best time to go, where to stay, and what to do.

List of Places to Visit in Skiathos

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Skiathos is also rich in history and culture, with many must-see historical landmarks and museums, including:

  • Evangelistria Monastery: Located in the hills above Skiathos Town, this 18th-century monastery is a testament to the strength and resilience of the Greek Orthodox faith. It was once a meeting place for the Greeks to plan the start of the 1821 revolution, which led to Greece’s independence from the Ottoman Empire. Now it is a major tourist attraction, and visitors can explore its beautiful architecture and holy relics.

  • Bourtzi: This small peninsula located in the heart of Skiathos Town was once a Venetian fortress that protected the island from pirates. Today, it is an open-air museum and a perfect place to watch the sunset.

  • Skiathos Old Town: A charming, traditional Greek town with narrow, winding streets, white houses, and colorful flower-filled balconies. Perfect for a leisurely stroll, shopping, and dining.

Natural Attractions of Skiathos: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Skiathos is blessed with natural beauty, from its pine forests to its beautiful beaches. Here are some attractions not to miss:

  • Koukounaries Beach: Known as the “golden sand” beach, this stunning, crescent-shaped beach is considered one of the most beautiful beaches in the world. Its crystal-clear blue waters, soft white sand, and lush green pine forest backdrop make it the perfect place for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing.

  • Lalaria Beach: Accessible only by boat and located on the northern coast of Skiathos, this pebble beach is famous for its white cliffs and turquoise waters, making it especially popular with nature lovers and adventurous swimmers.

  • Kastro: A former medieval fortress city that offers a magnificent view of the island’s beautiful coastline. Hike up to the ruins, and soak in the magnificent views of the mountains and sea.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Skiathos

If you love hiking or biking, Skiathos has plenty of trails to explore. Here are some of our recommended routes:

  • Hiking: The Skiathos trail network covers 200 km (124 mi) of trails that offer stunning views of the island, including views of Skiathos Town and the surrounding islands of Skopelos and Alonissos.

  • Biking: For a unique experience, cycle around Skiathos on a guided bike tour. From mountain biking to road cycling, there are plenty of trails and routes to choose from that cater to different ability levels.

Best Things to Do in Skiathos: Activities and Tours

For a holiday packed with adventure and excitement, Skiathos has a range of activities and tours to offer. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Boat trip to the Sporades Islands: Skiathos is the perfect base to explore the beautiful Sporades Islands. Join in a guided boat tour, and get a chance to visit Skopelos and Alonissos, or explore the remote islands of Gioura or Piperi.

  • Water sports: From jet skiing to windsurfing and kiteboarding, Skiathos has a wide range of water sports activities available on its beautiful beaches.

  • Nightlife: Skiathos undoubtedly has one of the most vibrant nightlife scenes in the Greek islands. From beach clubs to cocktail bars, the nightlife in Skiathos is a must-see and a perfect way to spend a night out with your friends.

Week-Long Itinerary for Skiathos

Here is an itinerary to help you plan your week-long vacation in Skiathos:

Day 1: Explore Skiathos Old Town and Bourtzi

Day 2: Visit the Evangelistria Monastery

Day 3: Spend the day hiking or biking around the island.

Day 4: Relax at Koukounaries Beach.

Day 5: Boat trip to the Sporades Islands.

Day 6: Explore the Kastro and Lalaria Beach.

Day 7: Spend your last day trying out some water sports activities.

Food and Drink Scene of Skiathos

Must-Try Dishes

Skiathos is a food lover’s paradise, with a range of fresh seafood dishes, traditional Greek cuisine, and international cuisines. Here are some of the must-try dishes:

  • Revithada: A traditional Greek dish made from chickpeas, onions, tomatoes, and olive oil. Usually served with bread.

  • Skordalia: Another traditional dish made from mashed potatoes and garlic, and seasoned with olive oil.

  • Baklava: A sweet pastry made from layers of thin dough filled with chopped nuts and honey syrup.

Local Beverages

In addition to its food, Skiathos is known for its wine, which is made using local grape varieties. The island also produces a range of spirits, including ouzo and tsipouro, a type of brandy.

Where to Stay in Skiathos: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Skiathos has a range of accommodation options that cater to all budgets. Whether you are looking for luxury or a more budget-friendly option, here are some recommended places to stay:

  • Skiathos Palace Hotel: A luxury resort located on a hillside overlooking the Aegean Sea. It has a private beach area, a spa, and a restaurant serving traditional Greek cuisine.

  • Hotel Morfo: A boutique hotel located near the town center. Its rooms are furnished in a modern style and feature a balcony with a sea view.

  • Morfo Apartments: A budget-friendly option near the town center with well-equipped apartments and friendly staff.

What to Buy in Skiathos: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

As with most Greek islands, Skiathos has a range of unique souvenirs and local products to bring home. Here are some of our favorites:

  • Olive oil and honey: Skiathos produces some of the best olive oil and honey in Greece.

  • Dried herbs: Skiathos is home to a wide range of herbs, and many are dried and sold as seasoning.

  • Furs: Skiathos has many shops selling high-quality furs made from mink and fox.

Local Culture and Customs of Skiathos

The locals in Skiathos are warm and friendly, and they take pride in their culture and traditions. Like all Greek people, they value family, food, and hospitality.

Best Time to Visit Skiathos

The best time to visit Skiathos is from May to September when the weather is warm and sunny. July and August are peak tourist season, so expect crowds of people and higher prices.

Do’s and Don’ts of Skiathos

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Explore the island at your own pace.
  • Try local food and drinks.
  • Wear sunscreen and hats.

Don’t:

  • Litter.
  • Wear inappropriate clothing in religious sites.
  • Drink and drive.
  • Take wildlife as souvenirs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Skiathos

Skiathos is a safe destination for tourists. However, always be aware of pickpockets and petty theft, especially in busy tourist areas.

Health in Skiathos

Skiathos has good hospitals and medical facilities with English-speaking doctors. However, it’s essential to travel with proper insurance that covers medical expenses.

Getting to Skiathos: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Skiathos

Skiathos is served by Skiathos International Airport, which is located on the northeast coast of the island. There is also a frequent ferry service departing from Volos and other ports on the Greek mainland.

Bus Stations Near Skiathos

The Skiathos KTEL bus service provides frequent services to Skiathos Town and to other towns on the island.

Other Options

Taxis are readily available for hire, and a water taxi service can take you to other smaller, nearby islands.

How to Get Around in Skiathos

To explore the island, the most convenient option is to rent a car. You can also rent a scooter or a bicycle.

Boat rentals are also available for exploring the nearby islands.

Where to Go Next from Skiathos: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Skiathos is an excellent base for exploring the Sporades Islands, including Skopelos and Alonissos.

Conclusion

Skiathos is a beautiful and vibrant destination with something for everyone. Whether you love sunbathing on pristine beaches, exploring ancient ruins, or trying local cuisine, Skiathos has it all. Use this comprehensive guide to plan your ultimate Skiathos adventure.
