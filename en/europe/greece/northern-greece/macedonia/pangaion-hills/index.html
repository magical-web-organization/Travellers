Introduction to Pangaion Hills

Pangaion Hills is a mountain range in the northern part of Greece’s Macedonia region. It is a fascinating natural wonderland that boasts an array of outdoor adventures and scenic views. The region is also home to various cultural attractions such as museums and historical places that showcase the rich Greek history and heritage. Whether you’re an adventure seeker or a culture enthusiast, Pangaion Hills has something in store for you.

In this comprehensive tour guide, we will explore the must-visit places, hidden gems, hiking and biking routes, food and drink scene, local culture and customs, and essential travel tips.

List of Places to Visit in Pangaion Hills

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Philippi Archaeological Site and Museum - Explore the ancient city of Philippi, the place where the apostle Paul first preached Christianity to Europeans, and learn about the history of the Roman, Byzantine, and Ottoman empires. The Philippi Archaeological Museum exhibits unique collections of artifacts from the excavations of the site.
  • Drama Archaeological Museum - Discover the ancient civilizations of Macedonia through the unique collection of artifacts from different eras, including the prehistoric and classical periods, at the Drama Archaeological Museum.
  • Lydia Lithos Museum - This museum in Kavala showcases the rich industrial history of the area and the art of the local craftsmen.

Natural Attractions of Pangaion Hills: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Pangaion Hills National Park - A protected area of outstanding natural beauty, Pangaion Hills National Park offers a wide range of outdoor activities such as hiking, mountain biking, and horse riding. The park boasts an array of endemic flora and fauna, including over 40 species of orchids.
  • Dadia Forest Reserve - Located north of Pangaion Hills, Dadia forest reserve is a unique ecological system that houses a variety of bird species, including some endangered ones.
  • Lake Kerkini - Located in the Serres region of Greece, Lake Kerkini is a stunning natural paradise that offers a wide range of activities such as bird watching, hiking, and cycling.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pangaion Hills

Hiking Routes

  • Agios Belessis – Livaditis Route - A five-hour-long hike, this route takes you through a charming landscape of lush vegetation, wildflowers, and rock formations. You will also discover a stunning waterfall and a chapel tucked away on the mountain.
  • Zygos Route - A four-hour hike, this route takes you to the top of the Zygos Peak, where you’ll be rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and the sea.
  • Kroussia – Jura Village Route - This six-hour hike is perfect for nature lovers who want to soak in the beauty of Pangaion Hills. The route passes through the Kroussia forest and leads to the picturesque village of Jura.

Biking Routes

  • Asprovalta – Volvi Lake Route - This 45-km-long route starts in the town of Asprovalta and takes you along the coast of the Strymon Gulf. The route offers stunning views of the sea and leads to the picturesque Volvi Lake.
  • Kavala – Filippoi Route - This 60-km-long route takes you through some of the most beautiful parts of Pangaion Hills, including the ancient city of Philippi. The route offers some challenging climbs but is also incredibly rewarding in terms of scenic beauty.
  • Nine Springs Route - This 30-km-long route takes you through the picturesque valleys of Pangaion Hills and leads to the stunning Nine Springs waterfall.

Best Things to Do in Pangaion Hills: Activities and Tours

  • Wine Tasting Tours - Pangaion Hills is famous for its wine production, and the region boasts some of the best wineries in Greece. Take a wine tasting tour to discover the unique flavors of the local wines and learn about their production process.
  • Jeep Safari Tours - Explore the hidden gems of Pangaion Hills through an exhilarating jeep safari tour. You can choose from a range of tours that take you through the mountainous terrain, picturesque villages, and ancient ruins.
  • Hot Air Balloon Rides - Experience the breathtaking beauty of Pangaion Hills from up above through a hot air balloon ride. The ride offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and is an unforgettable experience.

Week-Long Itinerary for Pangaion Hills

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrive in Kavala and spend the day exploring the city, including the Kavala Castle and the Old Town.
Day 2 Visit the ancient city of Philippi and the Philippi Archaeological Museum.
Day 3 Go on a Wine Tasting Tour and explore the local wineries.
Day 4 Discover the Dadia Forest Reserve and go bird watching.

Food and Drink Scene of Pangaion Hills

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kavourmas - A classic Macedonian dish, Kavourmas is a type of slow-cooked meat that is typically made with pork, chicken, or lamb. It is usually served with potatoes and peppers.
  • Kasseri Cheese - Made from sheep’s milk, Kasseri is a traditional cheese that is widely popular in Macedonia. It is a semi-hard cheese that is perfect for grilling or frying.
  • Soutzoukakia - A type of Greek meatball, Soutzoukakia is a popular dish in Pangaion Hills. It is usually made with ground beef and served with tomato sauce and rice.

Local Beverages

  • Ouzo - A Greek anise-flavored aperitif, Ouzo is a popular drink in Greece. It is usually served with ice and water and is best enjoyed with some traditional Greek meze.
  • Tsipouro - Tsipouro is a type of Greek brandy that is made from grapes. It is typically served as a digestive after a meal and is often accompanied by some dried fruits and nuts.

Where to Stay in Pangaion Hills: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Akropolis Hotel - Located in the heart of Kavala, this hotel offers stunning panoramic views of the city and the sea. The hotel boasts a rooftop bar and a restaurant that serves traditional Greek cuisine.
  • Elia Traditional Guesthouse - Located in the picturesque village of Kato Nevrokopi, this guesthouse offers comfortable accommodation in a traditional setting. The guesthouse is also home to a restaurant that serves delicious local cuisine.
  • Guesthouse To Perasma - Located in the village of Nikisiani, this guesthouse is housed in a beautifully restored mansion and offers comfortable accommodation in a serene setting. The guesthouse boasts a courtyard garden and a restaurant that serves traditional Greek dishes.

What to Buy in Pangaion Hills: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Wine - Pangaion Hills is famous for its wine production, and you can buy some of the best local wines as souvenirs.
  • Kasseri Cheese - Bring home some locally produced Kasseri Cheese, which is perfect for grilling and frying.
  • Handmade Jewelry - There’s a wide range of handmade jewelry available in Pangaion Hills that showcases the unique style and art of the local craftsmen.

Local Culture and Customs of Pangaion Hills

Pangaion Hills is home to a vibrant and diverse community that celebrates its rich history and heritage. The locals are friendly and hospitable and welcome visitors with open arms. The region is also known for its unique traditional dances and music, which are often performed during the local festivals and celebrations.

Best Time to Visit Pangaion Hills

The best time to visit Pangaion Hills is during the spring and summer months (April to September) when the weather is mild and sunny, and the landscape is at its most beautiful. During these months, you can enjoy a range of outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and bird watching.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pangaion Hills

Do:

  • Learn a few basic Greek phrases
  • Be mindful of the environment and avoid littering

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing when visiting religious sites
  • Disrespect the local culture and traditions
  • Ignore safety precautions when engaging in outdoor activities

Safety and Health

Safety in Pangaion Hills

Pangaion Hills is a safe destination, and the locals are friendly and hospitable. However, it’s essential to take necessary safety precautions when engaging in outdoor activities such as hiking and biking. Always wear appropriate gear, carry enough water and food, and be mindful of the weather conditions.

Health in Pangaion Hills

Pangaion Hills has a good healthcare system, and there are several medical facilities and hospitals in the area. However, it’s always advisable to carry a basic first-aid kit and any necessary medication with you during your travels.

Getting to Pangaion Hills: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Pangaion Hills

The nearest airport to Pangaion Hills is the Kavala International Airport “Alexander the Great,” which is located about 30 km south of Kavala. Other airports near the region include the Thessaloniki International Airport and the Sofia Airport in Bulgaria.

Bus Stations Near Pangaion Hills

KTEL is the public bus service that runs throughout Greece, and there are several bus stations around Pangaion Hills that connect the region to various cities and towns in Greece.

Other Options

You can also travel to Pangaion Hills by car, taxi, or private transfer. It’s advisable to book your transportation in advance to avoid any last-minute hassles.

How to Get Around in Pangaion Hills

The best way to explore Pangaion Hills is by car, as it offers the most flexibility and allows you to explore the region at your own pace. You can also rent a bike or a scooter to explore the area.

Where to Go Next from Pangaion Hills: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Thessaloniki - The second-largest city in Greece, Thessaloniki is a vibrant and cultural destination that is home to several ancient monuments and landmarks, including the White Tower and the Aristotelous Square.
  • Skiathos Island - Located in the North Sporades island group, Skiathos is a stunning island that offers some of the best beaches in Greece. You can access the island by ferry or plane from Thessaloniki or Athens.
  • Chalkidiki - A peninsula in northern Greece, Chalkidiki boasts a stunning natural landscape that includes golden beaches, crystal-clear waters, and dense pine forests.

Conclusion

Pangaion Hills is a hidden gem in Northern Greece, offering an array of natural wonders, cultural attractions, hiking routes, and outdoor adventures. Whether you’re a culture enthusiast or an adventure seeker, Pangaion Hills has something for everyone.
