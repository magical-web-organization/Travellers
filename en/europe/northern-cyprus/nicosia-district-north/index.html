Introduction to Nicosia District (North)

Nicosia district (North) is a district located in Northern Cyprus, Europe. The region is known for its natural beauty, history and cultural importance. Nicosia is a beautiful city that is divided into two parts – North Nicosia under the Turkish Cypriot administration and South Nicosia under the Greek Cypriot administration. The city is home to several mosques, churches and museums and is considered to be the cultural center of Northern Cyprus.

Local Culture and Customs of Nicosia district (North)

Nicosia district (North) has a rich and diverse culture that is influenced by Greek, Turkish, and Arabic traditions. The locals are friendly and hospitable, and they value their traditions and customs. Here are some cultural practices that you should be aware of:

  • Remove your shoes before entering a mosque or someone’s home
  • Respect local customs and traditions
