Introduction to Perm Krai

Located in the Urals of Russia, in Eastern Europe, Perm Krai boasts natural beauty with plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures. It houses multiple museums and historical sites to cater to the culturally inclined. If you’re planning a trip to Perm Krai, this travel guide will provide you with all the information you need to make the most of your trip.

Local Culture and Customs of Perm Krai

The local culture in Perm Krai is rich and diverse, with a blend of ethnic cultures and traditions. The people of Perm Krai are friendly and hospitable, and it is common to greet each other with a handshake or a kiss on the cheek. When in a social setting, it is considered proper to address others by their first name.

Best Time to Visit Perm Krai

The best time to visit Perm Krai is from May to September. During this period, the weather is mild, and you can enjoy outdoor activities without encountering snow.

Do’s and Don’ts of Perm Krai

Do:

  1. Respect local customs and traditions.
  2. Try local cuisine and beverages.
  3. Take part in outdoor activities and sightseeing tours.

Don’t:

  1. Wear inappropriate clothing in religious places.
  2. Be loud and disruptive in public places.
  3. Engage in illegal activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Perm Krai

Perm Krai is generally safe for tourists. However, it is advised to be aware of your surroundings and take necessary precautions like keeping your valuables safe and avoiding dark alleys and places.

Health in Perm Krai

Medical facilities in Perm Krai are generally reliable and are available in most cities. However, it would be best if you had travel insurance to ensure you are covered for any medical emergencies during your trip.
