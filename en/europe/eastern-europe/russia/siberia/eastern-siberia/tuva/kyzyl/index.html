Introduction to Kyzyl

Kyzyl, the capital of the Tuva Republic, is a city surrounded by mountains and stunning landscapes, located in Siberia, Russia. The city has unique cultural and natural attractions that attract travelers from all over the world. This travel guide will help you plan your trip to Kyzyl with information about the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Kyzyl

Here is a list of the top places to visit in Kyzyl:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tuva National Museum of Republic: This museum showcases the rich history and culture of the Tuva Republic through its exhibits and artifacts.
  • Monument to Center of Asia: It is a popular landmark of Kyzyl city located in Kyzyl. This monument marks the geographical center of Asia and offers great views of the surrounding landscapes.
  • The House of Culture: Don’t miss the opportunity to witness the culture and traditions of the Tuvan people through music, dance, and art performances.

Natural Attractions of Kyzyl: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Shunut Mountain: Located just a few miles from Kyzyl, Shunut Mountain offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Chadynka River: Enjoy fishing or a leisurely walk through the picturesque Chadynka River.
  • Kyzyl-Tuu: Kyzyl-Tuu is located in the southern part of the Tuva Republic. It is a picturesque area that is perfect for hiking and camping.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kyzyl

Kyzyl has some of the impressive biking and hiking trails, including:

  • Bike ride through Chadynka River
  • Hiking tour of Ubsunurskaya Kotlovina National Park
  • Exciting off-road biking route through Shunut Mountain trails

Best Things to Do in Kyzyl: Activities and Tours

  • Take a city tour of Kyzyl: A city tour is the perfect way to explore the cultural attractions of Kyzyl.
  • Rafting trip through the Chadynka River: Take a rafting trip to enjoy the natural beauty and adventure of the Chadynka River.
  • Visit the throat singing performances: Experience the unique Tuvan tradition of throat singing performances.

Food and Drink Scene of Kyzyl

Must-Try Dishes

  • Tuvan khuushuur: Deep-fried meat-filled dumplings that are a popular local dish.
  • Chai: Traditional tea served with milk and salt.
  • Boortsog: A type of deep-fried dough bread that is served as a dessert or snack.

Local Beverages

  • Airag: Fermented horse milk is a popular beverage in Kyzyl.
  • Kumys: Fermented mare’s milk is another traditional beverage enjoyed by the locals.

Where to Stay in Kyzyl: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Here are some of the best places to stay in Kyzyl:

  • Altyn-Kol guesthouse: A budget-friendly option located near the city center.
  • Tuva Hotel: A comfortable hotel with great amenities located in the heart of the city.
  • Kyzyl Hotel: A luxurious hotel with beautiful views of the surrounding landscapes.

What to Buy in Kyzyl: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Tuvan throat singing CDs: Bring home a CD of traditional Tuvan throat singing.
  • Tuvan souvenirs: Purchase locally made souvenirs such as shamanic drums, jewelry, and leather products.

Local Culture and Customs of Kyzyl

The Tuvan people have their unique set of traditions and customs that travelers should be aware of during their visit. It is customary to take off your shoes when entering someone’s home in Kyzyl, and traditional Tuvan attire is worn on special occasions.

Best Time to Visit Kyzyl

The best time to visit Kyzyl is from May to September when the weather is mild and comfortable for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kyzyl

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions of the Tuvan people.
  • Tip your tour guides and waiters.

Don’t:

  • Wear shoes inside someone’s home.
  • Disrespect the natural environment by littering or polluting.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kyzyl

Kyzyl is considered a safe city for travelers, but it is recommended to take precautions and keep your belongings safe when out and about in the city.

Health in Kyzyl

It is recommended to drink bottled water and to bring your prescription medications with you.

Getting to Kyzyl: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Kyzyl

  • Kyzyl Airport (KYZ): Located just 3 kilometers from the city center and served by domestic flights.

Bus Stations Near Kyzyl

  • Kyzyl Bus Station: Located in the city center and served by local buses.

Other Options

  • Train: The nearest train station is located in Abakan and is connected to Kyzyl by bus.

How to Get Around in Kyzyl

A taxi or a private car is the most convenient and reliable way to get around Kyzyl and explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Kyzyl: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Shagonar: Known for its beautiful landscapes and rich history, Shagonar is just a short drive from Kyzyl.
  • Khakassia: A neighboring republic, Khakassia is known for its natural beauty and historical sites.

Conclusion

Kyzyl is a hidden gem in Siberia that has something to offer every traveler. From cultural attractions to outdoor adventures, traditional cuisine and shopping for local trinkets, Kyzyl will leave you with unforgettable memories. Don’t hesitate to begin planning your trip to Kyzyl today!
