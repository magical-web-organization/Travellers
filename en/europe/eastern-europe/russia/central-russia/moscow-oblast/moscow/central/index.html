Introduction to Central

Central Moscow is the cultural and political center of Russia and one of the most important regions in Eastern Europe. With its rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning architecture, Central Moscow attracts millions of visitors every year. This comprehensive tour guide will provide you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Central Moscow.

Local Culture and Customs of Central

  • Learn some basic Russian phrases before traveling
  • Remove shoes upon entry to host’s or hotel’s apartment
  • Dress appropriately when visiting religious sites

Best Time to Visit Central

The best time to visit Central Moscow is from late May to early September when the weather is warm and pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Central

Do:

  • Be respectful to locals and their culture

Don’t:

  • Photograph locals without permission
  • Cut lines at any venues
  • Try to haggle at retail stores

Safety and Health

Safety in Central

Central Moscow is generally safe for tourists. However, be cautious of pickpockets and scam artists.

Health in Central

Check with your doctor for the recommended vaccinations before traveling to Moscow. Drink bottled water and avoid uncooked food.
