Introduction to Taganrog

Located in Rostov Oblast, southern Russia, Taganrog is a gem of a city boasting a rich history, unique culture, and stunning natural beauty. Founded in 1698, the city has witnessed many important historical events such as the Battle of Taganrog during the Crimean War. Today, it is a bustling port city and an important destination for history and culture lovers.

Local Culture and Customs of Taganrog

Taganrog is known for its historical significance and traditional Russian culture. Visitors are encouraged to dress modestly and be respectful of local customs, including the practice of removing shoes when entering someone’s home.

Best Time to Visit Taganrog

The best time to visit Taganrog is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and the city is bustling with outdoor events and festivals.

Do’s and Don’ts of Taganrog

Safety and Health

Safety in Taganrog

Taganrog is generally a safe city for visitors. However, it is important to be aware of your surroundings and take precautions to avoid petty crime and pickpocketing.

Health in Taganrog

Visitors to Taganrog are advised to ensure they have adequate medical insurance before traveling. It is also recommended to drink bottled water and take necessary precautions against insect bites and mosquito-borne illnesses.
