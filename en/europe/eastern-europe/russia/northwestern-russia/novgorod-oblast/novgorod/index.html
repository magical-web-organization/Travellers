Introduction to Novgorod

Novgorod, also known as Veliky Novgorod, is one of the oldest and most historic cities in Russia. Located in the Northwestern region of Russia, Novgorod is known for its rich culture and fascinating historical landmarks. The city offers plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures, scenic views, and cultural exploration. It is also home to some of the best examples of Medieval architecture in the country.

Local Culture and Customs of Novgorod

The people of Novgorod are proud of their traditions and history. Here are some customs and cultural practices to keep in mind:

  1. Dress modestly when visiting religious landmarks
  2. Remove shoes before entering a Russian home
  3. Greet people with a handshake
  4. Offer to share food and drink with locals
  5. Learn a few basic Russian phrases

Best Time to Visit Novgorod

The best time to visit Novgorod is during the summer months, between June and August. The weather is warm, and there are several outdoor activities to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Novgorod

Do:

  1. Dress appropriately for the weather
  2. Keep valuables secure
  3. Try the local cuisine
  4. Respect local customs and traditions
  5. Take part in outdoor activities

Don’t:

  1. Leave your passport and identification at the hotel
  2. Walk in unlit areas at night
  3. Speak loudly in public places
  4. Take photographs without asking for permission
  5. Touch or climb on historical landmarks

Safety and Health

Safety in Novgorod

Novgorod is generally a safe city, but visitors should exercise caution in crowded areas and tourist spots. Pickpocketing can occur, so it’s best to keep valuables secure. It’s also recommended to avoid walking in unlit areas at night.

Health in Novgorod

There are no specific health concerns in Novgorod. It’s recommended to have travel insurance and to carry necessary medication for any pre-existing conditions.

Getting to Novgorod: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Novgorod

The nearest airport to Novgorod is Pulkovo Airport in St. Petersburg. From there, visitors can take a taxi or train to Novgorod.

Bus Stations Near Novgorod

The Novgorod Bus Station is located in the city center and offers services to neighboring cities and towns.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel to Novgorod by train or car. Several companies provide rental cars and taxis within the city.

How to Get Around in Novgorod

The best way to get around Novgorod is by public transport, which includes buses and trolleybuses. Taxis are also readily available but can be expensive. Walking and cycling are recommended for shorter distances.
