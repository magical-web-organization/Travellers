Introduction to Staraya Russa

Located in the Novgorod Oblast of Northwestern Russia, Staraya Russa is a charming town known for its beautiful natural scenery and rich cultural heritage. The town is home to several museums, historical sites, and outdoor attractions that attract visitors all year round.

Established in the early 11th century, Staraya Russa became a prominent trade center in the medieval period and played an important role in the political and economic life of Russia. Today, the town is a popular destination for travelers who want to experience the authentic Russian culture and traditions.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore the top places to visit, things to do, and places to eat and stay in Staraya Russa.

List of Places to Visit in Staraya Russa

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Staraya Russa State Museum of Regional History and Art

The Staraya Russa State Museum of Regional History and Art is a must-visit destination for those who want to learn about the history and culture of the region. The museum features exhibits on the archaeology, history, and art of Staraya Russa and the surrounding area.

Tikhvin Cemetery

The Tikhvin Cemetery is a historic cemetery located in Staraya Russa that is the final resting place of several prominent Russian writers, artists, and political figures. The cemetery is a serene and peaceful place and is a popular attraction for tourists and locals alike.

Kazansky Cathedral

The Kazansky Cathedral is a beautiful Russian Orthodox cathedral located in the heart of Staraya Russa. The cathedral was built in the 19th century and features stunning architecture and intricate decorations.

Natural Attractions of Staraya Russa: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lake Ilmen

Lake Ilmen is a large freshwater lake located near Staraya Russa. The lake is a popular destination for boating, fishing, and other water activities.

Staraya Russa Mineral Springs

The Staraya Russa mineral springs are famous for their healing properties and are a popular destination for those seeking natural remedies. Visitors can bathe in the mineral waters and enjoy the rejuvenating effects of the springs.

Staraya Russa Park

The Staraya Russa Park is a beautiful park located in the center of the town. The park features beautiful gardens, walking paths, and stunning views of the surrounding area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Staraya Russa

Staraya Russa is surrounded by beautiful natural scenery and is a great destination for hiking, biking, and other outdoor activities. The following are some of the top hiking and biking routes in the area.

  • The Staraya Russa Reserve
  • The Valdai National Park
  • The Ilmen National Park

Best Things to Do in Staraya Russa: Activities and Tours

Guided Tours of Staraya Russa

A guided tour of Staraya Russa is a great way to learn about the history and culture of the town. Several local tour companies offer guided tours in English and Russian.

Horseback Riding

Horseback riding is a popular activity in Staraya Russa. Visitors can take guided tours of the surrounding area or rent horses to explore on their own.

Winter Sports

Staraya Russa is a great destination for winter sports such as skiing, snowboarding, and ice skating. The town has several ski resorts and ice skating rinks that are open during the winter months.

Food and Drink Scene of Staraya Russa

Must-Try Dishes

Staraya Russa is known for its delicious traditional Russian cuisine. Some of the must-try dishes include:

  • Borscht (beet soup)
  • Pelmeni (dumplings)
  • Blini (Russian pancakes)
  • Shchi (cabbage soup)

Local Beverages

The local beverages in Staraya Russa include:

  • Kvass (a fermented drink made from bread)
  • Birch Juice
  • Medovukha (a type of honey wine)

Local Culture and Customs of Staraya Russa

The culture and customs of Staraya Russa are steeped in tradition and are influenced by its fascinating history. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions such as removing shoes before entering a home, showing deference to elders, and dressing modestly in religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Staraya Russa

The best time to visit Staraya Russa is between May and September when the weather is mild and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy. The fall is also a beautiful time to visit when the leaves change colors, and the winter is perfect for winter sports.

Safety and Health

Safety in Staraya Russa

Staraya Russa is generally a safe place for travelers, with low levels of crime. However, visitors should always be aware of their surroundings and take precautions to protect themselves and their belongings.

Health in Staraya Russa

Visitors to Staraya Russa should be aware of the risk of tick-borne illnesses such as Lyme disease. It is recommended to wear long-sleeved shirts and pants and use insect repellent when hiking or spending time outdoors.
