Introduction to Komsomolsk-on-Amur

Komsomolsk-on-Amur is a city in Khabarovsk Krai, Russia. The city is located in the Russian Far East, at the confluence of Amur and Kuban rivers. Founded in 1932, Komsomolsk-on-Amur is an important industrial center in the region. The city boasts a number of cultural attractions, natural wonders, adventure activities, and excellent accommodation options.
