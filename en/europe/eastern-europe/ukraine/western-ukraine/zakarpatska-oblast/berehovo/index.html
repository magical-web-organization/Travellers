Introduction to Berehovo

Berehovo is a small and charming town located in Zakarpatska Oblast, in Western Ukraine. It is a popular destination for both locals and tourists due to its rich culture, beautiful natural surroundings, and historic landmarks. The town is situated in a valley surrounded by the Carpathian Mountains, which makes it ideal for hiking and outdoor activities.

List of Places to Visit in Berehovo

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Berehovo Castle: The castle dates back to the 14th century and is a great example of medieval architecture. Visitors can explore the interior and enjoy the beautiful views of the town from the castle’s towers.

  • Saint Elizabeth Church: Built in the early 20th century, the church is a prime example of art nouveau architecture. It features beautiful stained glass windows and intricate carvings.

  • The Synagogue: The synagogue was built in the 19th century and is one of the few Jewish landmarks in the town. It is a reminder of the town’s rich Jewish heritage.

Natural Attractions of Berehovo: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Berehovo Thermal Baths: The natural thermal baths are one of Berehovo’s main attractions. The water is believed to have medicinal properties and is said to relieve stress and fatigue. Visitors can relax in the warm waters and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

  • Berehove Vineyards: The town is home to several vineyards that produce high-quality wine. Visitors can take a guided tour of the vineyards and enjoy wine tasting sessions.

  • Kamyanka Waterfall: Located in the nearby village of Kamyanka, the waterfall is a popular spot for hikers and nature lovers. Visitors can enjoy the stunning views of the waterfall and surrounding forests.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Berehovo

  • Mount Svydovets: This mountain range is located in the Carpathian Mountains and offers some of the most stunning views of the region. Visitors can hike or bike along the trails and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.

  • Myhomolosky Waterfall: This waterfall is located in the nearby village of Myhomolosky. It is a popular spot for hikers and nature lovers due to its stunning views and peaceful surroundings.

Best Things to Do in Berehovo: Activities and Tours

  • Traditional Folk Shows: Berehovo is known for its rich folk culture, and visitors can enjoy traditional music and dance performances at various events throughout the year.

  • Wine Tasting Tours: Visitors can take a tour of the local vineyards and enjoy wine tasting sessions of the region’s finest wines.

Food and Drink Scene of Berehovo

Must-Try Dishes

  • Banosh: A traditional dish made from cornmeal and served with sour cream, cheese, and bacon.

  • Transcarpathian Varenyky: A type of boiled dumpling filled with potato, cheese, and sauerkraut.

Local Beverages

  • Local Wines: Berehovo is known for its high-quality wines. Visitors can enjoy wine tasting sessions at various vineyards throughout the town.

  • Palinka: A traditional Hungarian fruit brandy that is popular in the region.

Where to Stay in Berehovo: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Berehove Hotel: A centrally located hotel that offers comfortable rooms and modern amenities.

  • Vasvilla Guesthouse: A cozy guesthouse located in a quiet residential area. The rooms are spacious and offer beautiful views of the town.

What to Buy in Berehovo: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Traditional Handicrafts: Berehovo is known for its traditional crafts, including pottery, woodcarving, and embroidered textiles. Visitors can find these items at various local markets and shops.

  • Local Wines: Visitors can bring home bottles of the region’s finest wines as souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Berehovo

Berehovo has a rich cultural heritage that is shaped by its location on the border of Ukraine, Hungary, and Slovakia. Visitors may notice the town’s Hungarian and Slovakian influences in its food, music, and architecture.

Best Time to Visit Berehovo

The best time to visit Berehovo is during the summer months (June to September) when the weather is warm and sunny. However, visitors can also enjoy the town’s natural beauty during the fall season when the leaves change color and create a stunning backdrop for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Berehovo

Do:

  • Try the local wines and traditional dishes.

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Take pictures of or intrude on religious ceremonies.

  • Litter or damage the natural surroundings.

Safety and Health

Safety in Berehovo

Berehovo is a safe town to visit. However, visitors should be aware of their surroundings and take precautions against pickpocketing and theft.

Health in Berehovo

Visitors should ensure that they have appropriate travel health insurance and are up to date with routine vaccinations. It is also recommended to carry a mosquito repellent due to the presence of mosquitoes in the area.

Getting to Berehovo: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Berehovo

  • Lviv Danylo Halytskyi International Airport: Located approximately 243 km from Berehovo, the airport offers domestic and international flights.

  • Kosice International Airport: Located approximately 193 km from Berehovo, the airport offers domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Berehovo

  • Berehove Bus Station: Located in the town center, the station offers both domestic and international routes.

Other Options

  • Train: There is a train station in the nearby city of Mukachevo, which is approximately 34 km from Berehovo.

How to Get Around in Berehovo

The best way to get around Berehovo is on foot, as the town is relatively small. Alternatively, visitors can rent a bike or take a taxi to explore the outskirts of the town.

Where to Go Next from Berehovo: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Uzhgorod: Located approximately 60 km from Berehovo, Uzhgorod is the capital of Zakarpatska Oblast and offers a range of architectural and cultural attractions.

  • Lviv: Located approximately 243 km from Berehovo, Lviv is one of Ukraine’s most beautiful and historic cities. It offers a range of museums, architecture, and cultural activities.

Conclusion

Berehovo is a unique and beautiful town that offers a range of attractions for visitors to explore. From its rich culture and history to its stunning natural surroundings, Berehovo is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Ukraine.
