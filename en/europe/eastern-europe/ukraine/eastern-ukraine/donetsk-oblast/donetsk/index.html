Introduction to Donetsk

Located in the eastern part of Ukraine, Donetsk is a beautiful city with rich history and culture. It is the fifth-largest city in Ukraine and the capital of the Donetsk Oblast. Donetsk has seen many changes throughout history, from being a small village to a bustling city. Today, it is a modern city with a range of attractions for visitors to explore. In this travel guide, we will take you through the various places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more in Donetsk;

The best time to visit Donetsk is during the summer months from June to August when the weather is pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Donetsk

Do:

  • Learn some basic Ukrainian phrases
  • Dress smartly especially when going to religious sites
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages

Don’t:

  • Don’t smoke in public places
  • Don’t take pictures of the Donbass Arena without permission
  • Don’t talk politics with locals

Safety and Health

Safety in Donetsk

Donetsk can be considered safe for tourists. However, as with any city, it is important to take precautions while traveling.

Health in Donetsk

The healthcare system in Donetsk can be inadequate for foreigners. It is advisable to have travel insurance that covers medical treatment abroad.
