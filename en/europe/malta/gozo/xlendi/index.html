Introduction to Xlendi

Xlendi is a charming fishing village located on the western coast of Gozo, an island in the Maltese archipelago. Its scenic beauty, breathtaking views, deep blue waters, and hospitable locals attract tourists from all parts of the world. From historical landmarks to outdoor adventures to food and drink scene, Xlendi has everything which makes it a must-visit destination in Malta, Europe.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Ta’ Kola Windmill
  • Ggantija Temples
  • Victoria Citadel

Natural Attractions of Xlendi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Xlendi Bay
  • The Blue Hole
  • Wied il-Mielah Window

Food and Drink Scene of Xlendi

Xlendi has an excellent food and drink scene, with many restaurants, cafes, and bars serving local cuisine and drinks.

Must-Try Dishes

Some of the must-try dishes in Xlendi include:

  • Rabbit Stew
  • Pastizzi (Stuffed Pastry)
  • Ftira Għawdxija (Local Bread with Tuna, Tomatoes, and Onions)

Local Beverages

Some of the popular local beverages in Xlendi include:

  • Kinnie (Fizzy Orange Soft Drink)
  • Ġbejniet (Local Cheese)

Xlendi is a relatively safe destination for visitors. However, always be cautious of your surroundings, especially when traveling at night.

Getting to Xlendi: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Xlendi

The nearest airport to Xlendi is Malta International Airport (MLA), located in Luqa, Malta.

Bus Stations Near Xlendi

Xlendi is well-connected to the rest of Gozo via public buses.

Other Options

You can also reach Xlendi via ferry, which takes around 20 minutes to cover the distance between Malta and Gozo.

How to Get Around in Xlendi
