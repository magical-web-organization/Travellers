A Complete Travel Guide to Marijampolė, Lithuania

Marijampolė is a hidden gem in Lithuania, a small city located in the southwestern part of the country, known for its rich history, unique attractions, and beautiful surroundings. If you are planning a visit to Lithuania, make sure to include Marijampolė in your itinerary, and discover what this charming city has to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Marijampolė

Marijampolė boasts a number of culturally important attractions, natural wonders, and exciting activities. Here are the top things to see and do in the city:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Marijampolė Regional Museum - This museum houses a collection of local history and culture artifacts, including an exhibition on the life of local composer and conductor Juozas Naujalis.
  • Marijampolė Synagogue - The building dates back to the 19th century and is the only surviving synagogue in Marijampolė.
  • Saint Michael the Archangel Church - A neo-Gothic Roman Catholic church built in the 20th century.
  • Marijampolė Town Hall - This stunning neoclassical building dates back to the 19th century and is a symbol of the city’s prosperity.

Natural Attractions of Marijampolė: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Silainiai Forest - A beautiful forest located on the outskirts of the city, ideal for hiking, biking, and picnicking.
  • Svencionys Lake - A picturesque lake located near the city center, a perfect spot for fishing, kayaking, and other water sports.
  • Aukštumas Hill - This scenic hill offers stunning panoramic views of the city and its surroundings and is an ideal spot for hiking and nature walks.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Marijampolė

Marijampolė offers several hiking and biking routes that allow visitors to explore the city’s beautiful surroundings and enjoy the fresh air. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Silainiai Forest trail - A 5 km trail that runs through the beautiful forest and offers stunning views of the city and the surrounding countryside.
  • Svencionys Lake trail - A 3 km trail that runs along the shore of the lake and offers beautiful views of the water and wildlife.
  • Aukštumas Hill trail - A 3 km trail that leads to the top of Aukštumas Hill, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the city and the surrounding area.

Best Things to Do in Marijampolė: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are plenty of other activities to enjoy in Marijampolė, such as:

  • Kayaking on Svencionys Lake - Rent a kayak and explore the beautiful lake at your own pace, or join a guided tour.
  • Visiting the Tolerance Center - Learn about the history of the Jewish community in Marijampolė and Lithuania at this educational center.
  • Explore the city’s architecture - Take a walking tour of the city’s historic buildings and sights, including the Town Hall and Saint Michael the Archangel Church.

Week-Long Itinerary for Marijampolė

If you’re planning a longer trip to Marijampolė, here’s a suggested itinerary to make the most of your stay:

Day Activity
1 Arrive in Marijampolė and take a walking tour of the city center.
2 Hike the Silainiai Forest trail and have a picnic in the woods.
3 Visit the Marijampolė Regional Museum and see the exhibition on Juozas Naujalis, and then explore the city’s Jewish heritage at the Tolerance Center.
4 Take a kayaking tour on Svencionys Lake in the morning and visit Aukštumas Hill in the afternoon.
5 Visit the Marijampolė Synagogue and learn more about the Jewish history of the city.
6 Take a day trip to the nearby city of Kaunas and visit the iconic Ninth Fort and the Old Town.
7 Depart from Marijampolė with amazing memories of your visit.

Food and Drink Scene of Marijampolė

Marijampolė’s food scene is a mix of traditional Lithuanian cuisine and international flavors. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cepelinai - A national dish consisting of potato dumplings stuffed with meat, served with sour cream.
  • Blynai - Lithuanian pancakes often served with sour cream or fruit preserves.
  • Šaltibarščiai - A cold soup made with beets, buttermilk, and cucumber.

Local Beverages

  • Beer - Lithuanians love their beer, and Marijampolė has several breweries that produce quality beer.
  • Krupnikas - A traditional Lithuanian honey liqueur, often served as a digestif.
  • Kefyras - A sour milk drink that is a popular Lithuanian beverage.

Where to Stay in Marijampolė: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Marijampolė has plenty of accommodation options for every budget and preferences, from luxury hotels to cozy guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay in the city:

  • Atostogu Parkas Guesthouse - A charming guesthouse located in the scenic countryside near Marijampolė, offering cozy rooms and a peaceful atmosphere.
  • Hotel Sermutas - A modern hotel located in the city center, offering spacious rooms, a sauna, and a restaurant.
  • Pas Telmops Guesthouse - A budget-friendly guesthouse located in a quiet neighborhood, offering simple yet comfortable rooms.

What to Buy in Marijampolė: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs and local products to bring home, Marijampolė has several options. Here are some ideas:

  • Amber jewelry - Lithuania is famous for its amber, and you can find beautiful amber jewelry in Marijampolė.
  • Linen products - Lithuania is also known for its high-quality linen products, including tablecloths, towels, and clothing.
  • Local honey - Lithuania has a rich beekeeping tradition, and you can find delicious local honey in Marijampolė.

Local Culture and Customs of Marijampolė

Marijampolė has a rich cultural heritage, with a mix of Lithuanian and Jewish traditions. Visitors should respect local customs and be aware of the city’s history and cultural significance, especially when visiting sites such as the Marijampolė Synagogue and Tolerance Center.

Best Time to Visit Marijampolė

The best time to visit Marijampolė is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny, and outdoor activities are in full swing. However, for those who prefer a quieter and more budget-friendly experience, visiting in the off-season, from September to May, can be a great alternative.

Do’s and Don’ts of Marijampolė

Do:

  • Learn some basic phrases in Lithuanian, such as “labas” (hello) and “aciu” (thank you).
  • Respect local customs, especially at historical and cultural sites.
  • Tip at restaurants and cafes if you are satisfied with the service.

Don’t:

  • Smoke in public places, as it is illegal in Lithuania.
  • Be loud or disruptive in public spaces.
  • Leave your belongings unattended in busy areas.

Safety and Health

Marijampolė is generally a safe and healthy city to visit, but it’s always good to take precautions, such as:

Safety in Marijampolė

  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid walking alone at night.
  • Keep your valuables, such as money and passports, in a safe place.
  • Use licensed taxis or public transportation to get around the city.

Health in Marijampolė

  • Make sure to have updated vaccines before traveling to Lithuania.
  • Bring necessary medication and a first aid kit.
  • Drink bottled water or boil tap water before use.

Getting to Marijampolė: Transportation Options and Logistics

Marijampolė is located in the southwestern part of Lithuania and is easily accessible by several transportation options.

Airports Near Marijampolė

The nearest airport to Marijampolė is Kaunas Airport, located 78 km away. Another option is Vilnius Airport, located 155 km away.

Bus Stations Near Marijampolė

Marijampolė has a central bus station located near the city center, offering daily connections to major cities in Lithuania and neighboring countries.

Other Options

There are also trains that connect Marijampolė with other Lithuanian cities, such as Kaunas and Vilnius. Private car rentals are also available for those who prefer to drive on their own.

How to Get Around in Marijampolė

Marijampolė is a compact city that can easily be explored on foot, but for those who prefer other options, here are some transportation options:

  • Buses - Marijampolė has a public bus system that connects the city center with the suburbs and neighboring cities.
  • Taxis - Taxis are widely available in Marijampolė.
  • Bicycles - Bicycle rentals are available in some hotels and guesthouses for visitors who prefer to cycle around the city.

Where to Go Next from Marijampolė: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have more time to explore Lithuania, here are some nearby destinations and excursions from Marijampolė:

  • Kaunas - A charming city located 60 km from Marijampolė, known for its beautiful Old Town and cultural attractions.
  • Druskininkai - A spa resort town located 70 km from Marijampolė, famous for its mineral water springs and relaxation options.
  • Curonian Spit - A unique natural wonder located 130 km from Marijampolė, a narrow strip of sand dunes and pine forests that separates the Curonian Lagoon from the Baltic Sea.

Conclusion

Marijampolė is a hidden gem in Lithuania, offering a mix of historic and cultural attractions, beautiful scenery, and exciting outdoor activities. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can plan your next trip to Marijampolė and discover this charming city on your own.
