Introduction to Daugavpils

Daugavpils is a city located in Latgale, Latvia. It is the second-largest city in the country, with a population of approximately 90,000 residents. The city is located near the Daugava River and features a wide range of cultural and natural attractions.

List of Places to Visit in Daugavpils

Daugavpils has a rich history and culture, with plenty of attractions to keep visitors entertained. Here is a list of some of the must-visit places in Daugavpils:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Latgale Culture and History Museum: This museum features various exhibits that showcase the history and culture of the Latgale region.
  • Daugavpils Fortress: The fortress was built in the 19th century and is a unique example of military architecture. The fortress has several museums, including the Mark Rothko Art Center.
  • Mark Rothko Art Center: This art center displays the works of famous artist Mark Rothko, who was born in Daugavpils.

Natural Attractions of Daugavpils: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Dubrovin Park: This park is perfect for those who want to enjoy nature and scenic views. It offers various activities like boating, fishing, picnicking and taking a walk.
  • Bird Watching Tower: Located in the Nemunas Delta Regional Park, this tower offers bird lovers the opportunity to observe the various birds species and the delta views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Daugavpils

Daugavpils offers several biking and hiking trails through the beautiful nature and different parks. The Dubrovin park and the forests around Svente Manor offer some great hiking opportunities.

Best Things to Do in Daugavpils: Activities and Tours

Apart from the museums, forests, and parks, Daugavpils has many activities and tours for travelers. Below are some of the best things to do in Daugavpils:

  • Castle Dinner: Enjoy a unique dining experience in Daugavpils fortress where actors will present a medieval age-themed script that will add to the unique ambience of your evening.
  • Hot-air balloon ride: Discover the beauty of Daugavpils from above and experience the thrill of flying, a breathtaking view guaranteed.
  • Segway tour: A fun and informative way to see the city of Daugavpils, with a certified guide.

Week-Long Itinerary for Daugavpils

Here’s a week-long itinerary for Daugavpils.

  • Day 1: Visit Daugavpils Fortress and the Mark Rothko Art Center.
  • Day 2: Visit the Latgale Culture and History Museum and the Unity Square.
  • Day 3: Explore the Dubrovin Park and the Nemunas Delta Regional Park.
  • Day 4: Go on a Segway tour to explore the city center and nearby attractions.
  • Day 5: Take a hot-air balloon ride over Daugavpils and enjoy the beautiful views.
  • Day 6: Visit St. Boris and Gleb Cathedral and the Svente Manor.
  • Day 7: Visit the Daugavpils Zoo and the Bat Center.

Food and Drink Scene of Daugavpils

Latvian cuisine is becoming popular among travelers, and Daugavpils has some great places to offer without breaking your budget.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kastaņas zupa: This is a chestnut soup that is very popular in Latvian cuisine during the late fall season.
  • Karbonāde: A bone-in ribeye steak, usually grilled, and served with potatoes and salad.

Local Beverages

  • Midus: A locally made honey wine
  • Cido: A locally made cider that is made with different fruits, enjoyed as a refreshing drink, especially in the summer.

Where to Stay in Daugavpils: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Duets: Hotel Duets features comfortable and clean rooms with excellent service and breakfast.
  • Park Hotel Latgola: Park Hotel Latgola has a central location and offers various amenities like sauna, pool, fitness centre and a bar.

What to Buy in Daugavpils: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Daugavpils has a vibrant culture, and you can buy some souvenirs from the various shops and markets as reminders of your trip. Here are some typical items you should try:

  • Amber jewelry: Amber is a natural resin that is found in the Baltic region, and you can find various types of jewelry made with amber.
  • Wood carving: Wood carving is a traditional craft in Latvian culture, and you can buy various carved objects like spoons, bowls, and plates.

Local Culture and Customs of Daugavpils

Latvian culture is rich and unique. Some customs you may notice in Daugavpils include:

  • Latvian Song and Dance Festival: The festival is held every five years and is an essential cultural event for the people of Latvia. It’s a gathering of people who sing and dance together to celebrate the country’s heritage.
  • Jani Festival: Jani is the summer solstice, and on that day, people celebrate by eating, drinking, and dancing around a bonfire.

Best Time to Visit Daugavpils

The best time to visit Daugavpils is from May to September when the weather is mild to warm, and the forests and parks are green and lively. Besides, during summer, many cultural events like the Latvian Song and dance festival are held.

Do’s and Don’ts of Daugavpils

Do:

  • Try the local food and drinks, especially the honey wine
  • Visit the museums, parks and historical attractions
  • Buy some souvenirs made by locals
  • Respect the local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in public areas
  • Don’t drink tap water, always buy bottled water
  • Don’t take pictures of random people without asking their permission
  • Don’t forget to carry cash as some smaller shops may not accept credit cards.

Safety and Health

Safety in Daugavpils

Daugavpils is a safe city for tourists, but like with any other travel destination, you always need to watch out for pickpockets in crowded areas.

Health in Daugavpils

Daugavpils has several hospitals and medical centers, and emergency medical services are available 24/7.

Getting to Daugavpils: Transportation Options and Logistics

Daugavpils is well connected, with several transportation options available.

Airports Near Daugavpils

The closest airport to Daugavpils is the Riga International Airport, which is about a 200 km drive from the city.

Bus Stations Near Daugavpils

Daugavpils Central Bus Station is situated on 2 Aleksandra Street. You can take the bus from different cities in Latvia, Russia, and Belarus.

Other Options

You can also visit Daugavpils by train. Daugavpils train station connects the city with the capital Riga.

How to Get Around in Daugavpils

Daugavpils has an extensive network of buses and minibusses that connect different parts of the city. Taxis are also available 24/7. You can use the mobile app Bolt for taxi booking.

Where to Go Next from Daugavpils: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Daugavpils is situated in the Latgale region, and some nearby destinations and excursions include:

  • Rēzekne: Rēzekne is located about 50 km from Daugavpils and is known for its unique architecture and the Rēzekne castle ruins.
  • Aglona: Aglona is located about 35 km from Daugavpils and is famous for the Basilica of the Assumption of the Virgin Mary and Aglona Hill.

Conclusion

Daugavpils is a great travel destination in Latvia, featuring a unique blend of natural beauty, culture, history, and modern amenities. It is an ideal place for travelers looking for a tranquil and peaceful atmosphere away from the hustle and bustle of big cities. We hope this guide has helped you plan your trip to Daugavpils. Have a great time in Daugavpils!
