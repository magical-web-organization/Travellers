Introduction to Mustvee

Mustvee is a small town in East Estonia, a picturesque region of the country. Located on the eastern shore of Lake Peipsi, it is a popular summer destination for travelers looking to enjoy the beach and water activities. The town has a rich history and cultural heritage, with numerous landmarks and attractions to explore.

List of Places to Visit in Mustvee

Mustvee has a wide range of tourist attractions, from museums and historical sites to natural wonders and outdoor adventures. Here is a list of the must-visit places in Mustvee.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Mustvee Museum: The museum showcases the traditional lifestyle of Estonian fishermen and features a collection of artifacts such as fishing equipment, boats, and nets.

  2. Oskar Luts House Museum: The house museum honors the famous Estonian writer, Oskar Luts, who was born in Mustvee.

  3. Kalkuni Museum: This museum displays the history and culture of the Seto people, a minority group in Estonia, known for their unique folk costumes and traditions.

Natural Attractions of Mustvee: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Lake Peipsi: The largest and fourth deepest lake in Europe, Lake Peipsi, is a beautiful natural attraction with crystal-clear water, sandy beaches, and numerous water activities.

  2. Mustvee Beach: Mustvee Beach is the perfect spot to relax and enjoy the sun. The beach offers beautiful views of Lake Peipsi.

  3. Alatskivi Castle: About 20 km south of Mustvee, Alatskivi Castle is a stunning neo-gothic castle, located on the shores of Lake Peipsi. The castle offers beautiful views of the lake and is a popular destination for visitors.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mustvee

Mustvee offers plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities, such as hiking and biking. Here are some of the best hiking/biking routes in Mustvee.

  1. Mustvee-Pala hiking trail: This trail takes you through scenic forests and offers breathtaking views of Lake Peipsi.

  2. Alatskivi Castle Biking Trail: This 30-km trail takes you through beautiful countryside, past the historic Alatskivi Castle and through charming villages.

  3. Kiidi Hiking Trail: This trail stretches over 7 km and takes you through beautiful natural surroundings, including wetlands, forests, and meadows.

Best Things to Do in Mustvee: Activities and Tours

Mustvee offers many fun activities and tours for all ages. Here are some of the top things to do in Mustvee.

  1. Boating: Rent a boat and explore the scenic Lake Peipsi.

  2. Fishing: Take a fishing trip and try your hand at catching some of the lake’s famous fish species such as pike and bream.

  3. Seto Craftsmanship Tour: Take a guided tour and discover the unique Seto culture and craft traditions.

Week-Long Itinerary for Mustvee

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Mustvee, here’s an itinerary that will help you make the most of your stay.

Day 1: Arrival and check-in at your hotel

Day 2: Visit Kalkuni Museum and Mustvee Museum

Day 3: Explore the beautiful Alatskivi Castle

Day 4: Take a boating trip on Lake Peipsi

Day 5: Hike the Mustvee-Pala hiking trail

Day 6: Take a Seto Craftsmanship Tour

Day 7: Relax on the beach and take in the spectacular views of Lake Peipsi.

Food and Drink Scene of Mustvee

Estonian cuisine is unique and hearty, and Mustvee has plenty of restaurants and cafes where you can try the local cuisine.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Peipsi Pike Soup: A hearty soup made with freshwater fish, vegetables, and potatoes.

  2. Pork Sirloin in Mushroom Sauce: Pork Sirloin served with creamy mushroom sauce and boiled potatoes is a must-try Estonian dish.

  3. Kama Cream: A traditional sweet dish made with flour, yogurt, and berries.

Local Beverages

  1. Vana Tallinn: A traditional Estonian liqueur, made with a blend of rum, vanilla, and citrus flavors.

  2. Saaremaa Island Beer: A locally crafted beer, made on the small island of Saaremaa.

  3. Kase Kama: A milk-like drink made with traditional Estonian ingredients like kama, sour cream, and birch juice.

Where to Stay in Mustvee: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are many accommodation options in and around Mustvee. Here are some of the best hotels, hostels, and guesthouses in Mustvee.

  1. Hotel Kantri: Located in the heart of Mustvee, this hotel offers comfortable rooms and great views of Lake Peipsi.

  2. Mustvee Guesthouse: A cozy guesthouse located just a few minutes’ walk from the beach.

  3. Metsavana Maja: A peaceful and rustic guesthouse located in the heart of Alatskivi countryside.

What to Buy in Mustvee: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  2. Handmade Craft Items: Mustvee is known for its traditional handicrafts such as pottery, woodcarving, and embroidery.

Local Culture and Customs of Mustvee

Best Time to Visit Mustvee

Where to Go Next from Mustvee: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
