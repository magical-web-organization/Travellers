Introduction to San Agustín de Valle Fértil

San Agustín de Valle Fértil is a picturesque town located in the San Juan province of Argentina, which boasts a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. The town earned its name from its patron saint, Saint Augustine, and the fertile valley that surrounds it. San Agustín de Valle Fértil is located in the Cuyo region of Argentina, known for its warm and arid climate, vast vineyards, and fruit orchards.

The town is surrounded by rugged mountains, deep valleys, and winding rivers, making it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts. In addition, it is home to several historical landmarks and museums that display the unique cultural and architectural heritage of the region.

List of Places to Visit in San Agustín de Valle Fértil

Visitors to San Agustín de Valle Fértil will be mesmerized by the town’s wealth of natural and cultural attractions. Here are the top places to visit in San Agustín de Valle Fértil:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The town offers a range of cultural landmarks and museums that showcase its unique architectural heritage. You won’t want to miss the following:

  • Iglesia San Agustín: The oldest church in San Agustín de Valle Fertil, Iglesia San Agustín dates back to the 18th century, Spanish-colonial style architecture.
  • Museo Alfonso Lafarge: This museum displays more than 2000 archaeological objects related to the indigenous population of the region, famous for its displays of zonda replicas.
  • Casa de la Cultura: Home to the town’s library and amphitheater, the Casa de la Cultura is the hub for cultural events and performances.

Natural Attractions of San Agustín de Valle Fértil: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

San Agustín de Valle Fértil offers visitors an incomparable experience of natural beauty and outdoor adventures. The vast array of natural attractions in and around the town is sure to keep travelers occupied. Check out these natural sites:

  • Valle de la Luna: Meaning Valley of the Moon in Spanish, this stunning natural reserve boasts stunning rock formations, dunes, and desert terrain, making it a perfect option for a day hiking adventure.
  • Parque Nacional San Guillermo: Located to the south of San Agustin, National Park San Guillermo is home to Andean wildlife, as well as ancient rock paintings and geological formations.
  • Laguna del Diamante: Laguna del Diamante is an idyllic high-altitude lake that lies at the foot of a stratovolcano and is surrounded by snow-capped mountains.

Hiking/Biking Routes of San Agustín de Valle Fértil

San Agustín de Valle Fértil is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, featuring a plethora of hiking and biking routes. Here are a few popular options:

  • Cerro Overo: This stunning volcanic mountain is located north of the town and offers a challenging and scenic hike.
  • Río Valle Fértil: Follow the gentle waters of the Rio Valle Fértil as they lead you through scenic valleys and past the town’s ancient rock formations.
  • Piedra Pintada: A hike to Piedra Pintada is a must-do for anyone interested in the region’s ancient history and culture. The hike takes you past a boulder covered in prehistoric rock art.

Best Things to Do in San Agustín de Valle Fértil: Activities and Tours

There is no shortage of things to do and see in San Agustín de Valle Fértil. Below are some of the best activities and tours available:

  • Horseback Riding Tours: choose between a range of horse-back tours takes you thorough the valleys, waterfalls, and vineyards of the valley.
  • Hot Springs: Relax in one of the many natural hot springs found in and around the town. The most popular hot springs are Termas de Fiambalá.
  • Stargazing: The Cuyo region is well-known for its clear night skies, and there are ample opportunities for stargazing trips in the area.

Food and Drink Scene of San Agustín de Valle Fértil

San Agustín de Valle Fértil is a gastronomic delight with an emerging food and drink scene. The town’s vineyards and fruit orchards supply the locals and visitors with a range of fresh ingredients. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Empanadas: a traditional meat pie, empanadas in this region are typically stuffed with meats and vegetables
  • Locro: a hearty stew made with corn, meat, and white beans, usually served with toasted corn.
  • Humitas: Another traditional dish made from fresh corn, usually served wrapped in corn husks

Local Beverages

  • Wine: San Agustín de Valle Fértil is located within the San Juan wine region, producing dry, red and Malbec wines, among others.
  • Cerveza artesanal: handcrafted beer, made with local ingredients comes from independent breweries

Local Culture and Customs of San Agustín de Valle Fértil

Residents of San Agustín de Valle Fértil are typically warm and welcoming, hospitable, and proud of their culture and traditions. Some customary customs are:

Best Time to Visit San Agustín de Valle Fértil

The best time to visit San Agustín de Valle Fértil is during the autumn and spring months, from March to May and September to November. You will enjoy the mild temperatures and nature is at its most beautiful during these months.

Do’s and Don’ts of San Agustín de Valle Fértil

Here are some recommendations of do’s and don’ts while traveling in San Agustín de Valle Fértil:

Do:

  • Respect the culture: San Agustín de Valle Fértil has a unique heritage, leaning towards natural environment and colonial history.
  • Carry the necessary trekking gear: bring sunscreen, windbreakers and other essentials items. The sun can be strong, and the unpredictability of weather can change quickly.
  • Try the local wine: San Juan is known for its wine, and you can’t leave without trying local wine.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing: A conservative town, dress modestly when visiting churches, museums and galleries.
  • Litter: as with any natural reserve, take note you cannot litter or leave your trash.

San Agustín de Valle Fértil and the surrounding area are generally safe in terms of health issues. However, due to the region’s semi-arid climate, travelers should carry plenty of water and protect themselves from the sun’s rays.

Getting to San Agustín de Valle Fértil: Transportation Options and Logistics

There are various options for getting to San Agustín de Valle Fértil:

Airports Near San Agustín de Valle Fértil

The nearest airports are Domingo Faustino Sarmiento Airport (SJU) and La Rioja Airport

Bus Stations Near San Agustín de Valle Fértil

San Agustín de Valle Fértil does not have its bus station but has connections with San Juan Bus Station. You can find numerous reputable bus companies that serve the town.

Other Options

If you prefer to take a private vehicle, you will find car rental options at major cities’ airports.

How to Get Around in San Agustín de Valle Fértil

While San Agustín de Valle Fértil is small enough to explore on foot, you will need to hire a vehicle if you want to explore the surrounding natural attractions. A bike or ATV rental can be a great option for short-day trips.
