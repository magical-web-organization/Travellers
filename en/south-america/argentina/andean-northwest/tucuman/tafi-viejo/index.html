Introduction to Tafi Viejo

Tafi Viejo is a charming town located in Tucumán, in Andean Northwest, Argentina. Surrounded by lush greenery and snow-capped mountains, Tafi Viejo has its own unique charm that sets it apart from other destinations. Known for its historic landmarks, fascinating culture, and magnificent scenery, Tafi Viejo has become increasingly popular among travelers worldwide.

This comprehensive tour guide will provide you with all the information you need to know before embarking on your trip to Tafi Viejo.

List of Places to Visit in Tafi Viejo

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The historic Nuestra Señora del Rosario church is a must-visit for history and architecture buffs. Built in the 18th century, it boasts breathtaking paintings and sculptures.

  • Museo Miguel Lillo is another highlight of Tafi Viejo. It is a museum of natural history that showcases an extensive collection of fossils and minerals.

  • The Museo Jesuitico is a perfect spot to learn about the history of the Jesuit missionaries in Argentina. The museum exhibits some of the original artifacts and documents from the era.

  • Casa de la Cultura showcases local art and is a great place to get to know the cultural scene of Tafi Viejo.

Natural Attractions of Tafi Viejo: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Reserva Natural Alpina y Centro de Visitantes is a perfect spot for nature lovers with its variety of wildlife and stunning mountainous landscapes.

  • The Tafi del Valle is a spectacular high-altitude valley with magnificent views of the Cordillera de los Andes mountain range. It is perfect for hiking and enjoying the scenery.

  • The Quebrada de Los Sosa is another fascinating natural attraction. It is a series of rocky valleys and canyons with numerous trekking trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tafi Viejo

  • The trails of the Reserva Natural Alpina y Centro de Visitantes offer four different trails with varying degrees of difficulty. These routes provide excellent opportunities for hiking and trekking enthusiasts.

  • The Tafi del Valle is a perfect spot to explore the region by mountain biking, allowing you to get close to nature while enjoying the scenic mountainous landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Tafi Viejo: Activities and Tours

  • La Santísima Concepción de Tafí del Valle is a church located in the picturesque town of Tafi del Valle, which offers a scenic route.

  • Visit the artisanal handicraft market famous for its crafts, such as ceramics, woven textiles, and woodcarvings.

  • The Tafí del Valle tour provides an opportunity to experience the stunning landscapes, culture, and delicious local cuisine.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tafi Viejo

Food and Drink Scene of Tafi Viejo

Must-Try Dishes

  • Locro is a hearty corn stew made with corn, beans, meat, and vegetables and is a typical dish of the region.

  • Empanadas are a traditional Argentine food that come in a variety of flavors, such as meat, cheese, or spinach.

  • Locally brewed artisanal beers that offer a unique twist to classic brews and provide an excellent way to relax.

Local Beverages

  • Mate is a traditional South American hot drink, and it’s a must try.

Where to Stay in Tafi Viejo: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hosteria El Remanso is a hotel that offers comfortable rooms and other amenities such as a pool and free Wi-Fi.

  • Cerro Rubio Lodge offers a mix of rustic and modern accommodation options.

  • Cabañas de Anta offers cozy cabins surrounded by luscious greenery.

What to Buy in Tafi Viejo: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handicrafts such as ceramics, woven textiles, and woodcarvings are perfect souvenirs.

  • Local wines and locally brewed artisanal beer are perfect gifts for your loved ones.

Local Culture and Customs of Tafi Viejo

  • The Tafí culture is a mix of Spanish and Native American cultures, providing a unique blend of traditions.

  • It’s common courtesy to greet locals with a warm “hola” or “buenos dias”.

  • It’s customary to dress modestly when entering churches.

Best Time to Visit Tafi Viejo

The best time to visit Tafi Viejo is during the spring (September to November) when the weather is pleasant and the hills are lush with vegetation.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tafi Viejo

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and traditions.

  • Explore the natural attractions of the region.

  • Try the local dishes and drinks.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in natural areas.

  • Don’t be disrespectful towards locals.

  • Don’t forget to carry sunscreen and water bottles.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tafi Viejo

Tafi Viejo is a safe place to visit. However, it is always advisable to take appropriate precautions and keep an eye on your belongings while travelling.

Health in Tafi Viejo

Travelers should take necessary precautions, such as carrying insect repellants and wearing protective clothing to avoid mosquito bites.

Getting to Tafi Viejo: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tafi Viejo

The closest airport, Teniente General Benjamín Matienzo International Airport, is 50km from Tafi Viejo, with daily flights to Buenos Aires.

Bus Stations Near Tafi Viejo

The Tafi Viejo bus station is around 15km from the city center and offers several routes to surrounding destinations.

Other Options

Car rental services are available at the airport and the city center for those who prefer driving.

How to Get Around in Tafi Viejo

Local buses are the primary means of transportation for traveling within Tafi Viejo, while taxis are also readily available.

Where to Go Next from Tafi Viejo: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Tucumán is one of the largest cities in the region and is known for its rich history and architecture, located only 15km from Tafi Viejo.

  • Salta is an attraction-filled city a 3-hour car ride from Tafi Viejo, where you can explore history, culture, and wild Andes mountains.

Conclusion

Tafi Viejo is a remarkable destination for travelers seeking a unique cultural and natural experience. With its stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, delicious cuisine, and fascinating history, it is a perfect mix of adventure and relaxation. Pack your bags and get ready to indulge in what Tafi Viejo has to offer.
