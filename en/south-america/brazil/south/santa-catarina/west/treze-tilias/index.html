Introduction to Treze Tílias

Treze Tílias is a beautiful town located in the west region of Santa Catarina, Brazil. With a population of approximately 7,500, the town was founded in 1933 by Austrian immigrants and still proudly holds on to its Austrian roots.

Treze Tílias is surrounded by natural beauty, making it an excellent spot for outdoor enthusiasts. In this guide, we will explore the different places to visit, things to do, and where to stay in Treze Tílias.

List of Places to Visit in Treze Tílias

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Austrian Heritage Museum: A museum nestled in the heart of the town that displays the town’s Austrian heritage and culture.
  • Cultural Center: A modern facility built in 2002 that offers various cultural activities such as art exhibits, dance performances, and concerts.
  • Historical Municipal Park: A beautiful park that contains historical buildings that offer a glimpse of the town’s past.

Natural Attractions of Treze Tílias: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Fortaleza Canyon: A breathtaking natural formation located approximately 60 km southwest of Treze Tílias. The canyon is 7.5 km long, 900 meters deep, and is located within Parque Nacional da Serra Geral.
  • Cascata do Maratá: A scenic waterfall located approximately 25 km from Treze Tílias, with a height of 120 meters and nestled amid beautiful natural surroundings.
  • Vale Sonho Verde: A family-friendly park with hiking trails, a small lake, playgrounds, and green areas ideal for a picnic.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Treze Tílias

  • Caminho das Borboletas: A 12 km hiking trail that starts from Vale Sonho Verde. The trail offers incredible views of the surrounding countryside and nature.
  • Rota Rural: An 18 km biking route that starts from the Historical Municipal Park and offers a glimpse into the rural life of the area.

Best Things to Do in Treze Tílias: Activities and Tours

  • Austrian Dancing Classes: Attend an Austrian dance class and learn traditional dances like waltz, polka, and schuplattler.
  • Horseback Riding: Enjoy horse riding through the countryside or even along the waterfall trail.
  • Wine Tasting: Visit the local wineries and try some excellent Brazilian wine.

Week-Long Itinerary for Treze Tílias

Day Activity
Day 1 Get to know the town, visit the Austrian Heritage Museum and the Historical Settlement

Food and Drink Scene of Treze Tílias

Must-Try Dishes

  • Knödel: A savory dumpling made from bread and potato dough served with various fillings.
  • Strudel: A stuffed pastry filled with apple or cheese and baked until golden brown.
  • Kaiserschmarrn: A sweet Austrian dish made of shredded pancake, raisins, and served with fruit compote.

Local Beverages

  • Brazilian Wine: Produced locally in the region, the delicious wines are a must-try.
  • Cerveja Artesanal (Craft beer): With many local breweries, the beer scene in Treze Tílias is thriving.

Where to Stay in Treze Tílias: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Urubici Eco Hostel: A charming hostel located amid the natural beauty of Treze Tílias.
  • Hotel Schneider: A beautiful family-run hotel with comfortable rooms and contemporary amenities.
  • Pousada Belluno: A cozy guesthouse with an outdoor swimming pool and a great atmosphere.

What to Buy in Treze Tílias: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Local handicrafts: Buy traditional paintings, clothes, and other handmade items from the local artisans.
  • Cachaça: A distilled local spirit made from sugarcane, perfect for those who like to take home local drinks and beverages.
  • Cheese: The region is famous for producing delicious artisanal cheese ideal for foodies.

Local Culture and Customs of Treze Tílias

Treze Tílias proudly displays its Austrian roots, so be prepared to see many residents wearing traditional Austrian clothes, celebrating Austrian festivals, and speaking German. Try to learn a few German phrases and join in on the fun!

Best Time to Visit Treze Tílias

The best time to visit Treze Tílias is between November and March when the weather is warm and sunny.

Do’s and Don’ts of Treze Tílias

Do:

  • Try the traditional Austrian dishes and local Brazilian wine.
  • Attend local festivals and events.
  • Learn a few German phrases and join in on the fun.

Don’t:

  • Underestimate the power of the sun, pack sunscreen and a hat.
  • Ignore local customs and traditions.
  • Litter and damage the environment. The town takes pride in its natural beauty and eco-friendliness.

Safety and Health

Safety in Treze Tílias

Treze Tílias is a generally safe place, but it is essential to practice basic safety rules. Keep your valuables in a hotel safe, avoid walking alone at night, and use a registered taxi service if needed.

Health in Treze Tílias

Treze Tílias has a well-equipped and modern hospital to help with any medical issue that may arise. However, it is essential to have health insurance when traveling to Brazil.

Getting to Treze Tílias: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Treze Tílias

The nearest airport is Hercílio Luz International Airport in Florianópolis, located approximately 302 km from Treze Tílias.

Bus Stations Near Treze Tílias

The closest bus station is the Joaçaba Bus Terminal, located approximately 22 km from Treze Tílias.

Other Options

Renting a car is a great option for exploring the surrounding natural areas comfortably.

How to Get Around in Treze Tílias

Walking is an excellent option to explore the town. Taxis are also available, but it is advisable to negotiate and confirm the fare before boarding. Bicycle rentals are available for those who want to explore the surrounding countryside on two wheels.

Where to Go Next from Treze Tílias: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Chapecó: A bustling town located approximately 150 km from Treze Tílias, home to some of the best universities in Brazil.
  • Joaçaba: A charming city located 22 km from Treze Tílias, known for its beautiful parks and river activities.

Conclusion

Treze Tílias is a small piece of Austria in Brazil, full of charm, tradition, and natural beauty. Explore the town, taste the local cuisine, and take in the unique culture and customs. Plan your trip to this beautiful town and experience an unforgettable adventure.
