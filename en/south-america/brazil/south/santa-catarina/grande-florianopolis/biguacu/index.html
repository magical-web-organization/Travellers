Introduction to Biguaçu

Located in Grande Florianópolis in the state of Santa Catarina, Biguaçu is a charming city in southern Brazil. Known for its beautiful landscapes, outdoor adventures, and cultural attractions, Biguaçu is a must-visit destination for those looking to experience the best of Brazil.

List of Places to Visit in Biguaçu

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Biguaçu has a rich history, and there are several museums and historical sites worth visiting. Some of the top attractions include:

  • Museu Histórico de Biguaçu: This museum showcases the cultural and historical heritage of Biguaçu and has an extensive collection of art and artifacts.

  • Casa da Cultura de Biguaçu: This cultural center hosts various exhibitions and events throughout the year, showcasing the history and traditions of the region.

  • Igreja Matriz de São João Batista: This beautiful church was built in the 18th century and is a wonderful example of colonial architecture.

Natural Attractions of Biguaçu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Biguaçu is known for its stunning natural beauty, and there are several outdoor adventures and scenic views to be enjoyed. Here are a few places to visit:

  • Parque Estadual da Serra do Tabuleiro: This park is home to a vast area of Atlantic Forest and is perfect for hiking and birdwatching.

  • Praia de São Miguel: This beach is tranquil and pristine, offering a relaxing atmosphere perfect for swimming and sunbathing.

  • Morro da Cruz: This lookout point offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city and surrounding landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Biguaçu

For outdoor enthusiasts, Biguaçu has many trails and routes that offer fantastic hikes and bike rides with stunning views. Here are some highlights:

  • Trilha do Morro do Macaco: This trail leads to the top of Morro do Macaco, offering incredible views of the surrounding lands.

  • Trilha do Papagaio: This trail leads through the tropical atmosphere of Parque Estadual da Serra do Tabuleiro and is perfect for birdwatching enthusiasts.

  • Ciclovia Beira-Mar: A scenic cycle path that runs along the coast and offers a pleasant ride with sea views.

Best Things to Do in Biguaçu: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of fun activities and tours to take in Biguaçu. Here is a list of popular options:

  • Surfing: With some of the most beautiful and consistent waves in Brazil, Biguaçu’s beaches offer great surfing opportunities.

  • Boat tours: Book a boat ride to explore the coast of Biguaçu, which offers stunning views of rugged cliffs and calm waters.

  • Paragliding: Go paragliding to explore the city from a new perspective and take in the stunning views from the sky.

Week-Long Itinerary for Biguaçu

Here’s a sample itinerary for those wanting to spend a week in Biguaçu:

Day Plan
Day 1 Hiking in Parque Estadual da Serra do Tabuleiro

Food and Drink Scene of Biguaçu

Must-Try Dishes

With a mix of Portuguese, Italian, and German cuisine influenced by the region’s history, visitors to Biguaçu will want to sample these dishes:

  • Churrasco: Brazil’s famous barbecue-style meat dish, typically served with rice and beans.

  • Pão de Queijo: Delicious savory cheese balls made with tapioca flour.

  • Pastel de camarão: Fried empanadas filled with shrimp and usually served with salsa.

Local Beverages

Don’t miss out on trying some of Biguaçu’s local beverages:

  • Caipirinha: Brazil’s national drink made with cachaça, lime, and sugar.

  • Cerveja artesanal: Locally brewed craft beer, a perfect accompaniment to the region’s culinary offerings.

  • Mate: A traditional herbal tea that is popular in the region.

Where to Stay in Biguaçu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Here are some of the best places to stay in Biguaçu:

  • Pousada da Vigia: A charming guesthouse located on a hilltop offering panoramic views of the city and sea.

  • Hostel Sereia do Mar: A cozy hostel located just a short walk from the beach and offering budget-friendly accommodation.

  • Slaviero Essential Palhoça Viacatarina: A modern hotel located a short drive from the beach, offering comfortable and luxurious accommodation.

What to Buy in Biguaçu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don’t leave Biguaçu without buying some souvenirs and local products to bring home. Here are some top picks:

  • Brazilian swimwear: Brazil is famous for its swimwear, and the local shops in Biguaçu offer many beautiful options.

  • Handicrafts: Biguaçu has a thriving handicraft community, and you can find many beautiful handmade items at local markets.

  • Bottles of local cachaça: Take home a bottle of this traditional Brazilian spirit as a unique souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Biguaçu

The most common language spoken in Biguaçu is Portuguese, and the local currency is the Brazilian real. Brazilians are known for their outgoing and friendly nature, so don’t be surprised if you’re greeted with a warm smile and handshake. It’s also worth noting that tipping is customary in Brazil, usually 10% of the total bill in restaurants and cafes.

Best Time to Visit Biguaçu

The best time to visit Biguaçu is from April to November, which is the dry season. During this time, the weather is sunny and pleasant, and there is less chance of rain.

Do’s and Don’ts of Biguaçu

Do:

  • Learn a few basic Portuguese phrases before your trip.

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

  • Take advantage of the outdoor adventures and scenic views.

Don’t:

  • Wear expensive jewelry or carry large sums of cash.

  • Leave valuables unattended on the beach.

  • Rely solely on credit cards; it’s always a good idea to carry some cash.

Safety and Health

Safety in Biguaçu

Biguaçu is considered a relatively safe city, but it’s always best to take the usual precautions to avoid any type of crime. Don’t flaunt valuables, stay in well-lit areas at night, and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Biguaçu

It’s always a good idea to check with your doctor before traveling to Brazil. Visitors should also be up to date on routine vaccinations, including measles, mumps, rubella, tetanus, and hepatitis A and B. Take precautions against mosquito-borne diseases like dengue fever and Zika, including using insect repellent and wearing protective clothing.

Getting to Biguaçu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Biguaçu

The closest airports to Biguaçu are:

  • Florianópolis-Hercilio Luz International Airport (FLN), around 16 km.

Bus Stations Near Biguaçu

  • Terminal Rodoviário Rita Maria, in the centre of Florianópolis, is about 10 km away from Biguaçu.

  • Interpraias Terminal, close to Porto Belo, which is about 32 km away.

Other Options

Taxis and rental cars are also available for transport within and around Biguaçu.

How to Get Around in Biguaçu

The best way to get around in Biguaçu is by car, motorcycle, or bike. Taxis are also available, as well as public transportation options like buses.

Where to Go Next from Biguaçu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are many nearby destinations worth visiting from Biguaçu, including:

  • Florianópolis: The capital of Santa Catarina, Florianópolis is known for its beautiful beaches, lively nightlife, and cultural events.

  • Garopaba: A charming coastal town known for its pristine beaches and charming atmosphere.

  • Bombinhas: A popular destination for snorkeling and diving enthusiasts, Bombinhas offers clear water and abundant marine life.

Conclusion

Biguaçu is a remarkable destination in Brazil, offering beautiful natural landscapes, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures. Don’t miss out on this hidden gem of Santa Catarina!
