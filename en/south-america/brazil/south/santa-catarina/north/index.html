Food and Drink Scene of North

North boasts a rich culinary heritage, with dishes and drinks unique to the region. Here are a few must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Barreado: a hearty beef stew slow-cooked in a clay pot and served with cassava flour and rice
  • Pastel de Pinhão: a savory pastry filled with pinhão (pine nuts), cheese, beef, or chicken
  • Churrasco: a Brazilian-style barbecue food that features beef, pork, chicken, or fish skewer grilled over an open flame

Local Beverages

  • Cachaça: a distilled sugarcane liquor that is popular all over Brazil
  • Vinho de altitude: a variety of wine produced in the high-altitude vineyards of North

Where to Stay in North: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

North has several options for accommodation, from budget hostels to luxurious resorts. Here are a few recommendations:

  • Pousada Fazenda Verde: a charming ranch-style guesthouse with horse riding and hiking activities
  • Imigrantes Hotel: a modern and comfortable hotel located in Sao Bento do Sul
  • Hotel Solarium: a luxurious hotel with an outdoor pool, sauna, and spa facilities.

What to Buy in North: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

North is known for its unique handicrafts, wines, and other local products. Here are a few souvenirs to buy and bring back from North:

  • Pinhão: roasted pine nuts to snack on or cook with
  • Handmade hats, bags, and other accessories made of straw
  • Hand-painted ceramic items and pottery from the local artisans

Local Culture and Customs of North

North has a rich cultural heritage that is unique to the region. Here are a few customs and traditions that you should know about:

  • Cow herding and agriculture activities are an essential part of the culture and history of North
  • The native Guarani tribe has its own language, culture, and customs that are still prevalent in the region.
  • The Festa do Pinhão is a popular festival where locals celebrate the harvest of the pinhão (pine nuts).

Best Time to Visit North

The best time to visit North is during the dry season, which lasts from May to September. The weather during these months is mild and pleasant, and you can indulge in outdoor activities to your heart’s content.

Do’s and Don’ts of North

Do:

  • Respect the cultural heritage and traditions of the locals.
  • Try the local cuisine and delicacies.
  • Explore the natural and cultural attractions at your own pace and time.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural attractions or archaeological sites.
  • Disrespect the local culture and customs.
  • Visit any restricted or closed sites without prior permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in North

North is generally a safe destination, but it’s always important to be aware of your surroundings and take adequate precautions.

Health in North

It’s important to take precautions against mosquito bites, as there have been cases of dengue fever and other mosquito-borne illnesses in the region. It’s also advisable to drink bottled water to avoid any gastrointestinal infections.

Getting to North: Transportation Options and Logistics

North is well-connected to other major cities in Brazil and can be reached via multiple transportation options.

Airports Near North

  • Joinville-Lauro Carneiro de Loyola Airport
  • Navegantes-Ministro Victor Konder International Airport
  • Afonso Pena International Airport

Bus Stations Near North

  • Canoinhas Bus Station
  • São Bento do Sul Bus Station
  • Mafra Bus Station

Other Options

  • Private car or taxi: Traveling by car or taxi is a convenient option to explore North comfortably at your own time.
  • Renting a car: Visitors can rent cars from several car rental companies at Joinville-Lauro Carneiro de Loyola airport.

How to Get Around in North

North has an efficient bus service that connects all the major attractions and cities in the region. Taxis and private cars are also available for hire.

Where to Go Next from North: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

North is surrounded by several beautiful destinations, making it a perfect base for further exploration. Here are a few nearby destinations worth exploring:

  • Curitiba: a charming city known for its unique architecture and lush green parks
  • Florianopolis: a popular coastal city with stunning beaches and vibrant nightlife
  • Joinville: the largest city in Santa Catarina known for its Germanic immigration heritage, and cultural attractions.

Conclusion

North is a unique destination that offers the perfect combination of natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and adventure activities. Plan a visit to this picturesque region to create unforgettable memories.
