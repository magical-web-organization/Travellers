Introduction to São Luiz do Paraitinga

São Luiz do Paraitinga is a charming colonial town that is situated in North Coast, São Paulo state, Southeast Brazil, South America. It has a rich cultural and historical background that dates back to the 18th century. The American Architectural Society classified the town as the “National Heritage” which emphasizes the preservation efforts put into retaining its cultural heritage. São Luiz do Paraitinga was also declared as the “Capital of Carnival” since the town is known for its lively carnival festivities.
