Introduction to São José do Rio Preto

São José do Rio Preto is a vibrant city located in the northwestern region of São Paulo, Brazil. The city is famous for its stunning natural beauty, vibrant culture, and rich history. It is a popular tourist destination and a hub for industrial and agricultural activities. São José do Rio Preto has a tropical climate with an average temperature of 24°C, making it a perfect destination to visit all year round. This comprehensive travel guide provides an overview of various attractions, natural wonders, and local customs to help you plan your vacation in São José do Rio Preto.

Safety and Health

Safety in São José do Rio Preto

São José do Rio Preto is generally safe for tourists to visit. However, it is essential to take necessary precautions and remain vigilant, particularly when travelling at night, and avoid carrying valuable items while exploring the city.

Health in São José do Rio Preto

Travellers should ensure they have received the required vaccinations before visiting São José do Rio Preto. It is also advisable to carry mosquito repellent, as there are cases of dengue fever and other mosquito-borne illnesses in the region.
