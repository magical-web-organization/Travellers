Armação dos Búzios, also known as Búzios, is a beautiful resort town located in the Lakes Region of Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The town’s stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and unique cultural attractions have made it a popular destination for tourists from around the world. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll explore all the best things to see and do in Armação dos Búzios, from its cultural attractions to its natural wonders to its food and drink scene.

Introduction to Armação dos Búzios

Armação dos Búzios was discovered in the 16th century by Portuguese navigators, but it wasn’t until the 1960s that it began to gain international attention as a resort town. Today, it is known for its beautiful beaches, charming downtown area, and unique cultural attractions.

List of Places to Visit in Armação dos Búzios

If you’re planning a trip to Armação dos Búzios, you’ll want to check out these must-visit attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Brigitte Bardot Statue: A bronze statue of the French actress, who visited the town in the 1960s, is a popular tourist attraction located in the main square of Armação dos Búzios.
  • Casa da Flor: A museum dedicated to the life and work of artist Carlos Scliar, located in a beautiful colonial house.
  • Rua das Pedras: A charming pedestrianized street filled with shops, restaurants, and bars that comes alive at night.

Natural Attractions of Armação dos Búzios: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • João Fernandes Beach: A beautiful beach with crystal clear waters, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.
  • Ferradura Beach: A horseshoe-shaped beach with calm waters, ideal for families and those looking for a relaxing day in the sun.
  • Ilha Feia: A small islet that can be reached by a short hike from Brava beach. Once there, you can enjoy stunning views of the ocean and nearby cliffs.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Armação dos Búzios

Armação dos Búzios has several routes for hiking and biking that offer beautiful views of the surrounding area, including:

  • Rota Velha Trilha: A 5-kilometer trail that begins in downtown Búzios and leads through lush greenery to the beautiful beach of Azeda.
  • Mirante do Forno: A beautiful lookout point located on a hill above Forno beach, accessed by a hike through native vegetation.
  • Praia da Foca: A secluded beach accessible only by a 30-minute hike through the forest, perfect for those looking for a peaceful and quiet spot.

Best Things to Do in Armação dos Búzios: Activities and Tours

  • Boat Tours: Take a boat tour of the surrounding islands and beaches, including Angra dos Reis and Ilha Grande.
  • Surfing: Rent a surfboard and hit the waves at one of the town’s many beaches, including Geribá, Praia Brava, and João Fernandes.
  • Capoeira Lessons: Learn the art of capoeira, a martial art with roots in African traditions, at one of the town’s schools.

Week-Long Itinerary for Armação dos Búzios

If you’re planning to spend a week in Armação dos Búzios, here’s a sample itinerary to help you make the most of your time:

Day 1: Arrive in Armação dos Búzios and check into your hotel. Spend the afternoon exploring the downtown area. Day 2-3: Spend two days at the beach, exploring different beaches and participating in water sports and other activities. Day 4: Take a boat tour of the surrounding islands and beaches. Day 5: Take a hike or bike ride through the forest to Praia da Foca. Day 6: Learn about the town’s history and culture by visiting museums and historical attractions. Day 7: Relax and shop for souvenirs in Rua das Pedras before departing.

Food and Drink Scene of Armação dos Búzios

Armação dos Búzios is known for its fresh seafood and vibrant nightlife. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Moqueca: A traditional Brazilian seafood stew made with coconut milk, palm oil, and an assortment of seafood.
  • Feijoada: A hearty bean stew made with pork and beef, typically served with rice and greens.
  • Coxinha de Frango: A popular Brazilian snack made with shredded chicken and cream cheese, wrapped in dough and deep-fried.

Local Beverages

  • Caipirinha: Brazil’s national cocktail, made with cachaça, sugar, and lime.
  • Chopp: A Brazilian term for draft beer, which is popular in Armação dos Búzios.

Local Culture and Customs of Armação dos Búzios

Armação dos Búzios has a unique culture influenced by its indigenous, African, and Portuguese roots. The town is known for its vibrant music and dance scene, with frequent festivals and performances throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Armação dos Búzios

The best time to visit Armação dos Búzios is during the summer months (December to February) when temperatures are warm and the beaches are at their best. However, the town is also beautiful during the spring (September to November) and fall (March to May) when the crowds are smaller.

Do’s and Don’ts of Armação dos Búzios

Do:

  • Learn a few Portuguese phrases before you go.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Wear sunscreen and a hat when spending time in the sun.

Don’t:

  • Assume that everyone speaks English.
  • Wear revealing clothing in public areas.
  • Leave valuables unattended on the beach.

Safety and Health

Safety in Armação dos Búzios

Armação dos Búzios is generally a safe destination for tourists, but always be aware of your surroundings and take precautions to protect your valuables.

Health in Armação dos Búzios

Visitors to Armação dos Búzios should be up to date on routine vaccinations and take precautions to prevent mosquito bites, which can transmit diseases like dengue fever.

Getting to Armação dos Búzios: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Armação dos Búzios

The closest airport to Armação dos Búzios is Rio de Janeiro-Galeão International Airport (GIG), approximately 180 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Armação dos Búzios

The town has a bus station that connects it to other cities and towns in the region.

Other Options

Alternatively, you can arrange for a private transfer from Rio de Janeiro or another nearby city.

How to Get Around in Armação dos Búzios

The town is small enough to explore on foot, but there are also taxis and vans that can take you around the area.

Where to Go Next from Armação dos Búzios: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have time to explore beyond Armação dos Búzios, here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Cabo Frio: A nearby town with beautiful beaches and rich history.
  • Búzios Eco Adventure: A tour company that offers hiking, biking, and other outdoor activities in the surrounding area.

Conclusion

Armação dos Búzios is a beautiful and unique destination that offers a perfect mix of outdoor adventure, cultural attractions, and vibrant nightlife. Whether you’re looking to lounge on the beach, explore local history and culture, or try new food and drink, you’ll find plenty of options to make your trip unforgettable.
