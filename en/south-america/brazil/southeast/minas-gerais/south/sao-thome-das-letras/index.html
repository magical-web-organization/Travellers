Introduction to São Thomé das Letras

São Thomé das Letras is a charming town located in the southeastern region of Minas Gerais, Brazil. The town is nestled in the Serra da Mantiqueira mountain range with an elevation of 1,440 meters above sea level. São Thomé das Letras is a popular tourist destination known for its stunning landscapes, mystical atmosphere, and vibrant local culture.

List of Places to Visit in São Thomé das Letras

Here are some of the top places to visit in São Thomé das Letras:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Igreja Matriz de São Thomé das Letras: A beautiful church that dates back to the 18th century and boasts magnificent baroque and rococo architecture.
  • Museu do Território de São Thomé das Letras: This museum showcases the history and culture of the region with a collection of artifacts and documents.

Natural Attractions of São Thomé das Letras: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Gruta do Carimbado: An impressive natural limestone cave that is known for its stunning rock formations and underground lakes.
  • Vale das Borboletas: A natural reserve with lush green forests, cascading waterfalls, and colorful butterflies.
  • Pedra da Bruxa: This mystical rock formation is believed to have supernatural powers and is a popular spot for sunset gazing.
  • Cachoeira da Eubiose: A beautiful waterfall that is surrounded by lush greenery and tranquil pools.

Hiking/Biking Routes of São Thomé das Letras

São Thomé das Letras is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes to explore in the area:

  • Trilha do Cruzeiro: A panoramic trail that takes you to the top of a hill overlooking the town.
  • Trilha do Sobradinho: A scenic trail that leads you through beautiful forests and past natural springs.
  • Caminho das Águas: A challenging but rewarding route that takes you through the heart of the Serra da Mantiqueira mountains.

Best Things to Do in São Thomé das Letras: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re looking for adventure or relaxation, São Thomé das Letras has plenty of activities and tours to offer. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Paragliding: Take to the skies and soar over the stunning landscapes of São Thomé das Letras.
  • Horseback Riding: Explore the scenic countryside on horseback and enjoy the tranquil views.
  • Yoga and Meditation: Unwind and connect with nature with a yoga or meditation session amidst the beautiful landscapes of São Thomé das Letras.

Week-Long Itinerary for São Thomé das Letras

Here is a suggested week-long itinerary for São Thomé das Letras:

Food and Drink Scene of São Thomé das Letras

São Thomé das Letras has a vibrant food and drink scene with plenty of delicious local specialties to try. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pão de Queijo: A popular Brazilian dish made with cheese and tapioca flour.
  • Feijão Tropeiro: A traditional Minas Gerais dish made with beans, pork, and spices.
  • Doce de Leite: A creamy and sweet dessert made with condensed milk.

Local Beverages

  • Cachaça: A traditional Brazilian liquor made from sugarcane that is often used to make the popular caipirinha cocktail.
  • Café Mineiro: A strong and rich coffee that is produced in Minas Gerais.

Getting to São Thomé das Letras: Transportation Options and Logistics

São Thomé das Letras is located approximately 300 km from São Paulo and 400 km from Rio de Janeiro. Here are some transportation options for getting to São Thomé das Letras:

Airports Near São Thomé das Letras

  • Aeroporto Internacional de São Paulo (GRU): This is the nearest international airport to São Thomé das Letras, located approximately 250 km away.
  • Aeroporto Internacional Tom Jobim (GIG): Located in Rio de Janeiro, approximately 350 km away from São Thomé das Letras.

Bus Stations Near São Thomé das Letras

  • Terminal Rodoviário Tietê: This is the main bus station in São Paulo, which has daily buses going to São Thomé das Letras.
  • Terminal Rodoviário Novo Rio: This is the main bus station in Rio de Janeiro, which has regular buses to São Thomé das Letras.

Other Options
