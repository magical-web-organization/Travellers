Introduction to Rio Novo do Sul

Nestled in the heart of Brazil’s Central Espirito Santo region, Rio Novo do Sul is a hidden gem that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, rich history, and culture. The town was officially founded in 1890, and over the years, it has witnessed a fascinating transformation, from a colonial town to a thriving agricultural hub.

The town’s name translates to “New River of the South,” and it is a testament to the rich waters that surround it. Rio Novo do Sul is a perfect destination for those seeking an escape from the hustle and bustle of big cities, offering visitors a tranquil and serene environment to explore.

Week-Long Itinerary for Rio Novo do Sul

Here’s a week-long itinerary that covers the best of Rio Novo do Sul:

  • Day 1: Visit the Museu do Imigrante and Colônia Imperial
  • Day 2: Hike the Pedra do Garrafão Trail and enjoy the scenic views
  • Day 3: Visit the Cachoeira dos Pancas waterfall and explore the Pancas Circuit trail
  • Day 4: Take a tour of the town’s agricultural farms and indulge in agrotourism
  • Day 5: Visit the Nossa Senhora da Conceição Church and the Old Central Station
  • Day 6: Taste Rio Novo do Sul’s Cachaça and go on a river rafting adventure
  • Day 7: Visit the Parque Estadual da Pedra Azul and enjoy its natural beauty.

The best time to visit Rio Novo do Sul is during the dry season, which is from June to September. During this period, visitors can enjoy the pleasant weather and indulge in outdoor activities.

Getting to Rio Novo do Sul: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Rio Novo do Sul

The nearest airport to Rio Novo do Sul is the Eurico de Aguiar Salles Airport in Vitória, which is 97.7 km away from Rio Novo do Sul.

Bus Stations Near Rio Novo do Sul

The closest bus stations are the Guarapari Bus Station and the Cachoeiro de Itapemirim Bus Station.

Other Options

You can also get to Rio Novo do Sul by car. Several national highways connect Rio Novo do Sul to major cities in the region.

How to Get Around in Rio Novo do Sul

The best way to get around Rio Novo do Sul is by car. You can rent a car at the Vitória airport or hire a taxi. Biking and walking are also great options for short distances
