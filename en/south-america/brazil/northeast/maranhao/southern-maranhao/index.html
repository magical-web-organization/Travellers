Introduction to Southern Maranhão

Southern Maranhão is a region located in the northeast of Brazil, in South America. It is known for its beautiful natural landscapes, rich culture and history, and welcoming locals. Its location near the coast provides ample opportunity for outdoor activities such as hiking, swimming, and surfing.

List of Places to Visit in Southern Maranhão

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Dona Júlia Alves Museum

Located in the city of Balsas, the Dona Júlia Alves Museum showcases a wide variety of artifacts and exhibits related to the history and culture of Southern Maranhão.

São Francisco de Assis Church

A historical landmark of Southern Maranhão, the São Francisco de Assis Church is a must-visit attraction when exploring the region.

Casa do Divino Museum

The Casa do Divino Museum is located in the city of São João dos Patos and highlights the rich religious traditions of the region.

Natural Attractions of Southern Maranhão: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lençóis Maranhenses National Park

One of Southern Maranhão’s biggest draws is the Lençóis Maranhenses National Park, a unique natural wonder composed of striking sand dunes and crystal-clear lagoons.

Parnaíba Delta

The Parnaíba Delta is a breathtakingly beautiful network of lagoons and rivers that is perfect for kayaking, boat rides, and bird watching.

Barreirinhas

Barreirinhas is a charming fishing village located on the edge of the Lençóis Maranhenses National Park, offering stunning views and plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Southern Maranhão

Itapecuru Waterfall Trail

This trail is located near the city of Caxias and offers a scenic hike through lush forests and picturesque waterfalls.

Chapada das Mesas National Park Trail

Located near the town of Carolina, this trail is perfect for hiking or mountain biking and offers beautiful views of the surrounding landscape and rock formations.

Best Things to Do in Southern Maranhão: Activities and Tours

Kitesurfing Lessons

Southern Maranhão, with its long, expansive beaches and perfect wind conditions, is an excellent place to learn or perfect the art of kitesurfing.

Pioca Sandboarding

Pioca is a small village located near the entrance to Lençóis Maranhenses National Park and is known for its stunning sand dunes, which are perfect for sandboarding.

Canoeing in the Parnaíba Delta

Get a unique perspective of the Delta and its wildlife by taking a canoe trip down the river.

Week-Long Itinerary for Southern Maranhão

Day Activities
1 Explore São Luis, the capital city
2 Visit the São Francisco de Assis Church and nearby historical sites
3 Go sandboarding at Pioca
4 Take a guided tour of the Lençóis Maranhenses National Park
5 Visit the Parnaíba Delta and go canoeing
6 Hike or bike the trails at Chapada das Mesas National Park
7 Relax at the beach in Barreirinhas

Food and Drink Scene of Southern Maranhão

Must-Try Dishes

Cuxá

A perfect blend of African and indigenous cuisine, cuxá is a traditional dish made from dried shrimp, crab, and okra, served over rice.

Arroz de Cuxá

Similar to cuxá, this dish includes shrimp, crab, and okra, mixed in with a delicious blend of spices and rice.

Tiquira

This native alcoholic beverage of Maranhão is made from cassava and served in a traditional gourd cup.

Where to Stay in Southern Maranhão: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hotel Theo

Located in the city of Balsas, Hotel Theo offers comfortable rooms and modern amenities.

Pousada Paraíso dos Lençóis

A charming guesthouse located in Barreirinhas, Pousada Paraíso dos Lençóis offers breathtaking views of the Lençóis Maranhenses National Park.

Delta Park Hotel

Located in the city of Parnaíba, Delta Park Hotel offers comfortable accommodations and excellent amenities.

What to Buy in Southern Maranhão: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Handmade Hammocks

Traditional Maranhão hammocks are comfortable, colorful, and make for the perfect souvenir to bring home.

Artisanal Ceramics

Local artisans craft beautiful ceramics using native materials and traditional techniques.

Local Culture and Customs of Southern Maranhão

Southern Maranhão is a region known for its rich cultural heritage, which is still visible in its architecture, music, cuisine, and religious customs. The population is predominantly Catholic, and various celebrations are held throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Southern Maranhão

The best time to visit Southern Maranhão is during the winter months (June to September), when the temperatures are cooler and the skies are clear.

Do’s and Don’ts of Southern Maranhão

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine and drinks
  • Learn about the local culture and history
  • Respect the natural environment and local customs

Don’t:

  • Swim or bathe in any rivers or lagoons without a trained guide
  • Disrespect any religious customs or practices
  • Leave trash or litter in the natural areas

Safety and Health

Safety in Southern Maranhão

Overall, Southern Maranhão is a safe region to visit. However, it is always advisable to take standard precautions, such as not carrying large amounts of cash and being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Southern Maranhão

Visitors to Southern Maranhão should ensure they are up to date on routine vaccinations, such as measles, mumps, and rubella. Additionally, wearing insect repellent and long-sleeved clothing can help prevent mosquito-borne illnesses like dengue fever.

Getting to Southern Maranhão: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Southern Maranhão

The nearest airport to Southern Maranhão is São Luís International Airport, located in the state capital. From there, visitors can take a bus or hire a car to explore the region.

Bus Stations Near Southern Maranhão

Several bus stations in Maranhão offer connections to Southern Maranhão, including the main bus station in the capital city of São Luís and the bus station in Imperatriz.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or hire a taxi to explore Southern Maranhão on their own.

How to Get Around in Southern Maranhão

The best way to get around Southern Maranhão is by car, as many of the main attractions in the region are located outside of the cities and towns.

Where to Go Next from Southern Maranhão: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

After exploring Southern Maranhão, visitors can head to nearby destinations such as the state capital of São Luís, the beaches of Jericoacoara, or the city of Fortaleza.

Conclusion

Southern Maranhão is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in the natural beauty, rich culture, and unique history of Brazil. With its stunning beaches, beautiful parks, and welcoming locals, it is sure to provide an unforgettable travel experience.
