Introduction to Paulino Neves

Located in the Northern region of Maranhão, Paulino Neves is a peaceful municipality that boasts of an array of natural attractions, including pristine coastline, exotic wildlife, and stunning landscapes. While not as well-known as other destinations in Brazil, Paulino Neves is an ideal destination for those looking for a retreat away from the crowds. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll explore the best things to do, see, and eat in Paulino Neves, as well as provide tips on getting here, getting around, and staying safe during your visit.

List of Places to Visit in Paulino Neves

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Casa do Divino Espírito Santo: A historic building that serves as a center for food, fun, and music. Visitors can also learn about the history of the town and the region.
  • Igreja de Nossa Senhora do Perpétuo Socorro: A stunning Catholic church that features impressive architecture and intricate details.

Natural Attractions of Paulino Neves: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Praia do Rio Novo: A beautiful beach known for its crystal-clear waters and stunning sunsets.
  • Parque Nacional dos Lençóis Maranhenses: A vast expanse of sand dunes, lagoons, and unique wildlife that offers endless opportunities for hiking, swimming, and exploring.
  • Rio Preguiças: A picturesque river that flows through the city and offers visitors the chance to see local flora and fauna while taking a relaxing boat tour.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Paulino Neves

  • The trail from the town to Praia do Rio Novo offers a scenic walk through the surrounding nature and wildlife.
  • For more experienced hikers, the trek to the top of the sand dunes in Parque Nacional dos Lençóis Maranhenses rewards visitors with breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.

Best Things to Do in Paulino Neves: Activities and Tours

  • Take a boat tour down the Rio Preguiças and visit Mandacaru Lighthouse and Vassouras Village.
  • Get an adrenaline rush by sandboarding down the towering sand dunes of Parque Nacional dos Lençóis Maranhenses.
  • Rent a bike to explore the town and its surroundings at your own pace.

Week-Long Itinerary for Paulino Neves

Day Activities
1 Arrive in Paulino Neves and enjoy a relaxing day at the beach.
2 Take a guided tour of Parque Nacional dos Lençóis Maranhenses.
3 Visit Casa do Divino Espírito Santo and Igreja de Nossa Senhora do Perpétuo Socorro.

Food and Drink Scene of Paulino Neves

Must-Try Dishes

  • Feijoada Completa: A hearty Brazilian traditional dish consisting of pork and black beans stew, rice, and farofa (toasted manioc flour).
  • Arroz de Cuxá: A tasty regional specialty made with shrimp, herbs, and rice.

Local Beverages

  • Cachaça: A strong spirit made from fermented sugar cane juice
  • Caipirinha: Brazil’s national cocktail, made with cachaça, sugar, and lime.

Where to Stay in Paulino Neves: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Pousada Vida Boa: A budget-friendly, clean, and comfortable guesthouse located downtown.
  • Casa do Pescador: A cozy hotel located next to the Rio Novo beach that offers amazing views and comfortable room options.

What to Buy in Paulino Neves: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handmade jewelry and accessories made from local materials.
  • Local artisanal handicrafts, such as woven baskets and ceramics.

Local Culture and Customs of Paulino Neves

  • The locals in Paulino Neves are friendly and welcoming, and visitors should expect a warm and casual atmosphere.
  • The town has a deep Catholic heritage, with many of the important local landmarks being churches and chapels.

Best Time to Visit Paulino Neves

The best time to visit Paulino Neves is between July and September, which is the dry season and when there are fewer mosquitoes. The temperature is mild, and the water levels of lagoons and rivers are low, making the natural attractions easily accessible. However, due to the popularity of the region, expect it to be busy during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Paulino Neves

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Take mosquito repellent and drink plenty of water, especially during hikes.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural attractions.
  • Ignore safety guidelines when engaging in adventurous activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Paulino Neves

Paulino Neves is a relatively safe town for travelers. However, visitors should exercise common sense when exploring the town and the natural attractions and be aware of their surroundings. It’s advisable to take the usual precautions such as not carrying excessive cash, keeping valuable possessions close, and not walking alone at night.

Health in Paulino Neves

Visitors traveling to Paulino Neves should take the necessary precautions to protect themselves from malaria and yellow fever, especially during the wet season. It’s also important to carry and drink bottled water and use mosquito repellent when outdoors.

Getting to Paulino Neves: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Paulino Neves

  • São Luís International Airport (SLZ)
  • Parnaíba International Airport (PHB)

Bus Stations Near Paulino Neves

  • Rodoviária de São Luís
  • Rodoviária de Parnaíba

Other Options

  • Private transfer services are available from the airports or major cities in Maranhão.

How to Get Around in Paulino Neves

The best way to get around Paulino Neves is on foot or by bike, as the town is small and easily navigable. Tours and activities can be arranged through local guides, and taxis are available for longer distances.

Where to Go Next from Paulino Neves: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Barreirinhas: A popular stop for visitors exploring the Lençóis Maranhenses region.
  • São Luís: The state capital of Maranhão and a UNESCO World Heritage site with a rich history and culture.

Conclusion

Paulino Neves is a hidden gem in the state of Maranhão, Brazil, with plenty of natural beauty, adventure, and cultural experiences to offer visitors. From stunning beaches to sand dunes and lagoons, this tranquil town is a must-visit for those looking to relax and escape from everyday life. With the tips and recommendations provided in this comprehensive guide, you’re sure to have an unforgettable experience in Paulino Neves.
