Introduction to Lençóis Maranhenses

Located in the Northern Maranhão state in Northeast Brazil, Lençóis Maranhenses is a true natural wonder with its expansive sand dunes and crystal-clear lagoons. The name “Lençóis Maranhenses” literally translates to “bedsheets of Maranhão,” which is a nod to the sand dunes that resemble crinkled sheets.

The national park covers over 1,500 square kilometers, with a vast range of flora and fauna species, including indigenous birds, turtles and fish. With its landscapes that oscillate between intense blues and greens, the park offers excellent recreational activities such as hiking, swimming, and sandboarding.

List of Places to Visit in Lençóis Maranhenses

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museu Histórico de Alcântara - Located in the nearby town of Alcântara, this museum offers an insight into the town’s rich colonial history and culture.
  • São Luís Historic Centre - The historic center of São Luís, the capital of Maranhão, features Portuguese colonial architecture and was declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1997.

Natural Attractions of Lençóis Maranhenses: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lagoa Azul - Known for its picturesque blue waters, Lagoa Azul is a popular spot for swimming and relaxation.
  • Lagoa Bonita - This lagoon offers breathtaking views of the surrounding sand dunes and is a popular spot for sandboarding.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lençóis Maranhenses

  • The circuit of Lagoa Bonita and Lagoa Azul - This route takes visitors from one lagoon to the other and offers stunning views of the sand dunes.

Best Things to Do in Lençóis Maranhenses: Activities and Tours

  • Sandboarding - Lençóis Maranhenses is known for its sand dunes and visitors can take a sandboarding excursion for some adventurous fun.
  • Boat Tours - Visitors can take a boat tour of the lagoons and explore the rich natural habitat.

Week-Long Itinerary for Lençóis Maranhenses

Day Activity
1 Arrive in São Luís International Airport and transfer to Barreirinhas.
2 Explore the park’s northern lagoons and landscapes. Swim in Lagoa Azul.
3 Take a guided sandboarding excursion and dune hiking tour near Lagoa Bonita.
4 Visit the Betania village and get a better understanding of local culture.
5 Take a Jeep tour to explore the southern part of the park, stopping at various lagoons along the way.
6 Day trip to Atins village, where visitors can enjoy a relaxing day at the beach.
7 Return to São Luís International Airport and depart.

Food and Drink Scene of Lençóis Maranhenses

Must-Try Dishes

  • Arroz de cuxá - A flavorful rice dish made with ground nuts, dried shrimp and herbs.
  • Peixada - A fish stew with garlic, tomato, and onion.

Local Beverages

  • Caipirinha - Traditional Brazilian cocktail made with cachaça, sugar, and lime.
  • Cerveja - Brazilian beer, with Skol and Brahma being popular brands.

Where to Stay in Lençóis Maranhenses: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Barreirinhas - Barreirinhas is the main gateway to Lençóis Maranhenses National Park and offers a range of accommodation options, including luxury hotels, hostels, and guesthouses.
  • Atins - Atins offers a more secluded and laid-back alternative to Barreirinhas, with guesthouses and pousadas along the beach.

What to Buy in Lençóis Maranhenses: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • São Luís Clay Pots - Made in the nearby city of São Luís, these clay pots are intricately painted and make for a beautiful and unique souvenir.
  • Havaianas - A Brazilian brand of flip-flops that are a staple of Brazilian beach culture.

Local Culture and Customs of Lençóis Maranhenses

  • Religious beliefs in the area are predominantly Catholicism, which can be seen in many of the traditional festivals and celebrations throughout the year.
  • The indigenous people in the area have their own cultural traditions and languages, some of which remain in use today.

Best Time to Visit Lençóis Maranhenses

The best time to visit Lençóis Maranhenses is during the dry season from June to September, as the lagoons will be full and the sand dunes are easier to traverse. Avoid the rainy season from January to May, as the lagoons will be flooded, and the sand dunes harder to cross.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lençóis Maranhenses

Do:

  • Bring sunscreen and mosquito repellent.
  • Plan to arrive early in the morning or late in the afternoon to avoid the hot midday sun.
  • Respect the natural environment by not littering or damaging the sand dunes.

Don’t:

  • Swim in the lagoons with sunscreen or other lotions on, as it can affect the water’s pH levels and harm the local ecosystem.
  • Hire guides or tour companies that do not prioritize sustainability or the protection of the natural environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lençóis Maranhenses

  • Visitors should exercise caution when swimming and should only swim in designated areas.
  • Travelers should be aware of the potential risks of petty theft and should keep their valuables secured and out of sight.

Health in Lençóis Maranhenses

  • Visitors should drink only bottled water and avoid food from street vendors.
  • Protect against mosquito bites with insect repellent, long-sleeved clothing, and mosquito nets when sleeping.

Getting to Lençóis Maranhenses: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lençóis Maranhenses

  • São Luís International Airport is the closest airport to Lençóis Maranhenses and is located in the capital city of São Luís.

Bus Stations Near Lençóis Maranhenses

  • Barreirinhas Bus Station is the main bus station, located in the town of Barreirinhas.

Other Options

  • Car Rental - Car Rental services are offered at São Luís International Airport and in the nearby town of Barreirinhas.

How to Get Around in Lençóis Maranhenses

  • Many lagoons can only be reached by 4x4 vehicles, and visitors can hire a driver from Barreirinhas or Atins.
  • Another popular way to explore Lençóis Maranhenses is by hiking or sandboarding.

Where to Go Next from Lençóis Maranhenses: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Jericoacoara, Ceará - Known for its stunning beaches and dunes, Jericoacoara is a popular destination for those looking to extend their beach vacation.
  • Chapada das Mesas National Park, Maranhão - This national park is home to a variety of waterfalls, canyons, and caves, making it a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts.

Conclusion

Lençóis Maranhenses is a unique and beautiful destination with unparalleled natural beauty. With its sand dunes, lagoons, and diverse culture, this national park is perfect for outdoor adventurers and culture enthusiasts alike. By following this comprehensive guide, you’ll be sure to have an unforgettable experience in Lençóis Maranhenses.
