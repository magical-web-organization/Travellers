Ilha do Caju is a hidden paradise located in Eastern Maranhão in Northeast Brazil. This small island, surrounded by the Delta of Parnaíba, offers travelers diverse natural and cultural attractions. With its untouched beauty, this destination is perfect for adventurous seekers and nature lovers. In this comprehensive guide, we will take you through everything you need to know about traveling to Ilha do Caju.

Introduction to Ilha do Caju

Ilha do Caju is a small island located in the middle of the Delta of Parnaíba. The island is a natural haven of mangroves, dunes, beaches, and lagoons. The island is accessible only by boat, making it an isolated paradise. The island has a small community of fishermen and their families, who preserve the island’s cultural heritage.

Local Culture and Customs of Ilha do Caju

Ilha do Caju is a place of unique culture. The islanders have various traditions that reflect their way of life. One of the most important events for them is the Festa do Caju (Caju Festival) that happens in October in honor of Saint Francis.

Best Time to Visit Ilha do Caju

The best time to visit Ilha do Caju is during the dry season from June to December when the weather is drier and pleasant for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ilha do Caju

Safety and Health

Safety in Ilha do Caju

Ilha do Caju is a safe place to visit, but you should take precautions like not leaving your belongings unattended, especially on the beaches.

Health in Ilha do Caju

You should take care of your health while traveling to Ilha do Caju. Be sure to have your vaccines up to date and bring first aid kits to prevent minor accidents.

Getting to Ilha do Caju: Transportation Options and Logistics

The nearest cities to Ilha do Caju are Parnaíba and São Luís. From there, you can take a boat to the island.

Airports Near Ilha do Caju

The nearest airports to Ilha do Caju are Parnaíba International Airport and São Luís International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Ilha do Caju

There are no bus stations near Ilha do Caju.

Other Options

You can rent a private car or take a taxi to reach the nearest port.

How to Get Around in Ilha do Caju

The best way to get around in Ilha do Caju is on foot. You can also rent a bike or book guided tours that include transportation.
