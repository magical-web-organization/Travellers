Introduction to Ilha das Canárias

Ilha das Canárias is an island located in the Parnaíba River Delta, surrounded on one side by the Atlantic Ocean and on the other by the Parnaíba River. The island is known for its rich culture, history, and incredible natural beauty. Here are some must-see places during your visit:

Local Culture and Customs of Ilha das Canárias

Ilha das Canárias is home to a vibrant culture influenced by indigenous, European, and African heritage. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and it is considered respectful to greet people with a handshake or hug. Portuguese is the official language, but many locals speak Parnaíba Portuguese Creole and the Tupi Guaraní language.

Best Time to Visit Ilha das Canárias

The best time to visit Ilha das Canárias is from July to December, during the dry season when there is less rainfall and better weather for outdoor activities. Temperatures can reach up to 32°C during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ilha das Canárias

Do:

  1. Learn some Portuguese before your trip
  2. Respect the local culture and customs
  3. Take advantage of outdoor activities such as hiking and biking
  4. Try local cuisine

Don’t:

  1. Wear revealing clothing in public
  2. Touch or disturb the wildlife at the Delta do Parnaíba National Park
  3. Display public affection
  4. Leave your belongings unattended

Safety and Health

Safety in Ilha das Canárias

Ilha das Canárias is a relatively safe destination, but as with any travel, it is advisable to take some basic precautions. Avoid carrying large amounts of cash and leave your valuables in a safe place. It is also recommended that you travel with a guide when exploring the Delta do Parnaíba National Park.

Health in Ilha das Canárias

Travelers should be up-to-date on routine vaccinations and consider getting vaccinated for yellow fever if they will be visiting other areas of Brazil. It is also recommended to bring insect repellent, as mosquitoes and other insects can transmit diseases such as dengue fever.

Getting to Ilha das Canárias: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ilha das Canárias

Lençóis Maranhenses National Park Airport (LSS) is the closest airport to Ilha das Canárias, located about 130 km away. Another option is Marechal Cunha Machado International Airport (SLZ), located 300 km away.

Bus Stations Near Ilha das Canárias

The nearest bus station is located in the city of Tutoia, about 46 km from Ilha das Canárias.

Other Options

Private transfers and taxis are also available from nearby cities such as Parnaíba and Barreirinhas.

How to Get Around in Ilha das Canárias

The easiest way to get around Ilha das Canárias is by car or taxi. Renting a car is a good option as it gives you the freedom to explore the island at your own pace.
