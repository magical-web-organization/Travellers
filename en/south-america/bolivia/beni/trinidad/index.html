Introduction to Trinidad

Trinidad is a charming city with a population of approximately 150,000 people. It is the capital of the Beni Department and is often referred to as the “City of the Holy Trinity.” It is surrounded by vast plains, forests, and rivers, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Trinidad is also known for its rich history and culture, which can be seen in its museums and historical landmarks. Visitors can immerse themselves in local traditions, sample delicious food and drink, and enjoy a wide range of activities and tours.
