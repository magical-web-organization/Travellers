Puerto Suárez, a city located in the Santa Cruz department of Bolivia, is a must-visit destination for those who are looking for an authentic experience in South America. This guide is designed to provide you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Puerto Suárez, from the best things to do and see to where to stay and how to get around.

Introduction to Puerto Suárez

Puerto Suárez is a small city that sits at the border of Brazil and Bolivia, known for its rich history and beautiful natural attractions. The city itself is small, but it makes up for its size with its vibrant culture and friendly people.

List of Places to Visit in Puerto Suárez

Puerto Suárez is blessed with a number of culturally important attractions as well as stunning natural attractions. Here are some of the most notable places to visit in the city:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum of the War of the Chaco
  • Archeology Museum
  • San Antonio de Padua Church

Natural Attractions of Puerto Suárez: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Pantanal de Mato Grosso (Brazil)
  • Noel Kempff Mercado National Park (Bolivia)
  • Serra do Amolar mountain range (Brazil)

Hiking/Biking Routes of Puerto Suárez

If you love hiking or biking, Puerto Suárez is the perfect destination. The city offers a number of hiking and biking routes that take you through some of the most stunning natural attractions of the region.

Best Things to Do in Puerto Suárez: Activities and Tours

Puerto Suárez offers a variety of activities and tours that cater to different interests. Here are some of the best things to do in Puerto Suárez:

  • Wildlife tours

  • Fishing trips

Local Culture and Customs of Puerto Suárez

Puerto Suárez is a city with a rich history and culture. Here are some customs to keep in mind when visiting the city:

  • Greetings are important. Always greet people with a smile and a handshake.
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
  • Tipping is not expected, but it is appreciated.

Best Time to Visit Puerto Suárez

The best time to visit Puerto Suárez is from May to September when temperatures are cooler and rainfall is less frequent. If you don’t mind the heat, you can visit the city during the summer months (December to February), but be prepared for high humidity levels.

Do’s and Don’ts of Puerto Suárez

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local cuisine.
  • Keep an eye on your belongings.

Don’t:

  • Drink tap water
  • Wander around alone at night
  • Leave your valuables in plain sight

Safety and Health

Safety in Puerto Suárez

Puerto Suárez is a safe city, but it’s always important to take precautions when traveling. Here are some safety tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid walking alone at night.
  • Keep your valuables out of sight.
  • Use a reputable tour company when going on outdoor activities

Health in Puerto Suárez

It’s recommended that travelers get vaccinated against yellow fever and typhoid before traveling to Puerto Suárez. Mosquito repellent is also recommended since the city is in a tropical region.

Conclusion

Puerto Suárez is a city that should be on every traveler’s bucket list. Its rich history, stunning natural attractions, and vibrant culture make it a truly unique destination. With this guide, you should be able to plan a trip that will give you a taste of all that Puerto Suárez has to offer.
