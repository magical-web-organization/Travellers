Introduction to Barranquilla

Nestled on the western coast of Colombia, Barranquilla is a vibrant city known for its colorful Carnaval de Barranquilla celebrations and rich cultural heritage. This bustling metropolis is home to bustling streets, colonial architecture, and friendly locals. As you explore the city, you’ll discover a vibrant arts scene, delicious food, and fascinating museums. Whether you’re a history buff, nature lover, or foodie, there’s something for everyone in Barranquilla.

Local Culture and Customs of Barranquilla

Barranquilla is known for its vibrant culture and rich traditions. One of the most important events in the city is the Carnaval de Barranquilla, a four-day festival celebrating the city’s ethnic diversity. The people of Barranquilla are known for their warm hospitality, and it is considered impolite to arrive late for appointments.

Best Time to Visit Barranquilla

The best time to visit Barranquilla is from December to April, when the weather is dry and sunny. Keep in mind that February is the busiest time of the year, thanks to the Carnaval de Barranquilla.

Do’s and Don’ts of Barranquilla

Do:

  • Sample the local cuisine
  • Attend the Carnaval de Barranquilla
  • Learn a little bit of Spanish before your trip

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing in public places
  • Drink tap water
  • Forget to bring sunscreen and insect repellent

Safety and Health

Safety in Barranquilla

While Barranquilla is considered to be a safe city for travelers, there are still a few precautions you should take. Don’t carry large amounts of cash, avoid walking alone in secluded areas at night and keep your belongings close to you.

Health in Barranquilla

Before your trip, make sure you are up to date with all of the necessary vaccinations.
It is also advisable to carry a first aid kit with you and avoid eating food from street vendors.

Getting to Barranquilla: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Barranquilla

If you’re arriving by air, your best bet is the Ernesto Cortissoz International Airport. From there, you can take a taxi or a bus to the city center.

Bus Stations Near Barranquilla

If you’re arriving by bus, Arcotrans operates a number of routes to and from Barranquilla.

Other Options

There are also several car rental companies that operate in Barranquilla, and taxis are readily available.

How to Get Around in Barranquilla

The best way to explore Barranquilla is by taxi or bus. Taxis are cheap and widely available throughout the city. The local buses are a bit more challenging but are an affordable and authentic experience.
