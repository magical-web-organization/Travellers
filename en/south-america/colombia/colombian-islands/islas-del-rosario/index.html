Comprehensive Travel Guide to Islas del Rosario, Colombia

Colombia is a country with diverse geography and culture. If you are a lover of stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and exotic marine life, Islas del Rosario is the perfect travel destination for you. Located just off the coast of Cartagena, it is a group of small coral islands in the Caribbean Sea.

Introduction to Islas del Rosario

With its beauty, tranquility, and unique ecosystem, Islas del Rosario is a top tourist destination in Colombia. The islands are a protected national park and are made up of 27 small islands, where snorkeling and scuba diving are popular activities. The park is home to more than 1,000 species of marine life and coral reefs.

Islas del Rosario can be reached by boat from Cartagena, with the journey lasting around one hour. The best time to visit Islas del Rosario is from December to April when the weather conditions are favourable.

List of Places to Visit in Islas del Rosario

Islas del Rosario has a range of natural and cultural attractions to explore. Here are some of the top places to visit on the islands.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • San Fernando & San José Fortresses - The fortress complex is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The forts were built between 1751 and 1753 to protect Cartagena from pirate attacks.
  • Oceanarium - The Oceanarium was established in 1989. It offers visitors an opportunity to see a variety of marine life and participate in snorkeling and diving classes.
  • Rosario Islands Museum - The museum features pre-Columbian artefacts and provides an insight into the indigenous culture of the Rosario Islands.

Natural Attractions of Islas del Rosario: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Playa Blanca - Playa Blanca is a stunning pristine beach with white sand and clear blue water. Visitors can swim, sunbathe, or engage in various water sports activities.
  • Isla Grande - Isla Grande is the largest island in Islas del Rosario. Visitors can participate in diving and snorkelling or take a hike to the peak of the island for a panoramic view of the ocean.
  • Coral Reef - Islas del Rosario is famous for its coral reefs and colourful fish. You can explore the marine world of the islands through snorkeling and scuba diving.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Islas del Rosario

The best way to explore the natural beauty of the islands is by hiking or biking. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Trail to Punta Norte - The trail offers spectacular views of the Caribbean sea and coral reefs. The route is 8 km long and takes around 3 hours to complete.
  • Isla Grande Mangrove Walk - The mangroves are a vital ecosystem in the Rosario Islands, and this walk will take you on a journey of discovery about the importance of mangroves.

Best Things to Do in Islas del Rosario: Activities and Tours

Isla del Rosario is an adventure paradise. Here are some of the top things to do while on the islands:

  • Snorkeling - Islas del Rosario is home to the largest coral reef in the Colombian Caribbean. With crystal-clear waters, visitors can explore the vibrant coral reefs and marine life.
  • Scuba diving - The underwater world of Islas del Rosario is a must-see. Diving enthusiasts can spend hours witnessing fantastic marine life and exploring wreck sites.
  • Island Hopping - Explore the spectacular islands, beaches, lagoons, and mangroves while hopping around the islands.

Week-Long Itinerary for Islas del Rosario

Here is a week-long itinerary for those planning to visit Islas del Rosario:

Days Activities
Day 1 Arrival in Cartagena and check into your hotel.
Day 2 Explore the highlights of Cartagena, including Castillo San Felipe de Barajas and the Walled City.
Day 3 Take a boat ride to Islas del Rosario, participate in a snorkelling tour in the morning.
Day 4 Participate in a scuba diving tour in the morning, then take a hike in the afternoon.
Day 5 Enjoy one of the scenic beaches in Playa Blanca.
Day 6 Take a boat tour of the Rosario Islands and explore the mangroves and lagoons of the park.
Day 7 Visit the Oceanarium, Museum, and Fortresses.

Food and Drink Scene of Islas del Rosario

The Rosario Islands offer a range of delicious local dishes to savour. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Ceviche - fresh raw fish served with chopped onions, tomatoes, cilantro, cucumber, and lime juice.
  • Arepa de Huevo - Colombian-style arepa stuffed with meat and egg.
  • Arroz con Coco - Coconut rice served with fried fish, coconut milk, and plantains.

Local Beverages

  • Coco Loco - a cocktail of coconut, rum, and fruit.
  • Aguapanela - a traditional Colombian drink made of panela, a type of unrefined sugar.
  • Limonada de Coco - a refreshing drink made from lime juice, coconut water, and sugar.

Where to Stay in Islas del Rosario: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Islas del Rosario provides a range of accommodations to suit any budget. Here are some options:

  • Coralina Island - one of the best eco-hotels in the Rosario Islands. It features private beaches, bungalows, and a natural pool.
  • Hotel San Pedro de Majagua - a sustainable hotel and a former plantation house transformed into a hotel, featuring ocean-facing rooms and a pool.
  • Hostel Isla Roots - it offers a bed and breakfast style accommodation in a friendly atmosphere.

What to Buy in Islas del Rosario: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Those visiting the Rosario Islands can purchase local souvenirs and products to bring back home. Here are some options:

  • Handmade Jewelry - The local craftswomen offer an array of handmade jewellery made of pearls and other local gemstones.
  • Coconut oil - The Rosario Islands are famous for their natural and pure coconut oil, ideal for skin care.
  • Coffee - Colombia is known for its coffee, and the Rosario Islands have a range of locally sourced coffee.

Local Culture and Customs of Islas del Rosario

The culture and customs of Islas del Rosario are a blend of Caribbean, indigenous, Spanish, and African influences. The locals are friendly and known for their hospitality. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Respect the indigenous culture of the islands
  • Dress modestly
  • Learn basic Spanish, as the majority of the residents speak Spanish.

Best Time to Visit Islas del Rosario

The best time to visit Islas del Rosario is from December to April when the weather is dry and sunny, ideal for beaches and outdoor activities. The rainy season lasts from May to November.

Do’s and Don’ts of Islas del Rosario

Here are some do’s and don’ts during your visit to Islas del Rosario:

Do:

  • Respect the natural environment and the local culture.
  • Take some cash and a credit card, as not all businesses accept cards.
  • Apply sunscreen and use appropriate clothes to avoid sunburns.

Don’t:

  • Leave trash on the beach or in the park.
  • Touch or disturb the marine life and coral reefs.
  • Disrespect the locals’ religious beliefs and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Islas del Rosario

The Rosario Islands are generally safe for visitors. However, it is best to follow the following safety tips:

  • Avoid walking alone at night in unlit areas.
  • Take extra precautions in crowded areas against pickpocketing.
  • Seek advice from reputable tour operators before planning any trips.

Health in Islas del Rosario

It is advisable to take the necessary vaccinations before your visit to Colombia. Visitors to the islands should take precautions against mosquito bites as they can cause diseases like dengue fever and Zika virus.

Getting to Islas del Rosario: Transportation Options and Logistics

Visitors can travel to Islas del Rosario through the following means:

Airports Near Islas del Rosario

The closest airport to Islas del Rosario is Rafael Nuñez International Airport (CTG) in Cartagena.

Bus Stations Near Islas del Rosario

From Cartagena, visitors can take a bus to Barú, a town that connects the Rosario Islands to the mainland.

Other Options

If you want to experience an adventure from the mainland to the islands, then take a boat ride from the Cartagena dock.

How to Get Around in Islas del Rosario

The best way to explore the Rosario Islands is through boats and walking tours.

Where to Go Next from Islas del Rosario: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

From Islas del Rosario, visitors can explore other nearby destinations, including:

  • Cartagena - Explore the city’s colonial architecture and rich history.
  • Mompox - A historic town known for its well-preserved colonial architecture.

Conclusion

Islas del Rosario is an ideal destination to experience Colombia’s beauty and tranquillity. The unspoiled natural environment, pristine beaches, and marine life have made Islas del Rosario one of the most popular travel destinations in Colombia. With this comprehensive guide, you can now plan your next vacation to Islas del Rosario.
