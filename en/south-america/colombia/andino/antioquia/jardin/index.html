Introduction to Jardín

Jardín is a picturesque town located in the Andino region of Antioquia Department, Colombia. Studded with beautiful colonial buildings, stunning natural attractions, and a rich cultural history, Jardín is often regarded as one of the hidden gems of Colombia. With numerous activities such as hiking or riding on cable cars, a range of affordable accommodations, and a friendly local population, Jardín is the perfect destination for travelers looking for an exciting and authentic Colombian experience.

List of Places to Visit in Jardín

Here are the must-visit places in Jardín:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Basilica Menor De La Inmaculada Concepcion
  2. Museo Casa de la Cultura
  3. Casa de la Dona Paula Restrepo de Posada
  4. Casa Tulio Ospina

Natural Attractions of Jardín: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Charco Corazon
  2. Waterfall La Escalera
  3. La Garrucha Viewpoint
  4. Cable Aérea Jardín – An urban cablecar that provides panoramic views of the town

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jardín

  1. La Pierna de Indio Hike – A 2-hour walk to the Pierna Del Indio monument, on top of a hill.
  2. La Cueva del Esplendor Hike – Takes you to a marvelous cave with a waterfall with a 200m high drop.
  3. The Recreational Park El Salto – A popular destination for biking and hiking

Best Things to Do in Jardín: Activities and Tours

  1. Horseback Riding – Horseback riding is a popular activity in Jardín and several local tour operators offer tours in the countryside.
  2. Coffee Farm Tour – Enjoy an educational experience of the coffee history and daily processes of coffee-making.
  3. The Weekend Plaza Bazaar – A popular local market where you can buy local crafts.

Food and Drink Scene of Jardín

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Bandeja Paisa - a typical dish in Antioquia that comes with beans, rice, fried eggs, meat, and chicharron.
  2. Ajiaco - a delicious soup made of three types of potatoes, corn, ají pepper, and cream.

Local Beverages

  1. Tinto - a native roasting-ground coffee drink that is an essential part of local morning routines.
  2. Hot Chocolates - a popular drink in Jardín with rich Colombian cacao.

Where to Stay in Jardín: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Hosteria del Parque
  2. La Casa Amarilla
  3. Hotel Brisas del Rio

What to Buy in Jardín: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Dulce de leche – A popular and delicious caramel treat made from reduced milk.
  2. Local Coffee – High-quality coffee sourced from local farms.

Local Culture and Customs of Jardín

The friendly and welcoming locals of Jardín emanate a sense of pride and respect for their hometown. The town is known for its cultural festivities like the festival of flowers, including parades, and live music events that take place yearly.

Best Time to Visit Jardín

The ideal time to visit Jardín is from December to March - Colombia’s dry season, with the average temperature staying between 18°C to 25°C.

Do’s and Don’ts of Jardín

Do:

  1. Try the local coffee or hot chocolate.
  2. Respect the local culture and customs of the town.

Don’t:

  1. Litter
  2. Wear revealing clothing while visiting the churches.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jardín

Jardín is a safe and friendly town. As with all foreign destinations, tourists should take precautions with valuable belongings.

Health in Jardín

It’s important to carry some medicines, especially mosquito repellants, sunscreen, and warm clothes, when traveling to Jardín.

Getting to Jardín: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Jardín

The closest airport to Jardín is Jose Maria Cordova Airport in Medellin city.

Bus Stations Near Jardín

The bus station in Jardín is a central location and one can hire a taxi upon arrival to move around.

Other Options

One can rent a car or catch a shared minivan from Medellin to Jardín, which takes around four hours.

How to Get Around in Jardín

Jardín is a relatively small town, so everything is within walking distance. However, shared taxis or private transportation can be rented for some sites outside of the town.

Where to Go Next from Jardín: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Medellin
  2. Salento and the Coffee Region
  3. El Penol and Guatape – These destinations are perfect for outdoor adventures and offer breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Jardín is a fantastic destination for a varied and exciting Colombian experience. The picturesque buildings of the town combined with the warm local culture, variety of activities, and delicious food, make Jardín a perfect place to explore both Colombia’s beauty and history.
