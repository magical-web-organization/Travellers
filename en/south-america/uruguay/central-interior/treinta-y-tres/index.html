Introduction to Treinta y Tres

Nestled in the heart of Central Interior, Uruguay, Treinta y Tres is a beautiful city renowned for its rich history, natural beauty, and cultural landmarks. The city derives its name from the revolutionary Thirty-Three Orientals, a group of patriots who fought for the country’s independence. From heritage sites to ecological treasures, there are plenty of attractions to explore in Treinta y Tres. Let us take you on a journey through this vibrant destination and help you plan an unforgettable trip.
