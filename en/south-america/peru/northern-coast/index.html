Introduction to Northern Coast

The Northern Coast of Peru is a hidden gem that is still largely undiscovered by tourists. The region boasts stunning scenery, a rich history and culture, and delicious cuisine. Whether you are an adventure seeker or a history buff, Northern Coast has something for everyone.

Local Culture and Customs of Northern Coast

The people of Northern Coast are friendly and welcoming. They are proud of their culture and heritage and are happy to share it with visitors. It is customary to greet people with a handshake and to say “buenos dias” or “buenas tardes” in passing.

Best Time to Visit Northern Coast

The best time to visit Northern Coast is between May and September when the weather is dry and sunny. This is also peak tourist season so expect higher prices and larger crowds.

Safety and Health

Safety in Northern Coast

Northern Coast is generally safe for tourists. However, it is always wise to keep an eye on your belongings and to avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Northern Coast

It is recommended to have vaccinations for hepatitis A and B, typhoid, and yellow fever before visiting Northern Coast. It is also important to use mosquito repellent to avoid mosquito-borne illnesses such as dengue fever and malaria.
