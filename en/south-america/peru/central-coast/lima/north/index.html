Introduction to North

North is a fascinating region located in Lima, Central Coast, Peru. It spans from the north of Lima to the border of Ecuador, covering more than 1,400 kilometers of coastline. The region is characterized by its impressive archaeological sites, which are a testament to the legacy of the ancient Moche, Chimu, and Lambayeque cultures. In addition to its cultural heritage, North is also home to natural wonders such as the Cordillera Blanca mountain range, the Huascaran National Park, and the picturesque beaches in Mancora.

Best Time to Visit North

The best time to visit North is from May to October. During this period, the weather is dry and sunny, and the temperature is comfortable for outdoor activities. November to April is the rainy season, which can make hiking and other outdoor activities difficult.
