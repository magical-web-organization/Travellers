Introduction to East

Located in Central Coast of Peru, East Lima is a vibrant destination that offers a blend of cultural attractions, natural wonders, adventure activities, and more. The district of Lima is divided into four parts, and East is one of them. East Lima is known for its lush greenery, unspoilt beaches, and top-rated restaurants. It’s a perfect place for those who wish to experience the authentic Peruvian culture.

List of Places to Visit in East

East Lima is a place where history, culture, and nature come together. Here is a list of the top places to visit in East:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Huaca Huallamarca

Huaca Huallamarca is an ancient pyramid located in San Isidro. This pyramid dates back to 200 BC and is a great place to learn about the Andean culture. There is also a museum here that showcases the different artifacts found in the area.

Huaca Pucllana

Huaca Pucllana is an archaeological site consisting of a massive adobe and clay pyramid. This pyramid belongs to the Lima culture and dates back to 400 AD. Huaca Pucllana is an excellent example of Lima’s pre-Columbian past.

Casa de la Gastronomía

Located in Lima’s Historic Center, Casa de la Gastronomía is a museum dedicated to Peruvian cuisine. Here, you can learn about the different traditional dishes, ingredients, and cooking techniques used in Peru.

Natural Attractions of East: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

El Olivar Park

El Olivar Park is a beautiful park located in San Isidro. The park is over 400 years old and is home to over 1,500 olive trees. This park is a perfect place for a relaxing stroll, picnic, or bike ride.

Pantanos de Villa Wildlife Refuge

Located in the district of Chorrillos, Pantanos de Villa Wildlife Refuge is a protected area that is home to a diverse range of animals and plants. Here, you can see different species of birds, reptiles, amphibians, and more.

Morro Solar

Morro Solar is a hill located in Chorrillos that provides stunning views of the Pacific Ocean. The hill is a popular destination for hikers and offers several well-marked trails with varying difficulty levels.

Local Culture and Customs of East

Peruvians are proud of their culture and heritage. Here are some local customs to keep in mind:

  • It’s polite to greet people with a handshake or a kiss on the cheek.
  • Tipping is not mandatory, but it’s appreciated.
  • Peruvians value punctuality, so it’s important to be on time for appointments and meetings.

Best Time to Visit East

The best time to visit East Lima is between December and April, which is the dry season. However, keep in mind that this is also peak tourist season. The months of May to November are cooler and more humid, but you can avoid the crowds and still have a great time.

Do’s and Don’ts of East

Do:

  • Learn some basic Spanish before your trip.
  • Try the famous Peruvian dishes.
  • Explore the local markets for souvenirs.

Don’t:

  • Hail a taxi off the street. Instead, have your hotel call one for you.
  • Leave your valuable belongings unattended in public places.
  • Drink tap water.

Safety and Health

Safety in East

East Lima is a relatively safe place to visit, but it’s important to take precautions like:

  • Avoid walking alone at night.
  • Only use registered taxis with the official logo and phone number on them.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid displaying expensive items in public.

Health in East

It’s important to take precautions with the local water and food to avoid traveler’s diarrhea. Here are some tips:

  • Drink bottled water and avoid ice in drinks.
  • Only eat cooked food.
  • Plan ahead and bring any necessary medications with you.

Getting to East: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near East

The nearest airport to East Lima is Jorge Chavez International Airport, located about 30 minutes away by car.

Bus Stations Near East

The bus station closest to East is Javier Prado Bus Terminal, which services buses from Lima and other major cities in Peru.

Other Options

Taxis and ridesharing services like Uber and Cabify are also available in East Lima.

How to Get Around East

Getting around East is easy with various transportation options, including taxis, buses, and ridesharing services like Uber and Cabify.
