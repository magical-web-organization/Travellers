Introduction to Saunders Island

Saunders Island is a beautiful and pristine island located in the Falkland Islands, a British overseas territory in South America. It is known for its expansive grasslands, rugged coastlines, and incredible wildlife, making it a popular destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

List of Places to Visit in Saunders Island

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Battle Memorial - This monument is dedicated to the soldiers who died during the 1982 Falklands War. It is located on the eastern coast of Saunders Island.
  • The Wildlife Conservation Center - This center is dedicated to the conservation of diverse wildlife species found on the island.
  • Port Egmont Historic Site - This is the site where the British first landed in the Falkland Islands in 1765. Today, it has become a major tourist attraction for history enthusiasts.

Natural Attractions of Saunders Island: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Rockhopper Penguins - Saunders Island is home to a variety of penguin species, including the Rockhopper Penguins. These beautiful creatures are a must-see for any visitor to the island.
  • The Elephant Seals - Visitors can also witness the elephant seals basking in the sun on the rugged coastlines of the Island.
  • The Mount Richards Hiking Trail - The 8km long hiking trail is a perfect way to explore the beauty of Saunders Island. The route offers stunning views of the island’s landscapes, from towering cliffs to rolling hills and grasslands.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Saunders Island

  • Mount Richards Hiking Trail - The hiking trail is the most popular one among visitors, offering a challenging but rewarding experience with stunning views of the island.
  • The Bicycle Trail - Visitors can also rent bicycles from the Wildlife Conservation Center to enjoy a scenic ride around the island.

Best Things to Do in Saunders Island: Activities and Tours

  • Wildlife Watching - Saunders Island is a paradise for wildlife enthusiasts, offering opportunities to see various species of penguins, elephant seals, and other wildlife.
  • Fishing Tours - Visitors can take fishing tours and try their luck at catching the famous squid and the other fish species found in the waters surrounding Saunders Island.
  • Boat Tours - For those who want to experience the island’s beauty from a different perspective, boat tours are a must. Boat tours take visitors around the island, letting them witness its rugged coastlines, pristine waters, and stunning landscapes.

Week-Long Itinerary for Saunders Island

Here is an itinerary for visitors who want to make the most of their week on Saunders Island:

Day Activities
Day 1 Arrive in Saunders Island and take some rest.
Day 2 Visit the Wildlife Conservation Center and enjoy watching penguins and elephant seals.
Day 3 Take a hiking/trekking tour of Mount Richards or explore the island on rented bicycles.
Day 4 Take a boat tour to witness the island’s beauty from a different vantage point.
Day 5 Visit the Port Egmont Historic Site and the Battle Memorial to learn more about the Falklands War.
Day 6 Go fishing and try to catch some fish or squids.
Day 7 Relax and enjoy your last day on the island before departing.

Food and Drink Scene of Saunders Island

Must-Try Dishes

  • Falkland’s Lamb - Saunders Island is known for its delicious lamb dishes, and visitors must try it during their visit.
  • Squid - The waters surrounding Saunders Island are rich in squid, and the locals prepare it in many delicious ways.

Local Beverages

  • Malbec - Malbec wines are quite popular in Falkland Islands, and visitors must try it while visiting Saunders Island.

Where to Stay in Saunders Island: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

The Saunders Island Lodge is the only accommodation available on the island that offers luxurious accommodations and all the amenities. Visitors must book in advance to ensure availability since it is quite popular and in demand

What to Buy in Saunders Island: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Falkland Island Woolens - Visitors can buy locally made woolens, including sweaters, gloves, socks, and scarves.
  • Falkland Island Souvenirs - Visitors can also buy souvenirs such as postcards, bookmarks, and keychains from the gift shops on the island.

Local Culture and Customs of Saunders Island

The locals of Saunders Island are warm and welcoming to visitors. Tourists must respect the locals’ customs and traditions, such as not entering private properties without permission.

Best Time to Visit Saunders Island

The best time to visit Saunders Island is from December to March, which is the summer season in the Falkland Islands. This is when the weather is ideal for outdoor activities, and visitors can witness the island’s wildlife at its best.

Do’s and Don’ts of Saunders Island

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and culture of the island.
  • Try the local food and beverages.
  • Experience the wildlife and scenic beauty of the Island.

Don’t:

  • Litter on the island and keep it clean and pristine.
  • Enter private property without permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Saunders Island

Saunders Island is a very safe destination for tourists. However, visitors must take care while exploring the trails and coasts and must not venture out late at night without proper guidance.

Health in Saunders Island

The island does not have a hospital. Visitors must carry enough prescribed medications and other necessary medications during their visit. Also, make sure to carry protective clothing while venturing out to protect from cold weather.

Getting to Saunders Island: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Saunders Island

The Mount Pleasant Airport on the Falkland Islands is the nearest airport to Saunders Island. Visitors can take a connecting flight or hire a charter flight to Saunders Island from the airport.

Bus Stations Near Saunders Island

There are no bus stations on Saunders Island.

Other Options

Visitors can reach Saunders Island by taking a boat from Stanley, the capital of the Falkland Islands.

How to Get Around in Saunders Island

The best way to explore Saunders Island is by taking a guided tour, hiking, or renting bicycles or boats. The island is small and easy to navigate, and visitors must carry maps and adequate safety gear while venturing out.

Where to Go Next from Saunders Island: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can head to the capital city of Stanley, located on the nearby islands. Stanley offers several attractions, including the Falkland Islands Museum, the Christ Church Cathedral, and several restaurants, and shops.

Conclusion

Saunders Island is a spectacular destination that offers a perfect blend of nature, adventure, and history. Visitors must explore the island’s stunning landscapes, wildlife, and cultural attractions while enjoying the warm hospitality of the locals. Whether it’s relaxing on the coastlines, hiking the rolling hills, or simply absorbing the local culture, Saunders Island is sure to leave visitors spellbound.
