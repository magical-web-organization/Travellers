Introduction to Kourou

Kourou is a beautiful town located in French Guiana, South America. It is the main center for space exploration in the region and is home to the Guiana Space Center. However, there’s much more to Kourou than just space exploration.

From the stunning natural beauty of the rainforest to the rich cultural heritage, Kourou has many hidden gems just waiting to be explored. This comprehensive travel guide to Kourou will help you plan the ultimate trip to this fascinating destination.

List of Places to Visit in Kourou

There are plenty of places to explore in Kourou. To make things easier for you, we’ve divided them into two main categories, Cultural Attractions, and Natural Attractions.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kourou has a rich cultural and historical heritage that you can explore by visiting its museums and historical places.

  1. Guiana Space Center
  2. Kourou Museum of History and Culture
  3. Saint Joseph Church
  4. Fort Diamant

Natural Attractions of Kourou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kourou also has a wealth of natural attractions that are worth discovering. These are the must-visit natural destinations of Kourou:

  1. Iles du Salut
  2. Kourou River
  3. Kaw Marsh

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kourou

Kourou is an excellent destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the best hiking/biking routes of Kourou:

  1. Sentier du Rorota

Best Things to Do in Kourou: Activities and Tours

Local Culture and Customs of Kourou

The culture and customs of Kourou are an amalgamation of French, Creole, and Incan influences. Kourou celebrates its traditions and customs through a range of cultural events and festivals.

Best Time to Visit Kourou

The best time to visit Kourou is during the dry season from May to November, when the weather is warm and sunny, and there are fewer chances of rain.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kourou

Here are some essential Do’s and Don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Kourou:

Do:

  1. Try the local food and drinks
  2. Respect local customs and traditions
  3. Learn some basic French phrases

Don’t:

  1. Wear revealing clothing
  2. Litter
  3. Ignore the natural beauty and cultural heritage of the region

Safety and Health

Kourou is relatively safe for tourists. However, it is always essential to take some precautions to ensure your safety.

Safety in Kourou

  1. Watch your belongings
  2. Be cautious while exploring unknown areas

Health in Kourou

  1. Make sure you get a Yellow Fever vaccination before arriving in Kourou.
  2. Stay hydrated and carry sunscreen when exploring outside.
