Introduction to Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

Located in the heart of French Guiana, South America, Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni is a charming town that offers a unique blend of history, culture, and adventure. The town is home to various museums, historical sites, and natural wonders that make it an ideal destination for travelers seeking a truly authentic experience.

With its beautiful architecture, winding streets, and lively atmosphere, Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni is a place that pleases everyone. Whether you are a history buff, an outdoor enthusiast, or a food lover, this town has something for everyone.

In this travel guide, we will walk you through the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more in Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni. So, let’s get started!

List of Places to Visit in Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

Whether you are looking for a cultural experience or an outdoor adventure, Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni offers plenty of options for visitors to explore. Here are some of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni Museum of History and Ethnography - This museum is dedicated to preserving and promoting the cultural heritage of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni. Visitors can take a walk through the history of the town and explore its rich cultural traditions through various exhibits and collections.

  • Camp de la Transportation - This historical site was once a notorious prison camp that housed convicts during the French colonial period. Today, visitors can tour the site and learn about its dark history.

  • Papaïchton Village - This village is located just outside of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni and offers a unique opportunity to experience the traditional way of life of the Amerindian people who live there. Visitors can learn about their customs, traditions, and way of life.

Natural Attractions of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Maroni River - The Maroni River flows through the heart of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni and offers stunning views of the surrounding rainforest. Visitors can take a boat tour along the river or go fishing.

  • Nouragues Nature Reserve - This vast reserve covers over 100,000 hectares of pristine rainforest and is home to a variety of rare and exotic wildlife. Visitors can take guided tours and explore the hiking trails.

  • Les Cascades de Tonate - These beautiful waterfalls are located just outside of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni and offer a refreshing escape from the heat of the day. Visitors can relax in the cool waters or take a hike through the surrounding rainforest.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

If you’re looking for a bit of adventure, Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni offers plenty of options for hikers and bikers. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Sentier du Rorota - This hiking trail offers stunning views of the Maroni River and is perfect for beginners.

  • Sentier des Cattleyas - This moderate hiking trail takes you through the heart of the Nouragues Nature Reserve and offers a chance to see rare wildlife.

  • Vélo Route de Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni - This 20 km bike route takes you through the heart of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni and offers a chance to explore the town’s beautiful architecture and vibrant culture.

Best Things to Do in Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni: Activities and Tours

Aside from museums, historical sites, and natural wonders, Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni offers a range of activities and tours for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • City Tour of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni - This guided tour takes you through the town’s winding streets and offers a chance to learn about its history and culture.

  • Amerindian Tour - This tour takes you to the nearby Amerindian village of Papaïchton where you can experience the traditional way of life of the Amerindian people.

  • Maroni River Tour - This boat tour takes you along the Maroni River and offers stunning views of the surrounding rainforest.

Food and Drink Scene of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni’s food and drink scene is heavily influenced by its African, European, and Amerindian roots. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Jambalaya - This spicy rice dish is a staple of Creole cuisine and is made with a variety of meats and seafood.

  • Pimentade - This spicy fish stew is made with fresh fish, tomatoes, onions, and a healthy dose of hot peppers.

  • Boudin - This blood sausage is a traditional dish in French Guiana and is made with pig’s blood, spices, and rice.

Local Beverages

  • Rum - French Guiana is known for its rum, which is made from locally grown sugarcane.

  • Koukouy - This beer is brewed in French Guiana and is named after the local firefly.

  • Awara Juice - This refreshing juice is made from the fruit of the awara palm and is a popular drink throughout French Guiana.

Local Culture and Customs of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni’s culture is heavily influenced by its African, European, and Amerindian roots. The town has a rich history and traditions that are still celebrated today. Visitors should respect the town’s customs and traditions and be mindful of local etiquette.

Best Time to Visit Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

The best time to visit Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni is during the dry season, which runs from June to September. The weather during this time is warm and pleasant, and there is less rainfall, making it easier to explore the town’s outdoor attractions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

To ensure a pleasant and respectful stay in Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni, here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Be courteous and polite to locals.
  • Dress appropriately when visiting places of worship or historical sites.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs without permission.
  • Touch sacred objects or monuments.
  • Disrupt religious or cultural ceremonies.

Safety and Health

Safety in Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni is a relatively safe town for visitors, but it is always a good idea to take precautions when traveling. Visitors should be aware of their surroundings and protect their belongings from theft.

Health in Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

Visitors to Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni should take basic health precautions, such as drinking bottled water and wearing insect repellent to prevent mosquito-borne illnesses.

Getting to Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

The nearest airport to Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni is Cayenne - Rochambeau International Airport, which is located approximately 170 km away.

Bus Stations Near Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni is serviced by several bus companies, including Guyane Transport.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni by boat from neighboring towns along the Maroni River.

How to Get Around in Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni

The easiest way to get around Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni is on foot or by bike. Taxis are also available, but they can be expensive.

Where to Go Next from Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore more of French Guiana, here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Cayenne - The capital city of French Guiana is a bustling metropolis that offers a blend of French, African, and Amerindian culture.

  • Kourou - Home to the European Space Agency, Kourou offers a unique blend of science and nature.

  • Îles du Salut - These small islands located off the coast of French Guiana were once a notorious prison colony and today offer a chance to explore the ruins and enjoy the beautiful beaches.

Saint-Laurent-du-Maroni is a fascinating town that offers a range of activities and attractions for visitors to explore.
