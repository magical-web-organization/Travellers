Puerto López is a quaint coastal town in the province of Manabí, Ecuador. From historical sites to stunning beaches, there is something for everyone in Puerto López. In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ll take a look at the must-see attractions, top things to do, popular dishes to try, and much more.

Introduction to Puerto López

Puerto López is a small but vibrant fishing village located on the coast of Ecuador. With its deep-blue waters, white-sand beaches, and year-round sunshine, Puerto López is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers. The town itself is filled with lively markets, delicious traditional restaurants, and friendly locals who are always ready to share a story or two.

List of Places to Visit in Puerto López

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museo Arqueológico - The museum showcases the history and culture of the Manabí region, including an extensive collection of artifacts from the ancient Valdivia culture.

  • Agua Blanca - This pre-Columbian town is a great place to learn more about the local indigenous heritage, including a mystical mud volcano, several hot springs, and a small church.

  • Isla de la Plata - Also known as the “Poor Man’s Galapagos,” this island is home to unique flora and fauna including blue-footed boobies, frigate birds, and sea lions.

Natural Attractions of Puerto López: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Los Frailes Beach - This beautiful secluded beach, located in Machalilla National Park, offers crystal clear waters, golden sand, and a peaceful atmosphere.

  • La Plata Island - A small tour boat can take you to the island, which offers great snorkeling, diving, and hiking.

  • Aqua Party - Take a snorkeling tour of the coral reef and then enjoy a relaxing afternoon on a beautiful beach.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Puerto López

  • The Manaba Rainforest Trail - This trail takes you through the lush rainforest of the Manabí province, giving you an up-close look at the local flora and fauna.

  • La Rinconada - A moderate hike with a beautiful view of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

Best Things to Do in Puerto López: Activities and Tours

  • Whale watching in June - Take a boat tour from Puerto López to spot the migrating humpback whales.

  • Scuba Diving - Explore the beautiful coral reefs, shipwrecks, and underwater caves on the coast of Puerto López.

  • Kayaking on Rio Blanco - This tropical river provides a serene setting for a quiet paddle, surrounded by lush jungle scenery.

Food and Drink Scene of Puerto López

Must-Try Dishes

  • Ceviche - A traditional dish made with fresh raw fish, onions, lime juice, and spices.

  • Arroz con menestra y carne asada - Rice with stewed beans and grilled meat.

  • Bolon de verde - Fried mashed plantains stuffed with cheese, chicharron, or meat.

Local Beverages

  • Canelazo - A warm spiced drink with cinnamon, clove, and aguardiente.

  • Chicha - A fermented drink made from maize, water, and panela (sugar).

Where to Stay in Puerto López: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hostal Kundalini - A budget-friendly accommodation with stunning ocean views, outdoor pool, and terrace.

  • Hotel Machalilla - Located in a quiet neighborhood, the hotel offers comfortable rooms, a courtyard garden, and an on-site restaurant.

  • Mantaraya Lodge - A luxurious eco-lodge with stunning views of the Pacific Ocean, private bungalows, and a pool.

What to Buy in Puerto López: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Tagua carvings - Hand-carved figurines made from tagua, a type of nut.

  • Hamaca hammocks - Traditional hammocks woven by local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Puerto López

Puerto López is a place with a strong culture of fishing and community. Fishermen here go out early in the morning and return with their catch. The community here is very close-knit, and it is common to see neighbors spending time together in the town square.

Best Time to Visit Puerto López

The best time to visit Puerto López is between June and September when the weather is warm and dry, and the humpback whales migrate near the coast. However, the town is delightful to visit year-round.

Do’s and Don’ts of Puerto López

Do:

  • Learn some basic Spanish when visiting.

  • Take time to respect and appreciate the local culture.

  • Be prepared for the hot tropical climate.

Don’t:

  • Wear bathing suits outside of designated beach areas.

  • Take photos of the locals without asking for permission.

  • Hesitate to try the local cuisine.

Safety and Health

Safety in Puerto López

Puerto López is generally a safe place, but like with any traveler destination, it is important to exercise caution. Avoid walking alone late at night, and keep an eye on your valuables.

Health in Puerto López

It’s important to drink bottled water and use insect repellent to avoid mosquito-borne illnesses. Be sure to pack sunscreen to protect yourself in the tropical climate.

Getting to Puerto López: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Puerto López

The nearest airport to Puerto López is Manta Eloy Alfaro International Airport (MEC), which is located about an hour’s drive away.

Bus Stations Near Puerto López

The bus terminal in Jipijapa, about 30 minutes away by car, is the closest major bus station to Puerto López.

Other Options

Several private car services are available as well as taxis and car rentals.

How to Get Around in Puerto López

The preferred mode of transportation in Puerto López is walking, as everything is within a few minutes’ stroll. Taxis and motorbike taxis are also readily available for those who prefer to travel by vehicle.

Where to Go Next from Puerto López: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Manta - Explore the nearby city of Manta, known for its massive statues, beautiful beaches, and local handicrafts.

  • Isla de la Plata - Take a multi-day tour to the Isla de la Plata, known for its blue-footed boobies and beautiful beaches.

  • Salango - Take a quick trip to see the historic archeological sites in Salango, known for their ancient ceramic artifacts.

Conclusion

Puerto López is a true gem on the Ecuadorian coast, offering a wide range of activities, sights, and experiences for travelers of all ages. Whether you’re looking to relax on a pristine beach or hike through the rainforest, Puerto López has something for everyone. Plan your trip with this comprehensive tour guide and make the most of your visit to this charming little town.
