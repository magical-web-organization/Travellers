Introduction to Providencia

Providencia is a bustling neighborhood of Santiago de Chile, located in the Central Valley of Chile. It’s situated close to the downtown area of the city, as well as many interesting landmarks, such as the Cerro San Cristóbal. Providencia is a friendly and safe neighborhood, making it a great choice for travelers of all ages and backgrounds. The weather is mild year-round, with average temperatures ranging from the 50s to the 70s.
