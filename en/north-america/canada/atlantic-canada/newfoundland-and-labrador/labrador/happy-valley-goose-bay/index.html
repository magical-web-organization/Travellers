Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Happy Valley-Goose Bay has a rich cultural heritage that is worth exploring. Here are some of the top museums and historical places that you shouldn’t miss:

  • Labrador Interpretation Centre: This museum offers a fascinating glimpse into the history and culture of Labrador and its indigenous peoples. You can explore the exhibits, watch videos, and enjoy some stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

  • North West River Beach Heritage Park: This beautiful park is a great place to learn about the early history of the indigenous peoples of Labrador. You can explore the well-preserved buildings and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding nature.

  • Craft Council of Newfoundland and Labrador Gallery: This gallery showcases the traditional arts and crafts of Newfoundland and Labrador. You can see some beautiful works of art and even take part in some workshops.

Natural Attractions of Happy Valley-Goose Bay: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Happy Valley-Goose Bay is surrounded by stunning natural beauty that is waiting to be explored. Here are some of the top outdoor adventures and scenic views in the region:

  • Mealy Mountains National Park Reserve: This park offers some of the most beautiful and unspoiled wilderness in the region. You can explore the park’s many trails and enjoy some stunning views of the surrounding mountains and lakes.

  • Ossokmanuan National Park: This park is home to some of the most amazing wildlife in the region, including caribou, moose, and black bears. You can explore the park’s hiking trails and enjoy some breathtaking views of the surrounding nature.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Happy Valley-Goose Bay

Happy Valley-Goose Bay offers some of the best hiking and biking routes in the region. Here are some of the top trails you should check out:

  • Goose River Trail: This 4.5 km trail offers some stunning views of the Goose River and the surrounding wilderness. You can see some beautiful wildlife and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

  • Hamilton River Trail: This 16 km trail offers a challenging but rewarding hiking experience. You can enjoy some amazing views of the Hamilton River and the surrounding mountains.

  • Labrador West Mines Trail: This 90 km trail is a great place to explore the mining history of the region. You can enjoy some beautiful views of the surrounding nature and learn about the history of the local mining industry.

Food and Drink Scene of Happy Valley-Goose Bay

Happy Valley-Goose Bay offers a wide range of traditional dishes and beverages that you can try. Here are some of the must-try dishes and drinks:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Jiggs Dinner: This traditional Newfoundland dish consists of salt beef, cabbage, potatoes, carrots, and turnip.

  • Toutons: These deep-fried dough balls are a Newfoundland favorite and are often served with molasses or maple syrup.

  • Fish and Brewis: This dish consists of salt cod and hardtack biscuits that are soaked in water and served with scrunchions (pieces of fried salt pork).

Local Beverages

  • Screech Rum: This traditional Newfoundland rum is a must-try for anyone looking to experience the local culture.

  • Iceberg Vodka: This vodka is made from icebergs that are harvested off the coast of Newfoundland and Labrador and is a unique and delicious drink.
