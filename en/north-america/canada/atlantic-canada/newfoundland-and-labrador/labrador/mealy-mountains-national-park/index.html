Introduction to Mealy Mountains National Park

Nestled in the heart of Labrador in Atlantic Canada, Mealy Mountains National Park is one of the most stunning natural attractions in the region. The sprawling park covers over 118,000 hectares of pristine wilderness with stunning views, glaciers, and stunning coastline.

List of Places to Visit in Mealy Mountains National Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Visitors to Mealy Mountains National Park can explore several culturally important attractions, including the following museums and historical places:

  • The Them Days museum
  • The Labrador Interpretation Centre
  • Point Amour Lighthouse
  • The Pinware River Provincial Park

Natural Attractions of Mealy Mountains National Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Mealy Mountains National Park is known for its stunning natural beauty, offering visitors plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures and scenic views. Some of the natural attractions of Mealy Mountains National Park that visitors should not miss include:

  • The Eagle River Canyon
  • The Tuckamore Forest
  • The sandy beaches of the coastline
  • The Eastern Habitat Mountains

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mealy Mountains National Park

Mealy Mountains National Park offers visitors a variety of hiking and biking trails that take them through the stunning landscape of the park. Some of the most popular routes for hiking and biking include:

  • The Long Range Traverse
  • The Sand Hill River Trail
  • The Battle Hill Trail
  • The Strathcona Trail

Best Things to Do in Mealy Mountains National Park: Activities and Tours

Safety and Health

Safety in Mealy Mountains National Park

Mealy Mountains National Park is one of the safest nature reserves in the region, but visitors must take care to follow safety guidelines and instructions while exploring the park. Visitors should also take care while hiking or biking along the trails, as some of them are challenging and require caution.

Health in Mealy Mountains National Park

Visitors to Mealy Mountains National Park should ensure they have the necessary vaccinations and medications before traveling.
