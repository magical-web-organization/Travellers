Introduction to Glovertown

Glovertown is a scenic town located in Central Newfoundland in Newfoundland and Labrador, Canada. It is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts, with plenty of opportunities for hiking, biking, and scenic drives. The town features a vibrant local culture, charming accommodations, and delicious food and drink options. Whether you’re looking for a weekend getaway or a longer trip, Glovertown won’t disappoint.

List of Places to Visit in Glovertown

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The James Peyton Heritage Interpretation Center

The James Peyton Heritage Interpretation Center is a must-visit cultural attraction featuring exhibits that tell the story of Glovertown’s early days. The center features interactive exhibits that give visitors a glimpse into the region’s rich history.

The Trinity Historical Society

The Trinity Historical Society is a local organization dedicated to preserving the region’s unique heritage. The society operates several museums, including the Glovertown Museum & Cultural Centre, which offers a fascinating look into local history.

Natural Attractions of Glovertown: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Terra Nova National Park

Terra Nova National Park is a popular destination for travelers seeking outdoor adventures. The park features over 200 square kilometers of wilderness, with hiking trails, bike paths, and stunning scenic views. Visitors can also engage in a variety of water-based activities, such as kayaking and fishing.

Gander River

Gander River is a popular destination for anglers, with its clear, cold waters teeming with trout and salmon. The river is also a great place to hike or bike, with several trails along its banks offering beautiful views of the surrounding area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Glovertown

Terra Nova National Park Hiking Trails

Terra Nova National Park offers a variety of hiking trails that range in difficulty, from easy strolls to challenging treks. The Coastal Trail is a favorite among visitors, offering stunning views of the rugged coastline and the Atlantic Ocean.

The Catamaran Trail

The Catamaran Trail is a popular hiking trail that winds through the dense forest and rugged terrain around Catamaran Park. The trail offers stunning views of the surrounding wilderness, and is perfect for hikers of all skill levels.

Best Things to Do in Glovertown: Activities and Tours

Kayaking and Canoeing

Glovertown’s pristine waterways are perfect for kayaking and canoeing. There are several local outfitters that offer guided tours and equipment rentals, making it easy to explore the region’s unique landscapes by water.

Zip Lining

For those seeking a bit more adventure, zip lining is a thrilling way to experience the stunning beauty of Glovertown. The local area has several zip lining courses that offer stunning views of the surrounding forests and waterways.

Food and Drink Scene of Glovertown

Must-Try Dishes

Fish and Chips

No visit to Glovertown is complete without trying the local fish and chips. The fish is caught fresh from nearby waters and is usually served with crisp French fries and tangy coleslaw.

Moose Burger

Moose burgers are a local delicacy, with juicy patties made from fresh ground moose meat. They’re usually served on a soft bun with all the fixings, making for a hearty and flavorful meal.

Local Beverages

Quidi Vidi Brewery Beer

Quidi Vidi Brewery is a popular local brewery that produces a range of distinctive beers. Their flagship brand is the Quidi Vidi Iceberg Beer, a light and refreshing brew made with water sourced from icebergs.

Screech Rum

Screech rum is a Newfoundland and Labrador tradition, and Glovertown has several bars and pubs that offer this iconic liquor. Be sure to try it in a traditional Screech-In ceremony.

Where to Stay in Glovertown: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Twin Falls Gorge Chalet

The Twin Falls Gorge Chalet is a charming guesthouse located near Terra Nova National Park. The chalet features cozy rooms, all with scenic views of the surrounding wilderness.

The Osprey Trail B&B

The Osprey Trail B&B is a popular bed and breakfast that offers comfortable rooms and a delicious breakfast. The B&B is located near several hiking trails, making it a perfect base for outdoor adventures.

What to Buy in Glovertown: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Traditional Knitted Items

Glovertown has several local markets where visitors can buy traditional knitted items like hats, gloves, and sweaters. These items are made from high-quality wool and are perfect for keeping warm in the colder months.

Local Artwork

Glovertown is home to many talented artists, and visitors can buy a range of unique and beautiful artwork in local galleries and boutiques.

Local Culture and Customs of Glovertown

Traditional Music and Dance

Newfoundland and Labrador has a rich cultural heritage, and Glovertown is no exception. Visitors can experience traditional music and dance through local gatherings and events, including pub nights and kitchen parties.

Friendliness and Hospitality

Glovertown is known for its friendly and hospitable locals, who are always happy to welcome visitors and help them get the most out of their stay.

Best Time to Visit Glovertown

The best time to visit Glovertown is between late spring and early fall (May to early October), when the weather is mild and the outdoor activities are at their best. The fall foliage in particular is spectacular, with the colorful leaves providing a stunning backdrop for hiking and biking trips.

Do’s and Don’ts of Glovertown

Do:

  • Embrace the local culture and try new experiences, such as traditional music and dance
  • Respect the natural environment by practicing Leave No Trace principles
  • Take advantage of the numerous outdoor activities and scenic drives

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave trash behind when enjoying the outdoors
  • Approach wildlife, as this can be dangerous for both humans and animals
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions

Safety and Health

Safety in Glovertown

Glovertown is a safe destination for travelers, with low crime rates and welcoming locals. Visitors should take normal precautions as they would in any other destination, such as locking their cars and securing their belongings.

Health in Glovertown

Glovertown has several medical clinics and pharmacies, as well as a hospital located in nearby Gander. Visitors should ensure they have adequate health insurance coverage and carry any necessary medications with them.

Getting to Glovertown: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Glovertown

The nearest airport to Glovertown is Gander International Airport, which is located approximately 30 minutes away. The airport offers connections to major Canadian cities and some international destinations.

Bus Stations Near Glovertown

Glovertown has a bus station located in the town center. The bus service connects the town to other destinations in Newfoundland and Labrador.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Glovertown, either by renting a car or driving their own. The town is located on the Trans-Canada Highway, making it easily accessible from other parts of the province.

How to Get Around in Glovertown

The best way to get around Glovertown is by car, as the town is relatively spread out and many of the attractions are located outside of the town center. Visitors can rent a car at Gander International Airport or in nearby Gander.

Where to Go Next from Glovertown: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Gander

Gander is a nearby town located approximately 30 minutes away from Glovertown. It is home to several museums, including the North Atlantic Aviation Museum, and offers a range of dining and shopping options.

Twillingate

Twillingate is a picturesque town located on the northeast coast of Newfoundland, approximately 2 hours’ drive from Glovertown. It is known for its stunning coastal scenery and is a popular destination for whale watching and iceberg viewing.

Conclusion

Glovertown is a hidden gem in Newfoundland and Labrador, with plenty of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and delicious food and drink options. With this comprehensive guide, visitors can plan the perfect trip to experience all that this charming town has to offer.
