Introduction to Port Hawkesbury and Port Hastings

Port Hawkesbury and Port Hastings are two neighboring towns located in Cape Breton Island, in Nova Scotia, in Atlantic Canada, in Canada, in North America. The two towns lie on opposite sides of the Canso Causeway, which connects Cape Breton Island to mainland Nova Scotia. Port Hawkesbury is the larger of the two towns, while Port Hastings has a population of around 1,000.

Despite being small towns, Port Hawkesbury and Port Hastings offer a variety of attractions for visitors to enjoy. From museums and historical places to outdoor adventures and scenic views, there is plenty to explore. In this travel guide, we will take you through some of the must-visit places and experiences in Port Hawkesbury and Port Hastings.
