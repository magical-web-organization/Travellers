Introduction to Fundy National Park

Welcome to the stunning Fundy National Park, located in the gorgeous Bay of Fundy of New Brunswick, Canada. The park features 206 square kilometers of natural beauty, which includes 120 kilometers of scenic coastline with stunning views of the Bay of Fundy, the highest tides in the world, pristine forests, and winding rivers. The park offers a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, canoeing, kayaking, fishing, bird watching, and more. This comprehensive travel guide will provide you with all the information you need to plan an unforgettable trip to Fundy National Park.

Local Culture and Customs of Fundy National Park

The people of Fundy National Park are friendly and welcoming. Most locals speak English but may also speak French, as New Brunswick is a bilingual province. The park is committed to protecting the natural environment and visitors are expected to follow the Leave No Trace principles and respect the local culture and customs.

Best Time to Visit Fundy National Park

The best time to visit Fundy National Park is from mid-May to mid-October, when the park’s facilities and attractions are open, and the weather is pleasant. However, the features of the park are unique year-round, so it’s worth planning to visit at any time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Fundy National Park

Do:

  1. Respect the natural environment and avoid leaving any traces behind.
  2. Stay on marked trails.
  3. Use bear-proof containers to store food and trash.

Don’t:

  1. Feed wildlife.
  2. Stray from marked trails.
  3. Use plastic bags as food storge.

Safety and Health

Safety in Fundy National Park

  1. Always pack warm and waterproof clothing layers - weather can change suddenly.
  2. Never hike alone, especially during the off-season.
  3. Mind your step on rocky and slippery trails.

Health in Fundy National Park

  1. Bring sunscreen and a hat to protect exposed skin during long hikes.
  2. Treat freshwater sources before drinking to avoid waterborne diseases.
  3. Bring anti-poison ivy medication and watch out for the poisonous plant.
