Introduction to Machias Seal Island

Machias Seal Island is a tiny, remote island situated in the Bay of Fundy, off the coast of New Brunswick, Atlantic Canada, in North America. The island is only 19 acres in size but is home to thousands of Atlantic puffins, razorbills, and common murres, making it a top destination for birdwatchers and nature lovers.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Despite its small size, Machias Seal Island has a rich history and cultural significance. Visitors can discover this through visiting the island’s interpretive panels or participating in a guided tour.

Natural Attractions of Machias Seal Island: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Machias Seal Island is an excellent destination for those seeking outdoor adventures or stunning scenic views. The island’s rugged coastline offers plenty of opportunities for hiking, biking, and kayaking while taking in the breathtaking views of the Gulf of Maine.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Machias Seal Island

One of the best ways to experience the natural beauty of the island is through hiking or biking. The Machias Seal Island trail system offers a variety of routes ranging in difficulty, allowing visitors to explore the island’s diverse terrain.

Best Things to Do in Machias Seal Island: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, Machias Seal Island offers a variety of activities and tours, including birdwatching, guided nature walks, lighthouse tours, and wildlife photography expeditions.

Food and Drink Scene of Machias Seal Island

Machias Seal Island is a remote island with limited dining options. However, visitors can still enjoy fresh seafood and local beverage options during their stay on the island.

Must-Try Dishes

When visiting Machias Seal Island, be sure to try the freshly caught lobster and other seafood dishes.

Local Beverages

Machias Seal Island does not have a brewery or winery on the island. However, visitors can still enjoy local and regional beer or wine at nearby establishments on the mainland.

Where to Stay in Machias Seal Island: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Machias Seal Island is a wildlife sanctuary; therefore, no overnight stays are allowed on the island. However, there are nearby options available, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses, offering easy access to the island.

What to Buy in Machias Seal Island: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When visiting Machias Seal Island, take home souvenirs such as photographs, postcards, T-shirts, pins, or other locally-made products that celebrate the island’s natural beauty and wildlife. These can be found at nearby gift shops or on the mainland.

Local Culture and Customs of Machias Seal Island

Machias Seal Island is a wildlife sanctuary, and visitors are expected to respect the island’s natural environment and abide by rules and regulations implemented to protect the wildlife. Visitors must also be aware of and respectful of the island’s First Nations heritage and culture.

Best Time to Visit Machias Seal Island

The best time to visit Machias Seal Island is during the summer months of June to August, when the weather is mild, and the island’s puffins and other seabirds are nesting.

Do’s and Don’ts of Machias Seal Island

Safety and Health

Safety in Machias Seal Island

Visitors to Machias Seal Island should be aware of the natural hazards that come with exploring a remote island. They should wear appropriate footwear and clothing, be cautious on uneven terrain, and have essential first aid and rescue skills.

Health in Machias Seal Island

Visitors to Machias Seal Island should carry their medications and priority medications for injuries or illness.

Getting to Machias Seal Island: Transportation Options and Logistics

Machias Seal Island is only accessible by boat, with tours departing from mainland communities. Visitors should book their tour well in advance as only a limited amount of tour operators are available.

Airports Near Machias Seal Island

The nearest airport to Machias Seal Island is Eastport Municipal Airport in Eastport, Maine, United States, followed by Fredericton International Airport in Fredericton, New Brunswick, Canada.

Bus Stations Near Machias Seal Island

There are no bus stations near Machias Seal Island. However, visitors can access areas close to the island by bus.

Other Options

Visitors can also opt for private chartered flights to nearby airports or rent boats to reach Machias Seal Island.

How to Get Around in Machias Seal Island

Machias Seal Island is only 19 acres, and visitors can explore the island on foot, following the hiking/biking trail system.
