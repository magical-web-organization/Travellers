Food and Drink Scene of Tantramar

Tantamar has a unique food and drink scene, best known for fresh seafood and locally-sourced ingredients. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lobster Rolls: Discover the taste of Tantamar’s unique lobster rolls. Prepared with freshly caught lobster and locally-made rolls!
  • Fiddlehead Greens: This wild green is a favourite in Tantamar, served lightly sautéed.
  • Poutine Râpée: A classic Acadian comfort food made with grated potatoes filled with pork, beef or chicken.

Local Beverages

  • Wine and Beer: Try locally brewed beer and wine at Tantamar’s own microbreweries and wineries.
  • Cider: Some Tantamar establishments offer locally-made cider using local apples.

Where to Stay in Tantramar: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

The region has plenty of options for hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are some great choices:

  • Coastal Inn Sackville: Affordable and comfortable, this hotel has great views of the ocean.
  • Marshlands Inn: Located in an historic mansion, this inn offers a unique and charming stay.
  • Mount Allison University: During the summer months, visitors can stay in the university’s dorms at an affordable rate.

What to Buy in Tantramar: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Looking for a unique souvenir to bring home from Tantamar? Here are some ideas:

  • Handmade baskets and pottery from local artisans
  • Maple Syrup: Produced from nearby sugar shacks, you’ll find this delicious syrup in various packaging, from plastic jugs to glass flasks.
  • Wild berry preserves: Local artisanal jams and jellies are sold throughout the area.

Local Culture and Customs of Tantramar

Tantamar has a deep connection to its Acadian roots; many of the locals speak both French and English. It’s important to respect the customs and traditions of the area, which include:

  • Acadian French: Many locals speak both English and French, and it’s important to respect this cultural heritage.
  • Local Music: Traditional Acadian music is well loved and celebrated in the area.
  • The Shorebird Migration: Tantramar is a sanctuary for migrating shorebirds in the fall, and locals take great pride in protecting these birds.

Best Time to Visit Tantramar

Tantamar is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit depends on your interests. Late summer and early fall are ideal seasons for birdwatching as the shorebird migration is in full swing and the forests offer beautiful foliage. Summer is a fantastic time to explore the beaches and local markets, while winter is perfect for snowmobiling and cross-country skiing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tantramar

To make the most of your Tantramar trip and stay respectful to locals, follow these do’s and don’ts:

Do:

  • Be respectful of the local language and customs
  • Dress appropriately for the season and activities you plan to do
  • Take time to get to know the local people and culture

Don’t:

  • Litter in outdoor spaces
  • Be loud or disrespectful in public places
  • Overlook local businesses, such as locally owned restaurants and shops.

Safety and Health

Tantamar is a relatively safe place to travel, but it’s still important to take precautions for your health and safety.

Safety in Tantamar

  • Use common sense and be aware of your surroundings, especially at night.
  • Take necessary safety precautions for water activities and adventures such as kayaking, zip-lining, to avoid injuries.

Health in Tantamar

  • Bring sunscreen and insect repellent especially for outdoor activities.
  • In emergencies, dial 911

Getting to Tantramar: Transportation Options and Logistics

Tantamar is easily accessible by air and by car. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Tantramar

  • Moncton International Airport is the closest and offers direct flights to several Canadian cities.

Bus Stations Near Tantramar

  • Maritime Bus offers services to Sackville and Amherst in Tantramar region, and other towns in the Maritimes.

Other Options

  • Taxis are available in the area, but it’s best to call ahead to ensure availability.

How to Get Around in Tantramar

Driving in Tantramar is the most convenient way to get around. Bike rentals are available from local businesses.

Where to Go Next from Tantramar: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re planning a longer trip, there are plenty of nearby destinations worth exploring! Here are some possibilities:

  • Hopewell Rocks: Check out the famous rock formations and experience the world’s highest tides!
  • Fundy Trail Parkway: Offering beautiful coastal scenery, hiking paths and wildlife sightings.
  • Les P’tits Coeurs de Jesus Province: Discover local culture and enjoy some of Canada’s best French cuisine.

Conclusion

Tantamar is a little slice of paradise in Atlantic Canada, offering something for everyone in a picturesque and charming setting. Be sure to follow these tips and suggestions, and enjoy your time in this beautiful region of New Brunswick.
