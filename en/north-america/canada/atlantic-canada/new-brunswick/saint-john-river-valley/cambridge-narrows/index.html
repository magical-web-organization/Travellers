Introduction to Cambridge-Narrows

Cambridge-Narrows is a charming town located in the Saint John River Valley of New Brunswick, Canada. This picturesque town has a population of just over 400 and is known for its stunning natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and rich cultural heritage. Whether you’re looking to explore the great outdoors, immerse yourself in local culture, or simply relax and take in the serene surroundings, Cambridge-Narrows is the perfect destination for you.
