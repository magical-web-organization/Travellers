Introduction to Nackawic

Located in the Saint John River Valley of New Brunswick, Nackawic is a picturesque town in Atlantic Canada. Known for its stunning natural beauty and small-town charm, Nackawic offers a range of experiences for all types of travelers. Whether you’re seeking outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, or simply a peaceful retreat, Nackawic has something for you.

List of Places to Visit in Nackawic

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • W.W. Boyce Potato House: Built in 1912, this historic building is now home to the Nackawic Potato World museum, which showcases the region’s potato farming heritage.
  • Nackawic Heritage Centre: This museum displays the town’s history, including various artifacts, photographs, and documents from the past.
  • St. John the Baptist Anglican Church: Built in 1888, this beautiful church features Gothic Revival architecture and stained-glass windows.

Natural Attractions of Nackawic: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Nackawic Walking Trail: This 4 km trail is a great option for a leisurely walk or jog and offers stunning views of the Nackawic stream.
  • Lower Kintore Covered Bridge: This historic wooden bridge is located on Lower Kintore Road and offers scenic views of the Saint John River Valley.
  • MC Culloch Pond: This tranquil spot is perfect for fishing, kayaking, or simply relaxing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Nackawic

  • Nackawic-Devon Trail: This 24-km trail offers scenic views of the Saint John River and an opportunity to explore the Nackawic countryside.
  • Washademoak Loop: This 73-km trail is a great option for more experienced bikers and offers stunning views of the Washademoak Lake.

Best Things to Do in Nackawic: Activities and Tours

  • Nackawic Potato World: Learn about the potato, one of New Brunswick’s most important crops.
  • River Run Adventures: Enjoy a paddleboarding or kayaking excursion on the Saint John River.
  • Nackawic Golf and Country Club: Tee off at this beautiful golf course surrounded by natural beauty.

Week-Long Itinerary for Nackawic

Day Activity
1 Explore the Nackawic Walking Trail and enjoy lunch at one of the local cafes.
2 Visit the W.W Boyce Potato House and take a stroll along the Lower Kintore Covered Bridge.
3 Go on a kayaking excursion with River Run Adventures and enjoy a picnic by the Nackawic stream.
4 Go golfing at the Nackawic Golf and Country Club followed by a relaxing spa treatment at a local spa.
5 Explore the Nackawic Heritage Centre and visit the St. John the Baptist Anglican Church.
6 Take a scenic drive along the Washademoak Loop and stop at the scenic lookout areas.
7 Spend the day fishing or relaxing by the MC Culloch Pond.

Food and Drink Scene of Nackawic

Must-Try Dishes

  • Poutine: A local favorite, this dish features crispy french fries topped with cheese curds and gravy.
  • Fish and Chips: Made with local fresh fish and served with homemade tartar sauce, this dish is a must-try for seafood lovers.
  • Lobster Roll: Served on a hot dog bun, this delicious sandwich is filled with fresh lobster meat and a light dressing of mayo and lemon.

Local Beverages

  • Picaroons Traditional Ales: One of the famous local breweries, Picaroons offers a range of craft beers.
  • Petit-Sault Brewers: This microbrewery offers a range of beers, including a Belgian-style witbier that’s a must-try.
  • Wine and Spirits: New Brunswick produces a range of wines, ciders, and spirits. Be sure to sample some of the local varieties during your trip.

Where to Stay in Nackawic: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Lakeway Suites: Located near the Nackawic Golf and Country Club, Lakeway Suites offers spacious and comfortable accommodation complete with kitchens and living areas.
  • Nackawic Motor Inn: This budget-friendly option offers clean and comfortable rooms with free Wi-Fi.
  • McCain Hill Guest Suite: This cozy guest suite is located near the town center and features a private entrance and free parking.

What to Buy in Nackawic: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Potatoes: New Brunswick is famous for its potatoes, so why not take some home as a souvenir?
  • Maple Syrup: Another local specialty, maple syrup is a great souvenir to bring back from your trip.
  • Handcrafted Gifts: Nackawic is home to many talented local artisans, so be sure to check out their handcrafted gifts and souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Nackawic

  • Language: English is the primary language spoken in Nackawic.
  • Tipping: Tipping is customary in restaurants and bars in Nackawic, typically 15-20% depending on the service received.
  • Dress Code: Nackawic is a casual town, and most places have relaxed dress codes.

Best Time to Visit Nackawic

The best time to visit Nackawic is from May to October when the weather is warm and pleasant. Summer months are the busiest, so be sure to book your accommodation in advance.

Do’s and Don’ts of Nackawic

Do:

  • Do explore the town on foot or by bike.
  • Do try the local cuisine.
  • Do be respectful of the locals and their culture.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter.
  • Do not be disrespectful to the locals or their culture.
  • Do not ignore safety precautions when engaging in outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Nackawic

Nackawic is a safe town with low crime rates. However, it’s always important to take precautions when traveling, such as keeping an eye on your belongings and not walking alone at night.

Health in Nackawic

In case of a medical emergency, contact 911 or go to the nearest hospital. The Nackawic Medical Clinic is a great option for non-emergency medical needs.

Getting to Nackawic: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Nackawic

  • Fredericton International Airport: 74 km away.
  • Greater Moncton Roméo LeBlanc International Airport: 192 km away.
  • Halifax Stanfield International Airport: 250 km away.

Bus Stations Near Nackawic

  • Maritime Bus: Maritime offers regular bus services to Nackawic from various destinations across New Brunswick and Nova Scotia.

Other Options

  • Rental Cars: Renting a car is a great option for exploring Nackawic and the surrounding areas.
  • Taxi Services: Nackawic has several taxi services that can be hired for transportation.

How to Get Around in Nackawic

Nackawic is a small town with most attractions located within walking distance of each other. Biking and driving are also great options for exploring the town and surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Nackawic: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Fredericton: Capital city of New Brunswick, Fredericton offers a range of cultural attractions and outdoor activities and is located about an hour’s drive from Nackawic.
  • Fundy National Park: This stunning national park located about two hours’ drive from Nackawic offers hiking, camping, and wildlife viewing opportunities.
  • Hopewell Rocks: These unusual rock formations are located about two hours’ drive from Nackawic and offer stunning views of the Bay of Fundy.

Conclusion

Nackawic is a beautiful and charming town that offers a range of attractions, experiences, and adventures for all types of travelers. With its stunning natural beauty, rich history, and warm hospitality, Nackawic is a true gem in Atlantic Canada. Plan your trip today and discover the beauty of this quaint town.
