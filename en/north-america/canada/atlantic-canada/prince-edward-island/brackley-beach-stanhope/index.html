Introduction to Brackley Beach-Stanhope

Brackley Beach-Stanhope is a picturesque coastal destination located in the beautiful province of Prince Edward Island, Atlantic Canada. This area is known for its breathtaking beaches, rolling hills, and idyllic scenery, making it a top choice for travelers seeking a serene and peaceful getaway.

Getting to Brackley Beach-Stanhope: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Brackley Beach-Stanhope

The nearest international airport is Charlottetown Airport, which is about 12 km from Brackley Beach-Stanhope.

Bus Stations Near Brackley Beach-Stanhope

Maritime Bus is the main bus company serving Prince Edward Island and has connections to Charlottetown, Summerside, and surrounding areas.

Other Options

Car rentals from Charlottetown Airport are available and can be booked in advance. Taxis are also available.

How to Get Around in Brackley Beach-Stanhope

The best way to get around Brackley Beach-Stanhope is by car or bicycle. Several car rental and bike rental companies are available in the area.
