Introduction to Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

Located in the Maskinongé Region of Mauricie, Central Quebec, Saint-Alexis-des-Monts boasts stunning natural landscapes, exciting outdoor adventures, and rich cultural heritage. Its location on the shores of Lac Saint-Alexis makes it a popular destination for fishing and water sports enthusiasts. Add to that the region’s picturesque forests, gentle hills, and untouched wilderness and it’s easy to see why Saint-Alexis-des-Monts is a top destination in Quebec.

List of Places to Visit in Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Musée des Abénakis: This museum showcases the history, culture, and traditions of the Abenaki people who have lived in Quebec for centuries. Visitors can explore exhibits on the tribe’s traditions, language, spirituality, and art.
  • La Maison Antoine-Lacombe: This old stone house dates back to the 1850s and has been turned into a museum. It is home to exhibits on the history of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts, as well as the arts and crafts of the region.
  • La Seigneurie des Patriotes: This grand old mansion was once home to a wealthy landowner and political activist during the Patriote Rebellion of 1837-38. Today, it has been restored to its former glory and offers guided tours of its opulent interiors.

Natural Attractions of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lac Saint-Alexis: This picturesque lake is the main attraction of the region, and rightfully so. Visitors can rent boats, kayaks, and canoes for a leisurely paddle on its calm waters, or try their hand at fishing.
  • Parc régional Mastigouche: This vast park is a playground for outdoor enthusiasts. Visitors can go camping, hiking, biking, and horseback riding on its trails, or take a dip in one of its many lakes and rivers.
  • Les Chutes à Magnan: These stunning waterfalls cascade down a granite escarpment, offering a breathtaking view of the surrounding forest. The area around the falls is perfect for a peaceful picnic.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

  • Sentier National: This 1,000-km trail runs through the beautiful forests of Quebec, including Saint-Alexis-des-Monts. It offers hikers and bikers a chance to experience some of the most picturesque landscapes in the region.
  • Le Circuit des Trois Montagnes: This 30-km trail is perfect for mountain bikers looking for a challenge. It winds through the hills of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • La Traversée de la Mauricie: This 86-km hiking and biking trail takes visitors through the heart of the Mauricie region, including Saint-Alexis-des-Monts. It’s a great way to experience the culture and natural beauty of the region in one trip.

Best Things to Do in Saint-Alexis-des-Monts: Activities and Tours

  • Water Sports: Saint-Alexis-des-Monts is a hub for water sports enthusiasts. Visitors can rent a boat, kayak, or paddleboard to explore Lac Saint-Alexis or try their hand at fishing.
  • Helicopter Tours: For a bird’s-eye view of the region, visitors can take a helicopter tour over the forested hills and crystal-clear lakes of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts.
  • Spas and Wellness: The region is home to a number of spas and wellness centers, offering visitors the chance to relax and unwind after a day of outdoor activities.

Week-Long Itinerary for Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

  • Day 1: Explore the historic sites of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts, including La Maison Antoine-Lacombe and La Seigneurie des Patriotes.
  • Day 2: Spend the day on the water, renting a boat or kayak to explore the calm waters of Lac Saint-Alexis.
  • Day 3: Hike or bike the Sentier National, taking in the stunning natural landscapes of the region along the way.
  • Day 4: Go fishing on one of the many lakes and rivers in the region, or take a guided fishing tour.
  • Day 5: Take a helicopter tour over the hills and lakes of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts for a unique view of the region.
  • Day 6: Relax at one of the area’s spas, enjoying massages and other wellness treatments.
  • Day 7: Take a day trip to explore nearby Mauricie National Park, one of Quebec’s most beautiful natural treasures.

Food and Drink Scene of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

Must-Try Dishes

  • Poutine: A Quebec classic, poutine is a dish made with french fries, cheese curds, and gravy.
  • Meat Pie: A flavorful dish made with ground pork, beef, or game meat, encased in a flaky pastry.
  • Tourtière: Another iconic Quebec dish, tourtière is a savory meat pie typically made with ground pork and/or beef, potatoes, onions, and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Unibroue Beer: This popular Quebec-based brewery offers a range of delicious Belgian-style beers.
  • Ice Cider: Made from frozen apples, this sweet dessert wine is a local specialty not to be missed.
  • Caribou: A warming winter drink made with red wine, whiskey, and spices, perfect for a cold night around the fire.

Where to Stay in Saint-Alexis-des-Monts: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Auberge du Lac-à-l’Eau-Claire: This luxurious hotel on the shores of Lac-à-l’Eau-Claire offers a range of activities and amenities, including spa treatments and fine dining.
  • Parc St-Alexis: This camping and RV site is the perfect choice for visitors who want to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of the region.
  • La Porte de la Mauricie: This cozy bed and breakfast offers comfortable rooms and a delicious breakfast to fuel your adventures in Saint-Alexis-des-Monts.

What to Buy in Saint-Alexis-des-Monts: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Maple Syrup: Quebec is known for its delicious maple syrup. Visitors can find a range of brands and varieties at local shops and markets.
  • Local Art and Crafts: From jewelry to pottery to paintings, the region is home to many talented artists and artisans.
  • Ice Cider: This sweet dessert wine is a unique and delicious souvenir to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

Saint-Alexis-des-Monts is proud of its rich cultural heritage, which is reflected in the many historic sites, museums, and art galleries found in the region. Visitors should also be aware of the importance of the French language in Quebec, as it is the official language of the province.

Best Time to Visit Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

The best time to visit Saint-Alexis-des-Monts is during the summer months (June to September) when the weather is warm and sunny. However, visitors can also enjoy the region’s winter wonderland from December to February, with plenty of opportunities for skiing, snowshoeing, and other winter sports.

Do’s and Don’ts of Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine, including poutine, tourtière, and ice cider.
  • Do bring appropriate gear for the outdoor activities you plan to do.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget to pack sunscreen and insect repellent.
  • Don’t leave any litter behind when exploring the natural beauty of the region.

Safety and Health

Safety in Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

Saint-Alexis-des-Monts is a safe destination for travelers. However, visitors should take precautions when hiking in the wilderness, including staying on marked trails and carrying appropriate gear.

Health in Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

There are no major health risks in Saint-Alexis-des-Monts, but visitors should pack any necessary medications and consult with a physician before traveling if they have any health concerns.

Getting to Saint-Alexis-des-Monts: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

  • Trois-Rivières Airport: Located about one hour from Saint-Alexis-des-Monts, this airport offers flights to several domestic destinations.
  • Montreal-Pierre Elliott Trudeau International Airport: This major airport is located about two hours from Saint-Alexis-des-Monts and offers flights to many domestic and international destinations.

Bus Stations Near Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

  • Saint-Tite: This nearby town offers bus connections to several destinations in Quebec.
  • Trois-Rivières: This larger city offers bus options to destinations across Quebec and beyond.

Other Options

  • Driving: Saint-Alexis-des-Monts is easily reached by car, with several major highways connecting it to nearby cities and towns.
  • Train: The closest train station is located in Trois-Rivières, with regular connections to Montreal and other destinations in Quebec.

How to Get Around in Saint-Alexis-des-Monts

The best way to get around Saint-Alexis-des-Monts is by car, as many of the attractions and outdoor activities are spread throughout the region. However, visitors can also rent bicycles or take taxis for shorter distances.

Where to Go Next from Saint-Alexis-des-Monts: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Mauricie National Park: A short drive from Saint-Alexis-des-Monts, this vast park offers stunning natural landscapes and excellent hiking trails.
  • Trois-Rivières: This historic city on the St. Lawrence River offers a range of cultural attractions, including museums, theaters, and galleries.
  • Quebec City: Canada’s only walled city, Quebec City is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must-see destination for history buffs and culture enthusiasts.

Conclusion

With its picturesque natural landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and exciting outdoor activities, Saint-Alexis-des-Monts is a top destination in Quebec. From the stunning waters of Lac Saint-Alexis to the majestic forests of the region’s parks, visitors will find no shortage of things to see and do. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an adrenaline-fueled adventure, Saint-Alexis-des-Monts is the perfect destination.
