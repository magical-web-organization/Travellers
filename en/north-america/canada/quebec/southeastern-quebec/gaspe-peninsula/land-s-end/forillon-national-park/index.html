Introduction to Forillon National Park

Located at Land’s End, in Gaspé Peninsula, in Southeastern Quebec, in Quebec, in Canada, in North America, Forillon National Park is one of the most stunning natural wonders that you will ever come across. The park is known for its scintillating hiking and biking trails, picturesque views, and an array of cultural and natural attractions.

Local Culture and Customs of Forillon National Park

The local culture and customs of Forillon National Park are deeply rooted in the French-Canadian heritage and the natural surroundings of the park. When visiting, you should try to learn some French phrases and be respectful to the locals and the environment.

Best Time to Visit Forillon National Park

The best time to visit Forillon National Park is during the summer months, from June to September, when the weather is mild, and all attractions are open. However, if you’re up for a winter adventure, the park also offers cross-country skiing and snowshoeing during the winter months.

Safety and Health
