Introduction to Chaleur Bay

Chaleur Bay is a stunning destination located in the Gaspé Peninsula in Southeastern Quebec, Canada. It is a vast semi-circular bay that is encompassed by mountains, forests, crystal-clear water, and picturesque towns. Known for its breathtaking scenery, exceptional outdoor activities, and rich history, Chaleur Bay is a must-visit for any travel enthusiast.
