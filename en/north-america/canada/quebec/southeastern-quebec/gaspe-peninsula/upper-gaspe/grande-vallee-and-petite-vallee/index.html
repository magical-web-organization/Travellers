Introduction to Grande-Vallée and Petite-Vallée

Grande-Vallée and Petite-Vallée are two beautiful villages situated in Upper Gaspé, in Gaspé Peninsula, in Southeastern Quebec, in Quebec, in Canada, in North America. These two villages are known for their picturesque landscapes, stunning beaches, and charming culture. They are perfect holiday destinations for those looking to experience outdoor adventure and seek peace and quiet amidst natural beauty.
