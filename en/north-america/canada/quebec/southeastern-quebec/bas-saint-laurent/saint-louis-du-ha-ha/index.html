Introduction to Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha! is a charming town located in the Bas-Saint-Laurent region of Québec, Canada. Despite its funny name (which means ‘Saint-Louis of the Ha! Ha!’ in French), this place offers a wide range of cultural, historical, and natural attractions, making it a must-visit destination in Eastern Canada. In this travel guide, we’ll explore the best things to do in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!, including outdoor adventures, scenic views, cultural landmarks, food, and drink scene, and travel logistics. Let’s get started!

List of Places to Visit in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Saint-Louis Church: a beautiful neo-Gothic style church built in the 19th century with intricate stained-glass windows and enormous wooden doors.
  • Musée de la Mémoire Vivante: a museum dedicated to showcasing the personal testimonies and artifacts of local people and their families, illustrating the rural life and agricultural heritage of the region.
  • Murals of Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!: walk around and explore the colorful murals plastered on various buildings throughout the town, portraying the local history and culture.

Natural Attractions of Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Parc National du Bic: located about 30km from Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!, this park has spectacular hiking trails along the limestone cliffs, offering stunning views of the Saint Lawrence River and abundant wildlife like seals, porcupines, and eagles.
  • Véloroute des Basques: a 78 km cycling trail passing through the picturesque landscapes of Bas-Saint-Laurent region, including Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!.
  • Route des Navigateurs: also known as the ‘Navigator’s Route,’ offers a scenic drive along the St. Lawrence River, passing through picturesque towns, historic sites, and stunning viewpoints.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

Hiking Routes

  • Sentier des Caps: a 2km boardwalk trail along the marshes, ponds, and fields between Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha! and Dégelis, offering bird-watching opportunities and beautiful scenery.
  • Sentier de la Rivière: a 2km loop trail along the Rivière Touladi, surrounded by lush green forests and small lakes, perfect for a peaceful walk or picnics.

Biking Routes

  • La Véloroute des Baleines: a 400 km cycling trail along the St. Lawrence River that passes through Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!.
  • La Route Verte: a network of cycling trails connecting all of Québec’s regions, offering breathtaking scenery while cycling.

Best Things to Do in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!: Activities and Tours

  • Whale Watching: explore the nearby St. Lawrence River to observe the incredible marine life, including beluga whales and minke whales.
  • Kayaking: see the stunning coastlines and forests from a different perspective by kayaking through the nature reserves near the town.
  • Guided Tours: learn more about Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!’s history, culture, and architecture by taking a guided walking or biking tour through the town.

Week-Long Itinerary for Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

  • Day 1: Arrive in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha! and explore the town’s murals and local shops.
  • Day 2: Visit the Musée de la Mémoire Vivante and take a guided tour of the historical landmarks in the town.
  • Day 3: Go whale-watching on the St. Lawrence River.
  • Day 4: Hike on the Sentier de la Rivière trail and have a picnic in nature.
  • Day 5: Cycle along the Véloroute des Baleines.
  • Day 6: Visit Parc National du Bic and hike along the cliffs and shores of the St. Lawrence River.
  • Day 7: Relax and enjoy the natural beauty of Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha! before departing.

Food and Drink Scene of Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

Must-Try Dishes

  • Poutine: a classic Québécois dish made with french fries, cheese curds, and gravy. Try it at Poutineville, a local eatery.
  • Tarte aux Bleuets: a local blueberry pie that you can find at many bakeries and cafes in the town.
  • Smoked Salmon Plate: a specialty of this region, served with cream cheese and bagel, you’ll find it in the local hotels.

Local Beverages

  • Microbrasserie Le Ketch: a local brewery that produces craft beers with a variety of flavors, including a blonde ale, a pale ale, and an IPA.
  • La Boul’verte: a juice bar inspired by the flavors and scents of the nearby orchards in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

Where to Stay in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Auberge du Portage: a cozy inn located on the shores of the St. Lawrence River, offering stunning views and classic rooms.
  • Gîte du Moulin: a traditional Quebecois style B&B that offers a charming countryside experience.
  • Hotel Saint-Louis: a modern hotel located in the heart of the town and offering comfortable accommodation.

What to Buy in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Blueberry Jams and Syrups: buy some delicious blueberry jams and syrups that are locally made and sold at stores throughout the town.
  • Local Crafts and Artwork: visit the local artisan shops and markets to buy some local crafts and handmade artwork as souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha! is a town proud of its cultural heritage and agricultural roots. Visitors are encouraged to respect the local traditions and customs, such as taking off shoes before entering homes or places of worship. Respect privacy and refrain from taking photographs without permission.

Best Time to Visit Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

The best time to visit Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha! is from June to September when the weather is warm, and the outdoor activities and natural attractions are in full swing. The autumn months also offer beautiful foliage, while the winter months offer snow sports like snowshoeing and skiing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Spend time hiking and biking through the nature reserves and parks.
  • Take a guided tour to fully appreciate the local history and culture.

Don’t:

  • Litter in the natural areas.
  • Bring pets without prior permission.
  • Take pictures of the locals without their permission.

Safety in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

Health in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

Medical facilities are available in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha! in case of emergencies. It’s always important to bring any necessary medication and purchase travel insurance before embarking on your journey.

Getting to Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!: Transportation Options and Logistics

How to Get Around in Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!

The best way to explore Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha! is on foot or bike. Many of the town’s attractions and landmarks are within walking or biking distance of one another, making it an ideal way to see the town at a leisurely pace.

Where to Go Next from Saint-Louis-du-Ha! Ha!: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Conclusion
