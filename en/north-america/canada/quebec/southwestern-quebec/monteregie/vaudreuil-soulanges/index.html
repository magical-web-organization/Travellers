Introduction to Vaudreuil-Soulanges

Vaudreuil-Soulanges is a picturesque region in Montérégie, Southwestern Quebec, Canada. It’s a popular destination for tourists seeking outdoor adventure and scenic views. The region is rich in history, culture, and natural beauty, which makes it an ideal destination for travelers of all ages. In this guide, we’ll explore the best things to do, places to visit, and food to eat in Vaudreuil-Soulanges.

List of Places to Visit in Vaudreuil-Soulanges

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Vaudreuil-Soulanges is home to several culturally rich destinations that offer an insight into the region’s history and heritage.

Musée de Vaudreuil-Soulanges

Located in Vaudreuil-Dorion, the Musée de Vaudreuil-Soulanges is a cultural hub that showcases the history and traditions of the region. Visitors can explore the various exhibits and collections related to agriculture, industry, and local artwork.

Lieu historique national du Canada du Canal-de-Soulanges

The Canal-de-Soulanges is a national historic site that played a significant role in the economic development of Canada. It’s an ideal destination for those who love boating, picnicking, and outdoor activities. Visitors can rent a boat, canoe, or kayak and explore the canal while admiring the stunning views.

Natural Attractions of Vaudreuil-Soulanges: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Vaudreuil-Soulanges boasts beautiful natural landscapes that are perfect for outdoor adventures and scenic drives. Here are some of the top attractions:

Parc nature les Forestiers-de-Saint-Lazare

The Parc nature les Forestiers-de-Saint-Lazare is a great place to hike, bike, or walk in the forest. The park features several kilometers of trails, ponds, and forests that are home to a diversity of wildlife. Visitors can also enjoy a picnic or a swim in the park’s pool.

L’Île-de-la-Visitation Nature Park

Located in the Riviere-des-Prairies-Pointe-aux-Trembles borough of Montreal, the L’Île-de-la-Visitation Nature Park is a great place to escape the city hustle and explore the outdoors. The park offers trails for hiking and biking, as well as picnic areas and fishing spots.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Vaudreuil-Soulanges

Vaudreuil-Soulanges is home to some of the best hiking and biking trails in Quebec. Here are some of the top routes:

Parc nature les Forestiers-de-Saint-Lazare

The Parc nature les Forestiers-de-Saint-Lazare offers several kilometers of hiking and biking trails that wind through the forest and across streams. The trails are marked and suitable for hikers and bikers of all skill levels.

Circuit des Fleurs

The Circuit des Fleurs is a scenic bike route that takes cyclists through the beautiful countryside of Vaudreuil-Soulanges. The route features picturesque views of forests and farmland, as well as several historic landmarks.

Best Things to Do in Vaudreuil-Soulanges: Activities and Tours

Vaudreuil-Soulanges offers a variety of activities and tours that are sure to please travelers of all ages.

Wine Tours

The region is famous for its vineyards and wineries. Visitors can take a wine tour and sample some of the region’s finest wines.

Vignoble Coteau Rougemont

The Vignoble Coteau Rougemont is a family-run vineyard that offers guided tours and tastings. Visitors can explore the vineyard and learn about the wine-making process while enjoying the stunning views of the region.

Adventure Sports

For adventure seekers, Vaudreuil-Soulanges offers a variety of adrenaline-pumping activities.

Arbraska Rigaud

Arbraska Rigaud is an adventure park that features ziplines, aerial courses, and other outdoor activities. It’s perfect for those who love adventure and want to experience the thrill of flying through the air.

For those who want to explore the region in-depth, here’s a week-long itinerary for Vaudreuil-Soulanges:

Day 1: Visit the Musée de Vaudreuil-Soulanges and explore Vaudreuil-Dorion.

Day 2: Hike or bike the trails at Parc nature les Forestiers-de-Saint-Lazare.

Food and Drink Scene of Vaudreuil-Soulanges

Vaudreuil-Soulanges boasts a rich culinary scene that offers a range of local and international dishes.

Must-Try Dishes

Here are some of the must-try dishes in Vaudreuil-Soulanges:

Poutine

Poutine is a quintessential Quebecois dish that consists of French fries, cheese curds, and gravy. It’s a staple in Vaudreuil-Soulanges and can be found at many local restaurants.

Tourtière

Tourtière is a traditional meat pie that’s often served during the holiday season. It’s made with a variety of meats, including beef, pork, and veal, and is flavored with spices such as cinnamon and cloves.

Local Beverages

Vaudreuil-Soulanges is famous for its cider and wine. Visitors can sample a variety of ciders and wines at local wineries and vineyards.
