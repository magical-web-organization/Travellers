Located in Montérégie, in southwestern Quebec, Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is a charming town that has attracted visitors for its rich culture, natural beauty, and fascinating history. In this comprehensive guide, we will provide you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu, including the best places to visit, things to do, hiking and biking routes, where to stay, and more.

Introduction to Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is a small town with a population of approximately 1,800 people, situated on the banks of the Richelieu River. The town has a rich history, dating back to the early 18th century, and was an important trading post for fur traders. Today, it is a peaceful community known for its beautiful landscapes, historic buildings, and cultural events.

List of Places to Visit in Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Old Prison of Saint-Marc: Built in 1845, the Old Prison of Saint-Marc is a national historic site that has been restored to its 19th century appearance. Visitors can tour the cells and learn about the lives of the prisoners who were once incarcerated here.
  • The Richelieu Valley Historical Society Museum: This museum is dedicated to preserving the history and culture of the Richelieu Valley. Visitors will find exhibits showcasing the town’s fur trading roots, as well as local art and artifacts from the 18th and 19th centuries.
  • Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu Church: This beautiful church, built in 1834, is a national historic site and a must-see for visitors who appreciate architectural history.

Natural Attractions of Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Île aux Noix: This small island in the Richelieu River offers excellent hiking trails and stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Chambly Canal National Historic Site: Located just a short drive from Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu, the Chambly Canal is a popular destination for hiking, cycling, and boating.
  • Mont Saint-Hilaire: This mountain, located just outside of town, is a popular destination for hikers who want to explore the beautiful scenery and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts who love hiking and biking. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • The Richelieu River Route: This scenic route, which runs along the banks of the Richelieu River, is perfect for cycling and offers beautiful views of the surrounding countryside.
  • The Chambly Canal Route: This popular route follows the historic Chambly Canal, passing through wooded areas and offering views of the canal and its locks.
  • The Sentiers de l’Estriade: This 41-kilometer trail runs through the heart of the Eastern Townships and offers stunning views of the countryside.

Best Things to Do in Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu: Activities and Tours

In Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu, visitors will find a wide variety of activities and tours to choose from, including:

  • Quebec National Parks: Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is located near several Quebec National Parks, including Mont-Saint-Bruno, Mont-Orford, and Yamaska. These parks offer a wide range of activities, including hiking, biking, and wildlife viewing.
  • Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu Annual Winter Carnival: This popular festival takes place every February and offers a wide range of winter activities, including skating, sledding, and snowshoeing.
  • Espace culturel Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu: This cultural center offers a variety of events, including concerts, plays, and exhibitions. Visitors can check the schedule to see what events are coming up during their visit.

Food and Drink Scene of Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

Must-Try Dishes

Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is known for its delicious cuisine, with plenty of local dishes to tempt your taste buds. Some of the must-try dishes include:

  • Tourtière: This traditional Quebecois meat pie is a must-try for visitors who want to experience the local cuisine.
  • Poutine: A Quebec classic, poutine consists of French fries topped with cheese curds and gravy.
  • Sugar Pie: This sweet treat is made with brown sugar, cream, and eggs, and is a staple of Quebecois cuisine.

Local Beverages

When it comes to beverages, Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu has plenty to offer. Some of the must-try drinks include:

  • Craft Beer: Quebec is known for its excellent craft beer scene, and Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu has several local breweries worth a visit.
  • Ice Cider: Made with apples that have been frozen on the tree, ice cider is a unique and delicious local drink.
  • Maple Syrup: Quebec is famous for its maple syrup, and Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is no exception. Visitors can enjoy maple syrup tastings and tours at local sugar shacks.

Where to Stay in Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu has a variety of accommodations to suit all budgets and needs. Some of the top options include:

  • Auberge Handfield: This charming inn offers cozy rooms and is located near many of the town’s attractions.
  • Le Passe-Partout: This is a budget-friendly guesthouse that offers comfortable and spacious rooms.
  • Hotel Les Trois Tilleuls: This upscale hotel is known for its beautiful views of the Richelieu River and offers luxurious amenities such as an on-site spa and restaurant.

What to Buy in Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu will find a wide variety of local products and souvenirs to bring home, including:

  • Maple Syrup Products: From maple candies to maple spread, Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu offers plenty of maple syrup products to take home.
  • Local Crafts: Visitors can browse local craft stores and pick up handcrafted pottery, jewelry, and other unique gifts.
  • Local Wine and Beer: Quebec has a thriving wine and beer industry, and visitors can stock up on local favorites to enjoy at home.

Local Culture and Customs of Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is a friendly town with a strong sense of community. Visitors should be prepared to greet locals with a friendly “bonjour” or “hi” and should be respectful of local customs. French is the predominant language in this region, so visitors who speak French will find it easier to communicate with locals.

Best Time to Visit Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

The best time to visit Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is during the summer months (June to August) when the weather is warm and sunny. However, visitors who enjoy winter activities will find plenty to do in town during the colder months.

Do’s and Don’ts of Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

  • Respect local customs
  • Be polite and friendly with locals

Safety and Health

Safety in Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is a safe town, with low crime rates. Visitors should still take basic precautions, such as locking their doors at night and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu has a good healthcare system, with clinics and hospitals in the area. Visitors should have travel insurance in case of an emergency.

Getting to Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

The closest airport to Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is Montreal-Pierre Elliott Trudeau International Airport (YUL), which is approximately one hour away by car.

Bus Stations Near Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

The closest bus stations to Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu are in Montreal and Saint-Hyacinthe, both of which are approximately a 30-minute drive from town.

Other Options

Visitors who prefer to drive can rent a car at the Montreal airport or in downtown Montreal, and then drive to Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu.

How to Get Around in Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu

Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is a small town that can easily be explored on foot or by bike. Visitors who want to explore the surrounding area may want to rent a car.

Where to Go Next from Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is located in a beautiful region of Quebec, with many nearby destinations and excursions worth exploring. Some of the top options include:

  • Montreal: This vibrant city is a must-see for visitors to Quebec and is located just an hour away from Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu.
  • Eastern Townships: This region is famous for its stunning landscapes, picturesque towns, and excellent food and drink.
  • National Parks: Quebec has several national parks that offer hiking, camping, and wildlife viewing.

Conclusion

Saint-Marc-sur-Richelieu is a hidden gem in Quebec, offering visitors a rich cultural experience, stunning natural landscapes, and endless opportunities for outdoor adventure.
