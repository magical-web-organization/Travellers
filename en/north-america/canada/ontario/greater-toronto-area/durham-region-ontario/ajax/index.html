Introduction to Ajax

Located in Durham Region, Ontario, Ajax is a beautiful town in the Greater Toronto Area of Canada. The town has a rich history, which dates back to the early 1940s when a portion of land in Pickering Township was acquired by the Government of Canada to build a munitions plant. Today, Ajax is a growing community that offers visitors a mix of cultural and natural attractions.

List of Places to Visit in Ajax

There’s a lot to see and do in Ajax, so make sure you plan your itinerary well in advance. Here’s a list of top attractions worth visiting in Ajax:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Visual Arts Centre of Clarington: Located just a short drive from Ajax, this art center is a must-visit for art enthusiasts.
  • Pickering Museum Village: This living museum showcases what life was like in the 19th century in Upper Canada.
  • Heritage Square: This historical landmark features beautiful architecture and is home to the Ajax Public Library.

Natural Attractions of Ajax: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Waterfront Trail: This 28-kilometer trail runs through Ajax and offers stunning views of Lake Ontario.
  • Greenwood Conservation Area: This beautiful conservation area is a haven for nature lovers, with hiking trails and picnic areas.
  • Ajax Waterfront Park: This park is perfect for a family day out with its playgrounds, splash pad, and picnic areas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ajax

Ajax is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts, and one of the best ways to explore the area is by hiking or biking. Here are some popular routes:

  • Duffins Creek Trail: This 12-kilometer trail is ideal for both hiking and biking, and offers stunning views of the creek and forested areas.
  • Greenwood Conservation Area Trails: The conservation area has over nine kilometers of hiking trails.
  • Waterfront Trail: This trail runs along the shore of Lake Ontario, passing through the Ajax Waterfront Park and Rotary Park trail networks.

Best Things to Do in Ajax: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re traveling solo or with friends and family, there’s something for everyone in Ajax. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Ajax Escape Rooms: If you’re a fan of puzzles and brain teasers, this escape room is a must-visit.
  • Treetop Trekking: This adventure park offers thrilling zip-lining and treetop courses.
  • Archibald’s Estate Winery: Take a tour of the vineyard and enjoy a wine tasting experience.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ajax

To make the most of your visit to Ajax, plan your trip ahead of time with this week-long itinerary:

Day 1 Arrive in Ajax and take a stroll around Heritage Square.
Day 2 Visit the Pickering Museum Village and Greenwood Conservation Area.
Day 3 Take a nature walk along the Duffins Creek Trail.
Day 4 Explore the town’s art scene at the Visual Arts Centre of Clarington and spend the afternoon at the Ajax Waterfront Park.
Day 5 Take a guided tour of Archibald’s Estate Winery.
Day 6 Have an adventurous day at Treetop Trekking.
Day 7 Visit the local shops and artisans to buy souvenirs and local products.

Food and Drink Scene of Ajax

Ajax has a diverse food scene, with a range of options to suit every taste. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Butter Chicken: This Indian dish is a staple in Ajax, and can be found at many local restaurants.
  • Poutine: A classic Canadian dish, Ajax has many eateries that specialize in poutine.
  • Fish and Chips: With access to Lake Ontario, it’s no surprise that Ajax has some of the best fish and chips around.

Local Beverages

  • Archibald’s Estate Winery: The winery offers a range of locally produced wines that are perfect for pairing with a meal.
  • Junction Brewery: Ajax’s only brewery offers locally brewed craft beers.

Where to Stay in Ajax: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ajax offers a range of accommodation options to suit every budget. Here are some top picks:

  • Hilton Garden Inn Toronto/Ajax: This hotel offers comfortable rooms, a fitness center, and an indoor pool.
  • Comfort Inn: This budget-friendly hotel is perfect for those on a shoestring budget.
  • Ajax Inn: This family-run motel offers spacious rooms and a central location in the town.

What to Buy in Ajax: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When in Ajax, there are some unique souvenirs that you should consider buying:

  • Artisanal Soaps: There are many local shops that sell handmade soaps that are great for gifts.
  • Hockey Memorable: Canada’s national sport, hockey, is celebrated in Ajax. You can buy hockey sticks, skates and other accessories to bring home.
  • Maple Syrup: Maple syrup is a classic Canadian product and can be found in many local shops in Ajax.

Local Culture and Customs of Ajax

Ajax has a diverse cultural scene, with a mix of international communities calling the town home. Residents are friendly and hospitable, and visitors can expect to be welcomed with open arms.

Best Time to Visit Ajax

The best time to visit Ajax is during the summer months, when the weather is warm and sunny. The town is full of life during this time, with many events and festivals happening around town.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ajax

Here are some tips to keep in mind during your visit to Ajax:

Do:

  • Be respectful of the local customs and cultural practices.
  • Explore the town’s outdoor attractions, like hiking trails and parks.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Litter in public areas.
  • Be loud and disruptive in residential areas.
  • Ignore safety guidelines while participating in adventure activities.

Safety and Health

Ajax is a safe town, and visitors are unlikely to face any major safety or health concerns during their stay. However, it’s always best to take a few precautions to avoid any unforeseen circumstances:

Safety in Ajax

  • Stay alert and aware of your surroundings, especially in unfamiliar areas.
  • Keep your valuables locked in a safe place.
  • Always follow safety guidelines while participating in adventure activities.

Health in Ajax

  • Stay hydrated and wear appropriate clothing during the summer months.
  • Carry insect repellent to avoid bug bites.
  • Familiarize yourself with the local healthcare facilities in case of an emergency.

Getting to Ajax: Transportation Options and Logistics

Ajax is well-connected to major cities in Canada, making it easy to get to. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Ajax

  • Toronto Pearson International Airport: About a 40-minute drive from Ajax.
  • Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport: About an hour from Ajax.

Bus Stations Near Ajax

  • GO Transit: Serves the Greater Toronto Area with stops at Ajax GO Station.
  • Durham Region Transit: Provides local bus service in the town.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Many car rental companies operate in the area.
  • Uber and Taxis: Options for local commuting and airport transfers.

How to Get Around in Ajax

The best way to get around Ajax is by car or bike. However, there are other options as well, including:

  • Public Transit: Durham Region Transit provides local bus service around town.
  • Taxis: Several taxi companies operate in the town.
  • Walking: Most attractions in the town are within walking distance of each other.

Where to Go Next from Ajax: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several nearby destinations from Ajax that are worth visiting:

  • Toronto: The bustling metropolitan city is just a 30-minute drive from Ajax.
  • Oshawa: Just a 20-minute drive from Ajax, Oshawa is another charming town full of history and culture.
  • Pickering: A short 10-minute drive from Ajax, Pickering boasts beautiful natural attractions like the waterfront and conservation areas.

Conclusion

Ajax is a hidden gem in Ontario, Canada, offering a mix of cultural and natural attractions that are perfect for visitors of all kinds. Plan your trip ahead of time and make the most of your time in this beautiful town.
