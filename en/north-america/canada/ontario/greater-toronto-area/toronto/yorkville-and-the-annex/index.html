Introduction to Yorkville and the Annex

Yorkville and the Annex is one of the most scenic and cultural neighborhoods in Toronto, with tree-lined streets, art galleries, museums, boutique shops, top-rated restaurants, and cafes. The neighborhoods share their boundaries and overlap with each other, making them a vital cultural epicenter of Toronto.

List of Places to Visit in Yorkville and the Annex

Visiting Yorkville and the Annex, Toronto, is a must-do activity for every traveler. Below is a comprehensive list of places to visit during your trip:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Royal Ontario Museum: Canada’s largest museum with a diverse collection of art, design, and natural history.

  • Bata Shoe Museum: An unusual museum with a fantastic collection of shoes from different cultures and time periods.

  • Gardiner Museum: One of the top ceramics museums in the world, housing an extensive collection of ceramic art.

  • Spadina Museum: A historic mansion built in the 1860s and a museum showcasing the history of Toronto’s prominent Austin family.

Natural Attractions of Yorkville and the Annex: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Philosopher’s Walk: A picturesque walk leading to the University of Toronto, providing stunning views of the campus and the city.

  • Queens Park: A sprawling public park with lush greenery and the University of Toronto’s Gothic-style buildings.

  • Village of Yorkville Park: A verdant park featuring sculptures, waterfalls, and a vibrant atmosphere.

  • Ramsden Park: A vast park featuring a splash pad, tennis courts, a playground, a skating rink, and picnic areas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Yorkville and the Annex

Yorkville and the Annex offer several cycling and hiking routes around the neighborhood. Some popular ones include:

  • Annex Bike Tour: A guided bike tour through the winding streets of the Annex, featuring beautiful mansions, parks, and University of Toronto landmarks.

  • Yorkville Highlights Walking Tour: A walking tour through the best of Yorkville, including antique shops, iconic landmarks, and public art.

  • Casa Loma to Queen’s Park: A scenic cycling route from Casa Loma to the University of Toronto and Queens Park, taking in the greenery and historic sites.

Best Things to Do in Yorkville and the Annex: Activities and Tours

With a range of activities and tours available, Yorkville and the Annex offer visitors a unique experience. Some of the best things to do include:

  • Food tour of the neighborhood’s culinary scene, savoring street foods, cafes, and top-rated restaurants in the area.

  • Explore the art and sculpture in the neighborhood’s parks, including the outdoor sculptures at the Village of Yorkville Park.

  • Take a historical walking tour of the Annex and learn about its rich architectural and cultural history.

Food and Drink Scene of Yorkville and the Annex

Yorkville and the Annex offer an array of food and drink options, from classic street foods to high-end restaurants. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Poutine: A Canadian comfort dish with fries, gravy, and cheese curds.

  • Peameal Bacon Sandwich: A quintessentially Canadian take on the bacon sandwich with peameal bacon on a Kaiser roll.

  • Butter Chicken: A classic Indian dish that has become a staple in Canadian cuisine.

Local Beverages

  • Craft beer from Bellwoods Brewery.

  • Wine from the LCBO.

  • Toronto’s famous Caesar cocktail.

Where to Stay in Yorkville and the Annex: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Yorkville and the Annex offer several accommodations options ranging from luxurious hotels to budget-friendly guesthouses. Some of the top-rated accommodations in the area include:

  • Four Seasons Hotel Toronto: A five-star hotel located in the heart of Yorkville, offering luxurious rooms, suites, and amenities.

  • Kimpton Saint George Hotel: A boutique hotel located near the Bata Museum, featuring luxurious rooms and fine dining.

  • HI Toronto: A hostel located in the Annex neighborhood, offering budget-friendly accommodations.

  • Casa Loma Inn: A beautiful guesthouse located in a historic castle.
