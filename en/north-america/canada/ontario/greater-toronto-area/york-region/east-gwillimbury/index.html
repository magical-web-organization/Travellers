Introduction to East Gwillimbury

East Gwillimbury is a charming town nestled in the heart of York Region, in the Greater Toronto Area in Canada. Situated in the northern region of York Region, it is known for its natural beauty, inviting ambiance, and friendly locals. With its rich history, cultural attractions, recreational activities, and stunning landscapes, East Gwillimbury has something for everyone. This comprehensive travel guide will outline the top places to visit, things to do, and where to stay in this hidden gem of Canada.

List of Places to Visit in East Gwillimbury

Whether you’re interested in history, culture, or nature, East Gwillimbury has plenty of places to visit and explore. Here are some of the must-see attractions in this charming town:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sharon Temple National Historic Site and Museum: This beautiful structure is a National Historic Site of Canada and a designated Ontario Heritage Property, which is famous for its unique architecture and history.

  • Museum of East Gwillimbury: Explore the town’s rich history through interactive exhibits and collections of artifacts, photos, and documents.

  • Queensville Historic Society: Visit this small but fascinating museum to learn about the town’s early settlers and their lives during the 19th century.

Natural Attractions of East Gwillimbury: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Rogers Reservoir Conservation Area: Take in the serene beauty of the area as you stroll along the trails, fish in the reservoir, or have a picnic in the park.

  • Lake Simcoe: This lake is a popular spot for swimming, boating, fishing, and other water activities.

  • Green Lane Park: Whether you enjoy hiking, bird watching, or just soaking up the natural surroundings, this park has something for you.

Hiking/Biking Routes of East Gwillimbury

East Gwillimbury boasts several scenic hiking and biking trails that offer stunning views of the surrounding landscapes. Whether you’re a seasoned hiker or a beginner, here are some of the best trails to explore in the area:

  • Holland Landing Prairie Provincial Nature Reserve Trail: This trail is home to a variety of wildflowers, butterflies, and birds, and is perfect for nature lovers.

  • Scanlon Creek Conservation Area Trail: With over 10 km of trails, this conservation area offers plenty of options for hiking, cycling, and cross-country skiing.

  • Vivian Forest Trail: This trail winds through mature forests and meadows, making for a peaceful and relaxing walk.

Best Things to Do in East Gwillimbury: Activities and Tours

East Gwillimbury has a variety of activities and tours for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the best things to do in the area:

  • Horseback riding: Explore the beautiful countryside on horseback with local ride stables.

  • Golfing: Tee off at some of the best golf courses in the area, such as Silver Lakes Golf & Conference Centre and The Briars Golf Club.

  • Wine tasting: Visit Holland Marsh Wineries to sample award-winning wines made from locally grown grapes.

Week-Long Itinerary for East Gwillimbury

To make the most of your trip to East Gwillimbury, follow this week-long itinerary:

Day 1: Visit the Museum of East Gwillimbury and Queensville Historic Society to learn about the town’s rich history.

Day 2: Take a stroll along the trails at Rogers Reservoir Conservation Area.

Day 3: Play a round of golf at one of the area’s top golf courses.

Day 4: Go horseback riding through the picturesque countryside.

Day 5: Explore the unique architecture of Sharon Temple National Historic Site and Museum.

Day 6: Discover the natural beauty of Green Lane Park.

Day 7: Sample some award-winning wines at Holland Marsh Wineries.

Food and Drink Scene of East Gwillimbury

East Gwillimbury has a thriving food and drink scene, with plenty of options for locals and tourists to enjoy. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fish and chips: Enjoy this classic Canadian dish at Fryer Tuck’s, a local favourite.

  • Apple fritters: Sample these delicious treats at Applewood Farm and Winery.

  • Poutine: Indulge in this quintessentially Canadian dish at local restaurants such as Moustache Burger and Zarky’s Fine Foods.

Local Beverages

  • Craft beer: Enjoy locally brewed beer at Lake Wilcox Brewing Company and The Second Wedge Brewing Company.

  • Wine: Sample award-winning wines made from locally grown grapes at Holland Marsh Wineries.

Where to Stay in East Gwillimbury: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

East Gwillimbury has a variety of accommodations to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best options:

  • The Briars Resort and Spa: This beautiful resort offers luxurious accommodations, top-notch amenities, and breathtaking views of Lake Simcoe.

  • Silver Lakes Golf & Conference Centre: This resort features comfortable guest rooms, a championship golf course, and stunning lake views.

  • Bradford Inn: This budget-friendly option offers cozy rooms and a convenient location near local attractions.

What to Buy in East Gwillimbury: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

For a unique souvenir or gift, consider purchasing some of these local products:

  • Maple syrup: Buy some of the best maple syrup in the area at Brooks Farms.

  • Fresh fruits and vegetables: Visit local farmers’ markets such as the Holland Marsh Soupfest & Farmer’s Market for the freshest produce.

  • Handmade crafts: Browse the selection of handmade crafts and gifts at Sharon Temple National Historic Site and Museum.

Local Culture and Customs of East Gwillimbury

East Gwillimbury is known for its friendly locals, welcoming atmosphere, and love for the outdoors. Visitors should respect local customs and traditions and be mindful of their impact on the environment.

Best Time to Visit East Gwillimbury

The best time to visit East Gwillimbury is during the summer months (June to August) when the weather is warm and sunny, making it ideal for outdoor activities. Winter months (December to February) can also be a great time to visit for winter sports enthusiasts.

Do’s and Don’ts of East Gwillimbury

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.

  • Take care of the environment when participating in outdoor activities.

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • litter or harm wildlife when exploring the natural surroundings.

  • Ignore safety warnings when participating in outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in East Gwillimbury

East Gwillimbury is a safe and peaceful town, but visitors should take the usual precautions when travelling, such as keeping valuables out of sight, locking car doors, and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in East Gwillimbury

East Gwillimbury has excellent healthcare facilities, and visitors should ensure they have adequate travel insurance and any necessary vaccinations before arriving.

Getting to East Gwillimbury: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near East Gwillimbury

The closest international airports to East Gwillimbury are Toronto Pearson International Airport and Billy Bishop Toronto City Airport.

Bus Stations Near East Gwillimbury

Greyhound and Coach Canada offer bus service to the area. The closest bus terminal is in Newmarket.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi to East Gwillimbury from nearby towns.

How to Get Around in East Gwillimbury

The town of East Gwillimbury is best explored by car. Alternatively, visitors can use local taxi services or ride-sharing apps.

Where to Go Next from East Gwillimbury: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Toronto: Canada’s largest city is just a short drive away and offers endless opportunities for entertainment, culture, and sightseeing.

  • Lake Simcoe: This beautiful lake is perfect for boating, swimming, and fishing.

  • Algonquin Provincial Park: This wilderness park is a must-visit for outdoor enthusiasts, with its pristine lakes, hiking trails, and wildlife viewing opportunities.

Conclusion

East Gwillimbury is a hidden gem in Canada, and with its unique combination of history, culture, nature, and recreation, it offers something for everyone. Use this comprehensive travel guide to plan your ultimate trip to East Gwillimbury and discover all this charming town has to offer.
