Introduction to Owen Sound

Nestled in the heart of Grey County, Owen Sound is a vibrant city located in Southwestern Ontario, Canada. This beautiful city is known for its stunning natural landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. Owen Sound is the perfect destination for those seeking outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, and delicious cuisine.

List of Places to Visit in Owen Sound

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Owen Sound is home to several museums and historical landmarks that offer a glimpse into the city’s past. The Grey Roots Museum and Archives immerse visitors in the history and culture of Grey County with over 15,000 artifacts, exhibits, and interactive displays.

The Tom Thomson Art Gallery showcases contemporary Canadian art and celebrates the life and legacy of renowned artist, Tom Thomson. Visitors can explore the gallery’s permanent collection and temporary exhibitions featuring local and national artists.

Another must-visit attraction is the Billy Bishop Home and Museum, which is dedicated to the life and career of World War I fighter pilot Billy Bishop. Guided tours of his childhood home and museum displays provide a unique perspective on Canadian military history.

Natural Attractions of Owen Sound: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Owen Sound is known for its natural beauty, which offers a wide variety of outdoor activities. The Sydenham River Trail is a popular destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts, featuring 27 kilometres of trails that connect the city to scenic views along the river’s banks.

Visitors can explore the Bruce Peninsula National Park, a UNESCO World Biosphere Reserve, which is located less than an hour away from Owen Sound. The park features over 150 kilometres of hiking trails, pristine beaches, and crystal-clear waters.

The Harrison Park is another outdoor attraction, perfect for picnics, hiking, fishing, and swimming. This beautiful park spans over 40 acres and includes scenic trails, playgrounds, and a stunning waterfall.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Owen Sound

Owen Sound is a paradise for adventure enthusiasts who love hiking or biking. Travellers can take in the stunning scenery and explore Owen Sound’s natural beauty on the trails mentioned in the previous section. Other popular trails include the Georgian Trail, which runs from Collingwood to Meaford and offers scenic views of Georgian Bay and the Niagara Escarpment.

Another popular route is the Owen Sound Rail Trail, which features a combination of paved and unpaved paths, making it ideal for walkers and cyclists of all skill levels.

Best Things to Do in Owen Sound: Activities and Tours

In addition to the natural attractions, Owen Sound offers numerous activities and tours for visitors. Travellers can visit the Georgian Bay Symphony or catch a show at the Roxy Theatre, a historic theatre that showcases live performances year-round.

For a unique experience, visitors can opt for a horse-drawn carriage tour with Sunny Valley Park Carriages. The tour takes visitors through the scenic countryside surrounding Owen Sound.

Visitors can also experience the local culture firsthand by attending one of many festivals and events held throughout the year, such as Summerfolk, Harvest Festival, and Festival of Northern Lights.

Week-Long Itinerary for Owen Sound

Day 1: Explore the city of Owen Sound, visiting the Grey Roots Museum and Archives, Tom Thomson Art Gallery, and Billy Bishop Home and Museum.

Day 2: Hike the Sydenham River Trail and explore Harrison Park.

Day 3: Take a tour of the Georgian Bay Lighthouse and visit the Owen Sound Farmers’ Market.

Day 4: Visit the Bruce Peninsula National Park and take a scenic boat tour of Georgian Bay.

Day 5: Visit the Inglis Falls Conservation Area and enjoy a picnic beside the waterfall.

Day 6: Take a horse-drawn carriage tour and enjoy a meal at one of Owen Sound’s many restaurants.

Day 7: Attend one of the many festivals and events held in Owen Sound throughout the year.

Food and Drink Scene of Owen Sound

Must-Try Dishes

Owen Sound is known for its delicious cuisine, with an emphasis on local and seasonal ingredients. The city’s culinary scene includes everything from casual diners and cafes to fine dining restaurants.

Must-try dishes include the fish and chips at the Rockford Restaurant, the North Pacific Halibut at Casero Kitchen Table, and the pad Thai at the Milk Maid Fine Cheese & Gourmet Shop.

Local Beverages

Owen Sound is home to several breweries, including the Kilannan Brewing Company, which offers a range of year-round and seasonal beers.

Another local favourite is Coffin Ridge Winery, which produces award-winning wines made from grapes grown on the Niagara Escarpment.

Where to Stay in Owen Sound: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Owen Sound offers a variety of accommodations for travellers, including hotels, motels, guesthouses, and hostels.

Some of the top-rated hotels include the Best Western Inn on the Bay, Quality Inn Owen Sound, and Comfort Inn Owen Sound. Budget-friendly options include the Travellers Motel and the Owen Sound Inn.

What to Buy in Owen Sound: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Travellers can take home a piece of Owen Sound with them by purchasing locally-made products. These include artisanal cheeses from the Milk Maid Fine Cheese & Gourmet Shop, handmade pottery from the Mudtown Pottery, and baked goods from the Milk Maid Fine Cheese & Gourmet Shop.

Local Culture and Customs of Owen Sound

Owen Sound is known for its warm hospitality and friendly locals. Visitors should be prepared to be greeted with a smile and engage in friendly conversation.

The city is also proud of its British heritage and celebrates this with events such as the Owen Sound Celtic Festival.

Best Time to Visit Owen Sound

The best time to visit Owen Sound is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and ideal for outdoor activities. Fall is another beautiful time to visit, with the changing foliage providing a stunning backdrop for hiking and biking trails.

Do’s and Don’ts of Owen Sound

Do:

  • Explore the city’s natural beauty by hiking or biking along the trails.
  • Support local businesses by visiting farmers’ markets and purchasing locally-made products.
  • Attend one of the many festivals and events held throughout the year in Owen Sound.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural beauty of the parks and trails.
  • Disrespect the local culture and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Owen Sound

Owen Sound is a relatively safe city, with a low crime rate. Visitors should be aware of their surroundings and take precautions such as locking car doors and securing valuables.

Health in Owen Sound

Owen Sound has several medical facilities, including the Grey Bruce Health Services, which offers emergency and urgent care services.

Getting to Owen Sound: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Owen Sound

The closest airports to Owen Sound are the John C. Munro Hamilton International Airport, which is about 150 kilometres away, and the Toronto Pearson International Airport, which is about 190 kilometres away.

Bus Stations Near Owen Sound

Owen Sound is served by the Greyhound Canada bus line, which has a station located at 955 9th Ave E.

Other Options

Travellers can also drive to Owen Sound, as it is conveniently located near Highway 6 and Highway 26.

How to Get Around in Owen Sound

Travellers can get around Owen Sound by car, bike, or public transit. The Owen Sound Transportation Company operates a public bus system with several routes throughout the city.

Alternatively, visitors can rent a car from one of the several car rental companies located within the city.

Where to Go Next from Owen Sound: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can explore Grey County’s other charming communities like the idyllic beaches of Sauble Beach, the charming town of Thornbury, and the scenic village of Flesherton.

Conclusion

Owen Sound is a must-visit destination for anyone visiting Ontario. This city offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and warm hospitality that will leave travellers with memories to last a lifetime. Use this comprehensive tour guide to plan your perfect trip to Owen Sound and experience all that this wonderful city has to offer.
