St. Thomas Travel Guide - A Comprehensive Tourist Guide

St. Thomas is a charming city located in Southwestern Ontario, Canada. With its rich cultural history and natural beauty, it has become a popular tourist destination. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore the best places to visit and things to do in St. Thomas.

Introduction to St. Thomas

St. Thomas is a city in the Elgin County of Ontario. The city is known for its rich heritage and cultural diversity, with several museums and galleries that offer a glimpse into its history. St. Thomas is also a hub of outdoor activities and scenic views, with its location on the shores of Lake Erie. Its proximity to other cities and towns makes it an ideal base for exploring the region.

List of Places to Visit in St. Thomas

St. Thomas offers a variety of cultural and natural attractions for visitors. Here is a list of must-visit places in St. Thomas:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Elgin County Railway Museum: A museum dedicated to the history of railways in the region.

  2. St. Thomas-Elgin Public Art Centre: A public art gallery that houses works by local and national artists.

  3. Elgin County Museum: A museum that showcases the history and culture of the region.

Natural Attractions of St. Thomas: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Pinafore Park: A park that offers scenic views of Lake Erie and hiking trails.

  2. Dalewood Conservation Area: A conservation area that offers hiking trails, fishing, and picnicking.

  3. Lake Margaret Trail: A scenic trail that offers views of the lake and surrounding hills.

Hiking/Biking Routes of St. Thomas

St. Thomas is a hub for outdoor activities like hiking, mountain biking, and cycling. Here are some of the popular routes:

  1. The Great Trail: A long-distance trail that passes through St. Thomas, offering scenic views of the countryside.

  2. Jumbo Creek Conservation Area: A conservation area that offers hiking trails and cycling routes.

  3. Dan Patterson Conservation Area: A conservation area that offers hiking trails, cycling routes, and birdwatching.

Best Things to Do in St. Thomas: Activities and Tours

St. Thomas offers several activities and tours for visitors. Here are some of the best things to do in St. Thomas:

  1. St. Thomas Elevated Park: A park that offers an immersive experience, with its floating walkway and panoramic views.

  2. North America Railway Hall of Fame: A museum that celebrates the history of railways in North America.

  3. Port Stanley Terminal Rail: A scenic train ride that takes visitors through the countryside and along the shores of Lake Erie.

Week-Long Itinerary for St. Thomas

Here is a sample itinerary for spending a week in St. Thomas:

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit the Elgin County Railway Museum and St. Thomas-Elgin Public Art Centre
Day 2 Explore Pinafore Park and hike Lake Margaret Trail
Day 3 Take a scenic train ride with Port Stanley Terminal Rail
Day 4 Visit Elgin County Museum and hike Jumbo Creek Conservation Area
Day 5 Spend the day at Port Stanley Beach and enjoy water sports
Day 6 Visit the North America Railway Hall of Fame and go birdwatching at Dan Patterson Conservation Area
Day 7 Relax and enjoy local food and drink scene in St. Thomas

Food and Drink Scene of St. Thomas

St. Thomas offers a variety of food and drink options for visitors. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Fish and Chips: A local specialty made with fresh fish, crispy batter, and homemade chips.

  2. Butter Tart: A sweet pastry that is a staple dessert in Canada.

  3. Poutine: A dish made with French fries, gravy, and cheese curds.

Local Beverages

  1. Craft Beer: St. Thomas has several local breweries that offer craft beer with unique flavors.

  2. cider: St. Thomas is known for its apple orchards and offers several cider options.

Where to Stay in St. Thomas: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

St. Thomas offers a variety of options for accommodation, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. Here are some of the best places to stay in St. Thomas:

  1. Best Western Stoneridge Inn & Conference Centre: A luxurious hotel with modern amenities and scenic views.

  2. Comfort Inn St. Thomas: A budget-friendly hotel that offers comfortable rooms and modern amenities.

  3. Glenhaven Motel: A family-friendly, pet-friendly motel that offers spacious rooms and a tranquil location.

What to Buy in St. Thomas: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

St. Thomas offers a variety of souvenirs and local products that visitors can take back home. Here are some of the best local products to buy in St. Thomas:

  1. Fresh Apples: St. Thomas is known for its apple orchards, and freshly-picked apples are a popular souvenir.

  2. Local Crafts: St. Thomas has several local artisans who create unique crafts, including pottery and woodwork.

Local Culture and Customs of St. Thomas

St. Thomas is a culturally diverse city, with a rich history and traditions. Visitors should respect the local culture and customs, including the use of formal titles and greetings.

Best Time to Visit St. Thomas

The best time to visit St. Thomas is between May and September when the weather is warm and sunny, and outdoor activities are in full swing.

  2. Take precautions while hiking and participating in outdoor activities.

Don’t:

Safety and Health

Safety in St. Thomas

St. Thomas is a safe city to visit, with a low crime rate. However, visitors should take standard precautions, such as locking their doors and not leaving valuables unattended.

Health in St. Thomas

St. Thomas has several hospitals and medical facilities that offer quality healthcare services. Visitors should ensure they have appropriate health insurance coverage before traveling to St. Thomas.

Getting to St. Thomas: Transportation Options and Logistics

St. Thomas is easily accessible by various modes of transportation. Here are some transportation options to reach St. Thomas:

Airports Near St. Thomas

  1. London International Airport: The nearest airport to St. Thomas, which is approximately a 30-minutes drive from the city.

  2. Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport: A major international airport, approximately a 2-hour drive from St. Thomas.

Bus Stations Near St. Thomas

  1. Greyhound: A popular bus service that operates daily from Toronto and other nearby cities.

Other Options

  1. Car: Visitors can drive to St. Thomas, as it is well-connected by highways.

How to Get Around in St. Thomas

St. Thomas offers several transportation options for getting around the city, including taxis, buses, and rental cars. Visitors can also explore the city on foot or bicycle.

Where to Go Next from St. Thomas: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

St. Thomas is located in a scenic region of Ontario, with several destinations and excursions nearby. Visitors can explore these places after visiting St. Thomas:

  1. Port Stanley: A charming town located on the shores of Lake Erie, known for its sandy beaches and water sports.

  2. London: A vibrant city that offers a variety of cultural attractions, shopping, and dining options.

  3. Stratford: A town known for its Shakespearean Festival and beautiful gardens.

Conclusion

St. Thomas is an ideal destination for those looking for a mix of culture and nature. With its variety of attractions and cultural heritage, visitors are sure to have a memorable experience in this charming city.
