Tillsonburg is a scenic town located in Southwestern Ontario, Canada. This hidden gem boasts a rich cultural heritage, lovely tree-lined streets, and a variety of activities and attractions that promise to keep visitors engaged. Whether you’re planning a family vacation, a romantic getaway, or a solo adventure, Tillsonburg has something for everyone.

In this Tillsonburg travel guide, we’ll take you on a tour of the town, highlighting the best things to see and do, where to stay, what to eat, and how to get around safely and comfortably. So, pack your bags and get ready to explore this charming town.

List of Places to Visit in Tillsonburg

Tillsonburg has numerous attractions to keep visitors engaged. Here are some places you shouldn’t miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Annandale National Historic Site: This grand manor was built in 1880 and is now a museum that gives visitors a glimpse into Tillsonburg’s rich history.
  • Station Arts Centre: The former Tillsonburg train station is now a vibrant arts centre that features exhibitions, workshops, concerts, and more.

Natural Attractions of Tillsonburg: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Lisgar Waterpark: A popular spot for fishing, canoeing, kayaking, and swimming.
  • The Bridges of Tillsonburg: A scenic cycling route that will take you over several beautiful bridges and through scenic countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tillsonburg

For outdoor enthusiasts, Tillsonburg offers plenty of hiking and biking routes. Here are some of the top picks:

  • Tillsonburg Trans Canada Trail: This trail runs through Tillsonburg and is perfect for hiking, biking, and snowshoeing.
  • The Glasgow Trail: A picturesque pathway that takes you through lush forests and past serene, rolling hills.

Best Things to Do in Tillsonburg: Activities and Tours

Tillsonburg is full of activities and tours to keep visitors busy. Here are some options:

  • Otter Creek Golf Club: A scenic golf course that offers a challenging round of golf.
  • Tillsonburg Farmer’s Market: A bustling market that takes place every Saturday and offers locally grown produce, handmade crafts, and more.

Local Culture and Customs of Tillsonburg

Tillsonburg is a friendly and welcoming town with a rich culture and customs. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • It’s customary to hold the door open for others.
  • Avoid littering and keep public spaces clean.
  • Respect the privacy and property of residents.

Best Time to Visit Tillsonburg

The best time of year to visit Tillsonburg is during the summer months (June to August), when the weather is warm and the town is bustling with activities and events.
