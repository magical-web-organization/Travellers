Welcome to Stratford, a charming town located in Southwestern Ontario, Canada. Known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and beautiful natural landscapes, Stratford is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking a unique experience. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know to plan the perfect trip to Stratford, including top attractions, local customs and traditions, must-try dishes, and nearby destinations worth exploring.

Introduction to Stratford

Founded in 1832, Stratford is a town steeped in history and culture. It is famously known for the Stratford Festival, a world-renowned theater event that attracts visitors from all over the world. But there’s much more to Stratford than its theater scene. From historic landmarks and museums to natural attractions and outdoor adventures, there’s something for everyone in this charming town.

List of Places to Visit in Stratford

Here are some of the top attractions to put on your list when visiting Stratford:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Stratford Perth Museum: Discover the town’s rich history and culture at this world-class museum, featuring exhibits and artifacts that showcase the region’s indigenous history, pioneer life, and cultural heritage.

  • Gallery Stratford: Visit this contemporary art gallery, featuring an impressive collection of Canadian and international art. Admission is free.

  • Wellington County Museum and Archives: Learn more about the area’s history and culture at this museum, which showcases artifacts related to agriculture, technology, and local industry.

Natural Attractions of Stratford: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Stratford Rotary Complex: This expansive sports complex features an Aquatic Center, Field House, and Agriplex. Enjoy swimming, ice skating, or fitness classes all under one roof.

  • Tom Patterson Island: Located in the middle of Avon River, this small island is a peaceful oasis where you can take a leisurely walk or have a picnic.

  • Stratford Farmers Market: Providing locally grown produce, baked goods, and other full-flavored delicacies, this is a must-visit on Saturdays from May to October.

Food and Drink Scene of Stratford

Stratford has a thriving culinary scene, offering visitors a variety of delicious dishes and locally-produced beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and drinks:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Peameal Bacon: A staple of Canadian cuisine, this smoked pork loin is a must-try dish in Stratford.

  • Butter Tarts: A small pastry filled with butter, sugar, syrup, and eggs, a butter tart is a sweet treat York and Midland settlers enjoyed when they arrived in Canada.

  • Stratford Whitefish: A dish from the area’s Kanienkehaka people that consists of smoked fish with wild rice and root vegetables.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beer: Stratford is home to several breweries producing unique and flavorful craft beers.

  • Ontario Wines: The region has some of the best wine in Canada due to its temperate climate. It particularly produces Riesling and Cabernet Franc wines.
