Smiths Falls is a charming town located in Eastern Ontario, Canada. With a rich cultural heritage, beautiful natural landscapes, and exciting outdoor activities, this hidden gem is perfect for a weekend getaway or a week-long vacation. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take you on a virtual tour of Smiths Falls, with details on where to stay, what to do, and how to get around this enchanting town.

Introduction to Smiths Falls

Smiths Falls is situated on the banks of the historic Rideau Canal, a UNESCO World Heritage Site that connects Ottawa, Canada’s capital, with Kingston on Lake Ontario. Its location makes it a popular destination for hikers, bikers, boaters, and nature lovers. Smiths Falls is also home to numerous museums, galleries, and historical attractions that offer a glimpse into the town’s rich history.

List of Places to Visit in Smiths Falls

Here are some of the must-visit attractions in Smiths Falls:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Heritage House Museum: This museum is housed in a restored mid-19th-century home and features exhibits on the history of Smiths Falls and the surrounding area.

  2. Rideau Canal Visitor Centre: This center offers information on the Rideau Canal’s rich history, its role in the development of Canada, and its current use for recreational boating.

  3. Smiths Falls Railway Museum of Eastern Ontario: The museum displays artifacts, photographs, and model trains related to the town’s railway heritage.

Natural Attractions of Smiths Falls: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Lower Reach Park: A beautiful park with walking trails, picnicking areas, and river views.

  2. Victoria Park: A popular spot for locals and tourists alike, with a splash pad, playground, beach volleyball courts, picnic areas and walking trails.

  3. Murphy’s Point Provincial Park: A scenic park with hiking and biking trails, beaches, campgrounds, and opportunities for canoeing, kayaking, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Smiths Falls

Smiths Falls is surrounded by beautiful parks and natural landscapes, making it an excellent destination for outdoor enthusiasts who love hiking and biking. Here are some of the best trails and routes to explore:

  1. Rideau Trail: This scenic 327-kilometer trail traverses through forest, wetlands, and meadows, and offers stunning views of the Rideau Canal.

  2. Cataraqui Trail: A 104-kilometer trail that winds through picturesque towns, farmland, and forests.

  3. Hike and Bike Trail: A scenic 4-kilometer trail that runs along the shoreline of the Rideau Canal, passing by the historic lock stations.

Best Things to Do in Smiths Falls: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, there are several exciting things to do in Smiths Falls:

  1. Kayaking and Canoeing: Rent a kayak or a canoe and paddle along the Rideau Canal, enjoying beautiful views of the town and its natural surroundings.

  2. Boat Tours: Take a leisurely boat tour of the Rideau Canal and learn about its history and significance.

  3. Horseback Riding: Go on a horseback riding adventure through the beautiful countryside around Smiths Falls.

Week-Long Itinerary for Smiths Falls

If you’re planning to spend a week in Smiths Falls, here’s an itinerary to make the most of your stay:

Day 1: Arrive in Smiths Falls and explore the town’s historic sites, such as the Heritage House Museum and the Rideau Canal Visitor Centre.

Day 2-3: Spend time in Murphy’s Point Provincial Park, hiking, biking, swimming, and canoeing or kayaking.

Day 4-5: Discover the town’s natural beauty by exploring the Cataraqui Trail and the Hike and Bike Trail.

Day 6-7: Experience the town’s culture and cuisine by visiting local galleries, wineries, and restaurants.

Food and Drink Scene of Smiths Falls

Smiths Falls has a vibrant food and drink scene, with a variety of local cuisines and beverages to try. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Poutine: A classic Canadian dish made of French fries, cheese curds, and gravy.

  2. Maple-glazed Porkchop: A flavorful dish made with local, seasonal ingredients, featuring the distinctive taste of Canadian maple.

  3. Butter Tarts: A popular dessert made with a sweet, syrupy filling and a flaky crust.

Local Beverages

  1. Local Beer: Smiths Falls is home to multiple microbreweries where you can sample locally brewed beer.

  2. Wine: Smiths Falls and the surrounding region also have several wineries producing award-winning ice wines.

Where to Stay in Smiths Falls: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Smiths Falls offers a wide variety of accommodations to suit different tastes and budgets. Here are some options:

  1. Best Western Smiths Falls Hotel: A modern hotel with comfortable rooms, a restaurant, and easy access to the town’s attractions.

  2. Parkview Guest House: A renovated century-old home with four well-appointed guest rooms and river views.

  3. Roger’s Motel: A budget-friendly option with clean rooms and easy access to the town’s amenities.

What to Buy in Smiths Falls: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When in Smiths Falls, don’t forget to pick up some unique souvenirs and locally made products to take home. Some popular options include:

  1. Maple Syrup: A classic Canadian product, made from the sap of maple trees.

  2. Handmade Crafts: Visit local galleries and markets to discover handmade crafts by local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Smiths Falls

Smiths Falls has a friendly and welcoming culture, with a deep appreciation for its natural and cultural heritage. It’s customary to greet people with a smile and engage in friendly conversation. Respect for the environment is also highly valued.

Best Time to Visit Smiths Falls

Smiths Falls is enjoyable all year round, with each season offering its unique beauty and experiences. Summer is perfect for outdoor activities, while fall brings stunning autumn foliage. In winter, enjoy ice-skating, snowshoeing, and cross-country skiing. Spring is ideal for birdwatching, and nature walks along the blooming wildflowers.

Do’s and Don’ts of Smiths Falls

Do:

  2. Explore the town’s history and culture.

  3. Spend time outdoors.

Don’t:

  2. Ignore safety rules while participating in outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Smiths Falls

Smiths Falls is generally a safe town, but it’s always a good idea to take precautions when traveling. Keep valuables in a secure place and be aware of your surroundings.

Health in Smiths Falls

Smiths Falls has several medical facilities, including hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies, providing visitors with easy access to healthcare services.

Smiths Falls is a small town, and you can easily explore it on foot, by bike, or by car.

Where to Go Next from Smiths Falls: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
