Introduction to Trenton

Trenton, Ontario, is a charming town in Quinte-Northumberland, located in Eastern Ontario, Canada. Known for its beautiful natural landscapes and rich cultural heritage, Trenton is a popular tourist destination that offers a wide range of activities, attractions, and experiences for visitors of all ages.

This comprehensive travel guide contains everything you need to know to plan your trip to Trenton, including a list of the top places to visit, the best hiking and biking routes, the best things to do, the must-try dishes and local beverages, where to stay, what to buy as souvenirs, and much more. So whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, read on to discover the best of what Trenton has to offer.
