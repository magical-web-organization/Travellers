Introduction to Edson

Edson, located in Foothills, Central Alberta, in Alberta, in Prairies, in Canada, in North America, is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. Nestled in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains, Edson is a small town known for its natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and rich history. Whether you’re a nature lover, adventurer, history buff, or culture enthusiast, Edson offers something for everyone.
