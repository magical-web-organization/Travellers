Entwistle-Evansburg Travel Guide: Explore Alberta’s Foothills

Located in Central Alberta, Entwistle-Evansburg offers a charming blend of small-town charm, natural beauty, and rich cultural heritage. From fascinating museums to spectacular hiking trails, this destination has something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll introduce you to the best things to see and do in Entwistle-Evansburg, help you plan your itinerary, and provide practical tips on where to stay, what to eat, and how to get around.

List of Places to Visit in Entwistle-Evansburg

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re interested in Alberta’s history and culture, Entwistle-Evansburg won’t disappoint. Here are some notable cultural attractions you can explore:

  • Evansburg Heritage Park: This museum showcases the early settlement of the area, including artifacts, buildings, and equipment that offer a glimpse into the past.
  • Yellowhead Centre: It is a cultural hub of the region with its library, conference facilities, and the Wildwood Playhouse Theatre.
  • Entwistle Station Bed and Breakfast: This beautifully restored railway station offers a unique stay in a historic setting.

Natural Attractions of Entwistle-Evansburg: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Entwistle-Evansburg is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. Whether you prefer hiking, biking or just enjoying scenic views, this place has everything you need.

  • Pembina River Provincial Park: Located just a short drive from Entwistle-Evansburg, this park offers outstanding natural beauty and fun outdoor activities such as tubing, canoeing, and camping.
  • Wildwood Trail System: These trails offer an opportunity to hike, bike or walk through stunning countryside, and take in the views of the area’s natural beauty.
  • Adams Wilderness Adventures: This company offers guided tours of the region and can take you to the best spots for fishing, hunting, and outdoor recreation.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Entwistle-Evansburg

  • Wildwood Trail System: This 54 km long network of trails offers incredible scenery, with the route winding through forests and meadows, and by creeks and rivers.
  • Evansburg Trail System: This system features a variety of short and long trails that cover a range of terrains from steep climbs to easy flat walks.
  • Pembina River Trail: This 18 km long trail follows the stunning Pembina River, and offers unmatched views of the river rapids and surrounding scenery.

Best Things to Do in Entwistle-Evansburg: Activities and Tours

Entwistle-Evansburg offers an array of exciting activities and tours that will keep you entertained throughout your visit.

  • Evansburg Golf Course: This 18-hole championship golf course offers a challenging and fun experience for golf lovers of all levels.
  • Pembina River Tubing: This adventure provides an exceptional water experience in a natural setting, perfect for families or groups.
  • Adams Wilderness Adventures: This company offers a range of guided tours that take you into the wilderness, where you can fish, hunt, or explore to your heart’s content.

Week-Long Itinerary for Entwistle-Evansburg

Day 1: Arrive at Entwistle-Evansburg. Day 2: Explore Evansburg Heritage Park and Yellowhead Centre. Day 3: Enjoy the natural beauty of Wildwood Trail System and Pembina River Trail. Day 4: Visit Entwistle Station Bed and Breakfast, and try horseback riding with Adams Wilderness Adventures. Day 5: Take a day trip to Pembina River Provincial Park, try tubing, and enjoy a picnic lunch. Day 6: Play golf at Evansburg Golf Course and visit nearby attractions. Day 7: Depart from Entwistle-Evansburg.

Food and Drink Scene of Entwistle-Evansburg

Must-Try Dishes

  • The Yellowhead Inn: A family-friendly restaurant serving delicious dishes like Alberta beef burger, fish and chips, and Poutine.
  • Entwistle Ye Olde Farmhouse & Tea Room: This cozy café serves freshly brewed coffee and tea, home-baked cakes, and offers a unique high-tea experience.

Local Beverages

  • Prairie Breweries: Appreciate locally crafted beers from breweries in the surrounding areas, such as the Wild Mountain Brewery in Hinton.

Where to Stay in Entwistle-Evansburg: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Yellowhead Inn: This is a top destination to stay, with fully-furnished rooms, free wifi, and delicious restaurant.
  • Entwistle Station Bed and Breakfast: Re-live the history by staying at this beautifully restored railway station.
  • Wildwood Escape Bed and Breakfast: This elegant B&B offers a range of amenities, as well as great views of surrounding scenery.

What to Buy in Entwistle-Evansburg: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Evansburg Farmers’ Market: Find fresh and locally grown produce and homemade crafts & gifts.
  • Bearsun Studios Art Gallery: Bring home some beautiful paintings or sculptures that capture the essence of the area.

Local Culture and Customs of Entwistle-Evansburg

Entwistle-Evansburg is home to a rich cultural heritage that’s worth exploring.

  • Wildwood Playhouse Theatre: Watch a local play or concert, and enjoy the vibrant cultural scene in the region.
  • River Road Trading Post: Discover handmade crafts and check out the collection of unique artifacts, reflecting the heritage of the region.

Best Time to Visit Entwistle-Evansburg

The best time to visit Entwistle-Evansburg is between May and September when the weather is warm and sunny, and outdoor activities can be fully enjoyed.

Do’s and Don’ts of Entwistle-Evansburg

Do:

  • Take your time to explore and appreciate the history and natural beauty of this charming Alberta destination.
  • Attend local festivals or shows to experience the unique culture and traditions of the area.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural environment.
  • Engage in activities that could harm wildlife or the environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Entwistle-Evansburg

  • Take precautions from wildlife encountered on the hiking trails, they can be aggressive if they feel threatened.
  • Be aware of the sudden weather changes in the region.

Health in Entwistle-Evansburg

  • The nearest medical center is the Evansburg Health Center (20 minutes drive) available from Monday to Friday.

Getting to Entwistle-Evansburg: Transportation Options and Logistics

There are several transportation options available to get to Entwistle-Evansburg.

Airports Near Entwistle-Evansburg

The nearest international airport is in Edmonton International Airport (EIA), located 2 hours west of Entwistle-Evansburg.

Bus Stations Near Entwistle-Evansburg

The closest bus station is in Edson, which is 45 minutes west of Entwistle-Evansburg.

Other Options

Renting a car or driving your car to Entwistle-Evansburg is recommended. Major highways connect the town to Edmonton and Calgary, making it an easy drive to reach this small town.

How to Get Around in Entwistle-Evansburg

To explore the surrounding area, renting a car is recommended. Alternatively, you can bring your own car for the trip.

Where to Go Next from Entwistle-Evansburg: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Jasper National Park (1.5 hours drive).
  • Hinton (1 hour drive).
  • Fort Assiniboine (45 minutes drive).

Conclusion

Entwistle-Evansburg is a hidden gem in Central Alberta, offering a perfect mix of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and scenic beauty. Whether you want to explore the region’s history, take a leisurely hike or bike ride, or indulge in delicious local dishes, this destination will exceed your expectations. So, plan your visit today, and discover the wonders of Entwistle-Evansburg for yourself.
