Introduction to Tofield

Located in East Central Alberta, Tofield is a charming town that offers visitors a glimpse into the rich history and culture of Alberta. With a population of just over 2,000 people, Tofield offers a peaceful oasis away from the bustling cities and offers outdoor adventurers ample opportunities to explore Alberta’s natural scenery.

List of Places to Visit in Tofield

If you’re planning a trip to Tofield, make sure to check out the following tourist spots.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Tofield and Area Museum: Discover the history of Tofield and explore the exhibits showcasing the lives and contributions of Tofield’s early pioneers.
  2. Bruce Stampede: Experience the thrill of a traditional rodeo event and take part in various cultural activities.
  3. Ukrainian Cultural Heritage Village: Immerse yourself in the vibrant Ukrainian culture and history by exploring the reconstructed village which includes more than 30 historical buildings.

Natural Attractions of Tofield: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Beaverhill Lake Nature Centre: Take a stroll along the boardwalk trail and indulge in birdwatching activities.
  2. Rundle’s Mission: Explore the scenic view of the North Saskatchewan River Valley and the 1880s mission site.
  3. Tofield Golf Course: Play a round of golf amidst the scenic and serene beauty of Tofield’s golf course.
  4. Cooking Lake-Blackfoot Provincial Recreation Area: Enjoy the beauty of the natural park by hiking, horseback riding, picnicking, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tofield

Tofield and its surroundings offer excellent opportunities for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Some of the best routes for hiking and biking in and around Tofield are:

  1. Tofield Trail System: The Tofield trail system connects Tofield’s residential and industrial area and extends through the Beaverhill Lake Nature Centre.
  2. Rundle’s Mission Nature Reserve Trail: Take the scenic route towards Rundle’s Mission Nature Reserve Trail and experience the outstanding natural splendor of the North Saskatchewan River Valley.

Best Things to Do in Tofield: Activities and Tours

  1. Tofield Corn Maze: Experience the thrill of getting lost in a giant corn maze adventure.
  2. Tofield Snow Goose Chase: Take part in an early spring birding festival to witness the migration of snow geese and swans.
  3. Tofield Curling Club: Enjoy a game of curling and socialize with other club members or visitors.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tofield

Day 1: Visit Tofield and Area Museum and explore the agricultural and cultural history of Tofield.

Day 2: Take the scenic Rundle’s Mission Nature Reserve Trail and discover the North Saskatchewan River Valley.

Day 3: Go birdwatching at Beaverhill Lake Nature Centre and spend a peaceful afternoon with local fauna.

Day 4: Try horseback riding and fishing or kayaking in the Cooking Lake-Blackfoot Provincial Recreation Area.

Day 5: Visit the Ukrainian Cultural Heritage Village and discover the beautiful Eastern European culture.

Day 6: Take a day trip to Elk Island National Park for exploring numerous hiking/biking trails, camping, and night sky observation.

Day 7: Experience the thrill of a traditional rodeo event at the Bruce Stampede.

Food and Drink Scene of Tofield

Tofield offers an eclectic mix of restaurants, cafes, and bars serving a variety of local and international cuisines. Some must-try dishes include:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Poutine: A Canadian specialty of French fries topped with gravy and cheese curds.
  2. Perogies: A staple Ukrainian dish made with dough filled with various fillings such as cheese, potato, or sauerkraut.
  3. Bison Burgers: A flavorful and lean alternative to beef burgers, and a favorite of the region.

Local Beverages

  1. Big Rock Traditional Ale: A popular Alberta-based craft beer that offers a perfect blend of malt and hops.
  2. Caesars: A uniquely Canadian drink made with tomato juice, vodka, Clamato juice, and spices.

Where to Stay in Tofield: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Tofield has a variety of accommodation options to suit every budget and preference. Some of the top-rated ones include:

  1. Heartland Hotel: A cozy hotel with all modern amenities and a fitness center.
  2. Radway Lodge: A budget-friendly option with basic amenities and comfortable rooms.
  3. Super 8 by Wyndham: A budget-friendly hotel that provides all contemporary amenities and a continental breakfast.

What to Buy in Tofield: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Handmade Crafts by Tofield Art Club: Visit the Tofield Art Club and purchase unique handmade crafts such as paintings, pottery, and glassware.
  2. Saskatchewan Berry Products: Take home some delectable homemade berry products such as preserves, jams, and syrups made exclusively with fresh and locally produced berries.

Local Culture and Customs of Tofield

Tofield celebrates a rich history of Ukrainian settlement, and visitors can enjoy the local culture through various activities, festivals, and exhibits.

Best Time to Visit Tofield

The best time to visit Tofield is from May to September when the weather is pleasant, and numerous outdoor activities and festivals are hosted.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tofield

Do:

  1. Respect the local culture and customs.
  2. Explore the beautiful natural scenery and indulge in outdoor activities.
  3. Visit and support local businesses.

Don’t:

  1. Litter in public places.
  2. Drink and drive.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tofield

Tofield is a safe place for tourists, and visitors must take the necessary precautions to ensure their safety. Keep an eye out for local wildlife in natural areas following Tofield’s safety guidelines.

Health in Tofield

Medical facilities in Tofield are limited, and visitors must carry their necessary medications and medical supplies.

Getting to Tofield: Transportation Options and Logistics

Tofield is accessible from various modes of transportation, including:

Airports Near Tofield

  1. Edmonton International Airport - 70 km
  2. Calgary International Airport - 338 km

Bus Stations Near Tofield

  1. Greyhound: A convenient bus service link from Edmonton, the nearest city to Tofield.

Other Options

  1. Driving: Tofield lies just off Highway 14 between Edmonton and Viking, which provides an ideal route from neighboring locations.

How to Get Around in Tofield

Tofield is a small town, and walking or cycling would be the best option to explore the town. If you’re planning to visit the natural areas around Tofield, renting a car would be an excellent option.

Where to Go Next from Tofield: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Elk Island National Park: A beautiful park located 50 km away from Tofield offers numerous hiking trails, camping, fishing, and night sky observation.
  2. Vegreville: A small town that showcases the work of a Ukrainian sculptor and painter, Paul Kane.

Conclusion

Tofield is an incredible destination that offers a blend of history, culture, and outdoor adventure. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can explore the best tourist spots, activities, food, and drink scene of Tofield and plan your trip accordingly. Enjoy every moment of your trip to this serene Alberta town and explore the natural and cultural wonders that Tofield has to offer.
