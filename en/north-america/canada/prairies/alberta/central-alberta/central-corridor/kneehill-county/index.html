Introduction to Kneehill County

Kneehill County is a charming destination in Central Alberta that offers a wealth of attractions and activities for visitors of all ages. Home to scenic vistas, historic landmarks, and natural wonders, Kneehill County is a must-visit destination for those seeking an authentic rural experience in Alberta.

Located in the Central Corridor of Alberta, Kneehill County is easily accessible by car, making it a convenient destination for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. With rolling hills, winding rivers, and vibrant communities, Kneehill County is a perfect destination for nature lovers and culture enthusiasts alike.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take you on a tour of Kneehill County, highlighting the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay. So pack your bags and get ready for an unforgettable adventure in the heart of Alberta!

List of Places to Visit in Kneehill County

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kneehill County is steeped in history and boasts a number of museums and historical sites that showcase the region’s rich heritage. Some of the must-visit attractions include:

  • Three Hills Museum: Located in the town of Three Hills, this museum offers a glimpse into the past of Kneehill County, with exhibits showcasing local history, culture, and industry.

  • Torrington Gopher Hole Museum: Located in the village of Torrington, this quirky museum features stuffed gophers dressed up as locals and famous figures, providing a one-of-a-kind window into the town’s past.

  • Morrin Cultural Centre: Located in the village of Morrin, this beautifully restored building was once a schoolhouse and now serves as a cultural centre, hosting concerts and exhibits throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of Kneehill County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kneehill County is home to a diverse range of natural wonders, from rolling hills and winding rivers to pristine forests and rocky canyons. Whether you’re an avid hiker or simply enjoy taking in the scenery, there’s something for everyone in Kneehill County. Some of the must-visit natural attractions include:

  • Dry Island Buffalo Jump Provincial Park: This beautiful park is home to a number of unique geological formations, including hoodoos, badlands, and canyons. Visitors can hike, bike, or horseback ride on one of the many trails that wind through the park, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

  • Horseshoe Canyon: This impressive canyon is a natural wonder that will take your breath away. With towering cliffs, unique rock formations, and stunning vistas, Horseshoe Canyon is a must-visit destination for anyone visiting Kneehill County.

  • Kneehill Creek Nature Trail: This beautiful trail offers a leisurely walk along the Kneehill Creek, showcasing the natural beauty of the region. With ample opportunities for bird-watching and wildlife-spotting, this trail is a great option for families with young children.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kneehill County

For those looking to get active and explore the natural beauty of Kneehill County, there are many hiking and biking routes to choose from. Some of the top options include:

  • Cottonwood Coulee Trail: This 10-kilometre loop trail offers beautiful views of the surrounding countryside, with wildflowers, wildlife, and historic landmarks along the way. Hiking is a popular activity on this well-maintained trail, but it is also suitable for mountain biking and horseback riding.

  • Kneehill Valley Trail: This scenic trail takes visitors through the heart of Kneehill County, passing through charming communities and offering stunning views of rolling hills, prairies, and forests along the way. The trail is suitable for both hiking and biking and is well-marked and maintained.

Best Things to Do in Kneehill County: Activities and Tours

In addition to the natural and cultural attractions that Kneehill County has to offer, there are also many activities and tours to enjoy. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Go on a wildlife safari: Kneehill County is home to a variety of wildlife, including deer, elk, moose, and coyotes. Join a guided wildlife safari to get up close and personal with these majestic creatures and learn about their habits and habitats.

  • Taste local specialties: Kneehill County is known for its delicious local cuisine, such as Alberta beef, bison, and wild rice. Visit one of the many local restaurants or food stalls to sample these local specialties and get a taste of the local flavour.

  • Join a guided tour: There are many guided tours available in Kneehill County, including those that focus on history, culture, and nature. Join an experienced guide to learn more about the region and its many attractions.

Week-Long Itinerary for Kneehill County

For those looking to spend an extended amount of time in Kneehill County, here is a suggested week-long itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrive in Three Hills and explore the town’s many museums and cultural attractions.

  • Day 2: Visit Dry Island Buffalo Jump Provincial Park for a day of hiking, biking, or horseback riding.

  • Day 3: Explore the natural beauty of Horseshoe Canyon and take in the stunning vistas.

  • Day 4: Enjoy a wildlife safari and get up close and personal with the region’s many animals.

  • Day 5: Sample local specialties and visit local restaurants and food stalls to taste the local flavour.

  • Day 6: Join a guided tour of Kneehill County’s many cultural and historical landmarks.

  • Day 7: Depart Kneehill County, taking with you memories of a truly unforgettable experience.

Food and Drink Scene of Kneehill County

Must-Try Dishes

Kneehill County is known for its delicious local cuisine, with many dishes featuring locally sourced ingredients. Some of the must-try dishes include:

  • Alberta beef: Alberta is known for producing some of the best beef in the world, and Kneehill County is no exception. Enjoy a juicy steak or burger at one of the many local restaurants or food stalls.

  • Bison burgers: Bison is another locally sourced ingredient that is frequently featured on Kneehill County menus. Enjoy a bison burger for a unique taste of the region’s local cuisine.

  • Wild Rice Salad: Kneehill County is surrounded by pristine forests, including the boreal forest, which is home to wild rice. Try a wild rice salad as a tasty and healthy snack.

Local Beverages

In addition to delicious local cuisine, Kneehill County also has a burgeoning craft drink scene. Here are some of the top local beverages to try:

  • Locally roasted coffee: Kneehill County is home to a number of coffee roasters who take pride in sourcing only the finest beans. Enjoy a cup of rich, flavourful coffee with the local flavour.

  • Craft beer: Several local microbreweries have popped up in Kneehill County in recent years. Sample a range of unique craft beers to get a taste of the local brewing scene.

Where to Stay in Kneehill County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kneehill County offers a range of accommodation options to suit every budget and taste. Some of the top options include:

  • Three Hills Inn and Suites: This hotel is located in the heart of Three Hills and offers comfortable rooms, free WiFi, and a complimentary breakfast.

  • The AmeriBox Hostel: This charming hostel is located in Trochu and offers affordable dormitory-style accommodation for travellers on a budget.

  • The Historic Acme Hotel: This beautifully restored hotel is located in the village of Acme and offers a unique glimpse into the region’s history.

What to Buy in Kneehill County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kneehill County is known for its locally made products and crafts, making it a great destination for souvenir shopping. Some of the top local products to bring home include:

  • Handmade quilts: Kneehill County is home to a number of artisans who create beautiful handmade quilts using traditional techniques.

  • Pottery: Many local potters create unique and beautiful ceramics, including dishware, vases, and decorative items.

  • Local honey: Kneehill County is home to a number of apiaries that produce delicious local honey, perfect for sweetening your tea or for use in cooking.

Local Culture and Customs of Kneehill County

Kneehill County has a rich cultural heritage and is home to a number of customs and traditions that have been passed down through generations. Some of the local customs and traditions to keep in mind include:

  • Respect for nature: Kneehill County is home to a diverse range of natural wonders, and locals place a high value on preserving these natural treasures for future generations.

  • Hospitality: Kneehill County is known for its friendly locals who place a high value on hospitality and welcoming visitors into their communities.

Best Time to Visit Kneehill County

Kneehill County is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit depends on your interests and preferences. Here’s a breakdown of the best times to visit based on the season:

  • Spring: Spring is a great time to visit Kneehill County, especially for those who enjoy hiking and bird-watching. Wildflowers are in bloom, and migrating birds can be seen passing through the region.

  • Summer: Summer is the peak tourist season in Kneehill County, with warm weather and a wide range of outdoor activities and events taking place.

  • Fall: Fall is a beautiful time to visit Kneehill County, with the leaves changing colours and many harvest-themed events taking place.

  • Winter: Winter is a great time to visit Kneehill County for those who enjoy winter sports. The region is home to several ski resorts and outdoor skating rinks.
