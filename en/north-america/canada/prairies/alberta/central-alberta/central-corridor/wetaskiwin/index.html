Nestled in Central Alberta, Wetaskiwin is a hidden gem that offers an abundance of natural beauty, rich history, and intriguing culture. This charming town boasts a plethora of exciting activities and attractions such as historical sites, museums, scenic views, outdoor adventures, delicious food, and much more.

In this travel guide, we have compiled a comprehensive list of must-visit places, things to do, and local arts and culture of Wetaskiwin. Whether you are a history buff, an adventure seeker, or a food connoisseur, there’s something for everyone in Wetaskiwin.

List of Places to Visit in Wetaskiwin

Wetaskiwin is endowed with numerous fascinating attractions that are sure to take your breath away. From museums, historical sites, outdoor adventures to scenic views, the following list of must-visit places in Wetaskiwin has it all.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Wetaskiwin is steeped in history, and there’s no better way to discover it than to visit its museums and historical places.

  1. Reynolds-Alberta Museum

    This museum is home to an extensive collection of vintage vehicles, aircraft, and agricultural equipment that documents Alberta’s history of transportation and technology.

  2. Canada’s Aviation Hall of Fame

    This museum is dedicated to recognizing and honoring the pioneers who have contributed significantly to Canada’s aviation industry.

  3. Wetaskiwin & District Heritage Museum

    This museum showcases the history and culture of Wetaskiwin and the surrounding district through its exhibits of artifacts, photographs, and documents.

Natural Attractions of Wetaskiwin: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Wetaskiwin offers various outdoor adventures and scenic views perfect for nature lovers.

  1. By-the-Lake Park

    Located beside the stunning Pigeon Lake, By-the-Lake Park provides a peaceful and picturesque setting for a picnic, stroll, or a swim.

  2. Peace Hills Park

    This park provides a serene spot to relax and enjoy nature with various amenities such as hiking routes, picnic areas, and fishing spots.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Wetaskiwin

Wetaskiwin offers a plethora of hiking and biking trails that provide stunning views of nature. Whether you are a beginner or an expert, there’s a route suited for you.

  1. Pigeon Lake Provincial Park Hiking Trail

    This hiking trail meanders through stunning forests, wetlands, and lakeshore, providing a unique opportunity to see the wildlife and enjoy the scenery.

  2. By-the-Lake Park Trail

    This trail loops around By-the-Lake Park, offering an easy walk with beautiful views of the lake.

Best Things to Do in Wetaskiwin: Activities and Tours

Wetaskiwin has a plethora of exciting things to do for visitors of all ages. From outdoor adventures to unique tours, there’s something for everyone.

  1. Boyle Street Community Services Walking Tours

    These walking tours, organised by Boyle Street Community Services, take you on a journey through Wetaskiwin’s history, from its humble beginnings to its current status as a thriving community.

  2. Pigeon Lake Provincial Park Boat Tours

    Take a scenic boat ride on Pigeon Lake and enjoy the stunning views while learning about the local history, ecology, and culture of the area.

Week-Long Itinerary for Wetaskiwin

A week in Wetaskiwin is a perfect way to experience all the town has to offer. Here is a recommended itinerary:

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit the Reynolds-Alberta Museum and the Canada’s Aviation Hall of Fame
Day 2 Take a walking tour of the Wetaskiwin & District Heritage Museum and explore the town’s local shops
Day 3 Hike the Pigeon Lake Provincial Park Hiking Trail or take a boat tour
Day 4 Visit Peace Hills Park and enjoy a picnic
Day 5 Try out some of Wetaskiwin’s local cuisine at one of its restaurants
Day 6 Take a bike ride around By-the-Lake Park Trail
Day 7 Visit one of the nearby towns such as Camrose or Lacombe and explore their attractions

Food and Drink Scene of Wetaskiwin

Wetaskiwin’s food and drink scene is a culinary gem that offers a variety of dishes and beverages sure to tantalize your tastebuds.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Perogies

    These boiled dough dumplings stuffed with mashed potatoes, onions, and cheese are a must-try dish in Wetaskiwin.

  2. Sausages

    Wetaskiwin is famous for its sausages made from the highest quality meat and traditional seasoning.

Local Beverages

  1. Local Micro-Breweries

    Wetaskiwin boasts several local micro-breweries that offer a variety of unique craft beers brewed by local artisans.

  2. Sweet Sips Sugar Shack

    This quaint local business offers handcrafted sodas, gourmet hot chocolate, and specialty coffee.

Where to Stay in Wetaskiwin: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Wetaskiwin has a variety of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences.

  1. Wetaskiwin Motel

    This budget-friendly motel is located close to all the popular attractions and provides comfortable accommodation for travellers.

  2. Super 8 By Wyndham Wetaskiwin

    This stylish hotel provides modern amenities and spacious rooms to make your stay comfortable and enjoyable.

What to Buy in Wetaskiwin: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Wetaskiwin is home to several local businesses selling unique souvenirs and local products that reflect the town’s heritage.

  1. Museums Gift Shops

    The gift shops of the Reynolds-Alberta Museum and the Wetaskiwin & District Heritage Museum offer a variety of souvenirs such as books, toys, and apparel.

  2. Wetaskiwin Farmers Market

    The Wetaskiwin Farmers Market, held every Saturday, offers fresh fruits, vegetables, baked goods, and crafts made by local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Wetaskiwin

Wetaskiwin’s culture is rooted in history and embraces diversity. The town is home to various cultural activities and events that help preserve and celebrate the town’s rich heritage.

Best Time to Visit Wetaskiwin

The best time to visit Wetaskiwin is during the summer months from June to August when the weather is warm and pleasant, and most attractions are open.

Do’s and Don’ts of Wetaskiwin

Do:

  1. Respect Wetaskiwin’s diverse culture and heritage.

  2. Follow all rules and regulations of the parks and attractions.

Don’t:

  1. Litter or damage the parks or attractions.

  2. Disrespect the local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Wetaskiwin

Wetaskiwin is a safe town with low crime rates.

Health in Wetaskiwin

The town has several medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics, to cater to the health needs of visitors.

Getting to Wetaskiwin: Transportation Options and Logistics

Wetaskiwin can be reached by air, road, and rail, with various transport options available.

Airports Near Wetaskiwin

Edmonton International Airport, situated 75 km north of Wetaskiwin, is the closest airport to the town.

Bus Stations Near Wetaskiwin

Greyhound operates several bus services that connect Wetaskiwin with other towns and cities.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent cars from the Edmonton International Airport or other nearby locations to explore the town and its surroundings.

How to Get Around in Wetaskiwin

Wetaskiwin is a small town that can be easily explored on foot, bicycle, or private vehicle.

Where to Go Next from Wetaskiwin: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Wetaskiwin’s proximity to several other towns and cities makes it an excellent base for exploring the surrounding area. Some popular nearby destinations include:

  1. Camrose

    Located 30 km east of Wetaskiwin, Camrose offers a variety of cultural attractions, shopping venues, and outdoor adventures.

  2. Lacombe

    Situated 60 km west of Wetaskiwin, Lacombe’s highlights include historic sites, cultural attractions, and scenic parks.

Conclusion

Wetaskiwin, a hidden gem in Central Alberta, is a must-visit destination for those seeking a diverse blend of natural beauty, rich history, and intriguing culture. This comprehensive travel guide provides all the necessary information to help you plan a fun-filled and memorable trip to Wetaskiwin. Plan your visit now and discover all the town has to offer.
