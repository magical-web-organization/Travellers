Introduction to Westlock

Nestled in the Northern Alberta region of Prairies, Westlock is a beautiful town enriched with authentic Alberta culture and history. Known for its peaceful countryside and small-town charm, Westlock has a lot to offer to visitors. From historical museums to scenic hiking and biking trails, and exciting outdoor adventures, there is something for everyone in Westlock.

List of Places to Visit in Westlock

To explore Westlock’s charm and beauty, here is a comprehensive list of places that you can visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Westlock Pioneer Museum: Explore the history of Westlock through the collection of artifacts, photographs, and stories featured at the museum.
  • Westlock & District Agricultural Fair and Heavy Horse Pulling Competition: If you are visiting in the summer, don’t miss out on the fair and the heavy horse pulling competition, which celebrates the tradition of farming in Alberta.
  • Spirit River Museum: Experience the rich culture and history of indigenous peoples of Alberta by visiting the Spirit River Museum.

Natural Attractions of Westlock: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Westlock Rotary Park Trail: Scenic and family-friendly, the Westlock Rotary Park Trail offers a scenic stroll around the town.
  • Pembina River Tubing Adventure: Embark on a journey of adventurous water tubing down the Pembina river that offers scenic views of the countryside.
  • Hidden Valley Wilderness Retreat: Get away from the hustle and bustle of daily life by staying at the Hidden Valley Wilderness Retreat, which offers camping, hiking, and bird watching.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Westlock

Westlock offers a variety of hiking and biking trails for people of all skills level, which includes:

  • The Westlock Rotary Trail: Suitable for all level hikers, the trail takes you through the scenic corners of the town.
  • Westlock Wheat Kings Trail: An 11.3-kilometre hike, the trail showcases striking views of the wheat fields surrounding the town.
  • Manawan Recreation Park: A beautiful woodland park with picnic areas, playgrounds, and hiking trails that suit all levels of hikers.

Best Things to Do in Westlock: Activities and Tours

  • Westlock Cultural Arts Centre: Offers a range of exciting activities like dance, music classes, arts, and crafts workshops.
  • Westlock and District Aquatic Centre: A family-friendly attraction with water slides, diving boards, and a hot tub.
  • Big Knife Provincial Park: Ideal for camping and outdoor activities like kayaking, fishing, and wildlife viewing.

Food and Drink Scene of Westlock

Westlock offers a range of culinary indulgences to visitors. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Alberta’s homemade sausages
  • Edmonton’s Famous Gateway Grill
  • Blueberry Buckle Cake, which is the town’s specialty dessert.

### Local Beverages

  • Local craft beers, including Peace River Brewing’s “Devil’s Coulee” and Town Square Brewing’s “Westlock Wheat.”
  • Honey mead from Rosewood Estates.

Where to Stay in Westlock: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Westlock has a range of accommodation options, including hotels, guesthouses, and hostels, to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some top recommendations:

  • Westlock Inn: A three-star hotel that offers comfortable rooms and convenient amenities like an on-site restaurant and bar.
  • Elysa’s Retreat: A serene and peaceful retreat for those who want to get away from the hustle and bustle of daily life.
  • Westlock Vacation Farm: An ideal place for a family vacation with a farm setting and pet-friendly accommodation options.

Local Culture and Customs of Westlock

Westlock is enriched with traditions of Alberta’s farming community. The spirit of community involvement and rural-based festivals are well imbibed in the local culture. Visitors can experience this by attending cultural events and local fairs during their stay.

Best Time to Visit Westlock

The best time to visit Westlock is between June and August when the weather is pleasant, and various outdoor activities like tubing and camping are more accessible.

Do’s and Don’ts of Westlock

Do:

  • Respect local customs and beliefs
  • Explore the town by foot or bicycle rather than by car, which will allow you to soak in the local charm.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages to experience the local culture fully.

Don’t:

  • Litter around natural attractions or historical sites.
  • Be loud and aggressive in public spaces
  • Ignore safety guidelines while indulging in outdoor activities like hiking and camping.

Safety and Health

Safety in Westlock

Westlock is a peaceful and safe town with low crime rates. However, visitors must take necessary precautions to avoid any mishaps and follow general precautions like keeping their valuables safe, not leaving their belongings unattended, and following traffic guidelines.

Health in Westlock

The healthcare system in Westlock is well equipped, and visitors have access to emergency services and medical facilities in case of emergency or need.

Getting to Westlock: Transportation Options and Logistics

Westlock is easy to access via major highways and is conveniently located only 90 minutes from Edmonton, Alberta.

Airports Near Westlock

The nearest airport to Westlock is Edmonton International Airport, which is 114 km away.

Bus Stations Near Westlock

Greyhound provides bus services from Edmonton to Westlock, which takes around 2 hours and 30 minutes.

Other Options

Visitors can also access the town through personal vehicles, intercity buses, and car rentals.

How to Get Around in Westlock

The best way to get around Westlock is through personal vehicles or bicycles. The town is small, and the majority of attractions are located within walking or cycling distance. Visitors can also opt for car rental services for ease of access.

Where to Go Next from Westlock: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Westlock is surrounded by beautiful towns and natural attractions that one can explore after visiting Westlock:

  • Pembina River Provincial Park
  • Athabasca Sand Dunes Provincial Park
  • Sturgeon County

Conclusion

Westlock is an ideal town for people who want to enjoy the peaceful countryside and explore Alberta’s rich history and tradition. With a range of attractions, cultural experiences, and outdoor adventures, Westlock has something for everyone to indulge in. Plan your visit today to experience the magic of Westlock.
