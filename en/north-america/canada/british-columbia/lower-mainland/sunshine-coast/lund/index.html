Introduction to Lund

Lund is a small village located in Sunshine Coast, in Lower Mainland, in British Columbia, Canada. It is surrounded by natural beauty and offers a variety of outdoor activities. Lund is a gateway to Desolation Sound and is known as the seafood capital of the Sunshine Coast.

List of Places to Visit in Lund

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Historic Lund Hotel: This hotel was built-in 1895 and is a significant landmark in Lund. It serves as an important stopover for hikers and is known for its rich history.
  • Powell River Historical Museum: This museum showcases the history of Powell River, and its exhibits include photographs, maps, original artifacts and historical reconstructions.

Natural Attractions of Lund: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Desolation Sound: Desolation Sound is a rugged, wilderness area that provides an outstanding recreational destination for kayakers, boaters, and hikers. Visitors can enjoy fishing, swimming, and wildlife sightings while basking in spectacular scenery.
  • Sunshine Coast Trail: It is a 180 km recreational trail system that covers all kinds of terrain, including forest, coastal, and mountain settings. The trail is open all year round and is perfect for hiking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lund

  • Sunshine Coast Trail: The trail is a combination of trails that run for about 180 km. The trail starts from Sarah Point in Desolation Sound and ends at Saltery Bay Ferry Terminal.
  • Okeover Arm Provincial Park: The park offers a variety of trails that run through the lush, old-growth forest, marshes, and along the rocky coastline. The hiking trails are suitable for all skill levels.

Best Things to Do in Lund: Activities and Tours

  • Kayaking/Canoeing: Visitors can enjoy kayaking/canoeing in Desolation Sound, where they can experience the stunning beauty of the surrounding landscape and be in close proximity to the marine wildlife.
  • Fishing: Lund is a haven for anglers. Fishing can be done in the local lakes, rivers, and bays.
  • Boating Tour: Visitors can enjoy a breathtaking boating tour of Desolation Sound. The boat tours take visitors through beautiful waterways, past towering mountains, and offshore islands.

Food and Drink Scene of Lund

Must-Try Dishes

  • Seafood Chowder: A rich and creamy soup made with locally sourced seafood, it is a must-try when in Lund.
  • Baked Salmon: Freshly caught salmon baked with herbs and spices and served with vegetables.
  • Clam Chowder: A classic clam chowder is made with fresh clams and vegetables, making it a delicious meal.

Local Beverages

  • BC Craft Beer: Lund has a large selection of craft beer to try, made locally in British Columbia.
  • Wine: British Columbia is known for its wide selection of wines. Visitors should try some of the locally produced wines while in Lund.

Where to Stay in Lund: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Historic Lund Hotel: This hotel has been standing for over 100 years, offering visitors comfortable accommodation with beautiful views. It is an ideal base to explore the surrounding area.
  • Backeddy Resort and Marina: This resort offers a range of accommodation options, including rooms, suites, and cabins. It is located right on the waterfront, offering guests perfect views and direct access to water-based activities.

What to Buy in Lund: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handmade Artifacts: Visitors can purchase locally made artifacts, including pottery, jewelry, and wood carvings, which serve as perfect souvenirs.
  • Seafood: Lund is known for its fresh seafood, including salmon, crabs, and clams. Visitors can purchase a variety of seafood on-site from local fishermen.

Local Culture and Customs of Lund

  • Respect the natural environment: Visitors are expected to respect the natural environment while enjoying the local beauty. This includes avoiding littering, respecting the wildlife, and staying on designated trails and paths.
  • Friendliness of locals: Visitors should expect friendly and hospitable locals. Visitors should also treat them with respect and courtesy while participating in traditional customs.

Best Time to Visit Lund

The best time to visit Lund is between June and September. During this time, visitors can enjoy the mild, sunny climate and participate in summer activities such as kayaking, boating, and hiking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lund

Do:

  • Respect the natural environment.
  • Try the fresh seafood.
  • Explore the historical and cultural landmarks.

Don’t:

  • Litter or harm the wildlife.
  • Disturb the locals’ peace of mind.
  • Disrespect the culture and customs of the locals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lund

Lund is a safe place to visit. Visitors should take usual precautions and avoid dark and unfamiliar areas at night.

Health in Lund

Lund has a clinic with medical staff on-site. Visitors should also have travel insurance before arriving in Lund.

Getting to Lund: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lund

  • Powell River Airport: Located a 30-minute drive from Lund, this airport provides direct flights to Vancouver Island and Vancouver.

Bus Stations Near Lund

  • Powell River Bus Depot: Located in Powell River, this station provides daily bus services to various destinations in British Columbia.

Other Options

  • Ferry: Visitors can take a ferry from Vancouver to Powell River and then drive to Lund.
  • Car Hire: Visitors can rent a car in Vancouver or Powell River and drive to Lund.

How to Get Around in Lund

Lund is a small village, and visitors can walk or bike around to explore the local attractions. Visitors can also rent cars or bicycles to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Lund: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Powell River: A nearby town located just 30 minutes away from Lund, it provides unique historical sites, scenic mountain views, and numerous attractions.
  • Campbell River: A town located on Vancouver Island, it offers historical sites and beautiful scenery.
  • Desolation Sound Provincial Park: It is located to the north of Lund and offers stunning natural wonders and recreational adventures.

Conclusion

Lund is a small village that packs a big punch when it comes to natural beauty, outdoor activities, and fresh seafood. Visitors can enjoy a variety of activities, including kayaking, hiking, and fishing, while staying in comfortable accommodations. Lund is the perfect destination for anyone who loves the outdoors and wants to experience the beauty of British Columbia.
