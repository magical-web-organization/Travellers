Introduction to New Denver

Nestled in the West Kootenays of British Columbia, Canada, lies the charming town of New Denver. With a rich history dating back to its mining roots, New Denver has now transformed into a hub for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. This quaint town boasts stunning views of the Selkirk Mountains and offers activities such as hiking, biking, fishing, and skiing.

List of Places to Visit in New Denver

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Nikkei Internment Memorial Centre: This museum commemorates the internment of Japanese Canadians during World War II, offering insight into the hardships faced by the community.
  • Silvery Slocan Museum: Learn about the history of New Denver and the surrounding areas, from the mining industry to the indigenous communities.

Natural Attractions of New Denver: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Valhalla Provincial Park: With hiking trails ranging from easy to difficult, visitors can take in the stunning views of the Selkirk Mountains, Slocan Lake, and the surrounding forests.
  • Slocan Lake: Get out on the water and enjoy canoeing, kayaking or fishing while surrounded by the breathtaking views of the mountains.

Hiking/Biking Routes of New Denver

From the easy trails in the downtown area to the more challenging hikes in the Valhalla Provincial Park, New Denver has something for every level of hiker or biker. Some popular trails include the Galena Trail and the Champion Lakes.

Best Things to Do in New Denver: Activities and Tours

  • Whitewater Rafting: Get your heart racing with a thrilling ride through the rapids of the nearby Slocan River.
  • Slocan Lake Paddleboard Rentals: Get a view of the stunning lake from a new vantage point with paddleboard rentals available in town.

Food and Drink Scene of New Denver

One of the best ways to experience a new place is through its food and New Denver offers delicious choices for every palate.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Homemade pies from The Apple Tree Cafe: These freshly baked pies are made with local fruit and the crust is always flaky and delicious.
  • Japanese-inspired dishes from Hume Hotel: From sushi to katsu curry, this restaurant offers a unique twist on traditional Japanese cuisine.

Local Beverages

  • Jammers Craft Brewery: Enjoy craft beer in a cozy and rustic setting in downtown New Denver.
  • J.L. Crowe Distillery: Sample locally made spirits such as gin, vodka, and whisky.

Where to Stay in New Denver: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Hume Hotel: This historic hotel offers comfortable rooms and is located in the center of downtown New Denver.
  • The Adventure Hotel: Affordable and modern, this hotel is a great option for those on a budget.
  • The Argenta Lodge: Located just outside of New Denver, this lodge offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and forests.

What to Buy in New Denver: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • New Denver Hoodies: Stay cozy in a hoodie that shows off your love for this hidden gem of a town.
  • Freshly made jams and preserves from the farmers’ market: These locally sourced treats make for the perfect gift or souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of New Denver

New Denver has a rich history, with a diverse mix of cultures and communities that have all left their mark on the town. Visitors can explore this history by visiting museums and historical sites and engaging with the locals, who are always happy to share their stories and traditions with visitors.

Best Time to Visit New Denver

The best time to visit New Denver is during the summer months, between June and August. During this time, the weather is warm and sunny, making it perfect for outdoor activities and exploring the town. Fall is also a great time to visit, with the foliage transforming into a stunning display of colors.

Do’s and Don’ts of New Denver

Do:

  • Respect the outdoor spaces and wildlife by following Leave No Trace principles.
  • Try the local cuisine and support small businesses.

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave trash behind on the trails or in public spaces.
  • Take advantage of the natural resources in an unethical way.

Safety and Health

New Denver is a relatively safe town and visitors should not encounter any safety issues during their stay. It is recommended to have travel health insurance as medical services are limited in the area.

Safety in New Denver

Visitors should take precautions while hiking or biking in the wilderness areas and always be aware of their surroundings. The town has a low crime rate, but visitors should always be careful with their valuables.

Health in New Denver

Visitors should be aware of the risks associated with outdoor activities, such as ticks, heatstroke, and hypothermia. It is recommended to bring appropriate clothing, sunscreen, and insect repellent.

Getting to New Denver: Transportation Options and Logistics

New Denver is located in a remote area of British Columbia, making it somewhat difficult to access by public transportation.

Airports Near New Denver

The closest airport to New Denver is Castlegar Airport which is approximately 1.5 hours away by car.

Bus Stations Near New Denver

Greyhound Bus Lines does not serve New Denver directly, but there are bus services to nearby towns such as Nelson and Castlegar.

Other Options

Renting a car or taking a private shuttle is the easiest way to access New Denver.

How to Get Around in New Denver

New Denver is a small town and can easily be explored on foot. Alternatively, visitors can rent bicycles or drive a car to explore further afield.

Where to Go Next from New Denver: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Nelson, BC: A quirky and charming town known for its arts scene.
  • Kootenay Lake: A stunning lake with plenty of activities offered year-round, such as canoeing, skiing, snowshoeing, and hiking.
  • Ainsworth Hot Springs: Relax in the hot springs or go for a hike in the surrounding wilderness.

Conclusion

New Denver offers a unique blend of history, culture, and outdoor adventures. Whether you are looking for a relaxing getaway or an adrenaline-filled vacation, this charming town has something for everyone. Plan your trip and discover the hidden gem that is New Denver.
