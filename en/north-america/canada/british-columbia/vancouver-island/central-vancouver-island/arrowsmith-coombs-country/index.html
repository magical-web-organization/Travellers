Introduction to Arrowsmith Coombs Country

Arrowsmith Coombs Country is a region located in Central Vancouver Island, British Columbia, known for its stunning natural landscapes, cultural landmarks, and adventurous outdoor activities.

The region consists of Coombs, Errington, Hilliers, Meadowood, and Whiskey Creek, and is perfect for travelers looking for an escape from the bustling city life. Arrowsmith Coombs Country has something to offer for everyone, whether you wish to explore the region’s rich cultural history, hike in the stunning wilderness, or simply unwind and enjoy some of the best food and drink in British Columbia.

List of Places to Visit in Arrowsmith Coombs Country

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The region has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors can explore the charming historical locations, such as:

  • Coombs Old Country Market
  • Milner Gardens and Woodland
  • McLean Mill National Historic Site

Natural Attractions of Arrowsmith Coombs Country: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Arrowsmith Coombs Country is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, providing endless adventure opportunities all year round. Some of the must-visit natural attractions in the region include:

  • Little Qualicum Falls Provincial Park
  • Englishman River Falls Provincial Park
  • Mount Arrowsmith Biosphere Reserve

Hiking/Biking Routes of Arrowsmith Coombs Country

The region boasts an abundance of stunning trails that offer breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains, forests, and rivers. Some of the best hiking and biking routes in the region include:

  • Top Bridge Trail
  • Link Trail
  • Englishman River Falls Trail.

Best Things to Do in Arrowsmith Coombs Country: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of thrilling activities and tours to experience in Arrowsmith Coombs Country, including:

  • Whale watching
  • Spas and wellness centers
  • Kayaking and canoeing

Week-Long Itinerary for Arrowsmith Coombs Country

Here is a suggested itinerary for a week-long visit to Arrowsmith Coombs Country:

Day 1: Arrival and settling in at a local hotel, guesthouse, or hostel.

Day 2: Visit the Coombs Old Country Market, Milner Gardens and Woodland, and the McLean Mill National Historic Site.

Day 3: Explore Little Qualicum Falls Provincial Park and have a picnic in the park.

Day 4: Experience whale watching and marine wildlife tours with a local tour provider.

Day 5: Visit Englishman River Falls Provincial Park and enjoy the Englishman River Falls Trail.

Day 6: Take a break and relax at one of the local spas and wellness centers.

Day 7: Wrap up your trip with a day of kayaking and canoeing in the Mount Arrowsmith Biosphere Reserve.

Arrowsmith Coombs Country has a unique culture and customs to discover, including the following:

Getting to Arrowsmith Coombs Country: Transportation Options and Logistics

The nearest airport is the Nanaimo Airport, which is approximately 30 minutes drive from the region.

Bus Stations Near Arrowsmith Coombs Country

The Greyhound bus service provides reliable bus services to and from Victoria and Qualicum Beach.

Other Options

You can also rent a car or take a taxi service to explore the region.
