Introduction to Desolation Sound

Desolation Sound is a hidden gem nestled in the Discovery Islands region of Vancouver Island in British Columbia, Canada. This remote wilderness area is a sea kayaking and boating paradise, offering breathtaking scenic views of mountains, forests and wildlife. The region has been home to the Klahoose First Nations for thousands of years and is rich with cultural history. Desolation Sound is the perfect destination for those looking for a peaceful retreat far from the bustling crowds.
