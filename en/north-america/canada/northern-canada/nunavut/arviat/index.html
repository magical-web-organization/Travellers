Introduction to Arviat

Located in Nunavut, Northern Canada, Arviat is a small community in the Kivalliq Region. It is known for its awe-inspiring natural beauty, and as a hub for the Inuit people. With its rich history, unique culture, and stunning landscapes, Arviat is a perfect destination for those looking for a different kind of travel experience.

List of Places to Visit in Arviat

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Arviat has several museums and historical sites that showcase the Inuit culture and history:

  • Arviat Cultural Interpretive Centre: This centre showcases the traditional and contemporary lifestyle of the Inuit.
  • Arviat Museum: This museum explores the cultural and social history of the Inuit people.
  • Hudson’s Bay Company Post: This post tells the story of the fur trade that was once an important industry in the area.

Natural Attractions of Arviat: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Arviat is also known for its natural beauty and outdoor adventures:

  • Clearwater Lake: This glacial lake offers scenic views and is perfect for canoeing and kayaking.
  • Omallik Island: This island is a popular spot for bird watching and fishing.
  • Naajuuq: This nearby mountain offers breathtaking views of the region and is a great spot for an adventurous hike.
  • Suppaqtuit River: This river is ideal for fishing and canoeing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Arviat

The area surrounding Arviat is perfect for hiking and biking. Some popular routes include:

  • Kijanniaq Territorial Park Trail: This trail takes you through a beautiful ravine, and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Naajuuq Trail: This trail is a challenging hike but offers some of the best views of the region.

Best Things to Do in Arviat: Activities and Tours

Arviat offers many exciting activities and tours that cater to different interests.

  • Dog-sledding: Experience the traditional Inuit mode of transportation and explore the snowy wilderness.
  • Ice fishing: Try your hand at ice fishing for Arctic char and lake trout.
  • Northern lights viewing: Watch the stunning aurora borealis dance across the sky on a clear night.
  • Inuit cultural experience: Immerse yourself in the Inuit culture with a traditional feast or drum dancing.

Food and Drink Scene of Arviat

Must-Try Dishes

The Inuit cuisine is unique and influenced by the local environment. Here are some must-try dishes in Arviat:

  • Arctic Char: This fish is a staple of the Inuit diet and is often smoked or baked.
  • Caribou Stew: This hearty stew is made with traditional spices and vegetables.
  • Bannock: This flatbread is ubiquitous in Inuit cuisine and is often served with jam or honey.

Local Beverages

In Arviat, you must try:

  • Kvass: This traditional Russian drink is popular in Arviat due to the strong Russian influence in the region.
  • Arctic Berry Juice: Made from local berries, this refreshing drink is perfect for the summer months.

Where to Stay in Arviat: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are a few options for accommodation in Arviat:

  • Tundra Inn: This hotel offers comfortable rooms and is centrally located in town.
  • Inns North Location: This hostel provides simple, affordable accommodation in a prime location.
  • Local Guesthouse: This is a great option for those looking for a more authentic experience in Arviat.

What to Buy in Arviat: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Arviat is known for its local arts and crafts, which make great souvenirs. Here are some ideas:

  • Inuit carvings: These carvings showcase the traditional Inuit techniques and can be found at most local shops.
  • Qiviut: This wool is derived from the muskox and is used to make warm, soft clothing items.
  • Inuit-style clothing: These clothing items are made using traditional Inuit techniques and are perfect for those looking for a unique garment.

Local Culture and Customs of Arviat

The Inuit people have a rich culture and unique customs that visitors should be aware of. It is always important to respect the local culture and traditions:

  • Elders are highly respected in the community and should be addressed as such.
  • Drum dancing is an important cultural tradition and is often found at community events.
  • It is customary to remove shoes before entering a home or other buildings.

Best Time to Visit Arviat

The best time to visit Arviat is during the summer months, from late-June to early-September. This is when the weather is mild, and most of the outdoor activities are available.

Do’s and Don’ts of Arviat

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Take part in the outdoor activities and tours.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local culture or environment.
  • Litter or damage the natural surroundings.
  • Make loud noises or disturb the peaceful environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Arviat

Arviat is a safe destination, but visitors should take appropriate precautions:

  • Make sure to dress warmly and appropriately for the weather.
  • Take care when participating in outdoor activities and tours.
  • Follow the instructions of the tour operator and local guides.

Health in Arviat

Visitors should be aware of the healthcare options in Arviat:

  • The local health centre provides basic medical services.
  • In case of an emergency, a medevac service is available for evacuation to a larger medical centre.

Getting to Arviat: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Arviat

  • Arviat Airport is the only airport serving the area, with daily flights from Winnipeg, Manitoba.

Bus Stations Near Arviat

There are no bus stations in Arviat, but the town is small enough to navigate on foot.

Other Options

  • Charter flights are available from neighbouring towns.

How to Get Around in Arviat

Arviat is a small town that can be easily explored on foot. Taxis are also available for those who prefer not to walk.

Where to Go Next from Arviat: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Churchill, Manitoba: This neighbouring town is known for its polar bear tours and unique cultural experiences.
  • Rankin Inlet, Nunavut: This nearby town is the hub of the Kivalliq Region and offers many outdoor activities.

Conclusion

Arviat is a unique destination that offers visitors a chance to experience a different type of travel. With its stunning natural landscapes, unique culture and history, and exciting activities and tours, Arviat is definitely worth a visit.
