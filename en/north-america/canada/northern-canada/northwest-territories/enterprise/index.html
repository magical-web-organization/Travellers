Introduction to Enterprise

Nestled in the heart of the Northwest Territories, Enterprise is a small community that is rich in culture and natural beauty. It is an ideal destination for travelers who want to experience the peace and tranquility of the North. This comprehensive travel guide to Enterprise will highlight the best places to visit and activities to do in the region.

List of Places to Visit in Enterprise

Here are some of the top places to visit in Enterprise:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Hay River Heritage Centre: This museum is home to an impressive collection of artifacts that showcase the history and heritage of Hay River and the surrounding region. It houses exhibits that cover a range of topics, from the fur trade to the construction of the Alaska Highway.
  • Woodland Cree Cultural Institute: The Woodland Cree Cultural Institute is a non-profit organization that is dedicated to preserving the cultural heritage of the Woodland Cree people. It offers programs and workshops that focus on traditional arts, crafts, and culture.

Natural Attractions of Enterprise: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Alexandra Falls: Located just outside of Enterprise, Alexandra Falls is one of the most stunning waterfalls in the Northwest Territories. The falls drop for 32 meters into a dramatic canyon below.
  • Lady Evelyn Falls: Lady Evelyn Falls is another impressive waterfall that can be found within a short drive of Enterprise. It is surrounded by lush forests and features crystal clear waters that are perfect for swimming.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Enterprise

Enterprise is home to some of the best hiking and biking routes in the Northwest Territories. Here are a few to consider:

  • The Hay River Trail: This scenic trail follows the historic route of the Mackenzie River, and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • The Enterprise Trail: The Enterprise Trail is a challenging mountain biking trail that winds through dense forests and rocky terrain.

Best Things to Do in Enterprise: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best activities and tours to experience in Enterprise:

  • Northern Lights Viewing Tour: The Northern Lights are a spectacular natural phenomenon that can be seen in Enterprise during the fall and winter months. Take a tour to witness the breathtaking beauty of the aurora borealis.
  • Fishing: The Northwest Territories boasts some of the best fishing in the world, and Enterprise is no exception. Cast a line in the pristine waters of the Mackenzie River, and try your luck at catching some of the region’s famous fish, such as northern pike and Arctic grayling.

Week-Long Itinerary for Enterprise

Here is a week-long itinerary for your trip to Enterprise:

Day 1 Visit the Hay River Heritage Centre, and learn about the history of the region

Food and Drink Scene of Enterprise

When it comes to food and drink, Enterprise has something to offer every traveler. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Northern Pike: The northern pike is a popular fish in the Northwest Territories and can be prepared in a variety of ways.
  • Bannock: Bannock is a traditional bread that is usually cooked on an open fire and served with honey or butter.

Local Beverages

  • Icewine: Icewine is a sweet dessert wine made from frozen grapes. It is a unique and delicious taste of the North.
  • Arctic Char Beer: This locally brewed beer is made with Arctic char and has a distinct taste and flavor that is sure to delight beer lovers.

Where to Stay in Enterprise: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Enterprise has a range of accommodation options to suit every budget and taste. Here are some of the best places to stay in Enterprise:

  • Airbnbs: Airbnb offers a number of unique and affordable accommodation options in Enterprise, including cabins, yurts, and private rooms in local homes.
  • Enterprise Hotel: The Enterprise Hotel is a cozy and comfortable hotel that offers all the amenities you need for a comfortable stay.
  • Mackenzie Rest Inn: This charming bed and breakfast is the perfect place to stay if you want to experience the warmth and hospitality of the North.

What to Buy in Enterprise: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some of the best local products and souvenirs to bring home from Enterprise:

  • Traditional Art and Crafts: Wood carvings, beadwork, and traditional clothing are all examples of the unique and beautiful traditional art and crafts that can be found in Enterprise.
  • Wild Rice: Wild rice is grown in the nearby region and is a staple in indigenous cuisine in the Northwest Territories.

Local Culture and Customs of Enterprise

The local culture of Enterprise is deeply rooted in the traditions and customs of the indigenous people of the region. Visitors to Enterprise are encouraged to be respectful of these traditions and to learn about the local culture during their stay.

Best Time to Visit Enterprise

The best time to visit Enterprise is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and the days are long. However, those interested in witnessing the Northern Lights should plan a trip during the fall or winter months, from September to March.

Do’s and Don’ts of Enterprise

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and traditions of the indigenous people of the region.
  • Go fishing on the Mackenzie River and try to catch some of the region’s famous fish, such as northern pike and Arctic grayling.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter or leave your trash behind. The North is a pristine and beautiful place, and it is important to keep it that way.
  • Do not disturb wildlife or engage in any activities that may harm the environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Enterprise

Enterprise is a safe and friendly community, with low crime rates and a welcoming and supportive atmosphere. However, visitors should still take the usual precautions, such as locking their doors and keeping an eye on their belongings, when out and about in the town.

Health in Enterprise

The Northwest Territories is home to a number of unique health challenges, such as extreme temperatures and exposure to the sun. Visitors should be prepared to protect themselves from these challenges by dressing appropriately and wearing sunscreen and other protective gear.

Getting to Enterprise: Transportation Options and Logistics

Enterprise is accessible by air and road. Here are some transportation options to consider:

Airports Near Enterprise

The closest airport to Enterprise is the Hay River/Merlyn Carter Airport (YHY), which is located just a short drive away from the town.

Bus Stations Near Enterprise

There are no bus stations in Enterprise, but visitors can take a Greyhound bus to Hay River, which is located just a short drive from the town.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi from the airport or bus station in Hay River to Enterprise.

How to Get Around in Enterprise

The best way to get around Enterprise is by car. Visitors can rent a car at the airport or in Hay River. There are also taxis available in Hay River.

Where to Go Next from Enterprise: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider after your visit to Enterprise:

  • Yellowknife: Yellowknife is the largest city in the Northwest Territories and is home to a range of cultural and natural attractions, including the Northern Lights.
  • Fort Providence: Fort Providence is a small community that offers an authentic taste of life in the North. It is located just a short drive from Enterprise and is home to a number of cultural and natural attractions.

Conclusion

Enterprise is a hidden gem in the Northwest Territories, with a rich culture and natural beauty that is sure to charm and delight visitors. From hiking and biking to fishing and cultural experiences, there is something for everyone in this beautiful town.
