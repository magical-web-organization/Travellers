Introduction to Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

Located in the north of Costa Rica, Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí is a little-known gem that is perfect for nature lovers and adventure seekers visiting the country. The town is surrounded by lush rainforests, beautiful waterfalls, and rivers that make it a paradise for eco-tourism.

List of Places to Visit in Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. INBio Park: An interactive park that offers visitors a chance to experience the biodiversity of Costa Rica.
  2. La Selva Biological Station: A research station where visitors can learn about the fauna and flora of the local area.
  3. The House of Culture and Museum: A museum highlighting the history of the local community and its traditions.
  4. San Juan Bosco Church: A beautiful church with an interesting history.

Natural Attractions of Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Sarapiquí River: A popular site for river rafting, canoeing, and kayaking.
  2. La Selva Biological Station: Tons of nature trails for hiking and bird watching.
  3. Sarapiquí Waterfall: A breathtaking waterfall with stunning views.
  4. La Tirimbina Rainforest: A rainforest reserve that offers guided tours for hiking and exploring the flora and fauna of the area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

  1. Tirimbina Rainforest Trail: A guided hike through the rainforest followed by a chance to explore the cacao plantation and learn about chocolate production.
  2. Chilamate Rainforest Trail: A self-guided hike with stunning views of the rainforest, river, and mountains.
  3. La Selva Biological Station: Over 15 miles of trails that offer visitors a chance to experience the flora and fauna of the area.

Best Things to Do in Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí: Activities and Tours

  1. River Rafting: An adrenaline-filled experience on the rapids of the Sarapiquí River.
  2. Canopy Tour: An opportunity to zipline through the treetops of the rainforest.
  3. Horseback Riding: A chance to explore the stunning scenery of the area on horseback.
  4. Chocolate Tour: An experience that takes visitors through the process of cacao to chocolate.

Week-Long Itinerary for Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

Day Activities
1 Explore the Tirimbina Reserve and go on a river rafting adventure on the Sarapiquí River.
2 Visit the La Selva Biological Station and hike along one of their trails.
3 Go on a canopy tour and visit one of the local coffee plantations.
4 Spend the day horseback riding and hiking around the local area.
5 Take a day trip to the Sarapiquí Waterfall and enjoy a picnic lunch.
6 Learn about chocolate production on the Chocolate Tour and then go on a nighttime wildlife tour.
7 Relax by the pool at one of the local hotels or take a guided birdwatching tour.

Food and Drink Scene of Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Gallo Pinto: A traditional breakfast dish of rice and beans.
  2. Casado: A typical lunch dish consisting of rice, beans, plantains, salad, and a choice of meat.
  3. Arroz con Pollo: A delicious chicken and rice dish.
  4. Delicious fresh tropical fruits such as pineapples and papayas.

Local Beverages

  1. Guaro: A sugar cane liquor that is a popular drink in Costa Rica.
  2. Horchata: A milk and rice drink that is a refreshing choice for hot days.

Where to Stay in Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Selva Verde Lodge & Rainforest Reserve: This hotel offers guests a chance to experience the rainforest and wildlife of the area.
  2. La Quinta Sarapiquí Lodge and Spa: This hotel is set in a beautiful location and offers all the necessary amenities for a comfortable stay.
  3. Ara Ambigua Lodge: A hotel with a focus on eco-tourism and sustainability.

What to Buy in Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Coffee: Costa Rican coffee has a reputation for being one of the best in the world.
  2. Handmade crafts: The local artisans produce beautiful handmade crafts, including pottery, jewelry, and woodwork.
  3. Cacao products: Chocolate is an important part of the local culture and visitors can bring home artisan chocolates and other cacao products.

Local Culture and Customs of Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

The local culture revolves around a deep respect for nature and the environment. The local people celebrate festivals and events with traditional music and dance. Visitors are encouraged to be respectful of the culture and customs of the area.

Best Time to Visit Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

The best time to visit Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí is between December and April. This is the dry season, and the weather is ideal for outdoor activities and tours.

Do’s and Don’ts of Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

Do:

  1. Take a guided tour through the rainforest.
  2. Try the local chocolate and coffee.
  3. Rent a bike and explore the local area.

Don’t:

  1. Leave your valuables unattended.
  2. Ignore safety instructions during adventure activities.
  3. Take the flora and fauna with you (leaves, flowers, seeds, animals, etc.)

Safety and Health

Safety in Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí is generally considered a safe destination for travelers. Visitors are recommended to use common sense measures to stay safe, such as not walking alone at night and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

Visitors are recommended to have up-to-date vaccinations and take other precautions such as using insect repellent to prevent mosquito-borne diseases.

Getting to Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

  1. San José International Airport
  2. Limón International Airport

Bus Stations Near Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

  1. San José Bus Station
  2. La Fortuna Bus Station

Other Options

  1. Car rental: Renting a car is a great way to explore the area on your own.
  2. Private shuttle: Private shuttles offer a comfortable and convenient way to travel.

How to Get Around in Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí

The best way to get around Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí is by bike, as it is an eco-friendly way to navigate the area. Taxis are also readily available.

Where to Go Next from Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Tortuguero National Park: A beautiful national park on the Caribbean coast known for turtle nesting.
  2. Arenal Volcano: A popular destination offering visitors a chance to see a live volcano and hot springs.
  3. La Fortuna: A charming town near the Arenal Volcano with beautiful scenery.

Conclusion

Puerto Viejo de Sarapiquí serves as an ideal destination for eco-tourism with its lush natural landscapes, diverse wildlife, and fascinating culture. This travel guide has covered everything you need to know to plan an unforgettable trip to this hidden gem of Costa Rica.
