Introduction to Noord

Noord is a small town located in the northern coast of Aruba, a popular Caribbean destination known for its pristine beaches and crystal-clear waters. It offers a blend of historic landmarks and natural attractions that appeals to all types of travelers, from families to adventure enthusiasts. Whether you want to explore its cultural heritage, go on an outdoor adventure, or simply relax by the beach, Noord will not disappoint.

List of Places to Visit in Noord

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Aruba Aloe Factory and Museum

The Aruba Aloe Factory and Museum is one of Noord’s most popular cultural attractions where you can learn about the island’s most valuable export, aloe vera. You can tour the production process, explore the museum, and even sample and purchase the products at their factory store.

Alto Vista Chapel

Alto Vista Chapel is a historic and picturesque church located on a hill overlooking Noord. It’s a place of worship for locals and a popular stop for visitors exploring the island’s cultural heritage. It offers stunning views of the Caribbean sea and the surrounding countryside.

Natural Attractions of Noord: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

California Lighthouse

The California Lighthouse is a historic and iconic landmark of Noord that offers panoramic views of the island’s northwest coast. It’s the perfect spot to watch the sunset or sunrise and is a popular destination for visitors exploring the island.

Arikok National Park

Arikok National Park is a vast natural reserve located in the heart of Aruba that covers 20% of the island’s landmass. It features diverse landscapes, including rocky coasts, desert hills, and lush vegetation, and offers a range of outdoor activities such as hiking, bird-watching, and swimming in natural pools.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Noord

Hooiberg

Hooiberg is a mountain located in the center of Aruba, offering stunning views of the island from its peak. The hike up is about 600 feet and involves a narrow staircase, but the views are worth the effort. You’ll get an unobstructed 360-degree panorama of the island’s coastline and rugged interior.

Best Things to Do in Noord: Activities and Tours

Water Sports

Noord’s beaches are ideal for water sports, such as snorkeling, diving, windsurfing, and kitesurfing. If you’re a thrill-seeker, sign up for a flyboarding or jet-skiing adventure and enjoy the adrenaline rush while exploring the Caribbean Sea’s pristine waters.

Jeep Tours

Jeep tours are a popular way to explore Noord’s natural attractions, such as the Arikok National Park, the Natural Pool, and the Alto Vista Chapel. You’ll go off-road and discover hidden sights of the island’s rugged interior while your guide shares stories and insights about the island’s history and culture.

Food and Drink Scene of Noord

Must-Try Dishes

Aruban cuisine is influenced by Indian, Spanish, and African traditions. Some must-try dishes in Noord include:

  • Keshi Yena: a traditional Aruban dish with spicy chicken and melted Gouda cheese
  • Aruban-style seafood: fresh catch of the day prepared with a tangy marinade
  • Funchi: a cornmeal-based side dish typically served with stews or grilled meat
  • Pan Bati: a sweet cornmeal pancake served with syrup or jams

Local Beverages

Aruba is famous for its Balashi beer, which is locally brewed and widely available. You can also try Coecoei, a traditional spirit made from the sap of agave plants, or sample some of the island’s flavorful tropical cocktails.

Where to Stay in Noord: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Noord offers a range of accommodation options for different budgets. Some of the best options include:

  • Ritz-Carlton, Aruba: a luxurious beachfront resort with five-star amenities, including a casino, pool, and spa
  • Boardwalk Small Hotel Aruba: a charming boutique hotel with colorful cottages, tropical gardens, and a swimming pool
  • Palm Beach Guesthouse: a budget-friendly option with cozy and bright rooms, a shared kitchen, and a rooftop terrace

What to Buy in Noord: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs or gifts, head to the Aruba Aloe Factory and Museum for some high-quality aloe vera products or visit the Caribbean Queen for handmade jewelry and crafts made by local artisans. You can also buy some Balashi beer or Coecoei to bring home as a taste of Aruba.

Local Culture and Customs of Noord

Aruba has a diverse and vibrant culture influenced by its indigenous, European, African, and Latin American heritage. Some customs and traditions to keep in mind when visiting Noord include showing respect for the local religion, which is predominantly Catholic, and learning some basic Papiamento phrases, the local language.

Best Time to Visit Noord

The best time to visit Noord is from January to April when the weather is dry and sunny, and temperatures range from 75°F to 85°F. The island gets a bit crowded during this period, so make sure to book your accommodation and tours in advance.

Do’s and Don’ts of Noord

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local food and drinks.
  • Take care of the environment and natural resources.

Don’t:

  • Leave valuables unattended.
  • Wear inappropriate clothing when visiting religious sites.
  • Harm marine life when engaging in water sports or other activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Noord

Noord is a relatively safe destination, but visitors should take some precautions, such as avoiding areas that are known for crime, locking valuables, and keeping an eye on their surroundings.

Health in Noord

Noord has a reliable healthcare system, and most hotels offer medical assistance to their guests. However, visitors should take standard precautions, such as wearing sunscreen, drinking plenty of water, and consulting a healthcare provider if necessary.

Getting to Noord: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Noord

The Queen Beatrix International Airport is the main gateway to Aruba and is located about six miles (10 kilometers) southeast of Noord. It offers direct flights to and from major North American, European, and South American destinations.

Bus Stations Near Noord

Arubus is the island’s public bus system that connects Noord with other towns on the island. The bus stops are conveniently located along the main roads, and the fares range from $2 to $5, depending on the route and distance.

Other Options

Taxis are widely available in Noord, but fares can be relatively high compared to other options. Renting a car is a popular choice for visitors who want to explore the island’s natural attractions on their own.

How to Get Around in Noord

Noord is a small town, and most of the main attractions are easily accessible by walking or cycling. Taxis and public buses are also available, and rental cars provide more flexibility and convenience.

Where to Go Next from Noord: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some nearby destinations and excursions that you can visit from Noord include:

  • Eagle Beach: a pristine and idyllic beach located about 10 minutes away from Noord
  • Oranjestad: the capital city of Aruba, located about six miles (10 kilometers) southeast of Noord, featuring historic landmarks and a vibrant shopping and entertainment scene
  • Baby Beach: a tranquil and secluded beach located on the southern coast of Aruba, featuring shallow and crystal-clear waters ideal for swimming and snorkeling.

Conclusion

Noord, Aruba, is a hidden gem that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and outdoor adventures, making it an ideal destination for all types of travelers. Whether you want to explore the island’s cultural heritage, engage in water sports, or simply relax by the beach, Noord has something to offer for everyone. Plan your trip today and discover the secrets of this Caribbean paradise.
