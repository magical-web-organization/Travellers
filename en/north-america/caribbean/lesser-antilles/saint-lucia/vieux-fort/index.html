Introduction to Vieux Fort

Vieux Fort is a charming town located on the southern tip of Saint Lucia, in the Lesser Antilles, in the Caribbean region of North America. It is named after the French word for “old fort”, in reference to the historic Moule a Chique lighthouse that was used to guide ships into the harbor. With its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural scenery, and vibrant food and drink scene, Vieux Fort is a must-visit destination in Saint Lucia.

Local Culture and Customs of Vieux Fort

  • English is the official language, but Creole is widely spoken.
  • Vieux Fort celebrates its cultural heritage with lively festivals, such as the La Rose flower festival and the La Marguerite flower festival.
  • Saint Lucians are known for their warm hospitality, and visitors are welcomed with open arms.

Best Time to Visit Vieux Fort

The best time to visit Vieux Fort is from December to April, when the weather is sunny and dry. May to June is the shoulder season, with fewer crowds and lower prices, but with a higher chance of rain. July to November is the off-season, with occasional hurricanes and rainstorms, but with the lowest prices.

Safety and Health

Safety in Vieux Fort

Vieux Fort is a safe destination for travelers, but it is always important to be aware of your surroundings and take precautions to avoid theft and accidents. Keep your valuables in a safe place and avoid walking alone at night. Use licensed taxis and avoid unmarked vehicles. Check with the local authorities and your embassy regarding any safety or security concerns.

Health in Vieux Fort

Vieux Fort has a good healthcare system, with several hospitals and clinics that cater to both locals and tourists. It is recommended to have travel insurance and bring any necessary medications and medical supplies with you. Stay hydrated, avoid tap water, and practice good hygiene to prevent illness.

Getting to Vieux Fort: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Vieux Fort

Hewanorra International Airport (UVF) is the main airport serving Vieux Fort, with daily flights from major cities in the US, Canada, Europe, and Latin America. It is located about 2 miles from the town center and is easily accessible by taxi, shuttle, or rental car. George F.L. Charles Airport (SLU) is another airport in Saint Lucia, but it is located on the northern side of the island, about 40 miles from Vieux Fort.

Bus Stations Near Vieux Fort

Buses are a common way of transportation in Saint Lucia, with several bus stations located throughout the island. The Vieux Fort bus station is located near the town center, and buses run to and from major cities and towns in Saint Lucia.

Other Options

Private taxis, shuttles, and rental cars are also available for transportation to and from Vieux Fort and around the island. It is recommended to book in advance and confirm prices before the ride.

How to Get Around in Vieux Fort

Walking and biking are the best ways to explore Vieux Fort, especially in the historic town center. Taxis and rental cars are also available for longer distances and day trips. Buses are a cheap and reliable option for transportation to and from other cities and towns in Saint Lucia.
