Introduction to Southern Barbados

Southern Barbados is a beautiful region located in the southernmost part of the Caribbean island of Barbados. This part of the island is famous for its crystal-clear waters, powdery white sands, and towering cliffs.

If you’re planning a trip to Southern Barbados, here are some must-visit destinations:

Week-Long Itinerary for Southern Barbados

Day 1: Arrival and check-in at hotel. Visit Crane Beach.

Day 2: Explore Holetown and visit St. Nicholas Abbey.

Day 3: Take a guided island safari adventure tour.

Day 4: Visit Sunbury Plantation House and explore the nearby historic town of Speightstown.

Day 5: Take a day excursion to Harrison’s Cave.

Day 6: Visit Andromeda Botanic Gardens and snorkel at Coral Reef.

Day 7: Relax on one of the Southern Barbados pristine beaches and indulge in some local cuisine.

Local Culture and Customs of Southern Barbados

Barbadians are known for their warm and welcoming nature and are proud of their island’s British heritage. It is customary to greet locals with a “Good morning” or “Good afternoon,” and it is considered impolite to not acknowledge someone when they greet you.

Best Time to Visit Southern Barbados

The best time to visit Southern Barbados is between December and April, which is the island’s dry season.

Do’s and Don’ts of Southern Barbados

Do:

  • Wear sunscreen, hats, and stay hydrated to avoid sunburn and heat exhaustion.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try some local cuisine.

Don’t:

  • Don’t touch or take photographs of the wild sea turtles or marine life. They are protected by law.
  • Don’t participate in any illegal drug activity.

Safety and Health

Safety in Southern Barbados

Southern Barbados is considered a safe place for travelers. However, it is always advisable to take precautions such as avoiding isolated areas and always being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Southern Barbados

The main health concern in Southern Barbados is mosquito-borne diseases, such as dengue fever or Zika virus. It is advisable to wear long sleeves and use mosquito repellent when outdoors.

Getting to Southern Barbados: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Southern Barbados

Grantley Adams International Airport is the island’s only airport and located in the south of the island, close to popular hotel and resort areas.

Bus Stations Near Southern Barbados

The bus terminal in the capital, Bridgetown, is where all buses arrive and depart from to and from the rest of the island.

Other Options

Taxis are readily available across the island and can be a reliable and convenient way to get around.
