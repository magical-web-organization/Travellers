Comprehensive Travel Guide to Netherlands Antilles

Netherlands Antilles is a beautiful Caribbean country that consists of five islands. The country has a rich cultural history, breathtaking natural beauty, and delicious cuisine. If you’re planning a trip to Netherlands Antilles, this comprehensive travel guide will provide you with all the information you need to plan the perfect vacation.

Introduction to Netherlands Antilles

Netherlands Antilles is a former Dutch colony that consists of five islands - Bonaire, Curacao, Saba, Sint Eustatius, and Sint Maarten. The country is located in the Caribbean and is a popular tourist destination due to its warm weather, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural heritage. The official language of the country is Dutch, but English and Spanish are also widely spoken.

Food and Drink Scene of Netherlands Antilles

Must-Try Dishes

  • Keshi Yena - traditional stuffed cheese dish
  • Stobá - a stew made from goat, beef, or chicken
  • Funchi - cornmeal dish
  • Bakbanaan - deep-fried plantains
  • Pastechi - filled pastry

Local Beverages

  • Blue Curaçao - a sweet, blue liquor made from the dried peels of the laraha fruit
  • Ponche Crema - a creamy liqueur flavored with vanilla and rum
  • Amstel Bright - a local beer

Local Culture and Customs of Netherlands Antilles

Netherland Antilles is a melting pot of different cultures – Dutch and Afro-Caribbean.

Best Time to Visit Netherlands Antilles

The best time to visit Netherlands Antilles is between December and April when the weather is warm and dry.

Do’s and Don’ts of Netherlands Antilles

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions
  • Learn some basic Dutch and Papiamento before visiting the country
  • Try the local cuisine

Don’t:

  • Do not litter
  • Do not smoke in public places
  • Do not touch or interfere with the wildlife

Safety and Health

Safety in Netherlands Antilles

Overall, Netherlands Antilles is a safe country, but tourists are advised to exercise caution, especially in isolated areas, and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Netherlands Antilles

No special vaccinations are needed to travel to Netherlands Antilles, but it’s always good to check with your doctor before traveling.
