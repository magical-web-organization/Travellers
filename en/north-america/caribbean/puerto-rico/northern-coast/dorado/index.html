Introduction to Dorado

Dorado is a picturesque coastal town located in the northern region of Puerto Rico. The name Dorado means “golden” in Spanish, and the town has lived up to its name with its wealth of stunning beaches, lush forests, and sparkling waters. It is a popular vacation spot for both locals and tourists seeking a sun-drenched vacation with plenty of adventures.

From hiking in the forest to relaxing on the sandy beaches and experiencing the local cuisine and culture, Dorado offers something for everyone. Here is a comprehensive guide to the best things to do in Dorado to help you plan your trip to this slice of paradise.

List of Places to Visit in Dorado

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Museo de Arte Francisco Oller

Museo de Arte Francisco Oller is an art museum in Dorado that celebrates the life and works of the famous Puerto Rican artist Francisco Oller. The museum showcases a collection of Oller’s paintings, sculptures, and photographs along with other contemporary art pieces.

Casa del Rey

Casa del Rey is a 400-year-old mansion that was once the residence of the Spanish governor of Puerto Rico. It is now a museum and cultural center that looks back on the history and culture of the region. The house and the grounds are beautiful and have been restored to including a little chapel and courtyards filled with tropical plants.

Natural Attractions of Dorado: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Parque Nacional Torre de Canon

Parque Nacional Torre de Canon is a beautiful forest park located in the heart of Dorado. The park is home to numerous hiking and biking trails, which offer stunning views of the forests, streams, and waterfalls. If you love to explore the beautiful outdoors, then this park is a must-visit destination. Don’t forget to pack insect repellent, and water, too.

Balneario Manuel Morales

Balneario Manuel Morales is one of the most popular beaches in Dorado. The beach is perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and enjoying water sports like kayaking and paddleboarding. The beach is well-maintained and offers a host of facilities, including restrooms, lifeguards, and water equipment rentals.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dorado

Dorado offers an extensive network of hiking and biking trails through its natural parks, nature reserves, and forests. Here are some of the best hiking trails in Dorado.

  • Rio Lajas Trail: 6 miles long
  • Mediodía Trail: 5.6 miles long
  • El Bosquecito Trail: 3.6 miles long
  • Aventuras Tierra Adentro: Offers guided hikes through the tropical forests

Best Things to Do in Dorado: Activities and Tours

Dorado offers a wide range of activities and tours that cater to all interests and preferences. Here are some of the best things to do in Dorado.

Tanamá River Adventures

Tanamá River Adventures offers guided tours of the Tanamá River and its surrounding forests. You can try kayaking, hiking, swimming, and caving, and you can explore the extensive cave systems and underground rivers. The tours can be adapted to suit all levels of fitness and experience.

Dorado Beach & Golf Resort

The Dorado Beach & Golf Resort is a luxurious resort that offers world-class amenities and services. The resort offers activities like golfing, tennis, and water sports and is home to an exclusive spa and fitness center. It is a great place to relax and enjoy the tropical surroundings.

Week-Long Itinerary for Dorado

Day 1: Visit Museo de Arte Francisco Oller, Casa del Rey, and walk around Plaza de Recreo.

Day 2: Explore Parque Nacional Torre de Canon and hike some of its trails.

Day 3: Spend a day at Balneario Manuel Morales beach.

Day 4: Visit Aventuras Tierra Adentro or take a guided tour of the nearby Tanamá River.

Day 5: Play some rounds of golf or tennis at the Dorado Beach & Golf Resort.

Day 6: Take a stroll through the historic town of Vega Alta and enjoy the small food stalls try alcapurrias, pinchos, and lechón.

Day 7: Spend the final day in Dorado strolling around the town, visiting local shops, and picking up some souvenirs.

Food and Drink Scene of Dorado

Dorado’s food and drink scene is heavily culturally influenced but it also serves a distinctive mix of local and international cuisines, including traditional Puerto Rican food. Here’s a list of must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Mofongo
  • Pastelillos
  • Chillo Frito (fried Red snapper fish)
  • Lechón asado
  • Tostones

Local Beverages

  • Piña colada
  • Medalla beer
  • Coquito (a coconut and rum drink)

Local Culture and Customs of Dorado

It is worth understanding and respecting some of the customs and practices of the local people in Dorado when visiting. The island community trusts on family relationships, they have a strong bond of family and religious traditions since Puerto Rico is a primarily Christian country.

Best Time to Visit Dorado

The best time to visit Dorado is between mid-December to April. This is the peak tourist season when the weather is usually consistent and is perfect for any outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Dorado

Do:

  • Try the local food and drinks.
  • Learn about the local culture and history.
  • Respect the customs and practices of the local people.

Don’t:

  • Get involved in illegal activities.
  • Wear camouflage clothing; it is illegal in Puerto Rico.
  • Go off hiking or exploring unaccompanied in the forests.

Safety and Health

Safety in Dorado

Dorado is generally a safe place to visit, like any other place, it is important to remain vigilant and practice common sense safety measures. Don’t leave any valuables visible in the car. Plan activities during daylight hours, avoid going to unsafe neighborhoods, and take taxis at night.

Health in Dorado

Visitors should have current vaccinations before visiting Puerto Rico. Zika and Dengue are common in the region, so bring insect repellent, and wear pants and long sleeves to prevent bites.

The best way to get around Dorado is by car, many places are not within walking distance. For car rental, there are several car rentals available, or you can also go to sites, which offer guided tours and transportation.

Conclusion

Dorado is a beautiful coastal town with a lot of things to discover and explore, it offers amazing landscapes, beaches, cultural places, traditional food, and plenty of outdoor activities all in one place. Use this guide when planning your trip and make the most of your vacation in Dorado.
