Introduction to Gran Parque Natural Topes de Collantes

Gran Parque Natural Topes de Collantes is a nature reserve located in the Escambray Mountains of central Cuba. The park covers an area of over 200 square kilometers and is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna. Visitors to Gran Parque Natural Topes de Collantes will find an abundance of natural beauty, outdoor activities, and cultural treasures.
