Jardines del Rey Travel Guide: Discover Cuba’s Hidden Treasure

If you’re looking for a tropical paradise filled with natural landscapes, turquoise waters, and perfect beaches, you can’t go wrong with Jardines del Rey. Located in the central part of Cuba, Jardines del Rey is a beautiful archipelago of islets and keys that will take your breath away. It’s home to some of the best resorts and hotels in Cuba, and it is rapidly becoming one of the hottest tourist destinations in the Caribbean.

Whether you’re a solo traveler, a family, or a group of friends, Jardines del Rey offers something for everyone, from pristine beaches to outdoor activities and cultural tours. In this travel guide, we’ll help you plan your unforgettable trip to Jardines del Rey. Let’s get started!

List of Places to Visit in Jardines del Rey

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re interested in learning about the rich history and culture of Cuba, Jardines del Rey has a lot to offer. Whether you’re a history buff, an art lover, or just curious, these museums and historical places are worth a visit:

  • Cayo Coco History Museum: This museum features exhibitions about the history of the island, including its pre-Columbian era, colonization, and modern times.

  • La Cueva del Jabali: This cave was once used as a hideout by pirates and smugglers. Today, it’s a popular tourist attraction that offers stunning views of the coastline and the surrounding area.

  • San Jose Church: Located in Moron, this church is one of the most important historical and cultural landmarks in the region. It was built in the 19th century and is famous for its beautiful architecture and religious significance.

Natural Attractions of Jardines del Rey: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

One of the main reasons visitors flock to Jardines del Rey is for its natural beauty. From national parks to off-road safaris, this place has it all. Here are some of the top outdoor attractions to explore:

  • Parque Natural El Bagá: This beautiful national park is home to a range of ecosystems, from forests and mangroves to lagoons and coral reefs. You can also spot a wide variety of bird species and other wildlife.

  • Playa Flamenco: This stunning beach is one of the most beautiful in Cuba. With its clear waters, white sand, and swaying palms, it’s the perfect place to relax and unwind.

  • Cayo Guillermo: This is one of the smaller islands in Jardines del Rey and it is characterized by its rich marine life and coral formations. It is a popular spot for diving and snorkeling.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jardines del Rey

Jardines del Rey has a range of hiking and biking trails to explore. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced hiker, these routes offer stunning views and a chance to experience the natural beauty of Cuba. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • El Pilar Nature Trail: This trail is located in the El Pilar National Park and offers stunning views of the area. It is a relatively easy hike and takes around two hours to complete.

  • Cayo Coco Bike Trail: This trail is a great way to explore Cayo Coco’s many natural attractions. It takes you through mangroves, forests, and beaches, and is suitable for all levels of bikers.

Best Things to Do in Jardines del Rey: Activities and Tours

Jardines del Rey is filled with exciting activities and tours to enjoy. Whether you’re looking for water sports, cultural tours, or adventure activities, there’s something for everyone. Here are some of the top activities and tours to consider:

  • Catamaran Tour: Explore the coast on a catamaran tour and enjoy snorkeling, swimming, and relaxing on the beach.

  • Horseback Riding: Take a guided horseback ride through the stunning Jardines del Rey landscape, and enjoy the beautiful scenery around you.

  • Deep-Sea Fishing: Experience the thrill of the catch with a deep-sea fishing trip. You can catch a range of fish, including sailfish, marlin, and barracuda.

Food and Drink Scene of Jardines del Rey

One of the best things about visiting a new place is trying the local food and drink. Jardines del Rey offers a range of delicious dishes and refreshing beverages that you shouldn’t miss. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Arroz con Pollo: This is one of Cuba’s most famous dishes. It’s made with rice and chicken, and is seasoned with a range of herbs and spices.

  • Ropa Vieja: This is another popular dish in Cuba. It’s made with shredded beef, vegetables, and spices, and is usually served with rice and beans.

  • Lechon Asado: This is a traditional Cuban roasted pork dish. It’s marinated with garlic and sour orange juice, and is often served with yuca or plantains.

Local Beverages

  • Mojito: This is a classic Cuban cocktail made with rum, sugar, lime juice, and mint leaves. It’s refreshing and perfect for a hot day.

  • Cuba Libre: This is another famous Cuban cocktail made with rum and Coca-Cola. It’s simple, delicious, and perfect for any occasion.

Where to Stay in Jardines del Rey: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Jardines del Rey has a range of accommodation options to suit every budget and preference. From all-inclusive resorts to cozy guesthouses, there’s something for everyone. Here are some top options:

  • Melia Cayo Coco All-Inclusive Resort: This is a luxury resort located on the beachfront. It offers a range of amenities, including a spa, several restaurants, and a pool.

  • Hostal 1511 Cubanacan: This is a cozy guesthouse located in the heart of Moron. It’s perfect for budget travelers and offers a range of amenities, including air conditioning and free Wi-Fi.

  • Iberostar Selection Holguin: This is a beautiful beachfront resort located in Holguin. It offers a range of amenities, including several restaurants, a pool, and a spa.

What to Buy in Jardines del Rey: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for unique souvenirs and local products to bring home from Jardines del Rey, here are some top options:

  • Cigars: Cuba is famous for its cigars, and Jardines del Rey offers a range of options to choose from.

  • Rum: Cuba is also famous for its rum, and there are plenty of brands to choose from in Jardines del Rey.

  • Artisanal Crafts: You can find a range of handmade crafts, such as pottery, woven baskets, and embroidered fabrics, in the local markets and shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Jardines del Rey

To fully appreciate the local culture and customs of Jardines del Rey, it’s important to understand its history and traditions. The Cuban people are friendly and welcoming, and it’s easy to connect with them through their love of music, dance, and food.

Best Time to Visit Jardines del Rey

The best time to visit Jardines del Rey is during the dry season, which runs from November to April. During this time, the weather is warm and sunny, with little to no rainfall. The high season runs from December to March, so keep that in mind if you want to avoid crowds and high prices.
