Introduction to Ixtapan de la Sal

Located in Mexico State, in Central Mexico, Ixtapan de la Sal is a beautiful town renowned for its hot springs, stunning scenery, and rich history. Nestled in the midst of the Sierra Madre mountains, this town has always been a popular spot for relaxation and rejuvenation, as well as adventure. From exploring its museums and historical sites to hiking along idyllic pathways, Ixtapan de la Sal has a lot to offer for every kind of traveler.

This travel guide will take you through the top places to visit, adventure activities, food and drink experiences, accommodations, and everything you need to know to make the most of your trip to Ixtapan de la Sal.

List of Places to Visit in Ixtapan de la Sal

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Ixtapan de la Sal Archaeological Museum - Learn about the history and cultures of the town and its surrounding regions. This museum features exhibits of pre-Hispanic and colonial artifacts, including pottery, stone carvings, and ceramics.
  2. San Felipe Neri Church - Also known as the Parish of San Felipe Neri, this beautiful temple from the 17th century is adorned with stunning Baroque altarpieces.
  3. Capilla de Santa Barbara - Located on a hilltop, this small chapel offers amazing panoramic views of the town and surrounding mountains.
  4. Teotenango Archaeological Site - A short drive from Ixtapan de la Sal, this ancient site is a must-visit, featuring towering pyramids and structures from the Pre-Columbian era.

Natural Attractions of Ixtapan de la Sal: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Grutas de la Estrella - Explore an underground world of stalagmites and stalactites along well-lit walkways.
  2. Ixtapan Park - A great place for a relaxing day out, this park boasts tranquil lakes, colorful gardens, and picnic areas.
  3. El Salto Waterfall - Take a refreshing break and admire the power and beauty of this majestic waterfall.
  4. Cerro de la Guitarra - Hike up to the top of this hill for breathtaking views of the town, the valley, and the surrounding mountains.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ixtapan de la Sal

  1. El Paraiso Trail - A 4-km (approx 2.5 miles) trail that passes by jungles, waterfalls, and scenic viewpoints.
  2. Monte de la Cruz Trail - A longer trail, stretching around 20 km (approx 12.5 miles) and offering a comfortable pace hike through forests and rural communities.

Best Things to Do in Ixtapan de la Sal: Activities and Tours

  1. Hot Spring Soaking - The highlight of Ixtapan de la Sal is its hot spring culture. Taking a dip in natural hot springs is a must-do when you’re here. There are several spas and resorts in Ixtapan de la Sal that offer hot spring pools, massages, sauna, yoga, and other wellness treatments.
  2. Temascal Experience - A ceremony that involves entering a type of ancient sauna, where natural herbs are burned to create a soothing steam. This experience is said to purify the body and mind, and to have healing effects.
  3. Canopy Tours - If you love adventure, take a zip line tour through the jungle with amazing views of waterfalls and forests.
  4. Culinary Tours - Take a cooking class and learn to make traditional Mexican dishes such as mole and tamales, or go on a food tour and taste the local cuisine, including street tacos, tamales, and churros.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ixtapan de la Sal

Food and Drink Scene of Ixtapan de la Sal

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Mole - This thick, flavorful sauce made with spices, nuts, and chili peppers is a classic Mexican dish.
  2. Tamales - Masa dough wrapped around savory fillings such as pork, chicken, or vegetables, then steamed in banana leaves.
  3. Churros de Azucar - Sweet, deep-fried dough pastry coated with sugar and cinnamon.

Local Beverages

  1. Agua Fresca - A refreshing, fruity drink made with fresh fruits and sugar water, served chilled.
  2. Tequila - A popular alcoholic drink made from the blue agave plant, originating in the Jalisco state of Mexico.

Where to Stay in Ixtapan de la Sal: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Ixtapan Spa Hotel and Health Center - Located in the center of town, this hotel offers luxurious rooms with hot spring bath access, spa treatments, and local cuisine.
  2. Hotel Rancho San Diego Grand Spa Resort - With its scenic location, this resort offers spacious suites, outdoor pools, and a variety of wellness services.
  3. Hotel Matea Inn - A beautifully restored heritage building featuring rustic-style rooms, a charming garden, and a rooftop terrace.

What to Buy in Ixtapan de la Sal: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Huichol Art - Intricately-designed beadwork and embroidery, traditional of the Huichol people, a pre-Hispanic and indigenous culture in Mexico.
  2. Handcrafted Pottery - Traditional ceramic pottery available in different designs and colors made by local artisans.
  3. Tequila and Mezcal - Bring home the authentic taste of Mexico with a bottle of tequila or mezcal, produced in the Jalisco and Oaxaca states in Mexico.

Local Culture and Customs of Ixtapan de la Sal

The town of Ixtapan de la Sal has a rich cultural heritage that highlights the indigenous and pre-Hispanic traditions of Mexico. Visitors are encouraged to respect the local customs and traditions, and take part in cultural activities when possible. The people of Ixtapan de la Sal are friendly and welcoming towards travelers.

Best Time to Visit Ixtapan de la Sal

Ixtapan de la Sal can be visited year-round, but the best time to visit would be between October and May. This is the dry season, with more pleasant weather and less rainfall. The summer months can be hot and humid with frequent rain.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ixtapan de la Sal

Do:

  • Do take a dip in hot springs and indulge in some wellness treatments.
  • Do bring comfortable shoes for hiking and exploring.
  • Do immerse yourself in the local history and culture.
  • Do try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget to apply sunscreen and bring a hat for protection from the sun.
  • Don’t litter in natural sites and trails.
  • Don’t disturb wildlife and plants.
  • Don’t forget to respect the local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ixtapan de la Sal

Ixtapan de la Sal is considered a safe town for travelers. However, it’s always advisable to take basic precautions to ensure a safe trip. Avoid walking alone at night, and keep an eye on your belongings when in crowded places.

Health in Ixtapan de la Sal

As with any travel outside your home country, make sure to have travel insurance and to keep an emergency kit with essential medication and first aid supplies. The town has medical facilities available for minor ailments and injuries.

Getting to Ixtapan de la Sal: Transportation Options and Logistics

Ixtapan de la Sal can be reached by road and air, with a variety of transportation options.

Airports Near Ixtapan de la Sal

The closest international airport is Licenciado Adolfo López Mateos International Airport, located 60 km (approx. 37 miles) from Ixtapan de la Sal.

Bus Stations Near Ixtapan de la Sal

Ixtapan de la Sal is connected to major cities in Mexico via bus services such as ADO and Flecha Amarilla.

Other Options

Private car rentals and private shuttle services are also available.

How to Get Around in Ixtapan de la Sal

The town of Ixtapan de la Sal is small and can mostly be explored on foot. For longer distances, taxis and car rentals are available.

Where to Go Next from Ixtapan de la Sal: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Valle de Bravo - A charming town located 70 km (approx. 43 miles) from Ixtapan de la Sal. This town offers stunning views of a lake and mountains.
  2. Toluca - The capital city of the State of Mexico and home to many cultural and historical sites.
  3. Nevado de Toluca - A volcanic mountain located 60 km (approx. 37 miles) from Ixtapan de la Sal, offering amazing vistas of a high-altitude crater lake.

Conclusion

Ixtapan de la Sal offers a unique blend of cultural and adventure experiences, making it a great destination for travelers of all types. From natural wonders to culinary delights, there’s something for everybody here. Follow this guide and create amazing memories in the beautiful town of Ixtapan de la Sal.
