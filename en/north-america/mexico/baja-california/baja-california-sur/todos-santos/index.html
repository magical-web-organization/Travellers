Introduction to Todos Santos

Located in the southern part of Baja California Sur in Mexico, Todos Santos is a charming town known for its stunning beaches, historic buildings, and cultural attractions. Surrounded by the Sierra de la Laguna mountain range and the Pacific Ocean, Todos Santos boasts a unique blend of natural beauty and artistic culture. Whether you are looking to explore the town’s museums and galleries or embark on outdoor adventures, Todos Santos has something to offer for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Todos Santos

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museo de la Casa de Cultura – This museum houses a collection of artworks and artifacts that showcase the history and culture of Todos Santos.
  • Mission Santa Rosa de Todos Santos – Built in the 18th century, this mission is considered one of the most important historic landmarks in the town.
  • Galeria de Todos Santos – This gallery features contemporary artworks created by local artists.

Natural Attractions of Todos Santos: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Playa Los Cerritos – This scenic beach is popular among surfers and beachgoers for its stunning sunsets and ocean waves.
  • Sierra de la Laguna Biosphere Reserve – This reserve offers hiking and camping opportunities for visitors who want to explore the natural beauty of Todos Santos.
  • Punta Lobos Beach – This isolated beach is known for its tranquility and crystal-clear waters, perfect for snorkeling and swimming.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Todos Santos

  • Todos Santos Eco Adventures – This tour company offers guided hikes through the Sierra de la Laguna mountain range.
  • Baja Sierra Adventures – This tour company specializes in mountain biking tours for visitors who want to experience the local terrain up close.

Best Things to Do in Todos Santos: Activities and Tours

  • Whale Watching Tours – Visitors can take boat tours to witness the majestic whales that migrate down to the Baja California Sur coast every year.
  • Surfing Lessons – With a number of surf schools in Todos Santos, visitors can learn how to ride the waves of the Pacific Ocean.
  • Horseback Riding Tours – Guided tours on horseback are available for visitors who want to explore Todos Santos’ natural trails and beaches in a unique way.

Food and Drink Scene of Todos Santos

Must-Try Dishes

  • Tacos de pescado – Fish tacos are a must-try local delicacy, made with fresh local catches of fish.
  • Chiles Rellenos – This dish consists of chili peppers stuffed with cheese or meat and served with tomato sauce.
  • Ceviche – This popular seafood dish is made with fresh fish, lime juice, and vegetables.

Local Beverages

  • Tequila – Try a tequila tasting tour to learn about the different types of tequila produced in the region.
  • Margarita – Made with tequila, lime juice, and salt, the margarita is a popular cocktail in Todos Santos.
  • Mexican Craft Beer – Sample some of the local craft beers at small bars and restaurants throughout the town.

Where to Stay in Todos Santos: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hotel San Cristobal – This luxury hotel offers stunning ocean views and features modern amenities and sleek decor.
  • Todos Santos Inn – This boutique hotel is set in a restored 19th-century building and offers charming rooms and a beautiful interior courtyard.
  • Posada La Poza – This serene inn is located on the edge of a lagoon and offers a relaxing atmosphere for guests looking to unwind.

What to Buy in Todos Santos: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handicrafts – The local artisan community creates handcrafted items such as pottery, textiles, and jewelry.
  • Tequila and Margarita Mix – The town is known for its production of high-quality tequila and margarita mix.
  • Artwork – The local art scene is thriving, with many galleries offering a variety of artworks to purchase.

Local Culture and Customs of Todos Santos

Todos Santos is a small town with a rich history and culture. The locals are friendly and welcoming to visitors, and they take great pride in their heritage. It is important to respect the local customs, and dress modestly when visiting religious sites such as the Mission Santa Rosa de Todos Santos.

Best Time to Visit Todos Santos

The ideal time to visit Todos Santos is between December and May when the weather is mild, and the town is less crowded. During the summer months, the temperatures can be hot and humid, and the town can get busy with tourists.

Do’s and Don’ts of Todos Santos

Do:

  • Embrace the local culture and try the local food and drink.
  • Take the time to explore the town’s museums, galleries, and historical landmarks.
  • Respect the environment and follow Leave No Trace principles when exploring the natural attractions.

Don’t:

  • Leave your valuables unattended when visiting public places.
  • Disrespect the local customs and dress codes at religious sites.
  • Engage in harmful or unethical activities that could harm the natural environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Todos Santos

Todos Santos is a generally safe town, with a low crime rate. However, visitors should take basic safety precautions such as locking their valuables in a safe when leaving their accommodations and being aware of their surroundings when walking around at night.

Health in Todos Santos

Visitors should be aware of the quality of the local drinking water, which may be contaminated. It is recommended to drink bottled or filtered water instead. Additionally, visitors should bring insect repellent to protect against mosquito-borne illnesses such as Zika or Dengue fever.

Getting to Todos Santos: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Todos Santos

The nearest international airport to Todos Santos is the Los Cabos International Airport, which is located approximately 50 miles away. From the airport, visitors can take a taxi or shuttle bus to Todos Santos.

Bus Stations Near Todos Santos

The closest bus station to Todos Santos is located in Cabo San Lucas, approximately 45 miles away. From the bus station, visitors can take a local bus or taxi to Todos Santos.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car and drive to Todos Santos from Cabo San Lucas or La Paz. This option allows for more flexibility and freedom to explore the surrounding areas at your leisure.

How to Get Around in Todos Santos

The most popular way to get around Todos Santos is on foot or by bicycle. Taxis are also available, but the town is small enough to navigate on foot. Visitors who plan on exploring the natural attractions may want to consider renting a car or booking a guided tour to ensure they can access the remote areas safely.

Where to Go Next from Todos Santos: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Cabo San Lucas – This bustling beach town is known for its nightlife, shopping, and beautiful beaches.
  • La Paz – The capital city of Baja California Sur offers a range of cultural attractions and natural wonders, such as the Espiritu Santo Island.
  • Todos Santos Islands – This group of uninhabited islands is located off the coast of Todos Santos and offers stunning scenery and opportunities for snorkeling and diving.

Conclusion

From its stunning beaches to its vibrant art scene, Todos Santos is a hidden gem in Baja California Sur. Visitors can immerse themselves in the local culture, explore the natural wonders, and indulge in the delicious local food and drink. With so much to offer, Todos Santos is the perfect destination for a memorable vacation.
