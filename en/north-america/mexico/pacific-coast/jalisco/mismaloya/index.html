Introduction to Mismaloya

Located in the south of Puerto Vallarta on the Pacific coast of Mexico, Mismaloya is a hidden gem nestled between the mountains and the ocean. It’s a relatively quiet and serene village that offers a great escape for tourists looking for a secluded, peaceful vacation with a touch of adventure.

Mismaloya is also rich in history and culture, and tourists can explore the remains of ancient civilizations, visit museums, and indulge in amazing local cuisine that highlight the vibrant mix of cultures here. So, come, let’s take a virtual tour of Mismaloya and explore all the possibilities.

List of Places to Visit in Mismaloya

Mismaloya has a lot of attractions that will keep you entertained. From awe-inspiring landmarks to adrenaline-pumping outdoor adventures, and everything in between, here are some of the top places to visit in Mismaloya:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Playa Mismaloya Beach: This is one of the most famous beaches in the area. It attracts locals and tourists alike for its beautiful views, crystal-clear waters, and variety of water sports and activities available.
  • El Eden: Famously known as the location for the movie “Predator,” it’s a natural paradise where you can take a dip in the refreshing river or zipline through the tree canopy.
  • Vallarta Botanical Gardens: This is a great way to connect with nature and learn more about the diverse flora and fauna of the region.
  • Los Arcos National Marine Park: A group of tiny rocky islands that boasts colorful marine life and offers great opportunities for snorkeling and diving.
  • Archaeological Sites: Check out the ancient ruins located in the surrounding areas such as the Ixtapa Prehispanic Site, where you can learn about the pre-Columbian civilizations and their way of life.

Natural Attractions of Mismaloya: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mismaloya Canopy Tour: A great activity for adrenaline junkies, this canopy tour allows you to zipline through the lush forest canopy and witness breathtaking views of the Pacific ocean.
  • Horseback Riding Adventure: Explore the countryside and nearby mountains on horseback, enjoy spectacular views, and learn about the local flora and fauna.
  • Whale Watching Tours: Get up close and personal with one of the region’s magnificent mammals — the humpback whale. These tours provide an exhilarating experience that you won’t soon forget.
  • Stand-up Paddleboarding (SUP): Explore the coastline and wildlife on the paddleboard tour available in Mismaloya.
  • Hiking Trails: Take a guided hike through the jungle and mountain paths for a wonderful nature experience

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mismaloya

Mismaloya has several excellent hiking and biking trails, perfect for adventure enthusiasts looking to explore the wilderness. Some of the most popular routes are:

  • Boca De Tomatlan Trail: This is a 45-minute moderate hike from Mismaloya beach that leads to a small fishing village called Boca de Tomatlan. Along the way, you’ll encounter a lush jungle filled with wildlife and scenic vistas of the seashore.
  • El Nogalito Trail: A 1.5-hour tour through the Nogalito Ecological Reserve, this trail takes you through the tropical jungle of Mismaloya while crossing rivers, walking on hanging bridges, and rewarding you with beautiful waterfalls for a refreshing dip.
  • Los Largatos Trail: This 3-hour trail features 360-degree views of Mismaloya’s lush greenery and is the perfect choice for those who prefer more challenging hikes. Enjoy the breathtaking views from the summit.

Best Things to Do in Mismaloya: Activities and Tours

Mismaloya has an array of activities and tours that cater to different preferences and interests. Here are some of the best things to do while visiting Mismaloya:

  • Sunset Cruise: Hop on a catamaran or yacht for a glorious sunset tour, sipping your favorite drinks while enjoying the stunning views of the coastline.
  • River Rafting: For those who love adventure, take a river rafting tour along the Mismaloya River with different difficulty levels.
  • Cooking Classes: Learn how to prepare authentic Mexican dishes with the guidance of expert chefs available in some local restaurants.
  • Spa Experience: Treat yourself to a relaxing day at any of the wellness centers and spas in Mismaloya, offering different healing techniques and treatments.
  • Scuba Diving and Snorkeling: Explore the crystal-clear waters and rich marine life available in Mismaloya.

Food and Drink Scene of Mismaloya

Mismaloya’s culinary landscape is a blend of fresh ingredients, bold spices, and savoury flavors that are unique to the region. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages that showcase the best of Mismaloya:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Tacos De Pescado: A seafood taco that extensively uses the area’s regional fish and spices. The mix of fresh ingredients and seasonings, and unique textures is nothing short of exceptional.
  • Pulpo Al Ajillo: This seafood takes its unique taste from the octopus being cooked in garlic and other fresh spices that are indigenous to the region.
  • Tequila Lime Shrimp: Shrimps are marinated in tequila lime dressing to create a vibrant, zesty flavor that will tantalize your taste buds.

Local Beverages

  • Margarita: A classic cocktail made from tequila, triple sec, and lime juice, but the natural touch from their locally grown limes acts as a secret ingredient.
  • Piña Colada: A refreshing and creamy cocktail that has its roots in the Puerto Rican territory but has quickly become the Mismaloya specialty, they use fresh pineapples and rum to create the perfect blend.

Where to Stay in Mismaloya: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Mismaloya offers plenty of options for accommodation that cater to all budgets. Here are a few options that we recommend:

  • Garza Blanca Preserve Resort and Spa: A luxurious resort that boasts fantastic ocean views, a pristine private beach, and several dining, and recreational activities.
  • Barceló Puerto Vallarta: A resort located right on shore’s edge amidst lush jungles, that provides a secluded and authentic environment. Elegant rooms, sophisticated restaurant selections, and impressive amenities outshine this resort.
  • Hotel Casa Iguana Mismaloya: A charming boutique hotel located in Mismaloya’s heart, surrounded by breathtaking scenery. From the ocean view balconies to the outdoor pool to excellent service, guests won’t regret their stay.

What to Buy in Mismaloya: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Mismaloya offers many opportunities to take home some fantastic souvenirs and products. Here are some of the best options:

  • Mole Sauce: A traditional Mexican sauce made from different types of chili peppers and other ingredients. Try the local version of this fantastic sauce and bring a bottle back home.
  • Handicrafts: Mismaloya’s local artisans produce beautiful and unique handicrafts with different materials, including ceramics, mosaics, and more. Browse local markets for hand-made jewelry, sculptures, and other souvenirs.
  • Tequila: Treat yourself or someone else special to high-quality tequila from the local distilleries. Ensure to purchase from the authorized stores for guaranteed authenticity.

Local Culture and Customs of Mismaloya

Mismaloya is comprised of a multicultural community, which gives it an exceptional cultural flair. Many cultural festivals and celebrations occur throughout the year. Their primary cultural event, Day of the Dead (Dia de Los Muertos), is a colorful and vibrant event that’s celebrated with great enthusiasm in Puerto Vallarta and surrounding areas between November 1 and November 2.

Best Time to Visit Mismaloya

January to June prime time to visit Mismaloya given warm, sunny weather and low chances of rain. If you’re looking to save on accommodation, try visiting from July to December, but keep in mind that it tends to get a bit humid and stormy during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mismaloya

Do:

  • Respect local customs and culture and try to learn as much as you can about them.
  • Be aware of and follow appropriate dress codes for different places.
  • Be cautious and stay safe, especially when it comes to adventure tours and outdoor activities.
  • Carry cash and change, as most local shops and vendors only accept cash payments.

Don’t:

  • Do not ever tarnish Mexican culture and customs.
  • Do not wander alone in less-lit or less-known areas during late nights.
  • Do not leave your valuable items in visible spots in vehicles tempt thieves.

Safety and Health

Safety in Mismaloya

Mismaloya is a safe place for tourists, but don’t get complacent. You should take all the necessary precautions to stay safe while traveling. Remember that pickpocketing and theft are prevalent in some areas.

Health in Mismaloya

Mismaloya is primarily responsible when it comes to health and hygiene. However, drink only bottled water due to water contamination issues. Protect yourself from dengue, chikungunya, Zika, and other mosquito-borne illnesses by wearing clothes to cover your skin and use anti-mosquitoes repellants with DEET.

Getting to Mismaloya: Transportation Options and Logistics

Mismaloya is accessible by both air or highway highways.

Airports Near Mismaloya

Puerto Vallarta International Airport (PVR) would be the closest airport to Mismaloya, just half an hour away. If you book your flights early, you can get some great deals.

Bus Stations Near Mismaloya

Mismaloya doesn’t have its bus station, but visitors can take one of the several buses that run from Puerto Vallarta to Mismaloya. Buses run throughout the day, and it’s a cheap and comfortable mode of transportation.

Other Options

Taxis are available in Puerto Vallarta. Consider selecting the authorized taxi services to avoid overcharging. Additionally, several car rental companies operate in Puerto Vallarta, offering different vehicle options and packages.

How to Get Around in Mismaloya

Walking is the best way to explore the area, given its size. Bicycles and motorbikes are also available for rent in town and are reasonably inexpensive. Taxis are an option, but they tend to be a bit expensive.

Where to Go Next from Mismaloya: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Mismaloya is the gateway to exploring the surrounding areas including:

  • Puerto Vallarta: This nearby town offers visitors lots of shops, restaurants and bars, cultural and historical sites, beautiful beaches, and a more bustling atmosphere.
  • Sayulita: The beautiful town of Sayulita, famous for its bohemian beachy vibes and activities like surfing, fishing, and horseback riding.
  • Yelapa: This picturesque fishing village is the epitome of paradise. Accessible only by boat, the village offers a calming environment of sun-kissed beaches, stunning landscapes, and waterfalls.

Conclusion

Mismaloya is a destination worth visiting for the adventurous and curious. Mismaloya offers tourists unparalleled access to exciting outdoor adventures, magnificent natural wonders, and a vibrant cultural scene that will keep you entertained throughout your entire stay. Plan a trip to Mismaloya and take away memories that will last a lifetime.
