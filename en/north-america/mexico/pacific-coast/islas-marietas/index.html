Introduction to Islas Marietas

Islas Marietas is a spectacular island located in the Pacific Coast of Mexico, known for its crystal-clear waters, diverse marine life and stunning white-sand beaches. The region’s natural beauty is highlighted by the abundance of unique flora and fauna that thrive there, making it a must-visit destination for travelers looking for an unforgettable getaway.

List of Places to Visit in Islas Marietas

There are several incredible attractions to see and explore in Islas Marietas. Here are some of the most noteworthy:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Islas Marietas National Park
  • El Faro Lighthouse, Punta Mita
  • Archaeological site of Las Palmas
  • The Malecón, Puerto Vallarta’s Boardwalk

Natural Attractions of Islas Marietas: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hidden Beach
  • Birdwatching in the nature reserve
  • Snorkeling and scuba-diving
  • Kayaking and paddleboarding
  • Whale watching (in season)

Hiking/Biking Routes of Islas Marietas

There are many excellent hiking and biking trails throughout Islas Marietas, offering unparalleled views of the island’s natural beauty. Some of the best trails include:

  • The Marietas Islands Marine Reserve Trail
  • The Corral del Risco Trail
  • The Punta de Mita Court Trail
  • The Anclote-Mirador del Mono trail

Best Things to Do in Islas Marietas: Activities and Tours

There are numerous things to do and see in Islas Marietas, whether you’re looking for adventure or relaxation. Some of the top activities and tours include:

  • Hidden Beach boat tour
  • Marietas Islands Bird Watching Tour
  • Snorkeling and scuba-diving tours
  • Whale-watching tours (in season)
  • Stand-up paddleboard rentals
  • Kayaking adventures

Local Culture and Customs of Islas Marietas

The people of Islas Marietas are known for their welcoming nature and deep-rooted traditions. Here are some of the local customs and cultural practices to keep in mind during your visit:

  • Respect the local people and their traditions
  • Avoid wearing revealing clothing in public places
  • Always ask permission before taking photographs of people
  • Tipping is customary in restaurants and for tour guides

Best Time to Visit Islas Marietas

The best time to visit Islas Marietas is from November to April, when the weather is pleasant and dry. The high season tends to be from January to March, when the whale-watching season is in full swing, so it’s important to book your accommodations and activities early.

Do’s and Don’ts of Islas Marietas

Do:

  • Respect the natural environment and avoid littering
  • Try the local cuisine and drinks
  • Stay hydrated and protect yourself from the sun
  • Learn basic Spanish phrases to communicate with locals

Don’t:

  • Venture too far off the beaten path alone
  • Ignore advice from tour guides or locals
  • Disrespect cultural customs and traditions
  • Leave valuables unattended on the beach

Safety and Health

Safety in Islas Marietas

Islas Marietas is considered a safe destination for tourists, but it’s always important to take precautions when traveling. Here are some safety tips to keep in mind:

  • Avoid walking alone in remote areas after dark
  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially in crowded places
  • Store your valuables in a secure location, like a safe in your hotel room
  • Take proper precautions against sunburn and dehydration

Health in Islas Marietas

It’s always important to take care of your health when traveling, especially in a foreign country. Here are some tips to stay healthy during your trip:

  • Drink bottled water and avoid ice cubes made from tap water
  • Use bug spray to prevent mosquito bites
  • Consult a doctor before traveling and make sure you have all necessary vaccinations
  • Carry basic first aid supplies with you, like band-aids and pain medication

Getting to Islas Marietas: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Islas Marietas

The closest airport to Islas Marietas is the Puerto Vallarta International Airport, located about 40 minutes away from Punta Mita by car. There are several direct flights from major cities in the US and Canada, making it easy to reach Islas Marietas.

Bus Stations Near Islas Marietas

The main bus terminal in Puerto Vallarta, the Vallarta Central Camionera, offers regular services to Punta Mita and other destinations in the surrounding area.

Other Options

Renting a car is also a popular option for those who like to be able to explore on their own terms.

How to Get Around in Islas Marietas

The best way to get around and explore the island is by rental car or by hiring a private driver. Taxis are also available, but can be expensive.

Where to Go Next from Islas Marietas: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore more of the surrounding area, there are numerous nearby destinations and excursions worth considering:

  • Sayulita - a charming beach town with a bohemian vibe
  • San Pancho - a sleepy coastal town with incredible surf beaches
  • Puerto Vallarta - a bustling city with a rich cultural heritage and vibrant nightlife

Conclusion

Islas Marietas is an incredible destination with something to offer every type of traveler. With enough natural beauty, cultural attractions, and adventure experiences to explore, you’ll never run out of things to do and discover. By following the tips and advice outlined in this guide, you’ll be well-prepared to make the most of your trip to this stunning Pacific Coast island.
