Introduction to Puerto Escondido

Located on the Pacific coast of Mexico in the state of Oaxaca, Puerto Escondido is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Known for its stunning beaches and laid-back vibe, Puerto Escondido has something for everyone. Whether you’re a lover of culture, adventure, or simply looking to relax, Puerto Escondido has it all.

List of Places to Visit in Puerto Escondido

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Casa de la Cultura: This cultural center features art exhibits and live performances.
  • Museum of Oaxacan Handcrafts: This museum showcases handmade crafts from the area including textiles and pottery.
  • Church of San Pedro y San Pablo: A beautiful church that overlooks the entire town.

Natural Attractions of Puerto Escondido: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Carrizalillo Beach: A small, secluded beach perfect for swimming, snorkeling, and surfing.
  • La Punta: A popular spot for surfing and swimming, La Punta also offers stunning views of the ocean.
  • Chacahua National Park: This national park is home to diverse wildlife and features beautiful lagoons and mangroves.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Puerto Escondido

  • Punta Zicatela Trail: This scenic trail offers great views of the beach and ocean.
  • Lomas de San Juan: A challenging hike with rewarding views of the town and coastline.

Best Things to Do in Puerto Escondido: Activities and Tours

  • Surfing: Puerto Escondido is known for its world-class surfing. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced surfer, there’s something for everyone.
  • Boat Tours: Take a boat tour to see the diverse marine life and beautiful coastline of Puerto Escondido.
  • Turtle Watching: From June to December, you can witness the release of baby sea turtles on the beaches of Puerto Escondido.

Food and Drink Scene of Puerto Escondido

Must-Try Dishes

  • Mole: A traditional Oaxacan dish made with a sauce of spices, chocolate, and chilies.
  • Tlayudas: A large tortilla filled with refried beans, cheese, and your choice of meat.
  • Mezcal: A popular spirit made from the agave plant and valued for its unique smoky flavor.

Local Beverages

  • Fruit Juices: Try a refreshing glass of agua fresca made from local fruits like pineapple, mango, and papaya.
  • Cervezas: Enjoy a cold beer from one of the local breweries like Cerveza Nao or Cerveza Tierra Blanca.

Where to Stay in Puerto Escondido: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Villa Mozart y Macondo: A stunning, oceanfront hotel with a pool and restaurant.
  • Hostel Shalom: A budget-friendly hostel located in the heart of town.
  • Hotel Santa Fe: A charming hotel with a central location and beautiful views of the ocean.

What to Buy in Puerto Escondido: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Textiles: Bring home a handmade textile like a colorful table runner or a woven rug.
  • Pottery: Pick up some unique pottery pieces like ceramic cups or bowls.
  • Mezcal: Take home a bottle of the locally made mezcal to remember your trip by.

Local Culture and Customs of Puerto Escondido

Puerto Escondido is a friendly and welcoming town with a rich cultural history. The locals are proud of their traditions and heritage and are happy to share them with visitors. Respect for elders and family values are important in Mexican culture, so it’s important to be courteous and polite while visiting.

Best Time to Visit Puerto Escondido

The best time to visit Puerto Escondido is from November to April when the weather is dry and sunny. The summer months can be rainy, but they also bring the opportunity for turtle watching and other unique experiences.

Do’s and Don’ts of Puerto Escondido

Do:

  • Learn some Spanish phrases to help you communicate with the locals.
  • Respect the culture and customs of Puerto Escondido.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Drink tap water.
  • Wear revealing clothing in public.
  • Take photos of people without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Puerto Escondido

Puerto Escondido is generally a safe place to visit, but like any tourist destination, it’s important to take precautions. Be aware of your surroundings, don’t carry large amounts of cash or valuables, and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Puerto Escondido

It’s important to stay hydrated while visiting Puerto Escondido as the weather can be hot and humid. Make sure to drink bottled water and avoid drinking tap water. Mosquito-borne diseases like dengue fever can also be a risk, so it’s important to take precautions like wearing insect repellent and long-sleeved clothing.

Getting to Puerto Escondido: Transportation Options and Logistics

Puerto Escondido can be reached by air or land.

Airports Near Puerto Escondido

  • Puerto Escondido International Airport (PXM): This airport is the closest to Puerto Escondido and is served by Mexican airlines like Aeromar and Viva Aerobús.
  • Bahías de Huatulco International Airport (HUX): This airport is about a two-hour drive from Puerto Escondido and is served by major airlines like United and Delta.

Bus Stations Near Puerto Escondido

  • ADO Bus Terminal: This bus terminal is located in the center of town and offers services to destinations throughout Mexico.

Other Options

Taxis and private transfers are also available for those who prefer not to rent a car.

How to Get Around in Puerto Escondido

The best way to get around Puerto Escondido is by foot or taxi. Buses are also available, but they can be crowded and confusing for tourists.

Where to Go Next from Puerto Escondido: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Puerto Escondido is located in a beautiful region of Mexico and there are many nearby destinations worth exploring:

  • Mazunte: This nearby town is home to a turtle sanctuary and stunning beaches.
  • Oaxaca City: One of the most beautiful cities in Mexico, Oaxaca City is known for its stunning architecture, rich history, and vibrant culture.
  • Hierve el Agua: These natural hot springs and petrified waterfalls are a unique and beautiful sight to see.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking for adventure, relaxation, or simply a stunning beach to soak up the sun, Puerto Escondido has it all. With its rich culture and warm hospitality, you’ll feel right at home in this beautiful corner of Mexico.
