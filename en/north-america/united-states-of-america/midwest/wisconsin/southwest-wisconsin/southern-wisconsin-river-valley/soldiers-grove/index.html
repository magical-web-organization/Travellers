Introduction to Soldiers Grove

Nestled in the verdant Southern Wisconsin River Valley, Soldiers Grove is a charming small town that boasts an abundance of natural beauty, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures. Known for its scenic views, historic sites, and vibrant local culture, this hidden gem of Southwest Wisconsin is a must-visit destination for nature lovers, history buffs, and adventure seekers. Whether you’re planning a solo trip, a romantic getaway, or a family vacation, Soldiers Grove has something to offer for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Soldiers Grove

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Soldiers Grove is steeped in history and culture that dates back centuries. Visitors can experience the rich cultural legacy of the region by visiting the following iconic landmarks and museums:

  • Veterans Memorial Park: A tranquil park dedicated to soldiers who fought in various wars and conflicts. The park features a moving tribute to the soldiers who gave their lives for their country.

  • Soldiers Grove Historical Society Museum: A fascinating museum that showcases the history and heritage of the Soldiers Grove area. It features a variety of exhibits that highlight the town’s past, including artifacts, photographs, and memorabilia.

  • Watershed Cafe: This cozy cafe in Viroqua (just a few miles away from Soldiers Grove) is a hub for local artists, musicians, and writers. It offers delicious coffee and home-baked treats, and live performances and art shows take place here regularly.

Natural Attractions of Soldiers Grove: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Soldiers Grove is blessed with an abundance of natural beauty that is sure to take your breath away. Here are some popular natural attractions that you must visit:

  • Kickapoo Valley Reserve: A stunning nature reserve that spans over 8,000 acres and offers a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, fishing, and bird-watching. Spread over four counties, this reserve features unique natural landscapes, including valleys, ridges, and bluffs.

  • Driftless Area: A unique geographical region characterized by its steep hills, narrow valleys, and jagged rock formations. Visitors can explore the area by hiking, biking, or driving.

  • Copper Creek Stream Restoration Area: A conservation project that aims to restore the ecological balance of the creek. Visitors can explore the area on foot or by bike, and enjoy the beautiful scenery and abundant wildlife.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Soldiers Grove

Soldiers Grove is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and thrill-seekers. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes that you should explore:

  • Kickapoo Valley Reserve Trail: This 21-mile trail offers a variety of hiking, biking, and horseback riding options. It offers stunning views of the river valley, and visitors can explore the forest and wildlife along the trail.

  • Wildcat Mountain State Park Trail: This 11-mile trail takes visitors through the rugged hills and sandstone outcroppings of the area. It features beautiful views and is perfect for hiking, biking, and cross-country skiing.

  • Great River State Trail: This 24-mile trail is a converted railroad track that offers scenic views of the Mississippi River. It is perfect for biking and offers a flat, easy ride for all skill levels.

Best Things to Do in Soldiers Grove: Activities and Tours

Soldiers Grove is packed with exciting things to do and see. Here are some of the best activities and tours that you should not miss:

  • River Rafting: For a thrilling adventure, try river rafting down the Kickapoo River. Companies like Drifty’s Canoe Rental provide rental services, and guided tours are also available.

  • Fishing: The Kickapoo River is teeming with fish, including trout, bass, and walleye. Fishing is a popular activity for visitors and locals alike.

  • Orchard Tours: Soldiers Grove is known for its apple orchards, and visitors can take tours to learn about apple farming and sample delicious apple products.

Food and Drink Scene of Soldiers Grove

Must-Try Dishes

Soldiers Grove offers a unique blend of culinary styles that reflects the local tastes and flavors. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • Fish Fry: A classic Wisconsin dish made with fish and served with fries, coleslaw, and tartar sauce.

  • Cheeseburger: A juicy burger topped with Wisconsin cheddar and served with crispy fries.

  • Apple Pie: Made with fresh apples sourced from the local orchards, this pie is a classic dessert that is sure to satisfy your sweet tooth.

Local Beverages

Wisconsin is known for its beer and cheese, and Soldiers Grove is no exception. Visitors can enjoy the following local beverages:

  • Spotted Cow: A highly popular local beer made by the New Glarus Brewing Company.

  • Cheese Curds: A classic Wisconsin snack made with fresh cheese curds and served with dipping sauce.

Where to Stay in Soldiers Grove: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Soldiers Grove offers a range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels and guesthouses. Here are some popular options:

  • Kickapoo Valley Ranch Guest House: A charming guesthouse that offers comfortable rooms, delicious food, and stunning views of the valley.

  • Hotel de Ville: A historic hotel that dates back to the 1890s and offers a blend of modern amenities and classic charm.

  • Buffalo Motel: A budget-friendly motel that offers basic amenities and easy access to local attractions.

What to Buy in Soldiers Grove: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors can take home a piece of Soldiers Grove’s unique culture and heritage by buying the following souvenirs and local products:

  • Apple Products: Soldiers Grove is known for its apple orchards, and visitors can buy fresh apples, apple cider, apple butter, and other apple-based products.

  • Local Crafts: Artisans in the area create unique handmade crafts, including pottery, jewelry, and paintings.

Local Culture and Customs of Soldiers Grove

Soldiers Grove has a vibrant local culture that reflects the area’s history and heritage. Visitors can experience the following cultural customs and traditions:

  • Local Festivals: Soldiers Grove hosts several festivals throughout the year, including the Apple Fest, Independence Day Celebration, and Fall Festival.

  • Farmers Market: A popular gathering place for locals where visitors can buy fresh produce, baked goods, and other locally made products.

Best Time to Visit Soldiers Grove

The best time to visit Soldiers Grove is during the summer and fall months when the weather is pleasant, and the natural landscapes are in full bloom. The apple harvest season (September to October) is a great time to visit the town and engage in apple-related activities.

Safety and Health

Soldiers Grove is a safe and friendly town, and visitors need not worry about safety concerns. However, it is always advisable to be cautious while exploring the natural landscapes and engaging in outdoor activities.

Health in Soldiers Grove

Soldiers Grove has access to several healthcare facilities, including hospitals and clinics. Visitors should ensure they have adequate health insurance coverage before traveling.

Getting to Soldiers Grove: Transportation Options and Logistics

The nearest airports to Soldiers Grove are:

  • Dubuque Regional Airport: Located about 65 miles from Soldiers Grove.

Bus Stations Near Soldiers Grove

The nearest bus station to Soldiers Grove is:

  • Greyhound Bus Station in La Crosse: Located about 45 miles from Soldiers Grove.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Soldiers Grove or use the local taxi and shuttle services.

How to Get Around in Soldiers Grove

The best way to get around Soldiers Grove is by car, as the town is spread over a large area, and the natural attractions and cultural landmarks are dispersed.

Where to Go Next from Soldiers Grove: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
