Introduction to Camp Douglas

Camp Douglas is a charming town nestled in the Baraboo River Valley in Southwest Wisconsin. It is located in the Midwest region of the United States of America. Camp Douglas is a popular destination with plenty of natural beauty, cultural attractions, outdoor adventures, and a rich history.

Best Time to Visit Camp Douglas

Do’s and Don’ts of Camp Douglas

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions.
  • Explore the beautiful natural scenery of Camp Douglas.

Don’t:

  • Leave any trash or litter behind.
  • Harm or disturb local wildlife.
  • Ignore safety guidelines when engaging in outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Camp Douglas

  • Always be aware of your surroundings.
  • Follow safety guidelines when engaging in outdoors activities.
  • Take precautions against ticks during tick season.

Health in Camp Douglas

  • The nearest medical facilities are in Tomah or Mauston.
