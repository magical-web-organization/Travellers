Introduction to Prairie du Sac

Prairie du Sac is a hidden gem located in the Baraboo River Valley, in Southwest Wisconsin. Known for its charming small-town atmosphere, it offers visitors a chance to experience the beauty and tranquility of the Midwest. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or just a relaxing getaway, Prairie du Sac has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Prairie du Sac

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Wollersheim Winery & Distillery

The Wollersheim Winery & Distillery is a must-visit attraction in Prairie du Sac. Located on the banks of the Wisconsin River, this historic winery has been in operation since the mid-1800s and produces award-winning wines and spirits.

Wisconsin Opry

The Wisconsin Opry is a unique experience for visitors who want to enjoy live country music in a down-home atmosphere. With regular shows featuring local talent, this venue offers a glimpse into Wisconsin’s rich music heritage.

Natural Attractions of Prairie du Sac: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Pewits Nest State Natural Area

Pewits Nest State Natural Area is a breathtakingly beautiful nature reserve that offers visitors a chance to explore ancient rock formations, waterfalls, and scenic views of the Baraboo River. Perfect for hiking, photography, and picnics.

Devil’s Lake State Park

Devil’s Lake State Park is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. With miles of hiking trails, rock climbing, swimming, and fishing opportunities, this park has something for everyone. Don’t miss the stunning views of Devil’s Lake, surrounded by towering cliffs.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Prairie du Sac

Great Sauk State Trail

The Great Sauk State Trail is a 6.2-mile trail that offers visitors a chance to explore the beautiful Wisconsin countryside by foot, bike, or horseback. Running from Prairie du Sac to Sauk City, the trail passes through wooded areas, fields, and scenic views of the Wisconsin River.

Prairie du Sac Dam Trail

The Prairie du Sac Dam Trail is a great option for visitors looking for a shorter, easier hike. The 2-mile trail winds through prairie grasslands and offers stunning views of the Wisconsin River, the Prairie du Sac Dam, and the Wollersheim Winery & Distillery.

Best Things to Do in Prairie du Sac: Activities and Tours

River Arts Inc.

River Arts Inc. is a non-profit organization that promotes the arts in the Prairie du Sac community. With regular art exhibits, live performances, and classes for all ages, River Arts Inc. is the perfect destination for art lovers.

Wisconsin River Outings

Wisconsin River Outings offers visitors a chance to explore the Wisconsin River by kayak, canoe, or paddleboard. With a variety of guided tours available, visitors can experience the beauty of the river while learning about its history and ecology.

Food and Drink Scene of Prairie du Sac

Must-Try Dishes

Cheese Curds

No visit to Wisconsin would be complete without trying the state’s famous cheese curds. Made from fresh cheese curdled from milk, these delicious bites are deep-fried and served hot.

Bratwurst

Wisconsin is also known for its delicious bratwurst. Grilled to perfection and served on a bun with sauerkraut and mustard, this classic Wisconsin dish is a must-try.

Local Beverages

Craft Beer

A trip to Wisconsin wouldn’t be complete without trying some of the state’s famous craft beers. With a variety of breweries in the area, visitors can enjoy unique and flavorful brews made with local ingredients.

Wine

Prairie du Sac is home to the Wollersheim Winery & Distillery, where visitors can enjoy award-winning wines and spirits made from locally grown grapes.

Where to Stay in Prairie du Sac: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Victorian Dreams Bed & Breakfast

Located in a beautifully restored Victorian-era home, the Victorian Dreams Bed & Breakfast offers guests a charming and relaxing getaway. With comfortable rooms and a delicious breakfast served each morning, it’s the perfect place to stay while exploring Prairie du Sac.

Badger Aire Motel

For visitors looking for a more budget-friendly option, the Badger Aire Motel offers clean and comfortable rooms at an affordable price.

What to Buy in Prairie du Sac: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Wollersheim Wines and Spirits

A bottle of Wollersheim wine or spirits is the perfect souvenir to bring home from Prairie du Sac. Made from locally grown grapes, these award-winning products are a great way to remember your trip to Wisconsin.

Local Crafts

Prairie du Sac is known for its thriving arts community, and visitors can find a variety of unique and handmade crafts at local shops and galleries.

Local Culture and Customs of Prairie du Sac

Wisconsin is known for its friendly and welcoming locals. Visitors to Prairie du Sac can expect to be greeted with a warm Midwest hospitality and a genuine interest in their travels.

Best Time to Visit Prairie du Sac

The best time to visit Prairie du Sac is in the summer when the weather is warm and sunny. Visitors can enjoy a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking, swimming, and kayaking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Prairie du Sac

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine, including cheese curds and bratwurst.
  • Do explore the area’s natural beauty by hiking or kayaking.
  • Do support local businesses, from shops to breweries and wineries.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget to bring comfortable shoes for hiking and walking.
  • Don’t litter or leave trash in the local parks and natural areas.
  • Don’t forget to bring sunscreen and insect repellent for outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Prairie du Sac

Prairie du Sac is a safe and friendly community, but visitors should be aware of their surroundings and take normal safety precautions, such as locking car doors and keeping valuables out of sight.

Health in Prairie du Sac

Visitors should be aware of the potential for tick-borne illnesses, such as Lyme disease, when spending time outdoors. It’s important to wear long sleeves and pants and use insect repellent to avoid tick bites.

Getting to Prairie du Sac: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Prairie du Sac

The closest major airport to Prairie du Sac is the Dane County Regional Airport in Madison, Wisconsin, which is approximately 35 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Prairie du Sac

Greyhound offers bus service to the nearby city of Baraboo, Wisconsin, which is approximately 10 miles away.

Other Options

Driving to Prairie du Sac is the most convenient option for visitors. From Madison, take US-12 W and WI-188 N to reach Prairie du Sac.

How to Get Around in Prairie du Sac

Most visitors to Prairie du Sac will want to rent a car to explore the area’s beautiful countryside and nearby attractions. Local bike rental shops are also available for visitors who prefer to explore on two wheels.

Where to Go Next from Prairie du Sac: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Baraboo, Wisconsin

Located just 10 miles from Prairie du Sac, Baraboo is home to a variety of attractions, including the Circus World Museum, Devil’s Lake State Park, and the International Crane Foundation.

Madison, Wisconsin

Madison, the capital city of Wisconsin, is located approximately 35 miles from Prairie du Sac. With a thriving food and drink scene, numerous cultural attractions, and unique shopping experiences, Madison is a must-visit destination in the Midwest.

Conclusion

Prairie du Sac offers visitors a chance to experience the beauty and tranquility of the Midwest. With a variety of outdoor activities, cultural experiences, and delicious food and drink options, it’s the perfect destination for a relaxing and memorable getaway.
