Introduction to Black River Falls

Nestled in the heart of Northwest Wisconsin, Black River Falls is a charming destination that’s perfect for outdoor enthusiasts, history buffs, and anyone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. The city boasts an impressive selection of natural and cultural attractions, making it an excellent location for a week-long trip or a relaxing weekend getaway.

List of Places to Visit in Black River Falls

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re interested in learning about the history and culture of Black River Falls, you’re in luck. The city is home to a variety of museums and historical landmarks that are worth a visit. Be sure to check out:

  1. Jackson County Historical Society Museum: Explore the history of Jackson County and Black River Falls through a range of exhibits and artifacts.

  2. Ho-Chunk Gaming Black River Falls: This casino hotel offers a variety of entertainment options, including table games, slot machines, and live music.

  3. Castle Mound Pine Forest State Natural Area: This park features a historic fire tower and a network of hiking trails that offer stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Natural Attractions of Black River Falls: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Black River Falls is known for its stunning natural beauty and abundance of outdoor activities. Whether you’re an avid hiker, a wildlife enthusiast, or just looking to enjoy the fresh air, there’s something for everyone in this charming destination. Here are a few highlights:

  1. Black River State Forest: This expansive park boasts over 68,000 acres of forests, rivers, and lakes, making it an ideal destination for hiking, fishing, and camping.

  2. Wazee Lake Recreation Area: This unique park features a man-made lake that’s over 350 feet deep, making it a popular spot for scuba diving.

  3. Sand Creek Brewing Company: After a day of exploring the great outdoors, head to Sand Creek Brewing Company for a refreshing local beer and a meal on their outdoor patio.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Black River Falls

Whether you prefer hiking or biking, Black River Falls is a great destination for exploring the great outdoors. Some popular trails include:

  1. Castle Mound Trail: This challenging 5.7-mile loop offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape, including the historic fire tower at the summit.

  2. Pigeon Creek Trail: This family-friendly trail is just over 2 miles long and takes you through a beautiful forested area next to a scenic river.

  3. Skyline Trail: This 5.4-mile out-and-back trail offers breathtaking views of the Black River Valley and is perfect for more experienced hikers.

Best Things to Do in Black River Falls: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking, biking, and exploring the natural beauty of Black River Falls, there are plenty of other activities and tours to enjoy. Here are a few options:

  1. ATV Tours: Take an ATV tour of the local landscape and explore the backcountry of the Black River Falls area.

  2. Horseback Riding: Go horseback riding through the beautiful natural landscapes of Black River Falls and Jackson County.

  3. Zip Line Tours: Experience the thrill of flying through the trees with a zip line tour of the area.

Week-Long Itinerary for Black River Falls

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Black River Falls, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day 1: Arrive in Black River Falls, settle into your accommodations, and check out the local restaurants and bars.

Day 2: Head to Wazee Lake Recreation Area for a day of diving, fishing, and swimming.

Day 3: Take a tour of the Jackson County Historical Society Museum and learn about the history of the area.

Day 4: Explore the Black River State Forest on foot or by bike.

Day 5: Go horseback riding through the countryside and forests of Jackson County.

Day 6: Take an ATV tour of the Black River Falls area and discover the back roads and hidden gems of the region.

Day 7: Take it easy and enjoy a relaxing day at your hotel or vacation rental. Maybe, get yourself some local souvenirs or fresh produce at the farmer’s market.

Food and Drink Scene of Black River Falls

No trip to Black River Falls is complete without trying some of the local cuisine and beverages. Here are a few must-try dishes and drinks:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Fried Cheese Curds: A Wisconsin classic, these bite-sized nuggets of deep-fried cheese are the perfect appetizer or midday snack.

  2. Cranberry Relish: Made with fresh cranberries, sugar, and a hint of orange, this dish is the perfect addition to any Thanksgiving feast.

  3. Fish Fry: A staple of Wisconsin cuisine, the fish fry typically consists of fried cod or perch, served with coleslaw, fries, and tartar sauce.

Local Beverages

  1. Beer: Wisconsin is known for its beer, and Black River Falls is no exception. Check out Sand Creek Brewing Company or Reconstructed Soul to sample some of the best local brews.

  2. Wine: The area around Black River Falls is home to several wineries, including River Valley Winery and Sandhill Winery. Be sure to try a glass of their award-winning fruit wines.

Where to Stay in Black River Falls: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options in Black River Falls, whether you’re looking for a budget-friendly hostel, a cozy guesthouse, or a luxury hotel. Here are a few options to consider:

  1. Best Western Arrowhead Lodge & Suites: This hotel offers comfortable rooms, an indoor pool, and a sauna, making it the perfect choice for a relaxing stay in Black River Falls.

  2. The Falls Motel: This budget-friendly option offers clean, simple rooms at a great price.

  3. The Lunda Community Center: This community center and hostel offers affordable accommodations for groups and individuals, as well as access to a fitness center and other amenities.

What to Buy in Black River Falls: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for the perfect souvenir or gift to bring home from Black River Falls, there are a few local products to consider:

  1. Cranberry products: Jackson County is home to some of the best cranberry bogs in the country, so be sure to pick up some fresh cranberries or locally made cranberry relish to take home with you.

  2. Maple Syrup: Wisconsin is one of the top maple syrup producers in the world, and you can find a wide range of locally made maple syrup and maple products in Black River Falls.

  3. Handmade crafts: Visit the local farmer’s market or stop by one of the many artisan shops in Black River Falls to find unique, handmade crafts and gifts.

Local Culture and Customs of Black River Falls

Black River Falls is steeped in local culture and customs, including a rich Native American heritage. Be sure to respect the local customs and traditions, and take the time to learn about the rich history and culture of the area.

Best Time to Visit Black River Falls

The best time to visit Black River Falls is during the summer and fall months, when the weather is warm and the landscape is ablaze with color. However, the city is also a great destination in the winter months, when you can enjoy snowmobiling and other winter sports.

Do’s and Don’ts of Black River Falls

Do:

  • Take the time to explore the natural beauty of the area, including the Jackson County forests and waterways.

  • Respect the local culture and customs, and take the time to learn about the history of the region.

  • Try some of the local cuisine and beverages, including Wisconsin beer and cranberry products.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural landscape of the area.

  • Don’t disrespect the local culture or customs.

  • Don’t leave without trying some of the local cuisine and beverages.

Safety and Health

Safety in Black River Falls

Black River Falls is generally a safe destination, but it’s always a good idea to take basic safety precautions. Stay aware of your surroundings, especially when hiking or exploring the backcountry, and keep your valuables secured in your hotel or rental property.

Health in Black River Falls

The city has a variety of medical facilities and pharmacies, so visitors can easily access healthcare services if needed.

How to Get Around in Black River Falls

The best way to get around Black River Falls is by car or bike, especially if you plan on exploring the surrounding natural areas and attractions. There are also a number of taxi and ride-sharing services available in the city.

Where to Go Next from Black River Falls: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore more of the Midwest, there are plenty of fascinating destinations and excursions within a few hours of Black River Falls. Some options to consider include:

  • Wisconsin Dells, WI: A popular family-friendly destination known for its water parks, amusement parks, and outdoor activities.

  • Duluth, MN: This historic port city on Lake Superior is known for its scenic beauty, delicious cuisine, and rich cultural heritage.

  • Chicago, IL: The Windy City is just a few hours’ drive from Black River Falls and offers an endless array of attractions, including world-class museums, iconic architecture, and a thriving food and drink scene.

Conclusion

Black River Falls is a hidden gem in the heart of the Midwest, offering visitors a wide range of cultural and natural attractions, as well as plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures and relaxation. Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or an outdoor adventure with friends, Black River Falls has something to offer everyone.
