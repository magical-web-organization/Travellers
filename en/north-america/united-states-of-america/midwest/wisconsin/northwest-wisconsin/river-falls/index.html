Introduction to River Falls

Located in Northwest Wisconsin, River Falls combines the charm of a small town with natural beauty and outdoor adventures. This vibrant and culturally rich city offers something for everyone, whether you want to explore history and culture, enjoy outdoor activities, or simply relax and unwind in a peaceful setting.

List of Places to Visit in River Falls

Here are some of the must-see attractions in River Falls:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Kinnickinnic River Land Trust Heritage Preserve: Explore the history and natural beauty of the Kinnickinnic River Valley at this 300-acre preserve.
  • The Old Toll Bridge: This historic bridge spans the Kinnickinnic River and offers stunning views of downtown River Falls.
  • The River Falls Historical Society: Visit the museum to learn about the history of the area and its people.

Natural Attractions of River Falls: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hoffman Park: This 196-acre park has something for everyone, including hiking trails, picnic areas, a disc golf course, and more.
  • White Pathway: A beautifully scenic walking path that takes you through the heart of River Falls.
  • Glen Park: A 41-acre park with biking and hiking trails, a playground, and a scenic waterfall.

Hiking/Biking Routes of River Falls

River Falls offers plenty of hiking and biking routes to explore the natural beauty of the surrounding areas. Some of the best routes include:

  • White Pathway: A 1.5-mile paved trail that winds through downtown River Falls and offers stunning views of the area.
  • Baldwin Wildlife Area: A 1,200-acre wooded area with hiking and biking trails that lead through prairies, forests, and wetlands.
  • Kinnickinnic River Trail: A 5.8-mile trail that follows the Kinnickinnic River and offers beautiful views of the surrounding scenery.

Best Things to Do in River Falls: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the top activities and tours to experience in River Falls:

  • Floating on the Kinnickinnic River: A unique and relaxing way to explore the beauty of River Falls.
  • Farm Tour: Get an up-close look at the farming and animal husbandry practices in the area.
  • Brewery Tour: Sample some of the delicious local beers at local River Falls breweries.

Food and Drink Scene of River Falls

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cheese Curds: Wisconsin is famous for its cheese curds, and River Falls is no exception. Try them at local eateries like West Wind Supper Club.
  • Brats: Another Wisconsin staple, brats are a must-try in River Falls. Be sure to check out the local farmers market for fresh brats to grill up yourself.
  • Fish Fry: Friday night fish fry is a beloved tradition in Wisconsin, and River Falls has some of the best. Try it at local restaurants like the Dish.

Local Beverages

  • Local microbreweries like Rush River Brewing Company and The Division Brewery offer a variety of delicious brews to sample.
  • Local wineries like BelleVinez Vineyard and Winery offer tastings of their award-winning wines.

Where to Stay in River Falls: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Best Western Plus Campus Inn: A comfortable and convenient option located near the University of Wisconsin-River Falls.
  • Don’s Motel: A budget-friendly option that offers clean and cozy rooms and easy access to downtown River Falls.
  • Airbnbs in the area offer affordable and comfortable stay options for those looking for a more homely feeling.

What to Buy in River Falls: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Local cheese from the Farmers Market is a must.
  • Locally made crafts and jewelry from local curated shops like Riverwalk Art and Antiques.
  • Locally made beers from local breweries like Rush River Brewing Company can be brought home as a souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of River Falls

River Falls is a friendly and welcoming community where visitors are treated like locals. The area is known for its love of the outdoors, good food, and community events. Visitors should be sure to interact with locals and take part in community events like farmers markets, community festivals, and outdoor activities.

Best Time to Visit River Falls

Summertime is the best time to visit River Falls when temperatures are mild and the outdoors are open for exploration.

Do’s and Don’ts of River Falls

Do:

  • Do interact with locals and try to immerse yourself in local culture.
  • Do take advantage of outdoor activities like hiking, biking, fishing and floating on the Kinnickinnic River.
  • Do try local specialties like brats, cheese curds, and fish fry.
  • Do take time to explore the history and cultural landmarks like the River Falls Historical Society.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter and help keep the outdoors spotless.
  • Do not violate safety regulations, age requirements initiated at various trips.

Safety and Health

Safety in River Falls

River Falls is generally a safe place to visit. Visitors should take standard safety precautions to avoid theft and keep themselves safe while exploring the outdoors.

Health in River Falls

Visitors should be sure to bring any medications or medical supplies they need, and be prepared for emergencies with local pharmacies and hospitals nearby.

Getting to River Falls: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near River Falls

The nearest airport is Minneapolis-St. Paul International Airport, approximately 40 miles from River Falls.

Bus Stations Near River Falls

Greyhound offers bus service to nearby Hudson, Wisconsin, a short drive from River Falls.

Other Options

Renting a car or using a ride-sharing service is the most convenient option to travel around River Falls and nearby areas.

How to Get Around in River Falls

Visitors can get around River Falls easily by foot, bicycle, or car. Bicycles and cars can also be rented from local rental shops.

Where to Go Next from River Falls: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

River Falls is located near several other popular destinations in the Midwest, including:

  • Stillwater, Minnesota: A charming and picturesque town located just across the border from Wisconsin.
  • Minneapolis, Minnesota: A vibrant and cultural-rich city located just 1 hour away from River Falls.
  • Eau Claire, Wisconsin: A scenic and charming town located just 30 minutes away from River Falls.

Conclusion

River Falls is a hidden gem in Wisconsin that offers visitors a unique combination of natural beauty and cultural richness. From outdoor adventures to cultural landmarks, this vibrant city has something for everyone.
