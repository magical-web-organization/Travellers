Food and Drink Scene of Freedom

Must-Try Dishes

  • Wisconsin Cheese Curds: These deep-fried cheese curds are a local favorite and can be found at many restaurants and pubs.
  • Bratwurst: Wisconsin is known for its bratwurst, and Freedom is no exception. Be sure to try a classic brat on a bun with sauerkraut.
  • Fish Fry: On Fridays, many local restaurants serve up a traditional Wisconsin fish fry, consisting of battered and fried fish, coleslaw, and potato pancakes.

Local Beverages

  • Wisconsin Beer: Wisconsin is home to many craft breweries. Be sure to try local favorites like New Glarus’ Spotted Cow or Titletown Brewing Company’s Johnny Blood Red.
  • Door County Wine: Wisconsin’s Door County is known for its wineries and produces some delicious wines. Visitors can sample the wine at one of the many tasting rooms in the area.

Where to Stay in Freedom: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Copperleaf Boutique Hotel & Spa: This luxurious hotel in downtown Appleton offers spa services and elegant accommodations.
  • La Quinta Inn & Suites by Wyndham: This budget-friendly hotel offers comfortable rooms and a complimentary breakfast.
  • Airbnb: Many local residents offer their homes for rent on Airbnb. This can be a great option for visitors looking for a unique and authentic experience.

What to Buy in Freedom: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Cheese: Wisconsin is known for its cheese, and visitors can bring home some of the best cheddar, curds, and cheese spreads as souvenirs.
  • Maple Syrup: Wisconsin produces some delicious maple syrup. Visitors can purchase bottles of syrup to bring home as a tasty souvenir.
  • Wisconsin Apparel: Show your Wisconsin pride by purchasing a t-shirt, hat, or hoodie featuring the state’s logo or a cheesehead design.

Local Culture and Customs of Freedom

Freedom, like much of Wisconsin, prides itself on its strong German heritage. Visitors may notice locals celebrating traditional German holidays like Oktoberfest and decorating their homes with Bavarian-style architecture.

Best Time to Visit Freedom

The best time to visit Freedom is during the summer months (June-August), when the weather is warm and the outdoor activities are in full swing. However, fall is also a great time to visit, as the changing leaves offer a stunning backdrop for hiking and biking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Freedom

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine like cheese curds and bratwurst.
  • Do take advantage of the outdoor activities like hiking and biking.
  • Do dress in layers, as the weather can be unpredictable.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget bug spray if you plan on being outdoors.
  • Don’t leave without trying some Wisconsin beer or wine.
  • Don’t be afraid to strike up a conversation with a friendly local.

Safety and Health

Safety in Freedom

Freedom is a relatively safe town, but visitors should still take precautions like locking their car doors and not leaving valuables in plain sight.

Health in Freedom

In case of a medical emergency, visitors can go to ThedaCare Medical Center in nearby Appleton.

Getting to Freedom: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Freedom

  • Appleton International Airport: Located just 10 miles from Freedom, this airport offers flights from major airlines like Delta, United, and American Airlines.

Bus Stations Near Freedom

  • Greyhound Bus Station: Located in downtown Appleton, this bus station offers routes throughout the United States.

Other Options

Many visitors choose to rent a car to explore the area.

How to Get Around in Freedom

The best way to get around Freedom is by car. Alternatively, visitors can bike or walk to explore the town’s downtown area.

Where to Go Next from Freedom: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Door County: Located just an hour and a half from Freedom, Door County is known for its stunning scenery, outdoor activities, and delicious local cuisine.
  • Green Bay: This city, located just 30 minutes from Freedom, offers museums, shopping, and dining options.

Conclusion

Freedom may be a small town, but it has plenty to offer visitors seeking outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, and delicious food and drink. Plan your trip today and experience the best of Northeast Wisconsin.
