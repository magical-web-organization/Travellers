Introduction to Ledgeview

Ledgeview is a charming village located in Northeast Wisconsin, United States of America. The village is a part of the Green Bay metropolitan area and boasts a perfect blend of man-made and natural attractions. The place is known for its lush greenery, bike routes, and scenic landscapes. The main highlights of the village are Green Bay Trail and Heritage Hill State Historical Park.

List of Places to Visit in Ledgeview

Here is a list of places you must visit in Ledgeview.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Heritage Hill State Historical Park - This living history museum is spread over 50 acres and showcases a unique collection of historical houses and buildings.

  2. Neville Public Museum - The museum has a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits related to the history and culture of Northeast Wisconsin.

  3. Green Bay Botanical Garden - This botanical garden has an exquisite collection of plants from different parts of the world.

Natural Attractions of Ledgeview: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Green Bay Trail - The trail is 88 miles long and runs from the outskirts of Green Bay to the southern part of Brown County. It is perfect for hiking, biking, and birdwatching.

  2. Ledgeview Nature Center - The center is spread over 70 acres and has five miles of hiking trails.

  3. The Bay Beach Wildlife Sanctuary - A perfect spot for nature lovers, this sanctuary has a wide range of animals, including bison, deer, and birds.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ledgeview

Ledgeview has some of the best hiking and biking routes in Wisconsin. Here are a few notable routes:

  1. Mountain Bay State Trail - The trail runs 83 miles from Green Bay to Weston and offers stunning views of the countryside.

  2. Baird Creek Trail - This trail is perfect for hiking, biking, and skiing, depending on the season. It is 2.5 miles long and runs along the Baird Creek Greenway.

  3. Fox River State Trail - This 25-mile trail runs parallel to the Fox River and provides breathtaking views of the surrounding forests and waterfalls.

Best Things to Do in Ledgeview: Activities and Tours

Ledgeview has an array of exciting activities and tours to offer. Here are some of the top things you must try:

  1. Fishing on Bay Lake - Bay Lake is known for its excellent fishing opportunities. Hire a guide to fish for large-mouth bass, northern pike, and other freshwater fish.

  2. Green Bay Escape & Axe - Test your critical thinking skills in this interactive escape room game.

  3. Skiing and Snowboarding at the local ski park - The ski park has a variety of winter sports options, including skiing, snowboarding, and tubing.

Food and Drink Scene of Ledgeview

No trip is complete without experiencing the local food and drink scene. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in Ledgeview:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Wisconsin Cheese Curds - Ledgeview is known for its crispy and delicious Wisconsin cheese curds. You can find them in restaurants and food trucks throughout the village.

  2. Bratwurst - You can’t visit Wisconsin without trying bratwurst. It’s a grilled sausage made from ground pork and beef and is served with sauerkraut and mustard.

  3. Fish Fry - Fish Fry is a local favorite in Ledgeview. It’s usually made with fried cod and served with fries, coleslaw, and tartar sauce.

Local Beverages

  1. Spotted Cow Beer - This beer is brewed locally in New Glarus and is a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

  2. Old Fashioned Cocktails - Old fashioned cocktails are a Wisconsin classic made with brandy, sugar, bitters, and a cherry.

  3. Bloody Marys - Wisconsin has some of the best Bloody Marys in the country. They are usually garnished with cheese curds, sausages, and pickles.

Getting to Ledgeview: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ledgeview

The nearest airport to Ledgeview is Austin Straubel International Airport, located in Green Bay. The airport is 10 miles from Ledgeview, and you can hire a taxi or rent a car from the airport.

Bus Stations Near Ledgeview

Greyhound Bus Lines and Jefferson Lines have bus stations near Ledgeview. The nearest bus stations are in Green Bay and De Pere.

Other Options

You can also reach Ledgeview by car or train. The nearest Amtrak stations are in Milwaukee and Chicago.

How to Get Around in Ledgeview
