Introduction to Inver Grove Heights

Located in the Twin Cities of Minnesota in the Midwest region of the United States, Inver Grove Heights is a charming city that blends urban amenities with outdoor adventures. Home to over 35,000 residents, Inver Grove Heights is known for its beautiful parks, historic landmarks, and recreational areas.

List of Places to Visit in Inver Grove Heights

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Inver Grove Heights Heritage Village: This open-air museum showcases what life was like in the city in the 19th and early 20th centuries. Visitors can see original buildings, including a one-room schoolhouse, an old church, and a blacksmith shop.
  • Inver Wood Golf Course: A popular golf course with beautiful greens and challenging holes. It is one of the most popular attractions in the city and a must-visit for any golf enthusiast.
  • Inver Grove Heights Days: This annual event celebrates local history with food, games, and live music. It is a great way to experience the city’s culture.

Natural Attractions of Inver Grove Heights: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Salem Hills Park: A popular park with a playground, picnic areas, hiking trails, and a disc golf course. It is a great destination for families.
  • Pine Bend Scientific and Natural Area: A unique natural area with rare plant species, similar to a nature reserve. Visitors can view the unique plant ecology and see the stunning views of the surrounding area.
  • Simley Island Park: A park with a great view of the river, perfect for picnics and scenic walks.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Inver Grove Heights

  • Mendota-Lebanon Hills Greenway: A popular trail connecting the two cities, the trail goes through forested areas and is great for biking and hiking enthusiasts.
  • Mississippi River Trail: A scenic trail that overlooks the Mississippi River. It passes through parks, nature preserves, and historic landmarks.

Best Things to Do in Inver Grove Heights: Activities and Tours

  • Gravity Trails: A thrilling adventure that involves zip lining, tower climbing, and ropes courses. The activity is great for families and adventure enthusiasts.
  • Minnesota Raceway Park: A great place to experience motorsports and auto racing. Visitors can see different races, from stock car racing to drag racing.
  • K-1 Speed: An indoor go-kart racing attraction with challenging tracks and a family-friendly environment.

Food and Drink Scene of Inver Grove Heights

Must-Try Dishes

  • Juicy Lucy: A popular burger variation with cheese melted inside the meat.
  • Tater Tot Hotdish: A classic Minnesota dish consisting of tater tots, vegetables, and meat baked in a casserole dish.
  • Fried Walleye: A popular fish in Minnesota, particularly in Inver Grove Heights, usually battered and fried to perfection.

Local Beverages

  • Surly Furious: A popular beer brewed in Minneapolis, available in many bars and restaurants in Inver Grove Heights.
  • Summit EPA: Another popular beer brewed in St. Paul, known for its rich, hoppy flavor.

Local Culture and Customs of Inver Grove Heights

Inver Grove Heights is known for being a friendly, family-oriented city that values community. Many residents take pride in their history, culture, and beautiful natural surroundings.

Best Time to Visit Inver Grove Heights

The best time to visit Inver Grove Heights is during the summer months when the weather is warm and sunny, making it an ideal time for outdoor activities. Fall is also a great time to visit, with beautiful fall foliage and pleasant temperatures.

Do’s and Don’ts of Inver Grove Heights

Do:

  • Take comfortable shoes and clothing for outdoor activities.
  • Respect local customs, such as the importance of community and family bonds.
  • Take advantage of the local amenities, such as parks and outdoor trails.

Don’t:

  • Litter in the parks or natural areas.
  • Ignore the natural environment - be aware of the plants, wildlife, and natural features.
  • Disrespect locals or their culture.

Safety and Health

Safety in Inver Grove Heights

Inver Grove Heights is a relatively safe city, but visitors should still be aware of their surroundings and take basic safety precautions. It is important to keep valuables locked in the car or hotel room and to not leave items unattended.

Health in Inver Grove Heights

Inver Grove Heights has several medical facilities, including clinics and hospitals. Visitors should ensure they have comprehensive travel insurance to cover any medical emergencies.

Getting to Inver Grove Heights: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Inver Grove Heights

  • Minneapolis-St. Paul International Airport is the closest airport to Inver Grove Heights, located around 15 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Inver Grove Heights

  • Amtrak Station St. Paul is the closest train station to Inver Grove Heights, located around 12 miles away.

Other Options

  • Car rental options are available at the airport.

How to Get Around in Inver Grove Heights

  • The best way to get around Inver Grove Heights is by car, either by rental or taxi.
  • The nearby cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul are easily accessible by public transportation, including buses and light rail.

Where to Go Next from Inver Grove Heights: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Minneapolis/St. Paul: The Twin Cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul are just a short drive away and offer a wealth of cultural attractions, including museums, art galleries, and historical sites.
  • Mississippi River Valley: The Mississippi River Valley is a beautiful and scenic area perfect for outdoor adventures, such as hiking, camping, and fishing.

Conclusion

Inver Grove Heights is a beautiful and historic city with plenty to offer visitors. From its natural attractions and outdoor adventures to its rich cultural heritage and food and drink scene, there is something for everyone in this charming city.
