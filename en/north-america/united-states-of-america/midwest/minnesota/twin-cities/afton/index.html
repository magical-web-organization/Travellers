Introduction to Afton

Located in the heart of the Midwest in the state of Minnesota, Afton is a small, picturesque town that offers visitors an idyllic retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life. The town is situated on the eastern banks of the St. Croix River and is known for its scenic beauty and outdoor recreational opportunities. Afton is a perfect destination for anyone looking to escape into nature or experience a slower and more relaxing pace of life. This travel guide will provide you with all the information you need to make the most of your visit to Afton.

List of Places to Visit in Afton

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Afton’s rich history is well preserved through its museums and historical sites. Some of the top cultural attractions to visit in Afton include:

  • Afton Historical Museum: Located in the old railroad depot, this museum features exhibits showcasing the town’s history from its early days as a pioneer settlement to its present-day identity as a quaint river town.
  • Squire House Gardens: This beautifully landscaped garden is a serene space to get away from the city and enjoy the scenic beauty of Afton and its surroundings. Visitors can wander through the gardens and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.
  • St. Croix River Crossing: A picturesque bridge that provides stunning views of the St. Croix River and its surroundings. A perfect place to watch the sunset.

Natural Attractions of Afton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Afton is a nature lover’s paradise, offering a wide variety of outdoor activities and natural attractions. Some of the top-rated natural attractions and outdoor activities in Afton include:

  • Afton State Park: Afton State Park covers over 1,700 acres and offers a variety of outdoor activities, including camping, hiking, fishing, and cross-country skiing. The park is home to a variety of wildlife, including deer, foxes, raccoons, and various bird species.
  • Afton Alps Ski Resort: Afton Alps is Minnesota’s largest ski resort and offers visitors over 300 acres of skiable terrain, a variety of runs for all skill levels, and beautiful winter scenery.
  • Applewood Hills Golf Course: A beautiful and well-maintained golf course that offers golfers panoramic views of the St. Croix River Valley. The course is challenging but enjoyable for golfers of all skill levels.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Afton

Afton is home to miles of scenic hiking and biking trails that allow visitors to experience the natural beauty of the area up close and personal. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Afton:

  • Brown’s Creek State Trail: This beautiful 5.9-mile trail runs from Stillwater to Grant and offers visitors stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
  • Afton State Park Hiking Trails: Afton State Park is home to over 20 miles of hiking trails that wind through the park’s beautiful forests, prairies, and bluffs.
  • St. Croix River Trail: This scenic trail runs along the eastern banks of the St. Croix River and offers breathtaking views of the river and its surroundings.

Best Things to Do in Afton: Activities and Tours

Aside from hiking, biking, skiing, and golfing, there are plenty of other things to do in Afton. Here are some of the best activities and tours to check out:

  • Hot Air Balloon Rides: Experience the beauty of Afton from high above with a hot air balloon ride. This unforgettable experience provides stunning views of the area and its surroundings.
  • Horseback Riding: Saddle up and explore Afton on horseback. Several local stables offer guided tours through the countryside.
  • Wine Tasting: Afton is home to several local wineries that offer guided tours and wine tastings. This is a perfect way to relax and unwind after a long day of outdoor activities.

Week-Long Itinerary for Afton

For those planning a longer stay in Afton, here’s a week-long itinerary to help you make the most of your visit:

  • Day 1: Explore Afton Historical Museum and Squire House Gardens.
  • Day 2: Hit the slopes at Afton Alps Ski Resort.
  • Day 3: Go hiking in Afton State Park.
  • Day 4: Play a round of golf at Applewood Hills Golf Course.
  • Day 5: Take a hot air balloon ride over Afton.
  • Day 6: Go horseback riding through the countryside.
  • Day 7: Visit a local winery and enjoy a relaxing wine tasting experience.

Food and Drink Scene of Afton

Afton offers a wide variety of dining options, from casual cafes to fine dining restaurants. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to taste:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Walleye fish fry: A classic Minnesota dish that can be found at many local restaurants and cafes.
  • Wild rice soup: A creamy and delicious soup made with locally sourced wild rice.
  • Cheese curds: A Wisconsin staple that can be found at many local bars and restaurants.

Local Beverages

  • Beer from Lift Bridge Brewery: A locally brewed craft beer that has won numerous awards and is enjoyed by locals and visitors alike.
  • Wine from Saint Croix Vineyards: A local winery that produces award-winning wines using locally grown grapes.

Where to Stay in Afton: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Afton offers a variety of accommodation options to suit all budgets and tastes. Here are some of the best places to stay in Afton:

  • Afton House Inn: A beautifully restored inn that offers comfortable rooms and suites, a fine dining restaurant, and stunning views of the St. Croix River.
  • Cottage Guest House: A charming guesthouse that offers cozy rooms, a delicious breakfast, and access to the nearby St. Croix River.
  • Afton Alps Ski Resort Lodging: Ski-in/ski-out accommodation that offers comfortable rooms, a variety of dining options, and easy access to the slopes.

What to Buy in Afton: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to Afton can bring home a wide variety of souvenirs and local products that reflect the area’s unique identity and charm. Some popular souvenirs to look for include:

  • Wild rice: A local specialty that can be found at many local markets and shops.
  • Handcrafted pottery: Many local artists and artisans create beautiful pottery inspired by the natural beauty of the area.
  • Maple Syrup: Afton is surrounded by maple forests, making locally produced maple syrup a popular souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Afton

Afton is known for its strong sense of community and rich cultural heritage. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, and take the time to learn about the area’s history and culture.

Best Time to Visit Afton

Afton’s beauty can be enjoyed year-round, but the best time to visit depends on your interests and preferences. For outdoor enthusiasts, spring, summer, and fall are great times to visit, as the weather is warm and sunny, and the scenery is at its best. Winter is an excellent time to visit for skiing and other winter sports, as well as a chance to enjoy some of the area’s winter festivals and events.

Do’s and Don’ts of Afton

  • Take the time to learn about the local history and culture.
  • Support local businesses and artisans.

Don’t:

  • Litter or engage in any activity that may damage the local environment.
  • Disrespect local customs or traditions.
  • Ignore safety regulations and guidance.

Safety and Health

Safety in Afton

Afton is a relatively safe destination, and visitors should not encounter any major safety concerns during their visit. However, as with any outdoor destination, visitors should be aware of the inherent risks of outdoor activities and take appropriate precautions.

Health in Afton

Afton is a relatively healthy destination, with clean air and water, and generally good access to healthcare services.

Getting to Afton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Afton is located just 25 miles from the Twin Cities metropolitan area, making it easily accessible by car, bus or air.

Airports Near Afton

The nearest major airport is Minneapolis–Saint Paul International Airport, which is located approximately 20 miles from Afton.

Bus Stations Near Afton

Greyhound Lines and Jefferson Lines both offer bus services to the Twin Cities area, with connections to Afton available via local bus or shuttle services.

Other Options

A car or rental car is the most convenient way to get around Afton and the surrounding area.

How to Get Around in Afton

Afton is a small town that can easily be explored on foot or by bike. However, for those who prefer to drive, car rental is also readily available.

Where to Go Next from Afton: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Afton’s location makes it an excellent starting point for exploring other nearby destinations and attractions. Some popular day trips and excursions from Afton include:

  • Stillwater: A charming river town located just a short drive from Afton, Stillwater offers visitors a variety of shops, restaurants, and cultural attractions.
  • The Twin Cities: Just 25 miles away, the Twin Cities metropolitan area offers visitors a wide variety of cultural attractions, dining options, and shopping opportunities.
  • Wisconsin Dells: Located approximately 3 hours from Afton, the Wisconsin Dells is a popular destination for family-friendly activities and attractions, including water parks, go-karts, and mini-golf courses.

Conclusion

Afton offers visitors a unique combination of natural beauty, outdoor recreation, cultural attractions, and small-town charm. It’s an excellent destination for anyone looking to escape into nature or experience a slower and more relaxed pace of life.
