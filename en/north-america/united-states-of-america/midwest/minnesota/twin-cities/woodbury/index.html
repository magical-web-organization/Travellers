Introduction to Woodbury

Woodbury is a city located in the Twin Cities, situated in Minnesota, in the Midwest region of the United States of America. The city is renowned for its rich history and beautiful natural scenery that makes it a perfect destination for travelers seeking an outdoor adventure. Woodbury is home to several natural attractions, outdoor activities, diverse culture, and a welcoming local community that makes it an excellent travel destination.

In this comprehensive tour guide, we will explore the best places to visit, hiking/biking routes, best things to do, where to stay, what to buy, and provide essential tips to ensure you have a memorable trip to Woodbury.

List of Places to Visit in Woodbury

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Woodbury Heritage House and Garden

The Woodbury Heritage House and Garden is a historical landmark that showcases the history of Woodbury. The house has been restored to its original condition and is open to the public for guided tours. Visitors can admire the beautiful gardens surrounding the house and learn about the lifestyle of early settlers in the area.

Indian Mounds Park

Indian Mounds Park is a vast park that contains several prehistoric burial mounds constructed by the Native people in the area. The park features stunning views of the Mississippi River and the downtown skyline of St. Paul. Visitors can enjoy hiking, picnicking, and learning about the rich history of the area.

Natural Attractions of Woodbury: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Carver Lake Park

Carver Lake Park is a beautiful outdoor destination with a pristine lake, hiking and biking trails, and stunning natural views. Visitors can indulge in several water activities like fishing, boating, and swimming, and enjoy the stunning views of the lake’s flora and fauna.

Tamarack Nature Preserve

Tamarack Nature Preserve is a stunning nature preserve that offers visitors a unique opportunity to experience the beauty of the Minnesota wetlands. Visitors can hike through the trails and observe several wildlife species, including deer, muskrats, and raptors, or take photography of the scenic views of the area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Woodbury

Carver Lake Park Trail

Carver Lake Park Trail is a popular hiking and biking trail that loops around Carver Lake Park. The trail is 3.8miles long and offers stunning views of wetlands, woodlands, and the lake. The area is inhabited by several bird species, and visitors can enjoy bird watching while exploring the trail.

Woodbury Lakes Trail

Woodbury Lakes Trail is a 1.8-mile trail located near Woodbury in the Battle Creek Regional Park. The trail traverses through beautiful scenery offering visitors a chance to experience the natural beauty of the area. The trail is ideal for hiking, jogging, and biking, and visitors can enjoy several outdoor activities while exploring the trail.

Best Things to Do in Woodbury: Activities and Tours

Go Shopping at Woodbury Lakes

Woodbury Lakes is a popular shopping destination with several boutiques, department stores, and retail shops. Visitors can explore the area, shop for souvenirs, and enjoy several events organized throughout the year.

Attend Woodbury Days

Woodbury Days is an annual festival held in August that celebrates the city’s heritage, culture, and local community. The festival features several activities like live music performances, food vendors, games, and contests, and a grand parade at the end of the festival.

Week-Long Itinerary for Woodbury

Day 1: Visit Woodbury Heritage House and Garden followed by a walk at Indian Mounds Park.

Day 2: Indulge in water activities at Carver Lake Park and enjoy the views of the lake.

Day 3: Explore the hiking trails at Tamarack Nature Preserve followed by a picnic lunch.

Day 4: Go shopping at the Woodbury Lakes shopping center and explore the local retail shops.

Day 5: Attend a concert or show at the Woodbury Community Theatre.

Day 6: Go on a hiking adventure at the Woodbury Lakes Trail, followed by a visit to a local brew pub.

Day 7: Participate in the Woodbury Days festival, enjoy the rides, food, and live music performances.

Food and Drink Scene of Woodbury

Must-Try Dishes

J. Selby’s

J. Selby’s is a vegan restaurant located in Woodbury that serves delicious plant-based dishes. Visitors can enjoy savory vegan burgers, nachos, soups, and salads, among other vegan delicacies.

Angelina’s Kitchen Italian Restaurant

Angelina’s Kitchen Italian Restaurant is a Woodbury-based restaurant that offers authentic Italian dishes. Visitors can indulge in delicious pasta dishes, salads, seafood, and Italian desserts, all prepared in a beautiful rustic ambiance.

Local Beverages

3rd Act Brewery

3rd Act Brewery is a renowned brewery located in Woodbury that offers delicious craft beers, appetizers, and snacks. Visitors can enjoy the local brews and explore the brewery’s history while indulging in several outdoor activities.

Where to Stay in Woodbury: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Courtyard by Marriott St. Paul Woodbury

Courtyard by Marriott St. Paul Woodbury is a luxurious hotel that offers stunning views of the area and a comfortable stay for its visitors. The hotel is located near several popular destinations and has several amenities like a fitness center, an indoor pool, and a spa.

Sheraton St. Paul Woodbury Hotel

Sheraton St. Paul Woodbury Hotel is a world-class hotel that offers elegant suites, a fitness center, an indoor pool, and several high-end amenities. The hotel is located near several landmarks and offers its visitors a comfortable and luxurious stay.

What to Buy in Woodbury: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

The Woodbury Lakes Mall

The Woodbury Lakes Mall is a popular shopping destination that offers several local and international brands. Visitors can shop for clothes, jewelry, souvenirs, and local crafts, among other products, and bring a piece of Woodbury home with them.

Mitrebox Framing Studio

Mitrebox Framing Studio is a local studio that offers custom-framing services for artwork, photographs, and other items. Visitors can explore the studio, purchase local artwork, or have their art pieces custom-framed before taking them home.

Local Culture and Customs of Woodbury

Woodbury is a family-oriented community that values local culture and custom. Visitors are encouraged to learn and appreciate the rich history and culture of the area and to respect local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Woodbury

The best time to visit Woodbury is during the summer months from July to August when the weather is warm, and several outdoor events and festivals are organized. Travelers may also consider visiting in the fall when the area’s foliage is beautiful and the weather is pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Woodbury

  • Participate in local festivals and events.

Don’t:

Safety and Health

Safety in Woodbury

Woodbury is a safe destination, and visitors are encouraged to observe standard safety measures such as being aware of their surroundings and keeping their valuables secure.

Health in Woodbury

Visitors are advised to carry a sufficient stock of prescription medicine and personal hygiene items and seek medical attention in case of any medical emergencies.

Getting to Woodbury: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Woodbury

The nearest airport to Woodbury is the Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport, located approximately 30 miles from the area.

Bus Stations Near Woodbury

Visitors can access several bus stations near Woodbury, including the Metro Transit in Saint Paul and Greyhound in Minneapolis.

Other Options

Visitors can also opt for car rentals, taxi services, and ridesharing services to access the area.

How to Get Around in Woodbury

Visitors can use several transportation services like taxis, rental cars, and ridesharing services to explore the area.

Where to Go Next from Woodbury: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

The Minnesota State Capitol

The Minnesota State Capitol is located in Saint Paul and is a significant landmark in the area. Visitors can explore the historic building and learn about Minnesota’s history and government.

Lake Minnetonka

Lake Minnetonka is located approximately 25 miles from Woodbury and is a popular destination among locals and tourists. Visitors can indulge in several water activities, including boating, fishing, and swimming, and enjoy the stunning scenery of the area.

Conclusion

Woodbury is a beautiful destination that offers visitors a chance to explore its rich history, natural beauty, and local culture. The area’s natural attractions, outdoor activities, and welcoming local community make it an excellent travel destination that must be explored.
