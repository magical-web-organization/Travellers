Introduction to Richfield

Nested in Twin Cities, Minnesota, Richfield is a charming Minneapolis suburb that offers plenty of attractions, breathtaking scenery, and cultural experiences. Richfield is home to several nature parks, shopping centers, and a unique and vibrant cityscape.

If you’re planning to visit Minnesota, be sure to include a trip to Richfield in your itinerary. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take a look at all the exciting things to see and do in this lovely city.

List of Places to Visit in Richfield

Here’s a list of the must-visit places in Richfield, Minnesota, that will keep you entertained throughout your stay here.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Richfield Historical Society Museums: Learn about the rich history of Richfield through exhibits at Richfield Historical Society Museums.
  • Minnesota Valley National Wildlife Refuge: This beautiful wildlife refuge boasts over 14,000 acres of rivers, swamps, and forests, making it the perfect spot for a walk, hike, or birdwatching.

Natural Attractions of Richfield: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Wood Lake Nature Center: This nature center offers visitors the chance to explore and enjoy nature on over 150 acres of woods, marshes, and sparkling lakes. Visitors can also attend workshops and educational programs.
  • Veterans Park: A picturesque place with a stunning lake, this park is a perfect spot to relax, have a picnic, or spend an afternoon fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Richfield

  • Veterans Memorial Park Trail: A 2.3-mile-long asphalt trail, this bike path is suitable for all levels of mountain bikers.
  • Woodlake Nature Center Trail: A two-mile-long walking trail through wetlands and woodlands, this nature trail is a perfect place to go for a peaceful hike.

Best Things to Do in Richfield: Activities and Tours

  • Mall of America: This massive mall has over 520 stores and attractions, including a theme park, miniature golf, and an aquarium.
  • Pinstripes: This unique venue boasts a bowling alley, bocce ball, and an Italian-American restaurant.

Here is a week-long itinerary for Richfield that will help you make the most out of your stay.

Food and Drink Scene of Richfield

Must-Try Dishes

  • Juicy Lucy: This cheeseburger dish is a must-try when you’re in Minnesota, and Matt’s Bar is one of the best places to enjoy it.
  • Tater Tots: These crispy and delicious tater tots are perfect to enjoy as a side dish when you’re eating at a local restaurant.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beer: Minnesota is home to many breweries, and visitors should take advantage of the opportunity to sip on a locally made beer.
  • Fruit Wines: Richfield offers visitors a taste of the famed cold-hardy fruit wines made from plums, pears, and other fruits.

Where to Stay in Richfield: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

From luxurious hotels to budget-friendly guesthouses, Richfield has it all when it comes to accommodations. Here are a few options to suit your travel needs:

  • Home2 Suites by Hilton Bloomington: This recently opened hotel offers spacious and comfortable rooms that come with modern amenities and free breakfast.
  • Richfield Hospitality House: This is a homey option that offers reasonable rates and home-cooked breakfasts.

What to Buy in Richfield: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re wondering what souvenirs or gifts you can bring back home, check out these local products:

  • Spoon and Cherry Memorabilia: The spoon and cherry sculpture by Claes Oldenburg is a Richfield landmark, and you can find many souvenirs inspired by this statue.
  • Cold Climate Grape Wines: Minnesota is known for its cold-hardy grapes so why not bring back a bottle of the local wine.

Local Culture and Customs of Richfield

Richfield is home to a diverse community with unique customs and traditions. Visitors can experience local cultural festivals and events such as the Richfield Farmers Market.

Best Time to Visit Richfield

The best time to visit Richfield is during summer (June to August) as the weather is pleasant with temperatures averaging around 80 degrees Fahrenheit.

Do’s and Don’ts of Richfield

Do:

  • Pay for parking: Always make sure to pay for your parking to avoid getting a ticket.
  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions: When traveling to a new place, it’s important to be respectful of their culture and customs.

Don’t:

  • Litter: Avoid littering and ensure that you dispose of your trash properly
  • Carry large amounts of cash: Avoid carrying large amounts of cash, and instead opt for credit or debit cards.

Safety and Health

Safety in Richfield

Richfield is a generally safe city, but visitors should still take safety precautions and be aware of their surroundings.

Health in Richfield

It’s important to take care of your health when traveling to a new destination. Visitors should drink plenty of water and carry all essential medications.

Getting to Richfield: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Richfield

  • Minneapolis–Saint Paul International Airport A major airport about 15 minutes from Richfield.

Bus Stations Near Richfield

  • Richfield Bus Station A top travel hub for visitors who arrive via bus.

Other Options

  • Amtrak: The nearest Amtrak station is in Saint Paul, which is just a 20-minute drive from Richfield.

How to Get Around in Richfield

Richfield has an excellent public transportation system with buses regularly plying the city’s routes. Visitors can also opt for taxis, ride-shares, or rental cars to move around the city.

Where to Go Next from Richfield: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations that visitors can explore after their visit to Richfield:

  • Minneapolis: A vibrant downtown area, great restaurants, and exciting nightlife make Minneapolis a must-visit place for visitors.
  • Saint Paul: The capital city of Minnesota offers visitors art galleries, museums, and historic architecture that they can explore.

Conclusion

Richfield is a hidden Minnesota gem that’s worth exploring. The city offers unforgettable cultural and nature experiences, exciting activities, and delicious cuisine. Plan your trip now and dive into the best of Richfield.
