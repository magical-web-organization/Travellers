Introduction to Miesville

Miesville is a charming community located in Dakota County in Twin Cities, Minnesota. Despite its small size, Miesville is a popular tourist destination thanks to its rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and exciting outdoor activities. The town is home to just under 150 residents, giving it a cozy atmosphere that’s perfect for those who are looking for a relaxing vacation.

List of Places to Visit in Miesville

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re interested in learning about the history and culture of Miesville, there are several museums and historical places that you should visit:

  • Miesville Historic District - This district is on the National Register of Historic Places and is home to several 19th-century buildings, including the Miesville Church, which was built in 1870.

  • Cannon River STEM School & Minnesota Academy - This school is a unique educational institute that focuses on science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. The academy is also home to a museum that features artifacts relating to the history of Minnesota.

  • Dakota County Historical Society - Located in nearby Hastings, this museum is dedicated to preserving the history of Dakota County and is home to a collection of over 25,000 items.

Natural Attractions of Miesville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Miesville is known for its beautiful natural landscapes and outdoor activities. Here are some of the top places to visit:

  • Cannon River Wilderness Area - This beautiful area is perfect for hiking, fishing, and camping. The forest is home to a variety of wildlife, including deer, raccoons, and foxes.

  • Spring Lake Park Reserve - This 1,500-acre park is located just a few miles away from Miesville and has several trails for hiking and biking, as well as fishing and swimming areas.

  • Nerstrand-Big Woods State Park - Just a short drive from Miesville, this state park is home to one of the last remaining stands of the big woods that once covered much of southern Minnesota. Visitors can hike through the woods and along the Hidden Falls Trail to discover a 20-foot waterfall.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Miesville

If you’re looking for the best routes for hiking and biking in Miesville, these are some popular choices:

  • Cannon Valley Trail - This 19.7-mile trail runs from Cannon Falls to Red Wing and passes through Miesville. The trail is open to walkers, runners, and bikers, and offers scenic views of the Cannon River Valley.

  • Spring Lake Park Reserve Trails - This park has over 10 miles of trails for hiking and biking, as well as several scenic overlooks.

  • Nerstrand-Big Woods State Park Trails - This park has several trails for hiking and biking, including the Hidden Falls Trail, which is 2.6 miles long and takes visitors to the beautiful Hidden Falls.

Best Things to Do in Miesville: Activities and Tours

Miesville has plenty of activities to keep visitors entertained. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Visit the Miesville Historic District - This historic district is home to several 19th-century buildings, including the Miesville Church, which is the oldest continuously used Catholic church in Minnesota.

  • Go Fishing - Miesville is home to several lakes and rivers that offer excellent fishing opportunities.

  • Tour the Cannon River STEM School & Minnesota Academy Museum - This museum is home to artifacts relating to the history of Minnesota, including Native American artifacts, settlement artifacts, and a collection of 19th-century farming tools.

Week-Long Itinerary for Miesville

If you’re planning to spend a week in Miesville, here’s a suggested itinerary:

  • Day 1: Visit the Miesville Historic District and the Cannon River STEM School & Minnesota Academy Museum.
  • Day 2: Explore the Cannon River Wilderness Area.
  • Day 3: Go biking on the Cannon Valley Trail.
  • Day 4: Visit Spring Lake Park Reserve.
  • Day 5: Tour Nerstrand-Big Woods State Park.
  • Day 6: Relax and enjoy the local food and drink scene.
  • Day 7: Go fishing in one of Miesville’s nearby lakes or rivers.

Food and Drink Scene of Miesville

Must-Try Dishes

If you’re a foodie, Miesville won’t disappoint. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • Juicy Lucy - This hamburger is stuffed with cheese and is a popular local dish.

  • Walleye - This fish is a local favorite and is often served fried.

  • Lefse - A traditional Norwegian flatbread that is often served with butter and brown sugar.

Local Beverages

Miesville is home to several microbreweries and wineries. Here are some local beverages to try:

  • Miesville Mud - A locally brewed beer that’s named after the town.

  • Cannon River Winery - This winery produces several award-winning wines, including a red blend called the Sogn Blanc.

  • Falconer Vineyards - This winery produces a variety of wines, including a popular red blend called the Frontenac.

Where to Go Next from Miesville: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
