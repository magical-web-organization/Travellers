A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Grand Marais, Minnesota

Grand Marais is a charming harbor town located in North Shore, Northeastern Minnesota, with a rich cultural heritage and a stunning natural landscape that offers outdoor adventure activities and scenic views.

Discover all the best places to visit, where to eat and drink, where to stay, and what to do while visiting Grand Marais in this comprehensive travel guide that covers everything from cultural attractions to outdoor adventures.

Introduction to Grand Marais

Grand Marais is a small harbor town located on the shores of Lake Superior, known for its unique charm, rich cultural history, and beautiful natural attractions. It is a perfect destination for all kinds of travelers, whether you want to explore the great outdoors or learn about the local culture.

Week-Long Itinerary for Grand Marais

If you plan on spending a week in Grand Marais, here’s a perfect itinerary:

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit Artist’s Point, Cook County Historical Society Museum, and enjoy a sunset at Gunflint Trail
Day 2 Hike the Superior Hiking Trail during the day and go on a Grand Marais Harbor Tour in the evening
Day 3 Drive to Judge C.R. Magney State Park, hike the Devil’s Kettle Trail, and have a picnic on Lake Superior
Day 4 Go on a kayaking tour at Lake Superior
Day 5 Visit Cascade River State Park and North Shore Winery
Day 6 Take a scenic drive to Grand Portage and explore its beautiful wilderness
Day 7 Relax, unwind, and enjoy the sights and sounds of Grand Marais

Best Time to Visit Grand Marais

The best time to visit Grand Marais is during the summer and fall season, from June to October. In these months, the weather is perfect for outdoor activities, and the foliage in the forests is breathtakingly beautiful.

Safety and Health

Safety in Grand Marais

Grand Marais is generally a safe town to visit. However, you should always practice standard safety measures, such as:

  • Carry a Map
  • Keep Your Valuables Safe
  • Beware of Wildlife

Health in Grand Marais

Grand Marais has a health center that provides primary care services. For serious health issues, it is best to visit a hospital located in the nearby town of Duluth.
