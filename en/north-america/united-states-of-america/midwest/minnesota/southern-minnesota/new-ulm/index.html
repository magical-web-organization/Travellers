New Ulm, located in southern Minnesota, is a hidden gem in the Midwest. With its rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty, New Ulm offers a unique travel experience that’s perfect for all types of travelers. In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ll take you through the must-visit places, best hiking and biking routes, and top things to do in New Ulm. We’ll also provide you with useful tips and insights to make your trip hassle-free and enjoyable.

Introduction to New Ulm

New Ulm is a charming city located in southern Minnesota, home to rich German heritage and stunning natural beauty. It boasts a variety of cultural and historical attractions, including museums, festivals, and iconic landmarks. It’s also an outdoor lover’s paradise, with scenic bike trails and hiking routes in every corner of the city.

List of Places to Visit in New Ulm

Here’s our list of must-visit places in New Ulm:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Schell’s Brewery - Founded in 1860, Schell’s Brewery is a historical landmark in New Ulm and one of the oldest family-owned breweries in the United States.
  • Hermann Monument - Standing tall at 102 feet, Hermann Monument is a symbol of German heritage and one of the city’s most iconic landmarks.
  • Brown County Historical Society Museum - This museum showcases the fascinating history of Brown County, featuring exhibits on Native American culture, pioneer life, and the impact of World War II on the region.
  • Glockenspiel Clock Tower - This colorful clock tower chimes three times a day and features figures that reenact the story of Hermann the German.

Natural Attractions of New Ulm: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Flandrau State Park - This beautiful park boasts hiking trails, fishing spots, and a swimming pond for visitors to explore.
  • Harkin Store - Located near the Minnesota River, this historic general store has been preserved in its original state and offers a glimpse into life in the 1800s.
  • Morgan Creek Vineyards - Visitors can enjoy the serene beauty of the vineyards while sipping on award-winning wines and indulging in delicious food.
  • Minneopa State Park - This park features stunning waterfalls, hiking trails, and a bison herd that calls it home.

Hiking/Biking Routes of New Ulm

New Ulm is a city that caters to outdoor enthusiasts, with a variety of hiking and biking trails to suit every skill level. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Minnesota River Trail - This challenging yet rewarding 17-mile trail runs alongside the picturesque Minnesota River, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Flandrau State Park Trails - With over 10 miles of trails winding through the park, visitors can choose from a variety of routes that offer scenic views of the forest, river, and prairie.
  • Riverside Park Trail - A popular choice for families or visitors looking for a short, easy walk, this 1.5-mile trail follows the winding banks of the Cottonwood River.
  • Kiesling Park Trail - This 4-mile trail takes hikers and bikers through dense forests and stunning prairie lands, offering ample opportunities for bird watching and wildlife sightings.

Best Things to Do in New Ulm: Activities and Tours

New Ulm has something for everyone, from art lovers to foodies. Here are some of the top things to do in New Ulm:

  • Take a brewery tour - Indulge in a brewery tour of Schell’s Brewery or August Schell Brewing Company and learn about the rich history of beer-making in New Ulm.
  • Explore the local art scene - Visit the Grand Center for Arts and Culture or the Portrait Museum to see works by local artists.
  • Take a wine tour - Explore the vineyards of Morgan Creek Vineyards or Minnesota Wine Company and sample award-winning wines while taking in the scenic beauty of the region.
  • Attend a festival - From Oktoberfest to Bavarian Blast, New Ulm has a variety of festivals throughout the year that celebrate the city’s German heritage.
  • Visit the local farmers market - Every Tuesday and Saturday, the New Ulm Farmers Market offers fresh produce, handmade crafts, and delicious food from local vendors.

Week-Long Itinerary for New Ulm

Here’s our recommended week-long itinerary for New Ulm:

Day 1: Arrive in New Ulm and check into your hotel. Day 2: Explore the historical landmarks, including Hermann Monument and Brown County Historical Society Museum. Day 3: Take a brewery tour and indulge in a beer tasting. Day 4: Hike or bike your way through Flandrau State Park. Day 5: Visit the wineries and vineyards of the region. Day 6: Attend a festival or explore the local art scene. Day 7: Spend a day relaxing at a local spa or indulging in some retail therapy at the local shops.

Food and Drink Scene of New Ulm

New Ulm has a diverse food and drink scene that caters to every taste bud. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Schweinshaxe - A classic German dish consisting of a large pork knuckle that’s been roasted until tender and crispy.
  • Riesenpretzel - A giant, soft warm pretzel that’s served with a variety of dipping sauces including beer cheese and honey mustard.
  • German potato salad - A tangy and savory potato salad that’s traditionally served warm.
  • Hotdish - A classic Midwestern casserole that combines ground beef, vegetables, and tater tots.

Local Beverages

  • Schell’s Beer - A range of award-winning beers with different styles and flavors.
  • Morgan Creek Wines - Locally produced wines that boast amazing flavors and aromas.

Where to Stay in New Ulm: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

New Ulm has a variety of accommodation options for every budget and preference. Here are our top picks:

  • The Bohemian Inn - A beautifully restored 1920s-era mansion that’s been converted into a quaint guesthouse.
  • Best Western Plus - A popular hotel chain that provides comfortable rooms and modern amenities.
  • Microtel Inn & Suites by Wyndham New Ulm - An eco-friendly hotel that offers spacious rooms and free breakfast.

What to Buy in New Ulm: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Looking for a unique souvenir to bring back home? Here are some must-buy items in New Ulm:

  • Schell’s Beer - Take home a six-pack (or two) of Schell’s award-winning beers.
  • German Christmas Ornaments - A variety of beautifully crafted ornaments and decorations to add to your Christmas tree.
  • Ceramic Steins and Mugs - Pick up a traditional German stein or mug that celebrates the city’s heritage.

Local Culture and Customs of New Ulm

New Ulm is known for its close-knit community, rich heritage, and friendly locals. Visitors should be aware of the city’s customs, which include drinks at the local pubs and a love for all things German.

Best Time to Visit New Ulm

The best time to visit New Ulm is during the summer months (June to August), where you can enjoy the outdoor activities and festivals in beautiful weather.

Do’s and Don’ts of New Ulm

Do:

  • Indulge in German cuisine - New Ulm’s food scene is heavily influenced by German cuisine, so make sure you try some of the local dishes.
  • Attend a festival - New Ulm’s festivals are full of energy and a great way to experience the local culture.
  • Explore the outdoors - New Ulm’s scenic beauty is best explored through hiking, biking, and other outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Be disrespectful of German heritage - New Ulm takes pride in its German heritage, so it’s important to be respectful of local customs and practices.
  • Traverse unknown areas alone at night - It’s important to stay safe while exploring the city, so make sure you take necessary precautions while out at night.

Safety and Health

Safety in New Ulm

New Ulm is a relatively safe city, but it’s important to take necessary precautions to ensure a safe trip. It’s recommended to avoid walking alone at night and to keep valuables out of sight.

Health in New Ulm

New Ulm has several medical facilities and hospitals that provide adequate healthcare. However, it’s important to take necessary precautions when out hiking or exploring the outdoors, including wearing sunscreen and drinking plenty of water.

Getting to New Ulm: Transportation Options and Logistics

New Ulm is easily accessible by both air and land transportation. Here are some of the best options:

Airports Near New Ulm

  • Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport - Located 90 miles northeast of New Ulm, this airport is the closest major airport to the city.
  • Mankato Regional Airport - Just 15 miles from New Ulm, this smaller airport serves regional airlines.

Bus Stations Near New Ulm

  • Greyhound - Greyhound buses stop at the local bus station in Mankato, just 15 miles from New Ulm.
  • Jefferson Lines - Jefferson Lines buses stop at several locations in Minnesota, including Mankato and Minneapolis.

Other Options

  • Car Rentals - Several car rental companies have offices in Minneapolis and Mankato, providing visitors with the option to drive to New Ulm.
  • Taxis and Ride-Sharing Services - Several taxi and ride-sharing companies provide services in the region.

How to Get Around in New Ulm

New Ulm is a relatively small city, so it’s easy to get around on foot or by bike. Rental cars and taxi services are also available for visitors who want to explore further afield.

Where to Go Next from New Ulm: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

When you’re ready to explore further afield, check out these nearby destinations:

  • Mankato - Just 15 miles north of New Ulm, Mankato is a small college town known for its lively downtown area and scenic beauty.
  • Minneapolis - Located about 90 miles northeast, Minneapolis is a must-visit destination for its vibrant food and arts scene, along with stunning architecture.
  • The Twin Cities - Visitors can also explore both Minneapolis and St. Paul in the Twin Cities, experiencing everything from live theatre shows to internationally themed cuisine.

Conclusion

New Ulm is a hidden gem in the Midwest, offering a unique blend of cultural and natural attractions that cater to all types of travelers. Whether you’re interested in beer, food, or outdoor adventures, New Ulm has something for you. We hope this comprehensive tour guide helps you plan your perfect trip to this charming city. Enjoy your travels!
