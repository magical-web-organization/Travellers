Introduction to Flandrau State Park

Flandrau State Park is a hidden gem located in Southern Minnesota. Spanning over 1,100 acres, the park is named after Charles Eugene Flandrau, a prominent figure in the state’s history. The park offers visitors a wide variety of outdoor activities, from hiking and fishing to camping and picnicking. Additionally, it is home to culturally important attractions, making it a must-visit destination for history buffs and nature lovers alike.

List of Places to Visit in Flandrau State Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Flandrau State Park is rich in history and culture, with many museums and historical sites to explore. These include:

  • Flandrau House State Historic Site - A beautifully preserved mansion built in the early 1900s, offering a glimpse into the lifestyles of early Minnesota aristocrats.
  • August Schell Brewing Company - Established in 1860, the brewery is one of the oldest in the state and offers tours and tastings year-round.
  • Brown County Historical Society - Dedicated to preserving the history of Brown County, the society’s museum features exhibits on the county’s early settlers, indigenous people, and agricultural heritage.

Natural Attractions of Flandrau State Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Flandrau State Park offers visitors a breathtaking landscape, complete with rivers, lakes, and lush forests. Some of the park’s natural attractions include:

  • Cottonwood River - Flowing through the park, the river is a popular spot for fishing and boating.
  • Flandrau Lake - This scenic lake is perfect for swimming, boating, and fishing.
  • Hermann Heights Monument - A towering statue located on a hill overlooking the city. Its observation deck provides panoramic views of the surrounding area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Flandrau State Park

Flandrau State Park is a hiker’s paradise, with trails of varying lengths and difficulty levels.

  • Flandrau Trail - A 2.5-mile loop trail that takes hikers through the heart of the park, showcasing some of its best views and natural features.
  • Minnesota River Bottoms Trail - A 9-mile trail that follows the banks of the Minnesota River through the park.
  • Meadow Trail - A 1.5-mile trail that winds through the park’s lush meadows, offering a peaceful and serene hiking experience.

Best Things to Do in Flandrau State Park: Activities and Tours

Flandrau State Park has endless activities and tours for visitors to enjoy, including:

  • Camping - The park has many campsites available, offering a true outdoor experience.
  • Picnicking - With several picnic areas throughout the park, visitors can enjoy a relaxing afternoon surrounded by stunning scenery.
  • Nature Programs - From guided nature walks to bird watching programs, the park offers a variety of educational opportunities for visitors of all ages.
