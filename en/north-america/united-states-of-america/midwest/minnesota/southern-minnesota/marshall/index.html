Introduction to Marshall

The city of Marshall is located in Southwest Minnesota, about 210 miles southwest of Minneapolis. It is a regional center of culture, education, and commerce with a population of approximately 14,000 people. Known for its vibrant outdoor recreation scene, urban amenities, arts and culture, and rich history, Marshall is a dynamic destination that offers something for everyone.

Local Culture and Customs of Marshall

Marshall is a friendly, welcoming city that values family, education, and community. Visitors are encouraged to participate in local events and traditions, such as the Lyon County Fair and the “Sounds of Summer” concert series. Respect for local customs and traditions is appreciated.

Best Time to Visit Marshall

The best time to visit Marshall is during summer and fall when the weather is mild and sunny. The city bustles with outdoor activities and cultural events. If you prefer fewer crowds, visit during the winter months, but make sure to pack warm clothing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Marshall

Do:

  • Visit the Cottonwood Lake Recreation Area.
  • Participate in local events and traditions.
  • Respect local customs and culture.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural areas.
  • Ignore traffic and parking laws.
  • Disrespect the locals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Marshall

Marshall is a safe and friendly city with low crime rates. Avoid walking alone at night and be mindful of your surroundings. Emergency services are available at 911.

Health in Marshall

Be aware of ticks and mosquitos when spending time outdoors. Use insect repellents and wear protective clothing. Medical facilities are available at Avera Marshall Regional Medical Center.

Getting to Marshall: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Marshall

The nearest major airport to Marshall is Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport (MSP). Regional airports include Southwest Minnesota Regional Airport (MML) and Marshall Municipal Airport (MML).

Bus Stations Near Marshall

Marshall has a Greyhound bus station located at 1110 E College Dr.

Other Options

Taxis, rental cars, and ride-sharing services such as Uber and Lyft are available in Marshall.

How to Get Around in Marshall

Walking, biking, and driving are convenient ways to get around Marshall. Rental cars and taxis are also available.
