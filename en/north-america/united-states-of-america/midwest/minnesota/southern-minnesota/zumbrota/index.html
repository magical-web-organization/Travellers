Introduction to Zumbrota

Located in the heart of Southern Minnesota, Zumbrota is a small but lively town bursting with natural and cultural landmarks. Whether you’re looking to hike through beautiful landscapes, learn about local history, or simply indulge in delicious local cuisine, Zumbrota has something for everyone. This travel guide provides a comprehensive overview of the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay in Zumbrota.

List of Places to Visit in Zumbrota

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re interested in learning about local history, be sure to check out these culturally important landmarks:

  • The Covered Bridge: built in 1869, this iconic bridge is Zumbrota’s only remaining covered bridge.

  • Zumbrota Area Historical Society Museum: explore exhibits of Zumbrota’s rich history, from early settlers to important industries.

  • Zumbrota Carnegie Library: this beautiful, historic library was recently renovated and is open to visitors.

  • Stary-Yerka Museum: home to paintings by local artists and a collection of vintage clothing and memorabilia.

Natural Attractions of Zumbrota: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For those who love nature, Zumbrota offers a wide variety of outdoor activities and beautiful landscapes:

  • Zumbrota Covered Bridge Park: a scenic park located on the Zumbro River, featuring picnic areas, canoeing, and hiking trails.

  • Zumbrota-Mazeppa High School Trails: a network of hiking and biking trails that wind through beautiful forests and prairies.

  • Willow Creek Reservoir: a popular spot for fishing and boating, with stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

  • Minnesota Equestrian Center: horse lovers will enjoy this sprawling facility, with lessons, boarding, and competitive events.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Zumbrota

Zumbrota is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, with miles of scenic hiking and biking routes. Our top picks include:

  • Goodhue Pioneer State Trail: a 12.5-mile trail that stretches through wooded areas and wetlands, perfect for bird watching and nature lovers.

  • Cannon Valley Trail: a 20-mile trail connecting Cannon Falls and Red Wing, boasting stunning vistas of the Cannon River Valley.

  • Pine Island Trailhead: a 10-mile trail that starts in Pine Island and winds through forests and rolling hills.

Best Things to Do in Zumbrota: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re traveling solo or with family and friends, Zumbrota offers plenty of exciting things to do and see:

  • Zumbrota Farmers Market: held on Saturday mornings May through October, this lively market features fresh produce, homemade crafts, and live music.

  • Zumbrota Covered Bridge Music & Arts Festival: annual event held on the third weekend in June, which features a parade, live music, and art exhibits.

  • Scenic Valley Winery: sample local wines and enjoy beautiful views of the surrounding countryside.

  • Trampled By Turtles Amphitheater: a popular outdoor venue featuring live music and performances.

Week-Long Itinerary for Zumbrota

To help you plan your trip, here’s a sample itinerary for a week in Zumbrota:

Day 1: Arrive in Zumbrota, explore the town and visit the Covered Bridge

Day 2: Hike or bike the Cannon Valley Trail

Day 3: Visit the Zumbrota Area Historical Society Museum and have lunch at a local restaurant

Day 4: Canoe the Zumbro River or go fishing at Willow Creek Reservoir

Day 5: Take a tour of the Minnesota Equestrian Center

Day 6: Visit the Stary-Yerka Museum and have dinner at Scenic Valley Winery

Day 7: Attend the Zumbrota Farmers Market and head back home

Food and Drink Scene of Zumbrota

Must-Try Dishes

Zumbrota’s food scene is not to be missed, with a variety of local specialties to try:

  • Wild Rice Soup: a creamy, savory soup made with wild rice and chicken

  • Hotdish: a hearty casserole made with ground beef, vegetables, and tater tots

  • Juicy Lucy: a burger stuffed with cheese, typically served with fries and onion rings

  • Lefse: a traditional Norwegian flatbread made with potatoes and often served with butter and cinnamon sugar

Local Beverages

Don’t forget to try these local beverages during your stay in Zumbrota:

  • Surly Furious IPA: a hoppy, flavorful beer brewed in Minnesota

  • Four Daughters Vineyard & Winery: an award-winning winery with a variety of wines to try

  • Elevate Espresso: a locally-owned coffee shop with a wide variety of beverages and homemade pastries

Where to Stay in Zumbrota: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Zumbrota offers a variety of options for accommodations:

  • AmericInn by Wyndham: a conveniently located hotel with an indoor pool and free breakfast

  • Zumbrota Campground: a scenic campground for RVs and tents, with amenities including showers, laundry, and playground.

  • Crossings by GrandStay Inn & Suites: a charming bed and breakfast featuring a full breakfast and elegant guest rooms.

What to Buy in Zumbrota: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don’t forget to take home a little piece of Zumbrota with you! Here are some of our favorite souvenirs:

  • Covered Bridge T-shirt: a classic souvenir for any visitor of Zumbrota

  • Local honey: produced by nearby beekeepers, this honey is a delicious and eco-friendly option.

  • Handcrafted pottery: made by local artists and sold at the Zumbrota Farmers Market

Local Culture and Customs of Zumbrota

Zumbrota prides itself on its community spirit and small-town charm. Visitors should be prepared to take things slow and savor the natural beauty and local culture.

Best Time to Visit Zumbrota

Spring and summer are the perfect times to visit Zumbrota, with pleasant temperatures and plenty of outdoor activities and events to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Zumbrota

Do:

  • Take a hike or bike ride through the Goodhue Pioneer State Trail

  • Sample local cuisine and beverages at Scenic Valley Winery and Elevate Espresso

  • Visit Zumbrota Area Historical Society Museum to learn about the town’s rich history

Don’t:

  • Feed wildlife, as it is harmful to both the animals and the ecosystem

  • Litter or leave trash behind when exploring the natural areas of Zumbrota

  • Be disrespectful when visiting historic and cultural landmarks

Safety and Health

Safety in Zumbrota

Zumbrota is a generally safe town, and visitors can easily explore without any concerns for crime or violence. However, visitors should be aware of their surroundings at night and take necessary precautions.

Health in Zumbrota

Zumbrota offers a variety of medical services, including a hospital and several clinics.

Getting to Zumbrota: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Zumbrota

  • Rochester International Airport: located 40 miles from Zumbrota, the Rochester International Airport offers many flight options.

  • Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport: located 85 miles from Zumbrota, the Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport is the largest airport in the region.

Bus Stations Near Zumbrota

  • Rochester Greyhound Station: located 40 miles from Zumbrota, the Greyhound Station in Rochester offers bus transportation from many destinations.

Other Options

Zumbrota can also be reached by car or bike, just off of Highway 52.

How to Get Around in Zumbrota

Visitors can easily explore Zumbrota by car, bike, or foot. Biking and hiking trails are well-maintained, and roads are generally safe for drivers.

Where to Go Next from Zumbrota: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Zumbrota is conveniently located near several other interesting destinations:

  • Red Wing, MN: a charming town located 30 miles from Zumbrota, known for its historic district and scenic bluffs.

  • Rochester, MN: just a 40-minute drive from Zumbrota, Rochester offers a variety of medical facilities, shopping, and dining.

  • Minneapolis-St. Paul, MN: the vibrant cities of Minneapolis and St. Paul are both located within a two-hour drive of Zumbrota, each with plenty of cultural landmarks, events, and activities.

Conclusion

Zumbrota offers a unique blend of natural beauty, rich history, and delicious cuisine. Whether you’re looking for a peaceful getaway or an exciting adventure, this charming town is the perfect destination for your next trip.
