Introduction to Worthington

Worthington is a small city located in the southwestern portion of Minnesota. It has a population of just over 12,000 people and is known for its diverse culture and beautiful natural attractions. From hiking and biking trails to unique museums and historical sites, Worthington offers something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Worthington

If you are planning a trip to Worthington, these are the top places you must visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Nobles County Historical Society: Learn about the history and culture of Worthington and its surrounding areas in this fascinating museum.
  • Pioneer Village: A restored village featuring 24 original buildings from the 1800s, including a schoolhouse, blacksmith shop, and general store.
  • Historic Dayton House: A beautifully restored house that was once owned by one of Worthington’s prominent citizens.
  • Spomer Classics and Museum: A unique museum featuring classic cars and farm machinery.

Natural Attractions of Worthington: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Okabena: A beautiful lake that offers a variety of outdoor activities, including boating and fishing.
  • Prairie View Park: A serene park that offers hiking and walking trails, as well as a playground and picnic area.
  • Monument Park: A small but beautiful park that is home to several monuments and memorials, including a Civil War monument and a World War II memorial.
  • Lake Bella: A secluded lake that is perfect for fishing and birdwatching.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Worthington

If you enjoy hiking or biking, Worthington has some great options for you. Check out these routes:

  • Okabena-Ocheda Trail: A 22-mile trail that takes you around Lake Okabena and through the beautiful prairies surrounding Worthington.
  • Chautauqua Trail: A 7.5-mile trail that takes you through the hills and forests of Chautauqua Park.
  • Lake Bella Trail: A 3-mile trail that takes you around the serene Lake Bella.

Best Things to Do in Worthington: Activities and Tours

Worthington offers plenty of activities and tours for visitors, including:

  • Prairie Ecology Bus Center: An interactive center that teaches visitors about local ecology and environmental issues.
  • Saturday Farmers Market: A weekly market where you can purchase fresh produce, crafts, and baked goods from local vendors.
  • Worthington Golf Course: A challenging and beautiful 18-hole course that is perfect for golfers of all levels.
  • Night Sky Tours: Guided tours that take you under the stars for an evening you’ll never forget.

Week-Long Itinerary for Worthington

If you have a week to spend in Worthington, here is a suggested itinerary:

Day 1: Spend the day exploring the Nobles County Historical Society and Pioneer Village.

Day 2: Take a hike along the Okabena-Ocheda Trail and spend some time fishing on Lake Okabena.

Day 3: Spend the day at Prairie View Park, enjoying the beautiful scenery and hiking trails.

Day 4: Visit the Spomer Classics and Museum and take a tour of the Historic Dayton House.

Day 5: Spend the day playing golf at Worthington Golf Course.

Day 6: Take a guided Night Sky Tour and learn about the stars and constellations.

Day 7: Spend the day at Lake Bella, fishing and birdwatching.

Food and Drink Scene of Worthington

Worthington has a great food and drink scene, with plenty of delicious options to choose from. Here are a few must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Walleye: A local favorite, served breaded or fried.
  • Jucy Lucy: A cheese-stuffed burger that is a popular dish in Minnesota.
  • Hotdish: A casserole-style dish that is a Minnesota tradition.

Local Beverages

  • Schell’s Beer: A popular brewery located just a short drive from Worthington.
  • Bundt’s Apple Cider: A delicious local cider that is perfect for autumn.

Where to Stay in Worthington: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Worthington offers a variety of accommodations, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are a few options:

  • Holiday Inn Express & Suites Worthington: A modern and comfortable hotel located in the heart of downtown Worthington.
  • AmericInn by Wyndham: A budget-friendly option that offers comfortable rooms and a relaxing hot tub.
  • The Worthington Guest House: A charming and cozy guesthouse located just a short drive from downtown Worthington.

What to Buy in Worthington: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you are looking for souvenirs or local products to bring home, Worthington has plenty of options. Here are a few ideas:

  • Cornhusk dolls: A unique and traditional gift that is handmade by local artisans.
  • Wild rice: A delicious and nutritious grain that is harvested in Minnesota.
  • Schell’s Beer: Any beer lover will appreciate a six-pack of Schell’s beer, brewed just a short drive from Worthington.

Local Culture and Customs of Worthington

Worthington is known for its diversity, with a large population of immigrants from around the world. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions and take the time to learn about the rich cultural heritage of the community.

Best Time to Visit Worthington

The best time to visit Worthington depends on your interests. If you enjoy outdoor activities, summer is the best time to visit, when the weather is warm and sunny. If you prefer a quieter and more relaxed experience, consider visiting in the fall or winter, when the leaves are changing and the snow has transformed Worthington into a winter wonderland.

Do’s and Don’ts of Worthington

Do:

  • Do take the time to explore the natural beauty of Worthington, including its parks and hiking trails.
  • Do respect local customs and traditions, and take the time to learn about the diverse cultures of the community.
  • Do try the local cuisine, including Minnesota classics like walleye and hotdish.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural beauty of Worthington’s parks and outdoor spaces.
  • Don’t be disrespectful of local customs or traditions.
  • Don’t miss out on the opportunity to learn something new about the community and its rich cultural history.

Safety and Health

Safety in Worthington

Worthington is a relatively safe community, with low rates of violent crime. Visitors should be aware of their surroundings and take common-sense precautions to stay safe.

Health in Worthington

Worthington has a high quality of health care, with several hospitals and medical centers located in the area. Visitors should make sure they have adequate health insurance coverage before traveling.

Getting to Worthington: Transportation Options and Logistics

Worthington is located in southwestern Minnesota and is easily accessible by car, bus or plane.

Airports Near Worthington

  • Sioux Falls Regional Airport: The closest airport to Worthington, located about an hour’s drive away in Sioux Falls, South Dakota.

Bus Stations Near Worthington

  • Jefferson Lines: Offers daily bus service to and from Worthington.
  • Greyhound: Offers several bus routes that pass through Worthington.

Other Options

  • Car rental: Rental cars are available at many airports and in nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Worthington

Worthington is a small town, and most major attractions are located within walking or biking distance. However, car rentals are available for visitors who want to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Worthington: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have some extra time to explore after visiting Worthington, consider these nearby destinations:

  • Sioux Falls: Just a short drive across the border into South Dakota, Sioux Falls offers a variety of museums, parks and other attractions.
  • Pipestone National Monument: Located about an hour’s drive from Worthington, take a tour of the ancient quarries and intricate carvings at Pipestone National Monument.

Conclusion

Worthington is a hidden gem in the Midwest, with a unique culture and beautiful natural attractions. Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast or a history buff, there is something for everyone in Worthington. Plan your trip today and discover all that this charming city has to offer.
