Introduction to Troy

Located in the Miami-Scioto basins in the Midwest region of the United States, Troy, Ohio is a charming and vibrant city that is rich in history and cultural diversity. Home to numerous attractions and natural wonders, Troy provides visitors with a plethora of experiences that will leave them eager to return. If you’re planning a trip to Troy, here’s everything you need to know:

List of Places to Visit in Troy

Troy offers a range of attractions that cater to all interests. Here are some of the must-visit places in Troy:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Troy Hayner Cultural Center - Home to galleries, performance spaces, and event rooms, the Troy Hayner Cultural center is a must-visit for art lovers.
  2. Overfield Tavern Museum - Built-in 1804, the Overfield Tavern is one of the oldest buildings in Troy and offers a glimpse into its fascinating history.
  3. Waco Air Museum - If you’re an aviation enthusiast, the Waco Air Museum is a must-visit attraction in Troy.

Natural Attractions of Troy: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Troy-Miami County Public Library - Home to a wide range of books, magazines, and other reading materials, visitors can relax and enjoy the serenity of the library’s park-like atmosphere.
  2. Hobart Urban Nature Preserve - The nature preserve offers visitors a chance to explore the great outdoors, thanks to its scenic hiking trails, flora, and fauna.
  3. Great Miami Riverway - An ideal spot for canoeing, kayaking, and fishing, visitors can also enjoy the scenic views surrounding the Riverway.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Troy

Troy offers an array of options for outdoor activities. Here’s a list of the best hiking and biking routes in Troy:

  • Troy-St. Paris Connector Bike Trail
  • Great Miami Riverway Recreation Trail
  • Brukner Nature Center Trails

Best Things to Do in Troy: Activities and Tours

Apart from visiting popular attractions, Troy offers visitors a wide range of fun experiences. Here are some of the best things to do in Troy:

  • Attend a concert at the Hobart Arena
  • Explore local eateries on the Troy Food Tour

Food and Drink Scene of Troy

Troy’s food and drink scene is diverse and rich in local flavor, making it a must-visit destination for foodies. Here are some delicious dishes and beverages to try:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Troy Pie - A local specialty that is similar to a pecan pie.
  2. Buckeye Candy- A mixture of peanut butter and chocolate that resembles the state tree.
  3. Kandy’s Grilled Pizza - A pizza made using fresh ingredients and grilled to perfection.

Local Beverages

  1. The Moeller Brew Barn - A brewery that produces high-quality craft beers inspired by local flavors.
  2. The Winery at Versailles - A winery that produces wines made with estate-grown grapes and other seasonal fruits.
