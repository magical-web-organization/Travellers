Introduction to Piqua

Piqua is a charming city located in the Miami-Scioto basins in Ohio’s Midwest region. The city has rich historical significance that dates back to the early 1800s and has an impressive array of natural attractions, cultural hotspots, and outdoor adventure options that are sure to impress and entertain any visitor.

List of Places to Visit in Piqua

During your visit to Piqua, you will find a wealth of fascinating places to explore. Here are some of the top destinations that you should add to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Piqua Historical Museum: This museum is a must-see for history buffs. The museum displays collections related to Piqua’s rich history, including exhibits on transportation, communication, and agriculture. You will find a variety of artifacts that cover the city’s history from ancient times to the present.

  • Statue of Piqua’s Founder: This statue is located in the downtown area and honors Piqua’s founder, General Anthony Wayne. This statue is a great photo opportunity and serves as an excellent starting point to explore the city’s many cultural and historical landmarks.

  • Johnston Farm and Indian Agency: Located on the outskirts of the city, Johnston Farm and Indian Agency is a historical landmark that transports visitors back in time to the early 1800s. Visitors can explore the 1815 Johnston home and farm and learn about the Shawnee Indians who resided there.

  • Fountain Park: This beautiful park features a stunning fountain and plenty of recreational facilities such as picnic areas, a playground, and walking paths. Fountain Park is the perfect place to relax and enjoy the great outdoors.

Natural Attractions of Piqua: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Tawawa Park: This 220-acre park is a must-visit destination for nature lovers. You can take a stroll along the Miami River, enjoy a picnic with your family, or explore the many jogging and biking trails that wind through its scenic landscape.

  • Hueston Woods State Park: Located just outside of the city, Hueston Woods State Park offers 3,000 acres of recreational activities that include hiking, fishing, swimming, and boating. The park also features cozy cabins, tent camping, and nature programs designed for children.

  • The Great Miami Riverway: This beautiful riverway is the perfect place to experience scenic views and enjoy outdoor activities such as kayaking, canoeing, or paddleboarding. You can also hike or bike along the riverbanks and enjoy breathtaking views of Ohio’s Great Miami River.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Piqua

Piqua features some of Ohio’s most scenic hiking and biking trails. Depending on your preference and skill level, here are some of the best options:

  • Piqua Country Club Path: This 5.5-mile hiking and biking trail meanders through Piqua’s beautiful countryside, showcasing rolling hills, farmland, and wooded areas. The trail is relatively flat and easy, making it an excellent option for beginners.

  • Great Miami Riverway Trail: This beautiful 75-mile trail follows Ohio’s Great Miami River and offers a variety of hiking, biking, and water activities. The trail features paved paths, scenic bridges, and breathtaking river views that will delight outdoor enthusiasts of all levels.

  • Tawawa Park Trail: This 3.9-mile trail winds through Tawawa Park, offering picturesque river views, lush forests, and open meadows. The trail is perfect for hiking, biking, or leisurely strolls.

Best Things to Do in Piqua: Activities and Tours

Whether you are looking for indoor attractions or outdoor adventures, Piqua has many activities that cater to all interests. Here are some of the best things to do in Piqua:

  • Piqua Antique Mall: This antique mall is a treasure trove for antique lovers. You will find a vast collection of vintage clothing, furniture, and other collectibles that will take you back in time.

  • Miami Valley Pottery: This pottery shop offers a unique shopping experience that allows visitors to watch the artists at work. You can purchase handmade pottery pieces and attend workshops to learn the art of pottery-making.

  • Kayaking and Canoeing on the Great Miami River: The Great Miami River offers some of the best places to canoe and kayak in Ohio. You can rent equipment or take a guided tour to explore the river’s beauty and enjoy a peaceful day in nature.

Week-Long Itinerary for Piqua

If you are planning a week-long trip to Piqua, here’s a sample itinerary that showcases some of the best things to do and see in the city:

Day 1: Visit the Piqua Historical Museum and the Statue of Piqua’s Founder.

Day 2: Explore Tawawa Park’s hiking trails and enjoy a picnic lunch.

Day 3: Spend the day kayaking or canoeing on the Great Miami River.

Day 4: Visit the Hueston Woods State Park and enjoy a day of outdoor activities such as hiking or wildlife watching.

Day 5: Browse the antiques at Piqua Antique Mall and Miami Valley Pottery.

Day 6: Take a guided tour of the Johnston Farm and Indian Agency.

Day 7: Relax and enjoy the scenic views of Fountain Park.

Food and Drink Scene of Piqua

Piqua’s food and drink scene serves up a range of local and international flavors to delight every palate.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Buckeye Candy: This delicious candy is made from a peanut butter mixture covered in chocolate and is a beloved Ohioan treat.

  • Barbecue: Piqua has some of the best BBQ spots in the state, with local favorites being Hickory River Smokehouse and Mo’s BBQ.

  • Fried Chicken: The Piqua area is home to some of the best fried chicken in Ohio. Be sure to try the chicken at Hot Head Fried Chicken or Dot’s Market.

Local Beverages

  • Local Craft Beer: Piqua has a unique craft beer scene that includes both large and small breweries. Warped Wing Brewing Company and Crooked Handle Brewing Company are two of the city’s best.

  • Wine: The Miami Valley Wine Trail passes through the Piqua area and features several award-winning wineries.

Where to Stay in Piqua: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Piqua offers a range of accommodations that cater to all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best options:

  • La Quinta Inn & Suites Piqua: This hotel offers comfortable accommodation and is conveniently located near local attractions such as the Piqua Historical Museum.

  • Comfort Inn Miami Valley Centre Mall: This hotel is located next door to the Miami Valley Centre Mall and offers easy access to major highways.

  • The Golden Bear Bed and Breakfast: This charming B&B features cozy rooms and a charming garden setting.

What to Buy in Piqua: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Piqua has a vibrant shopping scene with plenty of unique stores and boutiques. Here are some of the best types of souvenirs to bring home:

  • Antiques: Piqua is famous for its antique malls and vintage shops, making it the perfect place to hunt for unique treasures.

  • Pottery: Miami Valley Pottery is just one of the shops that showcases local pottery-making skills.

Local Culture and Customs of Piqua

The culture of Piqua is rooted in its rich history and heritage. Ohioans are friendly and welcoming, so don’t be afraid to ask locals for recommendations on what to do or where to eat.

Best Time to Visit Piqua

Piqua’s climate is typical of the Midwest region, with warm summers and cold winters. The best time to visit Piqua is during the summer months (June-August) when you can enjoy the city’s outdoor activities and attractions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Piqua

Here are some of the do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Piqua:

Do:

  • Do explore Piqua’s outdoor attractions, including the Great Miami Riverway and Tawawa Park.

  • Do attend cultural events and festivals such as the Piqua Heritage Festival, which takes place in September.

  • Do try local dishes such as Buckeye Candy and local craft beer.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in Piqua’s parks or public spaces.

  • Don’t be afraid to ask locals for recommendations; they are friendly and knowledgeable about their city.

  • Don’t miss out on the city’s rich historical and cultural landmarks.

Safety and Health

Piqua is a peaceful city with a low crime rate. However, it’s always best to take precautions, including keeping valuables out of sight and staying aware of your surroundings.

Safety in Piqua

Piqua is a safe city, but you should always exercise caution when traveling alone at night or in unfamiliar areas. Keep your belongings close to you, and be aware of any suspicious activity.

Health in Piqua

In the case of an emergency, Piqua has several hospitals and medical facilities that offer quality care. These include Piqua Family Practice and the Upper Valley Medical Center.

Getting to Piqua: Transportation Options and Logistics

Piqua is easily accessible by car, bus, or plane. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Piqua

  • Dayton International Airport (DAY) is the closest airport, approximately 30 miles from Piqua.

  • Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky International Airport (CVG) is approximately 60 miles from Piqua.

Bus Stations Near Piqua

  • Greyhound Bus Station in Sidney, OH is the closest bus station, approximately 10 miles from Piqua.

  • Dayton RTA bus system also offers public transportation to and from Piqua.

Other Options

  • Amtrak stations are located in nearby cities such as Springfield, OH and Cincinnati, OH.

How to Get Around in Piqua

Piqua is a walkable city with several public transportation options available. You can also rent a car or bike to explore the city at your leisure.

Public Transportation

Piqua Transit offers a bus service that operates on weekdays throughout the city and surrounding areas.

Car Rentals

Car rental companies such as Enterprise and Hertz offer rental services in Piqua and surrounding areas.

Bike Rentals

Bike rentals are available at the Piqua Area Chamber of Commerce. You can also find bike rental services at local parks like Tawawa Park.

Where to Go Next from Piqua: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Piqua’s central location in Ohio makes it a great starting point for exploring other nearby destinations such as:

  • Dayton, OH: This city is home to the National Museum of the US Air Force, the Dayton Art Institute, and the Oregon Historic District.

  • Columbus, OH: Columbus is the capital of Ohio and features attractions such as the Franklin Park Conservatory and Botanical Gardens, the Ohio Statehouse, and the Columbus Museum of Art.

Conclusion

Piqua is a hidden gem in Ohio’s Midwest region. It offers visitors a unique blend of outdoor adventures, rich cultural heritage, and charming local experiences. From exploring the city’s historical landmarks to kayaking along the Great Miami River, there’s something for everyone to love in Piqua.
