Food and Drink Scene of Ashland

Ashland has a vibrant culinary scene that caters to all tastes. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Barbecue ribs at Buehler’s Fresh Foods BBQ Pit
  • Fried chicken at Buckeye Express Diner
  • Apple butter at Country Hearth

Local Beverages

  • Ashland Brewing Company’s Kolsch beer
  • White dragon tea at Sojourners Coffee & Tea

What to Buy in Ashland: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Take home a piece of Ashland by purchasing local products and souvenirs. Here are some ideas that you can explore:

Local Culture and Customs of Ashland

Ashland’s cultural heritage is rich and diverse, and locals take pride in their traditions. Here are some cultural customs that you can expect when visiting Ashland:

  • A friendly and welcoming community
  • Celebration of festivals and events such as the Ashland BalloonFest and the Ashland County Fair
  • Emphasis on local food and craft traditions
  • Love for nature and outdoor activities

Best Time to Visit Ashland

The best time to visit Ashland is from spring to fall when the weather is pleasant and outdoor activities can be enjoyed. Summers can be hot and humid, so it’s best to pack lightweight clothing and sunscreen. Winters can be bitterly cold with snow and ice, so pack accordingly.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ashland

Make the most of your trip to Ashland by following these do’s and don’ts:

Do:

  • Try local cuisine such as fried chicken and apple butter
  • Explore Ashland’s scenic parks and hiking trails
  • Attend local festivals and events

Don’t:

  • Litter in public places
  • Ignore safety signs and warnings
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions

Safety and Health

Ashland is generally a safe and healthy place to visit, but it’s always best to take precautions. Here are some safety and health tips to keep in mind while visiting Ashland.

Safety in Ashland

  • Always be aware of your surroundings and stay in well-lit areas
  • Secure valuables and lock car doors when parked
  • Follow safety instructions when engaging in outdoor activities

Health in Ashland

  • Drink bottled water to avoid water-borne diseases
  • Wear insect repellent when spending time outdoors to avoid tick and mosquito bites
  • Consult a doctor if feeling unwell during your trip

Getting to Ashland: Transportation Options and Logistics

Ashland is well-connected to neighboring cities through several transportation options. Here are some ways to reach Ashland:

Airports Near Ashland

  • Cleveland Hopkins International Airport (84 miles away from Ashland)
  • Akron-Canton Regional Airport (55 miles away from Ashland)

Bus Stations Near Ashland

  • Greyhound bus station in Wooster, OH (24 miles away from Ashland)
  • Mansfield Transit Systems (14 miles away from Ashland)

Other Options

  • Renting a car is a convenient way to explore Ashland and its surroundings on your own
  • Taxis and ride-sharing apps like Uber and Lyft are available for around-town trips

How to Get Around in Ashland

Ashland is easily navigable by car, bike, or on foot. Renting a car, bike, or scooter is the most convenient way to get around the city. Ashland also offers public transportation through the Ashland Transit System.
