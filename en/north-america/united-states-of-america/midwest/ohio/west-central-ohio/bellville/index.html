Introduction to Bellville

Bellville is a charming village located in West Central Ohio, part of the Midwest region of the United States. Known for its natural beauty, Bellville and its surrounding areas offer a plethora of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and historical landmarks.

List of Places to Visit in Bellville

If you are planning a trip to Bellville, here are the must-visit places to add to your itinerary.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Ohio State Reformatory

The Ohio State Reformatory is a former prison that now serves as a museum showcasing the history of Ohio’s penitentiary system. Take a guided tour to see the Gothic architecture or participate in a ghost hunt.

Kingwood Center Gardens

The Kingwood Center Gardens is a beautiful estate created by Charles Kelley King in 1926. Visitors can wander through 47 acres of landscaped gardens, fountains, and greenhouses.

Gorman Nature Center

The Gorman Nature Center is an educational facility designed to teach visitors about Ohio’s wildlife and ecosystems. The center features beautiful hiking trails and hands-on exhibits for all ages.

Natural Attractions of Bellville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Mohican State Park

Mohican State Park offers a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking, fishing, camping, and horseback riding. The park is also known for its stunning views of the Clear Fork Gorge.

Clear Fork Reservoir

Clear Fork Reservoir is a serene oasis surrounded by rolling hills and stunning scenery. Visitors can fish, boat, and swim in the clear waters or hike the surrounding trails.

Kokosing Gap Trail

The Kokosing Gap Trail is a scenic bike trail that follows the old Penn Central Railroad corridor. The trail offers views of Ohio’s farmland and passes through several quaint towns.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bellville

If you’re an outdoor enthusiast, be sure to check out these hiking and biking trails in and around Bellville.

Mohican State Park Trail

The Mohican State Park Trail is a 4.5-mile loop that winds through the forest and offers scenic views of the Clear Fork Gorge.

Richland B&O Bike Trail

The Richland B&O Bike Trail is a 18-mile rail-trail that passes through quaint towns, farmland, and forested areas.

Mohican Valley Trail

The Mohican Valley Trail is a 4.5-mile trail that follows the old Penn Central Railroad corridor and offers views of Ohio’s countryside and the Mohican River.

Best Things to Do in Bellville: Activities and Tours

Whether you are traveling with your family, your friends, or on your own, there is something for everyone in Bellville.

Ghost Hunts at Ohio State Reformatory

Join the ghost hunt at the Ohio State Reformatory to explore the haunted prison under the guidance of a professional ghost hunting team.

Canoeing or Kayaking in the Mohican River

Rent a canoe or kayak to explore the scenic Mohican River and enjoy the peacefulness of nature.

Wine Tasting at Cypress Hill Winery

Visit Cypress Hill Winery and enjoy a tour of the winery followed by a tasting of their unique and award-winning wines.

Food and Drink Scene of Bellville

No trip is complete without indulging in local cuisine and drinks. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages in Bellville.

Must-Try Dishes

Buckeye Candy

Buckeye Candy is a Ohioan specialty that combines peanut butter and chocolate to create a mouth-watering treat.

Coney Dog

The Coney Dog is a traditional Ohioan dish that includes a hot dog, chili sauce, and a sprinkle of cheese on top.

Local Beverages

Ohio Wine

Ohio is home to some award-winning wineries, and a visit to Bellville is not complete without trying some of the local wines.

Mohican Cream Ale

The Mohican Cream Ale is a popular beer brewed by the Mohican Brewing Company, located in nearby Loudonville.

Where to Stay in Bellville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several accommodation options in Bellville, ranging from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxury hotels.

Hotel - The Kenyon Inn

The Kenyon Inn is a charming hotel located in nearby Gambier, featuring comfortable rooms and beautiful gardens.

Guesthouse - The Tree House Village

The Tree House Village is the ultimate nature getaway, offering cozy treehouses set in the heart of Mohican State Park.

What to Buy in Bellville: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don’t forget to grab souvenirs and local products to remember your trip to Bellville.

Mohican State Park Merchandise

Pick up some Mohican State Park merchandise, including t-shirts, hats, and mugs, to commemorate your visit.

Amish Goods

Nearby Amish communities offer crafts, furniture, and baked goods that make for unique souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Bellville

Ohioans are known for their friendliness and hospitality. The Amish community near Bellville are known for their rural lifestyle, horse-drawn buggies, and craftsmanship.

Best Time to Visit Bellville

The best time to visit Bellville is from May to October, when the weather is mild and outdoor activities are available.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bellville

Here are some Dos and Don’ts to keep in mind while exploring Bellville.

Do:

  • Respect the Amish community’s privacy and customs.

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

  • Be prepared for changing weather conditions.

Don’t:

  • Take photos of the Amish community without permission.

  • Litter or damage natural areas.

  • Underestimate the importance of sunscreen and insect repellent.

Safety and Health

While Bellville is a relatively safe area, it is always important to take precautions when traveling.

Safety in Bellville

Be aware of your surroundings and take precautions to protect yourself and your belongings.

Health in Bellville

It is important to drink plenty of water and protect yourself against insects and ticks when exploring the outdoors.

Getting to Bellville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here are some transportation options for getting to Bellville.

Airports Near Bellville

The closest airport to Bellville is the Port Columbus International Airport, located 60 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Bellville

The nearest bus station to Bellville is the Greyhound Bus Station in Mansfield, which is approximately 15 miles away.

Other Options

Renting a car is also an option for those who prefer to drive to Bellville.

How to Get Around in Bellville

The best way to get around Bellville is by car or bike.

Where to Go Next from Bellville: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some destinations near Bellville that are worth exploring.

Columbus, Ohio

Columbus, Ohio is the capital of Ohio and a vibrant city filled with historic sites, art galleries, and trendy neighborhoods.

Lake Erie Islands

The Lake Erie Islands offer a relaxing retreat from busy city life, featuring beaches, camping, and water sports.

Conclusion

Bellville, Ohio, is a great destination for those looking for a combination of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and natural beauty. With so much to see and do, make sure to add Bellville to your list of must-visit destinations in the Midwest.
