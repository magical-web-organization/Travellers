Upper Sandusky Travel Guide

Welcome to Upper Sandusky - a picturesque small town located in West Central Ohio! Known for its incredible natural beauty, culture, and history, this city is waiting to be explored. Whether you are a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, our comprehensive travel guide will help you plan the perfect trip to Upper Sandusky.

List of Places to Visit in Upper Sandusky

Here is a list of some of the best places to visit in Upper Sandusky:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Wyandot County Historical Society and Museum
  2. Indian Mill State Memorial
  3. John Stewart United Methodist Church and Museum
  4. The Ritz Theatre

Natural Attractions of Upper Sandusky: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Harrison Smith Park
  2. Upper Sandusky Reservoir Number Two
  3. Killdeer Plains Wildlife Area
  4. Miami Sandusky Scenic River

Hiking/Biking Routes of Upper Sandusky

For hiking and biking enthusiasts, Upper Sandusky offers a plethora of fun and challenging routes. Some of the best routes to explore include:

  1. Upper Sandusky River Walkway
  2. Killdeer Plains Wildlife Area Trail
  3. Harrison Smith Nature Trail

Best Things to Do in Upper Sandusky: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Upper Sandusky:

  1. Take a scenic drive along the Miami Sandusky Scenic River
  2. Enjoy a live performance at the Ritz Theatre
  3. Visit the Wyandot County Historical Society and Museum
  4. Go fishing at Upper Sandusky Reservoir Number Two

Week-Long Itinerary for Upper Sandusky

Here’s a week-long itinerary to explore Upper Sandusky and its surrounding areas:

Day Activities
Day 1 Visit Wyandot County Historical Society and Museum, have lunch at local restaurants
Day 2 Explore Indian Mill State Memorial, go hiking on Killdeer Plains Wildlife Area Trail, and enjoy a scenic drive along the Miami Sandusky Scenic River
Day 3 Visit John Stewart United Methodist Church and Museum, enjoy the Upper Sandusky River Walkway, and have dinner at local restaurants
Day 4 Visit Harrison Smith Park, go shopping at local boutiques
Day 5 Take a day trip to Toledo to visit the Toledo Art Museum
Day 6 Go fishing at Upper Sandusky Reservoir Number Two, have lunch at local restaurants
Day 7 Enjoy a live performance at the Ritz Theatre, have dinner at local restaurants

Local Culture and Customs of Upper Sandusky

Upper Sandusky has a rich and interesting culture. Some of the customs and traditions to explore include:

  1. The Ohio State Fair
  2. The Indian Mill Festival
  3. The Wyandot County Fair

Best Time to Visit Upper Sandusky

The best time to visit Upper Sandusky is from June to September. During this time, the weather is pleasant, and many of the outdoor attractions are open.

Do’s and Don’ts of Upper Sandusky

Do:

  1. Respect the local culture and traditions
  2. Explore the outdoors
  3. Try local food and drinks

Don’t:

  1. Litter
  2. Ignore the local customs
  3. Disrespect wildlife

Safety and Health

Safety in Upper Sandusky

It is generally safe to travel to Upper Sandusky. However, visitors should always take basic precautions, such as locking their vehicles and not leaving valuables unattended.

Health in Upper Sandusky

Upper Sandusky has good medical facilities. In case of an emergency, visitors can contact the Wyandot Memorial Hospital.
