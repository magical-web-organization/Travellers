Introduction to Milford-Miami Township

Located in Clermont County, Greater Cincinnati, Milford-Miami Township is the perfect destination for nature lovers and history enthusiasts. Surrounded by lush forests and rolling hills, this charming township offers a serene and peaceful getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life.

List of Places to Visit in Milford-Miami Township

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Promont House Museum: This historic house museum is a great example of Victorian architecture and offers a glimpse into the lives of the Milford-Miami Township’s affluent families during the 19th century.
  • Milford Historical Society Museum: This museum showcases the township’s rich history, including artifacts and exhibits dating back to its founding in 1796.
  • Old Milford Parsonage: Built in 1825, this restored parsonage is a unique and interesting attraction that offers an insight into the early lives of the township’s settlers.

Natural Attractions of Milford-Miami Township: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Little Miami Scenic Trail: This 78-mile paved trail runs along the Little Miami River and offers scenic views of the lush forests and tranquil rivers of Milford-Miami Township. It’s perfect for hiking, walking, cycling, and even horseback riding.
  • Milford Memorial Park: Located on the east bank of the Little Miami River, this park is an ideal spot for picnicking, hiking, and fishing. It offers stunning views of the river and the surrounding forests.
  • Shor Park: This park features playgrounds, picnic areas, and sports fields and is an excellent place for families to enjoy outdoor activities together.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Milford-Miami Township

  • The Loveland Bike Trail: This 70-mile trail is known as one of the best bike trails in Ohio and offers breathtaking views of the Little Miami River. It’s a great trail for cycling, jogging, or just taking a leisurely walk.
  • Eagle Trail: This hiking trail covers 3.4 miles and winds through the beautiful forests of Milford-Miami Township. It’s perfect for bird watching, nature photography, and a peaceful afternoon hike.
  • Little Miami State Park Trail: This 50-mile trail follows the peaceful waters of the Little Miami River and is one of the most popular biking and hiking trails in Ohio. It offers stunning views of the surrounding forests and wildlife.

Best Things to Do in Milford-Miami Township: Activities and Tours

  • Go Zip Lining: Get a bird’s-eye view of the forests and valleys of Milford-Miami Township by going zip-lining. There are several companies that offer zip-lining tours in the area.
  • Canoeing and Kayaking on the Little Miami River: Rent a canoe or kayak and explore the scenic waters of the Little Miami River. It’s a relaxing and peaceful way to spend an afternoon and take in the surrounding beauty of the area.
  • Go Horseback Riding: Several companies offer horseback riding tours through the forests and fields of Milford-Miami Township. It’s a unique and exciting way to experience the beauty of the area.

Week-Long Itinerary for Milford-Miami Township

Day 1: Visit the Milford Historical Society Museum and Promont House Museum. Day 2: Hike the Eagle Trail in the morning and go zip-lining in the afternoon. Day 3: Rent a canoe and tour the Little Miami River. Day 4: Visit the Old Milford Parsonage in the morning and go horseback riding in the afternoon. Day 5: Explore the Loveland Bike Trail and have a picnic in Milford Memorial Park. Day 6: Take a guided tour of the historic sites of Milford-Miami Township. Day 7: Relax and enjoy the natural beauty of the area with a leisurely walk or bike ride on the Little Miami State Park Trail.

Food and Drink Scene of Milford-Miami Township

Must-Try Dishes

  • Skyline Chili: This famous Cincinnati dish can be found at one of the many Skyline restaurants in Milford-Miami Township.
  • Goetta: A regional meat-and-grain sausage served at several local restaurants and diners.
  • Buckeyes: This popular Ohio dessert is a peanut butter ball dipped in chocolate.

Local Beverages

  • Rhinegeist Brewery: Located in Cincinnati, Rhinegeist is one of the top breweries in the region and offers a wide selection of craft beers.
  • Wooden Cask Brewery: This local brewery in Newport, KY offers a range of handcrafted ales and lagers.
  • Rivertown Brewery and Barrel House: Located in Monroe, OH, Rivertown Brewery is known for its delicious beers and unique flavors.

  • Comfort Suites Cincinnati East: With spacious rooms, complimentary breakfast, and an indoor pool, this hotel is a popular choice for visitors to the area.

  • Local Honey: Pick up a jar of local honey, which is known for its unique and delicious flavors.

Local Culture and Customs of Milford-Miami Township

Milford-Miami Township is known for its rich history and natural beauty. It’s a close-knit community that values hard work, honesty, and kindness. Residents are proud of their town and eager to share its unique charm and personality with visitors.

Best Time to Visit Milford-Miami Township

The best time to visit Milford-Miami Township is during the fall when the foliage is a riot of colors and the weather is mild and comfortable for outdoor activities. Spring is another good time to visit as the flowers are in bloom and the weather is pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Milford-Miami Township

Do:

  • Respect the natural beauty of the area and pack out your trash.
  • Be friendly and courteous to locals and fellow visitors.
  • Take the time to explore the unique history and culture of the area.

Don’t:

  • Leave valuables in your car when parked at trailheads or other public areas.
  • Litter or damage the local flora and fauna.
  • Block access to the local roads and trails with your car.

Safety and Health

Safety in Milford-Miami Township

Milford-Miami Township is a safe and welcoming community, but visitors should take standard precautions to ensure their safety. Lock car doors, park in well-lit areas, and exercise caution when hiking or exploring the area’s natural beauty.

Health in Milford-Miami Township

There are several hospitals and medical facilities in the Cincinnati area that provide excellent medical care and emergency services. It’s always a good idea to have travel insurance in case of medical emergencies and to consult with your doctor before any outdoor activities.

How to Get Around in Milford-Miami Township

The best way to get around Milford-Miami Township is by car or bike. There are several parking areas and biking trails throughout the township that make it easy to explore the area’s natural beauty and historical attractions.

Where to Go Next from Milford-Miami Township: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Cincinnati: Just a short drive from Milford-Miami Township, Cincinnati is a vibrant and exciting city with plenty of attractions and things to do.
  • Mammoth Cave National Park: Located in Kentucky, Mammoth Cave National Park is a unique and interesting destination for nature lovers.
  • Hocking Hills State Park: Located in Ohio, Hocking Hills State Park is a popular hiking destination with beautiful waterfalls and unique rock formations.
