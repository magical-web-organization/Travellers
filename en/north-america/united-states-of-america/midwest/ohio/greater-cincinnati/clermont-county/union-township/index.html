Introduction to Union Township

Union Township is a charming suburb in Clermont County, in Greater Cincinnati, Ohio. It is an excellent destination for anyone looking for a mix of outdoor adventure, culture, and history. This travel guide will provide you with all the necessary information on the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay in Union Township.

List of Places to Visit in Union Township

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

1. Harmony Hill Vineyards

Harmony Hill Vineyards is a beautiful winery that specializes in crafting high-quality wines. The vineyard features a large tasting room, an outdoor patio with stunning views, and frequent live music events. From the vineyard, you can also take a winery tour and learn more about the winemaking process.

2. Clermont County Historical Society

The Clermont County Historical Society contains artifacts, photographs, and exhibits documenting the history of Union Township and the surrounding areas. It is an excellent place to learn about the rich cultural heritage of this region.

3. East Fork State Park

East Fork State Park is a stunning natural area that covers over 4,870 acres. It is a popular destination for hiking, picnicking, boating, fishing, and swimming. The park also has a nature center, where you can learn about the diverse wildlife that inhabits the area.

Natural Attractions of Union Township: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

1. Cincinnati Nature Center

The Cincinnati Nature Center is a beautiful nature preserve that covers over 1,800 acres of wooded hills, valleys, and streams. It features miles of hiking trails, wildlife exhibits, and educational programs. The Nature Center also provides a guided night program where visitors can explore the forest with a naturalist.

2. Lake Harsha

Lake Harsha is a stunning artificial lake that covers over 2.160 kilometers. It is a popular destination for boating, fishing, kayaking, and swimming. The lake has several boat launches and fishing piers, as well as several hiking trails around it.

3. Cherry Grove Park

Cherry Grove Park is a beautiful park that features several amenities such as a large playground, picnic shelters, hiking trails, and an off-leash dog park. The park also has a lake where visitors can enjoy fishing or boating.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Union Township

Union Township has several hiking and biking routes that offer stunning scenery and outdoor experience. Some of the best routes include:

  • East Fork State Park Trail
  • Little Miami Scenic Trail
  • Horseshoe Falls Trail

Best Things to Do in Union Township: Activities and Tours

1. Union Township Farmers Market

The Union Township Farmers Market is a popular event that takes place every Saturday morning from May to October. Visitors can browse fresh produce, baked goods, handmade crafts, and enjoy live music.

2. Vista Verde Art Glass

Vista Verde Art Glass is a beautiful art studio that creates unique glass pieces and offers workshops. Visitors can take a workshop and learn the basics of glassblowing and make their glassware.

3. Flying Axe Factory

The Flying Axe Factory is a fun and unique way to spend an afternoon. Visitors can throw axes at a target while enjoying beer on tap. It’s a great way to unwind and release your inner lumberjack.

Week-Long Itinerary for Union Township

Day 1: Check into your hotel and spend the afternoon exploring the historic sites of Clermont County. Day 2: Visit East Fork State Park and experience the natural beauty of the area. Day 3: Take a winery tour and tasting at Harmony Hill Vineyards. Day 4: Visit Cincinnati Nature Center for a guided night hiking tour. Day 5: Enjoy some outdoor activities such as kayaking or fishing at Lake Harsha. Day 6: Take a glassblowing workshop at Vista Verde Art Glass. Day 7: Explore the local farmers market, do some shopping, and enjoy some local cuisine.

Food and Drink Scene of Union Township

Must-Try Dishes

1. Cincinnati-style Chili

Cincinnati-style chili is a unique & delicious take on the classic chili. It’s spicier, thicker, and sweeter than your average chili, and it’s often served over spaghetti.

2. Goetta

Goetta is a uniquely Cincinnati dish that features a blend of ground meat, oats, and spices. It’s often served with eggs and toast for breakfast or in a sandwich or burger.

3. Montgomery Inn Ribs

The Montgomery Inn is famous for its mouth-watering ribs, which are soaked in a special marinade and cooked to perfection.

Local Beverages

1. Rhinegeist Brewery

Rhinegeist Brewery is a popular brewery that creates a range of craft beers, including IPAs and lagers.

2. Ault Park Coffee

Ault Park Coffee is a local coffee roaster that creates high-quality coffee blends. Visitors can enjoy a cup of coffee at their café or buy a bag to take home.

Where to Stay in Union Township: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hotels

1. Holiday Inn & Suites Cincinnati-Eastgate

The Holiday Inn & Suites Cincinnati-Eastgate is a modern hotel with a range of amenities such as a fitness center, indoor pool, and a restaurant and bar.

2. Comfort Inn & Suites Eastgate

The Comfort Inn & Suites Eastgate is a budget-friendly hotel that offers comfortable rooms, a fitness center, and a complimentary breakfast.

Hostels

There are no hostels in Union Township.

Guesthouses

1. The Inn at Woodhaven

The Inn at Woodhaven is a stunning bed and breakfast that offers luxurious amenities such as a Jacuzzi, a fireplace, and a beautiful garden.

2. Casa Stepanie Guest House

The Casa Stephanie Guest House is a charming guesthouse that offers cozy rooms and a relaxing atmosphere.

What to Buy in Union Township: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

1. Handmade Glassware

Vista Verde Art Glass creates unique handblown glassware that would make a beautiful souvenir or gift.

2. Local Honey

The Union Township Farmers Market offers fresh local honey, which would make a sweet and thoughtful gift.

3. T-Shirts and Souvenirs

The Ohio River Scenic Byway Visitors Center has an array of t-shirts and other souvenirs from around the area.

Local Culture and Customs of Union Township

Union Township residents take pride in their history and natural beauty. It’s important to be respectful of the wildlife and leave the area cleaner than you found it.

Best Time to Visit Union Township

The best time to visit Union Township is during the fall, when the leaves are changing, and the weather is cooler. Spring and summer are also great times to visit as the weather is warm, and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Union Township

Do:

  • Respect the wildlife and the environment.
  • Explore the area’s natural beauty and historical sites.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Take part in outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the environment.
  • Ignore the historical significance of the area.
  • Be disrespectful to the locals or wildlife.

Safety and Health

Safety in Union Township

Union Township is generally a safe area, but it’s always important to take basic safety precautions such as locking your car and being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Union Township

There are several hospitals and medical centers located in the area, and emergency services are available.

Other Options

There are several car rental companies in Union Township, and you can also use taxi or rideshare services to get around the area.

How to Get Around in Union Township

The best way to get around Union Township is by car, as public transportation can be limited.

Where to Go Next from Union Township: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

1. Cincinnati

Cincinnati is a vibrant city known for its rich history, arts, sports, and culture. It’s a great place to explore and only 25 minutes away from Union Township.

2. Newport Aquarium

The Newport Aquarium is a popular attraction that features a glass tunnel shark tank, penguins, and exotic marine life. It’s only a 30-minute drive from Union Township.

Conclusion

Union Township is a charming suburb that offers something for everyone, from outdoor adventures to cultural experiences. This travel guide provides all the necessary information to plan a perfect trip to Union Township. Whether you’re interested in history, nature, food, or local culture, Union Township won’t disappoint you.
