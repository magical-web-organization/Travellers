Introduction to Wauseon

Wauseon is a quaint little town in the middle of Fulton County, Ohio, located in the Northwest region of Ohio. This charming town is known for its rich history, cultural significance, and natural beauty. The town is named after Samuel Waugh, an early settler, and is now home to a thriving community that welcomes tourists from across the globe.
