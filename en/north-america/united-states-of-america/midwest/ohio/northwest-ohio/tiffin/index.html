Tiffin, Ohio, is a small town located in the northwest corner of the state. Despite its size, the town is rich in history and offers plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures. Whether you’re looking to explore the natural beauty of the area or learn more about its history, Tiffin has something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore the best places to visit, eat, stay, and more in Tiffin, Ohio.

Introduction to Tiffin

Tiffin is a small town located in northwest Ohio, surrounded by beautiful farmland and rolling hills. The town has a rich history dating back to the 1800s when it was founded as a center for the glass-making industry. Today, Tiffin is known for its charming downtown area, beautiful parks, and outdoor recreation opportunities.

List of Places to Visit in Tiffin

If you are visiting Tiffin, there are several places you should not miss. We have divided the list into two sections:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Seneca County Museum: This museum offers an opportunity to learn more about the history of Tiffin and Seneca County. Located in an 1850s farmhouse, the museum features exhibits on local history, including the glass-making industry and the Underground Railroad.

  2. Tiffin Glass Museum: This museum showcases the history of Tiffin’s glass-making industry. Visitors can see examples of Tiffin glass from the late 1800s and learn more about the glass-making process.

  3. Loudon Meadows Golf Club: This golf course opened in 1965, and it features 18 holes of challenging golf. The course offers beautiful views of the surrounding countryside.

Natural Attractions of Tiffin: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Hedges-Boyer Park: This park is a popular spot for picnics, hiking, and fishing. It features a pond, playgrounds, and plenty of space for relaxation.

  2. Rock Creek Trail: This trail is a popular spot for hiking and biking. The trail is 12 miles long and winds through forests, fields, and wetlands.

  3. Tiffin Parks and Recreation: The Tiffin Parks and Recreation Department offers a variety of outdoor activities, including swimming, tennis, and basketball. They also host several events throughout the year, including summer concerts and movies in the park.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tiffin

If you enjoy hiking or biking, Tiffin has several great trails to explore. Some of the best include:

  1. North Coast Inland Trail: This trail is 14 miles long and runs from Bellevue to Clyde. It features beautiful views of the countryside and passes through several small towns.

  2. Tiffin Heritage Trails: This network of trails covers over 6 miles and takes visitors through historic neighborhoods and downtown Tiffin.

  3. Riverwalk: This .25-mile trail runs along the Sandusky River and is a great spot for a leisurely stroll or bike ride.

Best Things to Do in Tiffin: Activities and Tours

If you’re looking for something to do in Tiffin, there are several activities and tours you can enjoy. Some of the best include:

  1. Tiffin Art Guild Gallery: This gallery features local artwork and hosts several exhibitions throughout the year.

  2. Drive-in Movie Theater: The Field of Dreams Drive-in movie theater is a great place to catch a movie on a warm summer night.

  3. Tiffin Flea Market: This outdoor market offers a variety of antiques, collectibles, and other items for sale.

Food and Drink Scene of Tiffin

Must-Try Dishes

  2. Pizzas at Reino’s Pizza and Pasta: Reino’s is known for its delicious pizzas and Italian specialties.

  3. Craft beer at the Brewery: The Brewery offers a rotating selection of craft beers and small bites.

Local Beverages

  1. Wine at Gillig Winery: This local winery features a variety of reds, whites, and fruit wines.

  2. Craft beer at the Laird Arcade Brewery: The Brewery offers a rotating selection of craft beers and small bites.

What to Buy in Tiffin: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for a souvenir or local product to bring home, there are several options to choose from. Some of the best include:

  1. Tiffin Glass: Tiffin was once home to a thriving glass-making industry, and you can still find Tiffin glass for sale at local antique shops and flea markets.

  2. Handmade Crafts: Tiffin is home to several artists and crafters who create beautiful handmade items like pottery, jewelry, and textiles.

  3. Local Food Products: You can find a variety of local food products like honey, jams, and cheeses at the Tiffin Farmers Market.

Local Culture and Customs of Tiffin

Tiffin is a small town with a close-knit community. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, especially when it comes to preserving the town’s history.

Best Time to Visit Tiffin

The best time to visit Tiffin is during the summer months when the weather is warm, and outdoor activities are in full swing. The fall months are also a great time to visit when the leaves change color, and the weather is mild.
