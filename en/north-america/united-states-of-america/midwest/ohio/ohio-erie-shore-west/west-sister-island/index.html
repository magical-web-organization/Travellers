Introduction to West Sister Island

Welcome to West Sister Island, a hidden gem located on the Ohio Erie shore west, in Ohio. This small island is just 80 acres but offers a rich cultural and natural heritage that is sure to delight every visitor.

List of Places to Visit in West Sister Island

If you’re planning a trip to West Sister Island, make sure to visit these top attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • West Sister Island State Wildlife Area: This wildlife reserve is part of the US Fish and Wildlife Service and offers excellent opportunities for bird watching and observing other wildlife species.
  • The Keeper’s House: This historic lighthouse was built in 1848 and is still operational today. Visitors can take a guided tour to learn about the island’s maritime history.
  • Perry’s Victory and International Peace Memorial: This impressive monument was erected in 1910 to commemorate the naval victory of the Battle of Lake Erie and to honor the peace that followed.

Natural Attractions of West Sister Island: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • West Sister Island National Wildlife Refuge: This nature reserve is home to a wide variety of birds and other wild animals. Visitors can see rare bird species, such as the Kirtland’s warbler and the piping plover, and hike along the island’s beautiful landscape.
  • South Bass Island State Park: This park boasts stunning views of Lake Erie and offers opportunities for fishing, boating, and hiking.
  • Lonz Winery: This historic winery dates back to the mid-1800s and offers wine tastings and tours.

Hiking/Biking Routes of West Sister Island

Hiking and biking are two of the most popular activities on West Sister Island. Some of the best routes to explore on foot or by bike include:

  • West Sister Island Trail: This three-mile trail is a great way to explore the island’s natural beauty and abundant wildlife. The trail is well-marked and offers stunning views of the surrounding area.
  • South Bass Island Loop Trail: This 7.5-mile loop around the island offers a mix of lake views, wooded areas, and scenic vistas. Bikers will love this relatively easy ride.

Best Things to Do in West Sister Island: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of fun things to do in West Sister Island, regardless of your interests. Some of the best activities and tours to consider include:

  • Bird Watching Tours: West Sister Island is home to a diverse range of bird species, and bird watching tours are a popular way to observe them in their natural habitat.
  • Fishing Trips: Lake Erie is known for its exceptional fishing opportunities, and West Sister Island is no exception. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a beginner, guided fishing trips are a great way to get out on the water.
  • Kayaking and Canoeing: The calm waters surrounding West Sister Island are perfect for kayaking and canoeing. Rentals are available both on and off the island.

Food and Drink Scene of West Sister Island

No trip to West Sister Island is complete without trying the local cuisine and beverages. Here are some of the must-try dishes and drinks:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Walleye Sandwich: This local favorite sandwich consists of a fresh walleye fillet, lettuce, tomato, and tartar sauce served on a roll.
  • Lake Erie Perch: Another delicious fish dish made from locally caught perch, fried or grilled.
  • Cherry Muffin: Made using the locally grown cherries, this muffin adds a sweet flavor to your morning breakfast.

Local Beverages

  • Wine: Lonz Winery offers an extensive selection of wines, including several made from locally grown grapes.
  • Beer: Several local breweries offer a variety of craft beers, including Great Lakes Brewing Company.
  • Cherry Juice: Known for their cherry orchards, West Sister Island produces a wide range of cherry juices that feature a naturally sweet, tangy flavor.

Where to Stay in West Sister Island: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options on West Sister Island, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Some of the most popular options include:

  • Put-in-Bay Resort and Conference Center: This waterfront resort offers comfortable rooms and suites, as well as a full-service restaurant and bar.
  • Victorian Inn Bed and Breakfast: This charming guesthouse features six beautifully appointed rooms and a delicious breakfast each morning.
  • Soak City Cedar Point Shores: This family-friendly waterpark resort offers a range of accommodations, including cabins, cottages, and hotel rooms.

What to Buy in West Sister Island: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Looking for an unforgettable souvenir from your trip to West Sister Island? Here are some of the best local products to bring home:

  • Lake Erie Perch Spice Blend: This local spice blend is perfect for seasoning fish dishes and features a unique blend of herbs and spices.

Local Culture and Customs of West Sister Island

The people of West Sister Island are friendly and welcoming, and visitors are expected to show respect for their culture and customs. Some tips to keep in mind include:

  • Be respectful of the wildlife and natural environment: West Sister Island is home to a variety of rare and endangered species, and visitors should take care to minimize their impact on the island’s wildlife and natural resources.
  • Dress appropriately: Visitors should dress modestly when visiting religious sites and always cover their shoulders and knees.
  • Keep noise levels low: It’s best to keep noise levels low, especially during the early morning and late evening.

Best Time to Visit West Sister Island

The best time to visit West Sister Island is in the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny. However, visitors should be prepared for crowds during this time. If you prefer a quieter experience, consider visiting in the spring or fall.

Safety and Health

Safety in West Sister Island

West Sister Island is a very safe destination for visitors, but it’s always a good idea to take basic precautions, such as:

  • Be aware of your surroundings: Visitors should always be aware of their surroundings, especially if traveling alone or at night.
  • Secure your valuables: Visitors should keep their valuables, such as passports and wallets, secure at all times.
  • Be careful when swimming or boating: Visitors should exercise caution when swimming or boating in Lake Erie, as the water can be unpredictable and there has been several occurrences of algae blooms in the lake.

Health in West Sister Island

West Sister Island does not have any major health concerns, but visitors should take basic precautions to stay healthy, such as:

  • Drink plenty of water: It’s important to stay hydrated, especially during the hot summer months.
  • Apply insect repellent: Mosquitoes and ticks can be present in West Sister Island, so visitors should use insect repellent to prevent bites.
  • Wear sunscreen: The sun can be strong on the island, so visitors should always wear sunscreen to protect their skin.
