University Area Travel Guide: Explore the Best of Columbus, Ohio

Located in the heart of Columbus, Ohio, University Area is a culturally rich and vibrant neighborhood that has something for everyone. Whether you are a history buff, outdoor enthusiast, or food lover, University Area has it all. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take you on a tour of the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and where to eat in University Area. So, let’s get started!

List of Places to Visit in University Area

There are plenty of attractions to explore in University Area that cater to all tastes. Here are some of the must-visit places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Ohio State University - Founded in 1870, The Ohio State University is one of the most prestigious public universities in the U.S. It is home to numerous museums, including the Billy Ireland Cartoon Library and Museum, the Wexner Center for the Arts, and the OSU Museum of Art.

  • Ohio Stadium - Known as “The Horseshoe,” Ohio Stadium is home to the Ohio State Buckeyes football. The stadium has a seating capacity of over 100,000 and is one of the largest stadiums in the country.

  • The Thurber House - This historic home-turned-museum was once the residence of James Thurber, a famous writer and cartoonist. The museum showcases Thurber’s life and work and hosts various literary events throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of University Area: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Scioto Mile - This 233-acre park along the Scioto River offers scenic views, walking and biking trails, fountains, and public art installations. It is a great place for a picnic or an evening stroll.

  • Battelle Darby Creek Metro Park - Located just a short drive from University Area, this sprawling park features hiking and biking trails, fishing ponds, and a nature center. It is home to numerous species of wildlife, including bison and deer.

  • Olentangy Trail - This 13.75-mile trail is perfect for biking, running or walking. It runs from Worthington to downtown Columbus and offers scenic views of the Olentangy River.

Hiking/Biking Routes of University Area

For outdoor enthusiasts, University Area offers some of the best hiking and biking routes in the region. Here are some of the top routes to explore:

  • Olentangy Trail - This 13.75-mile trail is perfect for biking, running or walking. It runs from Worthington to downtown Columbus and offers scenic views of the Olentangy River.

  • Alum Creek Trail - This 22-mile trail runs through the Alum Creek State Park and is a popular spot for hiking, biking, and fishing.

  • Scioto Trail - This 11-mile trail runs from downtown to Scioto Grove Metro Park and offers stunning views of the Columbus skyline.

Best Things to Do in University Area: Activities and Tours

Whether you are looking for a relaxing day out or an adrenaline-fueled adventure, University Area has got you covered. Here are some of the best things to do in the neighborhood:

  • Take a Brewery Tour - Columbus is home to several breweries, including the Land-Grant Brewing Company and the Seventh Son Brewing Company. Book a tour and enjoy a beer tasting session while learning about the brewing process.

  • Go Shopping - The Short North Arts District is home to numerous boutiques and art galleries. You can also find many local vendors at the North Market, which offers a wide range of locally-sourced produce, meats, and other food items.

  • Catch a Game - Whether it’s football at Ohio Stadium or basketball at the Schottenstein Center, watching a game at one of Ohio State’s venues is a must-do for sports fans.

Week-Long Itinerary for University Area

If you’re planning to spend a week in University Area, here’s a sample itinerary to help you make the most of your trip:

1 Explore the Ohio State University Campus and the Wexner Center

6 Watch a football or basketball game at Ohio State
7 Relax at the Scioto Mile and enjoy the stunning views of the river

Food and Drink Scene of University Area

Columbus has a thriving food and drink scene, and University Area is no exception. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Buckeye Candy - This peanut butter and chocolate candy is a favorite among Ohioans.

  • Jeni’s Splendid Ice Creams - This local ice cream chain has gained national recognition for its unique and delicious flavors.

  • Thurman Burger - This iconic burger from the Thurman Cafe is known for its massive size and delicious toppings.

Local Beverages

  • BrewDog Beers - This Scottish brewery has a taproom in the Short North Arts District, where you can try their craft beers.

  • Columbus Coffee - Columbus is known for its local coffee roasters, including Crimson Cup and Stauf’s.

  • Watershed Distillery Gin - This local distillery produces award-winning gin and other spirits.

Where to Stay in University Area: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

University Area offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best places to stay in the neighborhood:

  • The Blackwell Inn - Located on the Ohio State University campus, this luxurious hotel offers stunning views of the university and excellent amenities.

  • The Wayfaring Buckeye Hostel - This cozy hostel offers budget-friendly accommodation and a friendly atmosphere.

  • Airbnb - There are plenty of Airbnb options available in University Area, from private rooms to entire apartments and houses.

What to Buy in University Area: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs and local products to bring home, there are plenty of options in University Area. Here are some of the best places to shop:

  • The Ohio State University Bookstore - This massive bookstore offers a range of Buckeye-themed merchandise, including t-shirts, hats, and other memorabilia.

  • Grandview Mercantile - This antique store in the Short North Arts District offers a range of unique and vintage items.

  • North Market - This indoor market offers a wide range of locally-sourced produce, meats, and other food items, as well as artisanal crafts and products.

Local Culture and Customs of University Area

Ohioans are known for their friendly and welcoming nature, and University Area is no exception. The neighborhood has a diverse and inclusive community, and visitors are encouraged to respect local customs and cultures.

Best Time to Visit University Area

The best time to visit University Area is from April to October, when the weather is mild and there are plenty of outdoor events and festivals. However, if you prefer fewer crowds and lower prices, you can also visit in the off-season (November to March).

Do’s and Don’ts of University Area

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting University Area:

Do:

  • Respect local customs and cultures.

  • Try local food and drinks.

  • Explore the parks and outdoor attractions.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage public property.

  • Ignore pedestrian signals or traffic rules.

  • Be disrespectful to Ohio State fans or alumni.

Safety and Health

As with any urban area, it’s important to take precautions to stay safe and healthy in University Area.

Safety in University Area

University Area is generally a safe neighborhood, but visitors should still take basic precautions to avoid theft and other crimes. Keep your valuables out of sight, and be aware of your surroundings when walking alone at night.

Health in University Area

Columbus has several world-class medical facilities, including the Ohio State University Wexner Medical Center and Mount Carmel Health System. If you need medical assistance during your visit, these facilities can provide top-notch care.

Getting to University Area: Transportation Options and Logistics

University Area is easily accessible by car, bus, or plane.

Airports Near University Area

  • Port Columbus International Airport - This airport is located about 15 minutes by car from University Area.

  • Rickenbacker International Airport - This airport is located about 20 minutes by car from University Area.

Bus Stations Near University Area

  • Greyhound Bus Lines - This bus station is located in downtown Columbus, about 10 minutes by car from University Area.

  • Megabus - This bus stop is located at the Ohio State University campus, within walking distance from University Area.

Other Options

  • Taxi/Lyft/Uber - Taxis and ride-sharing services are readily available in Columbus.

  • Car Rental - Car rental services are available at the airports and in downtown Columbus.

How to Get Around in University Area

University Area is an easily walkable neighborhood, and many of the top attractions are within walking distance of each other. You can also explore the area by bike, which is a popular way to get around in Columbus.

Where to Go Next from University Area: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have extra time to explore the region, here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • The Columbus Zoo and Aquarium - This nationally recognized zoo is located about 25 minutes by car from University Area.

  • German Village - This historic neighborhood in Columbus is known for its charming brick houses and German restaurant scene.

  • Hocking Hills State Park - This stunning state park is located about an hour’s drive from University Area and offers hiking, waterfalls, and scenic views.

Conclusion

University Area is a vibrant and diverse neighborhood that offers something for every visitor.
