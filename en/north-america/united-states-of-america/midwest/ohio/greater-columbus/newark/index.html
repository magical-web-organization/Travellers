Introduction to Newark

Newark, a city located in the Greater Columbus area of Ohio, is a vibrant destination with much to offer visitors. This city has a rich history, a lively cultural scene, and a variety of natural attractions. From museums and historical sites to outdoor activities and scenic views, this city is a great place to explore. This guide will give you a comprehensive overview of the top places to visit, things to do, local culture, food, where to stay, and transportation options in Newark.

List of Places to Visit in Newark

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Newark has an interesting history that has informed its present. Some of the must-visit historical places, such as Newark Earthworks, which are ancient earthworks that were once part of the Hopewell culture, can be found in the city.

  • Newark Earthworks
  • The Webb House Museum
  • Buckingham Meeting House
  • The Licking County Courthouse

Natural Attractions of Newark: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Newark is also home to some natural attractions where visitors can experience nature in all its glory.

  • Dawes Arboretum
  • Blackhand Gorge State Nature Preserve
  • The Newark Canal District
  • The Great Circle Museum

Hiking/Biking Routes of Newark

Newark has several beautiful trails for hiking, and visitors have plenty of options for biking as well. These trails offer panoramic views and are well maintained.

Visitors to Newark have plenty to choose from when it comes to activities and tours. Some of the top activities to consider include:

Week-Long Itinerary for Newark

Here’s a week-long itinerary to help you explore all the top attractions in Newark.

Day 1 - Visit The Webb House Museum, a fine federal-style mansion built-in 1822.

Day 2 - Explore nature at Dawes Arboretum, a 1,800-acre nature preserve that includes a Japanese Garden, a Dawes Lake, and over 5,000 different species of plants.

Day 3 - Explore the largest set of geometric earthen enclosures in the world at the Newark Earthworks.

Day 4 - Bike along the scenic Ohio to Erie Trail that runs through Newark.

Day 5 - Take a Hot Air Balloon tour and experience scenic views of Newark from above.

Day 6 - Visit The Licking County Courthouse, a grand courthouse built-in 1876.

Day 7 - Hike along the Buckeye Scenic Trail and enjoy the beautiful landscape of this region.

Local Culture and Customs of Newark

Newark is a welcoming city with a diverse population. It is a city with a rich culture, with vibrant traditions that visitors can learn and enjoy during their stay.

Best Time to Visit Newark

The best time to visit Newark is from April to October. During this time, the weather is pleasant, and most of the attractions are open.
