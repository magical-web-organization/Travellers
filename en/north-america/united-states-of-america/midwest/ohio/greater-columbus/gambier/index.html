Introduction to Gambier

Nestled in the heart of the Midwest, Gambier is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. It is a small picturesque village located in Knox County, Ohio, and home to Kenyon College. With its charming architecture, vibrant culture, and picturesque landscapes, Gambier is the perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Whether you’re a history buff, nature lover, or food enthusiast, Gambier has something for everyone. So pack your bags and get ready for an exciting adventure to this gem in the Midwest.

List of Places to Visit in Gambier

Here’s a list of top places to visit in Gambier:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. The Kenyon College - Admire the neo-gothic architecture and take a stroll around one of the most beautiful campuses in the US.
  2. Gambier Historical Society Museum - Discover the rich history of Gambier and its influence on Ohio’s settlement.
  3. Brown Family Environmental Center - Immerse yourself in nature while learning about the environmental initiatives of Gambier and Kenyon College.

Natural Attractions of Gambier: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. The Kokosing Gap Trail - Walk, run or bike on this popular trail that stretches for 14 miles across stunning views of Gambier’s countryside.
  2. The Wildflower Garden Trail - A verdant hike with a rich variety of wildflowers, birds, and other wildlife.
  3. The Kenyon College Cemetery - A picture-perfect memorial garden featuring historic monuments, ancient trees, and stunning views of the surrounding hills.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Gambier

Take on the Kokosing Gap Trail, a 14-mile stretch that links Gambier to Mount Vernon and has breathtaking views of dense forest and rolling, green hills. On this trail, you’ll get to experience Gambier’s natural beauty and its outdoor adventures. Each season offers something unique, making this trail a must-do activity during your trip.

Best Things to Do in Gambier: Activities and Tours

  1. The Kenyon Review Literary Festival: This annual event features Pulitzer prize-winning authors.
  2. Distinguished Lecture Series: Listen to guest speakers from around the world, from famous politicians to great leaders in science.
  3. Gambier Craft & Vendor Show: This showcases the work of over 40 local artisans and crafters, from gourmet foods to handcrafted jewelry and textiles.

Week-Long Itinerary for Gambier

Day 1: Visit Brown Family Environmental Center and enjoy the natural scenery of Gambier.

Day 2: Explore the Kenyon College campus and Gambier Historical Society Museum.

Day 3: Take a bike ride on the Kokosing Gap Trail and enjoy the stunning views of the Midwestern countryside.

Day 4: Take a day trip to Mount Vernon and explore the Kenyon Glass Museum and the Knox County Historical Society Museum.

Day 5: Go shopping at the Gambier Craft & Vendor Show and taste the delicious local food.

Day 6: Attend a Kenyon Review Literary Festival and listen to a Pulitzer prize-winning author.

Day 7: Relax and enjoy Gambier and its surrounding nature.

Food and Drink Scene of Gambier

Gambier’s food scene is a mix of classic Midwestern comfort food with exotic artisanal ingredients. Here’s a list of must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. The Gambier Grill’s Burgers: This local restaurant specializes in fresh and juicy burgers that have been voted the best in the region.
  2. The Brandiwine: A delicious chicken sandwich with spicy mayonnaise and crispy fries.
  3. Rumi’s Kabob: Authentic Middle Eastern delights served with a selection of salads

Local Beverages

  1. The Village Inn: A classic Midwestern bar with an extensive selection of craft beers and American classics.
  2. The Gambier Deli - Offers the best selections of wines and house-made teas
  3. The Coffee Cup: A cozy café serving locally roasted coffee and homemade pastries.

Where to Stay in Gambier: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

You can stay at one of Gambier’s charming guesthouses, inns, or hotels for an authentic Gambier experience. Here are some of the best options:

  1. The Gambier House: A historic bed and breakfast with cozy guest rooms and traditional breakfast.
  2. The Kenyon Inn: This charming hotel has a Greek revival architecture and offers luxury suites with spa amenities.
  3. The Gambier Loft: Located in the heart of Gambier, these spacious apartments offer a home-away-from-home feel.

What to Buy in Gambier: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Take home a piece of Gambier’s culture and history with these souvenirs:

  1. Gambier Pottery: Embrace the village’s rich pottery legacy by purchasing original Gambier-made pottery.
  2. Kenyon College T-Shirts: Show your Kenyon College pride by purchasing a t-shirt with Kenyon’s iconic logo.
  3. The Gambier Thrift Shop: This local shop offers artisanal crafts and vintage furniture that are uniquely Gambier.

Local Culture and Customs of Gambier

Gambier’s cultural scene is flavored by the history of the town and Kenyon College. The village has an appreciative atmosphere, is liberal and accepting of diverse cultures, lifestyles, and perspectives. It also retains a strong sense of community spirit and caring for all its inhabitants. Visitors are welcomed, and the village prides itself in preserving its rich history while embracing the future.

Best Time to Visit Gambier

The best time to visit Gambier is during spring and fall when the weather is mild, and the trees are in their full glory. The Kokosing Gap Trail is especially beautiful during these times of the year. For winter activities, Gambier spruces up for the holidays with lights and Christmas decorations making it the perfect time to experience the picturesque village.

Do’s and Don’ts of Gambier

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Appreciate Gambier’s history and heritage
  • Take time to enjoy the natural scenery and outdoors
  • Use the local transport to travel within Gambier or rent a bike

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage public property
  • Cause excessive noise and disturbance
  • Ignore local laws and customs

Safety and Health

Safety in Gambier

Gambier is a safe and peaceful village, and visitors are not likely to face any safety concerns. However, visitors should exercise caution while on the Kokosing Gap Trail but it remains safe and secure with routine patrols. .### Health in Gambier

Gambier is committed to the community’s health and safety and taking necessary steps to prevent illness and disease outbreaks. The Kenyon Student Health and Counseling Center is available to residents and visitors alike on campus. Additionally, travelers should remain up-to-date with CDC guidelines and seek medical attention as necessary.

Getting to Gambier: Transportation Options and Logistics

Gambier is easily accessible by several routes of transportation.

Airports Near Gambier

The nearest airport to Gambier is the Columbus International Airport (CMH), which is 61 miles away from Gambier.

Bus Stations Near Gambier

Greyhound buses pass through Mount Vernon, 4 miles southeast of Gambier.

Other Options

A car is the most convenient method of transport as it offers optimal flexibility and control. Local cab services are also available for transport within Gambier’s city limits.

How to Get Around in Gambier

Gambier has an efficient bus system that is served by Four County Transit, with routes that serve Gambier during peak times.

Bicycle rental is also widely available, providing visitors with the opportunity to enjoy Gambier’s outdoors.

Where to Go Next from Gambier: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Within a short drive, there are also major cities such as Pittsburgh, Cleveland, Cincinnati, and Columbus. Here are some popular excursions from Gambier:

  1. The Columbus Museum of Art and Ohio State Fair (60 mi): These attractions are a must-visit for art enthusiasts and those who love fairs.
  2. Amish Country (35 mi): Experience the history and culture of the Amish community, including handmade products, horse-drawn agriculture, lodging, and restaurants.
  3. Cedar Point Amusement Park (100 mi): Enjoy America’s Roller Coast Park, which features the most exhilarating roller coasters in the USA.

Conclusion

Gambier is an idyllic village that offers visitors a chance to experience Midwestern culture, history, and picturesque landscapes. With incredible attractions ranging from museums, outdoor adventures, and local cuisine, Gambier is a must-visit destination in the Midwest. The village is committed to its community’s safety and health, making it a perfect spot to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. Pack your bags and head out to Gambier for an unforgettable experience!
