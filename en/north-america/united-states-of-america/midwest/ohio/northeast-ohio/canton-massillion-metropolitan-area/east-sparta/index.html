Introduction to East Sparta

East Sparta is a charming village located in the Canton-Massillon Metropolitan Area, in Northeast Ohio, Midwest, United States of America. The village is part of Stark County and is bordered by the Tuscarawas River to the east and the Ohio and Erie Canalway to the west. East Sparta is known for its scenic countryside and rural charm, making it a popular tourist destination for those who want to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

List of Places to Visit in East Sparta

If you’re planning a trip to East Sparta, here are the must-visit places that you shouldn’t miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The East Sparta Historical Society. Located in the heart of the village, this historical society features a collection of artifacts and exhibits that showcase the history of East Sparta and the surrounding area. The society also hosts special events throughout the year, including craft shows, flea markets, and historical reenactments.

  • St. Jacob’s Lutheran Church. This historic church is known for its beautiful stained glass windows and intricate woodwork. The church was founded in 1825 and is still in use today.

  • The McKinley Presidential Library and Museum. Located just a short drive from East Sparta, this museum features exhibits on the life and presidency of William McKinley, the 25th President of the United States.

Natural Attractions of East Sparta: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Ohio and Erie Canalway. This historic canal is a popular spot for hiking and biking, and offers beautiful views of the Tuscarawas River and the surrounding countryside.

  • The Atwood Lake Region. Located just a few miles from East Sparta, this region is home to Atwood Lake, which offers fishing, boating, and swimming. The area is also known for its beautiful hiking trails and scenic views.

  • The Sandy and Beaver Canal. This historic canal runs through East Sparta and is a great spot for hiking and exploring.

Hiking/Biking Routes of East Sparta

East Sparta is a great place for outdoor enthusiasts, with plenty of hiking and biking trails to explore. Here are some of the best routes in the area:

  • The Sandy and Beaver Canal Trail. This 8.4-mile trail runs alongside the historic Sandy and Beaver Canal and offers beautiful views of the Tuscarawas River.

  • The Ohio and Erie Canal Towpath Trail. This 80-mile trail runs through several states, including Ohio, and offers beautiful views of the countryside. The East Sparta section of the trail is a popular spot for biking and hiking.

  • The Atwood Lake Region Trails. The Atwood Lake Region is home to several hiking trails, including the Lakeview Trail, the Buckhorn Trail, and the Atwood Trail.
