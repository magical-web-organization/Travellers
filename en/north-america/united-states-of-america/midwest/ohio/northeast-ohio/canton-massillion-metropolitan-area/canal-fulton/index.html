Introduction to Canal Fulton

Canal Fulton is a small city located in the Canton-Massillon Metropolitan Area in Northeast Ohio, United States. This charming city offers visitors a glimpse into the past with its well-preserved historic buildings and abundance of outdoor recreation opportunities.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore the best things to do in Canal Fulton, including a list of places to visit, hiking and biking routes, and must-try foods.

List of Places to Visit in Canal Fulton

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Canal Fulton is rich in history, and visitors can explore the city’s past at several museums and historic sites.

Canal Fulton Canalway Center

The Canal Fulton Canalway Center is the perfect starting point for exploring the city. This museum showcases the history of the Ohio and Erie Canal, which played a crucial role in Ohio’s development in the 19th century. Visitors can watch a short film about the canal or take a guided tour to learn more about its history.

St. Helena III Canal Boat

Another must-see attraction for history buffs is the St. Helena III Canal Boat. Visitors can take a ride on this replica of a 19th-century canal boat and experience what it was like to travel on the canal.

Canal Fulton Glassworks

For a unique experience, head to the Canal Fulton Glassworks. This studio offers visitors the chance to watch skilled glassblowers create beautiful pieces of art in front of their eyes.

Natural Attractions of Canal Fulton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Canal Fulton is surrounded by scenic forests, parks, and waterways, making it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts.

Towpath Trail

One of the most popular outdoor destinations in Canal Fulton is the Towpath Trail. This trail runs parallel to the Ohio and Erie Canal and stretches for 84 miles, making it perfect for hiking, biking, and horseback riding.

Sippo Lake Park

Sippo Lake Park is another popular destination for nature lovers. This 202-acre park features a beautiful lake, hiking trails, and picnic areas. Visitors can also rent kayaks and canoes to explore the lake and its wildlife.

Exploration Gateway at Sippo Lake Park

At Exploration Gateway at Sippo Lake Park, visitors can learn about Ohio’s natural history through interactive exhibits and educational programs. There is also an observation tower that offers stunning views of the park and its surroundings.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Canal Fulton

In addition to the Towpath Trail, Canal Fulton offers many other hiking and biking routes that showcase the city’s beautiful natural scenery.

Clinton Como Park

Clinton Como Park is a beautiful nature reserve that offers visitors several hiking trails. These trails wind through forests and fields and feature stunning views of the park’s wildlife and flora.

Stark Parks Trailhead at Canal Fulton

The Stark Parks Trailhead at Canal Fulton is a great starting point for exploring the city’s biking and hiking routes. This trailhead offers access to the Towpath Trail as well as several other nearby trails.

Best Things to Do in Canal Fulton: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of activities and tours available in Canal Fulton to keep visitors entertained.

Bike Tours

For those who prefer guided tours, several companies offer bike tours along the Towpath Trail. These tours give visitors a chance to learn about the canal’s history while enjoying the scenic views.

Ghost Tours

Canal Fulton is also known for its haunted history, and visitors can explore this spooky side of the city on ghost tours. Several companies offer guided tours, taking visitors to some of the city’s most haunted sites.

Vintage Car Tours

For a unique experience, hop on a vintage car tour of Canal Fulton. These tours take visitors through the city’s historic districts and offer a glimpse into its past.

Week-Long Itinerary for Canal Fulton

For those planning a week-long trip to Canal Fulton, here is a sample itinerary to make the most of your visit:

Day 1 Visit the Canal Fulton Canalway Center and take a guided tour.
Day 2 Take a ride on the St. Helena III Canal Boat and explore the historic district.

Food and Drink Scene of Canal Fulton

Canal Fulton has a vibrant food and drink scene that showcases local flavors and ingredients.

Must-Try Dishes

Biscuits and Gravy

Biscuits and gravy is a staple in Canal Fulton’s breakfast scene. Made with homemade biscuits and savory gravy, this dish is a must-try for visitors.

Fried Chicken

Fried chicken is another popular dish in Canal Fulton. Many local restaurants serve this Southern classic with a unique twist.

Buckeye Candy

For those with a sweet tooth, Buckeye Candy is a must-try treat. This candy consists of a peanut butter ball dipped in chocolate and is a favorite among Ohioans.

Local Beverages

Craft Beer

Canal Fulton has a growing craft beer scene, with several local breweries serving up unique and flavorful beers.

Wine

Ohio is also known for its wine, and Canal Fulton is home to several wineries that offer tastings and tours.

Where to Stay in Canal Fulton: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Canal Fulton has a variety of accommodation options, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses.

Cassaday House

The Cassaday House is a historic bed and breakfast that offers guests a charming and comfortable stay. This beautifully restored mansion features luxurious rooms and amenities, including a gourmet breakfast each morning.

St. Helena III Canal Boat

For a unique experience, consider staying on the St. Helena III Canal Boat. This boat offers overnight stays and allows visitors to experience what it was like to live on the canal.

Canal Fulton RV Park

For those who prefer camping, the Canal Fulton RV Park offers affordable and convenient accommodations. This park is located near the Towpath Trail, making it perfect for outdoor enthusiasts.

What to Buy in Canal Fulton: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors can take home a piece of Canal Fulton’s charm with these must-buy souvenirs and local products.

Local Crafts and Artwork

Canal Fulton is home to many talented artists and craftsmen, and visitors can find unique and handmade gifts in the city’s shops and galleries.

Ohio and Erie Canal Souvenirs

Visitors can also bring home souvenirs that showcase the city’s rich history. From t-shirts to mugs, there are many options available that feature the Ohio and Erie Canal.

Local Culture and Customs of Canal Fulton

Canal Fulton is a friendly and welcoming city, and visitors should expect to be greeted with open arms. The city’s residents take pride in its history and natural beauty and are happy to share it with visitors.

Best Time to Visit Canal Fulton

The best time to visit Canal Fulton is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny. Visitors can also enjoy fall foliage in October and snow sports in the winter.

Getting to Canal Fulton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Canal Fulton

The nearest major airport to Canal Fulton is Akron-Canton Regional Airport. This airport is located just 10 miles away and offers daily flights to major cities in the United States.

Bus Stations Near Canal Fulton

Greyhound and Megabus both offer bus service to Canton, Ohio, which is located just 10 miles away from Canal Fulton.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi to get to Canal Fulton from surrounding cities.

How to Get Around in Canal Fulton

The best way to get around Canal Fulton is by car or bike. Visitors can rent a car from the Akron-Canton Regional Airport or rent a bike from one of the many rental shops in the city.

Where to Go Next from Canal Fulton: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several nearby destinations and excursions that visitors can explore after their visit to Canal Fulton.

The Cuyahoga Valley National Park is located just a short drive away from Canal Fulton and offers visitors stunning natural scenery and outdoor recreation opportunities.
