Introduction to Dellroy

Dellroy is a small village located in the Canton-Massillon metropolitan area of Northeast Ohio. Known for its scenic views, outdoor adventures, and rich history, Dellroy attracts visitors who enjoy exploring natural beauty, cultural attractions, and local events.

List of Places to Visit in Dellroy

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re interested in exploring museum and historical places, Dellroy has some great options, including:

  • St. Joseph’s Cultural Center: Originally built as a church in 1877, this stunning building has been restored and is now a cultural center, showcasing local art, music, and history.
  • The Great Trail Festival: Held annually in August, this festival celebrates traditional American crafts, music, and food.
  • The Carroll County Historical Society: Located in nearby Carrollton, this society has a museum with exhibits on local history, including the Underground Railroad and the Ohio Erie Canal.

Natural Attractions of Dellroy: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Dellroy is known for its natural beauty and outdoor adventures. Here are some of the top natural attractions to check out:

  • Leesville Lake: This 1,000-acre lake is perfect for swimming, fishing, boating, and other water sports.
  • Atwood Lake: Another popular lake for activities such as boating, fishing, and camping.
  • Mohican State Park: Located about an hour from Dellroy, this park offers a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, horseback riding, and camping.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dellroy

If you’re looking for some great hiking or biking routes in and around Dellroy, here are some recommendations:

  • The Carrollton Greenway Trail: This 3.4-mile trail is perfect for biking or walking and provides scenic views of the surrounding countryside.
  • The Ohio & Erie Canal Towpath Trail: This 101-mile trail is located about an hour from Dellroy and offers a variety of activities, including hiking, biking, and horseback riding.
  • The Mohican State Park Trails: This park has over 20 miles of hiking trails, ranging from easy to challenging, and offers scenic views of the Mohican River.

Best Things to Do in Dellroy: Activities and Tours

In addition to the natural beauty and cultural attractions, Dellroy has plenty of activities and tours to keep visitors entertained. Here are some of the top things to do:

  • Go Fishing: With several lakes and rivers in the area, Dellroy is a great place for fishing, whether you prefer fly fishing or spinning.
  • Visit a Vineyard: The Canton-Massillon area is home to several wineries and vineyards, including Atwood Lake Winery, Troutman Vineyards, and Breitenbach Wine Cellars.
  • Take a Hot Air Balloon Ride: Located in nearby Canton, the Canton Hot Air Balloon Company offers scenic flights over the surrounding countryside.

Week-Long Itinerary for Dellroy

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Dellroy, here’s a sample itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrive in Dellroy and settle into your accommodations.
  • Day 2: Explore the local museums and historical sites, including St. Joseph’s Cultural Center and the Carroll County Historical Society.
  • Day 3: Go hiking or biking along the Carrollton Greenway Trail or the nearby Mohican State Park Trails.
  • Day 4: Take a scenic flight over the Canton-Massillon area with the Canton Hot Air Balloon Company.
  • Day 5: Go fishing at Leesville Lake or Atwood Lake.
  • Day 6: Visit a local vineyard, such as Troutman Vineyards or Atwood Lake Winery.
  • Day 7: Spend the day relaxing and exploring Dellroy’s local restaurants and shops.

Food and Drink Scene of Dellroy

Must-Try Dishes

No trip to Ohio would be complete without trying some of the local specialties. Here are some must-try dishes in Dellroy:

  • Ohio Buckeye Candy: This delicious candy resembles a buckeye nut and is made with peanut butter and chocolate.
  • Pierogis: With a large Polish community in Ohio, pierogis are a must-try dish. These dumplings are filled with potatoes, cheese, and onions.
  • Buckeye Cream Pie: Another dessert specialty in Ohio, this pie is made with a chocolate crumb crust and a sweet cream cheese filling.

Local Beverages

If you’re looking for local beverages to try in Dellroy, here are some recommendations:

  • Ohio Craft Beer: Ohio has a thriving craft beer scene, and Dellroy is no exception. Some popular breweries in the area include Royal Docks Brewing Company and Muskellunge Brewing Company.
  • Wine: Ohio is one of the top wine-producing states in the country, and there are several vineyards and wineries in the Canton-Massillon area to visit and sample.

Where to Stay in Dellroy: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

If you’re planning on staying in Dellroy, there are several options for accommodations, including:

  • The Atwood Lake Resort: This resort offers rooms, cottages, and villas, as well as several amenities, including a golf course, indoor pool, and spa.
  • The Candlewood Suites: This hotel offers fully equipped suites with kitchens, as well as a fitness center and outdoor pool.
  • Airbnb: There are several Airbnb options in and around Dellroy, including cabins and cottages for a more rustic experience.

What to Buy in Dellroy: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for some unique souvenirs or local products to bring home from Dellroy, here are some ideas:

  • Handmade Crafts: Dellroy is part of the Great Trail Festival, which celebrates traditional American crafts. You can find a variety of unique handmade products, such as pottery, wood carvings, and textiles.
  • Local Wine: As mentioned above, Ohio is a top wine-producing state, and there are several vineyards and wineries in the area where you can purchase local wines to bring home.
  • Amish Goods: The Canton-Massillon area has a large Amish community, and you can find a variety of Amish-made products, such as furniture, quilts, and jams.

Local Culture and Customs of Dellroy

Ohio has a diverse population with a mixture of different cultural groups, including Amish, Polish, and African American. Religion is also an important aspect of Ohio culture, with a large Christian population.

Best Time to Visit Dellroy

The best time to visit Dellroy is in the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny and there are plenty of outdoor activities and events to enjoy.

Safety and Health

Safety in Dellroy

Dellroy is a relatively safe area, but it’s always important to take general safety precautions, such as locking your doors and windows and being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Dellroy

If you need medical attention while in Dellroy, there are several medical centers and hospitals in the area, including the Carroll County Health Department and Mercy Medical Center.

Getting to Dellroy: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Dellroy

The closest airport to Dellroy is Akron-Canton Airport, which is about 30 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Dellroy

There are no bus stations in Dellroy, but there are several Greyhound bus stations in nearby cities, such as Canton and New Philadelphia.

Other Options

If you’re driving to Dellroy, there are several major highways that connect to the area, including I-77 and US-30.

How to Get Around in Dellroy

The best way to get around Dellroy and the surrounding area is by car. There are several car rental companies in the area, and there are also several cab companies and ride-sharing services, such as Uber and Lyft.

Where to Go Next from Dellroy: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore more of Ohio and the surrounding area, here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Canton, Ohio: Located about 30 miles from Dellroy, Canton is known for its Pro Football Hall of Fame and the William McKinley Presidential Library and Museum.
  • Amish Country: The Canton-Massillon area has a large Amish community, and there are several Amish towns and attractions to visit, such as Berlin and Walnut Creek.
  • Cleveland, Ohio: Located about 90 miles from Dellroy, Cleveland is a major city and home to several attractions, such as the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame and Cleveland Museum of Art.

Conclusion

Dellroy, Ohio is a charming village located in the Canton-Massillon metropolitan area of Northeast Ohio.
