Introduction to Elyria

Located in Lorain County, in Greater Cleveland, in Northeast Ohio, Elyria is a charming city with a rich history. Whether you’re looking to explore the city’s cultural landmarks, natural attractions, or delicious food scene, Elyria has something for everyone. In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ll take a closer look at the top things to do and see in Elyria, exploring everything from its museums and historical sites to hiking trails and local cuisine.

List of Places to Visit in Elyria

If you’re planning a trip to Elyria, there are plenty of attractions that you won’t want to miss. Here’s a list of the city’s top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Lorain County Historical Society: This museum houses a collection of artifacts and exhibits that highlight the history of Lorain County and its people.
  • Hickories Museum: Managed by the Lorain County Metro Parks, this 1895 mansion features rich architecture and antique furnishings.
  • Elyria Arts Council: This is a collective of several galleries in downtown Elyria exhibiting works of art across all forms of media.

Natural Attractions of Elyria: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Cascade Park: This sprawling park offers a variety of activities, including hiking and biking trails, picnic areas, and a beautiful waterfall.
  • Black River Reservation: Spanning over six miles, the Black River Reservation is home to some of Elyria’s most beautiful natural scenery, including wetlands, forest, and streams.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Elyria

Elyria is surrounded by beautiful nature and offers a wide range of hiking and biking trails for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best routes in Elyria:

  • North Coast Inland Trail: This trail connects cities and towns in Northeast Ohio, passing through Elyria.
  • Black River Parkway Trail: This paved trail winds its way through the Black River Reservation, offering stunning views of the park’s natural beauty.
  • Cascade Park Trail Loop: This trail in Cascade Park takes hikers on a scenic journey through forests, waterfalls, and streams.

Best Things to Do in Elyria: Activities and Tours

When it comes to things to do in Elyria, there’s no shortage of options. Here are some of the best activities and tours:

  • Ghostly Manor Thrill Center: Featuring virtual reality adventures, an indoor roller coaster, and a 3D blacklight mini-golf course, this center is ideal for thrill-seekers.
  • Breweries and Wineries Tour: Elyria is home to several breweries and wineries, making for an excellent tour that can be customized according to your preferences.
  • Elyria Farmer’s Market: If you’re a foodie, don’t miss this farmer’s market in downtown Elyria, where you can buy locally grown produce and artisanal goods.

Week-Long Itinerary for Elyria

If you’re visiting Elyria for a week or longer, here’s a suggested itinerary to help you make the most of your time:

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit the Lorain County Historical Society
Day 2 Explore the Black River Reservation and hike the Black River Parkway Trail
Day 3 Take a tour of Elyria’s breweries and wineries
Day 4 Visit the Hickories Museum and Elyria Arts Council
Day 5 Go shopping at the Elyria Farmer’s Market
Day 6 Visit the Ghostly Manor Thrill Center
Day 7 Relax at Cascade Park and hike the Cascade Park Trail Loop

Food and Drink Scene in Elyria

When it comes to food and drinks, Elyria’s dining scene is diverse and vibrant. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Smoked Brisket: Head to Midway Oh Boy to try their famous smoked brisket, served with a variety of sauces.
  • Pierogies: This Polish dish is a staple in many of Elyria’s restaurants. Don’t miss the pierogies at Rubin’s Restaurant and Deli.
  • The ‘Big E’: This massive burger at Midway Oh Boy is a must-try for burger enthusiasts.

Local Beverages

  • Lorain County Wine: Stop by Matus Winery, a family-owned winery, to sample their locally made wines.
  • Elyria Brewing Company: This brewery offers a wide range of craft beers, including stouts, IPAs, and lagers.

Where to Stay in Elyria: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Elyria offers a range of hotels, hostels, and guesthouses to suit all budgets and preferences.

  • Hyatt Place Cleveland/Westlake/Crocker Park: This modern hotel offers spacious rooms with all necessary amenities.
  • Red Roof Inn & Suites Cleveland - Elyria: This budget-friendly hotel offers a complimentary breakfast and an outdoor pool.
  • SpringHill Suites Cleveland/Avon: This hotel features an indoor pool and fitness center and is located close to many of the city’s top attractions.

What to Buy in Elyria: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Looking for souvenirs or gifts to bring home? Elyria offers a variety of options, including:

  • Lorain County Wine: Grab a bottle of locally made wine from Matus Winery.
  • Harry London Chocolates: Harry London chocolates are famous in Northeast Ohio and are available at the Harry London Chocolate Factory in Elyria.
  • Handcrafted Jewelry: Downtown Elyria is home to several boutiques that sell handcrafted jewelry crafted by local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Elyria

Elyria is a modern, diverse city with a strong sense of community. Visitors should respect the local customs and culture, which often emphasize a laid-back and friendly demeanor.

Best Time to Visit Elyria

The best time to visit Elyria is during the spring and summer months when the weather is perfect for outdoor activities, and many of the city’s festivals and events take place.

Do’s and Don’ts of Elyria

Do:

  • Visit the Lorain County Historical Society and learn about the city’s rich history.
  • Explore the Black River Reservation and hike the Cascade Park Trail Loop.
  • Sample locally made wine at Matus Winery.

Don’t:

  • Ignore the city’s cultural landmarks and museums.
  • Be disrespectful of the community’s customs and traditions.
  • Miss the opportunity to try locally made chocolates and handcrafted jewelry.

Safety and Health

Safety in Elyria

Elyria is a relatively safe city, but visitors should be aware of their surroundings at all times and stay aware of any potential risks.

Health in Elyria

Elyria has several hospitals and healthcare centers, including the University Hospitals Elyria Medical Center and Mercy Health - Lorain Hospital.

Getting to Elyria: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Elyria

The closest airport to Elyria is Cleveland Hopkins International Airport, located approximately 19 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Elyria

Elyria is connected to several cities and towns in Ohio via the Greyhound bus line.

Other Options

Elyria is easily accessible by car, with several major highways passing through the area.

How to Get Around in Elyria

Elyria is a relatively small city, and most of its attractions can be reached by car or bike. Alternatively, visitors can take public transportation, including buses and trains.

Where to Go Next from Elyria: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore some nearby destinations, here are some excellent options:

  • Cleveland: Located just 30 minutes away, Cleveland is a vibrant city with a thriving cultural scene.
  • Lake Erie Islands: Head to the Lake Erie Islands to enjoy scenic views, water sports, and fun activities for the whole family.
  • Cedar Point: This amusement park, located approximately an hour away, is home to some of the world’s best roller coasters and thrill rides.

Conclusion

Elyria is a charming city with plenty of attractions, natural beauty, and a rich history. Whether you’re looking to explore its museums and historical sites or its outdoor adventures and scenic views, Elyria has something for everyone. Use this comprehensive tour guide to plan your trip to Elyria and make the most of your visit.
