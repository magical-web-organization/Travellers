Avon Travel Guide: A Complete Tour Guide

Avon is a small city situated in Lorain County, in Greater Cleveland, Northeast Ohio, in the Midwest region of the United States of America. With a population of just over 22,000, it offers visitors a unique blend of urban amenities and natural attractions. From historical landmarks and museums to outdoor adventures, Avon has something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ve compiled all the information you need to plan your next trip to Avon.

List of Places to Visit in Avon

Avon has plenty of attractions that cater to a wide range of interests. Here are some of the must-visit places when in Avon.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Miller Nature Preserve – A unique large glasshouse conservatory that showcases various plants and flowers of different species

  • Sprenger Stadium – Home of the Lake Erie Crushers, a minor league baseball team

  • Fowler’s Milling Company – A historical landmark that’s been operational since the 1830s. Fowler’s Mill grinds and distributes locally produced grains.

  • The History and Heritage of Avon – A museum that highlights Avon’s rich history

Natural Attractions of Avon: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • French Creek Reservation – A sprawling park boasting several miles of hiking trails and streams

  • Lake Erie Beach – A perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, or simply enjoying the beautiful views

  • North Coast Inland Trail – A 14-mile trail that offers wonderful views of the countryside

  • Avon Lake Reservoir – A scenic lake with walking paths that surround it

Hiking/Biking Routes of Avon

Avon boasts several beautiful hiking and biking trails that cater to all levels of outdoor enthusiasts. Some of the popular ones include:

  • French Creek Reservation Trail – This 3.3-mile trail takes hikers and bikers through lush forests, streams, and wetlands.

  • Richard E. Jacobs Health Campus Trail – A 3.5-mile loop trail around the Richard E. Jacobs Health Campus, offering stunning views of the surrounding area.

  • North Coast Inland Trail – A 14-mile trail that passes through three counties, including Avon

Best Things to Do in Avon: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the top activities and tours that every visitor must try out in Avon:

  • Kayak through Lake Erie – Rent a kayak and explore the beautiful shoreline of Lake Erie

  • Visit the Avon Lake Heritage Society – A visit to the Avon Lake Heritage Society is a must if you’re interested in the history of Avon.

  • Go on a Wine Tour – Lorain County has several wineries offering tours and tastings.

  • Explore the Cuyahoga River – Take a leisurely canoe or kayak ride down the scenic Cuyahoga River.

Week-Long Itinerary for Avon

Here’s a recommended week-long itinerary for Avon:

Day 1: Arrive in Avon, check-in at your hotel, and explore the local area

Day 2: Visit Miller Nature Preserve and French Creek Reservation

Day 3: Explore Sprenger Stadium and take a wine tour of the local wineries.

Day 4: Go on a hike or bike ride along the North Coast Inland Trail

Day 5: Spend the day relaxing at Lake Erie Beach

Day 6: Take a canoe or kayak ride down the Cuyahoga River and visit the Avon Lake Heritage Society

Day 7: Leave Avon and begin your journey to Nearby Destinations and Excursions.

Food and Drink Scene of Avon

Avon has a diverse food scene that caters to all types of cuisine lovers. Here are some of the best dishes and local products to try when in Avon:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pierogi – A traditional Polish dish filled with mashed potatoes, onions, and cheese

  • Melted Grilled Cheese Sandwiches – A local food truck that makes some of the best grilled cheese sandwiches in Avon.

  • Walleye Fish – A freshwater fish found in Lake Erie. Walleye is a popular dish in Avon.

  • Fowler’s Mill Cornmeal – Locally produced cornmeal that is perfect for making cornbread.

Local Beverages

  • Lorain County Wine – Lorain County has several wineries that produce award-winning wines.

  • Great Lakes Brewing Co. – A popular brewery in Cleveland that produces a wide range of beers.

Where to Stay in Avon: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Residence Inn – A comfortable and spacious hotel ideal for families or group travelers.

Local Culture and Customs of Avon

The people of Avon are friendly and welcoming to visitors. It’s always good to respect local customs and traditions, especially when visiting places like the historic Fowler’s Mill. Be polite and courteous when interacting with locals.

Best Time to Visit Avon

The best time to visit Avon is in the summer months of June, July, and August, when the weather is warm and perfect for outdoor activities.
