Introduction to Ashtabula County

Ashtabula County is a charming destination located in the heart of Northeast Ohio. It is known for its scenic beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture. Whether you’re a nature lover, history buff, or foodie, Ashtabula County has something to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Ashtabula County

  • The Hubbard House Underground Railroad Museum
  • Lake Shore Park
  • Geneva State Park
  • Ashtabula Maritime Museum
  • The Victorian Perambulator Museum
  • Lake Erie Bluffs
  • Indian Trails Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Ashtabula County has a rich history, and its museums and historical sites offer a glimpse into the region’s past. One of the must-visit museums in the county is the Hubbard House Underground Railroad Museum. This museum provides visitors with a deep understanding of the Underground Railroad and the important role that Ashtabula County played in it. The Ashtabula Maritime Museum is also a fascinating destination, offering an in-depth look at the region’s maritime history.

Natural Attractions of Ashtabula County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Nature lovers will not be disappointed with a trip to Ashtabula County. The county has plenty to offer in terms of outdoor adventures and scenic views. One of the top natural attractions in the area is Lake Shore Park. The park is located along Lake Erie and offers visitors scenic views, hiking trails, and a beach for swimming. Geneva State Park is another popular destination, with its sandy beaches, picturesque lighthouse, and opportunities for water sports. Lake Erie Bluffs is a 600-acre nature preserve that offers visitors panoramic views of Lake Erie and opportunities for hiking and birdwatching.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ashtabula County

Ashtabula County has plenty of hiking and biking trails for visitors to explore. Some of the top trails in the area include:

  • Western Reserve Greenway Trail
  • The Buckeye Trail
  • Grand River Canoe and Kayak Trail

Best Things to Do in Ashtabula County: Activities and Tours

Besides hiking and biking, Ashtabula County has plenty of other activities and tours to offer visitors. One of the most popular tours is the Wine Country Tour. This tour takes visitors on a journey through the region’s wineries, giving them a chance to taste some of the area’s best wines. Visitors can also take a fishing charter on Lake Erie or enjoy a horse-drawn carriage ride through the countryside.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ashtabula County

Day 1: Visit the Hubbard House Underground Railroad Museum and the Ashtabula Maritime Museum.

Day 2: Explore Lake Shore Park and Geneva State Park.

Day 3: Take a wine country tour and taste some of the region’s best wines.

Day 4: Rent a canoe or kayak and explore the Grand River Canoe and Kayak Trail.

Day 5: Visit the Victorian Perambulator Museum and take a horse-drawn carriage ride through the countryside.

Day 6: Hike the Western Reserve Greenway Trail.

Day 7: Enjoy a fishing charter on Lake Erie.

Food and Drink Scene of Ashtabula County

Must-Try Dishes

Ashtabula County is known for its delicious cuisine, and there are some must-try dishes for visitors. One of the most popular dishes in the area is Lake Erie Perch. This freshwater fish is caught locally and can be found on many restaurant menus. Another must-try dish is the signature Italian-American dish, spaghetti and meatballs, which is served at many restaurants in the county.

Local Beverages

Ashtabula County is known for its wine and has several wineries in the area that offer tastings. Visitors can also enjoy locally brewed craft beer at several breweries in the county.

Where to Stay in Ashtabula County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ashtabula County has a variety of accommodations to suit different budgets and preferences. Visitors can choose from hotels, motels, bed and breakfasts, and vacation rentals. Some of the top hotels in the area include the Lodge at Geneva-on-the-Lake and the Quail Hollow Resort.

What to Buy in Ashtabula County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Ashtabula County has several local products that make great souvenirs. Visitors can purchase locally made wine, cheese, and maple syrup. The county also has a thriving arts community, and visitors can purchase unique and handmade items from local artists and artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Ashtabula County

The people of Ashtabula County are known for their friendly and hospitable nature. Visitors are encouraged to engage with the locals and experience the region’s culture firsthand.

Best Time to Visit Ashtabula County

The best time to visit Ashtabula County is from May to October when the weather is pleasant, and the outdoor attractions are open.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ashtabula County

Do:

  • Explore the outdoors and take advantage of the region’s many natural wonders.
  • Take a wine country tour and taste the local wines.
  • Bring home some locally made products and souvenirs.

Don’t:

  • Litter or harm the natural environment.
  • Disrespect the locals or their customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ashtabula County

Ashtabula County is generally safe for visitors. However, visitors should practice normal safety precautions, such as not leaving valuables in their cars and staying in well-lit areas at night.

Health in Ashtabula County

Ashtabula County has several medical facilities for visitors who need medical attention. Visitors are encouraged to have travel insurance.

Getting to Ashtabula County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ashtabula County

Cleveland Hopkins International Airport is the closest airport to Ashtabula County. It is located approximately 60 miles from the county.

Bus Stations Near Ashtabula County

The closest bus station is the Greyhound Bus Station in Cleveland, located approximately 60 miles from Ashtabula County.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Ashtabula County, as it is located within driving distance of several major cities, including Cleveland, Buffalo, and Pittsburgh.

How to Get Around in Ashtabula County

The best way to get around Ashtabula County is by car. Visitors can rent a car from the airport or drive their own car to the county.

Conclusion

Ashtabula County is a charming destination with plenty to offer visitors. It has a rich history, natural beauty, delicious cuisine, and friendly locals. With this comprehensive guide, visitors can explore the best of Ashtabula County and make the most of their trip.
