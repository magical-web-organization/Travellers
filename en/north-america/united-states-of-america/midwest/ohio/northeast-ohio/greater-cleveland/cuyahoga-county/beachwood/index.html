Introduction to Beachwood

Located in Cuyahoga County in Northeast Ohio, Beachwood is a charming city that offers visitors a unique mix of urban landscape and natural beauty. Known for its thriving business community, Beachwood is also home to an abundance of cultural landmarks, recreational activities, and local gems that are just waiting to be explored. Whether you’re looking to reconnect with nature, indulge in local flavors, or dive into history and heritage, this vibrant city has something for you.

List of Places to Visit in Beachwood

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Beachwood is steeped in history and boasts several museums and historical places that offer a glimpse into the city’s past.

Maltz Museum of Jewish Heritage

This museum chronicles the Jewish experience from Biblical times to the present day, showcasing art, artifacts, and interactive exhibits that explore the history, culture, and traditions of Jewish people.

Beachwood Historical Society Museum

Housed in an 1876 schoolhouse, the Beachwood Historical Society Museum is a fascinating blend of local history and unique exhibits. The museum is open seasonally and features a range of exhibits, including a re-creation of a WWII Beachwood living room and a replica of a General Store.

Natural Attractions of Beachwood: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

With an abundance of parks and natural reserves, Beachwood is an outdoor lover’s paradise.

Acacia Reservation

This 155-acre nature preserve is a perfect destination for a peaceful day out in the fresh air. Wander through the tranquil woods, hike on one of the many trails, or just relax and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

Beachwood City Park East

Located in the heart of the city, this park covers over 100 acres of green space and offers a wide range of facilities and amenities, including playgrounds, walking trails, basketball courts, and picnic areas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Beachwood

Whether you’re an experienced hiker or just looking for a leisurely bike ride, Beachwood has plenty of options for exploring nature in this stunning setting.

North Chagrin Reservation

Located just a few miles from Beachwood, this 1,700-acre park features over 15 miles of hiking trails, a nature center, and stunning waterfalls.

Beachwood Community Center Trail

This 1.5-mile paved trail is perfect for a leisurely walk, jog, or bike ride. The trail winds through the scenic community center park and offers views of the wooded surroundings.

Best Things to Do in Beachwood: Activities and Tours

From food tours to outdoor adventures, there are plenty of activities in Beachwood to keep visitors entertained.

L’Nique Specialty Linen Rentals

Unleash your creative spirit and design the perfect tablescape! L’Nique Specialty Linen Rentals offers guests the unique opportunity to select designer linen, table runners, napkins, and chair covers for creating their one-of-a-kind dining experience at home.

Escape Hunt Cleveland

The Escape Hunt Experience is an immersive real-life game where players are locked in a themed room and must solve challenging puzzles to escape before time runs out. Put your wits to the test at Escape Hunt Cleveland.

Week-Long Itinerary for Beachwood

Day 1: Explore the rich Jewish history at the Maltz Museum and enjoy a laid-back lunch at Beachwood’s finest, Corky & Lenny’s.

Day 2: Start off with a morning nature walk at Acacia Reservation then indulge in an afternoon wine tasting at Heinemann Winery

Day 3: Take a bike ride down the Beachwood Community Center Trail and then spend the afternoon exploring the exhibits at the Beachwood Historical Society Museum.

Day 4: Spend the day in nature at North Chagrin Reservation, take a hike through the woods and visit the stunning waterfalls.

Day 5: Curate the perfect tablescape at L’Nique Specialty Linen Rentals for a unique and personalized dining experience at home.

Day 6: Put your puzzle solving skills to the test at Escape Hunt Cleveland, then head out for a delicious dinner at The Capital Grille.

Day 7: Rediscover the charm of the city at Beachwood City Park East, with walking trails, basketball courts and an area for playing sports.

Food and Drink Scene of Beachwood

Must-Try Dishes

Beachwood is a gastronomic destination that prides itself on its eclectic cuisine and local flavors.

Corned Beef on Rye at Corky & Lenny’s

If you’re a fan of corned beef, don’t miss the chance to try the famous Corned Beef on Rye at Corky & Lenny’s, which has been the talk of the town for over 60 years.

Brisket Burger at Cedar Creek Grille

Satisfy your burger craving with the mouthwatering Brisket Burger at the Cedar Creek Grille, made with brisket, Chuck, and short rib, then topped with bacon and caramelized onions.

Local Beverages

Heinemann Winery

Located in picturesque Ohio Wine Country, Heinemann Winery offers guests the chance to sample delicious wines, while surrounded by scenic countryside views.

Cleveland Whiskey

Sip the perfect Manhattan made with the award-winning bourbon whiskey from Cleveland Whiskey, which ages their whiskey in American oak barrels and is distilled from locally sourced ingredients.

Where to Stay in Beachwood: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Whether you prefer luxury or a budget-friendly option, Beachwood has a wide range of accommodation options to suit all tastes and budgets.

Embassy Suites Cleveland Beachwood

Experience luxurious amenities at this stylish hotel, including a fitness center, indoor pool, hot tub, and complimentary breakfast cooked to order.

Hotel Indigo Cleveland Beachwood

Located in the heart of Beachwood, the Hotel Indigo is a stylish boutique hotel with a gym and indoor pool. Enjoy a delicious meal at the on-site restaurant and bar.

What to Buy in Beachwood: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Bring home unique mementos of Beachwood to remind you of your trip.

The Horowitz Family Company

This artisanal food company offers gourmet sweets, spreads, and condiments made with natural and locally sourced ingredients. Take home a jar of the delicious preserves or the award-winning barbecue sauce.

Shop at Beachwood Place

This upscale mall features over 170 of the world’s top brands and local stores. Find unique souvenirs, from fashion to personal care items, at Beachwood Place.

Local Culture and Customs of Beachwood

Beachwood’s Neighborly Person to Person Service

Beachwood is dedicated to providing residents and visitors with exceptional customer service, known as N2PS (Neighborly Person to Person Service).

The Greater Cleveland Aquarium

The Greater Cleveland Aquarium is a non-profit educational attraction dedicated to inspiring people to save the Great Lakes and protect the world’s oceans.

Best Time to Visit Beachwood

The best time to visit Beachwood is from May to October when the weather is perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Beachwood

Do:

  • Explore the city’s cultural landmarks and natural attractions
  • Indulge in the delectable local cuisine
  • Experience the city’s neighborly service firsthand

Don’t:

  • Miss the chance to explore the hidden gems of Ohio Wine Country
  • Forget to pack for outdoor adventures
  • Leave without taking home a jar of The Horowitz Family Company’s delicious spread

Safety and Health

Safety in Beachwood

Beachwood is a safe and welcoming city, but visitors should still be mindful of their surroundings and take basic precautions such as locking their doors and not leaving valuable possessions visible in their cars.

Health in Beachwood

Beachwood boasts high-quality healthcare facilities that offer world-class care and service.

Getting to Beachwood: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Beachwood

The Cleveland Hopkins International Airport is the closest airport to Beachwood, located just 25 minutes away.

Bus Stations Near Beachwood

The Greyhound Bus Station in Cleveland is located just a few miles from Beachwood.

Other Options

To get around Beachwood, you may need to rent a car or use a taxi service.

How to Get Around in Beachwood

Beachwood is a perfect destination for exploring on foot, bike or car.

The Suburban Mobility Authority for Regional Transportation (SMART)

An easy and inexpensive way to get around town is through SMART buses which offer comfortable air-conditioned, non-smoking buses.

Where to Go Next from Beachwood: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Cleveland

Just 20 minutes from Beachwood, Cleveland is a vibrant metropolis that offers a range of attractions, including the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, Cleveland Zoo, and a thriving arts and cultural scene.

Lake Erie

Lake Erie is the fourth-largest Great Lake and is located just 30 minutes away from Beachwood. Explore the gorgeous lakeside scenery or enjoy water activities, including boating, fishing, and kayaking.

Conclusion

There’s no shortage of things to do and see in Beachwood, Ohio. From its natural beauty and recreational activities to its thriving food scene and cultural landmarks, this vibrant city offers visitors a unique, unforgettable experience. Start planning your trip today to discover the hidden gems nestled in the heart of Ohio.
