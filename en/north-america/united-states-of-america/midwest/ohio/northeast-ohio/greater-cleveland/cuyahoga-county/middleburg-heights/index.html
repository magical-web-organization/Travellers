Introduction to Middleburg Heights

Located in Cuyahoga County in Greater Cleveland, Northeast Ohio, Middleburg Heights is a peaceful suburb with a great mix of modernity and natural beauty. It has a small-town feel, but it is just a stone’s throw from the bustling city of Cleveland. The town offers an array of attractions and activities for tourists of all ages, from hiking and biking trails to museums and historical landmarks. When planning your trip to Middleburg Heights, consider visiting these must-see places and trying these exciting activities.

List of Places to Visit in Middleburg Heights

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Baldwin Wallace University German Language and Culture Center: This cultural center offers visitors a chance to learn more about German culture, history, and language.

  • Museum of Divine Statues: This museum features a collection of antique religious statues and artifacts from Europe and Asia. It is a great place to marvel at some of the world’s most beautiful works of art.

  • Middleburg Heights Historical Society Museum: This museum showcases the history of Middleburg Heights and its surrounding areas. Visitors can see displays of artifacts, photographs, and other memorabilia about the town’s past.

Natural Attractions of Middleburg Heights: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Big Creek Reservation: Visitors can hike through the serene forests, fishing, and explore the creeks that are teeming with wildlife.

  • Mill Stream Run Reservation: This is one of the most popular natural attractions in the area. It features a wide range of outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, horseback riding, and more.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Middleburg Heights

Middleburg Heights has an abundance of hiking and biking trails. Here are some of the best:

  • Rocky River Reservation: This park offers 7.6 miles of hiking trails and 8.9 miles of biking trails. It also has a picnic area where visitors can enjoy a scenic lunch.

  • South Chagrin Reservation: This reservation features 10.1 miles of hiking and biking trails, as well as a golf course and stables for horseback riding.

Best Things to Do in Middleburg Heights: Activities and Tours

  • The Jump Yard: This indoor trampoline park offers a fun and safe way for visitors of all ages to jump, bounce, and play.

  • Laser Quest: This is a great place for group activities. Visitors can play laser tag, enjoy arcade games, and more.

  • Cahoon-Memorial Park: This park offers a range of activities such as hiking and biking trails and a playground for kids.

Week-Long Itinerary for Middleburg Heights

Day 1: Visit the Museum of Divine Statues and explore the creeks and forests at Big Creek Reservation.

Day 2: Hike the trails at Mill Stream Run Reservation and play laser tag at Laser Quest.

Day 3: Take a tour of Baldwin Wallace University German Language and Culture Centre, enjoy a picnic lunch at the Rocky River Reservation.

Day 4: Visit the Middleburg Heights Historical Society Museum and go horseback riding at South Chagrin Reservation.

Day 5: Play sports at Cahoon-Memorial Park and explore the town’s local food scene.

Day 6: Bike through the city’s scenic routes and purchase local products at the shops.

Day 7: Attend the annual Middleburg Heights festival and enjoy the bustling atmosphere of the town.

Food and Drink Scene of Middleburg Heights

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sloppy Joe’s: This is a local favorite dish that consists of ground beef cooked in a sweet and tangy sauce.

  • Perogies at Sokolowski’s University: Sokolowski’s University serves the best perogies in Cleveland.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beers at Fat Head’s Brewery: Try Fat Head’s craft beers and catch a game or two, making for a modern pub experience.

  • Wine at 750ml Wines: 750ml Wines provides a unique atmosphere which is perfect for wine tasting and purchasing.

Where to Stay in Middleburg Heights: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Sonesta ES Suites Cleveland Airport: This is our top recommendation where you can enjoy a spacious room, a complimentary breakfast buffet, and a fitness center.

  • Red Roof Inn Cleveland Airport: This is a budget-friendly option hotel near popular tourist sites and has excellent dining options, a comfortable bed, and great amenities.

What to Buy in Middleburg Heights: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Pierogies at Sokolowski’s: Bring home some of the best pierogies in Cleveland as a tasty souvenir.

  • Cleveland Indians Merchandise: Visit Target or Dick’s Sporting Goods for unique baseball-themed souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Middleburg Heights

  • The locals of Middleburg Heights are friendly and welcoming. Visitors are expected to reciprocate the friendliness.

Best Time to Visit Middleburg Heights

The best time to visit Middleburg Heights is during the late spring or early summer months when the weather is clear and mild.

Do’s and Don’ts of Middleburg Heights

Do:

  • Do explore the natural beauty of the area by visiting the hiking trails and parks.

  • Do try some Cleveland’s most renowned foods, such as Sloppy Joe’s and pierogies.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or disturb the local ecosystem.

  • Don’t miss out on local festivals and events. Make sure to take part in the town’s cultural experiences.

Safety and Health

Safety in Middleburg Heights

Middleburg Heights is a safe town with a low crime rate. Visitors are advised to take necessary precautions, such as avoiding dark streets at night and securing valuable belongings.

Health in Middleburg Heights

Middleburg Heights has a range of medical facilities and clinics that provide excellent health care services for locals and visitors.

Getting to Middleburg Heights: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Middleburg Heights

  • Cleveland Hopkins International Airport: Cleveland Hopkins airport is 4 miles from Middleburg Heights.

Bus Stations Near Middleburg Heights

  • Greyhound Station: The Greyhound station is located in Cleveland, approximately 10 miles from Middleburg Heights.

Other Options

  • Rental Cars: Rental cars are widely available near the airport and other transportation hubs.

How to Get Around in Middleburg Heights

Middleburg Heights is easy to navigate on foot or by bike. Taxis and ride-sharing services are available as well for longer distances.

Where to Go Next from Middleburg Heights: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Cleveland: Cleveland is a city located only a few miles from Middleburg Heights and offers a range of tourist hotspots like Cleveland Museum of Art and Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.

  • Cedar Point Amusement Park: Just 1.5 hours from Middleburg Heights, visit Cedar Point Amusement Park, which offers some of the best roller coasters and family-friendly activities in the region.

Conclusion

Middleburg Heights is a fantastic town to visit for anyone looking for a mixture of culture and nature. Visitors can enjoy hiking and biking trails, explore cultural landmarks and museums, sample the local cuisine, and much more. It is the perfect destination for a summer vacation or long weekend getaway.
