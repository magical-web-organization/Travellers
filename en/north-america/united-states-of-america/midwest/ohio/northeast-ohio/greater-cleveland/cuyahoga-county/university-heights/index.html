Introduction to University Heights

Located in Cuyahoga County, Greater Cleveland, Northeast Ohio, University Heights is a city in Ohio, Midwest, United States of America, North America. This city has a vibrant college town feel, as it is home to John Carroll University. University Heights’ population is around 13,000 people and it covers an area of 1.8 square miles. There are many places to visit in University Heights, including museums, parks, and restaurants.

List of Places to Visit in University Heights

If you are planning to visit University Heights, here are some places you should not miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Maltz Museum of Jewish Heritage: This museum explores the history, culture, and traditions of Jewish people in Northeast Ohio.

  • Cleveland Museum of Art: The world-renowned Cleveland Museum of Art is home to over 61,000 artworks from different parts of the world, spanning over 5,000 years.

  • Western Reserve Historical Society: This museum features exhibits and artifacts that tell the story of the Western Reserve in Northeast Ohio.

Natural Attractions of University Heights: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Fairmount Circle: This area is the heart of University Heights and is home to many restaurants, shops, and cafes.

  • South Taylor Road: Home to the popular Cedar-Lee district and bustling with activity, this destination is a hub of shops, restaurants, and theaters.

  • Glenville Lake: This lake is a popular spot for birdwatchers and hikers, offering scenic views and a tranquil experience.

Hiking/Biking Routes of University Heights

University Heights offers many scenic and enjoyable hiking and biking routes for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best:

  • Beachwood Canyon Trail

  • Acacia Reservation Trail

  • Lakeview Park Trail

Best Things to Do in University Heights: Activities and Tours

University Heights has a range of activities for people of all ages. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Visit Cedar Lee Theatre: This historic theater shows a range of films, from independent movies to the latest blockbusters.

  • Cleveland Heights Community Center: This center offers a range of classes and activities for people of all ages and fitness levels.

  • The BottleHouse Brewery and Meadery: A small batch brewery, here you can taste and enjoy many different types of beer and mead.

Week-Long Itinerary for University Heights

If you plan to spend a week in University Heights, here’s an itinerary to follow:

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit the Cleveland Museum of Art and have lunch at one of the museums’ cafes.
Day 2 Visit the Maltz Museum of Jewish Heritage, followed by dinner at Cedar-Lee District or South Taylor Road.
Day 3 Explore the Acacia Reservation Trail and enjoy a picnic lunch at Glenville Lake.
Day 4 Attend a class at Cleveland Heights Community Center or visit the Cleveland Botanical Gardens.
Day 5 Visit the Western Reserve Historical Society, then enjoy dinner at Fairmount Circle.
Day 6 Go shopping at the Cleveland Heights or Beachwood Place Mall.
Day 7 Explore the Lakeview Park Trail and end your day with a beer tasting at The BottleHouse Brewery and Meadery.

Food and Drink Scene of University Heights

University Heights has a diverse food and drink scene that caters to all kinds of tastes. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pierogies: This Eastern European dish is a staple in Northeast Ohio and can be found at many restaurants.

  • Polish Boy: This sandwich features kielbasa, BBQ sauce, coleslaw, and french fries and is a popular choice at many local eateries.

  • Corned Beef Sandwiches: This is a Cleveland classic - delicious corned beef combined with tangy Swiss cheese and spicy mustard.

Local Beverages

  • Great Lakes Brewing Company: This brewery is known for its excellent beers, such as the Dortmunder Gold and Eliot Ness Amber Ale.

  • Masthead Brewing Company: This brewery produces a range of IPAs, stouts, and other beers that can be enjoyed at their taproom.

Where to Stay in University Heights: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

University Heights has a range of accommodation options for travelers of all kinds. Here are some of the best:

  • Homewood Suites by Hilton Cleveland-Beachwood: This hotel offers spacious rooms and a range of amenities, including an indoor pool and fitness center.

  • Courtyard by Marriott Cleveland-Beachwood: This hotel offers comfortable rooms, free Wi-Fi, and easy access to many of University Heights’ top attractions.

  • The Glidden House: This guesthouse is a historic mansion that has been converted into a luxurious inn. The rooms are elegant and feature modern amenities.

What to Buy in University Heights: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs or local products to take home, here are some things you might be interested in:

  • Polish Pottery: Beautiful and colorful pottery can be found at many local gift shops.

  • T-shirts and Hoodies: University Heights has a range of t-shirts and sweatshirts available at their gift shops and souvenir stores.

Local Culture and Customs of University Heights

University Heights is a diverse and welcoming city, known for its vibrant arts scene and friendly people. The local culture includes many different ethnic groups who have contributed to the city’s unique character. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit University Heights

The best time to visit University Heights is from May to September, when the weather is warm and many outdoor activities can be enjoyed. However, the city has a range of activities and attractions that can be enjoyed year-round.

Do’s and Don’ts of University Heights

Do:

  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions.

  • Take advantage of the city’s arts scene.

  • Try the local cuisine.

Don’t:

  • Be disrespectful to locals or other visitors.

  • Litter or damage public property.

  • Engage in illegal activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in University Heights

University Heights is a relatively safe city overall. Visitors should still be aware of their surroundings and take necessary precautions like securing their belongings and not walking or being alone late at night.

Health in University Heights

University Heights has high health standards with an extensive network of hospitals and medical centers available in case of emergency.

Getting to University Heights: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near University Heights

  • Cleveland Hopkins International Airport

  • Akron-Canton Regional Airport

Bus Stations Near University Heights

  • Greyhound Bus Lines

  • Cleveland RTA

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Visitors can rent a car from any of the car rental agencies available at the Cleveland Hopkins International Airport.

  • Taxi or Uber: Taxis and Uber are available for visitors who need transportation around the city.

How to Get Around in University Heights

Visitors can get around University Heights easily by renting a car, taking a taxi, or riding the Cleveland RTA. Walking and biking are also popular options.

Where to Go Next from University Heights: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you want to explore nearby destinations after visiting University Heights, here are some places you should check out:

  • Cleveland: Known as “The Rock and Roll Capital of the World,” Cleveland is home to a range of cultural attractions, including The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame and the Cleveland Museum of Natural History.

  • Cedar Point: This amusement park is a popular destination for families, with thrilling rides and fun attractions.

Conclusion

University Heights is a vibrant and diverse city with plenty of attractions for visitors to explore. From museums and hiking trails to great food and drink, this city offers something for everyone. Plan your trip accordingly and experience all that University Heights has to offer.
