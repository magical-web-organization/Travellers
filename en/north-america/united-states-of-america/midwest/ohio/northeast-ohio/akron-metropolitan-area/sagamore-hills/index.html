Introduction to Sagamore Hills

Sagamore Hills is a beautiful village located in the Akron Metropolitan Area, in Northeast Ohio, the Midwest region of the United States of America. It is surrounded by stunning landscapes that offer plenty of outdoor activities and breathtaking views. If you’re planning to visit Sagamore Hills, this comprehensive tour guide will provide you with all the information you need.

List of Places to Visit in Sagamore Hills

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Cuyahoga Valley National Park

Cuyahoga Valley National Park is located between Akron and Cleveland and is a unique treasure in Northeast Ohio. This national park features more than 125 miles of hiking trails where you can see Ohio’s most astonishing landscapes and wildlife.

Brandywine Falls

A must-visit destination in Sagamore Hills is Brandywine Falls, a lovely 65-foot waterfall that flows into Cuyahoga River. The site offers several walking paths that lead you close to the waterfall, and several observation decks that provide a closer view.

Blossom Music Center

Blossom Music Center is an outdoor concert venue that hosts several events throughout the year. The amphitheatre is in the middle of Cuyahoga Valley National Park and housed by the Cleveland Orchestra. It is the perfect place to enjoy music, and the view of the Cuyahoga River valley is breathtaking.

Natural Attractions of Sagamore Hills: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Bike and Hiking Trail

The Bike and Hiking Trail is a perfect opportunity to explore the gorgeous Ohio scenery while enjoying hiking, biking, or running. It is 17.4 miles long and runs through Summit County, from Akron to Cleveland.

Blue Hen Falls

Located in the Cuyahoga Valley National Park, Blue Hen Falls is one of the most beautiful and peaceful places in Sagamore Hills. The 15-foot waterfall offers a scenic backdrop for photography, nature walks, or a peaceful picnic.

Virginia Kendall Ledges

The Virginia Kendall Ledges are a unique geological formation located in Cuyahoga Valley National Park. The ledges are made up of Sharon conglomerate, and it is a popular tourist spot and great for rock climbing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sagamore Hills

Ledges Trail

The Ledges Trail is a 2.2-mile loop that takes you to the top of the Virginia Kendall Ledges. It provides a breathtaking view of the northeast Ohio landscape and is a great place for adventurers who love to hike.

Buckeye Trail

The Buckeye Trail in Sagamore Hills provides an opportunity to explore the Cuyahoga Valley National Park. It is a 9.3-mile hike, and it takes you from the parking lot near Ohio 303 along the Buckeye Trail.

Best Things to Do in Sagamore Hills: Activities and Tours

Kayaking in Cuyahoga River

The Cuyahoga River is one of the most scenic rivers in Ohio, and kayaking down the river is a thrilling experience. Several local rental companies offer kayaking, so you can explore a different side of Sagamore Hills.

Segway Tour

A fun and unique way to explore Sagamore Hills is by taking a Segway tour. The tour takes you around the Cuyahoga Valley National Park in style; you can explore the village from a different perspective and enjoy stunning views that you won’t forget.

Food and Drink Scene of Sagamore Hills

Must-Try Dishes

Ohio Buckeye Candy

Ohio Buckeye Candy is a sweet treat that originated from Ohio. It is made by shaping peanut butter mixture into balls and dipping it in chocolate.

Pierogies

Pierogi is an old-world dish from Poland that is traditional to many Midwestern states, and Ohio is no different. They are delicious pockets of dough filled with potatoes, cheese, or sauerkraut.

Local Beverages

Great Lakes Brewing Company

Great Lakes Brewing Company founded in 1988 and since then, it has continued to grow and become one of the area’s favorite beer companies. They have a variety of beers in their menu, seasonal and classic.

Wineries

Ohio produces several exceptional wines, and Sagamore Hills offers wine tastings from different wineries in the area. Ohio is known for their sweet wines.

Where to Stay in Sagamore Hills: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Sagamore Hills has various accommodations you can choose from, whether you’re traveling on a budget or want something more luxurious.

The Courtyard by Marriott Akron Stow is a fantastic option if you’re looking for a luxurious place to stay. The hotel has several amenities, including a fitness center, indoor pool, and a bistro serving Starbucks coffee.

Hampton Inn Akron-Fairlawn

Hampton Inn Akron-Fairlawn offers welcoming and comfortable accommodation with excellent customer service. It has amenities like complimentary breakfast and fitness center.

What to Buy in Sagamore Hills: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Cuyahoga Valley National Park Merchandise

Several souvenir shops around Sagamore Hills offer a variety of merchandise, including Cuyahoga Valley National Park’s branded gear and merchandise such as t-shirts, hats, and stickers.

Ohio-made Crafts

Many craftsmen in Sagamore Hills sell homemade crafts at the local arts and crafts shows. Items for sale include handmade jewelry, pottery, and wooden items.

Local Culture and Customs of Sagamore Hills

Sagamore Hills is laidback and casual, and the people are friendly and welcoming. It is crucial to show respect to local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Sagamore Hills

The best time to visit Sagamore Hills is from May to September when the weather is warm and perfect for outdoor activities.
