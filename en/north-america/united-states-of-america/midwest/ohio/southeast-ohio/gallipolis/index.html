A Comprehensive Guide to Gallipolis, Ohio

Located in Southeast Ohio, Gallipolis is a charming small town that offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of big cities. The town is situated on the Ohio River and is known for its rich history, outdoor adventures, and local culture. In this comprehensive guide, we will cover all the must-visit places and things to do in Gallipolis, from exploring its cultural and natural attractions to hiking and biking routes, where to eat, stay, and shop.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Our House Museum

Your visit to Gallipolis is incomplete without exploring its rich history, and the Our House Museum is the perfect place to start. Built in the early 19th century, this beautiful Federal-style home was once the residence of Adam R. Johnson, a prominent politician, and businessman. Today, the house has been restored and turned into a museum where you can explore well-preserved artifacts and learn about the town’s history.

French Art Colony

The French Art Colony is a cultural gem in Gallipolis, which strives to promote the visual and decorative arts. Established in 1963, the colony features a gallery where you can explore unique art collections, take part in artist workshops, and attend art events.

Natural Attractions of Gallipolis: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Wahkeena Nature Preserve

If you want to explore the beauty of nature in Gallipolis, Wahkeena Nature Preserve is the place to be. The 150-acre preserve is home to a wide variety of flora and fauna, including rare native species that you won’t find elsewhere. You can join guided tours to explore the trails and learn about the diverse ecosystem of the preserve.

Gallipolis City Park

Gallipolis City Park is a beautiful urban park that offers various recreational activities for visitors. The park includes a swimming pool, skate park, playgrounds, pavilions, and more. You can enjoy a peaceful walk in the greenery or have a fun day out with your family and friends.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Gallipolis

Ohio River Scenic Byway

The Ohio River Scenic Byway is an excellent option if you want to explore the beautiful natural scenery of Gallipolis while driving. The 52-mile route offers stunning views of the Ohio River and its banks, and you can stop at several points to take in the beauty of the region.

Buckeye Trail

The Buckeye Trail is around 1,444 miles long and passes through many scenic locations, including Gallipolis. The trail is perfect for a day-long hike or a multi-day backpacking trip, depending on your preference, and offers opportunities to explore the region’s scenic beauty.

Best Things to Do in Gallipolis: Activities and Tours

Ariel Opera House

The historical Ariel Opera House is a treat for music lovers, presenting a variety of musical performances throughout the year. The opera house prides itself on hosting nationally renowned musicians, theater productions, and special events.

Gallipolis River Recreational Festival

June is a fun time in Gallipolis where the Gallipolis River Recreational Festival stands as the town’s largest annual event. The festival has been celebrated since 1963 and features a wide variety of activities like music entertainment, food vendors, bike races, and more. It’s the perfect way to experience the local culture and celebrate the town’s heritage.

Week-Long Itinerary for Gallipolis

Day 1: Visit French Art Colony and Wahkeena Nature Preserve to explore the local art scene and enjoy the beautiful natural scenery.

Day 2: Explore the town’s history with a visit to the Our House Museum, the Gallia County Historical Society Museum, and the Silver Bridge Memorial.

Day 3: Take a bike ride on the Buckeye Trail or enjoy the scenic drive along the Ohio River Scenic Byway.

Day 4: Take a break and spend a relaxing day at the Gallipolis City Park, enjoying the pool, skate park, and other fun activities.

Day 5: Attend a musical performance at the Ariel Opera House or other music events in town.

Day 6: Join a local tour guide and discover the hidden gems of Gallipolis, such as local markets and art galleries.

Day 7: Spend the last day shopping for souvenirs and exploring the local food and drink scene.

Where to Stay in Gallipolis: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hampton Inn Gallipolis

What to Buy in Gallipolis: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Ohio Valley Defense Products

Ohio Valley Defense Products is a store that specializes in self-defense products like stun guns, pepper spray, and more. You can also find other unique souvenirs here, making it the perfect place to shop for gifts and memorabilia.

Ariel-Ann Carson Dater Performing Arts Centre Gift Shop

If you want to support local artists, head to the Ariel-Ann Carson Dater Performing Arts Centre Gift Shop. The shop features unique and beautiful art pieces produced by the local community and other artists.

Local Culture and Customs of Gallipolis

The locals in Gallipolis are known for their friendly and welcoming nature, making it easy for visitors to immerse themselves in the local culture. You can expect to find a deep appreciation of the town’s history and heritage, and you’ll find that Gallipolis has a proud tradition of music, with a wide variety of genres represented.

Best Time to Visit Gallipolis

The best time to visit Gallipolis is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm, and the town is alive with events and festivals. Spring and fall are also good times to visit, with the town’s natural beauty on full display.

Safety and Health

Safety in Gallipolis
