Introduction to Southeast Ohio

Tucked away in the rolling Appalachian hills of Ohio, Southeast Ohio is the perfect destination for those seeking a break from the hustle and bustle of the city. The region is known for its scenic landscape, rich history, and unique culture. Whether you’re a fan of outdoor adventures, rich history, or local cuisine, Southeast Ohio has something to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Southeast Ohio

  1. Hocking Hills State Park
  2. The Wilds
  3. Ohio University
  4. Athens County Historical Society and Museum
  5. The Dairy Barn Arts Center
  6. Serpent Mound
  7. Burr Oak State Park
  8. Lake Hope State Park
  9. Wayne National Forest
  10. Little Cities of Black Diamonds

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Southeast Ohio is a treasure trove of museums and historical sites, offering a glimpse into the region’s rich history. Some of the must-visit cultural attractions include:

  1. Athens County Historical Society and Museum: This museum showcases Athens County’s rich history through exhibits, artifacts, and archived materials.

  2. The Dairy Barn Arts Center: This center features a range of exhibits, workshops, and programs that celebrate art and culture in Southeast Ohio.

  3. Ohio University: This university is not only a world-renowned academic institution but also showcases the region’s cultural heritage through its architecture and landmarks.

Natural Attractions of Southeast Ohio: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Southeast Ohio is a nature lover’s paradise, offering endless opportunities for outdoor adventures with its lush forests, expansive lakes, and breathtaking vistas.

  1. Hocking Hills State Park: Home to stunning waterfalls and rock formations, Hocking Hills is a must-visit for outdoor enthusiasts.

  2. The Wilds: This 10,000-acre wildlife conservation center offers a range of outdoor activities, including safaris, horseback riding, and zip-lining.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Southeast Ohio

Southeast Ohio is a prime destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts with its numerous trails offering spectacular views. Some of the best routes include:

  1. Buckeye Trail: This 1,400-mile trail passes through the heart of Southeast Ohio and offers stunning views of the region’s landscape.

  2. Athens Bike Path: This 21-mile trail connects Athens city with surrounding communities and is perfect for leisurely bike rides.

Best Things to Do in Southeast Ohio: Activities and Tours

From exploring natural wonders to experiencing local culture, Southeast Ohio offers a range of activities and tours to engage visitors.

  1. Hocking Hills Canopy Tours: This zip-lining experience takes you through the lush canopy of Hocking Hills’ forests.

  2. The Athens Farmers Market: A vibrant local market offering fresh produce, artisan products, and entertainment.

Week-Long Itinerary for Southeast Ohio

Day 1: Visit Hocking Hills State Park and Hocking Hills Canopy Tours

Day 2: Explore Ohio University and visit Athens County Historical Society and Museum

Day 3: Take a trip to The Wilds and experience a safari expedition

Day 4: Visit Serpent Mound and Burr Oak State Park

Day 5: Explore Wayne National Forest and Lake Hope State Park

Day 6: Experience local culture at The Dairy Barn Arts Center and Athens Farmers Market

Day 7: Take a leisurely bike ride on the Athens Bike Path or hike Buckeye trail

Food and Drink Scene of Southeast Ohio

Southeast Ohio has a diverse food and beverage scene that blends local and international flavors, with an emphasis on fresh and locally sourced ingredients.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Buckeye Candy: These delicious balls of peanut butter fudge dipped in chocolate are a Southeast Ohio specialty.

  2. Paw Paw Fruit: A unique local delicacy with a tropical flavor that’s a must-try for visitors.

Local Beverages

  1. Jackie O’s Beer: This Athens-based brewery is known for its craft beer, made with locally sourced ingredients.

  2. Shade Winery: A family-owned vineyard producing delicious wines with local Ohio grapes.

Where to Stay in Southeast Ohio: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Southeast Ohio has an excellent range of accommodation options, catering to all budgets and preferences.

  1. Hocking Hills Cabins: These cozy cabins offer amazing views of the Hocking Hills State Park and are perfect for a romantic getaway.

  2. Ohio University Inn & Conference Center: A centrally located hotel that offers luxurious accommodations and facilities.

What to Buy in Southeast Ohio: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Pottery: Southeast Ohio is known for its rich pottery heritage, with skilled artisans producing beautiful pieces that make excellent souvenirs.

  2. Appalachian Quilts: These beautiful, handcrafted quilts are traditional to the region and make a thoughtful souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Southeast Ohio

Southeast Ohio’s culture is a blend of traditions brought by Appalachian settlers and indigenous cultures. The region is known for its music, art, and festivals, and visitors can immerse themselves in the local community by attending cultural events and exploring local art galleries.

Best Time to Visit Southeast Ohio

The best time to visit Southeast Ohio is during the fall season when the leaves begin to change colors resulting in a breathtaking natural scenery. Springtime is also a popular season for those who are interested in going on outdoor activities and witnessing the blooming of colorful flowers.

Do’s and Don’ts of Southeast Ohio

Do:

  1. Respect the local communities and their customs.

  2. Explore the region’s natural beauty and culture.

Don’t:

  1. Litter or cause damage to natural landmarks and trails.

  2. Disrespect the region’s culture and customs.

Safety and Health

Southeast Ohio is a safe destination, but it’s always good to take some precautions while traveling.

Safety in Southeast Ohio

  1. Be aware of weather conditions, especially during the winter season.

  2. Stay alert and cautious while hiking and biking.

Health in Southeast Ohio

  1. Bring sunscreen, Mosquito repellant, and other necessary accessories while going for outdoor activities.

  2. Make sure to have health insurance before going on a trip.

Getting to Southeast Ohio: Transportation Options and Logistics

Southeast Ohio can be reached by road, air, and bus connections. Some of the popular options are:

Airports Near Southeast Ohio

  1. John Glenn Columbus International Airport (CMH)

  2. Rickenbacker International Airport (LCK)

Bus Stations Near Southeast Ohio

  1. Athens Bus Terminal

  2. Megabus Terminal Columbus

Other Options

Renting a car is the most popular option for traveling in Southeast Ohio as it gives you the flexibility and freedom to explore the region at your pace.

How to Get Around in Southeast Ohio

Public transportation is limited in Southeast Ohio, and renting a car is highly recommended for exploring the region. Alternatively, visitors can opt for taxi services or private tours.

Where to Go Next from Southeast Ohio: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Southeast Ohio is located near several exciting destinations, including:

  1. Cincinnati, Ohio

  2. Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania

  3. Louisville, Kentucky

Conclusion

Southeast Ohio is a hidden gem that offers a wealth of natural and cultural experiences. With its lush forests, stunning landscapes, and unique cuisine, Southeast Ohio is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for an escape from the city. So pack your bags and explore the best this region has to offer.
