Introduction to Jackson

Jackson is a charming city nestled in Southeast Ohio and is part of the Midwest region of the United States. Known as the “Apple Capital of Ohio,” Jackson is surrounded by lush countryside and rolling hills, making it a popular destination for nature enthusiasts, history buffs, and foodies alike.

With so many things to see and do, planning a trip to Jackson can be overwhelming. That’s why we’ve created this comprehensive travel guide to help you make the most of your visit. From must-visit attractions and natural wonders to unbeatable food and drink spots, we’ve got you covered.

List of Places to Visit in Jackson

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Leo Petroglyphs State Memorial

Located just a few miles east of Jackson, the Leo Petroglyphs State Memorial is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in history. This sacred site features over 37 prehistoric rock carvings believed to have been made over 1,000 years ago by prehistoric Native Americans.

Lillian E. Jones Museum

The Lillian E. Jones Museum is located in the heart of Jackson and is dedicated to preserving the city’s rich history. Visitors can explore exhibits that showcase artifacts from Jackson’s early years, including furniture, clothing, and photographs.

Natural Attractions of Jackson: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lake Katherine State Nature Preserve

Located just a few miles from Jackson, Lake Katherine State Nature Preserve is an outdoor enthusiast’s paradise. This 232-acre nature preserve features hiking trails, fishing, and wildlife watching opportunities. Don’t miss the chance to see the breathtaking Wildcat Hollow Trail, which is known for its stunning views of the countryside.

Jackson Lake State Park

Jackson Lake State Park is a popular destination for swimming, boating, and camping. With a sandy beach, fishing opportunities, and miles of hiking trails, this beautiful state park has something for everyone.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jackson

Bob Evans Farms Trail

The Bob Evans Farms Trail is an 8-mile trail that starts at the Bob Evans Farms restaurant and winds through the beautiful Ohio countryside. This easy-to-moderate trail is perfect for hiking, biking, and horseback riding.

Best Things to Do in Jackson: Activities and Tours

Canter’s Cave 4-H Camp

For a unique adventure, head to Canter’s Cave 4-H Camp. This beautiful wilderness retreat offers a variety of activities, including ziplining, archery, and hiking. Visitors can also stay in rustic cabins and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Food and Drink Scene of Jackson

Must-Try Dishes

Apple Butter

No trip to Jackson is complete without trying the city’s most famous food: apple butter. Made from locally grown apples, this sweet spread is the perfect accompaniment to toast, crackers, and pancakes.

Local Beverages

Iron City Draft House

Iron City Draft House is a casual dining spot that offers a wide selection of craft beers from local breweries. Visitors can also enjoy classic pub fare, including burgers and wings.

Where to Stay in Jackson: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Quality Inn

The Quality Inn in Jackson offers comfortable, affordable accommodations and is conveniently located near many of the city’s top attractions. Amenities include free breakfast and Wi-Fi, as well as an indoor pool.

What to Buy in Jackson: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Jackson Apple Festival

If you happen to visit Jackson during the fall, be sure to check out the Jackson Apple Festival. This annual event celebrates the city’s apple heritage and features a variety of food, crafts, and live entertainment.

Local Culture and Customs of Jackson

Jackson prides itself on its strong sense of community, and visitors will find that locals are friendly and welcoming. Be sure to take some time to explore the city’s rich history and try the local cuisine.

Best Time to Visit Jackson

The best time to visit Jackson is during the fall when the leaves change color and the city comes alive with apple festivals and other fall activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Jackson

Do:

  • Explore the natural beauty of the Ohio countryside.
  • Try the local food and drink.
  • Take time to learn about the city’s history and culture.

Don’t:

  • Overlook the smaller, lesser-known attractions.
  • Miss out on a chance to attend the Jackson Apple Festival.
  • Forget to pack comfortable shoes for hiking and exploring.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jackson

Jackson is a safe city, but visitors should still be aware of their surroundings and take standard precautions. Keep valuables out of sight and be cautious when walking alone at night.

Health in Jackson

Visitors to Jackson should take standard health precautions, including washing hands regularly, staying hydrated, and wearing sunscreen and insect repellent when spending time outdoors.

Getting to Jackson: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Jackson

The closest airports to Jackson are the Port Columbus International Airport and the Ohio University Airport, each located about an hour’s drive away.

Bus Stations Near Jackson

The closest Greyhound bus station to Jackson is located in Chillicothe, Ohio, about 50 miles away.

Other Options

Many visitors choose to drive to Jackson, as the city is easily accessible via several major highways, including Interstate 77 and US Route 35.

How to Get Around in Jackson

The best way to get around Jackson is by car. However, visitors who don’t have access to a car can use rideshare services or taxis to get around the city.

Where to Go Next from Jackson: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Athens, Ohio

Located just 30 miles from Jackson, Athens is home to Ohio University and is known for its vibrant arts and music scenes.

Hocking Hills State Park

Hocking Hills State Park is located about an hour’s drive from Jackson and is a popular destination for hiking, camping, and outdoor adventure.

Conclusion

Jackson, Ohio, may be a small city, but it’s packed with history, natural beauty, and unbeatable food. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an adventurous outdoor experience, Jackson has something for everyone. Use this guide to plan your perfect trip to this charming Midwest destination.
