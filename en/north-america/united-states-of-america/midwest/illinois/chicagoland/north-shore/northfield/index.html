Introduction to Northfield

Northfield is a quaint village located 30 miles north of Chicago on the North Shore of Lake Michigan. The town is surrounded by lush greenery and offers visitors a perfect blend of natural beauty and urban convenience. It is known for its beautiful parks, museums, and historic buildings that reflect the town’s rich history.

List of Places to Visit in Northfield

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The Wagner Farm

The Wagner Farm is a well-preserved historic farmstead that offers visitors a glimpse into the farming life of the late 19th and early 20th centuries. It features a farmhouse, barn, and outbuildings, and offers tours, workshops, and demonstrations.

The Northfield Historical Society

The Northfield Historical Society is a must-visit attraction for history lovers. It features rotating exhibits, educational programs, and events that showcase the town’s rich history and cultural heritage.

Natural Attractions of Northfield: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The Skokie Lagoons

The Skokie Lagoons are a series of interconnected ponds and lagoons that offer visitors a variety of outdoor activities. From fishing and hiking to picnicking and kayaking, this picturesque area offers something for everyone.

The Northfield Park District

The Northfield Park District features several beautiful parks and recreation areas, including Clarkson Park, which boasts a playground, walking trails, athletic fields, and more.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Northfield

The North Shore Channel Trail and the Green Bay Trail are two popular biking and hiking routes in Northfield. The North Shore Channel Trail runs along the Chicago River, while the Green Bay Trail offers stunning views of Lake Michigan and the surrounding landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Northfield: Activities and Tours

Rent a Kayak or Canoe

One of the best ways to explore the Skokie Lagoons is by renting a kayak or canoe. Rentals are available at the Lagoons, and paddling around these beautiful bodies of water is sure to be a highlight of your trip.

Take a Bike Tour

A bike tour is a great way to explore the community surrounding Northfield. Several tour companies offer guided bike tours of the North Shore, with stops at several local landmarks and attractions.

Food and Drink Scene of Northfield

Must-Try Dishes

The Mustard Seed

The Mustard Seed is a classic American bistro that serves delicious dishes made with locally sourced ingredients. The menu changes seasonally, but be sure to try their signature dish, the Mustard Seed Burger.

Happ Inn Bar and Grill

The Happ Inn Bar and Grill offers a range of classic American dishes, from ribs and steaks to salads and sandwiches. Be sure to try their signature dish, the BBQ Baby Back Ribs.

Local Beverages

Sketchbook Brewing Co.

Sketchbook Brewing Co. is a local brewery that offers a variety of craft beers, from pale ales to stouts. They also host events and educational classes on beer brewing.

North Shore Distillery

North Shore Distillery is a small batch distillery that produces award-winning spirits, including gins, vodkas, and rums. They offer tours and tastings, and their on-site store sells bottles of their delicious products.

Where to Stay in Northfield: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

The North Shore Hotel

The North Shore Hotel is a historic property located in the heart of downtown Northfield. It features beautifully appointed rooms, a fitness center, and a restaurant.

Airbnb

Airbnb has several listings in Northfield, ranging from guest rooms in private homes to entire apartments.

What to Buy in Northfield: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Earth and Spice

Earth and Spice is a locally owned shop that features a variety of artisanal and organic spices, teas, and herbs. They also offer cooking classes and workshops.

Sterling Company

Sterling Company is a boutique shop that sells a variety of jewelry and home decor items. They also offer custom design services.

Local Culture and Customs of Northfield

Northfield is a friendly and welcoming community, known for its Midwestern charm and hospitality. Visitors are encouraged to take part in local events and activities, respect cultural and religious traditions, and be courteous to others.

Best Time to Visit Northfield

The best time to visit Northfield is from May to September, when the weather is warm and sunny, and outdoor activities are in full swing. The fall foliage is also a beautiful sight to see, making October and November another great time to visit.

Do’s and Don’ts of Northfield

Do:

  • Enjoy the local parks and natural attractions.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Respect cultural and religious traditions.
  • Be courteous to others.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural or historical landmarks.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  • Engage in activities that may cause harm or injury to others or yourself.

Safety and Health

Safety in Northfield

Northfield is a safe community with low crime rates. Visitors are advised to take the usual precautions when traveling, such as locking car doors and securing valuables.

Health in Northfield

Northfield has several healthcare facilities, including NorthShore University HealthSystem, which offers a range of medical services and specialties.

Getting to Northfield: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Northfield

The two closest airports to Northfield are O’Hare International Airport and Midway International Airport. Both airports offer a variety of ground transportation options, including taxis, car rentals, and public transportation.

Bus Stations Near Northfield

Several bus companies including Greyhound and Megabus have stations in Chicago, which is just a short train or taxi ride away from Northfield.

Other Options

The Northfield Metra station offers commuter rail service to and from Chicago.

How to Get Around in Northfield

Northfield is a walkable community, and many attractions and amenities are within walking distance of one another. Taxis and ride-sharing services are also available, as well as bike rentals from local stores and shops.

Where to Go Next from Northfield: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Chicago

Chicago is just a short train or taxi ride away from Northfield, and offers visitors a wealth of attractions and activities, from world-class museums and theaters to shopping and dining.

Lake Geneva

Lake Geneva is a beautiful resort town located in southeastern Wisconsin, and is just a short drive from Northfield. Visitors can enjoy swimming, boating, and hiking, as well as shopping and dining in the charming downtown area.

Conclusion

Northfield is a beautiful, vibrant community that offers visitors a perfect blend of natural beauty and urban convenience. With its rich history, beautiful parks and gardens, and diverse culinary and cultural offerings, Northfield is a destination that should not be missed.
