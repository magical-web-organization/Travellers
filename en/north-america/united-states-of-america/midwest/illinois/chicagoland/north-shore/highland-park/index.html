Highland Park Tour Guide: A Comprehensive Travel Guide

Located on the North Shore of Chicagoland, Highland Park is a suburban city in Lake County, Illinois. Known for its picturesque neighborhood, thriving art scene, and outdoor activities, Highland Park is a popular destination among tourists. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take you through the top attractions, things to do, places to stay, and more!

Local Culture and Customs of Highland Park

Highland Park has a unique culture that’s worth exploring. Some interesting local customs include:

  • (1) Shaking hands is the most common form of greeting.
  • (2) Proper attire is expected at high-end restaurants and theaters.
  • (3) Tipping 15-20% in the service industry is customary.

Best Time to Visit Highland Park

The best time to visit Highland Park is during the summer when the weather is pleasant, and outdoor activities are in full swing. Keep in mind that this is also the peak tourist season, and prices for accommodations and attractions may be higher.
