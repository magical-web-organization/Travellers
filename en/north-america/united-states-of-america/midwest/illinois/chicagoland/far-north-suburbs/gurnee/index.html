A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Gurnee, Illinois

Gurnee is a beautiful village located in the Far North Suburbs of Chicago in Lake County, Illinois. It is a unique destination that has something to offer everyone, from its historical museums and natural attractions to exciting theme parks and shopping centers. This comprehensive travel guide will provide you with all the information and details you need to plan your trip to Gurnee, Illinois.

Introduction to Gurnee

Gurnee is a vibrant community located close to Lake Michigan and is home to over 31,000 residents. This village is known for its exceptional quality of life, beautiful scenery, and an array of attractions that make it an excellent destination for travelers year-round. With easy access to Chicago and surrounding areas, Gurnee is a perfect location for a quick weekend getaway or an extended vacation.

List of Places to Visit in Gurnee

Gurnee is home to a plethora of attractions, and it can be challenging to know where to begin. To help you make the most of your trip, we have curated a list of the top places to visit in Gurnee that should not be missed.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Gurnee Historical Society and Museum: This museum is a must-visit destination for history buffs, providing a fascinating glimpse into the history and culture of Gurnee. The permanent collection includes artifacts and memorabilia from the village’s founding in 1928 to the present.
  2. Bittersweet Golf Course and Museum: This unique museum features a collection of golf equipment, including antique clubs, balls, bags, and much more. Guests can also enjoy a round of golf while marveling at the history of the sport.

Natural Attractions of Gurnee: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Six Flags Great America: This legendary theme park features over 100 rides, live shows, and attractions making it one of the best theme parks in the country. It is a perfect destination for families, friends, and thrill-seekers alike, with something to offer everyone.
  2. Independence Grove Forest Preserve: This 1,100-acre forest preserve is an oasis of natural beauty and tranquility. Visitors can enjoy fishing, hiking, biking, boating, and picnicking amidst nature’s beauty.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Gurnee

Gurnee features an extensive network of exceptional trails for hiking and biking. These trails offer guests the opportunity to explore and enjoy the village’s natural scenery, including lush forests, sparkling lakes, and rolling terrain. Two trails that are not to be missed are the Des Plaines River Trail and the Millennium Trail.

Best Things to Do in Gurnee: Activities and Tours

When you visit Gurnee, you will never run out of things to do, from thrilling theme parks to outdoor adventures. Here are some of the best things to do in Gurnee.

  1. Six Flags Great America: This theme park is a top attraction in Gurnee, offering exciting rides, live shows, and attractions that are perfect for all ages.
  2. Gurnee Mills: This popular shopping center is one of the largest in Illinois and offers guests a vast selection of stores, restaurants, and experiences.
  3. Zip Chicago: This zip-lining adventure park is sure to get your adrenaline pumping. You can soar through the treetops over 100 feet in the air, enjoying stunning views of the forest and wildlife.
  4. Rink Side Sports: This entertainment destination features ice-skating rinks, laser tag, mini-golf, and many other exciting attractions.

Food and Drink Scene of Gurnee

Gurnee is home to a diverse food and drink scene, ranging from casual bistros to fine dining establishments serving up local flavors and international cuisine.

Must-Try Dishes

When you visit Gurnee, be sure to try some of the must-try local dishes, such as the Chicago-style hotdog, Italian beef sandwich, and deep-dish pizza.

Local Beverages

Illinois is famous for its craft beer scene, and Gurnee is no exception. Check out Only Child Brewing Company, which offers a range of brews, including seasonal and experimental flavors.

Where to Stay in Gurnee: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Gurnee offers a variety of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Whether you’re looking for a luxurious hotel or a cozy guesthouse, you will find a place that suits your needs.

What to Buy in Gurnee: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When you visit Gurnee, be sure to check out the local markets and stores for unique souvenirs and local products to bring home. Some of the popular souvenir options include golf gear, local honey, and craft beer.

Local Culture and Customs of Gurnee

Gurnee is a friendly and inviting community where visitors are welcomed with open arms. The local culture is vibrant and lively, with a host of festivals, events, and activities throughout the year. Be sure to respect local customs and traditions during your visit.

Best Time to Visit Gurnee

The best time to visit Gurnee is during the summer months, from May to September, when the weather is warm, and the attractions are in full swing. However, Gurnee is a beautiful destination year-round, with something to offer during every season.

Safety and Health

Safety in Gurnee

Gurnee is a safe destination, and visitors can confidently explore the village and its attractions without any safety concerns. However, it is always a good practice to take general safety precautions, such as being aware of your surroundings and belongings.

Health in Gurnee

Gurnee is served by several hospitals and healthcare facilities, providing visitors with access to quality medical care when needed. It is a good idea to take standard health precautions during your visit, such as washing your hands frequently and staying hydrated.

Getting to Gurnee: Transportation Options and Logistics

Gurnee is easily accessible from Chicago and surrounding areas via car, bus, or plane. The village is located close to several major highways, including I-94, making it easy to reach from Chicago or Milwaukee.

Airports Near Gurnee

The closest major airport to Gurnee is O’Hare International Airport, which is located approximately 34 miles from Gurnee. Other airports nearby include General Mitchell International Airport and Chicago Midway International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Gurnee

Gurnee is served by several bus stations, including Greyhound and MegaBus. These bus stations offer regular trips to and from Chicago and other surrounding areas.

Other Options

Gurnee is also served by several taxi and ride-sharing services, including Lyft and Uber. Visitors can also rent a car, which is an excellent option for exploring the surrounding areas at your own pace.

How to Get Around in Gurnee

Gurnee is a small village that can be easily explored on foot or by bike. However, visitors can also use the local transportation services, including the Pace Bus and Metra Rail, to get around the village and surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Gurnee: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Gurnee is located close to several popular destinations, making it an excellent base for exploring the surrounding areas. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions worth checking out:

  • Chicago: This vibrant city is located just a short drive or train ride away from Gurnee and offers a host of attractions and experiences.
  • Lake Michigan: This beautiful lake is located just a few miles east of Gurnee and offers visitors stunning views and water-based activities.
  • Milwaukee: This charming city is located around a 90-minute drive from Gurnee and is home to several exciting attractions, including the Milwaukee Art Museum and historic Third Ward.

Conclusion

Gurnee is an excellent destination for travelers looking for a unique blend of history, culture, and outdoor adventure. Whether you’re looking for a fun-filled day at Six Flags Great America or a relaxing stroll through an expansive forest preserve, Gurnee has something for everyone. So, pack your bags, and plan your trip to Gurnee today!
