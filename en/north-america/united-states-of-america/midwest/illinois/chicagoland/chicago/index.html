Food and Drink Scene of Chicago

Chicago is known for its incredible food scene. These are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chicago-style hot dogs
  • Deep-dish pizza
  • Italian beef sandwiches
  • Garrett Popcorn
  • Rainbow Cone ice cream

Local Beverages

  • Malort
  • Local craft beer from Goose Island, Revolution Brewing, or Half Acre
  • The Chicago Cocktail, a variation of the Manhattan made with local bitters

What to Buy in Chicago: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don’t leave Chicago without bringing home some local souvenirs and products. Some ideas include:

  • Cubs or White Sox memorabilia
  • Garrett Popcorn
  • Lou Malnati’s pizza
  • Frango Mints
  • Chicago-style beef jerky

Local Culture and Customs of Chicago

Chicagoans are friendly and welcoming people. When visiting, it’s a good idea to remember the following cultural customs:

  • Tipping is generally expected
  • Chicagoans are proud of their city and enjoy showing it off to visitors
  • The weather can be unpredictable, so be prepared for anything

Best Time to Visit Chicago

The best time to visit Chicago is during the summer months, when the weather is warm and there are plenty of events and festivals happening. However, if you’re looking to avoid crowds, consider visiting in the spring or fall.

Do’s and Don’ts of Chicago

When visiting Chicago, keep these do’s and don’ts in mind:

Do:

  • Try the deep-dish pizza
  • Take a boat tour of the architecture on the river
  • Attend a jazz or blues concert

Don’t:

  • Wear a Green Bay Packers jersey (unless you want some playful ribbing from locals)
  • Litter
  • Call it “Chi-town”

Safety and Health

Chicago is a generally safe city, but like any urban area, there are precautions to take. Here are some safety and health tips for your visit:

Safety in Chicago

  • Avoid walking alone in deserted areas
  • Keep your belongings in sight or locked up
  • Use common sense when interacting with strangers

Health in Chicago

  • Be aware of the air quality, especially during summer months
  • Stay hydrated
  • Protect yourself from mosquitoes during the summer months

Getting to Chicago: Transportation Options and Logistics

Getting to Chicago is easy thanks to a variety of transportation options:

  • Chicago O’Hare International Airport
  • Midway International Airport
  • Amtrak
  • Greyhound

How to Get Around in Chicago

Once you’re in Chicago, there are several ways to get around the city:

  • The “L” train
  • Taxis and ride-sharing services
  • Biking
  • Walking

Where to Go Next from Chicago: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore some nearby destinations from Chicago, consider these options:

  • Milwaukee, Wisconsin
  • Indiana Dunes State Park
  • Mackinac Island, Michigan
  • Traverse City, Michigan

Conclusion

Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, Chicago is a city that will surprise and delight you at every turn. From the incredible food to the rich cultural attractions, Chicago has everything you need for an unforgettable vacation. Use our comprehensive guide to plan your trip today!
