Introduction to Near North

Situated in Chicago’s downtown area, Near North is a vibrant neighborhood known for its lively social scene, excellent food, and abundant recreational options. Divided into two separate districts - Gold Coast and River North - Near North attracts tourists and locals alike. This neighborhood is an excellent choice for those who prefer to be close to the bustling city while also enjoying a quieter vibe.

List of Places to Visit in Near North

If you’re planning a vacation to Near North, we’ve put together a must-visit list of places to help you make the most of your trip.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Museum of Contemporary Art: This museum houses a large collection of contemporary art and offers special exhibitions throughout the year.

Chicago History Museum: Here you’ll find exhibits that portray the history of Chicago and the Midwest.

Lookingglass Theatre Company: This theatre is home to numerous plays, performances and events every year - a must-visit for art aficionados.

Natural Attractions of Near North: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lakefront Trail: Experience the beautiful views of Lake Michigan while cycling or walking the Lakefront Trail. One of the best ways to unwind and get fresh air, it’s a place you would love to visit again and again.

Chicago Riverwalk: Take in the city’s skyline along the Riverwalk. It is open year-round and has multiple stops where visitors can join a boat or kayak tour.

Oak Beach: Enjoy a lovely picnic or spend some time sunbathing, reading a book or simply relaxing. It’s a charming little haven in the midst of the city.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Near North

The 606 Trail: This former rail line turned trail spans through a few neighborhoods of Chicago’s North. Escape the city and go for a long adventure ride with The 606 Trail.

Lincoln Park Zoo: A famed destination that includes nature preserve areas. Stroll through this park to get fresh air and natural thrill with all the animals around.

Best Things to Do in Near North: Activities and Tours

Taste of River North: A food festival where restaurants feature their signature dishes on the Riverwalk and how well they can serve Chicago classic dishes.

Bike and Roll Chicago: An excellent option for visitors to explore the city with a guided tour on a bike or Segway.

Week-Long Itinerary for Near North

Day 1: Visit Chicago History Museum & then go strolling at the Chicago Zoo Day 2: Bike through River North and explore the city’s culinary delights Day 3: Oak Street beach, one of the most beautiful beaches in Illinois Day 4: Kayak or boat ride at the Chicago Riverwalk Day 5: Museum of Contemporary Art, get lost in the artistic aura Day 6: Visit Wrigley field and buy souvenirs to take back home Day 7: Join a 606 Trail adventure and indulge in natural beauty

Food and Drink Scene of Near North

Must-Try Dishes

Gibsons Bar and Steakhouse: The Chicago-style burger cannot be missed, it’s juicy and delicious. Deep-dish pizzas: Near North offers several options to try the famous deep-dish pizza, for instance, Pequod’s Pizza and Lou Malnati%27s. Allium Restaurant: Visit Allium to taste classic American dishes with a modern twist.

Local Beverages

Revolution Brewing: The brewery is known for its rotating selection, from stouts to IPAs. Hopewell Brewing Company: A cosy taproom that serves refreshing beer every week, meant for people who love good atmosphere along with their beer.

Where to Stay in Near North: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

The Thompson Hotel: A luxury hotel located in the River North neighborhood. Cambria Chicago Magnificent Mile: Centrally located in Near North, guests can easily explore the neighborhood. The Freehand Hotel: If you’re looking for an affordable option, Freehand provides cheap room arrangements to stay comfortably.

What to Buy in Near North: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Magnificent Mile is where you can find a lot of souvenirs, from Chicago Cubs memorabilia to Magnificent Mile Magnets.

Local Culture and Customs of Near North

Chicago has always been regarded as a melting pot of culture with lots of traditions that locals uphold. Bear in mind it’s essential to be respectful of the local culture and take the time to understand customs, traditions, and values.

Best Time to Visit Near North

September through November is the best time to visit if you want to avoid the summer crowds and indulge in local festivities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Near North

Do:

  • Explore the beaches.
  • Walk instead of driving.
  • Attend local festivals like Taste of River North.

Don’t:

  • Litter on the beaches or anywhere else in the neighborhood.
  • Talk on your phone or play loud music on public transportation.
  • Ignore the pedestrian signals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Near North

There is nothing to worry about as the neighborhood is generally a safe place. However, it’s always good to take basic safety measures like staying in groups and avoiding secluded areas at night.

Health in Near North

The closest medical center is Northwestern Memorial Hospital. Please do check your insurance policies before visiting any medical facilities.

Getting to Near North: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Near North

O’Hare International Airport and Midway International Airport are the two closest airports.

Bus Stations Near Near North

The closest bus stations to Near North are Greyhound, MegaBus, and Peter Pan Bus Lines.

Other Options

Another way to reach Near North is by the Metra Rail. Union Station, Ogilvie Transportation Center, and Millennium Station are all in proximity.

How to Get Around in Near North

Walking, biking, or jogging is the best way of exploring Near North.

Where to Go Next from Near North: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Lincoln Park: The popular attraction of Chicago’s North Side boasts beautiful parks, exceptional museums, and delicious restaurants.

Magnificent Mile: A great place for shopping and exploring the local shops.

Conclusion

With a plethora of world-class attractions, energetic nightlife, and delicious cuisine, Near North is a traveler’s paradise. Whether you’re a local or a tourist, this vibrant neighborhood has so much to offer, and you’re sure to find something that will amaze and entertain you. Plan out your next trip to Near North, and we’re sure you won’t regret it.
