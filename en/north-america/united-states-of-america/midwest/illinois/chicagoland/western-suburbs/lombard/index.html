Introduction to Lombard

Lombard is a tranquil, small town located in the Western Suburbs of Chicago. Situated in Illinois, United States of America, Lombard is a picturesque destination that boasts of rich cultural heritage, ample outdoor activities, and vibrant food and drink scene. Known as the “Lilac Village,” Lombard is home to its annual Lilac Festival, which provides a beautiful backdrop to the town with over 700 lilac bushes.

Lombard offers something for everyone, whether you are a history buff, an outdoors enthusiast, or a food lover. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take you on an in-depth tour of Lombard, highlighting the top attractions, best hiking and biking routes, delicious food and drinks, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in Lombard

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Lombard Historical Society

The Lombard Historical Society is a must-visit attraction if you are interested in learning about the town’s rich history. This museum showcases exhibits that explore the town’s growth and development over the years, including its famous architectural landmarks.

Sheldon Peck Homestead

Sheldon Peck Homestead is a nationally registered historical site that takes you through the life of Sheldon Peck, one of Lombard’s most famous residents. The homestead features original artifacts, including Sheldon Peck’s paintings, furnishings, and tools.

First Church of Lombard

Located in Downtown Lombard, First Church of Lombard is a historic church that dates back to the 1860s. This beautiful building boasts a stunning stained glass and unique architecture that is worth a visit.

Natural Attractions of Lombard: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lombard Park District

Lombard Park District is a six-time National Gold Medal Award-winning park district that offers outdoor enthusiasts a plethora of activities, including hiking and biking trails, swimming, and nature programs for kids and adults.

Lilacia Park

Lilacia Park is a beautiful public park that features over 700 lilac bushes. The park is renowned for its picturesque setting and is a prime location for picnic enthusiasts and photography enthusiasts.

Yorktown Center

If you are in the mood for shopping, Yorktown Center is the premier shopping destination in Lombard. This massive mall has everything you need, from high-end retailers to cozy cafes, providing shoppers with a delightful experience.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lombard

Great Western Trail

One of Lombard’s most popular biking and hiking trails, the Great Western Trail spans over 17 miles and is open year-round. The trail features beautiful natural settings, including forests, meadows, and wetlands.

Lombard Prairie Path

The Lombard Prairie Path is a six-mile trail that offers a scenic view of beautiful wildflowers and rolling dunes. This trail is perfect for hiking, biking, or strolling amidst nature.

Best Things to Do in Lombard: Activities and Tours

Lombard Roller Rink

Lombard Roller Rink is a great place for families to enjoy a fun-filled afternoon of skating. Bring your own skates or rent them and enjoy the retro-themed rink, complete with disco lights and a live DJ.

Lombard Ghost Tours

Lombard Ghost Tours is the perfect way to explore the spooky side of Lombard. This ghost tour makes stops at some of Lombard’s most haunted places while regaling you with eerie tales and legends.

Food and Drink Scene of Lombard

Must-Try Dishes

Portillo’s Hot Dogs

Portillo’s Hot Dogs is a popular local fast-food chain that’s famous for their Chicago-style hot dogs and Italian beef sandwiches.

Egg Harbor Cafe

Egg Harbor Cafe is a popular breakfast and brunch spot known for their unique blend of classic comfort food and healthy options.

Local Beverages

Noon Whistle Brewing

Noon Whistle Brewing is a local microbrewery that features a wide range of brews, including classic pilsners, IPAs, and stouts.

Barrel House Social

Barrel House Social is a local bar that offers an extensive selection of craft cocktails made with local spirits.

Where to Stay in Lombard: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Fairfield Inn & Suites Chicago Lombard

Fairfield Inn & Suites Chicago Lombard offers comfortable and stylish accommodation, including free wifi and a complimentary breakfast buffet.

Lombard House

If you’re looking for a more intimate stay, Lombard House is a cozy guesthouse that is perfect for couples or solo travelers.

What to Buy in Lombard: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Lilac Festival Souvenirs

Bring home a piece of Lombard’s famous Lilac Festival with souvenirs such as t-shirts, caps, and other memorabilia.

Lombard Historical Society Books

The Lombard Historical Society offers a range of books and publications that delve deeper into the town’s rich history.

Local Culture and Customs of Lombard

Lombard is primarily a suburban, Midwestern town, but it has a rich cultural heritage and prides itself on celebrating its diversity. Visitors can expect warm hospitality, a strong sense of community, and a relaxed, laid-back pace of life.

Best Time to Visit Lombard

The best time to visit Lombard is from May to September when the weather is warm, and the town is bustling with outdoor activities and events, including the famous Lilac Festival held in May.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lombard

Do:

  • Try the famous Chicago-style hot dogs and Italian beef sandwiches.
  • Take a stroll through Lilacia Park.
  • Explore Lombard’s rich history at the Lombard Historical Society and Sheldon Peck Homestead.
  • Enjoy the beautiful natural settings and hiking trails.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural surroundings.
  • Don’t forget to tip your servers at restaurants and cafes.
  • Don’t be loud and boisterous in public places.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lombard

As with any town, Lombard has its fair share of petty crime, but overall, it is a safe, family-friendly destination. Visitors should take normal safety precautions, like being aware of their surroundings and not leaving valuables in their cars.

Health in Lombard

Lombard has top-notch medical facilities, including Advocate Good Samaritan Hospital, which provides high-quality healthcare services to residents and visitors.

Getting to Lombard: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lombard

The closest airport to Lombard is O’Hare International Airport, located about 15 miles away. Midway International Airport is also a viable option, located about 25 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Lombard

Greyhound and Megabus are the two major bus services that operate in and out of Lombard.

Other Options

If you prefer to drive, Lombard is easily accessible via major highways like I-294, I-355, and I-88.

How to Get Around in Lombard

The best way to get around Lombard is by car, but the town is also easily navigable on foot, and you can rent bikes from Lombard Park District.

Where to Go Next from Lombard: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Chicago

Just half an hour away from Lombard is Chicago, the Windy City, which is famous for its world-class architecture, museums, and booming food scene.

Naperville

Located just ten miles from Lombard, Naperville is a charming town that offers a blend of classic and modern attractions, from historic sites like the Naperville Settlement to modern art galleries and museums.

Conclusion

Lombard is a delightful destination that has something for everyone. Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, a history buff, or a food lover, this charming town promises an unforgettable experience.
