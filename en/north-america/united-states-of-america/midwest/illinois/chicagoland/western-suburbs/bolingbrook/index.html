Introduction to Bolingbrook

Welcome to Bolingbrook, a charming suburb located in the Western Suburbs of Chicagoland! With a rich history, unparalleled natural beauty, cultural attractions, and a plethora of exciting things to do, Bolingbrook makes for an ideal destination for travelers seeking an offbeat experience.

In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ll explore all that Bolingbrook has to offer, from the must-visit attractions to the best hiking and biking routes, from the local food and drink scene to the top places to stay. So, pack your bags, and let’s get started!

List of Places to Visit in Bolingbrook

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Bolingbrook Historic Preservation Commission: The commission was set up to protect the historical and architectural integrity of Bolingbrook. Visit them to learn more about the town’s history and how it has developed over the years.
  • Boughton Ridge Golf Course: Golf lovers must visit the beautiful 18-hole course located on Boughton Road. The course is open to the public and has a clubhouse where guests can relax after a game.
  • Bolingbrook Park District: This district is home to a variety of parks, facilities, and events, including a skate park, a nature center, a playground, and more. It’s the ideal spot for nature lovers and recreational enthusiasts.

Natural Attractions of Bolingbrook: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hidden Oaks Nature Center: This nature center boasts several hiking trails that weave through a verdant wooded area, marsh, and a pond. The center educates visitors on the local ecology and natural history of Bolingbrook and the surrounding area.
  • The DuPage River: A scenic river that meanders through Bolingbrook, the DuPage river is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. Kayaking, canoeing, and fishing are popular activities in the river.
  • Whalon Lake Forest Preserve: This preserve has a large lake that’s ideal for fishing, boating, and picnicking. It also has several hiking trails that offer scenic views of the natural landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bolingbrook

  • The High School to Community Park Trail: This trail is a 5.5-mile hiking and biking route that stretches from Bolingbrook High School to the Community Center. It’s a paved trail and perfect for families.
  • The DuPage River Trail: This is a 5-mile scenic hiking route that follows the DuPage River. It’s perfect for nature lovers as it offers stunning views of the river and wildlife.
  • The Cog Hill Trail: This is a 4-mile hiking and biking trail that starts at the Cog Hill Golf and Country Club and winds through a beautiful forested area. It’s ideal for those seeking a quiet escape.

Best Things to Do in Bolingbrook: Activities and Tours

  • Bolingbrook Golf Club: This club is perfect for avid golfers. It has an 18-hole championship golf course and a pro shop where visitors can rent golf equipment.
  • Rocket Ice Skating Rink: This indoor skating rink offers figure skating and hockey classes in addition to public skating hours.
  • Bolingbrook Aquatic Center: This center offers a variety of water features, including pools, a lazy river, and water slides, making it the ideal spot for families looking for a fun-filled day.

Food and Drink Scene of Bolingbrook

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chicago-Style Pizza: You must try the famous Chicago-style pizza at Gino’s East in Bolingbrook.
  • Hot Dogs: Visit Portillo’s for a classic Chicago-style dog with all the toppings.
  • Italian Beef Sandwich: Head to Al’s Beef for the best Italian beef sandwich in town.

Local Beverages

  • Goose Island Beer: This award-winning brewery offers a variety of locally brewed beers.
  • Illinois Wines: Try some locally brewed wines at Fox Valley Winery and Cooper’s Hawk Winery.
  • Intelligentsia Coffee: Visit their location at the nearby Logan Square in Chicago for a perfect cup of coffee.

Where to Stay in Bolingbrook: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Holiday Inn Hotel & Suites Bolingbrook: Located near the Bolingbrook Golf Club, this hotel is perfect for visitors looking to spend some time on the greens.
  • Aloft Bolingbrook: This hotel offers easy access to the nearby Chicago Premium Outlets and the Rocket Ice Skating Rink.
  • Best Western Plus Bolingbrook: This hotel is conveniently located near the Boughton Ridge Golf Course and the Bolingbrook Aquatic Center.

What to Buy in Bolingbrook: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Chicago Sports Team Merchandise: Visit the nearby United Center to buy merchandise of the Chicago Bulls or Blackhawks.
  • Artwork: Head to one of several art fairs held throughout the year, such as the Two Rivers Arts Fest, to purchase unique and one-of-a-kind artwork.
  • Wine: Grab a bottle of locally brewed wine from the Fox Valley Winery to bring back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Bolingbrook

  • Respect for the Elders: In Bolingbrook, it’s customary to show respect to elders.
  • Punctuality: Being on time is highly valued in Bolingbrook culture.
  • Friendliness: Bolingbrook residents are known for their friendliness and hospitality towards visitors.

Best Time to Visit Bolingbrook

The best time to visit Bolingbrook is from May to September when the weather is pleasant, and the town comes alive with several festivals and events. However, if you’re looking for a winter wonderland experience, visit during December when the town is decorated with lights and offers several holiday-themed activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bolingbrook

Do:

  • Explore the local historical and cultural attractions.
  • Take advantage of the beautiful natural landscape and head out for a hike or bike ride.
  • Try the local food and drink offerings.

Don’t:

  • Litter in natural areas and preserves.
  • Be loud in residential areas.
  • Disrespect the culture and customs of the local population.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bolingbrook

Bolingbrook is generally a very safe place, but visitors should take care when traveling alone at night or in secluded areas. Always keep an eye on your belongings in crowded areas and practice caution while driving.

Health in Bolingbrook

Bolingbrook has several medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics, that offer top-notch health care services to visitors and residents alike.

Getting to Bolingbrook: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bolingbrook is easily accessible via several transportation modes.

Airports Near Bolingbrook

  • O’Hare International Airport (ORD): Located 29 miles away.
  • Midway International Airport (MDW): Located 22 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Bolingbrook

  • Greyhound Bus Station: Located in downtown Chicago, a 40-minute drive from Bolingbrook.
  • Suburban Express: Located in nearby Naperville, a 10-minute drive from Bolingbrook.

Other Options

  • Rental Cars: Renting a car is an ideal option for visitors who want to explore Bolingbrook and the nearby areas at their own pace.
  • Taxi Services: Several taxi companies offer services in and around Bolingbrook.

How to Get Around in Bolingbrook

Bolingbrook has an extensive public transportation system, including buses and trains, operated by Pace Suburban Bus. However, renting a car or using a taxi service is ideal for visitors who want to explore Bolingbrook and the surrounding areas at their own pace.

Where to Go Next from Bolingbrook: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Chicago: Just a 35-minute drive away, the Windy City has a plethora of attractions, activities, and events that will keep visitors busy for weeks.
  • Fermilab: Located in nearby Batavia, Fermilab is a particle physics laboratory that offers tours and educational programs to visitors.
  • Starved Rock State Park: Located 80 miles from Bolingbrook, the park boasts stunning views, hiking trails, and waterfalls.

Conclusion

Bolingbrook offers an eclectic mix of attractions that cater to all types of visitors. Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, a culture lover, or a foodie, Bolingbrook has something for everyone. So, next time you’re visiting Chicago or the Midwest, be sure to add Bolingbrook to your itinerary!
