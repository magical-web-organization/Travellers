Introduction to Elmhurst

Elmhurst is a charming suburban town located in the Chicagoland area of Illinois, situated in the Midwest region of the United States of America. The town is known for its scenic views, outdoor activities, and cultural attractions.

With a population of just over 44,000 and a prime location close to downtown Chicago and O’Hare International airport, Elmhurst offers the perfect mix of small-town living and big city amenities. In this travel guide, we’ll highlight the best things to do and see in Elmhurst for a memorable vacation.

List of Places to Visit in Elmhurst

Elmhurst offers a wide range of attractions for visitors of all ages. From cultural landmarks and museums to outdoor parks and hiking trails, here are the best places to visit in Elmhurst:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Elmhurst Art Museum: This museum features rotating exhibits of contemporary art, installations, and architecture.

  • Lizzadro Museum of Lapidary Art: This museum showcases a unique collection of rare stones, minerals, and geological specimens.

  • Elmhurst History Museum: Step back in time and learn about the history of Elmhurst and its role in US history.

Natural Attractions of Elmhurst: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Wild Meadows Trace: Take a scenic stroll through this restored prairie landscape filled with wildflowers and diverse wildlife.

  • Berens Park: This expansive park offers numerous amenities, including an outdoor pool, splash pad, and sand volleyball courts.

  • Salt Creek Greenway Trail: Bike or hike this beautiful 33-mile trail, which runs through the heart of Elmhurst and surrounding towns.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Elmhurst

Elmhurst boasts many scenic outdoor locations that are perfect for hiking and biking. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Salt Creek Greenway Trail: This 33-mile trail runs through the heart of Elmhurst and the surrounding towns. It offers beautiful views of nature and is perfect for hikers and bikers.

  • Illinois Prairie Path: This popular biking and hiking trail spans 61 miles and connects several towns, including Elmhurst.

Best Things to Do in Elmhurst: Activities and Tours

For those looking for adventurous things to do, Elmhurst offers several exciting activities and tours. Here are some of the best options:

  • Escape Factor: This escape room game is the perfect way to challenge your friends and family while enjoying some quality time together.

  • Elmhurst Ghost Tour: Learn about the haunted history of Elmhurst on this spooky walking tour.

  • Urban Adventure Quest: Go on a scavenger hunt and explore Elmhurst’s unique neighborhoods while solving clues and riddles.

Week-Long Itinerary for Elmhurst

For those planning a week-long vacation in Elmhurst, here are some activities and attractions that should be included in your itinerary:

Food and Drink Scene of Elmhurst

Elmhurst offers a wide range of dining options and has a rich food and drink scene. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Italian Beef Sandwich: This local delicacy features hot beef layered with peppers and a tangy sauce on a soft roll.

  • Deep Dish Pizza: No visit to Chicago would be complete without trying this classic staple of the city’s cuisine.

  • Chicago-style Hot Dog: A beef hot dog dressed with mustard, chopped onions, and bright-green relish served on a steamed poppy-seed bun.

Local Beverages

  • Goose Island Beer Company: This local brewery serves excellent craft beer, including its signature Goose IPA and 312 Urban Wheat Ale.

  • Koval Distillery: This small-batch distillery produces top-notch whiskey, gin, and other spirits using organic, locally sourced ingredients.

What to Buy in Elmhurst: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Local Culture and Customs of Elmhurst

The people of Elmhurst are friendly and welcoming to visitors. The town is known for its cultural landmarks and attractions, and visitors should show respect when visiting these places. It is also customary to tip service staff for good service in restaurants and bars.

Best Time to Visit Elmhurst

The best time to visit Elmhurst is in the summer months between June and August when the temperatures are mild and the parks and trails are in full swing. Spring and fall are also beautiful times of the year to visit, with pleasant temperatures, fewer crowds, and vibrant foliage.

Safety and Health

Safety in Elmhurst

Elmhurst is a safe town, but visitors should remain cautious when visiting busy areas, especially at night. It is always advisable to be aware of your surroundings and to keep your valuables close.

Health in Elmhurst

Elmhurst has excellent health care facilities and clinics, and travelers can rest assured that medical assistance is readily available if needed. It is always advisable to have comprehensive travel insurance when visiting any destination.

Getting to Elmhurst: Transportation Options and Logistics

Elmhurst is easily accessible by air, road, and train. Here are some of the most popular transportation options and logistics for getting to Elmhurst:

Airports Near Elmhurst

  • O’Hare International Airport: Located 12 miles from Elmhurst, O’Hare is the closest international airport.

  • Midway International Airport: Located 22 miles away from Elmhurst, Midway is a popular regional airport that offers quick connections to various airlines.

Bus Stations Near Elmhurst

  • Elmhurst Metra Station: This station is the main hub for train services within Elmhurst.

  • Pace Bus Services: Pace buses offer numerous options for intra-town and inter-town travel.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Various rental car agencies operate in the area, including Avis, Hertz, and Enterprise.

How to Get Around in Elmhurst

Elmhurst has an efficient and reliable public transportation system, including buses and trains. Taxis and ride-sharing services are also readily available for those who prefer private transportation. For those looking for some exercise, biking and walking remain great options given the town’s slow pace and low traffic congestion.

Where to Go Next from Elmhurst: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have time and are looking to explore further afield, there are many nearby destinations and excursions you can visit from Elmhurst. Here are some popular destinations:

  • Chicago: The windy city is just a short drive or train ride away.

  • Naperville: This quaint town is great for shopping and offers several scenic outdoor locations.

  • Starved Rock: This beautiful state park boasts gorgeous waterfalls and fantastic hiking trails.

Conclusion

Elmhurst is a fantastic destination that offers a mix of natural beauty, cultural landmarks, and exciting outdoor adventures. From exploring parks and hiking trails to enjoying the food and drink scene and unique local culture, Elmhurst has something for everyone. Plan your next vacation to this lovely town for an unforgettable holiday experience.
