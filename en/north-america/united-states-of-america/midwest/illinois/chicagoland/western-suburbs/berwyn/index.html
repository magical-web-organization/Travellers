Introduction to Berwyn

Located in the Western Suburbs of Chicagoland, Berwyn is a vibrant city filled with rich history, culture, and charm. Berwyn is a part of Illinois, a Midwestern state located in the United States of America. Berwyn is known for its diverse neighborhoods, tree-lined streets, friendly people, and excellent dining options.

Berwyn’s location provides easy access to all that Chicagoland has to offer, making it an excellent choice for travelers interested in exploring the Greater Chicago Area.

In this comprehensive travel guide to Berwyn, we will cover everything you need to know to plan your perfect trip to this charming city.

List of Places to Visit in Berwyn

Berwyn is a city with plenty of exciting things to see and do. Here is a list of the top places to visit in Berwyn:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Berwyn is proud of its cultural heritage, and there are several museums and historic places that reflect this.

Berwyn Route 66 Museum

The Berwyn Route 66 Museum is a must-visit for history buffs and car enthusiasts alike. The museum showcases the rich history of the famous Route 66, which passed through Berwyn, and has exhibitions dedicated to vintage cars, local landmarks, and more.

Cermak Plaza

Cermak Plaza is a shopping center that has been a landmark of the Berwyn community since 1956. The unique design of the plaza, which features a broad concrete canopy supported by giant white pillars, is a testament to Berwyn’s architectural heritage.

Berwyn’s Historic Depot District

Berwyn’s Depot District is a historical neighborhood that was once home to the town’s train station and is now filled with charming businesses, boutiques, and distinctive architecture. Walking tours are available, and visitors can join group tours to learn all about the district’s history.

Natural Attractions of Berwyn: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Berwyn is home to several natural attractions that offer unique outdoor experiences and breathtaking views.

Horse Thief Hollow

Horse Thief Hollow is a nature preserve located on the Des Plaines River that is perfect for hiking, biking, birdwatching, and fishing. Visitors can enjoy the natural beauty of the area while taking a break from the city’s hustle and bustle.

Houby Day Parade

The annual Houby Day Parade celebrates Berwyn’s Czech heritage, with a lively procession of floats, marching bands, and performers. The parade has been a Berwyn tradition since 1968 and attracts thousands of visitors from all over the Chicagoland area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Berwyn

Berwyn is an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts who love hiking and biking. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a beginner, there are several routes in Berwyn that cater to all levels of experience.

Salt Creek Greenway Trail

The Salt Creek Greenway Trail is a popular route that stretches from Brookfield Zoo to Busse Woods in Elk Grove Village. This trail passes through Berwyn and offers a scenic ride or hike with plenty of wildlife viewing opportunities.

Riverside Golf Club

Riverside Golf Club is a popular destination for cyclists due to its scenic views and challenging terrain. Cyclists can enjoy 20 miles of marked cycling routes through picturesque golf courses located near Berwyn.

Best Things to Do in Berwyn: Activities and Tours

Berwyn offers plenty of exciting activities and tours that you can enjoy during your visit to the city.

Visit the Krew Rock Lounge

The Krew Rock Lounge has been a Berwyn institution since the 1940s and is a must-visit for anyone looking for a great night out. The lounge features live music, karaoke, and dancing, making it the perfect place to let loose and have some fun.

Take a Culinary Tour

Berwyn is home to some of the best dining options in the Chicagoland area, and a culinary tour is an excellent way to sample the city’s local cuisine. Berwyn’s Culinary Tour takes visitors to some of the city’s best-known eateries and offers a chance to taste the local delicacies.

Where to Stay in Berwyn: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Whether you’re looking for luxury or budget-friendly accommodations, Berwyn has plenty of options to suit all preferences. Here are the best places to stay in Berwyn:

Best Western Plus
