Introduction to Crestwood

Crestwood is a quaint village located in the South Suburbs of Chicago, Illinois, and is known for its picturesque outdoor destinations, fascinating cultural sites, and great food scene. It is perfect for anyone looking for a break from the hustle and bustle of city life. Whether you are looking to explore historical sites, indulge in outdoor activities or simply relax, Crestwood is the ideal destination.

List of Places to Visit in Crestwood

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Exploring the museums and historical sites in Crestwood is an excellent way to learn about the rich history of this village. Some of the must-visit museums and historical places in Crestwood are:

  • Midlothian Historical Society - This museum showcases exhibits and artifacts that illustrate the history of the village of Midlothian and the surrounding communities.

  • Robinson Engineering Museum - This museum explores the history of civil engineering in the Chicagoland area.

Natural Attractions of Crestwood: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Crestwood is home to several picturesque outdoor destinations that offer breathtaking views and a chance to indulge in outdoor activities. Here are some of the natural attractions of Crestwood:

  • Midlothian Meadows - This park is an excellent place to take a walk and enjoy nature. It has several hiking trails and picnic areas.

  • Calumet Memorial Park - This park has two lakes and is perfect for fishing, kayaking, and hiking.

  • Little Red Schoolhouse Nature Center - This nature center has several hiking trails and educational exhibits that showcase the flora and fauna of the area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Crestwood

Crestwood has several hiking and biking routes that offer an opportunity to explore the beauty of nature. Some of the popular routes are:

  • Calumet Sag Trail - This 26.7-mile trail goes through several towns and villages, including Crestwood, and offers beautiful views of the surrounding area.

  • Midlothian Meadows Trail - This 2.7-mile trail goes through the Midlothian Meadows, a beautiful park in the heart of Crestwood.

Best Things to Do in Crestwood: Activities and Tours

Crestwood offers several activities and tours that cater to all interests and age groups. Here are some of the best things to do in Crestwood:

  • Paint and Sip - This activity is perfect for adults who want to unwind and get creative while sipping on wine.

  • Escape Room - Solve puzzles and riddles with your friends and family to escape the room before time runs out at this popular attraction.

  • Crestwood Bowl - This bowling alley is perfect for families and friends looking for some indoor fun.

Week-Long Itinerary for Crestwood

Day 1: Visit Midlothian Historical Society and Robinson Engineering Museum

Day 2: Explore the natural beauty of Midlothian Meadows and Little Red Schoolhouse Nature Center

Day 3: Take a relaxing picnic at Calumet Memorial Park

Day 4: Go kayaking or fishing at Calumet Memorial Park

Day 5: Take a bike ride on the Calumet Sag Trail

Day 6: Try Paint and Sip or the escape room

Day 7: Enjoy a game of bowling at Crestwood Bowl
