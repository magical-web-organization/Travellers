Introduction to Palos Heights

Located in the south suburbs of Chicagoland, Illinois, Palos Heights is a charming community that offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. With its natural beauty, diverse attractions, and cultural heritage, Palos Heights is a must-visit destination.

List of Places to Visit in Palos Heights

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Lake Katherine Nature Center and Botanic Gardens

Lake Katherine Nature Center and Botanic Gardens is a picturesque spot that offers visitors a serene and beautiful environment to walk, bike, or simply relax. It features walking trails, a lake, and a variety of flora and fauna. In addition, it hosts several events throughout the year, including outdoor concerts, food festivals, and art shows.

Palos Heights Historical Society Museum

The Palos Heights Historical Society Museum is a one-of-a-kind destination that showcases the rich history and culture of Palos Heights. Through exhibits, artifacts, and programming, the museum explores the history of the city, highlighting the stories of its residents, landmarks, and events.

Natural Attractions: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Swallow Cliff Woods

Swallow Cliff Woods is a must-visit destination for nature lovers. It features hiking and biking trails, picnic areas, and scenic views of the surrounding forest. In addition, it offers a challenging and popular stair climb known as the Swallow Cliff Stairs, which is a favorite among local fitness enthusiasts.

Lake Metroparks Palos

Nestled within a forest preserve, Lake Metroparks Palos is a tranquil destination that offers outdoor activities like fishing, boating, and birdwatching. Visitors can also hike or bike along the scenic trails that wind through the woods and around the lake.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Palos Heights

Palos Heights is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, offering a variety of hiking and biking trails that cater to all skill levels. Some of the best hiking and biking routes include:

  • The Sag Valley Trail System
  • Tinley Creek Trail
  • Cal-Sag Trail
  • Little Red Schoolhouse Nature Center Trail
  • Palos Trail System

Best Things to Do in Palos Heights: Activities and Tours

Waterfall Glen Forest Preserve Segway Tour

The Waterfall Glen Forest Preserve Segway Tour is an adventurous way to explore the scenic beauty of Palos Heights. Glide along the trails on a Segway, while taking in the beauty of the forest preserve and its unique flora and fauna.

Horseback Riding at Forest View Farms

Experience the beautiful Palos Heights countryside on horseback at Forest View Farms. This family-owned stable offers scenic trail rides, riding lessons, and horse boarding.

Palos Heights Farmers Market

Palos Heights Farmers Market is a weekly event where visitors can buy fresh and locally grown produce and locally made artisan products. In addition to shopping, the market offers live music, kids’ activities, and food trucks.

Week-Long Itinerary for Palos Heights

Day 1: Explore Lake Katherine Nature Center and Botanic Gardens

Day 2: Hike or bike through Swallow Cliff Woods and the Palos Trail System

Day 3: Visit the Palos Heights Historical Society Museum

Day 4: Take a Waterfall Glen Forest Preserve Segway Tour

Day 5: Relax at Lake Metroparks Palos

Day 6: Horseback riding at Forest View Farms

Day 7: Enjoy the Palos Heights Farmers Market

Food and Drink Scene of Palos Heights

Must-Try Dishes

Spicy Italian Beef at Al’s Beef

Al’s Beef is a local chain that serves up one of Palos Heights’ most iconic dishes: Spicy Italian Beef. This hearty sandwich is made with tender beef, spicy giardiniera, and juicy sweet peppers.

Pizza at Capri Ristorante

Capri Ristorante’s pizza is hands down, some of the best in the area. The thin crust and high-quality toppings make for a delicious and satisfying meal.

Local Beverages

Craft Beer at Imperial Oak Brewing

Imperial Oak Brewing is a beloved local brewery that offers a rotating selection of craft beers. Visitors can enjoy a flight or a pint in the taproom or on the outdoor patio.

Where to Stay in Palos Heights: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Best Western Inn & Suites - Midway Airport

Best Western Inn & Suites - Midway Airport is a convenient and affordable hotel that offers modern amenities like free Wi-Fi and a complimentary breakfast. It’s located near many of Palos Heights’ top attractions.

La Quinta Inn & Suites by Wyndham Chicago Tinley Park

La Quinta Inn & Suites by Wyndham Chicago Tinley Park is a popular choice for visitors seeking comfortable and affordable accommodations. It offers spacious rooms and suites, a fitness center, and free breakfast.

What to Buy in Palos Heights: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Palos Heights Farmers Market

The Palos Heights Farmers Market is the perfect place to shop for locally made souvenirs, like artisanal jams, handcrafted pottery, and homemade soaps.

Local Culture and Customs of Palos Heights

Palos Heights has a diverse population and prides itself on welcoming visitors from all walks of life. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and follow general etiquette and common sense.

Best Time to Visit Palos Heights

The best time to visit Palos Heights is from April to October when the weather is mild, and the outdoor activities are in full swing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Palos Heights

Do:

  • Respect the natural beauty of Palos Heights’ outdoor attractions
  • Enjoy the local food and drink scene
  • Be respectful of local customs and etiquette

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural habitats
  • Disrespect local cultures or customs
  • Leave valuables unattended in vehicles or public areas

Safety and Health

Safety in Palos Heights

Palos Heights is a generally safe destination. Visitors are advised to take common sense safety precautions, like locking car doors and avoiding unfamiliar areas after dark.

Health in Palos Heights

Visitors should stay hydrated, wear appropriate clothing and sunscreen, and take breaks when participating in outdoor activities.

Getting to Palos Heights: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Palos Heights

Palos Heights is served by several airports, including Chicago O’Hare International Airport, Chicago Midway International Airport, and Gary/Chicago International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Palos Heights

Greyhound Lines and Megabus offer bus services to and from Palos Heights.

Other Options

Palos Heights is easily accessible by car, with several major highways, including I-294 and I-55, nearby.

How to Get Around in Palos Heights

Palos Heights is a walkable community, and many attractions are easily accessible by foot or bike. Rental cars are also available for those who prefer to drive.

Where to Go Next from Palos Heights: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Chicago

Chicago, with its world-class museums, restaurants, and shopping, is just a short drive or train ride from Palos Heights and is a must-visit destination.

Starved Rock State Park

Starved Rock State Park is a stunning natural wonder located about two hours from Palos Heights. Visitors can explore the park’s canyons, waterfalls, and hiking trails.

Conclusion

Palos Heights offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural history, and outdoor adventure. From hiking and biking to exploring museums and trying local cuisine, there’s something for everyone in this lovely destination. Plan your trip and discover the best of Chicagoland in Palos Heights.
