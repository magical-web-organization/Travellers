Introduction to McLean

McLean is a small, charming village located in Central Illinois. It offers a peaceful atmosphere, unique attractions, and a wide range of outdoor activities. Whether you’re interested in history, nature, or just relaxation, McLean has something to offer for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in McLean

  • McLean Depot Museum and Historical Society
  • Funks Grove Maple Sirup
  • Route 66 Association Hall of Fame and Museum
  • Sugar Grove Nature Center
  • Walter S. and Olive P. Davis Nature Trail

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

McLean Depot Museum and Historical Society

The McLean Depot Museum and Historical Society is a small historical museum located in the McLean train depot. The museum showcases artifacts, photographs, and memorabilia related to the history of McLean and the surrounding areas. It is open on Saturdays and Sundays from May through October.

Funks Grove Maple Sirup

Funks Grove Maple Sirup is a family-owned farm that has been producing pure maple sirup since the late 1800s. Visitors can take a tour of the farm, see the production process, and sample the delicious maple products.

Route 66 Association Hall of Fame and Museum

The Route 66 Association Hall of Fame and Museum is a must-visit attraction for anyone interested in the history of Route 66. The museum showcases artifacts and memorabilia related to the famous highway, as well as information about the history of McLean as a Route 66 town.

Natural Attractions of McLean: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Sugar Grove Nature Center

The Sugar Grove Nature Center is a 500-acre nature preserve located just a short drive from McLean. The preserve features miles of hiking and biking trails, an interpretive center, and a variety of educational programs and events.

Walter S. and Olive P. Davis Nature Trail

The Walter S. and Olive P. Davis Nature Trail is a beautiful four-mile hiking trail that winds through the woods and along the Mackinaw River. The trail is well-maintained and offers beautiful views of the surrounding countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of McLean

Local Culture and Customs of McLean

McLean is a friendly and welcoming community. Visitors are encouraged to explore the historic downtown area, attend local events and festivals, and engage with the local community.

Best Time to Visit McLean

The best time to visit McLean is during the fall, when the foliage is at its most vibrant and the weather is pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of McLean

Do:

  • Attend local events and festivals
  • Take a hike on one of the many scenic trails in the area
  • Visit Funks Grove Maple Sirup and sample their famous maple products

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or disturb wildlife while exploring the natural areas
  • Don’t forget to bring appropriate clothing and gear for outdoor activities
  • Don’t miss the chance to try some of the local specialties, like frozen custard and maple sirup

Safety and Health

Safety in McLean

McLean is a safe and welcoming community. Visitors should exercise normal safety precautions and be aware of their surroundings.

Health in McLean

There are no major health concerns for visitors in McLean. It is always recommended to have comprehensive travel insurance in case of emergency situations.

Getting to McLean: Transportation Options and Logistics

  • McLean is easily accessible by car via I-55.
  • The nearest major airport is the Central Illinois Regional Airport, which is located 20 miles away in Bloomington-Normal.
  • There are also several bus lines that service McLean, including Greyhound and Megabus.

Airports Near McLean

  • Central Illinois Regional Airport (BMI)
  • Peoria International Airport (PIA)
  • Abraham Lincoln Capital Airport (SPI)

Bus Stations Near McLean

  • Bloomington-Normal Bus Station
  • Lincoln Bus Station
  • Peoria Bus Station

Other Options

  • Taxis and ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft are available in the area.
  • Car rental services are also available at the Central Illinois Regional Airport and in nearby Bloomington-Normal.

How to Get Around in McLean

The best way to get around McLean is by car. There are local taxis available but they are limited. Biking and exploring on foot are also great ways to see the town.
