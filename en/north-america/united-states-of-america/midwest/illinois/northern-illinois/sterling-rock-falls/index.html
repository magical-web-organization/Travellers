Introduction to Sterling-Rock Falls

Sterling and Rock Falls are two neighboring cities that make up a vibrant community in Northwestern Illinois. Sterling is known for its historic landmarks and cultural attractions, while Rock Falls boasts beautiful parks and outdoor activities. The cities are located along the Rock River, making them ideal for water-based sports and activities.
