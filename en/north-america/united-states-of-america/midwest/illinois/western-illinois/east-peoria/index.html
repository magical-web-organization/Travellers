Introduction to East Peoria

East Peoria is a beautiful city in Western Illinois, known for its breathtaking sceneries, rich culture, and history. It’s located in Tazewell County and situated on the western bank of Illinois River. East Peoria boasts an array of attractions, including museums, parks, hiking trails, local food, and drinks, making it an ideal destination for anyone looking for a perfect getaway.

List of Places to Visit in East Peoria

East Peoria has a plethora of places to visit, each with its unique charm and appeal. Whether you’re a culture lover, an outdoor enthusiast, or a foodie, East Peoria has something to offer everyone. Here is a list of must-visit places in East Peoria:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fondulac District Library – This historic library features an array of books, films, and other materials on history, literature, and more.
  • Historic Oakdale Cemetery – Oakdale Cemetery is a landmark and a final resting place of some of the most prominent people in Peoria’s history.
  • Peoria Riverfront Museum – This museum provides an excellent opportunity for visitors to explore the art and natural history behind Peoria.

Natural Attractions of East Peoria: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Rock Island Trail – This 26-mile trail offers a perfect opportunity for biking and hiking.
  • Spring Bay Fen Nature Preserve – Spring Bay is an excellent destination for birdwatchers, nature lovers, and hikers.
  • Pekin Lake Park – Pekin Lake Park is a peaceful oasis surrounded by stunning vistas and perfect for fishing and picnicking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of East Peoria

East Peoria offers plenty of biking and hiking trails, suitable for everyone, including beginners and experts. Here are a few notable trails:

  • Rock Island Trail – This 26-mile trail offers a perfect opportunity for biking and hiking.
  • Fondulac Park District Trail – It offers biking and hiking trails through wooded areas and scenic vistas.
  • EastSide Center Trail – This 2.5-mile long in-and-out trail is perfect for a quick, refreshing bike ride or walk.

Best Things to Do in East Peoria: Activities and Tours

Visitors to East Peoria will find numerous activities and tours to keep them engaged and entertained. Here are some exciting things to do in East Peoria:

  • Whitetail Ridge Golf Club – Named one of the best courses in the state, Whitetail Ridge is perfect for golf lovers.
  • Peoria Charter Coach Company – Take a tour and discover the secrets behind East Peoria’s hidden treasures.
  • Peoria Zoo – Peoria Zoo is an excellent destination for families and nature lovers, featuring a variety of animals and plants.

Week-Long Itinerary for East Peoria

For visitors wanting to spend a week in East Peoria, here’s an itinerary to help plan their trip:

Day 1 – Explore the Peoria Riverfront Museum
