Introduction to Lake Michigan Beachtowns

Nestled on the shores of Lake Michigan, the Beachtowns of West Michigan offer visitors a charming mix of outdoor adventures, cultural landmarks, and scenic beauty. Explore the local history at museums and historical sites, soak up the sun on sandy beaches, hike or bike through the forests and dunes, indulge in local delicacies, or enjoy a variety of water sports. This travel guide will help you plan your trip to the Beachtowns with ease.

Best Things to Do in Lake Michigan Beachtowns: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Lake Michigan Beachtowns:

  • Visit the iconic Grand Haven Lighthouse and climb to the top for stunning views.
  • Explore the Muskegon Museum of Art and marvel at the exhibits.
  • Take a sunset sail on a Tall Ship or charter boat.
  • Rent a kayak or paddleboard and explore the Lake Michigan shoreline.
  • Ride the dunes on a guided dune tour or rent a dune buggy.
  • Take a fishing charter and try your hand at catching salmon and trout.
  • Enjoy a wine tour through the region’s many wineries.
  • Attend an outdoor music festival, like the Venetian Festival or Coast Guard Festival.

Food and Drink Scene of Lake Michigan Beachtowns

The Beachtowns offer a variety of delicious local cuisine and beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Whitefish from local restaurants like The Lake House Waterfront Grille and Dockers Fish House.
  • Cherry-inspired dishes such as cherry pie, cherry salsa, cherry salad and more.
  • Local farm fresh vegetables from local farmers.

Local Beverages

  • Michigan Beer: Enjoy a glass of local beer from Saugatuck Brewing Company or New Holland Brewing Company.
  • Apple Cider: Try an apple cider from Virtue Cider or Crane’s Pie Pantry and Orchard.
  • Wine: Sip some of the region’s best wines from Fenn Valley Vineyards or Round Barn Winery.

Local Culture and Customs of Lake Michigan Beachtowns

The Beachtowns have a relaxed, laid-back culture. It is common to see people walking or biking around town, especially during the summer months. People in the Beachtowns tend to be friendly and welcoming to visitors, so don’t be shy to ask around for recommendations of things to do or places to eat.

Best Time to Visit Lake Michigan Beachtowns

The best time to visit the Beachtowns is between May and October, when the weather is warm and sunny. The months of July and August tend to be the busiest, due to school holidays, music festivals, and other events.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lake Michigan Beachtowns

Do:

  • Apply sunscreen regularly when out in the sun.
  • Make reservations for popular restaurants, hotels, or tours ahead of time.
  • Try local cuisine, beverages, and products.
  • Respect local customs and culture.

Don’t:

  • Litter on the beach or trails.
  • Smoke or vape in public spaces.
  • Ignore safety guidelines when participating in outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lake Michigan Beachtowns

The Beachtowns are generally safe, but it is important to remember general safety tips when traveling. Be cautious of rip currents when swimming in Lake Michigan and obey all warning signs. When driving or biking, be aware of other vehicles and pedestrians.

Health in Lake Michigan Beachtowns

The Beachtowns offer excellent medical care, with hospitals and medical clinics in the region readily available.
