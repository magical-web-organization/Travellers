Introduction to Union Pier

Located in Harbor Country in West Michigan, Union Pier is a charming township in Berrien County. The area boasts some of the most picturesque scenery in the Midwest, from pristine beaches and towering sand dunes to lush forests and meandering rivers. Union Pier is also rich in history, culture and outdoor activities, making it an ideal destination for those seeking a relaxing yet adventurous getaway.

List of Places to Visit in Union Pier

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Union Pier is home to a number of culturally significant landmarks and heritage sites, including:

  • Chikaming Country Club, an idyllic spot for golf enthusiasts with stunning views of Lake Michigan
  • The New Buffalo Railroad Museum, which showcases the history of railroads in southwest Michigan and beyond
  • The Michigan Maritime Museum, a must-visit for maritime history buffs with a range of informative exhibits and displays
  • The Galien River County Park, a favorite among bird watchers and nature lovers alike, offering serene walking trails and scenic picnic spots

Natural Attractions of Union Pier: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For those who enjoy the great outdoors, Union Pier is a paradise with a variety of scenic attractions including:

  • Warren Dunes State Park, home to some of the most impressive sand dunes in the Midwest and offering breathtaking views of Lake Michigan
  • The Indiana Dunes National Park, just a short drive away across the Michigan-Indiana border, features a diverse range of habitats, from wetlands and prairies to savannas and forests
  • Red Arrow Highway, a winding route that offers scenic drives with spectacular views of the countryside, charming towns, and picturesque lakeshore communities

Hiking/Biking Routes of Union Pier

The trails and paths of Union Pier offer ample opportunities for hiking, biking, and other outdoor adventures. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Union Pier:

  • The Pigeon Creek Trail, a picturesque trail that winds through the woods and offers stunning views of Lake Michigan
  • The Galien River Trail, a tranquil trail that follows the course of the Galien River, offering beautiful views of wetlands and wildlife
  • The Warren Woods State Park, an idyllic park with a dense forest of towering trees and a variety of wildlife

Best Things to Do in Union Pier: Activities and Tours

Union Pier offers a multitude of exciting activities and tours that the whole family can enjoy. Here are some must-do activities in Union Pier:

  • Relax on the pristine beaches of Union Pier with a refreshing drink and a good book.
  • Explore the local wineries and sample some of the region’s best vintages.
  • Go kayaking or canoeing along the calm waters of the Galien River.
  • Take a scenic drive along Red Arrow Highway and experience the natural beauty of southwest Michigan.
  • Visit the farmer’s markets and immerse yourself in the local culture.
  • Go apple picking or pumpkin picking in the fall with your family.

Week-Long Itinerary for Union Pier

With so much to see and do in Union Pier, it’s easy to fill up your itinerary with great activities. Here’s a suggested itinerary for a week in Union Pier:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Blueberry Pancakes at Black Currant Bakehouse
  • Smoked Fish or Steaks at Redamak’s Tavern
  • Brick Oven Pizza at Stop 50 Wood Fired Pizzeria
  • A hearty breakfast at Grampa Boo’s Kitchen

Local Beverages

  • Michigan Craft Beers at Noteworthy Brewery & Taproom
  • Wine Tasting at Round Barn Winery and Lemon Creek Winery

Where to Stay in Union Pier: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Union Pier offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best places to stay in Union Pier:

  • Gordon Beach Inn, a luxurious hotel offering spectacular views of Lake Michigan
  • The Inn at Union Pier, a charming bed and breakfast with organic, farm-to-table breakfast
  • Camping at Warren Dunes State Park, an ideal option for budget-conscious travelers
  • Renting a cabin at Hearth & Timber

What to Buy in Union Pier: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Union Pier offers a wealth of souvenirs and local products that make for great memories of your trip. Here are some of the best things to buy in Union Pier:

  • Wines from local vineyards
  • Fresh produce and homemade jams from local farmers’ markets
  • Handcrafted pottery and artwork from local artists and galleries
  • Body products like soaps, lotions, and candles made from local herbs and ingredients

Local Culture and Customs of Union Pier

Union Pier is known for its friendly and welcoming locals who embrace their heritage and local traditions. Visitors are encouraged to respect the local customs and engage with the locals to learn more about their unique way of life.

Best Time to Visit Union Pier

The best time to visit Union Pier is during the summer months, between June and August when the weather is warm and sunny, and the beaches are open for swimming and sunbathing. Fall is another great time to visit, with the gorgeous foliage and delicious harvest festivals.

Do’s and Don’ts of Union Pier

Here’s a quick list of do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Union Pier:

Do:

  • Try the local wines and beers
  • Respect the local customs and traditions
  • Take advantage of outdoor activities

Don’t:

  • Litter on the beaches or in the parks
  • Ignore the safety rules when swimming or boating
  • Mistreat the wildlife

Safety and Health

Safety in Union Pier

Union Pier is a very safe holiday destination, but it’s always wise to take the usual precautions when traveling. Visitors should be aware of their surroundings and avoid leaving valuables unattended.

Health in Union Pier

Union Pier is a relatively safe destination from a health perspective with no major health concerns. However, it’s always best to pack sunscreen, bug spray, and any other medications you may need.

Getting to Union Pier: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Union Pier

The closest commercial airports to Union Pier are:

  • Chicago Midway International Airport, located in Chicago, approximately 80 miles away
  • Chicago O’Hare International Airport, located in Chicago, approximately 95 miles away

Bus Stations Near Union Pier

The nearest bus stations to Union Pier are:

  • Greyhound Station, located in Michigan City, approximately 12 miles away
  • Amtrak Station, located in New Buffalo, approximately 7 miles away

Other Options

Visitors can also opt to drive to Union Pier, or hire a car from the nearby airports.

How to Get Around in Union Pier

The most convenient way to get around Union Pier is by car. However, visitors can also rent bicycles from local rental shops to explore the area at a leisurely pace.

Where to Go Next from Union Pier: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Union Pier is conveniently located near many other exciting destinations that make great day trips. Here are some suggestions:

  • Saugatuck, Michigan: A quaint town known for its art galleries and charming shops, approximately a 90-minute drive up the coast
  • South Haven, Michigan: A picturesque town with a lighthouse and a beautiful beach, approximately a 75-minute drive up the coast
  • Chicago, Illinois: A major city famed for its architecture, museums, and delicious food scene, approximately a 90-minute drive
  • Indiana Dunes National Park: Just over the border in Indiana, this beautiful national park offers stunning natural beauty and endless outdoor activities.

Conclusion

Union Pier is an ideal retreat for those looking for a relaxing yet adventurous getaway. With its natural beauty, rich history, and friendly locals, Union Pier offers something for everyone. Plan your trip now and explore all the wonders this charming destination has to offer!
