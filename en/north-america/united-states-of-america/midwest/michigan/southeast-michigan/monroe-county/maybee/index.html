Introduction to Maybee

Maybee is a small village located in Monroe County, in the southeastern part of Michigan, known for its lush natural beauty, rich history, and charming small-town feel. It offers a perfect escape for those looking to explore the idyllic countryside of Michigan, away from the hustle and bustle of city life. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take you through the best places to visit, what to do, where to eat and stay, and the safety tips to keep in mind while exploring Maybee.

List of Places to Visit in Maybee

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Oakwoods Metropark

Oakwoods Metropark is a 1553-acre park that offers a variety of recreational opportunities for visitors, including hiking, biking, fishing, golfing, and picnicking. It is also home to the Oakwoods Metropark Nature Center, where visitors can learn about the natural history of the area through interactive exhibits, live animal displays, and nature-inspired activities. The park is open year-round and is a popular destination for birdwatchers in the winter months.

Monroe County Historical Museum

The Monroe County Historical Museum is located in the heart of downtown Monroe, just a short drive from Maybee. It provides visitors with a glimpse into the county’s rich past through its extensive collection of artifacts, exhibits, and archives. The museum is open year-round and offers guided tours as well as special events and activities throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of Maybee: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Sterling State Park

Sterling State Park is located on the shores of Lake Erie, just a short drive from Maybee, and is Michigan’s only state park located on the Great Lakes. The park offers a variety of recreational opportunities, including swimming, camping, fishing, and boating. Visitors can also take a leisurely walk along the park’s scenic trails or enjoy a picnicking in one of the many designated areas.

Lower Huron Metropark

Lower Huron Metropark is a 1645-acre park that boasts of scenic picnic areas, an 18-hole golf course, and winding trails that traverse woodlands, wetlands, and rivers. Visitors can go kayaking or canoeing along the Huron River, fish in the park’s ponds and streams, or play games in the park’s designated fields and courts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Maybee

Oakwoods Metropark Trail

The Oakwoods Metropark Trail is a 15-mile trail that winds through the park and offers scenic views of the Huron River and the surrounding woodlands and wetlands. The trail is open to both hikers and bikers and is a perfect way to explore the natural beauty of the area.

Huron River Greenway

The Huron River Greenway is a 275-mile trail that provides visitors with a unique way of exploring the natural beauty of southeast Michigan. The trail passes through several small towns and villages, including Maybee, and offers stunning views of the Huron River and the surrounding landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Maybee: Activities and Tours

River Raisin National Battlefield Park

The River Raisin National Battlefield Park is located in nearby Frenchtown Charter Township and is a great place for visitors to learn about the region’s rich history. The park features several exhibits that showcase the area’s role in the War of 1812, as well as various interactive activities, tours, and ranger programs.

Local Farm Tours

Maybee is surrounded by farmland and offers visitors a chance to experience the region’s vibrant agricultural heritage and culture. Several local farms offer tours, including apple orchard tours, cider mill tours, and wine tasting events.

Food and Drink Scene of Maybee

Must-Try Dishes

Cider mill donuts

When in Michigan, one must try their classic cider mill donuts. The main ingredient in these donuts is the apple cider, which gives it a sweet and tangy flavor that’s perfect for fall. Blake’s Orchard and Cider Mill and Wiard’s Orchard are popular places to try these donuts.

Detroit-style pizza

Detroit-style pizza is a local specialty that is a must-try for visitors to the area. It is a rectangular-shaped pizza with a thick, fluffy crust and a crispy exterior, topped with a blend of meats, cheese, and vegetables.

Local Beverages

Michigan craft beer

Michigan is known for its craft beer scene, and Maybee is no exception to this. Visitors can enjoy a wide range of locally brewed beers at various breweries and taprooms in the area, including the Jolly Pumpkin Artisan Ales and Witch’s Hat Brewing Company.

Michigan wine

Michigan’s wine industry is growing, with several vineyards and wineries located in the southeast Michigan region. Visitors can enjoy a variety of locally produced wines at spots such as Sandhill Crane Vineyards and Chateau Aeronautique Winery.

Where to Stay in Maybee: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hampton Inn Monroe is a modern hotel located in nearby Monroe. It features comfortable rooms, free Wi-Fi, complimentary hot breakfast, fitness center, and a heated indoor pool.

Holiday Inn Express & Suites Monroe

Another popular hotel in the area, Holiday Inn Express & Suites Monroe, offers comfortable rooms with free Wi-Fi, complimentary breakfast, fitness center, and an indoor pool.

What to Buy in Maybee: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Apples

Michigan is known for producing some of the best apples in the country, and Maybee is surrounded by orchards that provide visitors with a chance to taste these crisp, juicy fruits directly from their source.

Honey

Maybee is also home to several beekeepers who produce high-quality honey that captures the unique flavors of the region’s wildflowers and orchards.

Local Culture and Customs of Maybee

Maybee has a rich farming heritage, and visitors can learn more about the local culture and customs by participating in farm tours, cider tastings, and other agricultural events.

Best Time to Visit Maybee

The best time to visit Maybee is from late spring to early fall when the weather is mild, and the outdoor activities are available. Fall is also a great time to visit because of the stunning fall foliage that adorns the region at this time of the year.

Do’s and Don’ts of Maybee

Do:

  • Take the time to explore the region’s natural beauty
  • Try the local cider donuts and Detroit-style pizza
  • Visit local farms for tours and tastings
  • Check out the local craft beer and wine scene

Don’t:

  • Forget to pack comfortable shoes for hiking and exploring

Safety and Health

Safety in Maybee

Maybee is generally safe for visitors, but it is important to take the usual precautions when traveling, such as keeping an eye on your belongings and avoiding walking alone at night in unfamiliar areas.

Health in Maybee

There are no major health concerns in Maybee, but it is always a good idea to pack medication if you have any pre-existing medical conditions.

Getting to Maybee: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Maybee

The closest major airport to Maybee is Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport, located about 25 miles from the village. It is served by several major airlines and offers both domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Maybee

Greyhound bus service operates buses to and from Monroe, which is just a few miles from Maybee.

Other Options

The best way to get around in Maybee is by car or bike, as the village is quite spread out, and public transportation is limited.

Where to Go Next from Maybee: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Ann Arbor

Ann Arbor, located about 40 miles from Maybee, is a vibrant college town that is home to the University of Michigan. It is known for its rich cultural scene, excellent restaurants, and unique shops.

Detroit

Detroit, Michigan’s largest city, is located about 35 miles from Maybee and offers visitors a chance to explore its rich history, culture, and architecture. It is home to several museums, galleries, and theaters, as well as a thriving food and nightlife scene.

Conclusion

Maybee is a hidden gem in southeastern Michigan that offers visitors a chance to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and immerse themselves in the region’s natural beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture. With this comprehensive travel guide, we hope you’ll be inspired to plan your trip to Maybee and discover all that this charming village has to offer.
