Introduction to Belleville

Nestled in Wayne County, Belleville is a charming city located in Metro Detroit, Southeast Michigan. With a rich history, diverse culture, and breathtaking scenery, Belleville has become a popular destination for both domestic and international travelers. From outdoor adventures to cultural attractions, Belleville has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Belleville

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Yankee Air Museum

The Yankee Air Museum is a must-visit for aviation enthusiasts. This museum is dedicated to preserving the history of aviation and educating the public on the role aviation played in America’s history. Visit the museum’s exhibits, which include aircraft, military uniforms, and machinery. Take a tour of the aircraft restoration facility and witness the restoration of vintage planes.

Van Buren Township Historic Park

Step back in time at the Van Buren Township Historic Park, which encompasses three historic buildings. The park features the Eyry House Museum, which dates back to the 1860s, the Grist Mill, which was built in the 1930s, and the Schoolhouse, which is a one-room school built in the late 19th century. Visitors can take a guided tour to learn about the history of the park and its structures.

Natural Attractions of Belleville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Belleville Lake

Belleville Lake is the perfect spot for water sports enthusiasts. This 1,270-acre lake is located in the heart of Belleville and offers numerous opportunities for fishing, swimming, and boating. Take a boat tour of the lake or rent a paddleboard or kayak to explore its beauty.

Lower Huron Metropark

Hike, bike, or horseback ride along the scenic trails of the Lower Huron Metropark. This park encompasses 1,258 acres and features numerous recreational activities, such as picnicking, fishing, and golfing. The park also has a water park, which is ideal for families that want to cool off on a hot summer day.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Belleville

Belleville Lake Loop Trail

The Belleville Lake Loop Trail is a 6.6-mile multi-use trail that circles around Belleville Lake. This trail is ideal for walking, jogging, or cycling and offers scenic views of the lake and the surrounding landscape. The trail is relatively flat and is suitable for all skill levels.

Best Things to Do in Belleville: Activities and Tours

Belleville Downtown Development Authority (DDA) Art Walk

Take a stroll through the Belleville Downtown Development Authority (DDA) Art Walk to admire the works of local artists. The Art Walk features more than 30 murals, sculptures, and installations located throughout downtown Belleville.

Tipping Point Theatre

Catch a play, musical, or comedy act at the Tipping Point Theatre. This professional theatre company produces high-quality performances that are both entertaining and thought-provoking.

Food and Drink Scene of Belleville

Must-Try Dishes

The Woodshop

The Woodshop is a must-visit for barbecue lovers. This restaurant serves up slow-smoked meats, such as brisket, pulled pork, and ribs, as well as delicious sides, such as mac and cheese, coleslaw, and potato salad.

East Lake Chinese Restaurant

East Lake Chinese Restaurant is a favorite among the locals. This family-owned restaurant serves up delicious Chinese classics, such as General Tso’s chicken, orange beef, and vegetable lo mein.

Local Beverages

North Center Brewing Company

North Center Brewing Company is a cozy taproom that serves up craft beers, ciders, and meads. The taproom also features a small menu of pub fare, such as nachos and pretzels.

Belleville Brewing Company

Belleville Brewing Company is a small microbrewery that produces artisanal beers using locally sourced ingredients. Visitors can sample a flight of beers or purchase a growler to take home.

Where to Stay in Belleville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hyatt Place Detroit/Ann Arbor

The Hyatt Place Detroit/Ann Arbor is a modern hotel that offers spacious rooms and suites, as well as a range of amenities, such as a fitness center, pool, and restaurant. The hotel is located near Belleville and is only a short drive away from downtown Ann Arbor.

Holiday Inn Express & Suites Belleville

The Holiday Inn Express & Suites Belleville is a budget-friendly hotel that offers comfortable rooms and suites, as well as a range of amenities, such as a fitness center and complimentary breakfast. The hotel is located near downtown Belleville and is only a short drive away from Belleville Lake.

What to Buy in Belleville: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Van Buren Township Farmer’s Market

The Van Buren Township Farmer’s Market is a great place to buy locally grown produce, baked goods, and artisanal products. Visitors can also enjoy live music and entertainment while shopping.

Local Culture and Customs of Belleville

Belleville is a friendly and welcoming city that embraces its history and culture. Visitors should take the time to learn about the city’s rich heritage, traditions, and customs.

Best Time to Visit Belleville

The best time to visit Belleville is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny. Visitors can enjoy outdoor activities, such as hiking, boating, and swimming, and attend festivals and events, such as the Belleville National Strawberry Festival.

Do’s and Don’ts of Belleville

Do:

  • Do try the local barbecue and Chinese food.
  • Do take a boat tour of Belleville Lake.
  • Do visit the Yankee Air Museum.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural environment.
  • Don’t disregard local culture and traditions.
  • Don’t be disrespectful to the locals or fellow travelers.

Safety and Health

Safety in Belleville

Belleville is a safe city that experiences low crime rates. Visitors should exercise common sense and take precautions to safeguard their personal belongings.

Health in Belleville

Healthcare facilities are readily available in Belleville and the nearby cities. Visitors should carry adequate travel insurance and have a basic first-aid kit.

Getting to Belleville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Belleville

The nearest airport to Belleville is Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport, which is located 11 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Belleville

The Greyhound Bus Lines station in Ann Arbor is the closest bus station to Belleville and is located approximately 15 miles away.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel to Belleville by car or taxi from nearby cities, such as Ann Arbor, Detroit, and Ypsilanti.

How to Get Around in Belleville

Visitors can easily get around Belleville by car, bike, or on foot. Rental cars and bicycles are available from local vendors.

Where to Go Next from Belleville: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Ann Arbor

Ann Arbor is a vibrant college town located approximately 17 miles away from Belleville. Visitors can explore the city’s museums, theaters, and historic landmarks, as well as enjoy its vibrant nightlife and dining scene.

Detroit

Detroit is a dynamic city located approximately 30 miles away from Belleville. Visitors can discover the city’s rich history, architecture, and cultural offerings, such as the Detroit Institute of Arts, the Motown Museum, and Campus Martius Park.

Conclusion

Belleville is a hidden gem in Southeast Michigan that offers a rich blend of history, culture, and outdoor adventures. With its friendly locals, excellent cuisine, and picturesque scenery, Belleville is the perfect destination for travelers who want to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and experience the beauty of rural America.
