Introduction to Canton

Located in Wayne County in Metro Detroit, Canton is a charming town in Southeast Michigan. Known for its rich history, natural beauty, and cultural attractions, Canton offers visitors a range of exciting activities and must-see sights.

Whether you are exploring the town’s landmarks, enjoying the great outdoors, or indulging in some of the local food and drinks, Canton has something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll explore the top things to do, places to visit, and where to eat and stay in Canton. Let’s get started!

List of Places to Visit in Canton

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. The Village Theater - Enjoy world-class performances at The Village Theater, a popular cultural destination in Canton that frequently hosts musicals, comedy shows, and other live events.

  2. Canton Historical Museum - Learn about the town’s rich past at the Canton Historical Museum, which offers exhibits on Native American history, the Underground Railroad, and the Vietnam War.

Natural Attractions of Canton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Heritage Park - Spend a day at Heritage Park, a beautiful 300-acre park in Canton that offers hiking, fishing, and picnicking opportunities, as well as a water park and playground area for kids.

  2. Lower Rouge River Trail - Take a leisurely stroll or bike ride along the Lower Rouge River Trail, which stretches for several miles and offers stunning views of the river and surrounding natural scenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Canton

  1. Lower Rouge River Trail - This scenic trail offers plenty of spots for picnic breaks, wildlife sightings, and even fishing. It stretches for 27 miles and is perfect for a leisurely day out.

  2. Canton Dog Park - This dog-friendly park offers several paved and unpaved hiking trails for visitors and their furry friends.

Best Things to Do in Canton: Activities and Tours

  1. Pheasant Run Golf Club - Enjoy a round of golf at Pheasant Run Golf Club, a beautiful 18-hole course that offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

  2. SkyZone Trampoline Park - For a fun day out with the kids, head to SkyZone Trampoline Park, which offers a range of activities including trampoline basketball, dodgeball, and foam pit jumping.

Food and Drink Scene of Canton

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Cantonese Pork - Made with stir-fried pork and mixed veggies, this is a must-try dish that perfectly blends sweet and savory flavors.

  2. Lamb Vindaloo - An Indian classic, this spicy lamb dish is sure to please those who love bold flavors and plenty of heat.

Local Beverages

  1. The Canton Creamery - This ice cream shop is a local favorite, offering a wide range of delicious flavors crafted from locally sourced ingredients.

  2. Canton Brew Works - For a refreshing beer or cider, head to Canton Brew Works, a new brewery that offers a range of craft beers and tasty appetizers.

Where to Stay in Canton: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Hyatt Place Detroit/Novi - This stylish hotel is located near the town center and offers comfortable rooms, a fitness center, and an indoor pool.

  2. La Quinta Inn & Suites - This budget-friendly option offers clean, comfortable rooms, a complimentary breakfast, and a central location close to many area attractions.

What to Buy in Canton: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Canton Farmers Market - This bustling market is a great place to pick up fresh produce, artisanal cheeses, and handcrafted gifts made by local artisans.

  2. Leslie Science & Nature Center - Stop by this local nature center to pick up unique gifts, including handmade birdhouses, woolen sweaters, and other nature-themed souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Canton

Canton is a town that is proud of its rich cultural heritage, and visitors can expect a warm welcome and plenty of opportunities to learn more about the town’s traditions and customs. From exploring the town’s museums and landmarks to indulging in the local food and drink scene, there is always something new and exciting to discover here.

Best Time to Visit Canton

The best time to visit Canton is during the months of April through October, when the weather is mild and pleasant and there are plenty of outdoor activities and events to enjoy. If you’re planning a winter visit, be sure to pack warm clothing as temperatures can drop below freezing.

Do’s and Don’ts in Canton

Do:

  • Show respect for the town’s local customs and traditions.

  • Try as many local dishes as possible.

  • Spend time outdoors enjoying the town’s natural beauty.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the town’s public spaces.

  • Be overly loud or disruptive in public areas.

  • Ignore the town’s safety guidelines or warnings.

Safety and Health in Canton

Safety in Canton

Canton is generally a safe town for visitors, but it’s always a good idea to exercise caution and common sense when exploring new areas. Be sure to keep your valuables close at hand, avoid walking alone at night, and follow any safety guidelines provided by local authorities or hosts.

Health in Canton

Canton offers plenty of healthcare options for visitors, including several hospitals and clinics throughout the town. If you experience any health issues during your visit, be sure to seek out medical attention as soon as possible.

Getting to Canton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Canton

  1. Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport - Located just 10 miles away from Canton, this airport is a convenient option for travelers arriving by airplane.

  2. Toledo Express Airport - This airport is located approximately 45 miles south of Canton.

Bus Stations Near Canton

  1. Greyhound Bus Station - Located in nearby Detroit, this bus station offers connections to a range of domestic and international destinations.

  2. Michigan Flyer - Provides service to Ann Arbor and Detroit Metropolitan Wayne County Airport with stops in Canton.

Other Options

  1. Car Rental - Several car rental companies operate within the Canton area, offering visitors the freedom and flexibility to explore the town and surrounding areas at their own pace.

  2. Local Taxi And Ride Share Services - Ride share services like Uber and Lyft are available throughout Canton, as well as several local taxi companies.

How to Get Around in Canton

The best way to get around Canton is by car, as the town is spread out and many of the top attractions are located a few miles apart from each other. Visitors can easily rent a car through one of the several rental companies operating in the town, and many of the hotels offer complimentary shuttles to local attractions.

Where to Go Next from Canton: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Ann Arbor - Located just an hour’s drive from Canton, Ann Arbor is a college town known for its cultural attractions, including the University of Michigan and the Ann Arbor Art Fair.

  2. Detroit - Just a 30-minute drive from Canton, Detroit is a bustling city offering a range of cultural attractions, including the Detroit Institute of Arts, the Motown Museum, and the Detroit Historical Museum.

Conclusion

Canton is a charming town with plenty to offer visitors of all ages and interests. Whether you’re exploring the town’s rich history, enjoying the great outdoors, or indulging in some of the local food and drinks, you’re sure to have an unforgettable vacation in Canton. Plan your trip today and get ready to discover all that this fascinating town has to offer!
