Introduction to Franklin

Franklin is a charming village located in Oakland County and a popular destination in Metro Detroit. The village boasts a perfect blend of natural beauty, historical landmarks, and modern amenities that make it an excellent base for exploring the area. The village has a population of over 3000 and covers a total area of 2.7 square miles.

List of Places to Visit in Franklin

Franklin has something for everyone, from cultural landmarks to natural attractions. Here are some of the top places to visit in Franklin:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Franklin Historical Museum

Franklin Historical Museum tells the story of Franklin through its artifacts, photographs, and exhibits. The museum also hosts events throughout the year, including an annual open air market in the summer.

  • The Franklin Cider Mill

The Franklin Cider Mill is a historic water-powered cider mill that has been operating since the 1830s. Visitors can watch cider being made, purchase homemade donuts, and other goodies here.

Natural Attractions of Franklin: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Heritage Park

Covering over 211 acres, Heritage Park is a huge park located in Franklin. The park features several picnic areas, hiking and biking trails, playgrounds, and even a splash park.

  • Franklin River

Franklin River is an excellent location for swimming, fishing, and kayaking. The river is surrounded by dense forests and offers a serene and picturesque setting.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Franklin

Franklin boasts several hiking and biking trails that offer stunning views and access to its natural beauty. Some popular routes include:

  • Franklin Hike and Bike Trail

The Franklin Hike and Bike Trail runs for over five miles through the woods, along the river, and around local neighborhoods. It offers an excellent opportunity for visitors to discover the beauty of the village.

  • Paint Creek Trail

Paint Creek Trail is a 8.9-mile paved trail that passes through natural forests and prairies, providing stunning views of the surrounding area.

Best Things to Do in Franklin: Activities and Tours

Besides outdoor activities and sightseeing, Franklin has several things to do for those seeking more adrenalin-pumping and thrilling activities like:

  • Escape Room Zone

Escape Room Zone is an interactive adventure game where players are challenged to solve puzzles and escape within a limited time frame.

  • Sailing on Cass Lake

Sailing on Cass Lake is a fun way to explore the village’s natural beauty. Visitors can rent a sailboat or take a sailing lesson to experience the lake fully.

Food and Drink Scene of Franklin

Franklin offers a range of culinary delights to satisfy every palate, from traditional American cuisine to gourmet European fare.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Coney Dogs

Coney Dogs are a must-try food in Detroit. These hot dogs are a local specialty, served with chili, mustard, and chopped onions.

  • Detroit-style Pizza

Detroit-style pizza is a must-try dish that originated in the city. It features a square-shaped crust that’s thick and crispy with toppings placed under the cheese.

Local Beverages

  • Vernors Ginger Ale

Vernors Ginger Ale is a signature drink of Detroit, often referred to as “Detroit’s Drink.” It’s a delicious blend of ginger, vanilla, and spices, and is thought to have medicinal benefits.

  • Beer and Microbreweries

There are several local bars and breweries in Franklin that offer a range of craft beer and microbrews for beer enthusiasts.

Where to Stay in Franklin: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Franklin has several accommodation options, ranging from budget hostels to luxury hotels.

  • The Franklin Village Boutique Hotel

The Franklin Village Boutique Hotel offers luxurious accommodation in the heart of the village. It features a spa, outdoor pool, and a fine dining restaurant.

  • HI Detroit Hostel

HI Detroit Hostel is a budget-friendly option that offers clean and comfortable rooms, a shared kitchen, and a lounge area.

What to Buy in Franklin: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Franklin is home to several boutique shops and artisan stores that sell unique souvenirs and locally made products that visitors can take home as a memento of their trip.

  • Franklin Village Boutique

The Franklin Village Boutique offers a range of locally crafted jewelry, clothing, and home decor items.

  • Rodnick Chieftain Shop

Rodnick Chieftain Shop offers a range of Detroit-themed gifts, including vintage T-shirts, hats, and posters.

Local Culture and Customs of Franklin

Franklin, being a Metro Detroit village, has a rich cultural heritage and is diverse in its people, history, and traditions. Visitors should be respectful of these local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Franklin

The best time to visit Franklin is between April and October when the weather is pleasant, and outdoor activities can be enjoyed to the fullest.
