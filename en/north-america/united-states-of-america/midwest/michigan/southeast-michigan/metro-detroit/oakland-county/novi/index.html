Introduction to Novi

Located in Oakland County, Metro Detroit, Southeast Michigan, Novi is a charming destination in the Midwest of the United States of America. It is a bustling city with a vibrant culture and many must-visit attractions. Novi is home to a thriving business community, many natural attractions, and cultural hotspots worth exploring. Here’s a comprehensive guide to Novi for your upcoming trip.

List of Places to Visit in Novi

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Novi Historical Museum: A small but significant museum, the Novi Historical Museum is one of the best places to visit if you want to learn about the city’s history. It houses artifacts, photographs, and documents showcasing the city’s past.
  • The Holocaust Memorial Center: This educational center is dedicated to preserving the memory of the Holocaust. It has an extensive collection of exhibits, art, and literature related to the Holocaust.

Natural Attractions of Novi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lakeshore Park: One of the best parks in Novi, it has more than 10 miles of hiking and biking trails, picnic areas, and a beach where visitors can swim, boat, or fish.
  • Maybury State Park: This park has more than 1,000 acres of land, multiple hiking trails, a working farm, a picnic area, and a fishing pond.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Novi

  • ITC Corridor: This 2.4 miles biking trail is an unpaved, natural surface trail ideal for hiking and cycling. It is a beautiful trail that will take you through picturesque landscapes.
  • Lakeshore Park Trail: This 3.5 miles biking trail offers stunning lake views and occasional wildlife sightings along the way.

Best Things to Do in Novi: Activities and Tours

  • Paradise Park: This amusement park features go-karts, mini-golf, batting cages, laser tag, and an indoor ropes course.
  • Emagine Novi: A movie theater that offers luxury seating, dining options, and state-of-the-art technology.
  • AirTime Trampoline: A trampoline park that offers various activities like trampoline dodgeball, slam dunk basketball, and fitness classes.

Food and Drink Scene of Novi

Must-Try Dishes

  • Coney Dog: a Detroit classic made with hot dog, chili, mustard, and onions.
  • Greek Salad: Fresh, crisp greens, Greek olives, tomatoes, and feta cheese.

Local Beverages

  • Michigan Cider: Made from locally grown apples, this refreshing beverage is a must-try when in Novi.
  • Vernors Ginger Ale: A potent mix of ginger and soda that will give your taste buds a unique flavor.

Where to Stay in Novi: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hyatt Place Detroit/Novi: A popular hotel that includes an indoor pool, free breakfast, and a fitness center.
  • Four Points by Sheraton Detroit Novi: This hotel has a prime location that offers access to many attractions and amenities.

What to Buy in Novi: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Founder’s Mad Tea: This locally made tea is a perfect souvenir to take home.
  • Sanders Chocolate: Satisfy your sweet tooth with delicious chocolates that are made locally.

Local Culture and Customs of Novi

  • Novi is a diverse city with a strong Arab-American community.
  • The city hosts a variety of cultural and religious events throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Novi

The best time to visit Novi is during the summer months (June to August) when the weather is warm and the outdoor activities are at their best.

Do’s and Don’ts of Novi

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and cultures.
  • Dress accordingly when visiting religious or cultural sites.

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave trash in public areas.
  • Drink and drive.

Safety and Health

Safety in Novi

Novi is generally safe. However, it is essential to take the usual precautions and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Novi

Novi has excellent healthcare facilities. In case of emergency, the nearest hospital is Ascension Providence Hospital in Novi.

How to Get Around in Novi

Novi is a small city, and it is easy to navigate by car, taxi, or public transportation.

Where to Go Next from Novi: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Detroit: The Motor City is a vibrant city with multiple attractions, from music to sports.

Conclusion

Novi is a fantastic destination for anyone looking for an exciting and memorable trip. With a range of attractions, outdoor activities, and delicious food options, Novi is a city that has something for everyone.
