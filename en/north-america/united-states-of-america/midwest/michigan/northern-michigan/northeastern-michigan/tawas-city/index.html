Introduction to Tawas City

Tawas City is a charming city in northeastern Michigan, located in the northern part of Lower Peninsula. This stunning destination is well-known for its natural beauty, scenic hiking trails, and local culture. Tawas City is a perfect vacation spot for those looking for a relaxing and peaceful vacation in a serene environment. This travel guide is created to help visitors experience the best of Tawas City.

List of Places to Visit in Tawas City

Whether you are a nature lover, history enthusiast, or adventure seeker, there is something for everyone in Tawas City. Here is a list of some of the must-visit places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tawas Point Lighthouse: Built in 1876, this historic lighthouse has been invaluable for the Great Lakes’ maritime traffic.
  • Iosco County Historical Museum: This museum houses numerous valuable exhibits that showcase the rich history of the Tawas City area.
  • Au Sable River Queen: Enjoy an exciting riverboat ride and explore the beautiful Au Sable River.

Natural Attractions of Tawas City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Huron National Forest: Home to over 330,000 acres of forests, lakes, and rivers.
  • Tawas Bay Beach: A beautiful sandy beach along Lake Huron is a perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, and playing volleyball.
  • Tawas Point State Park: Explore the scenic trails and sandy beaches and enjoy swimming, wildlife watching, or fishing at this beautiful park.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tawas City

If you are an adventure enthusiast, Tawas City offers several hiking and biking trails with breathtaking views of the wilderness. Here is a list of some of these trails:

  • Corsair Trail: A 9-mile trail that winds over hills, climbs over rocks and runs through a dense, beautiful forest.
  • Chippewa Trail: A 4.5-mile loop through the Huron-Manistee National Forest that offers beautiful views of the Au Sable River.
  • Tawas Bay Pedestrian and Bike Path: A scenic 6.4-mile trail that cuts through lush green forests and offers views of Lake Huron.

Best Things to Do in Tawas City: Activities and Tours

Tawas City offers visitors some exciting things to do, whether it’s exploring the beaches, hiking the trails, or learning about the local history. Some of the best activities to do in Tawas City are:

  • Fishing: Tawas Bay is an excellent place for fishing, with several species of fish like salmon, walleye, and trout.
  • Kayaking and Canoeing: Explore the scenic Au Sable River from a kayak or canoe.
  • Tour of Tawas Bay: Explore the scenic Tawas Bay and enjoy beautiful views aboard the Lady of the Lake.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tawas City

If you are planning a week-long vacation, here is a recommended itinerary to help you maximize your stay in Tawas City:

Day Activities
Day 1 Visit Tawas Point Lighthouse and stroll along Tawas Bay Beach.
Day 2 Rent a bike or hike through the Huron National Forest trails.
Day 3 Visit Iosco County Historical Museum and explore local history.
Day 4 Take a riverboat cruise on the Au Sable River.
Day 5 Enjoy water sports at Tawas Point State Park.
Day 6 Visit the Corsair Trail and hike around.
Day 7 Take a sightseeing tour of Tawas Bay.

Food and Drink Scene of Tawas City

Tawas City has a thriving local cuisine scene, with several restaurants serving delicious dishes made from fresh local produce. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pasties: A meat-filled pastry of Finnish origin that is popular in the area.
  • Whitefish: A delicious lake fish that can be prepared in several different ways.
  • Porketta: A mouth-watering slow-roasted pork dish that originated from Italy.

Local Beverages

  • Ciders: Michigan is known for its excellent apple ciders, and Tawas City is no exception.
  • Wine: Tawas City has some wineries that produce some fantastic wines made from locally grown grapes.
  • Beer: Tawas City has a few microbreweries that produce some unique craft beers.

Where to Stay in Tawas City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Tawas City has several excellent accommodation options, ranging from luxury hotels to budget guesthouses. Here are some of the best options:

  • Tawas Bay Beach Resort: A beautiful resort that offers breathtaking views of Lake Huron.
  • Bay Inn: A budget-friendly motel that is well-equipped with all essential amenities for a comfortable stay.
  • Tawas Bed and Breakfast: A charming guesthouse that offers beautiful views and delicious homemade breakfast.

What to Buy in Tawas City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Tawas City is home to several local shops that sell unique souvenirs and products that reflect the area’s culture. Here are some of the must-buy items:

  • Handmade Jewelry: Tawas City has a few local artisans that produce beautiful handmade jewelry.
  • Maple Syrup: Michigan is famous for its delicious maple syrup, and Tawas City is no exception.
  • Locally made candles and soaps: Tawas City has a few shops that sell locally made candles and soaps that make for great gifts.

Local Culture and Customs of Tawas City

Tawas City is a friendly community that values its local traditions and culture. Visitors are expected to show respect to locals and their customs. Tawas City hosts several festivals and events throughout the year that showcase the area’s culture and history.

Best Time to Visit Tawas City

The best time to visit Tawas City is during the summer months, from June to September. The weather is warm, and there are several outdoor activities to enjoy during this time.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tawas City

Tawas City is a safe destination for tourists, with low crime rates. However, it’s always advisable to follow basic safety precautions, such as locking car doors and securing your valuables.

Health in Tawas City

Tourists traveling to Tawas City should ensure they are up to date with routine vaccinations. Visitors should also carry necessary medications and follow basic hygiene practices to prevent illness.

Getting to Tawas City: Transportation Options and Logistics

Tawas City is well-connected by several transportation options, making it easy to reach.

Airports Near Tawas City

The nearest major airport is Alpena County Regional Airport, located about 40 miles from Tawas City. Flint Bishop International Airport is located about 120 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Tawas City

There are no direct bus services to Tawas City. However, several bus services connect nearby cities like East Tawas and Detroit.

Other Options

Visitors can reach Tawas City by driving or hiring a taxi. Tawas City is well-connected by several highways, making it easy to get to.

How to Get Around in Tawas City

The best way to get around Tawas City is by car. Visitors can also rent a bike or walk around the neighborhoods to explore the area’s natural beauty.

Where to Go Next from Tawas City: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Mackinac Island: A beautiful island that is famous for its stunning natural beauty, horse-drawn carriages, and historic architecture.

Conclusion

Tawas City is a hidden gem located in northeastern Michigan, offering visitors stunning natural beauty, exciting activities, and a friendly local culture.
