Introduction to Interlochen

Interlochen, located in the Grand Traverse Bay Area of Northern Michigan, is a small town that is famous as a cultural hub. It is home to the Interlochen Center for the Arts, a prestigious institution for music, theater, and dance. The town is surrounded by natural wonders, including shimmering lakes, green forests, and rolling hills, making it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Whether you enjoy hiking and biking, exploring cultural attractions, or simply relaxing in beautiful surroundings, Interlochen has something to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Interlochen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Interlochen Center for the Arts

The Interlochen Center for the Arts is the town’s most famous attraction. The campus includes the Interlochen Arts Academy, a high school for the arts, and the Interlochen Arts Camp, a summer camp for children and young adults. The center is home to several performance venues, including the Kresge Auditorium and the Dendrinos Chapel and Recital Hall. Visitors can attend concerts, plays, and other performances throughout the year.

Music House Museum

The Music House Museum is a unique museum that showcases antique music machines and instruments from the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Visitors can see and hear player pianos, phonographs, and other musical machines in action, as well as browse exhibits on the history of recorded music.

Sleeping Bear Dunes National Lakeshore

Sleeping Bear Dunes National Lakeshore is a stunning natural attraction that offers visitors the chance to explore towering sand dunes, clear lakes, and lush forests. The park includes several hiking trails, scenic drives, and beaches that are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing.

Natural Attractions of Interlochen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Interlochen State Park

Interlochen State Park is a gorgeous park that is popular for its hiking and biking trails, picnic areas, and beaches. The park features several small lakes that are ideal for fishing and boating, as well as campsites for overnight stays.

Boardman River

The Boardman River is a scenic river that runs through the town of Interlochen. Visitors can go kayaking, canoeing, or tubing down the river, or simply enjoy a peaceful stroll along its banks.

Betsie River

The Betsie River is another popular outdoor attraction that offers visitors the chance to go fishing, kayaking, or canoeing. The river is surrounded by lush forests and provides stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Interlochen

Betsie Valley Trail

The Betsie Valley Trail is a beautiful trail that runs for 22 miles from Thompsonville to Frankfort. The trail is perfect for biking and hiking, and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

North Country Trail

The North Country Trail is a 4,600-mile hiking trail that spans seven states, including Michigan. The trail passes through Interlochen and is a popular spot for hiking, backpacking, and wildlife viewing.

Best Things to Do in Interlochen: Activities and Tours

Interlochen Arts Festival

The Interlochen Arts Festival is a series of cultural events that takes place throughout the summer. The festival includes concerts, theater performances, and other cultural activities, and attracts visitors from all over the world.

Cherry Festival

The Cherry Festival is an annual event that takes place in nearby Traverse City. The festival celebrates the region’s famous cherry harvest and includes parades, concerts, and other fun activities.

Food and Drink Scene of Interlochen

Must-Try Dishes

Cherry Pie

Cherry pie is a popular dessert in Northern Michigan, and Interlochen is no exception. The region’s cherries are used to make delicious pies that are served in many local restaurants and bakeries.

Whitefish

Whitefish is a type of fish that is commonly caught in the nearby Great Lakes. It is often served smoked or grilled and is a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

Local Beverages

Cherry Wine

Cherry wine is a unique local beverage that is made from the region’s cherries. It has a sweet and fruity taste and is perfect for sipping on a warm summer evening.

Craft Beer

Interlochen is home to several great craft breweries that produce a variety of delicious beers. Be sure to try some of the local favorites while you’re in town.

Where to Stay in Interlochen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Interlochen Motel

The Interlochen Motel is a comfortable and affordable option that is located within easy walking distance of the Interlochen Center for the Arts. The motel offers clean and cozy rooms, as well as free Wi-Fi and parking.

Interlochen House

The Interlochen House is a charming guesthouse that is perfect for families or small groups. The house features three bedrooms, a full kitchen, and a cozy living room, as well as a private yard and patio.

What to Buy in Interlochen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Cherry Products

Cherry products, such as jams, jellies, and syrups, are popular souvenirs from Northern Michigan. Many local stores and markets sell these delicious products, so be sure to pick up a few jars to take home with you.

Artwork

Interlochen is known for its thriving arts community, and visitors can purchase artwork, such as paintings and sculptures, from local galleries and artists.

Local Culture and Customs of Interlochen

Interlochen is a small town with a strong sense of community. Locals are friendly and welcoming, so visitors should feel free to strike up a conversation or ask for recommendations on things to do and see.

Best Time to Visit Interlochen

The best time to visit Interlochen is during the summer months, when the weather is warm and sunny and there are plenty of outdoor activities and festivals to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Interlochen

  • Do visit the Interlochen Center for the Arts and attend a performance or event
  • Do explore the natural beauty of Interlochen State Park and the Sleeping Bear Dunes National Lakeshore
  • Do try some of the local cherry products and craft beer

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural surroundings
  • Don’t be disrespectful to locals or other visitors

Safety and Health

Safety in Interlochen

Interlochen is generally a safe place to visit, and visitors should feel comfortable walking around town and exploring its many attractions. As with any destination, visitors should take common sense precautions such as locking doors and securing valuables.

Health in Interlochen

Interlochen is home to several medical facilities, including the Munson Medical Center in nearby Traverse City. Visitors should make sure they have adequate health insurance coverage before traveling and bring any necessary medications with them.

Getting to Interlochen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Interlochen

The nearest airports to Interlochen are Cherry Capital Airport in Traverse City and Manistee County Blacker Airport in Manistee. Both airports offer daily flights to several major cities in the region.

Bus Stations Near Interlochen

The nearest bus stations to Interlochen are located in Traverse City and Cadillac. Both stations offer regular service to several major cities in the region.

Other Options

Renting a car is also a good option for visitors to Interlochen, as it allows for easier exploration of the surrounding countryside.

How to Get Around in Interlochen

The best way to get around in Interlochen is by car or bicycle. Car rentals are available at the nearby airports, and several local shops rent bicycles for visitors to use.

Where to Go Next from Interlochen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Traverse City

Traverse City is a charming town located just a short drive from Interlochen. It is known for its picturesque downtown area, beautiful beaches, and thriving food and drink scene.

Mackinac Island

Mackinac Island is a popular tourist destination located in Lake Huron. Visitors can explore the island’s historic sites, enjoy the beaches and hiking trails, and sample the famous Mackinac fudge.

Conclusion

Interlochen is a must-visit destination for anyone who loves natural beauty, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures. With its stunning scenery, friendly locals, and vibrant arts scene, Interlochen is the perfect vacation spot for families, couples, and solo travelers alike.
