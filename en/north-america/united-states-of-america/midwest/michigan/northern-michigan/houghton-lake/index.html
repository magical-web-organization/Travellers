Introduction to Houghton Lake

Houghton Lake is a popular lake town located in Northern Michigan, in the Midwest region of the United States of America. The town is home to Michigan’s largest inland lake, which offers plenty of opportunities for water sports and fishing. With several natural attractions and cultural institutions, Houghton Lake is a great destination for families, couples, and solo travelers alike.

List of Places to Visit in Houghton Lake

Whether you’re interested in exploring natural wonders or cultural landmarks, Houghton Lake has something to offer. Here’s a list of must-visit places in Houghton Lake.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re a history buff or curious about local culture, Houghton Lake has several museums and historical sites to explore.

  • Hartwick Pines State Park: This park features Michigan’s largest known pine forest and features a museum showcasing the state’s logging history.
  • Ralph A. MacMullan Conference Center: Offers a unique opportunity to explore conservation and the environment; it is also a great place for conferences, meetings, and events

Natural Attractions of Houghton Lake: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Houghton Lake is home to multiple natural attractions, and outdoor enthusiasts will enjoy exploring the lush forests, lakes, and other natural attractions in the area.

  • Houghton Lake: Enjoy boating, swimming, and waterskiing in Michigan’s largest inland lake.
  • Tisdale Triangle Pathway: A scenic hiking and biking trail winding through the woods and along the lake

Hiking/Biking Routes of Houghton Lake

If you’re looking for outdoor adventures, Houghton Lake is an ideal destination. Some of the best hiking and biking trails in the area include:

  • North Higgins Lake State Park Trail: A 6.5-mile loop trail featuring stunning lake views and plenty of nature sightings.
  • Markey Township Trailhead: A great spot to start exploring the Houghton Lake area on foot or bicycle.

Best Things to Do in Houghton Lake: Activities and Tours

With plenty of attractions to explore, you’ll never run out of things to do in Houghton Lake.

  • Fishing: Houghton Lake is home to several species of fish, and fishing is a popular pastime in the area.
  • Water Sports: Enjoy boating, jet-skiing, and waterskiing on Michigan’s largest inland lake.
  • Golf: Several championship golf courses offer beautiful fairways and scenic views in the Houghton Lake area.

Week-Long Itinerary for Houghton Lake

If you’re planning a longer visit to Houghton Lake, consider this itinerary:

Day 1 - Explore the natural beauty of Houghton Lake Day 2 - Visit the Hartwick Pines State Park and learn about Michigan’s logging history. Day 3 - Take a leisurely bike ride through Tisdale Triangle Pathway. Day 4 - Enjoy a round of golf at one of Houghton Lake’s many golf courses. Day 5 - Spend the day fishing on Houghton Lake. Day 6 - Take a tour of the Ralph A. MacMullan Conference Center. Day 7 - Enjoy water sports and beach activities on Houghton Lake.

Food and Drink Scene of Houghton Lake

Houghton Lake has a fantastic food and drink scene with a range of local cuisine and craft beer. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pasties: This is a UP classic meal, which consists of mincemeat, root vegetables, and gravy, served in a pastry crust.

Local Beverages

  • Michigan Craft Beer: Houghton Lake offers several craft breweries, which produce fantastic beer with local ingredients.

Where to Stay in Houghton Lake: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Houghton Lake offers several accommodation options for travelers of all ages and budgets, including hotels, hostels, and guest houses.

Budget Accommodation

  • Sunset Resort: A budget-friendly option located near the lake, featuring basic amenities and a lovely view.

Mid-Range Accommodation

  • Lakeside Resort and Conference Center: A mid-range accommodation option near the lake and Convention Center. It features spacious rooms and stunning lake views.

High-End Accommodation

  • Houghton Lake Resorter: A high-end, deluxe accommodation experience that features private beach front and waterfront suites styled for comfort with luxury amenities.

What to Buy in Houghton Lake: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When visiting Houghton Lake, it’s worth picking up some souvenirs and locally-made products. Here are some ideas:

  • Stone Art: Local artisans create stunning pieces from raw stones such as Petoskey Stones.
  • Maple syrup: Made in Michigan, this delicious syrup makes for a great souvenir or gift.
  • T-shirts Charm bracelets, shot glasses: Houghton Lake features various souvenir shops that items you can bring home with you.

Local Culture and Customs of Houghton Lake

In Houghton Lake, people are friendly and tend to embody the best of Northern hospitality.

Best Time to Visit Houghton Lake

While Houghton Lake is a great destination year-round, the best time to visit is during Michigan’s summer months.

Safety and Health

Safety in Houghton Lake

Houghton Lake is a generally safe area, but it’s always important to take precautions when exploring a new destination. Use common sense, such as locking your car and keeping valuables out of sight.

Health in Houghton Lake

It’s important to stay hydrated, wear sunscreen, and practice good hygiene when traveling to Houghton Lake.

Getting to Houghton Lake: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Houghton Lake

The nearest airport to Houghton Lake is Cherry Capital Airport, located in Traverse City, approximately 65 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Houghton Lake

Greyhound offers bus service to a nearby location, Harrison, but there are several bus companies with access to the area and major cities in the Midwest.

Other Options

Renting a car is the best option when traveling around in Houghton Lake and the surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Houghton Lake

The best way to get around in Houghton Lake and the surrounding areas is by car. Taxis and ride-sharing services tend to be less available in these rural areas.

Where to Go Next from Houghton Lake: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Several great destinations are within a short drive from Houghton Lake.

  • Sleeping Bear Dunes: Explore the towering sand dunes and scenic Lake Michigan views in this unique destination.
  • Traverse City: Michigan’s Cherry Capital is home to many local artisan and craft beer establishments.
  • Michigan’s Upper Peninsula: The UP provides a chance for visitors to experience scenic waterfalls, national parks, and stunning views of Lake Superior.

Conclusion

Houghton Lake is a hidden gem in Michigan’s tourism sector. With natural beauty, outdoor adventure opportunities, cultural landmarks, and a vibrant food and drink scene, Houghton Lake is an ideal destination for any type of traveler.
