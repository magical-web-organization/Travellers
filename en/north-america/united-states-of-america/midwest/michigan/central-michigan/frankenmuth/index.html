Introduction to Frankenmuth

Frankenmuth is a unique city located in Central Michigan with a rich German heritage. From its charming Bavarian architecture to its delicious authentic German cuisine, Frankenmuth is a true gem for tourists. The city is known for its beer festivals, world’s largest Christmas store, and more. This travel guide will provide you with all the necessary information you need for exploring Frankenmuth.

List of Places to Visit in Frankenmuth

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum of Military and Space Education: Learn about the history of the US military and space exploration.
  • Frankenmuth Historical Museum: An interactive museum that displays the history of Frankenmuth from the year 1845.
  • Bavarian Belle Riverboat: Experience a leisurely narrated riverboat tour of the Cass River.
  • St. Lorenz Lutheran Church: A beautiful Lutheran church with German-style architecture.
  • Bronner’s Christmas Wonderland: The world’s largest Christmas store. Bronner’s features over 50,000 Christmas items for shoppers.

Natural Attractions of Frankenmuth: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Frankenmuth River Place Shops: A Bavarian-themed outdoor mall featuring boutique shops and eateries.
  • Riverwalk Pier: A scenic pier overlooking the Cass River. A perfect spot for taking photos and enjoying the beautiful views.
  • Heritage Park: A tranquil park in the heart of Frankenmuth with picnic areas, river access, and beautiful walking paths.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Frankenmuth

  • Bavarian Belle Riverboat Trails: A scenic trail located in downtown Frankenmuth, perfect for walking, biking or jogging.
  • Rails to Trails: A packed trail that runs through Saginaw County and into Frankenmuth. The trail is perfect for biking and hiking.
  • Cass River Greenway Trail: A picturesque trail that runs along the Cass River and connects many parks in Saginaw County.

Best Things to Do in Frankenmuth: Activities and Tours

  • Frankenmuth FunShips: A variety of boat tours that showcase the beauty of Frankenmuth.
  • Frankenmuth Brewery Tour: Learn about the rich history of Frankenmuth brewery and sample beers, root beer, and snacks.
  • Papa’s Pumpkin Patch: A favorite family also a perfect spot for group photos every autumn.
  • Escape Room Frenzy: A fun (and challenging) group activity perfect for families.

Food and Drink Scene of Frankenmuth

Must-Try Dishes

  • Schnitzel: A traditional German dish of boneless meat, coated in breadcrumbs and served fried.
  • Bratwurst: A famous sausage dish usually grilled and served with sauerkraut.
  • Pretzels: An iconic German dish, pretzels in Frankenmuth are made fresh daily.
  • Apple Strudel: A classic German dessert, filled with cinnamon, apples, and raisins and served with vanilla ice cream.

Local Beverages

  • Beer: With a long history of brewing beer, Frankenmuth is the perfect destination to explore local breweries.
  • Root Beer: The Frankenmuth Brewery has been brewing root beer for over 100 years, and it’s a must-try when in town.

Where to Stay in Frankenmuth: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Bavarian Inn Lodge: One of the most popular hotels in Frankenmuth, offering Bavarian-inspired rooms.
  • Frankenmuth Motel: A budget-friendly option located right next to the River Place Shops.
  • Drury Inn & Suites: A hotel just a short walk from downtown Frankenmuth that offers free breakfast and evening snacks.

What to Buy in Frankenmuth: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Christmas Collectibles: Bronner’s Christmas Wonderland is a must-visit if you’re looking to grab souvenirs.
  • Pretzels: Purchase freshly baked pretzels from the pretzel shop in the River Place Shops.
  • Beer: Grab some beer-making kits and beer glasses from the local brewers.

Local Culture and Customs of Frankenmuth

Frankenmuth is known for its German heritage. The locals showcase their culture through the architecture of their buildings, food, and festivals. In addition, the locals are friendly and always willing to share about their culture with visitors.

Best Time to Visit Frankenmuth

The best time to visit Frankenmuth is during the summer months (June-August). The weather is perfect for outdoor activities, and there are plenty of festivals to attend.

Do’s and Don’ts of Frankenmuth

Do:

  • Take part in the annual Bavarian festivals, like Oktoberfest.
  • Be respectful and open to learning about the German culture.
  • Try out traditional German dishes and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Wear inappropriate attire when visiting the churches or festivals.
  • Speak loudly or use profanity in public spaces.
  • Litter or disrespect landmarks and parks.

Safety and Health

Safety in Frankenmuth

Frankenmuth is a safe city to visit. However, it’s still important to be aware of your surroundings as you would in any new place.

Health in Frankenmuth

The local medical center is accessible to anyone who needs it. If you have a medical emergency, always call 911.

Getting to Frankenmuth: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Frankenmuth

  • MBS International Airport: Located only 20 miles from Frankenmuth, MBS is the nearest airport.

Bus Stations Near Frankenmuth

  • Indian Trails Bus Line: The nearest stop to Frankenmuth is in Saginaw.

Other Options

  • Car: Frankenmuth is easily accessible by car, located off I-75.

How to Get Around in Frankenmuth

Frankenmuth is only 3.7 square miles, and most attractions are located within walking distance from one another. However, if walking does not work for you, bike rentals and cars are available for rent.

Where to Go Next from Frankenmuth: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Saginaw: Located only 15 miles from Frankenmuth, Saginaw is a city with many different activities to enjoy.
  • Michigan’s Upper Peninsula: If you’re looking to explore other parts of Michigan, the Upper Peninsula is perfect for outdoor adventures like hiking, camping, and boating.

Conclusion

Frankenmuth is the perfect destination for anyone looking to explore European architecture, history, and German food. With its natural beauty, unique culture, and warm hospitality, Frankenmuth is the perfect destination for families, couples, and solo travelers. Plan your next trip to Frankenmuth and experience the charm and beauty of this wonderful city.
