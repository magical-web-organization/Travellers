Introduction to St. Louis County

St. Louis County, located in the St. Louis Area of Missouri and the Midwest region of the United States, is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and natural beauty. The county boasts a diverse range of attractions, from award-winning museums and historical landmarks to picturesque parks and outdoor adventures.

Local Culture and Customs of St. Louis County

St. Louis County has a rich cultural heritage, with influences from German, Irish, Italian, and African American communities. Some local customs to keep in mind include:

  • Midwestern hospitality and friendliness
  • Rooting for the St. Louis Cardinals and St. Louis Blues sports teams
  • Enjoying local beers and BBQ
  • Visiting historical landmarks and museums

Best Time to Visit St. Louis County

The best time to visit St. Louis County is from late spring to early fall, when the weather is mild and outdoor activities are in full swing. The summer months can be hot and humid, so be sure to pack light clothing and sunscreen. Fall is a popular time to visit for the colorful foliage, while winter can be cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of St. Louis County

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your visit to St. Louis County:

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine, including toasted ravioli and St. Louis-style pizza
  • Visit the Gateway Arch and Museum
  • Explore Forest Park, home to the St. Louis Zoo and Missouri History Museum
  • Tour the Anheuser-Busch Brewery and enjoy a cold beer
  • Attend a St. Louis Cardinals or St. Louis Blues game

Don’t:

  • Rely solely on public transportation - consider renting a car or using a ride-sharing app
  • Underestimate the amount of time needed to explore the Gateway Arch - allow at least 2-3 hours
  • Wander around deserted areas at night, and always avoid dangerous areas of the city
  • Go alone in natural attraction areas and always follow posted safety guidelines

Safety and Health

When traveling to St. Louis County, it’s important to keep safety and health in mind. Here are some tips:

Safety in St. Louis County

  • Avoid dangerous areas of the city, such as East St. Louis
  • Always lock your car and secure valuables
  • Be aware of your surroundings at night and in isolated areas
  • Follow safety guidelines while hiking and biking

Health in St. Louis County

  • Bring sunscreen and insect repellent, especially during the summer months
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated, especially during outdoor activities
  • Be aware of ticks and Lyme disease while hiking
