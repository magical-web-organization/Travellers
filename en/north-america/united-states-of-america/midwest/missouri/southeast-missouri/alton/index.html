Introduction to Alton

Alton is a quaint little town situated in Southeast Missouri - known for its outdoor adventures, scenic views, and historical significance. This town is a perfect destination for nature lovers, history buffs, and adventure enthusiasts. There are several places to explore in Alton that make it a charming vacation spot for travelers of all ages.

List of Places to Visit in Alton

Here’s a list of top-rated destinations in Alton, Missouri:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Oregon County Courthouse Museum
  • Alton Haunted Tours
  • Grand Gulf State Park
  • Mark Twain National Forest

Natural Attractions of Alton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Current River State Park
  • Big Spring
  • Peck Ranch Conservation Area
  • Eleven Point National Scenic River

Hiking/Biking Routes of Alton

Alton has a number of hiking and biking routes that are suitable for adventurers of all skill levels. Some popular trails include:

  • Ozark Trail
  • Mark Twain National Forest

Best Things to Do in Alton: Activities and Tours

Alton has a lot to offer in terms of activities and tours. Here are some of the most popular things to do:

  • Go hiking or kayaking in the surrounding natural areas
  • Explore the haunted sites around the city
  • Attend local festivals and events throughout the year
  • Take a scenic drive on Highway 19

Local Culture and Customs of Alton

When visiting Alton, it’s important to be respectful of local customs and traditions. This is a small town, so people tend to be friendly and welcoming. Some of the local customs include:

  • Saying “hi” to people on the street
  • Holding the door open for others
  • Tipping at least 15% in restaurants

Best Time to Visit Alton

The best time to visit Alton is from late spring to early fall, when weather conditions are pleasant for outdoor activities. The weather tends to be warm and sunny during these months, and there are plenty of outdoor events and festivals to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Alton

Do:

  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions
  • Take advantage of the outdoor activities and scenic views
  • Try the local food and beverages

Don’t:

  • Litter or harm the natural environment
  • Disrespect cultural or historical sites
  • Be rude to locals or other tourists

Safety and Health

Safety in Alton

Alton is a safe town to visit, with low crime rates. However, it’s important to take basic precautions, such as locking your car and not leaving valuables unattended.

Health in Alton

There are a few medical facilities in Alton, including the Ozark Medical Center. However, it’s important to have proper health insurance before traveling to ensure access to necessary medical care.

Getting to Alton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Alton

The nearest airports to Alton are Springfield–Branson National Airport and Memphis International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Alton

Greyhound has a bus station in nearby Poplar Bluff, Missouri.

Other Options

Driving is the most convenient way to get around Alton and the surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Alton

Alton is a small town, so walking is often the best way to get around. However, if you’re planning to explore the natural areas around the city, it’s best to have your own transportation or rent a car.

Where to Go Next from Alton: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have extra time, here are some nearby destinations worth exploring:

  • Branson, Missouri
  • Memphis, Tennessee
  • St. Louis, Missouri

Conclusion

Alton is a hidden gem in Missouri that offers plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures, cultural sightseeing, and local dining experiences. Make sure to respect the local customs and traditions, and get ready for a memorable trip to this charming Southeast Missouri town.
