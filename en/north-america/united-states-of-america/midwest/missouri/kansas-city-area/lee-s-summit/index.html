Introduction to Lee’s Summit

Lee’s Summit is a picturesque city in the heart of Midwest, in the Kansas City area of Missouri, in the United States of America. It is home to more than 100,000 people and boasts a rich cultural heritage, a bustling downtown, and abundant natural beauty, making it a perfect destination for travelers seeking both urban and outdoor adventures. With its excellent location, affordable cost of living, and friendly locals, Lee’s Summit has emerged as a popular destination for tourists from all over the world. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore the top places to visit, eat, and stay in Lee’s Summit, along with some insider tips and practical information to help you plan your trip.

List of Places to Visit in Lee’s Summit

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Lee’s Summit has a rich history that is reflected in its many cultural and historical attractions. Some of the must-visit places to explore the city’s heritage include:

  • Historic Downtown Lee’s Summit: A charming district lined up with shops, boutiques, restaurants, and cafes, housed in beautifully restored buildings dating back to the 19th century.
  • Lee’s Summit History Museum: A small museum located in the heart of downtown that showcases the history of the city and its people, including exhibits on the Civil War and the Missouri Pacific trains that once ran through the area.
  • Missouri Town 1855: A living history museum that allows visitors to experience life in mid-19th-century Missouri. It features authentic period buildings, tradespeople, and interpreters, giving you a glimpse of what life was like in the past.

Natural Attractions of Lee’s Summit: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lee’s Summit is blessed with abundant natural beauty, from sprawling parks to serene lakes and forests. Here are some of the top natural attractions you won’t want to miss:

  • Longview Lake: A massive, 930-acre lake located just southwest of Lee’s Summit, offering activities such as boating, fishing, swimming, and hiking. It also features a beach, a marina, and several picnic areas.
  • Unity Village and Rose Garden: A stunning campus with beautifully manicured gardens, water features, and meditative spaces, perfect for picnicking and strolling.
  • Powell Gardens: A botanical garden located just outside Lee’s Summit, featuring a wide variety of fascinations, from prairie landscapes and water gardens to forests and meadows.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lee’s Summit

For outdoor enthusiasts, Lee’s Summit has endless opportunities for hiking, biking, and exploring. Here are some of the top trails in the area:

  • Little Blue Trace Trail: A 15-mile paved trail that extends from Kansas City to the town of Blue Springs, winding along the scenic Little Blue River.
  • Lake Jacomo Trail: A 10-mile trail around a reservoir that offers both paved and unpaved sections, perfect for hiking, biking, and nature-watching.
  • Legacy Park Trail: A 4.6-mile trail that loops through the sprawling Legacy Park, offering scenic views of the lake, fields, and woods.

Best Things to Do in Lee’s Summit: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re interested in history, arts, or outdoor adventures, Lee’s Summit has something for everyone. Here are some of the top activities and tours you won’t want to miss:

  • Trolley Tour of Historic Downtown: A fun and informative tour of the city’s charming downtown on a vintage trolley, complete with colorful commentary from a knowledgeable guide.
  • Mahaffie Stagecoach Stop and Farm: A living history museum that recreates life on the frontier, featuring authentic period buildings, a stagecoach ride, and demonstrations of blacksmithing, farming, and cooking.
  • Paradise Park: A family entertainment center with a wide variety of attractions, including mini-golf, go-karts, laser tag, a petting zoo, and a playground.

Food and Drink Scene of Lee’s Summit

Lee’s Summit has a vibrant food and drink scene that showcases the best of the Midwest, from classic BBQ to craft beer. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kansas City-style BBQ: Lee’s Summit is just a short drive from Kansas City, which is famous for its BBQ. Some of the local favorites include burnt ends, brisket, ribs, and pulled pork, served with tangy BBQ sauce.
  • Fried chicken: A Southern classic that is done particularly well in Lee’s Summit. Try it at Stroud’s or RC’s.
  • Burgers: There are several excellent burger joints in Lee’s Summit, including Summit Grill, Five Guys, and Freddy’s.

Local Beverages

  • Boulevard Beer: This Kansas City-based brewery produces a wide variety of award-winning beers, including the popular Unfiltered Wheat and Tank 7.
  • Cafe Corazon Coffee: A locally roasted coffee that is sold at many cafes and restaurants in Lee’s Summit. Try it at Post Coffee or Primitivo.

Local Culture and Customs of Lee’s Summit

Lee’s Summit is a friendly and welcoming city that is proud of its heritage and culture. Here are some of the customs and traditions that you might encounter:

  • Midwest hospitality: Locals are known for their friendly and helpful nature, making visitors feel right at home.
  • Love of BBQ: Kansas City-style BBQ is a local specialty, and you’ll find it on menus throughout the city.
  • Respect for history: Lee’s Summit has a rich history that is celebrated and preserved through its many cultural and historical attractions.

Best Time to Visit Lee’s Summit

The best time to visit Lee’s Summit is during the spring (March to May) or the fall (September to November) when the weather is mild and the city’s natural beauty is at its peak. Summer (June to August) can be hot and humid, while winter (December to February) can be cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lee’s Summit

Here are some of the things to keep in mind when visiting Lee’s Summit:

Do:

  • Explore Historic Downtown: Lee’s Summit’s charming main street is lined with shops, boutiques, and restaurants, making it a must-visit for any traveler.
  • Try the BBQ: You can’t come to Kansas City and not try the BBQ. Make sure to try burnt ends, brisket, and ribs with the famous KC-style BBQ sauce.
  • Visit the natural attractions: Lee’s Summit is blessed with abundant natural beauty, from serene lakes and forests to sprawling parks and gardens. Make sure to spend some time in nature while you’re here.

Don’t:

  • Overlook Missouri Town 1855: This living history museum is a hidden gem and a must-visit for anyone interested in the region’s history.
  • Skip the trolley tour: The vintage trolley tour of downtown Lee’s Summit is a fun and informative way to learn about the city’s heritage and culture.
  • Underestimate the locals: Lee’s Summit locals are friendly and welcoming, so don’t be afraid to strike up a conversation with them.

Safety and Health

Lee’s Summit is a safe and healthy city to visit, with a low crime rate and excellent medical facilities. Here are some things to keep in mind when traveling:

Safety in Lee’s Summit

  • Avoid the south side of town after dark: While Lee’s Summit is a safe city, the south side of town can be sketchy at night. Avoid walking alone in this area after dark.
  • Lock your car doors: Car theft is a problem in some parts of the city, so make sure to lock your car doors and don’t leave valuables in plain sight.

Health in Lee’s Summit

  • Drink plenty of water: Lee’s Summit can be hot and humid, especially in the summer, so make sure to stay hydrated.
  • Apply sunscreen: The city is known for its outdoor adventures, so make sure to apply sunscreen and wear a hat when you’re out in the sun.

Getting to Lee’s Summit: Transportation Options and Logistics

Lee’s Summit is easily accessible from Kansas City and other nearby cities. Here are some of the transportation options:

Airports Near Lee’s Summit

  • Kansas City International Airport: Located about 40 minutes north of Lee’s Summit. This airport offers direct flights to many domestic and international destinations.

Bus Stations Near Lee’s Summit

  • Greyhound Bus Lines: There is a Greyhound bus station in Kansas City, about 20 minutes from Lee’s Summit.

Other Options

  • Driving: Lee’s Summit is located just off of I-470, making it easily accessible by car.
  • Ridesharing: Services like Uber and Lyft are available in Lee’s Summit.

How to Get Around in Lee’s Summit

Lee’s Summit is a walkable and bike-friendly city, with several biking and hiking trails and a charming downtown district. However, if you need to rent a car, here are some of the top rental options:

  • Enterprise Rent-A-Car: This nationwide chain has several locations throughout Lee’s Summit.
  • Hertz: Another popular car rental company with local locations.

Where to Go Next from Lee’s Summit: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re interested in exploring nearby destinations and excursions, here are some of the top options:

  • Kansas City: Just a short drive from Lee’s Summit, Kansas City is a vibrant city with a rich culture, excellent dining, and entertainment options.
  • Clinton Lake: A beautiful lake with scenic hiking and boating opportunities, located about an hour west of Lee’s Summit.
  • Mark Twain Boyhood Home and Museum: A must-visit for literature lovers, the Mark Twain Boyhood Home and Museum is located about two hours north of Lee’s Summit in Hannibal, Missouri.

Conclusion

Lee’s Summit is a hidden gem that has something for everyone, from history buffs to outdoor enthusiasts. With its natural beauty, rich culture, and friendly locals, it is a perfect destination for travelers looking for a Midwest adventure. Whether you’re exploring the city’s many attractions, indulging in its delicious BBQ, or simply taking in the sights and sounds of this picturesque town, Lee’s Summit is a destination you won’t soon forget.
