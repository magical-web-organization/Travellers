Introduction to Merrillville

Located in Lake County in Northwestern Indiana, Merrillville is a charming town that offers a perfect mix of cultural and natural attractions. From museums and historical places to scenic views and outdoor adventures, there’s something for everyone in Merrillville. Whether you’re a history buff, nature lover, or adventure seeker, this town is a must-visit destination in Indiana.

List of Places to Visit in Merrillville

Here are the top places to visit in Merrillville:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Old Lake County Courthouse: This historic courthouse was built in 1874 and was the seat of Lake County until the 1970s. It is now a museum and is listed on the National Register of Historic Places.

  • Merrillville-Ross Township Historical Society Museum: A great place to learn about the town’s rich history, this museum showcases artifacts and exhibits that tell the story of Merrillville and Ross Township.

  • Deep River County Park: This park is home to the Historic Wood’s Grist Mill, which dates back to the early 1900s. The mill is still operational and is a great place to learn about the town’s agricultural history.

Natural Attractions of Merrillville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Turkey Creek Nature Preserve: With over 500 acres of forest, wetlands, and meadows, the Turkey Creek Nature Preserve is a paradise for nature lovers. You can enjoy hiking, birdwatching, and fishing here.

  • Hidden Lake Park: This park is a popular spot for families and offers a playground, walking path, and a lake where you can fish and paddleboat.

  • Lake County Fairgrounds: The Lake County Fairgrounds hosts several events throughout the year, including the popular Lake County Fair. The fairgrounds also have a campground and RV park.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Merrillville

Merrillville has several great hiking and biking routes for outdoor enthusiasts. Some of the top trails to explore include the Erie Lackawanna Trail, the Calumet Trail, and the Oak Savannah Trail. These trails offer scenic views and a chance to get up close with nature.

Best Things to Do in Merrillville: Activities and Tours

From indoor activities to outdoor adventures, Merrillville has something for everyone. Here are some of the best things to do in the town:

  • Catch a movie at AMC Showplace Merrillville 12: This state-of-the-art theater has 12 screens and shows the latest Hollywood releases.

  • Go shopping at Southlake Mall: With over 130 stores, including popular brands like JCPenney, Macy’s, and H&M, Southlake Mall is a shopping paradise.

  • Take a hot air balloon ride: If you’re looking for a unique way to explore the town, take a hot air balloon ride and enjoy panoramic views of the town and the surrounding countryside.

Food and Drink Scene of Merrillville

Merrillville has a vibrant food and drink scene with a variety of options to suit all tastes and budgets. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chicago-style deep dish pizza: A staple of the Midwest, this hearty pizza is a must-try when in Merrillville. Giordano’s and Rosati’s are two popular pizzerias in the town.

  • Pierogi: These Polish dumplings are a popular dish in Merrillville. Try them at the famous Polish-style buffet, Old Warsaw Restaurant.

Local Beverages

  • Three Floyds Brewing Company: This local craft brewery is known for its award-winning beers and is a must-visit for beer lovers.

  • Indiana wineries: Indiana is known for its wineries, and you can sample some of the best local wines at wineries like Butler Winery and Shady Creek Winery.

Where to Stay in Merrillville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Merrillville has a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. From luxurious hotels to budget-friendly guesthouses, you’ll find something to suit your needs.

Some of the top hotels to consider include:

  • Radisson Hotel at Star Plaza: This luxurious hotel features an indoor pool, fitness center, and an on-site restaurant and bar.

  • Hampton Inn Merrillville: This budget-friendly hotel offers comfortable rooms, free breakfast, and an indoor pool.

What to Buy in Merrillville: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs and local products to bring home, here are some of the best places to shop in Merrillville:

  • Southlake Mall: With over 130 stores, including local boutiques and souvenir shops, Southlake Mall is a great place to shop for gifts and souvenirs.

  • Merrillville-Ross Township Historical Society Museum Gift Shop: This museum has a gift shop that sells local history books, souvenirs, and other items.

Local Culture and Customs of Merrillville

Merrillville has a friendly and welcoming culture, and locals are proud of their town’s rich history and natural beauty. It’s important to respect the town’s culture and customs, including its agricultural history and Midwest hospitality.

Best Time to Visit Merrillville

The best time to visit Merrillville is from May to September when the weather is warm and sunny. This is also the peak tourist season, and you can enjoy numerous outdoor activities and events during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Merrillville

Safety and Health

Safety in Merrillville

Merrillville is a generally safe town, but it’s important to take normal safety precautions. Don’t leave valuables unattended in public places and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Merrillville

There are several hospitals and medical centers in Merrillville, including Methodist Hospitals Southlake and Franciscan Health. It’s recommended to carry travel insurance to cover any medical emergencies.

Getting to Merrillville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Merrillville is easily accessible via several transportation options, including:

  • Gary/Chicago International Airport: This airport is about 16 miles away from Merrillville and offers flights to several domestic destinations.

  • South Shore Line: This commuter rail line connects Chicago to South Bend, and there’s a station in nearby Gary.

  • Interstate 65: This interstate runs through Merrillville and connects it to several major cities and destinations in the region.

How to Get Around in Merrillville

Merrillville has several transportation options to get around the town, including:

  • Rental Cars: Several car rental services are available in Merrillville.

  • Bus Services: The Gary Public Transportation Corporation offers bus services within the town and connects it to nearby towns and cities.

  • Taxis: Taxis are widely available in Merrillville, and you can hail one from the street or book one from a taxi service.

Where to Go Next from Merrillville: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Merrillville is a great base to explore other nearby towns and cities in Indiana. Some of the top destinations to consider include:

  • Chicago, Illinois: Just a short drive from Merrillville, Chicago is a bustling city with world-class museums, restaurants, and a vibrant culture.

  • Indiana Dunes National Park: Located about 20 miles away from Merrillville, this national park offers stunning beaches, sand dunes, and scenic hiking trails.

Conclusion

Merrillville is a hidden gem in the Midwest, with a rich history and a stunning natural beauty. From cultural attractions to outdoor adventures, there’s something for everyone in this charming town. Use this comprehensive guide to plan your trip to Merrillville and enjoy a memorable vacation in Indiana.
