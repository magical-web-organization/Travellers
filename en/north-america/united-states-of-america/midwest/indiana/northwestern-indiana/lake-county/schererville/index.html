Introduction to Schererville

Welcome to Schererville, Indiana – a small town in the northwest part of the state that has a lot to offer. Schererville is a rapidly growing community that prides itself on being one of the safest places to live in Indiana. It’s also a great destination for those looking to experience the outdoors, learn about history, and indulge in local cuisine. Here’s your ultimate travel guide to Schererville!

List of Places to Visit in Schererville

Whether you’re interested in history, outdoor adventure, or local culture, Schererville has plenty of attractions to keep you busy. Here are some must-visit places in Schererville:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Schererville Historical Society Museum: Learn about the town’s history through exhibits showcasing old postcards, photos, and artifacts.
  • Cedar Lake Historical Association Museum: Explore local history through exhibits on Native American life, early settlers, and famous residents.
  • Annie’s Attic & Museum: Discover antique toys and dolls from the 1800s to the mid-1900s.

Natural Attractions of Schererville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Oak Ridge Prairie and Oak Savannah Trail: Hike through prairies and woods, watching for native species of flowers and animals.
  • Schererville Parks: Check out the town’s parks and trails, including Redar Park, Schererville Community Center, and the Schererville Skate Park.
  • Deep River Waterpark: Cool off on hot summer days by floating down a lazy river or plunging down one of the park’s many water slides.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Schererville

If you’re an avid hiker or biker, Schererville has plenty of options for you. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Oak Savannah Trail: This 9-mile trail runs through Schererville and Cedar Lake, offering plenty of scenery and nature to explore.
  • Schererville Park Trails: The town has several short trails that offer a quick escape, including the Schererville Community Center Trail and Rosewood Park Trail.
  • Erie Lackawanna Trail: This 17-mile trail runs through several northwest Indiana towns, including Schererville, and offers a scenic view of the region’s natural beauty.

Best Things to Do in Schererville: Activities and Tours

If you’re looking for activities and tours in Schererville, here are some ideas:

  • Lake County Winery Tour: Sample local wines and learn about the wine-making process at some of the region’s best wineries.
  • Lake Michigan Charter Fishing Excursions: Cast a line with an experienced captain and catch salmon, trout, and other fish in the deep waters of Lake Michigan.
  • Family Fun at Zig-E’s Funland: This family entertainment center has mini-golf, go-karts, laser tag, batting cages, and more.
