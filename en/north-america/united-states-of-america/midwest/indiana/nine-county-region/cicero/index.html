Introduction to Cicero

Nestled in the beautiful Nine-County Region of Indiana, in the heart of the Midwest, Cicero is a hidden gem known for its scenic views, outdoor adventures, and rich cultural heritage. With a population of just over 5,000, Cicero is a quaint and charming town that offers visitors an authentic slice of small-town America.

List of Places to Visit in Cicero

Whether you’re a nature lover, history buff, or an adventure seeker, there’s something for everyone in Cicero. Here’s a list of the top attractions to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Cicero Historical Society Museum - Get a glimpse into the town’s fascinating past by visiting this museum, which features exhibits on local history and artifacts.
  • Strawtown Koteewi Park - A stunning park filled with Native American history and artifacts.
  • The Apple Store & Bakery - A local favorite, with the most delicious apple cider and donuts in the area.

Natural Attractions of Cicero: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Morse Reservoir - Ideal for boating, fishing, and other water activities, this scenic reservoir offers beautiful views and serene natural surroundings.
  • Cicero Pines Golf Course - Enjoy a round of golf while admiring the tranquil beauty of the area.
  • White River Canoe Company - Embark on a canoeing adventure down the picturesque White River and admire the beautiful scenery along the way.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Cicero

Cicero is a hiker’s and biker’s paradise, with numerous trails and routes that offer stunning views and fresh air. Some of the top routes to explore include:

  • The Monon Trail - A 20-mile trail that passes through central Indiana and passes through Cicero. The trail is ideal for biking, hiking, and jogging.
  • The White River Greenway Trail - Follow the White River through scenic areas of Cicero on this trail, which is suitable for walkers, runners, and cyclists of all fitness levels.
  • The Strawtown Koteewi Trail - This trail takes hikers through the stunning Strawtown Koteewi Park, with plenty of opportunities to see local wildlife and admire the natural beauty of the area.

Best Things to Do in Cicero: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are plenty of other fun activities and tours to enjoy in Cicero:

  • White River Zip Line Tours - Glide over the treetops on this thrilling zip line tour that offers incredible views of the White River and the surrounding area.
  • Prairie Winds Nature Farm - Enjoy a guided tour of this charming and picturesque farm that’s filled with lush gardens, farm animals, and plenty of opportunities to relax and enjoy the scenic surroundings.
  • Cicero Artisan and Farmers Market - Take a stroll through this popular market, which offers a wide selection of local produce, crafts, and handmade goods.

Food and Drink Scene of Cicero

Cicero is a haven for foodies and lovers of local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to enjoy:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pork Tenderloin Sandwich - A local favorite, this sandwich features crispy and flavorful pork tenderloin served on a bun with toppings like lettuce, tomato, and mayo.
  • Indiana Sugar Cream Pie - A rich and creamy dessert that’s the official state pie of Indiana.

Local Beverages

  • Apple Cider - Made from locally grown apples, this sweet and refreshing drink is a staple in Cicero.
  • Wine - Indiana is known for its fantastic wineries, and Cicero is no exception. Try a local wine like Chardonel or Traminette.

Where to Stay in Cicero: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Cicero offers a range of accommodations to suit different budgets and tastes. Some of the top options include:

  • Old Towne Lodging - A charming guesthouse that’s centrally located and offers comfortable and affordable accommodation.
  • Harbour Trees Golf & Beach Club - A luxurious and scenic golf and resort club that’s ideal for couples and families.

What to Buy in Cicero: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Take a piece of Cicero home with you by purchasing some of the following local products and souvenirs:

  • Apple Cider - Bring back a bottle of locally made apple cider to enjoy at home.
  • Handcrafted Jewelry - Explore local artisan markets and shops for unique and beautiful handmade jewelry.

Local Culture and Customs of Cicero

Cicero is a warm and welcoming town that values community and hospitality. Locals are friendly and always ready to strike up a conversation, so don’t be afraid to say hello and make some new friends during your visit.

Best Time to Visit Cicero

The best time to visit Cicero is during the summer and fall months, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities. June through September is the peak tourist season, so plan accordingly and book your accommodations in advance.

Do’s and Don’ts of Cicero

Safety and Health

Safety in Cicero

  • Keep your valuables secure and avoid displaying them in public.

Health in Cicero

  • Drink plenty of water and stay hydrated, especially in the summer months.

Getting to Cicero: Transportation Options and Logistics

Cicero is easily accessible by car, bus, and plane. Here are some transportation options to consider:

Airports Near Cicero

  • Indianapolis International Airport - Located just over an hour’s drive from Cicero, this airport serves major airlines and offers both domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Cicero

  • Greyhound Bus Station - Located in Indianapolis, this bus station is just over an hour’s drive from Cicero and offers daily bus services to and from major cities across the country.

Other Options

  • Car Rental - Renting a car is a convenient option for those who prefer to have their own transportation during their stay.
  • Private Car Services - For a more luxurious and comfortable transportation experience, consider booking a private car service.

How to Get Around in Cicero

Cicero is a small town that’s easy to explore on foot or by bike. However, if you prefer to drive, there are plenty of car rental options available.

Where to Go Next from Cicero: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Cicero is located in a beautiful part of Indiana that’s filled with other charming towns and cities to explore. Some nearby destinations to consider include:

  • Zionsville - A charming and picturesque town that’s perfect for a day trip. Explore the local art galleries, boutiques, and coffee shops.
