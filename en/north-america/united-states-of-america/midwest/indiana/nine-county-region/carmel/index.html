Introduction to Carmel

Nestled in the heart of Nine-County Region in the Midwest, Carmel is a quaint but bustling city in Indiana, United States of America. The city is known for its vibrant cultural scene, outdoor adventures, and culinary experiences. Carmel’s unique blend of cosmopolitan and small-town charm makes it the perfect destination for all kinds of travelers.

Local Culture and Customs of Carmel

Carmel is a culturally diverse city that celebrates its history and traditions. Here are some local customs and norms that you should be aware of:

  • Indiana’s state flower is the peony, which is celebrated every year with a festival.
  • Tipping is customary in restaurants and bars.
  • Smoking is banned in public places.

Best Time to Visit Carmel
