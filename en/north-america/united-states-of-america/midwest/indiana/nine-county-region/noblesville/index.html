A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Noblesville, Indiana

Located in the Nine-County Region of Indiana, Noblesville is a charming city with lots to offer. From natural attractions to cultural landmarks and outdoor adventures, there’s something for everyone in this lively Midwestern hub.

Introduction to Noblesville

Noblesville was founded in 1823 and is the county seat of Hamilton County. It’s known for its vibrant downtown area, historic architecture, and scenic parks. The city is easily accessible and located just 30 minutes northeast of downtown Indianapolis.

List of Places to Visit in Noblesville

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Hamilton County Historical Society Museum: Explore local history and artifacts in this small museum located in the historic county sheriff’s residence.
  • The Indiana Transportation Museum: This museum showcases unique exhibits on the history of transportation in Indiana, with a focus on trains.
  • The White River State Park is a personal favourite, featuring the Indianapolis Zoo and an array of activities.

Natural Attractions of Noblesville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Morse Park and Beach: Located on the northern edge of the city, Morse Park and Beach is a popular spot for swimming, picnicking, and boating.
  • Forest Park: This scenic park is located in the heart of Noblesville and features playgrounds, trails, and a golf course.
  • Federal Hill Commons: This park is located adjacent to several shops in downtown Noblesville and is known for its summer concert series and walking trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Noblesville

There are plenty of outdoor recreation options in Noblesville, including several hiking and biking trails.

  • White River Greenway Trail: This paved trail follows the White River for six miles and offers beautiful views of the river and the surrounding woods.
  • Midland Trail: This 1.8-mile trail runs through Forest Park and is popular with both hikers and bikers.
  • Noblesville Cultural Arts District Trail: This 2.5-mile trail runs through downtown Noblesville and is lined with public art and historic landmarks.

Best Things to Do in Noblesville: Activities and Tours

  • Klipsch Music Center: This outdoor amphitheatre hosts a variety of music acts throughout the year.
  • The Escape Room: Put your problem-solving skills to the test in this exciting game of clues and challenges.
  • Nickel Plate Arts: This arts organization hosts classes, workshops, and exhibits throughout the year.

Week-Long Itinerary for Noblesville

Day 1: Arrive in Noblesville and visit the Hamilton County Historical Society Museum, followed by an afternoon swim at Morse Park and Beach.

Day 2: Take a hike on the White River Greenway Trail, followed by lunch in downtown Noblesville and a visit to Federal Hill Commons.

Day 3: Spend the day at the Indiana Transportation Museum, followed by an evening concert at Klipsch Music Center.

Day 4: Explore the Midland Trail in Forest Park and visit the Noblesville Cultural Arts District for some unique local art and souvenir shopping.

Day 5: Enjoy a day of golf at one of Noblesville’s top-rated courses.

Day 6: Visit Nickel Plate Arts for a class or workshop, and then try your hand at the Escape Room in the evening.

Day 7: Depart Noblesville and check out any other nearby attractions or neighboring towns.

Food and Drink Scene of Noblesville

Must-Try Dishes

  • Grindstone Public House: This popular gastropub is known for its craft beers and delicious burgers.
  • Rosie’s Place: This cozy diner serves up breakfast and lunch classics with a twist, including their famous cinnamon roll pancakes.
  • Matteo’s Ristorante Italiano: Enjoy a hearty Italian meal at this family-owned restaurant.

Local Beverages

  • Noble Coffee and Tea Company: Sip on a locally roasted artisan coffee or a hot cup of tea in this cozy downtown spot.
  • Barley Island Brewing Company: This popular brewery serves up a variety of craft beers in a laid-back atmosphere.
  • Redemption Alewerks: Sample some of the best locally brewed beer in Noblesville at this unique brewery.

Where to Stay in Noblesville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Courtyard by Marriott Indianapolis Noblesville: This modern hotel is located in the heart of downtown Noblesville and offers easy access to local attractions.
  • Baymont by Wyndham Noblesville: This budget-friendly hotel is located just a few miles outside of downtown and offers comfortable rooms and amenities.
  • The Prairie Guest House: This charming bed and breakfast is located just outside of Noblesville and offers a peaceful retreat in a country setting.

What to Buy in Noblesville: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Hamilton County Farmer’s Market: This outdoor market features a variety of crafts, produce, and artisan products made by local vendors.
  • Noblesville Antiques on the Square: Browse a variety of unique antiques and collectibles in this charming downtown shop.
  • Logan Village Mall: This indoor shopping center features a variety of locally-owned shops and boutiques.

Local Culture and Customs of Noblesville

Noblesville is known for its friendly locals, strong sense of community, and historic landmarks. Visitors are encouraged to respect local customs, including showing courtesy and kindness towards others.

Best Time to Visit Noblesville

The best time to visit Noblesville is in the summer months between June and August. However, visitors can enjoy a variety of outdoor activities and events throughout the year in this vibrant Midwestern city.

Do’s and Don’ts of Noblesville

Do:

  • Take a hike on one of Noblesville’s scenic trails.
  • Visit the historic Hamilton County Sheriff’s Residence and Jail.
  • Attend a concert at Klipsch Music Center.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural and historic landmarks.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  • Leave valuables unattended in public areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Noblesville

Noblesville is known for its low crime rates and safe environment. Visitors should still take necessary precautions when traveling, including avoiding areas with low lighting at night, keeping valuables locked up and not leaving them unattended, and being aware of surroundings at all times.

Health in Noblesville

Noblesville has several hospitals and medical facilities to meet the medical needs of visitors. It is recommended that visitors carry health insurance and have documentation ready in case of a medical emergency.

Getting to Noblesville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Noblesville

The Indianapolis International Airport is located approximately 45 minutes southwest of Noblesville and offers several transportation options, including taxis, rideshare companies, and rental cars.

Bus Stations Near Noblesville

Greyhound Bus Lines and Miller Transportation both have bus stops in nearby Indianapolis, approximately 45 minutes southwest of Noblesville.

Other Options

Visitors can also arrive by car, with several major highways within close proximity.

How to Get Around in Noblesville

Visitors can get around Noblesville by car, bike, or foot. There are also several public transportation options, including the IndyGo bus system and the Noblesville Access Transit.

Where to Go Next from Noblesville: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Indianapolis: The state capital of Indiana offers a wide range of attractions, including the Indianapolis Motor Speedway, the Indiana State Museum, and the Children’s Museum of Indianapolis.
  • Carmel: This nearby suburb offers several shopping and dining options, as well as several parks and recreational areas.
  • Conner Prairie: This unique outdoor living history museum is located just a few miles north of Noblesville and offers a glimpse into early 19th-century life in Indiana.

Conclusion

Noblesville is a charming Midwestern town that offers something for everyone, from natural attractions to cultural landmarks and outdoor adventures. With friendly locals and a strong sense of community, it’s a great destination for travelers looking for a quintessential American experience.
