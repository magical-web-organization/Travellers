Introduction to Georgetown (Floyd County, Indiana)

Nestled in Southern Indiana, Georgetown (Floyd County, Indiana) is a small town brimming with history and natural beauty. It is a perfect destination for those who seek peaceful, small-town charm with the added benefit of being close to urban areas such as Louisville, Kentucky. Here’s a comprehensive guide on everything you need to know to plan your perfect trip to Georgetown.
