New Albany, Indiana is a charming city located in the southern part of Indiana, in the Midwest region of the United States. Known for its rich history, natural beauty, and friendly locals, New Albany is a popular destination for travelers from all over the world. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will highlight the best places to visit, eat, stay, and more in New Albany, Indiana.

Introduction to New Albany

Founded in 1813, New Albany is the largest city in Floyd County, Indiana. The city is home to a variety of historic landmarks, charming shops, and delicious local restaurants. Visitors can explore the city’s vibrant arts scene, take in stunning views of the Ohio River, or enjoy a relaxing day in one of the many local parks.

List of Places to Visit in New Albany

New Albany offers a wide range of attractions for visitors to explore. From museums and historical landmarks to outdoor adventures and scenic views, there’s something for everyone in this charming city.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

New Albany is home to a number of culturally significant attractions, including several museums and historical landmarks. Some of the must-visit sites include:

  • Culbertson Mansion State Historic Site
  • Carnegie Center for Art and History
  • Underground Railroad Freedom Center
  • Schimpff’s Confectionery

Natural Attractions of New Albany: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For nature lovers, New Albany offers a wealth of outdoor adventures and breathtaking scenic views. Some of the top natural attractions in the area include:

  • Falls of the Ohio State Park
  • The Lillian T. Crawley Nature Preserve
  • Mount Saint Francis Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Clark State Forest

Hiking/Biking Routes of New Albany

New Albany’s scenic trails and parks make it a great destination for hikers and bikers. Some of the best trails in the area include:

  • The Ohio River Greenway
  • Deam Lake State Recreation Area
  • The Knobs State Forest
  • The Bales Trail

Best Things to Do in New Albany: Activities and Tours

In addition to its natural and cultural attractions, New Albany offers a number of fun activities and tours for visitors to enjoy. Some of the top things to do in the area include:

  • Take a riverboat tour of the Ohio River
  • Visit the local farmer’s market
  • Attend a concert or play at the RiverStage
  • Explore the city’s vibrant arts scene
