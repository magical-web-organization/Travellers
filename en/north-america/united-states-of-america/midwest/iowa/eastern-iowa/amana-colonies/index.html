Introduction to Amana Colonies

Nestled in Eastern Iowa, in the heart of America’s Midwest, lies the historic town of Amana Colonies. Amana Colonies is a cluster of seven villages, founded by German settlers in the 1850s. These villages have been preserved for their cultural and historic significance, giving visitors a glimpse of the past.

The town has become a popular destination for travelers, with its quaint architecture, delightful markets, unique shops, and delicious cuisine. The historic sites, along with outdoor recreation offerings, make Amana Colonies the perfect place for a relaxing getaway.

List of Places to Visit in Amana Colonies

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Amana Heritage Society Museum: showcasing the history and culture of Amana colonies through artifacts, exhibits, and guided tours.
  • Amana Woolen Mill: a historic mill dating back to 1857, a must-visit place for those interested in the town’s industrial history.
  • High Amana General Store: a cultural symbol of Amana people, a unique place where you can buy German delicacies, antic items, clothes, hats, and many more rare finds.

Natural Attractions of Amana Colonies: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Iowa River Corridor Trail: a beautiful 6-mile trail, great for hiking, biking, and nature walks. The trail features scenic views of the Iowa River and diverse wildlife.
  • Lily Lake: a serene lake surrounded by lush trees and marshes, perfect for kayaking, bird-watching, or fishing.
  • Tannenbaum Forest: a 20-acre forest that becomes an enchanted wonderland during the holiday season, an excellent choice for families with kids to experience the holiday magic.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Amana Colonies

Amana Colonies offers numerous hiking and biking routes perfect for outdoor enthusiasts, travelers who love adventure and nature. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • Amana Colonies Nature Trail: a paved trail spanning over six miles and an ideal place for biking, hiking, or walking through the charming scenery.
  • Amana Colonies RV Park Trail: a short trail leading through the scenic and natural preserve for nature lovers.
  • Lily Lake Trail: a trail that encircles the lake, with beautiful views of wildlife, perfect for a relaxing nature walk.

Best Things to Do in Amana Colonies: Activities and Tours

  • Wine Tasting at Ackerman Winery: The winery is home to some of Iowa’s best wines. Visitors can enjoy tasting some of the region’s finest wines here.
  • Take a Guided Tour or Ghost Tour: These tours will give you a glimpse of the region’s history, and the ghost tour is a thrilling experience for those who enjoy the eerie.
  • Attend Theatre Performances at Amana Colonies: A great entertainment option for all ages, the theater features live performances every weekend.

Food and Drink Scene of Amana Colonies

Must-Try Dishes

  • German Potato Salad: A traditional German dish made from boiled potatoes and bacon, seasoned with vinegar and spices.
  • Bierocks: A savory roll filled with ground beef, onions, and cabbage.
  • Schnitzel Sandwich: A breaded and fried pork sandwich topped with lettuce, tomato, and creamy German Mustard.

Local Beverages

  • Sarsaparilla: a non-alcoholic beverage with an earthy and sweet taste, considered an Amana Colony staple.
  • Ackerman Winery Wine: A vineyard tucked away in the Amana Colonies, offering a multitude of wine options for every palate.

Local Culture and Customs of Amana Colonies

Amana Colonies is a destination that has been hailed for its cultural touchpoints. The customs and traditions of the German settlers are still alive and thriving in this town. Amana Colony people are known for their hospitality, hard-working ethics, and impressive craftsmanship.

Best Time to Visit Amana Colonies

The best time to visit Amana Colonies is between April to October. During this period, the weather is warm, and outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and walks are perfect to do. From Christmas through February, Tannenbaum Forest and the Midwest Mushroom Festivals flourish this area.
