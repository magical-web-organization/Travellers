Introduction to DeWitt

DeWitt, Iowa, is a charming small town located in Eastern Iowa, in the United States of America. Known for its rich history and beautiful natural landscapes, DeWitt is a great destination for those seeking an authentic American experience. This guide will take you through the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more in DeWitt, Iowa.

List of Places to Visit in DeWitt

When it comes to things to see and do, DeWitt has plenty of options to offer. Below is a list of must-see attractions in DeWitt.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

DeWitt is home to several museums and historical landmarks that provide visitors with insights into the town’s culture and history.

  • The General Tommy Franks Museum: Featuring exhibits that explore the life and career of General Franks, a DeWitt native who served as the commander-in-chief of the United States Central Command.
  • The LincolnWay Heritage Trail: A historic trail that showcases the history and culture of the region.
  • The DeWitt Operahouse Theatre: A beautifully restored historical landmark that hosts theatrical and musical productions throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of DeWitt: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For those seeking outdoor adventures and scenic views, DeWitt has several natural attractions worth exploring.

  • The Springbrook State Park: A beautiful park that offers hiking, camping, fishing, and picnicking opportunities.
  • The West Brook Park: A local park with playgrounds, picnic areas, and hiking trails.
  • The Sweet Woodland Prairie Nature Preserve: A scenic nature preserve that features hiking trails and wildlife viewing opportunities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of DeWitt

DeWitt is surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes, making it a great destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Below is a list of the best routes to explore.

  • The LincolnWay Heritage Trail: A 25-mile trail that passes through the heart of DeWitt, showcasing the town’s unique charm and beauty.
  • The Wapsi River Trail: A 30-mile trail that offers scenic views of the Wapsipinicon River and its surrounding woodlands.
  • The Sweet Woodland Prairie Nature Preserve Trail: A short but scenic trail that leads visitors through the stunning prairies of the nature preserve.

Best Things to Do in DeWitt: Activities and Tours

DeWitt has plenty of activities and tours to offer visitors, ranging from historical tours to water activities.

  • The DeWitt Aquatic Center: A great place to cool off during the hot summer months, featuring a large pool, waterslides, and water toys.
  • The DeWitt Historical Museum: Offering visitors a glimpse into the town’s past through exhibits and artifacts.
  • Canoe and Kayak Rentals: Explore the beautiful Wapsipinicon River by renting a canoe or kayak.

Food and Drink Scene of DeWitt

DeWitt has a thriving food and drink scene, with several restaurants and bars offering tasty and unique options.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Breaded Pork Tenderloin Sandwich: A classic Iowa dish that can be found at several local restaurants.
  • Sweet Corn: A staple of Midwest cuisine, best enjoyed during the summer months.
  • Maid-Rite Sandwich: Another Midwest classic, this sandwich features loose ground beef and a special sauce, served on a bun.

Local Beverages

  • Iowa Craft Beer: Iowa is home to several award-winning craft breweries, and DeWitt has several bars that offer local beer options.
  • Wine: Iowa is rapidly becoming known for its wineries, and DeWitt is home to several vineyards and tasting rooms.

Where to Stay in DeWitt: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

DeWitt has several options for accommodations, ranging from cozy guesthouses to comfortable hotels.

  • The Hotel Blackhawk: A luxurious hotel with modern amenities, located in nearby Davenport.
  • The Victorian Inn: A charming bed and breakfast that offers comfortable accommodations and a delicious breakfast.
  • The Super 8 by Wyndham DeWitt: A budget-friendly option with free breakfast and a convenient location near many of DeWitt’s attractions.

What to Buy in DeWitt: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When it comes to souvenirs and local products, DeWitt has several options to choose from.

  • DeWitt T-Shirts and Apparel: Show off your love for DeWitt with a locally-themed t-shirt or sweatshirt.
  • Locally Made Crafts and Pottery: Several local shops offer handmade crafts and pottery, perfect for unique souvenirs.
  • Sweet Corn: Take home a taste of Iowa with some fresh sweet corn, available during the summer months.

Local Culture and Customs of DeWitt

DeWitt has a rich history and culture, with several annual events and traditions that celebrate the town’s unique charm and character.

  • The DeWitt Ox Roast: An annual event that features live music, food, and carnival rides.
  • The DeWitt Farmers Market: A weekly market that offers fresh local produce, handmade crafts, and more.
  • The DeWitt Opera House Theatre: A historical theater that hosts productions and events throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit DeWitt

DeWitt is a great destination year-round, but the best time to visit depends on your preferences and interests. For those seeking outdoor adventures and natural beauty, the summer months are ideal. If you’re interested in cultural events and festivals, plan your visit around the DeWitt Ox Roast or the Farmers Market season in the fall.

Do’s and Don’ts of DeWitt

Do:

  • Do take time to explore the small downtown area and check out the locally-owned shops.
  • Do try the locally sourced sweet corn - it’s a local specialty!
  • Do bring comfortable shoes for hiking and walking.

Don’t:

  • Don’t miss out on the opportunity to explore DeWitt’s beautiful natural landscapes.
  • Don’t forget to check out the town’s rich history and culture at the local museums and landmarks.

Safety and Health

Safety in DeWitt

DeWitt is generally a safe and friendly town. Visitors should take the same precautions they would in any other small town or rural area, such as being aware of their surroundings at night and not leaving valuables in plain sight in their cars.

Health in DeWitt

DeWitt has several medical facilities and clinics in town, as well as nearby hospitals in Davenport and Cedar Rapids. Visitors should have comprehensive health insurance and take necessary precautions.

Getting to DeWitt: Transportation Options and Logistics

DeWitt is easily accessible by car or by public transportation.

Airports Near DeWitt

The closest airports to DeWitt are the Quad City International Airport in Moline, Illinois, and the Eastern Iowa Airport in Cedar Rapids, Iowa.

Bus Stations Near DeWitt

DeWitt is serviced by several bus companies, including Greyhound and Jefferson Lines.

Other Options

For those traveling from nearby cities, driving or renting a car may be the most convenient option.

How to Get Around in DeWitt

DeWitt is a small town that can easily be explored on foot or by bicycle. Visitors can rent bikes from several local shops, and taxis and ride-sharing services are also available.

Where to Go Next from DeWitt: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

DeWitt is located in close proximity to several other charming towns and cities, making it a great home base for exploring the region.

  • Davenport, Iowa: Located just 20 minutes away, Davenport offers a vibrant downtown area, a variety of cultural attractions, and delicious dining options.
  • Iowa City, Iowa: A charming college town that offers beautiful architecture, cultural events, and a thriving food scene, located about an hour from DeWitt.
  • Galena, Illinois: A picturesque town that offers a variety of shopping, dining, and outdoor activities. Located about an hour and a half from DeWitt.

Conclusion

DeWitt, Iowa, is a charming small town with a rich history and beautiful natural landscapes. Whether you’re seeking outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or simply a relaxing vacation, DeWitt has plenty to offer. Plan your visit today and experience the best of this authentic American destination.
