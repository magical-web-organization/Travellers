Introduction to Ankeny

Ankeny is a charming city located in Central Iowa, United States of America. With a population of approximately 70,000, Ankeny has a small-town feel but offers big-city amenities. The city is filled with green spaces, art galleries, museums, restaurants, and shops, making it a great place to visit.

List of Places to Visit in Ankeny

If you’re planning a visit to Ankeny, here are some of the must-see attractions in the area.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • John Deere Des Moines Works Tours: Take a tour of the John Deere factory and see the construction of industrial and agricultural equipment.
  • Polk County Historical Museum: Learn about the history and culture of Central Iowa, including exhibits on agriculture, industry, transportation, and Native American artifacts.
  • Iowa State Capitol Building: Explore the impressive architecture and political history of Iowa by visiting this state capitol building.

Natural Attractions of Ankeny: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Neal Smith National Wildlife Refuge: A stunning wildlife refuge spanning over 5000 acres, offering hiking trails and opportunities to see bison, elk, and other native wildlife.
  • Prairie Ridge Sports Complex: An outdoor sports complex offering football, soccer, baseball, and softball fields, as well as plenty of walking and biking trails.
  • Ankeny Community School District Prairie Trail: A peaceful 9-mile trail for walking, hiking, or biking, perfect for soaking up the beauty of the Midwestern landscapes.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ankeny

Ankeny is surrounded by natural beauty, and there are plenty of great hiking and biking trails to explore.

  • High Trestle Trail: A popular trail located just 15 minutes north of Ankeny that passes 13 scenic bridges and offers stunning views of the Des Moines River Valley.
  • Saylorville Lake Trail: A 27-mile trail that offers gorgeous views of the lake and surrounding woodlands, with opportunities for hiking, biking, and kayaking.
  • John & Mary Pappajohn Sculpture Park: Explore the outdoor sculpture park and its 28 unique sculptures, perfect for a leisurely walk or bike ride.

Best Things to Do in Ankeny: Activities and Tours

Ankeny has plenty of attractions and activities that will keep you entertained during your visit.

  • Splash Park at Hawkeye Park: A fun and refreshing water park suitable for the whole family, complete with water slides, spraygrounds, and shaded seating areas.
  • The Warrior Run Golf Course: A challenging golf course with unique and stunning views of the surrounding farmland and waterways.
  • Old Town Ankeny: Explore the historical downtown district of Ankeny with plenty of shops, restaurants, and sights to see.

Food and Drink Scene of Ankeny

Ankeny has a diverse food scene, with plenty of options to please all palates. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pork Tenderloin: A popular Iowa specialty, this generous-sized breaded and fried pork tenderloin sandwich is a must-try while in Ankeny.
  • Sweet Corn: This summertime staple is especially delectable in Iowa and is a must-try when in season.
  • Beef Brisket: Juicy tender brisket is a must-taste while dining in Ankeny.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beer: The Ankeny area has several breweries like Fenders Brewing and Firetrucker Brewery, which offer craft beers, and ales.
  • Wine Tasting: D’Vine Wine and Vino209 are some popular wineries in the area.

Where to Stay in Ankeny: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ankeny has several options for accommodations, ranging from luxurious hotels to budget-friendly guesthouses.

  • Courtyard by Marriott: A hotel with spacious rooms and modern amenities.
  • Holiday Inn Express and Suites: Well-equipped rooms and excellent amenities, including a pool, hot tub, and fitness center.
  • Comfort Inn & Suites: A budget-friendly hotel offering comfortable rooms and free breakfast.
  • Airbnb and Vrbo: Private rooms, or full-house rentals, at a variety of price points, can be booked via online rental platforms.
  • Farmhouse Bed & Breakfast: Located on a picturesque farm near Ankeny, this cozy bed and breakfast offers a peaceful retreat from the city.

What to Buy in Ankeny: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs or unique local products to bring home, here are some suggestions;

  • Soliday’s Wildlife Shoppe: Offers various hunting and fishing gear in addition to a range of animal mounts.
  • Gong Fu Tea: A unique and stylish tea shop offering a range of high-quality tea blends and accessories.
  • Ankeny Art Center Gift Shop: A gift shop located inside the Ankeny Art Center, with a variety of artworks ranging from pottery and jewelry to paintings and prints.

Local Culture and Customs of Ankeny

Ankeny is a welcoming community that represents American Midwestern culture, which places great importance on family, kindness, and hospitality. Visitors should note that Iowans are known for their politeness and friendliness with requests made pleasantly and politely.

Best Time to Visit Ankeny

Iowa has a four-season climate, which means that there is always something to do in Ankeny no matter the time of year. The best time to visit Ankeny is from April to October when the weather is mild, and nature is in full bloom.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ankeny

Here are some Do’s and Don’ts to ensure you have a hassle-free trip to Ankeny:

Do:

  • Bring sunscreen and insect repellent when exploring the outdoors.
  • Follow the local hand gestures while driving. Some of them may differ from other regions.
  • Dress in layers, as the weather can be unpredictable.

Don’t:

  • Littering is not tolerated and can lead to a hefty fine.
  • Smoking in public places is prohibited, and certain restaurants/bars have smoke-free policy.
  • Underage drinking is strictly prohibited and can lead to arrest and hefty fines.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ankeny

Ankeny is a very safe city, and violent crime is rare. Visitors should remain alert and take the same safety precautions they would in any American city: avoid walking alone at night, lock the car while parked, and keep valuables out of sight.

Health in Ankeny

Ankeny has several medical facilities that offer various levels of medical care, and the city has a good health system that meets international standards. Visitors should have adequate health insurance coverage before entering the United States.

Getting to Ankeny: Transportation Options and Logistics

Ankeny is well-connected and easy to navigate, regardless of whether you’re coming from within Iowa or any other part of the world.

Airports Near Ankeny

The Des Moines International Airport is 16 miles southwest of Ankeny and is the closest airport, and it offers flights to and from many national and international destinations.

Bus Stations Near Ankeny

Jefferson Lines operates a bus service that connects numerous Midwest destinations, including Ankeny.

Other Options

Ankeny has excellent roads that connect the town to other Iowa locations, making it easy to drive to. Many car rental companies operate out of Des Moines International Airport, making it easy to rent a car and explore the area at your own pace.

How to Get Around in Ankeny

Ankeny is a very walkable town, and many attractions are within easy reach. However, a car is recommended if you plan to visit other attractions in the surrounding area.

Where to Go Next from Ankeny: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Ankeny is surrounded by many scenic destinations and attractions that make for great day-trips, including:

  • Des Moines: Iowa’s capital, known for its impressive heritage and cultural attractions, like the Pappajohn Sculpture Park and the Science Center of Iowa.
  • Saylorville Lake: A beautiful lake located in Polk County, offering activities like fishing, bird watching, and boating.
  • Ames: Home to Iowa State University and the Reiman Gardens, a beautiful botanical garden with a butterfly exhibit and art displays.

Conclusion

Ankeny is a charming small town that offers plenty of attractions, making it an excellent destination for a fun and relaxing vacation. Whether you love the outdoors, history, art, or just good food, Ankeny has something for everyone. Visit Ankeny, and you’ll discover what makes it such an amazing place!
