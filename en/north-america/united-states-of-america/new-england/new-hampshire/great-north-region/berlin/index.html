Introduction to Berlin

Berlin is a picturesque town located in the Great North region of New Hampshire, in New England, in the United States of America. It is a nature lovers paradise and a perfect spot for adventure enthusiasts. The town offers many outdoor activities, from hiking and biking trails to aerial zip lines and scenic drives.

List of Places to Visit in Berlin

Berlin is a small town but has numerous natural attractions, historical places, and cultural amenities. In this section, we provide you with a list of recommended places to visit during your stay in Berlin:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Northern Forest Heritage Park: This park is a treasure trove of information and artifacts about the history of logging in Northern New England.
  • Androscoggin Wayside Park: This park is perfect for those interested in the history of NH’s grand hotels, as it features an exhibit that tells the story of the nearby Mount Pleasant Hotel, which was destroyed by fire in 1939.
  • Berlin & Coos County Historical Society & Moffett House Museum: This museum is dedicated to preserving the history of Berlin and Coos County and is housed in the former home of a local businessman.
  • St. Kieran Community Center for the Arts: This center offers a year-round program of live performances, as well as art exhibits, classes, and workshops.

Natural Attractions of Berlin: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mt. Washington Auto Road: Take a scenic drive to the summit of Mt. Washington. The breathtaking views of the White Mountains are worth the trip alone.
  • Jericho Mountain State Park: This park is perfect for outdoor adventure enthusiasts. It’s home to over 80 miles of off-road vehicle trails, as well as hiking and biking trails.
  • Androscoggin River: The river offers an abundance of activities such as canoeing, kayaking, and fishing. It is a great spot for nature lovers and perfect for some peaceful relaxation.
  • Moore Dam Reservoir: This reservoir is a beautiful spot for boating, fishing, and swimming. It is a great spot to visit for a picnic or some fun in the water.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Berlin

Berlin offers beautiful hiking trails with scenic views for outdoor lovers. Some of the recommended hiking and biking trails are as follows:

  • Presidential Rail Trail: This trail is perfect for biking, walking, and jogging, and offers stunning views of the beautiful White Mountains.
  • Androscoggin River Trail: The trail is an easy walk along the river suitable for people of all ages. It’s best to visit during fall, as the foliage is breathtaking.
  • Moose Path Trail: This trail is a 1.6-mile loop trail in White Mountains National Forest. It is ideal for hiking and bird watching.
  • ATV Trails: There are many ATV trails near Berlin, and they are perfect for a fun and adventurous day trip.

Best Things to Do in Berlin: Activities and Tours

Berlin has many activities for tourists and visitors. Here are some of the best things to do in Berlin:

  • Jericho Mountain State Park Adventure Tour: This half-day tour includes ATV riding, hiking, fishing, and even a picnic lunch!
  • Moose Safari: Join a guided tour to see these majestic creatures in their natural habitat.
  • Gorham Moose Tours: This tour offers a two and a half hour safari trip to see moose and other wildlife in their natural habitat.
  • Androscoggin Valley ATV Club: This club offers guided ATV tours through the beautiful landscape of the Northern Forest.

Week-Long Itinerary for Berlin

Here is a sample 7-day itinerary for Berlin:

Day Activities
Day 1 Explore the Northern Forest Heritage Park and the Berlin & Coos County Historical Society & Moffett House Museum
Day 2 Visit the Mt. Washington Auto Road and take a scenic drive
Day 3 Take a hike on Moose Path Trail and enjoy the natural beauty
Day 4 Go for some ATV riding at Androscoggin Valley ATV Club
Day 5 Take a day trip to visit the nearby towns of Gorham and Jefferson
Day 6 Enjoy a Moose Safari or Jericho Mountain State Park Adventure Tour
Day 7 Spend a relaxing day at Moore Dam Reservoir and indulge in some water activities

Food and Drink Scene of Berlin

Food lovers can indulge in local delicacies during their stay in Berlin. Here is what you should try.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Burgers at Mr. Pizza: Mr. Pizza is known for their juicy burgers and sides, along with other dishes as well. When you visit Berlin, you can’t miss this.
  • Berlin Hot Dog: Get a traditional New England-style hot dog in Berlin. Do not forget to try it with mustard, onion, and relish.
  • German cuisine: German cuisine is popular in Berlin. You can try staples like pretzels, schnitzel, and sausages.

Local Beverages

  • Local Beers: Berlin has many small breweries that serve local beers. Make sure you try one of them during your stay in Berlin.
  • Specialty Coffee: Berlin has many cafes that serve specialty coffee, which is a must-try for coffee lovers.

Where to Stay in Berlin: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Here are some of the recommended places to stay during your visit to Berlin:

  • Northern Comfort Motel: This motel offers comfortable rooms, a heated indoor pool and hot tub and is located in the heart of the city.
  • The Log Haven: This inn is set in a log cabin and offers cozy, country-style rooms as well as a sauna and a hot tub.
  • The Bunk House: It is a comfortable and budget-friendly hostel for those who want to explore Berlin on a tight budget.

What to Buy in Berlin: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some of the recommended souvenirs and local products to bring home from Berlin:

  • Maple Syrup: NH is known for its maple syrup, and you can get pure and organic syrup from Berlin to bring back home.
  • Handmade Crafts: Berlin has various shops with handmade crafts. You can find various items like pottery, jewelry, and clothing to bring back as souvenirs.
  • Craft Beer: Berlin has many breweries that offer bottles of beer as a souvenir, so make sure you grab a bottle or two to enjoy back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Berlin

Berlin is a beautiful town full of friendly people. It is always best to be polite and courteous to the locals you interact with, respecting their privacy and culture.

Best Time to Visit Berlin

The best time to visit Berlin is the summer months from June to September. The weather is warm, and the town is bustling with tourists and various outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Berlin

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your visit to Berlin.

Do:

  • Be respectful towards the locals, their culture, and customs.
  • Carry a water bottle and wear comfortable shoes while exploring the town.
  • Try to leave the town in a better condition than you found it in.

Don’t:

  • Litter at a public place. Berlin is a small town and as it attracts tourism, it’s important to keep it clean.
  • Intrude or disturb wildlife in their natural habitat.
  • Smoke or litter in a park or forest area.

Safety and Health

Berlin is a safe town with few incidents of crime. However, like any small town, ensure that you take basic precautions, such as locking your car and your room, and being aware of your surroundings.

Safety in Berlin

  • Do not leave valuables in your car or unattended in public places.
  • Always lock your car and room door before leaving.
  • Keep emergency contact and hospital numbers at hand for any contingencies.

Health in Berlin

  • Carry your necessary medication/first aid kit with you.
  • Drink bottled or boiled water, especially if you are not used to the water in town.
  • Check your skin for ticks after a hike in the forest area during tick season.

Getting to Berlin: Transportation Options and Logistics

Berlin is located in the Northern Forest of New Hampshire, accessible by car or bus.

Airports Near Berlin

The nearest airport to Berlin is Portland International Jetport, which is approximately 2.5 hours away by car.

Bus Stations Near Berlin

There is no direct bus service to Berlin. However, several bus companies provide services to nearby towns like Gorham.

Other Options

The best way to travel to Berlin is by car. You can rent a car from rental services in nearby towns.

How to Get Around in Berlin

Berlin is a small town, and most of the tourist attractions are located within a short distance. However, it is best to either bring your car or rent one while visiting Berlin to enjoy the scenic drives and visit nearby attractions.

Where to Go Next from Berlin: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Berlin is located near many beautiful places with various attractions. Some of the recommended nearby destinations to visit are:

  • Mount Washington: Explore the scenic view from the highest mountains in the Northeast.
  • White Mountains: Visit this beautiful scenic mountain range which deserves a detour from your visit to Berlin.
  • North Conway: Visit the famous village town of North Conway, which is known for its shopping, eateries, and stunning scenic drives.

Conclusion

Berlin is a beautiful and scenic town that offers a perfect balance of nature and city life. It is a perfect destination for adventure enthusiasts, history buffs, and nature lovers. With this comprehensive tour guide, you can plan your visit to Berlin with ease and enjoy all that the town has to offer.
