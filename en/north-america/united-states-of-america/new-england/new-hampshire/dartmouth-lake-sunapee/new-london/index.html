Introduction to New London

New London is a town in the Dartmouth Lake Sunapee region of New Hampshire. It is home to about 4,500 people and is a popular destination for tourists looking to explore the area’s natural beauty. The town is located about 90 miles north of Boston and 40 miles west of Concord.

Local Culture and Customs of New London

The people of New London are friendly and hospitable, and the town has a strong sense of community. Visitors are encouraged to explore the local shops and restaurants and to take part in community events and festivals.

Best Time to Visit New London

New London is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit depends on what activities you’re interested in. Summer is great for outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and swimming, while winter is perfect for skiing and snowboarding. Fall is a beautiful time to visit, with the leaves changing colors and plenty of harvest festivals taking place.
