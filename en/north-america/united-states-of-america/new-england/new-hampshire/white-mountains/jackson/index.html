Introduction to Jackson

Jackson is a picturesque town located in White Mountains, New Hampshire. It is a popular destination among nature lovers, hikers, and adventure enthusiasts. The town is surrounded by majestic mountains, picturesque valleys, and cascading rivers making it an ideal spot for outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, skiing, and fishing. Whether you’re looking for some thrilling adventure or just want to relax and soak in the serenity of nature, Jackson has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Jackson

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum of White Mountains: This museum showcases the history, culture, and ecology of the White Mountains. It features a collection of artifacts, photographs, and interactive exhibits that tell the story of the region.

  • Jackson Historical Society Museum: The museum is housed in a 19th-century farmhouse and displays artifacts that illustrate the history of Jackson and its inhabitants.

Natural Attractions of Jackson: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Jackson Falls: Located in the village of Jackson, the falls are a popular spot for picnics and relaxation.

  • Eagle Mountain House and Golf Club: Apart from its stunning golf course, the resort has access to scenic trails that offer breathtaking views of the White Mountains.

  • Black Mountain Ski Area: A popular ski resort in Jackson that offers a variety of trails for skiers of all levels.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jackson

Jackson has a variety of scenic trails for hikers and bikers. Some popular hiking and biking routes include:

  • The Jackson Trail Network: This network offers over 60 miles of scenic trails ranging from easy to difficult.

  • Ellis River Trail: This trail meanders alongside the Ellis River and offers serene views of the waterfalls and scenic foliage.

  • Jackson Ski Touring Foundation: This foundation offers over 150 km of cross-country ski trails that pass through beautiful valleys, knolls, and bridges.

Best Things to Do in Jackson: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Jackson:

  • Skiing and Snowboarding: The Black Mountain Ski Area is a popular ski resort in Jackson that offers a variety of trails for skiers and snowboarders of all levels.

  • Zip-lining: Soar through the trees with zip-lining adventures offered by various adventure companies in Jackson.

  • Horseback Riding: Experience the beauty of White Mountains on horseback with various horseback riding tours offered in the area.

Week-Long Itinerary for Jackson

Here’s how to spend a week in Jackson:

Day Plan
Day 1 Explore the Village of Jackson, visit the Jackson Historical Society Museum & Jackson Falls
Day 2 Hike the Jackson Trail Network or Ellis River Trail, and enjoy the scenic views
Day 3 Visit the Museum of White Mountains, and soak in the beauty of nature
Day 4 Hit the slopes at the Black Mountain Ski Area or enjoy cross-country skiing
Day 5 Experience the thrill of zip-lining and horseback riding
Day 6 Take a scenic drive along the White Mountains and explore nearby towns
Day 7 Relax and rejuvenate at the Eagle Mountain House and Golf Club.

Food and Drink Scene of Jackson

Must-Try Dishes

  • Champagne Chicken: A classic dish in Jackson, made with chicken, mushrooms, and a creamy champagne sauce.

  • Maple Syrup: Jackson is known for its maple syrup production. Try it in pancakes, waffles, or drizzled over ice cream.

Local Beverages

  • Moat Mountain Brewery: This brewery located in nearby North Conway offers a variety of craft beers and pub food.

  • White Mountain Cider Company: Try their signature cider or enjoy a meal at the restaurant.

Where to Stay in Jackson: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Eagle Mountain House and Golf Club: This historic resort offers stunning views of the mountains and features a golf course, spa, and restaurant.

  • Inn at Jackson: This cozy inn features traditional New England architecture and offers comfortable accommodation for travelers.

  • Whitney’s Inn: This charming former farmhouse offers cozy rooms and suites and is located near the Black Mountain Ski Area.

What to Buy in Jackson: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Maple Syrup: Bring home the unique and authentic flavor of New Hampshire with some locally produced maple syrup.

  • Handcrafted Items: Shop for handcrafted items such as pottery, jewelry, and clothes made by local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Jackson

  • Respect for nature: Jackson is known for its pristine natural beauty, and visitors are expected to respect and preserve it.

  • Friendly locals: The residents of Jackson are well known for their friendly and welcoming nature.

Best Time to Visit Jackson

The best time to visit Jackson is from late spring to early fall when the weather is mild, and the scenery is at its best. Summer brings the best weather for outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and fishing. The fall foliage is also a big attraction in Jackson, and hundreds of visitors flock to see the vibrant colors of autumn.

Do’s and Don’ts of Jackson

Do:

  • Take part in outdoor activities like hiking, skiing, and fishing.

  • Show respect for nature and preserve the natural beauty of the area.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or disturb the wildlife.

  • Don’t try to pet or feed wild animals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jackson

  • Check weather conditions and trail conditions before going hiking or skiing.

  • Be aware of black bears and moose in the area and take necessary precautions.

Health in Jackson

  • It is recommended to have travel insurance when visiting Jackson.

  • Be cautious if you have any allergies and always carry any necessary medication.

Getting to Jackson: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Jackson

  • Portland International Jetport: Located 70 miles from Jackson, this is the closest major airport.

  • Manchester-Boston Regional Airport: Located 105 miles from Jackson, this airport has more flights and destinations available.

Bus Stations Near Jackson

  • Conway Scenic Railroad: This scenic railway connects to North Conway and other nearby towns.

  • Dartmouth Coach: This bus service connects Jackson to Hanover and other towns in the region.

Other Options

  • Car Rentals: Rent a car from nearby towns and drive yourself to Jackson.

  • Private Transportation: Many private transportation companies offer shuttle services from nearby airports and towns to Jackson.

How to Get Around in Jackson

The best ways to get around in Jackson are:

  • Car: Rent a car or bring your own to explore the town and surrounding areas.

  • Bike: Rent a bike and explore the many trails and scenic routes of Jackson.

  • Public Transportation: Shuttle services and buses are available to take visitors to nearby towns and attractions.

Where to Go Next from Jackson: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • North Conway: A popular resort town known for its skiing, shopping, and dining.

  • Franconia Notch State Park: A scenic park with hiking trails, biking paths, and stunning views of the White Mountains.

Conclusion

Jackson is a charming town with plenty of outdoor and cultural attractions. It’s a great place to visit for those looking for a summer adventure or a relaxed getaway. With this comprehensive travel guide, visitors can discover all the best things to do, where to stay, and how to get around Jackson.
