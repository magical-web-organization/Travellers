Introduction to Littleton

Littleton, located in the heart of White Mountains in New Hampshire, is a charming small town that offers visitors an array of activities and attractions to explore. With its idyllic location just a couple of hours from Boston and Portland, it’s an ideal getaway for those seeking an escape from their busy lives.

List of Places to Visit in Littleton

Here’s a list of top attractions in Littleton that you shouldn’t miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Littleton Area Historical Society Museum
  • Rocks Estate
  • Bethlehem Heritage Society Museum
  • The Museum of White Mountain Art at Jackson

Natural Attractions of Littleton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Franconia Notch State Park
  • Pemi Valley Blueberry Farm
  • Cannon Mountain
  • The Basin

Hiking/Biking Routes of Littleton

If you’re an adventure enthusiast, Littleton offers plenty of opportunities to explore the stunning White Mountains on foot or on a bike. Some popular trails and routes to consider include:

  • Ammonoosuc Rail Trail
  • Cannon Mountain summit hike
  • Bridal Veil Falls Hike
  • The Flume Gorge Trail

Best Things to Do in Littleton: Activities and Tours

Littleton offers a wide range of activities and tours suitable for all ages and interests. Here are a few favorites:

  • Ice Castles at Loon Mountain
  • Alpine Adventures Outdoor Excursions
  • Hero’s Welcome fly fishing tours
  • Artistic Roots classes and workshops

Week-Long Itinerary for Littleton

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Littleton, here’s a suggested itinerary that will help you make the most out of your visit:

Day Activity
Day 1 Explore the Littleton Area Historical Society Museum, followed by shopping and dining in downtown Littleton
Day 2 Hike Mount Lafayette Trail, followed by dinner at Polly’s Pancake Parlor
Day 3 Spend the Day at Franconia Notch State Park, followed by dinner at Black Mountain Burger Co.
Day 4 Take a scenic drive along the Kancamagus Highway, and stop to explore natural attractions along the way
Day 5 Go fly fishing with Hero’s Welcome, followed by a guided tour of the Rocks Estate
Day 6 Visit the Museum of White Mountain Art at Jackson, followed by a tour and tasting at Schilling Beer Co.
Day 7 Hike the Flume Gorge and have dinner at Woodstock Inn Station and Brewery

Food and Drink Scene of Littleton

Littleton has a vibrant food and drink scene that combines classic New England cuisine with innovative twists and ethnic fusion. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages that you shouldn’t miss:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pancakes and waffles at Polly’s Pancake Parlor
  • Lobster roll at Chang Thai Cafe
  • Pretzels and beer cheese at Schilling Beer Co.
  • Maple-glazed doughnuts at Littleton Diner

Local Beverages

  • Woodstock Inn Brewery beer
  • Wine tasting at Seven Birches Winery
  • Coffee and pastries at Chutters candy store

What to Buy in Littleton: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for unique souvenirs and gifts, Littleton has plenty of shops and boutiques that sell locally-made and sourced goods. Some popular items to bring home include:

  • Maple syrup and candies from Chutters candy store
  • Pottery from The Kiln
  • Artwork from Littleton Studio School Gallery
  • Local cheeses from Meadowstone Farm

Local Culture and Customs of Littleton

Littleton is known for its welcoming and friendly community. Visitors should be respectful of locals and their customs, and embrace the slower pace of life that defines the town.

Best Time to Visit Littleton

The best time to visit Littleton is during the summer and fall months when the weather is pleasant and outdoor activities abound. The fall foliage in the area is also a sight to behold, making it a popular time to visit.

Do’s and Don’ts of Littleton

Do:

  • Observe the local customs and respect the environment
  • Tip generously at restaurants and cafes
  • Take advantage of the outdoor activities and attractions

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural surroundings
  • Be too loud or disruptive in residential areas
  • Forget to carry sun protection or bug spray if you’re hiking or camping

Safety and Health

Safety in Littleton

Littleton is a relatively safe place to visit. However, visitors should take standard safety precautions such as locking their cars and valuables, and being aware of their surroundings when hiking or camping.

Health in Littleton

Littleton has several pharmacies and medical clinics that offer basic healthcare services. However, visitors should bring any necessary medications with them and have adequate health insurance coverage.

Getting to Littleton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Littleton

The closest airports to Littleton are Manchester-Boston Regional Airport (MHT) and Burlington International Airport (BTV), both of which are approximately two hours away by car.

Bus Stations Near Littleton

Visitors can take a Greyhound or Concord Coach bus to nearby communities such as St. Johnsbury or Littleton itself.

Other Options

For those who prefer to drive, Littleton is conveniently located near the east-west US Route 2 and north-south US Route 93.

How to Get Around in Littleton

Renting a car is the best option for getting around Littleton and the surrounding areas. Alternatively, visitors can hire a taxi or use ridesharing services in the area.

Where to Go Next from Littleton: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Littleton is ideally located for those who want to explore other parts of New Hampshire or Vermont. Here are some nearby destinations you should consider:

  • Mount Washington and the White Mountains
  • The Montshire Museum of Science in Norwich, Vermont
  • Saint-Gaudens National Historic Park in Cornish, New Hampshire

Conclusion

Littleton offers visitors the perfect combination of outdoor adventure and cultural experiences. Whether you’re into hiking, biking, or just exploring the charming downtown area, there’s something for everyone. Plan your next trip to Littleton today and enjoy all that this beautiful part of New England has to offer.
