Introduction to East Matunuck

East Matunuck is a beautiful coastal village located in South County, Rhode Island, that offers visitors a unique combination of history, natural beauty, and outdoor adventure. With its sandy beaches, picturesque harbors, and vibrant culture, East Matunuck is a perfect destination for a summer vacation or a weekend escape.

Local Culture and Customs of East Matunuck

East Matunuck has a laid-back culture with an emphasis on community and conservation. Visitors are encouraged to enjoy the natural beauty and respect the local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit East Matunuck

The best time to visit East Matunuck is from June to September when the weather is warm, and the beaches are open. Fall is also an excellent time to visit when the leaves change colours, and the temperatures are mild.

Do’s and Don’ts of East Matunuck

Do:

  • Respect the environment and wildlife.
  • Be mindful when parking in residential areas.
  • Try the local seafood.

Don’t:

  • Litter
  • Go off designated trails in protected areas.
  • Disturb the wildlife.
