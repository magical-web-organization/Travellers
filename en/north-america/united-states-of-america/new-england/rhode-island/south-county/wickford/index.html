Introduction to Wickford

Located in South County, Rhode Island, Wickford is a charming little town with a rich history and natural beauty. The town is known for its picturesque harbor, vibrant art scene, and outdoor activities. Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway, family vacation, or solo adventure, Wickford has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Wickford

Exploring Wickford’s attractions is a great way to enjoy your trip to the fullest. Here are some of the top places to visit in Wickford:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Smith’s Castle: A 17th-century plantation house and museum that offers a glimpse of Wickford’s colonial history.
  • North Kingstown Free Library: A historic library in the heart of town that serves as an active community center.
  • The Gilbert Stuart Birthplace and Museum: A great place to learn about the life and work of the renowned American portrait painter, Gilbert Stuart.

Natural Attractions of Wickford: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Wickford Harbor: A picturesque harbor with stunning views of the boats and surrounding scenery.
  • Wilson Park: A great place to enjoy a picnic or take a stroll along the water.
  • Wickford Village: A charming historic village with many local businesses and shops.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Wickford

Wickford offers many hiking and biking routes that take you through stunning scenery. Here are some of the best routes to try:

  • Wickford North/South Trail: This scenic trail takes you through some of the most beautiful parts of town and is perfect for a leisurely hike or bike ride.
  • Cocumcussoc Trail: This trail takes you through diverse ecosystems and starts at the Smith’s Castle parking lot.
  • Wickford/Quonset Point Bike Path: This scenic path takes you along the waterfront and offers stunning views of the harbor.

Best Things to Do in Wickford: Activities and Tours

Wickford offers many exciting activities and tours that are perfect for all ages. Here are some of the best things to do in Wickford:

  • Wickford Harbor Tours: Take a boat tour of the harbor with a local guide and get an up-close look at the town’s historic sites and beautiful scenery.
  • Kayaking: Spend an afternoon exploring Wickford’s natural beauty with a guided kayak tour.
  • Wickford Art Walk: Explore the town’s vibrant art scene and visit local galleries, studios, and workshops.

Week-Long Itinerary for Wickford

Here’s a comprehensive itinerary for a week-long visit to Wickford:

Day Activity
1 Explore historic Smith’s Castle and its museum.
2 Take a boat tour of the harbor and enjoy the stunning views.
3 Go hiking or biking on the Cocumcussoc Trail.
4 Spend a day kayaking with a local guide.
5 Explore the Wickford Art Walk and visit local galleries and studios.
6 Take a stroll around Wickford Village and check out the local businesses.
7 Enjoy a picnic at Wilson Park before heading back home.

Food and Drink Scene of Wickford

Wickford is known for its vibrant food and drink scene, offering delicious local cuisine and refreshing beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Clam Chowder: A local Rhode Island specialty, this delicious soup is made with freshly caught clams and potatoes.
  • Stuffies: A flavorful dish made with quahog clams, bread crumbs, and spices.
  • Lobster Roll: A classic New England dish that can be enjoyed in most restaurants in Wickford.

Local Beverages

  • Del’s Lemonade: This refreshing lemonade is a Rhode Island favorite and perfect for hot summer days.
  • Providence Coal Fired Pizza Brew: This beer is locally brewed and pairs well with the delicious pizza.

Where to Stay in Wickford: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Wickford offers many accommodation options that cater to various budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best places to stay in Wickford:

  • Wickford Motor Inn: This cozy inn offers comfortable rooms and is located in the heart of town.
  • TownePlace Suites by Marriott: This hotel is perfect for families or solo travelers and offers modern amenities.
  • Quonset Motor Inn: This budget-friendly inn is located near the airport and is perfect for short stays.

What to Buy in Wickford: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Wickford is known for its unique local products and souvenirs that make perfect gifts for loved ones back home. Here are some of the best products to buy in Wickford:

  • Wickford Candle Company: These handmade candles are made with locally sourced ingredients and come in a variety of scents.
  • Rhode Island Red Food Tours: Buy a food tour ticket and explore the local food scene with a local guide.
  • Local Art: Visit local art galleries and studios and bring home a piece of Wickford’s vibrant art scene.

Local Culture and Customs of Wickford

Wickford residents are friendly and welcoming and appreciate visitors who respect their customs and traditions. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Dress appropriately: Respect the local culture and dress modestly when visiting places of worship or other culturally significant sites.
  • Tipping: Tipping is expected in restaurants, bars, and other service industries.
  • Manners: Common courtesy and respect for others are appreciated.

Best Time to Visit Wickford

The best time to visit Wickford is from May to September, when the weather is mild and allows for outdoor activities. However, if you’re looking for beautiful fall foliage, visit in October.

Do’s and Don’ts of Wickford

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your visit to Wickford:

Do:

  • Explore the local art scene and visit local galleries and studios.
  • Try local cuisine and beverages.
  • Respect the local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural areas.
  • Disrespect locals or their traditions and customs.
  • Leave valuables unattended.

Safety and Health

Wickford is a safe place to visit, but it’s important to take necessary precautions to ensure a safe and healthy trip.

Safety in Wickford

  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid dark or isolated areas.
  • Keep your valuables close and secure.
  • Follow all warning signs and guidelines when participating in outdoor activities.

Health in Wickford

  • Be prepared for the weather and wear appropriate clothing and footwear.
  • Stay hydrated and drink plenty of water, especially during outdoor activities.
  • Use sunscreen to protect yourself from the sun’s harmful UV rays.

Getting to Wickford: Transportation Options and Logistics

Wickford is easily accessible by air, road, and water.

Airports Near Wickford

  • T.F. Green Airport is approximately 20 miles away from Wickford and is the closest major airport.

Bus Stations Near Wickford

  • The Rhode Island Public Transit Authority (RIPTA) offers bus services to and from Wickford.

Other Options

  • Uber and Lyft also operate in the area and offer easy and convenient transportation.

How to Get Around in Wickford

Wickford is a small town that can easily be explored on foot, bike, or car. However, if you’re planning to visit nearby attractions, it’s recommended to rent a car or take a taxi.

Where to Go Next from Wickford: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore nearby destinations, here are some great options:

  • Newport: A historic city known for its beautiful mansions, museums, and shopping.
  • Block Island: A beautiful island destination with stunning beaches and nature trails.
  • Providence: A vibrant city with a great food and cultural scene.

Conclusion

Wickford is a charming coastal town that offers a unique blend of history, natural beauty, and cultural attractions. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an exciting adventure, Wickford is definitely worth a visit. With our comprehensive travel guide, you’re well on your way to planning the trip of a lifetime.
