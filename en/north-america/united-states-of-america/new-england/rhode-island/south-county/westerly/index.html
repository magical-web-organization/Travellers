Introduction to Westerly

Westerly, located in South County, Rhode Island, is a charming coastal town known for its beautiful beaches, historic sites, and natural beauty. The town has a rich history, dating back to the 1600s, when it was settled by English colonists. Today, Westerly is a popular tourist destination that attracts visitors from all over the world.

List of Places to Visit in Westerly

There are plenty of things to see and do in Westerly. Here’s a list of the top attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Westerly Armory: Built in 1901, this historic armory is now a museum that showcases the military history of the area.
  • Babcock-Smith House: This historic house was built in 1734 and is now a museum that displays artifacts from the colonial era.
  • Granite Theatre: This historic theatre, dating back to 1921, hosts performances by local and touring theater groups.

Natural Attractions of Westerly: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Misquamicut State Beach: Located on the Atlantic Ocean, this scenic beach is one of the most popular attractions in the town.
  • Napatree Point: This beautiful peninsula is known for its scenic views and is a popular spot for hiking and bird-watching.
  • Westerly Land Trust Trails: The Westerly Land Trust maintains several trails that offer scenic views of the area’s natural beauty.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Westerly

Westerly is home to several hiking and biking routes that cater to all skill levels. Some of the popular routes include:

  • Napatree Point Conservation Area Trail: This trail offers picturesque views of the Atlantic Ocean and the surrounding area.
  • Maxwell Wildlife Refuge Trail: This trail is home to a variety of wildlife, including deer, foxes, and hawks.
  • Westerly Land Trust Trails: The Westerly Land Trust maintains several trail systems that offer a range of hiking and biking options.

Best Things to Do in Westerly: Activities and Tours

Westerly offers a range of fun and exciting activities and tours. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Sunset Sail: Take a sunset sail on a boat and enjoy scenic views of the town.
  • Beach Day: Spend the day at the beach and enjoy swimming, sunbathing, and beach volleyball.
  • Wine Tasting: Take a tour of one of the local wineries and sample some of the finest wines in the town.

Food and Drink Scene of Westerly

Westerly has a vibrant food and drink scene, with plenty of options for all tastes and budgets.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Clam Chowder: Westerly is known for its delicious clam chowder, which is filled with fresh clams and vegetables.
  • Stuffies: This local specialty is a stuffed clam that’s baked to perfection.
  • Lobster Roll: Westerly’s lobster rolls are filled with fresh lobster, served on a buttery bun, and are a local favorite.

Local Beverages

  • Beer: Westerly has a few microbreweries that brew their beer on location, offering a variety of unique flavors.
  • Wine: Westerly is home to several wineries that offer tastings and tours of their facilities.
  • Coffee: There are several local coffee shops around town that offer a variety of coffee blends and flavors.
