Fox Point Travel Guide: Explore the Cultural and Natural Beauty of Rhode Island

Are you looking for a place that offers both history and natural beauty? Then Fox Point in Providence, Rhode Island, is the perfect destination for you! Located in the eastern part of Providence, Fox Point offers a blend of cultural and natural attractions that every traveler would love to explore. In this travel guide, we will take you on a virtual tour of Fox Point, showcasing the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more.

Introduction to Fox Point

Fox Point is a charming neighborhood nestled in the heart of Providence, Rhode Island. It’s named after its location on the eastern waterfront, facing the Fox Point Hurricane Barrier. Fox Point became a significant commercial hub in the late 18th century, and its maritime importance continued through the 19th century. Today, Fox Point is home to many historical buildings, parks, and museums, making it an ideal destination for history buffs and nature lovers alike.

List of Places to Visit in Fox Point

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. The John Brown House Museum: This historic mansion was the home of John Brown, an entrepreneur, and merchant. It’s an exceptional example of American Georigan architecture, with colonial-era furnishings that transport visitors back to the 18th century.

  2. The Rhode Island School of Design Museum: The museum houses an impressive collection of 100,000 works of art, including ancient Greek vases, French Impressionist paintings, and contemporary American art.

  3. The Museum of Natural History and Planetarium: This museum offers a fantastic journey into the universe. Visitors can explore the solar system and planets’ formation and evolution through various exhibits and shows.

Natural Attractions of Fox Point: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. India Point Park: Located near the Barrington River tidal inlet, this park offers beautiful views of the Providence skyline, perfect for photography enthusiasts. Visitors can also enjoy the walking trails, picnic areas, and playgrounds in the park.

  2. Waterplace Park: This breathtaking park offers a stunning view of the city’s skyline and the WaterFire, a fantastic spectacle of bonfires on the river that takes place on selected weekends throughout the year.

  3. Roger Williams Park Zoo: Located just south of Fox Point, this zoo offers an opportunity to explore over 160 species from around the world through its walking trails and interactive exhibits.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fox Point

You can explore the beauty of Fox Point by hiking or biking through various parks and trails. Some popular trails include

  1. The East Bay Bike Path: This 14.5-mile trail runs along the east shore of the Narragansett Bay and offers scenic views of the island’s historical landmarks.

  2. Blackstone River Bikeway: This 11-mile trail takes you through historical rural Rhode Island from downtown Providence to the Massachusetts border.

  3. Woonasquatucket River Greenway Bike Path: This 8-mile bike path takes you through the heart of Providence’s urban landscape, with expansive parklands and wetlands along the way.

Best Things to Do in Fox Point: Activities and Tours

  1. Take a Gondola ride: Experience Venice in Providence by gliding on the Waterplace Park Riverwalk in a Venice-style gondola.

  2. Shopping at Thayer Street: Stroll through this pedestrian street lined with unique boutiques, independent shops, cafes, and restaurants, all surrounded by an eclectic ambiance.

  3. Enjoy a Food Tour: Take a gastronomic journey through Fox Point and its surroundings. Savor the best local and international cuisine the city has to offer.

Food and Drink Scene of Fox Point

Rhode Island has a rich culinary scene, and Fox Point boasts some of the best food and drink spots in the city.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Stuffies: Local quahog clams baked with stuffing made of herbs, bread crumbs, and other seafood.

  2. Clam Chowder: A creamy soup made from clams and potatoes that is a classic Rhode Island dish.

  3. Pizza Strips: A rectangular-shaped pizza topped with crushed tomato sauce and grated Pecorino Romano cheese.

Local Beverages

  1. Del’s Lemonade: A frozen lemonade slush that’s only available in Rhode Island.

  2. Narragansett Beer: A local brewery that produces six types of beer, including the famous Narragansett Lager.

  3. Coffee Milk: A sweet coffee-flavored milk drink that is the state drink of Rhode Island.

Where to Stay in Fox Point: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Fox Point has plenty of accommodation options for every type of traveler, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious boutique hotels. Some top picks include

  1. The Dean Hotel: This hotel offers stylish rooms with custom-made furniture and local art. It features a cocktail bar, coffee shop, and karaoke lounge.

  2. The Wayfinder Hotel: This hotel features unique rooms designed with a retro beach-inspired style, a food truck-oriented dining experience, and outdoor pool and lounge areas.

  3. HI Providence Hostel: This budget-friendly hostel is ideal for solo travelers, offering a communal kitchen, activities, and tours.

What to Buy in Fox Point: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Providence-made Jewelry: Local artisans craft beautiful and unique jewelry inspired by Providence’s culture and architecture.

  2. Coffee Syrup: A sweet syrup made of coffee and sugar that’s used to flavor milk and coffee to make a Rhode Island favorite drink - coffee milk.

  3. Nautical Gifts: Fox Point’s location on the waterfront makes it an ideal location to purchase nautical gifts such as ship models, anchors, and lighthouse replicas.

Local Culture and Customs of Fox Point

Providence is a diverse and tolerant city, and is known for its lively arts and culture scene. The city celebrates various festivals and events throughout the year, including the Women’s Film Festival, PrideFest, and WaterFire.

Best Time to Visit Fox Point

The best time to visit Fox Point is during the summer months between June and August, when the weather is warm and the city is buzzing with festivals and outdoor events.

Do’s and Don’ts of Fox Point

Do:

  1. Explore the city’s history by visiting the various museums and historical places.
  2. Try the local food and drink scene.
  3. Relax and enjoy the stunning natural beauty of the waterfront.

Don’t:

  1. Litter or disturb the natural environment.
  2. Engage in illegal activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Fox Point

Fox Point is a safe neighborhood, but as with any urban area, it’s wise to take precautions such as not leaving valuables unattended.

Health in Fox Point

Providence is a well-equipped city with good medical care facilities. Visitors should bring their health insurance and take general health precautions as advised by their health practitioner during their stay in the city.

Getting to Fox Point: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Fox Point

The nearest airport to Fox Point is the TF Green Airport in Warwick, which is approximately 10 miles south of Providence.

Bus Stations Near Fox Point

The Kennedy Plaza in downtown Providence is the central bus station and hub for local and interstate buses.

Other Options

Travelers can also reach Fox Point by train or car. There are regular train services to Providence’s main Amtrak station from Boston, New York City, and Washington DC.

How to Get Around in Fox Point

Fox Point is a walkable neighborhood, and visitors can explore the area on foot, bike, or by taking a ride on the local bus.

Where to Go Next from Fox Point: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Fox Point is an ideal location to explore other attractions and destinations in Rhode Island, including Newport, Block Island, and the Cape Cod region of Massachusetts.

Conclusion

Fox Point is an incredible destination that offers something for everyone. From its rich cultural heritage to the natural beauty of its shoreline, this neighborhood offers a unique blend of history, culture, and adventure that travelers will relish. So pack your bags and come visit the stunning Fox Point in Providence, Rhode Island, and experience an unforgettable adventure of a lifetime!
