Introduction to Smith Hill

Located in Providence County, Rhode Island, Smith Hill is a charming neighborhood that serves as the cultural heart of Providence. This historic area offers visitors plenty of attractions to explore, from museums and art galleries to scenic parks and hiking trails. If you’re planning a trip to Smith Hill, read on for our ultimate travel guide to this picturesque destination.

List of Places to Visit in Smith Hill

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Rhode Island State House

The Rhode Island State House is an impressive building that dominates the skyline of Providence. Visitors can tour the interior of the building, which features stunning architecture and artwork.

Providence Children’s Museum

The Providence Children’s Museum is a great place to take kids. The museum features interactive exhibits and educational programs that are both entertaining and educational for children of all ages.

John Brown House Museum

History buffs will love the John Brown House Museum, which offers a glimpse into life during the colonial period. The museum is housed in a historic mansion and features exhibits that highlight the rich history of Rhode Island.

Natural Attractions of Smith Hill: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Roger Williams Park

Roger Williams Park is a sprawling urban park that offers visitors a variety of activities to enjoy. The park boasts a zoo, botanical gardens, a museum of natural history, and plenty of trails for hiking and biking.

Wanskuck Park

Wanskuck Park is a hidden gem in the Smith Hill neighborhood. This small park features an impressive waterfall and plenty of hiking trails that are perfect for a leisurely stroll or an afternoon picnic.

Blackstone River Bikeway

The Blackstone River Bikeway is a popular destination for cyclists. The trail winds its way through the city of Providence and offers cyclists stunning views of the city skyline.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Smith Hill

Smith Hill Loop Trail

The Smith Hill Loop Trail is a scenic hiking trail that offers visitors stunning views of the city skyline. The trail is approximately 3 miles long and takes hikers through some of Smith Hill’s most beautiful natural areas.

East Bay Bike Path

The East Bay Bike Path is a popular destination for cyclists looking for a longer ride. The trail stretches for over 14 miles and offers stunning views of the Narragansett Bay.

Best Things to Do in Smith Hill: Activities and Tours

Providence Ghost Tour

The Providence Ghost Tour is a popular activity for visitors to Smith Hill. The tour takes visitors through some of the city’s most haunted locations and offers a thrilling glimpse into the city’s dark past.

Paddleboarding on the River

For a unique outdoor adventure, visitors can try paddleboarding on the river. The calm waters of the river offer the perfect opportunity for beginners to learn how to paddleboard.

Week-Long Itinerary for Smith Hill

Day 1: Visit the Rhode Island State House and the Providence Children’s Museum.

Day 2: Take a hike on the Smith Hill Loop Trail and enjoy a picnic in Wanskuck Park.

Day 3: Tour the John Brown House Museum and explore the historic neighborhoods of Smith Hill.

Day 4: Bike the East Bay Bike Path and enjoy lunch in one of Providence’s many restaurants.

Day 5: Take a ghost tour of Providence and experience the city’s spooky side.

Day 6: Try paddleboarding on the river and enjoy the scenic views of the city.

Day 7: Explore Roger Williams Park and visit the zoo, botanical gardens, and museum of natural history.

Food and Drink Scene of Smith Hill

Must-Try Dishes

Stuffies

Stuffies are a local favorite in Rhode Island. These stuffed clams are a must-try for anyone visiting Smith Hill.

Clam Chowder

Rhode Island clam chowder is a unique take on the classic New England dish. The chowder is made with clear broth and plenty of fresh clams.

Local Beverages

Del’s Frozen Lemonade

Del’s Frozen Lemonade is a Rhode Island classic. This refreshing beverage is perfect for a hot summer day.

Coffee Milk

Coffee milk is a local drink that is unique to Rhode Island. It is made by adding coffee syrup to milk and is a popular drink among locals.

Where to Stay in Smith Hill: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hilton Providence

The Hilton Providence is a luxury hotel located in the heart of the city. The hotel offers plenty of amenities, including an on-site restaurant and a fitness center.

Hampton Inn & Suites Providence Downtown

The Hampton Inn & Suites Providence Downtown is a popular choice among travelers. The hotel is conveniently located near some of Smith Hill’s top attractions and offers free breakfast to guests.

What to Buy in Smith Hill: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Rhode Island Red Hot Sauce

Rhode Island Red Hot Sauce is a local favorite in Smith Hill. This spicy condiment is made with locally grown hot peppers and is the perfect souvenir to bring home.

Coffee Syrup

Coffee syrup is another local favorite that is unique to Rhode Island. It is the perfect gift for anyone who loves coffee and is looking for a new twist on their morning cup of joe.

Local Culture and Customs of Smith Hill

Smith Hill is a unique neighborhood that is proud of its history and culture. Visitors should take the time to appreciate the local architecture and explore the many museums and historical sites that the area has to offer.

Best Time to Visit Smith Hill

The best time to visit Smith Hill is in the spring or fall, when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller than during the summer months. Visitors should be prepared for occasional rain showers, as Rhode Island weather can be unpredictable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Smith Hill

Do:

  • Take a scenic hike or bike ride through Smith Hill’s many parks and trails.
  • Try some of the local cuisine, such as stuffies and clam chowder.
  • Learn about the local history and culture by visiting the area’s many museums and historical sites.

Don’t:

  • Forget to bring comfortable walking shoes, as many of Smith Hill’s attractions are best explored on foot.
  • Skip a visit to Roger Williams Park, one of the area’s most popular attractions.
  • Neglect to try some of Rhode Island’s famous coffee milk.

Safety and Health

Safety in Smith Hill

Smith Hill is generally a safe neighborhood, but visitors should take the usual precautions to ensure their safety. This includes keeping valuables out of sight and avoiding walking alone at night.

Health in Smith Hill

Visitors to Smith Hill should be aware that the sun can be intense during the summer months. It is important to wear sunscreen and stay hydrated to avoid heat exhaustion or heat stroke.

Getting to Smith Hill: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Smith Hill

The nearest airport to Smith Hill is T.F. Green Airport, which is located approximately 10 miles south of Providence.

Bus Stations Near Smith Hill

The Providence Bus Station is located in downtown Providence and offers bus service to many destinations throughout Rhode Island.

Other Options

Smith Hill is easily accessible by car or taxi, and visitors can also take advantage of the area’s many bike trails and walking paths to explore the neighborhood on foot.

How to Get Around in Smith Hill

Smith Hill is a walkable neighborhood, and visitors can easily explore the area on foot. However, visitors who prefer to bike can take advantage of the many bike trails and paths that crisscross the neighborhood.

Where to Go Next from Smith Hill: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Newport, Rhode Island

Newport, Rhode Island, is located just a short drive from Smith Hill. This historic city is famous for its stunning mansions, gorgeous beaches, and charming downtown area.

Boston, Massachusetts

Boston, Massachusetts, is located approximately 50 miles north of Providence and is a great destination for a day trip. Visitors can explore the city’s many historical sites, including the Freedom Trail and the USS Constitution Museum.

Conclusion

Smith Hill is a charming neighborhood that is rich in history and culture. Whether you’re interested in hiking, biking, or exploring the local museums and historical sites, there is plenty to see and do in this picturesque destination. So pack your bags and get ready to discover the best that Smith Hill has to offer!
