Introduction to New Ashford

Nestled in the picturesque Berkshires region of Western Massachusetts, New Ashford is a charming town that offers a quintessential New England experience. It’s a great destination for both outdoor enthusiasts as well as history and culture buffs, all of whom will find plenty of things to see and do in this idyllic New England town.

List of Places to Visit in New Ashford

Whether you’re interested in exploring the history and culture of the town or enjoying the natural beauty of the area, there are plenty of places to visit in New Ashford. Here are some of the must-see attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • New Ashford Historical Society: This small museum chronicles the town’s rich history and features exhibits on agriculture, Civil War history, and the local people who made this place their home.
  • Lanesborough-Williamstown-Lee Appalachian Trail Community: New Ashford lies in the heart of the Appalachian Trail. This unique museum celebrates the history and culture of the Appalachian Trail by featuring exhibits, films, and stories from hikers who have travelled its entire length.
  • The Mount: Former home of author Edith Wharton, The Mount is a historic house museum and cultural center that offers informative tours, workshops, and events throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of New Ashford: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Greylock: Standing 3,491 feet tall, Mount Greylock is the tallest mountain in Massachusetts and offers amazing scenery of the surrounding areas.
  • Windsor Lake: This serene lake provides ample opportunities for swimming, boating, and fishing, as well as several hiking and biking trails.
  • Berkshire Botanical Garden: The Berkshire Botanical Garden is a must-visit for nature lovers, featuring a wide variety of plants and beautiful gardens that are breathtaking all year round.

Hiking/Biking Routes of New Ashford

New Ashford and the Berkshires are renowned for its hiking and biking trails. Here are some of the best hiking and biking trails in the area:

  • Ashuwillticook Rail Trail: This 11-mile paved path is perfect for biking and hiking. It’s mostly flat, making it suitable for all fitness levels.
  • Mount Greylock: Hiking to the summit of Mount Greylock is a challenging but rewarding experience that offers breathtaking views of Berkshire County.
  • Hoosac Ridge Trail: Stretching for over 20 miles, this picturesque trail provides hikers with stunning views and is an amazing opportunity to explore the area’s unique flora and fauna.

Best Things to Do in New Ashford: Activities and Tours

There is so much to see and do in New Ashford, you won’t be bored for a second. Here are some of the best activities in New Ashford:

  • Berkshire Scenic Railway Museum: Take a ride on a vintage train and explore the rich history of the region.
  • Jiminy Peak Mountain Resort: This popular mountain resort offers a variety of recreational activities, including skiing, snowboarding, and ziplining.
  • Guided Tours: Various tour providers offer guided tours of the town that covers its history and landmarks.

Week-Long Itinerary for New Ashford

If you’re planning a week-long trip to New Ashford, here’s an itinerary that allows you to experience everything the town and the region have to offer:

Day 1: Arrive in New Ashford, settle in your accommodations and explore the town.

Day 2: Hiking at Mount Greylock with picnic lunch.

Day 3: Explore the museums and cultural landmarks

Day 4: Take a guided tour and explore the area’s rich culinary scene.

Day 5: Spend a relaxed day at Lanesborough-Williamstown-Lee Appalachian Trail Community.

Day 6: Try your hand at outdoor sports at Jiminy Peak Mountain Resort.

Day 7: Go souvenir shopping and head to nearby destinations like Williamstown and Pittsfield.

Food and Drink Scene of New Ashford

New Ashford offers a variety of must-try dishes and local beverages that will delight any foodie.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pork pie: A local delicacy that is a must-try when visiting New Ashford.
  • Fresh Maple syrup: A great souvenir and an excellent addition to your breakfast.
  • Berkshire pork chops: Meat lovers will relish this local specialty.

Local Beverages

  • Hard cider: A crisp and refreshing drink made from locally grown apples.
  • Berkshire Mountain Distillers whiskey: Made from locally sourced ingredients, it is something to definitely try.

Where to Stay in New Ashford: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

New Ashford offers a variety of accommodations to suit every budget and preference, ranging from cozy bed and breakfasts to luxurious resorts. Here are some recommendations:

  • Lake House Guest Cottages of the Berkshires: Off-the-grid cottages with stunning views of the Hoosac Range.
  • The Porches Inn: A boutique hotel with an artsy vibe and eclectic decor.
  • Jiminy Peak Mountain Resort: Perfect for families and groups wanting to indulge in different adventure sports.

What to Buy in New Ashford: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When in New Ashford, don’t forget to get these amazing souvenirs to bring back home:

  • Maple syrup: A sweet and delicious souvenir that will bring back memories of your trip.
  • Handmade leather goods: Leather goods made from locally sourced materials, such as belts and shoes, are special souvenirs.
  • Handcrafted pottery: These are unique pieces of art that make for great decorative pieces for your home.

Local Culture and Customs of New Ashford

New Ashford is a small town with a warm and friendly community. When visiting, it’s important to respect local customs. Tipping for services is expected, and visitors should be polite and courteous to locals.

Best Time to Visit New Ashford

The best time to visit New Ashford is during the summer months between June and August when the weather is mild and comfortable for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of New Ashford

Do:

  • Explore the town’s cultural and natural landmarks.
  • Respect the customs and traditions of the town.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Experience the town’s outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Litter and leave garbage at the attraction sites or along the hiking trails.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  • Ignore the safety guidelines when indulging in outdoor activities.
  • Be loud and disruptive in public places.

Safety and Health

Safety in New Ashford

Overall, New Ashford is a safe and welcoming town. Visitors should take precautions when indulging in outdoor activities, such as always wearing appropriate clothing and footwear and following safety guidelines.

Health in New Ashford

New Ashford offers state-of-the-art medical facilities in case of an emergency. Visitors should carry their health insurance card to get prompt medical care if needed and stay hydrated, especially during outdoor activities.

Getting to New Ashford: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near New Ashford

The closest airport is Bradley Airport, which has direct flights to several major cities in the U.S. Other airports include Albany International Airport and Boston’s Logan International Airport.

Bus Stations Near New Ashford

Peter Pan Bus Lines is a major bus service provider in the region, with several daily buses running between New Ashford and Boston.

Other Options

Renting a car from nearby cities like Boston or Albany and driving to New Ashford is also an option. Uber and Lyft are also available in the area.

How to Get Around in New Ashford

The best way to get around New Ashford is by car. Alternatively, you can rent a bike or go hiking to explore the natural beauty of the area.

Where to Go Next from New Ashford: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

New Ashford is surrounded by several charming towns that are worth visiting, including:

  • Williamstown: Home to the prestigious Williams College and renowned Mass MoCA museum.
  • Pittsfield: The city features several historical sites, cultural attractions and fantastic nature explorations around the city.

Conclusion

New Ashford is a small town that perfectly encapsulates the charm and beauty of Western Massachusetts. Whether you’re an outdoor adventurer or a culture-vulture, there are plenty of things to see and do in this picturesque New England town. We hope this comprehensive travel guide will help you plan your trip and make the most of your visit to New Ashford!
