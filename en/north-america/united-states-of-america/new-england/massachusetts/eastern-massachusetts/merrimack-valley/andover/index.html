Introduction to Andover

Nestled in the heart of Merrimack Valley, Andover is a picturesque town in Eastern Massachusetts. With its tree-lined streets, historic buildings, and stunning natural scenery, Andover is a must-visit for travelers looking to explore New England’s hidden gems.

Andover has a rich history, dating back to the early 1600s when it was first settled by English colonists. Today, it is a vibrant community of artists, intellectuals, and entrepreneurs, boasting a thriving culture and arts scene, world-class museums, and an abundance of outdoor activities.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take you on a tour of Andover’s best attractions, activities, and local culture. Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, a history buff, or a foodie, Andover has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Andover

Here are some of the top places to visit in Andover:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Addison Gallery of American Art: Featuring one of the most extensive collections of American art in the country, the Addison Gallery is not to be missed.
  • The Museum of Printing: This fascinating museum offers a glimpse into the history of printing and graphic arts.
  • Andover Historical Society: Immerse yourself in the town’s history at this comprehensive museum showcasing Andover’s past.
  • Phillips Academy Andover: Tour the grounds of one of the country’s most prestigious and historic boarding schools.

Natural Attractions of Andover: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Harold Parker State Forest: With over 3,000 acres of woodland, lakes, and streams, this forest is a paradise for hikers, bikers, and nature enthusiasts.
  • Pomps Pond: In the summer, cool off at this beautiful pond with a public beach and swimming area.
  • Mount Pisgah: Hike to the top of Andover’s highest peak and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding valleys.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Andover

Andover is a great place to explore on foot or on a bike. Here are some of the top hiking and biking routes in the area:

  • The Bay Circuit Trail: This 200-mile trail winds its way through Andover, passing through some of the area’s most beautiful landscapes.
  • The Merrimack River Trail: Follow the river on this scenic trail and enjoy views of the waterfalls and rapids.
  • The Andover Village Improvement Society Trail: This network of trails takes you through some of Andover’s most historic and beautiful landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Andover: Activities and Tours

Andover offers a wide range of activities and tours for visitors of all ages. Here are some local favorites:

  • The Andover Farmer’s Market: Running from June to October, this vibrant market features fresh produce, crafts, and live music.
  • The Andover Bookstore: Browse the aisles of this cozy independent bookstore and find your next read.
  • Andover Flight Academy: Take to the skies with a scenic flight over the town and surrounding area.
  • The Tour of the Andovers: Take a guided tour of the town’s historic homes and landmarks.

Week-Long Itinerary for Andover

Here is a suggested week-long itinerary for exploring Andover and its surroundings:

Day 1 Arrive in Andover and explore the town center
Day 2 Visit the Addison Gallery of American Art and the Andover Historical Society
Day 3 Hike the Bay Circuit Trail and explore the Harold Parker State Forest
Day 4 Tour Phillips Academy Andover and take a scenic flight from the Andover Flight Academy
Day 5 Visit the Museum of Printing and explore the village of Andover
Day 6 Bike the Merrimack River Trail and spend the afternoon swimming at Pomps Pond
Day 7 Take a guided tour of the town’s historic homes and landmarks

Where to Stay in Andover: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Andover offers a wide range of accommodations to suit every budget and preference. Here are some top picks:

  • The Andover Inn: This elegant hotel is located on the campus of Phillips Academy, offering comfortable rooms and a fine dining restaurant.
  • Sonesta ES Suites Andover: These spacious suites feature separate living and sleeping areas, perfect for families or extended stays.
  • Brew House Suites: This historic building houses modern, fully-equipped apartments in the heart of Andover.

Best Time to Visit Andover

The best time to visit Andover is in the late spring and early fall, when the weather is mild, and the foliage is at its peak. The summer months are also a great time to visit, with plenty of outdoor activities and events taking place.

Do’s and Don’ts of Andover

Do:

  • Do explore the town center and village of Andover
  • Do hike or bike the Bay Circuit Trail and Merrimack River Trail
  • Do take a scenic flight from the Andover Flight Academy

Don’t:

  • Don’t miss out on the Addison Gallery of American Art and Andover Historical Society
  • Don’t forget to try a lobster roll or clam chowder

Safety and Health

Safety in Andover

Andover is a safe town, with low crime rates and a friendly community. However, visitors should take precautions to ensure their safety, such as locking doors and windows, and being aware of their surroundings at all times.

Health in Andover

Andover has several hospitals and medical facilities, including the Lawrence General Hospital, which offers a range of medical services and specialties.

Andover is a walkable town, but for longer distances, a car or bike is recommended. There are also several taxi and ride-share services available.
