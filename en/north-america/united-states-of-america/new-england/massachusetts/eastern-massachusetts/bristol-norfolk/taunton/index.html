Taunton, located in Bristol-Norfolk County, Massachusetts, is a charming town with a rich history and plenty of outdoor adventures. This guide is designed to help you plan your trip to Taunton, MA, with detailed information on the best things to see, do, eat, and where to stay.

Introduction to Taunton

Taunton is a small town in southeastern Massachusetts, located between Boston and Cape Cod. It is known for its historic downtown district, rich colonial history, and beautiful outdoor spaces.

List of Places to Visit in Taunton

When it comes to things to do in Taunton, there is no shortage of activities. Here is a list of the top attractions to check out in the area:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Old Colony History Museum: This museum celebrates the rich history of Taunton and the surrounding areas. It has exhibits on the town’s industrial past, as well as an extensive collection of historic artifacts.

  • Taunton Green: The town green in Taunton is a historic and picturesque location, surrounded by beautiful old architecture and shaded by large trees. It’s a great place to go for a relaxing stroll, enjoy a picnic, or people-watch.

  • Taunton Public Library: This beautiful library, built in the French Norman style, is worth a visit for its beautiful architecture alone. Inside, there’s a great selection of books and resources, as well as a variety of public events and programs.

Natural Attractions of Taunton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Watson Pond State Park: Located just a few miles outside of downtown Taunton, this park offers opportunities for hiking, fishing, boating, and more. The park has a beautiful lake and several trails winding through the woods.

  • Massasoit State Park: This park, located just south of Taunton, is a beautiful place for hiking, picnicking, and fishing. The park has several miles of trails, beautiful views of the surrounding landscape, and a variety of wildlife.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Taunton

If you’re looking to get outside and explore, Taunton has plenty of great hiking and biking routes. Here are a few to check out:

  • Taunton River Trail: This scenic trail follows the Taunton River for several miles, offering beautiful views and opportunities for fishing and kayaking.

  • Myles Standish State Forest: Located just a short drive from Taunton, this state forest has several miles of hiking and biking trails, as well as a variety of other outdoor activities.

Best Things to Do in Taunton: Activities and Tours

In addition to the above attractions and outdoor adventures, Taunton has plenty of other things to see and do. Here are a few ideas:

  • Tour the historic district: Taunton’s downtown district is full of beautiful old buildings, many of which date back to the 18th century. Consider taking a guided tour to learn more about the town’s history and architecture.

  • Attend a festival or event: Taunton hosts a variety of events throughout the year, including an annual fall festival, a summer concert series, and more.

Week-Long Itinerary for Taunton

If you have a full week to spend in Taunton, here is a suggested itinerary:

Day Activity
1 Visit the Old Colony History Museum and take a walking tour of the historic downtown district

Food and Drink Scene of Taunton

Taunton has a small but lively food and drink scene. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Clam chowder: As a coastal town in Massachusetts, Taunton is known for its delicious clam chowder.

  • Lobster roll: Fresh lobster is abundant in the area, and many local restaurants offer delicious lobster rolls.

Local Beverages

  • Beers from Shovel Town Brewery: This local brewery offers a variety of delicious craft beers, including several IPAs and stouts.

  • Wine from Leeward Winery: This boutique winery offers a variety of locally-grown wines, as well as tastings and tours.

Where to Stay in Taunton: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

While Taunton is a relatively small town, there are several great options for accommodations. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Holiday Inn Taunton-Foxboro Area: This centrally-located hotel offers comfortable rooms and a variety of amenities, including a fitness center and indoor pool.

  • Morton House Bed & Breakfast: This charming B&B offers cozy rooms, a freshly-prepared breakfast each morning, and easy access to downtown Taunton.

Local Culture and Customs of Taunton

Taunton is a friendly and welcoming town where visitors are always welcome. It’s polite to be courteous and respectful to locals, and to follow common etiquette, such as saying “please” and “thank you.”

Best Time to Visit Taunton

The best time to visit Taunton is during the spring or fall, when temperatures are mild and the natural scenery is beautiful. Summers can be hot and crowded, while winters can be cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Taunton

Do:

  • Take the time to explore the town’s historic district

  • Enjoy the local food and drink scene

  • Be respectful and courteous to locals

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the area’s natural scenery

  • Be rude or disrespectful to locals

Safety and Health

Safety in Taunton

Taunton is a safe town for visitors, with a low crime rate and friendly locals. However, it’s always a good idea to exercise caution and be aware of your surroundings when traveling.

Health in Taunton

Taunton has several medical facilities, including a hospital and several clinics.

Getting to Taunton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Taunton is easily accessible by car, bus, or train.

Airports Near Taunton

  • Boston Logan International Airport (BOS) is the nearest major airport, located about 45 miles north of Taunton.

Bus Stations Near Taunton

  • Greyhound Bus Station: Located in nearby Providence, RI, about 18 miles from Taunton.

Other Options

  • Amtrak train service: The nearest station is located in Providence, RI, about 18 miles from Taunton.

How to Get Around in Taunton

The best way to get around Taunton is by car. There are also several taxi services and ride-sharing options available, as well as a local bus service.

Where to Go Next from Taunton: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have some additional time to explore the area, consider checking out these nearby destinations and excursions:

  • Boston: The historic city of Boston is located about an hour’s drive north of Taunton.

  • Cape Cod: This beautiful peninsula, located just a short drive from Taunton, is known for its beautiful beaches and charming villages.

Conclusion

Taunton, Massachusetts, may be a small town, but it’s full of charm, natural beauty, and interesting history. We hope this guide has helped you plan your trip and discover all the best things to do, see, eat, and experience in Taunton!
