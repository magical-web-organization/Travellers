Introduction to Foxborough

Foxborough is a charming town located in Bristol-Norfolk, Eastern Massachusetts, USA. Known as the home of the New England Patriots, Foxborough offers much more than football. The town is small enough to walk around yet big enough to provide endless entertainment. With its rich history, beautiful parks, museums, and natural attractions, Foxborough is an ideal vacation spot for any traveler.

List of Places to Visit in Foxborough

Whether you’re staying for a day or a week, there are plenty of things to see and do in Foxborough. Here’s a curated list of must-visit places in the town.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Gillette Stadium: Home of the New England Patriots, this stadium has a seating capacity of over 65,000 and is a must-visit for football fans.
  • The Hall at Patriot Place: This indoor/outdoor museum features interactive exhibits, displays, and rare artifacts that showcase the history of the New England Patriots.
  • Foxborough State Hospital: This abandoned former mental hospital was built in 1903 and served as a state-of-the-art medical facility until it was closed in 1976. Now, it is a popular spot for urban exploration and photography.
  • Mansfield Historical Society’s Museum: The museum showcases the history of Mansfield and Foxborough through exhibits of artifacts, photographs, and documents.

Natural Attractions of Foxborough: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • F. Gilbert Hills State Forest: Covering over 1000 acres, this forest offers a range of outdoor activities such as hiking, mountain biking, horseback riding, and fishing.
  • Nature Trail and Cranberry Bog: Take a leisurely stroll along the 1-mile long trail and discover the beauty of a cranberry bog.
  • Cocasset River Recreation Area: This beautiful park features a playground, hiking trails, and a picnic area next to the scenic Cocasset River.
  • Walpole Town Forest: Located near Foxborough, this forest offers numerous hiking trails, scenic views, and a variety of flora and fauna.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Foxborough

Foxborough is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, with beautiful parks and trails waiting to be discovered. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in town:

  • F. Gilbert Hills State Forest Trail: This trail takes you through the beautiful forest and offers scenic views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Nature Trail and Cranberry Bog: An easy 1-mile trail that takes you through a tranquil cranberry bog.
  • Cranberry Bog Trail: A 2-mile trail through the woods and a cranberry bog, offering great wildlife viewing opportunities.
  • Wrentham Swamp Trail: A 1.5 mile peaceful hike/walk through the Wrentham Swamp, visitors can enjoy nature in its purest form along with birdwatching.

Best Things to Do in Foxborough: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and exploring museums, there are plenty of other activities to do in Foxborough:

  • Specialty tours: Take a tour of the local wineries, breweries, and distilleries to learn the art of making your favorite spirits and beverages.
  • Shopping at Patriot Place: This shopping center, located adjacent to Gillette Stadium, offers a range of shopping options plus dining and entertainment for all ages.
  • Go-Karting: Have a thrilling experience by renting a go-kart and racing around the track at F1 Boston.

Food and Drink Scene of Foxborough

When it comes to local cuisine, Foxborough has plenty to offer. Below are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lobster Roll: This classic dish is a staple of New England cuisine and is especially popular during the summer season.
  • Clam Chowder: Another classic dish that is creamy, hearty, and rich with the flavor of clams.
  • Cranberry Bog Turkey Burger: A unique burger made with cranberry sauce, turkey, and melted Brie cheese.

Local Beverages

  • Sam Adams Boston Lager: This flagship beer of the Boston Beer Company is brewed using traditional methods.
  • Cape Codder: A popular cocktail that combines cranberry juice and vodka to create a sweet and fruity drink.

Where to Stay in Foxborough: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Foxborough offers a range of accommodation options that fit every budget and preference. Here are some recommendations:

  • Patriot Place Hotel: Located in the heart of the Patriot Place complex with direct access to Gillette Stadium, this hotel features luxurious rooms with modern amenities.
  • Residence Inn Boston Foxborough: This all-suites hotel offers top-notch facilities like a fitness center, indoor pool, and free breakfast.
  • Red Roof Inn Boston Mansfield/Foxboro: An affordable option for those on a budget, this hotel offers basic amenities and comfortable rooms.

What to Buy in Foxborough: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

No trip to Foxborough is complete without bringing back some souvenirs and local products. Below are some recommendations:

  • Patriots Merchandise: You can find official jerseys, hats, and other memorabilia at The Hall at Patriot Place or in the pro-shop at Gillette Stadium.
  • Cranberry Products: Foxborough is known for its cranberry bogs, and you can find a range of products like jams, sauces, and sweets at local shops.
  • Local Artwork: Take home a piece of Foxborough by buying artwork from local artists at galleries and shops in the town.

Local Culture and Customs of Foxborough

Respect for elders and the community is a key element in the local culture of Foxborough. The town values its rich history, tradition, and outdoor lifestyle. It is not uncommon to see residents engaging in outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and running.

Best Time to Visit Foxborough

The best time to visit Foxborough is during the fall season (September-November) when the weather is mild, and the foliage turns a stunning shade of orange and red. If you’re a football fan, you might want to plan your trip around a home game of the New England Patriots.

Do’s and Don’ts of Foxborough

Do:

  • Do visit the local museums and explore the natural beauty of the town.
  • Do respect the community and follow local laws and regulations.
  • Do try the local cuisine and drinks.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural surroundings of the town.
  • Don’t be disrespectful to local traditions and cultures.
  • Don’t forget to tip in restaurants and bars.

Safety and Health

Safety in Foxborough

Foxborough is generally a safe town, with low crime rates and a friendly community. However, common sense should be used, and standard safety precautions should be taken.

Health in Foxborough

Foxborough has excellent healthcare facilities, with hospitals and clinics conveniently located nearby. It’s always a good idea to have adequate travel insurance when traveling to foreign countries.

Getting to Foxborough: Transportation Options and Logistics

Foxborough is easily accessible via various modes of transportation.

Airports Near Foxborough

  • Boston Logan International Airport (BOS) - 26 miles away
  • T. F. Green Airport (PVD) - 31 miles away

Bus Stations Near Foxborough

  • Mansfield Station - 2.5 miles away
  • Attleboro Station - 7.1 miles away

Other Options

  • Taxi and Ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft are widely available in Foxborough.
  • Car rentals are also available at both airports and in town.

How to Get Around in Foxborough

Walking and biking are the best ways to explore the town, but you can also use ride-sharing services, taxis, and car rentals for longer distances. Public transportation is also available via buses and trains.

Where to Go Next from Foxborough: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have extra time, here are some recommended destinations near Foxborough:

  • Boston: The capital city of Massachusetts is just 25 miles north of Foxborough and can be reached in under an hour by car or train. Explore the historic Freedom Trail, sample local cuisine, or enjoy the nightlife.
  • Cape Cod: A popular summer vacation destination, Cape Cod is home to some of the best beaches, seafood restaurants, and scenic drives in New England.
  • Newport, Rhode Island: Just a short drive from Foxborough, Newport is famous for its stunning coastal landscapes and historic mansions.

Conclusion

Foxborough is a town with much to offer, from its beloved football team to its rich history and natural beauty. This guide has provided a comprehensive overview of the best places to visit, eat, and stay in Foxborough. Whether you’re a sports enthusiast, history buff, or just looking for an outdoor adventure, Foxborough is the perfect destination for your next vacation.
