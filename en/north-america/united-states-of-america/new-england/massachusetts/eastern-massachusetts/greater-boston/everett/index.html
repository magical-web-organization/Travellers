Introduction to Everett

Everett is a city in Middlesex County, located in Greater Boston, in Eastern Massachusetts, in Massachusetts, in New England, in the United States of America. Everett covers approximately 3.4 square miles and is home to over 41,000 people. Everett is a vibrant city with a rich history and an array of attractions and activities for visitors to enjoy.

List of Places to Visit in Everett

If you’re planning a trip to Everett, there are plenty of places to explore. Here are some of the top attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Parlin Memorial Library: This historic library was named after George W. Parlin, a local businessman, and philanthropist who financed the building of this library. It has been serving the people of Everett since 1902.
  • New England Motorcycle Museum: The Museum has a collection of motorcycles, memorabilia, and a gallery of motorcycle artwork that spans the 20th century. The museum showcases vintage motorcycles of every make and model.
  • Everett City Hall: Everett’s City Hall is a stunning Art Deco building that is worth a visit. Built-in the 1930s, it features a clock tower with an illuminated clock and beautiful architectural details.

Natural Attractions of Everett: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ferry Street Park: Located at the end of Ferry Street, the park offers stunning views of the Mystic River, a playground, picnic tables, and a baseball field.
  • The Wynn Boston Harbor: The Wynn Boston Harbor is a local casino and resort that features a variety of games, fine dining, and shopping. The resort offers an array of entertainment options for visitors to enjoy.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Everett

If you’re an outdoor enthusiast, you’ll love hiking and biking in Everett. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Northern Strand Community Trail: This trail covers over 7 miles between Everett and Lynn, offering beautiful views of the city and the waterfront.
  • Mystic River Reservation: The park offers hiking, biking, fishing, and kayaking opportunities along the Mystic River.

Best Things to Do in Everett: Activities and Tours

  • The Encore Boston Harbor: The Encore Boston Harbor offers an array of entertainment options including casino games, live music, a luxury spa, and shopping.
  • The Boott Mills: The Boott Mills is a collection of spinning mills that were once used to create textiles during the Industrial Revolution. Today, the Mills preserve a piece of history, offering tours and exhibitions for visitors.

Food and Drink Scene of Everett

Everett has a variety of restaurants and bars that offer something for everyone. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Everett Square Pizza: This popular pizza place in Everett serves delicious pizzas with unique toppings.
  • Mexican hits at Tacos Lupita: Serves tender tacos, stuffed quesadillas, and flavorful Mexican classics.
  • New York-style bagels from Sip and Spoke Bike Kitchen: Offers vegan, halal dishes made with local ingredients, like New York-style everything bagels filled with cream cheese, lox, and capers.

Local Beverages

  • Night Shift Brewing: This brewery offers a variety of beers and a taproom where visitors can enjoy delicious beer.
  • Down The Road Beer Co.: The company is passionate about crafting unique beers with a huge variety of flavors, it’s worth trying their Double Bold Porter.

  • Encore Boston Harbor: Offers luxury accommodation and entertainment options for visitors.

Local Culture and Customs of Everett

Everett is a diverse community that celebrates a variety of cultures. Visitors to the City can participate in various cultural activities hosted throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Everett

The best time to visit Everett is during the months of April through November when the weather is mild. The summer months can be hot and humid while the winter months can be cold and snowy.

Getting to Everett: Transportation Options and Logistics

If you’re planning a trip to Everett, there are several transportation options available:

Airports Near Everett

  • Boston Logan International Airport: Located approximately 6 miles from Everett, this Airport serves domestic and international flights.
  • Manchester-Boston Regional Airport: Located in Manchester, New Hampshire, 57 miles from Everett, this airport offers domestic flights.

Bus Stations Near Everett

  • Everett Bus Station: Located near the city center, the station provides transportation to various destinations in the region.

Other Options

  • Taxi/Shared Ride Services: Visitors can hire a taxi or use shared ride services from Uber, Lyft, or other providers.

How to Get Around in Everett

Public transportation in Everett is available through the MBTA Bus services, which provides transportation to various destinations in Everett and throughout the region.
