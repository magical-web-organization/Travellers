Introduction to Sharon

Welcome to Sharon, a picturesque town nestled in the Litchfield Hills of Connecticut! Known for its natural beauty, rich history, and charming architecture, Sharon attracts visitors from all over the world. Get ready to explore the town through its museums and historical sites, hiking and biking trails, and delicious food and drinks. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know to make the most of your trip to Sharon.

List of Places to Visit in Sharon

Sharon is a treasure trove of cultural and natural attractions. Here is a list of places that you must visit while in Sharon.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sharon Historical Society Museum
  • Hotchkiss-Fyler House Museum
  • Sharon Audubon Center
  • Indian Mountain School

Natural Attractions of Sharon: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mohawk Mountain State Forest
  • Sharon Land Trust
  • Macedonia Brook State Park
  • Kent Falls State Park

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sharon

Sharon is a hiker’s and biker’s paradise with its scenic trails, varying terrain, and breathtaking views. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes that you can explore in Sharon.

  • Appalachian Trail
  • Mohawk Trail
  • Harlem Valley Rail Trail
  • Sharon Valley Loop

Best Things to Do in Sharon: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, Sharon offers a range of other activities and tours for visitors. Here are some of the best things to do in Sharon.

  • Canoeing and kayaking in the Housatonic River
  • Wine tasting tours at nearby vineyards
  • Ice-skating and skiing at Mohawk Mountain Ski Area
  • Horseback riding at the Sharon Riding School

Must-Try Dishes

Local Beverages

Local Culture and Customs of Sharon

Sharon is a small town that is proud of its heritage and culture. Make sure to respect the local customs and traditions while you’re here. Here are some customs to be aware of when in Sharon.

  • Dress appropriately for the season and the occasion.
  • Practice good manners and respect local customs and traditions.
  • Say “hello” and “thank you” while interacting with locals.

Best Time to Visit Sharon

Sharon is beautiful year-round, but the best time to visit is during the fall (September to November) when the foliage is at its peak. Spring (March to May) and summer (June to August) are also great seasons to visit Sharon.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sharon

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your visit to Sharon.

Do:

  • Take advantage of the natural beauty and explore the hiking trails.
  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local delicacies and drinks.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural surroundings.
  • Be disrespectful to the locals or their properties.
  • Overpack or carry too many things while exploring the town.

Safety and Health

Sharon is a safe and healthy place to visit, but it’s always helpful to take precautions.

Safety in Sharon

  • Keep an eye on your belongings while exploring the town.
  • Travel in groups while hiking or exploring the natural surroundings.
  • Be cautious of ticks and wear appropriate clothing.

Health in Sharon

  • In case of a medical emergency, dial 911.
  • Carry necessary medications and prescriptions.
  • Stay hydrated while exploring the town.

Getting to Sharon: Transportation Options and Logistics

Sharon is easily accessible by various modes of transportation.

Airports Near Sharon

  • Bradley International Airport (BDL) - 50 miles from Sharon
  • Westchester County Airport (HPN) - 70 miles from Sharon
  • John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK) - 100 miles from Sharon

Bus Stations Near Sharon

  • Peter Pan Bus Lines - nearby stations in New Milford and Torrington
  • Greyhound Bus Lines - nearby station in Torrington

Other Options

  • Renting a car from Bradley International Airport or Westchester County Airport.
  • Taking a train to Wassaic Station (Metro-North Railroad), which is about 10 miles from Sharon.

How to Get Around in Sharon

The best way to get around in Sharon is by car. Renting a car from the airport or nearby locations is a great option. Another option is to book a private car/taxi or use ride-sharing services like Uber or Lyft.

Conclusion

We hope this guide has provided you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Sharon. Whether you’re here for the hiking trails, museums, or delicious food and drinks, Sharon has something for everyone. Come and explore this enchanting town in the Litchfield Hills of Connecticut!
