Introduction to New Canaan

New Canaan is a beautiful town located in Fairfield County, Connecticut. It is renowned for its picturesque landscapes, rich history, vibrant culture, and welcoming community. There’s a lot to see and do in this charming town, making it an ideal destination for those who want to enjoy a relaxing vacation or explore the beauty of nature. In this tour guide, we’re going to explore the best of New Canaan, including the places to visit, hiking and biking routes, local culture, safety, and transportation options.

List of Places to Visit in New Canaan

New Canaan boasts various cultural and natural attractions worth visiting. Let’s explore some of the must-visit places in the town.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

New Canaan has numerous historic and cultural places that offer visitors insights into the town’s rich heritage. Some of these places include:

  • New Canaan Historical Society Museum: This museum has exhibits that showcase the town’s history, including its development over the years.

  • The Glass House: The Glass House, also known as the Philip Johnson Glass House, is a significant architecture site that houses various exhibitions and collections of art and design.

  • Selleck’s Woods: Selleck’s Woods is a park that offers hiking trails, picnic areas, and historical sites, including the remnants of a Native American fort.

Natural Attractions of New Canaan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

New Canaan has an abundance of scenic views and natural attractions that visitors can explore. Here are some of the natural attractions in the town:

  • Waveny Park: Waveny Park is a 250-acre park that boasts beautiful gardens, hiking trails, sports facilities, and playgrounds.

  • Silvermine Arts Center: Silvermine Arts Center is a cultural institution that offers visitors a chance to explore the art and history of the area with exhibitions, events, and hands-on workshops.

  • Grace Farms: Grace Farms is a public space that offers various outdoor activities, including hiking, birdwatching, and landscape photography.

Hiking/Biking Routes of New Canaan

New Canaan has various hiking and biking trails that visitors can explore. These trails offer visitors a chance to explore the town’s natural beauty while getting some exercise. Here are some of the hiking and biking trails in the town:

  • New Canaan Nature Center: The nature center has several trails that visitors can explore, including the Woodland Trail, the Maple Syrup Trail, and the Bird Watching Trail.

  • Merritt Parkway Trail: The trail is a paved path that runs parallel to the Merritt Parkway Road and is ideal for cycling, running, and walking.

  • Devil’s Den Preserve: The Devil’s Den Preserve is a natural reserve that offers hiking trails of varying lengths and difficulty levels.

Best Things to Do in New Canaan: Activities and Tours

New Canaan has several activities and tours that visitors can enjoy. These activities offer visitors a chance to explore the town’s culture, history, and natural beauty. Here are some of the best things to do in New Canaan:

  • New Canaan Farmers Market: This market offers visitors a chance to explore the town’s local produce and products, including fresh fruits, vegetables, and homemade products.

  • New Canaan Artisans: New Canaan Artisans is a cooperative art gallery that displays and sells artwork from various local artists.

  • New Canaan Library: The library offers visitors a chance to explore the town’s literary culture by browsing through an extensive collection of books, attending literary events, and participating in workshops and classes.
