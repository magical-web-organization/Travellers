Fairfield, Connecticut: A Comprehensive Travel Guide

Welcome to Fairfield, a charming town nestled in the heart of Connecticut’s Fairfield County, in New England, in the United States of America. With a rich history, beautiful natural surroundings, and a thriving cultural scene, Fairfield is a great destination for travelers of all types.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take a closer look at what makes Fairfield such a special place to visit. From top-rated attractions to hidden gems, hiking and biking trails, food and drink spots, and much more. Let’s dive in!

List of Places to Visit in Fairfield

There are plenty of great attractions in Fairfield that are worth checking out. Here are some of the top spots to add to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fairfield Museum and History Center: The Fairfield Museum and History Center is dedicated to preserving and sharing the history of Fairfield and its surrounding areas. With exhibits ranging from Native American history to the town’s role in the American Revolution, the museum offers an educational and engaging experience for visitors of all ages.

  • Pequot Library: This beautiful library was built in 1893 and is now listed on the National Register of Historic Places. The library’s collection contains over 100,000 volumes, including a rare books collection with first editions from some of the world’s most renowned authors.

Natural Attractions of Fairfield: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Mohegan Recreation Area: This 170-acre recreation area offers hiking trails, picnic areas, a sandy beach, and a swimming hole for visitors to enjoy. Whether you’re looking for a quick nature walk or a full day out, Lake Mohegan is a great place to relax and enjoy the natural beauty of Fairfield.

  • Connecticut Audubon Society Birdcraft Museum and Sanctuary: This wildlife sanctuary and museum is home to over 200 species of birds and other wildlife. Visitors can enjoy hiking trails, birdwatching, and educational programs designed to teach visitors about local flora and fauna.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fairfield

Fairfield is home to some beautiful hiking and biking trails, offering opportunities to explore the area’s natural beauty:

  • The Smith Richardson Wildlife Preserve, which offers a trail system that covers 36 acres and boasts a scenic overlook of wetlands and streams.
  • The Ash Creek Open Space, which offers a network of hiking and cycling trails that lead through beautiful marshlands.

Best Things to Do in Fairfield: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re looking to relax or get your adrenaline pumping, Fairfield has plenty of things to do. Here are some of our top picks:

  • Jennings Beach: With its long, wide beach, Jennings Beach is a popular destination for swimming, sunbathing, and other beach activities. The beach is well-maintained, and there are also plenty of food and drink options nearby.

  • Captain’s Cove Seaport: This charming fishing village is home to a variety of shops, restaurants, and activities for all ages. Visitors can take a tour of the harbor, enjoy fresh seafood, and even catch a live performance or outdoor movie screening.

Week-Long Itinerary for Fairfield

If you’re planning a longer trip to Fairfield, here’s a sample itinerary to help you make the most of your visit:

Day 1: Visit the Fairfield Museum and History Center, followed by a stroll through the picturesque Southport Village.

Day 2: Take a leisurely stroll along the scenic Ash Creek Open Space and enjoy lunch at one of the area’s many diners or cafes.

Day 3: Visit the Connecticut Audubon Society Birdcraft Museum and Sanctuary, followed by a picnic at Lake Mohegan.

Day 4: Spend a day at Jennings Beach and soak up the sun and sea.

Day 5: Take a tour of the Captain’s Cove Seaport, and enjoy an evening performance.

Day 6: Enjoy a morning hike at Smith Richardson Wildlife Preserve, followed by lunch at one of the area’s many seafood restaurants.

Day 7: Take a day trip to nearby attractions like the Mystic Seaport and Mystic Aquarium.

Food and Drink Scene of Fairfield

Fairfield is home to a burgeoning food and drink scene, with everything from fine dining to casual cafes on offer. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to sample:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lobster Bisque: This creamy soup made with fresh lobster and cream is a popular dish in many of the area’s seafood restaurants.

  • New Haven-style Pizza: This thin-crust pizza is a local favorite, with many restaurants in the area offering their own unique twists on the classic pie.

Local Beverages

  • Two Roads Brewing Company: This local brewery is known for its innovative and award-winning craft beers, with several tasting rooms open to visitors.

  • Aspetuck Brew Labs: This brewery serves up delicious, small-batch beers in a friendly and cozy atmosphere.

Where to Stay in Fairfield: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of great accommodation options in Fairfield, from charming B&Bs to modern hotels. Here are a few we’d recommend:

  • Delamar Southport: This luxurious hotel is located in the heart of Southport Village and boasts a spa, restaurant, and beautiful guest rooms.

  • The Inn at Fairfield Beach: This cozy inn on the beach offers comfortable rooms and unparalleled access to the water.

What to Buy in Fairfield: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs or local products to take home from your trip to Fairfield, here are a few options to consider:

  • Bigelow Tea: This family-owned tea company has been a fixture of Fairfield for over 70 years, and their teas make for a delicious and unique gift.

  • Simms Jewelers: This local jeweler offers a selection of handcrafted jewelry, including unique pieces inspired by the coastal landscape of Fairfield.

Local Culture and Customs of Fairfield

Fairfield has a strong sense of community and a rich cultural heritage. Visitors are encouraged to respect local customs and traditions, including a deep appreciation for nature and the importance of preserving the area’s history and heritage.

Best Time to Visit Fairfield

The best time to visit Fairfield is in the spring and fall when the weather is mild, and the area’s natural surroundings are at their most beautiful. Summer can be crowded but perfect for beach activities, while winter can be cold but offers a cozy, festive atmosphere around town.

Do’s and Don’ts of Fairfield

Do:

  • Respect nature and the area’s protected wildlife.

  • Take some time to explore the town’s vibrant cultural scene.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural habitats.

  • Ignore historical markers and important sites around town.

Safety and Health

Visitors to Fairfield can enjoy a safe and healthy trip by following a few simple guidelines:

Safety in Fairfield

  • Always lock your car doors and park in well-lit areas.

  • Avoid walking alone at night in unfamiliar areas.

Health in Fairfield

  • Be aware of ticks in the area and take appropriate precautions when hiking or spending time outdoors.

  • Make sure to bring any necessary medications with you, as pharmacy options may be limited.

Getting to Fairfield: Transportation Options and Logistics

Travelers to Fairfield have several transportation options available to them:

Airports Near Fairfield

  • Bradley International Airport (BDL) is located approximately 50 miles from Fairfield and offers flights from a variety of major carriers.

Bus Stations Near Fairfield

  • The Greater Bridgeport Transit Authority operates several bus lines that run through Fairfield and offer transportation to other areas in Fairfield County.

Other Options

  • Car rental services are available at several locations in Fairfield, including the airport and nearby train stations.

How to Get Around in Fairfield

Fairfield is a walkable town, but visitors may also wish to use a car or bicycle to explore the area’s natural surroundings. There are also several taxi and rideshare services available in the area.

Where to Go Next from Fairfield: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to extend your trip beyond Fairfield, here are a few nearby destinations to consider:

  • Mystic Seaport and Mystic Aquarium: Located less than an hour’s drive from Fairfield, Mystic offers a charming historic district, world-class attractions, and beautiful natural surroundings.

  • New York City: A day trip to the Big Apple is always an exciting option, with plenty of transportation options available from Fairfield.

Conclusion

We hope this comprehensive travel guide has given you a better idea of all the great things Fairfield has to offer. From world-class museums and beautiful natural surroundings to delicious food and drink options and plenty of outdoor activities, Fairfield truly has something for everyone. Happy travels!
