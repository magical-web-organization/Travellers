Introduction to Westport

Located in Fairfield County, Connecticut, Westport is a charming town famous for its natural beauty, vibrant cultural scene, and historic attractions. Named as one of the “Best Places to Live” in the United States, Westport is a popular destination for tourists, attracting visitors from all over the world.

List of Places to Visit in Westport

Westport has a lot to offer for tourists, from historic landmarks and museums to outdoor adventures and scenic drives. Here is a list of the best places to visit in Westport:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Westport Historical Society
  • Sherwood Island State Park
  • Earthplace
  • National Hall
  • The Westport Country Playhouse

Natural Attractions of Westport: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Compo Beach
  • Longshore Golf Course
  • Wakeman Town Farm
  • Saugatuck River
  • Connecticut Audubon Society

Hiking/Biking Routes of Westport

Best Things to Do in Westport: Activities and Tours

Aside from hiking and biking, there are many other activities and tours to do in Westport. Here is a list of the best things to do in Westport:

  • Kayaking and Stand Up Paddleboarding with Downunder
  • Westport Farmers Market
  • Westport Cinema Initiative Screenings
  • Creative Arts Workshop
  • Black Duck Sailing

Week-Long Itinerary for Westport

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit the Westport Historical Society, Sherwood Island State Park, National Hall, and The Westport Country Playhouse
Day 2 Go hiking on Aspetuck Trail and grab lunch at Westport Farmers Market
Day 3 Visit Earthplace and go kayaking or paddleboarding with Downunder
Day 4 Take a tour of Saugatuck River and explore Wakeman Town Farm
Day 5 Go bird watching on the Connecticut Coastal Birding Trail
Day 6 Enjoy a show at Westport Cinema Initiative Screenings and visit Creative Arts Workshop
Day 7 Relax at Compo Beach and play golf at Longshore Golf Course

Local Culture and Customs of Westport

Westport is a welcoming town where visitors can explore the rich history, vibrant culture, and beautiful scenery of Connecticut. The local culture is influenced by the New England region, and the customs reflect the town’s charming character.

Best Time to Visit Westport

The best time to visit Westport is in the fall, when the leaves change color and the weather is perfect for outdoor activities. Spring and summer are also popular times to visit, as the weather is warm and sunny.

Do:
