Introduction to Clinton

Clinton, located in the Greater New Haven area of Connecticut, is a charming town that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures. With its serene beaches, scenic hiking trails, and historic landmarks, Clinton is a perfect place for a family vacation, a romantic getaway, or a solo adventure. This comprehensive guide highlights the must-see attractions, activities, and dining options in Clinton to help you plan your perfect trip.

  • Chamard Vineyards

Natural Attractions of Clinton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hammonasset Beach State Park

Hiking/Biking Routes of Clinton

Clinton offers a plethora of hiking and biking routes suitable for all levels of adventurers. The trails provide stunning views of the coast and are accessible year-round. Some notable hiking and biking routes include:

  • Indian River Park Trail
  • Hammonasset Beach State Park Trail
  • Clinton Land Conservation Trust Trails

Best Things to Do in Clinton: Activities and Tours

  • Kayaking and paddleboarding at Clinton Harbor

  • Tours of Clinton’s historic district
  • Wine tasting at Chamard Vineyards

Local Culture and Customs of Clinton

Clinton is a small town that prides itself in its unique and close-knit community. Visitors are encouraged to respect this culture by being courteous and friendly towards locals.

Best Time to Visit Clinton

The best time to visit Clinton is from May to October when the weather is mild, and most of the outdoor attractions and activities are open.

Do’s and Don’ts of Clinton

Do:

  • Visit Hammonasset Beach State Park
  • Explore Clinton’s historic district
  • Try the seafood chowder from Lobster Landing

Don’t:

  • Litter in public places
  • Disturb the local wildlife
  • Dare to swim outside the designated areas at the beach

Safety and Health

Safety in Clinton

Clinton is a safe town with a low crime rate. Visitors are advised to take standard safety precautions, such as locking their cars and doors, and not leaving valuables in plain sight.

Health in Clinton

Visitors should take standard health precautions including getting their vaccines up to date and keeping a close eye on the weather and any anticipated natural disasters.

Getting to Clinton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Clinton is accessible by car, train, and bus.

Airports Near Clinton

  • Tweed New Haven Regional Airport (HVN) - 12 miles
  • Westchester County Airport (HPN) - 58 miles

Bus Stations Near Clinton

  • Greyhound Bus Lines in New Haven, CT - 16 miles
  • Peter Pan Bus Lines in New Haven, CT - 16 miles

Other Options

  • By train - Metro-North Railroad serves New Haven, CT (16 miles from Clinton)

How to Get Around Clinton

Clinton is a small town, and most attractions are within walking distance. However, visitors can also rent a car, hire a taxi, or use a ride-sharing service.
