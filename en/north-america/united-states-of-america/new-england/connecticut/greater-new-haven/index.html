Introduction to Greater New Haven

Located in Connecticut, New England, United States of America, Greater New Haven is a region that is abundant in history, natural beauty, and cultural attractions. It is a year-round destination that offers an ideal mix of outdoor adventures, scenic beauty, and urban amenities.

Best Things to Do in Greater New Haven: Activities and Tours

  • Take a guided tour of Yale University
  • Explore the Connecticut shoreline by bike
  • Visit Skappo Merkato Food Hall
  • Visit the Shubert Theater
  • Enjoy a beer tasting at Two Roads Brewery
  • Take a hot air balloon ride
  • Take a yoga class at the Stand-Up Paddle Boarding and Yoga Center
  • Attend a festival or event at the Connecticut Tennis Center
  • Kayak down the Housatonic River

Week-Long Itinerary for Greater New Haven

Day 1: Visit Yale University Art Gallery, explore New Haven’s downtown, and attend a concert at College Street Music Hall.

Day 2: Hike the East Rock Park Trail and visit the Peabody Museum of Natural History.

Day 3: Take a bike tour of the Connecticut shoreline and visit Lighthouse Point Park and Hammonasset Beach State Park.

Day 4: Visit Stony Creek Museum and take a hot air balloon ride.

Day 5: Visit the Beinecke Rare Book & Manuscript Library and attend a show at Shubert Theater.

Day 6: Hike Sleeping Giant State Park Trail and kayak down the Housatonic River.

Day 7: Visit the New Haven Museum and enjoy a beer tasting at Two Roads Brewery.

Local Culture and Customs of Greater New Haven

Greater New Haven has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its architecture, art, music, and festivals. The area is home to many iconic landmarks, institutions, and traditions that are important to locals and visitors alike.

Best Time to Visit Greater New Haven

The best time to visit Greater New Haven is during the months of September through November, when the weather is mild, and the fall foliage is at its peak.
