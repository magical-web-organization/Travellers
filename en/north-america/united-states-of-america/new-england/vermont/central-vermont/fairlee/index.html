Introduction to Fairlee

Fairlee, located in the central part of Vermont, offers visitors a tranquil escape to the scenic beauty of New England. The town is situated on the banks of the beautiful Lake Morey and provides a perfect blend of historical and natural attractions.

Whether you are looking for an adventurous outdoor adventure or a peaceful retreat, Fairlee has something to offer everyone. This comprehensive travel guide will help you explore the town, its diverse attractions, and plan the perfect itinerary for your trip.

List of Places to Visit in Fairlee

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fairbanks Museum & Planetarium: A visit to this museum will make you feel like you have stepped back in time. The museum features a diverse collection of natural science specimens, historical artifacts, and interactive exhibits. And if you are lucky, you might get to enjoy a starry night at the planetarium.

  • Bradford Historical Society: History buffs will love this museum that offers insights into the town’s rich history. The museum features exhibits on topics like early settlers, railroads, and historic buildings.

Natural Attractions of Fairlee: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Morey: This picturesque lake spans over 600 acres and offers a range of activities like swimming, kayaking, paddleboarding, and fishing. During winter, it transforms into a hub for ice-skating, ice-fishing, and ice boating.

  • Fairlee Town Forest: This vast forest has over 7 miles of well-marked hiking trails, taking you through a diverse landscape of beech, maple, and birch trees. The forest is also popular among mountain bikers.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fairlee

  • Lake Morey Loop Trail: This 7.5-mile trail offers scenic views of Lake Morey and takes you through the town forest.

  • Cross Rivendell Trail: A 36-mile trail that runs through the town of Fairlee and other surrounding towns, offering fantastic hiking options.

Best Things to Do in Fairlee: Activities and Tours

  • Fairlee Drive-In Movie Theater: A unique experience that shouldn’t be missed. Watch classic movies under the stars in your car with a wide range of snacks to enjoy.

  • Lake Morey Resort: Enjoy a day at the resort, playing golf, tennis, or enjoying the indoor pool and sauna. You can also hire a bike or kayak and explore the stunning landscapes around the resort.

Week-Long Itinerary for Fairlee

Day Itinerary
1 Explore the town and its historical and cultural attractions.
2 Hike the Lake Morey Loop Trail and visit the Fairlee Town Forest.
3 Visit the Bradford Historical Society Museum and take a scenic drive around the town.
4 Enjoy a day at the Lake Morey Resort and indulge in some water activities
5 Take a drive to the nearby towns like Thetford to explore their cultural and natural attractions.
6 Hire a bike and explore the Cross Rivendell Trail.
7 End the trip with a drive-in movie experience at Fairlee Drive-In Movie Theater.

Food and Drink Scene of Fairlee

Must-Try Dishes

  • Vermont Maple Syrup: A must-try delicacy that is widely available in Fairlee.

  • Apple Cider Donuts: Enjoy fresh apple cider donuts at the King Arthur Flour Bakery.

Local Beverages

  • Vermont Craft Beers: The town has a vast collection of microbreweries, offering a range of unique craft beers.

  • Maple Cream Liqueur: A smooth and delicious alcoholic beverage made using Vermont Maple Syrup.

Where to Stay in Fairlee: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Lake Morey Resort: One of the most popular resorts in the area, offering excellent accommodation, dining, and activities.

  • Fairlee Motel and Drive-In: If you are looking for budget-friendly accommodation, this motel offers excellent value for money.

  • Morey Hill Campground: For an outdoor adventure, stay at this picturesque campground that offers tent and RV sites.

What to Buy in Fairlee: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Vermont Maple Syrup: This is a must-buy. You can find it at various farms and markets in and around Fairlee.

  • Handcrafted Pottery: Talented local artisans create stunning pottery that makes excellent souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Fairlee

Fairlee has a strong agricultural heritage, and maple sugaring is one of its oldest traditions. Visitors are encouraged to learn about the maple syrup-making process by participating in a “sugar on snow party.”

Best Time to Visit Fairlee

The best time to visit Fairlee is during the summer months (June-August) when the weather is mild and perfect for outdoor activities. Fall (September-November) is another picturesque season in Fairlee with breathtaking foliage. Winter (December-February) offers a unique set of activities like ice-fishing, skiing, and snowmobiling.

Do’s and Don’ts of Fairlee

Do:

  • Enjoy the local cuisine and try Vermont maple syrup.

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Litter or harm the wildlife.

  • Trespass on private property.

Safety and Health

Safety in Fairlee

Fairlee is generally a very safe town, but visitors should take the necessary precautions, especially while hiking or participating in outdoor activities.

Health in Fairlee

Medical facilities are limited in Fairlee, and visitors are advised to bring their medicines or necessary equipment. The nearest hospital is in nearby towns like Hanover or Littleton.

Getting to Fairlee: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Fairlee

  • Lebanon Municipal Airport: The nearest airport to Fairlee, located around 27 miles away.

  • Burlington International Airport: Located about 80 miles away from Fairlee.

Bus Stations Near Fairlee

  • Dartmouth Coach: A popular bus service that connects Boston and New York with Dartmouth.

Other Options

If you are traveling from nearby towns, renting a car is the most convenient option. You can also use ride-hailing services like Uber or Lyft to travel within Fairlee.

How to Get Around in Fairlee

The most convenient way to travel around Fairlee is by car. However, if you are visiting without a vehicle, you can use local taxi services or bike rentals to get around.

Where to Go Next from Fairlee: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Hanover, NH: Located around 21 miles away from Fairlee, Hanover is home to the prestigious Dartmouth College and offers a range of cultural and natural attractions.

  • Woodstock, VT: A picturesque town famous for its stunning covered bridges and a vibrant food and drink scene.

Conclusion

Fairlee offers a perfect escape to the scenic natural beauty of Vermont, with its picturesque lake, diverse hiking trails, delicious local cuisine, and historical attractions. This travel guide will help you plan a memorable trip to the town, exploring its hidden gems and soaking in its rich culture and traditions.
