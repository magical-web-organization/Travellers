Introduction to Bellows Falls

Bellows Falls is a charming town nestled in Southern Vermont, a region renowned for its breathtaking natural beauty, abundant recreational opportunities, and intriguing history. Situated on the banks of the Connecticut River, Bellows Falls boasts stunning views of three surrounding states - Vermont, New Hampshire, and Massachusetts. Whether you are a nature lover, history buff, or simply looking for a peaceful retreat, Bellows Falls is the perfect destination for you.

List of Places to Visit in Bellows Falls

Below is the list of must-visit places in Bellows Falls:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Bellows Falls Opera House: This iconic venue is a great example of a classic Victorian theater. It is renowned for its acoustics, hosting various events, and also functioning as a cinema with the latest films in the schedule.

  • Hall Art Foundation: Home to an impressive collection of contemporary art, this museum is housed within a restored industrial building.

  • The Rockingham Meetinghouse: This historic, early 19th-century meeting house is an architectural gem and believed to be the oldest historical public building in Vermont.

Natural Attractions of Bellows Falls: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Vermont Country Store: A haven for anyone who adores everything about New England, Vermont Country Store sources classic brands and provides a memorable shopping experience.

  • Bellows Falls Canal Trail: The Bellows Falls Canal Trail traces the route of the old canal between a picturesque cove and the Connecticut River, providing scenic views of the river, the canal, and the surrounding landscapes.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bellows Falls

The hiking/biking routes in Bellows Falls are some of the most picturesque in Vermont. Here are two of the best:

  • Rockingham Recreational Trail: The four-mile paved trail is perfect for hiking, biking, jogging, and walking. It offers spectacular views of the Connecticut River and provides access to many recreational parks.

  • Bellows Falls Bike Club: The bike club offers riders breathtaking views of mountains, hillside scenery, and picturesque hamlets at a relaxed pace.

Best Things to Do in Bellows Falls: Activities and Tours

  • Bellows Falls Farmers Market: Bellows Falls Farmer’s Market is a lively event that takes place between June and October, where visitors can sample unique farm-fresh food.

  • Riverfront Experience: A charming historic district with many shops, art galleries, and dining options set on the banks of the beautiful Connecticut River.

Food and Drink Scene of Bellows Falls

The food and drink scene in Bellows Falls is a blend of contemporary and classic. Here are two of the best:

Must-Try Dishes

The Bellows Falls Sweet Treats and Dishes are some of the best in Vermont. Some absolutely must-try dishes include:

  • Maple Breakfast Sandwich - As a New England staple, this maple breakfast sandwich is made up of sausage, egg, and cheese on maple-flavored bread.

  • Sweet potato and bacon soup - The Sweet potato and bacon soup is a hearty, filling, and flavorful soup that can be found in many restaurants in Bellows Falls.

Local Beverages

Vermont is known for its craft beer, and Bellows Falls has a variety of local breweries for visitors to try. The following are some of the best breweries to try:

  • Harpoon Brewery - Harpoon brews ales and lagers for all types of beer lovers using local and imported ingredients, which you can taste by taking a tour of their facility.

  • Rock Art Brewery - Located in nearby Morrisville, Rock Art Brewery specializes in small-batch beer-crafting and offers a variety of different options for visitors to try.

Where to Stay in Bellows Falls: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bellows Falls features many quality accommodations, from budget inns to high-end hotels.

  • The Grafton Inn: This charming historic inn is located in the heart of Bellows Falls, where you can enjoy elegant guest rooms with great amenities to make your stay memorable.

  • The Bellows Falls Lodge: This modern hotel is outfitted with contemporary amenities and provides excellent value to budget-minded travelers.

What to Buy in Bellows Falls: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Maple-related products: Maple syrup is a Vermont specialty, and you will find stores all over Bellows Falls selling all kinds of maple-related items, from maple candies and fudge to maple vinaigrettes and dressings.

  • Handcrafted artisan goods: Bellows Falls has a thriving artisan community, with many talented artisans specializing in everything from pottery and glassware to woodworking and quilting.

Local Culture and Customs of Bellows Falls

Bellows Falls is a town that celebrates its unique New England roots and deeply influenced by nature, the environment, and the community. It is customary to acknowledge people you come across, say “hello,” or ask how their day is going. Residents pride themselves on the town’s rich history and value the traditions that have been upheld for generations.

Best Time to Visit Bellows Falls

The best time to visit Bellows Falls is between June and October, when the weather is mild, and the scenery of the area is in full bloom.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bellows Falls

Bellows Falls is a safe community popular with families and outdoor enthusiasts. However, as anywhere else, it’s always necessary to be aware of safety concerns while traveling.

Health in Bellows Falls

Travelers should ensure that they have comprehensive travel health insurance when they are in Bellows Falls. In addition, they should take the necessary precautions to prevent illnesses, including washing hands regularly and maintaining good hygiene.

Getting to Bellows Falls: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bellows Falls

The nearest airport to Bellows Falls is The Bradley International Airport, located approximately 80 miles south in Windsor Locks, Connecticut.

Bus Stations Near Bellows Falls

Greyhound provides daily bus service to important Vermont points, while Green Mountain Transit is the primary mass transit organization in Vermont, providing regional bus service.

Other Options

  • Rail Service: Amtrak provides service to the nearby town of Brattleboro, Vermont.
  • By Car: You can easily drive to Bellows Falls from nearby towns in Massachusetts, New Hampshire, or Vermont.

How to Get Around in Bellows Falls

Biking and walking are popular means of transportation in Bellows Falls. The town is small enough that many attractions are within walking distance of each other. There are also a few taxi services in town for visitors who don’t want to walk.

Where to Go Next from Bellows Falls: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Brattleboro, Vermont - This charming town is a popular destination for its vibrant arts scene, great shopping options, and thriving farmers market.

  • Mount Monadnock, New Hampshire – Known for its breathtaking views, Mount Monadnock is a beloved hiking destination among outdoor enthusiasts.

Conclusion

Bellows Falls is an ideal spot for people seeking a peaceful place in Vermont. With a wide range of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and excellent dining options, this charming town has something to offer every kind of traveler. To create a memorable vacation, visit Bellows Falls and discover its unique charm and hidden treasures.
