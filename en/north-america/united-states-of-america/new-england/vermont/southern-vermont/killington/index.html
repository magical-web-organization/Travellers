Introduction to Killington

Set amidst the lush Green Mountains of Vermont, Killington is a top-rated ski resort town, offering stunning views, a great food and drink scene, and a plethora of outdoor adventures throughout the year. Killington is famous for its skiing and snowboarding, as it has the largest ski area in the Eastern United States, but it is also a beautiful town for hiking, mountain biking, and enjoying the autumn foliage.
