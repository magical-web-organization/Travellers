Introduction to Newfane

Located in Southern Vermont, Newfane is a picturesque small town that showcases the beauty of New England. This quaint town is perfect for a weekend getaway or a full-fledged vacation. Newfane is known for its scenic beauty, outdoor adventures, rich history, and cultural attractions. In this travel guide, we will explore the best things to do in Newfane, places to visit, hiking/biking routes, local food and drink, where to stay, local culture and customs, safety, transportation options, and nearby destinations.

List of Places to Visit in Newfane

Newfane is home to several culturally important attractions, museums, historical places, natural attractions, outdoor adventures, and scenic views. Here is a list of must-visit places in Newfane:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Newfane Village Historic District: This is a well-preserved and visually stunning colonial village.
  • Newfane Congregational Church Cemetery: A spooky but alluring site, this cemetery is known for its historic headstones and breathtaking views.
  • The Green: A historic town common that is home to numerous annual events and festivals throughout the year.
  • Windham County Historical Society Museum: Located in the county courthouse, this museum boasts several exhibits that detail the rich history of Southern Vermont.

Natural Attractions of Newfane: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Snow: The ideal spot for outdoor activities such as skiing and snowboarding, hiking, and other outdoor adventures.
  • Prospect Mountain State Park: This park offers breathtaking views of Southern Vermont, including Mount Snow and the Deerfield River Valley.
  • Townshend State Park: Known for its birdwatching, scenic views, and hiking trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes in Newfane

Newfane offers several excellent hiking and biking trails, where you can explore the natural beauty of Southern Vermont. Here are some of the best hiking and biking trails in Newfane:

  • Mount Snow: This is a popular destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. The mountain features a range of trails, from beginner to advanced.
  • West River Trail: This is an easy, flat trail that runs along the West River.
  • Molly Stark State Park Trail System: This trail system has over 10 miles of hiking and biking trails.

Best Things to Do in Newfane: Activities and Tours

Newfane offers many exciting things to do, from outdoor adventures to cultural excursions. Here are some must-try activities:

  • Go skiing or snowboarding: Mount Snow is the perfect place to enjoy winter sports activities.
  • Go apple picking: The orchards around Newfane and Southern Vermont offer a fun activity to enjoy with family and friends.
  • Visit a Sugarhouse: Learn about the process of making maple syrup at a local sugarhouse.
  • Take a winery tour: Tasting wines and touring a vineyard is a great way to relax and unwind.

Food and Drink Scene of Newfane

Newfane has a thriving food and drink scene, with plenty of options for visitors to explore. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Maple syrup: Vermont is famous for its maple syrup, and Newfane hosts a large number of sugarhouses, where you can learn about the process of making it.
  • Vermont cheddar cheese: Vermont is known for producing delicious cheddar cheese.
  • Apple cider donuts: Apple cider donuts are a staple in most orchards in Southern Vermont.

Local Beverages

  • Craft beer: Vermont is home to over 100 breweries, with several located in Southern Vermont.
  • Vermont cider: Vermont cider is made from locally sourced apples, and many cideries offer tours and tastings.

Where to Stay in Newfane: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Newfane has a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets. Options include hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay in Newfane:

  • Four Columns: This hotel is a historical property situated at the heart of Newfane village, where you can enjoy the ideal comfortable stay.
  • The Inn at Newfane: This 19th-century inn offers classic New England charm and modern amenities.
  • The Moore Free Library: This historical library, turned into a guesthouse for travellers, offers a unique experience in a beautiful setting.

What to Buy in Newfane: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Newfane offers many souvenirs and local products for visitors to take home. Here are some ideas:

  • Maple syrup: Buy some local Vermont pure maple syrup as a delicious souvenir.
  • Craft beer: Take home some locally brewed craft beer as a gift or souvenir.
  • Cheddar cheese: Vermont cheddar cheese is a great gift for the foodie in your life.

Local Culture and Customs of Newfane

Newfane hosts several annual cultural events and festivals every year, honoring the town’s history, festivals, and traditions. Additionally, Newfane has many long-established traditions like a Halloween costume parade and an annual pig roast. As a visitor, make sure to respect local customs and traditions and be mindful of local residents by acting courteously and responsibly.

Best Time to Visit Newfane

The best time to visit Newfane is from June to October when the weather is mild, and the foliage is at its peak. Additionally, if you like winter sports, you can visit from December to March to enjoy skiing and snowboarding activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Newfane

As a visitor, here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Newfane:

Do:

  • Explore Newfane’s scenic beauty through the many trails and parks available.
  • Support local businesses.

Don’t:

  • Litter.
  • Disrespect local residents or their traditions.
  • Overlook the safety codes when performing outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Newfane is a safe place to visit, but it is always wise to take necessary precautions as you would during any travel. Here are some safety tips to ensure a safe and healthy trip to Newfane:

Safety in Newfane

  • Always observe safety codes and take adequate precautions while exploring the outdoors.
  • Be conscious of the wildlife, particularly when hiking in wooded areas.
  • Keep an eye on the weather.

Health in Newfane

  • It is advisable to have a medical and travel insurance while traveling.
  • Carry any necessary medication with you.
  • Be cautious about tick bites, particularly while hiking, which can cause Lyme disease.

Where to Go Next from Newfane: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several nearby destinations worth exploring once you’ve had your fill of Newfane. Here are some of them:

Conclusion

Newfane is truly a hidden gem in Southern Vermont that offers something for everyone: from outdoor adventures and scenic views to cultural attractions and delicious food and drink.
