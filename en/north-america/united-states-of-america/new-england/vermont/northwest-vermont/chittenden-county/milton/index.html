Introduction to Milton

Milton is a charming town located in the Chittenden County of Northwest Vermont. It is known for its natural beauty, historical significance, and peaceful ambiance. The town boasts of rolling hills, picturesque landscapes, and historic sites that are sure to leave an indelible impression on any traveler. This travel guide will help you explore the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and what to eat in Milton, Vermont.

List of Places to Visit in Milton

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Milton Historical Society Museum

The Milton Historical Society Museum is a must-visit for history buffs. This museum boasts of a vast collection of artifacts, memorabilia, and exhibits that take visitors through the town’s rich past. It is open year-round and offers guided tours.

Milton Independent

The Milton Independent is the town’s newspaper and is still in operation today. Visitors can take a tour of the newspaper’s offices and see how it operates.

Natural Attractions of Milton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Arrowhead Mountain Lake

Arrowhead Mountain Lake is a serene lake that is perfect for swimming, kayaking, or fishing. Visitors can rent kayaks or take a dip in the crystal-clear water.

Lamoille River

The Lamoille River is a great spot for fishing enthusiasts. It is home to Trout, Salmon, and Bass.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Milton

Arrowhead Mountain

Arrowhead Mountain is a great hiking spot in the town. It offers breathtaking views of the surrounding foliage and nature. Hikers of all skill levels can take the trails at Arrowhead Mountain.

Best Things to Do in Milton: Activities and Tours

The Flying V Farm

The Flying V Farm is a working farm that offers visitors tours and tastings. Visitors can taste fresh produce, cheese, and milk while learning about the daily operations of a farm.

Lake Champlain Chocolates

Lake Champlain Chocolates is known for its handcrafted chocolates made with local ingredients. Visitors can take a tour of the factory while indulging in delicious chocolates.

Week-Long Itinerary for Milton

Food and Drink Scene of Milton

Must-Try Dishes

Maple Syrup

Vermont is known for its delicious maple syrup, and Milton is no exception. Visitors should try the town’s local maple syrup, which can be found in many establishments throughout the town.

Vermont Cheeses

Vermont cheeses are famous throughout the country. Milton has many dairy farms, making it the perfect spot to try some delicious Vermont cheeses.

Local Beverages

Vermont Craft Beers

Vermont is home to many craft breweries, and visitors should try some local beers while in Milton. The beers are made with local ingredients and have a unique taste that you won’t find anywhere else.

Cider

Cider is a popular drink in Vermont, and visitors should try some while in Milton. Many local establishments sell fresh cider made with local apples.

Where to Stay in Milton: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Milton Motel

The Milton Motel is a great lodging option for travelers on a budget. It is clean, comfortable, and affordable.

The Inn at Charlotte

The Inn at Charlotte is a great option for those looking for upscale accommodation. It is located in a beautiful Victorian mansion and offers an unforgettable experience.

What to Buy in Milton: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Vermont Maple Syrup

Vermont Maple Syrup is the perfect gift to bring home to family and friends.

Vermont Cheeses

Vermont’s cheeses are a must-try and make for the perfect gift to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Milton

The town of Milton is known for its friendly, welcoming locals. Visitors should take the time to explore the town’s historic sites and beautiful natural landscapes.

Best Time to Visit Milton

The best time to visit Milton is during the fall when the foliage is at its peak. The colors are vibrant and the town is buzzing with activity.

Do’s and Don’ts of Milton

Do:

  • Explore the town’s historic sites and natural landscapes.
  • Try the local maple syrup and cheeses.
  • Shop for souvenirs to bring back home.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in natural areas.
  • Don’t be disrespectful to the locals or their culture.

Safety and Health

Safety in Milton

Milton is a safe town, and visitors should take basic precautions against theft and fraud.

Health in Milton

Milton has a community health center that offers basic medical services. Visitors should have travel insurance for more significant health-related issues.

Getting to Milton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Milton

The Burlington International Airport is the nearest airport to Milton. It is about 25 miles away from the town.

Bus Stations Near Milton

The Greyhound Bus station is the nearest bus station to Milton.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to the town or use a taxi service.

How to Get Around in Milton

Visitors can rent a car or bike to travel around the town easily. There are also taxi services available.

Where to Go Next from Milton: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Burlington

Burlington is a nearby town that is known for its vibrant food and drink scene, shopping, and natural beauty.

Stowe

Stowe is a town known for its mountain skiing, outdoor activities, and beautiful hotel resorts.

Conclusion

Milton is a small town that offers a wide range of natural beauty, historical sites, and cultural attractions. Visitors can enjoy their time in the town by exploring the local culture, tasting delicious local foods, and shopping for souvenirs to bring back home. Plan your trip to Milton using this comprehensive travel guide, and you are sure to have an unforgettable experience in this charming town.
