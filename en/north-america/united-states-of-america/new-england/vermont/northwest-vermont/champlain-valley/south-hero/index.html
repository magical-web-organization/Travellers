Introduction to South Hero

Located in the Champlain Valley of Northwest Vermont, South Hero is a tranquil and charming rural town that boasts scenic views, abundant greenery, and a vibrant local culture. With a population of just over 1,600 people, this picturesque destination is perfect for travelers who are looking for a peaceful getaway from the hustle and bustle of the city. South Hero is a must-visit for those who seek outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, and a taste of Vermont’s local cuisine.

List of Places to Visit in South Hero

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

South Hero Historical Society Museum

Located in the heart of South Hero, the Historical Society Museum takes visitors on a journey back in time with its collection of exhibits, artifacts, and archives. Get a glimpse of what life was like for South Hero’s early settlers and learn about the town’s rich history.

Snow Farm Vineyard and Winery

Get a taste of Vermont’s local wine scene at Snow Farm Vineyard and Winery. Established in 1996, this winery offers visitors the opportunity to savor their award-winning wines while enjoying stunning views of Lake Champlain.

Natural Attractions of South Hero: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Allenholm Farm

Allenholm Farm is a working farm in South Hero that offers visitors the opportunity to pick their own fruits and vegetables, visit the farm animals, and enjoy the stunning views of Lake Champlain.

Sand Bar State Park

Located on the outskirts of South Hero, Sand Bar State Park is a must-visit destination for nature lovers. The park offers a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking, biking, swimming, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of South Hero

South Hero Bike Trail

This 9-mile paved bike trail runs along the western shore of South Hero and offers stunning views of Lake Champlain and the Adirondack Mountains.

The Island Line Trail

The Island Line Trail is a 14-mile recreational trail that connects South Hero to Burlington. The trail runs along the waterfront and features stunning views of Lake Champlain.

Best Things to Do in South Hero: Activities and Tours

Champlain Valley Alpacas

Experience the unique charm of rural Vermont at Champlain Valley Alpacas, where visitors can interact with these friendly animals, learn about their care and maintenance, and purchase alpaca products.

Island Arts

Discover the vibrant local arts scene of South Hero at Island Arts, a non-profit organization that promotes local art and culture. The shop features a variety of locally-made arts and crafts, including paintings, ceramics, textiles, and more.

Week-Long Itinerary for South Hero

Day Activity
Day 1 Explore the South Hero Historical Society Museum and Snow Farm Vineyard and Winery.
Day 2 Take a bike ride on the South Hero Bike Trail and visit Allenholm Farm.
Day 3 Hike at Sand Bar State Park and relax by the lake.
Day 4 Spend the day in Burlington, visit the nearby Church Street Marketplace and go on a Lake Champlain cruise.
Day 5 Visit Champlain Valley Alpacas and Island Arts.
Day 6 Drive to nearby Stowe and go on a hike in Mt. Mansfield State Forest.
Day 7 Relax and unwind by the lake with a picnic or try some local Vermont delicacies.

Food and Drink Scene of South Hero

Must-Try Dishes

Maple Syrup

No visit to Vermont is complete without trying some of the state’s famous maple syrup, which is made from the sap of sugar maple trees.

Vermont Cheddar Cheese

Vermont is renowned for its cheddar cheese, made from the milk of local dairy cows. Take a tour of a local cheese factory and savor the unique flavors of Vermont’s cheddar cheese.

Local Beverages

Vermont Craft Beer

Vermont is home to a thriving craft beer scene, with a variety of breweries scattered throughout the state. Try some of Vermont’s best craft beers at local breweries such as Fiddlehead Brewing Company and Zero Gravity Craft Brewery.

Cider

Vermont is also known for its delicious apple cider. Take a tour of one of the local cider mills and savor the refreshing taste of Vermont’s apple cider.

Where to Stay in South Hero: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

The North Hero House Inn and Restaurant

This charming inn is located just a short drive from South Hero and features stunning views of Lake Champlain. The inn offers cozy and comfortable rooms and a Vermont-style restaurant.

The Snow Farm Vineyard and Winery

For a unique and luxurious experience, stay at The Snow Farm Vineyard and Winery’s luxury suite, which features stunning views of the vineyard and Lake Champlain. Guests can enjoy exclusive access to the vineyard and winery, as well as a complimentary tasting of their award-winning wines.

What to Buy in South Hero: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Vermont Maple Syrup

Take home a taste of Vermont with some locally-made maple syrup. Stop by a local sugarhouse and pick up some of Vermont’s famous maple syrup to enjoy at home.

Handmade Crafts and Artwork

Support the local arts scene and take home a piece of Vermont’s charm with some handmade arts and crafts, such as pottery, textiles, paintings, or sculptures.

Local Culture and Customs of South Hero

Vermont has a rich and unique culture, shaped by its local traditions, history, and natural surroundings. Residents of Vermont are known for their down-to-earth and friendly demeanor, and visitors are encouraged to embrace the state’s relaxed and inviting vibe.

Best Time to Visit South Hero

The best time to visit South Hero is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and the town comes alive with outdoor activities and local events.

Do’s and Don’ts of South Hero

Do:

  • try some of Vermont’s famous maple syrup and cheddar cheese
  • explore the local arts and crafts scene
  • embrace the relaxed and friendly vibe of Vermont

Don’t:

  • forget to pack comfortable shoes for hiking and biking
  • litter or damage the natural environment
  • assume that all of Vermont is rustic and rural - there are plenty of urban areas as well.

Safety and Health

Safety in South Hero

South Hero is a safe destination for travelers, but visitors should exercise caution when participating in outdoor activities, such as hiking, biking, or swimming. Wear appropriate safety equipment and be mindful of your surroundings.

Health in South Hero

The local hospital in South Hero is Northwest Medical Center, located in nearby St. Albans. Visitors should also take precautions to protect themselves from tick-borne illnesses, such as Lyme disease, by wearing long-sleeved clothing and using insect repellent.

Getting to South Hero: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near South Hero

The nearest airport to South Hero is the Burlington International Airport, which is located approximately 17 miles from South Hero.

Bus Stations Near South Hero

The main bus station in the area is the Burlington Greyhound Station, located approximately 20 miles from South Hero.

Other Options

Travelers can also rent a car or take a taxi from the airport or bus station to South Hero.

How to Get Around in South Hero

The best way to get around South Hero is by car or bike. Visitors can also rent kayaks or stand-up paddleboards to explore the lake.

Where to Go Next from South Hero: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Burlington

Located just a short drive from South Hero, Burlington is a vibrant and bustling city that offers visitors a variety of cultural attractions, shopping, restaurants, and bars.

Stowe

Drive to nearby Stowe and enjoy the stunning views of Mt. Mansfield, Vermont’s highest peak. Go on a hike, take a scenic drive, or relax in one of the town’s many spas and resorts.

Conclusion

South Hero is a small town with a big heart and a lot of charm. With its stunning natural beauty, vibrant local culture, and delicious local cuisine, South Hero is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking a relaxing and authentic Vermont vacation. Whether you’re a nature lover, culture seeker, or foodie, South Hero has something for everyone.
