Surry, a small town in Hancock County, Down East, Maine, is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. This cozy town may not be on every traveler’s radar, but once you visit, you’ll be smitten with its quaint charm and stunning natural beauty. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know to make the most of your trip to Surry. From top places to visit and things to do, to where to eat and stay, we’ve got you covered.

Introduction to Surry

Surry is a picturesque town nestled on the Union River estuary, surrounded by breathtaking views of water, mountains, and woodland. Located in Down East, Maine, this small town is rich in maritime history, bustling with culture and outdoor adventures. Surry is known for its lobstering industry, hiking and biking trails, stunning scenic drives, historic landmarks, and small-town charm. Whether you’re seeking a peaceful retreat, outdoor adventure, or cultural experience, Surry has something to offer everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Surry

Surry is full of hidden gems waiting to be discovered. Here are the top places you must visit while in Surry:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Surry Historical Society & Museum – This museum showcases the rich history of Surry, from early settlement to modern times. It’s a great way to learn about the town’s rich maritime history and local culture.

  2. Ellsworth Public Library – Though not strictly in Surry, Ellsworth’s public library has a collection of antique maps of Surry and the surrounding region that are truly fascinating.

Natural Attractions of Surry: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Acadia National Park – A short drive from Surry, Acadia National Park offers some of the most breathtaking views in the state of Maine. Take a scenic drive around the Park Loop Road, enjoy hiking up Cadillac Mountain, or relax on the picturesque Sand Beach.

  2. Surry Forest – Explore the serene forests of Surry and experience nature at its best. This beautiful forest offers ample opportunities for hiking, cycling, or even camping.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Surry

Surry is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, with plenty of hiking and biking trails to explore. Here are some popular routes to check out:

  1. Acadia’s Carriage Trails & Jordan Pond – This 10-mile loop trail offers stunning views of Jordan Pond and the surrounding mountains. The trail is accessible to walkers and bikers and is an easy way to spend a few hours in nature.

  2. Surry Forest Trail – Take a hike on the trails of Surry Forest for a peaceful and immersive experience. The trails can be customized for length and level of difficulty, making it a great option for hikers of all levels.

Best Things to Do in Surry: Activities and Tours

  1. Go Lobstering – One of the must-do activities in Surry is to go lobstering. Join one of the local lobstermen on their boat and learn how to catch and cook lobsters.

  2. Visit Surry Arts & Events – A community-based nonprofit promoting the arts in Hancock County. Attend one of their local events ranging from visual arts shows to singing and instrument-playing workshops.

Week-long Itinerary for Surry

Here’s a suggested itinerary to help you make the most of your week in Surry:

  • Day 1: Visit Surry Historical Society & Museum and Ellsworth Public Library
  • Day 2: Hike the Acadia’s carriage trails & Jordan Pond
  • Day 3: Go Lobstering and enjoy a Lobster feast for dinner
  • Day 4: Explore Acadia National Park, visiting Sand Beach for a relaxing afternoon
  • Day 5: Visit Surry Forest Trail for a day of hiking and picnicking
  • Day 6: Attend one of the Surry Arts & Events show or workshop
  • Day 7: Take a scenic drive around the town and end the day enjoying a local beverage from one of Surry’s small breweries.

Food and Drink Scene of Surry

Surry offers a delightful food scene, with plenty of fresh seafood and local cuisines to sample. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to have in Surry:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Lobster roll – Savor the town’s famous lobster roll made from locally caught lobsters.

  2. Blueberry pie – Try the local specialty, Blueberry Pie, made from freshly picked blueberries from the surrounding fields for a sweet ending to your meal.

Local Beverages

  1. Maine craft beer – Surry has two breweries, Airline Brewing Company and Fogtown Brewing Co that offer fresh and locally brewed craft beers.

  2. Maine Blueberry wine – You must try the local Maine Blueberry wine, available in various fruit flavors like blueberries, cranberries, and apples, among others.

Where to Stay in Surry: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Surry offers a range of accommodations, from cozy bed and breakfasts to luxurious resorts. Here are some great options to call home during your stay in Surry:

  1. The Surry Inn – Surrounded by lush gardens, this Inn offers a cozy and comfortable stay with scenic views of the nearby river.

  2. Forest Ridge Campground – If you’re looking for an outdoor adventure, stay at this campground near the Surry Forest to experience nature at its best.

What to Buy in Surry: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Blueberry Jam – Take home the local Maine Blueberry jam as a souvenir and delight your taste buds long after your trip.

  2. Handmade Pottery – The town has various pottery shops where you can pick up local and handmade collectibles as keepsakes.

Local Culture and Customs of Surry

Surry is a small town that prides itself on retaining its local culture and customs. The town has a friendly and welcoming community that will make you feel at home. Be sure to check out the local festivals, fairs, and events to get a glimpse of Surry’s rich culture and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Surry

The best time to visit Surry is from May through October. The weather during this time is pleasant, and there are ample opportunities for outdoor activities. The town is bustling with life during the summer months, with many festivals, fairs, and outdoor events.

Do’s and Don’ts of Surry

Do:

  • Do respect the town and its local customs.
  • Do try the locally sourced fresh seafood.
  • Do hike, bike, or even camp for a unique experience.
  • Do attend the local events and fairs.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or harm the local flora and fauna
  • Don’t explore restricted areas without permission.
  • Don’t be rude to the locals or the town’s law enforcement officials.

Safety and Health

Safety in Surry

Surry is a safe and friendly town with very low crime rates. However, you should follow standard safety precautions like locking your doors, being aware of your surroundings, and staying in well-lit areas at night.

Health in Surry

There are no known health risks specific to Surry. However, it’s always best to bring a basic first aid kit and any necessary prescription medication with you.

Getting to Surry: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Surry

The nearest airport is Bangor International Airport, situated around 45 minutes northeast of Surry.

Bus Stations Near Surry

The nearest bus station is in Ellsworth, a 12-minute drive southeast of Surry.

Other Options

Driving to Surry is the best option as it offers the flexibility to explore nearby attractions.

How to Get Around in Surry

A car is the most convenient way to get around in Surry, providing easy access to nearby attractions. Bicycles are also a fun way to explore the town and nearby cycling trails.

Where to Go Next from Surry: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Surry is close to many popular tourist destinations in Maine. Take a drive to:

  1. Bar Harbor – Visit the iconic village of Bar Harbor just an hour’s drive from Surry, known for its stunning rugged coastline, quaint shops and restaurants, and the stunning Acadia National Park.

  2. Camden Hills State Park – Take a two-hour drive to this beautiful park, famous for its scenic views of the Camden Harbor and hiking trails.

Conclusion

Surry is a hidden gem tucked away in the picturesque coast of Maine. With unmatched natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and a friendly community, Surry offers the perfect blend of new experiences and nostalgic charm. Plan your trip with our comprehensive guide, and you’ll be sure to fall in love with this unique and unforgettable town.
