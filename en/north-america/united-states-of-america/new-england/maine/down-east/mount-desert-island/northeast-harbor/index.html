Introduction to Northeast Harbor

Northeast Harbor is a charming seaside village located on Mount Desert Island in Down East, Maine. This delightful harbor community, situated in the heart of Acadia National Park, offers visitors the perfect combination of scenic natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and cultural and historical landmarks, making it the ideal vacation destination for individuals, couples, or families.

List of Places to Visit in Northeast Harbor

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Visitors to Northeast Harbor can immerse themselves in the rich cultural heritage of the area by visiting some of the region’s most culturally significant attractions, such as:

  • Asticou Azalea Garden
  • Great Harbor Maritime Museum
  • Abbe Museum
  • Maine Granite Industry Museum
  • Wendell Gilley Museum

Natural Attractions of Northeast Harbor: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Northeast Harbor is a hiker’s paradise, offering visitors miles of beautiful trails through breathtaking natural scenery. The most popular hiking routes in Northeast Harbor include:

  • Jordan Pond Path
  • Champlain Mountain & Beehive Trail
  • Hadlock Brook Trail
  • Sargent Mountain Loop Trail
  • Cadillac Summit Loop Trail

In addition, visitors can explore the natural beauty of Northeast Harbor by kayaking, canoeing, sailing, or just sightseeing. The area also offers a wide range of water sports, such as fishing, swimming, and windsurfing.

Best Things to Do in Northeast Harbor: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and outdoor adventures, there are plenty of other activities and tours to enjoy in Northeast Harbor:

  • Take a scenic boat tour
  • Rent a bicycle and explore the area on two wheels
  • Visit a local winery or brewery
  • Attend a craft fair or music festival
  • Go golfing at Kebo Valley Golf Club

Week-Long Itinerary for Northeast Harbor

For visitors planning to spend a week in Northeast Harbor, we recommend the following itinerary:

Day Activity
1 Hike the Jordan Pond Path and enjoy lunch at the Jordan Pond House
2 Go sailing or kayaking in the harbor
3 Tour the Great Harbor Maritime Museum and the Maine Granite Industry Museum
4 Take a bike ride through Acadia National Park
5 Visit the Wendell Gilley Museum and take a bird carving workshop
6 Golf at the Kebo Valley Golf Club and have lunch at the clubhouse
7 Attend a local craft fair or music festival

Safety and Health

Safety in Northeast Harbor

Although Northeast Harbor is relatively safe, visitors should be aware of some safety precautions, such as:

  • Staying aware of wildlife dangers, such as tick bites and bear attacks
  • Taking care when hiking or biking on rugged terrain
  • Being cautious when driving on mountain roads

Health in Northeast Harbor

Visitors to Northeast Harbor should be aware of some of the health risks commonly associated with outdoor activities, such as:
