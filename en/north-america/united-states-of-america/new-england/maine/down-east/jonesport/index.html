Introduction to Jonesport

Nestled in the heart of Down East Maine, Jonesport is a picturesque seaside village that boasts of breathtaking natural beauty and rich maritime history. Situated on the coast of the Gulf of Maine, Jonesport is home to some of the nation’s finest lobstermen and fishermen. In this travel guide, we will explore the top attractions, activities, and local culture of Jonesport to help you plan a memorable vacation in this charming town.

List of Places to Visit in Jonesport

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Roy Toy Manufacturing Factory - This family-owned business manufactures eco-friendly wooden toys that are loved by kids and adults alike. The factory tour includes a visit to the manufacturing area and a gift shop.
  • Great Wass Island Preserve - Spread over 1,500 acres, Great Wass Island Preserve is a natural and cultural treasure. Visitors can enjoy hiking trails, scenic views, and bird watching in the preserve.
  • Jonesport Historical Society - This museum showcases the rich maritime history of Jonesport and displays artifacts and photographs of the town’s past.

Natural Attractions of Jonesport: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Jonesport-Beals Island Bridge - This drawbridge connects Jonesport to neighboring Beals Island and offers stunning views of the harbor.
  • Jasper Beach - This unique black-and-white sand beach is a must-see attraction. The beach is named after the smooth, polished stones called jasper that line the shoreline.
  • Moosehorn National Wildlife Refuge - Located just a short drive from Jonesport, Moosehorn National Wildlife Refuge offers visitors a chance to explore the natural beauty of Maine. The refuge is home to over 250 species of birds, moose, black bears, and other wildlife.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jonesport

  • Great Wass Island Preserve Hike - This 4-mile round trip hike takes you through the rugged coastal landscape of Great Wass Island Preserve. The trail includes steep climbs and descents, but the breathtaking views are worth the effort.
  • Bold Coast Scenic Bike Route - This 25-mile bike route takes you through the picturesque coastline of Down East Maine. Visitors can enjoy stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean and explore fishing villages along the way.

Best Things to Do in Jonesport: Activities and Tours

  • Lobster Fishing Tour - Experience the best of Jonesport’s maritime culture by going on a lobster fishing tour. Visitors can learn about the lobster industry and even catch their own lobster.
  • Jonesport Craft Fair - Held every summer, the Jonesport Craft Fair features over 50 vendors selling handmade crafts, food, and drink. Visitors can enjoy live music and entertainment during the fair.
  • Kayak Rental - Rent a kayak and explore the rugged coastline of Down East Maine. Visitors can paddle through scenic coves and inlets, and even spot wildlife such as seals and bald eagles.

Food and Drink Scene of Jonesport

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lobster Roll - A quintessential Maine dish, the lobster roll can be found at almost every restaurant in Jonesport. A warm buttered roll filled with fresh lobster meat is a delicious and decadent treat.
  • Blueberry Pie - Maine is famous for its blueberries, and Jonesport is no exception. A slice of blueberry pie, made with fresh Maine blueberries, is a must-try dessert.

Local Beverages

  • Allen’s Blueberry Wine - Made from Maine’s native blueberries, Allen’s Blueberry Wine is a refreshing and unique wine that is perfect for sipping on a warm summer day.
  • Shipyard Brewing Co. Beer - Located just a short drive from Jonesport, Shipyard Brewing Co. offers a wide selection of beer. Visitors can enjoy a cold brew in the brewery’s tasting room or take a tour of the facility.

Where to Stay in Jonesport: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Peacock House Bed and Breakfast - This charming bed and breakfast offers comfortable rooms and a delicious breakfast. Visitors can enjoy the peaceful setting and scenic views of the harbor.
  • Jonesport Motel - Located in the heart of town, the Jonesport Motel offers clean and comfortable rooms at an affordable price.

What to Buy in Jonesport: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Maine Blueberry Jam - Bring home a taste of Maine with a jar of blueberry jam. Made with fresh Maine blueberries, this jam is a delicious and unique souvenir.
  • Roy Toy Building Blocks - Made in Jonesport, these eco-friendly wooden building blocks are a timeless toy that kids and adults will love.

Local Culture and Customs of Jonesport

Jonesport is known for its strong maritime culture. Visitors can learn about the town’s lobster and fishing industry by visiting local landmarks and attending cultural events. The town also has a rich shipbuilding history, and visitors can explore the town’s shipbuilding heritage at the historical society museum.

Best Time to Visit Jonesport

The best time to visit Jonesport is during the summer months from June to August. The weather is warm and sunny, and visitors can enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, kayaking, and fishing. Fall is also a great time to visit, with the foliage changing colors and the town’s craft fairs and harvest festivals taking place. Winter and spring are less crowded and offer a chance to experience the town’s quiet and peaceful side.

Do’s and Don’ts of Jonesport

Do:

  • Do try the local seafood, especially the lobster.
  • Do explore the natural beauty of Jonesport by going on a hike or kayak adventure.
  • Do attend the Jonesport Craft Fair to support local artisans.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter on the beaches or trails.
  • Don’t disturb the wildlife in their natural habitats.
  • Don’t trespass on private property.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jonesport

Jonesport is a small, friendly town that is generally safe for visitors. However, visitors should exercise caution when hiking or kayaking, as the terrain can be rugged and unpredictable. Visitors should also be aware of the town’s maritime heritage and follow all safety guidelines when on a fishing or lobster tour.

Health in Jonesport

There are no major health concerns in Jonesport. Visitors should be aware of the sun exposure during outdoor activities and take proper precautions such as using sunscreen and staying hydrated.

Getting to Jonesport: Transportation Options and Logistics

Jonesport is located in Down East Maine, approximately a 6-hour drive from Boston, Massachusetts. The town can be accessed by car, bus, or plane.

Airports Near Jonesport

The closest airport to Jonesport is the Bangor International Airport, located approximately 2 hours west of Jonesport. Visitors can rent a car or take a shuttle service from the airport to Jonesport.

Bus Stations Near Jonesport

The nearest bus station is in Bangor, which is approximately 2 hours away from Jonesport. Visitors can rent a car or take a shuttle service from Bangor to Jonesport.

Other Options

Visitors can also take a ferry from neighboring Eastport to Bar Harbor, which is a popular tourist destination in Maine.

How to Get Around in Jonesport

Jonesport is a small town that can be easily explored on foot or by bike. Visitors can also rent a car to explore nearby attractions.

Where to Go Next from Jonesport: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Bar Harbor - Located on Maine’s stunning Mount Desert Island, Bar Harbor is a popular tourist destination that is famous for its charming downtown, Acadia National Park, and scenic beauty.
  • Eastport - Just across the water from Jonesport, Eastport is a quaint fishing village known for its beautiful architecture, galleries, and seafood.
  • Acadia National Park - A short drive from Jonesport, Acadia National Park offers visitors stunning views of rugged coastline, Cadillac Mountain, and wildlife.

Conclusion

Jonesport is a charming seaside village that offers visitors a chance to escape the hustle and bustle of everyday life and immerse themselves in Maine’s natural and cultural beauty. From exploring the town’s maritime history to enjoying outdoor activities such as hiking and kayaking, there is something for everyone in Jonesport. Use this comprehensive travel guide to help plan your trip and make the most of your time in this charming town.
