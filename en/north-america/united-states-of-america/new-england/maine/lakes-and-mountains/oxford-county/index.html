Introduction to Oxford County

Located in the Lakes and Mountains region of Maine, Oxford County is a charming and picturesque area filled with natural beauty and cultural gems. With numerous outdoor adventures, historical sites, and local attractions, Oxford County is an ideal destination for travelers looking to experience the unique beauty of New England. This comprehensive guide will provide everything you need to know about visiting Oxford County, from the best places to stay to the top things to see and do.

Local Culture and Customs of Oxford County

Oxford County’s heritage is steeped in Native American, French, and English customs. The locals cherish their traditions, and visitors are encouraged to respect the customs and culture of the area.

Best Time to Visit Oxford County

The best time to visit Oxford County is during autumn when the leaves change color, or in winter when you can enjoy skiing and snowboarding. In the summer, there are numerous outdoor activities to enjoy, while spring brings blooming flowers and fresh greenery.

Do’s and Don’ts of Oxford County

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and culture
  • Try the local cuisine
  • Take advantage of outdoor activities

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural areas
  • Be disruptive or disrespectful to locals

Safety and Health

Safety in Oxford County

Oxford County is a safe and welcoming area with low crime rates. However, it is recommended to take precautions when exploring natural areas such as Grafton Notch State Park.

Health in Oxford County

Oxford County is served by a range of medical facilities, including several hospitals and clinics. It is advised to obtain proper travel insurance and carry any necessary medications and prescriptions.

How to Get Around in Oxford County

The best way to get around Oxford County is by car, as many of the area’s attractions are spread out. However, there are also shuttle services available for those without their own transportation.
