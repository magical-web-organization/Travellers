Introduction to Newry

Newry is a charming town located in Oxford County, Maine, in the heart of the Lakes and Mountains region in New England. It is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, offering a variety of recreational activities such as hiking, skiing, and fishing. Newry is also home to several cultural attractions, including museums and historical sites. Whether you are planning a weekend getaway or a longer vacation, Newry has something to offer for everyone.

  • Bethel Historical Society
  • Sunday River Covered Bridge

Natural Attractions of Newry: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Hiking/Biking Routes of Newry

Newry offers a variety of hiking and biking trails for visitors of all skill levels. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • Eyebrow Trail
  • Sunday River Whitecap Trail
  • Step Falls Trail

Best Things to Do in Newry: Activities and Tours

  • Go skiing or snowboarding at Sunday River Ski Resort
  • Take a scenic drive along the Grafton Notch Scenic Byway
  • Go fly-fishing in the Androscoggin River

Food and Drink Scene of Newry

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lobster Roll: A classic Maine dish made with fresh lobster meat and mayonnaise, served on a soft bun.
  • Blueberry Pie: A sweet treat made with fresh Maine blueberries and a flaky crust.
  • Clam Chowder: A creamy soup made with clams, potatoes, and herbs.

Local Beverages

  • Shipyard Brewing Company: A local brewery that produces a variety of beers, including their signature Pumpkinhead Ale.
  • Tumbledown Brewing: A craft brewery that offers a rotating selection of beers made with locally sourced ingredients.
  • Maine Mead Works: A meadery that produces a variety of honey-based wines and ciders.

Where to Stay in Newry: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Bethel Inn Resort: A historic resort that offers golf, skiing, and other recreational activities.
  • The Grand Summit Hotel at Sunday River: A ski-in/ski-out hotel located at the base of Sunday River Ski Resort.
  • The Chapman Inn: A cozy bed and breakfast located in the heart of Bethel.

What to Buy in Newry: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handcrafted Jewelry: Maine has a thriving craft scene, and you can find unique handmade jewelry made with gemstones and local materials.
  • Maple Syrup: Maine is known for its delicious pure maple syrup, which makes a perfect souvenir or gift.
  • Handmade Soap: There are several local soap makers in the area that create all-natural soaps using locally sourced ingredients.

Local Culture and Customs of Newry

Newry is a small town with a strong sense of community. Visitors should be respectful of local customs, including:

  • Dressing appropriately for outdoor activities and weather conditions.
  • Removing shoes before entering someone’s home.
  • Being mindful of noise levels in residential areas.

Best Time to Visit Newry

The best time to visit Newry depends on your preferences and activities. Summer and fall offer mild temperatures and excellent hiking and biking conditions. Winter is perfect for skiing and snowboarding, and cold temperatures provide a scenic winter wonderland. Spring is an excellent time to see the area’s wildflowers and waterfalls.

Do’s and Don’ts of Newry

Do:

  • Respect local customs and be mindful of noise levels in residential areas.
  • Take advantage of outdoor recreational opportunities.
  • Support local businesses and artisans.

Don’t:

  • Leave trash or litter on hiking trails or other public areas.
  • Ignore signs and instructions related to safety.
  • Disturb local wildlife.

Safety and Health

Safety in Newry

Newry is generally a safe and peaceful town. Visitors should take the usual precautions, such as locking car doors and being aware of their surroundings. Hikers and outdoor enthusiasts should be prepared with appropriate equipment and clothing.

Health in Newry

Visitors to Newry should be aware of their personal health and safety. Local medical facilities are available in the nearby town of Bethel, but visitors should also be prepared with any prescribed medications and first aid supplies.

Getting to Newry: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Newry

  • Portland International Jetport: Located approximately 75 miles from Newry.
  • Manchester-Boston Regional Airport: Located approximately 140 miles from Newry.

Bus Stations Near Newry

  • Concord Coach Lines: Bus stops in Bethel, which is approximately 10 miles from Newry.

Other Options

  • Car rental: Car rental agencies are available at most airports and major cities.
  • Private transportation: Local taxi and shuttle services are available for transportation to and from Newry.

How to Get Around in Newry

The best way to get around in Newry is by car, as public transportation options are limited. Biking and walking are also popular options for exploring the town and its surroundings.

Where to Go Next from Newry: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Portland, Maine: A vibrant coastal city with excellent dining, shopping, and cultural attractions.
  • White Mountain National Forest: A vast wilderness area spanning New Hampshire and Maine, with excellent hiking, camping, and scenic drives.

Conclusion

Newry is a hidden gem in the Lakes and Mountains region of Maine, offering a variety of outdoor recreational activities, cultural attractions, and culinary experiences.
