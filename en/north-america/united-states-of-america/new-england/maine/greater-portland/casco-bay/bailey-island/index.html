Introduction to Bailey Island

Bailey Island is located in Casco Bay, in Greater Portland, Maine. With a small population of around 400 people, the island offers a tranquil and peaceful environment for travelers looking for a relaxing getaway. Nestled in the heart of Casco Bay, this island is home to breathtaking coastlines, scenic vistas, and cultural attractions that attract visitors from across the globe.
