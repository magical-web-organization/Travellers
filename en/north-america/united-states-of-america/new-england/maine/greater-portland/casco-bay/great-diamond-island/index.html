Introduction to Great Diamond Island

Great Diamond Island is a picturesque island located in Casco Bay, Maine, just a short ferry ride from Portland. With a rich history and breathtaking scenery, it is a perfect destination for a tranquil getaway. The island features stunning views of the ocean, miles of scenic hiking and biking trails, historical sites, and museums.

List of Places to Visit in Great Diamond Island

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Diamond Cove Historical Society Museum: This museum showcases the island’s rich history, including the military fortifications and the Victorian-era summer colony. Admission is free.

  • Fort McKinley: This former military installation is now a national historic district, featuring restored buildings and scenic views of the harbor.

  • Peaks Island Museum: This museum features exhibits showcasing the history of the island, including the marine industry and the summer colony. Admission is free.

Natural Attractions of Great Diamond Island: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Diamond Cove Beach: This secluded beach is perfect for sunbathing and swimming, with crystal clear waters and soft sand.

  • Tennis Court Trail: This scenic hiking trail offers beautiful ocean views and takes you past the historical tennis court and lighthouse.

  • Great Diamond Island Loop Trail: This 5-mile loop trail is perfect for hiking or biking, offering breathtaking views of the ocean and the surrounding islands.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Great Diamond Island

Great Diamond Island offers some of the most scenic hiking and biking trails in Maine. Some of the popular routes are:

  • Diamond Cove Loop Trail: This 3-mile loop trail takes you around the southern end of the island, passing by Diamond Cove Beach, Fort McKinley, and the historical tennis court.

  • Great Diamond Island Loop Trail: This 5-mile loop trail takes you around the entire island, offering breathtaking views of the ocean and the surrounding islands.

  • Circumnavigation Trail: This 10-mile trail takes you around the entire Casco Bay, offering stunning views of the coastline and the islands.

Best Things to Do in Great Diamond Island: Activities and Tours

  • Kayaking Tours: Explore the waters around Great Diamond Island with a kayaking tour, and enjoy stunning views of the coastline and the islands.

  • Fishing Tours: Try your luck at catching some of the local fish species, including striped bass, mackerel, and bluefish, with a fishing tour.

  • Sunset Cruise: Enjoy a romantic sunset cruise around Casco Bay, and take in the breathtaking views of the ocean and the islands.

Week-Long Itinerary for Great Diamond Island

Day 1-2: Explore Diamond Cove Beach and the Fort McKinley historic district.

Day 3-4: Hike or bike around the island on the Great Diamond Island Loop Trail or Tennis Court Trail.

Day 5-6: Take a kayak or fishing tour, or go for a sunset cruise around Casco Bay.

Food and Drink Scene of Great Diamond Island

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lobster Roll: A classic lobster roll is a must-try when visiting Maine, and you can find some of the best ones on Great Diamond Island.

  • Clam Chowder: This creamy and hearty soup is a staple of Maine cuisine, and is a must-try when visiting Great Diamond Island.

  • Blueberry Pie: Maine is known for its blueberries, and you can enjoy them in a delicious blueberry pie on Great Diamond Island.

Local Beverages

  • Allagash Beer: This local brewery offers a variety of handcrafted beers, including their flagship Allagash White Ale.

  • Shipyard Brewing Company: This popular brewery offers a variety of beers, including their famous Pumpkinhead Ale.

Where to Stay in Great Diamond Island: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Diamond’s Edge: This upscale hotel offers oceanfront rooms and suites, a full-service restaurant, and a spa.

  • Inn at Diamond Cove: This historic inn offers cozy rooms and suites, a restaurant, and access to the island’s amenities.

  • Great Diamond Rentals: This vacation rental agency offers a variety of rental properties on the island, including cottages, apartments, and houses.

What to Buy in Great Diamond Island: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handcrafted Jewelry: You can find beautiful handcrafted jewelry made by local artists on the island.

  • Local Artwork: The island is home to many talented artists, showcasing their works in local galleries and shops.

  • Maine Blueberry Jam: Bring home a jar of homemade blueberry jam, made from fresh local blueberries.

Local Culture and Customs of Great Diamond Island

Residents of Great Diamond Island take pride in their island community, which is known for its close-knit and friendly atmosphere. Visitors are encouraged to respect the island’s natural beauty and history, as well as the local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Great Diamond Island

The best time to visit Great Diamond Island is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny, and the island is bustling with activity.

Do’s and Don’ts of Great Diamond Island

Do:

  • Respect the island’s natural beauty and history.
  • Explore the island’s hiking trails and scenic views.
  • Try the local cuisine, including seafood and blueberries.
  • Check out the local artwork and handcrafted products.

Don’t:

  • Leave trash or litter around the island.
  • Disturb the local wildlife or plant life.
  • Trespass on private property.
  • Be rude or disrespectful to local residents.

Safety and Health

Safety in Great Diamond Island

Great Diamond Island is a safe destination, with low crime rates and a friendly community. Visitors are encouraged to be aware of their surroundings, avoid leaving valuables unattended, and follow basic safety precautions.

Health in Great Diamond Island

There are no major health concerns on Great Diamond Island, but visitors are advised to bring any necessary medications and take precautions against sunburn and insect bites.

Getting to Great Diamond Island: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Great Diamond Island

The closest airport to Great Diamond Island is Portland International Jetport, located just a short drive from the ferry terminal.

Bus Stations Near Great Diamond Island

The Concord Coach Lines bus station in Portland offers regular service to Boston, New York, and other destinations.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Great Diamond Island by car or bike, using the ferry service from Portland.

How to Get Around in Great Diamond Island

Great Diamond Island is a small island, and most of the attractions are within walking or biking distance. Visitors can also explore the island by golf cart or scooter, which are available for rent on the island.

Where to Go Next from Great Diamond Island: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Portland: The bustling city of Portland is just a short ferry ride away, offering a variety of shopping, dining, and cultural attractions.

  • Bailey Island: This scenic island is known for its rugged coastline, charming lighthouses, and fresh seafood.
