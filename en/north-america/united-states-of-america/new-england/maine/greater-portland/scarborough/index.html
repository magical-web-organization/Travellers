Introduction to Scarborough

Scarborough is a charming coastal town located in Greater Portland, Maine. This popular vacation destination is known for its beautiful beaches, outdoor activities, and rich history. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an adventure-filled vacation, Scarborough has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Scarborough

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Scarborough Historical Society and Museum: This museum gives visitors a glimpse into the town’s history, with exhibits featuring artifacts from the 1600s to the present day.

  • Higgins Beach: This popular beach is a great spot to relax and take in the stunning scenery. There are also several restaurants and ice cream shops nearby.

  • Scarborough Marsh Nature Center: This nature center offers a variety of educational programs and tours. Visitors can learn about the marsh’s ecosystem and wildlife, and even take a guided kayak tour.

Natural Attractions of Scarborough: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Scarborough Beach State Park: This beach is a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, and surfing. It also features picnic areas, a snack bar, and a gift shop.

  • Eastern Trail: This scenic trail spans from South Portland to Kennebunk, offering stunning views of the Maine coastline.

  • Black Point Trail: This trail is a 1.5-mile loop that offers beautiful views of the Scarborough River and Salt Marsh.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Scarborough

  • Saco Bay Trails: This network of trails spans across 6 towns, with over 30 miles of trails to explore. Visitors can enjoy hiking, biking, and even horseback riding.

  • Prouts Neck Cliff Walk: This scenic 2-mile walk offers breathtaking views of the ocean and rocky coastline.

Best Things to Do in Scarborough: Activities and Tours

  • Dunstan Schoolhouse: This historic one-room schoolhouse was built in 1856 and now serves as a museum. Visitors can learn about the town’s early education system and take a tour of the schoolhouse.

  • Scarborough Wine Tasting Tours: This tour company offers guided tours of local wineries and vineyards, with tastings and transportation included.

  • Libby River Farm: This farm offers horseback riding lessons and trail rides through the beautiful Maine countryside.

Food and Drink Scene of Scarborough

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lobster Roll: A classic Maine dish, the lobster roll is a must-try when visiting Scarborough.

  • Clam Chowder: Another New England staple, this creamy soup is made with fresh clams and potatoes.

  • Blueberry Pie: Maine is known for its delicious blueberries, and blueberry pie is a perfect way to enjoy this local favorite.

Local Beverages

  • Shipyard Brewing Company: This local brewery offers a variety of beers, including their famous Pumpkinhead ale.

  • Cellardoor Winery: Located just a short drive from Scarborough, this award-winning winery offers tastings and tours.

Where to Stay in Scarborough: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Black Point Inn: This historic inn offers comfortable rooms with stunning ocean views, as well as on-site dining and a spa.

  • The Higgins Beach Inn: This cozy inn is located just steps from Higgins Beach and features a restaurant and bar.

  • The Scarborough Inn: This budget-friendly inn offers comfortable rooms and easy access to all of Scarborough’s attractions.

What to Buy in Scarborough: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Maine Blueberry Jam: Made with fresh Maine blueberries, this jam is a sweet and tangy souvenir to bring home.

  • Lobster-related products: Lobster-themed souvenirs are abundant in Scarborough, with everything from t-shirts to magnets to stuffed animals.

Local Culture and Customs of Scarborough

Maine is known for its friendly locals and laid-back atmosphere. Visitors should be respectful of the town’s history and traditions, and should also be aware of the town’s strict noise ordinances.

Best Time to Visit Scarborough

The best time to visit Scarborough is in the summer, when the weather is warm and the beaches are open for swimming and sunbathing. Fall is also a beautiful time to visit, with the changing leaves and cooler temperatures.

Do’s and Don’ts of Scarborough

Do:

  • Visit the beaches: Scarborough is known for its beautiful beaches, so be sure to spend some time soaking up the sun.

  • Try the seafood: Maine is famous for its fresh seafood, so make sure to indulge in some lobster, clams, or other local favorites.

Don’t:

  • Be too loud: Scarborough has strict noise ordinances, so be aware of your noise level and respect your neighbors.

  • Leave trash on the beaches: Help keep Scarborough’s beaches clean by properly disposing of your trash.

Safety and Health

Safety in Scarborough

Scarborough is a safe town, but visitors should still take common sense precautions like locking their doors and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Scarborough

There are several medical clinics and hospitals located in nearby Portland for any medical needs that may arise.

Getting to Scarborough: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Scarborough

  • Portland International Jetport (PWM): Located just 10 miles from Scarborough, this airport offers flights to several major cities in the US.

Bus Stations Near Scarborough

  • Concord Coach Lines: This bus company offers daily service to and from Portland.

Other Options

  • Rental Cars: Rental cars are available at all major airports and in nearby Portland.

  • Taxis and Ride-Sharing Services: Uber and Lyft are available in the Portland area.

How to Get Around in Scarborough

The best way to get around Scarborough is by car, as many of the town’s attractions are spread out. There are also several bike rental shops located in town for those who prefer to explore on two wheels.

Where to Go Next from Scarborough: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Portland: This bustling city is known for its food scene and historical attractions.

  • Kennebunkport: This charming coastal town offers beautiful beaches and picturesque streets.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach vacation or an adventure-filled trip, Scarborough has something for everyone. With its beautiful scenery, rich history, and friendly locals, it’s no wonder why visitors flock to this charming coastal town year after year.
