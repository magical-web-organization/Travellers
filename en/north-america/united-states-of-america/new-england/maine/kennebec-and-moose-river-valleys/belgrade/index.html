Introduction to Belgrade

Belgrade, a small town in the heart of the Kennebec and Moose River Valleys, in Maine, is a popular tourist destination known for its scenic beauty, bustling food scene, and rich cultural heritage. Located in an idyllic setting with the Belgrade Lakes region, the town boasts pristine landscapes, clear waters, and an abundance of wildlife, which make it an ideal destination for nature lovers, hikers, and outdoor enthusiasts.

Whether you are looking to explore the cultural gems of the town or seeking an adventure in the wilderness, Belgrade has something for everyone. In this travel guide, we have compiled a comprehensive list of the best places to visit, dine, and stay in Belgrade, along with tips on how to plan your trip.

List of Places to Visit in Belgrade

When it comes to exploring the attractions of Belgrade, you will be spoilt for choice. From fascinating museums to hiking trails and scenic drives, this small town has a lot to offer for every traveler. Here is a list of must-visit attractions and experiences in Belgrade that you cannot afford to miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Belgrade is home to several museums and historical landmarks that provide a glimpse into its rich heritage and culture. Here are some of the top attractions that you should add to your list:

  • Belgrade Historical Society Museum: The Belgrade Historical Society Museum is a local history museum featuring exhibits on the town’s early settlers and their daily lives. The museum is located in the historic Union Meeting House, which was built in 1795.

  • Wings Hill Inn & Restaurant: Wings Hill Inn & Restaurant is a historic inn and restaurant built in 1880. It is a popular attraction for tourists who want to experience the town’s rural charm.

  • Village Inn & Tavern: The Village Inn & Tavern is a historic building that dates back to 1794. It houses a bar and restaurant that serves local cuisine and drinks.

  • Waterville Opera House: Waterville Opera House is a historic performing arts venue located in the nearby town of Waterville. The theater has been a cultural landmark in the region since it opened in 1902.

Natural Attractions of Belgrade: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Belgrade’s natural beauty is one of its biggest draws, with stunning scenery and wildlife viewing opportunities. Here are some of the top outdoor attractions and scenic views in the area:

  • Belgrade Lakes Region: This region is located in the central part of the state and is popular among tourists for its pristine beauty, including crystal-clear waters and the surrounding hills.

  • Mount Phillip: Located to the south of Belgrade, Mount Phillip is a popular destination for hiking and bird-watching. The summit offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding hills and woodlands.

  • Meadow Brook Wildlife Sanctuary: This sanctuary is a 217-acre nature preserve that features hiking trails, bird-watching, and cross-country skiing trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Belgrade

Belgrade is a paradise for hikers, bikers, and nature lovers with plenty of hiking and biking trails to explore. Here are some of the top hiking and biking routes around Belgrade:

  • Belgrade Lakes Trail: This trail is a popular scenic drive that runs through the Belgrade Lakes region. It offers stunning views of natural beauty, including mountains, forests, and pristine waters.

  • Belgrade Stream Trail: This trail is a popular hiking and biking route that runs along the stream, passing through the beautiful countryside and offering a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the town.

  • Long Pond Trail: This trail is a popular hiking route that runs along the edges of Long Pond, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding woods and waters.

Best Things to Do in Belgrade: Activities and Tours

Belgrade offers plenty of activities and tours to choose from to explore everything the town has to offer. Here are some of the top things to do in Belgrade:

  • Fishing and Boating: With the Belgrade Lakes in close proximity, fishing and boating are popular activities for tourists.

  • Sightseeing Tours: Several sightseeing tours are available, including boat tours of the lakes and town, bird-watching tours, and wildlife observation tours.

  • Cultural Events and Festivals: Belgrade hosts several cultural events and festivals throughout the year, such as the Belgrade Days festival in July and the Belgrade Central Fair in September.

Food and Drink Scene of Belgrade

Belgrade has a rich culinary heritage and is famous for its local cuisine. From seafood to red meat, the town offers a variety of dishes that will tantalize your taste buds. Here are some of the must-try dishes in Belgrade:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lobster Rolls: This popular seafood dish is a must-try when you visit Belgrade.

  • Maine Blueberry Pie: This sweet dish is a staple of the local cuisine and a favorite among tourists.

  • Mussels: Another popular seafood dish, mussels, are often served steamed with white wine or in a creamy sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Beer: Belgrade has several breweries that produce a variety of craft beers, ales, and lagers.

  • Wine: The town has several wineries offering tours and tastings of their award-winning wines.

  • Coffee: Belgrade has many local cafes serving signature coffee blends and baked goods.

Local Culture and Customs of Belgrade

Belgrade is known for its rich and vibrant local culture and customs. Here are some of the customs and practices that you should be aware of:

  • Friendliness: The people of Belgrade are known for their friendly and welcoming nature.

  • Rural Charm: Belgrade has retained its rural charm, and the locals take pride in preserving their traditions and way of life.

Best Time to Visit Belgrade

The best time to visit Belgrade is during the fall season when the town is decked in colors of the changing leaves. The weather is pleasant, and the town is less crowded during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Belgrade

When visiting Belgrade, there are some Dos and Don’ts to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Respect the Environment: Belgrade is known for its natural beauty, which you must respect and help preserve during your stay.

  • Be Polite: The people of Belgrade are friendly and welcoming. Be polite to them and respect their customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Litter: Littering is a punishable offense in Belgrade, so make sure you dispose of your waste responsibly.

  • Trespass: Always respect private property and do not trespass on someone’s land without permission.

Safety and Health

Belgrade is a safe town with low crime rates. Here are some tips to stay safe during your trip:

Safety in Belgrade

  • Travel Safely: Always follow safety rules while driving, biking, or hiking in the town.

  • Avoid Small-Town Crime: Belgrade is a small town, so petty theft can occur. Always keep an eye on your belongings.

Health in Belgrade

  • Avoid Tick Bites: Belgrade is in a region where tick-borne diseases are common. Make sure you spray tick repellent and check yourself after being outdoors.
