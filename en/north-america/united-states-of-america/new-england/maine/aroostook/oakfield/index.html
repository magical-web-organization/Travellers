Introduction to Oakfield

Oakfield is a charming town located in Aroostook County, Maine. This small community is surrounded by stunning natural attractions that make it the ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts seeking adventures in untouched wilderness. Oakfield is rich in history and tradition and welcomes visitors with open arms. Whether you are looking to explore the cultural attractions or explore the great outdoors, Oakfield is guaranteed to leave you feeling amazed and rejuvenated.

List of Places to Visit in Oakfield

Oakfield is a small town, but it has a variety of attractions to offer. Here are the must-see places in Oakfield:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Oakfield Historical Society Museum: This museum showcases the town’s history, from its earliest days to the present.

Natural Attractions of Oakfield: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Deer Farm Camps and Campground: This is the perfect spot to set up camp and experience the beauty of Oakfield’s wilderness.
  • Oakfield Hills Trails: Enjoy some of the best scenic views Oakfield has to offer by hiking the Oakfield Hills Trails.
  • Oakfield Wind Farm Tour: Take a guided tour of the Oakfield Wind Farm, the largest wind project in New England.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Oakfield

The hiking and biking trails around Oakfield are some of the best in Maine. Here are some of the top routes:

  • Aroostook State Park: This park features several hiking trails with varying levels of difficulty, as well as a scenic lake.
  • Presque Isle Stream Trail: This beautiful trail follows the stream as it winds through the town of Presque Isle.

Best Things to Do in Oakfield: Activities and Tours

Oakfield is brimming with things to do, no matter your interests. Here are some of the best activities and tours:

  • Fishing: Oakfield is home to many great fishing spots, so be sure to cast a line!
  • Snowmobiling: With over 300 miles of trails, Oakfield is the perfect spot to take a snowmobile ride.
  • ATVing: Explore Oakfield’s wilderness on an ATV tour.

Week-Long Itinerary for Oakfield

Day 1: Arrive in Oakfield and settle into your accommodations.

Day 2: Start your day off with a hike on the Oakfield Hills Trails. In the afternoon, take a guided tour of the Oakfield Wind Farm.

Day 3: Spend the day fishing in one of Oakfield’s many great fishing spots.

Day 4: Explore the town’s history at the Oakfield Historical Society Museum. In the afternoon, visit the Aroostook State Park for a hike.

Day 5: Take an ATV tour of Oakfield’s wilderness.

Day 6: Spend the day snowmobiling on the town’s many trails.

Day 7: Depart Oakfield.

Food and Drink Scene of Oakfield

Oakfield is known for its hearty dishes that will keep you warm on chilly days. Here are the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Ployes: A traditional Acadian dish made with buckwheat flour.
  • Baked Beans: A classic Maine dish that is hearty and delicious.

Local Beverages

  • Maine Beer Company: This brewery is known for its award-winning craft beers.
  • Central Maine Distillery: Try some of the best spirits Maine has to offer.

Where to Stay in Oakfield: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Oakfield has many options for accommodations to suit any budget. Here are some popular options:

  • Deer Farm Camps and Campground: This is the perfect spot for those looking to enjoy the outdoors while camping.
  • Aroostook Hospitality Inn: This hotel is great for those looking for a comfortable stay in town.

What to Buy in Oakfield: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Wood Crafts: There are many local artisans in Oakfield who create beautiful wood crafts and furniture.

Local Culture and Customs of Oakfield

Oakfield is a town that is proud of its history and traditions. Here are some local customs and culture to be aware of:

  • Maine Potato Blossom Festival: This festival celebrates the state’s potato industry.
  • Acadian Traditions: Oakfield has a strong Acadian heritage, which is reflected in its food and culture.

Best Time to Visit Oakfield

The best time to visit Oakfield is during the summer months when the weather is warm and the days are long. However, if you enjoy winter sports, the town is also a fantastic winter destination.

Airports Near Oakfield

Bus Stations Near Oakfield
