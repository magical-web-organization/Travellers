Introduction to Houlton

Located in Aroostook County, in the northern part of Maine, Houlton is a small town with plenty of charm and natural beauty. Surrounded by lush forests, rolling hills, and fertile farmland, Houlton is the perfect destination for nature lovers and those seeking peace and tranquility. Visitors to Houlton can enjoy a wide range of activities and attractions, including museums and historical sites, outdoor adventures, scenic views, hiking and biking, exceptional food and drink, and much more.

Local Culture and Customs of Houlton

Houlton is known for its friendly and welcoming locals, who are always ready to help visitors. Houlton’s culture is heavily influenced by its rural roots, and traditional values such as hard work, family, and community spirit are still an integral part of the town’s identity.

Best Time to Visit Houlton

The best time to visit Houlton is during summer, from June to August. It is also the peak tourist season, so expect higher rates and crowds.

Safety and Health

Safety in Houlton

  • Be cautious of bears and moose in the forests.
  • Avoid hiking alone in remote areas.
  • Follow all safety guidelines when participating in outdoor activities.

Health in Houlton

  • Bring bug spray and sunscreen for outdoor activities.
  • Make sure your vaccinations are up-to-date.
