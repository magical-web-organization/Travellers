Introduction to Shawnee on Delaware

Shawnee on Delaware is a scenic village nestled in the heart of the Pocono Mountains of Northeastern Pennsylvania. It is a popular destination for visitors seeking outdoor adventures and breathtaking natural landscapes.

The village offers several cultural attractions such as museums and historical places, making it a great destination for history buffs. Shawnee on Delaware also has a vibrant food and drink scene and options for accommodation to suit different budgets. Let’s explore the various places to visit and things to do in Shawnee on Delaware.

List of Places to Visit in Shawnee on Delaware

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Worthington State Forest - A top attraction in Shawnee on Delaware, Worthington State Forest is a historical park with hiking trails and natural landmarks.

  • Delaware Water Gap National Recreation Area - It is an excellent location to learn about the area’s natural history and see the beautiful landscapes. Visitors can take guided tours or enjoy the park at their leisure.

  • Pocono Indian Museum - The museum showcases the history and culture of the Delaware Indians who once inhabited the region.

Natural Attractions of Shawnee on Delaware: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Shawnee Mountain Ski Area - The ski area provides visitors with excellent skiing opportunities during the winter season.

  • Shawnee Inn Golf Course - It is an 18-hole golf course for golf enthusiasts to practice their skills while enjoying the scenic views of the Pocono Mountains.

  • Mount Tammany - This mountain peak offers hikers spectacular scenic views of the Delaware Water Gap.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Shawnee on Delaware

  • Appalachian Trail - A well-known hiking trail that spans over 2000 miles connecting Maine to Georgia.

  • Mount Mohican and Buttermilk Falls Trail - It is a 4-mile trail that features Mohican Indian history and a waterfall.

  • Coppermine Trail - A popular trail that offers hikers an opportunity to hike along the picturesque Coppermine Stream.

Best Things to Do in Shawnee on Delaware: Activities and Tours

  • Pocono Whitewater Rafting - Guests can take a rafting trip down the Lehigh River or indulge in other water sports such as kayaking.

  • Kittatinny Zip Lines - Visitors can experience thrill and adrenaline by zip-lining through the beautiful Pocono Mountains.

  • Edge of the Woods Outfitters - The outfitter offers guided hiking, biking, and kayaking tours for visitors to explore the wilderness of Northeastern Pennsylvania.

Food and Drink Scene of Shawnee on Delaware

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chicken and Waffles - A local favorite that combines fried chicken with waffles.

  • Pierogies - A traditional Polish dish consisting of boiled dumplings filled with mashed potatoes, onions, and cheese.

  • Scrapple - A breakfast food made of pork scraps and cornmeal.

Local Beverages

  • Pocono Brewing Company - A local microbrewery that offers a wide range of beer options for visitors.

  • Stroudsburg Winery - Visitors can sample and buy locally produced wines.

Where to Stay in Shawnee on Delaware: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Local Culture and Customs of Shawnee on Delaware

Shawnee on Delaware has a rich history and was once the home of the Lenape tribe, indigenous peoples of the region. Visitors should respect local customs and traditions when visiting the village.

Best Time to Visit Shawnee on Delaware
