Introduction

Located in the Pocono Mountains in Northeastern Pennsylvania, Tannersville is a charming town that offers visitors a perfect blend of fascinating history, breathtaking outdoor adventures and scenic views. The town’s cultural attractions, such as museums and historical places, are complemented by its natural resources, including hiking trails, skiing slopes, and waterfalls.

Visitors can also indulge in various outdoor activities such as skiing, snowboarding, hiking, camping, fishing, and river rafting and can witness the spectacular views of the fall foliage. This Tannersville travel guide will provide you with all the information you need to make the most out of your visit to this beautiful town.

List of Places to Visit in Tannersville

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

1. Quiet Valley Living Historical Farm

Quiet Valley Living Historical Farm is a living museum that showcases how life was on a Pennsylvania German farm in the 18th and 19th centuries. The museum offers interactive exhibits and regular events to give visitors a hands-on experience of the 19th-century rural farm life.

2. The Crossings Premium Outlets

The Crossings Premium Outlets is an outdoor shopping center that houses many designer and brand-name stores. Visitors can enjoy shopping at the outlet’s 100 stores, including Tommy Hilfiger, Calvin Klein, American Eagle, and Nike.

3. Camelback Mountain Resort

Camelback Mountain Resort is one of the most popular ski resorts in Pennsylvania. It offers year-round attractions such as skiing, snowboarding, tubing, zip-lining, and mountain coaster rides.

4. Camelbeach Mountain Waterpark

Camelbeach Mountain Waterpark is located adjacent to the Camelback Mountain Resort. The waterpark is spread over 35 acres and features more than 40 slides and attractions.

Natural Attractions of Tannersville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

1. Delaware Water Gap National Recreation Area

Delaware Water Gap National Recreation Area is 67,000-acres of protected land along the Delaware River. It offers visitors numerous recreational activities such as hiking, fishing, paddling, swimming and camping along with scenic views of the waterfalls and natural landscapes.

2. Big Pocono State Park

Big Pocono State Park offers visitors panoramic views from atop Camelback Mountain. The park also offers hiking trails along with picnic and camping facilities.

3. Brodhead Creek Heritage Center and Library

Brodhead Creek Heritage Center and Library were established to preserve the natural environment of Pocono Mountains and the Brodhead Creek watershed. The center provides educational programs and events with a focus on conservation and community stewardship.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tannersville

Tannersville offers a range of hiking and biking routes to suit every ability. Here are some of the best:

  • Indian Mountain Lakes Preserve
  • Pocono Environmental Education Center
  • McMichael Creek Trail
  • Cherry Creek Trail
  • BML Rail Trail

Best Things to Do in Tannersville: Activities and Tours

1. Snow tubing

Snow tubing is one of the most popular activities in Tannersville. Visitors can enjoy snow tubing at Camelback Mountain Resort which has 42 tubing lanes over 16 different ski slopes and is open year-round.

2. Pocono Raceway

Pocono Raceway is a popular attraction for car racing enthusiasts. The venue hosts the renowned NASCAR races and provides guided tours to visitors of the racetrack.

3. Skytop Lodge

Skytop Lodge is a family-oriented resort located atop a hill in the Pocono Mountains. The resort offers indoor and outdoor activities such as hiking, skiing, swimming, golfing, and boating.

Food and Drink Scene of Tannersville

Must-Try Dishes

1. Pierogies

Pierogies are a traditional Polish dish made up of potato-filled dumplings. Visitors can try some of the best pierogies in the area at Johnnie’s Restaurant.

2. Cheese steak sandwich

A cheese steak sandwich is a must-try at Village Farmer and Bakery. The sandwich consists of thinly sliced steak, melted cheese served on a hoagie roll.

Local Beverages

1. Pocono Brewery Company

The Pocono Brewery Company is known for its wide variety of beers. Besides beer, visitors can enjoy food, mini-golf, and outdoor activities at the brewery.

2. Mountain View Vineyard Winery & Brewery

Mountain View Vineyard is a local favorite winery that features wine and beer tastings, tours, and outdoor events for visitors.

Where to Stay in Tannersville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

1. Chateau Resort and Conference Center

Chateau Resort and Conference Center offers pet-friendly accommodations and features amenities including indoor and outdoor swimming pools, hot tubs, spa, and fitness centers.

2. Camelback Lodge and Aquatopia Indoor Waterpark

Camelback Lodge and Aquatopia Indoor Waterpark is an ideal place to stay for families, offering indoor and outdoor pools, a waterpark, kid’s activities and guest rooms to fit any budget.

What to Buy in Tannersville: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

1. Handmade candles and soaps

The Mountain Home Candle Company sells handmade candles and soaps using natural ingredients. The store also features unique home décor accessories.

2. The Cottage Craftsman

The Cottage Craftsman is known for its fine arts and crafts like handmade pottery, wrought-iron sculptures, and furniture designed by local artists.

Local Culture and Customs of Tannersville

Tannersville is a melting pot of different cultures, with a rich history of the Pennsylvania German, Dutch, and Native American influence. The town hosts numerous festivals and events, including the Fall festival, Harvest Fest, and Winter Fest.

Best Time to Visit Tannersville

Tannersville is a year-round destination. However, the best time to visit Tannersville is during the autumn season when visitors can witness the picturesque fall foliage over the Pocono Mountains.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tannersville

Do:

  • Check the weather and pack accordingly for outdoor activities.
  • Respect the environment and follow the local rules and regulations.
  • Visit the local museums, historical places and explore the cultural heritage of Tannersville.

Don’t:

  • Litter or disturb the natural habitat of Pocono Mountains.
  • Ignore the safety guidelines for outdoor activities.
  • Disrespect the local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tannersville

Tannersville is a relatively safe town. However, visitors are advised to be cautious while hiking or camping in the area and follow the safety guidelines for each activity. It is also advisable to park in well-lit areas and lock the vehicle before leaving it.

Health in Tannersville

Tannersville has numerous medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics, in case of emergencies. Additionally, visitors are advised to carry insect repellents during summer and fall seasons to prevent tick bites.

Tannersville is a pedestrian-friendly town with various trails and footpaths. However, visitors can also opt for public transportation, rental cars, or taxi/ride-sharing services.

Conclusion

It is a perfect destination for explorers and adventure enthusiasts, offering outdoor activities, shopping centers, and cultural excursions.
