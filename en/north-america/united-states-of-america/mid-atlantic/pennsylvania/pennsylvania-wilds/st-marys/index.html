Discovering the Charm of St. Marys, Pennsylvania

Welcome to St. Marys, a pristine and charming town located in Pennsylvania Wilds. Surrounded by lush green forests, breathtaking landscapes, and stunning waterways, St. Marys is a perfect haven for outdoor enthusiasts, nature lovers, and culture vultures alike. With its fascinating history, vibrant culture, and exceptional outdoor adventures, St. Marys has something for everyone. Let’s dive into this ultimate guide and explore the best of St. Marys.

List of Places to Visit in St. Marys

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

St. Marys is blessed with an exceptional cultural heritage, enriched by its vast history and diverse communities. Here are some of the must-visit museums and historical places in St. Marys:

  • Elk County Historical Society - Located in the heart of St. Marys, the Elk County Historical Society showcases the rich history and cultural heritage of the region. The museum houses a vast collection of artifacts, exhibits, and archives, spanning from the pre-colonial period to the present.

  • Bavarian Fall Festival - Held annually on the first weekend of October, the Bavarian Fall Festival is a celebration of St. Marys’ German heritage. The festival features live music, traditional Bavarian food and drink, crafts, and much more.

  • Decker’s Chapel - The Decker’s Chapel is a historical landmark and the oldest church in the area, built-in 1852 by German Lutheran settlers. The chapel is still active and holds regular services.

Natural Attractions of St. Marys: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The wilderness of Pennsylvania Wilds surrounds St. Marys, and you have plenty of options to explore and connect with nature. Here are some of the best natural attractions in St. Marys:

  • Elk State Forest - Elk State Forest is a haven for hiking, camping, fishing, hunting, cross-country skiing, and mountain biking enthusiasts. Spread around 200,000 acres, the forest offers some of the most spectacular views in the region.

  • East Branch Clarion River Lake - The East Branch Clarion River Lake is an excellent spot for fishing, boating, swimming, picnicking, and camping. The lake is surrounded by thick forests and has stunning scenic views.

  • Quehanna Wild Area - The Quehanna Wild Area is a massive wilderness area, spanning around 50,000 acres, and is home to several endangered species. The area is known for its spectacular views, wildlife, and unique ecosystem.

Hiking/Biking Routes of St. Marys

St. Marys is a paradise for hiking and biking enthusiasts, with ample trails and paths to explore. Some of the popular hiking and biking routes in St. Marys are:

  • Little Drummer Historical Pathway - The Little Drummer Historical Pathway is a 1.2 miles trail, passing through the beautiful countryside of St. Marys. The trail is marked with historical markers, highlighting the town’s rich history and cultural heritage.

  • Quehanna Trail - The Quehanna Trail is a 75-mile hiking trail, passing through some of the most beautiful and rugged terrains of Pennsylvania Wilds. The trail is divided into sections, and you can choose to hike any of the sections as per your preference.

  • The Ghost Town Trail - The Ghost Town Trail is a 46-mile biking trail, passing through some of the historic ghost towns of Pennsylvania Wilds. The trail offers scenic views, unique experiences, and an exciting biking adventure.

Best Things to Do in St. Marys: Activities and Tours

St. Marys has a wide range of activities and tours to offer, catering to different preferences and interests. Here are some of the best things to do in St. Marys:

  • The Elk Country Visitor Center - The Elk Country Visitor Center is an award-winning nature center, offering an insight into the region’s elk population and ecology. The center has several interactive exhibits, multimedia presentations, and outdoor viewing areas.

  • Pennsylvania Wilds Artisan Trail - The Pennsylvania Wilds Artisan Trail is a self-guided tour that takes you through the studios and workshops of some of the most talented artisans and craftsmen in the area. You can shop, observe, and learn from the artisans at their workplaces.

  • St. Marys Brewery Tour - The St. Marys Brewery Tour takes you through the historic Straub Brewery. You can learn about the brewing process, sample some of the finest beers, and take a walk through the historic facility.

Food and Drink Scene of St. Marys

St. Marys has a vibrant and diverse food and drink scene, offering a range of local and international cuisine. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chislic - Chislic is a local delicacy, consisting of small pieces of meat, typically lamb, marinated in garlic and herbs, and grilled to perfection.

  • Pierogies - Pierogies are a local specialty, resembling dumplings, and stuffed with mashed potatoes, cheese, or meat.

  • Peaches and Cream - Peaches and cream are a classic Pennsylvania Wilds dessert, made with freshly picked peaches, whipped cream, and sugar.

Local Beverages

  • Straub Beer - Straub Beer is a classic Pennsylvania Wilds beer, brewed in St. Marys since 1872. The brewery offers an array of traditional and modern beers, including American lagers, IPAs, and stouts.

  • Elk County Wines - Elk County Wines is a local winery, producing some of the finest wines in the region. The winery offers tastings, tours, and private events.

Where to Stay in St. Marys: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

St. Marys has several options for accommodations, ranging from luxurious hotels to budget guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay in St. Marys:

  • The Queen Hotel - The Queen Hotel is a historic and elegant hotel, located in the heart of St. Marys. The hotel offers spacious rooms, upscale amenities, and an exceptional dining experience.

  • Comfort Inn St. Marys - The Comfort Inn St. Marys is a budget-friendly hotel, offering excellent value for money. The hotel has comfortable rooms, free breakfast, and an indoor pool.

  • The Little Inn - The Little Inn is a charming guesthouse, offering a cozy and homely atmosphere. The inn has comfortable rooms, home-cooked breakfast, and an excellent location.

What to Buy in St. Marys: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

St. Marys has several options for souvenir shopping and local product buying. Here are some of the best things to buy in St. Marys:

  • Elk Antler Products - Elk Antler products are a classic souvenir from St. Marys, available in several stores and shops in the area. You can buy antler knives, bottle openers, jewelry, and other souvenirs made from elk antlers.

  • The Elk Company Store - The Elk Company Store is a specialty shop, offering an array of elk-inspired products, including candles, lotions, and clothing.

  • Straub Brewery Souvenirs - Straub Brewery Souvenirs are popular among beer enthusiasts, and you can buy t-shirts, coasters, glasses, and other memorabilia from the Straub Brewery Gift Shop.

Local Culture and Customs of St. Marys

St. Marys is a friendly and welcoming town, with warmth and hospitality at its core. Here are some of the local customs and etiquettes to keep in mind while visiting St. Marys:

  • Respect Nature - St. Marys’ natural beauty is its biggest asset, and it’s essential to respect and protect it. Follow the trails, avoid littering, and leave no trace.

  • Observe Quiet Hours - St. Marys is a peaceful town, and it’s customary to observe quiet hours between 10 pm, and 7 am.

  • Treat Everyone with Respect - St. Marys is a diverse town, with people from different backgrounds and cultures. It’s customary to treat everyone with respect and tolerance.

Best Time to Visit St. Marys

St. Marys is a year-round destination, offering different attractions and activities in every season. However, the best time to visit St. Marys is between May and October, when the weather is mild, and the natural beauty is at its peak.

Do’s and Don’ts of St. Marys

Do:

  • Do respect the local customs and etiquettes.
  • Do carry bug spray and sunscreen while hiking or biking.
  • Do plan ahead and make reservations for accommodations, tours, and activities.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or harm the natural wildlife.
  • Don’t enter private properties without permission.
  • Don’t ignore the weather advisories before heading out for outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in St. Marys

St. Marys is a safe town, with a low crime rate and friendly locals. However, it’s always advisable to take necessary precautions and keep yourself safe.

Health in St. Marys

St. Marys has several medical facilities and emergency services, and you need not worry about health issues. However, it’s advisable to carry your health insurance and necessary medications.

Getting to St. Marys: Transportation Options and Logistics

St. Marys is located around 200 miles from Pittsburgh and can be accessed through different transportation options.

Airports Near St. Marys

  • Pittsburgh International Airport - Pittsburgh International Airport is the nearest airport, located approximately 139 miles from St. Marys.

  • University Park Airport - University Park Airport is another nearby airport, located approximately 83 miles from St. Marys.

Bus Stations Near St. Marys

  • Greyhound Bus Station - The Greyhound Bus Station is located in DuBois, around 28 miles from St. Marys.

Other Options

  • Private Car and Taxi Services - You can hire private car and taxi services from Pittsburgh, State College, or DuBois to reach St. Marys.

  • Car Rentals - Car rentals are available at all major airports and cities, and you can drive to St. Marys.

How to Get Around in St. Marys

St. Marys is a small town, and you can explore most of the attractions on foot or by bike. However, if you need a car, you can rent one from the nearby cities.

Where to Go Next from St. Marys: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

St. Marys is surrounded by several charming towns, national parks, and natural wonders. Here are some of the nearby destinations and excursions from St. Marys:

  • Punxsutawney - Punxsutawney is famous for its Groundhog Day celebrations and has several historical landmarks.

  • Parker Dam State Park - Parker Dam State Park is a beautiful park with fishing spots, swimming areas, and hiking trails.

  • Kinztua Bridge State Park - Kinztua Bridge State Park is a stunning park with excellent views of the Kinztua Bridge and several hiking trails.

Conclusion

St. Marys is a hidden gem in Pennsylvania Wilds, offering a unique blend of cultural heritage, natural beauty, and outdoor adventures. From hiking and biking to fishing and camping, St. Marys has something for everyone. With this comprehensive guide, you can explore the best of St. Marys and experience its charm, warmth, and hospitality.
