List of Places to Visit in Dubois

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Dubois has a rich history and offers a host of museums and historical places that tourists can visit. These places offer an insight into the history and culture of the region.

  • Erie – Lackawanna Dining Car Preservation Society (ELDCPS): The ELDCPS is a fantastic place to learn about the rich history of the legendary Pullman dining cars. The museum features several authentic pieces, including several dining cars, observation cars, and sleeper cars.

  • Pennsylvania State University North Central Agricultural Research Laboratory: The research lab is home to several exhibits that pay tribute to the agricultural industry in the region. It is a perfect place to learn about the history of farming practices in Pennsylvania.

  • Jefferson County History Center: The Jefferson County History Center is a great place to learn about the history of Jefferson County in Pennsylvania. The museum features exhibits, artifacts, and photographs that offer an insight into the daily lives of the region’s residents throughout the centuries.

Natural Attractions of Dubois: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Dubois is a beautiful city that boasts of some of the best outdoor spaces in Pennsylvania. From hiking trails to waterfalls, Dubois is an ideal destination for nature lovers.

  • Bilger’s Rocks: Bilger’s Rocks is a natural formation of rocks that tells a compelling story of the geological past of Dubois. The rocks offer an excellent location for hiking, camping, and picnicking.

  • Gobbler’s Knob: Gobbler’s Knob is a famous attraction in Dubois, known for the annual Groundhog Day celebrations. The area offers an excellent location for hiking, camping, and picnicking.

  • Clear Creek State Park: The Clear Creek State Park is a recreational area in Dubois that offers an array of outdoor activities like kayaking, canoeing, fishing, and hiking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dubois

Dubois is known for its fantastic hiking and biking trails that offer an outdoor adventure to visitors. Below are the top hiking and biking routes in Dubois:

  • Redbank Valley Trail: The trail is located in the Clarion County area and offers a paved path for walking, biking, and hiking. Visitors can enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding areas.

  • Black Moshannon State Park Trails: The Black Moshannon State Park Trails offer a rewarding hiking and biking experience. The trails are well-marked and vary in difficulty, ranging from easy to challenging.

  • State Game Lands 44 Trails: The State Game Lands 44 Trails offer beautiful views of Dubois and the surrounding areas. The trails are well-marked and offer an excellent hiking and biking experience for visitors.

Food and Drink Scene of Dubois

Dubois has a diverse food and drink scene that caters to different tastes and preferences. Below are some must-try dishes and local beverages in Dubois:

Must-Try Dishes

  • The Philly Cheese Steak: Dubois is home to several restaurants that serve the Philly cheese steak – a sandwich made with thinly sliced beef steak and melted cheese in a long hoagie roll.

  • The Dubois Diner Burger: The Dubois Diner Burger is a classic burger made with a juicy patty, American cheese, onions, and pickles.

  • Pierogi: Pierogi is a traditional dish in Dubois, made with boiled dough and filled with mashed potatoes, cheese, and meat.

Local Beverages
