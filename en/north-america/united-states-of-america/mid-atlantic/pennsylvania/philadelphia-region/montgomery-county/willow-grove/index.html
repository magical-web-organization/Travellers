Introduction to Willow Grove

Willow Grove is a charming borough located in Montgomery County, in the Philadelphia region, Pennsylvania. The town is known for its lovely parks, recreational activities, historical attractions, and vibrant food scene.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take a closer look at the top places to visit, things to do, and where to stay in Willow Grove. We’ll also explore the local culture and customs, the best time to visit, transportation options, and safety tips.

So let’s get started on your Willow Grove adventure!

List of Places to Visit in Willow Grove

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Willow Grove has a rich history, and there is no better place to start than exploring the cultural and historical sites of the borough.

Abington Art Center

Abington Art Center is a local cultural institution that aims to inspire and engage artists, learners, and audiences. The center has rotating exhibitions, classes, workshops, and events that showcase contemporary arts and culture.

Grove Park

Grove park is considered an oasis in the hustle and bustle of Willow Grove. The park features a pool, picnic areas, and even a music venue!

Willow Grove Park Mall

The Willow Grove Park Mall is a large indoor shopping mall that has over 120 stores, including Macy’s and Bloomingdale’s. The mall also features a foot court and a movie theater.

Natural Attractions of Willow Grove: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Willow Grove has some stunning natural attractions that are worth exploring.

Abington Memorial Hospital Butterfly Garden

If you’re a nature lover, then Abington Memorial Hospital Butterfly Garden is a must-visit destination. This hidden gem is a tranquil oasis with different species of butterflies, birds, and flowers.

Pennypack Ecological Restoration Trust

The Pennypack Ecological Restoration Trust is a beautiful 771-acre preserve located near Willow Grove. The Trust offers nature trails alongside the Pennypack Creek that offer scenic views and opportunities for bird-watching and fishing.

Masons Mill Park

Masons Mill Park is the perfect place to escape the city’s hustle and bustle and immerse yourself in nature. The park features walking trails, sports fields, and picnic areas all surrounded by gorgeous natural scenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Willow Grove

Willow Grove offers several hiking and biking paths that offer stunning natural views.

Abington Township Trail

The Abington Township Trail is a five-mile bike trail that takes riders through forests and fields, offering beautiful views of nature. The trail is available for hiking, dog-walking, running and biking.

Masons Mill Park Trail

Masons Mill Park Trail is a 1.5-mile loop that circles around Masons Mill Pond. It’s perfect for a leisurely stroll and to watch the sunset or sunrise

Best Things to Do in Willow Grove: Activities and Tours

Salsa in the Suburbs Dance Studio

If you’re into dancing, head to Salsa in the Suburbs Dance Studio. They offer classes for all skill levels, including salsa, Zumba, and hip hop.

Next Stop Brew Co.

Next Stop Brew Co. is a local brewery that offers a rotating selection of delicious beers and ales. Stop by and try their signature brews, such as the Next Stop Pale Ale.

Week-Long Itinerary for Willow Grove

Day 1 - Explore Willow Grove Park Mall and grab lunch at Capital Grille

Day 2 - Take a relaxing stroll at the Abington memorial hospital butterfly garden

Day 3 - Visit Grove Park and enjoy its music and historical appeal

Day 4 - Take a walk through the Abington Township Trail

Day 5 - Be a part of a dance class at Salsa in the Suburbs Dance Studio

Day 6 - Taste the local brews at Next Stop Brew Co.

Day 7 - Go for a hike on the Masons Mill Park Trail

Food and Drink Scene of Willow Grove

Must-Try Dishes

Cheese Steak

When in Pennsylvania, one can’t miss a cheese steak. Head to Steve’s Prince of Steaks. Try their famous Philly Cheesesteak- grilled beef, onions, and cheese on a fresh roll.

Shoo-Fly Pie

To satisfy your sweet tooth try the Shoo-fly pie. You can get the best ones at the Pie and Plate Cafe.

Local Beverages

Roy Pitz Barrel House

If you’re in Willow Grove, then you must try the local brews. Roy Pitz Barrel House is a local brewery that offers its own unique craft beer selection.

What to Buy in Willow Grove: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Auntie Anne’s Pretzels

Head to the Willow Grove Park Mall and grab a hot and soft pretzel from Auntie Anne’s. It’s a local favorite snack and makes for an excellent souvenir to take back home.

Fresh Produce

Visit one of the weekly farmers’ markets in Willow Grove and get some fresh produce to take back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Willow Grove

Willow Grove is a friendly town that values hospitality. It’s customary to greet strangers with a smile and engage in small talk.

Best Time to Visit Willow Grove

The best time to visit Willow Grove is during the fall when the leaves change color, and the weather is pleasant. However, spring and summer also have their own charm, with plenty of outdoor activities and colorful blooms.

Do’s and Don’ts of Willow Grove

Do:

  • Explore the local culture, history, and attractions
  • Try the local food and drinks
  • Make new friends and engage in small talk

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local culture and customs
  • Leave your trash behind in parks and natural attractions
  • Walk alone in isolated areas at night

Safety and Health

Safety in Willow Grove

Willow Grove is a safe town, and crime rates are relatively low. However, it’s always best to be aware of your surroundings and avoid isolated areas at night.

Health in Willow Grove

Willow Grove offers excellent healthcare services, and there are several hospitals and clinics in the area. Be aware of ticks and Lyme disease when spending time outdoors.

Getting to Willow Grove: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Willow Grove

The closest airport to Willow Grove is Philadelphia International Airport, which is about a 45-minute drive away.

Bus Stations Near Willow Grove

The Greyhound Bus Station in Philadelphia is 17 miles away, but Willow Grove is just five miles from the SEPTA Regional Rail Line Hatboro Station.

Other Options

If you’re taking a road trip, you can get to Willow Grove via the Pennsylvania Turnpike I-276 or US Route 611. Additionally, the town is just a 25-minute drive from Center City Philadelphia.

How to Get Around in Willow Grove

Willow Grove is an easy town to navigate, and most attractions are within walking distance. However, if you need to get around quickly, you can rent a car or bike, or take a taxi or Uber.

Where to Go Next from Willow Grove: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Philadelphia

The City of Brotherly Love is just a short drive away from Willow Grove, and there are plenty of things to see and do. Visit the Liberty Bell, Independence Hall, and the Philadelphia Museum of Art.

Valley Forge National Historical Park

Valley Forge National Historical Park is just a 30-minute drive away from Willow Grove. This beautiful park has hiking trails, historic buildings, and museums that showcase the Revolutionary War history.

Conclusion

Willow Grove is a charming and friendly town that offers plenty of things to see and do. From exploring the local culture and history to hiking in nature, there’s something for everyone. We hope this comprehensive guide helps you plan your Willow Grove adventure.
