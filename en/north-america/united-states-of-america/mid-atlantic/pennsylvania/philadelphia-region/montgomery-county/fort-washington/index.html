Introduction to Fort Washington

Fort Washington is a historic community situated in Montgomery County, in Philadelphia Region, in Pennsylvania, in the Mid-Atlantic region, United States. This community was named after a historic fort built here during the American Revolution. Today, this charming community is known for its rich history, scenic views, and outdoor activities.

List of Places to Visit in Fort Washington

Visitors to Fort Washington have a plethora of attractions to choose from, ranging from historical landmarks to scenic hiking trails. Below is a list of the top places to visit in Fort Washington.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fort Washington State Park
  • Hope Lodge
  • Highlands Mansion and Gardens
  • Clifton House
  • Revolutionary Germantown Trail

Natural Attractions of Fort Washington: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Wissahickon Valley Park

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fort Washington

Best Things to Do in Fort Washington: Activities and Tours

Whether you enjoy history, nature, or outdoor adventures, Fort Washington offers plenty of activities and tours to keep you entertained. Here are some of the best things to do in Fort Washington:

  • Take a historical walking tour of Fort Washington State Park
  • Zip line and climb at the Go Ape Treetop Adventure Course
  • Enjoy a scenic drive along the Wissahickon Valley Park’s scenic loop drive
  • Go kayaking or canoeing along the Schuylkill River
  • Shop and dine at the Flourtown Farmers Market

Week-Long Itinerary for Fort Washington

If you’re planning a trip to Fort Washington, here’s a week-long itinerary to help you make the most of your stay:

Day Activity
1 Visit Fort Washington State Park in the morning and explore the Highlands Mansion and Gardens in the afternoon
2 Go on a historical walking tour of Clifton House, followed by an afternoon of kayaking on the Schuylkill River
3 Take a day trip to Philadelphia and visit attractions, such as Independence Hall, the Liberty Bell, and the Philadelphia Museum of Art
4 Enjoy a leisurely day at the Morris Arboretum of the University of Pennsylvania
5 Experience outdoor adventure with the Zip line and climb at the Go Ape Treetop Adventure Course
6 Take a scenic drive or bike ride on the Wissahickon Gorge Trail or the Forbidden Drive Trail
7 Visit the Flourtown Farmers Market for some local shopping, followed by lunch at the nearby restaurants

Local Culture and Customs of Fort Washington

Fort Washington has rich historical and cultural significance, which is reflected in its antique architecture and museums. The people of Fort Washington are warm and friendly, and visitors are typically greeted with open arms.

Best Time to Visit Fort Washington

The best time to visit Fort Washington is during the Summer and Fall months, especially from June through August when the weather is at its best. However, if you plan to go hiking, Spring months can be ideal.

Do’s and Don’ts of Fort Washington

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Bring comfortable walking shoes for hiking trails
  • Take a historical walking tour of Fort Washington State Park
  • Shop and dine at the Flourtown Farmers Market

Don’t:

  • Litter
  • Take any objects from historical sites
  • Disturb wildlife in their natural habitat
  • Ignore signs while hiking

Safety and Health

Safety in Fort Washington

Fort Washington is generally a safe community. However, visitors should always take precautions to safeguard themselves as pickpocketing and theft can occur, particularly in crowded areas.

Health in Fort Washington

Fort Washington has excellent health facilities, including Fort Washington Hospital. Visitors should carry any necessary prescriptions and basic first aid kits.
