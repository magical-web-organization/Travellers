Introduction to Bensalem

Located in Bucks County, Philadelphia Region of Pennsylvania, Bensalem is a historic town that dates back to the 17th century. The town has a rich heritage, vibrant culture, and natural beauty making it an ideal destination for history buffs, nature lovers, and adventure seekers.

In this travel guide, we’ll highlight the best things to do, see, eat, and experience in Bensalem, Pennsylvania. From culturally important attractions, natural wonders, and outdoor adventures to food and drinks and tips for safe travel, we’ve got you covered.

List of Places to Visit in Bensalem

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Bensalem is known for its rich history, and visitors to the town can explore the many museums and historical places that showcase the town’s heritage.

Bensalem Historical Society

The Bensalem Historical Society’s museum showcases the town’s history, architecture, and people. The two-story museum is home to an extensive collection of artifacts and photographs that date back to the 1600s.

Pen Ryn Estate

The Pen Ryn Estate is a historic mansion that dates back to the 18th century. The estate is open to visitors who can take a guided tour of the mansion and enjoy the beautiful gardens and grounds.

Andalusia

Andalusia is a historic mansion that dates back to the 19th century. The mansion is located on a 225-acre estate that features stunning gardens and an outdoor amphitheater.

Natural Attractions of Bensalem: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Bensalem is home to several natural attractions that are perfect for outdoor adventures and scenic views.

Neshaminy State Park

Neshaminy State Park is a beautiful park that features a 375-acre lake, hiking trails, picnic areas, and playgrounds. Visitors can enjoy fishing, boating, and swimming in the lake.

Pennypack Preserve

The Pennypack Preserve is a 1,600-acre nature preserve that features over 10 miles of hiking trails, wetlands, and a creek. Visitors can enjoy bird watching, hiking, and picnicking in the preserve.

Core Creek Park

Core Creek Park is a 1,200-acre park that features a lake, hiking trails, playgrounds, and picnic areas. Visitors can enjoy fishing, boating, and hiking in the park.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bensalem

Bensalem is a great destination for hikers and bikers, with several trails to explore.

Neshaminy State Park Trail

The Neshaminy State Park Trail is a 4.5-mile trail that runs along the lake and through the woods. The trail is perfect for hiking and biking and offers scenic views of the lake.

Pennypack Preserve Trail

The Pennypack Preserve Trail is a 5-mile trail that runs along the Pennypack Creek and through the woods. The trail is perfect for hiking and biking and offers scenic views of the creek.

Core Creek Park Trail

The Core Creek Park Trail is a 4-mile trail that runs along the lake and through the woods. The trail is perfect for hiking and biking and offers scenic views of the lake.

Best Things to Do in Bensalem: Activities and Tours

Bensalem has something to offer everyone, from thrill-seekers to history buffs and foodies.

Parx Casino

Parx Casino is a popular attraction in Bensalem, with over 3,500 slot machines, table games, and a poker room. The casino also features several restaurants and bars.

Ghost Tours of New Hope

Ghost Tours of New Hope is a nighttime walking tour that explores the town’s haunted history. The tour is led by a local historian and storyteller and is perfect for history buffs and thrill-seekers.

Culinary Adventures

Bensalem is home to several great restaurants and food establishments that offer unique culinary experiences. Some of the local favorites include the Philly cheese steak sandwich, Italian hoagie, and tomato pie.

Food and Drink Scene of Bensalem

Must-Try Dishes

Bensalem is known for its unique food culture, and visitors should try some of the local delicacies.

Philly cheese steak sandwich

The Philly cheese steak sandwich is a local favorite, made with thin-sliced steak and melted cheese on a hoagie roll.

Italian hoagie

The Italian hoagie is a sandwich made with ham, salami, pepperoni, and provolone cheese on a hoagie roll.

Tomato pie

Tomato pie is a savory pie made with tomatoes, garlic, and herbs on a thick dough crust.

Local Beverages

Bensalem is known for its craft beers and wines.

Neshaminy Creek Brewing Co.

Neshaminy Creek Brewing Co. offers a variety of craft beers, including IPAs, stouts, and lagers.

Sand Castle Winery

Sand Castle Winery produces a variety of wines, including Chardonnay, Cabernet Sauvignon, and Pinot Noir.
