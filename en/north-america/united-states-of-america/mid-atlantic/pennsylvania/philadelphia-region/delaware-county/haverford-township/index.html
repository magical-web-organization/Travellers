Introduction to Haverford Township

Haverford Township is a small township located in Delaware County, in the Philadelphia region of Pennsylvania. The township is home to historical places, stunning natural landmarks, and a unique food and drink scene. It is a perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts, history buffs, and food lovers.

Haverford Township offers a plethora of fascinating attractions that will keep visitors occupied for days. Here are some of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Haverford Heritage Trail
  • Nitre Hall
  • Lawrence Cabin
  • Federal School
  • Grange Estate
  • Haverford Township Historical Society
  • Thomas Garrett House

Natural Attractions of Haverford Township: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views
