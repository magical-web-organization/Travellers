Introduction to Latrobe

Latrobe is a picturesque town located in the Laurel Highlands of Pennsylvania, in the Pittsburgh region. It is known for its beautiful countryside, cultural attractions, outdoor adventures, and historic sites.

List of Places to Visit in Latrobe

Latrobe has many places to visit and things to see. Here’s our list of top attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The Fred Rogers Center

The Fred Rogers Center is a museum that celebrates the life and legacy of the beloved children’s television host, Fred Rogers. The museum invites the public to explore his work, philosophy, and his impact on children’s education. Visitors can learn about the history of his show, see original artifacts, and participate in hands-on exhibits.

The Latrobe Area Historical Society Museum

The Latrobe Area Historical Society Museum showcases the history of Latrobe and the surrounding area. The museum features exhibits on the town’s famous residents, local sports teams, and the city’s industrial heritage. There are also galleries of art and photography displays showcasing the history of the region.

Natural Attractions of Latrobe: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Unity Township Park

Unity Township Park is a beautiful outdoor space that covers 203 acres of land. It is a perfect spot for picnics, hiking, biking, and wildlife watching. The park has many trails, a lake, and a playground for children. There is also a dog park for pet owners to enjoy.

The Arnold Palmer Regional Airport Observation Deck

The Arnold Palmer Regional Airport Observation Deck is a great place to watch airplanes take off and land. It is an excellent spot for aviation enthusiasts. The observation deck offers a beautiful view of the surrounding area and is a great place to take photographs. Visitors can enjoy the planes taking off and landing from a safe distance.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Latrobe

Latrobe is known for its scenic beauty and is a great place for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best hiking and biking trails in the area:

The Keystone State Park Trail

The Keystone State Park Trail is a beautiful hiking and biking trail that offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside. The trail is perfect for beginners and experienced hikers alike. It offers a peaceful hike through wooded areas, past streams and even to the beach of Keystone Lake.

The Westmoreland Heritage Trail

The Westmoreland Heritage Trail is a converted railroad trail that stretches from Export, Pennsylvania, to Saltsburg, Pennsylvania. The trail is perfect for biking, walking, and jogging. It also has picnic areas and scenic overlooks with views of the surrounding woodland.

Best Things to Do in Latrobe: Activities and Tours

Latrobe offers many activities and tours for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the best things to do:

The Fort Ligonier Museum Tour

The Fort Ligonier Museum Tour is a must-do for history buffs. The museum tells the story of the French and Indian War and the battle that took place at Fort Ligonier. The tour includes a walking tour of the fort and an interactive exhibit explaining the events of the war. It is a fun and informative way to learn about the region’s history.

The Idlewild & SoakZone Amusement Park

Idlewild & SoakZone Amusement Park is a fun-filled amusement park that offers rides, games, and attractions for visitors of all ages. The park has many roller coasters, water rides, and shows. It is a perfect place to spend a day with the family.

Week-Long Itinerary for Latrobe

Here is a suggested week-long itinerary for visitors to Latrobe:

Day Activity
1 Visit the Fred Rogers Center Museum and the Latrobe Area Historical Society Museum
2 Hike the Keystone State Park Trail and explore the Unity Township Park
3 Take a guided tour of Fort Ligonier and visit the shops and cafes in downtown Latrobe
4 Spend the day at Idlewild & SoakZone Amusement Park
5 Take a scenic flight over the Laurel Highlands or visit the Arnold Palmer Regional Airport Observation Deck
6 Go on the Westmoreland Heritage Trail and see the beautiful fall color
7 Relax and unwind at the Latrobe Country Club Spa and Lounge or enjoy a wine tasting at Mazza Vineyards

Food and Drink Scene of Latrobe

Latrobe has a vibrant food and drink scene, boasting diverse cuisine and locally sourced produce. Here are some dishes and beverages you must try:

Must-Try Dishes

Pittsburgh-Style Sandwich

A Pittsburgh-style sandwich is a must-try while visiting Latrobe. This sandwich typically features two slices of Italian bread filled with meat, cheese, coleslaw, and french fries. It is a hearty meal that represents the Pittsburgh region’s unique food culture.

Latrobe Beer Cheese

Latrobe Beer Cheese is a mild cheddar cheese that is infused with beer and other spices. This cheese is a local favorite and is often used in sandwiches and dips.

Local Beverages

Rolling Rock Beer

Rolling Rock Beer is the most famous beer in Latrobe. It is a light beer that has a crisp, clean taste and is a perfect drink to have on a hot summer day. The beer is made locally and is available in most bars and restaurants in the area.

R Wine Cellar

The R Wine Cellar is a boutique winery that offers wine tasting and tours. It is located in the heart of Latrobe and is a great place to relax and unwind after a day of sightseeing.

Where to Stay in Latrobe: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Latrobe has many options for places to stay, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. Here are some options:

Budget-friendly options

Wingate by Wyndham

Wingate by Wyndham is a budget-friendly hotel that offers clean and comfortable rooms. The hotel is located in the heart of Latrobe, making it a great location for exploring the town and nearby attractions.

Valley Motel

The Valley Motel is a budget-friendly option for travelers who are looking for a simple yet comfortable room. The motel offers free parking and is located near the Westmoreland Heritage Trail.

Mid-range options

Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott

Fairfield Inn & Suites by Marriott is a mid-range hotel that offers comfortable rooms and excellent amenities. The hotel features a fitness center, swimming pool, and free Wi-Fi. It is located near the Arnold Palmer Regional Airport and is a great location for business travelers.

SpringHill Suites by Marriott

SpringHill Suites by Marriott is a mid-range hotel that offers spacious suites and a complimentary breakfast. The hotel features a fitness center and swimming pool and is a great location for families.

Luxury options

Latrobe Country Club

Latrobe Country Club is a luxurious hotel that features a spa, golf course, and private suites. The hotel is situated on a beautiful property and is a perfect location for a romantic getaway or a corporate retreat.

What to Buy in Latrobe: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Latrobe is known for its unique souvenirs and locally made products. Here are some items to consider when shopping:

Pittsburgh Steelers Merchandise

Latrobe is the birthplace of the Pittsburgh Steelers, and you can buy many sports-related souvenirs in the city. From jerseys to mugs, you can find Steelers merchandise in most stores in Latrobe.

Latrobe Beer Cheese

Latrobe Beer Cheese is a local delicacy that is a must-buy when visiting the city. You can find the cheese in most grocery stores and delis in the area.

Local Culture and Customs of Latrobe

Latrobe is a small-town America with a friendly and welcoming culture. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and norms, such as dressing modestly and avoiding loud, boisterous behavior in quiet areas.

Best Time to Visit Latrobe

The best time to visit Latrobe is from March to May or from September to November. The temperatures during this time are mild, and the crowds are typically less than during the summer.

Do’s and Don’ts of Latrobe

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine, such as Pittsburgh-style sandwiches and Latrobe Beer Cheese.
  • Take advantage of the outdoor activities, such as hiking and biking.
  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions.
  • Visit the cultural and historical attractions of the city.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural areas.
  • Smoke in public places.
  • Be loud or disruptive in quiet areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Latrobe

Latrobe is generally a safe city, but visitors should be cautious when walking alone at night. It is recommended to stay in well-lit areas and avoid carrying large sums of cash.

Health in Latrobe

Latrobe has excellent medical facilities and hospitals, which offer high-quality treatments and procedures. Visitors should bring their healthcare records and insurance cards when traveling.

Getting to Latrobe: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Latrobe

The Arnold Palmer Regional Airport is the main airport in the Latrobe area. It offers daily flights to major cities such as Chicago and Orlando.

Bus Stations Near Latrobe

The Greyhound Bus Station in Greensburg is the closest station to Latrobe. There are regular buses from this station to Pittsburgh, Philadelphia, and New York.

Other Options

Latrobe is also accessible by car, and visitors can use rental cars or taxis to get around the city.

How to Get Around in Latrobe

Latrobe is a small city that is easy to navigate. Visitors can walk or bike to many of the attractions in the area. Taxis and rental cars are also available.

Where to Go Next from Latrobe: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Latrobe is a great base to explore other destinations in the region. Here are some nearby destinations to consider:

Frank Lloyd Wright’s Fallingwater

Frank Lloyd Wright’s Fallingwater is a famous architectural masterpiece located in Mill Run, Pennsylvania. Visitors can take a guided tour of the house and the surrounding grounds.

Laurel Caverns Park

Laurel Caverns Park offers visitors the opportunity to explore miles of underground caves. It is a popular destination for adventurers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Conclusion

Latrobe is a charming and vibrant city that offers visitors the opportunity to explore the rich history and natural beauty of the region. Whether you are interested in museums, outdoor adventures, or trying the local cuisine, Latrobe has something for everyone.
