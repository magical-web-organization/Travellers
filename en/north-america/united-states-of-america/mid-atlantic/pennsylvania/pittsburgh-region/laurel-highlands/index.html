Introduction to Laurel Highlands

Laurel Highlands, located in the Pittsburgh Region of Pennsylvania, is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts and history buffs alike. This region is home to scenic countryside, sparkling rivers, rolling hills, museums, state parks, and charming small towns.

The sprawling beauty of Laurel Highlands with its colorful foliage and vibrant wildlife attracts visitors year-round. Whether you are a nature lover looking for outdoor adventures or a history buff who loves to explore museums and historical sites, Laurel Highlands promises an unforgettable experience.

Here is our comprehensive tour guide featuring the best places to visit, hiking/biking routes, things to do, food and drink scene, where to stay, and more.

  • Respect the natural environment and leave no trace while hiking or camping.

Best Time to Visit Laurel Highlands

The best time to visit Laurel Highlands is during the fall season when the foliage is at its most vibrant. However, visitors can enjoy a range of activities in this region year-round, with skiing and snowboarding being popular winter activities.
