Introduction to Homewood

Located in the North Pittsburgh Region of Pennsylvania, Homewood is a historic neighborhood that has long been an important cultural center. Its tree-lined streets, beautiful parks, and mix of urban and suburban areas make it a popular destination for travelers seeking a unique experience.

List of Places to Visit in Homewood

There are a lot of places to visit in Homewood, with something for everyone. Here are some of the must-see places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Homewood Cemetery: Founded in 1878, this historic cemetery serves as the resting place for many prominent Pittsburghers, including famous steel magnates, industrialists, and politicians.
  • August Wilson House: This National Historic Landmark is the childhood home of Pulitzer Prize-winning playwright August Wilson.
  • Carnegie Library of Pittsburgh - Homewood: This Carnegie Library has been serving the community for over 100 years and is home to a range of collections, including local history archives.
  • Black Love Mural Project: This beautiful mural project celebrates black love and pride, with stunning art by local artists.

Natural Attractions of Homewood: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Busway Trail: This beautiful multi-use trail runs along the Martin Luther King Jr. East Busway, offering excellent views of the city and the surrounding nature.
  • Frick Park: A sprawling park with over 600 acres of greenery, walking trails, and picnic spots.
  • Homewood Community Garden: This local garden is a vibrant hub of community activity and offers visitors the chance to see what local residents are growing.
  • Kuntu Repertory Theatre: This theater company offers a range of performances that reflect and celebrate the Black experience, and is a must-visit for fans of African American theater.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Homewood

Homewood offers many great hiking and biking routes, from the Busway Trail to the Frick Park trails. The Busway Trail is a particularly popular choice, offering excellent views of the city and the countryside as well as a convenient location.

Best Things to Do in Homewood: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of things to do in Homewood, from visiting local museums and galleries to exploring nature and trying out delicious local food. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Take a walking tour of the August Wilson House and learn about the playwright’s life and career.
  • Visit the Frick Park Environmental Center to see local wildlife up close and learn about the conservation efforts underway in the area.
  • Stop by the Homewood Community Garden to see what local residents are growing and learn about the importance of sustainable agriculture.
  • Check out the Black Love Mural Project and see some of the beautiful art created by local artists.
  • Try some of the delicious local food available at Homewood’s many restaurants and cafes.
