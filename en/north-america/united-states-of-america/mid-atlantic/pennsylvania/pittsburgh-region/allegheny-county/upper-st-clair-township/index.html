Introduction to Upper St. Clair Township

Upper St. Clair Township is a beautiful suburban community located in Allegheny County, in the Pittsburgh Region of Pennsylvania. Nestled in the Appalachian Mountains, this charming township boasts a rich history dating back to the late 1700s and offers visitors a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural attractions. Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, history buff, or foodie, there’s something in Upper St. Clair Township that caters to everyone’s interests.

List of Places to Visit in Upper St. Clair Township

Here are some of the top attractions that you should not miss in Upper St. Clair Township.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Pittsburgh Library - Upper St. Clair - This beautiful library building was constructed in 2019, replacing the old one which was originally built in 1963. The new library offers a wide selection of books, digital resources, and educational programs for all ages.

  • Montour Trail - This historic rail-trail in Upper St. Clair Township was once a part of the Montour Railroad, which played a significant role in the area’s coal mining industry. Today, the Montour Trail is a popular spot for walking, hiking, and biking, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Natural Attractions of Upper St. Clair Township: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Boyce Mayview Park - Covering more than 475 acres, this beautiful park offers visitors an array of recreational activities, including hiking, biking, fishing, and picnicking. The park features several nature trails that cater to hikers of all skill levels, as well as a playground, picnic pavilions, and a dog park.

  • Upper St. Clair Community & Recreation Center - This state-of-the-art facility boasts an impressive list of amenities, including fitness rooms, basketball and volleyball courts, walking and jogging tracks, and swimming pools.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Upper St. Clair Township

Upper St. Clair Township offers a wide range of hiking and biking trails that cater to all skill levels. Here are some of the popular routes that you should check out:

  • Boyce-Mayview Perimeter Trail - This 2.8-mile trail loops around the entire park, offering stunning views of the surrounding nature.

  • Montour Trail - This scenic rail-trail extends for more than 60 miles, connecting Upper St. Clair Township with other communities in the Pittsburgh area, including Bethel Park, Coraopolis, and Clairton.

Best Things to Do in Upper St. Clair Township: Activities and Tours

No matter what your interests may be, Upper St. Clair Township has something for everyone. Here are some of our top recommendations for things to do and see in the area:

  • Visit the Allegheny County Parks - Upper St. Clair Township is located in close proximity to several beautiful county parks, including South Park, Settlers Cabin Park, and North Park. These parks offer a wide range of recreational activities, including hiking, biking, fishing, and picnicking.

  • Explore the City of Pittsburgh - Located just a short drive away from Upper St. Clair Township, the city of Pittsburgh offers visitors an array of cultural attractions, including museums, art galleries, and theaters.

Local Culture and Customs of Upper St. Clair Township

Upper St. Clair Township has a rich cultural heritage, with a history that dates back to the late 1700s. Local customs and traditions are greatly influenced by the area’s coal mining and industrial history, which has been a significant driver of the local economy for many years.
