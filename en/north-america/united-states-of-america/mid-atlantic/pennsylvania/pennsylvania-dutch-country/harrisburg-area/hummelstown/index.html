Introduction to Hummelstown

Hummelstown is a small borough located in the heart of Pennsylvania Dutch Country, in the Harrisburg area. The borough is famous for its quaint charm, natural beauty, and rich cultural heritage. Hummelstown is an ideal destination for those who want to explore the outdoors, soak up history and culture, and discover the local food and drink scene.

List of Places to Visit in Hummelstown

Below are some of the best places to visit and explore in Hummelstown, Pennsylvania.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The Hershey Story

The Hershey Story Museum is a must-visit place for anyone who is interested in the history of Hershey’s Chocolate. The museum tells the inspiring story of how Milton Hershey transformed a small town into a booming chocolate empire.

Pennsylvania State Capitol Building

The Pennsylvania State Capitol Building is a stunning architectural marvel that features grand halls, magnificent staircases, and impressive murals. Visitors can take guided tours of the building and learn about the state’s history and politics.

Natural Attractions of Hummelstown: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Indian Echo Caverns

The Indian Echo Caverns are natural limestone caves that were discovered in the early 1800s. The caverns feature stunning mineral formations and underground streams. Visitors can take guided tours of the caverns and explore the natural beauty of the caves.

Hershey Gardens

Hershey Gardens is a 23-acre botanical garden that showcases a variety of themes, including the Japanese Garden, the Rose Garden, and the Butterfly House. Visitors can enjoy the beauty of different flowers, plants, and trees in the garden.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hummelstown

Hummelstown is known for its picturesque nature and scenic routes for hiking and biking. There are several trails that attract visitors from far and wide, including:

Safety and Health

Safety in Hummelstown

Hummelstown is a safe and peaceful borough, with low crime rates and friendly locals. However, visitors are advised to take common-sense precautions to ensure their safety, such as:

  • Keep valuables out of sight
  • Stay in well-lit and populated areas after dark
  • Always lock your car and hotel room

Health in Hummelstown

Hummelstown has good healthcare facilities, including hospitals and clinics. Visitors are advised to have adequate travel insurance and to carry any necessary medications with them.
