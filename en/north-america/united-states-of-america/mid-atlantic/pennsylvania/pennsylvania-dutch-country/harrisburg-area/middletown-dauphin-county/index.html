Introduction to Middletown (Dauphin County)

Middletown (Dauphin County) is a charming town located in the Harrisburg area of Pennsylvania Dutch Country. Known for its rich history, picturesque surroundings, and friendly community, Middletown is a must-visit destination for travelers exploring the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States. Whether you’re interested in exploring local museums, hiking scenic trails, or indulging in local cuisine and drinks, there’s something for everyone in Middletown.

List of Places to Visit in Middletown (Dauphin County)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Middletown & Hummelstown Railroad

The Middletown & Hummelstown Railroad is a popular attraction for train enthusiasts and history buffs alike. This historic steam locomotive offers scenic tours of the countryside, giving visitors a glimpse into the area’s transportation history.

Pennsylvania State Capitol

Located just a short drive away in Harrisburg, the Pennsylvania State Capitol is a must-visit destination for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts. This beautiful building is home to the state’s legislative branch and allows visitors to explore historic chambers, artwork, and artifacts.

Natural Attractions of Middletown (Dauphin County): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Koser Park

Koser Park is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts looking to explore the great outdoors. This gorgeous park features hiking trails, picnic areas, a playground, and a disc golf course.

Swatara State Park

Swatara State Park is another popular destination for outdoor adventures in Middletown. This beautiful park features hiking trails, fishing opportunities, and scenic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Middletown (Dauphin County)

Conewago Recreation Trail

The Conewago Recreation Trail is a popular destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Stretching for over 5 miles, this scenic trail runs along the beautiful Conewago Creek and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

Best Things to Do in Middletown (Dauphin County): Activities and Tours

Zeroday Brewing Company

Zeroday Brewing Company is a must-visit destination for beer enthusiasts visiting Middletown. This popular brewery offers a wide range of award-winning craft beers, including IPAs, stouts, and sours.

Middletown Area Historical Society

The Middletown Area Historical Society is dedicated to preserving and promoting the area’s rich history. Visitors can explore local exhibits, participate in historical programs and events, and learn about the town’s founding and development.

Week-Long Itinerary for Middletown (Dauphin County)

Day 1: Visit the Middletown & Hummelstown Railroad and explore local museums and historical sites. Day 2: Hike the Conewago Recreation Trail and enjoy a scenic picnic lunch at Koser Park. Day 3: Take a beer tour of Zeroday Brewing Company and explore Middletown’s thriving craft beer scene. Day 4: Visit the Pennsylvania State Capitol in Harrisburg and learn about the state’s legislative history. Day 5: Day trip to Hershey, PA, home of Hershey’s Chocolate World and Hersheypark. Day 6: Enjoy outdoor adventures at Swatara State Park, including hiking, fishing, and canoeing. Day 7: Explore the local culture and customs of Middletown and bring home souvenirs and local products to remember your trip.

Food and Drink Scene of Middletown (Dauphin County)

Must-Try Dishes

Chicken and Waffles

Chicken and waffles is a classic Southern dish that has become a favorite in Middletown. This delicious dish features crispy fried chicken paired with sweet, fluffy waffles and drizzled in syrup.

Soft Pretzels

Soft pretzels are a popular snack in Middletown, featuring chewy, salty dough and served with mustard or cheese sauce.

Local Beverages

Root Beer

Middletown is home to several local breweries and root beer is a popular local drink. Crafted using a secret recipe, Middletown’s root beer is a must-try for visitors.

Wine

Pennsylvania is known for its wine, and Middletown is no exception. Visitors can enjoy a wide range of local wines, including reds, whites, and rosés.

Where to Stay in Middletown (Dauphin County): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Middletown has a wide range of accommodation options for visitors, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. Some popular options include the Comfort Inn & Suites, the Red Lion Hotel Harrisburg Hershey, and the Bluegreen Vacations Suites at Hershey.

What to Buy in Middletown (Dauphin County): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to Middletown can bring home a piece of the town’s history and culture with a wide range of souvenirs and local products. Some popular options include locally crafted root beer, handmade pottery and ceramics, and locally made jams and jellies.

Local Culture and Customs of Middletown (Dauphin County)

Middletown has a strong sense of community and history, and visitors can explore local cultural institutions like the Middletown Area Historical Society or attend township events and festivals to learn more about the town’s heritage.

Best Time to Visit Middletown (Dauphin County)

The best time to visit Middletown is in the spring and fall months, when temperatures are mild and the local scenery is at its most beautiful. Summer can be hot and humid, while winter can be cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Middletown (Dauphin County)

Do:

  • Explore local museums and historical sites.
  • Try the local cuisine and drinks.
  • Participate in outdoor adventures at parks and trails.

Don’t:

  • Overlook the town’s rich history and cultural offerings.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  • Leave Middletown without trying the famous chicken and waffles.

Safety and Health

Safety in Middletown (Dauphin County)

Middletown is a relatively safe town, but visitors should still take precautions to safeguard their belongings and avoid walking alone late at night.

Health in Middletown (Dauphin County)

Visitors to Middletown should take normal health precautions, including staying hydrated during outdoor activities and utilizing sunscreen to protect against sunburn.

Getting to Middletown (Dauphin County): Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Middletown (Dauphin County)

The nearest airport to Middletown is Harrisburg International Airport, located just a short drive away.

Bus Stations Near Middletown (Dauphin County)

Several bus companies service the Harrisburg area, including Greyhound and Megabus.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Middletown via car, taxi, or rental vehicle.

How to Get Around in Middletown (Dauphin County)

Middletown is a relatively small town that can easily be explored on foot or by bike. Visitors can also utilize local transportation options, including taxis and rental cars.

Where to Go Next from Middletown (Dauphin County): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors to Middletown can explore a wide range of nearby destinations and excursions, including the Hershey’s Chocolate World and Hersheypark, Amish Country, and Gettysburg National Military Park.

Conclusion

Middletown (Dauphin County) is a charming and picturesque town that offers a wide range of attractions, activities, and cultural offerings. Whether you’re interested in outdoor adventures, history, or local cuisine and drinks, Middletown is the perfect destination for your next Mid-Atlantic vacation.
