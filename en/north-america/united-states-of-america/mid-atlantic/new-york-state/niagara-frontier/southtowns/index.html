Introduction to Southtowns

Southtowns, located in the Niagara Frontier region of New York state, is a beautiful and diverse region with a rich history and culture. The area is named after its location south of Buffalo and features a mix of modern amenities and natural beauty.

Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventure, cultural experiences, or just a relaxing getaway, Southtowns has something to offer. This comprehensive travel guide will help you plan your trip, including where to go, what to see, and where to stay.

List of Places to Visit in Southtowns

Southtowns is home to many cultural and natural attractions. Some of the must-visit places in the region include:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Darwin D. Martin House: A unique home designed by Frank Lloyd Wright and listed as a National Historic Landmark.
  • Buffalo & Erie County Naval & Military Park: A museum housing artifacts and exhibits dedicated to military history.
  • Roycroft Campus: A collection of historic buildings in East Aurora with a focus on arts and crafts.
  • Theodore Roosevelt National Inaugural Site: A museum dedicated to the life and presidency of Theodore Roosevelt.
  • Buffalo History Museum: A museum with exhibits and collections that focus on the history of Buffalo and Western New York.

Natural Attractions of Southtowns: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Zoar Valley: A natural area offering hiking, fishing, and hunting opportunities, as well as scenic views of the gorge.
  • Chestnut Ridge Park: A park with over 1,200 acres of land and featuring hiking trails, picnic areas, and a toboggan run.
  • Buffalo and Erie County Botanical Gardens: A beautiful garden featuring a wide range of plant species and unique exhibits.
  • Niagara Falls: One of the most iconic attractions in the United States, offering breathtaking views and various tours.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Southtowns

Southtowns boasts many scenic hiking and biking trails for nature enthusiasts. Here are some of the most popular trails:

  • The Erie Canalway Trail: A scenic trail that follows the historic Erie Canal and runs through cities such as Buffalo and Tonawanda.
  • The South Park System: A network of interconnected parks that offer hiking, biking, and picnicking areas.
  • Chestnut Ridge Park Trail: A 13-mile trail that offers stunning views of Buffalo, Lake Erie, and the Niagara River.

Best Things to Do in Southtowns: Activities and Tours

  • Take a food tour: Buffalo & Western New York is known for its world-renowned cuisine, and food tours are a great way to experience it all.
  • Visit local breweries and vineyards: The Niagara Frontier region is home to many amazing breweries and vineyards, perfect for sampling the local beverages.
  • Shop at local farmers’ markets and street fairs: Southtowns is home to many summer and fall farmers’ markets and festivals, offering a variety of food, crafts, and entertainment.
  • Visit the Buffalo Zoo: A world-class zoo that features various animal exhibits and attractions.
  • Attend a sporting event: Buffalo is home to several professional sports teams, and catching a game is a great way to experience the city’s sports culture.

Week-Long Itinerary for Southtowns

Day 1: Explore the city of Buffalo and visit famous attractions such as the Buffalo & Erie County Naval & Military Park and Darwin D. Martin House.

Day 2: Experience a nature hike or bike ride along Chestnut Ridge Park Trail and enjoy the scenic views.

Day 3: Take a food tour and explore the city’s cuisine, or visit local breweries and vineyards.

Day 4: Enjoy a day trip to Niagara Falls for breathtaking views and various tours.

Day 5: Visit cultural landmarks such as Roycroft Campus and Buffalo History Museum.

Day 6: Shop at local farmers’ markets and street fairs or attend a sporting event.

Day 7: Relax at one of Southtowns’ many parks and explore the beautiful outdoors.

Food and Drink Scene of Southtowns

Southtowns offers a dining scene like no other, blending traditional American cuisine with food influences from around the world. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Beef on weck: A roast beef sandwich served on a kummelweck roll, a unique local specialty.
  • Sponge candy: A delicate and crunchy candy made with sugar, corn syrup, and chocolate.
  • Chicken wings: A dish invented in Buffalo that features crispy wings tossed in hot sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Labatt Blue: A Canadian beer that is popular in the Niagara Frontier region.
  • Niagara Wine Trail: A collection of wineries in the Niagara Frontier region, offering various wine tastings and tours.
  • Tom & Jerry: A holiday cocktail made with brandy, rum, and a special batter.

Where to Stay in Southtowns: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Southtowns offers various accommodation options that cater to all budgets and lifestyles. Here are some of the best places to stay in the area:

  • Seneca Allegany Resort & Casino: A luxury resort and casino located in Salamanca.
  • The Mansion on Delaware Avenue: A luxurious hotel located in downtown Buffalo.
  • Roycroft Inn: A historic inn located in East Aurora, featuring unique art and crafts décor.
  • Red Carpet Inn Orchard Park: A budget-friendly option located near the city of Buffalo.
  • Courtyard by Marriott Buffalo Hamburg: A modern hotel in the town of Hamburg, offering various amenities.

What to Buy in Southtowns: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Southtowns offers many unique souvenirs and local products that reflect the region’s culture and history. Here are some of the best things to buy in Southtowns:

  • Local wine and beer: Bottles of wine and beer from the Niagara Wine Trail and local breweries make great souvenirs.
  • Art and crafts: Roycroft Campus is a great place to shop for unique, handcrafted items.
  • Buffalo-themed gifts: Buffalo-themed souvenirs such as t-shirts and home décor can be found at many local shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Southtowns

Southtowns has a rich history and cultural heritage, which can be seen in the architecture, art, and cuisine of the region. The locals are known for their friendly and welcoming attitude, making it easy for visitors to feel at home.

Best Time to Visit Southtowns

The best time to visit Southtowns is generally between June and September when the weather is warm and the attractions are in full swing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Southtowns

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine, such as the beef on weck and chicken wings.
  • Visit the Niagara Wine Trail and local breweries.
  • Explore the city of Buffalo and its cultural landmarks.

Don’t:

  • Forget to check the weather before heading out for outdoor activities.
  • Miss out on visiting Niagara Falls, one of the world’s most famous waterfalls.
  • Underestimate the Buffalo winters, which can be harsh and snowy.

Safety and Health

Safety in Southtowns

Southtowns is a relatively safe area, but visitors should still take necessary precautions such as not leaving valuables in their cars and being aware of surroundings while exploring.

Health in Southtowns

Visitors should take caution with their health, particularly in the summer months when the risk of West Nile virus is higher. Sunscreen and insect repellent are recommended when hiking or spending time outdoors.

Getting to Southtowns: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Southtowns

Buffalo Niagara International Airport is the closest major airport, located about 15 miles from Southtowns.

Bus Stations Near Southtowns

The Buffalo Greyhound Bus Terminal is located in the city of Buffalo, providing convenient bus service to and from the region.

Other Options

Car rentals are available at the Buffalo Niagara International Airport and various locations throughout the region.

How to Get Around in Southtowns

The easiest way to get around Southtowns is by car, but there are also local bus services available.

Where to Go Next from Southtowns: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Southtowns is in close proximity to several interesting destinations in New York state and beyond. Some worth visiting include:

  • Rochester, NY: A historic city with a rich cultural scene.
  • Allegheny National Forest: A beautiful outdoor destination in northwestern Pennsylvania.
  • Toronto, Canada: A vibrant city located a few hours away from Southtowns.

Conclusion

Southtowns in Niagara Frontier, New York, is a wonderful location for anyone looking for a unique blend of culture, history, and nature. With a variety of attractions and activities, there is something for everyone in this beautiful region. Use this travel guide for a memorable tour!
