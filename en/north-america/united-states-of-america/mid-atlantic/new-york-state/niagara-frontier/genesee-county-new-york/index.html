Introduction to Genesee County (New York)

Located in the Niagara Frontier region of New York, Genesee County is a beautiful and historic destination that offers a range of attractions and activities for travelers. Home to charming towns, sprawling nature reserves, and fascinating cultural landmarks, Genesee County is sure to impress visitors of all interests.

List of Places to Visit in Genesee County (New York)

Here is a list of some of the top places to visit in Genesee County:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Genesee Country Village & Museum
  • Holland Land Office Museum
  • Oliver’s Candies
  • Richmond Memorial Library
  • Woodward Memorial Library

Natural Attractions of Genesee County (New York): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Genesee Valley Greenway State Park
  • Iroquois National Wildlife Refuge
  • Le Roy House & Union Free Schoolhouse
  • Oakfield-Alabama Swamp

Hiking/Biking Routes of Genesee County (New York)

Genesee County is home to some of the best hiking and biking routes in the region. Here are a few of our favorites:

  • Genesee Valley Greenway
  • Leroy-Oatka Creek Walkway
  • Tonawanda Creek Trail
  • Ellicott Creek Trailway

Best Things to Do in Genesee County (New York): Activities and Tours

There are plenty of things to do in Genesee County, from outdoor adventures to cultural experiences. Here are a few must-try activities:

  • Take a hot air balloon ride with Balloons Over Letchworth
  • Visit Darien Lake Theme Park for an adrenaline-pumping day
  • Go fishing at Lake Alice or Horseshoe Lake
  • Attend a concert or show at the Batavia Downs Gaming & Hotel

Week-Long Itinerary for Genesee County (New York)

Here is a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to Genesee County:

Day 1: Explore Genesee Country Village & Museum Day 2: Spend the day hiking in the Genesee Valley Greenway State Park Day 3: Visit the Holland Land Office Museum and Richmond Memorial Library Day 4: Take a hot air balloon ride with Balloons Over Letchworth Day 5: Go fishing at Lake Alice or Horseshoe Lake Day 6: Visit the Darien Lake Theme Park and attend a concert at the Batavia Downs Gaming & Hotel Day 7: Relax in Iroquois National Wildlife Refuge

Local Culture and Customs of Genesee County (New York)

Genesee County has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its traditions, customs, and festivals. The county folk music and fiddling festival held annually, along with numerous cultural events throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Genesee County (New York)

Genesee County is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit depends on your interests. For outdoor activities like hiking and fishing, the best time to visit is during the summer and fall months. Winter is the perfect time to enjoy skiing and other snow-related activities. Springtime is ideal for cultural festivals and events.

Do’s and Don’ts of Genesee County (New York)

Do:

  • Visit the Genesee Country Village & Museum
  • Try the local specialties, such as Beef on Weck and White Hots
  • Attend a cultural event or festival
  • Take a hot air balloon ride

Don’t:

  • Ignore the local traditions and customs
  • Litter in the beautiful parks and nature reserves
  • Forget to try the local craft beers
