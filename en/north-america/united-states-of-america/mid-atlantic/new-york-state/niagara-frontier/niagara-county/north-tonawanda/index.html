Introduction to North Tonawanda

North Tonawanda is a charming city located in Niagara County, New York, with a rich and fascinating history. It is situated in the Niagara Frontier and is part of the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States. The city is known for its beautiful natural attractions, outdoor activities, and cultural landmarks, making it a popular tourist destination. Whether you’re a history buff or simply looking for an adventure in nature, North Tonawanda has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in North Tonawanda

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • 2nd Ward Alderman’s House - This historic home was built in the 1860s and is now a museum showcasing the history of North Tonawanda.
  • Riviera Theatre and Performing Arts Center - This Art Deco-style theater hosts a variety of shows and events throughout the year.
  • Herschell Carrousel Factory Museum - Learn about the history of carousels and the Herschell family, who were instrumental in the development of this classic amusement ride.
  • Niagara Aerospace Museum - This museum features exhibits on aerospace technology, including military and civilian aircraft.

Natural Attractions of North Tonawanda: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Niagara River - Take a scenic stroll beside the Niagara River, which flows through North Tonawanda.
  • Gateway Harbor Park - This park offers beautiful views of the Niagara River and is home to outdoor concerts and events throughout the summer.
  • Deerwood Golf Course - This beautiful 18-hole golf course is nestled beside the Niagara River and offers stunning views of the water.

Hiking/Biking Routes of North Tonawanda

  • Erie Canalway Trail - This trail runs along the Erie Canal and offers great views of the water and the surrounding forests.
  • Tonawanda Rails to Trails - This trail follows an old railway line and offers a peaceful and scenic route for hiking or biking.
  • Niagara River Greenway Trail - This trail passes through several scenic parks and is perfect for a leisurely walk or bike ride.

Best Things to Do in North Tonawanda: Activities and Tours

  • Lockport Cave & Underground Boat Ride - Explore the underground caves and waterfalls of the Erie Canal on this unique boat tour.
  • Niagara Jet Adventures - Get your adrenaline pumping with a high-speed jet boat tour of the Niagara River.
  • Erie Canal Cruise - Take a relaxing boat tour along the historic Erie Canal and learn about its history and significance.

Week-Long Itinerary for North Tonawanda

Day 1: Explore the historic 2nd Ward Alderman’s House and catch a show at the Riviera Theatre.

Day 2: Learn about the history of carousels and take a ride on one at the Herschell Carrousel Factory Museum.

Day 3: Take a leisurely stroll along the Niagara River and enjoy the views at Gateway Harbor Park.

Day 4: Hit the links at Deerwood Golf Course, which offers stunning views of the Niagara River.

Day 5: Explore the Niagara River on a thrilling jet boat ride with Niagara Jet Adventures.

Day 6: Take a relaxing boat tour along the Erie Canal and learn about its importance to the region.

Day 7: Spend the day hiking or biking along the Erie Canalway Trail or the Tonawanda Rails to Trails.

Food and Drink Scene of North Tonawanda

Must-Try Dishes

  • Beef on Weck - This classic Buffalo sandwich features thinly sliced roast beef on a kummelweck roll.
  • Fish Fry - A Friday night tradition in Western New York, featuring fresh fried fish with a side of fries and cole slaw.
  • Buffalo Wings - Invented in nearby Buffalo, these spicy chicken wings are a must-try.

Local Beverages

  • Labatt Blue - This Canadian beer is popular throughout Western New York and is a staple at local bars.
  • Loganberry - This sweet-tart fruit drink is a Buffalo favorite.
  • Niagara Region Wines - The Niagara region is known for its vineyards and produces some excellent wines.

Where to Stay in North Tonawanda: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Comfort Inn - This hotel is located near the Niagara River and offers comfortable rooms and amenities.
  • Angel’s Landing Bed and Breakfast - This charming B&B is located in a historic home and offers personalized attention and a relaxing atmosphere.
  • Budget Inn - If you’re looking for an affordable option, this motel has basic amenities and is located near local attractions.

What to Buy in North Tonawanda: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • North Tonawanda Postcards - Send a postcard to your loved ones featuring beautiful views of North Tonawanda.
  • Herschell Carrousel Factory Museum Souvenirs - Bring home a carousel horse figurine or other souvenirs from this local museum.
  • Local Wines - Take home a bottle of wine from one of the many vineyards in the Niagara region.

Local Culture and Customs of North Tonawanda

North Tonawanda is a welcoming and friendly community, with a strong appreciation for its history and natural beauty. Visitors are encouraged to explore the city’s cultural landmarks and participate in local events and traditions.

Best Time to Visit North Tonawanda

The best time to visit North Tonawanda is in the summer, when the weather is warm and outdoor events and activities are plentiful. However, the city is also beautiful in the fall, with stunning foliage and cooler temperatures.

Do’s and Don’ts of North Tonawanda

Do:

  • Do explore the city’s cultural landmarks and natural attractions.
  • Do participate in local traditions and events.
  • Do try the regional cuisine, including beef on weck and Buffalo wings.
  • Do bring comfortable walking shoes for exploring the city’s many trails and parks.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in local parks and natural areas.
  • Don’t forget to bring sunscreen and bug spray for outdoor activities.
  • Don’t skip the chance to try local wines from the Niagara region.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask locals for recommendations on things to see and do.

Safety and Health

Safety in North Tonawanda

North Tonawanda is a safe and friendly city, but visitors should take common-sense precautions like keeping their belongings close and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in North Tonawanda

Visitors should be up to date on routine vaccinations and consult with a healthcare provider if traveling during flu season. Bring any necessary medications and be prepared for hot and humid weather in the summer months.

Getting to North Tonawanda: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near North Tonawanda

  • Buffalo Niagara International Airport - Located 13 miles south of North Tonawanda, this airport offers domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near North Tonawanda

  • Greyhound Bus Station - Located in downtown Buffalo, this station offers connections to North Tonawanda.

Other Options

  • Amtrak - There are several Amtrak stations in the Buffalo-Niagara region, including one in downtown Buffalo.
  • Car Rental - Renting a car is a good option for exploring the Niagara region at your own pace.

How to Get Around in North Tonawanda

Walking, biking, and driving are the best ways to get around North Tonawanda. There are also several taxi and ride-sharing services available.

Where to Go Next from North Tonawanda: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Niagara Falls - This iconic natural wonder is located just a short drive from North Tonawanda.
  • Buffalo - This vibrant city is known for its architecture, food, and art scene and is located just 15 miles south of North Tonawanda.
  • Letchworth State Park - Known as the “Grand Canyon of the East,” this stunning park offers hiking trails, waterfalls, and panoramic views of the Genesee River.

Conclusion

North Tonawanda is a hidden gem in Western New York, with something to offer every type of traveler. From exploring the city’s cultural landmarks to hiking and biking along scenic trails, there’s no shortage of things to do and see. With its welcoming community and beautiful natural surroundings, North Tonawanda is the perfect destination for your next travel adventure.
