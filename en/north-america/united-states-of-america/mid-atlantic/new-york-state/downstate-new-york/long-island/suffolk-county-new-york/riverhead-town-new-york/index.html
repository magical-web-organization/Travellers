Introduction to Riverhead (Town, New York)

Riverhead is a quaint town located in Suffolk County, Long Island, New York, that has something to offer to every type of traveler. Famed to be the gateway to the East End of Long Island, this town is the perfect destination to experience a blend of history, culture, nature, and adventure. Whether you are a history buff or a foodie, a nature lover or an adventure enthusiast, Riverhead will definitely attract you.

List of Places to Visit in Riverhead (Town, New York)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Start your trip to Riverhead by exploring some of the important cultural attractions and historical places.

1. Suffolk County Historical Society Museum

Visit the Suffolk County Historical Society Museum to dive deep into the area’s history. The museum’s galleries showcase artifacts, documents, and interactive exhibits that tell the story of the people that resided on Long Island for centuries.

2. Hallockville Museum Farm

The Hallockville Museum Farm is a historic farmstead where historians can discover the beauty and richness of rural Long Island history. The museum showcases 18 historic buildings and shelters an extensive collection of historic artifacts, furniture, tools, and farm equipment.

3. Long Island Aquarium

Take a break from the history and visit the Long Island Aquarium for a chance to experience close encounters with marine wildlife and learn about the aquatic life of Long Island’s surrounding waters. The aquarium features various exhibits, including a shark exhibit, sea lion shows, and a butterfly exhibit.

Natural Attractions of Riverhead (Town, New York): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Besides the cultural attractions, Riverhead is also famous for its beautiful natural attractions.

1. Calverton National Cemetery

Located in Riverhead, the Calverton National Cemetery is an iconic national cemetery that provides a serene and scenic environment to pay tribute. The cemetery is also a recognized place of beauty, where visitors can observe unique sculptures and artistic features as their eyes marvel at the surrounding landscapes.

2. Tanger Outlets

Indulge in some retail therapy and check out the Tanger Outlets located in Riverhead. These outlet stores feature a wide range of merchandise from some of the most coveted brands in the fashion industry, including Armani, Gap, Nike, and more.

3. Long Island Sound

Take a moment to appreciate the beauty of the Long Island Sound by visiting the Sound View Beach. As visitors stroll on the nearby boardwalk, they can take in magnificent views of the Long Island Sound as they watch the waves gently roll up to the shore.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Riverhead (Town, New York)

For those looking for outdoor adventure, Riverhead features several hiking and biking routes that offer scenic views and unique experiences.

1. Hallock State Park Preserve

Hallock State Park Preserve is a natural treasure located in Northville that features hiking trails winding through some of the most scenic vistas on Long Island’s North Fork. Fitted with a dune environment and a habitat for shorebirds and sea life, this is the perfect outdoor recreational area for nature lovers.

2. Pine Barrens Trail

The Pine Barrens Trail is a scenic route that takes hikers and bikers through one of the greatest wilderness areas on the East Coast. Featuring winding, sloping trails, the route is great for both beginners and experienced hikers.

3. The Hamlet and Village Green Trail

The Hamlet and Village Green Trail is a great starting point for beginners. The somewhat circular trail takes hikers through beautiful white oak trees and meadows, with interpretive signs providing cultural, historical, and environmental information along the way.

Best Things to Do in Riverhead (Town, New York): Activities and Tours

From leisurely walks to adrenaline-pumping adventures, Riverhead offers visitors various memorable tours and activities.

1. Riverhead Raceway

If you’re a fan of high-octane sports, then the Riverhead Raceway should be on your list of things to do. Experience the thrill of racing as you watch high-speed stock car races or even take a behind-the-scenes tour to see the inner workings of the track.

2. East End Bike Tour

Blaze through Riverhead’s countryside on a bike tour of vineyards and sprawling farmland. The tour features several wineries as well as tasting rooms, providing visitors with a taste of North Fork’s rich agricultural history.

3. North Fork Helicopter Tour

Take to the skies on this unique adventure tour of the North Fork. This helicopter tour takes visitors to some of the most picturesque vineyards, farms, and estates, providing a bird’s-eye view of the Long Island countryside.

Food and Drink Scene of Riverhead (Town, New York)

Must-Try Dishes

Visitors looking to savor some of Riverhead’s culinary delights should try:

1. Clam Chowder

Riverhead is known for preparing some of the best clam chowder in the New York area, made with fresh local clams and vegetables.

2. Lobster Rolls

Another must-try delicacy is the Lobster Roll, made with fresh lobster, celery, and spices on a perfect New England style-bun.

3. Peconic Bay Scallops

Riverhead is famous for its seafood, and Peconic Bay Scallops are a local favorite. The scallops are succulent, tender, and sweet, and served in various dishes across town.

Local Beverages

1. Long Island Wines

Long Island vineyards are famous for their excellent wines, including delicious Chardonnay, Merlot, and Sauvignon Blanc.

2. Local Craft Beer

Long Island is home to a thriving beer scene, with breweries offering everything from classic lagers to hoppy IPAs.

Where to Stay in Riverhead (Town, New York): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Riverhead offers a wide variety of accommodation options to suit every taste and budget, ranging from luxurious hotels to budget-friendly guesthouses.

1. Hilton Garden Inn Riverhead

The Hilton Garden Inn Riverhead offers luxurious lodgings with top-quality amenities, including an indoor pool, fitness center, and complimentary Wi-Fi.

2. Jedediah Hawkins Inn

Jedediah Hawkins Inn is renowned for its old-world charm and irresistible rustic elegance. The inn is a splendidly restored 1863 mansion complete with modern-day conveniences and romantic ambiance.

3. Budget Host East End Hotel

The Budget Host East End Hotel is an affordable hotel that offers clean, comfortable rooms and is conveniently located near many attractions.

What to Buy in Riverhead (Town, New York): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

1. Wine

Long Island wines are a must-buy souvenir when visiting Riverhead. Some of the wineries in the area offer unique tasting experiences as well as shipping services for tourists.

2. Local Produce

Riverhead is known for its agricultural excellence, so visitors should try to bring back local products such as fruits, vegetables, and handmade wines, beers, and ciders.

3. Artisan Crafts

Visitors can purchase various artisan hand-made crafts, like pottery, glassworks, and original paintings.

Local Culture and Customs of Riverhead (Town, New York)

Riverhead is a close-knit community that values its history, traditions, and heritage. Visitors should respect this and try to learn as much as possible from the locals and immerse themselves in the area’s culture.

Best Time to Visit Riverhead (Town, New York)

The best time to visit Riverhead is from June to October when the weather is generally warm, and the region’s wineries are in full bloom.

Do’s and Don’ts of Riverhead (Town, New York)

Do:

  • Respect the area’s cultural heritage, traditions, and customs.
  • Take time to explore the area’s nature parks, historical sites, and cultural attractions.
  • Try Riverhead’s culinary delicacies.

Don’t:

  • Ignore the area’s natural beauty.
  • Overlook historical and cultural attractions.
  • Disrespect the locals and their customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Riverhead (Town, New York)

Riverhead is a safe destination, but visitors should take all the necessary precautions, especially when hiking or biking on unmarked trails. Locking one’s car when shopping at retail locations is also advised.

Health in Riverhead (Town, New York)

Riverhead is a clean and healthy destination, but visitors should ensure that they carry sufficient sunscreen and insect repellent, especially when trekking or visiting the beach.

Getting to Riverhead (Town, New York): Transportation Options and Logistics

Visitors can easily get to Riverhead via air, land, or sea. The town is conveniently located along the Long Island Expressway’s Route 58, making it accessible by car and public transportation.

Airports Near Riverhead (Town, New York)

The nearest commercial airport to Riverhead is MacArthur Airport, located in Islip, approximately 27-miles away.

Bus Stations Near Riverhead (Town, New York)

The Hampton Jitney bus line offers shuttle service to and from Riverhead to various locations in the tri-state area.

Other Options

Visitors can catch a train to local destinations from the Riverhead Long Island Railroad Station.

How to Get Around in Riverhead (Town, New York)

Visitors can easily get around Riverhead using a rental car, bike, or public transportation. Taxis, Uber, and Lyft ride-sharing services, and local bus transit systems are also available options for transportation.

Where to Go Next from Riverhead (Town, New York): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Riverhead is situated near several unique destinations worth exploring, including:

1. Hamptons

The Hamptons are just a short drive from Riverhead and feature gorgeous beaches, luxurious shopping and dining experiences, and some of the best cultural attractions in the area.

2. Greenport

Greenport is a charming village located on the North Fork of Long Island that features cobblestone streets, antique shops, and galleries offering artisan crafts.

3. Montauk

Montauk is a vibrant beach community located on the eastern end of Long Island. Visitors can enjoy a range of fun activities such as hiking, surfing, deep-sea fishing, and whale watching.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking for cultural attractions, outdoor adventure, or just some good old-fashioned relaxation, Riverhead has something exciting for everyone. A great destination for a family vacation, romantic getaway or solo adventure; it is a place that visitors will remember for years to come. Book your trip to Riverhead today!
