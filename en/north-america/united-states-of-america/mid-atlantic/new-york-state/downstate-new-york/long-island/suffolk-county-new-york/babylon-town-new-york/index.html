Introduction to Babylon (Town, New York)

Babylon is a charming town located on Long Island, in Downstate New York. It is a popular destination among tourists, offering both natural and cultural attractions, as well as a bustling food and drink scene. Whether you’re looking to explore museums and historical places, hike or bike through scenic routes, or simply relax on the beach, Babylon has something to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Babylon (Town, New York)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Babylon Historical Society Museum: A must-visit for history buffs, this museum houses a collection of artifacts, documents, and photographs that tell the story of Babylon’s rich history.
  • The Fire Island Lighthouse: Located within Fire Island National Seashore, this lighthouse is a significant historical landmark and offers breathtaking views of the Atlantic Ocean.
  • The Argyle Park and Preserve: A scenic park that features a playground, boat launch, picnic areas, walking trails, and a duck pond.
  • The Touro Synagogue: Built-in 1756, this temple is the oldest synagogue in North America and now serves as a museum.

Natural Attractions of Babylon (Town, New York): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Fire Island National Seashore: A serene and picturesque beach that stretches for over 26 miles, offering scenic views, wildlife sightings, and surfing opportunities.
  • Belmont Lake State Park: This park is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts and features a lake for boating and fishing, a playground, picnic areas, and a network of hiking and biking trails.
  • Captree State Park: A popular spot for fishing and boating, this park offers stunning views of the Great South Bay.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Babylon (Town, New York)

Babylon is home to several scenic hiking and biking routes that offer breathtaking views of the town’s natural beauty. Some of the most popular trails include:

  • The Argyle Park Loop Trail: A beautiful trail that starts at the park’s playground and takes you through wooded areas and along the shoreline of the pond.
  • The Massapequa Preserve Trail: A well-maintained trail that offers an easy hike or bike ride through wooded areas, streams, and ponds.
  • The Robert Moses State Park Trail: A challenging but rewarding trail that takes you through dunes and offers stunning views of the beach and shoreline.

Best Things to Do in Babylon (Town, New York): Activities and Tours

Babylon offers a wide range of activities and tours, catering to different interests and preferences. Here are a few must-try experiences:

  • The Babylon Village Farmers Market: A weekly farmers market that features a variety of fresh, locally grown produce, artisanal products, and live music.
  • The Babylon Carriage House Art Gallery: An art gallery that features the works of local and regional artists, with rotating exhibits throughout the year.
  • The Babylon Village Arts Council Cultural Walking Tour: A guided walking tour that takes you through the town’s historic district, highlighting its cultural and architectural treasures.
  • The Babylon Village Ghost Tour: A spooky but fun tour that takes you through the town’s haunted spots, with tales of ghostly apparitions and supernatural occurrences.

Week-Long Itinerary for Babylon (Town, New York)

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Babylon, here’s a sample itinerary that will help you make the most of your time:

Day 1: Explore the town’s Historical Places and Museums Day 2: Hike and bike through one of the scenic routes of the town. Day 3: Spend a relaxing day on the Fire Island National Seashore Day 4: Visit the Argyle Park and Preserve and enjoy a picnic by the duck pond. Day 5: Take a guided walking tour around the town’s historic district Day 6: Try some local cuisines and beverages from the restaurants and bars around the town. Day 7: Shop for souvenirs at the local shops and boutiques.

Food and Drink Scene of Babylon (Town, New York)

Babylon has a burgeoning food and drink scene with a variety of options available for every taste and budget. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lobster Rolls from Nautical Mile: A famous seafood dish served in local restaurants, this dish is a must-try.
  • Pizza from Gino’s Pizzeria and Restaurant: One of the best places for a slice of pizza.
  • Seafood and Pasta from Tres Palms: A local favorite that serves delicious seafood and pasta dishes.

Local Beverages

  • Blue Point Brewing Company Beers: A local brewery that offers delicious ales, stouts, and lagers.
  • Wine from The Lenz Winery: A family-owned winery that produces award-winning wines.

Where to Stay in Babylon (Town, New York): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Babylon offers a variety of accommodation options, ranging from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxurious hotels. Some of the best places to stay in Babylon are:

  • The Babylon Beach House: A charming guesthouse that offers stunning views of the ocean and excellent amenities.
  • The Houseboat: A unique and cozy option for those looking for a waterfront accommodation.
  • The Gateway Inn: A budget-friendly accommodation option that offers clean and comfortable rooms.

What to Buy in Babylon (Town, New York): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Babylon is known for its thriving arts and culture scene, and visitors can find a variety of souvenirs and local products to bring home. Some of the best places to shop for these items are:

  • The Village Pharmacy: A local pharmacy that sells unique gifts and souvenirs.
  • The Argyle Cheese Factory: A local cheese factory that produces high-quality, artisanal cheeses.

  • Respecting the town’s laws and regulations, such as no smoking in public places.
