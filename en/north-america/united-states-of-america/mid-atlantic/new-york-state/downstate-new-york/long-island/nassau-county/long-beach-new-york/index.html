Introduction to Long Beach (New York)

Located in Nassau County in Long Island, Long Beach (New York) is a charming beach town known for its beautiful sandy beaches and lively boardwalk. This vibrant summer destination is a perfect place for a family-friendly vacation or a romantic getaway. With a long list of exciting landmarks and attractions, Long Beach offers something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Long Beach (New York)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Long Beach Historical Museum

The Long Beach Historical Museum is the perfect place to learn about the history and culture of the city. This museum exhibits many historical artifacts that are profoundly related to the city’s history. For a better understanding of the museum, the expert guides are available to help you to navigate, and they share the rich history of Long Beach through their informative way.

The Holocaust Memorial and Tolerance Center of Nassau County

The Holocaust Memorial and Tolerance Center of Nassau County is a unique attraction located in nearby Glen Cove. This center is dedicated to the remembrance of the Holocaust and the promotion of tolerance and understanding. This center is visited by thousands of people every year from across the world, and it is a site that offers a profound educational experience.

Natural Attractions of Long Beach (New York): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Long Beach Boardwalk

The Long Beach Boardwalk is a popular landmark and is part of the charm of this unique destination. This boardwalk is perfect for an afternoon stroll or a morning jog, and many visitors come here for the views of the beautiful ocean and the stunning sunsets.

Nickerson Beach Park

Nickerson Beach Park is a great place to relax and enjoy the sunshine. With its soft sand, clear water, and stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean, this park is perfect for a day out with the family or a romantic stroll. Nickerson Beach Park offers playgrounds, picnic areas, and BBQ areas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Long Beach (New York)

Long Beach Boardwalk and Beyond

The long beach boardwalk is a great spot for a walk, but it also has plenty of biking spots available. The 2.2-mile boardwalk features bike racks, so you can easily park your bike and walk, especially the local cyclist.

Best Things to Do in Long Beach (New York): Activities and Tours

Surfs Up NY

Surfs Up NY offers surf lessons for people of all ages, even beginners. The instructors are highly experienced and can teach you not just surfing but all the lessons that come along with riding the waves.

Long Beach Adventure Park

Long Beach Adventure Park is a popular spot for zip-lining, rope courses, and many other adventure-related activities. This place is perfect for family bonding or a group of friends looking for a fun, challenging experience.

Food and Drink Scene of Long Beach (New York)

Must-Try Dishes

Bagels

Long Beach has a thriving bagel scene, and there are many different bagel cafes to choose from. Try Bagel Boss or Beach Bagel Cafe.

Burgers

If you’re a burger lover, you’ll find plenty to satisfy your cravings in Long Beach. Try the best burger at The Saloon.

Local Beverages

Long Beach Brewing Company

The Long Beach Brewing Company is the first and only brewery in Long Beach, and it offers a wide range of craft beers.

Where to Stay in Long Beach (New York): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Allegria Hotel

The Allegria Hotel is nestled at the beachfront with 156 guest rooms. The lobby bar, rooftop spa, and on-site restaurant it has to offer is truly exceptional.

What to Buy in Long Beach (New York): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

T-Shirts

If you’re looking for a souvinier, you can’t go wrong with a Long Beach t-shirt. These shirts are available at many retailers in the city.

Local Culture and Customs of Long Beach (New York)

The culture of Long Beach is unique, with a vibrant artistic community and a passion for outdoor activities. The people here are very friendly and welcoming, and there are many local customs that reflect the city’s relaxed vibe.

Best Time to Visit Long Beach (New York)

The best time to visit Long Beach is during the summer months, which is between late May and early September. The weather is warm and sunny, and the city is alive with activity.

The Do’s and Don’ts of Long Beach (New York)

Do:

  • Use sunscreen and wear hats
  • Go to Good Food Company to try their shrimp & grits
  • Walk the boardwalk in the early morning

Don’t:

  • Ignore the rules of beaches
  • Litter, always throw trash properly
  • Don’t disturb the wildlife

Safety and Health

Safety in Long Beach (New York)

Long Beach is a safe city, but visitors should take some precautions, just like any other tourist destination. Avoid deserted places, stay aware, and always lock the doors of your car.

Health in Long Beach (New York)

The emergency services are available almost anytime in any medical situation. There are also many health clinics and hospitals all over the city, so finding quality medical care will not be an issue.

Getting to Long Beach (New York): Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Long Beach (New York)

The nearest airport to Long Beach is John F. Kennedy International Airport, which is around 12 miles away from the town. From JFK Airport, you can travel via public transport or a private taxi.

Bus Stations Near Long Beach (New York)

There are many bus stations in and around Long Beach that you can use to get to. You can take buses from the Long Beach Bus Terminal, which is also known as the Bradley Beach Train Station.

Other Options

Visitors can rent bicycles, taxis or cars to get around. There are also many bike rental services available.

How to Get Around in Long Beach (New York)

The best way to get around Long Beach is to rent a car or a bicycle. Public transportation services are also available.

Where to Go Next from Long Beach (New York): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Jones Beach State Park

Jones Beach State Park is a popular destination located just ten miles west of Long Beach. This park has many exciting attractions, including a stunning boardwalk and many biking trails.

Conclusion

Overall, Long Beach is an excellent destination for travelers who want to experience the charm of a traditional beach town. With its beautiful beaches, natural attractions, and exciting activities, there is something for everyone in Long Beach. Plan your trip today and experience the unique beauty of this city for yourself.
