Introduction to Port Washington (New York)

Port Washington is a charming little town located on the north shore of Long Island, just a short drive from New York City. It is known for its beautiful waterfront, historic downtown, and quaint atmosphere. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or a fun-filled adventure, Port Washington has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Port Washington (New York)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sands-Willets House: This historic house museum is a must-visit for history buffs. Built in 1735, it’s one of the oldest houses in Port Washington and has been beautifully preserved.
  • Cow Neck Peninsula Historical Society Museum: Learn about the history of Port Washington through exhibits, artifacts, and events at this local museum.
  • Manorhaven Village Hall: This iconic building has been a fixture of the community since 1927. It’s now home to the village government, but visitors can still admire its beautiful architecture.

Natural Attractions of Port Washington (New York): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Sands Point Preserve: This 216-acre nature preserve offers stunning views of the Long Island Sound and rolling hills. Visitors can hike, bike, or simply relax and enjoy the scenery.
  • Hempstead Harbor: Take a leisurely stroll along the harbor and admire the boats, birds, and beautiful sunsets. You can also go boating or fishing if you’re feeling adventurous.
  • North Hempstead Beach Park: This waterfront park offers a variety of activities, including swimming, kayaking, and picnicking. It’s the perfect place to spend a sunny day with family and friends.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Port Washington (New York)

  • Port Washington Greenbelt Trail: This 3-mile hiking trail runs through a wooded nature preserve and offers stunning views of the surrounding area. It’s a great option for a quick outdoor adventure.
  • The John Philip Sousa Memorial Band Shell: Take a stroll around this iconic band shell and enjoy the beautiful views of the water.
  • Plandome Country Club Trail: This easy 2.5-mile hike is perfect for beginners and offers beautiful views of the area.

Best Things to Do in Port Washington (New York): Activities and Tours

  • Marina Park: This park features a scenic waterfront and a variety of activities, including tennis courts, basketball courts, and playgrounds.
  • Bow Tie Cinemas: Catch a movie at this classic cinema, which has been a fixture of the community for decades.
  • Tri-Sport: Rent a bike, kayak, or paddleboard from Tri-Sport and explore the beautiful Long Island Sound.

Food and Drink Scene of Port Washington (New York)

Must-Try Dishes

  • Port Jefferson Lobster Roll: This classic sandwich features juicy chunks of fresh lobster meat served on a toasted hot dog bun.
  • Clam Chowder: Port Washington is famous for its delicious clam chowder, made with fresh local ingredients.
  • Bagels: New York is known for its bagels, and Port Washington is no exception. Head to one of the local bagel shops for a fresh and tasty breakfast.

Local Beverages

  • Local Beers: Try a beer from Port Washington’s local brewery, Lithology Brewing Company.
  • Wine: Long Island is known for its excellent wines, and Port Washington is home to several wineries.
  • Coffee: Stop by one of the local coffee shops for a hot cup of coffee or a refreshing iced latte.

Where to Stay in Port Washington (New York): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Andrew: This charming boutique hotel features comfortable rooms, great amenities, and a convenient location in the heart of downtown.
  • The Harborview: This waterfront hotel offers stunning views of the Long Island Sound and easy access to all the best attractions in Port Washington.
  • The Sheerwater at the Piermont: This elegant guesthouse is located in a historic building and offers a luxurious and peaceful retreat for visitors.

What to Buy in Port Washington (New York): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Port Washington T-Shirt: Show off your love for Port Washington with a stylish and comfortable t-shirt.
  • Hempstead Harbor Soap Company Products: These handmade soaps and lotions are made with locally sourced ingredients and are perfect for pampering yourself or giving as a gift.
  • Long Island Wines: Bring home a bottle of Long Island wine to enjoy with friends and family.

Local Culture and Customs of Port Washington (New York)

Port Washington is a friendly and welcoming community, and visitors are always greeted with a smile. The town is proud of its history and traditions, and locals are happy to share their knowledge and love of the area with visitors.

Best Time to Visit Port Washington (New York)

The best time to visit Port Washington is during the spring, summer, and fall months, when the weather is mild and the outdoor activities are plentiful. However, each season has its own charm, and visitors can enjoy Port Washington year-round.

Do’s and Don’ts of Port Washington (New York)

Do:

  • Do take a walk along the waterfront: The picturesque waterfront is one of the best things about Port Washington, and it’s the perfect place to relax and unwind.
  • Do try the local seafood: Port Washington is famous for its fresh and delicious seafood, so be sure to give it a try.
  • Do explore the downtown area: Port Washington’s historic downtown is full of charm and character, with great shops, restaurants, and cafes to discover.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter: Port Washington is a beautiful and pristine town, and locals take great pride in keeping it that way. Please dispose of your trash properly.
  • Don’t forget to tip: If you’re dining out or using a taxi service, be sure to leave a tip for your server or driver.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for directions: Locals are happy to help visitors navigate their way around town, so don’t hesitate to ask for help if you need it.

Safety and Health

Safety in Port Washington (New York)

Port Washington is a safe and welcoming town, with low crime rates and friendly locals. However, visitors should always take the usual precautions while traveling, such as keeping valuables secure and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Port Washington (New York)

Port Washington has several hospitals and medical centers that provide high-quality healthcare to residents and visitors alike. It’s always a good idea to have travel insurance and to be familiar with the local healthcare facilities in case of an emergency.

Getting to Port Washington (New York): Transportation Options and Logistics

Port Washington is easily accessible by car, bus, or train. It’s located just a short drive from New York City, and there are several convenient transportation options available.

Airports Near Port Washington (New York)

  • LaGuardia Airport (LGA): This airport is located just 20 miles away from Port Washington and offers domestic and international flights.
  • John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK): This airport is located 25 miles away from Port Washington and offers domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Port Washington (New York)

  • Port Washington LIRR Station: This is a convenient option for travelers coming from New York City or other locations on Long Island.

Other Options

  • Driving: Port Washington is easily accessible by car, with several major highways running nearby.
  • Taxi and Ride-Sharing Services: Uber and Lyft are available in Port Washington, as well as a variety of local taxi companies.

How to Get Around in Port Washington (New York)

Port Washington is a walkable town, with most attractions located within easy walking distance of each other. However, there are also several transportation options available, including taxis, buses, and rental cars.

Where to Go Next from Port Washington (New York): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • New York City: Just a short drive or train ride away, New York City offers endless attractions, from world-famous museums and restaurants to iconic landmarks and Broadway shows.
  • Long Island Wine Country: Explore the scenic vineyards and tasting rooms of Long Island’s wine country, just a short drive from Port Washington.
  • Fire Island National Seashore: This stunning national park offers beautiful beaches, hiking trails, and wildlife watching opportunities.

Conclusion

Port Washington is a charming and beautiful town that offers something for everyone. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, or simply a peaceful retreat, Port Washington is the perfect destination. With its friendly locals, delicious food and drink, and stunning scenery, you’re sure to have a memorable trip to this delightful town.
