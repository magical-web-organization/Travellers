Introduction to Suffern

Located in Rockland County, Suffern is a quintessential American small town that offers a wide range of activities and attractions for tourists. From historic museums to natural vistas, there are plenty of things to do and see in this charming town. Let’s take a closer look at some of the top things to do and see in Suffern.

List of Places to Visit in Suffern

Here is a list of places to visit in Suffern:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. The Museum at the Station: This museum is located in a restored 1913 Erie Railroad station and features exhibits on the history of Suffern and the Erie Railroad.

  2. Kalmar Nyckel Foundation: A replica of the 17th-century Swedish tall ship fills the museum, as well as offers a treasure-trove of artifacts.

  3. The Suffern Village Museum: Offers a great insight into the village’s history and traditions.

Natural Attractions of Suffern: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. HarrimanState Park: This state park is located just southeast of Suffern and offers numerous hiking and camping opportunities amid the natural beauty of the region.

  2. Ramapo Valley County Reservation: Perfect spot for hiking or picnicking.

  3. Bear Mountain State Park: Offers spectacular scenic vistas, a zoo, and a plethora of outdoor activities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Suffern

  1. Bear Mountain/Harriman State Park trails: A plethora of hiking and biking options for all skill levels is available in this area.

  2. Ramapo Valley County Reservation trails: From wide hiking paths to narrow singletracks, this park has it all.

Best Things to Do in Suffern: Activities and Tours

  1. Peter’s Kill Area: This sports climbing location offers a range of cliffs and challenging routes.

  2. Hudson Valley Balloon Rides: Capture breathtaking views of Suffern from above with a hot air balloon ride.

  3. Jolly Onion Brewery: Enjoy some of the best craft beer in town, paired with live music on the weekends.

Week-Long Itinerary for Suffern

Food and Drink Scene of Suffern

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Craft House: Famous for their buffalo cauliflower and meatloaf sandwiches.

  2. Trattoria Viviano: Recommended for their pizzas and pasta dishes.

  3. Union Restaurant and Bar Latino: Ideal for trying out the Latin American dishes.

Local Beverages

  1. Jolly Onion Brewery: All the brews here are exceptional.

  2. The Torne Valley Vineyard: The wines here are fairy made and the tasting room is a must-visit.

Where to Stay in Suffern: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Crowne Plaza Suffern-Mahwah: A luxurious option near some of the town’s top attractions.

  2. Homewood Suites by Hilton Mahwah: Featuring all-suites accommodation with great service.

  3. Airmont Acres: Offers charming, budget-friendly accommodations.

What to Buy in Suffern: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Old Tappan Farms: Grab some of the locally grown produce and preserves for home.

  2. Listowel Winery: Buy some wine at this vineyard and take a piece of Suffern home with you.

Local Culture and Customs of Suffern

  1. The town has strong traditions when celebrating St. Patrick’s Day, with a parade and festivities.

  2. The Suffern Farmers’ Market, held weekly from June through October, highlights locally grown foods and crafts.

Best Time to Visit Suffern

  1. The best time to visit Suffern is during the summer months as the weather is ideal for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Suffern

Do:

  1. Do participate in the St. Patrick’s Day parade and festivities.

  2. Do try out the local craft beer and wine.

  3. Do take the trail hikes and biking routes available.

Don’t:

  1. Don’t litter the natural spaces.

  2. Don’t forget to tip your servers when dining out.

  3. Don’t leave valuables visible in your vehicle.

Safety and Health

Safety in Suffern

  1. Suffern is a safe place, but it is always best to take precautions wherever you go.

  2. Watch your beverages, especially in bars and crowded areas.

Health in Suffern

  1. Take precautions to avoid tick bites during peak tick season.

  2. It is recommended to carry insect repellent and wear long sleeves and pants while hiking.

Getting to Suffern: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Suffern

  1. Newark Liberty International Airport is the closest major airport. Other options include LaGuardia and JFK airports.

Bus Stations Near Suffern

  1. Shortline Bus offers bus services to and from Suffern.

Other Options

  1. There are a plethora of car rentals and ride-share options, such as Uber and Lyft, available.

How to Get Around in Suffern

  1. Suffern is small enough that just walking is an excellent option.

  2. However, a rental car or ride-share service can help you explore the outskirts of town.

Where to Go Next from Suffern: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. New York City is just about 35 miles away and a perfect way to explore the hustling city, and Bear Mountain State Park is a short drive away.

Conclusion

Suffern is an excellent destination for travelers seeking a small-town experience with plenty of activities and attractions. Go ahead and dive into the rich history, scenic beauty, delectable cuisine, and warm hospitality of this charming town.
