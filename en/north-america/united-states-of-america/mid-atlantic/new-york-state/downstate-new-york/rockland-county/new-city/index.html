Introduction to New City

Located in Rockland County, New City is a beautiful town in Downstate New York. It is known for its rich history, natural beauty, and adventurous outdoor activities. This guide will help you discover the best things to do and see in New City and help to make your trip a memorable one.

List of Places to Visit in New City

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

New City is rich with museums and historical sites that highlight the town’s history. Some of the top attractions include:

  • Historical Society of Rockland County: The Historical Society offers a fascinating look into the town’s past with exhibits and programs focusing on local architecture, art, and culture.

  • Bear Mountain State Park: This stunning park has plenty of beautiful trails which lead to some of the most jaw-dropping views of the Hudson River and the surrounding mountains.

  • Rockland Center for the Arts: This venue offers an outstanding look into the world of visual arts. With unique display of exhibits from various artists, this is a must-visit spot in New City!

  • The Kingsland Manor: History buffs will enjoy a visit to this beautifully preserved estate which dates back to the 1700s. It offers tours and events throughout the year to give visitors a brief of the town’s rich history.

Natural Attractions of New City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For the nature enthusiasts, New City has a lot of offer in terms of natural beauty and adventures. Below are some of the top recommendations for outdoor activities:

  • Hudson River: The beautiful Hudson River offers a wide range of activities from water sports to hiking and biking on scenic routes.

  • Nyack Beach State Park: Surrounded by lush trees and bushes, the Nyack Beach State Park has a great sandy beach, and trails with oceanside vistas for hikers, cyclists and picnickers.

  • High Tor State Park: High Tor State Park is cherished for its rushing streams, picturesque waterfalls, and picturesque views of the Hudson valley. Hikers can opt for guided tours from locals.

  • Croton Gorge Park: With plenty of trails, fishing spots, and picnic areas- Croton Gorge Park is a perfect spot for families and picnickers alike.

Hiking/Biking Routes of New City

New City has a variety of serene and well-kept hiking trails for long and short hikes. Here are some great trails you can enjoy if you are an adventure buff:

  • Rockland Lake State Park: Located in the stunning Hudson Valley, Rockland Lake State Park is a great place for hiking and offers some of the most scenic views of all Rockland County. You can also opt for cycling and boating activities here.

  • South Mountain Road: South Mountain Road is a popular destination for hikers and nature enthusiasts. South Mountain Road has various hiking trails and streams to enjoy.

  • Bear Mountain: Bear Mountain is a less strenuous hike and boasts fantastic views of the surrounding countryside. It offers hikers an awe-inspiring view of Hudson River and the Bear Mountain Bridge.

Best Things to Do in New City: Activities and Tours

There’s plenty of things to do and see in New City. From exploring historical sites to wine tasting, here are some recommendations on what to experience in New City:

  • Wine Tasting Tours: Rockland County offers an array of wineries and vineyards to explore. Book a wine tour to experience the palpable essence of New City’s wine country.

  • Historical Tours: Hop on a guided tour to allow explorers to delve deeper into New City’s rich history and modern architecture.

  • Neversink River Unique Area: Anyone interested in fishing will love fishing on Neversink River. Rent some gear, book a guided tour, and enjoy the tranquillity of the river.

Food and Drink Scene of New City

New City has a great food scene offering cuisines from around the world. Here are some dishes and local beverages you must try:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pierogi Tacos: A unique and delicious blend of Polish and Mexican cuisine and is available in many cafes and restaurants around town.

  • Crittenden Hill Chicken: If you like your main course with a side of local history, Crittenden Hill Chicken is the perfect one to try. It’s inspired by the battle that took place near the hill during the revolutionary war.

  • Lobster Roll: A New England speciality which can be found in all the best seafood restaurants around town.

Local Beverages

  • Hudson Valley Wine: The Hudson Valley has a thriving collection of wineries where visitors can soak up the local flavours and learn about the wine-making process.

  • New City Ale: If you’re a beer lover then New City Ale should be on your list to try out. You can find this locally brewed beer in almost every bar and restaurant in the town.

Where to Stay in New City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

New City has a comfortable range of accommodation for visitors. Here are some options you can consider:

  • The Time Nyack: A beautifully designed hotel with modern amenities, it is a perfect spot to explore the stunning Hudson Valley.

  • HNA Palisades Premier Conference Center: Built in Georgian-style, the HNA Palisades Premier Conference center offers the perfect retreat for travelers looking for comfort and hospitality.

  • Hilton Pearl River: The Hilton Pearl River is a traditional, family-friendly hotel with a 24-hour fitness center, sauna, and outdoor pool.

What to Buy in New City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

New City’s quaint shops and boutiques offer unique souvenirs for travelers to bring back home. Here are some souvenirs to check out during your stay:

  • Hudson Valley Wine: Bring back the locally made wines home with you and enjoy the local flavors even after your visit.

  • Local Honey: For a sweet memento of New City, seek out some of the local honey.

  • New City Memorabilia: The town has many souvenir shops that sell wall arts and other gift items showcasing the town’s rich history and art.

Local Culture and Customs of New City

New City is a charming town with a rich history, where visitors are welcomed with open arms. It’s important to remember to respect the local culture and customs. Here are some things to keep in mind when visiting:

  • Dress Appropriately: Dressing modestly, especially when entering religious sites, is considered respectful.

  • Be Polite and Courteous: Politeness and respect go a long way in any culture. Be sure to practice good manners when interacting with locals.

  • Tip for Good Service: It is customary to tip wait staff and other service providers if you are happy with their services.

Best Time to Visit New City

The best time to visit New City is during the spring (April - June) and the summer( June-August) season. During this time, the nature looks its best and there are plenty of outdoor activities to explore.

Do’s and Don’ts of New City

Do:

  • Respect Local Culture: As in any travel destination, it’s important to show respect for the local culture to foster positive relationships with locals.

  • Take Care of Environment and Wildlife: When heading out on outdoor activities, be sure to leave the environment undisturbed and not harm the wildlife.

  • Ask for Directions: If you’re lost or can’t locate a destination, don’t be afraid to ask locals for directions.

Don’t:

  • Leave Litter: Be sure to dispose of your trash in designated bins or take it with you when leaving a picnic or outdoor spot.

  • Argue with Locals: If you find yourself in a disagreement with a local, remain calm and respectful to avoid any unpleasant situations

Safety and Health

Safety in New City

Overall, New City is a safe destination. However, visitors should take the usual precautions, such as:

  • Awareness in Crowded Streets: Always be aware of your surroundings in crowded areas

  • Protect Yourself from the Sun: During hot weather, be sure to wear sunscreen and a hat to protect against harmful UV rays.

Health in New City

New City has an average health rating. It offers many medical facilities and some of the best medical professionals in the state.

Getting to New City: Transportation Options and Logistics

New City is conveniently located and accessible by a variety of transportation options. Here are some of the most popular options:

Airports Near New City

  • John F. Kennedy International Airport: This international airport is the closest to New City and is about 70 miles away.

  • LaGuardia Airport: LaGuardia is about 60 miles away and offers a variety of international and domestic flights.

Bus Stations Near New City

  • Coach USA: The Coach USA bus service is available from the Port Authority Bus Terminal in New York City.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Renting a car is a good option to explore the countryside of New City.

  • Train: The nearest train station is the Nanuet Train Station where you can take a train to New York City or other nearby destinations.

How to Get Around in New City

With its organized public transportation and good road network, getting around in New City is easy. Taxis and buses are readily available. You can also opt for car rental services if you prefer to drive yourself.

Where to Go Next from New City: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

New City is located in an area of New York state rich with interesting towns and attractions. Here are some places you may want to visit if you have extra time:

  • Harlem Valley Rail Trail: The Harlem Valley Rail Trail stretches over 15 miles offering the perfect cycling or walking trail.

  • The Palisades Mall: If you’re looking to do some shopping, the Palisades Mall, located just fifteen minutes from New City, has a vast array of shops and retail outlets.

  • Tarrytown: Tarrytown, located just down the Hudson River from New City offers same amazing Hudson River views and historic landmarks.

Conclusion

New City offers a rich experience for visitors looking to explore the Hudson Valley’s natural beauty and history. With its rich culture, delicacies, outdoor adventures, and comfortable accommodation options, it’s no surprise that visitors flock to this amazing town. With this comprehensive guide, now you are well equipped to plan your perfect trip to New City!
