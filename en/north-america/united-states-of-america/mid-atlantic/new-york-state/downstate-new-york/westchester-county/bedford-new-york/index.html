Introduction to Bedford (New York)

Bedford is a picturesque town located in Westchester County, New York. It lies in the Downstate New York region in Mid-Atlantic, United States of America. The town is well-known for its cultural and natural attractions, making it an ideal destination for tourists looking for a peaceful getaway.

The town was founded in 1680 and has kept its historic charm intact, making it a popular spot for history buffs. With its scenic views, hiking and biking routes, museums, and delicious cuisine, Bedford has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Bedford (New York)

If you’re planning a trip to Bedford (New York), make sure you explore these exciting places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

John Jay Homestead State Historic Site

Located at 400 Jay St, Katonah, NY 10536, the John Jay Homestead State Historic Site is the ancestral home of one of the Founding Fathers of the United States, John Jay. The beautiful mansion and its sprawling gardens are a testament to Jay’s legacy.

Bedford-Arms Hotel and Inn

The Bedford-Arms Hotel and Inn is a historic landmark constructed in 1812. It’s located at 640 Old Post Rd, Bedford, NY, and is a prime example of Federal-style architecture.

The Katonah Museum of Art

The Katonah Museum of Art is a non-profit institution that exhibits traditional and contemporary art. It’s located at 134 Jay St, Katonah, NY and is worth a visit for its fascinating exhibitions that change throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of Bedford (New York): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Ward Pound Ridge Reservation

Ward Pound Ridge Reservation is Westchester’s largest park and covers over 4,315 acres. Visitors can hike, bike, or horseback ride through miles of scenic trails, take a picnic and enjoy the best views in the area.

Caramoor Center for Music and the Arts

Caramoor Center for Music and the Arts is located at 149 Girdle Ridge Rd, Katonah, NY 10536. It’s a stunning estate that hosts year-round musical performances, art exhibits, and garden tours.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bedford (New York)

For nature lovers, Bedford (New York) offers various hiking and biking routes to explore the outdoors. The best routes include:

  • The Bedford Riding Lanes Association
  • The Indian Hill Trail
  • The Westmoreland Sanctuary Trail System

Food and Drink Scene of Bedford (New York)

When in Bedford (New York), make sure to try the following local dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Bedford Ham – A dry-cured ham from Bedford that’s been hailed as one of the best in the world.
  • Bedford Cheese Shop – A specialty cheese shop that offers a variety of locally-sourced cheeses that you can try in-store or take home.

Local Beverages

  • Bedlam Vodka – Produced by a Katonah-based distillery, this vodka is made from 100% New York grains.

Where to Stay in Bedford (New York): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are various options when it comes to accommodations in Bedford (New York). Here are a few choices to consider:

  • Bedford Post Inn – A charming hotel housed in a restored 1700s building.
  • Brickhouse Bed and Breakfast – An award-winning bed and breakfast that offers true New England charm.
  • The Hopp Ground – A newly opened hotel that offers stunning views of the area.

What to Buy in Bedford (New York): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re searching for souvenirs and local products, make sure to check out the Bedford Cheese Shop. Additionally, you can buy a Bedford Ham to bring the local ham home with you.

Local Culture and Customs of Bedford (New York)

Bedford (New York) is a small town that takes pride in maintaining its rich history and preserving its natural resources. To show respect for the town’s culture and customs, dress modestly when visiting religious sites, keep noise down after dark, and always be courteous to locals.

Best Time to Visit Bedford (New York)

The best time to visit Bedford (New York) is between June and September when the weather is pleasant, and the outdoor activities are at their best. Additionally, autumn in Bedford is breathtaking when the leaves start to change their color.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bedford (New York)

Do:

  • Enjoy the scenic views on the hiking and biking trails.
  • Visit the farmers’ market.
  • Respect the town’s culture and customs.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage local wildlife.
  • Speak loudly or cause disturbances in public places.
  • Ignore traffic and parking rules.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bedford (New York)

Bedford is a safe town with low rates of crime. However, it’s always wise to be aware of your surroundings and keep an eye on your belongings, especially in crowded areas.

Health in Bedford (New York)

Bedford has a healthcare system that offers a range of medical facilities, including hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies. The town’s tap water is safe to drink, but visitors may want to stick to bottled water if facing nausea or digestive problems while traveling.

Getting to Bedford (New York): Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bedford (New York)

The closest airports to Bedford (New York) are:

  • Westchester County Airport (HPN) – about 30 minutes away
  • LaGuardia Airport (LGA) – about 1 hour and 15 minutes away
  • John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK) – about 1 hour and 30 minutes away

Bus Stations Near Bedford (New York)

There are various buses available from New York City that go through Bedford.

Other Options

If you’re already landed at JFK or LGA, you can take a train to Bedford.

How to Get Around in Bedford (New York)

The best way to get around Bedford (New York) is by car. While there are local buses, they may not run at convenient times for tourists.

Where to Go Next from Bedford (New York): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Storm King Art Center

Conclusion

Bedford (New York) is an ideal destination for tourists looking for a peaceful getaway that offers a mix of culture and nature. With its scenic views, hiking and biking trails, museums, and delicious cuisine, it’s sure to impress any traveler looking to explore what the United States has to offer.
