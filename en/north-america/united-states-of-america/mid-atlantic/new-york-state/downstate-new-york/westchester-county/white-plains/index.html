Introduction to White Plains

If you’re looking for a perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of New York City, White Plains might just be the destination for you. Located in Westchester County, just 25 miles north of Manhattan, White Plains is a charming city. Named after the white balsam tree, this city has a lot to offer to all types of travelers.

List of Places to Visit in White Plains

Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor adventurer, a foodie, or a culture seeker, there’s something for everyone in White Plains. Here’s a list of must-visit places in White Plains.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The White Plains Historical Society and Museum – This museum showcases the history of White Plains, including its role in the American Revolution.
  • Jacob Purdy House and Burial Grounds – This historical site was built in the late 18th century and is a testament to the city’s rich history.
  • The Westchester Philharmonic – Head to the symphony and experience the unique cultural ambiance.

Natural Attractions of White Plains: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Saxon Woods Park – A perfect spot for outdoor activities such as hiking, swimming and golfing.
  • Kensico Dam Plaza – A must-visit park for families, providing trails, puppey park, a playground and a glistening lake.
  • Silver Lake Preserve – Walk through the trails and experience nature’s beauty at its best.

Hiking/Biking Routes of White Plains

White Plains has a beautiful landscape that is perfect for hiking/biking. The city’s trails give you a chance to explore local flora and fauna.

  • Bronx River Greenway Trail – This paved trail spans approximately 23 miles and offers views of the Bronx River and beautiful landscapes.
  • Rockefeller State Park Preserve – This park is a perfect destination for family picnics and hiking trips with its extensive network of walking and hiking trails.
  • North County Trailway – This 22-mile trailway used to be a railway and is a beautiful path for biker riders, runners, and walkers.

Best Things to Do in White Plains: Activities and Tours

  • Take a tour of the Kykuit Estate – This historic property, once owned by John D. Rockefeller, offers stunning gardens and a stunning inside view of the modern houses of the Rockefeller family.
  • Head out to Westchester County Center – Here you can watch live events such as basketball, wrestling, circus, and hockey.
  • Visit the Galleria at White Plains – An shopping center with over 150 stores.

Week-Long Itinerary for White Plains

Day 1: Visit the White Plains Historical Society and Museum, then dine at Sonora Restaurant.

Day 2: Head out to Saxon Woods Park for hiking and golfing.

Day 3: Take a tour of Kykuit Estate and enjoy magnificent scenery.

Day 4: Spend the day shopping at The Galleria at White Plains.

Day 5: Take a drive through the scenic views of Rockfeller Park Reserve.

Day 6: Take a family picnic at the Silver Lake Preserve.

Day 7: Spend a lazy day listening to the symphony orchestra at The Westchester Philharmonic.

Food and Drink Scene of White Plains

From world-renowned restaurants to local bistros, White Plains has a plethora of food options to choose from. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in White Plains.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chicken Parmigiana from Amore Pizza – A classic Italian dish, this pizza parlor serves great pizza that you’ll fall in love with.
  • Peri-Peri Chicken from Nando’s Peri-Peri – Made to perfection with a little bit of spice and everything nice.
  • New England Clam Chowder from Legal Sea Foods – A savoring soup made with a mix of spice and clams.
  • Ribs from Ribs on the Run – Soft and juicy ribs with great BBQ seasoning? Sounds good to us

Local Beverages

  • Local Breweries – Yonkers Brewery, Captain Lawrence Brewery, and Sing Sing Kill Brewery.
  • Apple Cider from Wilkens Fruit and Fir Farm – A historic spot with great cider and a farm to explore.

Where to Stay in White Plains: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

White Plains has many options for accommodation that cater to all budgets and preferences.

  • Ritz-Carlton Westchester – A luxury hotel located in the heart of downtown White Plains offering spectacular views and impeccable service.
  • Cambria Hotel - This lodging offers sleek on-site dining options and impressive meeting facilities in convenient proximity to downtown White Plains.
  • The Esplanade Hotel – This guesthouse offers cozy and comfortable accommodation at an affordable price.

What to Buy in White Plains: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When it comes to souvenirs, White Plains offers a range of options for all types of travelers. Some of the items that you must buy before you leave the White Plains include specialist white wines, organic apple cider, and artisanal jams. Visit “The Westchester” mall, where you can find a large variety of luxury goods and boutique shopping.

Local Culture and Customs of White Plains

White Plains is a welcoming city that values diversity and inclusion. It is customary to greet people with a friendly, “Hi, How’s it going?” and enjoy the many outdoor facilities the city has to offer.

Best Time to Visit White Plains

Summer is the best time to visit White Plains. The weather is warm, and there are many outdoor activities to enjoy. If you want to escape the humid summers, Spring is also a great time to visit.

Do’s and Don’ts of White Plains

Do:

  • Come Prepared for outdoor activities and bring suitable clothing.
  • Respect cultural and religious customs.
  • Plan to visit nearby destinations like Greenwich and Stamford.
  • Try to merge into the communities of White Plains to experience the true spirit of the city

Don’t:

  • Commit road rules violations or generally drive recklessly.
  • Litter the city or disrespect historical monuments.
  • Use derogatory language towards the city’s rich cultural diversity.

Safety and Health

Safety in White Plains

White Plains is generally a very safe city to visit. The area offers a good quality of life, and the crime rate is low. However, that doesn’t mean that visitors should lower their guard. It’s always important to be aware of your surroundings and take precautions when it comes to personal safety and security.

Health in White Plains

White Plains has a range of medical facilities available, including White Plains Hospital and Westchester Medical Center.

Getting to White Plains: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near White Plains

  • Westchester County Airport – The airport is located just eight miles from the city of White Plains.

Bus Stations Near White Plains

  • White Plains Bus Station – Located in the downtown area and offering intercity and local transport services.

Other Options

  • Metro North Railroad – There are two Metro-North stations in White Plains, a great option for visitors traveling from New York City.

How to Get Around in White Plains

Public buses, trains, and taxis are all available to get around White Plains. The city also has a bike-sharing program called LimeBike that allows you to get around the city on a budget.

Where to Go Next from White Plains: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re planning a longer trip, there are many amazing destinations nearby that you can visit from White Plains, including:

  • Stamford, Connecticut – A diverse city located just 25 miles from White Plains with plenty of attractions like Stamford Museum & Nature Center and Chelsea Piers Stamford.
  • Greenwich, Connecticut – The famed Town is home to several historic sites and luxury shopping, often referred to as the “Hamptons of Connecticut”.

Conclusion

White Plains is a delightful city filled with activities, attractions, and a range of food options to indulge in. Visitors to this city must explore the many parks and natural landscapes. White Plains is a city for all types of travelers; whether you’re traveling solo or with family, there’s something for everyone. Explore and experience all that the city of White Plains has to offer today!
