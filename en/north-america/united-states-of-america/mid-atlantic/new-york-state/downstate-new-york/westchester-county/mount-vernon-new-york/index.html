Introduction to Mount Vernon (New York)

Located in Westchester County in Downstate New York, Mount Vernon is a small city with a rich cultural and historical heritage. It is known for its beautiful parks, natural attractions, and vibrant food and drink scene. Mount Vernon is a perfect destination for travelers who are looking for an authentic and off-the-beaten-path travel experience.

List of Places to Visit in Mount Vernon (New York)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • St. Paul’s Church National Historic Site - Built in 1765, this church serves as a museum and national historic site. It is a must-visit for history buffs.
  • Mount Vernon Hotel Museum and Garden - An 18th-century building that houses a museum showcasing life in the early 19th century.
  • Hutchinson River Parkway - A scenic drive that showcases the beautiful surroundings of Mount Vernon.

Natural Attractions of Mount Vernon (New York): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Wilson’s Woods Park - This park features over 23 acres of forest, a lake, and various outdoor amenities.
  • Roosevelt Square Park - A small urban park that features basketball and tennis courts.
  • Hart’s Brook Park and Preserve - A nature preserve with over 170 acres of hardwood forests and wetlands. It offers various hiking trails and birdwatching opportunities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mount Vernon (New York)

  • Bronx River Parkway Trail - A 9-mile paved trail that passes through Mount Vernon and other nearby towns. It is perfect for both biking and walking.
  • Ward Pound Ridge Reservation - A 4,315-acre park that offers various hiking trails and scenic views.

Best Things to Do in Mount Vernon (New York): Activities and Tours

  • Outdoor Yoga Classes - Enjoy a rejuvenating yoga session in the beautiful parks of Mount Vernon.
  • Local Food Tours - Explore the local food scene and taste some of the best dishes in the city.
  • City Walking Tours - Get to know the history and culture of Mount Vernon, guided by expert local guides.

Food and Drink Scene of Mount Vernon (New York)

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pizza - Mount Vernon is known for its delicious pizza, with local favorites such as Johnny’s Pizzeria and Pizza Rock.
  • Soul Food - Mount Vernon is home to some of the best soul food restaurants in the state, including The Bayou and Lolo’s Seafood Shack.
  • Italian Cuisine - With a significant Italian population, Mount Vernon boasts several Italian restaurants serving traditional dishes such as pasta and meatballs, chicken parmigiana, and more.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beer - Mount Vernon has a growing craft beer scene with local breweries such as Gun Hill Brewing Company and Broken Bow Brewery.
  • Wine and Spirits - Mount Vernon is just a short drive from the Hudson Valley region, known for its wine and spirits.

Where to Stay in Mount Vernon (New York): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Bronx Guest House - A budget-friendly guesthouse located in the Bronx, just a few minutes’ walk from Mount Vernon.
  • Ramada by Wyndham - A mid-range hotel located in the heart of Mount Vernon.
  • Renaissance Westchester Hotel - A luxury hotel located just a short drive from Mount Vernon, offering stunning views and upscale amenities.

What to Buy in Mount Vernon (New York): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Mount Vernon Hoodies and T-Shirts - Show your Mount Vernon pride with a hoodie or t-shirt.
  • Local Craft Beer - Bring home a taste of Mount Vernon with local craft beer from Gun Hill Brewing Company and Broken Bow Brewery.
  • Soul Food Spices - Take home the taste of Mount Vernon with a selection of soul food spices and sauces.

Local Culture and Customs of Mount Vernon (New York)

Mount Vernon is a diverse and vibrant city with a rich cultural heritage. The city’s history showcases the stories of many different communities, including African American, Italian, and Puerto Rican cultures.

Best Time to Visit Mount Vernon (New York)

The best time to visit Mount Vernon is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny. However, Mount Vernon is a year-round destination with activities and events happening throughout the year.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mount Vernon (New York)

Do:

  • Explore the local food scene.
  • Take part in outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and yoga.
  • Visit the local museums and historic sites.

Don’t:

  • Leave your belongings unattended in public places.
  • Wear revealing clothing while visiting religious and cultural sites.
  • Be disrespectful to the local culture and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Mount Vernon (New York)

Mount Vernon is a safe destination for travelers, but it is always advisable to use common sense and take necessary precautions. It is recommended that visitors avoid walking alone at night and keep valuables out of sight.

Health in Mount Vernon (New York)

The health services in Mount Vernon are of good quality and easily accessible. There are several hospitals and medical clinics located in the city, providing medical care to both residents and visitors.

Getting to Mount Vernon (New York): Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Mount Vernon (New York)

  • John F. Kennedy International Airport - Located in Queens, about an hour’s drive from Mount Vernon.
  • LaGuardia Airport - Located in Queens, about 45 minutes’ drive from Mount Vernon.

Bus Stations Near Mount Vernon (New York)

  • Yonkers Bus Terminal - Located in Yonkers, about 10 minutes’ drive from Mount Vernon.
  • Bronx Bus Depot - Located in the Bronx, about 20 minutes’ drive from Mount Vernon.

Other Options

  • Car Rental - Several car rental agencies operate in the area, providing visitors with the option to rent a car and explore Mount Vernon on their own.
  • Uber/Lyft - Ride-sharing services such as Uber and Lyft are available in Mount Vernon.

How to Get Around in Mount Vernon (New York)

Mount Vernon is a small city that can easily be explored on foot or by bike. Renting a car is also an option for those who prefer to drive. Public transportation options, including bus and train services, are available for those who need them.

Where to Go Next from Mount Vernon (New York): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • New York City - Just a short train ride away, New York City offers endless opportunities to explore and experience the city that never sleeps.
  • Hudson Valley - A scenic region known for its natural beauty, charming towns, and historic sites.
  • Bear Mountain State Park - A beautiful park located just a short drive from Mount Vernon, offering hiking trails, picnic areas, and stunning views.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or delicious food, Mount Vernon has something for everyone. Follow this travel guide to make the most of your visit to this beautiful and vibrant city in Westchester County.
