Introduction to Purchase

Purchase is a vibrant hamlet, located in Westchester County, Downstate New York. It is known for its natural beauty, top-rated attractions, and rich cultural heritage. The region is home to many well-established international companies, including MasterCard, PepsiCo, and Atlas Air.

List of Places to Visit in Purchase

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Neuberger Museum of Art showcases contemporary art exhibitions throughout the year. It houses over 7,500 artworks of American, African, and Latin American artists.
  • The Donald M. Kendall Sculpture Gardens offers a unique and stunning collection of sculptures from around the world. Visitors can explore winding paths that lead to interesting and hidden garden spots, with sculptures placed throughout.
  • The Manhattanville College is home to Reid Castle, a medieval-style castle built in the late 1800s that is now on the National Register of Historic Places.

Natural Attractions of Purchase: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Purchase College Farm is a student-run farm that’s open to visitors and sells local produce, flowers, and other farm products.
  • The Kensico Reservoir offers a beautiful and relaxing atmosphere for picnics, fishing, and hiking. It has stunning views of the surrounding mountain ranges.
  • The Rockefeller State Park Preserve offers 1,233 acres of natural woodland and wetland habitats with beautiful, scenic trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Purchase

  • The Appalachian Trail passes through the eastern edge of Purchase. It is a part of the longest hiking-only footpath in the world and offers a beautiful experience of the region.
  • The Cass Gallagher Nature Trail loop is a 1.5-mile loop trail that is suitable for all ages and attracts many visitors.

Best Things to Do in Purchase: Activities and Tours

  • The Ice Hutch is a popular ice-skating rink that has public skate sessions, hockey games, and figure skating lessons.
  • Visit nearby White Plains, for shopping in the upscale boutiques, dining in world-class restaurants, or watching a movie in the cinema screens.

Week-Long Itinerary for Purchase

Monday Visit the Neuberger Museum of Art and spend the afternoon hiking in the Rockefeller State Park Preserve

Food and Drink Scene of Purchase

Must-Try Dishes

  • Try the burgers at Bobby’s Burger Palace, Iron Tomato, and other local burger joints, which are said to be the best in the region.
  • The Young Shanghai restaurant has the best Chinese cuisine, and the Polpettina is the perfect place for pizzas and Italian food.

Local Beverages

  • Visit Squire’s of Briarcliff, a local brewery that has an extensive range of IPAs and seasonal brews.
  • The Harrison Wine Vault has a vast selection of wines from around the world, and they offer wine-tasting events.

Where to Stay in Purchase: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Crowne Plaza Hotel is a popular choice, located just a few minutes from the Neuberger Museum of Art and a ten-minute drive from the White Plains Metro-North train station.
  • Doral Arrowwood Resort offers luxurious amenities, including a pool, spa, and golf course.

What to Buy in Purchase: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Gift yourself or a loved one an artwork from the Neuberger Museum of Art.
  • Visit the Purchase College Farm and shop for the organic produce, flowers, and other farm products.

Local Culture and Customs of Purchase

Purchase prides itself on its diverse cultural heritage. Respect the culture and customs of the region by being courteous and friendly to everyone you meet.

Best Time to Visit Purchase

Late spring and early fall are the best times to visit Purchase, as the weather is mild and perfect for hiking and outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Purchase

Do:

  • Do try local beer and wines to take in the authentic flavor of Purchase.
  • Do respect the local culture and natural environment.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in public areas. Purchase has strict policies against littering and you could be fined.
  • Don’t be rude or disrespectful to anyone you meet.

Safety and Health

Safety in Purchase

Purchase is a relatively safe region, but it would be best if you exercised common sense and precautions such as locking your belongings in your car and being cautious when hiking the trails alone.

Health in Purchase

The region is home to many well-established medical facilities, including the Purchase Community House and the White Plains Hospital.

Getting to Purchase: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Purchase

Westchester County Airport is the closest airport to Purchase. It is a thirty-minute drive from the town center.

Bus Stations Near Purchase

The Bee-Line Bus operates a regular service to Purchase from White Plains, which has both trains to NYC and buses to regional destinations.

Other Options

Purchase is a well-connected town, with well-maintained roads that make it easy to navigate with your vehicle.

How to Get Around in Purchase

The ordinary commute in Purchase is by car. You can also rent a car or use taxis or ride-share services like Uber or Lyft to travel around.

Where to Go Next from Purchase: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Go for a relaxing day at the bustling Greenwich Avenue.
  • Take a wander through the Bronx Zoo, one of the largest metropolitan zoos in the world, located just thirty minutes from Purchase.

Conclusion

Travel to Purchase, New York, and discover the stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and top-rated attractions. Indulge in local cuisine, shop for farm-fresh produce, and explore the region’s history and culture. Start planning your trip to Purchase today!
