Introduction to Hammondsport

Hammondsport is a charming village tucked away in the Western Finger Lakes region of New York. It is situated on the southern tip of Keuka Lake, one of the eleven Finger Lakes in the state. Hammondsport is known for its rolling hills, wineries, and historical significance. This guide will take you on a tour of the must-visit places and activities in Hammondsport, along with some useful tips to make your trip a memorable one.

List of Places to Visit in Hammondsport

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Glenn H. Curtiss Museum

Located in the heart of Hammondsport, the Glenn H. Curtiss Museum is a must-visit for aviation enthusiasts. It is dedicated to the life and legacy of Glenn Curtiss, who was a pioneer in aviation and motorcycle manufacturing. The museum houses a vast collection of vintage airplanes, motorcycles, and boats that highlight Curtiss’s accomplishments.

Finger Lakes Boating Museum

This museum showcases the rich boating heritage of the Finger Lakes region. The Finger Lakes Boating Museum features various exhibits, including boats, motors, and marinas, highlighting the evolution of boating and its impact on the local economy.

Natural Attractions of Hammondsport: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Keuka Lake State Park

Keuka Lake State Park is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. The park offers numerous recreational activities like hiking, fishing, swimming, and boating. It includes two stunning natural waterfalls, accessible by hiking trails.

Bully Hill Vineyards

Bully Hill Vineyards is a popular winery in the area, known for its award-winning wines and beautiful views. Visitors can enjoy a wine tasting, stroll through the vineyards, or even have a picnic with a panoramic view of the surrounding vineyards.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hammondsport

Hammondsport offers various hiking and biking trails that showcase its natural beauty. The region is hilly terrain, which can be challenging for some, so be sure to plan accordingly.

Keuka Lake Outlet Trail

This trail is an easy stroll through the beautiful countryside. The trail follows the Keuka Lake Outlet, which was once used to transport goods like coal and lumber.

Pinnacle State Park

The park offers a network of trails, each with a different level of difficulty. The trails offer sweeping views of the surrounding countryside, including Keuka Lake and the neighboring towns.

Best Things to Do in Hammondsport: Activities and Tours

Wine Tours

The Finger Lakes region is known for its wineries, and Hammondsport is no exception. Visitors can enjoy a variety of wine tours with options such as open-air buses and limousines. The tours often include stops at multiple wineries to give visitors a taste of the local wine culture.

Water Sports

There are many opportunities for water sports on Keuka Lake, including canoeing, kayaking, paddleboarding, and boating. Rentals are available at several locations around the lake.

Week-Long Itinerary for Hammondsport

Day 1: Arrive in Hammondsport and explore downtown. Day 2: Visit the Glenn H. Curtiss Museum, followed by a wine tasting at Bully Hill Vineyards. Day 3: Hike the Keuka Lake Outlet Trail. Day 4: Visit the Finger Lakes Boating Museum. Day 5: Take a leisurely day on the lake or another favorite activity. Day 6: Visit Pinnacle State Park for hiking and scenic views. Day 7: Wrap up your trip with a visit to a nearby town.

Food and Drink Scene of Hammondsport

Must-Try Dishes

Beef on Weck Sandwich

This sandwich is a regional specialty consisting of thinly sliced roast beef served on a Kimmelweck roll, which is a round roll topped with salt and caraway seeds.

Grape Pie

Made from locally grown grapes, this dessert is a must-try for anyone visiting the Finger Lakes region. The famous grape pie is available at several restaurants in the area.

Local Beverages

Wine

Wine is the most popular drink in the Finger Lakes region, with over 100 wineries in the area. Visitors can sample various wines, including Rieslings, Cabernet Franc, and Chardonnay.

Beer

The region’s craft beer scene has been growing in recent years, and visitors can now enjoy some local brews at several microbreweries in the area.

Where to Stay in Hammondsport: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Best Western Plus The Hammondsport Hotel

This hotel is located in the heart of Hammondsport, offering convenient access to attractions, restaurants, and shops.

Black Sheep Inn and Spa

This luxurious inn features charming rooms and suites, a spa, and a restaurant serving farm-to-table cuisine.

What to Buy in Hammondsport: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Keuka Lake Wine

The region produces some of the best wines in New York, making bottles of wine a great souvenir or gift to bring back home.

Jewellery

Several local jewelry stores sell beautifully crafted pieces inspired by the surrounding natural beauty.

Local Culture and Customs of Hammondsport

Hammondsport is a friendly, welcoming village with a rich history and a close-knit community. Visitors are expected to be respectful of the local culture and customs, and manners are highly valued.

Best Time to Visit Hammondsport

The best time to visit Hammondsport is during the summer and fall when the weather is pleasant, and the leaves change their colors. The busiest times are from June to August and during the fall foliage season from September to November.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hammondsport

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Take in the stunning scenery and natural beauty.
  • Try local wine and food.

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave waste behind.
  • Be loud and disruptive.
  • Ignore the community’s safety guidelines.

Safety and Health

Safety in Hammondsport

Hammondsport is generally a safe place to visit, but travelers should still exercise caution. Petty theft, such as pickpocketing, can occur in tourist areas.

Health in Hammondsport

The village has a small hospital and a pharmacy. Visitors with pre-existing medical conditions should ensure they have adequate supplies before traveling. Emergency services are available by dialing 911.

Getting to Hammondsport: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Hammondsport

The nearest airports are Elmira, Rochester, and Syracuse. Private and charter flights can land at the Elmira Corning Regional Airport.

Bus Stations Near Hammondsport

The closest bus stations are in Bath and Elmira, serviced by Greyhound and Trailways.

Other Options

Travelers can also reach Hammondsport by car or train. There are several car rental companies and Amtrak stations in nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Hammondsport

Hammondsport is a small village, and most attractions are within walking distance of one another. Bicycles are available for rent at several locations.

Conclusion

Hammondsport is a hidden gem in the Finger Lakes region and offers endless possibilities for adventure and relaxation. Plan your visit today and make sure to explore the rich cultural heritage and natural beauty of this charming village.
