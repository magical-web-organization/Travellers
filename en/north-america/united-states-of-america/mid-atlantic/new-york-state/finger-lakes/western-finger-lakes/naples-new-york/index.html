Introduction to Naples (New York)

Naples is a charming town located in the beautiful Finger Lakes region of New York State, in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States. Naples is famous for its stunning natural beauty, outdoor recreational activities, and its rich history and culture. The town has a population of just under 1,200 and is home to several unique and exciting attractions that are definitely worth checking out.

List of Places to Visit in Naples (New York)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Cumming Nature Center: This 900-acre nature preserve is perfect for hiking, birdwatching, and enjoying the beautiful scenery of the region. The center also houses a museum that showcases the local flora and fauna of the Finger Lakes region.

  • Inspire Moore Winery and Vineyard: This winery is Naples’ premier destination for wine enthusiasts. The winery offers a variety of wines, including Chardonnay, Riesling, and Cabernet Franc, and also has a beautiful outdoor patio where you can relax and enjoy the stunning views of the vineyards.

  • Mill Art Center and Gallery: This unique art center showcases the work of local and regional artists. The center also has several galleries, a gift shop, and a studio that offers various art classes and workshops.

Natural Attractions of Naples (New York): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Grimes Glen: This gorgeous park is situated along Naples Creek and is home to several waterfalls and stunning natural rock formations. Visitors can hike the park’s many trails and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

  • Bristol Mountain: This ski resort is located just outside of Naples and offers several opportunities for skiing, snowboarding, and other winter activities. The resort also has several trails for hiking and biking during the summer months.

  • Conesus Lake: This beautiful lake is located just a short drive from Naples and is perfect for fishing, boating, and swimming. There are several parks along the lake that offer picnic areas, hiking trails, and playgrounds.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Naples (New York)

Naples is a great destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. There are several trails and parks in the region that offer beautiful views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Some popular hiking and biking routes include:

  • High Tor Wildlife Management Area: This 6,100-acre wildlife management area is perfect for hiking, fishing, and hunting. The park also has several trails for biking and hiking.

  • Mendon Ponds Park: This park is located just outside of Naples and is home to several hiking and biking trails. The park also has several picnic areas, playgrounds, and a nature center.

Best Things to Do in Naples (New York): Activities and Tours

  • Grape Pie Trail: This self-guided tour takes visitors through the picturesque countryside of Naples and showcases the area’s famous grape pies. The tour includes several stops at local bakeries and vineyards.

  • Wine Tasting Tour: Naples is home to several world-renowned wineries and vineyards. Taking a wine tasting tour is a great way to explore the area and learn about the local wine industry.

  • Hot Air Balloon Rides: Hot air balloon rides are a unique and exciting way to see the Finger Lakes region. Several companies in Naples offer hot air balloon tours.

Week-Long Itinerary for Naples (New York)

Here’s a week-long itinerary that highlights some of the best things to do and places to visit in Naples:

  • Day 1: Visit the Cumming Nature Center and hike the park’s trails.
  • Day 2: Explore the Mill Art Center and Gallery and take an art class.
  • Day 3: Take a wine tasting tour of the area’s vineyards.
  • Day 4: Visit Grimes Glen and hike the park’s trails.
  • Day 5: Spend the day skiing or snowboarding at Bristol Mountain.
  • Day 6: Take a hot air balloon ride over the Finger Lakes region.
  • Day 7: Explore Conesus Lake and enjoy water activities like fishing, boating, and swimming.

Food and Drink Scene of Naples (New York)

Must-Try Dishes

  • Grape Pie: Naples is famous for its grape pies, which are made from the area’s famous Concord grapes and have a sweet and tart flavor.

  • Chicken French: This local dish consists of chicken cutlets that are coated in an egg batter and sauteed with lemon butter.

  • Fried Dough: This delicious treat is a local favorite and is served at several bakeries around town.

Local Beverages

  • Finger Lakes Wines: Naples is home to several world-renowned wineries and vineyards that produce award-winning wines.

  • Genny Cream Ale: This locally brewed beer is a favorite among residents and visitors alike.

Where to Stay in Naples (New York): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Naples Hotel: This historic hotel is situated in the heart of Naples and offers beautifully appointed rooms and a cozy lounge where you can relax and unwind.

  • Bristol Harbour Resort: This luxurious resort is located just outside of Naples and offers stunning views of Canandaigua Lake. The resort has several restaurants, a spa, and a golf course.

What to Buy in Naples (New York): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Grape Pies: Naples’ famous grape pies are a must-buy souvenir for anyone visiting the area.

  • Local Wines and Beer: Finger Lakes wines and locally brewed beer are also great souvenirs to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Naples (New York)

Naples is a small town with a strong sense of community. Residents here are friendly and welcoming to tourists, and you’ll likely find yourself struck by the town’s charm and hospitality. One thing to note is that Naples is a dry town, meaning that there are no bars or liquor stores in the area.

Best Time to Visit Naples (New York)

The best time to visit Naples is during the summer months, when the weather is mild and pleasant and there are several outdoor recreational opportunities available. However, the fall months are also a great time to visit, as the trees in the region begin to change colors and the vineyards become a beautiful mosaic of oranges, reds, and golds.

Do’s and Don’ts of Naples (New York)

Do:

  • Do try the local grape pies and wine.
  • Do take advantage of the area’s beautiful natural scenery and go hiking, biking, or skiing.

Don’t:

  • Don’t expect to find any bars or liquor stores in Naples, as the town is dry.
  • Don’t leave without trying the chicken French!

Safety and Health

Safety in Naples (New York)

Naples is a safe town with very low crime rates. However, it’s always a good idea to take general safety precautions like locking your car and keeping your valuables safe.

Health in Naples (New York)

Naples has several medical clinics and hospitals in the area, so you’ll be able to get medical help if needed.

Getting to Naples (New York): Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Naples (New York)

  • Greater Rochester International Airport: This is the closest major airport to Naples, located about an hour’s drive away.

  • Elmira Corning Regional Airport: This airport is located about an hour and a half’s drive away.

Bus Stations Near Naples (New York)

  • Greyhound: There are several Greyhound bus stations located within a 30-minute drive of Naples.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Renting a car is a convenient and easy way to get around the area.

  • Private Car Services: Several private car services operate in the region and offer convenient and comfortable transportation options.

How to Get Around in Naples (New York)

One of the best ways to get around Naples is by car, as the town is spread out and there are several scenic drives in the area. However, there are also several bike and hiking trails that offer beautiful views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

Where to Go Next from Naples (New York): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Canandaigua: This charming town is located just a short drive from Naples and is home to several great wineries, restaurants, and outdoor recreational activities.

  • Watkins Glen: This beautiful town is situated at the southern end of Seneca Lake and is home to one of the most famous state parks in New York State.

Conclusion

There’s so much to see and do in Naples, New York. From exploring the area’s rich history and culture to hiking through stunning natural scenery, there’s something here for everyone. Plan your trip today and experience the beauty of this charming town for yourself!
