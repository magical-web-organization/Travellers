Introduction to Ellenville

Ellenville, located in Ulster County in the Catskills region of Upstate New York, is a beautiful town known for its rich history, natural beauty, and cultural offerings. From exploring museums and historical landmarks to hiking and biking through scenic trails, Ellenville offers a wide range of experiences for visitors seeking adventure, relaxation, or a perfect getaway destination.

List of Places to Visit in Ellenville

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Ellenville is rich in history and culture, and visitors can explore various landmarks and museums to know more about the town’s past. The following are some of the must-visit places:

  • Shadowland Theatre: Established in 1919, Shadowland Theatre is an iconic landmark known for its avant-garde productions as well as classic plays and musicals. It’s a perfect place to enjoy an evening of entertainment and cultural enrichment.
  • The Ellenville Public Library & Museum: The library’s museum section is a great place to learn about the town’s history, with exhibits about Ellenville’s industrial revolution and the D&H Canal.
  • Hungry Joe’s Vintage Lanes: Built in the 1950s, this rustic and retro bowling alley is a local favorite. It is a perfect spot to mingle with the locals and enjoy a game of bowling.

Natural Attractions of Ellenville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Ellenville is a perfect destination for adventure enthusiasts, with its hiking trails, waterfalls, and scenic views. Here are some of the must-visit natural attractions:

  • Sam’s Point Preserve: Located on the Shawangunk Ridge, this 5,400-acre nature preserve is home to unique plant and animal species, stunning rock formations, and panoramic views of the Hudson River Valley. Visitors can hike along the trails and explore the ice caves, waterfalls, and scenic overlooks.
  • Wawarsing Historical Trail: This 2.6-mile trail located on the old Delaware and Hudson Canal towpath offers scenic views of the Rondout Creek and majestic Shawangunk Mountains. It is a great spot for hiking, biking, and birdwatching.
  • Neversink Gorge and Multiple Use Area: This 6,500-acre wilderness area features a stunning gorge with steep cliffs, waterfalls, and rocky pools. It is a perfect spot for hiking, fishing, and camping.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ellenville

Ellenville has several hiking and biking trails, offering visitors the opportunity to explore the town’s natural beauty up close. Some of the popular trails include:

  • Shawangunk Ridge Trail: This 25.7-mile trail traverses the eastern ridge of the Shawangunk Mountains, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape. It is ideal for hikers, bikers, and trail runners.
  • Long Path Trail: This 350-mile trail stretches from New York City to Albany, passing through Ellenville. It offers scenic views of the Hudson River Valley and the Catskill Mountains.
  • Mountain House Trail: This 4.6-mile trail leads hikers through the scenic Shawangunk Mountains, featuring waterfalls, rock formations, and stunning vistas.

Best Things to Do in Ellenville: Activities and Tours

Ellenville offers plenty of exciting activities and tours suitable for visitors of all ages. Here are some of the best things to do in Ellenville:

  • Hang-gliding and Paragliding: Visitors can enjoy this unique experience by signing up for a hang-gliding or paragliding lesson in the Shawangunk Mountains.
  • Forestburgh Playhouse: This award-winning theater features a variety of musicals, plays, and comedy acts year-round.
  • Shawangunk Wine Trail: This scenic trail features several wineries and vineyards, offering visitors the opportunity to taste some of the best local wines.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ellenville

Day 1: Visit the Shadowland Theatre and enjoy a show. Day 2: Explore the Ellenville Public Library & Museum and learn about the town’s history.
Day 3: Hike at Sam’s Point Preserve and explore the ice caves and waterfalls. Day 4: Take a hang-gliding lesson and soar over the Shawangunk Mountains. Day 5: Visit the Wawarsing Historical Trail and enjoy scenic views of the Rondout Creek. Day 6: Taste local wine by visiting the Shawangunk Wine Trail’s vineyards and wineries. Day 7: Relax and recharge by camping at the Neversink Gorge and Multiple Use Area.

Food and Drink Scene of Ellenville

Must-Try Dishes

Ellenville is known for its diverse culinary scene, offering visitors a range of dining options to choose from. Here are some of the must-try dishes:

  • Ellenville Tofu: This locally-made tofu is a healthy, delicious snack that is perfect for vegetarians and vegans.
  • Maple Syrup: Ellenville is known for its high-quality maple syrup, which is perfect for sweetening pancakes, waffles, or oatmeal.
  • Venison and Wild Game: The Shawangunk Mountains are home to several wild game species, such as deer, bear, and turkey. Many restaurants in Ellenville serve venison and other wild game dishes.

Local Beverages

Ellenville offers a variety of local beverages that are perfect for quenching your thirst. Here are some of the local favorites:

  • Hudson Valley Cider: This locally-made and delicious cider is a perfect beverage to sip and relax after a long day of exploring.
  • Lake Minnewaska Brewing Company: This local microbrewery offers a range of beers, including IPA, Pilsner, and Porter.
  • Shawangunk Wine Trail: The trail offers several wineries that produce some of the best wines in the region.

Where to Stay in Ellenville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ellenville offers a range of accommodations that will suit any traveler’s budget and preferences. Here are some of the popular options:

  • Honor’s Haven Resort and Spa: This luxurious resort features a full-service spa, an indoor pool, and several restaurants. It’s perfect for those who want to unwind and relax.
  • Aroma Thyme Bed and Breakfast: This charming bed and breakfast features stylish rooms and a delicious breakfast. It’s perfect for couples and families.
  • Cold Spring Lodge: This rustic lodge is located in the scenic Catskill Mountains, offering visitors the opportunity to hike, fish, and explore the natural beauty of the area.

What to Buy in Ellenville: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors can find a range of souvenirs and local products to bring home as a memory of their trip to Ellenville. Here are some of the popular options:

  • Handmade Soaps and Candles: Many local shops and farmers’ markets sell handmade soaps and candles, which make perfect gifts for family and friends.
  • Hudson Valley Maple Syrup: This locally-made maple syrup is a delicious and unique souvenir to bring home.
  • Local Art and Crafts: Ellenville is home to several artisans and craftspeople who create unique and beautiful pieces of art, including pottery, jewelry, and paintings.

Local Culture and Customs of Ellenville

Ellenville is a diverse community that values its history and culture. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, such as:

  • Respecting Private Property: Visitors should respect private property and refrain from trespassing on land that is not open to the public.
  • Leave No Trace: Visitors should take care not to litter when exploring the natural attractions of Ellenville.
  • Friendly Conversations: Ellenville is a close-knit community, and visitors should feel free to engage in friendly conversation with the locals.

Best Time to Visit Ellenville

The best time to visit Ellenville is during the spring, summer, and fall months when the weather is mild, and the outdoor attractions are in full bloom. June through August is the peak tourist season, and visitors should plan accordingly to avoid crowds and higher prices.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ellenville

Do:

  • Explore the natural beauty of the town, including its hiking trails and waterfalls.
  • Engage in friendly conversations with the locals, and learn more about their culture and traditions.
  • Visit the local wineries and breweries and taste the locally-made beverages.

Don’t:

  • Trespass on private property or disturb wildlife.
  • Litter or leave trash in the natural attractions of Ellenville.
  • Engage in activities that harm the local environment or put wildlife at risk.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ellenville

Ellenville is a relatively safe town, and visitors should take the same precautions they would take in any other area. The following are some tips for staying safe in Ellenville:

  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid walking alone at night.
  • Keep your valuables safe and secure.
  • Respect private property and obey posted signs and regulations.

Health in Ellenville

Visitors should be prepared for outdoor activities and take precautions to avoid heatstroke, dehydration, and other common health problems related to outdoor activities. Visitors should also bring sunscreen, insect repellent, and any necessary medication.

Getting to Ellenville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ellenville

The nearest airports to Ellenville are the Stewart International Airport (SWF), located about 36 miles away, and the Albany International Airport (ALB), located about 80 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Ellenville

Trailways New York provides bus service to Ellenville from New York City and other nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Ellenville, with Interstate 87 or the New York State Thruway providing convenient access to the town.

How to Get Around in Ellenville

Visitors can explore Ellenville by car, bike, or foot, with several car rental companies and bike rental shops available in the area.

Where to Go Next from Ellenville: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can explore several nearby destinations and take exciting excursions from Ellenville, including:

  • Kingston, a historic town located about 25 miles northwest of Ellenville, known for its museums, art galleries, and waterfront restaurants.
  • Catskill Mountains, a scenic mountain range located about 30 miles west of Ellenville, offering visitors opportunities to hike, bike, and enjoy stunning views.
  • Poughkeepsie, a historic town located about 35 miles southeast of Ellenville, known for its mansions, museums, and riverside parks.

Conclusion

Ellenville is a charming and vibrant town that offers a range of activities and experiences for visitors seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural enrichment. From hiking and biking scenic trails to sampling local dishes and wines, Ellenville is a perfect getaway destination for everyone.
