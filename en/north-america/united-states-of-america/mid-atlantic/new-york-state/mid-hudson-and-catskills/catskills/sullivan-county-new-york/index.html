Introduction to Sullivan County (New York)

Nestled in the picturesque Catskill Mountains in New York state, Sullivan County is a beautiful and historic region that has become a popular destination for tourists seeking outdoor adventure and relaxation. With its vibrant culture, stunning natural scenery, and rich history, Sullivan County offers something for everyone.

Whether you’re looking to explore the great outdoors, immerse yourself in local culture and history, or simply relax and unwind in a peaceful setting, Sullivan County is the perfect place to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

Local Culture and Customs of Sullivan County (New York)

Sullivan County celebrates its rich history and vibrant arts scene through a variety of festivals and events throughout the year. Visitors are encouraged to explore the local arts scene, sample the region’s delicious local cuisine, and learn about the area’s storied past through visits to historical sites and museums.

Best Time to Visit Sullivan County (New York)

The best time to visit Sullivan County is between May and October, when the weather is mild and outdoor activities abound. During the fall, the region’s stunning fall foliage attracts visitors from all over the world.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sullivan County (New York)

Do:

  • Do take advantage of the many outdoor activities available in the region.
  • Do explore the local arts scene and historical attractions.
  • Do sample the region’s delicious local cuisine and beverages.
  • Do bring warm clothing, regardless of the season.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the area’s fragile natural habitats.
  • Don’t forget to leave a tip at restaurants and cafes.
  • Don’t forget to pack appropriate footwear and clothing for your outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sullivan County (New York)

Sullivan County is a relatively safe area, but like any tourist destination, visitors should exercise caution when exploring unfamiliar areas. Hiking and other outdoor activities can be hazardous, so make sure to take appropriate precautions to stay safe.

Health in Sullivan County (New York)

Sullivan County has a number of healthcare facilities, including hospitals, clinics, and urgent care centers. Visitors to the area should make sure they have adequate health insurance coverage before traveling.
