Introduction to Roxbury (New York)

Nestled in the heart of Delaware County, Roxbury is a charming town that boasts of natural beauty, rich history, and a vibrant cultural scene. With its picturesque neighborhoods, scenic trails, serene lakes, and fascinating landmarks, Roxbury promises to offer an unforgettable experience to all visitors.

List of Places to Visit in Roxbury (New York)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Roxbury is home to a plethora of museums and historical landmarks, each presenting a unique insight into the town’s rich past and vibrant culture. Some of the best places to visit include:

  • The Old Schoolhouse Museum: A fascinating museum that showcases the history and cultural heritage of Roxbury, featuring a collection of antique furniture, vintage photographs, and other interesting artifacts.
  • The Jay Gould Memorial Church: An imposing structure located in the heart of Roxbury, the Jay Gould Memorial Church is a beautiful example of Gothic Revival architecture.
  • The Kirkside Park and the Grand Gorge Railroad Museum: The Kirkside Park boasts of lush greenery, a tranquil lake, and a fascinating railroad museum that chronicles the Grand Gorge Railroad’s history.

Natural Attractions of Roxbury (New York): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Roxbury is a nature lover’s paradise, offering you an endless array of outdoor activities and breathtaking views. Some of the top natural attractions in Roxbury include:

  • Hiking Trails: Roxbury is blessed with several scenic hiking trails that cater to both beginners and seasoned hikers. Some of the trails worth exploring include the Pakatakan Mountain Trail and the Catskill Scenic Trail.
  • Fishing and Boating: The town has several serene lakes, including Roxbury Hollow Lake, that offer excellent opportunities for boating and fishing.
  • Scenic Views: Roxbury’s natural beauty is at its best during the fall season when the foliage displays a range of vibrant colors.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Roxbury (New York)

Roxbury offers a vast network of hiking and biking trails, each presenting a unique opportunity to explore the town’s natural beauty. Some of the popular trails worth exploring include:

  • Pakatakan Mountain Trail: A challenging yet rewarding trail that offers panoramic views of the surrounding forests and valleys.
  • Catskill Scenic Trail: A moderate trail that spans over 20 miles, offering scenic views of farmsteads, forests, and waterways.

Best Things to Do in Roxbury (New York): Activities and Tours

Roxbury offers a diverse range of activities and tours that cater to all tastes and preferences. Some of the top things to do in Roxbury include:

  • Culinary Tours: Roxbury is home to several artisanal food producers, where you can taste the best of local cuisine.
  • Festival and Events: Roxbury hosts several cultural and musical events throughout the year, including the Roxbury Arts Group Summer Gala and the Roxbury Art Walk.
  • Golfing: Roxbury boasts of several championship golf courses, including the Shephard Hills Golf Course.

Food and Drink Scene of Roxbury (New York)

Must-Try Dishes

Roxbury offers a range of delicious dishes that reflect the town’s rich culinary heritage. Some of the must-try dishes include:

  • Maple Syrup: Roxbury is known for its artisanal maple syrup, which is used in several dishes.
  • Catskill Trout: Freshly caught from the town’s tranquil lakes, Catskill Trout is a delicacy that should not be missed.

Local Beverages

Roxbury offers a range of unique local beverages, including ciders, wines, and handmade beers. Some of the best places to enjoy a drink in Roxbury include Wayside Cider, Union Grove Distillery, and Huckleberry Mountain Farm and Brewery.

Where to Stay in Roxbury (New York): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Roxbury has several accommodation options, ranging from cozy bed and breakfasts to luxury resorts. Some of the best places to stay in Roxbury include:

  • The Roxbury Motel: A quirky and colorful motel that offers spacious rooms, unique amenities, and personalized service.
  • The Colonial B&B: A charming bed and breakfast located in the heart of Roxbury, offering a quaint and comfortable stay experience.
  • The Emerson Resort and Spa: A luxurious resort that offers a range of amenities and services, including a spa, a golf course, and a restaurant.

What to Buy in Roxbury (New York): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Roxbury has a range of souvenirs and local products available for purchase, including:

  • Maple Syrup: A signature culinary product of Roxbury that is available in several grades.

  • Cutting Boards: Made by the local craftsmen, the cutting boards come in a range of sizes and designs.

Local Culture and Customs of Roxbury (New York)

Roxbury’s culture is a perfect blend of traditional and contemporary elements, reflecting the town’s rich history and artistic heritage. The locals are known for their warm hospitality and friendly demeanor.

Best Time to Visit Roxbury (New York)

The best time to visit Roxbury is during the fall season, between September to November, when the foliage turns into vibrant hues of oranges, yellows, and reds. The town is also pleasant during the summer season, between June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny.

Safety and Health

Safety in Roxbury (New York)

Roxbury is a safe town with low crime rates. However, visitors should take precautions while hiking or exploring the natural areas, as accidents and injuries can occur.

Health in Roxbury (New York)

Visitors to Roxbury should carry necessary medications and bug sprays to avoid illness. The town does not have any major health concerns, but it is advisable to take necessary precautions.

Getting to Roxbury (New York): Transportation Options and Logistics

Roxbury is easily accessible by road, rail, and air. Some of the top transportation options include:

  • By Car: Roxbury is located around 4 hours away from New York City, and visitors can take the I-87 or the NY-17 to reach the town.
  • By Train: The closest rail station to Roxbury is the Amtrak station in Hudson, New York, which is around 50 miles away from the town.
  • By Air: The closest airports to Roxbury are the Albany International Airport, the Stewart International Airport, and the Syracuse Hancock International Airport.

Airports Near Roxbury (New York)

  • Albany International Airport: Around 75 miles away from Roxbury.
  • Stewart International Airport: Around 80 miles away from Roxbury.
  • Syracuse Hancock International Airport: Around 100 miles away from Roxbury.

Bus Stations Near Roxbury (New York)

How to Get Around in Roxbury (New York)

Visitors can get around Roxbury by car, bike, or on foot. Several bike rental shops are available in the town, and visitors can explore the scenic trails at their leisure. The town also has a taxi service for visitors who prefer to travel by car.

Where to Go Next from Roxbury (New York): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Roxbury is located in the heart of the Catskill Mountains, making it an ideal base for exploring nearby towns and attractions. Some of the best places to visit from Roxbury include:

  • Woodstock: Located around 40 miles away from Roxbury, Woodstock is a charming town that is known for its artistic heritage and music festivals.
  • Hunter Mountain: Located around 20 miles away from Roxbury, Hunter Mountain is a popular ski resort that offers excellent skiing and snowboarding opportunities.
  • Kaaterskill Falls: Located around 40 miles away from Roxbury, Kaaterskill Falls is a picturesque waterfall that offers scenic hiking trails.

Conclusion

Roxbury is a charming town that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, rich history, and vibrant culture. From exploring its museums and historical landmarks to hiking through its scenic trails and enjoying the local cuisine, Roxbury promises an unforgettable experience to all its visitors.
