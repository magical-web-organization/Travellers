Introduction to Cazenovia

Cazenovia is a town in Madison County, New York, in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States of America. It is located about 20 miles southeast of Syracuse and is known for its picturesque beauty and historic landmarks. Founded in 1793, Cazenovia has a rich history that is evident in its well-preserved architecture and cultural attractions.

List of Places to Visit in Cazenovia

Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast or a history buff, Cazenovia has something for everyone. Here are some must-visit attractions in the area:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Lorenzo State Historic Site: This beautiful mansion was built in 1807 and is now a museum showcasing the history of the town and its residents.
  • Cazenovia Public Library: The library’s collection boasts over 80,000 volumes and is housed in a stunning neoclassical building that was constructed in 1887.
  • Stone Quarry Hill Art Park: This outdoor art museum features contemporary works of art within a natural landscape.
  • Catherine Cummings Theatre: This fully restored 138-seat theatre hosts live performances and events throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of Cazenovia: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Cazenovia Lake: This serene lake is a popular spot for swimming, boating, and fishing.
  • Chittenango Falls State Park: Just a short drive from Cazenovia, this state park boasts a stunning 167-foot waterfall surrounded by hiking trails and picnic areas.
  • Cazenovia Trail System: The trail system consists of over 20 miles of hiking and biking trails that wind through beautiful natural landscapes and provide stunning views of Cazenovia Lake.
  • Carpenter Falls: This hidden gem is a must-see for nature lovers. The 90-foot waterfall is surrounded by lush greenery and is easily accessible via a short hike.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Cazenovia

If you’re looking to explore the stunning natural landscapes of Cazenovia, there’s no better way than hitting the trails. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in the area:

  • The Cazenovia Trail System: This network of trails spans over 20 miles and provides plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventure.
  • The Link Trail: This 14-mile trail connects Cazenovia to Chittenango Falls and offers stunning views along the way.
  • Fiddler’s Green: This trail is perfect for hikers and bikers of all skill levels and offers scenic views of the surrounding countryside.

Best Things to Do in Cazenovia: Activities and Tours

In addition to the natural and cultural attractions, Cazenovia also offers a variety of activities and tours for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the best:

  • Cazenovia Artisans: This cooperative features the works of over 20 local artists and craftsmen, making it the perfect place to pick up a unique souvenir.
  • Cazenovia Golf Club: This 18-hole golf course offers stunning views of Cazenovia Lake and challenging greens for golfers of all levels.
  • Critz Farms: This family-owned farm offers seasonal events, such as apple picking and pumpkin picking, as well as tastings of local hard cider and wine.
  • Cazenovia Farmers Market: This weekly market features fresh produce, baked goods, and handmade crafts from local vendors.

Food and Drink Scene of Cazenovia

Cazenovia has a thriving food and drink scene, featuring locally sourced ingredients and craft beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cazenovia Cutlets: These thin slices of breaded veal are a local favorite.
  • Finger Lakes Cheese: This region of New York is known for its variety of artisanal cheeses.
  • Pies: Cazenovia is home to several bakeries that are famous for their homemade pies, including apple, blueberry, and pumpkin.

Local Beverages

  • Local Hard Cider: Critz Farms and Hauser Estate Winery both offer tastings of locally made hard cider.
  • Beer: Empire Brewing Company and Good Nature Brewing both feature a variety of craft beers made on-site.
  • Wine: Owera Vineyards and Lakeland Winery both offer tastings of local wines.

Where to Stay in Cazenovia: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several options for accommodations in Cazenovia, ranging from cozy bed and breakfasts to historic hotels. Here are some options to consider:

  • Brae Loch Inn: This historic inn offers cozy rooms and a restaurant featuring local cuisine.
  • Lincklaen House: This former stagecoach stop now serves as a historic hotel with elegant rooms and a restaurant.
  • The Brewster Inn: This stunning lakeside inn features luxurious rooms and a gourmet restaurant.

What to Buy in Cazenovia: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Safety and Health

Cazenovia is generally a safe and healthy destination, but it’s always important to take precautions when traveling. Here’s what you need to know:

Getting to Cazenovia: Transportation Options and Logistics

Cazenovia is easily accessible by car or bus. Here are some transportation options to consider:

  • Airports Near Cazenovia: Syracuse Hancock International Airport (SYR) is about a 45-minute drive from Cazenovia.
  • Bus Stations Near Cazenovia: The nearest bus station is in Syracuse, which is about a 20-minute drive from Cazenovia.
  • Other Options: Cazenovia is also accessible by taxi or rideshare services.

How to Get Around in Cazenovia

The best way to get around Cazenovia is by car, bike, or on foot. Many attractions and landmarks are within walking distance of one another, and the area’s natural beauty is best appreciated on foot or bike.

Where to Go Next from Cazenovia: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
