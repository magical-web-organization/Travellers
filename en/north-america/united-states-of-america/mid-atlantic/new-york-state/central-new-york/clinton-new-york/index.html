Clinton (New York) is a picturesque town located in Central New York, United States of America. Known for its rich history, gorgeous natural landscape, and vibrant local culture, Clinton is a must-visit destination for travelers looking to discover the heart and soul of New York. In this guide, we’ll take you on a tour of all the top attractions, best activities, and most exciting experiences that Clinton has to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Clinton (New York)

Here’s a list of the best places to visit in Clinton (New York):

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Clinton Historical Society Museum: This museum showcases the history and heritage of Clinton, New York, through an impressive collection of artifacts, documents, and photographs. Visitors can take a guided tour of the museum and learn about the town’s past, including its role in the American Revolution and the Underground Railroad.
  • Kirkland Town Library: Established in 1812, the Kirkland Town Library is one of the oldest surviving libraries in New York State. The library building is an architectural masterpiece, featuring a classical design and unique collections of books and manuscripts.
  • Hamilton College: Founded in 1793, Hamilton College is a private liberal arts college that boasts a rich intellectual and cultural tradition. Visitors can explore the beautiful campus, attend concerts and lectures, and admire the impressive artwork at the Wellin Museum of Art.

Natural Attractions of Clinton (New York): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Clinton Cider Mill: Located on the picturesque Oriskany Creek, the Clinton Cider Mill is a historic landmark that has been making fresh cider for over 100 years. Visitors can enjoy a tour of the mill, taste delicious apple cider, and buy fresh produce and baked goods.
  • Perry Hill State Forest: With over 1,500 acres of pristine wilderness, Perry Hill State Forest is a nature lover’s paradise. Visitors can hike, bike, and ski on the park’s well-marked trails, enjoy a picnic in the scenic meadows, and watch for wildlife such as deer and foxes.
  • Waterville Plateau: The Waterville Plateau is a stunning natural wonder that offers panoramic views of the surrounding countryside. Visitors can take a scenic drive along Route 12B, hike to the top of the plateau, and enjoy a picnic while taking in the breathtaking scenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Clinton (New York)

Clinton (New York) is home to some of the best hiking and biking routes in the region. Here are some of the most popular trails:

  • Kirkland Rail Trail: This 2.5-mile trail takes visitors on a scenic journey through the heart of Clinton, with stunning views of the historic town center, the lush countryside, and the nearby forests.
  • Chenango Canal Towpath Trail: Located just outside of Clinton, this 12-mile trail follows the old towpath of the Chenango Canal and offers beautiful views of the surrounding wetlands, forests, and farmlands.
  • Mohawk-Hudson Bike-Hike Trail: This 86-mile trail runs from Albany to Rotterdam Junction and passes through Clinton, offering amazing views of the Mohawk River, the Erie Canal, and the surrounding towns and villages.

Best Things to Do in Clinton (New York): Activities and Tours

Here are some of the most exciting activities and tours to try in Clinton (New York):

  • Explore the Local Art Scene: Visit the Clinton Arts Gallery and the Kirkland Art Center to admire the beautiful artwork of local artists and attend workshops and events.
  • Go Apple Picking: Visit the many local orchards in the area for a fun-filled day of apple picking, cider tasting, and pumpkin carving.
  • Take a Winery Tour: Clinton is home to several world-class wineries, such as the Owera Vineyards and the Prospect Falls Winery, where visitors can take a tour of the vineyards, taste the delicious wines, and enjoy the stunning views.

Food and Drink Scene of Clinton (New York)

Clinton (New York) is a food lover’s paradise, with a vibrant culinary scene that showcases the best of local and international cuisine. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Spiedies: This traditional dish consists of marinated cubes of meat (usually chicken, beef, or lamb) skewered and grilled to perfection. One of the best places to try Spiedies in Clinton is at the Parkside Diner.
  • Tomato Pie: A uniquely Clinton dish, Tomato Pie features a thick, buttery crust filled with a savory tomato sauce and topped with grated cheese. You can try it at the Clinton Pizza Pub.
  • Pierogies: A local favorite, Pierogies are stuffed dumplings filled with mashed potatoes, cheese, and other savory fillings. You can try them at the Polish Community Club in Utica.

Local Beverages

  • Apple Cider: Made from fresh apples harvested at local orchards, Apple Cider is a refreshing and delicious beverage that is perfect for fall.
  • Erie Canal Pale Ale: Brewed by the Erie Canal Brewing Company, this craft beer is a must-try for beer lovers.
  • Napa Valley Cabernet Franc: Produced by the Owera Vineyards, this rich and flavorful wine is a perfect complement to any meal.

Where to Stay in Clinton (New York): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Clinton (New York) offers a range of accommodation options, from cozy bed and breakfasts to modern hotels and apartments. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • The Arbor Inn: This charming bed and breakfast features comfortable rooms, a beautiful garden, and a delicious breakfast served every morning.
  • The Clinton Inn: Located in the heart of Clinton Village, this historic inn offers beautifully appointed rooms, a cozy restaurant, and a friendly staff.
  • The Village Suites at Turning Stone Resort Casino: This luxurious hotel and resort is located just a short drive from Clinton and boasts world-class amenities, including a full-service spa, a golf course, and a casino.

What to Buy in Clinton (New York): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Clinton (New York) is a great place to find unique souvenirs and locally made products. Here are some of the best things to buy:

  • Antiques and Collectibles: Browse the many antique shops and vintage stores in the area to find one-of-a-kind treasures and unique collectibles.

Local Culture and Customs of Clinton (New York)

Clinton (New York) is known for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant arts scene, and friendly locals. Here are some of the customs and traditions that are unique to the area:

  • Historical Preservation: The people of Clinton take great pride in their town’s history and work tirelessly to preserve its historic buildings, artifacts and landmarks.
  • Love of Art and Culture: Clinton has a strong tradition of supporting and promoting the arts, with several galleries, museums, and cultural attractions located in the area.
  • Community Spirit: The people of Clinton are known for their strong sense of community and their willingness to come together to celebrate local events and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Clinton (New York)

Clinton (New York) is a great destination to visit year-round. However, for the best experience, we recommend visiting in the fall, when the weather is mild, the foliage is at its peak, and several local festivals and events take place.

Do’s and Don’ts of Clinton (New York)

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Clinton (New York):

  • Try the Local Food and Drink: From Spiedies to Tomato Pie, Clinton has a unique culinary scene that is a must-try for foodies.
  • Engage with the Locals: The people of Clinton are friendly and welcoming, so take the time to meet some locals and learn about the town’s history and culture.
