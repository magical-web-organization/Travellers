Introduction to Cattaraugus County

Nestled in Southern Tier, New York, Cattaraugus County is a gem waiting to be discovered. Known for its beautiful scenery and outdoor adventures, the county boasts of the Allegheny National Forest, Enchanted Mountains, and Zoar Valley to name a few. Besides natural beauty, Cattaraugus is also rich in history and culture that can be explored through museums and historical sites.

List of Places to Visit in Cattaraugus County

Visitors will be spoilt for choice when it comes to attractions in Cattaraugus County. From museums and historical places to outdoor adventures and scenic views, there is something for everyone.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

History buffs will love the historical places and museums in Cattaraugus County. The Seneca-Iroquois National Museum in Salamanca is a unique, interactive museum that showcases the history, culture, and traditions of the Seneca Nation. The Ellicottville Historical Society Museum features exhibits on local history, including the Underground Railroad. The Pfeiffer Nature Center and Arboretum in Portville is a must-visit for those interested in nature and birdwatching, as it has several trails and offers guided nature walks.

Natural Attractions of Cattaraugus County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Cattaraugus County is the perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts. It has several state parks, hiking trails, and waterfalls. The Allegany State Park is the largest state park in New York, with miles of trails for hiking, biking, and horseback riding. The Zoar Valley Multiple Use Area is a 3,014-acre area with a gorge, waterfalls, and hiking trails. The Enchanted Mountains is a prime spot for fall foliage, offering scenic views and miles of trails for hiking, biking, and skiing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Cattaraugus County

Cattaraugus County boasts several trails for hiking and biking enthusiasts. The Allegany State Park Trail System has over 80 miles of trails, ranging from easy to challenging. The Pat McGee Trail in Salamanca is a 12-mile trail that follows the Allegany River and has several scenic spots for picnicking. The Nannen Arboretum Trail is a 2-mile trail that takes visitors through a beautiful landscape garden and features several sculptures and a butterfly garden.

Best Things to Do in Cattaraugus County: Activities and Tours

Apart from outdoor activities and attractions, Cattaraugus County offers several other activities for visitors. The Holiday Valley Resort in Ellicottville offers downhill skiing, snowboarding, and ziplining. The Seneca Allegany Resort and Casino in Salamanca is a popular destination for gaming and entertainment. Visitors can take a guided white water rafting tour in Zoar Valley or go on a hot air balloon ride to enjoy panoramic views of the county.

Week-Long Itinerary for Cattaraugus County

A week in Cattaraugus County is not enough to see everything, but here is a sample itinerary to make the most of your visit:

Day Activity
1 Explore the Allegheny State Park
2 Visit the Seneca-Iroquois National Museum and Ellicottville Historical Society Museum
3 Take a white water rafting tour in Zoar Valley
4 Enjoy downhill skiing and snowboarding at Holiday Valley Resort
5 Take a hot air balloon ride over the county
6 Visit the Pfeiffer Nature Center and Arboretum
7 Relax and enjoy the scenic views of the Enchanted Mountains

Food and Drink Scene of Cattaraugus County

Cattaraugus County is home to several fine dining restaurants and local eateries that serve delicious cuisine using locally sourced ingredients.

Must-Try Dishes

Some of the must-try local dishes include the Beef on Weck sandwich, Lake Erie perch, and Allegany Indian Reservation Corn Soup.

Local Beverages

Cattaraugus County has several breweries and wineries. Four Mile Brewing in Olean serves a variety of craft beers, and Ellicottville Brewing Company in Ellicottville is known for its award-winning beers and ales. Merritt Estate Winery in Forestville offers tours and tastings of their wines.

Where to Stay in Cattaraugus County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Cattaraugus County has several accommodation options for visitors. The Holiday Valley Resort offers ski-in/ski-out accommodations and has several lodging options, including hotels, condos, and chalets. The Tarry Here Bed and Breakfast in Salamanca is a cozy, historic bed and breakfast. The Microtel Inn and Suites by Wyndham in Olean is a modern, budget-friendly option.

What to Buy in Cattaraugus County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to Cattaraugus County can bring back home several local products and souvenirs. The Ellicottville Country Store and Antiques in Ellicottville has several antiques and handmade crafts. The Amish Trail in Cattaraugus County features several Amish shops that sell handmade quilts, baskets, and furniture.

Local Culture and Customs of Cattaraugus County

Cattaraugus County is home to several Native American tribes, and visitors should respect their culture and customs. Visitors should not take photographs of Native Americans without their permission and should not touch or remove artifacts from historical sites and museums.

Best Time to Visit Cattaraugus County

Cattaraugus County is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit is from June to September when the weather is pleasant, and outdoor activities are in full swing. Fall in Cattaraugus County is also a popular time to visit for foliage peeping.

Do’s and Don’ts of Cattaraugus County

Do:

  • Take advantage of the outdoor activities and attractions
  • Respect the Native American culture and customs
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages

Don’t:

  • Take photographs of Native Americans without their permission
  • Remove or touch artifacts from historical sites and museums

Safety and Health

Safety in Cattaraugus County

Cattaraugus County is a safe destination, but visitors should be vigilant and take necessary precautions while hiking, biking, or participating in outdoor activities. Visitors should carry plenty of water and wear appropriate clothing and gear for outdoor activities.

Health in Cattaraugus County

Visitors should be up-to-date with their regular vaccinations and should bring any necessary medication with them. Visitors should also be aware of the risk of Lyme disease and other tick-borne illnesses and take necessary precautions.

Getting to Cattaraugus County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Cattaraugus County is easily accessible by car and several airports and bus stations are nearby.

Airports Near Cattaraugus County

  • Buffalo Niagara International Airport
  • Chautauqua County-Jamestown Airport

Bus Stations Near Cattaraugus County

  • Greyhound Bus Lines in Olean
  • Trailways Transportation System in Salamanca

Other Options

Visitors can also arrive by train, as there is an Amtrak station in Buffalo, about 72 miles from Cattaraugus County.

How to Get Around in Cattaraugus County

The best way to get around Cattaraugus County is by car, as public transportation is limited. Visitors can also take a taxi or rideshare service, but it may be expensive for longer distances.

Where to Go Next from Cattaraugus County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Cattaraugus County is a great starting point to explore nearby destinations, including:

  • Niagara Falls, NY
  • Chautauqua Lake, NY
  • Allegheny National Forest, PA

Conclusion

Cattaraugus County is a beautiful county that has something for everyone. From outdoor adventures and scenic views to historical places and museums, visitors will have a memorable experience exploring this hidden gem in New York.
