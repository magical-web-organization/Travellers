Introduction to Olean

Welcome to Olean, NY, a small city nestled in Cattaraugus County, in the Southern Tier region of New York. With its rich history, beautiful landscape, and friendly locals, Olean makes the perfect destination for a weekend getaway or a longer vacation. Whether you’re looking for adventure or relaxation, culture or nature, Olean has something for everyone.

Week-Long Itinerary for Olean

Day Activity
1 Visit the Olean Heritage Museum and the Fannie E. Bartlett House
2 Hike the Cutco Trail and enjoy the views from Bartlett Hill
3 Explore Allegany State Park and take a picnic lunch
4 Go golfing at the Bartlett Country Club
5 Take a whitewater rafting trip down the Allegheny River
6 Visit Rock City Park and Pfeiffer Nature Center
7 End the trip with a hot air balloon ride with Balloons Over Allegany

Local Culture and Customs of Olean

  • Respect the natural environment and outdoor spaces

Best Time to Visit Olean

The best time to visit Olean is from late spring to early fall when the weather is mild and outdoor activities are in full swing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Olean

Do:

  • Explore the natural beauty of Allegany State Park
  • Visit the local museums and historical sites
  • Try the local food and drink specialties
  • Support the local businesses and artisans

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural environment
  • Be disrespectful to the locals or their customs
  • Skip out on the local attractions and activities
  • Forget to take plenty of pictures!

Safety and Health

Safety in Olean

Olean is a friendly and safe city, but it’s always important to be aware of your surroundings and take precautions to stay safe.

Health in Olean

Olean has a number of medical facilities, including Olean General Hospital, which is located in the heart of the city.

Getting to Olean: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Olean

  • Buffalo Niagara International Airport (BUF)
  • Bradford Regional Airport (BFD)

Bus Stations Near Olean

  • Olean Bus Terminal
  • Megabus stop in Buffalo, NY

Other Options

  • Driving: Olean is easily accessible by car via I-86.
  • Train: The nearest Amtrak station is in Buffalo, NY, about 80 miles from Olean.

How to Get Around in Olean

The best way to get around Olean is by car or bike, but there are also local taxis and buses available for transportation.
