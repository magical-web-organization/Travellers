Introduction to Endicott

Endicott is a charming village situated in Broome County in the Southern Tier region of New York State. This peaceful hamlet is a hidden gem that offers a perfect blend of stunning natural beauty and cultural landmarks.

List of Places to Visit in Endicott

If you are planning a trip to Endicott, then here are some must-visit places you shouldn’t miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • George F. Johnson Memorial Library: Step into this historic library and find yourself surrounded by a wealth of knowledge and history.
  • The IBM Endicott Visitor Center: This iconic center gives visitors an opportunity to learn about IBM’s rich history and its contribution to the field of technology.
  • Huron Campus: A former IBM facility, Huron Campus now houses several companies and unique buildings that are worth exploring, including Endicott-Johnson admin building.

Natural Attractions of Endicott: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • George W. Johnson Park: This tranquil park is a popular spot for outdoor enthusiasts who love to hike, picnic, and fish.
  • Greenway Trail: Enjoy a scenic walk or bike ride along this 4-mile trail that offers picturesque views of the Susquehanna River.
  • Hiawatha Island Wildlife Refuge: Get up close and personal with nature at this stunning wildlife refuge that offers opportunities for birdwatching, kayaking, and hiking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Endicott

Endicott is home to several diverse hiking and biking routes. Here are some of the best trails to explore:

  • Jones Park Trails: A 3.5-mile loop trail that offers a peaceful and scenic walk.
  • North Side Mtn Biking Trail: A moderate biking trail through the woods that offers stunning panoramic views of the area.
  • Chug-A-Mug Trail: A challenging 8.5-mile biking trail that takes you on an adventurous journey through parks, forests, and past scenic vistas.

Best Things to Do in Endicott: Activities and Tours

If you are looking for exciting things to do in Endicott, then check out some of these options:

  • Schorr Family Firehouse Stage: Enjoy music, theater, and dance performances at this intimate theater in the heart of the village.
  • Riverow Bookshop: This independent bookstore is a must-visit for book lovers who enjoy browsing through thousands of titles.
  • Endicott Performing Arts Center: Watch live performances of your favorite plays and musicals at this state-of-the-art theater venue.

Week-Long Itinerary for Endicott

Here’s a week-long itinerary for Endicott to help you plan your trip:

  • Day 1: Visit the George F. Johnson Memorial Library and the IBM Endicott Visitor Center.
  • Day 2: Explore the Greenway Trail and Jones Park Trails.
  • Day 3: Visit Hiawatha Island Wildlife Refuge and enjoy bird watching and kayaking.
  • Day 4: Take a bike ride on the North Side Mountain Biking Trail.
  • Day 5: Shop at the Riverow Bookshop and explore the local food scene.
  • Day 6: Hike the Chug-A-Mug Trail and visit the Endicott Performing Arts Center.
  • Day 7: Relax and unwind at George W. Johnson Park.

Food and Drink Scene of Endicott

Endicott is home to some amazing culinary delights and local specialties. Here are the must-try dishes and local beverages you shouldn’t miss:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Spiedies: A classic dish of marinated meat skewered on a stick, grilled, and served in a bread roll.
  • Salt Potatoes: Small white potatoes boiled in highly concentrated saltwater until the skin becomes slightly wrinkled.
  • Beef on Weck: A traditional sandwich served with roast beef and horseradish on a kimmelweck roll.

Local Beverages

  • Ithaca Beer Company: An award-winning brewery that offers a wide range of craft beers.
  • Endless Brewing: This local brewery offers handmade beers curated with locally sourced ingredients.

Best Time to Visit Endicott
