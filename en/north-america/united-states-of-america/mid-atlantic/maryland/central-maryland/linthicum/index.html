Introduction to Linthicum

Located in Central Maryland, Linthicum is a charming town known for its rich history and natural beauty. The town is situated in Anne Arundel County and boasts a convenient location that offers easy access to major cities like Baltimore and Annapolis. Linthicum is a popular destination for travelers who want to explore the region’s cultural attractions, enjoy outdoor activities, and discover the town’s unique charm.

List of Places to Visit in Linthicum

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • BWI Thurgood Marshall Airport Observation Gallery - Located at the airport, this observation gallery is a great place to watch planes taking off and landing while learning about aviation history.
  • National Electronics Museum - This museum showcases the history of electronic technology and is a must-visit for tech enthusiasts.
  • Benson-Hammond House - Built in the early 19th century, this historic house museum is an excellent representation of life during that time period.
  • Maryland Live! Casino - This casino features live entertainment and a wide variety of gaming options.
  • Patapsco Valley State Park - This park is a great place to explore the outdoors and learn about the area’s natural history.

Natural Attractions of Linthicum: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Patapsco Valley State Park - This beautiful park offers a wide range of outdoor activities, including hiking, fishing, camping, and more.
  • Schooley Mill Park - This scenic park features a historic mill, hiking trails, picnic areas, and more.
  • Quiet Waters Park - Located in nearby Annapolis, this park offers stunning views of the Chesapeake Bay and is a popular spot for picnicking and water activities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Linthicum

Linthicum is surrounded by beautiful parks and trails, making it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Some of the best hiking and biking routes in the area include:

  • Patapsco Valley State Park Trails - This park offers a wide range of hiking and biking trails, ranging from easy to challenging.
  • BW&A Trail - This 14-mile trail runs through Anne Arundel and connects with trails in other parts of the Baltimore-Washington area.
  • Quiet Waters Park Trails - This park features several miles of scenic trails that are perfect for hiking and biking.

Best Things to Do in Linthicum: Activities and Tours

  • Maryland Live! Casino - This casino features live entertainment and a wide variety of gaming options.
  • National Electronics Museum - This museum showcases the history of electronic technology and is a must-visit for tech enthusiasts.
  • BWI Thurgood Marshall Airport Observation Gallery - Located at the airport, this observation gallery is a great place to watch planes taking off and landing while learning about aviation history.
  • Ghost Tour of Annapolis - This spooky tour takes visitors through the streets of historic Annapolis, where they’ll hear ghost stories and learn about the town’s haunted history.

Week-Long Itinerary for Linthicum

Day 1: Explore the National Electronics Museum and enjoy dinner at one of the town’s local restaurants. Day 2: Spend the day at Patapsco Valley State Park, hiking, fishing, and picnic at one of the park’s scenic spots. Day 3: Take a tour of BWI airport’s observation gallery and learn about aviation history, then head to Maryland Live! Casino for dinner and entertainment. Day 4: Check out the historic Benson-Hammond House and take a scenic drive or hike through Schooley Mill Park. Day 5: Visit Quiet Waters Park in nearby Annapolis, where you can enjoy a picnic lunch and water activities. Day 6: Take a ghost tour of historic Annapolis, then enjoy dinner at one of the town’s many restaurants. Day 7: Spend the day exploring the town’s local shops and attractions, then head to BW&A Trail for an afternoon bike ride.

Local Culture and Customs of Linthicum

Linthicum is known for its friendly locals and laid-back atmosphere. Visitors should be sure to try some of the area’s local cuisine, which features plenty of fresh seafood and regional specialties. When visiting historic sites and museums, it’s important to be respectful of the town’s rich history and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Linthicum

The best time to visit Linthicum is between March and May or between September and November, when the weather is mild and comfortable. However, summer (June - August) is also a popular time to visit, as the area offers plenty of outdoor activities and events during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Linthicum

Do:

  • Try some of the area’s local cuisine, especially the fresh seafood and crab dishes.
  • Explore the town’s outdoor attractions, including parks and hiking trails.
  • Take a tour of historic sites and museums to learn about the town’s rich history.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage any of the town’s attractions or outdoor spaces.
  • Be disrespectful of the town’s history or traditions.
  • Ignore safety guidelines when participating in outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Linthicum

Linthicum is generally a safe town for visitors, but it’s always important to take precautions when traveling. Be aware of your surroundings and avoid walking alone at night. When participating in outdoor activities, be sure to follow safety guidelines and avoid risky behaviors.

Health in Linthicum

Linthicum has plenty of healthcare facilities and hospitals, so visitors should have no trouble finding medical assistance if needed. It’s always a good idea to bring any necessary medications and to have travel insurance to cover unexpected health issues.

Getting to Linthicum: Transportation Options and Logistics

Linthicum is conveniently located near major cities and transportation hubs, making it easy to get to and from the town.

Airports Near Linthicum

  • Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport (BWI) - This airport is located just a few miles from Linthicum and offers a variety of domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Linthicum

  • Greyhound Bus Station - Baltimore - Located about 7 miles north of Linthicum, this bus station offers a variety of routes throughout the East Coast and beyond.

Other Options

  • Amtrak - BWI Rail Station - Located just a few miles from Linthicum, this station offers routes throughout the Northeast Corridor.
  • Car Rental - Visitors can rent a car from a variety of rental companies at the airport or in nearby cities like Annapolis and Baltimore.

How to Get Around in Linthicum

Linthicum is relatively small and easy to navigate by car, bike, or on foot. Visitors can also take advantage of rideshare services like Uber and Lyft to get around town.

Where to Go Next from Linthicum: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

After exploring Linthicum, there are plenty of nearby destinations and excursions to check out, including:

  • Baltimore - Just a short drive north of Linthicum, Baltimore offers a wide variety of cultural attractions, including museums, theaters, and galleries.
  • Annapolis - This historic Chesapeake Bay town is less than 30 miles from Linthicum and offers plenty of shopping, dining, and sightseeing opportunities.
  • Washington D.C. - The nation’s capital is just a short drive or train ride from Linthicum and features plenty of world-class museums, monuments, and cultural attractions.

Conclusion

Whether you’re interested in exploring the town’s rich history, enjoying outdoor activities, or simply relaxing and enjoying the local cuisine, Linthicum has something for everyone. With its convenient location, friendly locals, and unique charm, it’s no wonder that this town is a perennial favorite for travelers from all over the world.
