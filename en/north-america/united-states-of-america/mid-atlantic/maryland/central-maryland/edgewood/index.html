Introduction to Edgewood

Edgewood is a charming town located in Central Maryland, with a rich cultural heritage, scenic beauty, and natural wonders. This small town has a lot to offer for tourists, including historic landmarks, outdoor adventure, local cuisine, and cultural experiences.

Whether you’re a history buff, nature lover, adventure seeker, or foodie, Edgewood is sure to surprise and delight you with its diverse range of attractions and experiences. In this comprehensive travel guide, you’ll discover the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and what to eat in Edgewood.

List of Places to Visit in Edgewood

Here’s a quick overview of the top attractions and places to visit in Edgewood:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Historic Jerusalem Mill Village: A restored 18th-century mill village with a rich history, located in the Gunpowder Falls State Park.
  • Harford Artists’ Gallery: A showcase of local artwork, pottery, jewelry, and crafts created by Harford County artists.
  • The Steppingstone Museum: An open-air museum showcasing the rural life and history of Harford County.

Natural Attractions of Edgewood: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Gunpowder Falls State Park: A vast wilderness area covering 18,000 acres, with more than 120 miles of trails, picnic areas, and stunning scenic views.
  • Mariner Point Park: A recreational park with a playground, pavilion, fishing pier, and stunning views of the Chesapeake Bay.
  • Flying Point Park: A wildlife refuge with hiking trails, birdwatching opportunities, and stunning views of the Gunpowder River.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Edgewood

Edgewood is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, with a vast network of hiking and biking trails that offer stunning views of the scenic landscapes, rivers, and wildlife. Here are some of the top hiking and biking routes in Edgewood:

  • The Ma and Pa Trail: A 2.8-mile trail that runs along the banks of the Bush River, with scenic views of the waterfalls, rapids, and wildlife.
  • The Gunpowder Falls State Park: With over 120 miles of hiking and biking trails, Gunpowder Falls State Park offers plenty of options for outdoor enthusiasts of all levels.

Best Things to Do in Edgewood: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re looking for adventure, relaxation, or cultural experiences, here are some of the best things to do in Edgewood:

  • Take a scenic boat tour of the Chesapeake Bay
  • Go fishing in the Gunpowder River
  • Explore the rich history and culture of Edgewood through guided tours
  • Sample the delicious local cuisine, including Maryland crab cakes, chowder, and oysters

Week-Long Itinerary for Edgewood

If you’re planning to spend a week in Edgewood, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day 1: Visit the Historic Jerusalem Mill Village Day 2: Explore the Steppingstone Museum and Gunpowder Falls State Park Day 3: Take a boat tour of the Chesapeake Bay Day 4: Go hiking in the Ma and Pa Trail Day 5: Visit the Harford Artists’ Gallery and Flying Point Park Day 6: Go fishing in the Gunpowder River Day 7: Sample the local cuisine and relax in Mariner Point Park

Local Culture and Customs of Edgewood

Edgewood has a rich cultural heritage, with a strong influence of the Native American tribes who historically inhabited the region. The town also has a strong maritime history, due to its close proximity to Chesapeake Bay and Gunpowder River.

Best Time to Visit Edgewood

The best time to visit Edgewood is during the spring and fall seasons when the weather is mild, and the foliage is at its peak. Summers can be hot and humid, and winters can be chilly, with occasional snowfall.

Safety and Health

Safety in Edgewood

Edgewood is a relatively safe town, with low crime rates and a friendly local community. However, it’s always advisable to take precautions against pickpocketing and petty theft, especially in crowded tourist areas.

Health in Edgewood

Edgewood has reliable healthcare facilities and hospitals, with trained medical professionals. Tourists are advised to carry a basic first-aid kit and medical insurance while traveling.
