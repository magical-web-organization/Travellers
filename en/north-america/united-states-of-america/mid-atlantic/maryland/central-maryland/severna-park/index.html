Introduction to Severna Park

Severna Park is a charming and picturesque waterfront town located in Central Maryland, in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States. It’s a lovely area to explore, with a delightful blend of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and scenic views. Severna Park is easily accessible via several airports, as well as bus and train services.

List of Places to Visit in Severna Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you have a fondness for history or art, you’ll want to visit some of these museums and historical sites.

  • The Banneker-Douglass Museum - This institution is dedicated to preserving the history and culture of African American communities in Maryland. It showcases exhibitions, workshops, and educational programs.
  • Hammond-Harwood House - This well-preserved historic house is a prime example of Colonial architecture with an extensive collection of Maryland colonial furniture, and fine and decorative arts from the 18th and 19th centuries.

Natural Attractions of Severna Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers, there are several parks and scenic areas to explore.

  • Severna Park Lagoon - This area is perfect for kayaking, paddleboarding, and fishing. The scenic lagoon is surrounded by a picturesque shoreline, and you can rent equipment from several rental shops nearby.
  • Sandy Point State Park - This is an ideal location for picnicking, camping, swimming, and bird-watching. The area has a beautiful sandy beach, and is also home to a marina, snack bar, and several trails for hiking and biking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Severna Park

The region has several scenic and challenging biking and hiking trails worth exploring. Here are some high points:

  • B&A Trail - This is a 13-mile paved trail that runs through several towns in Anne Arundel County. It’s ideal for biking, walking, and jogging, and offers picturesque views of the surrounding woods and creeks.
  • Generals Highway Corridor Park - This park boasts over six miles of hiking and biking trails, including the 1.7-mile Jones Falls Trail and the 1.6-mile Rock Creek Trail. The park is home to several historic sites and offers rugged and picturesque scenery.

Best Things to Do in Severna Park: Activities and Tours

From visiting museums and historical sites to outdoor activities and water sports, there are plenty of things to do in Severna Park. Here are some highlights:

  • Take a scenic boat tour - Several companies offer waterfront tours of the area, including private yacht tours, sailing excursions and public cruises.

  • Visit a vineyard or winery - The area is rich in vineyards and wineries.

Food and Drink Scene of Severna Park

Must-Try Dishes

From seafood to barbecue and more, Severna Park offers many culinary delights. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • Crab cakes - No trip to Maryland is complete without sampling the area’s mouth-watering crab cakes.
  • Pit beef - This is a Maryland staple - tender and juicy beef, thinly sliced, and served on a sandwich.

Local Beverages

Maryland’s thriving craft beer and wine industry offer an assortment of locally produced beer, wine, liquor, and whisky.

Where to Stay in Severna Park: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Severna Park has a variety of lodging options to fit all budgets. Here are some top picks:

  • Severn Inn - Situated on a hill overlooking the Severn River, the Severn Inn offers spectacular views of the water. It’s a great place for a romantic getaway.
  • Residence Inn Annapolis - This hotel is strategically located just minutes away from several attractions, such as Sandy Point State Park, Annapolis, and the state capital.

What to Buy in Severna Park: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Severna Park’s local markets offer unique and quirky souvenirs to bring back home. Here are a few spots to check out:

  • Severna Park Marketplace - This market offers an assortment of custom-made products such as pottery, jewelry, and local preserves.

Local Culture and Customs of Severna Park

Maryland’s culture blends Southern hospitality and East Coast charm. The people are friendly and welcoming and appreciate good conversation and sports.

Best Time to Visit Severna Park

Autumn is the best time to visit Severna Park. The weather is cool, and the leaves change colors into beautiful shades of orange and yellow.

Do’s and Don’ts of Severna Park

Do:

  • Do visit historical sites and museums in the area.
  • Do check out the numerous parks, hiking and biking trails in and around Severna Park.

Don’t:

  • Don’t visit during the peak summer months if you are not used to hot and humid weather.
  • Don’t leave trash or damage park property.

Safety and Health

Maryland is a safe and relatively healthy state. However, visitors should always take precautions when exploring an unfamiliar area.

Safety in Severna Park

Severna Park’s crime rate is relatively low, with petty crimes being the most common.

Health in Severna Park

Severna Park has several great hospitals and clinics, with highly trained medical professionals.

Getting to Severna Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Severna Park

The Baltimore-Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport is located about 15 minutes away from Severna Park.

Bus Stations Near Severna Park

Severna Park is easily accessible via public transportations, including buses.

How to Get Around in Severna Park

Severna Park is a small town with many areas that are easy to navigate on foot or by bike. Public transportation options such as buses are available.

Where to Go Next from Severna Park: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Annapolis, The United States Naval Academy, and Baltimore are within just a few minutes drive from Severna Park, so visitors can take full advantage and explore what these nearby cities and attractions offer.

Conclusion

Severna Park is a delightful waterfront town situated in Central Maryland. With plenty of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and charming neighborhoods, it is no surprise why it is a great place to visit. We hope you enjoyed our comprehensive travel guide, and we look forward to seeing you in Severna Park.
