Introduction to Prince George’s County

Located in the Capital Region of Maryland, United States, Prince George’s County is a bustling county that offers a unique blend of city and outdoor experiences. Known for its rich history dating back to the colonial era, the county has a lot to offer to tourists and locals alike. From exploring museums and historical sites to enjoying outdoor adventures and scenic vistas, Prince George’s County has something for everyone.

Local Culture and Customs of Prince George’s County

  • Respect the locals and their culture
  • Tipping 15-20% is a common practice
  • Dress appropriately for the season and occasion

Best Time to Visit Prince George’s County

The best time to visit Prince George’s County is in the spring and fall when the temperatures are mild and the trees are in bloom or changing color, respectively.

Do’s and Don’ts of Prince George’s County

Do:

  • Do try the local seafood and Old Bay seasoning dishes
  • Do explore the county’s rich history and museums
  • Do check out the scenic parks and natural attractions

Don’t:

  • Don’t disrespect the local culture or traditions
  • Don’t litter or damage the nature parks or historical sites
  • Don’t visit during extreme weather conditions

Safety and Health

Safety in Prince George’s County

Prince George’s County is generally safe for tourists, but like any other big city or county, some areas have higher crime rates. Avoid walking alone in deserted areas at night.

Health in Prince George’s County

There are several hospitals and medical centers in Prince George’s County. It is important to check with your insurance provider about coverage before visiting.
