Introduction to Berwyn Heights

Berwyn Heights is a quaint, small town located in Prince George’s County, Maryland, United States. It is a beautiful place with lush green trails, waterways, picturesque landscapes, and a rich history. Despite being a small town, Berwyn Heights offers many unique activities that cater to people of all ages. So whether you are visiting for business or recreation, there is something for everyone. Discover Berwyn Heights today!

List of Places to Visit in Berwyn Heights

If you are looking for things to do in Berwyn Heights, then you’re in for a treat! Here are some of the top attractions to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Berwyn Heights is a town steeped in history and culture. Visit the following places to learn more:

  • Marietta House Museum - A historic plantation and museum that preserves the legacy of the enslaved residents who lived and worked on the site from the early 1800s to the end of the Civil War.
  • College Park Aviation Museum - A museum that commemorates the history of aviation and its pioneers in the College Park region.
  • Beltsville Agricultural Research Center Visitor Center - A visitor center that offers a glimpse into the fascinating research in progress at the Beltsville Agricultural Research Center.

Natural Attractions of Berwyn Heights: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Berwyn Heights offers many natural attractions for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the most popular outdoor activities:

  • Lake Artemesia Park - A beautiful park with scenic trails, bike paths, fishing lakes, and green spaces.
  • St. Jerome Creek Trail - A beautiful woodland trail that offers an easy walk along the creek banks, with plenty of opportunities for wildlife spotting.
  • Indian Creek Trail - A popular and scenic trail that runs through the same designated Greenway as the Lake Artemesia Park. It offers a chance to explore the beautiful vistas of the creek and the lake itself.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Berwyn Heights

Berwyn Heights is a great destination for people who love hiking and biking. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes for enjoying the outdoors:

  • Trolley Trail - This bike path that extends from University Park to Hyattsville and Riverdale Park is a favorite among the locals. It offers scenic views of the community and passes through wooded areas, parks, and the University of Maryland campus.
  • Greenbelt Park Trails - With 12 miles of hiking trails and 3 camp sites, Greenbelt Park Trails are ideal for anyone looking for a quiet hike in nature. The trails range from easy to moderate.
  • Indian Creek Trail - An 8-mile trail that passes through the Lake Artemesia Park and features stunning views of the water and greenery.

Best Things to Do in Berwyn Heights: Activities and Tours

Berwyn Heights is a fantastic place that offers many activities and tours to make your vacation memorable. Here are some of the best things to do in Berwyn Heights:

  • River Road Hiking Trail - This 3-mile trail along the Potomac River offers a beautiful view of the Great Falls, one of the most beautiful natural attractions in Maryland.
  • Paint Your Own Pottery Studio - Those who want to tap into their creative side can visit the Potomac Art Studio for painting workshops and private parties.
  • Urban Axes - Berwyn Heights is home to Urban Axes, a unique axe-throwing venue.

Food and Drink Scene of Berwyn Heights

Visiting Berwyn Heights means not only exploring the beauty of nature but also savoring the local cuisine and drinks. Try some of the following must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Old Bay Wings - A popular chicken wing dish coated in the iconic Maryland spice blend, Old Bay Seasoning.
  • Crab Cakes - A Maryland must-try dish made with fresh crab meat and a combination of breadcrumbs, eggs, and spices.
  • Steamed Crabs - For the ultimate Maryland seafood experience, try steamed blue crabs seasoned with Old Bay Spice.

Local Beverages

  • National Bohemian (Natty Boh) - This is a locally made beer, popular in Maryland region. This beer is considered a staple amongst the locals for it’s easy-to-drink flavor and affordability.
  • Old Dominion Root Beer - A locally made non-alcoholic drink that is famous for its creamy, smooth texture and rich flavor.

Where to Stay in Berwyn Heights: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Berwyn Heights offers a variety of accommodation options, ranging from affordable hostels to luxurious hotels. Here are some of the best options for your stay:

  • Ivy City Hotel - This luxury hotel is located in the heart of the city and offers modern rooms with exclusive amenities.
  • The Hotel at University of Maryland - This hotel is adjacent to the University of Maryland and features luxurious rooms, an on-site restaurant, fitness center, and an Olympic-size pool.
  • College Park Marriott Hotel & Conference Center - This newly renovated Marriott offers comfortable rooms, spacious meeting facilities, and on-site dining options.

What to Buy in Berwyn Heights: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Shopping is a must-do when on vacation. Here are some of the best souvenirs and local products to bring home from Berwyn Heights:

  • Crab-themed souvenirs - Maryland is famous for its blue crabs, so why not bring home some souvenirs like crab-themed coasters, magnets, shot glasses, t-shirts, and hats?
  • Local Art and Crafts - Berwyn Heights is home to many skilled artists and craftsmen, so don’t forget to visit local shops and galleries to buy unique, handcrafted souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Berwyn Heights

Berwyn Heights is a town that is rich in culture. Here are some customs and cultural norms to keep in mind when visiting:

  • In public spaces, it is customary to say “hello” or “good morning” to people you may pass by.
  • When attending cultural events or visiting historical landmarks, it is important to show respect and observe the dress code, especially in religious structures.

Best Time to Visit Berwyn Heights

Berwyn Heights is a great vacation destination any time of the year, each season offering unique experiences:

  • Spring is the perfect time to visit Berwyn Heights; the trails are in full bloom, and the weather is pleasant.
  • Summer is an ideal season for those who want to explore the scenic beauty of the water trails and enjoy swimming in the lake.
  • Fall is an excellent season to visit Berwyn Heights as the foliage is breathtaking, and the weather is mild.
  • Winter is a quieter time to visit Berwyn Heights; the town is covered in snow and offers unique winter experiences.

Do’s and Don’ts of Berwyn Heights

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and cultural norms when visiting religious shrines.
  • Explore the scenic beauty throughout the city by hiking and biking.
  • Attend the local cultural and historical events.

Don’t:

  • Litter in public spaces.
  • Ignore trail instructions while hiking.
  • Be rude or impolite to locals or other tourists.

Safety and Health

Safety in Berwyn Heights

Berwyn Heights is a safe and secure town with a low crime rate. However, visitors should still exercise caution and common sense while traveling, such as locking your car doors, and keeping valuables in a safe place.

Health in Berwyn Heights

Berwyn Heights has several medical facilities, including urgent care centers and hospitals. Ensure that you keep a track of your medical conditions and medications and stay hydrated, especially during the warmer months.

Getting to Berwyn Heights: Transportation Options and Logistics

Berwyn Heights is a well-connected town with many transportation options. The following are some of the best options for getting here:

Airports Near Berwyn Heights

  • Baltimore International Airport (BWI) - This airport is located 30 miles north of Berwyn Heights.
  • Ronald Reagan National Airport (DCA) - This airport is located 15 miles south of Berwyn Heights.
  • Washington Dulles International Airport (IAD) - This airport is located approximately 50 miles west of Berwyn Heights.

Bus Stations Near Berwyn Heights

  • Greyhound Bus Lines - Greyhound has a bus stop in nearby Landover, approximately 5 miles southeast of Berwyn Heights.
  • Megabus - Another option for bus travel is Megabus. There is a stop in Union Station in Washington DC, which is approximately 10 miles south of Berwyn Heights.

Other Options

  • Metro Rail - Berwyn Heights is within access to the Green Line on the Metrorail system, with the closest stations being College Park Metro and Greenbelt Metro Station.
  • Uber/Lyft/cab services - Taxis and ride-sharing services can be found throughout the region.

How to Get Around in Berwyn Heights

Berwyn Heights is a small town that is easy to navigate. The following are some of the best ways to get around:

  • Bicycle - Biking is a great way to explore Berwyn Heights’s many trails and bike paths.
  • Walking - Berwyn Heights is a pedestrian-friendly town, and many destinations are within walking distance of one another.
  • Car Rental/Taxi - Taxis are readily available throughout the town and can be used for those who plan to take day trips or visit nearby attractions.

Where to Go Next from Berwyn Heights: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Berwyn Heights is close to many exciting destinations in Maryland and the nearby states of Virginia and Washington DC. Some of the nearby places worth visiting include:

  • Washington, DC - Visit the nation’s capital and its iconic landmarks, including the White House, Capitol Hill, Smithsonian Museums, and more.
  • Annapolis - Maryland’s capital city is located less than an hour’s drive from Berwyn Heights and is famous for its historic downtown, charming boutiques, and scenic waterfront.
  • Baltimore - Baltimore is less than an hour’s drive from Berwyn Heights and has many attractions, including the National Aquarium, historic buildings, and vibrant nightlife.
