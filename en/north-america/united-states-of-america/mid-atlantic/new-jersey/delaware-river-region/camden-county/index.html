A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Camden County, New Jersey

Camden County can be found in the Delaware River Region of New Jersey, in Mid-Atlantic region of the United States of America, in North America. It is a region with a rich history, a vibrant culture, and scenic natural attractions. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will provide you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Camden County, including a list of places to visit, the best things to do, where to eat and drink, where to stay, and much more.

Introduction to Camden County

Located in southern New Jersey, Camden County is a diverse and dynamic region that boasts a rich history, vibrant culture, and beautiful natural surroundings. With year-round attractions and activities, visitors to Camden County can experience the best that New Jersey has to offer.

Camden County has a variety of places that are worth visiting. Here is a list of some of the must-visit places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Battleship New Jersey Museum and Memorial
  • Camden County Historical Society Museum
  • Indian King Tavern Museum
  • Walt Whitman House
  • Camden Waterfront

  • Cooper River Park

Hiking/Biking Routes of Camden County

For outdoor enthusiasts, Camden County has several hiking and biking trails that offer stunning views and a chance to experience nature. Here are a few favorite routes:

  • Cooper River Trail
  • Timber Creek Trail
  • Pennsauken Creek Trail
  • Black Run Preserve Trail
  • Pine Barrens Trail

Best Things to Do in Camden County: Activities and Tours

Camden County offers a vast array of activities and tours that cater to different interests. Here are some of the best things to do in Camden County:

  • Visit Adventure Aquarium
  • Take a sunset cruise along the Delaware River
  • Go brewery hopping
  • Attend a concert at BB&T Pavilion
  • Take a hot air balloon ride

If you are planning to spend a week in Camden County, here is a suggested itinerary:

Local Culture and Customs of Camden County

Camden County has a distinct local culture that is influenced by its location in New Jersey, as well as its proximity to Philadelphia. People in Camden County are known for their love of diners, sports teams, and music.

Best Time to Visit Camden County

The best time to visit Camden County is between April to June and September to November when the weather is mild, and the crowds are thinner. The peak season of July and August can be hot and humid, and the crowds can be substantial.

Do’s and Don’ts of Camden County

Do:

  • Visit the Battleship New Jersey Museum and Memorial and explore the history of the region
  • Go hiking in Pine Barrens
  • Try local specialties, such as pork roll and tomato pie
  • Attend a concert at BB&T Pavilion

Don’t:

  • Leave trash in the parks or on the trails
  • Smoke in public places
  • Jaywalk

Safety and Health

Safety in Camden County

Camden County is generally safe for visitors. However, it is always recommended to stay aware of your surroundings and take the usual precautions.

Health in Camden County

Visitors to Camden County should ensure that they have appropriate health insurance before traveling. There are several hospitals and medical centers in the region, including Cooper University Health Care, Virtua Health, and Jefferson Health - New Jersey.
