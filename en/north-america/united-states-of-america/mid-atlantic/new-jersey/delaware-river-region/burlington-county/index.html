Introduction to Burlington County

Burlington County is a charming destination situated in the Delaware River Region, New Jersey. Its strategic location makes it a popular gateway destination to explore the Mid-Atlantic region. Burlington County is known for its rich history, natural beauty, and vibrant culture. Visitors can explore its many museums, parks, hiking trails, and experience its warm hospitality.

List of Places to Visit in Burlington County

Whether you’re a history buff, nature lover, or adventure seeker, Burlington County has something to offer everyone.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Burlington County Historical Society Museum: Explore the rich history of Burlington County and see fascinating exhibits, artifacts, and documents that tell the story of the region’s past.
  • Smithville Mansion: Take a guided tour of this stunning mansion that dates back to the 1840s and learn about its history and architecture.
  • Mount Holly Prison Museum: Discover the history of Mount Holly Prison and learn about the lives of inmates and the correctional system that spanned over 200 years.

Natural Attractions of Burlington County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Rancocas Nature Center: Experience the beauty of nature in this serene and scenic park that features over 200 acres of forests, creeks, and ponds, and offers various trails for hiking and biking.
  • Bass River State Forest: Explore the expansive forest with its peaceful streams, serene lakes, and wildlife. Anglers can enjoy the park’s diverse fishing opportunities.
  • Historic Smithville Park: This picturesque park features over 1,300 acres of natural beauty, including open fields, wooded trails, and a lake. Visitors can enjoy fishing, picnicking, hiking, and birdwatching.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Burlington County

Burlington County has plenty of trails for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Below are some top routes to explore:

  • Burlington County Bike Trails: This massive network of bike trails spans over 10 miles and offers various trails for riders of different skill levels.
  • Bass River State Forest: With its diverse terrain and winding paths, this state forest is a favorite for bikers and hikers alike.
  • Rancocas State Park: This beautiful park offers over 17 miles of hiking trails that wind through fields, forests, and along the Rancocas Creek.

Best Things to Do in Burlington County: Activities and Tours

  • Ghost Tour of Burlington: Take a spooky and informative tour of Burlington’s dark past and explore its haunted places and chilling mysteries.
  • Hot Air Balloon Rides in the Skies of Burlington: Experience Burlington County’s beauty from a new perspective with a hot air balloon ride that offers breathtaking views of the region’s natural beauty.
  • Whitesbog Village: Take a tour of this historic village and learn about its cranberry farming culture and its connections to the famous author, J.D. Salinger.

Week-Long Itinerary for Burlington County

Here is a suggested itinerary for a week in Burlington County:

Day Activities
1 Visit Smithville Mansion and take a tour of the adjacent Historic Smithville Park.
2 Visit the Burlington County Historical Society Museum and take a stroll through the charming town of Mount Holly.
3 Tour the beautiful Bass River State Forest and enjoy a picnic lunch among the beautiful trees.
4 Explore the diverse trails of Rancocas State Park and treat yourself to some local food at the nearby restaurants.
5 Enjoy the thrills of a hot air balloon ride above Burlington County’s natural beauty.
6 Take a hike through Wharton State Forest and visit Whitesbog Village.
7 Enjoy a leisurely day at an area winery and sample some of the best local wines.

Food and Drink Scene of Burlington County

Must-Try Dishes

Burlington County is famous for its diverse food scene, with plenty of options for every palate. Here are some local dishes that you must try:

  • Salt Water Taffy: This classic candy is a New Jersey staple, and Burlington County has some of the best.
  • Jersey Tomato: Enjoy a juicy and fresh tomato in salads, sandwiches, or as an accompaniment to any meal.
  • Pork Roll: This breakfast meat is a cult favorite in New Jersey. Try it in sandwiches or mixed with eggs for a hearty breakfast.

Local Beverages

  • River Horse Brewing Company: This brewery offers a range of handcrafted beers that are a must-try for beer lovers.
  • Valenzano Winery: Visit this beautiful winery and sample excellent wines that are made with local fruit.
  • Drip N Scoop: Enjoy some of the best artisanal coffee and ice cream in the area at this popular local shop.

Where to Stay in Burlington County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Burlington County offers plenty of options for comfortable and affordable lodging. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • Hampton Inn Burlington/Mount Holly: This hotel offers comfortable rooms, a pool, and a fitness center at a reasonable price.
  • Isaac Hilliard House: This charming bed and breakfast is located in a historic building and features antique furnishings and a beautiful garden.
  • Fort Dix Hotel: This hotel offers affordable rooms and is conveniently located near various attractions.

What to Buy in Burlington County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Burlington County has plenty of local products that make perfect souvenirs. Here are some items that are worth bringing home:

  • New Jersey- Themed Apparel: Get a New Jersey-themed t-shirt or sweatshirt with Burlington County’s name on it.
  • Burlington County Map: A colorful map of Burlington County makes a great decoration piece for your home.
  • Local Honey and Jams: Get some delicious and original honey and jams made with local fruits and flowers at the local farmers market.

Local Culture and Customs of Burlington County

Burlington County is known for its diverse culture and warm hospitality. Visitors should be respectful of local customs, which include:

  • Politeness: New Jerseyans are known for their directness, but visitors should still be polite and courteous.
  • Tipping: Tipping is customary in most service industries, such as restaurants, hair salons, and taxis.
  • Beach etiquette: Visitors should be mindful of their litter when visiting the beach and clean up after themselves.

Best Time to Visit Burlington County

The best time to visit Burlington County is during the spring and fall seasons when the weather is temperate, and there are plenty of outdoor activities and events to enjoy. Summer is also a popular time to visit, but accommodations can be costly, and the weather can be hot and humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Burlington County

Do:

  • Take a historical tour: Learn about the history of Burlington County with one of the many guided tours available.
  • Try the local cuisine: Burlington County has a diverse food scene, so make sure to try some local dishes.
  • Visit the local farmers market: Burlington County is famous for its Farmers Markets, which offer fresh produce, honey, jams, and more.

Don’t:

  • Litter: Visitors should be mindful of litter when visiting natural parks and beaches and make sure to clean up after themselves.
  • Be Loud: Visitors should be mindful of their noise levels and avoid being too loud in quiet neighborhoods.
  • Be Rude: Visitors should be polite and courteous to the locals and avoid being rude or confrontational.

Safety and Health

Safety in Burlington County

Burlington County is generally a safe destination, but visitors should still take precautions, including:

  • Traveling with a companion: It’s always a good idea to travel with someone, even if you’re exploring during the day.
  • Know emergency numbers: Be aware of emergency numbers so you can get help if needed.
  • Don’t carry too much cash: Avoid carrying too much cash and use credit cards or debit cards instead.

Health in Burlington County

Burlington County has excellent healthcare facilities, and visitors can access medical services if necessary. It’s always a good idea to have travel insurance that covers medical emergencies.

Getting to Burlington County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Burlington County

  • Philadelphia International Airport: Located approximately 30 miles from Burlington County, Philadelphia International Airport is the most convenient option for visitors flying in.

Bus Stations Near Burlington County

  • NJ Transit: NJ Transit operates numerous bus routes throughout the county, providing excellent connectivity to nearby destinations.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Car rental services are available throughout the county, and visitors can enjoy the freedom to explore at their leisure.

How to Get Around in Burlington County

Burlington County offers various modes of transportation, including:

  • Buses: NJ Transit bus services provide convenient connectivity throughout the county and beyond.
  • Taxi Services: Taxis are available in the county’s major towns and cities.
  • Car Rental: Car rental services are available throughout the county, providing visitors with the freedom to explore at their pace.

Where to Go Next from Burlington County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are many exciting destinations nearby Burlington County that visitors can explore, including:

  • Atlantic City: Enjoy the excitement of casinos, nightlife, and luxury resorts in this famous seaside city.
  • Philadelphia: Just 30 minutes away, Philadelphia is a vibrant city full of history, culture, and entertainment.
  • Cape May: Experience Victorian charm in this picturesque town that features stunning beaches, scenic tree-lined streets, and historic Victorian architecture.

Conclusion

Burlington County is a charming destination that offers something for everyone. Whether you enjoy hiking, biking, exploring history, or trying local cuisine, Burlington County has got you covered. Plan your visit today and experience the warm hospitality and natural beauty of the Delaware River Region.
