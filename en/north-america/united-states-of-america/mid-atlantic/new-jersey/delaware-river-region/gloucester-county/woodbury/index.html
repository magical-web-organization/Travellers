Introduction to Woodbury

Woodbury, located in Gloucester County in Delaware River Region in New Jersey, is a small yet charming town with rich culture and history nestled in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States of America. This quaint town boasts of beautiful historic buildings, lovely parks, museums, and a variety of recreational activities making it an ideal place for a day trip or a weekend getaway.

List of Places to Visit in Woodbury

Here is a list of the top attractions to discover in Woodbury:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Gloucester County Historical Society Museum: This museum showcases the history and culture of Gloucester County through exhibits, artifacts, and interactive displays.
  • Greenwich Township Historical Society Museum: Learn about the history and heritage of Gloucester County through the various exhibits and tours offered at this museum.
  • Woodbury African American Museum: This museum is dedicated to preserving and promoting the African American heritage of Woodbury and the surrounding region.
  • Paddock Mansion: This beautiful historic building, now serving as a bed and breakfast, was built in 1882. You can take a tour of the mansion and learn about its rich history.

Natural Attractions of Woodbury: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Bell Lake Preserve: This beautiful nature preserve has hiking trails, a lake, and scenic views, making it perfect for a day out in nature.
  • Woodbury Creek Park: Enjoy fishing, boating, and picnicking at this lovely park located along the Woodbury Creek.
  • Tinicum Creek Preserve: This preserve features a variety of habitats and landscapes, including forests, fields, wetlands, and streams, along with a six-mile-long trail that is perfect for hiking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Woodbury

Woodbury has a variety of hiking and biking routes to cater to visitors of all skill levels. Here are some of the most popular ones:

  • Woodbury Woodlands Trail: This 2.6-mile-long trail loops around the Woodbury city limits and is perfect for a leisurely walk.
  • Bell Lake Preserve Trail: This trail is perfect for a scenic hike or bike ride around the beautiful Bell Lake Preserve.
  • Tinicum Creek Trail: Enjoy a six-mile-long hike or bike ride through the varied landscapes of Tinicum Creek Preserve.

Best Things to Do in Woodbury: Activities and Tours

Looking for some fun things to do in Woodbury? Here are some of the best activities and tours in town:

  • Woodbury Ghost Tour: Take a spooky guided tour of Woodbury’s haunted buildings, graveyards, and hidden secrets.
  • Woodbury Farmers Market: Head to the farmers market for fresh produce, artisanal products, and live music every Saturday morning between May and November.
  • Woodbury Fall Arts Festival: Don’t miss this annual arts festival that takes place in the fall, where you can enjoy live music, food vendors, art exhibits, and more.

Week-Long Itinerary for Woodbury

Day 1: Start your day with a visit to the Gloucester County Historical Society Museum, followed by a relaxing picnic at Woodbury Creek Park. End your day with a delicious dinner at the Paddock Mansion.

Day 2: Take a scenic hike through the Tinicum Creek Trail, followed by lunch at one of the town’s local cafes. Spend the evening at the Woodbury African American Museum, followed by dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 3: Explore the Bell Lake Preserve Trail and enjoy a picnic lunch at the preserve’s beautiful lake. In the evening, attend a show at the Broadway Theatre of Pitman.

Day 4: Take a guided tour of the Greenwhich Township Historical Society Museum, followed by lunch at the farmers market. End your day with a visit to the Woodbury Fall Arts Festival.

Day 5: Take a day trip to Philadelphia and explore the city’s rich history and culture.

Day 6: Spend the day shopping for souvenirs in the downtown area and enjoy dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 7: Relax and rejuvenate with a spa day at one of the town’s award-winning spas and end your vacation with a quiet dinner at your hotel.

Food and Drink Scene of Woodbury

Woodbury has a unique and thriving food and drink scene, with a variety of locally owned restaurants and cafes. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cheesesteak: A classic Philly cheesesteak made with thinly sliced steak and melted cheese in a soft roll.
  • Chicken Cutlet Sandwich: A delicious sandwich made with crispy breaded chicken cutlets topped with lettuce, tomato, and mayonnaise.
  • Pork Roll: A New Jersey staple, pork roll is a delicious type of processed meat that is served grilled or fried on a sandwich or as a breakfast meat.

Local Beverages

  • Apple Cider: Made with freshly pressed apples, this refreshing drink is perfect for autumn.
  • Beer: Woodbury has a growing craft beer scene with several local breweries, including Death of the Fox Brewing Co. and Eight & Sand Beer Co.
  • Wine: Head to Heritage Vineyards to sample some local wines and enjoy the beautiful countryside scenery.

Where to Stay in Woodbury: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Woodbury has a variety of accommodation options to cater to all budgets and preferences. Here are some options to consider:

  • The Colonial Inn: An elegant bed and breakfast with beautiful gardens and historic decor.
  • Hampton Inn & Suites: This modern hotel features comfortable rooms and a complimentary breakfast.
  • Airbnb: Opt for a more authentic and budget-friendly experience by renting an Airbnb.

What to Buy in Woodbury: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Looking for some unique souvenirs and locally made products to bring home? Here are some places to consider:

  • Artists’ Market: Pick up some unique handmade art pieces, vintage items, and crafts from local artists.
  • Woodbury Antiques & Collectibles: This antique store features vintage furniture, clothing, and collectibles from different eras.
  • Woodbury Station Cafe: Head to this cafe for a cup of locally roasted coffee and pick up some bags of coffee beans to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Woodbury

Woodbury has a rich history and culture, with many festivals and events that celebrate the town’s heritage. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and it is common to engage in small talk with strangers.

Best Time to Visit Woodbury

The best time to visit Woodbury is during the spring and fall, when the weather is pleasant, and the town’s outdoor attractions are at their best. The fall is also the time for the annual Woodbury Fall Arts Festival, which is a must-see event for art enthusiasts.

Do’s and Don’ts of Woodbury

Do:

  • Respect the town’s historic buildings and structures.
  • Explore the beautiful natural attractions around the town.
  • Shop at local stores and browse through antique shops to discover unique finds.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the town’s natural beauty.
  • Be disrespectful to the local culture and customs.
  • Take photographs of locals without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Woodbury

Woodbury is a safe and welcoming town with a low crime rate, making it an ideal destination for families and solo travelers. However, it is always advisable to be cautious and aware of your surroundings, especially when exploring natural areas.

Health in Woodbury

Woodbury has several hospitals and healthcare facilities that offer a full range of medical services. It is recommended to have health insurance while traveling to the United States.

Getting to Woodbury: Transportation Options and Logistics

  • Atlantic City International Airport: Located about 50 miles from Woodbury.

Bus Stations Near Woodbury

  • Greyhound Bus Station: Located in Philadelphia, about 20 miles away from Woodbury.
  • Peter Pan Bus Station: Located in Philadelphia, about 20 miles away from Woodbury.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Renting a car is a great option for those who want to explore the surrounding areas.
  • Taxi Services and Ride-Sharing Apps: Uber and Lyft operate in the Woodbury area.

How to Get Around in Woodbury

Woodbury is a small town that can easily be explored on foot. However, if you don’t want to walk, you can rent a bike or use a taxi or ride-sharing app.

Where to Go Next from Woodbury: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Philadelphia: Explore the birthplace of America and indulge in the city’s rich cultural heritage.
  • Newark: Discover the charming small-town vibe of Newark and its beautiful parks and natural attractions.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an adventurous weekend, Woodbury offers something for everyone. From exploring the town’s fascinating history to hiking through beautiful nature preserves, Woodbury is the perfect destination for a memorable vacation.
