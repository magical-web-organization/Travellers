Introduction to Ewing

Ewing is a charming township located in Mercer County, in the Delaware River Region of New Jersey. This city features stunning natural beauty, a bustling food and drink scene, and an array of cultural attractions that make it an ideal destination for travelers seeking an unforgettable experience.

Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, a history buff, or a foodie, Ewing has something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll guide you through the best places to visit, the top activities to do, and the local culture and customs of this charming city.

List of Places to Visit in Ewing

Ewing is home to a diverse range of attractions, including historical sites, museums, natural parks, and cultural hotspots. Here are the top places to visit during your trip to Ewing:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • New Jersey State Museum - The New Jersey State Museum is a fascinating destination for history lovers. This museum features exhibits on art, archaeology, history, science, and more, and is a great place to explore New Jersey’s rich cultural heritage.

  • Old Barracks Museum - The Old Barracks Museum is a fascinating exhibit that showcases New Jersey’s military history. This museum features life-sized exhibits of soldiers’ quarters, kitchens, and more, giving visitors an immersive experience of colonial times.

  • Grounds for Sculpture - Grounds for Sculpture is a sprawling 42-acre sculpture park that features works by renowned artists from around the world. This park is a great place to explore art in a natural setting and to take beautiful photos.

Natural Attractions of Ewing: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mercer County Park - Mercer County Park is a stunning natural park that features beautiful lakes, trails, and picnic areas. This park is a great destination for travelers who love hiking, biking, or enjoying the Great Outdoors.

  • D&R Canal State Park - The D&R Canal State Park is a picturesque park that stretches over more than 70 miles from Trenton to New Brunswick. This park features an array of activities, including hiking, biking, fishing, and more.

  • Washington Crossing State Park - Washington Crossing State Park is a historic park that commemorates George Washington’s 1776 Delaware River crossing. This park features education exhibits, beautiful hiking trails, and stunning views of the Delaware River.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ewing

Ewing is an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts who enjoy hiking, biking, or running. Here are the top hiking and biking routes in Ewing:

  • Baldpate Mountain Trail - The Baldpate Mountain Trail is a beautiful hiking trail that offers stunning views of the Delaware River and the surrounding countryside. This three-mile trail is a great option for beginners and experienced hikers alike.

  • Mercer County Park Marina Trail - The Mercer County Park Marina Trail is a scenic two-mile trail that winds through the park’s wetlands, lakes, and picnic areas. This trail is a great option for families with children or travelers who want to enjoy a leisurely stroll amidst nature.

  • D&R Canal State Park Trail - The D&R Canal State Park Trail is a stunning 70-mile trail that is ideal for bikers, hikers, and runners. This trail features multiple access points, restrooms, and picnic areas, making it a convenient option for travelers of all ages.

Best Things to Do in Ewing: Activities and Tours

Ewing offers an array of activities and tours that cater to diverse interests. Here are the top things to do in Ewing:

  • Ewing Green Team Farmers Market - The Ewing Green Team Farmers Market is a popular destination for foodies and those who are interested in sustainable agriculture. This market features an array of fresh, local, and organic produce, as well as artisanal cheeses, baked goods, and more.

  • Terhune Orchards and Winery - Terhune Orchards and Winery is a family-owned farm that has been in operation for over 40 years. This farm offers tours, tastings, and an array of seasonal activities that are fun for the whole family.

  • Princeton Ghost Tour - The Princeton Ghost Tour is a unique and spooky experience that offers a guided tour of the city’s haunted sites. This tour is a great option for travelers who love history and enjoy a good scare.
