Introduction to Millville

Located in Cumberland County in the Delaware River Region of New Jersey, Millville is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Situated in the heart of Mid-Atlantic region of the United States of America, Millville boasts of a rich history, cultural attractions, scenic views, and outdoor adventures. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will help you discover the must-visit places, the best things to do, where to eat, and where to stay in Millville. Let’s get started!

Here is a list of the top attractions and places to visit in Millville:

  • Millville Army Air Field Museum
  • Wheaton Arts and Cultural Center

Best Things to Do in Millville: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re travelling with family or solo, there are many things to do in Millville. Here are some of them:

  • Take a hot air balloon ride with Sky High Hot Air Balloons
  • Go on a Sunset Wine Cruise with Belleview Winery
  • Attend a live theater show at the Levoy Theatre
  • Take a glass blowing workshop at Wheaton Arts and Cultural Center
  • Visit the Millville Army Air Field Museum

Local Culture and Customs of Millville

Millville is known for its rich glassblowing tradition and its connection to the military. Visitors are encouraged to take part in the various cultural events, festivals, and celebrations held throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Millville

The best time to visit Millville is during the spring (April to June) and fall (September to November) when the weather is mild and pleasant. The summer months (July and August) can be hot and humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Millville

Do:

  • Visit the Millville Army Air Field Museum
  • Take a glass blowing workshop at Wheaton Arts and Cultural Center
  • Try the local cuisine and wine
  • Attend a live theatre show at the Levoy Theatre

Don’t:

  • Pick wildflowers or plants in the natural areas
  • Litter in the parks or natural areas
  • Ignore safety rules while engaging in outdoor adventures

Safety and Health

Safety in Millville

Millville is a safe place to visit. However, visitors are advised to take standard safety precautions to avoid petty crimes like pickpocketing and theft.

Health in Millville

Millville has decent health facilities, and visitors can access medical attention in case of emergencies. It is advisable to have medical insurance when traveling to Millville.
