Introduction to Southern Shore

Southern Shore is a beautiful region located in New Jersey, United States. This region is known for its stunning beaches, rich history, and diverse culture. The region is home to several well-known cities, such as Cape May, Stone Harbor, and Wildwood. Southern Shore is a popular tourist destination, especially during the summer months when visitors flock to enjoy the sandy beaches and warm weather.

List of Places to Visit in Southern Shore

There are many amazing places to visit in Southern Shore. Here are the top attractions in the region:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Cape May Lighthouse: Visit this iconic lighthouse and enjoy the panoramic views of the surrounding area.

  • Naval Air Station Wildwood Aviation Museum: This museum showcases the history of aviation and military personnel in the region.

  • Cape May County Historical Museum: Learn about the history of Cape May County and see a collection of local artifacts from the region.

Natural Attractions of Southern Shore: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Cape May Point State Park: This state park is a natural paradise that offers hiking trails, birdwatching, and stunning views of the shoreline.

  • Wildwood Beach: This beautiful beach is perfect for relaxing and soaking up the sun.

  • Corson’s Inlet State Park: This park offers visitors a chance to explore the native wildlife and ecosystem of the region.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Southern Shore

Southern Shore is a great destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the best routes in the region to explore:

  • Cape May Point Lighthouse Loop Trail: This 2.3-mile trail offers stunning views of the lighthouse and surrounding landscape.

  • Cape May County Park & Zoo Trail: This 3.1-mile trail is perfect for hiking and biking and offers visitors a chance to see many native wildlife species.

  • Cape May Bird Observatory Trails: These trails are perfect for birdwatchers and feature over 400 different bird species in the region.

Best Things to Do in Southern Shore: Activities and Tours

There are many fun activities to enjoy in Southern Shore. Here are some of the best things to do in the region:

  • Jet Ski Tours: Take a thrill ride on the open waters of Southern Shore with a guided Jet Ski tour.

  • Cape May Food & Wine Tours: Discover the local cuisine and wines of Southern Shore with a guided food and wine tour.

  • Sunset Bay Cruise: Enjoy a relaxing cruise of the gorgeous sunsets of the region.

Safety and Health

Safety in Southern Shore

Southern Shore is a generally safe region to visit. However, visitors should be mindful of their safety and take precautions when exploring the region.

  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially in busy areas like the boardwalks and beaches.

  • Take care when swimming in the ocean and be aware of any potential dangers like rip currents.

  • Practice good hygiene and take precautions against insect bites like ticks and mosquitoes.

Health in Southern Shore

Southern Shore is a relatively healthy region to visit. Visitors should be mindful of any potential health risks and take precautions as necessary.

  • Stay hydrated and avoid overexertion in the heat.

  • Wear appropriate clothing and use sunscreen to protect against the sun’s harmful rays.

  • Seek medical attention if you begin to feel unwell while visiting the region.
