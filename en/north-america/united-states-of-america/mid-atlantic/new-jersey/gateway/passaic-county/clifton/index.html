A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Clifton, NJ

Introduction to Clifton

Located in Passaic County, New Jersey, Clifton is a small city with a population of just over 85,000. It’s located in the Gateway region of the state, and is just a short drive from New York City, making it a popular destination for those looking to explore the surrounding area. Clifton is known for its rich history, its beautiful natural surroundings, and its diverse cultural attractions.

List of Places to Visit in Clifton

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re interested in history and culture, Clifton has a number of unique attractions to explore. Some of the must-visit museums and historical places include:

  • Clifton Arts Center: The Clifton Arts Center is a community arts center that hosts a wide range of exhibitions and events throughout the year. It’s housed in a historic building that was once a library, and is a must-visit for anyone interested in the local arts scene.
  • Lambert Castle: One of Clifton’s most famous landmarks, Lambert Castle is a historic castle that was built in 1892. Today, it’s a museum that offers a fascinating glimpse into Clifton’s history and culture.
  • Garis Gallery of the American West: This small museum is dedicated to preserving the history and culture of the American West, and features a range of exhibits and artifacts from the region’s past.

Natural Attractions of Clifton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you’re looking to explore Clifton’s natural beauty, there are a number of outdoor attractions worth checking out. Here are a few of the top options:

  • Garret Mountain Reservation: This large park is home to over 500 acres of lush forests, rolling hills, and scenic views. It’s a popular spot for picnics, hiking, and other outdoor activities.
  • Clifton Skateworld and Fun Center: Located in the heart of Clifton, this family-friendly entertainment center features an ice rink, a roller skating rink, and a range of other fun activities.
  • Weasel Brook Park: This beautiful park features a winding creek, a range of manicured gardens and lawns, and several walking trails to explore.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Clifton

Clifton is home to a number of great hiking and biking routes, offering visitors the chance to explore the beautiful natural scenery of the area. Some of the top options include:

  • The Lenape Trail: This hiking trail stretches over 30 miles through Northern New Jersey, and runs right through Clifton. It offers hikers the chance to explore a range of natural habitats, from lush forests to rolling hills.
  • The Morris Canal Greenway Trail: This biking trail follows the path of the historic Morris Canal, which was once an important transportation route in the area. Today, the trail offers bikers a chance to explore the beautiful scenery of the area, including the Passaic River and the Great Falls of Paterson.

Best Things to Do in Clifton: Activities and Tours

If you’re looking for fun things to do in Clifton, there are a range of activities and tours to consider. Here are a few of the top options:

  • Clifton Paintball: This popular paintball park is a great option for those looking for an adrenaline rush. It features a range of different playing fields, from forested areas to open fields.
  • The Clifton Wine Tasting Tour: If you’re a wine lover, this tour is a must-visit. It takes you on a journey through some of Clifton’s best wineries, giving you the chance to taste the local flavors and learn about the winemaking process.
  • The Clifton Food Tour: This guided tour takes you on a culinary journey through some of Clifton’s best restaurants and eateries. You’ll have the chance to taste a range of different dishes, from classic American fare to international cuisine.

Week-Long Itinerary for Clifton

If you’re planning to spend a week in Clifton, here’s a suggested itinerary to help you make the most of your time:

Day 1 Explore the Garret Mountain Reservation and visit the Lambert Castle
Day 2 Take the Clifton Food Tour and explore the local dining scene

Day 7 Relax at Weasel Brook Park and enjoy a leisurely picnic

Food and Drink Scene of Clifton

Must-Try Dishes

Clifton is known for its diverse dining scene, with a range of tasty dishes to sample. Here are a few must-try dishes:

  • The Famous Clifton Hot Dog: This classic hot dog is a staple of the local food scene, and has been around for over 80 years. It’s topped with chili, mustard, and onions, and is a must-try for any hot dog lover.
  • Italian Ice: Clifton is home to a number of great Italian ice stands, offering visitors a refreshing treat on a hot day.
  • The Clam Pie: This unique pizza topping is a must-try for any pizza lover. It features clams, cheese, garlic, and other toppings, and is a local favorite.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beer: Clifton is home to a number of great craft breweries, offering visitors a chance to sample a range of local beers.

Best Time to Visit Clifton

The best time to visit Clifton is during the spring and fall, when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller. The summer months can be hot and humid, while the winter months can be cold and snowy. If you’re planning to visit during the summer, be sure to pack sunscreen and stay hydrated.
