Haworth is a charming small town located in Bergen County, New Jersey. It is known for its well-preserved historic homes, beautiful natural scenery, and rich cultural heritage. Whether you’re a history buff, a nature lover, or just looking for a peaceful getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life, Haworth has something to offer everyone. In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ll take you on a journey through the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and local culture and customs of Haworth, New Jersey.

Introduction to Haworth

Haworth is a small town in Bergen County with a population of just over 3,000 people. It was founded in 1904 and has a rich history, which is reflected in the many well-preserved homes and historic sites located throughout the town. Haworth is also known for its natural beauty, with several parks and nature reserves located within and around the town.

List of Places to Visit in Haworth

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Haworth Historical Society Museum: This museum is housed in the Haworth Library and features exhibits on the town’s history, including its founding, early settlement, and notable residents.

  • Zabriskie House: Built in the mid-18th century, this historic home is one of the oldest in Haworth and is listed on the National Register of Historic Places.

  • Haworth Post Office: This historic building dates back to the early 1900s and is a great example of early 20th-century architecture.

Natural Attractions of Haworth: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Haworth Nature Reserve: This 25-acre park features walking trails, a pond, and scenic views of the surrounding woodland.

  • Tamaques Park: Located just outside Haworth, this park features hiking trails, a playground, and picnic areas.

  • Overpeck County Park: This expansive park features walking trails, a lake, and a bird observatory.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Haworth

  • The Haworth Nature Reserve Trail: This easy trail is just over a mile long and offers great views of the surrounding woods and pond.

  • The Tamaques Park Trail: This moderate trail is just over three miles long and features scenic views of the Tamaques nature preserve.

  • The Overpeck Park Trail: This trail is just over five miles long and features scenic views of the Overpeck Creek and the surrounding woodland.

Best Things to Do in Haworth: Activities and Tours

  • Guided Tours of Haworth: The Haworth Historical Society offers guided tours of the town’s historic buildings and sites.

  • Scenic Drives: Haworth is located in a beautiful area of New Jersey, and there are many scenic drives that you can take to enjoy the natural beauty of the surrounding countryside.

  • Wine Tasting: Haworth is located near several wineries that offer tours and tastings, so if you’re a wine enthusiast, this is a great activity to explore.

Week-Long Itinerary for Haworth

Day 1: Visit the Haworth Historical Society Museum and take a guided tour of the town’s historic sites.

Day 2: Explore the Haworth Nature Reserve and take a hike on one of its many trails.

Day 3: Visit the Zabriskie House and learn about its history and significance to Haworth.

Day 4: Take a scenic drive through the countryside surrounding Haworth and enjoy the natural beauty of the area.

Day 5: Visit a local winery and enjoy a wine tasting tour.

Day 6: Spend the day at Tamaques Park and take a picnic lunch.

Day 7: Visit Overpeck County Park and go bird-watching at the bird observatory.

Food and Drink Scene of Haworth

Must-Try Dishes

  • New Jersey-style pizza: Haworth is located near several pizzerias that serve delicious New Jersey-style pizza.

  • Bagels: New Jersey is known for its delicious bagels, and Haworth is no exception.

  • Seafood: Given Haworth’s location near the coast, seafood is a must-try dish while visiting the town.

Local Beverages

  • Wine: Haworth is located near several wineries that produce high-quality wines.

  • Craft Beer: New Jersey is known for its thriving craft beer scene, and there are many local breweries located near Haworth.

Where to Stay in Haworth: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Inn at Canal Square: This charming hotel is located in the heart of Haworth and features beautifully decorated rooms and a cozy atmosphere.

  • The Summer House: This guesthouse is located just outside Haworth and features comfortable rooms and a beautiful garden.

  • The Grand Cascades Lodge: This luxurious hotel is located near Haworth and offers amenities such as a spa, an indoor pool, and a golf course.

What to Buy in Haworth: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handmade crafts and jewelry: Haworth is home to several artisanal shops that sell handmade crafts and jewelry.

  • Local produce: There are several farmers’ markets near Haworth that sell fresh, locally grown produce.

  • Wine: Haworth is located near several wineries that produce high-quality wines, making it a great place to find unique and delicious souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Haworth

Haworth is known for its rich cultural heritage and tight-knit community. Residents often participate in community events such as the annual Haworth Arts Festival and the Haworth Harvest Fest. Visitors are welcomed with open arms and are encouraged to immerse themselves in the local culture during their stay.

Best Time to Visit Haworth

The best time to visit Haworth is in the spring or fall, when the weather is mild and the natural scenery is at its most beautiful. Summer can be quite hot, and the town is often crowded with tourists during this season. Winter can be cold and snowy, which may limit outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Haworth

Do:

  • Take a guided tour of the town’s historic sites.

  • Explore the natural beauty of Haworth’s many parks and nature reserves.

  • Try the local cuisine, including seafood and New Jersey-style pizza.

Don’t:

  • Miss out on the Haworth Arts Festival or Haworth Harvest Fest.

Safety and Health

Safety in Haworth

Haworth is a safe town with relatively low crime rates. Visitors should still take basic precautions, such as locking their car doors and not leaving valuables in plain sight.

Health in Haworth

Haworth has several medical facilities located within and near the town, including hospitals and urgent care centers. Visitors should also be aware of any health risks associated with outdoor activities, such as hiking.

Getting to Haworth: Transportation Options and Logistics

  • Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR): Newark is the closest major airport to Haworth, located about 30 minutes away by car.

  • LaGuardia Airport (LGA): LaGuardia is another major airport located about an hour away from Haworth.

Bus Stations Near Haworth

  • Haworth is served by several local bus routes that connect it to other nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

How to Get Around in Haworth

Haworth is a small town, and most of its attractions are located within walking distance of each other. However, visitors who prefer to drive can rent a car or use a taxi or rideshare service.
