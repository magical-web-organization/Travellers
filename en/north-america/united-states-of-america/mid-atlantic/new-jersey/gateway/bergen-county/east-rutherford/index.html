Introduction to East Rutherford

Located in Bergen County, East Rutherford is a small but bustling town in New Jersey that serves as a gateway to the greater Bergen County and nearby New York City. Its strategic location, affordable accommodations, and proximity to major transportation hubs, make it a great starting point for exploring the region.

List of Places to Visit in East Rutherford

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

East Rutherford may be small, but it’s rich in history and culture. Below are some of the top cultural attractions to visit:

  • MetLife Stadium: Home of the NFL’s New York Giants and Jets, it’s a must-visit destination for football fans. It’s also a hub for concerts and other events.
  • Meadowlands Racing and Entertainment: A unique entertainment complex that brings together horse racing, casino games, and fantastic dining and nightlife.
  • Felician University Castle: A beautiful example of Gothic architecture, the castle is part of the Felician University campus and offers guided tours to visitors.

Natural Attractions of East Rutherford: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For nature lovers, East Rutherford has no shortage of outdoor adventures and breathtaking views. Here are some of the best:

  • Richard W. DeKorte Park: A bird-watching haven that offers beautiful views of the New York City skyline.
  • NJSEA Natural Area: Another great bird-watching spot with 600 acres of tidal marsh and grasslands.
  • Liberty State Park: Not technically in East Rutherford, but just a short drive away, this park offers unbeatable views of the Manhattan skyline and the Statue of Liberty.

Hiking/Biking Routes of East Rutherford

For those who want to explore the beauty of East Rutherford on foot or pedal, there are several options to choose from.

  • Meadowlands River Trail: A 5.5 miles bike ride that stretches from Lyndhurst to Carlstadt and offers stunning views of the Hackensack River.
  • Ponderosa Park Trail: A short hiking trail that boasts of the beautiful duck pond, picnic area and a playground for children.
  • Richard W. DeKorte Park Trail: A three-mile hike that offers fertile and wildlife-rich salt marshes, tidal mudflats, and floating vegetation islands.

Best Things to Do in East Rutherford: Activities and Tours

Apart from the attractions mentioned above, East Rutherford also offers a variety of fun activities and tours to suit different tastes.

  • Escape the Room: An escape game experience that challenges your puzzle-solving skills with different themed rooms.
  • Enigma Escape Rooms: Another puzzle-themed interesting game that requires teamwork and creativity.
  • Humdingers: An indoor socializing spot with a full-service bar and a bowling alley.

Food and Drink Scene of East Rutherford

No trip to any place is complete without trying out their local cuisine. Here are some of the must-try dishes and beverages in East Rutherford:

Must-Try Dishes:

  • Italian Hot Dogs: Deep-fried hot dogs served on Italian bread with onions, peppers, and potatoes.
  • Beef Brisket: Fatty and juicy oriental-style brisket prepared with a unique BBQ sauce
  • Pizza: Thin crust, fresh and tasty pies topped with fresh tomatoes, basil, and mozzarella cheese.

Local Beverages:

  • Craft Beer: East Rutherford has several microbreweries that produce unique craft beer flavors that you can’t find anywhere else.
  • Clamato Juice: A flavorful tomato cocktail, a favorite beverage of many East Rutherford residents.

Where to Stay in East Rutherford: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

East Rutherford offers a mix of accommodations that cater to different budgets and tastes. Here are some of the top options:

  • Residence Inn by Marriott: An all-suite hotel that provides a comfortable stay and is close to popular attractions and major highways
  • Meadowlands View Hotel: A budget-friendly hotel located right next to MetLife Stadium with fantastic city views and easy transport links.
  • Hyatt Place Secaucus/Meadowlands: A casual, high-end hotel that offers excellent service and amenities and is close to the airport and sporting arenas.

What to Buy in East Rutherford: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some unique local products to take home with you:

  • Giants and Jets Memorabilia: East Rutherford is home of both NFL teams and you will find plenty of merchandise to take home to remember your trip.
  • Tony’s Pizza located on Park Ave, and they make a great take-off souvenir of East Rutherford’s famous local food.

Local Culture and Customs of East Rutherford

East Rutherford is a friendly, vibrant town that welcomes visitors. The town respects its diverse community and promotes peace and harmony throughout the neighborhoods.

Best Time to Visit East Rutherford

The best time to visit East Rutherford is during the spring and fall months when the weather is mild and enjoyable, and the crowds are less compared to the summer months.

Do’s and Don’ts of East Rutherford

Do:

  • Do try the local food and drink scene. East Rutherford is a unique town with a culinary scene that is sure to entice you.
  • Do explore the natural beauty of East Rutherford. From hiking to bird watching, there’s plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter. East Rutherford takes pride in its clean and beautiful environment. Please do not litter on the streets or in any natural areas.
  • Don’t be late. Punctuality is essential for businesses, restaurants, and any appointments. Ensure you plan and get to your destinations on time.

Safety and Health

Safety in East Rutherford

East Rutherford is generally a safe town, however, some precautions can ensure you have a worry-free time.

  • Avoid walking in unknown areas alone at night.
  • Stay in well-lit areas.
  • Do not leave valuables inside your car.

Health in East Rutherford

The town has an accessible modern healthcare system, and the Rutgers Health Center is available to provide medical assistance if needed.

Getting to East Rutherford: Transportation Options and Logistics

East Rutherford’s central location provides plenty of transportation options, making it easily accessible by car, train, or bus. The following transport options will get you to the town:

Airports Near East Rutherford

  • Newark Liberty International Airport is the most easily accessible airport from East Rutherford, located approximately 12 miles away.

Bus Stations Near East Rutherford

  • NJ Transit Bus – Major bus lines that connect to New York and different parts of the state.

Other Options

  • Taxis -East Rutherford has many taxi services
  • Rental cars -if you plan on exploring the surrounding area, renting a car at any car rental companies would be the best idea.

How to Get Around in East Rutherford

The town has several transportation options to get around, whether you travel by car, taxi, or walking. The most common methods of transportation are:

  • Car rentals: Renting a car and driving will give you the freedom to explore at your leisure
  • NJ Transit Bus: The bus is the most accessible and affordable public transportation option.
  • Taxi: There are plenty of taxi services to help you if you need a ride.

Where to Go Next from East Rutherford: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

East Rutherford has some gems that are worth exploring if you have additional time during your visit:

  • New York City: America’s most significant city and just a short drive or train ride away from East Rutherford.
  • Paterson: Home to the Great Falls, the second-highest waterfall east of the Mississippi, worth a visit.
  • Hackensack: County seat of Bergen County, Hackensack has beautiful parks, museums, and a historic district.

Conclusion

East Rutherford is a destination that offers a unique experience to visitors, from rich cultural experiences and natural beauty to great food, great shopping, and much more. Plan your trip today using our comprehensive guide and get ready for a memorable time!
