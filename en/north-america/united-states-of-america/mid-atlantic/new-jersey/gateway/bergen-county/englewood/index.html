Introduction to Englewood

Located in Bergen County, New Jersey, Englewood is a charming city known for its rich culture and history, natural beauty, and outdoor adventures. The city offers a range of attractions for all types of travelers, from museums to hiking trails, from local cuisine to unique shopping experiences. Whether you are a foodie, a history buff, or an outdoor enthusiast, Englewood has something exciting to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Englewood

  • The Bergen Performing Arts Center
  • The Flat Rock Nature Center
  • The Englewood Public Library
  • The Englewood Historical Society & Museum
  • Bergen County Zoological Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Englewood is a city rich in history and culture, with several museums and historical places to explore. Some of the must-visit cultural attractions in Englewood include:

  • The Englewood Historical Society & Museum: This museum showcases Englewood’s history and culture through various exhibits and artifacts.

  • The Bergen Performing Arts Center: Known as the “Jewel of Bergen County,” this performing arts center hosts a variety of concerts, plays, and other cultural events.

  • The Englewood Public Library: This library not only offers an extensive collection of books, but it also hosts various cultural and educational events for all ages.

Natural Attractions of Englewood: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Englewood boasts of several natural attractions that offer a myriad of outdoor adventures and scenic views. Here are some of the most popular natural attractions in Englewood:

  • The Flat Rock Nature Center: This nature center offers several hiking trails, bird-watching opportunities, and educational exhibits on the area’s flora and fauna.

  • The Bergen County Zoological Park: This park is home to several species of animals, from tigers to monkeys, and offers a fun day out for families.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Englewood

Englewood has plenty of opportunities for outdoor enthusiasts to explore its natural beauty through hiking and biking. Some of the best hiking and biking trails in Englewood include:

  • The Palisades Interstate Park: This park boasts of several hiking trails that offer stunning views of the Hudson River and New York City skyline.

  • The Tenafly Nature Center: This nature center has several hiking trails that wind through a mixture of forests, meadows, and ponds, making it an ideal location for birdwatching.

Best Things to Do in Englewood: Activities and Tours

Englewood has several activities and tours that cater to all types of travelers. Here are some of our favorites:

  • The Food Tour: This tour lets you explore the city’s food scene by taking you to some of the best restaurants and cafes in Englewood.

  • The Wine Tasting Tour: This tour is a must for wine lovers, as it lets you sample some of the region’s best wines.

Week-Long Itinerary for Englewood

Day 1: Explore downtown Englewood and visit the Englewood Historical Society & Museum.

Day 2: Hike the trails at the Flat Rock Nature Center and enjoy a picnic in the park.

Day 3: Take a food tour and discover the city’s best bites.

Day 4: Visit the Bergen Performing Arts Center for a concert or play.

Day 5: Take a wine tasting tour and explore some of the region’s best wineries.

Day 6: Spend the day at the Bergen County Zoological Park.

Day 7: Enjoy a relaxing day exploring Tenafly Nature Center and its hiking trails.

Food and Drink Scene of Englewood

Englewood has a diverse and vibrant food and drink scene that offers something for every taste bud. Some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in Englewood include:

Must-Try Dishes

  • The Englewood Clam Broth: This dish is a favorite of locals, made with fresh clams, garlic, and parsley, it is perfect for a chilly day.

  • The Cheeseburger: Several restaurants in Englewood serve up juicy, mouth-watering cheeseburgers that are hard to resist.

Local Beverages

  • A&W Root Beer: This classic soda drink was first introduced in Englewood in 1919 and is still a beloved soft drink today.

  • Painted Turtle Pale Ale: A local craft beer that is brewed using only the freshest hops and barley.

What to Buy in Englewood: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Englewood has several unique shopping experiences that offer a range of souvenirs and local products to bring back home. Here are some of the best places to shop in Englewood:

Local Culture and Customs of Englewood

Englewood is a diverse city where various cultures coexist harmoniously. The locals are friendly and welcoming, with a strong sense of community. It is important to be respectful of the local customs and traditions, especially when visiting museums and historical sites.

Best Time to Visit Englewood

The best time to visit Englewood is during the spring or fall when the weather is mild, and the foliage is at its peak. Summers can get hot and humid, and winters can be cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Englewood

  • Take advantage of the city’s natural beauty by exploring its hiking trails and parks.

Don’t:

Safety and Health

Englewood is a safe city, with low crime rates. However, it is always essential to be aware of your surroundings and take necessary precautions when traveling, such as keeping your belongings safe and being cautious when walking alone at night.

Health in Englewood

Englewood has several medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics, that offer a wide range of healthcare services to visitors.

Englewood is easily accessible by several transportation options, including:

  • Airports Near Englewood: Newark Liberty International Airport and LaGuardia Airport are the nearest airports to Englewood.

  • Bus Stations Near Englewood: Englewood is well-connected with several bus stations that offer services to and from other cities in New Jersey and New York.

  • Other Options: Travelers can also take taxis or private cars to reach Englewood.

How to Get Around in Englewood

Englewood is a relatively small city, and most of the popular attractions are within walking or biking distance of each other. However, travelers can also use public transportation, including buses and taxis, to explore the city.

Where to Go Next from Englewood: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Englewood is surrounded by several fantastic destinations, making it an ideal base for day trips or weekend getaways. Some of the nearby destinations and excursions to explore include:

  • New York City: Just a short train or bus ride away, New York City offers endless entertainment, shopping, and dining options.

  • Jersey Shore: The Jersey Shore is a popular holiday destination and offers several beaches, boardwalks, and nightlife options.

Conclusion

Englewood is a charming city that offers a range of attractions for all types of travelers. From cultural attractions to outdoor adventures, from local cuisine to unique shopping experiences, Englewood has something for everyone. Plan your visit today and discover the unique charm of this city.
