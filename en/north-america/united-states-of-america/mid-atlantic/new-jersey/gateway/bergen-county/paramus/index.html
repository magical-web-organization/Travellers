Introduction to Paramus

Paramus is a borough located in Bergen County, New Jersey, just 15 miles northwest of New York City. Known for its upscale shopping centers, scenic parks, world-class dining, museums, and historical landmarks, Paramus offers visitors a range of things to see and do. Whether you’re coming to Paramus for business or pleasure, this comprehensive travel guide will help you plan an unforgettable trip to the Garden State.

List of Places to Visit in Paramus

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Bergen County Zoological Park and Gardens: Home to more than 100 species of exotic animals, a petting zoo, and various botanical gardens.
  • Van Saun County Park: Features a zoo with animals from all over the world, a train ride, and pony rides.
  • Westfield Garden State Plaza: One of the largest shopping malls in the country with over 300 stores, restaurants, and entertainment options.
  • The Hermitage: A historic house museum showcasing the art, architecture, and history of the 18th and 19th centuries.

Natural Attractions of Paramus: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Saddle River County Park: A great place to enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, biking, playing sports, picnicking, and fishing.
  • Overpeck County Park: A picturesque park with a lake for boating and fishing, picnic areas, and a playground.
  • Paramus Golf Course: A public golf course featuring scenic views and well-maintained playing grounds.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Paramus

  • Saddle River County Park Bike Path: A multi-use trail that runs through Saddle River County Park, connecting Paramus to neighboring towns.
  • Dorchester Road and Paramus Road: A four-mile cycling route that winds through quiet neighborhoods and picturesque countryside.

Best Things to Do in Paramus: Activities and Tours

  • Paramus Farmers Market: Held every Wednesday late spring through late fall, this farmers market features fresh produce, baked goods, and handmade crafts.
  • Garden State Plaza Shopping: Known for its high-end stores, luxury brands, and fine dining.
  • Paramus Local History Tours: Learn about the history, culture, and traditions of Paramus on a guided walking tour.

Food and Drink Scene of Paramus

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pizza: Try Jersey-style thin crust pizza, loaded with your favorite toppings, at local favorites like The Brick House or Gencarelli’s Pizzeria.
  • Tacos and Margaritas: Find fresh, authentic Mexican cuisine at La Fortaleza or Margaritas Mexican Restaurant.
  • Bagels: New Jersey is known for having some of the best bagels in the world, so make sure to stop by Bagel Nosh or Bagel Buffet for a delicious breakfast.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beer: Sample local brews at Alementary Brewing Co. or Hackensack Brewing Company for a taste of the Garden State.
  • Wine: Sip on award-winning wines at Demarest Hill Winery or Stag’s Leap Wine Cellars.

Where to Stay in Paramus: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hyatt Place Fair Lawn/Paramus: A spacious and comfortable hotel with modern amenities and easy access to popular attractions.
  • Holiday Inn Express Paramus: A great option for budget-conscious travelers, with comfortable rooms and complimentary breakfast.
  • Courtyard by Marriott Paramus: A stylish and modern hotel featuring luxurious amenities, complimentary breakfast, and on-site dining.

What to Buy in Paramus: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • T-Shirts: Look for T-shirts featuring the Paramus seal or other designs unique to the borough at local gift shops and boutiques.
  • Jersey Tomatoes: Jersey tomatoes are famous for their rich flavor and texture, so bring home a few from the Paramus Farmers Market or local supermarkets.
  • Craft Beer: Grab a few bottles of local craft beer from Alementary Brewing Co. or Hackensack Brewing Company to enjoy a taste of the Garden State back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Paramus

Paramus is home to a diverse community that is respectful of different cultures and beliefs. When visiting Paramus, be respectful of local customs and traditions, and try to learn as much as you can about the local culture from the people you meet.

Best Time to Visit Paramus

The best time to visit Paramus is from May to October when the weather is sunny, warm, and perfect for outdoor activities. The peak tourist season starts in June when schools go on summer break, but if you’re looking for lower rates and fewer crowds, consider visiting in May or September.

Do’s and Don’ts of Paramus

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine, including New Jersey-style pizza, bagels, and diner fare.
  • Do explore the scenic parks and outdoor trails for hiking, biking, and picnic activities.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or be disrespectful of the natural environment in Paramus.
  • Don’t forget to tip your server at restaurants and bars in Paramus, as it is customary in the United States.

Safety and Health

Safety in Paramus

Overall, Paramus is a safe place to visit, with low crime rates and a well-maintained town. However, as with any destination, it is important to take basic precautions such as keeping your valuables safe and being aware of your surroundings, particularly in crowded areas such as shopping malls or the farmers market.

Health in Paramus

Paramus has quality healthcare facilities and hospitals throughout the area, making it easy to seek medical attention if needed. However, it is always recommended to carry health insurance when traveling.

Getting to Paramus: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Paramus

  • Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR)
  • LaGuardia Airport (LGA)
  • John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK)

Bus Stations Near Paramus

  • New Jersey Transit Bus Terminal
  • George Washington Bridge Bus Station

Other Options

  • Car rental companies like Hertz, Avis, and Budget are available near Paramus for convenient transportation.
  • Taxis and ride-sharing options like Uber and Lyft are also available in Paramus.

How to Get Around in Paramus

The best way to get around Paramus is by car, as most of the attractions are spread out throughout the borough. Taxis and ride-sharing services are also available.

Where to Go Next from Paramus: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • New York City: Just a 30-minute drive from Paramus, visitors can explore the world-renowned sights and sounds of New York City.
  • Atlantic City: A popular getaway for gambling, beach activities, and boardwalk attractions, Atlantic City is about a two-hour drive from Paramus.

Conclusion

With a variety of options for outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, shopping centers, and world-class dining, Paramus is the perfect destination for a memorable getaway. Whether you’re traveling alone, with family, or friends, make sure to add Paramus to your list of must-visit destinations in the United States.
