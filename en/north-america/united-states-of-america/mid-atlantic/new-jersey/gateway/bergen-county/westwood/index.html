Introduction to Westwood

Westwood is a small and charming borough located in Bergen County, New Jersey. It is a part of the Gateway region and Mid-Atlantic. Westwood is known for its rich cultural heritage, scenic beauty, and warm hospitality. The borough is located just 20 miles northwest of New York City, making it an ideal location for a quick getaway or a weekend trip.

Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor enthusiast, or a foodie, Westwood has something to offer to everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take a closer look at Westwood and its various attractions, activities, and experiences.
