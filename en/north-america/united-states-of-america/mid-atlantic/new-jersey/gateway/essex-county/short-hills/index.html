Introduction to Short Hills

Located in Essex County, New Jersey, Short Hills is a quaint, upscale suburb situated just 30 minutes from New York City. Known for its beautiful residential properties, high-end shopping centers, and lush greenery, Short Hills is a popular getaway destination for those seeking a peaceful retreat in the countryside’s heart.

List of Places to Visit in Short Hills

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Short Hills is home to several culturally significant attractions that provide visitors with a glimpse of the area’s rich history and heritage. Some of the must-visit museums and historical places in Short Hills include:

  • The Paper Mill Playhouse: A renowned regional theater that has been showcasing musicals, plays, and concerts for over 80 years. The theater is famous for producing many Tony Award-winning shows like “Newsies” and “Hairspray.”

  • The Greenwood Gardens: An enchanting 28-acre garden estate that showcases over 7,000 plant species, a labyrinth garden, and a tea house. The gardens are open year-round and offer visitors a chance to learn about the area’s history and outdoor beauty.

  • The Cora Hartshorn Arboretum and Bird Sanctuary: A stunning 16-acre preserve that houses exotic trees, gardens, and bird observatories. Visitors can take guided tours or embark on self-guided hikes to explore the breathtaking beauty of the gardens.

Natural Attractions of Short Hills: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Short Hills is a nature lover’s paradise with its vast network of parks, reserves, and trekking trails. Here are some of the best natural attractions in Short Hills:

  • The South Mountain Reservation: A vast wildlife preserve spread over 2,112 acres of land that offers visitors a chance to hike, bike, and fish in natural surroundings. The reservation is home to brooks, waterfalls, and several trails with varying degrees of difficulty.

  • The Turtle Back Zoo: A popular wildlife sanctuary that houses over 1000 animals from all over the world. Visitors can enjoy live animal shows, feeding events, and even take part in animal encounters.

  • The Reeves-Reed Arboretum: Spanning over 13.5 acres, the arboretum boasts a diverse collection of plants and trees. It is home to several gardens, including a herb garden, cutting flower garden, and a woodland bloom garden.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Short Hills

Short Hills has numerous trails and scenic routes that offer unique views of the area’s natural beauty. Some of the best hiking and biking trails in Short Hills include:

  • The Summit Loop Trail: A scenic 2.2-mile trail that takes visitors through the South Mountain Reservation and offers stunning views of the New York skyline.

  • The Millburn Rail-Trail: A 3.5-mile trail that was once a rail line running from Milburn through the Hoboken Terminal. It’s an easy trail and perfect for a scenic walk or bike ride.

  • The Rahway River Park Trail: A 4.2-mile trail that runs through the Rahway River Park. The trail is popular for its scenic views of the river and is ideal for hiking, biking, and nature walks.

Best Things to Do in Short Hills: Activities and Tours

Short Hills has plenty of activities and tours that visitors can enjoy to make their stay enjoyable. Here are some of the best things to do in Short Hills:

  • Take a Hot Air Balloon Ride: Experience the beauty of Short Hills from the sky by taking a hot air balloon ride. Several companies offer balloon rides, and visitors can enjoy stunning views of the area’s natural beauty and the New York skyline.

  • Shop at the Mall at Short Hills: With over 150 stores, restaurants, and high-end boutiques, the Mall at Short Hills is a shopper’s paradise. The mall features luxury brands like Chanel, Gucci, Tiffany & Co., and Prada.

  • Kayak and Canoe Tours: Explore the area’s waterways by taking a guided kayak or canoe tour. Visitors can enjoy the scenic views of the Delaware River and the Corbin City Wildlife Management Area while gliding on the water.

Week-Long Itinerary for Short Hills

For visitors looking to explore Short Hills thoroughly, a week-long itinerary should include the following activities:

Food and Drink Scene of Short Hills

Must-Try Dishes

Short Hills has a diverse food scene with several award-winning restaurants and eateries. Here are some of the must-try dishes in Short Hills:

  • The Original Pancake House: Famous for their colossal-sized pancakes, freshly-squeezed orange juice, and homemade whipped cream.

  • Piattino: A popular trattoria that serves delicious handmade pasta, seafood, and wood-fired pizzas.

  • Boxcar Bar & Grill: Renowned for their mouthwatering burgers, chicken wings, and an extensive selection of beer and cocktails.

Local Beverages

Short Hills has several bars and breweries that offer local beers and cocktails. Here are some of the local beverages to try in Short Hills:

  • Turtle Back Brewery: Their locally brewed beers, such as the Monkey Flight IPA and the Paddle Wheeler Pale Ale, are popular amongst locals and visitors.

  • Twisted Tree: A cozy wine bar that serves an extensive selection of wines, beers, and charcuterie boards.

Where to Stay in Short Hills: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Short Hills has several accommodation options for visitors. Here are some of the best places to stay in Short Hills:

  • Hilton Short Hills: A luxurious hotel situated in the heart of Short Hills with top-notch amenities and excellent service.

  • The Grand Summit Hotel: A historic hotel built-in 1868 with a beautiful Georgian-style architecture and and top-notch amenities.

  • Millburn Hotel: An affordable hotel located near the Rahway River Park Trail and the Short Hills Train Station.

What to Buy in Short Hills: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Short Hills has several local shops and boutiques that offer unique souvenirs and locally-made products. Here are some of the best places to shop in Short Hills:

  • The Short Hills Mall: The mall features luxury brands and high-end boutiques that offer unique clothing, accessories, and gift items.

  • The Bee Shop: A local shop that offers honey, beeswax candles, honeycomb, and other bee-related products.

Local Culture and Customs of Short Hills

Short Hills is an upscale neighborhood, and visitors are expected to dress appropriately when visiting restaurants, bars, and other public places. It’s essential to be respectful of the local culture and customs and follow any rules and regulations while visiting.

Best Time to Visit Short Hills

The best time to visit Short Hills is during the fall season (September-November) when the weather is pleasant, and the foliage is beautiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of Short Hills

Safety and Health

Safety in Short Hills

Short Hills is a safe neighborhood, but visitors should still take the necessary precautions to ensure their safety. It’s recommended to keep valuables in a secure location and be mindful of your surroundings while walking late at night.

Health in Short Hills

Short Hills has several hospitals and clinics that offer top-notch medical care to visitors. However, it’s still essential to carry any necessary medications and have health insurance coverage.

Getting to Short Hills: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Short Hills

The nearest airport to Short Hills is the Newark Liberty International Airport, which is approximately 15 miles from Short Hills.

Bus Stations Near Short Hills

The closest bus station to Short Hills is the Millburn Train Station, which offers access to NJ TRANSIT rail and bus service.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel to Short Hills by car or taxi, or via ride-hailing services like Uber or Lyft.

How to Get Around in Short Hills

Short Hills is a walkable neighborhood, and visitors can easily explore the downtown area on foot. Alternatively, visitors can rent a bike or use ride-hailing services to reach further destinations.

Where to Go Next from Short Hills: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Short Hills is within close proximity to several popular destinations, making it an ideal base for day trips. Some of the nearby destinations and excursions include:

  • New York City: Located just 30 miles from Short Hills, New York City is one of the world’s top tourist destinations, offering visitors access to iconic landmarks, museums, and sights.

  • Jersey Shore: The Jersey Shore is located approximately 45 miles from Short Hills and features stunning beaches, charming boardwalks, and seafood restaurants.

  • Six Flags Great Adventure: Located approximately 60 miles from Short Hills, Six Flags Great Adventure is a popular amusement park that features thrilling roller coasters and water rides.

Conclusion

Short Hills is a charming, upscale suburb that offers visitors a chance to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and indulge in natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and luxury shopping. With its diverse attractions, activities, and tours, Short Hills has something for every type of traveler, making it a must-visit destination in New Jersey.
