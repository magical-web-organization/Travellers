Introduction to Union County

Union County is a picturesque county located in the Gateway region of New Jersey, situated between New York City and Philadelphia. With a population of over 550,000 residents, it is a bustling county filled with history, culture, and natural beauty.

Travelers to Union County will be captivated by its diversity, both in terms of natural landscape and cultural attractions. This guide aims to highlight some of the best experiences the county has to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Union County

Union County is home to several awe-inspiring attractions, whether you are looking to learn about its rich history or explore its outdoor recreation opportunities. Here are some of the places you should not miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Liberty Hall Museum: This historic estate once belonged to New Jersey’s first elected governor. Today it’s a museum where visitors can explore exhibits featuring over 240 years of history.

  • The Cranford Historical Society: A must-visit for history buffs, the Cranford Historical Society offers a glimpse into the past with its museum that houses over 20,000 artifacts.

  • The Union County Performing Arts Center: The restored 1928 theater regularly hosts concerts, plays, and famous performers.

Natural Attractions of Union County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Echo Lake Park: With more than 100 acres of land, Echo Lake Park is filled with natural beauty.

  • Watchung Reservation: This 1,945-acre park offers plenty of outdoor activities, such as fishing, hiking, and biking.

  • Warinanco Park: The greenery and beauty of Warinanco Park should definitely be on your itinerary! It’s the perfect spot to relax, have fun, and enjoy a picnic with family and friends.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Union County

Union County boasts several trails that offer a unique way to explore the county’s outdoors. They vary in difficulty, so every hiker or biker can find what suits them. Here are some of the most popular:

  • Lenape Trail: This 34-mile path winds through Union and several other nearby counties, featuring both a paved and natural surface.

  • Watchung Reservation: The park features several trails of varying lengths and difficulty levels.

  • Rahway River Parkway: This 9-mile trail runs through Union and is perfect for those looking for a leisurely stroll or bike ride through nature.

Best Things to Do in Union County: Activities and Tours

Union County offers several activities that cater to all kinds of travelers. From water sports to food tours, here are our top picks:

  • Kayaking and Canoeing: Head to the Rahway River for a leisurely ride on the water.
  • Food tours: Sample delicious local cuisine through Taste of Elizabeth, which offers food tours of the city.

Week-Long Itinerary for Union County

With so much to see and do, it can be hard to plan your itinerary for visiting Union County. Here’s a sample itinerary to make the most of your visit:

Day 1: Visit the Cranford Historical Society and Liberty Hall Museum.

Day 2: Explore Echo Lake Park in the morning before heading for a food tour of Elizabeth in the afternoon.

Day 3: Spend the day hiking or biking one of the many trails in Watchung Reservation.

Day 4: Discover Warinanco Park and learn about the region’s history at the Union County Performing Arts Center.

Day 5: Visit the Reeves Reed Arboretum in the morning before heading to the Miller-Cory House Museum in the afternoon.

Day 6: Embark on a kayaking adventure along the Rahway River

Day 7: Spend the day relaxing at one of Union County’s scenic spots, like Warinanco Park or Echo Lake Park.

Food and Drink Scene of Union County

No trip to Union County would be complete without trying its many delicious local dishes and drinks. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Taylor Ham: Also called pork roll, it’s a popular breakfast item throughout New Jersey and is frequently paired with cheese on a hard roll.

  • Tomato pie: A type of pizza unique to New Jersey featuring the tomato sauce on top of the cheese.

Local Beverages

  • Cape May Brewing Company: As the state’s largest microbrewery, Cape May Brewing Co. offers locals and visitors dozens of beers on tap.

  • Just Jersey Food Tours: Take a guided tour of craft breweries, distilleries, and wineries in Union County, sampling only the best in local beverages.

Where to Stay in Union County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Union County has no shortage of accommodation options, whether you’re looking for luxury or budget-friendly lodgings. Some options include:

  • The Kenilworth: Located in Kenilworth, this boutique hotel offers high-end amenities, stylish decor, and a central location.

  • The Summit DeBary Inn: Tucked away in the historic town of Summit, this 19th-century mansion turned bed and breakfast offers guests a unique experience.

What to Buy in Union County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Union County is home to several small businesses that offer unique locally made products. Here are some of the best souvenirs to take home:

  • Honey from Hilltop Honey in Westfield: This local business is known for its high-quality honey varieties.

  • Handcrafted pottery from The Kitao Gallery in Westfield: Their beautiful selection of pottery pieces is made by local artists.

Local Culture and Customs of Union County

As a diverse region, Union County’s culture and customs are shaped by many influences. It is home to a mix of ethnicities and is committed to promoting inclusivity and diversity.

Best Time to Visit Union County

The best time to visit Union County is during the spring (April to June) or fall (September to November) seasons when the weather is pleasant, and the outdoor recreation opportunities are plentiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of Union County

Do:

  • Dress appropriately for the weather and any outdoor activities you will be engaging in.

  • Be respectful of the region’s diverse population.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage any historical or natural landmarks.

  • Trespass on private property.

Safety and Health

Union County is a generally safe and healthy area. Visitors should take appropriate precautions when visiting natural areas, such as hiking or biking to prevent injuries. Sunscreen and insect repellent are recommended when spending time outdoors.

Getting to Union County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Union County is easily accessible by car, bus, or train. Some popular options include:

Airports Near Union County

  • Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR)

Bus Stations Near Union County

  • Elizabeth Bus Station

Other Options

  • NJ Transit train: Union County has several train stations, each of which offers access to major cities like Newark and New York City.

How to Get Around in Union County

Union County is easily navigable by car. Rental services and ride-sharing options like Uber and Lyft are also available. Many of the outdoor recreation areas can be reached via public transportation.

Where to Go Next from Union County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Union County’s location offers easy access to several other exciting destinations, like New York City and Philadelphia. Should you choose to venture further, consider these popular destinations:

  • Cape May: This historic resort town is known for its Victorian architecture, beaches, and distinctive lighthouse.

  • Princeton: Home to Princeton University, this town is perfect for those looking to discover local art and culture.

Conclusion

Union County’s unique mix of history, culture, and natural beauty makes it the perfect destination for travelers. With outdoor recreation opportunities, history lessons, and delicious local dishes, there is something for everyone in this charming county.
