Introduction to Union (Union County)

Union County is situated in the heart of Gateway, New Jersey, in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States of America. This charming county offers an incredible mix of urban, suburban, and rural attractions, making it a perfect destination for every type of traveler. Whether you’re interested in exploring museums, hiking trails, or local restaurants, Union County has something to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Union (Union County)

Union County is home to a wide range of cultural and natural attractions. Here are some of the must-visit places in Union County:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Liberty Hall Museum: This impressive museum is located on the Kean University campus and is known for its extensive collection of artifacts from the American Revolution and beyond.
  • The Cranford Historical Society: This museum offers an overview of the history of Cranford through exhibits and educational programs.
  • Boxwood Hall: This historic house was once the home of Elias Boudinot, a prominent Patriot during the Revolutionary War. Today, it is open to the public for guided tours.

Natural Attractions of Union (Union County): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Watchung Reservation: This sprawling park is home to over 13 miles of hiking trails, picnic areas, and a beautiful lake.
  • Echo Lake Park: This park boasts picturesque walking paths, fishing spots, and a boathouse.
  • Lenape Park: This beautiful green space is located along the Rahway River and is ideal for hiking, biking, and picnicking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Union (Union County)

Union County has some of the best hiking and biking trails in the region. Here are some of the top trails to check out:

  • Watchung Reservation Trail: This 13.5-mile trail offers stunning views of the surrounding forests and is perfect for hiking, biking, and horseback riding.
  • Lenape Trail: This 34-mile trek is ideal for long-distance hiking and offers a unique perspective on the diverse ecosystems of Union County.
  • Rahway River Parkway: This 9.2-mile path offers plenty of picturesque scenery, and is perfect for both hiking and biking.

Best Things to Do in Union (Union County): Activities and Tours

Union County offers a wide range of exciting activities and tours for visitors. Here are some must-do experiences in the area:

  • Ghost Tour of Union: This spooky walking tour takes visitors through some of the most haunted spots in Union County.
  • Elizabeth Food Tour: This culinary tour of Elizabeth offers a chance to taste some of the city’s best food.
  • Wine Tasting at Tomasello Winery: Visitors can relax and sip on delicious wines while enjoying picturesque views of the vineyards.

Week-Long Itinerary for Union (Union County)

If you have a week to spend in Union County, you can cover all the cultural, natural, and adventure attractions the county has to offer. Here’s a sample itinerary:

  • Day 1: Visit Liberty Hall Museum and explore Kean University’s campus.
  • Day 2: Hike the Watchung Reservation Trail.
  • Day 3: Visit Echo Lake Park and enjoy the boathouse.
  • Day 4: Take a food tour of Elizabeth and sample the city’s best dishes.
  • Day 5: Take a ghost tour of Union and visit the most haunted spots in the county.
  • Day 6: Visit the Cranford Historical Society and Boxwood Hall.
  • Day 7: Visit Lenape Park and explore the Rahway River Parkway.

Food and Drink Scene of Union (Union County)

Union County’s food and drink scene is thriving, with plenty of options for both foodies and casual diners. Here are some must-try foods and drinks in the area:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Taylor Ham and Cheese: This iconic snack is a staple of New Jersey cuisine. It’s a pork roll served with cheese on a roll or bagel.
  • Tomato Pie: A tomato pie is similar to a pizza, but with a thin, crispy crust and a thick layer of tomato sauce on top.
  • Italian Hot Dogs: An Italian hot dog is a deep-fried hot dog topped with peppers, onions, potatoes, and mustard.

Local Beverages

  • Tomasello Winery: This family-owned winery offers a wide range of wines, including red, white, and dessert wines.
  • Climax Brewing Company: This award-winning brewery boasts a wide range of craft beers, including IPAs, stouts, and lagers.
  • Carton Brewing Company: This small brewery is known for its unique flavors, including a tart fruit beer called “Monkey Chased the Weasel.”

Where to Stay in Union (Union County): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Union County has a wide range of accommodation options, ranging from upscale hotels to cozy guesthouses. Here are some top picks:

  • Hilton Garden Inn Springfield: This comfortable hotel is located in downtown Springfield and offers an indoor pool, fitness center, and on-site restaurant.
  • The Kenilworth: This boutique hotel offers world-class amenities, including an on-site spa, fitness center, and upscale dining.
  • The Summit DeBary Inn: This cozy bed and breakfast is located in Summit and features a charming mix of modern and historic features.

What to Buy in Union (Union County): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Local Culture and Customs of Union (Union County)

Union County is known for its diverse and friendly population. Visitors can expect a warm welcome and a laid-back atmosphere throughout the county.

Best Time to Visit Union (Union County)

The best time to visit Union County is during the fall or spring, when the weather is temperate and the foliage is at its peak. In the fall, visitors can enjoy colorful leaves and seasonal festivals, while spring offers the chance to enjoy blooming flowers and outdoor activities.

Getting to Union County is easy, with several transportation options available:

  • Airports Near Union (Union County): Newark Liberty International Airport is the closest major airport, located approximately 10 miles from Union County.
  • Bus Stations Near Union (Union County): NJ Transit offers several bus routes to and from Union County, making it easy to travel around the county and beyond.
  • Other Options: Visitors can also take the train to Union County, as NJ Transit offers several train stops in the area.

How to Get Around in Union (Union County)

Getting around Union County is primarily done by car, but visitors can also take advantage of bike-friendly roads and trails. There are several rental car options available, as well as ride-sharing and taxi services.

Where to Go Next from Union (Union County): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Conclusion

Union County is a hidden gem in New Jersey, offering a unique mix of natural beauty, cultural attractions, and adventure opportunities. With so many things to see and do, visitors are sure to fall in love with this charming county in the heart of Gateway.
