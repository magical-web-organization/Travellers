A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Long Hill, New Jersey

Located in Morris County, in the scenic Skylands region of New Jersey, Long Hill is a wonderful destination for those seeking a peaceful and natural escape from the city. From hiking and biking trails to historic sites and local eateries, there’s something for every type of traveler in Long Hill. In this comprehensive travel guide to Long Hill, we’ll provide everything you need to know to plan your trip.

List of Places to Visit in Long Hill

Long Hill offers a mix of cultural and natural attractions that are sure to appeal to every visitor. Here are some of the must-visit places in Long Hill:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Ralston Cider Mill: A historic site that served as the hub of cider-making in the area in the 19th century.
  • The Jacobus Vanderveer House: A beautiful colonial-era home that now serves as a museum of local history.
  • The Miller-Cory House Museum: An 18th-century living history museum that provides insight into colonial-era life in New Jersey.

Natural Attractions of Long Hill: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Central Park of Morris County: A sprawling park with hiking trails, a lake, and an abundance of wildlife.
  • The Passaic River, which runs through Long Hill, offers opportunities for kayaking, fishing, and picnicking.
  • Great Swamp National Wildlife Refuge: A protected area of wetlands that is home to hundreds of bird species and other wildlife.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Long Hill

Long Hill has a range of trails for hikers and bikers, from easy walks in the park to challenging mountain hikes. Some popular routes include:

  • The Passaic River Trail follows the river through Long Hill and is ideal for a leisurely walk or bike ride.
  • Patriots’ Path is a 35-mile trail that passes through Long Hill and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
  • The Great Swamp National Wildlife Refuge Trail is a relatively easy hike that showcases the area’s natural beauty.

Best Things to Do in Long Hill: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, Long Hill has a range of activities and tour options for visitors. Some top picks include:

  • Visit local farm markets like Stony Hill Farms for fresh produce and baked goods.
  • Take a tour of The Ralston Cider Mill to learn about the history of cider-making in the area.
  • Go on a hot air balloon ride with Balloons Aloft.
  • Enjoy a wine tasting at Alba Vineyard, a picturesque winery located in nearby Milford, New Jersey.

Week-Long Itinerary for Long Hill

Here’s a suggested itinerary for a week-long stay in Long Hill:

Day Activity
1 Hike the Passaic River Trail
2 Visit The Jacobus Vanderveer House and the Miller-Cory House Museum
3 Take a hot air balloon ride with Balloons Aloft
4 Spend a day at Central Park of Morris County
5 Visit Great Swamp National Wildlife Refuge
6 Enjoy a wine tasting at Alba Vineyard
7 Spend a day on the Passaic River, kayaking or fishing

Food and Drink Scene of Long Hill

Long Hill has a growing food and drink scene, with plenty of options for local and international cuisine. Some must-try dishes and local beverages include:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Taylor ham sandwiches: a New Jersey classic made with sliced and grilled pork roll, served on a bagel or roll.
  • Tomato pies: a thin-crust pizza topped with tomato sauce and grated cheese that originated in Trenton, New Jersey.
  • Lobster rolls: a seafood favorite made with chunks of fresh lobster meat, served in a buttered and toasted roll.

Local Beverages

  • Apple cider: a must-try drink at The Ralston Cider Mill.
  • Craft beer from Flounder Brewing Co, one of the few breweries in Long Hill.
  • Wines from Alba Vineyard, one of the region’s premier wineries.

Where to Stay in Long Hill: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

While Long Hill doesn’t have a wide range of accommodation options, there are some great choices for those looking to stay in the area:

  • The Bernards Inn: A luxury hotel located in nearby Bernardsville, known for its elegant and cozy ambiance.
  • Whistling Swan Inn: A charming bed and breakfast located in nearby Stanhope.
  • Holiday Inn Express & Suites: A modern and affordable option located in Stirling, just a short drive from Long Hill.

What to Buy in Long Hill: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Some popular souvenirs and products to bring home from Long Hill include:

  • Fresh produce from local farm markets, like apples and pumpkins from Donaldson Farm.
  • Apple cider and honey from The Ralston Cider Mill.
  • Crafts and gifts from local stores like Atlantis and The Basket Case.

Local Culture and Customs of Long Hill

Similar to the rest of New Jersey, Long Hill has a diverse population and culture. Visitors should respect local customs and traditions, and be aware of the region’s history and cultural significance.

Best Time to Visit Long Hill

The best time to visit Long Hill is during the fall season, which runs from September to November, when the air is cooler and the foliage is at its peak. Spring and summer are also great times to visit, although the summer months can be crowded.

Do’s and Don’ts of Long Hill

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Sample the local cuisine.
  • Enjoy scenic outdoor activities like hiking and biking.
  • Take a hot air balloon ride to view the area from above.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural areas.
  • Be disrespectful to the local community and culture.
  • Ignore safety guidelines and warnings.

Safety and Health

Safety in Long Hill

Long Hill is a relatively safe area, but visitors should exercise caution and common sense, especially when participating in outdoor activities like hiking and biking. Always follow safety guidelines and be prepared for unexpected emergencies.

Health in Long Hill

Long Hill is a generally healthy area, but visitors should be aware of tick-borne diseases like Lyme disease, which are prevalent in wooded areas. Always wear long sleeves and pants, and use insect repellent when hiking or spending time outdoors.

Getting to Long Hill: Transportation Options and Logistics

Long Hill is located in north-central New Jersey, and is easily accessible by car or public transportation.

Airports Near Long Hill

  • Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR): The closest major airport, located approximately 28 miles from Long Hill.
  • LaGuardia Airport (LGA): Located approximately 50 miles from Long Hill.

Bus Stations Near Long Hill

  • New Jersey Transit: The closest station is in nearby Basking Ridge, which is approximately 3 miles from Long Hill.

Other Options

  • Car rental: A great option for those who want to explore the region at their own pace.
  • Taxis and ride-sharing services: Available in most areas of the region.

How to Get Around in Long Hill

While Long Hill is a small community, a car is recommended for getting around and exploring the surrounding areas. Bike rentals are also available in some areas, and there are some public transportation options for those who prefer not to drive.

Where to Go Next from Long Hill: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

From Long Hill, there are plenty of nearby destinations and excursions to explore, such as:

  • The historic city of Morristown.
  • The charming town of Chester, with its quaint main street filled with boutiques and restaurants.
  • The Delaware Water Gap National Recreation Area, located on the border of New Jersey and Pennsylvania.

Conclusion

Long Hill is a wonderful destination that offers both cultural and natural attractions. Whether you’re interested in hiking and biking, exploring historic sites, or enjoying local cuisine and wine, you’ll find something to love in Long Hill, New Jersey. Plan your trip today and experience the best that this scenic region has to offer.
