Introduction to Succasunna

Succasunna is a charming town located in Morris County, New Jersey. It is part of the Skylands region in the Mid-Atlantic state of the United States of America. The town offers a perfect blend of natural and cultural attractions, making it an ideal destination for travelers. Whether you are looking to relax in nature or explore history and art, Succasunna has something for everyone. In this guide, we will take a deep dive into the town’s best attractions, food and drink scene, accommodations, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Succasunna

Succasunna boasts a plethora of attractions and experiences for visitors. Here are some of the top places to visit in Succasunna:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Roxbury Township Historical Society Museum: This museum showcases the town’s rich history and culture through various exhibits and artifacts.

  • The Barn Theatre: The Barn Theatre is a community theater that hosts a diverse range of performances, from plays to musicals and everything in between.

Natural Attractions of Succasunna: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Horseshoe Lake: Horseshoe Lake is a picturesque lake situated in the heart of the town. Visitors can enjoy swimming, boating, and hiking around the lake.

  • Morris Canal Park: Morris Canal Park is a serene park that offers stunning views of the Morris Canal and the surrounding countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Succasunna

Succasunna is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts with numerous hiking and biking trails. Some of the most popular hiking and biking routes are:

  • Clyde Potts Reservoir Trail: The Clyde Potts Reservoir Trail is a scenic hiking trail that offers stunning views of the reservoir.

  • The Patriots’ Path: The Patriots’ Path is a 35-mile long trail that runs through Morris County. It offers beautiful views of nature and historic landmarks.

Best Things to Do in Succasunna: Activities and Tours

Succasunna provides a plethora of activities for travelers of all ages. Here are some must-try things to do:

  • Wharton State Forest Adventure Tour: This guided tour takes visitors to Wharton State Forest, one of the largest protected wilderness areas in New Jersey.

  • Wine Tasting Tour: Succasunna is home to numerous wineries that offer visitors the chance to sample some of the region’s best wines.

Week-Long Itinerary for Succasunna

Here’s a sample itinerary for a week-long visit to Succasunna:

Day 1: Arrive in Succasunna and explore the town’s historic district.

Day 2: Visit the Roxbury Township Historical Society Museum and The Barn Theatre.

Day 3: Hike the Clyde Potts Reservoir Trail and explore Horseshoe Lake.

Day 6: Take a scenic drive through Morris Canal Park and enjoy the breathtaking views of the countryside.

Day 7: Depart Succasunna.

Food and Drink Scene of Succasunna

Succasunna offers a diverse range of cuisines to satisfy all kinds of cravings. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

  • Taylor Ham Sandwich: No trip to New Jersey is complete without trying this local delicacy, which consists of pork roll, egg, and cheese served on a bagel or English muffin.
