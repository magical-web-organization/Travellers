Introduction to East Hanover

East Hanover is a small town located in Morris County, in Skylands, New Jersey. It is situated in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States and offers a wide range of activities for travelers. East Hanover is a great destination for those who love the outdoors, as it offers beautiful natural scenery and a variety of outdoor adventures. The town also boasts a rich history and cultural heritage, with several museums and historical sites worth visiting.

List of Places to Visit in East Hanover

Whether you’re interested in history, nature, or simply relaxing, East Hanover has plenty of options for you to explore. Here is a list of the top places to visit in the town:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Whippany Railway Museum: This museum is dedicated to preserving the history of the Whippany Railway and features various exhibits, including vintage train cars, engines, and other railway artifacts.
  • The Stickley Museum: The museum is home to the Stickley family’s original workshop, which is known for its fine furniture made in the Arts and Crafts style. The museum also offers guided tours and educational programs.
  • Morris Museum: This museum showcases a wide range of art, music, science, and cultural exhibits. The museum’s permanent collection includes over 40,000 artifacts, including American Indian artifacts, fine art, and natural history specimens.

Natural Attractions of East Hanover: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Eagle Rock Reservation: This park offers breathtaking views of Manhattan and the surrounding areas. It is a perfect spot for hiking and biking, and the park also has picnic areas and playgrounds.
  • Central Park of Morris County (Greystone Park): This park stretches over 127 acres and is home to a variety of species of plants and animals. The park offers hiking trails, a pond, and a wildlife observation area.
  • Pyramid Mountain Natural Historic Area: This park is a great destination for those who enjoy hiking and exploring the outdoors. The park features scenic views, ancient rock formations, and a variety of trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of East Hanover

For those interested in hiking and biking, East Hanover has a variety of trails to choose from. Here are a few popular options:

  • Patriots’ Path: This multi-use trail is over 35 miles long and travels through several towns in Morris County, including East Hanover. The trail offers scenic views and is great for hiking, biking, and horseback riding.
  • South Mountain Reservation: This park offers several hiking trails with varying levels of difficulty. The trails are open year-round and offer beautiful views of the surrounding area.
  • Lewis Morris Park: This park offers over 12 miles of hiking trails that wind through forests, fields, and wetlands. The trails are well-marked and offer great views of the surrounding area.

Best Things to Do in East Hanover: Activities and Tours

East Hanover offers a wide range of activities and tours to make your visit a memorable one. Here are some top things to do:

  • Go shopping at the East Hanover Mall: Located in the heart of the town, the mall has a wide range of stores and restaurants to explore.
  • Visit Hanover Lanes: This bowling alley offers fun for the whole family. The alley features 48 lanes, an arcade, and a snack bar.
  • Take a tour of the Red Mill Museum Village: This museum offers a glimpse into the history of the town and the surrounding area.

Week-Long Itinerary for East Hanover

If you’re planning a longer stay in East Hanover, here’s a sample itinerary to help you make the most of your time:

Day 1: Visit the Whippany Railway Museum and take a walk on the Patriots’ Path trail. Day 2: Explore the Stickley Museum and the Morris Museum. Day 3: Go hiking at Pyramid Mountain Natural Historic Area. Day 4: Visit the Central Park of Morris County and the Lewis Morris Park. Day 5: Take a trip to the Eagle Rock Reservation and enjoy the scenic views. Day 6: Go bowling at Hanover Lanes and spend the afternoon shopping at the East Hanover Mall. Day 7: Take a tour of the Red Mill Museum Village and enjoy a relaxing afternoon at a local coffee shop.

Food and Drink Scene of East Hanover

East Hanover has a vibrant food and drink scene that offers a taste of local flavors. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pizza: East Hanover is home to several great pizza places, including Casa Di Pizza and Bella Gente.
  • Italian food: The town has a variety of Italian restaurants, including Marino’s, which is known for its homemade pasta dishes.
  • Bagels: East Hanover Bagel & Deli is a popular spot for locals and visitors alike.

Local Beverages

  • Craft beer: The Gaslight Brewery & Restaurant is a must-visit for beer lovers. The brewery offers a variety of craft beers and a full menu of food.
  • Wine: There are several wineries located in Morris County, including the Old York Cellars Vineyard and Winery.

Getting to East Hanover: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bus Stations Near East Hanover

Other Options

Where to Go Next from East Hanover: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
