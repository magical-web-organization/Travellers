Introduction to Sussex County

Located in the Skylands region of New Jersey, Sussex County is a hidden gem known for its natural beauty, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures. With lush forests, sparkling lakes, and stunning mountain views, Sussex County is the perfect destination for anyone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

List of Places to Visit in Sussex County

Whether you’re an art lover, history buff, nature enthusiast, or adventure seeker, there’s something for everyone in Sussex County. Here are just a few of the must-see attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Franklin Mineral Museum
  • The Sterling Hill Mining Museum
  • The Space Farms Zoo and Museum

Natural Attractions of Sussex County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • High Point State Park
  • Stokes State Forest
  • Delaware Water Gap National Recreation Area

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sussex County

Sussex County is home to some of the best hiking and biking trails in the state. Here are a few of the most popular routes:

  • Sussex Branch Trail
  • Paulinskill Valley Trail
  • Appalachian Trail

Best Things to Do in Sussex County: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are plenty of other things to do in Sussex County. Here are a few ideas:

  • Visit a local winery or brewery
  • Go apple picking at a local orchard
  • Take a hot air balloon ride over the county

Week-Long Itinerary for Sussex County

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Sussex County, here’s a sample itinerary to help you make the most of your time:

Day Activity
1 Visit High Point State Park and hike to the summit

Local Culture and Customs of Sussex County

Sussex County is known for its rich history and strong sense of community. Visitors are often struck by the kindness and hospitality of the locals, as well as the area’s commitment to preserving its natural beauty and heritage.

Best Time to Visit Sussex County

While Sussex County is beautiful year-round, the best time to visit depends on your interests and preferred activities. For hiking and outdoor adventures, spring and fall are ideal. For apple picking and enjoying the fall foliage, plan a trip in September or October. Winter is great for skiing and other winter sports, while summer offers warm temperatures and plenty of opportunities for swimming and water activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sussex County

Do:

  • Respect the natural surroundings and wildlife
  • Take advantage of the area’s outdoor activities and scenic views
  • Engage with the local community and learn about the area’s history and culture

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural environment
  • Ignore local customs and traditions
  • Be disrespectful to others

Safety and Health

Safety in Sussex County

Overall, Sussex County is a safe and welcoming destination for travelers. However, it’s always important to take basic safety precautions, such as locking your car and being aware of your surroundings when hiking or exploring remote areas.

Health in Sussex County

Travelers to Sussex County should be aware of tick-borne illnesses that are common in the area, such as Lyme disease. Be sure to take appropriate precautions, such as wearing long sleeves and pants, using insect repellent, and checking for ticks regularly.

Getting to Sussex County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Getting to Sussex County is easy, whether you’re flying in or driving from a nearby city. Here are a few transportation options to consider:

Airports Near Sussex County

  • Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR)
  • Lehigh Valley International Airport (ABE)
  • John F. Kennedy International Airport (JFK)

Bus Stations Near Sussex County

  • Greyhound Bus Station in Newton, NJ
  • Lakeland Bus Lines in Sparta, NJ

Other Options

  • Rental cars (available at most airports and in nearby cities)
  • Amtrak (nearest station is in Newark, NJ)

How to Get Around in Sussex County

While having a car is helpful for getting around Sussex County, there are other transportation options available as well. These include:

  • Local bus services (such as Sussex County Skylands Ride)
  • Taxi services (such as TranState Taxi)
  • Biking or hiking (when exploring local trails or parks)

Where to Go Next from Sussex County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Looking to extend your trip beyond Sussex County? Here are a few nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • New York City (less than two hours away by car or bus)
  • Philadelphia (about two hours away by car or bus)
  • The Pocono Mountains (about an hour away by car)

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking to explore local history and culture, take in stunning natural scenery, or simply relax and unwind, Sussex County is an ideal destination for any traveler. With so much to see and do, it’s no wonder that visitors return year after year to this beautiful and welcoming corner of New Jersey.
