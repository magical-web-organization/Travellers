Introduction to Belle Mead

Belle Mead, a small unincorporated community located in Somerset County, New Jersey, is a hidden gem that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and history. One of the most historic towns in the state, Belle Mead, provides an unforgettable experience to visitors with its historic buildings, lush parks, and diverse culture. This travel guide will help you plan your trip to Belle Mead with ease.

List of Places to Visit in Belle Mead

Belle Mead is known for its rich history, natural beauty, and bustling food scene. Here are some of the best places to visit in the town:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Operative Plasterers’ and Cement Masons’ International Association (OPCMIA) Local 29 Labor Museum: This museum is dedicated to preserving the history of labor unions in New Jersey. It showcases various artifacts, photographs, and documents related to the labor movement.
  • Rockingham Historic Site: Rockingham is a beautifully restored 18th-century mansion that served as General George Washington’s temporary headquarters during the American Revolution.
  • The Griggstown Canal and Locktender’s House: The canal and locktender’s house offer a glimpse of Belle Mead’s industrial past.

Natural Attractions of Belle Mead: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • D&R Canal State Park: This park offers visitors an opportunity to explore the canal, which served as a major transportation route in the early 19th century. It provides opportunities for hiking, biking, and fishing.
  • Harlingen Road Trail: This trail offers a scenic view of the Belle Mead countryside.
  • Stony Brook Millstone Watershed Reserve: This nature reserve spans across 1,000 acres and offers visitors an array of outdoor activities, including hiking and kayaking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Belle Mead

Belle Mead is home to several hiking and biking trails that offer stunning views of the surrounding countryside. Here are some of the best routes:

  • D&R Canal State Park Trail: This trail spans 77 miles and offers visitors a chance to explore the canal and surrounding forests.
  • Harlingen Road Trail: This 3.3-mile trail is perfect for hikers and offers stunning views of the Belle Mead countryside.
  • Six Mile Run Reservoir Site Trail: This 5.2-mile trail is perfect for mountain bikers and hikers and offers scenic views of the reservoir.

Best Things to Do in Belle Mead: Activities and Tours

Belle Mead has something to offer for everyone. Here are some of the best things to do in the town:

  • Visit the Winery at Belle Mead: This winery offers visitors an opportunity to explore the vineyards and taste some delicious wines.
  • Take a Hot Air Balloon Ride: Experience the beauty of Belle Mead from the sky with a thrilling hot air balloon ride.
  • Visit the Sourland Mountain Spirits Distillery: This distillery offers a unique opportunity to sample some locally made spirits.

Food and Drink Scene of Belle Mead

Belle Mead has a bustling food and drink scene, with several options for foodies. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Blueberry Pancakes: Made with fresh blueberries, these pancakes are a perfect breakfast option.
  • Pizza: Belle Mead is home to some of the best pizzerias in the state, serving up delicious slices.

Local Beverages
