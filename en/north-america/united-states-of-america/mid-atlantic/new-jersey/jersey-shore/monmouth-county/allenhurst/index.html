Introduction to Allenhurst

Allenhurst is a charming seaside town located in Monmouth County, New Jersey, USA. Allenhurst is a popular Jersey Shore destination renowned for its pristine beaches, scenic views, fantastic food and drink scene, culture and customs, and friendly locals. Whether you are looking for outdoor adventures, historical landmarks, or just relax and soak up the sun, Allenhurst is the perfect vacation spot for you.

List of Places to Visit in Allenhurst

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Historical Society of Ocean Grove: The museum houses an extensive collection of photography, manuscripts, textiles, and more. The museum provides visitors with an insight into Allenhurst’s rich history.
  • Asbury Park Historical Society Museum: The museum provides an excellent opportunity for the visitors to learn about Allenhurst’s history and heritage. There are many exhibits and artifacts relating to Asbury Park historical facts and cultural information.

Natural Attractions of Allenhurst: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Allenhurst Beach: A beautiful stretch of pristine sandy beach that is perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and surfing. The beach is perfect for taking long walks along the water’s edge and admiring beautiful sunsets.
  • Allenhurst Borough Park: The park provides visitors with a serene and peaceful atmosphere. It is ideal for picnics, nature walks, and hiking.
  • The Alan I. Pope Garden: The garden is renowned for its stunning display of flowers, plants, and trees. Visitors can take a leisurely stroll and admire the garden’s beauty.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Allenhurst

Allenhurst offers many scenic hiking and biking trails for visitors of all levels of experience. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • Greenway Trail: A scenic trail that follows the path of an old railway line, which is now a beautiful greenway. The trail is ideal for hiking and biking.
  • Allenhurst Beach Boardwalk: A boardwalk that stretches for several miles, providing an ideal scenic route for a leisurely walk, jog, or bike ride.

Best Things to Do in Allenhurst: Activities and Tours

  • Boat Tours: Visitors can take a boat tour and explore the beautiful coastline.
  • Fishing: Allenhurst offers some of the best fishing spots in New Jersey. Visitors can hire a charter boat and try their luck at catching fish.
  • Kayaking: Visitors can rent a kayak and explore the local creeks, rivers, and bays.

Food and Drink Scene of Allenhurst

Must-Try Dishes

  • Rock Lobster Bisque: A delicious soup made with fresh rock lobster and cream, garnished with chives.
  • Clam Chowder: This New England-inspired dish is made with clams, potatoes, and cream, served with crackers.
  • Scallops: Freshly caught scallops are a must-try dish in Allenhurst. They can be cooked in various ways, and every restaurant has its take.

Local Beverages

  • Monmouth Mead Works: A local meadery serving a variety of meads made from locally sourced honey.
  • Carton Brewery: A microbrewery that serves a variety of craft beers made from local ingredients.

Where to Stay in Allenhurst: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Asbury Hotel: A trendy, modern hotel located in the heart of Asbury Park. The hotel features an outdoor pool, rooftop bar, and excellent dining options.
  • The Ocean Plaza Hotel: A beautifully restored Victorian-era hotel located just a few steps away from the beach. The hotel has tastefully decorated rooms, a restaurant, and a bar.
  • The Majestic Hotel: A charming and elegant boutique hotel that is perfect for a romantic getaway. The hotel offers luxurious suites, a spa, and a restaurant.

What to Buy in Allenhurst: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Saltwater Taffy: A popular candy that is made in many flavors and colors.
  • Jersey Shore Art: The region is packed with talented artists, and there is artwork available to fit every taste.
  • Local Wines: The area is known for its excellent wines, and there are many wineries to visit and try samples.

Local Culture and Customs of Allenhurst

Allenhurst is a laid-back beach town known for its relaxed and friendly atmosphere. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, and take care to follow the town’s guidelines when it comes to beach etiquette, noise levels, and littering.

Best Time to Visit Allenhurst

The best time to visit Allenhurst is during the warm summer months between June and August when temperatures are ideal for outdoor activities. The town’s beach is the main attraction, so most tourists plan their visit to coincide with the warmer months.

Do’s and Don’ts of Allenhurst

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Follow the beach etiquette.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Explore the local hiking and biking trails.

Don’t:

  • Litter on the beach or in public areas.
  • Make excessive noise.
  • neglect the beach’s safety precautions

Safety and Health

Safety in Allenhurst

Allenhurst is a safe town with a low crime rate. However, visitors should always take the necessary precautions when traveling alone or in areas that are less populated. Beach safety is important, so it is essential to swim only in designated areas and to follow the guidelines set forth by the county.

Health in Allenhurst

Allenhurst is well equipped with pharmacies and medical facilities. In case of a medical emergency, dial 911 or go to the nearest hospital.

Getting to Allenhurst: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Allenhurst

The nearest airport to Allenhurst is Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR). The airport is approximately 50 miles north of the town.

Bus Stations Near Allenhurst

New Jersey Transit provides bus service to the town. The nearest bus stations are in Long Branch and Asbury Park.

Other Options

Many visitors drive to Allenhurst, as the town is located just a few miles from the Garden State Parkway. Train service is also available via NJ Transit and Amtrak, with dozens of trains daily to Atlantic City, New York City and other nearby towns.

How to Get Around in Allenhurst

The town is small enough to explore on foot or by bike. Visitors may also use a car or call a taxi service which is readily available.

Where to Go Next from Allenhurst: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Asbury Park: Explore the beautiful beach and boardwalk, parks, and restaurants.
  • Long Branch: Enjoy large parks, great dining, or browse shops in a variety of stores.
  • Ocean Grove: A serene and peaceful town with beautiful beaches and charming architecture.

Conclusion

Allenhurst is a hidden gem on the Jersey Shore. With its beautiful beaches, scenic views, hiking and biking trails, delicious cuisine, and warm and friendly locals, Allenhurst has something for everyone. No matter your interests, you will find something fun, exciting, and rejuvenating in Allenhurst.
