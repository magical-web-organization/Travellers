Introduction to Spring Lake

Spring Lake is a charming seaside town located in Monmouth County, in the heart of the Jersey Shore. This stunning town is known for its quaint atmosphere, scenic beaches, and unique culture. The town is filled with Victorian architecture, winding tree-lined streets, and award-winning restaurants, making it the perfect spot for a relaxing getaway.

List of Places to Visit in Spring Lake

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Spring Lake Historical Society Museum

The Spring Lake Historical Society Museum is the perfect place to learn about the town’s rich history. The museum features artifacts from the early days of Spring Lake, including photos, documents, and objects that tell the story of the town’s development. The museum also features exhibits on the unique architecture of the town’s homes and buildings.

St. Catharine Catholic Church

The St. Catharine Catholic Church is a beautiful landmark in Spring Lake that showcases the town’s religious traditions. The church’s stunning architecture is a feast for the eyes, highlighted by intricate stained glass windows, ornate woodwork, and soaring ceilings.

Natural Attractions of Spring Lake: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Spring Lake Beach

Spring Lake Beach is one of the most stunning beaches on the Jersey Shore. The beach has an expansive stretch of clean white sand, crystal clear waters, and awesome waves that are perfect for surfing and bodyboarding. It’s an ideal spot to sunbathe, play beach volleyball, or take a relaxing stroll along the shore.

Allaire State Park

For an outdoor adventure, head to Allaire State Park, located just a few miles from Spring Lake. This beautiful park offers a range of activities, including hiking, camping, horseback riding, and fishing. The park’s historic village is also a must-see attraction, featuring an 1830s iron-making town, blacksmith shop, and charming general store.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Spring Lake

Coastal Heritage Trail

The Coastal Heritage Trail is a scenic biking and hiking trail that stretches along the coast from Sandy Hook to Cape May. The trail passes through Spring Lake, offering breathtaking views of the ocean and the town’s lush surroundings. This is a great way to explore the beauty of Spring Lake and the surrounding area.

Best Things to Do in Spring Lake: Activities and Tours

Spring Lake Boardwalk

A walk along the Spring Lake Boardwalk is a must-do activity when visiting the town. This mile-long boardwalk offers stunning views of the ocean and is surrounded by beautiful gardens and crabapple trees. You can also rent a bike or a surrey to explore the boardwalk in style.

Spring Lake Theatre Company

The Spring Lake Theatre Company is a local gem, showcasing the town’s vibrant arts scene. The company produces a range of performances throughout the year, including musicals, plays, and concerts, making it a great spot for a night out with friends or family.

Food and Drink Scene of Spring Lake

Must-Try Dishes

Seafood

Spring Lake is known for its seafood, which is always fresh, and delicious. Top seafood dishes to try include lobster, crab cakes, and fresh oysters.

Pizza

Pizza is also a popular dish in Spring Lake with many local pizzerias offering some of the best in the state. You must try the famous Margherita pizza for an authentic taste of the town’s cuisine.

Local Beverages

Beer

Craft beer is big in Spring Lake, and you will find plenty of local breweries that produce a range of flavors. The Last Wave Brewing Company is a must-visit spot to try some of the town’s best craft beer.

Wine

The Spring Lake Wine Cellar is the place to be for wine lovers. This local wine shop offers a wide range of high-quality wines, including some of the best from both New Jersey and beyond.

Where to Stay in Spring Lake: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

The Chateau Inn and Suites

The Chateau Inn and Suites is a luxurious hotel located in the heart of Spring Lake. This elegant hotel features beautifully decorated rooms, gourmet breakfast, and a range of amenities, including a pool, hot tub, and fitness center.

The Hewitt Wellington Hotel

The Hewitt Wellington Hotel is another great place to stay in Spring Lake. This beautiful boutique hotel offers stunning views of the lake, beautiful gardens, and comfortable rooms.

What to Buy in Spring Lake: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

The Spring Lake Beach Badge

The Spring Lake beach badge is a popular souvenir to take home, showcasing the town’s magnificent beach.

Handcrafted Goods

Spring Lake is also known for its handcrafted goods, such as pottery, jewelry, and artwork. Local shops like The Emporium and The Gilded Lily offer great options for finding unique and memorable souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Spring Lake

Spring Lake is known for its friendly locals, hospitable community, and vibrant cultural scene. The town is known for hosting a range of festivals and events throughout the year, including the Spring Lake Five Mile Run, Christmas tree lighting, and fireworks displays.

Best Time to Visit Spring Lake

Spring Lake is a wonderful destination to visit at any time of year but is particularly popular in the summer months when the beach is in full swing. However, the town has plenty of activities and attractions throughout the year, making it a great place to visit anytime.

Do’s and Don’ts of Spring Lake

Do:

  • Bring your sunscreen, sunglasses, and beach gear when visiting Spring Lake Beach.
  • Take a bike or surrey ride on the Spring Lake Boardwalk.
  • Try the famous seafood and pizza from the local restaurants.
  • Take a hike or bike ride on the Coastal Heritage Trail.

Don’t:

  • Smoke on the beach.
  • Litter or pollute the environment.
  • Ignore the local rules and regulations.

Safety and Health

Safety in Spring Lake

Spring Lake is a safe and hospitable town. However, visitors are advised to exercise caution when swimming in the ocean or engaging in outdoor activities. Visitors should keep themselves aware of local weather and hazards.

Health in Spring Lake

Spring lake is relatively free of diseases or illnesses. However, visitors are advised to take necessary health precautions against the sun and water-borne diseases.

Getting to Spring Lake: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Spring Lake

The closest major airport to Spring Lake is Newark Liberty International Airport (EWR). The airport is approximately 60 miles from Spring Lake and has several transportation options, including trains, buses, and taxis.

Bus Stations Near Spring Lake

The nearest bus station to Spring Lake is located in nearby Asbury Park. This station offers connections to several major cities, including New York City, Philadelphia, and Baltimore.

Other Options

Visitors can also access Spring Lake by car, driving along the Garden State Parkway. Taxis and shuttle services are also available in Spring Lake.

How to Get Around in Spring Lake

One of the best ways to get around town in Spring Lake is to walk or bike. The town is pedestrian and bike-friendly and offers many hiking trails, bike paths, and scenic routes that are perfect for exploring the area.

Where to Go Next from Spring Lake: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Asbury Park

Asbury Park is a nearby town that offers many restaurants, shopping, art galleries, and theaters. The town is also known for its famous boardwalk and beachfront amusement park.

Ocean Grove

Ocean Grove is another small town located near Spring Lake. The town is known for its beach, historic hotels, and a range of shops and restaurants.

Conclusion

Spring Lake is a fantastic destination for anyone looking for a relaxing vacation with plenty of activities, culture, and nature. This town is perfect for a solo traveler, couples on a romantic getaway, or families looking for a fun-packed trip. Use this guide to discover all the unique features of Spring Lake and have the experience of a lifetime.
