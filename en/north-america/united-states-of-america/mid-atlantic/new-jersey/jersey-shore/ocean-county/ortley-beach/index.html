Introduction to Ortley Beach

Located in Ocean County on the Jersey Shore, Ortley Beach is a small, family-friendly beach town known for its beautiful views, bustling boardwalk, and a variety of outdoor activities. With plenty of options for dining, entertainment, and shopping, this seaside community is an excellent destination for a weekend getaway or a longer beach vacation.

List of Places to Visit in Ortley Beach

Here is a list of the top attractions to check out while visiting Ortley Beach:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum of NJ Maritime History: This museum showcases the history of New Jersey’s maritime industry and features exhibits on famous shipwrecks, the area’s fishing industry and boating history.

Natural Attractions of Ortley Beach: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ortley Beach Boardwalk: This boardwalk features stunning ocean views and a variety of rides and attractions for visitors of all ages.
  • Seaside Heights Beach: A short walk from Ortley Beach’s boardwalk, Seaside Heights is another popular beach destination in the area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ortley Beach

Visitors to Ortley Beach can enjoy a scenic bike ride along the boardwalk or take a leisurely stroll through the area’s many parks and nature preserves. A popular option is the Island Beach State Park, which offers miles of hiking and biking trails as well as excellent fishing and birdwatching opportunities.

Best Things to Do in Ortley Beach: Activities and Tours

  • Barbara Beach at Ocean Beach 3: One of the best beaches in the area, Barbara Beach is a great spot for swimming, sunbathing, and catching some waves.
  • Lighthouse Center for Natural Resource Education: This educational center offers a variety of programs for visitors of all ages, including kayaking tours, nature walks, and wildlife exhibits.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ortley Beach

Day 1: Arrival and beach time at Ortley Beach Day 2: Explore the Island Beach State Park Day 3: Visit the Museum of NJ Maritime History Day 4: Take a bike ride along the Ortley Beach boardwalk Day 5: Learn about nature at the Lighthouse Center for Natural Resource Education Day 6: Take a day trip to nearby Seaside Heights Beach Day 7: Enjoy a relaxing day at the beach or grab souvenirs to bring home

Food and Drink Scene of Ortley Beach

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pizza: Visitors to Ortley Beach must try some of the area’s famous New York-style pizza at Two Brothers from Italy.
  • Seafood: With its location on the Atlantic Ocean, Ortley Beach is known for its fresh seafood. Try the fried clams or lobster rolls at Joey Harrison’s Surf Club.

Local Beverages

  • Jersey Wine: Explore some of the best vineyards in New Jersey, including 4JGs Winery and Laurita Winery.
  • Craft Beer: Sample some of the best craft breweries in the area, including Icarus Brewing and Kane Brewing Company.

Where to Stay in Ortley Beach: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Anchor Motel: This budget-friendly motel is located just steps from Ortley Beach and offers clean, comfortable rooms.
  • The White Pearl Hotel: With stunning ocean views and luxurious rooms, this boutique hotel is perfect for a romantic getaway.

What to Buy in Ortley Beach: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Jersey Shore T-Shirts: A trip to the Jersey Shore isn’t complete without some local-themed t-shirts and souvenirs. Check out Shore Store for some popular options.
  • Beach Gear: Visitors can pick up everything they need for a day at the beach at rental shops like Ortley Beach Watersports.

Local Culture and Customs of Ortley Beach

Visitors to Ortley Beach should be aware of the area’s casual beach culture. Dressing in beach attire and flip flops is the norm, and visitors should always practice beach safety and respect the environment.

Best Time to Visit Ortley Beach

The best time to visit Ortley Beach is during the summer months, when the weather is warm and the beaches are easy to access. However, visitors can also enjoy the area’s natural beauty in the spring and fall when the crowds are thinner and the weather is still pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ortley Beach

Do:

  • Enjoy the scenic coastline and outdoor activities.
  • Respect the area’s natural beauty and wildlife.

Don’t:

  • Litter or pollute the beach or ocean.
  • Swim in areas where swimming is prohibited or when lifeguards are not on duty.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ortley Beach

Visitors should be aware of sun safety while enjoying the outdoors in Ortley Beach. Wear sunscreen and stay hydrated, and be familiar with local ocean conditions and currents.

Health in Ortley Beach

Visitors should be aware of the risks of tick-borne illnesses when spending time outdoors in New Jersey. Use bug spray with DEET and avoid areas of tall grass.

Getting to Ortley Beach: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ortley Beach

  • Atlantic City International Airport: Located about an hour south of Ortley Beach, this airport is served by several major airlines.

Bus Stations Near Ortley Beach

  • Bus Station in Toms River: This station is located just 10 minutes from Ortley Beach and is served by several bus companies.

Other Options

  • Car Rentals: Visitors can rent a car at nearby airports or in Toms River to explore the area on their own.

How to Get Around in Ortley Beach

Visitors can easily walk or bike around Ortley Beach to access the area’s many attractions and amenities. Buses and taxis are also available for those who prefer not to walk.

Where to Go Next from Ortley Beach: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Atlantic City: Just over an hour south of Ortley Beach, Atlantic City is a popular destination for its casinos, shopping, and entertainment.
  • Cape May: Located about an hour and a half south of Ortley Beach, Cape May is a charming, historic beach town known for its Victorian architecture and beautiful beaches.

Conclusion

Ortley Beach is a beautiful, family-friendly beach destination on the Jersey Shore. With plenty of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and excellent dining and shopping options, there’s something for everyone in this charming New Jersey beach town. Whether you’re looking for a weekend getaway or a longer beach vacation, Ortley Beach won’t disappoint.
