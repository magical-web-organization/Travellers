Introduction to Beach Haven

Located in Long Island Beach, New Jersey, Beach Haven is a charming coastal town that offers a perfect destination for a relaxing getaway. Known for its beautiful beaches, natural attractions, vibrant culture, and historic landmarks, Beach Haven has a lot to delight visitors.

List of Places to Visit in Beach Haven

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Long Beach Island Historical Museum
  • The Museum of NJ Maritime History
  • Surflight Theatre

Natural Attractions of Beach Haven: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Edwin Forsythe National Wildlife Refuge
  • Beach Haven Parasail
  • Thundering Surf Waterpark

Hiking/Biking Routes of Beach Haven

  • Barnegat Branch Trail

Best Things to Do in Beach Haven: Activities and Tours

  • Fantasy Island Amusement Park

Week-Long Itinerary for Beach Haven

Local Culture and Customs of Beach Haven

  • Respect the beach and keep it clean
  • Bike trails have rules and guidelines
  • Tipping at restaurants and hotels is a common practice

Best Time to Visit Beach Haven

The best time to visit Beach Haven is between June and September when the weather is sunny and warm.

Do’s and Don’ts of Beach Haven

Do:

  • Try local seafood dishes
  • Visit all popular landmarks
  • Respect the local culture

Don’t:

  • Litter on beaches
  • Break rules of hiking/biking trails
  • Be disrespectful towards locals

Safety and Health

Safety in Beach Haven

Beach Haven is a safe place to travel. However, remember to keep an eye on your belongings and follow the safety guidelines of the beaches.

Health in Beach Haven

Beach Haven has clean drinking water and medical facilities readily available if needed.

Conclusion
