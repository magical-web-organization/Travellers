Introduction to Jackson Township

Nestled in Ocean County in the Jersey Shore region, Jackson Township is a quaint town located in the heart of New Jersey. Known for its rich history and natural beauty, Jackson Township is a popular destination for visitors looking to experience the best that New Jersey has to offer. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, or thrilling amusement park rides, Jackson Township has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Jackson Township

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re a history buff, you won’t want to miss these must-see museums and historical attractions in Jackson Township:

  • Johnson History Museum: This museum chronicles the region’s history and the development of Ocean County with exhibits about early settlers, life in the late 1800s, and the rise of the automotive industry.

  • Six Flags Great Adventure & Safari: Take a trip to the world-renowned Six Flags amusement park, which boasts thrilling roller coasters and other rides, as well as a safari adventure where you can get up close and personal with exotic animals.

  • Old Brick Reformed Church: Dating back to 1836, this historic church is a must-visit for architecture enthusiasts and anyone interested in the area’s religious roots.

Natural Attractions of Jackson Township: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For those looking to get some fresh air and enjoy the great outdoors, you can check out these stunning natural attractions:

  • Jackson Pathfinders Trail: This scenic trail offers a perfect opportunity to take a hike and explore the town’s beautiful wilderness on foot.

  • Turkey Swamp Park: This wildlife preserve is great for cycling, hiking, fishing, kayaking, and wildlife observation. You can take a walk around the lake and enjoy panoramic views of the park’s lovely flora and fauna.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jackson Township

If you’re up for an active adventure, head to the following hiking and biking routes in Jackson Township:

  • Jackson Pathfinders Trail: This scenic multi-use trail is perfect for hiking, biking, and horseback riding. The path is well maintained, and you can explore the beautiful natural areas of the region.

  • Colliers Mills Wildlife Management Area: A popular spot for nature enthusiasts and hikers, this management area offers multiple routes that will lead you through a variety of landscapes and habitats, from dense forests to open fields and grasslands.

Best Things to Do in Jackson Township: Activities and Tours

In addition to exploring the natural and cultural highlights of Jackson Township, there are many other exciting activities you can enjoy during your visit. Here are some of the top things to do:

  • Six Flags Great Adventure & Safari: This amusement park is one of the state’s most popular attractions, featuring thrilling rides such as the Kingda Ka roller coaster, the tallest and fastest in the world.

  • Jenkinson’s Boardwalk: This classic Jersey Shore boardwalk is a great place to grab some classic carnival food, such as cotton candy and hot dogs, and enjoy the beautiful views of the beach and the Atlantic Ocean.

  • Climbing at Gravity Vault Climbing Gym: Experience the thrill of climbing on the colorful and challenging walls of this top-rated indoor climbing gym.

Week-Long Itinerary for Jackson Township

Here’s an itinerary for a week-long trip to Jackson Township:

Day Activity
1 Visit the Johnson History Museum and learn about the history of the region.
2 Take a hike along the Jackson Pathfinders Trail and explore the natural beauty of the town.
3 Enjoy some exciting rides at Six Flags Great Adventure & Safari amusement park.
4 Spend a day at Jenkinson’s Boardwalk, soaking up the sun and enjoying the carnival food.
5 Take a guided tour of the historic Old Brick Reformed Church.
6 Experience the adrenaline rush of indoor rock climbing at Gravity Vault Climbing Gym.
7 Go fishing or kayaking at Turkey Swamp Park and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Food and Drink Scene of Jackson Township

Must-Try Dishes

No trip to Jackson Township is complete without indulging in the local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • Saltwater Taffy: This classic Jersey Shore candy can be found at many local shops and vendors.

  • Boardwalk Fries: Thinly sliced and crispy, these fries are a staple of the Jersey Shore.

  • Taylor Ham, Egg, and Cheese Sandwich: If you’re looking for a classic breakfast sandwich, this is it.

Local Beverages

If you’re feeling thirsty, make sure to try these local drinks:

  • Craft Beer: New Jersey is home to a thriving craft beer scene, and many breweries and taprooms are located throughout the state, including Jackson Township.

  • Jersey-made Wine: Wineries in southern New Jersey, such as Valenzano Winery, produce excellent red, white, and fruit wines.

Where to Stay in Jackson Township: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Jackson Township offers plenty of lodging options, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. Here are some top recommendations:

  • Ramada by Wyndham Toms River: A modern hotel located just a short drive from Six Flags Great Adventure & Safari.

  • Quality Inn Toms River: This budget-friendly hotel is a great option for those looking to stay close to Six Flags and the beach.

What to Buy in Jackson Township: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don’t leave Jackson Township empty-handed. Here are some souvenirs and local products to bring back home:

  • Jersey Shore T-Shirts: These classic T-Shirts are a must-have for any visitors to the Jersey Shore.

  • Saltwater Taffy: Delicious candy perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Local Culture and Customs of Jackson Township

The people of Jackson Township are known for their warm hospitality, and visitors can expect a friendly welcome during their stay. The township is proud of its heritage and traditions, which often revolve around beach season and family vacations.

Best Time to Visit Jackson Township

The best time to visit Jackson Township is during the summer months (June through August), when beach season is in full swing, and visitors can enjoy the many outdoor activities and attractions in the area.

Do’s and Don’ts of Jackson Township

Do:

  • Do take advantage of all the outdoor activities in Jackson Township, such as hiking, fishing, and kayaking.

  • Do try the local food and drink, especially classic Jersey Shore snacks and craft beer.

Don’t:

  • Don’t miss out on the region’s cultural highlights, such as the historic Old Brick Reformed Church.

  • Don’t forget to pack sunscreen and other beach essentials if you plan to spend time at the shore.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jackson Township

Jackson Township is a safe destination overall, but visitors should take precautions and practice common sense. Avoid walking alone at night or in unfamiliar areas and keep an eye on your surroundings.

Health in Jackson Township

The town has great healthcare facilities, and visitors can access excellent medical care if needed.

Getting to Jackson Township: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Jackson Township

The nearest airport to Jackson Township is Newark Liberty International Airport, just over an hour’s drive away.

Bus Stations Near Jackson Township

Jackson Township is serviced by NJ Transit bus services from the nearby Toms River bus station.

Other Options

If you’re driving to Jackson Township, take the New Jersey Turnpike or I-195 East to reach the town.

How to Get Around in Jackson Township

The best way to get around Jackson Township is by car, but there are also public transportation options available through NJ Transit buses.

Where to Go Next from Jackson Township: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore other destinations in the region, consider visiting:

  • Atlantic City: Home to the best casinos, nightclubs, and boardwalks, located less than an hour from Jackson Township.

  • Philadelphia: The birthplace of America, where you can visit Independence Hall and the Liberty Bell, is only 90 minutes away.

Conclusion

Jackson Township offers a rich blend of natural beauty, cultural attractions, and exciting activities for visitors of all ages. From hiking and kayaking to amusement park rides and classic Jersey Shore snacks, there’s plenty to explore in this gem of the Jersey Shore region.
