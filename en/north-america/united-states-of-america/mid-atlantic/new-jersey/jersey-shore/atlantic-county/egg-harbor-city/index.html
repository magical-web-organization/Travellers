Introduction to Egg Harbor City

Egg Harbor City is a small yet fascinating town located in Atlantic County, New Jersey. It was founded in 1854 by German immigrants who were seeking to establish a utopian society. Today, Egg Harbor City is a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. It boasts a range of attractions that keep visitors enthralled and entertained. In this guide, we’ll explore the best of Egg Harbor City, including places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Egg Harbor City

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Egg Harbor City is home to several museums and historical places that are worth a visit. These include:

  • The Egg Harbor City Historical Society Museum: This museum is dedicated to preserving the history of Egg Harbor City. Visitors can learn about the town’s founding, its growth, and its people.

  • The Caldwell-Edwards House Museum: This house was built in the mid-19th century and has been restored to its former glory. Visitors can tour the house and see how people lived in Egg Harbor City during the 1800s.

Natural Attractions of Egg Harbor City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For those who want to experience the great outdoors, Egg Harbor City has plenty of natural attractions to explore. Some of the best include:

  • The Batsto River: This river is a popular destination for kayaking, canoeing, and fishing. It’s surrounded by beautiful forests and wildlife.

  • The Wharton State Forest: This huge forest covers over 100,000 acres and offers miles of hiking and biking trails as well as several campsites for overnight stays.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Egg Harbor City

Egg Harbor City has several options for hikers and bikers. Some of the best routes include:

  • The Batsto River Trail: This 3.5-mile trail follows the Batsto River and offers stunning views of the surrounding forest.

  • The Mullica River Trail: This 8.7-mile trail is perfect for more experienced hikers, offering stunning views of the Mullica River and the Wharton State Forest.

Best Things to Do in Egg Harbor City: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of things to do in Egg Harbor City, from exciting activities to informative tours. Here are some of the best:

  • Kayaking and Canoeing on the Batsto River: This is a great way to discover the beauty of Egg Harbor City’s natural surroundings.

  • Wine Tasting Tours at Egg Harbor City’s Wineries: Visit the local wineries, like the Bellview Winery and the Plagido’s Winery to sample some of the best wines in the area.

Week-Long Itinerary for Egg Harbor City

Here’s a suggested itinerary for a week-long visit to Egg Harbor City:

  • Day 1: Visit the Egg Harbor City Historical Society Museum and learn about the town’s fascinating history.

  • Day 2: Take a kayaking or canoeing trip down the Batsto River.

  • Day 3: Take a scenic hike in the Wharton State Forest and enjoy the stunning views.

  • Day 4: Visit the Caldwell-Edwards House Museum and learn about life in Egg Harbor City during the mid-19th century.

  • Day 5: Sample some of the local wines at Egg Harbor City’s wineries.

  • Day 6: Enjoy some outdoor fun at the Pinelands Adventures, which offers various outdoor activities, including whale watching to off-road adventures.

  • Day 7: Explore the local shops and restaurants in downtown Egg Harbor City.

Food and Drink Scene of Egg Harbor City

Egg Harbor City has an excellent food and drink scene, with several options for local cuisine and beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • The Cheese Steak Sub at Tony’s Baltimore Grill: This authentic Philadelphia-style cheese steak sub is a must-try for carnivores.

  • The Lobster Bisque at The Crab Trap: This creamy bisque is made with fresh, local lobster.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beers at Forgotten Boardwalk Brewing: This local brewery offers a range of delicious brews, including the Funnel Cake Ale.

  • Wines at Bellview Winery and Plagido’s Winery: These local wineries offer a range of wines, including the award-winning Pinot Noir.

Where to Stay in Egg Harbor City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several lodging options in Egg Harbor City, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Some of the best include:

  • The Colonial Inn at Historic Smithville: This beautiful inn offers cozy rooms and stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

  • The Atlantic City North Family Campground and RV Park: This is the perfect option for campers and RV enthusiasts, with several spacious campsites surrounded by nature.

What to Buy in Egg Harbor City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Egg Harbor City has several souvenir shops and local stores selling unique items. Some of the best things to buy in Egg Harbor City include:

  • Local wines and craft beers: These can be purchased at several local wineries and breweries.

  • Local handicrafts: These can be bought at the local markets and souvenir shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Egg Harbor City

Egg Harbor City has a rich cultural heritage, influenced by the German immigrants who founded the town. These influences can be seen in the town’s architecture, food, and language.

Best Time to Visit Egg Harbor City

The best time to visit Egg Harbor City is during the spring or fall months when the weather is mild. Summer can be quite hot and humid, while winter can be cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Egg Harbor City

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.

  • Take care of the natural surroundings while hiking and biking.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Egg Harbor City

Egg Harbor City is a safe destination, with low crime rates. However, visitors should still take precautions, such as locking cars and securing valuables.

Health in Egg Harbor City

Egg Harbor City has several medical facilities and pharmacies for any health emergencies that may arise during your visit.

Getting to Egg Harbor City: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Egg Harbor City

The nearest airport to Egg Harbor City is the Atlantic City International Airport, which is about 12 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Egg Harbor City

The Egg Harbor City Bus Station is located in town, with several connections to other cities in the area.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Egg Harbor City or take a train to the nearby Egg Harbor City Station on the NJ Transit line.

How to Get Around in Egg Harbor City

The best way to get around Egg Harbor City is by car or bike, as most attractions and natural areas are spread out.

Where to Go Next from Egg Harbor City: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Egg Harbor City is located in a region of New Jersey that is full of great destinations and attractions. Some nearby places to explore include:

  • Atlantic City: This exciting city offers casinos, shopping, and nightlife.

  • Cape May: This historic town is known for its beautiful beaches and Victorian architecture.

Conclusion

Egg Harbor City is a charming town with a rich history and natural beauty. Visitors will find plenty to see and do, from exploring the local museums and historical places to enjoying outdoor adventures in the surrounding area.
