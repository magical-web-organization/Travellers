Introduction to Somers Point

Somers Point is a charming coastal town nestled in Atlantic County, New Jersey. Once a popular hub for shipbuilding and seafood harvesting, the town has transformed into a thriving tourist destination offering a plethora of natural and cultural attractions. Its proximity to the coastline and rich history make it a popular stop for tourists. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take you through the best things to do and places to visit in Somers Point.

List of Places to Visit in Somers Point

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Somers Point has a rich history and is home to several museums and historical landmarks. Here are a few must-visit cultural attractions:

  • Somers Mansion: Built-in 1725, the Somers Mansion is a beautifully restored colonial house that offers an insight into the history of the town.
  • Great Egg Harbor Bay: A picturesque harbor, Great Egg Harbor Bay is a popular stop for boaters, sailors, and visitors alike.
  • Gateway Playhouse: A local treasure, Gateway Playhouse is an intimate theater that stages high-quality performances throughout the year.
  • John F. Kennedy Park: A memorial park dedicated to John F. Kennedy, this peaceful and serene park offers a great view of the bay and a popular picnic spot.

Natural Attractions of Somers Point: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Somers Point is surrounded by natural beauty and offers several outdoor activities for visitors to enjoy. Some of the most popular natural attractions include:

  • Somers Point Beach: A beautiful sandy beach that offers a great way to enjoy the outdoors and take a dip in the Atlantic Ocean.
  • Dolfin Dock: This recreational dock offers a chance to view local wildlife up close, including dolphins, birds, and seals.
  • New Jersey Audubon Society’s Nature Center of Cape May: A serene nature reserve offering a perfect setting for quiet walks and birdwatching.
  • Great Egg Harbor River: The picturesque Great Egg Harbor River offers several water-based activities, including canoeing, kayaking, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Somers Point

Somers Point is a great place for biking and hiking enthusiasts. The town offers several trails for outdoor adventurers to explore, including:

  • Somers Point Bike Path: A paved bike trail that winds along the bay, offering stunning views of the ocean.
  • Bayside Trail: This trail winds through John F. Kennedy Park, offering scenic views of the bay.
  • Holly Hills Trail: This hiking trail winds through a protected forest, offering a great chance to see local flora and fauna.

Best Things to Do in Somers Point: Activities and Tours

When in Somers Point, there’s no shortage of things to do. Here are a few must-try activities and tours:

  • Sightseeing Cruise: A bay cruise is a great way to see the town from a different perspective and appreciate its beauty.
  • Charter Fishing Trips: With its thriving marine life, Somers Point offers excellent opportunities for fishing enthusiasts.
  • Water sports: From kayaking to paddleboarding, Somers Point offers a plethora of water sports options for visitors to indulge in.
  • Wine Tasting: Take a tour of the town’s vineyards and taste the local wine.
  • Golfing: Somers Point is home to several world-class golf courses, making it a popular destination for golf enthusiasts.

Week-Long Itinerary for Somers Point

Here’s a week-long itinerary for those looking to experience everything Somers Point has to offer:

Day Activity
Monday Visit the Somers Mansion and Gateway Playhouse.

Food and Drink Scene of Somers Point

The town’s thriving food and drink scene offers a great taste of Somers Point’s local cuisine. A few must-try dishes and local beverages include:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Crab cakes: This local delicacy will have your taste buds tingling.
  • Pork Roll: This locally produced cured pork is a must-try for breakfast.
  • Sausage and Peppers: An authentic taste of Italian cuisine.

Local Beverages

  • Watermelon wine: A fruity, locally produced wine
  • Cape May Brewery: An award-winning brewery producing high-quality beer.

  • Sonesta ES Suites Somers Point: A welcoming and spacious hotel perfect for families and business travelers, offering scenic views of Great Egg Harbor Bay.

What to Buy in Somers Point: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When in Somers Point, take home a piece of the town’s charm with these must-buy souvenirs:

  • Seafood: Somers Point’s thriving seafood industry offers fresh and high-quality seafood.
  • Cape May Diamonds: These rocks polished by the ocean waves make unique and beautiful souvenirs.
  • Jersey Shore Memorabilia: T-shirts, keychains, mugs, and other items will make great souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Somers Point

Somers Point is a friendly and welcoming town with a strong sense of community. The town’s rich history and culture are evident in its many cultural landmarks and festivals. Visitors should respect the town’s customs and traditions and be mindful of their surroundings.

Best Time to Visit Somers Point

Somers Point offers something to enjoy year-round, but the best time to visit is during the summer months (June to August), when the weather is warm and sunny, and the town’s outdoor activities are in full swing.

Safety and Health

Safety in Somers Point

Somers Point is a relatively safe place to visit, but visitors should still exercise caution and be aware of their surroundings, particularly when participating in outdoor activities. Visitors should follow all safety guidelines and precautions.

Health in Somers Point

Somers Point has several medical facilities, including urgent care clinics and hospitals, that visitors can access if needed. Visitors should take all necessary precautions to prevent illness, including staying hydrated and using sunscreen.

Getting to Somers Point: Transportation Options and Logistics

Somers Point is easily accessible by various modes of transportation, including:

Airports Near Somers Point

  • Atlantic City International Airport: The nearest airport to Somers Point, located just fifteen miles away.

Bus Stations Near Somers Point

  • Atlantic City Bus Terminal: Served by various bus lines and located about nine miles from Somers Point.

Other Options

  • Taxi and ride-sharing services are widely available for visitors to get around Somers Point.

How to Get Around in Somers Point

Somers Point is a walkable town, and visitors can easily explore most attractions on foot. Other transportation options include bikes and car rentals.

Where to Go Next from Somers Point: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Somers Point’s location makes it an ideal starting point to explore nearby destinations, some of which include:

  • Atlantic City: Famous for its beaches, casinos, and boardwalk, Atlantic City is located just ten miles away from Somers Point.
  • Cape May: Located just twenty miles away, Cape May is a charming coastal town offering beautiful beaches, Victorian architecture, and a vibrant art scene.

Conclusion

Somers Point offers a perfect mix of natural attractions, cultural landmarks, and outdoor activities that make it an ideal destination for visitors looking to experience the best of New Jersey. Follow this comprehensive guide to make the most of your Somers Point experience.
