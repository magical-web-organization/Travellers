Introduction to Bethany Beach

Bethany Beach is a charming coastal town located in Delaware, in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States of America. Known for its stunning beaches, iconic boardwalk, and lively town center, Bethany Beach is a popular vacation destination for families, couples, and solo travelers alike. Whether you’re seeking adventure, relaxation, or simply a change of scenery, Bethany Beach has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Bethany Beach

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Bethany Beach Museum: Discover the history and heritage of the town through exhibits, photographs, and artifacts at the Bethany Beach Museum.
  • Indian River Life-Saving Station Museum: Learn about the history of the United States Life-Saving Service and how it became the U.S. Coast Guard at the Indian River Life-Saving Station Museum.
  • The Freeman Stage at Bayside: Catch a live performance of music, theater, or dance at The Freeman Stage at Bayside, an open-air performing arts venue.

Natural Attractions of Bethany Beach: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Bethany Beach Boardwalk: Take a leisurely stroll on the iconic boardwalk and enjoy the breathtaking views of the ocean.
  • Delaware Seashore State Park: Explore the stunning natural beauty of Delaware Seashore State Park, which features miles of pristine beaches, sand dunes, and salt marshes.
  • Fenwick Island State Park: Go fishing, kayaking, or paddleboarding in the calm waters of Fenwick Island State Park.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bethany Beach

  • Bethany Loop Trail: Embark on an easy 2.7-mile hike through the town and along the beach with the Bethany Loop Trail.
  • Junction and Breakwater Trail: Enjoy a scenic bike ride on the 5-mile Junction and Breakwater Trail, which runs through tranquil woodlands and farmlands.

Best Things to Do in Bethany Beach: Activities and Tours

  • Coastal Kayak: Go on a guided kayak tour and explore the stunning waterways of Bethany Beach.
  • Bethany Beach Farmers Market: Shop for fresh produce, artisanal products, and locally-made crafts every Sunday at the Bethany Beach Farmers Market.
  • Funland: Have some fun with your family and friends at Funland, an amusement park with rides, games, and food.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bethany Beach

Day Activities
1 Relax on the beach, take a walk on the boardwalk, and dine at a seafood restaurant.
2 Go biking on the Junction and Breakwater Trail, have a picnic, and visit the Delaware Seashore State Park.
3 Take a guided kayak tour of Bethany Beach waterways and enjoy some fishing.
4 Visit the Indian River Life-Saving Station Museum and have lunch at a local restaurant.
5 Shop at the Bethany Beach Farmers Market, have some ice cream, and catch a live performance at The Freeman Stage at Bayside.
6 Explore the Fenwick Island State Park, hike the Bethany Loop Trail, and have lunch at a seaside restaurant.
7 Spend the day at Funland amusement park, have dinner at a local restaurant, and enjoy the sunset at the beach.

Food and Drink Scene of Bethany Beach

Must-Try Dishes

  • Blue crabs: Savor the taste of the Chesapeake Bay with delicious blue crabs, Steamed or served as crab cake burgers.
  • Grotto Pizza: Have a slice of the iconic Grotto Pizza, a thin-crust pizza topped with a mixture of cheddar and mozzarella cheese, and a tangy tomato sauce.
  • Crab bisque: Warm up on a chilly day with a bowl of creamy and savory Maryland-style crab bisque.

Local Beverages

  • Dogfish Head Brewery: Visit the Dogfish Head Brewery and enjoy a craft beer made from locally sourced ingredients.
  • Nassau Valley Vineyard: Sip on a glass of award-winning wine made from grapes grown in the heart of Sussex County at Nassau Valley Vineyard.
  • Ale House: Try some artisanal cocktails, craft beers, and wine at Ale House, a hip and trendy bar in the heart of Bethany Beach.

Where to Stay in Bethany Beach: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Bethany Beach Ocean Suites Residence Inn: Stay at a luxurious hotel with an ocean view, featuring comfortable rooms, a rooftop pool, and direct access to the beach.
  • Holiday Inn Express Bethany Beach: Enjoy a comfortable and affordable stay at the charming Holiday Inn Express Bethany Beach, which is conveniently located close to the town center and the beach.
  • Addy Sea Bed & Breakfast: Indulge in a romantic and cozy stay at Addy Sea Bed & Breakfast, a historic and charming guesthouse located in the heart of Bethany Beach.

What to Buy in Bethany Beach: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Beach-themed souvenirs: Pick up seashell picture frames, ocean-themed decorative items, and quirky t-shirts that read “Bethany Beach”.
  • Tax-free shopping: Head to Tanger Outlets Rehoboth Beach and browse through a variety of shops offering clothing, footwear, home furnishing, and more - all tax-free.
  • Local produce: Visit the Bethany Beach Farmers Market and shop for fresh produce, baked goods, homemade jams, and artisanal crafts.

Local Culture and Customs of Bethany Beach

In Bethany Beach, like many seaside towns, relaxation is the name of the game. The pace is slower, and the locals are friendly and welcoming. Expect to see people dressed in shorts and flip-flops, and lots of families packed onto the beach. They also have a ‘quiet time ordinance’ during night hours, and beach patrol monitors noise levels during the day.

Best Time to Visit Bethany Beach

The best times to visit Bethany Beach is in late spring to early summer when the weather is warm and sunny, and the tourist crowds are not as heavy. September and October are also an excellent time to visit when the temperature is comfortable, and the fall foliage is stunning.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bethany Beach

Do:

  • Do take a walk on the boardwalk and enjoy the scenic views and fresh air.
  • Do try the local cuisine, craft beer, and wine.
  • Do follow the beach town’s quiet hours and respect the neighbors.
  • Do spend some time exploring the natural beauty of the area.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter on the beach.
  • Don’t be disrespectful to the locals and fellow travelers.
  • Don’t violate any beach safety rules.
  • Don’t be excessively loud and disturb the peace.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bethany Beach

Bethany Beach is a relatively safe coastal town with low crime rates. However, it’s always a good idea to practice basic safety precautions, such as keeping your valuables safe, locking your car and hotel room, and being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Bethany Beach

Bethany Beach is a healthy and welcoming tourist destination where you can enjoy outdoor activities, fresh air, and locally sourced food and beverages. However, it’s always a good idea to bring basic medicines and consult your doctor for any specific medical needs before your trip.

Getting to Bethany Beach: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bethany Beach

The nearest airports to Bethany Beach are Salisbury-Ocean City Wicomico Regional Airport (SBY), which is about one hour’s drive, and Baltimore/Washington International Thurgood Marshall Airport (BWI), which is about two hours’ drive. Both airports offer rental car services, shuttle, and taxi services.

Bus Stations Near Bethany Beach

There is no bus station in Bethany Beach. However, there is Greyhound Bus Lines station in nearby Philadelphia for those coming from/going to long distances. Whenever one reaches Philadelphia, there are taxis available to reach Bethany Beach.

Other Options

Another option to reach Bethany Beach apart from driving on their own: i) hiring a taxi, ii) ride-sharing service, iii) taking Amtrak train to Wilmington, DE and then travelling by taxi to Bethany Beach.

How to Get Around in Bethany Beach

Walking or a bike ride is the best way to explore Bethany Beach and the surrounding areas. Several biking and walking trails run throughout the town and the state parks, making it accessible for sightseeing and enjoying the stunning views.

Where to Go Next from Bethany Beach: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Rehoboth Beach: Drive down to the nearby Rehoboth Beach town, which is known for its vibrant boardwalk, charming boutiques, and amusement park.
  • Ocean City: Explore the exciting beach town of Ocean City, which offers a wide variety of entertainment, food, and shopping options.
  • Cape May: Take a day trip to the charming seaside city of Cape May, located in New Jersey, and explore its historic sites, restaurants, and beaches.

Conclusion

Bethany Beach is a must-visit destination that offers a rich blend of culture, history, nature, and adventure. With its stunning beaches, charming town center, and endless activities and attractions, a vacation to Bethany Beach is sure to be a memorable experience that will leave travelers refreshed and revitalized.
