Introduction to New Castle

New Castle is a city located in the state of Delaware, in the Mid-Atlantic region of the United States of America. Founded in 1651, New Castle has a rich history and a charming atmosphere that makes it a popular tourist destination.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take you through the best things to do, see and eat in New Castle, as well as provide some essential travel tips and information to make your trip a seamless one.

List of Places to Visit in New Castle

If you’re planning a trip to New Castle, there are several places you must visit to get a full understanding of the city’s fascinating history and heritage. Here are some of the must-visit places in New Castle:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • New Castle Court House Museum - This museum is the oldest building in the state of Delaware and was used as the meeting place for the colonial assembly.
  • Amstel House - This historic home is a testament to the wealth and opulence of colonial times. The house is fully furnished and features beautiful gardens.
  • Old Library Museum - This museum showcases the history of the New Castle Public Library and has a collection of rare books and manuscripts dating back to the 16th century.

Natural Attractions of New Castle: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Battery Park - Located on the Delaware River, Battery Park offers stunning views of the river and the surrounding city. The park also has walking trails and picnic areas.
  • Read House and Gardens - This historic home has beautiful gardens that are open to the public. The gardens have a variety of plants and flowers and offer a serene escape from the hustle of the city.

Hiking/Biking Routes of New Castle

If you’re looking to explore the beautiful outdoors in New Castle, there are several hiking and biking routes you can take. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • Michael N. Castle Trail - This trail is a 15-mile loop that runs along the Delaware River and is perfect for biking, running, or walking.
  • Old-New Castle Trail - This 1.8-mile trail runs through the historic district of New Castle and is perfect for a leisurely stroll.

Best Things to Do in New Castle: Activities and Tours

Aside from the cultural and natural attractions in New Castle, there are several activities and tours you can take to experience the city in a different way. Here are some of the best things to do in New Castle:

  • Delaware River and Bay Lighthouse Cruise - This tour takes you on a boat ride along the Delaware River and Bay where you can see several historic lighthouses.
  • Fort Delaware State Park - This fort was used during the Civil War and is now a museum and a state park. You can take a tour and learn about its rich history.

If you’re planning to spend a week in New Castle, here’s a sample itinerary that will take you through the best places to visit and things to do:

Food and Drink Scene of New Castle

New Castle has a thriving food and drink scene with several restaurants and bars that offer a variety of cuisines and local specialties. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in New Castle:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Crab Cakes - Made with fresh Delaware blue crab meat, these crab cakes are a must-try in New Castle.
  • Peach Pie - Delaware is known for its peaches, and the peach pie in New Castle is a delicious way to experience this local specialty.

Local Beverages

  • Dogfish Head Craft Brewery - This brewery is located in nearby Milton and is known for its unique and flavorful craft beers.
  • Peach Wine - Made with locally-grown peaches, peach wine is a sweet and refreshing beverage that’s perfect for a warm day.

Where to Stay in New Castle: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

New Castle has several options for accommodation, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are some of the top places to stay in New Castle:

  • Courtyard by Marriott - This hotel is located in the heart of New Castle and offers comfortable rooms and amenities.
  • Penn’s Place Bed and Breakfast - This historic bed and breakfast features beautifully decorated rooms and a charming atmosphere.
  • Frenchtown Inn - This inn is located just outside of New Castle and offers beautiful views of the Delaware River.

What to Buy in New Castle: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Local Culture and Customs of New Castle

New Castle has a rich history and a unique culture that visitors should try to experience during their trip. Here are some local customs and cultural practices to keep in mind:

  • Respect for history - New Castle is a city with a rich history, and visitors should show respect when visiting historic sites and monuments.
  • Friendly people - Delaware is known for its friendly locals, and visitors to New Castle will find the same warm welcome.

Best Time to Visit New Castle

The best time to visit New Castle is during the spring and fall when the weather is mild, and the crowds are smaller. Summer is also a great time to visit, but expect warmer temperatures and larger crowds.
