Introduction to Billings

Billings is a city located in Custer Country, Montana, USA. The city is known for its historical importance and natural scenic beauty. Nestled in the Rocky Mountains and surrounded by beautiful landscapes, Billings is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The city has several hiking and biking trails to explore along with museums, galleries, and cultural attractions.

List of Places to Visit in Billings

  • Yellowstone Art Museum: This is a must-visit place for art lovers. The museum features contemporary art exhibits and rotating shows from international artists.
  • Western Heritage Center: This center showcases the cultural and historical importance of the Yellowstone River Valley and the Northern Plains.
  • Pictograph Cave State Park: This is an ancient site containing cave art from the prehistoric period.
  • ZooMontana: This zoo houses more than 80 species of animals and is spread over 70 acres.
  • Moss Mansion: A beautiful 20th century mansion that showcases the opulence of the era.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Yellowstone County Museum

The Yellowstone County Museum features exhibits and collections about the history of Yellowstone Valley. The museum displays several artifacts from the region, including photographs, paintings, and objects from pioneer settlements.

Western Heritage Center

Western Heritage Center is a cultural attraction that features the history of Northern Plains and Yellowstone River Valley. The museum features arts, history, and cultural artifacts from the region.

Natural Attractions of Billings: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Pictograph Cave State Park

Pictograph Cave State Park is an ideal destination for nature lovers and hiking enthusiasts. The park features several hiking trails with scenic views and numerous caves with ancient rock art.

Two Moon Park

Two Moon Park is another ideal destination for hiking and biking. The park features several trails, along with picnic areas, and playgrounds.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Billings

If you are an adventure buff, Billings is the perfect destination for you with numerous hiking and biking routes. Take on the Rimrocks trail, or the Four Dances Trail for the ultimate hiking experience. For biking enthusiasts, the Zimmerman Park trail is a must-visit.

Best Things to Do in Billings: Activities and Tours

Billings Scavenger Hunt Adventure

Explore the city’s famous landmarks on an interactive scavenger hunt. The hunt takes you through the downtown areas, where you can interact with locals and learn about the city’s history and famous attractions.

Montana Brewing Company Tour

The Montana Brewing Company offers tours that enable beer lovers to learn the process behind beer making. This tour is an excellent way to explore the craft beer scene in the city.

Week-Long Itinerary for Billings

  • Day 1: Visit the Western Heritage Center and the Yellowstone County Museum
  • Day 2: Go on a hike along the Rimrocks trails and visit Pictograph Cave State Park.
  • Day 3: Explore downtown Billings and visit the Yellowstone Art Museum and Moss Mansion.
  • Day 4: Go on a brewery tour and visit Montana’s Brewing Company and Uberbrew.
  • Day 5: Take a day trip to Red Lodge Mountain for skiing and snowboarding.
  • Day 6: Visit ZooMontana, the Rimrocks Park, and Lake Elmo State Park.
  • Day 7: Relax and unwind at the Billings Hot Springs.

Where to Stay in Billings: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Northern Hotel

This hotel features modern amenities, including an on-site restaurant and bar, an outdoor Hot Tub, and a fitness center. The hotel is located in downtown Billings, offering panoramic city views.

Kelly Inn Billings

This hotel features a fitness center, an outdoor pool, and an on-site restaurant. The hotel is located only two miles away from the airport and offers free shuttle rides to the guests.

What to Buy in Billings: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Montana Silversmiths

Montana Silversmiths is the top retailer in Billings for authentic western-style jewelry. You can buy their expertly crafted accessories such as belt buckles, jewel-studded necklaces, and earrings.

Western Ranch Supply

Stock up on exotic Montana handmade gifts at the Western Ranch Supply. Treat yourself or your loved ones to the finest products like cowboy hats, leather belts, boots, and historic books.

Local Culture and Customs of Billings

Billings has a rich history of Native American culture and was named after Frederick H. Billings, an American financier and railroad executive. The city is also famous for modern western art and boasts of several art galleries showcasing contemporary art.

Best Time to Visit Billings

The best time to visit Billings is during the spring and summer. The temperature during these seasons is pleasant, and the weather is ideal for outdoor activities such as hiking and biking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Billings

Do:

  • Respect Montana’s wildlife and maintain a safe distance during activities like hiking and camping.
  • Follow the recommended guidelines for responsible travel during COVID-19.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in natural sites and avoid picking up any of the unique plants and flowers of the region.
  • Don’t consume alcohol in public areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Billings

Billings is a generally safe city, but travelers should still take precautions like avoiding high crime areas and keeping valuables safe. It is recommended to take care while hiking, camping, and exploring the natural sites as there is a possibility of dangerous wildlife.

Health in Billings

Travelers must take note of the high altitude and dry climate, which can cause dehydration and altitude sickness. Additionally, one must take note of the ongoing pandemic and follow COVID-19 restrictions and guidelines.
