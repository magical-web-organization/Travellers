Introduction to Harlowton

Harlowton is a small town located in the heart of Montana’s Russell Country. It is situated in the Rocky Mountains alongside sprawling open plains and offers visitors an opportunity to enjoy the stunning beauty of the great outdoors. The town has a rich history, and it has been an important transportation hub since its founding in 1900. Harlowton is a great destination for anyone who enjoys hiking, fishing, hunting, and exploring the great outdoors.

List of Places to Visit in Harlowton

Here are some of the top attractions to see and places to visit in Harlowton:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Harlowton Historical Museum – This museum features a collection of artifacts and exhibits that detail the history of the town from its early beginnings to modern times. Visitors can explore the exhibits to learn about the local history of the area.

Natural Attractions of Harlowton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Spring Creek Reservoir – Fishermen will love visiting Spring Creek Reservoir. This popular fishing spot is known for its excellent trout fishing.

  • Harlowton Reservoir – The Harlowton Reservoir is a great spot to enjoy water activities like kayaking, swimming, and boating. Visitors can also hike along the shoreline to enjoy the scenic views.

  • Crazy Mountains – The Crazy Mountains are located to the south of Harlowton and offer breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Visitors can enjoy hiking, camping, and horseback riding in the area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Harlowton

Harlowton has many hiking and biking trails that visitors can explore. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Harlowton Railroad Grade Trail – This easy walking trail follows the old Milwaukee Road railroad grade and offers beautiful views of the surrounding area.

  • Deadman’s Basin Loop – This intermediate hiking trail provides stunning views of the surrounding landscape and passes through some historic cattle ranches.

  • Middle Creek Loop – This intermediate hiking trail winds through a beautiful valley and provides stunning views of the Crazy Mountains.

Best Things to Do in Harlowton: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Harlowton, Montana:

  • Visit the Harlowton Historical Museum to learn about the local history of the town.

  • Fish at Spring Creek Reservoir.

  • Go boating, kayaking, or swimming at Harlowton Reservoir.

  • Explore the Crazy Mountains on horseback.

Week-Long Itinerary for Harlowton

Here is a suggested week-long itinerary for visitors to Harlowton:

Day 1: Arrive in Harlowton and explore the town’s historic buildings.

Day 2: Go fishing at Spring Creek Reservoir.

Day 3: Take a scenic drive to the Crazy Mountains and explore the stunning landscape.

Day 4: Visit the Harlowton Historical Museum.

Day 5: Go hiking on the Deadman’s Basin Loop.

Day 6: Explore the Middle Creek Loop hiking trail.

Day 7: Enjoy your last day in Harlowton by relaxing at the Harlowton Reservoir.

Food and Drink Scene of Harlowton

Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in Harlowton:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Montana Bison Burgers – These burgers are made with locally raised bison and are a must-try for meat lovers.

  • Huckleberry Ice Cream – Huckleberries are a local favorite in Montana, and the ice cream made with them is a must-try.

Local Beverages

  • Montana Wheat Beer – This local beer is made with wheat grown in Montana and has a light, refreshing taste.

Where to Stay in Harlowton: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several good accommodation options for visitors to Harlowton. Here are some of the best options:

  • Wheatland Motel – This cozy motel is located in downtown Harlowton and offers visitors comfortable accommodations at an affordable price.

  • Western Motel – This motel is located on Highway 12, just a short drive from the center of town. It offers comfortable accommodations and a friendly staff.

What to Buy in Harlowton: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some of the best souvenirs and local products to bring home from Harlowton:

  • Bison Jerky – This locally made jerky is a great snack to bring on hikes or long car rides.

  • Huckleberry Jam – Huckleberries are a local delicacy in Montana, and the jam made with them is a must-try.

Local Culture and Customs of Harlowton

Harlowton is a small town with a close-knit community that values hard work and the great outdoors. Visitors should be prepared to embrace the local way of life and enjoy the simple pleasures of the town.

Best Time to Visit Harlowton

The best time to visit Harlowton is during the summer months when the weather is warm and sunny. Visitors can enjoy outdoor activities like hiking and fishing during this time.

Getting to Harlowton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here are some transportation options to get to Harlowton:

Airports Near Harlowton

  • Helena Regional Airport – This airport is located about 90 miles east of Harlowton.

  • Billings Logan International Airport – This airport is about 130 miles southeast of Harlowton.

Bus Stations Near Harlowton

  • Harlowton Bus Station – Montana Transportation Company offers bus service to Harlowton.

Other Options
