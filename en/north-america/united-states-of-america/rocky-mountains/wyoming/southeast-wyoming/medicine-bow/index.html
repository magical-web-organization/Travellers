Introduction to Medicine Bow

Nestled in the southeast corner of Wyoming lies the charming town of Medicine Bow. With a population of just over 250 people, Medicine Bow makes for the perfect destination when you’re looking to get away from the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse yourself in the wilderness.

Located in the Rocky Mountains, this small town offers incredible natural beauty and a serene atmosphere. Visitors can explore miles of hiking and biking trails, go fishing in one of the many rivers, or simply take in the stunning scenery.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll provide you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Medicine Bow. From the best attractions and places to stay to where to eat and what to do, you’ll find everything you need to make the most of your trip.

List of Places to Visit in Medicine Bow

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Medicine Bow Museum

The Medicine Bow Museum is a must-visit attraction when in town. The museum provides visitors with a glimpse into the history of the town and the surrounding area. You’ll be able to see artifacts and exhibits that tell the story of the pioneers who settled in Medicine Bow.

Virginian Hotel

Another significant landmark in Medicine Bow is the Virginian Hotel. Built-in 1911, the hotel has a rich history and is a treasured part of the town’s culture. Visitors can take a tour of the hotel to explore its stunning architecture and learn more about its history.

Natural Attractions of Medicine Bow: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Medicine Bow Peak

Medicine Bow Peak is the highest peak in the Snowy Range Mountains and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. The peak’s hiking trails are well-maintained and offer hikers of all levels a fun challenge. The summit provides unparalleled views, making it a must-visit attraction for any nature lover.

Medicine Bow River

The Medicine Bow River is renowned for its beautiful scenery and is a popular spot for fishing. The river is home to many different species of fish, including rainbow and brown trout. Whether you’re an experienced angler or a beginner, you’ll enjoy the fishing opportunities on the Medicine Bow River.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Medicine Bow

Medicine Bow offers some of the best hiking and biking trails in Wyoming. With more than 400 miles of trails to choose from, there’s something for hikers and bikers of all levels. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Medicine Bow:

  • Medicine Bow Peak Trail
  • Lake Marie Trail
  • Medicine Bow Rail Trail
  • West Elk Ridge Trail
  • Sheep Lake Trail

Best Things to Do in Medicine Bow: Activities and Tours

Medicine Bow River Adventure

Take a guided tour of the Medicine Bow River with expert guides who will lead you on an exciting adventure. You’ll enjoy some of the best fly fishing in the Rocky Mountains and the opportunity to view the stunning scenery from the river.

Snowy Range Scenic Byway

The Snowy Range Scenic Byway is a breathtaking drive through the Snowy Range Mountains. The drive takes you through some of the most stunning landscapes in Wyoming and offers plenty of opportunities for hiking and photography.

Week-Long Itinerary for Medicine Bow

If you’re planning to spend a week in Medicine Bow, here’s a sample itinerary to help you make the most of your trip:

  • Day 1: Explore the Medicine Bow Museum and take a tour of the Virginian Hotel
  • Day 2: Hike the Lake Marie Trail and enjoy a picnic lunch
  • Day 3: Fish on the Medicine Bow River
  • Day 4: Take a guided tour of the Medicine Bow River
  • Day 5: Drive the Snowy Range Scenic Byway and go for a hike
  • Day 6: Visit the Medicine Bow Peak and enjoy the stunning views
  • Day 7: Relax and unwind in town

Food and Drink Scene of Medicine Bow

Must-Try Dishes

Baked Trout

Baked trout is a local favorite in Medicine Bow. This dish is made with fresh trout caught in the nearby river and is baked with herbs and butter for a delicious and flavorful meal.

Buffalo Burger

Buffalo burgers are a popular choice among locals and visitors alike. The meat is lean and flavorful, making it a healthy and tasty alternative to beef burgers.

Local Beverages

Medicine Bow Brewing Company

The Medicine Bow Brewing Company is a popular spot for locals and visitors alike. The brewery offers a variety of craft beers, including some made with local ingredients. Stop by for a pint and enjoy the cozy atmosphere and friendly staff.

Where to Stay in Medicine Bow: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Virginian Hotel

The Virginian Hotel is a popular choice for travelers visiting Medicine Bow. The hotel offers comfortable accommodations and is located in the heart of downtown, making it easy to explore all that the town has to offer.

Pole Creek Ranch

Pole Creek Ranch is a beautiful guesthouse located just outside of Medicine Bow. The property offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities.

What to Buy in Medicine Bow: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Medicine Bow Earrings

Medicine Bow Earrings are a popular souvenir among visitors. These beautiful earrings are made with stones found in the nearby Medicine Bow River and are a unique and beautiful reminder of your trip.

Handmade Soaps

Handmade soaps made with natural ingredients are also popular souvenirs in Medicine Bow. These soaps are perfect for anyone looking for natural and organic skincare products.

Local Culture and Customs of Medicine Bow

The people of Medicine Bow are friendly and welcoming to visitors. It’s important to show respect for local customs and traditions during your visit. Be sure to take the time to learn about the town’s history and culture.

Best Time to Visit Medicine Bow

The best time to visit Medicine Bow is during the summer months when the weather is mild and the outdoor activities are at their best. However, if you’re looking to experience the town’s winter activities, such as skiing and snowmobiling, then the winter months are also a great time to visit.

Do’s and Don’ts of Medicine Bow

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Take care of the natural environment
  • Enjoy the outdoor activities

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural environment
  • Ignore local customs and traditions
  • Disturb the wildlife

Safety and Health

Safety in Medicine Bow

Medicine Bow is a safe town, but visitors should still take the necessary precautions to ensure their safety. Be aware of your surroundings and follow any safety guidelines provided by the local authorities.

Health in Medicine Bow

The small town of Medicine Bow does not have a hospital, but there is a medical clinic that can provide basic medical care. Visitors should ensure that they have adequate health insurance coverage before traveling to Medicine Bow.

Getting to Medicine Bow: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Medicine Bow

The closest airport to Medicine Bow is Laramie Regional Airport, which is located about an hour’s drive away.

Bus Stations Near Medicine Bow

Greyhound and Jefferson Lines both have bus stops in Laramie, which is about an hour’s drive from Medicine Bow.

Other Options

Renting a car is the best option for getting around Medicine Bow and the surrounding region. Taxis and ride-sharing services are not available in the town.

How to Get Around in Medicine Bow

The best way to get around Medicine Bow is on foot or by bike. The town is small, and most attractions are within walking distance. However, renting a car is essential for exploring the surrounding region.

Where to Go Next from Medicine Bow: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Snowy Range Mountains

The Snowy Range Mountains are a popular destination for visitors to Medicine Bow. The range offers stunning scenery and plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities, such as hiking and skiing.

Laramie

The nearby town of Laramie is also worth a visit. Laramie is home to the University of Wyoming and offers a variety of cultural attractions, such as museums and art galleries.

Conclusion

Medicine Bow is a charming town that offers visitors a glimpse into the beauty of the Rocky Mountains. With plenty of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and delicious food, there’s something for everyone in Medicine Bow. Use this guide to plan your trip and make the most of your time in this beautiful town.
