Introduction to Afton

Nestled in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, Afton is a small town in southwestern Wyoming that offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and outdoor adventure. Whether you’re a history buff, a nature lover, or an adrenaline junkie, Afton has something for everyone. In this guide, we will take a closer look at the top attractions, activities, and experiences that Afton has to offer, as well as practical information to help you plan your trip.

List of Places to Visit in Afton

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Afton is rich in cultural and historical attractions that offer visitors a glimpse into the town’s fascinating past. Some must-visit sites include:

  • [[St. Anne’s Episcopal Church St. Anne’s Episcopal Church]]: Built in 1907, this charming church is a landmark of Afton.
  • [[ Star Valley Historical Society Museum Star Valley Historical Society Museum]]: This museum showcases the history of Star Valley and features exhibits on the area’s Native American heritage, pioneer life, and more.
  • [[The Intermittent Spring The Intermittent Spring]]: A natural wonder that spouts water every 15 to 20 minutes.

Week-Long Itinerary for Afton

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Afton, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day 1: Visit the Star Valley Historical Society Museum and St. Anne’s Episcopal Church.

Day 2: Explore the Bridger-Teton National Forest and go hiking on the Squaw Creek Trail.

Day 3: Go fishing in the Salt River Range.

Day 4: Take a scenic drive to The Intermittent Spring and enjoy the beautiful views along the way.

Day 5: Go skiing on Thayne Snowmobiling.

Day 6: Explore the Fall Creek Falls Trail on foot or by bike.

Day 7: End your trip with a horseback riding tour and a sightseeing tour of Afton.

Local Culture and Customs of Afton

Afton is a small town that takes pride in its western heritage. Here are some local customs and culture to be aware of:

  • Always greet people with a smile and a friendly hello.
  • Cowboy hats and boots are a common sight in Afton.
  • Hunting and fishing are popular activities in Afton.

Best Time to Visit Afton
