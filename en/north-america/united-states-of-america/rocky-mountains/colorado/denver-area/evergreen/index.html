Introduction to Evergreen

Located in the Denver area of Colorado, Evergreen is a charming mountain community that attracts visitors with its scenic surroundings and opportunities for outdoor activities. Set in the foothills of the Rocky Mountains, Evergreen offers a perfect getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life. Whether you are looking for adventure or relaxation, Evergreen has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Evergreen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Hiwan Homestead Museum: Explore the history of Evergreen and the region at this museum, which is a restored 1893 homestead.
  • Evergreen Fine Art Gallery: Home to a collection of contemporary fine art, this gallery showcases works from local and national artists.

Natural Attractions of Evergreen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Evergreen Lake: A popular spot for fishing, ice skating, and boating, Evergreen Lake offers beautiful views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Alderfer/Three Sisters Park: This 1,127-acre park has hiking trails, rock formations, and scenic overlooks.
  • Mount Evans Scenic Byway: Take a drive on this scenic byway to see stunning views of the Rocky Mountains.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Evergreen

  • Three Sisters Park Trails: With over 15 miles of trails through scenic wooded areas and rock formations, this park is perfect for a day hike or bike ride.
  • Elk Meadow Park Trails: Enjoy several biking and hiking trails in this park, with stunning views of meadows and forests.

Best Things to Do in Evergreen: Activities and Tours

  • Evergreen Golf Course: This 18-hole golf course is set in a beautiful location among the mountains.
  • Ziplining: Fly through the forest canopy with the zipline tour in nearby Conifer.
  • Horseback Riding: Take a horseback tour through the mountains and scenic surroundings.

Week-Long Itinerary for Evergreen

Day Activity
1 Explore Hiwan Homestead Museum and downtown Evergreen

Food and Drink Scene of Evergreen

Must-Try Dishes

  • Bistro Colorado: This restaurant is a favorite among locals, serving up delicious Colorado cuisine using locally sourced ingredients.
  • The Woodcellar: With a cozy atmosphere and delicious food, this restaurant is known for its wood-fired pizzas.

Local Beverages

  • Evergreen Brewery and Tap House: Enjoy a cold beer at the tap room of this locally owned brewery.
  • El Rancho Brewing Co.: This brewery and restaurant offers a selection of handcrafted beers and delicious pub fare.

Where to Stay in Evergreen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Bears Inn Bed and Breakfast: Located in a picturesque setting, this bed and breakfast offers comfortable accommodations and delicious breakfasts.
  • Evergreen Lodge at Vail: This lodge offers luxurious rooms, a spa, and an outdoor pool in a beautiful mountain setting.

What to Buy in Evergreen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Evergreen Gallery: Find local art and crafts at this gallery, which features a variety of works from Colorado artists.
  • Cactus Jack’s General Store: This store sells souvenirs, gifts, and local products like jams and honey.

Local Culture and Customs of Evergreen

Evergreen is known for its laid-back mountain town vibe and friendly locals. The community values nature and outdoor recreation, and visitors are encouraged to enjoy the natural beauty of the area while respecting the environment.

Best Time to Visit Evergreen

The best seasons to visit Evergreen are spring, summer and fall, when the weather is mild and ideal for outdoor activities. Winter offers beautiful scenery and snow sports, but may not be suitable for all visitors.

Do’s and Don’ts of Evergreen

Do:

  • Respect nature and leave no trace while enjoying the outdoors.
  • Visit local shops and restaurants to support the community.
  • Enjoy the scenic views and outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Leave trash or litter in public areas.
  • Trespass on private property.
  • Disturb local wildlife.

Safety and Health

Safety in Evergreen

Evergreen is a safe community, but visitors should take precautions when hiking or engaging in outdoor activities. Always be prepared for changing weather conditions and be aware of potential wildlife encounters.

Health in Evergreen

The high altitude of Evergreen can cause altitude sickness in some visitors. Be sure to drink plenty of water and take time to acclimate to the elevation. Visitors should also take precautions against sun exposure and use appropriate clothing and sunscreen.

Getting to Evergreen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Evergreen

The closest major airport to Evergreen is Denver International Airport, which is about an hour’s drive away.

Bus Stations Near Evergreen

Greyhound Bus Lines has a station in nearby Golden, about a 20-minute drive from Evergreen.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car to drive to Evergreen or take a shuttle from the airport.

How to Get Around in Evergreen

A car is recommended for easier access to the hiking trails and attractions in Evergreen. There are also local taxi services available, as well as bike rentals and public transportation options.

Where to Go Next from Evergreen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Golden: Visit this nearby historic town, home to charming shops, restaurants, and galleries.
  • Vail: Take a drive to this world-renowned ski resort town, which offers year-round activities and events.

Conclusion

Evergreen is a wonderful destination for visitors looking for a peaceful retreat in the mountains. With a variety of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and delicious local food options, it’s no surprise that Evergreen is a popular vacation spot for Coloradans and tourists alike. Plan your trip to Evergreen today and discover the beautiful Rocky Mountains.
