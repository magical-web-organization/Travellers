Introduction to Fairplay

Located in South Central Colorado, Fairplay is a charming town situated in the heart of Rocky Mountains. Also known as the “official trout fishing capital of Colorado,” Fairplay offers a plethora of outdoor activities, nature trails, and scenic views that attract tourists all year round. This guide will help you discover the best things to do and see in Fairplay.

List of Places to Visit in Fairplay

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

South Park City Museum

Founded in 1959, the South Park City Museum is a replica of a 19th-century mining town. The museum features 43 authentic buildings, including a blacksmith shop, a saloon, a schoolhouse, and a jail. Visitors can experience a glimpse of life during the gold rush era and learn about the history of the town.

Fairplay Valiton Hotel

Built in 1923, the Fairplay Valiton Hotel is a landmark historic building that has been restored and converted into a museum. Visitors can learn about the history of Fairplay and explore the exhibits featuring antique furnishings, photographs, and artifacts.

Natural Attractions of Fairplay: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The Bristlecone Pine Scenic Area

Located in the Pike and San Isabel National Forests, the Bristlecone Pine Scenic Area is home to the oldest living trees in the world, the Bristlecone Pine. The scenic area offers a quiet retreat for nature lovers and hikers. Visitors can enjoy the scenic views of the mountains and observe the unique flora and fauna.

Montgomery Reservoir

Montgomery Reservoir is a popular destination for fishing, canoeing, and picnicking. The reservoir is surrounded by beautiful views of the mountains, and visitors can explore the nearby hiking trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fairplay

Mosquito Pass

Mosquito Pass is a challenging hiking and biking trail that is only recommended for experienced hikers and bikers. The trail offers breathtaking views of the Continental Divide and the surrounding peaks.

Fourmile Creek Trail

Located near the town of Fairplay, the Fourmile Creek Trail is a popular hiking trail that offers scenic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. The trail is dog-friendly and suitable for all skill levels.

Best Things to Do in Fairplay: Activities and Tours

Trout Fishing

Fairplay is known as the “official trout fishing capital of Colorado.” Visitors can catch brown, brook, and rainbow trout in the nearby streams and rivers.

White-water Rafting

The Arkansas River, located near Fairplay, offers some of the best white-water rafting experiences in Colorado. Visitors can book rafting tours to experience the thrill of the rapids.

Food and Drink Scene of Fairplay

Must-Try Dishes

Rocky Mountain Oysters

A popular local delicacy, Rocky Mountain Oysters are deep-fried bull testicles served with dipping sauce.

Green Chili

Green chili is a hearty stew made with pork, green chilies, and a variety of spices.

Local Beverages

Breckenridge Brewery

Located in nearby Breckenridge, the Breckenridge Brewery offers a wide selection of craft beers that are brewed on-site. Visitors can sample seasonal brews and enjoy the mountain views.

South Park Brewing Colorado

South Park Brewing Colorado is a local microbrewery that offers a variety of handcrafted beers. Visitors can take a tour of the brewery and learn about the beer-making process.

Where to Stay in Fairplay: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Fairplay Valiton Hotel

The Fairplay Valiton Hotel is a landmark historic building that has been converted into guestrooms. Guests can enjoy comfortable accommodations in a unique setting.

Pillow Reisdence

Pillow Reisdence is a cozy bed and breakfast located in a picturesque setting. Guests can enjoy a home-cooked breakfast and relax in the peaceful surroundings.

What to Buy in Fairplay: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Handcrafted Jewelry

Fairplay has a number of artisan jewelry shops. Visitors can purchase handcrafted jewelry made with Colorado gems and minerals.

Local Honey

Fairplay is home to several apiaries that produce local honey. Visitors can purchase jars of honey as souvenirs or gifts.

Local Culture and Customs of Fairplay

Fairplay is a small, rural community with a friendly and welcoming atmosphere. Visitors are encouraged to respect local customs, including dress codes at local attractions and outdoor activities.

Best Time to Visit Fairplay

Fairplay is a year-round destination with something to offer in all seasons. The best time to visit depends on personal preferences and interests. Summer is perfect for outdoor adventures and white-water rafting, while winter offers skiing and snowboarding opportunities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Fairplay

Do:

  • Respect local customs and dress codes
  • Be friendly and courteous to locals
  • Enjoy outdoor activities safely

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural areas
  • Trespass on private property
  • Engage in disrespectful behavior

Safety and Health

Safety in Fairplay

Fairplay is generally a safe community with low crime rates. Visitors should be aware of the risks associated with outdoor activities, such as hiking and white-water rafting. Proper preparation and gear are essential for a safe and enjoyable experience.

Health in Fairplay

Fairplay is located at 9,953 feet above sea level, which can cause altitude sickness in some visitors. Drinking plenty of water and avoiding alcohol can help prevent symptoms.

Getting to Fairplay: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Fairplay

The nearest airport to Fairplay is Denver International Airport, which is approximately 105 miles away. Rental cars and shuttles are available at the airport.

Bus Stations Near Fairplay

There are no bus stations in Fairplay. The nearest bus station is in Buena Vista, approximately 20 miles away.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Fairplay or book a private shuttle service.

How to Get Around in Fairplay

Fairplay is a small town that can be easily explored on foot. Rental cars are also available for those who prefer to drive.

Where to Go Next from Fairplay: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Breckenridge

Located approximately 25 miles from Fairplay, Breckenridge is a popular destination for skiing and snowboarding in the winter and hiking and biking in the summer.

Buena Vista

Located approximately 20 miles from Fairplay, Buena Vista is a quaint mountain town that offers a variety of outdoor activities and cultural attractions.

Conclusion

Fairplay, Colorado is a charming town with a rich history and a variety of outdoor activities and scenic views. Whether you’re looking for trout fishing, white-water rafting, hiking or just relaxation, Fairplay has something for everyone. With this travel guide, you’re well-equipped to explore Fairplay and make the most of your visit.
