Introduction to Cañon City

Nestled in the heart of South Central Colorado, Cañon City is a charming city located in the Rocky Mountains. Known for its rich history, breathtaking scenery, and outdoor adventures, Cañon City is a must-visit destination for travelers from all over the world. Whether you’re a history buff, adrenaline junkie, or culinary enthusiast, Cañon City has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Cañon City

Here’s a list of the top places to visit in Cañon City that should be on your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Royal Gorge Route Railroad

A trip to Cañon City is not complete without a ride on the Royal Gorge Route Railroad. This historic train passes through the stunning Royal Gorge, offering panoramic views of the canyon and the surrounding mountains. The train features luxurious coaches, fine dining, and open-air cars for the ultimate scenic experience.

Museum of Colorado Prisons

The Museum of Colorado Prisons offers a unique glimpse into the state’s penal system, from the frontier days to the present. The museum is housed in the former women’s correctional facility and features interactive exhibits, artifacts, and onsite tours.

Natural Attractions of Cañon City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Royal Gorge Bridge and Park

Spanning 1,270 feet and towering 956 feet above the Arkansas River, the Royal Gorge Bridge is one of the world’s highest suspension bridges. The park offers breathtaking views of the canyon, as well as thrilling rides, zipline tours, and a skycoaster for adrenaline junkies.

Arkansas River

The Arkansas River is a popular destination for whitewater rafting, kayaking, and fishing. The river offers challenging rapids for experienced rafters and calmer sections for beginners.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Cañon City

Skyline Drive

Skyline Drive is a scenic, one-way road that offers panoramic views of the city and surrounding mountains. The road is paved and suitable for cycling, and there are several hiking trails that lead to stunning lookout points.

Tunnel Drive

Tunnel Drive is a three-mile hiking trail that follows an old railroad bed and passes through four tunnels. The trail offers stunning views of the Arkansas River and the surrounding mountains.

Best Things to Do in Cañon City: Activities and Tours

Royal Gorge Helicopter Tour

Get a bird’s eye view of the Royal Gorge on a helicopter tour. The tour includes stunning views of the canyon, the surrounding mountains, and the Arkansas River.

Royal Gorge Via Ferrata

For an adrenaline-pumping adventure, try the Royal Gorge Via Ferrata. This guided climbing experience takes you along the cliff faces of the Royal Gorge, offering stunning views and a thrilling challenge.

Week-Long Itinerary for Cañon City

Day 1: Arrive in Cañon City and take a scenic drive along Skyline Drive

Day 2: Visit the Royal Gorge Bridge and Park and take a thrilling zipline tour

Day 3: Explore the Museum of Colorado Prisons and take a train ride on the Royal Gorge Route Railroad

Day 4: Hike Tunnel Drive and go rafting on the Arkansas River

Day 5: Take a helicopter tour of the Royal Gorge and try the Royal Gorge Via Ferrata

Day 6: Enjoy a picnic at Red Canyon Park and visit the Fremont Peak Observatory

Day 7: Relax and unwind at a local spa or winery

Food and Drink Scene of Cañon City

Must-Try Dishes

Rocky Mountain Oysters

One of the most unique and stereotypically “western” foods is the Rocky Mountain Oyster. These deep-fried bull testicles are a local delicacy and can be found on many restaurant menus throughout the region.

Green Chili

Green chili is a staple of Colorado cuisine, and Cañon City is no exception. This spicy stew is made with green chilies, pork, and other ingredients and can be found on many local menus.

Local Beverages

Colorado Wine

The region is home to several wineries and tasting rooms that offer locally produced wines. Try a glass of Colorado chardonnay or cabernet and enjoy the stunning mountain scenery.

Craft Beer

Cañon City has several breweries that offer a variety of craft beers. Stop by one of the local tasting rooms and savor a cold pint of IPA, stout, or lager.

Where to Stay in Cañon City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

The Abbey Inn

The Abbey Inn is a historic hotel located in downtown Cañon City. The hotel offers comfortable rooms, a fitness center, and an onsite restaurant.

The Loft B&B

The Loft B&B is a charming bed and breakfast that offers panoramic views of the city and the surrounding mountains. The guesthouse features comfortable rooms, a hot tub, and a delicious daily breakfast.

What to Buy in Cañon City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Royal Gorge Merchandise

Pick up a souvenir or gift from the Royal Gorge Bridge and Park souvenir shop. Choose from t-shirts, hats, mugs, and other items that feature stunning photos of the canyon.

Handmade Pottery

Cañon City is home to several pottery studios that produce beautiful handmade ceramics. Shop for a unique vase, plate, or mug as a one-of-a-kind souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Cañon City

Cowboy Culture

The cowboy culture is deeply ingrained in the region’s history and can be seen in the rodeos, fairs, and festivals that take place throughout the year. Attend a local rodeo or bull riding competition to experience the wild west firsthand.

Best Time to Visit Cañon City

The best time to visit Cañon City is from June to September, when the weather is warm and sunny. However, the fall months offer stunning foliage that is perfect for hiking and outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Cañon City

Do:

  • Take a train ride on the Royal Gorge Route Railroad
  • Explore the Museum of Colorado Prisons
  • Try the local cuisine, including Rocky Mountain Oysters and green chili
  • Take a hike or bike ride along Skyline Drive

Don’t:

  • Forget to pack sunscreen and bug spray
  • Leave your hiking boots at home
  • Miss the chance to go whitewater rafting on the Arkansas River

Safety and Health

Safety in Cañon City

Cañon City is a safe destination for travelers. However, be sure to take precautions when hiking or biking in the mountains and stay aware of your surroundings.

Health in Cañon City

The city is located at high altitude, so it’s important to stay hydrated and take it easy the first few days to avoid altitude sickness.

Getting to Cañon City: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Cañon City

The closest major airport is Denver International Airport, which is located about 120 miles from Cañon City.

Bus Stations Near Cañon City

There are several bus stations located in nearby Pueblo, including Greyhound and Amtrak.

Other Options

Renting a car is the most convenient way to get around Cañon City and the surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Cañon City

The city is small enough to explore on foot, but a car or bike is needed to reach the more remote areas. Several bike rental shops are located downtown.

Where to Go Next from Cañon City: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Great Sand Dunes National Park

Located about 100 miles from Cañon City, Great Sand Dunes National Park features the tallest sand dunes in North America and offers hiking, sandboarding, and stunning views of the mountains.

Colorado Springs

Located about 45 miles from Cañon City, Colorado Springs is a must-visit destination known for its stunning natural scenery and cultural attractions.

Conclusion

Cañon City is a truly unique destination that offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and outdoor adventures. Whether you’re taking a train ride through the Royal Gorge, hiking the trails of Tunnel Drive, or trying Rocky Mountain Oysters, this charming city is sure to capture your heart. Plan your trip today and discover the beauty of Cañon City.
