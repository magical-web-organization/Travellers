Introduction to Westcliffe

Westcliffe is a charming mountain town located in the north-central part of Custer County, Colorado. Located in the Wet Mountain Valley, surrounded by the beautiful Sangre de Cristo Mountains, it is a perfect spot for nature lovers, outdoor enthusiasts, and those seeking an escape from the hustle-bustle of city life. The town is popular for its extraordinary landscapes, vast open spaces, dark skies, and friendly locals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Westcliffe

Westcliffe is generally a safe place to visit, but it’s crucial to take some precautions while traveling.

  • Don’t leave valuables visible in your car
  • Stay away from Wildlife
  • Carry plenty of water

Health in Westcliffe

Westcliffe is at high altitude, and visitors may experience altitude sickness. It is essential to stay hydrated and take it easy during the first few days of your arrival.
