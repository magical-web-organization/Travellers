Introduction to Black Hawk

Black Hawk is a charming mountain town located in the Front Range of Colorado in the Rocky Mountains, United States of America. The town is known for its rich history dating back to the mid-19th century, world-class entertainment, and casino scene. The town is also surrounded by picturesque landscapes that make it a hub for outdoor enthusiasts. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore all the amazing things to do in Black Hawk, including cultural attractions, outdoor adventures, dining, lodging, and transportation options.

List of Places to Visit in Black Hawk

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Gilpin Historical Society Museum: This museum offers a glimpse into the famous gold rush that took place in Black Hawk in the 1800s. Visitors can explore the mining equipment, view photos of the past, and experience what life was like during the gold rush.
  • Central City Opera House: Built in 1878, the opera house is an exceptional example of 19th-century architecture. Visitors can enjoy live performances, guided tours, and exhibits.
  • The Teller County Jail Museum: Located in nearby Central City, this museum offers a unique insight into the history of law enforcement and the daily lives of people in the area during the 1800s.

Natural Attractions of Black Hawk: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Golden Gate Canyon State Park: This state park features several trails for hiking and mountain biking, as well as campsites and picnic areas. Visitors can also enjoy fishing in the pristine lakes and streams or take in mountain vistas.
  • Mount Evans Scenic Byway: This is one of the highest paved roads in North America, featuring breathtaking views of the surrounding Rocky Mountains. Visitors can reach the summit of Mount Evans by car or hike to the top for an unforgettable experience.
  • Ameristar Casino Black Hawk: Not only is this casino renowned for its thrilling gaming action, but it also offers stunning views of the Rocky Mountains.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Black Hawk

  • Oh My God Road: This road is a thrilling scenic drive that winds through the mountains and offers beautiful views of the surrounding area. It’s a popular route for bikers and hikers, but drivers should be aware that the road is narrow and winding and requires careful driving.
  • Eagle Trail: This trail is an easy hike that offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains. Visitors can take in the beauty of the Rockies while walking alongside a creek and through meadows of wildflowers.

Best Things to Do in Black Hawk: Activities and Tours

  • Black Hawk Ziplining: This tour is perfect for adventure seekers who want to experience the unique beauty of the Rocky Mountains from above. Visitors can zip along six different lines, taking in the scenery and experiencing the thrill of the activity.
  • Whitewater Rafting: This is a fantastic way to explore the beauty of the Clear Creek River while also experiencing a thrilling activity. Visitors can enjoy a guided tour or rent equipment for a self-guided adventure.

Week-Long Itinerary for Black Hawk

Day Activity
1 Visit the Gilpin Historical Society Museum and explore the town’s mining history.

3 Go whitewater rafting on Clear Creek River.

7 Relax and enjoy the amenities at one of Black Hawk’s many resorts and spas.

Food and Drink Scene of Black Hawk

Must-Try Dishes

  • Rocky Mountain Oysters: This delicacy is a must-try for visitors to Black Hawk. These bull testicles are breaded and fried for a unique flavor that is beloved by locals and visitors alike.
  • Smoked Trout: This local favorite is caught fresh from nearby streams and is smoked to perfection.

Local Beverages

  • Coors Brewery: Visitors to Black Hawk can take a short drive to the Coors Brewery in nearby Golden to sample some of the region’s famous beer.
  • Blackjack Brewery: This brewery is located in the heart of Black Hawk and serves a variety of craft beers.

Where to Stay in Black Hawk: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Ameristar Casino Resort Spa Black Hawk: This resort offers luxurious accommodations and an array of amenities, including a spa, casino, and restaurants.
  • Century Casino & Hotel: This hotel is located in the heart of Black Hawk and offers comfortable accommodations and easy access to all of the town’s attractions.
  • Isle Casino Hotel Black Hawk: This casino is located in the heart of Black Hawk and offers a variety of gaming options and comfortable hotel rooms.

What to Buy in Black Hawk: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Gold and Silver Jewelry: Visitors can find a variety of beautiful jewelry made from locally-mined precious metals.
  • Black Hawk T-Shirts and Hats: Visitors can take home a souvenir to remember their visit to this unique mountain town.

Local Culture and Customs of Black Hawk

Black Hawk has a rich history woven with the mining industry and the gold rush of the 1800s. Visitors can learn about the town’s past and people by visiting local museums and historical landmarks. The town is also known for its thriving gaming and entertainment scene, attracting visitors from all over the world.

Best Time to Visit Black Hawk

The best time to visit Black Hawk is during the summer months, where visitors can enjoy the town’s outdoor activities without the cold temperatures of winter. Fall is also a beautiful time to visit the area when the leaves change colors.

Do’s and Don’ts of Black Hawk

Do:

  • Do take time to visit the local museums and learn about the town’s history.
  • Do try the local cuisine, including Rocky Mountain oysters and smoked trout.
  • Do enjoy the beautiful scenery, especially during the summer and fall months.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the environment while exploring the outdoors.
  • Don’t forget to bring warm clothing, even in the summer months.
  • Don’t fail to try your luck at one of the many casinos in town.

Safety and Health

Safety in Black Hawk

Black Hawk is considered a safe town, but visitors should take precautions when exploring the outdoors, such as hiking or rafting. Visitors should also be aware of the potential hazards of driving the Oh My God Road and other narrow mountain roads.

Health in Black Hawk

Visitors should ensure they have proper health insurance coverage before visiting Black Hawk. There is a hospital in nearby Golden and emergency services available in case of illness or injury.

Getting to Black Hawk: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Black Hawk

The closest airport to Black Hawk is Denver International Airport, which is approximately one hour and 20 minutes away by car.

Bus Stations Near Black Hawk

Greyhound offers bus service to nearby cities, including Denver.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi from Denver to Black Hawk.

How to Get Around in Black Hawk

Visitors can easily explore the town on foot or rent a bike to explore nearby trails.

Where to Go Next from Black Hawk: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Golden: Located about 30 minutes away by car, Golden offers a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking, rafting, and rock climbing.
  • Denver: Located about an hour away by car, Denver offers a variety of cultural and historical attractions, as well as an array of dining and entertainment options.

Conclusion

Black Hawk is a hidden gem in the Rocky Mountains, with something to offer for everyone. From its rich history and cultural attractions to its beautiful natural surroundings and thrilling outdoor adventures, Black Hawk is truly an unforgettable destination. Visitors can enjoy locally-inspired cuisine, luxurious resort accommodations, and easy access to nearby cities and attractions. Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, thrill seeker, or just looking for a relaxing getaway, Black Hawk is the perfect destination for your next vacation.
