Nederland, Colorado is a quaint mountain town nestled in the Rocky Mountains, just a short drive from Boulder. Known for its eclectic art community, outdoor adventures, and scenic views, this small town has something for everyone. Plan your visit to Nederland with our comprehensive travel guide filled with things to do, places to see, and essential travel tips.

Introduction to Nederland

Nederland is located in Front Range, in the heart of the Rocky Mountains, and is known for its natural beauty and laid-back vibe. While the town may be small, it’s surprisingly lively thanks to its burgeoning arts and culture scene. Whether you’re looking for an outdoor adventure or a relaxing weekend getaway, you’ll find it in Nederland.

List of Places to Visit in Nederland

If you’re planning a visit to Nederland, make sure to check out these top-rated attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Carousel of Happiness: This whimsical carousel features hand-carved animals and is sure to delight visitors of all ages.
  • Nederland Mining Museum: Learn about the town’s mining history at this fascinating museum, which features exhibits and artifacts from the area’s mining era.

Natural Attractions of Nederland: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Eldora Mountain Resort: Ski, snowboard, or sled down the slopes of this popular ski resort.
  • Brainard Lake Recreation Area: Take a scenic hike or picnic in this beautiful alpine setting.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Nederland

Nederland is a hiker’s paradise, with plenty of trails for all skill levels. Here are some of our favorites:

  • Hessie Trailhead: This popular trailhead offers access to several scenic hikes, including the Lost Lake Trail and the King Lake Trail.
  • Ginny Trail: This scenic trail offers stunning views of the Continental Divide and is a favorite among hikers and bikers alike.

Best Things to Do in Nederland: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re an adrenaline junkie or just looking for a relaxing day out, Nederland has plenty of activities and tours to keep you entertained. Here are some of our top picks:

  • Nederland Zip Line Tours: Soar through the beautiful Colorado Rockies on a zip line.
  • Wild Bear Nature Center: This nature center offers hands-on exhibits and educational programs for visitors of all ages.

Week-Long Itinerary for Nederland

Day 1: Explore the town’s art galleries and gift shops

Day 2: Take a scenic hike or bike ride in the nearby mountains

Day 3: Visit the Nederland Mining Museum and Carousel of Happiness

Day 4: Ski or snowboard at Eldora Mountain Resort

Day 5: Experience the thrill of zip lining with Nederland Zip Line Tours

Day 6: Relax at a local hot spring

Day 7: Have a picnic at the gorgeous Brainard Lake Recreation Area

Food and Drink Scene of Nederland

Nederland may be a small town, but it’s got a big food and drink scene. Make sure to check out these must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Ned’s Wonder: A local favorite, this sandwich features roasted turkey, avocado, bacon, and cream cheese on ciabatta bread.
  • Colorado Green Chili: This spicy stew made with pork, chili peppers, and tomatoes is a true Colorado classic.

Local Beverages

  • Very Nice Brewing Company: Sample some of the town’s best craft beers at this popular brewery.
  • Mountain People’s Co-Op: Grab a cup of fair-trade coffee or tea at this charming co-op.

Where to Stay in Nederland: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Nederland has plenty of options for lodging, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious guesthouses. Here are some of our top picks:

  • Lazy Mountain Retreat: This charming guesthouse offers stunning mountain views and cozy amenities.
  • The Boulder Adventure Lodge: This nearby hostel is a popular spot for adventure-seekers and budget-conscious travelers.

What to Buy in Nederland: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs or locally made products to bring home, Nederland has plenty of options. Here are some of our top picks:

  • Blue Owl Books and Boutique: This charming shop offers a unique selection of books, gifts, and jewelry.
  • Mountain Man Outdoor Store: Stock up on outdoor gear and clothing at this popular retailer.

Local Culture and Customs of Nederland

Nederland has a laid-back and friendly culture, with a strong emphasis on community and the outdoors. The town is home to a thriving arts scene, with plenty of galleries and cultural events throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Nederland

The best times to visit Nederland are during the spring and summer months, when the weather is mild and the wildflowers are in bloom. However, the town is also beautiful in the fall, when the leaves change colors, and in the winter, when the snow-covered mountains offer a picturesque backdrop.

Do’s and Don’ts of Nederland

Do:

  • Take advantage of the town’s outdoor activities, such as hiking and skiing.
  • Explore the local arts and culture scene.
  • Respect the town’s natural beauty and wildlife.

Don’t:

  • Leave trash or litter on the trails or in town.
  • Interrupt the town’s peaceful vibe with loud noises or disruptive behavior.
  • Ignore warning signs or disobey park rules.

Safety and Health

Nederland is a safe and healthy town, but it’s important to be prepared for the mountain environment. Here are some tips for staying safe and healthy during your visit:

Safety in Nederland

  • Stay on marked trails and obey park rules and regulations.
  • Watch out for wildlife, such as bears and mountain lions.
  • Be prepared for changing weather conditions, including sudden rain or snowstorms.

Health in Nederland

  • Stay hydrated at all times, especially at higher elevations.
  • Wear sunscreen and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.
  • Be prepared for altitude sickness if you are not used to the high elevation.

Getting to Nederland: Transportation Options and Logistics

Nederland is located about 17 miles west of Boulder, and is easily accessible by car or bus. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Nederland

  • Denver International Airport (DEN): Approximately 45 miles southeast of Nederland.

Bus Stations Near Nederland

  • Regional Transportation District (RTD): Provides bus service to Boulder and surrounding areas.

Other Options

  • Rent a car: Several car rental companies operate out of Denver International Airport, as well as in Boulder.
  • Uber or Lyft: Both ride-sharing services are available in the area.

How to Get Around in Nederland

The best way to get around Nederland is by car or bike, but the town is also easily walkable. Many hiking trails and outdoor attractions are accessible by foot or bike.

Where to Go Next from Nederland: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have more time to explore the region, make sure to check out these nearby destinations and excursions:

  • Boulder, Colorado: Just a short drive from Nederland, Boulder offers a vibrant arts scene, excellent dining, and plenty of outdoor activities.
  • Rocky Mountain National Park: One of Colorado’s top attractions, this national park offers stunning mountain views, hiking trails, and wildlife viewing opportunities.

Conclusion

Whether you’re seeking outdoor adventures or a relaxing weekend getaway, Nederland is the perfect destination. With its natural beauty, thriving arts scene, and friendly community, you’ll never run out of things to do and see. Plan your visit today and experience the best that Colorado has to offer.
