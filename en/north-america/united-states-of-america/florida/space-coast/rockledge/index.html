Introduction to Rockledge

Located in Space Coast, Florida, Rockledge is a charming city that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural richness. With its beaches, parks, museums, trails, and delicious cuisine, Rockledge has something for everyone. In this travel guide, we will take you on a tour of the best things to do, see, and eat in Rockledge.

List of Places to Visit in Rockledge

Here is a list of the top places to visit in Rockledge:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Brevard Museum of History and Natural Science: Explore the history and natural science of Brevard County through interactive exhibits and displays.
  • Cocoa Village Playhouse: Watch a live theatrical show, musical, or comedy performance at this historic theater located in Downtown Cocoa.
  • Jackie Robinson Ballpark: See the site where Jackie Robinson broke the segregation barrier in baseball during his first Spring Training game with the Montreal Royals in 1946.

Natural Attractions of Rockledge: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Riverfront Park: Enjoy fishing, kayaking, picnic areas, and playgrounds in this beautiful park that overlooks the Indian River Lagoon.
  • Rockledge Park: Swim, sunbathe, and explore the beachside trails at this beautiful park on the shores of the Atlantic Ocean.
  • Turkey Creek Sanctuary: Hike, bike, or birdwatch through the lush mangrove forests and wetlands of this 130-acre wildlife preserve.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Rockledge

  • East Central Regional Rail Trail: Walk, jog, or bike through 48.5 miles of scenic trails that pass through Brevard and Volusia counties.
  • Wickham Park: Enjoy hiking and biking trails, playgrounds, and picnic areas in this expansive park that covers 391 acres.

Best Things to Do in Rockledge: Activities and Tours

  • AirBoat Rides Melbourne: Take an airboat tour through the Thousand Islands Conservation Area and witness the beauty of the Indian River Lagoon and its diverse wildlife.
  • Kennedy Space Center Visitor Complex: Learn about the history of space exploration, see rockets and spaceships up close, and watch a live rocket launch if you’re lucky.
  • Treetop Trek Aerial Adventures: Experience an exciting aerial adventure with zip lines, obstacles, and climbing challenges in the treetops of Melbourne.

Food and Drink Scene of Rockledge

Rockledge has a diverse and delicious food and drink scene that you won’t want to miss. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Seafood: Enjoy fresh seafood dishes such as Shrimp Scampi, Crab Cakes, and Fish Tacos at The Twisted Birch Sports Bar and Grill.
  • Barbeque: Savor mouthwatering barbeque ribs, pulled pork, and brisket at Slow & Low Barbeque.
  • Pizza: Try the famous New York-style pizza at DeVinci’s Pizza.

Local Beverages

  • Florida Beer Company: Visit the tasting room and brewery of Florida’s largest craft beer brewer and sample some of their award-winning brews.
  • Straight Whisky: Enjoy whiskey flights and craft cocktails at this trendy whiskey bar located in Downtown Melbourne.
  • The Salty Fox: Sip on coffee, tea, and other hot or cold drinks, and enjoy a cozy atmosphere with a touch of vintage charm.

Where to Stay in Rockledge: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Rockledge has a variety of accommodation options that cater to all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best places to stay in Rockledge:

  • Parrish Grove Inn: This charming bed and breakfast located in a historic house offers comfortable rooms and personalized service.
  • Hilton Melbourne Beach Oceanfront: Enjoy beachfront views, outdoor pools, and on-site restaurants at this luxurious hotel.
  • Budget Inn Melbourne: Stay in this budget hotel that offers simple and clean rooms and easy access to the beaches and attractions.

What to Buy in Rockledge: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Looking for souvenirs and local products to take home? Here are some ideas:

  • Space-themed souvenirs: Visit the Kennedy Space Center Gift Shop and buy t-shirts, caps, and other memorabilia that commemorate your visit to the space center.
  • Citrus fruit products: Try and buy locally-made orange, grapefruit, and other citrus fruit products such as jams, jellies, and marmalades at the Rockledge Farmers Market.
  • Handmade jewelry and art: Browse and buy unique and creative handmade jewelry, ceramics, and art pieces at the Downtown Melbourne Art Festival.

Local Culture and Customs of Rockledge

Rockledge has a friendly and hospitable local culture that values good manners, modesty, and respect. Here are some customs and etiquette tips to keep in mind:

  • Greeting: It is polite to greet people with a smile, handshake, or nod, and use titles and last names when appropriate.
  • Dress code: Dress respectfully and modestly, especially in religious or cultural sites.
  • Tipping: A tip of 15-20% is expected in restaurants and bars, and for services such as hairdressers, taxi drivers, and tour guides.

Best Time to Visit Rockledge

The best time to visit Rockledge is from March to May and from September to November when the weather is mild and the crowds are fewer. However, if you want to witness a rocket launch, plan your visit around the launch schedule.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rockledge

Here are some do’s and don’ts of Rockledge:

Do:

  • Do try new foods and drinks: Rockledge has a diverse food and drink scene, so be adventurous and try something new.
  • Do respect nature: Follow all safety rules and regulations when exploring natural sites and do not disturb the wildlife.
  • Do learn about the local culture: Read about the history, customs, and traditions of Rockledge and its people, and respect their beliefs and practices.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter: Keep the environment clean and dispose of trash properly.
  • Don’t haggle: Unless you are in a market or bazaar, haggling is not customary in Rockledge and can be considered impolite.
  • Don’t forget to tip: Tipping is customary in most places, so make sure to tip accordingly.

Safety and Health

Safety in Rockledge

Rockledge is generally a safe place to visit, but like any other city, there are some safety precautions that you should take:

  • Watch your belongings: Keep your valuables such as wallets and phones safe and beware of pickpockets in busy tourist areas.
  • Be careful when driving: Follow all traffic rules and regulations and be cautious when driving in unfamiliar areas.
  • Avoid dangerous areas: Stay away from neighborhoods or areas that are known for crime or violence.

Health in Rockledge

To stay healthy during your visit to Rockledge, here are some health tips:

  • Drink water: Florida’s weather can be hot and sunny, so make sure to stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Use sunscreen: Protect your skin from the sun’s harmful rays by wearing sunscreen with a high SPF.
  • Watch out for bugs: Florida is home to mosquitoes and other bugs that can transmit diseases, so use repellent and cover exposed skin when necessary.

Getting to Rockledge: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Rockledge

  • Orlando Melbourne International Airport (MLB): Located about 20 minutes south of Rockledge, MLB is the closest airport.
  • Orlando International Airport (MCO): Located about 45 minutes west of Rockledge, MCO is a major international airport with many flight options.

Bus Stations Near Rockledge

  • Greyhound: Located about 15 minutes north of Rockledge, Greyhound offers bus services to many cities in Florida and the USA.

Other Options

  • Train: Amtrak offers train services to the nearby cities of Cocoa and Melbourne.
  • Car rental: Renting a car is a convenient and flexible option for exploring Rockledge and nearby areas.

How to Get Around in Rockledge

The best way to get around Rockledge is by car or bike. There are several car rental agencies in the city, and many hotels offer bike rentals. There is also a public transportation system called Space Coast Area Transit that offers bus services to different parts of the city.

Where to Go Next from Rockledge: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you want to explore other destinations near Rockledge, here are some ideas:

  • Cocoa Beach: Visit the famous Cocoa Beach and enjoy surfing lessons, beach volleyball, and other water sports.
  • Orlando: Explore the theme parks, museums, and cultural attractions of the vibrant city of Orlando.
  • St. Augustine: Discover the historic charm, architecture, and museums of St. Augustine, the oldest city in the USA.

Conclusion

Rockledge is a beautiful and fascinating city that has a lot to offer to travelers who love nature, culture, and adventure. We hope this travel guide has helped you plan your visit to Rockledge and make the most of your trip. Happy travels!
