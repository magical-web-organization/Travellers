Introduction to Melbourne

Melbourne is a city located in Space Coast, in the state of Florida, United States of America. It is a charming small city known for its beautiful beaches, natural parks, and rich cultural heritage that dates back to the early 20th century. Melbourne has something for everyone, from art museums to outdoor adventures, making it a perfect destination for travelers in search of fun and relaxation.

List of Places to Visit in Melbourne

Here is a list of the top places to visit when in Melbourne:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Brevard Museum of History and Natural Science: A museum that showcases the history of Brevard County and its natural wonders.
  • Foosaner Art Museum: A museum that features art exhibitions and educational programs.
  • Melbourne Beach Library: A historic library that also serves as a museum dedicated to the town’s history.

Natural Attractions of Melbourne: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Sebastian Inlet State Park: A state park that offers opportunities for fishing, swimming, surfing, and camping.
  • Indian River Lagoon: A scenic lagoon that is home to a variety of wildlife, including dolphins and manatees.
  • Wickham Park: A large park that offers picnic areas and hiking trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Melbourne

Melbourne has several hiking and biking routes that offer stunning views of the natural landscapes. Some of the popular trails include:

  • Turkey Creek Sanctuary: A 130-acre nature preserve that is ideal for hiking, biking, and birdwatching.
  • The North Tropical Trail: A scenic route that runs along the Indian River Lagoon and offers views of historic estates and wildlife.
  • The Eau Gallie River: A scenic river that offers opportunities for kayaking and paddleboarding.

Best Things to Do in Melbourne: Activities and Tours

Melbourne has several activities and tours that cater to all interests and ages. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Go Fishing: Melbourne is known for its fishing opportunities, and several charters offer deep-sea fishing trips.
  • Visit the Brevard Zoo: A zoo that features over 900 animals from around the world and offers educational programs.
  • Take an Eco-Tour: Several tour operators offer eco-tours to explore the natural beauty of Melbourne.

Week-Long Itinerary for Melbourne

Here is a sample week-long itinerary for exploring Melbourne:

Day Activity
1 Visit the Foosaner Art Museum and explore downtown Melbourne
2 Spend the day at Sebastian Inlet State Park
3 Take an eco-tour of the Indian River Lagoon
4 Visit Brevard Zoo and have lunch at one of the nearby restaurants
5 Spend the day hiking at Turkey Creek Sanctuary
6 Go kayaking on the Eau Gallie River
7 Relax at Melbourne Beach and enjoy the sunset

Food and Drink Scene of Melbourne

Melbourne has a vibrant food and drink scene that reflects the city’s diverse cultures and influences. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Stone Crab Claws: A local delicacy that is served in most seafood restaurants.
  • Key Lime Pie: A dessert made with fresh Key Limes and a graham cracker crust.

Local Beverages

  • Florida Orange Juice: Known for its sweet and tangy flavor, Florida orange juice is a must-try when in Melbourne.
  • Space Coast Brews: Melbourne has several microbreweries that craft unique brews inspired by the space industry.

Where to Stay in Melbourne: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Melbourne has several options for accommodations, ranging from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels and guesthouses. Some of the best places to stay in Melbourne include:

  • Hilton Melbourne Beach Oceanfront: A luxury hotel located on the beachfront.
  • Radisson Suite Hotel Oceanfront: A mid-range hotel that offers ocean views and easy access to the beach.
  • Melbourne All Suites Inn: A budget-friendly guesthouse with convenient access to downtown and the beaches.

What to Buy in Melbourne: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Melbourne offers several unique souvenirs and local products that make for great gifts or keepsakes. Some of the must-buy items include:

  • Space-Themed Merchandise: As the home of the Kennedy Space Center, Melbourne has several stores that sell space-themed gifts and souvenirs.
  • Local Honey: Made from the nectar of local Florida orange blossoms, Brevard County honey is a delicious and healthy souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Melbourne

Melbourne has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its music, art, and festivals. Some of the notable customs and traditions include:

  • Space Industry Celebrations: Melbourne hosts several festivals and events to celebrate the history and achievements of the space industry.
  • Art Walks and Festivals: Downtown Melbourne has several art galleries and hosts monthly art walks.

Best Time to Visit Melbourne

The best time to visit Melbourne is from December to April when the weather is mild, and the beaches and outdoor attractions are open. The summer months of June to August can be hot and rainy, while the fall and winter months are mild and dry.

Do’s and Don’ts of Melbourne

Do:

  • Explore the Outdoors: Melbourne has several natural parks, beaches, and outdoor attractions that are a must-see.
  • Try Local Cuisine: Melbourne has a vibrant food scene that is influenced by its multicultural heritage.

Don’t:

  • Litter: Keep Melbourne’s beaches and parks clean by disposing of trash properly.
  • Ignore Safety Warnings: Follow all safety warnings at the beach and during outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Melbourne

Melbourne is a safe city for tourists, but it is always recommended to take precautions when traveling. Some safety tips to keep in mind include:

  • Stay in Well-Lit Areas: Avoid walking alone at night in dark or unfamiliar areas.
  • Lock Your Car and Hotel Room: Keep all valuables locked in your car or hotel room when not in use.

Health in Melbourne

Melbourne has several healthcare facilities and pharmacies that are easily accessible. It is recommended to carry a medical insurance card and any necessary medications when visiting.

Getting to Melbourne: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Melbourne

Melbourne International Airport (MLB) is the nearest airport to Melbourne, located about 3 miles from the downtown area. Alternatively, Orlando International Airport (MCO) is located about 70 miles from Melbourne.

Bus Stations Near Melbourne

The Greyhound Bus Station is located in downtown Melbourne and offers several daily routes to and from other cities in Florida and the United States.

Other Options

Several rental car companies operate in Melbourne, and there is also a local bus system that serves the city and surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Melbourne

Melbourne is easy to navigate, and there are several options for getting around the city, including:

  • Car Rental: Several rental car companies operate in Melbourne, and renting a car is a convenient option for exploring the city and surrounding areas.
  • Bike Rentals: Several bike rental companies offer bike rentals for exploring the beaches and parks.
  • Taxi and Ride-Sharing: Taxi and ride-sharing services are also available in Melbourne.

Where to Go Next from Melbourne: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Melbourne is located in an ideal location for nearby excursions and day trips. Some nearby destinations to consider include:

  • Kennedy Space Center: Located about an hour away from Melbourne, the Kennedy Space Center offers tours and exhibits on the history and future of space exploration.
  • Orlando: Located about an hour and a half from Melbourne, Orlando is home to several theme parks, including Walt Disney World and Universal Studios.
  • St. Augustine: Located about two hours away from Melbourne, St. Augustine is a historic city that is known for its beautiful beaches, Spanish colonial architecture, and museums.

Conclusion

Melbourne is a hidden gem in Florida, with beautiful beaches, natural parks, and a rich cultural heritage. Whether you are seeking relaxation or outdoor adventures, Melbourne has something to offer everyone. With this comprehensive travel guide, you are sure to have an enjoyable and memorable trip to Melbourne.
