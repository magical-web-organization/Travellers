Introduction to Melbourne Beach

Melbourne Beach is a small, charming town located on the Space Coast of Florida in the United States. It is renowned for its beautiful beaches, abundant wildlife, and excellent outdoor recreational opportunities. Whether you are an adventure seeker, a nature lover, or a history buff, Melbourne Beach offers something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Melbourne Beach

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Melbourne Beach has a rich history and a vibrant cultural scene. Here are some must-visit museums and historical places to explore in the area:

  • The Sebastian Inlet State Park: Situated at the southern end of Brevard County, this park offers visitors a glimpse of the region’s natural beauty and history. You can explore the park’s hiking trails, go fishing or surfing, or simply relax on the beaches.
  • The Brevard Museum of History and Natural Science: The museum showcases artifacts and exhibits that offer insights into the history and natural wonders of Melbourne Beach, Florida, and the entire Space Coast area.
  • The Liberty Bell Memorial Museum: Dedicated to the memory of the brave men and women who served the United States military, this museum features interactive exhibits and artifacts related to the armed forces.

Natural Attractions of Melbourne Beach: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Melbourne Beach is a paradise for nature lovers. Here are some natural attractions that you don’t want to miss:

  • The Archie Carr National Wildlife Refuge: This 20-mile stretch of protected coastline is home to nesting turtles, shorebirds, and other sea creatures. Visitors can explore the area on guided tours or hike along the beach on their own.
  • The Pelican Island National Wildlife Refuge: Established in 1903, Pelican Island was the first National Wildlife Refuge in the United States. It offers visitors unparalleled opportunities to observe a variety of birds, reptiles, and marine life in their native habitats.
  • The Florida Tech Botanical Garden: This 15-acre garden features a diverse collection of plants and trees from around the world, as well as eco-parks, nature trails, and a native plant nursery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Melbourne Beach

Melbourne Beach has numerous scenic hiking/biking routes that offer breathtaking views of the area. Here are some of the best ones to explore:

  • The Turkey Creek Sanctuary: This 130-acre nature preserve has a network of hiking trails that lead through hardwood forests, pine flatwoods, and salt marshes.
  • The Maritime Hammock Sanctuary: This 150-acre park has over three miles of hiking trails that meander through a lush tropical forest and beautiful mangrove swamps.
  • The Jungle Trail: This historic road runs for seven miles along the Indian River Lagoon and offers visitors stunning views of the river, as well as the opportunity to spot manatees, dolphins, and other marine life.

Best Things to Do in Melbourne Beach: Activities and Tours

Melbourne Beach offers a wide variety of activities and tours for visitors of all ages. Here are some must-try experiences:

  • Surfing: Melbourne Beach is known for its excellent surf breaks, and there are several surf shops in town that offer rentals and lessons.
  • Deep-Sea Fishing: Charter a boat and spend the day casting for fish like mahi-mahi, kingfish, and grouper in the Atlantic Ocean.
  • Kayaking and Paddleboarding: The Indian River Lagoon is a perfect place for kayaking or paddleboarding. There are several rental companies in town that offer guided tours or rentals.

Food and Drink Scene of Melbourne Beach

Melbourne Beach has a vibrant culinary scene that reflects the town’s diverse cultural influences. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Seafood: Fresh seafood dishes are popular in Melbourne Beach, with fresh fish, shrimp, and oysters being the most commonly served.
  • Key lime pie: This delicious dessert is a Florida staple, and Melbourne has some of the best key lime pies in the state.
  • BBQ: Southern style BBQ is popular in Melbourne Beach, with pulled pork and smoked brisket being some of the favorites.

Local Beverages

  • Florida Orange Juice: Florida is known for its delicious and sweet oranges, and the fresh juice is a must-try when in Melbourne Beach.
  • Craft Beer: The Space Coast is home to many excellent craft breweries, and the Melbourne area has some of the best.

Where to Stay in Melbourne Beach: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Melbourne Beach has a wide variety of accommodation options to suit every budget and preference. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • Hilton Melbourne Beach Oceanfront: This beachfront hotel offers stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean, as well as an outdoor pool, a fitness center, and a restaurant.
  • Sea View Inn: This cozy and affordable guesthouse is located just a short walk from the beach and offers comfortable rooms and a friendly staff.
  • Coconut Cove Suites: This boutique hotel is located in the center of Melbourne Beach and offers spacious rooms, a beautiful pool, and a prime location close to restaurants and shopping.

What to Buy in Melbourne Beach: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Melbourne Beach is known for its unique and locally made products. Here are some souvenirs and local products to bring home with you:

  • Sea Glass Jewelry: Sea glass jewelry, made by local artists, is a popular souvenir in Melbourne Beach.
  • Local Honey: Florida is famous for its honey, and you can find many local varieties in Melbourne Beach.
  • Handmade Soaps and Bath Products: There are several stores in Melbourne Beach that sell locally made soaps and bath products that are perfect for gifts or personal use.

Local Culture and Customs of Melbourne Beach

Melbourne Beach has a laid-back and friendly culture that reflects the town’s beautiful natural surroundings. Here are a few customs to keep in mind:

  • Respect Wildlife: Melbourne Beach is home to many protected species of wildlife, and visitors should respect their natural habitats and refrain from disturbing them.
  • Beach Etiquette: Visitors should clean up their trash and avoid disturbing other beachgoers.
  • Southern Hospitality: Melbourne Beach locals are known for their friendly and welcoming attitude, and visitors should reciprocate this by being polite and respectful.

Best Time to Visit Melbourne Beach

Melbourne Beach has a temperate climate year-round, but the best time to visit is from December to May when the weather is mild and dry. This is also the prime time for surfing, deep-sea fishing, and other outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Melbourne Beach

Do:

  • Do try some fresh seafood and local craft beer.
  • Do explore the natural beauty of Melbourne Beach, including the wildlife refuges and hiking trails.
  • Do be respectful of the local culture and customs.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural habitats of the wildlife.
  • Don’t forget to protect yourself from the sun, as it can be very strong in Florida.
  • Don’t forget to tip your servers, as it’s customary in the United States.

Safety and Health

Safety in Melbourne Beach

Melbourne Beach is a very safe destination, with a low crime rate. However, visitors should still take precautions to ensure their safety, such as keeping valuables out of sight and locking their vehicles.

Health in Melbourne Beach

The quality of medical care in Melbourne Beach is excellent, with several hospitals and clinics in the area. Visitors should also take precautions against the sun and heat, such as wearing sunscreen and staying hydrated.

Getting to Melbourne Beach: Transportation Options and Logistics

Melbourne Beach is easily accessible by plane, bus, or car. Here are some transportation options and logistics to keep in mind:

Airports Near Melbourne Beach

  • Orlando International Airport (MCO): Located 65 miles northwest of Melbourne Beach, this is the closest major airport.
  • Melbourne International Airport (MLB): Located just 8 miles from Melbourne Beach, this airport offers several daily flights to major U.S. cities.

Bus Stations Near Melbourne Beach

  • The Greyhound Bus Station: Located in the center of Melbourne, this station provides daily service to several major U.S. cities.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Car rentals are available from several major rental companies at both airports.
  • Taxi and ride-sharing services are also readily available in the area.

How to Get Around in Melbourne Beach

Melbourne Beach is a small town, and the best way to get around is by car. However, there are also several bike rental companies in the area that allow visitors to explore the town and its surroundings on two wheels.

Where to Go Next from Melbourne Beach: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Melbourne Beach is located in the heart of the Space Coast, and there are several nearby destinations and excursions that visitors can explore. Here are some popular ones:

  • The Kennedy Space Center: This world-famous center is located just a short drive from Melbourne Beach and offers visitors insights into the history and future of space exploration.
  • Orlando: This city, located just a one-hour drive from Melbourne Beach, is home to several famous theme parks and other attractions.
  • Palm Beach: This exclusive coastal town is located just a three-hour drive south of Melbourne Beach and is known for its luxury resorts and high-end shopping.

Conclusion

Melbourne Beach is a hidden gem in Florida, offering visitors a unique blend of natural beauty, outdoor recreation, and culture. So pack your bags, and get ready to explore this charming town and its surrounds.
