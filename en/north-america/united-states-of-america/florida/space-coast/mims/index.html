Introduction to Mims

Located in the heart of Space Coast, in Florida, Mims is an ideal destination for travelers seeking outdoor adventures and scenic views. It is a small town with a rich history and culture, making it a perfect place for those who love to explore and learn about new places. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and local culture and customs of Mims, Florida.

List of Places to Visit in Mims

Whether you are traveling with friends or family, Mims has something to offer to everyone. From culturally important attractions to natural wonders, here are some of the top places to visit in Mims.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you are interested in history and culture, there are several museums and historic sites to explore in Mims. Some of the must-see places include:

  • Parrish Grove Heritage Museum: This museum showcases the history and culture of North Brevard County, including Mims. It features exhibits that date back to the 1800s and provides an insight into the town’s rich history.

  • Valiant Air Command Warbird Museum: This museum features a collection of aircraft used in different wars throughout history, including World War I and II. It is a must-visit for aviation enthusiasts.

  • Kennedy Space Center: Located just a few miles away from Mims, the Kennedy Space Center is a popular attraction that showcases the history and future of space exploration. Visitors can take a bus tour of the facility, view rocket launch sites, and visit the Astronaut Hall of Fame.

Natural Attractions of Mims: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For nature lovers, Mims has plenty of outdoor activities and scenic views to offer. Here are some of the top natural attractions in Mims:

  • Merritt Island National Wildlife Refuge: This refuge is home to over 500 species of wildlife, including manatees, alligators, and various bird species. Visitors can hike, bike, or drive through the refuge to view the wildlife and enjoy the scenery.

  • Indian River Lagoon: The Indian River Lagoon is the perfect place for fishing, kayaking, and paddleboarding. It is a popular spot for bird watching, and visitors can spot different bird species, including bald eagles and ospreys.

  • Titusville Playhouse: This performing arts center features a variety of shows and concerts throughout the year and is a must-visit for performing arts enthusiasts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mims

Mims has several hiking and biking trails that offer scenic views and outdoor adventures. Here are some of the top routes to explore:

  • Black Point Wildlife Drive: This five-mile drive takes visitors through Merritt Island National Wildlife Refuge, where they can view different wildlife and enjoy the scenic views.

  • Cape Canaveral Seashore Trail: This four-mile trail takes visitors along the Atlantic coast, where they can view different species of birds and wildlife and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

Best Things to Do in Mims: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking, biking, and visiting museums and historic places, Mims has several other activities and tours to offer. Here are some of the top things to do:

  • Rocket Launch Viewing: Visitors can view rocket launches at Kennedy Space Center or Cape Canaveral. It is a unique experience that everyone should try at least once.

  • Airboat Tours: Adventure seekers can enjoy airboat tours through the swamps and marshes of Mims, providing an opportunity to view different wildlife and nature.

Food and Drink Scene of Mims

Mims has several food and drink options to explore, ranging from local cuisine to international dishes. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Seafood: Being situated near the coast, Mims has several seafood restaurants that serve fresh and delicious seafood dishes.

  • Barbecue: Mims is known for its barbecue, with several restaurants offering mouthwatering barbecue dishes.

  • Gator Tail: Adventurous foodies can try gator tail dishes at local restaurants.

Local Beverages

  • Florida Orange Juice: Florida is known for its orange juice, and Mims is no exception. Visitors can try fresh and delicious orange juice at local restaurants.

  • Space Coast Brew: Craft beer enthusiasts can try local brews at different breweries and bars in Mims.

Where to Stay in Mims: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Mims has several accommodation options to choose from, ranging from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels. Here are some top options:

  • Royal Mansions Resort: A beachfront resort that offers luxury and comfort for travelers.

  • Cape Crossing Resort & Marina: A waterfront resort that offers boat rides and other outdoor activities.

  • Days Inn by Wyndham: A budget-friendly hotel located near the Kennedy Space Center.

What to Buy in Mims: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When traveling to Mims, visitors can bring home several souvenirs and local products that represent the town’s culture and history. Some top options include:

  • Space Coast Memorabilia: Visitors can buy space-related souvenirs at the Kennedy Space Center and other local shops.

  • Handcrafted Jewelry and Artwork: Several local artists sell handcrafted jewelry and artwork that highlights Mims’ culture and history.

Local Culture and Customs of Mims

Mims has a rich culture and history that visitors can explore and learn about during their stay. The town is known for its space-related history and culture, with several attractions dedicated to space exploration. Mims also has a vibrant arts and cultural scene, with several art galleries and performing arts centers.

Best Time to Visit Mims

The best time to visit Mims is between November and April. The weather is mild during this time, making it ideal for outdoor activities and sightseeing. However, visitors should avoid the peak tourist season during the summer months, as the weather can be hot and humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mims

Do:

  • Do explore the natural wonders of Mims, including its wildlife and scenic views.

  • Do visit the Kennedy Space Center and other historic sites to learn about the town’s rich history and culture.

  • Do try local cuisine and beverages, including seafood, barbecue, and Florida orange juice.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or harm wildlife during your visit.

  • Don’t visit during the peak tourist season if you want to avoid crowds and hot weather.

Safety and Health

Safety in Mims

Mims is relatively safe for visitors, and tourists can enjoy a hassle-free trip. However, visitors should be cautious when hiking or biking through natural trails and bring appropriate gear for outdoor activities.

Health in Mims

Visitors should remain hydrated during their trip, especially during the summer months, to avoid heat-related illnesses. Visitors should also protect themselves from mosquito-borne diseases by wearing appropriate clothing and using insect repellent.

How to Get Around in Mims

Mims is best explored on foot or by car. Visitors can also rent a bike to explore the natural trails and scenic views.

Where to Go Next from Mims: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Mims is located near several popular destinations, making it an ideal base to explore the surrounding areas. Some top places to visit include:

  • Cocoa Beach: A popular beach destination located about 30 minutes from Mims.

  • Port Canaveral: A cruise ship terminal that offers various activities and excursions.

Conclusion

Mims is a charming town that offers an authentic Florida experience. Whether you are interested in history, culture, or outdoor adventures, Mims has something for everyone. This comprehensive travel guide will help you plan your trip and make the most of your visit to Mims.
