Cantonment is a beautiful small town nestled in the Florida Panhandle, surrounded by lush green forests and stunning natural attractions. It’s a hidden gem that’s worth exploring for anyone looking to escape the bustling city life and experience peace and tranquility.

Introduction to Cantonment

Cantonment, Florida, is a quiet and peaceful town located in Escambia County in the westernmost part of Florida. This small town is known for its serene natural beauty, crystal-clear lakes, unspoiled beaches, and a fascinating history. Cantonment is a perfect destination for anyone looking to escape the noise and chaos of big cities and take a break in a tranquil environment.

List of Places to Visit in Cantonment

Cantonment has several attractions that are worth exploring during your visit. We’ve divided them into two categories: culturally important attractions and natural attractions.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Pensacola Lighthouse and Museum - A historic lighthouse that offers a panoramic view of the Gulf of Mexico.
  2. Fort Barrancas - A historic fort built by the Spanish in 1797 and later used by the US Army.
  3. National Naval Aviation Museum - A museum that displays the history of naval aviation in the United States.
  4. Pensacola Museum of Art - An art museum that showcases the works of local and international artists.

Natural Attractions of Cantonment: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Perdido Key State Park - A pristine beach with crystal-clear waters that is perfect for swimming, fishing, and sunbathing.
  2. Blackwater River State Park - A park with a river that offers canoeing, kayaking, and fishing opportunities.
  3. Big Lagoon State Park - A park that has a lake and several hiking trails that offer scenic views of the surrounding forests.
  4. Gulf Islands National Seashore - A national park that has several beaches, hiking trails, and campgrounds that offer scenic views of the Gulf of Mexico.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Cantonment

Cantonment has several hiking and biking routes that offer scenic views of the surrounding forests, lakes, and rivers. Here are some of the most popular ones:

  1. UWF Nature Trails - A 2.8-mile trail that offers scenic views of the UWF campus and the surrounding forests.
  2. Florida Trail - A trail that stretches over 1,300 miles and offers scenic views of the Florida Panhandle region.
  3. Blackwater Heritage Trail - A 9.5-mile trail that runs through the Blackwater River State Park and offers views of the river.
  4. Tarklin Bayou Preserve State Park - A park with a 4.5-mile trail that offers views of the Tarkiln Bayou and a wide variety of wildlife.

Best Things to Do in Cantonment: Activities and Tours

Cantonment offers a wide variety of activities and tours that provide a unique and memorable experience for tourists. Here are some of the most popular ones:

  1. Dolphin-Watching Tours - A tour that takes you on a boat ride to see dolphins in their natural habitat.
  2. Zipline Adventures - A zipline course that offers scenic views of the forests and lakes of the area.
  3. Deep-Sea Fishing Charters - A tour that takes you on a fishing trip to catch a wide variety of fish in the Gulf of Mexico.
  4. Ghost Tour of Pensacola - A tour that takes you on a journey through the haunted streets of Pensacola, with stops at historic buildings with paranormal activity.

Food and Drink Scene of Cantonment

Cantonment has a diverse food and drink scene that offers a mix of local and international cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Fried Catfish - A popular dish that consists of crispy fried catfish and a side of hushpuppies.
  2. Grits & Grillades - A breakfast dish that consists of grilled beef with a tangy tomato-based sauce served over creamy grits.
  3. Seafood Boil - A southern classic that features a variety of boiled seafood such as shrimp, crab, and crawfish.
  4. Dreamland BBQ - A local barbecue restaurant that serves some of the best ribs in the town.

Local Beverages

  1. Sweet Tea - A classic southern beverage that is made with tea and sugar and served over ice.
  2. Bayou Beer - A locally brewed beer that is available in several restaurants and bars in the town.
  3. Bushwacker - A popular cocktail that consists of vanilla ice cream, dark rum, and chocolate syrup.

Local Culture and Customs of Cantonment

Cantonment is a small town with a friendly and welcoming community. The locals are proud of their heritage and culture and are happy to share it with tourists. Best Time to Visit Cantonment

The best time to visit Cantonment is in the fall and winter months when the weather is cool and comfortable, and the crowds are fewer. The spring and summer months can be hot and humid and are popular with tourists, but it can be crowded at tourist attractions, and the prices can be high.
