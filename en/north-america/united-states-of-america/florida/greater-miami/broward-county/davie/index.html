Introduction to Davie

Located in Broward County, Greater Miami, in Florida, Davie is a picturesque town that boasts of a rich natural landscape, fascinating local culture, and a vibrant food scene. With a population of around 106,000 residents, this charming community offers an excellent blend of urban convenience and small-town charm. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, Davie has something to offer everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Davie

If you’re looking for things to do in Davie, check out our list of top attractions to visit during your trip:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Old Davie School Historical Museum - Located in the heart of the town, the Old Davie School is an iconic landmark that has been turned into a museum. Here, you can learn about the town’s history and culture through interactive exhibits, programs, and events.

  2. Young at Art Museum - Featuring rotating exhibitions, this museum offers hands-on experiences for kids and families, making it an excellent destination for a family-friendly afternoon.

Natural Attractions of Davie: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Flamingo Gardens - Home to over 3000 species of plants and animals, Flamingo Gardens is a popular attraction for nature lovers. Visitors can explore the botanical gardens, see exotic birds, and take a tram ride through the wildlife sanctuary.

  2. Robbins Preserve - This beautiful nature preserve has hiking trails that offer a chance to explore the natural beauty of Davie. The preserve houses native wildlife and plants and is a perfect spot for birdwatching, hiking, and biking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Davie

There are several hiking and biking trails in Davie for outdoor enthusiasts. Some of the popular routes include:

  • Pine Island Ridge Trail
  • Shenandoah Park Trail
  • Tree Tops Park Trail

Best Things to Do in Davie: Activities and Tours

  1. Horseback Riding - Davie is known for its equestrian facilities, and visitors can take horseback riding lessons at one of the many farms and ranches around the town.

  2. Airboat Tours - Take an airboat tour of the Everglades and explore the wetlands unique to South Florida.

Week-Long Itinerary for Davie

Day 1: Visit the Old Davie School Historical Museum and then take a leisurely stroll through Robbins Preserve.

Day 2: Explore Flamingo Gardens and see the exotic birds and plants.

Day 3: Go on a horseback riding adventure at one of the many farms in the area.

Day 4: Take an airboat tour through the Everglades.

Day 5: Visit the Young at Art Museum and enjoy the interactive exhibits.

Day 6: Take a bike ride through Shenandoah Park Trail or Tree Tops Park Trail.

Day 7: Enjoy a relaxing day at one of the many beaches in the area.

Food and Drink Scene of Davie

Davie has a diverse food scene with options that range from upscale restaurants to rustic diners. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Key lime pie - A classic Florida dessert that is a must-try when visiting Davie.

  2. Stone crab claws - Another local delicacy that is available in many seafood restaurants in the town.

Local Beverages

  1. Cuban coffee - A strong espresso-based coffee that is a favorite among locals.

  2. Orange juice - Florida produces some of the best oranges in the world, and the fresh orange juice is a must-try when in Davie.

What to Buy in Davie: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When in Davie, you can bring back some souvenirs that reflect the local culture. Some popular items to buy include:

  • Seminole art and crafts
  • Key lime pie

Local Culture and Customs of Davie

Davie has a rich cultural heritage shaped by its Native American and Hispanic roots. Visitors can experience the unique blend of cultures through the various art exhibits and festivals around the town.

Best Time to Visit Davie

The best time to visit Davie is between November and April when the temperatures are mild, and there are fewer chances of rain. The peak tourist season is between December and March.

Do’s and Don’ts of Davie

Do:

  • Do explore the natural beauty of Davie by visiting one of the many parks and nature reserves.

  • Do taste the local cuisine, including the Key lime pie and stone crab claws.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget to bring sunscreen and mosquito repellent when exploring the outdoors.

  • Don’t litter or disturb the wildlife in the nature reserves and parks.

Safety and Health

Safety in Davie

Davie is a safe place to visit, with low crime rates. However, visitors should take usual precautions like keeping an eye on their belongings and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Davie

Visitors should take usual precautions bout mosquitoes and the risk of mosquito-borne illnesses such as Zika, Chikungunya, and Dengue. Bring mosquito repellent and use it as directed.

Getting to Davie: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Davie

The nearest airport to Davie is Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport, which is approximately a 20-minute drive away. Miami International Airport is also nearby and is about a 45-minute drive from Davie.

Bus Stations Near Davie

Broward County Transit and Miami-Dade Transit operate several bus routes that connect Davie to other cities in the area. The Tri-Rail train also stops in Davie and provides access to Miami and Fort Lauderdale.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car for more flexibility in exploring the area.

How to Get Around in Davie

Davie has a well-connected network of roads and highways, making it easy for visitors to get around by car. Taxis and ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft are also available in the area.

Where to Go Next from Davie: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Miami (approx. 30-minute drive)
  • Fort Lauderdale (approx. 20-minute drive)
  • Hollywood Beach (approx. 30-minute drive)

Conclusion

Davie is a hidden gem in Florida that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and a vibrant food and drink scene. With so much to explore and experience, this town is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to have an authentic Florida experience.
