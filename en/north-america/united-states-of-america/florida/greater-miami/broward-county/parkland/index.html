Introduction to Parkland

Parkland is a charming city located in the heart of Broward County, Florida. Often referred to as the “Tree City,” Parkland boasts a lush landscape filled with parks, nature preserves, and scenic trails. With a population of just over 30,000 people, this tight-knit community offers a warm and welcoming atmosphere, making it the perfect getaway destination for families, couples, and solo travelers alike.

List of Places to Visit in Parkland

Here is a list of the top attractions to visit in Parkland:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Parkland Heritage Park: Discover the city’s rich history and cultural heritage at this historic site, featuring historic buildings, a butterfly garden, and a farmers market.
  • Sawgrass Nature Center and Wildlife Hospital: Explore the beauty of Parkland’s natural landscapes and wildlife at this educational and interactive nature center.

Natural Attractions of Parkland: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Pine Trails Park: Enjoy a picnic, walk, or jog in this beautiful park, featuring a playground, splash pad, and sports fields.
  • Loxahatchee Slough Natural Area: Go on a hiking or biking adventure at this nature reserve, offering scenic vistas of the Florida wilderness.
  • Everglades Wildlife Management Area: Take a walk on the wild side at this vast wilderness area, home to a diverse range of plant and wildlife species.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Parkland

Parkland offers an extensive network of hiking and biking trails, catering to outdoor enthusiasts of all levels. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • Pine Trails Park Loop: A scenic 2.2-mile loop trail around Pine Trails Park, suitable for all skill levels.
  • Cypress Preserve Loop: A moderate 4.3-mile trail through the scenic Cypress Preserve, featuring stunning views of the surrounding wetlands.

Best Things to Do in Parkland: Activities and Tours

From kayaking and paddleboarding to golfing and fishing, Parkland offers a wide range of fun activities for travelers to enjoy. Here are some must-try experiences:

  • Kayaking at the Hillsboro Canal: Paddle your way through the scenic Hillsboro Canal, and enjoy the beautiful sights and sounds of Parkland’s natural landscapes.
  • Parkland Golf & Country Club: Hit the links at this premier golf course, featuring 18 holes of world-class golf and stunning scenic views.

Week-Long Itinerary for Parkland

Here is a suggested week-long itinerary for travelers to get the most out of their visit to Parkland:

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit Parkland Heritage Park and Sawgrass Nature Center
Day 2 Explore Pine Trails Park and go on the Pine Trails Park Loop
Day 3 Go on a hiking adventure at Loxahatchee Slough Natural Area
Day 4 Enjoy a day of kayaking at the Hillsboro Canal
Day 5 Play a round of golf at Parkland Golf & Country Club
Day 6 Take a day trip to nearby attractions, such as Fort Lauderdale and Miami
Day 7 Relax and unwind at Everglades Wildlife Management Area

Food and Drink Scene of Parkland

Parkland offers a diverse dining scene, with restaurants serving up a range of international dishes, seafood specialties, and local favorites. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Grouper sandwich: A Florida specialty, featuring fresh grouper fillets served on a toasted bun with lettuce, tomato, and tartar sauce.
  • Key lime pie: A classic dessert made with tart key limes, graham cracker crust, and whipped cream.

Local Beverages

  • Orange juice: Florida is known for its fresh, citrusy orange juice, which is a must-try for visitors to Parkland.

Where to Stay in Parkland: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Parkland offers a range of accommodation options, catering to all types of travelers and budgets. Here are some recommended hotels, hostels, and guesthouses:

  • Marriott Coral Springs Hotel: A luxurious hotel offering spacious rooms, an outdoor pool, and a fitness center.
  • Getaways at Eagle Trace Golf Club: A budget-friendly option featuring cozy guestrooms, an outdoor pool, and access to a top-rated golf course.

What to Buy in Parkland: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Parkland offers a range of unique souvenirs and local products for visitors to bring home, such as key lime pie, orange marmalade, and postcards featuring local landmarks.

Local Culture and Customs of Parkland

Parkland is known for its friendly and welcoming atmosphere, with locals embracing a relaxed and laid-back lifestyle. The city is home to a diverse range of cultures and ethnicities, making it a melting pot of food, music, and art.

Best Time to Visit Parkland

The best time to visit Parkland is during the winter months, from December to March, when the weather is mild and dry, and the humidity is low. Spring and fall are also pleasant times to visit, with comfortable temperatures and fewer crowds than peak summer months.

Do’s and Don’ts of Parkland

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Embrace the city’s natural beauty and explore the scenic hiking and biking trails.
  • Try the local cuisine and sample the fresh orange juice and key lime pie.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural habitats and wildlife in the area.
  • Disrupt the peace and quiet of the local neighborhoods and parks.
  • Ignore the local traffic and parking regulations.

Safety and Health

Safety in Parkland

Parkland is considered a safe city, with low crime rates and a strong sense of community. However, travelers should still take basic precautions to ensure their safety, such as being aware of their surroundings and avoiding unlit areas at night.

Health in Parkland

Parkland offers a range of quality healthcare facilities and services, catering to both residents and visitors. Travelers should ensure they have comprehensive travel insurance before visiting, to cover any unexpected illnesses or accidents.

Getting to Parkland: Transportation Options and Logistics

Parkland is easily accessible by car, with several major highways and interstates passing through the city. Here are some transportation options for travelers:

Airports Near Parkland

  • Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport: Located 26 miles south of Parkland, this airport offers daily flights to major U.S. cities and international destinations.
  • Miami International Airport: Located 39 miles south of Parkland, this airport offers daily flights to domestic and international destinations.

Bus Stations Near Parkland

  • Greyhound Bus Lines: Located in Fort Lauderdale, 16 miles south of Parkland, this bus station offers daily bus services to major U.S. cities.

Other Options

  • Car rental: There are several major car rental companies located near Parkland, offering a convenient way to explore the city and its surroundings.
  • Taxi and rideshare: Uber and Lyft are widely available in the area, offering a cost-effective and convenient way to get around.

How to Get Around in Parkland

Parkland is easily navigable by car, and there are plenty of public parking options available. Travelers can also explore the city on foot, or by biking along the extensive network of trails and bike lanes.

Where to Go Next from Parkland: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Parkland is located in the heart of South Florida, offering easy access to a range of nearby destinations and excursions. Here are some recommended places to visit:

  • Fort Lauderdale: Known as the “Venice of America,” Fort Lauderdale offers stunning beaches, waterways, and cultural attractions, just a short drive from Parkland.
  • Miami: A vibrant and cosmopolitan city, Miami offers a range of cultural landmarks, world-class dining, and nightlife, only an hour’s drive from Parkland.
  • Everglades National Park: Home to a diverse range of wildlife and ecosystems, the Everglades is a must-see destination for nature lovers, located just west of Parkland.

Conclusion

Parkland is a hidden gem in the heart of South Florida, offering a diverse range of cultural landmarks, natural attractions, and outdoor adventures. From hiking and kayaking to golfing and dining, there is something for everyone in this charming city. Plan your trip today and discover the beauty of Parkland for yourself!
