Your Comprehensive Travel Guide to Hollywood, Florida

Located in Broward County in Greater Miami, Florida, United States of America, North America, Hollywood is a vibrant coastal city that offers visitors a diverse range of attractions. From outdoor adventures to cultural experiences, the city has something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you through the must-visit places, things to do, and local customs to help you plan the perfect trip to Hollywood, Florida.

Introduction to Hollywood

Hollywood is a city situated on the east coast of Florida, along the beaches of the Atlantic Ocean. The city has a rich history and a strong cultural identity that is reflected in its numerous attractions. Hollywood has more than 60 parks, seven golf courses, and three miles of beaches, making it a popular destination for tourists looking to experience the outdoors and natural beauty.

List of Places to Visit in Hollywood

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Art and Culture Center of Hollywood: This art museum features contemporary art exhibitions, performances, and education programs.

  • Hollywood Historical Society Museum: Explore the history of Hollywood through the artifacts, documents, and photographs on display at this museum.

  • Anne Kolb Nature Center: Discover the natural history of South Florida through interactive exhibits, nature trails, and boat tours.

Natural Attractions of Hollywood: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hollywood Beach: Enjoy the sun, sand, and warm waters of the Atlantic Ocean at this beautiful beach.

  • Hollywood Boardwalk: Take a stroll along the boardwalk, which is lined with restaurants, shops, and entertainment venues.

  • John U. Lloyd Beach State Park: Go fishing, camping, or kayaking at this state park, which also offers stunning views of the Atlantic Ocean.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hollywood

  • Hollywood Beach Broadwalk Trail: Rent a bike and ride along this paved trail that offers stunning ocean views.

  • Anne Kolb Nature Center Trail: Hike through this scenic trail that takes you through mangroves, marshes, and the Intracoastal Waterway.

Best Things to Do in Hollywood: Activities and Tours

  • Hollywood Trolley: Take a ride on the trolley to explore the city’s attractions, museums, and beaches.

  • Everglades Holiday Park: take an airboat tour through the Everglades to see alligators, snakes, and other wildlife.

  • Seminole Hard Rock Hotel and Casino: Experience the thrill of gambling and enjoy live entertainment at this famous casino.

Week-Long Itinerary for Hollywood

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit Hollywood Beach and take a stroll along the Hollywood Boardwalk.
Day 2 Explore the Art and Culture Center of Hollywood.
Day 3 Enjoy a day at John U. Lloyd Beach State Park.
Day 4 Go shopping and try local cuisine at Downtown Hollywood.
Day 5 Take a trolley tour of the city and visit the Hollywood Historical Society Museum.
Day 6 Go on an airboat tour of the Everglades from Everglades Holiday Park.
Day 7 Relax at the Seminole Hard Rock Hotel and Casino.

Food and Drink Scene of Hollywood

Must-Try Dishes

  • Ceviche at Savannah’s: Try the delicious ceviche made from the freshest seafood at this restaurant.

  • The Diplomat Prime: Indulge in the finest steaks and seafood at this upscale restaurant.

  • Greek Joint: Enjoy traditional Greek dishes such as dolmades, tzatziki, and souvlaki at this eatery.

Local Beverages

  • Key Lime Pie Martini: Try this unique cocktail made with key lime juice, coconut rum, and whipped cream.

  • Cuban Coffee: Sip on this strong, sweet coffee that’s a staple drink in Miami.

Where to Stay in Hollywood: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Diplomat Beach Resort Hollywood: This high-end resort offers luxurious rooms and suites, a private beach, and numerous dining and entertainment options.

  • Hollywood Beach Marriott: Overlooking the ocean, this hotel boasts a heated pool, spa, and a variety of restaurants and bars.

  • Big City Hostel: This budget-friendly hostel is located in the heart of Hollywood and offers comfortable accommodation and a shared kitchen.

What to Buy in Hollywood: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Art Deco Memorabilia: Buy souvenirs that capture the unique Art Deco style that Hollywood is famous for.

  • Citrus Products: Bring home locally-made orange juice, marmalade, or key lime pie to savor the taste of Florida.

Local Culture and Customs of Hollywood

  • Beach Etiquette: When visiting the beach, be sure to respect the local customs and avoid littering or disturbing nesting sea turtles.

  • Respect for Diversity: Hollywood has a rich cultural diversity, and visitors should be respectful of the different customs, languages, and beliefs of its residents and visitors.

Best Time to Visit Hollywood

The best time to visit Hollywood is between March and May or September and November, when temperatures are mild, crowds are smaller, and hotel rates are affordable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hollywood

Do:

  • Enjoy the Outdoors: Take advantage of Hollywood’s numerous parks, beaches, and natural attractions.

  • Try Local Cuisine: Indulge in the delicious seafood and traditional Cuban and Latin American dishes that are popular in Hollywood.

Don’t:

  • Litter: Respect the local environment and avoid littering or disturbing natural habitats.

  • Expect a Quiet Beach: Some of the beaches in Hollywood can get crowded, especially during peak tourist seasons.

Safety and Health

Safety in Hollywood

Hollywood is a relatively safe city, but visitors should be cautious when walking alone at night or leaving valuables unattended in their cars.

Health in Hollywood

Visitors should take precautions against the Florida heat and sun by wearing sunscreen, drinking plenty of water, and seeking shade during the hottest parts of the day.

Getting to Hollywood: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Hollywood

  • Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport: Located just 7 miles from Hollywood, this airport offers domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Hollywood

  • Broward County Transit: This public transportation system offers numerous bus routes that connect Hollywood to surrounding cities and towns.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Visitors can rent cars at the airport or in Downtown Hollywood to explore the city and surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Hollywood

  • Trolley: Hollywood offers a free trolley service that runs along Hollywood Beach and Downtown Hollywood.

  • Bus: Broward County Transit provides bus services to Hollywood and surrounding areas.

  • Rideshare and Taxis: Visitors can also use rideshare and taxi services to get around the city.

Where to Go Next from Hollywood: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Miami: Explore the vibrant cultural scene, nightlife, and beaches of Miami, located just 30 minutes south of Hollywood.

  • Ft. Lauderdale: Visit Ft. Lauderdale to experience its rich cultural heritage and scenic waterways.

Conclusion

Hollywood, Florida, is a beautiful coastal city that offers visitors a diverse range of attractions, natural scenery, and cultural experiences. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can plan the perfect trip to explore this vibrant city and its surroundings. Don’t forget to pack sunscreen and an appetite for the delicious local cuisine!
