Introduction to Weston

Located in Broward County, Greater Miami, Weston is a picturesque planned community that has been ranked as one of the best places to live in Florida. It is known for its lush green surroundings, clean streets, and a family-friendly atmosphere. In this travel guide, we will take you on a journey to explore the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more in Weston, Florida.

List of Places to Visit in Weston

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Weston Historical Society Museum: Learn about the history of Weston and its early settlers at the Weston Historical Society Museum. It has a collection of artifacts, documents, and photographs that showcase the life of the communities in Weston.

Natural Attractions of Weston: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Markham Park: Spread over 666 acres, Markham Park is one of the largest parks in Broward County. It has several hiking trails, bike paths, picnic areas, and a shooting range.

  • Everglades Holiday Park: Take an airboat tour of the River of Grass and see some of the most amazing wildlife in Florida. You can spot alligators, turtles, snakes, and more.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Weston

  • Weston Regional Park: Features a two-mile-long paved walking trail that loops around the park. It is perfect for hiking, biking, or jogging.

Best Things to Do in Weston: Activities and Tours

  • Bonaventure Country Club: Play a round of golf on one of the most famous courses in Florida. This golf course is known for its challenging layout and beautiful scenery.

  • Weston Town Center: Visit Weston’s premier shopping and dining destination. It has several boutiques, restaurants, and cafes, all housed in a charming Mediterranean-style building.

Food and Drink Scene of Weston

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cuban Cuisine at Havana: Experience the flavors of Cuba with dishes like vaca frita, ropa vieja, and lechon asado.

  • Italian Cuisine at Casa D’Angelo: Savor the taste of Italy with dishes like risotto, pasta, and seafood.

Local Beverages

  • Coffee at Don Pan International Bakery: Try the aromatic coffee and pastries at this popular Cuban bakery.

  • Cocktails at Prime Cigar & Wine Bar: Sip on a hand-crafted cocktail while enjoying a fine cigar in this swanky bar.

Where to Stay in Weston: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Marriott’s Vacation Club at Weston: A luxurious resort that offers spacious villas and world-class amenities like a spa, fitness center, and golf courses.

  • Bonaventure Resort & Spa: A 120-acre resort that has several swimming pools, tennis courts, and a fitness center. It also offers a range of spa services.

What to Buy in Weston: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Arts and Crafts at Weston Art Guild: Buy some unique and original artwork from local artists at the Weston Art Guild.

Local Culture and Customs of Weston

Weston is a melting pot of different cultures and nationalities, which is reflected in its food, traditions, and festivals. The city has several cultural events throughout the year, including the Chinese New Year Festival, Oktoberfest, and the Weston Art Festival.

Best Time to Visit Weston

The best time to visit Weston is from December to April when the weather is pleasant, and there are several outdoor activities and events to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Weston

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Stay on the designated hiking trails at Markham Park.
  • Visit the Weston Art Guild.

Don’t:

  • Litter in the park or on the streets.
  • Trespass on private property.
  • Travel alone in the evening.

Safety and Health

Safety in Weston

Weston is a safe city, but like any other place, it is essential to keep your wits about you and take precautions against theft and other crimes.

Health in Weston

Weston has several hospitals and medical facilities, which provide top-quality healthcare services.

Getting to Weston: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Weston

  • Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport (FLL): Located about 30 minutes from Weston.

  • Miami International Airport (MIA): Located about 40 minutes from Weston.

Bus Stations Near Weston

  • Greyhound Bus Station in Fort Lauderdale: Located about 20 minutes from Weston.

Other Options

  • Car rental is a convenient and practical way to explore Weston and the surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Weston

The best way to get around Weston is by car or bike. Taxis and bus services are also available, but they are less frequent and less convenient.

Where to Go Next from Weston: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Miami: Explore the vibrant city of Miami, which is just a short drive from Weston. Visit the beaches, art galleries, and cultural landmarks.

  • Key West: Take a day trip to Key West and experience the laid-back island lifestyle. Go snorkeling, visit the Hemingway House, and enjoy the sea breeze.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or just a relaxing vacation, Weston has something to offer everyone. This travel guide has provided all the necessary information to plan your trip to Weston and make the most of your visit.
