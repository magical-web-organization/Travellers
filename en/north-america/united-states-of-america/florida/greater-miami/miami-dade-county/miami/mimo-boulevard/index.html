Introduction to MiMo Boulevard

MiMo Boulevard, which stands for Miami Modern, is a historic district in Miami that stretches along Biscayne Boulevard from NE 50th Street to NE 77th Street. The area is known for its unique architecture, featuring mid-century modern design and vintage motels refurbished and restored to their former glory. This eclectic neighborhood is a must-visit for anyone interested in history, architecture, and culture.

List of Places to Visit in MiMo Boulevard

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Miami Modern Architecture Walk

The MiMo Architecture Walk is the best way to witness and learn about the unique and artistic architecture that is featured throughout the MiMo neighborhood. Led by renowned historical preservationist and architect, Allan T. Shulman, the tour highlights both new and distinctive architecture designs that evoke the history of the classic city.

Miami Modern Historic District

The Miami Modern Historic District is a unique area that features the consistent mid-century modern designs that were popularized in the 1940s and 1950s in Miami Beach. This walkable district features shops, restaurants, art galleries, and other businesses and is easily one of the most popular attractions for visitors and locals alike.

Vagabond Hotel

The iconic Vagabond Hotel in the historic district of Miami’s MiMo Boulevard features a modern, mid-century design. The hotel was first opened in the late 1950s and has since been restored to its original condition, becoming one of the go-to places to stay for visitors to the area.

MiMo Biscayne Boulevard Historic District

The MiMo Biscayne Boulevard Historic District is a great place to view the historic mid-century modern buildings that make up MiMo Boulevard. It features buildings from 1920s and 1950s, including motels designed by prominent mid-century architects.

Natural Attractions of MiMo Boulevard: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Legion Park

Legion Park is a beautiful park located in MiMo Boulevard that features both natural and recreational settings. Its winding trails offer a scenic view of the bay, making it an ideal location for outdoor enthusiasts looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

Morningside Park

Morningside Park is a waterfront park located in MiMo Boulevard that offers an exceptional view of the Biscayne Bay. From here, visitors can take in the stunning views while enjoying picnics, outdoor sports, and nature walks.

Hiking/Biking Routes of MiMo Boulevard

Exploring MiMo Boulevard by bike is one of the most exciting things to do in the area. From cycling down the numerous trails at Legion Park to cruising along the shores of Biscayne Bay, visitors can take in the colorful, natural beauty of the city.

Best Things to Do in MiMo Boulevard: Activities and Tours

Miami Modern Architecture Walk

The Miami Modern Architecture Walk is a must-do activity for visitors to MiMo Boulevard. Notable architectural landmarks include the Bacardi Building and the Biltmore Tower.

Art Walk

The MiMo Art Walk is a popular event that takes place every second Saturday of the month, where visitors can explore the many art galleries within the district.

Food and Drink Scene of MiMo Boulevard

Must-Try Dishes

The food scene in MiMo Boulevard is excellent. Some of the must-try dishes include seafood, Cuban, and international cuisine.

Local Beverages

The area is home to various bars and local breweries that specialize in craft beers and eclectic cocktails.

Where to Stay in MiMo Boulevard: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

MiMo Boulevard offers a variety of accommodation options for visitors, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Prices range from budget to luxurious, catering to all budgets and preferences.

What to Buy in MiMo Boulevard: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors can take home various souvenirs from the area, including mid-century modern decor, vintage motels postcards, and vinyl records.

Local Culture and Customs of MiMo Boulevard

The local culture in MiMo Boulevard is heavily influenced by its mid-century modern design. Visitors can also witness cultural diversity expressed in their cuisine, art, architecture, music, and fashion scenes.

Best Time to Visit MiMo Boulevard

The best time to visit MiMo Boulevard is during the winter months, between December and April. The weather is mild, and the area is less crowded.

Do’s and Don’ts of MiMo Boulevard

Do:

  • Respect the area’s history and architecture.
  • Explore the local food and drink scene.
  • Take a guided tour of the Mid-Century Modern buildings.
  • Take a stroll along the scenic trails in the parks.

Don’t:

  • Litter in public areas.
  • Touch or damage the historic buildings.
  • Disrespect the local culture and customs.
  • Ignore safety guidelines.

Safety and Health

Safety in MiMo Boulevard

MiMo Boulevard is a relatively safe area to explore, but visitors should take the usual precautions to avoid being a victim of petty crime.

Health in MiMo Boulevard

Visitors to MiMo Boulevard should take standard health precautions, including staying hydrated and protected from the sun.

Getting to MiMo Boulevard: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near MiMo Boulevard

The closest airports to MiMo Boulevard are Miami International Airport (MIA) and Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport (FLL).

Bus Stations Near MiMo Boulevard

The Downtown Miami Bus Terminal is the closest bus station to MiMo Boulevard.

Other Options

Visitors can also take the Miami Metrorail, which offers a reliable and affordable way to explore the area.

How to Get Around in MiMo Boulevard

Visitors can walk or rent a bike to explore MiMo Boulevard, but ride-sharing services are also an excellent option for getting around.

Where to Go Next from MiMo Boulevard: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Little Havana

Little Havana is a culturally rich neighborhood in Miami, famous for its food, music, and historic landmarks.

Key Biscayne

Key Biscayne is a scenic island located just south of Miami, known for its beautiful beaches, wildlife, and nature trails.

Conclusion

MiMo Boulevard is a must-visit destination in Miami, offering something for everyone to enjoy. From its rich history and diverse culture to its natural beauty and eclectic food scene, MiMo Boulevard is a unique and exciting place to explore.
