Introduction to North Miami Beach

North Miami Beach is a stunning city located in Miami-Dade County, Florida, in the United States. It’s known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant culture, and lively nightlife. Visitors to North Miami Beach can look forward to exploring a wide range of attractions and outdoor activities, and indulging in the city’s delicious food and drink scene. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll detail everything you need to know before visiting North Miami Beach, including the top places to visit, where to stay, local customs, and more.

List of Places to Visit in North Miami Beach

North Miami Beach is full of vibrant and exciting attractions that will delight visitors of all ages. Below, we’ve compiled a list of the top things to see and do in this beautiful city:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Ancient Spanish Monastery: This picturesque monastery was shipped over from Spain, centuries-old and rebuilt in Miami in the 1960s.
  • Museum of Contemporary Art: Visit this museum to explore a diverse collection of modern and contemporary works.
  • Oleta River State Park: This state park offers a viewing tower, birding trails, bicycle paths, fishing, and kayak rentals.

Natural Attractions of North Miami Beach: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Haulover Park: This popular beach is the perfect place to sunbathe, swim, and partake in a variety of water sports, like paddleboarding.
  • Greynolds Park: Explore the park on foot or by bike, and relish in the beauty of Miami’s nature.
  • Aventura Mall: While a mall may not seem like an outdoor attraction, Aventura has some beautiful outdoor courtyards and gardens to explore.

Hiking/Biking Routes of North Miami Beach

North Miami Beach is paradise for hikers and bikers, as the city boasts miles of scenic trails that weave through stunning natural landscapes. Here are two routes to consider:

  • Enchanted Forest Elaine Gordon Park: This serene park boasts a picturesque bike trail that winds through a dense forest and end at the red sand lakeshore.
  • Oleta River State Park: This state park offers a variety of trails, including the popular five-mile bike path.

Best Things to Do in North Miami Beach: Activities and Tours

  • Food tour: Embark on a food tour to explore the best cuisine and drinks that North Miami Beach has to offer.
  • Stand-up paddleboarding: Get out on the ocean and paddle through North Miami Beach’s warm waters in this relaxing activity.
  • Catch a dolphin tour: See dolphins and other marine life up close on a guided tour.

Week-Long Itinerary for North Miami Beach

If you’re planning on spending a week in North Miami Beach, we recommend the following itinerary:

  • Day 1: Spend the day relaxing at Haulover Park Beach.
  • Day 2: Explore the cultural landmarks, such as Ancient Spanish Monastery and Museum of Contemporary Art.
  • Day 3: Embark on a food tour to try some of the city’s best eats and drinks.
  • Day 4: Rent a paddleboard and spend the day on the water enjoying the city’s stunning views.
  • Day 5: Visit Greynolds Park and take in the beauty of Miami’s nature.
  • Day 6: Head to Aventura Mall to shop and explore the outdoor courtyards and gardens.
  • Day 7: Take a dolphin tour to see marine life up close.

Food and Drink Scene of North Miami Beach

North Miami Beach is a melting pot of flavors, with options ranging from Cuban to Peruvian to Haitian cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to add to your list:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cuban sandwich: Juicy pork, ham, Swiss cheese, pickles, and mustard, served with crunchy bread.
  • Ropa vieja: A Cuban stew of shredded beef, tomatoes, onions, and bell peppers.
  • Arroz con pollo: Savory chicken and rice made with locally-grown ingredients.

Local Beverages

  • Mojito: Refreshing cocktail made of rum, lime, and mint.
  • Cafecito: A Cuban-style espresso, sweetened with sugarcane.
  • Materva: A sweet and herbal soft drink from Argentina.

Where to Stay in North Miami Beach: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

North Miami Beach has a wide range of accommodations suitable for any budget. Below are our top picks:

  • AC Hotel by Marriott Miami Aventura: Offers a sleek design and ideal location near Aventura Mall.
  • Newport Beachside Hotel & Resort: A cozy establishment located right on the beach.
  • Lighthouse Cove Resort: This resort is located in a quiet neighborhood but still allows for easy access to North Miami Beach’s attractions.

What to Buy in North Miami Beach: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

North Miami Beach is home to many local shops and boutiques, offering visitors the chance to buy unique souvenirs and local products. We recommend checking out these two places:

  • Oleta River Outdoor Center: Take home some North Miami Beach zip lining paraphernalia.
  • Aventura Mall: This shopping mall offers many stores with Miami-made souvenirs & specialty products.

Local Culture and Customs of North Miami Beach

North Miami Beach is a melting pot of cultures, with a strong Cuban influence, paired with influences from other parts of Latin America, the Caribbean, and beyond. Whether you’re exploring local museums, trying local cuisine, or immersing yourself in the city’s nightlife, you’ll find that North Miami Beach is a city steeped in rich cultural traditions.

Best Time to Visit North Miami Beach

The best time to visit North Miami Beach is between December and May, when the weather is pleasant and there are many outdoor activities to enjoy. However, the high season comes with high prices, so be prepared to budget accordingly.

Do’s and Don’ts of North Miami Beach

Do:

  • Enjoy the beach and outdoor attractions.
  • Try local cuisine and beverages.
  • Respect local culture and customs.

Don’t:

  • Leave valuables unattended on the beach.
  • Drink and drive.

Safety and Health

Safety in North Miami Beach

As with any urban area, there are safety hazards to be aware of while in North Miami Beach. Be sure to take the following precautions:

  • Always stay aware of your surroundings.
  • Don’t leave valuables unattended on the beach.

Health in North Miami Beach

North Miami Beach does not have any unique health concerns, and visitors can enjoy their time here without any major health risks. If you’re swimming in the ocean, be sure to stay aware of jellyfish that may sometimes be present.

Getting to North Miami Beach: Transportation Options and Logistics

North Miami Beach is easily accessible through a variety of transportation methods. Here are some options to consider:

Airports Near North Miami Beach

  • Miami International Airport - 14 miles from North Miami Beach
  • Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport - 14 miles from North Miami Beach

Bus Stations Near North Miami Beach

  • Greyhound: 3 miles from North Miami Beach
  • Megabus: 9 miles from North Miami Beach

Other Options

  • Private car: Use car-sharing services or ride-sharing services such as Uber or Lyft to get around the city.
  • Public transportation: Tri-Rail and Metrorail trains offer services for locals and tourists alike.

How to Get Around in North Miami Beach

North Miami Beach is a walkable city. However, other transportation options include public buses, bike rentals, and car rentals.

Where to Go Next from North Miami Beach: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

North Miami Beach is a great starting point to explore other destinations in the Miami area. Here are some nearby destinations to add to your itinerary:

  • Miami Beach: Just a short drive away, Miami Beach offers a stunning beach, lively nightlife and a pulsating art scene.
  • Hollywood Beach: Only a short 25-minute drive, Hollywood Beach is a beautiful stretch of the coast, and is well-known for its charming boardwalk and beachside shops.

Conclusion

North Miami Beach is a city that offers the perfect combination of culture, nature, adventure, and relaxation. With its warm climate year-round, delicious cuisine, and plenty of outdoor activities, it’s no wonder this stunning destination has become a popular vacation spot for many. Whether you’re looking to soak up the sun, explore the city’s vibrant culture, or indulge in its mouth-watering cuisine, North Miami Beach has something for everyone.
