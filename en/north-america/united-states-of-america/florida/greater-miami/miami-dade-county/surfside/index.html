Introduction to Surfside

Located in Miami-Dade County, Greater Miami, in Florida, Surfside is a charming seaside town perfect for a relaxing vacation. With its pristine beaches, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures, there’s no shortage of things to do in this town.

Whether you’re in the mood for a leisurely stroll on the beach or an action-packed adventure, Surfside has something for everyone. In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ll take you through the must-visit places, best things to do, where to stay, and local culture and customs of Surfside.

List of Places to Visit in Surfside

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • ﻿﻿(1) The Biltmore Hotel: It’s a luxurious hotel known for its magnificent Mediterranean architecture and extravagant interiors. The hotel also has a championship golf course, a spa, and an outdoor pool.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Vizcaya Museum and Gardens: A National Historic Landmark, this museum is famous for its stunning architecture, European-style gardens, and exhibits featuring art and decorative objects from the 15th to the 19th centuries.

Natural Attractions of Surfside: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • ﻿﻿(1) Surfside Beach: A pristine white sand beach dotted with palm trees and crystal-clear water, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and surfing.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Bal Harbour Beach: A secluded beach with crystal-clear waters and breathtaking views. This beach is perfect for a relaxing day in the sun, swimming, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Surfside

  • ﻿﻿(1) North Shore Open Space Park: A beautiful park with walking trails, picnic areas, and an expansive beach perfect for a peaceful walk or jog and an amazing sunset view.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Oleta River State Park: Florida’s largest urban park is famous for its scenic biking and hiking trails, kayak and canoe rentals, and stunning views of the mangroves.

Best Things to Do in Surfside: Activities and Tours

  • ﻿﻿(1) Miami Aquatic Adventures: Take a jet ski tour of Miami’s downtown skyline or go on a dolphin-watching tour where you can see these intelligent marine mammals in their natural habitat.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Bass Museum of Art: A contemporary art museum in Miami Beach, featuring collections of modern and contemporary art, as well as temporary exhibitions and programs.

Food and Drink Scene of Surfside

Don’t miss the chance to try the local cuisine and drinks in Surfside. Here are the must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • ﻿﻿(1) Stone Crabs: Freshly shucked stone crabs served with mustard sauce, a famous Florida delicacy.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Key Lime Pie: A creamy and tangy dessert made from Florida’s famous key limes, graham cracker crust, and whipped cream topping.

Local Beverages

  • ﻿﻿(1) Mojito: A refreshing Cuban cocktail made from rum, sugar, lime juice, mint leaves, and soda water.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Cuban Coffee: A strong and sweetened espresso-like coffee, traditionally served in a small cup.

Where to Stay in Surfside: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • ﻿﻿(1) Grand Beach Hotel Surfside: A luxurious beachfront hotel with modern amenities, ocean views, and world-class service.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Four Seasons Hotel at The Surf Club: A historic hotel with stunning art deco architecture, spacious suites, and a private beach.

What to Buy in Surfside: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • ﻿﻿(1) Surfside T-Shirts and Caps: Take home a souvenir from your trip to Surfside by buying a T-shirt or cap with the town’s iconic logo.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Florida Key Lime Products: Look for key lime pies, key lime candies, and key lime sauces made from Florida’s famous key limes.

Local Culture and Customs of Surfside

Surfside is a multicultural town that celebrates diversity, and its residents are known to be friendly and welcoming. Respect for local traditions and customs is appreciated, and visitors are encouraged to experience the local festivals, food, and music.

Best Time to Visit Surfside

The best time to visit Surfside is from March to May or from October to November when the weather is mild, and the crowds are thin. It’s also a great time to enjoy outdoor activities and festivals. The summer months can be quite hot and humid, so plan accordingly.

Do’s and Don’ts of Surfside

Do:

  • ﻿﻿(1) Respect the local culture and customs.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Visit the beaches early in the morning to avoid the crowds.

Don’t:

  • ﻿﻿(1) Litter on the beach or any public place.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Use drones without permission for photography or filming.

Safety and Health

Safety in Surfside

Surfside is generally a safe town, but visitors are advised to take precautions like locking doors and windows at night and not leaving valuables unattended.

Health in Surfside

The water quality at the beaches is regularly monitored, and it’s safe for swimming and other water activities. However, visitors should stay hydrated and wear sunscreen to protect from the sun’s harmful rays.

Getting to Surfside: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Surfside

The nearest major airport to Surfside is Miami International Airport (MIA), which is about 13 miles away. Visitors can also arrive at Fort Lauderdale-Hollywood International Airport (FLL), which is about 26 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Surfside

The nearest bus station to Surfside is Miami Beach Bus Station.

Other Options

Visitors can also arrive in Surfside by car or taxi, or take a shuttle service from the airport.

How to Get Around in Surfside

Visitors can get around Surfside by walking, biking, renting a car, or taking a taxi or public transportation.

Where to Go Next from Surfside: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • ﻿﻿(1) Miami Beach: Explore Miami’s iconic beaches, Art Deco district, and nightlife.

  • ﻿﻿(2) Naples: A charming seaside town in Southwest Florida, home to stunning beaches, upscale shopping, and fine dining.

Conclusion

Surfside is the perfect destination for a relaxing getaway with its beautiful beaches, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures. This comprehensive tour guide has hopefully given you an idea of what to expect when you visit Surfside. From must-visit places to food and drink scenes, and local culture and customs, Surfside has something for everyone.
