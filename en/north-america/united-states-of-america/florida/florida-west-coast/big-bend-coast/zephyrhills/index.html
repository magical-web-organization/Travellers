Introduction to Zephyrhills

Zephyrhills is a small town in the Big Bend Coast region of West Florida. It is situated approximately 25 miles northeast of Tampa and named after the fresh water springs located in the area. This quaint town offers a variety of activities and attractions that make it a great stop for those traveling through Florida.

List of Places to Visit in Zephyrhills

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Zephyrhills Depot Museum: a train depot that has been converted into a museum dedicated to showcasing the history of the railway system and its impact on the town.
  • Zephyrhills Museum of Military History: a museum that honors veterans by showcasing a large collection of military artifacts, ranging from the Civil War to the present day.

Natural Attractions of Zephyrhills: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Zephyrhills Waterfall: a small waterfall nestled amongst some of the area’s beautiful natural surroundings.
  • Giraffe Ranch: a unique and educational wildlife park that allows visitors to see and interact with giraffes and other exotic animals in their natural habitat.
  • Skydive City: one of the world’s premier skydiving centers, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding areas during free fall.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Zephyrhills

For outdoor enthusiasts, there are several great hiking and biking trails that offer stunning views of the surrounding areas. Some of the best routes in Zephyrhills include:

  • The Withlacoochee State Trail: a 46-mile paved trail offering views of nature and wildlife.
  • The Zephyrhills/Wesley Chapel Cycling Route: a scenic 24-mile ride that winds through some of the area’s most beautiful scenery.

Best Things to Do in Zephyrhills: Activities and Tours

  • Visit the Zephyrhills Brewing Company for a taste of local brews and a tour of the facilities.
  • Take a scenic hot air balloon ride over Zephyrhills and the surrounding areas.
  • Attend the Zephyrhills Fall Festival, which takes place every October and features live music, food vendors, and carnival rides.

Food and Drink Scene of Zephyrhills

Must-Try Dishes

  • Zephyrhills Beef Brisket: a classic barbequed beef brisket that is a local specialty.
  • Zephyrhills Seafood Chowder: a hearty soup made with fresh local seafood.
  • Fried Catfish: fried to a golden brown, this dish is a southern favorite and a must-try in Zephyrhills.

Local Beverages

  • Zephyrhills Brewing Company: this craft brewery serves up delicious local brews that are perfect any time of the year.
  • Keel and Curry Winery: offering visitors delicious wine tastings and tours of the property.

Local Culture and Customs of Zephyrhills

Zephyrhills is a small town that values community and tradition. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and engage with locals to learn more about the area’s rich history and culture.

Best Time to Visit Zephyrhills

The best time to visit Zephyrhills is from December to May when the weather is mild, and outdoor activities are at their best.

Do’s and Don’ts of Zephyrhills

Do:

  • Explore the town’s rich history by visiting local museums and historical landmarks.
  • Go skydiving or take a hot air balloon ride for unforgettable views of the surrounding areas.
  • Enjoy fresh seafood and local cuisine at one of the town’s many restaurants and eateries.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage any of the area’s natural surroundings.
  • Neglect to engage with locals and learn about the local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Zephyrhills

Zephyrhills is generally a safe town, but visitors should take necessary precautions and be aware of their surroundings. Always lock your car and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Zephyrhills

Visitors to the area should be aware of the high humidity and heat during the summer months. It’s essential to stay hydrated and take breaks in the shade when participating in outdoor activities.

Getting to Zephyrhills: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Zephyrhills

  • Tampa International Airport: Located approximately 30 miles southwest of Zephyrhills.
  • St. Petersburg-Clearwater International Airport: Located approximately 60 miles southeast of Zephyrhills.

Bus Stations Near Zephyrhills

  • Greyhound Bus Station: Located in Tampa, FL.
  • Pasco County Public Transportation: Offers local bus service in Zephyrhills.

Other Options

  • Driving: Zephyrhills is located off of I-4, making it easily accessible by car.
  • Taxis and ride-sharing services: Available in the area as well.

How to Get Around in Zephyrhills

The best way to get around Zephyrhills is by car. Car rental services are available at nearby airports, or guests can use ride-sharing services.

Where to Go Next from Zephyrhills: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Tampa: Located approximately 30 miles southwest of Zephyrhills, Tampa is a bustling city with a variety of activities and attractions.
  • Orlando: Located approximately 80 miles east of Zephyrhills, Orlando is home to major theme parks such as Walt Disney World, Universal Studios, and SeaWorld.

Conclusion

Zephyrhills has something for everyone, whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures or cultural experiences. From natural attractions to interesting museums and rich historical landmarks, you won’t be bored in this charming town. It’s the perfect stop for travelers who want to experience the best of Florida’s West Coast.
