Introduction to Mount Dora

Located in the heart of Lake County, Mount Dora is a picturesque city in Inland Florida, known for its beautiful lakes, tree-lined streets, and charming architecture. Offering a perfect escape from the hustle-bustle of big cities, Mount Dora is a popular tourist destination that attracts visitors from all walks of life. Whether you are looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or simply want to relax and unwind, Mount Dora has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Mount Dora

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Mount Dora Historical Society Museum: Located in the historic Royellou School building, this museum features exhibits that showcase the history and culture of Mount Dora and the surrounding areas.
  • Mount Dora Center for the Arts: This non-profit art center offers art classes, workshops, and exhibitions featuring local and national artists.
  • Mount Dora Lighthouse Museum: Situated on the shores of Lake Dora, this museum features artifacts and exhibits that showcase the area’s rich maritime history.

Natural Attractions of Mount Dora: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Dora: The largest lake in the Mount Dora area, Lake Dora is perfect for boating, fishing, and other water activities.
  • Mount Dora Canal: Also known as the “Venice of Florida,” the Mount Dora canal is a scenic waterway that connects Lake Dora to Lake Eustis.
  • Trimble Park: This park is a popular spot for camping, hiking, and picnicking, with scenic views of Lake Beauclair.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mount Dora

  • Lake Apopka Loop Trail: This 30-mile trail offers scenic views of the lake, as well as opportunities for birdwatching and wildlife viewing.
  • Wekiva Riverwalk: This 14-mile trail meanders through the Wekiva River Basin, with stunning views of the river and lush vegetation.
  • Ocala National Forest Trail: Located just a short drive from Mount Dora, this trail offers miles of hiking and biking opportunities through one of Florida’s largest and most diverse forests.

Best Things to Do in Mount Dora: Activities and Tours

  • Mount Dora Ghost Walk: Take a spooky tour of Mount Dora’s historic district, and learn about the city’s haunted past.
  • Mount Dora Trolley Tours: Hop on a trolley for a narrated tour of the city’s historic landmarks and scenic vistas.
  • Mount Dora Marketplace: This popular farmers market features fresh produce, arts and crafts, local cuisine and live music.

Food and Drink Scene of Mount Dora

Must-Try Dishes

  • Grouper sandwich: A popular Florida delicacy, this grilled or fried sandwich features fresh grouper and is often topped with coleslaw and tartar sauce.
  • Fried green tomatoes: A Southern classic, these crispy slices of green tomatoes are served with a side of remoulade sauce.
  • Key lime pie: Named after Florida’s state citrus fruit, this tangy dessert features a graham cracker crust and a creamy filling made from fresh key lime juice.

Local Beverages

  • Citrus-infused cocktails: Given Florida’s abundance of citrus fruits, many local bars feature cocktails with fresh-squeezed orange, grapefruit, and lime juice.
  • Iced tea: Often served with a sprig of fresh mint, iced tea is a refreshing drink that is perfect for hot Florida afternoons.
  • Local craft beer: Several microbreweries in the Mount Dora area offer a selection of locally crafted beers, including IPAs, stouts, and lagers.

Where to Stay in Mount Dora: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Lakeside Inn: This historic hotel, located on the shores of Lake Dora, offers luxurious accommodations, fine dining, and breathtaking views of the lake.
  • Heron Cay Lakeview Bed and Breakfast: This charming B&B offers comfortable rooms, a delicious breakfast, and stunning views of Lake Dora.
  • Farnsworth House Bed and Breakfast: This Victorian-style B&B is located in the heart of downtown Mount Dora and offers guests easy access to local shops, restaurants, and attractions.

What to Buy in Mount Dora: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Antiques: Mount Dora is known for its numerous antique shops and boutiques, where visitors can find a variety of vintage items ranging from furniture to jewelry.
  • Candles and soaps: Several local shops offer handmade candles and soaps, often scented with local herbs and citrus fruits.
  • Crafts and artwork: The Mount Dora Center for the Arts and other local galleries feature works by local and national artists, including paintings, sculptures, and jewelry.

Local Culture and Customs of Mount Dora

  • Southern hospitality: Mount Dora and the surrounding areas are known for their friendly and welcoming residents, who are always happy to help visitors with directions, recommendations, and other needs.
  • Charming architecture: Mount Dora’s downtown area is lined with historic buildings and picturesque Victorian homes, giving the city a unique and charming atmosphere.
  • Love of the outdoors: With its many lakes, parks, and recreational areas, Mount Dora is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, who can enjoy hiking, biking, boating, and fishing in the area’s pristine natural environments.

Best Time to Visit Mount Dora

Mount Dora enjoys a subtropical climate that is warm and humid year-round. The best time to visit the city is during the fall and winter months, when temperatures are cooler and there are fewer crowds. Spring is also a great time to visit, when the flowers are blooming and the city is alive with outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mount Dora

Do:

  • Do take a boat tour of Lake Dora and the surrounding lakes.
  • Do visit the Mount Dora Center for the Arts and other local galleries.
  • Do try the local cuisine, including fresh seafood and citrus-infused cocktails.

Don’t:

  • Don’t feed the wildlife, including alligators and birds.
  • Don’t litter or leave trash in public areas.
  • Don’t forget to wear sunscreen and insect repellent when spending time outdoors.

Safety and Health

Safety in Mount Dora

Mount Dora is generally a safe city for tourists and residents alike. However, visitors should take common-sense precautions to ensure their safety, such as locking car doors and not leaving valuables visible in parked cars.

Health in Mount Dora

Mount Dora has a number of hospitals and medical clinics, should visitors require medical attention. Visitors should also be aware of Florida’s hot and humid climate, and take precautions to avoid heat-related illnesses.

Getting to Mount Dora: Transportation Options and Logistics

Mount Dora is located about 30 miles northwest of Orlando, and is easily accessible by car, bus, or plane.

Airports Near Mount Dora

Visitors can fly into several airports near Mount Dora, including Orlando International Airport (MCO), Orlando Sanford International Airport (SFB), and Daytona Beach International Airport (DAB).

Bus Stations Near Mount Dora

Greyhound operates a bus station in nearby Eustis, while LakeXpress provides local bus service throughout Lake County.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Mount Dora by train, via the Amtrak station in nearby Tavares. Alternatively, visitors can rent a car or arrange for airport shuttle service or private transportation to Mount Dora.

How to Get Around in Mount Dora

Mount Dora is a walkable city, with many attractions, shops, and restaurants located within easy walking distance of each other. Visitors can also rent bikes or take a trolley tour to explore the city.

Where to Go Next from Mount Dora: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Orlando: Just a short drive away, Orlando is home to numerous theme parks, shopping centers, and cultural attractions.
  • Tampa: Located about two hours south of Mount Dora, Tampa is home to several museums, theaters, and outdoor attractions, including the famous Busch Gardens amusement park.
  • St. Augustine: Known as the oldest city in the United States, St. Augustine is a charming historic city that is located about two hours northeast of Mount Dora.

Conclusion

Mount Dora is a charming and picturesque city in the heart of Florida, with something to offer everyone. Whether you are looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or simply want to relax and unwind in a beautiful setting, Mount Dora is the perfect destination. Plan your visit today and discover everything this charming city of lakes has to offer.
