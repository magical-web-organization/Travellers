Introduction to Frostproof

Nestled in the heart of Central Florida, Frostproof is a hidden gem for travelers seeking peace, serenity, and a slower pace of life. Located in Polk County, this quaint city of just under 3,000 residents is a delightful place to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. Known for its orange groves, astonishing natural landscapes, and vibrant history, Frostproof is unquestionably one of Florida’s must-visit destinations.

List of Places to Visit in Frostproof

If you’re itching for a respite from your daily routine, Frostproof has a wealth of experiences and places to offer. Here are some of the most popular sites to see and things to do in Frostproof:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Frostproof Historical Museum: Experience the rich local history, including the old phone booth, the original jail cell, and vintage firefighting equipment.
  2. Latt Maxcy Memorial Library: This historic library houses a diverse collection of books, art, and artifacts, including the original Florida Cracker homes.
  3. The Depot Museum: Learn about the Central Railroad and how it affected the growth of Frostproof.

Natural Attractions of Frostproof: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Crooked Lake Wildlife and Environmental Area: A sprawling landscape of excellent fishing, picturesque vistas, and hiking trails meandering through the hardwood hamlets.
  2. Highland Park Fish Camp: Take a guided fishing tour of the famous Lake Kissimmee. Perfect for anglers who like to reel in largemouth bass and other local species.
  3. Lake Clinch Park: It is great for family picnics, paddleboard ventures, kayaking adventures, and camping.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Frostproof

If hiking and biking are your passions, then you’re in luck. Frostproof offers excellent terrain and well-marked trails to explore:

  1. Lake Wales Ridge State Forest: Free bike rentals and hundreds of acres of endless trails in this 27,000-acre state forest make it a must-visit place for biking enthusiasts.
  2. Kissimmee Chain of Lakes Trail: This 21-mile trail offers superb vistas, including marsh and prairie ecosystems, perfect for enjoying an early morning run or an afternoon hike.

Best Things to Do in Frostproof: Activities and Tours

When you are ready to hit the town, Frostproof offers plenty of activities to keep you entertained:

  1. Lake Wales Arts Center: If you’re looking for cultural offerings, head to this center to see the broad array of art exhibitions, concerts, and elite theatre performances.
  2. Frostproof Walking Tour: Take a guided tour and hear unforgettable stories of Frostproof’s unique past and present.
  3. Frostproof Outdoor Adventure Tour: Experience an adrenaline-pumping adventure in the form of airboats, horseback tours, and four-wheel-drive safaris.

Food and Drink Scene of Frostproof

Frostproof offers a range of dining options, from beloved family restaurants to chain fast food establishments.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Florida Gator Bites: This local delicacy is considered an exotic treat by many visitors.
  2. Fried Catfish: Fried catfish is popular in Frostproof, served with hushpuppies and hot buttered cornbread.
  3. Key Lime Pie: Refreshing and tangy, this Florida classic dessert is sweet and zesty.

Local Beverages

  1. Florida Orange Juice: Savor this refreshing taste of fresh oranges with Farmer’s Market or CitraRoy to enjoy the taste of local citrus.
  2. Florida Beer: Local breweries like Grove Roots Brewing Company and The Wine Stable offer some of the finest local craft brews.

Where to Stay in Frostproof: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Frostproof has an excellent assortment of lodging options available to fit any budget or need:

  1. The Lakehouse: Perfect for a romantic getaway, this charming bed and breakfast caters to guests looking for peacefulness and relaxation.
  2. Ramada by Wyndham: This budget-friendly hotel is great for golfing enthusiasts as it offers a package that includes club rentals, cart fees, and breakfast.
  3. Mount Dora Historic Inn: Step into a bygone era at this picturesque inn with Victorian-style bed and breakfast.

Local Culture and Customs of Frostproof

  1. Friendly People: Frostproof residents are known for their friendliness. Take the time to say “hi” to the locals, and they’ll likely respond with a smile and a warm welcome.
  2. Southern Hospitality: Frostproof also has a lot of southern hospitality. Here, people will often greet strangers with offers of food, drink, and general southern cordiality.

Best Time to Visit Frostproof

Florida has a mild climate, making Frostproof a year-round destination. However, the best time to visit is from November through April when the weather is pleasant for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Frostproof

Do:

  1. Tip generously for good service
  2. Take advantage of the natural outdoors.
  3. Respect the privacy of the wildlife.

Don’t:

  1. Litter around local outdoor sites.
  2. Consume alcohol in public places.
  3. Disrespect the local parks and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Frostproof

Frostproof is a safe city, with a low crime rate. However, it’s essential to keep all doors and windows locked, both at home and in your car, to prevent theft and other criminal activity.

Health in Frostproof

Avoiding dehydration is essential when visiting Florida, particularly during hot and humid summers. Wear loose and light-colored clothing, sunblock, and insect repellant. It’s also crucial to have access to clean and drinkable water.

Getting to Frostproof: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Frostproof

The nearest international airport to Frostproof is the Orlando International Airport, located approximately 68 miles east of Frostproof. Southwest Florida International Airport is an alternative option, located approximately 133 miles south.

Bus Stations Near Frostproof

There is a Greyhound bus station in nearby McLean, two miles north of downtown Frostproof.

Other Options

The best way to explore Frostproof is by driving, as this gives you more flexibility with your itinerary and allows you to discover lesser-known gems in the area.

How to Get Around in Frostproof

The best way to get around Frostproof is by car or bike. It is advisable to rent a car if you will be spending several days touring the city. There are several bike rental companies available in Frostproof for biking enthusiasts.

Where to Go Next from Frostproof: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have additional time to explore Florida, here are a few magnificent places you can visit near Frostproof:

  1. Bok Tower Gardens: Located in neighboring Lake Wales, Bok Tower Gardens is a beautiful spot to witness Florida’s vibrant flora and fauna.
  2. LEGOLAND Florida: If you are travelling with kids, Legoland is a must-visit in Winter Haven.
  3. Everglades National Park: An incredible national park filled with wildlife and breathtaking beauty.

Conclusion

Frostproof is a charming and beautiful city that has retained its natural and cultural heritage, making it the perfect destination for visitors seeking an authentic Florida experience. With its rich history, beautiful scenery, abundant outdoor activities, friendly people, and delicious food, Frostproof is undoubtedly worth a visit.
