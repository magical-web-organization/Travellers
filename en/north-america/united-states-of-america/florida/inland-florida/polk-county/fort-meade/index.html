Introduction to Fort Meade

Fort Meade is a small historic city in Polk County, Florida. It is known for its abundant natural beauty, rich history, and cultural attractions. The city is situated between Orlando and Tampa, making it a perfect getaway destination for travelers looking for peace and tranquility.

List of Places to Visit in Fort Meade

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Fort Meade Historical Museum

The Fort Meade Historical Museum is a must-visit attraction for history buffs. The museum showcases the history and heritage of Fort Meade and features a collection of artifacts and exhibits from the various eras of the city’s history.

Old Fort Meade Schoolhouse

The Old Fort Meade Schoolhouse is another must-see attraction in the city. It is a well-preserved 19th-century building that served as a school for nearly a century. The schoolhouse has been restored and transformed into a museum showcasing the history of education in the area.

Natural Attractions of Fort Meade: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Peace River

Peace River is a waterway that runs through the heart of the city. It is famous for its scenic beauty and offers a great opportunity for outdoor adventure. Visitors can go kayaking, canoeing, fishing, swimming, and more.

Mosaic Peace River Park

Mosaic Peace River Park is a beautiful park situated along the Peace River. The park features camping areas, hiking trails, and picnic facilities. The park is also an excellent spot for birdwatching.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fort Meade

Fort Meade is home to numerous scenic trails and paths that are perfect for hiking and biking. Some of the popular trails that you can explore include the Fort Fraser Trail, the General James A. Van Fleet State Trail, and the Peace River Trail.

Best Things to Do in Fort Meade: Activities and Tours

Fort Meade Outdoor Recreation

Fort Meade Outdoor Recreation offers a wide range of outdoor activities for visitors of all ages. You can go fishing, hunting, camping, and engage in other adventure activities such as ATV riding, shooting, and archery.

Week-Long Itinerary for Fort Meade

Day 1: Start your trip with a visit to the Fort Meade Historical Museum. Learn more about the city’s past and admire the collection of historical artifacts.

Day 2: Go on a kayaking adventure on Peace River and get close to nature. Spend the day exploring the river and its surroundings.

Day 3: Take a guided tour of Mosaic Peace River Park. Experience the natural beauty of Fort Meade and learn more about the area’s ecosystems.

Day 4: Explore the hiking trails around the city. The Fort Fraser Trail and General James A. Van Fleet State Trail are popular choices.

Day 5: Visit the Old Fort Meade Schoolhouse and take a journey back in time. The museum offers a unique insight into the history of education in the area.

Day 6: Rent an ATV or go shooting at Fort Meade Outdoor Recreation. Spend the day indulging in adventure activities and honing your skills.

Day 7: Wrap up your trip with a delicious meal at one of the city’s restaurants. Don’t forget to try some of the local dishes and beverages.

Food and Drink Scene of Fort Meade

Must-Try Dishes

Fort Meade is famous for its Southern cuisine, and there is no shortage of delicious dishes to try. Some of the must-try dishes include:

  • Fried chicken
  • Collard greens
  • Grits
  • Biscuits and gravy
  • Key Lime pie

Local Beverages

The city has a thriving craft beer scene, and visitors can sample some of the local brews at the city’s breweries. Some of the popular breweries include Grove Roots Brewing Co. and Two Henrys Brewing Co.

Where to Stay in Fort Meade: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Fort Meade offers a range of accommodation options to suit different budgets and preferences. Some popular options include:

  • Streamsong Resort
  • Hampton Inn & Suites Bartow
  • Westgate River Ranch Resort & Rodeo

What to Buy in Fort Meade: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Some of the popular souvenirs and local products that you can buy in Fort Meade include:

  • Citrus fruits
  • Honey
  • Handmade crafts

Local Culture and Customs of Fort Meade

Fort Meade has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors can experience the local customs and traditions by attending events such as the Founders Day Festival and the Florida Flywheelers Antique Engine Club Show.

Best Time to Visit Fort Meade

The best time to visit Fort Meade is between the months of October and April. The weather during this time is pleasant, and there are fewer crowds than during the peak summer season.

Do’s and Don’ts of Fort Meade

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Take care of the natural environment.
  • Carry a sunscreen, hat and sunglasses while heading out for outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Litter or harm the natural habitat.
  • Disrespect the customs or traditions of the locals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Fort Meade

Fort Meade is a safe tourist destination, however, it’s always best to be alert of your own surroundings when traveling. Carrying a pepper spray is advisable.

Health in Fort Meade

In case of any medical emergency, visitors can go to Lakeland Regional Health Medical Center, which is the nearest hospital.

Getting to Fort Meade: Transportation Options and Logistics

Fort Meade is easily accessible by road and air. The nearest airport is the Orlando International Airport, which is a 1-hour 15-minute drive from the city. Visitors can also arrive at the Tampa International Airport, which is a 1-hour and 45-minute drive from the city.

Airports Near Fort Meade

  • Orlando International Airport
  • Tampa International Airport

Bus Stations Near Fort Meade

Greyhound Bus Lines is the nearest bus service provider to Fort Meade and has stops in nearby towns and cities.

How to Get Around in Fort Meade

The best way to get around Fort Meade is by car. Visitors can rent a car at the Orlando International Airport or Tampa International Airport. Taxis and ride-sharing services like Uber are also available.

Where to Go Next from Fort Meade: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can explore nearby attractions like Legoland Florida, which is a 30-minute drive from Fort Meade. Other nearby destinations include Tampa, Orlando, and Lakeland.

Conclusion

Fort Meade is a beautiful city that offers visitors a perfect blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or just a laid-back, relaxing getaway, Fort Meade has something for everyone. From its stunning natural attractions to its rich cultural heritage, Fort Meade is a destination that shouldn’t be missed. Plan your trip today and get ready to experience the best of Fort Meade.
