Introduction to Davenport

Welcome to Davenport, a charming city in the heart of Inland Florida, surrounded by beautiful landscapes and a rich cultural heritage. Known for its outdoor adventures, scenic views, and warm weather all year round, Davenport is the perfect destination for those seeking an off-the-beaten-track experience.

In this guide, we’ll take you on a tour of Davenport’s best attractions, recommend the finest restaurants and accommodations, and offer tips on how to get around town and what to expect from local culture and customs.

List of Places to Visit in Davenport

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Davenport has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its many museums and historical places. Here are our top picks:

  • Polk County History Center: A fascinating museum that tells the story of Polk County’s past through interactive exhibits and artifacts.
  • Ridge Art Association Gallery: A nonprofit gallery that hosts monthly exhibits featuring local artists with a variety of styles and mediums.

Natural Attractions of Davenport: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

From hiking and biking to kayaking and fishing, Davenport offers endless opportunities for outdoor activities and fun. Here are some of our favorite outdoor places to explore:

  • Lake Kissimmee State Park: A pristine area that offers hiking trails, camping, fishing, boating, and wildlife viewing.
  • Bok Tower Gardens: Enjoy a leisurely stroll through these beautiful gardens and experience the tranquil beauty of the Singing Tower, a gothic masterpiece that houses a carillon of 60 bells.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Davenport

For those who love nature and adventure, Davenport has some fantastic hiking and biking trails. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • West Orange Trail: A paved, scenic 22-mile trail that links Davenport with other small towns and parks in Central Florida.
  • Green Swamp Preserve: A nature reserve with 11 trails for hiking, biking, and horseback riding, showcasing diverse ecosystems, including cypress swamps, oak hammocks, and pine flatwoods.

Best Things to Do in Davenport: Activities and Tours

Whether you want to explore the local culture, or just kick back and relax, Davenport has plenty of activities and tours. Here are some of our favorite things to do:

  • Skydive Spaceland Florida: For an adrenaline rush, experience a tandem skydive over scenic Central Florida and take in breathtaking vistas of Lake Wales, Kissimmee, and more.
  • Davenport Art and Wine Walk: This monthly event lets you sample fine wines from around the world while enjoying art and music at various locations throughout downtown Davenport.

Week-Long Itinerary for Davenport

If you’re planning a week-long visit to Davenport, here’s a suggested itinerary:

  • Day 1: Visit the Polk County History Center and enjoy a sunset view at Lake Eva Park.
  • Day 2: Explore Lake Kissimmee State Park and end the day with a sunset cruise on Lake Hamilton.
  • Day 3: Rent a bike and explore the West Orange Trail, including the historic town of Winter Garden.
  • Day 4: Relax at Bok Tower Gardens and enjoy lunch at Chalet Suzanne, a historic restaurant nearby.
  • Day 5: Go skydiving with Skydive Spaceland and enjoy dinner and drinks at a local brewery.
  • Day 6: Take part in the Davenport Art and Wine Walk and explore the boutique shops downtown.
  • Day 7: Go fishing in Lake Marion or take a hot air balloon ride over the area.

Food and Drink Scene of Davenport

Must-Try Dishes

Davenport has a diverse food scene that caters to all tastes and budgets. Here are some of the dishes you shouldn’t miss:

  • Pierogies from Bruno’s Cheesesteaks: A delicious blend of potatoes, cheese, and meat in a pocket of dough, served with onions and sour cream.
  • Catfish Nuggets from Buster’s Bistro: Bite-sized pieces of catfish deep-fried to crispy perfection, served with a side of slaw and fries.

Local Beverages

Davenport has some unique drinks that reflect the local culture and heritage. Here are some of the must-try beverages:

  • Sweet Magnolia Tea: A refreshing sweet tea with a hint of vanilla, served at local diners and restaurants.
  • Orange Juice: Sourced from the many citrus groves in the area, Davenport’s orange juice is a must-try for any visitor.

Where to Stay in Davenport: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Davenport has plenty of accommodations to suit any traveler’s needs and budget. Here are some of our recommendations:

  • Bahama Bay Resort: A family-friendly resort with spacious villas, a splash pad, and a beach volleyball court.
  • Holiday Inn Express & Suites: A modern hotel with an outdoor pool, fitness center, and complimentary breakfast.

What to Buy in Davenport: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs or gifts from Davenport, here are some of the local products to consider:

  • Citrus: Fresh oranges, grapefruits, and lemons from the local groves.
  • Florida Art: Unique paintings, sculptures, and pottery from local artists showcasing Florida’s natural beauty and diversity.

Local Culture and Customs of Davenport

Davenport has a laid-back, friendly, and welcoming culture that reflects the Southern hospitality and values. Here are some of the local customs to keep in mind:

  • Small-Town Charm: Davenport is a small town, and locals take pride in their community. Respect and politeness go a long way.
  • Sports Enthusiasm: Central Florida is a hotbed for sports, and locals love their high school and college teams. Be prepared for enthusiastic discussions about football and basketball.

Best Time to Visit Davenport

Davenport has a warm climate all year round, with highs averaging between 70-90 degrees. However, the best time to visit Davenport is from November to April when the weather is milder and less humid, making it perfect for outdoor adventures and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Davenport

Do:

  • Explore the Lake Kissimmee State Park and Bok Tower Gardens.
  • Try local dishes, including pierogies and catfish nuggets.
  • Get outdoors and go hiking, biking, fishing, or kayaking.

Don’t:

  • Forget to wear sunscreen and insect repellent when outdoors.
  • Overlook the small town charm of Davenport and the friendliness of its residents.
  • Miss out on the unique local products, including citrus fruits and Florida art.

Safety and Health

Safety in Davenport

Davenport is generally a safe town with low crime rates. However, it’s always good to practice common sense safety precautions, such as locking car doors and being aware of your surroundings at night.

Health in Davenport

Davenport has access to quality medical care with several hospitals and clinics in the area. However, it’s always best to take proper precautions against heatstroke, dehydration, and other weather-related health hazards if outdoors for an extended period.

Getting to Davenport: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Davenport

  • Orlando International Airport (MCO): Located 34 miles north of Davenport, Orlando International is the closest major airport and offers domestic and international flights.
  • Tampa International Airport (TPA): Located 69 miles west of Davenport, Tampa International offers domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Davenport

  • Greyhound Station: Located in nearby Kissimmee, about 17 miles away, Greyhound offers bus services to various cities in the U.S.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: A car is the most convenient mode of transportation in Davenport and the surrounding areas. Renting a car is easy from the airport or nearby rental companies.
  • Taxi/Uber/Lyft: Taxis, Uber, and Lyft are available in Davenport and the surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Davenport

Davenport is relatively easy to navigate, so getting around is not a problem. If you don’t have a car, you can rent one or take a taxi, Uber, or Lyft. If you prefer to bike or hike, the West Orange Trail is perfect for exploring the downtown area and the surrounding natural wonders.

Where to Go Next from Davenport: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to extend your trip or explore more of Central Florida, here are some must-see destinations and excursions:

  • The Wizarding World of Harry Potter: Visit the famous Hogwarts castle and explore Diagon Alley at this theme park located in nearby Orlando.
  • Kennedy Space Center: Learn about NASA’s space exploration history, witness rocket launches, and take part in interactive exhibits at this popular attraction located east of Davenport.

Conclusion

We hope this guide has inspired you to explore the best of Davenport and its surrounding areas. From outdoor adventures and cultural attractions to dining and shopping, Davenport has something for everyone. Whether you’re a nature lover, an art enthusiast, or a sports fan, Davenport will charm you with its small-town charm, warm weather, and welcoming culture.
