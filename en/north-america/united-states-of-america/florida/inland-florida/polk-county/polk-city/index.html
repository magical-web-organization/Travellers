Polk City is a charming city located in Polk County, in Inland Florida, United States of America, North America. It is a hidden gem that offers visitors a unique experience. From its top attractions, hiking, and biking routes to its local culture and customs, Polk City has something for everyone. If you are planning to visit Polk City, this comprehensive travel guide will help you plan your trip and maximize your experience.

Introduction to Polk City

Polk City is a small town in Central Florida, with a population of about 2,000 people. It is located between Orlando and Tampa, making it a convenient location for those who want to explore both cities.

This city is known for its rich history and natural beauty. It was historically a hub for the railroad, and visitors can still see the remnants of the railway lines today. Polk City is also home to many lakes and parks, offering visitors an opportunity to explore the outdoors.

List of Places to Visit in Polk City

Polk City has a range of attractions for visitors to explore. Here are some notable places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums, and Historical Places

  • Polk County Historical Museum: This museum showcases the history of Polk County, with exhibits on Native Americans, early settlers, and more.
  • Fantasy of Flight: This attraction is a must-visit for aviation enthusiasts. It features rare and vintage aircraft, as well as daily aerial demonstrations and exhibits.
  • Florida Citrus Center: For those interested in learning about Florida’s famous citrus industry, this is a great attraction to visit.

Natural Attractions of Polk City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Circle B Bar Reserve: This nature reserve boasts over 1,200 acres of wetlands, hiking trails, and wildlife viewing opportunities.
  • Lake Agnes: This lake is known for its crystal-clear waters and is a popular spot for fishing, swimming, and boating.
  • Gatorland: This popular attraction offers visitors the opportunity to see over 2,000 alligators, crocodiles, and other exotic animals.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Polk City

Polk City offers a range of hiking and biking routes for visitors to explore. Here are some notable options:

  • Fort Fraser Trail: This 7.3-mile trail connects Polk City to Lakeland and passes through beautiful scenic areas.
  • Van Fleet Trail: This 29.2-mile trail spans across Polk, Sumter, and Lake counties and offers visitors a chance to experience the Florida wilderness.
  • Auburndale Trail System: This series of trails offers visitors a chance to explore the natural beauty of Auburndale.

Best Things to Do in Polk City: Activities and Tours

Polk City offers visitors a range of activities and tours to enjoy. Here are some recommended things to do:

  • Airboat Rides at Wild Florida: This attraction offers visitors a chance to experience the thrill of an airboat ride through the Florida Everglades.
  • Swamp Buggy Tour: For those interested in exploring the wilderness of Polk County, this tour offers a unique experience.
  • Kayaking and Canoeing Tours: Polk City offers many opportunities for kayaking and canoeing, allowing visitors to explore the area’s lakes and waterways.

Week-Long Itinerary for Polk City

If you have a week to explore Polk City, here is a suggested itinerary to get the most out of your trip:

Day 1: Visit Polk County Historical Museum and then head to Fantasy of Flight.

Day 2: Explore the Circle B Bar Reserve and then enjoy a relaxing afternoon by Lake Agnes.

Day 3: Take an airboat ride at Wild Florida and then visit Gatorland.

Day 4: Explore the Van Fleet Trail and have a picnic lunch.

Day 5: Enjoy a kayaking or canoeing tour in one of the area’s lakes.

Day 6: Take a bus tour of the area and explore local parks and scenic views.

Day 7: Enjoy a leisurely day in the city, visit local shops and restaurants, and buy souvenirs to take home.

Food and Drink Scene of Polk City

Polk City has a range of dining options, from local delis to international restaurants. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Florida Stone Crab Claws: A local favorite, this dish is available at many seafood restaurants in Polk City.
  • Key Lime Pie: This dessert is a must-try for anyone visiting Florida.

Local Beverages

  • Orange Juice: Florida is famous for its citrus, and visitors can enjoy fresh-squeezed orange juice at many locations.
  • Craft beer: Polk City has a growing craft beer scene, with many local breweries offering unique and flavorful beers.

Where to Stay in Polk City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Polk City offers a range of accommodation options for visitors. Here are some recommendations:

  • Hampton Inn & Suites Lakeland-South Polk Parkway: This hotel offers comfortable rooms and is conveniently located near many attractions.
  • Old West Inn: This guesthouse is located in the heart of Polk City and offers a unique western-themed experience.
  • Hostelling International: This hostel offers affordable accommodation and is a great option for budget travelers.

What to Buy in Polk City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Polk City offers many unique and locally-made products that make great souvenirs. Here are some recommendations:

  • Citrus products: Visitors can bring home locally-made citrus products, such as jams, marmalades, and sauces.
  • Vintage aircraft merchandise: Fantasy of Flight offers an extensive gift shop that sells aviation memorabilia, clothing, and more.

Local Culture and Customs of Polk City

Polk City has a friendly community that values hospitality and kindness. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, and avoid sensitive topics in conversations.

Best Time to Visit Polk City

Polk City is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit is from March to May and from September to November. During these months, the weather is mild, and the crowds are fewer.

Do’s and Don’ts of Polk City

Here are some things to keep in mind when visiting Polk City:

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try new foods and beverages.
  • Explore the outdoors and take advantage of the area’s natural beauty.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the environment.
  • Rely on public transportation, as it can be limited in some areas.
  • Ignore safety precautions when exploring the outdoors.

Safety and Health

While Polk City is generally safe, visitors should keep these tips in mind:

Safety in Polk City

  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially at night.
  • Keep valuables locked up and out of sight.
  • Be cautious when driving, as some roads can be narrow and winding.

Health in Polk City

  • Wear sunscreen and insect repellant when exploring the outdoors.
  • Stay hydrated and be mindful of the heat, especially during the summer months.
  • Seek medical attention if you experience any health issues.

Getting to Polk City: Transportation Options and Logistics

Polk City is located between Orlando and Tampa and is easily accessible by car.

Airports Near Polk City

  • Orlando International Airport (MCO)
  • Tampa International Airport (TPA)

Bus Stations Near Polk City

  • Polk County Transit Authority
  • Greyhound Bus Station

Other Options

  • Taxis and ride-sharing services, such as Uber and Lyft, are available in the area.
  • Visitors can rent a car at the airport or in the city.

How to Get Around in Polk City

While public transportation is limited, visitors can explore the city by car or on foot. Biking and hiking are also popular ways to get around the city and explore its natural beauty.

Where to Go Next from Polk City: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Polk City is situated between two major cities, Orlando and Tampa, offering visitors many opportunities for day trips and excursions.

  • Walt Disney World: Located in Orlando, this world-renowned theme park is just a short drive away.
  • Clearwater Beach: Located in Tampa, this pristine beach is a popular destination for visitors to Florida.
  • Busch Gardens Tampa Bay: This theme park and zoo is another popular attraction located in Tampa.

Conclusion

Polk City is a hidden gem that offers visitors a unique experience. With its range of attractions, outdoor adventures, and natural beauty, this city is perfect for those who want to explore Florida’s more authentic side. Use this guide to plan your visit to Polk City and enjoy everything this charming city has to offer.
