Introduction to The Villages

The Villages is a master-planned community located in North Central Florida, just an hour’s drive from Orlando. With more than 120,000 residents, it is one of the fastest-growing communities in the United States. The Villages is a great place to live and an even better place to visit. The community has a lot to offer with its unique architecture, friendly locals, and recreational opportunities.

List of Places to Visit in The Villages

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The Villages Museum and Historical Society

The Villages Museum and Historical Society offers a glimpse of The Villages’ history and culture. Exhibits include artifacts from the first settlers to the present day. Admission is free, but donations are welcomed.

Spanish Springs Town Square

Spanish Springs Town Square is the epicenter of The Villages’ entertainment district. With restaurants, shops, live music, and a movie theater, there is something for everyone. Come and enjoy the Charm of Old Florida present in the Spanish Architecture.

Sharon L. Morse Performing Arts Center

The Sharon L. Morse Performing Arts Center is an exquisite theater in the heart of The Villages. The Center hosts live stage performances, concerts, and shows throughout the year. It’s a great place to spend an evening be it with friends or family.

Orange Blossom Opry

The Orange Blossom Opry is an iconic music venue in The Villages. The Opry stages great country, bluegrass, and gospel music shows, sometimes featuring big-name performers. Come and enjoy the tunes of Florida’s music heritage and enjoy your night out.

Natural Attractions of The Villages: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lake Sumter Landing

Lake Sumter Landing is one of the three main town squares in The Villages, and it offers visitors a beautiful view of Lake Sumter. The square is home to numerous boutiques, restaurants, live music, and a movie theater. A serene view and a serene time, it’s hard not to fall in love with this beautiful location.

Lake Griffin State Park

Lake Griffin State Park is a place for outdoor adventurers. The park offers camping, fishing, hiking, and boating opportunities. The park is home to a diverse range of wildlife, including deer, alligators, and various bird species. It’s a perfect place to spend a day with nature.

Ocala National Forest

The Ocala National Forest is a vast and diverse forest with numerous outdoor activities to do. The forest offers camping, hiking, cycling, fishing, bird watching, and kayaking. The Ocala National Forest is home to some of the most amazing and ancient trees and a network of magnificent waterways.

Hiking/Biking Routes of The Villages

The Villages has some of the best hiking and biking trails in all of Florida.

Silver Lake Nature Preserve

The Silver Lake Nature Preserve offers biking and hiking trails through some of The Villages’ most beautiful terrain. The Preserve is home to wildlife and plant species not found elsewhere in the region. A beautiful scenic trail in the town of Wildwood, not to be missed.

The Villages Trail

The Villages Trail is a flat, paved 12-mile path through the community. The trail is perfect for biking, walking, jogging, roller skating, and even golf cart riding. It’s a great way to explore the beautiful community and enjoy its lush greens and serene views.

Best Things to Do in The Villages: Activities and Tours

Golf

The Villages is a golfer’s paradise, with 12 championship-level courses within the community, which are each unique and challenging. You can choose between public and private courses, any of them will surely provide a great experience to golfers of all levels

Hot Air Balloon Tours

The Villages offers hot air balloon tours to visitors. See the beauty of the community from way up high and experience the serenity of hot air ballooning, floating over the lush greens and enjoying the beautiful surroundings.

Segway Tours

The Villages Segway Tours allow visitors to explore the community on two wheels, with expert guides leading the way. You get to see everything while moving at a fair pace and enjoy the wind blowing the Florida sun on your face.

Week-Long Itinerary for The Villages

Day 1

Arrive in The Villages, check-in at a hotel or guesthouse, and head to La Hacienda Recreation Center for some pickleball.

Day 2

Start your day at the Evans Prairie Country Club, send some time playing golf. After Lunch, head downtown to explore La Plaza Grande before a show at the Orange Blossom Opry.

Day 3

Spend the day relaxing at Lake Sumter Landing, take a stroll around the Square, rent a boat or kayak at the Fishing Boat Marina, and enjoy a live music performance in the evening.

Day 4

Explore the natural beauty of Ocala National Forest, go hiking and kayaking, take a quick lunch in between, and return to The Villages in the evening to relax.

Day 5

Head to the Tierra del Sol Country Club, golf, and spend some time around the pool.

Day 6

Rent a bike and explore the Silver Lake Nature Preserve. Take a packed lunch and spend the day out amidst nature, then return in the evening to enjoy a fine dinner.

Day 7

Get picked up for a hot air balloon tour and see The Villages from the sky. Afterward, spend the afternoon playing at a golf course before returning for a farewell dinner.

Local Culture and Customs of The Villages

The Villages is a thriving community that celebrates its history and diversity. Visitors will find that The Villages has a welcoming local culture that embraces all visitors. Golf carts are the preferred method of transport around the community, and all drivers must have a valid driver’s license.

Best Time to Visit The Villages

The best time to visit The Villages is during the fall, winter, or spring months when the weather is mild and cooler. This time offers enjoyable activites to do outdoors without city life’s humidity and scorching heat. The weather is perfect and allows for outdoor adventures and long walks around the town.

Do’s and Don’ts of The Villages

Do:

  • Try out the Pickleball courts.
  • Rent a bike and enjoy the trails.
  • Visit the Villages winery and enjoy wine tasting.
  • Take a hot air balloon tour.

Don’t:

  • Try driving your car on the golf courses.
  • Go golfing without a tee time
  • Forget your sunscreen!

Safety and Health

Safety in The Villages

The Villages is one of the safest communities in Florida, with low crime rates and a friendly atmosphere. The Villages also has a well-established emergency response system and medical facilities, making it a safe place to visit or live.

Health in The Villages

The Villages has plenty of medical facilities and hospital centers for all your health concerns. The community has access to numerous doctors and specialists, ensuring the best medical care without having to travel far.

How to Get Around The Villages

The best way to get around The Villages is by golf cart. The community has plenty of designated golf cart paths, and golf carts can be rented at several locations. You can also cycle or walk around the community as the trails have space for bikers and walkers alike.
