Introduction to Avon Park

Avon Park is a small city located in Highlands County, in the Florida Heartland region of Florida, United States of America. Founded in 1886, this quaint city is often overlooked by tourists venturing to more popular destinations in Florida, but it is a hidden gem that is worth a visit. Avon Park boasts beautiful natural scenery, rich history, and a relaxed, small-town atmosphere.

List of Places to Visit in Avon Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re interested in history and culture, Avon Park has plenty of attractions to offer.

South Florida State College Museum of Florida Art & Culture

The South Florida State College Museum of Florida Art & Culture houses the largest collection of Florida art in the state, with works from renowned Florida artists such as Harold Newton and Mary Ann Carroll. Admission is free.

Depot Museum

The Depot Museum is located in downtown Avon Park and showcases the city’s rich history, including its early settlers and development of the railroad. Admission is free.

Pinecrest Golf Club

The Pinecrest Golf Club is a renowned historical golf course, designed by famous golf architect Ross Witherspoon in 1926. It has a long and rich history of hosting golf tournaments and championships.

Natural Attractions of Avon Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Avon Park is renowned for its natural beauty and outdoor activities.

Avon Park Air Force Range

The Avon Park Air Force Range is a vast swath of land that is home to over 30 endangered species and offers a variety of outdoor recreational activities such as hiking, hunting, and fishing.

Lake Verona

Lake Verona is a pristine lake nestled in the heart of Avon Park and is a perfect spot for fishing, kayaking, or relaxing on the sandy beach.

Donaldson Park

Donaldson Park is a beautiful park that sits on the edge of Lake Verona and offers picnic areas, playgrounds, and hiking trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Avon Park

If you’re an outdoor enthusiast, Avon Park has several hiking and biking trails to explore.

Avon Park Hiking Trail

The Avon Park Hiking Trail is a 10-mile trail that winds through the Avon Park Air Force Range and offers spectacular views of Florida’s natural beauty.

Tanglewood Nature Center

The Tanglewood Nature Center is a beautiful nature preserve that features over eight miles of hiking and biking trails that wind through pine forests, oak hammocks, and wetlands.

Best Things to Do in Avon Park: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of activities and tours to keep you entertained during your visit to Avon Park.

Red Alert Air Adventures

Red Alert Air Adventures offers an adventurous ride over Avon Park in a vintage WWII bi-plane, allowing you to experience the thrill of flying like a fighter pilot.

Sebring International Raceway

Located just a few miles from Avon Park, the Sebring International Raceway hosts several high-profile auto races throughout the year.

Week-Long Itinerary for Avon Park

Here’s a sample week-long itinerary for Avon Park:

  • Day 1: Visit the South Florida State College Museum of Florida Art & Culture.

  • Day 2: Explore the Depot Museum and enjoy lunch at one of the local diners downtown.

  • Day 3: Tee off at the Pinecrest Golf Club.

  • Day 4: Take a hike on the Avon Park Hiking Trail or explore the Tanglewood Nature Center.

  • Day 5: Go fishing or kayaking on Lake Verona.

  • Day 6: Take a ride in a vintage WWII bi-plane with Red Alert Air Adventures.

  • Day 7: Visit the Sebring International Raceway.

There are several accommodation options in Avon Park to suit every budget.

Reeds Motel & Oasis Bar

Local Culture and Customs of Avon Park

Southern hospitality is a hallmark of Avon Park culture, so expect to be greeted with a warm welcome everywhere you go.

Best Time to Visit Avon Park

The best time to visit Avon Park is during the winter months, from November to April, when the weather is mild and pleasant.

Safety and Health

Safety in Avon Park

Avon Park is a relatively safe city, but be mindful of your belongings and surroundings, particularly in crowded areas.

Health in Avon Park

If you need medical assistance while visiting Avon Park, there are several hospitals and urgent care clinics in the area.

Getting to Avon Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Avon Park

The nearest large airports to Avon Park are Tampa International Airport (TPA) and Orlando International Airport (MCO). Avon Park’s small municipal airport is also an option for private pilots.

Bus Stations Near Avon Park

Greyhound Bus Lines has a station in Sebring, just a few miles from Avon Park.

Other Options

The closest major train station is in Tampa, which offers services to several destinations within Florida and beyond.

How to Get Around in Avon Park

The easiest way to get around Avon Park is by car. You can rent a car at the nearest major airport or take a taxi.
