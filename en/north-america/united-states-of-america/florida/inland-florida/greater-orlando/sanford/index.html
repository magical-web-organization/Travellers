Introduction to Sanford

Located in the Greater Orlando region of Inland Florida, Sanford is a charming city that is brimming with history and culture. Sanford is known for its scenic views, outdoor adventures, and vibrant food and drink scene. This guide will take you through the must-visit attractions, hiking and biking routes, best things to do, food and drink scene, where to stay, local culture and customs, safety and health, and transportation options for getting to and around Sanford.

List of Places to Visit in Sanford

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Historic Sanford Welcome Center: This charming center offers a range of information about Sanford’s rich history, culture, and architecture. Take a walking tour to see the city’s historic landmarks, or enjoy a movie at the center’s movie theater.
  • Sanford Museum: Learn about Sanford’s history and culture from the city’s early years to the present day through a range of exhibits and artifacts.
  • Wayne Densch Performing Arts Center: Located in the historic district, this theater offers a variety of musical and theatrical performances throughout the year.
  • St. Johns Rivership Company: This riverboat cruise offers the perfect way to explore the natural beauty of the stunning St. Johns River.

Natural Attractions of Sanford: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Central Florida Zoo & Botanical Gardens: Home to hundreds of animals from around the world, this zoo also has a variety of beautiful botanical gardens.
  • Lake Monroe: The largest lake in Central Florida, Lake Monroe offers plenty of activities including boating, fishing, kayaking, and paddleboarding.
  • Gallagher Park: Located on the shore of Lake Monroe, this park features a scenic boardwalk, playground, picnic area, and beautiful views.
  • Black Bear Wilderness Area: This beautiful nature reserve features a range of hiking and biking trails, with plenty of opportunities to spot local wildlife.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sanford

  • Seminole Wekiva Trail: This 14-mile trail offers a paved surface for both walking and biking, and runs through Sanford and the surrounding area.
  • Lake Monroe Loop Trail: This scenic trail circles Lake Monroe and offers beautiful views of the lake and the surrounding landscape.
  • Black Bear Wilderness Area Trail: This 7.1-mile trail is perfect for hiking and observing local wildlife.

Best Things to Do in Sanford: Activities and Tours

  • Explore Historic Downtown Sanford: Take a stroll around Sanford’s historic district and explore the city’s eclectic mix of parks, museums, restaurants, and shops.
  • Catch a Game at Sanford Memorial Stadium: Watch Minor League Baseball with the Thrill Mechanics, and enjoy the city’s laid-back atmosphere.
  • Eat and Drink on the Sanford Ale Trail: Sanford features several breweries, distilleries, and wine bars. Take a self-guided Sanford Ale Trail tour and taste some local favorites.
  • Take a Hot Air Balloon Ride: Take to the skies for incredible views of Sanford and the surrounding area.
  • Take a Ghost Tour: Explore Sanford’s haunted history on a spooky ghost tour.

Food and Drink Scene of Sanford

Must-Try Dishes

  • Shrimp and Grits: A classic Southern dish, often served with Andouille sausage and a spicy creole sauce.
  • Cuban Sandwich: A classic Florida sandwich, made with ham, roasted pork, Swiss cheese, pickles, and mustard.
  • Gator Tail: A unique delicacy, gator tail is often served fried with a variety of dipping sauces.

Local Beverages

  • Sanford Brewing Company: Try the brewery’s own house-made beers, as well as a variety of guest beers from around the state.
  • Luisa’s Cellar: This wine bar offers a selection of wines from around the world, as well as light bites to accompany your drink.
  • Jeanine Taylor Folk Art Gallery: This gallery offers a range of folk art souvenirs, including mugs, teapots, and more.

Where to Stay in Sanford: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Eo Inn & Spa: This beautiful hotel is located in a historic building in the heart of downtown Sanford. Enjoy luxurious amenities and stunning views of the city.
  • Comfort Inn & Suites: This affordable hotel offers comfortable rooms and plenty of amenities, including a fitness center, outdoor pool, and free breakfast.
  • The Higgins House Bed & Breakfast: This charming bed and breakfast is located in a beautiful historic building and offers a range of comfortable rooms with a homey feel.

What to Buy in Sanford: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Folk Art and Crafts: Sanford has a vibrant folk art community, and you’ll find a range of unique and beautiful art pieces at local galleries and craft shops.
  • Local Honey: Sanford is surrounded by orchards and farms, and you’ll find plenty of local honey for sale in stores and markets around the city.
  • Vintage Finds: Sanford is known for its antique shops and vintage markets. Browse the city’s flea markets and thrift stores for unique souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Sanford

Sanford is a welcoming and friendly city. The locals are proud of their history and heritage, and are happy to share it with visitors. Don’t be surprised if you strike up a conversation with a local, and take time to appreciate the town’s rich cultural offerings.

Best Time to Visit Sanford

The best time to visit Sanford is in the fall, winter, or early spring, when the weather is mild and comfortable. Summers in Florida can be quite hot and humid, so plan accordingly. Please note that during the peak tourist season, some attractions and accommodations may be booked up well in advance.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sanford

Do:

  • Take a walking tour of the historic district.
  • Try the local cuisine and craft beer.
  • Go on a hot air balloon ride for a unique perspective of Sanford.

Don’t:

  • Overlook the natural beauty of nearby Lake Monroe.
  • Neglect to visit the Sanford Museum.
  • Forget to pack sunscreen and insect repellent.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sanford

Sanford is a safe city, and most areas are well-lit and well-patrolled. However, it is always wise to take precautions when traveling, such as keeping your valuables secure and being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Sanford

In terms of health, Sanford has a range of medical facilities, including urgent care clinics and hospitals, all of which offer quality healthcare services.

Getting to Sanford: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sanford

The closest airport to Sanford is Orlando International Airport (MCO), which is a 25-minute drive from the city center. Other options include Tampa International Airport and Daytona Beach International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Sanford

Greyhound and Megabus offer bus services to Sanford from several major cities in Florida.

Other Options

Amtrak offers train services to the nearby city of Sanford, which is just a short drive from the city center.

How to Get Around in Sanford

Getting around Sanford is easy, with several options including rental cars, taxis, and ride-sharing services such as Uber and Lyft. Many of the city’s main attractions are within walking distance of one another.

Where to Go Next from Sanford: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Daytona Beach: A popular seaside city that is famous for its beaches and NASCAR racing events.
  • Kennedy Space Center: Learn about the history of space exploration at this iconic NASA facility.
  • St. Augustine: The oldest city in the United States, St. Augustine is a charming historic town that offers a range of attractions and activities.

Conclusion

Sanford, Florida is a charming city that is brimming with history, culture, and natural beauty. Whether you’re interested in exploring the city’s historic landmarks, hiking and biking the surrounding trails, or simply relaxing by the lake, Sanford has something to offer everyone. With this comprehensive guide, you can plan your perfect trip and experience all that Sanford has to offer.
