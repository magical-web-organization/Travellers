Introduction to Disney Springs

Located within Walt Disney World in Greater Orlando, Florida, lies Disney Springs, a vibrant and exciting entertainment complex. Adorned with lakes, natural springs, and beautiful gardens, Disney Springs is the perfect family destination for those looking to enjoy a mix of outdoor adventures, live entertainment, fantastic food, and shopping.

List of Places to Visit in Disney Springs

Disney Springs caters to the needs of all visitors, including cultural, natural, and adventure tourists. So, whether you’re looking for museums, historical sites, parks, hiking/biking trails, shopping spots, restaurants or live performances, you will find something to suit you. Here are the top places to visit when you come to Disney Springs:

Local Culture and Customs of Disney Springs

When in Disney Springs, it’s essential to be respectful of local culture and customs. Keep in mind that it’s customary to tip servers 15-20% of the bill amount and to show appreciation for the entertainment.

Best Time to Visit Disney Springs

The best time to visit Disney Springs is between November and December or between April and May when there are fewer crowds. Additionally, visiting in the evening or at night will ensure that you get the best experience as you get to explore the lit streets with nice breezes.

Do’s and Don’ts of Disney Springs

Do:

  • Take time to explore all the corners of Disney Springs
  • Make sure you carry enough cash or a credit card with you
  • Dress comfortably as there is a lot to do
  • Enjoy local dishes and drinks
  • Attend live performances and events

Don’t:

  • Vandalize or damage any property
  • Bring outside food or drink into entertainment venues
  • Litter
  • Queue jump
  • Smoke in non-designated areas

Safety and Health

When visiting Disney Springs, it’s important to take precautions to ensure your safety and health.

Safety in Disney Springs

  • Avoid walking alone at night in dimly lit or unfamiliar areas
  • Keep your valuables safe in either your hotel room or at the front desk
  • be vigilant of your surroundings and belongings
  • Follow the safety rules and regulations as stipulated on all rides and attractions

Health in Disney Springs

  • Wear sunscreen when exploring in the sun
  • Stay well hydrated, especially during long bouts of activity or when it is hot
  • Wash and sanitize your hands before and after meals
  • Check out the location and opening times of the nearest hospital or medical clinic
