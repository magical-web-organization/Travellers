Introduction to Walt Disney World

Walt Disney World is the ultimate vacation destination for families, couples, and people young at heart. Located in Greater Orlando, in Inland Florida, Walt Disney World is a massive entertainment complex spanning over 47 square miles. With something for everyone, including theme parks, water parks, golf courses, shopping, restaurants, and more, Walt Disney World is sure to make your dream vacation come true.

List of Places to Visit in Walt Disney World

Walt Disney World is divided into four theme parks: Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Hollywood Studios, and Animal Kingdom. Each park offers a unique experience with a collection of attractions, shows, and rides that cater to all ages and interests. Here’s a breakdown of the top attractions in each park:

Magic Kingdom

Magic Kingdom is the most popular and iconic park in Walt Disney World. Here are some must-visit attractions:

  • Cinderella Castle
  • Pirates of the Caribbean
  • Haunted Mansion
  • It’s a Small World
  • Splash Mountain
  • Space Mountain

Epcot

Epcot is divided into two distinct areas: Future World and World Showcase. Here are some top attractions:

  • Spaceship Earth
  • Soarin’
  • Test Track
  • The Seas with Nemo & Friends
  • World Showcase Pavilions

Hollywood Studios

Hollywood Studios is all about movies and entertainment. Here are some top attractions:

  • Rock ‘n’ Roller Coaster Starring Aerosmith
  • The Twilight Zone Tower of Terror
  • Star Wars: Galaxy’s Edge
  • Toy Story Land
  • Indiana Jones Stunt Spectacular

Animal Kingdom

Animal Kingdom is the largest of the four parks and features attractions centered around animals and conservation. Here are some top attractions:

  • Kilimanjaro Safaris
  • Avatar Flight of Passage
  • Expedition Everest
  • Kali River Rapids
  • Festival of the Lion King

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Walt Disney World not only offers entertainment attractions but also museums and historical places where visitors can learn about the rich cultural heritage and history of various countries. Here are some must-visit cultural attractions:

  • American Adventure in Epcot
  • Walt Disney World Railroad
  • Hall of Presidents
  • Maharajah Jungle Trek in Animal Kingdom
  • Walt Disney: One Man’s Dream

Natural Attractions of Walt Disney World: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

A vacation in Walt Disney World is not just about fast rides and shows. The theme park also offers a plethora of natural attractions, including outdoor adventures and scenic views. Here are some must-do activities:

  • Disney’s Wilderness Lodge
  • The Boneyard in Animal Kingdom
  • Tom Sawyer Island in Magic Kingdom
  • Rivers of Light in Animal Kingdom
  • Discovery Island Trails in Animal Kingdom

Hiking/Biking Routes of Walt Disney World

Walt Disney World is home to multiple hiking and biking routes. Here are some of the best trails and routes for hiking and biking:

  • The Wilderness Back Trail Adventure
  • Fort Wilderness Trail
  • Epcot Resort Area/Wide World of Sports Trail
  • Perimeter Road Trail

Best Things to Do in Walt Disney World: Activities and Tours

Walt Disney World offers fantastic activities and tours that visitors should experience. Here are a few of the best:

  • Bibbidi Bobbidi Boutique in Magic Kingdom
  • Pirates & Pals Fireworks Dessert Voyage in Magic Kingdom
  • Keys to the Kingdom Tour in Magic Kingdom
  • The Void: Star Wars Secrets of the Empire in Disney Springs

Local Culture and Customs of Walt Disney World

Walt Disney World is an iconic tourist destination with a rich culture and traditions that visitors should be aware of. Here’s an overview of local customs:

  • Respect the characters, actors, and performers
  • Refrain from using profanity at all times
  • Avoid littering and help maintain the park’s cleanliness
  • Follow the park rules and regulations

  • Weather and seasons

  • Special events and Holidays

Do’s and Don’ts of Walt Disney World

Here are some dos and don’ts to make your Walt Disney World vacation memorable for all the right reasons:

Do:

  • Plan ahead
  • Pack sunscreen and comfortable walking shoes
  • Drink plenty of water throughout the day
  • Obtain FastPass+ reservations
  • Be courteous to other visitors and park staff

Don’t:

  • Show up late to your reservations
  • Litter or smoke in non-designated areas
  • Stand up on rides or disobey ride rules
  • Attempt to touch or harass the characters or performers
  • Start arguments or engage in physical altercations

Safety and Health

Walt Disney World prioritizes the safety and health of its visitors. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Safety in Walt Disney World

  • Follow all ride safety instructions provided by staff
  • Use hand sanitizer provided by the park
  • Secure all valuables and personal items
  • Stay hydrated and wear comfortable clothes and shoes

Health in Walt Disney World

  • Refrain from visiting the park if you feel unwell
  • Commendable Sanitization for frequently touched surfaces.
  • Precaution measures in place like social distancing, regular temperature checks, etc.

Airports Near Walt Disney World

  • Orlando International Airport – MCO
  • Orlando Sanford International Airport – SFB

Bus Stations Near Walt Disney World

  • Disney’s Hollywood Studios Bus Station
  • Transportation and Ticket Center

Other Options
