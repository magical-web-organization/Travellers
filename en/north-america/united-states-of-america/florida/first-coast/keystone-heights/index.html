Introduction to Keystone Heights

Nestled in the heart of Florida’s First Coast, Keystone Heights is a charming town situated in Clay County. Known for its natural beauty and outdoor recreation opportunities, Keystone Heights is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. From hiking and biking trails to picturesque lakes and historical landmarks, Keystone Heights has something for everyone. This travel guide will help you plan your perfect trip to Keystone Heights with comprehensive information on the best things to see, do, and eat in town.

List of Places to Visit in Keystone Heights

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Keystone Heights is rich in history, and there are plenty of museums and historical places to explore. Some of the must-visit attractions include:

  • Keystone Heights Historic District: This beautiful district features several historic buildings and landmarks, including the Keystone Heights Woman’s Club and City Hall.

  • Florida Twin Theatre: A local landmark, this historic cinema has been operating since 1956 and still shows the latest releases.

  • Camp Blanding Museum & Memorial Park: Located nearby in Starke, Florida, this museum showcases the history of Camp Blanding, a former military installation that played a crucial role in World War II and the Korean War.

Natural Attractions of Keystone Heights: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Keystone Heights is known for its beautiful lakes and outdoor recreation opportunities. Here are some of the best natural attractions to check out in town:

  • Lake Geneva: This 600-acre lake is the largest in Keystone Heights and is perfect for fishing, boating, and water sports.

  • Mike Roess Gold Head Branch State Park: This park features over 2,000 acres of stunning natural beauty, including hiking trails, camping sites, and a freshwater swimming lake.

  • Keystone Heights Airport Park: This park offers stunning views of Keystone Heights and is a great spot for picnics, hiking, and birdwatching.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Keystone Heights

For outdoor enthusiasts, Keystone Heights offers plenty of hiking and biking trails to explore. Here are some of the best routes to check out:

  • Gold Head Ravine Trail: This 3.5-mile hiking trail takes you through the heart of Gold Head Branch State Park and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

  • Keystone Heights Trail: This 10-mile paved trail takes you through the heart of Keystone Heights and offers access to several of the town’s best attractions, including the Historic District and Lake Geneva.

  • Rice Creek Conservation Area Trail: Located nearby in Palatka, Florida, this 7-mile trail offers stunning views of the Rice Creek and is a popular spot for hiking and mountain biking.

Best Things to Do in Keystone Heights: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are plenty of other activities and tours to enjoy in Keystone Heights. Here are some of the best things to do in town:

  • Bass Fishing: With several lakes and waterways in Keystone Heights, the town is a prime spot for bass fishing. Local guides offer guided trips and can take you to some of the best spots in town.

  • Horseback Riding: Keystone Heights offers several horseback riding trails, and local stables offer guided tours for riders of all skill levels.

  • Airplane Tours: Keystone Heights Airport offers scenic airplane tours of the town and surrounding landscape. Choose from several packages to see the best of Keystone Heights from above.

Food and Drink Scene of Keystone Heights

Must-Try Dishes

Keystone Heights offers a wide variety of dining options, from classic southern cuisine to modern fusion dishes. Here are some of the must-try dishes in town:

  • Fried Chicken: A southern staple, fried chicken is a must-try dish in Keystone Heights. Check out local favorites like Fried Chicken Kitchen for some of the best chicken in town.

  • Seafood: With several lakes in the area, seafood is a popular choice in Keystone Heights. Try the seafood platter at White Sands Lakefront Restaurant for a feast of local delicacies.

  • Barbecue: There are several great barbecue joints in town, but Mojo’s BBQ Grill & Tavern is a local favorite for its delicious smoked meats and classic sides.

Local Beverages

If you’re looking for something to wash down your meal, Keystone Heights offers plenty of local beverage options:

  • Water: With so many lakes in the area, water is a local specialty in Keystone Heights. Try the filtered water at the Taste of Keystone Waterhouse for a refreshing drink.

  • Craft Beer: Keystone Heights is home to several craft breweries, including the popular Keystone Heights Brewery. Try their IPA or Blonde Ale for a taste of local flavor.

Where to Stay in Keystone Heights: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Keystone Heights offers several accommodation options for visitors, from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. Here are some of the best places to stay in town:

  • Hampton Inn Keystone Heights: This modern hotel offers comfortable rooms, a fitness center, and a hot breakfast each morning.

  • Keystone Inn: This budget-friendly motel offers clean and comfortable rooms at a great price.

  • The Rose Cottage Bed & Breakfast: This charming guesthouse offers a quiet and romantic escape in the heart of Keystone Heights.

What to Buy in Keystone Heights: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs and local products to bring home, Keystone Heights offers plenty of options. Here are some of the best places to shop in town:

  • Keystone Heights Farmers Market: This weekly farmers market offers fresh produce, local crafts, and unique souvenirs from local vendors.

  • Keystone Heights Antique Mall: This antique mall offers a wide variety of vintage souvenirs, including postcards, maps, and historic photographs.

Local Culture and Customs of Keystone Heights

Keystone Heights is a friendly and welcoming community with a strong sense of local pride and traditions. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions and be mindful of preserving the town’s natural beauty and resources.

Best Time to Visit Keystone Heights

The best time to visit Keystone Heights is from October to May, when temperatures are mild and pleasant and there is little chance of rain. The summer months can be hot and humid, with frequent thunderstorms and the possibility of hurricanes.

Do’s and Don’ts of Keystone Heights

Do:

  • Do bring sunscreen and insect repellent, as there are plenty of outdoor activities in Keystone Heights.

  • Do try local cuisine and beverages, including fried chicken and craft beer.

  • Do respect local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or disturb natural habitats.

  • Don’t trespass on private property or engage in illegal activities.

  • Don’t forget to bring appropriate outdoor gear, such as hiking shoes and rain jackets.

Safety and Health

Safety in Keystone Heights

Keystone Heights is a safe and friendly town, but visitors should still take common sense precautions to stay safe. Be aware of your surroundings, avoid walking alone at night, and keep valuables out of sight.

Health in Keystone Heights

For emergency medical care, the closest hospital is Shands Starke Regional Medical Center in nearby Starke, Florida. Visitors should also be aware of the potential health risks associated with outdoor activities, such as tick-borne illnesses and heat exhaustion.

Getting to Keystone Heights: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Keystone Heights

The closest major airport to Keystone Heights is Jacksonville International Airport, which is approximately 55 miles away. Car rentals and private shuttle services are available from the airport to Keystone Heights.

Bus Stations Near Keystone Heights

The closest bus station to Keystone Heights is the Greyhound station in Gainesville, Florida, which is approximately 30 miles away. From there, visitors can rent a car or take a taxi to Keystone Heights.

Other Options

There are several other transportation options available for visitors to Keystone Heights, including private shuttle services, limousine rentals, and taxi services.

How to Get Around in Keystone Heights

Keystone Heights is a small town, and many of the main attractions are within walking or biking distance. Visitors can also rent a car to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Keystone Heights: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Keystone Heights is located in the heart of Florida’s First Coast, and there are plenty of nearby destinations and excursions to explore. Some popular options include:

  • St. Augustine: This historic town is just a short drive from Keystone Heights and offers plenty of attractions, including the famous Castillo de San Marcos and the St. Augustine Lighthouse.

  • Gainesville: Home to the University of Florida, Gainesville offers plenty of cultural attractions and outdoor activities, including the Florida Museum of Natural History and the Paynes Prairie Preserve State Park.

  • Jacksonville: Florida’s largest city is just a short drive from Keystone Heights and offers a wide variety of attractions, including the Jacksonville Zoo and Gardens and the Cummer Museum of Art and Gardens.

Conclusion

Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, or delicious cuisine, Keystone Heights has something to offer everyone. Plan your trip today and discover the charm and beauty of this hidden gem in Florida’s First Coast.
