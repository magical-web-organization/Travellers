DeLand Travel Guide - A Comprehensive Tour Guide

Introduction to DeLand

DeLand is a charming city located in Volusia County, Florida, which is famous for its rich history, beautiful natural attractions, and diverse cultural offerings. The city is well-known for being the county seat of Volusia County, and home to Stetson University, a renowned educational institution.

DeLand is an ideal destination for travelers who want to experience the best of both worlds - a laid-back, small-town atmosphere combined with modern amenities and exciting attractions. Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor enthusiast, or a foodie, DeLand has something to offer for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in DeLand

To make the most of your trip to DeLand, check out our list of must-visit places in the city.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

DeLand has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its historic sites, museums, and artistic offerings. Here are some of the most important cultural attractions in DeLand.

  • Stetson Mansion: This is a historic mansion built in 1886 that has been restored and is now open to the public for tours.
  • DeLand Naval Air Station Museum: This museum is dedicated to preserving the history of the DeLand Naval Air Station, which played a crucial role in World War II.
  • West Volusia Historical Society: This museum is located in the historic Henry A. DeLand House Museum and offers a glimpse into the lives of the city’s early settlers.

Natural Attractions of DeLand: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

DeLand is surrounded by scenic natural beauty, making it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts. From kayaking and canoeing to hiking and biking, there are plenty of ways to explore the great outdoors in DeLand.

  • Blue Spring State Park: This park is nestled along the St. Johns River and is famous for its blue spring, which is a popular spot for snorkeling and diving.
  • Hontoon Island State Park: This park is only accessible by boat and is home to diverse wildlife, scenic trails, and beautiful campsites.
  • Lake Woodruff National Wildlife Refuge: This wildlife refuge is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and offers plenty of opportunities for hiking, bird watching, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of DeLand

If you’re looking for a way to explore the natural beauty of DeLand on foot or by bike, you’re in luck. The city offers several well-maintained trails that are perfect for both beginners and experienced hikers and bikers.

  • DeLeon Springs State Park Trail: This trail is an easy 4.2-mile loop that is perfect for novice hikers and bikers. The trail takes you through scenic forests and along the DeLeon Springs.
  • Seminole State Forest Trail: This trail is a challenging 15.2-mile loop that is ideal for experienced hikers and bikers. The trail takes you through dense forests and along the lake shore, offering incredible views of the surrounding landscape.

Best Things to Do in DeLand: Activities and Tours

There’s no shortage of fun and exciting things to do in DeLand. From exciting tours to unique dining experiences, here are some of the best things to do in the city.

  • Stetson Mansion Tours: Take a guided tour of this historic mansion and learn about its fascinating history and architecture.
  • Birds of Prey Show at Orlando-Melbourne International Airport: This exciting show features trained birds of prey and offers a unique experience for animal lovers.
  • DeLand Boat Tour: Take a guided boat tour along the scenic St. Johns River and see local wildlife and beautiful landscapes.

Food and Drink Scene of DeLand

DeLand has a thriving food and drink scene that is sure to please even the pickiest of eaters. Whether you’re looking for Southern comfort food or international cuisine, the city has plenty of options to choose from.

Must-Try Dishes

Some of the must-try dishes during your visit to DeLand include:

  • Shrimp and Grits: This Southern staple is a must-try dish in DeLand, and it’s available in many local restaurants.
  • Key Lime Pie: This tangy and refreshing dessert is a quintessential Florida dish that you don’t want to miss.
  • Barbecue Ribs: DeLand is home to some of the best barbecue joints in Florida, so make sure to try some finger-licking ribs during your stay.

Local Beverages

If you’re looking for a refreshing drink during your visit to DeLand, make sure to try some of the local beverages, including:

  • Craft Beer: DeLand is home to numerous craft breweries, such as Persimmon Hollow Brewing Co. and Central 28 Beer Co.
  • Wine: Check out the local vineyards and wineries like the Hontoon Island Winery that produce award-winning wines in the region.

Where to Stay in DeLand: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

DeLand offers plenty of accommodation options for every budget and preference. Here are some of the top places to stay in the city:

  • Artisan Downtown: This boutique hotel is located in the heart of downtown DeLand and features upscale amenities and stylish rooms.
  • Hampton Inn & Suites DeLand: This popular chain hotel is located in a prime spot and offers comfortable rooms and convenient facilities.
  • The DeLand Hotel: This charming guesthouse is housed in a historic building and features a quaint atmosphere and personalized service.

What to Buy in DeLand: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs and locally made products to bring home, DeLand has plenty of options to choose from. Here are some of the must-buy items in the city:

  • Stetson University Gear: Show your support for the local education institution by purchasing some gear or memorabilia.
  • Blooming Artisan: This store sells handmade pottery, ceramics, and glassware that are unique to the region.
  • DeLand Indie Market: This monthly market features handmade goods, art, and jewelry from local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of DeLand

DeLand is a culturally rich city that values its history and traditions. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and visitors are encouraged to explore the city’s local culture during their stay.

Best Time to Visit DeLand

The best time to visit DeLand is from December to April when the weather is comfortable and dry. This is also the peak season, so make sure to book your accommodation and activities in advance.

Do’s and Don’ts of DeLand

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your visit to DeLand.

Do:

  • Respect local culture and traditions.
  • Explore the natural attractions.
  • Try the local cuisine.
  • Take advantage of the city’s cultural offerings.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural attractions.
  • Disrespect locals or their property.
  • Ignore safety warnings or rules.

Safety and Health

DeLand is generally a safe city, but visitors should still exercise caution and obey safety rules and warnings. It’s also important to take care of your health during your stay.

Safety in DeLand

  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid walking alone at night.
  • Keep an eye on your belongings and avoid leaving valuables in your car.
  • Follow all safety rules and guidelines at natural attractions.

Health in DeLand

  • Stay hydrated during your stay, especially if visiting during the summer months.
  • Wear sunscreen and protective clothing to avoid sunburn.
  • Bring insect repellent to protect against mosquitoes and other pests.

Getting to DeLand: Transportation Options and Logistics

DeLand is easily accessible by several transportation options, including airports and bus stations.

Airports Near DeLand

  • Orlando International Airport (MCO) - 45 miles away
  • Daytona Beach International Airport (DAB) - 18 miles away

Bus Stations Near DeLand

  • DeLand Intermodal Transportation Facility
  • Greyhound Bus Station

How to Get Around in DeLand

DeLand is a pedestrian-friendly city with several options for getting around, including:

  • Walking or biking
  • Public transportation
  • Taxis and ride-sharing services
  • Car rentals

Where to Go Next from DeLand: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore the surrounding region, there are several nearby destinations and excursions worth checking out, including:

  • Daytona Beach for a beach day
  • St. Augustine, the oldest city in the United States
  • Kennedy Space Center for a behind-the-scenes tour of NASA facilities

Conclusion

DeLand is a beautiful and vibrant city that offers an ideal blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and modern amenities. Whether you’re a history buff, foodie, or outdoor enthusiast, DeLand has something to offer everyone.
