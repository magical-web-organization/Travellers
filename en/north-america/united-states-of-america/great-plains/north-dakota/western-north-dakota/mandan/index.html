Introduction to Mandan

Welcome to Mandan, a city located in Western North Dakota in the Great Plains region of the United States of America. The city is nestled alongside the mighty Missouri River and boasts an excellent blend of adventure, culture, and history. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll help you explore the best of Mandan, including top attractions, activities, hiking/biking routes, where to stay, local culture, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Mandan

There are many places to visit in Mandan, whether you’re interested in history, culture, or outdoor adventure. Here are the top destinations to put on your list:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fort Abraham Lincoln State Park: This park is a must-visit for history buffs and nature lovers. It features a reconstructed military fort, including General Custer’s home, and hiking trails along the Missouri River.

  • North Dakota State Railroad Museum: The museum boasts an impressive collection of historic train artifacts and a life-size replica of a Northern Pacific Railroad depot.

  • Bismarck Art & Galleries Association: This center hosts rotating exhibits of local, national, and international art and regularly hosts events and workshops for art enthusiasts.

Natural Attractions of Mandan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lewis and Clark Riverboat Cruise: This relaxing paddleboat offers stunning views of the Missouri River and Bismarck Mandan.

  • Mystical Horizons: Experience unique geological formations, lush flora, and diverse fauna on this stunning nature preserve.

  • Prairie Knights Casino and Resort: This is a great place to enjoy some casino entertainment and fishing in Lake Oahe.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mandan

Hikers and bikers alike will enjoy the scenic trails in Mandan. Here are some of the best hiking/biking routes in the area:

  • Fort Mandan Loop Trail: This historic trail offers a glimpse into the civilization of the past as visitors can see the reconstructed Fort Mandan.

  • Apple Creek Trail: This dirt trail weaves through the picturesque landscape of wooded stretches, prairie grasslands, and beautiful wetlands.

  • Dacotah Centennial Park Trail: This paved trail is a fantastic leisure walk with park benches, picnic areas, and fishing docks, all accompanied by the beautiful scenery of the river.

Best Things to Do in Mandan: Activities and Tours

Mandan offers a range of activities and tours for families, groups, and individuals. Here are the best things to do in Mandan:

  • River Rafting and Kayaking: Mandan is a great place for rafting and kayaking enthusiasts, and the nearby Missouri River offers a variety of water-based activities.

  • Horseback Riding: Take an idyllic horseback ride across the stunning North Dakota countryside.

  • Golfing: There are several high-quality golf courses with scenic views in and around Mandan.

Food and Drink Scene of Mandan

Expect a fantastic culinary experience in Mandan. From farm-to-table cuisine to steakhouses, you will enjoy delicious food dishes that are sure to tickle your taste buds. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Knoephla Soup: This is a savory soup dish also known as German Potato soup.

  • Cheese Buttons: These are dumplings made with flour dough and filled with a mixture of mashed potatoes and cheese.

  • Wild Game: North Dakota is known for its rich and flavorful wild game dishes, including elk, bison, and deer.

Local Beverages

  • Chokecherry Wine: This is a beverage made from the chokecherries that grow wildly in North Dakota.

  • Hi-Line Brewing Company: This brewery offers a rotating selection of local, seasonal, and unique beer options.

  • The Tavern Grill: Sip on a local beer while you indulge in delicious food at this upscale, casual dining restaurant.

Where to Stay in Mandan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Whether you’re looking for luxury hotels, budget-friendly options, or a charming guesthouse, Mandan has got you covered. Here are some of the best options to choose from:

  • The Baymont by Wyndham Mandan: This hotel offers a complimentary breakfast buffet, an indoor pool and hot tub, and a fitness center.

  • Sleep Inn & Suites: This hotel has an outdoor patio with firepits, a hot tub, a fitness center, and easy access to Lewis and Clark Riverboat tours.

  • Gwein’s Lodge and Trading Post: This lodge has a private setting and provides access to walking trails and the bike path.

What to Buy in Mandan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Looking for a unique souvenir to remember your visit to Mandan? Here are some local products to bring home:

  • A Bison Leather Product: North Dakota has a large bison population, and bison leather products are a popular item to bring back home.

  • Local Art: Explore the art scene in Mandan and bring home pottery, paintings, and ceramics made by local artists.

  • Scenic Postcards: Postcards featuring North Dakota’s beautiful countryside and landmarks are a classic way to remember your trip to Mandan.

Local Culture and Customs of Mandan

Mandan boasts a unique cultural heritage, shaped by the traditions of the Native American Mandan tribe and the pioneers who settled the area. Here are a few customs and cultural events to keep in mind when visiting:

  • Native American Powwows: These events are celebrations of Native American culture, featuring traditional music, dancing, and dress.

  • Wacipi: This word means “dance” in Lakota. The Native American tribes in North Dakota host several powwows throughout the year.

  • History Tours: Take a guided tour of the historic sites to learn about the culture and history of the state.

Best Time to Visit Mandan

The best time to visit Mandan is during the summer months between May and September. The temperatures are mild, and the weather is perfect for outdoor adventures and events. Fall also offers beautiful foliage and harvest festivals, while winter offers snow sports and holiday celebrations.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mandan

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your visit to Mandan:

Do:

  • Do explore the city’s natural attractions and parks.

  • Do try local dishes and shop for local products.

  • Don’t forget to pack sunscreen, bug spray, and a camera for your outdoor adventures.

Safety and Health

Ensuring your safety and health while traveling is crucial. Here are some safety and health tips to keep in mind during your visit to Mandan:

Safety in Mandan

  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially when you are out alone at night.

  • Don’t leave your belongings unattended in public places.

  • Use caution when exploring natural areas to avoid accidents.

Health in Mandan

  • Pack insect repellent to avoid tick bites and protect yourself from West Nile Virus.

  • Stay hydrated and avoid sunburn by wearing sunscreen and drinking plenty of water.

  • If you are traveling during the COVID-19 pandemic, make sure to follow the guidelines and restrictions set in place by the state and local authorities.

Getting to Mandan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here are the transportation options to Mandan:

Airports Near Mandan

  • Bismarck Municipal Airport: Located about ten minutes south of Mandan, this airport is home to several airlines and offers direct flights to various destinations.

  • Hector International Airport: Located about two-and-a-half hours east of Mandan in Fargo, this airport is another option.

Bus Stations Near Mandan

  • Jefferson Bus Lines: Services run from Minneapolis to Bismarck, with buses arriving at the Indian Hills Shopping Center, north of Mandan.

  • Greyhound: Greyhound has a station in Bismarck and has routes across the United States.

Other Options

  • Rental Cars: Renting a car is an excellent way to get around and visit the sights around Mandan.

  • Taxis and Ride-Sharing Services: Taxis and ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft are available in Mandan and provide a convenient way to get around.

How to Get Around in Mandan

Getting around in Mandan is easy with these options:

  • Walking and Biking: Many areas of Mandan are accessible by foot or bike, including downtown and the bike path.

  • Public Transportation: Mandan has excellent public transportation services. The Bis-Man Transit System offers bus services throughout the city.

  • Rental Cars: Renting a car is an excellent way to get around and visit the sights around Mandan.

Where to Go Next from Mandan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Mandan is an excellent starting point for exploring the stunning natural beauty of North Dakota. Here are some nearby destinations worth visiting:

  • Theodore Roosevelt National Park: This park is around a four-hour drive from Mandan and is home to beautiful badlands and abundant wildlife.

  • Medora: This charming historic town is a two-hour drive from Mandan and offers live music shows, horseback riding, and an excellent golf course.

  • The Enchanted Highway: This is a scenic drive consists of several large scale metal sculptures on the highway north of Mandan.

Conclusion

Mandan in North Dakota has a lot to offer visitors, from historic sites and natural beauty to great outdoor adventures and local cuisine. We hope our comprehensive travel guide has inspired you to plan your trip to Mandan and explore all the city has to offer.
