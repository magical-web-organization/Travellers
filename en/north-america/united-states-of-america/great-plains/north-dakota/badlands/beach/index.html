Introduction to Beach

Beach is a picturesque town situated in the Badlands of North Dakota in the United States of America. It is located in the Great Plains region, known for its striking landscapes, unique rock formations, and rich cultural history. The town is renowned for its natural beauty and scenic views, making it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. In this guide, we’ve curated a comprehensive list of things to see and do in Beach to help you plan your perfect vacation.

List of Places to Visit in Beach

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Beach has a significant cultural history with a range of museums and historical places. These include:

  • Golden Valley County Historical Society Museum: This museum displays the rich history of Golden Valley County, from Native American culture to the pioneers who settled in the region.

  • Prairie Fire Pottery: Here, you can witness firsthand demonstrations of hand-thrown pottery, which is an important and authentic Native American art form.

Natural Attractions of Beach: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Beach is endowed with a variety of scenic views and natural attractions, including:

  • Theodore Roosevelt National Park: This park is located in the Badlands and is known for its natural beauty, featuring stunning rock formations, valleys, and canyons.

  • Medora Musical: This celebrates life in the Badlands with a lively musical show that features the history and culture of the region.

  • Badlands Trail Rides: Get a unique view of the area with a guided horseback riding tour through the rolling hills and valleys.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Beach

Beach’s picturesque trails offer many hiking and biking opportunities through its scenic landscapes and diverse wildlife. These include:

  • Maah Daah Hey Trail: A 146-mile trail that winds through the Badlands, showcasing its panoramic vistas and unique terrain.

  • Sully Creek State Park: It offers varied trail systems that are suitable for both novice and experienced hikers and bikers.

Best Things to Do in Beach: Activities and Tours

Beach has an array of activities and tours that will give you an unforgettable experience in the Badlands:

  • Outdoor Adventure Tour: An off-road guided tour that provides a unique opportunity to explore the Badlands’ beautiful scenery and geologic wonders.

  • Air Tours: Take a scenic aerial tour of the Badlands and witness its unique terrains from a bird’s eye view.

Week-Long Itinerary for Beach

Here’s a recommended itinerary for a week-long trip to Beach:

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit the Golden Valley County Historical Society Museum

Food and Drink Scene of Beach

Must-Try Dishes

Here are some must-try dishes in Beach:

  • Knoephla Soup: A local favorite, this soup is made with dumplings and vegetables and is a hearty meal on a chilly day.

  • Bison Burger: The bison burger is another local favorite here in North Dakota and is composed of a mouth-watering patty of bison meat.

Local Beverages

When it comes to beverages, North Dakota has a vibrant beer scene, with craft breweries throughout the state. Some popular beers include:

  • Fargo Brewing Company’s Iron Horse Pale Ale

  • Bent Paddle Brewing Company’s Black Ale

Where to Stay in Beach: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Beach has a range of accommodation options that suit different budgets and preferences, including:

  • Rough Riders Hotel: Offering luxurious accommodations, this hotel is situated in the heart of Medora, and is close to many of Medora’s attractions.

  • Cottonwood Campground: A campground situated on the Little Missouri River, ideal for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers.

What to Buy in Beach: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Some of the best things to buy as souvenirs or gifts in Beach include:

  • Hand-crafted Pottery: Prairie Fire Pottery creates authentic and unique hand-crafted pottery, which is an important and authentic Native American art form.

  • Bison Jerky: Bison meat is a delicacy that is unique to the region and is delicious as jerky.

Local Culture and Customs of Beach

North Dakotans are hospitable, friendly, and are proud of their state and its unique culture. Visitors to the state are welcomed and treated with respect and kindness. It’s also important to note that North Dakota has a strong sense of community, with many people working together to support their neighbors and their town.

Best Time to Visit Beach

The best time to visit Beach is during the spring, summer, and fall months when the weather is mild, and outdoor activities are in full swing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Beach

Do:

  • Do dress in layers when visiting, as the weather can vary greatly during the day.

  • Do pack comfortable hiking shoes and other outdoor gear to make the most of your outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Don’t underestimate the power of the sun and the wind, and make sure to bring plenty of sunscreen and a hat.

  • Don’t leave your trash on the trails or in the National Park, follow strict ‘leave no trace’ principles to help preserve the natural environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Beach

Beach is generally a safe place to visit, but it’s essential to take precautions such as safeguarding your valuables and locking your car doors when parking.

Health in Beach

Beach is considered a safe destination from a health perspective. However, it’s advisable to pack insect repellant to combat insects and ticks, which can cause diseases.

Getting to Beach: Transportation Options and Logistics

Beach is accessible by several modes of transport:

Airports Near Beach

  • Theodore Roosevelt Regional Airport, Dickinson, ND

  • Billings Logan International Airport, Billings, MT

Bus Stations Near Beach

  • Beach Area Transit System

Other Options

  • Driving north from Bismarck or south from Minot on Highway 85

How to Get Around in Beach

Beach is a small town, and most attractions can be accessed by foot or bike. However, if you want to explore the Badlands further, a car or an SUV is recommended.

Conclusion

Beach is a stunning and unique destination that is brimming with natural beauty and character. The town is a must-visit for outdoor enthusiasts, and you’ll find plenty of exciting activities and attractions that will make your visit to Beach unforgettable.
