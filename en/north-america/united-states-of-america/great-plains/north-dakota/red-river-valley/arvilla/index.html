Arvilla Travel Guide: Explore the Hidden Gem of North Dakota

Nestled in the beautiful Red River Valley of North Dakota, Arvilla is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. This small town in the Great Plains region of the United States of America holds a charm that is unmatched, with its stunning natural landscapes, rich history, and warm hospitality. If you’re planning a trip to Arvilla, this comprehensive travel guide will give you all the information you need to make the most of your visit.

List of Places to Visit in Arvilla

Arvilla may be a small town, but it has plenty to offer visitors. Whether you’re interested in history, nature, or outdoor adventures, there’s something for everyone. Here are some of the best places to visit in Arvilla:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Arvilla Historical Society Museum: Housed in a restored 1910 schoolhouse, the museum showcases the town’s rich history through artifacts, photos, and exhibits.

  • Pioneer Heritage Center: Located on the University of North Dakota campus, the center features a historical village, museum, and research center dedicated to preserving the region’s pioneer history.

Natural Attractions of Arvilla: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Turtle River State Park: Just a short drive from Arvilla, this state park offers hiking trails, camping, fishing, and stunning views of the Turtle River.

  • Red River State Recreation Area: Another nearby state park, the Red River State Recreation Area offers beautiful views of the Red River, with opportunities for camping, fishing, and boating.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Arvilla

Arvilla is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, with plenty of hiking and biking trails to explore. Here are some of the best routes in the area:

  • Turtle River Trail: This 22-mile trail connects Turtle River State Park with the city of Grand Forks, and offers beautiful views of the Turtle River and surrounding countryside.

  • Greenway Trail: Located in Grand Forks, this 20-mile trail runs along the banks of the Red River, offering stunning views of the water and surrounding wildlife.

Best Things to Do in Arvilla: Activities and Tours

Looking for some fun activities to do in Arvilla? Here are some of the best:

  • Horseback Riding: The Turtle River State Park offers horseback riding trails, with guided tours available.

  • Fishing: With plenty of nearby lakes and rivers, Arvilla is a great place for fishing enthusiasts. Some of the most popular spots include the Turtle River, Red River, and Lake Arvilla.

Week-Long Itinerary for Arvilla

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Arvilla, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day Activity
1 Visit the Arvilla Historical Society Museum
2 Explore Turtle River State Park
3 Take a horseback ride in the park
4 Go fishing on the Red River
5 Visit the Pioneer Heritage Center
6 Bike the Greenway Trail
7 Relax on the banks of Lake Arvilla

Food and Drink Scene of Arvilla

Arvilla may be a small town, but it has a surprisingly diverse food and drink scene. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Knoephla Soup: This traditional German soup is a North Dakota favorite, with potato dumplings, vegetables, and sometimes meat.

  • Lefse: A traditional Norwegian flatbread made with potatoes, flour, and cream.

Local Beverages

  • Beer: North Dakota has a growing craft beer scene, with several local breweries producing unique and delicious brews.

Where to Stay in Arvilla: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Arvilla has a few options for accommodations, ranging from hotels to guesthouses:

  • Turtle River State Park Campgrounds: With both RV and tent sites available, this is a great option for those looking to stay in the great outdoors.

  • Holiday Inn Express & Suites Grand Forks: Located just a short drive from Arvilla, this hotel offers comfortable rooms and amenities.

What to Buy in Arvilla: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When in Arvilla, be sure to pick up some of these local products as souvenirs:

  • Wild Rice: A staple of the Great Plains region, wild rice is a nutritious and tasty grain that’s also a great souvenir to bring home.

  • Arvilla Honey: Locally produced honey that’s delicious on toast or in recipes.

Local Culture and Customs of Arvilla

Arvilla is a friendly and welcoming town, with a strong sense of community and pride in its history and heritage. Visitors should be respectful of the local culture and customs, and take the time to learn about the town’s rich history.

Best Time to Visit Arvilla

The best time to visit Arvilla is during the summer months, when the weather is warm and sunny, and outdoor activities are plentiful. However, fall is also a great time to visit, as the changing leaves and cooler temperatures make for a beautiful and scenic trip.

Do’s and Don’ts of Arvilla

When visiting Arvilla, here are some things to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.

  • Take the time to learn about the town’s history and heritage.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural resources.

  • Be disrespectful to locals or their property.

Safety and Health

Arvilla is a safe and welcoming town, with a low crime rate and friendly locals. However, visitors should still take precautions to ensure their safety and health.

Safety in Arvilla

  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially when hiking or biking in remote areas.

  • Keep valuables in a secure location.

Health in Arvilla

  • Be prepared for outdoor activities by bringing sunscreen, bug spray, and appropriate clothing.

  • Drink plenty of water to avoid dehydration during outdoor activities.

Getting to Arvilla: Transportation Options and Logistics

Arvilla is accessible by car or bus. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Arvilla

  • Grand Forks International Airport - 20 miles away

Bus Stations Near Arvilla

  • Greyhound Bus Lines - located in Grand Forks, 10 miles away

Other Options

  • Car rental services are available at nearby airports and in Grand Forks.

How to Get Around in Arvilla

The best way to get around Arvilla is by car. However, biking is also a popular way to explore the town and surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Arvilla: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to continue your North Dakota adventure, here are some nearby destinations to consider:

  • Fargo: The largest city in North Dakota, Fargo offers plenty of attractions, including museums, art galleries, and shopping.

  • Theodore Roosevelt National Park: Located in western North Dakota, this national park is home to stunning landscapes and diverse wildlife.

Conclusion

Arvilla is a small town with a big heart, offering visitors a chance to experience the natural beauty and rich history of North Dakota. Whether you’re interested in outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, or simply a peaceful getaway, Arvilla has something to offer. With this comprehensive travel guide, you’re ready to explore all that Arvilla has to offer.
