Introduction to Kenmare

Welcome to Kenmare, a charming town in North Dakota, located in the Lakes and Gardens region. Kenmare is a peaceful and beautiful place filled with natural wonders and cultural attractions. The town has a rich history and unique traditions that have been preserved for generations. In this comprehensive guide, we will take you through the best places to visit, exciting activities to do, and helpful tips on how to get around and explore this hidden gem of North Dakota.

List of Places to Visit in Kenmare

Kenmare is packed with attractions that will keep you entertained throughout your stay. Whether you are looking for a dose of history or want to get outdoors and explore the scenic beauty, Kenmare has something for everyone. Let’s take a look at some of the must-visit places.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kenmare Pioneer Museum: Take a trip back in time and explore the rich history of Kenmare and the surrounding region at this museum. The museum features exhibits on early settlers, Native American heritage, agriculture, and much more.

  • Kenmare Cemetery: This cemetery is not only a resting place but also a significant historical site. Many of the town’s pioneers are buried here, and you’ll be able to learn about their lives and contributions to the community by touring the cemetery.

  • Kenmare Opera House: Catch a show or watch a movie at this historic building, built in 1905. The Opera house has been beautifully restored and remains a cultural landmark of the town.

Natural Attractions of Kenmare: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Des Lacs National Wildlife Refuge: Explore the natural beauty of Kenmare and the surrounding region by heading to this wildlife refuge. You’ll be able to spot a diversity of birds and other wildlife, hike the beautiful trails, or enjoy a picnic by the lake.

  • Mikkelson Trail: This beautiful trail runs through Kenmare, offering breathtaking views of the town’s landscape. You can hike or bike the trail and enjoy the fresh air and stunning scenery.

  • Bowbells Dam: This dam is a popular spot for fishing and boating. You can enjoy a relaxing day by the water or take a rented boat out to explore the lake and its surroundings.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kenmare

Kenmare offers several hiking and biking trails that will take you through some of the town’s most beautiful natural landscapes. Here are some of these trails:

  • Mikkelson Trail: This trail is ideal for biking and hiking, and it takes you through the scenic beauty of Kenmare. The trail is 8.5 miles long and is well-maintained, making it accessible for beginners.

  • Turtle Mountain Scenic Byway: This 48-mile byway takes you through the Turtle Mountains, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. You can stop along the way and hike or bike the trails or enjoy a picnic by the water.

Best Things to Do in Kenmare: Activities and Tours

Kenmare offers various fun activities and tours that will keep you entertained throughout your stay. Here are some of our top recommendations:

  • Fishing at Bowbells Dam: Enjoy a relaxing afternoon fishing for trout or walleye in the clear waters of Bowbells Dam.

  • Kayaking on Des Lacs Lake: Rent a kayak and explore the lake’s stunning beauty. You’ll be able to spot various wildlife species and enjoy the peaceful sounds of nature.

  • Horseback Riding: Experience the beauty of Kenmare while riding a horse. You can enjoy a guided trail ride and explore the scenic views of the town and its surroundings.

Food and Drink Scene of Kenmare

Kenmare has a wealth of local dishes and beverages that you must try during your stay.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Knoephla Soup: This creamy soup is a local favorite in Kenmare. Made with potatoes, dumplings, and chicken broth, it’s warm, comforting, and a perfect choice for a chilly day.

  • Barbecue Ribs: Kenmare is known for its delicious Barbecue ribs, which are smoky, tender, and cooked to perfection.

  • Homemade Pies: Kenmare’s homemade pies are a must-try. Made with fresh berries and ingredients, these pies are a crowd favorite.

Local Beverages

  • Prairie Rose Meadery: Head to the Prairie Rose Meadery and try their delicious mead, made with locally grown honey and fruits. You can enjoy a tasting flight or a glass of your favorite mead.

  • Souris River Brewing: Try some of the best craft beer in Kenmare at Souris River Brewing. You’ll find a variety of unique flavors, from ales to stouts, a perfect choice for beer enthusiasts.

Where to Stay in Kenmare: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kenmare offers a variety of accommodation options that cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of our top recommendations:

  • Kenmare Inn: This cozy inn offers comfortable rooms with all the necessary amenities. Its central location makes it convenient for exploring the town’s attractions.

  • Cobblestone Inn and Suites: This modern hotel offers spacious and luxurious rooms with excellent views of the surrounding landscape. The hotel also has a restaurant and a bar, perfect for a relaxing evening after a day of exploration.

  • Kountry Korner: This charming guesthouse offers cozy and comfortable rooms with a shared kitchen and common room. Its location near the Mikkelson Trail makes it an excellent choice for hikers and bikers.

What to Buy in Kenmare: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kenmare offers a variety of souvenirs and local products that are perfect for bringing home as a reminder of your stay. Some of our recommendations include:

  • Handmade Quilts: Kenmare’s handmade quilts are a perfect addition to your home decor. These quilts are made with love and skill and can be passed down as a family heirloom.

  • Local Honey: Kenmare is famous for its delicious and locally produced honey. You can find a variety of flavors, from wildflower to clover, at the local markets.

  • Handcrafted Pottery: Kenmare’s local artisans produce beautiful and unique pottery that makes for an excellent gift or a souvenir for yourself.

Local Culture and Customs of Kenmare

Kenmare has a vibrant culture and history that is reflected in its festivals, traditions, and local customs. Some of the most significant cultural events in Kenmare include:

  • Kenmare Goosefest: Celebrated every September, this fun festival includes a parade, live music, food, and various other events that showcase the local culture.

  • Pioneer Picnic: Held every July, this picnic is a celebration of Kenmare’s pioneer history. You can enjoy traditional food, games, and demonstrations of pioneer skills.

Best Time to Visit Kenmare

The best time to visit Kenmare is between June and August when the weather is pleasant, and the region’s natural beauty is at its best. The town is also lively during this time, with various events and festivals taking place.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kenmare

Kenmare is a safe town, and visitors are unlikely to experience any significant safety issues. However, you should always take standard precautions, such as keeping your doors locked and valuables secure.
