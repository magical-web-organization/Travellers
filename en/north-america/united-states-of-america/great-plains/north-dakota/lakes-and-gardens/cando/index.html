Introduction to Cando

Located in the heart of North Dakota’s Great Plains, Cando is a picturesque city that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural attractions. The city is known for its vibrant art scene and warm hospitality, making it a popular travel destination among tourists. If you are planning a trip to this charming city, our comprehensive travel guide will help you make the most of your visit.

List of Places to Visit in Cando

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

McVille Dam State Recreation Area

McVille Dam State Recreation Area is a popular spot for fishing and boating enthusiasts. It also serves as a prime location for picnics in the park.

Dakota College at Bottineau Heritage Center

The museum houses a fascinating collection of exhibits, artifacts and photographs representing the regional Native American culture and history from the early settlers’ time to present day.

Roosevelt Park Zoo

Located in Minot, the Roosevelt Park Zoo is a perfect family outing. The zoo has over 200 animals and covers 100 acres.

Natural Attractions of Cando: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Pelican Point Landing

Pelican Point Landing is a perfect place to experience a serene outlook of the Cando Lake. The lake is perfect for paddle boating, canoeing and fishing.

Cando Area Wildlife Club

With over 2000 acres of land, the wildlife area is an ideal location to see native North Dakota wildlife in their natural habitat.

Sheyenne River Valley

A guided tour of the Sheyenne River Valley is a must-do when visiting Cando. The valley is the country’s longest wooded river valley and is an ideal site for open-air adventure activities such as canoeing, hiking and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Cando

Pelican Point Bike Trail

Pelican Point Bike Trail is a scenic riding and hiking trail that spans 5 miles. It is home to ample wildlife and expansive natural scenery along the way.

Goose River Trail

The Goose River Trail is a 6-mile multi-use trail ideal for hiking, running, and biking. It winds its way past dense mature woodlands and abundant wildlife.

Best Things to Do in Cando: Activities and Tours

Explore the Cando Farmers Market

Visitors can sample locally grown produce and experience the genuine hospitality of Cando’s friendly locals at the Cando Farmers Market located on Main Street.

Take a ride on the Soo Line Railway

The Soo Line Railway runs through Cando and offers an excellent opportunity for a ride into the surrounding countryside. The scenic railway ride offers a glimpse into the region’s rich history and breathtaking scenery.

Food and Drink Scene of Cando

Must-Try Dishes

Knoephla Soup

A traditional German dish that is popular in North Dakota, Knoephla Soup is a hearty stew with dumplings, potatoes, and sausage.

Bison Burger

Bison meat is indigenous to the Great Plains area and is a healthy, low-fat alternative to beef. Visitors can make a stop at Burgermaster for an authentic bison burger.

Local Beverages

Prairiewood Brewery

Prairiewood Brewery is a popular bar and restaurant that serves signature craft beers, and has live music nights.

Where to Stay in Cando

Windsor Hotel

Located in the heart of downtown, the Windsor Hotel is a historic spot that has been fully renovated while still preserving its original charm. The hotel has 28 rooms that are perfect for travelers who appreciate history and personalized services.

Lakeview Motel

The Lakeview Motel is located on the south shore of Cando Lake and is ideal for those looking for a peaceful, nature-inspired experience.

What to Buy in Cando

Cheese Curds

North Dakota’s cheese curds are a top seller for tourists, and it’s not surprising - they are absolutely delicious. Cheese lovers can find fresh cheese curds at Cando Farmers Market.

Local Art

Cando is known for its vibrant local arts scene, and visitors can purchase authentic works of art in one of the many art galleries located in the city.

Local Culture and Customs of Cando

While in Cando, visitors should be aware of the city’s strong historic influence, and respect the heritage in the region. It is also important to respect and follow local customs and traditions, and be mindful of the region’s weather conditions.

Best Time to Visit Cando

Cando experiences the best weather from June to August, and is an ideal time to enjoy the natural beauty of the Great Plains area.

Do’s and Don’ts of Cando

Do:

  • Try local cuisine, including Bison Burger and Knoephla Soup.
  • Respect the environment by adhering to no littering rules.
  • Embrace the unique art culture and purchase from galleries.

Don’t:

  • Ignore Cando’s rich cultural heritage.
  • Be disrespectful of locals or litter in public areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Cando

Cando is an overall safe place to visit, however, visitors are advised to be cautious when traveling in unfamiliar areas.

Health in Cando

Visitors should be aware of changes in altitude and weather, and stay hydrated as the Great Plains region can experience dry heat. Health care services are readily available in Cando.

Getting to Cando: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Cando

The nearest airport to Cando is the Grand Forks International Airport, located just under two hours away.

Bus Stations Near Cando

The Greyhound Bus Station is located in Devils Lake, which is around 25 miles from Cando.

Other Options

Taxis, rental cars, and ride-share services are also available to visitors looking to explore Cando and its surroundings.

How to Get Around in Cando

Renting a car or bike is the best option for visitors to travel conveniently and at their own pace. The walking trails provide a great way to enjoy the local areas, and local transportation is also available.

Where to Go Next from Cando: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Devil’s Lake

Devils Lake, located only 25 miles from Cando, is a popular tourist destination for outdoor enthusiasts with a vast amount of spiritual and panoramic beauty.

Grand Forks

Grand Forks, located 2 hours away from Cando, is a vibrant college town with a bustling downtown area, outdoor attractions, and historic museums.

Conclusion

Cando is a beautiful city that offers a wide range of attractions and activities for travelers. Its natural beauty, flourishing arts scene, and fascinating historical sites make it a must-visit destination for tourists. With this comprehensive guide, you can explore the charm of Cando with confidence and ease.
