Introduction to Howard

Located in the heart of the Flint Hills, Kansas, Howard offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. This picturesque town boasts a myriad of natural wonders, cultural attractions, and exciting outdoor activities. Whether you are a history buff, a nature lover, or an adventure seeker, Howard has something to offer for everyone. Explore the serene beauty of this charming town with our comprehensive travel guide.

List of Places to Visit in Howard

If you’re visiting Howard, these are the must-visit places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Chautauqua-Granada Theater: This beautiful theater has been recently restored, and it hosts multiple events throughout the year. It’s also one of the last remaining Chautauqua Theaters in the United States.
  • Elk Falls Pottery: Visit this artist-owned pottery studio and enjoy a unique shopping experience with a wide variety of handmade pottery.

Natural Attractions of Howard: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Cross Timbers State Park: Enjoy hiking, camping, fishing, and boating in this beautiful state park that covers nearly 1,000 acres of the shoreline of Toronto Lake.
  • Sand Hills State Park: This park offers a unique prairie experience with sand dunes, wildflowers, and scenic views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Howard

The beautiful natural landscapes of Howard offer great hiking and biking routes that allow you to immerse yourself in nature. Here are some popular trails:

  • Elk River Hiking Trail: This trail follows the Elk River and offers scenic views of the surrounding area. It’s a leisurely hike that is perfect for families.
  • Elk River Bike Trail: This route is great for biking enthusiasts. It’s a 15-mile trail that runs between Independence and Elk City Lake.

Best Things to Do in Howard: Activities and Tours

  • Fall River Lake: Enjoy swimming, boating, and fishing in this beautiful 2,500-acre lake that’s surrounded by rolling hills.
  • Flint Hills National Scenic Byway: This drive offers some of the best scenic views of the Flint Hills region.
  • Prairie Ridge Golf Course: This 9-hole golf course offers beautiful views of the surrounding hills.

Week-Long Itinerary for Howard

Here’s a suggested itinerary for visitors staying for a week in Howard:

Day 1 - Visit the Chautauqua-Granada Theater

Day 2 - Explore the Sand Hills State Park

Day 3 - Hike/bike along the Elk River Trail

Day 4 - Enjoy fishing and boating at Cross Timbers State Park

Day 5 - Take a scenic drive along the Flint Hills National Scenic Byway

Day 6 - Visit Elk Falls Pottery and enjoy a unique shopping experience

Day 7 - Relax and enjoy the beautiful scenery at Prairie Ridge Golf Course

Food and Drink Scene of Howard

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kansas City BBQ: This mouth-watering dish consists of slow-cooked meat coated with a sweet and tangy BBQ sauce. You can find some of the best BBQ restaurants near Howard.
  • Fried Chicken: This southern classic is available at many of the local diners and cafes in Howard.
  • Kansas-Style Chili: This is a unique regional take on chili that often includes cinnamon, allspice, and sometimes even chocolate.

Local Beverages

  • Kansas Craft Beer: Many local breweries offer some of the best craft beers in Kansas.
  • Kansas Wine: Kansas is known for its wineries, and you can taste some of the best wines in the region at Howard.

Where to Stay in Howard: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Here are some of the best accommodation options in Howard:

  • The Lodge at Chautauqua-Granada Theater: This beautifully renovated historic theater also offers overnight accommodation.
  • Kansas Bed and Breakfast: Stay in a cozy bed and breakfast and enjoy a traditional Kansas breakfast.

What to Buy in Howard: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Elk Falls Pottery: Buy some locally made pottery that will remind you of your visit to Howard.
  • Kansas Barbeque Sauce: Get a bottle of the tangy Kansas City BBQ sauce to bring back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Howard

Howard is a small, rural town that enjoys a slow-paced lifestyle. It’s a friendly and welcoming community that values its traditions and local heritage.

Best Time to Visit Howard

The best time to visit Howard is between April and October when the weather is mild, and outdoor activities are in full swing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Howard

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Explore the natural beauty of Howard.
  • Shop locally and support small businesses.

Don’t:

Safety and Health

Safety in Howard

Howard is a safe and friendly community, but visitors should practice common safety precautions such as locking your car and staying aware of your surroundings.

Health in Howard

There are no major health concerns in Howard. However, visitors should carry any necessary medication if required.

Getting to Howard: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Howard

  • Wichita Dwight D. Eisenhower National Airport (ICT): 103 miles from Howard.
  • Tulsa International Airport (TUL): 163 miles from Howard.

Bus Stations Near Howard

  • Greyhound Bus Lines: The closest stop is 30 miles from Howard.

Other Options

  • Car rental: A car rental is recommended, as there is limited public transportation available.

How to Get Around in Howard

The best way to get around Howard is by car. There are no public transportation options within the town.

Where to Go Next from Howard: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
