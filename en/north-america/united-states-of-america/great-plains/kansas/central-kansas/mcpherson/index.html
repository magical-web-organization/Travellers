Introduction to McPherson

Nestled in the heart of the Great Plains, McPherson offers a unique blend of outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, and small-town charm. From exploring museums and historical landmarks to hiking and biking scenic routes, McPherson is a picturesque destination for all types of travelers to enjoy.

List of Places to Visit in McPherson

If you’re planning a trip to McPherson, be sure to check out these top attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • McPherson Museum and Arts Foundation: Learn about local history and art at this museum, which features interactive exhibits and displays showcasing McPherson’s heritage.
  • Inman Historic Museum: Explore the historic Inman community and learn about its settlers and way of life.
  • The Stiefel Theatre for the Performing Arts: Catch a live show or concert at this beautifully-restored historic theater.

Natural Attractions of McPherson: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • McPherson Water Park: Cool off and have fun at this family-friendly water park, which features a lazy river, water slides, and more.
  • McPherson Valley Wetlands: Hike and explore over 4,000 acres of wetlands, prairies, and woodlands.
  • Turkey Creek Golf Course: Enjoy a round of golf while taking in scenic views of the surrounding countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of McPherson

When it comes to exploring the outdoors, McPherson offers a variety of scenic hiking and biking routes. Some top options include:

  • McPherson Trail: This 8-mile trail winds through the heart of McPherson and showcases the city’s natural beauty and historic landmarks.
  • Turkey Creek Trail: This 5-mile trail takes hikers and bikers through the Turkey Creek Valley, offering stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

Best Things to Do in McPherson: Activities and Tours

From cultural experiences to outdoor adventures, McPherson offers plenty of things to see and do. Here are some top activities and tours to consider:

  • McPherson Opera House Tour: Take a behind-the-scenes tour of this restored opera house, which boasts stunning architecture and a rich history.
  • Kansas Largest Night Rodeo: Check out this annual rodeo, which features bull riding, barrel racing, and other thrilling events.
  • Old Settlers Day Festival: Celebrate McPherson’s pioneer and settler heritage at this popular annual festival, which features live entertainment, food vendors, and more.

Week-Long Itinerary for McPherson

To make the most out of your week-long trip to McPherson, consider the following itinerary:

Day Activity
1 Explore the McPherson Museum and Arts Foundation
2 Hike the Turkey Creek Trail and visit the Inman Historic Museum
3 Relax at the McPherson Water Park
4 Go golfing at Turkey Creek Golf Course
5 Attend a show at the Stiefel Theatre for the Performing Arts
6 Take a tour of the McPherson Opera House
7 End your trip with a visit to the McPherson Valley Wetlands

Food and Drink Scene of McPherson

When it comes to dining and drinking, McPherson offers a variety of options to suit any taste. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to consider:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kansas BBQ: Whether you prefer brisket, ribs, or pulled pork, Kansas BBQ is a must-try for any meat lover.
  • Apple Cider Donuts: Made with fresh apple cider, these tasty donuts are a McPherson specialty.

Local Beverages

  • Kansas Craft Beer: Check out one of the local breweries in the area for a taste of the best craft beer in Kansas.
  • Prairie Fire Coffee: This local coffee shop offers a variety of delicious hot and cold drinks, including their famous Cafe Mocha.

Where to Stay in McPherson: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Whether you’re looking for a luxury hotel or a budget-friendly option, McPherson has plenty of accommodations to choose from. Some top options include:

  • Holiday Inn Express & Suites McPherson: This modern hotel offers comfortable rooms and a convenient location near top attractions.
  • The Gallery Bed & Breakfast: Stay in a beautifully-restored 19th-century home and enjoy a gourmet breakfast each morning.
  • Thunderbird Motel: This budget-friendly motel offers clean and comfortable rooms at a great value.

What to Buy in McPherson: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Looking for a unique souvenir to take home with you? Consider the following options:

  • McPherson Tartan: This unique tartan pattern was designed specifically for McPherson and makes for a one-of-a-kind souvenir.
  • Prairie Lavender Farm Products: Check out the products from this local lavender farm, including soap, lotion, candles, and more.

Local Culture and Customs of McPherson

McPherson is known for its friendly and welcoming locals, who are proud of their community and eager to share it with visitors. Here are some local customs to keep in mind:

  • Say “hello” to strangers: It’s common in McPherson for people to greet each other on the street, so don’t be surprised if someone says hello to you.
  • Respect local history: McPherson has a rich history and is proud of its heritage, so be sure to show respect when visiting museums and historical landmarks.

Best Time to Visit McPherson

The best time to visit McPherson is in the spring or fall, when the weather is mild and the outdoor activities are at their best. Summer can be hot and humid, while winter can get quite cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of McPherson

Here are some important do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting McPherson:

Do:

  • Take the time to explore the natural beauty of the area by hiking or biking the local trails.
  • Chat with locals and learn about the history and culture of the community.

Don’t:

  • Leave without trying some local cuisine, such as Kansas BBQ or apple cider donuts.
  • Neglect to show respect for the local customs and history of McPherson.

Safety and Health

When visiting McPherson, it’s important to stay safe and healthy. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

Safety in McPherson

  • Stay aware of your surroundings when hiking or biking the local trails, and be prepared for changes in weather conditions.
  • Keep an eye on your belongings and avoid leaving valuables in your car.

Health in McPherson

  • Use sunscreen and stay hydrated when spending time outdoors in the summer months.
  • Be prepared for allergies if you suffer from hay fever or other seasonal allergies.

Getting to McPherson: Transportation Options and Logistics

Whether you’re flying in or driving, here are some transportation options to consider when getting to McPherson:

Airports Near McPherson

  • Wichita Dwight D. Eisenhower National Airport (ICT)
  • Salina Regional Airport (SLN)

Bus Stations Near McPherson

  • Greyhound Bus Lines (Newton, KS)

Other Options

  • Car rental companies are available at both airports.

How to Get Around in McPherson

Getting around McPherson is easiest by car, although there are taxis and ride-sharing services available in the area as well.

Where to Go Next from McPherson: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to extend your trip and explore more of the area, consider these nearby destinations:

  • Hutchinson, Kansas: Explore the famous Kansas Cosmosphere and Space Center, or check out the local museums and shops.
  • Wichita, Kansas: As the largest city in Kansas, Wichita offers a variety of shopping, dining, and entertainment options.
  • Cimarron National Grassland: Enjoy outdoor activities like hiking, fishing, and wildlife watching at this natural wonder located just a short drive from McPherson.

Conclusion

With its picturesque natural beauty, rich history, and friendly locals, McPherson is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling through Kansas. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an adventure-filled trip, McPherson has something for everyone to enjoy.
