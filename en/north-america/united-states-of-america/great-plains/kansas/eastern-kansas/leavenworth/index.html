Introduction to Leavenworth

Nestled in Eastern Kansas lies the charming city of Leavenworth. Known for its rich history and scenic views, Leavenworth attracts visitors from across the globe. With a wide range of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, great food, and drinks, there’s something for everyone in Leavenworth.

List of Places to Visit in Leavenworth

Leavenworth offers plenty of attractions for visitors to explore. Whether you’re interested in learning more about the city’s history or enjoying the great outdoors, there’s something for you. Here are some top places you must visit in Leavenworth:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fort Leavenworth: Established in 1827, Fort Leavenworth is the oldest active military installation west of the Mississippi River. Visitors can explore the Fort Leavenworth National Cemetery, Frontier Army Museum, and Buffalo Soldier Monument.

  • Leavenworth County Historical Society Museum: This museum features exhibits on the county’s founding, growth, and development.

  • The Carroll Mansion Museum: Built in 1867, this restored mansion offers a glimpse of life in Leavenworth during the 19th century.

Natural Attractions of Leavenworth: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Leavenworth State Fishing Lake: This lake offers opportunities for fishing, boating, and hiking while enjoying the scenic views.

  • Leavenworth Outdoor Recreation: Enjoy kayaking, canoeing, camping, and other outdoor activities with Leavenworth Outdoor Recreation.

  • Riverfront Park: A perfect destination for family picnics and relaxation. The park offers great views of the Missouri River.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Leavenworth

Leavenworth is a popular destination for hikers and bikers, with beautiful trails snaking throughout the city. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Leavenworth:

  • Frontier Army Trail: This historic trail takes you on a 3-mile self-guided tour of Fort Leavenworth.

  • Riverfront Heritage Trail: This 3.5-mile trail is perfect for a scenic walk, pass by the beautiful Missouri River.

  • Leavenworth State Fishing Lake Trail: Hike through the natural prairie and woodland surroundings that surround this lake.

Best Things to Do in Leavenworth: Activities and Tours

Leavenworth has a wide range of activities for visitors to enjoy. Here are a few of them:

  • Glacial Hills Scenic Byway: This scenic driving route goes through the picturesque countryside, perfect for taking in the beautiful scenery.

  • Trolley Tours: Take a tour around Leavenworth with the trolley tours and learn about the city’s rich history while enjoying the picturesque views.

  • Wine Tasting at Holy-Field Vineyard & Winery: Experience wine-tasting at its finest with the Holy-Field Vineyard and Winery, a favorite spot for wine lovers.

Week-Long Itinerary for Leavenworth

Here is a week-long itinerary for exploring the wonders of Leavenworth:

Day Activity
1 Visit the Carroll Mansion Museum and indulge in local cuisine.
2 Take a trip to Fort Leavenworth, visit the Frontier Army Museum, and have lunch at the General Patton Restaurant.
3 Enjoy the great outdoors at the Leavenworth State Fishing Lake and go fishing or camping.
4 Hike the Riverfront Heritage Trail and enjoy the beautiful views of the Missouri River.
5 Go on a trolley tour of Leavenworth and learn about the city’s history.
6 Take a winery tour and indulge in wine-tasting.
7 Explore the city’s parks and take a stroll around Riverfront Park.

Food and Drink Scene of Leavenworth

Leavenworth has a unique food and drink scene that you definitely don’t want to miss out on. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Leavenworth Fling: A delicious dessert made with fluffy sponge cake, topped with chocolate sauce and whipped cream.

  • The Hangover Burger: A hearty burger made with bacon, eggs, and cheese served between two waffles.

  • Kansas City BBQ: A popular dish, often served with smoked beef brisket coated in thick barbecue sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Hernando’s Hideaway: Relax with local beer at Hernando’s Hideaway, a popular spot among locals and tourists.

  • Holy-Field Vineyard & Winery: Try local wine at Holy-Field Vineyard and Winery and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

  • Frontier Army Museum Gift Shop: This gift shop sells local sodas, including the famous Henry Weinhard’s Root Beer.

Where to Stay in Leavenworth: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hampton Inn Leavenworth: A comfortable and modern hotel, perfect for business travelers and tourists.

  • The Inn at 427: A cozy bed-and-breakfast in a historic neighborhood that offers a unique lodging experience.

  • Candlewood Suites: A great option for budget travelers looking for a comfortable stay with great amenities.

What to Buy in Leavenworth: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Looking for souvenirs to remember your trip to Leavenworth? Here are some ideas:

Local Culture and Customs of Leavenworth

Respect towards local culture is an important value to uphold in any destination you visit. Here are some cultural tips to keep in mind:

  • Neat dressing is encouraged.

  • A polite and friendly demeanor is appreciated.

  • Smoking is prohibited in public areas.

Best Time to Visit Leavenworth

April to October is the best time of year to visit Leavenworth. During the summer (June to August), temperatures are warm, perfect for outdoor activities. Winter (December to February) also has activities related to Christmas festivals and winter sports. However, the temperature gets quite cold in winter, so make sure to pack plenty of layers.

Safety and Health

Safety in Leavenworth

Leavenworth is a safe city, with a low crime rate. Generally, if you take the same precautions you would take when traveling to any city, you should have a smooth and safe trip. Keep an eye out for your belongings in busy areas to avoid being pickpocketed.

Health in Leavenworth

Like most US cities, Leavenworth has great healthcare facilities. Should you require medical attention, you can get it at the closest hospital, Saint John Hospital, located at 3500 S 4th St., Leavenworth, KS 66048.

Getting to Leavenworth: Transportation Options and Logistics

Leavenworth is easily accessible by air, train, or road.

Airports Near Leavenworth

The nearest airports to Leavenworth are:

  • Kansas City International Airport (MCI)

  • Wheeler Downtown Airport (MKC)

Bus Stations Near Leavenworth

Greyhound Lines provide bus services to Leavenworth, and the nearest station is:

  • Lawrence Greyhound Bus Station

Other Options

Leavenworth is also accessible by car, with frequent bus services, and taxis are also available throughout the day. Leavenworth is located by US 73, Kansas 7, and US 40 highways.

How to Get Around in Leavenworth

Leavenworth is a small city, and it’s easy to get around on foot. However, if you’re interested in exploring the outskirts of the city, consider renting a car or bike. Taxi services are also available throughout the day.

Where to Go Next from Leavenworth: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Leavenworth is a great starting point to visit other destinations, including:

  • Kansas City: Drive 30 minutes to reach the sprawling city of Kansas City, Missouri.

  • Lawrence: Drive 40 mins to reach Lawrence, home to the University of Kansas.

  • Weston: Just 15 mins north of Leavenworth lies Weston, a charming town that offers unique shopping and dining experiences.

Conclusion

Whether you’re interested in immersing yourself in the history of Leavenworth, enjoying outdoor activities or simply indulging in local cuisine, the city has something for everyone. So plan your trip to Leavenworth and explore this wonderful destination.
