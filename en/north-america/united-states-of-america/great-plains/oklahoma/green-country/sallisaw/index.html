Introduction to Sallisaw

Sallisaw is a charming town located in Green Country, Oklahoma. This quaint destination features a rich culture, beautiful natural scenery, and a variety of activities and attractions that are perfect for travelers of all ages. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing getaway or an exciting adventure, Sallisaw is the place to be.
