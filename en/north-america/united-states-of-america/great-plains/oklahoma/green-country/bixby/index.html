Introduction to Bixby

Bixby, located in Green Country, Oklahoma, is a picturesque city that perfectly blends small-town charm with modern amenities. Known for its vibrant culture, rich history, and breathtaking natural beauty, it has become a popular destination for travelers looking for an authentic experience in the heart of America.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take you through some of the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay while in Bixby. Whether you’re a history buff, foodie, outdoor enthusiast, or just looking for a relaxing getaway, Bixby has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Bixby

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Bixby Historical Society Museum - A must-visit for history lovers, the Bixby Historical Society Museum is dedicated to showcasing the city’s rich history. Here, you’ll see exhibits that highlight the pioneers who settled in Bixby, the oil boom that drove its growth, and the impact it had on American history.

  • Washington Irving Memorial Park and Arboretum - Named after the famous author, Washington Irving Memorial Park and Arboretum is a haven for nature lovers. With trails, gardens, and a lake, it’s the perfect spot for a picnic, a scenic walk or a bit of bird-watching.

  • Tulsa Air and Space Museum & Planetarium - Located just a short drive from Bixby, the Tulsa Air and Space Museum & Planetarium is a world-class attraction that explores the history of aviation and space exploration. As you journey through the galleries, you’ll see vintage aircraft, rockets, and artifacts from the world’s most significant moments in space travel.

Natural Attractions of Bixby: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ray Harral Nature Park - A hidden gem of Bixby, Ray Harral Nature Park is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts. With hiking trails, bird-watching opportunities, and scenic views, it’s the perfect place to spend an afternoon in nature.

  • Lake Bixhoma - This 1,200-acre lake is an outdoor paradise that offers swimming, fishing, and boating opportunities. With several picnic areas, it’s the perfect spot for a family day out or a romantic picnic.

  • Redbud Valley Nature Preserve - This stunning 300-acre nature preserve is a hidden gem of Bixby. With hiking trails, wildlife watching opportunities, and breathtaking waterfalls, it’s a natural wonder that must not be missed.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bixby

Bixby is a haven for hikers and bikers with miles of scenic trails that take you through some of the most beautiful natural settings in the area. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • Washington Irving Memorial Park Trail - A scenic trail that takes you through the park’s beautiful gardens and groves of redbud trees.

  • Ray Harral Nature Park Trail - A popular trail that offers stunning views of the lake and the surrounding area.

  • River Trail - This 22-mile trail takes you along the Arkansas River, offering stunning views of the water and the surrounding area.

Best Things to Do in Bixby: Activities and Tours

  • Tulsa Zoo - Just a short drive away from Bixby, the Tulsa Zoo is a world-class attraction that showcases an array of animals from around the world. With exhibits ranging from the African savannah to the Arctic tundra, it’s a must-visit for animal lovers of all ages.

  • Tulsa Art Deco Walk - Take a walk through time in Tulsa’s Art Deco district. Admire the stunning architecture, ranging from Georgian Revival to Gothic, then finish it up with a drink in one of the area’s many historic bars.

  • Crystal Bridges Museum of American Art - Located just outside of Bixby, Crystal Bridges Museum of American Art is a premier art museum that showcases some of the best American artwork of the past 400 years. With a collection that spans everything from colonial portraits to contemporary installations, it’s a world-class attraction that cannot be missed.

Food and Drink Scene of Bixby

Must-Try Dishes

  • Burgers from Freddy’s Frozen Custard & Steakburgers - Locals swear by the burgers at this popular chain, which features thin patties and crispy fries.

  • Steak at Cattleman’s Steakhouse - No trip to Oklahoma is complete without a steak dinner, and Cattleman’s is the perfect spot for your indulgence. This classic steakhouse has been serving up juicy cuts for over 100 years.

  • Fried Chicken at Mamma Lacy’s Soul Food Kitchen - Mamma Lacy’s soul food is a favorite among locals, and her fried chicken is the stuff of legends. If you’re looking for some real southern comfort food, this is the spot for you.

Local Beverages

  • Cherry Limeade from Sonic - Sonic, the popular drive-in chain, originated in Oklahoma and is famous for its cherry limeade.

  • Craft Beers from The Prairie Brewpub - This local brewery is known for its unique beers and homemade pizza. The cozy ambiance and friendly service make it a favorite among locals.

Where to Stay in Bixby: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bixby offers a wide range of accommodations, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. Some of the most popular options include:

  • Fairfield Inn & Suites Tulsa Southeast/Crossroads Village - This popular hotel offers comfortable rooms and easy access to popular attractions in the area.

  • The Campbell Hotel - A boutique hotel in Tulsa, The Cambell Hotel is a historic property that offers luxurious amenities and a fusion of modern and classical elements.

  • Aloft Tulsa Downtown - Another boutique hotel located in Tulsa, Aloft Tulsa Downtown is known for its stylish rooms, excellent service, and convenient location.

What to Buy in Bixby: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Pecans from The Peanut Shoppe - The Peanut Shoppe has been a Tulsa staple for over 100 years and is known for their delicious pecans.

  • Pottery from Ceramics Studio - Ceramics Studio offers a variety of handmade pottery crafts that are perfect for gifts or souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Bixby

Bixby is known for its deeply-rooted southern culture. Locals are friendly and welcoming to visitors, and tradition is highly valued in the area. When visiting Bixby, it’s important to respect the city’s traditions and customs.

Best Time to Visit Bixby

The best time to visit Bixby is in the spring or fall when the weather is mild, and the scenery is stunning. Summers can be hot and humid, while winters can be cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bixby

Do:

  • Do try some of the local cuisine and drinks.

  • Do pack comfortable shoes for hiking and walking.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter when hiking or enjoying nature.

  • Don’t forget to tip when dining out or using other services.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bixby

Bixby is a generally safe city, but as with any place, it’s essential to take precautions when traveling. Be aware of your surroundings, and don’t leave valuables unattended in public places.

Health in Bixby

Bixby has excellent medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics, making it a safe place to visit.

Getting to Bixby: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bixby

  • Tulsa International Airport (TUL) - Located just 20 miles from Bixby, Tulsa International Airport is the most convenient airport to fly into.

Bus Stations Near Bixby

  • Greyhound Bus Station - The Greyhound Bus Station is located in downtown Tulsa, just a short drive away from Bixby.

Other Options

  • Car Rental - Renting a car is a popular option for travelers who want to explore outside of Bixby.

  • Taxi - Taxis are available in Bixby, but they can be expensive.

How to Get Around in Bixby

The best way to get around in Bixby is by car, bike, or on foot. Car rentals are available at the airport and other locations in the area. Bixby is a bike-friendly city, with many routes dedicated to cyclists.

Where to Go Next from Bixby: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Tulsa, Oklahoma - Just a short drive from Bixby, Tulsa is a vibrant city that offers an array of cultural attractions, nightlife, and shopping opportunities.

  • Hot Springs National Park, Arkansas - If you’re looking for a bit of relaxation, consider a day trip to Hot Springs National Park in Arkansas. Known for its natural hot springs and bathhouses, it’s the perfect spot for some R&R.

Conclusion

Bixby is a charming city that offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. From its rich history to its breathtaking outdoor adventures, there’s something for everyone in Bixby. Whether you’re looking for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a solo adventure, this Midwestern gem is waiting to be explored.
