Introduction to Altus

Nestled in Great Plains Country, in the heart of Oklahoma, Altus is a charming city that offers a glimpse into rural America. This vibrant community is a great destination for those seeking an authentic taste of Oklahoma’s culture and heritage. Altus is renowned for its friendly people, rich history, and beautiful natural scenery, making it perfect for a weekend getaway or a longer vacation. Here is everything you need to know for an unforgettable trip to Altus.

List of Places to Visit in Altus

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Museum of the Western Prairie

If you are interested in the history of Western Oklahoma and the Great Plains, don’t miss the Museum of the Western Prairie. This museum features exhibits on the Native American tribes of the region, early settlers, and the military history of Altus Air Force Base. The highlight of the museum is the reconstructed homestead that allows visitors to experience what life was like in pioneer times.

Altus City Reservoir

The Altus City Reservoir is a popular spot for fishing, picnics, and enjoying the great outdoors. The reservoir is surrounded by beautiful natural scenery and offers plenty of opportunities for birdwatching and nature photography.

Natural Attractions of Altus: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Quartz Mountain Nature Park

Quartz Mountain Nature Park is a hidden gem that is worth the trip to Altus alone. This beautiful park features breathtaking panoramic views of the majestic Wichita Mountains. Visitors can enjoy hiking or biking the many trails, picnicking by the lake, or camping under the stars.

Altus Air Force Base

The Altus Air Force Base is an important part of the region’s history and a fascinating place to visit. Visitors can tour the base and learn about the history of the military in Oklahoma. The base also hosts an airshow every year, which is a highlight for visitors of all ages.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Altus

Baldwin Hills Trail

The Baldwin Hills Trail is a scenic hiking and biking trail that offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside. The trail winds through the Baldwin Hills, a series of low hills that are covered in grasses and wildflowers. The trail is suitable for all skill levels and is a popular destination for nature enthusiasts and outdoor adventurers alike.

Best Things to Do in Altus: Activities and Tours

Wine Tasting at Tidal School Winery

Take a tour of the Tidal School Winery in nearby Drumright, Oklahoma, and sample some of Oklahoma’s finest wines. The winery is housed in a historic school building that has been lovingly restored and is a unique destination for wine lovers.

Altus Speedway

If you are a fan of fast cars and adrenaline, don’t miss the Altus Speedway. This quarter-mile dirt track is the home of some of the region’s most exciting races and events.

Food and Drink Scene of Altus

Must-Try Dishes

Fried Catfish

One of the most popular dishes in Oklahoma is fried catfish, and Altus is no exception. Enjoy this delicious local specialty at any of the town’s seafood restaurants.

Chicken Fried Steak

Another classic Oklahoma dish is chicken-fried steak. This hearty dish is served up at many of Altus’ diners and cafes.

Local Beverages

Marble Brewery

Marble Brewery is one of Oklahoma’s best craft breweries and offers tours and tastings of its award-winning beers. Visit the tasting room and enjoy a cold pint or a flight of the brewery’s latest creations.

Where to Stay in Altus: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Best Western Altus

The Best Western Altus is a popular choice among travelers looking for comfortable and affordable accommodations in the heart of Altus. This hotel features spacious rooms, a swimming pool, and free breakfast.

Quartz Mountain Resort

For a luxurious stay in a stunning natural setting, check out the Quartz Mountain Resort. This resort offers beautiful rooms, a golf course, spa, and top-rated restaurants.

What to Buy in Altus: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Native American Crafts

Altus is surrounded by Native American tribes, and visitors can find beautiful handmade crafts at the Museum of the Western Prairie gift shop or local markets.

Oklahoma Barbecue Sauce

Oklahoma is known for its delicious barbecue, and you can take a taste of it home with you by purchasing local barbecue sauce at any of the town’s barbecue restaurants or specialty shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Altus

Altus is a friendly and welcoming community that takes pride in its Southern hospitality. Visitors are sure to feel at home in this charming town and will appreciate the laid-back pace of life.

Best Time to Visit Altus

The best time to visit Altus is during the spring and fall when the weather is pleasant, and the natural scenery is at its best. Summers can be hot and humid, while winters can be cold and snowy, so plan accordingly.

Do’s and Don’ts of Altus

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine, especially the fried catfish and chicken-fried steak.
  • Do take a tour of the Altus Air Force Base to learn about the region’s military history.
  • Do explore the great outdoors at Quartz Mountain Nature Park.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget to tip your server at restaurants.
  • Don’t litter in public parks or natural areas.
  • Don’t forget to bring sunscreen and insect repellent for outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Altus

Altus is a safe city, but visitors should take basic precautions like locking their cars and not leaving valuables in plain sight.

Health in Altus

Altus has several medical facilities, including a hospital, and clinics. Visitors should make sure they have adequate health insurance before traveling.

Getting to Altus: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Altus

The closest airport to Altus is the Lawton-Fort Sill Regional Airport, which is approximately 40 miles away. Another option is the Oklahoma City International Airport, which is approximately 120 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Altus

Greyhound offers bus service to Altus from major cities like Oklahoma City and Dallas.

Other Options

Renting a car is the most convenient way to get around Altus and the surrounding area. There are several car rental companies located at the Lawton-Fort Sill Regional Airport and in Altus.

How to Get Around in Altus

The best way to get around Altus is by car, but there are also taxi services available for those who don’t have a car or prefer not to drive.

Where to Go Next from Altus: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Wichita Mountains Wildlife Refuge

Just a short drive from Altus is the Wichita Mountains Wildlife Refuge, which offers stunning views of the Wichita Mountains and plenty of opportunities for hiking, biking, and wildlife watching.

Oklahoma City

Oklahoma City is the largest city in Oklahoma and offers a wealth of attractions, including the National Cowboy and Western Heritage Museum, the Oklahoma City Zoo, and the Oklahoma City Museum of Art.

Conclusion

Altus is a wonderful destination for those looking for an authentic taste of rural Oklahoma. From museums and historical sites to outdoor adventures and great food and drink, Altus has something for everyone. Plan your trip today and discover the charm of Altus for yourself.
