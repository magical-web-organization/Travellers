Introduction to Spiro

Spiro is a small town located in Choctaw Country in Oklahoma, on the banks of the Arkansas River. The town has a rich history, dating back to the ancient times. One of the most significant archaeological sites in North America, the Spiro Mounds, is located here. Spiro is a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. The town has many scenic views, outdoor adventures, historical sites, and a vibrant food and drink scene.

In this comprehensive travel guide to Spiro, we will explore the town’s best attractions, activities, and accommodations. Learn about the local culture and customs, the best time to visit, and important safety and health information.

Local Culture and Customs of Spiro

Spiro has a mix of cultures, and the people are known for their hospitality and warm personalities. The people of Spiro celebrate their traditions, and you may witness some of the unique customs while you visit.

Best Time to Visit Spiro

The best time to visit Spiro is from March to May and September to November. During these months, the weather is mild, and outdoor activities are pleasant. The peak tourist season is from June to August, and during this time, the rates of accommodation and travel tend to go up.

Do’s and Don’ts of Spiro

Do:

  • Respect the local cultures and traditions
  • Tidy up after yourself
  • Explore the natural wonders and cultural attractions of Spiro

Don’t:

  • Litter
  • Ignore the local customs
  • Disturb the wildlife. Spiro is a natural habitat for many animals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Spiro

Spiro is a relatively safe town, and there are no significant safety concerns to worry about. However, like any travel destination, one should take precautions and be aware of their surroundings.

Health in Spiro

There are no major health concerns in Spiro, and the town has a few clinics and hospitals to take care of any medical emergencies.

Getting to Spiro: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Spiro

The closest airport to Spiro is the Fort Smith Regional Airport, located about 30 miles from Spiro.

Bus Stations Near Spiro

The nearest bus stations are in Poteau and Fort Smith.

Other Options

One can also fly to Tulsa International Airport or Little Rock International Airport and drive to Spiro.

How to Get Around in Spiro

The best way to get around in Spiro is by car. Rental cars are available at the airports and in town. Bikes are also an excellent option for exploring the town, especially for hikers and bikers.
