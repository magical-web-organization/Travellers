Introduction to Enid

Enid is a city located in north-central Oklahoma, in the United States of America. Known for its vibrant arts scene, rich history and outdoor recreation activities, Enid is a popular destination for tourists from across the country. The city is located in the Great Plains and is in close proximity to several other interesting towns and cities, making it a great base for exploring this part of Oklahoma.

List of Places to Visit in Enid

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Cherokee Strip Regional Heritage Center

The Cherokee Strip Regional Heritage Center is dedicated to preserving the history and culture of the Cherokee Strip, which was once a part of the Indian Territory. Exhibits at the museum cover topics such as the land runs that took place in the region, the lives of pioneers and early settlers, and the Native American tribes that once called the area home.

Enid Symphony Orchestra

The Enid Symphony Orchestra is the oldest symphony orchestra in Oklahoma and has been entertaining audiences in Enid for over 100 years. The orchestra performs a variety of classical music concerts throughout the year, and the annual holiday pops concert is particularly popular.

Simpson’s Old Time Museum

Simpson’s Old Time Museum is a tribute to the rich history of Enid and Oklahoma. The museum has a collection of rare and unique antiques, including items from the American Civil War, World War I, and World War II. It also features a replica of a 1920s street scene, complete with a general store, schoolhouse, and train depot.

Natural Attractions of Enid: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Leonardo’s Discovery Warehouse and Adventure Quest

The Leonardo’s Discovery Warehouse and Adventure Quest is a hands-on science museum that allows visitors to explore different scientific concepts through interactive exhibits. The museum is particularly popular with families and has a range of exhibits focused on topics such as space, energy, and the environment.

Meadowlake Park

Meadowlake Park is a beautiful park in Enid that offers several options for outdoor recreation. The park has a large lake where visitors can fish, boat, or simply enjoy the views. There are also several walking trails, playgrounds, and picnic areas throughout the park.

Denny Price Family YMCA Trail

The Denny Price Family YMCA Trail is a 6-mile trail that runs through Enid and offers beautiful views of the surrounding landscape. The trail is accessible to walkers, runners, and cyclists, and there are several rest areas along the way.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Enid

Enid Bicycle and Pedestrian Trails

Enid Bicycle and Pedestrian Trails is a network of trails that runs through Enid and the surrounding area. The trails are suitable for both hiking and biking, and there are several different routes to choose from. The trails are well-maintained and offer beautiful views of the surrounding landscape.

Best Things to Do in Enid: Activities and Tours

Sunset Strip Car Cruise

The Sunset Strip Car Cruise is an annual event that takes place in Enid and is dedicated to classic cars and hot rods. The event includes a car show, live music, food vendors, and a parade of cars.

Gaslight Theatre

The Gaslight Theatre is a community theatre in Enid, Oklahoma that offers a wide range of productions throughout the year. The theatre is known for its high-quality productions and is a popular destination for both locals and tourists.

Food and Drink Scene of Enid

Must-Try Dishes

Chicken Fried Steak

Chicken Fried Steak is a popular dish in Oklahoma, and Enid is no exception. The dish is made from a tenderized steak that is coated in seasoned flour and then fried until crispy.

Barbecue

Barbecue is another popular dish in Oklahoma, and Enid has several great barbecue restaurants to choose from. A few local favorites include Jumbo’s Burger and BBQ, Rib Crib, and Swadley’s Barbecue.

Local Beverages

Prairie Wolf Vodka

Prairie Wolf Vodka is a local distillery located in Guthrie, Oklahoma, that produces high-quality vodka using locally sourced ingredients. The distillery offers tours and tastings, and their vodka can be found in many bars and restaurants throughout Enid.

Cabin Boys Brewery

Cabin Boys Brewery is a craft brewery located in Tulsa, Oklahoma, that produces a wide range of beers using locally sourced ingredients. Their beers can be found in many bars and restaurants throughout Enid.

What to Buy in Enid: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Oklahoma-shaped Pecan Brittle

Oklahoma-shaped Pecan Brittle is a popular snack food in Oklahoma and makes for a great souvenir or gift. The brittle is made with locally sourced pecans and is shaped like the state of Oklahoma.

Route 66 Memorabilia

Enid is located along historic Route 66, and there are several shops in town that sell Route 66 memorabilia, including t-shirts, mugs, and keychains.

Local Culture and Customs of Enid

Enid has a rich history and a unique culture that is heavily influenced by its location in the Great Plains. Visitors to the city should be respectful of local customs and traditions, including the importance of agriculture and the outdoors.

Best Time to Visit Enid

The best time to visit Enid is in the spring or fall, when the weather is mild and the outdoor activities are at their best. Summer can be hot and humid, while winter can be cold and snowy.

Safety and Health

Safety in Enid

Enid is a relatively safe city, but visitors should still exercise caution and be aware of their surroundings. It is always a good idea to lock car doors and keep valuables out of sight.

Health in Enid
