Introduction to Del City

Del City is a city in Frontier Country, Oklahoma, home to the Tinker Air Force Base, a major economic and employment center in the region. Despite being overshadowed by Oklahoma City, Del City has a unique charm and plenty of attractions to offer visitors. Known for its scenic views, outdoor adventures, and cultural significance, Del City is quickly gaining popularity as a must-visit destination in Oklahoma.

List of Places to Visit in Del City

If you’re planning a trip to Del City, make sure to add these attractions to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tinker Heritage Air Park: Located in Tinker Air Force Base, the Tinker Heritage Air Park is a unique attraction for aviation enthusiasts. You can explore over 20 aircraft, including helicopters, fighter jets, and bombers, all displayed in an outdoor park.

  • Oklahoma History Center: The Oklahoma History Center is a comprehensive museum that showcases the state’s rich history and culture, from prehistoric times to modern-day. It’s also home to the Oklahoma Hall of Fame, which honors outstanding Oklahomans who have contributed to the state’s development.

Natural Attractions of Del City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Stanley Draper: Lake Stanley Draper is an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts who enjoy activities like fishing, boating, and water skiing. The lake covers over 1,000 acres and is surrounded by scenic views of the Great Plains.

  • Hudiburg Chevrolet Center: The Hudiburg Chevrolet Center is a modern performing arts venue that hosts various events throughout the year, from live concerts to Broadway shows.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Del City

If you’re looking for hiking and biking routes in Del City, be sure to check out the following:

  • Del City Community Trail: The Del City Community Trail is a paved path that runs for three miles, connecting several parks and recreational areas in the city. It’s a great option for a leisurely bike ride or an afternoon jog.

Best Things to Do in Del City: Activities and Tours

  • Skydive Airtight: Skydive Airtight is a thrilling adventure that offers tandem skydiving experiences for visitors. You can enjoy a bird’s-eye view of Del City, Oklahoma City, and beyond from a 13,000-feet altitude!

  • Andy Alligator’s Fun Park & Water Park: The Andy Alligator’s Fun Park & Water Park is a family-friendly amusement park with over 20 attractions, including go-karts, mini-golf, bumper boats, and a water park.

Week-Long Itinerary for Del City

Day 1: Visit Tinker Heritage Air Park and Oklahoma History Center

Day 2: Go hiking/biking on the Del City Community Trail and enjoy the scenic views of the Great Plains

Day 3: Take part in a tandem skydiving experience with Skydive Airtight

Day 4: Explore Lake Stanley Draper and enjoy water activities like fishing, boating, and water skiing

Day 5: Visit Hudiburg Chevrolet Center for a live performance or show

Day 6: Spend the day at Andy Alligator’s Fun Park & Water Park

Day 7: Shop for souvenirs at local markets and prepare to leave Del City for another adventure!

Food and Drink Scene of Del City

Del City has several restaurants and bars that offer a variety of cuisines and flavors. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chicken Fried Steak: A Southern classic, chicken fried steak is a breaded steak served with mashed potatoes and gravy. You can find it at many local diners and cafes around Del City.

  • BBQ Ribs: Del City has several barbecue joints that offer mouth-watering ribs, brisket, and pulled pork. Be sure to try the smoked meat at Ray’s BBQ in Del City!

Local Beverages

  • Oklahoma Craft Beer: Oklahoma has several local breweries, including Prairie Artisan Ales, Coop Ale Works, and Anthem Brewing Company. You can try their signature beers at local bars and taverns around Del City.

Where to Stay in Del City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Del City has several accommodation options that cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best options:

  • Hampton Inn Oklahoma City I-40 East/ Tinker AFB: A modern hotel located near Tinker Air Force Base, with complimentary hot breakfast and indoor pool and fitness center.

  • Best Western Plus Midwest City Inn & Suites: A cozy hotel with an indoor pool and hot tub, located near several shopping and dining options.

What to Buy in Del City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Tinker Air Force Base Souvenirs: Tinker Air Force Base has various souvenir shops that sell unique merchandise like aircraft models, patches, and clothing items.

Local Culture and Customs of Del City

Del City is a friendly and welcoming community, with a rich military heritage. It’s important to respect the service men and women that serve at Tinker Air Force Base and to show appreciation for their sacrifices and contributions to the local economy.

Best Time to Visit Del City

The best time to visit Del City is in the spring or fall when the weather is mild and pleasant. Summers in Del City can be hot and humid, and winters can be cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Del City

Do:

  • Respect the local military community and their contributions to the city
  • Enjoy the scenic views and outdoor adventures that Del City has to offer
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the military community or the Tinker Air Force Base
  • Litter or damage natural attractions and parks
  • Engage in illegal or reckless behavior

Safety and Health

Safety in Del City

Del City is a safe city, with low crime rates and a friendly community. As with any city, though, it’s important to be aware of your surroundings and take common-sense safety precautions.

Health in Del City

Del City has several medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics, in case of emergency.

Getting to Del City: Transportation Options and Logistics

Del City is located just east of Oklahoma City and can be easily accessed by several transportation options, including:

Airports Near Del City

  • Will Rogers World Airport Located in Oklahoma City, Will Rogers World Airport is the closest airport to Del City, just a 20-minute drive away.

Bus Stations Near Del City

  • Greyhound Bus Station The Greyhound Bus Station is located in downtown Oklahoma City and offers bus services to several destinations, including Del City.

Other Options

  • Car Rental Services Several car rental services are available at Will Rogers World Airport and downtown Oklahoma City, providing transportation to Del City and beyond.

How to Get Around in Del City

Del City is a small city that can easily be explored on foot, bike, or car. Local taxis and rideshare services like Uber and Lyft are also available, providing easy transportation to different attractions and destinations in the city.

Where to Go Next from Del City: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Del City is located near several other attractions and destinations, including:

  • Oklahoma City: Just a 10-minute drive away from Del City, Oklahoma City is home to several museums, restaurants, and shopping centers.

  • Midwest City: Located just east of Del City, Midwest City is home to several parks, shopping centers, and the Rose State College.

Conclusion

Del City is a hidden gem in the Great Plains region of the United States, with a unique blend of culture, history, and outdoor adventures. From exploring the Tinker Heritage Air Park to hiking and biking the Del City Community Trail, there’s plenty to see and do in Del City. With this comprehensive travel guide, you’re ready to plan the ultimate Del City adventure!
