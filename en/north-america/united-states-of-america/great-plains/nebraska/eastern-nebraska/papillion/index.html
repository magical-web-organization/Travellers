Introduction to Papillion

Located in Eastern Nebraska, in the Great Plains region of the United States, Papillion is a charming city with a population of over 20,000. The city’s name comes from the French word for “butterfly.” Papillion is known for its parks, trails, and friendly community. Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, history buff, or foodie, there’s something for everyone to enjoy in Papillion.

List of Places to Visit in Papillion

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sarpy County Museum: This museum showcases the history of Sarpy County and features various exhibits, including a 1900’s classroom and a replica train station.

  • Papillion Historical Society: The Papillion Historical Society is dedicated to preserving and sharing the history of Papillion. They offer guided tours of historic homes and buildings.

  • Gerald R. Ford Birthsite and Gardens: The 38th U.S. President, Gerald R. Ford, was born in Papillion. His birthplace has been turned into a park and features various monuments and gardens.

Natural Attractions of Papillion: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Schwer Park: A beautiful park with scenic walking trails and playgrounds.

  • Prairie Queen Recreation Area: This recreation area is a popular spot for fishing and boating. It also has several trails for hiking and biking.

  • Walnut Creek Lake and Recreation Area: This lake offers swimming, boating, and fishing opportunities. It has several hiking trails and picnic areas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Papillion

  • Papio Creek Trail: This trail is over 17 miles long and runs through several parks in Papillion. It’s a great trail for biking, jogging, and walking.

  • Prairie Queen Trail: This paved trail is four miles long and runs through the Prairie Queen Recreation Area. It’s a great trail for biking and walking.

Best Things to Do in Papillion: Activities and Tours

  • Papillion Days: This annual festival takes place in June and features live music, food, and various activities for the whole family.

  • SumTur Amphitheater: This outdoor amphitheater hosts various events throughout the year, including concerts and movie nights.

  • Golfing: There are several golf courses in Papillion, including the Eagle Hills Golf Course and the Tara Hills Golf Course.

Food and Drink Scene of Papillion

Must-Try Dishes

  • Runza Sandwich: This famous Nebraska sandwich is made with ground beef, onions, and cabbage, all wrapped in homemade bread.

  • Reuben Sandwich: Though disputed, some believe the Reuben sandwich was created in Omaha. This sandwich is made with corned beef, Swiss cheese, sauerkraut, and Thousand Island dressing.

Local Beverages

  • Kinkaider Brewing Company: This local brewery offers a variety of craft beers, including IPAs, stouts, and ales.

  • Nebraska Brewing Company: Another local brewery, this one offers a wide selection of craft beers and pub food.

Where to Stay in Papillion: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hampton Inn & Suites: This hotel offers spacious rooms and suites and is located near several attractions in Papillion.

  • Courtyard by Marriott: Located near the SumTur Amphitheater, this hotel offers comfortable rooms and a convenient location.

What to Buy in Papillion: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Nebraska Cornhuskers merchandise: The Nebraska Cornhuskers are a beloved local college football team. Bring home a t-shirt or hat to support the team.

  • Nebraska-made jams and jellies: Several local shops offer jams and jellies made from Nebraska-grown fruits.

Local Culture and Customs of Papillion

Papillion is a friendly community that values its parks and trails. It’s not uncommon to see locals out walking or biking on a nice day. In the fall, football games are a popular pastime, and the community comes together to support their local teams.

Best Time to Visit Papillion

The best time to visit Papillion is during the spring or fall when the weather is mild and there are fewer crowds. However, if you’re a fan of outdoor activities, summer is also a great time to visit.

Do’s and Don’ts of Papillion

Do:

  • Use the parks and trails: Papillion has several beautiful parks and trails that are open year-round.

  • Try a Runza sandwich: This local sandwich is a must-try for any visitor.

Don’t:

  • Forget sunscreen and insect repellant: With the outdoor activities available, it’s essential to protect yourself from the sun and insects.

Safety and Health

Safety in Papillion

Papillion is generally a safe community, but it’s always important to take precautions when traveling. Lock your car doors, keep valuable items out of sight, and be aware of your surroundings.

Health in Papillion

Papillion has several medical facilities and pharmacies, including CHI Health Midlands Hospital and CVS Pharmacy.

Getting to Papillion: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Papillion

  • Omaha Eppley Airfield: This major airport is located about 20 miles from Papillion.

  • Lincoln Airport: This regional airport is located about 60 miles from Papillion.

Bus Stations Near Papillion

  • Omaha Greyhound Station: Located about 15 miles from Papillion, this bus station offers service to several major cities in the region.

Other Options

  • Car rental: Several car rental companies, including Budget and Enterprise, offer services at Omaha Eppley Airfield.

How to Get Around in Papillion

The best way to get around in Papillion is by car. However, if you’d like to explore the city on foot or bike, several trails and sidewalks make it easy to do so.

Where to Go Next from Papillion: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Omaha: Papillion is located only a few miles from Omaha, a major city in Nebraska with several cultural attractions, including the Henry Doorly Zoo and Aquarium.

  • Lincoln: Lincoln, the capital of Nebraska, is located about 60 miles from Papillion and offers several museums and cultural attractions.

Conclusion

Papillion, Nebraska, is a lovely city with a strong sense of community and plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy. Whether you’re interested in history, hiking, or food, Papillion has something for everyone. Plan a trip to Papillion today, and discover all that this charming city has to offer.
