Introduction to Holdrege

Holdrege, located in South Central Nebraska, is a city full of surprises. Offering something for everyone, Holdrege is a blend of rich history and exciting modern-day attractions. Explore museums and historical places, scenic parks and hiking trails, local cuisine, and much more. This city, situated in the heart of the Great Plains, is sure to leave visitors with unforgettable memories. In this travel guide, we will take you through everything you need to know about Holdrege to plan your perfect trip.

List of Places to Visit in Holdrege

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Holdrege is proud of its heritage and offers plenty of cultural attractions for visitors to explore. Here are some of the must-visit museums and historical sites:

  1. Nebraska Prairie Museum - This museum showcases life on the Great Plains over the years and houses exhibits on old cars, farm equipment, and even a one-room schoolhouse.
  2. Holdrege Area Public Library and Heritage Center - This library displays various exhibits and collections of the history and heritage of Holdrege and the surrounding area.
  3. Phelps County Historical Society Museum - This museum tells the story of the county’s history through its artefacts and displays that feature the early pioneers and the everyday lives of the locals.

Natural Attractions of Holdrege: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you’re looking for a break in nature, Holdrege has got you covered. Here are some natural attractions that are worth visiting:

  1. Brewster Park - This park features a small lake that’s perfect for fishing, swimming, and boating. There are also picnic shelters and playgrounds for kids.
  2. Southside Park - This park is a favourite spot for jogging, walking, and cycling. It features a half-mile paved trail that winds through the park’s wooded area and along the Sandy Creek.
  3. Davis Creek Recreation Area - This park offers hiking and biking trails, campsites, pond fishing opportunities, as well as paddle boats and kayaking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Holdrege

Holdrege’s natural beauty can best be explored through its hiking and biking trails. Here are some popular routes:

  1. Davis Creek Trail - The trail runs along the creek for almost two miles, providing a beautiful view of the surrounding hills and the creek.
  2. Southside Park Trail - This trail offers a pleasant walk through the wooded area in the Southside Park.
  3. Holdrege Bike Route - This twelve-mile trail offers a scenic view of the countryside and is a fun way to see the area’s farms and wildlife.

Best Things to Do in Holdrege: Activities and Tours

Holdrege has something for everyone, from exciting outdoor activities to leisurely tours of its unique attractions. Here are some ideas to get you started:

  1. Visit the local museums and historical places.
  2. Take a walk along the scenic parks and nature trails.
  3. Go fishing or boating at Brewster Lake.
  4. Try the local cuisine at one of the city’s many restaurants.
  5. Attend a local event, such as the Phelps County Fair or the Annual Iron Horse Festival.

Food and Drink Scene of Holdrege

Holdrege offers a variety of local cuisine, ranging from traditional to modern. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Nebraska-style Runza Sandwich - This sandwich is a traditional dish of the region and consists of a baked roll filled with ground beef, onion, and cabbage.
  2. Kool-Aid Pie - This dish is a sweet and tangy dessert that’s made with Kool-Aid powder, cream cheese, and whipped cream.
  3. Corn on the Cob - Nebraska is famous for its Golden Corn, which is rich and flavourful.

Local Beverages

  1. Kool-Aid - This flavoured drink was invented in Hastings, Nebraska, and is available in many flavours in Holdrege.
  2. Nebraska Wines - Nebraska offers a variety of unique wines produced in local vineyards.

Local Culture and Customs of Holdrege

Holdrege is a friendly and welcoming city that is proud of its local culture and customs. Residents value hard work, community, and a simple way of life.

Best Time to Visit Holdrege

Holdrege experiences warm summers and cold winters, so the best time to visit is from May to September. During this time, the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities and sightseeing.
