Introduction to Wallace

Welcome to Wallace, a charming small town located in South Central Nebraska. Despite its size, there is plenty to see and do here, from exploring museums and historical sites to enjoying outdoor adventures. Whether you’re a history buff, nature lover, or simply looking for a quiet getaway, Wallace has something for everyone. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take you through the top attractions, hiking and biking routes, must-try dishes, where to stay, and much more.

Week-Long Itinerary for Wallace

Day 1: Visit the Wallace W. Waterman Buffalo Interpretive Center and explore the Lincoln County Museum.

Day 2: Take a hike on the Fort McPherson Trail or enjoy a round of golf at the Valentine Course.

Day 3: Head to the Enders Reservoir State Recreation Area for fishing, boating, and hiking.

Day 4: Have a picnic at Riverside Park and enjoy the scenic views.

Day 5: Visit the Sutherland Reservoir State Recreation Area and go kayaking or paddleboarding.

Day 6: Take a tour of the Lincoln Highway and explore the historic landmarks.

Day 7: Relax and enjoy the local food scene or venture to nearby attractions.
