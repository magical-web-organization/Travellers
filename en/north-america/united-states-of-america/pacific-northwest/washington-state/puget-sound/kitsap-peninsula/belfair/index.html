Introduction to Belfair

Located in Kitsap Peninsula, in Puget Sound, Belfair is a beautiful small town in Washington state that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and cultural attractions. Surrounded by stunning forests, lakes, and mountains, Belfair is an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts, nature lovers, and those looking for a peaceful getaway. Here’s everything you need to know to plan the perfect trip to Belfair.

List of Places to Visit in Belfair

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Log Cabin Museum: This small museum features exhibits on the history of the town, including the early pioneers, area industries, and the first log cabin built in 1888.

  • Hood Canal Ranger Station: This historical building was constructed in 1925 and serves as a great source of information on the history of the Hood Canal area and the surrounding forests.

Natural Attractions of Belfair: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Twanoh State Park: This 182-acre state park features a beautiful sandy beach, picnic areas, and a playground. It’s also a great spot for swimming, fishing, and hiking.

  • Belfair State Park: This 119-acre state park is located on Hood Canal and features several hiking trails, picnic areas, a boat ramp, and a popular swimming area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Belfair

  • Gold Creek Trail: This 5.5-mile-long trail is a popular destination for hikers and mountain bikers. It offers stunning views of the Olympic Mountains and passes through old-growth forests.

  • Green Mountain State Forest: This vast forest features over 20 miles of trails for hiking, mountain biking, and equestrian use.

Best Things to Do in Belfair: Activities and Tours

  • Boating and Fishing: Hood Canal is a great spot for boating and fishing. Rent a boat or bring your own and explore the beautiful waters of the canal.

  • Oyster and Clam Digging: Belfair is famous for its oysters and clams. Head to the beach and dig for your own, or visit one of the local restaurants and try them prepared in various ways.

Food and Drink Scene of Belfair

Must-Try Dishes

  • Oysters: Belfair is known for its delicious oysters. Try them raw, grilled, or fried.

  • Seafood Chowder: This local specialty is a creamy soup made with clams, mussels, salmon, and other local seafood.

Local Beverages

  • Hazelnut Coffee: Belfair is known for its hazelnut coffee. Stop by one of the local cafes and give it a try.

  • Local Beer and Wine: Belfair has a growing craft beer and wine scene. Visit one of the local breweries or wineries and sample the local brews.

Where to Stay in Belfair: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Robin Hood Village Resort: This charming resort features cozy cabins and cottages set amid beautiful gardens and tall trees.

  • Alderbrook Resort & Spa: This luxurious waterfront resort features a spa, a restaurant, and elegant rooms with stunning views of the Hood Canal.

What to Buy in Belfair: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handmade crafts: Belfair has a strong craft community. Stop by one of the local shops and buy a unique handmade item as a souvenir.

  • Oyster and Clam Shells: Bring home a piece of Belfair by collecting oyster and clam shells from the beach.

Local Culture and Customs of Belfair

Belfair has a relaxed and friendly culture. It’s common for locals to greet each other on the street, and visitors will find the community welcoming and helpful.

Best Time to Visit Belfair

The best time to visit Belfair is between May and September when the weather is warm and sunny. However, Belfair is a year-round destination and offers some unique experiences during the off-season too.

Do’s and Don’ts of Belfair

Do:

  • Do explore the local state parks and nature reserves.

  • Do try the fresh seafood and locally-brewed beer.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or pollute the local lakes, rivers, and beaches.

  • Don’t disturb the wildlife or the local ecosystem.

Safety and Health

Safety in Belfair

Belfair is a safe destination with a low crime rate. However, visitors should take normal safety precautions and be aware of their surroundings, especially when hiking or exploring remote areas.

Health in Belfair

Belfair has several medical facilities, including hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies, in case of emergency.

Getting to Belfair: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Belfair

  • Seattle-Tacoma International Airport: Located 77 miles southeast of Belfair, it’s the closest major airport.

Bus Stations Near Belfair

  • Olympia Transit Center: From here, visitors can take a bus to Belfair.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Renting a car is the best way to get around Belfair and the surrounding areas, as there are several car rental companies available.

How to Get Around in Belfair

The best way to get around Belfair is by car, bike, or foot. Belfair is a small town, and most attractions and accommodations are located within a short distance of each other.

Where to Go Next from Belfair: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Belfair is located in a great spot to explore some of the nearby attractions, including:

  • Olympic National Park: Located just a short drive from Belfair, Olympic National Park is a stunning natural wonder and a must-visit destination.

  • Seattle: Just a little over an hour’s drive from Belfair, Seattle offers a myriad of attractions, including the Space Needle, Pike Place Market, and the Seattle Aquarium.

Conclusion

From the stunning natural beauty to the rich cultural history, Belfair has something for every type of traveler. Plan your perfect trip to Belfair today and experience the best of the Pacific Northwest.
