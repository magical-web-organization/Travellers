Introduction to Mountlake Terrace

Located in the North Sound region of Puget Sound in Washington state, Mountlake Terrace is a peaceful and charming suburb of Seattle. Its beautiful natural landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture make it an ideal destination for tourists looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the city and immerse themselves in the beauty of the Pacific Northwest.

List of Places to Visit in Mountlake Terrace

Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor enthusiast, or a foodie, Mountlake Terrace has something to offer for everyone. Here are the must-visit attractions in Mountlake Terrace that you should add to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Mountlake Terrace Historical Society: A small museum that showcases the history and culture of Mountlake Terrace through photographs, artifacts, and exhibits.

  • Veterans Memorial Park: A peaceful park that pays tribute to the brave men and women who have served in the military with memorials and displays.

  • Lake Ballinger: A scenic lake that offers fishing, kayaking, and other water activities, surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes.

Natural Attractions of Mountlake Terrace: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Cedar Way Trail: A picturesque trail that stretches for 1.6 miles and offers stunning views of Mount Rainier, lush forests, and serene wetlands.

  • Mountlake Terrace Disc Golf Course: A popular disc golf course that features 18 holes and beautiful surroundings for players of all skill levels.

  • Nile Shrine Golf Course: An 18-hole golf course surrounded by stunning natural landscapes and offering a challenging game.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mountlake Terrace

If you’re looking for an outdoor adventure, Mountlake Terrace offers plenty of hiking and biking trails that cater to all levels of experience. Here are some of the best routes that you should explore:

  • Lake Ballinger Loop Trail: A 2-mile loop around Lake Ballinger that offers stunning views of the lake and surrounding natural landscapes.

  • Interurban Trail: A 14-mile trail that connects the cities of Everett and Seattle, passing through Mountlake Terrace and offering beautiful sights of forests, hills, and streams.

  • Mountlake Terrace Park to Meadowdale Beach Park Trail: A 3.1-mile trail that takes you through lush forests, beautiful wetlands, and quiet beaches, offering an immersive outdoor experience.

Best Things to Do in Mountlake Terrace: Activities and Tours

From cultural attractions to outdoor adventures, Mountlake Terrace has plenty of things to do for tourists looking for a fun and exciting experience. Here are some of the best activities and tours in Mountlake Terrace to add to your itinerary:

  • Cedar Way Elementary Farmers Market: A weekly farmers market that offers fresh produce, handcrafted goods, and local food vendors.

  • Popcorn at the Pond: A fun event that takes place at Terrace Creek Park, where you can enjoy a free popcorn and watch a movie under the stars.

  • Edmonds Arts Festival: An annual event that takes place in the neighboring city of Edmonds and features local artists, live music, and delicious food.

Food and Drink Scene of Mountlake Terrace

Mountlake Terrace offers a diverse food and drink scene that showcases the local flavors and culture of the region. Here are the must-try dishes and local beverages that you should sample:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Seafood Chowder: A hearty and flavorful soup that features fresh seafood, vegetables, and herbs, served in most local restaurants.

  • Grilled Salmon: A Pacific Northwest favorite, grilled salmon is served with a variety of sides, such as rice, salad, and vegetables, and features the region’s freshest seafood catch.

  • Huckleberry Pie: A delicious and sweet dessert made from locally-sourced huckleberries, a unique berry that grows in the Pacific Northwest.

Local Beverages

  • Local Craft Beer: Mountlake Terrace is home to several local microbreweries that offer a range of craft beers, from IPAs to stouts.

  • Washington Wines: The state of Washington is known for its world-class wines, and Mountlake Terrace offers plenty of bars and restaurants that serve local wines from the region.
