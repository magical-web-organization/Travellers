Introduction to Ridgefield (Washington)

Located in Southwest Washington, Ridgefield (Washington) is a small city in Clark County. It is nestled in the Pacific Northwest region of the United States, surrounded by scenic views, natural attractions, and outdoor adventures. Ridgefield (Washington) is home to approximately 8,500 residents and offers a laid-back and relaxed atmosphere.
