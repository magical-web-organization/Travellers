Are you planning a trip to the beautiful state of Washington, and wondering where to go? Then, Ashford, Washington could be the perfect destination for you! This small town is located in Southwest Washington, in the heart of the Pacific Northwest. With its natural wonders and rich cultural heritage, Ashford offers something for every type of traveler. In this ultimate travel guide, we will give you all the information you need to plan your trip to Ashford.

Introduction to Ashford (Washington)

Ashford is a small town with a population of around 200 people, located at the base of Mount Rainier, a massive and beautiful mountain that dominates the Pacific Northwest skyline. With its picturesque landscape, outdoor adventures, and rich history, Ashford has become a popular destination for tourists and locals alike in recent years. Whether you are an outdoor enthusiast, a history buff, or just looking for a peaceful getaway, Ashford has something to offer you.

List of Places to Visit in Ashford (Washington)

Are you wondering what to do and see in Ashford? Here is a list of some of the top places to visit in the city.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • (1) Mount Rainier Visitor Center: The Mount Rainier Visitor Center offers a wealth of information about the history and geology of the area, as well as guided tours and programs.
  • (2) Longmire Historic District: The Longmire Historic District is a national historic landmark that offers a glimpse into the past, including a hotel, museum, and cabins that have been preserved from the early 20th century.

Natural Attractions of Ashford (Washington): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • (3) Mount Rainier National Park: Mount Rainier National Park is a natural wonder, with breathtaking scenery, hiking trails, and opportunities for outdoor activities like camping, climbing, and fishing.
  • (4) Alder Lake Park: Alder Lake Park is a beautiful park with a lake that offers opportunities for boating, swimming, and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ashford (Washington)

Ashford is a paradise for hikers and bikers with a ton of trails and routes to choose from. Here are a few of the best routes to get started on.

  • (1) Mount Rainier Trail: This 14.3 miles loop offers breathtaking views of Mount Rainier from all angles.
  • (2) Nisqually Vista Trail: This 1.2 miles long trail offers views of Mount Rainier’s glaciers, meadows, and peaks, and it’s short enough for families with kids.

Best Things to Do in Ashford (Washington): Activities and Tours

From outdoor activities to indoor attractions, Ashford has something for everyone. Here are a few of the best things to do in Ashford.

  • (1) Kayak tour of Alder Lake: Take a kayak tour to explore the hidden coves and calm waters of Alder Lake
  • (2) Horseback Riding: Horseback riding tours by the Rainier Stables offer scenic views of the mountains and valleys.
  • (3) Mount Rainier Railroad: Step back in time with a tour on a steam-powered train that takes you through the forests and hills of Ashford.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ashford (Washington)

Are you planning to spend an entire week in Ashford? Here is a sample itinerary that will help you make the most of your time.

Day 1 - Visit the Mount Rainier Visitor Center

Day 2 - Explore Longmire Historic District

Day 3 - Hike the Mount Rainier Trail

Day 4 - Take a horseback riding tour by Rainier Stable

Day 5 - Visit Alder Lake for kayaking and fishing

Day 6 - Train ride by Mount Rainier Railroad

Day 7 - Shop for the local souvenirs in the town market

Food and Drink Scene of Ashford (Washington)

Are you a foodie? Then you are going to love the gastronomic scene in Ashford. Here are a few must-try dishes and beverages in Ashford.

Must-Try Dishes

  • (1) Huckleberry Pancakes: This sweet and tangy dish is made with local huckleberries.
  • (2) Rainbow Trout: The fresh, locally caught rainbow trout is a must-try for seafood lovers.

Local Beverages

  • (1) Local Brews: Ashford’s brewing scene is thriving, with many breweries offering unique blends. Head to Whittaker’s Bunkhouse for a pint.
  • (2) Cider: Cider is a popular drink in Ashford, and Paradise Creek Brewery offers a range of ciders.

Where to Stay in Ashford (Washington): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

When it comes to accommodation in Ashford, you have many options. Here are a few of the best places to stay.

  • (1) Nisqually Lodge: A cozy resort with a rustic feel.
  • (2) Alexander’s Country Inn: A historic bed and breakfast that has been in operation for over a century.
  • (3) Copper Creek Inn: A boutique inn that offers luxurious cabins with hot tubs and mountain views.

What to Buy in Ashford (Washington): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Are you wondering what souvenirs to buy from Ashford? Here are a few local products that you can take back home.

  • (1) Mount Rainier Hats: The perfect way to protect yourself from the sun and show your love for Mount Rainier.
  • (2) Huckleberry Jam: A sweet treat to enjoy at home or gift to your friends and family.

Local Culture and Customs of Ashford (Washington)

If you want to immerse yourself in the local culture and customs, here are a few things to keep in mind when visiting Ashford:

  • (1) Respect nature and leave no trace.
  • (2) Tipping is customary in restaurants.
  • (3) If you are visiting during winter, snow chains are a must for your vehicle.

Best Time to Visit Ashford (Washington)

The best time to visit Ashford is from June to August when the weather is warm and sunny, and the mountain trails are open. However, if you are a winter sports enthusiast, visiting during the winter months offers skiing and snowboarding opportunities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ashford (Washington)

Here are a few things to keep in mind during your stay in Ashford:

Do:

  • (1) Respect nature and wildlife.
  • (2) Follow the trail guidelines.
  • (3) Keep a map and compass handy on hikes.

Don’t:

  • (1) Smoke in the National Park.
  • (2) Feed wildlife.
  • (3) Litter.

Safety and Health

When visiting Ashford, take the following precautions to stay safe and healthy.

Safety in Ashford (Washington)

  • (1) Keep an eye on weather forecasts if you plan hiking or camping in the area.
  • (2) Follow safe driving techniques if visiting during the winter months.
  • (3) Keep your valuables in a safe place in your vehicle.

Health in Ashford (Washington)

  • (1) Use sunscreen and insect repellent.
  • (2) Bring any necessary medications with you.
  • (3) Drink plenty of water, especially when hiking or doing outdoor activities.

Getting to Ashford (Washington): Transportation Options and Logistics

Ashford is located a couple of hours drive from Seattle, the nearest major city. Here are some transportation options and logistics:

Airports Near Ashford (Washington)

  • (1) Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA) - 1 hour, 45 minutes away from Ashford.

Bus Stations Near Ashford (Washington)

  • (1) Ashford Transit Center: Served by Pierce Transit and Sound Transit.

Other Options

  • Car rentals: Car rentals are available at Seattle airports like SEA-TAC International Airport.
  • Private Tours: Many companies provide shuttle services to Ashford and Mount Rainier on private shuttle tours.

How to Get Around in Ashford (Washington)

The best way to get around in Ashford is by car. If you don’t come with your vehicle, rent one from one of the car rental companies in Seattle or Tacoma.

Where to Go Next from Ashford (Washington): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Are you looking to explore more of the Pacific Northwest? Here are a few nearby destinations worth considering:

  • (1) Seattle: The bustling and thriving city of Seattle is just over two hours drive from Ashford.
  • (2) Tacoma: Famous for its Tacoma Museum of Glass and beautiful parks, Tacoma is an ideal day trip destination.
  • (3) Olympia: The capital of Washington state, Olympia, is just a short drive from Ashford.

Conclusion

Ashford is a must-visit destination for any traveler visiting the Pacific Northwest. With its natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and rich cultural heritage, there’s something for everyone in Ashford. We hope this travel guide has helped you plan the perfect trip to Ashford, Washington. Happy travels!
