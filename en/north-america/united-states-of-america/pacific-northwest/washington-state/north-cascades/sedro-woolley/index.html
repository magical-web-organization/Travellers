Introduction to Sedro-Woolley

Sedro-Woolley is a charming city in Skagit County, Washington. Located in the heart of the Pacific Northwest, Sedro-Woolley is surrounded by natural beauty and boasts a rich history and culture. The city is home to many attractions and activities that are sure to delight travelers of all ages.

List of Places to Visit in Sedro-Woolley

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sedro-Woolley Museum: The Sedro-Woolley Museum is a must-visit attraction for all history buffs. The museum features exhibits that showcase the cultural heritage and history of the region. The museum is open from Tuesday to Sunday, from 12:00 PM to 4:00 PM.

  • The Campbell House: The Campbell House is a historic home that was built in the early 1900s. The house has been beautifully restored and is now open to the public as a museum. The house is open from Wednesday to Sunday, from 12:00 PM to 4:00 PM.

  • Northern State Hospital Museum: The Northern State Hospital Museum is located on the former site of Northern State Hospital, a psychiatric hospital that operated from 1912 to 1973. The museum features exhibits that explore the history of the hospital and its impact on the community. The museum is open every Saturday, from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

Natural Attractions of Sedro-Woolley: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Skagit River Park: Skagit River Park is a beautiful park that spans across 240 acres and offers stunning views of the Skagit River. The park is a popular spot for fishing, hiking, and picnicking.

  • Cascades Trail: The Cascades Trail is a beautiful hiking trail that offers stunning views of the Cascade Mountains. The trail is approximately 3.1 miles long and is a moderate hike.

  • Baker Lake Trail: The Baker Lake Trail is a popular hiking trail that winds its way through a beautiful forest and offers stunning views of the Baker Lake. The trail is approximately 10 miles long and is a moderate hike.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sedro-Woolley

Sedro-Woolley is home to many beautiful hiking and biking trails. Some of the best hiking and biking routes in Sedro-Woolley include the Cascades Trail, Baker Lake Trail, and the Pacific Northwest Trail.

Best Things to Do in Sedro-Woolley: Activities and Tours

  • River Rafting: River rafting is a popular activity in Sedro-Woolley. The Skagit River provides the perfect setting for a thrilling river rafting experience.

  • Fishing: Fishing is a popular activity in Sedro-Woolley. The Skagit River is home to a variety of fish species, including salmon and trout.

  • Wine Tasting: Sedro-Woolley is home to several wineries that offer wine tasting tours. The wineries in Sedro-Woolley are known for producing high-quality wines using locally grown grapes.
