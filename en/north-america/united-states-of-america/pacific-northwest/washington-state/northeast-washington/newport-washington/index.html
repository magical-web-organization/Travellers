Introduction to Newport (Washington)

Nestled in the scenic hills of Northeast Washington, Newport is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. This charming town offers a perfect mix of outdoor adventures, cultural attractions and local delicacies.

List of Places to Visit in Newport (Washington)

Here are some must-visit places in Newport:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Pend Oreille County Historical Museum: Explore the rich history and culture of Pend Oreille County at this fascinating museum.
  • Newport Cemetery: Visit the final resting place of some of Newport’s earliest settlers.
  • Oldtown Historic District: Take a stroll through this charming district and admire the well-preserved storefronts and architecture.

Natural Attractions of Newport (Washington): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Pend Oreille River: Enjoy boating, fishing, and swimming at this beautiful river that runs through Newport.
  • Kalispell Island Trail: Hike through lush forests and spot wildlife along this picturesque trail.
  • Tiger Inlet Trail: Take a leisurely walk through meadows and forests to reach the stunning Tiger Inlet.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Newport (Washington)

Newport has a great selection of trails for hikers and bikers:

  • Little Spokane River Trail: This 11-mile trail takes you through a beautiful river valley with stunning views of the surrounding hills.
  • Klondike Trail: This 4.5-mile hike is perfect for hikers looking for a moderate challenge and great views of Pend Oreille River.
  • Pend Oreille County Park: This park offers multiple trails for hiking and biking, with varying levels of difficulty and lengths.

Best Things to Do in Newport (Washington): Activities and Tours

There’s no shortage of fun things to do in Newport:

  • Cycling Tour: Take a guided cycling tour through scenic back roads of Newport.
  • Whitewater Rafting: Experience the thrill of rapids on the Pend Oreille River with a guided rafting tour.
  • Fishing Trip: Spend a day fishing with an experienced fishing guide and catch some delicious trout.

Food and Drink Scene of Newport (Washington)

Newport has a thriving food and drink scene. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Huckleberry Pie: Enjoy a slice of tart and sweet huckleberry pie, made with locally sourced berries.
  • Smoked Trout: Sample some delicious smoked trout, caught fresh from the Pend Oreille River.
  • Wild Mushroom Ravioli: Indulge in some creamy wild mushroom ravioli, made with locally foraged mushrooms.

Local Beverages

  • Huckleberry Wine: Savor the unique flavor of huckleberry wine, made from hand-picked berries.
  • Spokane Beer: Try some locally brewed beer from Spokane, just a short drive from Newport.

Where to Stay in Newport (Washington): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Newport has a range of accommodation options to suit every budget:

  • Newport Antler Motel: A family-friendly motel located in the heart of Newport.
  • Pend Oreille RV Park: A full-service RV park with hookups and amenities.
  • Little Diamond RV and Campground: A beautiful campground located on the shores of Little Diamond Lake.

What to Buy in Newport (Washington): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Take some memories from Newport home with you:

  • Huckleberry Jam: Stock up on jars of delicious huckleberry jam, made with locally sourced berries.
  • Handcrafted Pottery: Browse through handmade pottery and ceramics from local artisans.
  • Local Honey: Try some of the sweet honey produced by bees foraging in the local area.

Local Culture and Customs of Newport (Washington)

Newport residents are proud of their town and culture. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Respect for Nature: Newport residents take great pride in nature and preserving the environment. Follow Leave No Trace principles while enjoying outdoor activities.
  • Small Town Atmosphere: Newport has a close-knit community, where everyone knows each other. Be friendly and respectful to residents and locals.

Best Time to Visit Newport (Washington)

The best time to visit Newport is during summer, from June to September when the weather is warm and perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Newport (Washington)

Do:

  • Respect nature and the environment
  • Be friendly and polite to residents and locals
  • Try the local food and drink scene
  • Take time to explore the local history and culture

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural environment
  • Be noisy or disruptive in residential areas
  • Ignore traffic rules and regulations

Safety and Health

Safety in Newport (Washington)

Newport is a relatively safe town, but it’s always a good idea to take precautions:

  • Avoid walking alone at night in less populated areas
  • Keep an eye on your belongings in crowded areas

Health in Newport (Washington)

Newport has a medical center, but for serious issues, it’s recommended to seek medical attention in a larger nearby town. Remember to keep safe distance and hygiene measures in mind due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic.

Getting to Newport (Washington): Transportation Options and Logistics

Newport is located around 50 miles north of Spokane in northeast Washington State. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Newport (Washington)

The nearest airport to Newport is Spokane International Airport, located around 50 miles south of Newport.

Bus Stations Near Newport (Washington)

There is no direct bus service to Newport. However, the nearby town of Spokane has bus services available, which can be reached by taxi, shuttle bus, or rental car.

Other Options

The best way to get to Newport is by rental car as it allows you to explore the surrounding areas. Taxi and shuttle services are also available from the Spokane airport.

How to Get Around in Newport (Washington)

Newport is a small town that you can easily explore on foot or bike. It’s also possible to hire a car or taxi to get to other nearby attractions.

Where to Go Next from Newport (Washington): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Make the most of your trip to the Pacific Northwest by visiting nearby destinations:

  • Spokane: Explore the many parks, museums, and historic sites in Spokane, located just 50 miles south of Newport.
  • Lake Roosevelt National Recreation Area: Go camping, fishing, or boating at this scenic recreation area, located just a short drive from Newport.

Conclusion

Newport is the perfect destination for those looking for an adventurous and relaxing trip. With a mix of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and local delicacies, Newport offers something for every kind of traveler. Visit Newport to discover the hidden beauty of Northeast Washington.
