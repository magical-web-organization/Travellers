Food and Drink Scene of Prineville

Must-Try Dishes

Prineville Fried Chicken

Prineville Fried Chicken is a regional specialty that visitors must try when visiting the city. It’s a crispy, juicy, and delicious dish that locals take pride in. You can find it at various local restaurants and cafes.

Local Beverages

Prineville Coffee

Prineville Coffee is another specialty that visitors shouldn’t miss. It’s a locally roasted coffee that comes in various flavors and is served in many local cafes and restaurants.

Where to Stay in Prineville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Brasada Ranch

Brasada Ranch is a luxury resort located just a few miles outside of Prineville. It’s a beautiful property that offers guests a tranquil and relaxing environment, while still being close to all the city’s attractions.

Prineville Reservoir State Park Campground

The Prineville Reservoir State Park campground is an affordable option for visitors who want to be close to nature. The campground offers various RV and tent sites, as well as cabins for rent.

What to Buy in Prineville: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Prineville Olive Oil

Prineville Olive Oil is a locally produced and delicious specialty that visitors can take home as a souvenir. It’s an excellent souvenir to remind you of your trip to Prineville and its unique cuisine.

Local Culture and Customs of Prineville

Prineville’s locals are known for their friendly and welcoming nature. Visitors are encouraged to engage with the locals to learn more about their community’s traditions and customs.

Best Time to Visit Prineville

The best time to visit Prineville is from May to September when the weather is warm, and outdoor activities are in full swing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Prineville

Do:

  • Respect the natural surroundings and clean up after yourself.
  • Try the local dishes and beverages.
  • Engage with the locals and learn about their culture and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Litter.
  • Ignore the safety rules when engaging in outdoor activities.
  • Disrespect the local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Prineville

Prineville is a safe community, but visitors should still practice common sense and safety precautions, particularly when hiking or engaging in other outdoor activities.

Health in Prineville

Prineville has a few medical facilities that visitors can use if they need medical assistance. Visitors are advised to bring any necessary medication and first-aid kit with them on their trip.

Getting to Prineville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Prineville

The nearest airports to Prineville are Redmond Municipal Airport and Portland International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Prineville

Prineville doesn’t have a bus or train station, but various transportation companies offer bus and shuttle services to and from the city. Greyhound and Central Oregon Breeze are two popular options.

Other Options

Prineville is easily accessible by car or motorcycle. The city is connected to major highways and scenic routes, making it an ideal road trip destination.

How to Get Around in Prineville

Prineville has a limited public transportation system, so visitors are encouraged to rent a car or bike to explore the city and its surroundings. Walking is an excellent option if you’re staying in the city center.

Where to Go Next from Prineville: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bend, Oregon

Bend is a beautiful city located just a few miles from Prineville. It’s famous for its outdoor activities, including hiking, skiing, mountain biking, and fishing.

Crater Lake National Park

Crater Lake National Park is located about two and a half hours from Prineville. It’s the deepest lake in the United States and offers visitors incredible views of the surrounding mountains and forests.

Conclusion

Prineville, Oregon is a beautiful and charming city that has something to offer for every type of visitor. With its natural beauty, outdoor activities, and friendly locals, Prineville is a must-visit destination in the Pacific Northwest. We hope this travel guide helps you plan your trip and make the most of your time in Prineville.
