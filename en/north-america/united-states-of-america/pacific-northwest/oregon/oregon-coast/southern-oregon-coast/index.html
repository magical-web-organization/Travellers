Introduction to Southern Oregon Coast

Located on the southern part of Oregon Coast, Southern Oregon Coast is a breathtaking destination that offers a diverse range of attractions and activities for visitors. From its rich natural scenery to its cultural and historical attractions, Southern Oregon Coast is the perfect place to unwind, relax, and explore.

List of Places to Visit in Southern Oregon Coast

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Southern Oregon Coast is home to several culturally important attractions that offer insights into the region’s history and heritage. These include:

  • Oregon Caves National Monument: A unique natural cave system that offers guided tours and spectacular views of underground crystal formations.
  • Coquille River Lighthouse: A historic lighthouse that was first built in the late 19th century.
  • The Bandon Historical Society Museum: A museum that showcases the history of Bandon and the surrounding areas.

Natural Attractions of Southern Oregon Coast: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Southern Oregon Coast’s natural landscape is one of its biggest attractions with stunning outdoor adventures and scenic views. Here are some of the must-visit natural attractions:

  • Samuel H. Boardman State Scenic Corridor: A state park that offers some of the most breathtaking views along the coast.
  • Shore Acres State Park: A park that features a beautiful garden, trails, and striking ocean vistas.
  • Bandon Dunes Golf Resort: A scenic golf resort that offers world-renowned courses that blend in with the rugged Oregon coast landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Southern Oregon Coast

Southern Oregon Coast offers several hiking and biking routes that offer stunning views of its natural beauty. Some of the most popular include:

  • Oregon Coast Trail: A hiking trail that spans the entire Oregon Coast, offering hikers unparalleled views of the Pacific Ocean.
  • Whiskey Run Mountain Biking Trails: A set of mountain biking trails nestled in the forest that offer bikers challenging routes with spectacular views.

Best Things to Do in Southern Oregon Coast: Activities and Tours

Southern Oregon Coast offers visitors a wide range of activities to explore and enjoy. Some of the best things to do include:

  • Fishing and Crabbing: The region offers several fishing and crabbing opportunities along its coastline and lakes.
  • Wildlife Watching: The region is home to diverse species of birds, sea lions, and whales, making it an excellent destination for wildlife watching.
  • Brewery Tours: Southern Oregon Coast offers several brewery tours that showcase the region’s craft beer scene.

Week-Long Itinerary for Southern Oregon Coast

A week-long itinerary for Southern Oregon Coast could include:

  • Day 1: Visit Bandon’s historic old town and explore its galleries, antique stores, and local restaurants.
  • Day 2: Take a scenic drive on the Roosevelt Elk Tour Route or explore Gold Beach and the surrounding area.
  • Day 3: Go hiking at Samuel H. Boardman State Scenic Corridor or explore the stunning gardens at Shore Acres State Park.
  • Day 4: Visit the historic Coquille River Lighthouse and explore the surrounding attractions.
  • Day 5: Explore local breweries and wineries or go fishing or crabbing.
  • Day 6: Take a guided tour of the Oregon Caves National Monument or hike on the Oregon Coast Trail.
  • Day 7: Relax on the beach or explore nearby destinations like Redwood National Park or Crater Lake National Park.

Food and Drink Scene of Southern Oregon Coast

Must-Try Dishes

Southern Oregon Coast offers a range of locally sourced dishes that are a testament to the region’s rich culinary heritage. Some of the must-try dishes include:

  • Dungeness Crab: Freshly caught crab is served in many local restaurants and seafood markets.
  • Wild Berries: The region is home to an abundance of wild berries used in preserves, pies, and other dishes.
  • Artisan Cheese: Southern Oregon Coast is home to several artisan cheese makers, offering a range of tasty cheeses.

Local Beverages

The region also offers a range of locally produced beverages, including:

  • Craft Beer: Southern Oregon Coast has a thriving craft beer scene, with several local breweries offering unique, locally sourced brews.
  • Wine: The region’s wine country offers many vineyards that produce excellent varietals.
  • Artisan Spirits: Southern Oregon Coast is home to several craft distilleries producing locally sourced spirits.

Where to Stay in Southern Oregon Coast: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Southern Oregon Coast offers a range of accommodation options for travellers, including:

  • Hotels and motels: The region offers several comfortable hotels and motels that cater to all budgets.
  • Vacation rentals: There are several vacation rentals available, including cabins, cottages, and beach houses.
  • Hostels and Guesthouses: Southern Oregon Coast also offers several budget-friendly hostels and guesthouses.

What to Buy in Southern Oregon Coast: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Southern Oregon Coast offers several unique products and souvenirs that make perfect gifts or keepsakes. These include:

  • Seafood: Freshly caught seafood, such as crab, is available in several markets along the coast.
  • Arts and Crafts: The region is home to several artisan shops and galleries that offer unique crafts and artwork.
  • Locally Sourced Products: Several local farmers’ markets offer locally grown produce, including berries, apples, and pears.

Local Culture and Customs of Southern Oregon Coast

Southern Oregon Coast is known for its laid-back, friendly culture, and hospitality. Visitors are encouraged to immerse themselves in the local customs and traditions, such as:

  • Participating in local festivals and community events.
  • Exploring the region’s rich culinary heritage and engaging with local food producers.
  • Taking part in outdoor activities and enjoying the natural beauty of the region.

Best Time to Visit Southern Oregon Coast

The best time to visit Southern Oregon Coast is during the summer months, from June to September, when the weather is mild, and the region gets the most sunshine. However, visitors can also enjoy the region’s beauty during the off-season, with winter offering a unique opportunity to see migratory wildlife and storm watching.

Safety and Health

Safety in Southern Oregon Coast

Southern Oregon Coast is a safe destination for travellers, but visitors should take precautions and be aware of the natural environment. Some tips for staying safe include:

  • Be aware of the tides and ocean conditions when visiting the coast.
  • Carry a first aid kit and be prepared for emergencies.
  • Stay on marked trails and respect the local wildlife.

Health in Southern Oregon Coast

Southern Oregon Coast has several medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics. Visitors are advised to bring any necessary medication and seek medical help in case of an emergency.
