Introduction to Moncks Corner

Located in Santee Cooper Country in South Carolina, Moncks Corner is a small town with a rich history and plenty of natural beauty. With its charming atmosphere, friendly locals, and a wide variety of things to see and do, this town is quickly becoming a popular travel destination. Whether you’re interested in outdoor adventures, cultural landmarks, or just a peaceful escape, Moncks Corner has something for you. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know when planning your visit to this charming Southern gem.

List of Places to Visit in Moncks Corner

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re a history buff or just enjoy learning about the past, Moncks Corner has plenty of cultural landmarks to explore.

  • Old Santee Canal Park: This historic park offers visitors an opportunity to explore the natural beauty of the South Carolina low country while learning about the historic Santee Canal.
  • Mepkin Abbey: Founded in 1949, Mepkin Abbey is a beautiful monastery that offers visitors guided tours of the grounds, as well as a glimpse into the lifestyle of the Trappist Monks who call it home.
  • Berkeley County Museum and Heritage Center: This interesting museum showcases the history and culture of Moncks Corner and Berkeley County, with exhibits on everything from Native American culture to rice plantations.

Natural Attractions of Moncks Corner: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you’re looking for outdoor adventure and breathtaking natural landscapes, Moncks Corner won’t fail to impress.

  • Cypress Gardens: A stunning 170-acre garden that features a variety of plant and animal life. Visitors can explore the garden’s three miles of walking trails, rent a canoe and paddle through the swamp, or take a guided tour.
  • Lake Moultrie: This beautiful lake offers visitors a chance to fish, swim, and enjoy a range of water sports. With its stunning views of the surrounding scenery, Lake Moultrie is the perfect destination for relaxation and outdoor fun.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Moncks Corner

The Moncks Corner area has plenty of hiking and biking routes that will allow you to get out and enjoy the beautiful natural landscapes.

  • The Palmetto Trail: This trail spans the entire state of South Carolina and includes a 15-mile section that passes through Moncks Corner. The section of the trail through Moncks Corner is mostly flat and provides stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
  • Francis Marion National Forest: With over 250,000 acres of land, this forest offers plenty of hiking and biking opportunities. Visitors can explore the many trails that wind through the forest, including the popular Awendaw Passage.

Best Things to Do in Moncks Corner: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re looking for adventure or a more relaxed experience, Moncks Corner has something to offer.

  • Water Activities: From kayaking and canoeing to fishing and swimming, Moncks Corner offers plenty of opportunities to enjoy the water. Lake Moultrie is particularly popular for its fishing and boating opportunities.
  • Horseback Riding: At Tucker’s Riding Stables, visitors can explore the beautiful low country scenery on horseback. With experienced guides and a variety of trails to choose from, this is a great activity for riders of all levels.
  • Ghost Tours: If you’re looking for a spooky evening activity, check out one of the many ghost tours in the area. You’ll visit historic sites and learn about the local legends and folklore, all while getting a little scared along the way.

Week-Long Itinerary for Moncks Corner

If you’re planning to spend a week in Moncks Corner, here’s a sample itinerary to help you make the most of your time:

Day Activity
1 Explore the Berkeley County Museum and Heritage Center
2 Visit Cypress Gardens
3 Take a guided tour of Mepkin Abbey
4 Go kayaking or fishing on Lake Moultrie

Food and Drink Scene of Moncks Corner

Moncks Corner offers a range of delicious local cuisine and drinks to satisfy every taste.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Frogmore Stew: A popular local dish that features a combination of shrimp, sausage, potatoes, and corn on the cob.
  • Low Country Boil: Similar to Frogmore Stew, this dish features a mix of seafood and vegetables boiled together with seasonings.
  • Pimento Cheese Sandwich: A simple but delicious sandwich made with pimento cheese spread and white bread.

Local Beverages

  • Sweet Tea: A Southern classic, sweet tea is a refreshing way to cool off on a hot day.
  • Palmetto Brewing Company: Moncks Corner is home to the Palmetto Brewing Company, which offers a range of craft beers for visitors to enjoy.

Where to Stay in Moncks Corner: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Whether you’re looking for a budget-friendly hostel or a luxurious hotel, Moncks Corner has plenty of options for every budget.

  • Cypress Inn Bed & Breakfast: This beautiful bed and breakfast is located on Main Street and offers guests elegant rooms and delicious breakfasts.
  • Holiday Inn Express & Suites: This comfortable hotel is located just off Highway 52 and offers free breakfast, an outdoor pool, and comfortable rooms.
  • Goose Creek Inn Bed & Breakfast: Located just outside Moncks Corner in charming Goose Creek, this B&B offers guests a quiet escape and comfortable accommodations.

What to Buy in Moncks Corner: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Looking for a unique souvenir to bring home from Moncks Corner? Here are some local products you won’t find anywhere else:

  • Sweetgrass Baskets: Made by local artisans, sweetgrass baskets are a beautiful and unique souvenir.
  • Homemade Jams and Jellies: With so many local farms and markets in the area, you’ll find plenty of delicious jams and jellies to take home.
  • Palmetto Moon T-Shirts: A popular souvenir, Palmetto Moon t-shirts feature the iconic South Carolina state symbol.

Local Culture and Customs of Moncks Corner

Moncks Corner is known for its friendly locals, Southern hospitality, and a laid-back vibe.

Best Time to Visit Moncks Corner

The best time to visit Moncks Corner is in the spring or fall when the weather is mild, and the crowds are thinner. However, any time of year can be a great time to visit this charming Southern town.

Do’s and Don’ts of Moncks Corner

Do:

  • Embrace the local Southern hospitality and friendly vibe.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Explore the natural beauty of the area.

Don’t:

  • Ignore the history and culture of the area.
  • Forget to pack comfortable shoes for all the walking and hiking you’ll be doing.
  • Leave without trying a sweet tea or craft beer!

Safety and Health

Safety in Moncks Corner

Moncks Corner is a relatively safe destination, but visitors should always use common sense and be aware of their surroundings.

Health in Moncks Corner

There are no specific health concerns in Moncks Corner, but visitors should always take precautions to stay healthy and safe on their trip.

Getting to Moncks Corner: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Moncks Corner

The closest major airport to Moncks Corner is the Charleston International Airport, located about 30 miles south of Moncks Corner.

Bus Stations Near Moncks Corner

There is no bus station in Moncks Corner, but visitors can take a bus to nearby Charleston and then rent a car to get to Moncks Corner.

Other Options

Renting a car is the most convenient way to get to Moncks Corner.

How to Get Around in Moncks Corner

The best way to get around Moncks Corner is by car. There is no public transportation in the area, and walking or biking may not always be practical for longer distances.

Where to Go Next from Moncks Corner: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to continue your explorations of Santee Cooper Country, here are some nearby destinations worth checking out:

  • Charleston: Just 30 miles south of Moncks Corner, Charleston is a charming Southern city with a rich history and a vibrant arts scene.
  • Santee State Park: Located about an hour’s drive from Moncks Corner, Santee State Park is a beautiful outdoor destination for camping, fishing, and exploring the natural beauty of the area.
  • Myrtle Beach: About a two-hour drive from Moncks Corner, Myrtle Beach is a popular tourist destination for its stunning beaches, boardwalk attractions, and great restaurants.

Conclusion

With its stunning natural beauty, rich history, and friendly locals, Moncks Corner is a must-visit destination in South Carolina. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventure, cultural landmarks, or just a relaxing escape, this charming Southern town won’t disappoint. Use this comprehensive travel guide to plan your trip and make the most of your time in Moncks Corner.
