Introduction to Sumter

Sumter, located in the heart of Santee Cooper Country in South Carolina, is a charming town that offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and outdoor adventure. Named after Revolutionary War hero General Thomas Sumter, the town has a rich history dating back to the 18th century. From exploring historical landmarks, hiking trails, and indulging in mouth-watering Southern cuisine to exploring scenic drives and enjoying outdoor adventures, Sumter has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Sumter

Here’s a list of the top places to visit in Sumter that you simply can’t miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sumter County Museum - Discover the history and culture of Sumter at the Sumter County Museum, which features exhibits, artifacts, and collections that showcase the town’s story. The museum also has a beautiful botanical garden and a heritage village.
  • Uptown Historic District - Take a walk through Sumter’s Uptown Historic District, which boasts beautiful architecture, quaint streets, and charming shops and restaurants.
  • Swan Lake Iris Gardens - This stunning botanical garden is home to a vast array of colorful flowers, including Japanese irises, camellias, and azaleas. The park also features a black swan colony and a swan house.

Natural Attractions of Sumter: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Poinsett State Park - Enjoy the great outdoors and indulge in hiking and camping at Poinsett State Park, which features picturesque trails through forests, lakes, and swamps, as well as campsites and cabins.
  • Manchester State Forest - Experience the beauty of Sumter’s wilderness at Manchester State Forest, which boasts miles of hiking trails, hunting opportunities, and fishing.
  • Iris Trail - Take a stroll on the Iris Trail, which winds through Sumter’s beautiful Swan Lake Iris Gardens and offers spectacular views of the garden’s stunning flora.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sumter

Sumter is an adventurer’s paradise with numerous hiking and biking trails. Here are some of the top routes:

  • Poinsett State Park Trail - This 10-mile trail system offers hikers a chance to explore the natural beauty and diversity of Poinsett State Park’s flora and fauna.
  • Iris Nature Trail - Take a leisurely stroll through Swan Lake Iris Gardens and enjoy the beauty of nature on this 0.7-mile trail.
  • Manchester State Forest Trail - Experience the rugged beauty of Sumter’s wilderness on this 7-mile trail that winds through the dense forest.

Best Things to Do in Sumter: Activities and Tours

Sumter offers a range of exciting activities and tours that are ideal for everyone. Here are the best things to do in Sumter:

  • Fishing and Boating on Lake Marion - Enjoy a day of fishing and boating on Lake Marion, which covers over 172,000 acres and has an abundance of fish species.
  • Segway Tour of Sumter - Take a guided Segway tour of Sumter and explore the town in style.
  • Farmers Market - The Sumter Farmers Market is a great place to experience the local culture, meet the people, and enjoy some fresh produce and handmade products.

Week-Long Itinerary for Sumter

Use this week-long itinerary to make the most of your Sumter trip:

  • Day 1: Visit the Sumter County Museum and Heritage Village, followed by a stroll through Uptown Historic District.
  • Day 2: Indulge in outdoor activities at Poinsett State Park.
  • Day 3: Explore Swan Lake Iris Gardens and take a hike on the Iris Nature Trail.
  • Day 4: Take a Segway tour of Sumter and enjoy some delicious Southern cuisine.
  • Day 5: Visit Manchester State Forest and indulge in hiking, fishing, and hunting.
  • Day 6: Visit the Sumter Farmers Market and enjoy some local produce and handmade products.
  • Day 7: Spend a day fishing and boating on Lake Marion.

Food and Drink Scene of Sumter

Sumter is known for its Southern cuisine, which is best enjoyed at the town’s many local restaurants. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fried Chicken - Sumter is famous for its Southern-style fried chicken, which is crispy and delicious.
  • Shrimp and Grits - Try this classic Southern dish that is made with fresh shrimp, creamy grits, and spicy sausage gravy.
  • Collard Greens - Indulge in a plate of savory collard greens, which make for a delicious and healthy side dish.

Local Beverages

  • Sweet Tea - No visit to Sumter is complete without a refreshing glass of sweet tea, which is a beloved Southern tradition.
  • Peach Cobbler Cocktail - This delicious cocktail is made with Southern Comfort, peach schnapps, and cream soda, and is a popular beverage in Sumter.

Local Culture and Customs of Sumter

Sumter has a unique culture and customs that stem from its rich history and Southern heritage. It’s important to be respectful of these customs, which include:

  • Southern hospitality - Sumter locals are known for being welcoming and friendly, so it’s important to reciprocate this hospitality.
  • Respect for historical landmarks - Sumter has a proud history that is reflected in its many historical landmarks, so it’s important to be respectful of these sites.
  • Love of Southern cuisine - Sumter is famous for its delicious Southern cuisine, which is a big part of the town’s culture.

Best Time to Visit Sumter

The best time to visit Sumter is from March to May and from September to November, when the weather is pleasant and outdoor activities can be enjoyed to the fullest.

Getting to Sumter: Transportation Options and Logistics

Sumter is located in central South Carolina and is easily accessible via several transportation options. Here are some ways to get to Sumter:

Airports Near Sumter

  • Columbia Metropolitan Airport - Located about an hour from Sumter, this airport offers flights from major cities and is served by major airlines.

Bus Stations Near Sumter

  • Greyhound Bus Lines - This bus station is located in downtown Sumter and offers regional and national bus services.

Other Options

  • Driving - Sumter is located off US-76 and is easily accessible by car.
  • Rideshare and Taxi Services - Several rideshare and taxi services are available in Sumter for local transportation.

How to Get Around in Sumter

Sumter is a small town that is easily navigable by car or rideshare services. Rental car services are also available for those who prefer to drive themselves.
