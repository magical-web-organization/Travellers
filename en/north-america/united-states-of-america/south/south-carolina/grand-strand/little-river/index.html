Little River is a charming town located in the Grand Strand area of South Carolina. This small fishing village is known for its historic charm, scenic landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. Whether you are looking to immerse yourself in the local culture, explore the natural beauty of the area, or simply relax by the beach, Little River has something for everyone. In this comprehensive tour guide, we will take you through the best places to visit, where to stay, and what to do in Little River, South Carolina.

Introduction to Little River

Little River is a small town on the Atlantic coast of South Carolina, bordering North Carolina. The town was established in the late 17th century and has since grown into a popular tourist destination. Known for its Southern charm, Little River is home to a mix of historical landmarks, natural wonders, and scenic vistas. The town is also home to several annual festivals and events, making it an ideal destination all year round.

List of Places to Visit in Little River

Little River is home to several cultural and natural attractions that are worth visiting. Here are our top picks for places to visit in Little River.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Little River is rich in history and culture, with several attractions that showcase this heritage.

Vereen Memorial Historical Gardens

The Vereen Memorial Historical Gardens is a 115-acre forested preserve that serves as a natural learning center for visitors. The park features nature trails, boardwalks, and a butterfly garden, among other attractions.

Little River Heritage Museum

The Little River Heritage Museum features artifacts, documents, and photographs that showcase the town’s history and heritage. Among the museum’s exhibits are a re-created general store, a restored farming equipment house, and a 1900s-era train depot.

Natural Attractions of Little River: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Little River is home to several natural attractions that offer visitors a chance to experience the great outdoors.

North Myrtle Beach Park and Sports Complex

The North Myrtle Beach Park and Sports Complex features a variety of outdoor activities, including picnic areas, a walking trail, a dog park, and sports fields for baseball, soccer, and softball. The park is an ideal destination for families, sports enthusiasts, and nature lovers.

Cherry Grove Pier

The Cherry Grove Pier is a 985-ft fishing pier that extends out into the Atlantic Ocean. The pier is a popular destination for fishing, sightseeing, and bird-watching. Visitors can also enjoy the beautiful views of the ocean and the surrounding coastline.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Little River

Little River offers several hiking and biking routes that allow visitors to explore the town’s natural beauty.

North Myrtle Beach Park and Sports Complex

The North Myrtle Beach Park and Sports Complex features a 1.5-mile walking trail that is ideal for hiking and biking. The trail winds through the park’s wooded areas, offering scenic views of the surrounding landscape.

Vereen Memorial Historical Gardens

The Vereen Memorial Historical Gardens features a network of nature trails and boardwalks that offer visitors a chance to explore the park’s forests, wetlands, and riverfront areas. Visitors can also spot a variety of wildlife, including birds, reptiles, and mammals.

Best Things to Do in Little River: Activities and Tours

Little River offers a variety of activities and tours that cater to different interests and preferences. Here are some of the best things to do in Little River.

Little River Fishing Fleet

The Little River Fishing Fleet offers a variety of fishing trips and charters for visitors of all ages and experience levels. Visitors can choose from half-day, full-day, or even overnight trips, depending on their preferences.

La Belle Amie Vineyard

La Belle Amie Vineyard is a family-owned winery that produces a variety of award-winning wines. Visitors can enjoy wine tastings, tours, and live music events in the vineyard’s beautiful setting.

Food and Drink Scene of Little River

Little River offers a variety of local dishes and beverages that are worth trying out. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in the town.

Must-Try Dishes

Calabash Seafood

Calabash Seafood is a local specialty that features lightly battered and fried seafood, served with hushpuppies and coleslaw.

Shrimp and Grits

Shrimp and Grits is a popular Southern dish that features sautéed shrimp served over creamy grits.

Local Beverages

Carolina Moonshine

Carolina Moonshine is a local spirit that is made from corn and has a smooth, sweet taste. Visitors can try this drink at several local bars and restaurants.

Sweet Tea

Sweet Tea is a popular Southern drink that is sweetened with sugar and served over ice.

Where to Stay in Little River: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Little River offers a variety of accommodation options that cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best places to stay in Little River.

Best Western Plus Myrtle Beach Hotel

The Best Western Plus Myrtle Beach Hotel is a comfortable hotel that offers visitors easy access to the town’s attractions and activities. The hotel features an outdoor pool, a fitness center, and a complimentary breakfast.

Anchor Inn

The Anchor Inn is a charming bed and breakfast that offers visitors a cozy and comfortable stay. The inn features a garden, a library, and a daily breakfast served in the room.
