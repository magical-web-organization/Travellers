Introduction to Simpsonville

Simpsonville is a small city located in Upcountry South Carolina, in the United States of America. It is a perfect destination for those looking for a peaceful and relaxing break from their hectic routines. Simpsonville offers a variety of activities and attractions that cater to all types of travelers. Whether you want to explore its history, indulge in outdoor adventures, or simply enjoy its local food and culture, Simpsonville has plenty to offer. In this travel guide, we will take a closer look at the best things to do, places to visit, cultural significance, and local customs of Simpsonville.

List of Places to Visit in Simpsonville

Simpsonville has several attractions that are worth visiting. Here is a list of places that you shouldn’t miss:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Heritage Park: It is a 100-acre park that houses several sports facilities, amphitheater, and the Upcountry History Museum. It is a perfect destination for those interested in local history.

  2. Simpsonville Clock Tower: A historical landmark that dates back to the early 1900s. It is a great place to take pictures and learn about the history of the city.

  3. Historic Old Town Simpsonville: The main street of Simpsonville that boasts of several shops, art galleries, and restaurants. It is a great place to walk around and experience the local culture.

Natural Attractions of Simpsonville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Lake Conestee Nature Preserve: A 400-acre nature reserve that offers hiking trails, camping sites, and kayaking opportunities. It is a perfect destination for nature lovers.

  2. Southside Park: A great place for families with children. It has several sports facilities, playgrounds, and picnic areas.

  3. The Cedar Falls Park: A scenic park that offers hiking trails, waterfalls, and swimming areas. It is a great place to spend a day in nature.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Simpsonville

Simpsonville has several hiking and biking trails that offer breathtaking views of its natural beauty. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  1. Three Bridges Trail: A 6.5-mile hiking trail that offers several scenic views of the Reedy River and Three Bridges. It is a moderate-level trail that is perfect for those looking for a challenging hike.

  2. Lake Conestee Nature Preserve Trail: A 7.5-mile biking and hiking trail that runs along the Reedy River. It offers several scenic views and is suitable for all levels of hikers and bikers.

  3. Pleasant Ridge County Park Trail: A 5.5-mile hiking trail that offers scenic views of the surrounding forests and lakes. It is a great place for families with children.

Best Things to Do in Simpsonville: Activities and Tours

Simpsonville offers several activities and tours that cater to all types of travelers. Here are some of the best things to do in Simpsonville:

  1. Go on a Food Tour: Simpsonville has several local restaurants that offer unique and delicious food. You can go on a food tour and taste some of the best dishes that the city has to offer.

  2. Attend a Concert at the Heritage Park Amphitheater: The Heritage Park Amphitheater is a great place to enjoy live music concerts and events. It hosts several cultural events throughout the year.

  3. Visit Local Vineyards: Simpsonville is surrounded by several vineyards and wineries. You can take a wine tour and taste some of the finest wines that the region has to offer.

Week-Long Itinerary for Simpsonville

Day 1: Visit the Heritage Park and Upcountry History Museum. Day 2: Go hiking at the Cedar Falls Park. Day 3: Visit the Simpsonville Clock Tower and walk around Historic Old Town Simpsonville. Day 4: Go kayaking or camping at the Lake Conestee Nature Preserve. Day 5: Attend a concert at the Heritage Park Amphitheater. Day 6: Take a wine tour and visit local vineyards. Day 7: Relax and enjoy the local food and culture.

Food and Drink Scene of Simpsonville

Simpsonville has several local restaurants that offer unique and delicious food. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. BBQ Ribs: South Carolina is famous for its BBQ Ribs, and Simpsonville is no exception. You can try some of the best BBQ Ribs at CC’s BBQ and Grill.

  2. Fried Chicken: If you are a fan of fried chicken, you must try the fried chicken at Stella’s Southern Brasserie.

  3. Shrimp and Grits: A popular Southern dish that you can try at Chef 360.

Local Beverages

  1. Sweet Tea: Sweet Tea is a staple beverage in the South, and you can find some of the best sweet tea at Stomping Grounds Coffee House and Wine Bar.

  2. Craft Beer: Simpsonville has several local breweries that offer craft beer. You can try some of the best craft beer at Growler Haus.

Local Culture and Customs of Simpsonville

Simpsonville is a friendly and welcoming city that values its culture and traditions. Here are some of the local customs that you should be aware of:

  1. Southern Hospitality: The people of Simpsonville are known for their welcoming nature and friendly attitude.

  2. Respect for Heritage: Simpsonville takes pride in its history and traditions. You should respect the cultural heritage of the city.

Best Time to Visit Simpsonville

The best time to visit Simpsonville is from March to May and from September to November. The weather during these months is pleasant, and the crowds are manageable.
