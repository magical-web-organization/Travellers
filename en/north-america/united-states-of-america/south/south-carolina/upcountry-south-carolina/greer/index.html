Introduction to Greer

Located in Upcountry South Carolina, Greer is a small town with a lot to offer. With a population of just over 30,000, Greer is a friendly and welcoming community that is perfect for anyone looking to explore the natural beauty of South Carolina while enjoying a range of cultural and recreational activities.

List of Places to Visit in Greer

Here are some of the best places to visit in Greer:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Greer Heritage Museum - This museum is devoted to preserving the history and culture of Greer and the surrounding area. With a range of exhibits and interactive displays, it is a great place to learn about the town’s past and present.

  2. Pelham Mill Park - This park is home to the ruins of an old textile mill that played an important role in the history of the area. It is a popular spot for picnicking, hiking, and relaxing by the water.

  3. Upcountry History Museum - Located in nearby Greenville, this museum is dedicated to preserving the history and culture of the Upstate region of South Carolina. With a range of exhibits and events, it is a must-visit for anyone interested in the area’s rich history.

Natural Attractions of Greer: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Lake Robinson - This beautiful lake is a popular spot for boating, fishing, and picnicking. With a range of trails and camping options, it is a great place for outdoor enthusiasts.

  2. Paris Mountain State Park - Located just a few miles from Greer, this park is home to a range of hiking and biking trails, as well as scenic overlooks and picnic areas.

  3. Blue Ridge Parkway - This iconic road runs through the heart of the Appalachian Mountains and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Greer

Here are some of the best hiking/biking routes in Greer:

  1. Lake Robinson Loop Trail - This 5-mile trail circles the perimeter of Lake Robinson and offers stunning views of the lake and surrounding landscape.

  2. Alder Trail - Located in Paris Mountain State Park, this 6.4-mile trail winds through the forest and offers some great views of the surrounding area.

  3. Sulphur Springs Trail - This 3.5-mile trail is a great option for hikers and bikers who want to explore the natural beauty of the area without straying too far from town.

Best Things to Do in Greer: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Greer:

  1. Greer City Park - This park is a great place for a stroll, a game of frisbee, or a picnic with friends and family.

  2. Cannon Centre - This performing arts venue hosts a range of productions throughout the year, including plays, concerts, and dance performances.

  3. Bank of America Fall for Greenville - This annual festival is held in nearby Greenville and features food, music, and entertainment from a range of local and regional artists.

Week-Long Itinerary for Greer

Day 1: Explore Greer City Park and the nearby shops and restaurants Day 2: Take a hike in Paris Mountain State Park Day 3: Visit the Greer Heritage Museum Day 4: Go fishing or boating on Lake Robinson Day 5: Take a scenic drive on the Blue Ridge Parkway Day 6: Attend a performance at the Cannon Centre Day 7: Explore nearby Greenville and attend the Bank of America Fall for Greenville festival

Food and Drink Scene of Greer

Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in Greer:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. South Carolina BBQ - South Carolina is famous for its BBQ, and Greer has some of the best BBQ spots in the state. Make sure to try the pulled pork and the mustard-based BBQ sauce.

  2. Fried Chicken and Biscuits - Another Southern classic, fried chicken and biscuits are a must-try in Greer.

  3. Pimento Cheese - This cheesy spread is a staple in Southern cuisine and is often served with crackers or bread.

Local Beverages

  1. Sweet Tea - No visit to the South would be complete without trying some sweet tea. Greer has some great locally-made tea options.

  2. Craft Beer - With a range of local breweries, Greer is a great place to sample some of South Carolina’s best craft beers.

Where to Stay in Greer: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Here are some of the best options for accommodation in Greer:

  1. Hampton Inn Greer - Located just a few miles from downtown Greer, this hotel is a great option for visitors who want to be close to the action.

  2. Holiday Inn Express Greer/Taylors - This modern hotel is located just a few miles from Paris Mountain State Park and is a great option for outdoor enthusiasts.

  3. Inn on Tyger Meadow - This cozy bed and breakfast is located in the heart of Greer and offers a range of comfortable rooms and suites.

What to Buy in Greer: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some of the best souvenirs and local products to bring home from Greer:

  1. South Carolina BBQ Sauce - Bring home a bottle of the famous mustard-based BBQ sauce to share with friends and family.

  2. Pimento Cheese - This tasty spread makes a great gift for foodies.

  3. Local Crafts - Greer is home to a range of local artisans who create beautiful pottery, textiles, and other crafts.

Local Culture and Customs of Greer

Greer is a friendly and welcoming community that values Southern hospitality and family values. Visitors should expect to be greeted with a smile and treated like one of the family.

Best Time to Visit Greer

The best time to visit Greer is from April to October, when the weather is warm and sunny and there are plenty of outdoor activities and events to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Greer

Do:

  1. Do take the time to explore the natural beauty of the area, including Paris Mountain State Park and Lake Robinson

  2. Do try some of the local cuisine, including South Carolina BBQ and fried chicken and biscuits

  3. Do take advantage of the friendly and welcoming community by striking up a conversation with locals

Don’t:

  1. Don’t forget to pack sunscreen and bug spray, especially if you plan to spend time outdoors

  2. Don’t be afraid to ask for recommendations for restaurants and activities from locals

  3. Don’t forget to respect the local customs and traditions, including Southern hospitality and family values

Safety and Health

Safety in Greer

Greer is a safe and friendly community, but visitors should still take the usual precautions when traveling, including locking car doors and securing valuables.

Health in Greer

Greer has a range of medical facilities and hospitals, including Greer Memorial Hospital and St. Francis Hospital. Visitors should make sure to have adequate health insurance coverage before traveling.

The best way to get around Greer is by car, but the town also offers a range of public transportation options, including buses and taxis.

Where to Go Next from Greer: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions that are worth exploring:

Conclusion

Greer is a hidden gem in Upcountry South Carolina, offering a range of natural beauty, cultural attractions, and recreational activities. Whether you’re looking to relax and unwind or explore the great outdoors, Greer has something for everyone.
