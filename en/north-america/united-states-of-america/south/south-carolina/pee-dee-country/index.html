Introduction to Pee Dee Country

Located in South Carolina, Pee Dee Country is a region that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and urban attractions. Pee Dee Country is named after the Pee Dee River, a major waterway that flows through the region. From scenic hiking trails and beautiful rivers to rich history and vibrant culture, Pee Dee Country has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Pee Dee Country

Whether you are a history buff or an outdoor enthusiast, Pee Dee Country won’t disappoint you. Here are some of the must-visit places in the region:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Florence County Museum
  • Darlington Raceway Museum
  • South Carolina Cotton Trail
  • Hartsville Museum
  • Florence National Cemetery

Natural Attractions of Pee Dee Country: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Week-Long Itinerary for Pee Dee Country

If you are planning a week-long trip to Pee Dee Country, here is a suggested itinerary:

Day Activities
Day 1 Explore the Florence County Museum and take a historic walking tour of Florence.
Day 2 Visit the Darlington Raceway Museum and take a tour of the Darlington Raceway.
Day 3 Go for a hike in Cheraw State Park and take a kayaking tour down the Lynches River.
Day 4 Visit the South Carolina Cotton Trail and the Hartsville Museum.
Day 5 Spend the day at Little Pee Dee State Park and go for a bike ride.
Day 6 Explore the Waccamaw National Wildlife Refuge and Pee Dee National Wildlife Refuge.
Day 7 Relax and enjoy a local beverage while soaking up the local culture in Pee Dee Country.

Food and Drink Scene of Pee Dee Country

Pee Dee Country’s food and drink scene is a reflection of its rich agricultural heritage. The region is famous for its barbecue, fried chicken, and locally sourced ingredients. Here are some of the must-try dishes and beverages in Pee Dee Country:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pee Dee Country-style barbecue
  • Fried chicken
  • Shrimp and grits
  • Hoppin’ John (a dish made with black-eyed peas and rice)
  • Pimento cheese

Local Beverages

  • Sweet tea
  • Muscadine wine
  • Carolina golden ale
  • Yaupon tea
  • Cheerwine

Local Culture and Customs of Pee Dee Country

Pee Dee Country is known for its warm hospitality and welcoming locals. The region has a rich history that has shaped its culture and customs. Here are some of the things you should know about Pee Dee Country’s local culture and customs:

  • Hospitality is important in Pee Dee Country, and locals are known to be friendly and welcoming to visitors.
  • Barbecue is a way of life in Pee Dee Country, and many families have their secret recipes for the perfect sauce.
  • The region has a strong African American heritage, which is reflected in its music, art, and food.

Best Time to Visit Pee Dee Country

The best time to visit Pee Dee Country is in the spring and fall, when the temperatures are mild and the foliage is at its peak. Summers can be hot and humid, while winters can be relatively mild but can experience some colder snaps.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pee Dee Country

Here are some of the do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting Pee Dee Country:

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine, especially the barbecue and fried chicken.
  • Explore the region’s scenic parks and hiking trails.
  • Learn about the region’s rich history and cultural heritage.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or harm the natural environment while exploring the outdoor attractions in the region.
  • Don’t disrespect the local customs and culture.

Safety and Health

Here is some information to keep in mind regarding safety and health in Pee Dee Country:

Safety in Pee Dee Country

Pee Dee Country is a relatively safe region to visit, but visitors should still take precautions to ensure their safety. Here are some safety tips to keep in mind:

  • Lock your car and keep valuables out of sight.
  • Stay aware of your surroundings, especially at night.
  • Be cautious when exploring natural attractions, such as hiking trails and rivers.

Health in Pee Dee Country

Pee Dee Country has a good healthcare system, and visitors should not have any major health concerns. Visitors should still take precautions to avoid common illnesses, such as the flu and colds. It is always a good idea to bring any necessary medications with you.

How to Get Around in Pee Dee Country

Renting a car is the best way to get around Pee Dee Country as public transportation, especially outside of Florence City, is limited. There are a few taxi and ride-share options in the area.
