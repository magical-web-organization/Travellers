Lewisburg Travel Guide: Discover the Best Attractions, Hikes, Food, and More!

Lewisburg is a charming town located in the heart of Kentucky, in the south of the United States of America. Known for its rich history, stunning natural beauty, and warm hospitality, it’s a great destination for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the big city and immerse themselves in a relaxed and welcoming atmosphere. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you through the best things to see and do in Lewisburg, from hiking and biking trails to cultural attractions and mouth-watering cuisine.

List of Places to Visit in Lewisburg

Here’s a list of top places to visit in Lewisburg:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • African American Heritage Center: This museum showcases the rich history and culture of African Americans in the region through a variety of exhibits and programs.
  • The Baker Arboretum & Downing Museum: This beautiful botanical garden and museum is the perfect destination for nature lovers, featuring a wide variety of plants and art installations.
  • Civil War Fort at Russellville: History buffs will love this unique attraction, featuring the remains of a fort that dates back to the Civil War.

Natural Attractions of Lewisburg: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Shaker Museum at South Union: Explore the fascinating history and culture of the Shaker community at this informative museum, featuring original buildings and artifacts.
  • Lost River Cave: Discover the underground wonders of Lost River Cave, featuring boat tours, hiking trails, and zip-lining adventures.
  • Chandler Park: This beautiful park is the perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts, featuring hiking and biking trails, a fishing pond, and picnic areas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lewisburg

Lewisburg offers plenty of opportunities for hiking and biking, with scenic trails for all levels of fitness and experience. Some of the best trails include:

  • Shanty Hollow Lake Trail: This 4.8-mile trail features beautiful lake views, shaded paths, and picturesque waterfalls.
  • Green River Bluffs Trail: This 3-mile trail offers stunning views of the Green River and its surrounding bluffs, with plenty of wildlife and unique rock formations to admire.
  • Mammoth Cave Railroad Bike & Hike Trail: This 9-mile paved trail takes you through the scenic countryside and historic railway tunnels of Mammoth Cave National Park.

Best Things to Do in Lewisburg: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are plenty of other fun activities and tours to enjoy in Lewisburg, including:

  • Bourbon Distillery Tours: Explore the rich history and complex flavors of Kentucky’s famous bourbon with a distillery tour and tasting.
  • Hot Air Balloon Rides: Soar over the stunning countryside of Lewisburg with a hot air balloon ride, featuring breathtaking views and a unique perspective.
  • Horseback Riding: Embrace the spirit of the Old West with a guided horseback riding tour through some of Kentucky’s most beautiful countryside.

Week-Long Itinerary for Lewisburg

Day 1: Explore the fascinating history and culture of the region at the African American Heritage Center and The Baker Arboretum & Downing Museum.

Day 2: Discover the natural wonders of Lost River Cave and Chandler Park, featuring hiking, zip-lining, and scenic views.

Day 3: Get your adrenaline pumping with a hot air balloon ride or horseback riding tour, followed by a relaxing afternoon at one of Lewisburg’s top-rated spas.

Day 4: Embark on a bourbon distillery tour and tasting, followed by a laid-back evening exploring the town’s vibrant art and music scene.

Day 5: Take a bike ride on the Mammoth Cave Railroad Bike & Hike Trail, followed by a picnic lunch at one of the local parks.

Day 6: Spend a day immersed in the beauty of Mammoth Cave National Park, featuring stunning cave tours, hiking trails, and wildlife.

Day 7: End your trip on a high note with a delicious dinner at one of the town’s top-rated restaurants, featuring locally sourced ingredients and innovative cuisine.

Food and Drink Scene of Lewisburg

Lewisburg boasts a vibrant food and drink scene, featuring locally sourced ingredients, classic Southern comfort food, and international cuisine options. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Burgoo: This traditional stew features a mix of meats and vegetables, slow-cooked to perfection with special spices and seasonings.
  • Hot Brown: This unique sandwich features turkey, bacon, tomato, and a creamy Mornay sauce, topped off with a sprinkle of cheese and broiled to perfection.
  • Derby Pie: A classic Kentucky dessert, this pie is made with chocolate, pecans, and a touch of Kentucky bourbon.

Local Beverages

  • Kentucky Bourbon: No trip to Kentucky is complete without trying some of the state’s famous bourbon, featuring complex flavors and a rich history.
  • Mint Julep: This classic cocktail features bourbon, sugar, water, and fresh mint, served over crushed ice for a refreshing and delicious drink.
  • Ale-8-One: This unique soda is a Kentucky favorite, featuring a blend of ginger and citrus flavors for a sweet and tangy taste.

Where to Stay in Lewisburg: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Lewisburg offers a variety of accommodations to suit every budget and travel style, from cozy guesthouses to luxurious hotels. Some recommended options include:

  • The Brown Hotel: This historic hotel is a Louisville landmark, featuring stylish rooms, fine dining options, and a prime location in the heart of the city.
  • 21c Museum Hotel: This innovative hotel combines luxury accommodations with world-class art exhibits, featuring curated galleries throughout the property.
  • The Seelbach Hilton Louisville: This elegant hotel features old-world charm and modern amenities, with stylish rooms and a popular dining destination.

What to Buy in Lewisburg: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Lewisburg offers plenty of unique souvenirs and local products to bring home as a reminder of your trip, including:

  • Bourbon: Take home a bottle of Kentucky’s famous bourbon, featuring distinctive flavors and a rich history.
  • Handcrafted Pottery: Kentucky is known for its beautiful, handcrafted pottery, featuring unique designs and high-quality materials.
  • Bourbon Balls: These delicious treats feature a mix of chocolate, bourbon, and pecans, for a sweet and decadent taste of Kentucky.

Local Culture and Customs of Lewisburg

Lewisburg is known for its warm hospitality and friendly locals, with a focus on family, community, and tradition. Visitors can expect to encounter a strong sense of Southern charm and hospitality, with a laid-back atmosphere and a deep appreciation for the natural beauty and rich cultural history of the region.

Best Time to Visit Lewisburg

The best time to visit Lewisburg is in the spring or fall, when the weather is mild and comfortable, and the surrounding countryside is at its most beautiful. Summer can be hot and humid, while winter can be cold and snowy, so plan accordingly.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lewisburg

Do:

  • Do explore the natural wonders of the region, including Mammoth Cave National Park and Lost River Cave.
  • Do sample the local cuisine and beverages, including bourbon, Burgoo, and Hot Browns.
  • Do take time to appreciate the charming Southern hospitality and warmth of the local community.

Don’t:

  • Don’t underestimate the importance of good manners and etiquette, including saying “please” and “thank you” and dressing appropriately for the occasion.
  • Don’t forget to practice good environmental stewardship and respect for the natural beauty of the region.
  • Don’t leave without taking time to relax and enjoy the laid-back pace of life in Lewisburg.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lewisburg

Lewisburg is a safe and welcoming destination, with a low crime rate and a friendly community. Visitors should exercise normal precautions, including staying aware of their surroundings, locking doors and windows, and safeguarding valuables.

Health in Lewisburg

Visitors should take care to stay hydrated and protected from the sun, especially during the hot summer months. It’s also important to practice good hygiene, including washing hands frequently and covering coughs and sneezes.

Getting to Lewisburg: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lewisburg

The closest airport to Lewisburg is Nashville International Airport, located approximately one hour’s drive away. Other options include Louisville International Airport and Evansville Regional Airport.

Bus Stations Near Lewisburg

Greyhound and Megabus operate bus stations in nearby Bowling Green, a short distance from Lewisburg.

Other Options

Car rentals, ride-sharing services, and private transportation are also available for those looking to explore the region on their own schedule.

How to Get Around in Lewisburg

The best way to get around Lewisburg is by car or bike, as the town is relatively small and spread out. Taxis and ride-share services are also available for those who prefer not to drive.

Where to Go Next from Lewisburg: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Lewisburg is located in the heart of Kentucky, surrounded by a variety of other exciting destinations and attractions. Some recommended nearby places to visit include:

  • Mammoth Cave National Park: Featuring stunning cave tours, hiking trails, and wildlife, this natural wonder is a must-see for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers.
  • Bardstown: Known as the “Bourbon Capital of the World,” this charming town boasts a vibrant food and drink scene, historic architecture, and plenty of Southern charm.
  • Louisville: Just an hour’s drive from Lewisburg, Louisville offers a variety of attractions, including the Kentucky Derby Museum, the Muhammad Ali Center, and the Louisville Slugger Museum & Factory.

Conclusion

Whether you’re a history buff, outdoor enthusiast, foodie, or simply looking to escape the pressures of day-to-day life, Lewisburg has something for everyone. From its charming small-town atmosphere and friendly locals to its stunning natural beauty and variety of attractions and activities, it’s the perfect destination for a relaxing and exciting getaway. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has helped you discover the best of what Lewisburg has to offer, and we can’t wait to see you on your next adventure!
