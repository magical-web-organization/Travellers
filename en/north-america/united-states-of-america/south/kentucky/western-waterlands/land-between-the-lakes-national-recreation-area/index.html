Introduction to Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area

Located in Western Waterlands, Kentucky, in the United States of America, Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area is a 170,000-acre peninsula that lies between Lake Barkley and Kentucky Lake. It offers a variety of recreational activities for visitors, including hiking, fishing, boating, and wildlife viewing.
