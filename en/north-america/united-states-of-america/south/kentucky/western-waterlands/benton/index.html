Introduction to Benton

Located in the Western Waterlands region of Kentucky, Benton is a charming city which is an ideal destination for people who love nature and outdoor adventures. Benton offers a perfect blend of fun, adventure, tranquility and natural beauty, making it a perfect getaway for all types of travellers.

List of Places to Visit in Benton

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Benton is home to several culturally significant attractions that are worth exploring:

Kentucky Opry

The Kentucky Opry is a popular performing arts theatre that hosts several musical performances throughout the year. From country music to comedy shows, Kentucky Opry offers a wide range of entertainment options for visitors to Benton.

Marshall County Historical Museum

The Marshall County Historical Museum is a must-visit for history buffs. The museum showcases the rich history of Benton and the surrounding region. It has an impressive collection of artefacts, photographs, and documents that showcase the region’s past.

Calvert City Drive-in

The Calvert City Drive-in is a historic theatre that has been in operation since the 1950s. It is a favourite spot for families and couples looking for a unique movie-watching experience.

Natural Attractions of Benton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Benton is surrounded by natural beauty and offers a variety of outdoor activities that visitors can enjoy:

Kentucky Lake

Kentucky Lake is the largest man-made lake in the eastern United States and is a top attraction in Benton. Visitors can enjoy fishing, boating, water sports and camping in the area.

Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area

Land Between the Lakes National Recreation Area is a protected area that offers visitors a unique outdoor experience. Spanning over 170,000 acres, the park is home to hiking and biking trails, camping sites, fishing and boating areas, and numerous recreational opportunities.

Benton City Park

Benton City Park is a beautiful park located in the heart of the city. It offers visitors a peaceful oasis with walking trails, picnic areas, and playgrounds. It is a great destination for a family day out.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Benton

With its beautiful natural surroundings, Benton offers plenty of hiking and biking routes for enthusiasts:

Central Park Trail

Central Park Trail is a hiking and biking trail located in Benton City Park. It offers visitors a beautiful view of the park’s flora and fauna and is perfect for leisurely walks or bike rides.

Hematite Lake Trail

The Hematite Lake Trail is a 5-mile loop trail that winds through a beautiful forested area. It is a moderate hike, and visitors can enjoy scenic views of the surrounding area.

Canal Loop Trail

The Canal Loop Trail is a 11.7-mile trail that circles around the Kentucky Lake. It is a favorite among hikers and bikers and offers breathtaking views of the lake and the surrounding woods.

Best Things to Do in Benton: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of activities and tours available for visitors to Benton:

Kentucky Lake Tours

Kentucky Lake Tours offer visitors the opportunity to explore the lake on a guided boat tour. Visitors can enjoy the beautiful scenery and wildlife of the area while learning about the lake’s history and ecology.

Jet Ski Rentals

Jet ski rentals are available at several locations around Benton and Kentucky Lake. Visitors can enjoy the lake’s natural beauty while experiencing the thrill of jet skiing in the beautiful waters.

Kentucky Opry Dinner Show

The Kentucky Opry Dinner Show is a popular attraction in Benton. Visitors can enjoy a delicious meal while watching live musical performances by talented local artists.

Food and Drink Scene of Benton

Must-Try Dishes

Benton is known for its southern-style cuisine. Here are a few must-try dishes:

BBQ Ribs

BBQ Ribs are a favorite among locals and visitors alike. The meat is slow-cooked and seasoned with a special blend of spices, making them juicy and flavorful.

Fried Catfish

Fried Catfish is a southern delicacy that is popular in Benton. The catfish is coated in a seasoned flour mixture and fried to perfection, making it crispy on the outside and tender on the inside.

Bourbon

Bourbon is Kentucky’s most famous beverage. Benton has plenty of options for visitors looking to try this famous whiskey.

Local Beverages

In addition to bourbon, Benton is known for its sweet tea and lemonade. Local cafes and restaurants serve the beverage, which is perfect for hot summer days.

Where to Stay in Benton: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Benton offers a range of accommodation options, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels.

Country Hearth Inn

Country Hearth Inn offers affordable rooms with clean, comfortable accommodations. It is perfect for budget-conscious travellers.

Malcolm Creek Resort

Malcolm Creek Resort is one of the popular resorts in Benton. It is located on Kentucky Lake and offers visitors a beautiful view of the surroundings.

Americas Best Value Inn

Americas Best Value Inn offers comfortable rooms and excellent customer service. It is an ideal option for visitors looking for a good value for their money.

What to Buy in Benton: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

For visitors looking to shop for souvenirs or gifts, Benton has plenty of options. Local stores offer handmade crafts, specialty foods, and souvenirs.

Land Between the Lakes Gift Shop

The Land Between the Lakes Gift Shop has a variety of souvenirs, including t-shirts, hats, keychains, and postcards.

Kentucky Lake Outfitters

Kentucky Lake Outfitters offer visitors the opportunity to purchase outdoor gear, including camping supplies, fishing gear, and boating equipment.

Local Culture and Customs of Benton

Benton has a rich cultural heritage, which is reflected in its music, food, and festivals. The city celebrates its heritage throughout the year, with music festivals, food fairs, and cultural events.

Best Time to Visit Benton

Benton is a year-round destination, with different activities and events taking place at different times of the year. Visitors can enjoy the city’s natural beauty and outdoor activities throughout the year, although summer is the peak tourist season.

Do’s and Don’ts of Benton

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and culture.
  • Enjoy the local food and drink scene.
  • Explore the natural beauty of Benton.

Don’t:

  • Leave trash or litter behind, always clean up after visit.
  • Ignore safety precautions while enjoying outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Benton

Benton is generally a safe destination for visitors. However, visitors are advised to take basic safety precautions, such as locking their vehicles and securing valuables.

Health in Benton

Visitors are advised to carry basic medications and first aid supplies, especially if they plan to spend a lot of time outdoors. It is also important to carry a water bottle and stay hydrated. Benton has a few medical clinics for people who need medical assistance.

Getting to Benton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Benton

The nearest airports to Benton are the Barkley Regional Airport and Nashville International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Benton

Greyhound has a bus station in Paducah which is the nearest bus station to Benton.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Benton using major highways such as I-24 and US Highway 641.

How to Get Around in Benton

Visitors can get around Benton by car or bicycle. Car rentals are available at the airport, while bicycles can be rented from several vendors in the city.

Where to Go Next from Benton: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Benton is located near some popular tourist destinations and attractions such as the Mammoth Cave National Park, Nashville, and the city of Memphis.

Conclusion

Benton, Kentucky, is a charming city that offers something for everyone. From natural beauty to outdoor adventures and cultural attractions, Benton is a destination worth exploring. Visitors can enjoy the city’s southern hospitality and sample its delicious food, making it a perfect destination for a refreshing getaway.
