Introduction to Fort Thomas

Fort Thomas is a charming city in the Northern Ohio River Region of Kentucky, located in the southern part of the United States of America. The city is known for its rich history, stunning natural landscapes, and vibrant culture. Fort Thomas is a popular destination for those who love outdoor activities such as hiking, cycling, and sightseeing. This comprehensive travel guide will give you everything you need to know to plan your trip to Fort Thomas.

List of Places to Visit in Fort Thomas

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fort Thomas Military and Community Museum: The Fort Thomas Military and Community Museum is a must-visit attraction for history buffs. The museum features exhibits on the history of the city, its military heritage, and the role it played during World War I and II. Admission is free, and the museum is open from Tuesday to Saturday.

  • Highland Cemetery: Highland Cemetery is a beautifully landscaped cemetery that has become a popular attraction for visitors. It serves as a final resting place for many famous residents of Kentucky, including brigadier general James Taylor Jr. and Governor Frank W. Owsley.

  • Monmouth Street Historic District: The Monmouth Street Historic District features a collection of historic buildings that date back to the early 1900s. The district is home to several museums, art galleries, and shops, making it a great place to spend an afternoon.

Natural Attractions of Fort Thomas: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Tower Park: Tower Park is a 120-acre park that serves as the centerpiece of Fort Thomas. It is home to several walking trails, picnic areas, playgrounds, and sports facilities. Visitors can enjoy beautiful views of the city and the Ohio River from the top of the park.

  • Marianist Environmental Education Center: The Marianist Environmental Education Center is a nature preserve that features several walking trails, birdwatching areas, and a butterfly garden. The center is a great place to observe the native flora and fauna of Kentucky, as well as to learn about environmental conservation.

  • Campbell County Environmental Education Park: The Campbell County Environmental Education Park is a 700-acre nature preserve located just outside of Fort Thomas. The park features several hiking trails, fishing ponds, birdwatching areas, and camping facilities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fort Thomas

  • Highland Hills Park: Highland Hills Park is a great place to start your hiking or biking journey in Fort Thomas. It features several trails that offer scenic views of the Ohio River, as well as the neighboring cities of Cincinnati and Newport.

  • Riverfront Commons: Riverfront Commons is a 11.5-mile trail that connects several cities and towns along the Ohio River, including Fort Thomas. The trail is perfect for cycling, walking, or even rollerblading.

  • Fort Thomas Landmark Tree Trail: The Fort Thomas Landmark Tree Trail is a 1.5-mile hiking trail that takes visitors through some of the city’s most beautiful natural landscapes. The trail is home to several historic trees, including some that are over 200 years old.

Best Things to Do in Fort Thomas: Activities and Tours

  • Riverboat Cruises: Riverboat cruises are a popular way to explore the Ohio River and its surrounding cities. Several companies offer cruises that depart from the nearby city of Newport, Kentucky.

  • Kentucky Symphony Orchestra: The Kentucky Symphony Orchestra is a professional orchestra that performs several concerts throughout the year. The orchestra is based in Fort Thomas, and its concerts are held at the nearby St. Peter and Paul Church.

  • Newport Aquarium: The Newport Aquarium is a popular attraction that is located just a short distance from Fort Thomas. The aquarium features several exhibits that showcase the aquatic life of the Ohio River, including a stingray touch tank and a shark bridge.

Food and Drink Scene of Fort Thomas

Must-Try Dishes

  • Goetta: Goetta is a traditional sausage dish that is popular in Kentucky and the surrounding states. It is made from a combination of ground meat, steel-cut oats, and spices, and is often served with eggs for breakfast.

  • Bourbon: Kentucky is famous for its bourbon, and there are several distilleries in the surrounding area that offer tours and tastings.

Local Beverages

  • Ale-8-One: Ale-8-One is a locally made soft drink that is often referred to as “Kentucky’s Original Soft Drink.” It has a crisp, refreshing flavor and is perfect for hot summer days.

  • Goldenrod Cake: Goldenrod Cake is a classic Kentucky dessert that is made from sponge cake, boiled frosting, and a sprinkle of goldenrod flowers.

Where to Stay in Fort Thomas: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Dharma House: The Dharma House is a cozy bed and breakfast that offers comfortable rooms and a peaceful atmosphere. The B&B is located just a short distance from Tower Park and is surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes.

  • Hilton Cincinnati Netherland Plaza: The Hilton Cincinnati Netherland Plaza is a luxurious hotel that is located just across the Ohio River in Cincinnati. The hotel features several restaurants and bars, as well as a fitness center and pool.

What to Buy in Fort Thomas: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Kentucky Bourbon: Kentucky bourbon is a popular souvenir item that you can bring home to remind you of your trip to Fort Thomas. Several distilleries in the area offer souvenirs and bourbon-related products.

  • Locally Made Crafts: There are several shops in Fort Thomas that sell locally made crafts, including pottery, jewelry, and paintings.

Local Culture and Customs of Fort Thomas

Fort Thomas is a friendly and welcoming city that values its rich history and cultural heritage. The locals are proud of their military heritage and often participate in events and festivals that celebrate the sacrifices made by the city’s veterans. Visitors to Fort Thomas should respect the city’s customs and etiquette, and should make an effort to learn about its history and culture.

Best Time to Visit Fort Thomas

The best time to visit Fort Thomas is during the spring and fall seasons when the weather is mild and comfortable. The summer months can be hot and humid, while the winters can be cold and snowy.

Safety and Health

Safety in Fort Thomas

Fort Thomas is a safe city with low crime rates. Visitors should take standard precautions to protect themselves and their belongings, such as locking their car doors and keeping valuable items out of sight.

Health in Fort Thomas

Fort Thomas has several hospitals and medical centers that offer quality medical care. Visitors should ensure that they have appropriate health insurance coverage before traveling to the city.

Getting to Fort Thomas: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bus Stations Near Fort Thomas

How to Get Around in Fort Thomas

Fort Thomas is a small city that is easily walkable. Visitors can also rent bicycles or use public transportation to get around.

Where to Go Next from Fort Thomas: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Cincinnati, Ohio: Cincinnati is a vibrant city that is located just across the Ohio River from Fort Thomas. It is home to several museums, art galleries, and cultural attractions.

  • Newport, Kentucky: Newport is a historic city that is located just a short distance from Fort Thomas. It features several attractions, including the Newport Aquarium and the Newport on the Levee shopping center.
