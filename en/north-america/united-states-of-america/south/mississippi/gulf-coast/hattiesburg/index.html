Introduction to Hattiesburg

Nestled in the heart of the Gulf Coast region of Mississippi, Hattiesburg is a lively city that boasts a rich history and vibrant culture. With a myriad of cultural attractions, excellent food options, and beautiful natural scenery all around, this city is a must-visit destination for travelers looking to experience all that the South has to offer.

Here is a list of the top attractions to visit when you come to Hattiesburg:

  • Hattiesburg Historic Neighborhoods
  • Saenger Theater

Local Culture and Customs of Hattiesburg

  • Respect the locals’ use of ‘ma’am’ and ‘sir’
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for directions or walking tours
  • Be cautious of humidity during summer months

Best Time to Visit Hattiesburg

The best times to visit Hattiesburg are in the spring and fall months to avoid the intense summer heat and occasional hurricanes that occur during the summer.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hattiesburg

When visiting Hattiesburg, keep these things in mind:

Do:

  • Try Southern cuisine
  • Visit the local parks and natural attractions
  • Attend a local music or cultural event

Don’t:

  • Ignore the local customs and manners
  • Underestimate the humidity during the summer
  • Miss out on experiencing the vibrant culture and history of Hattiesburg

Safety and Health

Safety in Hattiesburg

Hattiesburg is a relatively safe city, but it is always important to take basic safety measures such as locking doors and securing valuables. It’s also recommended to avoid walking in unfamiliar areas alone, especially at night.

Health in Hattiesburg

As with any city, take precautions to prevent mosquito bites, which can transmit illnesses such as West Nile virus. It’s also important to stay hydrated during hot and humid summer months.

Getting to Hattiesburg: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Hattiesburg

Gulfport-Biloxi International Airport is the closest major airport to Hattiesburg, located about 70 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Hattiesburg

Greyhound Station is located in downtown Hattiesburg, offering bus services to and from the city daily.

Other Options

There are also several car rental companies with offices in Hattiesburg for those wanting to explore the area on their own.

How to Get Around in Hattiesburg

In Hattiesburg, the best way to get around is by car rental, taxi, or rideshare services like Uber or Lyft.

Conclusion

Hattiesburg is a city with something for everyone, from the historic downtown area to the many natural attractions and cultural experiences. Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor adventurer or just someone in search of a great Southern meal, you’re sure to find what you’re looking for in Hattiesburg. So pack your bags, and head south to discover all that this vibrant Gulf Coast city has to offer!
