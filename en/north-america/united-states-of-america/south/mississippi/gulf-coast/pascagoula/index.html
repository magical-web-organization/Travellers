Introduction to Pascagoula

Pascagoula is a vibrant city located in the Gulf Coast region of Mississippi. With a rich history and a scenic landscape, this coastal city has something to offer for everyone. From fascinating museums to breathtaking natural attractions and delectable cuisine, Pascagoula is a hidden gem waiting to be explored.

List of Places to Visit in Pascagoula

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • La Pointe-Krebs House: Built in 1757, this is the oldest standing structure in the Mississippi Valley and a testament to the rich history of the area.
  • Pascagoula River Audubon Center: This educational facility showcases the unique ecosystem of the Pascagoula River and offers interactive exhibits and outdoor activities.
  • Round Island Lighthouse: This historic lighthouse, located on an island in the Gulf of Mexico, has guided boats into the Pascagoula River for over 150 years.

Natural Attractions of Pascagoula: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Pascagoula Beach: With its white sand and crashing waves, this beach is the perfect spot for swimming, sunbathing, and beachcombing.
  • Singing River Nature Preserve: This beautiful nature preserve features hiking trails, bird watching, and kayaking opportunities.
  • Point Park and War Memorial: This park offers stunning views of the Pascagoula River and features a memorial honoring local veterans.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pascagoula

  • Singing River Trail: This 17-mile paved trail winds through Pascagoula and Gautier and offers scenic views of the Singing River.
  • Cherokee Rose Trail: Located near Pascagoula Beach, this 1.3-mile trail is perfect for a leisurely stroll or a bike ride.

Best Things to Do in Pascagoula: Activities and Tours

  • Boat Tour of Pascagoula River: Take a boat tour of the Pascagoula River to explore the area’s rich ecosystem and spot local wildlife.
  • Fishing Charters: Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced angler, Pascagoula offers a variety of fishing charters to suit your needs.
  • Golfing: Pascagoula boasts several golf courses catering to players of all skill levels.

Food and Drink Scene of Pascagoula

Must-Try Dishes

  • Seafood Platter: This dish features fresh-caught seafood, such as shrimp, oysters, and fish, served with hushpuppies and coleslaw.
  • Poboys: These delicious sandwiches are filled with fried shrimp, oysters, or crawfish and served on a French roll.
  • Gumbo: This savory stew features a variety of seafood, sausage, and vegetables served over a bed of rice.

Local Beverages

  • Sweet Tea: A southern classic, sweet tea is a refreshing drink made with black tea and sugar.
  • Mule Kick: This specialty drink made with ginger beer and lime juice is a popular choice in Pascagoula.

Where to Stay in Pascagoula: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hilton Garden Inn Pascagoula: This hotel is a great option for those seeking modern amenities and a convenient location.
  • Grand Magnolia Ballroom and Suites: This historic guesthouse offers elegant rooms and a romantic ambiance.
  • Home2 Suites by Hilton Pascagoula: This hotel provides spacious suites and a great selection of amenities.

What to Buy in Pascagoula: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Seafood: Take home fresh-caught shrimp, oysters, and fish from local fish markets.
  • Handmade Pottery: The Mississippi Gulf Coast is renowned for its beautiful handcrafted pottery.
  • Jewelry: Pascagoula has several jewelry boutiques offering unique and locally-made pieces.

Local Culture and Customs of Pascagoula

  • Pascagoula is a friendly and welcoming community, with a strong emphasis on family values.
  • The area is known for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and love of seafood.
  • Visitors are encouraged to participate in local celebrations, such as the Pascagoula Mardi Gras parade and the Annual Blessing of the Fleet.

Best Time to Visit Pascagoula

The best time to visit Pascagoula is during the spring and fall, when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller. Summer can be hot and humid, but it is also a great time to enjoy the beaches and outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pascagoula

Do:

  • Do take the time to explore the local shops and cuisine.
  • Do visit the natural attractions and experience the area’s unique ecosystem.
  • Do respect local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter on the beaches or in public areas.
  • Don’t ignore safety warnings regarding hurricanes or severe weather.
  • Don’t be afraid to ask for recommendations from locals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Pascagoula

Pascagoula is a safe community, but visitors should take precautions when exploring the beaches and natural areas. Always pay attention to weather reports and follow the instructions of lifeguards.

Health in Pascagoula

Pascagoula has several medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics, offering a range of medical services. Visitors should make sure they have adequate health insurance and take necessary precautions, such as wearing sunscreen and staying hydrated in the hot and humid weather.
