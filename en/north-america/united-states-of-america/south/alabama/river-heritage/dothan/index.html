Introduction to Dothan

Located in the heart of Alabama’s Wiregrass region, Dothan is a vibrant city that serves as a hub of culture, recreation, and business. Known for its rich history, diverse culture, and Southern hospitality, Dothan boasts a wide range of attractions and activities for all types of travelers.

List of Places to Visit in Dothan

From historic landmarks to natural wonders, Dothan has plenty of places to explore and discover. Here are some of the most popular attractions in Dothan:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Wiregrass Museum of Art: This contemporary art museum features rotating exhibitions and permanent collections of regional art.
  2. Landmark Park: This 100-acre park features an open-air museum and nature trails that take visitors through the history of the Wiregrass region.
  3. Dothan Area Botanical Gardens: This beautiful 50-acre botanical garden has a variety of native plants, water features, and sculptures.

Natural Attractions of Dothan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Forever Wild Trails at Beaver Creek: This 11-mile trail system is perfect for hiking, mountain biking, and trail running.
  2. Dothan Forever Wild Trail: This 5-mile trail loops through the Dothan Area Botanical Gardens and is great for a leisurely stroll.
  3. Westgate Recreation Center: This park offers a variety of activities, including fishing, picnicking, and birdwatching.

Hiking/Biking Routes in Dothan

The Wiregrass Trail is a 6-mile bike and pedestrian path that runs through the heart of downtown Dothan. The Forever Wild Trails at Beaver Creek and the Dothan Forever Wild Trail are also great options for hiking and mountain biking.

Best Things to Do in Dothan: Activities and Tours

  1. Adventureland Theme Park: A family-friendly amusement park with rides, games, and attractions for all ages.
  2. Water World: A water park with slides, wave pools, and lazy rivers.
  3. Dothan Escape Room: A fun puzzle-solving activity for groups of friends or family.

Week-Long Itinerary for Dothan

Day 1: Visit the Wiregrass Museum of Art and explore downtown Dothan’s shops and restaurants. Day 2: Spend the day at Adventureland Theme Park. Day 3: Explore the Dothan Area Botanical Gardens and Landmark Park. Day 4: Take a hike on one of the Forever Wild Trails at Beaver Creek. Day 5: Go fishing at Westgate Recreation Center. Day 6: Visit Water World and enjoy some water park fun. Day 7: Try to escape the Dothan Escape Room with friends or family.

Local Culture and Customs in Dothan

Dothan is known for its Southern hospitality and friendly locals. It’s important to be respectful and courteous to everyone you meet.

Best Time to Visit Dothan

The best time to visit Dothan is during the warm weather months of April through October when you can enjoy all the outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Dothan

Do:

  2. Try Alabama-style barbecue.
  3. Visit the Wiregrass Museum of Art.

Don’t:

  2. Disrespect Southern culture and customs.
  3. Leave valuables in your car while in public areas.
