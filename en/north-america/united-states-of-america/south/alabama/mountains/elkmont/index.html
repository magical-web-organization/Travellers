Elkmont, a charming town in South Alabama sitting at the foothills of the Appalachian Mountains, is a hidden gem that offers wonderful outdoor adventures, remarkable southern culture, rich history, and a peaceful escape from the city’s hustle and bustle.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll explore the best places to visit, things to do, where to eat, sleep, and what souvenirs to take home. Plan your trip with this guide and get ready for a memorable experience.

Introduction to Elkmont

With a population of around 400 people, Elkmont is a small town that is big on Southern charm. Surrounded by the beauty of the Appalachian Mountains, the town offers scenic vistas and a tranquil atmosphere to make it an ideal place for nature lovers.

The town’s rich history is an added bonus for history buffs who would love to explore the museums and historical sites that depict the town’s past.

There’s no shortage of things to do in Elkmont. From exploring historical landmarks to outdoor adventure, here are the top places to visit in Elkmont:

Best Things to Do in Elkmont: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re interested in exploring Southern culture or outdoor activities, Elkmont has something to offer. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Visit Belle Chevre Cheese Shop and Tasting Room
  • Take a guided tour around Athens Story Museum and Athens State University Historic District
  • Kayak in the nearby Joe Wheeler State Park
  • Go zip-lining in the forest adventure park

Week-Long Itinerary for Elkmont

Here’s a sample itinerary for visitors who plan to spend a week in Elkmont,

Day 1: Explore the Athens Story Museum and Athens State University Historic District. Day 2: Visit the Belle Chevre Cheese Shop and Tasting Room and take a guided tour. Day 3: Hike the Gatlin Point Trail. Day 4-5: Go kayaking and zip-lining in Joe Wheeler State Park. Day 6-7: Explore the Walls of Jericho Trail and take a tour around Elkmont Rails to Trails.

Local Culture and Customs of Elkmont

Southern hospitality is the signature of the culture in Elkmont. People are friendly and welcoming, and they expect their guests to show respect and courtesy in return.

Best Time to Visit Elkmont

Spring and fall are the best times to visit Elkmont as the weather is mild, and outdoor activities are at their best. However, the town’s charm and attractions make it a great place to visit year-round.

Getting to Elkmont: Transportation Options, and Logistics

Elkmont is accessible by car, and the nearest airports are the Huntsville International Airport, Chattanooga International Airport, and Birmingham International Airport. Bus services are available, but car rentals are recommended for easy transportation within the town.

Airports Near Elkmont

  • Huntsville International Airport
  • Chattanooga International Airport
  • Birmingham International Airport

Bus Stations Near Elkmont

  • Greyhound Bus Station
  • Athens Transit System

Other Options

  • Car rentals

How to Get Around in Elkmont

The best way to explore Elkmont is by car or bike. Bicycle rentals are available in the town, and taxis services are also available.
