Introduction to Mountains and Lakes Country

Nestled in the southern part of West Virginia, Mountains and Lakes Country is an outdoor paradise with rolling hills, soaring peaks, crystal clear lakes and rivers. This region spans across several counties including Monroe, Fayette, Summers, Greenbrier, Mercer, Wyoming and Raleigh. If you’re an adventure enthusiast looking for some time off in the wilderness, Mountains and Lakes Country is the place for you.

Local Culture and Customs of Mountains and Lakes Country

Mountains and Lakes Country is known for its rich cultural heritage and traditional customs. Some of the popular customs include coal mining, hunting, and fishing.

Best Time to Visit Mountains and Lakes Country

The best time to visit Mountains and Lakes Country is from June to September, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mountains and Lakes Country

Do:

  • Respect the locals’ privacy
  • Take trash with you when visiting parks and trails
  • Dress appropriately for outdoor activities

Don’t:

Safety and Health

Safety in Mountains and Lakes Country

  • Be cautious of snakes and other wildlife when hiking
  • Avoid hiking or biking alone
  • Watch out for sudden weather changes

Health in Mountains and Lakes Country

  • Drink plenty of water when doing outdoor activities
  • Wear sunscreen and protect yourself from the sun
  • Seek immediate medical attention in case of emergencies
