Introduction to Spencer

Nestled in the heart of West Virginia, Spencer is a small town that boasts of natural beauty, rich culture, and friendly locals that make it an ideal destination for travelers seeking a glimpse into the Appalachian way of life. The town is surrounded by rolling hills, picturesque mountains, and pristine lakes, making it a paradise for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. In this travel guide, we will explore the top places to visit in Spencer, hiking and biking routes, things to do, where to stay, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Spencer

Whether you’re a history buff, a museum lover, or simply appreciate quaint, small-town charm, Spencer has something for everyone. Here are some of the top attractions to visit in Spencer:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Roane County Historical Society Museum

Located in the heart of Spencer, the Roane County Historical Society Museum is a must-visit attraction for history buffs and museum lovers alike. The museum is home to a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits that showcase the rich history and culture of Roane County. From Native American artifacts to Civil War memorabilia, the museum offers a fascinating glimpse into the region’s past.

The Jackson County Jail

The Jackson County Jail is a historic landmark in Spencer that has been converted into a museum that offers visitors a unique insight into what life was like for inmates in the early 20th century. The jail is home to a vast collection of artifacts and exhibits that showcase the harsh conditions that inmates endured during their stay.

Natural Attractions of Spencer: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Calhoun County Park

Located just a short drive from Spencer, Calhoun County Park is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The park is home to a variety of hiking trails, picnic areas, and a beautiful lake that offers excellent fishing opportunities. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing day in nature or an adventurous day of hiking and fishing, Calhoun County Park is the perfect destination.

Charles Fork Lake

Charles Fork Lake is a hidden gem in Spencer that offers stunning scenic views and plenty of outdoor activities. The lake is popular among anglers for its abundance of largemouth bass, bluegill, and catfish. Visitors can also enjoy hiking, swimming, and wildlife viewing in the surrounding areas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Spencer

Spencer is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, with a variety of hiking and biking routes that offer stunning scenic views and plenty of opportunities to connect with nature. Here are some of the top hiking and biking routes in Spencer:

The Kanawha Trace Trail

The Kanawha Trace Trail is a 35-mile hiking and biking trail that stretches from Jackson County to Clay County. The trail offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains, rolling hills, and forests, making it a popular destination for hikers and bikers.

The Little Kanawha River Trail

The Little Kanawha River Trail is a 16-mile trail that follows the Little Kanawha River and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside. The trail is relatively easy and suitable for hikers and bikers of all skill levels.

Best Things to Do in Spencer: Activities and Tours

From exploring the town’s rich history and culture to experiencing the beauty of the surrounding landscape, Spencer offers plenty of activities and tours that will keep visitors entertained. Here are some of the best things to do in Spencer:

Visit The Mountaineer Opry

The Mountaineer Opry is a unique destination in Spencer that offers visitors a chance to experience the rich musical heritage of West Virginia. The Opry features live performances by some of the region’s most talented musicians and offers a unique insight into the history and culture of the Appalachian region.

Take A Horse-Drawn Carriage Ride

For a truly unique experience, visitors can take a horse-drawn carriage ride through the town of Spencer. The carriage ride offers a unique glimpse into the town’s rich history and culture and is a perfect way to take in the stunning scenery and charming architecture.

Best Time to Visit Spencer
