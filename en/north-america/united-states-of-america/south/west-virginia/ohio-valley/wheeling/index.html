Introduction to Wheeling

Located in the Ohio Valley region of West Virginia, Wheeling is a historic city full of cultural attractions and outdoor adventures. Boasting a rich history, stunning natural beauty, and a vibrant food scene, Wheeling is a must-visit destination for anyone exploring the United States.

List of Places to Visit in Wheeling

Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor enthusiast, or a foodie, Wheeling has something for everyone. Here’s a list of must-visit places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Capitol Theatre: This stunning theater is a must-visit for anyone interested in the performing arts. From Broadway shows to local performances, the Capitol Theatre has something for everyone.
  • The Wheeling Artisan Center: Located in a former hardware store, this center celebrates the artisanal traditions of the Ohio Valley. With artist studios and monthly events, it’s a great place to explore local art.
  • The Grave Creek Mound Archaeological Complex: This museum explores the prehistoric Native American culture that once thrived in the Ohio Valley. With exhibits on archaeology, history, and culture, the Grave Creek Mound is a fascinating destination for history buffs.

Natural Attractions of Wheeling: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Oglebay Park: This 1,700-acre park is a stunning natural oasis in the heart of Wheeling. With a golf course, hiking trails, and beautiful gardens, it’s a great place to explore the outdoors.
  • The Ohio River Trail: This 160-mile trail runs along the banks of the Ohio River and offers stunning views of the surrounding hillsides. Whether you’re hiking, biking, or kayaking, the Ohio River Trail is a must-visit for outdoor enthusiasts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Wheeling

With its beautiful natural scenery and well-maintained hiking and biking trails, Wheeling is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best routes in the area:

  • The Wheeling Heritage Trail: This 10-mile trail runs through the heart of Wheeling and offers stunning views of the Ohio River and downtown Wheeling.
  • The National Road Bikeway: This 25-mile trail follows the historic National Road and offers beautiful views of the surrounding hillsides.
  • The Ohio River Trail: As mentioned earlier, this 160-mile trail runs along the Ohio River and offers great opportunities for hiking, biking, and kayaking.

Best Things to Do in Wheeling: Activities and Tours

From sightseeing tours to culinary experiences, there are plenty of things to do in Wheeling. Here are some must-try activities:

  • Take a historic trolley tour: Learn about Wheeling’s rich history on a trolley tour of the city. With stops at the Capitol Theatre, Centre Market, and other historic landmarks, it’s a great way to see the city.
  • Take a food tour: Wheeling is known for its diverse food scene, and a food tour is a great way to explore the city’s culinary offerings.
  • Visit the Oglebay Resort: This sprawling resort offers a wide range of activities, including golfing, spa treatments, and outdoor adventures.

Food and Drink Scene of Wheeling

Wheeling’s food scene is a diverse mix of local specialties and international cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • The pepperoni roll: This local specialty is a must-try for anyone visiting Wheeling. A delicious combination of bread, spicy pepperoni, and cheese, the pepperoni roll can be found at local bakeries and restaurants.
  • The fish sandwich: Another local specialty, the fish sandwich is a must-try for seafood lovers. Made with freshly-caught fish and topped with tartar sauce and cole slaw, it’s a true Wheeling classic.

Local Beverages

  • WV Brewing Company: This local brewery specializes in handcrafted beers made with locally-sourced ingredients.
  • Wheeling Coffee Company: This coffee shop offers a wide range of specialty drinks made with locally-roasted coffee beans.

Where to Stay in Wheeling: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Whether you’re looking for a luxury hotel or a budget-friendly guesthouse, Wheeling has plenty of options. Here are some top choices:

  • The Oglebay Resort: This sprawling resort offers luxury accommodations, outdoor adventures, and a wide range of activities.
  • The McLure Hotel: Located in the heart of downtown Wheeling, the McLure Hotel is a historic hotel with beautifully-appointed rooms and great amenities.
  • The Wheeling YWCA: A budget-friendly option, the Wheeling YWCA offers clean, comfortable rooms in a convenient location.

Safety and Health

Safety in Wheeling

Wheeling is a safe city, but visitors should still take common sense precautions to ensure their safety. Be aware of your surroundings, avoid walking alone at night, and take precautions to protect your valuables.

Health in Wheeling

Wheeling has a good healthcare system, with several hospitals and clinics in the area. Be sure to pack any necessary medications, and make sure you have adequate travel insurance before your trip.

Getting to Wheeling: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Wheeling

The closest airport to Wheeling is the Pittsburgh International Airport, which is about an hour’s drive from the city.

Bus Stations Near Wheeling

Greyhound and Megabus both have stops in Wheeling, making it an easily accessible destination by bus.

Other Options

Renting a car is another great option for getting to Wheeling. The city is easily accessible from major highways, making it an easy drive from nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Wheeling

While many of Wheeling’s top attractions are within walking distance of each other, visitors may also want to consider renting a car or using the city’s public transportation system.

Where to Go Next from Wheeling: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Wheeling is located in the heart of the Ohio Valley, making it a great starting point for exploring the region. Nearby destinations and excursions include:

  • Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania: Just an hour’s drive from Wheeling, Pittsburgh is a vibrant city full of culture, history, and great food.
  • The Hocking Hills: Located about 3 hours south of Wheeling, the Hocking Hills is a stunning natural area with great hiking, biking, and outdoor adventures.

Conclusion

From its rich history and vibrant culture to its stunning natural beauty and diverse food scene, Wheeling is a must-visit destination for anyone exploring the United States. Plan your trip today and discover all that this beautiful city has to offer.
