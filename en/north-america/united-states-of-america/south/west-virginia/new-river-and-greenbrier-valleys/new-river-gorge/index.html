Introduction to New River Gorge

New River Gorge is a popular tourist destination located in New River and Greenbrier Valleys in West Virginia. This beautiful destination is surrounded by the Appalachian Mountains making it the perfect spot for outdoor activities.

With over 70,000 acres of land, the New River Gorge National Park and Preserve offers an array of activities and attractions, making it a must-visit place for all adventure seekers and nature lovers.

In this comprehensive guide, we will provide you with the best places to visit, food options, accommodations, activities, and more. So, let’s get started and explore the beauty of New River Gorge.

List of Places to Visit in New River Gorge

If you are planning to visit New River Gorge, then don’t miss the opportunity to explore the following places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Beckley Exhibition Coal Mine: This attraction provides insights into West Virginia’s coal mining history, which is an essential part of the state’s heritage. Here, you can hop on a mine cart and take a guided tour through the underground mine.

  • New River Gorge Bridge: One of the most iconic landmarks of the region, this bridge provides breathtaking views of the surrounding areas. There’s also a visitor center where you can learn about the history and construction of the bridge.

  • Tamarack: This is a cultural center that showcases the work of local artists and artisans. You can buy souvenirs, handmade crafts, and fine arts here.

Natural Attractions of New River Gorge: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Endless Wall: A popular hiking spot that provides magnificent views of the New River Gorge.

  • Whitewater Rafting: With an experienced guide leading the way, whitewater rafting is the perfect way to explore the beauty of the New River Gorge’s nature.

  • Kayaking: Get close and personal with the New River by kayaking.

  • Fayetteville Town Park: Enjoy family-friendly outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and disc golf.

Hiking/Biking of New River Gorge

New River Gorge is a paradise for hikers and bikers. Whether you are an experienced hiker/biker or a novice, there’s something for everyone. Some popular trails include:

  • Endless Wall Trail: This trail is 2.4 miles long and one of the most popular hiking spots in New River Gorge.

  • Long Point Trail: This trail is 3.8 miles long and provides panoramic views of the New River and surrounding areas.

  • Kaymoor Trail: This trail is 4.5 miles long and rewards hikers with breathtaking views of the Kaymoor Mine and New River Gorge.

Best Things to Do in New River Gorge: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do while exploring New River Gorge:

  • Whitewater Rafting Tours: Experience adrenaline rush with a whitewater rafting tour through the New River Gorge.

  • Zip-lining Tours: Get a bird’s eye view of the surrounding areas by going on a zip-lining tour.

  • Horseback Riding Tours: Take a horseback riding tour through the scenic areas of New River Gorge and make unforgettable memories.

Here’s a perfect week-long itinerary for those who want to spend a week exploring New River Gorge:

Food and Drink Scene of New River Gorge

When it comes to food, New River Gorge offers a unique culinary experience that caters to everyone’s taste buds.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pepperoni Rolls: A West Virginia classic, pepperoni rolls are a grab-and-go delicacy that you simply can’t miss.

  • Hot Dogs: West Virginia’s hot dogs are served with mustard, homemade chili, and coleslaw.

Local Beverages

  • Moonshine: West Virginia is famous for its moonshine, which is made using local apples and peaches.

  • Craft Beer: The state has an impressive craft brewery scene offering a variety of IPAs, stouts, and lagers.

Where to Stay in New River Gorge: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

New River Gorge offers several types of accommodations, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious lodges. Here are some of the best options:

  • Chestnut Boutique Hotel: This luxurious hotel is located in downtown Fayetteville and offers unmatched comfort and amenities.

  • The Historic Morris Harvey House Bed and Breakfast: This charming 1902 Victorian-era mansion is perfect for those who seek a cozy and relaxing environment.

  • ACE Adventure Resort: A budget-friendly option offering a range of accommodations, from camp-style cabins to deluxe suites and vacation homes.

What to Buy in New River Gorge: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some of the best souvenirs that will make your trip to New River Gorge unforgettable:

  • Handmade Crafts: Tamarack is the perfect place to buy handmade crafts and unique souvenirs.

  • Moonshine: Bring home a bottle of genuine West Virginia moonshine.

Local Culture and Customs of New River Gorge

West Virginia has a unique local culture that honors traditions and heritage. People of the state are friendly and welcoming, and they respect nature and the environment.

Best Time to Visit New River Gorge

The best time to visit New River Gorge is from April through October. During these months, the weather is pleasant and allows visitors to indulge in various outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of New River Gorge

Do:

  • Respect the environment and follow the Leave No Trace principles.

  • Take advantage of the local cuisine and try pepperoni rolls and local craft beers.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter. Keep the environment clean and beautiful for future generations.

  • Don’t trespass on private lands. Respect the landowners and local community.

Safety and Health

Safety in New River Gorge

  • Wear appropriate gear while indulging in outdoor activities.

  • Follow the safety protocols of various activities, especially when going for whitewater rafting or zip-lining.

Health in New River Gorge

  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.

  • Carry a first-aid kit and essential medicines.

Where to Go Next from New River Gorge: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Conclusion

New River Gorge is a must-visit destination for all adventure seekers and nature lovers.
