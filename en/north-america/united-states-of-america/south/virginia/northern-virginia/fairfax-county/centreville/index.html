Introduction to Centreville

Centreville is a small suburban town located in Fairfax County, Northern Virginia. It is known for its rich history and cultural attractions. Centreville is also a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, with its scenic views, hiking trails, and outdoor adventures. In this travel guide, we will take you through the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and what to eat in Centreville, Virginia.

List of Places to Visit in Centreville

If you are planning a trip to Centreville, add these places to your itinerary for an unforgettable experience.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sully Historic Site: A National Historic Landmark, this 18th-century mansion was once the home of Richard Bland Lee, Northern Virginia’s first representative in Congress.
  • Ellanor C. Lawrence Park: A 650-acre park featuring trails, gardens, and historical buildings, including the Walney Visitor Center, where you can learn about the history and culture of the area.
  • Civil War Interpretive Center at Historic Blenheim: A must-visit for history buffs, this museum offers insight into the Civil War and how it affected the local area.

Natural Attractions of Centreville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Bull Run Regional Park: Spread over 1,500-acres, this park is known for its beautiful trails, picnic areas, and scenic views.
  • Manassas National Battlefield Park: Explore the historic Civil War battlefield through its walking trails and ranger-led tours.
  • Green Spring Gardens Park: A beautiful park featuring botanical gardens and trails that are perfect for taking a peaceful stroll.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Centreville

Centreville is a haven for hikers and bikers. Here are some popular trails you can explore:

  • Bull Run-Occoquan Trail: A challenging trail that spans over 18 miles and offers a variety of terrains to explore.
  • Cub Run Stream Valley Trail: A beginner-friendly trail that takes you through the picturesque Cub Run Stream Valley.
  • Virginia Central Railway Trail: A 44-mile long trail that passes through Centreville and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Best Things to Do in Centreville: Activities and Tours

Centreville offers a range of fun and exciting activities that are perfect for families, couples, and solo travelers.

  • Visit Cox Farms: This family-owned farm offers various activities such as hayrides, pumpkin patches, and a Cornundrum maze.
  • Go on a Wine Tasting Tour: Visit the Paradise Springs Winery, known for its award-winning wines. The winery offers guided tours and tastings.
  • Try Your Hand at Archery: Head to the Bull Run Shooting Center for an exciting archery experience.

Food and Drink Scene of Centreville

Centreville offers a diverse food and drink scene. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Gyro: Gyro offers an authentic Mediterranean experience with its delicious gyro wraps and plates.
  • Pho: Try the classic beef or chicken pho at Xuan Saigon for a delicious Vietnamese experience.
  • Peri-Peri Chicken: Nando’s offers the peri-peri chicken, famous for its African roots, marinated in a spicy sauce and grilled to perfection.

Local Beverages

  • Beer: Head to the BadWolf Brewing Company for some locally brewed beer.
  • Wine: Try award-winning wines at the Paradise Springs Winery.

Where to Stay in Centreville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There is no shortage of accommodation options in Centreville. Here are some options to consider:

  • Westfield Marriott Washington Dulles: A luxurious hotel that offers comfortable rooms and amenities such as a gym, pool, and a restaurant.
  • Holiday Inn Express Fairfax-Centreville: A budget-friendly option that offers clean rooms and a complimentary breakfast.
  • Oakton’s Carriage House: A charming guesthouse offering a peaceful, country-style experience.

Best Time to Visit Centreville

The best time to visit Centreville is from April to September, during the spring and summer months. The weather is pleasant, and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy during this time.
