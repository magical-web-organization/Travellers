Introduction to Chantilly

Located in Northern Virginia, Chantilly is a beautiful town in Fairfax County that is known for its rich history, scenic views, and top-quality restaurants. It is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts, history buffs, and foodies alike. Chantilly is just a 15-minute drive from Washington Dulles International Airport, and it offers plenty of opportunities for visitors to enjoy the natural beauty of the area.

List of Places to Visit in Chantilly

Chantilly offers a wide range of attractions to explore. Here are some of the top places to visit in Chantilly:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • National Air and Space Museum Steven F. Udvar-Hazy Center: This museum is a must-visit for anyone interested in aerospace technology. It features a huge collection of aircraft and spacecraft, and visitors can also see restoration projects in progress.

  • Sully Historic Site: Sully is a beautiful plantation house that was built in 1794. It was the home of Richard Bland Lee, a founding member of Congress, and it is now open to the public for tours.

  • Ellanor C. Lawrence Park: This park features an 18th-century farmstead and a nature center that showcases the natural history of the area. Visitors can enjoy hiking trails, picnicking areas, and wildlife viewing here.

Natural Attractions of Chantilly: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Cub Run Stream Valley Trail: This well-maintained trail is perfect for hiking, biking, and jogging. The trail runs through a beautiful natural area that is home to many species of wildlife.

  • Bull Run Regional Park: Bull Run Regional Park is a great place to spend a day with the family. It features a water park, mini-golf, disc golf, and scenic picnic areas.

  • Frying Pan Park: Frying Pan Park is a 135-acre park that is home to a working farm and country store. Visitors can enjoy walking trails, a playground, and a carousel ride.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Chantilly

Chantilly offers many great hiking and biking routes for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the top routes to check out:

  • Cub Run Trail: This 4.1-mile trail runs through a beautiful natural area that is home to many species of wildlife.

  • Bull Run Occoquan Trail: This 18.8-mile trail runs through Bull Run Regional Park and features a mix of natural terrain and scenic views.

  • Fairfax County Parkway Trail: This 45-mile trail runs through several parks and natural areas in Fairfax County, including Chantilly.

Best Things to Do in Chantilly: Activities and Tours

Chantilly offers many great activities and tours for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the top things to do in Chantilly:

  • Explore the historic town of Occoquan

Food and Drink Scene of Chantilly

Chantilly is home to many great restaurants and bars that offer a wide range of cuisine and drinks.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Crab cakes: Chantilly is known for its delicious crab cakes, which are a must-try for seafood lovers.

  • Steak: Many local restaurants serve up top-quality steak that is cooked to perfection.

  • Italian food: There are many Italian restaurants in the area that offer tasty pasta dishes and specialty pizzas.

Local Beverages

  • Wine: Chantilly is home to several wineries that offer tastings and tours.

  • Craft beer: There are many local breweries in the area that serve up delicious, hand-crafted beers.
