Introduction to Bridgewater

Welcome to Bridgewater, a charming town located in the Shenandoah Valley, Virginia. This quaint town is known for its university, Bridgewater College, and its rich history. Bridgewater is home to several museums, hiking/biking trails, and other outdoor recreational activities. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will give you an overview of Bridgewater and highlight the best things to do and see in the area.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bridgewater

Day 1-2: Visit the Valley Turnpike Museum, explore the Skidmore Fork Trail, and see the Union Church.

Day 3-4: Take a tour of the Manassas Gap Railroad Trail, hike the Wild OakTrail, and enjoy the Natural Chimneys Regional Park.

Day 5-6: Visit the Bridgewater College Art Gallery and hike the North River Gorge Trail.

Day 7: Relax and enjoy the scenic views of Bridgewater.
