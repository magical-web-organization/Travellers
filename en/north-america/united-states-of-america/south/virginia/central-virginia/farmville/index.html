Introduction to Farmville

Farmville is a charming town located in Central Virginia, United States. Known for its rich history, stunning natural beauty, and outdoor activities, Farmville attracts countless visitors every year. Whether you are interested in exploring local museums and historical sites or seeking adventure in Virginia’s great outdoors, Farmville has something for everyone.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we will provide information about the top places to visit, must-try dishes, where to stay, and what to do in Farmville. Whether you are planning a week-long getaway or a short day trip, Farmville is the perfect destination for your next vacation.

List of Places to Visit in Farmville

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. The Longwood Center for the Visual Arts - This museum offers an impressive collection of contemporary art and exhibits. Admission is free, and the museum is open year-round.

  2. Appomattox Court House National Historical Park - Explore the site where the American Civil War ended and visit the museum to learn about the history of the event.

  3. The Robert Russa Moton Museum - This museum honors the civil rights movement and the significance of Prince Edward County’s public school desegregation case.

Natural Attractions of Farmville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. High Bridge Trail State Park - This historic trail provides scenic hiking and biking routes that include beautiful views of the Appomattox River and the surrounding forests.

  2. Sailor’s Creek Battlefield State Park - This park showcases the history of the Civil War with a museum, various walking trails, and an outdoor adventure center that offers kayaking, boating, and fishing activities.

  3. Green Front Furniture - This world-renowned furniture retailer has an impressive 15-building campus spread throughout downtown Farmville. Experience the unique shopping area and discover Americana, International and leather furniture options.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Farmville

  1. High Bridge Trail State Park - With over 31 miles of trail for hiking, biking, and horseback riding, the High Bridge Trail offers endless opportunities for outdoor adventures. The well-paved trails and lush forests offer stunning views all year round.

  2. Tobacco Heritage Trail - The trail has 22 miles of biking, walking, and horseback riding routes that lead visitors through historic tobacco farms and old towns, providing a unique perspective of the local history.

  3. Sandy River Reservoir - Enjoy the beautiful scenery of this 740-acre reservoir located a short drive from Farmville. With fishing, boating, and hiking opportunities, the reservoir is a great destination for a day trip.

Best Things to Do in Farmville: Activities and Tours

  1. Farmville Wine and Brew Fest - This annual event offers tastings from the region’s best wineries and breweries, paired with delicious locally sourced food.

  2. Farmville Historic Walking Tour - Explore the historic buildings of Farmville on an informative guided tour. You’ll learn about the town’s rich history and get to see unique architectural features from various eras.

  3. Wilck’s Lake - Spend a day at this pristine lake where you can fish, have picnics, or enjoy a day of swimming.

Food and Drink Scene of Farmville

Must-Try Dishes

  1. BBQ - The Fishin’ Pig BBQ is a local favorite for authentic BBQ sandwiches, plates, and sides to enjoy with your family and friends.

  2. Fried Chicken - Walker’s Diner is the go-to spot for southern comfort food. The diner’s fried chicken is a must-try dish for anyone visiting the area.

  3. Southern Seafood - Charlie’s Waterfront Cafe, located in the heart of downtown, provides an extensive seafood menu with hush puppies and local produce.

Local Beverages

  1. Appomattox River Brewing Company - This brewery has a variety of craft beer options that perfectly complement the flavors of Farmville.

  2. Fiery Golden Cider - The Virginia Wine & Cider shop provides a unique cider experience like the Fiery Golden Cider that is made with habanero and golden delicious apples.

Where to Stay in Farmville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Hampton Inn Farmville - This economical hotel, located at the edge of downtown, offers amenities such as complimentary breakfast, a fitness center, and an outdoor pool.

  2. Longwood Bed and Breakfast - This quaint accommodation provides guests with a cozy atmosphere complete with luxurious amenities, such as fireplaces and queen-sized beds.

  3. Quality Inn - Located in the center of town, the Quality Inn offers affordable rooms and close proximity to the town’s attractions and restaurants.

What to Buy in Farmville: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Virginia Peanuts - Farmville is known for its gourmet peanuts. Be sure to visit Virginia Gourmet on Main street to try different varieties and bring them home as unique souvenirs.

  2. Local Wine and Cider - Make sure to purchase a few bottles of Virginia’s wine or cider to enjoy back at your home. The Virginia Wine & Cider shop provides some unique choices to taste.

Local Culture and Customs of Farmville

Farmville residents are known for their hospitality and friendliness towards visitors. It’s considered common courtesy to say “hello” to locals while walking around town, and don’t be surprised if you are met with smiling faces and southern hospitality. The community is also collection driven and always work towards the betterment of the area.

Best Time to Visit Farmville

The best time to visit Farmville depends on your preferences. The summer months are a great time to enjoy outdoor activities like hiking and boating, though tourist-like crowds may be high. Fall is perfect for those who enjoy cooler weather and beautiful fall foliage, and winter provides unique indoor experiences with museums and cultural exhibits.

Do’s and Don’ts of Farmville

Do:

  1. Try the local BBQ and seafood.

  2. Take time to explore the local history and enjoy outdoor adventures in the area.

  3. Say hello to locals and participate in the community.

Don’t:

  1. Litter or leave your waste while exploring the area.

  2. Forget to tip your servers and people working in the service industry.

  3. Ignore the local customs and traditions of the town.

Safety and Health

Safety in Farmville

Farmville has a welcoming and hospitable community located in a low-crime area ensuring tourist are safe and sound while exploring the area.

Health in Farmville

Farmville is in a rural area of Virginia and has a few small medical clinics; there is no severe health threats making Farmville an ideal destination to enjoy your vacation stress-free.

Getting to Farmville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Farmville

  1. Richmond International Airport - 75 miles away

  2. Charlottesville - Albemarle Airport - 96 miles away

Bus Stations Near Farmville

  1. Farmville Bus Station - Located in the center of town, operated by Bay Transit.

  2. Richmond Greyhound Station - 67 miles away from Farmville.

Other Options

  1. Renting a Car - All major rental companies provide rental cars from nearby airports.

  2. Amtrak - Amtrak has an excellent train service with a station located in downtown Farmville.

How to Get Around in Farmville

Getting around Farmville is efficient using a personal car or renting one. Taxis are a viable option for those who prefer not to drive. The town is a small size and walkable, and biking is a transportation under option.

Where to Go Next from Farmville: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Richmond - Explore Virginia’s capital city and enjoy its impressive art scene, restaurants, and historic landmarks.

  2. Charlottesville - A charming college town that offers incredible wineries, breweries, and dining options, located in the heart of Virginia’s wine country.

  3. Williamsburg - A historical city that offers visitors a unique colonial experience through tours, museums, attractions, and restaurants.

Conclusion

Farmville is a charming and dynamic town that provides visitors with a unique experience. Whether you are interested in exploring the natural beauty of Virginia’s great outdoors or passionate about immersing yourself in local history and culture, Farmville has something to offer every traveler. Start planning your trip to Farmville today and discover all this charming town has to offer.
