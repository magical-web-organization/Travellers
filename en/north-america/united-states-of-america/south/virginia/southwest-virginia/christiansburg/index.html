Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Christiansburg boasts several museums and historical places that are worth visiting.

Christiansburg Institute

The Christiansburg Institute is a historic campus on the outskirts of the town. Founded in 1866, it was a school for African American students until 1966. Today, it stands as a monument to the struggles and successes of African Americans in education.

Montgomery Museum of Art & History

The Montgomery Museum of Art & History is an interesting museum that tells the story of Montgomery County, Virginia. The museum features exhibits on local history, art, and culture and is a great place to learn about the town’s heritage.

Natural Attractions of Christiansburg: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Christiansburg is situated amidst the beautiful Blue Ridge Mountains and has plenty of outdoor activities to offer visitors.

Hahn Horticulture Garden

The Hahn Horticulture Garden is a beautiful botanical garden nestled on Virginia Tech’s campus. The garden has over 8 acres of display gardens, a greenhouse, and a pavilion. It is a popular destination for nature enthusiasts and gardeners.

Pandapas Pond

Pandapas Pond is a stunning nature preserve with over 75 acres of lush forests, creeks, and hiking trails. The preserve is home to various species of plants and animals and is a great place for birdwatching and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Christiansburg

Christiansburg has plenty of opportunities for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the top trails in the area.

Huckleberry Trail

The Huckleberry Trail is a 10.3-mile paved trail that runs from Blacksburg to Christiansburg. It is a popular destination for walkers, joggers, bikers, and rollerbladers and offers scenic views of the surrounding area.

Mountain Lake Conservancy Trail

The Mountain Lake Conservancy Trail is a 5.2-mile hiking trail that runs through the beautiful Appalachian Mountains. The trail is relatively easy and offers breathtaking views of the forest and the lake.

Best Things to Do in Christiansburg: Activities and Tours

Visitors to Christiansburg will find plenty of activities and tours to keep them entertained.

Roanoke Food & Culture Tour

The Roanoke Food & Culture Tour is a popular tour that takes visitors on a culinary journey through the town of Roanoke. Visitors can sample some of the best local cuisine while learning about the town’s history and culture.

Montgomery County Wine Trail

The Montgomery County Wine Trail is a great way to explore the local wineries and vineyards in Christiansburg and the surrounding area. Visitors can sample the finest wines while enjoying the beautiful views of the mountains.
