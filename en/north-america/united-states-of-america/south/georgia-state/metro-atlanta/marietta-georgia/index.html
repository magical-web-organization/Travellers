Introduction to Marietta (Georgia)

Marietta is a charming city located in Metro Atlanta, Georgia. With a rich history and a vibrant culture, it is a popular destination for travelers seeking a variety of experiences.

List of Places to Visit in Marietta (Georgia)

Here are some must-visit places in Marietta:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Marietta Museum of History
  • Gone with the Wind Museum
  • Kennesaw Mountain National Battlefield Park
  • Marietta Confederate Cemetery
  • Root House Museum

Natural Attractions of Marietta (Georgia): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Chattahoochee River National Recreation Area
  • Marietta Square
  • Marietta Garden Center and Gardens

Week-Long Itinerary for Marietta (Georgia)

Here is a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to Marietta:

Day Activities
1 Visit Marietta Square, Marietta Museum of History, and Marietta Garden Center and Gardens
2 Take a hiking or biking tour of Kennesaw Mountain Trail and Sope Creek Trail
3 Visit the Southern Museum of Civil War and Locomotive History, Marietta Confederate Cemetery, and Marietta Fire Museum
4 Take a trolley tour of Marietta and visit the Root House Museum
5 Visit the Gone with the Wind Museum and Marietta National Battlefield Park
6 Take a wine tour of Marietta and enjoy the local food scene
7 Explore the Chattahoochee River National Recreation Area and depart for your next destination

The best time to visit Marietta is in the spring (March to May) or fall (September to November) when the weather is temperate and there are plenty of outdoor activities available. However, Marietta is a year-round destination, and each season has its unique charms.
