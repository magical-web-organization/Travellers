Introduction to Chamblee

Chamblee is a vibrant city located in Metro Atlanta, Georgia, USA. With its rich history and diverse culture, it’s no surprise that Chamblee has become a popular destination for both locals and tourists alike. Whether you are interested in exploring the city’s museums, hiking trails, or savoring the local cuisine, there is something for everyone in Chamblee.

List of Places to Visit in Chamblee

Here are some of the must-visit attractions in Chamblee:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Chamblee Antique Row: This historic district on Chamblee’s Main Street is home to a wide variety of antique shops and boutiques where you can find unique treasures.
  • Chamblee Train Depot: Built in 1916, the Train Depot is an iconic landmark in Chamblee. It currently serves as a museum and community space.
  • Southbound Brewing Company: Beer enthusiasts should definitely visit this brewery, which offers tours and tastings of their local brews.
  • Atlanta Chinatown Mall: Located off Buford Highway, this shopping mall offers visitors a taste of Asian culture, including amazing food and unique shops.

Natural Attractions of Chamblee: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Keswick Park: This park is great for a picnic or a game of frisbee, with plenty of green space and a playground for kids.
  • Murphey Candler Park: Fishing, boating, and jogging trails, all surrounded by stunning lake views, make this park a favorite attraction of Chamblee locals.
  • Blackburn Park: The park offers an outdoor swimming pool, tennis courts, and gardens with beautiful walking paths.
  • Peachtree Creek Greenway: This 12-mile bike trail connects Chamblee to Doraville, offering scenic views of the Chattahoochee River and a great route for nature enthusiasts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Chamblee

For nature enthusiasts, Chamblee offers a variety of hiking and biking trails that offer an escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Some popular hiking and biking routes in Chamblee include Peachtree Creek Greenway and Keswick Park.

Best Things to Do in Chamblee: Activities and Tours

  • Historic Chamblee Walking Tour: A great way to learn about the city’s history is by taking a walking tour through Chamblee’s historic district.
  • Wildlife Wonders: An indoor wildlife sanctuary where visitors can see and learn about exotic animals from around the world.
  • Escape the Mystery Room: A fun activity for groups of friends or families where you are locked in a room and must solve puzzles and clues to escape.
  • Atlanta Food Walks: Discover the city’s diverse and delicious food scene with guided food tours.

Week-Long Itinerary for Chamblee

Here’s a sample itinerary for a week-long stay in Chamblee:

Day Activities
1 Visit the Chamblee Antique Row, explore the Train Depot Museum and get a beer tasting at Southbound Brewing Company.
2 Hike the Peachtree Creek Greenway and have a picnic at Keswick Park.
3 Take the Historic Chamblee Walking Tour and visit Atlanta Chinatown Mall.
4 Visit the Wildlife Wonders sanctuary and experience the Escape the Mystery Room.
5 Explore the hiking opportunities and scenic views of Blackburn Park and Murphey Candler Park.
6 Enjoy guided food tours with Atlanta Food Walks.
7 Check out local farmer’s markets and indulge in local cuisine.

Food and Drink Scene of Chamblee

Chamblee boasts a diverse food and drink scene, offering everything from Southern comfort food to exotic international fare. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fried Chicken: There’s no better place to try this southern classic than at Matthews Cafeteria, which has been serving up delicious fried chicken since 1955.
  • BBQ: Get your fix of delicious barbecue at Pit Boss BBQ or Fox Bros. Bar-B-Q.
  • Vietnamese Food: Buford Highway is known for its Vietnamese cuisine, with popular restaurants like Lee’s Bakery and Pho Dai Loi.
  • Ethiopian Food: Desta Ethiopian Kitchen offers authentic Ethiopian dishes, including injera bread and spicy stews.

Local Beverages

  • Sweet Tea: A southern staple, sweet tea can be found at almost every restaurant in Chamblee.
  • Atlanta Brewing Company: Founded in 1993, this brewery offers a variety of locally made craft beers.
  • Chateau Elan Winery: Just a short drive from Chamblee, this winery and resort offer delicious wine tastings and events.

Where to Stay in Chamblee: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of options for accommodation in Chamblee, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are some top picks:

  • DoubleTree by Hilton Atlanta Perimeter Dunwoody: A popular option for business travelers and families, this hotel offers free shuttle service and a swimming pool.
  • La Quinta Inn & Suites by Wyndham Atlanta Perimeter Medical: A budget-friendly option with comfortable rooms and free breakfast.
  • Georgia Tech Hotel and Conference Center: A modern hotel, perfect for conferences and events with easy access to Chamblee’s attractions.

What to Buy in Chamblee: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking to bring home a unique souvenir, Chamblee has plenty of options. Visit the antique shops and boutiques in the historic district, pick up some local honey from farmers’ markets, or grab a bottle of sweet tea from any local restaurant to take home with you.

Local Culture and Customs of Chamblee

Chamblee is a welcoming and diverse city with a vibrant community. Respectful treatment of individuals from all backgrounds is a valued part of the local culture.

Best Time to Visit Chamblee

The best time to visit Chamblee is in the spring and fall, when the weather is mild and comfortable. Summers can be hot and humid in Chamblee, while winters can be chilly and rainy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Chamblee

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Chamblee:

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine.
  • Be respectful of local customs and culture.
  • Take a walking tour of the historic district.

Don’t:

  • Leave valuables in plain sight in your car.
  • Litter or damage local parks.
  • Disrespect locals or their beliefs.

Safety and Health

While Chamblee is generally a safe and healthy city, it’s always a good idea to take precautions when traveling. Here are some safety tips to keep in mind:

Safety in Chamblee

  • Park your car in well-lit areas.
  • Keep valuables hidden.
  • Stay aware of your surroundings, especially when hiking.

Health in Chamblee

  • Stay hydrated, especially during hot and humid weather.
  • Wear sunscreen when spending time outdoors.
  • Seek medical attention immediately if you experience any serious health issues.

Getting to Chamblee: Transportation Options and Logistics

Chamblee is easily accessible via car and public transportation. Here are some options for getting to Chamblee:

Airports Near Chamblee

  • Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport: Located just 30 minutes away from Chamblee, this airport is the most convenient option for air travelers.

Bus Stations Near Chamblee

  • Doraville Transit Station: Located just outside Chamblee, this station offers local bus services to the city.

Other Options

  • Car rental: Many major car rental companies have branches in Chamblee.
  • Ride-hailing services: Uber and Lyft are both available in Chamblee.

How to Get Around in Chamblee

Chamblee is a small city, making it easy to get around on foot or by bike. You can also drive or take public transportation.

Where to Go Next from Chamblee: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are plenty of nearby destinations and day trips that you can take from Chamblee, including:

  • Atlanta: Just a short drive or train ride away, Atlanta offers world-class museums, delicious food, and a bustling nightlife.
  • Stone Mountain: The largest exposed granite dome in North America is only a 30-minute drive from Chamblee.
  • Savannah: A bit further away, but worth the trip for its beautiful coastal scenery, historic landmarks, and amazing food.

Conclusion

Chamblee is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in exploring the rich culture and history of the American South. With its diverse food scene, stunning natural beauty, and vibrant community, there is something for everyone in Chamblee. Plan your trip today and experience the best of southern hospitality and charm in this charming city.
