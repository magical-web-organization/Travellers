Introduction to Conyers

Welcome to Conyers, Georgia! Known as the “Crepe Myrtle City,” Conyers is a charming Southern city located in the heartland of Georgia. It is a city that has something for everyone, from historical sites and museums to outdoor adventures and scenic views.

Whether you’re visiting as a family, couple, or solo traveler, Conyers promises to be a delightful destination. In this complete travel guide, we will present you with a plethora of information about Conyers that will help you plan your trip with ease.

List of Places to Visit in Conyers

Listed below are the top places to visit in Conyers:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The Olde Town Conyers Historic District

Take a stroll through the Olde Town Conyers Historic District and step back in time. Cited on the National Register of Historic Places, this 19th-century district features several unique and well-preserved buildings.

The Georgia International Horse Park

Visitors can enjoy various events at the Georgia International Horse Park, which hosted equestrian and mountain bike competitions during the 1996 Olympics.

The Monastery of the Holy Spirit

The Monastery of the Holy Spirit offers a peaceful retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life. It is home to Trappist monks who have been serving the community for over 70 years.

Costley Mill Park

The historic gristmill along the Yellow River offers beautiful scenery and outdoor activities to visitors.

Natural Attractions of Conyers: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Black Shoals Park

Black Shoals Park on the shores of Lake Jackson is perfect for swimming, fishing, and picnicking. It also features a boat ramp and a fishing pier.

Arabia Mountain National Heritage Area

Arabia Mountain is an awe-inspiring natural wonder and part of the Arabia Mountain National Heritage Area. It is a unique granite outcropping with lovely views and hiking trails.

Panola Mountain State Park

Panola Mountain State Park is the perfect destination for hiking, mountain climbing and enjoying beautiful views of Atlanta skyline from the mountaintop.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Conyers

One of the most exceptional outdoor activities in Conyers, Georgia, is hiking. Following are the top hiking/biking routes in Conyers:

Rockdale River Trail

The Rockdale River Trail is 12 miles long and runs along the South River, a tributary of the Oconee River. It is just perfect for hiking and cycling.

Arabia Mountain Trail

The Arabia Mountain Trail is known for its scenic beauty, high elevation, and extensive network of trails.

Black Shoals Biking Trail

Black Shoals Biking Trail in Black Shoals Park is perfect for cycling enthusiasts. The trail is 2.3 miles long and offers panoramic views of Lake Jackson.

Best Things to Do in Conyers: Activities and Tours

Conyers, Georgia, offers a lot of fun things to do for both adults and children. It is a city teeming with natural wonders, excellent restaurants, indoor and outdoor activities, and exciting entertainment options. Below we have outlined some of the best things to do in Conyers:

Go kart racing at Andretti Indoor Karting and Games

Andretti Indoor Karting and Games in Marietta are less than 40 miles away from Conyers, but it’s well worth the drive. The facility has an indoor track, and you can rent go-karts for racing, play arcade games, and enjoy an array of other games.

Visit the Georgia Aquarium

The Georgia Aquarium in Atlanta is less than 30 miles away from Conyers and is one of the biggest and most visited aquariums in the United States.

Attend the Black Arts and Music Festival

July is the perfect time to visit Conyers and participate in the Black Arts and Music Festival. This festival showcases African American culture with music, food, games, and more.

Week-Long Itinerary for Conyers

Below is an itinerary for a week-long trip to Conyers:

Monday: Visit the Olde Town Conyers Historic District

Tuesday: Explore the Monastery of the Holy Spirit

Wednesday: Visit Black Shoals Park

Thursday: Hike into the Panola Mountain State Park

Friday: Visit Georgia International Horse Park

Saturday: Join a guided tour of the Arabia Mountain Trail

Sunday: Relax on the banks of Yellow River at Costley Mill Park

Food and Drink Scene of Conyers

Conyers food scene is a melting pot of several flavors, and you’ll have plenty of options to pick from. Here the list of must-try dishes and local beverages in Conyers:

Must-Try Dishes

Fried Chicken at The House

The House is known for its delicious fried chicken, which is crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside.

Georgia Peach Cobbler at Sweet Charlie’s Baked Goods

Sweet Charlie’s Baked Goods offers a wide range of baked goods, including Georgia Peach Cobbler, which is tangy, sweet, and perfectly baked.

Grilled salmon at Stone Mountain Grill

Stone Mountain Grill meticulously prepares its grilled salmon, which is seasoned, grilled to perfection and served with yellow rice

Local Beverages

Sweet Tea

Sweet tea is a staple in Georgia and is one of their most popular local beverages. It is the perfect thirst quencher for hot and sultry summer days.

Southern Style Cocktails

Southern-style cocktails are made from locally grown ingredients and offer a unique taste experience that you’ll never forget. Try the Georgia Peach Margarita if you want to taste something exceptional.

Where to Stay in Conyers: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Conyers offers a wide range of options when it comes to accommodation. You can choose from luxury hotels, budget-friendly hostels, or charming guesthouses. Some of the top accommodation options in Conyers are:

Hyatt Place Atlanta / East - Lithonia

Hyatt Place Atlanta / East – Lithonia is perfect for those looking for a luxurious and comfortable stay. It’s less than ten miles from Conyers and offers modern amenities and comfortable rooms.

Holiday Inn Express & Suites Atlanta - Conyers

Holiday Inn Express & Suites Atlanta - Conyers is perfect for budget travelers who want to stay in a comfortable, clean, and centrally located hotel. The hotel offers complimentary breakfast and is just minutes away from the major attractions

Hometowne Studios by Lit

Hometowne Studios by Lit is an excellent option for budget-conscious travelers. You can save money by cooking your meals as all rooms have a kitchenette.

What to Buy in Conyers: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Apart from the memories, you can bring a piece of Conyers back home through some local products and souvenirs:

Sweet Treats from Sweet Charlie’s Baked Goods

Sweet Charlie’s Baked Goods offers many delicious souvenirs, including a wide range of cupcakes and baked goods, not to mention their signature Georgia Peach Cobbler.

Local Art

Many local artists exhibit and sell their work in galleries and stores across the city. Stop by the Conyers Rockdale Council for the Arts gallery to find unique pieces of local art to take home with you.

Local Culture and Customs of Conyers

Southern hospitality is one of the most significant cultural aspects of Conyers. It is customary to greet everyone with a smile and be polite when asking for help. Conyers has a rich history, and residents often take pride in their traditions, which visitors should respect and honor.

Best Time to Visit Conyers

The best time to visit Conyers is from March to May and September to November. These months bring comfortable weather, fewer crowds, and an array of outdoor activities. If you want to enjoy bustling festivals and activities, visit Conyers in July.

Do’s and Don’ts of Conyers

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Treat everyone with respect and kindness.
  • Try the local foods and drinks.

Don’t:

  • Litter on the roads or trails.
  • Be loud and disruptive to wildlife.
  • Ignore safety procedures when enjoying outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Conyers

Conyers is a safe and secure destination with minimal cases of crime against tourists. However, it is always wise to stay vigilant and take necessary precautions like keeping your belongings safe while traveling.

Health in Conyers

Visitors have access to excellent medical facilities in Conyers, including high-quality medical professionals, hospitals, clinics, and medical centers. However, travelers must maintain a healthy lifestyle and regularly follow WHO guidelines to stay healthy during their trip.

Getting to Conyers: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Conyers

Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport (ATL) is the closest major airport to Conyers, which is about 32 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Conyers

Greyhound Station in Atlanta downtown is the closest bus station to Conyers.

Other Options

The Martin Luther King Jr. Driveway and the Interstate 20 Eastbound both pass through Conyers, making it accessible by car from all significant nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Conyers

Public transportation is not very reliable in Conyers, making it necessary to rent a car to get around. Several car rental companies are present in Conyers or nearby, making it quite convenient to rent a car. Bicycles are another option for getting around in Conyers, given its bike-friendly roads and scenic trails.

Where to Go Next from Conyers: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have more time at hand, visit the following attractions that are close to Conyers:

Atlanta

Atlanta is located approximately 35 miles west of Conyers. It is Georgia’s most prominent city and home to many historical and cultural sites, including CNN World Headquarters, the Martin Luther King Jr. National Historic Site, and the Georgia Aquarium.

Athens

Athens is located about 50 miles east of Conyers and is known for its beautiful gardens, historic architecture, and vibrant nightlife. Visit the beautiful State Botanical Garden of Georgia or take a stroll through the Georgia Museum of Art while in Athens.

Conclusion

Conyers is a fantastic travel destination that caters to a broad range of interests. It has unique natural wonders such as the Arabia mountain, historical sites and museums, and delicious local foods and drinks. So, what are you waiting for? Visit Conyers to have a memorable vacation that you’ll never forget!
