Introduction to DeRidder

Located in the central part of Louisiana, DeRidder is a small city known for its rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and charming small-town feel. Founded in 1893, the city has a long history and a vibrant community that welcomes visitors with open arms. If you’re looking for an off-the-beaten-path travel destination that offers plenty of fun activities and adventures, DeRidder is definitely worth a visit.

List of Places to Visit in DeRidder

DeRidder offers something for everyone, whether you’re interested in history, culture, outdoor activities, or just relaxing in a scenic location. Here’s a list of must-visit attractions that you should add to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Beauregard Parish Historical Society Museum - a local museum that showcases the rich history and culture of Beauregard Parish.
  • DeRidder Army Air Base Museum - a military museum that honors the contribution of DeRidder to the war effort.
  • Gothic Jail - a historic jail that was built in the early 20th century and features unique architectural elements.

Natural Attractions of DeRidder: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Bundick Lake - a popular spot for fishing, boating, and camping.
  • Kisatchie National Forest - a vast wilderness area that offers hiking, camping, and wildlife-watching opportunities.
  • Sabine National Wildlife Refuge - a wetlands habitat that is home to a variety of bird species and other wildlife.

Hiking/Biking Routes of DeRidder

DeRidder is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts who enjoy hiking, biking, and other outdoor activities. Here are some of the best routes you can explore:

  • The Backbone Trail - a 5-mile hiking trail that offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
  • The Outback Trail - a 6-mile hiking trail that takes you through the heart of the Kisatchie National Forest.
  • The Whiskey Chitto Trail - a 15-mile hiking trail that follows the Whiskey Chitto Creek and features several swimming holes.

Best Things to Do in DeRidder: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re traveling solo, with friends, or with your family, DeRidder has plenty of fun things to do and see. Check out these popular activities and tours that you can enjoy:

  • Beauregard Parish Fair - an annual event that features live music, carnival rides, and agricultural shows.
  • Lake Vernon Boat Tour - a scenic boat trip that takes you around the beautiful Lake Vernon.
  • Beauregard Parish Watermelon Festival - a fun-filled event that celebrates the summer harvest with live music, arts and crafts, and plenty of juicy watermelon to sample.

Food and Drink Scene of DeRidder

Must-Try Dishes

DeRidder may be a small city, but it offers some unique food and drink options that you won’t find anywhere else:

  • Cajun-style crawfish boil - a spicy seafood dish that is popular in Louisiana.
  • Boudin - a sausage made from pork, rice, and spices, served as a snack or as a main dish.
  • Southern-style BBQ - a slow-cooked meat dish that is flavored with a tangy BBQ sauce.

Local Beverages

If you’re looking for a refreshing drink to beat the Louisiana heat, make sure to try these local beverages:

  • Sweet tea - a sweetened iced tea that is a staple of Southern cuisine.
  • Abita Beer - a brand of beer that is brewed in Louisiana and comes in a variety of flavors.
  • The Hurricane - a fruity cocktail that originated in New Orleans but is now popular throughout Louisiana.

Where to Stay in DeRidder: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

DeRidder offers a range of accommodation options that cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the top places to stay:

  • Best Western Plus DeRidder Inn & Suites - a modern hotel that offers comfortable rooms and amenities like a pool and fitness center.
  • Relax Inn - a budget-friendly motel that is conveniently located near downtown DeRidder.
  • Southern Serenity B&B - a charming guesthouse that features a lovely garden and home-cooked breakfasts.

What to Buy in DeRidder: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you want to take a piece of DeRidder home with you, here are some of the best souvenirs and local products to look for:

  • Cajun spices - a blend of seasonings that is used in many Louisiana dishes.
  • Homemade jams and jellies - made from local fruits like blueberries and blackberries.
  • Handmade crafts - like pottery, quilts, and woodcarvings, that are sold at local artisan markets.

Local Culture and Customs of DeRidder

DeRidder is steeped in traditional Southern culture and hospitality. Here are some customs and practices to keep in mind:

  • Always greet people with a smile and a friendly “hello”.
  • Take time to appreciate the local music and art scenes.
  • Observe the traditional manners of the South, including saying “please” and “thank you” and holding doors open for others.

Best Time to Visit DeRidder

The best time to visit DeRidder is during the spring or fall, when the weather is mild and pleasant. Summertime can be hot and humid, while winter can be chilly and wet.

Do’s and Don’ts of DeRidder

Do:

  • Take time to mingle with the locals and learn about their culture.
  • Try the local delicacies, like crawfish and boudin.
  • Explore the surrounding natural areas, like Kisatchie National Forest and Sabine National Wildlife Refuge.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or leave trash in public places.
  • Don’t ignore the local customs and traditions.
  • Don’t underestimate the power of the Louisiana sun - make sure to wear sunscreen and stay hydrated.

Safety and Health

Safety in DeRidder

DeRidder is generally a safe destination for travelers, but it’s always a good idea to take basic safety precautions like locking your car and not leaving valuables exposed. Also, be mindful of wildlife when hiking and camping in the local natural areas.

Health in DeRidder

The local healthcare facilities in DeRidder are generally well-equipped and staffed with experienced medical professionals. However, it’s always advisable to check your health insurance coverage and get any necessary vaccinations before your trip.

Getting to DeRidder: Transportation Options and Logistics

DeRidder is easily accessible by car or bus, and there are several transportation options available for travelers:

Airports Near DeRidder

The nearest airports to DeRidder are Lake Charles Regional Airport, which is about 65 miles away, and Alexandria International Airport, which is about 70 miles away.

Bus Stations Near DeRidder

Greyhound operates a bus service that stops in DeRidder.

Other Options

Renting a car is a popular option for travelers who want to explore the surrounding areas and natural attractions at their own pace.

How to Get Around in DeRidder

DeRidder is a small city that is easy to get around on foot or by bike. However, if you need to travel further, there are several taxi and ride-sharing services available, as well as car rental options.

Where to Go Next from DeRidder: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have more time to explore the region, here are some nearby destinations and excursions that you can visit:

  • Lake Charles - a larger city with a thriving arts and culinary scene.
  • Alexandria - a historic city that is home to several museums and cultural attractions.
  • Natchitoches - a charming town that is known for its Christmas lights festival and historic district.

Conclusion

DeRidder may be a small city, but it offers a wealth of attractions, activities, and experiences for travelers of all interests. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or just a relaxing vacation, DeRidder has something to offer. So pack your bags, plan your itinerary, and get ready to explore this hidden gem of Louisiana.
