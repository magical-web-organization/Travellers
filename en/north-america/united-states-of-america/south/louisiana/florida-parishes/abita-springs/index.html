Introduction to Abita Springs

Nestled in the heart of Florida Parishes, in Louisiana, Abita Springs is a small town that boasts abundant natural beauty and a rich culture dating back to the 1800s. The town is known for its historic sites, including the Abita Springs Hotel, which was once a popular destination for wealthy tourists. There are also several outdoor activities to enjoy, like hiking and biking on the Tammany Trace Trail or kayaking on the Abita River.

List of Places to Visit in Abita Springs

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Abita Springs Museum: a local history museum showcasing artifacts and memorabilia documenting the town’s past.
  • UCM Museum: a quirky roadside attraction featuring a collection of folk art made from found objects.

Natural Attractions of Abita Springs: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Tammany Trace Trail: a scenic 31-mile bike trail running through Abita Springs and the surrounding areas.
  • Abita River: a picturesque river popular for kayaking and fishing.
  • Fontainebleau State Park: a beautiful park with campsites, trails and a lake.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Abita Springs

The Tammany Trace Trail is a popular biking and hiking route through Abita Springs. It is a flat and easy route that also provides scenic views of the town and its surrounding areas.

Best Things to Do in Abita Springs: Activities and Tours

  • Take a tour of the Abita Springs Brewery, known for its award-winning beers.
  • Experience the Abita Springs Opry, a live music event featuring local talent.
  • Visit the Paddle Bayou Lafitte, a guided kayaking tour through the scenic Abita River.

Week-Long Itinerary for Abita Springs

Day Activities
1 Explore the Abita Springs Museum and attend a live music show at the Abita Springs Opry.
2 Take a scenic drive to the nearby Honey Island Swamp and experience a swamp tour.
3 Visit the Fontainebleau State Park and spend the day hiking and exploring the park’s trails.
4 Experience the Abita Springs Brewery tour and tasting.
5 Go kayaking on the Abita River and enjoy the picturesque scenery.
6 Take a bike ride on Tammany Trace Trail and enjoy the sunset views.
7 Spend a relaxing day at the Pelican Park and enjoy the nature and picnic.

Food and Drink Scene of Abita Springs

Must-Try Dishes

  • Abita Beer-Battered Fish: fresh-caught fish battered with Abita beer and served with fries and hushpuppies.
  • Crawfish Étouffée: a classic Louisiana dish made with crawfish, onions, peppers, and roux served over rice.

Local Beverages

  • Abita Beer: a local brewery known for their award-winning beers, including their signature Abita Amber.

Where to Stay in Abita Springs: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Abita Springs Hotel: a historic hotel turned bed and breakfast with a peaceful, serene ambiance.
  • Maison Du Chene: a beautiful guesthouse with large porches, surrounded by gardens.

What to Buy in Abita Springs: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Abita Beer merchandise: bring home a piece of Abita Springs with merchandise from the local brewery.
  • Local art: there are several galleries and artists in town offering unique pieces inspired by the local culture.

Local Culture and Customs of Abita Springs

Abita Springs has a rich cultural heritage, steeped in traditions dating back to the 19th century. The town is known for its vibrant arts scene and annual events, including the Abita Springs Busker Festival and the Abita Springs Art and Farmers Market.

Best Time to Visit Abita Springs

The best time to visit Abita Springs is in the spring and fall months when the weather is mild, and there are several outdoor activities to enjoy. The winter months can be chilly, and the summers can be hot and humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Abita Springs

Do:

  • Sample the local cuisine and drinks.
  • Explore the town’s rich cultural heritage and history.
  • Take advantage of the outdoor activities and scenic views.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural surroundings.
  • Disrespect the local culture and traditions.
  • Take unauthorized tours of private properties.

Safety and Health

Safety in Abita Springs

Abita Springs is a relatively safe town, but visitors should take standard precautions to ensure their safety, such as locking doors and windows and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Abita Springs

Visitors to Abita Springs should drink bottled water or boiled tap water to avoid any health issues related to the local water supply. Visitors should also be aware that the town is in an area prone to mosquitoes and may want to take necessary precautions to avoid mosquito-borne illnesses.

Getting to Abita Springs: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Abita Springs

  • Louis Armstrong New Orleans International Airport: 1-hour drive from Abita Springs

Bus Stations Near Abita Springs

Other Options

How to Get Around in Abita Springs

Most attractions and points of interest in Abita Springs are within walking or biking distance. Visitors can also rent cars or take taxis to get around the town and its surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Abita Springs: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • New Orleans: a vibrant city known for its rich culture and delicious cuisine, about an hour’s drive from Abita Springs.
  • Honey Island Swamp: a beautiful and mysterious swamp known for its unique flora and fauna, only a short drive from Abita Springs.

Conclusion

Abita Springs is a charming town with a rich history and culture and abundant outdoor activities. Visitors to the town can enjoy outdoor adventures, explore local history and culture, and sample delicious food and drinks. With this comprehensive guide, visitors can make the most out of their trip to Abita Springs.
