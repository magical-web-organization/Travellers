Introduction to Faubourg Marigny

Nestled just east of the infamous French Quarter, Faubourg Marigny is one of the oldest neighborhoods in New Orleans, Louisiana, exuding charm and character that can rival that of its more popular neighbor. Founded in 1805, Marigny was named after its founder, Bernard Marigny. The neighborhood boasts a rich history, colorful architecture, and numerous cultural attractions that make for an unmissable experience in New Orleans.
