Introduction to Shreveport

Shreveport is a beautiful city located in northern Louisiana, United States of America. It is known for its rich culture, history, natural beauty, and friendly people. Shreveport is a perfect destination for those looking for some outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, and great food. With plenty of things to do and see, here is your ultimate travel guide to Shreveport.

List of Places to Visit in Shreveport

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Louisiana State Exhibit Museum

The Louisiana State Exhibit Museum is a beautiful museum that showcases the history and culture of Louisiana. It features many exhibits and artifacts that represent the state’s evolution in a unique way.

Shreveport Municipal Memorial Auditorium

The Shreveport Municipal Memorial Auditorium is an excellent example of Art Deco architecture and has hosted many famous musicians and artists. Be sure to attend a concert if you have the chance.

Shreveport Water Works Museum

Take a step back in time and visit the Shreveport Water Works Museum. This museum displays the antiquated methods and systems that were used for providing clean water in the city.

Natural Attractions of Shreveport: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Red River National Wildlife Refuge

The Red River National Wildlife Refuge is a nature reserve that offers stunning views and a serene atmosphere. The area is inhabited by many endangered species and is perfect for bird watching and hiking.

Betty Virginia Park

Betty Virginia Park is a beautiful park that features numerous walking trails, stunning gardens, and a lake. The park is perfect for picnics, walking, and relaxing in the outdoors.

Walter B. Jacobs Memorial Nature Park

The Walter B. Jacobs Memorial Nature Park is a massive nature reserve that includes many trails, fishing spots, and recreational areas. The nature reserve is home to a wide range of wildlife, including birds and deer.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Shreveport

Red River Bicycle Trail

The Red River Bicycle Trail is a beautiful trail that stretches alongside the Red River. It is perfect for bikers, walkers, and hikers and offers stunning views of the city and the river.

Cross Lake Trail

The Cross Lake Trail is a beautiful trail that circles the magnificent Cross Lake. The trail offers beautiful views of the lake and is perfect for jogging and cycling.

Best Things to Do in Shreveport: Activities and Tours

Gators and Friends Alligator Park and Exotic Zoo

Visit the Gators and Friends Alligator Park and Exotic Zoo to see a wide assortment of exotic animals, including over 150 alligators. While there, visitors can also enjoy zip-lining and animal shows.

Louisiana Boardwalk

The Louisiana Boardwalk is a beautiful shopping center located along the Red River. The area comes alive at night with bright neon lights and offers a wide range of shopping and dining options.

Shreveport Aquarium

The Shreveport Aquarium is a beautiful aquarium that features an array of sea creatures from all around the world. The aquarium also offers many interactive exhibits, shows, and educational programs.

Food and Drink Scene of Shreveport

Must-Try Dishes

Crawfish Etoufee

Crawfish Etoufee is a southern classic that is made with crawfish, a spicy Roux, and served over rice. The dish is rich, flavorful, and one of the most popular dishes in Shreveport.

Po’ Boy Sandwich

Po’ Boy Sandwich is a Louisiana classic that is made with crispy fried seafood, lettuce, tomatoes, and served on a baguette. The sandwich is so filling that it is named after a “poor boy” who cannot afford to eat a lot.

Beignets

Beignets are a must-have dessert in Shreveport. These small, square doughnuts are fried until fluffy and served with powdered sugar on top.

Local Beverages

Sazerac

Sazerac is a New Orleans classic cocktail that is made from whiskey, sugar, bitters, and absinthe. It has a bitter-spicy flavor and is perfect for sipping during happy hour.

Abita Beer

Abita Beer is a Louisiana-based brewery that creates delicious and refreshing beers that embody the Southern spirit. Their products include a variety of pale ales, lagers, and stouts.

Where to Stay in Shreveport: Hotels, Hostels, and Guest Houses

Sam’s Town Hotel & Casino

Sam’s Town Hotel & Casino is a luxurious hotel that offers stunning city views, spacious rooms, and excellent amenities. It also features a casino, multiple restaurants, and a swimming pool.

The Remington Suite Hotel and Spa

The Remington Suite Hotel and Spa is a boutique hotel that offers luxury and comfort in a serene and tranquil environment. The hotel features 23 spacious suites, a spa, a restaurant, and a fabulous rooftop bar.

Hostel Shreveport

Hostel Shreveport is a budget-friendly accommodation option that offers dormitory-style rooms and private rooms. The hostel also features a shared kitchen, lounge, and communal areas for guests to socialize.

What to Buy in Shreveport: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Louisiana Hot Sauce

Louisiana Hot Sauce is a staple in Shreveport and is used in many of the local dishes. A bottle of hot sauce makes for an excellent souvenir and is perfect for anyone who loves spicy food.

Handmade Crafts

Shreveport is home to many talented crafters and artisans who sell handmade crafts like pottery, jewelry, and home décor at local markets.

Local Beers

Local breweries like Great Raft Brewing offer a wide variety of beers that reflect the local culture and flavors of Shreveport. A six-pack of these beers is an excellent souvenir to take back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Shreveport

Shreveport is known for its hospitality and friendliness. The locals take pride in their city and are always happy to share their traditions and customs with visitors. The city has a unique blend of cultures, including African American, native American, and French Creole.

Best Time to Visit Shreveport

The best time to visit Shreveport is during the spring and fall seasons. These seasons offer mild temperatures, clear skies, and fewer crowds. Summer months can be very hot, while winters can get quite cold.

Do’s and Don’ts of Shreveport

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine and regional specialties.
  • Do be respectful of the local customs and traditions.
  • Do visit the local markets and festivals.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or leave trash behind.
  • Don’t be rude or disrespectful to the locals.
  • Don’t miss out on the chance to explore the outdoors.

Safety and Health

Safety in Shreveport

Shreveport is a safe city to visit, but it is always wise to take necessary precautions for safety. Be sure to lock your car doors, avoid walking around alone at night, and keep your valuables safe.

Health in Shreveport

Shreveport is a healthy city with numerous healthcare facilities and hospitals. However, visitors should carry their necessary medication and have insurance coverage for emergencies.

Getting to Shreveport: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Shreveport

The Shreveport Regional airport serves the city and has connections to many major cities in the United States.

Bus Stations Near Shreveport

Greyhound Bus Line serves Shreveport and has connections to many other major cities in Louisiana and Texas.

Other Options

Shreveport is also accessible by car, train, and taxi services.

How to Get Around in Shreveport

Shreveport is a walkable city, and most of the attractions are located in the downtown area. Visitors can also use taxis, buses, and bike rentals to travel around the city.

Where to Go Next from Shreveport: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Natchitoches

Natchitoches is a charming and historic town located about two hours from Shreveport. It is famous for its beautiful architecture, unique shops, and traditional Creole cuisine.

Dallas

Dallas is a vibrant city located only a few hours from Shreveport. It boasts a thriving arts scene, great restaurants, and plenty of outdoor attractions.

Lake Charles

Lake Charles is a lovely town located on the southern coast of Louisiana, known for its beautiful beaches, excellent seafood, and historic landmarks.

Conclusion

Shreveport is an excellent destination for travelers looking for history, culture, nature, and fantastic cuisine. With plenty of things to do and see, visitors to Shreveport can have a memorable experience and create unforgettable memories. Plan your next trip to Shreveport today!
