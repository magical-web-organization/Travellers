Introduction to North Carolina

North Carolina is situated in the southeastern region of the United States of America. The state’s terrain ranges from stunning coastlines to majestic mountains, offering various outdoor adventures for tourists. Known for its rich history and culture, North Carolina is a must-visit destination for those seeking to immerse themselves in the southern lifestyle.

List of Places to Visit in North Carolina

North Carolina is home to a plethora of attractions, ranging from natural wonders to historical sites. Here are some of the best places to visit in North Carolina.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Biltmore Estate: One of the most prominent landmarks in North Carolina, the Biltmore Estate is a 19th century chateau-like mansion that stands on an immense 8,000-acre estate.

  • Wright Brothers National Memorial: A tribute to the Wright brothers, who conducted the first successful powered flight in Kitty Hawk, North Carolina.

  • Asheville Art Museum: A museum dedicated to regional art and contemporary craft in downtown Asheville.

Natural Attractions of North Carolina: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Great Smoky Mountains National Park: One of the most visited national parks in the United States, offering brilliant hiking trails and stunning landscapes.

  • Blue Ridge Parkway: A 469-mile drive through the mountains, the Blue Ridge Parkway is a scenic drive that offers breathtaking views.

  • Outer Banks: A 200-mile-long stretch of barrier islands, that boasts sandy beaches, lighthouses, and historical sites.

Hiking/Biking Routes of North Carolina

North Carolina is filled with natural beauty, and the state is ideal for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the best trails and routes in North Carolina.

  • Mount Mitchell Trail: A challenging hike that leads to the highest peak east of the Mississippi River.

  • Art Loeb Trail: A 30-mile trail that winds through the stunning Pisgah National Forest.

  • Virginia Creeper Trail: A 34-mile trail that allows bikes, horses, and hikers to explore the wilderness of the Appalachian Mountains.

Best Things to Do in North Carolina: Activities and Tours

North Carolina has something for everyone, no matter what your interests are. Here are some of the best things to do in North Carolina.

  • Visit the Beach: With over 300 miles of coastline, North Carolina is home to some of the best beaches in the country.

  • Explore the Downtowns: Downtown Asheville, Charlotte, and Raleigh offer plenty of shopping, dining, and cultural activities.

  • Tour the Breweries: With over 300 breweries in North Carolina, take a tour and learn about the local craft beer scene.

Week-Long Itinerary for North Carolina

To make the most out of your trip to North Carolina, follow this seven-day itinerary that covers some of the best attractions and activities in the state.

Day Itinerary
1 Start your journey in Asheville, exploring the Biltmore Estate and downtown area
2 Spend the day hiking in the Great Smoky Mountains National Park
3 Drive down the Blue Ridge Parkway, stopping at scenic viewpoints
4 Visit the Outer Banks, touring historical sites and lighthouses
5 Explore the downtown area of Raleigh
6 Tour the breweries in Charlotte
7 End your trip with a relaxing day at the beach

Food and Drink Scene of North Carolina

North Carolina is renowned for its unique cuisine, including slow-cooked barbecue, fresh seafood, and craft beer. Here’s what you can’t miss when you visit.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Barbecue: Slow-cooked meat served with a tangy vinegar-based sauce- a North Carolina staple.

  • Cherokee Fry Bread: A fried bread typically served with savory toppings.

  • Cheerwine: A cherry-flavored soft drink that originated in North Carolina.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beer: North Carolina is home to over 300 breweries, so make sure you try some of the local craft beers during your visit.

  • Sweet Tea: A favorite southern beverage, sweet tea is a refreshing drink served in most restaurants throughout the state.

Where to Stay in North Carolina: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

From luxury hotels to cozy bed and breakfasts, North Carolina offers accommodation for every budget and preference. Here are some of the best options for lodging.

  • The Umstead Hotel and Spa: A luxurious spa and resort located in Cary, North Carolina.

  • Asheville Cabins of Willow Winds: A peaceful, family-owned retreat near Asheville, nestled in a forest of mountain laurels.

  • Hostelling International Asheville: A budget-friendly hostel located in the heart of downtown Asheville.

What to Buy in North Carolina: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

While in North Carolina, make sure you take some local goodies home with you. Here are some of the best souvenirs and local products to buy.

  • Cherokee Baskets: Handcrafted baskets made by a Cherokee tribe member.

  • North Carolina BBQ Sauce: Bring a taste of North Carolina home with you with some local barbecue sauce.

  • Pottery: North Carolina has a rich history of pottery-making, and you’ll find plenty of beautiful pieces to take home as a souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of North Carolina

North Carolina has a rich history and culture, with deep roots in southern traditions. Here are some customs to keep in mind when visiting the state.

  • Southern Hospitality: North Carolina residents are known for their hospitality and friendliness.

  • Religion: Christianity is the most prevalent religion in North Carolina.

Best Time to Visit North Carolina

The best time to visit North Carolina is in the spring and fall (April-June and September-November), when the weather is mild, and the foliage displays stunning colors.

Do’s and Don’ts of North Carolina

Here are some additional travel tips to keep in mind when visiting North Carolina.

Do:

  • Do try North Carolina barbecue: Whether you prefer pork or beef, make sure you try some local barbecue during your visit.

  • Do visit the state parks: North Carolina is home to some of the most stunning state parks in the country.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter: Leave no trace when visiting natural attractions and parks.

  • Don’t be rude: Remember to practice good manners and respect the locals during your visit.

Safety and Health

North Carolina is generally a safe destination for travelers. However, it’s good to keep these safety and health tips in mind.

Safety in North Carolina

  • Be aware of wildlife: North Carolina is home to a diverse array of animals, including black bears and venomous snakes.

  • Use caution when driving: Rural roads in North Carolina can be windy and narrow.

Health in North Carolina

  • Stay hydrated: During the summer months, temperatures in North Carolina can reach high levels, stay hydrated to avoid heat stroke.

  • Beware of ticks: North Carolina is a hotspot for ticks that carry Lyme disease, make sure to use insect repellent to avoid getting bitten.

Airports Near North Carolina

  • Raleigh-Durham International Airport
  • Piedmont Triad International Airport

Bus Stations Near North Carolina

  • Megabus

Other Options

  • Car Rental Services: Renting a car is the most convenient way to explore North Carolina.

How to Get Around in North Carolina

North Carolina is vast, so having transportation is essential to explore all the stunning scenery and attractions. Here are some of the best ways to get around North Carolina.

  • Car Rental: Renting a car is the most convenient way to get around and explore North Carolina.

  • Public Transportation: North Carolina offers bus, trolley, and subway services in most major cities.

Where to Go Next from North Carolina: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to continue your adventure, here are some nearby destinations and excursions that are worth a visit.

  • Charleston, South Carolina: A southern city known for its charm and history.

  • Chattanooga, Tennessee: A scenic city known for its outdoor activities and natural beauty.

  • Savannah, Georgia: A charming southern coastal city, famous for its historic sites and architecture.

Conclusion

North Carolina is a diverse and charming destination, packed with natural beauty, history, and southern tradition. Plan your trip using this comprehensive guide, and discover all the exciting things North Carolina has to offer.
