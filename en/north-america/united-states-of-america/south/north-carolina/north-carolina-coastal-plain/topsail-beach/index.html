Introduction to Topsail Beach

Topsail Beach is a beautiful town located on the southern coast of North Carolina. It is a hidden gem of the state, famous for its scenic beauty, sandy beaches, and year-round pleasant weather. The town stretches for 26 miles and is composed of three separate communities, each with its distinct charm and attractions.

Topsail Beach a perfect vacation spot for those who want to escape crowded tourist destinations and enjoy a more serene and peaceful experience. Whether you are looking to enjoy the water or indulge in outdoor adventures, Topsail Beach has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Topsail Beach

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Topsail Beach is home to a variety of cultural attractions that offer a glimpse into the town’s history and heritage. Some of the must-visit museums and historical sites include:

  • Missiles and More Museum: An informative and interactive museum that showcases the town’s rich naval and military history.
  • Assembly Building: A historic landmark that features art exhibits, concerts, and other cultural events.
  • Historical Society of Topsail Island: An organization dedicated to preserving the town’s history and culture, offering walking tours and lectures.

Natural Attractions of Topsail Beach: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Topsail Beach boasts numerous natural attractions, ranging from secluded beaches to serene marshlands. Some of the best outdoor adventures include:

  • Topsail Island Beaches: A collection of pristine beaches known for their crystal-clear waters and white sand.
  • Topsail Island State Preserve: A protected area with hiking trails, birdwatching opportunities, and beautiful views of the coastline.
  • Serenity Point Trail: A beautiful trail that cuts through a nature preserve, offering beautiful views of the Intracoastal Waterway and the ocean.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Topsail Beach

Topsail Beach is also a premier hiking & biking destination, thanks to the numerous trails and routes available for outdoor enthusiasts. Some of the top trails include:

  • Topsail Hill Preserve State Park Trail: A 5.2-mile trail that offers stunning views of the coastline and the surrounding area.
  • Karen Beasley Sea Turtle Rescue and Rehabilitation Center Trail: A short and easy trail that takes hikers through a renowned sea turtle rescue and rehabilitation facility.
  • Surf City Trail: An easy and scenic trail that runs along the coast, offering panoramic views of the Atlantic Ocean.

Best Things to Do in Topsail Beach: Activities and Tours

Apart from natural attractions and outdoor adventures, Topsail Beach offers a variety of exciting activities and tours. Some of the best things to do in Topsail Beach include:

  • Surfing Lessons: Topsail Beach’s pristine beaches and consistent waves make it an excellent destination for surfing lessons.
  • Kayak Tours: Explore the nearby marshlands and estuaries with guided kayak tours.
  • Fishing Charters: Anglers can charter local fishing boats to fish for a variety of species, including red drum, flounder, and spotted sea trout.

Week-Long Itinerary for Topsail Beach

A week-long itinerary for Topsail Beach can be a mix of water activities, outdoor adventures, and cultural exploration. Here is a sample itinerary for your next trip:

  • Day 1 - Hit the beach and enjoy swimming or sunbathing
  • Day 2 - Explore the nearby nature preserves, hiking trails, and scenic views
  • Day 3 - Visit cultural attractions such as the Missiles and More Museum and the Assembly Building
  • Day 4 - Go on a kayaking adventure, or a fishing charter in the Atlantic Ocean
  • Day 5 - Learn about the region’s marine life and the work of rescuers at the Karen Beasley Sea Turtle Rescue and Rehabilitation Center Trail
  • Day 6 - Take a surf lesson or rent a bike and explore the town’s local shops and markets
  • Day 7 - Relax on the beach or head out for some seafood and local beer at one of the area’s top restaurants.

Food and Drink Scene of Topsail Beach

Topsail Beach’s food scene is a mix of southern and coastal cuisine, with an emphasis on seafood and local delicacies.

Must-Try Dishes

Some of the must-try dishes in Topsail Beach include:

  • Carolina BBQ: A southern classic made with pork or beef, smoked low and slow, and served with a tangy vinegar or mustard-based sauce.
  • Shrimp and Grits: A southern delicacy, this dish consists of shrimp sautéed in butter and garlic, served atop a bed of creamy grits.
  • Blue Crab: A regional favorite, this sweet and succulent crab is best enjoyed with butter and Old Bay seasoning.

Local Beverages

Topsail Beach is home to a growing craft beer scene, with several microbreweries producing unique and locally-inspired brews. Some of the top local beers include:

  • Topsail Steamer Oyster Stout
  • Barley Hollow Sea Sprite IPA
  • Salty Turtle Sugar Sneaker Belgian Triple

Where to Stay in Topsail Beach: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Topsail Beach offers a range of accommodation options, from luxury resorts to budget-friendly hotels and guesthouses. Some of the top stay options include:

  • Jolly Roger Inn and Pier: A family-friendly hotel with an oceanfront location, pool, and private pier.
  • Villa Capriani Resort: A luxurious resort featuring oceanfront villas, hot tubs, and a range of on-site amenities.
  • Topsail Shores Inn: A budget-friendly motel with an outdoor pool and easy access to nearby beaches.

What to Buy in Topsail Beach: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Topsail Beach is an excellent destination to purchase locally made handicrafts and souvenirs. Some of the best souvenirs to buy include:

  • Handmade Jewelry: A popular souvenir item in the town’s specialty shops, decorated with seashells and other local treasures.
  • Local Art: The city is home to numerous art galleries where you can purchase original works created by local artists.
  • Seafood: Head to the local seafood markets to buy fresh seafood such as shrimp, oysters, and crabs.

Local Culture and Customs of Topsail Beach

Topsail Beach’s unique culture is a blend of the region’s southern hospitality and coastal charm. Visitors can expect to encounter friendly locals, hearty seafood cuisine, and a laid-back atmosphere.

Best Time to Visit Topsail Beach

The best time to visit Topsail Beach is between May and September when the weather is warm and the town’s beaches and outdoor attractions are in full swing. However, visitors can also enjoy Topsail Beach in the offseason, with fewer crowds and lower prices.

Do’s and Don’ts of Topsail Beach

Do:

  • Respect the environment and keep the beaches clean.
  • Check the tide schedule before heading out to the beach.
  • Bring sunscreen and plenty of water to stay hydrated.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage beach habitats.
  • Touch or interfere with any wildlife, including sea turtles and birds.
  • Block access to the public beach.

Safety and Health

Safety in Topsail Beach

Topsail Beach is a safe destination to visit, with a low crime rate. Visitors should take general precautions, such as locking their valuables in their hotel rooms and being aware of their surroundings at night.

Health in Topsail Beach

Topsail Beach has several medical facilities and hospitals, including the Pender Memorial Hospital, for any health care needs that may arise during your visit.

Getting to Topsail Beach: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Topsail Beach

The nearest airport to Topsail Beach is the Wilmington International Airport, located approximately 35 miles away. Other regional airports that are within driving distance include the Albert J Ellis Airport in Jacksonville and Myrtle Beach International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Topsail Beach

Greyhound Bus Lines and Concord Coach Lines operate several routes that stop in nearby Wilmington, which is a 35-mile drive from Topsail Beach.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Topsail Beach by car, taking Route 17 or I-40. Several local car rental companies operate in the area, providing a convenient way to explore the town and nearby attractions.

How to Get Around in Topsail Beach

Topsail offers rented golf carts, bike rentals, and taxis that provide visitors with a range of transportation options. Visitors can also park their cars at designated public lots and walk or bike around the town.

Where to Go Next from Topsail Beach: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
