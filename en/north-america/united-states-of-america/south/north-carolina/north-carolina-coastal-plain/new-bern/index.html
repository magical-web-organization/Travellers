Introduction to New Bern

New Bern is a charming coastal city located in the Coastal Plain region of North Carolina. Founded in 1710, it is known for its rich history and beautiful natural landscapes. New Bern was the first capital of North Carolina, and it is home to many historic landmarks, museums, and colonial-era architecture. It is also situated on the shores of the Neuse and Trent Rivers, providing plenty of opportunities for outdoor recreation.

List of Places to Visit in New Bern

New Bern is full of attractions and historic sites. Here are the top places to visit in New Bern:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tryon Palace: This historic site was the first permanent state capital of North Carolina and was the home of Royal Governor William Tryon in the late 1700s. Today, it is a museum showcasing colonial-era living, including beautiful gardens and a working kitchen.
  • New Bern Firemen’s Museum: This museum offers visitors a glimpse into the history of firefighting equipment and techniques in the area.
  • Birthplace of Pepsi-Cola: This store and museum is housed in the pharmacy where Caleb Bradham, the creator of Pepsi-Cola, first sold the soft drink.
  • New Bern Civic Theatre: This theater showcases local plays and musical performances throughout the year.
  • New Bern Historical Society: This society offers a variety of tours and events throughout the year, including walking tours, lectures, and special events.

Natural Attractions of New Bern: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Union Point Park: This park offers stunning views of the Neuse and Trent Rivers, as well as a playground, picnic areas, and a boat launch.
  • Croatan National Forest: This forest is a great place for outdoor recreation, including hiking, hunting, fishing, and camping.
  • Lawson Creek Park: This park offers a boat ramp, kayak rentals, and a fishing pier, as well as beautiful views of the Neuse River.
  • Neuseway Nature Park: This park features an interactive science museum, a planetarium, hiking trails, and a wildlife rehabilitation area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of New Bern

New Bern has plenty of options for hiking and biking in the area. Here are some popular routes:

  • Neuse River Trail: This 27-mile trail along the Neuse River offers beautiful views of the river and plenty of opportunities for hiking, biking, and fishing.

Best Things to Do in New Bern: Activities and Tours

New Bern has plenty of activities and tours to keep visitors entertained. Here are some of the best things to do in New Bern:

  • Take a trolley tour: This tour takes visitors through the historic district of New Bern, highlighting the city’s rich history and architecture.
  • Go on a ghost tour: This guided tour takes visitors through New Bern’s most haunted locations.
  • Take a riverboat tour: This tour offers beautiful views of the Neuse and Trent Rivers, as well as information about the area’s history and wildlife.
  • Visit the Farmer’s Market: Held every Saturday, this market features local produce, crafts, and baked goods.
  • Attend a festival: New Bern hosts a variety of festivals throughout the year, including the Mumfest, the Brew Bern Beer and Wine Festival, and the Carolina Chamber Music Festival.

Week-Long Itinerary for New Bern

Here is a suggested week-long itinerary for visitors to New Bern:

Day 1: Arrive in New Bern, check into your accommodations, and take a walking tour of the historic district.

Day 2: Visit Tryon Palace and the North Carolina History Center.

Day 3: Explore Union Point Park and take a riverboat tour.

Day 4: Go hiking or biking on the Neuse River Trail.

Day 5: Visit the New Bern Firemen’s Museum and the Birthplace of Pepsi-Cola.

Day 6: Take a day trip to the nearby beaches of the Crystal Coast.

Day 7: Enjoy the local food scene and do some shopping for souvenirs.

Food and Drink Scene of New Bern

New Bern has a vibrant food and drink scene, featuring many local specialties and traditions. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Barbecue: North Carolina is known for its delicious barbecue, and New Bern is no exception. Try the pulled pork or brisket at one of the local barbecue joints.
  • Seafood: Being a coastal city, New Bern has plenty of fresh seafood options. Try the local scallops, shrimp, or oysters.
  • Skyline chili dog: This unique dish, featuring hot dogs with chili and cheese, is a favorite among locals.
  • Sweet potato pie: This classic Southern dish is a must-try dessert in New Bern.

Local Beverages

  • Pepsi-Cola: Try the original soft drink, first created in New Bern by Caleb Bradham in the late 1800s.
  • Beer: New Bern has a variety of craft breweries, including the popular Brewery 99 and the self-proclaimed “smallest brewery in America”, Half-Witted Brewing Company.
  • Wine: The region surrounding New Bern is known for its wineries, including the popular award-winning Bannerman Vineyard.

Where to Stay in New Bern: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

New Bern has no shortage of accommodation options for visitors. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • The DoubleTree by Hilton New Bern Riverfront: This waterfront hotel offers stunning views of the Neuse River, as well as a fitness center, pool, and on-site restaurant.
  • The Hanna House Bed & Breakfast: This historic B&B offers a cozy, romantic atmosphere with comfortable rooms and a gourmet breakfast.
  • The BridgePointe Hotel & Marina: This hotel offers a variety of rooms and suites, as well as a marina, pool, and complimentary breakfast.
  • The Courtyard by Marriott New Bern: This hotel is conveniently located in the historic district and offers a fitness center, pool, and on-site restaurant.

What to Buy in New Bern: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

New Bern has plenty of options for unique souvenirs and local products. Here are some ideas:

  • Pottery: The region surrounding New Bern is known for its pottery creations, and there are many shops in the area where you can buy handmade pottery pieces.
  • Crafts: The local Farmer’s Market and art stores offer a variety of handmade crafts and gifts.
  • Antiques: The historic district of New Bern has plenty of antique shops, where you can find unique and valuable pieces.

Local Culture and Customs of New Bern

New Bern has a rich and unique local culture. Here are some customs and traditions to be aware of before visiting:

  • Southern hospitality: New Bern is known for its welcoming and friendly locals, who embody the classic Southern hospitality.
  • Boating culture: Being situated on the Neuse and Trent Rivers, boating and water activities are a big part of the local lifestyle.
  • Colonial history: New Bern is steeped in colonial history, and much of the city’s architecture and traditions reflect this heritage.

Best Time to Visit New Bern

The best time to visit New Bern is during the spring and fall when the weather is mild and pleasant. Summer can be hot and humid, while winter can be chilly and rainy.

Do’s and Don’ts of New Bern

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting New Bern:

Do:

  • Visit the historic district and immerse yourself in the local history and architecture.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Take advantage of the outdoor recreation opportunities.

Don’t:

  • Miss out on the local festivals and events.
  • Overlook the natural beauty of the area, including the rivers and forests.
  • Forget to be respectful of the local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

New Bern is generally a safe and healthy destination, but visitors should still take basic precautions. Here’s what you need to know:

Safety in New Bern

  • Use common sense and be aware of your surroundings, especially at night.
  • Lock your car and secure your valuables.
  • Be cautious when boating or swimming in the rivers and ocean.

Health in New Bern

  • Use sunscreen and stay hydrated during the hot summer months.
  • Be aware of ticks and other outdoor pests, which can carry diseases like Lyme disease.
  • Check with your doctor before visiting if you have any health concerns.

Getting to New Bern: Transportation Options and Logistics

New Bern is easily accessible by car, air, or bus. Here are your transportation options:

Airports Near New Bern

The closest airport to New Bern is Coastal Carolina Regional Airport (EWN), which is located just outside of the city.

Bus Stations Near New Bern

The local Greyhound station is located in nearby Havelock, NC.

Other Options

New Bern is located just off US-70, making it easy to access by car. There are also several rental car companies located in the city.

How to Get Around in New Bern

New Bern is a walkable city, but there are also several transportation options available. Here are your options:

  • Walking: The historic district is easily walkable, and many attractions are within walking distance of each other.
  • Trolley: The local trolley runs through the historic district, offering guided tours and transportation.
  • Taxi: Several taxi companies operate in the city.
  • Bike rental: There are several bike rental locations in the city, offering visitors a fun way to explore.

Where to Go Next from New Bern: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

New Bern is conveniently located near several other popular destinations and attractions. Here are some ideas for nearby excursions:

  • The Crystal Coast: This beautiful stretch of beaches is just a short drive from New Bern, and offers plenty of outdoor recreation and water activities.
  • Wilmington: Just a few hours’ drive to the south, Wilmington is a charming coastal city with a bustling downtown area and plenty of history and culture.
  • The Outer Banks: A few hours to the north, the Outer Banks are an iconic North Carolina destination with beautiful beaches and historic lighthouses.

Conclusion

New Bern is a charming and historic coastal city that offers plenty of attractions, activities, and natural beauty. Whether you’re interested in colonial history, outdoor recreation, or local cuisine, there’s something for everyone in New Bern. Be sure to plan your trip ahead of time, taking advantage of this comprehensive guide, and enjoy all that this charming city has to offer.
