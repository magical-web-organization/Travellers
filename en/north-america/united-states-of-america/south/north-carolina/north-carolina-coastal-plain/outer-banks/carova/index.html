Carova is a small and secluded town located in the Outer Banks of North Carolina, known for its unspoiled beaches, wild horses, and rugged landscapes. If you’re looking for a peaceful and laid-back vacation destination away from the crowds, then Carova is the perfect place for you. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ve compiled everything you need to know about Carova, including the best places to visit, where to stay, and how to get around.

Introduction to Carova

Carova is a unique destination that offers a tranquil and serene atmosphere, unlike any other. Located in the North Carolina Coastal Plain, Carova is known for its stunning beaches, charming boutiques, and breathtaking natural landscapes. The town boasts of rugged sand dunes, maritime forests, and miles of pristine shoreline that make it a haven for adventure enthusiasts and nature lovers.
