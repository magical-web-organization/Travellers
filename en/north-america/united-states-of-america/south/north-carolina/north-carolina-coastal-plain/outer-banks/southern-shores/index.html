Introduction to Southern Shores

Southern Shores is a beautiful coastal town located in the Outer Banks region of North Carolina. Known for its stunning beaches, outdoor activities, and rich history, Southern Shores is a popular destination for tourists and locals alike.

The town offers a diverse range of attractions, from historic sites and museums to natural wonders and outdoor adventures. Whether you are looking for a relaxing beach vacation or an action-packed getaway, Southern Shores has something to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Southern Shores

Here is a list of the top places to visit in Southern Shores:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Wright Brothers National Memorial - a tribute to the pioneers of aviation, with exhibits and artifacts related to the Wright brothers’ historic flight
  • Roanoke Island Festival Park - a living history museum that showcases the cultural heritage of the Outer Banks and features interactive exhibits on boating, fishing, and more
  • Elizabethan Gardens - a beautiful botanical garden that pays homage to the first English colonists who settled in the area

Natural Attractions of Southern Shores: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Jockey’s Ridge State Park - home to the tallest sand dunes on the East Coast, where visitors can hike, hang glide, and sandboard
  • Alligator River National Wildlife Refuge - a vast wilderness area that is home to a variety of wildlife, including black bears, red wolves, and alligators
  • Cape Hatteras National Seashore - a stunning stretch of coastline that offers opportunities for swimming, fishing, and beachcombing

Hiking/Biking Routes of Southern Shores

There are many great hiking and biking routes in Southern Shores to explore. Some of the best ones include:

  • Duck Trail - a 6-mile paved trail that runs alongside the shoreline and offers stunning views of the ocean
  • Kitty Hawk Woods Coastal Reserve - a network of trails that wind through a lush maritime forest and lead to the sound
  • Roanoke Island Greenway - a 10-mile paved trail that runs from Manteo to the Dare County Regional Airport and offers scenic views of the Roanoke Sound

Best Things to Do in Southern Shores: Activities and Tours

Some of the best things to do in Southern Shores include:

  • Surfing lessons - with miles of pristine coastline, Southern Shores is a great place to catch a wave
  • Dolphin watching tours - a unique opportunity to see these majestic creatures up close in their natural habitat
  • Hang gliding - Jockey’s Ridge State Park is a popular spot for hang gliders, offering spectacular views from atop the sand dunes

Week-Long Itinerary for Southern Shores

Here is a sample week-long itinerary for exploring Southern Shores:

Day Activities
1 Visit the Wright Brothers National Memorial, followed by an afternoon swim at Coquina Beach
2 Take a surfing lesson in the morning, then visit the Alligator River National Wildlife Refuge in the afternoon
3 Spend the day exploring the Roanoke Island Festival Park
4 Hike the Duck Trail in the morning, then take a dolphin watching tour in the afternoon
5 Visit the Elizabethan Gardens, followed by an evening hang gliding lesson at Jockey’s Ridge State Park
6 Spend the day at the Cape Hatteras National Seashore, with opportunities for swimming, fishing, and beachcombing
7 Relax on the beach and enjoy the sun and surf

Food and Drink Scene of Southern Shores

Southern Shores is known for its seafood, southern comfort food, and local craft beer scene. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Shrimp and grits
  • Clam chowder
  • Fried chicken
  • Hushpuppies

Local Beverages

  • Outer Banks Brewing Station - a popular brewery that features a rotating selection of craft beers and live music
  • Sanctuary Vineyards - a local winery that offers tastings and tours of their vineyards
  • Kill Devil Rum - a local distillery that produces handcrafted rum using locally sourced ingredients

Where to Stay in Southern Shores: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Southern Shores offers a variety of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious beachfront resorts. Here are some of the top places to stay:

  • The Inn at Corolla Light - a boutique hotel that offers modern amenities and stunning ocean views
  • Comfort Inn South Oceanfront - a budget-friendly hotel located right on the beach
  • The Sanderling Resort - a luxurious resort that features spa services, gourmet dining, and a private beach

What to Buy in Southern Shores: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you are looking for souvenirs and local products to bring home, consider these options:

  • Beach gear and apparel from local surf shops
  • Handcrafted pottery and art from local galleries
  • Local honey and seafood products from area markets

Local Culture and Customs of Southern Shores

Southern Shores has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its cuisine, music, and arts scene. Visitors are encouraged to respect the local customs and traditions, and to be mindful of the natural environment.

Best Time to Visit Southern Shores

The best time to visit Southern Shores is in the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is warm and sunny and beaches are open for swimming. However, the area is also beautiful in the offseason, with fewer crowds and more affordable accommodation options.

Do’s and Don’ts of Southern Shores

Do:

  • Respect the local wildlife and natural environment
  • Try the local seafood and craft beer scene
  • Take advantage of the many outdoor activities, such as surfing and hang gliding

Don’t:

  • Leave trash on the beach or in other natural areas
  • Disturb the wildlife or their habitat
  • Ignore beach safety warnings or guidelines

Safety and Health

Safety in Southern Shores

Southern Shores is generally a safe place to visit, but visitors should be aware of the risks of swimming in the ocean and should follow the posted beach safety guidelines.

Health in Southern Shores

Visitors should be aware of the risks of sunburn and dehydration, and should take precautions to protect themselves by wearing sunscreen, drinking plenty of water, and seeking shade during the hottest parts of the day.

Getting to Southern Shores: Transportation Options and Logistics

Southern Shores is easily accessible by car, with several major highways leading to the area. The nearest airports are Norfolk International Airport and Raleigh-Durham International Airport, both of which offer shuttle services to the area.

Airports Near Southern Shores

  • Norfolk International Airport (ORF) - 80 miles
  • Raleigh-Durham International Airport (RDU) - 200 miles

Bus Stations Near Southern Shores

  • Greyhound Bus Station - 70 miles

Other Options

  • Car rental services are available at both airports

How to Get Around in Southern Shores

The best way to get around Southern Shores is by car, as public transportation options are limited. Biking is also a popular mode of transportation, with many bike rental services available in the area.

Where to Go Next from Southern Shores: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you are looking for nearby destinations and excursions, consider these options:

  • Asheville, NC - a vibrant city known for its craft beer scene, live music, and beautiful mountain views
  • Virginia Beach, VA - a popular beach destination that offers a variety of attractions, such as the Virginia Aquarium and Marine Science Center
  • Nags Head, NC - a nearby beach town that is known for its historic lighthouses and natural beauty

Conclusion

Southern Shores is a beautiful coastal town that offers a wealth of attractions and activities for visitors to explore. With its stunning beaches, historic sites, and natural wonders, Southern Shores is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to the Outer Banks region of North Carolina.
