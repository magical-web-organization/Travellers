Introduction to Atlantic Beach

Located in the Crystal Coast region of North Carolina, Atlantic Beach is a popular destination for beach lovers with its crystal clear waters and powdery sand beaches. Atlantic Beach offers visitors a plethora of outdoor activities, seafood specialties, and friendly local culture. Whether you’re looking for a perfect family vacation, romantic getaway, or just want a serene place to unwind and relax, Atlantic Beach won’t disappoint you. Let’s explore Atlantic Beach and its top attractions and activities.

List of Places to Visit in Atlantic Beach

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fort Macon State Park: A well-preserved Civil War fort where you can learn about the battles and history of the region. There’s also a visitor center, museum, and nature trails.
  • North Carolina Maritime Museum: This museum showcases the history, culture, and biodiversity of the North Carolina coast. Features include a shipwreck exhibit, hands-on activities, and educational programs for all ages.
  • Cape Lookout Lighthouse: A significant historical landmark and a representative of the diamond patterned towers built in the mid-nineteenth century, Cape Lookout Lighthouse, offers a mesmerizing view of the Crystal Coast.

Natural Attractions of Atlantic Beach: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Beaufort Historic District: A picturesque waterfront town known for its maritime history, charming architecture, and serene location. You can enjoy a boat ride and watch wild horses and dolphins too.
  • Rachel Carson Reserve: This pristine reserve comprises three islands that are home to endangered bird species, wild horses and is an environmental reserve to protect the ecosystem.
  • Atlantic Beach: Explore the gently shelving beach that stretches for miles in Atlantic Beach. Get some sun, read your favorite book and bask in tranquility.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Atlantic Beach

  • Neusiok Trail: An 18-mile trail located within the Croatan National Forest, the Neusiok trail leads to beautiful backcountry campgrounds, streams, and wildlife habitats.
  • Theodore Roosevelt Natural Area Trail: A scenic hiking trail situated in Blockade Runner Beach Resort and features incredible views of the Bogue Sound and the coastal wetlands.

Best Things to Do in Atlantic Beach: Activities and Tours

  • Fishing Charters: Enjoy deep-sea fishing/crabbing on a charter boat with experienced skippers, hire a boat to catch shrimp or fish off one of the many piers along the coast in Atlantic beach.
  • Kayaking and Stand-Up Paddleboarding: Kayak to the Rachel Carson Reserve or stand-up paddleboard at Bogue Inlet Pier and Triangle Beach to experience Atlantic Beach maritime beauty.
  • Scuba Diving and Snorkeling: Enjoy the amazing dive sites and underwater coral reefs on Atlantic Beach. From beginner divers to experienced ones, the waters here are perfect for learning.

Food and Drink Scene of Atlantic Beach

Must-Try Dishes

  • Calabash-style seafood: A classic seafood specialty consisting of lightly breaded and fried fish, shrimp, and scallops. The North Carolina coast is famous for its Calabash-style delicacies, which always include hush puppies, coleslaw, and French fries.

Local Beverages

  • Sweet tea: A refreshing classic Southern sweetened iced tea that’s famous in many parts of the South, including Atlantic Beach.
  • North Carolina Craft Beer: Several local breweries across the Crystal Coast offer an excellent selection of craft beer that is perfect for accompanying a seafood dinner or a live music performance.

Where to Stay in Atlantic Beach: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Islander Hotel and Resort: This luxurious resort features oceanfront rooms, pools, a beach bar, watersports rentals, and an indulging spa.
  • DoubleTree by Hilton Oceanfront: Located on the beach with oceanside dining, an outdoor pool, and an onsite restaurant serving seafood and local specialties, this hotel is perfect for couples, family, and friends alike.

What to Buy in Atlantic Beach: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Sea Salt: The Florida-based Fusion Gourmet company sells several unique flavors of sea salt, including smoked applewood, black truffle, and citrus herb, that will give your dishes a unique flavor.
  • Beachwear and Accesories: Banana Republic’s beach collection and Boutique 290 have great stores that have everything you need to look and feel beach-friendly.

Local Culture and Customs of Atlantic Beach

Atlantic Beach locals are known for their southern charm, hospitality and love for the sea. Visitors to this coastal community are always welcome with warm smiles and southern hospitality.

Best Time to Visit Atlantic Beach

The best time to visit Atlantic Beach is during the late spring and early fall seasons. Avoid the peak summer season to avoid the crowds and get a better deal on hotels and other activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Atlantic Beach

Do:

  • Wear sunscreen.
  • Take your trash back.
  • Try the Calabash-style seafood.

Don’t:

  • Litter on the beaches.
  • Touch the wild horses of the Rachel Carson Reserve.
  • Over-tip.

Safety and Health

Safety in Atlantic Beach

Atlantic Beach is at high risk for hurricanes during the late summer and early fall months. Visitors should stay informed about weather conditions, and pay attention to guidance from local officials.

Health in Atlantic Beach

Visitors should be aware of the harmful effects of sun exposure and wear sunscreen, even on cloudy days. Swimmers should avoid jellyfish stings at the coast.

Getting to Atlantic Beach: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Atlantic Beach

  • Coastal Carolina Regional Airport - New Bern - 50 miles away
  • Pitt-Greenville Airport - Greenville - 85 miles away
  • Wilmington International Airport - Wilmington - 92 miles away

Bus Stations Near Atlantic Beach

  • Greyhound Bus Station - Morehead City - 5 miles away
  • Wright Brothers Travel Plaza Bus Terminal - Havelock - 13 miles away

Other Options

A rental car, Uber, or taxi is generally the most convenient option for getting around Atlantic Beach.

How to Get Around in Atlantic Beach

Walking, biking, and renting a golf cart will provide an adventure in finding everything from a quick bite to eat to beautiful beaches and hidden gems in roadways and scenic byways.

Where to Go Next from Atlantic Beach: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Cape Hatteras National Seashore: Just a couple of hours away from Atlantic Beach, the long stretchy beach, unique dunes, and the lighthouse make this place exceptional.
  • Ocracoke Island: Take a day trip to Ocracoke Island to visit the Ocracoke Lighthouse, swim in the sound, and enjoy delicious seafood.

Conclusion

Atlantic Beach, North Carolina is a perfect holiday destination that offers a perfect mix of relaxation and adventure. From sun-soaked beaches to historical landmarks and natural reserves, you’ll find a perfect blend of activities to immerse yourself in the North Carolina coast. Visit Atlantic Beach now and have the best vacation ever!
