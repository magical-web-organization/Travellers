Introduction to Rocky Mount

Located in the North Carolina Coastal Plain, Rocky Mount is a vibrant city that offers visitors a taste of classic Southern charm with a twist of contemporary flair. Its rich history, outdoor adventures, and cultural events make it an ideal destination for families, solo travelers, and couples.

List of Places to Visit in Rocky Mount

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Rocky Mount Mills: This former cotton mill site has been repurposed into a mixed-use development with restaurants, breweries, music venues, and retail shops. Take a tour of the mill, grab a craft beer, and enjoy the vibes.
  2. Rocky Mount Fire Museum: Discover the history of firefighting in North Carolina at this fascinating museum.
  3. Douglas Block: Visit this historically African American neighborhood and learn about its role in shaping the city’s culture.
  4. Imperial Centre for the Arts and Sciences: See art exhibits, science displays, and live performances at this unique cultural center.

Natural Attractions of Rocky Mount: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Tar River Trail: Hike or bike along this 20-mile trail that runs through Rocky Mount and offers beautiful views of the Tar River.
  2. Battle Park: This 52-acre park offers nature trails, picnic areas, and a playground.
  3. Rocky Mount Sports Complex: Play a game of soccer, baseball, or volleyball at this state-of-the-art sports complex.
  4. City Lake: Rent a paddleboat or kayak and explore the 53-acre lake.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Rocky Mount

Rocky Mount offers several hiking and biking routes that provide stunning scenery and outdoor adventures. Some of the top routes include:

  • Tar River Trail: 20 miles of trail that follow the Tar River through beautiful scenery.
  • Sunset Park Trail: A one-mile paved loop that is perfect for a leisurely walk or bike ride.
  • Battle Park Trail: A two-mile trail that winds through the woods and offers stunning views of the Tar River.

Best Things to Do in Rocky Mount: Activities and Tours

  1. Horseback Riding: Take a guided horseback ride through the scenic countryside with Cedarock Farm Trail Rides.
  2. Rocky Mount Brew Tour: Enjoy a guided tour of local breweries and tasting rooms while learning about the craft beer scene in Rocky Mount.
  3. Haunted Downtown Tour: Explore the spooky side of Rocky Mount with a guided tour that takes you to historic sites and tells ghost stories.

Food and Drink Scene of Rocky Mount

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Eastern North Carolina BBQ: Try the region’s famous BBQ at Doug Sauls’ BBQ & Seafood or The Prime Smokehouse.
  2. Fried Chicken: Don’t miss this Southern classic at Bojangles’ or Mama’s Pizza.
  3. Sweet Potato Biscuits: Savor the taste of this local favorite at Mayflower Seafood Restaurant.

Local Beverages

  1. Craft Beer: Sample local brews at Koi Pond Brewing Company or HopFly Brewing Company.
  2. Wine: Sip on North Carolina wines at Rose Hill Plantation Winery.
  3. Cocktails: Enjoy innovative cocktails at Tap @ 1918 and The Tipsy Tomato.

Where to Stay in Rocky Mount: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. DoubleTree by Hilton Hotel Rocky Mount: This modern hotel offers an outdoor pool, fitness center, and on-site restaurant.
  2. Residence Inn by Marriott Rocky Mount: This extended-stay hotel features a pool, hot tub, and complimentary breakfast.
  3. Quality Inn: Located near the Rocky Mount Sports Complex, this hotel offers a free breakfast and fitness center.

What to Buy in Rocky Mount: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Rocky Mount Mills Merchandise: Bring home a souvenir from the unique shops at Rocky Mount Mills.
  2. Sweet Potato Biscuits: Buy a few boxes of this local delicacy at Mayflower Seafood Restaurant.
  3. Souvenir from the Imperial Centre: Pick up a piece of local art or a science-inspired souvenir from the gift shop at the Imperial Centre.

Local Culture and Customs of Rocky Mount

Rocky Mount has a rich African American history and culture. Visitors should take time to learn about the significant role that the Douglas Block neighborhood played in this history and visit the African American Cultural Center.

Best Time to Visit Rocky Mount

The best time to visit Rocky Mount is during the spring or fall when temperatures are mild, and the outdoor activities are in full swing. Summer is also an excellent time to visit, but be prepared for hot and humid weather.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rocky Mount

Do:

  • Do try the local BBQ.
  • Do bring comfortable shoes for hiking and exploring.
  • Do visit the African American Cultural Center.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget the bug spray if visiting during the summer.
  • Don’t litter in the parks and natural areas.
  • Don’t forget to tip your servers and tour guides.

Safety and Health

Safety in Rocky Mount

Rocky Mount, like any city, has its share of crime. Visitors should be aware of their surroundings and take necessary precautions such as locking car doors and avoiding walking alone at night.

Health in Rocky Mount

Rocky Mount has several hospitals and clinics to treat any medical needs visitors may have.

Getting to Rocky Mount: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Rocky Mount

  • Pitt-Greenville Airport (PGV)
  • Raleigh-Durham International Airport (RDU)

Bus Stations Near Rocky Mount

  • Greyhound Bus Lines
  • Carolina Trailways

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Rocky Mount via Interstate 95 or take a train to the Rocky Mount Amtrak Station.

How to Get Around in Rocky Mount

Visitors can rent a car, take a taxi, or use public transportation to get around Rocky Mount.

Where to Go Next from Rocky Mount: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. North Carolina Zoo: A two-hour drive from Rocky Mount, the North Carolina Zoo is one of the nation’s largest natural habitats for wildlife.
  2. Wilmington: A three-hour drive from Rocky Mount, Wilmington boasts historic charm, beaches, and a bustling downtown.
  3. Asheville: A four-hour drive from Rocky Mount, Asheville offers stunning views and outdoor activities in the Great Smoky Mountains.

Conclusion

Rocky Mount is a charming Southern city that offers a unique mix of culture, history, and outdoor activities. With so much to see and do, it’s a must-visit destination for any traveler to North Carolina.
