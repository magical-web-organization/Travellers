Introduction to Tarboro

Tarboro is a charming town situated in the North Carolina Coastal Plain in North Carolina, United States of America. Known for its historic architecture, rich cultural heritage, and natural beauty, Tarboro is a favorite destination among travelers visiting North Carolina.

With its fascinating museums, beautiful parks, scenic views, and outdoor activities, Tarboro is an ideal place for a family vacation, a romantic getaway, or a solo trip. Whether you’re a nature lover, a history buff, or a foodie, there’s something for everyone in Tarboro.

List of Places to Visit in Tarboro

Here are some of the top attractions to visit during your trip to Tarboro:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Edgecombe County Veterans’ Military Museum: This museum showcases the history of American military service and honors the men and women from Edgecombe County who served their country.
  • Historic District: Home to over 300 buildings, including several 18th and 19th century homes, this district offers a glimpse into Tarboro’s rich architectural heritage.
  • Tarboro Town Common: With its beautiful fountains, amphitheater, picnic areas, and historic sites, the Tarboro Town Common is a must-visit attraction in Tarboro.
  • Braswell Memorial Library: This impressive library is home to an extensive collection of books, manuscripts, and other materials that document Tarboro’s history and culture.

Natural Attractions of Tarboro: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Indian Lake: This lake is a great spot for fishing, picnicking, and boating.
  • Riverfront Park: This scenic park offers stunning views of the Tar River and is an ideal place for a leisurely stroll or a picnic.
  • Hardee Creek Recreation Area: This park offers hiking and biking trails, fishing, and camping opportunities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tarboro

Tarboro offers several hiking and biking trails that are perfect for exploring the town’s beautiful natural scenery. Some of the best routes include:

  • Tarboro Loop Trail: This 6.7-mile trail takes you through the Tarboro countryside and offers stunning views of the Tar River.
  • Riverfront Park Trail: This easy 1.1-mile trail follows the Tar River and is perfect for a leisurely walk or bike ride.
  • Hardee Creek Trail: This 2.7-mile trail takes you through the Hardee Creek Recreation Area and offers beautiful views of the surrounding forest.

Best Things to Do in Tarboro: Activities and Tours

Aside from hiking and biking, Tarboro offers several other exciting activities and tours that are worth trying, including:

  • Tarboro Brewing Company Tour: Take a tour of the Tarboro Brewing Company and learn about the beer-making process while sampling delicious local brews.
  • Tarboro Historical Walking Tour: Explore Tarboro’s rich cultural heritage on this informative walking tour through the town’s historic district.
  • Shopping and Dining: Explore Tarboro’s unique boutiques, antique shops, and restaurants for a taste of local culture and cuisine.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tarboro

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Tarboro, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day Activity
Day 1 Explore Tarboro's Historic District and visit the Edgecombe County Veterans' Military Museum.
Day 2 Try hiking or biking on one of Tarboro's trails, then spend the afternoon picnicking at Riverfront Park.
Day 3 Visit Hardee Creek Recreation Area for camping, hiking, and fishing.
Day 4 Take a tour of Tarboro Brewing Company and sample some of the best local brews.
Day 5 Explore the Tarboro Town Common, home to beautiful fountains, picnic areas, and historic sites.

Food and Drink Scene of Tarboro

Tarboro is renowned for its tasty and unique cuisine, drawing inspiration from Southern, African, and European cooking.

Must-Try Dishes

  • BBQ Ribs: A Southern favorite, BBQ ribs are a Tarboro specialty, often slow-cooked and served with homemade barbecue sauce.
  • Herring Roe: A popular North Carolina delicacy, herring roe is a unique and flavorful dish that’s often served with eggs.
  • Pimento Cheese: A popular Tarboro spread made from cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, and pimentos, served on crackers or sandwiches.

Local Beverages

  • Tarboro Brewing Company: A local favorite, this brewery offers a range of delicious local brews, including IPAs, stouts, and porters.
  • Southern Tea: A refreshing drink made from sweet tea, lemonade, and sometimes whiskey.

Where to Stay in Tarboro: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Tarboro has a variety of accommodation options to choose from, whether you’re looking for a budget-friendly hostel or a luxury hotel. Here are some of the best options:

  • The Tarboro Hotel: This boutique hotel is situated in the heart of Tarboro’s historic district and offers luxury rooms and top-notch amenities.
  • Holiday Inn Express & Suites Tarboro: A popular budget-friendly option, this hotel offers comfortable rooms and a complimentary breakfast.
  • The Combs’ Guesthouse: This charming guesthouse offers cozy rooms and a home-away-from-home atmosphere.

What to Buy in Tarboro: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for a unique souvenir or a gift for a loved one, Tarboro offers plenty of options. Here are some of the best things to buy in Tarboro:

  • Handmade pottery and ceramics: Tarboro is home to several talented local ceramicists, creating unique and beautiful pottery pieces.
  • Craft beer: Grab a few bottles of local craft beer from the Tarboro Brewing Company to bring home and enjoy.
  • Local honey: Made from the nectar of local flowers, Tarboro honey is a delicious treat that’s unique to the region.

Local Culture and Customs of Tarboro

Tarboro’s rich cultural heritage is evident in its architecture, museums, and festivals. Visitors to Tarboro are encouraged to respect the town’s history and to learn about the local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Tarboro

The best time to visit Tarboro is during the spring and fall, when the weather is mild and comfortable, and the scenery is at its most beautiful. Summers can be hot and humid, while winters can be chilly and unpredictable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tarboro

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Tarboro:

Do:

  • Explore Tarboro’s Historic District and museums.
  • Try the local cuisine, including barbecue and craft beer.
  • Respect the local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Ignore the town’s rich history and architecture.
  • Leave without trying some of the local specialties.
  • Disrespect the local culture or traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tarboro

Tarboro is a safe and friendly town, but visitors should take standard safety precautions, such as being aware of their surroundings and avoiding unfamiliar areas at night.

Health in Tarboro

Visitors to Tarboro should be up-to-date on routine vaccinations and should take precautions to prevent Lyme disease, which is prevalent in the region.

Getting to Tarboro: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tarboro

The closest airport to Tarboro is the Pitt-Greenville Airport (PGV), located approximately 30 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Tarboro

The Greyhound Bus Station in Rocky Mount, about 20 miles away, is the closest bus station to Tarboro.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Tarboro by car, with several major highways running through the region, including I-95 and US-64. Additionally, visitors can take the Amtrak train to the nearby Rocky Mount Station and hire a cab or rental car to reach Tarboro.

How to Get Around in Tarboro

While Tarboro is a walkable town, visitors can also opt to rent a car or bike or take a taxi to explore the region more easily.

Where to Go Next from Tarboro: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Greenville: Located about 25 miles away, Greenville offers a variety of shopping, dining, and cultural attractions.
  • Rocky Mount: Rocky Mount is located about 20 miles away and offers a variety of outdoor activities, including biking, hiking, and fishing.
  • Wilmington: Located about 120 miles away, Wilmington is a popular coastal town known for its beaches, water activities, and historic sites.

Conclusion

Tarboro is a town steeped in history and culture, offering visitors a unique and unforgettable experience. With its combination of natural beauty, fascinating museums, and delicious cuisine, Tarboro is the perfect destination for travelers looking for something off the beaten path.
