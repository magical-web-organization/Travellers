Introduction to Havelock

Located in the North Carolina Coastal Plain, Havelock is a small town known for its natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and rich history. Whether you’re looking to explore the great outdoors, immerse yourself in local culture, or simply relax and unwind, Havelock offers an exciting range of activities and experiences.

In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ll take a closer look at the top places to visit, things to do, and sights to see in Havelock. From scenic hikes and outdoor adventures to museums and historical attractions, we’ve got you covered.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Havelock boasts a rich history, and there are several museums and historical sites that are worth visiting in the area. Here are some of the top attractions:

  • Havelock Cultural Arts Center: This center serves as a hub for local artists and features rotating exhibits, performances, and workshops.

  • Cherry Point History Museum: Located on Cherry Point Marine Corps Air Station, this museum tells the story of the base’s history and its impact on the local community.

  • Havelock Historical Tour and Trail: Take a walking tour of Havelock’s historic downtown area and learn about local landmarks and buildings.

Natural Attractions of Havelock: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Havelock is surrounded by natural beauty, and there are plenty of outdoor adventures and scenic views to explore. Here are some of the top attractions:

  • Croatan National Forest: This vast forest offers hiking, camping, fishing, and wildlife watching opportunities.

  • Neusiok Trail: This scenic trail winds through Croatan National Forest, offering stunning views of the forest and nearby waterways.

  • Cherry Point Bay: This picturesque bay is a popular spot for boating, fishing, and swimming.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Havelock

Havelock is surrounded by scenic trails that are perfect for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the top routes:

  • Neusiok Trail: This 20-mile trail winds through Croatan National Forest and offers stunning views of the forest and nearby waterways.

  • Slocum Creek Trail: This 7-mile trail can be accessed from the Croatan National Forest Visitor Center and offers a scenic hike through the woods.

  • Havelock City Trail: This 2-mile trail winds through the heart of Havelock and offers a leisurely stroll through the town’s historic district.

Best Things to Do in Havelock: Activities and Tours

From outdoor adventures to cultural experiences, there’s plenty to see and do in Havelock. Here are some of the top activities and tours:

  • Fishing Charters: Take a guided fishing tour of Cherry Point Bay and try your luck at catching flounder, trout, and drum.

  • Kayaking and Canoeing: Explore the area’s waterways on a guided kayaking or canoeing tour.

  • Swimming and Beaches: Havelock has several nearby beaches, including Pine Knoll Shores and Atlantic Beach.

Week-Long Itinerary for Havelock

Day 1: Explore downtown Havelock and visit the Havelock Cultural Arts Center.

Day 2: Take a hiking or biking tour of Croatan National Forest.

Day 3: Visit the Cherry Point History Museum and learn about the base’s history.

Day 4: Take a guided fishing tour of Cherry Point Bay.

Day 5: Learn about the town’s history on the Havelock Historical Tour and Trail.

Day 6: Visit nearby beaches, including Atlantic Beach and Pine Knoll Shores.

Day 7: Take a guided kayaking or canoeing tour of the area’s waterways.

Food and Drink Scene of Havelock

Havelock offers a range of dining options, from casual cafes to upscale restaurants. Here are some of the must-try dishes:

  • Fried Shrimp: Havelock is known for its fresh seafood, and fried shrimp is a local favorite.

  • Barbecue: Eastern North Carolina-style barbecue is a must-try, with pulled pork and vinegar-based sauces.

  • Sweet Tea: A southern staple, sweet tea is the perfect thirst quencher on a hot day.

Where to Stay in Havelock: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Havelock offers a range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. Here are some of the best options:

  • Hampton Inn Havelock: This hotel offers comfortable rooms and easy access to local attractions.

  • Sheraton Atlantic Beach: This beachfront hotel offers stunning views and a range of amenities, including an outdoor pool and fitness center.

  • Camping: There are several campsites in and around Croatan National Forest for those who prefer to be closer to nature.

What to Buy in Havelock: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Havelock offers a range of local products that make great souvenirs. Here are some of the top options:

  • North Carolina BBQ Sauce: Bring home a bottle of the state’s famous vinegar-based BBQ sauce.

  • Havelock T-Shirts: Show off your love for Havelock with a locally-made t-shirt.

  • Seafood: Buy fresh seafood from local markets and cook up a southern feast at home.

Local Culture and Customs of Havelock

Havelock residents are known for their southern hospitality and welcoming spirit. Visitors should expect to be greeted with a smile and a friendly conversation.

Best Time to Visit Havelock

The best time to visit Havelock is during the spring and fall months when the weather is mild and outdoor activities are in full swing. Summer is also a popular time to visit, but be prepared for hot temperatures and crowded beaches.

Do’s and Don’ts of Havelock

Do:

  • Do explore the area’s natural beauty.

  • Do try the local seafood and barbecue.

  • Do bring comfortable shoes for hiking and biking.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or leave trash behind on hiking trails and beaches.

  • Don’t forget to wear sunscreen and bug spray.

  • Don’t ignore signs warning of unsafe swimming conditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Havelock

Havelock is a safe and welcoming town, but visitors should still take common sense precautions when exploring the area’s natural beauty. Be aware of your surroundings and avoid hiking or swimming alone.

Health in Havelock

There are several medical clinics and pharmacies in Havelock, but visitors with serious health concerns should seek medical attention at nearby hospitals in New Bern or Morehead City.

Getting to Havelock: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Havelock

The closest airport to Havelock is Coastal Carolina Regional Airport in New Bern, which is approximately 20 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Havelock

There are no bus stations in Havelock, but Greyhound operates a station in New Bern, which is approximately 20 miles away.

Other Options

Car rental services are available at both the airport and in town, making it easy to explore the area at your own pace.

How to Get Around in Havelock

The best way to get around Havelock is by car or bike. There are several bike rental companies in town, and most local attractions are within driving distance.

Where to Go Next from Havelock: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Havelock is centrally located, making it easy to explore nearby attractions. Here are some of the top excursions and destinations:

  • New Bern: This historic town is approximately 20 miles away and offers a range of cultural and historical attractions.

  • Atlantic Beach: This nearby beach destination offers stunning views and a range of water activities.

  • Morehead City: This coastal town is approximately 25 miles away and offers a range of shopping, dining, and entertainment options.

Conclusion

Havelock is an outdoor enthusiast’s dream, offering stunning natural beauty, rich history, and a welcoming southern spirit. Whether you’re looking to hike, swim, or simply relax and unwind, Havelock has something for everyone. Follow this tour guide to make the most out of your trip to this charming coastal town.
