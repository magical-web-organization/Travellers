Introduction to Greenville

Greenville is a vibrant city located in the Coastal Plain region of North Carolina, United States. It is home to East Carolina University, one of the largest research universities in the state. Greenville is known for its rich history, lively arts and cultural scene, and picturesque natural beauty.

Local Culture and Customs of Greenville

The people of Greenville are known for their warm hospitality and Southern charm. The culture is influenced by the region’s rich history and art, particularly in music and literature. In Greenville, it’s important to be respectful of local customs, including Southern manners and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Greenville

The best time to visit Greenville is in the fall, when the weather is mild and comfortable. In September, the city holds the Street Dances and Food Truck Festival, which is a great way to experience local culture and food. Spring is also a good time to visit, as the city comes alive with blooming flowers.

Safety and Health

Safety in Greenville

Like any city, Greenville has its share of crime. Travelers should exercise common sense and take precautions to stay safe, especially at night. Some areas to avoid include the westside and downtown eastside.

Health in Greenville

In terms of health, Greenville has excellent medical facilities, including Vidant Medical Center and the ECU Health Sciences Campus.

Getting to Greenville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Greenville

The nearest airport to Greenville is Pitt-Greenville Airport, which is located just a few miles outside the city center. Raleigh-Durham International Airport is about a 90-minute drive from Greenville.

Bus Stations Near Greenville

The Greyhound Bus Station is located in Kinston, about 25 minutes from Greenville. The Amtrak station is also located in Kinston and provides daily service to locations throughout the country.

Other Options

If you’re traveling by car, Greenville is conveniently located off of Highway 11 and Highway 264.

How to Get Around in Greenville

The best way to get around in Greenville is by car. There are several car rental agencies located at the airport and downtown. Taxis and ride sharing services are also available.
