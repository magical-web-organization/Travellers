Introduction to Statesville

Located within the Charlotte Metro area, in Piedmont, North Carolina, Statesville is a stunning city situated in the south of the United States of America. The city has a rich history and cultural heritage, with a diverse range of activities and experiences. Statesville has become a popular destination for tourists, who come to experience the natural beauty and cultural attractions that await them.

List of Places to Visit in Statesville

Statesville offers numerous tourist attractions that cater to different interests and preferences. Here is a list of the top places you should visit in Statesville:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fort Dobbs State Historic Site: This historic site is an 18th-century fort that played a crucial role in the French and Indian War. Visitors can explore the fort, museum, and outdoor exhibits.
  • Iredell Museums: This museum showcases the cultural heritage of the region through exhibits on art, history, and science.
  • Zootastic Park: This zoo houses numerous animal species from around the world, including tigers, lions, and giraffes.

Natural Attractions of Statesville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Norman State Park: This park is located on the largest man-made lake in North Carolina and offers opportunities for fishing, boating, hiking and camping.
  • South Mountain State Park: This park is known for its stunning views of the mountains, hiking trails, picnic areas, and waterfalls.
  • Daveste Vineyards: This vineyard has stunning views and offers tours, wine tastings, and live music.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Statesville

Statesville is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some popular hiking/biking routes:

  • Carolina Thread Trail: This trail spans across 15 counties in North and South Carolina and offers several trails for hiking and biking.
  • Lake Norman State Park: This park has more than 30 miles of trails, including the 6.5-mile Lake Shore Trail, which offers stunning views of the lake.
  • Mountain Biking Trails at Foothills Outdoor Recreation Center: This recreational center offers more than 7 miles of mountain biking trails, providing a thrilling experience for bikers.

Best Things to Do in Statesville: Activities and Tours

Statesville has a range of activities and tours that cater to different interests and preferences. Here are some of the top things to do in Statesville:

  • Hot Air Balloon Ride: Enjoy breathtaking views of the countryside by taking a hot air balloon ride over Statesville.
  • Wine Tasting Tours: Statesville has numerous vineyards and wineries that offer tours and tastings of their locally made wines.
  • Racing Experience: Statesville is famous for its racing heritage; try driving a race car at the NASCAR Racing Experience offered at the Charlotte Motor Speedway or the Bowman Gray Stadium in Winston-Salem.

Food and Drink Scene of Statesville

Statesville has a diverse range of restaurants and bars that offer a unique blend of local and international cuisines. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Carolina BBQ: Statesville is famous for its barbecue dishes; try the pulled pork, brisket, or ribs.
  • Sweet Potato Hash: This dish is a local favorite, made with sweet potatoes, cinnamon, and other spices.
  • Cheerwine: Try a refreshing local soda; Cheerwine is a cherry-flavored drink that originated in North Carolina.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beer: Statesville has several breweries that offer unique, locally brewed beers. Try the Third Creek Brewery or the Lineberger’s Maple Springs Brewery.
  • Wine: Statesville has numerous vineyards and wineries that produce excellent local wines. Be sure to try the cabernet sauvignon or chardonnay!

Local Culture and Customs of Statesville

Statesville has a unique and rich culture that is influenced by its historic importance and natural beauty. Here are some cultural customs to keep in mind while in Statesville:

  • Respect Heritage Sites: Statesville has numerous heritage sites and monuments, be sure to treat them with respect and do not damage or vandalize them.
  • Southern Hospitality: Statesville has a tradition of Southern hospitality, where people are friendly and welcoming. Be sure to reciprocate the kindness!
  • Appreciate Nature: Statesville is known for its natural beauty and outdoor adventures. Be sure to appreciate and respect the environment by keeping the trails clean and following the rules while hiking and camping.

Best Time to Visit Statesville

The best time to visit Statesville is during the fall season (September to November) when temperatures are comfortable and the forests are vibrant with the colors of autumn. The spring season (March to May) is also a great time to visit, with mild temperatures and blooming flowers.

Getting to Statesville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Statesville:

  • Piedmont Triad International Airport is located approximately 55 miles from Statesville.
  • Charlotte Douglas International Airport located approximately 49 miles from Statesville.

Bus Stations Near Statesville:

  • Greyhound Bus Station is located at 719 Sullivan Rd, Statesville, NC.

Other Options:

  • Amtrak Train Station is located approximately 22 miles east of Statesville in Spencer, NC.
  • Charlotte Area Transit System provides bus options for commuting within Charlotte Metro area.

How to Get Around in Statesville

The best way to get around Statesville is by car; however, renting a bike or using a ride-sharing app such as Uber or Lyft is also an option.
