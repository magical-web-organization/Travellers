Introduction to Matthews

Matthews, North Carolina, is a charming town located in the Charlotte Metro area. This quaint town offers an abundance of cultural attractions, outdoor adventures, and delicious food and drink options. Whether you’re a history buff, nature lover, or foodie, Matthews has something to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Matthews

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Matthews Heritage Museum

The Matthews Heritage Museum is a must-visit attraction for history buffs. This museum showcases the history of Matthews, including the town’s early settlement, the arrival of the railroad, and the development of the cotton mill industry.

Reid House

The Reid House is a historic home located in downtown Matthews that has been restored to its original glory. Visitors can take a guided tour of the home and learn about its history and architecture.

Mint Hill Country Doctor’s Museum

The Mint Hill Country Doctor’s Museum is a unique attraction that offers a glimpse into the lives of rural doctors in the 19th and early 20th centuries. This museum showcases medical tools, equipment, and other artifacts from that era.

Natural Attractions of Matthews: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Four Mile Creek Greenway

The Four Mile Creek Greenway is a 2.5-mile trail that offers a peaceful escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. This trail is perfect for hiking, jogging, or biking and provides scenic views of the surrounding nature.

Squirrel Lake Park

Squirrel Lake Park is a beautiful park that offers a range of outdoor activities, including fishing, hiking, and picnicking. This park boasts a picturesque lake, walking trails, playgrounds, and sports facilities.

McDowell Nature Preserve

The McDowell Nature Preserve is a 1,132-acre park that features miles of hiking and biking trails, as well as a boat launch and picnic areas. This park is a popular spot for birdwatchers and wildlife enthusiasts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Matthews

Matthews offers an excellent selection of hiking and biking trails for outdoor enthusiasts. The Four Mile Creek Greenway is a popular paved trail that offers views of the local flora and fauna. The Colonel Francis Beatty Park offers several trails of varying difficulties for hikers and bikers of all levels. The Squirrel Lake Park also offers trails suitable for hiking and biking.

Best Things to Do in Matthews: Activities and Tours

Wine Tasting Tours

Matthews offers several wine tasting tours that allow visitors to sample locally produced wines while learning about the winemaking process.

Segway Tours

Segway tours offer a unique way to explore Matthews and experience its local attractions. Tour guides will take you through the town’s historic districts, parks, and cultural attractions while providing interesting insights into the area.

Matthews Farmers Market

The Matthews Farmers Market is a must-visit attraction for foodies. This market offers fresh, locally grown produce, meats, and dairy products, as well as locally made artisanal products.

Food and Drink Scene of Matthews

Must-Try Dishes

Matthews Farmers Market

A trip to the Matthews Farmers Market is incomplete without trying its freshly grilled corn, baked bread, and the local honey.

Grilled Steaks

Matthews is well-known for its delicious grilled steaks, which are prepared with a unique blend of spices and seasonings.

Local Beverages

Local Craft Beer

Matthews has several breweries that offer a variety of craft beers. Be sure to try the locally brewed IPAs during your stay.

Bourbon

North Carolina also has a thriving bourbon industry, so be sure to try the local bourbon, which has a unique flavor profile.

Where to Stay in Matthews: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Matthews offers an extensive selection of accommodations, ranging from budget-friendly options to luxurious four-star hotels. Some popular options include the Courtyard Charlotte Matthews, the Hilton Garden Inn Charlotte-Matthews, and the Sleep Inn Matthews. There are also several local guesthouses and vacation rentals available for travelers who prefer a more personalized experience.

What to Buy in Matthews: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

JM Parker Pottery

Located in downtown Matthews, this pottery shop offers locally made ceramic products, including vases, bowls, mugs, and other decorative items.

Renfrow Hardware

Renfrow Hardware is a historic hardware store that has been in operation since 1900. This store offers locally sourced products, including woodworking tools, gardening supplies, and home goods.

Local Culture and Customs of Matthews

Matthews is a Southern town that prides itself on its hospitality and charm. Locals are friendly and welcoming, and visitors are encouraged to immerse themselves in Matthews’ culture by attending local events and festivals.

Best Time to Visit Matthews

The best time to visit Matthews is during the spring and fall when the weather is mild, and the town is alive with local events and festivities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Matthews

Safety and Health

Safety in Matthews

Matthews is a safe town, and visitors should take basic safety precautions, such as keeping belongings safe and secure while traveling.

Health in Matthews

There are several medical facilities available in Matthews, including urgent care clinics and hospitals. Visitors should also take necessary precautions to protect themselves from insect bites and other outdoor hazards.

Getting to Matthews: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Matthews

Charlotte Douglas International Airport is the closest airport to Matthews, located about 17 miles away. The airport is served by several major airlines, including American Airlines, Delta, and United.

Bus Stations Near Matthews

The Greyhound station in Charlotte is the closest bus station to Matthews, located about 15 miles away.

Other Options

Matthews is easily accessible by car via I-485, which runs around the city of Charlotte. Taxis, ride-sharing apps, and rental cars are also available for travelers who prefer not to drive themselves.

How to Get Around in Matthews

The best way to get around Matthews is by car, but there are also several taxi services and ride-sharing apps available. Matthews also has a bike-friendly community, making cycling a popular alternative to driving.

Where to Go Next from Matthews: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several nearby destinations and excursions that are easily accessible from Matthews. Charlotte, North Carolina’s largest city, is only a short drive away and offers a variety of cultural attractions, shopping, dining, and entertainment options. Other nearby destinations include the Blue Ridge Mountains, which offer spectacular views and outdoor adventures, and the Outer Banks, a popular destination for beach lovers.

Conclusion

Matthews, North Carolina, has something to offer every traveler, from history and culture to nature and adventure. With its friendly locals, delicious cuisine, and charming atmosphere, Matthews is a must-visit destination for anyone exploring North Carolina.
