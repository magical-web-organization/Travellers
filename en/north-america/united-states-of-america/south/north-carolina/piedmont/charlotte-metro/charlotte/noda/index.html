Introduction to NoDa

North Davidson, also known as NoDa, is a vibrant arts district located in Charlotte, North Carolina. This bustling neighborhood is filled with art galleries, music venues, and other creative spaces that make it an exciting place to explore.

List of Places to Visit in NoDa

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • NoDa Brewing Company: Founded in 2011, this brewery is known for its craft beer and laid-back atmosphere. Visitors can enjoy tours, tastings, and special events.
  • Ciel Gallery: This contemporary art space features works by local and national artists. It also hosts workshops and other events for art lovers.
  • The Evening Muse: This intimate venue is a favorite among music lovers in NoDa. It hosts a variety of live music shows, including local and national acts.
  • Neighborhood Theatre: This historic theater has been entertaining audiences since 1945. Today, it hosts a variety of events, including concerts, comedy shows, and film screenings.

Natural Attractions of NoDa: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • North Davidson Park: This park is a great spot for picnicking, playing sports, or simply relaxing. It also has a playground for children.
  • Little Sugar Creek Greenway: This scenic trail is perfect for walking, jogging, or biking. It runs along a creek and features fantastic views of NoDa and the surrounding area.
  • DNC Rail Trail: This former railway track has been converted into a walkway that connects NoDa with other neighborhoods in Charlotte. It’s a great way to explore the city on foot.

Hiking/Biking Routes of NoDa

  • The Plaza Midwood and NoDa Loop: This 7.5-mile loop runs through both NoDa and the nearby Plaza Midwood neighborhood. It’s a great way to explore the area on foot or bike.
  • Little Sugar Creek Greenway: This trail runs through NoDa and other parts of Charlotte. It’s a popular spot for hiking, jogging, and biking.

Best Things to Do in NoDa: Activities and Tours

  • NoDa Brewery Tour: Take a tour of one of the city’s most popular breweries and sample its delicious craft beer.
  • Art Walk: NoDa hosts a monthly art walk on the first Friday of every month. Visitors can explore galleries, studios, and other creative spaces in the neighborhood.
  • Food Tour: NoDa is home to some fantastic restaurants and cafes. Take a tour and sample some of the best food the neighborhood has to offer.

Food and Drink Scene of NoDa

Must-Try Dishes

  • Biscuits and gravy: Try this southern staple at Smelly Cat Coffee House and Roasters.
  • Barbecue: NoDa’s barbecue joint, Heirloom, is a must-visit for anyone looking for classic southern BBQ.
  • Tacos: Cabo Fish Taco serves up delicious fish tacos and other Mexican fare.

Local Beverages

  • Craft beer: NoDa Brewing Company is the neighborhood’s most popular brewery, but there are plenty of other great options in the area.
  • Coffee: Smelly Cat Coffee House and Roasters serves up delicious coffee drinks in a cozy atmosphere.

Where to Stay in NoDa: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Parkwood Inn: This charming guesthouse is located in nearby Plaza Midwood and is within walking distance of NoDa.
  • The VanLandingham Estate: This historic inn is located in Plaza Midwood and offers luxurious accommodations in a beautiful setting.

What to Buy in NoDa: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handmade art: NoDa is a great place to shop for one-of-a-kind pieces of art. Check out Ciel Gallery and other local galleries for unique finds.
  • Craft beer: NoDa Brewing Company and other local breweries offer great souvenirs in the form of craft beer cans, growlers, or glasses.

Local Culture and Customs of NoDa

  • Artistic community: NoDa is known for its thriving arts scene and creative community. Expect to see plenty of artists, musicians, and other creatives in the neighborhood.
  • Laid-back atmosphere: NoDa is a welcoming and friendly neighborhood where residents and visitors alike can relax and enjoy the local culture.

Best Time to Visit NoDa

The best time to visit NoDa is during the spring and fall when the weather is mild and the outdoor activities are at their best. The neighborhood has a variety of events and festivals throughout the year, so be sure to check the calendar before planning your trip.

Do’s and Don’ts of NoDa

Do:

  • Do explore the neighborhood on foot or bike to fully appreciate its charm.
  • Do attend the monthly art walk to discover new galleries and artists.
  • Do try the local beer and food scene.

Don’t:

  • Don’t miss out on the neighborhood’s many outdoor activities and scenic views.
  • Don’t forget to bring comfortable shoes for walking and biking.
  • Don’t be afraid to strike up a conversation with friendly locals.

Safety and Health

Safety in NoDa

NoDa is a safe neighborhood with low crime rates. However, visitors should still exercise common sense and take precautions to ensure their safety.

Health in NoDa

NoDa is home to several medical facilities, including Novant Health Presbyterian Medical Center and Carolinas Medical Center. Visitors should also be sure to bring any necessary medications with them on their trip.

Getting to NoDa: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near NoDa

  • Charlotte Douglas International Airport: This airport is located just a few miles from NoDa and offers numerous flights to domestic and international destinations.

Bus Stations Near NoDa

  • Greyhound Bus Station: This bus station is located in downtown Charlotte, just a few miles from NoDa.

Other Options

  • Cabs and ride-shares: There are plenty of cabs and ride-share services, like Uber and Lyft, available in NoDa and throughout Charlotte.

How to Get Around in NoDa

NoDa is a walkable neighborhood that is also bike-friendly. Visitors can easily explore the area on foot or by bike. There are also cabs and ride-shares available for longer trips.

Where to Go Next from NoDa: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • U.S. National Whitewater Center: This outdoor adventure park is located just a few miles from NoDa and offers a variety of outdoor activities, including white-water rafting, zip-lining, and rock climbing.
  • Discovery Place: This science museum in downtown Charlotte is a great destination for families with children.
  • Charlotte Motor Speedway: Racing fans can catch a NASCAR race or take a tour of this historic speedway located just a few miles from NoDa.

Conclusion

NoDa is an exciting neighborhood that offers something for everyone. Whether you’re an art lover, a foodie, or an outdoor enthusiast, you’re sure to find plenty to explore and enjoy in this vibrant part of Charlotte.
