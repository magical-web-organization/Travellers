Introduction to Plaza-Midwood

Plaza-Midwood is a vibrant neighborhood located in Charlotte, North Carolina. The area is known for its tree-lined streets, historic homes, and eclectic mix of shops, restaurants, and bars. With a diverse community and rich history, Plaza-Midwood is a must-visit destination for both locals and tourists alike.

List of Places to Visit in Plaza-Midwood

Here are some top attractions to check out in Plaza-Midwood:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Charlotte Museum of History: Learn about the city’s history and heritage by exploring interactive exhibits, historical buildings, and beautiful gardens.
  • The Light Factory: This contemporary photography museum offers exhibitions, classes, workshops, and events that celebrate the artistic and cultural achievements of photographers from around the world.
  • The VanLandingham Estate: This historic mansion and gardens dates back to 1913 and now operates as a bed and breakfast and wedding venue.

Natural Attractions of Plaza-Midwood: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Charlotte Greenway: This 40-mile trail provides access to parks, nature preserves, and other scenic attractions throughout the city, including Plaza-Midwood.
  • The Little Sugar Creek Greenway: This 7.2-mile trail follows the meandering creek through urban and natural landscapes, and has several access points in and around Plaza-Midwood.
  • The UNC Charlotte Botanical Gardens: Explore this beautiful 10-acre park with gardens, fountains, and sculptures that showcases the diverse flora of North Carolina and beyond.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Plaza-Midwood

Hiking and biking enthusiasts will love the many trails and parks in and around Plaza-Midwood. Some popular options include:

  • McAlpine Creek Greenway: This 8-mile trail offers paved and unpaved paths that wind through tranquil forests, over picturesque bridges, and alongside babbling creeks.
  • The Carolina Thread Trail: Another great option for hikers and bikers, this 15-mile trail connects communities, parks, and preserves throughout the region, and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Plaza-Midwood Neighborhood: The neighborhood itself is perfect for an afternoon bike ride or leisurely stroll, thanks to its wide sidewalks, peaceful side streets, and charming architecture.

Best Things to Do in Plaza-Midwood: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re interested in history, art, or food, Plaza-Midwood has plenty of activities and tours to choose from. Here are a few ideas:

  • Charlotte Brews Cruise: Visit local microbreweries and sample some of the best craft beer in the city on this guided tour.
  • The Plaza-Midwood Art Crawl: Check out some of the latest works by local artists at this monthly event, which includes gallery tours, live music, and food trucks.
  • The Plaza-Midwood Food Tour: Satisfy your taste buds with this guided culinary tour, which takes you to some of the best eateries in the neighborhood.

Food and Drink Scene of Plaza-Midwood

Plaza-Midwood is known for its vibrant food and drink scene, which features a diverse mix of cuisines and styles. Here are a few must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fried chicken at Price’s Chicken Coop: This neighborhood institution has been serving up mouth-watering fried chicken since 1962.
  • Pork belly sliders at Soul Gastrolounge: This trendy spot offers an upscale take on classic gastropub fare, including these delicious sliders.
  • Chorizo tacos at Que Onda Tacos: This colorful food truck serves up some of the best Mexican street food in the city.

Local Beverages

  • Craft beer at Heist Brewery: Try some of the best beers in town at this popular brewpub, which offers both indoor and outdoor seating.
  • Cocktails at Soul Gastrolounge: Enjoy expertly crafted cocktails in a chic and comfortable atmosphere at this hip gastropub.
  • Coffee at Central Coffee Co.: Wake up with a strong cup of joe at this beloved local coffee shop, which also offers snacks and light meals.

Where to Stay in Plaza-Midwood: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Plaza-Midwood offers a range of accommodation options to suit any budget or preference. Here are a few top picks:

  • The VanLandingham Estate: This historic mansion and gardens operates as a bed and breakfast, and offers charming rooms and suites with antique furnishings and modern amenities.
  • The Plaza Hotel: This recently renovated boutique hotel is located in the heart of Plaza-Midwood, and offers comfortable and stylish rooms with a range of amenities.
  • The Airbnb Treehouse Experience: For a truly unique stay, consider booking this cozy treehouse, which offers a peaceful retreat in the heart of the city.

What to Buy in Plaza-Midwood: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Plaza-Midwood has plenty of shops and markets selling unique souvenirs and local products. Here are a few top picks:

  • The Common Market: This quirky neighborhood market sells a range of locally made goods, including craft beer, vintage clothing, and artisanal snacks.
  • The Charlotte Regional Farmers Market: Browse fresh produce, handmade crafts, and other local products at this popular farmers market, which is open year-round.
  • The Customshop Handmade: This boutique offers a range of handmade goods, including jewelry, ceramics, and leather products, all crafted by local artists.

Local Culture and Customs of Plaza-Midwood

Plaza-Midwood is known for its diverse and accepting community, with a rich history of activism and social progress. Visitors are encouraged to embrace the area’s unique culture and customs, including its emphasis on art, music, and good food.

Best Time to Visit Plaza-Midwood

Plaza-Midwood is a year-round destination, with mild temperatures and plenty of activities and events throughout the year. However, the best time to visit is typically in the spring or fall, when the weather is pleasant and outdoor activities are in full swing.

Safety and Health

Safety in Plaza-Midwood

Plaza-Midwood is generally a safe neighborhood, with a low crime rate and friendly community. However, visitors should still exercise common sense and take basic safety precautions, such as locking doors and keeping valuables out of sight.

Health in Plaza-Midwood

Plaza-Midwood offers plenty of opportunities for outdoor recreation and exercise, including hiking, biking, and walking. However, visitors should still take basic health precautions, such as wearing sunscreen and drinking plenty of water.

Getting to Plaza-Midwood: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Plaza-Midwood

The nearest airport to Plaza-Midwood is Charlotte Douglas International Airport, which is just a 20-minute drive from the neighborhood.

Bus Stations Near Plaza-Midwood

The Charlotte Area Transit System (CATS) operates several bus routes in and around Plaza-Midwood, with stops throughout the neighborhood.

Other Options

Plaza-Midwood is also easily accessible by car or bike, with several major highways and bike paths running through the area.

How to Get Around in Plaza-Midwood

Plaza-Midwood is a relatively small neighborhood, and it’s easy to get around on foot or by bike. There are also several public transportation options, including buses and the light rail system, which connects Plaza-Midwood to other parts of Charlotte.

Where to Go Next from Plaza-Midwood: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore more of North Carolina, here are a few nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • The U.S. National Whitewater Center: This outdoor adventure park offers rafting, kayaking, zip-lining, and other exciting activities, and is just a 20-minute drive from Plaza-Midwood.
  • The Blue Ridge Mountains: Take a scenic drive to the picturesque Blue Ridge Mountains, which offer hiking, camping, and stunning views just a few hours from Plaza-Midwood.
  • The North Carolina Zoo: This massive zoo is located in Asheboro, about 90 minutes from Plaza-Midwood, and features over 1800 animals from around the world.

Conclusion

Plaza-Midwood is a vibrant and welcoming neighborhood with something for everyone. From its beautiful parks and trails to its unique shops and restaurants, it’s the perfect destination for a weekend getaway or longer vacation.
