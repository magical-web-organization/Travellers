Introduction to Concord

Concord is a charming city located in Charlotte Metro, in Piedmont, North Carolina, in the South of the United States. With a history dating back to the 18th century, Concord boasts a unique mix of cultural and natural attractions, making it an ideal destination for travelers seeking a blend of adventure and relaxation. In this travel guide, we’ll explore the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay in Concord, along with tips on how to get around and make the most of your trip.

List of Places to Visit in Concord

Here is a list of the top places to visit in Concord:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Reed Gold Mine: Located at the site of the first documented gold find in the United States, this is a great place to learn about the history of gold mining.
  • Cabarrus Arts Council: Showcasing works of local artists, this art gallery offers a unique glimpse into the art scene of Concord.
  • Historic Downtown Concord: Stroll the streets of Historic Downtown Concord and immerse yourself in the city’s rich history.
  • Charlotte Motor Speedway: A must-visit for car enthusiasts, this monster speedway offers various events throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of Concord: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Frank Liske Park: A perfect spot for a family picnic, Frank Liske Park is also home to various sports facilities.
  • Rocky River Golf Club: A beautiful golf course that offers breath-taking views of the Rocky River.
  • Great Wolf Lodge Water Park: Perfect for families, this indoor water park is open year-round and features plenty of fun water attractions.
  • Dorton Park: Located in the heart of Concord, this public park is a great place to practice some outdoor activities like hiking and biking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Concord

Concord boasts some excellent hiking/biking routes, including:

  • McDowell Creek Greenway: This 2.8-mile trail offers scenic views of the McDowell Creek.
  • Rob Wallace Park Trail System: Offering over eight miles of trails, this park is perfect for hiking, biking, and horse riding.
  • Rocky River Trail: An easy 5.8-mile trail that follows the Rocky River, offering stunning views of the water and surrounding scenery.

Best Things to Do in Concord: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Concord:

  • Gordon Parks Exhibit: This exhibit showcases the works of renowned African-American photographer Gordon Parks.
  • Concord Mills Mall: A shopper’s paradise, this large shopping center offers a wide range of shops and restaurants for all tastes and budgets.
  • Whiskey Prison: Offering guided tours of the Old Cabarrus County Jail, Whiskey Prison is an excellent place to learn about the history of liquor production in the area.
  • Great Wolf Lodge Indoor Water Park: Perfect for families, this water park features numerous fun attractions like water slides, wave pools, and lazy rivers.

Week-Long Itinerary for Concord

Here’s a suggested 7-day itinerary for your trip to Concord:

Day Activities
Day 1 Visit Historic Downtown Concord and stroll the streets
Day 2 Take a guided tour of Whiskey Prison and explore the art scene of Cabarrus Arts Council
Day 3 Spend the day at Great Wolf Lodge Indoor Water Park
Day 4 Explore the Rocky River Trail and Dorton Park
Day 5 Visit the Charlotte Motor Speedway
Day 6 Play golf at the Rocky River Golf Club
Day 7 Go underground for a tour of Reed Gold Mine

Food and Drink Scene of Concord

Concord offers some tasty dishes and local beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and drinks:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Barbecue: A local favorite, Concord’s barbecue is a must-try.
  • Pimento Cheese: A traditional Southern dish, pimento cheese is a popular appetizer in Concord.
  • Fried Chicken: A classic Southern dish, you can find some of the best-fried chicken in Concord.

Local Beverages

  • Coca-Cola: As the home of the world-famous soft drink, you can’t leave Concord without trying a cold Coca-Cola.
  • Craft Beer: Concord has a growing craft beer scene with breweries like Cabarrus Brewing Company and Twenty-Six Acres Brewing Company.

Where to Stay in Concord: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Concord offers a wide range of accommodation options for all kinds of travelers. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • Embassy Suites by Hilton: A luxury hotel in the heart of Concord.
  • Country Inn & Suites: A great option for families, this hotel offers comfortable rooms and suites.
  • Home2 Suites by Hilton: Ideal for extended stays, this hotel offers spacious suites with kitchenettes.

What to Buy in Concord: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Concord offers some unique souvenirs and local products to take back home. Here are some of the best:

  • Gold jewelry: With a history of gold mining, Concord is an excellent place to buy unique gold jewelry pieces.
  • Local crafts: Local artists offer a range of crafts, including pottery, paintings, and wood carvings.
  • Cabarrus County Wine: With several vineyards in the area, Cabarrus County offers some excellent wines to try.

Local Culture and Customs of Concord

Concord has a rich history and a unique culture. The locals are friendly and welcoming to visitors, and you’ll likely encounter some Southern hospitality during your stay. It’s important to respect local customs, including dining etiquette and language usage.

Best Time to Visit Concord

The best time to visit Concord is during the fall months of September through November. The weather is mild, and the fall foliage adds an extra charm to the city’s already stunning scenery. Spring and summer are also great times to visit, but they can be hot and humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Concord

Do:

  • Do try the local barbecue and craft beer.
  • Do visit the Concord Mills Mall for some shopping and dining.
  • Do explore the city’s rich history and natural attractions.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in public parks or natural areas.
  • Don’t use offensive language or behavior.
  • Don’t be disrespectful of the city’s history and culture.

Safety and Health

Safety in Concord

Concord is a safe city, but like any city, it’s important to take precautions. Avoid walking alone at night and keep your valuables out of sight.

Health in Concord

In case of a medical emergency, you can call 911 or visit one of the city’s medical facilities, such as Atrium Health Cabarrus or Carolinas HealthCare System Urgent Care.

Getting to Concord: Transportation Options and Logistics

Concord is easily accessible via several transportation options:

Airports Near Concord

  • Charlotte Douglas International Airport (CLT): Located 26 miles south of Concord, CLT is the largest airport in the region.
  • Concord-Padgett Regional Airport (USA): Located just outside of the city, USA offers a convenient option for private planes.

Bus Stations Near Concord

  • Concord Kannapolis Area Transit (Rider): Offers local bus service throughout the city and surrounding areas.

Other Options

  • Driving: Concord is easily accessible via car, with several major highways running through the city.
  • Uber/Lyft: Both ride-sharing services operate in Concord.

How to Get Around in Concord

Concord is easily navigable via car or public transportation, and several rental car companies operate in the city. Local bus service is also available via Concord Kannapolis Area Transit (Rider). Biking and walking are also great options for getting around the city, especially in the downtown area.

Where to Go Next from Concord: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Concord is located in a beautiful part of North Carolina, and there are several nearby destinations and excursions to explore. Here are some of the best:

  • Charlotte: Just a short drive south of Concord, Charlotte is a bustling city with a rich history and diverse attractions.
  • Uwharrie National Forest: Located to the east of Concord, Uwharrie National Forest offers excellent hiking and camping opportunities.
  • Winston-Salem: Located about two hours northwest of Concord, Winston-Salem is a beautiful city known for its art, culture, and local cuisine.

Conclusion

Concord is a hidden gem in North Carolina, offering a unique blend of cultural and natural attractions. From exploring the city’s rich history and outdoor adventures to indulging in some of the best food and drink the South has to offer, there’s something for everyone in Concord. Plan your trip today and experience the charm of this beautiful city for yourself.
