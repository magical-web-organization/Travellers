Introduction to Sandhills

Sandhills is a region located in the Piedmont area of North Carolina, in the south of the United States of America. It is known for its beautiful landscapes, diverse wildlife, and outdoor activities. Whether you’re interested in hiking, biking, or sightseeing, Sandhills offers plenty of opportunities to experience everything North Carolina has to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Sandhills

Sandhills is a vast area with many noteworthy attractions. Here is a list of places you must visit while you’re here:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tufts Archives: a local history museum located in Pinehurst, which features artifacts and documents from the 19th and 20th centuries.
  • Malcolm Blue Farm and Museum: a living history museum that features a farmhouse, a barn, and other historic buildings that depict life in the 19th century.
  • Weymouth Center for the Arts and Humanities: a cultural center located in Southern Pines that offers art exhibits, concerts, and literary events throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of Sandhills: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Sandhills Horticultural Gardens: this garden in Pinehurst offers a botanical garden, nature trails, and a lake for fishing and boating.
  • Reservoir Park: a scenic park in Southern Pines that offers hiking trails, a lake, and picnic areas.
  • Raven Rock State Park: located along the Cape Fear River, this park offers hiking trails, birdwatching, and fishing opportunities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sandhills

Sandhills is an excellent destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. The following routes offer breathtaking views and an unforgettable experience:

  • The Pinehurst Greenway Trail: a four-mile trail that runs through the heart of Pinehurst and includes scenic views of Pinehurst Lake and the surrounding countryside.
  • The Rassie Wicker Park Trail: a three-mile trail that winds through the park and the neighboring residential areas.
  • Southern Pines Greenway Trail: this trail runs through Southern Pines and offers a scenic route for hikers and bikers.

Best Things to Do in Sandhills: Activities and Tours

  • Golfing at one of the many championship golf courses in and around Pinehurst.
  • Horseback riding at a local equestrian center.
  • Taking a guided walking or kayaking tour of the Sandhills area.

Week-Long Itinerary for Sandhills

A week-long itinerary in Sandhills can include:

Food and Drink Scene of Sandhills

Sandhills has a unique food and drink scene. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Barbecue: try the local pulled pork barbecue served with coleslaw and hushpuppies.
  • Sweet tea: the quintessential Southern drink, try it with a slice of lemon.
  • Country ham: a local delicacy that’s often served with biscuits and gravy.

Local Beverages

  • Craft beer: Sandhills has several craft breweries that offer unique beers made from local ingredients.
  • Carolina Sweet Tea Vodka: a locally-produced spirit that’s perfect for sipping on a hot day.

Where to Stay in Sandhills: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Sandhills has several options for accommodation, ranging from luxury hotels to cozy guesthouses. Here are some options to consider:

  • Pinehurst Resort: a luxury golf resort located in Pinehurst that offers a spa, dining options, and golf courses.
  • The Shaw House Bed and Breakfast: a charming bed and breakfast in Southern Pines that offers cozy rooms and a homemade breakfast.
  • TownePlace Suites by Marriott Southern Pines Aberdeen: a mid-range hotel located in Aberdeen that’s perfect for families or those on a budget.

What to Buy in Sandhills: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking to bring home a piece of Sandhills, here are some local products to consider:

  • Pottery: Sandhills is known for its pottery, and you can find unique pieces at local shops and galleries.
  • Sweet treats: try some locally-made fudge or candy.
  • Wine: Sandhills has several wineries that offer tours and tastings of their locally-produced wines.

Local Culture and Customs of Sandhills

Sandhills has a rich culture that’s deeply rooted in its Southern heritage. Locals are known for their hospitality and friendliness.

Best Time to Visit Sandhills

The best time to visit Sandhills is during the spring and fall, when the weather is mild and the foliage is beautiful. Summers can be hot and humid, while winters can be cold and rainy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sandhills

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your trip to Sandhills:

Do:

  • Plan your trip ahead of time, and book accommodations and activities in advance.
  • Take advantage of the many outdoor activities and scenic views that the area offers.
  • Get to know the friendly locals.

Don’t:

  • Underestimate the humidity and heat in the summer months.
  • Leave valuables in your car while exploring the area.
  • Forget to pack comfortable shoes for hiking and exploring the outdoors.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sandhills

Sandhills is a safe area to visit, but like any other place, visitors should take precautions to protect themselves and their belongings.

Health in Sandhills

The area has several hospitals and medical centers, so visitors can rest assured that they’ll receive quality medical care if needed.

Getting to Sandhills: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sandhills

The closest airports to Sandhills are Fayetteville Regional Airport, located about 30 miles away, and Raleigh-Durham International Airport, located about 75 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Sandhills

Greyhound and Amtrak offer bus and train service to the nearby city of Fayetteville.

Other Options

Renting a car or using a ride-sharing service can be a convenient way to get around Sandhills.

How to Get Around in Sandhills

A car is the most convenient way to get around Sandhills, but there are also several biking and walking trails available for those who want to explore the area on foot or bike.

Where to Go Next from Sandhills: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Fayetteville: a nearby city with a rich history, great shopping, and delicious food.
  • Raleigh: the state capital and a thriving cultural hub with great museums, restaurants, and nightlife.
  • Outer Banks: a beautiful coastal region that’s perfect for a beach vacation or weekend getaway.

Conclusion

Sandhills is a beautiful and diverse region that’s perfect for outdoor enthusiasts, history buffs, and anyone looking for a unique Southern experience. With its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and friendly locals, Sandhills is a destination that shouldn’t be missed.
