Introduction to Mount Airy

Nestled in the Piedmont Triad region of North Carolina, Mount Airy is a historic town known for its charming downtown, picturesque views, and deep connection to the country music legend, Andy Griffith. The town of Mount Airy is a mix of modern and traditional, with well-preserved colonial-era architecture, giving you a glimpse of the rich history of the area.

List of Places to Visit in Mount Airy

If you’re planning a trip to Mount Airy, consider adding the following attractions to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Mount Airy has a rich history, and several museums and historical places that showcase it, like:

  • Andy Griffith Museum: This museum showcases the life and career of the famous North Carolina native and actor, Andy Griffith.
  • Mount Airy Museum of Regional History: This museum highlights the history of the town, region, and the people who called it home.
  • Siamese Twins Exhibit: The museum exhibits the history of Eng and Chang Bunker, the famous conjoined twins who lived in Mount Airy.
  • Mayberry Squad Car Tours: Fans of the Andy Griffith Show will enjoy a trip in a replica of the iconic Mayberry squad car while touring the town’s attractions.

Natural Attractions of Mount Airy: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Mount Airy has a mixture of natural beauty and outdoor adventures that make it an ideal destination for nature lovers. Some of the top attractions include:

  • Pilot Mountain State Park: Hiking and rock climbing enthusiasts will love this state park. Whether you’re looking to hike to the peak of Pilot Mountain, or take a leisurely walk on the trails, you’ll find a trail that suits you.
  • Blue Ridge Parkway: This scenic drive takes in some of the best views of the Blue Ridge Parkway and is a popular destination for motorcyclists and road trippers.
  • Yadkin Valley Wine Region: This beautiful wine country, a perfect mix of rolling hills and lush grapevines, is home to some of the finest wineries in North Carolina.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mount Airy

Mount Airy has several hiking and biking trails that are suitable for beginners and experienced hikers alike. Here are some of the best trails to explore in Mount Airy:

  • The Ararat River Greenway: This 2.2-mile trail follows the Ararat River from Tharrington Elementary School to Riverside Park, offering walkers and cyclists beautiful scenic views.
  • The Granite City Greenway: This trail is a 2.7-mile walking and biking path that follows the Granite City Park in Mount Airy.
  • Pilot Mountain State Park: With over 25 miles of hiking and biking trails, Pilot Mountain State Park is a perfect destination for hikers and bikers seeking a challenging workout.

Best Things to Do in Mount Airy: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there is plenty to see and do in Mount Airy. Consider adding the following attractions to your list of things to do:

  • Floyd’s City Barber Shop: Step back in time and visit Floyd’s City Barber Shop, the inspiration for Floyd’s Barber Shop on the Andy Griffith Show.
  • Snappy Lunch: Sample the famous pork chop sandwich at Snappy Lunch, named as one of the best diners in the country by the Food Network.
  • Mount Airy Ghost Tours: Join a guided walking tour through the town’s historic sites, where local guides recount tales of ghosts, legends, and hauntings.
  • The Lantern Tour: This unique tour offers visitors the chance to experience the gorgeous town at night with a guide.

Week-Long Itinerary for Mount Airy

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Mount Airy, here’s a sample itinerary to help you make the most of your time:

Day Activities
Day 1 Visit the Andy Griffith Museum, followed by lunch at Snappy Lunch.
Day 2 Explore Pilot Mountain State Park and hike the Ledge Spring Trail.
Day 3 Visit the Siamese Twins Exhibit and take a guided tour of the town’s historic sites.
Day 4 Sample the local wines on the Yadkin Valley Wine Tour.
Day 5 Take a bike ride on the Ararat River Greenway, followed by a picnic lunch at Riverside Park.
Day 6 Join the Mount Airy Ghost Tour and experience a spooky night out.
Day 7 Visit Floyd’s City Barber Shop and explore the town’s quaint shops and boutiques.

Food and Drink Scene of Mount Airy

Mount Airy’s food scene is heavily influenced by Southern cuisine, and restaurants in the area offer a mix of classic dishes and contemporary fare. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pork Chop Sandwich at Snappy Lunch: This sandwich has been a favorite of locals and visitors alike.
  • Carolina-style Barbecue: Mount Airy is known for its North Carolina-style barbecue, where the meat is smoked and served with a tangy vinegar-based sauce.
  • Fried Chicken: Several restaurants in the area serve up classic fried chicken dishes.

Local Beverages

  • Wine: The Yadkin Valley Wine Region is a popular destination for wine lovers, with over 20 wineries in the area.
  • Moonshine: Several distilleries produce their moonshine in the area, and some are open for tastings.

Where to Stay in Mount Airy: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Whether you’re looking for a luxury hotel or a cozy bed and breakfast, Mount Airy has plenty of options to suit almost every budget. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • The Hampton Inn: Located in the heart of downtown, the Hampton Inn offers modern amenities and a central location, making it ideal for exploring the town.
  • Pilot Knob Inn: This Bed and Breakfast is located on a 100-acre farm that offers stunning views of the Pilot Mountain. The Inn has several cabins to choose from, each with its own privacy and charm.
  • The Mayberry Motor Inn: This unique hotel harkens back to the 1960s and the heyday of the Andy Griffith Show, with each room decorated in classic 60s style.

What to Buy in Mount Airy: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Whether you’re looking for souvenirs or local products to bring back home, Mount Airy has plenty of options. Here are some gifts to consider:

  • Mount Airy T-Shirts: Several shops in the area sell Mount Airy-themed t-shirts that make excellent souvenirs.
  • Local Wines: The Yadkin Valley Wine Region has several wineries that sell wine by the bottle.
  • Local Crafts: Several shops sell handmade crafts and pottery made by local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Mount Airy

Mount Airy is known for its friendly Southern hospitality, and visitors are welcomed with open arms. When visiting the town, it’s essential to take notice of the locals’ customs and ways of life. The town’s residents are known for being polite and helpful, so don’t hesitate to ask for directions or assistance.

Best Time to Visit Mount Airy

The best time to visit Mount Airy is during the fall months (September to November). The weather is mild, and the leaves’ changing colors create an autumnal ambiance that is ideal for exploring the town’s outdoor attractions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mount Airy

When visiting Mount Airy, keep in mind the following do’s and don’ts:

Do:

  • Explore the town’s historic sites to get a glimpse into its rich history.
  • Sample the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Take a guided ghost tour of the town for a spooky night out.

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave trash in public places.
  • Be disrespectful to the town’s residents or other visitors.
  • Ignore the town’s customs and way of life.

Safety and Health

When traveling to Mount Airy, you should be aware of the following tips for staying safe and healthy:

Safety in Mount Airy

  • Lock your vehicle when you park it.
  • Be aware of your surroundings when walking alone at night.
  • Follow all safety guidelines when hiking and biking.

Health in Mount Airy

  • Stay hydrated during outdoor activities, especially during the summer months.
  • Use sunscreen to protect your skin from the sun’s rays.
  • Wear hiking shoes or appropriate footwear when exploring the hiking trails.

Getting to Mount Airy: Transportation Options and Logistics

Mount Airy is accessible by several means of transportation, including:

Airports Near Mount Airy

  • Piedmont Triad International Airport: This airport is located approximately 50 miles from Mount Airy.

Bus Stations Near Mount Airy

  • Mount Airy Transportation Center: The transportation center offers bus service to several destinations in North Carolina.

Other Options

  • Car Rentals: You can rent a car from several rental agencies at the airport.

How to Get Around in Mount Airy

The best way to get around Mount Airy is by car, although walking and biking are alternatives for exploring downtown. Use the following transportation options to get around:

  • Car rentals: You can rent a car from several rental agencies at the airport.
  • Bike rentals: Mount Airy has several bike rental shops throughout the town.
