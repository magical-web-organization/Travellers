Introduction to West Valley City

West Valley City is a beautiful city located in the Wasatch Range, Utah, Southwest, United States of America. The city offers scenic natural views, outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, and a lively food and drink scene. Whether you are an avid hiker, a history buff, a foodie, or a culture enthusiast, West Valley City has something for everyone. In this comprehensive tour guide, we have curated a list of the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, what to buy, and local customs to make your trip to West Valley City unforgettable.

List of Places to Visit in West Valley City

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Utah Cultural Celebration Center: This center hosts various cultural events and exhibitions throughout the year and has an art gallery and theaters.

  2. Wheeler Historic Farm: This is a historic farm showcasing the rural way of life and traditional agricultural practices.

  3. Fort Douglas Military Museum: This museum tells the story of the American soldiers and their role in the history of Utah.

Natural Attractions of West Valley City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Jordan River Parkway Trail: This picturesque trail runs along the Jordan River and is ideal for hiking, biking, and picnicking.

  2. Red Butte Garden and Arboretum: The garden is home to a wide variety of plants and flowers, and has scenic views of the surrounding mountains.

  3. Big Cottonwood Canyon: This is a must-visit for outdoor enthusiasts and offers hiking, mountain biking, and skiing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of West Valley City

  1. Oquirrh Lake Trail: This trail offers scenic views of the lake and is a popular spot for birdwatching.

  2. Grizzly Ridge Trail: This trail is ideal for advanced mountain bikers and offers a challenging ride with beautiful scenic views.

  3. Yellow Fork Canyon Trail: This trail is perfect for hiking and offers stunning views of the canyon and surrounding mountains.

Best Things to Do in West Valley City: Activities and Tours

  1. Wairhouse Trampoline Park: This indoor park is a great place for families and offers a variety of trampolines, foam pits, and ninja courses.

  2. The Escape Key: This escape room offers a challenging and fun interactive experience for groups.

  3. The Drive-In: This outdoor movie theater offers a unique and nostalgic experience and is a perfect date spot.

Food and Drink Scene of West Valley City

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Fry Sauce: This is a popular condiment in Utah and is a delicious combination of mayo and ketchup.

  2. Navajo Tacos: This is a traditional dish that consists of fry bread with toppings such as beef, beans, cheese, and lettuce.

  3. Jell-O: This is Utah’s official state dessert and is a sweet and refreshing treat.

Local Beverages

  1. Utah Craft Beer: Utah has a thriving craft beer scene, with popular breweries such as Uinta Brewing and Squatters Craft Beers.

  2. Snowbird Bloody Mary: This spicy cocktail is made with vodka, tomato juice, Worcestershire sauce, and hot sauce.

  3. Caffeine Fix: If you’re a coffee lover, check out local coffee shops such as Jolt Coffee and Tea Co. and Publik Coffee Roasters for a caffeine fix.

Where to Stay in West Valley City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Comfort Inn & Suites West Valley: This hotel offers comfortable rooms and a complimentary breakfast.

  2. Staybridge Suites Salt Lake-West Valley City: This hotel offers spacious suites with a full kitchen and is close to various attractions.

  3. TownePlace Suites Salt Lake City-West Valley: This hotel offers apartment-style rooms with a full kitchen and is perfect for a long stay.

What to Buy in West Valley City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Honey from local farms: Utah is known for its delicious honey, and local farms such as Cox Honeyland and Clifford Family Farms offer a variety of honey products.

  2. Native American Crafts: West Valley City is near various Native American reservations, and local shops such as The Indian Store and Gypsy Moon Emporium offer beautiful crafts made by Native American artisans.

  3. Utah Chocolate: Utah has a thriving chocolate scene, with popular chocolatiers such as Hatch Family Chocolates and The Chocolate Conspiracy.

Local Culture and Customs of West Valley City

  1. Mormon Culture: Utah has a large Mormon population, and visitors should respect the local customs and traditions.

  2. Winter Sports: Utah is known for its winter sports, and visitors should be prepared for the cold and snowy weather.

  3. Nature appreciation: Utah has stunning natural landscapes, and visitors are encouraged to respect the environment and practice Leave No Trace principles.

Best Time to Visit West Valley City

The best time to visit West Valley City is from May to October, as this is when the weather is mild and outdoor activities are in full swing. However, winter sports enthusiasts may want to visit during the winter months, when skiing and snowboarding are popular activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of West Valley City

Do:

  1. Respect the local customs and traditions.

  2. Appreciate the natural beauty of the area and practice Leave No Trace principles.

  3. Visit local attractions and support local businesses.

Don’t:

  1. Litter or damage the local environment.

  2. Disrespect the local culture or religion.

  3. Engage in unsafe or illegal activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in West Valley City

West Valley City is a safe city, but visitors should take common sense precautions such as locking doors and keeping valuables out of sight.

Health in West Valley City

Visitors should be aware of the high altitude and stay hydrated to avoid altitude sickness. Also, visitors should be prepared for the weather and dress accordingly to avoid hypothermia.

How to Get Around in West Valley City

The best way to get around West Valley City is by car, as public transportation is limited. Visitors can also use ride-sharing services such as Uber and Lyft.

Where to Go Next from West Valley City: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Salt Lake City: This vibrant city is known for its history, culture, and outdoor activities.

  2. Park City: This historic mining town is now a popular ski destination with numerous attractions such as the Sundance Film Festival and the Kimball Arts Festival.

Conclusion

West Valley City is a beautiful and diverse destination offering cultural attractions, outdoor adventures, and a unique food and drink scene. This comprehensive tour guide is designed to help you plan your trip and make the most of your visit to West Valley City.
