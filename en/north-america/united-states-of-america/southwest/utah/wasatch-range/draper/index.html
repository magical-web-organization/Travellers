Introduction to Draper

Draper is a beautiful city located in the Wasatch Range in Utah, United States. It is a perfect place for outdoor enthusiasts due to its scenic views and outdoor adventures. Draper is a great destination for those who want to take a break from their busy life and immerse themselves in nature.

List of Places to Visit in Draper

Whether you are a history buff or a lover of the outdoors, Draper has something for everyone. Here is a list of top attractions in Draper:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Draper Historical Society and Museum: Learn about the history of Draper and its inhabitants through exhibits, artifacts, and interactive displays.
  • South Mountain Golf Club: This beautiful golf club offers stunning views of the mountains, as well as a challenging 18-hole course.
  • The Loveland Living Planet Aquarium: This aquarium is home to over 4,500 animals and 550 species.

Natural Attractions of Draper: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Corner Canyon Trail System: Besides hiking, you can also enjoy mountain biking and horseback riding on the trails.
  • Timpanogos Cave National Monument: This cave system is filled with stalactites, stalagmites, and a breathtaking view of Wasatch Range.
  • Hidden Falls: This hiking trail offers beautiful views of the forest, and a scenic waterfall that is worth the trip.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Draper

Draper is an outdoor lover’s paradise, with miles of hiking and biking trails. Here are the best routes to explore:

  • Corner Canyon Trail System: This trail system has over 90 miles of trails for hikers, mountain bikers, and equestrians to choose from.
  • Hog Hollow: This 4.6-mile trail is a favorite among hikers and mountain bikers due to its beautiful views of the valley.
  • Draper Canal Trail: This 5.2-mile trail runs along the canal and provides hikers with a unique experience.

Best Things to Do in Draper: Activities and Tours

  • Rock Climbing: The Lone Peak Cirque offers some of the best rock climbing in Utah.
  • Paragliding: Draper is famous for paragliding due to its beautiful landscape with views of the lake.
  • ATV Tour: Take an ATV tour to explore the beautiful terrain of Draper.

Food and Drink Scene of Draper

Draper offers a variety of dining options, ranging from casual eateries to fine dining options. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fry Sauce: A condiment that is unique to Utah and a perfect pairing for fries.
  • Jumbaco: A combination of a taco and a burrito, this dish is a perfect option for those who can’t choose between the two.
  • Funeral Potatoes: This cheesy potato dish is a staple at church functions and potlucks in Utah.

Local Beverages

  • Apple Beer: This non-alcoholic soda has a crisp apple flavor and is perfect for those who don’t drink.
  • Squatters Beer: This local brewery offers a selection of craft beers for those who want to sample the local brews.
  • High West Whiskey: This local distillery offers a selection of whiskey drinks and cocktails.

Where to Stay in Draper: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Draper has a variety of accommodation options for travelers, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. Here are some recommendations:

  • Hyatt House Salt Lake City/Sandy: This hotel is located near the mountains and offers stunning views, comfortable rooms, and excellent amenities.
  • The Hilton Garden Inn: This hotel is located near downtown Draper and offers comfortable rooms and excellent amenities at an affordable price.

What to Buy in Draper: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Draper offers a variety of souvenir shops where travelers can find unique gifts to bring home. Here are some recommendations:

  • Cottage Retreat: This shop offers handcrafted home decor and unique gifts that are perfect for those who want to bring home something special from their trip.
  • Draper Farmers Market: This market offers a selection of locally made products, including honey, homemade jams, and fresh produce.

Local Culture and Customs of Draper

Utah is known for its conservative culture and adherence to religious traditions. Visitors should be aware of these traditions and show respect while they are in Draper.

Best Time to Visit Draper

The best time to visit Draper is from May to October when the weather is perfect for outdoor activities such as hiking and biking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Draper

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Dress appropriately while visiting religious sites and attending events.

Don’t:

  • Be loud and disruptive in public places.
  • Engage in behavior that is disrespectful to the local community.

Safety and Health

Safety in Draper

Draper is generally a safe city, but travelers should take the same precautions that they would take while visiting any other city, such as being aware of their surroundings and avoiding unfamiliar areas at night.

Health in Draper

Travelers should ensure that they have all required vaccinations before traveling to Utah. They should also bring ample supplies of sunscreen and water due to the high altitude and strong sun.

Getting to Draper: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Draper

  • Salt Lake City International Airport: This airport is the closest airport to Draper, with a 20-minute drive.

Bus Stations Near Draper

  • Greyhound: This bus station is located in Salt Lake City and offers service to various destinations in Utah and other states.

Other Options

  • Car rental: Renting a car can be a convenient option for travelers who want to explore the area on their own.

How to Get Around in Draper

Travelers can use public transportation, ride-sharing services, or rent a car to get around in Draper. The best way to explore the area is by taking a scenic drive on the nearby mountain roads.

Where to Go Next from Draper: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Salt Lake City: This vibrant city is known for its art and culture, and is a great place to explore museums, art galleries, and restaurants.
  • Park City: This ski town is located nearby and offers plenty of outdoor adventure activities such as snowboarding, skiing, and snowshoeing.

Conclusion

Draper is a destination that offers something for everyone. Whether you are an outdoor enthusiast or a lover of history and culture, there is plenty to see and do in this beautiful city. Use this comprehensive guide to plan your trip to Draper and make the most of your time here.
