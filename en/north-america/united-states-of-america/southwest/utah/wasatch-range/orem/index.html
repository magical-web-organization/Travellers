Introduction to Orem

Nestled in the heart of Utah Valley, Orem offers visitors stunning mountain views, unique cultural attractions, and endless outdoor adventures. Known for its welcoming community and family-friendly atmosphere, Orem is the perfect destination for those looking to experience the best of Utah.

List of Places to Visit in Orem

Here are some of the top attractions to add to your itinerary when visiting Orem:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The John Hutchings Museum of Natural History: This museum features a large collection of fossils, minerals, and taxidermy animals from around the world. Visitors can also participate in educational tours and classes.

  • Timpanogos Cave National Monument: Just a short drive from Orem, this natural wonder offers guided tours through stunning limestone caves filled with geological formations and unique wildlife.

  • SCERA Center for the Arts: This performing arts center features live music, theater productions, and art exhibits throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of Orem: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Timpanogos: Hike to the top of this majestic mountain for breathtaking views of Utah Valley and the surrounding Wasatch Range.

  • Cascade Springs: This natural spring features a series of cascading waterfalls surrounded by lush greenery, making it the perfect spot for a picnic or nature walk.

  • Bridal Veil Falls: This stunning waterfall is accessible by foot or car and offers a great photo opportunity for visitors.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Orem

Orem is surrounded by spectacular hiking and biking trails, including:

  • Provo River Parkway Trail: This trail follows the meandering Provo River for 15 miles, offering breathtaking views of the valley and passing through several parks and nature preserves.

  • Squaw Peak Trail: This challenging hike climbs through scenic forests and rocky terrain, offering stunning views of Utah Valley and the surrounding mountains.

  • Timpanogos Cave National Monument Trails: The Cave Trail and the Battle Creek Trail both offer unique views of the national monument and are great for hikers of all levels.

Best Things to Do in Orem: Activities and Tours

From family-friendly activities to extreme sports, there’s something for everyone in Orem:

  • Seven Peaks Water Park: Beat the heat with thrilling water slides and lazy rivers at this popular water park.

  • Thanksgiving Point Farm Country: This family-friendly attraction features interactive exhibits, a petting zoo, and horse-drawn wagon rides.

  • Skydive the Wasatch: Experience Utah from a new perspective with a tandem skydiving adventure over the scenic Wasatch Mountains.

Week-Long Itinerary for Orem

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Orem, here’s a sample itinerary to help you make the most of your visit:

Day 1 Timpanogos Cave National Monument & John Hutchings Museum of Natural History
Day 2 Mount Timpanogos Hike & Cascade Springs
Day 3 Provo River Parkway Trail & Bridal Veil Falls
Day 4 Thanksgiving Point & Skydive the Wasatch
Day 5 SCERA Arts Center & Squaw Peak Trail
Day 6 Seven Peaks Water Park & Brigham Young University
Day 7 Relaxing Day in Town

Food and Drink Scene of Orem

No visit to Orem is complete without trying some of the local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes:

  • Fry Sauce: This unique sauce, made with ketchup and mayonnaise, is a Utah staple and pairs perfectly with fries and burgers.

  • Funeral Potatoes: Another Utah favorite, this cheesy casserole is a must-try for comfort food enthusiasts.

  • Navajo Tacos: Made with fry bread and topped with chili, cheese, lettuce, and tomatoes, Navajo Tacos are a delicious and filling meal.

Local Beverages:

  • Caffe Ibis Coffee Roasters: This local coffee roaster offers a variety of ethically sourced, organic coffee blends.

  • Wasatch Brewery: Sample locally brewed beers at this popular brewery in nearby Park City.

Where to Stay in Orem: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options in Orem, including:

  • Comfort Inn & Suites Orem: This budget-friendly hotel features an indoor pool, hot tub, and complimentary breakfast.

  • La Quinta Inn & Suites Orem University Parkway: Located near Brigham Young University, this hotel offers free Wi-Fi and a fitness center.

  • Hines Mansion Bed and Breakfast: For those seeking a more luxurious experience, this historic B&B features elegant rooms and beautiful gardens.

What to Buy in Orem: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are a few unique items to look for during your visit to Orem:

  • Chocolate Covered Cinnamon Bears: These sweet and spicy candies are a popular local treat.

  • Utah Honey: Sample locally sourced honey at the Orem Farmers Market.

  • Wildflower Seeds: Bring a piece of Utah home with you by purchasing seeds from native wildflowers.

Local Culture and Customs of Orem

Orem is known for its friendly, family-oriented community. Be sure to respect local customs, which may include traditional dress and religious observances.

Best Time to Visit Orem

Utah experiences four distinct seasons, making the best time to visit Orem depend on your interests and preferences. The summer months offer the most temperate weather for outdoor activities, while winter brings opportunities for skiing and snowboarding in nearby mountain resorts.

Do’s and Don’ts of Orem

Do:

  • Do be respectful of local customs and traditions.

  • Do take advantage of the outdoor recreation opportunities in the area.

  • Do try local cuisine and products.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage natural landmarks.

  • Don’t disrespect cultural or religious practices.

  • Don’t underestimate the altitude and potential for altitude sickness in the mountains.

Safety and Health

Safety in Orem

Orem is a safe community, but travelers should still take precautions to protect themselves and their belongings.

Health in Orem

Be aware of the potential for altitude sickness when traveling to high elevations such as on Mount Timpanogos. Stay hydrated and take appropriate precautions.

Getting to Orem: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Orem

Salt Lake City International Airport is just over an hour’s drive from Orem.

Bus Stations Near Orem

The Provo Amtrak Train Station and Greyhound Bus Station are both located in nearby Provo, just a 10-minute drive from Orem.

Other Options

Many visitors also choose to rent a car or use ride-sharing services like Uber or Lyft to get around Orem and the surrounding area.

How to Get Around in Orem

Orem is easily navigable by car, but visitors may also want to explore the area by bike or foot using the many trails and paths in the area.

Where to Go Next from Orem: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Orem’s central location makes it the perfect jumping-off point for nearby destinations such as:

  • Park City: This charming mountain town is just an hour’s drive from Orem and offers world-class skiing, shopping, and dining.

  • Salt Lake City: Utah’s capital city is just over an hour’s drive from Orem and features cultural attractions such as the Utah State Capitol and Temple Square.

  • Bryce Canyon National Park: Located just a few hours’ drive from Orem, Bryce Canyon offers visitors stunning geological formations and unique wildlife.

Conclusion

With its spectacular natural beauty, unique cultural attractions, and friendly community, Orem is a must-visit destination in Utah. Plan your trip today and discover all that Orem has to offer.
