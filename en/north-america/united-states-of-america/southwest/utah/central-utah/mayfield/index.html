Mayfield is a small town located in the central part of Utah in the Southwest region of the United States. Introduction to Mayfield

Mayfield is located in Sanpete County, nestled in the mountains of central Utah. Surrounded by beautiful landscapes, Mayfield is an outdoor enthusiast’s paradise. From hiking and biking to fishing and hunting, there is something for everyone to enjoy. The town’s rich history and local traditions make it a unique destination.
