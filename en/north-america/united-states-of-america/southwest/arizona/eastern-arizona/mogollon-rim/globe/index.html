Introduction to Globe

Nestled within the scenic Mogollon Rim in Eastern Arizona, the town of Globe is a hidden gem filled with rich cultural history, outdoor adventures, and breathtaking scenery. Founded in the late 19th century as a mining town, Globe has since evolved into a charming community perfect for visitors looking to explore the beauty of the Southwest.

List of Places to Visit in Globe

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Besh Ba Gowah Archaeological Park and Museum

Step back in time at the Besh Ba Gowah Archaeological Park and Museum, home to the remains of a 700-year-old Salado Indian pueblo. Explore the museum’s exhibits featuring artifacts and interactive displays to discover the history of the Salado people and the town of Globe.

Cobre Valley Center for the Arts

Experience the arts and culture of Globe at the Cobre Valley Center for the Arts, featuring monthly exhibits and events showcasing local and regional artists. The center also offers learning opportunities for all ages, from pottery classes to theater workshops.

Old Gila County Jail

Visit the Old Gila County Jail to see firsthand the history of the mining town’s law enforcement. Take a guided tour to learn about the jail’s infamous prisoners, including the legendary thief and murderer Pearl Hart.

Natural Attractions of Globe: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Tonto National Forest

Explore the vast beauty of Tonto National Forest, spanning over three million acres. Hike along scenic trails, take a refreshing swim in crystal-clear lakes, and discover ancient cliff dwellings.

The Apache Trail

Take a scenic drive on the historic Apache Trail, winding through the Superstition Mountains and along the Salt River. Stop at Fish Creek Hill for panoramic views of the stunning landscape.

Pinal Mountains

Escape the heat and explore the cooler climate of the Pinal Mountains. Hike through the pristine wilderness to discover stunning waterfalls and unique rock formations.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Globe

Icehouse Canyon Trail

This five-mile hike offers stunning views of the Mogollon Rim and takes hikers through a peaceful canyon. The trail is rated as moderate, making it suitable for those looking for a challenging but enjoyable hike.

Bell Trail

Discover the beautiful landscape of the Red Rock area on the eight-mile Bell Trail. Hikers will trek through lush forest areas, along creeks and rivers, and past stunning rock formations.

Best Things to Do in Globe: Activities and Tours

Visit the World’s Smallest Museum

Stop by the Guinness Book of World Records’ smallest museum, featuring a collection of vintage carpentry tools, dental tools, and other quirky items.

Ride the Historic Apache Trail Tours

Hop on board the historic Apache Trail Tours to explore the scenic drive through the Superstition Mountains. Learn about the history of the Apache Trail and discover the stunning views along the way.

Week-Long Itinerary for Globe

Day 1: Explore the Besh Ba Gowah Archaeological Park and Museum

Day 2: Hike the stunning Icehouse Canyon Trail

Day 3: Take a scenic drive on the Apache Trail

Day 4: Discover the arts and culture of Globe at the Cobre Valley Center for the Arts

Day 5: Escape the heat for a day and hike through the Pinal Mountains

Day 6: Visit the Old Gila County Jail for a unique historical experience

Day 7: Enjoy the World’s Smallest Museum and ride the Historic Apache Trail Tours

Food and Drink Scene of Globe

Must-Try Dishes

Mexican Food

Savor the authentic flavors of Mexico at one of Globe’s many Mexican restaurants, serving up mouth-watering dishes such as tacos al pastor and chile rellenos.

Barbecue

Sink your teeth into delicious barbecue at one of the town’s many grill and smoke houses.

Local Beverages

Copper City Bourbon

Enjoy a glass of locally crafted Copper City Bourbon, distilled by the Desert Diamond Distillery in Kingman.

Where to Stay in Globe: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Historic Western Motel

Stay in the heart of Globe’s historic downtown at the Western Motel, offering comfortable rooms and easy access to nearby attractions.

Dream Manor Inn

Enjoy stunning views of the mountains from the Dream Manor Inn, featuring luxurious rooms and suites, a pool and spa, and on-site dining.

What to Buy in Globe: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Copper Arts and Crafts

Take home a piece of Globe’s rich mining history with locally crafted copper arts and crafts.

Native American Art

Discover stunning Native American art, pottery, and jewelry at one of the town’s many art galleries.

Local Culture and Customs of Globe

Globe is a vibrant community with a rich cultural history, shaped by the town’s mining industry and diverse population. Visitors can embrace the local customs and traditions by attending local festivals and events, such as the annual Apache Jii Festival and the Historic Home and Building Tour.

Best Time to Visit Globe

The best time to visit Globe is during the spring and fall, when the temperatures are mild and the landscape is at its most beautiful. Summer can be extremely hot and winter can bring snow, limiting outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Globe

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Practice good hiking etiquette, such as staying on marked trails.
  • Take advantage of the outdoor adventures and scenic views in the area.

Don’t:

  • Leave trash or belongings behind on hiking trails.
  • Damage the landscape or natural resources.
  • Disrespect the historical significance of the town’s monuments and landmarks.

Safety and Health

Safety in Globe

While Globe is a generally safe community, visitors should take precautions when hiking or exploring the natural areas. Be aware of wildlife and carry plenty of water and sunscreen.

Health in Globe

Visitors should be aware of the high altitude and dry climate of the region, as well as the heat during the summer months. Carry plenty of water and be aware of signs of dehydration or heat exhaustion.

Getting to Globe: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Globe

The closest major airport to Globe is Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport, located approximately 90 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Globe

Greyhound bus lines offer service to Globe from major cities throughout Arizona.

Other Options

Renting a car is an easy way to travel to and around Globe, with several rental options available at Phoenix Sky Harbor International Airport.

How to Get Around in Globe

While a car is the easiest way to get around Globe and the surrounding areas, bike rentals and public transportation are also available in town.

Where to Go Next from Globe: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Sedona

Take a scenic three-hour drive to Sedona, famous for its stunning red rock formations and New Age culture.

Tucson

Head south for a two-hour drive to Tucson, home to fascinating museums and exciting outdoor adventures, such as exploring the stunning Saguaro National Park.

Conclusion

Globe may be a small town, but visitors will find an abundance of history, culture, and natural beauty to explore. From hiking scenic trails to discovering the town’s rich mining history, Globe is a destination perfect for those seeking an authentic Southwest experience.
