Introduction to Chloride

Chloride is a historic mining town located in Northern Arizona, surrounded by breathtaking natural scenery. The town was discovered in 1863, and it became a significant mining center in the late 1880s. Today, Chloride is an ideal vacation destination for outdoor enthusiasts looking to explore the natural beauty of the Southwest.

Week-Long Itinerary for Chloride

If you plan on spending a week in Chloride, consider this itinerary:

Day 1: Arrive in Chloride

Get settled into your hotel or vacation rental and explore the town center before heading to dinner at one of Chloride’s top restaurants.

Day 2: Chloride Historical District

Spend the day taking a step back in time and exploring the Chloride Historical District, including the Roy Purcell Murals, Chloride Cemetery, and the Dolan Spring.

Day 3: Outdoor Adventures

Spend the day exploring the natural beauty of Chloride. Hike or bike one of the trails in the Cerbat Mountains or the Cerbat Foothills Recreation Area before taking a scenic drive to the Hoover Dam or the Kingman Army Airfield.

Day 4: ATV Tours

Book an ATV tour and explore the surrounding desert terrain. Experience the thrill of off-road riding and see some of the most spectacular views of the area.

Day 5: Horseback Riding

Saddle up and take a horseback ride through the foothills of the Cerbat Mountains. You’ll see breathtaking views of the Sonoran Desert and the surrounding mountains.

Day 6: Jeep Tours

Take an off-road Jeep tour of the rugged hills and valleys surrounding Chloride. Experience the thrill of climbing hills and valleys, and take in the stunning views of the surrounding area.

Day 7: Depart from Chloride

Have a final breakfast in town before heading back home or continuing on further adventures.

Local Culture and Customs of Chloride

Chloride is a town that values both its history and local traditions. The residents of Chloride places a significant emphasis on community involvement through the many events they host year-round. Be sure to observe and embrace the town’s unique culture.

Best Time to Visit Chloride

The best time to visit Chloride is between May and October, when the weather is mild and allows for plenty of outdoor activities. The weather can be quite cold between November and April, so make sure to bring warm clothing if you visit during this time.

  • Take part in the town’s community events

Don’t:

Safety and Health

Safety in Chloride

Chloride is a very safe place to visit. Make sure to take the standard precautions while hiking or biking in the wilderness. It is always advisable to carry enough water and a first aid kit.

Health in Chloride

Chloride is hot and arid, so it is important to stay hydrated if you plan to spend time outdoors. Carry sunscreen and a hat to avoid heat stroke or other heat-related illness.

Getting to Chloride: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Chloride

The closest international airport to Chloride is the McCarran International Airport in Las Vegas, 100 miles away from Chloride.

Bus Stations Near Chloride

The Greyhound bus services Kingman, about 25 miles away from Chloride.

Other Options

You can travel via a shuttle or taxi on the select routes too.

How to Get Around in Chloride
