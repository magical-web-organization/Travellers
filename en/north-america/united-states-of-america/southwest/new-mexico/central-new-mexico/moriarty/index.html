Introduction to Moriarty

Nestled in the heart of Central New Mexico, Moriarty is a charming small town that offers a perfect blend of nature, history, and adventure. It is located at an altitude of 6,197 feet with a population of just over 2,200. Moriarty is a great destination for those seeking a peaceful getaway in the midst of scenic surroundings.

List of Places to Visit in Moriarty

Here are some of the must-visit attractions in Moriarty:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Moriarty Historical Society and Museum: Dive into Moriarty’s rich history by visiting the Moriarty Historical Society and Museum where you can explore the town’s past through a unique collection of artifacts and exhibits.

  • Whitfield Wild West Museum: This museum offers an exciting glimpse into the Wild West era with exhibits featuring guns, outlaws, and lawmen.

Natural Attractions of Moriarty: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Moriarty Hill: Perfect for hiking or taking a leisurely stroll, Moriarty Hill offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

  • Salinas Pueblo Missions National Monument: Located just a short drive from Moriarty, this national monument is home to three well-preserved ancient ruins that have survived for over 1,000 years.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Moriarty

  • Moriarty Hill Trail: A moderate hike through rugged terrain, revealing stunning views at the summit of Moriarty Hill.

  • Estancia Valley Trail: Suitable for bikers and hikers of all expertise, this trail takes you through lush vegetation and desert landscape.

Best Things to Do in Moriarty: Activities and Tours

  • Hot air balloon rides: Enjoy the bird’s eye view of Moriarty by taking a hot air balloon ride at dawn or dusk.

  • Horseback riding: Book a guided horseback ride to explore the beauty of the New Mexico countryside.

Food and Drink Scene of Moriarty

Moriarty offers a variety of dining options ranging from classic American fare to traditional New Mexican cuisine.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Green Chile Cheeseburger: A classic dish of New Mexico, the Green Chile Cheeseburger is a must-try when in Moriarty.

  • Sopapillas: A famous dessert in the Southwest, Sopapillas are light, puffed pastries that are best served with honey.

Local Beverages

  • Sangria Wine: Made with locally-sourced fruits and wine, this fruity drink is a perfect refreshment on a hot summer day.

  • Craft Beer: Moriarty is home to several local breweries, each offering their unique take on craft beer.

Where to Stay in Moriarty: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Moriarty offers a range of accommodation options catering to all budgets and preferences. Here are some recommendations:

  • American Inn: A budget-friendly option with comfortable rooms and a convenient location in the heart of the town.

  • Sunset Motel: A cozy, family-run motel with pet-friendly rooms and a peaceful location outside the town center.

  • Route 66 Hostel: For an authentic hostel experience, book a bed at Route 66 Hostel, where you can meet other travelers and enjoy the quirky decor.

What to Buy in Moriarty: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Chile Ristras: Beautifully woven strands of dried chile peppers, popular decorations for Southwestern homes.

  • Pottery and Handcrafted Goods: Handmade pottery and artistic desert-inspired goods such as woven baskets and blankets make excellent souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Moriarty

Moriarty has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its traditions, art, and cuisine, and festivals. The town is known for its annual Pinto Bean Fiesta, a celebration of the humble pinto bean.

Best Time to Visit Moriarty

Moriarty is a year-round destination with something to offer in every season. However, the best time to visit is during the fall (September to November) when the weather is pleasant, and the fall foliage is at its peak.

Do’s and Don’ts of Moriarty

Do:

  • Take a hot air balloon ride at sunrise or sunset.

  • Try the local New Mexican cuisine, especially the Green Chile Cheeseburger

  • Visit the Moriarty Historical Society and Museum to learn about the town’s rich history.

Don’t:

  • Ignore safety regulations when participating in outdoor activities

  • Leave trash on the hiking trails

Safety and Health

Safety in Moriarty

Moriarty is a safe town for visitors. However, it is recommended to take standard safety precautions such as locking cars and keeping valuables in a safe place.

Health in Moriarty

The town has several medical facilities, including Moriarty Urgent Care and Estancia Valley Medical Center, which offer medical assistance in case of emergency.

Getting to Moriarty: Transportation Options and Logistics

Moriarty is easily accessible via car or public transportation.

Airports Near Moriarty

  • Albuquerque International Sunport: Located approximately 44 miles south of Moriarty, this is the closest international airport.

  • Santa Fe Regional Airport: Located approximately 67 miles north of Moriarty, this is the second-closest airport.

Bus Stations Near Moriarty

  • Greyhound Bus Station: Located approximately 35 miles south of Moriarty.

  • Limon Coach Stop: Located approximately 0.8 miles south of the town center.

Other Options

  • Rental Cars: Several car rental agencies operate in Albuquerque and Santa Fe.

  • Taxis and Ride-Sharing Services: Taxis and ride-sharing services such as Uber and Lyft are available in Albuquerque.

How to Get Around in Moriarty

Moriarty is a small town that can easily be explored on foot or by car. Visitors can rent cars from several agencies such as Enterprise and Hertz.

Where to Go Next from Moriarty: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Albuquerque: Located approximately 40 miles south of Moriarty, Albuquerque is a vibrant city known for its rich history, delicious cuisine, and cultural heritage.

  • Santa Fe: Located approximately 67 miles north of Moriarty, Santa Fe is a world-renowned destination for art, culture, and authentic New Mexican cuisine.

Conclusion

Moriarty, New Mexico, does not disappoint visitors looking for a relaxing getaway with plenty of adventure and scenic vistas. With this comprehensive guide, you can plan your ultimate trip to Moriarty, covering everything from where to stay, what to eat, and what to do. Happy travels!
