Introduction to Laughlin

Located in Southern Nevada, Laughlin is a small town on the Colorado River that offers a unique blend of outdoor adventures, scenic views, and adrenaline-packed activities. Named after Don Laughlin, who opened a casino in the town in 1966, Laughlin has become a popular destination for tourists from around the world. In this comprehensive tour guide, we will explore all of the top attractions, things to see, and do in Laughlin, Nevada.

List of Places to Visit in Laughlin

If you are planning a trip to Laughlin, there are plenty of must-visit places that you should include in your itinerary. Here is a list of some of the top places to visit in Laughlin:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Colorado River Museum: Take a journey through time and learn about the history of the Colorado River and the surrounding area at this unique museum.
  • Don Laughlin’s Classic Car Collection: Be transported back to the days of yesteryear as you explore a vast collection of classic cars and automobiles.

Natural Attractions of Laughlin: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Davis Dam: This impressive dam provides an incredible view of the Colorado River and the surrounding landscape.
  • Gold Strike Canyon Hot Springs: A challenging hike will take you to this hidden gem, where you can soak in the natural hot springs and enjoy the beautiful desert scenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Laughlin

If you love the great outdoors, Laughlin offers plenty of opportunities for hiking and biking. Here are some popular routes you should consider:

  • River Mountains Loop Trail: This 34-mile trail encircles the River Mountains, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape and the Colorado River.
  • Talc Mine Trail: This off-road trail offers a challenging ride for experienced mountain bikers, with plenty of steep switchbacks and technical sections.

Best Things to Do in Laughlin: Activities and Tours

From thrilling water sports to exciting river tours, Laughlin offers plenty of activities and tours that are sure to keep you entertained. Here are some of the best things to do in Laughlin:

  • Water Sports: Enjoy a variety of water sports, including jet skiing, kayaking, and paddleboarding.
  • Riverboat Cruises: Take a relaxing cruise down the Colorado River and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Week-Long Itinerary for Laughlin

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Laughlin, here is a suggested itinerary for your stay:

Day Activities
1 Arrive in Laughlin and check into your hotel. Relax and enjoy the amenities.
2 Take a riverboat cruise and explore the Colorado River.
3 Visit the Colorado River Museum and explore downtown Laughlin.
4 Hike to Gold Strike Canyon Hot Springs and soak in the natural hot springs.
5 Go on a jet ski tour of the Colorado River.
6 Take a scenic drive to Davis Dam and enjoy the breathtaking views.
7 Leave Laughlin and head to your next destination.

Food and Drink Scene of Laughlin

Laughlin offers a variety of dining options to suit every taste and budget. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Buffalo wings: Enjoy a plate of spicy buffalo wings at one of the local sports bars.
  • Fish tacos: Grab a delicious fish taco from one of the waterfront restaurants.

Local Beverages

  • Laughlin River Ale: This tasty local beer is brewed at the local brewery and is a must-try for beer lovers.
  • Colorado River Cocktails: Indulge in a refreshing cocktail made with local ingredients and served in a souvenir glass.

Where to Stay in Laughlin: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Laughlin offers a wide range of accommodation options, from affordable motels to luxurious hotels and resorts. Here are some recommended places to stay:

  • Aquarius Casino Resort: This upscale hotel and casino offers stunning views of the Colorado River and an exciting casino experience.
  • Golden Nugget Laughlin: This stylish hotel and casino is located right on the waterfront and features a private beach.

What to Buy in Laughlin: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you are looking for a unique souvenir to bring home from your trip to Laughlin, here are some local products to consider:

  • Colorado River Rocks: These colorful river rocks are a great memento of your time spent on the banks of the Colorado River.
  • Laughlin T-Shirts and Hats: Show off your love for Laughlin with a stylish t-shirt or hat from a local souvenir shop.

Local Culture and Customs of Laughlin

The people of Laughlin are friendly and welcoming to tourists. However, it is important to be respectful of local customs and traditions. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Tipping: It is customary to tip 15-20% in restaurants and bars.
  • Dress Code: Dress casually but respectfully for most occasions.

Best Time to Visit Laughlin

The best time to visit Laughlin is in the fall and winter months, as the temperatures are mild and the crowds are smaller. However, if you want to enjoy the water sports and outdoor activities, the summer months are ideal.

Do’s and Don’ts of Laughlin

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Laughlin:

Do:

  • Try new foods: Laughlin offers a diverse range of dining options, so be sure to try some local specialties.
  • Explore the outdoors: Take advantage of Laughlin’s natural beauty by going on a hike or water sport activity.

Don’t:

  • Litter: Always dispose of your trash properly to keep the environment clean.
  • Overdrink: Enjoy the local beverages, but drink in moderation to avoid any unnecessary accidents.

Safety and Health

Here are some safety and health tips to keep in mind when visiting Laughlin:

Safety in Laughlin

  • Avoid walking alone at night: Stick to well-lit areas and walk with a group or a trusted companion.
  • Wear sunscreen: Protect your skin from the sun’s harmful rays.

Health in Laughlin

  • Stay hydrated: Be sure to drink plenty of water, especially during the hot summer months.
  • Avoid overheating: Avoid spending too much time in the sun to prevent heat exhaustion or heat stroke.

Getting to Laughlin: Transportation Options and Logistics

Laughlin is easily accessible by car, bus, or plane. Here are some transportation options to consider:

Airports Near Laughlin

  • Laughlin/Bullhead International Airport: This small airport is located just across the river in Bullhead City, Arizona.

Bus Stations Near Laughlin

  • Greyhound Bus Station: This bus station is located in Bullhead City, Arizona, just a short 10-minute drive from Laughlin.

Other Options

  • Car rental: You can rent a car at most major airports and cities.
  • Taxi or ride-sharing service: Taxis and ride-sharing services are available in Laughlin and the surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Laughlin

Laughlin is a relatively small town and can be easily explored on foot or by bicycle. If you need to travel further, taxis and rental cars are available.

Where to Go Next from Laughlin: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have some extra time, there are plenty of nearby destinations and excursions to explore:

  • Grand Canyon: This popular attraction is located just over 2 hours away from Laughlin and offers breathtaking views of one of the world’s natural wonders.
  • Lake Havasu: Located just over an hour from Laughlin, Lake Havasu is a popular destination for water sports and outdoor activities.

Conclusion

Whether you are looking to enjoy beautiful scenery, adrenaline-packed activities, or a relaxing getaway, Laughlin has something to offer everyone. With a rich history and unique culture, Laughlin is truly a hidden gem in the Southwest. Plan your ultimate trip with our comprehensive tour guide, and experience all that Laughlin has to offer.
