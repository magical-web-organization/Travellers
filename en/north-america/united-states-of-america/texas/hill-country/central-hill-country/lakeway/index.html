Introduction to Lakeway

Located in the heart of Central Hill Country in the gorgeous state of Texas, lies the beautiful and serene city of Lakeway. Surrounded by breathtaking scenic views and home to a wealth of natural and cultural attractions, Lakeway is a must-visit destination, offering something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Lakeway

Whether you’re a history buff or an outdoor enthusiast, Lakeway has a host of attractions to explore. Here are some of the top places to visit in Lakeway:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Lakeway Heritage Center: Take a trip down memory lane and discover the rich history of Lakeway at this award-winning museum.

  • Westlake Hills Presbyterian Church: Admire the stunning architecture and soak in the peaceful atmosphere at this historic church.

Natural Attractions of Lakeway: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Travis: Explore the clear, turquoise waters of Lake Travis, one of the most beautiful and largest lakes in Texas.

  • Hamilton Greenbelt Trail: Discover the lush natural beauty of Lakeway by hiking along this scenic trail.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lakeway

Cyclists and hikers will love the numerous trails that wind through the city and provide breathtaking panoramic views of the Texas Hill Country. Some of the best trails include the Hamilton Greenbelt Trail, the Canyonlands Trail, and the Lakeway Canyonlands Trails.

Best Things to Do in Lakeway: Activities and Tours

  • Watersports: Enjoy thrilling water sports activities like wakeboarding, kayaking, stand-up paddleboarding, and boat tours on the sparkling waters of Lake Travis.

  • Golf: Take advantage of Lakeway’s amazing weather and hit a few rounds of golf at one of the numerous world-class golf courses.

Week-Long Itinerary for Lakeway

Day 1: Visit the Lakeway Heritage Center and learn about the history of Lakeway.

Day 2: Go for a scenic hike on the Hamilton Greenbelt Trail and enjoy the natural beauty of Lakeway.

Day 3: Get your adrenaline pumping with exciting watersports like wakeboarding or kayaking on Lake Travis.

Day 4: Spend the day golfing on one of Lakeway’s many world-renowned courses.

Day 5: Explore the Canyonlands Trail, enjoying the spectacular views and serenity of the Texas Hill Country.

Day 6: Relax and unwind at one of Lakeway’s luxurious spas, indulging in a pampering spa treatment.

Day 7: Take a boat tour of Lake Travis and soak in the stunning scenery.

Food and Drink Scene of Lakeway

Lakeway’s culinary scene is a melting pot of flavors and cultures, offering an array of delicious dishes to suit every taste bud.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Texas-style BBQ brisket: Savor the slow-cooked, smoked beef brisket, a staple of Texas cuisine.

  • Tex-Mex cuisine: Try classic Tex-Mex dishes like tacos, enchiladas, and fajitas, which are found in most restaurants in Lakeway.

Local Beverages

  • Craft beer: From light lagers to hoppy IPAs, craft beer enthusiasts will love the variety of brews available at Lakeway’s local breweries.

  • Wine: Sip on a glass of exquisite Texas wine, produced by local wineries in the Texas Hill Country.

Where to Stay in Lakeway: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

From luxury resorts to quaint bed and breakfasts, Lakeway offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets. Some of the top-rated hotels in Lakeway include:

  • Lakeway Resort and Spa: This luxury resort boasts stunning views, top-notch amenities, and world-class dining options.

  • Sonesta Bee Cave Austin Hotel: Located just a few minutes’ drive away from Lakeway, this hotel offers modern, spacious rooms, and exceptional service.

What to Buy in Lakeway: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don’t leave Lakeway empty-handed! Take home some of the unique, locally-made souvenirs that reflect the city’s rich culture and heritage. Some popular items include:

  • Handmade Jewelry: Pick up exquisite pieces of jewelry made by local artisans.

  • Texas-themed T-shirts: Show off your Texan pride with a funky Texas-themed T-shirt.

Local Culture and Customs of Lakeway

Lakeway is a friendly, welcoming, and culturally diverse city that prides itself on celebrating and preserving its rich history and tradition. The locals are warm and hospitable and respect their city’s heritage and natural beauty.

Best Time to Visit Lakeway

The best time to visit Lakeway is between March to May and September to November when the weather is mild and ideal for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lakeway

Do:

  • Do respect local customs: Be mindful of local customs and traditions and respect the natural beauty of the city.

  • Do come prepared for outdoor activities: Bring comfortable shoes, sunscreen, and plenty of water for outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter: Keep Lakeway’s natural beauty intact by not littering in public places.

  • Don’t forget to tip: Show appreciation to the locals and service staff by tipping generously.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lakeway

Lakeway is a safe city, with low crime rates and a safe and secure environment. However, it’s always prudent to take the usual precautions, such as being aware of your surroundings and valuables, and staying in well-lit areas at night.

Health in Lakeway

Lakeway has access to quality healthcare facilities, including emergency services and medical clinics that provide expert medical care.

Getting to Lakeway: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lakeway

The nearest airports to Lakeway are:

  • Austin-Bergstrom International Airport: Located 25 miles away, this is the closest international airport to Lakeway.

  • San Antonio International Airport: Located 95 miles from Lakeway, this airport is another option for accessing the city.

Bus Stations Near Lakeway

Greyhound and Megabus are two prominent bus companies that operate services to and from Lakeway.

Other Options

Ride-share services like Uber and Lyft, along with taxi and car rental services, are also available for those who prefer private transportation.

How to Get Around in Lakeway

Lakeway is a small, walkable city with bike-friendly trails that offer easy access to all major attractions. Taxis, ride-share services, and car rentals are also available to get around the city.

Where to Go Next from Lakeway: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Lakeway’s central location makes it the perfect base from which to explore the numerous nearby destinations. Some popular destinations include:

  • Austin: Just a short drive away from Lakeway lies the vibrant city of Austin, known for its music, food, and nightlife.

  • Burnet: Visit the charming city of Burnet, located about 30 miles from Lakeway, and explore its history and natural beauty.

Conclusion

Lakeway is a charming, serene city that boasts stunning natural scenery, rich history and culture, and plenty of exciting activities and attractions. No matter what your interests, Lakeway has something to offer, making it a must-visit destination for travelers seeking a unique and unforgettable experience.
