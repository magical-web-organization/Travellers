Introduction to Surfside

Surfside is a small town located on the Gulf Coast of Texas, known for its beautiful beaches, fishing, and outdoor activities. The town is located in the North Barrier Coast and offers stunning views of the Gulf of Mexico. With its warm weather, friendly people, and beautiful scenery, Surfside is the perfect destination for a relaxing and adventurous vacation.

List of Places to Visit in Surfside

Surfside has a range of cultural and natural attractions, making it the perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts and history buffs. Here are some of the must-see places to visit in Surfside.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Surfside Beach Historical Museum The museum features displays of Surfside’s history, artifacts, pictures, and maps. Admission is free, but donations are always welcome.

  • Brazoria National Wildlife Refuge The Brazoria National Wildlife Refuge is a 44,414-acre refuge filled with wetlands, woods, and prairies. It is home to over 300 species of birds as well as alligators, turtles, and snakes.

  • Quintana Neotropical Bird Sanctuary The bird sanctuary is a 52-acre wildlife preserve that is an important stop for migratory birds. Visitors can take a guided tour to learn about the amazing birds and trees that are found in the area.

Natural Attractions of Surfside: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Surfside Beach The beach is the perfect place to relax and enjoy the beautiful scenery. Fishing, surfing, and swimming are popular activities at the beach, but there are also plenty of opportunities for sunbathing and relaxing.

  • Stahlman Park Stahlman Park has plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities such as hiking, volleyball, and camping. It is also home to an observation tower which offers stunning views of the Gulf of Mexico.

  • Freeport Municipal Golf Course The golf course is located just a few miles from Surfside and offers 18 holes of challenging play. It’s the perfect way to spend a day outdoors while enjoying the beautiful scenery of the Gulf Coast of Texas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Surfside

Surfside offers plenty of opportunities for hiking and biking. Here are some of the popular routes in the area.

  • Surfside Jetty Park The park is a great place to hike or bike along the beach. The trail is about 2 miles long and is perfect for beginner to intermediate hikers.

  • Velasco Marsh Preserve The Velasco Marsh Preserve is a 163-acre preserve with a 2.5-mile trail that offers stunning views of the marsh and the Gulf of Mexico.

  • Quintana Beach County Park The park has a 1.5-mile trail that offers beautiful views of the Gulf of Mexico and the dunes.

Best Things to Do in Surfside: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of things to do in Surfside, and many of them are perfect for families and groups. Here are some of the best things to do in Surfside.

  • Fishing Surfside has some of the best fishing in Texas. You can fish on the beach, in the Gulf of Mexico or in one of the many bays in the area. Charter boats are available for deep-sea fishing trips as well.

  • Kayaking Kayaking is a great way to explore the local waterways and enjoy the beautiful scenery. Rentals are available in town and guided tours are available as well.

  • Dolphin Watching Dolphin watching is a popular activity in Surfside. Visitors can take a guided tour to see the dolphins up close.

Food and Drink Scene of Surfside

Surfside has a variety of restaurants and bars that serve delicious local cuisine and drinks. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fried Catfish Fried catfish is a popular dish in Surfside. It’s usually served with coleslaw and hush puppies.

  • Shrimp and Grits Shrimp and grits is another popular dish in Surfside. The dish is typically made with a creamy grits base and topped with sauteed shrimp and a delicious sauce.

  • Gumbo Gumbo is a hearty stew made with seafood, sausage, and vegetables. It’s a staple dish in Gulf Coast cuisine.

Local Beverages

  • Shiner Bock Beer Shiner Bock beer is a Texas classic and is a popular drink in Surfside. The beer is made in Shiner, Texas and is known for its rich flavor and smooth finish.

  • Sweet Tea Sweet tea is a staple drink in the South and is a popular beverage in Surfside. It’s usually served cold and sweetened with sugar.

Where to Stay in Surfside: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Surfside has plenty of options for accommodations, from hotels to guesthouses. Here are some of the best places to stay in Surfside.

  • Surfside Beach Resort Surfside Beach Resort is a popular hotel located right on the beach. The hotel offers a range of amenities, including a pool, spa, and on-site restaurant.

  • Island Treasure Beach House Island Treasure Beach House is a guesthouse located right on the beach. The building is a beautiful 4-bedroom house decorated with tasteful furnishings and a modern design that will make you feel at home.

  • Freeport RV Resort Freeport RV Resort is a campground that offers RV sites and cottages. The campground has a pool, playground, and picnic area.

What to Buy in Surfside: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Surfside has plenty of local products and souvenirs that visitors can take home. Here are some of the best options.

  • Shell Jewelry Surfside is known for its beautiful seashells, and many local shops sell jewelry made from the shells.

  • Gulf Shrimp Gulf shrimp is a delicious local delicacy that visitors can take home and cook themselves.

Local Culture and Customs of Surfside

Surfside is a friendly town with a laid-back culture. Visitors should be respectful of the local customs, which include being polite and friendly to those you meet.

Best Time to Visit Surfside

The best time to visit Surfside is during the spring and fall when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller.

Do’s and Don’ts of Surfside

Do:

  • Do take time to explore the natural beauty of the area.

  • Do try the local cuisine and beverages.

  • Do take a dolphin and/or bird-watching tour.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the beautiful surroundings.

  • Don’t forget sunscreen and mosquito repellent.

  • Don’t forget to respect local culture and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Surfside

Surfside is a safe place to visit, but visitors should be aware of the weather conditions and potential hazards. During hurricane season, visitors should monitor the weather and follow any evacuation orders if necessary.

Health in Surfside

Visitors should take care of themselves by wearing sunscreen and staying hydrated in the hot and humid weather. Mosquitoes and other insects can be a problem, so visitors should also bring insect repellent.

Getting to Surfside: Transportation Options and Logistics

Surfside is easily accessible by car, bus, or plane. Here are some of the options for getting to Surfside.

Airports Near Surfside

  • George Bush Intercontinental Airport George Bush Intercontinental Airport is located in Houston, Texas. It is about a one-hour drive from Surfside.

  • William P. Hobby Airport William P. Hobby Airport is located in Houston, Texas. It is about a one-hour drive from Surfside.

Bus Stations Near Surfside

  • Greyhound Bus Lines Greyhound Bus Lines has a station in Houston, making it easy to transfer to a bus to Surfside.

  • Megabus Megabus also has a station in Houston with service to Surfside.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Surfside from Houston or take a ride-sharing service like Uber or Lyft.

How to Get Around in Surfside

Surfside is a small town, so walking or biking is a great way to get around. Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi if needed.

Where to Go Next from Surfside: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are plenty of nearby destinations and excursions that visitors can take from Surfside. Here are some of the top options.

  • Galveston Galveston is a popular tourist destination known for its beaches, historic architecture, and entertainment.

  • Houston Houston is a vibrant city with a rich history and culture. Visitors can explore museums, art galleries, and restaurants.

  • Brazosport Brazosport is an area just south of Surfside that offers plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities and cultural attractions.

Conclusion

Surfside is a small town with a lot to offer visitors. With beautiful beaches, outdoor activities, and delicious food and drink, it’s the perfect destination for a relaxing and fun vacation. Visitors to Surfside will leave feeling refreshed and rejuvenated, and eager to plan their next trip to this charming Texas town.
