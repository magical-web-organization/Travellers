Introduction to Mount Pleasant

Located in Northeastern Texas, Mount Pleasant is a small town in the heart of Piney Woods. Known for its rich history and natural beauty, it is an excellent destination for travelers looking for an outdoor adventure. Whether you want to explore the local culture, hike through scenic routes, or just relax and unwind, Mount Pleasant has everything to offer. In this travel guide, we bring you a comprehensive list of things to do and see in Mount Pleasant.

List of Places to Visit in Mount Pleasant

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Titus County Courthouse: Built in 1895, the courthouse is a symbol of the town’s rich history. Today, it is a popular tourist attraction that serves as the hub of the downtown area.
  • Mount Pleasant Heritage Park: Take a stroll through the Mount Pleasant Heritage Park to take in the town’s history. This park contains a museum, a replica of a former train depot, and other historical sites that detail the founding and early days of the town.
  • Texas Music Barn: This musical venue hosts shows from world-renowned artists, local musicians, and artists from across the state.
  • Mount Pleasant Railroad Museum: Located on the site of the former International & Great Northern Railroad Depot, the museum offers an extensive collection of railroad memorabilia.

Natural Attractions of Mount Pleasant: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lake Bob Sandlin State Park: A popular destination for hiking, fishing, and boating, Lake Bob Sandlin State Park encompasses over 638 acres of pine and hardwood forest. This scenic park has 75 RV and tent campsites and features a boat ramp, fishing dock, fish cleaning station, and kayak rentals.
  • Mount Pleasant Country Club: A great place for golf enthusiasts, the country club features two PGA golf courses with breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside.
  • Daingerfield State Park: The park is a popular destination for hiking, fishing, camping, and swimming. It has almost 600 acres of forest with multiple trails for hiking and mountain biking. It is also home to the beautiful Daingerfield Lake.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mount Pleasant

  • Northeast Texas Trail: This trail stretches for 130 miles and runs through the heart of Mount Pleasant. It is a popular destination for hiking, biking, and horseback riding and features stunning vistas of the Piney Woods.
  • Oak Creek Hike/Bike Trail: This trail is located in Mount Pleasant’s Herndon Park. It is relatively easy and meanders through the oak and pine forest beside the creek.

Best Things to Do in Mount Pleasant: Activities and Tours

  • Patriot Golf Course: The Patriot Golf Course is a fantastic way to enjoy the outdoors. It features 18 holes and has an excellent driving range.
  • Maxey House: This is the former home of Samuel Bell Maxey, a prominent Texas senator and Civil War general. The house now serves as a museum and features period-accurate furniture and artifacts.
  • Wineries of Mount Pleasant: With several wineries operating within the town limits, there’s something for everyone. Take a tour, sample some wines, and enjoy the local taste of this unique region.

Week-Long Itinerary for Mount Pleasant

Day Activity
Day 1 Explore the Titus County Courthouse and the Mount Pleasant Heritage Park.

Food and Drink Scene of Mount Pleasant

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fried Catfish: A Southern favorite, fried catfish is a must-have dish when in Mount Pleasant.
  • Southern Fried Chicken: Another Southern classic, enjoy a plate of crispy fried chicken with all the fixings.
  • Pecan Pie: A sweet treat perfect for a post-meal dessert.

Local Beverages

  • Sweet Tea: This Southern staple is a refreshing drink that’s perfect on a hot day.
  • Texas Beer: Try a locally brewed beer or ale, and sample everything from IPAs to stouts.

Where to Stay in Mount Pleasant: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Holiday Inn Express & Suites Mount Pleasant: This hotel offers air-conditioned rooms, free Wi-Fi, and a complimentary breakfast.
  • Hampton Inn & Suites Mount Pleasant: Located next to the Titus County Courthouse, this hotel features spacious rooms with air conditioning and free Wi-Fi.
  • The Redbud Inn: Overlooking Mount Pleasant Country Club, this hotel offers affordable rooms and an outdoor pool.

What to Buy in Mount Pleasant: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Local Honey: There’s nothing sweeter than local honey. Bring back a jar or two to enjoy on toast, yogurt, or in tea.
  • Pecans: Texas is well-known for its pecans, and Mount Pleasant is no exception. Bring back a bag of roasted or salted pecans.
  • Local Wines: Taste some of the best Texas wines and bring home a bottle or two from the wineries.

Local Culture and Customs of Mount Pleasant

Mount Pleasant is a charming, small town that maintains its Southern roots. As a visitor, it’s essential to be respectful of the locals and their customs. Some general etiquette tips include speaking politely, dressing appropriately, and avoiding being overly loud or boisterous.

Best Time to Visit Mount Pleasant

The best time to visit Mount Pleasant is in the fall, from September through November, when the weather is mild and optimal for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mount Pleasant

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Dress appropriately for the weather and occasions.
  • Speak politely and practice good manners.

Don’t:

  • Be overly loud or disrespectful.
  • Leave trash behind when hiking or enjoying outdoor activities.
  • Speak negatively of the town or its residents.

Safety and Health

Safety in Mount Pleasant

Mount Pleasant is a relatively safe town with a low crime rate. It’s essential to take normal precautions to keep yourself and your belongings safe during your visit.

Health in Mount Pleasant

There are several medical facilities, including hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies, in and around Mount Pleasant. It is recommended to carry the necessary prescriptions and health insurance documents with you.

Getting to Mount Pleasant: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Mount Pleasant

The closest airports to Mount Pleasant are the Dallas Love Field Airport (DAL) and the Dallas/Fort Worth International Airport (DFW). Private aircraft can also arrive at Mount Pleasant’s Northeast Texas Regional Airport.

Bus Stations Near Mount Pleasant

Greyhound has a bus station located in the town.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive or take a taxi to Mount Pleasant from surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Mount Pleasant

Renting a car, taxi cabs, and bike rentals are all great ways to get around the town. It’s also a great place to explore by foot, especially downtown and the parks.

Where to Go Next from Mount Pleasant: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Dallas: Dallas is within a two-hour drive from Mount Pleasant. With several attractions, it is a great destination for the next stop on your Texas adventure.
  • Clarksville: This town is located about an hour’s drive to the north of Mount Pleasant and is known for the Red River County Courthouse and Downtown Historical District.
  • Tyler: This city is located about an hour’s drive to the east and is known for its flower farms and the Tyler Rose Garden.

Conclusion

Mount Pleasant, Texas, is a hidden gem worth exploring. From natural attractions and hiking trails to historical sites and wineries, this town has it all. With our comprehensive guide, you can gain insight into the city’s culture and discover activities and sights that appeal to your preferences. Whether you’re looking for a weekend adventure or a week-long nature retreat, Mount Pleasant is the perfect destination for you.
