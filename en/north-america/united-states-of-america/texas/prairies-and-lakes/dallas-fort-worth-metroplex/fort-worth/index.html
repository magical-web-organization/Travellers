Introduction to Fort Worth

Welcome to Fort Worth, a dynamic city located in Texas, USA. Also known as Cowtown, Fort Worth is famous for its Western heritage and rich history. From museums and galleries to world-class restaurants, the city has something for everyone. With this travel guide, we’ll make sure you don’t miss any hidden gems.

List of Places to Visit in Fort Worth

Fort Worth is packed with amazing attractions worth exploring. Here’s a list of our top picks!

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Kimbell Art Museum - showcases a collection of art from around the globe, including works by European masters and Impressionists.
  • The National Cowgirl Museum and Hall of Fame - a must-visit for anyone interested in Western heritage and the contributions of women in history.
  • Fort Worth Stockyards National Historic District - home to daily cattle drives and weekly rodeo shows that celebrate the city’s Ranching heritage.
  • Fort Worth Museum of Science and History - offers interactive exhibits on natural history, science, and popular culture.

Natural Attractions of Fort Worth: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Trinity River - a vast network of waterways perfect for kayaking, fishing, and paddleboarding.
  • Fort Worth Botanic Garden - a sprawling green oasis that boasts over 2,500 species of plants from around the world.
  • Fort Worth Nature Center and Refuge - a 3,600-acre park with hiking and biking trails, as well as opportunities for birdwatching and wildlife spotting.
  • Amon Carter Museum of American Art - a museum with extensive collection of American art that reflects the country’s history.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fort Worth

Fort Worth has a variety of scenic trails that are perfect for hiking and biking.

  • Trinity Trails - A 72-mile hike and bike trail system that winds along the Trinity River and connects many of Fort Worth’s top attractions.
  • Eagle Mountain Park - A park with more than 5 miles of hiking trails, including paths that lead to stunning cliff-side views of the lake and surrounding landscape.
  • Fort Worth Nature Center and Refuge - A wildlife preserve that has more than 20 miles of hiking trails that wind through forests, prairies, and wetlands.

Best Things to Do in Fort Worth: Activities and Tours

Fort Worth is brimming with exciting things to do. Here are some top-notch activities and tours that you shouldn’t miss.

  • Fort Worth Food Tours - A guided walking tour of the city’s top restaurants and food stalls. Sample the city’s most delicious dishes while learning about the history and culture of the local cuisine.
  • Fort Worth Helicopter Tours - See the city from a whole new perspective. Fly over Fort Worth’s skyline and see its famous landmarks.
  • Segway Fort Worth Tours - Tour the city’s top attractions while cruising on a segway.
  • Chesapeake Energy Arena Tours- Book a tour of the man-made iconic location and learn its history.

Food and Drink Scene of Fort Worth

Fort Worth’s food and drink scene is a foodie’s paradise. Here’s a list of must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Texas BBQ - Texas is famous for its BBQ, and Fort Worth has some of the best in the state.
  • Chicken Fried Steak - This dish consists of a steak that is breaded and deep-fried, and usually served with cream gravy.
  • Tex-Mex Cuisine - Fort Worth’s Tex-Mex cuisine is a blend of American and Mexican flavors, usually served with plenty of spice.

Local Beverages

  • Shiner Bock Beer - This Texan beer is brewed with German hops and has a crisp, refreshing taste.
  • Sweet Tea - A traditional southern beverage that can be found in most restaurants and cafes.
  • Margaritas - A staple drink in Texas restaurants, margaritas can be found in a variety of flavors and strengths.

Where to Stay in Fort Worth: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Fort Worth offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some of our top picks.

  • The Ashton Hotel - a boutique hotel that combines modern amenities with historic charm. It’s located in the heart of downtown Fort Worth.
  • Fort Worth Hostel - A budget-friendly hostel located in the Cultural District, close to many of Fort Worth’s top attractions.
  • The Omni Fort Worth - A luxury hotel located in the heart of downtown Fort Worth with a pool and spa facilities.

What to Buy in Fort Worth: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

No trip to Fort Worth is complete without bringing home a souvenir or two. Here are some ideas for unique and locally-made products to buy.

  • Cowboy Boots and Hats - Fort Worth is in the heart of cowboy country, and you’ll find plenty of stores selling authentic cowboy boots and hats.
  • Western Art - Fort Worth is home to several art galleries that specialize in Western art, where you can find unique local pieces.
  • Hot Sauce - Many restaurants and shops sell their own versions of hot sauce that make for a spicy souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Fort Worth

Fort Worth is proud of its Western heritage and cowboy culture, which is reflected in many local customs and traditions. Visitors should be prepared to see plenty of cowboy hats and boots around the city.

Best Time to Visit Fort Worth

Fort Worth has a subtropical climate with hot summers and mild winters. The best time to visit is in the spring or fall when the weather is comfortable enough to enjoy outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Fort Worth

Do:

  • Do embrace the cowboy culture and dress up in cowboy boots and hats.
  • Do try the local cuisine, especially the BBQ and Tex-Mex dishes.
  • Do visit the Stockyards National Historic District and watch the daily cattle drive.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget to stay hydrated, especially during the hot summer months.
  • Don’t visit Fort Worth without trying the fried chicken steak.
  • Don’t miss out on the city’s vibrant arts scene.

Safety and Health

Safety in Fort Worth

Fort Worth is generally a safe city, but visitors should still take precautions to avoid becoming victims of crime. Stay aware of your surroundings, and don’t leave valuables in plain sight in your car or hotel room.

Health in Fort Worth

Fort Worth is home to several excellent medical facilities, including Baylor All Saints Medical Center and JPS Health Network.

Getting to Fort Worth: Transportation Options and Logistics

Fort Worth is easily accessible by air, car, and public transportation.

Airports Near Fort Worth

  • Dallas-Fort Worth International Airport is the primary airport that serves Fort Worth and is located less than 30 minutes away from the city center.

Bus Stations Near Fort Worth

  • Fort Worth Intermodal Transportation Center - A bus station located in downtown Fort Worth that serves Greyhound and Amtrak buses.

Other Options

  • Taxis and ride-sharing apps such as Uber and Lyft are widely available throughout the city.

How to Get Around in Fort Worth

Fort Worth is a sprawling city, but it’s easy to get around by car, bike or public transportation. Some of the popular ways to explore the city include:

  • Public Transportation - Fort Worth’s public transportation system, Trinity Metro, includes bus and train services that cover most parts of the city.
  • Biking - Fort Worth has an extensive network of bike lanes and trails, making it easy to explore on two wheels.
  • Car Rental - Renting a car is another option that gives you the freedom to explore the city at your own pace.

Where to Go Next from Fort Worth: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore more of Texas, there are several nearby destinations that are worth a visit. Some popular options include:

  • Dallas - Just a short drive away, Dallas is home to several top-notch museums, restaurants, and outdoor attractions.
  • Austin - About a three-hour drive away from Fort Worth, Austin is a vibrant city that’s known for its thriving music scene and delicious food.

Conclusion

Fort Worth is a vibrant city that has something to offer everyone. From its rich Western heritage to its vibrant arts scene and delicious cuisine, visitors are sure to fall in love with this Texas gem. Use this travel guide to plan your ideal trip and discover everything that Fort Worth has to offer.
