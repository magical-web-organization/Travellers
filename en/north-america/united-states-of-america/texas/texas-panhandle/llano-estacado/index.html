Nestled in the Texas panhandle, Llano Estacado is a region full of diverse attractions and cultural experiences. This vast, arid area has a rich history, and visitors are sure to find plenty to explore amidst its miles of open space.

Week-Long Itinerary for Llano Estacado

To make the most of your visit to Llano Estacado, we’ve put together a recommended week-long itinerary to help you plan your trip.

Day 1: Visit the National Ranching Heritage Center and take a guided tour. Day 2: Explore Lubbock Lake Landmark and experience the interactive exhibits. Day 3: Spend the day hiking and exploring Palo Duro Canyon State Park. Day 4: Check out the Buddy Holly Center and Lubbock High School. Day 5: Take a guided tour of the Caprock Canyons State Park. Day 6: Drive the scenic routes and visit the local vineyards. Day 7: Visit the Mackenzie Museum and go on a ghost town tour.
