Introduction to Wolfforth

Welcome to Wolfforth, a charming small town located in the Llano Estacado region of Texas. Known for its warm hospitality, rich history, and scenic vistas, Wolfforth is a perfect destination for those who want to escape the hustle and bustle of big cities and experience the authentic Texas way of life.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take you on a tour of Wolfforth’s top attractions, hiking/biking routes, best things to do, food and drink scene, where to stay, and much more.

So pack your bags and get ready to explore the beauty of Wolfforth!

List of Places to Visit in Wolfforth

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Wolfforth Area Museum - Located on the Texas Tech University Health Sciences Center campus, this museum features exhibits on the history of Wolfforth and its surrounding areas, including artifacts from the pioneer days and the oil boom era.

  • National Ranching Heritage Center - A must-visit for history buffs, the center has over 50 authentic buildings and exhibits that showcase the history of ranching in Texas and the American West.

  • Buddy Holly Center - Located in nearby Lubbock, this museum celebrates the life and legacy of the rock ‘n’ roll icon Buddy Holly, who was born in Lubbock.

Natural Attractions of Wolfforth: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Prairie Dog Town - Home to hundreds of prairie dogs, this park is a fun and unique spot to visit. You can watch the prairie dogs interact with each other, and even feed them.

  • Lake Alan Henry - Just a short drive from Wolfforth, Lake Alan Henry is a popular spot for fishing, boating, and water sports. The lake is surrounded by scenic rolling hills and is a great place for a picnic or to relax in nature.

  • Caprock Canyons State Park - Located about an hour from Wolfforth, this park is famous for its stunning red rock canyons and bison herd. You can hiking/biking trails, go camping, and take a guided bison tour.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Wolfforth

  • Mae Simmons Park Trail - A 1.25-mile nature trail that winds through the park, this trail is perfect for a leisurely walk or a morning jog. You’ll see a variety of plants and wildlife along the way.

  • Yellowhouse Canyon Trail - This 9-mile trail offers some of the best mountain biking in the area. Be prepared for some challenging uphill climbs and technical descents.

  • Texas Tech University Trails - Located in Lubbock, the university has over 10 miles of trails that are open to the public. The trails wind through the campus and offer great views of the city.

Best Things to Do in Wolfforth: Activities and Tours

  • Wine Tasting at Trilogy Cellars - Wolfforth is home to the award-winning Trilogy Cellars, where you can taste a variety of wines produced with locally grown grapes. You can choose from several tasting options and enjoy a glass of wine on their outdoor patio.

  • Art Galleries in Lubbock - Lubbock is home to numerous art galleries, where you can view works by local and national artists. Some of the top galleries include the Louise Hopkins Underwood Center for the Arts, the Buddy Holly Center, and the Charles Adams Gallery.

  • Texas Tech University Games - If you’re a sports fan, catch a game at the Texas Tech University. The university has teams in football, basketball, baseball, and other sports.

Food and Drink Scene of Wolfforth

Must-Try Dishes

  • Barbecue - Texas is famous for its barbecue, and Wolfforth is no exception. Some of the top spots for barbecue in Wolfforth include Evie Mae’s Pit Barbecue and Shockley’s Barbecue.

  • Tex-Mex - You can’t visit Texas without trying some Tex-Mex. Some of the top Tex-Mex spots in Wolfforth include Taqueria Autlan and La Sirena.

  • Fried Chicken - Another Texas staple, fried chicken is a must-try dish. Check out The Shack BBQ for some of the best fried chicken in the area.

Local Beverages

  • Wine - As mentioned above, Trilogy Cellars is a top spot for wine tasting.

  • Beer - Lubbock is home to several great craft breweries, including Two Docs Brewing Co. and The Brewery LBK.

Where to Stay in Wolfforth: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Wolfforth Inn - Located in the heart of town, this budget-friendly hotel offers comfortable rooms and free breakfast.

  • Hilton Garden Inn Lubbock - Just a short drive from Wolfforth, this hotel offers upscale rooms and amenities, including an outdoor pool and fitness center.

  • Bandera Guest House - This charming guesthouse is located in Lubbock and offers cozy rooms and a homey atmosphere.

What to Buy in Wolfforth: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Barbecue Sauce from Evie Mae’s Pit Barbecue - Evie Mae’s Pit Barbecue is famous for its barbecue sauce, which is available for purchase.

  • Local Wine from Trilogy Cellars - Bring home a bottle of wine from the award-winning Trilogy Cellars.

Local Culture and Customs of Wolfforth

  • Southern Hospitality - Texans are known for their warm and welcoming hospitality, so expect to be treated like family while visiting Wolfforth.

  • Religious Faith - Many residents of Wolfforth are deeply religious, and the town has several churches of various denominations.

Best Time to Visit Wolfforth

The best time to visit Wolfforth is during the spring and fall, when the weather is pleasant and comfortable for outdoor activities. Summers can be hot and humid, while winters can be cold and windy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Wolfforth

Do:

  • Explore the Outdoors - Wolfforth has plenty of natural attractions to explore, so don’t miss out on hiking, fishing, or just relaxing in nature.

  • Try the Local Cuisine - Texas is known for its great food, so be sure to try some local favorites like barbecue and Tex-Mex.

Don’t:

  • Be Disrespectful of Local Customs - Texans take their traditions and customs seriously, so be polite and respectful of local culture.

  • Litter - Keep Wolfforth beautiful by disposing of trash properly and respecting the environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Wolfforth

Wolfforth is generally a safe town, but visitors should still take basic precautions like locking car doors and avoiding walking alone at night.

Health in Wolfforth

There are several quality medical facilities in the area, including the University Medical Center and Covenant Health.

Getting to Wolfforth: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Wolfforth

  • Lubbock Preston Smith International Airport - Located about 16 miles from Wolfforth, this airport offers domestic flights to several major cities.

Bus Stations Near Wolfforth

  • Greyhound Bus Lines - Lubbock has a Greyhound bus station with connections to several major cities.

Other Options

  • Renting a Car - The easiest way to get around Wolfforth and the surrounding areas is by renting a car.

How to Get Around in Wolfforth

The best way to get around Wolfforth is by car. Taxis and Uber/Lyft are also available, but availability may be limited.

Where to Go Next from Wolfforth: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Lubbock - Just a short drive from Wolfforth, Lubbock is a vibrant city with plenty to do. Visit the Texas Tech University campus, explore the art galleries, or catch a performance at the city’s many music venues.

  • Palisades Canyon - Located just south of Amarillo, this stunning canyon offers scenic hiking trails, wildlife viewing, and beautiful views.

  • Carlsbad Caverns National Park - Located in New Mexico, this park features a vast network of underground caves and stunning rock formations.

Conclusion

We hope this travel guide has inspired you to visit Wolfforth and experience all that this charming town has to offer. From natural wonders to cultural landmarks, there’s something for everyone in Wolfforth. So pack your bags and start planning your trip today!
