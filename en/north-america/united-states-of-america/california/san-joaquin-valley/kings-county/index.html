Introduction to Kings County

Nestled in the heart of California’s San Joaquin Valley, Kings County is a pristine destination that showcases the region’s natural beauty and cultural heritage. The county boasts a diverse range of attractions, from historical landmarks and museums to outdoor activities and awe-inspiring views. In this guide, you’ll discover the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Kings County

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Hanford Carnegie Museum

The Hanford Carnegie Museum is housed in a beautiful, 1905 mission-style building that was once a Carnegie Library. It features a collection of artifacts and exhibits that showcase the cultural history and legacy of Kings County, including vintage clothing, historic photographs, and Native American artifacts.

Lemoore Naval Air Station Museum

The Lemoore Naval Air Station Museum is a must-visit destination for military history enthusiasts. The museum showcases the rich history and legacy of the NAS Lemoore and the U.S. Navy’s contributions to aviation.

Colonel Allensworth State Historic Park

Colonel Allensworth State Historic Park is a historic, African American township that was founded in 1908. The park features restored buildings, including a schoolhouse, church, and hotel, that offer a glimpse into the lives of the town’s residents, who aimed to create a self-sufficient community free from discrimination and prejudice.

Natural Attractions of Kings County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lemoore City Lake

Lemoore City Lake is a tranquil oasis that is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts who love fishing, boating, picnicking, and hiking. The lake is surrounded by scenic walking trails and lush greenery that provide ample opportunities to experience the natural beauty of Kings County.

Kettleman Hills

The Kettleman Hills are one of the most breathtaking natural attractions in Kings County. This range of hills provides an idyllic setting for hikers, bikers, and nature lovers who want to experience an up-close view of the county’s unique geography.

Corcoran Historic Walking Tour

The Corcoran Historic Walking Tour is a must-visit destination for those who want to explore the rich history and architecture of Kings County. The tour takes visitors through the heart of downtown Corcoran, and highlights historic buildings, landmarks, and cultural sites.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kings County

Kings County offers numerous hiking and biking routes for outdoor enthusiasts of all skill levels. Here are some of the best options:

  • Lemoore Canal Trail: a 4.5-mile trail that runs along the Lemoore Canal and offers scenic views of the county’s agricultural areas.
  • Corcoran Rail Trail: a 1.6-mile trail that follows the route of the historic Southern Pacific Railroad line through the heart of downtown Corcoran.
  • Kettleman Hills Hiking Trail: a 3.5-mile trail that leads hikers through the heart of the Kettleman Hills and offers stunning views of the county’s natural beauty.

Best Things to Do in Kings County: Activities and Tours

Skydive Lemoore

Skydive Lemoore offers adrenaline junkies the ultimate thrill by providing unforgettable skydiving experiences over the county’s breathtaking scenery.

Kings Fair

The Kings Fair is a family-friendly event that takes place annually during the summer months. The event features live entertainment, carnival rides, petting zoos, and an array of classic fair food and drink options.

Tachi Palace Casino Resort

The Tachi Palace Casino Resort is a world-class entertainment destination that features an array of luxury amenities, including a world-class casino, a spa, an indoor pool, and several dining and entertainment options.

Food and Drink Scene of Kings County

Must-Try Dishes

Kings County is renowned for its variety of delectable dishes, including;

  • Tri-Tip sandwich: a popular tri-tip dish that features marinated beef on a grilled garlic bread sandwich.
  • Basque cuisine: a unique culinary style that blends traditional European and Latin American dishes with California flavors.
  • Pupusas: a beloved Central American dish that features dough filled with cheese and other flavorful fillings.

Local Beverages

Kings County’s craft beverage scene is thriving. Locally brewed beers and distilled spirits are ubiquitous in restaurants and bars throughout the region. Some popular options include;

  • Lemoore Lemonade: a tangy, locally made, refreshing beverage,
  • Kings County Distillery: an award-winning distillery that offers a range of premium spirits, including whiskey and bourbon.

Where to Stay in Kings County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kings County offers a wide range of accommodation options for travelers of all budgets:

  • Tachi Palace Casino Resort: a luxurious, all-inclusive resort that features a casino, a spa, and several dining and entertainment options.
  • Sierra Lodge: a cozy motel that offers comfortable rooms and amenities.

What to Buy in Kings County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kings County is known for producing a wide range of locally made products, including:

  • Almonds: The region is the largest almond producer in the United States, making it the perfect place to stock up on almond-based products like almond butter and almond milk.
  • Artisan Crafts: Kings County features many talented artisans who produce a variety of beautiful handmade crafts.

Local Culture and Customs of Kings County

Kings County has a rich and diverse cultural heritage that is reflected in its art, music, and cuisine. The region is home to a large Hispanic population, which contributes heavily to its unique identity.

Best Time to Visit Kings County

The best time to visit Kings County is from March to May and September to November when the weather is mild and comfortable. The summer months can be hot and dry, and the winter months can be rainy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kings County

Do:

  • Respect local cultures and traditions.
  • Enjoy the region’s natural beauty, but leave only footprints and take only memories.
  • Try the locally sourced food and drink options.
  • Plan ahead and make reservations.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage local attractions and landmarks.
  • Trespass on private property.
  • Ignore safety precautions while participating in outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kings County

Kings County is a relatively safe destination, but visitors should take standard safety precautions, such as locking doors and windows, staying aware of their surroundings, and being cautious while driving.

Health in Kings County

Visitors to Kings County should be aware of the region’s air quality. The region has some of the worst air pollution levels in the United States. Visitors should also take precautions to protect themselves from the sun during the summer months.

Getting to Kings County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Kings County

  • Fresno Yosemite International Airport (FAT)
  • Visalia Municipal Airport (VIS)

Bus Stations Near Kings County

  • Greyhound Station: Hanford

Other Options

  • Car Rental: There are several car rental companies located throughout Kings County.
  • Amtrak: The nearest Amtrak station is located in Hanford.

How to Get Around in Kings County

Renting a car is the most convenient way to get around Kings County. Taxis, ride-sharing services, and public transportation options are also available but may be limited in some areas of the county.

Where to Go Next from Kings County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Sequoia National Park

Sequoia National Park is a stunning destination that is known for its world-famous giant sequoia trees. The park offers visitors the opportunity to explore the natural beauty of the Sierra Nevada mountains, and engage in outdoor activities such as hiking, camping, and fishing.

Yosemite National Park

Yosemite National Park is a must-visit destination for any nature lover. The park offers breathtaking views and a vast array of outdoor recreational activities, including hiking, camping, fishing, and rock climbing.

Conclusion

Kings County is a hidden gem that offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. With its array of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and delicious cuisine, Kings County is a destination that truly has something for everyone. Plan your visit today and discover the beauty of California’s San Joaquin Valley.
