Introduction to Inyokern

Inyokern is a small unincorporated community in Kern County, located in San Joaquin Valley, California, United States. The community has a rural ambiance and is surrounded by natural beauty and stunning landscapes. Inyokern is a perfect destination for those who are looking for a relaxing getaway mixed with outdoor adventures.

The museum displays the rich history of Kern County and has exhibits on Native American cultures, early settlers, and the oil industry.   2. Maturango Museum: Maturango Museum is a natural history and cultural museum in Ridgecrest, California. The museum is dedicated to preserving the natural and cultural history of the Northern Mojave Desert. Visitors can explore exhibits on geology, botany, and archaeology.
