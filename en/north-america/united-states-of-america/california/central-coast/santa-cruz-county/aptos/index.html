Introduction to Aptos

Aptos is a charming coastal town situated in central Santa Cruz County, California. The town boasts stunning beaches, redwood forests, and a rich cultural heritage. Whether it’s outdoor adventures, beach fun, museum visits, or any other activity that you’re interested in, Aptos has something for everyone. This travel guide is here to help you explore the best of Aptos and make the most of your visit.

List of Places to Visit in Aptos

With so much to see and do in Aptos, it can be a challenge to decide where to start. Here’s a list of must-visit attractions in Aptos, divided into two categories:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Aptos History Museum - The museum showcases the rich history of Aptos, reflecting the life of the Native Americans, pioneers, and the people who call Aptos home today.
  • Seacliff State Beach - Known for the historic SS Palo Alto pier, which is one of California’s most historic landmarks. Explore maritime history and visit the nearby Aptos Museum.
  • George Washington Grove - George Washington Grove is a redwood forest with giant trees that are over 1,000 years old. The grove provides a perfect spot for hikers and nature lovers.

Natural Attractions of Aptos: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Nisene Marks State Park - The park is over 10,000 acres of forested land, with over 30 miles of hiking and biking trails.
  • The Forest of Nicene Marks State Park - Here you can explore the wilderness of Aptos hiking through the forest, observing wildlife and observing history.
  • Seacliff State Beach - With a mile-long beach and stunning views of the Monterey Bay, Seacliff State Beach offers a breathtaking picnic spot.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Aptos

Aptos offers stunning hiking and biking opportunities for anyone who wants to connect with nature. Here are some popular trails:

  • Aptos Creek Trail - A 6.5-mile trek through Aptos that offers a scenic view of the madrones and the creek.
  • Fall Creek State Park - A 2,637-acre park that offers hiking and biking on over 20 miles of trails.
  • Nisene Marks State Park - With over 30 miles of trails for hiking, biking, and horse-back riding, the park offers the perfect outdoor adventure for families and adventure seekers alike.

Best Things to Do in Aptos: Activities and Tours

Aptos offers a wide range of activities for visitors to enjoy. Here are some of the best things to do in Aptos:

  • Fly-fishing on the Aptos Creek - Aptos Creek is home to rainbow trout, steelhead, and Chinook salmon. It’s the perfect spot in Aptos for fly-fishing enthusiasts.
  • Kayaking on Monterey Bay - Rent a kayak and explore Monterey Bay, one of the largest marine sanctuaries, and home to a vast array of marine life.
  • Wine Tasting in Aptos Village - There are several wineries in Aptos that offer tours and tastings, including the Nicholson Vineyards and the Downhill Winery.

Food and Drink Scene of Aptos

When it comes to food and drink, Aptos won’t disappoint. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fish Tacos - Aptos is known for its delicious fish tacos, which are made with locally-sourced fish and garnished with fresh veggies.
  • Garlic Fries - A popular snack in Aptos, which can be found at various sporting events and food stands.
  • California Burritos - A classic burrito, but with a blend of fresh guacamole, cheese, beans, fries, and meat.

Local Beverages

  • Beer from Sante Adairius Rustic Ales - A brewery that offers a wide range of IPAs, ales, and lagers that are freshly brewed on site.
  • Wine from Nicholson Vineyards - A winery that offers tours and tastings, which includes a variety of wines produced from grapes grown on their estate.

Where to Stay in Aptos: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several options for accommodations in and around Aptos that will fit your budget and preferences. Some of the recommended ones are:

  • Seascape Beach Resort - Located on the picturesque Monterey Bay, this beachfront resort provides world-class amenities for a luxurious stay.
  • Rio Sands Hotel - A mid-range hotel that offers comfortable rooms, outdoor pool, and a hot tub.
  • Santa Cruz/Monterey KOA - A campground, recommended for camping enthusiasts, which offers RV sites, tent sites, and cabins.

What to Buy in Aptos: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs and local products to take back home, Aptos has several options to choose from. Here are some recommendations:

  • Jewelry from Seacliff Village Artisans - The perfect place to shop for locally made and unique pieces of jewelry.
  • Wine from Nicholson Vineyards - Bring home a bottle of locally produced wine from Nicholson Vineyards as a gift for friends and family.
  • Beach items from Seascape Beach Resort - The gift shop at Beach Resort offers a variety of beach items, including sun hats, beach towels, and more.

Local Culture and Customs of Aptos

Aptos residents are known for their warm and welcoming nature. The locals take great pride in their town’s natural beauty and history. It’s important to respect the environment and preserve the natural heritage of Aptos.

Best Time to Visit Aptos

Aptos is beautiful all year round. It’s a popular destination in the summer months; however, visitors can enjoy the town’s charm in the spring and fall shoulder seasons. Keep in mind that rainy season begins in November and lasts through April.

Do’s and Don’ts of Aptos

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting Aptos:

Do:

  • Do bring sunscreen/hat/sunglasses - The California sun can be bright and intense, so it’s essential to protect your skin and eyes while outdoors.
  • Do observe local driving laws - Aptos, like many parts of California, has strict traffic laws, so be sure to always wear your seatbelt and follow the speed limits.
  • Do respect local wildlife - Aptos has a range of wildlife, including deer, coyotes, and sea otters. Always observe them from a distance and don’t approach or feed them.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter - Keep the environment clean by disposing of trash and recyclables properly.
  • Don’t disturb the natural environment - Refrain from removing natural objects from parks or beaches.
  • Don’t underestimate the power of the ocean - Always obey beach signs and warnings

Safety and Health

Safety in Aptos

Aptos is a relatively safe town; however, visitors should always take precautions to ensure their safety:

  • Be aware of your surroundings - Stay alert in crowded public places and shady neighborhoods.
  • Lock your car and hotel room doors - Always lock your belongings while you’re away.
  • Be careful while driving - Follow traffic laws and stay alert while driving on local roads and highways.

Health in Aptos

Aptos boasts a modern and sophisticated healthcare system. In case of emergency, call 911 or go to the nearest emergency room.

Getting to Aptos: Transportation Options and Logistics

Aptos is accessible by several transportation options. Here are some important details to keep in mind:

  • Airports Near Aptos - Norman Y. Mineta San Jose International Airport (SJC) is the nearest airport to Aptos, located approximately 45 miles away. San Francisco International Airport (SFO) is another popular choice, located approximately 78 miles away.
  • Bus Stations Near Aptos - The nearest bus station to Aptos is the Santa Cruz Metro Bus Station, located approximately 8 miles away.
  • Other Options - Private shuttle services, taxis, and rental cars are also available for travelers to get to and from Aptos.

How to Get Around in Aptos

It’s relatively easy to get around Aptos, with several transportation options:

  • Biking - Biking is a popular way to get around Aptos, and several bike rental shops offer a variety of bike choices for visitors.
  • Driving - Renting a car is an easy and flexible way to explore Aptos, with rental car agencies available on-site at the airports or town center.
  • Public Transportation - Santa Cruz Metro Buses offer public transportation options around Aptos with convenient stops throughout the town.

Where to Go Next from Aptos: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Aptos’ location offers numerous options for day trips and excursions:

  • Santa Cruz - Just a few miles from downtown Aptos, Santa Cruz offers an encompassing beach and boardwalk experience along with plenty of bars, cafes, and restaurants to explore.
  • Monterey - Located approximately 30 miles from Aptos, Monterey offers stunning ocean views, and an abundance of marine attractions such as the Monterey Bay Aquarium.
  • Carmel - A scenic 45-minute drive from downtown Aptos, Carmel offers historic architecture, boutique shopping, and wineries.

Conclusion

Aptos is a gem of central California, that offers a combination of outdoor adventures, natural beauty, and rich history.
