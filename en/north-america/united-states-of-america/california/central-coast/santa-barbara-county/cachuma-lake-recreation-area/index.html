Cachuma Lake Recreation Area is an oasis of natural beauty in Southern California, with stunning landscapes, exciting outdoor activities, and rich cultural heritage. Introduction to Cachuma Lake Recreation Area

Cachuma Lake Recreation Area is located in the central coast of California, around 20 miles north of Santa Barbara. The area is spread over 9,000 acres and is a popular destination for camping, hiking, boating, fishing, wildlife watching, and picnicking. The lake is surrounded by the Santa Ynez Mountains and offers breathtaking views and natural beauty.
