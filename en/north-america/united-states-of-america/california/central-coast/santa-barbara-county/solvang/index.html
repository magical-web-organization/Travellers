Introduction to Solvang

Nestled in Santa Ynez Valley, Solvang is a charming Danish village in the heart of Central Coast, California. Its picturesque architecture, quaint shops, and delicious pastries attract tourists from all around the world. Founded by a group of Danish-Americans in 1911, Solvang has proudly preserved its Danish heritage and culture. Visitors can explore its museums, historical landmarks, and outdoor activities to get a taste of the Danish experience. Here is your comprehensive travel guide to Solvang.

List of Places to Visit in Solvang

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Solvang is home to several museums and historical sites that provide an insight into its Danish heritage and culture. Visit:

  • Elverhøj Museum of History and Art: Housed in a historic Danish-style building, this museum showcases the history and culture of Solvang through art exhibits and artifacts.
  • The Copenhagen House: This authentic Danish-style house was built in 1936 and is now a museum that features Danish furnishing, clothing, and souvenirs.
  • Old Mission Santa Ines: This 1804 mission is a beautiful example of Spanish mission architecture and has been an important part of the Solvang community for over 200 years.

Natural Attractions of Solvang: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The natural beauty of Santa Ynez Valley provides ample opportunities for outdoor adventures and scenic views. Don’t miss these:

  • Solvang Vintage Motorcycle Museum: This destination is a must-see for every traveler interested in classic motorcycles. You can admire vintage models and learn about the history of motorcycle racing.
  • Solvang Park: This lovely park is the hub of community activities in Solvang, with BBQ facilities, playgrounds, and picnic areas.
  • OstrichLand USA: At OstrichLand, you’ll come face-to-face with the world’s largest birds. In addition to taking a tour of the ostrich farm, you can feed the ostriches and emus.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Solvang

Solvang offers many opportunities for hiking and biking in its scenic surroundings. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Santa Ynez River and Canyon Loop: This beautiful loop around the Santa Ynez River provides stunning views of the valley. The trail makes for an easy hike or a moderate bike ride.
  • Nojoqui Falls Trail: Only a few miles away from Solvang, this trail is a short but pleasant walk that leads to a stunning cascade of waterfalls. This hike is perfect for families with kids.
  • Aliso Canyon Trail: If you’re looking for a more strenuous hike or bike ride, this trail is a great option. The trail is challenging but rewarding with magnificent views of the valley.

Best Things to Do in Solvang: Activities and Tours

Solvang has plenty of activities and tours to offer visitors. Here are some of the best:

  • Solvang Trolley and Carriage Tours: Hop on a trolley or carriage tour and take a ride around historic Solvang while learning about its Danish heritage.
  • Wine Tours: Explore the Santa Ynez wine country with a guided wine tour, sampling its famous wines along the way.
  • Horseback Riding: Enjoy a scenic horseback ride through the countryside surrounding Solvang.

Week-Long Itinerary for Solvang

To get the most out of your Solvang vacation, here is a week-long itinerary that covers the highlights of the Danish village.

Day Itinerary
1 Arrive in Solvang and explore the village on foot. Have dinner at the Solvang Restaurant, where you can sample some Danish cuisine.
2 Visit the Elverhøj Museum of History and Art, followed by a walk through Solvang Park. Have lunch at Root 246, which offers farm-fresh cuisine.
3 Take a guided wine tour of Santa Ynez Valley and have a picnic lunch at one of the wineries.
4 Visit the Copenhagen House to learn about Danish culture. Later, take a scenic bike ride along the Aliso Canyon Trail.
5 Go horseback riding in the Santa Ynez Valley.
6 Visit Old Mission Santa Ines and then head to OstrichLand USA for a unique experience. Have lunch at the Hitching Post II, a popular steakhouse.
7 Spend the day at Jalama Beach, located just a short drive from Solvang. Return to Solvang in the evening for a farewell dinner at Cecco Ristorante.

Food and Drink Scene of Solvang

Solvang is known for its delightful Danish pastries and cuisine. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Aebleskiver: These traditional Danish pancakes are fluffy and delicious, served with raspberry jam and powdered sugar.
  • Frikadeller: These Danish meatballs are a comfort food staple and are served with gravy and mashed potatoes.
  • Smørrebrød: This open-faced sandwich is a classic Danish dish, consisting of rye bread, pickled herring, smoked salmon, or other cold cuts.

Local Beverages

  • Glogg: This spiced warm drink is a traditional winter holiday drink in Scandinavia. It is made with red wine, spices, and almonds, and is often served with raisins.
  • Beer: Solvang has a thriving microbrewery scene; trying some of its local beer is a must for beer enthusiasts.
  • Wine: Santa Ynez Valley is renowned for its wine. Sample some of its premium local wines with a vineyard tour.

Where to Stay in Solvang: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Solvang offers a wide range of accommodations to suit every budget. Here are some of the best options:

  • Royal Copenhagen Inn: This cozy inn features traditional Danish architecture and cozy rooms, and it is located in the heart of Solvang.
  • The Landsby: This upscale hotel offers a combination of Danish charm and modern amenities in the heart of the village.
  • Solvang Gardens Lodge: This guesthouse is surrounded by beautiful gardens and is located within easy walking distance of Solvang’s attractions, making it perfect for those on a budget.

What to Buy in Solvang: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Solvang is full of charming boutiques and specialty shops that offer unique souvenirs and local products to take home. Here are some of the best:

  • Solvang Antiques: This shop is a good bet for antique enthusiasts, who enjoy collectibles and vintage decor.
  • Rasmussen’s Danish Imports: This authentic shop sells many items imported from Denmark, including Danish clogs, woolen wear, candles, and jewelry.
  • Jule Hus: This lovely Christmas-themed shop offers unique ornaments, holiday decor, and hand-crafted gifts.

Local Culture and Customs of Solvang

Solvang’s heritage is reflected in its customs and culture. Some of the elements of Solvang’s local culture include:

  • Celebration of Danish Holidays: Solvang celebrates several Danish holidays throughout the year, including Julefest, the Danish version of Christmas, and Danish Days, an annual festival honoring Solvang’s Danish heritage.
  • Architecture: Solvang’s charming architecture is a reflection of its Danish heritage. Most of its buildings are designed in traditional Danish style, with winding streets and half-timbered homes.
  • Special Events: The Solvang Farmers Market happens every Wednesday afternoon and offers a chance to meet local farmers and enjoy fresh produce and local cuisine.

Best Time to Visit Solvang

Solvang is an ideal place to visit year-round, as it is known for its mild climate with little rainfall. The busiest times of the year in Solvang are during Christmas and summer. The summer months are perfect for outdoor activities, and the Christmas season offers a chance to enjoy festive lights and decorations.

Do’s and Don’ts of Solvang

Do:

  • Do try some Danish pastries or cuisine: The Danish pastries are delicious and are a must-try when visiting Solvang.
  • Do take a wine tour: The Santa Ynez Valley is a wine lover’s paradise; take a tour and sample the wine.
  • Do explore the Danish heritage: Solvang’s Danish heritage is an integral part of its culture and history, explore museums and historical landmarks to learn more.

Don’t:

  • Don’t skip a Danish pastry: Do not leave Solvang without trying at least one Danish pastry that will melt in your mouth.
  • Don’t forget your camera: The picturesque architecture and stunning natural beauty of Solvang are to be captured forever.
  • Don’t litter: Keep Solvang clean, pick up after yourselves and dispose of trash responsibly.

Safety and Health

Safety in Solvang

Solvang is a very safe and welcoming destination for tourists. However, maintaining vigilance and discretion is always recommended. Always pay attention to your surroundings and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Solvang

Solvang is a pretty safe and healthy destination with generally clean air and few health hazards. The region’s natural scenery attracts many hikers, so be sure to dress appropriately and bring sunscreen and plenty of water.

Getting to Solvang: Transportation Options and Logistics

Solvang is a great destination just a few miles north of Santa Barbara and easily accessible by car, bus, or plane. Here are some of the transportation options available to get to Solvang:

Airports Near Solvang

  • Santa Barbara Municipal Airport: Located just 40 miles south of Solvang, this is the closest airport with regular flights to major cities in the US.
  • Los Angeles International Airport: As the largest airport in Southern California, LAX and Solvang are separated by about 130 miles, and it takes about 2.5 hours to commute.

Bus Stations Near Solvang

  • Solvang-Santa Ynez Valley Transit: This reliable transit company provides local and express bus service throughout the area.
  • Santa Barbara Metropolitan Transit District: This public transit system serves the Santa Barbara Metropolitan area connects to Solvang.

Other Options

  • Car Rentals: A great way to explore the area on your own schedule, rental cars are available at airports or at one of the nearby car rental offices.
  • Biking: If you’re already nearby and seeking a little exercise, the town centre is only six blocks away, and biking is an excellent alternative way to visit all the great sites of Solvang.

How to Get Around in Solvang

Solvang is a charming village that is easy to explore. You can rent a bike or stroll through the village along the streets to explore its shops, museums and restaurants. Alternatively, several bike rental companies provide access to bicycles for taking a day trip out of town. You can also take local transportation through Solvang-Santa Ynez Valley Transit or opt to have a car rental to explore the greater Santa Barbara area.

Where to Go Next from Solvang: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Solvang is conveniently located near several exciting destinations in the Central Coast region of California, including:

  • Santa Barbara: Just 30 minutes by car from Solvang, this beautiful coastal city offers beautiful beaches, historic sites, and upscale shopping.
  • Los Olivos: This charming wine town is just eight miles northwest of Solvang, and it’s one of the best places to explore the local wine country.
  • Pismo Beach: This quaint beach town is just an hour drive from Solvang and is known for its scenic coastline, endless beaches, and unique wildlife.

Conclusion

Solvang is a charming Danish village that offers something for everyone. Whether you’re interested in history, outdoor activities, or culinary delights, Solvang is sure to capture your heart. Plan your trip to Solvang with this comprehensive travel guide, exploring its museums, natural attractions, delicious cuisine and surrounding area for an unforgettable vacation.
