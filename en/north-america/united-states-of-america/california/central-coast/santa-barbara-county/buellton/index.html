Introduction to Buellton

Buellton, located in the Santa Barbara County of California, is a charming city that boasts of its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and delicious food and beverage scene. Founded in 1920, the city has come a long way to be recognized as a popular tourist destination today. Whether you’re a solo traveler, a family with kids, or a group of friends, there’s something for everyone in Buellton.

List of Places to Visit in Buellton

Buellton has something for everyone. Here’s a list of must-visit places in Buellton:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Mendenhall’s Museum of Gasoline Pumps & Petroliana: Explore the largest private petroleum museum in the world with rare vintage gas pumps, road maps, oil cans, and petroliana collectibles.
  • Elverhoj Museum of History and Art: Learn about the Danish culture and art with this museum’s exhibits, events, and workshops.
  • Old Mission Santa Ines: Visit the historical 19th-century mission-style church to appreciate the architectural beauty and learn about local history.

Natural Attractions of Buellton: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ostrichland USA: Get up close to exotic ostriches and emus and feed them in this wildlife sanctuary.
  • Lake Cachuma: Enjoy fishing, boating, kayaking, and camping amidst the stunning natural beauty of this serene lake.
  • Hike and Bike in Ballard Canyon: Explore the picturesque hills and vineyards of Ballard Canyon with various hiking and biking trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Buellton

Buellton offers several amazing hiking and biking trails. Here are a few of them:

  • Nojoqui Falls Trail: A 2.5-mile hike through scenic oak groves, leading to a mesmerizing waterfall.
  • Pioneer Park Trail: A 4.9-mile hike with breathtaking views of the Santa Ynez Valley and the Gaviota Coast.
  • Buellton Bike Loop: A 5-mile beginner-friendly road bike loop through the city.

Best Things to Do in Buellton: Activities and Tours

Here are some exciting things to do in Buellton:

  • Wine and Beer Tastings: Buellton is known for its wineries and breweries. Try local favorites like Alma Rosa Winery, Margerum Wine Company, Firestone Walker Brewing Company, and Figueroa Mountain Brewing Co.
  • Golfing at Zaca Creek Golf Course: Enjoy playing golf at one of the most picturesque golf courses in California.
  • Horseback Riding at Vino Vaqueros: Explore the stunning vineyards of Santa Barbara County on horseback.
  • Skydiving at Skydive Santa Barbara: Experience the thrill of skydiving with scenic views of the Pacific coastline.

Week-Long Itinerary for Buellton

Here’s a sample itinerary for a week-long trip to Buellton:

Day Activities
1 Visit Mendenhall’s Museum of Gasoline Pumps & Petroliana, Elverhoj Museum of History and Art, and dine at Industrial Eats.
2 Explore Old Mission Santa Ines and enjoy wine and beer tasting at local wineries and breweries.

Food and Drink Scene of Buellton

Buellton offers a variety of dining options with local specialties and international cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Santa Maria-Style BBQ: Delicious barbecued meat, usually tri-tip grilled over red oak wood, served with pinquito beans, salad, and salsa.
  • Artisan Pizza at Industrial Eats: Try unique pizza toppings made with local ingredients and traditional, naturally leavened dough.
  • Fish and Chips at The Habit: Enjoy crisp and flaky fish and chips at this popular fast-food joint.

Local Beverages

  • Wine: Buellton is located in the Santa Ynez Valley, a renowned wine region that produces high-quality wines. Try local wines like Pinot Noir, Chardonnay, and Syrah.
  • Beer: Buellton is home to many award-winning breweries like Firestone Walker Brewing Company, Figueroa Mountain Brewing Co, and Solvang Brewing Company.

Where to Stay in Buellton: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Buellton offers many accommodation options for all types of travelers, such as:

  • Flying Flags RV Resort and Campground: Offers campgrounds, RV sites, and luxury cottages.
  • Hampton Inn & Suites Buellton/Santa Ynez Valley: A comfortable hotel with free Wi-Fi and breakfast, located near popular attractions.
  • Pacifica Suites: A luxury hotel with spacious suites, a pool, and a fitness center, located 20 minutes away from Buellton.

What to Buy in Buellton: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Remember your trip by getting these unique souvenirs and locally made products:

  • Ostrich Feather Duster: Get an ostrich feather duster from Ostrichland USA.
  • Santa Barbara Wine: Bring back a bottle or two of local wine from wineries like Alma Rosa Winery or Margerum Wine Company.
  • Danish Pastries: Indulge in delicious Danish pastries and bread from Mortensen’s Danish Bakery.

Local Culture and Customs of Buellton

Buellton is known for its Danish heritage and is often called the ‘Danish Capital of the USA.’ It celebrates its culture and traditions with events like Solvang Danish Days, Julefest, and Taste of Solvang. The city’s central location makes it perfect for a relaxing getaway with beautiful scenery and a laid-back lifestyle.

Best Time to Visit Buellton

The best time to visit Buellton is during the fall (September to November) or spring (March to May) when the weather is mild, and the tourist crowds are low. Summer can get hot and crowded, and winter can be wet and cold.

Do’s and Don’ts of Buellton

Here are some Do’s and Don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Buellton:

Do:

  • Try local wines and beers.
  • Visit historical places and museums to learn about the culture and heritage of the city.
  • Explore the hiking and biking trails.

Don’t:

  • Litter on the trails or in public places.
  • Ignore traffic signals while driving or cycling.
  • Disturb the wildlife in natural areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Buellton

Buellton is a relatively safe city with low crime rates. However, it’s always a good idea to take necessary precautions like locking your car and valuables, being aware of your surroundings, and carrying a map when exploring hiking trails.

Health in Buellton

Buellton has adequate healthcare facilities and access to emergency services. However, do carry necessary medication and insurance details with you, as a precaution.

Getting to Buellton: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Buellton

  • Santa Barbara Municipal Airport (SBA) - 31 miles away
  • San Luis Obispo County Regional Airport (SBP) - 57 miles away

Bus Stations Near Buellton

  • Amtrak Station - 7 miles away
  • Goleta Amtrak Station - 22 miles away

Other Options

  • Car rentals: Hertz, Avis, and Enterprise all have locations near Buellton.
  • Ride-Sharing Apps: Uber and Lyft operate in Buellton.

How to Get Around in Buellton

Renting a car is the best option to get around Buellton. Bike rentals are also available from many of the local stores and businesses, there is also a bus system the Easy Lift in the Santa Ynez Valley that connects the greater valley area.

Where to Go Next from Buellton: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Santa Barbara: A beautiful coastal town, 30 minutes south of Buellton, with beaches, parks, museums, and shopping.
  • Solvang: A quaint Danish-style village famous for its unique architecture, bakeries, and shops, located just 5 miles from Buellton.
  • San Luis Obispo: A charming college town with a lively downtown, hiking trails, and outdoor activities, located about 1.5 hours away from Buellton.

Conclusion

Buellton is a traveler’s paradise that offers a mix of cultural heritage, outdoor activities, delicious food and drink scene, and scenic beauty. Whether you’re a history buff, an adventure seeker, or a foodie, there’s something for everyone in Buellton. Use this comprehensive guide to plan your perfect trip to this charming city in California’s Central Coast.
