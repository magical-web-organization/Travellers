Introduction to La Habra

Located in Orange County, Southern California, La Habra is a thriving city known for its diverse culture, rich history, and vibrant community. This city has something for everyone, from outdoor adventures to culturallly enriching experiences. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you through the best things to do in La Habra, where to stay, what to eat, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in La Habra

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

La Habra Depot Theatre

Known as the jewel of La Habra, this historic railway depot has been transformed into a theatre that showcases musicals, plays, and other live performances. The theatre also has a variety of educational workshops and summer camps that cater to all ages.

La Habra Historical Museum

Managed by the La Habra Historical Society, this museum educates visitors about the history of La Habra through its exhibits and artifacts. Visiting the museum will give you a glimpse into the storied past of La Habra.

Our Lady of Guadalupe Shrine

Built in 1771, this historic chapel is dedicated to the Virgin Mary and stands as a testament to the religious history of the city.

Natural Attractions of La Habra: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

La Bonita Park

A popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts, this park offers a wide range of outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and picnicking. The park also has playgrounds, sports fields, and a beautiful lake.

Hacienda Hills Trail

For a more challenging hike, head to Hacienda Hills Trail, which offers a 2.4-mile round trip with stunning views of the city. The trail is ideal for hiking, biking, and even horseback riding.

Clark Regional Park

If you’re looking for a place to relax and enjoy nature, then Clark Regional Park is perfect for you. This park has picnic areas, playgrounds, and a scenic nature trail that’s perfect for a leisurely stroll.

Hiking/Biking Routes of La Habra

Hacienda Hills Trail

As mentioned earlier, Hacienda Hills Trail provides a challenging hike and biking experience that’s worth the effort. You’ll find yourself treated to amazing views of the city and wildlife along the way.

La Habra Loop Trail

This is a shorter and easier 1.3-mile trail loop that that’s great for taking in the beautiful scenery of the park while getting some exercise.

Murphy Ranch Trail

A hidden gem in La Habra, Murphy Ranch Trail, is 6 miles round trip. With moderate difficulty, this trail is perfect for experienced hikers who enjoy a challenging workout.

Best Things to Do in La Habra: Activities and Tours

La Habra Children’s Museum

Targeted at children from 1 to 10 years old, the museum offers a range of interactive exhibits that will engage and educate young minds. The museum features a dinosaur exhibit, art studio, and interactive play areas.

La Habra Theatre Guild

For a night of musical entertainment, head to the La Habra Theatre Guild. This community theatre group brings productions of Broadway musicals, plays, and more to the La Habra community.

Farmers Market

The La Habra Farmers Market is held every Thursday evening during the summer season. With local vendors selling fresh produce, baked goods, and much more, it’s an excellent way to explore the local cuisine and meet the friendly people of La Habra.

Food and Drink Scene of La Habra

Must-Try Dishes

Lomo Saltado

A Peruvian dish made with beef, onions, tomatoes, and potato fries.

Caldo de Res

A hearty beef soup packed with vegetables and served piping hot.

Pho

A Vietnamese noodle soup served with a variety of herbs and optional meats.

Local Beverages

Horchata

A sweet milky drink made from ground almonds, rice or chufas.

Agua Fresca

A refreshing beverage made by blending fruit and water for a light and sweet taste.

Where to Stay in La Habra: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

La Habra Motel

Located near La Bonita Park, this motel offers simple, clean, and affordable accommodation. It’s perfect for the budget-conscious traveler.

Holiday Inn La Mirada

Located just a few minutes away from La Habra, this lodging has a heated outdoor pool, fitness center, and a restaurant.

Embassy Suites by Hilton Brea

This luxurious hotel is the perfect place for those looking for a comfortable and relaxing stay in La Habra. It features an onsite restaurant, indoor swimming pool, and a fitness center.

What to Buy in La Habra: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Ceramics

The city is known for its colorful pottery, and there are many shops in the area where you can pick up handmade ceramics.

Avocado Products

La Habra has a lot of avocado farms, so there are a variety of avocado-based products available, including guacamole, avocado oil, and soap.

Local Culture and Customs of La Habra

The culture of La Habra is a vibrant blend of Hispanic and American traditions. Spanish is spoken by a significant portion of the population. Visitors are encouraged to respect local customs and traditions, particularly when it comes to dress attire within certain religious venues.

Best Time to Visit La Habra

The months of March to May and September to November are the ideal times to visit La Habra. During these times, the weather is cool, and the crowds are smaller in number.

Do’s and Don’ts of La Habra

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Dress appropriately when visiting religious venues.
  • Visit the local farmers’ market.
  • Explore the parks and hiking trails.

Don’t:

  • Litter the parks and public areas.
  • Leave any valuable items visible in your car when parking on the streets.

Safety and Health

Safety in La Habra

La Habra is a relatively safe city. However, visitors are advised to stay alert and aware of their surroundings, especially during the night. It is also advisable to lock your car and keep valuable items out of sight.

Health in La Habra

For medical services, head to the St. Jude Medical Centre, which provides a comprehensive range of medical services including emergency room care, mental health services, and more.

Getting to La Habra: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near La Habra

John Wayne Airport

Located in Santa Ana, John Wayne Airport is the closest airport to La Habra. It’s about a 30-minute drive to La Habra.

Los Angeles International Airport

Located in Los Angeles, LAX is about an hour’s drive from La Habra.

Bus Stations Near La Habra

Fullerton Transportation Center

The Fullerton Transportation Center is the closest bus station to La Habra, and many bus routes stop at this station.

Other Options

Taxis and ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft are widely available in La Habra.

How to Get Around in La Habra

The most convenient way to get around La Habra is by car. Rental cars are available at both John Wayne Airport and Los Angeles International Airport. Another option is to use ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft, which are widely available in La Habra.

Where to Go Next from La Habra: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Los Angeles

Just 30 minutes from La Habra, Los Angeles offers endless entertainment options. It is home to iconic landmarks such as the Hollywood Walk of Fame, Universal Studios, and the Getty Center.

Knott’s Berry Farm

Located in Buena Park, Knott’s Berry Farm is an amusement park that features rollercoasters, water rides, and other fun activities for all ages.

Conclusion

Whether you’re interested in immersing yourself in La Habra’s history, tackling challenging hikes, or enjoying the local cuisine, La Habra has something for everyone. This comprehensive travel guide provides all the information you need to make your travels to La Habra a memorable experience.
