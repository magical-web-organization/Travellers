Introduction to Balboa Park-Hillcrest

Balboa Park-Hillcrest is a vibrant neighborhood located in the heart of San Diego, known for its historic landmarks, cultural attractions, and scenic views. This area encompasses the famous Balboa Park, which boasts a diverse range of museums, gardens, and performance venues, as well as the bustling Hillcrest neighborhood, renowned for its eclectic dining scene and LGBTQ+ community. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned local, there’s always something new to explore in Balboa Park-Hillcrest.
