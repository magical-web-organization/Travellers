Introduction to Wilshire

Wilshire is a well-known and bustling district in the western part of Los Angeles. It’s a hub for business, entertainment, and culture that features luxury high-rise buildings, shopping districts, and beautiful neighborhoods.

Wilshire boasts a thriving arts and culture scene, numerous museums, outdoor activities, and delicious food options. The district is home to some of the most prominent landmarks, exclusive shops, museums, and fine-dining restaurants.

If you’re looking for a mix of luxury, culture, and entertainment, Wilshire is the perfect destination. Here’s a guide to help plan and make the most of your trip.

Best Things to Do in Wilshire: Activities and Tours

  • Enjoy a scenic drive on Mulholland Drive.
  • Explore the city on a bike tour.
  • Go on a food tour and taste the diverse cuisines of Wilshire.
  • Take a walking tour through the city and learn about its history and culture.
  • Attend a live performance at The Mark Taper Forum or the Ahmanson Theatre.

Week-Long Itinerary for Wilshire

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit the Getty Center and explore the beautiful gardens and artwork.

Day 6 Take a bike tour of the city and explore the streets and neighborhoods of Wilshire.

What to Buy in Wilshire: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • The Getty Store: presents all types of souvenirs, books, and art pieces.
  • Surfas Culinary District: Explore the store for cooking elements and souvenirs to carry back home.
  • The Beverly Hills Farmer’s Market: buy some organic fruits, vegetables, jams, and other locally made products.
  • Ladurée Bakery: indulge in some of its world-famous macarons as a tasty souvenir of your trip.

Local Culture and Customs of Wilshire

  • Respect cultural differences: Wilshire is a diverse and multicultural place, so respect other cultures and customs.
  • Dress appropriately: Wilshire is known for its upscale and fancy neighborhoods, so dress appropriately when visiting.
  • Tipping: Tipping is a common custom in Wilshire. 18-20% of the total bill is the accepted range for tipping in restaurants, cafes, and bars.

Best Time to Visit Wilshire

The best time to visit Wilshire is during the months of March to May and September to November. The weather is mild, and the crowds are less compared to the peak summer season.

Do’s and Don’ts of Wilshire

Do:

  • Respect the local customs, culture, and traditions.
  • Explore the diverse, upscale neighborhoods of Wilshire.
  • Take a bike or walking tour of the city.
  • Taste the diverse and delicious cuisines.
  • Visit the city’s museums and art galleries.
  • Shop for souvenirs and local products.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in the public areas of the city.
  • Don’t engage in any illegal activities.
  • Don’t carry or use any drugs.
  • Don’t miss the natural attractions of Wilshire.

Safety and Health

Safety in Wilshire

Wilshire is considered one of the safest neighborhoods in Los Angeles. However, it is always recommended to keep your belongings safe and secure while walking around the city.

Health in Wilshire

Wilshire is a developed area with easy access to medical facilities and hospitals. The tap water is safe to drink, and there are no major health concerns.
