Food and Drink Scene of Hollywood

Hollywood is a diverse place, and that diversity is reflected in its cuisine. Here are some dishes and drinks that you should try out during your visit.

Must-Try Dishes

  • In-N-Out Burger - A Southern Californian institution
  • The Original Farmer’s Market - A great spot to get tasty meals and local craft beer.
  • Thai Town - The place for some authentic Thai food in Northern Hollywood.

Local Beverages

  • California Wines - An excellent variety of white and red wines
  • IPA Beer - An extensive collection of Indian Pale Ales
  • Sunset Boulevard Cocktail - A delicious mixture of Vodka, Grapefruit, and Campari

Where to Stay in Hollywood: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hollywood has several accommodation options catering to various budgets. Here are our picks for the best places to stay:

  • The Hollywood Roosevelt - A luxuriuos 1920s era landmark hotel in Hollywood
  • Freehand Los Angeles - A trendy hostel with affordable rates and located in the heart of Hollywood
  • Mama Shelter - A fun and quirky boutique hotel with a remarkable rooftop bar overlooking Los Angeles

What to Buy in Hollywood: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Hollywood offers an extensive collection of goods, from vintage clothing to unique souvenirs. Here are some quirky items that you can buy to take home with you:

  • Vintage Vinyl Records
  • Local Art - A great way to bring home some local flair
  • Movie Props and Memorabilia - Take home a piece of Hollywood history

Local Culture and Customs of Hollywood

Hollywood is a diverse place, and its liberal culture encourages people from all walks of life to live the life they love. Here are a few things to keep in mind when you’re visiting:

  • Acceptance - Accepting people for the way they are, Hollywood encourages people to be themselves.
  • Open Mindedness - Hollywood is an open-minded place, home to all beliefs, religions, and ethnicities.
  • Movie-Going Culture - Movies are synonymous with Hollywood culture.

Best Time to Visit Hollywood

The best time to visit Hollywood is during the fall and winter months, specifically from November to February, when the weather is pleasant, and the crowds have thinned out. Avoid visiting during the summer months and holidays when Hollywood is too crowded.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hollywood

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your visit:

Do:

  • Go on a Hike - Hollywood has some beautiful hiking trails that offer fantastic views of the city.
  • Visit the Studios - Visit the famed studios, such as Paramount Studios and Warner Bros, to see the movie-making process.
  • People watch in Hollywood and Sunset Boulevard - Hollywood is a hub for people watching and spotting celebrities.

Don’t:

  • Don’t Trust Street Parking - Hollywood is notorious for its high tow-away zone, and it’s best to find a parking lot or garage.
  • Don’t Expect to See Celebrities All the Time - You may spot a celebrity by chance, but don’t make it the sole purpose of your visit.
  • Don’t Pick Fights in the Nightclubs or Bars - Hollywood is renowned for its nightlife, and things can get rowdy inside the nightclubs or bars.

Safety and Health

Here are a few things you should be aware of regarding safety and health in Hollywood:

Safety in Hollywood

  • Traffic - Hollywood is frequently congested, so it’s best to plan your journey accordingly.
  • Street Crime - Petty theft and street crime are common in Hollywood. Be vigilant while walking through unsafely-lit areas and alleys.
  • Earthquakes - California is prone to earthquakes. Make sure you’re familiar with earthquake safety practices.

Health in Hollywood

  • Sunburn and Heat Stroke - Hollywood gets very warm during the day and can cause sunburns, dehydration, and heatstroke.
  • Smog - Pollution can be high during the summer months. Those with respiratory issues should be extra cautious.

Getting to Hollywood: Transportation Options and Logistics

Hollywood is well-connected to various transportation options, including bus, train, car, and air. Here are some of the options you can use to get to Hollywood.

Airports Near Hollywood

  • Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) - The nearest major airport is a 30-minute drive from Hollywood.
  • Burbank Bob Hope Airport - Burbank Bob Hope Airport is located 10 miles from Hollywood.

Bus Stations Near Hollywood

  • Greyhound Bus Station - The Greyhound Bus Station is located East LA, and buses arrive from various destinations throughout the United States.
  • Union Station - Union Station is the primary transportation hub in the city, particularly for trains.

Other Options

  • Rental Cars - There are many car rental agencies available throughout Hollywood and Los Angeles.
  • Taxi/Uber/Lyft - Several taxis, rideshare, and car services are available throughout the city.

How to Get Around Hollywood

Hollywood is primarily a pedestrian-friendly city, and you can quickly navigate around the city on foot. Here are some other transportation options:

  • Bus Routes - The bus system in Hollywood is extensive, with buses available to neighbouring cities.
  • Metro Rail - The Metro Rail offers an efficient and fast way of getting around the city.
  • Taxis, Uber/Lyft, and Rental Bikes - Taxis and ride-sharing services are always available. You can also rent bicycles from various locations around the city.

Where to Go Next from Hollywood: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Hollywood’s proximity to other destinations makes it an ideal starting point for your next adventure. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions you can embark on:

  • Disneyland: The happiest place on earth, located an hour from Hollywood in Anaheim.
  • Santa Barbara: A two-hour drive from Hollywood, Santa Barbara is known for its beaches and wineries.
  • Palm Springs: A two-hour drive from Hollywood, Palm Springs is renowned for its hot springs and resorts.
  • San Diego: A two-hour drive from Hollywood, visit San Diego for the famous San Diego Zoo, beaches, and parks.

Conclusion

Hollywood is a vibrant destination that offers a blend of natural beauty, cultural history, exciting attractions, and culinary experiences. When you plan your trip, ensure you have the above information in mind to make the most of your visit to Hollywood.
