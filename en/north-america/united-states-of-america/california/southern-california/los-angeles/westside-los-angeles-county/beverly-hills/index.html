A Comprehensive Guide to Beverly Hills

Located in Los Angeles, Beverly Hills is a renowned tourist destination filled with attractions, scenic views, and cultural landmarks. It is also home to celebrity residents, high-end shopping, and luxurious accommodations. In this travel guide, we will provide a detailed overview of everything you need to know to plan your trip to this glamorous city.

List of Places to Visit in Beverly Hills

Beverly Hills offers a wide range of attractions to explore, including:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Getty Center: A high-end museum with impressive architecture and stunning gardens that feature a vast selection of art, ranging from medieval manuscripts to contemporary photography.
  • The Paley Center for Media: This center houses a vast collection of television and radio broadcasts, including classic TV shows, movies, and commercials from the past and present.
  • Beverly Hills City Hall: This landmark building is home to the city government and has recently undergone a complete restoration to reveal its original 1930s architecture.

Natural Attractions of Beverly Hills: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Will Rogers Memorial Park: This beautiful park honors the famous cowboy humorist Will Rogers, who used to spend time here with his family. It offers a playground, picnic area, and well-manicured gardens.
  • Beverly Hills Dog Park: This park offers ample space for dogs to run, play, and socialize with other furry friends. It is equipped with drinking stations and poop bags for pet owners to use.
  • Coldwater Canyon Park: This park is an excellent spot for hiking enthusiasts that offers scenic views of the city, valleys, and surrounding mountains.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Beverly Hills

Beverly Hills offers some of Southern California’s most scenic hiking and biking trails, including:

  • Franklin Canyon Park: This hidden gem in Beverly Hills offers an easy hiking trail that is great for children and people who are looking for a leisurely hike.
  • Rodeo Drive: A popular spot for tourists who want to experience Beverly Hills’ high-end shopping and glamorous atmosphere while taking a leisurely stroll.
  • The Beverly Hills Gateway: This hiking and biking trail is located in the Santa Monica Mountains and offers breathtaking views of the city and the ocean.

Best Things to Do in Beverly Hills: Activities and Tours

Beverly Hills offers many exciting activities and guided tours to experience, including:

  • Beverly Hills Trolley Tour: This entertaining tour takes visitors around the city and covers interesting landmarks and celebrity homes.
  • Celebrity Homes Tour: This tour allows visitors to experience the lives of Hollywood’s elite and see where they live and work.
  • Beverly Hills Walking Tour: A tour that allows visitors to explore Beverly Hills’s landmarks, such as Rodeo Drive, Beverly Hills City Hall, and the Beverly Hills Sign.

Food and Drink Scene of Beverly Hills

Beverly Hills is known for its high-end dining scene and offers a diverse range of cuisines and dining options. Some must-try dishes and local beverages include:

Must-Try Dishes

  • The Polo Lounge: Known for its signature dishes such as the McCarthy salad, lobster bisque, and grilled prime rib.
  • Il Pastaio: An Italian restaurant known for its homemade pasta, meatballs, and tiramisu.
  • Nic’s Beverly Hills: A sleek bar and restaurant offering a variety of American-style bar food such as burgers, hot dogs, and fries.

Local Beverages

  • The Beverly Hills Wine Merchant: A wine shop that specializes in selling rare and hard-to-find wines from around the world.
  • The Blvd at Beverly Wilshire: A bar that serves a range of cocktails and whiskey, champagne, and local craft beers.

Where to Stay in Beverly Hills: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Beverly Hills offers luxurious accommodation options, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Some popular options include:

  • The Beverly Hills Hotel: A glamorous hotel that maintains its traditional Hollywood glamour with decor featuring pink and green, and caters to celebrities and A-listers.
  • The Viceroy L’Ermitage Beverly Hills: A high-end hotel that offers luxury accommodation with breathtaking views of Los Angeles and the Hollywood Hills.
  • The Beverly Hills Hostel & Hotel: A budget-friendly hostel that offers dormitory-style rooms and private rooms with shared bathrooms.

What to Buy in Beverly Hills: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Beverly Hills offers many souvenir and shopping opportunities. Some of the best things to buy include Beverly Hills branded items, such as:

  • Beverly Hills Postcards: A classic souvenir that you can mail to your loved ones.
  • Beverly Hills T-Shirts: Available in various designs and styles and a great way to represent the city and its unique style.
  • Beverly Hills Keychains: A small, unique souvenir that is easy to carry and practical.

Local Culture and Customs of Beverly Hills

Beverly Hills is known for its glamorous lifestyle, high-end shopping, luxurious accommodations, and celebrity residents. As a tourist, it’s important to respect local customs and etiquette, such as:

  • Dressing well: Beverly Hills is known for its stylish locals, so be sure to dress appropriately when exploring the city’s trendy areas.
  • Respect celebrity privacy: Celebrities often live in Beverly Hills, so it’s essential to respect their privacy and avoid trespassing private property.

Best Time to Visit Beverly Hills

Beverly Hills is sunny and warm all year round, so you can visit at any time of the year. However, the best time to visit is during the peak season, from May to September, where the weather is perfect for outdoor exploration and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Beverly Hills

As a visitor, it’s important to follow some Do’s and Don’ts when in Beverly Hills, including:

Do:

  • Explore Rodeo Drive: A must-visit spot for shopping and exploring luxury boutiques and high-end designer stores.
  • Visit the Beverly Gardens Park: A great spot for a picnic, people-watching, and enjoying beautiful views of Beverly Hills.
  • Try the local food: From high-end dining to street food, Beverly Hills has something for everyone’s taste.

Don’t:

  • Litter or smoke in public areas: Beverly Hills is a clean and smoke-free city, so be sure to follow the local regulations.
  • Haggle at shops: In Beverly Hills, prices are usually fixed, so haggling is not a common practice.

Safety and Health

Safety in Beverly Hills

Beverly Hills is generally a safe city, but it’s important to take necessary precautions. Some safety tips include:

  • Avoid walking alone at night: Like any other city, walking alone at night is not recommended. It’s best to take designated transportation or a cab.
  • Stay alert: Always be aware of your surroundings and stay alert, especially in crowded areas.

Health in Beverly Hills

Beverly Hills offers excellent healthcare facilities, including hospitals, clinics, and pharmacies. If you need medical attention, some of the best hospitals in the area include:

  • Cedars-Sinai Medical Center: This hospital is renowned for its outstanding medical care and ranked among the top hospitals in the country.
  • UCLA Medical Center Santa Monica: A high-quality hospital that offers advanced medical treatment and personalized care.

Getting to Beverly Hills: Transportation Options and Logistics

Beverly Hills is easily accessible by various modes of transportation, including:

Airports Near Beverly Hills

  • Los Angeles International Airport (LAX): This is the largest airport in Southern California, located 16 miles away from Beverly Hills.
  • Burbank Bob Hope Airport (BUR): This airport is a smaller airport located 10 miles away from Beverly Hills and offers domestic flights.

Bus Stations Near Beverly Hills

  • Greyhound Bus Station: This bus station offers local, regional, and international bus transportation and is located 10 miles from Beverly Hills.

Other Options

  • Rental Cars: There are several rental car companies located in Los Angeles, which offer a convenient and comfortable way to explore Beverly Hills.
  • Taxis and Ride Share: Taxis and ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft are widely available in Beverly Hills and offer convenient and affordable transportation options.

How to Get Around in Beverly Hills

Beverly Hills is a small city that is easy to navigate by foot, taxi, or public transportation. Some options for getting around include:

  • Beverly Hills Trolley: A free trolley service that operates on weekends and holidays, covering the city’s major attractions and landmarks.
  • Bikes and Scooters: Beverly Hills offers several bike and scooter rental options that allow visitors to explore the city at their own pace.

Where to Go Next from Beverly Hills: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have some extra time, there are several exciting destinations and excursions you can explore nearby, including:

  • Universal Studios Hollywood: A fun-filled theme park that offers rides, shows, and attractions for visitors of all ages.
  • Santa Monica Beach: This beautiful beach spot is a great spot for sunbathing, swimming, and relaxing.
  • Venice Beach: A famous beach known for its vibrant boardwalk filled with street performers, shops, and restaurants.

Conclusion

Beverly Hills is an exciting and glamorous destination that offers a diverse range of attractions and activities for visitors of all ages. By following this comprehensive guide, you can plan your trip and make the most of your time in this beautiful city.
