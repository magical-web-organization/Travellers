Introduction to Manhattan Beach

Manhattan Beach is a coastal city located in South Bay, Los Angeles County, Southern California, United States of America. The city boasts of a long sandy beach, excellent surf, and an extended pier, making it one of the most sought-after travel destinations in California. There are also many opportunities to engage in outdoor activities, including surfing, beach volleyball, and bicycling, among others.

List of Places to Visit in Manhattan Beach

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Manhattan Beach Pier: The pier is a recreational landmark and serves as an iconic symbol of the city. It is perfect for fishing enthusiasts or taking a stroll to enjoy the ocean views.
  • Roundhouse Aquarium: This small aquarium located on the pier features marine life native to the Southern California area and has several interactive exhibits.
  • Comedy and Magic Club: The famous club hosts some of the biggest names in the comedy world, including Jay Leno and Jerry Seinfeld. It features a relaxed atmosphere and is perfect for an evening out with friends.

Natural Attractions of Manhattan Beach: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Manhattan Beach State Beach: The beach is a perfect place to soak up the sun and enjoy the cool breezes of the Pacific Ocean. It is also one of the top surfing destinations in the country.
  • Sand Dune Park: This park features a massive sand dune that is perfect for a quick hike or run. It is ideal for those looking for a quiet and peaceful retreat away from the hustle and bustle of the city.
  • The Strand: A pedestrian and bike path stretching along the beach, featuring breathtaking views of the ocean.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Manhattan Beach

  • Marvin Braude Bike Trail: This route runs along the coast from Torrance Beach to Will Rogers State Beach in Pacific Palisades. It features stunning views, and travelers can rent bikes in Manhattan Beach or bring their own.
  • Panorama Trail: This trail runs through the hills of Manhattan Beach and is perfect for a moderate hike or trail run. It is an excellent way to explore the natural beauty of the area.

Best Things to Do in Manhattan Beach: Activities and Tours

  • Surfing: Manhattan Beach is a surfing mecca, and there are many surf schools and rental shops where visitors can learn how to ride the waves.
  • Beach Volleyball: The beach has plenty of volleyball courts, and visitors can join in on a game with the locals.
  • Horseback Riding: There are several equestrian centers near Manhattan Beach that offer horseback riding on the beach.
  • Stand Up Paddleboarding: Visitors can rent stand up paddleboards and explore the nearby harbor and marina.

Food and Drink Scene of Manhattan Beach

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fish Tacos: A staple of California cuisine, fish tacos are a must-try when visiting Manhattan Beach. Many local restaurants offer this dish, but some of the best include Tin Roof Bistro and The Fish Bar.
  • Burgers: Manhattan Beach is home to several iconic burger joints, including The Shack and The Kettle.
  • Brunch: The city has many cafes and restaurants that serve brunch, but the local favorite is The Arthur J.

Local Beverages

  • Craft Beer: Manhattan Beach has several craft breweries, including The Dudes’ Brewing Company and Naja’s Place.
  • Wine: The city is known for its excellent selection of wine bars, including Barsha Wines and Spirits and The Bottle Inn.

Where to Stay in Manhattan Beach: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Belamar: This boutique hotel is located near the beach and offers a relaxed and comfortable atmosphere.
  • Wave Hotel: This budget-friendly hotel is perfect for travelers looking for affordable accommodations near the beach.
  • Beach House Hotel: This luxurious hotel overlooks the ocean and is perfect for a romantic getaway.

What to Buy in Manhattan Beach: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Surf Gear: Manhattan Beach is a surfing mecca, and visitors can find a wide selection of surf gear at local shops.
  • Beach Apparel: The city is known for its laidback beach lifestyle, and visitors can find a wide range of beachwear and apparel to take home.
  • Local Art: Many local artists showcase their work at the Manhattan Beach Art Center, and visitors can purchase unique art pieces and souvenirs to take home.

Local Culture and Customs of Manhattan Beach

Manhattan Beach has a relaxed and casual beach culture, and visitors should come prepared for the beach lifestyle. Local customs include a focus on sustainability, healthy living, and outdoor activities.

Best Time to Visit Manhattan Beach

The best time to visit Manhattan Beach is during the summer months from June to August when the weather is warm and sunny. However, the city has pleasant weather all year round, and visitors can enjoy outdoor activities and the beach throughout the year.

Do’s and Don’ts of Manhattan Beach

Do:

  • Try surfing: Manhattan Beach is one of the top surfing destinations in the country, and visitors should try their hand at surfing while in the city.
  • Explore the local cuisine: The city has an excellent selection of local cuisine, and visitors should try as many dishes as possible.
  • Respect the beach: Visitors should follow the local rules and regulations and make an effort to keep the beach clean and litter-free.

Don’t:

  • Leave valuables unattended on the beach: Visitors should not leave valuables unattended on the beach as thefts do occur.
  • Litter: Visitors should dispose of their trash properly and make an effort to keep the beach clean.

Safety and Health

Safety in Manhattan Beach

Manhattan Beach is a safe destination, and visitors should not have any concerns about their safety. However, visitors should always be aware of their surroundings and take standard safety precautions.

Health in Manhattan Beach

The city has excellent health facilities, and visitors should not have any health concerns. However, visitors should bring sunscreen and take standard precautions to prevent sunburn.

Getting to Manhattan Beach: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Manhattan Beach

  • Los Angeles International Airport: Located about six miles from Manhattan Beach, LAX is the closest major airport.

Bus Stations Near Manhattan Beach

  • Manhattan Beach Green Line Station: This station connects the city to the wider Los Angeles area.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Visitors can rent a car at LAX or in the city to explore the area.
  • Taxi/Uber/Lyft: Visitors can use taxi or ride-sharing services to get around the city.

How to Get Around in Manhattan Beach

Visitors can explore the city on foot, by bike, or by car. Renting a car or bike is a popular option as it allows visitors to explore the area at their own pace.

Where to Go Next from Manhattan Beach: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Redondo Beach: A neighboring city with a vibrant pier and beach clubs.
  • Hermosa Beach: Another nearby city known for its nightlife and beachside activities.
  • Los Angeles: The city is located just 20 miles from Manhattan Beach and is perfect for day trips to explore the city’s many attractions.

Conclusion

Manhattan Beach is a beautiful coastal city with plenty of outdoor activities, natural attractions, and delicious cuisine. It is an excellent travel destination for those looking to escape the city and enjoy a relaxing and laidback beach vacation. The city has a lot to offer, and visitors are sure to find something to suit their interests and preferences.
