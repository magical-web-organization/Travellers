Introduction to Two Harbors

Two Harbors is a charming village located on the western end of Santa Catalina Island, a little over 20 miles off the coast of Southern California. With minimal tourist traffic, Two Harbors boasts friendly locals and an untouched atmosphere.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Two Harbors

Two Harbors offers visitors a variety of scenic hiking and biking routes that take you through gorgeous valleys and offer incredible ocean views.

  • Trans-Catalina Trail: A challenging 37.2 mile hike that takes you from Avalon to Two Harbors. It can take 2-3 days to complete.
  • Two Harbors Loop: A moderate hike that covers 2.5 miles, with scenic views and ocean breezes.
  • Two Harbors Mountain Bike Trail: A 12-mile loop trail that takes you through canyons and unveils panoramic coastal views.

Local Culture and Customs of Two Harbors

Two Harbors is known for its friendly locals and laid back atmosphere. The locals are proud of their heritage and love sharing their knowledge of Santa Catalina Island’s rich history and secrets. Visitors are encouraged to respect the environment and leave it as they found it.

Best Time to Visit Two Harbors

The best time to visit Two Harbors is during the summer months between June and August. The weather is mild, the water is warm, and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Two Harbors

Do:

  • Respect the environment, wildlife, and locals
  • Explore the hiking trails and beaches
  • Take a guided tour of Catalina Island

Don’t:

  • Leave trash behind
  • Touch or disturb local wildlife
  • Ignore safety rules during water activities

Safety and Health

Safety in Two Harbors

Two Harbors is a safe destination, with little to no crime reported. Visitors should be mindful of their surroundings and keep an eye on their belongings.

Health in Two Harbors

Two Harbors has several medical facilities that can provide emergency care. Visitors should wear sunscreen, drink plenty of water, and bring a first aid kit when enjoying outdoor activities.

Getting to Two Harbors: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Two Harbors

The closest airport to Two Harbors is the Long Beach Airport, which is a 15-minute drive from the Catalina Island Express terminal.

Bus Stations Near Two Harbors

There are Greyhound and Amtrak stations in Long Beach that offer bus services to the Catalina Island Express terminal.

Other Options

Visitors can also arrive by private boat or helicopter. The Island Express helicopter provides a breathtaking scenic flight to the island from Long Beach and San Pedro.

How to Get Around in Two Harbors

Walking is the best way to explore Two Harbors. The village is small enough to navigate on foot, and there are scenic hiking trails and beaches within walking distance. Visitors can also rent bicycles or a golf cart for a fun and unique way to explore the island.

Conclusion

Two Harbors is an idyllic Southern Californian village that offers visitors a respite from the hustle and bustle of everyday life. With a culture rich in history and a natural landscape that is both pristine and breathtaking, Two Harbors should be on everyone’s travel list who is in search of a peaceful paradise.
