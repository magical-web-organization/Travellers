Introduction to Fish Camp

Fish Camp is a small, charming town located in Mariposa County, within the beautiful Sierra Nevada mountain range in California. It is a popular stopover for tourists exploring Yosemite National Park and its surrounding areas, as it is situated just a few miles south of the park. Fish Camp is an ideal base for outdoor enthusiasts looking to enjoy hiking, fishing, and other recreational activities, while also experiencing the local culture and history of the region.

List of Places to Visit in Fish Camp

Fish Camp offers a wide range of cultural and natural attractions that will appeal to visitors of all ages and interests. Here are some of the top places to visit in Fish Camp:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Yosemite Mountain Sugar Pine Railroad: A historic railroad that offers scenic tours through the Sierra National Forest.
  • Mariposa Museum and History Center: A local history museum that showcases the history of Mariposa County.
  • Wawona Hotel: A historic hotel that was built in 1876 and is now a National Historic Landmark.
  • Big Trees Lodge: A historic lodge that was built in 1916 and is now a National Historic Landmark.

Natural Attractions of Fish Camp: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Yosemite National Park: A world-renowned destination that offers breathtaking views and endless recreational opportunities.
  • Bass Lake: A scenic lake that is perfect for boating, swimming, and fishing.
  • Nelder Grove: A beautiful grove of giant sequoias that are over 2,000 years old.
  • Lewis Creek Trail: A hiking trail that offers stunning views of waterfalls and the Sierra Nevada mountain range.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fish Camp

Fish Camp offers a variety of hiking and biking trails that range in difficulty and length. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Fish Camp:

  • Yosemite Valley Loop Trail: A 13.7 mile loop trail that offers stunning views of Yosemite Valley.
  • Chilnualna Falls Trail: A 8.2 mile hiking trail that leads to the stunning Chilnualna Falls.
  • Glacier Point: A 4 mile hike that takes you to one of the best views in Yosemite National Park.
  • Mist Trail: A 3 mile trail that takes you to the top of Vernal Fall and Nevada Fall.

Best Things to Do in Fish Camp: Activities and Tours

Fish Camp offers a variety of activities and tours for visitors of all ages and interests. Here are some of the best things to do in Fish Camp:

  • Horseback Riding: Explore the beauty of the Sierra Nevada mountains on horseback with guided tours.
  • Zip Lining: Soar through the trees with guided zip line tours that offer breathtaking views of the forest.
  • Fishing: Fish for trout, bass, and other species in the clear waters of nearby lakes and streams.
  • Helicopter Tours: Enjoy a bird’s eye view of Yosemite National Park and the surrounding Sierra Nevada mountains.
