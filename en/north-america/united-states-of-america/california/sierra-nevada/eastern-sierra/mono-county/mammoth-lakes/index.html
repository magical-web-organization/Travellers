Introduction to Mammoth Lakes

Nestled amidst the stunning Sierra Nevada mountain range in Mono County, California, Mammoth Lakes is a year-round destination that offers a range of exciting outdoor activities, natural attractions, and cultural landmarks. From hiking and biking through breathtaking scenic routes to skiing and snowboarding on pristine slopes, Mammoth Lakes has something for everyone.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you through the best things to do, see, and explore in Mammoth Lakes. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, this guide will help you make the most of your trip to this beautiful destination.

Week-Long Itinerary for Mammoth Lakes

If you’re planning to spend a week in Mammoth Lakes, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrive and check into accommodation. Explore the unique shops and galleries in downtown Mammoth Lakes.
Day 2 Hike the Mammoth Crest Trail or Sherwin Lakes Trail. Visit the Mammoth Museum at Hayden Cabin to learn about the area’s history.
Day 3 Ski or snowboard in one of Mammoth Mountain’s famed ski resorts. Grab lunch at the on-mountain restaurants.
Day 4 Take a scenic drive to Mono Lake and explore the Mono Basin Scenic Area. Visit the South Tufa Reserve and take in the unique geology.
Day 5 Spend the day at Rainbow Falls or explore the Devils Postpile National Monument on foot.
Day 6 Take a guided wine tasting tour of the Eastern Sierra or go golfing at the Sierra Star Golf Course.
Day 7 Soak in the natural hot springs or go fishing in one of the many alpine lakes that surround Mammoth Lakes. Depart from Mammoth Lakes.

The best time to visit Mammoth Lakes depends on the type of trip you’re planning. Winter offers excellent skiing and snowboarding conditions, while summer is ideal for hiking, fishing, and other outdoor activities. The shoulder seasons, spring and fall, offer cooler temperatures and fewer crowds.

Safety and Health

Safety in Mammoth Lakes

Mammoth Lakes is generally a safe destination for tourists, but it’s important to take precautions to ensure your safety. Here are some safety tips to keep in mind while in Mammoth Lakes:

  • Stay hydrated and dress appropriately for the weather
  • Be prepared for sudden changes in weather conditions
  • Follow all safety warnings and guidelines at ski resorts
  • Always hike or bike with a buddy or let others know your intended route

Health in Mammoth Lakes

Mammoth Lakes enjoys clean air and clean water thanks to the pristine environment that surrounds it. However, you should always be careful when outdoors to avoid dehydration and sunburn.
