Introduction to Plumas County

Located in the Shasta Cascades region of California, Plumas County is a hidden gem that offers visitors a unique experience that showcases the beauty of Northern California. If you’re looking for peace and solitude, this is the perfect place to unwind and relax. Whether you’re an outdoor enthusiast, a history buff, or a foodie, Plumas County has something for everyone.

  3. Indian Valley Museum

  1. Lake Almanor

Hiking/Biking Routes of Plumas County

Plumas County is surrounded by natural beauty, with miles of hiking/biking routes. Whether you’re in the mood for a leisurely stroll or a challenging hike, Plumas County has it all. Some of the best hiking/biking routes in Plumas County include:

  1. Pacific Crest Trail
  2. Feather Falls Trail
  3. Lakes Basin Recreation Area Trails
  4. Middle Fork Feather River Trail

Best Things to Do in Plumas County: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the top activities and tours you can try in Plumas County:

  1. Whitewater Rafting on the Feather River
  2. Snowshoeing in Lassen Volcanic National Park
  3. Fly Fishing on Lake Almanor
  4. Hot Air Balloon Ride

Week-Long Itinerary for Plumas County

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Plumas County, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrive in Plumas County, check-in at your accommodation, explore Quincy
Day 2 Visit Indian Valley Museum, go for a hike at the Pacific Crest Trail or Middle Fork Feather River Trail
Day 3 Take a scenic drive to Bucks Lake, go fishing or kayaking
Day 4 Explore the historic town of Portola, visit the Western Pacific Railroad Museum
Day 5 Spend the day exploring Lassen Volcanic National Park, go for a snowshoeing tour
Day 6 Go on a hot air balloon ride, visit Lake Almanor
Day 7 Explore Plumas National Forest, go mountain biking on the Lakes Basin Recreation Area Trails

Local Culture and Customs of Plumas County

When visiting Plumas County, it’s important to understand and respect the local culture and customs. The area is known for its strong logging and mining culture, as well as its rich Native American heritage. Be sure to show respect for the land and its inhabitants.

Best Time to Visit Plumas County

The best time to visit Plumas County is between June and September when the temperatures are warm, and the outdoor activities are in full swing. If you’re planning to visit during the winter months, be sure to pack warm clothing and winter gear as the temperatures can drop below freezing.

Do:

  2. Dress appropriately for outdoor activities
  3. Leave no trace

Don’t:

Safety and Health
