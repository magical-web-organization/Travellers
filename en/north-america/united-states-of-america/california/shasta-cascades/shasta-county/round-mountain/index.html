Introduction to Round Mountain

Round Mountain is a small community located in Shasta County, in the Shasta Cascades region of Northern California. The town is situated at the foothills of the Trinity Mountains and is surrounded by beautiful forests, lakes, and mountains. Round Mountain is an ideal destination for those looking to escape the city and enjoy the natural beauty of Northern California.

List of Places to Visit in Round Mountain

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fort Crook Museum: The museum is dedicated to showcasing the history of Fort Crook, which served as an army post during the late 1800s. Visitors can view historic artifacts and exhibits, including the reconstructed barracks and officer’s quarters.
  • Burney Falls: Burney Falls is a must-see attraction located just a short drive from Round Mountain. The waterfall is 129 feet tall and is surrounded by beautiful scenery.
  • Lassen Volcanic National Park: Lassen Volcanic National Park is a popular destination for those visiting Round Mountain. The park features hiking trails, geothermal areas, and stunning scenery.

Natural Attractions of Round Mountain: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hiking Trails in Shasta-Trinity National Forest: The Shasta-Trinity National Forest offers plenty of hiking opportunities for visitors to Round Mountain. Trails range from short and easy to long and challenging, with options for all skill levels.
  • Lake Shasta Caverns: Lake Shasta Caverns is an attraction that features guided tours of the underground caves. Visitors can see unique rock formations, crystal-clear lakes, and even an underground waterfall.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Round Mountain

  • Round Mountain Trail: The Round Mountain Trail is a hiking trail that offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and forests. The trail is 5.6 miles long and is rated as moderate.
  • Trinity Alps Wilderness Trails: The Trinity Alps Wilderness offers plenty of hiking and biking trails for visitors to explore. The trails range from easy to difficult, with options for all skill levels.

Best Things to Do in Round Mountain: Activities and Tours

  • Fishing in Lake Shasta: Lake Shasta is a popular destination for fishing enthusiasts. The lake is home to a variety of fish species, including bass, trout, and catfish.
  • Rafting on Trinity River: Rafting on Trinity River is an exciting adventure for visitors to Round Mountain. The river offers a variety of rapids for all skill levels.

Food and Drink Scene of Round Mountain

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sierra Foothills Wineries: Round Mountain is a part of the Sierra Foothills wine region, which is home to a variety of wineries. Visitors can sample wines and enjoy the beautiful scenery of the vineyards.
  • Breweries in Redding: The city of Redding, located a short drive from Round Mountain, is home to several breweries. Visitors can sample craft beers and enjoy food from local food trucks.

Local Beverages

  • Coffee Creek Ranch: Coffee Creek Ranch is a historic guest ranch located near Round Mountain. The ranch offers guests the opportunity to experience life on a working ranch and taste local meat and produce.

Where to Stay in Round Mountain: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Coffee Creek Ranch: Coffee Creek Ranch offers guests a unique experience to stay on a working ranch. Guests can enjoy horseback riding, fishing, and relaxing in the beautiful surroundings.
  • Motel 6 Redding South: Located in nearby Redding, the Motel 6 offers affordable accommodations for visitors to Round Mountain.

What to Buy in Round Mountain: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Trinity River Rafting T-Shirts: Visitors who have a great time rafting on the Trinity River can bring home a t-shirt to remember their adventure.
  • Local Honey: Local honey is a great souvenir to bring home from Round Mountain. Flynn Creek Honey, located in nearby Redding, offers high-quality honey and other bee-related products.

Local Culture and Customs of Round Mountain

Round Mountain is a small community that is known for its friendly locals and laid-back atmosphere. Visitors should be respectful of the town’s natural surroundings and take care to leave no trace when hiking or camping in the area.

Best Time to Visit Round Mountain

The best time to visit Round Mountain is in the spring or fall, when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller. Summer can be hot, and winter can bring snow and icy road conditions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Round Mountain

Do:

  • Take advantage of the natural beauty of the area by going hiking or camping.
  • Respect the town’s culture and customs.

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave garbage on trails or in campsites.
  • Pick flowers or disturb wildlife in the area.

Safety and Health

Safety in Round Mountain

Visitors to Round Mountain should be aware of the potential for outdoor hazards such as snakes, bears, and mountain lions. Hikers should carry bear spray and be knowledgeable on how to use it.

Health in Round Mountain

Visitors to Round Mountain should take precautions against sunburn and dehydration, especially during the summer when temperatures can be high. Visitors should also be careful to drink clean water and take precautions against tick bites.

Getting to Round Mountain: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Round Mountain

The nearest airport to Round Mountain is Redding Municipal Airport, located approximately 50 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Round Mountain

Greyhound offers bus service to nearby Redding.

Other Options

Renting a car is the easiest way to get around Round Mountain and the surrounding area.

How to Get Around in Round Mountain

A car is necessary to get around Round Mountain and the surrounding area.

Where to Go Next from Round Mountain: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Mount Shasta: Mount Shasta is a popular destination for hikers, skiers, and outdoor enthusiasts.
  • Sacramento: California’s capital city is located a few hours’ drive from Round Mountain and offers a variety of attractions, including museums, restaurants, and shopping.

Conclusion

Round Mountain is a small town with big natural beauty. Visitors to Round Mountain can enjoy hiking, fishing, rafting, and other outdoor activities, as well as sample local wines and craft beers. With the town’s friendly locals and laid-back atmosphere, visitors are sure to have a memorable trip to Round Mountain.
