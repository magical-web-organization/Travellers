Introduction to Suisun City

Welcome to the charming little city of Suisun, located in Solano County in the North Bay area of the beautiful state of California. This small town is a hidden gem, offering a charming mix of local culture, stunning natural attractions, and exciting things to do and see for visitors. Unlike many other more heavily-visited destinations in California, Suisun City boasts a friendly, relaxed atmosphere with a welcoming community.

List of Places to Visit in Suisun City

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Suisun City Historical Museum

Learn about the rich history of Suisun City and the surrounding area with a visit to the Suisun City Historical Museum. Established in 1986, the museum houses numerous exhibits showcasing the city’s history, including photographs, documents, and artifacts from the early 20th century to the present day.

The Western Railway Museum

Founded in 1960, the Western Railway Museum is dedicated to preserving and showcasing the heritage of electric railways. The museum features more than 50 trains, trolleys, and streetcars representing over a century of railway history in the region.

Natural Attractions of Suisun City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Suisun Wildlife Center

The Suisun Wildlife Center is a perfect destination for nature lovers and animal enthusiasts. The center provides veterinary care and rehabilitation to injured and orphaned wildlife, including birds of prey, coyotes, bobcats, river otters, and turtles.

Grizzly Island Wildlife Area

Grizzly Island Wildlife Area covers over 8,000 acres of marshland, tidal sloughs, and upland grasslands that are home to an abundance of wildlife, including black bears, tule elk, and a variety of migratory birds. Visitors can enjoy hiking, hunting, fishing, and wildlife watching in this beautiful natural area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Suisun City

Rush Ranch

Rush Ranch is a 2073-acre nature preserve located just south of Suisun City. This natural park features over 10 miles of hiking and biking trails that wind through diverse habitats such as freshwater marshes, grazing pastures, and ancient oak woodlands. Visitors can also tour the historic Rush Ranch House and learn about the ranch’s long history.

Best Things to Do in Suisun City: Activities and Tours

Suisun Valley Wine Country

Take a scenic wine tour in Suisun Valley Wine Country and sample award-winning wines produced by over a dozen local vineyards. Located just a short drive from Suisun City, the valley’s picturesque landscape and charming wineries make it a perfect destination for a relaxing day trip.

Green Valley Country Club

Golf enthusiasts can tee off at the Green Valley Country Club, which offers an 18-hole championship course designed by the renowned architect, George Fazio. Designed to blend seamlessly with the natural landscape, this course provides challenging play with breathtaking views.

Food and Drink Scene of Suisun City

Must-Try Dishes

Suisun City Crab Grab

This is a popular local event that happens every January, and the highlight of the event is a crab feed, where visitors get to enjoy the freshest crab from the San Francisco Bay, salad, pasta, bread, and dessert.

Sourdough and Ciabatta

Sourdough and Ciabatta, artisan breads, are popular specialties of Suisun City.

Local Beverages

Anheuser-Busch Brewery Tours

Tour the famous Anheuser-Busch Brewery and learn about the history and production process of some of the most popular beer brands in the world, including Budweiser, Stella Artois, and Corona. Visitors can sample some of the brewery’s signature brews after the tour.

Green Valley Vineyards

Located in the beautiful Green Valley wine region, Green Valley Vineyards is a local winery known for producing high-quality, award-winning wines. Visitors can enjoy a tasting in the tasting room while taking in the vineyard’s lush surroundings.

Where to Stay in Suisun City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hampton Inn and Suites Suisun City Waterfront

Enjoy a comfortable, relaxing stay at the Hampton Inn and Suites Suisun City Waterfront, which is ideally situated on the waterfront and offers beautiful views of the marina. This hotel provides top-quality amenities such as an outdoor swimming pool and hot tub, fitness center, free breakfast, and complimentary Wi-Fi.

Hyatt Place Vacaville

The Hyatt Place Vacaville is a modern, stylish hotel located a short drive from Suisun City, which offers a variety of rooms and suites to fit any budget. The hotel features a range of amenities, including a fitness center, outdoor pool, and on-site dining options.

What to Buy in Suisun City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Suisun City Chocolate Company

Located in the heart of downtown Suisun City, the Suisun City Chocolate Company is a must-visit destination for chocolate lovers. This artisan chocolate shop sells homemade truffles, chocolate-covered fruits, and other sweet treats that make perfect souvenirs or gifts.

Local Culture and Customs of Suisun City

Suisun City Art Walk

Every August, Suisun City hosts an Art Walk event in the downtown area, featuring live music, food, and more than 100 regional artists showcasing their work. Visitors can enjoy strolling the streets and taking in the vibrant local arts scene.

Independence Day Parade and Fireworks

One of the most popular events in Suisun City is the annual Independence Day Parade and Fireworks. This festive event features a parade with floats, marching bands, and classic cars, followed by a stunning fireworks display that lights up the night sky.

Best Time to Visit Suisun City

The best time to visit Suisun City is in the spring or fall, when the weather is mild and comfortable, and there are plenty of outdoor activities and cultural events to enjoy. Avoid the hot, dry summer months, when temperatures can reach over 100 degrees.

Do’s and Don’ts of Suisun City

Do:

  • Do visit Rush Ranch Preserve and take a nature walk through its ancient oak woodlands.
  • Do try the local wine and food specialties.
  • Do visit the Suisun Wildlife Center
  • Do attend the Suisun City’s annual Crab Grab event.

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget your sunscreen and water during the hot summer months.
  • Don’t litter or damage the environment in any way while enjoying outdoor activities like hiking or wildlife watching.
  • Don’t forget to obtain proper permits and follow regulations when hunting or fishing.

Safety and Health

Safety in Suisun City

Suisun City is a generally safe destination for travelers. However, visitors should be aware of their surroundings and take basic safety precautions, such as locking car doors and securing valuables while exploring the area.

Health in Suisun City

The local health facilities and services in Suisun City are reliable and well-equipped. However, visitors should ensure they have adequate travel insurance, and be aware of the nearest hospital or emergency services in case of an emergency.

Getting to Suisun City: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Suisun City

The nearest major airport to Suisun City is San Francisco International Airport (SFO), which is approximately an hour and a half drive away. Oakland International Airport (OAK) and Sacramento International Airport (SMF) are also nearby.

Bus Stations Near Suisun City

Suisun City is well-connected by public transportation, with numerous bus lines running through the city. Greyhound buses stops at nearby Vacaville and Vallejo.

Other Options

Amtrak Train has trains that stops at Suisun Fairfield station which is 10-minutes drive to Suisun City.

How to Get Around in Suisun City

The best way to get around Suisun City is by car or bike as the area is well-connected by roads and highways. Alternatively, visitors can also utilize the public transportation system which includes buses.

Where to Go Next From Suisun City: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Lake Tahoe

Explore the beauty of the Sierra Nevada Mountains and hit the slopes in Lake Tahoe, a popular destination for skiing, snowboarding, and winter sports. The journey from Suisun City to Lake Tahoe takes approximately three hours by car.

San Francisco

Take a day trip to San Francisco, one of the most iconic cities in the United States. Experience world-renowned attractions such as the Golden Gate Bridge, Alcatraz Island, and Fisherman’s Wharf. The drive from Suisun City to San Francisco takes approximately an hour and a half.
