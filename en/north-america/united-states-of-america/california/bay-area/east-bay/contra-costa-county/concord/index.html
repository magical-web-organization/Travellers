Introduction to Concord

Concord is a city located in Contra Costa County, in the East Bay region of the San Francisco Bay Area, California. It is known for its beautiful parks, historical sites, and outdoor recreation opportunities. Whether you’re a history buff, outdoor enthusiast, or foodie, Concord has something to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Concord

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Concord Historical Society - Learn about Concord’s rich history through collections and exhibits showcasing the city’s pioneers and growth.
  • Galindo House and Gardens - Visit this 1856 landmark that offers a glimpse into the lives of the Galindo family, one of the most influential early settlers of Concord.
  • Markham Nature Park and Arboretum - Stroll through a self-guided tour of native California plants, trees, and other flora in this beautiful park and arboretum.
  • Pixieland Amusement Park - Bring the whole family to this classic amusement park that has been in operation since 1950s.
  • Todos Santos Plaza - Take a walk through this historic downtown public square and catch a live music performance, festival, or farmers’ market.

Natural Attractions of Concord: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mt. Diablo State Park - Hike, bike, or drive to the top of Mt. Diablo and enjoy breathtaking panoramic views of the Bay area.
  • Lime Ridge Open Space - Explore the natural beauty of Lime Ridge through hiking or biking trails. It is also home to an abundance of wildlife, including coyotes and owls.
  • Newhall Community Park - This beautiful park offers plenty of activities and amenities like a lake, playgrounds, picnic areas, and sports fields.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Concord

  • Mt. Diablo Summit Trail - A challenging hike with spectacular views of the Bay Area and Contra Costa County, including several different terrain types and amazing wildflower blooms.
  • Briones Regional Park Trail - This scenic trail offers a moderate yet beautiful hike with views of the valley and Mount Diablo.
  • Delta-De Anza Regional Trail - A mostly flat, paved trail that runs along the San Francisco Bay. It is the perfect trail to ride your bike or jog, and offers beautiful views of the Bay Area.

Best Things to Do in Concord: Activities and Tours

  • Markham Vineyards - Tour and taste in this beautiful vineyard that produces stunning wines and offers scenic views.
  • Concord Pavilion - Catch a live music performance by the biggest names in the music industry at this outdoor amphitheater.
  • Ygnacio Valley Park - Enjoy outdoor sports and recreational activities like soccer and baseball, as well as playgrounds and picnic areas.
  • Concord Farmers’ Market - Buy local produce and other products at this weekly farmers’ market that has been operated since 1992.

Food and Drink Scene of Concord

Must-Try Dishes

  • Garlic Fries at The Red Caboose - The iconic garlic fries at The Red Caboose is a must-try food in Concord, and it has been featured on the Food Network.
  • Lamb gyro at Istanbul Grill - Get a taste of the Mediterranean with the juicy lamb gyro at Istanbul Grill, a local favorite.
  • Tri-tip sandwich at Back Forty Texas BBQ - This sandwich is a Concord staple and is made with tender and juicy meat served with spicy BBQ sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Resilience IPA at Epidemic Ales - Enjoy a pint of Resilience IPA at Epidemic Ales, a local craft brewery that also serves delicious pub fare.
  • Wine Tasting at Markham Vineyards - Sip on award-winning wines and enjoy gorgeous vineyard views.
  • Coffee at Concord Coffee Shop - Savor a cup of organic, ethically sourced coffee and a delicious pastry at Concord Coffee Shop.

Where to Stay in Concord: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Motel 6 Concord - A budget-friendly option with easy access to shops, restaurants, and major highways.
  • Crowne Plaza Concord - A full-service hotel with excellent amenities and a restaurant known for its exceptional cuisine.
  • Galindo House and Gardens B&B - A charming bed and breakfast located in the historic Galindo house, offering a unique and authentic Concord experience.

What to Buy in Concord: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Peet’s Coffee and Tea - Purchase locally roasted coffee and premium tea from Peet’s Coffee and Tea, a Bay Area institution.
  • Concord Farmers’ Market - Buy artisanal goods like local honey, olive oil, and souvenirs like t-shirts and hats.
  • Lulou’s Boutique - Shop for unique and stylish clothing, jewelry, and accessories at Lulou’s Boutique.

Local Culture and Customs of Concord

  • Community spirit - The Concord community is known for its strong spirit of volunteerism and commitment to improving the quality of life for its residents.
  • Sports and outdoor recreation - Concord residents are avid sports fans and outdoor enthusiasts who enjoy hiking, biking, and other outdoor activities.
  • Foodie culture - Concord has a thriving foodie culture with a diverse selection of ethnic cuisines.

Best Time to Visit Concord

The best time to visit Concord is from April to October when the weather is mild, and outdoor activities are highly enjoyable. However, it is best to avoid visiting during peak summer months when temperatures can soar.

Do’s and Don’ts of Concord

Do:

  • Visit the Concord Historical Society and other culturally significant attractions.
  • Explore the beautiful parks and hiking trails in and around Concord.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter in public spaces.
  • Do not damage natural habitats or wildlife.
  • Do not disrespect local culture and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Concord

Concord is generally a safe destination, although some areas may be less safe than others. It is best to avoid walking alone at night in areas that are less populated and well lit.

Health in Concord

There are several hospitals and medical centers in Concord, including John Muir Health Concord Medical Center and the Contra Costa Regional Medical Center. It is advisable to carry travel insurance and check for emergency services in the area before arriving.

Getting to Concord: Transportation Options and Logistics

  • Airports Near Concord - The city is served by the Oakland International Airport, which is located approximately 30 miles away, and the San Francisco International Airport, which is located approximately 40 miles away.
  • Bus Stations Near Concord - The regional bus service, the County Connection, serves the Concord area with stops across the city.
  • Other Options - The nearby BART system provides rapid transit connections to San Francisco and other Bay Area destinations.

How to Get Around Concord

The best options for getting around Concord include your own vehicle, the County Connection, BART transit, or taxi and ride-sharing services like Uber and Lyft.

Where to Go Next from Concord: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • San Francisco - Drive or take BART and visit the cultural and financial capital of Northern California. Explore the Golden Gate Bridge, Fisherman’s Wharf, and Golden Gate Park.
  • Berkeley - A short drive from Concord, Berkeley is known for its arts scene, independent shops, and restaurants.

Conclusion

Concord, California, is a vibrant city with exciting cultural and recreational opportunities. With so much to see and do from historical sites, outdoor recreation, and delicious food options, this city has something for everyone. Plan your trip today and experience all that Concord has to offer.
