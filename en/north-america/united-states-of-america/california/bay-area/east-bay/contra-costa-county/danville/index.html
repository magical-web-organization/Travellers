Introduction to Danville

Danville is a charming town situated in Contra Costa County, in East Bay, California, United States. It is known for its small-town feel, picturesque landscapes, and rich history. Founded in 1858, the town has grown into a vibrant community that offers a wide range of attractions and activities for visitors.

With its warm weather, easy accessibility, and beautiful surroundings, Danville is a perfect destination for a weekend getaway, family vacation, or a solo trip. This comprehensive travel guide will help you plan your trip to Danville and make the most of your visit.

List of Places to Visit in Danville

  • Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places
    • The Museum Of The San Ramon Valley: Explore the history of the San Ramon Valley through exhibits, artifacts, and interactive displays.
    • The Eugene O’Neill National Historic Site: Visit the Tao House, where the famous playwright, Eugene O’Neill, lived and wrote some of his greatest works.
    • Blackhawk Museum: Admire the stunning collection of vintage cars, art, and artifacts from around the world.
  • Natural Attractions of Danville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views
    • Mount Diablo State Park: Enjoy hiking, biking, bird-watching, and breathtaking views from the summit of Mount Diablo, the highest peak in East Bay.
    • Las Trampas Regional Wilderness: Explore the rugged terrain of the park, which is home to a variety of flora and fauna.
    • Iron Horse Regional Trail: Bike or hike on this popular trail that stretches over 30 miles from Concord to Pleasanton.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Danville

Danville is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts with a variety of hiking and biking trails to choose from. The Mount Diablo Summit Trail, Las Trampas Ridge Trail, and Iron Horse Regional Trail are some of the popular options. These trails offer stunning views of the surrounding hills, valleys, and forests.

Best Things to Do in Danville: Activities and Tours

  • Danville Hot Air Balloon Rides: Experience the beauty of Danville from above with a hot air balloon ride.
  • Danville Farmers’ Market: Enjoy the freshest produce, freshly baked bread, and artisanal products from local vendors.
  • Danville Area Tours: Take a guided tour of the town with Danville Ambassador tours and see the sights and sounds of Danville with an expert guide.

Food and Drink Scene of Danville

Danville offers an impressive range of dining options from gourmet restaurants, casual cafes, to food trucks. The town is a foodie’s paradise, with something to suit every palate and budget.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Prime Rib at Forbes Mill Steakhouse
  • Grilled Chicken Sandwich at Sideboard
  • The Mess at The Peasant’s Courtyard.

Local Beverages

  • Craft beer at Danville Brewing Company
  • Wine tasting at The Vine at Bridges

Where to Stay in Danville: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Danville has plenty of accommodation options to choose from depending on your budget and preferences.

  • Best Western Danville Sycamore: This hotel is located on the east side of the town overlooking the valley, and is known for its comfortable rooms and amenities.
  • The Rose Hotel: A 4-star hotel located in the heart of the historic downtown area, this hotel offers luxury rooms, breakfast, and a wine bar.
  • Airbnb: For an authentic local experience, consider renting an Airbnb home or apartment within the town.

Local Culture and Customs of Danville

Danville is a friendly and welcoming community that values tradition, local heritage, and family. Visitors are encouraged to respect local traditions, dress codes, and social norms.

Best Time to Visit Danville

The best time to visit Danville is during the spring and fall when the weather is pleasant, and the outdoor activities are in full swing. The summer months can get hot, and winter can be rainy and chilly.

Do’s and Don’ts of Danville

Do:

  • Explore the local parks, gardens, and hiking trails
  • Sample the local food and drink scene
  • Respect local customs and traditions

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the local environment
  • Smoke or use e-cigarettes in public places
  • Use disrespectful language or behavior towards locals or fellow visitors.

Safety and Health

Safety in Danville

Danville is a safe and peaceful town with a low crime rate. However, visitors are advised to take normal precautions, such as locking cars and securing valuables.

Health in Danville

Danville has excellent medical facilities including John Muir Health in nearby Walnut Creek and San Ramon Valley Regional Medical Center. Make sure to have comprehensive travel insurance to cover any health emergencies.

Getting to Danville: Transportation Options and Logistics

Danville is easily accessible from San Francisco, Oakland, and San Jose airports. Visitors can also arrive by car, train, or bus.

Airports Near Danville

  • San Francisco International Airport (SFO) is 42 miles away
  • Oakland International Airport (OAK) is 27 miles away
  • Norman Y. Mineta San Jose International Airport (SJC), is 42 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Danville

  • County Connection
  • Tri-Delta Transit

Other Options

  • BART (Bay Area Rapid Transit) to Dublin/Pleasanton
  • Amtrak to Martinez station.

How to Get Around in Danville

The most convenient way to get around Danville is by car. Visitors can rent a car or take advantage of ride-hailing services such as Uber and Lyft. Bicycles are also an excellent option for exploring the town’s trails and parks.

Where to Go Next from Danville: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Danville is surrounded by other beautiful towns and attractions that are worth exploring:

  • San Francisco: A vibrant city that offers a diverse range of attractions, shopping, dining, and entertainment options.
  • Napa Valley: A world-famous destination that has lush vineyards, award-winning wineries, and gourmet dining options.
  • Sacramento: The capital city of California that has an impressive array of historic landmarks, museums, and outdoor attractions.

Conclusion

Danville is a charming town full of rich heritage, outdoor adventures, and world-class dining and entertainment options. With its friendly community, beautiful landscapes, and easy access, Danville is an excellent destination for a relaxing and memorable vacation.
