Located in Lake County, in the North Coast region of California, Kelseyville is a charming town that is perfect for a vacation or a weekend getaway. With its stunning natural beauty, rich history, and welcoming community, Kelseyville offers visitors a wealth of things to see and do. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore the best places to visit, activities to do, where to stay and eat, and how to get around Kelseyville.

Introduction to Kelseyville

Kelseyville is a small recreational town that is best known for its exceptional scenic beauty. The town is surrounded by lush mountains, valleys, and forests, and is home to Clear Lake, the largest natural lake in California. It is also known for its vineyards and wineries, and is a popular destination for wine lovers. However, there is more to Kelseyville than just its natural beauty and wineries. The town has a long and fascinating history, and visitors can explore its many museums and historical attractions to learn more about its past.

List of Places to Visit in Kelseyville

There are many interesting places to visit in Kelseyville, ranging from museums and historical sites to natural attractions and outdoor adventures. Here are some of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Lake County Historical Society Museum: This museum showcases the history of Lake County, with exhibits on the Pomo Indians, the pioneers and settlers, and the impact of the gold rush.
  • Kelsey Creek Brewing Company: This brewery is located in an old dairy barn that dates back to 1890. Visitors can taste a variety of handcrafted beers and enjoy live music.
  • Konocti Education Center: This is a former tuberculosis sanatorium that has been transformed into an art and cultural center. It features artists-in-residence, workshops, and exhibits.
  • Kelseyville Movie Theater: This historic movie theater was built in 1948 and features classic films as well as new releases.

Natural Attractions of Kelseyville: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Clear Lake State Park: This park features hiking trails, picnic areas, campgrounds, and boating and fishing opportunities.
  • Mount Konocti: This mountain offers stunning views of Clear Lake and the surrounding landscape. It is popular for hiking and birdwatching.
  • Anderson Marsh State Historic Park: This park features wetlands, wildlife, and a rich history. Visitors can explore the marshes and learn about the Native American tribes who once lived there.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kelseyville

Kelseyville is a great place for outdoor enthusiasts, with plenty of hiking and biking trails to explore. Here are some of the top routes:

  • Mount Konocti Trail: This trail takes hikers to the summit of Mount Konocti, which offers stunning views of Clear Lake and the surrounding landscape.
  • Westside Trail: This trail is a 3.1-mile loop that offers scenic views of Clear Lake.
  • Cache Creek Ridge Trail: This trail is a 17-mile loop that offers a challenging hike through forests and meadows.

Best Things to Do in Kelseyville: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and outdoor adventures, there are plenty of other things to do in Kelseyville. Here are some ideas:

  • Wine Tasting: Kelseyville is known for its vineyards and wineries, and visitors can go wine tasting at several local vineyards, such as Steele Wines and Wildhurst Vineyards.
  • Hot Air Balloon Ride: Take a hot air balloon ride over Lake County to experience panoramic views of the landscape.
  • Camping: Camping is a great way to experience the natural beauty of Kelseyville. There are several campgrounds in the area, including Clear Lake State Park and Highland Springs County Park.

Food and Drink Scene of Kelseyville

Kelseyville has a vibrant food and drink scene, with a wide range of restaurants, cafes, and bars to choose from. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lake County Pears: These pears are grown locally and are known for their juicy sweetness.
  • Grilled Lamb Burger: This burger is made with locally sourced lamb and is topped with feta cheese, caramelized onions, and tzatziki sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Lake County Wine: Lake County is known for producing high-quality wines, particularly Sauvignon Blanc, Cabernet Sauvignon, and Zinfandel.
  • Konocti Brewing Company Beer: This brewery produces a range of award-winning beers, including IPAs, stouts, and lagers.

Local Culture and Customs

Best Time to Visit Kelseyville
