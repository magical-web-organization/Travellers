Introduction to Clearlake

Situated in Lake County, California, Clearlake is a beautiful city known for its scenic natural landscapes, outdoor activities, and rich cultural heritage. With plenty of attractions, activities, accommodations, and dining options, Clearlake has something to offer for every type of traveler. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take a closer look at the top things to do, see, and experience in Clearlake.

List of Places to Visit in Clearlake

Clearlake has a plethora of attractions and places to visit. From museums and historical landmarks to natural wonders and scenic spots, here are some of the top places to check out during your visit.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Lake County Historical Society Museum: This museum showcases the history and culture of Lake County through exhibits, artifacts, and photographs.
  • Anderson Marsh State Historic Park: This park offers a glimpse into the rich cultural heritage of the area, with evidence of Native American habitation dating back thousands of years.
  • Lower Lake Historic Schoolhouse Museum: Located in a former schoolhouse, this museum highlights the history of the town of Lower Lake and features vintage photographs, artifacts, and displays.

Natural Attractions of Clearlake: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Clear Lake State Park: This park offers a range of outdoor activities including fishing, swimming, boating, hiking, and camping.
  • Cache Creek Wildlife Area: This area is home to a diverse range of wildlife including black bears, mountain lions, and bald eagles. Visitors can enjoy hiking, birdwatching, and hunting in designated areas.
  • Mount Konocti: This dormant volcano offers breathtaking views of Clearlake and the surrounding countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Clearlake

Clearlake is a hiker’s and biker’s paradise, with a variety of trails and routes to explore. Whether you prefer leisurely walks or challenging hikes, here are some of the top hiking and biking routes to explore.

  • Cache Creek Ridge Trail: This trail offers scenic views of Clearlake and Cache Creek Canyon and is suitable for intermediate to advanced hikers.
  • Indian Island Trail: This hiking trail offers a beautiful view of Indian Island and is suitable for all skill levels.
  • Clear Lake State Park Bike Trail: This scenic trail runs along the perimeter of Clear Lake State Park and is ideal for biking and walking.

Best Things to Do in Clearlake: Activities and Tours

Clearlake offers a diverse range of activities and tours to suit every interest. From wine tastings to water sports, here are some of the top things to do in Clearlake.

  • Wine tasting: Clearlake is located in the heart of wine country, with many vineyards and wineries to explore, including Vigilance Winery and Brassfield Estate Winery.
  • Water sports: Clear Lake is the largest natural freshwater lake in California, and visitors can enjoy a range of water sports such as fishing, boating, kayaking, and swimming.
  • Hot air balloon rides: Take a breathtaking hot air balloon ride over Clear Lake and admire the stunning scenery from above.

Week-Long Itinerary for Clearlake

Here is a week-long itinerary to help you make the most of your trip to Clearlake:

Day Activity
1 Visit Lake County Historical Society Museum and walk through Lower Lake
2 Go wine tasting at Brassfield Estate Winery and Vigilance Winery
3 Hike the Indian Island Trail and picnic at Anderson Marsh State Historic Park

5 Spend the day at Clear Lake State Park and enjoy boating and fishing

Food and Drink Scene of Clearlake

Clearlake offers a diverse range of dining options and local delicacies. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages to indulge in during your visit.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lake County Pears: This juicy, sweet fruit is a specialty of the region and is perfect for baking and cooking.
  • Local fish: Visitors can sample a variety of freshwater fish caught locally in Clear Lake, including bass, crappie, and catfish.
  • Grilled meats: Clearlake is famous for its barbecues and grilled meats such as tri-tip and chicken.

Local Beverages

  • Lake County Wine: Clearlake is located in the heart of Lake County wine country, with many local vineyards and wineries to explore.
  • Kelseyville Olive Oil: This locally produced olive oil is a must-try for foodies and is perfect for cooking and dipping.
  • Clearlake Brewing Company: This brewery offers a range of handcrafted beers, including IPAs, stouts, and lagers.

Where to Stay in Clearlake: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Clearlake offers a range of accommodation options to suit every budget and preference. From luxury hotels to cozy guesthouses, here are some of the top places to stay in Clearlake.

  • Clear Lake Cottages & Marina: This waterfront resort offers cottages and cabins equipped with kitchens, fireplaces, and stunning lake views.
  • Lamplighter Motel: This budget-friendly motel provides basic amenities and convenient access to downtown Clearlake.
  • Featherbed Railroad Bed and Breakfast: This unique bed and breakfast offers themed train cars converted into cozy guestrooms.

What to Buy in Clearlake: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Clearlake is known for its locally produced products and unique souvenirs. Here are some of the top items to bring home as a memento of your trip.

  • Fresh produce: Visiting local farmers’ markets is a great way to sample the region’s fresh fruits and vegetables.
  • Olive oil: Kelseyville Olive Oil is a locally produced and highly recommended product to take home.
  • Artworks and Handicrafts: Main Street Gallery and other local shops offer a variety of artworks and handcrafted items perfect for gifts and souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Clearlake

Clearlake has a rich cultural heritage, heavily influenced by its Native American roots and a mix of Latino and European cultures. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, including proper greetings and etiquette.

Best Time to Visit Clearlake

The best time to visit Clearlake is during the shoulder seasons of spring and fall, when the weather is milder, and the crowds are fewer. However, travelers looking for water activities and outdoor adventures can visit during the summer months.

Do’s and Don’ts of Clearlake

Do:

  • Explore the local cuisine and produce scene.
  • Respect the natural surroundings and wildlife.
  • Be respectful of local customs and culture.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the environment.
  • Disturb wildlife or natural habitats.
  • Partake in illegal activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Clearlake

Clearlake is generally a safe place for travelers, but visitors should exercise caution in isolated areas and be aware of their surroundings at all times. Additionally, visitors should take extra precautions against wildfires during dry and hot months.

Health in Clearlake

Clearlake has several clinics and medical centers to cater to visitors’ medical needs. However, travelers are advised to carry adequate medication and insurance coverage during their stay.

Getting to Clearlake: Transportation Options and Logistics

Clearlake is easily accessible by road, with several highways connecting it to major cities. Here are some of the transportation options to get to Clearlake.

Airports Near Clearlake

  • Sonoma County Airport (STS): Located 54 miles from Clearlake, this airport offers limited commercial flights and is mostly used for private aviation.

Bus Stations Near Clearlake

  • Lake Transit Authority: This local bus service connects Clearlake to surrounding towns and cities.

Other Options

  • Private Transportation: Visitors can hire private transportation services such as taxis or rental cars to reach Clearlake.

How to Get Around in Clearlake

Clearlake is a small city and can be easily explored on foot or by bike. However, visitors can also hire taxis or rental cars to get around and explore the nearby areas.

Where to Go Next from Clearlake: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Clearlake is surrounded by many attractions and nearby destinations that are worth exploring, including:

  • Napa Valley: Located an hour and a half away from Clearlake, this world-renowned wine country is a must-visit for wine lovers.
  • Mendocino: Located two hours away, this picturesque coastal town is known for its stunning natural scenery and charming downtown.
  • San Francisco: This iconic city is only a two and a half-hour drive from Clearlake and well worth the trip for its famous sights and attractions.

Conclusion

Clearlake is an ideal destination for travelers looking for a perfect combination of outdoor activities, scenic beauty, and rich cultural heritage. With plenty of attractions, activities, accommodations, and dining options, Clearlake has something to offer for everyone. We hope this comprehensive travel guide helps you plan your trip and enjoy everything Clearlake has to offer.
