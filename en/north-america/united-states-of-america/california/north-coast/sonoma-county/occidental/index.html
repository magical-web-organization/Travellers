Welcome to the charming town of Occidental, located in the heart of Sonoma County, California. This small yet vibrant community has something for everyone, from wine tasting and outdoor adventures to cultural attractions and delicious cuisine. In this travel guide, we’ll take a closer look at all that Occidental has to offer, including the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay during your trip.

Introduction to Occidental

Occidental is a hidden gem nestled in the hills of Sonoma County, surrounded by towering redwoods and scenic vineyards. This small town is famous for its bohemian lifestyle, art galleries, and eclectic boutiques. Visitors can enjoy outdoor activities, including hiking and biking, visit historical landmarks and museums, and indulge in fine dining and wine tasting experiences.

List of Places to Visit in Occidental

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Occidental has a rich cultural heritage that is evident from the number of museums and historical places that dot its landscape. The Occidental Cultural Center is a great place to start, where you can learn about the town’s history and view exhibitions by local artists. The Occidental Methodist Church, built-in 1877, is another historical landmark worth visiting, along with the Museum of West Sonoma County, which features exhibits on the area’s agricultural and logging history.

Natural Attractions of Occidental: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Occidental’s natural beauty is breathtaking, with its towering redwood trees, rolling hills, and scenic vineyards. The Armstrong Redwoods State Natural Reserve is a must-visit, where you can hike among towering trees and picnic amid serene creeks and meadows. The Sonoma Coast State Park, located just a short drive from town, offers miles of pristine coastline, rock formations, and tide pools.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Occidental

As an outdoor enthusiast, you will love the hiking and biking trails available throughout Occidental. For hiking, the Grove of the Old Trees is a great option, featuring dozens of 600-year-old redwoods. For the more adventurous, the Willow Creek Trail provides a challenging yet rewarding hike, with its stunning views of the Sonoma Valley. Biking enthusiasts can explore the Sonoma-Marin Rail Trail, a 35-mile trail that winds through picturesque vineyards and farmland.

Best Things to Do in Occidental: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are plenty of other activities and tours to enjoy during your stay in Occidental. Wine enthusiasts can explore the many vineyards and wineries in the area, including the Porter Bass Winery and the Iron Horse Vineyards. For a unique experience, take a hot air balloon ride over the stunning Sonoma wine country. For those looking for a relaxing experience, visit the Osmosis Day Spa Sanctuary, known for its cedar enzyme baths and various healing services.

Week-Long Itinerary for Occidental

To make the most of your visit to Occidental, we recommend the following itinerary:

Day 1-2: Explore Occidental’s natural beauty. Visit the Armstrong Redwoods State Natural Reserve and hike the Willow Creek Trail.

Day 3-4: Tour the vineyards and wineries in the area. Try the Porter Bass Winery and Iron Horse Vineyards.

Day 5-6: Indulge in the local cuisine and culture. Visit the Occidental Cultural Center and Museum of West Sonoma County, followed by dinner at Hazel Restaurant.

Food and Drink Scene of Occidental

Occidental offers plenty of delicious cuisine options, ranging from gourmet cuisine to casual dining. At Hazel Restaurant, you can enjoy farm-to-table cuisine featuring local ingredients, while Bistro Des Copains offers authentic French cuisine in a cozy setting. For a more casual dining experience, visit the Occidental Road Cellars Tasting Room, which offers wine flights and small bites.

Must-Try Dishes

Those looking for a foodie experience should try the locally sourced duck confit at Hazel Restaurant or the savory crepes at Bistro Des Copains.

Local Beverages

Sonoma County is known for its excellent wine, and Occidental is no exception. Visit Iron Horse Vineyards for a scenic view of the vineyard while sipping on their signature sparkling wine.

Where to Stay in Occidental: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Occidental offers a range of accommodation options, from quaint bed and breakfast inns to luxurious guesthouses. For a romantic getaway, stay at the Occidental Hotel, which offers charming rooms in a historic building. For a more rustic experience, stay at Grove of the Old Trees Cabin, located in the heart of the redwoods.

What to Buy in Occidental: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

The town’s eclectic boutiques are a great place to find unique souvenirs and locally made products. Visit Hand Goods, which features handmade pottery and other artisanal crafts. Local co-op Sonoma Market is a great place to buy locally sourced foods like cheese and bread.

Local Culture and Customs of Occidental

Occidental is well known for its bohemian and artsy lifestyle, reflected in its alternative and progressive community. The town’s unofficial motto, “we’re all here because we’re not all there,” encapsulates this free-spirited way of life.

Best Time to Visit Occidental

The best time to visit Occidental is from late spring to early fall when the weather is warm and sunny. However, any time of year offers breathtaking scenery and plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Occidental

Do:

  • Do bring your bike and take advantage of the countless trails throughout the area.
  • Do try the local wine and cuisine
  • Do visit the quirky shops and boutiques in town

Don’t:

  • Don’t forget to wear sunscreen when enjoying outdoor activities
  • Don’t litter or damage the natural surroundings
  • Don’t forget to bring layers as the weather can change quickly in the area

Safety and Health

Safety in Occidental

Occidental is a safe and welcoming community that prioritizes the safety of its residents and visitors. However, like any rural area, it is essential to take precautions when exploring the outdoors, such as wearing appropriate clothing, carrying plenty of water, and staying aware of your surroundings.

Health in Occidental

Occidental has access to modern medical facilities, including a hospital and emergency rooms. However, it’s always a good idea to have travel insurance and make sure you have necessary medications with you.

Getting to Occidental: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Occidental

The nearest airports to Occidental are Charles M. Schulz Sonoma County Airport and San Francisco International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Occidental

Occidental is not serviced by any major bus lines. However, visitors can take the bus from Santa Rosa to nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

Visitors can rent a car or bike to get around Occidental and the surrounding area.

How to Get Around in Occidental

The best way to explore Occidental is on foot or bike. The town’s small size makes it easy to reach most attractions, and biking is a great way to enjoy the scenic surroundings.

Where to Go Next from Occidental: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Occidental makes a great base for exploring the numerous attractions in Sonoma County. Head south to the vibrant city of San Francisco or continue wine tasting in Napa Valley. Alternatively, visit the picturesque seaside town of Bodega Bay or historic Petaluma.

Conclusion

Occidental offers a unique blend of outdoor adventure, scenic beauty, and artistic and cultural attractions. Whether you’re interested in hiking and biking, wine tasting and fine dining, or browsing local boutiques, Occidental offers something for everyone. We hope this travel guide has given you an idea of what to expect and provided insight into planning your visit to this beautiful part of Sonoma County, California.
