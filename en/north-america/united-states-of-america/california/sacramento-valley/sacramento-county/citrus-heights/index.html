Introduction to Citrus Heights

Located in Sacramento County in California, Citrus Heights is a small city that offers visitors plenty of things to do and see. With easy access to other major cities like Sacramento and San Francisco, Citrus Heights is a great place to explore the Golden State. The city is named after the many citrus fruit orchards that once dot the landscape. Visitors to Citrus Heights can explore its rich history, enjoy outdoor recreation, and savor local cuisine. In this guide, we’ll dive into everything that Citrus Heights has to offer, so you can plan your perfect trip.

List of Places to Visit in Citrus Heights

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sunrise Mall
    • Located near the heart of Citrus Heights, Sunrise Mall has over 100 stores and restaurants to enjoy. It’s also home to an indoor climbing gym, movie theater, and arcade for entertainment.
  • Antelope Crossing
    • This historic site is the crossing point where stagecoaches carrying mail and passengers stopped before continuing along the historic Nevada City-Sacramento-Washoe Wagon Road.
  • San Juan Cemetery
    • The San Juan Cemetery is the final resting place for many pioneers and early settlers in the area. Visitors can take a walking tour of the cemetery and learn about the history of the area.

Natural Attractions of Citrus Heights: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Folsom Lake State Recreation Area
    • This state park is located just west of Citrus Heights and offers visitors boating, fishing, and hiking opportunities. With over 75 miles of shoreline, Folsom Lake is a beautiful spot to enjoy the outdoors.
  • Effie Yeaw Nature Center
    • The Effie Yeaw Nature Center is located in the American River Parkway and offers visitors a chance to learn about the local flora and fauna through interactive exhibits. There are also hiking trails that offer scenic views of the river and the surrounding landscape.
  • Ancil Hoffman Park
    • This park offers visitors over 400 acres of natural beauty and recreational opportunities. There are several hiking trails to explore, as well as picnic areas, playgrounds, and a golf course.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Citrus Heights

Citrus Heights has many parks with hiking and biking trails that offer visitors a chance to explore the natural beauty of the area. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in the city:

  • Auburn State Recreation Area
    • This state park is located just west of Citrus Heights and offers visitors hiking, mountain biking, and horseback riding opportunities. With over 100 miles of scenic trails, Auburn State Recreation Area is a must-visit for outdoor enthusiasts.
  • American River Bicycle Trail
    • This 32-mile trail follows the American River from Sacramento to Folsom Lake and offers stunning scenery along the way. Visitors can rent bikes and enjoy the trail at their own pace.
  • Maidu Park
    • Maidu Park has several hiking and biking trails that offer visitors a chance to explore the park’s natural beauty. There are also picnic areas, playgrounds, and sports fields for visitors to enjoy.

Best Things to Do in Citrus Heights: Activities and Tours

  • Sky Zone Trampoline Park
    • This indoor trampoline park offers visitors a chance to bounce around and have fun in a safe environment. There are also dodgeball and basketball courts for visitors to enjoy.
  • Topgolf
    • Topgolf is a golf entertainment complex that offers visitors a chance to hit microchipped golf balls at targets on a driving range. There are also food and drinks available on-site.
  • Sacramento River Train
    • The Sacramento River Train is a historic train that takes visitors on a scenic journey through the Sacramento Valley. The train has several different tour options, including dinner and murder mystery rides.

Week-Long Itinerary for Citrus Heights

Day 1: Visit Sunrise Mall and explore its many shops and restaurants.

Day 2: Explore the outdoors at Folsom Lake State Recreation Area and enjoy boating, fishing, and hiking.

Day 3: Take a walking tour of San Juan Cemetery and learn about the history of the area.

Day 4: Bowl a few games at the Strikes Unlimited bowling alley.

Day 5: Visit Topgolf for a round of golf entertainment and food and drinks.

Day 6: Spend the day at the Effie Yeaw Nature Center and explore the interactive exhibits and hiking trails.

Day 7: Take a ride on the Sacramento River Train and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

Food and Drink Scene of Citrus Heights

Must-Try Dishes

  • The Squeeze Inn
    • The Squeeze Inn is famous for its cheeseburgers with a crispy cheese skirt. It’s a must-try for burger lovers.
  • The Clubhouse at Aquatic Park
    • The Clubhouse offers a range of American cuisine, including burgers, pizza, and salads. It also has a full bar with craft beer and cocktails.
  • The Olive Garden
    • This chain restaurant is known for its Italian-American cuisine, including unlimited soup, salad, and breadsticks.

Local Beverages

  • Loomis Basin Brewing Company
    • This craft brewery offers a range of locally brewed beers, including IPAs, stouts, and pilsners.
  • Jimboy’s Tacos
    • Jimboy’s Tacos is a local fast-food restaurant that offers a famous twist on traditional Mexican food. The restaurant is known for its crispy tacos with Parmesan cheese.

What to Buy in Citrus Heights: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Local Culture and Customs of Citrus Heights

Citrus Heights is a vibrant community with a strong sense of community. Locals pride themselves on their rich history and love to share it with visitors. The city is also known for its excellent schools and parks, which are often the center of community events.

Best Time to Visit Citrus Heights

The best time to visit Citrus Heights is during the spring and fall when the weather is mild and the crowds are smaller. Summers can be hot, with temperatures often reaching over 100 degrees Fahrenheit. Winters can be rainy and can make outdoor activities less enjoyable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Citrus Heights

Do:

  • Do explore the natural beauty of Citrus Heights.
  • Do try the area’s famous cheeseburgers with a crispy cheese skirt.
  • Do respect local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in natural areas.
  • Don’t forget to bring sunscreen and plenty of water when exploring outdoors.
  • Don’t trespass on private property.

Safety and Health

Safety in Citrus Heights

Citrus Heights is generally a safe city, but visitors should still take precautions to protect their belongings and themselves when in public areas. Avoid walking alone at night and keep valuables out of sight in your car.

Health in Citrus Heights

Citrus Heights has several hospitals and medical facilities, so visitors can expect to receive prompt medical attention if needed.

Getting to Citrus Heights: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Citrus Heights

  • Sacramento International Airport
    • Sacramento International Airport is located approximately 25 miles from Citrus Heights and is the closest airport to the city.

Bus Stations Near Citrus Heights

  • Greyhound Station
    • The Greyhound Station is located in downtown Sacramento, approximately 15 miles from Citrus Heights.

Other Options

  • Amtrak
    • The Amtrak station is located in downtown Sacramento, approximately 15 miles from Citrus Heights.

How to Get Around in Citrus Heights

The best way to get around Citrus Heights is by car, as there is limited public transportation in the city. Visitors can also use ride-sharing services like Uber or Lyft to get around town.

Where to Go Next from Citrus Heights: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Sacramento
    • The capital of California is located just a short drive from Citrus Heights and offers plenty of things to do and see, including the California State Capitol and Old Sacramento.
  • Lake Tahoe
    • Lake Tahoe is located approximately 90 miles from Citrus Heights and offers stunning natural scenery, water sports, and skiing in the winter months.

Conclusion

Citrus Heights is a small city with big attractions. With its natural beauty, rich history, and excellent food scene, it’s a must-visit destination in California. We hope this comprehensive guide has helped you plan your perfect trip to Citrus Heights.
