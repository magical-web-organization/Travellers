Introduction to Sutter County

Located in Sacramento Valley, California, Sutter County is an ideal destination for travelers who love the great outdoors, rich culture, and history. The county, named after pioneer John Sutter, is home to numerous historical landmarks, museums, scenic drives, and outdoor activities. The agricultural industry in Sutter County is also thriving, so be sure to taste the local cuisine and sample some of the region’s finest wines.

Best Things to Do in Sutter County: Activities and Tours

There is no shortage of activities in Sutter County. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Visit the local farmer’s markets and orchards for fresh produce
  • Attend the annual Yuba-Sutter Fair
  • Take a scenic drive through the Sutter Buttes
  • Go bird-watching at the Colusa National Wildlife Refuge
  • Take a wine tour of the local vineyards
  • Visit the historic Bok Kai Temple
  • Attend the weekly Music in the Park event in Yuba City
  • Explore the Sutter County Museum for a glimpse into the region’s history

Week-Long Itinerary for Sutter County

To make the most out of your trip to Sutter County, here’s a week-long itinerary for you to follow:

Day 1: Arrive in Sutter County and settle into your hotel or campground. Day 2: Hike the Sutter Buttes and take in the stunning views of the surrounding valley. Day 3: Get a taste of Sutter County’s agricultural industry by visiting local orchards and farmers’ markets. Day 4: Visit the Bok Kai Temple and learn about the region’s early Chinese population. Day 5: Take a wine tour of the local vineyards and taste some of the region’s finest wines. Day 6: Explore the Sutter County Museum and other historical landmarks. Day 7: Say goodbye to Sutter County and plan your next adventure!

Local Culture and Customs of Sutter County

Sutter County is a melting pot of cultures and customs. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions. Handshakes are a common greeting, and it is generally considered polite to make eye contact while speaking with someone.

Best Time to Visit Sutter County

The best time to visit Sutter County is during the spring and fall seasons, when temperatures are mild and the weather is pleasant. Summer is also a popular time to visit, but temperatures can rise quite high, reaching up to 100°F.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sutter County

Sutter County is generally safe for visitors. As with any destination, visitors should exercise caution, be aware of their surroundings, and take measures to keep their belongings safe.

Health in Sutter County

There are no major health risks associated with visiting Sutter County. Visitors should always stay hydrated, use sunscreen, and wear appropriate clothing when exploring the outdoors.

Getting to Sutter County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sutter County

The closest airport to Sutter County is Sacramento International Airport (SMF), which is approximately 45 miles south of the county.

Bus Stations Near Sutter County

The closest bus station to Sutter County is Yuba City Transit Center, which provides services to nearby destinations such as Sacramento and Marysville.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Sutter County by car via the nearby highways or by train via the Amtrak stations in Sacramento or Marysville.

How to Get Around in Sutter County

The best way to get around Sutter County is by car or bike. There are also several local transportation services, including the Yuba-Sutter Transit System and the Yuba City Taxi Service.
