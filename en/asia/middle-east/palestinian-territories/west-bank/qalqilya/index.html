Introduction to Qalqilya

Qalqilya is a quaint and charming city in the Palestinian territories, surrounded by beautiful hills and rolling countryside. It’s a great destination for travelers looking to explore a lesser-known part of the Middle East and discover a rich Arab culture in a peaceful and welcoming environment. Qalqilya is situated just 12 kilometers from Israel and is the capital city of the Qalqilya Governorate.

List of Places to Visit in Qalqilya

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Qalqilya is a city steeped in history and is home to many culturally significant attractions. One of the must-visit places in the city is the Qalqilya Museum. It’s a great place to learn about the history and culture of the city and is packed with exhibitions, artifacts, and interactive displays. The museum also showcases the vibrant and diverse Palestinian art and culture.

Another significant historical place that is worth a visit is the Old City of Qalqilya. Here, you can explore many narrow streets and alleys that are lined with beautiful traditional Palestinian houses that offer a peek into the city’s past.

Natural Attractions of Qalqilya: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

There are plenty of natural attractions in Qalqilya, offering beautiful views of the surrounding countryside. The Qalqilya Zoo is one of the most visited destinations in the city, boasting a range of wildlife and rare species. Another outdoor adventure to consider is the Ha’bonim Nature Reserve, which is located just a short distance away from the city. It is home to several natural wonders such as rock formations, flora, and fauna unique to the area.

If you’re looking for scenic views, then the Mount Gerizim Observation Point is an excellent destination to consider. It offers stunning views of the surrounding area and is a great spot to catch a great sunset.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Qalqilya

Qalqilya is a great destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts, with several stunning routes to choose from. The Wadi Qana Nature Reserve is a popular destination that offers some of the most breathtaking hiking trails and scenic views. The terrain’s ruggedness makes it a challenging experience, but the scenery and atmosphere are undoubtedly worth it.

If you prefer biking to hiking, then the route from Wadi Haramiyeh to Wadi Qana is an excellent option, offering a fun and challenging route that will test your biking skills.

Best Things to Do in Qalqilya: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking, biking, and visiting the city’s historical and natural attractions, there are plenty of other activities to enjoy in the city. A must-try activity in the city is the traditional Palestinian cooking classes.

If you’re looking for something more adventurous, you can also try rock climbing in the nearby hills, take a horseback riding tour, or join a guided walking tour of the city’s historical and cultural landmarks.

Week-Long Itinerary for Qalqilya

Qalqilya has plenty to offer for travelers, so it’s worth considering a week-long itinerary to make sure you experience everything the city has to offer. Here’s our recommended seven-day itinerary:

Day 1: Explore the Old City of Qalqilya Day 2: Visit Qalqilya Museum and the Ha’bonim Nature Reserve Day 3: Day trip to the ancient city of Jericho Day 4: Take a cooking class with locals Day 5: Hike the Wadi Qana Nature Reserve Day 6: Visit the Qalqilya Zoo and Mount Gerizim Observation Point Day 7: Join a guided walking tour of the city’s landmarks and enjoy some shopping

Food and Drink Scene of Qalqilya

Must-Try Dishes

Palestinian cuisine is rich in flavors and aromas and is something to look forward to on your visit to Qalqilya. Among the dishes to try are Palestinian Musakhan, which is a roasted chicken served with bread and toppings. You can also try Maqluba, which is a layered dish of rice, chicken, and vegetables.

Local Beverages

For drinks, Qalqilya is known for its authentic Arabic coffee and tea. Make sure to try the Palestinian tea, which is made with sage, mint, and sugar. Or enjoy the traditional Bedouin tea, which is served with whole mint leaves and sugar cubes.

Where to Stay in Qalqilya: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodations to choose from in Qalqilya, depending on your budget and preferences. If you’re looking for a luxurious stay, then the Kanaan Palace Hotel is the perfect choice. They offer guests traditional style rooms and excellent facilities. If you’re looking for something budget-friendly, there are plenty of guesthouses and hostels in the city that offer comfortable and affordable accommodation.

What to Buy in Qalqilya: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Qalqilya is known for its unique local products, including handmade embroidery products, traditional pottery, and olivewood carvings. You can find these items and more in the local markets and shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Qalqilya

Palestinian culture is rich and diverse, and the locals are proud of their traditions and heritage. When visiting Qalqilya, it’s best to dress modestly and be respectful of local customs and traditions. It’s also important to note that many locals observe religious traditions, so it’s best to be sensitive to this.

Best Time to Visit Qalqilya

The best time to visit Qalqilya is between March and May or in September and November. During these times, the weather is comfortable, and there aren’t as many tourists, making it easier to enjoy the city’s attractions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Qalqilya

Do:

  • Respect the culture and traditions of the locals
  • Dress modestly and appropriately
  • Try the local cuisine and products
  • Ask for permission before taking photographs of people

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public places
  • Take photographs of sensitive locations such as military checkpoints or government buildings
  • Display public displays of affection

Safety and Health

Safety in Qalqilya

Qalqilya is a safe destination for travelers, but it’s important to take necessary precautions such as not wandering alone in the city’s outskirts. It’s also advisable to avoid visiting during times of conflict or upheaval in the region.

Health in Qalqilya

There are no major health concerns in Qalqilya, but it’s still important to take the necessary precautions to avoid getting sick. Consider drinking bottled water and avoiding undercooked food.

Getting to Qalqilya: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Qalqilya

The closest airport to Qalqilya is Ben Gurion Airport in Tel Aviv, Israel, which is approximately 90 kilometers away from the city.

Bus Stations Near Qalqilya

There are several bus stations located in the city, making it an easy and convenient way to travel to and from Qalqilya. The nearest bus stations are in Tulkarm, located about 10 kilometers from the city.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire private taxis, which are readily available and convenient.

How to Get Around in Qalqilya

Getting around Qalqilya is easy, with many options such as walking, renting bicycles or cars, and taking public transportation. The city’s public transportation system is modern and easy to use, with several bus routes connecting the city’s various areas and attractions.

Where to Go Next from Qalqilya: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several destinations that travelers can consider visiting from Qalqilya, including the ancient city of Jericho, the city of Nablus, and the beautiful city of Bethlehem, all within a few hours’ drive from Qalqilya.

Conclusion

Qalqilya is a city rich in history, culture, and natural attractions that are worth exploring. It’s a perfect destination for curious travelers looking to explore an authentic Palestinian cultural experience. With this tour guide, you’re equipped with all the information you need to make your visit to Qalqilya an unforgettable experience.
