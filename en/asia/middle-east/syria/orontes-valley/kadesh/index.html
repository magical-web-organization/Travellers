Introduction to Kadesh

Kadesh is a hidden gem in the Orontes Valley of Syria, offering a rich culture, stunning nature, and outdoor adventures. This ancient city dates back to the Bronze Age and has been home to many civilizations, including the Hittites, Assyrians, and Romans. A visit to Kadesh will offer you a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in the rich history and natural beauty of this fascinating destination. In this comprehensive guide, we will provide you with all the information you need to plan a memorable trip to Kadesh.

List of Places to Visit in Kadesh

Kadesh is a city that is incredible to explore due to its blend of historical and natural beauty. Listed below are the top places to visit in Kadesh and explore its beauty.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Aleppo Museum of Anthropology and Ethnography
  • Saint Sarkis Cathedral
  • Al-Menha Palace

Natural Attractions of Kadesh: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Local Culture and Customs of Kadesh

Kadesh has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors are encouraged to respect local customs and traditions. Here are some important things to keep in mind:

  • Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites
  • Ask for permission before taking photographs of people
  • Greet people with “Assalam-Alaikum” and “Shukran” while leaving.

Best Time to Visit Kadesh

The best time to visit Kadesh is during spring (March to May) and fall (September to November) when the temperatures are moderate, and the weather is perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kadesh

Here are some essential do’s and don’ts to follow while traveling in Kadesh:

Do:

  • Learn some basic Arabic phrases before arriving
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Bargain while shopping

Don’t:

  • Express any political views
  • Drink alcohol in public
  • Wear revealing clothing.

Safety and Health

Kadesh is generally a safe destination for travelers; however, please keep in mind the following:

Safety in Kadesh

  • Refrain from exploring alone at night
  • Pay attention to your surroundings
  • Be wary of pickpockets and scammers

Health in Kadesh

  • Drink only bottled water
  • Wear insect repellent to avoid mosquito bites
  • Bring any necessary medication with you.
