Introduction to Homs

Homs is a city located in the Orontes Valley in Syria, a historically rich place with a rich culture and heritage that dates back to ancient times. Homs is famous for its fascinating museums, historical buildings, and beautiful natural scenery. As a traveler, you will find a lot of interesting things to discover in Homs, including vibrant markets, delicious cuisine, and outdoor adventures. Whether you are looking to uncover the city’s rich culture or simply enjoy a relaxing vacation, Homs has something to offer for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Homs

Here’s a comprehensive list of places you must explore in Homs.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Homs is home to a plethora of historical and cultural sites that show the rich heritage of Syria. These are the top must-visit culturally important attractions when in Homs:

  • Citadel of Homs
  • Khalid ibn al-Walid Mosque
  • An-Nouri Mosque
  • St. Mary Church
  • Museum of Homs

Natural Attractions of Homs: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The natural beauty of Homs is unrivaled, and exploring it offers a chance to break away from the city’s hustle-and-bustle experience. These are the top must-visit natural attractions:

  • Lake Homs
  • Al-Nuri Park
  • Mount Kassioun

Hiking/Biking Routes of Homs

Hiking and cycling are perfect ways to explore the scenic landscapes of Homs. These are some of the best biking/hiking trails in Homs:

  • The Al-Nuri Park hiking trail
  • The Mount Kassioun trail
  • The Homs to Ma’arrat al-Nu’man bike trail

Best Things to Do in Homs: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the most popular activities that you can undertake while exploring Homs:

  • Take a guided tour of the city
  • Enjoy a play or musical performances at the Al Khalid Ibn Al Waleed theatre
  • Explore the old city
  • Attend a traditional Arabic music concert at the An-Nouri Mosque

If you are planning a week-long trip to Homs, these are the top attractions that you cannot miss.

Food and Drink Scene of Homs

No trip to Homs is complete without indulging in its delicious cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages in Homs:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fattet hummus: a chickpea-based dish that is layered with bread, tahini, and meat or fried vegetables
  • Kibbeh: lamb or beef served with bulgur wheat and spices
  • Baba Ghanoush: grilled eggplant with tahini, garlic, and lemon juice

Local Beverages

  • Mint lemonade: a refreshing blend of lemon juice, sugar syrup, and mint leaves
  • Authentic Arabian coffee: strong, sweet coffee made with Arabian coffee beans

Where to Stay in Homs: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Homs has plenty of accommodation options that cater to every budget. Here are some of the best hotels/hostels/guesthouses in Homs:

  • Orient House Homs
  • Homs City Hotel
  • Al-Rawan Hotel

What to Buy in Homs: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Homs is famous for its traditional handicrafts and souvenirs. Here are some of the best things to buy in Homs:

  • Traditional Arabian sweets, such as baklava and halwa
  • Handcrafted soap and fragrances
  • Handwoven carpets and textiles

Local Culture and Customs of Homs

Homs has a rich cultural heritage, and it is important to respect the local customs of the city. Some of the things to keep in mind include:

  • Modesty in dress
  • Respectful behavior during visits to holy sites
  • Ablutions for prayer

Best Time to Visit Homs

The best time to visit Homs is between April and October, when the weather is warm and dry.

Do’s and Don’ts of Homs

Here are some of the do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting Homs:

Do:

  • Dress modestly while in mosques and holy sites
  • Respect local customs and traditions

Don’t:

  • Take photographs of people without their permission
  • Eat or drink in public places during Ramadan

Safety and Health

Homs is generally safe to visit, but it is essential to take the usual precautions while traveling to a foreign place.

Safety in Homs

  • Keep your valuables and passport secured at all times
  • Be vigilant while traveling on public transportation
  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions to avoid undue attention

Health in Homs

  • Drink bottled water
  • Get vaccinated before visiting Syria

Airports Near Homs

  • Damascus International Airport

How to Get Around in Homs

The best way to explore Homs is by hiring a taxi or rental car. Walking around the city center is also a great way to explore the local markets and culture.

Where to Go Next from Homs: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Conclusion

Homs is a historical city that is rich in culture, heritage, and natural beauty. This comprehensive guide can help you make the most of your visit and ensure a memorable vacation experience. So pack your bags and head off to Homs to explore the heart of Syria’s Orontes Valley.
