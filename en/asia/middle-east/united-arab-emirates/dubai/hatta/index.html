Introduction to Hatta

Located in the Eastern part of Dubai, Hatta is an ideal vacation spot for adventure seekers and nature lovers. This small town is nestled in the Hajar Mountains, offering a unique blend of history, culture and nature that all can appreciate.

List of Places to Visit in Hatta

If you are looking for a mix of natural beauty and cultural significance, Hatta is the perfect destination for you. Here we have curated the list of must-visit places in Hatta.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Hatta Heritage Village

Located in the foothills of Hatta Mountains, the Hatta Heritage Village is a real time capsule of the old UAE showcasing the traditional way of life before the discovery of oil. Visitors can explore the narrow alleys, mud houses, ancient irrigation system, watch traditional craftsmen at work and enjoy engaging cultural activities arranged by the village.

Hatta Dam

Built in 1990, the Hatta Dam is a beautiful feat of modern engineering that provides drinking water to Dubai. The dam is also a popular spot for recreational activities like fishing, kayaking, and hiking.

Natural Attractions of Hatta: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Hatta Pools

The Hatta Pools are a group of clear water natural pools and rocky cliffs formed by the water flowing down from the mountains. It’s a popular spot for swimming, picnicking and cliff jumping.

Hatta Hill Park

Set amidst the beautiful Hajar Mountains, the Hatta Hill Park is a perfect place for a family picnic or outdoor fun with friends. The lush green park offers spectacular views of the mountains and has various facilities including picnic tables, a children’s playground, and a walking/jogging track.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hatta

Hatta is a hiker’s haven offering trails suitable for all kinds of hikers, from beginners to experienced ones. If you love the great outdoors and want to explore Hatta’s scenic beauty and diverse landscapes, here are some popular tour routes that you can explore:

  • The Hatta Dam Route
  • The Hajar Mountains Route
  • The Hatta Hill Park Route

Best Things to Do in Hatta: Activities and Tours

Visit Hatta’s Local Souks and Markets

Go for traditional shopping and buy unique souvenirs, spices, perfumes, and textiles.

Take a Guided Tour of Hatta

Join a guided tour of Hatta’s famous landmarks and activities including the Hatta Heritage Village, Hatta Dam, and Hatta Pools.

Go Mountain Biking and Hiking

Explore the scenic beauty of Hatta’s mountain trails on foot, by bike, or both.

Week-Long Itinerary for Hatta

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrive in Hatta, check-in at hotel, enjoy a picnic at the Hatta Hill Park.
Day 2 Tour Hatta Heritage Village, then hike in the Hajar Mountains.
Day 3 Try some extreme sports, such as kayaking or rafting at the Hatta Dam.
Day 4 Take a sunrise balloon ride and watch the beautiful scenery below.
Day 5 Visit the Hatta Pools and have a picnic there.
Day 6 Spend a day browsing the local markets and souks, shop for souvenirs.
Day 7 Go biking and experience the mountain terrain of Hatta, then depart for Dubai.

Food and Drink Scene of Hatta

Must-Try Dishes

Balaleet

A sweet vermicelli pudding made with eggs, rose water, and saffron.

Madrooba

A traditional Emirati dish made with meat, rice, and spices that is slow-cooked until it’s creamy.

Local Beverages

Qahwa

A traditional Emirati coffee made with lightly roasted beans, blended with cardamom and saffron.

Laban

This refreshing drink is made with yogurt, salt, and water.

Local Culture and Customs of Hatta

Hatta is a Muslim country, so it is important to be respectful of their customs and traditions. Dress modestly and avoid eating and drinking during Ramadan (if traveling during that time).

Best Time to Visit Hatta

The best time to visit Hatta is from November to March when the temperatures are cooler, making outdoor activities more comfortable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hatta

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Dress modestly.
  • Take extra precautions when visiting remote mountain areas.
  • Stay hydrated.

Don’t:

  • Public displays of affection are not accepted in Hatta.
  • Drink alcohol in public.
  • Take photographs of people without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Hatta

Hatta is a safe place, but it’s always better to take precautions while traveling. Make sure you have travel insurance, take safety precautions when hiking or swimming, and avoid any activities that feel sketchy or unsafe.

Health in Hatta

The tap water in Hatta is safe to drink but it is always better to consider bottled water.

How to Get Around in Hatta

The best way to explore Hatta is by car or bike. Taxis are also available but they might be expensive compared to car rentals.
