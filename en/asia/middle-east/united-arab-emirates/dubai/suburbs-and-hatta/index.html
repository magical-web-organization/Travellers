Introduction to Suburbs and Hatta

Nestled in the stunning natural beauty of Dubai, Suburbs and Hatta are two must-visit destinations in the United Arab Emirates. Suburbs are located on the outskirts of Dubai, whereas Hatta is a small town at the foot of the Hajar Mountains. This travel guide will help you plan your trip to Suburbs and Hatta, from exploring top attractions to savoring local cuisine and experiencing the local culture.

List of Places to Visit in Suburbs and Hatta

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Suburbs and Hatta are rich in culture and history, and there are several museums and historical places worth visiting. Some of the must-visit attractions are:

  • Dubai Museum: Located in Al Fahidi Fort, this museum showcases the Emirate’s history and culture, including traditional architecture, cultural displays, and galleries.
  • Heritage Village: A recreated village showcasing the traditional life of Arabia before the discovery of oil.
  • Hatta Heritage Village: A restored heritage village featuring traditional houses, palm frond houses, a mosque, and a restored castle.

Natural Attractions of Suburbs and Hatta: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you enjoy outdoor adventures and beautiful scenery, Suburbs and Hatta have a lot to offer. Here are some of the top outdoor attractions:

  • Hatta Dam: A stunning man-made lake ideal for kayaking, fishing, and picnics.
  • Jebel Jais: The highest peak in the UAE offers spectacular views of the mountains and valleys.
  • Hatta Rock Pools: A series of pools formed by the Hajar mountains with cool and clear water.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Suburbs and Hatta

Suburbs and Hatta are ideal for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts, with several hiking and biking routes worth exploring. Some of the top routes for hiking/biking are:

  • Hatta Hill Park Trail: A 3-km hike through the beautiful hills of Hatta with stunning views of valleys and mountains.
  • Al Qudra Cycle Track: A 86-km track passing through beautiful nature reserves and lakes, ideal for cycling and bird watching.
  • Wadi Ghalilah: A beautiful valley with a 3-km hike through hills and waterfalls.

Best Things to Do in Suburbs and Hatta: Activities and Tours

Suburbs and Hatta have a range of activities and tours to suit all tastes and interests. Here are some of the top things to do:

  • Visit the Camel Racing Track: Watch the traditional sport of camel racing at Al Marmoum Racetrack.
  • Hot Air Balloon ride: Soar high above the desert and enjoy the stunning views of the landscape at sunrise.
  • Experience Arabian Night Safari: Take a safari into the desert and experience the traditional Bedouin way of life.

Week-Long Itinerary for Suburbs and Hatta

To make the most out of your trip to Suburbs and Hatta, we have prepared a week-long itinerary that covers the top attractions and activities:

  • Day 1: Explore Dubai Museum and Al Bastakiya neighborhood
  • Day 2: Visit Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, and Dubai Mall
  • Day 3: Take a hot air balloon ride in Hatta
  • Day 4: Go on a safari tour in the desert
  • Day 5: Hike in the Hajar mountains and visit Hatta Heritage Village
  • Day 6: Kayak or fish at Hatta Dam
  • Day 7: Enjoy a camel ride and watch camel racing at Camel Racing Track

Food and Drink Scene of Suburbs and Hatta

Must-Try Dishes

Suburbs and Hatta offer a variety of tasty and authentic dishes. Here are some dishes that you must try:

  • Hummus: A popular appetizer made from chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, and spices.
  • Shawarma: A Middle Eastern wrap filled with meat, vegetables, and sauces.
  • Machboos: A traditional rice dish prepared with spices and meats such as chicken or fish.

Local Beverages

When it comes to beverages, the UAE has a range of options to offer. Here are some popular local beverages:

  • Karak tea: A popular tea made with black tea, milk, and spices such as cardamom.
  • Laban: A refreshing buttermilk drink that is perfect for hot summer days.

Where to Stay in Suburbs and Hatta: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Suburbs and Hatta have a range of accommodation options suitable for all budgets. Here are some popular options:

  • JA Hatta Fort Hotel: A luxury hotel in Hatta that offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Fitt Hotel: A budget-friendly hotel located in the center of Suburbs.
  • Hatta Eco Lodge: A traditional guesthouse in Hatta with a focus on sustainable tourism.

What to Buy in Suburbs and Hatta: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Suburbs and Hatta are known for their handicrafts and traditional souvenirs. Here are some popular products that you can bring back as souvenirs:

  • Dates: A popular souvenir from the UAE, especially from Suburbs and Hatta, known for its sweet and juicy dates.
  • Pottery: Local ceramics that showcase traditional patterns and designs.
  • Arabic perfumes: Perfumes made using traditional methods and scents such as oud and roses.

Local Culture and Customs of Suburbs and Hatta

Visitors to Suburbs and Hatta are encouraged to respect local customs and traditions. Here are some tips:

  • Dress appropriately, especially in public places.
  • Do not drink alcohol in public places.
  • Be mindful of Ramadan, which is a holy month when Muslims fast from sunrise to sunset.

Best Time to Visit Suburbs and Hatta

The best time to visit Suburbs and Hatta is during the winter months (November to March), when the climate is mild and pleasant. The summer months are extremely hot, with temperatures reaching up to 50°C.

Do’s and Don’ts of Suburbs and Hatta

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Be mindful of the local dress code.
  • Be polite to the locals.

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public places.
  • Be disrespectful towards the locals or their beliefs.
  • Take photographs of people without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Suburbs and Hatta

Suburbs and Hatta are generally safe for tourists. However, it is always advisable to take precautions such as avoiding isolated areas and not carrying large amounts of cash.

Health in Suburbs and Hatta

Travelers to Suburbs and Hatta should take standard precautions to prevent common illnesses such as diarrhea and heatstroke. It is always advisable to drink bottled water and use a sunscreen with high SPF.

Getting to Suburbs and Hatta: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Suburbs and Hatta

The nearest airport to Suburbs and Hatta is Dubai International Airport, which is 35 km away. Visitors can take a taxi or rent a car to reach these destinations.

Bus Stations Near Suburbs and Hatta

Dubai’s public transportation system includes several bus stations that offer connections to Suburbs and Hatta. Some of the popular bus stations are Al Ghubaiba Bus Station and Ibn Battuta Bus Station.

Other Options

Visitors to Suburbs and Hatta can also rent a car or take a taxi to reach their destination.

How to Get Around in Suburbs and Hatta

Public transportation is limited in Suburbs and Hatta, and the best way to get around is by renting a car or taking a taxi. Visitors can also take guided tours or hire a private car with a driver.

Where to Go Next from Suburbs and Hatta: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Suburbs and Hatta are well-connected to several nearby destinations, making it easy for tourists to plan day trips or explore other attractions. Here are some popular options:

  • Ras Al Khaimah: A scenic Emirate with beautiful mountains, beaches, and cultural attractions.
  • Sharjah: A cultural hub with several museums, art galleries, and traditional souks.
  • Abu Dhabi: The capital city of the UAE and home to several iconic attractions like the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque and the Emirates Palace.

Conclusion

Suburbs and Hatta are two beautiful destinations in Dubai that offer a mix of culture, history, and outdoor adventures. With this comprehensive guide, you can plan your trip to Suburbs and Hatta with ease and explore the best of what the UAE has to offer.
