Introduction to Umm al Quwain

Umm al Quwain is a hidden gem in the United Arab Emirates and one of the oldest settlements in the region. Home to a variety of attractions, this destination offers a unique blend of modernity and tradition. The city is located in the northern part of the UAE and is bordered by Ajman, Sharjah, and Ras Al Khaimah.

Umm al Quwain is a great destination for families, adventure seekers, and anyone looking for a mix of culture and relaxation. The destination offers a wide variety of activities to participate in, including outdoor sports, beaches, and historical landmarks. The city’s rich cultural heritage and natural beauty make it a must-visit destination in the UAE.

List of Places to Visit in Umm al Quwain

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • UAQ National Museum - This museum offers an insight into the rich history of UAE and its people through interactive exhibits and artifacts.

  • UAQ Fort and Heritage Village - This historic site offers the chance to explore traditional Emirati architecture and learn about the history of the fort.

Natural Attractions of Umm al Quwain: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Dreamland Aqua Park - This water park offers thrilling rides, slides and family-friendly activities.

  • UAQ Marine Club - This club offers various water sports like jet skiing, fishing, and diving.

  • UAQ Beach - The city is home to some of the most beautiful white sand beaches and crystal clear waters.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Umm al Quwain

  • Al Sinniyah Island - This majestic island offers hiking and biking trails and scenic views.

Best Things to Do in Umm al Quwain: Activities and Tours

  • Island Hopping - Take a boat ride to explore the nearby islands and experience the stunning marine life.

  • Camel Racing - Experience the excitement of traditional Emirati camel racing.

  • Bird Watching - The local reserve offers bird watching tours where visitors can spot an array of native species.

Food and Drink Scene of Umm al Quwain

Must-Try Dishes

  • Mandi - A traditional Emirati dish made with rice, meat or fish and a blend of spices.

  • Luqaimat - A sweet treat made with fried dough and served with a side of honey.

Local Beverages

  • Karak Chai - A warm cup of tea infused with spices and milk, perfect for cold mornings.

  • Jallab - A refreshing drink made with dates, honey, and rose water.

Where to Stay in Umm al Quwain: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Barracuda Beach Resort - This luxury resort is located on the beachfront and offers breathtaking views and comfortable rooms.

  • Royal Residence Resort - This hotel features a private beach area, an outdoor pool, and a fitness center.

  • Vida Beach Resort - This resort is located on the edge of the mangroves and boasts stunning views and a relaxing atmosphere.

What to Buy in Umm al Quwain: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Pottery - Traditional pottery made by local artisans, featuring intricate designs and vibrant colors.

  • Perfumes - High-quality fragrances made from local ingredients and oils.

Local Culture and Customs of Umm al Quwain

  • Dress modestly while visiting cultural and historic places.

  • Respect local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Umm al Quwain

The best time to visit Umm al Quwain is from October to April when the weather is cooler and more comfortable for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Umm al Quwain

Do:

  • Dress modestly while visiting cultural and historic places.

  • Respect local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public spaces.

  • Disrespect the locals or their religious beliefs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Umm al Quwain

Umm al Quwain is a safe destination, but visitors should practice caution and take necessary safety precautions.

Health in Umm al Quwain

Medical facilities in Umm al Quwain are of a high standard, and visitors should have no concerns about medical treatment in the city.

Getting to Umm al Quwain: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Umm al Quwain

Dubai International Airport is the nearest airport to Umm al Quwain and is located approximately 50 km from the city.

Bus Stations Near Umm al Quwain

There are buses that travel to Umm al Quwain from several cities in the UAE, including Sharjah and Dubai.

Other Options

Taxis and private cars are also available for hire.

How to Get Around in Umm al Quwain

Taxis and buses are available for travel within the city.

Where to Go Next from Umm al Quwain: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • RAK - Ras Al Khaimah offers a variety of outdoor and cultural experiences.

  • Dubai - The cosmopolitan city of Dubai is less than an hour’s drive from Umm al Quwain.

Conclusion

Umm al Quwain is a fantastic destination for anyone looking to experience the unique mix of culture and adventure the UAE has to offer. From outdoor activities to historic landmarks to luxurious resorts, this city has something for everyone. Follow our guide, and you’re sure to have an unforgettable experience in Umm al Quwain.
