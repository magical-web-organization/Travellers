Welcome to Dibba - the hidden gem of Sharjah! Located in the northernmost part of United Arab Emirates, this peaceful town on the edge of rugged mountains and clear blue waters is an ideal destination for adventure and culture enthusiasts alike. With its rich heritage, stunning vistas, and exciting activities, there is something for everyone here.

Introduction to Dibba

Dibba is a quaint fishing village on the east coast of the UAE. It is divided into two portions - Dibba Al-Fujairah (in the Emirate of Fujairah) and Dibba Al-Hisn (in the Emirate of Sharjah). Dibba has a rich history and culture, which is reflected in the architecture of the old forts and mosques.

Dibba is surrounded by the Hajar Mountains and has an extensive coastline on the Arabian Gulf. The town boasts of breathtaking scenic views, rugged natural beauty, and pristine beaches. The best part about Dibba is its secluded location, which gives it an intimate charm that is perfect for relaxing getaways.

List of Places to Visit in Dibba

Whether you are an adrenaline junkie or a history buff, there is something for everyone in Dibba. Here is a list of places you must visit during your trip:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Dibba Fort: Built in the 15th century, this magnificent structure served as a fortress to protect the town from invaders. It has now been restored and converted into a museum that showcases the rich history of the region.
  • Al Badiyah Mosque: This is the oldest mosque in the United Arab Emirates and is a masterpiece of ancient architecture. It was built in the 15th century using only locally sourced materials.
  • Wadi Al-Helu: This ancient village dates back to the Iron Age and gives visitors a glimpse into the traditional way of life in this region.

Natural Attractions of Dibba: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Jabal Jais: The highest mountain in the UAE offers stunning vistas of the surrounding mountains and valleys. The mountain offers several hiking and biking trails for adventure enthusiasts.
  • Dibba Bay: This pristine bay offers crystal-clear waters that are ideal for swimming, snorkeling, and diving.
  • Hajar Mountains: These rugged mountains offer some of the most picturesque landscapes in the UAE.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dibba

Dibba offers several hiking and biking routes for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the popular ones:

  • Masafi Loop: This 47-kilometer loop offers scenic views of the Hajar Mountains and is a challenging ride for experienced cyclists.
  • Wadi Shawka Trail: This 9-kilometer trail takes you through the rugged terrain of the Hajar Mountains.
  • Jabal Shams: This 10-kilometer hike takes you to the highest peak in the UAE and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Best Things to Do in Dibba: Activities and Tours

Dibba offers a wide range of activities and tours that cater to all kinds of travelers. Here are some of the best things to do in Dibba:

  • Snorkeling and Diving: The crystal-clear waters of Dibba Bay offer an abundance of marine life that can be explored through snorkeling and diving tours.
  • Kayaking: Paddle your way through the calm waters of the bay and explore the hidden coves and rocky cliffs.
  • Dolphin Watching: Take a boat tour to witness these playful creatures in their natural habitat.
  • Desert Safari: Explore the desert dunes in a 4x4 vehicle and experience the Bedouin way of life.

Week-Long Itinerary for Dibba

Here is a recommended week-long itinerary for Dibba:

Food and Drink Scene of Dibba

The food in Dibba is heavily influenced by the traditional Arabic and Bedouin culture. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Machboos: A traditional Emirati dish made with spiced rice and meat or fish.
  • Luqaimat: Small balls of dough fried and drizzled with honey or date syrup.
  • Harees: A creamy porridge made from boiled wheat and meat.

Local Beverages

  • Karak Chai: Black tea brewed with spices and topped with frothy milk.
  • Laban: A refreshing yogurt-based drink that is perfect for hot sunny days.

Where to Stay in Dibba: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Dibba offers a wide range of accommodation options to cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best places to stay in Dibba:

  • Fairmont Fujairah Beach Resort: A luxurious resort with private beaches and stunning views.
  • Six Senses Zighy Bay: A luxurious resort with private villas and a secluded location.
  • Dibba Beach Resort: A beachfront resort with excellent facilities and family-friendly activities.

What to Buy in Dibba: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Dibba is famous for its traditional handicrafts and local products that make wonderful souvenirs. Here are some things you can buy in Dibba:

  • Handmade pottery and ceramics
  • Traditional Arabic incense and perfumes
  • Dates and date-based products like syrup and sweets

Local Culture and Customs of Dibba

Dibba has a rich cultural heritage that is deeply rooted in the Islamic traditions and Bedouin way of life. Visitors should be respectful of the local culture and dress modestly in public places. Alcohol consumption is not allowed in public areas, and visitors are advised to avoid public displays of affection.

Best Time to Visit Dibba

The best time to visit Dibba is between October to April when the weather is mild and pleasant. The summer months can be scorching hot, and humidity levels can be high.

Do’s and Don’ts of Dibba

Do:

  • Dress modestly and respect the local culture.
  • Carry a water bottle and sunscreen while exploring the outdoor activities.
  • Try the local food and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public areas or while driving.
  • Engage in activities that may harm wildlife or marine life.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Dibba

Dibba is generally a safe place to visit, but visitors should be cautious while traveling in remote areas and hiking trails.

Health in Dibba

The healthcare facilities in Dibba are excellent, and there are several hospitals and clinics that provide quality medical care. Visitors should carry their travel insurance and prescription medicines.

Getting to Dibba: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Dibba

The closest airport to Dibba is the Sharjah International Airport, which is approximately a 90-minute drive away.

Bus Stations Near Dibba

There are regular bus services from Sharjah and Dubai to Dibba.

Other Options

Private transportation options like taxis and rental cars are also available.

How to Get Around in Dibba

There are taxis and rental cars available for getting around Dibba, but the best way to explore the town is by foot or bicycle.

Where to Go Next from Dibba: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Dibba is well-connected to other popular destinations in the UAE. Here are some great places to visit after Dibba:

  • Fujairah: A laid-back coastal town with beautiful beaches.
  • Ras Al Khaimah: A popular destination for adventure activities like zip-lining and mountain biking.
  • Dubai: The bustling metropolis of the UAE, known for its shopping, dining, and entertainment.

Conclusion

Dibba is a perfect destination for travelers looking for an intimate and secluded getaway from the bustling city life. With its rich natural beauty, cultural heritage, and adventurous activities, Dibba has something to offer everyone. We hope this comprehensive guide has helped you plan your trip to Dibba and make the most of your experience. Happy traveling!
