Introduction to Sharjah

Sharjah is a beautiful city located in the United Arab Emirates. It is the third-largest emirate and is known for its rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and stunning architecture. This city is a cultural hub that attracts tourists from all over the world. Sharjah is a dry emirate, which means that it does not serve alcohol. However, its lively vibe and incredible attractions make it one of the most popular destinations in the Middle East.

List of Places to Visit in Sharjah

Sharjah is a treasure trove of cultural and natural landmarks. Here is a list of some of the must-visit places in Sharjah:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Sharjah Museum of Islamic Civilization. This museum is a perfect place to explore Islamic culture and history. It features a vast collection of Islamic artifacts, including maps, manuscripts, and ceramics.

  2. Sharjah Art Museum. This museum displays artwork from all over the world, with a special emphasis on Middle Eastern art. It features more than 500 pieces of art, including paintings, sculptures, and calligraphy.

  3. Al Noor Island. This stunning island is home to a Butterfly House, an art gallery, and a literature pavilion. It’s a great place for nature lovers and art enthusiasts.

Natural Attractions of Sharjah: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Al Majaz Waterfront. This waterfront is home to a beautiful lake and a fantastic musical fountain. It’s a great place to relax and watch the sunset.

  2. Mleiha Archaeological Centre. This center is perfect for history buffs who want to explore the ancient civilization of Sharjah. It features a vast collection of fossils, ancient artifacts, and archaeological sites.

  3. Al Qasba. This area is home to a wide range of attractions, including an indoor playground, a shopping mall, and a canal.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sharjah

Sharjah is an excellent place to explore on foot or by bike. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Sharjah:

  1. Al Noor Island. This island has a beautiful walking path that takes you through lush greenery and stunning gardens.

  2. The Heart of Sharjah. This area is home to a collection of traditional buildings and museums. It’s a great place to explore on foot.

  3. Kalba Mountain Trail. This trail is one of the most popular hiking trails in Sharjah. It’s a challenging route that rewards hikers with stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Best Things to Do in Sharjah: Activities and Tours

  1. Desert Safari. Sharjah is surrounded by a vast desert, and a Desert Safari is the perfect way to explore it. This tour includes dune bashing, camel rides, and a Bedouin-style dinner.

  2. Sharjah Light Festival. This festival takes place in February and features stunning light displays all over the city. It’s a perfect time to visit Sharjah.

  3. Sharjah Aquarium. This aquarium features a vast collection of marine life, including sharks, turtles, and rays.

Food and Drink Scene of Sharjah

Sharjah is a dry emirate, which means that alcohol is not served. However, its food scene is diverse and vibrant. Here are some must-try dishes in Sharjah:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Machboos. This is a traditional Emirati dish that consists of spiced rice and meat (usually chicken, fish, or lamb).

  2. Luqaimat. This is a sweet Emirati dessert made of fried dough balls that are drizzled with honey or date syrup.

  3. Falafel. This middle eastern favorite is a deep-fried ball made of ground chickpeas or fava beans.

Local Beverages

  1. Karak. This is a popular tea beverage that is made with milk, tea, and spices.

  2. Jallab. This is a refreshing drink made with dates, grape molasses, and rose water.

Where to Stay in Sharjah: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Sharjah has a wide range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. Here are some of the best places to stay in Sharjah:

  1. Sheraton Sharjah Beach Resort and Spa. This is a luxury resort that is perfect for families and couples. It features a private beach, a spa, and five restaurants.

  2. Centro Sharjah. This affordable hotel is perfect for budget-conscious travelers. It features a fitness center, an outdoor pool, and a 24-hour restaurant.

  3. Sharjah Heritage Hostel. This hostel is a budget-friendly option for backpackers. It features a shared kitchen, a terrace, and a library.

What to Buy in Sharjah: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Sharjah is known for its unique handicrafts, textiles, and spices. Here are some of the best souvenirs to bring home from Sharjah:

  1. Arabian perfumes. Sharjah is known for its traditional Arabic perfumes, which are made using natural ingredients like flowers and spices.

  2. Handmade carpets. Sharjah has a rich history of weaving and textiles. Handmade carpets are a perfect souvenir to bring home.

  3. Arabic spices. Arabic spices, like Sumac and Zaatar, are a must-try when visiting Sharjah. They make a unique and delicious souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Sharjah

Sharjah is a conservative emirate, and visitors should be mindful of local customs and laws. Here are some essential things to keep in mind:

  • Dress modestly, particularly when visiting religious or cultural sites.
  • Public displays of affection are not allowed.
  • Smoking is prohibited in public areas.

Best Time to Visit Sharjah

The best time to visit Sharjah is from October to April. During this time, the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities. However, keep in mind that this is also the peak tourist season, and prices can be higher.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sharjah

Do:

  • Respect local customs and laws.
  • Learn a few basic Arabic phrases.
  • Try local cuisine and traditional sweets.

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public.
  • Engage in public displays of affection.
  • Wear revealing clothing.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sharjah

Sharjah is a relatively safe city with a low crime rate. However, visitors should still take basic precautions, like avoiding travelling alone at night.

Health in Sharjah

Sharjah has a high standard of healthcare, and visitors can expect excellent medical facilities. However, it’s advisable to have comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies.

Getting to Sharjah: Transportation Options and Logistics

Sharjah is well-connected by air, road, and sea. Here are some of the ways to get to Sharjah:

Airports Near Sharjah

Sharjah International Airport is the primary airport in the Emirate of Sharjah. It is served by several airlines, including Air Arabia, Air India Express, and Flydubai.

Bus Stations Near Sharjah

Sharjah has several bus stations that are served by intercity and local buses. The main stations are Sharjah Transport Terminal and Al Jubail Bus Station.

Other Options

Visitors can also arrive in Sharjah by car or taxi from nearby cities like Dubai or Abu Dhabi. It’s also possible to arrive by boat from other ports in the UAE.

How to Get Around in Sharjah

Sharjah has an excellent public transportation system that includes buses, taxis, and water taxis. Here are some options for getting around:

  1. Public Buses. Sharjah has an extensive network of local and intercity buses that are cheap and reliable.

  2. Taxis. Taxis are an excellent option for getting around Sharjah. Make sure to agree on a fare before starting the journey.

  3. Water Taxis. Water taxis are available in certain areas of Sharjah, like Al Qasba and Al Majaz Waterfront.

Where to Go Next from Sharjah: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Sharjah is a perfect base for exploring nearby cities and attractions. Here are some of the best places to visit near Sharjah:

  1. Dubai. This vibrant city is just a short drive from Sharjah and features attractions like the Burj Khalifa, the Palm Jumeirah, and Dubai Mall.

  2. Abu Dhabi. This city is the capital of the UAE and home to attractions like the Grand Mosque, the Emirates Palace, and the Louvre Abu Dhabi.

  3. Fujairah. This emirate is known for its stunning beaches, historic forts, and scenic mountain ranges.

Conclusion

Sharjah is a gem of the UAE, packed with cultural and natural wonders. Whether you’re looking to explore its rich history, spend time in nature, or indulge in delicious food, Sharjah has something for everyone. Plan your trip now and explore all that this incredible city has to offer!
