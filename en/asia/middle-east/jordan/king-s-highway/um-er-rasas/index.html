Introduction to Um er-Rasas

Um er-Rasas is a historical site located in the heart of Jordan’s King’s Highway, about 30 miles southeast of Madaba. The site bears witness to the country’s rich history, especially during the Roman and Byzantine periods. The ruins of Um er-Rasas are a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and they offer visitors the chance to explore stunning architecture, appreciate intricate mosaic work, and learn about local culture and customs. In addition to its rich cultural heritage, the site is surrounded by beautiful natural scenery, making it an ideal tourist destination.

List of Places to Visit in Um er-Rasas

Whether you are looking to explore the rich history of the area or take in the natural surroundings, Um er-Rasas has something to offer for everyone. Here are some of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Church of Saint Stephen: This site is considered to be one of the most significant ruins at Um er-Rasas. The church boasts intricate mosaic floors that showcase scenes from the New Testament.
  • The Roman Fort: The remains of the fortress at Um er-Rasas represent Roman military architecture and engineering, offering a glimpse into the past.
  • The House of the Priest: Visitors to Um er-Rasas can explore the ruins of this house, which is considered one of the oldest structures on the site.

Natural Attractions of Um er-Rasas: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Wadi Mujib Reserve: Located nearby, the Wadi Mujib Reserve is an ideal spot for adventure seekers. The reserve offers scenic views, hiking trails, and rafting opportunities.
  • The Dead Sea: The iconic Dead Sea is only a short distance from Um er-Rasas. It is the lowest point on earth and offers visitors the chance to experience the unique sensation of floating on the water’s surface.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Um er-Rasas

Um er-Rasas has a variety of hiking and biking trails suitable for novice and experienced hikers alike. The Wadi Mujib Reserve is a popular spot for outdoor enthusiasts, thanks to its challenging trails and stunning views. Other popular routes include:

  • The Um er-Rasas Trail: This trail is relatively easy and suitable for beginners. It starts at the main entrance and encompasses the most significant ruins on the site.
  • The Burial Cave Trail: This trail is more challenging, and visitors can expect to encounter steep hills and rocky terrain. It leads to the impressive burial caves on the site.

Best Things to Do in Um er-Rasas: Activities and Tours

Um er-Rasas offers a variety of activities and tours, and there is something for everyone to enjoy. Here are some must-try activities in Um er-Rasas:

  • A guided tour of the site: A knowledgeable guide will escort visitors through the ruins of Um er-Rasas, providing historical context and background information on the significance of each ruin.
  • A visit to the local market: Visitors can explore the nearby market, which offers an array of local products and souvenirs.
  • A visit to a traditional Jordanian restaurant: Sample authentic Jordanian cuisine at one of the nearby restaurants, offering dishes such as falafel, shawarma, and hummus.

Food and Drink Scene of Um er-Rasas

Food and drink are an essential aspect of any trip, and Um er-Rasas does not disappoint. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Mansaf: Jordan’s national dish, Mansaf, consists of lamb cooked in yogurt, served with rice and topped with almonds and parsley.
  • Kebabs: Kebabs are a staple of Jordanian cuisine, and there are many varieties on offer, such as chicken, beef, and lamb.
  • Baklava: This sweet pastry consists of phyllo dough filled with chopped nuts and syrup.

Local Beverages

  • Sage Tea: Sage tea is a popular drink in Jordan, and it is often served with traditional sweets or as a refreshing drink on hot days.
  • Arak: Arak is an alcoholic beverage, and it is often served alongside appetizers or enjoyed on its own.

What to Buy in Um er-Rasas: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

As with other parts of Jordan, Um er-Rasas offers a variety of local products and souvenirs for visitors to buy. Here are some popular items:

  • Scarves and Shawls: Visitors can purchase beautifully embroidered scarves and shawls, an essential element of traditional Jordanian dress.
  • Mosaic work: Um er-Rasas is famous for its intricate mosaic work, and visitors can purchase mosaics to bring home as a reminder of their trip.
  • Olive Oil: Jordan is renowned for its high-quality olive oil, and Um er-Rasas is no exception.

Local Culture and Customs of Um er-Rasas

Jordan is a country rich in history and culture, and it is essential to respect local customs and traditions. Here are some things to keep in mind when visiting Um er-Rasas:

  • Dress respectfully when visiting religious sites or places of cultural significance.
  • It is customary to remove shoes before entering a home or mosque.
  • Always ask for permission before taking someone’s picture, especially women.

Best Time to Visit Um er-Rasas

Jordan has a desert climate, with long, hot summers and shorter, mild winters. The best time to visit Um er-Rasas is during the spring (March to May) or fall (September to November), when the weather is mild and pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Um er-Rasas

To ensure a comfortable and hassle-free trip, here are some essential do’s and don’ts to remember when visiting Um er-Rasas:

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water, as the weather can be hot and dry.
  • Always agree on the price before purchasing anything.

Don’t:

  • Don’t drink tap water. Stick to bottled water or boiled water to avoid stomach upset.
  • Don’t walk alone at night, especially for women, in isolated areas or streets with no people or lights.
  • Don’t wear revealing clothing, especially when visiting religious sites or places.

Safety and Health

Um er-Rasas is a safe destination for travelers. However, accidents and illness can happen anywhere. Here are some essential tips for staying safe and healthy in Um er-Rasas:

Safety in Um er-Rasas

  • Be aware of your surroundings and stay vigilant at all times.
  • Keep your valuables, such as cash and passports, in a secure place.
  • Avoid demonstrations and large gatherings, as they can quickly turn dangerous.

Health in Um er-Rasas

  • Make sure to get up to date on all necessary vaccinations before traveling to Jordan.
  • Avoid drinking tap water and brush your teeth with bottled water.
  • Be aware of the sun, wear sunscreen, and stay hydrated.
