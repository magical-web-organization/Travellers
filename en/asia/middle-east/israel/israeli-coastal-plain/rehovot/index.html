Introduction to Rehovot

Located in the Israeli Coastal Plain in Israel, Rehovot is a city that is rich in history and culture. Home to several museums and natural attractions, Rehovot is an ideal destination for those looking to explore the Middle East. The city is also known for its rich food and drink scene, as well as its hiking and biking routes.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore the various places to visit in Rehovot, its food and drink scene, cultural and natural attractions, and provide a week-long itinerary. Let’s start by looking at the top places to visit in Rehovot.

List of Places to Visit in Rehovot

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Weizmann Institute of Science

One of the main attractions in Rehovot is the Weizmann Institute of Science. It is a world-renowned research center that has contributed significantly to the fields of chemistry, physics, and biomedical research. Visitors can explore the campus and take tours of the laboratories and research facilities.

Ayalon Institute

Another popular attraction in Rehovot is the Ayalon Institute. It served as a secret underground ammunition factory during the British Mandate period. The museum showcases the various methods used to hide the factory from the British authorities.

The Museum of the Jewish People

The Museum of the Jewish People, also known as Beit Hatfutsot, is another must-visit museum in Rehovot. It explores the history of the Jewish people from its origins to modern times.

Natural Attractions of Rehovot: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Timna Park

Timna Park is a stunning natural attraction in Rehovot that offers scenic views and several outdoor adventures. Visitors can hike or take jeep tours of the park and marvel at the incredible rock formations, including the Mushroom, the Arches, and the Pillars of Solomon.

Nahal Soreq Nature Reserve

For those looking for outdoor adventures, Nahal Soreq Nature Reserve is a perfect destination in Rehovot. It offers several hiking trails that take visitors through lush forests, stunning waterfalls, and scenic views of the hills.

Yavne Vineyard

The Yavne Vineyard is another natural attraction in Rehovot that visitors should not miss. It is one of the oldest vineyards in Israel and offers visitors the chance to explore the vineyards and take part in wine tastings.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Rehovot

Rehovot is known for its incredible hiking and biking routes that take visitors through some of the most scenic areas in Israel. These include:

  • The Israel National Trail
  • The Anava Trail
  • The Obstacle Forest
  • Mini Israel Park

Best Things to Do in Rehovot: Activities and Tours

Aside from the various cultural and natural attractions, Rehovot offers several activities and tours that visitors can enjoy. These include:

  • ATV Tours
  • Hot Air Balloon Rides
  • Segway Tours
  • Night Tours of the Weizmann Institute of Science
  • Tour of the Ayalon Institute

Local Culture and Customs of Rehovot

Rehovot is a city with a rich culture and customs. To show respect, visitors should:

  • Dress modestly when visiting holy sites
  • Remove shoes in certain areas
  • Refrain from eating during Ramadan

Best Time to Visit Rehovot

The best time to visit Rehovot is between March and May when the weather is mild and there are fewer crowds.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rehovot

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions
  • Dress appropriately
  • Visit the museums and natural attractions

Don’t:

  • Drink or eat in public during Ramadan
  • Enter holy sites during prayer times
  • Take photographs without permission

Safety and Health

Safety in Rehovot

Rehovot is a relatively safe city. Visitors should avoid walking alone at night and should be wary of pickpockets.

Health in Rehovot

Healthcare in Rehovot is of high quality. Visitors should be up to date on routine vaccinations and take extra precautions during the summer months when heatstroke is a concern.

Getting to Rehovot: Transportation Options and Logistics

Rehovot is easily accessible by air or bus. The nearest airport is Ben Gurion International Airport, which is located about 22 km from the city. Buses are available from Tel Aviv and Jerusalem to Rehovot’s main bus station.

Airports Near Rehovot

  • Ben Gurion International Airport

Bus Stations Near Rehovot

Other Options

  • Renting a car or taking a taxi

How to Get Around in Rehovot

Rehovot is a relatively small city and is easy to navigate by foot or bike. Taxis are also readily available, and the city has a reliable bus system.
