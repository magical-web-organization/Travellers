Sur is a quaint and charming city located in the Central Coastal region of Oman, in the Middle East, in Asia. Sur is a fascinating place to visit for travelers seeking unique cultural experiences, natural attractions, outdoor adventures, and scenic views of the Arabian Sea. In this comprehensive Sur travel guide, we’ll give you an in-depth overview of the city, including the must-see tourist attractions, the best things to do, the top-rated places to eat, where to stay overnight, local customs, safety and health tips, and much more.

Introduction to Sur

Sur is a historic city that has been playing a prominent role in Oman’s history for centuries. It is known for its traditional dhow-building industry, which still thrives to this day, and has been declared a UNESCO World Heritage site. Sur is also a popular destination for a variety of outdoor activities, such as swimming, sunbathing, watching turtles, snorkeling, hiking, and visiting forts, castles, and other historical landmarks. Sur is home to many hotels, restaurants, and coffee shops serving authentic Omani cuisine, baked goods and refreshing drinks.

List of Places to Visit in Sur

Sur is full of tourist attractions that are worth visiting for their rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. Here are some of the top-rated places to visit in Sur:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Al Ayjah Fort: A historical landmark that dates back to the 16th century, located at the entrance of Sur.
  • Sur Maritime Museum: A museum that showcases the traditional dhow building industry and the history of Oman’s seafaring past.
  • Sur Calypso Dive Center: A diving center that offers scuba diving trips to famous shipwrecks, including that of the famous ship Calypso that sank in the Sur Bay.

Natural Attractions of Sur: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ras al Hadd Beach: A stunning beach with crystal-clear waters and breathtaking sunsets.
  • Turtle Beach: Another beach that is famous for hosting turtle-watching trips throughout the year.
  • Wadi Shab: A beautiful canyon and waterfall that offers hiking and swimming opportunities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sur

Sur offers some of the best hiking and biking routes for adventure seekers. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Sineslah to Ashwaqiyah: A moderate level of hiking along the beach, through the rocky hills and to the old village of Ashwaqiyah, also known as the “Hidden Treasure.”
  • Muntarib to Salmah Plateau: A challenging yet rewarding hiking trail for experienced hikers, which takes you through the spectacular mountain range of Wadi Shab.
  • Al Ayjah to Ras al Hadd: A scenic bike route that will give you a breathtaking view of the ocean, the desert and the city.

Best Things to Do in Sur: Activities and Tours

Sur offers various activities and tours that cater to different interests and age groups. Here are some of the best things to do in Sur:

  • Visit the Sur Castle: A historical landmark that was built in the 16th century and served as a fortress against foreign invasion.
  • Take a dhow building tour: Learn about the traditional techniques of dhow-building from local craftsmen.
  • Watch Turtle nesting: The turtles lay eggs at the Sur beach from May to September.
  • Explore natural beauty: Hiking and swimming in the fresh water pools of Wadi Shab are fascinating.

Week-Long Itinerary for Sur

If you’re planning a week-long vacation to Sur, here’s a suggested itinerary to help you make the most of your trip:

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit Sur Castle and Al Ayjah Fort
Day 2 Take a dhow-building tour and visit Sur Maritime Museum
Day 3 Explore Wadi Shab for hiking and swimming
Day 4 Watch turtles at Turtle Beach and visit Ras al Jinz Reserve
Day 5 Visit Ras al Hadd Beach for swimming and sunbathing
Day 6 Take diving to Calypso Wreck or Fahl Island
Day 7 Go biking or hiking along the shores of Sur or venture further up along the coast to ancient ruins and historic forts.

Food and Drink Scene of Sur

Omani cuisine is known for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and fresh seafood. Sur’s food and drink scene offers an array of culinary delights for tourists. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Shuwa: A delicious and traditional Omani meat dish that is slow-cooked for hours and served with rice, vegetables, and spices.
  • Kebabs: A grilled meat dish that is typically made with chicken, beef or lamb and served with yogurt sauce and naan bread.
  • Omani Halwa: A sweet dessert made from sugar, saffron and cardamom, with nuts and honey.

Local Beverages

  • Kahwa: A traditional Omani coffee that is often served with dates.
  • Laban: A refreshing drink made from yogurt, salt, and water.

Where to Stay in Sur: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Sur has a range of accommodation options such as 3-star hotels, hostels and guesthouses, which cater to all budgets and preferences. Here are a few of the best places to stay in Sur:

  • Sur Plaza Hotel: A 3-star hotel with modern amenities and an outdoor pool, located in the heart of Sur.
  • Masafi Hotel: A budget-friendly hotel that is family-friendly and located downtown.
  • Turtle Beach Resort: A luxurious resort, located near the beach, which offers stunning sea views, delicious food and plenty of entertainment.

What to Buy in Sur: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Sur offers a diverse range of souvenirs and local products that tourists can bring back home. Here are some of the most popular buys:

  • Omani Frankincense: A traditional aromatic resin that is used for perfumes and to create incense smoke.
  • Handmade Pottery: Decorative clay pots and ceramic pieces that are made by local craftsmen using traditional techniques.
  • Traditional Clothing: Beautiful abayas, thobes or khandurahs are often worn in Oman, and can be found at local markets and stores.

Local Culture and Customs of Sur

Oman is a conservative country, where modesty in dress and behavior is appreciated. Women are expected to cover their heads and dress conservatively when visiting public places, especially mosques. Alcohol is strictly forbidden in public places and should only be consumed in hotels, restaurants and bars that have a license.

Best Time to Visit Sur

The best time to visit Sur is between October to April when the temperature is comfortable and rain is at a minimum. Summers are extremely hot, which limits outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sur

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions of the country.
  • Dress modestly, covering your arms, legs and head.
  • Learn some basic Arabic phrases to communicate with locals.

Don’t:

  • Consume alcohol in public places.
  • Use your left hand when eating or greeting.
  • Touch women and men in public areas, as it is considered rude.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sur

Sur is generally a safe place for tourists. However, travelers should follow standard safety precautions, such as not to carry large amounts of cash, to be aware of their surroundings, and to lock their valuables in a safe place.

Health in Sur

There are no specific health risks in Sur other than staying hydrated in the hot weather. Travelers should carry insect repellent, sunscreen, and a first-aid kit with them.

Getting to Sur: Transportation Options and Logistics

Sur can be reached by air, road and sea. Here are some details on transportation options

Airports Near Sur

Muscat International Airport is the nearest airport to Sur, located about a 2-hour drive away.

Bus Stations Near Sur

There is a local bus service that links Sur with other cities in Oman. Sur is connected by road to Muscat and Salalah.

Other Options

Private taxis, rental cars, and buses are the other options for getting to Sur.

How to Get Around in Sur

Within Sur, tourists can easily get around by walking or using local taxis. Car rentals are practical for traveling to nearby attractions.

Where to Go Next from Sur: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Sur is conveniently located close to many fascinating destinations and attractions, that are worth exploring. Here are some places to visit near Sur:

  • Muscat: The capital city of Oman is known for its stunning architecture, forts, and museums, which are rich in historical and cultural significance.
  • Wadi Bani Khalid: A beautiful, lush oasis that is located in the desert area of Oman near Sur.
  • Wahiba Sands: A beautiful and expansive desert with towering sand dunes, where you can take dune bashing tours.

Conclusion

Sur is a beautiful and serene city, which offers a unique blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and outdoor adventures. Enjoy your trip to Sur by exploring its top-rated tourist attractions, sampling its delicious cuisine, and shopping for the souvenirs and local products.
