Introduction to Riffa

Located in the southern region of Bahrain, Riffa is a suburban city known for its scenic views and rich cultural heritage. With its friendly locals, stunning natural landscapes, and a range of activities to suit all tastes, Riffa is quickly becoming a must-visit destination for travelers.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit, things to do, and places to stay in Riffa. We’ll also cover important information about local culture and customs, safety and health, and transportation options.

List of Places to Visit in Riffa

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

For a taste of Bahrain’s rich history and culture, visit the following museums and historical places in Riffa:

Riffa Fort

Built in the 19th century, Riffa Fort is a historic fort that overlooks the city. It provides stunning views of Riffa and the surrounding areas.

Sheikh Salman Bin Ahmed Al-Fatih Fort

This fort was built during the 1700s, and it remains one of the most well-preserved forts on the island. It also houses the Sheikh Salman Bin Ahmed Al-Fatih Islamic Center, which is a great place to learn about Bahraini culture and traditions.

The Bahrain National Museum

The Bahrain National Museum houses a vast collection of historical artifacts, ranging from ancient pottery to contemporary art. It is an excellent place to learn about Bahraini heritage.

Natural Attractions of Riffa: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For those looking to explore the great outdoors, Riffa has plenty of natural attractions to visit:

The Tree of Life

One of the most popular natural attractions in Bahrain, the Tree of Life is a 400-year-old tree that stands alone in the middle of the desert. There is no visible source of water nearby, hence the tree’s resilience is still a mystery to scientists.

Wadi Al Areen Wildlife Reserve

Located near Riffa, the Wadi Al Areen Wildlife Reserve is a haven for Bahrain’s native wildlife. Visitors can take a guided tour of the reserve and see animals such as Arabian Oryx and gazelles in their natural habitat.

Al Jasra Oasis

Set amidst the charming villages of Al Jasra, this eco-tourism spot provides a glimpse into the traditional way of life in Bahrain. Visitors can walk around the oasis, visit local farms and gardens, and sample traditional food and drinks.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Riffa

Riffa has several hiking and biking routes for outdoor enthusiasts. Check out these scenic trails:

Hiking at Jebel Dukhan

Located 25km southwest of Riffa, Jebel Dukhan is the highest point in Bahrain. The hiking trail leads you through the Arabian Desert on a winding path up the mountain, providing stunning views of the island and the Gulf of Bahrain.

Biking at Sakhir Desert

For a unique experience, take a guided biking tour through the Sakhir Desert. The desert landscape will offer breathtaking views of the wildlife, the unique rock formations, the stunning sunset, and the clear Bahraini skies.

Best Things to Do in Riffa: Activities and Tours

Beyond hiking and biking, Riffa offers several other activities and tours:

Bahrain International Circuit

If you’re a motorsports fan, you can’t miss Bahrain International Circuit. Take a tour of the race track and garage, and know the history of the circuit from expert guides. You can also enroll in biking lessons and go-karting sessions.

Surprise Your Sense Tour

Get creative and experience Riffa through a different lens. Surprise Your Sense Tour is an interactive tour that promises an unforgettable sensory experience.

Moroccan Bath at Amara Spa

Finish off a day of touring and exploring with a rejuvenating spa treatment at Amara Spa. The Moroccan bath is a must-try, which is a traditional steam bath with natural and herbal products.

Food and Drink Scene of Riffa

Must-Try Dishes

Bahrain is well-known for its delicious food, and Riffa is no exception. Here are some must-try dishes:

Machboos

A traditional Bahraini rice dish made with spiced meat, fish or chicken, and a blend of spices, served with mixed veggies and yoghurt or tomato sauce.

Samboosa

This Bahraini specialty is similar to Indian samosas but has a different twist. Seasoned meat or vegetables are stuffed in perfectly fried pastry shells, and served with mint chutney.

Balaleet

This sweet rice dessert is a favorite among Bahrainis. It’s made with vermicelli, sugar, rose water, and garnished with almonds, saffron, and cardamom.

Local Beverages

Karak tea

Karak tea is a popular tea blend in Bahrain made with locally sourced tea, evaporated milk, and sweetened to taste with sugar.

Laban

Laban is a refreshing yoghurt-based drink that comes in sweet and salty varieties.

Where to Stay in Riffa: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Riffa offers a range of hotels, hostels, and guesthouses to suit all budgets:

Riffa Views International Hotel

This luxury hotel offers 18-hole golf course, outdoor tennis court, and a pool. The hotel has several restaurants, including international cuisine and a lounge for relaxation.

Riffa Boutique Hotel

This hotel provides sophisticated, welcoming luxury hotel rooms, serving warm hospitality and a taste of Bahrain’s modern culture.

Riffa Palms Hotel

Riffa Palms is a modest layover that offers simple overnight accommodations with all basic amenities sets as well as exceptional room services.

What to Buy in Riffa: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

For souvenirs, visit the traditional Riffa Bazaar, where you can find unique handicrafts, spices, and traditional Bahraini textiles.

Local Culture and Customs of Riffa

Respect is given great importance in Bahrain’s society, so dress conservatively, avoid public displays of affection, and be mindful of local customs when interacting with locals.

Best Time to Visit Riffa

The best time to visit Riffa is during Bahrain’s winter season, between November and April, when the temperatures are mild, and the weather is pleasant for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Riffa

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your visit:

Do:

  • Try local dishes
  • Wear conservative clothing
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Learn some Arabic words and phrases

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public areas
  • Smoke in public areas
  • Express open affection in public

Safety and Health

Safety in Riffa

Bahrain is considered one of the safest countries in the region, and Riffa is no exception. However, visitors are advised to remain vigilant and keep their valuables in safe places.

Health in Riffa

Bahrain has a well-established healthcare system with both public and private hospitals. Visitors are advised to have comprehensive travel insurance that covers medical emergencies.

Getting to Riffa: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Riffa

Bahrain International Airport is the main airport serving Riffa, located approximately 30km from the city centre.

Bus Stations Near Riffa

Public buses are an affordable mode of transportation in Bahrain. The Riffa Bus Station is located near the central market and operates daily.

Other Options

Taxis and ride-hailing services are widely available in Riffa, providing a convenient and safe mode of transportation for travelers.

How to Get Around in Riffa

It’s easy to get around Riffa on foot, but if you’re looking for a more comfortable mode of transportation, taxis and ride-hailing services are readily available.

Where to Go Next from Riffa: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bahrain is a small country, and several other interesting destinations are easily accessible from Riffa. Plan a trip to the following destinations for a day excursion:

Manama

The capital city of Bahrain, Manama is a vibrant city with several exciting activities, heritage sites, and modern architecture.

Muharraq

Muharraq is an island city located in the eastern region of Bahrain, known for its traditional architecture and cultural landmarks.

Al Budaiya

A quiet coastal town, Al Budaiya offers scenic views, a serene atmosphere, and picture-perfect beaches.

Conclusion

Riffa offers something for everyone – from adventure activities to cultural attractions, delicious food, and friendly locals. Plan your trip to this charming city, soak up all the local experiences and create unforgettable memories while discovering the heart of Bahrain.
