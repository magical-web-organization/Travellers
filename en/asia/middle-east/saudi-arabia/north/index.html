Introduction to North

Situated in the middle of Saudi Arabia and considered the largest region in the country, North is a hidden gem that boasts a mix of natural wonders and culturally significant sites. The region is home to historical places, natural attractions, outdoor adventures, and a vibrant food scene, all of which make it an ideal destination for travelers seeking an authentic Arabian experience.

List of Places to Visit in North

Here are some must-see destinations in North:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Al-‘Ula UNESCO World Heritage Site: This ancient settlement is home to well-preserved tombs and sandstone structures carved into the rock.
  • Madain Saleh: Another UNESCO World Heritage Site, this “City of the Dead” contains towering rock-cut tombs and temples that date back to the Nabateans.
  • Hejaz Railway: A historic railway line that runs through several cities in North and was used to transport goods and passengers in the early 20th century.

Natural Attractions of North: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Al Habbaniyah Valley: An oasis that is surrounded by sand dunes and canyons, featuring crystal-clear water and local flora and fauna.
  • Jabal Al-Lawz: The highest peak in Saudi Arabia, featuring snowcapped peaks that offer stunning views of the surrounding area.
  • Farasan Islands: A group of islands located south of Jeddah that offer pristine beaches, coral reefs, and diverse marine life.

Hiking/Biking Routes of North

North offers a diverse range of hiking and biking routes, including:

  • Wadi Al Disah: A natural canyon with rocky cliffs and waterfalls flowing into turquoise pools.
  • Wadi Tayib Al-Ism: A lush tropical oasis with plenty of vegetation and waterfalls.
  • Mount Tuwaiq: A mountain ridge that offers scenic hikes with views of the surrounding desert.

Best Things to Do in North: Activities and Tours

  • Desert Safari: Ride a 4x4 vehicle across the sand dunes and experience a traditional Bedouin dinner and entertainment.
  • Camel Riding: Explore the desert by riding on the back of a camel and enjoying the scenic views.
  • Stargazing: North is known for its clear night skies, so be sure to take a guided stargazing tour.

Week-Long Itinerary for North

Here is a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to North:

Day Activities
1 Visit Al-‘Ula UNESCO World Heritage Site
2 Explore Madain Saleh
3 Visit Hejaz Railway and hiking in Al Habbaniyah Valley
4 Hiking or biking in Wadi Al Disah
5 Explore Farasan Islands

Food and Drink Scene of North

North offers a unique cuisine that combines traditional Arabian dishes with flavors from neighboring countries. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kabsa: A rice and meat dish that is flavored with a mix of spices and often served with vegetables and hummus.
  • Motabbaq: A thin pancake filled with meat, vegetables, and spices.
  • Shawarma: Thinly sliced meat wrapped in a pita bread with vegetables and garlic sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Arabic Coffee: A traditional Arabian coffee that is often flavored with cardamom and served with dates.
  • Jallab: A sweet and refreshing drink made from grape molasses and served with pine nuts and raisins.
  • Tamar Hindi: A tangy drink made with tamarind syrup.

Where to Stay in North: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

North has a variety of accommodation options to suit any budget, from luxurious hotels to budget-friendly hostels and guesthouses. Here are some recommendations:

  • Shaden Resort & Rest House: A cozy and affordable guesthouse located in Al-‘Ula.
  • Movenpick Hotel: A luxurious hotel located in Madain Saleh with stunning views of the surrounding area.
  • Radisson Blu Hotel: A modern hotel located in Jeddah with easy access to the city’s attractions.

What to Buy in North: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Some popular souvenirs and local products to bring home include:

  • Dates: Saudi Arabia is known for its high-quality dates, and North produces some of the best in the country.
  • Carpets and Textiles: There are several markets throughout North that specialize in handmade carpets and textiles.
  • Oud: A traditional Arabian perfume made from agarwood that is often used during religious ceremonies.

Local Culture and Customs of North

Saudi Arabia is a conservative country, and visitors should dress modestly and respect local customs and traditions. It is also important to note that alcohol is prohibited in Saudi Arabia.

Best Time to Visit North

The best time to visit North is between October and March when the weather is milder and more conducive to outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of North

Here are some things to keep in mind while visiting North:

Do:

  • Dress modestly and respect local customs and traditions.
  • Be prepared for hot and dry weather, especially if visiting during the summer months.
  • Be respectful of Islamic practices, such as prayer times.

Don’t:

  • Consume alcohol in public or bring it into the country.
  • Engage in public displays of affection.
  • Take photographs of people without their consent.

Safety and Health

While North is generally a safe destination for travelers, it is important to take necessary precautions to ensure your safety:

Safety in North

  • Follow local laws and customs.
  • Avoid traveling alone at night.
  • Be vigilant when using public transportation.

Health in North

  • Drink bottled water to avoid getting sick.
  • Protect yourself from the sun by wearing sunscreen and a hat.
  • Be aware of potential food hygiene issues and avoid street food.

Getting to North: Transportation Options and Logistics

North is easily accessible via major airports and highways, as well as public transportation. Here are some options:

Airports Near North

  • King Abdulaziz International Airport (JED): Located in Jeddah, approximately 4 hours from Al-‘Ula and Madain Saleh.
  • Prince Mohammed bin Abdulaziz International Airport (MED): Located in Medina, approximately 3 hours from Al-‘Ula and Madain Saleh.

Bus Stations Near North

  • SAPTCO: A national bus company that offers routes throughout Saudi Arabia and has several stations in North.
  • Najran Bus Station: Offers routes from Najran to other cities in North.

Other Options

  • Private Car: Taxis and private cars are available throughout North, but it is important to negotiate the fare beforehand.

How to Get Around in North

The best way to get around North is by car, either by renting a car or hiring a private driver. However, there are also public transportation options available, including buses and taxis.

Where to Go Next from North: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

North is located in the middle of Saudi Arabia, and there are several nearby destinations that are worth exploring, including:

  • Riyadh: The capital of Saudi Arabia and home to several historical sites and museums.
  • Medina: The second holiest city in Islam and home to the Al-Masjid an-Nabawi mosque.
  • Jeddah: A coastal city known for its old town and Red Sea beaches.

Conclusion

North is a unique and authentic Arabian destination that offers a mix of cultural attractions, natural wonders, outdoor adventures, and delicious cuisine. Whether you’re interested in history, hiking, or just relaxing on a pristine beach, North has something for everyone. So, pack your bags and get ready to explore the beauty of North, Saudi Arabia.
