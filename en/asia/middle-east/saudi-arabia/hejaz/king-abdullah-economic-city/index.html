Introduction to King Abdullah Economic City

King Abdullah Economic City is a remarkable destination situated in Hejaz, Saudi Arabia. Nicknamed ‘The Future City,’ King Abdullah Economic City features modernity coupled with traditional Saudi Arabian culture. It was established in 2005 as a special economic zone covering around 181 square kilometers on the Red Sea coast. With its state-of-the-art infrastructure, world-class amenities, and a plethora of attractions, King Abdullah Economic City is a must-visit destination in the Middle East.

List of Places to Visit in King Abdullah Economic City

King Abdullah Economic City is a place where visitors can indulge in many indoor and outdoor activities. From cultural attractions and natural wonders to adrenaline-packed sports, this city caters to every type of traveler. Here are some must-visit places to add to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

1. The Islamic Heritage Museum

The Islamic Heritage Museum works to preserve the Islamic heritage in Saudi Arabia. The museum displays historic artifacts and has a library of Islamic books and manuscripts.

2. Yusr Mall

Yusr Mall is a modern shopping mall in King Abdullah Economic City. It has many popular stores, dining establishments, and a cinema.

Natural Attractions of King Abdullah Economic City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

1. Al-Makhrourah Beach

Al-Makhrourah Beach is a gorgeous sandy beach surrounded by crystal blue waters. It’s a perfect place for swimming, sunbathing or simply enjoying the scenic location.

2. KAEC Park

KAEC Park is a wonderful park in King Abdullah Economic City that features winding paths, a lush garden, and a beautifully designed mosque. It’s a great place to relax and spend an afternoon outdoors.

Hiking/Biking Routes of King Abdullah Economic City

For adventure seekers, King Abdullah Economic City has several hiking and biking trails with stunning views of the Red Sea. Among them, the Al Nawras cycle route, is one of the most famous trails. It is a 21-kilometer flat trail that runs along the waterfront with amazing views of the coastline.

Best Things to Do in King Abdullah Economic City: Activities and Tours

King Abdullah Economic City provides an array of immersive activities and tours for travelers. With something for everyone, here are some of the best things to do in King Abdullah Economic City:

1. Scuba Diving

For water enthusiasts, Scuba diving is a popular activity. Underwater enthusiasts can explore the vibrant marine life and the coral reefs in the Red Sea.

2. Golfing

For golfers, King Abdullah Economic City is home to an award-winning golf course, which is regarded as one of the best in Saudi Arabia.

Week-Long Itinerary for King Abdullah Economic City

Here’s a suggested week-long itinerary for King Abdullah Economic City:

Day 1: Islamic Heritage Museum and KAEC Mall

Day 2: KAEC Park and Blue Coral Beach

Day 3: Visit the thriving port city of Jeddah, explore Souk Al Alawi market

Day 4: Visit Al Tayebat Museum in Jeddah, ride on the Jeddah Eye

Day 5: Take a boat tour of Red Sea, try scuba diving, kayaking or snorkeling

Day 6: Enjoy the golf course at the Royal Greens Golf and Country Club

Day 7: Relax on the sandy beaches

Food and Drink Scene of King Abdullah Economic City

With a blend of cultures, the food and drink scene in King Abdullah Economic City is diverse and influenced by many cuisines. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

1. Shawarma

Shawarma is a popular Middle Eastern dish made of thin slices of marinated meat. It is served with vegetables and sauces, rolled in a pita bread.

2. Kabsa

Kabsa is a traditional Saudi Arabian dish made of rice, spices and meat, usually chicken, beef or lamb. It’s accompanied by sauces and garnished with nuts.

Local Beverages

1. Arabic coffee

Arabic coffee is a traditional beverage in Saudi Arabia, made by grinding roasted coffee beans and served with dates. It’s a staple at social events, and offers a unique taste.

2. Laban

Laban is a refreshing drink made from yogurt, mixed with salt and herbs. It’s a popular drink served across the Middle East, especially in Saudi Arabia.

Where to Stay in King Abdullah Economic City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

King Abdullah Economic City has several accommodation options for visitors, catering to all budgets. Here are some of the best places to stay:

Luxury Accommodation:

1. The Ritz-Carlton, King Abdullah Economic City

The Ritz-Carlton, King Abdullah Economic City, is a five-star hotel located in the heart of King Abdullah Economic City. It offers spacious rooms, an outdoor pool, and golf courses nearby.

Mid-Range Accommodation:

1. Bay La Sun Hotel and Marina

Bay La Sun Hotel and Marina is a beautiful hotel located on the Red Sea coast offering luxury amenities, beautiful ocean views and a welcoming ambiance.

What to Buy in King Abdullah Economic City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors to King Abdullah Economic City can take home souvenirs, traditional handicrafts, and local products such as dates, Arabic coffee and date syrup. One should visit the KAEC market, where souvenirs and handicrafts are sold in abundance.

Local Culture and Customs of King Abdullah Economic City

King Abdullah Economic City is a modern city, but it’s predominantly Islamic. Visitors should respect the Islamic culture, dress modestly, and cover themselves in public areas. They should also avoid public displays of affection.

Best Time to Visit King Abdullah Economic City

The best time to visit King Abdullah Economic City is from November to April when the weather is cooler and the humidity is lower.

Do’s and Don’ts of King Abdullah Economic City

Do:

  • Do respect the local culture and customs
  • Do dress modestly
  • Do try local delicacies

Don’t:

  • Don’t drink alcohol in public
  • Don’t eat or drink in public during fasting hours

Safety and Health

Safety in King Abdullah Economic City

King Abdullah Economic City is a safe destination that follows strict security measures. However, visitors should be mindful of their surroundings and take standard precautions such as not walking alone at night.

Health in King Abdullah Economic City

Healthcare in King Abdullah Economic City is excellent, with hospitals and clinics located throughout the city.

Getting to King Abdullah Economic City: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here are some of the transportation options to get to King Abdullah Economic City:

Airports Near King Abdullah Economic City

King Abdullah Economic City has a dedicated international airport, King Abdullah Economic City Airport (KAEC Airport), which has several airlines operating regularly.

Bus Stations Near King Abdullah Economic City

King Abdullah Economic City has several bus stations, including KAEC Bus Station.

How to Get Around in King Abdullah Economic City

King Abdullah Economic City is a modern city with excellent transportation facilities. Commuters can go around via buses and taxis. There are also private car rentals available around the city.

Where to Go Next from King Abdullah Economic City: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

King Abdullah Economic City is close to several well-known destinations, such as the port city of Jeddah, where visitors can explore the traditional marketplaces and dynamic commercial areas, as well as the serene deserts nearby.

Conclusion

In conclusion, King Abdullah Economic City is a must-visit destination in Saudi Arabia. From cultural attractions, outdoor adventures, and exciting activities, there is something for everyone in this vibrant city. Whether you’re traveling alone or in a group, King Abdullah Economic City offers a unique experience to its visitors, blending traditional Arabic culture with modern amenities.
