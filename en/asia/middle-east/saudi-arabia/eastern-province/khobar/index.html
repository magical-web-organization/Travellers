Introduction to Khobar

Khobar is a city located in the Eastern Province of Saudi Arabia. It is known for its beautiful beaches, shopping malls, and cultural landmarks. The city is an important industrial hub for the country with many oil and gas companies located here. Whether you are a business traveler or a tourist, Khobar has plenty to offer.

Experience the rich culture and enjoy the scenic beauty of Khobar by visiting these must-see destinations:

Local Culture and Customs of Khobar

Khobar is a conservative town, so it’s important to respect local culture and customs. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Dress modestly in public
  • Avoid eating or drinking in public during the month of Ramadan
  • Respect prayer times and avoid loud noise

Best Time to Visit Khobar

The best time to visit Khobar is from October to March, when the weather is mild and pleasant for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Khobar

Do:

  • Respect local culture and customs
  • Dress modestly in public places
  • Explore the natural scenery, museums, and historical places

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public
  • Engage in public displays of affection
  • Eat during Ramadan in public

Safety and Health

Safety in Khobar

Khobar is considered a safe city for tourists. However, it’s important to take standard safety precautions such as keeping valuable belongings safe and avoiding deserted areas at night.

Health in Khobar

Saudi Arabia mandates the yellow fever vaccine for those arriving from countries where the disease is prevalent. It’s important to consult your healthcare provider before your trip.
