Bojnourd, the capital of Khorasan Province in Iran’s northeast, is known for its stunning natural scenery and rich cultural attractions. This hidden gem offers visitors a unique glimpse into the country’s history, as well as a chance to experience its unspoiled landscapes. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ve covered everything you need to know to plan your trip to Bojnourd.

Introduction to Bojnourd

Bojnourd, which translates to “New Market,” is a small city with a population of around 200,000. It’s an ancient city with a rich history dating back to the Sassanid Empire. Today, it’s a modern city with a bustling bazaar, well-preserved historical sites, and stunning natural landscapes.

List of Places to Visit in Bojnourd

If you’re interested in exploring Bojnourd, make sure to check out these top attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Bojnourd has a rich cultural heritage that’s evident in its historical sites and museums. Some must-see attractions include:

  • Imamzadeh Sultan Mohammad-e-Termezi: This holy shrine is one of the most important pilgrimage sites in the region. It’s surrounded by a beautiful courtyard and has stunning blue mosaic tiles.
  • Bojnourd Museum: This small museum houses a collection of artifacts from the region’s history, including a 4,500-year-old goblet from the Bronze Age.
  • Khosrowabad Underground City: This ancient underground city dates back to the Parthian Empire and is a fascinating look into the past.

Natural Attractions of Bojnourd: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Bojnourd is blessed with picturesque natural landscapes that offer plenty of opportunities for outdoor adventures. Some of the top natural attractions in the area include:

  • Aladagh Mountains: This range offers some of the most stunning views in the region. There are several hiking trails in the area, which lead to scenic viewpoints.
  • Chehel Cheshmeh: This natural spring is a popular spot for picnicking and relaxing in the shade of the trees.
  • Garmab Hot Springs: These hot springs are famous for their therapeutic properties and are a great place to relax after a day of hiking or sightseeing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bojnourd

If you’re an avid hiker or biker, Bojnourd has plenty of great trails to explore. Some of the best routes include:

  • Aladagh Mountain Trail: This challenging trail takes you to the summit of the Aladagh Mountains, offering stunning views of the surrounding valleys.
  • Zizab Valley Trail: This moderate trail takes you through a lush valley filled with fruit trees and wildflowers.
  • Gholam Ali Salt Trail: This historic trail follows an ancient salt trade route and offers a glimpse into the region’s past.

Best Things to Do in Bojnourd: Activities and Tours

Bojnourd offers visitors a variety of activities and tours to make the most of their trip. Some popular options include:

  • Hot Springs Tour: This tour takes you to the Garmab Hot Springs and other natural hot springs in the area.
  • Cultural Tours: These tours offer a chance to explore the historical sites of Bojnourd and learn about the city’s rich cultural heritage.
  • Nature Tours: These tours take you into the stunning landscapes of the region, offering opportunities for hiking, birdwatching, and photography.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bojnourd

If you have a week to spend in Bojnourd, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day 1-2: Explore the historical sites of Bojnourd, including Imamzadeh Sultan Mohammad-e-Termezi and Khosrowabad Underground City.

Day 3-4: Take a hot springs tour and relax in the therapeutic waters of the Garmab Hot Springs.

Day 5-6: Hike in the Aladagh Mountains and enjoy the stunning views from the summit.

Day 7: Take a nature tour in the Zizab Valley and enjoy a picnic by the Chehel Cheshmeh spring.

Food and Drink Scene of Bojnourd

Bojnourd offers visitors a chance to sample some delicious local cuisine and beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Abgoosht: This hearty stew made with lamb or beef, beans, and vegetables is a traditional Iranian dish and a must-try in Bojnourd.
  • Ash Reshte: This hearty soup made with lentils, beans, and noodles is a popular dish in Bojnourd and perfect for chilly days.

Local Beverages

  • Sharbat-e Sekanjebin: This refreshing drink is made with vinegar, mint, and sugar and is the perfect way to cool off on a hot day.
  • Doogh: This refreshing yogurt-based drink is the perfect accompaniment to a hearty Iranian meal.

Where to Stay in Bojnourd: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bojnourd offers visitors a variety of accommodation options to suit every budget. Here are some top choices:

  • Homa Hotel: This modern hotel offers comfortable rooms and is conveniently located near the city center.
  • Bojnord Traditional Hostel: This charming guesthouse offers a glimpse into the traditional way of life in Bojnourd.
  • Ghasr Tarighat: This historic hotel is situated on the outskirts of Bojnourd and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

What to Buy in Bojnourd: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Bojnourd is known for its handicrafts and unique souvenirs. Here are some top items to bring home:

  • Turkmen Carpets: These handwoven carpets are a traditional craft in the region and make for a beautiful souvenir.
  • Pomegranate Paste: This tangy paste is a popular ingredient in Iranian cooking and a great gift for foodies.
  • Traditional Pottery: Bojnourd is renowned for its pottery, and you’ll find plenty of beautiful pieces in the local bazaars.

Local Culture and Customs of Bojnourd

Bojnourd is a conservative city, and visitors should dress modestly and respect local customs. Alcohol is forbidden in Iran, and visitors should avoid public displays of affection.

Best Time to Visit Bojnourd

The best time to visit Bojnourd is in the spring or fall when the weather is mild and comfortable for outdoor activities. Summer can be hot, and winters can be harsh, with heavy snowfall in the mountains.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bojnourd

Do:

  • Dress modestly and respectfully.
  • Remove your shoes before entering a home or mosque.
  • Accept invitations to tea from locals.

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol or smoke in public.
  • Express political dissent.
  • Take photos of women without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bojnourd

Bojnourd is a safe city, but visitors should exercise caution when traveling alone at night and keep their belongings secure.

Health in Bojnourd

Visitors should make sure they have adequate travel insurance and bring any necessary medications with them. It’s important to stay hydrated and avoid drinking tap water.

Getting to Bojnourd: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bojnourd is easily accessible by bus, train, or plane. Here are some options for getting there:

Airports Near Bojnourd

The nearest airport to Bojnourd is Bojnourd Airport, which offers domestic flights to other major cities in Iran.

Bus Stations Near Bojnourd

Bojnourd has several bus stations, including the South Bus Station and the West Bus Station. Buses run regularly to other major cities in Iran.

Other Options

Trains are another popular option for getting to Bojnourd. The city has a train station that connects to other major cities in Iran.

How to Get Around in Bojnourd

Bojnourd is a small city, and most attractions are within walking distance. Taxis are also widely available and affordable.

Where to Go Next from Bojnourd: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bojnourd is situated in a region with plenty of fascinating destinations to explore. Here are some nearby excursions:

  • Golestan National Park: This national park is a stunning wilderness area with diverse flora and fauna.
  • Mashhad: This holy city is one of the most important pilgrimage sites in the world for Shia Muslims.
  • Kalat-e Naderi: This historic fortress dates back to the Safavid era and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Conclusion

Bojnourd is a unique destination that offers visitors a chance to experience the rich culture and natural beauty of Khorasan. With its historical sites, beautiful landscapes, and delicious cuisine, Bojnourd has something to offer every traveler. We hope this travel guide has been helpful in planning your trip to this beautiful city.
