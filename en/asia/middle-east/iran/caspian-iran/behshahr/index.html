Introduction to Behshahr

Behshahr is a beautiful city located in the Caspian region of Iran. It is situated in the province of Mazandaran, which is in the northern part of the country. Behshahr is home to a large number of historical sites, natural attractions, and cultural landmarks. This makes it an ideal destination for travelers who want to experience the rich history and culture of Iran. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore the top attractions, best time to visit, local customs, and much more to make your trip to Behshahr unforgettable.

List of Places to Visit in Behshahr

Behshahr has several places to visit, catering to the interests of all types of travelers, including:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Amir Kabir Dam: Built during the Qajar era, this dam is one of the most impressive structures in Iran. It is named after Mirza Taqi Khan Amir Kabir, who was a prime minister during the Qajar dynasty.
  • Hasheminejad Museum: This museum is located in the city center and features a collection of artifacts, including works of calligraphy, paintings, and antique objects.
  • Behshahr Historical House: This historic house showcases typical Iranian architecture and serves as a museum.

Natural Attractions of Behshahr: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Sisangan Forest Park: This forest park is situated near the city and is a popular destination for picnics and outdoor activities. It is also possible to go hiking or mountain climbing in the area.
  • Caspian Sea Beaches: Behshahr is located near the Caspian Sea, and there are several beaches that can be visited for swimming, sunbathing, or simply taking in the beauty of the sea.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Behshahr

Behshahr is a wonderful destination for hikers and bikers. There are several hiking and biking trails that can be followed to explore the beautiful natural surroundings. Some popular trails include:

  • The Alborz Mountains: The Alborz Mountains are located near Behshahr, and there are several trails that lead to its peaks.
  • The Sisangan Hiking Trail: This hiking trail in Sisangan Forest Park is perfect for nature lovers and provides a panoramic view of the Caspian Sea.

Best Things to Do in Behshahr: Activities and Tours

Behshahr offers a wide range of activities catering to the interests of all types of travelers. Some of the best things to do in Behshahr are:

  • Attend a Folk Music Performance: Mazandaran province is known for its traditional music, and attending a live performance is a great way to immerse oneself in the local culture.
  • Visit the Caspian Sea: Behshahr is located near the Caspian Sea, and there are several beaches that can be visited for swimming, sunbathing, or simply taking in the beauty of the sea.
  • Take a Tour of Historic Sites: Behshahr has a rich history, and there are several historic sites that can be visited, such as Amir Kabir Dam and Behshahr Historical House.

Food and Drink Scene of Behshahr

Behshahr has a rich food and drink scene, with an emphasis on local ingredients and traditional dishes. Some must-try dishes and local beverages include:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Mirza Ghasemi: A local dish made from grilled eggplant, tomatoes, garlic, and eggs.
  • Sturgeon: The Caspian Sea is known for its sturgeon, which is usually prepared with rice and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Doogh: A refreshing drink made from yogurt, water, salt, and mint leaves.
  • Black tea with rock candy: A traditional drink, which is often served with dates or sweets.

Where to Stay in Behshahr: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Behshahr has several options for accommodation, catering to all types of travelers. Some of the best places to stay in Behshahr are:

  • Caspian Palace Hotel: This hotel features luxurious rooms with beautiful views of the Caspian Sea.
  • Green House Hostel: This hostel offers affordable accommodation with a friendly atmosphere.
  • Sefid Kenar Guesthouse: This guesthouse offers traditional Iranian hospitality and comfortable rooms.

What to Buy in Behshahr: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Behshahr offers several unique souvenirs and local products that are worth bringing back home. Some of the best souvenirs to buy in Behshahr are:

  • Handicrafts: The city is known for its beautiful handicrafts, such as pottery, silk textiles, and wood carvings.
  • Caspian Sea Caviar: The Caspian Sea is known for its high-quality caviar, which is often sold in local markets.

Local Culture and Customs of Behshahr

Behshahr has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors are expected to respect local customs and traditions. Some of the local customs and traditions in Behshahr include:

  • Dress Conservatively: Iran is a Muslim country, and visitors are expected to dress conservatively in public places.
  • Respect Religious Sites: Iran is a deeply religious country, and visitors should respect religious sites and customs.

Best Time to Visit Behshahr

The best time to visit Behshahr is during the spring and fall seasons when the weather is mild, and the landscape is lush and green. The summer months can be hot and humid, while the winter season can be cold and rainy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Behshahr

Do:

  • Learn Some Basic Farsi: Knowing a few basic phrases in Farsi can help you communicate with the locals and make your trip more enjoyable.
  • Respect Local Customs and Traditions: Behshahr has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors are expected to respect local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Drink Alcohol in Public: Alcohol consumption is prohibited in public places in Iran.
  • Engage in PDA: Public displays of affection (PDA) are not acceptable in Iran.

Safety and Health

Safety in Behshahr

Behshahr is a safe destination for travelers. However, visitors are advised to take the necessary precautions, such as keeping their valuables safe and staying aware of their surroundings.

Health in Behshahr

Visitors to Behshahr should be aware of potential health risks, such as heat stroke, sunburn, and dehydration. It is essential to drink plenty of water, wear appropriate clothing, and use sunscreen to protect oneself from the sun.

Getting to Behshahr: Transportation Options and Logistics

Behshahr is easily accessible by air, bus, or car.

Airports Near Behshahr

The closest airport to Behshahr is the Sari International Airport, which is located about 29 km away from the city center.

Bus Stations Near Behshahr

Behshahr has several bus stations, including the Behshahr Bus Station and the Sari Bus Station, which offer bus services to other cities in Iran.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Behshahr, and rent a car from a rental company in Tehran or other major cities in Iran.

How to Get Around in Behshahr

Behshahr is a small city, and most of the attractions can be reached on foot or by taxi. It is also possible to rent a bicycle to explore the city.

Where to Go Next from Behshahr: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Behshahr is a great starting point for exploring other destinations in Mazandaran province, such as:

  • Sari: The capital of Mazandaran province is known for its historic sites and beautiful landscapes.
  • Tonekabon: This small city is famous for its natural beauty and is home to several parks and forests.

Conclusion

Behshahr is a beautiful destination in Iran, with a rich history and culture. Visitors to Behshahr can enjoy hiking, biking, swimming, and experiencing traditional Iranian hospitality. With this guide, travelers can plan the perfect trip to Behshahr and fall in love with this unique destination.
