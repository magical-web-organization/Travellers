Introduction to Dezful

Dezful is a city located in southwestern Iran with a rich history and cultural significance. The city is known for its beautiful architecture, outdoor adventures, and delicious cuisine. With the Zagros Mountains to the east and the Dez and Karkheh rivers flowing through it, Dezful is a city of incredible natural beauty. In this guide, we’ll take you through all the must-visit places and experiences this city has to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Dezful

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Shah Abbas Bridge - Built in the 17th century, this bridge is a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the main historical landmarks of Dezful.
  • Kouhe Sangi Village - This ancient village is home to the remains of a Sassanid castle, as well as an old mosque and several other historical monuments.
  • Dezful Historic City Center - A walk through this historic city center will give you a feel for Dezful’s traditional architecture and rich cultural heritage.
  • Dezful Waterfall - Located north of the city, this waterfall is a must-visit for nature lovers.

Natural Attractions of Dezful: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Zard Kuh Mountain - This can be an excellent hiking destination with a 4,100-meter peak with a stunning view.
  • Dez River - Take a scenic boat ride on the Dez river and enjoy the beautiful natural surroundings.
  • Shahyar Cave - Explore this stunning cave system located near Dezful. With several layers and impressive rock formations, Shahyar Cave is a unique natural treasure.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dezful

Dezful is surrounded by the Zagros Mountains, providing a plethora of options for outdoor adventures. Here are some of the best hiking/biking routes:

  • Mount Paraw - A challenging day-hike, but the view from the peak is worth it.
  • Dezful to Shush Hike - A great hike along the river with a stop at the village of Bavi before arriving at its endpoint in Shush.
  • Dezful to Chenar Shahijan Bike Trail - This beginner-friendly route goes through the countryside starting from Shahid Gomnam Square and ends at a mountain near Chenar Shahijan.

Best Things to Do in Dezful: Activities and Tours

  • Discover dezful with a walking tour - Tour the historical sites of the city with local guides and make new friends.
  • Take a boat on Dez river - Tour the city from a riverside perspective, enjoying music and local food.
  • Explore the city by bike - Rent a bike and try out different routes and embrace a fun activity in the city.

Week-Long Itinerary for Dezful

Day 1: Arrival and city tour Day 2: Visit the historic center of Dezful and its landmarks Day 3: Take a boat ride on Dez and visit Shush, capital of the Elamite Kingdom Day 4: Explore the surrounding countryside by bike Day 5: Hike to one of the nearby mountains Day 6: Explore the ancient Sassanid castle at Kouhe Sangi Village Day 7: Departure

Food and Drink Scene of Dezful

Must-Try Dishes

  • Aash-e-Reshteh - A popular Persian soup made with beans, vegetables, and plenty of spices.
  • Ghormeh Sabzi - A stew made with herbs and kidney beans, traditionally served with rice.
  • Kebab - An iconic Persian dish that needs no explanation.
  • Qalyeh Mahi - A fish dish famous in Dezful and the surrounding area.

Local Beverages

  • Lemonade with Mint - A refreshing drink made with lemon juice and fresh mint leaves.
  • Sharbat-e-Helbeh - A drink made with fenugreek seeds, brown sugar and rose water.
  • Black tea with sugar cubes and Cardamom -A traditional drink served with pastries and sweets.

Where to Stay in Dezful: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Assaluye Platanus Hotel - A luxurious hotel situated in an ancient building.
  • Laleh Hotel - A comfortable hotel with modern amenities and central location.
  • Dezful Grand Hotel - A relatively new hotel in the city center.

What to Buy in Dezful: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Pottery and Ceramics - The city is known for its intricate pottery, and you’ll find plenty of shops selling beautiful ceramics.
  • Spices and Saffron - Persian spices are known for their unique flavors and are an excellent gift for food lovers.
  • Carpets and Kilims - Persian rugs are famous around the world and make excellent souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Dezful

  • Respect for elders - In Persian culture, elders are highly respected, and you should show them the utmost respect when you are in Dezful.
  • Islamic Dress Code - Keep in mind that Iran is an Islamic country, and it’s important to dress conservatively.
  • Hospitality - Persians are known for their hospitality, and you will likely be welcomed warmly into any home or establishment.

Best Time to Visit Dezful

The best time to visit Dezful is during the spring and fall months when the weather is mild, and the countryside is particularly beautiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of Dezful

Do:

  • Dress conservatively, particularly in religious sites.
  • Be polite and respectful to the locals.
  • Haggle in the bazaars.

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public places.
  • Eat in front of others who might be fasting during Ramadan.
  • Touch the opposite gender in public.

Safety and Health

Safety in Dezful

Dezful is generally a safe city but, as with anywhere, it’s always best to exercise caution when traveling.

Health in Dezful

Make sure you have all the necessary vaccinations before visiting Iran. It’s advisable to carry a first-aid kit, particularly if you are planning on doing any outdoor activities.

Getting to Dezful: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Dezful

The nearest international airport is Ahvaz International Airport, which is about an hour’s drive from Dezful.

Bus Stations Near Dezful

Dezful has a well-connected bus network that operates to and from major Iranian cities, such as Tehran and Shiraz.

Other Options

Taxis are also readily available in Dezful, and the city has a Central Station offering ground transportation facilities, including buses.

How to Get Around in Dezful

The best way to get around Dezful is on foot or by bike, as the city is relatively compact. Taxis and buses are also available.

Where to Go Next from Dezful: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some of the best destinations to visit after you’ve explored Dezful:

  • Shush - Capital of the Elamite Kingdom and home to the famous Chogha Zanbil Ziggurat.
  • Ahvaz - A large city by the river Karoun, the city has many parks, museums and bazaars.
  • Tchogha Zanbil (Jozan Pahlavan) - An ancient Elamite temple from 1250 BCE.

Conclusion

Dezful is a beautiful city with plenty to offer visitors, from its rich history and cultural significance to its stunning natural beauty and delicious cuisine. Whether you’re an outdoor adventurer or a history buff, Dezful has something for everyone. Use this guide while planning your trip to this exciting destination, and we’re sure you’ll have a memorable experience.
