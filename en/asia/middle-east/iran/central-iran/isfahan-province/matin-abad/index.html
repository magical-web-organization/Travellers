Introduction to Matin Abad

Matin Abad is one of the few desert eco-camps in the world, located in the heart of Iran’s central desert in Isfahan province. It is an ideal destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those who crave a unique cultural experience. This sustainable tourist destination offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation while embracing responsible travel.

List of Places to Visit in Matin Abad

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Jame Mosque of Ardestan: This beautiful mosque is located near Matin Abad and is one of the oldest and most significant mosques in Iran.

  • Nareen Castle: A historic castle dating back to the Sassanid era, located in Ardestan.

  • Historic Village of Abyaneh: Founded 2,500 years ago, this red brick village is known for its unique architecture, cultural heritage, and traditional dress.

Natural Attractions of Matin Abad: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Desert Safari: Experience the desert landscape of Matin Abad on a thrilling desert jeep ride or a camel ride.

  • Star Gazing: Matin Abad is famous for its clear skies and offers an ideal opportunity for stargazing.

  • Salt Lake: Visit the mesmerizing Salt Lake in Isfahan, a natural wonder where the salt crystals create a unique and beautiful landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Matin Abad

  • Shour River Hiking Trail: A 12-kilometer trail that takes you along the Shour River and is one of the most popular hiking trails in Matin Abad.

  • Biking Route between Matin Abad and Garmeh: A 15-kilometer bike ride that takes you through some of the most beautiful desert landscapes in the region.

Best Things to Do in Matin Abad: Activities and Tours

  • Silk Road Tour: Take a tour of the ancient Silk Road and discover the history and culture of the region.

  • Saffron Fields Tour: Learn about saffron cultivation and production on a tour of the local saffron fields.

  • Traditional Cooking Class: Learn how to cook traditional Iranian dishes with a cooking class led by locals.

Food and Drink Scene of Matin Abad

Must-Try Dishes

  • Gheymeh: A traditional Persian dish made with meat and split peas, served with rice.

  • Tahchin: A delicious Iranian rice cake with saffron and chicken.

  • Kebab: Grilled meat skewers, a popular dish in Iran.

Local Beverages

  • Doogh: A yogurt-based drink mixed with water, mint, and salt.

  • Tea: Tea is a cultural staple in Iran and is served in almost all cafes and restaurants.

Where to Stay in Matin Abad: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Matin Abad Eco-Camp: A sustainable tourist destination offering traditional hand-made yurts, tents and mud houses surrounded by nature.

  • Garmeh Laleh Guest House: A charming guest house located in the nearby village of Garmeh that offers comfortable rooms and traditional meals.

What to Buy in Matin Abad: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Saffron: Matin Abad is one of the biggest saffron producing areas in Iran, and you can purchase local, high quality saffron as a souvenir.

  • Pottery and Ceramics: Local pottery and ceramics are handmade and unique to the region.

Local Culture and Customs of Matin Abad

  • Dress Code: Women are required to wear a headscarf and dress modestly in public spaces.

  • Hospitality: Iranians are famously hospitable, and visitors can expect to be warmly welcomed in Matin Abad.

Best Time to Visit Matin Abad

The best time to visit Matin Abad is from October to May when the temperatures are mild and pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Matin Abad

Do:

  • Dress modestly.

  • Embrace the local culture and customs.

  • Try traditional Iranian dishes.

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public spaces.

  • Display public affection.

Safety and Health

Safety in Matin Abad

Matin Abad is a safe destination for tourists, with low crime rates and friendly people. However, visitors should be aware of the desert environment and take proper precautions when exploring the outdoors.

Health in Matin Abad

Visitors should drink bottled water and take precautions against the sun and heat when spending time outdoors.

Getting to Matin Abad: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Matin Abad

  • Isfahan International Airport: 191km away from Matin Abad.

Bus Stations Near Matin Abad

  • Isfahan Bus Terminal: 190km away from Matin Abad.

Other Options

  • Private car rental services are available from nearby cities like Isfahan.

How to Get Around in Matin Abad

The best way to get around in Matin Abad is by car or bike. Visitors can also arrange local tours with the eco-camp or local guides.

Where to Go Next from Matin Abad: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Isfahan: A historic and cultural destination known for its beautiful architecture, including the UNESCO world heritage site the Naghsh-e Jahan Square.

  • Yazd: A desert city known for its ancient winding streets, Zoroastrian culture, and beautiful mosques.

Conclusion

Matin Abad is a wonderful destination, offering a unique combination of outdoor adventures and cultural experiences. Visitors can explore the desert landscape, learn about traditional Iranian culture, and relax in a sustainable eco-camp. With its incredible natural beauty and welcoming locals, Matin Abad is an unforgettable destination for any traveler.
