Introduction to Aran va Bidgol

Located in Isfahan province, in central Iran, Aran va Bidgol is a small town with rich cultural and natural offerings. With a population of around 45,000, the town is situated between the cities of Kashan and Isfahan, and is easily accessible by road or air. Aran va Bidgol has a rich history with over 17 historical sites and numerous outdoor activities, making it a perfect destination for history buffs and nature enthusiasts.

List of Places to Visit in Aran va Bidgol

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Fin Garden

Fin Garden, also known as Bagh-e Fin, is a historical Persian garden and UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Kashan, a city near Aran va Bidgol. It was designed for Shah Abbas I during the Safavid era and features beautiful landscaped gardens, a palace, and numerous fountains. The garden is a great place to relax and appreciate the beauty of Persian art and architecture.

Bayazid Bastami Tomb

Bayazid Bastami Tomb is a beautiful historical site dedicated to one of the most famous Sufi mystics, Bayazid Bastami. The monument features beautiful tile works and stucco reliefs, and attracts visitors from all over the world.

Agha Bozorg Mosque

Agha Bozorg Mosque is another beautiful site located in Kashan. It was built during the Qajar dynasty and is praised for the unique blend of Iranian and Islamic architectural elements. The mosque is still used for daily prayers and is a great place to learn about Iranian architecture and religion.

Natural Attractions of Aran va Bidgol: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kavir National Park

Kavir National Park is a beautiful desert park located near Aran va Bidgol. It is the largest national park in Iran and features unique landscapes, rock formations, and wildlife such as the Asiatic cheetah.

Salt Lake Urmia

Salt Lake Urmia is a beautiful salt lake located near Aran va Bidgol. It’s surrounded by volcanic mountains and features pink and purple hues during sunrise and sunset, making it a beautiful sight for nature lovers and photographers alike.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Aran va Bidgol

Salt Lake Urmia Hiking Trail

The Salt Lake Urmia Hiking Trail is a beautiful, scenic route for nature enthusiasts. It’s located close to the Salt Lake Urmia and features unique landscapes, rock formations, and wildlife.

Desert Cycling Route

The Desert Cycling Route is a beautiful, scenic route for cycling enthusiasts. It’s located near Kavir National Park and features unique landscapes, rock formations, and wildlife such as the Asiatic cheetah.

Best Things to Do in Aran va Bidgol: Activities and Tours

Historical Tours

Historical Tours are a great way to explore the rich history of Aran va Bidgol and nearby cities such as Kashan and Isfahan. These tours are offered by local tour operators and include visits to historical sites like Fin Garden, Bayazid Bastami Tomb, and Agha Bozorg Mosque.

Outdoor Adventures

Outdoor Adventures are a great way to explore the natural beauty of Aran va Bidgol and the surrounding areas. These adventures include hiking, cycling, and wildlife spotting in Kavir National Park and Salt Lake Urmia.

Food and Drink Scene of Aran va Bidgol

Must-Try Dishes

Khoresht-e Khoresh Bademjan

Khoresht-e Khoresh Bademjan is a delicious Persian stew made with eggplant, tomato, and meat. It’s a popular dish in Aran va Bidgol and nearby cities and is often served with rice.

Kabab Koobideh

Kabab Koobideh is a traditional Iranian dish made with ground beef or lamb that is seasoned with herbs and spices and grilled on a skewer. It’s a must-try dish for meat lovers.

Local Beverages

Doogh

Doogh is a traditional Iranian drink made with yoghurt, water, and various herbs and spices. It’s a popular drink during hot weather and is often served with kababs.

Tea

Tea is a favorite drink in Iran and is often served with cookies and other sweets. It’s a must-try beverage for tea lovers.

Local Culture and Customs of Aran va Bidgol

Iranian culture is rich and diverse, and is heavily influenced by Islamic traditions. It’s important to respect the local culture and traditions, including following Islamic dress codes in mosques and other religious sites. Iranians are known for their hospitality, and visitors can expect to be welcomed with open arms.

Best Time to Visit Aran va Bidgol

The best time to visit Aran va Bidgol is during the spring (March to May) and fall (September to November) when the weather is mild and pleasant. Summers can be hot with temperatures reaching up to 40 degrees Celsius.
