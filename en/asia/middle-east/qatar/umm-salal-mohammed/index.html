Introduction to Umm Salal Mohammed

Umm Salal Mohammed is a small town located in Qatar’s northern region, around 25 kilometers north of the capital city, Doha. The town is named after the neighboring Umm Salal Ali village and is well-known for its historical significance, cultural heritage, and natural beauty. It’s the perfect destination for travelers looking for an immersive experience that blends Qatari traditions and modern life.

List of Places to Visit in Umm Salal Mohammed

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Umm Salal Fort: This ancient fort dates back to the 19th century and served as a strategic military post for guarding the water reservoirs.
  • Sheikh Faisal Bin Qassim Al Thani Museum: This museum holds one of the largest private collections of Islamic and Qatari art, including rare manuscripts, antique vehicles, and weapons.
  • Al Zubara Archaeological Site: This UNESCO World Heritage Site is one of the most well-preserved examples of an 18th and 19th century coastal town in the Persian Gulf.
  • Baladiyat Umm Salal: This municipality building is a great example of Qatari architecture, with its modern design inspired by traditional motifs.

Natural Attractions of Umm Salal Mohammed: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Umm Salal Natural Reserve: This nature reserve is home to several unique species such as the spiny-tailed lizard, sand gazelle, and desert hare.
  • Mystery of Qatar: Enjoy a desert safari and embark on an exploration of Qatar’s stunning landscapes, where you can experience camel rides, falconry, and traditional Bedouin-style dining.
  • Al Khor Beach: This peaceful beach is an ideal getaway from the busy city life, offering a relaxing atmosphere and clear blue waters.
  • Al Thakira Mangroves: Take a kayaking tour of the mangroves, where you can spot flamingoes, herons, and other bird species.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Umm Salal Mohammed

  • Purple Island: This island has a 5.5 km nature trail, where visitors can explore the mangroves, spot bird species, and enjoy a picnic.
  • Al Khor Park: The park has several trails through manicured gardens, playgrounds, and a mini-zoo.
  • Al Nakhsh Archaeological Park: This park has several trails that take you through ancient settlements, tombs, and defensive structures.

Best Things to Do in Umm Salal Mohammed: Activities and Tours

  • Camel Racing: Attend a camel race at the Sheikh Faisal Camel Race Arena, which is considered one of the most prestigious camel racing tracks in Qatar.
  • Traditional Cooking Classes: Join a traditional Arabic cooking class, where you can learn how to make mouth-watering dishes like Machboos (spiced rice with meat or fish) and Luqaimat (sweet dumplings).
  • Dune Bashing: Take a thrilling ride over the sand dunes, which is a perfect activity for adventure enthusiasts.
  • Offshore Fishing: Enjoy a day of cruising the sea while fishing, where you can catch popular fish like Hamour, Grouper, and Snapper.

Food and Drink Scene of Umm Salal Mohammed

Must-Try Dishes

  • Machboos: The national Qatari dish is a flavorful rice dish cooked with meat or fish, onions, tomatoes, and a blend of spices.
  • Thareed: This is a popular bread and stew dish prepared with lamb, beef, or chicken and vegetables like carrots, potatoes, and beans.
  • Balaleet: A dessert made of vermicelli noodles, sugar, cardamom powder, and a hint of saffron.

Local Beverages

  • Karake: A popular tea beverage prepared with strong black tea, sugar, cardamom, and saffron.
  • Laban: A local yoghurt drink, also known as buttermilk and traditionally enjoyed with mint.

Where to Stay in Umm Salal Mohammed: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Al Sultan Beach Resort: This beachfront resort offers multiple swimming pools, restaurants, and a variety of room options.
  • Retaj Al Rayyan Hotel: This 4-star hotel is located in the heart of Doha and offers luxurious rooms, multiple restaurants, and a rooftop pool.
  • Al Sultan Garden Compound: This resort-style compound offers spacious apartments and villas, a gym, swimming pool, and tennis court.

What to Buy in Umm Salal Mohammed: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Traditional Clothing: Kuwaiti Salwar Kameez, Farass, and Abaya are traditional clothing items for men and women.
  • Pottery: The ceramics and pottery items available in Umm Salal Mohammed are unique, handmade pieces that are a great souvenir.
  • Spices and Dates: The Souq Waqif Market in Doha is famous for its variety of spices and fresh dates.

Local Culture and Customs of Umm Salal Mohammed

Refrain from wearing revealing clothing in public, remove your shoes before entering houses or mosques, and follow Qatari customs like not using your left hand to eat. Respect the local traditions, customs, and cultural practices.

Best Time to Visit Umm Salal Mohammed

The best time to visit Umm Salal Mohammed is between October to April since the weather is pleasant, with clear skies and moderate temperatures (around 25°C).

Do’s and Don’ts of Umm Salal Mohammed

Do:

  • Respect the local traditions and customs.
  • Dress modestly in public areas.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public.
  • Show public displays of affection.
  • Take photographs of people without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Umm Salal Mohammed

Umm Salal Mohammed is relatively safe and has no significant security issues. However, it’s always advisable to take care of your belongings and remain vigilant in crowded public areas.

Health in Umm Salal Mohammed

Healthcare in Qatar is of high standards, and visitors can rely on the excellent private and public healthcare facilities available in the country.

Getting to Umm Salal Mohammed: Transportation Options and Logistics

There are various transportation options available to reach Umm Salal Mohammed, including:

Airports Near Umm Salal Mohammed

  • Hamad International Airport: Located in Doha, just a 30-minute drive from Umm Salal Mohammed.

Bus Stations Near Umm Salal Mohammed

  • Umm Salal Ali Bus Station: The nearest bus station is located a short distance away from Umm Salal Mohammed.

Other Options

  • Taxis are available from Doha, and travelers can also rent a car or take a private transfer to Umm Salal Mohammed.

How to Get Around in Umm Salal Mohammed

Car rental is the best option to get around the town since public transportation options are limited. Taxis are available in the town, but they can be expensive.

Where to Go Next from Umm Salal Mohammed: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Doha: The vibrant capital city of Qatar that offers a blend of traditional and modern experiences.
  • Al Wakrah: A coastal city that offers stunning beaches, beautiful architecture, and an old souq.
  • Al Khor: A scenic coastal town that offers beautiful beaches, mangroves, and an enchanting Corniche.

Conclusion

Umm Salal Mohammed is a beautiful town in Qatar that offers travelers a unique blend of traditional culture and modern experiences. Visitors can explore historical landmarks, immerse themselves in natural beauty, and indulge in local cuisine and activities.
