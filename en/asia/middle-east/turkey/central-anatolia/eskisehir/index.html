Introduction to Eskişehir

Eskişehir is a beautiful city located in the heart of Anatolia, Turkey. This charming city is famous for its rich culture, friendly people, beautiful architecture, museums, and natural beauty. Eskişehir has a unique vibe with many parks, green spaces, cafes, and restaurants.

List of Places to Visit in Eskişehir

Eskişehir is a city full of beautiful locations to visit. Whether your interests are in art, history, nature or just experiencing the local culture, Eskişehir has something for everyone. Here are our top picks for the best places to visit in Eskişehir:

  2. Eskişehir Ethnography Museum

Local Culture and Customs of Eskişehir

Eskişehir is a city renowned for its people’s hospitality, kindness, and a great local sense of humor. Here are some etiquette tips to keep in mind when visiting Eskişehir:

  1. Handshaking is an essential part of greeting
  2. Don’t forget to dress modestly while visiting the Mosque
  3. It is customary to take off shoes in mosques and homes
  4. Always leave a tip for good services

Best Time to Visit Eskişehir

The best time to visit Eskişehir is during the summer months, from June to September, when there are longer daylight hours and less rainfall. The weather during these months is pleasant and perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Eskişehir

Do:

Safety and Health

Safety in Eskişehir

Eskişehir is known as a safe place, but travelers should take standard precautions, such as keeping valuables out of sight, especially while walking in busy areas.

Health in Eskişehir

Eskişehir has no specific health concerns for travelers. Tap water is not drinkable, so visitors should drink bottled water throughout their stay.

Getting to Eskişehir: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Eskişehir

The closest airport to Eskişehir is the Eskisehir Anadolu Airport, which is located about 7 km from the city center. Other nearby airports include the Kütahya Zafer Airport and the Ankara Esenboga International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Eskişehir

Eskişehir has two main bus stations: the Eskişehir Bus Station and the Eskişehir Intercity Bus Terminal. These stations are well connected and offer daily bus services to several destinations throughout Turkey.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel to Eskişehir by train, as it is well-connected to several Turkish cities, including Istanbul, Ankara, and Izmir.

How to Get Around in Eskişehir

Eskişehir is an easy city to get around in, with a developed public transportation system that includes buses and trams. Visitors can also use taxis or simply walk around town, as many of the main attractions are in the city center.

Where to Go Next from Eskişehir: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Eskişehir is a great base for visiting nearby cities, historical sites, and other tourist attractions. Here are some ideas for excursions:

  1. Visit the hot springs in Afyonkarahisar
  2. Explore the ancient city of Hierapolis in Pamukkale
  3. Travel to the capital, Ankara, to discover Turkey’s history
  4. Visit the Sakarya River, located nearby

Conclusion

Eskişehir is a city that offers something for everyone, with abundant natural beauty, rich history and culture, delicious local cuisine and a friendly and welcoming population. With so much to see and do in Eskişehir, it is the perfect destination for any traveler. Plan your trip today to discover all that this beautiful city has to offer!
