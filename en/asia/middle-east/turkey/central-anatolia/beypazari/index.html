Beypazarı, a small town located in Central Anatolia, is a hidden gem to discover in Turkey. The town is known for its well-preserved traditional Ottoman architecture, natural beauty, and delicious cuisine. Whether you are interested in history, outdoor adventures, or just looking for a relaxing getaway, Beypazarı has something for you. In this ultimate travel guide, we will explore the best things to see, do, eat, and experience in Beypazarı.

Introduction to Beypazarı

Beypazarı is nestled in the hillsides of Central Anatolia, surrounded by beautiful mountain ranges and green valleys. The town is known for its unique architecture and cultural heritage, which dates back to the Ottoman Empire. The streets are lined with traditional Ottoman houses made of mudbrick, which were used to keep the houses cool in the hot, dry summers. The town’s history can be traced back to the 8th century BC, and it has been inhabited by different civilizations throughout the centuries.

List of Places to Visit in Beypazarı

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

One of the main attractions of Beypazarı is its historical and cultural sites that offer a glimpse into the town’s past. Here are some of the must-visit museums and historical places:

  • Beypazarı Museum: Located in a restored Ottoman mansion, the Beypazarı Museum showcases the town’s history, culture, and art. The museum contains artifacts and exhibits from different periods of Beypazarı’s history.

  • The Grand Mosque: The Grand Mosque is one of the most prominent landmarks of Beypazarı. The mosque was built in the 17th century and is an excellent example of Ottoman architecture. The interior of the mosque is beautifully decorated with colorful tiles and calligraphy.

  • Ince Minaret Medrese: Ince Minaret Medrese is a 13th-century theological school restored in the 19th century. The building is an architectural masterpiece, with intricate decorations and ornate carvings.

Natural Attractions of Beypazarı: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Beypazarı is home to some of the most beautiful natural attractions in Turkey. From stunning valleys to mountain peaks, here are some of the best places to explore:

  • Beypazarı Valley: Beypazarı Valley is a scenic valley that offers breathtaking views of the town and the surrounding mountains. The valley is perfect for hiking and exploring the local flora and fauna.

  • Ayasofya Mountain: Ayasofya Mountain is located just outside of Beypazarı town and is a popular spot for hiking and picnicking. The mountain offers stunning views of the town and the surrounding countryside.

  • Salt Lake: Salt Lake is a large saline lake located near Beypazarı. The lake is famous for its therapeutic properties and is a popular spot for swimming and sunbathing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Beypazarı

Beypazarı is a hiker’s paradise, with numerous hiking trails and biking routes to explore. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Beypazarı:

  • Beypazarı Trail: Beypazarı Trail is an 18-kilometer hiking trail that takes you through the town’s natural and cultural highlights. The trail passes through beautiful valleys, scenic viewpoints, and historical landmarks.

  • Ayasofya Mountain Trail: Ayasofya Mountain Trail is a challenging 9-kilometer hiking trail that takes you to the summit of Ayasofya Mountain. The trail offers fantastic views of the town and the surrounding mountains.

Best Things to Do in Beypazarı: Activities and Tours

Whether you are interested in exploring Beypazarı’s culture, history, or natural beauty, there are plenty of things to do and see in town. Here are some of the best activities and tours in Beypazarı:

  • Beypazarı Walking Tour: A walking tour of Beypazarı is the best way to explore the town’s architectural and cultural heritage. You’ll get to see Ottoman houses, historical landmarks, and traditional markets.

  • Beypazarı Cooking Class: Beypazarı is known for its delicious cuisine, and what better way to taste it than to learn how to cook it yourself? Take a cooking class and learn how to make traditional dishes like stuffed grape leaves and baklava.

Food and Drink Scene of Beypazarı

Beypazarı is known for its delicious cuisine, which is a blend of Ottoman, Persian, and Arab influences. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local drinks:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Beypazarı Tava: Beypazarı Tava is a traditional dish made with lamb or beef, peppers, and tomatoes. The dish is cooked in a clay pot and served with rice or bulgur.

  • Güvec: Güvec is a hearty stew made with meat, vegetables, and spices, cooked in a clay pot. It is served with bread or rice.

  • Baklava: Baklava is a sweet pastry made with layers of phyllo dough and honey syrup. Beypazarı is famous for its delicious baklava, which is made with local honey and pistachios.

Local Beverages

  • Boza: Boza is a traditional Turkish drink made with fermented wheat or barley. It has a sweet and sour taste and is usually served with cinnamon and roasted chickpeas.

  • Ayran: Ayran is a refreshing yogurt-based drink, popular in Turkey. It is served cold and is perfect for hot summer days.

Where to Stay in Beypazarı: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Beypazarı has a range of accommodation options for every budget. Here are some of the best places to stay in town:

  • Beypazarı Söğüt Hotel: Beypazarı Söğüt Hotel is a modern hotel located in the town center. The hotel offers comfortable rooms, a restaurant, and a sauna.

  • Beypazarı Guesthouse: Beypazarı Guesthouse is a cozy guesthouse located in the old town. The guesthouse offers traditional-style rooms, a garden, and a terrace to relax and unwind.

What to Buy in Beypazarı: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Beypazarı is famous for its local handicrafts and products. Here are some of the best souvenirs and local products to bring home:

  • Beypazarı Blankets: Beypazarı blankets are handmade woolen blankets, woven by local artisans. They come in different colors and designs and make a perfect souvenir.

  • Soapwort: Soapwort is a herb that grows locally in the Beypazarı region. It is used to make natural soap and is perfect for skincare and haircare products.

Local Culture and Customs of Beypazarı

Best Time to Visit Beypazarı

The best time to visit Beypazarı is during the spring and autumn seasons, between March and May or September and November. The weather is mild and pleasant, and the natural and cultural attractions are at their best. Summer months can be hot and dry, while winter can be cold and snowy.

Safety and Health

Safety in Beypazarı

Beypazarı is generally a safe town to visit, with low crime rates and a friendly local community. However, accidents and injuries can happen, especially during outdoor activities. Here are some safety tips:

  • Always wear appropriate clothing and footwear for outdoor activities.
  • Bring plenty of water and sun protection during hot weather.
  • Watch out for loose rocks and slippery trails during hiking and biking.

Health in Beypazarı

Beypazarı has a good healthcare system, with hospitals and clinics available in the town center. However, it’s always a good idea to bring a first-aid kit and any necessary medication.

Getting to Beypazarı: Transportation Options and Logistics

Beypazarı is located in Central Anatolia, approximately 100 kilometers from Ankara. Here are some transportation options to get to Beypazarı:

  • By Air: The nearest airport to Beypazarı is Ankara Esenboğa Airport, which is approximately 120 kilometers away. From the airport, you can take a bus or taxi to Beypazarı.

  • By Bus: Beypazarı has a bus station located in the town center, with frequent bus services to Ankara and other surrounding towns.

  • By Car: Beypazarı is located on the Ankara-Konya highway, which makes it easily accessible by car.

How to Get Around in Beypazarı

Beypazarı is a small town, and most of the attractions are within walking distance of each other. However, if you prefer to use public transportation, there are local buses available. Taxis are also available, and you can rent a car or bike to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Beypazarı: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions to continue your journey after Beypazarı:

  • Ankara: The capital city of Turkey, Ankara, is less than 2 hours away from Beypazarı. Explore the city’s history, culture, and cuisine.

  • Nallıhan: Nallıhan is a historic town located 50 kilometers away from Beypazarı. It is known for its natural beauty and traditional pottery.

  • Gordion: Gordion is an ancient city located 50 kilometers away from Beypazarı. It is home to the famous Phrygian tomb of King Midas.

Conclusion

Beypazarı is a beautiful town that offers a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. From exploring Ottoman houses to hiking in the mountains, Beypazarı has something for everyone. Plan your trip to Beypazarı and discover the hidden gem of Central Anatolia.
