Introduction to Divriği

Nestled in the heart of Central Anatolia, Divriği is a hidden gem that is known for its historical and cultural significance. The city is home to one of the most remarkable structures from the late 12th and early 13th centuries, the Great Mosque and Hospital of Divriği, which is a UNESCO World Heritage site. With its rich history, charming atmosphere, and stunning natural scenery, Divriği is a must-visit destination for anyone exploring Turkey.

List of Places to Visit in Divriği

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Divriği is a treasure trove of historical and cultural attractions that are waiting to be explored. Here are some of the must-visit museums and historical places in Divriği:

  • Great Mosque and Hospital of Divriği
  • Ulu Mosque
  • Divriği Castle
  • İbrahim Bey Mosque
  • Hamidiye Mosque
  • Abdi Pasha Mansion

Natural Attractions of Divriği: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you’re looking for outdoor adventures and breathtaking natural scenery, Divriği won’t disappoint. Here are some of the top spots to explore in and around the city:

  • Karagöl Tabiat Parkı
  • Vadi-i Sawra (Sawra Canyon)
  • Ağlayan Kaya (Weeping Rock)
  • Uzun Çayır Gölü (Long Meadow Lake)
  • Karaca Cave
  • Keban Dam

Hiking/Biking Routes of Divriği

If you’re an avid hiker or biker, Divriği is an excellent destination for you. With its stunning natural landscape and scenic routes, you can explore the beauty of the region on foot or by bike. Here are some popular routes:

  • Divriği - Güzeloluk Hike
  • Divriği - Sivas Hike
  • Divriği - Kartalkaya Mountain Hike
  • Çaltı Valley Trekking Route

Best Things to Do in Divriği: Activities and Tours

Divriği offers plenty of activities and tours for tourists to make the most of their trip. Here are some of the best things to do in Divriği:

  • Visit Ulu Mosque and the Great Mosque and Hospital of Divriği for its outstanding is architectural design.
  • Take a tour of the Abdi Pasha Mansion and learn about its historical significance.
  • Go hiking at the Sawra Canyon or Karagöl Tabiat Parkı for scenic views and fresh air.
  • Try traditional Turkish dishes such as kebab, baklava at local restaurants.
  • Shop for local handicrafts such as traditional carpets, antique jewellery and leather goods.

Week-Long Itinerary for Divriği

For those looking to spend complete time in Divriği here is a perfect week-long itinerary:

Day 1: Arrive in Divriği and visit the Great Mosque and Hospital of Divriği.

Day 2: Explore the Abdi Pasha Mansion and Ulu Mosque.

Day 3: Hike at Sawra Canyon, located about an hour from Divriği.

Day 4: Take a trip to Karagöl Tabiat Parkı to explore the lake and surrounding forest.

Day 5: Explore the Keban Dam and the surrounding area.

Day 6: Visit the Karaca Cave, around two hours from Divriği.

Day 7: Shop for local souvenirs and relax before departing.

Food and Drink Scene of Divriği

Must-Try Dishes

Turkish cuisine is known for its diverse flavours and mouth-watering dishes, and Divriği is no exception. Here are some local dishes to try:

  • Cılbır (poached eggs with yoghurt and spice oil)
  • Keskek (a hearty soup made with chicken or lamb and cracked wheat)
  • Testi Kebap (slow-cooked lamb or beef in a clay pot)
  • Ayran- refreshing savoury yoghurt drink mixed with ice water

Local Beverages

Turkish tea, coffee and local drinks such as Ayran and Salgam Suyu, a fermented juice made of turnips and carrots are popular in Divriği.

Where to Stay in Divriği: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Divriği is a small city, but there are plenty of accommodation options for those visiting. Here are some of the best places to stay in Divriği:

  • Divriği Ceylan Hotel
  • Taşpinar Hotel
  • Sev Sivas Hotel

What to Buy in Divriği: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Divriği is known for its traditional handicrafts, and there is plenty to buy as a souvenir for loved ones or to bring home a sense of the city. Here are some of the local items to look out for when shopping:

  • Handwoven carpets and kilims
  • Antique jewellery made of silver, copper, and bronze
  • Leather goods such as bags and belts

Local Culture and Customs of Divriği

Divriği has a rich cultural heritage, and the city is keen to preserve its traditions. When visiting, respect local customs and culture, and dress modestly while exploring historic and religious sites. Ramadan is an essential event in Divriği, and if you’re visiting during this time, it will be nice to experience the local community’s hospitality.

Best Time to Visit Divriği

The best time to visit Divriği is during the shoulder season from May to October when the weather is mild, and the scenery is at its most attractive. From December to February is winter, and if you’re looking to go winter sports activities like skiing, February is your best bet.

Do’s and Don’ts of Divriği

Do:

  • Respect local culture and traditions.
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
  • Try local food and beverages.
  • Seek out local handicrafts and products.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect locals or their customs and traditions.
  • Visit religious sites with shoes on.
  • Wear revealing clothing or clothes that are disrespectful to local culture.

Safety and Health

Safety in Divriği

Divriği is generally safe for visitors. Petty crime can happen in any tourist destination, so it’s wise to take precautions such as keeping an eye on your belongings and avoiding unlit areas at night.

Health in Divriği

Divriği is a small city, and medical facilities are limited. Always ensure you have comprehensive travel insurance covering any pre-existing health conditions. It’s advisable to take medication for any existing health conditions with you and, if needed, arrange for any prescriptions before departing.

Getting to Divriği: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Divriği

The nearest airport to Divriği is Sivas Airport, 100 km (1.5 hours) by car or bus ride from the city.

Bus Stations Near Divriği

Divriği has a well-connected bus station, with services provided by several major bus lines.

Other Options

Taxis are widely available in Divriği and can be used as a mobile guide to touring the city or getting around the vicinity.

How to Get Around in Divriği

Divriği is a small city, and it’s easy to explore it on foot. However, if you plan to visit nearby areas, taxis and buses are readily accessible.

Where to Go Next from Divriği: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some of the nearby destinations from Divriği, which is home to countless other historical and natural wonders include:

  • Sivas
  • Erzincan
  • Erzurum
  • Malatya
  • Kayseri

Conclusion

Divriği is an excellent place to visit for those looking to learn about Turkey’s rich history, culture, and natural beauty. With its top-notch historical sites, outdoor activities, delicious cuisine, and breathtaking views, it’s no wonder that Divriği is becoming an increasingly popular destination for tourists.
