Introduction to Elmali

Elmali is a stunning town situated in Lycia, a region located in Mediterranean Turkey. Nestled in the foothills of the Taurus Mountains, Elmali is a paradise for nature lovers and adventurers.

Elmali is a town steeped in history, with its roots dating back to the 14th century. It was an essential trading hub in the Ottoman Empire, and the influence of the empire can still be seen today. The town’s name derives from the Turkish word ‘elma,’ meaning apple, as it was once renowned for apple production.

Read on to discover the best places to visit and things to do in Elmali.

List of Places to Visit in Elmali

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Elmali Museum

The Elmali Museum is a must-visit for history buffs, displaying artifacts and exhibits from Ottoman times. You can learn about the town’s history, from its role in the Ottoman Empire to its apple and timber production.

Yapı Kredi Cultural Center

The Yapı Kredi Cultural Center is a cultural hub located in the town center. Here, you can explore authentic Turkish art and attend various cultural events such as art exhibitions, workshops, and concerts.

Natural Attractions of Elmali: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Elmali Forest

The Elmali Forest is a magical place to explore, with its winding pathways and abundance of natural beauty. You can hike or bike through the forest and admire the numerous waterfalls, including the Ucansu Waterfall.

Olympos National Park

If you enjoy outdoor activities, then Olympos National Park should be on your list of places to visit. This protected natural park is home to numerous hiking trails, stunning coastal views, and unique flora and fauna. The highlight is the Chimera, a natural phenomenon where flames shoot out of the ground.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Elmali

The Lycian Way

The Lycian Way is a 540-kilometer-long walking trail that starts from Elmali and ends in Antalya. It is perfect for hikers, nature lovers and offers breathtaking views of the mountainous region.

Mountain Biking

For a more adrenaline-fueled adventure, head out for mountain biking in the town’s rugged terrain. With its numerous trails and stunning landscapes, Elmali’s surroundings offer plenty of opportunities for off-road cycling.

Best Things to Do in Elmali: Activities and Tours

Hot Air Balloon Ride

Experience Elmali from above by taking a hot air balloon ride. This early morning activity offers a stunning sunrise view of the town and the mountains.

Turkish Bath and Massage

Relax and unwind with a traditional Turkish bath and massage. The town has several Turkish baths, including the Elmali Hamami, where you can experience a traditional Turkish spa treatment.

Week-Long Itinerary for Elmali

Day Activities
Day 1-2 Explore the town’s historical sites
Day 3-4 Hike through Elmali’s rugged mountain terrain
Day 5-6 Take a hot air balloon ride and relax in Turkish baths
Day 7 Visit the Chimera in Olympos National Park and experience the natural phenomenon

Food and Drink Scene of Elmali

Must-Try Dishes

Elmalı Tatlisi

Elmalı Tatlisi is a local specialty and a dessert made from apples, sugar, cinnamon, and nuts. It is the perfect end to a delicious meal.

Köfte

Köfte is a traditional Turkish meatball made from beef or lamb, and Elmali is famous for its delicious recipe.

Local Beverages

Turkish Tea

No visit to Elmali is complete without having a cup of Turkish tea. Served in small glasses, this tea is strong, sweet, and refreshing.

Raki

Raki is an anise-flavored alcoholic beverage that is a popular drink in Turkey. It is usually consumed with meze, a selection of small dishes served as an appetizer.

Where to Stay in Elmali: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Elmali has several hotels, hostels, and guesthouses to choose from, depending on your budget.

Luxx Cave Hotel

The Luxx Cave Hotel is a unique boutique hotel located in the town center, featuring cave-like rooms and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Akpinar Hotel

If you’re looking for a more luxurious stay, check out the Akpinar Hotel. This five-star hotel offers an outdoor pool, spa facilities, and beautiful mountain views.

What to Buy in Elmali: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Elmali Apples

As the name suggests, Elmali is famous for its apples, and you can pick up a box of fresh apples from the local market to take home.

Carpets and Rugs

Turkey is known for its beautiful handmade carpets and rugs, and Elmali is no exception. You can find several carpet shops in the town center.

Local Culture and Customs of Elmali

Elmali has a rich history and culture, and it’s essential to respect the local customs and traditions.

Dress Appropriately

When visiting mosques or other religious sites, make sure to dress modestly and cover your head.

Respect Elders

In Turkish culture, elders are accorded significant respect. Make sure to use respectful language and show deference to the elderly.

Best Time to Visit Elmali

The best time to visit Elmali is from March to October when the weather is warm and sunny. If you’re interested in seeing the apple blossoms, visit in the spring when the trees are in bloom.

Do’s and Don’ts of Elmali

Do:

  • Try traditional Turkish food
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Explore the town’s historical sites

Don’t:

  • Disrespect elders
  • Dress inappropriately when visiting religious sites
  • Litter in natural areas

Safety and Health

Safety in Elmali

Overall, Elmali is a safe place to visit. However, it’s essential to take standard precautions such as not flaunting expensive items and not walking alone at night.

Health in Elmali

The tap water in Elmali is safe to drink, but it’s always a good idea to carry a water bottle.

Getting to Elmali: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Elmali

The closest airport to Elmali is Antalya International Airport, located approximately 100 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Elmali

Elmali has a bus station located in the town center that offers transportation to and from neighboring cities and towns.

Other Options

Another way to reach Elmali is by renting a car or hiring a private transfer from Antalya Airport.

How to Get Around in Elmali

The best way to get around Elmali is by walking or renting a bicycle. The town center is compact and easy to navigate on foot or by bike.

Where to Go Next from Elmali: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Antalya

Antalya is a beautiful coastal city located approximately 100 kilometers away from Elmali. It is known for its beautiful beaches, ancient ruins, and vibrant nightlife.

Lycian Way

The Lycian Way is an extensive hiking trail that runs along the Mediterranean coast, covering over 500 kilometers. It is perfect for adventure enthusiasts who want to explore the region’s natural beauty

Conclusion

Elmali is a hidden gem in Turkey, offering something for everyone. Whether you want to soak up the town’s rich history or explore the rugged mountain terrain, Elmali is a must-visit destination for any traveler. Plan your trip now, and create unforgettable memories to last a lifetime.
