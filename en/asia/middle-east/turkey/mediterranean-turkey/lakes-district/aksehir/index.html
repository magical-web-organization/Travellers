Introduction to Akşehir

Located in the Lakes District of Mediterranean Turkey, Akşehir is a charming town with a fascinating history, stunning natural beauty, and a vibrant local culture. From its museums and historical attractions to its outdoor adventures and delicious local cuisine, Akşehir has something to offer every type of traveler.

List of Places to Visit in Akşehir

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Akşehir Museum: Learn about the history and culture of this fascinating region, from ancient civilizations to modern times.
  • Nasrettin Hoca Ceramics Museum: Discover the region’s rich tradition of ceramic arts and crafts.
  • Akşehir Castle: Explore the ruins of this ancient castle, which dates back to the Roman era.
  • Akşehir Mosque: Admire the beautiful architecture of this historic mosque, one of the oldest in the area.

Natural Attractions of Akşehir: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Akşehir Lake: Take a leisurely stroll along the shores of this picturesque lake, or rent a boat and explore the tranquil waters.
  • Eynif Plain: Enjoy a scenic hike through this stunning natural landscape, with beautiful views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Kızıldağ Nature Park: Discover the unique flora and fauna of this expansive park, which is home to a diverse range of plant and animal species.
  • Komando Canyon: Embark on a thrilling whitewater rafting adventure through this rushing river canyon, surrounded by breathtaking scenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Akşehir

  • The Eynif Plain Hiking Trail: This 12-km trail takes you through some of the most beautiful natural landscapes in the region, with stunning views of mountains, forests, and plains.
  • Kızıldağ National Park Trekking Route: Experience the best of the Kızıldağ Nature Park on this 20-km hike through mountainous terrain, with views of wildflowers, wildlife, and more.
  • Akşehir Mountain Biking Trail: Take your mountain bike along this scenic 15-km route, which includes challenging uphill climbs, steep descents, and thrilling singletrack paths.

Best Things to Do in Akşehir: Activities and Tours

  • Taste local cuisine: Don’t miss the chance to sample delicious local dishes, such as lamb kebab, Turkish delight, and baklava.
  • Explore historic sites: Discover the rich history of this ancient region, with visits to museums, castles, and other historical landmarks.
  • Experience local culture: Attend a traditional Turkish carpet-weaving demonstration, watch artisans at work in local ceramics workshops, or take part in a traditional Turkish tea ceremony.

Week-Long Itinerary for Akşehir

Day 1: Arrival and exploration of Akşehir Day 2: Visit the Akşehir Museum and the Nasrettin Hoca Ceramics Museum Day 3: Take a relaxing boat ride on Akşehir Lake, followed by a hike in the nearby Eynif Plain Day 4: Embark on a thrilling whitewater rafting adventure through Komando Canyon Day 5: Explore the Kızıldağ National Park on a guided trekking tour Day 6: Learn about local culture with visits to traditional carpet-weaving workshops and local tea and baklava shops Day 7: Departure

Food and Drink Scene of Akşehir

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lamb kebab: Tender and flavorful lamb, grilled to perfection and served with vegetables and rice.
  • Turkish delight: A sweet and fragrant confection made from sugar, starch, and flavored with rosewater, lemon, or mint.
  • Baklava: Layers of flaky pastry, honey, and nuts, baked to perfection and served with a warming cup of Turkish tea.

Local Beverages

  • Turkish tea: Served hot and strong, this ubiquitous drink is a staple of Turkish culture and hospitality.
  • Turkish coffee: Rich, dark, and aromatic, Turkish coffee is brewed in a traditional copper pot and served with a sweet treat.
  • Şalgam juice: Made from fermented carrots and turnips, this sour drink is a unique and refreshing local specialty.

Where to Stay in Akşehir: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Dedeman Konya Hotel & Convention Center: A modern and comfortable hotel with great amenities, including an outdoor pool, spa services, and a fitness center.
  • Otel Hışman: A cozy and affordable guesthouse with clean and comfortable rooms, located near the city center.
  • Akşehir Pınarbaşı Otel: A charming and welcoming guesthouse with stunning views of the surrounding countryside and a quiet, relaxed atmosphere.

What to Buy in Akşehir: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Turkish carpets: These beautiful and intricate hand-woven carpets are a beloved staple of Turkish culture and make a unique and timeless souvenir.
  • Ceramic arts and crafts: From pottery and ceramics to painted plates and figurines, the local artisans of Akşehir create beautiful and unique pieces that make perfect gifts or home decor items.
  • Local spices: Stock up on locally produced spices, such as sumac, saffron, and cumin, which make a fantastic addition to any kitchen.

Local Culture and Customs of Akşehir

The locals of Akşehir are known for their hospitality and warmth, welcoming visitors with open arms and sharing their traditions and customs with pride. Islam is the predominant religion, and visitors are encouraged to respect local customs and dress modestly when visiting cultural or religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Akşehir

The best time to visit Akşehir is in the spring or fall, when temperatures are mild and the weather is pleasant for outdoor exploration and hiking. Summers can be hot and humid, while winters can be cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Akşehir

Do:

  • Dress modestly when visiting cultural or religious sites
  • Learn a few Turkish phrases to help you communicate with locals
  • Try local cuisine and attend traditional cultural events and celebrations

Don’t:

  • Neglect to remove your shoes and cover your head when entering a mosque or other religious site
  • Drink tap water or eat uncooked fruits or vegetables if you have a sensitive stomach
  • Touch or disturb historic artifacts or landmarks

Safety and Health

Safety in Akşehir

Overall, Akşehir is a peaceful and safe destination, with low levels of crime and violence. Visitors should still take normal precautions when traveling, such as keeping valuables secured and avoiding isolated or unfamiliar areas at night.

Health in Akşehir

It is important to stay hydrated and take precautions against sunburn and insect bites, especially during outdoor activities in the summer months. Visitors should also be aware of potential foodborne illnesses and take necessary precautions, such as washing fruits and vegetables and drinking bottled water.

Getting to Akşehir: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Akşehir

  • Konya Airport: Located approximately 100 km from Akşehir, Konya Airport is the closest major airport with regular commercial flights from major cities throughout Turkey and Europe.

Bus Stations Near Akşehir

  • Akşehir Bus Station: A convenient and centrally located transportation hub, with regular service to major cities throughout Turkey.

Other Options

  • Renting a car: Visitors can rent a car and drive to Akşehir from major Turkish cities, which takes approximately 3-6 hours depending on the departure location.
  • Private shuttle service: Private shuttle services are available for hire and can provide reliable and convenient transportation to and from Akşehir for groups or individuals.

How to Get Around in Akşehir

Visitors can easily explore the city center on foot, or rent a bicycle to explore the surrounding countryside. Taxis and local buses are also available for transportation throughout the town and nearby areas.

Where to Go Next from Akşehir: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Pamukkale: Explore the unique natural beauty of Pamukkale, with its famous travertine pools and ancient ruins, located approximately 4 hours from Akşehir by car.
  • Cappadocia: Discover the otherworldly landscape of Cappadocia, with its towering rock formations, ancient cave dwellings, and unforgettable hot-air balloon rides, located approximately 6 hours from Akşehir by car.
  • Istanbul: Experience the vibrant culture and history of Istanbul, with its stunning mosques, bustling markets, and world-renowned cuisine, located approximately 7 hours from Akşehir by car or bus.

Conclusion

Akşehir is a must-visit destination in Turkey, with its rich history, beautiful natural landscapes, and welcoming locals. Whether you’re interested in hiking and outdoor adventures, exploring local culture and cuisine, or simply taking in the stunning scenery, Akşehir has something to offer. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has been helpful in planning your trip and discovering all that Akşehir has to offer.
