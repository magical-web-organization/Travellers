A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Iskenderun, Turkey

Iskenderun is a beautiful coastal city located in the Mediterranean province of Hatay, on the southern coast of Turkey. The city is situated at the foot of the picturesque Amanos Mountains and has a rich cultural heritage with a blend of Turkish and Arabic influences. With its stunning natural landscape and historical sites, Iskenderun is an ideal destination for travelers looking to explore the hidden gems of Turkey. In this guide, we’ll take a closer look at the top attractions, cuisines, accommodations, and travel tips you need to know to make the most of your visit to Iskenderun.

List of Places to Visit in Iskenderun

When it comes to exploring Iskenderun, there are countless places to visit and things to do.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

One of the main draws of Iskenderun is its rich cultural heritage. Here are some of the top cultural attractions to add to your itinerary:

  • Hatay Archaeology Museum
  • St. Pierre Church
  • Antakya Old City
  • İskenderun City Museum
  • Payas Castle

Natural Attractions of Iskenderun: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Iskenderun is also well-known for its breathtaking natural beauty. Here are some of the best places to explore the great outdoors:

  • Harbiye Waterfall
  • Tumlu Castle
  • Küpeli Mountain
  • Arsuz Beach
  • Aktas Beach

Hiking/Biking Routes of Iskenderun

With its scenic landscapes and mountainous terrain, Iskenderun is a popular destination for hikers and bikers. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • Sarıkaya Canyon hiking trail
  • Gözcüler Mountain hiking trail
  • Taslaközü biking trail
  • Amanos Mountains hiking trail

Best Things to Do in Iskenderun: Activities and Tours

Iskenderun offers a wide range of activities for travelers of all ages. Here are some of the best things to do in Iskenderun:

  • Take a boat tour around the harbor
  • Visit the local bazaar for shopping and souvenirs
  • Go on a jeep safari adventure in the mountains
  • Explore the caves of Arsuz
  • Enjoy a picnic at Harbiye Waterfall

Week-Long Itinerary for Iskenderun

If you have a week to spare, here’s a suggested itinerary to make sure you don’t miss any of the best sights in and around Iskenderun:

Day Activity
Day 1 Explore Antakya Old City and St. Pierre Church
Day 2 Visit Hatay Archaeology Museum and İskenderun City Museum
Day 3 Take a boat tour around the harbor and enjoy local seafood
Day 4 Go on a jeep safari adventure in the mountains
Day 5 Hike the Sarıkaya Canyon hiking trail
Day 6 Visit Harbiye Waterfall and enjoy a picnic
Day 7 Relax on the beaches of Arsuz or Aktas

Food and Drink Scene of Iskenderun

Iskenderun is renowned for its rich and diverse culinary scene. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kebab
  • Baklava
  • Antakya Künefe
  • Tandır
  • Çökelek

Local Beverages

  • Şalgam Suyu (turnip juice)
  • Ayran (yoghurt drink)
  • Rakı (aniseed-flavored liquor)

What to Buy in Iskenderun: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs to take home, here are some of the most popular items to buy in Iskenderun:

  • Turkish carpets and rugs
  • Copperware and ceramics
  • Jewelry and accessories

Local Culture and Customs of Iskenderun

Iskenderun is a culturally rich city with a mix of Turkish and Arabic influences. Here are some things to keep in mind when visiting:

  • Dress modestly in religious and cultural areas
  • Show respect for local customs and traditions
  • Be mindful of Islamic prayer times

Best Time to Visit Iskenderun

The best time to visit Iskenderun is during the spring and autumn months, from March to May and September to November, when the weather is milder and there are fewer tourists.

Do’s and Don’ts of Iskenderun

To ensure a smooth and enjoyable trip to Iskenderun, here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Learn some basic Turkish phrases
  • Hire a local guide for a more immersive experience
  • Try new foods and beverages

Don’t:

  • Photography is forbidden in some religious and cultural sites
  • Don’t visit during Ramadan without being mindful of local customs
  • Avoid engaging in public displays of affection

Safety and Health

Iskenderun is a safe and friendly city for travelers. Here are some health and safety tips to keep in mind:

Safety in Iskenderun

  • Be mindful of your surroundings, especially in crowded areas
  • Keep your valuables in a secure place
  • Practice safe driving habits

Health in Iskenderun

  • Drink bottled water
  • Wear sunscreen and a hat to avoid sunburn
  • Pack insect repellent to prevent mosquito bites

Conclusion

Iskenderun is a beautiful destination that offers something for everyone. From its rich cultural heritage to its stunning natural beauty, Iskenderun is a city worth visiting. Whether you’re interested in hiking, exploring historical sites, or indulging in delicious local cuisine, Iskenderun has something to offer. Use this comprehensive guide to help plan your travel itinerary and make the most of your visit to this hidden gem in southern Turkey.
