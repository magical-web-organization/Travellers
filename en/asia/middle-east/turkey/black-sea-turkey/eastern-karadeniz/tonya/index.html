Introduction to Tonya

Tonya is a picturesque district of Trabzon, located in the Eastern Karadeniz region of Turkey. Surrounded by striking landscapes, the district is an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. Tonya is popular for its lush forests, mountains, scenic valleys, and rivers. It’s a place where you can breathe in fresh air and enjoy the beauty of nature.

List of Places to Visit in Tonya

Tonya is a perfect place to indulge in both cultural and natural experiences. Here are some of the places that you must visit during your trip to Tonya:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tonya Museum: Located in the town center, Tonya Museum boasts a compelling collection of traditional costumes, jewelry, and other artifacts showcasing the local culture.

  • Sumela Monastery: A short drive from Tonya, Sumela Monastery is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Trabzon, renowned for its stunning setting in a cliff overlooking the Altindere Valley.

  • Atatürk Pavilion: Visit the historic building where Ataturk, the founder of the Turkish Republic, stayed during his visit to the region.

Natural Attractions of Tonya: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hidirnebi Plateau: This lush plateau, located at 1600 meters above sea level, offers mesmerizing scenery with its streams, green pastures, and wildflowers.

  • Köprübaşı Waterfall: Voted as one of the most beautiful waterfalls in Turkey, Köprübaşı Waterfalls, is situated in the city of Of about an hour’s drive from Tonya.

  • Karagol Sahil Tabiat Park: Take a hike to Karagol Sahil Tabiat Park for stunning views and fresh air. This park is a paradise for nature lovers with its dense forests, waterfalls, and crystal-clear lake.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tonya

Tonya is ideal for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the trails that you should explore:

  • Sahinkaya Canyon Trail: Experience the beauty of this 4.5 km trail, which follows the crystal-clear stream and takes you through lush forests.

  • Hidirnebi Plateau to Boztepe: This 6.5 km path offers breathtaking views of Tonya and the surrounding valleys.

  • Gölköy Trail: This 5.5 km trail takes you through the Gölköy Reservoir, a popular spot for fishing and picnicking.

Best Things to Do in Tonya: Activities and Tours

  • Rafting in the Firtina Valley: Take a thrilling ride through the foaming waters of the Firtina River, and enjoy the stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

  • Paragliding in Uzungöl: Soar above the scenic Uzungöl village and enjoy panoramic views of the nearby mountains and lakes.

  • ATV Riding: Rent an ATV and explore Tonya’s forest trails and winding roads.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tonya

Day 1: Visit Tonya Museum and Ataturk Pavilion.

Day 2: Hike on the Sahinkaya Canyon Trail.

Day 3: Explore the Hidirnebi Plateau and Boztepe.

Day 4: Take a day trip to Sumela Monastery and Altindere National Park.

Day 5: Enjoy rafting in the Firtina Valley.

Day 6: Paraglide over Uzungol Lake.

Day 7: Spend a relaxing day by the Köprübaşı Waterfall.

Food and Drink Scene of Tonya

Must-Try Dishes

  • Hamsiköy pide: Served fresh out of the oven, this pide is a staple of Eastern Karadeniz cuisine. Topped with local cheese, herbs, and butter, this dish is delicious comfort food.

  • Kuymak: A local specialty made from cornmeal, cheese, and butter.

  • Kabak tatlısı: A dessert made from pumpkin, sugar, and walnuts.

Local Beverages

  • Cay: A staple of Turkish culture, it is a highly popular hot beverage.

  • Rize tea: A black tea from Rize, a neighboring district of Tonya, is regarded to be among the finest teas in Turkey due to its unique taste and aroma.

  • Cornelian cherry juice: A tangy and refreshing juice made from local Cornelian cherries.

Where to Stay in Tonya: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Firtina Nehri Black River Lodge: A riverside lodge with an outdoor pool and restaurant.

  • Yenitur Hotel: A quiet and cozy hotel located in the center of Tonya.

  • Hurma Otel: A family-owned hotel with comfortable accommodation and a peaceful garden area.

What to Buy in Tonya: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Tonya Beeswax: Known for its quality, Tonya beeswax is used in various products, including candles and skin care items.

  • Wooden Souvenirs: Hand-carved wooden souvenirs like bowls, spoons, and plates reflect the traditional craftsmanship of the region.

  • Cornelian Cherry Products: Delicious preserves and jams made from locally grown Cornelian cherries.

Local Culture and Customs of Tonya

The people of Tonya are known for their friendly nature and hospitality. Visitors are encouraged to respect the local customs and traditions, which are deeply rooted in Islamic and local traditions. Women are expected to dress modestly in public places, including mosques and religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Tonya

Tonya experiences mild temperatures throughout the year but is best visited from May to October for outdoor activities. The fall months of September, October and November are great for hiking and the autumn foliage. The summer months of June, July, and August are ideal for water activities like rafting and swimming.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tonya

Do:

  • Show respect for local customs and traditions.

  • Dress modestly when visiting mosques and religious sites.

  • Try the local dishes and delicacies, including cornelian cherry juice and Hamsiköy pide.

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol in public places as the region is predominantly Muslim.

  • Wear revealing clothing, especially for women.

  • Litter or damage the natural environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tonya

Tonya is a safe destination for tourists. Still, it’s advisable to keep your valuables in a safe place and be aware of your surroundings. Take necessary precautions while camping and hiking to avoid accidents.

Health in Tonya

There are no reported health risks in Tonya. However, make sure to carry adequate medication if you have any pre-existing conditions.

Getting to Tonya: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tonya

The nearest airport to Tonya is Trabzon Airport, which is located about 50 km away.

Bus Stations Near Tonya

Tonya has a modern bus station that offers regular services to and from Trabzon and other major cities in Turkey.

Other Options

You can also hire a private car or taxi from Trabzon or other nearby cities to reach Tonya.

How to Get Around in Tonya

The best way to explore Tonya’s natural beauty is by car. However, if you prefer public transport, minibusses and taxis are available.

Where to Go Next from Tonya: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Uzungol: A picturesque village surrounded by mountains and forests, is only 30 minutes from Tonya.

  • Rize: Known for its tea and stunning mountain landscapes, is an hour’s drive from Tonya.

  • Artvin: The undiscovered gem of Eastern Karadeniz, Artvin offers unmatched scenic views, is just 2 hours away from Tonya.

Conclusion

If you’re looking for a perfect mix of nature, history, and culture, Tonya should be on your bucket list. With its stunning landscapes, rich history, and delicious cuisine, Tonya promises an unforgettable travel experience. Plan your trip today and explore the natural beauty of Eastern Karadeniz.
