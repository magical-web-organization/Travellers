Introduction to Kars

Kars is a city in Eastern Anatolia, Turkey, with a population of approximately 80,000 people. This beautiful city is located at an altitude of 1,750 meters, and it’s known for its cultural heritage, historical landmarks, and breathtaking natural beauty. Kars also boasts a rich cuisine and offers countless outdoor activities for tourists to enjoy.

List of Places to Visit in Kars

Before going to Kars, it’s essential to plan your itinerary to make the most of your time. Here are some of the top places to visit in Kars:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Ani Archaeological Site: Ani was once a bustling city with a population of 100,000 people, and it’s now an abandoned town with a cathedral, mosque, and fortress ruins.
  • Kars Castle: This ancient ruin has been reconstructed and converted into a museum. It’s an excellent spot to learn about the historical events of Kars.
  • Church of the Apostles: This 11th-century Armenian church is built on Mount Ararat.
  • Kars Museum: This museum houses artifacts of Kars’s cultural heritage, including old coins, handcrafted souvenirs, and shows off a significant part of Turkish history.

Natural Attractions of Kars: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Sarikamis Ski Centre: The Sarikamis Ski Centre, located in the middle of Kars, is a great place for skiers of all levels, from beginners to experts.
  • Kars-Ardahan Plateau: This plateau is a great location for trekking and hiking. It is home to many villages where you can experience an authentic taste of the Turkish lifestyle.
  • Cildir Lake: Located in the north-eastern part of Kars, this lake is the perfect spot for nature lovers to enjoy the scenic views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kars

One of the best ways to explore Kars is on foot or by bike. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Kars:

  • Kackar Mountains: This mountain range is located in northeastern Turkey, and it’s perfect for hiking and skiing.
  • Aras River Valley: The Aras River Valley is a popular spot for bird watching and fishing. You will also experience the pleasant scenery of the area.

Best Things to Do in Kars: Activities and Tours

Kars offers a variety of fun activities and tours for tourists. Here are some of the best things to do in Kars:

  • Visit the Kars Archaeological Museum
  • Explore the historical ruins of Kars Castle
  • Go bird-watching and rural exploring in the Aras Valley
  • Experience local cuisine with a Kars Tandir feast
  • Tour of historic churches, mosques and other cultural sites to learn about the history of Kars.

Week-Long Itinerary for Kars

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Kars, here is an itinerary you can follow:

  • Day 1: Tour Ani Archaeological Site
  • Day 2: Visit Kars Castle, the Kars Museum or the Sarikamis Ski Centre.
  • Day 3: Explore the Aras River Valley and go bird watching
  • Day 4: Take a hike or bike ride in the Kackar Mountains
  • Day 5: Visit the Cildir Lake
  • Day 6: Visit the Church of the Apostles and experience a Kars Tandir feast.
  • Day 7: Relax and enjoy the scenery of Kars.

Food and Drink Scene of Kars

The local cuisine of Kars is unique, and it’s known for its rich flavors and regional specialties. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kars Kugulu: A dish made of ground lamb meat and vegetables with a layer of cheese.
  • Etli Ekmek: A traditional meat pie made of dough, minced meat, and vegetables.
  • Kavurma: A dish made of lamb or beef, slow-cooked with spices until tender.

Local Beverages

  • Ayran: a popular yoghurt-based beverage
  • Turk Kahvesi: A strong, Turkish-style coffee

Where to Stay in Kars: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kars offers a variety of accommodation options for tourists, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are some recommended options:

  • Cheltikov Hotel: This hotel is in the city center and offers luxurious accommodations with fantastic views of the stone city.
  • Kar’s Hotel: This hotel is located near the castle and offers beautiful views and proximity to top attractions of Kars.
  • Hostel Kars: Great budget hotel with beautiful gardens and suites with private bathrooms.

What to Buy in Kars: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kars is known for its beautiful handicrafts and regional products. Here are some things to buy in Kars:

  • Handmade carpets and kilims
  • Handcrafted souvenirs like shoes, wallets, purses and slippers

Local Culture and Customs of Kars

To experience the local culture of Kars, you can attend the Kars Culture and Art Festival, which is usually held in August every year. During the festival, there are music and dance performances, traditional games and competitions, and exhibitions of local handicrafts.

Best Time to Visit Kars

The best time to visit Kars is during spring, summer, or fall, when the weather is mild and pleasant. Kars receives heavy snowfall during the winter season, which may not be suitable for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kars

Here are some essential things to keep in mind when traveling to Kars:

Do:

  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites
  • Try the local food and drink
  • Haggle in bazaars when buying souvenirs

Don’t:

  • Don’t smoke around religious sites
  • Don’t take photographs of military buildings or soldiers
  • Don’t take offense if locals are reserved or shy.

Safety and Health

Here are some essential health and safety tips to keep in mind when traveling to Kars:

Safety in Kars

  • Keep your valuables in a safe place and don’t flaunt your wealth
  • Keep an eye on your surroundings and don’t go out alone late at night

Health in Kars

  • Drink bottled water
  • Apply plenty of sunscreen to avoid sunburn in the hot summer season

Getting to Kars: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Kars

Kars Harakani Airport (KSY) is the nearest airport, approximately a 15-minute drive from the city center. Domestic flights operate to and from Kars Airport with airlines including Turkish Airlines and Pegasus Airlines.

Bus Stations Near Kars

The Kars Bus Terminal connects the city to many destinations in Turkey. The bus services are relatively comfortable and provide a cost-effective travel option.

Other Options

You can also opt for private car rental services, which can offer you flexibility and comfort while traveling.

How to Get Around in Kars

Kars is a small city, so most attractions are accessible on foot. Alternatively, you can use public transportation services, which include buses and taxis.

Where to Go Next from Kars: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are many exciting destinations near Kars, where tourists can go for short excursions, including:

  • Mount Ararat
  • Lake Van
  • Dogubeyazit
  • Erzurum

Conclusion

Kars is one of the best tourist destinations in Turkey, with an incredible cultural and historical heritage, and stunning natural beauty. It’s an ideal location for any traveler seeking an authentic experience of Turkish culture and plenty of outdoor adventures. We hope this travel guide has provided valuable information for your next trip to Kars, Turkey!
