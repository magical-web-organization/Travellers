Gaziantep Travel Guide - A Comprehensive Tour Guide for Gaziantep

Welcome to Gaziantep, a city situated in Southeastern Anatolia, Turkey. Gaziantep is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, delectable delicacies, and outdoor adventures. In this comprehensive Gaziantep travel guide, we will help you discover the best things to do, places to visit, where to stay, what to buy, and more in Gaziantep.

Introduction to Gaziantep

Gaziantep, also known as Antep, is a culturally and historically significant city in Turkey. It is situated between the Euphrates and Tigris rivers and has been inhabited since prehistoric times. The city has witnessed many empires such as the Roman, Byzantine, and Ottoman, which has left its significant marks on its architecture and culture.

List of Places to Visit in Gaziantep

There are many places to see and visit in Gaziantep, ranging from culturally important attractions to natural wonders. Here are some of the top attractions and places to visit in Gaziantep:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Gaziantep Zeugma Mosaic Museum: This museum houses the world’s most excellent mosaic collections from the ancient Roman city of Zeugma.
  • Gaziantep Castle: Gaziantep Castle, known as Gaziantep Kalesi, is a historical fortress overlooking the city.
  • Emine Gogus Culinary Museum: This museum showcasing the diverse culinary culture of Gaziantep.
  • Hasan Suzer Ethnography Museum: This museum gives visitors an insight into the local life and culture.

Natural Attractions of Gaziantep: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Yedikuyular: Yedikuyular is a nature reserve with magnificent waterfalls, natural pools, and hiking trails.
  • Omeriye Hill: Standing at an altitude of 820 meters, Omeriye Hill provides a panoramic view of Gaziantep city.
  • Zincirli Canyon: A spectacular canyon with steep cliffs and running water, surrounded by untouched nature.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Gaziantep

Gaziantep offers several hiking and biking spots for adventurous tourists. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Gaziantep:

  • Yedikuyular Nature Reserve: The nature reserve has many hiking trails and cycling routes, with distinctive routes and breathtaking views.
  • Beykoz Plateau: Beykoz Plateau is a picnicking and hiking spot in Gaziantep, located amid the pine trees and green shades.

Best Things to Do in Gaziantep: Activities and Tours

Gaziantep has numerous activities and tours that will satisfy different interests. Here are some of the top things to do in Gaziantep:

  • Turkish Bath Experience: Gaziantep offers the best Turkish Bath experience. The traditional hamams are complete with saunas, steam rooms, and a range of massage treatments.
  • Food Tours: Gaziantep is popularly known for its delicious culinary culture, and the best way to experience it is through food tours.
  • Bazaar Tours: Gaziantep has numerous bazaars that offer a fantastic shopping experience. Visitors can purchase handcrafted souvenirs, spices, and other local products.

Week-Long Itinerary for Gaziantep

Here’s a suggested week-long itinerary for Gaziantep:

Day Plan
Day 1 Arrive in Gaziantep and explore the city center

Food and Drink Scene of Gaziantep

Gaziantep’s culinary culture is renowned for being one of the best in Turkey. Visitors can sample different traditional dishes, drinks, and sweets. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Baklava: Gaziantep’s baklava is the best in the country. It is prepared with thin pastry layers, chopped pistachios, and honey syrup.
  • Lahmacun: A thin, crispy pizza-like dish with minced meat or vegetables, which is typically eaten as a snack.
  • Kebabs: Gaziantep offers various kinds of kebabs, including Adana Kebab, Beyti Kebab, and Ali Nazik Kebab.

Local Beverages

  • Şira: A non-alcoholic beverage, prepared from grapes or fruits and served cold.
  • Ayran: A refreshing, yoghurt-based drink, which is the perfect blend for spicy dishes.
  • Baklava Şerbeti: This is the syrup used to pour over baklava, and visitors can also try it as a dessert.

Where to Stay in Gaziantep: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Gaziantep offers various accommodation options, suitable for different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the top-rated options:

  • Divan Gaziantep: A luxurious hotel located in the city center, offering excellent service and amenities.
  • Zincirli Han Konak Hotel: A traditional guesthouse option with a Turkish feel and a historical ambiance.
  • Hotel Tilmen: A budget-friendly accommodation in the heart of the city.

What to Buy in Gaziantep: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Gaziantep has several bazaars and markets where visitors can purchase handcrafted souvenirs, spices, and other local products. Here are some of the best things to buy in Gaziantep:

  • Baklava: Gaziantep’s baklava is the best in the country, so bring some back home.
  • Pistachios: Gaziantep is famous for its pistachios, which are included in many local products.
  • Copperware: Gaziantep is known for its beautiful and intricate copper handicrafts.

Local Culture and Customs of Gaziantep

Gaziantep has its unique culture and customs. Visitors should respect the local traditions and abide by some cultural norms. For example, visitors should dress modestly, especially when entering mosques, and avoid public displays of affection.

Best Time to Visit Gaziantep

The best time to visit Gaziantep is from March to May and September to November. During these months, the temperature is mild, and the crowds are relatively little.

Do’s and Don’ts of Gaziantep

When visiting Gaziantep, it’s essential to adhere to certain do’s and don’ts. Here are some of them:

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and traditions.
  • Dress modestly when entering mosques and religious places.
  • Try the local cuisine and learn about the culinary culture.

Don’t:

  • Drink tap water as it is not drinkable.
  • Public displays of affection are frowned upon in public.
  • Photograph people without their consent.

Safety and Health

Gaziantep is a generally safe city to visit, but visitors should take precautions like in any city. Here’s what you need to know about safety and health in Gaziantep:

Safety in Gaziantep

  • Visitors should avoid showing off valuables in public places.
  • Be aware of pickpockets in crowded areas.
  • Visitors should be cautious when walking at night and travel in groups.

Health in Gaziantep

  • Visitors should drink bottled water and avoid drinking tap water.
  • Avoid street food from unhygienic places.
  • Visitors should take precautions against mosquito bites, as there have been cases of malaria in some areas.

Getting to Gaziantep: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here’s what you need to know about how to get to Gaziantep:

Airports Near Gaziantep

Gaziantep International Airport is the nearest airport to the city, located only 22 km away. Several domestic and international airline carriers operate to and from the airport.

Bus Stations Near Gaziantep

Gaziantep Bus Station is located in the city center and offers regular bus services to and from different destinations in Turkey.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel to Gaziantep by train or car.

How to Get Around in Gaziantep

Visitors can get around Gaziantep using different modes of transportation. Buses, taxis, and dolmus (public minibuses) are readily available in the city center.

Where to Go Next from Gaziantep: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Gaziantep serves as an excellent starting point to explore other sites and destinations in Turkey. Here some exciting destinations and excursions near Gaziantep:

  • Şanlıurfa: A city known for its many mosques, historic sites, and Urfa’s unique Kebab culture.
  • Mount Nemrut: A UNESCO World Heritage site with the tomb of the famous king Antiochus.
  • Adıyaman: A city in southeastern Turkey, home to many sites of historical and natural beauty.

Conclusion

Gaziantep is a culturally and historically significant city in Turkey, offering visitors an experience worth remembering. The city’s rich culinary culture, outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, and warm hospitality make it an ideal destination for travelers. With this comprehensive Gaziantep travel guide helping you plan your visit, you’re sure to have a fantastic experience.
