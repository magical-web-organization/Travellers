Introduction to Balıkesir Province

Balıkesir Province is a beautiful destination located in Turkey, part of the Middle East and Asia. It is a region with a rich history, lively culture and plenty of natural wonders. From the Aegean Turkish coast to the rugged highlands, visitors to Balıkesir are sure to find something to explore in this fascinating province.

List of Places to Visit in Balıkesir Province

Whether you’re interested in the cultural heritage, outdoor activities or just want to relax by the sea, Balıkesir Province has something for everyone. Below are the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Troy Ancient City
  2. Cunda Island
  3. Edremit Saatli Mosque
  4. Ayvalık Old Town
  5. Hasanboğuldu Canyon
  6. Altınoluk Thermal Springs
  7. Gömeç Akçay Thermal Springs

Natural Attractions of Balıkesir Province: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Madra Stream Valley
  2. Kazdağı National Park
  3. Mount Ida (Kazdağı)
  4. Kapıdağ Peninsula
  5. Ayvalık Islands Natural Park
  6. Arda Stream Valley

Hiking/Biking Routes of Balıkesir Province

  1. Kazdağı National Park Trek
  2. Madra Stream Valley Trek
  3. Hasanboğuldu Canyon Trek
  4. Kapıdağ Peninsula Trek
  5. Cunda and Ayvalık Old Town Bike Tour
  6. Edremit and Altınoluk Valley Cycling Route

Best Things to Do in Balıkesir Province: Activities and Tours

  1. Visit Troy Ancient City and learn about the legend of Trojan War
  2. Go on a boat tour to explore the stunning Aegean coast and islands
  3. Take a dip in the natural thermal springs of Gömeç Akçay or Altınoluk
  4. Trek up Mount Ida and enjoy the breathtaking views from the summit
  5. Visit Ayvalık Old Town and try local specialties such as Ayvalık toast.
  6. Join a nature tour and explore the wildlife and scenery of Kazdağı National Park.

Week-Long Itinerary for Balıkesir Province

  1. Day 1 - Arrive in Edremit and visit Edremit Saatli Mosque and Hasanboğuldu Canyon
  2. Day 2 - Trek up Kazdağı (Mount Ida) and visit the surrounding villages
  3. Day 3 - Explore the cultural heritage of Troy Ancient City and Cunda Island
  4. Day 4 - Relax at Gömeç Akçay Thermal Springs or Altınoluk thermal springs
  5. Day 5 - Take a boat tour around the Ayvalık archipelago
  6. Day 6 - Visit the Ayvalık Old Town and try local dishes
  7. Day 7 - Take a hike in Kapıdağ Peninsula or Madra Stream Valley

Food and Drink Scene of Balıkesir Province

Balıkesir cuisine is a mix of Islamic and Ottoman culinary traditions, highlighted by fresh ingredients, olive oil, and a mix of wild herbs. Below are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Ayvalık Toast
  2. Çakıldak Soup (made with partridge meat)
  3. Zerde (a dessert made with rice, cinnamon and saffron)
  4. Mihalic Cheese (an artisanal cheese similar to Parmigiano Reggiano)

Local Beverages

  1. Ayran (a yoghurt drink)
  2. Gum Mastic Liquor (a sweet liquor made with gum resin)
  3. Boza (a fermented drink made from a mixture of maize and sugar)

Local Culture and Customs of Balıkesir Province

Balıkesir is known for its hospitality, traditional values, and love for nature. Culture in Balıkesir is greatly influenced by its rural surroundings, and traditional lifestyles. Visitors are encouraged to respect local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Balıkesir Province

The best time to visit Balıkesir Province is from late spring to early autumn, with temperatures ranging from 20-30C. During the summer months, visitors can enjoy the beach, outdoor activities and festivities, whilst spring or autumn are perfect for hiking and exploring the surrounding nature.

Do’s and Don’ts of Balıkesir Province

Do:

  1. Respect local customs and traditions.
  2. Try local dishes and beverages.
  3. Hike up Kazdağı (Mount Ida) and enjoy the stunning scenery.
  4. Visit the ancient city of Troy.
  5. Take a boat tour around Ayvalık archipelago.

Don’t:

  1. Wear revealing clothing in religious places.
  2. Speak loudly or shout in public places.
  3. Leave trash or litter behind in public areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Balıkesir Province

Balıkesir Province is a safe destination for travelers, with low crime rates and friendly locals. However, visitors are encouraged to take normal safety precautions, such as safeguarding valuables and being aware of surroundings.

Health in Balıkesir Province

Visitors should be cautious of sunburn and sunstroke, especially during the summer months. Hydration is important, and visitors should drink plenty of water, especially when engaging in outdoor activities.

Getting to Balıkesir Province: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Balıkesir Province

Balıkesir Körfez Airport is the main airport serving Balıkesir Province. It serves local flights and offers connections to Istanbul.

Bus Stations Near Balıkesir Province

Balıkesir Bus Station is the largest transportation hub in the region, with connections to major cities across Turkey.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car, use a taxi or join a tour group to explore the province.

How to Get Around in Balıkesir Province

Public transportations like buses, taxis, and minibusses (or dolmuş) are available throughout Balıkesir province. Visitors might also want to hire a car or motorbike for flexibility and easy access to remote areas.

Where to Go Next from Balıkesir Province: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Izmir - a bustling metropolis on the Aegean coast
  2. Cannakale - visit the Gallipoli Battlefields and the ancient city of Troy
  3. Bursa - explore the historic city and relax in the natural thermal spas
  4. Istanbul - the world-famous city on the strait between Europe and Asia

Conclusion

Balıkesir is a province that offers travelers a mix of natural beauty, stunning ancient sites, and unique cultural experiences. From the beaches to the mountains, visitors can explore the natural wonders of the region, taste delicious local cuisine, and engage with friendly locals. Ensure you experience all that makes Balıkesir an unforgettable destination.
