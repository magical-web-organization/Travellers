Introduction to Bursa Province

Bursa Province is a historical and culturally rich region located in the Marmara region of Turkey. It is known for its stunning natural landscapes, tasty cuisines, and Ottoman heritage. The province is home to Turkey’s fourth-largest city, Bursa, which is often referred to as “Green Bursa” due to its lush greenery and gardens. With its fascinating history, unique culture, and stunning scenery, Bursa Province is a must-visit destination in Turkey.

Local Culture and Customs of Bursa Province

  • It is customary to remove one’s shoes before entering a mosque or someone’s home
  • Respectful dress is required while visiting religious sites
  • The locals greet each other by saying “Merhaba” (Hello) and “Tesekkurler” (Thank you)
  • Tipping in restaurants and cafes is common, usually rounding up to the nearest whole number

Best Time to Visit Bursa Province

The best time to visit Bursa Province is between April to June or from September to November, as the weather is mild, and the crowds are smaller. Summer months can become quite hot, and winter months can get quite cold, with plenty of snowfall on the higher elevations.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bursa Province

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Take off your shoes when entering a mosque or someone’s home.
  • Try local Turkish dishes and drinks.
  • Shop and bargain at the markets and bazaars.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing while visiting religious sites.
  • Take photographs without permission.
  • Be disrespectful to the locals or their beliefs.
  • Leave litter behind while hiking or visiting national parks.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bursa Province

Bursa Province is generally a safe and welcoming place, but visitors should take basic precautions, such as safeguarding their belongings, avoiding unlit areas at night, and being mindful of scams and pickpockets.

Health in Bursa Province

Visitors should be up to date on their routine vaccinations, including measles, mumps, and rubella (MMR) vaccine, and hepatitis A and B vaccines. The province has several excellent medical facilities in case of emergencies.

Getting to Bursa Province: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bursa Province

Bursa is located around 95 kilometers from the Sabiha Gokcen International Airport in Istanbul and around 163 kilometers from the Ataturk International Airport in Istanbul.

Bus Stations Near Bursa Province

Bursa has many bus stations that connect it to other cities in Turkey.

Other Options

One can take a ferry from Istanbul to Bursa.

How to Get Around in Bursa Province

The best way to get around Bursa Province is by using a rental car or a taxi. Buses and boats are also available for some routes.
