Introduction to Gebze

Gebze is a suburban town located in Eastern Marmara, Turkey. It is situated in the middle of the Istanbul-Ankara and Istanbul-Izmir highways, making it an easily accessible destination. The town is home to several cultural and historical sites, beautiful natural attractions, exciting activities, delicious cuisine, and traditional handicrafts.

List of Places to Visit in Gebze

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Osman Hamdi Bey Museum: This museum is dedicated to the famous Turkish painter and archaeologist Osman Hamdi Bey. It houses his personal collection of artifacts, pottery, and ceramic works. Visitors can learn about his life and career while admiring his original paintings.

  • Eskihisar Castle: It is located in the coastal district of Eskihisar and was built in the 14th century by the Genoese. The castle features a beautiful view of the sea and the surrounding area.

  • Darıca Zoo: It is one of the biggest zoos in Turkey and home to over 200 different animal species. Visitors can enjoy a full day of animal encounters and educational programs, and even stay overnight in the zoo’s accommodations.

Natural Attractions of Gebze: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ballıkayalar Nature Park: This beautiful park is located near the town of Gebze and features unique rock formations, cliffs, and caves. Visitors can enjoy hiking, rock climbing, and breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys.

  • Acarlar Longozu: It is a wetland conservation area with a diverse flora and fauna. Visitors can take a walk in the wilderness along the wooden trails and learn about the wetland’s ecosystem.

  • Sea of Marmara: Visitors can relax on the beautiful beaches, swim in the crystal-clear waters, and enjoy water activities like sailing, windsurfing, and sea kayaking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Gebze

  • Kemalpaşa-Gölcük National Park Trail: This trail is located within the Kemalpaşa-Gölcük National Park and features diverse terrain, including pine forests, canyons, and waterfalls. The trail is great for hiking and mountain biking.

  • Aydos Forest Trail: This trail is located in Istanbul’s Aydos Forest and features breathtaking views of the city and the Sea of Marmara. Visitors can enjoy hiking and mountain biking.

Best Things to Do in Gebze: Activities and Tours

  • Paragliding: Visitors can enjoy the beautiful aerial view of Gebze while paragliding in the Kemalpaşa-Gölcük National Park.

  • Horse Riding: Visitors can enjoy horse riding in the beautiful forests of Gebze.

  • Boat Tours: Visitors can take a boat tour around the Sea of Marmara and enjoy the beautiful views of Gebze’s coastline.

Food and Drink Scene of Gebze

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kebab: Gebze is well-known for its delicious kebabs, made with high-quality beef, lamb, or chicken.

  • Mantı: It is a type of Turkish dumpling, filled with ground meat and spices, and boiled or steamed. It is usually served with yogurt and tomato sauce.

  • Simit: It is a type of Turkish bagel, covered with sesame seeds and baked to perfection.

Local Beverages

  • Turkish Tea: A strong and flavorful hot tea, often served in small glasses.

  • Ayran: It is a traditional yogurt-based drink, mixed with water, salt, and sometimes herbs.

  • Turkish Coffee: A strong and aromatic coffee, brewed in a traditional pot and served with a small glass of water.

Where to Stay in Gebze: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Northstar Resort & Hotels: It is a luxurious hotel, located near the sea, featuring comfortable rooms, swimming pools, and spa services.

  • Wish More Hotel Istanbul: It is located in the heart of Gebze town and offers comfortable rooms, free Wi-Fi, and a fitness center.

  • Narköy: It is a traditional guesthouse located in the beautiful countryside, featuring comfortable rooms, organic food, and a relaxing atmosphere.

What to Buy in Gebze: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handmade Carpets: Visitors can purchase high-quality handmade carpets and kilims, made by local craftsmen.

  • Pottery Products: Gebze is well-known for its beautiful pottery products, such as bowls, plates, and vases.

  • Traditional Sweets: Visitors can try various types of traditional sweets, such as baklava, Turkish delight, and helva.

Local Culture and Customs of Gebze

  • Hospitality: Turkish people are famous for their hospitality, and visitors can expect to be welcomed warmly by the locals in Gebze.

  • Islam: Islam is the predominant religion in Turkey, and visitors should respect the local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Gebze

The best time to visit Gebze is during the spring and autumn seasons, from March to May and September to November, when the weather is mild, and the crowds are fewer.

  • Respect the local customs and traditions
  • Bargain in the local markets and shops

  • Show excessive public display of affection
  • Wear revealing clothing in public places
  • Disrespect the local religion and customs

Safety and Health

Safety in Gebze

Gebze is generally a safe destination for travelers, but visitors should take normal safety precautions, such as not carrying large amounts of cash and avoiding walking alone at night.

Health in Gebze

Visitors should follow standard safety and hygiene practices and avoid drinking tap water. It is recommended to carry personal health insurance during the trip.

Getting to Gebze: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Gebze

  • Istanbul Sabiha Gökçen International Airport: It is the closest airport to Gebze, located 25 kilometers away.

  • Istanbul International Airport: It is located 85 kilometers away from Gebze.

Bus Stations Near Gebze

  • Gebze Intercity Bus Terminal: It is the main bus station of Gebze town and offers daily services to major cities in Turkey.

  • İstanbul Esenler Bus Terminal: It is located 60 kilometers away from Gebze and offers regular intercity bus services to major cities in Turkey.

Other Options

  • Private Transfer: Visitors can hire a private transfer service from Istanbul or the airports to Gebze.

  • Car Rental: Visitors can rent a car from the airports or the city and drive to Gebze.

How to Get Around in Gebze

  • Bus: Gebze has a comprehensive public transportation system, with regular bus services to major destinations.

  • Taxi: Visitors can also use taxis for transportation, but should negotiate the fare before getting into the taxi.

Where to Go Next from Gebze: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Istanbul: The historic city of Istanbul is located only an hour away from Gebze and offers diverse cultural, historical, and social attractions.

  • Bursa: It is a city located south of Gebze and famous for its Ottoman-style architecture, hot springs, and natural beauty.

  • Yalova: It is a city located on the Marmara Sea coast, north of Gebze, and offers natural hot springs, beaches, and parks.

Conclusion

Gebze is a hidden gem in Turkey, offering a perfect blend of cultural, historical, and natural attractions. Visitors can enjoy a relaxing weekend, a family holiday, or an adventure-filled trip in this charming town. The delicious food, warm hospitality, and stunning landscapes of Gebze will leave visitors with unforgettable memories.
