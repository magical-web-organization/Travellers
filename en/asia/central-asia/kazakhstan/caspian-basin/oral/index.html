Introduction to Oral

Oral, also known as Uralsk, is a city located in the Caspian Basin of Kazakhstan. It is a popular destination for tourists due to its rich history, diverse culture, and natural beauty. The city is steeped in history and was once an important trading hub between Russia and Asia. Today, it is a bustling metropolis that is home to many museums, markets, and cultural landmarks.

List of Places to Visit in Oral

If you are planning a visit to Oral, here is a list of top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Oral Regional Museum: This museum is dedicated to the history and culture of the region. It features exhibits on Kazakh society, Kazakh folklore, and the history of the region from the Stone Age to the present.
  • Russian Orthodox Church: This church was constructed in the 19th century and is a beautiful example of Russian Orthodox architecture.
  • Karatomar Mausoleum: This mausoleum is an important religious site and burial ground for the Kazakh people.
  • Abai Museum: This museum is dedicated to the life and work of Abai Kunanbaev, a poet, writer, and philosopher who is considered one of the greatest literary figures in Kazakhstan.

Natural Attractions of Oral: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ural River Promenade: This beautiful promenade offers stunning views of the Ural River and is a great spot for a leisurely stroll.
  • Zhastar Park: This park is located on the banks of the Ural River and is a popular spot for picnics and outdoor activities.
  • Karakum Desert: This vast desert is located outside of Oral and offers opportunities for desert safaris and camping.
  • Bokey Khan Mosque: This mosque is located in the middle of the Karakum Desert and offers beautiful views of the surrounding landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Oral

  • Ural River Promenade: This paved walkway stretches along the Ural River and offers a scenic route for walking or biking.
  • Zhastar Park Trail: This trail runs through Zhastar Park and is popular with hikers and mountain bikers.
  • Karakum Desert Trail: This rough and rugged trail offers an intense hiking or mountain biking experience through the beautiful Karakum Desert.

Best Things to Do in Oral: Activities and Tours

  • Visit the Oral Regional Museum to learn about the history and culture of Kazakhstan.
  • Take a walking tour of the city to explore its many landmarks and historical sites.
  • Go on a desert safari in the Karakum Desert.
  • Take a river cruise on the Ural River.
  • Visit the Abai Museum to learn about the life and work of Abai Kunanbaev.
  • Take a guided tour of the Karatomar Mausoleum.

Food and Drink Scene of Oral

If you are a foodie, here are some must-try dishes and local beverages to try in Oral:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Beshbarmak: This traditional Kazakh dish consists of boiled meat (usually lamb or beef) served with flat noodles and onions.
  • Shashlik: This classic barbecue dish consists of skewered and grilled meat (usually lamb).
  • Manty: These steamed dumplings are filled with spiced meat and onions.
  • Laghman: This noodle dish is served with a variety of spicy vegetables and meat.

Local Beverages

  • Kumis: This traditional fermented drink is made from mare’s milk and has a slightly sour taste.
  • Shubat: This fermented drink is made from camel’s milk and is known for its tart, refreshing taste.

What to Buy in Oral: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When shopping in Oral, be sure to look for:

  • Traditional Kazakh clothing and accessories, such as shapan robes and kalpaks (traditional headwear for men).
  • Handmade ceramics and pottery.
  • Local handicrafts, such as embroidered textiles and rugs.

Local Culture and Customs of Oral

When visiting Oral, it is important to be respectful of local culture and customs. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Dress conservatively when visiting religious sites.
  • Remove your shoes when entering a Kazakh home.
  • Greetings are an important part of Kazakh culture, and it is customary to greet people with a handshake or nod.
  • Avoid discussing sensitive topics, such as politics or religion, unless you are invited to do so.

Best Time to Visit Oral

The best time to visit Oral is during the summer months (June-August), when temperatures are mild and there is plenty of daylight. However, keep in mind that this is also the peak tourist season, so be prepared for crowds and higher prices. If you prefer a quieter and more affordable trip, consider visiting in the spring (April-May) or fall (September-October), when the weather is still pleasant but tourists are fewer in number.

Safety and Health

  • Avoid walking alone at night.
  • Keep your valuables in a safe place, such as a hotel safe.

Before traveling to Oral, be sure to consult with your doctor about any necessary vaccinations or preventative measures. It is a good idea to bring a basic first-aid kit with you in case of minor injuries or illnesses.

Getting to Oral: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Oral

The nearest airport to Oral is the Oral Ak Zhol Airport (URO), which is located about 15 kilometers from the city center. Flights to Oral are available from Astana and Almaty.

Bus Stations Near Oral

The main bus station in Oral is located in the city center and offers connections to other cities in Kazakhstan, as well as neighboring countries such as Russia and Uzbekistan.

Other Options

If you prefer to travel by car, it is possible to rent a car in Astana or Almaty and drive to Oral. There are also train connections to Oral from other cities in Kazakhstan.

How to Get Around in Oral

Getting around in Oral is easy, with a variety of transportation options available:

  • Taxi: Taxis are readily available throughout the city and are a convenient way to get around.
  • Public Bus: Oral has a network of public buses that connect to various parts of the city.
  • Walking: Many of the main sights in Oral are located within walking distance of each other, making walking a convenient option.

Where to Go Next from Oral: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have extra time in the region, consider visiting these nearby destinations:

  • Aktobe: This city is located in the northern part of Kazakhstan and is known for its beautiful natural scenery and historic landmarks.
  • Ust-Kamenogorsk: This city is located in eastern Kazakhstan and is known for its stunning mountain scenery and cultural landmarks.
