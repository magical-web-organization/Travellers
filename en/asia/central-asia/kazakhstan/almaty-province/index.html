Introduction to Almaty Province

Located in Kazakhstan, Almaty Province is one of the most culturally rich and naturally stunning regions in Central Asia. Home to soaring mountains, dazzling lakes, and vibrant cities, this province is a must-visit destination for travelers who want to experience the best of Kazakhstan.

List of Places to Visit in Almaty Province

If you are planning your trip to Almaty Province and wondering which places to visit, here is a list of some must-visit attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Central State Museum - One of the oldest museums in Kazakhstan, it features an extensive collection of artifacts and exhibits that trace the country’s history from ancient times to the present day.
  2. Ascension Cathedral - A beautiful Russian Orthodox cathedral located in Almaty city, known for its stunning architecture and religious significance.
  3. Charyn Canyon - A unique natural attraction known for its spectacular rock formations, steep cliffs, and colorful scenery.
  4. Tamgaly Tas - This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a collection of petroglyphs dating back to the Bronze Age, depicting various scenes from daily life.

Natural Attractions of Almaty Province: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Big Almaty Lake - A pristine lake located in the Tian Shan Mountains, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes and perfect for hiking and camping.
  2. Shymbulak Ski Resort - A popular ski resort near Almaty city, famous for its snow-capped peaks and thrilling skiing and snowboarding experience.
  3. Medeu Ice Skating Rink - The highest ice skating rink in the world and a favorite spot for locals and tourists alike.
  4. Kok-Tobe Hill - A scenic hilltop that offers panoramic views of Almaty city, accessible by cable car or hiking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Almaty Province

Almaty Province is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, with numerous hiking and biking trails to explore. Some of the top routes include:

  1. Alatau Trails - A network of trails that run through the Charyn Canyon and the Tian Shan Mountains, perfect for hiking and biking.
  2. Turgen Gorge - A scenic gorge located near the city of Almaty, known for its stunning waterfalls and hiking trails.
  3. Chimbulak-Almaty Trail - A challenging hiking trail that starts at the Shymbulak Ski Resort and ends at Almaty city, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

Best Things to Do in Almaty Province: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, there are plenty of other activities and tours that you can enjoy in Almaty Province. Here are some of the best things to do:

  1. Take a city tour of Almaty to discover the city’s rich history and culture.
  2. Visit a local yurt and experience traditional Kazakh hospitality and cuisine.
  3. Take a hot air balloon ride over the stunning landscapes of Almaty Province.
  4. Go rock climbing or trekking in the mountains for a thrilling adventure.

Food and Drink Scene of Almaty Province

Apart from its natural and cultural attractions, Almaty Province is also famous for its rich culinary scene. Here are some must-try local dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Beshbarmak - A traditional Kazakh dish made with lamb or beef, served with noodles and onion sauce.
  2. Plov - A popular rice dish made with lamb, beef, or chicken and flavored with carrots, onions, and spices.
  3. Lagman - A hearty soup dish made with hand-pulled noodles, vegetables, and meat.
  4. Samsa - A savory pastry filled with meat, onions, and potatoes.

Local Beverages

  1. Kumys - A fermented drink made with mare’s milk, known for its health benefits and unique taste.
  2. Shubat - A drink made from fermented camel’s milk, known for its refreshing properties.
  3. Baursak - A traditional Kazakh pastry served with tea.

Where to Stay in Almaty Province: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options in Almaty Province, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. Some popular options include:

  1. Rixos Almaty Hotel - A luxurious hotel located in the heart of Almaty city, offering stunning views of the mountains and the city.
  2. Renion Hotel - A mid-range hotel located near the Medeu Ice Skating Rink, perfect for winter sports enthusiasts.
  3. Alma-Ata Hostel - A budget-friendly hostel located in the center of Almaty city, offering basic amenities and comfortable rooms.

What to Buy in Almaty Province: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you want to bring back some souvenirs or local products from Almaty Province, here are some things you can consider:

  1. Handmade rugs and textiles, featuring traditional Kazakh designs and patterns.
  2. Musical instruments, such as the dombra, a traditional Kazakh stringed instrument.
  3. Hand-carved wooden items, such as bowls, plates, and utensils.
  4. Traditional Kazakh costumes and clothing items, such as the kalpak, a traditional Kazakh hat.

Local Culture and Customs of Almaty Province

Kazakhstan is a multi-ethnic and multicultural country, and Almaty Province is no different. Some important cultural practices and customs to keep in mind include:

  1. Kazakh hospitality is famous around the world, and visitors are often invited for meals and gatherings with locals.
  2. Respect for elders is a significant cultural practice, and it is essential to show deference and courtesy to older people.
  3. Modesty is also an important cultural value, and visitors are advised to dress conservatively in public places.

Best Time to Visit Almaty Province

The best time to visit Almaty Province is during the summer months, from June to September, when the weather is warm and pleasant, and outdoor activities are in full swing. However, the winter months, from November to February, are also a great time to visit for skiing and snowboarding enthusiasts.

Do’s and Don’ts of Almaty Province

To have a hassle-free and enjoyable trip to Almaty Province, here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind:

Do:

  1. Respect local customs and traditions.
  2. Learn some basic phrases in Kazakh or Russian to communicate with locals.
  3. Try local cuisine and beverages.
  4. Shop at local markets and support small businesses.

Don’t:

  1. Disrespect religious and cultural practices.
  2. Over-tipping is not customary in Kazakhstan.
  3. Don’t take pictures of people without their consent.
  4. Don’t visit off-limits areas or sites.

Safety and Health

Almaty Province is generally a safe destination for tourists, and most visitors do not experience any major safety concerns. However, it is essential to keep some safety and health considerations in mind:

Safety in Almaty Province

  1. Be cautious while hiking or trekking and follow the recommended trails and routes.
  2. Be aware of pickpockets and keep your valuables safe while in crowded places.
  3. Follow traffic rules while driving or crossing roads.

Health in Almaty Province

  1. Make sure to drink bottled or boiled water and avoid tap water.
  2. Consult your doctor and take necessary vaccinations before your trip.
  3. Make sure to carry appropriate medication and insurance for emergencies.

How to Get Around in Almaty Province

Getting around in Almaty Province is easy, with several transportation options available:

  1. Bus - There are regular bus services in Almaty city and nearby areas.
  2. Taxi - Taxis are available throughout Almaty Province, and you can book them through mobile applications or local taxi stands.
  3. Bike - You can also rent a bike and explore the city and nearby areas at your own pace.

Where to Go Next from Almaty Province: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Almaty Province is located in a prime location, and there are plenty of destinations and excursions that you can explore after your visit. Some popular options include:

  1. Astana, the capital city of Kazakhstan.
  2. Bishkek, the capital city of Kyrgyzstan.
  3. Lake Issyk-Kul, a stunning lake located in Kyrgyzstan.

Conclusion

Almaty Province is a must-visit destination for travelers who want to experience the best of Kazakhstan. With its breathtaking natural landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality, this province offers a unique and unforgettable travel experience. Whether you are an outdoor enthusiast, a history buff, or a food lover, Almaty Province will not disappoint.
