Introduction to Kostanay

Kostanay is a beautiful city located in the northern part of Kazakhstan. It is a major agricultural and industrial center and has a rich history and culture that dates back to ancient times. Kostanay is surrounded by breathtaking natural landscapes, including the forest-covered hills, vast steppes, and the Irtish River.

The city of Kostanay is a perfect destination for adventure lovers, nature enthusiasts, and history buffs alike. This guide takes you through the best places to visit, things to do, food and drink scene, where to stay, and more in Kostanay.

List of Places to Visit in Kostanay

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Regional Museum of Kostanay

The Regional Museum of Kostanay is a must-visit for history buffs. The museum houses over 100,000 artifacts and displays showcasing the rich history and culture of Kostanay and its surrounding areas.

Museum of the History of Kostanay City

The Museum of the History of Kostanay City is dedicated to preserving the history of the city. Visitors can explore exhibits displaying artifacts, photographs, and stories that tell the story of this fascinating city.

Zhidebay Palace

The Zhidebay Palace is an impressive neoclassical building that was constructed in 1912. It was named after a famous Kazakh politician, and today it serves as the House of Culture “Zhidebai.”

Abay Opera and Ballet Theater

The Abay Opera and Ballet Theater is one of the oldest theaters in Kazakhstan. The theater hosts a variety of performances throughout the year and is a must-visit for performing arts enthusiasts.

Natural Attractions of Kostanay: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Altyn Emel National Park

Altyn Emel National Park is a nature reserve located in the middle of the desert. The park boasts impressive sand dunes, canyons, and a unique rock formation called the “Singing Dunes.” Visitors can explore the park on camelback or by hiking the trails.

Shalkar Lake

Shalkar Lake is a vast saltwater lake located near Kostanay. The lake is a popular spot for swimming, fishing, and camping. The stunning scenery surrounding the lake is perfect for picnicking and taking in the natural beauty of the area.

Irtish River

The Irtish River is the longest river in Kazakhstan, stretching over 4,250 km. The river is perfect for kayaking, fishing, and leisurely boat rides. Visitors can relax and take in the surrounding scenery or explore the many small islands and sandbars located along the river.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kostanay

Shalkar Steppe

The Shalkar Steppe is a vast open plain that is perfect for hiking or biking. The plain is surrounded by gentle hills and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding area.

Koktenkol Lake

Koktenkol Lake is a scenic spot located near Kostanay. The lake is surrounded by rolling hills and is a popular destination for hiking and biking. Visitors can explore the many trails that wind through the surrounding countryside and take in the stunning views.

Best Things to Do in Kostanay: Activities and Tours

Kostanay City Tour

A city tour is a great way to discover the history and culture of Kostanay. Visitors can explore the many landmarks and historical sites that make this city so unique.

Horseback Riding

Horseback riding is a popular activity in Kostanay, and there are many trails available for visitors to explore. Visitors can discover the stunning landscape while enjoying the thrill of riding through the countryside.

Food and Drink Scene of Kostanay

Must-Try Dishes

Beshbarmak

Beshbarmak is a traditional Kazakh dish that consists of boiled lamb or beef served with noodles and spices.

Kuurdak

Kuurdak is a popular dish made from fried lamb, potatoes, onions, and spices. It is often served with bread or noodles.

Local Beverages

Kumys

Kumys is a traditional Kazakh drink made from fermented mare’s milk. It is known for its healing properties and is a popular drink during the summer months.

Shubat

Shubat is a drink made from fermented camel’s milk. It has a sour taste and is often served as a refreshing drink during the summer months.

Where to Stay in Kostanay: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hotel Kostanay

Hotel Kostanay is a modern and comfortable hotel located in the heart of the city. The hotel offers a variety of room types to choose from and is perfect for travelers looking for comfort and convenience.

Guesthouse Shymbulak

Guesthouse Shymbulak is a cozy guesthouse located near the Altyn Emel National Park. The guesthouse offers comfortable rooms and a friendly atmosphere.

What to Buy in Kostanay: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kazakh Carpets

Kazakh carpets are a traditional souvenir that is perfect for bringing home. They are handmade and feature intricate designs that showcase the rich culture and history of Kazakhstan.

Local Culture and Customs of Kostanay

Kazakhstan is known for its hospitality, and this is evident in the culture and customs of Kostanay. Visitors are often welcomed with open arms and are treated to delicious food and drink.

Best Time to Visit Kostanay

The best time to visit Kostanay is during the summer months from June to August when the weather is warm and sunny. The winter months can be cold and snowy, making it difficult to explore the city and surrounding areas.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kostanay

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine
  • Explore the city and surrounding areas
  • Be respectful of the local culture and customs

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing in public
  • Take photographs without permission
  • Be disrespectful to locals

Safety and Health

Safety in Kostanay

Kostanay is generally a safe city, but visitors should be aware of their surroundings and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Kostanay

Visitors to Kostanay should ensure that they are up to date on their vaccinations and bring any necessary medications with them. It is important to drink bottled water and avoid drinking tap water.

Getting to Kostanay: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Kostanay

Kostanay International Airport is the closest airport to the city and is located approximately 16 km from the city center.

Bus Stations Near Kostanay

Kostanay Bus Station is located in the city center and offers regular services to nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

Train services are available from Astana and other major cities in Kazakhstan.

How to Get Around in Kostanay

Taxis and buses are readily available in Kostanay and are the easiest way to get around the city.

Where to Go Next from Kostanay: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bayanaul National Park

Bayanaul National Park is a nature reserve located in the eastern part of Kazakhstan. The park is known for its stunning scenery and is a popular destination for hiking and camping.

Nur-Sultan

Nur-Sultan is the capital of Kazakhstan and is located approximately 700 km from Kostanay. The city is known for its impressive architecture and cultural attractions.

Conclusion

Kostanay is a fascinating destination that offers visitors a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty. From exploring the city’s landmarks and historical sites to hiking in the surrounding countryside, there is something for everyone in Kostanay. With its welcoming locals and charming atmosphere, Kostanay is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Kazakhstan.
