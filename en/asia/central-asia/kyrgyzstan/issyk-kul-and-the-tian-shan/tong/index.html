Introduction to Tong

Tong is a charming town nestled in the heart of the Tian Shan mountains, located in Kyrgyzstan’s Issyk Kul region. Known for its stunning natural beauty, this town offers a range of outdoor activities, along with cultural and historical attractions.

List of Places to Visit in Tong

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tong Museum: The museum is located in the center of the town and showcases the local history and culture of the town and its people.
  • Tong Bazaar: An open-air market located in the center of the town that offers a variety of traditional Kyrgyz and Central Asian goods and clothing.
  • Sarychat-Ertash State Nature Reserve: Located just outside of Tong, this nature reserve is home to rare and endangered species, including snow leopards, lynx, and bears.

Natural Attractions of Tong: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Tong Canyon: Experience stunning natural beauty while hiking, trekking, or horseback riding through Tong Canyon.
  • Tong Waterfall: Located upstream from Tong Canyon, this waterfall is a popular spot for picnics and swimming.
  • Issyk Kul Lake: Kyrgyzstan’s famous Issyk Kul Lake is just a short drive from Tong and offers plenty of opportunities for swimming, fishing, and other water activities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tong

  • Tong Canyon: There are several hiking and biking routes that take you through Tong Canyon, ranging from easy to challenging.
  • Terskey Ala-Too: This mountain range, located near Tong, offers a range of hiking and biking routes for all skill levels.

Best Things to Do in Tong: Activities and Tours

  • Horseback Riding: Experience the nomadic lifestyle of Kyrgyzstan while horseback riding through the Tian Shan mountains.
  • Visit a yurt camp: Stay in a traditional yurt camp to experience the nomadic culture of Kyrgyzstan.
  • Hot Springs: Relax in the natural hot springs located in the mountains near Tong.

Food and Drink Scene of Tong

Must-Try Dishes

  • Plov: A traditional Central Asian dish made with rice, meat, and vegetables.
  • Lagman: A noodle dish with vegetables and meat in a savory broth.
  • Beshbarmak: A dish of boiled meat and noodles, topped with onion sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Kumis: A fermented dairy drink made from mare’s milk.
  • Chai: Central Asian tea served with sugar and milk.

Where to Stay in Tong: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Yurt camps: Experience the nomadic lifestyle by staying in a traditional yurt camp.
  • Hotels: Tong has several budget and mid-range hotels to choose from.
  • Guesthouses: Stay with a local family in a guesthouse for a more authentic experience.

What to Buy in Tong: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Felt products: Kyrgyzstan is known for its felt crafts, including hats, slippers, and rugs.
  • Jewelry: Silver and turquoise jewelry are popular in Kyrgyzstan.
  • Traditional clothing: Purchase traditional Kyrgyz clothing including the kalpak, a traditional Kyrgyz hat, and colorful embroidery.

Local Culture and Customs of Tong

  • Greet locals with a handshake and say “Salam” (hello) or “Jaksylyk” (how are you).
  • Remove your shoes when entering someone’s home.
  • Women should dress modestly, wearing long skirts and covering their hair in some areas.

Best Time to Visit Tong

The best time to visit Tong is during the summer months (June to August), when the weather is warm and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tong

Do:

  • Respect local traditions and customs.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Bargain at the local markets and bazaars.

Don’t:

  • Take pictures without permission from locals.
  • Disrespect religious or cultural monuments.
  • Drink tap water.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tong

Tong is generally a safe destination for travelers. However, it’s always recommended to take general safety precautions such as keeping valuable items secure and being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Tong

It’s important to have up-to-date vaccinations when traveling to Kyrgyzstan. It’s recommended to consult with a doctor before traveling.

Getting to Tong: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tong

  • Manas International Airport in Bishkek (280 km)

Bus Stations Near Tong

  • Bishkek Western Bus Station (245 km)

Other Options

  • Private taxis and shared taxis can be arranged for transport within Kyrgyzstan.

How to Get Around in Tong

Walking, biking, and horseback riding are great options to explore the town and its surroundings. To reach attractions further away, shared taxis or private hire taxis can be arranged.

Where to Go Next from Tong: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Karakol

A nearby town with a range of outdoor activities and cultural attractions, including the Dungan Mosque and the Przhevalsky Museum.

Bishkek

The capital of Kyrgyzstan is home to a range of museums, art galleries, and historical sites, such as the Ala-Too Square and the Osh bazaar.

Conclusion

Tong is a beautiful destination in Kyrgyzstan that offers a range of outdoor activities, cultural attractions, and delicious local cuisine. Whether you’re interested in hiking and biking, learning about local culture and customs, or simply relaxing in natural hot springs - Tong has something for everyone.
