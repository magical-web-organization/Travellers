Introduction to Dashoguz Province

Located in the northern part of Turkmenistan, Dashoguz Province is often overshadowed by the country’s capital city, Ashgabat. Nevertheless, the province has numerous attractions that can captivate any traveler. Known for its natural beauty and rich history, this Central Asian province is a hidden gem waiting to be explored.

List of Places to Visit in Dashoguz Province

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you are interested in Turkmenistan’s rich history and culture, make sure to visit the following places:

  • Konye-Urgench: An ancient city that was once a major center for Islamic religion and scholarship in Central Asia. It is now a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Dashoguz Museum of Local Lore: This museum houses a rich collection of artifacts, artworks, and ethnographic objects that showcase the history and culture of the province.
  • Abiverd Medieval Ruins: An ancient city that dates back to the 7th century. The ruins include a citadel, a mosque, and a bathhouse.

Natural Attractions of Dashoguz Province: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For nature lovers, here are some must-visit places in Dashoguz Province:

  • Yangykala Canyon: This unique geological formation features towering cliffs in stunning red, orange, and yellow colors.
  • Garlyk Nature Reserve: Home to brown bears, wolves, and various bird species. The reserve’s landscapes are breathtaking and include desert areas, mountains, and waterfalls.
  • Gozli Ata: A stunningly beautiful cave site that also includes an isolated mountain fortress.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dashoguz Province

Dashoguz Province has numerous hiking and biking trails that offer an immersive natural experience. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Yangykala Canyon trek: A challenging trek that is spread over three days and is considered one of the most beautiful hikes in Central Asia.
  • Garlyk Canyon trek: A four-day trek that takes you through the valley of the Garlyk Canyon and its wildlife.
  • Gozli Ata Cave route: A hike that will take you through the cave site and the mountain.

Best Things to Do in Dashoguz Province: Activities and Tours

For a taste of local culture and history, make sure to participate in some of the following tours and activities:

  • Equestrian tours: These tours will take you through the scenic landscapes of Dashoguz Province and introduce you to the region’s rich equestrian heritage.
  • Silk Road tours: These tours will take you through the ancient Silk Road trade route and introduce you to the various historical landmarks in the area.
  • Food tours: These tours will give you a chance to sample the local cuisine and get a taste of the region’s culinary diversity.

Week-Long Itinerary for Dashoguz Province

To make the most of your trip to Dashoguz Province, consider following this itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival and check-in at your preferred accommodation in Dashoguz city.
  • Day 2: Visit the Dashoguz Museum of Local Lore and explore the city.
  • Day 3-4: Trek through the Yangykala Canyon.
  • Day 5: Visit Konye-Urgench.
  • Day 6: Explore the Garlyk Canyon.
  • Day 7: Departure.

Food and Drink Scene of Dashoguz Province

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chuchvara: A type of soup with small dumplings filled with meat or vegetables.
  • Beshbarmak: A traditional Turkmen dish made with boiled meat and noodles.
  • Samsa: A savory pastry filled with meat, potatoes, onions, or cheese.

Local Beverages

  • Shorba: A hot soup made with water or meat broth, herbs, and vegetables.
  • Ayran: A refreshing yogurt-based drink that is popular during summer.

Where to Stay in Dashoguz Province: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Dashoguz Province has a variety of accommodations to suit every budget:

  • Hotel Uzboy: A three-star hotel that offers comfortable rooms, a restaurant, and a bar.
  • Dashoguz Hotel: A three-star hotel located in the city center that offers a range of amenities.
  • Guesthouses: A number of guesthouses offer affordable and comfortable accommodation in the region.

What to Buy in Dashoguz Province: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Carpets: Dashoguz Province is famous for its intricate and colorful carpets.
  • Yomut-style jewelry: The region is also known for its handmade jewelry, which is a part of the Yomut tribe’s heritage.
  • Spices: If you love cooking, consider buying some of the region’s spices and flavorings, including saffron, sumac, and cumin.

Local Culture and Customs of Dashoguz Province

  • As a predominantly Muslim country, dress modestly, especially when inside mosques or other religious buildings.
  • When greeting locals, men should shake hands with men, and women should shake hands with women.
  • Alcohol is restricted in Turkmenistan, and locals do not consume it.

Best Time to Visit Dashoguz Province

The best time to visit Dashoguz Province is from April to October, as the weather is mild and pleasant during this period. However, keep in mind that summers can be extremely hot, with temperatures reaching up to 45°C (113°F).

Do’s and Don’ts of Dashoguz Province

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Take off your shoes when entering a home or mosque.
  • Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious buildings.

Don’t:

  • Carry or consume alcohol in public places.
  • Photograph individuals or religious buildings without permission.
  • Show public displays of affection.

Safety and Health

Safety in Dashoguz Province

Dashoguz Province is a safe place to travel. However, as with any travel, take precautionary measures to keep your belongings safe and avoid traveling alone at night.

Health in Dashoguz Province

Make sure to take adequate precautions to protect yourself from the sun and heat during the summer months. It’s also advisable to carry mosquito repellent, especially if you are visiting natural areas with water bodies.

Getting to Dashoguz Province: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Dashoguz Province

The nearest airport to Dashoguz Province is Dashoguz International Airport, which has regular flights connecting to other major cities in Turkmenistan.

Bus Stations Near Dashoguz Province

Several long-distance bus services connect Dashoguz Province to other major cities in Turkmenistan.

Other Options

Traveling by car or taxi is also an option when visiting Dashoguz Province.

How to Get Around in Dashoguz Province

The best way to get around Dashoguz Province is by taxi or hiring a private car with a driver. You can also rent a car, but the road conditions can be challenging in some areas of the province.

Where to Go Next from Dashoguz Province: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you are interested in exploring more of Turkmenistan, consider the following destinations:

  • Ashgabat: The capital city of Turkmenistan is a vibrant city known for its striking architecture and friendly people.
  • Darvaza Gas Crater: A remarkable natural phenomenon that has become one of Turkmenistan’s most popular tourist attractions.
  • Mary: A city in southeastern Turkmenistan, known for its ancient history and rich cultural heritage.

Conclusion

Dashoguz Province is a unique and beautiful destination that is often overlooked by tourists visiting Turkmenistan. With its rich history, stunning natural landscapes, and vibrant culture, the province has something for every traveler. Whether you’re a history buff or an outdoor enthusiast, Dashoguz Province is a must-visit destination that will leave you with memories to last a lifetime.
