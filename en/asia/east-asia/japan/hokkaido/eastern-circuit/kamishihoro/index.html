Introduction to Kamishihoro

Kamishihoro is a picturesque town located in the eastern circuit of Hokkaido, Japan. It is famous for its stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. The town is surrounded by mountains, forests and rivers, making it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Visitors can also immerse themselves in the local culture by visiting the town’s museums, historical sites and local markets.

List of Places to Visit in Kamishihoro

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kamishihoro History Museum: This museum showcases the history of the town from the Jomon period to the modern era through its exhibits of artifacts, photographs, and documents.
  • Hamanasu-no-Oka Museum: This museum is dedicated to the Ainu people, the indigenous people of Hokkaido. It displays exhibits, artifacts, and photographs related to the Ainu culture, history and way of life.
  • Kamishihoro Shrine: This shrine is dedicated to the god of agriculture and is considered the protector of the town’s crops. The shrine has a serene and tranquil atmosphere and is a great spot for meditation and contemplation.
  • Satsunai Municipal Museum: This museum showcases the history and culture of the Ainu people and the Settler period.

Natural Attractions of Kamishihoro: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Ashibetsu: This mountain stands at an elevation of 1,611 meters and offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape. Visitors can hike the mountain to enjoy the breathtaking views.
  • Lake Shikaribetsu: This lake is the highest lake in Hokkaido and is located near the Kamikawa Pass. Visitors can enjoy scenic views of the surrounding mountains and forests while boating, fishing or hiking.
  • Shikaribetsu-Kotan: This is a winter-only ice village that is constructed each year on the frozen lake Shikaribetsu. Visitors can experience the unique Japanese ice village culture by staying in ice igloos, attending ice concerts and enjoying local cuisine.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kamishihoro

Kamishihoro offers plenty of hiking and biking routes for visitors to explore. Some of the top routes include:

  • Ashibetsu Traverse: This route offers a challenging hike up Mount Ashibetsu and provides stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Horokanai Traverse: This route runs through the scenic Horokanai area and offers a beautiful view of the Chubetsu Dam and the surrounding mountains.
  • Shikaribetsu Lake Circuit: This route offers a loop around the beautiful Shikaribetsu Lake and provides opportunities to stop and enjoy the scenic views along the way.

Best Things to Do in Kamishihoro: Activities and Tours

  • Soba Making Workshop: Visitors can experience the traditional soba-making process by taking part in a soba-making workshop.
  • Horseback Riding: Visitors can explore the scenic landscapes of Kamishihoro by taking a horseback ride through the mountains and valleys.
  • Hot Spring Tours: Kamishihoro is known for its numerous hot springs, and visitors can enjoy a relaxing soak in one of the many local onsens.

Food and Drink Scene of Kamishihoro

Must-Try Dishes

  • Soba: Kamishihoro is famous for its handmade soba noodles, made from a mixture of buckwheat flour and wheat flour.
  • Kamishihoro Curry: This curry is made with locally sourced vegetables and meat, and has a unique flavor that is worth trying.
  • Wild Game Meat: Visitors can enjoy a variety of wild game meat, including deer and bear meat, freshly caught from the surrounding forests.

Local Beverages

  • Shikaribetsu-no-oka Wine: This is a locally produced wine made from the grapes grown on the nearby Mount Shikaribetsu.
  • Kamishihoro Sake: This sake is made from locally sourced rice and has a unique flavor that is worth trying.

Where to Stay in Kamishihoro: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Kamishihoro Onsen Hotel: This hotel offers hot spring baths and comfortable rooms with mountain views.
  • Horokanai Kogen Hotel: This hotel is located in the scenic Horokanai area and offers comfortable rooms and hot spring baths.
  • Guest House Rindo: This guest house offers affordable accommodation and a friendly, welcoming atmosphere.

What to Buy in Kamishihoro: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Soba Noodles: Visitors can bring home handmade soba noodles as a unique and delicious souvenir.
  • Kamishihoro Honey: This honey is produced locally and has a unique flavor that is perfectly suited to the climate and natural environment around Kamishihoro.
  • Ainu Crafts: Visitors can purchase traditional Ainu crafts, including wood carvings, woven baskets, and embroidered fabrics.

Local Culture and Customs of Kamishihoro

Kamishihoro has a rich cultural heritage, with a unique blend of Ainu and Japanese traditions. Visitors should respect local traditions and customs, including removing shoes before entering homes and other buildings.

Best Time to Visit Kamishihoro

The best time to visit Kamishihoro is during the spring and autumn months, when the weather is mild and comfortable for activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kamishihoro

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Try the local food and drinks.
  • Enjoy outdoor activities and sights.
  • Take part in cultural workshops and experiences.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local traditions or customs.
  • Litter or damage the natural environment.
  • Ignore safety warnings and guidelines.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kamishihoro

Kamishihoro is a safe town with friendly locals. Visitors should always keep an eye on their belongings and avoid leaving valuables unattended.

Health in Kamishihoro

Visitors should always carry necessary medications and travel insurance. In case of an emergency, tourists can visit Kamishihoro Town Hospital, which offers quality health services.

Getting to Kamishihoro: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Kamishihoro

The nearest airport to Kamishihoro is the Obihiro Airport, which is approximately an hour away by car or bus.

Bus Stations Near Kamishihoro

Visitors can take a bus from Obihiro Station to Kamishihoro Bus Terminal, which is located in the town center.

Other Options

Visitors can also opt for a rental car or hire a taxi to reach Kamishihoro from nearby towns or airports.

How to Get Around in Kamishihoro

Visitors can easily explore Kamishihoro on foot, but rental car or bike rentals are also available for exploring the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Kamishihoro: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Asahikawa: This city is famous for its zoo and is the gateway to Daisetsuzan National Park.
  • Furano: This town is famous for its flower fields and ski resorts.
  • Tokachi: This region is known for its farm-to-table cuisine and hot springs.

Conclusion

Kamishihoro is a town rich in natural beauty, cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. Visitors can immerse themselves in the local culture, explore scenic landscapes and indulge in delicious local cuisine. A trip to Kamishihoro is an unforgettable experience for anyone seeking an authentic Japanese cultural and natural experience.
