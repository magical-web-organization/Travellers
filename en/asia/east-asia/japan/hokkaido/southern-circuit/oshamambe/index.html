A Comprehensive Travel Guide for Oshamambe, Japan

Located in Southern Circuit in Hokkaido, Japan, Oshamambe is a picturesque town that is often overlooked by tourists. However, this hidden gem is known for its natural beauty and cultural significance. In this guide, we will take you through the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and where to go next from Oshamambe.

Introduction to Oshamambe

Oshamambe is a small town surrounded by the scenic beauty of the rolling hills and the lush forests of Northern Japan. The town has a rich history and cultural significance that can be seen in its historical buildings and museums. Though it can be explored in a day or two, the town offers a tranquil and peaceful escape for travelers who want to get away from the hustle and bustle of the big cities.

List of Places to Visit in Oshamambe

Here are some of the must-visit attractions in Oshamambe:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Oshamambe Railway Heritage Park: This museum offers an insight into the history of the Oshamambe railway, which played an important role in the development of the town.
  • Oshamambe Town Museum: The museum showcases the history and culture of Oshamambe town, including its festivals and traditional crafts.
  • Oshamambe Castle Ruins: The ruins of this castle offer a glimpse into the era of the samurais and the history of the town.

Natural Attractions of Oshamambe: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Daibosatsu: This mountain is a popular spot for hiking and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and forests.
  • Oshamambe Onsen: This hot spring is a perfect way to unwind after a long day of exploring the town.
  • Lake Oshamambe: This serene lake is surrounded by beautiful cherry blossom trees in the spring and is a popular spot for fishing and boating in the summer.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Oshamambe

Oshamambe is full of hiking and biking trails that offer a stunning view of the town and its surroundings. Some of the best trails include:

  • Oshamambe Yakushi Mountain Trail
  • Oshamambe Kogen High Jyuku Trail
  • Oshamambe Lake Circuit Trail

Best Things to Do in Oshamambe: Activities and Tours

  • Take a day trip to Hakodate: This picturesque port city is only a short train ride away and offers a variety of eateries, shopping, and sightseeing spots.
  • Go bird-watching: Oshamambe is known for its rich bird life, and bird watchers can spot a variety of birds, including the white-tailed eagle and the northern pika.
  • Experience traditional Japanese culture: Visitors can participate in traditional Japanese tea ceremonies or try their hand at making traditional crafts such as pottery and calligraphy.

Week-Long Itinerary for Oshamambe

Day 1: Visit the Oshamambe Railway Heritage Park and the Oshamambe Castle Ruins Day 2: Explore the Oshamambe Town Museum and the Oshamambe Onsen Day 3: Hike the Oshamambe Yakushi Mountain Trail Day 4: Take a day trip to Hakodate Day 5: Go bird-watching around Lake Oshamambe Day 6: Experience traditional Japanese culture Day 7: Relax and unwind at the Oshamambe Onsen

Food and Drink Scene of Oshamambe

No trip to Japan is complete without trying some of the local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local drinks in Oshamambe:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kaisen-don: A dish made with fresh seafood atop a bed of sushi rice
  • Hokke: A grilled fish served with a special soy sauce
  • Uni: Fresh sea urchin, which is one of the culinary highlights of Hokkaido

Local Beverages

  • Sake: Oshamambe is known for its rice wine, and visitors can taste the local brew at the Oshamambe Sake Brewery.
  • Oshamambe Apple Cider: Made from locally grown apples, this drink is a refreshing and healthy thirst quencher.

Where to Stay in Oshamambe: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Oshamambe Station Hotel: This cozy hotel is conveniently located next to the Oshamambe train station and offers comfortable rooms.
  • Oshamambe Onsen Matsumotoro: This hot spring hotel offers stunning views of the mountains and the lake and is a great place to relax and unwind.
  • Oshamambe Guest House Traumerei: This guesthouse is located in the heart of the town and offers affordable and comfortable lodging for travelers.

What to Buy in Oshamambe: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Oshamambe Sake: Visitors can buy the locally brewed sake at the Oshamambe Sake Brewery.
  • Oshamambe Apple Cider: This refreshing drink is a great souvenir to take back home.
  • Oshamambe Pottery: Visitors can take their pick from a variety of traditional Japanese pottery, which is one of the town’s traditional handicrafts.

Local Culture and Customs of Oshamambe

Oshamambe has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors are advised to respect the local customs and traditions. One of the most important traditions of the town is its festivals, which are held throughout the year and can be an exciting and colorful experience for visitors.

Best Time to Visit Oshamambe

The best time to visit Oshamambe is from late April to early November, when the weather is mild and sunny, and the mountains and forests are alive with blooming flowers and colorful foliage. Winter, while beautiful, can be harsh and snowy, making it difficult to explore the town’s natural attractions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Oshamambe

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine and drinks
  • Participate in the town’s festivals and cultural events
  • Respect the local customs and traditions

Don’t:

  • Leave trash behind when hiking or biking
  • Walk on the street when using your phone
  • Speak loudly or disturb the peace and quiet of the town

Safety and Health

Safety in Oshamambe

Oshamambe is a safe town, but travelers are advised to take standard safety precautions when hiking or biking in the mountains, such as bringing enough water and food and wearing appropriate clothing and shoes.

Health in Oshamambe

Visitors should be aware of the risk of tick bites during their stay in Oshamambe. To avoid tick bites, travelers are advised to wear long-sleeved clothes and long pants and to use insect repellent.

Getting to Oshamambe: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Oshamambe

The nearest airport to Oshamambe is New Chitose Airport, which is located approximately 96 kilometers away. From the airport, visitors can take a train or bus to Oshamambe.

Bus Stations Near Oshamambe

Oshamambe Bus Station is located in the heart of the town and can be reached by bus from major cities in Hokkaido, such as Sapporo and Hakodate.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to Oshamambe or take a scenic train ride from Sapporo or Hakodate.

How to Get Around in Oshamambe

Visitors can easily explore the town on foot or by renting a bicycle. Taxis are also available for those who prefer not to walk or bike.

Where to Go Next from Oshamambe: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Hakodate: This picturesque port city is known for its stunning views of the coast and its fresh seafood.
  • Sapporo: Hokkaido’s capital city, Sapporo is a bustling city that offers a variety of cultural and culinary delights.
  • Lake Toya: This serene lake is surrounded by mountains and hot springs and is a popular destination for nature lovers.

Conclusion

Oshamambe is a hidden gem in Hokkaido that offers a unique blend of nature, culture, and tranquility. From its scenic mountains and forests to its traditional handicrafts and local cuisine, there is something for everyone in this charming town. We hope this guide has given you all the information you need to plan your trip to explore the best of Oshamambe.
