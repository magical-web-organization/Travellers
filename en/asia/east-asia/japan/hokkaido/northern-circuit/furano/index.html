Introduction to Furano

Furano is a charming city located in the Northern Circuit of the island of Hokkaido, Japan. Famous for its rolling hills, lavender fields, and pristine winter wonderland, Furano is a popular travel destination for tourists from all over the world. In this travel guide, we will explore the best places to visit, the best things to do and see, where to stay, and what to eat and drink while in Furano.

Local Culture and Customs of Furano

  • Respect the locals and their customs
  • Take off your shoes when entering a home or traditional Japanese accommodation

Best Time to Visit Furano

The best time to visit Furano is during the summer months (June-August) when the lavender fields are in full bloom, or during the winter months (December-March) for skiing and snow activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Furano

Do:

  • Respect local customs and culture
  • Take off your shoes when entering a home or traditional Japanese accommodation
  • Try local food and drink

Don’t:

  • Litter
  • Ignore wildlife

Safety and Health

Safety in Furano

Furano is generally a safe city for travelers, and crime rates are low. However, it is important to take basic safety precautions, such as securing your belongings and staying aware of your surroundings.

Health in Furano

Furano has a well-developed healthcare system, and medical facilities are available throughout the city. However, it is always advisable to have travel insurance and to research healthcare options before traveling.

Getting to Furano: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Furano

The closest airport to Furano is Asahikawa Airport, which is about 45 minutes away by car or bus. Sapporo’s New Chitose Airport is another convenient option, about 2 hours by car.

Bus Stations Near Furano

Furano Bus Station is located in the city center and is the main transportation hub for local and regional buses.

Other Options

Taxis and car rentals are also available, but having a valid Japanese driver’s license is required for driving in Japan.

How to Get Around in Furano

The best way to get around Furano is by car or by bicycle. Taxis and buses are also available, but may not be as convenient for exploring the surrounding countryside.
