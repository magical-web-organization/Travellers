Introduction to Rishiri Island

Rishiri Island is a beautiful and remote island located in the Northern Circuit of Hokkaido, Japan. The island is known for its stunning natural beauty, and is a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts looking for hiking, biking, and scenic views. Rishiri Island is also famous for its seafood, which can be enjoyed at many of the island’s local restaurants.

List of Places to Visit in Rishiri Island

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Rishiri Island may be known for its natural beauty, but the island also has a rich cultural history. Visitors can explore the island’s history and culture by visiting its museums and historical sites.

  • Rishiri Island Historical Museum: Learn about the island’s history and culture through displays and exhibits, including artifacts and photographs.
  • Rishiri Island Shrine: This beautiful shrine is located on the southwestern coast of the island, and is surrounded by stunning views of the sea.
  • Rishiri Island Lighthouse: This historic lighthouse is located on the northern tip of the island, and provides beautiful views of the surrounding area.

Natural Attractions of Rishiri Island: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Rishiri Island is known for its beautiful natural scenery, and offers many outdoor activities for visitors to enjoy.

  • Mount Rishiri: This beautiful mountain is the island’s main attraction, and can be hiked during the summer months. The trail is steep, but the views from the top are stunning.
  • Senhoshi Beach: This beautiful beach is located on the eastern coast of the island, and is known for its beautiful white sand and clear blue waters.
  • Oshidomari Beach: This beautiful beach is located on the northern coast of the island, and is known for its stunning views of the sea.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Rishiri Island

Rishiri Island is a popular destination for hiking and biking, with many beautiful trails and routes.

  • Mount Rishiri Hike: This popular hike takes visitors to the summit of the island’s beautiful mountain, and provides stunning views of the surrounding area.
  • Rishiri Nature Trail: This beautiful trail takes visitors through the island’s natural beauty, including forests and waterfalls.
  • Rishiri Island Bike Route: This scenic route takes visitors around the island’s beautiful coastline, and provides stunning views of the sea.

Best Things to Do in Rishiri Island: Activities and Tours

There are many things to do and see on Rishiri Island. Below are some of the best activities and tours:

  • Sea Kayaking: Explore the island’s beautiful coastline by sea kayak, and enjoy stunning views of the sea and surrounding area.
  • Boat Tours: Take a boat tour around the island, and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding sea and landscapes.
  • Seafood Restaurants: Enjoy the island’s famous seafood at one of the many local restaurants.

Week-Long Itinerary for Rishiri Island

To make the most of your visit to Rishiri Island, we recommend the following week-long itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrive on the island and explore the local area.
  • Day 2: Hike Mount Rishiri and enjoy stunning views of the surrounding area.
  • Day 3: Explore the island’s museums and historical sites.
  • Day 4: Take a sea kayak tour around the island’s beautiful coastline.
  • Day 5: Relax on one of the island’s beautiful beaches.
  • Day 6: Take a boat tour and explore the island’s stunning sea views.
  • Day 7: Enjoy the island’s famous seafood at one of the local restaurants.

Food and Drink Scene of Rishiri Island

Rishiri Island is famous for its seafood, and offers many other traditional Japanese dishes to enjoy as well.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kaisendon: A bowl of rice topped with fresh seafood, including salmon, squid, and sea urchin.
  • Hokkaido Ramen: Thick noodles in a rich and savory broth, topped with meat and vegetables.
  • Shishamo: Grilled smelt fish, often served with miso soup and rice.

Local Beverages

  • Sake: A traditional Japanese rice wine, often enjoyed with seafood and other Japanese dishes.
  • Shochu: A distilled alcoholic beverage made from barley, sweet potatoes, or other grains.

Where to Stay in Rishiri Island: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Rishiri Island is a remote island, but there are still several accommodation options available for visitors.

  • Rishiri Royal Hotel: A luxurious hotel located on the northeastern coast of the island, with stunning views of the sea.
  • Rishiri Green Hill Guest House: A cozy guesthouse located in the center of the island, with easy access to local attractions and restaurants.
  • Shimayado Chushin: A traditional Japanese inn located on the southern coast of the island, with beautiful views of the sea and surrounding landscapes.

What to Buy in Rishiri Island: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Rishiri Island offers several unique and traditional products that are perfect for souvenirs or gifts.

  • Rishiri Island Sea Salt: This high-quality sea salt is made from the island’s saltwater, and is prized for its unique taste.
  • Rishiri Island Handmade Soap: This natural soap is made from Rishiri Island’s natural ingredients, including kelp and sea salt.
  • Rishiri Island Souvenirs: Six Seasons: A popular souvenir shop, offering a wide range of local products and souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Rishiri Island

Rishiri Island has a unique and rich local culture and customs, including traditional festivals and ceremonies.

  • Rishiri Island Seaweed Festival: This festival is held annually in June, and celebrates the island’s famous seaweed.
  • Rishiri Island Shinto Ceremony: This traditional Shinto ceremony is held annually to pray for a bountiful harvest and safe fishing season.

Best Time to Visit Rishiri Island

The best time to visit Rishiri Island is during the summer months (June to September), when the weather is mild and pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rishiri Island

Here are some important do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Rishiri Island.

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local seafood and traditional Japanese dishes.
  • Be prepared for the weather, and bring appropriate clothing and gear for outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Leave trash or litter behind.
  • Disturb the island’s natural beauty or wildlife.
  • Ignore safety warnings and precautions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Rishiri Island

Rishiri Island is generally a safe destination, but visitors should still take precautions when exploring the island’s natural beauty.

  • Always follow safety guidelines for outdoor activities, such as hiking and biking.
  • Be aware of weather conditions and changes in tide levels when exploring the island’s beaches and coastline.

Health in Rishiri Island

Rishiri Island has a small medical clinic, but visitors should still bring any necessary medications or medical supplies with them.

Getting to Rishiri Island: Transportation Options and Logistics

Rishiri Island is a remote destination, but can still be accessed via several transportation options.

Airports Near Rishiri Island

  • Wakkanai Airport: The nearest airport to Rishiri Island, located in Wakkanai, Hokkaido.

Bus Stations Near Rishiri Island

  • Rishiri Island Bus Terminal: Buses run daily between Rishiri Island and Wakkanai.

Other Options

  • Ferry: Ferries run daily between Rishiri Island and Wakkanai.

How to Get Around in Rishiri Island

While Rishiri Island is small, there are still several transportation options available for visitors.

  • Rental Car: Car rentals are available on the island, and can be a convenient option for exploring the island’s sights.
  • Bicycle Rental: Bicycles can also be rented on the island, and are a great way to explore the island’s natural beauty.
  • Local Bus: The island’s bus system is frequent and reliable, and can be a convenient option for getting around.

Where to Go Next from Rishiri Island: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

While Rishiri Island may be a remote destination, there are still several nearby destinations and excursions to consider.

  • Rebun Island: This nearby island is known for its beautiful wildflowers and natural beauty, and is accessible by ferry from Rishiri Island.
  • Wakkanai, Hokkaido: This northernmost city in Japan is known for its stunning coastal views and unique culture.

Conclusion

Rishiri Island is a beautiful and remote destination, offering stunning natural beauty and a rich cultural history.
