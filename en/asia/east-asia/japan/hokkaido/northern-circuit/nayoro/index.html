Introduction to Nayoro

Nayoro is a hidden gem located in Northern Circuit, in Hokkaido, in Japan. The city boasts of its natural beauty with its rolling hills, scenic countryside, and breathtaking landscapes. Nayoro is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts with its hiking/biking routes, ski resorts, and nature parks. The city is also known for its rich culture, delectable cuisine, and warm hospitality.

Best Time to Visit Nayoro

The best time to visit Nayoro is from May to October when the weather is mild and ideal for outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and sightseeing.

Safety and Health

Safety in Nayoro

Nayoro is a very safe city, and crimes against tourists are rare. However, always be vigilant and keep your belongings safe.

Health in Nayoro

Nayoro has a good healthcare system, and hospitals and health clinics are easily accessible.
