Introduction to Muroran

Located in the central circuit of Hokkaido, Japan, Muroran is a beautiful port city surrounded by mountains and sea. It is a famous tourist destination, known for its stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and delicious cuisine. If you are planning a trip to Japan, a visit to Muroran is a must. In this comprehensive travel guide, we provide you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Muroran.

List of Places to Visit in Muroran

There is no dearth of attractions in Muroran. Here is a list of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Muroran City Museum of Natural History
  2. Muroran Museum of Art
  3. Cape Chikyu
  4. Hakucho Bridge
  5. Muroran City Folk Museum

Natural Attractions of Muroran: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Mount Sokuryo
  2. Toyomi Hot Springs
  3. Muroran City Aquarium
  4. Nakatoya Observatory
  5. Teine Volcano Group

Local Culture and Customs of Muroran

Muroran is proud of its unique culture and customs. Here are some of the most significant:

  1. Respect for elders
  2. Honoring the sea
  3. Love for nature
  4. Passion for food
  5. Appreciation of art

Best Time to Visit Muroran

The best time to visit Muroran is in the spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November) when the weather is milder and the foliage is breathtaking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Muroran

Do:

  1. Be respectful of local traditions and customs
  2. Try local cuisine
  3. Take off your shoes before entering homes and buildings
  4. Carry cash as not all shops and restaurants accept credit cards
  5. Learn some basic Japanese

Don’t:

  1. Smoke in public places
  2. Leave tips in restaurants
  3. Wear shoes inside homes and buildings
  4. Be loud or disrespectful
  5. Enter onsens (hot springs) if you have any open wounds

Safety and Health

Safety in Muroran

Muroran is a safe city with low crime rates. However, it is always advisable to take necessary precautions such as carrying identification and staying aware of your surroundings.

Health in Muroran

Muroran has an excellent healthcare system, and medical facilities are easily accessible. However, it is recommended to have travel insurance before embarking on your trip.

Getting to Muroran: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Muroran

New Chitose Airport is the nearest airport to Muroran, located about 90 kilometers away. From there, buses and taxis are available to reach Muroran.

Bus Stations Near Muroran

Muroran Bus Terminal is the main bus station in the city, connecting it to other locations in Hokkaido and Japan.

Other Options

Muroran can also be reached by train or car.

How to Get Around in Muroran

The best way to get around Muroran is by car or bus, both of which are readily available for rent.
