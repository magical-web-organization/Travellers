Introduction to Yurihama

Nestled in the picturesque Tottori Prefecture of Japan, Yurihama is a hidden gem that offers breathtaking scenery, rich culture, and exciting adventures for all types of travelers. Known for its scenic lookout points, hot spring resorts, and delicious cuisine, Yurihama is a top destination for those seeking a relaxing yet adventure-filled vacation.

In this travel guide, we have compiled a comprehensive list of all the must-see attractions, top activities, and best places to eat and stay in Yurihama. So put on your explorer’s hat and get ready to embark on an unforgettable journey!

List of Places to Visit in Yurihama

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Yoshida Family Residence - Visit this well-preserved Japanese-style house built in the early 1900s and learn about the Yoshida family’s fascinating history.
  2. Mizuki Shigeru Road - This street is dedicated to the works and life of Shigeru Mizuki, a famous manga artist from Tottori.
  3. Sakaiminato City Museum - Discover the history and culture of Sakaiminato, a neighboring city of Yurihama that played a significant role in Japan’s fishing industry.
  4. Kannon-in Temple - This beautiful temple, located on a mountainside, offers stunning views of the surrounding forest and countryside.

Natural Attractions of Yurihama: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Mt. Daisen - This majestic mountain offers hiking trails for all skill levels and is a popular spot for skiing and snowboarding in the winter.
  2. Togo Lake - Known for its crystal-clear waters, Togo Lake is a peaceful spot for boating, fishing, and taking in the scenery.
  3. Yumura Onsen - This hot spring resort town boasts mineral-rich waters that have been said to have healing properties.
  4. Seaside Cycling Road - This 22-kilometer route offers breathtaking ocean views and is perfect for cycling enthusiasts.
  5. Kotogahama Beach - This scenic beach is a great spot for swimming, sunbathing, and watching the sunset.
  6. Hiruzen Yakushi-no-Sato - This nature park features breathtaking views of the Chugoku Mountains and offers outdoor activities such as horseback riding and paragliding.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Yurihama

  1. Mt. Daisen Summit Hike - This 2-day hike takes you to the summit of Mt. Daisen, the highest peak in the Chugoku Mountains.
  2. Kotogahama Beach to Hiruzen Cycling Route - This 22-kilometer route offers scenic views of the ocean and the mountains and is perfect for cycling enthusiasts.

Best Things to Do in Yurihama: Activities and Tours

  1. Yumura Onsen Tour - Take a guided tour of the Yumura Onsen hot spring resort town and learn about its history and healing properties.
  2. Wine Tasting Tour - Visit the local wineries in Yurihama and sample their delicious wines.
  3. Fishing Experience - Try your hand at fishing in the crystal-clear waters of Togo Lake.
  4. Amarube Viaduct Sightseeing Train - Take a train ride over the stunning Amarube Viaduct, a bridge that spans the Sea of Japan.

Food and Drink Scene of Yurihama

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Sakaiminato seafood dishes - Savor fresh seafood from the nearby port city of Sakaiminato.
  2. Sanzoku-yaki - This grilled chicken dish is a local specialty in Tottori but can be found in Yurihama as well.
  3. Nijisseiki Pear - These juicy and sweet pears are a must-try in Yurihama and are known for their high quality.

Local Beverages

  1. Tottori Sake - Tottori Prefecture is famous for its sake and offers a wide range of flavors and brands to choose from.
  2. Yurihama Wine - The local wineries in Yurihama produce some delicious wines that are a must-try for wine enthusiasts.

Where to Stay in Yurihama: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Yurihama Onsen Yurayura - This hot spring resort offers traditional Japanese-style rooms and a relaxing atmosphere.
  2. Yurihama Kokusai Hotel - Located near Togo Lake, this hotel offers stunning views of the lake and the mountains.
  3. Yonago Washington Hotel Plaza - While not in Yurihama, this hotel is conveniently located near Yonago Airport and provides easy access to the surrounding areas.

What to Buy in Yurihama: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Kotogahama Beach Glassware - These beautiful glassware pieces are made from glass that has been smoothed and shaped by the ocean’s waves.
  2. Yurihama Onsen Skincare Products - Made with mineral-rich waters, these skincare products are a popular choice for those seeking natural and rejuvenating products.

Local Culture and Customs of Yurihama

  1. Onsen Etiquette - When visiting hot springs, it is important to note that most require guests to be completely naked in the baths.
  2. Greeting Customs - When meeting someone for the first time, it is customary to bow as a sign of respect.

Best Time to Visit Yurihama

The best time to visit Yurihama is in the spring and fall when the weather is mild, and the scenery is breathtaking. In the spring, visitors can enjoy viewing the cherry blossoms, and in the fall, the leaves change to vibrant colors, making for a picturesque landscape.

Do’s and Don’ts of Yurihama

Do:

  1. Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  2. Learn about the local culture and customs.
  3. Take advantage of the outdoor activities and scenery.

Don’t:

  1. Wear shoes inside traditional Japanese-style accommodations.
  2. Disrespect local customs or culture.
  3. Litter or damage the natural environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Yurihama

Overall, Yurihama is a safe destination. There are very few incidents of crime or violence in the area, and the locals are known for their hospitality and kindness.

Health in Yurihama

The water in Yurihama is generally safe to drink, and the food is prepared with high safety standards. If you have any concerns, it is always best to consult with a doctor or pharmacist before your trip.

Getting to Yurihama: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Yurihama

  1. Tottori Airport - The nearest airport to Yurihama is Tottori Airport, which offers domestic flights from major cities in Japan.

Bus Stations Near Yurihama

  1. Yurihama Station - The nearest bus station to Yurihama is located within walking distance of many of the main attractions in town.

Other Options

  1. Rental car - A rental car is a convenient option for exploring the surrounding areas of Yurihama.
  2. Taxi - Taxis are readily available and are a good option for those traveling in groups or with luggage.

How to Get Around in Yurihama

The best way to get around Yurihama is by rental car or by cycling. There are also buses and taxis available, but they may not be as convenient or flexible.

Where to Go Next from Yurihama: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Izumo Taisha - This ancient shrine is one of the oldest and most important shrines in Japan and is located only a short drive from Yurihama.
  2. Shimane Prefecture - Located to the west of Tottori, Shimane Prefecture offers a wide range of attractions, including the Adachi Museum of Art and the famous Izumo soba noodles.

Conclusion

Yurihama is a hidden gem that offers something for every type of traveler. From outdoor adventures to cultural experiences and relaxing hot springs, this town is a perfect destination for those seeking a unique and immersive vacation experience. So pack your bags and get ready to discover all that Yurihama has to offer!
