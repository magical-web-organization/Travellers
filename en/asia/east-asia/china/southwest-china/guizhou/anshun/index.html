Introduction to Anshun

Located in the southwestern province of Guizhou, Anshun is a city rich in both historical and natural wonders. With its picturesque landscapes, ancient architecture, and vibrant culture, Anshun has become a popular destination for both domestic and international tourists.

List of Places to Visit in Anshun

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Huangguoshu Waterfall

At a height of 77.8 meters, Huangguoshu Waterfall is one of the largest waterfalls in Asia, and the highlight of any visit to Anshun. You can take a boat ride to get an up-close view of the waterfall or hike around it for a panoramic view.

Dragon Palace Cave

The Dragon Palace Cave is a stunning natural wonder located near the Huangguoshu Waterfall. The cave features various stalactites, stalagmites, and underground rivers.

Tunpu Ancient Town

Tunpu Ancient Town is an ethnic Miao village located in the suburbs of Anshun. It offers a glimpse into the traditional way of life, architecture, and culture of the Miao people.

Natural Attractions of Anshun: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Zhijin Cave

Zhijin Cave, also known as the “Palace of Nature,” is a popular attraction among hikers and outdoor enthusiasts. The cave features an underground river, waterfalls, and several halls with unique natural features.

Dragon Waterfall Scenic Area

Located at the intersection of the Wujiang River and the Beipan River, the Dragon Waterfall Scenic Area offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Visitors can take a cable car or hike to the top of the mountain to enjoy the breathtaking scenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Anshun

Huangguoshu Waterfall Scenic Area

The Huangguoshu Waterfall Scenic Area offers various hiking and biking routes to explore the surrounding nature. The most popular route is the Water Curtain Cave Route, which takes you behind the waterfall and offers a unique perspective of the cascading water.

Best Things to Do in Anshun: Activities and Tours

Take a Miao Village Tour

A Miao Village Tour is an ideal way to experience the local culture and customs of the Miao people. The tour includes visits to ancient houses, a local market, and traditional Miao performances.

Taste the Local Cuisine

The local cuisine of Anshun is known for its spicy and sour flavors. Some must-try dishes include the sour fish hot pot, Guizhou-style spicy chicken, and the hot and sour noodles.

Food and Drink Scene of Anshun

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sour fish hot pot
  • Guizhou-style spicy chicken
  • Hot and sour noodles
  • Zongzi (sticky rice wrapped in bamboo leaves)
  • Miao-style glutinous rice dumplings

Local Beverages

  • Rice wine (Mijiu)
  • Soy milk tea (Doujiang Cha)
  • Sugar cane juice

  • Maotai liquor (a famous Chinese drink)

Local Culture and Customs of Anshun

Anshun has a rich and diverse cultural background, with a significant Miao ethnic group living in the region. Visitors should expect to experience traditional Miao clothing, music, dance, and customs throughout their visit.

Best Time to Visit Anshun

The best time to visit Anshun is during the autumn and spring seasons when the weather is mild, and the scenery is at its prime.

Do’s and Don’ts of Anshun

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine
  • Wear comfortable shoes for hiking and walking
  • Learn about the local culture and customs

Don’t:

  • Wear inappropriate clothing when visiting religious sites
  • Take photos of the Miao people without permission
  • Litter or damage the natural environment

Safety and Health

Safety in Anshun

Anshun is a relatively safe destination for tourists. However, visitors should be aware of their surroundings and take necessary precautions against petty theft and scams.

Health in Anshun

Visitors should avoid drinking tap water and stick to bottled water to avoid illnesses. It is also recommended to bring mosquito repellent, especially during the summer months.

Getting to Anshun: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Anshun

The nearest airport to Anshun is the Anshun Huangguoshu Airport, which connects the city to other major cities in China.

Bus Stations Near Anshun

Anshun has two main bus stations: Anshun West Station and Anshun Bus Station. Visitors can take a long-distance bus to Anshun from major cities in China.

Other Options

Anshun can also be reached by train, with regular services from Guiyang and other nearby provinces.

How to Get Around in Anshun

The best way to get around Anshun is by taxi or public transportation. Buses and trains are readily available in the city, while local travel agencies offer organized tours to popular destinations.

Where to Go Next from Anshun: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some popular destinations near Anshun include:

  • Guiyang: The capital city of Guizhou province, known for its rich history and culture.
  • Kaili: A city in southeast Guizhou, known for its ethnic minority culture and festivals.
  • Huangshan: A mountain range in eastern China, known for its stunning scenery.

Conclusion

Anshun is a beautiful destination that offers visitors a unique cultural and natural experience. With its stunning landscapes, rich history, and vibrant culture, Anshun is a must-visit destination for any traveler to China.
