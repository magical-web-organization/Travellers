Introduction to Tonghai

Tonghai is a small county located in Southeastern Yunnan, in Southwest China. It is a part of the Yuxi prefecture and has a historical significance in the region. Surrounded by hills and terraced fields, Tonghai is a beautiful destination that highlights the cultural heritage of Yunnan as well as its unspoiled natural scenery.

List of Places to Visit in Tonghai

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Tonghai County Museum

The Tonghai County Museum is a place to visit for history buffs. It features historical records and artifacts from Tonghai County and the surrounding Yuxi region. Exhibitions cover different topics including local customs, architecture, and folk art. The museum is located near the Nancheng Gate of Tonghai ancient city.

Nancheng Gate and City Wall

The Nancheng Gate and City Wall are a prime example of the ancient architecture found in Yunnan. The city gate and walls were built during the Ming Dynasty and protected the town from invaders. Visitors can walk on the walls and explore the towers, which offer a great vantage point of the city.

Guyun Pagoda

The Guyun Pagoda is a ten-story, octagonal tower made of brick and wood. The pagoda was first built during the Tang Dynasty and has since been reconstructed in the Ming Dynasty. The tower features intricate carvings and is considered a masterpiece of the Yuan Dynasty architecture.

Natural Attractions of Tonghai: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lijiashan Terraced Fields

The Lijiashan Terraced Fields are located in the southeast of Tonghai County. The grand terraces snake up the slopes and bend around the hillsides, which creates a beautiful display of Yuxi rural scenery. During harvest season, when the fields turn to gold, it is even more breathtaking.

Qilu Lake Scenic Area

Qilu Lake is a freshwater lake in Tonghai County featuring clean waters surrounded by scenic mountain beauty. You can take a boat out on the lake for a peaceful ride or explore the hiking trails in the area. Moreover, there is a folk park nearby that celebrates the local ethnic minority culture.

Tonghai Red Land

The Tonghai Red Land is a natural scenic area that creates a stunning view. This area covers over 4,000 hectares and is famous for its unique red earth terrain, which contrasts with the surrounding greenery. It is best to visit in the late afternoon when the sunset highlights the distinctive colors of the landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tonghai

Yunlong Mountain National Forest Park

The Yunlong Mountain National Forest Park is a popular hiking spot around Tonghai County. It offers an opportunity to explore the natural beauty of Yunnan, with many types of natural scenery along the mountain trails. The park also has other outdoor activities like camping, boating, and rock climbing.

Sanjie Village Hiking Trail

The Sanjie Village Hiking Trail is located in the southern part of Tonghai County, and offers stunning views of the Lijiashan Terraced Fields. Hiking along this trail, you’ll pass through many vibrant villages nestled in the mountains of Yunnan. The entire path is about 13 km long, ending at the Liuzi Terraced Fields, a sight to behold on its own.

Best Things to Do in Tonghai: Activities and Tours

Visit Local Markets

Exploring local markets in Yunnan is a must-do when visiting Tonghai, as it is an excellent opportunity to experience Yunnan culture. Mei Shan Market is a popular market and is located near the ancient city of Tonghai. It provides fresh produce, street food, and handicrafts that you can take home as souvenirs.

Take a Hot Air Balloon Ride

To have a bird’s eye view of Tonghai and its beautiful landscapes, taking a hot air balloon ride is something to consider for an unforgettable experience. The ride lasts around 30 minutes and can begin at any time of the day, depending on what view you prefer.

Food and Drink Scene of Tonghai

Must-Try Dishes

Tuanshanzi

Tuanshanzi is rice cakes made with rice flour and are a Yuxi’s speciality. Due to the abundance of rice fields, rice dishes are common here. The cakes can be steamed, fried or boiled and are often served as a dessert or snack.

Steamed Vegetables with Mushrooms

As a healthy option, steamed vegetables with mushrooms is a popular dish in Tonghai county. It features fresh mushrooms and vegetables that have been steamed to perfection, retaining their natural flavors and nutrients.

Local Beverages

Puer Tea

Also known as Puerh, Puer tea is a fermented tea that has become a global sensation. Yunnan is one of the few regions in the world with the right climate to grow Puer tea. So, it’s a must-try drink when visiting Tonghai.

Rice Wine

Rice wine is a traditional alcoholic drink in China made from fermented rice. Many locals brew rice wine in their homes, which is why it’s easy to find in local markets. Drink it with meals or as a treat after a long day.

Where to Stay in Tonghai: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Yida Resort

Yida Resort is located on the bank of Qilu Lake and offers scenic views of the natural surroundings. It’s a resort with a wide range of room types, from standard to deluxe rooms. The resort also has an outdoor pool, restaurant, and spa facilities, providing a relaxing stay in Tonghai.

Tonghai Lvye Inn

Tonghai Lvye Inn is located in the ancient city of Tonghai and is a renovated traditional building. The atmosphere of the inn is cozy and tranquil, which makes it the perfect place to unwind after a long day exploring the local attractions. The inn offers standard and family rooms, and free Wi-Fi throughout the property.

What to Buy in Tonghai: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Handicrafts

Being close to the city of Yuxi, a place well-known for its handicrafts, the markets in Tonghai are an excellent source of souvenirs. You can find a wide range of items like embroidered clothing, bamboo crafts, pottery, and more.

Puer Tea

Puer tea is something that you should bring back from Tonghai. Local speciality shops sell high-quality Puer tea at affordable prices. It’s a must-try and a great gift for friends and family.

Local Culture and Customs of Tonghai

Tonghai County is part of the Hani ethnic minority in China. Their dancing, songs and lifestyle are part of the attraction. Visitors are encouraged to observe the customs and traditions during their stay in Tonghai as they offer an insight into the region’s rich cultural heritage.

Best Time to Visit Tonghai

The best time to visit Tonghai is from March to May and from September to November. This period enjoys moderate temperatures and the most pleasant weather. Additionally, the terraced fields are at their greenest, and the flowers are bloomy, making it an ideal time for taking photographs.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tonghai

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions
  • Carry your identification documents around.
  • Sample local cuisine.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs of people without their permission.
  • Touch artifacts on display in local museums
  • Litter or dispose of trash improperly.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tonghai

Tonghai is a secure destination for tourists. However, always take the necessary precautions like keeping your valuables secure and watching your belongings when out in crowded areas. Also, avoid drinking tap water, and stick to bottled water.

Health in Tonghai

Yunnan Province has a warm and humid climate, and thus visitors are likely to experience tropical diseases like Dengue, SARS, and Malaria. Always ensure getting a full medical check-up before departing for the trip to ensure a healthy stay.

Getting to Tonghai: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tonghai

There are no airports in Tonghai, so visitors can fly to Kunming Changshui International Airport, which is 85km away. From Kunming, travelers can take a bus or drive to Tonghai.

Bus Stations Near Tonghai

There are regular buses to Tonghai from Kunming Eastern Bus Station and Yuxi Bus Station. The journey takes around three hours, and there is a bus every hour from 7 am to 5 pm every day.

Other Options

Visitors can take a taxi from Kunming to Tonghai, which takes approximately two hours.

How to Get Around in Tonghai

Visitors can explore Tonghai’s various attractions on foot, by bicycle, or car. Alternatively, there are electric scooters for rent which can be more convenient for those wanting to do a bit more exploring.

Where to Go Next from Tonghai: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Yuxi City

Yuxi is a popular destination in Yunnan province due to its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. This city has many interesting places to visit like Xiushan Park and Ancient Alu Cave.

Yuanjiang County

Yuanjiang County is located in the southern part of Yunnan province, and is known for its beautiful terraced fields and ethnic minority culture. Mountain hiking trails and hot springs are the highlights of a visit to the area.

Conclusion

Tonghai may be small, but it packs a punch when it comes to cultural heritage, natural beauty, and outdoor adventures. The attractions in Tonghai provide a glimpse into Yunnan’s unique culture, geography, and traditions. Whether you are a history buff, photographer, nature lover or a foodie, there’s something for everyone to enjoy in Tonghai.
