Introduction to Xichang

Located in South Sichuan, Xichang is a picturesque city in the southwest province of China. With a population of over 400,000 people, the city is known for its beautiful natural scenery and rich cultural heritage. Xichang is surrounded by mountains and forests, making it a popular destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The city is also famous for its stunning temples, museums, and historical sites that offer an insight into the city’s unique culture and history.

List of Places to Visit in Xichang

Xichang has a lot to offer for visitors. Here is a list of some of the most popular places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Xichang Satellite Launch Center: This is one of the most important space launch facilities in China, and a must-visit for space enthusiasts.
  • Xichang Qionghai Lake: This is a breathtaking freshwater lake surrounded by lush greenery and stunning scenery. Visitors can take boat rides, hike around the lake, or simply relax and take in the beauty of the surroundings.
  • Xichang Cultural Center: This museum has an impressive collection of artifacts and exhibits that showcase the region’s rich cultural heritage.
  • Nanting Temple: This ancient temple was built during the Tang Dynasty and is one of the oldest temples in the area. It is known for its beautiful architecture and serene setting.

Natural Attractions of Xichang: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ma’an Mountain: This stunning mountain range offers some of the most scenic views in the area. Visitors can hike up to the peak or take a cable car ride to enjoy panoramic views of the surroundings.
  • Liujiang River Scenic Area: This beautiful river is surrounded by lush greenery and stunning scenery. Visitors can take boat rides, hike along the river, or simply relax and take in the beauty of the surroundings.
  • Lugu Lake: Known as the “Pearl of the Plateau”, Lugu Lake is a stunning freshwater lake surrounded by mountains and forests. Visitors can take boat rides, hike around the lake, or simply relax and take in the beauty of the surroundings.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Xichang

Xichang has many beautiful hiking and biking trails that are perfect for outdoor enthusiasts. Some popular routes include:

  • Ma’an Mountain Hiking Trail: This 3km-long trail takes you up to the peak of Ma’an Mountain and offers stunning views of the surroundings.
  • Tianma Mountain Hiking Trail: This 6km-long trail takes you up to the peak of Tianma Mountain and offers breathtaking views of the city.
  • Xichang City Biking Trail: This 8km-long trail takes you on a scenic tour of the city’s most beautiful sights.

Best Things to Do in Xichang: Activities and Tours

Xichang has a lot to offer in terms of activities and tours. Here are some of the most popular things to do:

  • Take a Hot Air Balloon Ride: This is a unique and unforgettable way to see the stunning scenery of Xichang from above.
  • Visit the Local Markets: Xichang has many bustling markets where visitors can buy local produce, traditional crafts, and souvenirs.
  • Attend a Traditional Festival: Xichang is famous for its vibrant festivals, including the Torch Festival, the Water Splashing Festival, and the Lantern Festival.

Food and Drink Scene of Xichang

Xichang has a unique and delicious cuisine that visitors should try. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Xichang Roast Duck: This is a local twist on the famous Beijing dish and is a must-try for foodies.
  • Xichang Spicy Chicken: This tasty and spicy dish is made with local spices and chicken.
  • Luguhu Smoked Meat: This is a local specialty made by smoking meat over a wood-fired stove.

Local Beverages

  • Xichang Beer: This is a local beer brewed using traditional methods, and is a must-try for beer drinkers.
  • Butter Tea: This is a popular Tibetan beverage made with tea, butter, and salt.

Where to Stay in Xichang: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Xichang has a variety of accommodation options to suit different travel budgets. Some popular options include:

  • Hanting Express Xichang Government Center: This is a budget-friendly hotel located in the heart of the city.
  • InterContinental Xichang Resort: This is a luxury resort located near the beautiful Qionghai Lake.
  • Xichang Youth Hostel: This is a popular hostel located in the city center and is perfect for budget-conscious travelers.

What to Buy in Xichang: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Xichang is known for its rich cultural heritage and has many unique souvenirs and local products that visitors can bring home. Some popular choices include:

  • Tibetan-style Jewelry: Xichang has many beautiful and intricate pieces of jewelry that are inspired by traditional Tibetan designs.
  • Local Tea: Xichang is famous for its high-quality tea, and visitors can buy different varieties of tea to take home.
  • Handicrafts and Artwork: Xichang has many talented artists and craftsmen, and visitors can buy unique and beautiful pieces of artwork and handicrafts as souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Xichang

Xichang has a unique and vibrant culture that is influenced by its location on the edge of the Tibetan Plateau. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, especially when visiting temples and other cultural attractions.

Best Time to Visit Xichang

The best time to visit Xichang is between April and October when the weather is mild and pleasant. The summer months are the busiest and most crowded, so visitors should plan accordingly.

Do’s and Don’ts of Xichang

Do:

  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages
  • Visit the city’s museums and historical sites

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local customs and traditions
  • Cause damage to the natural environment
  • Photograph people or religious sites without permission

Safety and Health

Safety in Xichang

Xichang is a safe city for tourists, but visitors should take the usual precautions to protect their belongings and avoid becoming the target of pickpockets.

Health in Xichang

Visitors should take precautions to protect themselves from altitude sickness as the city is located at high altitude. Visitors should also stay hydrated and wear protective clothing and sunscreen when hiking or spending time outdoors.

Getting to Xichang: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Xichang

  • Xichang Qingshan Airport

Bus Stations Near Xichang

  • Xichang Long-Distance Bus Station
  • Xichang South Bus Station

Other Options

  • Train: There is no direct train to Xichang. The nearest train station is in Chengdu, which is approximately 400km away.

How to Get Around in Xichang

Xichang has a well-developed public transportation system that includes buses and taxis. Visitors can also rent bicycles or electric scooters to explore the city on their own.

Where to Go Next from Xichang: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Lijiang: This ancient city is located in Yunnan Province and offers stunning scenery, history, and culture.
  • Chengdu: This bustling city is known for its spicy food, friendly people, and vibrant culture.

Conclusion

Xichang is a beautiful and unique city that offers a blend of natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Visitors can explore the city’s stunning mountains and forests, try the local cuisine, and immerse themselves in the local culture and traditions. With so much to see and do, Xichang is a must-visit destination for any traveler to China.
