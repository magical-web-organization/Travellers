Introduction to Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve is a natural paradise located in the Aba Prefecture of Sichuan Province, in Southwest China. It is known for its stunning natural scenery, crystal-clear lakes, beautiful waterfalls, and colorful forests. The park covers an area of 720 square kilometers and is listed as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

List of Places to Visit in Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve has a lot to offer in terms of both natural and cultural attractions. Here’s a list of some of the most popular places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Zharu Tibetan Village: A quaint and picturesque Tibetan village that provides a glimpse into the local culture and customs.
  • Yinyuan Temple: A beautiful temple nestled in a valley, that has been preserved since the Ming Dynasty.
  • Tibetan Cultural Museum: A museum that showcases Tibetan culture and history, through exhibits and artifacts.

Natural Attractions of Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Nuorilang Waterfall: A breathtaking waterfall that cascades down from 24 meters.
  • Five-Color Pond: A spectacular pond that changes color throughout the day.
  • Long Lake: A serene lake surrounded by mountains, offering a peaceful getaway from the busy world.
  • Mirror Lake: A calm and tranquil lake, which provides a stunning reflection of the natural surroundings.
  • Shuzheng Valley: A valley that features a series of beautiful lakes, waterfalls, and forests.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve offers several hiking and biking routes for visitors to explore, the most popular of which include:

  • Shuzheng Valley: A relatively easy hiking trail, offering stunning views of the lakes and forests.
  • Zharu Valley: A medium-level hiking route that leads to the beautiful Zharu Tibetan Village, and through pristine forests.
  • Pearl Shoal Waterfall: A one-kilometer flat walkway leading to a stunning waterfall.

Best Things to Do in Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve: Activities and Tours

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve offers a range of activities and tours to suit different preferences and interests. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Trekking: Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve offers several trekking routes that offer scenic views of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Horse Riding: Enjoy a unique and unforgettable experience of riding a horse through the beautiful natural trails.
  • Photography Tour: If you are a photography lover, Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve provides ample opportunities to capture stunning landscapes, waterfalls, and forests.

Week-Long Itinerary for Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

If you have a week to spare, here’s a suggested itinerary to help you make the most of your time in Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve:

Day 1: Visit Yinyuan Temple and explore Shuzheng Valley.

Day 2: Take a day trip to Zharu Tibetan Village and explore its surroundings.

Day 3: Trek to Long Lake and enjoy a picnic by its serene waters.

Day 4: Enjoy a horseback riding experience through the dense forests.

Day 5: Explore the Pearl Shoal Waterfall and take a dip in its refreshing waters.

Day 6: Visit the Five-Color Pond and hike to the Nuorilang Waterfall.

Day 7: Take a leisurely stroll around Mirror Lake and bid Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve goodbye.

Food and Drink Scene of Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve offers a rich and diverse food and drink scene, which is heavily influenced by Tibetan cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages while in Jiuzhaigou:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Yak Butter Tea: A traditional Tibetan drink, made from yak butter, tea, and salt.
  • Thukpa: A hearty Tibetan noodle soup, loaded with vegetables, meat and spices.
  • Yak Meat: A lean and flavorful meat, served in various preparations such as grilled, roasted, or stewed.

Local Beverages

  • Chang: A locally brewed beer, made from barley and wheat.
  • Juema: A potent Tibetan liquor, made from fermented horse milk.

Where to Stay in Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve has several accommodation options to suit different preferences and budgets. Here are some of the best hotels, hostels, and guesthouses:

  • Sheraton Jiuzhaigou Resort: A luxurious 5-star hotel, surrounded by stunning natural scenery.
  • Jiuzhaigou Lakeside Inn: A comfortable guesthouse, situated close to the entrance of the park.
  • Nine Valleys Inn: A budget-friendly hostel, located in the heart of the park.

What to Buy in Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve offers several locally made souvenirs and products, which make for great gifts to bring back home. Here are some of the best buys:

  • Tibetan Carpets: Handmade carpets, woven with colorful designs and patterns.
  • Yak Bone Jewelry: Unique and beautiful jewelry, made from ethically sourced yak bones.
  • Tibetan Incense: Fragrant incense, used for spiritual and medicinal purposes.

Local Culture and Customs of Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve has a rich and vibrant local culture, heavily influenced by Tibetan traditions and beliefs. Here are some customs and practices that you may come across:

  • Prayer Flags: Colorful flags, inscribed with mantras and prayers, commonly seen in Tibetan areas.
  • Yak Caravans: Nomadic tribes can be seen transporting goods and supplies through herds of yaks.
  • Local Festivals: Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve hosts several festivals throughout the year, where you can enjoy traditional dances, music and cuisine.

Best Time to Visit Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

The best time to visit Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve is from March to November, when the climate is mild, and the skies are clear. However, the peak season falls between July and August, so plan your visit accordingly.

Do’s and Don’ts of Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Dress appropriately, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Take care of the environment by not littering or damaging the natural resources.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local beliefs or customs.
  • Disturb the wildlife, or feed them with human-made food.
  • Smoke, eat or drink in restricted areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

  • Always carry bottled water and snacks while hiking or trekking.
  • Wear sturdy shoes, and carry a first-aid kit.
  • Be cautious of slippery and uneven paths.

Health in Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

  • Be aware of the symptoms of altitude sickness, especially if you are trekking in high altitudes.
  • Carry necessary medications and consult a doctor beforehand if you have any pre-existing medical conditions.

Getting to Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve: Transportation Options and Logistics

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve is accessible by both air and land. However, there is no direct train service to the reserve. Here are some transportation options to consider:

Airports Near Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

  • Jiuzhai Huanglong Airport: The nearest airport, located around 50 km away from the reserve.

Bus Stations Near Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

  • Chuanzhusi Bus Station: The main bus station, located around 22 km away from the reserve.

Other Options

  • Private Car: Taxis and private cars are readily available in most cities, and it can be an easy and convenient option.

How to Get Around in Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve offers several transportation options to explore the park, including shuttle buses, taxis, and private cars. However, the shuttle buses are the most recommended, as they are eco-friendly and provide easy access to all parts of the park.

Where to Go Next from Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have more time, here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Huanglong National Scenic Reserve: Another stunning natural reserve, located around 140 km away from Jiuzhaigou.
  • Chengdu: The provincial capital of Sichuan, known for its spicy cuisine, and the Giant Panda Research Base.

Conclusion

Jiuzhaigou Nature Reserve is a must-visit destination in China, offering an unforgettable natural and cultural experience. Use this travel guide to plan your trip and make the most of your visit to this beautiful place.
