Introduction to Shunan Zhuhai National Park

Shunan Zhuhai National Park is one of the most stunning national parks in East Sichuan, China. It covers an area of 117 square kilometers, with a vast range of landscapes from forests, gorges, lakes, waterfalls, and hot springs. Visitors will be amazed by the scenic views and immersive cultural experiences in the park. Whether traveling solo, as a couple or a family, there are tons of exciting activities to participate in, as well as cultural and natural attractions to explore.

List of Places to Visit in Shunan Zhuhai National Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you are interested in immersing yourself in the local culture, history, and customs of East Sichuan, then there are several culturally important attractions to visit in Shunan Zhuhai National Park.

  • Shunan Bamboo Sea Museum: The Shunan Bamboo Sea Museum is a perfect place to learn about the history of bamboo cultivation, the innovations of the bamboo industry, and bamboo art. Visitors can also admire bamboo handicrafts and participate in traditional bamboo-handling activities.
  • Shuijing Ancient Town: Located on the east side of the park, the Shuijing Ancient Town has a history of over 1,600 years. Visitors can explore ancient alleys, bridges, and waterways while enjoying local street food, such as grilled meat skewers, bean curd, and hot pot.
  • Luzhou Gongjing Palace: Luzhou Gongjing Palace, also known as Gongjiang Temple, is a renowned Taoist temple built in the Tang Dynasty. With its elaborate architecture and beautiful surroundings, it is a must-visit destination if you want to experience Taoist culture.

Natural Attractions of Shunan Zhuhai National Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you are a nature lover, Shunan Zhuhai National Park is a perfect destination for you. The park boasts of several natural attractions, including:

  • Shunan Zhuhai Geopark: Shunan Zhuhai Geopark is an excellent place to explore the geological wonders of the park. Visitors can see peculiar rock formations, caves, and dinosaur fossils.

  • Wawu Mountain: Wawu Mountain is the highest peak in Shunan Zhuhai National Park, with an altitude of 3,240 meters. Hiking enthusiasts will be glad to know that there are several trails available. Visitors can see a range of unique plants and animals on their way up.

  • Nine-Dragon Heaven Waterfall: This waterfall gets its name from the nine separate cascades of water. Visitors can access the waterfall through hiking or on a raft. Besides, visitors can also try some of the local snacks while resting.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Shunan Zhuhai National Park

The park has numerous hiking and biking trails that lead to some of the park’s most exciting attractions. Some of the hiking and biking routes include:

  • Changqing Trail: The Changqing Trail, also known as the Bamboo Sea Trail, is a four-hour hike that showcases the park’s vast bamboo forests.

  • Wawu Mountain Trail: The Wawu Mountain Trail takes visitors across the highest peak in the park, with breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding scenery.

  • Guijia Trail: The Guijia Trail connects Shunanzhuhai and Shuangguishan villages. It provides an opportunity to explore some of the local farming villages and appreciate their unique lifestyle.

Best Things to Do in Shunan Zhuhai National Park: Activities and Tours

Shunan Zhuhai National Park is filled with fun activities that visitors can embark on. Some of the top activities and tours include:

  • Bamboo Rafting: Bamboo rafting is an excellent way to explore some of the waterfalls, streams and gorges in the park.

  • Bamboo Weaving: Visitors can learn how to make different bamboo handicrafts like baskets, wallets, and mats from the indigenous residents, who have mastered the art of weaving bamboo.

  • Hot Spring Bathing: Shunan Zhuhai National Park has several natural hot springs, which are perfect for visitors wanting to relax and unwind while enjoying the natural scenery of the park.

Week-Long Itinerary for Shunan Zhuhai National Park

If you are planning to explore the entire park, it is recommended that you spend at least a week to explore all of the attractions and participate in several of the park’s exciting activities. Below is a sample itinerary:

Day Activity
1 Arrive, check-in, and rest
2 Visit Luzhou Gongjing Palace and Bamboo Sea Museum
3 Hike the Wawu Mountain Trail
4 Explore the Shuijing Ancient Town
5 Try bamboo weaving and hot spring bathing
6 Visit Nine-Dragon Heaven Waterfall and bamboo rafting
7 Departure

Food and Drink Scene of Shunan Zhuhai National Park

Must-Try Dishes

The food in Shunan Zhuhai National Park is second to none. The park boasts of several regional dishes that are a must-try. Here are some popular local dishes that you should try:

  • Bamboo Rice: Bamboo rice is rice cooked in bamboo tubes with mushrooms, minced meat, and several spices. It has a unique aroma and taste that is well worth a try.

  • Bamboo Shoot Soup: Bamboo shoot soup is a popular Shunan dish that is made with fresh bamboo shoots, sliced pork, and various seasoning. It is refreshing and healthy.

  • Stir-Fried Potato: Stir-fried potato is a unique dish of Shunan, where potatoes are stir-fried with garlic, green pepper, and other vegetables. It is delicious and straightforward.

Local Beverages

  • Baijiu: Baijiu is a traditional Chinese liquor made from grains like sorghum, wheat, barley, and maize. It is recommended to taste baijiu as it is a popular alcoholic beverage in East Sichuan.

  • Herbal Tea: Herbal tea is a popular beverage in Shunan Zhuhai National Park, where it is made with various herbs, roots, flowers, and fruits.

Where to Stay in Shunan Zhuhai National Park: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several options for accommodation in and around Shunan Zhuhai National Park. Visitors can choose from hotels, hostels, and guesthouses, depending on their preferences and budgets. Some recommended places to stay are:

  • Wawu Mountain Villa: The Wawu Mountain Villa is a luxurious hotel situated on the banks of the Qingyi River. It offers a mesmerizing view of the Wawu Mountain.

  • Shunan Impression Theme Hotel: The Shunan Impression Theme Hotel is a unique hotel that reflects the local culture and heritage. The hotel features bamboo crafted decorations and furniture.

  • Shunan Bamboo Sea Youth Hostel: Shunan Bamboo Sea Youth Hostel is an affordable option for backpackers that want to explore the park. It has clean rooms, a shared kitchen, and laundry facilities.

What to Buy in Shunan Zhuhai National Park: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

There are several souvenirs and local products that you can bring back home from Shunan Zhuhai National Park, including:

  • Bamboo Handicrafts: Visitors can purchase bamboo-made handicrafts like baskets, hats, mats, and wallets. They make for excellent and eco-friendly souvenirs.

  • Shunan Tea: Shunan is famous for its tea production. Visitors can purchase various tea types like green tea, black tea, and oolong tea.

Local Culture and Customs of Shunan Zhuhai National Park

Shunan Zhuhai National Park is home to several ethnic groups, including the Zhuang, Miao, and Yi. Visitors can learn about their different cultures, customs, and traditional practices through interacting with them. It is recommended to respect their customs, beliefs, and practices throughout your stay.

Best Time to Visit Shunan Zhuhai National Park

The best time to visit Shunan Zhuhai National Park is from April to October, as the weather is mild and pleasant during this time. Visitors should avoid visiting during the winter months when it can get too cold.

Do’s and Don’ts of Shunan Zhuhai National Park

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and traditions.

  • Carry a first-aid kit when hiking.

  • Bring enough provisions and water when exploring the park.

Don’t:

  • Litter or pollute the environment.

  • Make loud noises that may disrupt the wildlife or local residents.

  • Cause any damage to the park’s facilities or infrastructure.

Safety and Health

Safety in Shunan Zhuhai National Park

Shunan Zhuhai National Park is generally safe for visitors. However, visitors should take necessary precautions while hiking or exploring the park. Visitors should avoid wandering off marked trails without company, as it might be challenging to find a way back.

Health in Shunan Zhuhai National Park

Visitors are advised to carry basic medication and medical equipment while exploring the park. Visitors should also stay hydrated and stick to healthy diets throughout their stay.

Getting to Shunan Zhuhai National Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Shunan Zhuhai National Park

  • Chengdu Shuangliu International Airport: Chengdu Shuangliu International Airport is the nearest international airport, about 150 kilometers from Shunan Zhuhai National Park. Visitors can find several domestic and international flights from this airport.

Bus Stations Near Shunan Zhuhai National Park

  • Yibin Bus Terminal: Yibin Bus Terminal is the nearest major terminal, about one hour’s drive from the park.

  • Luzhou Bus Terminal: Luzhou Bus Terminal is another bus terminal that is approximately two hours’ drive from the park.

Other Options

  • Visitors can also hire taxis or private transport from Chengdu or Yibin.

  • Visitors can also take a train to Yibin from Chengdu, then take a bus for the last leg.

How to Get Around in Shunan Zhuhai National Park

There are several ways to get around Shunan Zhuhai National Park:

  • Walking: Visitors can walk on marked trails to explore the park.

  • Biking: Visitors can rent a bike to explore the park, especially for shorter distances.

  • Taxis: Visitors can hire taxis to move around different attractions in the park.

Where to Go Next from Shunan Zhuhai National Park: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can also explore other nearby destinations after visiting Shunan Zhuhai National Park. Some recommended places to visit are:

  • Yibin City: Yibin City is a major city near the park that offers an immersive cultural experience, food tour, and modern amenities.

  • Leshan Giant Buddha: Leshan Giant Buddha is a UNESCO World Heritage site located just three hours’ drive from the park. It is the largest Buddha statue in the world.

Conclusion

Shunan Zhuhai National Park is an excellent destination for anyone looking to immerse themselves in local culture, enjoy picturesque scenery, and engage in adventurous activities. With this comprehensive travel guide, visitors can plan an exciting trip and get the most out of their experience in the park.
