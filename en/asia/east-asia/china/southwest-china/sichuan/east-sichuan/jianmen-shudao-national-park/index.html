Introduction to Jianmen Shudao National Park

Jianmen Shudao National Park is a popular tourist destination in East Sichuan, Sichuan Province, Southwest China. The park is known for its stunning landscapes, rich history and culture, and numerous outdoor recreational activities. The park covers an area of 60 square kilometers and is surrounded by the majestic Yunding Mountain Range.

List of Places to Visit in Jianmen Shudao National Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Jianmen Pass: a well-preserved pass between Sichuan and Hubei provinces that has played an important role in Chinese military history.
  2. Jianmen Shudao Ancient Road: a historic and important trade route originally built during the Qin Dynasty (221 BC to 206 BC).
  3. Jianmen Sun Bin Art Gallery: a museum dedicated to the renowned ancient Chinese military strategist Sun Bin.
  4. Jianmen Confucian Temple: a temple that pays tribute to Confucius, a revered Chinese philosopher.

Natural Attractions of Jianmen Shudao National Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Yunding Mountain Range: a beautiful and expansive mountain range that provides breathtaking views of the Jianmen Shudao National Park.
  2. Tianchi Lake: a stunning high-altitude lake that is surrounded by mountains and forests.
  3. Jianmen Shudao Gorge: a long and narrow gorge that cuts through the Yunding Mountains.
  4. Black Dragon Pond: a small but impressive pond that is surrounded by lush greenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jianmen Shudao National Park

There are several hiking/biking routes in Jianmen Shudao National Park that offer exciting outdoor experiences, including:

  1. Yunding Mountain Trail: a 15-kilometer trail that takes hikers up to the summit of Yunding Mountain.
  2. Jianmen Shudao Ancient Trail: a historic trail that connects Jianmen Pass and the Shu Road, offering hikers and bikers a glimpse into the region’s rich history and culture.
  3. Tianchi Lake Trail: a 7-kilometer trail that offers scenic views of the lake and its surroundings.

Best Things to Do in Jianmen Shudao National Park: Activities and Tours

There are numerous activities and tours available in Jianmen Shudao National Park, including:

  1. Camping: Jianmen Shudao National Park offers designated campsites for tourists who want to enjoy the outdoors and sleep under the stars.
  2. Rafting: The park is home to some impressive rapids, and rafting opportunities are available along the Jianmen Shudao Gorge.
  3. Horseback Riding: Horseback riding tours are available for those who want to explore the park’s vast landscapes in a unique way.
  4. Cable Car Ride: Take a cable car ride to the top of Yunding Mountain to enjoy sweeping panoramic views of the park.

Week-Long Itinerary for Jianmen Shudao National Park

Day 1: Visit Jianmen Pass and the Jianmen Shudao Ancient Road. Day 2: Take a hike or bike ride on the Yunding Mountain Trail. Day 3: Explore the Tianchi Lake Trail and take a dip in the crystal-clear waters. Day 4: Go camping in one of the park’s designated campsites and enjoy a night under the stars. Day 5: Take a horseback riding tour. Day 6: Go rafting on the Jianmen Shudao Gorge. Day 7: Take a cable car ride to the top of Yunding Mountain for stunning panoramic views of the park.

Food and Drink Scene of Jianmen Shudao National Park

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Noodles with Spicy Sichuan Sauce: Sichuan cuisine is well-known for its spicy flavor, and this dish is no exception. Made with handmade noodles and a fiery Sichuan sauce, this dish is not for the faint of heart.
  2. Sichuan Hot Pot: A favorite among locals and visitors alike, Sichuan hot pot is a bubbling pot of spicy broth that comes with an array of meats, vegetables, and seafood for dipping.
  3. Mapo Tofu: A popular Sichuan dish made with tofu, ground pork, and a spicy bean sauce.
  4. Kung Pao Chicken: Another classic Sichuan dish, Kung Pao chicken is a stir-fry of chicken, peanuts, and veggies tossed in a spicy Sichuan sauce.

Local Beverages

  1. Wuliangye: A popular Chinese liquor with a strong aroma and potent flavor.
  2. Tea: Sichuan is famous for its tea, and there are several varieties available to try, including jasmine, yellow, and black tea.

Where to Stay in Jianmen Shudao National Park: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Jianmen Shudao National Park Hotel: A mid-range hotel located near the park entrance, offering comfortable rooms and easy access to the park’s attractions.
  2. Shu Road Inn: A charming guesthouse located along the Jianmen Shudao Ancient Road, offering unique and rustic accommodations.
  3. Jianmen Shudao Youth Hostel: A budget-friendly option located within the park, offering dormitory-style rooms and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

What to Buy in Jianmen Shudao National Park: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Jianmen Shudao Tea: A variety of tea from the Shu Road region, renowned for its high quality and unique flavor.
  2. Jianmen Shudao Handicrafts: Handmade crafts, such as embroidery, pottery, and woodcarvings, are available for purchase along the Jianmen Shudao Ancient Road.

Local Culture and Customs of Jianmen Shudao National Park

The region around Jianmen Shudao National Park is steeped in history and culture, and visitors are encouraged to learn about and respect the local customs and traditions. One important custom is to give a small gift or token of appreciation when visiting someone’s home, such as a small trinket or box of tea.

Best Time to Visit Jianmen Shudao National Park

The best time to visit Jianmen Shudao National Park is during the spring and autumn months, when the weather is mild and comfortable, and the landscapes are at their most beautiful.

Getting to Jianmen Shudao National Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Jianmen Shudao National Park

The nearest airport to Jianmen Shudao National Park is the Chengdu Shuangliu International Airport, which is approximately 200 kilometers away from the park.

Bus Stations Near Jianmen Shudao National Park

There are several major bus stations in the area that offer regular service to nearby towns and cities, including the Leshan Bus Station and the Emeishan Bus Station.

Other Options

Taxis and private car services are also available for hire, but will likely be more expensive than taking public transportation.

How to Get Around in Jianmen Shudao National Park

Walking and biking are the best options for getting around Jianmen Shudao National Park, but private cars and public transportation are available for those who prefer a more comfortable or convenient mode of travel.

Where to Go Next from Jianmen Shudao National Park: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Mount Emei: One of the Four Sacred Mountains of Buddhism, Mount Emei is a popular pilgrimage site and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  2. Leshan Giant Buddha: Located in Leshan city, the Leshan Giant Buddha is a 71-meter statue that was built during the Tang Dynasty.
  3. Chengdu: The capital of Sichuan Province, Chengdu is a bustling metropolis that is famous for its spicy cuisine, giant pandas, and vibrant nightlife.
