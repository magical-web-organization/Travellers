Introduction to Baoguo

Baoguo is a small town located in East Sichuan, Southwest China. The town is nestled in the foothills of the Daba Mountains and is known for its stunning scenery and cultural attractions. Baoguo has a rich history and culture dating back to ancient times, making it an ideal destination for history lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

List of Places to Visit in Baoguo

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Baoguo has a number of museums and historical places worth visiting. These include:

  • Baoguo Temple: Built in the Tang Dynasty (618-907), the Baoguo Temple is the oldest and most famous temple in Baoguo. It is home to a number of ancient buildings, sculptures, and artifacts.
  • Mazu Temple: This temple is dedicated to the goddess of the sea, Mazu. It was built in the Ming Dynasty (1368-1644) and is a popular pilgrimage site for locals and tourists alike.
  • Yangtze River Cave Temple: Located on the banks of the Yangtze River, this temple is known for its intricate carvings and stunning views of the river.

Natural Attractions of Baoguo: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Baoguo is surrounded by natural beauty, making it a popular destination for outdoor activities and scenic views. The following are some of the top attractions to visit:

  • Daba Mountain Scenic Area: The Daba Mountains offer a range of outdoor activities like hiking and biking, as well as stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
  • Yangtze River Cruise: Take a cruise along the Yangtze River to enjoy stunning views of the river and the surrounding mountains.
  • Fengyu Suspension Bridge: This 800-meter-long suspension bridge is the longest in Sichuan and offers stunning views of the Daba Mountains and the Yangtze River.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Baoguo

Baoguo is a hiking and biking paradise. The following are some of the most popular routes:

  • Daba Mountain Hiking Trail: This trail is one of the most popular in the area and offers stunning views of the Daba Mountains and the Yangtze River.
  • Baoguo to Fengyu Cycling Route: This route takes you through some of the most scenic parts of Baoguo and is perfect for cycling enthusiasts.

Best Things to Do in Baoguo: Activities and Tours

Baoguo has plenty of activities and tours for tourists. Here are some of the best:

  • Yangtze River Cruise: Take a leisurely cruise along the Yangtze River to enjoy stunning views of the river and the surrounding mountains.
  • Daba Mountain Hiking Tour: Join a guided hiking tour of the Daba Mountains to explore the natural beauty of the area.
  • Baoguo Temple Tour: Discover the rich history and culture of Baoguo with a tour of the Baoguo Temple.

Must-Try Dishes

Baoguo is known for its delicious local cuisine. Here are some dishes you must try:

  • Baoguo Dougan: This tofu dish is made using local soybeans and is a must-try for tourists.
  • Baoguo Erkuai: Erkuai is a type of rice cake that is popular in Sichuan. The Baoguo version is made with local ingredients and is a must-try.
  • Yangtze River Fish: Fresh fish from the Yangtze River is a popular dish in Baoguo and is often served whole with a variety of spices and sauces.
