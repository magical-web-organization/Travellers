Introduction to Xiling Xueshan National Park

Xiling Xueshan National Park is located in East Sichuan, in Southwest China. The park stretches across an area of 483 km², and is one of the most popular destinations in China. The park is known for its spectacular scenery, which includes snow-capped mountains, crystal-clear lakes, lush forest areas, and unique wildlife. Visitors to the park can explore the many natural and cultural attractions, take part in outdoor adventures, and enjoy the local food and drink scene.

List of Places to Visit in Xiling Xueshan National Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Yuntaishan Temple: This ancient temple dates back to the Tang Dynasty, and is a must-visit for those interested in Chinese history and culture. The temple is located on the mountain slopes, and offers panoramic views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Jiyang Landscape Terraces: This UNESCO World Heritage Site is known for its beautifully terraced rice fields. Visitors to the park can take a guided tour of the fields, and learn about the traditional farming techniques used by the local people.
  • Anren Ancient Town: Anren Ancient Town is home to many historical buildings, including the former residence of Liu Wencai, a famous warlord during the Qing Dynasty.

Natural Attractions of Xiling Xueshan National Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mt. Xiling: The park’s namesake mountain is located in the eastern section of the park, and is the highest peak in the region. Visitors can take a cable car to the summit, where they can enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
  • Huanglongxi Ancient Town: This ancient town features traditional architecture, ancient temples, and beautiful gardens. Visitors can explore the town’s narrow streets, and marvel at its picturesque beauty.
  • Shuzheng Gully: This scenic area is known for its crystal-clear waters, towering waterfalls, and lush vegetation. Visitors can take a leisurely walk along the gully, and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Xiling Xueshan National Park

  • Mt. Xiling Hike: The hike to the summit of Mt. Xiling is one of the most popular routes in the park. The trail is well-marked and takes about 4-5 hours to complete.
  • Shuzheng Gully Hike: This hike takes visitors through the park’s scenic Shuzheng Gully. The trail is relatively easy, and takes about 3-4 hours to complete.
  • Horse Riding Adventure: Visitors can take a horseback ride through the park’s mountainous terrain. The ride takes about 2-3 hours, and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Xiling Xueshan National Park: Activities and Tours

  • Sunrise and Sunset Tours: Visitors can take guided tours to some of the park’s best sunrise and sunset viewing spots. These spots offer breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
  • Hot Springs Experience: Visitors can relax and unwind at one of the park’s many hot springs. The hot springs are known for their therapeutic properties, and offer a great way to rejuvenate tired muscles.
  • Camping Adventure: Visitors can camp in the park’s designated campsites, and enjoy the beauty of the park after dark.

Food and Drink Scene of Xiling Xueshan National Park

Must-Try Dishes

  • Spicy Hotpot: This dish is a Sichuan specialty, and is known for its spicy and flavorful broth. The hotpot is typically served with a variety of meats and vegetables.
  • Mapo Tofu: This dish features spicy tofu in a flavorful sauce, and is a must-try for those who love Sichuan cuisine.
  • Dan Dan Noodles: These noodles feature a spicy broth, and are typically served with meat and vegetables.

Local Beverages

  • Sichuan Tea: This tea is known for its refreshing taste, and is a popular choice among locals and tourists alike.
  • Baijiu: This Chinese liquor is made from sorghum, and is a popular drink in Sichuan.

Where to Stay in Xiling Xueshan National Park: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Xiling Snow Mountain Hotel: This hotel is located in the heart of the park, and offers comfortable accommodations and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Yanyu Boutique Hotel: This luxurious hotel offers spacious rooms, world-class amenities, and personalized service.
  • Hometown Youth Hostel: This budget-friendly hostel is perfect for backpackers and budget travelers, and offers comfortable accommodations and a friendly atmosphere.

What to Buy in Xiling Xueshan National Park: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Kangding Tea: This tea is a local specialty, and is known for its refreshing taste and medicinal properties.
  • Carpets and Rugs: These handmade carpets and rugs are a popular souvenir among visitors to the park.
  • Chinese Calligraphy: Visitors can purchase traditional Chinese calligraphy artwork, which is a great way to bring home a piece of Chinese culture and history.

Local Culture and Customs of Xiling Xueshan National Park

The local people of Xiling Xueshan National Park are known for their hospitality and friendly nature. Visitors to the park should be respectful of local customs and traditions, such as removing shoes when entering homes or temples, and bowing as a sign of respect.

Best Time to Visit Xiling Xueshan National Park

The best time to visit Xiling Xueshan National Park is during the spring and autumn months, when the weather is mild and the scenery is at its most beautiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of Xiling Xueshan National Park

Do:

  • Pack appropriate clothing and gear: Xiling Xueshan National Park can be chilly, especially at higher elevations. Visitors should bring warm clothing and appropriate footwear.
  • Respect local customs and traditions: Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, such as removing shoes when entering homes or temples, and bowing as a sign of respect.
  • Book accommodations in advance: Xiling Xueshan National Park can be busy during peak season. Visitors should book accommodations in advance to avoid disappointment.

Don’t:

  • Litter or pollute: Visitors should take care not to litter or pollute the park’s pristine natural environment.
  • Disturb wildlife: Visitors should not disturb or harm the park’s unique wildlife.

Safety and Health

Safety in Xiling Xueshan National Park

Xiling Xueshan National Park is generally a safe destination for visitors. However, visitors should exercise caution when trekking or hiking, and should always follow the park’s safety guidelines.

Health in Xiling Xueshan National Park

Visitors to Xiling Xueshan National Park should ensure that they have the necessary vaccinations and medical insurance before traveling. Visitors should also be aware of the risk of altitude sickness, and should take appropriate precautions if they plan to undertake any high-altitude activities.

Getting to Xiling Xueshan National Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Visitors to Xiling Xueshan National Park can access the park by air or by road.

Airports Near Xiling Xueshan National Park

  • Chengdu Shuangliu International Airport: This is the closest major airport to Xiling Xueshan National Park. The airport is located about 130 km from the park.

Bus Stations Near Xiling Xueshan National Park

  • Chengdu Xinnanmen Bus Station: This bus station offers frequent services to Xiling Xueshan National Park.

Other Options

  • Car Rental: Visitors can rent a car and drive to Xiling Xueshan National Park. This option is ideal for those who want to explore the park at their own pace.

How to Get Around in Xiling Xueshan National Park

Visitors to Xiling Xueshan National Park can get around by hiking or trekking, by taking a cable car, or by using a local guide.

Where to Go Next from Xiling Xueshan National Park: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Chengdu: This vibrant city is located about 150 km from Xiling Xueshan National Park, and is known for its rich culture, history, and cuisine.
  • Jiuzhaigou Valley: This UNESCO World Heritage Site is known for its stunning natural scenery, and is located about 250 km from Xiling Xueshan National Park.

Conclusion

Xiling Xueshan National Park is a must-visit destination for those who love nature, history, and culture. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, or delicious food and drink, Xiling Xueshan National Park has something for everyone. With its beautiful scenery, friendly locals, and rich cultural heritage, Xiling Xueshan National Park is an unforgettable destination that should be on every traveler’s bucket list.
