Introduction to Jinggangshan

Jinggangshan is a scenic mountainous region located in Jiangxi province, South-central China. This place has an important historical significance in the revolutionary history of the country. It was here where the Chinese Communist Party, led by Mao Zedong, started a guerrilla war in the 1920s. The region is filled with lush greenery, serene lakes, and picturesque waterfalls making it a nature lover’s paradise. With a perfect blend of historical significance and natural beauty, Jinggangshan is a must-visit destination.

List of Places to Visit in Jinggangshan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The region of Jinggangshan is best known for its historical significance. There are many museums and historical places that take you back in time and tell the tales of the Communist Revolution days. Some of the top attractions include:

    1. Jinggang Revolutionary Museum
    1. Yan’an Revolutionary Memorial Hall
    1. Wen Tianxiang Memorial Hall
    1. Jinggangshan Martyrs Cemetery

Natural Attractions of Jinggangshan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Apart from historical significance the region is also known for its natural beauty. Jinggangshan is a popular destination for hiking, trekking, and outdoor activities. Here are some of the top places to visit:

    1. Ciping Lake
    1. Five Finger Peak
    1. Tiantang Peak
    1. Huangyang Mountain Scenic Area

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jinggangshan

Jinggangshan is a perfect place for adventure seekers. There are many hiking and biking routes which offer amazing scenic views of the region. Here are some of the popular routes:

    1. Jinggangsan Big Scenic Circuit
    1. Ciping-Jinggangshan Scenic Route
    1. Tiantang Peak Hiking Trail

Best Things to Do in Jinggangshan: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and visiting museums, there are many more things you can do in Jinggangshan. Here are some of the best experiences you can have:

    1. Attend a Red Army rally

Local Culture and Customs of Jinggangshan

Jinggangshan has a rich history and culture. It is important for tourists to respect local customs and traditions. Here are some things to keep in mind:

    1. Dress modestly when visiting temples and historical places.
    1. Show respect to elders and authorities.
    1. Avoid smoking and littering in public places.

Best Time to Visit Jinggangshan

The best time to visit Jinggangshan is from March to November when the weather is pleasant and there are clear blue skies. Avoid visiting during the summer months (June-August) as it can get quite hot and humid.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jinggangshan

Jinggangshan is a relatively safe place to visit. However, tourists should take necessary precautions such as not leaving their belongings unattended and following all safety rules while hiking or engaging in adventure sports.

Health in Jinggangshan

Tourists should take necessary precautions to avoid getting sick. It is important to stay hydrated and eat only at clean and reputable restaurants.

Getting to Jinggangshan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Jinggangshan

The nearest airport to Jinggangshan is Ganzhou Huangjin Airport, which is 100 km away.

Bus Stations Near Jinggangshan

There are two bus stations in Jinggangshan. One is located in Xingguo, and the other is in Ciping.

Other Options

Tourists can also take a train to nearby cities such as Ji’an or Ganzhou and then take a bus or taxi to Jinggangshan.

How to Get Around in Jinggangshan

The best way to explore Jinggangshan is by foot, hiking or biking. Taxis and buses are also available for tourists to get around.
