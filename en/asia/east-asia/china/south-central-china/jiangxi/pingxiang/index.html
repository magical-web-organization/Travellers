Introduction to Pingxiang

Pingxiang is a small but culturally rich city located in Jiangxi, South-central China. It is known for its stunning natural scenery, historical landmarks, and unique traditions. The city has a population of roughly 1.5 million people and is surrounded by lush mountains and scenic landscapes. It offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and ancient culture that attracts visitors from all over the world.

List of Places to Visit in Pingxiang

Pingxiang offers a wide range of attractions that are worth visiting. Here is a list of some of the must-visit places in Pingxiang.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Guoqing Temple: Built during the Tang Dynasty, Guoqing Temple is one of the most popular attractions in Pingxiang. It is a Buddhist temple that features stunning architecture, beautiful gardens, and intricate carvings.
  • Fanghui Garden: This is a traditional Chinese garden featuring a picturesque landscape, pavilions, and ponds. It is a great place to relax and enjoy the natural beauty of Pingxiang.
  • Pingxiang Revolutionary Memorial Hall: This museum commemorates the revolutionary struggles of the Chinese people and showcases the important historical events in Pingxiang.
  • Mingyue Mountain: This is a sacred mountain that offers breathtaking views of the surrounding scenery. It is a popular pilgrimage destination for Buddhists and Taoists.

Natural Attractions of Pingxiang: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lushan National Park: This is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that features stunning mountain scenery, waterfalls, and creeks. It is a great place to go hiking, camping, and sightseeing.
  • Hutian Ancient Village: Located in a picturesque valley, Hutian Ancient Village is a well-preserved ancient village that offers a glimpse into the traditional Chinese way of life.
  • Mount Longhu: This is a mountain range that is known for its sacred status in Chinese Taoist culture. It offers scenic views, a cable car ride, and a Taoist temple.
  • Xianshui Rock: This is a natural water rock formation that is known for its beauty and tranquility. It is a great place to relax and enjoy the stunning scenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pingxiang

Pingxiang offers a range of outdoor activities such as hiking and biking. Here are some of the best hiking/biking routes in Pingxiang:

  • Lushan National Park: This is a great place for hiking and offers stunning mountain scenery and waterfalls.

Best Things to Do in Pingxiang: Activities and Tours

There are a lot of things to do in Pingxiang, aside from visiting the attractions. Here are some of the best activities and tours:

  • Tea-picking Experience: Pingxiang is known for its tea production, and visitors can participate in a tea-picking experience to learn about the tea culture and enjoy the fresh tea.
  • Rafting on the Xiu River: Visitors can enjoy a relaxing boat ride on the Xiu River, which offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Chinese Calligraphy Class: Guests can participate in a calligraphy class to learn about the Chinese script and its artistic culture.

  • Fried bamboo shoots: A common vegetable dish in Pingxiang that has a crunchy texture and a unique flavor.

Local Beverages
