Introduction to Anshan

Anshan is a city located in Liaoning, in Northeast China. It’s a beautiful destination flanked by natural scenery, luxurious parks, and vibrant cultural attractions. The city is home to approximately 3.65 million people making it the third-largest city in Liaoning. The city is also one of China’s top steel-producing cities with a rich industrial legacy that stretches back to the 1900s.

Anshan’s climate varies from moderate to continental-type climates. Summers are hot and humid while winters are severe and chilly. Travelers are advised to pack clothes accordingly.

List of Places to Visit in Anshan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Anshan Museum

The Anshan Museum is one of the best places to visit in Anshan for history buffs. The museum showcases the city’s history and development from the Stone Age to modern times. The museum exhibits over 40,000 ancient relics, including pottery, jade, bronze wares, and old-world currencies items.

Panshan Scenic Area

Panshan Scenic Area is a beautiful landscape surrounding the Panshan Mountain Range, located in the eastern part of Anshan. It has beautiful natural scenery featuring a mountain, the Xiliao River, waterfalls, and stone formations. It’s a perfect place to spend a day exploring the outdoors and hiking in Anshan.

Quan Mountain National Forest Park

Quan Mountain National Forest Park is one of the largest forest parks located in Anshan. It’s a beautiful place filled with serene natural surroundings, rich flora and fauna, and breathtaking views of the Quan Mountain Range. The park has many outdoor activities like hiking, mountaineering, and camping.

Natural Attractions of Anshan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Qianshan National Park

Qianshan National Park is one of China’s top natural attractions located in Anshan. The park is famous for its amazing mountainous terrain, lush forests, clear lakes, and picturesque valleys. Qianshan National Park is also known for its unique architecture which includes pagodas, pavilions, and ancient temples. Visitors can enjoy many outdoor activities like hiking, rock climbing, and mountain biking.

Jade Buddha Palace

The Jade Buddha Palace is a beautiful temple located in the western part of Anshan. It’s the largest temple in Northeast China and is famous for housing the largest Jade Buddha statue in the world. It has a beautiful landscape with natural surroundings, elegant halls, and large terraces.

Shengli Park

Shengli Park is one of the oldest parks in Anshan and is famous for its beautiful natural scenery, ancient architecture, and cultural relics. It has several lakes, pavilions, and gardens within the park. The park is a perfect place to relax in the peaceful environment and enjoy the scenic views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Anshan

Anshan has many hiking and biking opportunities for outdoor enthusiasts. The following are some of the most popular hiking and biking routes that offer exceptional scenic beauty.

Dashiqiao Scenic Loop

Dashiqiao Scenic Loop is one of the most popular bike routes in Anshan. The route covers about 50 kilometers, circling the Dashiqiao area surrounding Anshan. The route is picturesque and features views of the mountains, lakes, and countryside.

Aden Forest Park

Aden Forest Park is an excellent location for trail running, biking, and hiking. The park has trails that run through the mountain forests covering more than 2000 acres. The routes pass many scenic locations like streams, waterfalls, and springs. It’s perfect for those who want to experience adventure in nature.

Best Things to Do in Anshan: Activities and Tours

Be a Farmer at Shuangtaizi Farm

Shuangtaizi Farm is a beautiful place located in Anshan, where travelers can experience the farmer’s life. Visitors can learn the farming skills, harvest their crops that include apples, grapes, berries, and vegetables. The farm also offers farm-to-table dining experiences.

Visit Local Markets

Visiting local markets is one of the best things to do in Anshan. The markets offer a chance to experience the local culture, meet the locals, and taste their authentic foods. The stores sell a vast array of traditional handicrafts, fresh fruits, vegetables, and meat.

Take a Boat Ride at Yangzijiang Lake

Visitors can explore Yangzijiang Lake by taking a boat ride. The lake is surrounded by natural forests, clear waterfalls, and majestic mountains. Travelers can experience the calming effect of this scenic beauty while boating on the lake.

Week-Long Itinerary for Anshan

Here is a sample itinerary for a week-long trip to Anshan

Day 1: Arrive in Anshan, check into your hotel, and explore the night markets. Day 2: Visit the Anshan Museum and Jade Buddha Palace. Day 3: Hike at Qianshan National Park. Day 4: Cycle the Dashiqiao Scenic Loop. Day 5: Explore Shuangtaizi Farm and be a farmer for a day. Day 6: Visit Panshan Scenic Area. Day 7: Take a boat ride at Yangzijiang Lake and visit local markets.

Food and Drink Scene of Anshan

Must-Try Dishes

Anshan has a rich culinary tradition that offers unique and delicious dishes. Some of the must-try dishes include:

  • Liuhe Pagoda Meat Roast
  • Rice Cake with Bean Paste
  • Fried Rice Noodles

Local Beverages

Anshan offers a variety of beverages, including wine, beer, and tea. The most popular local beverages are:

  • Snow Beer
  • Da Hong Pao Tea
  • Jin Wine

Where to Stay in Anshan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Anshan Pullman Hotel

Anshan Pullman Hotel is one of the best hotels in Anshan, located in the city center. It offers 331 spacious rooms, modern amenities, and an outdoor pool. The hotel has several dining options and a fitness center.

Green Tree Inn

Green Tree Inn is a budget-friendly option located in the commercial district of Anshan. The hotel offers comfortable rooms, free Wi-Fi, and a restaurant.

Family Apartment Hotel

Family Apartment Hotel is an ideal option for families or group travelers. It offers spacious fully furnished apartments, a restaurant, and a fitness center. The hotel is located near many attractions, including Shengli Park and Anshan Museum.

Local Culture and Customs of Anshan

Anshan has a unique culture and customs that visitors can experience. The locals are friendly and hospitable, and it’s always advisable to show respect for their culture.

Best Time to Visit Anshan

The best time to visit Anshan is from March to October. During these months, the weather is more moderate, and travelers can enjoy outdoor activities like hiking and cycling.

Do’s and Don’ts of Anshan

Do:

  • Show respect for the local culture and customs
  • Try the delicious local cuisine
  • Take part in local activities and tours

Don’t:

  • Speak loudly or disrespectfully in public places
  • Wear revealing or inappropriate clothing
  • Smoke in non-smoking areas

Safety and Health

Safety in Anshan

Anshan is a relatively safe city, and visitors should take the usual precautions to avoid petty crimes. It is always advisable to keep an eye on personal belongings.

Health in Anshan

Visitors are advised to ensure they are up-to-date on vaccinations before visiting Anshan. Tap water is not safe, and visitors should drink bottled water only.

Getting to Anshan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Anshan

The nearest airport is Shenyang Taoxian International Airport, which is about 70 kilometers from Anshan. From there, visitors can reach Anshan via taxi or bus.

Bus Stations Near Anshan

Anshan South Bus Station is the main bus station for long-distance buses. Visitors can reach Anshan from several major cities, including Beijing, Shanghai, and Shenyang.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Anshan by train. There are three train stations in Anshan, which have high-speed trains, regular trains, and bullet trains that travel to many cities in China.

How to Get Around in Anshan

Anshan has an extensive public transportation system that includes buses, taxis, and a metro system that is currently under construction. Visitors can get around the city easily using these modes of transportation.

Where to Go Next from Anshan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Shenyang

Shenyang is a city located approximately 70 kilometers away from Anshan. It’s the capital of Liaoning Province and offers several cultural and historical attractions like Mukden Palace and the Shenyang Imperial Palace.

Dalian

Dalian is a coastal city located approximately 450 kilometers from Anshan. It’s famous for its beaches, seafood, and modern architecture. Visitors can visit the Dalian Discovery Kingdom, the Bangchui Island, and enjoy its beaches.

Conclusion

Anshan is a beautiful and vibrant city that offers visitors an array of activities and attractions. From hiking and cycling to exploring historical sites and enjoying the local cuisine, Anshan is a great destination for travelers seeking adventure and culture in China.
