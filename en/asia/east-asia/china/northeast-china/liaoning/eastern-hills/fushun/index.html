Introduction to Fushun

Fushun is a hidden gem in Liaoning province in Northeast China. Abundant in natural, cultural, and historical attractions, it’s a great destination for travelers who want to explore off-the-beaten-path destinations in China. Fushun is surrounded by mountains and forests, and the Fushun National Forest Park offers plenty of opportunities for outdoor activities such as hiking, cycling, and camping. The city is also home to several museums and historical sites that showcase China’s rich and diverse history.

Best Time to Visit Fushun

The best time to visit Fushun is from April to October when the weather is mild and pleasant. Autumn, in particular, is a popular season for travelers as the foliage in the Fushun National Forest Park is spectacular.

Safety and Health

Safety in Fushun

Fushun is a safe city to visit, but travelers should exercise caution in crowded areas and tourist attractions. Keep your valuables safe, and be aware of pickpockets and petty theft.

Health in Fushun

Tap water in Fushun is not drinkable, so travelers should stick to bottled water. Mosquito-borne diseases such as dengue fever can be a problem during the summer months, so visitors are advised to bring insect repellent.
