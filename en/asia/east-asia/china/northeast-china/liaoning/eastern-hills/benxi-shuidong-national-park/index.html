Introduction to Benxi Shuidong National Park

Nestled in the eastern hills of Northeast China’s Liaoning province, Benxi Shuidong National Park is a natural wonderland. The park’s name “Shuidong” means “water cave” in Chinese, referring to the park’s extensive cave system and large underground lake. The park spans over an area of around 130 square kilometers, and its diverse landscape includes dense forests, roaring waterfalls, and steep cliffs. Benxi Shuidong is an excellent destination for outdoor enthusiasts, cultural explorers, and anyone looking for a unique travel experience in China.

List of Places to Visit in Benxi Shuidong National Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Benxi Shuidong Cave Museum: The museum exhibits the geological and cultural history of the cave system and the surrounding region.

Natural Attractions of Benxi Shuidong National Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Shuidong Waterfall: It is the most magnificent waterfall in Benxi Shuidong National Park, and visitors can take a boat ride underneath it or hike to an observation deck for a panoramic view.
  • Shuidong Caverns: Visitors can take a unique boat ride around the underground lake and learn about the stalactites and stalagmites in the cave system surrounding it.
  • Jianshan Scenic Area: It offers stunning views of the landscape with a range of recreational activities, including hiking, rock climbing, paragliding, and zip-lining.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Benxi Shuidong National Park

  • Jianshan Scenic Area Hiking Trail: It is a 4-kilometer trail that includes steep steps leading to the mountaintop with a fantastic view of the park.
  • Shuidong Waterfall Hiking Trail: It is a 6.3-kilometer hiking trail that takes visitors around the waterfall, providing a chance to get a closer look and enjoy the scenic surroundings.

Best Things to Do in Benxi Shuidong National Park: Activities and Tours

  • Shuidong Cave and Island Tour: Visitors can take a boat tour around the underground lake and the surrounding caves in Shuidong Cave and island.
  • Jianshan Scenic Area Adventure Tour: Visitors can experience a range of outdoor activities, including rock-climbing, zip-lining, and bungee-jumping.

Week-Long Itinerary for Benxi Shuidong National Park

Day Activity
1 Arrive in Shenyang, stay overnight
2 Take a bus to Benxi Shuidong National Park, visit Shuidong Cave Museum and Shuidong Caverns

5 Take a tour of Shuidong Cave and Cave Island

Food and Drink Scene of Benxi Shuidong National Park

Must-Try Dishes

  • Daokou Roast Meat: It is a regional dish consisting of marinated pork that is slowly roasted over a wood fire.

Local Beverages

  • Hongqi Erguotou: It is a type of Chinese white liquor distilled from sorghum and has a pleasant aroma and smooth taste.

Local Culture and Customs of Benxi Shuidong National Park

  • Visitors should wear comfortable clothing and shoes, especially when hiking or biking in the park.
  • Respect the local culture and customs and ask permission before photographing people and traditional ceremonies or rituals.

Best Time to Visit Benxi Shuidong National Park

The best time to visit Benxi Shuidong National Park is between April and October, where visitors can enjoy the pleasant weather and natural scenery.

Do’s and Don’ts of Benxi Shuidong National Park

Do:

  • Respect and follow the park rules and regulations
  • Take care of the park’s natural environment
  • Wear appropriate clothing and footwear for outdoor adventures
  • Carry your own trash with you and dispose of it responsibly

Don’t:

  • Damage any natural or historical structures
  • Pluck flowers or pick up stones
  • Smoke in prohibited areas or litter the park with cigarette butts
  • Bring any pets or other animals into the park

Safety and Health

Safety in Benxi Shuidong National Park

  • Visitors should be aware of their surroundings and avoid taking unnecessary risks when hiking, climbing or participating in other outdoor activities.
  • Ensure to follow the parks signage and trail markers and wear suitable clothing when engaging in an activity in the park.

Health in Benxi Shuidong National Park

  • Bring along any necessary medication and enough drinking water.
  • Use sunscreen and insect repellent lotion to protect your skin.

Getting to Benxi Shuidong National Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Benxi Shuidong National Park

  • Shenyang Taoxian International Airport: It is the nearest airport located 90 km from the park.

Bus Stations Near Benxi Shuidong National Park

  • Benxi Bus Station: It is the nearest bus station to the park located 10 km from the park entrance.
  • Shenyang North Bus Station: It operates direct buses to Benxi and other locations in Liaoning province.

Other Options

  • Taxis, private cars or bikes can be rented for the 90-kilometer drive from Shenyang city.

How to Get Around in Benxi Shuidong National Park

  • Visitors can navigate through the park on foot, bicycle, or take a park shuttle bus.

Where to Go Next from Benxi Shuidong National Park: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Shenyang: It is the nearest major city located 90 km from the park and known as the “ancient capital of Manchuria” for its rich historical legacy.
  • Dandong City: It is located 170 km from the park and known for the famous Hushan Great Wall.

Conclusion

The Benxi Shuidong National Park offers travelers with a unique travel experience with an amazing natural wonder world. The park’s natural scenery and significant cultural attractions provide a dazzling array of value for visitors to enjoy.
