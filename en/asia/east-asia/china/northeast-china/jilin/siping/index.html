Introduction to Siping

Located in the northeast region of China, Siping is a hidden gem that is slowly gaining popularity among tourists. Offering a unique blend of history, culture, natural beauty, and adventure, Siping is the perfect destination for travelers looking for an off-the-beaten-path experience.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you through the best things to see and do in Siping, where to stay, and what to eat. So pack your bags and get ready to explore this charming city!

List of Places to Visit in Siping

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Siping is rich in history and culture, showcasing China’s diverse heritage through its museums and historical attractions.

Siping Museum

The Siping Museum is a great place to start your historical tour of the city. This museum showcases the history, culture, and development of Siping, including the region’s contributions to China’s revolution. You will also find exhibits on folk customs, natural wonders, local art, and more.

Sanhe Ancient City

Step back in time and visit the Sanhe Ancient City, which dates back to the Ming Dynasty. This ancient city is now a popular cultural attraction that features stunning architecture, including two towers and a gate.

Five Drum Mountain

Another historical site that is worth visiting is Five Drum Mountain. This mountain has been an important site for religious and cultural activities for over 1,000 years. Today, visitors can explore the ancient temples and shrines and enjoy the hiking trails.

Natural Attractions of Siping: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Siping offers stunning natural scenery, including rugged landscapes, clear lakes, and dense forests. You can explore these natural wonders through various outdoor activities and scenic spots.

Jingbo Lake Scenic Area

One of the most scenic spots in Siping is Jingbo Lake, which is known for its serene blue waters and tranquil setting. Visitors can explore the lake by boat or hike along the surrounding trails.

Phoenix Mountain Forest Park

For nature lovers, the Phoenix Mountain Forest Park is a must-visit. This park offers hiking trails for all levels of fitness, as well as breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

Zhaoling Scenic Area

Those seeking adventure can head to the Zhaoling Scenic Area, which offers activities such as rock climbing and camping. The area is also famous for its breathtaking views of the sunset and sunrise.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Siping

Siping is a fantastic destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts who want to explore its natural beauty on foot or by bike. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Five Drum Mountain Hiking Trail: a popular trail that leads to the top of Five Drum Mountain, where you can enjoy panoramic views of Siping.
  • Jingbo Lake Cycling Route: a scenic route that takes you around the picturesque Jingbo Lake.
  • Phoenix Mountain Hiking Trail: a moderate hike that offers views of the surrounding mountain and the stunning landscapes of Siping.

Best Things to Do in Siping: Activities and Tours

Siping offers a variety of activities that cater to all types of travelers, from cultural enthusiasts to adventure seekers.

Siping Revolutionary Sites Tour

For history buffs, the Siping Revolutionary Sites Tour is a must-try activity. This tour takes you to key sites related to China’s revolutionary history, including the Sanhe Ancient City and Siping Museum.

Siping Art District Tour

Art enthusiasts can explore the Siping Art District Tour, which takes you to various art museums, galleries, and studios throughout the city.

Jingbo Lake Scenic Area Boat Tour

Take a relaxing boat tour and admire the beauty of Jingbo Lake.

Week-Long Itinerary for Siping

If you’re planning to spend a week in Siping, here’s a suggested itinerary to make the most out of your stay:

Day 1: Visit the Siping Museum and Sanhe Ancient City. Day 2: Spend a day hiking the Five Drum Mountain or Phoenix Mountain hiking trail. Day 3: Explore the Siping Art District Tour. Day 4: Take a relaxing boat tour of Jingbo Lake. Day 5: Visit the Zhaoling Scenic Area for a day of adventure and excitement. Day 6: Relax and enjoy the city’s local cuisine and shopping scene. Day 7: Head home or explore nearby destinations.

Food and Drink Scene of Siping

Siping has a rich food and drink scene, offering a mix of local and international cuisines. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Jilin-style Dumplings: a popular local dish filled with pork, beef, or vegetables.
  • Spicy Fried Chicken: a flavorful and spicy chicken dish that pairs well with rice.
  • Sticky Rice with Sweet Bean Paste: a delicious dessert made with steamed sticky rice and sweet bean paste.

Local Beverages

  • Rice Wine: a traditional Chinese wine made from fermented rice.
  • Siping Tea: a local tea made from green tea leaves and various herbs.

Where to Stay in Siping: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Siping offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets, from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels and guesthouses.

  • Chuntianli Hotel: a luxury hotel located in the heart of Siping.
  • Home Inn: a budget-friendly hotel chain with multiple locations in Siping.
  • Siping Yike Guesthouse: a cozy and affordable guesthouse that offers basic amenities and comfortable rooms.

What to Buy in Siping: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for unique souvenirs and gifts to bring home, here are some local products to consider:

  • Siping Tea: souvenirs made from the famous local tea, such as tea cups and tea sets.
  • Red Dates: a type of Chinese fruit that is grown in Siping and is used in various traditional Chinese medicines.
  • Siping Embroidery: traditional cultural craft that reflects the rich history and culture of Siping.

Local Culture and Customs of Siping

Siping has a rich cultural heritage that reflects the influence of various ethnic groups and historical events. Here are some local customs and traditions to keep in mind:

  • Respect for elders is an essential aspect of local culture.
  • Nian Dance is a traditional dance performed during the Chinese New Year.
  • Local residents celebrate various traditional festivals throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Siping

The best time to visit Siping is during the spring and autumn seasons (April to June and September to November), when the weather is mild and pleasant. The summer months can be hot and humid, while winter can be chilly and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Siping

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious and cultural sites.
  • Try the local cuisine.

Don’t:

  • Throw trash on the streets or in public areas.
  • Take photos of people without their permission.
  • Touch religious objects or artifacts without permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Siping

Siping is generally a safe place for travelers. However, it’s always a good idea to take standard safety precautions, such as keeping your valuables secure and being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Siping

Travelers should be cautious when consuming street food and water. It’s best to stick to bottled water and cooked foods to avoid any contamination.

Getting to Siping: Transportation Options and Logistics

Siping is accessible through various modes of transportation, including flights, trains, and buses.

Airports Near Siping

The nearest airport is Changchun Longjia International Airport, which is approximately 120 km from Siping.

Bus Stations Near Siping

Siping Bus Station is the primary bus station that serves this area. It has regular bus services to and from major cities in China.

Other Options

The city is well connected by train, and the Siping Railway Station is the main station that serves this area.

How to Get Around in Siping

The most convenient way to get around Siping is by taxi or public bus. Bicycles and scooters are also available for rent in some areas.

Where to Go Next from Siping: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Siping is surrounded by beautiful cities and attractions that are worth exploring.

  • Changchun: the capital city of Jilin province, and home to the famous Changchun Movie Wonderland.
  • Jilin City: a beautiful city known for its stunning landscapes and natural beauty.
  • Changbai Mountain: a popular tourist destination that offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.

Conclusion

Siping is an excellent destination for travelers who want to experience China’s rich history and culture and explore its natural beauty. With its breathtaking scenery, adventurous activities, and delicious local cuisine, Siping is a destination that should be on every traveler’s bucket list. So pack your bags and head to Siping!
