Introduction to Jingpohu National Forest Park

Jingpohu National Forest Park is a natural reserve located in Heilongjiang in Northeast China. The park covers an area of over 200 square kilometers and is known for its beautiful forests, crystal-clear lakes, and stunning scenery. The park is free to enter and open all year round.

List of Places to Visit in Jingpohu National Forest Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Jingpohu National Geological Museum: This museum showcases the geological wonders of Jingpohu, including unique rock formations and fossils.

  • The Jingpo Lake Temple: This ancient temple is situated on a hill and offers spectacular views of the lake.

  • Fishing Village: A traditional fishing village where visitors can experience the life of a fisherman.

Natural Attractions of Jingpohu National Forest Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Jingpo Lake: The highlight of the park, Jingpo Lake, is a freshwater lake that stretches for over 100 kilometers in length. It is known for its clear water, incredible scenery, and abundant fish species.

  • Chuangwang Pavilion: Located on a hill overlooking Jingpo Lake, the Chuangwang Pavilion offers panoramic views of the surrounding area.

  • Mount Huang Shan: This mountain is the highest peak in the park and offers a challenging hike for adventurous visitors.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jingpohu National Forest Park

There are many hiking and biking trails in Jingpohu National Forest Park that offer visitors a chance to explore the natural beauty of the area. Some popular routes include:

  • Jingpo Lake Reservoir Scenic Route: This 10-kilometer trail offers stunning views of Jingpo Lake and is perfect for hiking or biking.

  • Huang Shan Mountain Climbing Route: This route takes visitors to the summit of Mount Huang Shan and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

  • Long Island Scenic Route: This 12-kilometer trail takes visitors on a journey around Long Island, an area within Jingpo Lake.

Best Things to Do in Jingpohu National Forest Park: Activities and Tours

There are many things to do in Jingpohu National Forest Park, including:

  • Boat Tour of Jingpo Lake: Visitors can enjoy a relaxing boat tour of Jingpo Lake, taking in the scenery and wildlife.

  • Fishing: Jingpo Lake is known for its abundance of fish species and visitors can try their hand at fishing in the lake.

  • Skiing: During winter, visitors can enjoy skiing on the slopes of Mount Huang Shan.

Week-Long Itinerary for Jingpohu National Forest Park

Day 1: Explore the Jingpo Lake Temple and the Fishing Village Day 2: Hike the Huang Shan Mountain Climbing Route Day 3: Take a boat tour of Jingpo Lake Day 4: Visit the Jingpohu National Geological Museum Day 5: Bike the Long Island Scenic Route Day 6: Ski on the slopes of Mount Huang Shan Day 7: Relax and enjoy the scenery

Food and Drink Scene of Jingpohu National Forest Park

Must-Try Dishes

  • Smoked Fish: Jingpo Lake is known for its delicious smoked fish, which is a local specialty.

  • Sturgeon: Sturgeon is a freshwater fish found in Jingpo Lake, and it is often served in local restaurants.

Local Beverages

  • Heilongjiang Rice Wine: This local rice wine is a popular beverage in the area.

Where to Stay in Jingpohu National Forest Park: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several accommodation options in Jingpohu National Forest Park, including:

  • Hanting Hotel Jingpo Lake Hongxing Road: This hotel is located near Jingpo Lake and offers comfortable rooms at an affordable price.

  • Jingpo Lake Hotel: This hotel is situated right on the shore of Jingpo Lake and offers spectacular views.

  • Jingpo Villa: This guesthouse is located in a quiet area of the park and is perfect for visitors looking for a peaceful retreat.

What to Buy in Jingpohu National Forest Park: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Smoked Fish: Jingpo Lake’s smoked fish is a popular souvenir for visitors to take home.

  • Jingpohu National Forest Park Memorabilia: Visitors can purchase t-shirts, hats, and other souvenirs to commemorate their trip to the park.

Local Culture and Customs of Jingpohu National Forest Park

Jingpohu National Forest Park is home to several ethnic minority groups, including the Manchu and Mongolian people. Visitors can experience their unique culture and customs by attending local festivals and events.

Best Time to Visit Jingpohu National Forest Park

The best time to visit Jingpo Lake is from June to September, when the weather is mild and the scenery is at its best.

Do’s and Don’ts of Jingpohu National Forest Park

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.

  • Follow the park rules and regulations.

  • Take your trash with you when you leave.

Don’t:

  • Disturb the local wildlife.

  • Leave a mess or damage the environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jingpohu National Forest Park

Jingpohu National Forest Park is a safe place to visit, but visitors should take precautions like:

  • Wear comfortable and sturdy shoes when hiking.

  • Stay on designated trails.

  • Bring a map of the park.

Health in Jingpohu National Forest Park

Visitors should exercise caution when eating local foods and make sure they are properly prepared. It is also important to bring any necessary medications with you on your trip.

Getting to Jingpohu National Forest Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Jingpohu National Forest Park

  • Mudanjiang Hailang Airport: Mudanjiang Hailang Airport is the nearest airport to Jingpohu National Forest Park, located around 80 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Jingpohu National Forest Park

  • Jingpo Lake Bus Station: This bus station has buses that run from Mudanjiang to Jingpohu National Forest Park.

Other Options

  • Private Car: Visitors can hire a private car to take them to and from the park.

How to Get Around in Jingpohu National Forest Park

The best way to explore Jingpohu National Forest Park is on foot, but visitors can also rent bicycles or take a boat tour of Jingpo Lake.
