Introduction to Zhalong National Nature Reserve

Zhalong National Nature Reserve, located in Heilongjiang province, is the largest reed marsh in China and a crucial habitat for waterfowl. The reserve spans across an area of 2,100 square kilometers and is a haven for both locals and tourists alike. The reserve boasts a diverse flora and fauna due to the wide range of habitats, including marshes, wetlands, and reeds.

This guide will take you through everything you need to know about planning your visit to Zhalong National Nature Reserve.

List of Places to Visit in Zhalong National Nature Reserve

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Zhalong Nature Museum

The Zhalong Nature Museum, located within the reserve, is dedicated to showcasing the unique flora and fauna of the area. The museum is a great place to learn about the different types of birds, mammals, reptiles, and plants that can be found in the reserve.

Yilaha Village

Yilaha Village is a small village within the reserve that has been inhabited for thousands of years. The village is a great place to learn about the local culture and customs of the area. The village is home to an ancient temple, which is still in use today.

Natural Attractions of Zhalong National Nature Reserve: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Birdwatching

The reserve is recognized as a birdwatching paradise, and there are over 300 species of birds to be found in the area. Visitors can choose from a variety of birdwatching tours or explore the area on their own.

Hiking and Biking

The reserve is home to a variety of hiking and biking trails, which offer stunning views of the natural habitat of the area. Visitors can choose from guided tours or explore the area on their own.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Zhalong National Nature Reserve

Tour of Jingbo Lake

This tour takes visitors on a hike around the beautiful Jingbo Lake, which is surrounded by lush forests and picturesque meadows. The tour takes approximately three hours to complete and is suitable for all skill levels.

Exploration of Reed Marshes

This tour takes visitors through the reed marshes of Zhalong National Nature Reserve, which are the largest in China. Visitors can see a variety of bird and plant species, including the rare white eared night heron.

Best Things to Do in Zhalong National Nature Reserve: Activities and Tours

Birdwatching Tour

A birdwatching tour is the perfect way to experience the stunning bird species of the reserve. Visitors can choose from a variety of tours, including walking tours and boat tours.

Local Village Tour

Exploring the local village of Yilaha is a great way to learn about the local culture and customs of the area. Visitors can tour the village and visit the ancient temple.

Local Culture and Customs of Zhalong National Nature Reserve

The local culture of Zhalong National Nature Reserve is heavily influenced by the fishing and farming communities that have lived in the area for generations. Visitors should respect the local customs and traditions when visiting the reserve.

Best Time to Visit Zhalong National Nature Reserve

The best time to visit Zhalong National Nature Reserve is from April to October, as the weather is mild and pleasant during these months. The birdwatching season peaks from May to June, while the fall foliage is at its best from September to October.

  • Follow the rules of the reserve.
  • Dress appropriately for outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Disturb the wildlife.
  • Ignore the warnings of the reserve staff.

Safety and Health

Safety in Zhalong National Nature Reserve

Visitors should be aware of their surroundings and take appropriate precautions when exploring the reserve. Visitors should also follow the warnings and advice of the reserve staff to ensure their safety.

Health in Zhalong National Nature Reserve

Visitors should take appropriate precautions to avoid mosquito bites and other insect bites when exploring the reserve. Visitors should also stay hydrated and carry any necessary medications with them.

Getting to Zhalong National Nature Reserve: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Zhalong National Nature Reserve

The nearest airport to Zhalong National Nature Reserve is Mudanjiang Hailang International Airport, which is approximately 50 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Zhalong National Nature Reserve

There are several bus stations that serve the area, including the Mudanjiang Bus Station and the Hailin Bus Station. Visitors can take a bus to the reserve from these stations.

Other Options

Visitors can also take a taxi or rent a car to get to Zhalong National Nature Reserve.

How to Get Around in Zhalong National Nature Reserve

Visitors can explore the reserve on foot, by bike, or by boat. Guided tours are also available for visitors who wish to learn more about the area.

Where to Go Next from Zhalong National Nature Reserve: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Harbin

Harbin, the capital of Heilongjiang province, is located approximately 250 kilometers from Zhalong National Nature Reserve. The city is famous for its annual ice and snow festival and its rich history and culture.

Jilin

Jilin is a city located approximately 350 kilometers from Zhalong National Nature Reserve. The city is home to several historical sites and cultural attractions, including the Jilin Confucian Temple and the Jilin Provincial Museum.
