Complete Travel Guide to Luqu, Gansu: Things to Do, Places to Visit, and More

Located in the Gannan prefecture in the northwest of Gansu province, Luqu is a county town not to be missed on your trip to China. Luqu is characterized by its stunning landscapes, picturesque villages, and rich culture. In this travel guide, we will take you on a journey through Luqu, from its cultural and natural attractions to its vibrant food scene and traditional customs. Are you ready?

Introduction to Luqu

Luqu is a charming county located between Xining and Lanzhou in Gansu province, China. It is known for its unique natural scenery, numerous cultural attractions, and ethnic diversity. The county covers an area of 2,200 square kilometers and has a population of around 25,000 people, most of whom belong to the Tibetan ethnic group.

Local Culture and Customs of Luqu

Luqu is a region with a rich cultural heritage, and people have a deep respect for their traditions. Here are some insights into the local customs you should be aware of:

  • It is customary to greet people with a “Tashi Delek” greeting
  • When receiving gifts, it’s respectful to use both hands
  • Removes shoes when entering houses or temples

Best Time to Visit Luqu

Luqu has four distinct seasons. The best time to visit is spring and autumn when the weather is pleasant and the landscapes are picturesque. Summer is also a good time to visit Luqu if you can bear the heat.

Do’s and Don’ts of Luqu

While you’re in Luqu, be mindful of the local customs and avoid any behavior that might be deemed disrespectful. Here are some essential tips to follow:

Do:

  • Dress modestly while visiting temples or monasteries
  • Remove shoes when entering houses or temples
  • Respect the local culture and customs

Don’t:

  • Take photographs without permission
  • Disrupt the peace and tranquility of local areas
  • Express negative comments about the culture and beliefs of the local people

Getting to Luqu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Getting to Luqu can be done using three primary routes:

Airports Near Luqu

The nearest airport to Luqu is the Tianshui Maijishan Airport, located about 200 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Luqu

Luqu Bus Station, situated near the town centre, is the primary station which provides daily transportation services to other destinations in the surrounding areas.

Other Options

Taking an express train to Lanzhou or Xining and then boarding a local train or bus to Luqu is a convenient option.

How to Get Around in Luqu

Luqu offers taxis, public buses, and rented cars to get around the area. You can also rent a bicycle or a scooter to explore the place by yourself, but it is not recommended to bike on the highways.
