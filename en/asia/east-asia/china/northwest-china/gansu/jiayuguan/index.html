Introduction to Jiayuguan

Located in the northwest of China, in the Gansu province, Jiayuguan is a historic city at the western end of the Great Wall of China. It is often referred to as the “First Pass Under Heaven” and is one of the best-preserved sections of the Great Wall. In this guide, you will discover the best places to visit, things to do, food and drink scene, where to stay, and much more in Jiayuguan.

List of Places to Visit in Jiayuguan

Jiayuguang has plenty of attractions to visit. Here are the highlights of the city.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Jiayu Pass: This is the main attraction of the city and is located west of Jiayuguan city. It was the last fortress of the Ming Dynasty Great Wall and is also called the “Impregnable Pass under Heaven.”
  • Great Wall Museum: It is a modern museum dedicated to the history of the Great Wall of China.
  • Wei-Jin Art Gallery: It houses a collection of ancient Wei and Jin tombs filled with murals and other artifacts.

Natural Attractions of Jiayuguan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mingsha Mountain and Crescent Lake: Visitors can take camel rides through the desert and sand dunes on this scenic route to witness the beautiful sunset over the crescent-shaped lake.
  • Qilianshan National Park: It is a beautiful reserve with stunning sceneries, beautiful plants, and wildlife that is protected by UNESCO World Heritage Site status.
  • Guoyuan Mountain Forest Park: It offers a beautiful natural landscape and is known for its beautiful view of the city.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jiayuguan

Jiayuguan offers beautiful hiking and biking routes that offer some stunning views. Some of the popular ones include:

  • West Route: This route begins from the Jiayu Pass and goes through the Hexi Corridor to reach Dunhuang.
  • East Route: This route goes from the city center to Guoyuan Mountain and offers some beautiful scenic views along the way.
  • South Route: This route starts from Shimenzi Pass in Anxi and ends at the Jiayu Pass.

Best Things to Do in Jiayuguan: Activities and Tours

  • Camel Trekking: Take a camel ride in the desert and enjoy the beautiful sunset along the way.
  • Horseback Riding: Horseback riding into the desert offers a unique way to explore the area.
  • Great Wall Hiking: Hiking along the Great Wall of China is one of the highlights of visiting Jiayuguan.
  • Jiayuguan Wine Tasting: Sample the local wine made from grapes grown in Gansu Province.

Week-Long Itinerary for Jiayuguan

Day 1: Visit Jiayu Pass and the Great Wall Museum Day 2: Visit Mingsha Mountain and Crescent Lake Day 3: Explore Guoyuan Mountain Forest Park Day 4: Visit Qilianshan National Park Day 5: Camel Riding or Horseback Riding in the desert Day 6: Wine Tasting and visit to Wei-Jin Art Gallery Day 7: Enjoy some local food before departing the city

Food and Drink Scene of Jiayuguan

Jiayuguan is known for its unique cuisine and variety of dishes. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Hezhuan, a type of flatbread that is crispy, flaky, and seasoned with sesame seeds.
  • Nang, a type of bread flavored with cumin and salt or sweetened with dates or raisins.
  • Roujiamo, a savory meat-filled sandwich or pita-like bread.

Local Beverages

  • Jiayuguan Wine: Made from grapes grown in Gansu Province, Jiayuguan Wine has a sweet aroma and is perfect for sipping on a hot day.
  • Qilian Mountain Tea: This tea boasts a unique flavor due to the high elevation of the tea plants.

Where to Stay in Jiayuguan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Jiayuguan offers a variety of options for accommodations, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Some popular options include:

  • Silk Road Dunhuang Hotel: This five-star hotel offers a luxurious and comfortable stay and is conveniently located near major attractions.
  • Jiayuguan Hotel: A budget-friendly option that offers clean and comfortable rooms.
  • Jiayuguan Pass Hotel: This unique hotel, designed to look like the nearby fortress, offers a memorable and authentic stay.

What to Buy in Jiayuguan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When visiting Jiayuguan, be sure to bring home some of the following as souvenirs:

  • Traditional Chinese tea sets
  • Handcrafted rugs or tapestries
  • Jewelry made from jade or other natural stones

Local Culture and Customs of Jiayuguan

Jiayuguan has a rich and fascinating culture influenced by its history and geography. Here are some of the customs and traditions unique to the area:

  • Chinese opera performances take place regularly, featuring local actors and music.
  • Traditional costumes and clothing are still seen in the rural areas.
  • Many locals practice Islam and observe halal traditions.

Best Time to Visit Jiayuguan

The best time to visit Jiayuguan is during the spring or fall when temperatures are mild and the weather is comfortable. Summer days are scorching, while winters are bitterly cold.

Do’s and Don’ts of Jiayuguan

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Visit Jiayu Pass, the Great Wall Museum, and other historical sites.
  • Sample the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Dress appropriately for the weather and activities.

Don’t:

  • Take pictures without permission, especially at religious sites or of locals without permission.
  • Haggle too much in local markets or shops.
  • Litter or damage the natural surroundings during outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jiayuguan

Jiayuguan is a relatively safe city to visit. However, visitors should exercise caution when exploring the city at night or in remote areas.

Health in Jiayuguan

Visitors should drink bottled water and avoid street food to prevent food poisoning. It is also advised to bring sunscreen and apply it frequently during outdoor activities.

Getting to Jiayuguan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Jiayuguan:

  • Jiayuguan Airport: It is a regional airport located in the city center.

Bus Stations Near Jiayuguan:

  • Jiayuguan Bus Terminal: It is the main bus station for long-distance buses and is located in the city center.
  • Jiayuguan North Bus Station: It is located near the Jiayu Pass and offers buses to and from nearby destinations.

Other Options:

  • Taxis: Taxis are available across the city and can be hired for a specific time period or trip.

How to Get Around in Jiayuguan

Jiayuguan is a small city, and visitors can easily explore on foot. Bicycles can be rented in many places, and local buses offer affordable transportation to nearby attractions. Taxis are also available.

Where to Go Next from Jiayuguan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Jiayuguan is a great starting point for further exploration of Gansu Province and beyond. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions:

  • Dunhuang: Visit the Mogao Caves or the Singing Sand Dunes.
  • Zhangye: Explore the colorful Danxia National Geological Park.
  • Lanzhou: Taste the local beef noodle soup and visit the Yellow River Park.

Conclusion

Jiayuguan is an amazing destination for those interested in Chinese history, stunning landscapes, and unique cultural experiences. Use this comprehensive guide when planning your trip, and make the most of your time in this wonderful city.
