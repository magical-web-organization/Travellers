Sayram Lake travel guide takes you on an exhilarating journey exploring one of the most beautiful mountain lakes in China. Sayram Lake, known as “the pearl of the Tian Shan Mountains,” is situated in the Dzungarian region, Xinjiang Province. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll show you the best things to do, must-see attractions, where to stay, what and where to eat, and practical tips for making the most of your trip to Sayram Lake.

Introduction to Sayram Lake

Sayram Lake is a pristine alpine lake, located about 90 kilometers northwest of Yining, in the northwestern part of Xinjiang Province. The lake is nestled between snow-capped mountains and has a surface area of around 452 square kilometers, making it the largest alpine lake in China. In Kazakh language, Sayram means “blessing,” and local people regard the lake as a holy site.

The natural beauty of Sayram Lake attracts a wide variety of birds and animals, including black-necked cranes, swans, and wild ducks. With crystal clear waters reflecting the surrounding mountains, Sayram Lake is an idyllic destination for nature lovers.

List of Places to Visit in Sayram Lake

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. The Sayram Lake National Nature Reserve - This local museum displays artifacts and relics from the region, including bones and fossils of ancient creatures that once lived in the area.

  2. Saihantala Ecological Park - This park is filled with rare plants and animals that are unique to the region. You can take a guided tour to learn about the indigenous culture and their traditional way of life.

  3. Mausoleum of Ahmadi Khoja - This is a holy place for Sufi Muslims. The mausoleum has been beautifully preserved and is a must-see destination for those interested in history and religion.

Natural Attractions of Sayram Lake: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Sayram Lake Scenic Area - This area includes a range of walking and hiking trails that offer stunning views of the mountains and surrounding lakes. The Sayram Lake Scenic Area is also home to several ancient stone carvings.

  2. Bayanbulak Grasslands - These grasslands are a natural haven for wildlife, including rare and endangered species such as the argali sheep, which is the largest wild sheep in the world.

  3. Tianshan Mountain Range - This mountain range is known for its picturesque vistas and is host to several hiking and trekking routes. One of the most famous is the Tianshan Grand Canyon, which spans 5 kilometers and is home to towering peaks, rushing streams, and dense forests.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sayram Lake

Sayram Lake offers a range of hiking and biking routes, from gentle family hikes to challenging treks for the more experienced adventurer. The most popular trails include:

  1. Shatan Gorge - This is a moderate trail that takes you through the rocky canyons, past waterfalls, and across mountain streams.

  2. Baihaba Village - This is a family-friendly trail that takes you through the Kazak village of Baihaba, where you can see traditional way of life and sample local food and drink.

  3. Tianshan Grand Canyon - This challenging trail is perfect for experienced hikers and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

Best Things to Do in Sayram Lake: Activities and Tours

  1. Horseback Riding - You can explore the surrounding mountains on horseback, allowing you to see the beauty of Sayram Lake from a unique perspective.

  2. Rafting and Kayaking - These thrilling water sports let you experience the natural wonders of Sayram Lake up close.

  3. Fishing - Sayram Lake is home to a variety of fish, including trout and salmon, making it the perfect spot for a relaxing day of fishing.

Food and Drink Scene of Sayram Lake

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Nan: A traditional bread made from wheat flour and baked in a clay oven.

  2. Kazakh-style Roasted Lamb: A succulent and flavorful roast lamb, seasoned with herbs and spices.

  3. Manta: A type of dumpling that’s stuffed with minced meat, onions, and spices.

Local Beverages

  1. Kumiss: A fermented drink made from mare’s milk, which is mildly alcoholic and has a slightly sour taste.

  2. Black Tea: Served in a traditional style with milk and sugar, this tea is perfect for keeping warm on chilly days.

Where to Stay in Sayram Lake: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several accommodation options in and around Sayram Lake, ranging from budget hostels to luxury hotels. Some popular options include:

  1. Sayram Lake Hotel - This hotel offers comfortable rooms and a range of amenities, including a restaurant, bar, and spa.

  2. Sayram Lake Guesthouse - This guesthouse offers budget-friendly accommodation in a scenic location, with stunning views of the lake.

  3. Aydingkol Lake Tent Guesthouse - This guesthouse offers a unique camping-style experience, with tents equipped with comfortable beds and heating, making it the perfect spot for an adventure lover.

What to Buy in Sayram Lake: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Handicrafts - There are several local handicraft markets where you can buy handmade souvenirs and gifts such as belts, hats, and bags.

  2. Kazakh-style Musical Instruments - Another unique souvenir item is Kazakh-style traditional musical instruments such as the Kyl-Kobyz or the Dombra.

  3. Local Products - Some local products that make great gifts include Kumiss and other dairy products, fur hats, and traditional Kazakh costumes.

Local Culture and Customs of Sayram Lake

The culture of Sayram Lake is strongly influenced by the indigenous Kazakh people who have lived here for generations. It’s important to respect local customs, such as removing your shoes when entering someone’s home, and dress modestly. It’s also polite to bring a small gift if you’re visiting someone’s home.

Best Time to Visit Sayram Lake

The best time to visit Sayram Lake is between May and October when the weather is mild and the natural scenery is at its peak. June and July are the best times to see wildflowers and various bird species while October has beautiful autumn foliage in the surrounding mountains.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sayram Lake

Do:

  • Dress modestly and respect the local culture.
  • Take care of the environment and follow local regulations.
  • Try the local food and drink.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the culture or customs of the local people.
  • Litter or pollute the environment.
  • Take photos of people without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sayram Lake

Sayram Lake is extremely safe for tourists, with a low crime rate. However, it’s still important to take basic precautions such as locking up valuables in your hotel room and being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Sayram Lake

It’s important to note that healthcare facilities in Sayram Lake are limited. It’s advisable to pack a basic medical kit, including over-the-counter medications, and first aid supplies.

Getting to Sayram Lake: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sayram Lake

The nearest airport to Sayram Lake is the Yining Airport, which is around 90 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Sayram Lake

Buses run from major cities in Xinjiang, including Urumqi, Kashgar, and Yining, to Sayram Lake. Buses are typically the most efficient and economical way to travel.

Other Options

Private car services and tour operators can arrange transportation to Sayram Lake.

How to Get Around in Sayram Lake

The best way to get around Sayram Lake is by private car or taxi. It’s important to negotiate price in advance with taxi services.

Where to Go Next from Sayram Lake: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Turpan City - This city is known for its unique landscape, including the dried-up salt lake, the hottest place in China, and numerous desert basin oases.

  2. Kanas Lake - Located in northern Xinjiang, Kanas Lake boasts stunning mountain scenery, and the pristine water is a perfect destination for those interested in hiking, photography, and outdoor activities.

  3. Urumqi - As the capital city of Xinjiang province, Urumqi has many must-see destinations such as the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region Museum, the Red Hill Park, and the International Grand Bazaar.

Conclusion

Sayram Lake is a hidden gem, tucked away in the mountains of northwest China. With its pristine beauty, rich culture, and unique local experience, it’s the perfect destination for adventure lovers and those interested in exploring the natural wonders of China.
