Introduction to Burqin

Burqin is a small town located in the Dzungarian, in Xinjiang province, Northwest China. The area is known for its unspoiled natural scenery, with towering mountains, vast grasslands, and pristine lakes. Burqin is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. This comprehensive guide will provide you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Burqin.

List of Places to Visit in Burqin

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Burqin County Museum - This museum showcases the local history of Burqin county, with exhibits and artifacts from various ethnic groups that call the area home. The museum is a great place to learn about the unique culture and history of the region.

  • Kanas Lake Scenic Area Museum - This museum is located within the Kanas Lake Scenic Area and offers an insight into the region’s history and natural environment. The museum showcases the traditional lifestyle of the local Kazakh people and their customs.

Natural Attractions of Burqin: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Kanas Lake Scenic Area - Kanas Lake is a clear, blue, and tranquil lake set amid breathtaking scenery, surrounded by towering mountains, and lush forests. The Kanas Lake Scenic Area is home to unique flora and fauna, including several rare and endangered species.

  • Jade Dragon Snow Mountain - Jade Dragon Snow Mountain is part of the eastern range of the Himalayas and is home to some of the most stunning glaciers in China. Visitors can hike to the base of the mountain to enjoy the panoramic view of the snow-capped peaks.

  • Kanas Nature Reserve - The Kanas Nature Reserve is a vast and untouched wilderness that is home to a wide variety of wildlife, including brown bears, lynx, and many species of birds. The reserve offers adventurous visitors the opportunity to hike through its forests, experience its rivers and relax in nature.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Burqin

  • Kanas Lake Scenic Area Hiking Trail - This hiking trail will take you past some of the most stunning scenery in the Kanas Lake Scenic Area. Visitors can hike through lush forests, cross crystal-clear streams, and enjoy panoramic views of snow-capped peaks.

  • Jade Dragon Snow Mountain Hiking Trail - This hiking trail leads visitors to view some of the most breathtaking glaciers in China. The trail is well-marked and has rest areas along the way to enjoy the scenic view fully.

Best Things to Do in Burqin: Activities and Tours

  • Kanas Lake Boat Tour - The boat tour of Kanas Lake is a must-do for visitors. The tour takes you around the lake, allowing you to see the stunning scenery from all angles. The unique glacial landscape and crystal-clear water of the lake create a serene and unforgettable experience.

  • Kanas Lake Night Tour - The Kanas Lake Night Tour is a unique and unforgettable experience that lets visitors witness the stunning transformation of the landscape under the moonlight. Explore the peaceful and tranquil countryside under a starry sky.

Food and Drink Scene of Burqin

Must-Try Dishes

  • Xinjiang Pilaf - also known as the “jiaozi”, this dish is made up of rice, mutton, and a variety of vegetables and spices. It is one of the most popular dishes in Xinjiang cuisine.

  • Lamb Kebabs - Xinjiang’s famous lamb kebabs are a must-try dish for any visitor. These kebabs are made with succulent pieces of lamb, marinated in a variety of spices, and grilled to perfection.

Local Beverages

  • Yogurt - Xinjiang’s fresh and creamy yogurt is a popular drink that is delicious, filling, and packed with nutrients.

  • Suleiman Tea - also known as “big tea”, is a popular beverage in Xinjiang. This tea is made with black tea leaves and a variety of local herbs and spices, creating a robust and flavourful taste.

Where to Stay in Burqin: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Burqin Kanas Youth Apartment - This hostel is located in Burqin County and offers budget travellers a comfortable and unique accommodation option. The hostel features a traditional Kazakh Yurt style room, creating a unique and immersive experience for visitors.

  • Kanas Mysterious Youth Hostel - The Kanas Mysterious Youth Hostel is located within the Kanas Lake Scenic Area. It offers a clean, affordable, and comfortable stay option for visitors looking to stay close to nature.

What to Buy in Burqin: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Kanas Lake Qiegao - the Qiegao is a traditional pastry made from wheat flour, eggs, and honey. It is a specialty of the Kanas Lake region and available in a variety of flavours.

  • Xinjiang Dried Fruit - Xinjiang is famous for its fresh and delicious dried fruit, including goji berries, raisins, apricots, and dates. These dried fruits are popular gifts to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Burqin

The local culture in Burqin is shaped by its ethnic diversity. Visitors to Burqin are encouraged to respect the local customs and dress appropriately. Women are advised to cover their hair while visiting mosques or religious sites, and it is customary to remove shoes before entering a local’s home.

Best Time to Visit Burqin

The best time to visit Burqin is between May and October when the weather is mild, and the scenery is at its most beautiful. Summers are sunny and warm, with average temperatures of 25-30℃. Autumn is cooler, with the changing colours of the leaves adding to its beautify.

Do’s and Don’ts of Burqin

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and dress appropriately.
  • Be mindful of your language and behaviour, as the area is made up of diverse ethnic groups.
  • Carry cash, as not all shops take payment cards.
  • Be prepared for sudden changes in weather conditions.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local culture or customs.
  • Take photographs without permission, especially at religious sites or of local people.
  • Engage in discussions about politics or sensitive topics.

Safety and Health

Safety in Burqin

Burqin is a safe region with low crime rates. However, visitors are advised to take necessary precautions, such as always carrying identification and avoiding isolated areas at night.

Health in Burqin

The area is generally considered safe with no significant health risks. Visitors should drink only bottled water, avoid uncooked or undercooked food, and carry necessary medication if required.

Getting to Burqin: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Burqin

  • Kanas Airport - Kanas Airport is located approximately 5 kilometres from Kanas Town and 50 kilometres from Burqin County. It offers flights to Urumqi, the capital city of Xinjiang, and other Chinese cities.

Bus Stations Near Burqin

  • Burqin Bus Station - Burqin Bus Station is the primary station for long-distance buses. It connects to nearby cities such as Altay, Urumqi, and Yining.

Other Options

Taxis, private cars, and rental cars are available in Burqin. However, it is essential to have a valid Chinese driving license to rent a car.

How to Get Around in Burqin

Local buses are the most convenient and cost-effective way to explore the region. Taxis and private cars are also available, but they can be expensive.

Where to Go Next from Burqin: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Kanas Lake Scenic Area - The surrounding area of Kanas Lake offers visitors exciting opportunities to explore the unspoiled natural beauty of the region.

  • Altay City - Altay is a charming city known for its unique Kazakh culture and is only a few hours drive from Burqin. Its bustling markets and historic sites make it a popular tourist destination.

Conclusion

Burqin is an unspoiled destination that is perfect for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. It offers a unique and immersive experience that is hard to find in other parts of China. We hope that this comprehensive guide has helped you plan your visit to Burqin and filled you with excitement to explore this stunning and culturally diverse region.
