Introduction to Tianshan Tianchi National Park

Located in the Dzungarian area of Xinjiang province in Northwest China, Tianshan Tianchi National Park showcases breathtaking views of snow-capped peaks, crystal-clear lakes and lush valleys. Covering an area of 1,600 square kilometers, this natural wonderland attracts visitors from around the globe who come to explore its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and outdoor adventures.

List of Places to Visit in Tianshan Tianchi National Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kazakh Yurt Camp: Experience the unique culture and hospitality of the Kazakh people while staying in their traditional yurts.
  • Fuyun County Museum: Discover the profound history and culture of the historic Fuyun county.
  • Tianshan Grand Canyon: Hike through the impressive Tianshan Grand Canyon which features towering sandstone cliffs and stunning rock formations.
  • Heavenly Lake Scenic Area: Explore the natural beauty of Heavenly Lake, located in the heart of the park, which offers crystal-clear water surrounded by snow-capped peaks.

Natural Attractions of Tianshan Tianchi National Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Yadan Landform: Marvel at the unusual geological formations of the Yadan Landform, characterized by towering rocks in bizarre shapes, creating a unique and awe-inspiring sight.
  • Tianshan Glacier: Trek through the glaciers to witness the awe-inspiring views of the towering snow-capped peaks and pristine glacial rivers.
  • Hemu Village: Discover the charming village of Hemu, nestled in the midst of towering peaks and wooded forests, with unique wooden houses and a rich local culture.
  • Laojun Mountain: Take a cable car tour up to Laojun Mountain, and witness the stunning panoramic views of the park from above the clouds.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tianshan Tianchi National Park

  • Heavenly Lake Trek: Trek around Heavenly Lake and discover its magical landscapes and views, a genuinely memorable experience!
  • Tianshan Glacier Trek: Follow the hiking trails through the Tianshan Glacier, with eyes on the stunning views of the snow-capped peaks, glaciers and fjords.
  • Yili River Hike: Follow the Yili River and through the valley, this trek offers stunning views of towering mountain ranges and verdant landscapes.
  • Yadan Landform Route: Take the Yadan Landform route and marvel at the celestial wonders of the towering rocks shaped in bizarre shapes that eerie feel.

Best Things to Do in Tianshan Tianchi National Park: Activities and Tours

  • Horseback Riding: Take a scenic horse ride through the lush hills and valleys of Tianshan Tianchi National Park.
  • Camping: Spend a night or two in the park for stars-gazing and a chance to hear the howling winds of nature.
  • Skiing: Hit the slopes during the winter months and experience the exciting skiing adrenaline rush.
  • Yurt Staying: Spend the night in a traditional Kazakh yurt camp and soak in the unique culture of the local nomadic community.

Food and Drink Scene of Tianshan Tianchi National Park

Must-Try Dishes

  • Dapanji: A hearty chicken stew loaded with potatoes, peppers, and spices, a speciality in the Xinjiang region.
  • Lagman: Hand-pulled noodles, traditionally eaten with beef or lamb served in soup or stir-fried.
  • Roasted Lamb: A speciality dish of the Kazakh nomadic people, slowly roasted over an open flame until tender and juicy.
  • Mantou: Steamed bread, a staple of the Chinese cuisine, served as a breakfast or side dish or to wrap flavor-loaded meats.

Local Beverages

  • Kumiss: A traditional fermented mare’s milk beverage, with a slightly sour, tangy taste, highly recommended for trying while you’re visiting Tianshan Tianchi National Park.
  • Xinjiang Black Beer: A unique beer brewed in the Xinjiang province with a dark, rich flavor and brewing using local grains.

Where to Stay in Tianshan Tianchi National Park: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Kazakh Yurt Camp: Experience authentic Kazakh hospitality while staying in a traditional yurt.
  • Heavenly Lake Hotel: A high-end hotel that offers stunning views of Heavenly Lake, highly recommended to stay here for honeymooners.
  • Fuyun County Hotel: An affordable option located in the heart of Fuyun county, a great base to explore the region.
  • Tianshan Grand Canyon Guesthouse: A guesthouse offering cozy accommodation while exploring the breathtaking Grand Canyon.

What to Buy in Tianshan Tianchi National Park: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Jade: Jade is a unique and valuable material produced in Xinjiang and used to make beautiful jewelry, sculptures, and souvenirs.
  • Hand-woven Carpets: The local carpets woven by the Kazakh women, are beautiful and intricate, a perfect souvenir to add a touch of the region’s culture to your home.

Local Culture and Customs of Tianshan Tianchi National Park

The local culture in the Tianshan Tianchi National Park is influenced by Kazakh traditions and nomadic living. Visitors should expect to encounter friendly locals who are proud to share their culture and history through dance, music, and traditional crafts. Cover yourself completely while visiting the urban areas, as the local community considers it respectful to dress modestly.

Best Time to Visit Tianshan Tianchi National Park

The best time to visit Tianshan Tianchi National Park is from June to August, when the weather is mild, and the park’s scenery is at its most beautiful. The region is significantly colder during the winter months, so it is best to avoid this season if possible.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tianshan Tianchi National Park

Do:

  • Respect the local culture, customs and people, taking an effort to ask for permission before photographing or filming.
  • Bring comfortable and robust walking shoes for exploring the park’s hiking trails.
  • Carry adequate clothing based on the season, as the temperature variation can be extreme.

Don’t:

  • Littering and damaging the environment isn’t appreciated by the locals, nature and must be avoided.
  • Encroach onto the cultural norms and rituals of the locals, respect the traditional values and preserve the etiquettes.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tianshan Tianchi National Park

Tianshan Tianchi National Park is generally safe for visitors; however, visitors should always be vigilant and take appropriate safety precautions, such as not venturing off the trails and avoiding potentially dangerous wildlife.

Health in Tianshan Tianchi National Park

Visitors should also be up-to-date on their routine vaccinations, always carry a medical kit and use sunscreen to protect themselves from the sun’s harsh UV rays.

Getting to Tianshan Tianchi National Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tianshan Tianchi National Park

The nearest airport is Kanas Airport, located approximately 30km from Tianshan Tianchi National Park. The airport serves domestic flights from major Chinese cities and a few international flights from nearby countries.

Bus Stations Near Tianshan Tianchi National Park

Multiple bus stations operate from nearby cities such as Urumqi, Yining, and Fuyun, that connect the park to other cities in the Xinjiang province.

Other Options

Tour operators offer private transportation options. Visitors may hire an independent Boho rickshaw or taxi to the park as well.

How to Get Around in Tianshan Tianchi National Park

Visitors may hire a motorbike or take a bus, taxi or tour van to get around Tianshan Tianchi National Park.

Where to Go Next from Tianshan Tianchi National Park: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Kanas Lake: A stunning destination located just a few hours from Tianshan Tianchi National Park, known for its blue-green water and surrounding scenery.
  • Urumqi: The capital of Xinjiang, this city is a must-visit destination to understand the region’s history, culture and check into a luxury hotel.
  • Turpan: On the path of the ancient Silk Road, an ideal location for those interested in history rich with a heritage of Byzantine, Sogdian, Persian, and Chinese influences.

Conclusion

Tianshan Tianchi National Park offers a rare blend of natural beauty, outdoor adventure, and rich culture. With endless opportunities to explore the park’s breathtaking landscapes and experience the local culture, this park is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking unspoiled nature and adventure. Use this comprehensive guide to plan your trip, enjoy the best of Tianshan Tianchi National Park, and create memories of a lifetime!
