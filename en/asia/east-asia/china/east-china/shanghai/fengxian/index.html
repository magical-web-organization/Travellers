Introduction to Fengxian

Located in the southernmost tip of Shanghai, Fengxian is a beautiful urban district rich in history and natural scenery. It is a place where culture and nature perfectly intertwine, providing visitors with an unforgettable experience.

List of Places to Visit in Fengxian

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fengxian Museum - Exhibits ancient artifacts, cultural relics and art pieces from local areas
  • Xidu Ancient Town - A well-preserved town built in the Ming Dynasty
  • Zhang Wentian Memorial Hall - The former residence of Chinese revolutionary Zhang Wentian
  • Biyun Temple - A 1,000-year-old temple with beautiful architecture and rich history

Natural Attractions of Fengxian: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Bihai Jinsha Beach - A beautiful beach with golden sand and crystal clear water
  • Fengxian Bay Wetland Park - A birdwatching paradise with over 400 species of birds
  • Dongping National Forest Park - Home to stunning forests, waterfalls, and hiking trails
  • Binjiang Forest Park - A peaceful park with beautiful views of the river and gardens

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fengxian

  • Dongping National Forest Park Trail - A challenging trail that takes visitors through the park’s stunning landscape
  • Fengxian Bay Wetland Park Trail - A leisurely stroll that features beautiful views of the wetlands and the bay
  • Bihai Jinsha Beach Trail - A scenic trail that offers breathtaking views of the beach and coastline
  • Qiangyuan Island Trail - A fun trail exploring the island’s dense forest and scenic views.

Best Things to Do in Fengxian: Activities and Tours

  • Bihai Jinsha Beach Water Sports - Visitors can enjoy a variety of water sports activities such as Jet Skiing, Kayaking, and Parasailing.
  • Fengxian Bay Wetlands Cruise - A relaxing cruise that offers stunning views of the wetlands and the bay
  • Exploring Xidu Ancient Town - Visitors can take a walk around the town to explore local food, markets and traditional architecture.
  • Fengxian Museum Tour - Visitors can learn about the history and development of the local area through various exhibits in the museum.

Food and Drink Scene of Fengxian

Must-Try Dishes

  • Xiaolongbao - A type of steamed bun filled with soup and meat
  • Youtiao - A popular breakfast food, deep-fried dough sticks
  • Fengxian Squirrel Soup - A local favorite soup with frog, turtle and chicken
  • “Seven-killer” Crab - A specialty seafood dish in Fengxian

Local Beverages

  • ”Meiguilu” Wine - A local wine made from roses
  • Jasmine Tea - A refreshing tea with a subtle floral aroma

Where to Stay in Fengxian: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Sheraton Shanghai Fengxian - A luxurious five-star hotel located in Bihai Jinsha Beach with great amenities
  • Ji Hotel Fengxian Xidu - A budget-friendly hotel located in Xidu Ancient Town with clean and comfortable rooms.
  • ** Fuyue Hotel** - A mid-range hotel located in Fengxian city center with 24 hours front desk.

What to Buy in Fengxian: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • “Meiguilu” Wine - A popular wine to take back home as a souvenir
  • Jasmine Tea - A refreshing tea with a subtle floral aroma, commonly local specialties
  • Handicrafts - Purchase traditional handmade bamboo baskets, silk products, and more

Local Culture and Customs of Fengxian

Fengxian has a rich cultural heritage deeply rooted in Taoism and Buddhism. Some local customs and etiquette include greeting seniors and elders first, avoiding talking about sensitive topics like politics or religion, and respecting Chinese National Flag.

Best Time to Visit Fengxian

The best time to visit Fengxian is from April to November. The weather is mild, and the scenery is at its best during this period.

Do’s and Don’ts of Fengxian

Do:

  • Learn basic Chinese phrases
  • Dress appropriately when visiting religious sites
  • Respect local culture and customs

Don’t:

  • Disrespect Chinese National Flag
  • Smoke indoors or outdoors where it is prohibited
  • Wear revealing clothing outside the beach area

Safety and Health

Safety in Fengxian

Fengxian is generally a safe place to visit, though visitors should remain vigilant and take standard safety precautions like not carrying large amounts of cash, staying in well-lit areas, and locking doors and windows at night. In case of an emergency, dial 110 or 120.

Health in Fengxian

Visitors should take standard preventative measures such as wearing sunscreen, drinking bottled water, and taking mosquito repellent due to the prevalence of mosquito borne diseases. Avoid consuming raw or uncooked food, especially seafood, and have preventive medication based on the need.

Getting to Fengxian: Transportation Options and Logistics

Fengxian is easily accessible from downtown Shanghai, and there are several transportation options available:

Airports Near Fengxian

  • Shanghai Pudong International Airport (PVG) - Located 30 km from Fengxian CBD, Pudong International provides flights to more than 50 countries and areas around the world.
  • Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport (SHA) - Located 35 km from Fengxian CBD, it handles domestic flights.

Bus Stations Near Fengxian

  • Shanghai South Bus Station - Located around 30 km from Fengxian CBD, various buses run here from other provinces.
  • Fengxian Bus Station - Located in the district center, provides buses to downtown Shanghai and other regional cities.

Other Options

  • Metro Line 5 - Stops at Fengxian Xincheng, providing access to central Shanghai
  • Taxi - Available around the city and hotels, for a higher fare but more convenience.

How to Get Around in Fengxian

Taxis and buses are the most popular forms of transportation within Fengxian, offering travelers easy access to various attractions. Additionally, visitors can choose to rent a bike to explore the area independently.

Where to Go Next from Fengxian: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Hangzhou - Home to the famous West Lake, it’s about 2 hours by train from Shanghai
  • Suzhou - Known for its classical gardens and silk factories, it takes about 1.5 hours by train from Shanghai
  • Shanghai Disney Resort - A popular destination for families, it takes around an hour from Fengxian

Conclusion

Fengxian is a gem of a place that perfectly blends culture with nature. With its beautiful beaches, serene parks, ancient temples, and museums, it offers something for everyone. Whether you are interested in exploring the rich culture, trying out delicious local cuisine, or just relaxing in nature, Fengxian is the perfect place for a memorable vacation.
