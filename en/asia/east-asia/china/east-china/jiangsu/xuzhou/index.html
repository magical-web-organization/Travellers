Xuzhou Travel Guide: Discover the Best of China’s East

Xuzhou is in Jiangsu, in East China. It is a city with a rich history, stunning natural scenery, and a vibrant food scene. This comprehensive travel guide will provide you with all the necessary information to make the most out of your visit to Xuzhou.

Introduction to Xuzhou

Xuzhou has been an important commercial and cultural center in China for more than 2000 years. Located in the northwest of Jiangsu province, Xuzhou is known for its extraordinary historical and cultural sites, abundant natural beauty, and a vibrant food scene.

  • Western Han Dynasty Terracotta Warriors and Horses Museum
  • Western Han Dynasty Terracotta Warriors and Horses Sculpture Museum

Xuzhou has a fantastic biking and hiking trail network that provides visitors with incredible views of the natural beauty. Some of the popular biking and hiking routes in Xuzhou are:

  • Yunlong Lake Hiking Trail

  • Baiyun Mountain Scenic Area Biking Trail

Best Things to Do in Xuzhou: Activities and Tours

  • Explore the underground Han tomb.
  • Take a walk around the Yunlong Lake scenic area.
  • Visit the Western Han Dynasty Terracotta Warriors and Horses Museum.
  • Relax at the Xuzhou Hot Spring Resort.
  • Take a dip in the Baiyun Mountain Hot Springs.
  • Go on a boat ride in the Weishan Lake Scenic Area.

Local Culture and Customs of Xuzhou

When visiting Xuzhou, it is important to understand the local culture and customs. Xuzhou has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors should take the time to explore the various historical and cultural sites in the city.

Best Time to Visit Xuzhou

The best time to visit Xuzhou is during the spring and autumn seasons when the climate is mild, and the natural scenery is beautiful. The temperature during these seasons ranges from 10°C to 25°C.

Do’s and Don’ts of Xuzhou

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Wear comfortable and appropriate clothing.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Be disrespectful to locals.
  • Wear revealing clothing.
  • Litter or damage historical and cultural sites.

Safety and Health

Safety in Xuzhou

Xuzhou is a safe city to visit, with low crime rates. However, visitors should take the usual precautions to keep their belongings safe and be aware of their surroundings.

Health in Xuzhou

The tap water in Xuzhou is not safe to drink, but bottled water is readily available. Visitors should also bring mosquito repellent, as there have been reported cases of dengue fever in the city.
