Introduction to Zhouzhuang

Zhouzhuang is a charming water town located in the Jiangsu province of China, known for its well-preserved architecture, cultural heritage, and scenic beauty. Dubbed as the “Venice of the East,” Zhouzhuang has flourished as a popular tourist destination and a glimpse of the old-world charm of China.

List of Places to Visit in Zhouzhuang

Zhouzhuang boasts several cultural, historical, and natural attractions that are worth exploring. Here are some of the must-visit sites in Zhouzhuang:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Zhouzhuang has a rich cultural heritage and a history dating back 900 years. Some of the most significant historical and cultural sites in the town are:

  • The Ancient Town Street
  • The Twin Bridges
  • Zhang House
  • Shen House
  • Ye Chucang’s ancestral hall

Natural Attractions of Zhouzhuang: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Zhouzhuang’s natural beauty is breathtaking, with serene waterways, lush greenery, and picturesque landscapes. Some of the best outdoor destinations and scenic spots to enjoy in Zhouzhuang are:

  • Zhouzhuang Water Town Boat Tour

  • Chengxu Taoist Temple
  • Quanfu Temple
  • Shuangqiao Scenic Spot
