Introduction to Yixing

Located in the province of Jiangsu in East China, Yixing is a charming city that offers the perfect mix of natural beauty and cultural experiences. Known for its historical landmarks, breathtaking mountains, and pottery traditions, Yixing has something to offer all travelers.

In this travel guide, we’ll explore the top attractions of Yixing, where to stay, what to eat, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Yixing

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Yixing Ceramics Museum: Founded in 1958, the Yixing Ceramics Museum is a must-visit destination for pottery lovers. The museum houses over 11,000 pieces of ceramics, including teapots, vases, and other sculptures.

  • Dingshu Ancient Town: Take a trip back in time by visiting Dingshu Ancient Town, a historical landmark that dates back to more than 1,500 years. Located on the banks of the Taihu Lake, this town offers a glimpse into China’s rich cultural heritage.

  • Yixing Bamboo Sea: Located on the outskirts of the city, the Yixing Bamboo Sea is a beautiful nature reserve that boasts stunning landscapes, walking trails, and bamboo forests.

Natural Attractions of Yixing: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Longbeishan: A scenic paradise that offers breathtaking views of the city and beyond, Mount Longbeishan is a must-visit destination for nature lovers. The mountain features several temples, waterfalls, and caves.

  • Taihu Lake National Wetland Park: The Taihu Lake National Wetland Park is a vast nature reserve that spans across nearly 1,350 acres. The park is home to over 200 species of birds, plants, and endangered animals.

  • Tianmu Lake Scenic Area: The Tianmu Lake Scenic Area is a beautiful natural resource that features a pristine lake, lush forests, and rolling hills. It is the perfect spot for hiking, trekking, and boating.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Yixing

  • Longbeishan Hiking Trail: This trail takes you on a scenic journey up Mount Longbeishan. The trail is challenging but worth it for the breathtaking views at the summit.

  • Taihu Lake Cycling Route: This cycling route takes you on a journey around Taihu lake, the third-largest freshwater lake in China. It’s a great way to see the scenic landscapes and immerse yourself in nature.

Best Things to Do in Yixing: Activities and Tours

  • Pottery-making classes: Yixing is famous for its pottery traditions, and taking a pottery-making class is a fun way to immerse yourself in the culture and learn a new skill.

  • Tea Tasting: Yixing is also known for its tea culture, and a tea-tasting tour is an excellent way to learn more about the tea-making process and enjoy some delicious brews.

  • Visit the Tanxi Mountain Hot Springs: Take a dip in the Tanxi Mountain Hot Springs to relax and rejuvenate after a long day of exploring.

Food and Drink Scene of Yixing

Must-Try Dishes

  • Yixing Bamboo Rice: This dish is made by cooking rice inside fresh bamboo, and it is flavored with pork, mushrooms, and other seasonings.

  • Wensi Tofu: This dish is made with tofu that has been fried and served in a spicy sauce.

  • Taihu Lake Fish: The Taihu Lake is famous for its freshwater fish, and there are plenty of delicious options to try in Yixing.

Local Beverages

  • Yixing Teapot Tea: Yixing Teapot Tea is a specialty tea that is brewed using a small clay teapot that is unique to the Yixing region.

  • Yellow Wine: Made from rice, yellow wine is a popular alcoholic drink in Yixing and surrounding regions.

Where to Stay in Yixing: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Yixing Royal Lake International Hotel: This luxurious hotel is located in the heart of Yixing and offers stunning lake views and top-notch amenities.

  • Yixing Manfulou Hotel: This budget-friendly hotel is located in the Dingshu Ancient Town, making it a perfect place to stay while exploring historical landmarks.

  • Tianmu Lake Moon Bay Hotel: This guesthouse is located near Tianmu Lake and offers a peaceful retreat for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

What to Buy in Yixing: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Yixing Teapots: Yixing Teapots are made of clay and are a staple of tea brewing in China. They are excellent souvenirs for tea lovers.

  • Yixing Pottery: Yixing Pottery is famous all over China and makes for a great conversation piece in any home.

Local Culture and Customs of Yixing

Yixing has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its ceramics and tea culture. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and traditional festivals are an essential part of local life.

Best Time to Visit Yixing

The best time to visit Yixing is during the spring and fall seasons. The weather is pleasant, and the landscapes are breathtaking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Yixing

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local food and tea.
  • Dress modestly when visiting temples and historical landmarks.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs of people without their permission.
  • Wear revealing clothing in public areas.
  • Touch or move pottery pieces in museums or stores without permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Yixing

Yixing is a generally safe city, and violent crimes are rare. Visitors should still exercise caution and be mindful of their surroundings, especially at night.

Health in Yixing

Visitors should be vaccinated against common illnesses like hepatitis A and B before traveling to Yixing. It’s also important to drink bottled or boiled water to avoid getting sick.

Getting to Yixing: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Yixing

The nearest airport to Yixing is the Sunan Shuofang International Airport, which is located about 80 kilometers from the city center.

Bus Stations Near Yixing

There are several bus stations in Yixing, including the Yixing Bus Station and the Dingshu Bus Station.

Other Options

Visitors can also take a train to Yixing Railway Station or hire a private car or taxi.

How to Get Around in Yixing

The best way to get around Yixing is by taxi or private car. Buses and bicycles are also available for those on a budget.

Where to Go Next from Yixing: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Suzhou: Located about 100 kilometers from Yixing, Suzhou is a picturesque canal town that is also known for its beautiful gardens and silk industry.

  • Hangzhou: Located about 150 kilometers from Yixing, Hangzhou is famous for its beautiful West Lake, tea plantations, and temples.

Conclusion

Yixing is a hidden gem of China. With its beautiful natural landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and delicious food and tea, Yixing offers something for everyone. Use this travel guide to plan your trip to Yixing and fall in love with this beautiful city.
