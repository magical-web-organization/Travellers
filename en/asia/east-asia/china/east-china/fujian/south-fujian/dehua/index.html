A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Dehua

Dehua is a beautiful county in South Fujian, known for its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. Located in Fujian province, Dehua is a popular destination for those who seek adventure and an opportunity to explore the beauty of China. In this comprehensive travel guide, you’ll discover the top attractions, things to do, where to stay, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Dehua

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Dehua is a county steeped in rich cultural history. With a rich collection of museums and historical places, a tour of these attractions is guaranteed to be both informative and entertaining.

  • Dehua Confucius Temple: This is a must-visit attraction for those interested in Chinese culture and history. Built-in 1211, the temple is the largest in Fujian and has a rich history dating back to the Ming Dynasty.
  • Dehua Ceramic Museum: This museum is located in the heart of Dehua and is dedicated to showcasing the exquisite ceramic art of the region. With a collection of over 100,000 ceramic pieces ranging from blue and white porcelain to celadon pottery, this museum is a must-visit for art lovers.
  • Manshan Temple: This temple is one of the most beautiful landmarks in Dehua. Built during the Tang Dynasty, it has beautiful architecture and is home to some of the most breathtaking sculptures in the region.

Natural Attractions of Dehua: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Dehua is home to some of the most stunning natural beauty in China. With some of the most magnificent outdoor adventures and scenic views, this region is a nature lover’s paradise.

  • Fangshan Scenic Area: Featuring impressive natural landscapes, stunning waterfalls, and beautiful lakes, Fangshan Scenic Area is a must-visit for those looking to explore the natural beauty of Dehua.
  • Beiyuan Scenic Area: This scenic area is home to a stunning waterfall that spans over 100 meters. Visitors can explore the surrounding forest, hike the trails, and enjoy the stunning views.
  • Xueyun Cave: Located in the mountains of Dehua County, Xueyun Cave is one of the most impressive karst caves in China.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dehua

Dehua boasts a wide variety of routes and trails that are perfect for hiking or biking. Whether you’re a seasoned pro or a beginner, there are many routes to choose from. Here are a few options:

  • Fangshan Hiking Trail: This trail is approximately 20 kilometers long and takes visitors through some of the most beautiful natural landscapes and scenic views in Dehua.
  • Jiankou Great Wall: This is undoubtedly one of the most famous hiking routes in Dehua. The trail winds through some of the most breathtaking landscapes and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Qianhuangping Forest Park: This park is home to a 15-kilometer cycling route that is perfect for those looking for a leisurely cycle through the beautiful scenery of Dehua.

Best Things to Do in Dehua: Activities and Tours

Dehua is filled with exciting activities and tours that are perfect for both solo and group travelers. Here are a few of the best things to do in Dehua:

  • Rafting on Beijiang River: This activity is perfect for those looking for an adrenaline rush. Visitors can raft on the pristine Beijiang River, taking in the stunning natural scenery.
  • Local Food Tour: Dehua is famous for its delicious food, and a food tour is the perfect way to experience the local cuisine. Sample some of the traditional dishes while learning about the region’s culinary history.
  • Ming Dynasty Ceramics Tour: This tour takes visitors through the Dehua Ceramic Museum, where they can take a closer look at some of the most stunning ceramic works from the Ming Dynasty.

Food and Drink Scene of Dehua

Must-Try Dishes

Dehua is known for its delicious cuisine, which features a wide range of dishes that are sure to tantalize your taste buds. Here are some of the must-try dishes in Dehua:

  • Buddha Jumps Over the Wall: A traditional stew made with over 30 ingredients, including abalone, sea cucumber, and ham.
  • Fish Soup with Rice Cake: A savory soup made with fresh fish and rice cake.
  • Dehua Fried Rice: A flavorful fried rice dish made with Shitake mushrooms, bamboo shoots, and other fresh vegetables.

Local Beverages

In addition to the delicious cuisine, Dehua is also home to some unique and flavorful beverages. Here are two of the best:

  • Qinghong Tea: A type of red tea produced in Dehua, Qinghong tea has a unique flavor that is sure to please tea lovers.
  • Pomelo Wine: A sweet and fruity wine made from local pomelos.

What to Buy in Dehua: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Local Culture and Customs of Dehua

Dehua is known for its vibrant culture and unique customs. Here are a few things to keep in mind during your trip:

  • Respect the elders: In Chinese culture, the elderly are highly respected. It’s important to show respect to the elders you meet during your trip.
  • Mind your chopsticks: When eating with chopsticks, be sure to use them correctly and avoid sticking them upright in your food.
  • Be polite: Politeness is highly valued in Chinese culture, so be sure to greet people with a smile and use polite language.

Best Time to Visit Dehua

The best time to visit Dehua is during the spring and autumn months when the weather is mild, and the scenery is at its most beautiful. The summer months can be hot and humid, while the winter months can be cold and rainy.

Safety and Health

Safety in Dehua

Dehua is generally a safe region, but it’s always a good idea to take precautions and be aware of your surroundings. Here are some tips to keep yourself safe during your trip:

  • Avoid walking alone at night: Stick to well-lit areas and avoid walking alone at night.
  • Watch your belongings: Keep your belongings close and be aware of your surroundings, especially in crowded areas.
  • Follow local laws and customs: Be sure to follow the local laws and customs, even if they are unfamiliar to you.

Health in Dehua

Dehua is a relatively safe region in terms of health. However, it’s important to take preventive measures to ensure you stay healthy during your trip. Here are some tips:

  • Stay hydrated: Dehydration can be a concern in hot weather, so be sure to drink plenty of water.
  • Protect yourself from the sun: The sun can be strong in Dehua, so be sure to wear sunscreen and a hat when you’re out in the sun.
  • Carry any necessary medication: If you have any pre-existing medical conditions, be sure to carry any necessary medication with you.

Getting to Dehua: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Dehua

The closest airport to Dehua is Xiamen Gaoqi International Airport, which is approximately 120 kilometers away. From the airport, visitors can take a bus, taxi, or rental car to reach Dehua.

Bus Stations Near Dehua

Dehua has several bus stations that offer transportation to nearby cities and attractions. Some of the most popular stations include Dehua Bus Station, Dehua North Bus Station, and Dehua South Bus Station.

Other Options

In addition to buses and taxis, visitors can also rent a car to explore the beauty of Dehua at their own pace. However, be aware that the roads in Dehua can be challenging, especially for those unfamiliar with the area.

How to Get Around in Dehua

Dehua is a relatively small region, making it easy to get around on foot, by bike, or by car. Taxis and buses are also available for those who prefer to use public transportation.

Where to Go Next from Dehua: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Dehua is located in a beautiful part of China and is surrounded by many other interesting destinations. Here are a few nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Xiamen: Located approximately 120 kilometers away, Xiamen is a beautiful coastal city with a rich cultural heritage.
  • Mount Wuyi: Located approximately 160 kilometers away, Mount Wuyi is a beautiful natural landscape filled with mountains, rivers, and waterfalls.
  • Quanzhou: Located approximately 60 kilometers away, Quanzhou is a historic city that features beautiful architecture and a rich cultural heritage.

Conclusion

Dehua is a beautiful region in China that is known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural beauty, and delicious cuisine. Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor enthusiast, or a foodie, Dehua has something for everyone. Plan your trip today to experience the beauty of this vibrant region.
