If you’re planning a trip to China, Fujian Tulou should definitely be on your list of must-visit destinations. Nestled in the inland region of Fujian, this UNESCO World Heritage site boasts stunning ancient earthen buildings, rich cultural heritage, scenic views, and outdoor adventures. In this comprehensive travel guide to Fujian Tulou, we’ll cover everything you need to know to plan an unforgettable trip, including top attractions, best things to do, where to stay, what to buy, and more.

Introduction to Fujian Tulou

Fujian Tulou is a group of over 46 ancient earthen buildings located in the mountainous region of Fujian, China. These unique buildings were built between the 12th and 20th centuries by the Hakka people, an ethnic group with a distinctive culture and language. Fujian Tulou is a remarkable example of Hakka architecture, featuring round or rectangular structures made of compressed earth, wood, and stone. These buildings were designed for communal living, with large families or clans living together in the same structure.

List of Places to Visit in Fujian Tulou

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Fujian Tulou Cluster

The Fujian Tulou Cluster is the main attraction in Fujian Tulou. You can explore the various earthen buildings, each with its unique design and history. Take a walking tour with a guide to learn more about the Hakka people’s culture and way of life.

  1. Yongding Earth Building Museum

The Yongding Earth Building Museum is an excellent place to learn about Fujian Tulou’s history and architecture. The museum provides informative exhibits showcasing the buildings’ intricate design and features.

Natural Attractions of Fujian Tulou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Chuxi Tulou Cluster

Chuxi Tulou Cluster is a group of earthen buildings located in the countryside. The buildings offer picturesque views of the surrounding hills, and it’s a great spot for nature lovers.

  1. Nanxi Tulou Cluster

The Nanxi Tulou Cluster is another place to experience the natural beauty of Fujian Tulou. The cluster is located near the mountains and water, adding a unique element to the traditional architecture.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fujian Tulou

If you’re an outdoor enthusiast, Fujian Tulou has plenty of hiking and biking routes to explore. Enjoy the scenic views and fresh air while exploring the stunning old buildings. Some recommended routes are:

  • Chuxi Tulou Cluster hiking trail
  • Gaobei Tulou cluster biking route

Best Things to Do in Fujian Tulou: Activities and Tours

  1. Try the traditional Hakka cuisine

Fujian Tulou is famous for its unique Hakka cuisine, which features an array of dishes prepared with locally-sourced ingredients. Try traditional dishes such as Yongding Hakka fried rice, earth chicken, and tea oil tofu.

  1. Visit a tea plantation

Fujian Tulou is known for its tea plantations, which offer guided tours to visitors. Learn the traditional tea-making process and try various types of tea.

Week-Long Itinerary for Fujian Tulou

If you have a week to spare, you can explore Fujian Tulou and the surrounding areas through the following itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrive in Xiamen and explore the city’s attractions.
  • Day 2-4: Visit Fujian Tulou and explore the various earthen buildings, museums, and scenic spots.
  • Day 5: Go on a tea plantation tour or try some Hakka cuisine.
  • Day 6-7: Visit the Wuyishan National Park and explore the scenic mountains.

Food and Drink Scene of Fujian Tulou

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Yongding Hakka fried rice

Yongding Hakka fried rice is a famous dish made with locally-sourced ingredients such as rice, pork, vegetables, and spices. It’s a favorite dish among the Hakka people and a must-try when visiting Fujian Tulou.

  1. Earth chicken

Earth Chicken is a traditional dish typically prepared during festivals or special occasions. It’s made by burying a whole chicken in the earth along with spices and vegetables for a unique, delicious taste.

Local Beverages

  1. Hakka Rice Wine

Hakka Rice Wine is a famous beverage made by fermenting sticky rice. It’s a popular drink during festivals and celebrations.

  1. Wuyi Rock Tea

Wuyi rock tea is a famous tea grown in the Wuyishan area. It’s a premium tea with a unique flavor and aroma and is considered one of the top three teas in China.

What to Buy in Fujian Tulou: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Tea

Fujian Tulou is famous for its tea plantations, so buying some Wuyi rock tea will make an excellent souvenir.

  1. Hakka Embroidery

Hakka Embroidery is a traditional handicraft with intricate designs and vibrant colors. It’s a great souvenir to bring back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Fujian Tulou

The Hakka people have a rich culture and history, and they cherish their traditions. Some of the cultural practices you can experience in Fujian Tulou include:

  • Traditional festivals, including the Festival of Lights and the Hakka Earth Building Festival.
  • Traditional crafts such as weaving, embroidery, and paper-cutting.

Best Time to Visit Fujian Tulou

The best time to visit Fujian Tulou is in the autumn from September-November, when the weather is cooler and mild. However, if you want to see the beautiful tea plantation in full bloom, visit in the spring from March to May.

Do’s and Don’ts of Fujian Tulou

Do:

  1. Respect the local culture and traditions.
  2. Dress appropriately when visiting religious sites or temples.
  3. Try the local cuisine

Don’t:

  1. Smoke or litter in public places.
  2. Take photos of people without their permission.
  3. Remove anything from the earthen buildings or their surroundings.

Safety and Health

Safety in Fujian Tulou

Fujian Tulou is a relatively safe destination with low crime rates. However, always exercise caution and take appropriate safety measures.

Health in Fujian Tulou

Make sure to have adequate travel insurance, including medical insurance. Drink only bottled water and use insect repellent to avoid insect bites and diseases.

Getting to Fujian Tulou: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Fujian Tulou

Xiamen Gaoqi International Airport is the nearest airport to Fujian Tulou, located about 120km away.

Bus Stations Near Fujian Tulou

There are several bus stations in Fujian Tulou, including Yongding Tulou Bus Station and Chengqi Tulou Bus Station.

Other Options

You can also hire a private car or join a tour group to visit Fujian Tulou.

How to Get Around in Fujian Tulou

You can get around Fujian Tulou by public buses or by renting a bike. Hiring a private car or hiring a driver is also an option.

Where to Go Next from Fujian Tulou: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Xiamen

Visit the beautiful coastal city of Xiamen, known for its vibrant nightlife, gourmet food scene, and beach resorts.

  1. Wuyishan

Explore the scenic Wuyishan National Park, a breathtaking destination known for its stunning mountains, lakes, and waterfalls.

Conclusion

Fujian Tulou is a hidden gem in China, offering a unique blend of architecture, culture, and natural beauty. Use this comprehensive travel guide to plan an unforgettable trip and discover all that this UNESCO World Heritage site has to offer.
