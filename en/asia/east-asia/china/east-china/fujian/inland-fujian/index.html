Introduction to Inland Fujian

Inland Fujian is located in the eastern part of China and is known for its stunning natural landscapes, abundance of cultural and historical sites, and delicious cuisine. The area is home to several stunning mountain ranges, picturesque lakes, and valleys that offer endless opportunities for outdoor activities.

Inland Fujian is also known for its rich cultural heritage, including its iconic tulou buildings, which have been declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site. These unique structures made from rammed earth are multi-story circular or rectangular buildings with interior courtyards and have been a way of living for generations, especially those in the Hakka ethnic minority.

Local Culture and Customs of Inland Fujian

Inland Fujian has a rich cultural heritage that is unique to the region. The Hakka people are the largest ethnic minority in the area and are known for their unique culture and customs. Visitors will be able to experience traditional Hakka music, dance, and architecture, which have been preserved for generations.

Best Time to Visit Inland Fujian

The best time to visit Inland Fujian is during the spring and autumn, from March to May and September to November. During these months, the weather is mild and comfortable, making it ideal for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Inland Fujian

Do:

  1. Respect local customs and traditions.
  2. Try the local cuisine.
  3. Be prepared for long drives and hikes.
  4. Purchase local products to help support the local economy.

Don’t:

  1. Wear revealing clothing when visiting temples or religious sites.
  2. Be late for appointments.
  3. Touch or lean on traditional buildings or temples.
  4. Litter or damage the natural attractions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Inland Fujian

Inland Fujian is a relatively safe place to visit, but visitors should be aware of any potential scams or pickpockets in tourist areas. Be cautious when hiking or cycling and wear proper protective gear.

Health in Inland Fujian

Visitors should get the necessary vaccinations before traveling to Inland Fujian. It is also important to observe proper hygiene practices and to drink bottled water.
