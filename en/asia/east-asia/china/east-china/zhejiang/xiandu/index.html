Introduction to Xiandu

Xiandu is a small town located in the mountainous region of Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province in East China. It is known for its breathtaking mountain views, pristine lake, ancient temples, and unique cultural heritage. The town has become a popular destination for tourists looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and immerse themselves in nature and culture.

List of Places to Visit in Xiandu

Xiandu has a variety of attractions for visitors to explore, ranging from cultural landmarks to natural wonders.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Xianhua Temple - This 1000-year-old temple, nestled in the scenic mountains, is a must-visit attraction for anyone interested in ancient Chinese architecture and religion.
  • Qiandao Lake - A stunning lake situated in the mountains, famous for its crystal-clear waters and untouched scenery.
  • Xianju National Geopark - Known for its unique geological formations, this national geopark is the perfect destination for those interested in natural history.

Natural Attractions of Xiandu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Taogong Cave - A popular hiking spot for tourists, Taogong Cave offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and natural scenery.
  • Bixiang Moutain - This mountain features several scenic spots, including Baizhu Peak, the sacred Bixia Temple, and the stunning natural scenery.
  • Daqinshan Provincial Forest Park - This park is a paradise for nature lovers and offers a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking, mountain biking, and camping.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Xiandu

Xiandu offers some of the best hiking and biking routes in China, thanks to its picturesque landscapes and well-maintained trails. Some of the best trails to explore include:

  • Yueliangwan Red Leaf Trail - A scenic and easy hike that offers unparalleled views of the surrounding mountains and stunning red leaves.
  • Huangtan Trail - A challenging trail perfect for experienced hikers, Huangtan Trail offers breathtaking views of the surrounding nature.
  • Jiangnan Dajian Mountain Trail - This trail features crystal-clear lakes and lush forests, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers.

Best Things to Do in Xiandu: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are plenty of other activities and tours that visitors can enjoy in Xiandu, including:

  • Boat tour of Qiandao Lake - Experience the crystal-clear waters and breathtaking scenery of Qiandao Lake with a boat tour.
  • Cultural tours - Discover the ancient history and culture of Xiandu with a guided tour of the town’s temples and historical landmarks.
  • Outdoor adventures - Take part in a variety of outdoor activities, including rock climbing, camping, and fishing, offered by the many natural attractions in Xiandu.

Local Culture and Customs of Xiandu

Xiandu has a unique cultural heritage that is influenced by Buddhism, Taoism, and traditional Chinese beliefs. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, including:

  • Covering your head when entering a temple.
  • Removing your shoes before entering a home or temple.
  • Refraining from taking photographs in temples or other sacred places.

Best Time to Visit Xiandu

The best time to visit Xiandu is between September and November when the weather is mild, and the leaves on the trees change color, making for a stunning natural spectacle.

Do’s and Don’ts of Xiandu

Do:

  • Try the local dishes and beverages.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Take part in outdoor adventures and cultural activities.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs in sacred places.
  • Wear inappropriate clothing when visiting temples or other cultural sites.

Safety and Health

Safety in Xiandu

Overall, Xiandu is a safe destination for visitors. However, visitors should be cautious when hiking or taking part in outdoor activities, as accidents can happen. It is also important to be cautious of pickpockets in busy areas.

Health in Xiandu

Visitors should take precautions against mosquito-borne illnesses, such as malaria and dengue fever, by wearing insect repellent and covering exposed skin. It is also recommended to drink only bottled water while in Xiandu.

How to Get Around in Xiandu

The best way to get around Xiandu is by foot or bicycle, as the town is relatively small and easy to navigate. Taxis and buses are also available for longer journeys or those with mobility issues.
