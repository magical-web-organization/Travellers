Introduction to Longyou County

Longyou County is a hidden gem in Zhejiang, East China, known for its stunning natural scenery and rich cultural history. This unspoiled region is perfect for travelers seeking an authentic experience away from the crowds.

List of Places to Visit in Longyou County

If you’re planning a trip to Longyou County, make sure to visit these top attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Longyou Grottoes: These ancient underground caves date back over 2,000 years and feature intricate carvings and Buddhist sculptures.
  • Longyou Museum: Learn about the history and culture of Longyou County at this top-rated museum featuring exhibits on art, archaeology, and local folklore.
  • Tongzi Bridge: This 600-year old bridge is an iconic landmark of Longyou County and a testament to ancient Chinese engineering.

Natural Attractions of Longyou County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Liuzhou Mountain National Forest Park: Explore this vast park featuring hiking trails and breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
  • Pujiang Scenic Area: This idyllic area offers scenic river views, waterfalls, and lush greenery.
  • Xinye Ancient Village: Take a step back in time and wander through the ancient village of Xinye, preserved for over 1,000 years.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Longyou County

Longyou County offers numerous trails for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Some must-visit routes include:

  • Liuzhou Mountain Trail: This trail takes you through the national forest park and offers stunning views of the mountains and valleys.
  • Pujiang River Trail: Follow the river for a scenic ride through the countryside.
  • Xinye Trail: Explore the ancient village and surrounding countryside on this easy-to-moderate hike.

Best Things to Do in Longyou County: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, Longyou County offers a range of activities and tours. Here are some must-try experiences:

  • Bamboo rafting on the Pujiang River: Drift along the tranquil waters of the river surrounded by stunning scenery.
  • Tea plantation visit: Learn about China’s tea-making traditions with a visit to a local plantation and tasting.
  • Traditional Chinese calligraphy class: Learn the art of calligraphy from a local expert and create your own beautiful work of art.

Week-Long Itinerary for Longyou County

If you have a week to spare, here is our recommended itinerary for a comprehensive trip to Longyou County:

  • Day 1-2: Explore the Longyou Grottoes and visit the Longyou Museum.
  • Day 3-4: Go hiking in Liuzhou Mountain National Forest Park and take a bamboo rafting trip on the Pujiang River.
  • Day 5-6: Visit Xinye Ancient Village and take a tea plantation tour.
  • Day 7: Relax and enjoy the scenic views at Pujiang Scenic Area.

Food and Drink Scene of Longyou County

Longyou County has a rich culinary heritage, with dishes that reflect the region’s agricultural heritage and traditional cooking techniques. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Longyou Beef Noodles: This hearty noodle soup is made with tender beef and flavorful broth.
  • Double-Cooked Pork: This savory dish features sliced pork belly stir-fried with vegetables.
  • Wansanxiao Rolls: These crispy rolls are filled with tender meat and vegetables and are a local favorite.

Local Beverages

  • Longjing Tea: This special green tea is grown in Zhejiang province and has a light, refreshing flavor.
  • Huangjiu: This traditional Chinese rice wine is a staple at local celebrations and festivities.

Where to Stay in Longyou County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Longyou County has a range of accommodation options to suit every traveler’s needs, from luxury hotels to budget-friendly hostels. Here are some of the best options:

  • Liuzhou International Hotel: This 5-star hotel offers comfortable rooms with modern amenities and a range of on-site dining options.
  • Pujiang Country House: This charming guesthouse features traditional Chinese architecture and is surrounded by peaceful gardens.
  • Longyou Youth Hostel: This budget-friendly hostel offers dorms and private rooms and is perfect for backpackers.

What to Buy in Longyou County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Longyou County is known for its handicrafts and local products, making it the perfect place to find unique souvenirs. Here are some of the best souvenirs to bring home:

  • Longyou Paper Umbrellas: These handcrafted umbrellas are made using traditional techniques and feature intricate designs.
  • Xinye Rice Wine: This local rice wine is a popular gift and souvenir.
  • Longyou Tofu: This savory snack is made using locally sourced soybeans and has a unique, satisfying flavor.

Local Culture and Customs of Longyou County

Longyou County has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its art, architecture, and traditional festivals. Some of the most important cultural customs to observe include:

  • Respecting elders: Chinese culture places a high value on respecting elders, so be sure to show deference to older people you meet.
  • Festivals: Longyou County celebrates a range of traditional festivals, including the Mid-Autumn Festival and the Lantern Festival.
  • Local dialect: The local dialect of Longyou County is distinct from other Chinese dialects, so learning a few words can help you integrate into the local culture.

Best Time to Visit Longyou County

The best time to visit Longyou County is in the spring and fall when the weather is mild and the mountains and countryside are at their most beautiful. Avoid the summer months when temperatures can soar and heavy rains are common.

Do’s and Don’ts of Longyou County

When traveling to Longyou County, it’s important to observe local customs and etiquette. Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Dress modestly and respectfully when visiting temples and other cultural attractions.
  • Try local foods and drinks to experience the region’s unique culinary heritage.
  • Learn a few basic phrases in Mandarin to show respect to the locals.

Don’t:

  • Touch or remove any artifacts or exhibits in museums or historical sites.
  • Disrespect local customs or traditions.
  • Take photographs of people without their permission.

Safety and Health

Longyou County is generally a safe destination for travelers, but it’s important to take basic precautions to ensure your trip is a success.

Safety in Longyou County

  • Carry a copy of your passport and important documents with you at all times.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid walking alone at night.
  • Follow local laws and regulations at all times.

Health in Longyou County

  • Drink only bottled or filtered water.
  • Be sure to wash your hands frequently to prevent the spread of illness.
  • Consider taking out travel insurance before your trip in case of emergency.

Getting to Longyou County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Longyou County is located about 200 kilometers southwest of Shanghai and is easily accessible by a range of transportation options.

Airports Near Longyou County

  • Hangzhou Xiaoshan International Airport: This is the closest major airport to Longyou County and offers flights from a range of domestic and international destinations.

Bus Stations Near Longyou County

  • Longyou Bus Station: This station provides direct connections to major cities in the region, including Hangzhou and Shanghai.

Other Options

  • Train: There are several train stations near Longyou County, with connections to major cities in China.
  • Car: It’s possible to rent a car or hire a private driver to explore the region at your own pace.

How to Get Around in Longyou County

Once you arrive in Longyou County, there are a range of transportation options to help you explore the region, including:

  • Bus: Local buses connect major attractions and cities in the region.
  • Taxi: Taxis are readily available in Longyou County and are an affordable option for short journeys.
  • Bike: Renting a bike is a great way to explore the countryside and scenic routes at your own pace.

Where to Go Next from Longyou County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have extra time to explore the region, here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Hangzhou: This picturesque city on the banks of West Lake is known for its stunning scenery and rich cultural heritage.
  • Huangshan: This UNESCO-listed mountain range is famous for its stunning views and unique rock formations.
  • Wuyi Mountains: These scenic mountains in Fujian Province are a UNESCO World Heritage Site and are known for their tea production and ancient rock carvings.
