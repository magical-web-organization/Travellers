Gouqi Island Travel Guide

Located in Zhejiang, East Asia, Gouqi Island is a picturesque destination in China that offers a stunning combination of natural beauty and cultural significance. This guide will take you through the best places to visit on the island, things to do, where to eat and stay, cultural customs, safety, and transportation options.

Introduction to Gouqi Island

Gouqi Island is one of the 394 islands that make up the Shengsi Archipelago. It is the largest and most populated island of the archipelago, with a population of over 10,000 people. Surrounded by clear blue waters, the island is a great place to relax, soak in the sun, and explore the natural beauty of East Asia.

List of Places to Visit in Gouqi Island

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Gouqi Island Folk Custom Museum: As the name suggests, this museum is a great place to learn about the cultural heritage and customs of the island’s inhabitants. You can see traditional clothing, artwork, and household items on display and learn how people lived on the island in the past.
  • Donglong Temple: This ancient temple was built in the 16th century and is a popular destination for visitors interested in Chinese Buddhism. You can see paintings, sculptures, and other religious artifacts at the temple.

Natural Attractions of Gouqi Island: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Nanwan Beach: This stunning beach is located on the southern part of the island and is a great spot for swimming, sunbathing, and water activities such as kayaking and snorkeling.
  • Huangqi Mountain: The highest peak on the island, Huangqi Mountain offers breathtaking views of the surrounding seascape. There are various hiking trails to reach to the mountain top.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Gouqi Island

One of the best ways to explore Gouqi Island is on foot or by bike. Here are some of the best trails to try:

  • Daqingshan Mountain: This trail leads up to the top of Daqingshan Mountain, which has some historic WWII ruins, such as tunnels and barracks, on the peak.
  • Beicang Island: This short hike takes you to an island off the coast of Gouqi Island. The trail provides amazing views of the crystal-clear water surrounding the island.

Best Things to Do in Gouqi Island: Activities and Tours

  • Boat tour around the island: A great way to discover the many beautiful coves and bays on the island. You can rent a private boat or join a group tour.
  • Snorkeling and Diving: Gouqi Island is home to some of the clearest and most beautiful waters in Asia, making it a popular spot for snorkeling and diving.
  • Island Cultural Tour: You can join one of the tours where you will explore and learn the local customs and art & craft.

Week-Long Itinerary for Gouqi Island

Here’s an itinerary for a week-long stay on the island:

  • Day 1-2: Explore the beaches and water activities around the island.
  • Day 3: Take a boat tour around the island and see the cliffs, caves, and other scenic spots.
  • Day 4: Hike Huangqi Mountain and experience the stunning views of the sea.
  • Day 5: Discover the local history and culture of the island.
  • Day 6: Rent a bicycle and explore the island from top to bottom.
  • Day 7: Relax and enjoy the island’s natural beauty.

Food and Drink Scene of Gouqi Island

Must-Try Dishes

  • Shengsi-style seafood: As Gouqi Island is surrounded by the sea, it is no surprise that seafood is the primary dish on the island. Try the traditional, fresh seafood dishes prepared in Shengsi style.
  • Braised pork: This signature dish is prepared with local raised pork and is slow-cooked in soy sauce and flavorful spices.

Local Beverages

  • Salty Tea: It is a traditional Chinese tea made by brewing tea leaves with salt and served hot.

Where to Stay in Gouqi Island: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options on Gouqi Island. Here are some of the best:

  • Shanshui Hotel: Located on the eastern coast of the island, it has beautiful views of the ocean and is outfitted with modern amenities.
  • Gouqi Island Guesthouse: A great option for travelers on a budget. Rooms have basic amenities and are affordable.

What to Buy in Gouqi Island: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Seafood: Buy local dried seafood like dried abalone, squid, and fish as a souvenir to bring back home.
  • Fishing gear: If you are an angler, stock up on fishing gear that’s affordable and widely available.

Local Culture and Customs of Gouqi Island

The local people on Gouqi Island are hospitable and friendly. Respect the Chinese culture and customs, learn a few Mandarin phrases, and always ask before taking photos of locals or participating in religious events.

Best Time to Visit Gouqi Island

The best time to visit Gouqi Island is from May to October when the weather is warm, and the island is bustling with events and activities. However, be prepared for crowds and higher hotel rates during peak seasons.

Do’s and Don’ts of Gouqi Island

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Wear comfortable shoes since the island is best explored on foot and bike.
  • Try the delicious local seafood and traditional dishes.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural environment.
  • Honeycomb coral is prohibited to be collected and/or purchased on this island.

Safety and Health

Safety in Gouqi Island

Gouqi Island is generally a safe place to visit, but it’s important to take necessary precautions:

  • Keep a copy of your passport and other important travel documents.
  • Be mindful of your belongings, esp. during touristy spots.
  • Always be aware of your surroundings, and if you encounter any suspicious activities or behavior, report it to the authorities immediately.

Health in Gouqi Island

Keep in mind that the water here is brackish, and it is not recommended to drink it directly. Always buy bottled water or boil the regular water before drinking. Do not eat street food from unlicensed vendors.

Getting to Gouqi Island: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Gouqi Island

  • Ningbo Lishe International Airport is the closest airport to Gouqi Island. Daily buses run from the airport to the island.

Bus Stations Near Gouqi Island

  • Shanghai South Bus Station: Buses depart daily from Shanghai to Gouqi Island. The journey is approximately 6 hours.
  • Ningbo South Bus Station: Buses depart daily from Ningbo to Gouqi Island. The journey is approximately 4 hours.

Other Options

  • Private transfer: Hire a private transfer to take you to Gouqi Island from the airport or coastal cities.
  • Ferry Boat: regularly operated between Gouqi Island and Shengshan Island of Shengsi by 30 minutes interval.

How to Get Around in Gouqi Island

Bicycles can be easily rented from hotel/guest houses. Also, there are taxis available on the island, however, they are in limited numbers.

Where to Go Next from Gouqi Island: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Shengsi Archipelago: The Shengsi Archipelago is home to over 394 islands, and hopping around is a great idea.
  • Putuo Island: Putuo Island is located in Zhejiang Province of China, and it is a renowned sacred Buddhist site.

Conclusion

Gouqi Island is a beautiful and fascinating destination that offers a combination of stunning natural beauty, rich history, and cultural heritage. Whether you’re interested in hiking, water activities, or immersing yourself in the island’s local culture, there is something for everyone. Be respectful of local customs and be cautious about safety and health precautions, and don’t forget to try the delicious seafood!
