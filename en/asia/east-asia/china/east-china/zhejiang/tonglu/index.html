Food and Drink Scene of Tonglu

Tonglu’s food culture is widely known for its uniqueness and variety. The region being geographically diverse, Tonglu offers diverse dishes to suit every palate. Here are a few must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Xinye Fried Rice – A local staple food of Tonglu made with pork, eggs, and rice fried in a wok with local vegetables and spices.
  2. Bian Rou Noodles – One of the famous regional specialties, Bian Rou Noodles, are thin noodles made of wheat flour and water with a rich variety of vegetables and pork meatballs.
  3. Huangshan Tofu – Made from soybeans and water from a natural spring, Huangshan Tofu is a type of soft tofu, commonly used in local dishes.

Local Beverages

  1. Tonglu Green Tea - One of the most traditional exports from Tonglu, green tea is consumed by locals and tourists alike.
  2. Qingyi Wine - A high-quality rice wine unique to Tonglu, Qingyi wine is known for its delicate, fragrant aroma and velvety texture.

Where to Stay in Tonglu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

The accommodation options in Tonglu range from luxurious hotels to comfortable guesthouses. Here are a few options to consider:

  1. Peach Blossom Resort – Located in the heart of Tonglu, the resort offers an idyllic hideaway with natural hot springs and scenic views of the mountains and valleys.
  2. Jinjiang Inn Tonglu – This hotel is an excellent option for those looking for a budget-friendly yet comfortable stay in Tonglu with all the basic amenities.
  3. Wuyue Guesthouse - The guesthouse gives its visitors a taste of traditional Chinese architecture with well-preserved buildings.

What to Buy in Tonglu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Tonglu offers a variety of local products for tourists to take back home. Here are a few popular ones:

  1. Tonglu Green Tea – As mentioned before, Tonglu green tea is famous for its fantastic taste and quality.
  2. Handicrafts and Traditional Artifacts – These can include items like embroidery, bamboo products, and other various arts and crafts made by local artisans.
  3. Bamboo Wares – Tonglu is known for its beautifully handcrafted bamboo wares, which make for unique gifts and souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Tonglu

Tonglu County is rich in customs and traditions, which have been preserved for centuries. The local people are known for their warmth and hospitality towards all visitors. Some of the customs and traditions of Tonglu include:

  • During the Dragon Boat Festival, people make and eat Zongzi, a traditional Chinese snack.
  • In early spring, locals host festivals to welcome the blooming Azalea flowers.
  • It is customary to serve tea to families and friends for socializing and building relationships.

Best Time to Visit Tonglu

The best time to visit Tonglu depends on the activities you want to indulge in. Here are the recommended periods for each activity:

  1. Hiking and Biking: Spring and autumn which generally between February - April and September - November, tend to be the best times to enjoy the most pleasant temperature with natural greenery.
  2. Rafting and Canyoning: During the summer months of June - August, the water levels are high and rapid.
  3. Hot Spring Bathing: Winter months are the best time for visiting hot springs to experience the famous natural hot springs of Tonglu.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tonglu

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and culture and learn about traditional practices.
  • Try the local delicacies and experience the unique food culture of Tonglu.
  • Engage in outdoor activities and indulge in the scenic beauty of Tonglu.

Don’t:

  • Litter in public places or parks.
  • Disrespect the local beliefs, culture, and customs.
  • Wear inappropriate clothing while visiting religious sites or historical places.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tonglu

Tonglu is generally a safe destination for tourists. However, tourists should always be careful and take necessary precautions while hiking, biking or indulge in outdoor activities.

Health in Tonglu

Tonglu’s hospitals have highly qualified doctors that provide quality healthcare. While it’s always advisable to carry a first-aid kit for minor cuts and bruises, one should check the availability of urgent care facilities while undertaking adventure activities.

Getting to Tonglu: Transportation Options and Logistics

There are various routes to get to Tonglu from different parts of China. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Tonglu

  1. Hangzhou Xiaoshan International Airport is the closest major airport.
  2. Yiwu International Airport is approximately 1 hour and 40 minutes drive from Tonglu.

Bus Stations Near Tonglu

  1. Hangzhou North Bus Station
  2. Hangzhou Long Distance South Coach Terminal.

Other Options

There are also speed train services from Shanghai or Hangzhou, which makes traveling reasonably fast and convenient.

How to Get Around in Tonglu

Tonglu has an excellent network of public transport, including buses, taxis, and tourist shuttles. Bicycles and scooters are also available on rent for tourists who wish to explore the town on their terms.

Where to Go Next from Tonglu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Tonglu’s neighboring towns and cities have their charm that is worth exploring. Here are some recommendations:

  1. Hangzhou: Hangzhou city is an hour’s drive away from Tonglu and has a lot to offer, from beautiful parks, temples to natural landscapes, especially the famous West Lake.
  2. Qiandao Lake: Located in the Jiangsu province, Qiandao Lake is famous for its crystal clear waters and lush greenery.
  3. Yellow Mountains: Known for its unique pine trees, hot springs, and steep granite peaks, the Yellow Mountains are a world-famous tourist destination.

Conclusion

Tonglu is an extraordinary destination which has something to offer for everyone. From cultural experiences to natural beauty, Tonglu provides a unique and exciting trip to its visitors. Get ready to indulge yourself in exciting outdoor adventures or explore the captivating local food culture – Tonglu never fails to surprise its visitors.
