Introduction to Yandangshan

Located in Zhejiang province, Yandangshan is a popular tourist destination for its stunning natural landscapes and rich cultural heritage. It’s named after the famous mountain range of Yandang, which covers an area of 450 square kilometers. The area was listed as a UNESCO Global Geopark in 2005 for its unique geological formations, biodiversity, and cultural heritage.

In this comprehensive travel guide to Yandangshan, we’ll explore some of the must-visit places, things to do, hiking and biking routes, food and drink scene, local culture, and tips for safe travels.

List of Places to Visit in Yandangshan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Baishuiyang Scenic Area: Known for its beautiful waterfalls, amazing rock formations, and ancient temples, this scenic area is a popular destination for tourists. It’s also a great spot for hiking and camping.

Lingfeng Mountain Scenic Area: This area is famous for its mountainous vistas, beautiful landscapes, and ancient temples. It’s a tranquil place to enjoy nature and meditate.

Yandang Mountain Scenic Area: This is the most famous tourist spot in Yandangshan. It has many beautiful natural landscapes, waterfalls, and hot springs. It’s also a popular destination for hiking and rock climbing.

Natural Attractions of Yandangshan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Jiangjun Gorge: This place is known for its crystal-clear river water, beautiful waterfalls, and natural forests. Visitors can go kayaking, river rafting, or just relax by the river.

Nanxi River: The Nanxi River stretches 172 kilometers from Wuyi County to Yandangshan. This area is known for its clear water, beautiful mountains, and lush forests. Visitors can go bamboo rafting, cliff climbing, or hiking.

Yao Dong Scenic Area: The Yao Dong scenic area is located in the southern part of Yandangshan. This area is known for its unique karst landscape, underground rivers, waterfalls, and ancient caves. It’s also a popular destination for hiking and camping.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Yandangshan

Yandang Mountain: This is the most popular hiking route in Yandangshan. It takes about 6 to 8 hours to hike to the summit of Yandang Mountain. The trail is considered moderately difficult, and visitors can enjoy beautiful landscapes and waterfalls along the way.

Lingfeng Mountain: This hike is perfect for those who love nature and hiking. The trail is not too difficult, and visitors can enjoy beautiful landscapes and ancient temples along the way.

Nanxi River: This area has many scenic hiking trails. Visitors can explore the beautiful mountains, waterfalls, and caves along the way.

Best Things to Do in Yandangshan: Activities and Tours

Rock Climbing: Yandangshan is a popular destination for rock climbing. The cliffs at the Yandang Mountain Scenic Area are ideal for climbing. Visitors can rent equipment and hire guides for this activity.

Bamboo Rafting: Visitors can enjoy a peaceful ride on the Nanxi River on a bamboo raft. This is a relaxing way to enjoy the beautiful scenery of Yandangshan.

Hot Springs: There are many hot springs in Yandangshan. Visitors can relax in the warm mineral water and enjoy the beautiful views.

Food and Drink Scene of Yandangshan

Must-Try Dishes

Bamboo shoots: Yandangshan is famous for its juicy and tender bamboo shoots. Visitors can enjoy this fresh produce in a variety of dishes, such as stir-fry, soup or salad.

Fried fish: Yandangshan’s streams and rivers are rich with fish. Fried fish is a local delicacy and it’s usually served with some soy sauce, salt, and ginger.

Local Beverages

Yandangshan tea: The mountainous terrain provides the perfect environment for tea farming. The tea produced here is famous for its rich aroma and unique taste.

Rice wine: This is a locally produced alcoholic beverage made from fermented rice. It’s a great way to experience the local culture.

Where to Stay in Yandangshan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Yandangshan Hotel: This is a luxury hotel located in the Yandang Mountain Scenic Area. Visitors can enjoy beautiful views and easy access to hiking trails.

Yandangshan Hot Springs Resort: This resort offers hot springs, spa treatments, and other leisure activities. It’s a great place to relax and rejuvenate.

Jiulongju Hostel: This budget-friendly hostel offers comfortable accommodation and a social atmosphere. It’s located in Yao Dong Scenic Area.

What to Buy in Yandangshan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Tea: Yandangshan tea is a great souvenir to bring home. Visitors can buy tea leaves, teapots, or tea cups.

Bamboo Crafts: The abundant bamboo resources in Yandangshan are used to make many different types of bamboo crafts, including baskets, hats, and mats.

Local Culture and Customs of Yandangshan

Yandangshan is a culturally rich area that has preserved many ancient traditions. The local people are known for their hospitality and kindness. Visitors should be respectful towards the local culture and customs.

Best Time to Visit Yandangshan

The best time to visit Yandangshan is from March to November when the weather is mild and pleasant. The summer months of July and August can be very hot, so it’s best to avoid this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Yandangshan

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Dress appropriately in temples and other religious sites.
  • Carry cash as credit cards are not widely accepted.

Don’t:

  • Take photos through the windows in people’s homes.
  • Litter in public areas.
  • Touch or remove any artifacts from historical sites or museums.

Safety and Health

Safety in Yandangshan

Yandangshan is a safe place to visit, but visitors should take basic precautions to protect themselves from natural hazards such as falls while hiking and rock climbing.

Health in Yandangshan

It’s important to drink bottled water and avoid tap water, which may be contaminated. Visitors should also bring insect repellent and wear long-sleeved clothes to avoid mosquito-borne diseases.

Getting to Yandangshan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Yandangshan

The nearest airport to Yandangshan is Taizhou Luqiao Airport, which is about 85 kilometers away. From there, visitors can take a taxi or bus to Yandangshan.

Bus Stations Near Yandangshan

There are several bus stations in the area, including Yandangshan Bus Station, Yandangshan South Bus Station, and Yueqing Bus Station. Visitors can take buses from major cities in Zhejiang Province.

Other Options

There are no train stations in Yandangshan, visitors can take a train to Wenzhou Railway Station or Taizhou Railway Station, and then take a bus to Yandangshan.

How to Get Around in Yandangshan

The best way to get around Yandangshan is by bus or taxi. Buses are a cost-effective and convenient way to explore the area. Taxis are more expensive, but they offer more comfort and privacy.

Where to Go Next from Yandangshan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Wenzhou City

Wenzhou is a major city in Zhejiang Province. It’s known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful landscapes, and delicious food. Visitors can explore the ancient temples, museums, and natural scenic spots.

Mount Putuo

Mount Putuo is one of the four famous Buddhist mountains in China. It’s located in Putuo District, Zhoushan City, Zhejiang Province. Visitors can explore the ancient temples, hike the trails, and enjoy the beautiful ocean views.

Conclusion

Yandangshan is a hidden gem in East China. It’s a great destination for those who love hiking, outdoor adventures, and exploring ancient cultural sites. It’s also a food lover’s paradise, where visitors can taste delicious local cuisine and enjoy hot springs. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can plan your trip to Yandangshan with confidence and enjoy all the best the area has to offer.
