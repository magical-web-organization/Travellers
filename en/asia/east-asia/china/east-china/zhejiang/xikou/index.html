Introduction to Xikou

Nestled in the lush hills of Zhejiang, a province in East China lies the beautiful and historic town of Xikou. This town is rich in culture, natural beauty, and historical significance, making it an ideal destination for travelers seeking to experience the best of China.

Xikou is also famous for its outdoor adventures, local cuisine, and traditional customs, as well as its hospitable and friendly locals. Whether you are traveling solo, with your family or friends, Xikou guarantees a memorable experience you will cherish forever.

In this comprehensive guide to Xikou, we will cover everything you need to know before visiting, including places to stay and visit, what to eat, where to go, and more. Read on for a detailed breakdown of Xikou, and everything this amazing town has to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Xikou

Xikou has many places to visit, including culturally significant attractions, natural landscapes, and outdoor adventure sites. Below is a list of places you must visit during your trip to Xikou.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai Memorial Hall: This museum is a celebration of one of the four great folk love stories of China. It’s a beautifully designed building that showcases the love story of Liang Shanbo and Zhu Yingtai. You will learn about the rich culture, and historic significance of their love story.

  2. Yinzhou Wanshou Palace: This impressive palace displays the famous portrait of Emperor Qianlong of the Qing dynasty. The palace is strikingly beautiful, and it is one of the essential historical buildings in Xikou.

  3. Fenghuang Mountain Scenic Area and the National Forest Park: Known for its natural beauty, the park offers stunning views of the lush forest and impressive waterfalls. There are several scenic trails in the area where you can hike, bike, or walk and enjoy the scenic beauty of the park.

  4. Wangyang Lake Scenic Area: This fantastic lake offers a unique blend of natural and cultural scenery. You will love the spectacular views of the lake, the surrounding mountains, and the numerous historic architecture that surrounds the area.

Natural Attractions of Xikou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Fenghua Xikou Xuedou Mountain Scenic Area: This park is known for its fabulous natural beauty – the fresh green of the trees, the serene lakes, and the stunning waterfalls are sure to leave you in awe. You can explore the park’s hiking trails, bike paths, and enjoy the adrenaline rush of biking downhill.

  2. Jiangnan Water Town: This charming water town has preserved its ancient architecture and history, making it a lovely tourist spot. The town is surrounded by beautiful waterways, cobblestoned pathways, and old-fashioned shops.

  3. Xikou Ancient Street: As the name suggests, this street is an excellent reflection of Xikou’s past. It is lined with shops that sell crafts and souvenirs, local food and beverages, traditional embroidery and art.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Xikou

Xikou has plenty of hiking and biking trails for adventure seekers. From scenic mountain routes to idyllic paths that wind through the town, there is something for everyone.

  1. Fengyang Mountain: This is a popular hiking trail that offers spectacular views of Xikou. Hikers can get breathtaking views of the town and the surrounding forests.

  2. Xikou Ancient Trail: An ideal route for cycling, walking or hiking, the Xikou Ancient Trail offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains, ancient structures, and the serene riverside environment.

Best Things to Do in Xikou: Activities and Tours

Xikou has plenty of exciting things to do, and whether you are traveling with your family or friends, or even solo, there is always something for everyone to enjoy. Below are some of the best activities and tours in Xikou.

  1. Camping in Fengyang Mountain: There is no better way to experience the natural beauty of Xikou than by camping. While at Fengyang Mountain, you can pitch a tent and spend a night under the stars, creating unforgettable memories.

  2. River Rafting: This is an exciting and thrilling outdoor activity that you can enjoy in Xikou. Travelers can raft down the river, navigating the wild and thrashing rapids.

  3. Night Walk in Xikou: A stroll through the streets of Xikou at night is a romantic, haunting, and thrilling experience. You can walk through the streets lined with lanterns, have a hot pot and enjoy the warm, hospitable locals.

Food and Drink Scene of Xikou

The food in Xikou is known for its flavors, homely atmosphere, and traditional-style cooking. Below is a list of some local dishes and beverages that you must try during your visit to Xikou.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Xikou Dumplings: These are a local delicacy, served with a variety of fillings, including meat, seafood, and vegetables. They are boiled or fried and served with different dipping sauces.

  2. Hot Pot: A popular Chinese dish, hot-pot consists of a simmering pot of broth, loaded with a variety of meats, vegetables, tofu, and seafood, which are then dipped in the broth to cook and then eaten.

Local Beverages

  1. Famous Xikou Wine: This is a refreshing, fruity wine that is known to have some medicinal properties. The wine has a pleasant flavor with an aromatic taste of herbs and fruit.

  2. Tea: Xikou is known for its exceptional tea culture, and there are numerous local tea plantations, where you can sample some of the best green tea and flower tea in China.

Where to Stay in Xikou: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Xikou has a vast range of hospitality options to choose from, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Below are some of the best places to stay in Xikou.

  1. Xikou Tianxiangju Guesthouse: This guesthouse is located close to the main attractions in Xikou, and it’s an ideal place for travelers who want to experience the local culture. The rooms are spacious, clean, and comfortable, and the staff is warm and hospitable.

  2. Juntian Hotel: This hotel is located in the heart of Xikou, and it offers great value for your money. The hotel’s rooms are clean, comfortable, and equipped with modern facilities, including Wi-Fi and air conditioning.

  3. Zhejiang Xikou Hotel: This is a luxury hotel that is ideal for travelers who want to experience the best of comfort and luxury. The hotel has spacious rooms, great amenities, and excellent customer service.

What to Buy in Xikou: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Xikou is known for its unique and beautiful souvenirs, traditional handicrafts, and products that make great gifts for friends and family back home. Below are some of the items you should consider buying in Xikou.

  1. Xikou Embroidery: This is one of the traditional crafts of Xikou, where artisans create intricate patterns on fabric using fine needles.

  2. Xikou Tea: This tea is known for its fresh, aromatic flavor, and it’s an ideal purchase for tea lovers.

  3. Xikou Snacks: These are a range of local snacks and desserts that are savory, sweet, and delicious.

Local Culture and Customs of Xikou

Xikou has a rich culture and traditional customs, and it’s essential to know some of them before visiting. Below are some of the cultural practices that will help you understand the locals better.

  1. Visit Temples and Museums: The people of Xikou are known for their love of museums, temples, and ancient sites. Before your trip, research some of the essential museums in the area, and make sure you visit them.

  2. Speak Mandarin: As with most parts of China, Mandarin is the most widely spoken language in Xikou. Some locals may know how to speak English, but the ability to communicate in Mandarin is critical.

Best Time to Visit Xikou

The best time to visit Xikou is during the spring and autumn seasons. During these seasons, the weather is mild, and the scenery is breathtaking, making it an ideal time to explore the town and its surrounding areas.

Do’s and Don’ts of Xikou

Do:

  1. Embrace the local customs and culture.
  2. Try the local food and beverages.
  3. Bring comfortable shoes for walking and hiking.

Don’t:

  1. Expect to find many English speakers.
  2. Ignore the traditional customs of the locals.
  3. Litter in public areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Xikou

Xikou is a safe town, and crime rates are low. However, travelers must take care of their belongings, especially in crowded areas, and watch out for pickpockets.

Health in Xikou

Xikou is a clean and healthy town. However, travelers are advised to drink bottled water, and carry any necessary medication with them.

Getting to Xikou: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Xikou

The nearest airport to Xikou is Ningbo Lishe International Airport, which is about 60 kilometers away. From the airport, you can take a taxi or a bus to Xikou.

Bus Stations Near Xikou

Xikou has no bus station, but visitors can take a bus or shuttle from central Ningbo to Xikou.

Other Options

Another option is taking high-speed trains. From Shanghai, one can take a fast train that will get them to Xikou within four hours.

How to Get Around in Xikou

Xikou is a small town, and the best way to explore it is on foot. However, for visitors who prefer other modes of transportation, there are taxis, buses, bike rentals, and even tuk-tuks.

Where to Go Next from Xikou: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

From Xikou, you can explore many other exciting destinations in China. Some of the popular destinations include Ningbo, Hangzhou, and Shanghai.

Conclusion

Xikou is a beautiful town in China known for its history, culture, natural beauty, outdoor adventures, local cuisine, and traditional customs. With this comprehensive guide, you have everything you need to know before exploring Xikou. From hiking trails, outdoor adventures, and cultural attractions to culinary delights and accommodations, Xikou is a town you must visit when in China. So pack your bags, put on your comfortable shoes, and embark on a memorable journey to Xikou.
