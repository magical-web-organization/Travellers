Introduction to Dongyang

Dongyang is a small city located in the northern region of Zhejiang province in East China. Its rich history dates back over 1,000 years, and it is known for its beautiful natural scenery, cultural attractions, and traditional arts and crafts. Dongyang has been voted as one of the top 10 most beautiful countryside destinations in China.

List of Places to Visit in Dongyang

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Dongyang Woodcarving Museum

The Dongyang Woodcarving Museum is one of the largest museums of its kind in China. It houses a collection of more than 10,000 pieces of woodcarving artwork, including furniture, screens, and other ornamental objects. The museum showcases the unique and intricate techniques used in Dongyang’s traditional woodcarving art.

Shenqi Memorial Hall

The Shenqi Memorial Hall is dedicated to the famous artist and calligrapher, Shenqi. The hall houses more than 100 of his original pieces of calligraphy and artwork. Visitors can also learn about the artist’s life and contributions to culture and art in Zhejiang.

Yuehu Park

Yuehu Park is a beautiful lake located in the center of Dongyang city. Visitors can take a stroll along the lake, enjoy the scenic views, and take part in various recreational activities, such as paddle-boating and fishing.

Natural Attractions of Dongyang: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Yaojiang River Scenic Area

The Yaojiang River Scenic Area covers 33.7 square kilometers and includes the famous Mao Mountain, which is covered in lush forests and streams. Visitors can hike along the mountainous trails or take a boat ride down the Yaojiang River to explore the beautiful scenery.

Shicai Mountain Scenic Area

The Shicai Mountain Scenic Area is a picturesque area covered in greenery and surrounded by natural beauty. Visitors can take a cable car up to the top of the mountain, where they can enjoy a panoramic view of Dongyang.

Tianning Temple Forest Park

The Tianning Temple Forest Park is a serene natural enclave located in the outskirts of the city. Visitors can take a peaceful walk through the park’s bamboo forest and enjoy the tranquility and beauty of the natural landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dongyang

Mao Mountain Hiking Trail

The Mao Mountain Hiking Trail is a beautiful and challenging hike located in the Yaojiang River Scenic Area. The trail spans seven kilometers and takes visitors through forests, streams, and beautiful scenery.

Shicai Mountain Biking Trail

The Shicai Mountain Biking Trail is a challenging and scenic route that takes visitors through stunning views of Dongyang’s natural beauty. The trail is roughly 15 kilometers long and provides a great workout for experienced bikers.

Best Things to Do in Dongyang: Activities and Tours

Dongyang Woodcarving Workshop

Visitors can take part in a traditional Dongyang woodcarving workshop to learn more about the art and techniques used in woodcarving. Participants can create their own woodcarving pieces with the help of an expert woodcarver.

Yaojiang River Sightseeing Cruise

The Yaojiang River Sightseeing Cruise takes visitors down the beautiful river, providing them with stunning views of Dongyang’s natural beauty along the way.

Shenqi Calligraphy and Art Class

The Shenqi Calligraphy and Art Class provides visitors with the opportunity to learn more about traditional Chinese calligraphy and painting. Participants can create their own calligraphy or art pieces under the guidance of a professional artist.

Food and Drink Scene of Dongyang

Must-Try Dishes

Dongyang Braised Pork

Dongyang Braised Pork is a traditional dish that uses soy sauce, sugar, and spices to create a delicious and savory flavor. It is served with rice and vegetables.

Dongyang Brown Sugar Cake

Dongyang Brown Sugar Cake is a famous local dessert made with brown sugar, peanut powder, and sesame seeds.

Local Beverages

Dongyang Rice Wine

Dongyang Rice Wine is a famous local alcoholic drink made from fermented rice. It has a strong fragrance and a subtle flavor.

Jasmine Tea

Jasmine Tea is a popular drink in Dongyang and is a great way to relax and wind down after a long day of exploring the city.

Where to Stay in Dongyang: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Jingdu Hotel

The Jingdu Hotel is a luxury hotel located in the center of Dongyang. The hotel offers world-class amenities, including a rooftop pool, fitness center, and a variety of restaurants and bars.

Dazhong Guesthouse

The Dazhong Guesthouse is a budget-friendly option located in the heart of Dongyang. It offers simple yet comfortable rooms and is a great option for travelers on a tight budget.

What to Buy in Dongyang: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Dongyang Woodcarvings

Dongyang Woodcarvings are famous throughout China for their intricate and beautiful designs. Visitors can purchase woodcarving pieces in various shapes and sizes at local markets and stores.

Brown Sugar Cake

Dongyang Brown Sugar Cake is a popular local dessert and makes for a great souvenir to take home and share with friends and family.

Local Culture and Customs of Dongyang

One of the most unique aspects of Dongyang is its traditional art and craft scene. The city is known for its intricate woodcarvings, calligraphy, and painting. Visitors can take part in workshops and classes to learn more about these traditional crafts and take home their own handmade pieces as souvenirs.

Best Time to Visit Dongyang

The best time to visit Dongyang is during the spring and fall months, when temperatures are mild and the scenery is at its most beautiful. Summers can be humid and hot, while winters can be chilly and sometimes snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Dongyang

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local cuisine and engage with the locals.
  • Take part in traditional art and craft activities.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local traditions and beliefs.
  • Overpay for goods or services at the local markets.

Safety and Health

Safety in Dongyang

Dongyang is a safe city for travelers. However, it is always important to take necessary precautions and be aware of your surroundings.

Health in Dongyang

Travelers should be aware of the air quality in Dongyang, as it can be poor at times. It’s important to wear a mask when the air quality is low and to stay hydrated and healthy during your stay in the city.

Getting to Dongyang: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Dongyang

The nearest airport to Dongyang is the Yiwu International Airport, which is located about 50 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Dongyang

The Dongyang Bus Station is located in the city center and provides bus services to various locations in Zhejiang province and other parts of China.

Other Options

There are also train services available, with the nearest train station located in Yiwu.

How to Get Around in Dongyang

The most convenient ways to get around Dongyang are by taxi or by rented car. Travelers can also take public transportation, such as buses and trains, which are affordable and available throughout the city.

Where to Go Next from Dongyang: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Hangzhou

Hangzhou is a popular city located about 100 kilometers from Dongyang. It is known for its beautiful West Lake and for being an important center for traditional Chinese medicine and culture.

Shanghai

Shanghai is a bustling city located about 317 kilometers from Dongyang. It is known for its vibrant nightlife, modern architecture, and rich cultural and historical attractions.

Conclusion

Dongyang is a hidden gem in Zhejiang province, with a rich cultural history, beautiful natural scenery, and a unique art and craft scene. Visitors can enjoy traditional workshops, scenic hikes, and delicious local cuisine, all while taking in the city’s beauty and charm. Plan your trip to Dongyang today and experience all that this amazing city has to offer.
