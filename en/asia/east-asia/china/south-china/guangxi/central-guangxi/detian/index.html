Introduction to Detian

Detian is a picturesque small town located in Central Guangxi, China. Known for its captivating natural scenery, rich culture and history, and friendly locals, Detian makes for a perfect destination for travelers who prefer off-the-beaten-path experiences.

List of Places to Visit in Detian

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums, and Historical Places

  • Detian Scenic Spot: A UNESCO World Heritage site that boasts one of the largest waterfalls in Asia
  • Xialaozuo Scenic Area: A serene and peaceful park with breathtaking scenery and historical landmarks
  • Tongdeng Ancient Town: A well-preserved ancient town that offers a glimpse of Detian’s fascinating history and culture
  • Guangxi Detian Musing: A museum dedicated to showcasing the rich history and culture of Detian and Guangxi

Natural Attractions of Detian: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Detian Waterfall: Known for its stunning beauty and power, this waterfall is a must-visit attraction in Detian
  • Mingshi Scenic Spot: A gorgeous nature reserve with lush greenery, winding rivers, and stunning panoramic views
  • Fenglin Gorge: A serene and untouched mountainous area that offers visitors a chance to hike and trek in unspoiled nature
  • Tongling Gorge: A deep ravine with remarkably high and steep cliffs that provide stunning views of the surrounding landscape

Hiking/Biking Routes of Detian

  • Detian Waterfall Loop: A popular hiking trail that offers stunning views of the Detian Waterfall and the surrounding landscape
  • Mingshi Nature Reserve Hike: A challenging and rewarding hiking trail that offers incredible panoramic views of the reserve’s stunning scenery
  • Fenglin Gorge Trek: A moderate hiking trail that allows visitors to explore the untouched beauty of the gorge
  • Tongling Gorge Hike: A difficult hiking trail that offers visitors a chance to challenge themselves while taking in the awe-inspiring views of the gorge

Best Things to Do in Detian: Activities and Tours

  • Detian Waterfall Cruise: A relaxing and scenic boat ride that offers visitors a unique perspective of the majestic waterfall
  • Detian Riverside Fishing and Picnic: A fun and refreshing outdoor activity ideal for families and groups
  • Caving Adventure: Explore the hidden caves within the nearby mountains with an experienced guide
  • Ethnic Minority Village Visit: immerse yourself in the captivating culture of Detian’s ethnic minorities by visiting their local settlements and communities

Food and Drink Scene of Detian

Must-Try Dishes

  • Liuzhou Noodle Soup: a popular noodle dish that originated in Liuzhou, just south of Detian
  • Yellow Croaker in Sweet and Sour Sauce: a delicious fish dish native to Guangxi and southern China
  • Oil Tea: a unique drink made from nuts and tea leaves, that is often consumed as a snack

Local Beverages

  • Rice Wine: a traditional alcoholic drink that is commonly consumed in Guangxi and China
  • Herbal Tea: a drink made from various local herbs, that is believed to have medicinal properties

Where to Stay in Detian: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Feeling Tourist Hostel: Twin rooms with en-suite bathrooms and air conditioning, offering a communal kitchen and laundry facilities
  • Detian Waterfall Resort: featuring a beautiful outdoor pool, spa facilities, and rooms with air conditioning and flat-screen TVs
  • Detian Paiyunlou Hotel: a budget-friendly option that offers simple yet clean rooms and a convenient location close to many attractions

What to Buy in Detian: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Detian Tea: made from a blend of rare and fragrant tea leaves grown in Guangxi, this tea is a great souvenir to bring back for family and friends
  • Medicinal Herbs: Guangxi is known for its rich herbal resources, and Detian is home to many pharmacies that sell traditional chinese medicines
  • Embroidered Crafts: Detian is famous for its embroidery techniques, and many local artisans create beautiful handcrafted pieces that make great mementos

Local Culture and Customs of Detian

Detian boasts an incredibly rich culture and history, which is deeply ingrained in the daily life of its people. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, and be sure to ask before taking photos or entering buildings out of respect.

Best Time to Visit Detian

The best time to visit Detian is during the spring and autumn months (March to May, and September to November), when the weather is mild and the scenery is at its most beautiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of Detian

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Try the local food and drink
  • Wear comfortable shoes and clothing when exploring outdoors

Don’t:

  • Touch or remove objects from historical and cultural sites
  • Litter or damage natural areas
  • Wear revealing clothing in public spaces

Safety and Health

Safety in Detian

Detian is generally a safe destination with low crime rates. However, visitors should take normal precautions like avoiding travelling alone at night and guarding against pickpockets in crowded areas.

Health in Detian

Visitors should drink bottled water and avoid uncooked and unpeeled fruits and vegetables to prevent potential illness. Medical services are available in Detian, but quality can vary depending on the facility.

Getting to Detian: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Detian

  • Nanning Wuxu International Airport (NNN): Approximately 185 kilometers from Detian

Bus Stations Near Detian

  • Detian Bus Station: Located in the town center, offering buses to various cities in Guangxi and nearby provinces
  • Fangcheng Bus Station: Located in the outskirts of Fangcheng County, and accessible via buses from Detian

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Detian via private car, motorcycle, or bicycle, and enjoy a scenic road trip through the beautiful countryside of Guangxi.

How to Get Around in Detian

The best way to get around Detian is by motorbike, as it offers the most freedom and flexibility to explore the beautiful landscape. Taxis, buses, and private tours are also available.

Where to Go Next from Detian: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Guangxi Beibu Gulf of the South China Sea: A coastal destination known for its beautiful beaches and scenery located approximately 100 km from Detian
  • Yangshuo: A popular tourist destination located approximately 220 km from Detian, known for its stunning scenery, outdoor activities, and relaxed vibe

Conclusion

Detian, with its breathtaking natural scenery, rich culture and history, and friendly locals, is an incredible destination for travelers looking for authentic and off-the-beaten-path experiences. So, why not plan your trip today and explore all this beautiful town has to offer?
