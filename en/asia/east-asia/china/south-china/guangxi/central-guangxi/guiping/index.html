Introduction to Guiping

Guiping is a small city located in Central Guangxi, in South China. Boasting rich cultural heritage and stunning natural landscapes, Guiping is a hidden gem in China that is worth exploring. It is a popular destination for eco-tourism, outdoor activities, and cultural experiences.

List of Places to Visit in Guiping

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Guiping has a rich cultural heritage with many museums and historical places to visit. Some must-visit places in Guiping are:

  • Guiping Museum: This museum showcases the history and culture of Guiping, including artifacts from the Tang and Song dynasties.
  • Wuxuan Lianhua Cave: This ancient cave dates back to the Tang Dynasty and is a popular site to explore.
  • Old Street in Guiping: Take a walk around Old Street to witness the charming buildings and shops, featuring the traditional architecture of Guiping.

Natural Attractions of Guiping: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Guiping is known for its stunning natural landscapes, where outdoor enthusiasts can head out for an adventure. Some of the best natural attractions in Guiping are:

  • Daming Mountain: A popular hiking spot, with stunning views of the surrounding nature and of the city below.
  • Qinlong Lake: This is a serene spot surrounded by nature, perfect for picnics or a relaxing walk.
  • Yaoshan National Forest Park: This park is home to ancient trees and offers plenty of hiking trails for nature enthusiasts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Guiping

Guiping offers many hiking and biking routes for the adventure seekers. Some popular routes include:

  • Daming Mountain Hiking Trail: This trail is a perfect day trip for hikers, with stunning scenery and views of the city below.
  • Qinlong Lake Biking Trail: This is a tranquil trail taking you around the lake and is suitable for beginners.

Best Things to Do in Guiping: Activities and Tours

  • Rafting in Lianhua Lake: A popular activity for those looking for adventure, rafting in Lianhua Lake is a thrilling experience.
  • ** Cooking Class in Guiping**: Experience local cuisine by taking a cooking class in Guiping, where you can learn to cook some of the famous local dishes.
  • Explore the Buddhist Temples: Guiping is also known for its Buddhist temples, where visitors can witness the beauty of Chinese Buddhist art and culture.

Food and Drink Scene of Guiping

Must-Try Dishes

Guiping is famous for its rich and flavorful local cuisine. Some must-try dishes are:

  • Braised Pork in Soy Sauce: A classic dish with tender and juicy meat.
  • Rice Noodles with Beef: This dish features locally produced rice noodles with savory beef broth and beef slices.
  • Fried Tofu: A Guiping delicacy, fried tofu is served with dipping sauce, making it a perfect appetizer.

Local Beverages

  • Chrysanthemum Tea: A refreshing tea made with fresh chrysanthemum flowers.
  • Er Guo Tou: A type of Chinese liquor.

Where to Stay in Guiping: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Guiping offers several accommodation options ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels. Some popular places to stay in Guiping are:

  • Jinjiang Hotel Guiping: A luxurious hotel located in the city center.
  • GreenTree Inn Guiping Municipal Government: A budget-friendly hotel with free WiFi, located in the downtown area.
  • Guiping Bamboo Hostel: A cozy hostel with a rooftop terrace, perfect for backpackers and budget travelers.

What to Buy in Guiping: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Guiping is known for its handcrafted products and souvenirs, such as:

  • Porcelain Tea Sets: Guiping is home to some of the best porcelain pottery in China; shops in Guiping Old Street have a great variety of porcelain tea sets.
  • Handmade Fans: Traditional Chinese fans are still made by hand in Guiping, making them unique keepsakes.
  • Famous Guiping Persimmons: Local guipi persimmons are known for their high-quality and juicy texture, making them a popular souvenir for visitors to take back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Guiping

Guiping’s rich cultural heritage is well-presented through its festivals and customs. Some of the famous celebrations in Guiping are:

  • The Damiaoyang Festival: A traditional festival where locals honor the ancient goddess Damiao, featuring performances and local food.
  • Lantern Festival: Celebrated on the 15th day of the Lunar New Year, this festival features stunning illuminated lanterns and performances.

Best Time to Visit Guiping

The best time to visit Guiping is from March to October, when the weather is sunny and warm, making it the perfect time for outdoor activities and exploring nature.

Do’s and Don’ts of Guiping

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Try the local cuisine, such as the famous braised pork in soy sauce.
  • Explore the natural attractions, such as Daming Mountain and Qinlong Lake.
  • Participate in local festivals and celebrations.

Don’t:

  • Wander off alone in remote areas or trails, especially after dark.
  • Leave trash or litter in natural areas.
  • Overstep when taking photographs in religious temples or monasteries.

Safety and Health

Safety in Guiping

Guiping is a relatively safe place for travelers. However, travelers should take precautionary measures, such as keeping personal belongings safe, avoiding wandering off alone in remote areas, and being mindful of scams or frauds.

Health in Guiping

There are no significant health concerns in Guiping. However, visitors should take necessary precautions to avoid getting bitten by bugs, such as using mosquito repellents and wearing long sleeves.

Getting to Guiping: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Guiping

The nearest airport to Guiping is Nanning Wuxu International Airport, which is about 150 km away.

Bus Stations Near Guiping

There are several bus stations in Guiping, with regular services connecting to cities such as Nanning, Guangzhou, and Guilin.

Other Options

Travelers can also reach Guiping by train or private car.

How to Get Around Guiping

Public transport options in Guiping are limited. Taxis are widely available, and there are a few bike rental shops that offer bikes for daily rental. Walking is also a great way to explore the city, especially around Old Street and the downtown areas.

Where to Go Next from Guiping: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Guiping is well-connected to other popular destinations in Guangxi, including:

  • Yangshuo: A famous destination known for its beautiful karst mountains and river cruises, located about 200km away from Guiping.
  • Baise: A beautiful city with stunning natural attractions, located about 300km away from Guiping.
  • Guilin: A famous tourist destination with famous natural scenery, located about 150km away from Guiping.

Conclusion

Guiping is a beautiful city with a rich cultural heritage and stunning natural beauty, perfect for those looking for a unique travel experience. Remember to explore the local cuisine, participate in the local festivals, and immerse yourself in the natural beauty of Guiping.
