Introduction to Hezhou

Hezhou is a city located in Central Guangxi, in Guangxi, South China. It is known for its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and vibrant food scene. Hezhou is also home to several culturally significant attractions, including museums and historical landmarks.

List of Places to Visit in Hezhou

Whether you are interested in exploring the city’s cultural heritage, immersing yourself in breathtaking natural landscapes, or trying local food and drinks, there are plenty of places to visit in Hezhou. Here is a list of must-see attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Hezhou Museum: A museum dedicated to the city’s history and culture, including artifacts from the Tang and Song dynasties.

  2. Bailian Cave Cultural Tourism Area: A Buddhist grotto featuring over 80 caves with statues and murals.

  3. Ziyun Mountain Scenic Area: A nature reserve with a historic temple and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

  4. Xuanwu Pagoda: A nine-story pagoda built during the Tang dynasty, offering panoramic views of the city.

Natural Attractions of Hezhou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Gupo Mountain Scenic Area: A nature reserve known for its rugged terrain, dense forests, and limestone caves.

  2. Jiuwanxi Rafting Area: A popular rafting spot with thrilling rapids and breathtaking scenery.

  3. Huangyao Ancient Town: A well-preserved ancient town surrounded by scenic hills and rivers.

  4. Chengyang Wind and Rain Bridge: A stunning bridge made of wood and stone, featuring traditional architecture and surrounded by breathtaking scenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hezhou

  1. Gupo Mountain Nature Trail: This trail takes you through the heart of the Gupo Mountain Scenic Area, passing through dense forests, underground rivers, and limestone caves.

  2. Ziyun Mountain Hiking Trail: This trail takes you up to the mountain peak, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscape and a glimpse into ancient Chinese history.

Best Things to Do in Hezhou: Activities and Tours

  1. Bike Tour of the Ancient Towns: Take a leisurely bike ride through the ancient towns of Hezhou, and admire the traditional architecture, bustling markets, and stunning natural scenery.

  2. Cruise on the Liujiang River: Take a relaxing boat ride on the Liujiang River, enjoying breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and ancient villages.

  3. Visit the Local Markets: Explore the vibrant local markets, where you can try local food and drinks, buy souvenirs, and experience the local culture.

Week-Long Itinerary for Hezhou

Day 1: Arrive in Hezhou and explore the Hezhou Museum and Xuanwu Pagoda. Day 2: Hike the Ziyun Mountain Trail and explore the Ziyun Mountain Scenic Area. Day 3: Take a bike tour of the ancient towns and visit the Chengyang Wind and Rain Bridge. Day 4: Explore the Gupo Mountain Scenic Area and go on a rafting trip on the Jiuwanxi River. Day 5: Relax and take a gentle boat ride on the Liujiang River. Day 6: Visit Huangyao Ancient Town and experience local markets and food. Day 7: Depart from Hezhou.

Food and Drink Scene of Hezhou

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Braised Tofu: A local specialty, tofu braised in soy sauce, chili oil, and other spices.

  2. Hezhou Tangyuan: A sweet dumpling dish made of glutinous rice flour and filled with sweet sesame paste.

  3. Spicy Chicken: Chicken marinated in a spicy sauce made of Sichuan peppercorns, chili peppers, and other spices.

Local Beverages

  1. Hezhou Wine: A locally-produced wine made of fermented glutinous rice.

  2. Herbal Tea: A popular local beverage made of various herbs and flower petals, believed to have medicinal properties.

Where to Stay in Hezhou: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Wanda Realm Hezhou: A luxurious hotel located in the heart of the city, with comfortable rooms and excellent amenities.

  2. Hezhou Jitangjia Inn: A friendly and affordable hostel located near the city center, with comfortable rooms and a relaxed atmosphere.

  3. Xiyue Hotel: A stylish and modern hotel located near the Gupo Mountain Scenic Area, with stunning views of the mountains.

What to Buy in Hezhou: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Bamboo Products: Hezhou is famous for its bamboo artwork, including baskets, fans, and sculptures.

  2. Stone Carvings: The city is also known for its stone carvings, including figurines and sculptures made of limestone and marble.

Local Culture and Customs of Hezhou

The people of Hezhou are known for their hospitality and warmth. Respect for senior citizens is important in the local culture, and it is common to see families and friends gathering for meals and social events.

Best Time to Visit Hezhou

The best time to visit Hezhou is from March to November, when the weather is mild and comfortable. Summers can be hot and humid, while winters can be chilly and rainy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hezhou

Do:

  1. Try local food and drinks.
  2. Explore the city’s cultural heritage and natural beauty.
  3. Respect local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  1. Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  2. Ignore safety precautions when exploring natural attractions.
  3. Engage in illegal activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Hezhou

Hezhou is generally a safe city for tourists, but it’s always important to take normal precautions such as keeping your valuables secure and being aware of your surroundings.

Health in Hezhou

Several hospitals and clinics are available in Hezhou, and most hotels and hostels can provide basic medical assistance in case of emergency. It’s always a good idea to carry a first aid kit and any necessary medications with you while traveling.

Getting to Hezhou: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Hezhou

  1. Hezhou Airport: The closest airport to Hezhou, with flights to major cities in China.

  2. Nanning International Airport: A larger airport located approximately 3 hours from Hezhou, with flights to major cities in China as well as international destinations.

Bus Stations Near Hezhou

  1. Hezhou Bus Station: The main bus station in Hezhou, with buses to major cities in Guangxi and neighboring provinces.

  2. Hezhou South Bus Station: A secondary bus station located south of the city, with buses to neighboring towns and villages.

Other Options

Several train stations are available in nearby cities, such as Liuzhou and Guilin, with frequent trains to major cities in China.

How to Get Around in Hezhou

The best way to get around Hezhou is by taxi, which is affordable and easily accessible throughout the city. Bikes and motorized scooters are also available for rent at many hotels and hostels.

Where to Go Next from Hezhou: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Liuzhou: Located approximately 2 hours from Hezhou, Liuzhou is known for its stunning karst landscapes and traditional Guangxi culture.

  2. Guilin: Located approximately 3 hours from Hezhou, Guilin is known for its scenic beauty and traditional Chinese architecture.

Conclusion

Hezhou is a hidden gem in Guangxi, South China, offering visitors stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and a vibrant food scene. Whether you’re exploring the city’s museums and historical landmarks, immersing yourself in the breathtaking natural landscapes, or trying local food and drink, there is always something to discover in Hezhou. Plan your itinerary, explore the local culture and customs, and discover the nearby destinations for a truly unforgettable experience.
