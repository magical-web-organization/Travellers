Introduction to Guigang

Located in the central part of Guangxi province in China, Guigang is a vibrant and culturally-rich city that offers plenty of attractions for visitors. The city is known for its beautiful natural scenery, stunning temples and museums, and bustling cityscape. In this travel guide, we’ll take a look at some of the best places to visit, things to do, and places to stay in Guigang.

List of Places to Visit in Guigang

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Guigang Liuzhou International Museum

The Liuzhou International Museum of Guigang is a must-visit for history enthusiasts. The museum houses a wide range of exhibits and artifacts, including pottery, ironware, bamboo articles, and other artwork from different periods.

Yufeng Mountain

Yufeng Mountain is not only famous for its picturesque views but also has a rich history. Visitors can explore the temples on the mountainside like the Yufeng Temple and the scenic Wuzhi Peak.

Natural Attractions in Guigang: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Caofeidian National Forest Park

Located in the Lianzhou Mountains, the Caofeidian National Forest Park offers breathtaking views and is a perfect spot for outdoor activities like hiking, cycling, and picnicking.

Pingtang Scenic Area

Pingtang Scenic Area is a breathtaking natural area that includes expansive lakes, lush forests, and stunning waterfalls. It’s a great place to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Guigang

Yufeng Mountain Hiking Route

The Yufeng Mountain Hiking Route is a four-hour hike that takes you to the peak of the mountain. The trail offers spectacular vistas of the surrounding countryside and is best done early in the day.

Lianzhou Ancient City Biking Route

The Lianzhou Ancient City Biking Route takes you through the ancient city and out into the surrounding countryside, where you can enjoy the natural beauty of the region at a leisurely pace.

Best Things to Do in Guigang: Activities and Tours

Experience Local Culture

Visitors can experience local culture and customs by attending local events, like the Lantern Festival or the Dragon Boat Festival, which are held annually in Guigang.

Explore Guigang’s Street Food Scene

Guigang has a vibrant street food scene offering plenty of tasty options for visitors. Be sure to try the city’s famous baba cakes, mifan, and rice noodles.

Food and Drink Scene of Guigang

Must-Try Dishes

Baba Cakes

Baba Cakes, a famous snack in Guigang, are made with glutinous rice flour and stuffed with a savory filling, like pickled vegetables, meat, or eggs.

Mifan

Mifan, or rice cooked in a clay pot, is a staple food in Guigang. The rice is cooked with different vegetables, pork, and other spices, making it a flavorful and comforting dish.

Local Beverages

Sweet Osmanthus Wine

Sweet osmanthus wine is a traditional Chinese wine made with sweet, aromatic osmanthus flowers. It’s the perfect drink for a relaxing evening out.

Where to Stay in Guigang: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Guigang Greenland Hotel

Located in the heart of Guigang, the Guigang Greenland Hotel offers comfortable, modern rooms and a range of amenities, including a fitness center and a restaurant serving delicious Chinese food.

Earth Tree Inn

The Earth Tree Inn is a cozy hostel that offers comfortable, affordable accommodation in the city center, making it a great option for backpackers or those on a budget.

What to Buy in Guigang: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Handicrafts

Guigang is famous for its handicrafts, including bamboo baskets, furniture, and artwork. Visitors can find unique souvenirs to bring home at local markets like the Guigang City Craft Fair.

Local Snacks

Guigang is also famous for its snacks and treats, including preserved winter melon, anying tea, and baba cakes. Visitors can pick up these delicious local treats at markets and shops throughout the city.

Local Culture and Customs of Guigang

One of the best ways to experience Guigang’s local culture is to attend one of the many festivals or events held throughout the year. The city is famous for its Lantern Festival and Dragon Boat Festival, among others.

Best Time to Visit Guigang

The best time to visit Guigang is in the spring and fall, when the weather is mild and comfortable. Summers can be hot and humid, while winters can be cold, so it’s best to plan your visit accordingly.

Do’s and Don’ts of Guigang

Do:

  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local food and drinks.
  • Take part in local events and festivals.

Don’t:

  • Speak loudly or make a scene in public.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  • Take photos without permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Guigang

Guigang is generally a safe city, but visitors should still take common-sense precautions to avoid petty theft or scams. Travelers are also advised to be cautious when traveling on public transportation.

Health in Guigang

Visitors to Guigang should take precautions to prevent illness, including drinking bottled water, washing their hands frequently, and avoiding raw or undercooked food.

Getting to Guigang: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Guigang

The nearest airport to Guigang is Liuzhou Bailian Airport, which is about 70 kilometers from the city center.

Bus Stations Near Guigang

Guigang has several bus stations that serve different parts of the city and surrounding areas. The main bus station is Guigang South Bus Station.

Other Options

Travelers can also take a train to Guigang, as the city is located on the main rail line between Nanning and Guangzhou.

How to Get Around in Guigang

Travelers in Guigang can get around by bus, taxi, or bike. Taxis are plentiful in the city center and are a convenient way to get around, while buses offer a more affordable option for longer journeys. Bike rentals are also available throughout the city for those who prefer to travel on two wheels.

Where to Go Next from Guigang: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Nanning

Nanning is the capital of Guangxi province and is located about 3 hours by car from Guigang. The city is known for its beautiful parks and nature reserves, as well as its lively nightlife and food scene.

Guilin

Guilin is a popular tourist destination located about 4 hours from Guigang. The city is known for its stunning karst landscapes and is a great place to explore the natural beauty of Guangxi.

Conclusion

Guigang is an exciting destination that offers a mix of natural beauty, cultural attractions, and delicious cuisine. Whether you’re interested in exploring the city’s history or enjoying outdoor adventures, there’s something for everyone in Guigang. Plan your trip today and discover all that this vibrant city has to offer.
