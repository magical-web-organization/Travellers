Introduction to Longzhou

Longzhou is a small town located in the Central Guangxi region of South China. It is popular for its natural beauty, cultural heritage, and historical significance. Longzhou is surrounded by stunning mountain ranges and dense forests, making it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The town’s rich history can be traced back to the Ming Dynasty, and numerous museums and historical sites can be found throughout the town.

List of Places to Visit in Longzhou

If you are planning to visit Longzhou, here are some of the top-rated places to explore:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Longzhou Museum: This museum is a must-visit attraction for history buffs. It showcases the region’s rich cultural heritage and history through various exhibits, including pottery, jade, bronze, and other historical artifacts.

  • Guangxi Revolutionary Martyrs Cemetery: This cemetery is a solemn tribute to the people who lost their lives in the revolutionary war against the Kuomintang. It is a place of great historical significance and a must-visit site for anyone interested in Chinese history.

  • Stone Drum Temple: This ancient temple dates back to the Tang Dynasty and is famous for its stunning architecture and intricate carvings. It is also home to a giant stone drum, which is considered a national treasure.

Natural Attractions of Longzhou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Longzhou Danxia Geological Park: This park is located in the Longzhou Danxia Scenic Area and is home to stunning red sandstone hills that have been carved into unique shapes by the wind and rain. Visitors can hike along the trails to enjoy panoramic views of the park.

  • Longzhou Tropical Mountain Forest Park: This park is located in the Longzhou Mountain Scenic Area and is known for its spectacular tropical flora and fauna. Visitors can enjoy a range of outdoor activities, including hiking, camping, and bird-watching.

  • Jahe Pingfeng Scenic Area: This scenic area is surrounded by beautiful mountains and features a stunning natural lake. Visitors can enjoy hiking, camping, and fishing in this peaceful and picturesque location.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Longzhou

Longzhou is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts who love hiking and biking. Here are some popular hiking/biking routes:

  • Longzhou Danxia Geological Park Trail: This trail is located in the Longzhou Danxia Geological Park and offers a challenging hike through stunning red sandstone hills. It is a popular route for experienced hikers.

  • Longzhou Mountain Trail: This trail is located in the Longzhou Mountain Scenic Area and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and forests. It is a great route for both hikers and bikers.

  • Jahe Pingfeng Trail: This trail is located in the Jahe Pingfeng Scenic Area and offers a peaceful hike through beautiful mountains and forests. It is a great route for nature lovers.

Best Things to Do in Longzhou: Activities and Tours

  • Take a Cultural Tour: There are several cultural tours available in Longzhou that will take you on a journey through the town’s rich history and cultural heritage. Visitors can explore ancient temples, local museums, and historical sites, and learn about the town’s unique customs and traditions.

  • Go Bird-Watching: Longzhou is home to a vast range of bird species, making it an ideal destination for bird-watchers. Visitors can explore the town’s lush forests and mountain ranges to spot a variety of rare and exotic bird species.

  • Try Local Cuisine: Longzhou is known for its delicious local cuisine, which includes dishes such as spicy fish, clay pot chicken, and bamboo rice. Visitors can try these traditional dishes at local restaurants and street food stalls.

Week-Long Itinerary for Longzhou

Day 1: Arrive in Longzhou and explore the town’s cultural sites, including the Longzhou Museum and Stone Drum Temple.

Day 2-3: Spend two days exploring Longzhou’s stunning natural scenery. Visit the Longzhou Danxia Geological Park, Longzhou Tropical Mountain Forest Park, and Jahe Pingfeng Scenic Area.

Day 4-5: Enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and bird-watching in Longzhou’s beautiful mountains and forests.

Day 6-7: Take a cultural tour of Longzhou and visit local markets, traditional villages, and historical sites. Discover the town’s unique customs, traditions, and local cuisine.

Food and Drink Scene of Longzhou

Must-Try Dishes

  • Spicy Fish: This spicy dish is made with fresh local fish, chili peppers, and other spices. It is a staple dish in Longzhou and is loved by locals and visitors alike.

  • Clay Pot Chicken: This savory dish is made with chicken, mushrooms, bamboo shoots, and other spices. It is cooked in a clay pot, giving it a unique flavor and texture.

  • Bamboo Rice: This traditional dish is made with rice, mushrooms, and other local ingredients. It is cooked in bamboo tubes, giving it an earthy flavor and aroma.

Local Beverages

  • Rice Wine: Rice wine is a popular local beverage in Longzhou. It is made by fermenting glutinous rice and has a mild, sweet flavor.

  • Herbal Tea: Herbal tea is a traditional beverage in Longzhou and is made with various herbs and flowers. It is believed to have many health benefits and is often served hot or cold.

What to Buy in Longzhou: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you would like to bring home some unique souvenirs from your trip to Longzhou, here are some options:

  • Bamboo Products: Longzhou is famous for its bamboo products, including baskets, mats, and furniture.

  • Handcrafted Pottery: Local artisans in Longzhou create beautiful handcrafted pottery, including tea sets, vases, and other decorative items.

  • Local Snacks: Longzhou is known for its delicious snacks, including candied fruit, roasted nuts, and honey cakes. These make great gifts to take home to family and friends.

Local Culture and Customs of Longzhou

Longzhou has a rich cultural heritage and unique customs and traditions. Some of the most interesting customs include:

  • Dragon Boat Festival: Longzhou celebrates the Dragon Boat Festival every year in June, with dragon boat races, lion dances, and other festive activities.

  • Carnival of Flowers: This annual event takes place in November and is a celebration of the town’s beautiful flora and fauna. Visitors can enjoy flower exhibitions, parades, and other colorful events.

  • Gongbei Customs: Gongbei is a traditional Longzhou custom that involves inviting guests into the home with a ritual ceremony. The hosts offer tea, snacks, and other gifts to their guests, and everyone sits together to enjoy each other’s company.

Best Time to Visit Longzhou

The best time to visit Longzhou is during the autumn months (September-November) when the weather is cool and dry, making it perfect for outdoor activities. Spring is also a great time to visit, when the town’s flowers are in full bloom.

Getting to Longzhou: Transportation Options and Logistics

Longzhou can be reached by air, train, or bus. Here are some transportation options:

  • Guilin Liangjiang International Airport: This airport is located around 3 hours’ drive from Longzhou.

  • Nanning Wuxu International Airport: This airport is located around 3.5 hours’ drive from Longzhou.

Bus Stations Near Longzhou

  • Longzhou Bus Station: This bus station is located in the center of Longzhou and offers numerous connections to nearby towns and cities.

  • Nanning Bus Station: This bus station is located in Nanning and offers connections to Longzhou.

Other Options

  • Train: The closest train station to Longzhou is located in Nanning. Visitors can take a train to Nanning and then transfer to a bus or taxi to reach Longzhou.

  • Private Transfer: Visitors can also arrange for private transfers from nearby towns or cities to reach Longzhou.

How to Get Around in Longzhou

Visitors can easily get around Longzhou using local buses, taxis, or rented bicycles. Many of the town’s attractions are within walking distance of each other, making it easy to explore on foot.

Where to Go Next from Longzhou: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Longzhou is located in a beautiful part of Guangxi, making it easy to explore other nearby destinations and attractions. Some popular options include:
