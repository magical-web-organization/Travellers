Introduction to Weizhou

Weizhou is a beautiful island situated in the Beibu Gulf of Guangxi Province, Southern China. The island is characterized by beautiful natural landscapes, pristine beaches, beautiful temples, and historical landmarks.

It is an ideal place to visit for those who are looking for a unique and authentic travel experience. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will walk you through everything you need to know about Weizhou, from the best places to visit to the local customs and culture.

List of Places to Visit in Weizhou

Weizhou is rich in natural beauty, culture, and history. Here is a list of the top attractions that you should visit while you are here.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Big Buddha Temple: A large Buddhist temple located on the island’s north shore. It features a 16-meter tall Buddha statue and stunning ocean views.
  • Cangpo Ancient Village: A well-preserved village that dates back to the Song and Yuan dynasties. It is an excellent place to experience rural life and enjoy the beautiful scenery.
  • Chongwu Ancient Town: An ancient town with a history of over 1,000 years. The defensive walls, towers, and gate sites provide visitors with a glimpse into a bygone era.

Natural Attractions of Weizhou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Gourd Island: This island is located 6 kilometers northeast of Weizhou Island and is home to stunning beaches and rock formations.
  • Xiachao Bay: A picturesque bay on the east coast of Weizhou Island, with crystal-clear water, reef-covered seabeds, and water sports activities such as kayaking and diving.
  • Moon Bay: A crescent-shaped bay surrounded by rocky cliffs, lush jungle, and a pristine beach.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Weizhou

Weizhou Island boasts a well-developed network of hiking and biking trails that provides visitors with an opportunity to explore the island’s natural beauty.

  • Beihai-Qinzhou National Highway Bike Tour: This tour takes you on a journey along the famous National Highway No. 209. It offers stunning views of the island’s mountain, sea, and rural landscapes.
  • Lover Road Trail: A romantic trail that links Big Buddha Temple to Ma’an Mountain Scenic Spot. The 6-kilometer trail goes through ancient forests, mountain streams, and bamboo groves, providing a unique hiking experience.
  • Ma’an Mountain Nature Reserve Trail: This trail goes from Ma’an Mountain Scenic Spot to the summit of Ma’an Mountain, where visitors can enjoy an incredible panoramic view of the island.

Best Things to Do in Weizhou: Activities and Tours

Weizhou Island is an ideal destination for those who are looking for adventures and unique experiences. Here are some of the best things you can do while you’re here.

  • Explore the Beaches: Weizhou has some of the most stunning beaches in China, including Moon Bay, Nanwan Beach, and Huizhou Bay. Spend an afternoon relaxing on the soft sand, soaking up the sun, and swimming in the pristine waters of the Beibu Gulf.
  • Go Diving: Weizhou is a great place for diving enthusiasts. The clear waters around the island are home to colorful coral reefs, tropical fish, and other marine creatures.
  • Try the Local Food: Weizhou has a rich culinary culture, and visitors can enjoy a wide range of local dishes. The must-try dishes include Guilin Rice Noodles, Beibu Gulf Seafood, and Hotpot.

Food and Drink Scene of Weizhou

Weizhou is known for its fresh seafood, spicy hotpot, and unique fusion dishes. Here are some of the must-try dishes and beverages in Weizhou.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Beibu Gulf Seafood: The seafood in Weizhou is delicious and fresh! You can find a variety of seafood dishes including shrimp, crabs, shellfish, and fish.
  • Guilin Rice Noodles: This dish is a signature dish in Guangxi Province. It consists of rice noodles cooked with meat, vegetables, and spices.
  • Spicy Hotpot: Weizhou’s hot pot is made with a unique blend of spices and has a rich, spicy flavor. It’s a must-try for those who love spicy food!

Local Beverages

  • Weizhou Coconut Milk: Weizhou is known for its fresh coconuts, and the coconut milk is a refreshing beverage that’s perfect for a hot day.
  • Rice Wine: Rice wine is a popular beverage in Guangxi Province. It is made from fermented rice, and it has a sweet and sour taste.

Where to Stay in Weizhou: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Weizhou Island has a range of accommodations to suit all budgets, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses, Here are some of the best options:

  • Weizhou Island Resort Hotel: This hotel is located near Nanwan Beach, and it offers luxurious rooms with ocean views.
  • Weizhou Island Youth Hostel: This hostel is perfect for budget travelers. It offers clean and comfortable rooms with a peaceful environment.
  • Weizhou Island Guesthouse: The guesthouse is located near the ferry terminal and offers clean, comfortable rooms at affordable prices.

What to Buy in Weizhou: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some souvenirs and local products that you can bring back home to commemorate your trip to Weizhou.

  • Coconut products: Weizhou is known for its fresh coconuts, and you can bring back products like coconut oil, coconut candy, and coconut shell handicrafts.
  • Pagoda embroidery: Pagoda embroidery is a traditional handicraft in Guangxi Province. It features intricate designs and bright colors, and it can be found in local markets around Weizhou Island.
  • Seafood: If you are a seafood lover, you can buy fresh seafood to bring back home. Be sure to check with local authorities regarding the rules and regulations surrounding transportation of food items before making purchases.

Local Culture and Customs of Weizhou

Weizhou Island has a unique culture and customs. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Language: The local dialect is Weizhou dialect, which is a combination of Cantonese, Hakka, and other dialects. Mandarin Chinese is widely spoken in tourist areas.
  • Temples: Temples are an important part of life on Weizhou Island, and visitors should be respectful when entering them.
  • Festivals: There are many festivals throughout the year, including the Weizhou Island Dragon Boat Festival and the Weizhou Island Nanshan Temple Fair.

Best Time to Visit Weizhou

The best time to visit Weizhou is between October to December when the weather is mild, and there is less rainfall. The temperature during this time ranges from 18℃ to 28℃.

Do’s and Don’ts of Weizhou

Do:

  • Be respectful: Chinese culture places a high value on respect. Greet locals with a smile and be respectful in temples and other cultural sites.
  • Try the local food: Guangxi Province is known for its delicious food. Don’t be afraid to try new dishes and explore the local food scene.
  • Plan ahead: Weizhou Island is small, but there is a lot to see and do. Plan your itinerary in advance to make the most of your time on the island.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing: Modesty is valued in Chinese culture. Cover your shoulders and dress conservatively when visiting temples and other cultural sites.
  • Litter: China places a high value on cleanliness, and littering is looked down upon.

Safety and Health

Safety in Weizhou

Weizhou is a safe destination for travelers. However, it’s always a good idea to take precautions, such as:

  • Watch your belongings: Theft can happen in tourist areas. Keep your valuables in a safe place and be aware of your surroundings.
  • Be cautious when swimming: Pay attention to signs and warnings on beaches, and never swim alone.

Health in Weizhou

Weizhou Island has good medical facilities. However, it’s always a good idea to be prepared when traveling. Be sure to:

  • Get vaccinated: Consult with a health professional to find out which vaccines you need for travel to China.
  • Bring insect repellent: Mosquitoes can be a problem, so bring insect repellent and consider wearing long sleeves and pants.

Getting to Weizhou: Transportation Options and Logistics

Weizhou Island can be reached by ferry from Beihai. Here are some transportation options to consider:

Airports Near Weizhou

The nearest airport to Weizhou is Beihai Fucheng Airport, which is about 30 kilometers away from the ferry terminal.

Bus Stations Near Weizhou

The nearest bus station to Weizhou is Beihai Bus Station. From there, you can take a shuttle bus to the ferry terminal.

Other Options

You can also hire a private car or take a taxi from Beihai to the ferry terminal.

How to Get Around in Weizhou

Weizhou Island is small and easy to explore. The best way to get around the island is by bicycle or scooter. Some hotels and guesthouses offer bike rentals, and scooter rentals are also available in some areas.

Where to Go Next from Weizhou: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

From Weizhou, you can explore other destinations in Guangxi Province. Here are some nearby places worth considering:

  • Beihai: A popular beach destination known for its seafood and cultural attractions.
  • Guilin: A famous tourist destination known for its stunning karst landscapes and natural wonders.
  • Nanning: The capital of Guangxi Province, famous for its vibrant culture and scenic areas.

Conclusion

Weizhou Island is a unique travel destination that offers a combination of stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and unique experiences. As a traveler, you’ll have the chance to immerse yourself in the local culture, try delicious food, and explore the island’s hiking and biking trails.

By following the tips and recommendations outlined in this travel guide, you’ll be able to make the most of your trip to Weizhou and create unforgettable memories.
