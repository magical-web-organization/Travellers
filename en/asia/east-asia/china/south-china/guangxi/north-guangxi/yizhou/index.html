Yizhou, a small city located in North Guangxi in South China, is a hidden gem that offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Whether you’re a history buff or an adventure seeker, Yizhou has something for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide to Yizhou, we’ve compiled a list of top attractions, hiking/biking routes, food and drink options, where to stay, and more to help you plan your trip to this charming city.

Introduction to Yizhou

Located in the northern part of the Guangxi Zhuang Autonomous Region, Yizhou is a city with a rich history and breathtaking natural scenery. Surrounded by mountains and water, Yizhou boasts stunning views and a laid-back vibe that makes it a perfect destination for a relaxing vacation. The city has a long and interesting past that dates back to the Tang Dynasty, making it a great place for history buffs to explore.

List of Places to Visit in Yizhou

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Yizhou Museum: This museum showcases the history and culture of Yizhou, with exhibits on the Tang Dynasty and local art.
  2. Tang Dynasty Mausoleums: These mausoleums are a must-visit for history buffs visiting Yizhou.
  3. Yizhou Ancient City: This ancient city, built during the Tang Dynasty, is a great place to explore and learn about local history.

Natural Attractions of Yizhou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Yan River Rafting: Take a scenic rafting trip down the Yan River, surrounded by lush greenery and stunning mountain views.
  2. Shatian Mountain Scenic Area: This scenic area offers breathtaking views of pristine natural beauty, with hiking trails and a cable car that takes you to the top.
  3. Yuquan Park: This park is home to a beautiful lake and stunning gardens, making it a great place for a picnic or a leisurely stroll.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Yizhou

  1. Shatian Mountain Hiking Trail: This hiking trail takes you to the top of Shatian Mountain, where you’ll be rewarded with breathtaking views of the surrounding area.
  2. Yan River Bike Trail: This bike trail follows the Yan River, offering stunning views of the surrounding mountains and countryside.

Best Things to Do in Yizhou: Activities and Tours

  1. Tang Dynasty Dance and Music Show: This show features traditional Tang Dynasty dance and music performances that will transport you back in time.
  2. Tea Ceremony Experience: Learn about the art of tea-making and sample local teas with a traditional tea ceremony experience.
  3. Yan River Rafting Tour: Take a guided tour of the Yan River and learn about the local ecology and history.

Week-Long Itinerary for Yizhou

Day 1-2: Explore Yizhou’s cultural attractions, including the Yizhou Museum and Tang Dynasty Mausoleums.

Day 3-4: Take in the natural beauty of Yizhou with a hike up Shatian Mountain, a bike ride along the Yan River, or a leisurely stroll through Yuquan Park.

Day 5-6: Participate in cultural activities and tours, such as the Tang Dynasty Dance and Music Show or a tea ceremony experience.

Day 7: Sample local food and drink and shop for souvenirs and local products to bring home.

Food and Drink Scene of Yizhou

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Guilin Rice Noodles: This local specialty is a must-try, with its savory broth and tender noodles.
  2. Luosifen: This spicy and sour noodle soup is a favorite among locals and visitors alike.
  3. Yizhou Three-Hour Chicken: This succulent chicken dish is marinated for three hours and steamed with herbs and spices for a delicious and flavorful meal.

Local Beverages

  1. Yizhou Wine: This local wine is made from high-quality grapes and has a sweet and fruity flavor.
  2. Gongming Tea: This tea is grown in Yizhou and has a light and fragrant taste, making it a great complement to local dishes.

Where to Stay in Yizhou: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Yizhou Hotel: This centrally-located hotel offers comfortable rooms and a convenient location close to many of Yizhou’s top attractions.
  2. Starway Hotel: This budget-friendly hotel offers basic amenities and a warm and welcoming atmosphere.
  3. Yulong Villa: This guesthouse is located in the scenic Shatian Mountain area and offers stunning views and a peaceful atmosphere.

What to Buy in Yizhou: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Yizhou Embroidery: This traditional craft is a hallmark of Yizhou and makes for a great souvenir or gift.
  2. Tea: Local teas such as Gongming Tea are a popular souvenir and make for a great way to share a taste of Yizhou with friends and family.
  3. Yizhou Wine: Yizhou’s famous wine makes for a great gift or souvenir to take home and enjoy.

Local Culture and Customs of Yizhou

Yizhou has a rich and unique culture that is heavily influenced by its history and natural surroundings. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions and take the time to learn about the local way of life.

Best Time to Visit Yizhou

The best time to visit Yizhou is from March to November, when the weather is mild and the natural scenery is at its most beautiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of Yizhou

Do:

  1. Do try local dishes and drinks.
  2. Do take the time to learn about local customs and traditions.
  3. Do engage with the local community and take part in cultural activities and tours.

Don’t:

  1. Don’t disrespect local customs and traditions.
  2. Don’t litter or damage local natural resources.
  3. Don’t forget to pack appropriate clothing and gear for outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Yizhou

Yizhou is generally a safe destination for travelers. However, visitors should still take basic precautions to ensure their safety, such as avoiding areas with high crime rates and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Yizhou

Visitors should take basic health precautions when traveling to Yizhou, such as staying hydrated, wearing sunscreen, and avoiding street food that may not be prepared in hygienic conditions.

Getting to Yizhou: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Yizhou

The nearest airport to Yizhou is Guilin Liangjiang International Airport, which is located approximately 3 hours away by car.

Bus Stations Near Yizhou

Yizhou Bus Station is the main bus station in the city and offers regular services to nearby destinations.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel to Yizhou by train or hire a private car or taxi.

How to Get Around in Yizhou

Visitors can get around Yizhou by public transportation, taxi, or bike rental.

Where to Go Next from Yizhou: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Guilin: This nearby city is famous for its natural beauty and picturesque scenery, making it a must-visit for anyone traveling to the area.
  2. Liuzhou: This historic city has a rich cultural heritage and is home to many important landmarks and museums.
  3. Yangshuo: This small town is a popular destination for adventure seekers and nature lovers, with hiking and biking trails and stunning natural scenery.

Conclusion

Yizhou is a hidden gem that offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. From stunning mountains and waterways to ancient artifacts and museums, Yizhou has something for everyone. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has helped you plan your trip to this charming city and discover all that it has to offer.
