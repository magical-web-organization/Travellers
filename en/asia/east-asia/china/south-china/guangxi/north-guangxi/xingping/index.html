Introduction to Xingping

Xingping is a small town located in North Guangxi, China. Surrounded by stunning karst mountains, lush greenery, and the Li River, Xingping is a popular destination among tourists and nature lovers. The town is well-known for its historic streets, traditional architecture, and numerous outdoor activities. Whether you’re interested in exploring the cultural and historical sites or simply enjoying the natural scenery, Xingping is a must-visit destination for any traveler.

List of Places to Visit in Xingping

Here are some of the top places to visit in Xingping:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Xingping Ancient Town: Explore the historic streets, traditional buildings, and charming shops of Xingping’s ancient town.
  • Xingping Fishing Village: Located along the Li River, this old fishing village offers a glimpse into the traditional way of life in rural China.
  • Xingping Town Wall: Built in the Ming Dynasty, this impressive wall encircles the entire town and offers panoramic views of the surrounding area.
  • Xingping Folk Custom Center: Learn about the unique customs and traditions of Guangxi’s minority groups at this informative center.

Natural Attractions of Xingping: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Li River Cruise: Take a leisurely cruise down the tranquil Li River and marvel at the breathtaking scenery along the way.
  • Laozhai Hill: Hike up to the top of this hill and enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding karst mountains and the Li River.
  • Yellow Cloth Shoal: This sandy beach area offers the perfect spot to relax, swim, and soak up the sun.
  • Lotus Peak: One of the tallest peaks in the area, Lotus Peak offers challenging hiking trails and stunning views from the top.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Xingping

Xingping is a perfect destination for hikers and bikers. Here are some of the best hiking and biking trails in the area:

  • Xingping to Yangshuo: This scenic route follows the Li River from Xingping to Yangshuo, passing by some of the most beautiful natural scenery in the area.
  • Xingping to Laozhai Hill: Hike up to the top of Laozhai Hill for panoramic views of the surrounding area.
  • Xingping to Nine Horse Fresco Hill: This challenging hike takes you to the top of the Nine Horse Fresco Hill, where you can enjoy stunning views of the surrounding karst mountains and the Li River.

Best Things to Do in Xingping: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Xingping:

  • Li River Cruise: Take a leisurely boat ride down the Li River and soak up the stunning scenery.
  • Bamboo Rafting: For a more adventurous river experience, try bamboo rafting down the Li River.
  • Chinese Cooking Class: Learn how to cook traditional Chinese dishes with a hands-on cooking class in Xingping.
  • Cycling Tours: Explore the town and the surrounding countryside on a scenic cycling tour.

Week-Long Itinerary for Xingping

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Xingping, here’s a sample itinerary:

Day 1 Arrive in Xingping, explore the ancient town
Day 2 Take a Li River cruise or a bamboo rafting tour
Day 3 Hike up to Laozhai Hill for stunning views
Day 4 Learn how to cook traditional Chinese dishes at a cooking class
Day 5 Explore the nearby town of Yangshuo
Day 6 Take a cycling tour of the Xingping countryside
Day 7 Relax and enjoy the town’s natural scenery

Food and Drink Scene of Xingping

Xingping is known for its delicious local cuisine and unique beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and drinks:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Guilin Rice Noodles: This popular dish is made with thin, round rice noodles served in a savory broth with meat and vegetables.
  • Stir-Fried Li River Snails: These freshwater snails are a local delicacy and are typically stir-fried with chili and garlic.
  • Braised Pork in Soy Sauce: This savory dish features tender pork cooked in a soy sauce and sugar marinade.

Local Beverages

  • Yangshuo Beer: This locally brewed beer is a refreshing drink after a day of outdoor activities.
  • Xingping Osmanthus Wine: Made with fragrant osmanthus flowers, this sweet wine is a popular drink in Xingping.

Where to Stay in Xingping: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Here are some of the best places to stay in Xingping:

  • Xingping This Old Place International Youth Hostel: This charming hostel is located in the heart of Xingping’s ancient town and offers comfortable dorm beds and private rooms.
  • Xingping Riverside Inn: This traditional inn is located along the banks of the Li River and offers stunning views of the river and the surrounding karst mountains.
  • Xingping Xianju Inn: This cozy inn is located in the center of the ancient town and offers comfortable rooms and a peaceful courtyard.

What to Buy in Xingping: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some of the best souvenirs and local products to buy in Xingping:

  • Traditional Garments: Xingping is known for its beautiful traditional garments, which make excellent souvenirs or gifts.
  • Scenic Paintings: Many local artists sell their paintings of the stunning Xingping scenery on the streets of the ancient town.
  • Tea and Herbs: Xingping is located in the heart of Guangxi’s tea-growing region, so it’s a great place to buy high-quality local tea and herbs.

Local Culture and Customs of Xingping

Xingping is home to several ethnic minority groups, including the Zhuang, Yao, and Miao people. Visitors to Xingping can experience their unique customs and traditions through local festivals, cultural performances, and handicrafts.

Best Time to Visit Xingping

The best time to visit Xingping is from April to October, when the weather is warm and dry, and the natural scenery is at its most beautiful. However, expect larger crowds during this time, especially during the peak summer months of July and August.

Do’s and Don’ts of Xingping

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine, especially the Guilin Rice Noodles and Li River Snails.
  • Do respect the local customs and traditions, especially during festivals and cultural performances.
  • Do wear comfortable shoes and clothing for hiking and outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter or damage the natural scenery.
  • Don’t disturb the local wildlife or stray from designated hiking trails.
  • Don’t take photos of people without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Xingping

Xingping is generally a safe destination for travelers, but visitors should still take basic precautions such as locking up valuables and staying aware of their surroundings. Be cautious when hiking in remote areas and when participating in outdoor activities.

Health in Xingping

Tap water in Xingping is not safe to drink, so be sure to drink bottled or boiled water only. Mosquitoes can also be a problem in the area, so bring appropriate insect repellent and clothing to avoid bites.

Getting to Xingping: Transportation Options and Logistics

Xingping can be reached by several transportation options:

  • Air: The nearest airport to Xingping is Guilin Liangjiang International Airport, which is about 70km away.
  • Bus: Buses run daily to Xingping from several nearby cities, including Guilin, Yangshuo, and Nanning.
  • Taxi: Taxis can be hired from nearby cities and the airport for a more convenient and direct journey.

How to Get Around in Xingping

Xingping is a small town, and most of the attractions and accommodations are within walking distance. Taxis and bicycles can also be rented for a more convenient way to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Xingping: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions to visit from Xingping:

  • Yangshuo: This vibrant town is located about an hour’s drive from Xingping and offers a variety of outdoor activities, nightlife, and cultural attractions.
  • Longsheng: Known for its stunning rice terraces, this area is about a three-hour drive from Xingping.
  • Guilin: This beautiful city is known for its unique rock formations and beautiful natural scenery and is about a two-hour drive from Xingping.

Conclusion

Xingping is a charming and beautiful destination in China that offers a unique blend of natural and cultural attractions. From hiking and biking to traditional cuisine and handicrafts, Xingping has something for every type of traveler. With this comprehensive travel guide, planning your trip to Xingping has never been easier.
