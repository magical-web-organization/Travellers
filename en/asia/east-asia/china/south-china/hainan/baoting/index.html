Introduction to Baoting

Located in the southwestern corner of Hainan, Baoting is a hidden gem that is often overlooked by tourists. However, those who venture into Baoting will be rewarded with a stunning natural landscape, rich cultural heritage, and exciting outdoor adventures.

Baoting is known for its tropical rainforest and hot springs, but it is also home to a number of cultural artifacts, including ancient Li ethnic minority villages, and traditional Chinese temples. This guide will help you discover the best of Baoting, from natural wonders to cultural treasures.

List of Places to Visit in Baoting

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Baoting is a treasure trove of cultural and historical landmarks. Some of the must-see attractions include:

  • Qixianling Hot Spring National Forest Park: This park is famous for its natural hot springs and breathtaking mountain scenery. It is also home to the Li Minority Cultural Exhibition Hall, which showcases the history and culture of the local ethnic group.

  • Nanqin Village: This ancient Li village is a living museum of Li culture, showcasing traditional clothing, music, cuisine, and customs. The village is also home to the Li Ethnic Minority Culture Museum, which houses a collection of historical artifacts and cultural relics.

  • Houjia Village: This traditional Chinese village is famous for its well-preserved architecture and cultural heritage. It is home to a number of ancient temples, including the Xianfo Temple, which dates back to the Ming dynasty.

Natural Attractions of Baoting: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Baoting is a paradise for nature lovers, with its lush rainforests, crystal-clear rivers, and stunning mountain views. Here are some of the best natural attractions you can visit in Baoting:

  • Yanoda Rainforest Cultural Tourism Zone: This rainforest park is a must-visit for outdoor enthusiasts. It features numerous hiking trails, suspension bridges, and zip lines, allowing visitors to explore the rainforest from different perspectives.

  • Jiulong Waterfall: This is one of the most spectacular waterfalls in Hainan, with seven cascading tiers surrounded by lush greenery. The waterfall is easily accessible by car and a perfect place to take a dip in the cool water on a hot day.

  • Baoting Reservoir: This large reservoir is a popular spot for fishing, boating, and water sports. It is also surrounded by scenic mountains and forests, making it a great place for picnics or leisurely walks.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Baoting

Baoting’s stunning natural scenery is best explored on foot or by bike. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes you can take in Baoting:

  • Seven Fairy Mountain Trail: This is a challenging but rewarding hike that takes you through lush rainforests and up to the summit of Seven Fairy Mountain. The trail is about 20 kilometers long and takes approximately six hours to complete.

  • Round-the-Lake Bike Trail: This is an easy and scenic bike trail that takes you around the Baoting Reservoir. The trail is about 20 kilometers long and is perfect for a leisurely bike ride or a picnic by the lake.

Best Things to Do in Baoting: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best activities and tours you can enjoy in Baoting:

  • Hot Springs Spa: Baoting is known for its natural hot springs, which are believed to have therapeutic properties. There are several hot spring resorts in Baoting where you can soak in the hot spring pools and enjoy the lush surroundings.

  • Rafting Adventure: The rivers in Baoting are perfect for rafting, with their clear water and thrilling rapids. You can book a guided rafting tour with local tour operators and enjoy a fun and adventurous day on the water.

Week-Long Itinerary for Baoting

Here is a suggested week-long itinerary for Baoting:

Day 1: Arrive in Baoting, check into your hotel, and relax in a hot spring spa.

Day 2: Visit Qixianling Hot Spring National Forest Park and explore the Li Minority Cultural Exhibition Hall.

Day 3: Take a hiking tour of Seven Fairy Mountain and enjoy the stunning views.

Day 4: Visit Nanqin Village and learn about Li culture and customs.

Day 5: Take a relaxing bike ride around the Baoting Reservoir and enjoy a picnic by the lake.

Day 6: Experience the thrill of rafting on the crystal-clear rivers of Baoting.

Day 7: Depart Baoting and continue your journey to other destinations in Hainan.

Food and Drink Scene of Baoting

Must-Try Dishes

Baoting’s cuisine is a blend of Li and Han Chinese culture, featuring fresh seafood, traditional Li dishes, and spicy Sichuan-style cuisine. Here are some of the must-try dishes in Baoting:

  • Baoting Chicken: A local specialty that is prepared with a secret blend of herbs and spices and served with rice.

  • Coconut Rice: A sweet and fragrant rice dish that is infused with coconut milk.

  • Seafood Hotpot: A spicy and flavorful hotpot that features a variety of fresh seafood, meat, and vegetables.

Local Beverages

Baoting is famous for its coconut water, which is sweet and refreshing. You can also try the local rice wine, which is made from glutinous rice and has a smooth and mellow flavor.

Where to Stay in Baoting: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are a variety of accommodation options in Baoting, ranging from luxury resorts to budget-friendly hostels. Some of the most popular options include:

  • InterContinental Sanya Resort: A luxurious resort that features a private beach, multiple swimming pools, and a spa.

  • Jenny’s Country Inn: A charming guesthouse that offers affordable rooms and a peaceful garden setting.

  • Baoting Hot Spring Resort: A hot spring resort that features private hot spring pools and beautiful mountain views.

What to Buy in Baoting: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for unique souvenirs or gifts to bring back from Baoting, here are some of the best options:

  • Coconut Products: Baoting is known for its high-quality coconuts, which are used to produce a variety of products, including coconut oil, coconut milk, and coconut candy.

  • Handicrafts: You can find a variety of beautiful handicrafts in Baoting, made by local artisans using traditional techniques. Look for handwoven baskets, pottery, and wood carvings.

Local Culture and Customs of Baoting

The Li ethnic minority is an integral part of Baoting’s culture and heritage. The Li people have their unique language, customs, and festivals. Be sure to respect their beliefs and values while visiting Li villages and attending traditional ceremonies.

Best Time to Visit Baoting

The best time to visit Baoting is from November to April when the weather is mild and dry. During this time, you can enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and rafting without being too hot or too wet.

Do’s and Don’ts of Baoting

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.

  • Dress modestly when visiting temples, villages, and other cultural landmarks.

  • Try the local cuisine and delicacies.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local customs or traditions.

  • Wear revealing clothing in public places.

  • Take pictures without asking for permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Baoting

Baoting is a safe and peaceful destination with a low crime rate. However, it is always advisable to take basic safety precautions such as keeping your valuables secure and staying in well-lit areas at night.

Health in Baoting

Baoting is generally a healthy destination, but it is advisable to take basic health precautions such as drinking bottled water, using sunscreen, and avoiding street food that may not be prepared in hygienic conditions.

Getting to Baoting: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Baoting

The nearest airport to Baoting is Sanya Phoenix International Airport, which is about 70 kilometers away. From the airport, you can take a taxi or shuttle bus to Baoting.

Bus Stations Near Baoting

Baoting has a bus station that connects with major cities in Hainan, including Haikou, Sanya, and Wuzhishan.

Other Options

You can also hire a car or take a private shuttle bus to Baoting from other destinations in Hainan.

How to Get Around in Baoting

There are several ways to get around in Baoting, including:

  • Taxis: Taxis are widely available in Baoting and offer a convenient way to get around town.

  • Buses: Baoting has a public bus system that connects with major attractions and destinations.

  • Bicycles: You can rent bicycles in Baoting and explore the town at your own pace.

Where to Go Next from Baoting: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Baoting is a great base from which to explore other destinations in Hainan, such as:

  • Sanya: A popular beach destination with white sand beaches and turquoise waters.

  • Wuzhishan: A mountainous area with stunning views and cultural landmarks such as the Five Finger Mountain and Wuzhishan National Park.

  • Haikou: The capital city of Hainan, known for its historical landmarks, seafood markets, and shopping districts.

Conclusion

Baoting is a hidden gem in Hainan that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and exciting outdoor adventures. Whether you’re looking to relax in a hot spring spa or go on a thrilling rafting adventure, Baoting has something for everyone. Use this guide to plan your trip and discover the best of Baoting.
