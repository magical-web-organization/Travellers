Introduction to Wanning

Located in the south of China’s Hainan province, Wanning is a seaside city blessed with stunning natural landscapes, rich culture, and warm hospitality. Known for its pristine beaches, tropical rainforests, and ancient temples, Wanning is a must-visit destination for any traveler seeking a unique holiday experience. In this guide, we will take a closer look at the best things to do, see, and taste in Wanning, as well as provide some tips on how to make the most of your trip.

List of Places to Visit in Wanning

Wanning offers a wide range of attractions that will satisfy any traveler’s interests, whether you’re a culture buff, nature lover, or adventurer. Here are the top must-see destinations in Wanning.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Wanning is home to many historical sites and museums that offer a glimpse into the city’s rich cultural heritage. Some of the most notable ones include:

  • Xinglong Tropical Botanical Garden: Located 45 km east of Wanning, this garden is the largest of its kind in China, covering over 1,000 hectares of land with more than 10,000 species of tropical plants.
  • Wanning Yanoda Rainforest Cultural Tourism Zone: This scenic area is surrounded by pristine rainforests, waterfalls, and streams, and is a great place for hiking, camping, and exploring local culture.
  • Li Minority Cultural Park: This park showcases the unique culture and traditions of the Li minority, one of the most ancient ethnic groups in China. Visitors can enjoy ethnic dances, music performances, and purchase local handicrafts.

Natural Attractions of Wanning: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Wanning is renowned for its stunning natural beauty, from pristine beaches to lush tropical forests. Here are some of the top natural attractions in Wanning:

  • Riyuewan Bay: This crescent-shaped bay, also known as the Sun Moon Bay, is one of the most beautiful beaches in China. With crystal clear waters, soft sand, and a serene atmosphere, Riyuewan Bay is a perfect place to unwind and soak up the sun.
  • Nanwan Monkey Island: Located 15 km south of Wanning, this island is home to over 2000 macaques, as well as other tropical animals such as parrots, flamingos, and peacocks. Visitors can watch the monkeys’ daily life, feed them, and even take part in monkey performances.
  • Hainan Qizhi Linghot Spring: This hot spring is situated in the middle of a lush rainforest, and boasts crystal clear water, a comfortable temperature. It is said to have healing properties, and is a popular destination for wellness seekers.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Wanning

Wanning has many scenic hiking and biking trails that will take you through lush rainforests, stunning coastlines, and charming villages. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Xinglong Tropical Botanical Garden Hiking Trail: This 6 km trail will take you through a stunning rainforest, past waterfalls and streams, and to the top of a hill that offers panoramic views of the garden and the sea.
  • Wanning Yanoda Rainforest Cultural Tourism Zone Biking Trail: This 20 km trail connects 11 scenic spots within the Yanoda rainforest, showcasing the diverse landscape and culture of the area.
  • Dongshan Ridge Hiking Trail: This 8 km trail takes you through a scenic area known for its stunning views of the coastline, hills, and rice terraces.

Best Things to Do in Cebu: Activities and Tours

Wanning offers many exciting activities and tours that will make your trip unforgettable. Here are some of the best things to do in Wanning:

  • Surfing at Riyuewan Bay: There are many surf schools and rental shops around Riyuewan Bay that offer lessons and equipment for both beginners and experienced surfers.
  • Hiking at Jianfengling National Forest Park: This national park is the largest tropical rainforest in China, and boasts stunning natural scenery, as well as rare wildlife and plants species.
  • Snorkeling and Diving at Xinglong Tropical Ocean World: This theme park has an indoor oceanarium that showcases over 10,000 marine species, as well as an outdoor snorkeling and diving area that allows visitors to get up close with sea turtles, stingrays, and other exotic creatures.

Food and Drink Scene of Wanning

Wanning is a foodie’s paradise, with a diverse range of local specialties that reflect the city’s rich cultural heritage and tropical surroundings. Here are some of the must-try dishes and drinks in Wanning:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Hainanese Chicken Rice: This dish consists of tender steamed chicken served with fragrant rice that has been cooked in chicken broth and pandan leaves. It is one of the most popular dishes in Hainan cuisine.
  • Hainanese Wenchang Chicken: This dish originated from Wenchang city in Hainan, and is made from a type of free-range chicken that has tender meat and a rich flavor.
  • Hele Crab: This type of crab is indigenous to Hainan, and is known for its succulent meat and unique flavor. It is often steamed or boiled, and served with a dipping sauce made from ginger and vinegar.

Local Beverages

  • Coconut Juice: Hainan is famous for its delicious and refreshing coconut juice, which can be found at most local restaurants and cafes.
  • Hainan Coffee: Hainan has a long history of coffee cultivation, and produces some of the finest coffee beans in China. Visitors can enjoy a cup of local coffee at the many coffee shops and cafes around Wanning.

Where to Stay in Wanning: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Wanning offers a wide range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxury resorts. Here are some of the best options:

  • Shangri-La’s Sanya Resort & Spa: This 5-star beachfront resort offers stunning sea views, luxurious amenities, and a range of activities for guests of all ages.
  • Starway Hotel Wanning Xinglong Bus Station: This budget-friendly hotel is located in the city center, and offers clean and comfortable rooms, as well as easy access to local attractions.
  • Hainan Lvyou Mei’er Guest House: This charming guesthouse is situated in a quiet area near Riyuewan Bay, and offers cozy rooms, friendly service, and a peaceful atmosphere.

What to Buy in Wanning: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Wanning has a rich and diverse culture, and offers many unique souvenirs and local products that make great gifts for friends and family back home. Here are some of the best things to buy in Wanning:

  • Wanning Traditional Carpets: These hand-woven carpets are made from natural materials and feature intricate designs and patterns. They are highly durable and make a great addition to any home decor.
  • Hainanese Coconut Candy: This sweet and delicious candy is made from fresh coconut milk, sugar, and other natural ingredients, and is a popular snack in Hainan.
  • Hainan Pepper: Hainan is known for its spicy and flavorful peppers, which can be used in a variety of dishes and sauces. They make a great gift for any foodie and spice lover.

Local Culture and Customs of Wanning

Wanning has a unique culture that is influenced by its tropical climate, ethnic diversity, and long history. Here are some of the customs and traditions that you might encounter during your visit:

  • Li Minority Culture: The Li minority is one of the oldest ethnic groups in China, and has a distinctive culture, language, and way of life. Visitors can learn about their traditions and customs through various cultural activities and performances.
  • Hainanese Hospitality: Hainanese people are known for their warm and friendly attitudes towards visitors, and will often go out of their way to make you feel welcome and comfortable.
  • Tropical Festivals: Wanning celebrates many festivals throughout the year, including the Hainan Coconut Festival, the Wanning Yachting Festival, and the Li Minority Singing Festival. These festivals showcase the rich culture and traditions of the region, and are a great way to experience local life.

Best Time to Visit Wanning

The best time to visit Wanning is from November to March when the temperatures are cooler and the skies are clear. During this time, visitors can enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and beach walks without the heat and humidity of the summer months. However, keep in mind that this is also the peak tourist season, so expect larger crowds and higher prices.

Do’s and Don’ts of Wanning

To help you make the most of your trip and avoid any cultural mistakes, here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine and drinks, as they are a big part of Wanning’s culture and identity.
  • Do dress appropriately when visiting temples or other religious sites, covering your shoulders and knees.
  • Do carry cash with you, as some smaller restaurants and shops may not accept credit cards.

Don’t:

  • Don’t take photos of local people or their belongings without asking for permission first.
  • Don’t litter or damage the natural environment, as Wanning is known for its pristine beaches and rainforests.
  • Don’t disrespect local customs or traditions, such as removing your shoes when entering someone’s home.

Safety and Health

Wanning is a safe and welcoming city, with a low crime rate and friendly locals. However, it is always important to take precautions when traveling to a new destination. Here are some tips for staying safe and healthy during your trip:

Safety in Wanning

  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially when walking alone or at night.
  • Avoid carrying too much cash or wearing valuable jewelry in public.
  • Always wear sunscreen and stay hydrated when going outdoors, especially during the summer months.

Health in Wanning

  • Check with your doctor before traveling to make sure that you are up-to-date with all necessary vaccinations.
  • Be cautious when consuming food or water from street vendors or local markets, as they may not be properly prepared or cleaned.
  • Carry a basic first-aid kit with you, including any necessary medications and treatments.

Getting to Wanning: Transportation Options and Logistics

Wanning is easily accessible by air, land, and sea, making it a convenient destination for travelers from all over the world.

Airports Near Wanning

The closest airport to Wanning is the Sanya Phoenix International Airport, which is located approximately 70 km away. From the airport, visitors can take a taxi or bus to Wanning city center.

Bus Stations Near Wanning

Wanning has two major bus stations, the Wanning Bus Station and the Xinglong Bus Station, which offer frequent connections to other cities in Hainan province and mainland China.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Wanning by train, ferry, or car. There are several train stations and ferry terminals in Hainan province that connect to major cities in mainland China, while rental cars are also available at many airports and train stations.

How to Get Around in Wanning

Wanning has a well-developed public transportation system, including buses, taxis, and trains. Buses and taxis are the most convenient and affordable ways to get around the city, with taxi fares starting at around CNY 10 and bus fares ranging from CNY 1-2. For longer distances, visitors can also take the train or rent a car.

Where to Go Next from Wanning: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Wanning is a perfect base for exploring the rest of Hainan province, which is home to many other scenic and cultural destinations. Some of the top nearby destinations and excursions include:

  • Sanya: Known as the “Hawaii of China,” Sanya is a popular beach and resort city with stunning scenery and tropical weather.
  • Haikou: The capital city of Hainan province, Haikou offers a mix of modern and traditional culture, as well as historic sites and scenic attractions.
  • Wuzhishan Mountain: Located in the center of Hainan, Wuzhishan Mountain is one of the most scenic mountain ranges in China, with lush forests, waterfalls, and hiking trails.

Conclusion

Wanning is a hidden gem of Hainan province, with stunning natural scenery, rich cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. Whether you’re looking for relaxation, adventure, or cultural immersion, Wanning has something to offer for every traveler. We hope this guide has been helpful in planning your trip, and that you have an unforgettable experience in Wanning.
