Introduction to Baisha

Baisha, a city located in the Hainan province of China, is known for its historical, cultural, and natural treasures. It is a popular tourist destination and offers a lot to explore, from its beautiful parks and scenic views to its ancient museums and cultural sites. Baisha is known for its traditional Hainanese architecture, friendly locals, succulent cuisine, and serene environment. Whether you are looking for a weekend getaway or a week-long vacation, Baisha is the perfect place to visit.

List of Places to Visit in Baisha

If you’re planning a trip to Baisha, you’d be spoilt for choice when it comes to sightseeing opportunities. Here are some of the most popular tourist attractions in Baisha:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Baisha Ancient City: Also known as the “Marine Silk Road Atlantis,” this ancient city dates back to the Song Dynasty and is a great place to learn about the history of Hainan Province.
  • Baisha Tianhou Temple: The temple is dedicated to Mazu, the goddess of the sea, and has a rich history.
  • Hainan Museum: The museum houses a vast collection of exhibits showcasing the rich culture and history of Hainan.

Natural Attractions of Baisha: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Baishaling Park: This park is known for its beautiful natural landscapes and has an artificial waterfall and a lake.
  • Jiefang Lane & Zhongshan Lane: These lanes are lined with old trees and offer great views of the city from the hilltop.
  • Jiaji Village: A picturesque and secluded village known for its beautiful lawns and coconut trees.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Baisha

Baisha offers a variety of hiking and biking trails for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the popular routes:

  • Baishaling to Baisha Tianhou Temple: This scenic route offers views of Baishaling Park, Baisha Ancient City, and Baisha Tianhou Temple.
  • Yangshan Mountain Scenic Area: This area is located along the coast and has scenic hiking trails offering beautiful views of the sea and the mountains.

Best Things to Do in Baisha: Activities and Tours

Baisha offers a lot to see and do for tourists. Here are some of the best things to do in Baisha:

  • Take a trip to the top of Jiefang Tower for panoramic views of the city.
  • Enjoy a hot spring spa at Guantang Hot Spring Resort.
  • Try the famous Hainanese dishes at the Baisha Sijia Restaurant.

Week-Long Itinerary for Baisha

If you’re planning to spend a week in Baisha, here’s a suggested itinerary:

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrive in Baisha and explore Baisha Ancient City, Baisha Tianhou Temple, and Hainan Museum
Day 2 Visit Baishaling Park and Jiefang Lane & Zhongshan Lane
Day 3 Take a hiking tour of Yangshan Mountain Scenic Area
Day 4 Explore the Jiaji Village and the Cunjin Bridge
Day 5 Relax in Guantang Hot Spring Resort
Day 6 Take a bike tour of Baisha and enjoy the local cuisine
Day 7 Depart from Baisha

Food and Drink Scene of Baisha

Baisha is known for its unique Hainanese cuisine, which is a combination of traditional Chinese and Southeast Asian flavors. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Wenchang Chicken: This dish is made with a special breed of chicken that’s known for its succulent meat.
  • Jiaji Noodle Soup: This hearty soup contains fresh greens and chewy noodles.
  • Baisha Rice Vermicelli: This dish is made with thin vermicelli noodles and seafood broth and topped with crispy fried peanuts and vegetables.

Local Beverages

  • Coconut Water: The island is known for its fresh and sweet coconut water, which is available all year round.
  • Hainanese Coffee: This coffee is brewed with local coffee beans and is best enjoyed with sweetened condensed milk.

Where to Stay in Baisha: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Baisha offers a range of options when it comes to accommodation. Here are some popular choices:

  • Hilton Haikou: A luxurious hotel with modern amenities and magnificent sea views.
  • Haikou Marriott Hotel: A five-star hotel with an outdoor pool and an on-site restaurant serving international cuisine.
  • Shuangyang Hotel: A budget-friendly hotel with clean and cozy rooms and friendly staff.

What to Buy in Baisha: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Baisha offers a range of souvenirs and local products that make great gifts for friends and family. Here are some popular choices:

  • Hainan Coffee: The island is known for its locally-grown coffee beans, which make a great gift for coffee lovers.
  • Hainan Pearls: The island is known for its pearls, which come in various shapes and sizes and make beautiful jewelry.
  • Hand-crafted Lanterns: These lanterns are handcrafted by local artisans and come in vibrant colors and designs.

Local Culture and Customs of Baisha

Baisha has a rich cultural heritage and some unique customs. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • It is customary to remove shoes before entering a temple or someone’s home.
  • It is impolite to touch someone’s head or point your finger at someone.

Best Time to Visit Baisha

The best time to visit Baisha is from October to May when the weather is comfortable and cool. Summers can be hot and humid, and rainfall is common.

Do’s and Don’ts of Baisha

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind during your trip:

Do:

  • Learn some basic Chinese phrases before you arrive.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing while visiting temples or other cultural sites.
  • Photograph locals without their permission.

Safety and Health

Here are some safety and health tips to keep in mind during your trip:

Safety in Baisha

  • Take care of your belongings and keep an eye on your surroundings.
  • Use licensed taxis or ride-sharing services.

Health in Baisha

  • Avoid drinking tap water and stick to bottled water.
  • Apply sunscreen regularly and wear a hat and sunglasses to protect yourself from the sun.

Getting to Baisha: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here are some transportation options to consider when traveling to Baisha:

Airports Near Baisha

  • Haikou Meilan International Airport: The airport is about 32 km from Baisha and is served by several international and domestic airlines.

Bus Stations Near Baisha

  • Haikou East Bus Station: The station is about 41 km from Baisha and offers bus services to several destinations.

Other Options

  • Taxis and ride-sharing services are available in Haikou and can be used to get to Baisha.

How to Get Around in Baisha

Baisha is a small city, and most tourist attractions are within walking distance of each other. Taxis and motorbikes are also available for hire.

Where to Go Next from Baisha: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Sanya: A popular tropical destination known for its beaches, hot springs, and rainforests.
  • Haikou: The capital city of Hainan Province and known for its diverse culture, cuisine, and architecture.
  • Wuzhishan: A mountainous area in central Hainan known for its scenic views and outdoor adventures.

Conclusion

Baisha is a hidden gem in the Hainan province and offers a lot to see, do, and explore. Whether you’re interested in learning about the history and culture of the island or want to relax and enjoy its natural beauty, Baisha has something for everyone. With its unique cuisine, friendly locals, and serene environment, Baisha is the perfect place to unwind and experience the best of Hainan.
