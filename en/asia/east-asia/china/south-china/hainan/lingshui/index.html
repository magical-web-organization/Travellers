Introduction to Lingshui

Located in the Hainan province of South China, Lingshui is a stunning tropical destination that offers an eye-catching blend of natural attractions, cultural landmarks, and heritage sites. The city boasts a beautiful coastline along with the magnificent Wuzhishan Mountain in the backdrop. Lingshui’s tranquil scenic beauty, along with its mouth-watering cuisine and recreational activities, makes it an ideal destination for tourists.

List of Places to Visit in Lingshui

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Yaji Ancient City: A historical town is known for preserving the indigenous cultural traditions and customs of the Li and Miao ethnic groups.
  • The Lingshui Cultural Palace: This museum showcases the rich history, culture, art, and handicrafts of Lingshui.
  • The Dongpo Academy: Built-in the memory of Su Shi or Su Dongpo, this academy preserves Chinese calligraphy, prose, and culture.

Natural Attractions of Lingshui: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Nantian Hot Spring: Boasting a collection of natural hot springs, it’s a perfect spot for tourists to relax and unwind from the hustle-bustle of daily life.
  • Diaoluoshan Jungle Park: The mountain is located in the southeast of Hainan Island and is 18.8km from Lingshui County. With pristine waterfalls, lush forests, and diverse flora and fauna, it is an excellent spot to experience the natural beauty of the jungle.
  • Dazhou Island: Known for its breathtaking scenery, this island is a must-visit attraction. Take a ferry to this beautiful island to witness the natural beauty of the sea.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lingshui

Hiking Routes

  • Diaoluoshan Nature Reserve Hiking Trail: Take the hiking trail to trek the mountains of Wuzhishan, which is the highest mountain in Hainan.
  • Lukou Rice Terrace: With a well-developed eco-tourism program, these rice terraces offer an excellent opportunity for hiking while enjoying the natural scenery.

Biking Routes

  • Coastline Road: This biking route stretches 60 km and offers stunning views of the coastline and sea.
  • Zuoling Mountain Road: This road comprises steep hills and mountain paths and is mostly preferred by adventure enthusiasts.

Best Things to Do in Lingshui: Activities and Tours

  • Beach Hopping: Lingshui has a long stretch of magnificent beaches that are perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water activities.
  • Lingshui Li Autonomous County Li & Miao Minority Culture Park: It’s a fascinating theme park that exhibits the rich traditions, customs, and arts of the Li and Miao minority communities.
  • Fishing Trip: Book a fishing trip at Yalong Bay and catch some local fresh fish.

Food and Drink Scene of Lingshui

Must-Try Dishes

  • Hainanese Chicken Rice: This dish is made of fragrant rice cooked with chicken and is a popular dish in Hainan.
  • Lingshui Seafood: Lingshui is known for its fresh seafood, including prawns, crabs, and fish.

Local Beverages

  • Coconut Water: As Lingshui is situated near the sea, coconut water is readily available and a popular choice for refreshing drinks.
  • Jiucaijang: A popular alcoholic beverage made from glutinous rice and local plant jiucai.

Where to Stay in Lingshui: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Lingshui offers a wide range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxurious hotels.

  • Sanya Wuzhishan International Tourism Resort: Surrounded by mountains, this resort is a great pick for travelers who love hiking and outdoor adventures.
  • Wuzhi Mountain Sheraton: This luxurious resort is located at the foot of Wuzhishan Mountain and offers a peaceful getaway for travelers.

What to Buy in Lingshui: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Xianliang Rice: Known for its tasty flavor and high nutritional value, this type of rice is a great souvenir to bring home.
  • Shell Handicrafts: Lingshui is known for its shell handicrafts, which make for perfect souvenirs and gifts.

Local Culture and Customs of Lingshui

  • The Li and Miao Ethnic Groups: The Li and Miao ethnic communities are the prominent cultural groups of Lingshui, with their unique customs, traditional dresses, and cultural practices.
  • Respecting Elders: In the Chinese culture, respecting elders is valued highly, and visitors are expected to follow this custom while interacting with the locals in Lingshui.

Best Time to Visit Lingshui

The best time to visit Lingshui is from November to April when the temperature is pleasant, and the weather is dry.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lingshui

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Carry identification documents at all times.
  • Try the local food and delicacies.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs or videos without permission.
  • Wear revealing clothes at religious or cultural sites.
  • Litter around natural or cultural landmarks.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lingshui

Lingshui is a relatively safe city with low crime rates. However, tourists should remain vigilant and follow safety rules and guidelines.

Health in Lingshui

It’s important to stay hydrated and protected from the sun while exploring the city. Tourists are advised to take necessary vaccines and medications before traveling.

Getting to Lingshui: Transport Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lingshui

  • Sanya Phoenix International Airport: The airport is located approximately 60 km from Lingshui and provides easy access to the city.

Bus Stations Near Lingshui

  • Lingshui Bus Station: Lingshui bus station is the primary bus station in the city that connects to nearby locations.

Other Options

  • Train: Lingshui does not have a railway station; however, Sanya and Haikou railway stations are the nearest railheads that connect to Lingshui.

How to Get Around in Lingshui

  • Taxi: Taxis are widely available, and it’s a convenient and comfortable option to travel around the city.
  • Bus: Buses are affordable and connect to major tourist attractions and neighborhoods in the city.

Where to Go Next from Lingshui: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Sanya: The city is renowned for its hot springs, beaches, and tropical landscapes.
  • Haikou: The capital of Hainan Province, Haikou, is a commercial hub and renowned for its historical landmarks, parks, and gardens.

Conclusion

Lingshui, with its stunning scenery, cultural landmarks, and heritage sites, offers a memorable tropical vacation experience. Tourists can hike the Wuzhishan Mountain, relax at the Nantian Hot Spring, explore the Diaoluoshan Jungle Park, and indulge in mouth-watering seafood cuisine. Plan a perfect trip to Lingshui with this comprehensive guide and explore the vast natural and cultural treasures the city has to offer.
