Introduction to Houjie

Houjie is a bustling town located in the heart of Pearl River Delta, the manufacturing hub of Guangdong Province. It is easily accessible from Shenzhen and Guangzhou, making it a popular destination for business travelers as well as tourists. The town has a rich cultural heritage and is home to a number of historic attractions, outdoor adventures, and delicious local cuisine. In this guide, we will explore everything Houjie has to offer, from top tourist destinations to hidden gems.

List of Places to Visit in Houjie

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Houjie is home to several museums and historical sites that showcase the town’s rich cultural heritage. Some of the must-visit places include:

  • Houjie Ancient Town: This is the heart of Houjie and boasts traditional architecture and ancient temples.
  • Huayi Recreation Creative Park: Located in the former Huayi Shoe Factory, this is the best place to learn about the history of footwear manufacturing in the town.
  • Taiping Old Village: This village showcases the traditional architecture and lifestyle of the Lingnan area.
  • Dongguan Science and Technology Museum: This museum has interactive exhibits that showcase the latest technologies and innovations.

Natural Attractions of Houjie: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Houjie has a lot to offer for outdoor enthusiasts with its scenic views and diverse landscape. Some of the top natural attractions of Houjie are:

  • Matsu Mountain: A popular hiking spot where you can enjoy panoramic views of Houjie and the surrounding areas.
  • Lotus Mountain: This scenic spot is located on the outskirts of town and is famous for its lotus ponds and ancient temples.
  • Shuilianshan National Forest Park: This is an ideal destination for eco-tourism with its lush greenery and rare species of plants and animals.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Houjie

Houjie offers many hiking and biking routes that connect various natural and cultural attractions. Some of the top routes are:

  • Lotus Mountain Loop: This route takes you through the scenic Lotus Mountain and its surrounding areas.
  • Houjie Ancient Town Trail: This route takes you through the historic lanes and alleys of Houjie Ancient Town.

Best Things to Do in Houjie: Activities and Tours

Houjie has plenty of activities and tours for travelers of all interests. Some of the must-try activities are:

  • Dongguan Lotus Hill Golf Course: This is one of the best golf courses in China with stunning views of Lotus Mountain.
  • Houjie Food and Culture Tour: This tour takes you to the best local eateries and cultural hotspots of the town.
  • Huayi Leisureland: This is an amusement park with thrill rides, games, and entertainment for all ages.

Week-Long Itinerary for Houjie

Day 1: Arrive in Houjie and explore Houjie Ancient Town Day 2: Visit Huayi Recreation Creative Park and Dongguan Science and Technology Museum Day 3: Take a hiking or biking tour around Lotus Mountain Day 4: Enjoy a round of golf at Dongguan Lotus Hill Golf Course Day 5: Take a day trip to nearby Shenzhen or Guangzhou Day 6: Explore the natural beauty around Shuilianshan National Forest Park Day 7: Try the local cuisine and buy souvenirs before departing Houjie

Food and Drink Scene of Houjie

Houjie is famous for its diverse cuisine, from traditional Cantonese dishes to international flavors. Some of the must-try dishes and local beverages are:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Shuijing Chicken: This dish is made by slow-cooking chicken with Shuijing rice wine, soy sauce, and spices.
  • Fried Shrimps with Euryale Ferox: This is a popular seafood dish made with fresh shrimps and Euryale Ferox, a local aquatic vegetable.
  • Hakka Stewed Pork: This is a classic pork stew made with a variety of herbs and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Shuixian Tea: This is a famous oolong tea from Shuixian village which has a unique flavor and aroma.
  • Houjie Beer: This is a locally brewed beer with a crisp and refreshing taste.

Where to Stay in Houjie: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Houjie has many options for accommodation ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. Some of the top picks include:

  • Pullman Dongguan Forum: This is a luxury hotel with elegantly designed rooms and exceptional service.
  • Home Inn: This budget-friendly hotel chain has several locations in Houjie and offers clean and comfortable rooms.
  • Yijia Holiday Hotel: This is a charming guesthouse with a traditional Chinese courtyard and modern amenities.

What to Buy in Houjie: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Houjie is famous for its shoe manufacturing industry, and you can find several local products related to this industry. Some of the top souvenirs to buy are:

  • Leather Shoes and Bags: You can find a variety of leather products like shoes, bags, and wallets from numerous shops in Houjie.
  • Ceramic Tea Sets: Houjie is also famous for its ceramic tea sets which make a perfect souvenir to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Houjie

Houjie has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its traditional architecture, music, and cuisine. The people of Houjie are warm and friendly and take pride in their local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Houjie

The best time to visit Houjie is between September and December when the weather is mild and pleasant. Avoid visiting during the summer months as it can be hot and humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Houjie

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Bargain when shopping in markets

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing in cultural and religious places
  • Speak too loudly in public places
  • Jaywalk on busy roads

Safety and Health

Safety in Houjie

Houjie is a safe place for travelers, with low crime rates and helpful locals. However, it’s always a good idea to keep your belongings safe and be aware of your surroundings.

Health in Houjie

Houjie has good medical facilities, and you can find pharmacies and hospitals in most areas. However, it’s essential to carry a first aid kit and necessary medications while traveling.

Getting to Houjie: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Houjie

The nearest airports to Houjie are Shenzhen Bao’an International Airport and Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport. You can take a bus or taxi from the airport to Houjie.

Bus Stations Near Houjie

Houjie has a well-connected bus station with regular buses to cities like Shenzhen, Guangzhou, and Hong Kong.

Other Options

You can also reach Houjie by train or private car. The town has a railway station, and several car rental services are available.

How to Get Around in Houjie

Houjie has an extensive public transportation system, including buses, taxis, and metro. You can also rent a bike or take a walking tour to explore the town’s attractions.

Where to Go Next from Houjie: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Houjie is an excellent base for exploring nearby attractions like Shenzhen, Guangzhou, and Hong Kong. You can take a day trip to these cities, or explore the natural beauty in the surrounding areas.

Conclusion

Houjie, with its cultural heritage, natural beauty, and delicious cuisine, is a perfect destination for travelers looking to explore the lesser-known areas of China. Plan your trip with this guide, and you will surely have a memorable experience.
