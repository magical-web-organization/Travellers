Introduction to Pearl River Delta

Located in the southern part of China, Pearl River Delta is a bustling region that comprises nine cities in Guangdong, including Guangzhou, Shenzhen, and Dongguan. It serves as a gateway to China from Hong Kong and Macau, and is also an important trade and manufacturing hub. Known for its rich culture, natural beauty, and gastronomic delights, Pearl River Delta is a great destination for travelers who want to experience the best of modern China.

Best Time to Visit Pearl River Delta

The best time to visit Pearl River Delta is during autumn (October-November) and spring (March-April), when the weather is pleasant and there is little rainfall.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pearl River Delta

Do:

  • Try the local food and drinks
  • Visit museums and historical sites
  • Learn some basic Cantonese phrases
  • Take public transportation
  • Be respectful to elders and locals

Don’t:

  • Speak loudly in public
  • Be late for appointments
  • Skip bargaining
  • Leave chopsticks standing vertically in a bowl
  • Touch someone’s head without permission

Safety and Health

Safety in Pearl River Delta

Overall, Pearl River Delta is safe for tourists. However, it’s always recommended to take precautions such as avoiding dark and secluded streets at night and securing your belongings.

Health in Pearl River Delta

It’s important to bring appropriate medication and to be up-to-date on your vaccinations before visiting Pearl River Delta. Also, remember to drink bottled water and be careful with street food.
