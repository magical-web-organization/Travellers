Jiangmen, a city situated in the Pearl River Delta of Guangdong, China, has a rich history that dates back to ancient times. Featuring unique artistry, architecture, landscapes, and customs, Jiangmen makes for an unforgettable traveler’s destination. The city is renowned for its colonial architecture, Chinese monastic temples, and scenic waterways.

Introduction to Jiangmen

Jiangmen city has a population of over four million people and is divided into five districts. The area is blessed with rivers, mountains, and plenty of fertile farmlands that produce some of China’s best tropical fruits. The city was once the home to many overseas Chinese communities, and as such, it displays a vibrant mix of cultures.

Jiangmen’s tourist sites include the Yueshan Temple, the famous Xinhui Doushan Street, Muk Ka Yuen Temple, and The former residence of Liang Qichao. The city is also famous for its local cuisine, featuring delectable dishes such as dim sum, roast goose, and juicy meat dumplings.

List of Places to Visit in Jiangmen

Jiangmen is rife with cultural and natural attractions.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Yueshan Temple: dating back to the Tang Dynasty is the most popular historical structure in Jiangmen. The Temple is on top of a hill and offers stunning views of the pearl river delta.

  • Xinhui Doushan Street: is one of the fascinating parts of Jiangmen, with an abundance of cultural landmarks, well-preserved architectures, and vibrant alleyways.

  • Muk Ka Yuen Temple: one of the oldest temples in China’s history preserves ancient architectural styles and offers visitors a serene atmosphere.

  • The former residence of Liang Qichao: is a museum that dedicatedly restores Liang’s ‘s living conditions to vividly showcase the life condition of intellectuals during the Qing dynasty.

Natural Attractions of Jiangmen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Guifeng Mountain Scenic Area: provides visitors with access to over 72 peaks scattered throughout the 28 square kilometers natural scenic area.

  • Bird Paradise Wetland Park: a man-made eco-park situated in the east of the city, making it a perfect place for birdwatching and hiking.

  • The Heshan Phoenix Ancient Village: preserves the traces of traditional village culture and exhibits ancient ancestral tombs, temples, and ancestral hall buildings.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Jiangmen

Exploring Jiangmen with a challenging hike or a serene bike ride is an excellent way to experience the city’s scenic splendor. The following are some of the recommended routes:

  • Guifengshan National Forest Park Hiking Trail: a 4 hours hike that will take you to magnificent peaks and by lakes.

  • Guangdong Asia-Pacific International Circuit: a world-class racetrack. You can cycle around the track, which provides a unique experience of the speedway.

  • Xihui Park: offering a tranquil setting for a leisurely bike ride or hike, the park is home to waterfalls, pristine lakes, and valleys.

Best Things to Do in Jiangmen: Activities and Tours

To maximize your experience in Jiangmen, try one of the following activities:

  • Take a Dim sum cooking class: Chinese cooking class will give you the chance to learn from an expert in a friendly and fun environment.

  • Go shopping in Hakka Village: This shopping district is the perfect place to pick up some local souvenirs and snacks.

  • Visit local blacksmiths: As this industry still dominates in certain parts of the city, there are opportunities to experience some amazing blacksmiths’ workshops in the city.

Week-Long Itinerary for Jiangmen

Below is an itinerary with recommendations for a week of sightseeing in Jiangmen:

Day 1: Visit the Yueshan Temple and Xinhui Doushan Street

Day 2: Explore the Muk Ka Yuen Temple and Heshan Phoenix Ancient Village.

Day 3: Hike to the peak of the Guifeng Mountain.

Day 4: Visit Guangdong Asia-Pacific International Circuit for cycling.

Day 5: Participate in a dim sum cooking class, then go shopping in Hakka Village.

Day 6: visit the waterfront of Wuyi Naval Port.

Day 7: Relax in Xihui Park and Wolf Mountain, then enjoy a massage at one of the local spas or resorts.

Food and Drink Scene of Jiangmen

Jiangmen is a foodie’s paradise, with a unique cuisine that is moderately sweet, well balanced (not too salty), and light on the palate. The following are must-try dishes in the city:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Jiangmen Char Siu: the juicy roast pork is fatty, fragrant, and caramelized to perfection.

  • Phoenix claws: made of chicken feet that are first marinated, then steamed, deep-fried, and seasoned with spices.

  • Top-shell Shrimp: lightly fried and served with a light vinegar sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Jianmen Jasmine Tea: The tea has long been appreciated for its efficiency in refreshing and calmness.

  • Huanghe Yangju ziyinbao: it is a locally produced Chinese liquor that has a strong, distinct flavor.

Where to Stay in Jiangmen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Jiangmen offers an array of accommodation options, from luxury hotels to a hostel. The following are some recommendations:

  • Enjoyable Hotel: A highly-rated modern hotel in the heart of Jiangmen city that features facilities such as an outdoor pool, bar, and restaurant.

  • Jiangmen Palace International Hotel: a luxurious five-star hotel in the city center, providing guests with an unforgettable experience.

  • Start Cap Hostel: affordable and cozy, the hostel provides a classic experience with bunk beds in dormitory-style rooms that are clean and comfortable.

What to Buy in Jiangmen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Jiangmen produces and exports an expansive range of products. When visiting Jiangmen, consider buying some local products to take back home.

  • Jiangmen Hand-painted porcelain: is the city’s local handicraft, famous for its beautiful design and unique shape.

  • Kaiping Diaolou and Villages: This is an excellent souvenir to bring home to your loved ones to get a glimpse of traditional rural life.

Local Culture and Customs of Jiangmen

When visiting Jiangmen, make sure to research and learn more about the culture and customs of the city to avoid offending the locals.

Best Time to Visit Jiangmen

The best time to visit Jiangmen is in the spring or fall when the weather is warm and comfortable. Summers in Jiangmen are usually hot and humid, while winters can be cold.

Do’s and Don’ts of Jiangmen

Here are some recommendations to keep in mind when visiting Jiangmen:

Do:

  • Try the local cuisine

  • Learn a few Mandarin phrases.

  • Dress appropriately and respectfully concerning the culture and customs.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing

  • Take pictures without asking for permission

  • Haggling in markets and shops.

Safety and Health

Safety in Jiangmen

Jiangmen is relatively safe for travelers. However, it is wise to take necessary precautions, such as not leaving your belongings unattended in public places, especially markets, and crowded areas. Avoiding unlicensed taxis is also recommended.

Health in Jiangmen

It is advisable to seek medical advice before traveling to Jiangmen. Like any other foreign country, it is important to carry a first aid kit to help in minor medical emergencies.

Getting to Jiangmen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Jiangmen

The nearest airport to Jiangmen is Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Jiangmen

The city’s central bus station is the Jiangmen Central Bus Station.

Other Options

Travelers can get to Jiangmen via taxis, trains, and ferries.

How to Get Around in Jiangmen

Jiangmen has excellent transportation options, including buses, taxis, and metro trains.

Where to Go Next from Jiangmen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Zhuhai: Known as the “City of Lover,” the coastal city’s theme park and duty-free shopping complexes are a joy to experience.

  • Shenzhen: The city is famous for its tech industry, which means that it is an excellent place to shop for technological gadgets.

Conclusion

Jiangmen is a fantastic city with a unique mix of culture, exquisite cuisine, and natural beauty. A trip to this city is an unforgettable experience that everyone should have at least once in their lifetime. Use this comprehensive guide to plan your trip and enjoy a memorable adventure in Jiangmen.
