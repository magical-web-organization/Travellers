Introduction to Panyu and Nansha

Panyu and Nansha are two districts located in the city of Guangzhou in the Pearl River Delta region of Guangdong province in South China. Panyu is the largest district in Guangzhou and has a rich history dating back over 2,000 years. Nansha, on the other hand, is a newly developed area that has become a popular destination for tourists due to its beautiful scenery, outdoor activities, and modern infrastructure.

List of Places to Visit in Panyu and Nansha

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Guangzhou Crocodile Park - a wildlife park dedicated to the conservation and breeding of crocodiles and alligators.
  2. Guangdong Museum of Art - a museum showcasing traditional and contemporary Chinese art.
  3. Baomo Garden - a traditional Chinese garden with beautiful architecture and landscaping.
  4. Chimelong Paradise - an amusement park with rides and attractions for all ages.
  5. Lianhua Mountain - a beautiful mountain range with stunning scenery and hiking trails.

Natural Attractions of Panyu and Nansha: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Chimelong Safari Park - a wildlife park where you can see a variety of animals, including pandas, tigers, and elephants.
  2. Nansha Wetland Park - a beautiful park with a variety of wetland habitats and bird-watching opportunities.
  3. Nansha Tianhou Palace - a temple dedicated to the sea goddess Mazu, with beautiful architecture and cultural significance.
  4. Panyu Lotus Hill Resort - a scenic area with beautiful views, hiking trails, and cultural attractions.
  5. Panyu Xiangjiang Safari Park - a safari park where you can see a variety of animals, including lions, tigers, and giraffes.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Panyu and Nansha

  1. Lianhua Mountain Hiking Trail - a moderate hike that takes you through beautiful scenery and offers stunning views at the top.
  2. Dafeng Mountain Hiking Trail - a challenging hike that takes you to the highest peak in Guangzhou, with beautiful views of the surrounding area.
  3. Xianmuyuan Biking Trail - a scenic biking trail that takes you through rice paddies, vegetable fields, and forests.
  4. Panyu Lotus Hill Forest Park Biking Trail - a moderate biking trail that takes you through a forested park with beautiful views and cultural attractions.

Best Things to Do in Panyu and Nansha: Activities and Tours

  1. Chimelong Water Park - a water park with a variety of rides and attractions for all ages.
  2. Guangzhou Baiyun Mountain - a mountain range with beautiful scenery and hiking trails.
  3. Panyu Ziyuan Garden - a traditional Chinese garden with beautiful architecture and landscaping.
  4. Canton Tower - an iconic landmark and observation tower with stunning views of Guangzhou.
  5. Pearl River Night Cruise - a boat cruise along the Pearl River with beautiful views of the city.

Food and Drink Scene of Panyu and Nansha

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Cantonese Dim Sum - a variety of small dishes, usually served on bamboo steamer baskets, such as Shumai, Har gow and Char siu bao.
  2. Claypot Rice - a signature dish of Guangdong province, a one-pot dish of rice, meats, and vegetables cooked over an open flame.
  3. Roast Goose - a Cantonese classic dish of succulent roasted goose meat.
  4. Wild Game - a regional specialty of Panyu, such as squirrel, pheasant, or snake meat.
  5. Herbal Tea - popular among locals, herbal tea is made from a variety of boiled herbs that are believed to have health benefits.

Local Beverages

  1. Pearl River Beer - produced in Guangzhou, a light and refreshing beer.
  2. Roselle Juice - a local drink made from hibiscus flowers that is believed to have cooling properties.
  3. Rice Wine - a traditional Chinese alcoholic beverage made from glutinous rice.

Local Culture and Customs of Panyu and Nansha

  1. Cantonese Culture - Guangdong province has a rich cultural heritage, including language, cuisine, music, and festivals, that is distinct from other regions of China.
  2. Lingnan Architecture - Lingnan architecture is a unique style of architecture found in Guangdong province characterized by decorative carvings and use of natural materials.
  3. Cantonese Opera - a traditional form of Chinese opera with distinctive singing style, costumes, and makeup that is popular in Guangdong province.

Best Time to Visit Panyu and Nansha

The best time to visit Panyu and Nansha is from October to December and from March to May when the weather is mild and pleasant.

Safety and Health

Safety in Panyu and Nansha

Panyu and Nansha are generally safe for tourists. However, be aware of pickpockets and scams that target tourists. It’s also important to be cautious when crossing the street as drivers in China can be aggressive.

Health in Panyu and Nansha

It’s recommended to drink bottled water and avoid drinking tap water. It’s also important to take precautions against mosquito bites, as they can transmit diseases such as dengue and malaria.

  2. Shenzhen Bao’an International Airport - located approximately 120 km away from Panyu and Nansha.

  2. Nansha Bus Station - located in Nansha district, it has regular bus services to other cities in Guangdong province.

  2. Taxi or Private Car - taxis and private cars are widely available in Guangzhou and can be a convenient option for getting around.

How to Get Around in Panyu and Nansha

  2. Bus - Guangzhou has an extensive bus network that serves Panyu and Nansha.
