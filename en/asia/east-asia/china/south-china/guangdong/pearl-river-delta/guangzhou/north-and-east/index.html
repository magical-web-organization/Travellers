Introduction to North and East

North and East Guangzhou is a region located in the Pearl River Delta, in the Guangdong Province of South China. This area is known for its rich history, beautiful natural landscapes, and bustling urban centers. In this travel guide, we will explore everything this region has to offer.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sun Yat-sen Memorial Hall

Natural Attractions of North and East: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Baiyun Mountain

  • Huadu Liuxi River National Forest Park

Local Culture and Customs of North and East

In North and East Guangzhou, people practice tea ceremonies, martial arts, and cuisine as their cultural traditions. It is important to be aware of customs and respect them while visiting this region.

Best Time to Visit North and East

The best time to visit North and East Guangzhou is in the spring or autumn when the weather is mild and the landscape is at its most beautiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of North and East

Do:

  • Respect Chinese culture by bowing instead of shaking hands
  • Try local cuisine and beverages
  • Be respectful with your clothing choices

Don’t:

  • Touch someone’s head, shoes, or books, as it is considered disrespectful
  • Point your finger or feet at someone, as it is impolite

Safety and Health

Safety in North and East

North and East Guangzhou is a relatively safe region, but it is always important to be aware of your surroundings and belongings, especially in crowded tourist areas.

Health in North and East

It is always recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before traveling to any foreign country to ensure you are up-to-date on any necessary vaccinations. Drink bottled water and avoid street food if you have a sensitive stomach.
