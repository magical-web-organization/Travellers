Introduction to Meizhou

Meizhou is a prefecture-level city located in the northern region of Guangdong, China. The city is situated in the northeastern part of the Guangdong province and is home to a population of approximately 5 million people. In Meizhou, visitors can experience the convergence of the Hakka and Cantonese cultures, leading to a unique blend of customs and traditions.

List of Places to Visit in Meizhou

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Hakka Earth Buildings

The Hakka Earth Building is a recognized UNESCO World Heritage Site and is a must-visit for anyone traveling to Meizhou. These buildings were constructed by the Hakka people centuries ago as a form of self-defense and to protect them from invaders. The unique design of these buildings makes them an excellent example of how man and nature can coexist.

Jianying Park

The Jianying Park is another notable attraction in Meizhou. It covers an area of 4,200 square meters and is situated in the center of Meijiang District. Visitors can take a stroll through the park and admire the picturesque scenery. Additionally, the park is home to the Jianying Tower, which contains the ancient history of Meizhou.

Natural Attractions of Meizhou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Fengshuishan National Forest Park

The Fengshuishan National Forest Park boasts a vast vegetation cover measuring over 92%. The park provides visitors with stunning views of the scenic mountain ranges and a tranquil atmosphere to relax in. This location makes it an excellent spot for outdoor activities such as hiking or camping.

Nanxi Hot Springs

For those looking to relax and unwind, Nanxi Hot Springs is an excellent place to visit. The natural hot springs are believed to have therapeutic properties, and visitors can soak in the warm and bubbly water while enjoying the stunning landscapes around them.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Meizhou

Meiyan Hakka Trail

The Meiyan Hakka Trail is an 18-kilometer-long trail that takes visitors through the scenic countryside of Meizhou. The trail offers breathtaking views of nature, ancient villages, rice paddies, forests, and rivers. Visitors can hike or bike the trail and stop alongside the way to explore the various attractions that the route offers.

Best Things to Do in Meizhou: Activities and Tours

Explore the Hakka Culture

Meizhou is the perfect place to immerse yourself in the Hakka culture. Visitors can experience unique Hakka customs, enjoy traditional cuisine, and explore the earth buildings that the Hakka people are famous for. A must-try Hakka dish is the Hakka stuffed tofu, which is made from tofu, mushrooms, and minced pork.

Take a Hot Air Balloon Ride

A hot air balloon ride in Meizhou offers visitors an unforgettable experience. The ride takes you through the scenic countryside, giving you a birds-eye view of the stunning landscapes, and several ancient towns dotted along the landscape.

Visit Meizhou Island

Meizhou Island is the largest island in Guangdong, and visitors can take a ferry to the island to explore the various attractions it has to offer. The island boasts stunning beaches, seafood restaurants, water activities, and several parks. Meizhou Island is the perfect place to relax, unwind and have fun with family and friends.

Food and Drink Scene of Meizhou

Must-Try Dishes

Meizhou Beef Hotpot

The famous Meizhou Beef Hotpot is a must-try when visiting Meizhou. The hotpot is made from high-quality beef, and diners can choose from a variety of dipping sauces ranging from spicy to sweet.

Hakka Stuffed Tofu

Hakka stuffed tofu is another popular dish in Meizhou. The dish is delicious and healthy, made from tofu, mushrooms, and minced pork.

Local Beverages

Hakka Rice Wine

Hakka rice wine is a unique alcoholic beverage made from black, sweet, and glutinous rice. The wine has a sweet taste and is an excellent pairing with the local cuisine.

Tea

Meizhou is also famous for its tea culture, and visitors can enjoy a cup of tea at one of the many teahouses located in the city. The most popular types of tea in Meizhou are black tea and Oolong tea.

Local Culture and Customs of Meizhou

Meizhou prides itself on preserving its rich history and culture. The Hakka culture is an essential part of Meizhou’s identity and is celebrated through various festivals and traditions. Visitors should familiarize themselves with some of the customs and etiquette observed in the region.

Best Time to Visit Meizhou

The best time to visit Meizhou is during the autumn season. Visitors can enjoy the stunning foliage and mild weather, not too hot nor too cold.

Do’s and Don’ts of Meizhou

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages
  • Visit the various attractions in the city
  • Familiarize yourself with the local language

Don’t:

  • Engage in behavior that is disrespectful to the locals
  • Use unfamiliar backstreets and lanes

Safety and Health

Safety in Meizhou

Meizhou is a relatively safe city with a low crime rate. However, visitors should exercise caution when traveling alone at night or in unfamiliar areas.

Health in Meizhou

Visitors should drink bottled water and be cautious when eating street food. Additionally, visitors should check if any required vaccinations are needed before visiting Meizhou.

Getting to Meizhou: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Meizhou

The nearest airport to Meizhou is the Jieyang Chaoshan International Airport. From here, visitors can take a bus, a taxi, or a train to Meizhou City.

Bus Stations Near Meizhou

Visitors can take a bus to Meizhou from Shenzhen, Guangzhou, Zhuhai, and Hong Kong. The major bus stations in Meizhou include Meizhou Bus Station and Meijiang Bus Station.

Other Options

Visitors can also take a train to Meizhou. The train station in Meizhou is located at Meizhou East railway station.

How to Get Around Meizhou

Visitors can get around Meizhou through buses, taxis, and bike rentals. Buses are the most convenient and affordable method of transportation in the city.

Where to Go Next from Meizhou: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can explore the nearby destinations of Chaoshan and Shantou City. Additionally, visitors can take a ferry to explore the nearby coastal cities and islands.

Conclusion

Meizhou is a city that offers visitors a unique blend of culture, history, and natural beauty. Be sure to experience everything the city has to offer, from the Hakka culture to the natural hot springs. With our comprehensive travel guide, visitors can plan their trip accordingly and enjoy all that Meizhou has to offer.
