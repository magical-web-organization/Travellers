Introduction to West Henan

West Henan is a beautiful region located in the north-central part of Henan province, China. The area is famous for its stunning natural landscapes, ancient cultural heritage, and traditional local customs. With a rich history dating back to the Xia Dynasty, West Henan is home to many historical sites and museums, making it a must-visit destination for history lovers. In this travel guide, we will explore the top attractions, hiking routes, food and drink scene, local culture, and transportation options in West Henan.

List of Places to Visit in West Henan

Visitors to West Henan are spoiled for choice with many exciting places to visit. Here is a list of the top attractions in the region:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Shaolin Temple: The Shaolin Temple is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and the birthplace of Shaolin Kung Fu. Visitors can enjoy watching Kung Fu demonstrations and learn about the history of the temple and its monks.

  • Yinxu Ruins: Yinxu Ruins is another UNESCO World Heritage Site that dates back to the Shang Dynasty. It features a palace area and oracle bones, which were used for divination purposes in ancient times.

  • China National Orchid Garden: The China National Orchid Garden houses over ten thousand orchids, giving visitors an excellent opportunity to see these beautiful plants up close.

  • Qingming Riverside Landscape Garden: Located on the banks of the Yellow River, this garden is based on the famous painting, “Along the River During the Qingming Festival.” It depicts life in a bustling Tang Dynasty city.

Natural Attractions of West Henan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Song: Mount Song is one of the Five Sacred Mountains of China and is home to several historic temples and monasteries, including the famous Shaolin Temple. Visitors can hike the mountain trails to enjoy stunning views of the surrounding countryside.

  • Longtan Grand Canyon: Longtan Grand Canyon is a beautiful, narrow gorge with steep cliffs, waterfalls, and streams. Visitors can hike through the canyon and enjoy the tranquil natural scenery.

  • Yellow River Scenic Area: The Yellow River Scenic Area is located near the city of Zhengzhou and features beautiful views of the river and surrounding countryside. Visitors can take a boat ride along the river or hike to the top of Yueshan Hill for panoramic views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of West Henan

West Henan is an excellent destination for outdoor enthusiasts, with many fantastic hiking and biking routes to explore. Here are some of the most popular:

  • Shaolin Temple to Wuru Peak: This challenging hike is approximately 12 kilometers long and takes visitors from Shaolin Temple to Wuru Peak. Along the way, hikers can enjoy stunning views of the surrounding countryside and visit several historic temples and monasteries.

  • Mount Yuntai: Mount Yuntai is a scenic mountain located in Jiaozuo City, with many hiking trails and natural attractions, including waterfalls and hot springs. One of the most popular trails is the South Peak Loop, which offers panoramic views of the mountain.

  • Chaya Mountain: Chaya Mountain is located in the city of Luoyang and offers several hiking trails, including a challenging climb up to the summit. From the top, visitors can enjoy panoramic views of the city and surrounding countryside.

Best Things to Do in West Henan: Activities and Tours

There are many exciting activities and tours to enjoy in West Henan, catering to both cultural and outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the most popular:

  • Shaolin Kung Fu Show: The Shaolin Kung Fu Show is a must-see cultural performance and includes demonstrations of traditional Kung Fu techniques, including martial arts, weapon skills, and acrobatics.

  • Yellow River Scenic Area Boat Ride: Visitors can take an hour-long boat ride along the Yellow River Scenic Area, enjoying breathtaking views of the surrounding countryside and learning about the history and culture of the region.

  • Longtan Grand Canyon Rafting: Adventure-seekers can enjoy a thrilling rafting trip down the Longtan Grand Canyon, navigating through narrow gorges and over rapids.

Food and Drink Scene of West Henan

One of the best ways to experience local culture is through the local cuisine. Here are some of the must-try dishes and beverages in West Henan:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Luoyang Water Banquet: The Luoyang Water Banquet is a famous local dish that consists of 24 different small dishes served in a large shallow bowl. It is usually enjoyed at special occasions such as weddings and birthdays.

  • Xinzheng Marinated Meat: Xinzheng Marinated Meat is made by rubbing pork with a mix of spices and then hanging it to dry in the open air. The meat is then deep-fried and served as a snack with beer or wine.

  • Kaifeng Steamed Buns: Kaifeng Steamed Buns are soft, fluffy buns with a savory pork filling. They are a popular local snack found in most street markets.

Local Beverages

  • Songshan Wine: Songshan Wine is a famous local wine made from wheat and barley, with a sweet smell and fruity taste.

  • Xinyang Maojian Tea: Xinyang Maojian Tea is a type of green tea from the Xinyang area, with a bright green color and distinctive floral aroma.

Where to Stay in West Henan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options in West Henan, ranging from budget hostels to luxury hotels. Here are some of the best:

  • Kung Fu Hostel: The Kung Fu Hostel is located near the Shaolin Temple and offers basic rooms with comfortable beds and modern amenities.

  • Luoyang Grand Hotel: The Luoyang Grand Hotel is a luxury hotel located near the Longmen Grottoes, with modern rooms, excellent facilities, and stunning views.

  • Yun Tai Shan Hotel: The Yun Tai Shan Hotel is located near Mount Yuntai and offers comfortable rooms, traditional-style architecture, and excellent cuisine.

What to Buy in West Henan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

West Henan is famous for its unique local products and souvenirs, making it an excellent place to shop for gifts and mementos. Here are some of the best items to buy:

  • Shaolin Kung Fu Apparel: Shaolin Kung Fu is a symbol of the region, and visitors can purchase traditional clothing and accessories, such as robes, shoes, and hats.

  • Luoyang Peonies: Luoyang is famous for its beautiful peonies, and visitors can purchase fresh flowers or items made from peony petals, such as tea or jam.

  • Xinyang Maojian Tea: Xinyang Maojian Tea is a popular local product and makes an excellent gift for tea lovers.

Local Culture and Customs of West Henan

West Henan has a rich cultural heritage, with many ancient customs and traditions still preserved. Visitors are encouraged to learn about and respect these traditions. Here are some of the customs and traditions:

  • Kung Fu Culture: Kung Fu is an essential part of the local culture, and visitors should respect the Shaolin Temple and its monks.

  • Religious Beliefs: West Henan has a strong religious tradition, with Taoism, Buddhism, and Confucianism being the primary belief systems.

  • Celebrations and Festivals: West Henan celebrates many traditional festivals throughout the year, such as the Chinese New Year, the Dragon Boat Festival, and the Mid-Autumn Festival.

Best Time to Visit West Henan

The best time to visit West Henan is from April to October when the weather is mild, and the natural scenery is at its best. Spring is a good time to enjoy the blooming peonies in Luoyang, while autumn is perfect for hiking and enjoying the fall foliage.

Do’s and Don’ts of West Henan

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting West Henan:

Do:

  • Respect Local Customs: Visitors should learn about and respect local customs and traditions.

  • Be Polite: Visitors should be polite and courteous to locals and ask for permission before taking photos.

  • Try the Local Cuisine: Visitors should try local dishes and beverages to experience the local culture.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect Religious Sites: Visitors should not disrespect religious sites and refrain from loud or disruptive behavior.

  • Litter: Visitors should not litter and help to keep the region clean and beautiful.

  • Do Not Touch the Monks: Visitors should not touch or disturb the monks at Shaolin Temple and should respect their privacy.

Safety and Health

West Henan is generally a safe region to visit, but visitors should take usual precautions when traveling. Here are some tips for safety and health:

Safety in West Henan

  • Carry Identification: Visitors should carry identification at all times.

  • Be Careful When Hiking: Visitors should be careful when hiking and bring enough food and water.

  • Take Precautions Against Pickpockets: Visitors should be vigilant against pickpockets, especially in crowded areas.

Health in West Henan

  • Get Vaccinated: Visitors should get vaccinated before traveling to China.

  • Drink Bottled Water: Visitors should drink bottled water or boiled water and avoid tap water.

  • Protect Against Mosquitoes: Visitors should protect against mosquitoes by wearing long-sleeved clothing and using insect repellent.

Getting to West Henan: Transportation Options and Logistics

West Henan is easily accessible by air, train, or bus. Visitors can fly into Zhengzhou Xinzheng International Airport or take a train or bus to one of the main cities in the region.

Airports Near West Henan

  • Zhengzhou Xinzheng International Airport: This airport is the largest airport in the region, with flights connecting to many domestic and international destinations.

  • Luoyang Beijiao Airport: This airport is located in the north of Luoyang City and operates flights to major Chinese cities.

Bus Stations Near West Henan

  • Zhengzhou Bus Station: This bus station serves as the main transportation hub in the region, connecting to many cities and towns within Henan province.

  • Luoyang Bus Station: This bus station is located in the city center and connects to many popular tourist destinations, such as Shaolin Temple and Longmen Grottoes.

Other Options

  • High-Speed Train: The high-speed train is a convenient and comfortable way to travel within China and connects to many major cities in the region, such as Zhengzhou and Luoyang.

  • Taxi: Taxis are widely available in the cities of West Henan and are an excellent option for short trips or airport transfers.

How to Get Around in West Henan

Getting around West Henan is straightforward, with many transportation options available. Visitors can take buses, taxis, or hire a car to travel between cities and tourist destinations.

Where to Go Next from West Henan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

West Henan is an excellent starting point for exploring other popular destinations in China, such as Xi’an, Beijing, and Shanghai. Visitors can take a high-speed train or a domestic flight to reach these cities and enjoy their unique culture and attractions.

Conclusion

West Henan is a hidden gem in China, with its stunning natural landscape, rich cultural heritage, and unique local customs. Visitors can explore ancient temples, hike through beautiful mountainscapes, and try delicious local cuisine. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can plan your trip to West Henan and make unforgettable memories.
