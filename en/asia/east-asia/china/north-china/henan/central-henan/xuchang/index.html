Introduction to Xuchang

Xuchang, a city located in the central region of Henan Province in North China, is known for its history and culture. The city has a vibrant food scene and offers plenty of outdoor adventures. Xuchang is the perfect destination for travelers seeking to explore China’s lesser-known but equally fascinating cities.

List of Places to Visit in Xuchang

Visitors to Xuchang can explore numerous interesting places that embody the cultural, natural and historical aspects of the city.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Xuchang has an abundance of museums and historical sites that allow visitors to learn about the city’s culture and heritage. Some of the must-visit museums and historic places include:

  • Guangwu Mountain Scenic Area: This park is a famous historical site, known for its Shang Dynasty archaeological ruins.
  • Xuchang Museum: This museum features a collection of irreplaceable relics and is known for its Xuchang Bronze Axe.
  • Zhongyuan Tower: This tower stands at the center of Xuchang and is surrounded by many historical sites such as the Wenfeng Pagoda, Wuhou Temple and the Xincheng Park.

Natural Attractions of Xuchang: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Xuchang has stunning natural scenery that offers visitors several outdoor recreation activities such as hiking and sightseeing. Here are some of the top natural attractions to explore:

  • Wolong Shan National Forest Park: This park is often referred to as the “lungs of Xuchang”. It spans over 17,000 hectares and houses more than 2000 species of plants and animals.
  • Baolong Resort: This resort offers visitors the opportunity to explore the region’s hot springs and scenic views.
  • Xincheng Park: This park covers an area of 160,000 square meters and is the ideal spot for a morning jog, picnic or stroll.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Xuchang

Xuchang offers endless opportunities for hiking and biking. Visitors can enjoy the scenic views while exploring Xuchang’s beautiful countryside. Here are some of the must-experience hiking and biking trails in Xuchang:

  • Guangwu Mountain Scenic Area: This park offers a perfect hiking experience to visitors who want to relive the history of the Shang Dynasty, and enjoy stunning natural scenery.
  • Xuchang Grand Canal Cultural Park: This park is the ideal spot for hiking enthusiasts with its scenic views and the Grand Canal as its centerpiece.
  • Xiaoqiao Village: This village is known for its pleasant climate and offers great biking routes for visitors.

Best Things to Do in Xuchang: Activities and Tours

Xuchang offers plenty of activities that cater to people of all ages. Below are some of the best things to do in Xuchang:

  • Dinner Cruise on the Grand Canal: Enjoy a delightful dinner cruise while admiring the beautiful scenery along the Grand Canal.
  • Visit a Local Market: Explore the bustling markets of Xuchang, where visitors can shop for unique local items such as the famous Xuchang Bronze Axe.
  • Nightlife in Xuchang: Xuchang has a vibrant nightlife scene. The city offers visitors many entertainment options such as bars, nightclubs and karaoke.

Week-Long Itinerary for Xuchang

Those planning to visit Xuchang for a week can follow the itinerary below that highlights the must-visit places, activities and food:

Food and Drink Scene of Xuchang

Xuchang is known for its local cuisine that boasts unique flavors and cooking techniques. Visitors should make a point to try some of the must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Xuchang Roasted Duck: A popular roasted duck dish that is crispy on the outside and juicy on the inside.
  • Xuchang Guantang Steamed Buns: These steamed buns are filled with pork, cabbage and other seasonings.
  • Xuchang Wanzi: These are pork meatballs that are boiled or fried with soy sauce and Sesame oil.

Local Beverages

  • Peach Blossom Wine: This wine is made from peach blossoms and has a pleasant taste and aroma.
  • Xuchang Baijiu: This is a strong liquor made from sorghum and is a popular drink in the city.

Where to Stay in Xuchang: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Xuchang has various hotel options that cater to all budgets. Some of the top hotels include:

  • New Century Grand Hotel Xuchang: Located at the city center and is known for its excellent service and luxurious amenities.
  • GreenTree Eastern Xuchang Minzhu Road Hotel: A budget-friendly option with a clean and comfortable stay.

For those looking for a more local experience, guesthouses are available at reasonable prices.

What to Buy in Xuchang: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors can buy several unique and exciting souvenirs and local products to take home. Some of the top items include:

  • Xuchang Bronze Axe: This is a unique and rare item that symbolizes the city’s history and heritage.
  • Yuzhou Embroidery: This is a traditional embroidery craft that is high-quality and made entirely by hand.

Local Culture and Customs of Xuchang

Xuchang has a long and rich history, and its culture has played a significant role in shaping the city. Visitors should take note of the following customs and culture when in Xuchang:

  • Respect for the Elderly: Respect for elders is a highly valued custom in Xuchang, and visitors should follow this custom.
  • Good Manners: It is considered polite to use both hands to give and receive gifts in Xuchang.

Best Time to Visit Xuchang

The best time to visit Xuchang is between March and November when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities. During this period, the city is full of life, and visitors can attend various festivals.

Do’s and Don’ts of Xuchang

Do:

  • Learn Some Mandarin: Learning a few Mandarin words can help visitors communicate with locals and enjoy their stay.
  • Respect Local Customs: Visitors should respect local customs and dress appropriately and avoid disrespectful behavior in public places.

Don’t:

  • Don’t Eat or Drink in Public Places: It is considered rude to eat or drink in public places in Xuchang.
  • Don’t Show Disrespect to Elders: Disrespect to elders is unacceptable and frowned upon in Xuchang.

Safety and Health

Safety in Xuchang

Xuchang is a safe city, and visitors should not have any significant safety concerns. However, visitors should avoid flashing any valuable items, especially in crowded areas.

Health in Xuchang

Visitors to Xuchang should carry a basic medical kit and remain vigilant in public areas to avoid any health risks. It’s also advisable to ensure personal hygiene and drink bottled water from reputable sources.

Getting to Xuchang: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Xuchang

The nearest airport to Xuchang is Zhengzhou Xinzheng International Airport, which is approximately 100km away. From the airport, visitors can take a taxi, bus or train to Xuchang.

Bus Stations Near Xuchang

Xuchang has two bus stations, Xuchang West Bus Station and Xuchang East Bus Station, that offer coach services to various popular cities within Henan Province.

Other Options

Visitors can also take the high-speed train from Beijing and Shanghai to Zhengzhou, then transfer to a train to Xuchang.

How to Get Around in Xuchang

Visitors can get around Xuchang by taking a taxi, public transportation or renting a bicycle. The city has excellent infrastructure and is well-connected.

Where to Go Next from Xuchang: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Xuchang’s central location makes it an ideal gateway to explore other cities within Henan Province. Visitors can consider the following destinations:

  • Luoyang: A popular destination known for its historical sites such as the Longmen Grottoes and White Horse Temple.
  • Zhengzhou: The capital city of the Henan Province, that has plenty of cultural and historical attractions.

Conclusion

Xuchang offers visitors a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and vibrant food scene. From exploring historical sites, hiking and biking to enjoying mouth-watering dishes, visitors are bound to have an unforgettable experience in Xuchang.
