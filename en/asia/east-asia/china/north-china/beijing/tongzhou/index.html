Introduction to Tongzhou

Located in the eastern part of Beijing, Tongzhou is a hidden gem that only a few travelers know about. Tongzhou is a district in Beijing that has a history of more than 1,000 years. It was once an important commercial center and served as the gateway to the Grand Canal, which is the longest and oldest canal in the world.

Today, Tongzhou is a popular destination for tourists who want to escape the hustle and bustle of downtown Beijing. This district has a lot to offer, including museums, historical landmarks, natural beauty, outdoor adventures, and delicious food. In this guide, we will take you on a journey to uncover the hidden gems of Tongzhou.

List of Places to Visit in Tongzhou

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Tongzhou has a rich history that is reflected in the museums and historical places in the district. Here are some of the must-visit places:

  • Tongzhou Museum: This museum is dedicated to the history of Tongzhou and the Grand Canal. The exhibits include artifacts, historical documents, models, and multimedia displays that showcase the development of Tongzhou and the canal.
  • Grand Canal Forest Park: Located in the southeast of Tongzhou, this park is the perfect place to learn about the history of the Grand Canal. It has several attractions, including the Grand Canal Museum, Yicheng Garden, and Haihe Wetland.
  • Jinhai Lake: This is a scenic freshwater lake that covers an area of 58 square kilometers. It is the largest lake in Beijing and is surrounded by mountains. The lake is a popular spot for swimming, fishing, boating, and other water activities.

Natural Attractions of Tongzhou: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Tongzhou has a diverse landscape that includes mountains, rivers, and forests. If you love nature and outdoor adventures, here are some places you can’t miss:

  • Yaji Mountain: Located in the northeast of Tongzhou, this mountain is known for its lush forests and scenic views. It has several hiking trails that lead to the top of the mountain, where you can see the beautiful landscape of Tongzhou.
  • Liangshui Natural Scenic Area: This area is located in the northwest of Tongzhou and is known for its natural beauty. It has several attractions, including Liangshui Zhongshan Park, Liangshui River, and Liangshui Forest Park.
  • Tongzhou Canal Park: This park is located in the center of Tongzhou and is a popular spot for jogging, cycling, and walking. It is also an ideal place for picnics and outdoor activities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tongzhou

Tongzhou has several hiking and biking routes that are suitable for both beginners and experts. Here are some of the most popular ones:

  • Yaji Mountain Hiking Trail: This trail is about 8 kilometers long and takes about 3-4 hours to complete. It is a moderately difficult trail that takes you through lush forests and scenic views.
  • Liangshui River Biking Trail: This trail is about 12 kilometers long and takes about 2-3 hours to complete. It is an easy trail that takes you along the Liangshui River and through the Liangshui Forest Park.
  • Tongzhou Canal Park Walking Trail: This trail is about 6 kilometers long and takes about 1-2 hours to complete. It is a leisurely trail that takes you along the Tongzhou Canal and through the Tongzhou Canal Park.

Best Things to Do in Tongzhou: Activities and Tours

Tongzhou has a lot to offer in terms of activities and tours. Here are some of the best things you can do in Tongzhou:

  • Visit the Tongzhou Museum: This museum is a great place to learn about the history of Tongzhou and the Grand Canal.
  • Explore the Jinhai Lake Scenic Area: This area is perfect for outdoor activities, including swimming, fishing, and boating.
  • Take a Cycling Tour: Tongzhou has several scenic cycling routes that take you through the countryside and along the canals.
  • Attend the Tongzhou International Dragon Boat Festival: This festival is held every year in June and is a great way to experience the culture and traditions of Tongzhou.

Food and Drink Scene of Tongzhou

Tongzhou is known for its delicious food that is a mix of Beijing cuisine and countryside flavors. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Tongzhou Roast Duck: This dish is similar to Beijing Roast Duck but has a unique flavor that is a result of using local spices and ingredients.
  • Jinhai Lake Fish: This freshwater fish is popular in Tongzhou and is often served steamed or grilled.
  • Tongzhou Fried Cake: This is a local specialty that is made with flour, sugar, and sesame seeds. It has a crunchy texture and a sweet taste.

Local Beverages

  • Tongzhou Wine: This is a distilled liquor that is made from sorghum and has a strong aroma and flavor.
  • Jinhai Lake Beer: This beer is made with fresh water from Jinhai Lake and has a crisp taste.

Local Culture and Customs of Tongzhou

Tongzhou is a district that has a rich cultural heritage and customs. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Respect the Local Customs: Tongzhou has a mix of urban and rural culture, so it is important to be respectful of local customs and traditions.
  • Experience the Local Festivals: Tongzhou has several festivals throughout the year that showcase the local culture and traditions.
  • Try Local Food: Tongzhou has a unique cuisine that is a mix of urban and rural flavors, so be sure to try the local food and beverages.

Best Time to Visit Tongzhou

The best time to visit Tongzhou is from March to May and September to November. The weather is mild and pleasant during these months, and there are fewer tourists.

Getting to Tongzhou: Transportation Options and Logistics

Tongzhou is located about 20 kilometers east of downtown Beijing and is accessible by several transportation options.

Airports Near Tongzhou

  • Beijing Capital International Airport: This is the nearest airport to Tongzhou, and it takes about 30-45 minutes to reach Tongzhou from the airport.

Bus Stations Near Tongzhou

  • Beijing Dongzhimen Transport Hub: This is the main bus station in Beijing, and it offers several bus services to Tongzhou.

Other Options

  • Taxi: Taxis are widely available in Beijing and can take you to Tongzhou.
  • Metro: Tongzhou is connected to Beijing’s metro system, and you can take Line 6 to reach Tongzhou.

How to Get Around in Tongzhou

Tongzhou is a relatively small district, and you can easily get around by:

  • Metro: Line Batong metro line (Line 6) covers a lot of important places in Tongzhou, and it costs about 2-3 Chinese yuan per ride
  • Taxi: Taxis are widely available, but always make sure to agree on a price before starting the ride.
  • Public bus: Public buses are available, but the routes can be confusing for those who can’t speak or read Chinese.
