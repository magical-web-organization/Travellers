If you’re looking for an off-the-beaten-path destination, Tongliao in Inner Mongolia might just be what you’re looking for. Located in central Inner Mongolia, Tongliao is a relatively small city filled with cultural and natural attractions that are worth exploring. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take a closer look at what the city has to offer and give you tips on how to make the most of your visit.

Introduction to Tongliao

Tongliao is a small but bustling city located in the eastern part of Inner Mongolia. The city is known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural scenery and outdoor adventures, which makes it an excellent destination for travelers seeking something unique and authentic. With a population of around 3 million people, Tongliao is a mix of modernity and tradition, where locals coexist with the past and present.

List of Places to Visit in Tongliao

Whether you’re a history buff, an outdoor enthusiast or an adventurous traveler, Tongliao has something for everyone. Here are some of the city’s must-visit attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tongliao Museum – Built in 2001, this museum houses ancient relics, cultural artifacts, and traditional clothing. It is a great place to learn about the history and culture of Tongliao.

  • Kerqin Grassland – Located about 20 miles outside of Tongliao, the Kerqin grassland is one of the largest grasslands in China. Visitors can explore this vast, rolling landscape on horseback or by hiking the trails.

Natural Attractions of Tongliao: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Xilamuren Grassland – This grassland is located in Xilamuren Banner, Tongliao. The vast, rolling plains, and clear blue skies provide stunning views and an opportunity to experience Mongolian culture up close.

  • Gegentala Grassland – Chimmed Horqin Banner’s Gegentala Grassland is in eastern Inner Mongolia’s Hinggan League. The area features unique cultural customs, picturesque scenery, and a lively, welcoming atmosphere.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tongliao

Tongliao is surrounded by stunning natural beauty and offers many opportunities for outdoor adventures. Here are some popular hikes and biking routes that you might want to check out:

  • Liangshuihe National Forest Park – This park offers hiking, camping, and outdoor activities in a beautiful forested setting.

  • Red Mountain – Located outside of Tongliao city proper, the trail offers a scenic hike to the top of Red Mountain, where you will enjoy a stunning view of the surrounding area.

Best Things to Do in Tongliao: Activities and Tours

  • Horse-riding Cultural Tour – This tour allows visitors to immerse themselves in Mongolian culture while exploring the vast grasslands of Tongliao on horseback.

  • Camel Trekking in the Gegentala Grassland – With the help of a guide, trek the vast grasslands of Gegentala in Mongolian style, atop a camel. Enjoy stunning views and an unforgettable experience.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tongliao

Here is a week-long itinerary that will allow you to explore the best of what Tongliao has to offer:

  • Day 1: Tongliao Museum and Daqing Mosque
  • Day 2: Xilamuren Grassland and Mongolian Camp Dinner
  • Day 3: Kerqin Grassland and Evening Dance Performance
  • Day 4: Hiking at Liangshuihe National Forest Park
  • Day 5: Red Mountain and Tongliao Winery Tour
  • Day 6: Gegentala Grassland Camel Trekking and Traditional Mongolian Lunch
  • Day 7: Explore Tongliao city

Food and Drink Scene of Tongliao

Tongliao is famous for its unique cuisines and alcoholic beverages. Here are some of the dishes and drinks you should try when visiting Tongliao:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Xiangzhi Pao Mo (Bread Stew) – A delicious bread soup served with mutton, this traditional dish is a Tongliao favorite.

  • Bakum (Steamed Stuffed Buns) – Filled with pork, beef or herbal stuffing, Bakum is a popular breakfast in Tongliao.

Local Beverages

  • Archa – A type of wine made from fermented milk, Archa has a sweet and sour taste and low alcohol content.

  • Sweet Fermented Rice Wine – This sweet alcoholic beverage is made from fermented rice and is popular across Inner Mongolia.

Where to Stay in Tongliao: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Tongliao offers a range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. Here are some of our top picks:

  • Genghis Khan International Hotel – This hotel combines traditional Mongolian architecture and modern comforts. It offers rooms and suites that are perfect for families and business travelers.

  • Bravo Hotel Tongliao – Located in the city center, this hotel offers modern amenities, spacious rooms, and friendly service.

What to Buy in Tongliao: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When visiting Tongliao, make sure to bring back some of the city’s unique souvenirs and products. Here are some ideas:

  • Pickled Radish – A delicious and healthy snack made by marinating radish in vinegar and spices.

  • Mongolian Costumes – These traditional costumes feature intricate designs and embroidery.

Local Culture and Customs of Tongliao

Tongliao is rich in cultural traditions, including Mongolian customs and cuisines. Here are some things to keep in mind when visiting:

  • Respect Local Customs – Dress appropriately and show respect when visiting local temples, mosques, and sacred sites.

  • Dining Etiquette – Mongolian culture places high value on hospitality and respect. When dining in Tongliao, always accept food and drinks offered by your host, and never leave the table before they do.

Best Time to Visit Tongliao

The best time to visit Tongliao is between May and October. During this time, the weather is pleasant and perfect for outdoor activities and exploration.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tongliao

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting Tongliao:

Do:

  • Dress appropriately when visiting religious sites and sacred places.
  • Take your shoes off when entering someone’s home.
  • Try the local cuisine and drinks.

Don’t:

  • Take pictures of people without their permission.
  • Make negative comments about Mongolian culture or customs.
  • Take rocks, plants or other natural parts of the area as souvenirs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tongliao

Tongliao is a safe city, but it is always important to take precautions when traveling. Here are some safety tips to keep in mind:

  • Always carry a copy of your passport with you, and keep the original in a safe place.
  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially at night.
  • Use a registered taxi or car-hailing service when traveling.

Health in Tongliao

Inner Mongolia is a region with a high incidence of air pollution. Always be sure to check the daily air quality and protect yourself accordingly (e.g., wear masks, avoid outdoor exercise during certain hours).

Getting to Tongliao: Transportation Options and Logistics

If you’re planning a trip to Tongliao, here are some transportation options to consider:

Airports Near Tongliao

  • Chifeng Yulong Airport (CIF)
  • Baotou Airport (BAV)

Bus Stations Near Tongliao

  • Tongliao Bus Station
  • Kerqin Bus Station

Other Options

If you prefer to travel by train, there are two railway stations in Tongliao: Tongliao Railway Station and Kerqin Railway Station. There are also many car rental services available in Tongliao if you prefer to drive yourself.

How to Get Around in Tongliao

Tongliao has a good public transportation system, including bus and taxi services. You can also rent a bike or hire a car to explore the city and its surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Tongliao: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have some extra time after exploring Tongliao, here are some nearby destinations that you might want to check out:

  • Chifeng – Known for its stunning natural scenery, Chifeng is a great destination for exploring the beauty of Inner Mongolia.

  • Hailar – Located in the northern part of Inner Mongolia, Hailar is a charming city filled with unique architecture and cultural attractions.

Conclusion

Tongliao is a hidden gem in Inner Mongolia, offering a unique blend of culture, history, natural beauty, and outdoor adventure. From the lush grasslands to the winding hiking trails and vibrant cultural traditions, there’s something for every traveler to enjoy. With this comprehensive guide, you can plan an unforgettable trip to Tongliao and experience the best that the city has to offer.
