Introduction to Xilinhot

Located in the heart of Inner Mongolia, Xilinhot is a beautiful city known for its natural beauty and cultural landmarks. Spread over an area of 42,757 square kilometers, Xilinhot is home to a vibrant local culture, delicious cuisine, and some of the most breathtaking natural landscapes in China. From the rugged mountains to the stunning grasslands, Xilinhot offers something for every kind of traveler. If you’re looking for an offbeat travel destination in China, Xilinhot is the perfect place to visit.

List of Places to Visit in Xilinhot

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Xilinhot Museum: This museum is dedicated to preserving the history and culture of the Mongolian ethnic group in Xilinhot. The museum houses a vast collection of artifacts, including traditional costumes, weapons, and household items.

  2. Mausoleum of Genghis Khan: Located on the outskirts of Xilinhot, the Mausoleum of Genghis Khan is a must-visit place for anyone interested in the history of the Mongolian Empire. The mausoleum is built on the spot where Genghis Khan is believed to have been buried.

Natural Attractions of Xilinhot: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Xilin River National Forest Park: This beautiful park is situated along the banks of the Xilin River and is home to a diverse range of wildlife, including deer, foxes, and birds.

  2. Xilin Hot Spring Resort: This resort is the perfect place to relax and unwind after a day of exploring Xilinhot. The hot springs at the resort are said to have healing properties and are a popular attraction among visitors.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Xilinhot

  1. Xilin River Trail: This scenic trail runs along the banks of the Xilin River and offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and grasslands.

  2. Heishui Trail: Located in the Heishui Valley, this trail takes you through some of the most rugged and beautiful landscapes in Xilinhot.

Best Things to Do in Xilinhot: Activities and Tours

  1. Horseback Riding: Xilinhot is known for its horse culture, and horseback riding is a popular activity among visitors. Take a guided tour through the grasslands and experience the unique way of life of the local Mongolian people.

  2. Jeep Safari: For adventurous travelers, a jeep safari through the rugged mountains and grasslands is a must-do activity.

Week-Long Itinerary for Xilinhot

Day 1: Visit the Xilinhot Museum and learn about the history and culture of the Mongolian people in Xilinhot. Day 2: Explore the Mausoleum of Genghis Khan and learn about the history of the Mongolian Empire. Day 3: Take a horseback riding tour through the grasslands and experience local Mongolian culture. Day 4: Relax and unwind at the Xilin Hot Spring Resort. Day 5: Go on a jeep safari through the rugged mountains and stunning landscapes. Day 6: Hike along the Xilin River Trail and enjoy the scenic views. Day 7: Visit the Heishui Valley and take a guided tour through the beautiful natural landscapes.

Food and Drink Scene of Xilinhot

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Roast Lamb: Xilinhot is famous for its roast lamb, which is marinated in local herbs and spices and cooked over an open flame.

  2. Mongolian Hot Pot: This dish is a popular local delicacy and consists of a simmering pot of broth served with meat, vegetables, and noodles.

Local Beverages

  1. Milk Tea: Milk tea is a staple beverage in Inner Mongolia and is a must-try for any traveler visiting Xilinhot.

  2. Mongolian Beer: The local beer in Xilinhot is a great way to cool off after a day of exploring the city.

Where to Stay in Xilinhot: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Howard Johnson Jinlian Business Club Hotel Xilinhot: This luxury hotel is located in the heart of the city and offers comfortable rooms and great amenities.

  2. Xilinhot Youth Hostel: If you’re looking for budget accommodation, this hostel is the perfect choice. It has clean rooms, friendly staff, and a great location.

What to Buy in Xilinhot: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Mongolian Horsehair Felt Products: These unique products are made of Mongolian horsehair and are a popular souvenir among visitors to Xilinhot.

  2. Handmade Yurt Decorations: Yurts are traditional Mongolian dwellings, and handmade yurt decorations make for beautiful souvenirs to take home with you.

Local Culture and Customs of Xilinhot

The local culture in Xilinhot is heavily influenced by the Mongolian ethnic group. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions and learn about the unique way of life of the local people.

Best Time to Visit Xilinhot

The best time to visit Xilinhot is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is pleasant and there are plenty of outdoor activities to enjoy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Xilinhot

Do:

  1. Learn about local customs and traditions.
  2. Be respectful of the local people.
  3. Try local cuisine.

Don’t:

  1. Wear revealing clothing in public.
  2. Take photos of people without their permission.
  3. Disrespect local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Xilinhot

Xilinhot is a relatively safe city, but travelers should take standard safety precautions, such as safeguarding their valuables and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Xilinhot

Travelers should drink bottled water to avoid any water-borne illnesses. It’s also recommended to bring insect repellent to avoid mosquito bites.
