Introduction to Rizhao

Nestled in the south of Shandong province, Rizhao city is a popular tourist destination that boasts a laid-back atmosphere, beautiful beaches, and scenic views. The city is located on the coast of the Yellow Sea, and is famous for its seafood, natural attractions, and ancient culture.

With its scenic mountains and water, long beaches, delicious cuisine, and unique local culture, Rizhao is a must-visit destination in China.

List of Places to Visit in Rizhao

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Rizhao Museum

One of the most popular museums in Rizhao, the Rizhao Museum, is home to a vast collection of artifacts that showcase the region’s culture and history. From ancient pottery and bronze statues to traditional clothes and daily utensils, the museum offers a glimpse into the city’s rich past.

Rizhao Seaside Ancient City

Located just a few miles from the city center, the Rizhao Seaside Ancient City is an archeological site that dates back to over 1700 years ago. It is one of the city’s most important historical sites and offers visitors a chance to explore the ruins of an ancient city.

Natural Attractions of Rizhao: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lighthouse Scenic Area

The Lighthouse Scenic Area is one of Rizhao’s most iconic landmarks. Situated on the southeastern coast, this area offers some of the most breathtaking views of the city’s coastline. Visitors can climb up to the top of the lighthouse and take in panoramic views of the sea and the city skyline.

Wanpingkou Beach

Wanpingkou Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Rizhao. With its long stretch of golden sand and pristine waters, it is perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and beach activities such as volleyball and frisbee. There are also a variety of food options nearby, from seafood to street snacks.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Rizhao

Golden Coast Hiking Trail

The Golden Coast Hiking Trail is an 8 km hiking trail that starts at the entrance of Rizhao Coastal Forest Park and runs along the coast towards the south. The trail takes hikers through stunning natural scenery such as rocky cliffs, forests, and beaches.

Rizhao City Biking Tour

Rizhao City Biking Tour is a 12km bicycling tour that starts from Rizhao Municipal Government Square and takes cyclists through the city’s most iconic landmarks, such as the Rizhao Olympic Stadium, Haiqu Park, and Rizhao Seaside Ancient City.

Best Things to Do in Rizhao: Activities and Tours

Fishing at East Lake

East Lake is a popular spot for fishing enthusiasts. Visitors can rent fishing gear and catch a range of fish species such as bass, catfish, and tilapia.

Rizhao Heilongjiang Road Night Market

For a lively evening experience, head over to the Rizhao Heilongjiang Road Night Market. Here, visitors can indulge in a variety of street food, snacks, and drinks while enjoying live performances and entertainment.

Food and Drink Scene of Rizhao

Must-Try Dishes

Rizhao Style Fried Oysters

One of Rizhao’s most famous dishes, Rizhao Style Fried Oysters is a local delicacy that is usually served with soy sauce, scallions, and ginger.

Seafood Congee

Seafood Congee is a traditional breakfast dish that is made with rice, fish, shrimp, and other seafood.

Local Beverages

Rizhao Yellow Wine

Rizhao Yellow Wine is a type of Chinese traditional alcoholic beverage that is made from fermented glutinous rice. It has a distinct aroma and is usually enjoyed with a variety of local dishes.

Rizhao Beer

Rizhao Beer is a crisp and refreshing beer that is brewed locally. It is a popular beverage and can be found in many of the city’s bars and restaurants.

Where to Stay in Rizhao: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Rizhao Wanpingkou Seaview Hotel

Rizhao Wanpingkou Seaview Hotel is a luxury hotel that is located just a few steps from Wanpingkou Beach. It offers stunning sea views, spacious and comfortable rooms, and a range of facilities such as swimming pool, fitness center, and restaurant.

Seaview Resort Hotel

Seaview Resort Hotel is a great choice for travelers who want to be close to the city center. It offers comfortable accommodation, modern amenities, and easy access to attractions such as the Rizhao Olympic Stadium and the Rizhao Museum.

What to Buy in Rizhao: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Rizhao Seashell Carving

Rizhao Seashell Carving is a unique form of art that uses seashells to create beautiful handicrafts such as jewelry, picture frames, and home decor items.

Rizhao Golden Corn

Rizhao Golden Corn is a popular local snack that is made from corn, sugar, and oil. It is crispy, sweet, and perfect for on-the-go snacking.

Local Culture and Customs of Rizhao

Rizhao is a city that is deeply rooted in ancient culture and traditions. Visitors should respect local customs and traditions, dress modestly, and follow local etiquette while visiting. Some of the most important customs and taboos include respecting elders, not touching someone’s head, and never pointing with your finger.

Best Time to Visit Rizhao

The best time to visit Rizhao is during the spring and autumn months (March to May and September to November), when the weather is mild and pleasant. However, visitors can also enjoy the city during the summer months (June to August) when Wanpingkou Beach and other water activities are at their peak.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rizhao

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Dress modestly
  • Follow local etiquette
  • Try local food and drinks

Don’t:

  • Touch someone’s head
  • Point with your finger
  • Engage in public displays of affection

Safety and Health

Safety in Rizhao

Rizhao is a relatively safe city, but visitors should take precautions when traveling to crowded areas, such as the night market, to avoid theft or pickpocketing.

Health in Rizhao

Visitors should be careful when eating street food or drinking tap water. It is advisable to drink bottled water, and to avoid raw or undercooked food to prevent stomach issues.

Getting to Rizhao: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Rizhao

The nearest airport to Rizhao City is Qingdao Liuting International Airport (TAO), which is located approximately 180 km away. From there, visitors can take a bus or taxi to reach Rizhao City.

Bus Stations Near Rizhao

Rizhao Bus Station is the main bus station in the city and offers direct connections to cities such as Qingdao and Jinan.

Other Options

Visitors can also take a train to Rizhao from major cities in China, such as Beijing and Shanghai.

How to Get Around in Rizhao

The best way to get around in Rizhao is by taking a taxi or using public transportation such as buses or the metro.

Where to Go Next from Rizhao: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Qingdao

Qingdao is a vibrant coastal city that is located approximately 180 km away from Rizhao. It is famous for its beautiful beaches, rich history, and lively nightlife.

Yantai

Yantai is another coastal city that is located 200 km away from Rizhao. It offers stunning seascapes, interesting cultural attractions, and delicious cuisine.

Conclusion

Rizhao is a city that has something for everyone. From its beautiful beaches, historic landmarks, and natural attractions to its delicious cuisine, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality, Rizhao is a must-visit destination in China. Plan your trip to Rizhao today, and discover all the wonders this amazing city has to offer.
