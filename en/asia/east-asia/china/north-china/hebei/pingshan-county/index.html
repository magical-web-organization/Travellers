Introduction to Pingshan County

Located in the north of Hebei Province, Pingshan County is a small city that boasts a rich history and natural beauty. It is known for its beautiful mountains, rivers, and ancient towns that are teeming with traditional Chinese culture. Pingshan County offers a unique travel experience that is perfect for those seeking adventure, history, and cultural immersion.

List of Places to Visit in Pingshan County

As one of the most beautiful and well-preserved ancient towns in northern China, Pingshan County offers a plethora of cultural, historical, and natural attractions. Here is a list of the top places to visit in Pingshan County:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Pingshan Grand Theater
  2. Guanting Pavilion
  3. Pingshan Ancient Town
  4. Pingshan Chateau

Natural Attractions of Pingshan County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Mount Wutai
  2. Xibaipo Scenic Area
  3. Lingshan National Forest Park
  4. Yuanyang Lake

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pingshan County

Pingshan County is full of excellent hiking and biking trails, allowing travelers to explore the natural beauty and history of the region. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Pingshan County:

  1. Yayunting Scenic Spot: a 12-kilometer cycling route that takes you through the incredible scenery of Lingshan National Park.
  2. Mount Wutai Trekking: a 38-kilometer trail that takes you through the stunning landscape of Mount Wutai, one of China’s most revered Buddhist sites.
  3. Pingshan Ancient Town Walking Tour: a scenic walk that takes you through the winding streets and narrow lanes of one of China’s best-preserved ancient towns.

Best Things to Do in Pingshan County: Activities and Tours

Pingshan County offers a wide range of activities and tours that cater to travelers of all ages and interests. Here are some of the best things to do in Pingshan County:

  1. Guanting Reservoir Boat Tour: Embark on a 2-hour boat tour of Guanting Reservoir, one of the largest reservoirs in northern China.
  2. Yuanyang Lake Wetland Park: Explore the wetlands and wildlife of Yuanyang Lake, one of the most biodiverse areas in Hebei Province.
  3. Pingshan Chateau Vineyard Tour: Take a guided tour of the Pingshan Chateau vineyards and cellar, and learn about the wine-making process in northern China.

Food and Drink Scene of Pingshan County

Foodies will love Pingshan County’s cuisine, which is known for its rich flavors and locally sourced ingredients. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Pingshan Lazi - Pingshan County’s signature dish made from local chili peppers and meat.
  2. Baishan Roasted Duck - A popular dish made from tender, roasted duck and spicy-seasoned rice.
  3. Zhongling Barbecue - A traditional Chinese BBQ dish made from lamb, beef, and pork skewers.

Local Beverages

  1. Pingshan Baijiu - A type of Chinese liquor made in Pingshan County that is recognized for its high quality and unique flavor.
  2. Pingshan Chateau Wine - A locally produced wine that is made from grapes grown in Pingshan County’s vineyards.
  3. Grape Juice - A refreshing and healthy beverage that makes use of the region’s many grape orchards.

Local Culture and Customs of Pingshan County

Pingshan County is steeped in rich Chinese culture and traditions, and it is essential to understand and respect local customs and etiquette. Examples of some of these customs include:

  • Greeting someone with a smile and appropriate level of formality and respect, based on rank and age.
  • Always being punctual and avoid being late as time consciousness holds great significance.
  • Dress modestly and appropriately, especially when visiting cultural sites.
  • If offering a gift, ensure it is appropriate and of high quality.

Best Time to Visit Pingshan County

The best time to visit Pingshan County is from May to October when the weather is warm and mild, and the scenery is at its most beautiful. It is best to avoid the winter months when temperatures can drop below freezing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pingshan County

Do:

  • Do try the local cuisine and wines in the area
  • Do respect the local culture and traditions
  • Do bring a reusable water bottle and a hat during outdoor activities

Don’t:

  • Don’t take photographs without respect or when visitors are asked not to do so
  • Don’t visit cultural sites in clothes that are not appropriate or revealing
  • Don’t be excessively loud or disruptive in public.

Safety and Health

Safety in Pingshan County

Pingshan County is a generally safe place to visit, but it is always important to take precautions while traveling to avoid any mishaps or dangers. Some tips for staying safe in Pingshan County include:

  • Avoid venturing too far off beaten paths or hiking trails alone
  • Be alert for any scams or theft incidents and always keep a copy of important paperwork
  • Always carry enough cash, and keep cash and valuables secure and hidden

Health in Pingshan County

Pingshan County is generally relatively healthy for travelers. All visitors should ensure that they have taken the necessary vaccinations before arriving in China; however, always keep medication and first-aid in case of emergencies.
