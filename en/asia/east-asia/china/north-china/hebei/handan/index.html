Introduction to Handan

Handan is a city located in Hebei Province in North China. It has a rich history that dates back to the Zhou Dynasty (1046-256 BC) and served as the capital of the Zhao State during the Warring States Period (475-221 BC). Today, Handan is a modern metropolis that offers a glimpse into China’s past and present. With its cultural landmarks, natural wonders, and local cuisine, Handan is a perfect destination for travelers looking to experience the best of China.

List of Places to Visit in Handan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Handan boasts several museums and historical sites that showcase the city’s cultural and architectural heritage. The following are some must-visit destinations in Handan:

  1. Zhaozhou Bridge - A stone arch bridge that dates back to 595 AD and is considered to be the oldest and largest stone arch bridge in the world.

  2. Dragon Pavilion Park - A beautiful park housing the famous Dragon Pavilion, a historic pavilion that was built in the Ming Dynasty (1368-1644).

  3. Guantao Relics - A historical site that has preserved the remains of the ancient city walls, tombs, and pottery of the Han Dynasty (206 BC - 220 AD).

  4. Handan Museum - A comprehensive museum that features over 30,000 cultural relics spanning from prehistoric times to the Ming and Qing dynasties.

Natural Attractions of Handan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Handan’s natural scenery and outdoor adventures offer a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Here are some top natural attractions to visit:

  1. Jingniang Lake - A picturesque lake surrounded by a scenic park that provides opportunities for boating, fishing, and hiking.

  2. Guangfu Ancient Town - A historic town situated at the foot of the Taihang Mountains with stunning ancient architecture, winding streets, and stunning scenic views.

  3. Taihang Grand Canyon - A scenic area that offers various outdoor activities, such as hiking, camping, rafting, and ziplining.

  4. Bailixia Scenic Area - A beautiful landscape of mountains, lakes, and waterfalls that provide opportunities for boating and hiking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Handan

Handan offers some of the best hiking and biking routes in North China. Here are some top trails that will take you through the city’s scenic landscapes:

  1. Taihang Mountain - A scenic mountain range that offers incredible hiking opportunities with stunning views of the surrounding scenery.

  2. Yueliangshan National Forest Park - A beautiful park with hiking trails that lead through lush forests, past waterfalls and streams, and up to scenic viewpoints.

  3. Handan Botanical Garden - A large garden with several walking paths that take you through a stunning array of plant life, including flowers, trees, and lily ponds.

Best Things to Do in Handan: Activities and Tours

Handan offers a wide range of activities and tours that cater to every kind of traveler. Here are some top things to do in Handan:

  1. Experience local culture - Immerse yourself in Handan’s local culture by attending a traditional Chinese opera, visiting a tea house, or sampling local cuisine at a local restaurant.

  2. Take a city tour - Take a guided tour of Handan’s major cultural and historical attractions, such as the Zhaozhou Bridge, Dragon Pavilion, or Handan Museum.

  3. Explore natural wonders - Visit Jingniang Lake, Taihang Grand Canyon, or Bailixia Scenic Area to witness Handan’s stunning natural beauty.

  4. Attend a festival - Handan hosts several festivals throughout the year, including the Spring Festival, the Lantern Festival, and the Dragon Boat Festival.

Week-Long Itinerary for Handan

Here’s a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to Handan:

Day 1 - Visit Handan Museum and Dragon Pavilion Park Day 2 - Explore Zhaozhou Bridge and Guantao Relics Day 3 - Take a hike in Taihang Mountain or Yueliangshan National Forest Park Day 4 - Visit Jingniang Lake and Bailixia Scenic Area Day 5 - Attend a traditional Chinese opera or visit a tea house for a cultural experience Day 6 - Take a guided city tour of Handan to see other significant landmarks and attractions Day 7 - Explore Handan’s local markets to discover regional specialties and souvenirs.

Food and Drink Scene of Handan

Must-Try Dishes

Handan is known for its traditional and delicious cuisine. Here are some top dishes that should not be missed during your visit:

  1. Yongnian Roast Duck - A local delicacy made by roasting duck with honey and other seasonings.

  2. Wancui Guozi - A specialty street food made from boiled mung beans and a unique blend of seasonings.

  3. Hebei Baked Pancake - A popular snack made from a mixture of wheat flour, hot water, oil, and scallions.

  4. Handan Juguo - A traditional dish similar to hot pot that is cooked in a copper pot with various meats, vegetables, and seasonings.

Local Beverages

Handan offers a variety of local drinks to try, including:

  1. Jiurenbai - A white spirit distilled from sorghum and wheat that has a subtle flavor and aroma.

  2. Hongniangzui - A red wine that is produced in Handan’s Yixian region and has a rich, full-bodied flavor.

  3. Handantuanjiu - A locally brewed liquor that is made by fermenting glutinous rice with a special blend of herbs and spices.

Where to Stay in Handan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Handan offers a range of accommodation options to suit every traveler’s budget and style. Here are some top options:

  1. Handan Shangri-La Hotel - A five-star hotel located in the city center with luxurious rooms and amenities.

  2. Jinjiang Inn Handan Railway Station - A budget-friendly hotel located close to the railway station that offers clean and comfortable rooms.

  3. Yijia Apartment Hotel - A self-catering hotel that provides private apartments with kitchens and living rooms.

What to Buy in Handan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Handan is known for its unique handicrafts, such as paper-cutting, embroidery, jade carving, and porcelain. Here are some local specialties to bring home as souvenirs:

  1. Embroidery - Traditional Handan-style embroidery is famous for its intricate designs and vibrant colors.

  2. Jade carvings - Handan’s Yixian region is known for producing high-quality jade that is meticulously carved into beautiful jewelry and sculptures.

  3. Hand-painted porcelain - Handan has a rich history of porcelain production, and you can find beautifully hand-painted porcelain pieces in local markets.

Local Culture and Customs of Handan

Handan has a rich and unique local culture that is distinct from other regions in China. Here are some customs and traditions to keep in mind:

  1. Handan Dialect - Handan has its own distinct dialect that differs from the standard Mandarin language used in most parts of China.

  2. Tea Culture - Handan has a vibrant tea culture, and it is common to see locals socializing in tea houses or enjoying tea in public parks.

  3. Martial Arts - Handan has a long history of martial arts, and it is common to see groups practicing Tai Chi or other forms of martial arts in public spaces.

Best Time to Visit Handan

The best time to visit Handan is during the spring and autumn seasons, when the weather is mild, and the scenery is at its most colorful. The summers in Handan can be hot and humid, while the winters can be cold and snowy.

Do’s and Don’ts of Handan

Do:

  1. Try to learn some basic Mandarin or Handan dialect to communicate with the locals.

  2. Respect the local culture and traditions and take off shoes before entering someone’s home or temple.

  3. Sample the local cuisine and try new dishes.

  4. Wear comfortable shoes for sightseeing and hiking.

Don’t:

  1. Dress inappropriately at cultural sites or religious centers.

  2. Act rudely or disrespectfully towards locals or their customs.

  3. Take photographs or record videos without permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Handan

Handan is a relatively safe city, but it’s still essential to exercise caution and be aware of your surroundings - especially at night or in crowded places. To stay safe, avoid carrying large sums of cash or valuables and keep a close eye on your belongings.

Health in Handan

Handan has good healthcare facilities, and it’s easy to find both western and traditional medicine practitioners. However, it’s recommended that you purchase travel insurance before your trip.

Getting to Handan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Handan is accessible by air, train, or bus. Here are some transportation options to consider:

Airports Near Handan

  1. Handan Airport - The city’s main airport with daily flight connections to Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, and other major cities in China.

Bus Stations Near Handan

  1. Handan Long-Distance Bus Station - Located in the city center with daily bus connections to other cities in Hebei Province and beyond.

  2. Handan Railway Station - A hub for both train and bus travel with regular connections to Beijing, Tianjin, and other destinations in China.

Other Options

Traveling within the city is easy and affordable by taxi, bus, or bicycle.

How to Get Around in Handan

Handan has efficient public transportation options available, such as buses and taxis. However, if you want to explore the city at your own pace, renting a bicycle or car is a good option.

Where to Go Next from Handan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Handan is situated in a region with several nearby destinations to explore, including:

  1. Taihang Mountain - A scenic mountain range with stunning views and excellent hiking trails.

  2. Cangyan Mountain - A natural scenic area with notable geological formations and ancient temples.

  3. Kaifeng - A historic city in Henan Province with a rich cultural heritage that dates back to ancient times.

Conclusion

Handan is a fascinating and dynamic city that has something for everyone, whether you’re interested in history, culture, nature, or adventure. This travel guide has provided you with a comprehensive list of places to visit, food to try, accommodation options, and transportation logistics to help plan your trip. With its unique local culture, fascinating history, and breathtaking landscapes, Handan is sure to leave a lasting impression on visitors.
