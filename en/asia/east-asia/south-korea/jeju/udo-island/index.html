A Comprehensive Guide to Udo Island, South Korea

Udo Island is a small island paradise located off the coast of Jeju, South Korea. With its pristine beaches, stunning landscapes, and unique culture, Udo has become a popular destination for travelers. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll explore the best places to visit, things to do, food and drink scene, and local culture of Udo Island.

Introduction to Udo Island

Udo Island, also known as Udo-gun or Cow Island, is located in the Sea of Japan and is about 3.5 kilometers east of Jeju Island. With a circumference of only 11 kilometers, it is easily accessible by ferry from Jeju Island. Udo Island is home to a population of around 2,000 people who mainly rely on fishing and farming for their income. Udo Island is famous for its green pastures, beaches, and unique rock formations.

List of Places to Visit in Udo Island

Udo Island has numerous places to visit. Some of the top places to visit in Udo Island are:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Haenyeo Museum: The Haenyeo Museum is dedicated to the cultural heritage of Jeju’s female divers, also known as Haenyeo.
  • Geommeolle Beach: A famous beach in Udo Island with white sand and clear blue waters.
  • Seobin Baeksa: A lighthouse built during the reign of King Gojong of Korea. The lighthouse provides spectacular views of the ocean and the surrounding areas.
  • Miro Beach: A picturesque beach with clear blue waters, unique rock formations and stunning sunset views.
  • Udo Folk Village: A village that showcases the traditional houses and lifestyle of the Jeju people.

Natural Attractions of Udo Island: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Seongsan Ilchulbong Peak: A dramatic volcanic cone in Jeju Island known as “Sunrise Peak”. It provides stunning panoramic views of the island and the sea.
  • Suwolbong Peak: A limestone peak located in the north of Udo Island.
  • Seobin Baeksa: A lighthouse built during the reign of King Gojong of Korea. The lighthouse provides spectacular views of the ocean and the surrounding areas.
  • Seonnyeotang: A pristine and secluded beach in Udo Island.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Udo Island

Udo Island has a few hiking and biking routes that offer breathtaking views of the island. The top hiking/biking routes in Udo Island are:

  • Olle Trail: A popular and well-marked trail that takes you along the coast of the island.
  • Suwolbong Peak Trail: A challenging but rewarding trail that takes you to the limestone peak of Suwolbong.
  • Seongsan Ilchulbong Sunrise Peak Trail: A popular trail that takes you to the summit of the volcanic cone for an unforgettable sunrise view.

Best Things to Do in Udo Island: Activities and Tours

  • Kayak Tour: Take a guided kayak tour around Udo Island’s coastline and explore the island’s hidden caves and coral reefs.
  • Snorkeling: Snorkel and explore Udo Island’s vibrant and colorful underwater world.
  • Visiting Udo Haenyeo: Watch a demonstration of Jeju’s female divers by visiting Udo Haenyeo.
  • Live Aquarium: Visit Udo Island’s marine park and enjoy the company of exotic marine life.
  • Jeju and Udo Island Tour: Take a guided tour to Jeju Island and Udo Island and explore the island’s beautiful landscape.

Week-Long Itinerary for Udo Island

  • Day 1: Explore the Haenyeo Museum and have lunch at one of the island’s seafood shacks.
  • Day 2: Rent a bike and explore the scenic routes of Udo Island.
  • Day 3: Take a ferry to Jeju Island and visit Seongsan Ilchulbong and the Manjanggul Cave.
  • Day 4: Snorkeling and relaxing at Seonnyeotang Beach.
  • Day 5: Take a kayak tour around Udo Island’s coastline.
  • Day 6: Visit Suwolbong Peak and hike the trail to the summit.
  • Day 7: Pay a visit to the Miro Beach and enjoy the beautiful scenery.

Food and Drink Scene of Udo Island

Udo Island offers a delicious and authentic South Korean cuisine. The must-try dishes on Udo Island are:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Seafood: Udo Island is famous for its fresh seafood. Try some grilled fish, octopus, and squid.
  • Odeng: A type of fish cake that is perfect for a snack.
  • Gimbap: A Korean sushi roll that is perfect for breakfast or a light snack.

Local Beverages

  • Jeju Tangerine Juice: A refreshing and tasty juice made from the tangerines grown in Jeju Island.
  • Makgeolli: Rice wine, also known as “Korean Sake.” It is a popular drink in South Korea.

Where to Stay in Udo Island: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Udo Island offers a range of accommodation options to suit every budget.

  • Jeju Udo Sky Palace Pension: A beautiful and cozy guesthouse that provides stunning views of the ocean.
  • Constellation Hotel: A new and modern hotel that is perfect for those who want to explore Udo Island in luxury.
  • Udo Guesthouse: A budget-friendly guesthouse that is perfect for backpackers and budget travelers.

What to Buy in Udo Island: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Udo Mandarins: Take home a bag of Udo Island’s famous mandarins.
  • Jeju Tangerines: A delicious and juicy fruit grown in Jeju Island.
  • Jeju Black Pork: A unique pork meat that is raised in Jeju Island.

Local Culture and Customs of Udo Island

Udo Island is heavily influenced by the culture and traditions of Jeju Island. The local culture and customs of Udo Island are:

  • Haenyeo: The Haenyeo, or female divers, are an important part of the culture and economy of Udo Island. They dive for seafood and shells to sell at the markets.
  • Jeju Folklore: The island has a rich history of folklore and legends, including a story about a famous general who fought against Korean pirates.

Best Time to Visit Udo Island

The best time to visit Udo Island is during the spring and fall seasons when the weather is mild and the crowds are thin. The summer season is also a popular time to visit Udo Island when the weather is warm and sunny, but it can be crowded.

Do’s and Don’ts of Udo Island

Do:

  • Do respect the local culture and customs
  • Do try the local seafood
  • Do explore the scenic routes on a bike or by foot
  • Do visit one of Udo Island’s pristine beaches

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter
  • Don’t touch or disturb the underwater life
  • Don’t forget to bring sunscreen

Safety and Health

Safety in Udo Island

Udo Island is a safe destination for travelers. However, as with any destination, you should take basic precautions such as locking your valuables, being aware of your surroundings, and not leaving your belongings unattended.

Health in Udo Island

The health services in Udo Island are limited. It is advisable to bring your medications, and consult your doctor before traveling to Udo Island. It is also important to bring enough sunscreen, insect repellent, and first aid kit.

Getting to Udo Island: Transportation Options and Logistics

To get to Udo Island, you can take a ferry from Jeju Island’s Seongsan Harbor or Cheonjiyeon Waterfall Harbor. The ferries operate every day, and the tickets cost around 5,000 KRW per person.

Airports Near Udo Island

The nearest airport to Udo Island is Jeju International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Udo Island

There is no direct bus service to Udo Island, but you can take a bus from the Jeju Bus Terminal to Seongsan Harbor.

Other Options

You can also hire a taxi or rent a car to travel to Udo Island.

How to Get Around in Udo Island

The best way to get around Udo Island is by bike or on foot. You can also take a taxi to explore the island.

Where to Go Next from Udo Island: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some of the nearby destinations and excursions from Udo Island are:

  • Jeju Island: Take a ferry to Jeju Island and explore its stunning landscape and culture.
  • Seongsan Ilchulbong: Hike to the summit of the volcanic cone for a breathtaking view.
  • Manjanggul Cave: A UNESCO World Heritage site that showcases the natural beauty of Jeju Island.

Conclusion

Udo Island is a small island paradise that offers breathtaking views, unique cultural heritage, and delicious seafood. Whether you are a solo traveler or traveling with family, Udo Island has something for everyone. Plan your trip using this comprehensive travel guide and create unforgettable memories.
