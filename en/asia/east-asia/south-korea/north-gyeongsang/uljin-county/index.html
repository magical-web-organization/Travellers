Introduction to Uljin County

Uljin County is situated in the northern part of Gyeongsang province, in South Korea. This region is known for its natural beauty, with stunning coastal views, glistening beaches, and lush mountains. Uljin County is a hidden gem that offers plenty of activities and attractions to visitors.

List of Places to Visit in Uljin County

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Cheokamjeong Pavilion

Cheokamjeong Pavilion is a popular cultural landmark that offers stunning views of the East Sea. This traditional-style pavilion was built during the Joseon Dynasty and is worth a visit for its historical significance and breathtaking views.

Huiwon, Seongryugul Cave

Huiwon, Seongryugul Cave is a natural cave that is famous for its gorgeous waterfalls and unusual rock formations. Visitors can take a guided tour of the cave and marvel at its mystical beauty.

Natural Attractions of Uljin County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Jangsa Beach

Jangsa Beach is a stunning stretch of sand that is ideal for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports. The beach is surrounded by lush mountains and offers spectacular views of the East Sea.

Seongryugul Canyon

Seongryugul Canyon is a natural wonder that is home to a stunning waterfall and a lush forest. Visitors can hike through the canyon and enjoy the fresh air and tranquil surroundings.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Uljin County

Dong Jeonsan Mountain

Dong Jeonsan Mountain is a popular hiking destination that offers stunning views of the East Sea. The mountain is located in the Uljin Mountain Range and features several hiking trails with varying levels of difficulty.

Biking Trails

Uljin County is known for its numerous biking trails that offer breathtaking views of the coast and mountains. The most popular biking trail is the Saemangeum Seawall Bikeway, which is a 33-kilometer route that runs along the seawall.

Best Things to Do in Uljin County: Activities and Tours

Whale Watching Tour

Whale watching is a popular activity in Uljin County. Visitors can take a boat tour to see minke whales, sperm whales, and dolphins in their natural habitat.

Seafood Market Tour

Uljin is famous for its high-quality seafood. Visitors can take a tour of the local seafood market, where they can taste fresh sea urchins, abalone, and other delicious seafood dishes.

Week-Long Itinerary for Uljin County

Day Itinerary
Day 1 Visit Cheokamjeong Pavilion and Jangsa Beach
Day 2 Take a whale watching tour and visit Seongryugul Canyon
Day 3 Explore Huiwon, Seongryugul Cave and Uljin Seo Market

Day 7 Relax and soak up the sun at Jangsa Beach or nearby beaches

Food and Drink Scene of Uljin County

Must-Try Dishes

Raw Fish

Uljin County is known for its high-quality raw fish dishes, which are usually served with soy sauce and wasabi. One popular dish is the Hoe, a plate of sliced raw fish that is accompanied by vegetables and spices.

Saeujeot

Saeujeot is a type of fermented seafood that is made with small shrimp, anchovies, and other seafood. The dish is often served as a condiment and is a popular ingredient in kimchi.

Local Beverages

Makkoli

Makkoli is a traditional Korean alcoholic beverage that is made by fermenting rice or wheat. The drink has a milky white color and a slightly sweet taste.

Hwangju Jeung

Hwangju Jeung is a type of traditional Korean medicine that is believed to have numerous health benefits. The drink is made with a variety of herbs and is often served hot.

Where to Stay in Uljin County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hotels

Ocean View Hotel

Ocean View Hotel is a popular hotel that offers stunning views of the East Sea. The hotel features comfortable rooms and a restaurant that serves delicious seafood dishes.

Uljin Grand Hotel

Uljin Grand Hotel is a luxurious hotel that offers a range of amenities, including an outdoor pool, fitness center, and spa. The hotel is located in a convenient location that is close to local attractions.

What to Buy in Uljin County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Dried Seafood

Uljin County is famous for its high-quality dried seafood, which includes squid, anchovies, and more. These make for perfect souvenirs to take back home as gifts.

Uljin Seo Ddeok

Uljin Seo Ddeok is a type of traditional Korean rice cake that is made with sticky rice and sugar. It is a popular snack that can be found in local markets and gift shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Uljin County

Uljin County is known for its rich history and culture. Many of the local festivals reflect these traditions, including the Uljin Pumba Festival, which celebrates the local Pumba dance.

Best Time to Visit Uljin County

The best time to visit Uljin County is during the spring and autumn months. The weather during this time is mild, and the scenery is breathtaking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Uljin County

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Try local cuisine and beverages
  • Visit Uljin’s famous landmarks and attractions

Don’t:

  • Litter in public places
  • Wear revealing clothing in temples or religious sites
  • Speak loudly or disrespectfully in public

Safety and Health

Safety in Uljin County

Uljin County is relatively safe for visitors. However, visitors should take care when hiking or biking on the local trails and should follow proper safety guidelines.

Health in Uljin County

Uljin County has several medical facilities that provide quality healthcare to residents and visitors. It is always a good idea to have proper travel insurance in case of emergencies.

Getting to Uljin County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Uljin County

The nearest airports to Uljin County are Pohang Airport and Ulsan Airport, both of which are located a few hours away by car.

Bus Stations Near Uljin County

Uljin has several bus stations that connect to nearby cities and towns, including Pohang and Gyeongju.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi to Uljin County from nearby cities and towns.

How to Get Around in Uljin County

Uljin County has several modes of transportation, including buses, taxis, and rental cars. Visitors can also rent bicycles to explore the local area.

Where to Go Next from Uljin County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Pohang

Pohang is a popular destination that is known for its beautiful beaches and historic landmarks. Visitors can explore the city’s cultural heritage at the Pohang Museum of Steel Art and the Hurshimchung Spa.

Gyeongju

Gyeongju is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that is known for its rich history and cultural significance. Visitors can explore the city’s famous landmarks, including the Bulguksa Temple and the Gyeongju National Museum.

Conclusion

Uljin County is a hidden gem in South Korea that offers something for everyone. From its stunning natural beauty to its rich cultural heritage, this region is a must-visit for anyone looking to explore the country’s hidden gems.
