Introduction to Suncheon

Nestled in the heart of South Jeolla province, Suncheon is a charming city that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and deep historical significance. Spread over an area of 907.45 km², Suncheon is a haven for nature enthusiasts, history buffs, and adventure seekers alike.

List of Places to Visit in Suncheon

Suncheon offers a plethora of attractions for visitors to explore. From culturally important landmarks to natural wonders, here’s our list of top places to visit in Suncheon:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Suncheon Open Film Set
  • Suncheon Bay History Museum
  • Nagan Eupseong Folk Village

Natural Attractions of Suncheon: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Suncheon Bay Wetland Reserve
  • Seonamsa Temple
  • Jogyesan Provincial Park
  • Yongsan Observatory

Hiking/Biking Routes of Suncheon

With its sprawling natural landscapes and mountainous terrain, Suncheon offers numerous hiking and biking trails that let you enjoy the great outdoors. Here are some popular routes:

  • Jogyesan Recreational Forest Trail
  • Suncheon Bay Eco Park Trail
  • Nogodan Summit Trail

Best Things to Do in Suncheon: Activities and Tours

There’s never a dull moment in Suncheon, and there’s something for everyone to enjoy. Some must-try activities and tours include:

  • Suncheon Bay Night View Kayaking Tour

Food and Drink Scene of Suncheon

No visit to Suncheon is complete without savoring its mouthwatering local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Dakgalbi (spicy stir-fried chicken)
  • Jeonbokjuk (abalone porridge)
  • Gomtang (beef bone soup)

Local Beverages

  • Korean Rice Wine (Makgeolli)
  • Soju
  • Green Tea

What to Buy in Suncheon: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Local Culture and Customs of Suncheon

Suncheon has a rich cultural heritage and traditions that visitors should be aware of. Here are some local customs to keep in mind:

  • Remove shoes before entering a Korean traditional house
  • Use both hands while giving or receiving objects

Best Time to Visit Suncheon

The best time to visit Suncheon is from April to November when the weather is mild and ideal for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Suncheon

Here are some essential do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting Suncheon:

Do:

  • Wear comfortable shoes while exploring the city’s natural attractions
  • Respect Korean manners and customs

Don’t:

  • Smoke in public places
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions

Safety and Health

It’s important to take care of your safety and health while in Suncheon. Here are some essentials to keep in mind:

Safety in Suncheon

  • Be mindful of your surroundings and keep an eye on your belongings
  • Don’t walk alone at night in unfamiliar areas

Health in Suncheon

  • It’s important to drink plenty of water to stay hydrated in the warm weather
  • Always carry your prescription medications with you

Suncheon is a relatively small city that’s easy to explore on foot or by using public transportation. You can also rent a bike or take a taxi to get around.
