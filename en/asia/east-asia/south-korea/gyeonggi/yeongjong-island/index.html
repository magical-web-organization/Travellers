Located in Gyeonggi, Yeongjong Island is a picturesque destination that boasts natural attractions, cultural landmarks, delicious dining options, and unique local customs. This comprehensive guide will take you through everything you need to know about planning a trip to Yeongjong Island.

Introduction to Yeongjong Island

Yeongjong Island is a small island located in the West Sea, just off the coast of Incheon in South Korea. The island is home to Incheon International Airport, the largest and primary airport in the country. Despite being best known for its airport, Yeongjong Island is also a popular tourist spot, offering a range of experiences for visitors to enjoy.

List of Places to Visit in Yeongjong Island

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Korean Traditional Archery Experience Center: Visitors can learn about and practice traditional Korean archery at this center.
  2. Wolmi Traditional Park: This historical park has traditional Korean buildings and hosts cultural events and performances.
  3. Songdo Central Park: Known for its numerous sculptures, water features, and fauna, this park has hosted international events like the G-20 Summit.

Natural Attractions of Yeongjong Island: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Muuido Island: This island is located off the coast of Yeongjong Island and is known for its pristine beaches and hiking trails.
  2. Eulwangri Beach: This is a picturesque beach on the west coast of Yeongjong Island, known for its clear blue water.
  3. Yongyu Island: This nearby island has rocky cliffs, beaches, and a lighthouse.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Yeongjong Island

  1. Gapado Trail: A 4.4-km long trail that starts at the park entrance of Gapado and takes about 2.5 hours to complete.
  2. Yonggungsa Temple: This is an easy hiking trail that leads to a well-known temple on the island.
  3. Haesindang Park: This park is known for its distinct phallic statues arranged along the hiking trail.

Best Things to Do in Yeongjong Island: Activities and Tours

  1. Parasailing: Experience breathtaking views of Yeongjong Island while soaring through the sky.
  2. Yacht Tours: Take a leisurely cruise on the open water and enjoy the island’s stunning coastline.
  3. Jade Garden: Walk through this large garden and soak in the natural beauty of the flowers, trees, and ponds.

Week-Long Itinerary for Yeongjong Island

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit the Songdo Central Park and Incheon Art Platform
Day 2 Hike/bike the Gapado Trail and Muuido Island
Day 3 Take a yacht tour of the surrounding small islands
Day 4 Explore the Eulwangri Beach and Nuri Peace Park
Day 5 Visit Oriental Medicine Theme Park and learn about traditional Korean medicine
Day 6 Try your hand at traditional Korean archery and visit Yongyu Island
Day 7 Make souvenirs at the Ganghwa Island Ginseng Farming Museum and departure from Incheon International Airport

Food and Drink Scene of Yeongjong Island

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Jajangmyeon: This is the Korean style of Chinese black bean noodles.
  2. Dakgalbi: A spicy chicken dish, usually cooked tableside.
  3. Haemul pajeon: A savory seafood pancake.

Local Beverages

  1. Bokbunja: A wine made from wild black raspberries.
  2. Makgeolli: A traditional Korean rice wine.
  3. Soju: A clear, distilled alcoholic beverage.

Where to Stay in Yeongjong Island: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Novotel Ambassador Incheon Airport: A modern, upscale hotel with great amenities and comfortable rooms.
  2. Incheon Airport Transit Hotel: Located inside the airport, this hotel offers a convenient place to stay for travelers with long layovers.
  3. Goodday Guesthouse: This cozy guesthouse is known for its friendly staff and comfortable rooms.

What to Buy in Yeongjong Island: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Ginseng: Known for its medicinal properties, ginseng products are a popular souvenir from Yeongjong Island.
  2. Incheon Ceramic Bowls: A traditional and beautiful souvenir that can be used for various purposes.
  3. Jeulmun Pottery: This a type of Korean pottery that has been made in the region for thousands of years.

Local Culture and Customs of Yeongjong Island

The residents of Yeongjong Island are proud of their heritage and traditional customs. One unique custom is that the local people do not discard their old underwear, but use it as stuffing material for pillows.

Best Time to Visit Yeongjong Island

The best time to visit Yeongjong Island is during the spring and fall seasons, as the weather is mild and pleasant during these times. However, tourists can visit the island year-round.

Do’s and Don’ts of Yeongjong Island

Do:

  1. Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  2. Visit the historical landmarks and natural attractions.
  3. Respect the customs and traditions of the local people.

Don’t:

  1. Litter on the island.
  2. Disrespect the culture and customs of the local people.
  3. Take rocks or plants from the natural attractions as souvenirs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Yeongjong Island

Yeongjong Island is a safe destination with a low crime rate. Visitors should take precautions with their valuables and stay aware of their surroundings, particularly in crowded areas or tourist spots.

Health in Yeongjong Island

Health facilities in Yeongjong Island are of high-quality and accessible to tourists. It is recommended that visitors have travel insurance to cover any unexpected medical emergencies.

Getting to Yeongjong Island: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Yeongjong Island

  1. Incheon International Airport: Located on Yeongjong Island, this is the primary airport in South Korea.
  2. Gimpo International Airport: Located in Seoul, this airport is about 33 km away from Yeongjong Island.

Bus Stations Near Yeongjong Island

  1. Incheon Bus Terminal: This bus terminal offers domestic and international transportation to other destinations in South Korea.
  2. Seoul Express Bus Terminal: This is one of the largest bus terminals in South Korea and offers transportation to other destinations in the country.

Other Options

  1. Taxi: Taxis are readily available in Yeongjong Island and are a convenient way to get around the island.
  2. Rental Car: Visitors can rent a car to explore Yeongjong Island and its surroundings independently.

How to Get Around in Yeongjong Island

  1. Bus: Buses are the most common form of transportation in Yeongjong Island and offer convenient routes to most tourist destinations on the island.
  2. Taxi: Taxis are readily available and are a convenient way to travel. Fares are reasonable, and all the taxis have meters.
  3. Bike Rental: Renting a bike is an excellent way to explore Yeongjong Island’s scenic routes.

Where to Go Next from Yeongjong Island: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Seoul: South Korea’s capital city is about an hour’s drive away from Yeongjong Island.
  2. Incheon: A bustling port city, Incheon is just across the bridge from the island.
  3. Gyeonggi Province: Explore the natural landmarks and cultural landmarks in the province.

Conclusion

Yeongjong Island offers visitors a unique blend of cultural landmarks, outdoor activities, delicious cuisine, and beautiful scenery. Whether you’re looking for a relaxing beach vacation or an adventurous trip filled with hiking and biking, Yeongjong Island has something for everyone. Plan your trip now and discover all that this beautiful island has to offer!
