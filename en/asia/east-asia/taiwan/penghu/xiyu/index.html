Introduction to Xiyu

Xiyu is a beautiful island located in the Penghu Archipelago, off the coast of Taiwan. With its picturesque beaches, stunning landscapes, and rich cultural heritage, it’s a top attraction for visitors to Taiwan. Xiyu is known for its crystal-clear waters, which are ideal for snorkeling and diving. There are also plenty of opportunities for hiking, biking, and exploring the island’s historical sites.

List of Places to Visit in Xiyu

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

There are a number of important cultural and historical sites in Xiyu, including:

  • Xiyu Western Fort
  • Xiyu Tianhou Temple
  • Zhaishan Tunnel
  • Penghu Ancient City
  • Guanyin Pavilion

Natural Attractions of Xiyu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Xiyu is also home to a number of natural wonders that are worth visiting, including:

  • Shanshui Beach
  • Chixi Bay
  • Yuwengdao Lighthouse
  • Lincoln Rock
  • Windmill Park

Hiking/Biking Routes of Xiyu

If you’re an adventurous traveler, you can explore Xiyu’s hiking and biking trails. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • Xiyu - Cimei Trail
  • Daguoye Coastal Hiking Trail
  • Jitou Mountain Trekking Trail
  • Banchiao Reservoir Bicycle Trail
  • Xiyu Peacock Bike Trail

Best Things to Do in Xiyu: Activities and Tours

There’s plenty to do on Xiyu, including:

  • Snorkeling and diving
  • Beach hopping
  • Taking a boat tour to the surrounding islands
  • Visiting local markets and shops
  • Participating in a temple ceremony

Week-Long Itinerary for Xiyu

If you’re planning to spend a week in Xiyu, you can follow this itinerary:

  • Day 1: Visit Xiyu Western Fort and Xiyu Tianhou Temple
  • Day 2: Explore Zhaishan Tunnel and Penghu Ancient City
  • Day 3: Hike the Xiyu-Cimei Trail
  • Day 4: Relax at Shanshui Beach and Chixi Bay
  • Day 5: Take a boat tour to the surrounding islands
  • Day 6: Visit Yuwengdao Lighthouse and Windmill Park
  • Day 7: Enjoy Peacock Bike Trail and local markets

Local Culture and Customs of Xiyu

Xiyu has deep cultural and religious traditions which are worth exploring, such as:

  • The Sea Goddess Festival
  • The Mazu pilgramage
  • The Penghu Fireworks Festival

Best Time to Visit Xiyu

The best time to visit Xiyu is from May to October, as the weather is sunny and humid, and perfect for outdoor activities. The water temperatures are also ideal during these months for swimming, snorkeling, and diving.

Do’s and Don’ts of Xiyu

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Wear sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat when you are outside
  • Try the seafood and local specialties

Don’t:

  • Litter on the beaches or at nature areas
  • Remove shells or rocks from the beaches
  • Enter temples with shoes on

Safety and Health

Safety in Xiyu

Xiyu is generally a safe destination to visit, but it’s important to be aware of the following:

  • Take precautions when swimming or diving in the ocean
  • Wear proper footwear when hiking or biking
  • Do not leave your belongings unattended in public areas

Health in Xiyu

The tap water in Xiyu is generally safe to drink, but it’s recommended that you drink bottled water. Mosquito-borne diseases such as Dengue fever and Zika virus can be a risk in some areas, so it’s important to take precautions when going outdoors.

Getting to Xiyu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Xiyu

The closest airport to Xiyu is Magong Airport, which is serviced by domestic airlines such as Uni Air and Mandarin Airlines. From Magong Airport, you can take a taxi or a bus to Xiyu.

Bus Stations Near Xiyu

There are several bus stations in Xiyu that offer transportation to other parts of Penghu and Taiwan, including:

  • Magong Bus Station
  • Xiyu Bus Station
  • Qimei Bus Station
  • Baisha Bus Station

Other Options

You can also take a ferry to Xiyu from nearby islands such as Baisha Island and Qimei Island. It’s also possible to rent a car or motorcycle on the island if you prefer to explore on your own.

How to Get Around in Xiyu

The easiest way to get around in Xiyu is by scooter or bicycle, which can be rented from shops and guesthouses on the island. Taxis are also available for hire, but they can be expensive.

Where to Go Next from Xiyu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore other destinations in Penghu, some popular options include:

  • Baisha Island
  • Qimei Island
  • Magong City
  • Jibei Island

Conclusion

Xiyu is a must-visit destination for those who are seeking a unique and enjoyable island experience. With its rich history, stunning natural sights, and delicious food, Xiyu offers something for everyone. We hope that this comprehensive guide will help you make the most of your trip to Xiyu.
