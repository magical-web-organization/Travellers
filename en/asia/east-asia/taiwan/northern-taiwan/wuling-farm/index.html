Introduction to Wuling Farm

Located in the Yilan County of Northern Taiwan, Wuling Farm is a perfect destination for those who are looking for an escape from the hustle and bustle of the city. Surrounded by lush mountains, crystal-clear streams, and stunning scenery, Wuling Farm offers a unique combination of outdoor activities, cultural experience, and relaxation.

The elevation of Wuling Farm ranges from 1,150 meters to 2,327 meters above sea level, making it a perfect place to escape the heat during the summer months. In the winter, the farm is covered in snow, and visitors can enjoy winter sports like skiing and snowboarding.

List of Places to Visit in Wuling Farm

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Wuling Farm is home to a rich cultural heritage, and visitors can explore it by visiting various museums and historical places. The following are some must-visit cultural attractions in Wuling Farm:

  • Wuling Farm Visitor Center: This is a great starting point for visitors who want to learn more about Wuling Farm. The center provides a lot of information about the history, culture, and natural scenery of the area.
  • Yilan Distillery Chia Chi Lan Wine Museum: This museum displays the history of the famous Chia Chi Lan wine and the distilling methods used to make it. Visitors can also sample some of the best wines produced in the region.
  • Taipingshan National Forest Recreation Area: This is a great destination for those who want to immerse themselves in the natural beauty of Taiwan. Visitors can explore the park’s scenic trails, waterfalls, and wildlife.

Natural Attractions of Wuling Farm: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Wuling Farm is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. There are many hiking trails, biking routes, and natural attractions to explore. Here are some of the most popular outdoor and natural attractions in the area:

  • Hehuanshan: Also known as “Joy Mountain”, Hehuanshan is a popular destination for hiking and mountaineering. The mountain range reaches up to 3,417 meters, and the views from the top are breathtaking.
  • Wuling Suspension Bridge: This is the highest suspension bridge in Taiwan, and it offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
  • Lavender Forest: This is a popular spot for photography enthusiasts. The lavender fields bloom from June to August, and the scenery is simply stunning.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Wuling Farm

Wuling Farm has an extensive network of hiking and biking trails that offer visitors a chance to explore the natural beauty of the area. Here are some of the most popular hiking and biking routes in Wuling Farm:

  • Taoshan Waterfall Trail: This trail is known for its stunning scenery, including waterfalls and streams. The hike takes around 2-3 hours and is suitable for hikers of all levels.
  • Hehuanshan East Peak Trail: This trail is for experienced hikers only, and it leads to the highest peak in the area. The trail is challenging but offers rewarding views.
  • Wuling Cycling Trail: This is a popular biking route that takes visitors through the scenic countryside of Wuling Farm.

Best Things to Do in Wuling Farm: Activities and Tours

Visitors to Wuling Farm can enjoy a variety of activities and tours. Here are some of the best things to do in Wuling Farm:

  • Hot Spring Experience: Wuling Farm is home to several hot spring resorts that offer a relaxing and rejuvenating experience. Guests can soak in the hot springs and enjoy the surrounding scenery.
  • Mountain Climbing: Experienced climbers can climb the steep mountain ranges of Wuling Farm.
  • Zip Lining: The zip line adventure here is one of the most thrilling in Taiwan. Visitors can enjoy the adrenaline rush and breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

Food and Drink Scene of Wuling Farm

Wuling Farm is a great destination for foodies. The region is famous for its local cuisine, including wild boar, mushrooms, and other fresh produce. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Wild Boar Wontons: This is a signature dish in Wuling Farm. The wontons are filled with juicy, tender wild boar meat and served in a savory broth.
  • Shitake Mushroom Stir-Fry: This dish features fresh shitake mushrooms that are stir-fried with garlic and green onions.
  • Roast Beef: Wuling Farm is known for producing high-quality beef. The roast beef is cooked to perfection and served with rice or noodles.

Local Beverages

  • Chia Chi Lan Wine: This is a famous wine produced in Wuling Farm. Visitors can visit the Yilan Distillery Chia Chi Lan Wine Museum to learn more about the wine-making process and sample some of the best wines in the region.
  • Herbal Tea: Wuling Farm has a rich tradition of herbal tea making. Visitors can try a variety of local herbal teas, which are made with local herbs and plants.

Where to Stay in Wuling Farm: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Wuling Farm has a variety of accommodations to choose from. Visitors can stay in cozy guesthouses or luxurious resorts. Here are some of the best options:

  • Wuling Sunrise House: This guesthouse offers cozy rooms and great views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Wuling Farm Villa: This is a 5-star resort that offers luxurious accommodations and great amenities.
  • Wuling Mountain Lodge: This is a great option for those who want to experience the local culture. The lodge offers traditional Taiwanese-style accommodations and great hospitality.

What to Buy in Wuling Farm: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Wuling Farm is a great place to buy unique souvenirs and local products. Here are some must-buy souvenirs:

  • Wild Boar Jerky: This is a unique snack made with tender, flavorful wild boar meat.
  • Herbal Tea: Visitors can buy a variety of locally made herbal teas, which are a great souvenir to bring home.
  • Handmade Crafts: Visitors can buy a variety of handmade crafts, including pottery and woodwork.

Local Culture and Customs of Wuling Farm

Wuling Farm has a rich cultural heritage that visitors can experience by attending local festivals, visiting traditional villages, and trying the local cuisine. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and visitors should respect their customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Wuling Farm

The best time to visit Wuling Farm is from April to June and from September to November. During these months, the weather is mild, and the scenery is stunning. Visitors can enjoy outdoor activities like hiking and biking without worrying about extreme heat or cold.

Safety and Health

Safety in Wuling Farm

Wuling Farm is a relatively safe destination, but visitors should be careful when hiking or engaging in outdoor activities. Visitors should wear appropriate clothing and footwear and be aware of their surroundings.

Health in Wuling Farm

Visitors to Wuling Farm should be aware of the potential risks of high altitude. Visitors with heart or respiratory conditions should consult their doctors before visiting Wuling Farm.
