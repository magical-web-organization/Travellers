Introduction to Pingxi

Pingxi, located in New Taipei, in Northern Taiwan, is a charming town in the mountains known for its beautiful vistas and stunning natural landscapes. The town is famous for its annual lantern festival, which attracts visitors from all over the world. This guide will provide you with all the necessary information to explore Pingxi, including the best things to see, do, eat, and experience in the town.

Local Culture and Customs of Pingxi

Pingxi is famous for its sky lanterns, which are released during the annual lantern festival. The lanterns are decorated with wishes and messages of hope and released into the air to bring good luck.

Best Time to Visit Pingxi

The best time to visit Pingxi is during the annual lantern festival, which falls sometime between January and March.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pingxi

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Be mindful of the environment and keep the streets clean.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Release sky lanterns outside of designated areas.
  • Damage local flora and fauna.
  • Litter in public spaces.

Safety and Health

Safety in Pingxi

Pingxi is a safe town with low crime rates. However, visitors should take usual precautions to protect themselves from pickpocketing and theft.

Health in Pingxi

The tap water is safe to drink in Pingxi, but visitors should avoid drinking from streams or unknown sources.

Getting to Pingxi: Transportation Options and Logistics

Pingxi is easily accessible by bus or train from Taipei.

Airports Near Pingxi

The nearest international airport to Pingxi is the Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport, which is approximately a 50-minute drive away.

Bus Stations Near Pingxi

Pingxi Bus Station is the main bus station in the town and provides connections to the surrounding areas and Taipei.

Other Options

Taxis and rental cars are also available for hire in Taipei.

How to Get Around in Pingxi

The best way to get around Pingxi is on foot or by bicycle. The town is small and easily navigable.
