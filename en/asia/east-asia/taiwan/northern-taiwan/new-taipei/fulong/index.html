Introduction to Fulong

Fulong is a charming coastal town located in Northern Taiwan. It boasts a beautiful beach, quaint streets, and a rich culture. Fulong is a popular destination for tourists, offering a variety of activities, from hiking and biking to swimming and surfing.

List of Places to Visit in Fulong

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fulong Old Street: A historic street lined with shops and restaurants, featuring traditional architecture.
  • Caoling Historical Trail: A hike to the top of a mountain, featuring stunning views and historical landmarks.
  • Houtong Cat Village: A village famous for its cat population and cat-themed attractions.
  • Fulong Railway Museum: A museum showcasing the history of the railway in Fulong.

Natural Attractions of Fulong: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Fulong Beach: A wide sandy beach with crystal-clear water, ideal for swimming and sunbathing.
  • Qiguangyan Scenic Area: A scenic area featuring unique geological formations and hiking trails.
  • Longmen Campsite: A peaceful campsite surrounded by nature, perfect for a weekend getaway.
  • Caves of the Eight Immortals: A series of caves featuring stunning natural formations.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fulong

  • Sandiaoling Waterfall Trail: A scenic hike to three waterfalls and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Longmen Tidal Cave Trail: A coastal hike featuring sea caves and tidal pools.
  • Caoling Historic Trail: A hike to the top of a mountain that provides panoramic views of Fulong town.

Best Things to Do in Fulong: Activities and Tours

  • Surfing: Fulong is a popular destination for surfers, with waves suitable for all levels.
  • River Tracing: An adventure activity that involves following a river’s rocky path through canyons.
  • Rafting: A popular activity in Fulong’s rivers, with rapids ranging from class II to class IV.
  • Food Tour: A culinary experience where you can sample local delicacies and snacks.

Best Time to Visit Fulong

The best time to visit Fulong is between May and October when the weather is warm and sunny, perfect for outdoor activities. The peak season is in July and August, and the town can get crowded.

Safety and Health

Safety in Fulong

Fulong is a safe town, and crime rates are low. However, visitors are advised to take standard safety precautions, such as not leaving valuables unattended.

Health in Fulong

No vaccines are required to visit Taiwan. However, it is advisable to bring sunscreen and insect repellent and to drink bottled water.
