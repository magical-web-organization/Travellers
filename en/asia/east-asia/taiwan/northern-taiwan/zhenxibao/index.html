Introduction to Zhenxibao

Zhenxibao is a charming village located in the mountainous region of Yilan County in Northern Taiwan. Known for its verdant mountains, crystal-clear streams, and breathtaking views, Zhenxibao is a paradise for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. The village is also steeped in rich cultural heritage, with several historical landmarks and museums that offer a glimpse into Taiwan’s history and traditions.

List of Places to Visit in Zhenxibao

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Wufongci Scenic Area

Wufongci Scenic Area is a popular tourist destination in Zhenxibao known for its unique rock formations and ancient temple. The temple is dedicated to the Chinese god of literature and music, Wen Chang, and attracts many visitors who come to seek blessings for academic success.

Yilan Museum of Art

Yilan Museum of Art is housed in a beautifully restored Japanese colonial-era building and features a fascinating collection of contemporary and traditional art. The museum has an impressive outdoor sculpture garden that showcases the work of local artists.

Zhenxibao Old Street

Zhenxibao Old Street is a quaint street lined with traditional Taiwanese houses, souvenir shops, and street vendors selling local delicacies. The street is a great place to admire the unique architecture of the old houses and shop for souvenirs.

Natural Attractions of Zhenxibao: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Taiping Mountain

Taiping Mountain is a must-visit destination for hikers and offers stunning views of Yilan County from the top. The mountain has several trails of varying difficulty levels, and the hike to the summit is a challenging but rewarding experience.

Guishan Island

Guishan Island is a small island located off the coast of Zhenxibao and is known for its stunning views and diverse marine life. Visitors can take a boat tour around the island or go scuba diving to explore the underwater world.

Jhuilu Old Trail

Jhuilu Old Trail is a historic trail that was used by the indigenous tribes of Taiwan to transport goods between different villages. The trail offers breathtaking views of the mountainous landscape and is a great place for a leisurely hike.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Zhenxibao

Taiping Mountain Hiking Trail

The Taiping Mountain Hiking Trail is a popular route that takes visitors through some of the most scenic parts of Zhenxibao. The trail is well-marked and offers spectacular views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

Yilan Bike Trail

The Yilan Bike Trail is a 130 km-long trail that offers visitors a chance to explore the beauty of Yilan County on a bike. The trail passes through several scenic spots, including Zhenxibao, and is a great way to experience the natural beauty of the region.

Best Things to Do in Zhenxibao: Activities and Tours

Wen Chang Temple Blessing Ceremony

The Wen Chang Temple in Wufongci Scenic Area hosts a blessing ceremony on the 1st and 15th day of every lunar month. The ceremony is performed by Taoist priests and is believed to bring good luck and academic success to those who participate.

Taiping Mountain Sunrise Hike

The Taiping Mountain Sunrise Hike is an early morning hike that takes visitors to the summit of Taiping Mountain to watch the sunrise. The hike is challenging but offers spectacular views of the mountains and valleys bathed in the golden light of the rising sun.

Food and Drink Scene of Zhenxibao

Must-Try Dishes

Yilan Scallion Pancake

Yilan Scallion Pancake is a popular snack in Taiwan and is made with a crispy, flaky crust and filled with scallions and other savory ingredients. The pancakes are a great snack to have while exploring Zhenxibao Old Street.

Yilan Rice Noodle Soup

Yilan Rice Noodle Soup is a specialty dish of Yilan County and is made with a flavorful broth, tender rice noodles, and succulent meat. The dish is best enjoyed at one of the local restaurants in Zhenxibao.

Local Beverages

Mei Hua Wine

Mei Hua Wine is a popular wine made from fermented plums and is a favorite among locals and tourists alike. The wine has a sweet, fruity taste and is best enjoyed chilled.

Kavalan Whiskey

Kavalan Whiskey is a world-renowned brand of whiskey that is produced in Yilan County. The distillery offers guided tours that take visitors through the whiskey-making process and offer tastings of their award-winning whiskey.

Where to Stay in Zhenxibao: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

B&B Zi Qing Mu

B&B Zi Qing Mu is a cozy bed and breakfast located in the heart of Zhenxibao. The rooms are well-appointed and offer stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

Dong Hui Hotel

Dong Hui Hotel is a stylish boutique hotel that offers comfortable rooms, a restaurant, and a bar. The hotel is located close to several tourist attractions in Zhenxibao and is a great base for exploring the region.

What to Buy in Zhenxibao: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Pineapple Cake

Pineapple Cake is a popular souvenir in Taiwan and is made with sweet, tangy pineapple jam encased in a buttery pastry. The cakes are available at most souvenir shops in Zhenxibao.

Yilan Tea

Yilan Tea is a high-quality tea that is grown in the mountains of Yilan County. The tea is available in several varieties and is a great gift for tea lovers.

Local Culture and Customs of Zhenxibao

Taiwanese culture is deeply rooted in Confucianism and Taoism, and many of the traditional customs and practices are still widely observed in Zhenxibao. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions, including removing shoes before entering homes and temples and avoiding loud and disruptive behavior in public places.

Best Time to Visit Zhenxibao

The best time to visit Zhenxibao is during the spring or fall when the temperatures are mild, and the scenery is at its most beautiful. The winter months can be cold and wet, while the summer months can be hot and humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Zhenxibao

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Dress modestly when visiting temples and other religious sites
  • Try the local food and beverages
  • Be prepared for outdoor activities

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage natural or cultural sites
  • Disrespect the local culture
  • Be loud or disruptive in public places

Safety and Health

Safety in Zhenxibao

Zhenxibao is a generally safe place for visitors, and crime rates are low. However, visitors should take the usual precautions to protect themselves and their belongings, such as locking their accommodation doors and avoiding carrying large amounts of cash or valuables.

Health in Zhenxibao

Zhenxibao has a modern healthcare system, and visitors can easily access medical services if needed. However, visitors are advised to take precautions against mosquito-borne diseases and to stay hydrated during outdoor activities.

Getting to Zhenxibao: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Zhenxibao

The closest airport to Zhenxibao is Taipei Songshan Airport, which is approximately a 2-hour drive from Zhenxibao.

Bus Stations Near Zhenxibao

Yilan Bus Station is the closest bus station to Zhenxibao and is located approximately 30 minutes away by car. The bus station offers connections to other major cities in Taiwan.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel to Zhenxibao by car or taxi. However, visitors should be prepared for winding mountain roads and should exercise caution when driving.

How to Get Around in Zhenxibao

The best way to get around in Zhenxibao is on foot or by bike. The village is relatively small and easy to navigate, and a bike rental service is available.

Where to Go Next from Zhenxibao: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Yilan City

Yilan City is a bustling city located approximately an hour’s drive from Zhenxibao. The city is known for its night market, hot springs, and beaches.

Jiaoxi Township

Jiaoxi Township is a small township located approximately 45 minutes away from Zhenxibao and is known for its hot springs and stunning views.

Conclusion

Zhenxibao is a hidden gem in Northern Taiwan and offers visitors a chance to explore Taiwan’s natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. Whether you are a nature lover, foodie, or history buff, there is something for everyone to enjoy in Zhenxibao.
