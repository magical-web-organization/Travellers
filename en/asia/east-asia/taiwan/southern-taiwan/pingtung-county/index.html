Introduction to Pingtung County

Located in Southern Taiwan, Pingtung County is an incredible destination that offers a perfect blend of traditional and modern attractions. With numerous natural wonders, cultural sites, and outdoor activities, there’s something for everyone in this region. Whether you’re seeking an adventure-filled vacation or a peaceful escape from city life, Pingtung County is the ultimate destination for you.

List of Places to Visit in Pingtung County

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

From ancient temples to modern museums, Pingtung County has an abundance of cultural sites to explore. Here are some of the must-visit cultural attractions in the region:

  • Fo Guang Shan Buddha Museum
  • Chihkan Tower
  • Kaohsiung Museum of Fine Arts
  • Meinong Hakka Cultural Museum
  • National Museum of Marine Biology and Aquarium
  • World Snake King Education Farm

Natural Attractions of Pingtung County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The natural beauty of Pingtung County is breathtaking. The region is home to numerous national parks, scenic mountains, sandy beaches, and other natural wonders. Here are some of the best nature spots to explore:

  • Kenting National Park
  • Maolin National Scenic Area
  • Sandimen Scenic Area
  • Sheding Nature Park
  • Longpan Park
  • Dapeng Bay National Scenic Area

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pingtung County

Pingtung County is a paradise for hikers and bikers with its diverse terrain and stunning vistas. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced adventurer, here are some of the best trails to explore:

  • Chiku Mountain Trail
  • Dajian Mountain Trail
  • Maolin Valley Trail
  • Sheding Nature Park Trail
  • Jhihben Hot Springs Trail
  • Yushan National Park Trail

Best Things to Do in Pingtung County: Activities and Tours

Pingtung County has a plethora of activities and tours to offer for all types of travel enthusiasts. Here are some of the must-do activities and tours to experience:

  • Surfing and windsurfing lessons in Kenting
  • Hot air balloon ride over Taitung
  • Stargazing at the National Museum of Marine Biology and Aquarium
  • Visiting the local night markets for street food
  • Fishing at Dapeng Bay
  • Tea plantation tour in Maokong

Week-Long Itinerary for Pingtung County

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Pingtung County, here’s a sample itinerary to help you maximize your time in the region:

Day 1: Visit Fo Guang Shan Buddha Museum, enjoy hot springs at Shanlinxi Forest Recreation Area.

Day 2: Hike the Chiku Mountain Trail and explore Longpan Park.

Day 3: Tour the National Museum of Marine Biology and Aquarium, and visit the Sheding Nature Park trail.

Day 4: Visit the Meinong Hakka Cultural Museum and experience tea plantation tours in the Maokong area.

Day 5: Explore the Kenting National Park and Kenting Beach.

Day 6: Enjoy surfing and windsurfing lessons at Little Bay/Kenting Beach or fishing at Dapeng Bay.

Day 7: Relax on the beach and explore the local night markets for street food.

Food and Drink Scene of Pingtung County

No visit to Pingtung County is complete without experiencing its unique food and drink scene. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages to sample:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Caesar Park Hotel Kenting
  • Chateau de Chine Hotel Kaohsiung

What to Buy in Pingtung County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Pingtung County is home to various local products and souvenirs that make for excellent keepsakes. Here are some of the top items to buy on your trip to Pingtung County:

  • Hakka Lei Cha
  • Taiwanese Sun Cake
  • Honey and tea from Maokong
  • Fruit jams and preserves
  • Sun Moon Lake tea
  • Handcrafted pottery

Local Culture and Customs of Pingtung County

Pingtung County is home to a diverse range of cultures and customs. Visitors are encouraged to explore and embrace the local customs and traditions to get a better understanding of the region’s heritage. Some cultural aspects to keep in mind include:

  • Bowing and greetings
  • Removing shoes when entering temples or homes
  • Covering shoulders and knees when entering religious sites
  • Abiding by the rules of conservative environments

Best Time to Visit Pingtung County

The best time to visit Pingtung County is from November to February when the temperature ranges between 20 to 25-degree Celsius. These months experience less rainfall and offer perfect weather for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pingtung County

Do:

  • Try local food and drink
  • Respect the local customs
  • Dress accordingly for religious sites
  • Carry cash instead of relying solely on credit or debit cards

Don’t:

  • Litter or pollute the environment
  • Take pictures without permission
  • Disrespect religious or cultural norms
  • Hesitate to ask for help or advice when needed

Safety and Health

Safety in Pingtung County

Pingtung County is generally a safe place to visit. Visitors are advised to take precautions to avoid petty crime and natural hazards like strong waves, slippery roads and wildlife, etc. Here are some safety tips to keep in mind:

  • Be aware of your surroundings
  • Secure your belongings
  • Use designated crossings
  • Avoid deserted areas at night

Health in Pingtung County

The health care system in Pingtung County is of high standard and easily accessible. Visitors are not required to vaccinate before entering Taiwan, but it’s recommended to have travel insurance that covers medical expenses. Moreover, visitors must carry mosquito repellent and remain protected from the sun.

Getting to Pingtung County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Pingtung County is well connected to other parts of Taiwan, and several transportation options are available to travel to the region.

Airports Near Pingtung County

The nearest airport is Kaohsiung International Airport (KHH).

Bus Stations Near Pingtung County

The bus is a cheap and convenient way to travel to Pingtung County. Some of the major bus stations are:

  • Kaohsiung Bus Station
  • Pingtung Bus Station
  • Kenting Bus Station

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi to travel to Pingtung County.

How to Get Around in Pingtung County

Getting around in Pingtung County is easy and affordable. Public transportation is well developed in the region.

  • Buses and trains are the most common way to get around.
  • Taxis and ride-hailing services are also available.
  • Renting a scooter or bicycle is a popular and convenient option.

Where to Go Next from Pingtung County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Pingtung County is located in a prime location for travellers, with several cities and towns worth visiting nearby. Here are some of the top destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Kaohsiung City
  • Tainan City
  • Taitung County
  • Alishan National Scenic Area
  • Sun Moon Lake

Conclusion

Pingtung County is an ideal destination for travellers who seek a perfect blend of nature, culture, and adventure. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has inspired you to plan your trip to this region and explore its captivating attractions, vibrant culture, and delicious cuisine.
