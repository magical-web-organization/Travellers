Introduction to Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area

Located in Yilan County in Eastern Taiwan, Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area is a unique travel destination that offers an immersive experience of Taiwan’s local culture and agriculture. The area provides a glimpse into the lives of farmers in the region, offering visitors opportunities to participate in farming activities, try local food, and learn about traditional agricultural practices.

In this guide, we will explore the best things to see and do in Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area, where to stay, what to eat, and how to get around. So, let’s get started!

List of Places to Visit in Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

1. Yilan Traditional Art Center

Located in a beautifully restored historic building, the Yilan Traditional Art Center showcases the region’s traditional arts and crafts. The center has several exhibition halls, a theater for traditional performances, and a cafe.

2. Yilan Distillery Chia Chi Lan Wine Museum

This museum has an extensive collection of antique wine-making equipment and offers visitors a guided tour to learn about the distillation process of Taiwan’s famous Chia Chi Lan wine. At the end of the tour, you can taste the different flavors of Chia Chi Lan wine.

3. Luodong Forestry Culture Park

This park has a large outdoor area where visitors can learn about forestry, traditional forestry techniques, and the history of forestry in Taiwan. There are also indoor exhibitions, a souvenir shop, and a restaurant serving local cuisine.

Natural Attractions of Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

1. Kaisa Heritage Trail

The Kaisa Heritage Trail is a beautiful hiking trail that takes you through the mountains and forests of Yilan County. Along the way, you can see beautiful waterfalls, suspension bridges, and wildlife.

2. Dongshan River Water Park

Offering a range of outdoor activities, including swimming, rafting, and kayaking, Dongshan River Water Park is a must-visit attraction for adventure seekers. There are also several restaurants and cafes in the park.

3. Wulaokeng Scenic Area

This beautiful scenic area is known for its stunning views of the Yilan countryside, hiking trails, and an observation deck. Visitors can enjoy a range of outdoor activities, including hiking, bird watching, and photography.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area

1. Taiping Mountain Trail

The Taiping Mountain Trail is a 16-kilometer hiking trail that takes you through the beautiful Taiping Mountain. The trail is known for its stunning views of the countryside and the coastline.

2. Dongshan River Cycling Trail

The Dongshan River Cycling Trail is a scenic biking route that follows the winding Dongshan River. The trail is relatively flat and suitable for cyclists of all levels, making it an excellent option for families or beginners.

Best Things to Do in Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area: Activities and Tours

1. Rural Experience Tour

The Rural Experience Tour is a guided tour that takes you through the countryside, offering visitors a chance to experience a day in the life of a local farmer. Visitors can participate in activities such as harvesting rice, catching fish, and milling soybeans.

2. Foodie Tour

The Foodie Tour takes you on a culinary journey through Yilan County, exploring the region’s traditional cuisine. You can taste local delicacies such as “oyster omelet,” “stinky tofu,” and “taroballs.”

Week-Long Itinerary for Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area

Day 1: Arrival in Yilan

Take a leisurely stroll around Yilan, enjoy a bowl of hot Taiwanese beef noodles, and visit the beautiful Kavalan distillery.

Day 2: Yilan Traditional Art Center

Spend the day at the Yilan Traditional Art Center, learning about the region’s traditional arts and crafts. In the evening, catch a traditional performance at the theater.

Day 3: Kaisa Heritage Trail

Hike the beautiful Kaisa Heritage Trail and enjoy spectacular views of the countryside.

Day 4: Dongshan River Water Park

Spend the day at the Dongshan River Water Park, where you can enjoy water activities such as swimming and kayaking.

Day 5: Rural Experience Tour

Take part in a Rural Experience Tour and get a firsthand experience of the daily life of local farmers.

Day 6: Wulaokeng Scenic Area

Explore the beautiful Wulaokeng Scenic Area, enjoy a scenic walk or hike, and take in the beautiful countryside views.

Day 7: Departure

Depart Yilan and head to your next destination.

Food and Drink Scene of Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area

Must-Try Dishes

1. Oyster Omelet

A local delicacy, the oyster omelet is a dish made with fresh oysters and eggs. It is typically served with sweet chili sauce.

2. Stinky Tofu

Known for its pungent aroma, stinky tofu is a Taiwanese street food made from fermented tofu. It is usually served with chili sauce and pickled vegetables.

3. Taroballs

Taroballs are a dessert made from mashed taro root, sugar, and rice flour. The balls are usually filled with sweet red bean paste.

Local Beverages

1. Chia Chi Lan Wine

Chia Chi Lan Wine is a famous wine produced in Yilan County. It is made with locally grown glutinous rice and is known for its unique flavor.

2. Green Tea

Yilan County is known for its high-quality green tea, which is a major export of the region. The tea is grown in the cool, misty mountains of Yilan and has a sweet, mellow flavor.

Where to Stay in Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

1. Silks Place Yilan

Silks Place Yilan is a luxurious hotel located in the heart of Yilan City. The hotel has several restaurants offering local and international cuisine, a fitness center, and a spa.

2. Gui Hua Hotel

Located in the countryside, Gui Hua Hotel is a charming hostel that offers traditional Taiwanese accommodation. The hostel has beautiful gardens, a restaurant serving local cuisine, and a homely atmosphere.

What to Buy in Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

1. Local Tea

Yilan County is known for its high-quality green tea, which makes an excellent souvenir to take home. You can find a wide range of tea varieties at local tea shops and markets.

2. Soy Sauce

Yilan soy sauce is another popular souvenir; it is made from locally grown soybeans and is known for its rich umami flavor.

Local Culture and Customs of Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area

The people of Yilan are known for their warm hospitality and embracing culture. Visitors to Yilan should embrace the local customs, which include respecting elders and greeting people with a smile. It is also important to remove your shoes before entering a person’s home.

Best Time to Visit Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area

The best time to visit Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area is during the autumn months (September to November) when the weather is cooler, making it an ideal time for outdoor activities.

Getting to Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area

The nearest airport to Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area is Taipei Songshan Airport, which is located approximately 60 km away. From the airport, visitors can take a bus or taxi to Yilan City.

Bus Stations Near Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area

Yilan Bus Station is the main bus station in the area, with regular buses connecting the region with other parts of Taiwan.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a taxi or rent a car to travel around Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area.

How to Get Around in Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area

Public transport in Tian Song Pi Agricultural Leisure Area is reliable and convenient. Visitors can use buses or taxis to travel around the region. Alternatively, visitors can rent a car to explore the area at their own pace.
