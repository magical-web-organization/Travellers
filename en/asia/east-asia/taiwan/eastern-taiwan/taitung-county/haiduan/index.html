Introduction to Haiduan

Haiduan is a small town located in Taitung County on Taiwan’s east coast, just a few hours away from the hustle and bustle of Taipei. Once a place for whaling, Haiduan is now a tranquil town that has preserved its past while embracing modernity. Home to stunning natural landscapes, cultural hotspots, and delicious food, it’s a perfect vacation destination for those looking for an offbeat experience in Taiwan.

List of Places to Visit in Haiduan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Haiduan has an abundance of cultural hotspots where history buffs can absorb themselves in the centuries-old traditions of Taiwan. The following are some of the must-visit cultural attractions in Haiduan:

  • Banji Bridge - This iconic landmark is Taiwan’s first suspension bridge and offers scenic views of the town.
  • Chihsingtan Beach - A beautiful and tranquil beach located in Chishang, popular among tourists and locals alike.
  • Haiduan Whale Watching Trail - A beautiful hiking trail that offers stunning views of the coast and opportunities to spot whales during the right season.
  • Haiduan Township History Museum - This museum showcases the history of Haiduan, offering a glimpse into the town’s whaling past.
  • Guanshan Town Historical Houses - A series of well-preserved old houses in the Guanshan area, offering a glimpse into the traditional way of life in Taiwan.

Natural Attractions of Haiduan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

With its stunning natural scenery, Haiduan is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts. From hiking and biking to water sports, there’s something for everyone in Haiduan. Here are some of the top natural attractions in Haiduan:

  • Liyu Lake - This beautiful lake is surrounded by mountains and offers scenic views and recreational activities like boating and fishing.
  • Water Running Up - This natural phenomenon occurs because of the topography of the area.
  • Baxian Cave - A cave located in the Guanshan area of Haiduan, where you can see unique geological formations and explore a subterranean world.
  • Wuling Green Tunnel - A beautiful cycling route that passes through lush greenery, tunnels, and a suspension bridge.
  • Luye Highland - A scenic plateau where you can enjoy hot air ballooning and paragliding, among other leisure activities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Haiduan

Haiduan is a hiking and biking paradise, and several routes offer beautiful scenery and a chance to explore the rugged coastlines and lush forests of the area. The following are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Haiduan:

  • Haiduan Whale Watching Trail - A beautiful hiking trail that offers stunning views of the coast and opportunities to spot whales during the right season.
  • Wuling Green Tunnel Bike Path - A scenic cycling path that passes through lush greenery, tunnels, and a suspension bridge.
  • Liyu Lake Hiking Trail - A moderate hiking trail that loops around Liyu Lake, offering scenic views and different recreational activities like boating and fishing.
  • Baxian Cave Trail - A walking trail that takes you through the Guanshan area of Haiduan, where you can explore a subterranean world.

Best Things to Do in Haiduan: Activities and Tours

Haiduan offers a wide range of activities, including hiking, biking, whale watching, exploring temples and museums, and indulging in delicious Taiwanese cuisine. Here are some of the best things to do in Haiduan:

  • Explore the town’s history and culture by visiting the Haiduan Township History Museum and Guanshan Town Historical Houses.
  • Hike on the Haiduan Whale Watching Trail and immerse yourself in the stunning coastal views.
  • Go whale watching and spot these magnificent creatures during the right season.
  • Indulge in delicious Taiwanese cuisine, including the famous Taiwan bento box at a local eatery.
  • Visit the hot springs at Guanziling to relax and rejuvenate in natural hot springs.

Food and Drink Scene of Haiduan

Taiwan is renowned for its delicious food, and Haiduan is no exception. The following are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages of Haiduan:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Taiwan Bento Box - This delicacy consists of rice, vegetables, and meat, packed in a compartmentalized box.
  • Pig Blood Cake - A local specialty, this dish is made by steaming pig’s blood and rice to create a firm cake that is deep-fried and served with a sweet and spicy sauce.
  • Oyster Pancake - A famous Taiwanese snack made with a batter of sweet potato flour, tapioca flour, and rice flour stuffed with juicy oysters and garnished with a sweet and spicy sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Pearl Milk Tea - Taiwan’s most famous beverage, pearl milk tea or boba tea, has its origins in Taiwan.
  • Tie Guan Yin - A type of oolong tea, which is popular in Taiwan and has a floral aroma and a smooth taste.

Where to Stay in Haiduan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Haiduan has a range of accommodation options, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Some of the best places to stay in Haiduan are:

  • Formosan Naruwan Hotel & Resort Hengchun - A luxury hotel that offers stunning views of the sea and mountains.
  • Living Guesthouse - A budget-friendly hostel that offers clean and comfortable rooms at affordable prices.
  • Flora B&B - A cozy guesthouse in a serene location that offers home-cooked meals and comfortable rooms.

What to Buy in Haiduan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs to remind you of your trip to Haiduan or to take back to your loved ones, we’ve got you covered. The following are some of the best souvenirs and local products to buy in Haiduan:

  • Hand-Made Pottery – Haiduan is known for its traditional pottery. Get your hands on some beautifully crafted pottery as a souvenir.
  • Beigang Cha - A blend of black tea, red sugar, and milk is a local specialty that you can take back home and enjoy.
  • Hakka Handmade Soap – Take home some all-natural, handmade soap created by the Hakka community in Haiduan.

Local Culture and Customs of Haiduan

Like the rest of Taiwan, Haiduan has its unique culture and customs. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and here are some customs and traditions to keep in mind while visiting:

  • Respect the elders and greet them first if you meet them in public.
  • Keep your shoes outside the religious places, including temples and shrines.
  • Dress modestly while visiting temples or any cultural attractions.

Best Time to Visit Haiduan

The best time to visit Haiduan is from November to April, during the cooler weather season. The temperature during this period ranges from 19 degrees Celsius to 25 degrees Celsius. This time of year attracts the most significant number of tourists.

Do’s and Don’ts of Haiduan

Do:

  • Respect the locals and their traditions.
  • Try the local food and beverages.
  • Explore the natural landscapes and outdoor adventures.

Don’t:

  • Litter the surroundings
  • Speak loudly in public places

Safety and Health

Safety in Haiduan

Haiduan is a safe place for travelers, and the locals are friendly and helpful. Still, like any other tourist destination, one must remain vigilant and take standard safety precautions like being aware of your surroundings and belongings.

Health in Haiduan

Haiduan has excellent medical facilities, including hospitals and clinics, and travelers can buy over-the-counter medicines from local drug stores. Don’t forget to take essential medication with you if you need them.

Getting to Haiduan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Haiduan

The nearest airport to Haiduan is Taitung Airport, which is located approximately 50 kilometers away from Haiduan. From the airport, you can take a taxi or a bus to reach Haiduan.

Bus Stations Near Haiduan

Haiduan Bus Station is the main bus stop in the town and provides public transportation to other parts of Taiwan.

Other Options

Travelers can also hire a car or a scooter to explore Haiduan and the surrounding areas. However, one must have a valid driving license.

How to Get Around in Haiduan

Haiduan is a small town, and most of the attractions are within walking distance. However, renting a car or a scooter is a convenient option for those who want to explore the more remote areas and avoid public transportation.

Where to Go Next from Haiduan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Haiduan is an excellent base to explore nearby attractions in Taitung County. Some of the top destinations and excursions near Haiduan are:

  • Chishang Town - Famous for its conical-shaped rice paddies and scenic cycling routes.
  • Green Island - Located off the coast of Taitung, this beautiful island has stunning beaches and coral reefs.
  • Taimali Beach - A beautiful beach with a relaxed vibe and clear waters.

Conclusion

Haiduan has everything a traveler can ask for - culture, outdoor adventures, natural landscapes, delicious food, and warm hospitality. We hope this comprehensive travel guide helps you plan a perfect vacation to this hidden gem of Taiwan. With a unique blend of history and modern amenities, Haiduan is a town that will leave an indelible mark on your heart.
