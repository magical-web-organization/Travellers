Introduction to Taitung County

Taitung County is located in the eastern part of Taiwan, and it is known for its beautiful scenery, vibrant culture, and delicious food. The county is home to a wide variety of natural and manmade attractions, making it a popular destination for tourists who want to experience Taiwan’s unique culture and stunning landscapes. In this guide, we’ll cover everything you need to know about planning a trip to this beautiful part of Taiwan.

List of Places to Visit in Taitung County

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Taitung County has a rich cultural history that is reflected in its many museums and historical attractions. Some of the top cultural attractions in the area include:

  • National Museum of Prehistory
  • Beinan Cultural Park
  • Taitung Railway Art Village
  • Tiehua Music Village

Natural Attractions of Taitung County: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Nature lovers will be in heaven when visiting Taitung County, as there are many natural attractions to explore. Here are some of the must-see sites:

  • East Coast Scenic Area
  • Sanxiantai
  • The Luye Plateau
  • Jhihben National Forest Recreation Area

Hiking/Biking Routes of Taitung County

Taitung County’s natural beauty can be best experienced through hiking and biking. Here are the top hiking/biking routes in the area:

  • Dawu Green Bikeway
  • Liji Badlands Trail
  • Walami Trail

Best Things to Do in Taitung County: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are many other activities and tours to enjoy in Taitung County, such as:

  • Whale watching tour
  • River rafting
  • Hot air balloon ride
  • Stargazing

Week-Long Itinerary for Taitung County

For those who want to maximize their time in Taitung County, here is a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip:

Day Activity
1 Arrive in Taitung and visit Tiehua Music Village
2 Explore Taitung’s natural attractions, including Sanxiantai and Luye Plateau
3 Visit the National Museum of Prehistory and Beinan Cultural Park
4 Take a whale watching tour
5 Go river rafting
6 Explore the East Coast Scenic Area
7 Enjoy a relaxing day at a hot spring or stargaze at night

Food and Drink Scene of Taitung County

Must-Try Dishes

Taitung County is known for its traditional dishes, which are influenced by the region’s geography and cultural heritage. Some of the most popular dishes to try include:

  • Sticky rice with wild vegetables
  • Pig’s blood cake
  • Rice noodle soup with fish
  • Taitung-style sausage

Local Beverages

Taitung County is famous for its beer production, and there are many local breweries to visit, such as Longteng Brewery and Taitung Guanshan Brewery. In addition, the county is also known for its coffee and tea production, with many cafes and tea houses to visit.

Where to Stay in Taitung County: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are many options for accommodation in Taitung County, from luxurious hotels to budget-friendly guesthouses. Some of the best places to stay include:

  • Formosa Naruwan Hotel Taitung
  • Taitung Bali Suites Hotel
  • Zabu Guesthouse
  • Yoho Bike Inn

What to Buy in Taitung County: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

There are many unique souvenirs and local products to bring home from Taitung County, including:

  • Aboriginal handicrafts
  • Taitung rice
  • Dragon fruit
  • Hakka mochi

Local Culture and Customs of Taitung County

Taitung County is known for its vibrant culture, which is influenced by the region’s Aboriginal and Han Chinese heritage. Visitors can experience this culture through the many festivals and events held throughout the year, such as the International Balloon Festival and the Donghe Anniversary Festival.

Best Time to Visit Taitung County

The best time to visit Taitung County is during the spring and fall, when the weather is mild and the landscapes are beautiful. However, the area can be visited year-round, with each season offering its own unique experiences.

Do’s and Don’ts of Taitung County

Do:

  • Learn about the Aboriginal culture and traditions
  • Hike or bike through the region’s natural attractions
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the Aboriginal culture or cultural sites
  • Litter or damage the natural environment
  • Ignore safety instructions during tours and activities

Safety and Health

Safety in Taitung County

Taitung County is generally a safe destination for tourists, with low crime rates and friendly locals. However, visitors should still take standard safety precautions, such as staying aware of their surroundings and belongings.

Health in Taitung County

The healthcare system in Taitung County is well-developed, with many hospitals and clinics available. Visitors should take standard health precautions, such as staying hydrated and wearing sunscreen.

Getting to Taitung County: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Taitung County

The nearest airport is Taitung Airport, which has many domestic and regional flights. Visitors can also fly into Kaohsiung International Airport or Taipei Songshan Airport, and then take a train or bus to Taitung County.

Bus Stations Near Taitung County

Taitung County is served by many bus stations, with regular buses connecting the county to other parts of Taiwan.

Other Options

Visitors can also travel to Taitung County by train, or by renting a car and driving.

How to Get Around in Taitung County

The best way to get around Taitung County is by car or scooter, as public transportation can be limited. Visitors can rent a car or scooter at the airport or in town.

Where to Go Next from Taitung County: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Taitung County is located in a beautiful part of Taiwan, with many nearby destinations to explore. Some of the best places to visit include:

  • Orchid Island
  • Hualien County
  • Kenting National Park

Conclusion

With its natural beauty, rich culture, and delicious food, Taitung County is a must-visit destination in Taiwan. By using this comprehensive travel guide, visitors can plan their trip to Taitung County with ease and make the most of their time in this stunning part of the world.
