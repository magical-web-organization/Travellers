Introduction to Zhunan

Zhunan, located in Miaoli County, is a small yet charming town in Central Taiwan. Known for its stunning natural beauty, Zhunan boasts of scenic hiking trails, picturesque countryside views, and serene hot springs. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you on a tour of everything Zhunan has to offer - from its must-see attractions to its food and drink scene, and everything in between.

List of Places to Visit in Zhunan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Jioufen Presbyterian Church - This church is an iconic sight in Zhunan, known for its unique architecture and stained glass windows.
  2. Tongluo Tain Temple - This temple is a must-visit for its intricate artwork and religious significance.
  3. Zhu Sheng Niang Cultural Center - A museum that showcases local culture and history through various exhibits.

Natural Attractions of Zhunan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Fuxing Suspension Bridge - This suspension bridge offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside and is a popular spot for hiking.
  2. Taian Hot Springs - These natural hot springs are a great spot for relaxing and rejuvenating, surrounded by lush greenery and fresh mountain air.
  3. Shitoushan Park - This park offers scenic hiking trails with breathtaking views of Zhunan and the surrounding mountains.

Hiking/Biking Routes in Zhunan

  1. Zhunan Taiping Mountain Trail - This trail offers panoramic views of the Taichung Basin and is perfect for hiking enthusiasts.
  2. Neigou River Bike Trail - This bike trail offers stunning views of the river, cycling through quaint villages, and historic farmlands.
  3. Lianhuafeng Trail - This trail offers picturesque views of Lianhuafeng and is a great spot for novice hikers.

Best Things to Do in Zhunan: Activities and Tours

  1. Lifting paragliding - Soar over the stunning hills and countryside of Zhunan on a paragliding tour.
  2. Tea picking and tasting experience - Take a tea plantation tour and learn about local tea culture, including tea picking, processing, and tasting.
  3. Strawberry Picking - Visit a strawberry farm and pick your strawberries, perfect for kids and families!

Week-Long Itinerary for Zhunan

Day 1: Visit the Jioufen Presbyterian Church, Zhu Sheng Niang Cultural Center, and Fuxing Suspension Bridge. Day 2: Hike the Zhunan Taiping Mountain Trail and relax in Taian Hot Springs. Day 3: Explore Tongluo Tain Temple and nearby villages. Day 4: Take a bike tour along the Neigou River Bike Trail and have a picnic in the countryside. Day 5: Visit the Lianhuafeng and Shimenniupu Trails. Day 6: Enjoy paragliding over Zhunan hills and fields, and have a tea plantation tour. Day 7: Shop for souvenirs and local products, then end the day with a strawberry picking tour and tasting.

Food and Drink Scene of Zhunan

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Hakka meals - Traditional Hakka dishes like Stewed Pork with Preserved Vegetables ( 梅菜扣肉, Mèi cài kòu ròu) and Lei Cha (擂茶) are must-tries in Zhunan.
  2. Rice noodles - The Yuan-Yuan Shan-Shan Rice Noodles (源源山珊珊米粉) are made from hand-ground rice and is a popular delicacy in Zhunan.
  3. Tea-flavored dishes - Zhunan is famous for its tea culture, and several restaurants offer tea-flavored dishes like tea-smoked chicken and tea-infused fried rice.

Local Beverages

  1. High mountain tea - Famous for its unique flavor and aroma, Zhunan is home to high-quality high mountain tea, which should be on every visitor’s list to try.
  2. Traditional Hakka Beverages - Must try the locally made Roselle juice (洛神花冰茶, Luòshénhuā bīngchá), which is a slightly tangy and earthy drink with great cooling properties.

Where to Stay in Zhunan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Yundeng Landscape Hotel - This hotel offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and countryside and excellent service.
  2. Wego Resort - This peaceful resort provides a natural hot spring experience, situated amidst lush greenery.
  3. Rainbow Villa - This guesthouse offers modern amenities, comfortable rooms, and a breezy terrace with stunning views of the countryside.

What to Buy in Zhunan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Giga Flowers and Fruits - These lovely souvenirs are made from paper and adorned with real flowers and fruits, a perfect memento from Zhunan.
  2. High Mountain Tea - There are various packaged high mountain tea blends available for sale, making it a great choice for a souvenir/gift from Zhunan.
  3. Handmade Hakka Products - Zhunan is known for its Hakka culture, and their traditional local crafts offer an excellent window into it. You can purchase handmade Hakka embroidery, woodcraft or pottery.

Local Culture and Customs of Zhunan

The Hakka culture dominates in Zhunan, which can be seen in their traditional homes, lifestyle, and art forms. Tourists are advised to respect local customs and traditions, particularly in worship places.

Best Time to Visit Zhunan

The best time to visit Zhunan is during the autumn season (September to November) when temperatures are milder and the surrounding hills are awash with vibrant, warm colors. The town experiences heavy rainfall during the monsoon season (June to October).

Do’s and Don’ts of Zhunan

Do:

  1. Respect local culture and customs.
  2. Tip your guides and drivers.
  3. Learn basic Mandarin phrases like “Ni hao” (Hello) and “Xie xie” (Thank you).

Don’t:

  1. Litter in public places.
  2. Wear revealing clothing in religious places or temples.
  3. Make loud noises, particularly after dark.

Safety and Health

Safety in Zhunan

Zhunan is a safe town, with low crime rates. It is advised to store valuables like cash, passports, and ID in secure places, and avoid displaying it in public to prevent theft.

Health in Zhunan

The local authorities recommend vaccination against Hepatitis A and Typhoid before traveling. Visitors are advised to take appropriate safety precautions and necessary precautions while swimming in natural bodies of water.

Getting to Zhunan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Zhunan

Taoyuan International Airport, located 82 km away, is the nearest international airport. There are local bus services available that run between the airport and Zhunan town.

Bus Stations Near Zhunan

Zhunan Bus Station is located near the train station and offers regular bus services to other destinations in Taiwan. Frequent bus services are also available for Taipei, Taichung and nearby cities.

Other Options

Car rental services are available in Taoyuan International Airport and Taichung City, making it possible to get around Zhunan on your own. Alternatively, visitors can take the train from Taipei or Taichung to Zhunan Train Station.

How to Get Around in Zhunan

The Zhunan bus service operates regularly and is the most popular mode of transport in Zhunan. Local taxis are also available, and some accommodation providers offer bike rental services.

Where to Go Next from Zhunan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Taipei - A bustling city with a vibrant night market, a lively art scene, and several historical landmarks. It is easily reachable by train or bus from Zhunan.
  2. Taichung - This vibrant city is known for its street art, local markets, and cultural landmarks. It is an hour’s drive away from Zhunan.
  3. Sun Moon Lake - A stunning natural attraction with serene and scenic views. It’s located an hour’s drive east of Zhunan.

Conclusion

Zhunan offers a wide range of natural and cultural attractions to keep tourists entertained. From its scenic hiking routes to its lush hot springs and traditional Hakka delicacies, Zhunan is a perfect destination for a rejuvenating and relaxing getaway. Plan your trip to Zhunan with our comprehensive travel guide and soak in the natural beauty of this charming Taiwanese town.
