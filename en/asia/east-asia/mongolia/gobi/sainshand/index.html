Introduction to Sainshand

Sainshand is a city located in the Gobi desert region of Mongolia, which is known for its vast landscapes and unique culture. Sainshand is often overlooked by travelers who opt to stay in more popular cities like Ulaanbaatar or Erdenet, but those who take the time to explore Sainshand will find a rich history, friendly locals, and stunning natural wonders. This comprehensive guide will help you plan your trip to Sainshand and make the most out of your visit.

List of Places to Visit in Sainshand

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Khamar Monastery: One of the oldest and most important Buddhist monasteries in Mongolia. This stunning complex was built in 1820 and offers a unique glimpse into Mongolian Buddhist culture.

  • Museum of the Sainshand Aimag: This museum is dedicated to the history and culture of the Sainshand region. It houses a collection of artifacts and exhibits that showcase the area’s unique heritage.

  • Red Wall: A historic landmark in Sainshand which was built in the 19th century to protect the city from invaders. Visitors can climb to the top of the wall for impressive views of the surrounding landscape.

Natural Attractions of Sainshand: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Gobi Gurvansaikhan National Park: This park is located a short distance from Sainshand and is home to some of the most stunning natural landscapes in Mongolia. Visitors can hike, camp, and explore the park’s unique geological formations.

  • Yolyn Am: A narrow gorge in the Gurvansaikhan Mountains that is lined with ice even in the height of summer. This stunning natural wonder is a popular destination for hikers and photographers alike.

  • Flaming Cliffs: A unique geological formation located in the Gobi desert. The cliffs are famous for their striking red color and are home to dinosaur fossils.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sainshand

  • Sainshand to Gobi Gurvansaikhan National Park: This hiking route takes visitors through stunning desert landscapes, past ancient rock formations, and into the heart of the national park.

  • Bike tour of the Gobi desert: Experienced cyclists can take on an adventurous bike tour through the Gobi desert, which offers stunning views and a unique way to explore the region.

Best Things to Do in Sainshand: Activities and Tours

  • Camel trekking: Visitors can take a camel trek through the Gobi desert to experience the same mode of transportation that has been used by nomadic people for centuries.

  • Gobi cultural tour: A guided tour through the Gobi desert region that offers visitors a chance to learn about the area’s unique culture and traditions.

Food and Drink Scene of Sainshand

Must-Try Dishes

  • Khuushuur: Traditional fried meat dumplings that are popular in Mongolia.

  • Buuz: A steamed dumpling filled with meat and vegetables, similar to dumplings in other Asian cuisines.

Local Beverages

  • Airag: Fermented mare’s milk that is popular in Mongolia. It has a slightly sour taste and is often served with meals.

  • Milk tea: A warm and comforting drink made with black tea, milk, and a touch of salt.

Where to Stay in Sainshand: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Gobi Guesthouse: A comfortable and affordable guesthouse that offers a variety of rooms and services.

  • Gobi Oasis Hotel: This hotel offers modern amenities and comfortable rooms for a reasonable price.

What to Buy in Sainshand: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Cashmere products: Mongolia is famous for its high-quality cashmere products, which make great souvenirs or gifts.

  • Handmade crafts: Visitors can find a variety of handmade crafts like pottery, jewelry, and textiles at local markets.

Local Culture and Customs of Sainshand

Mongolia is a unique country with a rich culture and set of customs. Visitors to Sainshand should be prepared to respect local traditions and to learn about Mongolian culture during their stay.

Best Time to Visit Sainshand

The best time to visit Sainshand is during the summer months, from June to August. During this time, temperatures are mild and there is plenty of sunshine. However, visitors should be prepared for cooler temperatures in the evenings and in higher elevations.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sainshand

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.

  • Take the time to learn about Mongolian culture.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local customs or traditions.

  • Rush through your visit without taking time to appreciate the unique beauty of Sainshand.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sainshand

Sainshand is a safe city for travelers, but visitors should take common sense precautions to protect themselves and their belongings.

Health in Sainshand

Visitors to Sainshand should be up to date on routine vaccinations, and travelers should take care to prevent mosquito bites to avoid diseases like malaria and dengue fever.

Getting to Sainshand: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sainshand

  • Sainshand Airport: The closest airport to Sainshand, which offers domestic flights within Mongolia.

Bus Stations Near Sainshand

  • Sainshand Bus Station: This is the main bus station in Sainshand, which offers regular services to other cities in Mongolia.

Other Options

  • Private car or tour: Visitors can hire a private car or book a guided tour to explore the area around Sainshand at their own pace.

How to Get Around in Sainshand

The best way to get around Sainshand is by foot, as the city is small and easily accessible. Visitors can also hire taxis or rent bicycles to explore the city and surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Sainshand: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Ulaanbaatar: The capital of Mongolia, which is known for its lively atmosphere, cultural attractions, and modern amenities.

  • Erdenet: A city known for its copper mines, which is located in the northern region of Mongolia.

Conclusion

Sainshand is a unique and beautiful city that is well worth a visit for travelers who want to experience the natural beauty and cultural treasures of Mongolia. With this comprehensive guide, visitors can plan their trip to Sainshand with ease and confidence.
