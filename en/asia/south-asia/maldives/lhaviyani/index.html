Introduction to Lhaviyani

Located in the famous Maldives, Lhaviyani is a beautiful atoll known for its crystal-clear waters, powder-white sand beaches, and serene surroundings. This tropical paradise is perfect for relaxation or water activities such as scuba diving, snorkelling or swimming with the beautiful marine life. Adorned with beautiful coral reefs and astounding scenic landscapes, Lhaviyani is a must-visit destination for those looking for an escape in nature’s calmness.

List of Places to Visit in Lhaviyani

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Lhaviyani Atoll’s capital city, Naifaru Island has some great museums that showcase the local history and culture of Maldives. A few popular ones are:

    • Lhaviyani Atoll Museum
    • Islamic Center

  • Visit the beautiful Masjidhul Aisha Mosque which dominates the skyline of Naifaru with its golden domes and tall minaret.

Natural Attractions of Lhaviyani: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • A world-famous dive spot, the Kuredu Express, a must-visit spot for any diving lovers.
  • Take a scenic walk/hike on the beautiful beaches of Fushifaru, Kuredu and Kanuhuraa.
  • Visit the beautiful Kuredu Island sandbank for serene and breathtaking views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lhaviyani

Lhaviyani is a great place to explore by foot or bicycle with a plethora of scenic routes such as:

  • Hiking to the top of Naifaru Island with a wonderful view of the coastline.
  • Biking from Naifaru to nearby beaches is an excellent way to explore the beautiful landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Lhaviyani: Activities and Tours

  • Take a scuba diving or snorkelling class to explore the beautiful coral reefs and marine life.
  • Experience the marine life up close with serene yet thrilling activities such as swimming with manta rays, dolphin watching, and Turtle conservation programs.
  • Take a boat ride to explore the nearby islands of Hinnavaru, Filaidhoo or Maafilaafushi and learn about the local cultures.

Week-Long Itinerary for Lhaviyani

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrival and check-in to your hotel
Day 2 Take a day trip to Fushifaru Thila, a world-class diving spot
Day 3 Visit Kuredu Island and its sandbank
Day 4 Take a boat trip to Filaidhoo Island and its local markets
Day 5 Relax and enjoy water activities such as snorkelling and swimming in the beautiful turquoise waters
Day 6 Take a scenic bike ride to Hinnavaru Island and explore the local culture
Day 7 Explore Naifaru Island by foot and visit the local museums and historical sites

Food and Drink Scene of Lhaviyani

Must-Try Dishes

  • Garudhiya - A local fish soup made with fresh tuna
  • Bis keemiya - A traditional pastry filled with vegetables or meat
  • Mas huni - A Maldivian breakfast dish made with tuna and grated coconut

Local Beverages

  • Kurumba - a popular coconut milk beverage in Maldives
  • Raa - is a locally produced alcoholic toddy made from the sap of coconut palms

Where to Stay in Lhaviyani: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Lhaviyani has a range of accommodations from luxurious resorts to budget-friendly guesthouses. A few popular options are:

  • Fushifaru Maldives
  • Kuredu Resort & Spa
  • Kanuhura Maldives

What to Buy in Lhaviyani: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Local handcrafted souvenirs such as wooden boats, woven baskets, traditional mats and ornaments.
  • Traditional Maldivian clothing, such as sarongs and traditional Dhoni boats replicas.

Local Culture and Customs of Lhaviyani

  • Islam is the dominant religion in Lhaviyani and as a visitor, it’s important to respect their beliefs and customs.
  • Maldives is a conservative country, and visitors are expected to dress modestly when outside the resorts, especially when visiting mosques or other religious institutions.

Best Time to Visit Lhaviyani

The best time to visit Lhaviyani is between November and April, typically the driest and coolest months of the year, with more sunshine and less rain.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lhaviyani

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and traditions.
  • Wear modest clothing when outside the resorts.
  • Remember to carry reef-friendly sunscreen and avoid touching marine life.

Don’t:

  • Sunbathe topless or wear revealing clothing outside resorts.
  • Drink alcohol in public places or during Ramadan.
  • Disrespect the local beliefs and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lhaviyani

Lhaviyani is a safe destination with low crime rates, but it’s important to take necessary precautions to safeguard your belongings. Avoid venturing out alone late at night and be aware of your surroundings at all times.

Health in Lhaviyani

It’s important to take necessary precautions to avoid health hazards such as sunstroke, dehydration, and food poisoning. Remember to drink plenty of water and carry necessary medication with you, especially if you have any pre-existing medical condition.

Getting to Lhaviyani: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lhaviyani

The closest airport to Lhaviyani is Velana International Airport (MLE), which offers several daily flights from major international airports around the world. From there, you can take a domestic flight, speedboat or seaplane to Lhaviyani.

Bus Stations Near Lhaviyani

There are no bus stations available in Lhaviyani. The best way to get around is by boat or seaplane.

Other Options

Private speedboat or sea plane transfers can be arranged directly with the resorts or other tour agencies based on your preference.

How to Get Around in Lhaviyani

The best way to explore Lhaviyani is by speedboat, seaplane or bicycle. Many resorts and guesthouses offer rented bicycles for a nominal fee, which is a great way to explore the island at your own pace.

Where to Go Next from Lhaviyani: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Maafushi - A beautiful island with an up-and-coming tourism scene, famous for its pristine beaches and clear waters.
  • Male - Capital city of Maldives, with popular attractions such as the Grand Friday Mosque, the Maldives National Museum, and the Presidential Palace.

Conclusion

Lhaviyani is a beautiful destination, with some of the best beaches and marine life in the world. Whether you’re looking to relax on the beach, explore the local culture, or dive into the crystal-clear waters, Lhaviyani offers something for everyone. Plan your next vacation to Lhaviyani today and experience a tropical paradise in its purest form!
