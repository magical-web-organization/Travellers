Introduction to Bandipur

Bandipur is a picturesque town located in the Middle Hills of Nepal, surrounded by lush green hills and breathtaking mountain views. This town is known for its authentic Newari culture, traditional architecture, and adventure activities. Bandipur was once an important stopover point for traders traveling between Tibet and India, and it still retains its historical charm.

List of Places to Visit in Bandipur

Bandipur has something to offer for everyone. Here are some of the top attractions to visit in Bandipur:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Bandipur Museum: This museum showcases the traditional Newari lifestyle and the history of the town. The exhibits include artifacts, photographs, and documents that provide a glimpse into the past of Bandipur.

  • Tundikhel: This place was once used as a parade ground for soldiers during the Rana period. It now serves as a recreational area for locals and tourists.

  • Gorkha Fort: This is an ancient fort located in the nearby town of Gorkha. The fort was built in the 17th century and is surrounded by stunning views of the Himalayan range.

Natural Attractions of Bandipur: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Siddha Cave: This cave is the largest in Nepal and is located near Bandipur. You can explore the cave’s passages and see majestic rock formations.

  • Muktinath Temple: This temple is a sacred Hindu site located on top of a hill near Bandipur. From the temple, you can witness breathtaking views of the Himalayan range.

  • Khadga Devi Temple: This temple is located on the outskirts of Bandipur and is dedicated to the goddess Khadga Devi. You can enjoy a scenic hike to the temple and witness panoramic views of the surrounding area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bandipur

Bandipur is a perfect destination for adventure enthusiasts. Here are some of the top hiking/biking routes in Bandipur:

  • Bandipur- Sundarijal Hike: This is a two-day hike that takes you from Bandipur to Sundarijal, passing through beautiful hills and forests.

  • Bandipur- Nagarkot Bike Ride: This is a three-day bike ride that takes you from Bandipur to Nagarkot, passing through scenic routes and traditional villages.

Best Things to Do in Bandipur: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the top things to do in Bandipur:

  • Paragliding: You can enjoy an exhilarating paragliding experience over the stunning landscape of Bandipur.

  • Sunset Views: You can witness a spectacular sunset over the Himalayan range from the viewpoint of Bandipur.

  • Cultural Tours: You can take a walking tour of the town and explore the hidden alleys and traditional architecture of Bandipur.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bandipur

Day 1: Arrival in Bandipur and explore the town. Day 2: Hike to Khadga Devi Temple and enjoy the views. Day 3: Go paragliding and explore the Siddha Cave. Day 4: Visit the Bandipur Museum and Tundikhel. Day 5: Take a cultural tour and enjoy the local cuisine. Day 6: Bike ride to Nagarkot and enjoy the panoramic views. Day 7: Depart from Bandipur.

Food and Drink Scene of Bandipur

Bandipur offers a great selection of traditional Nepali and Newari cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Dal Bhat: This is a staple dish in Nepal and is made with rice, lentil soup, vegetables, and pickles.

  • Momo: This is a popular snack in Nepal and is made with steamed dumplings filled with meat or vegetables.

  • Bara: This is a traditional Newari snack made with lentils and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Chyang: This is a traditional fermented rice beer.

  • Lassi: This is a sweet yogurt-based drink.

Where to Stay in Bandipur: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bandipur offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets. Here are some recommendations:

  • The Old Inn: This is a beautifully restored heritage hotel located in the heart of Bandipur.

  • Hotel Magar Village: This is a budget-friendly hotel located on the outskirts of Bandipur.

  • Gaun Ghar Hotel: This is a luxurious hotel with stunning mountain views.

What to Buy in Bandipur: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Bandipur is known for its traditional handicrafts and textiles. Here are some items to consider buying:

  • Dhaka cloth: This is a traditional Nepali fabric made of cotton or silk.

  • Wooden masks: These are traditional masks used in local festivals and rituals.

  • Handmade paper: This paper is made by traditional methods and is used for writing or arts and crafts.

Local Culture and Customs of Bandipur

Bandipur has a rich Newari culture and is known for its festivals and rituals. Here are some customs to be aware of:

  • Namaskar: This is the traditional way of greeting in Nepal, where you join your hands in front of your chest and bow slightly.

  • Dress Code: In temples and religious sites, you should dress conservatively and cover your shoulders and legs.

  • Respect for Elders: In Nepali culture, respect for elders is important. When entering a home or meeting an elder, you should fold your hands in a gesture of respect.

Best Time to Visit Bandipur

The best time to visit Bandipur is from October to March when the weather is dry and clear. The monsoon season from June to September can make travel difficult due to landslides and roadblocks.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bandipur

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Learn some basic Nepali phrases.
  • Enjoy the scenic views and adventure activities.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local culture and customs.
  • Drink tap water.
  • Leave trash on the trails or wildlife areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bandipur

Bandipur is a safe destination for tourists, but you should take precautions like wearing a helmet during adventure activities and not traveling alone at night.

Health in Bandipur

You should drink filtered or bottled water and get vaccinated before traveling to Nepal. Altitude sickness can be a concern when trekking in the Himalayas.

Getting to Bandipur: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bandipur is located 143 km from Kathmandu and is accessible by road. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Bandipur

  • Tribhuvan International Airport: This is the main airport in Kathmandu, and is located about 143 km from Bandipur.

Bus Stations Near Bandipur

  • Kathmandu Bus Park: This is the main bus station in Kathmandu, and you can take a bus to Bandipur from here.

Other Options

You can also hire a private car or taxi from Kathmandu to Bandipur.

How to Get Around in Bandipur

The best way to get around Bandipur is by foot or bicycle. You can also hire a motorcycle or car to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Bandipur: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Pokhara: This is a popular tourist destination known for its lakes and mountains. It is located about 76 km from Bandipur.

  • Chitwan National Park: This is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and is known for its wildlife and jungle safaris. It is located about 120 km from Bandipur.

  • Manakamana Temple: This temple is located on top of a hill and is accessible by cable car. It is located about 34 km from Bandipur.

Conclusion

Bandipur is a charming town that offers a rich cultural experience and stunning natural beauty. With its range of activities, attractions, and delicious cuisine, Bandipur is a perfect destination for travelers looking to experience the best of Nepal.
