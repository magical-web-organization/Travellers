Introduction to Middle Hills

Located in the central region of Nepal, Middle Hills is a picturesque destination known for its stunning landscapes, rich culture, and historical significance. The region stretches from the Kathmandu Valley to the high Himalayas in the north, and is surrounded by mountain ranges and lush forests, creating an ideal environment for outdoor activities and adventure.

Local Culture and Customs of Middle Hills

  • Dress modestly and respect local customs and traditions
  • Remove shoes before entering religious places
  • Avoid showing public display of affection
  • Seek permission before taking photographs of people
  • Use only the right hand while eating

Best Time to Visit Middle Hills

The best time to visit Middle Hills is from September to November and from March to May. These months offer clear skies and moderate weather, making it easier to trek and explore the outdoors.

Do’s and Don’ts of Middle Hills

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Carry sufficient cash for transactions, as ATMs are not readily available in some areas.
  • Bargain while shopping as it is part of the local culture.
  • Drink plenty of water to avoid altitude sickness.
  • Take appropriate precautions for trekking and outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local culture and customs.
  • Drink tap water.
  • Litter in the mountains or the countryside.
  • Leave any valuables unattended.

Safety and Health

Safety in Middle Hills

  • Always carry identification and your hotel address with you.
  • Be aware of trekking routes and possible natural hazards.
  • Take precautions against theft and pickpocketing.
  • Avoid uncooked or unpeeled food and drink bottled or boiled water.

Health in Middle Hills

  • Consult your doctor and take appropriate vaccinations before traveling.
  • Carry first-aid kits and any necessary medications.
  • Be aware of altitude sickness and take appropriate precautions.
  • Wear appropriate clothing and use insect repellent to avoid insect bites.
