Introduction to Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa is a beautiful coastal town located in Southern Province, Sri Lanka. It is known for its sandy beaches, warm waters, and a laid-back vibe. This small town has become a popular destination for tourists due to its natural beauty, cultural sites, and adventure activities. Hikkaduwa is a haven for those seeking sun, sand, and sea.

Local Culture and Customs of Hikkaduwa

Sri Lanka is known for its rich cultural heritage, and Hikkaduwa is no exception. Respect for cultural values and beliefs is essential while visiting Sri Lanka, and adherence to dress codes at religious sites is necessary.

Best Time to Visit Hikkaduwa

The best time to visit Hikkaduwa is between November and April, as this is the dry season, and ideal for outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Hikkaduwa

Hikkaduwa is generally a safe place. However, it is advisable to be careful while swimming in the sea and using water sports equipment. Always check weather conditions and follow safety guidelines provided by the operators.

Health in Hikkaduwa

It is essential to get vaccinated before visiting Sri Lanka. Malaria and dengue fever can be a concern, so it’s advised to take necessary precautions. Drinking bottled water and avoiding street food can help avoid health issues.
