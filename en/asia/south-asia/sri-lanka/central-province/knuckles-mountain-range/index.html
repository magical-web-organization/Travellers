Introduction to Knuckles Mountain Range

Located in the Central Province of Sri Lanka, Knuckles Mountain Range is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that boasts of breathtaking scenery, diverse wildlife, and a rich cultural heritage. Also known as Dumbara Kanduvetiya, the mountain range gets its name from its resemblance to the knuckles of a clenched fist. This mountain range is a must-visit destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and history buffs who want to learn about Sri Lanka’s fascinating past.

List of Places to Visit in Knuckles Mountain Range

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Knuckles Conservation Centre

The Knuckles Conservation Centre is an excellent starting point for visitors who want to learn about the diverse flora and fauna that inhabit the Knuckles Mountain Range. The centre has an extensive collection of books, exhibits, and guided tours that provide insights into the rich biodiversity of the region.

Huluganga Trail

History buffs will love the Huluganga Trail, which follows the path of the ancient canal system built by King Parakramabahu in the 12th century. The trail takes visitors through lush forests, rice paddies, and past ancient ruins.

Natural Attractions of Knuckles Mountain Range: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Mini World’s End

For some of the most spectacular views of the mountain range, visitors should head to Mini World’s End. This stunning viewpoint is situated at an elevation of 6,234ft and offers panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

Deanston Mini World’s End

Another popular spot for panoramic views is Deanston Mini World’s End. Unlike its slightly more famous namesake, this viewpoint is relatively unknown, making it a great choice for visitors who want to enjoy the scenery in peace.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Knuckles Mountain Range

Dothalugala Trail

One of the most popular hiking trails in the Knuckles Mountain Range, the Dothalugala Trail, takes visitors through lush jungles, past babbling brooks, and up to the summit of Dothalugala Mountain.

Riverston Trail

The Riverston Trail is another popular hiking trail that offers stunning views of the Knuckles Mountain Range. Visitors can take a dip in the crystal clear pools at the top of the trail or visit Sera Ella, the picturesque waterfall located nearby.

Best Things to Do in Knuckles Mountain Range: Activities and Tours

Trekking and Hiking

With over 34 peaks above 3,000ft, the Knuckles Mountain Range is a hiker’s paradise. Visitors can choose from a range of guided or self-guided trekking and hiking tours that take them through some of the most scenic and off-the-beaten-path trails in the region.

Camping

Camping is a great way to experience the rugged beauty of the Knuckles Mountain Range. Visitors can choose from a range of campsites located in the heart of nature, where they can enjoy stunning views, stargazing, and roasting marshmallows around a campfire.

Food and Drink Scene of Knuckles Mountain Range

Must-Try Dishes

Knuckles Mountain Range is a paradise for foodies, offering a range of Sri Lankan and international cuisine. Some of the must-try dishes include:

  • Kottu Roti: a popular Sri Lankan dish made with chopped roti, vegetables, and meat or seafood.
  • Rice and Curry: a quintessential Sri Lankan meal comprising rice, curry, and an array of side dishes and condiments.
  • Samosas: a popular snack made with savory filling and fried or baked in pastry.

Local Beverages

Visitors should try the local tea, which is grown in the nearby Nuwara Eliya region and is famous for its rich flavor and aroma. Other popular drinks include:

  • King coconut water: a refreshing and hydrating drink made from the juice of young coconuts.
  • Toddy: a fermented alcoholic drink made from the sap of coconut trees.

Where to Stay in Knuckles Mountain Range: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are a variety of accommodation options for visitors to the Knuckles Mountain Range, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxury resorts. Some of the top choices include:

  • Knuckles Forest Lodge: a rustic eco-lodge located in the heart of the mountain range, offering comfortable accommodations and guided tours.
  • Sir John’s Bungalow: a historic bungalow located near the Knuckles Conservation Centre, offering spacious rooms and stunning views.
  • Misty Village: a budget-friendly hostel located in Matale, offering comfortable dorm rooms and private cabins.

What to Buy in Knuckles Mountain Range: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors should check out the local markets and bazaars for a range of artisanal products and souvenirs. Some of the top choices include:

  • Handwoven textiles, such as saris and scarves
  • Spices and herbs, such as cinnamon and cardamom
  • Tea and tea-related products, such as infusers and teapots

Local Culture and Customs of Knuckles Mountain Range

The people of the Knuckles Mountain Range have a rich culture and heritage that is reflected in their traditions and customs. Visitors should respect local customs and dress modestly when entering religious sites or attending cultural events.

Best Time to Visit Knuckles Mountain Range

The best time to visit Knuckles Mountain Range is from December to March when the weather is dry and comfortable. However, visitors should be prepared for sudden changes in weather, and pack accordingly.

Do’s and Don’ts of Knuckles Mountain Range

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Carry appropriate clothing and gear for outdoor activities.
  • Hire a guide or take a guided tour for safety and local insights.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the natural environment.
  • Shop at markets or bazaars without negotiating prices.
  • Visit cultural or religious sites in inappropriate clothing.

Safety and Health

Safety in Knuckles Mountain Range

Visitors should be prepared for sudden changes in weather and carry appropriate gear and clothing. Hiking and trekking tours should be undertaken with a guide, and visitors should be cautious of wildlife and steep cliffs.

Health in Knuckles Mountain Range

Visitors should seek medical advice before visiting the Knuckles Mountain Range and take appropriate vaccinations and medications. Tick bites are a common problem in the area, and visitors should take appropriate precautions.

Getting to Knuckles Mountain Range: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Knuckles Mountain Range

The nearest airport to Knuckles Mountain Range is Bandaranaike International Airport in Colombo, which is approximately 143km away.

Bus Stations Near Knuckles Mountain Range

Buses to Knuckles Mountain Range are available from major cities and towns in Sri Lanka, including Kandy and Colombo. Visitors can take the bus to the town of Matale and then hire a taxi or take a tuk-tuk to their accommodation.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a private car or take a taxi from Colombo or Kandy to Knuckles Mountain Range.

How to Get Around in Knuckles Mountain Range

The best way to get around in Knuckles Mountain Range is by hiring a taxi or tuk-tuk. Visitors can also take local buses between towns or go on guided tours.

Where to Go Next from Knuckles Mountain Range: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some of the nearby destinations and excursions from Knuckles Mountain Range include:

  • Kandy: a historic city known for its iconic Temple of the Tooth and colonial-era architecture.
  • Sigiriya: a UNESCO World Heritage Site famous for its ancient rock fortress and frescoes.
  • Nuwara Eliya: a scenic hill town known for its tea plantations and cool climate.

Conclusion

Knuckles Mountain Range is a unique destination that offers a combination of natural beauty, cultural landmarks, and outdoor adventures. Visitors should take the time to explore the region’s many hiking trails, enjoy the local cuisine and beverages, and learn about the rich cultural heritage of the people who call this place home.
