Introduction to Horton Plains

Horton Plains is one of the most beautiful and breathtaking national parks in Sri Lanka. It is situated in the Central Province of the country and is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and hikers. The park is home to various species of flora and fauna and offers panoramic views of the hills and valleys. It has also been declared a UNESCO World Heritage Site for its outstanding natural beauty and uniqueness.
