Introduction to Ella

Ella is a charming town located in the Uva Province of Sri Lanka. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and outdoor adventures. The town is surrounded by lush green hills, tea plantations, waterfalls, and scenic viewpoints. It is a perfect destination for those looking for a peaceful and relaxing getaway from the hustle and bustle of city life.

List of Places to Visit in Ella

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Ella Spice Garden: This is a must-visit place in Ella. It is a garden that showcases the diverse spices used in Sri Lankan cuisine. Visitors can learn about the history, cultivation, and use of spices in various dishes.
  • Little Adam’s Peak: This hilltop offers panoramic views of the valley and the surrounding hills. Visitors can hike up to the top to enjoy the scenic beauty.
  • Ravana Falls: This magnificent waterfall is located a few kilometers away from the town. It is named after the mythological character Ravana and is believed to be the place where he hid Princess Sita.
  • Nine Arch Bridge: This iconic railway bridge is located just outside the town. It is a beautiful spot to enjoy the scenic beauty of the hills and take some beautiful photographs.
  • Demodara Loop: This is a unique architectural structure where the train goes around in a loop. It is a fascinating sight to watch the train pass over itself.

Natural Attractions of Ella: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ella Rock: This is a popular hiking trail in Ella. It takes around 3-4 hours to reach the top, but the views from the peak are worth the effort.
  • Buduruwagala Temple: This is a historical temple located near Ella. It is known for its ancient rock carvings of Buddha and other deities.
  • Ella Gap: This is a stunning natural viewpoint that offers panoramic views of the valley and the surrounding hills.
  • Lipton’s Seat: This is another beautiful viewpoint located a few kilometers away from the town. It is named after Sir Thomas Lipton, who used to come here to enjoy the views of his tea plantations.
  • Dowa Temple: This is a Buddhist temple located in the middle of the jungle. Visitors can enjoy a peaceful walk in the serene surroundings and admire the beautiful carvings of Buddha.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ella

Ella is a paradise for hikers and bikers. The town is surrounded by hills and valleys, with plenty of trails and paths to explore. Some of the best hiking and biking trails in Ella are:

  • Ella Rock Hike
  • Little Adam’s Peak Hike
  • Demodara Loop Hike
  • Tea Estate Cycling Tour
  • Halpewatte Tea Factory Cycling Tour
  • Namunukula Mountain Bike Trail

Best Things to Do in Ella: Activities and Tours

  • Tea Plantation Tour: Visitors can take a guided tour of the tea plantations and learn about the process of tea-making.
  • Cooking Classes: Visitors can take cooking classes and learn to prepare traditional Sri Lankan dishes.
  • Ayurvedic Spa: There are several Ayurvedic spas in Ella that offer various treatments for relaxation and rejuvenation.
  • Village Tour: Visitors can take a tour of the nearby villages and learn about the local culture and lifestyle.
  • Yoga Classes: There are several yoga centers in Ella that offer classes for all levels of practitioners.

Food and Drink Scene of Ella

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kottu Roti: a dish made with chopped roti bread, vegetables, and meat or chicken.
  • Hoppers: bowl-shaped pancakes made from rice flour and coconut milk.
  • Fish Curry: a curry made with fish, coconut milk, and spices.
  • Woodapple Juice: a refreshing drink made from the pulp of the woodapple fruit.
  • Egg Hoppers: hoppers with an egg cracked into the middle.

Local Beverages

  • Ceylon Tea: Ella is known for its tea plantations, and Ceylon tea is a must-try local beverage.
  • Arrack: a Sri Lankan alcoholic drink made from the sap of coconut flowers.

Where to Stay in Ella: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ella has a wide range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Some of the best places to stay in Ella are:

  • 98 Acres Resort and Spa: a luxury resort with stunning views of the surrounding hills.
  • Mountain Heavens: a boutique-style hotel with spacious rooms and excellent food.
  • Ella Jungle Resort: a unique jungle resort with eco-friendly accommodations.
  • Ella Hostels: budget-friendly hostels with comfortable beds and friendly staff.

What to Buy in Ella: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Tea: Ella is known for its tea plantations, and there are various kinds of tea available for purchase.
  • Handicrafts: There are several shops in the town that sell handcrafted items such as wooden carvings, batiks, and pottery.
  • Spices: Visitors can buy various spices such as cinnamon, pepper, and cloves from the Ella Spice Garden.

Local Culture and Customs of Ella

Ella is a town with a rich cultural heritage. The locals are warm and friendly, and visitors can learn about the local customs and traditions by interacting with them. Visitors should also dress modestly while visiting religious sites and temples.

Best Time to Visit Ella

The best time to visit Ella is from December to March when the weather is dry and sunny. The temperatures are mild, making it perfect for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ella

Do:

  • Dress modestly while visiting religious sites and temples.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Respect the local culture and customs.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local culture and customs.
  • Litter or damage the natural surroundings.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ella

Ella is a safe town for tourists. However, visitors should take precautions such as keeping their valuables safe and avoiding isolated areas at night.

Health in Ella

Visitors should drink only bottled water and avoid consuming unhygienic street food.

Getting to Ella: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ella

The nearest airport to Ella is Bandaranaike International Airport in Colombo, which is around 200 km away.

Bus Stations Near Ella

The main bus station in Ella is located in the center of the town, and buses run regularly to and from other towns and cities in Sri Lanka.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a car or a taxi to reach Ella from other parts of Sri Lanka.

How to Get Around in Ella

The best way to explore Ella is on foot or by bicycle. Visitors can also hire a tuk-tuk or taxi to visit the nearby attractions.

Where to Go Next from Ella: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some of the best destinations near Ella are:

  • Yala National Park: a popular wildlife sanctuary known for its leopards and elephants.
  • Mirissa: a beautiful coastal town known for its beaches and water sports.
  • Kandy: a historical city known for its temples and cultural heritage.

Conclusion

Ella is a beautiful town with a lot to offer to visitors. From natural beauty to historical and cultural heritage, there is something for everyone in Ella. With a little bit of planning and research, visitors can have a memorable and enriching experience in this charming town.
