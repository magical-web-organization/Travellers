Introduction to Puttalam

Puttalam is a charming coastal town located in the North Western Province of Sri Lanka. Known for its idyllic beaches, bustling fishing industry, and rich cultural heritage, Puttalam is a popular destination for tourists from around the world.

List of Places to Visit in Puttalam

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Puttalam District Museum
  • Sri Ram Temple
  • St. Anne’s Church
  • Ariyakulam Mosque

Natural Attractions of Puttalam: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Kalpitiya Beach
  • Wilpattu National Park
  • Anawilundawa Wetland Sanctuary
  • Dutch Fort in Kalpitiya

Hiking/Biking Routes of Puttalam

  • Kalpitiya Peninsula
  • Puttalam Lagoon
  • Wilpattu National Park

Best Things to Do in Puttalam: Activities and Tours

  • Kitesurfing in Kalpitiya
  • Dolphin and whale watching tour in Kalpitiya
  • Boat trips in Puttalam Lagoon
  • Jeep safaris in Wilpattu National Park

Food and Drink Scene of Puttalam

Must-Try Dishes

  • Puttalam prawn curry
  • Crab curry
  • Fish ambul thiyal

Local Beverages

  • Fresh king coconut water
  • Toddy (palm wine)
  • Arrack (Sri Lankan coconut liquor)

Local Culture and Customs of Puttalam

The people of Puttalam hold their traditions and customs dear. It is important to dress modestly, remove your shoes before entering religious places, and show respect to elders.

Best Time to Visit Puttalam

The best time to visit Puttalam is from December to March, when the weather is dry and pleasant. This is also the peak season, so expect crowds and higher prices.

Do’s and Don’ts of Puttalam

Do:

  • Try the local seafood
  • Take a boat tour of Puttalam Lagoon
  • Dress modestly and show respect to cultural sites

Don’t:

  • Take photos without permission
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions
  • Litter on beaches and in natural areas

Safety and Health

Safety in Puttalam

Puttalam is generally safe, though some areas can be prone to petty crimes like theft. Make sure to keep an eye on your belongings and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Puttalam

It is recommended to consult with a healthcare professional before visiting Sri Lanka to ensure that all necessary vaccinations are up to date. It is also important to practice good hygiene, drink bottled or boiled water, and use mosquito repellent.

Getting to Puttalam: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Puttalam

  • Bandaranaike International Airport in Colombo

Bus Stations Near Puttalam

  • Puttalam Bus Station
  • Kalpitiya Bus Station

Other Options

  • Private car rental
  • Tuk-tuk (local three-wheeler transportation)

How to Get Around in Puttalam

The best way to get around in Puttalam is by tuk-tuk or hired car. Bikes and scooters are also available for rent.

Where to Go Next from Puttalam: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Mannar Island
  • Jaffna
  • Anuradhapura

Conclusion

Puttalam offers an array of experiences ranging from cultural to adventure to relaxation. It’s no wonder why Puttalam is gaining popularity among tourists. With its beautiful beaches, rich cultural heritage, and stunning natural landscapes, Puttalam is truly a destination not to be missed.
