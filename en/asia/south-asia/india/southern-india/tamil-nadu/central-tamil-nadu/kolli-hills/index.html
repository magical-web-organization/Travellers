Introduction to Kolli Hills

Tucked away in central Tamil Nadu lies the hidden gem of Kolli Hills. This quaint and peaceful hill station is a treasure trove of scenic views, natural wonders, and unparalleled adventure. With its unique blend of cultural heritage, tradition, and modernity, Kolli Hills offers something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Kolli Hills

Whether you’re looking for cultural attractions or outdoor adventures, Kolli Hills has plenty to offer. Here’s a list of some of the must-visit places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Arappaleeswarar Temple
  • Siddhar Caves
  • Botanical Garden
  • Masila Falls
  • Agaya Gangai Waterfalls
  • Selur Viewpoint

Food and Drink Scene of Kolli Hills

Kolli Hills is a food lover’s paradise. Here are some dishes and beverages you must try:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kambu (Pearl Millet) Koozh
  • Varutha Kozhambu (Spicy-Sour Stew)
  • Vazhai Poo (Banana Flower) Vadai

Local Beverages

  • Panam Kalkandam (Palm Candy Juice)
  • Muttai Kose (Egg Soda)
  • Nannari Sherbet (Indian Sarsaparilla)

Local Culture and Customs of Kolli Hills

Kolli Hills is a proud repository of indigenous culture and customs. Here are some aspects to keep in mind:

  • Preferably wear conservative attire when visiting temples or religious sites.
  • Pongal, Diwali, and Karthigai Deepam are important festivals in Kolli Hills.
  • The locals are known for their hospitality so be sure to engage in conversation and try to learn more about their unique customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Kolli Hills

The best time to visit Kolli Hills is during the winter months from October to March. The pleasant weather during this period is perfect for exploring the hills, indulging in outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kolli Hills

Do:

  • Carry a first aid kit while hiking or trekking.
  • Respect the local culture and customs of the place.
  • Wear comfortable shoes and clothes when going on nature walks or treks.

Don’t:

  • Litter or pollute the natural surroundings.
  • Wear hats or shoes inside temples or other religious places.
  • Take photographs in places where it is forbidden.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kolli Hills

Kolli Hills is a relatively safe place for travellers. However, it is important to take basic precautions such as:

  • Staying attentive to your surroundings
  • Keeping your valuables safe
  • Avoiding walking alone at night

Health in Kolli Hills

Kolli Hills has basic medical facilities. It is advisable to carry a first-aid kit while travelling to Kolli Hills. In case of any medical emergencies, head to the nearest hospital or clinic.
