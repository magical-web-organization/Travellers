Introduction to Tiruvarur

Located on the North Coast of Tamil Nadu, Tiruvarur is a small town in Southern India with cultural and historical significance. The town is famous for its rich cultural heritage and is considered one of the most important pilgrimages for Hindus. Tiruvarur is home to several temples and is also known for its stunning natural beauty. In this guide, we will explore the best places to visit in Tiruvarur and provide you with all the information you need to plan your perfect trip.

List of Places to Visit in Tiruvarur

Tiruvarur has many interesting places to visit. Here is a list of some of the most popular tourist attractions in Tiruvarur.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Thyagaraja Temple: This temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the most famous temples in Tiruvarur. The temple is known for its elaborate architecture and is also home to several festivals throughout the year.

  • Rajarajeswara Temple: This temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is one of the oldest temples in the region. The temple has stunning sculptures, and it’s an excellent spot to explore the cultural heritage.

  • Kothanda Ramar Temple: This is one of the most important temples in Tiruvarur, known for its vibrant architecture and intricate sculptures.

  • Government Museum: This museum houses various exhibits that portray the rich history of the town, with displays ranging from ancient artifacts, weaponry, and sculptures.

Natural Attractions of Tiruvarur: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Muthupet Lagoon: This is an estuarine lagoon and is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna. Visitors can take boat tours to explore the lagoon and spot various migratory birds, including the flamingos.

  • Vaduvoor Bird Sanctuary: This bird sanctuary is a popular spot for birdwatching, especially during the migratory season, when different species of birds, including pelicans, ducks, and storks, flock here.

  • Kothayar Dam: The dam is a popular spot for a picnic and offers stunning scenic views, a perfect stop for nature enthusiasts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tiruvarur

Tiruvaur is an excellent spot for adventure and offers several hiking and biking trails for enthusiasts. One of the popular routes is a bike ride along the scenic East Coast Road that provides breathtaking views of the Bay of Bengal.

Best Things to Do in Tiruvarur: Activities and Tours

  • Go on a Temple Tour: Tiruvarur is known for its magnificent temples; a tour can help you tap into the spiritual essence of the place.

  • Spotting Local Wildlife: The town is surrounded by well-manicured forests and wetlands, making it an excellent spot for bird watching and wildlife photography.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tiruvarur

Here is a week-long itinerary for exploring Tiruvarur:

  • Day 1-2: Explore the famous temples in the town, including Thyagaraja, Rajarajeswara, and Kothanda Ramar temples.

  • Day 3: Visit the Government Museum and learn about the rich history of the region.

  • Day 4: Take a boat tour at Muthupet Lagoon and spot different migratory birds, including flamingos.

  • Day 5: Visit the Vaduvoor Bird Sanctuary and observe various species of birds.

  • Day 6: Hike the East Coast Road and enjoy the scenic views of the Bay of Bengal.

Food and Drink Scene of Tiruvarur

Must-Try Dishes

Tiruvarur has a rich culinary heritage, and here are some dishes you must try while in the town:

  • Kozhukattai: This dish is a sweet dumpling made of jaggery and steamed rice flour, and is usually offered to deities during Indian festivals.

  • Poriyal: Poriyal is a typical South Indian dry vegetable dish that is mildly spiced and served with rice.

  • Vada: Vada is a popular South Indian snack made from lentils and deep-fried.

Local Beverages

Tiruvarur is known for its refreshing coconut water and has a unique take on the classic Indian drink lassi, which is blended with local fruits.

Where to Stay in Tiruvarur: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Tiruvarur offers several accommodations for all types of travelers, ranging from budget guesthouses to luxury hotels. Some of the best accommodations include:

  • Hotel Selvies: Located near the railway station, this hotel is perfect for budget travelers, and offers neat and tidy rooms.

  • Hotel Sadhabishegam: This hotel is located close to the city center and provides excellent amenities at an affordable price range.

  • Lakshmi Residency: This hotel is a popular choice for luxury travelers, known for its excellent service and lavish rooms.

Local Culture and Customs of Tiruvarur

Tiruvarur is among the most culturally rich and ancient towns in Tamil Nadu, and it’s crucial that you respect its traditions and customs, particularly around religious sites. Dress modestly while visiting religious sites and remove your shoes before entering.

Best Time to Visit Tiruvarur

The best time to visit Tiruvarur is between October and March, as the weather is pleasant, and visitors can comfortably explore the town’s outdoor attractions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tiruvarur

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions, particularly around religious sites.

  • Keep the town clean and use trash cans for litter.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing while visiting religious sites.

  • Litter the streets and the town’s attractions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tiruvarur

Tiruvarur is a safe town, but it’s always advisable to be vigilant, especially when carrying valuable items. Always keep your belongings close to you, and avoid traveling alone during the night.

Health in Tiruvarur

There are several medical clinics and hospitals in Tiruvarur, ensuring you get suitable medical attention in case of an emergency.

Getting to Tiruvarur: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tiruvarur

The nearest airports to Tiruvarur are the Trichy International Airport, located around 110 kilometers away, and Chennai International Airport, located around 250 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Tiruvarur

Tiruvarur has a well-connected bus station that connects several towns and cities around the region.

Other Options

Taxis and rental cars are readily available in Tiruvarur.

How to Get Around in Tiruvarur

Tiruvarur has several transportation options, including public buses and taxis. Renting a bike or a private car is also an excellent option.

Where to Go Next from Tiruvarur: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Tiruvarur provides easy access to several towns and cities in the region, including Thanjavur and Nagapattinam, both of which offer stunning relics of their past.

Conclusion

Tiruvarur is a magnificent town in Tamil Nadu that offers visitors an exciting mix of natural beauty and cultural heritage. The town is a fantastic spot for tourists to explore and experience the rich culture, enjoy picturesque views, and cherish the serene surroundings. With our comprehensive guide, you are well equipped to make the most of your visit to this charming town.
