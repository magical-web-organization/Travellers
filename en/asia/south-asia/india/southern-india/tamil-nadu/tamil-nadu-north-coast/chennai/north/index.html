Introduction to North, Chennai

North is a beautiful coastline in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, which is located in Southern India. It is a fascinating blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage, and it attracts travelers from all around the world.

The North Coast is famous for its sun-kissed beaches, picturesque landscapes, historical monuments, and temples. This tour guide will provide you with an overview of the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, local culture and customs, and much more to explore in this picturesque destination.

Local Culture and Customs of North, Chennai

North, Chennai, is steeped in tradition and customs. It is a culturally diverse place where people from different religions, castes, and communities coexist. Here are some of the local customs and traditions to be aware of.

  • Dress modestly when visiting temples and religious places.
  • Remove footwear outside the temple premises.
  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Seek permission before taking photographs of anyone.
  • Carry a scarf or shawl to cover your head while entering a place of worship.

Best Time to Visit North, Chennai

The best time to visit North, Chennai is from November to February, when the weather is pleasant and enjoyable. The temperature during this time ranges from 20 to 25 degrees Celsius, which makes it the perfect time for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of North, Chennai

Do:

  • Try local food and cuisine.

Don’t:
