Introduction to Arcot

Arcot is a historic city located in the northern region of Tamil Nadu, in Southern India. The city played a significant role in the struggles between the British and the French during the 18th and 19th centuries. It is also known for its ancient temples, palaces, and other cultural attractions. Arcot is an ideal destination for travelers seeking to explore the rich heritage and natural beauty of Tamil Nadu.

List of Places to Visit in Arcot

  • Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

    • Vellore Fort: Built during the 16th century, Vellore Fort is a significant historical landmark of the region. It is known for its intricate carvings and architecture.

    • Jalakandeswarar Temple: It is an ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is known for its intricate carvings and is one of the most popular religious sites in the city.

    • Kondeshwar Temple: This temple is dedicated to Lord Murugan and is located on the top of a hill. The temple provides a panoramic view of the surroundings.

    • Ratnagiri Murugan Temple: The temple is dedicated to Lord Murugan and is known for its beautiful architecture and serene atmosphere.

  • Natural Attractions of Arcot: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

    • Yelagiri Hills: Yelagiri is a picturesque hill station located near Arcot, known for its serene beauty and outdoor activities like trekking, paragliding, and camping.

    • Amirthi Zoological Park: It is a wildlife sanctuary located near Varadarajaperumal Temple in Vellore district. The park is home to various species of animals, birds, and plants.

    • Swamimalai Hills: The hills are known for their scenic beauty and are popular for trekking and camping.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Arcot

  • Yelagiri Hills Trek: The trek to Yelagiri hills is a moderate level trek and offers stunning views of the valley and the surrounding hills.

  • Swamimalai Hills Trek: The trek to Swamimalai hills is a challenging trek and is suitable for experienced trekkers.

  • Vellore Fort Trek: The trek to Vellore fort is a moderate level trek and offers a glimpse of the rich history and architecture of the region.

Best Things to Do in Arcot: Activities and Tours

  • Take a Heritage Walk: Take a walk through the ancient temples and palaces of the city and witness the rich history and culture of Tamil Nadu.

  • Take a Food Tour: Taste the local delicacies and street food of Arcot and experience the unique flavors of the region.

  • Take a Cultural Tour: Experience the local dance forms and music of Tamil Nadu and appreciate the rich cultural heritage of the state.

Food and Drink Scene of Arcot

  • Must-Try Dishes

    • Arcot Biriyani: It is a special biriyani from the region, made with meat, vegetables, and local spices like cinnamon, cloves, and aniseed.

    • Pal Payasam: It is a sweet dish made from rice, milk, and sugar, and is a popular dessert in the region.

    • Angannan: It is a spicy gravy made from tender mutton and is a popular non-vegetarian dish in the region.

  • Local Beverages

    • Filter Coffee: A strong South Indian coffee served with milk, is a popular beverage in the region.

    • Buttermilk: A refreshing drink made from yogurt and spices and is a popular summer beverage in the region.

Where to Stay in Arcot: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Narayana Bhavanam: A budget hotel located in the heart of the city, offering comfortable rooms and good service.

  • GRT Regency: A luxury hotel located near Vellore Fort, offering spacious rooms and excellent amenities.

  • Poppys Anukula Residency: A mid-range hotel located near Yelagiri Hills, offering comfortable rooms and good food.

What to Buy in Arcot: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Silk Sarees: The region is known for its silk sarees and is a perfect souvenir to take home.

  • Handicrafts: The region has a rich tradition of handicrafts, including woodwork, pottery, and handloom products.

Local Culture and Customs of Arcot

  • The people of Arcot are friendly and welcoming to visitors.

  • Tamil is the official language of the region, but English is widely spoken.

  • It is important to dress modestly when visiting religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Arcot

The best time to visit Arcot is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.

  • Try the local food and beverages.

  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local culture and customs.

  • Drink tap water.

  • Wear revealing clothing in public places.

Safety and Health

  • Safety in Arcot

    • Arcot is a safe place for tourists, but it is advisable to carry a map and follow the local guidelines when traveling in the region.

    • Keep valuables like wallets and cameras safe and avoid traveling alone at night.

  • Health in Arcot

    • Drink bottled water and avoid eating street food to prevent stomach infections and other health issues.

    • Carry a first-aid kit with essential medicines and consult a doctor in case of any medical emergency.

Getting to Arcot: Transportation Options and Logistics

  • Airports Near Arcot

    • Chennai International Airport: It is the nearest airport to Arcot, located about 125 kilometers away.

    • Bangalore International Airport: It is located about 185 kilometers away from Arcot.

  • Bus Stations Near Arcot

    • Vellore Bus Terminal: It is located about 25 kilometers away from Arcot.

  • Other Options

    • Taxis and private cabs are available for hire to reach Arcot from nearby cities and towns.

How to Get Around in Arcot

  • Local buses, autos, and taxis are available for transportation within the city.

  • Hiking and biking are the best options to explore the natural beauty of the region.

Where to Go Next from Arcot: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Vellore: 25 kilometers from Arcot, Vellore is an important historical and cultural center in Tamil Nadu, known for its ancient temples and palaces.

  • Tirupati: 110 kilometers from Arcot, Tirupati is a popular pilgrimage destination known for its ancient temples, scenic beauty, and cultural significance.

Conclusion

Arcot is an ideal destination for travelers seeking to explore the rich heritage and natural beauty of Tamil Nadu.
