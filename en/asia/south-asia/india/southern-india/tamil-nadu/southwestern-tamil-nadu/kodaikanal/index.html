Introduction to Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is a charming hill station located in the district of Dindigul in Tamil Nadu, India. Known as the “Princess of Hill Stations,” it is situated at an altitude of 2,133 meters above sea level in the Western Ghats. The town is blessed with a pleasant climate, lush green surroundings, and breathtaking views of the valleys, making it a popular tourist destination for travelers looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

Local Culture and Customs of Kodaikanal

The culture of Kodaikanal is a mix of Tamil and Western culture. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and their way of life is deeply rooted in traditions and values.

Best Time to Visit Kodaikanal

The best time to visit Kodaikanal is from September to May when the weather is pleasant and the temperature ranges from 20-30 degrees Celsius. June, July, and August are the monsoon months, making it less favorable for tourists.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kodaikanal

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Kodaikanal:

Do:

  • Dress appropriately when visiting religious sites, and cover your head.
  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Carry warm clothes during the winter months.

Don’t:

  • Litter around the town or tourist spots.
  • Disturb the natural habitat of the indigenous flora and fauna.
  • Travel alone at night.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is a safe town for tourists, but it’s always better to be cautious. Here are some safety tips to keep in mind:

  • Keep an eye on your belongings.
  • Do not accept food or drinks from strangers.
  • Avoid traveling alone at night.

Health in Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal has a high altitude and may cause altitude sickness to some people. Ensure to take necessary precautions before indulging in adventure sports. It’s also advisable to carry essential medications as there is no high-quality medical infrastructure in and around the town.

Getting to Kodaikanal: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here are some of the transportation options available to reach Kodaikanal:

Airports Near Kodaikanal

Madurai Airport is the nearest airport, located about 120 km from Kodaikanal.

Bus Stations Near Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal has regular and frequent bus services from all nearby cities and towns.

Other Options

One can also opt for taxi/cab options or self-drive well-maintained cars to reach Kodaikanal from the nearest cities.

How to Get Around in Kodaikanal

Kodaikanal is a small town, and most tourist attractions are within walking distance. However, one can hire a cab, rent a bike or cycle to explore the town at your convenience.
