Introduction to Topslip

Topslip is a pristine natural haven nestled amidst the Anamalai Hills in Western Tamil Nadu, Southern India. Its serene surroundings, dense forests, and exotic flora and fauna make it a hit among nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers. The region is part of the Indira Gandhi Wildlife Sanctuary, which is home to a rich variety of wildlife, including tigers, elephants, leopards, and many more.

List of Places to Visit in Topslip

Whether you want to explore the rich cultural heritage, admire scenic views, or indulge in outdoor adventures, Topslip has something for everyone. Here are some of the top attractions you must visit during your trip:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Indira Gandhi Wildlife Sanctuary and National Park: Spanning over 958 sq.km, the park is a tropical paradise with a range of unique landscapes, including grasslands, moist deciduous forests, Shola forests and evergreen forests. Visitors can spot rare species of plants, birds, and animals, while traversing through the sanctuary’s scenic hills, rivers, and waterfalls.
  • Anamalai Tiger Reserve: A popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts, the reserve boasts of being home to over 160 species of birds and 25 species of mammals.
  • Parambikulam Wildlife Sanctuary: Located 90 km from Topslip, this sanctuary is home to several endangered species such as lion-tailed macaques, hornbills, and sloth bears. Visitors can go on a jungle safari, go boating in its lake or choose the trekking in its natural trails.
  • Aliyar Dam: A popular picnic spot, located around 20 km from Coimbatore, offers breathtaking views of the dam, mountains, and verdant surroundings.

Natural Attractions of Topslip: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Trekking in Topslip: The region is a trekkers’ paradise, offering a range of diverse trekking trails, such as Perambikulam Tiger Reserve Trek, Grass Hills Trek, and Anaikunthi Shola Trek.
  • Anaimalai Hills: If you love the great outdoors, a visit to the Anamalai Hills should be on your list. Its winding roads, lush green forest reserves and tranquil environment make it ideal for camping, trekking and birdwatching.
  • Grass Hills: Offering some of the best scenic views in Topslip, you can enjoy a total of 55 varieties of orchids found in grass hills.
  • Ponmudi dam: Located about 34 km from Topslip, Ponmudi Dam is surrounded by lush greenery with an awe-inspiring view of the surrounding mountains. Visitors can also boat or fish in the dam.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Topslip

Topslip offers an excellent opportunity for adventure lovers to indulge in hiking and biking, exploring the beautiful countryside while working up a sweat. Here are some of the top routes:

  • Topslip to Grass Hill Trek: This trekking route covers a distance of 9 km, which can be completed in 3-4 hours. Along the way, you will encounter panoramic views of the Anamalai hills and the grass hills of Topslip.
  • Parambikulam Wildlife Sanctuary Trek: This trek covers a distance of 25 km and is best taken over two days. You can experience the rich flora and fauna of the place making this an exciting option for nature enthusiasts.

Best Things to Do in Topslip: Activities and Tours

Topslip is an ideal destination for anyone looking for a range of activities to do amidst nature. Here are some of the things you can do in Topslip:

  • Jungle Safari: Explore the wildlife sanctuary on a jungle safari, where you can spot various species of wildlife, including elephants, tigers, leopards, and deer.
  • Bird watching: With over 160 species of birds, the Anamalai hills are a bird watcher’s paradise, with several unique bird species found nowhere else.
  • Camp Under the Stars: Topslip is an excellent place for camping, allowing you to soak in the natural beauty and serenity of the place.

Food and Drink Scene of Topslip

Topslip is known for its delicious food, particularly its spicy vegetarian food delicacies. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages that you should try during your trip.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Vada Curry: A delicious South Indian dish made with chana dal and vegetables that is often served with dosa or idli.
  • Coconut Chutney: It is a popular side dish served with South Indian breakfast items like idli, dosa, or vada. The chutney is made with freshly grated coconut, green chilies, and other spices, giving it a unique flavour.
  • Mysore Pak: A sweet dish made with gram flour, ghee, and sugar, making it a popular dessert in South India.

Local Beverages

  • Masala Chai: A popular Indian tea brewed with aromatic spices like cardamom, cinnamon and black pepper, and tea leaves. It is often served with milk and sugar.
  • Lassi: One can enjoy a tall glass of sweet or savoury lassi, a yoghurt-based drink, often served in earthen pots.

Where to Stay in Topslip: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Topslip has several options for accommodation, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious resorts. Here are some top choices:

  • Tiger Valley Resort: A luxurious resort with cottages and tree houses, offering a panoramic view of the surrounding mountains and forests.
  • Parampara Resort and Spa: A large resort that has both rooms and cottages, it offers an excellent opportunity to indulge yourself in a spa treatment.
  • Forest Guest House: Located within the Anamalai Tiger Reserve forest, offering the perfect ambiance for nature lovers.

What to Buy in Topslip: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Topslip has a range of unique and interesting souvenirs that you can buy to bring home as a memento of your trip. Here are some of the best things to buy in Topslip:

  • Teakwood Products: Topslip is famous for its exquisite teakwood products such as bowls, trays, and various other items.
  • Organic food products: The town is known for organic farm produce such as honey, coffee, and fruits.
  • Handicrafts: You can also find exquisite handicrafts made by local tribes in the top shop here, of the best things to take back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Topslip

The inhabitants of Topslip belong to various tribes. Tribes like Malasar, Muduvar, and Kadar constitute the primary population. The people of Topslip have a unique culture, custom, and language.

Best Time to Visit Topslip

The best time to visit Topslip is between the months of October to March, as it offers ideal weather conditions to explore nature with comfortable temperatures and a refreshing atmosphere.

Do’s and Don’ts of Topslip

Here are some essential do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while exploring Topslip:

Do:

  • Carry insect repellents, sunscreen, hats, and comfortable shoes while going for a trek or outdoor activities.
  • Follow the rules and regulations of National Parks and Wildlife Sanctuaries and do not litter.
  • Respect local traditions and culture when interacting with locals.

Don’t:

  • Do not carry or use plastic bags in national parks.
  • Do not disturb animals or their natural habitats.
  • Do not carry any weapons or any banned items with you.

Safety and Health

Safety in Topslip

Though Topslip is a relatively safe place, it is always essential to take precautions while exploring nature. Here are some safety tips:

  • Carry sufficient drinking water and snacks while hiking.
  • Avoid venturing into unknown places or territories.
  • Follow instructions given by local authorities and guides while trekking or camping.

Health in Topslip

  • Ensure that you are vaccinated against tetanus and other diseases before embarking on any outdoor adventure.
  • Drink mineral water and avoid eating street food.
  • Always carry a first-aid kit and antiseptic cream while camping or trekking.

Getting to Topslip: Transportation Options and Logistics

Topslip is located approximately 550 km from Chennai and 220 km from Coimbatore. Here are some transportation options to reach Topslip:

Airports Near Topslip

  • Coimbatore International Airport, approximately 70 km from Topslip.

Bus Stations Near Topslip

  • Pollachi Bus Station, which is about 28 km away from Topslip.

Other Options

  • You can also hire a taxi or drive down to Topslip.

How to Get Around in Topslip

To explore Topslip, it is best to hire a local guide or cab. You can also rent a bike or bicycle to explore the town and its surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Topslip: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Topslip being one of the less-known hill station, has a lot to offer nearby. Some of the popular destinations near Topslip are:

  • Tiruppur Cluster of Flowers: A popular destination among flower lovers, located around 65 km from Topslip.
  • Sholayar Dam: A perfect place for a picnic, located around 65 km from Topslip.
  • Valparai: A small hill station located in the Anamalai Hills, approximately 40 km from Topslip.

Conclusion
