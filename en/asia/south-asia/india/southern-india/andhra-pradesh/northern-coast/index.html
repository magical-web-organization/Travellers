Introduction to Northern Coast

Located in Andhra Pradesh, Southern India, Northern Coast is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered. The pristine beaches, historic sites, and rich cultural heritage make it a perfect spot for travelers looking for an off-beat experience. Northern Coast offers a blend of adventure and relaxation, making it a destination favored by adventure enthusiasts, nature lovers, and beach-combers alike.

Week-Long Itinerary for Northern Coast

Day 1-2: Visakhapatnam - Visakhapatnam Port, Kailasagiri, Simhachalam Temple, and Yarada Beach.

Day 3-4: Araku Valley - Take a train ride to explore the beauty of Araku Valley, visit Borra Caves.

Day 5-6: Kambalakonda Wildlife Sanctuary - Go for a jungle safari, visit Kotilingeswara Temple, and explore Thotlakonda Buddhist Complex.

Day 7: Rushikonda Beach - Enjoy water sports and relax on the beach.

Local Culture and Customs of Northern Coast

The people of Andhra Pradesh are known for their hospitality and friendliness. Traditional culture is rooted deep in the locals’ lifestyle, with many festivals and events celebrated throughout the year. Respectful attire is expected, and it’s common to take off shoes when entering religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Northern Coast

The best time to visit Northern Coast is from November to February when the weather is mild and pleasant. The monsoons arrive in June and last until September, which can be challenging for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Northern Coast

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Take your shoes off while entering places of worship.
  • Carry sunscreen and insect repellent.
  • Try local delicacies.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothes in public places.
  • Litter or harm the natural beauty of the area.
  • Engage in any illegal activities.
  • Consume alcohol in public areas.

Safety and Health

Safety in Northern Coast

Northern Coast is a relatively safe place to travel, but it is still advisable to take precautions. Be wary of pickpockets in crowded areas and avoid wandering off into isolated places after dark.

Health in Northern Coast

It’s always best to consult with a doctor before traveling to Andhra Pradesh. Stay hydrated throughout the trip and avoid drinking tap water. Bring any necessary medication and first aid supplies along with you.

Other Options

How to Get Around in Northern Coast

The best way to get around Northern Coast is by hiring a car, taxi, or motorbike. Local buses also ply to most of the tourist spots.
