Introduction to Nandyal

Nandyal is a city located in the Kurnool district of Andhra Pradesh, India. It is situated in the Rayalaseema region and is surrounded by hills and forests. Nandyal has a rich history and is said to have been ruled by the Mauryan Empire, the Cholas, and the Vijayanagar Empire. It is also said to be the birthplace of the famous musician Annamacharya.

Best Things to Do in Nandyal: Activities and Tours

Nandyal offers a variety of activities and tours for tourists. Here are some of the best things to do in Nandyal:

  • Visit Mahanandi, Sri Jagajjanani, and Nandyal Fort
  • Go on a wildlife safari at Gundla Brahmeswaram Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Take a road trip to the nearby town of Srisailam
  • Explore the local markets and try out the local cuisine

Local Culture and Customs of Nandyal

The people of Nandyal are friendly and hospitable. The city is known for its unique culture and traditions. Here are some of the customs and traditions to keep in mind when visiting Nandyal:

  • The locals follow traditional dress codes;
  • The locals are deeply rooted in their tradition and rituals;
  • The language spoken is mainly Telugu, though other vernaculars are also allowed.

Best Time to Visit Nandyal

The best time to visit Nandyal is during the winters, from October to February. The weather during this time is pleasant, and the city is at its beautiful best.
