Introduction to Belum Caves

Belum Caves is a popular tourist destination located in the Rayalaseema region of Andhra Pradesh, in Southern India. It is among the largest and longest underground cave systems in India and boasts of rich geological and historical importance. The cave formation stretches for about 3.5 kilometers, and visitors can experience the ancient times up close with the stalactites, stalagmites, and pathways presenting an awe-inspiring view. Belum Caves remains one of the most sought-after travel destinations in India, and with good reason; the beauty and magic of the place are eerily unique.

List of Places to Visit in Belum Caves

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Archaeological Museum Ananthapuram

Belum Caves is not only popular for its magnificent caves, but it also is rich in cultural heritage. The Archaeological Museum Ananthapuram contains artifacts from prehistoric times, including pottery and stone carvings. Additionally, there are various paintings depicting the history of the Rayalaseema region. The museum is open to tourists and visitors all year round.

Natural Attractions of Belum Caves: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Patalaganga- The Underground Water Flow

Patalaganga is a subterranean river located deep within the Belum Caves. It is said to have flowing water throughout the year, despite the lack of rain. This underground river source is believed to be from the river Krishna, and its beauty and sound make it a must-see attraction in Belum Caves.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Belum Caves

Belum Caves- Kolimigundla-Halaharvi Circuit

This route is ideal for experienced and professional hikers and bikers who enjoy a rigorous outdoor challenge. Stretching for approximately 45 kilometers, the trail takes visitors through the surrounding hills and forests, including the famous Kolimigundla and Halaharvi ranges, presenting beautiful scenic views of the lush Ampakam Reserve Forest on the way.

Best Things to Do in Belum Caves: Activities and Tours

Caving Tours

The Belum Caves offer educational and informative guided tours, where visitors can learn about the geological and cultural history of the site. The cave offers three different tour options, namely the pre-historic tour, the adventurous tour, and the religious tour. The tours are informative and entertaining and give visitors a thrilling and unforgettable experience.

Food and Drink Scene of Belum Caves

Must-Try Dishes

Gongura Pachchadi

Gongura Pachchadi refers to a well-known and widely savored pickle from the Rayalaseema region. It is made from sorrel leaves mixed with Indian spices, and locals love it.

Local Beverages

Tamarind Juice

Tamarind juice is a popular drink throughout South India and is ubiquitous in Andhra Pradesh. The juice has an unforgettable sweet and tangy taste, and customers can expect to have a refreshing and enjoyable experience.

Where to Stay in Belum Caves: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Belum Caves offers different accommodation options for every budget, ranging from budget hotels to luxury resorts. Some of the popular hotels in the area include the Narayana Palace and the Haritha Hotel. These hotels offer a friendly and comfortable environment while staying true to class and authenticity.

What to Buy in Belum Caves: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Belum Caves offers a variety of souvenirs and local products, including handicrafts, paintings, and sculptures. These products provide an excellent reminder of the unique experiences one has had while visiting the site.

Local Culture and Customs of Belum Caves

The local people of Belum Caves are friendly and welcoming, and they embrace the culture and traditions of the Rayalaseema region. Visitors should be respectful and mindful of the local customs, including dressing modestly and asking for permission before taking photos of people.

Best Time to Visit Belum Caves

The best time to visit Belum Caves is from October to February, during the winter season when the weather is mild and cool. The summer season, which stretches from March to May, is extremely hot and uncomfortable for visitors.

Do’s and Don’ts of Belum Caves

Do:

  • Follow the instructions of your guide while taking a caving tour
  • Dress appropriately, as the caves might be damp and muddy

Don’t:

  • Litter the caves or the surrounding areas
  • Touch or disturb the cave formations, as they are fragile and sensitive

Safety and Health

Safety in Belum Caves

Visitors to Belum Caves should take precautions and maintain caution while exploring the site, especially when climbing or walking through narrow pathways.

Health in Belum Caves

It is advisable to wear appropriate footwear and protective clothing while taking a tour in Belum Caves. Visitors with respiratory problems should also avoid the caves due to the damp and moldy environment.

Getting to Belum Caves: Transportation Options and Logistics

Belum Caves is accessible by road and rail, with the nearest airport being in Tirupati. The site is approximately 320 kilometers from Hyderabad, 33 kilometers from the town of Tadipatri, and 110 kilometers from Anantapur.

Airports Near Belum Caves

  • Tirupati Airport: Approximately 259 kilometers from Belum Caves

Bus Stations Near Belum Caves

  • Tadipatri Bus Stand: Approximately 33 kilometers from Belum Caves

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a taxi or a private car from nearby cities such as Hyderabad and Bengaluru.

How to Get Around in Belum Caves

The best way to explore Belum Caves is on foot or by cycle. Visitors can also hire a car or taxi to reach other destinations in the area.

Where to Go Next from Belum Caves: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Belum Caves are located near several other popular destinations in Andhra Pradesh, including the famous Puttaparthi town and the exotic Hampi town. These places offer a unique blend of culture, history, and natural features, providing tourists with unforgettable experiences.

Conclusion

Belum Caves presents a must-visit destination for anyone interested in experiencing the unique geological and cultural history of India. The site presents various activities and attractions that cater to people of different interests and ages, making it a perfect destination for families, solo travelers, and friends. Visitors can explore the site’s prehistoric and religious sites, hike, bike or camp around the surrounding forests and hill ranges, and even take unforgettable photographs. All these experiences culminate in an unforgettable and memorable adventure at Belum Caves.
