Introduction to Vijayawada

Located in the southern coast of Andhra Pradesh in Southern India, Vijayawada is a bustling city with a rich cultural heritage. Often referred to as the “business capital of Andhra Pradesh,” the city is famous for its historic monuments, delicious food, and scenic natural beauty.

In this travel guide, we explore the must-visit attractions, best things to do, where to stay, and what to eat in Vijayawada.

List of Places to Visit in Vijayawada

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kondapalli Fort: One of the most significant historical sites in Vijayawada, Kondapalli Fort is located around 16 km from the city. Built-in the 14th century, the fort is a major tourist attraction and home to several temples and palaces.

  • Victoria Museum: Located in the heart of the city, Victoria Museum is known for its extensive collection of Buddhist artifacts and art from the 2nd century BC to the 18th century AD.

  • Mogalarajapuram Caves: Mogalarajapuram Caves is a testament to the city’s rich cultural history. These rock-cut temples date back to the 5th century and are popular for their intricate carvings and sculptures.

Natural Attractions of Vijayawada: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Bhavani Island: A perfect getaway from the hustle-bustle of the city, Bhavani Island is located in the middle of the Krishna River. The island offers several fun activities, including boating, swimming, and fishing.

  • Undavalli Caves: Located 6 km away from the city, Undavalli Caves are known for their unique rock-cut architecture and sculptures. These caves are believed to have been carved out of solid sandstone in the 7th century AD.

  • Prakasam Barrage: Prakasham Barrage is a popular spot for locals to relax and enjoy the scenic views of the Krishna River. Visitors can take a stroll on the barrage and watch the sunset over the river.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Vijayawada

  • Mangalagiri: Located 12 km from Vijayawada, Mangalagiri is the perfect place for adventure enthusiasts. The place is famous for its scenic beauty and trekking trails.

  • Kondaveedu Fort: Kondaveedu Fort is another popular hiking spot located around 20 km from the city. The fort is located at an altitude of 1,700 feet and offers breathtaking views of the city.

Best Things to Do in Vijayawada: Activities and Tours

  • Krishna River Cruise: Take a cruise on the Krishna River and enjoy the serene beauty of the river while taking in the city’s views.

  • Kondapalli Toy Making Workshop: Kondapalli toys have been famous for generations. Visitors can attend a workshop and learn how to make these traditional toys.

  • Vijayawada Food Tour: Take a food tour of Vijayawada and indulge in some of the most delicious traditional dishes like Pesarattu, Guntur Chicken, and Gongura Pickle.

Food and Drink Scene of Vijayawada

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pesarattu: Pesarattu is a delicious breakfast dish made with green gram and toppings like onions, ginger, and green chilies.

  • Guntur Chicken: Guntur Chicken is a flavorful, spicy chicken dish made with freshly ground spices from Guntur.

  • Gongura Pickle: Gongura Pickle is a tangy, spicy pickle made with Sorrel leaves and a variety of spices.

Local Beverages

  • Jonna Kudu: Jonna Kudu is a refreshing, traditional beverage made with fermented sorghum flour.

  • Badam Milk: Badam Milk is a popular beverage made with almonds, milk, and sugar.

Where to Stay in Vijayawada: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Novotel Vijayawada Varun: The Novotel Vijayawada Varun is a luxurious 5-star hotel with modern amenities and an ideal location.

  • The Gateway Hotel: The Gateway Hotel is another luxurious 5-star hotel located in the heart of the city, making it convenient for travelers.

  • Riverside Guesthouse Vijayawada: The Riverside Guesthouse is a budget-friendly option that offers comfortable rooms and a convenient location.

What to Buy in Vijayawada: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Kondapalli Toys: Kondapalli Toys has been a significant cultural heritage of Vijayawada for centuries. These toys are made using light-weight and eco-friendly materials.

  • Mangalagiri Cotton Sarees: Mangalagiri Cotton Sarees are world-renowned for their unique designs and are made using 100% cotton.

Local Culture and Customs of Vijayawada

Vijayawada is known for its rich cultural heritage, and locals are known for their welcoming and friendly nature. The city has celebrated traditional festivals like Dasara and Deepavali with great fervor.

Best Time to Visit Vijayawada

The best time to visit Vijayawada is from October to March when the weather is mild and pleasant, making it the perfect time to explore the city.

Do’s and Don’ts of Vijayawada

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.

  • Try the local cuisine.

  • Keep yourself hydrated.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs of strangers without their permission.

  • Litter anywhere in the city.

  • Wear revealing clothing in public places.

Safety and Health

Safety in Vijayawada

Vijayawada is a relatively safe city, and Tourists are advised to stay cautious of pickpockets while traveling.

Health in Vijayawada

Tourists are advised to drink only bottled water and carry necessary medication. It is also recommended to visit a doctor before traveling.

Getting to Vijayawada: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Vijayawada

Vijayawada International Airport is located around 13km from the city center, and several airlines operate regular flights to major destinations across India.

Bus Stations Near Vijayawada

Vijayawada bus station is a major transportation hub, and several state-run and private buses operate regular services to nearby cities in South India.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a taxi or a car to reach Vijayawada from nearby cities like Hyderabad and Chennai.

How to Get Around in Vijayawada

The best way to get around Vijayawada is by taking local buses or hiring a taxi. Alternatively, visitors can also explore the city on foot or by renting a bicycle.

Where to Go Next from Vijayawada: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Amaravati: Visit the historic Buddhist site and explore the beautiful village.

  • Kolleru Lake: Kolleru Lake is an internationally renowned bird sanctuary located 65 km from Vijayawada.

Conclusion

With its rich cultural heritage, delicious food, and scenic natural beauty, Vijayawada is truly a city worth exploring. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has helped you plan your trip better and made your stay in Vijayawada a memorable one.
