Introduction to Kollam

Kollam, a picturesque city located in Kerala, India, is known for its natural beauty, cultural significance, and historic charm. It’s a popular destination for travelers seeking to have a unique experience on their journey in India. From tranquil beaches to serene backwaters, Kollam has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Kollam

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kollam has a rich cultural history that is evident in its museums and historical places. Some of the most prominent ones are:

  • The Ashtamudi Lake
  • The Kollam Beach
  • The Thangassery Light House
  • The Palaruvi Waterfalls
  • The Shenduruney Wildlife Sanctuary
  • The Clock Tower
  • The Mahatma Gandhi Park
  • The Kollam Adventure Park

Natural Attractions of Kollam: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Kollam boasts of a unique natural landscape, which is perfect for outdoor adventures and scenic views. Some of the most popular natural attractions in Kollam are:

  • The Kollam backwaters
  • The Munroe Island
  • The Thevalakkara beach
  • The Mampara Peak
  • The Jatayu Rock
  • The Thenmala Eco-tourism Project
  • The Palaruvi Waterfalls

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kollam

Kollam is an outdoor lover’s paradise, with numerous hiking and biking trails. Some of the most popular ones are:

  • The Munroe Island hiking trail
  • The Jatayu Rock hiking trail
  • The Thenmala biking trail
  • The Palaruvi Waterfalls hiking trail

Best Things to Do in Kollam: Activities and Tours

Kollam offers plenty of activities and tours to suit every traveler’s interests. Some of the best things to do in Kollam are:

  • Take a backwater cruise on the Ashtamudi Lake
  • Enjoy a traditional Kerala Ayurvedic massage
  • Go birdwatching in the Shenduruney Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Visit the Kollam Adventure Park
  • Explore the Thenmala Eco-tourism Project
  • Visit the Palaruvi Waterfalls

Food and Drink Scene of Kollam

Must-Try Dishes

Kollam offers a range of traditional Kerala delicacies that are a must-try. Some of the most popular dishes in Kollam are:

  • Kappa and Meen Curry (tapioca and fish curry)
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry (steamed rice cakes and chickpea curry)
  • Appam and Ishtu (rice pancakes with vegetable stew)
  • Naadan Kozhi Curry (Kerala-style chicken curry)
  • Karimeen Pollichathu (spicy pearl spot fish fry)

Local Beverages

Kollam offers a range of refreshing drinks that are ideal for the hot and humid weather. Some of the popular beverages in Kollam are:

  • Toddy (a local alcoholic beverage made from palm tree sap)
  • Filter coffee
  • Masala tea
  • Lemon soda
  • Rose milk

Where to Stay in Kollam: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kollam offers a range of accommodation options to cater to the needs of every traveler. Some of the best places to stay in Kollam are:

  • Ramada by Wyndham Alleppey
  • The Raviz Resort and Spa, Ashtamudi
  • Quilon Beach Hotel and Convention Centre
  • The Lake House
  • Fragrant Nature Backwater Resort

What to Buy in Kollam: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kollam is famous for its unique handicrafts and textiles. Some of the best things to buy in Kollam are:

  • Handcrafted bamboo products
  • Handloom sarees and fabrics
  • Spices and condiments
  • Jewellery made of coconut shells
  • Handmade soap and candles

Local Culture and Customs of Kollam

Kollam has a rich cultural history that is reflected in its customs and traditions. Some of the most significant ones are:

  • Kathakali, a traditional dance form
  • Onam, the harvest festival of Kerala
  • Vishu, the New Year festival of Kerala
  • Snake boat races, held during the monsoon season

Best Time to Visit Kollam

The best time to visit Kollam is from November to February when the weather is pleasant, and the humidity is bearable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kollam

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Dress appropriately, especially when visiting religious places.
  • Try the local food and beverages.
  • Take a backwater cruise on Ashtamudi Lake.
  • Explore the natural attractions.

Don’t:

  • Engage in public displays of affection.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  • Litter or harm the environment.
  • Wear revealing clothing.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kollam

Kollam is a relatively safe destination for travelers, but it’s always advisable to take basic precautions like traveling in groups, avoiding deserted areas at night, and taking care of personal belongings.

Health in Kollam

Kollam has good medical facilities, but it’s always advisable to carry basic medicines and take precautions like drinking purified water and avoiding street food.

Getting to Kollam: Transportation Options and Logistics

Kollam is well-connected to other parts of India, and the nearest international airport is Cochin International Airport. Some of the best ways to reach Kollam are:

Airports Near Kollam

  • Cochin International Airport (120 km)
  • Trivandrum International Airport (75 km)

Bus Stations Near Kollam

  • Kollam Bus Station
  • KSRTC Bus Station

Other Options

  • Hire a taxi from nearby destinations like Trivandrum or Alleppey.
  • Hire a self-drive car from major cities like Mumbai, Bangalore, or Delhi.

How to Get Around in Kollam

Kollam offers a range of transportation options to get around the city, including:

  • Taxis and auto-rickshaws
  • Buses and local trains
  • Self-drive cars and bikes

Where to Go Next from Kollam: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Kollam offers plenty of exciting nearby destinations and excursions that are worth exploring. Some of the best places to visit near Kollam are:

  • The backwaters of Alleppey
  • The beaches of Kovalam
  • The hill stations of Munnar
  • The temples of Madurai
  • The historical forts of Fort Kochi

Conclusion

Kollam is a unique destination that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, cultural significance, and historic charm. Whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or scenic views, Kollam has something for everyone. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can plan your trip to Kollam and explore everything this beautiful city has to offer.
