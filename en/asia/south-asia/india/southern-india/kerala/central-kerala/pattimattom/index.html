Introduction to Pattimattom

Located in Central Kerala, Pattimattom is a beautiful village that offers plenty of options for travellers exploring Kerala. Known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful landscapes, and adventure sports opportunities, Pattimattom is a popular destination among travellers.

List of Places to Visit in Pattimattom

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Pattimattom has a rich history and culture that is reflected in its various museums and historical places. Here are some must-visit attractions:

  • St. Mary’s Forane Church: This 1500-year-old church is one of the oldest in India and a popular pilgrimage site for Christians.

  • Kottayil Kovilakam Palace: This beautiful palace, located in the heart of the village, is a fine example of Kerala architecture and houses a museum showcasing the history of the region.

  • Cherai Beach: This picturesque beach is a popular spot for swimming, sunbathing, and beach sports.

Natural Attractions of Pattimattom: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Pattimattom is blessed with natural beauty, and there are plenty of outdoor adventures and scenic views to explore. Some of the must-visit natural attractions include:

  • Bhoothathankettu Dam: Located on the Periyar river, this dam is surrounded by lush greenery and is a popular spot for picnics and boating.

  • Thattekad Bird Sanctuary: This sanctuary is home to a variety of birds, including the rare and endangered Indian Pitta.

  • Kalady: Known as the birthplace of Adi Shankara, the founder of Advaita Vedanta, this town is home to several ancient temples and is a popular pilgrimage site.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pattimattom

Pattimattom is surrounded by beautiful hills and forests, making it a popular destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Some of the best routes to explore include:

  • Periyar Tiger Reserve: This reserve is home to a variety of wildlife, including tigers, elephants, and leopards, and offers several hiking trails.

  • Chalakudy River: The river is a popular spot for kayaking and rafting.

Best Things to Do in Pattimattom: Activities and Tours

Pattimattom has plenty of activities and tours to offer travellers. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Ayurvedic treatments: Kerala is known for its Ayurvedic treatments, and Pattimattom has plenty of centres offering traditional Ayurvedic massages and treatments.

  • Cultural shows: Several hotels and resorts in Pattimattom organize cultural shows that showcase the local dance forms and music.

Week-Long Itinerary for Pattimattom

If you’re planning to spend a week in Pattimattom, here’s an itinerary that covers all the top attractions:

  • Day 1: Visit St. Mary’s Forane Church and Kottayil Kovilakam Palace
  • Day 2: Explore Bhoothathankettu Dam and Thattekad Bird Sanctuary
  • Day 3: Visit Kalady and explore its ancient temples
  • Day 4: Go on a hiking/biking trail in Periyar Tiger Reserve
  • Day 5: Enjoy water sports at Chalakudy River and visit Cherai Beach
  • Day 6: Get an Ayurvedic treatment and attend a cultural show in the evening
  • Day 7: Visit nearby destinations like Kochi and Thrissur

Food and Drink Scene of Pattimattom

Must-Try Dishes

Kerala is known for its delicious cuisine, and Pattimattom has its own unique dishes that you must try. Some of the must-try dishes include:

  • Appam and stew: Appam is a fluffy rice pancake that is usually eaten with a savoury coconut milk stew.

  • Puttu and kadala curry: Puttu is a steamed rice cake and is usually served with a spicy chickpea curry.

Local Beverages

  • Kerala toddy: This is a fermented drink made from coconut palm sap and is a popular local drink.

Where to Stay in Pattimattom: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Pattimattom has several options for travellers looking for a place to stay. Some of the best options include:

  • The Surya Resort: This resort offers comfortable rooms and is located near several top attractions.

  • The Elephant Court: This luxury resort is known for its excellent service and amenities.

What to Buy in Pattimattom: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Some of the best souvenirs and local products to buy from Pattimattom include:

  • Handmade crafts: Pattimattom is known for its skilled craftsmen who make beautiful handicrafts like pottery, weaving, and woodworking.

Local Culture and Customs of Pattimattom

Pattimattom has a rich and diverse culture that is reflected in its festivals, languages, and food. The locals are friendly and hospitable, and it is recommended that you respect their customs and traditions during your stay.

Best Time to Visit Pattimattom

The best time to visit Pattimattom is between October and March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pattimattom

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Try the local cuisine
  • Explore the natural beauty of the region

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local cultures and beliefs
  • Engage in illegal activities, like drug use or gambling

Safety and Health

Safety in Pattimattom

Pattimattom is a relatively safe destination for travellers. However, it is recommended that you take standard safety precautions, like avoiding travelling alone at night and keeping your valuables safe.

Health in Pattimattom

Travellers should be cautious about water and food hygiene. It is recommended that you drink bottled water and avoid eating street food.

Getting to Pattimattom: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Pattimattom

Cochin International Airport is the nearest airport to Pattimattom and is located approximately 20 km away.

Bus Stations Near Pattimattom

The nearest bus station to Pattimattom is in Kothamangalam, which is approximately 12 km away.

Other Options

Travellers can also choose to take a taxi or hire a car to reach Pattimattom.

How to Get Around in Pattimattom

Travellers can get around Pattimattom by taking taxis, auto-rickshaws, or hiring a bike or car.

Where to Go Next from Pattimattom: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Travellers can explore nearby destinations like Kochi, Thrissur, and Munnar, which offer plenty of attractions and activities.

Conclusion

Pattimattom is a beautiful destination that offers travellers a mix of culture, nature, and adventure. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can explore the best attractions, enjoy the delicious cuisine, and experience the local culture and traditions.
