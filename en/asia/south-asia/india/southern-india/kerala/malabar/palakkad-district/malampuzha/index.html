Malampuzha is a town located in the Palakkad District of the Malabar region in Kerala, in Southern India. This breathtaking town boasts of scenic views, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures. Here is your ultimate travel guide to Malampuzha.

Introduction to Malampuzha

Malampuzha is renowned for the Malampuzha Dam, one of the largest reservoirs in Kerala. This town is a popular tourist destination, thanks to its picturesque scenery and cultural attractions.

List of Places to Visit in Malampuzha

Here is a list of the top must-visit spots in Malampuzha:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Palakkad Fort
  • Kollengode Palace
  • Tipu Sultan Fort

Natural Attractions of Malampuzha: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Malampuzha Dam and Gardens
  • Nelliyampathy Hills
  • Silent Valley National Park

Hiking/Biking Routes of Malampuzha

Malampuzha is a great place for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some popular hiking and biking routes:

  • Malampuzha to Palakkad
  • Kozhikkode-Bavali-Malampuzha

Best Things to Do in Malampuzha: Activities and Tours

  • Boating in the Malampuzha Reservoir
  • Paragliding at the Nelliyampathy Hills
  • Visit the Malampuzha Rope Way

Week-Long Itinerary for Malampuzha

Here is a sample itinerary for a week-long stay in Malampuzha:

Day Activity
1 Visit Malampuzha Dam and Gardens, and Palakkad Fort
2 Trek to the Silent Valley National Park and enjoy a picnic
3 Visit the Kollengode Palace and Tipu Sultan Fort
4 Paraglide in the Nelliyampathy Hills, relax in the resort
5 Go on a long bike ride from Kozhikkode to Malampuzha
6 Visit the Malampuzha Hanging Bridge and the Dhoni Waterfalls
7 Do some shopping for souvenirs and relax in the hotel or resort

Local Culture and Customs of Malampuzha

When visiting Malampuzha, it’s important to acquaint oneself with the local customs and traditions. Respect for elders and religious practices are crucial parts of the culture.

Best Time to Visit Malampuzha

The best time to visit Malampuzha is between September and May, which is when the weather is pleasant and dry.

Do’s and Don’ts of Malampuzha

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Dress appropriately when visiting the cultural attractions

Don’t:

  • Litter the streets and public places
  • Take photographs in restricted areas or of local people without permission

Safety and Health

Safety in Malampuzha

Malampuzha is generally considered safe, but it’s important to be cautious when traveling on foot, especially at night. Avoid venturing into secluded areas.

Health in Malampuzha

At times, the weather can be hot and humid, so it’s important to stay hydrated at all times. Take precautions against insects, especially mosquitoes, and carry sunscreen.

How to Get Around in Malampuzha

Malampuzha has local transportation options, such as buses and taxis. Renting a car or bike is also an option.

Where to Go Next from Malampuzha: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Conclusion

Malampuzha has something for everyone, from cultural attractions to outdoor adventures. We hope this guide has helped you plan your trip to Malampuzha. Enjoy your stay!
