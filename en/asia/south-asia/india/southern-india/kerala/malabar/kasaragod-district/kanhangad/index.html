Introduction to Kanhangad

Kanhangad, a small town located in the Kasaragod district of Kerala, is the perfect destination for a tranquil getaway. This charming destination is nestled among the lush mountain ranges of the Western Ghats and is known for its rich cultural heritage, beautiful temples, and serene natural beauty. Exploring Kanhangad will give you a chance to experience the authentic culture and traditions of Southern India.

List of Places to Visit in Kanhangad

If you’re planning to visit Kanhangad, make sure you take the time to explore these must-see attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Anandashram: Established in 1939, this ashram is famous for its charitable work.
  2. Hosdurg Fort: Known for its incredible architecture, this fort was built in the 16th century and is now a popular tourist attraction.
  3. The Bekal Fort: The largest fort in Kerala, this fort is famous for its beautiful architecture and stunning views.

Natural Attractions of Kanhangad: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Jawahar Bal Bhavan Park: This park is a great place to relax and take in the beautiful scenery.
  2. Bekal Hole Aqua Park: An ideal destination for adventure lovers who want to explore the beautiful waterfalls and indulge in thrilling water sports.
  3. Kappil Beach: A beautiful beach with calm and clear waters, it is perfect for swimming and sunbathing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kanhangad

For adventure enthusiasts, Kanhangad offers some incredible trails for hiking and biking. The following are some of the most popular routes:

  1. Maipady-Jalapadakettu Trail: This trail runs through the beautiful Western Ghats and is perfect for avid hikers.
  2. Chandragiri Fort Trail: Known as one of the most stunning trails in Kerala, this hike is around 3 km long and offers stunning views.

Best Things to Do in Kanhangad: Activities and Tours

Whether you’re an adventure lover or a culture enthusiast, Kanhangad has plenty of activities and tours to offer. Here are some of the best things you can do in Kanhangad:

  1. Ayurvedic Treatment: Experience the magic of Ayurveda by indulging in the town’s traditional Ayurvedic treatments.
  2. Traditional Dance Performance: Immerse yourself in the vibrant culture of Kannur by watching a traditional dance performance.
  3. Temple Tours: Explore the beautiful temples of Kanhangad and learn about the fascinating history behind them.

Week-Long Itinerary for Kanhangad

If you’re planning a week-long stay in Kanhangad, consider the following itinerary:

Day 1: Check-in at your hotel and relax.

Day 2: Visit the Bekal Fort and Jawahar Bal Bhavan Park.

Day 3: Explore the scenic Kappil Beach and enjoy water sports.

Day 4: Take a hike or bike along the Maipady-Jalapadakettu trail.

Day 5: Visit the Anandashram and learn about their charitable work.

Day 6: Take a temple tour and watch a traditional dance performance.

Day 7: Relax at your hotel or take a day trip to nearby destinations.

Food and Drink Scene of Kanhangad

Kanhangad is known for its delicious and healthy cuisine that features plenty of fresh seafood, coconut, and spices. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Kanji: A type of porridge made from rice or barley, this dish is famous for its nutritional value.
  2. Kozhi Curry: A spicy chicken curry, a must-try for all food lovers.
  3. Thalassery Biriyani: A delicious variation of the traditional Biriyani, this dish is a favourite of local foodies.

Local Beverages

  1. Toddy: A fermented drink made from coconut palm sap, the perfect way to chill on a hot day.
  2. Sambaram: A refreshing drink made from yogurt, coconut water, and spices, perfect to cool you off in the summers.

Where to Stay in Kanhangad: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kanhangad offers plenty of accommodation options that cater to all budgets. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  1. Hosdurg Palace: A heritage property that provides guests with a royal experience.
  2. Aryabavan Deluxe Lodge: A budget-friendly option that’s clean and comfortable.
  3. Nalanda Tourist Home: A guesthouse that provides guests with a homely experience.

What to Buy in Kanhangad: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking to take a piece of Kanhangad back home with you, here are some souvenirs and local products you should consider buying:

  1. Kasavu Sarees: A traditional handwoven saree that is famous in this region.
  2. Coir Products: Kanhangad is famous for its coir products like mats, rugs, and carpets.
  3. Kerala Spices: Invest in some of the finest and freshest spices from Kerala.

Local Culture and Customs of Kanhangad

Kanhangad is a town that is deeply rooted in its cultural and traditional values. Here are some customs and traditions you should keep in mind whilst visiting:

  1. Respect for Elders: It is customary to show respect to elders and people in positions of authority.
  2. Temple Etiquette: When visiting temples, make sure you dress modestly and follow the customs and rituals conducted within.
  3. Conservative Attire: Avoid dressing provocatively, as it may be deemed inappropriate in some areas.

Best Time to Visit Kanhangad

The best time to visit Kanhangad is during the winter months from November to February when the climate is mild and comfortable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kanhangad

Do:

  1. Respect the local culture and customs.
  2. Try the local cuisine.
  3. Keep your surroundings clean and tidy.

Don’t:

  1. Disrespect local dress codes.
  2. Litter or pollute the environment.
  3. Damage or deface cultural or historical sites.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kanhangad

Kanhangad is a relatively safe destination, and tourists rarely encounter any problems. However, it’s always a good idea to stay vigilant and take necessary precautions when travelling.

Health in Kanhangad

Kanhangad is a relatively healthy destination. However, It’s essential to carry mosquito repellent and follow basic hygiene practices to avoid any illnesses.

Getting to Kanhangad: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Kanhangad

The closest airport to Kanhangad is the Calicut International Airport, which is around 130 km away.

Bus Stations Near Kanhangad

Kanhangad has a well-connected bus station that offers frequent buses to major cities in Kerala and neighboring states.

Other Options

Taxis, auto-rickshaws, and motorbikes are also readily available in Kanhangad.

How to Get Around in Kanhangad

Kanhangad is a small town that can be easily explored on foot. However, if you are looking to travel further, you can take advantage of the local transport options, including taxis and auto-rickshaws.

Where to Go Next from Kanhangad: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Kanhangad is situated in a prime location, providing easy access to some other popular destinations in Southern India. Here are some nearby destinations:

  1. Bekal: Just 13 km away, Bekal is famous for its stunning fort and scenic beaches.

  2. Kasargod: Just 28 km away, Kasargod is another popular destination that is famous for its cultural heritage and unique cuisine.

Conclusion

Kanhangad is a hidden gem that is waiting to be explored. This destination offers something for everyone, be it adventure enthusiasts or culture lovers. So, pack your bags, book your tickets, and get ready to experience the authentic culture and traditions of Southern India.
