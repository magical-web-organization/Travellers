Introduction to Farook College Township

Farook College Township is a small town in the Kozhikode District of Kerala. The town is named after Farook College, a prestigious educational institution located in the heart of the town. Farook College Township is known for its rich cultural heritage and scenic beauty. It is a popular destination for travelers looking to experience the authentic culture of Southern India.

List of Places to Visit in Farook College Township

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Farook College is the most prominent cultural attraction in the town. The college is one of the oldest educational institutions in Kerala and has been producing scholars for over 70 years. The Farook College Museum is a must-visit attraction for history buffs. The museum showcases the history of the college through various artifacts and photographs.

The Koduvally Heritage Centre is another cultural attraction in the town. The centre is dedicated to preserving the traditional lifestyle, culture, and history of the Koduvally region. The centre features exhibits that showcase the history of the region, including old photographs, artifacts, and textiles.

Natural Attractions of Farook College Township: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The town is blessed with natural beauty and is surrounded by lush green forests and coconut palms. The Kakkayam Dam is a popular spot for picnicking and trekking. The dam is located on the Kakkayam River and offers a stunning view of the surrounding hills and forests.

The Vellari Mala is another natural attraction in the town. The mountain is a popular trekking spot and offers a breathtaking view of the surrounding hills and valleys. The trek to the top of the mountain is a challenging one, but the view from the top is worth the effort.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Farook College Township

Farook College Township is a perfect place for outdoor enthusiasts as there are several biking and hiking routes available in the town. The Kakkayam-Cheruvadi road is one of the most popular biking routes in the town. The route is situated amidst lush hills and serene forests, providing bikers with breath-taking scenery throughout the journey.

For hikers, the Vellari Mala trek is a challenging and rewarding experience. The trek takes you through dense forests and rocky terrain and provides hikers with panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and hills.

Best Things to Do in Farook College Township: Activities and Tours

Farook College Township has a wide range of activities and tours available for visitors. The town is famous for its Ayurvedic treatments and spas. Visitors can indulge in therapeutic treatments that include massages, yoga, and meditation to improve their physical and mental wellbeing.

Kalaripayattu, a martial art form, is another cultural activity that visitors can experience in Farook College Township. This ancient form of martial arts originated in Kerala and is still practiced by the locals. The town has several training centers where visitors can learn the art form or watch a live performance.

Week-Long Itinerary for Farook College Township

Day 1: Visit Farook College and the Farook College Museum

Day 2: Trek the Vellari Mala Mountain

Day 3: Go biking or hiking in the Kakkayam-Cheruvadi road

Day 4: Take a day trip to the Kakkayam Dam

Day 5: Experience an Ayurvedic treatment or visit a spa

Day 6: Go shopping for souvenirs at the local market

Food and Drink Scene of Farook College Township

Must-Try Dishes

Farook College Township boasts a rich food culture, with numerous delicacies on offer. The famous dishes of the region include Kappa and Meen Curry (Tapioca and Fish Curry), Beef Fry, Malabar biryani, and Pathiri.

Local Beverages

The most famous beverage of the region is Sulaimani, a black tea flavored with cardamom, ginger, and lemon. Other popular beverages include the Sambar-vada, a south Indian soup served with lentil donuts, and the cool and refreshing Jigarthanda.

Where to Stay in Farook College Township: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Farook College Township has a wide range of accommodation options to suit every budget. Hotels like The Raviz Resort and Spa, and Meenmutty Heights are prestigious and offer luxurious amenities. Hostels and guesthouses like Aashiana Residency and Chalet Hotel are perfect for budget travelers looking for comfortable and affordable accommodation.

What to Buy in Farook College Township: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Farook College Township is a haven for shoppers with its various markets and stalls offering unique and authentic souvenirs. Some of the most popular items include traditional jewelry, hand-woven textiles, and local spices.

Local Culture and Customs of Farook College Township

The people of Farook College Township are friendly and hospitable, and visitors are always welcome with open arms. It is essential to respect the local customs and traditions, and visitors should dress modestly while visiting religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Farook College Township

The best time to visit Farook College Township is between September and March. During these months, the weather is pleasant, making it ideal for outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Farook College Township

Farook College Township is a safe town, and the crime rate is low. However, visitors should take precautions to ensure their safety, such as avoiding isolated areas at night and being aware of their surroundings.

Health in Farook College Township

Visitors should carry insect repellent to prevent mosquito bites and take precautions against mosquito-borne diseases like dengue fever and malaria.

Getting to Farook College Township: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Farook College Township

The Calicut International Airport is the nearest airport to Farook College Township, located 28 km away from the town center.

Bus Stations Near Farook College Township

Farook College Township has good connectivity by the Kerala State Road Transport Corporation (KSRTC) and private bus operators, with several buses operating daily. The Kozhikode Bus Stand is the nearest bus station, located 15 km away from the town center.

Other Options

Visitors can book private taxis or cabs to get to Farook College Township from the airport or bus station.

How to Get Around in Farook College Township

The most convenient way to get around in the town is by hiring a private taxi or an auto-rickshaw.

Where to Go Next from Farook College Township: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Farook College Township is centrally located, making it an ideal base to explore the surrounding areas. The nearby destinations include Kozhikode, Wayanad, and Malappuram. Visitors can also plan a trip to the beautiful beaches of Kappad or the Athirapally Waterfalls.
