Introduction to Beypore Beach

Beypore Beach is a picturesque coastline located in the Kozhikode district of the Malabar region in Kerala, southern India. With its serene environment, clean sea, and beautiful landscape, it is considered to be one of the most stunning beaches in Kerala. The beach is popular among locals and tourists alike, and attracts visitors from all over the world.

List of Places to Visit in Beypore Beach

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you are interested in learning about the local culture and history of Beypore Beach, the following museums and historical places are must-visits:

  • Beypore Fort: This century-old fort was built by the Portuguese in the 16th century and was later used by the British. It is a splendid place to visit to see the fascinating mix of Indian and European architecture.

  • Kozhikode Beach: Kozhikode Beach is one of the most popular beaches in the region and offers a great spot to view stunning sunsets.

Natural Attractions of Beypore Beach: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Beypore Beach is surrounded by beautiful natural attractions, and you will find an abundance of outdoor activities to indulge in. Here are some of the most popular natural attractions:

  • Thusharagiri Falls: These beautiful waterfalls are surrounded by the lush greenery of the Western Ghats and are an ideal spot for trekking and rock climbing expeditions.

  • Vadakara Beach: This beach offers a serene and tranquil environment with crystal clear waters.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Beypore Beach

Hiking and biking are popular activities at Beypore Beach. The following are some of the best biking and hiking routes to explore:

  • Beypore-Chaliyam Trail: This route offers a glimpse of the village lifestyle and lets you see the serene beauty of the countryside while riding.

  • Kozhikode-Elathur Trail: This trail offers a mix of hilly terrain and rugged countryside, passing through small villages and scenic landscapes, making it an ideal option for adventurous excursionists.

Best Things to Do in Beypore Beach: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best activities that you can do in Beypore Beach:

  • Kerala Backwaters Tour: This is a scenic tour that takes you through the freshwater lagoons, lakes, and canals of the backwaters of Kerala.

  • Fishing Tour: Spending a day on the water is a great way to experience the local lifestyle and culture. You can hire a local fisherman to take you out to the sea to experience the thrill of catching a fish!

Week-Long Itinerary for Beypore Beach

Here is a sample itinerary for a week-long trip to Beypore Beach:

  • Day 1: Upon arrival, take a leisurely stroll on the beach and enjoy the sunset.

  • Day 2: Visit the Kozhikode beach and nearby attractions, such as the Beypore Fort.

  • Day 3: Take a backwater tour and witness the natural beauty of the region.

  • Day 4: Spend a day hiking or biking through the Beypore-Chaliyam Trail or Kozhikode-Elathur Trail.

  • Day 5: Visit the Thusharagiri Falls and enjoy the scenic surroundings.

  • Day 6: Take a fishing tour and experience the lifestyle and culture of the local fishermen.

  • Day 7: Spend the day relaxing on the beach and enjoying the serene environment.

Food and Drink Scene of Beypore Beach

Must-Try Dishes

Kerala is known for its unique and flavorful cuisine, and Beypore Beach is no exception. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • Kerala Parotta: A popular flatbread that is made with flour, oil, and water, and is served with spicy chicken or vegetable curry.

  • Meen Curry: A spicy fish curry that is made with sour tamarind paste and coconut milk.

Local Beverages

Here are some of the popular local beverages that you can find in Beypore Beach:

  • Toddy: A fermented beverage made from the sap of coconut trees.

  • Masala Chai: A spiced tea that is prepared with cardamom, cinnamon, and other aromatic spices.

Where to Stay in Beypore Beach: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Beypore Beach has a wide range of accommodation options that cater to all types of travelers. Here are some of the most popular options:

  • Marina Residency: This hotel offers spacious rooms with balconies overlooking the sea.

  • Beach Heritage Inn: This guesthouse offers a comfortable and budget-friendly stay with a great location.

What to Buy in Beypore Beach: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Beypore Beach offers a great variety of local products and souvenirs that you can take home as mementos. Some of the most popular local products include:

  • Coconut Shell Handicrafts: Handmade coconut shell products such as bowls, lamps, and other decorative items.

  • Banana Fiber Products: Environmentally friendly banana fiber products such as bags, slippers, and mats.

Local Culture and Customs of Beypore Beach

Beypore Beach is home to a rich and unique culture. Here are some customs and traditions that you can expect to encounter during your visit:

  • Theyyam: A traditional dance form that is performed during festivals and other special occasions.

  • Fishing: Fishing is an important part of the local lifestyle and culture, and visitors can observe or participate in fishing activities.

Best Time to Visit Beypore Beach

The best time to visit Beypore Beach is from October to May when the weather is generally pleasant and suitable for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Beypore Beach

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Litter the beach or surrounding areas.

  • Walk on the beach inappropriately dressed.

Safety and Health

Safety in Beypore Beach

Beypore Beach is generally quite safe for tourists. However, it is always advised to travel with caution and to be aware of your surroundings.

Health in Beypore Beach

It is always advisable to consult with your doctor and to take the necessary precautions before traveling to Beypore Beach. Make sure to carry any medications that you require.

Getting to Beypore Beach: Transportation Options and Logistics

Beypore Beach is well-connected by all modes of transportation. Here are some of the transportation options available:

Airports Near Beypore Beach

  • Calicut International Airport: This airport is the nearest international airport to Beypore Beach, and is located approximately 30 km away.

Bus Stations Near Beypore Beach

  • Beypore Bus Station: This bus station is located just a few minutes away from the beach.

Other Options

  • Private Cabs and Taxis: This is a convenient option for travelers who want to explore the area at their own pace.

How to Get Around in Beypore Beach

The best way to get around Beypore Beach is by walking or cycling. Visitors can also hire taxis or autos to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Beypore Beach: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations that you can visit from Beypore Beach:

  • Kannur: A historic city with a rich cultural heritage that is located just a few hours away.

  • Wayanad: A stunning hill station and wildlife sanctuary that is located approximately 90 km away.

Conclusion

Beypore Beach is an ideal destination for travelers seeking a peaceful and serene environment. With its natural beauty, cultural attractions, and delicious cuisine, it offers something for everyone. Whether you are an adventure enthusiast or simply want to relax and unwind, Beypore Beach is definitely worth a visit!
