Introduction to Ramanattukara

Nestled in the heart of Kozhikode District, Ramanattukara is a beautiful destination in Kerala, known for its natural beauty, history, and culture. The town is surrounded by lush green hills and is home to numerous attractions, including sacred temples, museums, and historical sites. Ramanattukara is an ideal destination for those who want to escape the hustle and bustle of city life and experience the beauty and tranquillity of nature.

List of Places to Visit in Ramanattukara

Ramanattukara is a town full of surprises and amazing places to visit. Here are some of the most popular attractions in the town:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Koodalmanikyam Temple: This ancient temple is dedicated to Lord Bharatha, the brother of Lord Rama. The temple is known for its unique architecture and is considered one of the most sacred temples in Kerala.
  • Kallai River: The Kallai River is the second largest river in Kozhikode. The river is known for its scenic beauty and is a popular spot for picnics and boating.
  • Beypore: The Beypore port is an ancient port and a major fishing spot in Ramanattukara. This port is also known for its shipbuilding industry, which dates back to the 17th century.
  • Kadalundi Bird Sanctuary: This bird sanctuary is located along the Kadalundi River and is home to a wide variety of migratory birds.

Natural Attractions of Ramanattukara: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Thusharagiri Waterfalls: Thusharagiri means ‘the misty mountain,’ and the waterfalls are located in the middle of a dense forest. It is a perfect place for trekking, rock climbing, and bird-watching.
  • Peruvannamuzhi Dam: Peruvannamuzhi Dam is a popular spot for boating, picnicking, and bird-watching. The dam is surrounded by hills covered in greenery, and the view from the dam is breathtakingly beautiful.
  • Kakkayam Dam and Reservoir: This dam is surrounded by hills and is a popular spot for trekking and camping. The view from the top of the dam is breathtakingly beautiful, and the reservoir is ideal for boating and fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ramanattukara

Ramanattukara is a paradise for trekkers and mountain bikers. Some of the popular hiking and biking routes in the town are:

  • Thusharagiri Waterfalls trek: This trek traverses through the lush green forest and leads to the beautiful Thusharagiri waterfalls.
  • Kakkayam Dam trek: This trek leads to the top of the Kakkayam Dam, offering breathtaking views of the scenic surroundings.
  • Karassery-Malapuzha biking route: This route is perfect for mountain bikers, with its rugged terrain and scenic views.

Best Things to Do in Ramanattukara: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of things to do in Ramanattukara to keep you busy and entertained. Here are some of the best things to do in Ramanattukara:

  • Visit Beypore Fort: Explore the ancient fort and get a glimpse of the history of the town.
  • Take a boat ride on the Kallai River: Enjoy the scenic beauty of the Kallai River from the comfort of a boat.
  • Explore the Thusharagiri Waterfalls: Trek through the dense forest and enjoy the beauty of the Thusharagiri Waterfalls.
  • Visit Koodalmanikyam Temple: Make a wish and seek blessings at the sacred Koodalmanikyam Temple.

Food and Drink Scene of Ramanattukara

Ramanattukara has a rich culinary tradition, with a variety of delicious local delicacies to tantalize your taste buds. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in Ramanattukara:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Thalassery Biriyani: This aromatic biriyani is a popular dish in Kerala, and Ramanattukara is no exception.
  • Fish curry: Ramanattukara is a coastal town, and the fish curry here is a must-try dish.
  • Appam and Stew: This is a popular breakfast dish in Ramanattukara and is generally served in all hotels and restaurants.

Local Beverages

  • Toddy: Also known as coconut beer, toddy is a popular local beverage in Ramanattukara.
  • Sulaimani tea: A spiced tea that is a local favorite in Ramanattukara.

Where to Stay in Ramanattukara: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ramanattukara offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets. You can choose from budget hostels, guesthouses, and luxury hotels. Here are some of the best places to stay in Ramanattukara:

  • Beypore Beach Hotel: This hotel offers stunning views of the Beypore Beach and is perfect for those who want to stay close to the beach.
  • The Raviz Kadavu: This luxury resort is located on the banks of the Chaliyar River and offers world-class amenities.
  • Thashmi Guest House: This budget guesthouse offers clean and comfortable rooms at affordable prices.

What to Buy in Ramanattukara: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Ramanattukara is known for its handicrafts, such as bamboo baskets, coir products, and handicrafts made of coconut shells. You can also buy spices and pickles, which are a specialty of Kerala.

Local Culture and Customs of Ramanattukara

The people of Ramanattukara have a rich cultural heritage, and their customs and traditions are rooted in the history of the town. The town is known for its festivals, such as Onam, Vishu, and Thiruvathira, which are celebrated with great pomp and splendor.

Best Time to Visit Ramanattukara

The best time to visit Ramanattukara is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant, and the town is at its most beautiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ramanattukara

When visiting Ramanattukara, it is important to be respectful of the local culture and traditions. Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Respect the customs and traditions of the locals.
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious places.
  • Try the local delicacies and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local customs and traditions.
  • Use foul language or litter the streets.
  • Participate in any illegal activities.

Safety and Health

While Ramanattukara is a safe place to visit, it is important to take some precautions to ensure your safety and health.

Safety in Ramanattukara

  • Avoid travelling alone at night.
  • Keep your valuable belongings with you at all times.
  • Be cautious when using public transportation.

Health in Ramanattukara

  • Drink only bottled water.
  • Carry a basic first-aid kit with you.
  • Use mosquito repellent to avoid mosquito-borne diseases.

Getting to Ramanattukara: Transportation Options and Logistics

Ramanattukara is well-connected by air, rail, and road, making it easy to reach the town.

Airports Near Ramanattukara

  • Calicut International Airport, which is about 25 km away from the town.

Bus Stations Near Ramanattukara

  • Kozhikode Bus Station, which is about 17 km away from the town.

Other Options

  • You can also hire a taxi or take a private car to reach Ramanattukara.

How to Get Around in Ramanattukara

The best way to get around Ramanattukara is by hiring a taxi or auto-rickshaw. These can be easily found in the town, and most drivers are friendly and helpful.

Where to Go Next from Ramanattukara: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are a number of nearby destinations that you can visit from Ramanattukara, including:

  • Kozhikode: This city is located just 17 km away from Ramanattukara and is known for its history, culture, and natural beauty.
  • Wayanad: This beautiful district is located about 87 km away from Ramanattukara and is known for its scenic beauty and wildlife sanctuaries.

Conclusion

Ramanattukara is a hidden gem in Kerala, with something for everyone. From its natural beauty to its rich history and culture, Ramanattukara has plenty to offer visitors. Use this guide to plan your trip and make the most of your time in this beautiful town.
