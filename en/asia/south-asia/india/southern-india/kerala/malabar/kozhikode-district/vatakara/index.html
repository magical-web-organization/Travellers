Introduction to Vatakara

Vatakara is a small town situated in the Kozhikode district of Kerala, India. It is also known as Vadakara and is a part of Malabar. Vatakara is a land of natural beauty, with serene beaches, lush greenery, and enchanting waterfalls. It is also a culturally rich place, with museums, temples, and historical monuments that attract tourists from all over the world. This travel guide will take you on a tour of Vatakara’s popular attractions, best things to do, food and drink scene, where to stay, and more.

Local Culture and Customs of Vatakara

Vatakara is a place of rich cultural traditions and customs. The people of Vatakara are predominantly Hindus and Muslims. The festivals and celebrations of Vatakara are a blend of different religious customs, including Onam, Vishu, Eid, and Christmas. The traditional art forms of Vatakara include Theyyam, Poorakkali, and Oppana.

Best Time to Visit Vatakara

The best time to visit Vatakara is from October to March as the weather is pleasant, and the humidity is low. During these months, the temperature ranges from 25-30 degrees Celsius. The monsoon season from June to September is also a good time to visit as the waterfalls and greenery are at their best.

Do’s and Don’ts of Vatakara

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Dress appropriately while visiting temples and religious places.
  • Carry mosquito repellent and sunscreen lotion.
  • Try the local food and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs without permission.
  • Litter in public places.
  • Touch or feed wild animals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Vatakara

Vatakara is generally a safe place, but you should be vigilant while traveling at night, and avoid carrying valuable items while walking on isolated roads. Also, follow traffic rules while driving.

Health in Vatakara

Vatakara has good medical facilities, but it is advised to carry a basic first aid kit while traveling. Drink only bottled water, and avoid consuming street food.

Getting to Vatakara: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Vatakara

The Calicut International Airport is the nearest airport to Vatakara, located 60 km away.

Bus Stations Near Vatakara

Vatakara is well connected by buses to all the major cities of Kerala.

Other Options

You can also hire a taxi or cab from Calicut to Vatakara or take the train to the Vatakara railway station.

How to Get Around in Vatakara

Auto-rickshaws, taxis, and buses are readily available in Vatakara for travel throughout the town.
