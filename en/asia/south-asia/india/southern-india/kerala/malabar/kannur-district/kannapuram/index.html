A Comprehensive Guide to Kannapuram: Explore the Best of Southern India

Are you planning a trip to Southern India? Then you must visit Kannapuram, a hidden gem in Kerala’s Malabar region. This stunning destination offers a mix of cultural attractions, breathtaking scenery, and outdoor adventures that will leave you awestruck. Here’s a comprehensive guide to Kannapuram that will help you plan your Southern India journey like a pro.

Introduction to Kannapuram

Kannapuram is a small town located in Kannur District, in Malabar, in Kerala, in Southern India. This charming destination is well-known for its cultural and historical significance, natural beauty, and delicious cuisine.

Kannapuram is an old port town that was once a prominent trading center. The town has a rich history that dates back to the Chirakkal Royal Family, who ruled the region for many years. Today, Kannapuram is a bustling town that attracts visitors from all over the world, who come to explore its unique charm and beauty.

List of Places to Visit in Kannapuram

Kannapuram has a lot to offer for tourists, including cultural attractions, natural wonders, and adventurous experiences. Here are the top places to visit in Kannapuram:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kannapuram Fort: This ancient fort was built by the Portuguese in the 16th century and served as a military base for many years. Today, the fort is open to tourists who can explore its rich history and architecture.

  • Arakkal Museum: The Arakkal Museum is located in Kannapuram and is one of the best museums in Kerala. The museum showcases the history of the Arakkal family, who were the only Muslim royal family in Kerala. You can explore a variety of exhibits, including weapons, dresses, and other artifacts.

  • Payyambalam Beach: Payyambalam Beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Kerala. It offers stunning views of the Arabian Sea and is a great place to relax and enjoy the scenery.

Natural Attractions of Kannapuram: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Muzhappilangad Beach: Muzhappilangad Beach is a stunning beach located just 15 kilometers from Kannapuram. This beach is known for its unique characteristics, including being the only drive-in beach in India. You can explore various activities such as beach sports, parasailing, and paragliding.

  • Madayipara: Madayipara is a mesmerizing destination located near Kannapuram. This scenic spot is known for its incredible natural beauty, including wildflowers, grasslands, and stunning rock formations. It’s a perfect hiking and photography spot.

  • Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary: Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary is located just 35 kilometers from Kannapuram. This sanctuary is home to a variety of wildlife, including elephants, tigers, and leopards. It’s a perfect place for nature enthusiasts and offers an excellent opportunity to explore the wilderness of the region.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kannapuram

Kannapuram is a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts who love to explore nature. Here are the top hiking/biking routes of Kannapuram:

  • Madayipara: Madayipara is one of the best hiking trails in Kannapuram. The trail offers breathtaking views of the region’s natural beauty and is home to a variety of flora and fauna.

  • Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary: The Aralam Wildlife Sanctuary offers a variety of hiking trails that cater to different fitness levels. The trails take you through dense forests, grasslands, and other natural habitats, providing a unique and unforgettable experience.

Best Things to Do in Kannapuram: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Kannapuram:

  • Trek to Ezhimala: Ezhimala is a stunning mountain located near Kannapuram. The trek to Ezhimala offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea and is a great opportunity to explore the natural beauty of the region.

  • Explore the Local Cuisine: Kannapuram is known for its delicious cuisine, including seafood and vegetarian dishes. You can explore the local food scene by visiting the night markets and street food stalls.

  • Take a Boat Ride: Kannapuram is surrounded by a network of backwaters and canals that offer a unique and unforgettable experience. Take a boat ride through these calm waters and explore the hidden beauty of the region.

Here’s a week-long itinerary for Kannapuram that will help you make the most of your journey:

Food and Drink Scene of Kannapuram

Kannapuram is known for its delicious cuisine, including seafood and vegetarian dishes. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Malabar Paratha and Chicken Curry: Malabar Paratha and Chicken Curry are one of the most popular dishes in Kannapuram. It is a savory, soft bread with tender chicken curry.

  • Meen Curry: Meen Curry is a spicy fish curry that is a must-try dish for seafood lovers. It is a perfect blend of spices, coconut milk, and fresh fish.

Local Beverages

  • Toddy: Toddy is a popular local beverage made from coconut palms. It’s a fermented drink with a mild alcoholic content and is a must-try for visitors.

  • Sulaimani: Sulaimani is a refreshing tea made with aromatic herbs and spices. It is a popular beverage in Kannapuram and is perfect to refresh under the sun.

Where to Stay in Kannapuram: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kannapuram offers a variety of accommodation options that cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best options:

  • Kannur Beach House: Located near the Payyambalam Beach, this beach house is an excellent option for those looking for a peaceful and relaxing stay.

  • Malabar Escapes: Malabar Escapes is a luxury resort that offers breathtaking views of the Arabian Sea. It’s an excellent option for those who want to indulge in luxury and comfort.

  • Wheels Backpackers Hostel: Wheels Backpackers Hostel is an affordable option for travelers on a budget. It’s a cozy and comfortable hostel that offers a variety of facilities and services.

What to Buy in Kannapuram: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kannapuram offers a variety of souvenirs and local products that make perfect gifts for loved ones. Here’s what to buy:

  • Spices and Tea: Kannapuram is known for its spices and tea, which are of high quality and are favored for their distinguishable aroma and flavor. It’s perfect to take home as souvenirs or gifts.

  • Handmade Crafts: Kannapuram is home to talented artisans who create exquisite crafts and souvenirs. You can buy handmade crafts such as wooden boxes, toys, and jewelry.

Local Culture and Customs of Kannapuram

Kannapuram is a culturally rich destination with unique customs and traditions. Here are some of the customs you should be aware of:

  • Dress Modestly: Kannapuram is a conservative town, and it is advisable to dress modestly, especially when visiting religious places.

  • Respect the Local Customs: The locals in Kannapuram are friendly and hospitable. Respect their customs and traditions, and you will be welcomed with open arms.

Best Time to Visit Kannapuram

The best time to visit Kannapuram is between October and February, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for exploring the region.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kannapuram

Here are some of the do’s and don’ts of Kannapuram:

Do:

  • Respect the Local Customs: As mentioned before, respect the local customs, and you will be treated kindly.

  • Explore the Local Food Scene: Kannapuram’s food scene is amazing, so do try out the various delicacies and cuisines.

Don’t:

  • Ignore the Dress Code: Kannapuram is a conservative town. So as a tourist, it’s essential to dress modestly and avoid wearing revealing clothes.

  • Litter the Environment: Kannapuram is blessed with natural beauty. Please do your part as a responsible visitor and avoid littering the environment.

Safety and Health

Kannapuram is generally safe and tourist-friendly. However, it’s essential to take necessary precautions to ensure a safe and healthy journey.

Safety in Kannapuram

  • Avoid Walking Alone at Night: Walking alone at night in Kannapuram is not advisable. Please ensure that you travel in groups or use modes of transportation.

  • Stay Alert: Beware of pickpockets and potential scams. Be vigilant and aware of your surroundings at all times.

Health in Kannapuram

  • Drink Bottled Water: It’s advisable to drink bottled water and avoid drinking tap water in Kannapuram.

  • Carry a First-Aid Kit: Carrying a first-aid kit is always advisable, especially when traveling to remote destinations.

Getting to Kannapuram: Transportation Options and Logistics

Kannapuram is well-connected to major cities in India, and you can reach the town through various transportation options.

Airports Near Kannapuram

  • Kozhikode International Airport: The Kozhikode International Airport is the nearest airport to Kannapuram, located nearly 143 kilometers away.

  • Kochi International Airport: The Kochi International airport is located approximately 273 kilometers away from Kannapuram.

Bus Stations Near Kannapuram

  • Kannapuram Bus Stand: The Kannapuram Bus Stand is the main bus stand in the town, offering regular services to nearby destinations.

Other Options

  • Car and Taxi Services: You can hire private cars and taxis to travel to Kannapuram from nearby cities and towns.

  • Train Services: The nearest train station to Kannapuram is the Kannur Railway Station, located around 62 kilometers away.

How to Get Around in Kannapuram

Kannapuram is a small town that can be explored on foot, bicycles or motorbikes. You can also hire taxis and autorickshaws to travel around the town.

Where to Go Next from Kannapuram: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some of the nearby destinations and excursions you can take from Kannapuram:

  • Thalassery: Thalassery is a beautiful town located just 30 kilometers from Kannapuram. It has a rich history, and you can explore its unique charm and beauty.

  • Tellicherry: Tellicherry is another excellent destination located close to Kannapuram. It’s known for its beautiful beaches, unique cuisine, and historic significance.

Conclusion

Kannapuram is a must-visit on your Southern India journey. This unique and mesmerizing destination offers a perfect blend of cultural attractions, natural beauty, and adventurous activities that will leave you awestruck. Plan your Kannapuram trip with our comprehensive guide and enjoy an unforgettable and extraordinary journey in Southern India.
