Introduction to Muzhakkunnu

Muzhakkunnu is a stunning place located in Kannur District, in Malabar, in Kerala, in Southern India, in India, in South Asia, in Asia. Muzhakkunnu is a hilly village that offers breathtaking views of nature, and it is an ideal getaway for those seeking peace and tranquillity. This small village has attractive tourist spots that can give you a perfect break from the routine hustle-bustle of life. This travel guide has everything you need to know before planning a trip to Muzhakkunnu.

List of Places to Visit in Muzhakkunnu

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Madayi Para Fort: Built-in the 16th century, this fort is a majestic ancient monument that offers a panoramic view of the Arabian sea.

  • Arakkal Museum: The museum features the history of Arakkal Dynasty, one of the oldest Muslim royal families in Kerala.

Natural Attractions of Muzhakkunnu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Kuyiloor Mala: It is one of the highest peaks in Kannur district and offers a challenging yet rewarding trekking experience to the visitors.

  • Pazhassi Dam: Located in the middle of the hilly terrains of Kannur, Pazhassi Dam is a perfect spot for a picnic with family and friends.

  • Payyambalam Beach: Stretching for about four kilometres, Payyambalam Beach is a popular spot for taking a relaxing walk or watching the sunset.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Muzhakkunnu

Muzhakkunnu is a haven for trekkers and bikers, with terrains that offer breathtaking views of nature. Some of the popular trekking/biking routes in Muzhakkunnu are:

  • Muzhappilangad Beach to Kavvayi Backwaters

  • Kottiyoor to Kuyiloor Mala

  • Ezhome to Kirenkara

Best Things to Do in Muzhakkunnu: Activities and Tours

  • Trekking and Biking: As mentioned, Muzhakkunnu is a perfect spot for trekking and biking.

  • Camping: The scenic views of Muzhakkunnu make it an excellent spot for camping.

  • Visit Historical Places: Explore the history and culture of Muzhakkunnu by visiting the historical places.

Food and Drink Scene of Muzhakkunnu

Must-Try Dishes

  • Malabar Parotta and Chicken Curry: A signature dish of Kerala that should be on your list.

  • Fish Moli: A traditional Kerala fish curry in a coconut milk base.

Local Beverages

  • Sulaimani: A tea with lemon and spices.

  • Toddy: A local alcoholic drink made from coconut trees.

Where to Stay in Muzhakkunnu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Lalit Resort and Spa Bekal: A luxury resort offering a spa and wellness centre.

  • Kairali Heritage Resort: A family-friendly resort with an outdoor pool and restaurant.

  • Malabar Ocean Front Resort and Spa: A beachfront resort with a spa and wellness centre.

What to Buy in Muzhakkunnu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Kannur Handloom: Buy the traditional handloom products like towels, bedsheets, and fabrics.

  • Cashews: Muzhakkunnu is famous for cashew nuts, and you can buy them in various flavours.

Local Culture and Customs of Muzhakkunnu

Muzhakkunnu is a mixture of cultures and religions. The people of Muzhakkunnu speak Malayalam, and it is advisable to learn a few common phrases before travelling.

Best Time to Visit Muzhakkunnu

The best time to visit Muzhakkunnu is between October and March when the weather is cool and dry.

Do’s and Don’ts of Muzhakkunnu

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and dress modestly.

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter, and keep the surroundings clean.

  • Do not take photographs without permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Muzhakkunnu

Muzhakkunnu is a safe place to travel, but it is always advisable to take precautions.

Health in Muzhakkunnu

It is better to carry a first-aid kit along with you while travelling. Hospitals and clinics are available in case of any emergency.

Getting to Muzhakkunnu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Muzhakkunnu

The nearest airport to Muzhakkunnu is the Calicut International Airport, which is around 113.7 kilometres away.

Bus Stations Near Muzhakkunnu

The nearest bus station to Muzhakkunnu is the Thalassery Bus Stand.

Other Options

Cabs and taxis are available for rent from major cities.

How to Get Around in Muzhakkunnu

The best way to get around in Muzhakkunnu is by hiring a bike or a car, or by walking.

Where to Go Next from Muzhakkunnu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Bekal: Visit Bekal Fort, the largest at Kasargod district of Kerala.

  • Kavvayi Backwaters: Take a boat ride from Kavvayi Backwaters.

Conclusion

Muzhakkunnu is a beautiful place to visit in Kannur, Kerala. From trekking routes to exploring the historical places, this town has something for everyone. The stunning views of nature and the cultural heritage of Muzhakkunnu make it a must-visit destination in Kerala.
