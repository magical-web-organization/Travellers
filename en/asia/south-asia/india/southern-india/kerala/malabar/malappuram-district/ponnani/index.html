Introduction to Ponnani

Ponnani is a small town in Malappuram District, located in Malabar, Kerala, India. Abundant with natural beauty and charming cultural experiences, this town is a perfect destination for travelers who want to immerse themselves in the local culture while enjoying breathtaking landscapes. Ponnani is home to historic sites, pristine beaches, and a unique food and drink scene that will satisfy foodies.

List of Places to Visit in Ponnani

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Kattil Mosque

The Kattil Mosque is an ancient mosque that dates back to the 14th century. The mosque is famous for its unique architecture, which combines traditional Indian and Islamic styles. The intricate designs and carvings of the mosque are stunning.

Juma Masjid

Juma Masjid is a famous mosque located in the heart of Ponnani. This mosque is famous for its beautiful wooden architecture, which is a blend of traditional Kerala and Mughal styles. The mosque also has a library containing rare and ancient books.

Kodikuthimala

Kodikuthimala, also known as the “Golden Peak,” is a beautiful hill located near Ponnani town. The hill offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Natural Attractions of Ponnani: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Nattika Beach

Nattika Beach is a picturesque beach located near Ponnani town. The beach is popular for its serene atmosphere and clean waters. Visitors can enjoy a number of water sports activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, and surfing.

Padinjarekara Beach

Padinjarekara Beach is one of the best beaches in Kerala. The beach is famous for its stunning sunset views and is a great place for a romantic walk. Visitors can also enjoy fishing and swimming in the clear waters.

Bharathapuzha River

The Bharathapuzha river is the longest river in Kerala and is also known as the “River of the Gods.” Visitors can enjoy a river cruise, which takes you through stunning landscapes, tranquil backwaters, and lush forests.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ponnani

The Beach Loop

The Beach Loop is a popular bike route in Ponnani. The route takes you through scenic beaches, serene backwaters, and historic sites. Visitors can also enjoy local cuisine and shopping along the way.

Best Things to Do in Ponnani: Activities and Tours

Bamboo rafting in the backwaters

Bamboo rafting in the Bharathapuzha backwaters is a unique experience that should not be missed. Visitors can enjoy a peaceful ride through serene backwaters and lush green surroundings, enjoying scenic views along the way.

Kalaripayattu Martial Arts Training

Kalaripayattu is an ancient martial art form that originated in Kerala. Visitors can take a training class to learn its techniques and history.

Food and Drink Scene of Ponnani

Must-Try Dishes

Puttu

Puttu is a traditional breakfast dish made from rice flour and served with coconut and chickpea curry. It is one of the most popular dishes in Ponnani and is a must-try.

Kallummakkaya (Mussels) Curry

Kallummakkaya Curry is a popular seafood dish made from mussels cooked in a rich blend of coconut milk and spices. It is a spicy dish and is best enjoyed with rice.

Local Beverages

Sulaimani

Sulaimani is a traditional tea made with lemon and spices. It is a refreshing drink and is a must-try.

Sharbat

Sharbat is a refreshing drink made from rose petals and sugar syrup. It is a popular drink in Ponnani and is perfect for hot summer days.

Where to Stay in Ponnani: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Boon Inn

Boon Inn is a budget-friendly hotel located in the heart of Ponnani. The hotel offers clean and comfortable rooms and excellent hospitality.

The Raviz Resort and Spa

The Raviz Resort and Spa is a luxurious resort located on the banks of the Bharathapuzha River. The resort offers luxurious rooms, an outdoor pool, and a spa.

What to Buy in Ponnani: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kurta Pyjama

Kurta pyjama is a traditional Indian outfit that is popular in Ponnani. Visitors can buy a Kurta pyjama as a souvenir.

Coconut Products

Coconut products, such as coconut oil and coconut-based sweets, are popular in Ponnani. Visitors can buy these products to take home.

Local Culture and Customs of Ponnani

Clothing

Ponnani is a conservative town, and visitors are expected to dress modestly. It is recommended to cover the arms and legs and avoid wearing revealing clothing.

Language

The main language spoken in Ponnani is Malayalam, but most people can speak English as well.

Best Time to Visit Ponnani

The best time to visit Ponnani is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and dry. The temperature during this time is around 30°C, making it perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ponnani

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Wear modest clothing.
  • Try the local cuisine.

Don’t:

  • Consume alcohol in public areas.
  • Visit religious places wearing shoes.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ponnani

Ponnani is a safe town, but travelers should take standard precautions like safeguarding their belongings and avoiding walking alone at night.

Health in Ponnani

Travelers are recommended to drink bottled water to avoid getting sick. It is also wise to carry insect repellent and sunscreen.

Getting to Ponnani: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ponnani

The nearest airport to Ponnani is Calicut International Airport, located approximately 75 km away.

Bus Stations Near Ponnani

Ponnani is well connected by bus to major cities in Kerala like Kochi, Kozhikode, and Thrissur.

Other Options

Traveling by taxi or hiring a car is also a convenient option for getting to Ponnani.

How to Get Around in Ponnani

The best way to get around in Ponnani is by auto-rickshaw or local buses. Taxis are also available for hire.

Where to Go Next from Ponnani: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Guruvayoor

Guruvayoor is a famous temple town located approximately 75 km from Ponnani.

Thrissur

Thrissur is a cultural hub located approximately 60 km from Ponnani. The town is famous for its cultural festivals such as Thrissur Pooram and Onam.

Conclusion

Ponnani is a hidden gem in Kerala that is waiting to be discovered. From stunning beaches to natural wonders and delicious cuisine, Ponnani has something for everyone. So, pack your bags and explore this beautiful town now!
