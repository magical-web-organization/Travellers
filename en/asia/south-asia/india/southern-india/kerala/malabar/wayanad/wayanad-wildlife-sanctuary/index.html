Introduction to Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is located in the Wayanad district of Kerala and is surrounded by the Western Ghats. This sanctuary is a part of the Nilgiri Biosphere Reserve and is home to an extensive range of flora and fauna. It covers an area of 344 square kilometers and is known for its lush green forests, rare and endangered species, and stunning landscapes.

Local Culture and Customs of Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

The culture of Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is predominantly influenced by the tribal population. The majority of people practice Hinduism, Christianity, and Islam. The local people are friendly and welcoming to tourists, and visitors are expected to respect their customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

The best time to visit Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is from November to February when the weather is pleasant and cool.

Do’s and Don’ts of Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

Do:

  • Wear comfortable clothing and shoes suitable for trekking
  • Make sure to carry insect repellent and first-aid kit
  • Respect the local people and their customs and traditions
  • Make sure to follow the rules and regulations of the sanctuary.

Don’t:

  • Don’t carry any plastic items into the sanctuary
  • Don’t harm any wildlife or plants in the sanctuary
  • Don’t litter.

Safety and Health

Safety in Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

The sanctuary is generally safe, but visitors are advised to take necessary precautions while trekking or hiking, and avoid going alone deep into the forest.

Health in Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

Visitors are advised to get vaccinated against diseases like malaria and typhoid before traveling to the sanctuary. It is also suggested to carry a mosquito repellent and drinking water at all times.

Getting to Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here are some transportation options and logistics for getting to Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary:

Airports Near Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

The nearest airports to Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary are Calicut International Airport and Bangalore International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

The nearest bus station to Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is Sulthan Bathery bus station, which is well-connected to major cities in Kerala.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a private taxi or drive their own car to reach the sanctuary.

How to Get Around in Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary

The best way to explore Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary is by hiking, trekking, or biking. Private taxis and jeeps are also available for tourists who prefer a comfortable ride.
