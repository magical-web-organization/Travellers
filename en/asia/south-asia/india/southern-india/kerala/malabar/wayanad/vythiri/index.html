Introduction to Vythiri

Vythiri is a breathtaking town located in the Wayanad district of Kerala, known for its scenic views and natural beauty. The place is surrounded by the Western Ghats and is home to various adventurous activities such as hiking, cycling, and wildlife treks. Vythiri is an ideal destination for nature-lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike, with a perfect balance of modern amenities and natural serenity.

List of Places to Visit in Vythiri

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Edakkal Caves: A prehistoric monument with rock carvings from the Neolithic and Mesolithic Ages.
  • Wayanad Heritage Museum: A museum that showcases the culture and heritage of Wayanad through various exhibits.
  • Pazhassi Raja Tomb: A monument built in memory of Kerala Varma Pazhassi Raja, a freedom fighter who fought against the British

Natural Attractions of Vythiri: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Chembra Peak: The highest peak in Wayanad, perfect for trekking and camping.
  • Pookode Lake: A serene lake surrounded by lush greenery, with boating and fishing opportunities.
  • Soochipara Falls: A beautiful waterfall with a pool perfect for swimming and picnicking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Vythiri

  • Chembra Peak Trek: A challenging trek to the highest peak in Wayanad, offering spectacular views of the surrounding hills.
  • Banasura Hill Trek: A trek to the second-highest peak in Wayanad, known for its vibrant flora and fauna.
  • Chain Tree Trek: A trek through the dense forest that leads to the Chain Tree, a historical site that tells a story of a tribal member who helped kill a British officer.

Best Things to Do in Vythiri: Activities and Tours

  • Wildlife Safari: Explore Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary on a jeep safari, spotting animals like elephants, tigers, and leopards.
  • Tea Estate Tour: Tour the vast tea plantations of Vythiri, learning about the tea-making process and enjoying some tea while taking in the views.
  • Rock Climbing: Test your climbing skills at the climbing wall for a thrill.

Week-Long Itinerary for Vythiri

Day Plan
1 Visit Edakkal Caves and Pazhassi Raja Tomb
2 Trek to Chembra Peak and visit Pookode Lake
3 Go on a wildlife safari at Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary
4 Explore Banasura Hill and enjoy the tea estate tour
5 Trek to Chain Tree and rock climbing
6 Relax and indulge in Ayurveda massages at Wayanad
7 Visit Soochipara Falls and indulge in outdoor activities

Food and Drink Scene of Vythiri

Must-Try Dishes

  • Malabar Biriyani: A popular rice dish made with an infusion of Indian spices and meat (chicken, beef, or lamb).
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry: A popular breakfast dish made with rice flour and steamed in a bamboo mould, served with a flavorful spiced chickpea curry.
  • Pathiri and Chicken Curry: A gluten-free dish made with rice flour and served with a spicy chicken curry.

Local Beverages

  • Sulaimani Tea: A refreshing and aromatic tea made with lemon and spices.
  • Tender Coconut Water: A healthy and refreshing drink that is naturally sweet and helps detoxify the body.

Where to Stay in Vythiri: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Vythiri Resort: A luxury resort known for its stunning location and excellent services.
  • Raindrops Resorts: A budget-friendly resort perfect for families and couples.
  • Hiliya Resort: A nature-based resort with an eco-friendly policy.

What to Buy in Vythiri: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Spices: Wayanad is known for producing high-quality spices that make excellent souvenirs.
  • Handicrafts: From wood carvings to bamboo products, Vythiri has a range of traditional handicrafts that make for great souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Vythiri

Vythiri is known for its rich culture and traditions that are closely tied to nature. The local tribe of Wayanad, Paniyas, forms the major part of the town. The language spoken here is Malayalam. The traditional dance form of Wayanad is known as Thirayattam, and some of the festivals celebrated here are Onam, Vishu, and Pongal.

Best Time to Visit Vythiri

The best time to visit Vythiri is from October to May when the weather remains pleasant, and the temperature ranges from 15°C to 30°C.

Do’s and Don’ts of Vythiri

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Dress modestly when visiting temples and other religious places.
  • Try the local cuisine.

Don’t:

  • Litter the natural surroundings.
  • Disrespect the environment or wildlife.
  • Take photographs without seeking permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Vythiri

Vythiri is a safe place to travel. However, it is recommended to take precautions and be aware of your surroundings, especially when travelling alone or at night.

Health in Vythiri

Vythiri has good healthcare facilities in case of emergencies. It is recommended to carry a first-aid kit and necessary medications.

Getting to Vythiri: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Vythiri

The nearest airport to Vythiri is Calicut International Airport, located approximately 70 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Vythiri

Vythiri is well-connected to major cities in Kerala through state-run buses.

Other Options

It is also possible to reach Vythiri by trains or rental cars.

How to Get Around in Vythiri

The best way to explore Vythiri is by renting a car or bike. Auto-rickshaws are also a convenient mode of transportation.

Where to Go Next from Vythiri: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Kozhikode: A coastal city that offers a variety of attractions such as historical landmarks, beach, food, and shopping.
  • Coorg: A scenic hill station famous for its coffee plantations, trekking trails, and waterfalls.
  • Mysore: A historic city known for its magnificent palaces and temples.

Conclusion

Vythiri is a perfect destination for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts. With its scenic beauty, rich culture, and variety of activities, it promises to be an unforgettable experience. So pack your bags, and get ready for an adventure of a lifetime!
