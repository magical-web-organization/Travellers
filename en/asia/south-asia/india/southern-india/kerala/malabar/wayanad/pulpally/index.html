Introduction to Pulpally

Pulpally is a small town located in the Wayanad district of Kerala in Southern India. The town is nestled between the Western Ghats, providing a lush green landscape and a serene environment. The town has an abundance of natural beauty, making it a perfect place for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. Pulpally is also home to a plethora of colorful festivals, each with their cultural and religious significance.

In this travel guide, we will explore the best places to visit, food and drink options, hiking and biking routes, local culture and customs, transportation options, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in Pulpally

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Pulpally has a rich cultural heritage, and it is dotted with various temples and ancient monuments.

  • Thirunelly Temple
  • Thrissilery Shiva Temple
  • Pallikkunnu Church
  • Edakkal Caves
  • Jain Temple Wayanad

Natural Attractions of Pulpally: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Pulpally is blessed with bountiful natural beauty, offering visitors an opportunity to escape the hustle-bustle of city life.

  • Chethalayam Waterfalls
  • Banasurasagar Dam
  • Kuruva Island
  • Tholpetty Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Brahmagiri Hills

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pulpally

Pulpally offers numerous exciting trails for hiking and biking enthusiasts.

  • Brahmagiri Peak Trek
  • Pakshipathalam Hiking Trail
  • Thollayiram Peak Trek
  • Thusharagiri Waterfall Trek

Best Things to Do in Pulpally: Activities and Tours

Pulpally has a lot to offer for adventure seekers and those who want to explore the local culture.

  • Ayurveda massage and spa treatments
  • Taste local cuisine
  • Experience Camping
  • Village visits
  • Elephant ride

Food and Drink Scene of Pulpally

Must-Try Dishes

Pulpally has a rich food culture – a blend of traditional Malabar and Kannur cuisines. Here are some must-try dishes while you’re in the town:

  • Appam and Stew
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry
  • Fish Curry and Rice
  • Erachi Pathiri
  • Neyyappam

Local Beverages

  • Black tea (kadak chai)
  • Sulaimani (lemon tea)

Local Culture and Customs of Pulpally

Pulpally has a unique cultural identity, and the town is known for its colorful festivals, ceremonial dances, and traditional way of living. Most of the population in Pulpally is Hindu, and they follow a distinctive lifestyle and customary rituals.

Best Time to Visit Pulpally

The best time to visit Pulpally is during the winter months, from December to February, when the climate is pleasant and mild.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pulpally

Do:

  • Respect and follow the local customs and traditions
  • Buy local handicrafts and souvenirs from authorized sellers
  • Try local food and drinks

Don’t:

  • Engage in any form of animal abuse or mistreatment
  • Litter or dispose of waste in a public place

Safety and Health

Safety in Pulpally

Pulpally is a safe destination for tourists. However, be cautious and avoid traveling alone at night. Beware of pickpockets in busy areas.

Health in Pulpally

Make sure to carry necessary medications and protect yourself from mosquito bites, especially during monsoons.

Getting to Pulpally: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Pulpally

Kozhikode International Airport is the nearest airport, located 105 km away from Pulpally.

Bus Stations Near Pulpally

Sulthan Bathery Bus Station is the nearest bus station, located just 28 km away from Pulpally.

Other Options

Taxis, auto-rickshaws, and motorcycles are available for hire to reach Pulpally.

How to Get Around in Pulpally

Pulpally is a small town, and most of the destinations are within walking distance. However, you can also rent bicycles, motorcycles, or hire a cab to explore the town.

Where to Go Next from Pulpally: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Muthanga Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Lakkidi
  • Thirunelli hill temple

Conclusion

Pulpally is a hidden gem of Kerala, offering a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural significance, and traditional lifestyle. Plan your visit to Pulpally now, and experience this beautiful destination.
