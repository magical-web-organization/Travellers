Introduction to Top Station

Top Station is a top-tier destination located in Northern Travancore, in Kerala, South India, known for its magnificent natural beauty and vibrant cultural traditions. The region boasts lush green hills, stunning vistas and breathtaking landscapes, making it a favorite spot for nature enthusiasts, hikers, and outdoor adventurers. This travel guide has curated a list of the top attractions, activities, and cultural experiences that visitors can look forward to when they visit Top Station.

List of Places to Visit in Top Station

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Top Station is home to several museums and historical landmarks that provide a glimpse of the rich and diverse cultural heritage of Southern India. Some of the must-visit places include:

  • Mattupetty Dam
  • Tea Museum
  • Kundala Dam Lake
  • Anamudi Peak
  • Carmelagiri Elephant Park

Natural Attractions of Top Station: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Top Station’s natural beauty is unparalleled, and there are plenty of outdoor adventures to embark on. Whether it’s hiking, cycling, or just enjoying the scenery, these natural attractions are sure to leave an impression:

  • Eravikulam National Park
  • Echo Point
  • Kolukkumalai Tea Plantation
  • Meesapulimala
  • Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary

Hiking/Biking Routes of Top Station

Top Station is a haven for hikers and bikers, and there are plenty of trails for all skill levels. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • Kolukkumalai Jeep Safari
  • Meesapulimala Trek
  • Chokramudi Peak Trek
  • Anamudi Trek

Best Things to Do in Top Station: Activities and Tours

Top Station is more than just its natural beauty and cultural landmarks; it offers many fun activities and tours for visitors to enjoy.

  • Tea Estate Tours
  • Kundala Lake Boating
  • Pedal Boating at Mattupetty Dam
  • Rock Climbing

Food and Drink Scene of Top Station

Must-Try Dishes

Top Station offers a unique cuisine that blends flavors from across the region. Here are some dishes to try on your visit:

  • Appam with Steamed Vegetables
  • Fish Molee
  • Kerala Prawn Curry
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry
  • Payasam

Local Beverages

Kerala is renowned for its herbal beverages known for their medicinal benefits. Here are some popular local drinks to try:

  • Masala Chai
  • Nannari Sharbat
  • Sulaimani Tea
  • Thandai

Where to Stay in Top Station: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Top Station offers visitors a variety of accommodation options, ranging from budget hostels to luxury resorts. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • Spice Tree Munnar
  • Windermere Estate
  • Glenmore Resort

What to Buy in Top Station: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Top Station is known for its vibrant arts and crafts scene, and there are plenty of souvenirs and local products to buy as a reminder of your trip. Some of the popular items include:

  • Spices and Tea
  • Handicrafts
  • Ayurvedic products

Local Culture and Customs of Top Station

Top Station is steeped in rich cultural traditions, some of which include:

  • Kathakali
  • Kalaripayattu
  • Theyyam
  • Attukal Pongala

Best Time to Visit Top Station

The best time to visit Top Station is during winter, from December to February, when the weather is pleasantly mild and comfortable for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Top Station

Do:

  • Follow local customs and traditions
  • Respect the locals and their culture
  • Keep the environment clean and tidy

Don’t:

  • Litter in public places
  • Disrespect religious sites
  • Harm the wildlife

Safety and Health

Safety in Top Station

Top Station is generally safe for visitors, but precautions should always be taken, such as checking for travel advisory updates and ensuring safe transportation arrangements.

Health in Top Station

Visitors to Top Station should be aware of potential risks like altitude sickness and insect bites and take preventative measures like carrying mosquito repellent and consulting a doctor before traveling to high altitudes.

Getting to Top Station: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Top Station

Cochin International Airport is the nearest airport to Top Station, located around 125 km away.

Bus Stations Near Top Station

Munnar Bus Stand is the nearest bus station to Top Station, located around 32 km away.

Other Options

Visitors can also opt for taxis or rental cars to reach Top Station.

How to Get Around in Top Station

The best way to get around Top Station is by hiring a taxi or renting a motorbike.

Where to Go Next from Top Station: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Top Station is a perfect base for exploring the beautiful surroundings of Southern India, with plenty of options for nearby destinations and excursions which include:

  • Munnar
  • Thekkady
  • Alleppey

Conclusion

Top Station is a scenic wonderland that offers something for everyone, from cultural attractions to outdoor adventures, stunning views, and delicious cuisine. With our comprehensive guide, you’re sure to have an unforgettable trip to this hidden gem of Kerala’s Southern India.
