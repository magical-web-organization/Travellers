Introduction to Kolenchery

Kolenchery is a beautiful town located in Northern Travancore, Kerala, Southern India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. The town is surrounded by lush green hills and is a popular tourist destination. The town is located around 20 kilometers from the city of Kochi, which is a major port city on the Malabar Coast.

Kolenchery is also known for its serene and peaceful environment. The town is well-connected with major cities in Kerala and is easily accessible. The town is populated by friendly and hospitable people who are always ready to help tourists.

In this travel guide, we will explore the attractions, food, culture, and logistics of Kolenchery.

Food and Drink Scene of Kolenchery

Kolenchery is known for its delicious Kerala cuisine. Visitors can try the following must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Puttu: Puttu is a popular breakfast dish in Kerala. It is made from rice flour and is typically served with black chickpeas or banana.
  • Appam: Appam is a type of pancake made from fermented rice batter. It is usually served with coconut milk or vegetable stew.
  • Fish Curry: Fish curry is a popular dish in Kerala. It is made from a variety of fish and is typically served with rice.
  • Porotta: Porotta is a type of Indian bread that is popular in Kerala. It is usually served with a curry.

Local Beverages

  • Toddy: Toddy is a fermented drink that is made from the sap of palm trees. It is a popular drink in Kerala and is usually served in local bars.
  • Filter Coffee: Filter coffee is a popular drink in Kerala. It is made from ground coffee beans and is typically served with hot milk.
