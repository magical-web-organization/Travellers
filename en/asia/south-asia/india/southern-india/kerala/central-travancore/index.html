Introduction to Central Travancore

Located in the southern part of India, Central Travancore is a region filled with picturesque landscapes, rich cultural history, and natural attractions. A former princely state, Central Travancore has a unique place in the country’s history and is a must-visit destination for history buffs, nature lovers, and adventure seekers. In this travel guide, we’ll take a closer look at what Central Travancore has to offer, including the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay.

List of Places to Visit in Central Travancore

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Central Travancore has a rich cultural history that can be experienced through its museums and historical places. Here are some of the must-see attractions:

  • Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple: This ancient temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is one of the most iconic landmarks of Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city of Kerala.

  • Krishnapuram Palace: A 16th-century palace located near Kayamkulam, Krishnapuram Palace has a rich history and a unique architectural style.

  • Karumadikuttan Statue: This 9th-century statue of Lord Buddha is located in Karumadi, near Alappuzha.

Natural Attractions of Central Travancore: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Central Travancore is home to several natural attractions that offer an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. Here are some of the top natural attractions:

  • Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary: Located in Thekkady, this wildlife sanctuary is home to a variety of animals such as tigers, elephants, and leopards.

  • Vagamon: This scenic hill station is located in the Idukki district and is known for its rolling hills, meadows, and pine forests.

  • Munroe Island: This group of small islands is located near Kollam and is home to backwaters, coconut groves, and a unique eco-system.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Central Travancore

Central Travancore is a great place for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Ponmudi Hills: Located near Thiruvananthapuram, this hill station is known for its tea plantations and offers some of the best hiking trails in the region.

  • Parunthumpara: This rocky terrain near Peerumedu is a popular spot for trekking and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys.

  • Idukki: This district is home to several trekking routes that offer stunning views of the Western Ghats.

Best Things to Do in Central Travancore: Activities and Tours

There is no shortage of things to do in Central Travancore. Here are some popular activities and tours:

  • Backwater Houseboat Tours: Experience the natural beauty of Kerala by exploring its backwaters on a houseboat.

  • Ayurvedic Massage: Unwind with a relaxing Ayurvedic massage, which is a popular practice in Kerala.

  • Kathakali Performance: Watch a traditional Kathakali performance, which is a form of Indian classical dance that originated in Kerala.

Week-Long Itinerary for Central Travancore

Here’s a suggested week-long itinerary for Central Travancore:

  • Day 1: Explore Thiruvananthapuram’s cultural and historical attractions, including the Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple and the Napier Museum.

  • Day 2: Visit Kovalam Beach and enjoy some water sports activities.

  • Day 3: Embark on a backwater tour in Alleppey.

  • Day 4: Visit the Periyar Wildlife Sanctuary and go on a jungle safari.

  • Day 5: Trek to the top of Ponmudi Hills for some stunning views.

  • Day 6: Relax and unwind with an Ayurvedic massage.

  • Day 7: Go on a shopping spree in the local markets and pick up some souvenirs.

Food and Drink Scene of Central Travancore

Must-Try Dishes

Central Travancore is known for its delicious cuisine, which is a blend of spices and flavors. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • Appam and Stew: A popular breakfast dish that consists of a fluffy pancake-like bread and a vegetable stew.

  • Kallummakkaya: A spicy mussel dish that is a popular seafood delicacy in the region.

  • Banana Chips: Thinly sliced and deep-fried until crispy, these chips are a popular snack in Kerala.

Local Beverages

In addition to its food, Central Travancore also has a unique selection of beverages. These include:

  • Toddy: A fermented coconut sap that is a popular local drink.

  • Elaneer: This refreshing drink is made from coconut water and is a popular summer beverage.

Where to Stay in Central Travancore: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Central Travancore has a wide range of accommodation options to suit every budget. Here are some options:

  • Luxury Hotels: Taj Malabar Resort & Spa, The Leela Kovalam Beach

  • Mid-Range Hotels: Poetree Sarovar Portico, Alleppey Beach Resorts

  • Budget Hotels and Guesthouses: Haveli Backwater Resorts, Homestays in various locations.

What to Buy in Central Travancore: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Central Travancore is a great place to shop for souvenirs and local products. Here are some options:

  • Handicrafts: The region is known for handicrafts such as woodcarvings, brassware, and coir products.

  • Spices: Kerala is known for its spices, and Central Travancore is no exception. You can find a wide variety of fresh, high-quality spices here.

  • Tea: The region is known for its tea plantations, and you can find a variety of high-quality teas here.

Local Culture and Customs of Central Travancore

Central Travancore has a rich cultural heritage, and it is important to understand and respect the customs and traditions of the locals. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Dress modestly when visiting temples or other religious places.

  • Remove your shoes before entering a temple or a home.

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Central Travancore

The best time to visit Central Travancore is from September to February, when the weather is pleasant and dry. However, keep in mind that this is also the peak tourist season, so be prepared for crowds and higher prices.

Do’s and Don’ts of Central Travancore

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.

  • Try the local cuisine.

  • Explore the backwaters on a houseboat.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing when visiting religious places or conservative areas.

  • Disrespect the local customs and traditions.

  • Damage the natural attractions by littering or interfering with the eco-system.

Safety and Health

Safety in Central Travancore

Central Travancore is generally safe for tourists, but it is important to exercise caution and keep your belongings safe.

Health in Central Travancore

Drinking bottled water is recommended, and it is also advised to take precautions against mosquito-borne diseases such as dengue fever and malaria.

Getting to Central Travancore: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Central Travancore

The nearest airport is the Trivandrum International Airport, which is located in Thiruvananthapuram. Other airports nearby include Cochin International Airport and Madurai Airport.

Bus Stations Near Central Travancore

Central Travancore is well-connected by bus, with major cities such as Thiruvananthapuram, Kochi, and Kozhikode accessible by bus.

Other Options

You can also reach Central Travancore by train or taxi.

How to Get Around in Central Travancore

The best way to get around Central Travancore is by hiring a taxi or an auto-rickshaw. Buses are also available, but they can be crowded and uncomfortable.

Where to Go Next from Central Travancore: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Central Travancore is well-connected to several other destinations in Kerala, and it is also possible to explore nearby states such as Tamil Nadu and Karnataka. Here are some nearby destinations:

  • Munnar: A scenic hill station located in the Idukki district, known for its tea plantations and stunning views.

  • Kanyakumari: Located at the southernmost tip of India, Kanyakumari is a popular destination known for its scenic beauty and cultural heritage.

  • Cochin: A historic port city known for its colonial architecture, Jewish heritage, and backwaters.

Conclusion

Central Travancore is a unique and beautiful region that has something to offer for everyone. Whether you’re interested in history, nature, or adventure, Central Travancore has it all. With this comprehensive travel guide, you’ll be able to plan your trip to this magical region with ease and confidence.
