Aranmula Travel Guide: Discover the Best of Kerala

Located in Central Travancore, in the southernmost state of India, Kerala, Aranmula is a small village famous for its rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and gastronomic delights. Known for the annual Aranmula Boat Race and the Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple, this charming village is a perfect getaway for those looking to explore the pristine beauty of Kerala.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll explore everything you need to know about Aranmula, including places to visit, things to do, local food and drink, where to stay, and what to buy.

List of Places to Visit in Aranmula

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple
  2. Heritage Museum
  3. Paliakkara Church
  4. The School for Traditional Arts and Crafts

Natural Attractions of Aranmula: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Aranmula Boat Race
  2. Pampa River
  3. Hanging Bridge
  4. Aranmula Mirror

Hiking/Biking Routes of Aranmula

  1. Aranmula to Kozhencherry
  2. Kadammanitta to Pamba

Best Things to Do in Aranmula: Activities and Tours

  1. Enjoy a traditional Kerala-style ayurvedic massage
  2. Take a boat ride on the Pampa river
  3. Take part in the annual Aranmula Boat Race
  4. Visit the School for Traditional Arts and Crafts
  5. Experience the local art of making Aranmula Mirror

Week-Long Itinerary for Aranmula

Day Activities
1 Arrival and check-in at a local guesthouse. Evening visit to Parthasarathy Temple
2 Visit the Heritage Museum and Paliakkara Church. Take part in a local art workshop
3 Take a boat ride on the Pamba river. Visit the Hanging bridge
4 Hike/Bike to Kadammanitta from Aranmula
5 Visit the School for Traditional Arts and Crafts. Take part in cooking classes
6 Explore the natural beauty of Pamba river. Try Aranmula Mirror making
7 Departure

Food and Drink Scene of Aranmula

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Kallu Shaapu (Toddy shop) delicacies
  2. Kerala Style Fish Curry
  3. Puttu and Kadala Curry
  4. Appam with Stew
  5. Dosa and Chutney

Local Beverages

  1. Toddy (Coconut Wine)
  2. Coffee made from hand-ground beans
  3. Tea with local spices

Where to Stay in Aranmula: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Kaivalyam Retreat – Authentic Kerala-Style cottages with all modern amenities
  2. Kudappanakunnu Heritage Homestay – Eclectic mix of traditional and modern decor
  3. Santhitheeram Homestay – Traditional Kerala-style house with modern amenities
  4. Aranmula Heritage Homestay – Comfortable rooms with personalized attention

What to Buy in Aranmula: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Aranmula Mirror
  2. Traditional Kerala sarees and dress materials
  3. Spices, Tea and Coffee
  4. Handicrafts and art objects

Local Culture and Customs of Aranmula

  1. The Aranmula Boat Race is a popular annual event and a major attraction in the region.
  2. The locals are known for their love of arts and crafts and traditional performing arts.
  3. The Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple is an important pilgrimage centre for Hindus.

Best Time to Visit Aranmula

The best time to visit Aranmula is from September to February when the climate is pleasant and the weather is comfortable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Aranmula

Do:

  1. Dress modestly while visiting temples and religious centres.
  2. Try the local cuisine.
  3. Be respectful of local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  1. Damage natural or heritage sites and objects.
  2. Litter or pollute the environment.
  3. Disrespect local traditions and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Aranmula

Aranmula is a safe and peaceful village. Visitors should take normal precautions while visiting tourist places and avoid going into the water during monsoon season.

Health in Aranmula

Visitors are advised to take precautions against mosquito-borne diseases like dengue and malaria. We advise carrying mosquito repellents and wearing long-sleeved clothes.

Getting to Aranmula: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Aranmula

The nearest airport to Aranmula is the Thiruvananthapuram International Airport, which is about 115 km away.

Bus Stations Near Aranmula

Aranmula Bus Terminal is well connected through the state bus service of Kerala. Private buses are also available from neighboring states.

Other Options

Taxis and auto-rickshaws are easily available in Aranmula from nearby towns and cities.

How to Get Around in Aranmula

Bicycles and motorbikes are available for rent in Aranmula. The best way to explore the region is to either hire a bicycle or motorbike or take a leisurely stroll around the village.

Where to Go Next from Aranmula: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Alleppey – Famous for its backwaters and houseboats
  2. Kollam – Known for its beaches, temples, and vibrant marketplaces
  3. Varkala – Popular for its clifftop beaches and scenic views
  4. Thekkady – Explore the wildlife at Periyar National Park

Conclusion

Aranmula is a hidden gem in Kerala, with its rich culture, natural beauty, and mouth-watering local delicacies making it a perfect destination for those looking to explore the southern region of India. This comprehensive travel guide is sure to help you plan your next visit to Aranmula and make the most of your trip.
