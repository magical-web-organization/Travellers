Introduction to Wagamon

Located in the Central Travancore region of Kerala, Wagamon is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. This small town is surrounded by lush greenery, rolling hills, and wildlife, offering a unique experience to visitors seeking a peaceful and adventurous getaway. Wagamon is the ideal destination for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and anyone looking to disconnect from the hustle and bustle of city life.

List of Places to Visit in Wagamon

Wagamon offers a plethora of attractions that cater to every type of traveler. Below is a list of must-visit places in Wagamon.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kurisumala Ashram
    • Located in the Western Ghats, Kurisumala Ashram is a must-visit destination for visitors seeking spiritual rejuvenation. The Ashram offers breathtaking views, meditation sessions and is open to visitors all year round.
  • Pine Forest
    • The Pine Forest is a unique attraction in Wagamon that should not be missed. The Pine Forest offers stunning views and an excellent opportunity to connect with nature, take long walks and breathe in the fresh air.
  • Indo-Swiss Dairy Farm
    • The Indo-Swiss Dairy Farm is a beautiful attraction that offers a glimpse of the dairy farming culture in Kerala. Visitors can witness firsthand the process of dairy farming from milking cows to the processing of milk.

Natural Attractions of Wagamon: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Marmala Waterfalls
    • Marmala Waterfalls is a serene and tranquil destination that is easily accessible by trekking. The trek offers breathtaking views of the lush greenery and is an ideal spot for a picnic.
  • Thangal Hill
    • Thangal Hill is a beautiful hilltop cemetery that offers panoramic views and an excellent opportunity to take photographs.
  • Vagamon Lake
    • Vagamon Lake is a popular destination for boating and offers stunning views of the surrounding hills.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Wagamon

Wagamon offers several hiking and biking trails perfect for adventure enthusiasts. Some of the most popular routes are:

  • Muruganmala Trek
    • This trek is a moderately difficult trek that offers stunning views of the surroundings. The trek takes about three hours to reach the peak of the mountain.
  • Vagamon to Parunthumpara
    • This route is ideal for mountain bikers and offers some of the most breathtaking views of the surroundings.

Best Things to Do in Wagamon: Activities and Tours

  • Paragliding
    • Visitors can experience the thrill of paragliding in the beautiful hills of Wagamon. The experience offers stunning views and an adrenaline rush that cannot be matched.
  • Jeep Safari
    • Jeep Safari is an excellent opportunity to explore the natural beauty of Wagamon. The safari offers a unique perspective of the hills, wildlife, and traditional lifestyle of the locals.

Week-Long Itinerary for Wagamon

Wagamon offers plenty of attractions that can be explored over a week-long trip. Below is an ideal itinerary that covers the best of Wagamon.

1 Visit Kurisumala Ashram and Pine Forest

Food and Drink Scene of Wagamon

Wagamon offers a unique and diverse range of local cuisine that is a must-try for any food enthusiast.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Nadan Kappa
    • Nadan Kappa is a traditional dish made from boiled tapioca and served with spicy fish curry.
  • Appam and Stew
    • Appam and Stew is a breakfast dish made from rice flour and coconut milk and served with a stew made from vegetables or meat.
  • Puttu and Kadala Curry
    • Puttu is a breakfast dish made from rice flour and coconut, and Kadala Curry is a spicy curry made from black chickpeas.

Local Beverages

  • Toddy
    • Toddy is a local alcoholic beverage made from the sap of the coconut tree. It is a favorite among locals and should not be missed.
  • Sulaimani
    • Sulaimani is a refreshing tea made with black tea, lemon, mint, and spices.

Where to Stay in Wagamon: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Wagamon offers a diverse range of accommodation options that cater to every traveler’s needs and requirements. Below are some of the best options in the area.

  • Green Hill Estate
    • Green Hill Estate is a luxury resort that offers stunning views, excellent food, and a range of activities to keep guests entertained.
  • Vagamon Heights
    • Vagamon Heights is a budget-friendly option that offers comfortable rooms, local food and a stunning view of the hills.
  • Bamboo Dale
    • Bamboo Dale is a unique option that offers guests the opportunity to stay in a bamboo house located in the heart of nature.

What to Buy in Wagamon: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Wagamon offers a range of souvenir options that are unique to the area. Below are some of the best options available.

  • Spices
    • Kerala is known for its spices, and Wagamon is no exception. Visitors can buy a range of local spices and take the flavors of Wagamon back home with them.
  • Handicrafts
    • Wagamon is home to talented local artisans who produce a range of handicrafts that make for perfect souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Wagamon

Wagamon is a city with a rich culture and customs that visitors must be aware of. Some of the things to keep in mind are:

  • Respect the Locals
    • The people of Wagamon are friendly and welcoming, so visitors should respect their culture and customs.
  • Dress Modestly
    • Visitors should dress modestly and avoid wearing revealing clothes, especially while visiting religious places.

Best Time to Visit Wagamon

The best time to visit Wagamon is from September to March when the weather is pleasant, and there is little to no rainfall. Visitors should avoid visiting during the monsoon season (June-September) when heavy rains can cause travel disruptions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Wagamon

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Try the local cuisine
  • Explore the natural attractions of Wagamon
  • Stay in eco-friendly accommodations

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothes while visiting religious places
  • Litter or vandalize the natural surroundings
  • Disrespect the locals

Safety and Health

Safety in Wagamon

Wagamon is a safe destination for travelers, and visitors should not experience any major safety issues. However, visitors should exercise caution while trekking or participating in adventure sports.

Health in Wagamon

Wagamon is a remote destination, and visitors should carry their medicines and stay prepared for any medical emergencies. Visitors should also ensure they consume only bottled water and avoid street food.

Getting to Wagamon: Transportation Options and Logistics

The most convenient mode of transportation to reach Wagamon is by road. Visitors can either take a local bus from Kottayam or hire a taxi to reach Wagamon. The nearest airport and railway station are located in Kochi, which is about 110 km from Wagamon.

Airports Near Wagamon

  • Cochin International Airport
    • The Cochin International Airport is the nearest airport to Wagamon and is located about 110 km away.

Bus Stations Near Wagamon

  • Kottayam Bus Station
    • Visitors can take a bus from Kottayam to reach Wagamon.

Other Options

  • Taxi Services
    • Visitors can hire a taxi to reach Wagamon from nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Wagamon

The best way to explore Wagamon is on foot or by renting a bicycle. Visitors can also hire a taxi to explore the area.

Where to Go Next from Wagamon: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Thekkady
    • Thekkady is a popular destination for wildlife enthusiasts and is located about 120 km from Wagamon.
  • Munnar
    • Munnar is a beautiful hill station located about 150 km from Wagamon and offers stunning views and a range of activities.

Conclusion

Wagamon is a hidden gem that offers visitors a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and spirituality. Visitors can indulge in local cuisine, explore the natural attractions of the area, and learn about the rich culture and customs of the people of Wagamon. A trip to Wagamon is a must for any traveler seeking a peaceful and adventurous getaway.
