Introduction to Kottayam

Kottayam is a city located in Central Travancore in the Indian state of Kerala. The city is known for its unique blend of traditional and modern lifestyle, breathtaking scenery, and cultural attractions. Tourists can visit Kottayam all year round, but the best time to visit is during the winter months (October to February) when the weather is cool and pleasant.

List of Places to Visit in Kottayam

Kottayam offers a range of attractions for tourists. Here’s a list of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kottayam Museum: This museum showcases various artifacts related to the history, culture, and tradition of Kottayam.
  • Velimattom Mahadeva Temple: A 500-year-old temple with a stunning architecture has a great history and is considered one of the most important temples in Kottayam.
  • Thirunakkara Mahadeva Temple: Another important temple, known for its intricate architecture.
  • Vaikom Mahadeva Temple: Considered one of the most sacred temples in Kerala, it was an integral in the Indian independence movement.
  • St. Mary’s Church: Located in the town of Bharananganam, it is considered an important pilgrimage site in Southern India.

Natural Attractions of Kottayam: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Marmala Waterfalls: A stunning waterfall surrounded by the lush greenery of the Western Ghats.
  • Aruvikkuzhi Waterfall: Another beautiful waterfall with a stunning view of the surrounding hills and valleys.
  • Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary: A must-visit location for birdwatchers and nature enthusiasts.
  • Vagamon Hill Station: Known for its lush green hills and meadows, it offers many outdoor activities such as trekking, paragliding, and rock climbing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kottayam

Kottayam offers a variety of hiking and biking routes perfect for adventure enthusiasts. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Ilaveezhapoonchira: This place is located 55 km away from Kottayam and is an excellent route for hiking and biking. The scenery is stunning and offers panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and hills.
  • Vagamon Meadows: Vagamon offers a variety of trails suitable for both novice and experienced hikers and bikers.

Best Things to Do in Kottayam: Activities and Tours

Kottayam offers many activities and tours for tourists to enjoy. Here are some of the best things to do while in Kottayam:

  • Backwater Tours: Enjoy a leisurely cruise on the backwaters of Kerala, which is considered one of the most serene experiences in India.
  • Ayurvedic Spa: Relax your body and mind with an Ayurvedic massage and spa session.
  • Sunset at Vembanad Lake: Vembanad Lake is a beautiful lake that offers stunning views of the sunset. This is a must-visit location while in Kottayam.

Week-Long Itinerary for Kottayam

Here’s a suggested week-long itinerary for Kottayam:

Food and Drink Scene of Kottayam

Kottayam offers a diverse food and drink scene with a mix of traditional and modern cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Appam with stew: Appam is a rice flour pancake that’s light and fluffy, and stew is made from chicken, vegetables, and coconut milk.
  • Kappa with meen curry: Kappa is cassava and is usually eaten with spicy fish curry.
  • Malabar Parotta with chicken curry: Malabar Parotta is a layered flatbread and is usually eaten with chicken curry.

Local Beverages

  • Toddy: A local drink made from the sap of the coconut tree.
  • Sulaimani Tea: A sweet and sour tea that’s perfect for a refreshing drink on a hot day.
  • Sharbat: A refreshing drink made from fruits and herbs.

What to Buy in Kottayam: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kottayam has a variety of souvenirs and local products that tourists can take home as a reminder of their trip. Here are some options:

  • Coconut shell handicrafts
  • Kerala sarees
  • Spices and tea

Local Culture and Customs of Kottayam

Kottayam has a rich cultural heritage that is deeply rooted in tradition. Visitors are expected to respect the local customs and traditions. Men and women are expected to dress conservatively.

Best Time to Visit Kottayam

The best time to visit Kottayam is between October and February when the weather is cool and pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kottayam

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting Kottayam:

Do:

  • Respect local culture and customs.
  • Carry appropriate clothing and footwear while hiking or trekking.
  • Explore the local markets for souvenirs and local products.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing in temples or other religious places.
  • Litter or pollute the environment.
  • Take pictures without asking for permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kottayam

Kottayam is a relatively safe destination for tourists. However, visitors should take standard precautions such as not leaving valuables unattended and avoiding dark and isolated areas at night.

Health in Kottayam

Kottayam has several pharmacies and healthcare centers. Visitors are advised to carry basic medicines and consult a doctor if they experience any health issues.

Getting to Kottayam: Transportation Options and Logistics

Kottayam is well-connected by air, rail, and road.

Airports Near Kottayam

  • Cochin International Airport (90 km)
  • Trivandrum International Airport (160 km)

Bus Stations Near Kottayam

  • Kottayam Bus Stand
  • KSRTC Bus Station

Other Options

  • Private taxis
  • Auto-rickshaws

How to Get Around in Kottayam

Kottayam has an efficient public transportation system that includes buses and taxis. Visitors can also use auto-rickshaws to navigate the city.

Where to Go Next from Kottayam: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destination and excursions from Kottayam:

  • Kumarakom: Known for its backwaters and bird sanctuary.
  • Allepey: Known for its serene backwaters and stunning beaches.
  • Thekkady: Known for its wildlife sanctuary and spice plantations.

Conclusion

Kottayam is a beautiful destination that offers a unique mix of traditional and modern attractions. Visitors can explore the cultural heritage, outdoor adventures, and natural beauty of the city. Travelers can indulge in the diverse food and drink scene and explore the local culture and customs. Kottayam is a must-visit destination for anyone looking to experience the wonders of South India.
