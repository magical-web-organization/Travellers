Introduction to East

East is a prominent region located in the heart of Hyderabad, Telangana, in the southern part of India. It is a melting pot of various cultural, historical, and natural attractions that can enchant any traveler visiting Hyderabad. The region has a strategic location and is easily accessible from all major cities in India, making it a must-visit destination.

In this travel guide, we have rounded up the best things to do and see in East, Hyderabad. Whether you are a nature lover, history buff, foodie or adventure junkie, East has something to offer to everyone.

List of Places to Visit in East

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Salar Jung Museum: This museum is home to an extensive collection of Indian art, artifacts, furniture, and jewelry, including one of the largest one-man collections in the world. The museum’s collection is a testament to India’s rich history and culture.

  • Charminar: One of the iconic landmarks of Hyderabad, Charminar is a must-visit attraction in East. This monument was built in 1591 by Sultan Muhammad Quli Qutb Shah and is a remarkable example of Islamic architecture.

  • Chowmahalla Palace: Built-in the 18th century, this palace was once the seat of the Asaf Jahi dynasty. The palace has an impressive collection of artifacts, including vintage cars, furniture, and paintings.

Natural Attractions of East: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hussain Sagar Lake: A stunning man-made lake built-in 1563, Hussain Sagar Lake is a perfect place to relax and enjoy a boat ride. You can also visit the famous Buddha statue located in the middle of the lake.

  • KBR National Park: This national park is a nature lover’s paradise and has a diverse range of flora and fauna. The park is spread over 155 acres of land and is a great place for hiking and bird watching.

  • Lumbini Park: The Lumbini Park is located on the banks of the Hussain Sagar Lake and is a great place to view the city skyline. The park has several attractions, including a musical fountain and a laser show.

Hiking/Biking Routes of East

  • Nandi Hills: Located near Hyderabad, Nandi hills is a popular spot for hikers and bikers. The area has breathtaking views of the surrounding hills and is an excellent place for a day trip.

  • Osman Sagar Lake: Osman Sagar Lake is another popular spot for hiking and biking. The lake is surrounded by lush green forests and has several trails that offer a great view of the surrounding area.

  • Durgam Cheruvu: Durgam Cheruvu is a small lake located in the middle of the city, surrounded by rocks and greenery. The area has several hiking trails that offer a magnificent view of the lake.

Best Things to Do in East: Activities and Tours

  • Hyderabad Walking Tours: Explore the city and its history by taking a walking tour of Hyderabad. You can choose from many tour companies that offer guided tours of various parts of the city.

  • Food Tours: Hyderabad is famous for its mouth-watering cuisine. Take a food tour of the city and taste the delicious biryani, kebabs, and sweets.

  • Charminar Night Tour: Experience the beauty of Charminar at night by taking a guided tour. The area is lit up beautifully and is a sight to behold.

Week-Long Itinerary for East

Day 1-2: Visit Salar Jung Museum, Charminar, and Chowmahalla Palace.

Day 3: Visit KBR National Park, Lumbini Park, and Osman Sagar Lake.

Day 4-5: Explore the city by taking walking and food tours.

Day 6: Visit Nandi Hills and Durgam Cheruvu.

Day 7: Relax and enjoy a boat ride on the Hussain Sagar Lake or visit the Ramoji Film City.

Food and Drink Scene of East

East is a gastronomic paradise and has several local delicacies that you must try during your visit to Hyderabad.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Hyderabadi Biryani: Hyderabad Biryani is a world-famous dish that is a combination of rice, spices, and meat. The dish is known for its flavor and aroma.

  • Haleem: Haleem is a popular Ramadan delicacy made of wheat, barley, lentils, and meat. The dish is slow-cooked for several hours and is served with naan.

  • Dosa: A South Indian staple, Dosa is a thin, crispy pancake made from fermented batter. The dish is served with coconut chutney and sambar.

Local Beverages

  • Irani Chai: Hyderabad is famous for its Irani chai, which is a sweet, milky tea served with biscuits.

  • Lassi: Lassi is a refreshing yogurt-based drink that is a perfect thirst-quencher on hot summer days.

Where to Stay in East: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are many accommodation options in East, from budget-friendly hostels to luxurious hotels.

  • Taj Falaknuma Palace: Located in the heart of East, Taj Falaknuma Palace is a 5-star luxury hotel housed in a magnificent palace. The hotel offers stunning views of Hyderabad and has world-class amenities.

  • Park Hyatt Hyderabad: Park Hyatt Hyderabad is another luxurious hotel option in East. The hotel has elegant rooms and suites with all modern amenities.

  • Zostel Hyderabad: Zostel Hyderabad is a budget-friendly option for backpackers and solo travelers. The hostel offers comfortable dorms and private rooms and is located near several tourist attractions.

What to Buy in East: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Pearls: Hyderabad is famous for its pearls, which are sold in several shops in the city. You can buy everything from classic pearl necklaces to trendy bangles.

  • Charminar Souvenirs: Charminar is an iconic landmark of Hyderabad, and you can find several souvenirs such as keychains, magnets, and t-shirts featuring the monument.

  • Hyderabadi Bangles and Handicrafts: Hyderabad is known for its intricate bangles and handicrafts. You can find several shops selling handcrafted items such as pottery, paintings, and textiles.

Local Culture and Customs of East

Hyderabad has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its hospitality. When visiting the city, it is important to respect the local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit East

The best time to visit Hyderabad is between October and February, as the weather is mild and pleasant. Summers can be harsh, with temperatures soaring up to 45°C.

Do’s and Don’ts of East

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.

  • Try the local cuisine and explore the city’s food scene.

  • Visit the historical monuments and museums to learn about the city’s rich culture.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing when visiting religious places.

  • Disrespect the local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in East

East is a relatively safe region in Hyderabad, but it is always important to take precautions like avoiding deserted areas at night or carrying a photocopy of your passport.

Health in East

Make sure you stay hydrated and carry sunscreen if you’re visiting during the summer season. If you require medical attention, several hospitals and pharmacies are available in the region.

Getting to East: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near East

The Rajiv Gandhi International Airport is the closest airport to East and is located about 30 km away.

Bus Stations Near East

The Mahatma Gandhi Bus Station is the most prominent bus station in Hyderabad, and many buses operate from the station. You can also hire a private taxi or take a shared auto-rickshaw to reach East from any part of Hyderabad.

Other Options

Hyderabad Deccan railway station is the closest railway station to East, Hyderabad.

How to Get Around in East

The best way to get around in East is by hiring a taxi or an auto-rickshaw. You can also take the local buses, but they can be crowded and confusing for first-time visitors.

Where to Go Next from East: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Golconda Fort: Golconda Fort is a magnificent fort located about 10 km away from East. The fort is a significant historical landmark and offers a magnificent view of the city.

  • Medak Church: Medak Church is a stunning Gothic-style church located about 100 km away from East. The church is one of the largest in India and is a must-visit attraction in the region.

Conclusion

East in Hyderabad is a cultural and natural wonderland. With its rich history, diverse landscapes, and exciting activities, this region has something to offer to every kind of traveler. Explore the region’s notable attractions, try the local cuisine, buy souvenirs, and spend your leisure time in the company of the welcoming locals. Happy traveling!
