Introduction to Narcondam Island

Located in the North and Middle Andaman region of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, Narcondam Island is a hidden gem for those looking for an off-the-beaten-path adventure. With its rich cultural history, natural beauty, and abundant wildlife, Narcondam Island offers a unique travel experience that is unmatched in the region.

List of Places to Visit in Narcondam Island

Below are some of the top attractions that travelers should consider visiting during their trip to Narcondam Island.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums, Historical Places, and Local Festivals

  • Narcondam Lighthouse: A beautiful and historic lighthouse built in 1926 by the British administration.
  • Narcondam Island Government School: A humble school preserving the local culture through the teachings of language and practices at school.
  • Narcondam Coal Mines: A historical colonial ruin that boasts of being Asia’s first coal mine.
  • Narcondam Island Carnival: A staple yearly event, the carnival is a multi-day celebration of the island’s cultural heritage featuring local foods, music, and dancing.

Natural Attractions of Narcondam Island: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Narcondam Island National Park: A haven for bird watchers, the island is home to a variety of rare and exotic bird species that can be seen perched on the trees or diving into the sea.
  • Narcondam Island Beaches: Untouched secluded beach for the perfect sunbath or beach walk.
  • Narcondam Island Volcano: Geologically young and the second-last active volcano in the Andaman basin, a moderate hike to the peak provides breathtaking scenic views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Narcondam Island

Narcondam Island is a small island and only one of a few in the Andaman archipelago which is devoid of roads, making it an ideal place for hiking/biking enthusiasts. The Narcondam Island-National Park is home to some early pathways, parts of the rainforest, and the only way to access the volcano peak.

Best Things to Do in Narcondam Island: Activities and Tours

Narcondam Island offers some unique activities and tours, including:

  • Bird Watching: Explore the Narcondam Island National Park and immerse yourself in the island’s exotic bird species with expert guides.
  • Snorkeling and Diving: Discover the local marine life and underwater coral reefs.
  • Boat Tours: Go for a cruise around the island and enjoy the scenic views or go for fishing with the locals.

Food and Drink Scene of Narcondam Island

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fish Curry: A staple of Andaman Islands cuisine, this dish is made with fresh fish cooked in a curry of coconut, onions and flavorful spices.
  • Lobster Curry: Locally caught, this dish is a coconut curry with onion and fiery spices.
  • Banana Flower soup: A vegetarian option, this soup is made from savory banana flowers with lentils and curry.
  • Andaman Islands fish fry: Fresh fish fried with a blend of local spices.

Local Beverages

  • Coconut water: A refreshing and hydrating drink straight from the tree
  • Andaman Islands Lime Juice: Freshly squeezed lime juice with local organic honey

Where to Stay in Narcondam Island: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Narcondam Island offers a limited selection of accommodations to choose from depending on your budget and amenities. Some of the options include:

  • The Narcondam Island Lighthouse Guesthouse: Located close to the lighthouse and provides the perfect scenic view.
  • The Narcondam Island Government Guest House: Budget accommodations located close to the Narcondam Lighthouse and Government school.
  • The Narcondam Island Beach Resort: Up-market private traditional Andaman Island experience.

What to Buy in Narcondam Island: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Andaman Palm Leaf Souvenirs: Palm leaf baskets, mats, hat or fans, intricately woven by hand from local seasoned Palm stack.
  • Electric-blue Georgette Saris: Traditional Andaman Island sarees with contrast zari border that makes vibrant memories to bring home.
  • Handmade Ornaments: Unique souvenirs, these ornaments are made of seashells, coconut shells or palm leaves.

Local Culture and Customs of Narcondam Island

Narcondam Island has a unique and distinct cultural heritage, which is a blend of Andamanian aboriginals, Bengali refugees, and Indian settlers. The locals have preserved ancient customs and beliefs and keep them alive through song and dance, education, religious practices, and festivals.

Best Time to Visit Narcondam Island

The best time to visit Narcondam Island is during the months of October to May when the climate remains salubrious with only light rainfall. This period offers ideal weather for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Narcondam Island

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs, such as following dress codes and being mindful of local taboos.
  • Be cautious while snorkeling and diving. Follow instructions from the guide for a safe and enjoyable experience.
  • Try the local seafood and delicacies. Narcondam Island offers authentic dishes that you won’t find anywhere else.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter or pollute the environment. Narcondam Island has strict environmental conservation rules, so be responsible.
  • Do not take photos or videos of the locals without their permission.
  • Do not harm any marine life, however, tempting the catch may be.

Safety and Health

Safety in Narcondam Island

Narcondam Island is a relatively safe place to visit, with very little crime. However, it is important to be aware of your surroundings and belongings, especially when traveling alone or at night, as there are no transportation options available on the island after dark.

Health in Narcondam Island

Narcondam Island does not have any medical facilities, so it is important to pack any necessary medications and first-aid supplies before your trip.

Getting to Narcondam Island: Transportation Options and Logistics

Getting to Narcondam Island is a bit challenging as it is only reachable by the local government vessel or private chartered boats. The transportation options are limited with high travel costs and a limited schedule, making it important to plan in advance.

Airports Near Narcondam Island

The nearest airports to Narcondam Island are the Veer Savarkar International Airport in Port Blair, which is located 227 km away from the island.

Bus Stations Near Narcondam Island

As Narcondam Island is located on an island, bus transportation is not available.

Other Options

Chartering a private boat from Port Blair or Havelock Island is the most feasible option for traveling to Narcondam Island.

How to Get Around in Narcondam Island

Getting around Narcondam Island is only possible on foot or bicycles. It is essential to have a reliable map as there are no roads or signposts. Alternatively, the government boat can be chartered for private exploration and sightseeing.

Where to Go Next from Narcondam Island: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Travelers can explore the neighboring islands in Andaman and Nicobar or continue their journey in mainland India. Some options are:

  • Havelock Island: Famous for its beaches, snorkeling activities and absolute relaxation, Havelock Island is an ideal next stop.
  • Ross Island: A former British colonial establishment stationed with a few historical ruins and beaches.
  • Diglipur Island: A small town famous for its organic farming and trekking activities.

Conclusion

Narcondam Island is a hidden paradise off the coast of India, offering travelers a unique cultural and natural experience. With its natural beauty, rich history and delicious cuisine, any traveler visiting the Andaman and Nicobar Islands will not regret taking the time to visit Narcondam Island.
