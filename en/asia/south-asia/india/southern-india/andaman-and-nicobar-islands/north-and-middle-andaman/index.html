Introduction to North and Middle Andaman

North and Middle Andaman is a stunning island located in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands of Southern India. The island is a hidden gem, offering a variety of outdoor adventures, including pristine beaches, snorkeling, scuba diving, hiking, and biking trails that are sure to appeal to adventure seekers.

When planning a trip to North and Middle Andaman, you will find plenty of attractions and must-see destinations. To make your planning a little easier, below is a list of the top places to visit on the island:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Anthropological Museum
  • Cellular Jail
  • Ross Island

Natural Attractions of North and Middle Andaman: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Radhanagar Beach
  • Elephant Beach
  • Neil Island
  • Long Island
  • Limestone Caves

Hiking/Biking Routes of North and Middle Andaman

Local Culture and Customs of North and Middle Andaman

North and Middle Andaman has a diverse culture influenced by several ethnic groups. The island is home to different communities like the Andamanese, Nicobarese, and Bengalis. The people of North and Middle Andaman are friendly and hospitable.

Best Time to Visit North and Middle Andaman

The best time to visit North and Middle Andaman is between October and May. During these months, the weather is pleasant, and the sea is calm, making it ideal for water sports and outdoor adventures.

Do’s and Don’ts of North and Middle Andaman

Do:

  • Respect the local cultures and customs.
  • Travel with a reputable tour operator.
  • Carry sunscreen, mosquito repellent, and a first aid kit.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs of the local tribe without permission.
  • Litter on the beach or any other tourist spots.
  • Purchase seashells or other endangered species.

Safety and Health

Safety is a significant concern for tourists visiting North and Middle Andaman. Here are some tips to ensure your safety and good health on the island:

Safety in North and Middle Andaman

  • Prioritize safety while engaging in adventure sports.
  • Avoid visiting isolated areas alone.
  • Keep your belongings close to you while traveling.

Health in North and Middle Andaman

  • Drink only bottled water or purified water.
  • Wear protective clothing in areas with dense vegetation.
  • Seek medical attention if you experience any symptoms of illness.

Getting to North and Middle Andaman: Transportation Options and Logistics

North and Middle Andaman is accessible only by sea or air. Here are the transportation options you can consider:

Airports Near North and Middle Andaman

  • Veer Savarkar International Airport at Port Blair
  • Durgapur Airport at Diglipur

Bus Stations Near North and Middle Andaman

  • Phoenix Bay Bus Terminal in Port Blair

Other Options

  • Inter-island ferries run by the Directorate of Shipping Services from Port Blair to North and Middle Andaman Island.

How to Get Around in North and Middle Andaman

The best way to get around North and Middle Andaman is by hiring a motorcycle, a bicycle, or a car. Taxis are also available to travel within the city limits.
