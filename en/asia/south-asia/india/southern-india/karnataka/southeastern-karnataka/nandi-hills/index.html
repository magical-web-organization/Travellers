Introduction to Nandi Hills

Nandi Hills is a stunning hill station located in the Southeastern part of Karnataka in South India. This serene location is alluring to travelers seeking peace and calm environment without having to compromise on natural beauty. The hills were named after the Nandi Bull statue which is situated near the Yoganandeeshwara Temple.

List of Places to Visit in Nandi Hills

Here are some of the must-visit sights in Nandi Hills:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tipu’s Summer Palace
  • Gavi Veerbhadra Swamy Temple
  • Bhoga Nandeeshwara Temple
  • Nehru Nilaya

Natural Attractions of Nandi Hills: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Amruth Sarovar
  • Nandi Hills National Park
  • Muddenahalli-Kanivenarayanapura Trek
  • Brahmashram

Hiking/Biking Routes of Nandi Hills

Nandi Hills offers a lot of opportunities for hikers and bikers to explore its beauty by taking various routes. Here are some of the popular ones:

  • Skandagiri Trek
  • Chikballapur Trek
  • Nandi Hills Trek
  • Gudibande fort trek

Best Things to Do in Nandi Hills: Activities and Tours

To make the most of your trip to Nandi Hills, here are some things that you should do:

  • Sunrise view from Tipu’s Drop
  • Paragliding at Nandi Hills
  • Visit to the vineyards
  • Ride ATV bikes

Week-Long Itinerary for Nandi Hills

Here is a suggested itinerary to make the most of your week in Nandi Hills.

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrival, Check-In, Rest
Day 2 Visit Amruth Sarovar, Gavi Veerbhadra Swamy Temple, and Nehru Nilaya

Day 7 Departure

Where to Stay in Nandi Hills: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Nandi Hills offers a wide range of accommodations to its visitors. Here are some of the best options:

  • Nandi Hills is a budget accommodation option with basic amenities
  • Discovery Village is for those who want to enjoy luxurious stays
  • Mount Palazzo comes with a swimming pool, jogging track, and other outdoor activities

What to Buy in Nandi Hills: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don’t forget to bring back some unique souvenirs and local products to remind you of your trip to Nandi Hills, including:

  • Sandalwood Products
  • Silk Saris and Dress Materials
  • Handicrafts
  • Coffee Beans and Wine Bottles

Local Culture and Customs of Nandi Hills

Nandi Hills celebrates its local festivals with great enthusiasm, which offer the perfect opportunity to experience the culture and customs of the place. The major festivals celebrated here are Ugadi, Ganesh Chaturthi and Dussehra.

Best Time to Visit Nandi Hills

The best time to visit Nandi Hills is between November and February when the weather is mild and pleasant. Avoid visiting during summer months when it is hot and humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Nandi Hills

Do:

  • Carry a pair of binoculars
  • Wear comfortable shoes and light clothes
  • Stay hydrated

Don’t:

  • Litter around
  • Make loud noises in residential areas
  • Smoke in public areas

Safety and Health

Safety in Nandi Hills

  • Watch your steps during trekking
  • Avoid coming in contact with wildlife

Health in Nandi Hills

  • Carry a first-aid kit
  • Use mosquito repellants

Getting to Nandi Hills: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Nandi Hills

  • The nearest airport is the Kempegowda International Airport in Bangalore, which is around 50 km away from Nandi Hills.

Bus Stations Near Nandi Hills

  • The nearest bus station is Chikkaballapur, which is around 4 km away from Nandi Hills.

Other Options

  • Taxis and private vehicles are also available.

How to Get Around in Nandi Hills

  • Hiking and biking are the two best ways to get around Nandi Hills.

Where to Go Next from Nandi Hills: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations that you can visit after exploring Nandi Hills:

  • Bangalore
  • Bannerghatta National Park
  • Chikmagalur

Conclusion

Nandi Hills is a breathtaking destination that offers a perfect blend of nature and culture. A visit to this place will refresh your mind and soul. Plan your trip well to make the most of your stay, and experience the charm of Nandi Hills!
