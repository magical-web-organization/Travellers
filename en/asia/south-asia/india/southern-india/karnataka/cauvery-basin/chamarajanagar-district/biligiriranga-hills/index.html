Biligiriranga Hills, located in the Chamarajanagar district of Karnataka, is a must-visit destination for travelers seeking a mix of natural beauty and cultural significance. From hiking and biking to exploring museums and historical places, Biligiriranga Hills has something for everyone. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more.

Introduction to Biligiriranga Hills

Biligiriranga Hills, also known as B.R. Hills, is a popular hill station located at an altitude of 5091 feet. Located in the Cauvery Basin in Southern India, this destination is known for its breathtaking scenic views, rich flora and fauna, and cultural significance. The Biligiriranga Hills is surrounded by a dense forest that boasts several wildlife sanctuaries, including the Biligiriranga Swamy Temple Wildlife Sanctuary (BRTWS). The sanctuary is home to several endangered species, making it a favorite spot for nature enthusiasts and wildlife photographers.

List of Places to Visit in Biligiriranga Hills

Visiting Biligiriranga Hills is an unforgettable experience, with several attractions to explore, museums, and historical places to visit. Here are a few must-visit places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Biligiriranga Swamy Temple

The Biligiriranga Swamy Temple is a significant attraction for both devotees and tourists. The temple, which is believed to be over 500 years old, is dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara, and its architecture is a blend of Hoysala and Dravidian styles. The temple has a large number of devotees who visit it every year during the Brahmotsava.

Biligiriranga Hills Wildlife Sanctuary

The Biligiriranga Hills Wildlife Sanctuary is an unmissable place to visit, with several endangered and rare species, including tigers, panthers, elephants, and giant squirrels, among others. Trekking through the sanctuary is a popular activity for travelers.

Natural Attractions of Biligiriranga Hills: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

BRT Wildlife Sanctuary

BRT Wildlife Sanctuary covers an area of 540 square kilometers, and features a diverse landscape, including riverine forests, dry deciduous forests, and scrub jungles. Visitors can explore this sanctuary to catch a glimpse of various wildlife species, including elephants, tigers, and more.

K.Gudi Wilderness Camp

Located in the middle of the Biligiri Ranganna Hills, K.Gudi Wilderness Camp offers amazing views of the stunning landscape. It is a popular spot for trekking, bird watching and other adventure activities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Biligiriranga Hills

With its beautiful scenery and diverse landscape, Biligiriranga Hills offers several exciting trekking routes for adventure enthusiasts. The popular hiking/biking routes include:

Dodda Sampige Mara Trek

Dodda Sampige Mara Trek offers beautiful views of the Dodda Sampige Mara forest and is a popular spot for trekkers.

Sathodi Falls Trek

Sathodi Falls Trek offers beautiful views of the Sathodi Waterfalls nestled deep in the Western Ghats. Trekking through the Sathodi Falls offers visitors the chance to witness some of the most awe-inspiring natural scenery in the area.

Best Things to Do in Biligiriranga Hills: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking/biking adventures, Biligiriranga Hills offers several other activities and tours to keep visitors entertained, here are a few popular ones:

Jungle Safari

Biligiriranga Hills’ wildlife sanctuary offers several jungle safaris to visitors traveling in a group of four. A quick drive through the sanctuary offers visitors a chance to catch sight of elephants, panthers, tigers, and more.

K.Gudi Trek and Nature Walk

K.Gudi Trek and Nature Walk offer visitors a chance to explore this stunning destination with an experienced guide. The hike offers several scenic views and lets you catch sight of numerous wildlife species.

Week-Long Itinerary for Biligiriranga Hills

Visitors can spend a week exploring Biligiriranga Hills, with plenty of activities and attractions to keep you engaged. Here is a suggested itinerary:

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrive at Biligiriranga Hills, explore the temple
Day 2 Go on a jungle safari
Day 3 Visit BRTWS, and enjoy a nature walk
Day 4 Embark on the Dodda Sampige Mara Trek
Day 5 Explore K.Gudi Wilderness Camp
Day 6 Explore Sathodi Falls Trek

Food and Drink Scene of Biligiriranga Hills

Biligiriranga Hills boasts a diverse food culture that offers visitors a chance to try out some of the best dishes in the region. Local cuisine is a blend of Kannada and South Indian dishes, and visitors can try out several dishes, including:

Must-Try Dishes

Jolada Rotti

Jolada Rotti is a staple food in Karnataka and forms an essential part of Kannada cuisine. It is triangular-shaped and made using corn flour and is typically served with dal, chutney or curry.

Pakoras

Pakoras are a popular street food in Biligiriranga Hills, made by deep-frying vegetables, particularly onions, in a spicy batter.

Local Beverages

Coffee and Tea

Biligiriranga Hills is known for its coffee, which is grown in the rich, fertile soil of the area. Visitors can also enjoy different varieties of tea, including Earl Grey and Darjeeling teas, among others.

Where to Stay in Biligiriranga Hills: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Biligiriranga Hills offers various accommodation options, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses, depending on visitors’ budget and preferences.

Biligiriranga Hills Jungle Lodges and Resorts

Biligiriranga Hills Jungle Lodges and Resorts offer visitors an authentic jungle experience, with tents set up amidst the forest. Visitors can enjoy various activities, including bird-watching, jungle treks, and safaris while staying at the resorts.

Biligiriranga Hills Homestays

Biligiriranga Hills Homestays offer visitors a chance to stay with the locals, enjoy local cuisine and culture, and explore the surrounding areas with an experienced guide.

What to Buy in Biligiriranga Hills: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Biligiriranga Hills is famous for its aromatic coffee beans and other local products, including honey, sandalwood products, and rosewood souvenirs. Visitors can purchase these products from local markets and shops or from gift shops located in the hotels and resorts.

Local Culture and Customs of Biligiriranga Hills

Biligiriranga Hills has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors can explore the different customs and traditions followed by the people of the region. The arts and crafts, dance, and music of the region are some of the popular cultural practices that visitors can enjoy.

Best Time to Visit Biligiriranga Hills

The best time to visit Biligiriranga Hills is from November to April, as this is when the weather is pleasant and perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Biligiriranga Hills

Do:

  • Engage in eco-tourism activities
  • Respect the environment and wildlife
  • Try out local cuisine and coffee

Don’t:

  • Litter while on your visit
  • Disturb wildlife or destroy natural habitats
  • Wear bright colors that scare wildlife.

Safety and Health

Safety in Biligiriranga Hills

Biligiriranga Hills is a safe destination with friendly locals. However, visitors are advised to follow basic safety precautions, such as not venturing into the forest at night and being mindful of the jungle animals.

Health in Biligiriranga Hills

Visitors should be careful of malaria and other mosquito-related diseases when traveling to Biligiriranga Hills. It is advisable to carry mosquito repellents.

Getting to Biligiriranga Hills: Transportation Options and Logistics

Biligiriranga Hills is well-connected by road, and visitors can reach the area by taking a bus from Bangalore or Mysore. The nearest airport is the Kempegowda International Airport in Bangalore, which is about 187 kilometers from Biligiriranga Hills.

Airports Near Biligiriranga Hills

  • Kempegowda International Airport (Bangalore)

Bus Stations Near Biligiriranga Hills

  • Mysore Bus Station
  • Bangalore Bus Station

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car to reach Biligiriranga Hills, although this option is recommended only for experienced drivers.

How to Get Around in Biligiriranga Hills

Biligiriranga Hills is best explored on foot, and visitors can also hire a car or a two-wheeler to explore the area.

Where to Go Next from Biligiriranga Hills: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can explore nearby destinations, including the city of Mysore, Kabini River, and Bandipur National Park.

Conclusion

Biligiriranga Hills has it all, from natural beauty to cultural significance. This destination is perfect for travelers looking to explore the wildlife and experience the local culture. With its breathtaking vistas and exciting activities, Biligiriranga Hills should definitely feature on your travel list.
