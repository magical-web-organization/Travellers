Introduction to Hariharapura

Hariharapura is a serene, small village located in Chikmagalur district, Karnataka, India, nestled in the lap of the Western Ghats, dotted with lush greenery, coffee plantations, and picturesque landscapes. It is a peaceful getaway for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and photography enthusiasts, with its unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage.

List of Places to Visit in Hariharapura

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Harihareshwara Temple is a famous ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, constructed in the Hoysala style of architecture, with intricate carvings and sculptures.
  • Annapoorneshwari Temple is another significant temple located in the vicinity of Hariharapura, which is one of the shakti peethas.
  • Mysore Palace is an iconic palace with a rich history, stunning architecture, and beautiful artwork.
  • Belur Chennakeshava Temple is one of the finest examples of Hoysala architecture, with exquisite carvings and sculptures.
  • Shravanabelagola is a Jain pilgrimage center with a 57-feet tall Gommateshwara statue, carved out of a single rock.

Natural Attractions of Hariharapura: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mullayanagiri Peak is the highest peak in Karnataka, and trekking to its summit provides a breathtaking view of the surrounding hills and valleys.
  • Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary is a wildlife reserve located near Hariharapura, with a diverse flora and fauna, including tigers, elephants, and birds.
  • Kudremukh National Park is another famous national park and tiger reserve, with stunning scenic views, waterfalls, and trekking trails.
  • Kallathigiri Falls is a mesmerizing waterfall surrounded by lush greenery and coffee plantations, providing an enchanting experience.
  • Baba Budangiri is a popular pilgrimage center and scenic spot, known for its beautiful sunsets, lush greenery, and the famous coffee plantations.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hariharapura

  • Mullayanagiri-Bababudangiri Trek is a challenging trek that provides stunning scenic views and requires trekking through coffee plantations, dense forests, and rocky terrain.
  • Kudremukh Trek is another famous trek known for its lush greenery, mountain peaks, and waterfalls, with a diverse flora and fauna.

Best Things to Do in Hariharapura: Activities and Tours

  • Coffee plantation tour is a must-do activity in Hariharapura, providing an insight into the coffee production process and the region’s history and culture.
  • River rafting is an adventurous activity, providing a thrilling experience by navigating through the rapids.
  • Bird watching is a peaceful activity, providing an opportunity to observe a diverse range of bird species in their natural habitat.
  • Camping is a unique and memorable experience, providing an opportunity to spend a night in the wilderness, under the starlit sky.

Food and Drink Scene of Hariharapura

Must-Try Dishes

  • Bisi Bele Bath is a famous spicy rice delicacy, with lentils, vegetables, and spices.
  • Maddur Vada is a popular snack made with a combination of flours and spices and deep-fried in oil.
  • Akki Roti is a crispy bread made with rice flour and spices, roasted using a tava or griddle.
  • Masala Dosa is a famous South Indian dish, with a crispy crepe-like exterior and a soft, savory filling of potatoes and onions.

Local Beverages

  • Filter Coffee is a famous local beverage, made with dark roasted coffee beans and served with milk and sugar, providing a unique taste and aroma.
  • Coconut Water is a refreshing drink, widely available in the region, providing a perfect way to quench the thirst, especially during summer.

Where to Stay in Hariharapura: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Planters Paradise Homestay is a cozy homestay located amidst coffee plantations, providing a warm and comfortable stay and serving delicious local cuisine.
  • Nature Craft Homestay is another famous homestay, providing a serene and tranquil environment, with beautiful views of the surrounding hills and valleys.
  • Trivik Hotels and Resorts is a luxurious resort, providing world-class amenities, with panoramic views of the mountains and valleys.

What to Buy in Hariharapura: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Coffee Powder and Beans are the region’s famous products, providing a rich and unique taste of the local coffee.
  • Handicrafts and Wooden Artifacts are famous in the region, with intricate designs and carvings, reflecting the local art and culture.

Local Culture and Customs of Hariharapura

The people of Hariharapura mainly follow Hinduism and Jainism, with a unique culture and traditions, reflected through their festivals, food, music, and clothing. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and visitors should respect the local culture and customs.

Best Time to Visit Hariharapura

Hariharapura can be visited throughout the year, but the best time to visit is from September to May, with pleasant weather, clear skies, and less rainfall.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hariharapura

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Carry adequate water, snacks, and first aid while trekking and hiking.
  • Bargain while shopping in local markets.

Don’t:

  • Dispose of trash and litter in public places.
  • Wear revealing or disrespectful clothing in religious places.
  • Hurt or harm the local flora and fauna.

Safety and Health

Safety in Hariharapura

Hariharapura is a safe and peaceful destination, with a low crime rate. Visitors should take care of their belongings and avoid going out alone at night.

Health in Hariharapura

Visitors should carry adequate medication and insect repellent, as the region is known for mosquito-borne illnesses. Visitors should also ensure they have adequate health insurance coverage and carry necessary vaccinations.

Getting to Hariharapura: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Hariharapura

  • Mangalore International Airport is the closest international airport, located 142 km away from Hariharapura.
  • Kempegowda International Airport is another famous airport, located 245 km away from the village.

Bus Stations Near Hariharapura

  • Chikmagalur Bus Station is the closest bus station, located 23 km away from Hariharapura.
  • KSRTC Bus Station is another famous bus station, located 244 km away from Hariharapura.

Other Options

  • Local taxis and cabs are easily available for hire from nearby towns and cities.

How to Get Around Hariharapura

The best way to get around Hariharapura is by hiring a local taxi or cab, which is readily available, or by trekking or hiking, which is an excellent way to explore the area’s beauty intimately.

Where to Go Next from Hariharapura: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Chikmagalur is a famous hill station located 23 km away from Hariharapura, known for its stunning natural beauty and coffee plantations.
  • Shivamogga is another famous destination, located 180 km away from Hariharapura, known for its historical places and scenic beauty.
  • Coorg is a popular destination located 120 km away from Hariharapura, known for its natural beauty, wildlife, and coffee plantations.

Conclusion

Hariharapura is a beautiful and serene destination, providing a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage, making it a perfect getaway for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and photography enthusiasts. By following this comprehensive travel guide for Hariharapura, visitors can make their trip memorable, adventurous, and enjoyable while exploring the region’s true essence.
