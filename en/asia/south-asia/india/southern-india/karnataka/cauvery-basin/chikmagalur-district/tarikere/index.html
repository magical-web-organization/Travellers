Tarikere is a charming town located in the Chikmagalur district of Karnataka, Southern India. The town is nestled in the picturesque Cauvery Basin surrounded by lush green mountains and is known for its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. In this travel guide, we will take you through all the must-visit places and provide you with essential travel tips to make your trip to Tarikere an unforgettable experience.

Introduction to Tarikere

Tarikere is a small town in the Chikmagalur district of Karnataka, known for its scenic views and rich cultural heritage. The town has a rich history and is home to several historical landmarks, including temples and forts that date back to ancient times. Tarikere also offers a range of outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and trekking, where visitors can immerse themselves in nature and take in the breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

List of Places to Visit in Tarikere

Tarikere offers a range of attractions to suit all types of travelers. Here is a list of the must-visit places in Tarikere.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Basadi Halli Jain temple
  • Hoysala Kere
  • Kunda Kalamma temple
  • Tarikere Fort
  • Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary

Natural Attractions of Tarikere: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Kunda Hills
  • Manikyadhara Waterfall
  • Kemmanagundi Hill Station
  • Baba Budangiri Hills
  • Hebbe Falls

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tarikere

For adventure enthusiasts, Tarikere offers some of the best trekking and biking trails located amidst the lush green hills and valleys. Some of the popular hiking/biking routes in Tarikere include:

  • Kunda Hills Trek
  • Kemmangundi Trek
  • Baba Budangiri Trek
  • Mullyangiri Biking Tour

Best Things to Do in Tarikere: Activities and Tours

Tarikere offers a range of activities and tours that visitors can enjoy to explore the local culture and lifestyle. Some of the best things to do in Tarikere include:

  • Visiting the local markets and shops
  • Trying local delicacies of Karnataka
  • Witnessing the cultural festivals and events
  • Participating in farming activities
  • Visiting the local tribal villages

Week-Long Itinerary for Tarikere

For visitors planning to spend a week in Tarikere, here is an itinerary that covers all the essential places and activities to make your trip memorable.

Day 1: Arrive in Tarikere and relax at the hotel or guesthouse

Day 2: Explore the local markets and shops and taste the delicious local delicacies of Tarikere

Day 3: Trek to the Kunda Hills and witness the breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys

Day 4: Visit Kemmangundi Hill Station and check out the famous Hebbe Falls

Day 5: Visit the Manikyadhara Waterfall and explore the nearby Bhadra Wildlife Sanctuary

Day 6: Participate in farming activities and learn about the local culture and lifestyle

Day 7: Depart from Tarikere and head to nearby destinations such as Chikmagalur or Hassan.

Local Culture and Customs of Tarikere

Tarikere is rich in cultural heritage and local customs that are unique to the town. Visitors should be aware and respectful of local customs and festivals, including the famous Dusshera festival, celebrated throughout the town with much joy and enthusiasm.

Best Time to Visit Tarikere

The best time to visit Tarikere is during the winter months of December to February, when the weather is pleasant, and the temperature is around 21-25 degrees Celsius.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tarikere

Do:

  • Dress modestly while visiting the temples and local villages
  • Taste the delicious local cuisine

Don’t:

Safety and Health

Visitors to Tarikere should take precautions to stay safe and healthy, including:

Safety in Tarikere

  • Stay aware of your surroundings at all times, especially when traveling alone or at night
  • Avoid walking alone in remote areas

Health in Tarikere

  • Drink only bottled water
