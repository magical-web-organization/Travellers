Introduction to Somnathapura

Somnathapura, a small village in the Mysore district of Karnataka, is known for its impressive 13th-century temple, the Chennakesava Temple. This temple, considered a masterpiece of Hoysala architecture, draws tourists from all over the world. However, Somnathapura has much more to offer than just this temple. From nature lovers to history buffs, there is something here for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Somnathapura

Here are the top attractions in Somnathapura:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Chennakesava Temple
  • Keshava Temple
  • Doddakere Maidan

Natural Attractions of Somnathapura: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Bheemeshwari Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Mekedatu Dam
  • Cauvery Fishing Camp

Hiking/Biking Routes of Somnathapura

  • Trek to Shivanasamudra Falls
  • Biking trail from Somnathapura to Mekedatu Dam

Best Things to Do in Somnathapura: Activities and Tours

  • Take a tour of the Chennakesava Temple
  • Go on a nature walk in Bheemeshwari Wildlife Sanctuary
  • Enjoy a scenic boat ride on Cauvery River

Week-Long Itinerary for Somnathapura

Day 1: Visit Chennakesava Temple Day 2: Trek to Shivanasamudra Falls Day 3: Explore Bheemeshwari Wildlife Sanctuary Day 4: Visit Keshava Temple Day 5: Enjoy a boat ride on Cauvery River Day 6: Visit Mekedatu Dam Day 7: Relax at Doddakere Maidan and go fishing

Local Culture and Customs of Somnathapura

  • Dress modestly when visiting temples and shrines.
  • Take off your shoes and hat before entering a temple.

Best Time to Visit Somnathapura

The best time to visit Somnathapura is between October and March when the weather is pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Somnathapura

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Carry enough cash as not all places accept credit cards
  • Try the local cuisine

Don’t:

  • Drink tap water
  • Smoke in public places
  • Disrespect any religious sites

Safety and Health

Safety in Somnathapura

Somnathapura is relatively safe, but it is recommended to take caution when travelling and avoid travelling alone at night.

Health in Somnathapura

It is important to carry mosquito repellent and sunscreen as there is a risk of mosquito-borne diseases and sunburn.

Getting to Somnathapura: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Somnathapura

The nearest airport to Somnathapura is Mysore Airport, which is 45 km away.

Bus Stations Near Somnathapura

The nearest bus station to Somnathapura is Srirangapatna Bus Station, which is 10 km away.

Other Options

  • Taxis and car rentals are available for hire from Mysore city
  • Trains run between Mysore and Bangalore

How to Get Around in Somnathapura

The best way to get around in Somnathapura is by hiring a taxi or renting a car.

Where to Go Next from Somnathapura: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Mysore city (35 km)
  • Bangalore (137 km)
  • Coorg (120 km)

Conclusion

Somnathapura is a beautiful village, filled with natural wonders, ancient temples, and rich cultural experiences. A trip to this charming destination is bound to create unforgettable memories.
