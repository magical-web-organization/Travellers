Introduction to Sakleshpur

Sakleshpur is a town in the Hassan district of Karnataka, India. It is known for its lush greenery, coffee plantations, waterfalls, and scenic beauty. This town is a popular destination for nature lovers, history buffs, and adventure enthusiasts. Sakleshpur is located at an altitude of 949 meters above sea level and surrounded by the Western Ghats. The town is well-connected to major cities and towns in Karnataka by road and rail.

List of Places to Visit in Sakleshpur

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you are a history enthusiast, then Sakleshpur has ample sites for you to visit.

Manjarabad Fort

Manjarabad Fort is a star-shaped fort built by Tipu Sultan in the 18th century. This fort has a unique design of a star with eight corners, and it offers magnificent views of the western ghats.

Bisle Ghat Viewpoint

Bisle Ghat ViewPoint, also known as Bisle Gudda, offers panoramic views of the Western Ghats. It is one of the best places to catch the sunrise and sunset in Sakleshpur.

Natural Attractions of Sakleshpur: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The natural beauty of Sakleshpur is breathtaking, and this town has plenty of outdoor activities that tourists can partake

Agni Gudda Hill

Agni Gudda Hill is also known as the Wangandla-Maradi range, and it offers a captivating view of the Western Ghats. The hill is famous for trekking, hiking and mountain climbing.

Jenukal Gudda

Jenukal Gudda is the second-highest peak in Karnataka and offers breathtaking views of the Western Ghats. Trekking is the only way to reach the summit of this exhilarating location.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sakleshpur

Sakleshpur offers ample opportunities for hiking and mountain biking enthusiasts.

Green Route Trek

Green Route Trek is a popular trekking spot in Sakleshpur, and it is the best way to explore the natural beauty of this town. The trek takes you across the railway line, through the Western Ghats, and over a bridge that offers a panoramic view of the forest.

Kaginahare Trek

Kaginahare Trek is a challenging trek that takes you through the dense forests and rocky terrain of the Western Ghats. The trek offers a panoramic view of the hills, forests, and waterfalls.

Best Things to Do in Sakleshpur: Activities and Tours

Sakleshpur offers a variety of activities for tourists to enjoy during their stay.

Coffee Estate Tour

Sakleshpur is known for its coffee plantations, and a coffee estate tour is a must-try experience. Visit a coffee estate, learn about the history of coffee, and taste different types of coffee.

Waterfall Tour

Sakleshpur is home to several waterfalls, and a waterfall tour is a must-try experience. Some of the most popular waterfalls to visit include the Manjehalli Waterfalls, the Bisle Waterfalls, and the Jenukallu Gudda Waterfalls.

Food and Drink Scene of Sakleshpur

Sakleshpur offers a variety of local and delicious dishes that tourists can enjoy.

Must-Try Dishes

Akki Rotti

Akki Rotti is a type of Indian Flat Bread made from rice flour. It is fried on an iron griddle and served hot with a variety of chutneys and curries.

Biryani

Biryani is a popular Indian dish that is made with rice, spices and vegetables, or meat. It is a must-try dish when visiting Sakleshpur.

Local Beverages

Coffee

Sakleshpur is known for its coffee plantations, and trying their freshly brewed coffee is a must-do activity.

Wine

Sakleshpur also has a few vineyards, which offer wine tasting tours. Local wines are a must-try beverage when visiting this town.

Where to Stay in Sakleshpur: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Sakleshpur has a wide range of accommodation options suitable for all budgets.

The Midway Jungle Resort

The Midway Jungle Resort is located amidst the Western Ghats and is perfect for those looking for a peaceful getaway. The rooms are spacious and luxurious, and the resort offers a range of outdoor activities.

The Green Path Eco Resort

The Green Path Eco Resort is a perfect place to stay for those interested in eco-tourism. The resort offers luxurious tents, cottages, and tree houses.

What to Buy in Sakleshpur: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Sakleshpur is known for its coffee, spices, and handicrafts. Buying souvenirs and local products is an excellent way to support the local community.

Coffee

Sakleshpur is famous for its coffee plantations, and buying some freshly roasted coffee beans is the perfect souvenir to bring home.

Handicrafts

Sakleshpur has a thriving handicrafts industry, and tourists can buy handmade clothes, jewelry, and home decor items.

Local Culture and Customs of Sakleshpur

Sakleshpur is a melting pot of various cultures and religions. The town has a rich history and a unique way of life.

Best Time to Visit Sakleshpur

The best time to visit Sakleshpur is during the winter months (October to March) when the weather is cool and pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sakleshpur

Here are some essential do’s and don’ts for tourists visiting Sakleshpur.

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Try the local food and beverages
  • Carry necessary medication and first-aid kits while trekking or hiking

Don’t:

  • Litter the natural surroundings
  • Wear inappropriate clothes in religious places
  • Carry any plastic items when trekking or hiking

Safety and Health

Safety in Sakleshpur

Sakleshpur is a safe town for tourists to visit, but it is always important to stay cautious and be aware of your surroundings.

Health in Sakleshpur

It is advisable to carry bottled water and necessary medication when visiting Sakleshpur. Before embarking on any physical activity, consult a doctor.

Getting to Sakleshpur: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sakleshpur

The nearest airport to Sakleshpur is the Mangalore International Airport, which is approximately 130 km away from the town.

Bus Stations Near Sakleshpur

Sakleshpur has a well-connected bus station, and buses run from all major cities in Karnataka.

Other Options

One can also hire a taxi/private car or hire a bike to reach Sakleshpur.

How to Get Around in Sakleshpur

The best way to explore Sakleshpur is to rent a bike or a car. This town also has several autorickshaws which are an affordable way of transportation.

Where to Go Next from Sakleshpur: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Sakleshpur is located in close proximity to several popular tourist destinations in Karnataka.

Chikmagalur

Chikmagalur is a popular hill station located about 60 km from Sakleshpur. This town is known for its coffee plantations, trekking and camping sites, and stunning waterfalls.

Shravanabelagola

Shravanabelagola is a historical town located about 110 km from Sakleshpur. It is famous for its ancient Jain temples, monolithic statue of Gommateshwara, and the annual Mahamastakabhisheka festival.

Conclusion

Sakleshpur is a beautiful and serene town that offers a plethora of activities suitable for everybody. This town is an ideal location for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and history buffs. With plenty of things to see, do, and explore in Sakleshpur, it is a must-visit destination on your next trip to Karnataka.
