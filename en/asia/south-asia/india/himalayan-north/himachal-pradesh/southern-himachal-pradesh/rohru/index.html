Introduction to Rohru

Located in Southern Himachal Pradesh, Rohru is a quaint little town nestled in the lap of the Himalayas. It is situated at an altitude of 1,524 meters above sea level, offering picturesque views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Rohru is known for its scenic beauty, diverse wildlife, and vibrant culture.

Rohru is a popular destination for adventure enthusiasts who come here to experience hiking, trekking, camping, and river rafting. It is also an ideal destination for those seeking tranquility and solitude amidst nature.

List of Places to Visit in Rohru

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Shikhru Devta Temple: Shikhru Devta Temple is a beautiful shrine dedicated to Lord Shikhru Devta, a local deity of Rohru. The temple is located on the top of a hill and offers stunning views of the valley.
  • Kupvi Monastery: Kupvi Monastery is one of the oldest and largest monasteries in Rohru. It is home to a large number of Buddhist monks and is a must-visit destination for those interested in Buddhism and spirituality.
  • Chanshal Pass: Chanshal Pass is a high mountain pass located at an altitude of 3,810 meters, offering panoramic views of the Himalayas. It is also an important historical site as it was a major trade route during ancient times.

Natural Attractions of Rohru: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Pabbar Valley: Pabbar Valley is a beautiful valley located in Rohru, surrounded by majestic mountains and pristine forests. It is an ideal destination for hiking and trekking, offering scenic views of the valley.
  • Chirgaon Wildlife Sanctuary: Chirgaon Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected area in Rohru, home to a diverse range of flora and fauna. It is an ideal destination for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts, offering opportunities for birdwatching and wildlife spotting.
  • Rohru Fishing Farm: Rohru Fishing Farm is a popular destination for fishing enthusiasts. It is located on the banks of the Pabbar River and is home to a large number of fish species, including trout and mahseer.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Rohru

  • Trek to Chanshal Pass: The trek to Chanshal Pass is a popular hiking route in Rohru, offering stunning views of the Himalayas and the surrounding valleys. The trek starts from Rohru and takes around 3-4 days to complete.
  • Hike to the Pabbar Valley: The hike to the Pabbar Valley is an easy trek that takes you through lush forests and pristine meadows. The trail starts from Rohru and takes around 2-3 days to complete.
  • Mountain Biking in Rohru: Rohru is a popular destination for mountain biking enthusiasts. There are several challenging routes in and around Rohru that offer breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.

Best Things to Do in Rohru: Activities and Tours

  • River Rafting in Pabbar River: Pabbar River is a popular destination for river rafting, offering thrilling experiences for adventure enthusiasts. The best time to go rafting in Pabbar River is from April to June and September to November.
  • Camping in Rohru: Rohru is an ideal destination for camping, offering opportunities to spend nights under the starry sky amidst nature. There are several camping sites in and around Rohru that provide camping equipment and facilities.
  • Village Tour of Rohru: A village tour of Rohru is a cultural experience that takes you through the local villages and gives you a glimpse into the lifestyle and traditions of the local people.

Food and Drink Scene of Rohru

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sidu: Sidu is a local delicacy of Rohru, made with wheat flour and stuffed with vegetables, meat, or lentils. It is served with ghee or chutney and is a must-try dish for food lovers.
  • Sepu Badi: Sepu Badi is a traditional dish of Rohru, made with lentil dumplings and a tangy gravy. It is a popular dish among the locals and is a must-try dish for those who love spicy food.

Local Beverages

  • Gahat Ki Daal ka Sharbat: Gahat Ki Daal ka Sharbat is a refreshing local drink made with horse gram and flavoured with spices. It is a popular summer drink in Rohru and is said to have cooling properties.
  • Apple Cider: Rohru is known for its high-quality apple production, and apple cider is a popular local beverage. It is made by fermenting apple juice and is a must-try drink for those who love cider.

Where to Stay in Rohru: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Rohru Homestay: Rohru Homestay is a cozy and comfortable guesthouse located in the heart of Rohru. It offers traditional Himachali hospitality and is a popular choice among budget travelers.
  • Hotel Sunrise: Hotel Sunrise is a modern and luxurious hotel located in Rohru. It offers comfortable rooms, modern amenities, and stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys.
  • Chanshal Nature Resort: Chanshal Nature Resort is a unique eco-resort located in the Chanshal Valley. It offers luxurious accommodation options, outdoor activities, and sustainable tourism practices.

What to Buy in Rohru: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handmade Shawls: Rohru is known for its high-quality wool production, and handmade shawls are a popular local product. They are available in a variety of colours and designs and are perfect for gifting or bringing home as a souvenir.
  • Local Honey: Rohru is known for its high-quality honey production, and local honey is a popular local product. It is available in different flavours and is said to have medicinal properties.

Local Culture and Customs of Rohru

Rohru is known for its vibrant cultural traditions, and the locals are known for their warm hospitality. The traditional dance forms of Rohru, such as Nati and Bihu, are popular cultural attractions, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the region.

Best Time to Visit Rohru

The best time to visit Rohru is from April to June and September to November when the weather is pleasant and perfect for outdoor activities. The monsoon season, from July to August, brings heavy rainfall, making it difficult to indulge in outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rohru

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Dress appropriately, especially while visiting the religious places.
  • Enjoy the local food and beverages.
  • Try to learn some local phrases to communicate with the locals.

Don’t:

  • Litter or pollute the surroundings.
  • Disrespect the local culture and customs.
  • Disturb the local wildlife and environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Rohru

Rohru is a safe destination to travel to. However, it is advisable to take precautions and follow safety guidelines.

  • Avoid travelling alone, especially at night.
  • Keep your valuables and important documents safe.
  • Follow safety guidelines and instructions while indulging in outdoor activities.

Health in Rohru

Rohru is a high-altitude destination, and it is important to acclimatize properly to avoid altitude sickness. It is advisable to carry necessary medication and consult a doctor before traveling to Rohru.

Getting to Rohru: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Rohru

The nearest airport to Rohru is Chandigarh International Airport, located at a distance of 161 km. Regular flights connect Chandigarh to major cities in India.

Bus Stations Near Rohru

Rohru is well-connected to major cities in Himachal Pradesh by regular bus services. The Shimla Bus Stand is the nearest major bus station to Rohru.

Other Options

One can also hire a taxi or self-drive to reach Rohru. However, the roads are narrow and winding, and it is advisable to be cautious while driving.

How to Get Around in Rohru

One can hire a taxi or use local buses to get around in Rohru. However, the best way to explore the nearby areas is to walk or hike.

Where to Go Next from Rohru: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Shimla: Shimla is a popular hill station located at a distance of 115 km from Rohru. It is known for its colonial architecture, lush green surroundings, and pleasant climate.
  • Kufri: Kufri is a scenic hill station located at a distance of 125 km from Rohru. It is known for its snow-capped mountains, skiing, and scenic beauty.
  • Rampur Bushahr: Rampur Busahr is a quaint little town located at a distance of 84 km from Rohru. It is known for its cultural heritage and is home to several temples and historical buildings.

Conclusion

Rohru is a hidden gem in Southern Himachal Pradesh, offering a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and cultural richness. It is an ideal destination for those seeking solitude and tranquility in the lap of nature. Plan your trip to Rohru and discover the beauty that lies in this quaint little town nestled in the Himalayas.
