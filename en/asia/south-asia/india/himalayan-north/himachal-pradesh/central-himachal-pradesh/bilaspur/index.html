Introduction to Bilaspur

Bilaspur is a magnificent city located in the central part of Himachal Pradesh state of India. The town of Bilaspur is considered as the first planned hill town of the country. The town, situated on the banks of River Sutlej, boasts of rich history and culture, breathtaking views, and numerous tourist attractions.

Here are some of the top places to visit in Bilaspur:

Best Things to Do in Bilaspur: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Bilaspur:

  • Visit the Bhakra Dam: A guided tour
  • Go boating at Gobind Sagar Lake.
  • Explore the Vyas Cave, known for its mythological significance.
  • Attend the Annual International Hill Surfing Festival
  • Dive into the devotional and cultural aspect of Bilaspur by attending the Naina Devi Mela.

Food and Drink Scene of Bilaspur

Must-Try Dishes

Bilaspur houses numerous food joints serving lip-smacking cuisines to every visitor. Make sure to try the following dishes while exploring Bilaspur:

  • Mittha – A sweet dish made with rice, coconut, and Sugar
  • Shabnam– An appetiser made with mushrooms and a tangy sauce.
  • Sepu badi– Lentil dumpling curry
  • Siddu – Wheat bread stuffed with vegetables, meat or cheese
  • Chha Gosht– Goat curry dish cooked in yoghurt.

Local Beverages

Bilaspur is known for its locally brewed alcoholic drink called ‘Desi’. It is a mildly fermented drink made from jaggery or dates. You can find it at every local bar or at the roadside shacks.

Local Culture and Customs of Bilaspur

Bilaspur has an ancient cultural heritage that reflects in its traditions, customs and festivals. It is home to a warm and hospitable community that welcomes every traveller with open arms. The prevalent language in Bilaspur is Hindi and Pahari.

Best Time to Visit Bilaspur

The best time to visit Bilaspur is from March to June and then again from September to December as the weather is favourable for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bilaspur

Do:

  • Respect the dress code of the temples while visiting them
  • Try to exchange greetings with the locals in their language
  • Always carry and drink bottled water.

Don’t:

  • Don’t indulge in drugs or any illegal activity.
  • Don’t harm any flora or fauna.
  • Don’t click pictures if it offends the locals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bilaspur

As a tourist, one should remain vigilant while traveling in Bilaspur. Avoid traveling alone in late-night hours, and always keep your belongings safe. Additionally, it’s better to avoid excessive drinking as well.

Health in Bilaspur

Carry a personal first-aid kit that includes typical medicines apart from prescribed medicines. Insect repellent cream is a must in your bag. Drink only bottled water and stay hydrated.

Getting to Bilaspur: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bilaspur

The nearest airport to Bilaspur is Chandigarh International Airport, located at a distance of approximately 123 km.

Bus Stations Near Bilaspur

Bilaspur is connected to other cities through a well-established network of buses. The Bilaspur Bus Stand is situated at the heart of the town.

Other Options

The nearest railway station to Bilaspur is Kiratpur Sahib Railway Station, located at a distance of approximately 60 km.

How to Get Around in Bilaspur

Taxis, bikes, and buses are the most common means of transportation available in Bilaspur, and are affordable and convenient options for traveling around.

Where to Go Next from Bilaspur: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions from Bilaspur:

  • Shimla – 112 km
  • Chandigarh – 123 km
  • Kasauli – 89 km
  • Solan – 82 km

Conclusion

Bilaspur is a hidden gem of Himachal Pradesh that should not be missed on any Himachal itinerary. With its unique culture, natural beauty, and adventurous streaks, Bilaspur offers an impressively diverse experience to travellers.
