Introduction to Kangra (district)

Kangra is a beautiful district located in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh, situated in the southern part of the Himalayan range. Home to many lakes, rivers, and valleys, Kangra is renowned for its stunning natural panoramas, and rich cultural heritage. The district is dotted with temples, monasteries, and forts, that serve as a testament to its glorious past. If you’re planning to visit Kangra, this travel guide will give you a rundown of its top attractions, activities and much more.

Local Culture and Customs of Kangra (district)

The local culture of Kangra is heavily influenced by its Hindu and Buddhist population. The region is known for its traditional folk music and dance, such as the Nati, which is performed during agricultural festivals, and the Gaddi dance which is performed during weddings. Visitors are encouraged to respect local customs and dress modestly when visiting temples and other religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Kangra (district)

The best time to visit Kangra is between September to November and March to June when the weather is mild and pleasant. The monsoon season from July to September is not recommended for traveling to Kangra as the heavy rainfalls can cause landslides and roadblocks.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kangra (district)

Kangra is generally a safe place, but visitors should exercise caution when traveling alone at night, or when trekking in the mountains. It’s advisable to carry a first-aid kit and insect repellent when visiting rural areas, and to avoid drinking tap water.

Health in Kangra (district)

Visitors should be aware of the high altitude of the region and take necessary precautions to avoid altitude sickness. It’s also advisable to carry adequate medication and vaccines when traveling to Kangra.

Getting to Kangra (district): Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Kangra (district)

The Kangra airport, also known as Gaggal airport, is the nearest airport to Kangra. It’s located approximately 10 kilometers from Dharamshala, the major city of Kangra district. The airport is well-connected to major Indian cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore.

Bus Stations Near Kangra (district)

Kangra has a well-established bus network with regular services connecting it to major cities of Himachal Pradesh, Punjab, and Delhi. The major bus stations in Kangra are located in Dharamshala, and Kangra town.

Other Options

Taxis and rental cars are readily available in Kangra, and it’s possible to hire a taxi from the airport or major bus stations. Some of the major highways connecting Kangra district to other parts of the region are well-maintained, and self-driving is possible if you’re an experienced driver.

How to Get Around in Kangra (district)

Kangra is a hilly region with winding roads and steep gradients, making it a challenging place to drive. Hiring a taxi or taking a bus is the most convenient option for getting around Kangra. Many local tour companies offer guided tours of the region that include transportation.
