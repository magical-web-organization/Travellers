Introduction to Auli

Auli is a picturesque skiing destination located in the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand, India. This Himalayan town attracts nature enthusiasts and adventure seekers with its scenic vistas, outdoor activities, and unique cultural experiences. Auli is also home to the Auli Ski Resort, which offers world-class skiing and snowboarding adventures. The town is accessible via the 4.5 km-long cable car ride that starts at Joshimath, and it takes around 25 minutes to reach Auli.

Local Culture and Customs of Auli

Auli is a predominantly Hindu town, and visitors should respect the local customs and traditions. The town celebrates several festivals, including the Badrinath Kedar Festival, Nanda Devi Festival, and the Holi Festival. Dress modestly when visiting temples or cultural sites, and remove your shoes when entering religious places.

Best Time to Visit Auli

The ideal time to visit Auli is from November to March when the town is covered with a thick blanket of snow, ideal for skiing and snowboarding. Summers (April to June) are pleasant and perfect for sightseeing and adventure activities. The monsoon season (July to September) should be avoided as the region experiences heavy rainfall and landslides.

Safety and Health

Safety in Auli

Auli is a safe town, but visitors are advised to exercise caution and stay alert while traveling alone or late at night. Always carry a first-aid kit and emergency contact details with you.

Health in Auli

Carry your prescribed medication, sunscreen, and insect repellent while traveling to Auli. Visitors should also carry important documents, including their medical history, insurance information, and identification.

Getting to Auli: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Auli

  • Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun (286 km away)

Bus Stations Near Auli

  • Joshimath Bus Stand (16 km away)
  • Rishikesh Bus Stand (251 km away)

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a private taxi or a car from nearby towns like Rishikesh and Dehradun to reach Auli.

How to Get Around in Auli

Visitors can explore Auli on foot, and auto-rickshaws or taxis can be hired for local travel. The Auli Ski Resort offers shuttle services for skiing and snowboarding enthusiasts.
