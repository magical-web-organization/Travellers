Introduction to Tehri

Tehri is a beautiful town located in the Garhwal district of Uttarakhand in India. It is situated in the Himalayan North and is known for its scenic views of the mountains and valleys. The town is surrounded by the beautiful Bhagirathi River and is popular among tourists for its adventure activities, natural beauty, and cultural richness.

List of Places to Visit in Tehri

Tehri offers a lot of attractions for tourists. Here is a list of some of the top tourist destinations in Tehri:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tehri Dam
  • Old Tehri
  • Khatling Glacier
  • Chandrabadni Temple
  • Divya Shila

Natural Attractions of Tehri: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Dhanaulti
  • Surkanda Devi Temple
  • Chamba
  • Kanatal
  • Tehri Lake

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tehri

Tehri is a hub for adventure activities and offers a lot of hiking and biking trails for tourists. Some of the popular hiking/biking routes in Tehri are:

  • Nag Tibba Trek
  • Kuari Pass Trek
  • Kedarkantha Trek
  • Bhrigu Lake Trek
  • Roopkund Trek

Best Things to Do in Tehri: Activities and Tours

Tehri has a lot of adventure activities and tours to offer for tourists. Some of the best things to do in Tehri are:

  • Boating and Kayaking in Tehri Lake
  • Trekking and Camping in the Himalayas
  • Mountain Biking in the hills
  • Rafting in the Bhagirathi River
  • Visiting local markets and traditional festivals

What to Buy in Tehri: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Tehri is famous for its local handicrafts, woolen clothing, and souvenir shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Tehri

The local culture of Tehri is influenced by the Himalayan region and is rich with traditions, music, and dance. Visitors must be respectful of these customs and traditions while in Tehri.

Best Time to Visit Tehri

The best time to visit Tehri is from March to June and September to December. The weather during these months is pleasant, and tourists can enjoy adventure activities and outdoor sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tehri

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Carry your jackets, sweaters, and woollen clothes if visiting during cold weather.
  • Participate in the local fairs and festivals.
  • Carry a sturdy pair of shoes for adventure activities.

Don’t:

  • Litter in the town or the hills.
  • Wear revealing clothes in religious places.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tehri

Tehri is a safe town, but tourists must avoid traveling alone in secluded areas. It is best to be accompanied by a guide when trekking and hiking in the hills.

Health in Tehri

Tourists must carry their medication and basic first aid kit while visiting Tehri. It is advisable to carry some warm clothes during winters.

Getting to Tehri: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tehri

The nearest airport to Tehri is Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun, which is around 93 km away.

Bus Stations Near Tehri

Tehri is well connected by road, and tourists can travel to Tehri via buses from many nearby cities, including Delhi, Haridwar, and Rishikesh.

Other Options

Taxis and private cabs are also available from nearby cities and are a popular mode of transportation.

How to Get Around in Tehri

Tourists can rent bikes and scooters to explore the nearby areas, or hire a private cab or taxi for a more comfortable ride.

Where to Go Next from Tehri: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some nearby destinations and excursions that tourists can plan to visit are:

  • Rishikesh
  • Haridwar
  • Dehradun
  • Mussoorie

Conclusion

Tehri is a beautiful town that offers a mix of outdoor adventures, cultural richness, and scenic beauty. Visitors can plan a trip with this comprehensive travel guide and explore the best of Tehri.
