Uttarkashi is a small and serene hill town located in the Garhwal region of Uttarakhand state in India. The town is situated on the banks of the River Bhagirathi, which is one of the two main tributaries of River Ganga. Uttarkashi is known for its cultural heritage, natural beauty, and outdoor recreational activities. If you’re planning a trip to Uttarkashi, here’s our comprehensive travel guide that will help you make the most out of your visit.

Introduction to Uttarkashi

Uttarkashi is a town located in the foothills of the Himalayas in northern India. The town is a significant religious and pilgrimage center and is a gateway to several popular Himalayan pilgrimage sites. It is also known for its natural beauty and outdoor recreational activities.

Local Culture and Customs of Uttarkashi

The people of Uttarkashi are predominantly Hindus and follow the customs and traditions of the Hindu religion. Locals are friendly and enjoy sharing the local customs and culture with visitors.

Best Time to Visit Uttarkashi

The best time to visit Uttarkashi is between April to June and September to December. The weather during these times is pleasant, with temperatures ranging from 10°C to 30°C.

Do’s and Don’ts of Uttarkashi

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions
  • Take off your shoes before entering religious places
  • Dress modestly while visiting religious places
  • Carry enough warm clothes if visiting in winters

Don’t:

  • Litter around or pollute the environment
  • Damage or deface any religious or natural site

Safety and Health

Uttarkashi is generally a safe place to visit. However, some precautions must be taken while visiting.

Safety in Uttarkashi

  • Avoid unknown and secluded areas after sunset
  • Keep the emergency numbers with you always

Health in Uttarkashi

  • Carry enough drinking water with you
  • Avoid street food and drink only filtered water

Getting to Uttarkashi: Transportation Options and Logistics

Uttarkashi is well-connected to major Indian cities via air, rail, and road networks. The nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport in Dehradun, which is about 160 km away.

Airports Near Uttarkashi

  • Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun

Bus Stations Near Uttarkashi

  • Uttarkashi Bus Station

Other Options

  • Taxis and private cars

How to Get Around in Uttarkashi

The best way to explore Uttarkashi is on foot. However, local transportation options include buses, taxis, and rented vehicles.
