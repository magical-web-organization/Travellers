Introduction to Naukuchiatal

Naukuchiatal is a small town located in the Kumaon region of Uttarakhand, India. Known for its serene lake, beautiful countryside, and picturesque views, the town has become a popular destination for travelers seeking a peaceful getaway. The name “Naukuchiatal” translates to “nine-cornered lake” and refers to the beautiful lake that the town is built around.

List of Places to Visit in Naukuchiatal

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Bhimtal Lake - This is the largest lake in the Kumaon region and is a popular destination for boating and fishing.
  • Nainital Lake - This is a beautiful lake located in the heart of Nainital and is surrounded by scenic views of the Himalayas.
  • Nainital Zoo - This is a large zoo that is home to a variety of animals, including tigers, leopards, and bears.
  • Nanda Devi Temple - This is a famous temple located in the heart of Naukuchiatal and is known for its beautiful architecture and important religious significance.

Natural Attractions of Naukuchiatal: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hanumangarhi Temple - This is a beautiful temple located on a hilltop and offers stunning views of the surrounding countryside.
  • Sunset Point - A beautiful vantage point to watch the sunset over the stunning Naukuchiatal Lake.
  • Kayaking and Canoeing at Naukuchiatal Lake - Enjoy canoeing down the serene waters of Naukuchiatal Lake and taking in the breathtaking views.
  • Bird Watching - Naukuchiatal is home to a wide variety of bird species, including the Himalayan eagle, which can be spotted at various points around the town.
  • Paragliding - Paraglide from one of the highest peaks in Naukuchiatal and experience a breathtaking view of the town from above.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Naukuchiatal

  • Kilbury Trek - This is a beautiful trek located at an altitude of 2194 meters and offers panoramic views of the Himalayas.
  • Hanumangarhi Trek - A moderate level trek, this route takes you through forest and villages while offering beautiful views of the valley and surrounding hills.
  • Jeep Safari to Pangot - Experience the beautiful wildlife of the region while exploring through a Jeep safari to Pangot, a nearby village known for its beautiful forest covers.
  • Chakrata Trek - This trek is a beautiful route that takes you through various forests and waterfalls, along with offering beautiful views of the valley and surrounding hills.

Best Things to Do in Naukuchiatal: Activities and Tours

  • Boating in Naukuchiatal Lake - Rent a boat or go on a guided tour around Naukuchiatal Lake and experience beautiful views of the surrounding hills.
  • Visit the local market - The local market at Naukuchiatal is full of beautiful handicrafts, locally made jewelry, and hand-woven clothes that make for beautiful gifts.
  • Try adventurous activities - Try adventurous activities like rock climbing, rappelling, zip lining, and camping in the beautiful vicinity of Naukuchiatal.

Food and Drink Scene of Naukuchiatal

Must-Try Dishes

  • Bhang Ki Chutney - This is a popular chutney made with a local herb, Bhang, which is used in various dishes throughout Uttarakhand.
  • Aloo Ke Gutke - This is a popular snack made with local potatoes, and is a must-try while exploring the town.
  • Phaanu - A lentil soup made with Urad and Arhar lentils , Phaanu, is a popular delicacy in Uttarakhand.

Local Beverages

  • Gahat Ki Dal - A lentil drink made from locally sourced lentils and spices.
  • Bhatt ki Churkani - A drink made from black beans, which is also known for its medicinal properties.

Where to Stay in Naukuchiatal: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • The Lake Resort - A luxurious hotel situated on the lakefront, offering beautiful views of the lake and surrounding mountains.
  • Jungle Lore Birding Lodge - A beautifully designed lodge located in the heart of the town and offering a peaceful stay in the midst of natural beauty.
  • Ghoomakad - A unique eco-friendly property with rustic interiors and beautiful views of the surrounding hills, this place offers a unique experience of living in the midst of nature.

What to Buy in Naukuchiatal: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handwoven Shawls - The local market is full of beautiful handwoven shawls made with local wool, which make for beautiful gifts.
  • Wooden Handicrafts - The town is famous for its beautiful wooden handicrafts, made by local artisans and woodworkers.
  • Locally Produced Honey - Uttrakhand is famous for its honey, and the local market at Naukuchiatal offers an abundance of locally produced honey.

Local Culture and Customs of Naukuchiatal

With a rich history and culture, the town of Naukuchiatal offers a unique blend of customs and traditions. The local population here is mostly Hindu, and cows are considered sacred. It is important to respect their beliefs and customs during your visit here.

Best Time to Visit Naukuchiatal

The best time to visit Naukuchiatal is from March to June, and from September to December, when the weather is pleasant and the skies are clear.

Do’s and Don’ts of Naukuchiatal

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs while exploring the town.
  • Try the local food and beverages which belong to the region.
  • Go on a guided tour while exploring the town to learn about its history and unique culture.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter in public places.
  • Do not disrespect the local population or their beliefs and customs.
  • Do not disturb the natural wildlife of the region.

Safety and Health

Safety in Naukuchiatal

Naukuchiatal is a peaceful and safe town, but it is always recommended to take precautions while exploring the town. Make sure to carry enough cash and keep important documents like passports and ID proof in a safe place.

Health in Naukuchiatal

The town has good medical facilities, and there are various hospitals and clinics that are well-equipped to handle any medical emergencies.

Getting to Naukuchiatal: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Naukuchiatal

The nearest airport to Naukuchiatal is the Pantnagar airport, which is located around 64 km away from the town.

Bus Stations Near Naukuchiatal

The town is well-connected by road and has a good network of buses that connect it to various nearby cities and towns.

Other Options

The town can be easily accessed by car or taxi from various nearby cities, including Nainital and Dehradun.

How to Get Around in Naukuchiatal

The town is small and easy to navigate on foot. However, you can also rent a bike or a car to explore the surroundings.

Where to Go Next from Naukuchiatal: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Nainital - A popular hill station located just 5 km away, known for its beautiful lake and stunning views.
  • Bhimtal - A small town located around 22 km away from Naukuchiatal, known for its scenic lake and beautiful countryside.
  • Kausani - A beautiful hill station located around 90 km away from Naukuchiatal, which is famous for its stunning views of the Himalayas.

Conclusion

Naukuchiatal is a beautiful town located in Uttarakhand, India, which offers a unique blend of natural beauty, adventure activities, and cultural attractions for travelers. From exploring the beautiful lake to hiking the stunning mountains, there is something for everyone in this serene little town.
