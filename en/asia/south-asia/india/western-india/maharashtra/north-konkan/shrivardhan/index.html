Introduction to Shrivardhan

Shrivardhan, a small town in the North Konkan region of Maharashtra, India, is a hidden gem that is known for its serene beaches, pristine natural beauty, and interesting historical sites. It is an ideal destination for tourists who are looking for a peaceful and relaxing beach holiday away from the hustle and bustle of busy cities.

List of Places to Visit in Shrivardhan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Lord Shiva Temple - Built in the 17th century, this ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva is a famous pilgrimage site.
  2. Kusuma Devi Temple - A tranquil temple with beautiful surroundings and a peaceful ambiance, it is a must-visit destination.
  3. Peshwa Smarak - A monument dedicated to Peshwa Balaji Vishwanath, located in the heart of Shrivardhan, it is worth visiting to get a glimpse of the history.

Natural Attractions of Shrivardhan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Shrivardhan Beach - This serene beach with crystal-clear water is a perfect place to relax and unwind.
  2. Harihareshwar Beach - Located at the mouth of the river Savitri, this beach is known for its stunning views of the Arabian Sea and mesmerizing sunrises and sunsets.
  3. Diveagar Beach - Famous for its picturesque beauty, clean sand, and calm waters, it’s an ideal spot for families and couples.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Shrivardhan

  1. Velas Beach to Anjarle Beach - A picturesque hike that takes you through a beautiful nature trail along the coastline.
  2. Shrivardhan to Harihareshwar - Known for its scenic beauty, this 30 km hike takes you through the dense woods of Konkan region.

Best Things to Do in Shrivardhan: Activities and Tours

  1. Watersports Activities - Jet Skiing, Paragliding, Banana Boat ride, and other adventurous water sports, are available in Shrivardhan and Diveagar.
  2. Underwater Diving - Explore the underwater world with certified divers.
  3. Cultural Sightseeing Tours - Explore the stunning architecture of ancient temples and historical buildings in the town with knowledgeable guides.

Week-Long Itinerary for Shrivardhan

Day Activities
1 Arrive and relax on Shrivardhan beach

Food and Drink Scene of Shrivardhan

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Konkani fish curry - A spicy and flavorful dish that is made with fresh fish and a blend of spices.
  2. Sol Kadhi - A refreshing drink made of Kokam, coconut milk, and green chilies
  3. Amboli - A soft and fluffy pancake made from rice batter.

Local Beverages

  1. Kokum juice - A healthy drink made from Kokum fruit.
  2. Cashew Feni - A popular alcoholic drink made from fermented cashew juice.

Where to Stay in Shrivardhan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  3. SeaWind Beach Resort.

What to Buy in Shrivardhan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Sea Shells - Shrivardhan beaches are famous for their shells that make for great souvenirs.
  2. Coconut oil - A popular product that is extracted from coconuts grown in the region.

Local Culture and Customs of Shrivardhan

The Konkani culture of the region is famous for its hospitality and warmth. It is customary to remove footwear before entering a temple or a local’s house. Also, the region is famous for its unique temple architecture.

Best Time to Visit Shrivardhan

The best time to visit Shrivardhan is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and ideal for exploring the region.

Do’s and Don’ts of Shrivardhan

Do:

  1. Respect local customs and traditions.
  2. Carry sunscreen lotion, as the sun can be harsh.
  3. Try the Konkan cuisine for a unique culinary experience.

Don’t:

  1. Litter on the beach or other public places.
  2. Ignore safety instructions while indulging in water sports activities.
  3. Photograph the locals or their houses without permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Shrivardhan

  1. Always follow safety instructions while indulging in water sports activities
  2. Beware of tides while swimming in the sea.
  3. Avoid visiting remote areas after dark.

Health in Shrivardhan

  1. Carry insect repellent as the region is known for its mosquitoes and other insects.
  2. Always carry a first-aid kit, as the area is relatively remote.

Getting to Shrivardhan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Shrivardhan

  1. Pune International Airport - 139 km away from Shrivardhan.
  2. Mumbai International Airport - 185 km away from Shrivardhan.

Bus Stations Near Shrivardhan

  2. Diveagar Bus Stop

Other Options

  1. Shared cabs and taxis
  2. Private car

How to Get Around in Shrivardhan

Auto-rickshaws and taxis are the primary modes of transportation in Shrivardhan.
