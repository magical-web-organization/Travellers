Introduction to Mahad

Mahad is a small town located in North Konkan, Maharashtra, Western India. The town is known for its significant role in the Indian Independence movement as it witnessed the historic “Chavdar Tank Satyagraha” led by Dr. B.R. Ambedkar. Today, Mahad is a peaceful town that boasts of several cultural and natural attractions that attract both domestic and international travelers.

In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you through everything you need to know about Mahad - from places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in Mahad

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Dr. Ambedkar Memorial

Dr. Ambedkar Memorial is a significant place for Indian history and is dedicated to Dr. Bhimrao Ambedkar, the father of the Indian Constitution. The memorial houses important artifacts, manuscripts, and photographs about Dr. Ambedkar’s life and work.

Raigad Fort

Raigad Fort is a historical fortress that was the capital of the Maratha Kingdom. It has significant historical importance as it was the site where Chhatrapati Shivaji formally ascended the throne and was crowned the king of the Marathi kingdom.

Natural Attractions of Mahad: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Mahad Dam

Mahad Dam is one of the prominent dams in Maharashtra that serves as a major source of water supply to parts of the state. The picturesque location with lush greenery and hills around make it an ideal spot for a picnic and a serene getaway.

Tamhini Ghat

Tamhini Ghat is the most popular and scenic route that connects Mumbai to Pune. It is ideal for a long drive or a bike ride, especially during monsoons when the ghat is in full bloom with waterfalls all around.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mahad

Mahad is known for its hiking and biking trails that offer an adventurous getaway to nature enthusiasts.

Raigad Fort Trek

Raigad Fort Trek is one of the most popular treks in the Sahyadri mountain range. The trek is moderately challenging and is approximately 4 hours long, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and forests.

Shivthar Ghal Trek

Shivthar Ghal Trek is a challenging trek that takes you to the hidden gem of Mahad- the Shivthar Ghal. The trek passes through dense forests and offers panoramic views of the Sahyadri range.

Best Things to Do in Mahad: Activities and Tours

Rafting on the Kundalika River

Kundalika River is a popular spot for rafting in Maharashtra. The rapids of the river stretch up to 14 km, and the duration of the activity is around 3 hours.

Shopping at Mahad’s Local Bazaar

Mahad bazaar is famous for its street shopping that offers local items and souvenirs, including traditional silk sarees, wood-carved items, and handloom products.

Food and Drink Scene of Mahad

Must-Try Dishes

Misal Pav

Misal Pav is a popular Maharashtrian breakfast item that consists of sprouts curry topped with farsan and served with bread.

Ukadiche Modak

Ukadiche Modak is a sweet delicacy that is made during the Ganesh festival in Maharashtra. It is a steamed dumpling stuffed with jaggery and coconut filling.

Local Beverages

Sol Kadhi

Sol Kadhi is a refreshing drink made with kokum and coconut milk. It is an ideal drink to beat the heat during summers.

Kokum Sharbat

Kokum Sharbat is a beverage made from kokum, a fruit that grows in Western India.

Where to Stay in Mahad: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Mahad offers various options to its visitors to stay; from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxurious hotels.

Budget-Friendly Options

Hotel Konkan Swami

Hotel Konkan Swami is a budget-friendly option located near Mahad bus stand. The hotel offers clean and spacious rooms with basic amenities.

Hotel Pooja Residency

Hotel Pooja Residency is another budget-friendly hotel located near Mahad bus stand. The hotel boasts clean, modern rooms with essential amenities.

Luxury Options

The Fern Residency

The Fern Residency is a lavish hotel that offers luxurious rooms and suites with modern amenities. It is located near Mahad’s famous attractions.

The Corinthians Resort and Club

The Corinthians Resort and Club is a luxurious resort spread over 25 acres of lush greenery. The resort features several facilities, including an outdoor swimming pool, spa, and multi-cuisine restaurants.

What to Buy in Mahad: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Silk Sarees

Mahad is famous for its traditional silk sarees that are a must-buy for any visitor. The sarees come in vibrant colors and intricate designs.

Wood-Carved Items

Mahad is known for its wood-carved items like furniture, kitchen ware, and other decorative pieces.

Local Culture and Customs of Mahad

Mahad is a small town with a primarily Hindu population. Visitors are expected to dress modestly and respect the local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Mahad

Mahad is an all-year destination. The best time to visit is during winter (November to February) when the weather is pleasant, making it the ideal time to explore the natural attractions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mahad

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions
  • Dress modestly
  • Try the local cuisine

Don’t:

  • Smoke or consume alcohol in public places
  • Enter the religious sites with footwear
  • Litter or harm the environment

Safety and Health

Safety in Mahad

Mahad is a relatively safe town with low crime rates. Travelers should take necessary precautions like avoiding deserted areas, carrying essentials with them, and staying alert while on solo expeditions.

Health in Mahad

Mahad has a good medical infrastructure with several clinics and hospitals, along with reputed private hospitals. However, travelers should follow basic hygiene practices like drinking bottled water, washing hands frequently, and carrying essential medicines.

Getting to Mahad: Transportation Options and Logistics

Mahad is well connected via roads, airways, and railways.

Airports Near Mahad

The nearest airport to Mahad is Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport, Mumbai, which is approximately 140 km away.

Bus Stations Near Mahad

Mahad has a bus stand that connects it to major cities and towns in Maharashtra like Mumbai, Pune, and Kolhapur.

Other Options

Mahad is located on the Konkan Railway line, and the nearest railway station is Roha Railway Station (45 km away).

How to Get Around in Mahad

The best way to get around Mahad is by hiring a private cab, auto-rickshaw, or a motorcycle. Visitors can also use public buses for shorter distances.

Where to Go Next from Mahad: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Mahad is situated in a region with several popular destinations that are ideal for day trips or weekend getaways.

Harihareshwar

Harihareshwar is a tranquil beach town located approximately 30 km from Mahad. The town is known for its clean beaches, vibrant temples, and scenic locations.

Shrivardhan

Shrivardhan is a popular destination located approximately 50 km from Mahad. The town boasts of historical significance and natural beauty.

Conclusion

Mahad is a hidden gem in Maharashtra that boasts of cultural and natural attractions. With its rich history, delicious cuisine, and scenic locations, Mahad makes an ideal getaway for solo travelers, families, and groups.
