Introduction to North

North is a region located in the north of Mumbai, in North Konkan, Maharashtra, in western India. It is an area of picturesque hills and verdant forests, bordered by the Arabian Sea to the west, and encompassing some of the most charming towns and villages in the region. From pristine beaches to historical buildings, from natural wonders to delicious food, North is a rich and diverse destination that is worth exploring.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

North has a rich history, and some of the cultural attractions worth visiting here are:

Kolaba Fort

Located in the town of Alibaug, Kolaba Fort is a 300-year-old fort built by the Maratha ruler, Shivaji. It is an excellent example of ancient Indian architecture and engineering, and boasts stunning views of the Arabian Sea.

Sawantwadi Palace

The Sawantwadi Palace is an intricate and beautiful example of Indian style architecture. Built in the 18th century, the palace is adorned with woodcarvings and intricate paintings.

Natural Attractions of North: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

From lush green forests to unspoiled beaches, North has plenty of natural wonders to offer.

Karnala Bird Sanctuary

Located near the village of Karnala, this bird sanctuary is home to several rare species of birds. You can enjoy bird-watching here, or take a trek to the Karnala Fort located within the sanctuary.

Vengurla Beach

Located at the southern end of the region, Vengurla Beach is a beautiful place to relax and enjoy the sunset. You can also take a stroll along the beach and try some of the local delicacies sold by the vendors.

Hiking/Biking Routes of North

North is an ideal location for nature lovers, and there are plenty of hiking and biking trails to explore.

Kanakeshwar Forest

Kanakeshwar Forest is home to a wide variety of flora and fauna, and is a great place for trekking and bird-watching. It is also home to the Kanakeshwar Temple, which is a popular pilgrimage site.

Best Things to Do in North: Activities and Tours

Apart from exploring the natural and cultural attractions of North, there are plenty of other activities and tours to consider.

Boat Ride to Murud-Janjira Fort

Murud-Janjira Fort is located on an island, and the best way to reach is by a boat ride. The fort is an architectural marvel, and is worth the trip.

Week-Long Itinerary for North

Here’s a week-long itinerary to help you explore North:

  • Day 1: Arrival and check-in at a hotel in Alibaug
  • Day 2: Visit Kolaba Fort and Alibaug Beach
  • Day 3: Trek to Karnala Fort and spend the afternoon at Karnala Bird Sanctuary
  • Day 4: Travel to Vengurla Beach and enjoy the sunset
  • Day 5: Hiking at Kanakeshwar Forest and visit to the Kanakeshwar Temple
  • Day 6: Boat ride to Murud-Janjira Fort and visit to the nearby beaches.
  • Day 7: Shopping for local souvenirs before returning home.

Local Culture and Customs of North

North has a rich cultural heritage, and the locals are known for their friendliness and hospitality. It is advisable to dress modestly and respect the local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit North

The best time to visit North is during the winter months, from October to February. The weather is pleasant, and it is a great time for outdoor activities.

Getting to North: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near North

The closest airport to North is the Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport in Mumbai.

Bus Stations Near North

There are several bus stations near North, the most notable being the Alibaug Bus Station.

Other Options

How to Get Around in North

The best way to get around in North is by hiring a private car or taxi. However, there are also local buses available for those on a budget.
