Introduction to Buldhana

Located in Vidarbha, Maharashtra, Buldhana is home to several historical, cultural, and natural attractions. The town is also known for its religious significance as it is believed that Lord Parshuram established the town. Buldhana is surrounded by hills and forests and offers a serene and picturesque landscape that is still relatively off the tourist trail. With its rich history, vibrant culture, and natural beauty, Buldhana promises a memorable travel experience.

List of Places to Visit in Buldhana

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Mamata Devi Mandir: This ancient temple dedicated to Goddess Mamata Devi is famous across the region and attracts thousands of devotees every year.
  • Gautam Buddha Caves: Located in the Loni village, these caves are believed to have been used by Gautam Buddha for meditation.
  • Anand Sagar Water Park and Garden: A popular recreational park and garden including a temple, zoo and fountains.
  • Lonar Crater Lake: This natural wonder is the third-largest crater in the world formed due to a meteorite hit on earth.
  • Sri Padmalaya: A temple dedicated to Lord Venkateshwara and his consort Padmavathi. The temple offers a great natural view of the surrounding hills and forests.

Natural Attractions of Buldhana: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Katepurna Sanctuary: The sanctuary is spread over 112.65 square km and offers a diverse range of flora and fauna. Visitors can enjoy trekking and camping within the sanctuary.
  • Shegaon Stupa and Wildlife Sanctuary: A large Buddhist monument and a wildlife sanctuary which is home to many rare and exotic species.
  • Patna Bird Sanctuary: The sanctuary is located on the banks of the Wainganga River and is home to various species of birds that migrate from Siberia.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Buldhana

  • Lonar Crater Lake Trek: This scenic trek offers breathtaking views of the Lonar Crater Lake and the surrounding forest.
  • Katepurna Wildlife Sanctuary Trek: The sanctuary has several trekking trails that offer great natural views.

Best Things to Do in Buldhana: Activities and Tours

  • Anand Sagar Water Park and Garden: A recreational park with a zoo, temple and beautiful fountains.
  • Cycling Tours: Visitors can explore the beautiful natural surroundings of Buldhana by cycling.
  • Waterfall Visit: Buldhana is surrounded by beautiful waterfalls like the Nalganga Waterfall and the Khadka Waterfalls.

Food and Drink Scene of Buldhana

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pithla (Besan curry): A popular dish made with chickpea flour, onion and garlic.
  • Zunka Bhakar: A traditional dish made with besan, onions and spices served with bhakri (roti made from jowar flour).
  • Karanji (coconut-filled sweet): A sweet pastry filled with grated coconut and jaggery.

Local Beverages

  • Solkadhi: A refreshing drink made with kokum and coconut milk.
  • Jaljira: A spiced drink made with cumin, mint and lemon.

Where to Stay in Buldhana: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hotel Sangam: An affordable hotel with comfortable rooms and clean facilities.
  • Mahajan Guest House: A guesthouse with authentic local charm and a relaxing courtyard.
  • Hotel Disha Palace: A luxurious hotel with excellent amenities and beautiful views of the surrounding hills.

What to Buy in Buldhana: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Paithani Sarees: A traditional handloom saree with intricate designs and patterns.
  • Buldhana Orange: Local oranges are famous for their unique taste and texture.
  • Bhuga Chawal: A popular rice dish made with onion and served with ghee and kadhi.

Local Culture and Customs of Buldhana

The local culture of Buldhana is heavily influenced by Hinduism and Buddhism. The town has several ancient temples and monuments that hold great religious significance. The locals are warm and friendly and take pride in their traditions and customs. It is advisable to dress modestly and respect local culture and customs.

Best Time to Visit Buldhana

The best time to visit Buldhana is from October to March when the climate is pleasant and perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Buldhana

Do:

  • Visit local temples and monuments.
  • Try local cuisine.
  • Dress modestly and respectfully.

Don’t:

  • Damage or litter natural surroundings.
  • Disrespect local culture and customs.
  • Engage in illegal activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Buldhana

Buldhana is generally safe for visitors. It is always advisable to take care of personal belongings and avoid traveling alone at night.

Health in Buldhana

It is essential to take precautions like drinking bottled water and avoiding street food to avoid any health issues. Visitors are advised to carry basic medication and a first aid kit.

Getting to Buldhana: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Buldhana

The nearest airport is Aurangabad Airport, which is approximately 165 km away. From here, visitors can take a taxi or a bus to Buldhana.

Bus Stations Near Buldhana

Buldhana has a well-connected network of buses, and visitors can reach the town via state-run buses or private taxis from nearby cities and towns.

Other Options

The nearest railway station is Shegaon Railway Station, which is only 20 km from Buldhana.

How to Get Around in Buldhana

The best way to explore Buldhana is on foot or by rented bicycles. Local taxis and buses are also available for travel around the town and to nearby attractions.

Where to Go Next from Buldhana: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Aurangabad: 165 km from Buldhana, known for its UNESCO World Heritage sites like Ajanta and Ellora Caves.
  • Nashik: 230 km from Buldhana, known for its vineyards and beautiful temples.
  • Pune: 355 km from Buldhana, known for its vibrant culture and historical significance.

Conclusion

Buldhana is a hidden gem in Vidarbha, Maharashtra, offering a mix of natural beauty and cultural heritage. Whether you are a history buff, nature lover or even just a curious traveler, Buldhana has something to offer for everyone. With its vast range of attractions, friendly locals, and exotic cuisine, Buldhana is the perfect destination for a memorable travel experience.
