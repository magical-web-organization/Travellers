Introduction to Lohgad

Located in South Maharashtra, India, Lohgad is a popular destination for travelers seeking a mix of outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, and breathtaking views. The region is known for its fortresses, historical significance, and natural beauty, making it a must-visit destination for anyone exploring India’s vast landscapes.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit in Lohgad, the top activities and tours to try, where to stay, eat and drink, and what to do to fully immerse yourself in the local culture.

List of Places to Visit in Lohgad

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Lohgad is rich in history and culture, and there are plenty of attractions to explore, such as:

  • Lohagad Fort: A 16th-century fort that was once an important strategic point during the Maratha empire.

  • Visapur Fort: A hill fort located close to Lohgad Fort and was built by the first Peshwa of Maratha Empire.

  • Bhaja Caves: A group of Buddhist caves that date back to 200 BC and provide a glimpse into the religious history of the region.

  • Karla Caves: One of the largest rock-cut cave temples in India, featuring beautiful Buddhist architecture and intricate carvings.

  • Rajmachi Fort: A fort built atop a hill that provides panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys.

Natural Attractions of Lohgad: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

For nature lovers, Lohgad offers some of the best scenic views and outdoor adventures in India. Here are some must-visit attractions:

  • Lohgad-Visapur Trek: This easy-to-modrate trek provides an opportunity to explore the beautiful forts of Lohgad and Visapur, while enjoying breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.

  • Tikona Fort Trek: A popular trek that takes you to the top of the Tikona Fort, where you can enjoy stunning views of Pawana Lake and the Sahyadri mountain range.

  • Pawna Lake: A popular camping and picnic spot, Pawna Lake offers beautiful views of the surrounding hills, and adventurous activities like kayaking, boating, and paragliding.

  • Lonavala and Khandala: Popular hill stations located close to Lohgad, offering scenic views and outdoor activities, such as hiking and rock climbing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lohgad

Lohgad is a haven for hikers and bikers, with plenty of trekking and biking routes to choose from. Here are some of the best:

  • Lohgad-Visapur Trek: A moderately challenging trek that takes you to the top of the Lohgad and Visapur forts.

  • Tikona Fort Trek: A relatively easy trek that takes you to the top of the Tikona Fort and offers stunning views of the Pawna lake and the surrounding hills.

  • Peth Fort Trek: A challenging trek that takes you through dense forests and rocky terrains to reach the top of the Peth Fort.

Best Things to Do in Lohgad: Activities and Tours

Apart from trekking and hiking, there are plenty of other activities and tours to try in Lohgad, such as:

  • Camping and Picnicking: Enjoy a serene and relaxing day with family and friends at Pawna Lake, surrounded by lush greenery and beautiful landscapes.

  • Paragliding: Soar over the hills and valleys of Lohgad with the help of experienced trainers and enjoy breathtaking views of the region.

  • Visit Local Markets: Explore the local markets of Lohgad and pick up souvenirs like handicrafts, pottery, and jewelry.

  • Historical Tours: Hire a local guide and explore the historical landmarks and forts of Lohgad to gain a better understanding of the region’s rich history and culture.

Food and Drink Scene of Lohgad

Must-Try Dishes

Lohgad is known for its delicious and diverse cuisine, influenced by the region’s rich cultural history. Here are some must-try dishes:

  • Vada Pav: A popular street food that consists of a potato dumpling sandwiched between two slices of bread.

  • Misal Pav: A spicy lentil-based curry served with a bread roll, yogurt, and onions.

  • Pav Bhaji: A hearty and flavorful vegetable curry served with fluffy bread rolls.

  • Bhel Puri: A savory snack made of puffed rice, vegetables, and spices.

Local Beverages

When in Lohgad, don’t miss out on trying some of the local beverages, such as:

  • Masala Chai: A spiced tea that is a staple in Indian households.

  • Coconut Water: A refreshing and hydrating drink that is widely available throughout the region.

  • Solkadhi: A traditional Konkani drink made with coconut milk, kokum, and spices.

  • Thandai: A cooling and refreshing drink made with milk, nuts, and spices.

Where to Stay in Lohgad: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options to choose from in Lohgad, catering to all budgets and preferences. Here are some suggestions:

  • Luxury Hotels: Hilton Shillim Estate Retreat & Spa, The Machan, and Lake Pavna Camping

  • Budget Hotels/Hostels: Durvankur residency, Hotel G, Backpackers Villa

  • Guesthouses: Sai Aashirwad Guesthouse, Zankar Guesthouse, and Morvekar’s Guesthouse

What to Buy in Lohgad: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some of the souvenirs and local products to bring back from Lohgad:

  • Handicrafts: Lohgad is known for its intricate handicrafts, featuring delicate filigree work, pottery, and woodcarving.

  • Jewelry: Lohgad’s jewelry is made of various materials including silver, gold and semi-precious stones.

  • Herbs and Spices: Lohgad is known for its medicinal herbs and aromatic spices, such as cloves, cardamoms, and fennel.

Local Culture and Customs of Lohgad

Lohgad is known for its friendly locals who are deeply proud of their cultural heritage. It is always recommended to respect the local customs and practices, dress modestly, and learn a few words of Marathi, which is the local language.

Best Time to Visit Lohgad

The best time to visit Lohgad is from October to February, when the weather is pleasant and suitable for outdoor activities like trekking and hiking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lohgad

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and practices.

  • Dress modestly.

  • Try the local cuisine.

  • Learn a few words of Marathi.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter.

  • Avoid consuming alcohol in public places.

  • Do not disrespect local religious practices.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lohgad

Lohgad is a relatively safe destination for travelers, but it is always recommended to take necessary precautions while exploring the region, such as:

  • Hire licensed and experienced guides for outdoor activities.

  • Avoid traveling alone at night.

  • Keep your valuables safe and secure.

Health in Lohgad

Travelers should take standard precautions when traveling to Lohgad, such as:

  • Drink bottled water and avoid consuming street food.

  • Bring mosquito repellent and use it when necessary.

  • Consider getting vaccinated for common illnesses like typhoid, hepatitis A, and polio before traveling.

Getting to Lohgad: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lohgad

The nearest airport to Lohgad is Pune International Airport (PNQ), which is around 50 kilometers away. The airport is well connected to major Indian cities like Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore and Kolkata.

Bus Stations Near Lohgad

Lohgad is well-connected by road to major cities in Maharashtra, such as Mumbai and Pune. There are frequent buses from Mumbai, Pune and other major cities to Lohgad.

Other Options

Private taxis and cars are also available from nearby cities like Pune and Mumbai.

How to Get Around in Lohgad

The best way to get around Lohgad is by hiring a private taxi or car. Alternatively, you can use public transportation, such as buses and rickshaws.

Where to Go Next from Lohgad: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions to explore after your visit to Lohgad:

  • Pune: A bustling city with a rich cultural history and a vibrant food and nightlife scene.

  • Matheran: A picturesque hill station known for its lush greenery and breathtaking views.

  • Mahabaleshwar: A popular hill station known for its strawberry plantations and scenic views.

Conclusion

Lohgad offers a mix of cultural attractions, natural beauty, and outdoor adventures that are sure to appeal to all types of travelers. With this comprehensive guide, you can plan the perfect trip to Lohgad, and explore all that this beautiful region has to offer.
