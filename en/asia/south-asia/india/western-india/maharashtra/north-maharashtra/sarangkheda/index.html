Located in North Maharashtra, Sarangkheda is a small town that offers a perfect blend of nature and culture. It is a hidden gem in Maharashtra and attracts tourists who are looking to explore offbeat destinations. In this comprehensive guide to Sarangkheda, we will take you through the top things to do, places to eat, and places to stay.

Introduction to Sarangkheda

Sarangkheda is a small town with a population of around 10,000 people. The primary occupation of the people living here is farming and fishing. The town is famous for the annual fair, which is organized to sell horses. People from all over Maharashtra come to Sarangkheda during the fair.

List of Places to Visit in Sarangkheda

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sarangkheda Fort: Built-in the 18th century, the fort is situated near the bank of the Tapi river. The fort has a rich history and is a must-visit place for history enthusiasts.

  • Chopda Mahadev Temple: Dedicated to Lord Shiva, the temple is a significant religious place in Sarangkheda. The temple is known for its magnificent architecture and peaceful surroundings.

Natural Attractions of Sarangkheda: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Tapi River: The Tapi river flows through the town and is an ideal place for nature lovers. You can take a boat ride or go for a picnic by the banks of the river.

  • Narmada Canal: The canal is located on the outskirts of the town and is a popular spot for bird watching.

  • Kurha Dam: The dam is located at a distance of 10 km from Sarangkheda and is a popular spot for hiking and nature walks.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sarangkheda

  • Sarangkheda to Kurha Dam: The route is around 10 km long and is perfect for those looking for a relatively easy hike. The trail passes through scenic villages and offers an opportunity to explore the rural landscape of Maharashtra.

Best Things to Do in Sarangkheda: Activities and Tours

  • Sarangkheda Horse Fair: The fair is organized in December every year and is the biggest attraction of Sarangkheda. During the fair, horses are brought from all over Maharashtra, and buyers come from different parts of the state to purchase them. The fair is a unique experience and a must-visit for those visiting Sarangkheda in December.

  • Boat Ride on Tapi River: Take a boat ride on the Tapi river and explore the scenic beauty of Sarangkheda.

Food and Drink Scene of Sarangkheda

Must-Try Dishes

  • Misal Pav: A popular breakfast dish in Maharashtra, Misal Pav is a delicacy that you must try while in Sarangkheda.

  • Puran Poli: A sweet flatbread made with jaggery and dal, Puran Poli is a dessert that you can’t miss.

Local Beverages

  • Nimbu Paani: Nimbu Paani or Lemonade is a refreshing drink that is perfect for the hot weather in Sarangkheda.

Where to Stay in Sarangkheda: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hotel Srushti: Located in the heart of the town, Hotel Srushti is a budget-friendly option for those looking for comfortable accommodation.

  • The Verandah: The Verandah is a luxury guesthouse located on the outskirts of Sarangkheda. It offers spectacular views of the Tapi river and is perfect for those looking for a relaxing stay.

  • Hotel Oasis: Hotel Oasis is a mid-range hotel located near the Tapi river. It offers comfortable rooms and excellent service.

What to Buy in Sarangkheda: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Horse Figurines: You can buy miniature horse figurines as souvenirs from Sarangkheda.

  • Traditional Jewellery: Maharashtra is known for its traditional jewellery, and you can find some unique pieces in Sarangkheda.

Local Culture and Customs of Sarangkheda

The people of Sarangkheda are warm and welcoming. The town has a rich cultural history, and the locals are proud of it. One thing you must keep in mind while visiting Sarangkheda is to respect the local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Sarangkheda

The best time to visit Sarangkheda is from October to March. The weather during this time is pleasant, and you can explore the town without any discomfort.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sarangkheda

  • Do respect the local customs and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Don’t disturb the wildlife.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sarangkheda

Sarangkheda is a safe town, and you don’t have to worry about safety issues. However, as with any place, you must take the necessary precautions and be careful while traveling.

Health in Sarangkheda

Sarangkheda has a decent healthcare system, and you can find hospitals and clinics in the town. However, it’s always a good idea to carry a first aid kit while traveling.

Getting to Sarangkheda: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sarangkheda

  • Aurangabad Airport (150 km)

  • Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport, Mumbai (370 km)

Bus Stations Near Sarangkheda

  • Dhule Bus Station (47 km)

  • Aurangabad Bus Station (150 km)

Other Options

You can also reach Sarangkheda by train. The nearest railway station is Nandurbar (47 km). From the railway station, you can hire a taxi to reach Sarangkheda.

How to Get Around in Sarangkheda

The best way to explore Sarangkheda is by hiring a taxi or taking a bus. You can also rent a bike and explore the rural landscape.

Conclusion

Sarangkheda is a hidden gem in Maharashtra that offers a mix of nature and culture. Explore the rural landscape, try the local cuisine, and attend the annual horse fair. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can plan your trip to Sarangkheda and make the most out of your visit.
