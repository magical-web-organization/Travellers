Introduction to Veraval

Located in the southern part of Gujarat, Veraval is a small coastal town known for its historical and cultural significance. The town was once ruled by various dynasties, including the Mauryas, Chavdas, Chudasamas, and Junagadh Nawabs, and has several temples, museums, and palaces that exhibit the blend of Hindu and Islamic architecture.

Apart from its cultural heritage, Veraval is also famous for its scenic beaches, adventure tourism, and lip-smacking seafood. In this guide, we will explore the best tourist attractions, must-try dishes, unique products, travel tips, and safety guidelines for an unforgettable trip to Veraval.

List of Places to Visit in Veraval

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Veraval has several museums and historical places that showcase the town’s rich cultural heritage. Some of the must-visit attractions are:

Somnath Temple

Somnath Temple is one of the most revered and ancient temples in India. The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is believed to have been built in the 4th century AD. The temple was destroyed and reconstructed many times due to invasions by foreign rulers, including the Arab and Turkish armies.

Junagadh Gate

Junagadh Gate is a historical monument located at the entrance of Veraval town. The gate was built in the early 19th century by the Nawabs of Junagadh as a symbol of their authority. The gate has beautiful Islamic architecture and intricate carvings on its walls.

Bhalka Tirtha

Bhalka Tirtha is a sacred site located on the beach where Lord Krishna was believed to have been hit by an arrow and left for his heavenly abode. The site has a magnificent white marble temple with a large statue of Lord Vishnu.

Diu Island

Diu Island is a small Portuguese colony located near Veraval. The island has several historical forts, churches, and beaches that reflect the blend of Indian and Portuguese cultures.

Natural Attractions of Veraval: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Veraval is blessed with scenic beaches, hills, and forests that offer various adventure activities and breathtaking views. Some of the must-visit natural attractions are:

Veraval Beach

Veraval Beach is a popular tourist spot known for its serene atmosphere, golden sand, and clear blue water. The beach also offers various adventure activities like parasailing, jet skiing, and banana boating.

Girnar Hills

Girnar Hills is a famous pilgrimage site located near Veraval. The hills are known for their scenic views, wildlife, and ancient Jain and Hindu temples.

Barda Wildlife Sanctuary

Barda Wildlife Sanctuary is a protected forest located near Veraval. The sanctuary is home to several endangered species like the Asiatic lion, leopard, and Indian pangolin.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Veraval

Veraval is surrounded by hills and forests, making it an excellent destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Some of the best routes are:

Junagadh to Veraval

Junagadh to Veraval is a scenic route that passes through hills, forests, and temples. The route is around 60 km long and takes around 2-3 hours to complete.

Girnar Parikrama

Girnar Parikrama is a pilgrimage route that involves climbing around 10,000 stairs on the Girnar Hills. The route is around 35 km long and is completed in one day.

Best Things to Do in Veraval: Activities and Tours

Apart from visiting the tourist attractions, Veraval offers several activities and tours that cater to different interests. Some of the best things to do are:

Seafood Tour

Veraval is famous for its fresh and delicious seafood. Visitors can take a seafood tour and taste various dishes like pomfret fry, prawn curry, and crab masala.

Beach Camping

Beach camping is a unique experience that allows visitors to spend a night on the beach and enjoy the bonfire, barbecue, and starry sky.

Temple Tour

Veraval has several ancient Hindu and Jain temples that reflect the town’s rich cultural heritage. Visitors can take a temple tour and learn about the history and significance of each temple.

Food and Drink Scene of Veraval

Veraval is a paradise for seafood lovers. The town has several restaurants and street food vendors that serve fresh and delicious seafood. Some of the must-try dishes are:

Must-Try Dishes

Pomfret Fry

Pomfret Fry is a popular seafood dish made with marinated pomfret fish coated with semolina and spices and deep-fried until crispy.

Prawn Curry

Prawn Curry is a spicy and tangy curry made with fresh prawns, coconut milk, and Indian spices.

Crab Masala

Crab Masala is a spicy and flavorful dish made with fresh crabs and a blend of Indian spices.

Local Beverages

Veraval has several locally brewed alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages that are worth trying.

Sugarcane Juice

Sugarcane juice is a refreshing and healthy drink made with fresh sugarcane juice and lime juice.

Feni

Feni is a locally brewed alcoholic beverage made with cashew nuts and is a popular drink among tourists.

Local Culture and Customs of Veraval

Veraval has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its art, architecture, festivals, and traditions. The town has a blend of Hindu and Islamic cultures, and its people are friendly and hospitable.

Festivals

Some of the popular festivals celebrated in Veraval are:

  • Shivratri
  • Navratri
  • Diwali
  • Holi
  • Eid

Customs

Some of the customs practiced in Veraval are:

  • Women wear colorful sarees, while men wear dhoti-kurta or shirt-pant.
  • Visitors are expected to remove their footwear before entering any temple or mosque.
  • Alcohol consumption and smoking in public places are not allowed.

Best Time to Visit Veraval

The best time to visit Veraval is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and comfortable for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Veraval

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Carry sunscreen, sunglasses, and comfortable footwear.
  • Try the fresh and delicious seafood.

Don’t:

  • Obsess over bargaining for each item while shopping.
  • Wear revealing clothes when visiting temples or mosques.
  • Take photographs without permission in religious places or with locals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Veraval

Veraval is a generally safe town, but visitors should take precautionary measures when traveling in unfamiliar areas. Some of the safety guidelines are:

  • Avoid traveling alone at night.
  • Keep important documents and valuables in a safe place.
  • Follow traffic rules and wear helmets while riding bikes.

Health in Veraval

Visitors should take necessary precautions to stay healthy while traveling in Veraval. Some of the health tips are:

  • Drink bottled and purified water.
  • Avoid consuming raw or uncooked food.
  • Apply insect repellent and carry a first-aid kit.

Bus Stations Near Veraval

Other Options

  • Trains from major Indian cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Ahmedabad stop at Veraval Railway Station.

How to Get Around in Veraval

Veraval is a small town, and most tourist attractions are located within walking or biking distance from each other. Auto-rickshaws are also available for hire.
