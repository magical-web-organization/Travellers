Introduction to New West Zone

New West Zone is a vibrant and bustling neighborhood located in the heart of Ahmedabad, in Central Gujarat, in Western India. It’s one of the city’s largest and most diverse zones, offering a wealth of cultural, historic, and natural attractions for travelers.

In this travel guide, we’ll take you on a tour of New West Zone’s must-see sights, best hiking and biking trails, top things to do, and more. Whether you’re a solo traveler, a couple, or a family, there’s something for everyone in New West Zone.

List of Places to Visit in New West Zone

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

New West Zone is home to some of the most significant museums and historical sites in Ahmedabad. Here are some of the top places you shouldn’t miss:

  • Sabarmati Ashram: Visit the former home of Mahatma Gandhi, where you can learn about his life and teachings and see his personal belongings, including his spinning wheel.
  • Calico Museum of Textiles: Explore the world-renowned textile collections at this museum, which boasts an impressive display of antique textiles from around the globe.
  • Sidi Saiyyed Mosque: Admire the intricate stone lattice work at this 16th-century mosque, which is considered one of the finest examples of Indo-Saracenic architecture in India.
  • Jama Masjid: Marvel at the impressive domes and minarets of this 15th-century mosque, which is one of the largest in India and a testament to Ahmedabad’s rich Islamic heritage.

Natural Attractions of New West Zone: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

New West Zone is also a great destination for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best natural attractions to visit:

  • Kankaria Lake: Take a boat ride or a leisurely stroll around this scenic lake, which is home to a zoo, children’s park, and a variety of food stalls.
  • Law Garden: Enjoy the lush greenery and serene atmosphere of this beautiful garden, which is perfect for picnicking, meditation, or yoga.
  • Sarkhej Roza: Visit the impressive complex of tombs and mosques that date back to the Mughal era and offers a glimpse into the city’s rich history and architecture.
  • Nal Sarovar Bird Sanctuary: Head out for a day trip to this wetland area, which offers stunning views, bird-watching opportunities, and boating.

Hiking/Biking Routes of New West Zone

New West Zone is also great for outdoor activities such as hiking and biking. Here are some of the best routes:

  • Riverfront Park: Walk or bike along the beautifully landscaped riverfront park, which provides a peaceful respite from the hustle and bustle of city life.
  • Thol Bird Sanctuary: Take a cycling tour of this scenic bird sanctuary, which is also great for hiking and picnicking.
  • Vastrapur Lake: Go for a morning jog or an evening sunset stroll around this tranquil lake, which is perfect for running and cycling.

Best Things to Do in New West Zone: Activities and Tours

Besides the places listed above, there are many other fun things to do in New West Zone, including:

  • Heritage Walk: Take a guided walking tour of the old city of Ahmedabad, which provides an intimate look at the city’s rich history, culture, and architecture.
  • Street Food Tour: Go on a culinary adventure and sample some of the best street foods from around the city, including chaat, paratha, and more.
  • Shopping: Explore the bustling markets and bazaars of New West Zone, where you can find everything from handicrafts and textiles to spices and jewelry.

Week-Long Itinerary for New West Zone

Here’s a suggested week-long itinerary for exploring New West Zone:

  • Day 1: Sabarmati Ashram and Riverfront Park
  • Day 2: Calico Museum of Textiles and Law Garden
  • Day 3: Sidi Saiyyed Mosque and Jama Masjid
  • Day 4: Vastrapur Lake and Thol Bird Sanctuary
  • Day 5: Kankaria Lake and Children’s Park
  • Day 6: Shopping in New West Zone
  • Day 7: Heritage Walk and Street Food Tour

Food and Drink Scene of New West Zone

Must-Try Dishes

New West Zone is a food lover’s paradise, and there are plenty of local dishes that you should try, including:

  • Gujarati Thali: This is a traditional Gujarati meal that’s served on a large platter with a variety of dishes, including dal, rice, vegetables, and sweets.
  • Khaman: This is a popular savory snack made of steamed gram flour and yogurt, topped with mustard seeds and coriander.
  • Dabeli: This is a delicious street food that consists of a spiced potato filling served on a bun with chutney, sev, and pomegranate seeds.

Local Beverages

New West Zone is also famous for its refreshing beverages, including:

  • Chai: This is a staple beverage in India and is made by brewing black tea with a blend of spices and milk.
  • Lassi: This yogurt-based drink is a popular summer beverage in India and comes in sweet and salty varieties.

Where to Stay in New West Zone: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options in New West Zone to suit every budget and taste. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • The House of MG: This heritage hotel is located in the heart of the old city and offers luxurious rooms and suites with traditional decor and modern amenities.
  • Hostel Mantra: This budget-friendly hostel is perfect for solo travelers and backpackers who want to meet other travelers and experience local culture.
  • Four Points by Sheraton Ahmedabad: This 4-star hotel provides excellent amenities, including a pool, fitness center, and on-site restaurant.

What to Buy in New West Zone: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

New West Zone is a shopper’s paradise, and there are plenty of souvenirs and products that you can buy to take home with you. Here are some of the best options:

  • Bandhani: This is a traditional tie-dye method used to make colorful fabric, which is then used to make clothes, scarves, and more.
  • Handicrafts: New West Zone is famous for its handicrafts, including wooden boxes, lanterns, and wall hangings.
  • Spices: Take home some of the aromatic spices that are used to make Gujarati cuisine, including cumin, turmeric, and coriander.

Local Culture and Customs of New West Zone

New West Zone is a melting pot of cultures, and there are many customs and traditions that you should be aware of when visiting. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Dress modestly: Wear conservative clothing that covers your shoulders and knees, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Remove your shoes: It’s customary to remove your shoes before entering places of worship or private homes.
  • Respect local customs: Don’t take photos of people without their permission or touch objects in religious sites without asking.

Best Time to Visit New West Zone

The best time to visit New West Zone is between October and March, when the weather is pleasant and comfortable for exploring the outdoors. Summers (April to June) can be hot and humid, while monsoon season (July to September) can be unpredictable and rainy.

Do’s and Don’ts of New West Zone

Do:

  • Do smile and say “namaste” (hello) to locals
  • Do haggle in markets and bazaars
  • Do try the street food

Don’t:

  • Don’t eat street food that looks unhygienic
  • Don’t take photographs of people without their permission
  • Don’t wear revealing clothing in religious sites

Safety and Health

Safety in New West Zone

New West Zone is a relatively safe neighborhood, but it’s always a good idea to take precautions, especially when traveling alone at night. Here are some safety tips:

  • Stick to well-lit areas and avoid dark alleys or empty streets.
  • Keep your valuables close to you and avoid wearing expensive jewelry or carrying a lot of cash.
  • Take a trusted taxi or Uber instead of using public transportation at night.

Health in New West Zone

It’s recommended to drink only bottled water and avoid tap water when in New West Zone. Also, pack sunscreen and mosquito repellent as temperatures can be high and mosquitoes can be active.

Getting to New West Zone: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near New West Zone

The nearest airport to New West Zone is Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport, which is located about 20 kilometers away. Taxis and rental cars are available at the airport.

Bus Stations Near New West Zone

There are several bus stations near New West Zone, including Ahmedabad Central Bus Station and Sarangpur Bus Station. Buses are a cheap and convenient way to get around the city.

Other Options

Taxis and rickshaws are also readily available in New West Zone, and there are several taxi-hailing apps such as Uber and Ola that operate in Ahmedabad.

How to Get Around in New West Zone

The best way to get around New West Zone is on foot or by bicycle. Many attractions are within walking distance of each other, and several bike rental shops operate in the area.

Where to Go Next from New West Zone: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several other places worth visiting near New West Zone, including:

  • Gandhinagar: Explore the capital city of Gujarat, which is located about 25 kilometers away from Ahmedabad.
  • Modhera: Visit the ancient Sun Temple in the town of Modhera, which is located about 100 kilometers from Ahmedabad.
  • Patan: Discover the ancient city of Patan, which is famous for its architectural heritage, textiles, and handicrafts.

Conclusion

New West Zone is an exciting and vibrant neighborhood that’s perfect for travelers who want to experience local culture, history, and nature. With so many things to see and do, it’s no wonder why Ahmedabad is such a popular destination in Western India. So what are you waiting for? Book your trip to New West Zone today!
