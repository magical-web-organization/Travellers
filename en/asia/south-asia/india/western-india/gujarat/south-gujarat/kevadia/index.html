Welcome to Kevadia, a stunning town located in the heart of South Gujarat, India. Kevadia is home to numerous natural and cultural attractions, making it an ideal destination for travelers interested in exploring both. In this comprehensive travel guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and what to eat in Kevadia.

Introduction to Kevadia

Located in the Narmada district of Gujarat, Kevadia is a town that sits on the banks of the Narmada river. The town is known for its natural beauty, stunning landscapes, and rich history. Kevadia is also home to the famous Sardar Sarovar Dam, which is the second-largest dam in the world.

List of Places to Visit in Kevadia

Kevadia has a lot to offer for tourists, from rich cultural experiences to stunning natural landscapes. Here are some of the must-visit places to explore when you’re in Kevadia.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Statue of Unity: Standing tall at 182 meters, the Statue of Unity is the world’s tallest statue and the main attraction in Kevadia. The statue is dedicated to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, one of India’s greatest leaders.

  2. Valley of Flowers: Located near the Statue of Unity, the Valley of Flowers is a beautiful garden that covers more than 70 acres of land. The valley is home to thousands of different kinds of flowers, making it a perfect spot for nature lovers and photographers.

  3. Sardar Patel Museum: The museum is located near the Statue of Unity and showcases various artifacts and exhibits related to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel’s life and achievements.

Natural Attractions of Kevadia: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Kevadia Eco Tourism: Kevadia is surrounded by nature, and one of the best ways to explore it is by taking a tour with Kevadia Eco Tourism. From wildlife safaris to trekking and camping, there’s something for everyone here.

  2. Zarwani Waterfall: Located in the nearby Zarwani Forest, the waterfall is a beautiful spot for picnics and swimming. The waterfall is also surrounded by lush green forests, making it an ideal place for nature lovers.

  3. Shoolpaneshwar Wildlife Sanctuary: Spread over 600 square kilometers, the sanctuary is home to various species of flora and fauna. Visitors can take a wildlife safari and get a chance to see animals like leopards, wild boars, and hyenas. The sanctuary is also a birdwatcher’s paradise.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kevadia

Kevadia is surrounded by beautiful landscapes, and hiking and biking are two of the best ways to explore them. Here are some of the best routes for hiking and biking in Kevadia.

  1. Sardar Sarovar Dam: The dam is one of the main attractions in Kevadia, and visitors can explore the area around it by hiking or biking.

  2. Valley of Flowers: The valley is surrounded by lush green forests, and visitors can take a leisurely hike to explore the area.

Best Things to Do in Kevadia: Activities and Tours

Kevadia offers plenty of activities and tours for travelers. Here are some of the best things to do when you’re in town.

  1. Statue of Unity Light and Sound Show: The light and sound show is a spectacular display that tells the story of Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel’s life and achievements. The show takes place at the Statue of Unity every evening.

  2. Kevadia Laser Fountain Show: Another must-see event in Kevadia is the Laser Fountain Show, which takes place near the Statue of Unity. The show features a stunning laser display set to music.

Week-Long Itinerary for Kevadia

If you’re planning to spend a week in Kevadia, here’s a suggested itinerary to make the most of your trip.

Day 1: Arrival in Kevadia and check-in at your hotel Day 2: Visit the Statue of Unity and take a tour of the Sardar Patel Museum Day 3: Spend the day exploring the Valley of Flowers and Zarwani Waterfall Day 4: Take a wildlife safari at the Shoolpaneshwar Wildlife Sanctuary Day 5: Explore the area around the Sardar Sarovar Dam Day 6: Take a leisurely hike or bike ride around Kevadia Day 7: Departure from Kevadia

Food and Drink Scene of Kevadia

Kevadia offers a variety of food and drinks, from local Gujarati cuisine to international dishes. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in Kevadia.

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Dhokla: A traditional Gujarati snack made with fermented batter, dhokla is a staple in Kevadia.

  2. Undhiyu: A mixed vegetable dish that’s a popular winter delicacy in Gujarat.

  3. Khaman: Another popular Gujarati snack, khaman is made with gram flour and is a great breakfast option.

Local Beverages

  1. Chaas: A refreshing yogurt-based drink that’s perfect for hot days.

  2. Jaljeera: A spicy, tangy drink made with cumin, mint, and other spices.

Where to Stay in Kevadia: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kevadia has a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets. Here are some of the best hotels, hostels, and guesthouses in Kevadia.

  1. The Taj Gateway: One of the best hotels in Kevadia, The Taj Gateway offers stunning views of the Narmada river and is located near the Statue of Unity.

  2. Royal Safari Camps: If you’re looking for a unique camping experience, Royal Safari Camps offer luxurious tents and a range of amenities.

What to Buy in Kevadia: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kevadia offers several souvenirs and local products that are perfect for taking home as a memento of your trip. Here are some of the best things to buy in Kevadia.

  1. Handicrafts: Kevadia is known for its handicrafts, including pottery, beadwork, and embroidery.

  2. Sweets: Gujarat is famous for its sweets, and Kevadia offers a range of delicious options like jalebi and peda.

Local Culture and Customs of Kevadia

Kevadia is a predominantly Gujarati town, and visitors should respect local customs and traditions. Dress modestly when visiting religious sites, and ask for permission before taking pictures of people or landmarks.

Best Time to Visit Kevadia

The best time to visit Kevadia is between November and February, as the weather is pleasant, and there’s less chance of rain.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kevadia

Do:

  1. Respect local customs and traditions.
  2. Be mindful of your language and behavior in public places.
  3. Take plenty of water and sunscreen when you’re out exploring.

Don’t:

  1. Don’t litter or damage natural and cultural attractions.
  2. Don’t take photographs of people without permission.
  3. Don’t wear revealing clothing when visiting religious sites.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kevadia

Kevadia is a safe place to travel, but visitors should be cautious when traveling alone at night or in isolated areas.

Health in Kevadia

Visitors should take precautions against mosquito-borne illnesses like malaria and dengue fever. It’s also a good idea to carry a first-aid kit with you.

Getting to Kevadia: Transportation Options and Logistics

Kevadia is well-connected by road, rail, and air. Here are some of the best ways to get to Kevadia.

Airports Near Kevadia

  1. Vadodara Airport: Located about 90 km from Kevadia, Vadodara Airport is the closest airport to the town.

  2. Ahmedabad Airport: Ahmedabad Airport is located about 210 km from Kevadia and offers a range of domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Kevadia

  1. Kevadia Bus Station: The bus station in Kevadia is located near the Statue of Unity and offers regular services to nearby towns and cities.

  2. Vadodara Bus Station: Vadodara Bus Station is one of the busiest in Gujarat and offers regular services to Kevadia.

Other Options

  1. Train: The closest railway station to Kevadia is Bharuch Junction, which is about 60 km away.

  2. Car rental: Visitors can rent a car in Vadodara or Ahmedabad and drive to Kevadia.

How to Get Around in Kevadia

Kevadia is a small town, and most attractions are located within walking distance of each other. Visitors can also hire a taxi or use public transport to get around.

Where to Go Next from Kevadia: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Kevadia is located near several other tourist destinations in Gujarat, making it an ideal base for exploring the region. Here are some of the best places to go from Kevadia.

  1. Vadodara: A vibrant city known for its palaces, museums, and art galleries.

  2. Pavagadh: A beautiful hill station known for its ancient temples and scenic views.

Conclusion

Kevadia is a hidden gem in Gujarat and offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural experiences. With this comprehensive guide, we hope you can make the most of your trip to Kevadia and explore all that the town has to offer.
