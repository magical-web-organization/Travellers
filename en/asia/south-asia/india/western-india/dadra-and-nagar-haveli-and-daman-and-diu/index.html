Introduction to Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu

Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu is a Union Territory in Western India, formed by the merger of Dadra and Nagar Haveli with Daman and Diu. The land is located by the Arabian sea and is known for producing high-quality fruits including Alphonso Mangoes. The place is a blend of different cultures and is rich in history, which is evident from its architectural marvels.

List of Places to Visit in Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu boasts several historical buildings and museums that give visitors a glimpse of the territory’s rich cultural heritage.

Diu Fort

Constructed between 1535 and 1541, Diu Fort is a magnificent historical building built by the Portuguese that has stood the test of time for over 400 years. The fort was built with the aim of protecting the island from enemies and has a lighthouse, a chapel, stables, and cannons.

Tribal Cultural Museum

Located in Silvassa, the Tribal Cultural Museum is a perfect place to learn about the heritage and lifestyle of the tribal people of Dadra and Nagar Haveli. The museum displays a range of artifacts, including masks, musical instruments, and household items.

Natural Attractions of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu have impressive natural landscapes, offering visitors opportunities for outdoor activities ranging from hiking, picnicking, and trekking.

Dudhni Lake

Located 20 kilometers from Silvassa, Dudhni Lake is a tranquil lake and is surrounded by forests and hills. Visitors can enjoy several activities like camping, kayaking, and boating on Dudhni Lake.

Bindrabin

Bindrabin is a famous tourist spot that offers serene surroundings covered with lush greenery. It is a perfect place if one wants to spend some quality time in nature.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu

Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu has an excellent network of biking and hiking routes, which are best suited for exploring the territory’s natural beauty.

Madhuban Dam

Madhuban Dam is a popular spot that offers hiking trails and is an ideal place for fitness enthusiasts. With its shaded trail and picturesque views, the Madhuban Dam biking route is an excellent place for moderate-level bikers.

Best Things to Do in Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu: Activities and Tours

Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu offers plenty of activities and tours that cater to a variety of interests.

Water Sports in Daman

The Arabian sea borders Daman, making it a perfect location for water sports activities like scuba diving, parasailing, and jet-skiing.

Heritage Walking Tours

Silvassa is a city with a rich heritage, and a walking tour of Silvassa can be an enriching and educational experience that allows tourists to witness the ancient landmarks, temples, and cultures from up close.

Day 1 - Reach Silvassa Day 2 - Visit Tribal Cultural Museum, and Lion Safari. Day 3 - Explore Diu, visit Diu Fort, Nagoa Beach, and Hoka Trees. Food and Drink Scene of Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu

Dadra and Nagar Haveli and Daman and Diu offer tourists a multitude of culinary experiences that are unique and delicious.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Farsan (snacks) are popular local food among tourists.
  • Una Masala is a dish made from mutton and a signature dish of Dadra and Nagar Haveli.
  • Seafood is also famous in Daman, offering a variety of options such as prawns, pomfret, and lobsters.

Local Beverages

Tourists must try local beverages such as tea, coffee, and beer, while fruit juices made from locally grown fruits such as Alphonso mangoes and milk is also very popular.
