Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary is a popular attraction in Goa, a small state located in the Western region of India. It covers an area of 8 sq. km and is home to a variety of flora and fauna species, including deer, elephants, and monkeys. In this travel guide, we will cover everything you need to know to plan your visit to Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary.

Introduction to Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary was established in 1969 and is one of the smallest wildlife sanctuaries in Goa. It serves as a natural habitat for plants and animals that have been displaced due to human activities. The sanctuary offers a unique opportunity for visitors to observe a variety of species of animals and birds in their natural habitat.

Besides the vast greenery and natural beauty, Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary also offers a range of activities for visitors. The sanctuary is popular among nature enthusiasts, birdwatchers, and families looking for a leisurely day out in nature.

List of Places to Visit in Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

There are several places to visit within the sanctuary, including cultural and natural attractions. Here is a list of some of the most popular places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Bondla Zoo: The zoo serves as a home to several animal species that have been rescued from poachers or injured in some way. It serves as a rehabilitation center for wildlife animals such as the Spotted Deer, Gaur, and Sambar.
  • Deer Safari park: This park serves as a home to rare and exotic animal species such as the barking deer and the mouse deer. The Safari Park offers visitors the chance to take a tour of the beautiful woods.
  • Botanical Gardens: The gardens house colorful species of plants and flowers procured from around the world. Visitors can enjoy a peaceful walk through the beautiful gardens and appreciate nature’s beauty.

Natural Attractions of Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Waterfalls: The Kesarval Spring is located within the sanctuary and is an excellent place to relax amidst the greenery and listen to the sound of the waterfall.
  • Trekking trails: There are several trails within the sanctuary that are perfect for trekking and hiking enthusiasts. Visitors can enjoy a peaceful walk amidst the greenery and enjoy the beautiful views of the jungle.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary has several trails perfect for adventure enthusiasts looking to explore the jungle. Some of the most popular hiking and biking routes within the sanctuary include the Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary loop, which is approximately 4 km long and offers beautiful scenic views of the jungle.

Best Things to Do in Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary: Activities and Tours

There are several activities to enjoy at Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary. Some of the most popular activities include:

  • Wildlife Safari: Visitors can enjoy a guided jeep safari or trekking tour through the sanctuary and observe a variety of species of animals and birds.
  • Nature Walks: The sanctuary offers several paths and trails perfect for nature walks.
  • Birdwatching: Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary is home to several species of exotic birds that visitors can spot during their visit.

Food and Drink Scene of Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

Goan cuisine is usually seafood-based and is known for its unique blend of Portuguese and Indian flavors.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Fish Curry: A spicy fish curry made with coconut milk and Goan masalas.
  • Pork Vindaloo: A spicy pork curry cooked with vinegar and Goan spices.

Local Beverages

  • Feni: Feni is a popular local alcoholic drink made from fermented cashews and is popular among locals and tourists alike.

Local Culture and Customs of Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

Goa is known for its diverse culture, which has been influenced by Portuguese traditions. Visitors are expected to respect local customs and traditions while visiting Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary.

Best Time to Visit Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

The best time to visit Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary is between November to March. The weather is pleasant during these months, and visitors can enjoy the beautiful natural beauty of the jungle.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

Don’t:

Safety and Health

Safety in Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary is a safe and secure destination. Visitors are advised to carry a bug spray and apply it before heading out into the jungle.

Health in Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

Visitors are advised to carry a basic first-aid kit when visiting the sanctuary. The sanctuary is known to have several mosquitoes and insects, so carrying a mosquito repellent is essential.

Getting to Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

The nearest airport to Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary is Goa International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

There are several bus stations near Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary that offer connections from various parts of Goa.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a taxi or take a private car to reach Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary.

How to Get Around in Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary

The best way to get around in Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary is by walking or taking a guided tour in a jeep or on foot.
