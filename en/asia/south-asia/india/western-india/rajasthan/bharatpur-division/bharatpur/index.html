Bharatpur - A Comprehensive Travel Guide

Bharatpur is a city steeped in history, culture and nature in the state of Rajasthan, India. It is known for its magnificent palaces, wildlife sanctuaries and rich cultural heritage. In this comprehensive travel guide to Bharatpur, we will be exploring the top places to visit, things to do, hiking and biking routes, cultural customs, safety, food and drink scene, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in Bharatpur

Bharatpur is home to several historically and culturally significant attractions. Here are some of the top places to visit:

Museums and Historical Places

  1. Deeg Palace - This beautiful palace complex is located about 32 km from Bharatpur and is famous for its stunning architectural beauty and unique water features, including fountains and cascades.
  2. Lohagarh Fort - Built in the 18th century, this fort is renowned for its rugged construction and impenetrable defense.
  3. Government Museum - This museum houses an impressive collection of historical artifacts, including sculptures, manuscripts, and paintings.

Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Keoladeo National Park - This UNESCO World Heritage Site is a birdwatcher’s paradise, with over 350 species of birds, including several migratory species. Visitors can explore the park through various guided tours and activities, including walking, cycling and boat rides.
  2. Banke Bihari Temple - This temple is known for its stunning architecture and beautiful setting amidst scenic surroundings.
  3. Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary - This sanctuary is home to several species of migratory and resident birds, including the Siberian crane, Pelicans etc. Visitors can explore the sanctuary through walking or cycling, and can take guided tours to learn more about the local flora and fauna.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bharatpur

Bharatpur offers some fantastic hiking and biking routes that allow visitors to explore the city’s natural beauty and scenic surroundings. Some popular routes include:

  1. Deeg Route - This scenic route starts from Bharatpur and takes visitors through the beautiful countryside, all the way to Deeg Palace.
  2. Keoladeo Route - This route takes visitors through the stunning Keoladeo National Park, allowing them to explore its vast array of flora and fauna on foot or by cycle.
  3. Lohagarh Fort Route - This route takes visitors through the rugged terrain surrounding Lohagarh Fort, offering mesmerizing views of the countryside.

Best Things to Do in Bharatpur: Activities and Tours

There are several exciting things to do in Bharatpur that cater to people of all ages and interests:

  1. Bird Watching in Keoladeo National Park - One of the top attractions in the city, visitors can take guided tours of the park with experienced bird watchers to see the vast array of birds that reside here.
  2. Take a Cycle Tour - Cycling is a great way to explore the city’s picturesque surroundings, and there are several tour operators that offer guided cycle tours around Bharatpur.
  3. Visit Deeg Palace - This gorgeous palace complex is a must-see attraction, and visitors can explore its different rooms and learn about its fascinating history.
  4. Explore Lohagarh Fort - Visitors can hike around the massive walls of the fort, learn about its impressive construction, and take in the stunning scenery around it.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bharatpur

Here is an itinerary for a week-long trip to Bharatpur:

Day 1: Arrive in Bharatpur, check-in to your hotel, and take a stroll around the city’s bustling markets. Day 2: Take a guided tour of Keoladeo National Park and learn about the different species of birds living in the sanctuary. Day 3: Explore the historic Lohagarh Fort and its magnificent architecture. Day 4: Visit Deeg Palace and admire its stunning water features and intricate design. Day 5: Take a guided cycle tour of the city’s charming surroundings. Day 6: Spend the day exploring the local food and drink scene and trying out some of the local delicacies. Day 7: Departure day - take one final stroll around the city before heading to your next destination.

Food and Drink Scene of Bharatpur

Bharatpur has a mouth-watering food and drink scene, offering visitors a chance to sample some of the city’s delicious local delicacies. Here are some of the must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Dal Puri - Made with lentils and flour, this dish is a popular breakfast item in Bharatpur.
  2. Ghewar - This sweet dish is made with flour, sugar syrup, and ghee, and is a must-try for anyone with a sweet tooth.
  3. Lal Mans - Made with succulent chunks of mutton, this spicy dish is a local favorite.

Local Beverages

  1. Buttermilk - This refreshing drink is made with yogurt, water, and spices and is a popular accompaniment to spicy meals.
  2. Lassi - This sweetened yogurt drink is a popular beverage served all over India, and is especially delicious in Bharatpur.
  3. Beer - There are several local brands of beer available in Bharatpur, and they are a great way to beat the heat after a long day of exploring.

Where to Stay in Bharatpur: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bharatpur has a range of accommodations to suit any budget or preference, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Some top options include:

  1. The Birder’s Inn - This hotel is located near Keoladeo National Park, offering visitors easy access to the bird sanctuary. The hotel also has a restaurant that serves delicious Indian and international cuisine.
  2. Hotel Sunbird - This boutique hotel is located in the heart of Bharatpur and offers comfortable rooms and great service.
  3. Chandra Mahal Haveli - This charming guesthouse is located close to the city’s markets and attractions and has a relaxed, homely ambiance.

What to Buy in Bharatpur: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Bharatpur has several unique products and souvenirs that make great gifts for friends and family back home. Some popular options include:

  1. Handicrafts and Artifacts - Bharatpur is famous for its handicrafts, including wooden toys, jewelry, and textiles. Visitors can find these items in several markets and shops around the city.
  2. Keoladeo Park Guidebooks - Visitors can purchase guidebooks that provide an overview of the bird species that can be found in Keoladeo National Park.
  3. Local Spices - Visitors can purchase locally produced spices and herbs, including garam masala, cumin, turmeric, and coriander.

Local Culture and Customs of Bharatpur

Bharatpur has a rich and unique culture, characterized by its vibrant festivals, delicious cuisine, and warm hospitality. Some customs and etiquette to keep in mind when in Bharatpur include:

  • Dress modestly, especially if visiting any religious or cultural sites.
  • Remove your shoes before entering someone’s home.
  • Show respect to elders and authority figures.
  • Tipping is not mandatory but is appreciated.

Best Time to Visit Bharatpur

The best time to visit Bharatpur is between November and February, when the weather is cooler and more pleasant, and bird activity is at its peak.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bharatpur

Here are some general do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when traveling in Bharatpur:

Do:

  • Carry cash in small denominations.
  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions.
  • Explore the city’s surroundings.
  • Try the local cuisine.

Don’t:

  • Drink tap water.
  • Engage in public displays of affection.
  • Wear revealing clothing in public places.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Bharatpur is a relatively safe destination for travelers, with violent crimes being rare. However, visitors should still exercise caution and be aware of their surroundings, especially when traveling alone or at night. Visitors should also take basic health precautions such as drinking bottled water, using sunscreen, and avoiding street food to prevent food poisoning.

Getting to Bharatpur: Transportation Options and Logistics

There are several transportation options available for travelers to get to Bharatpur:

Airports Near Bharatpur

The nearest airport to Bharatpur is the Jaipur International Airport, located about 180 km away. Visitors can take a taxi or bus from the airport to Bharatpur.

Bus Stations Near Bharatpur

Bharatpur is well-connected to neighboring cities by bus. There are several bus stations throughout the city, with buses departing regularly to destinations such as Jaipur, Agra, and Delhi.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Bharatpur by train, with several trains departing regularly from major Indian cities like Jaipur, Delhi, and Mumbai.

How to Get Around in Bharatpur

Bharatpur is a relatively small city that can be easily explored on foot or by cycle. Visitors can also hire taxis and rickshaws to travel around the city.

Where to Go Next from Bharatpur: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bharatpur is located in the heart of Rajasthan, making it an ideal base for exploring other nearby destinations. Some popular options include:

  • Jaipur - The Pink City is famous for its stunning palaces and vibrant markets.
  • Agra - Home to the iconic Taj Mahal, Agra is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to India.
  • Pushkar - This charming town is famous for its annual camel fair and beautiful temples.

Conclusion

Bharatpur is a culturally-rich and scenic city in Rajasthan that offers visitors a unique glimpse into Indian history and culture. With its stunning natural beauty, charming surroundings, and delicious food, Bharatpur is an unforgettable destination that is sure to leave a lasting impression.
