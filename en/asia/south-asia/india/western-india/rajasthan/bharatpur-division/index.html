Introduction to Bharatpur Division

Located in Rajasthan, India, Bharatpur Division is a popular tourist destination known for its rich heritage and cultural landmarks. The region is home to numerous historic forts, palaces, and temples that draw tourists from all over the world. Bharatpur is also known for its national park and wildlife sanctuary that provides visitors with an opportunity to explore the natural beauty of the area.

List of Places to Visit in Bharatpur Division

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Deeg Palace: Built in the 18th century, the Deeg Palace is an architectural marvel that bears witness to the royal splendor of the bygone era.
  • Lohagarh Fort: Known for its robust structure and impenetrable walls, Lohagarh Fort is a must-visit for history buffs and architecture enthusiasts alike.
  • Government Museum: Dedicated to preserving the heritage of Bharatpur Division, the Government Museum houses a vast collection of paintings, manuscripts, sculptures, and inscriptions.

Natural Attractions of Bharatpur Division: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Keoladeo National Park: Often referred to as the Bharatpur Bird Sanctuary, the Keoladeo National Park is home to over 370 species of birds, as well as various other flora and fauna.
  • Ranthambore National Park: If you are a wildlife enthusiast, then explore the Ranthambore National Park, the home to a wide variety of animals, including tigers, leopards, and sloth bears.
  • Deeg Water Palace: Explore the beautiful water gardens of the Deeg Water Palace, which was a summer resort of the Mughal rulers.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bharatpur Division

  • Bharatpur Cycling Tour: Take a cycling tour of the city to explore the cultural landmarks and the natural beauty of the area.
  • Bird Watching Tour: Go on a bird-watching tour in the Keoladeo National Park and capture the essence of the region’s diverse avian population.
  • Safari in Ranthambore National Park: Enjoy a safari ride in Ranthambore National Park and witness the majestic tigers in their natural habitat.

Best Things to Do in Bharatpur Division: Activities and Tours

  • Stepwell Tour: Explore the old stepwells of the region, which are a testament to the engineering skills of the ancient rulers.
  • Village Walks: Take a stroll into the local villages and get a glimpse into the day-to-day lives of the inhabitants.
  • Heritage Walks: Go on a heritage walk to explore the historical monuments that stand as a testament to the rich culture and heritage of the city.

Food and Drink Scene of Bharatpur Division

Must-Try Dishes

  • Laal Maas: A spicy mutton curry made with a blend of traditional Indian spices.
  • Ghevar: A crispy dessert made with flour and soaked in sugar syrup.
  • Kachori: A deep-fried pastry stuffed with lentils, peas, and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Lassi: A refreshing yogurt-based drink that is perfect for cooling down on hot summer days.
  • Chai: A traditional Indian tea that is enjoyed by locals and tourists alike.
  • Thandai: A sweet concoction of milk, nuts, and spices, perfect to quench your thirst.

Where to Stay in Bharatpur Division: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Laxmi Vilas Palace: A luxurious hotel that offers a blend of modern amenities and traditional Indian hospitality.
  • The Bagh: A boutique hotel that promises a tranquil and peaceful stay amid lush green gardens.
  • Kadamb Kunj Resort: A cozy resort that provides guests with an intimate and comfortable stay with excellent service.

What to Buy in Bharatpur Division: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Miniature Paintings: Take home a beautiful piece of art that celebrates the local history and culture.
  • Handicrafts: Buy handcrafted souvenirs, including handmade puppets, embroidered textiles, and pottery.
  • Spices: Take home some aromatic Indian spices to add some flavor to your cooking.

Local Culture and Customs of Bharatpur Division

Bharatpur Division has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its art, music, and dance. The local people are hospitable and welcoming, and it is customary to greet each other with a Namaste. It is also customary to remove your footwear before entering a temple or someone’s home.

Best Time to Visit Bharatpur Division

The best time to visit Bharatpur Division is from November to February, when the weather is pleasant, and you can witness the beautiful migratory birds in Keoladeo National Park.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bharatpur Division

Do:

  • Dress modestly and respect the local culture.
  • Carry sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.
  • Bargain while shopping to get the best deal.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter the streets and public places.
  • Do not carry plastic bags in the Keoladeo National Park.
  • Do not misuse or damage historical monuments and temples.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bharatpur Division

Bharatpur Division is a safe place to visit, but it is always advisable to keep your valuables safe and secure while traveling. Avoid visiting isolated places at night and always take registered taxis or buses while traveling.

Health in Bharatpur Division

It is advisable to take precautions against mosquito bites while visiting the Keoladeo National Park. Drink only bottled or filtered water and carry a basic first aid kit with you while traveling.

Getting to Bharatpur Division: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bharatpur Division

The nearest airport to Bharatpur Division is Indira Gandhi International Airport in New Delhi, approximately 190 kilometers away. From the airport, you can take a taxi or a bus to reach Bharatpur.

Bus Stations Near Bharatpur Division

There are regular bus services from Delhi, Jaipur, and Agra to Bharatpur. The Bharatpur Bus Stand, located in the city center, is well connected to nearby towns and cities.

Other Options

If you are traveling from Delhi, you can also hire a private car or taxi to reach Bharatpur.

How to Get Around in Bharatpur Division

Local transportation options include auto-rickshaws, taxis, and buses. It is advisable to hire a taxi or a car if you plan to visit nearby places like Ranthambore or Deeg.

Where to Go Next from Bharatpur Division: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Agra: Visit the iconic Taj Mahal and other historical monuments in the city famous for its rich Mughal architecture.
  • Jaipur: Enjoy the vibrant culture and rich history of the Pink City with its colorful bazaars and stunning palaces.
  • Delhi: Explore the capital city of India, home to numerous historical landmarks, cultural attractions, and street food scenes.

Conclusion

Bharatpur Division offers a unique blend of history, culture, and nature that makes it a must-visit destination in Rajasthan. Whether you are a wildlife enthusiast, a history buff or just seeking a peaceful getaway, Bharatpur Division has something to offer for everyone. Plan a trip to Bharatpur Division and witness the beauty of Rajasthan.
