Ultimate Travel Guide to Udaipur Division, Rajasthan

Udaipur Division is a beautiful region located in the western state of Rajasthan, India. With its rich cultural heritage, beautiful architecture, stunning lakes, and scenic landscapes, Udaipur Division has become a popular tourist destination in recent years. In this comprehensive guide, we will provide you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Udaipur Division, including the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and what to eat. Let’s get started!

Introduction to Udaipur Division

Udaipur Division is located in the western part of Rajasthan and is known for its beautiful lakes and palaces. The region is home to several important historical sites, including the famous City Palace, which was once the residence of the Maharana of Udaipur. In addition to its architectural beauty, Udaipur Division is also known for its vibrant culture, cuisine, and hospitality.

List of Places to Visit in Udaipur Division

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. City Palace
  2. Jagmandir Palace
  3. Fateh Prakash Palace
  4. Monsoon Palace
  5. Bagore Ki Haveli
  6. Kumbhalgarh Fort
  7. Chittorgarh Fort
  8. Eklingji Temple

Udaipur Division is home to several palaces and forts that served as the residence of the Maharaja of Udaipur. These majestic buildings showcase the region’s rich cultural history and are a treat for architecture enthusiasts. The City Palace, located in the heart of Udaipur, is a must-visit attraction that gives visitors a glimpse of the grandeur and opulence of the royal life. Jagmandir Palace, also known as Lake Garden Palace, is another important historical site that is located on an island in the middle of Lake Pichola. Monsoon Palace, also known as Sajjangarh Palace, offers stunning views of the city and is a popular spot to watch the sunset. Don’t miss the chance to visit Kumbhalgarh Fort and Chittorgarh Fort, both of which are UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Eklingji Temple, located 22 km from Udaipur, is one of the most famous temples in Udaipur Division and attracts thousands of devotees every year.

Natural Attractions of Udaipur Division: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Lake Pichola
  2. Lake Fatehsagar
  3. Jaisamand Lake
  4. Kumbhalgarh Wildlife Sanctuary
  5. Sajjangarh Wildlife Sanctuary

Udaipur Division is blessed with natural beauty and breathtaking landscapes. Lake Pichola, one of the most beautiful lakes in Rajasthan, is a popular spot for boat rides and offers stunning views of the surrounding hills and palaces. Don’t miss the chance to visit Lake Fatehsagar, another popular lake that is surrounded by lush green hills. Jaisamand Lake, located in the south of Udaipur, is one of the largest artificial lakes in Asia and is home to a variety of migratory birds. Kumbhalgarh Wildlife Sanctuary and Sajjangarh Wildlife Sanctuary are perfect destinations for nature lovers and offer a chance to spot several species of animals and birds.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Udaipur Division

  1. Aravalli Hills Trek
  2. Badi Lake to Tiger Lake Trek
  3. Udaipur City Heritage Walk

If you are looking for outdoor adventure, Udaipur Division has plenty of hiking and biking routes that offer stunning views of the region’s natural beauty. The Aravalli Hills Trek is a popular hiking trail that takes you through the scenic Aravalli Hills and offers mesmerizing views of the surrounding landscape. The Badi Lake to Tiger Lake Trek is another popular route that takes you through the lush green forests and offers a chance to spot various species of birds and animals. If you are interested in history and architecture, don’t miss the Udaipur City Heritage Walk, which takes you through some of the most famous historical sites in the city.

Best Things to Do in Udaipur Division: Activities and Tours

  1. Sunset Boat Ride in Lake Pichola
  2. Hot Air Balloon Ride
  3. City Palace Light and Sound Show
  4. Vintage Car Museum

Udaipur Division has plenty of activities and tours to offer visitors. A sunset boat ride in Lake Pichola is one of the most memorable experiences you can have in Udaipur. Hot air balloon rides give you a chance to see the region’s natural beauty from above and offer breathtaking views of the lakes and palaces. Don’t miss the City Palace Light and Sound Show, which brings the history and culture of the region to life through music and storytelling. Vintage Car Museum, located in the Garden Hotel, is a must-visit attraction for car enthusiasts and showcases a collection of rare and vintage cars.

Food and Drink Scene of Udaipur Division

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Dal Baati Churma
  2. Ker Sangri
  3. Gatte ki Sabzi
  4. Laal Maas
  5. Mawa Kachori

Udaipur Division is known for its diverse and delicious cuisine. Don’t miss the chance to try some of the region’s most famous dishes, such as Dal Baati Churma, a traditional Rajasthani dish made from lentils, wheat, and ghee. Ker Sangri is another popular vegetarian dish made from dried beans and berries. Gatte ki Sabzi is a famous Rajasthani curry made from gram flour dumplings. Laal Maas is a famous non-vegetarian dish made from mutton and a variety of spices. Don’t forget to try Mawa Kachori, a sweet pastry filled with milk solids and nuts.

Local Beverages

  1. Masala Chaas
  2. Thandai
  3. Lassi

Masala Chaas, a spicy buttermilk, is a popular local beverage in Udaipur Division and is perfect for hot summer days. Thandai is a refreshing drink made from milk, almonds, and saffron, and is a must-try during the Holi festival. Lassi is another popular drink made from yogurt and is available in various flavors.

Where to Stay in Udaipur Division: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. The Oberoi Udaivilas
  2. Taj Lake Palace
  3. Hotel Lakend
  4. Backpacker Panda
  5. Zostel Udaipur

Udaipur Division has a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. If you are looking for luxury, The Oberoi Udaivilas and Taj Lake Palace are two of the most famous and exquisite hotels in the region. Hotel Lakend is a popular mid-range option that offers stunning views of Lake Fatehsagar. If you are a budget traveler, Backpacker Panda and Zostel Udaipur are two of the most popular hostels in Udaipur Division.

What to Buy in Udaipur Division: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Handicrafts
  2. Embroidered Clothing and Textiles
  3. Rajasthani Jewelry
  4. Miniature Paintings

Udaipur Division is famous for its vibrant handicrafts and textiles. The region is home to several traditional handicrafts, including block printing, tie-dyeing, and mirror work, which can be bought at local markets. Don’t miss the chance to buy embroidered clothing and textiles, which are famous around the world for their intricate designs and patterns. Rajasthani jewelry, made from silver and gold, is another popular souvenir to take home. Miniature paintings, a traditional art form that dates back to the 16th century, are also worth buying as a precious memento of your trip to Udaipur Division.

Local Culture and Customs of Udaipur Division

Udaipur Division is a conservative region, and it is important to respect the local culture and customs. Visitors are advised to dress modestly and avoid public displays of affection. It is also important to remove your shoes before entering temples and other holy places. Don’t forget to greet people with a Namaste, which is the traditional way of greeting in India.

Best Time to Visit Udaipur Division

The best time to visit Udaipur Division is from September to March, when the weather is pleasant and offers perfect conditions for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Udaipur Division

Do:

  1. Respect the local culture and customs
  2. Try the local cuisine
  3. Bargain at local markets
  4. Carry sunscreen and a hat

Don’t:

  1. Wear revealing clothing
  2. Photograph people without their permission
  3. Disrespect religious sites
  4. Drink tap water

Safety and Health

Safety in Udaipur Division

Udaipur Division is a safe region to visit, but visitors are advised to take the necessary precautions to ensure their safety. It is important to be aware of your surroundings and avoid walking alone at night. Don’t carry large sums of cash or valuables while sightseeing and keep your belongings safe.

Health in Udaipur Division

Visitors to Udaipur Division are advised to drink only bottled water and avoid street food. It is also important to carry mosquito repellent to avoid mosquito-borne diseases.

Getting to Udaipur Division: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Udaipur Division

  1. Maharana Pratap Airport, Udaipur
  2. Ahmedabad Airport

The closest airport to Udaipur Division is Maharana Pratap Airport, located in Udaipur. Ahmedabad Airport is another major airport that is located about 250 km away from Udaipur.

Bus Stations Near Udaipur Division

  1. Udaipur City Bus Stand
  2. Mewar Bus Stand, Udaipur

Udaipur Division is well-connected by road, and there are several bus stations in Udaipur that offer bus services to nearby cities and states.

Other Options

  1. Trains: Udaipur City Railway Station is well-connected to major cities in India.
  2. Taxis and Private Cars: Taxis and private cars are readily available for hire in Udaipur Division.

How to Get Around in Udaipur Division

Udaipur Division has a well-developed public transport system, and visitors can use buses, taxis, and auto-rickshaws to get around the region. Bicycles and motorcycles are also available for rent.

Where to Go Next from Udaipur Division: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Mount Abu
  2. Jodhpur
  3. Ahmedabad
  4. Pushkar

Udaipur Division is located in the heart of Rajasthan and is well-connected to several nearby destinations that are worth visiting. Mount Abu, known for its natural beauty, is located just 160 km away from Udaipur. Jodhpur, famous for its blue-painted houses and grand palaces, is located about 250 km away. Ahmedabad, Gujarat’s largest city, is located about 260 km away from Udaipur. Pushkar, known for its famous Pushkar Fair, is located about 280 km away.
