Introduction to Aklera

Aklera is a small town located in the Kota division of Rajasthan, India. This tranquil town is surrounded by beautiful natural landscapes, historical monuments, and a rich local culture that reflects India’s vibrant heritage. Whether you’re looking to escape the city’s hustle and bustle or seeking an authentic Indian experience, Aklera has something to offer. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will help you plan your trip to Aklera with diverse activities and unforgettable experiences.

List of Places to Visit in Aklera

Aklera is a destination that provides a variety of attractions and experiences that cater to different tastes. Here is a list of places to visit in Aklera:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Juna Mahal: This fort was built in the 14th century and has an impressive architecture. It is said that the fort had around 52 rooms, but now only a few remain. It is an excellent place to visit to understand the town’s history and architecture.

  • Neeraj Cinema: This old cinema hall is still running and is a great place to watch a movie with a unique experience. It is a perfect combination of old-school and modernity with a dash of history added to it.

  • Ghantaghar: Ghantaghar, also known as the clock tower, is a landmark that represents the town’s identity. It was built in 1920 and is an excellent place to get a view of the town center.

Natural Attractions of Aklera: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Jhalrapatan: Jhalrapatan is a small town near Aklera, famous for its scenic beauty and forts. The place features beautiful lakes, temples, and historical monuments, making it a cultural hub.

  • Garadia Mahadev Temple: Garadia Mahadev Temple, located around 12 km away from Aklera, is a lovely place with beautiful scenic views. The temple is located on a hilltop and is accessible through winding roads that provide an adventure in themselves.

  • Sitabari: Sitabari is located 70 km away from Aklera and is famous for the Sitabari Temple. It is a beautiful place with lush green forests and a perfect place to escape the city’s hustle.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Aklera

For hikers and bikers, Aklera can be an ideal destination because of its scenic beauty and the beautiful winding roads that connect different towns. The perfect hiking/biking routes in Aklera are:

  • Jhalawar to Aklera: The route from Jhalawar to Aklera is famous for its beautiful roads that pass through green fields and small villages. It offers a unique experience to explore the countryside and the cultural events en route.

  • Aklera to Sitabari: The route from Aklera to Sitabari is perfect for biking enthusiasts as it passes through beautiful forests, lakes and offers a unique experience of the outdoors.

Best Things to Do in Aklera: Activities and Tours

Aklera is a destination that offers a variety of activities and tours that cater to different tastes. Here is a list of best things to do in Aklera:

  • Explore the town center: The town center is the heart of Aklera that reflects the town’s culture and heritage. It’s a great place to explore by foot and learn more about the town’s history.

  • Visit the nearby lake: The nearby lake is a beautiful place to take a break from the city’s hustle-bustle and enjoy nature’s tranquility.

  • Take a cooking class: Experience the rich local cuisine of Aklera by taking a cooking class with the locals.

Food and Drink Scene of Aklera

The food and drink scene of Aklera is vibrant and reflects the local culture’s rich heritage. Here is a list of must-try dishes and local beverages in Aklera:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Dal Baati Churma: Dal Baati Churma is a traditional dish in Rajasthan. It is made of lentils, wheat, and ground chilli paste, making it a healthy and spicy meal.

  • Laal Maas: Laal Maas is a meat dish that reflects the local culture’s love for spicy food. Its name originated as ‘laal’ means red in Hindi, which fits it perfectly because of its thick red color.

Local Beverages

  • Lassi: Lassi is a yogurt-based drink that is sweet and thick, making it a perfect refreshing drink for the summers.

  • Chai: Chai is a popular tea in India, and Aklera has many roadside chai wallas that offer tea with unique flavours that are hard to find anywhere else.

What to Buy in Aklera: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Aklera is famous for its handicrafts and art pieces that reflect the local culture’s vibrant heritage. Here’s a list of souvenirs and local products to bring back home:

  • Handicrafts: Handicrafts such as pottery, handcrafted clothes, and jewelry can be found in the local bazaars and markets.

  • Puppets: Puppets are famous in Rajasthan, and Aklera has some of the best handcrafted puppets that can be a great souvenir or gift to bring back home.

  • Paintings: Paintings that reflect the town’s architecture and culture can be found in the local markets and galleries.

Local Culture and Customs of Aklera

The local culture and customs of Aklera reflect the vibrant heritage of Rajasthan. The town’s people are very welcoming and hospitable, and it’s not uncommon for them to invite strangers over for a meal. The traditional dress for women is a Ghagra Choli, while the traditional dress for men is a dhoti and kurta.

Best Time to Visit Aklera

The best time to visit Aklera is during the winter months, from October to March. The weather during these months is pleasant, making it a perfect time for outdoor activities and exploring the town. The summers can be scorching, with temperatures rising to 45 degrees Celsius or higher.

Safety and Health

Safety in Aklera

Aklera is a safe town, and the locals are very welcoming and hospitable. However, it is advised to take standard precautions while traveling, such as not walking alone at night.

Health in Aklera

Aklera has a primary health center for medical emergencies, and the nearest hospital is located in Jhalawar. It’s advised to carry a first-aid kit and take good care of personal hygiene.

Getting to Aklera: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Aklera

The nearest airport to Aklera is the Kota Airport, located approximately 75 km away from the town center.

Bus Stations Near Aklera

Buses to Aklera are available from Jhalawar, Kota, and Jaipur.

Other Options

Taxis and private cars can be hired from nearby locations to reach Aklera.

How to Get Around in Aklera

Aklera is a small town that can be explored on foot or by hiring a bicycle or scooter. Taxis and private cars can be hired for longer distances.

Where to Go Next from Aklera: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider after visiting Aklera:

  • Jhalawar: Jhalawar is a beautiful city located near Aklera and is famous for its historical monuments, lakes, and temples.

  • Bundi: Bundi is located around 103 km away from Aklera and is famous for its painted stepwells, forts, and palace.

Aklera is a small town with a rich cultural heritage that reflects Rajasthan’s vibrant history.
