Introduction to Ajmer Division

Ajmer Division is located in the state of Rajasthan, in Western India. It is a historically rich region that is home to several fascinating monuments and cultural treasures. The city of Ajmer is the divisional headquarters.

List of Places to Visit in Ajmer Division

Ajmer Division is a tourist’s paradise, boasting a range of cultural and natural attractions to explore. Some of the must-visit destinations in the region include:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Ajmer Sharif Dargah: This is one of the most important Islamic shrines in the world, attracting devotees from all over the globe.
  • Taragarh Fort: This magnificent fortress was constructed in the 14th century and offers stunning views of Ajmer and its surroundings.
  • Ana Sagar Lake: This beautiful lake was built in 1135-1150 by Anaji Chauhan and is filled with stunning parks and gardens.

Natural Attractions of Ajmer Division: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Pushkar Lake: This tranquil lake is located in the city of Pushkar and is surrounded by 52 bathing ghats and hundreds of temples.
  • Sariska Wildlife Sanctuary: This stunning wildlife reserve is home to tigers, leopards, deer, hyenas, monkeys, and a range of bird species.
  • Mayo College: This iconic institution was originally built in 1875 and is one of the oldest public schools in India.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ajmer Division

Ajmer Division is an excellent place for adventure enthusiasts, with several hiking and biking trails across the region. Some of the top trekking and cycling routes to explore include:

  • Sariska National Park: This reserve offers several hiking options, ranging from easy to challenging, and provides the perfect opportunity to spot some of India’s magnificent wildlife.
  • The Pushkar Holy Lake: This cycling trail is relatively flat and is ideal for beginners or anyone looking for a leisurely ride while enjoying the stunning views of the lake and its surroundings.
  • Nag Tibba Trek: This challenging trek is ideal for experienced hikers and passes through stunning forests, mountain valleys, and local villages.

Best Things to Do in Ajmer Division: Activities and Tours

Ajmer Division offers numerous activities and tours for visitors to explore the region, including:

  • Camel Safari: A visit to Rajasthan is incomplete without experiencing a camel safari. The region offers several camel safari tours, which take visitors through stunning deserts and provide a glimpse into the local culture.
  • Street Food Tour: Rajasthan is famous for its delicious street food, and Ajmer Division has an array of culinary delights to offer. Take a street food tour and sample some of the delicious local dishes.
  • Sightseeing Tours: Discover the best of Ajmer Division with a range of sightseeing tours that take visitors to historical landmarks, natural wonders, and more.

Food and Drink Scene of Ajmer Division

The food scene in Ajmer Division is a treat for any foodie. The cuisine is a mix of Rajasthani and Mughlai flavors and has a distinct spicy, tangy, and sweet taste. Some of the must-try dishes and local beverages include:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Daal Baati Churma: A local Rajasthani dish made with lentils, wheat balls, and sweetened crumbled bread.
  • Laal Maas: A spicy meat curry made with mutton and a mix of chilies and whole spices.
  • Kachori: A popular street food prepared with a spicy and tangy filling of lentils or potatoes.

Local Beverages

  • Lassi: A refreshing yogurt-based drink that is perfect for the hot and dry climate.
  • Masala Chai: This spicy and fragrant tea is a staple in India and is brewed with aromatic spices and milk.

Where to Stay in Ajmer Division: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ajmer Division offers a range of accommodation options from luxurious five-star resorts to affordable guesthouses and hostels. Here are some of the top picks:

  • The Gateway Resort Pushkar Bypass: This stunning five-star resort is ideal for those looking for luxury accommodations within easy reach of the major attractions of Ajmer Division.
  • Dera Masuda: This beautiful heritage property in the heart of Ajmer offers a mix of traditional charm and modern amenities.
  • Zostel Pushkar: This fun and lively hostel is ideal for budget-conscious travelers who want to experience local culture and make new friends.

What to Buy in Ajmer Division: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Ajmer Division is known for its vibrant culture and traditional art and crafts. Visitors can take back souvenirs and local products that reflect the region’s cultural diversity, such as:

  • Blue Pottery: This beautiful pottery is made with glazed clay and is painted in a variety of blue shades.
  • Mojari Shoes: These traditional shoes are made with colorful silk or leather and are perfect for a night out in the town.
  • Bangles: These beautiful bracelets come in a range of colors and designs, making them the perfect souvenir for anyone looking to add a pop of color to their wardrobe.

Local Culture and Customs of Ajmer Division

Ajmer Division is a culturally rich region that celebrates festivities throughout the year. The region is known for its colorful attire, traditional dances, and stunning architecture. Visitors should show respect for local customs and traditions and dress modestly while visiting religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Ajmer Division

The best time to visit Ajmer Division is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant and suitable for outdoor activities. The winter months (December - February) are perfect for exploring the historical monuments and going on wildlife safaris.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ajmer Division

Here are some of the do’s and don’ts for visitors to Ajmer Division:

Do:

  • Dress modestly while visiting religious sites.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local cuisine and sample some street food.
  • Take a camel safari to explore the desert.

Don’t:

  • Engage in public displays of affection.
  • Enter a temple or mosque with shoes on.
  • Disrespect local customs and cultures.
  • Touch any of the historical monuments or artifacts.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ajmer Division

Ajmer Division is a safe destination for tourists, with low crime rates. However, visitors should exercise caution and be vigilant, especially in crowded public places.

Health in Ajmer Division

Visitors to Ajmer Division should take necessary precautions to avoid illnesses such as Hepatitis A and B, Typhoid, and Malaria. It is advisable to take out travel health insurance before visiting the region and consult a doctor before embarking on any activities.

Getting to Ajmer Division: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ajmer Division

The closest airport to Ajmer Division is the Jaipur International Airport, located approximately 135 km away. The airport is well-connected to major cities in India and abroad.

Bus Stations Near Ajmer Division

Ajmer is connected to other major cities in India by a network of state-run and private buses. The they operate from the Ajmer and Pushkar Bus Stand and offer affordable transportation options.

Other Options

Visitors can also opt for private taxis or car rentals for transportation within the region. Trains are another popular mode of transport, with Ajmer Junction being the main railway station in the region.

How to Get Around in Ajmer Division

Ajmer Division offers several transportation options for visitors to explore the region, including local buses, taxis, and autorickshaws.

Where to Go Next from Ajmer Division: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Ajmer Division is located in a region that offers several nearby destinations and excursions. Visitors can explore the following destinations and add to their itinerary:

  • Jaipur: The Pink City, known for its stunning architecture, palaces, and vibrant markets.
  • Jodhpur: The Blue City, home to the iconic Mehrangarh Fort and the bustling Sadar Bazar.
  • Udaipur: The City of Lakes, offering stunning palaces, gardens, and serene lakes.

Conclusion

Ajmer Division is a captivating region that has something for everyone. Its rich history, vibrant culture, stunning natural beauty, and range of activities makes it an ideal travel destination.
