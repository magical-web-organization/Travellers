Uniyara Travel Guide - Everything You Need to Know Before You Go!

Are you planning a trip to Rajasthan and looking for off-the-beaten-path destinations to visit? Look no further than Uniyara! This charming town is located in the Ajmer Division of Western India and offers a unique blend of cultural and natural attractions. In this travel guide, we’ll take a closer look at what you can expect from Uniyara and share some helpful tips to plan your trip.

Introduction to Uniyara

Uniyara is a small town located in Rajasthan, known for its stunning natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. While it may not be as well-known as some of the larger cities in the state, it’s definitely worth a visit for anyone looking to experience Rajasthan’s unique charm and hospitality.

The town is dotted with historic temples and monuments, dense forests, and scenic hills. It’s surrounded by the Aravalli mountain range, which makes it an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts who love hiking, biking, and nature trails. The best part? The town is still relatively undiscovered, so you can expect fewer crowds and more opportunities to explore on your own terms.

List of Places to Visit in Uniyara

There are plenty of things to see and do in Uniyara, whether you’re an avid history buff, nature lover, or adventure seeker. Here are some of the must-visit places in the town.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Khandela Haveli - This magnificent haveli is a tribute to traditional Rajasthani architecture, with intricate carvings and frescoes adorning the walls and ceilings. You can take a guided tour of the property to learn more about its history and architecture.
  • Uniyara Ki Chhatriyan - These impressive cenotaphs are dedicated to the rulers of Uniyara and were built in the 18th century. They are located on a hilltop, offering stunning views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Shri Devalaya Ji Maharaj Temple - This 200-year-old temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is famous for its intricate carvings and beautiful architecture. It’s a must-visit for anyone interested in Hindu mythology and culture.

Natural Attractions of Uniyara: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Uniyara Hill - This vast hill range is one of the best places to go hiking and offers incredible views of the town and its surroundings.
  • Bhimlat Mahadev Waterfall - Located about 17 km from Uniyara, this stunning waterfall is a popular spot for picnics and nature lovers. It’s also considered to be a sacred spot by the locals.
  • Uniyara Forest Reserve - This dense forest reserve is teeming with wildlife and offers fantastic opportunities for bird watching and trekking. You can spot mammals like jackals, hyenas, and leopards here.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Uniyara

The hills around Uniyara are some of the best places for hiking and biking in Rajasthan. There are plenty of routes to explore, ranging from easy to challenging. Some of the most popular trails include:

  • Uniyara Hill Loop Trail - This 9 km trek takes you through verdant forests and scenic viewpoints, offering stunning views of the town and the Aravalli range.
  • Bhimlat Mahadev to Jawsaiya Trail - This 21 km biking trail takes you through the heart of Uniyara’s rural landscape and passes through small villages and agricultural fields.

Best Things to Do in Uniyara: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking, biking, and exploring the town’s cultural and natural attractions, there are plenty of other activities and tours to choose from. Here are some of the best options:

  • Jeep Safari in Uniyara Reserve Forest - This guided safari takes you through the dense jungles of Uniyara and offers the chance to spot wildlife such as leopards, hyenas, and jackals.
  • Rural Village Tour - This tour takes you to the nearby villages where you can learn about the rural way of life and indulge in local customs and traditions. You can also witness local artisans making pottery, weaving, and other handicrafts.
  • Photography Tour - This tour takes you to some of the best viewpoints around Uniyara and is perfect for passionate photographers looking to capture the town’s beauty in all its glory.

Week-Long Itinerary for Uniyara

If you’re planning to spend a week in Uniyara, here’s a sample itinerary that will help you make the most of your time:

Food and Drink Scene of Uniyara

Uniyara is well-known for its rich and flavorful cuisine, which is a perfect blend of spices and local ingredients. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Dal Baati Churma - This quintessential Rajasthani dish features baked flour dumplings served with lentil curry and a sweet crumbly side dish made from wheat, ghee, and sugar.
  • Ker Sangri Sabzi - This spicy and tangy dish is made with a vegetable called ker, which is similar to capers, and sangri beans, which are the seeds of a tree that grows in arid regions.

Local Beverages

  • Lassi - This refreshing drink made from yogurt, water, and sugar is perfect to beat the heat during summer.
  • Chaach - Made from buttermilk, this soothing drink helps to cool down the digestive system and is perfect to enjoy with spicy dishes.

Where to Stay in Uniyara: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Uniyara has plenty of accommodation options to suit all budgets, from luxury heritage hotels to comfortable guesthouses and hostels. Some of the best options include:

  • Khandela Haveli Heritage Hotel - This beautifully restored haveli boasts of elegant rooms, traditional decor, and an ambiance of old-world charm.
  • Uniyara Exquisite Hotel - This modern hotel offers comfortable and spacious rooms equipped with all necessary amenities.
  • Maa Shakti Paying Guest House - This budget-friendly guesthouse is perfect for backpackers and solo travelers, with clean and cozy rooms and friendly service.

What to Buy in Uniyara: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When it comes to shopping in Uniyara, you won’t be disappointed. From handicrafts and textiles to jewelry and spices, there are plenty of local products and souvenirs to take back home. Here are a few things you should consider buying:

  • Kundan Jewelry - This traditional jewelry is made with precious stones and enameling and is popular among both locals and tourists.
  • POSHAK - This colorful and embellished traditional Rajasthani dress is made with silk and is perfect for special occasions.
  • Spices - Rajasthan is famous for its spices, including cumin, coriander, and turmeric, which are used in several Rajasthani dishes.

Local Culture and Customs of Uniyara

Uniyara is a town that prides itself on its rich cultural heritage, and it’s essential to respect local customs and traditions when visiting. Here are a few things you should keep in mind:

  • Clothing - Rajasthan is a conservative state, and it’s advisable to dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Footwear - You will need to remove your shoes before entering many temples and other religious sites. It’s best to wear slip-on shoes or sandals to make the process easier.
  • Greeting - It’s polite to greet the locals with a smile and a “Namaste” (hello) or “Sasriyakal” (goodbye).

Best Time to Visit Uniyara

The ideal time to visit Uniyara is between October and March, when the weather is pleasant, with clear skies and cooler temperatures. Summer months (April to June) can be quite hot, with temperatures soaring to 45 degrees Celsius or more.

Do’s and Don’ts of Uniyara

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Uniyara:

Do:

  • Dress appropriately when visiting religious sites.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Carry plenty of water and sunscreen when exploring outdoors.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter.
  • Do not indulge in any illegal or unethical activities.
  • Do not photograph people without their permission.

Safety and Health

Uniyara is a relatively safe town, but it’s always advisable to take some precautions while traveling. Here are some safety and health tips to keep in mind:

Safety in Uniyara

  • Always carry a copy of your ID and passport with you.
  • Avoid venturing out alone at night.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and be cautious of pickpockets in crowded areas.

Health in Uniyara

  • Drink only bottled water or properly boiled and filtered water.
  • Carry a basic first-aid kit with you.
  • Check for any travel advisories with the local consulate before you depart.

Getting to Uniyara: Transportation Options and Logistics

Uniyara is well-connected to major cities in Rajasthan through road, rail, and air. Here’s how you can get there:

Airports Near Uniyara

  • Jaipur International Airport - Located 191 km from Uniyara, Jaipur airport is the closest airport.
  • Indira Gandhi International Airport, Delhi - Located 318 km from Uniyara, Delhi airport is another option.

Bus Stations Near Uniyara

  • Kuchera Bus Stand - Located 15 km from Uniyara, this is the closest bus station.
  • Ajmer Bus Stand - Located 100 km from Uniyara, this is another option.

Other Options

  • Train - The nearest railway station is Kuchera, located 15 km from Uniyara.
  • Private Cabs/Taxis - You can also hire private cabs or taxis from Jaipur or Delhi.

How to Get Around in Uniyara

Uniyara is a relatively small town, and most of the attractions are within walking distance. You can also hire a bicycle or motorcycle to explore on your own or hire a taxi or auto-rickshaw for longer distances.

Where to Go Next from Uniyara: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have time to spare, there are plenty of nearby destinations and excursions you can explore from Uniyara. Here are a few options:

  • Pushkar - Located 125 km from Uniyara, Pushkar is a beautiful town known for its colorful bazaars and sacred lake.
  • Ranthambore National Park - Located 233 km from Uniyara, this national park is home to tigers, leopards, and other wildlife.
  • Chittorgarh Fort - Located 116 km from Uniyara, this historic fort is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and offers fantastic views of the Aravalli hills.

Conclusion

Uniyara is a hidden gem, waiting to be explored. Whether you’re looking for a cultural, natural, or adventurous experience, this charming town has something for everyone. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has helped you plan your trip to Uniyara and made your experience all the more enjoyable!
