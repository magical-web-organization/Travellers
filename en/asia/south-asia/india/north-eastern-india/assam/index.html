Introduction to Assam

Assam is a beautiful North-Eastern state in India, known for its lush tea plantations, wildlife reserves, and diverse culture. This region is tucked away in the Himalayan foothills and is home to many tribes, each with their unique traditions and customs.

Assam is also known for its wildlife sanctuaries, including the UNESCO World Heritage Site, Kaziranga National Park, which is home to the endangered one-horned rhinoceros, elephants, tigers, and many other animals. Apart from wildlife, there are plenty of other attractions to explore in Assam, including beautiful temples, historical sites, and scenic landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Assam: Activities and Tours

  1. Take a river cruise on Brahmaputra
  2. Experience the local handicrafts in Sualkuchi
  3. Visit tea plantations and taste the local tea
  4. Experience the tribal village life
  5. Attend the Bihu festival in January

Week-Long Itinerary for Assam

Day 1: Arrive in Guwahati and visit Kamakhya temple
Day 2: Explore the Brahmaputra river and take a river cruise
Day 3: Visit Pobitora Wildlife Sanctuary and Kaziranga National Park
Day 4: Experience the local culture and handicrafts in Sualkuchi
Day 5: Visit some serene temples and historical sites in Sivasagar
Day 6: Visit the UNESCO World Heritage Site Majuli Island
Day 7: Return to Guwahati and depart

Safety and Health

Safety in Assam

Assam is a relatively safe state to visit for travelers. However, it is advised to take precautions and avoid going out alone after dark.
