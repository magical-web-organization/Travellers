Introduction to Loktak Lake

Located in the state of Manipur in the North-Eastern region of India, Loktak Lake is the largest freshwater lake in the country. Spread over an area of 287 sq km, the lake is located at an altitude of 760 meters above sea level, and is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna. Loktak Lake is an important tourist destination for its natural beauty as well as cultural significance.

Food and Drink Scene of Loktak Lake

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chamthong: a vegetable stew made with seasonal vegetables and fish
  • Iromba: a dish made with mashed boiled vegetables and fish

Local Beverages

  • Chak-hao kheer: a dessert made with black rice and milk
  • Atingba: a local beer made with rice

Local Culture and Customs of Loktak Lake

The people of Manipur are known for their love of art and culture. The indigenous tribes of Manipur, collectively known as Meiteis, have a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in their art, music, dance, and festivals. The people of Manipur are also known for their excellent culinary skills and hospitality.

Best Time to Visit Loktak Lake

The best time to visit Loktak Lake is from November to February, when the weather is pleasant and cool. The monsoon season, from June to September, should be avoided due to heavy rainfall and the possibility of flooding.

Do’s and Don’ts of Loktak Lake

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and traditions
  • Carry insect repellent and warm clothing, especially in winter
  • Stay hydrated

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the environment
  • Disturb the wildlife or pluck flowers

Safety and Health

Safety in Loktak Lake

Loktak Lake is generally safe for tourists. However, it is advisable to take necessary precautions while travelling, especially in remote areas.

Health in Loktak Lake

Travellers should take appropriate health measures before visiting Loktak Lake. It is advisable to carry mosquito repellents and take precautions against water-borne diseases.

How to Get Around in Loktak Lake

Local taxis and auto-rickshaws are available for travel around the lake. Boat rides are also available for exploring the islands and scenic spots. Visitors can also opt for a guided tour to discover the best of Loktak Lake.
