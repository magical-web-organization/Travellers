Introduction to Gorumara National Park

Located in the Northern Plains of West Bengal, Gorumara National Park covers an area of 79.99 square kilometers. It houses various species of mammals, birds, and reptiles, making it a paradise for nature lovers. The park is open for visitors throughout the year, except from Monsoon months (mid-June to mid-September), when it remains closed due to heavy rainfall.

Local Culture and Customs of Gorumara National Park

The ethnic diversity of the place adds a unique charm to the culture of Gorumara. The native tribes like Lepchas and Sherpas are predominantly found in the vicinity. They generally offer a warm welcome to tourists. The festivals like Dasai, Durga Puja, and Diwali are amongst the most renowned ones.

Best Time to Visit Gorumara National Park

The ideal time to explore Gorumara is during the winter months from November to February. During this time, the sky remains clear with moderate temperature, making it optimal for wildlife safari and outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Gorumara National Park

Do:

  • Observe the animals from a distance and do not feed them.
  • Use eco-friendly products
  • Follow the directions of the guide strictly.

Don’t:

  • Carry plastics into the park.
  • Make loud noises in the forest
  • Smoke and Light fires inside the park.

Safety and Health

Safety in Gorumara National Park

Tourists are constantly under surveillance in the park, ensuring their safety throughout their trip. Visitors are advised to carry valid identification proof with them at all times. Hiring a registered guide is mandatory for wildlife safari adventurers.

Health in Gorumara National Park

While visiting Gorumara, ensure to carry required vaccinations and medication for malaria, typhoid, and other common diseases.

Getting to Gorumara National Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Gorumara is 80 km away from Bagdogra Airport, the closest major airport to the park. Visitors can take a taxi or hire a car to reach the park from the airport. Several state-run and private buses are also available from Siliguri to various parts of Dooars.

Airports Near Gorumara National Park
