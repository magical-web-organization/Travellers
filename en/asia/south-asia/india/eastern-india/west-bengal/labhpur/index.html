Labhpur, located in the Birbhum district of West Bengal, India, is a treasure trove of natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and tranquil villages. If you’re looking for a unique travel experience and want to explore a lesser-known place, Labhpur is an excellent destination for you. This comprehensive guide will provide everything you need to know to plan your excursion to Labhpur, from the best places to visit to the best things to do, where to stay, and what to eat.

Introduction to Labhpur

Labhpur is a small town situated in the western part of West Bengal, India. It’s known for its rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and peaceful surroundings. The town is surrounded by hills, forests, and streams, offering a tranquil haven from the hustle-bustle of the city.

List of Places to Visit in Labhpur

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Nandalal Basu Art Gallery & Museum

The Nandalal Basu Art Gallery & Museum is an institution dedicated to the works and life of Nandalal Basu, a prominent Bengali artist, and painter. The museum displays an extensive collection of Basu’s artwork and personal memorabilia.

Maa Tara Temple

The Maa Tara Temple is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to the goddess Tara Ma. The temple is said to grant wishes to devotees and is especially popular during the Navratri festival.

Natural Attractions of Labhpur: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The Hills of Labhpur

Labhpur is located amid the picturesque hills of the Chhota Nagpur Plateau. You can enjoy serene nature walks or hikes through the rolling hills while exploring the surrounding regions.

Murardi Lake

Murardi Lake is a serene and scenic lake located just outside of Labhpur town. The lake provides a perfect picnic spot and boating opportunity to the visitors.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Labhpur

Muradi Hill

The Muradi Hill is located 1.5 km away from the Muradi Lake. This hill is famous for hiking.

Amtora Hill

The Amtora Hill is 4 km away from Labhpur, and it is popular among hikers and trekkers.

Best Things to Do in Labhpur: Activities and Tours

Attend Local Festivals

Labhpur is known for its festivals, and many celebrations happen throughout the year. Attending festivals like Chaitra Sankranti, Laxmi Puja, and Biswa Karma Puja provide a unique and exciting experience.

Take a Nature Walk in Labhpur

Labhpur is surrounded by hills, trees, and streams, making it ideal for nature walks. The lush green vistas and the serene environment of Labhpur provide a perfect escape from the mundane city life.

Food and Drink Scene of Labhpur

Must-Try Dishes

Chhanabora

Chhanabora is a traditional dish made with paneer and besan, and it’s a must-try dish in Labhpur.

Shuktoni

Shuktoni is another traditional dish made with pumpkin and some spices. The dish is mildly spicy and is a perfect complement to the main course.

Local Beverages

Aam Panna

Aam Panna is a refreshing drink made with raw mangoes and various spices. It’s a perfect beverage to beat the summer heat.

Taal Kher

Taal Kher is a sweet and sour drink made with Taal fruit (a variety of palm fruit) and tamarind. It’s a popular and refreshing drink during the summer season.

Where to Stay in Labhpur: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Gurukul Lodge

Gurukul Lodge is located in the heart of Labhpur town and offers comfortable accommodation for travelers.

Labh Resort

Labh Resort is situated near Labhpur Lake and offers comfortable accommodation amid lush green surroundings.

What to Buy in Labhpur: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Terracotta Handicrafts

Labhpur is famous for its terracotta handicrafts, which include earthen pots, vases, and figurines. It’s an excellent souvenir to take back home.

Brass Items

Brass items such as lamps, wall hangings, and utensils are famous in Labhpur, and they make an excellent purchase for those interested in local handicrafts.

Local Culture and Customs of Labhpur

Labhpur is known for its rich cultural heritage that reflects the vibrant and colorful traditions of West Bengal. Bengali art, music, and cuisine are an essential part of the town’s cultural legacy.

Best Time to Visit Labhpur

The best time to visit Labhpur is from October to March when the weather is dry and pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Labhpur

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Dress modestly when visiting temples and other religious places.
  • Carry a power bank when traveling to remote areas.

Don’t:

  • Litter in public places.
  • Enter temples with shoes on.
  • Drink tap water.

Safety and Health

Safety in Labhpur

Labhpur is a relatively safe town, and the crime rate is low. It’s advisable to take precautions while traveling at night and avoid visiting unfamiliar places alone.

Health in Labhpur

Labhpur does not have many medical facilities. It’s essential to carry a basic first aid kit and medication. Dengue fever is prevalent here, so it’s advisable to use mosquito repellents.

Getting to Labhpur: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Labhpur

The nearest airport to Labhpur is the Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport in Kolkata, which is about 220 km away.

Bus Stations Near Labhpur

Buses regularly ply between Kolkata and Labhpur. The nearest bus station to Labhpur is the Bolpur Bus Terminal in Birbhum district, which is about 25 km away from Labhpur.

Other Options

Taxis, cabs, and private cars are also available for hire from Kolkata to Labhpur.

How to Get Around in Labhpur

Taxis, autos, and cycle rickshaws are readily available in Labhpur town for transportation. Local guide arrangements can also be executed through the resort where you are lodging.

Where to Go Next from Labhpur: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Labhpur is a perfect gateway to nearby destinations like Santiniketan, Purulia, and Kolkata. Santiniketan is a famous cultural destination located pretty close to Labhpur. The Visva Bharati University here holds a significant position in the cultural and creative spheres of India. Purulia, on the other hand, has natural beauty lying in its authentic form, and you can take a road trip option in Purulia for its untouched terrains, mighty hills, and picturesque waterfall views. Kolkata is a bustling city, and there are not one but many things you can do here.

Conclusion

Labhpur is a hidden gem that offers a unique travel experience. This comprehensive guide will help you plan and execute a perfect trip to Labhpur. So, pack your bags, get ready to explore the rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and serene surroundings of Labhpur.
