Introduction to Esplanade

Esplanade is one of the most vibrant areas in Kolkata, the capital city of West Bengal in Eastern India. This downtown commercial hub boasts a cosmopolitan mix of heritage landmarks, contemporary businesses, and cultural attractions, making it a top destination for tourists and locals alike. Whether you’re interested in discovering the history and art of the city or exploring its nature and outdoor activities, Esplanade has something for everyone. In this guide, we’ll give you an overview of the best things to do, see, and experience in Esplanade, including top attractions, hiking and biking routes, local food and drink, where to stay, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Esplanade

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Esplanade is home to some of the most iconic cultural landmarks in Kolkata, including the following:

  • Indian Museum: Established in 1814, Indian Museum is the oldest and largest museum in India, and one of the oldest in the world. It has a rich collection of artifacts, rare specimens, and objects of historical and cultural significance, showcasing the diversity and complexity of India’s heritage.
  • Victoria Memorial: Built in the early 20th century, Victoria Memorial is a stunning marble structure that houses a museum and art gallery. It commemorates the memory of Queen Victoria and is dedicated to the art and culture of India.
  • St. Paul’s Cathedral: Built in the Gothic Revival style, St. Paul’s Cathedral is an Anglican church that dates back to 1847. Its spire is the tallest in Kolkata, and it has a rich history and cultural significance.

Natural Attractions of Esplanade: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Esplanade is a bustling commercial district, but it also has some natural attractions and scenic views that are worth exploring. Here are a few options:

  • Maidan: Maidan is a vast open field that spans 3 km in length and 1 km in width. It is a popular spot for locals to hang out, exercise, and enjoy some fresh air. You can also take a horse carriage ride or enjoy a picnic with family and friends.
  • Eco-Tourism Park: Located on the outskirts of Kolkata, Eco-Tourism Park is a great place to spend a day outdoors. It has a lake, gardens, and plenty of recreational activities, including boating, cycling, and picnicking. There are also adventure sports facilities, such as ziplines and rope courses.
  • Hooghly River: The Hooghly River flows through Kolkata and is a great place to take a boat ride or enjoy a sunset view. You can also visit some of the iconic bridges that span the river, including the Howrah Bridge and the Vidyasagar Setu Bridge.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Esplanade

Esplanade is not necessarily known for its hiking and biking routes, but there are a few options for people who want to explore the area on foot or two wheels. Here are a couple of suggestions:

  • Maidan Walking Tour: You can take a walking tour of Maidan, which is a popular activity for visitors and locals alike. You can explore the wide-open field and historical landmarks, such as the Victoria Memorial and the Eden Gardens cricket stadium.
  • Cycling Tour: There are several companies that offer cycling tours of Kolkata, including some that pass through Esplanade. You can see the city from a different perspective and explore the hidden alleyways and neighborhoods that you might not find on foot.

Best Things to Do in Esplanade: Activities and Tours

Esplanade has a lot to offer in terms of experiences that will enrich your visit. Here are some top things to do and tours to take while in Esplanade:

  • Heritage Walk: You can take a guided heritage walk through Esplanade and learn about the history and architecture of the area. You’ll see some of the iconic landmarks and historical buildings that define this vibrant district.
  • Food Tour: Kolkata is considered a foodie’s paradise, and Esplanade is no exception. You can take a food tour of the area and try some of the local street food, such as puchkas and jhal muri. You can also visit some of the famous restaurants and cafes that serve traditional Bengali cuisine.
  • Cultural Performances: Kolkata has a rich cultural heritage, and Esplanade is a great place to experience it. You can attend a classical music concert, watch a traditional dance performance, or see a theater play at one of the many cultural centers and venues in the area.

Food and Drink Scene of Esplanade

Esplanade is a foodie’s paradise, with a wide array of street food, traditional Bengali cuisine, and international specialties. Here are a few must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Puchkas: Also known as gol gappas, these crisp puris filled with spiced potato and tamarind water are a Kolkata specialty.
  • Kathi Rolls: A popular street food in Kolkata, Kathi Rolls are a kind of wrap filled with veggies, meat, and sauces. They’re perfect for a quick and tasty snack on the go.
  • Chingri Malai Curry: A traditional Bengali dish made with prawns, coconut milk, and spices. This rich and flavorful curry is a must-try for seafood lovers.

Local Beverages

  • Cha/Kadak Chai: Tea is a staple drink in Kolkata, and you can find many street vendors and tea shops that serve it. Kadak chai, or strong tea, is a popular choice among locals.
  • Lassi: A refreshing yogurt-based drink that is often flavored with fruit or spices, lassi is a popular beverage in Esplanade and throughout Kolkata.
  • Jaljeera: A tangy and spicy drink made with cumin and mint, jaljeera is a refreshing cooler that is perfect for hot summer days.

Where to Stay in Esplanade: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Esplanade has a range of accommodations to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are a few options:

  • The Oberoi Grand: One of the most luxurious hotels in Kolkata, The Oberoi Grand is located in the heart of Esplanade and offers top-notch amenities and services. The hotel has a heritage charm and a timeless elegance that makes it a favorite among discerning travelers.
  • The Lindsay: A great mid-range option in Esplanade, The Lindsay is known for its exceptional service and cozy atmosphere. The hotel has a rooftop terrace that offers panoramic views of the cityscape and is a great spot to unwind after a busy day.
  • Nest International: This budget-friendly hotel is located just a few minutes’ walk from Esplanade Metro Station and is a great option for travelers on a budget. The rooms are clean and comfortable, and the staff is friendly and welcoming.

What to Buy in Esplanade: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Esplanade has a vibrant shopping scene, with plenty of options for unique souvenirs and local products. Here are a few ideas:

  • Terracotta Pottery: Terracotta pottery is a popular handicraft in West Bengal, and you can find many shops and stalls that sell it in Esplanade. You can buy items like vases, lamps, figurines, and more.
  • Saris and Textiles: Kolkata is famous for its handwoven saris and textiles, and Esplanade has many shops that specialize in them. You can find a wide variety of fabrics, patterns, and styles, ranging from traditional to modern.
  • Jewelry and Accessories: Kolkata has a rich tradition of jewelry making, and you can find many shops in Esplanade that sell beautiful and unique pieces. You can also find accessories like bags, scarves, and shawls that are made by local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Esplanade

Esplanade is a melting pot of cultures and traditions, and it’s essential to be respectful of the local customs and social norms. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Dress Conservatively: Kolkata is a conservative city, and it’s customary to dress modestly in public places. It’s advisable to cover your shoulders and knees, especially if you’re visiting religious sites.
  • Respect Religious Customs: Kolkata is home to many religious communities, and it’s important to be mindful of their customs and traditions. For example, you should remove your shoes before entering a temple or a mosque.
  • Greet People with Respect: In Kolkata, it’s customary to greet people with respect, especially elders. The traditional way is to use the word “Namaste” and fold your hands in front of your chest.

Best Time to Visit Esplanade

The best time to visit Esplanade is during the winter season, from October to February. The weather is pleasant and mild, with temperatures ranging from 10 to 25 degrees Celsius. You can enjoy outdoor activities, explore the cultural landmarks without the heat and humidity, and attend some of the festivals and events that take place during this time. However, keep in mind that this is also the peak tourist season, so you should book your accommodations and tours in advance.

Do’s and Don’ts of Esplanade

Do:

  • Explore the City on Foot: Esplanade is a great place to walk around and explore, and you’ll discover many hidden gems and local experiences by doing so.
  • Try the Local Cuisine: Kolkata is a foodie’s paradise, and Esplanade has some of the best street food and traditional Bengali cuisine in the city.
  • Be Mindful of the Local Customs: Kolkata has a rich culture and traditions, and it’s important to be respectful and mindful of them.

Don’t:

  • Bargain too Hard: Bargaining is common in the markets and stalls of Kolkata, but it’s important to do so respectfully and not too aggressively.
  • Litter or Pollute: Kolkata is already dealing with environmental challenges, and it’s crucial to do your part in keeping the city clean and sustainable.

Safety and Health

Safety in Esplanade

Esplanade is a safe and vibrant area in Kolkata, but you should take the usual precautions that you would when travelling in any big city. Here are a few tips:

  • Be Mindful of Your Belongings: Pickpocketing is a common risk in crowded places, so it’s essential to keep your valuables safe and secure.
  • Use Licensed Taxis: If you’re taking a taxi, make sure it’s licensed and metered. Avoid using unlicensed cabs or taxis that ask for inflated prices.
  • Stay Aware of Your Surroundings: Keep yourself informed about the local news, events, and political climate, and avoid areas that are known to be unsafe.

Health in Esplanade

Kolkata, like many parts of India, is prone to some health risks, especially for travelers. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Stay Hydrated: Kolkata can get hot and humid, especially during the summer months, so it’s essential to stay hydrated and drink plenty of water.
  • Use Mosquito Repellent: Kolkata is a high-risk area for mosquito-borne diseases like dengue, malaria, and chikungunya, so it’s important to use mosquito repellent and take other precautionary measures.
  • Get Vaccinated: Before travelling to Kolkata, consider getting vaccinated for diseases like typhoid, hepatitis A and B, and tetanus.

Getting to Esplanade: Transportation Options and Logistics

Esplanade is well-connected to the rest of Kolkata and other parts of West Bengal. Here are some options for getting to and around Esplanade:

Airports Near Esplanade

  • Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport: Located about 16 km from Esplanade, this is the main airport in Kolkata that serves both domestic and international destinations.

Bus Stations Near Esplanade

  • Kolkata Bus Stand: Located near the Esplanade Metro Station, this is the main bus stand that connects the area to different parts of Kolkata and West Bengal.

Other Options

  • Metro: Esplanade has its own metro station that connects it to other parts of Kolkata. The metro is a fast and affordable way to get around the city.
  • Taxi and Rickshaw: Taxis and rickshaws are readily available in Esplanade and are a convenient way to get around the area. Make sure to use licensed taxis and agree on the fare beforehand.

How to Get Around in Esplanade

Esplanade is a pedestrian-friendly area that you can explore on foot. However, if you want to cover more ground or get to other parts of Kolkata, here are some options:

  • Metro: The Esplanade Metro Station is located in the heart of the area and connects it to other parts of the city. The Kolkata Metro is clean, efficient, and affordable.
  • Bus: Kolkata has a vast network of buses that run through the city and connect different neighborhoods. The buses are affordable and frequent, but they can get crowded and chaotic.
  • Taxi and Rickshaw: Taxis and rickshaws are available in Esplanade and are a convenient way to get around. Make sure to use licensed taxis and agree on the fare beforehand.

Where to Go Next from Esplanade: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore more of West Bengal and its surrounding areas, here are a few options:

  • Darjeeling: Known for its tea plantations and beautiful scenery, Darjeeling is a popular destination in West Bengal that’s located about 600 km from Kolkata. You can take a train, bus, or car to get there.
  • Sundarbans: The Sundarbans is a UNESCO World Heritage Site that’s known for its mangrove forests and wildlife, including the Royal Bengal Tiger. You can take a boat ride and explore the area’s flora and fauna.
  • Bhutan: As a neighboring country, Bhutan is an excellent option for a longer excursion that will give you a glimpse into a different culture and landscape. You can take a flight or overland trip to reach Bhutan.

Conclusion

Esplanade is a unique and vibrant area in Kolkata that offers something for everyone, from cultural landmarks and outdoor activities to delicious food and warm hospitality. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, this travel guide will help you plan your trip and make the most of your time in Esplanade. So pack your bags, book your tickets, and get ready to explore this exciting and dynamic city!
