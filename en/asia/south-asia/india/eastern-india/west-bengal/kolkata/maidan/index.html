Introduction to Maidan

Maidan, also known as the “lungs of Kolkata,” is a vast stretch of greenery that covers around 400 hectares of land. It is situated in the heart of Kolkata city, in West Bengal, Eastern India. Maidan is a center of various cultural, social and historical activities that Kolkata is famous for. With its lush green fields and serene natural beauty, Maidan offers a unique experience to visitors.

List of Places to Visit in Maidan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Victoria Memorial

Victoria Memorial is an iconic monument that was built in 1921 as a tribute to Queen Victoria of England. It is a museum that showcases the history of British India and Kolkata. One can see various artifacts, paintings, and sculptures of British royals, Indian leaders, and different historical figures in the museum.

St. Paul’s Cathedral

St. Paul’s Cathedral is a famous church located in the heart of Kolkata. It was built in 1847, and its stunning design, including stained glass windows, attracts visitors from all over the world. The church’s peaceful atmosphere and serene beauty make it a perfect destination for those looking for some solitude.

Indian Museum

Indian Museum is one of the oldest museums in India, established in 1814. It has six sections that showcase different aspects of Indian history, including archeology, art, and culture. The museum offers a glimpse into India’s rich heritage and is an excellent place to explore for history enthusiasts.

Natural Attractions of Maidan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Eden Gardens

Eden Gardens is a massive cricket stadium located in the heart of Kolkata. It is one of the largest cricket stadiums globally and has a seating capacity of over 66,000. The stadium’s lush green fields and a magnificent view of the city’s skyline make it a must-visit destination for cricket fans and tourists alike.

Maidan Maidan

Maidan Maidan is the central park of Maidan and is a great place for outdoor activities like picnics, jogging and cycling. The park also has a lake where visitors can go boating and enjoy the tranquility of nature.

Fort William

Fort William is an ancient fort that was built in 1781 during the British rule. The fort is a mix of history and natural beauty, as it is situated on the banks of River Hooghly and has a lush green garden inside its premises.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Maidan

Southern Avenue to Fort William

This route takes visitors from the bustling Southern Avenue into the greenery of Maidan up to the ancient Fort William.

Victoria Memorial to Kolkata Race Course

The Victoria Memorial to Kolkata Race Course route gives visitors a glimpse into the history of the British Raj through Victoria Memorial then takes them to a scenic, sprawling race course.

Best Things to Do in Maidan: Activities and Tours

Horse Carriage Rides

Horse carriage rides are a unique way to explore Maidan’s lush greenery and historical sites. Visitors can hire carriages for specific routes or a complete tour of Maidan.

Nighttime Heritage Walk

A heritage walking tour after sunset is a different experience that allows visitors to appreciate the illumination of the various monuments and learn about the people and the events that shaped Kolkata.

Food and Drink Scene of Maidan

Must-Try Dishes

Puchkas

Puchkas or golgappas are a famous street food in Kolkata made with spiced chickpea filling and tangy tamarind water.

Kathi Rolls

Kathi rolls are a popular snack in Kolkata made with grilled meat or vegetables and wrapped in a flatbread.

Mughlai Paratha

Mughlai Paratha is a savory flatbread stuffed with minced meat, onions, and spices, and served with chutney and yogurt.

Local Beverages

Tea

Kolkata is famous for its teas, and visitors should not miss the opportunity to sample the local brews made with milk and spices.

Chaas

Chaas or buttermilk is a traditional Indian drink made with yogurt, water, and spices. It is a refreshing drink after a day of sightseeing or adventure sports.

Where to Stay in Maidan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hotels

The Oberoi Grand

The Oberoi Grand is a luxury hotel located near Maidan’s heart, offering excellent views of Victoria Memorial.

The Lalit Great Eastern

The Lalit Great Eastern is a heritage hotel that underwent a renovation and restoration process recently. It is a comfortable and luxurious option for visitors to Maidan.

Hostels

Zostel Kolkata

Zostel Kolkata is a budget-friendly hostel located near the center of Maidan. It is an excellent option for solo travelers or backpackers looking for a comfortable stay at a low price.

Guesthouses

Indismart Hotel

Indismart Hotel provides a comfortable stay with modern amenities at a reasonable price.

What to Buy in Maidan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Royal Bengal Tiger Souvenir

The Royal Bengal Tiger is India’s national animal, and visitors should not miss out on buying a souvenir made in its image as a keepsake from Maidan.

Bengali Sarees

Bengali sarees are famous for their intricate designs and colorful displays. They make an elegant gift or personal collecion.

Local Culture and Customs of Maidan

Maidan holds a vital place in Kolkata’s history and cultural heritage. Kolkata is known for its food, literature, music, and arts. Visitors should respect the local customs and traditions, including dress codes for religious and historical sites.

Best Time to Visit Maidan

The best time to visit Maidan is between October to March when the climate is pleasant and suitable for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Maidan

Do:

  • Try local cuisine and beverages
  • Visit historical monuments and museums
  • Respect local customs and traditions

Don’t:

  • Litter and pollute the natural surroundings of Maidan
  • Wear shorts or revealing clothes in religious or historical sites
  • Take photographs without the permission of locals or guards

Safety and Health

Safety in Maidan

Visitors should be careful when walking or cycling in rural areas, especially at night. It is also best to keep valuable items safe.

Health in Maidan

Visitors should look out for mosquitoes and stay protected from them to avoid diseases like dengue and malaria. It is best to carry a mosquito repellent to be safe.

Getting to Maidan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Maidan

Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose International Airport is located around 15 kilometers away from Maidan, and visitors can get to Maidan by hiring a taxi or a cab service.

Bus Stations Near Maidan

State-run and private-run buses serve Kolkata and can be a cost-effective means of getting to Maidan. Visitors can board buses from BBD Bagh or Howrah bus station.

Other Options

Visitors can also take a metro or a local train to get to Maidan. Bicycles, rickshaws, and taxis are also available and can provide a localized experience of the surroundings.

How to Get Around in Maidan

Visitors can explore Maidan by horse-drawn carriages, bicycles, or feet. The different transportation options available are taxis, auto-rickshaws, or buses.

Where to Go Next from Maidan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Kolkata

Kolkata is the cultural capital of India and is also known as the “City of Joy.” It offers a vibrant and unique experience to visitors with its rich history, food, music, and art.

Howrah Bridge

Howrah Bridge is an iconic landmark that is situated on River Hooghly and connects Kolkata to Howrah. It offers majestic views of the river and is one of the busiest bridges globally.

Conclusion

Maidan is a beautiful destination that offers a unique experience to visitors with its history, natural beauty, and cultural heritage.
