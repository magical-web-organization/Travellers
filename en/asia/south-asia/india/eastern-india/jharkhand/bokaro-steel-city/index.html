Introduction to Bokaro Steel City

Bokaro Steel City is a city in the Eastern Indian state of Jharkhand. It is known for its wide-ranging scenic beauty with mountains, rivers, and lakes. This industrial city also offers an insight into India’s steel production, with the Bokaro Steel Plant being the main attraction for travelers.

Bokaro Steel City was created in the 1960s as a state-owned company to cater to India’s growing steel production demand. Today, it is one of the leading industrial cities in India that offers a great travel package for globetrotters.

List of Places to Visit in Bokaro Steel City

When you plan a trip to Bokaro Steel City, there are several places to explore, including:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Bokaro Steel City offers an insight into India’s past with historical landmarks and museums such as:

  • Bokaro Steel Plant: The city’s prime attraction, the plant offers guided tours to observe the various manufacturing processes and the latest technology used in the production of steel.
  • Ram Mandir: A famous temple situated at Budh Bihar in Bokaro.
  • Jawaharlal Nehru Biological Park: A zoo located in the city that has several rare species of birds and animals.

Natural Attractions of Bokaro Steel City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The following natural attractions offer travelers picturesque surroundings and fun outdoor activities:

  • Parasnath Hills: A trekking point for the adventure lovers with a shrine of Lord Parasnath.
  • Garga Dam: This dam, located near Bokaro, offers fishing opportunities and a serene environment.
  • Jagannath Temple: Situated in the heart of the city, this temple is home to several festivals that the city celebrates with great enthusiasm.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bokaro Steel City

Hiking and biking are two of the best ways to explore Bokaro Steel City’s considerable scenic beauty. The preferred routes for these activities are the Parasnath Hills and Tilaiya Dam. With plenty of hills and forests in the vicinity, the city offers a thrilling experience to the hiking and biking enthusiasts.

Best Things to Do in Bokaro Steel City: Activities and Tours

Besides the above-mentioned tourist spots, several other activities and tours can be availed while in Bokaro Steel City:

  • A visit to the Tata Steel Zoological Park: The park has various animal species such as the Bengal tiger, crocodiles, and snakes.
  • Take a boat ride on the Garga Dam, and try your hands at fishing.
  • A visit to the Garga Dam Bird Sanctuary: The reserve offers an opportunity to witness the birdlife of the region.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bokaro Steel City

Here is a week-long itinerary to ensure you make the most of your trip to Bokaro Steel City:

Day 1:

  • Start by visiting the Bokaro Steel Plant, in the morning, to understand the city’s biggest industry.
  • After lunch, head to the nearby Parasnath Hills for a thrilling trekking experience and an opportunity to witness the beautiful sunset from the tallest peak.

Day 2:

  • Take a cycling tour of the city to explore the natural surroundings better.
  • Lunch at a local restaurant that serves the famous delicacies of the region.

Day 3:

  • Visit the Tata Steel Zoological Park and also take a walk around Jubilee Park, another city park where you can spot several species of plants and flowers.

Day 4:

  • After breakfast, head to Garga Dam for a boat ride and fishing experience.
  • For lunch, try out the street food of Bokaro Steel City.

Day 5:

  • Take a stroll of the city market, where you can shop for clothes, souvenirs, and accessories.
  • In the evening, join a cultural program and experience the city’s cultural heritage.

Day 6:

  • Take a bus ride to Jagannath Temple and enjoy the Puja rituals performed in the morning.
  • Visit Ram Mandir, a sacred Hindu shrine.

Day 7:

  • Explore some local businesses and Industries, take a tour of the Bokaro Industrial Area Facilities, and eat at a local restaurant.

Food and Drink Scene of Bokaro Steel City

The food scene of Bokaro Steel City offers several savory and sweet dishes that will satisfy travelers’ taste buds. The local cuisine has influences from the surrounding states, making it unique for the visitors.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Litti Chokha: A traditional dish made of wheat flour and stuffed with gram flour and mashed potatoes. It is served with chokha, made of roasted vegetables such as brinjal, tomato, and potato.
  • Churma: A sweet dessert made of wheat flour, jaggery, and ghee.
  • Dhuska: A snack made of rice flour and gram flour and is fried.

Local Beverages

Bokaro Steel City also has a specialty drink made from toddy, a fermented sap extracted from the coconut or palm trees. Toddy is available at the local bars and is best enjoyed with a serving of spicy chowmein.

Where to Stay in Bokaro Steel City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bokaro Steel City offers accommodations to suit all budgets, ranging from budget hostels to luxury hotels. Here are a few examples:

  • Hotel Bokaro Palace: A stunning boutique hotel with spacious rooms and excellent food.
  • Hotel Aryan: A budget-friendly option with clean and comfortable rooms.
  • Bokaro Hotel Canary: A luxurious hotel with several modern amenities and facilities.

What to Buy in Bokaro Steel City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

The city’s bustling markets offer several souvenirs and local products to take back home as memories. Handicraft shops are prevalent in Bokaro Steel City, which will give you a glimpse of the city’s unique art and crafts items. Some front-runner shops include:

  • City Centre Market: A bustling marketplace that caters to all your needs.
  • Wooden Craft Emporium: A shop that sells excellent hand-carved wooden items and souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Bokaro Steel City

The locals of Bokaro Steel City are hospitable and follow the traditional Indian way of living. The city celebrates all religions’ festivals with great enthusiasm and has a unique cultural heritage.

Best Time to Visit Bokaro Steel City

The best time to visit Bokaro Steel City is from November to March. The weather is pleasant, and the natural beauty of the region is at its best.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bokaro Steel City

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Do carry a pollution mask as the city sees a lot of pollution.

Don’t:

  • Don’t indulge in illegal activities.
  • Don’t litter the natural surroundings, as it will impact their beauty.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bokaro Steel City

Bokaro Steel City is a relatively safe place for tourists. However, it’s recommended to take reasonable precautions like not wandering alone at night and staying away from isolated areas.

Health in Bokaro Steel City

The city has several hospitals and pharmacies that offer adequate medical facilities. It is also essential to keep personal hygiene and carry any necessary medications.

Getting to Bokaro Steel City: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bokaro Steel City

Birsa Munda Airport in Ranchi is the nearest airport to Bokaro Steel City, which is approximately 110 km away. From there, you can hire private taxis or buses to reach Bokaro Steel City.

Bus Stations Near Bokaro Steel City

The Bokaro Bus Stand, located near the railway station, is the central bus station for all local and interstate buses.

Other Options

Bokaro Steel City lies on the Delhi-Kolkata route and has excellent railway connectivity to all major Indian cities.

How to Get Around in Bokaro Steel City

The city has an excellent public transportation system, with buses and private taxis available for commuting. If you are interested in leisurely exploration, renting a bicycle or scooter is a great idea.

Where to Go Next from Bokaro Steel City: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some of the nearby destinations that one could plan an excursion trip to from Bokaro Steel City:

  • Madhuban Dam: Situated at a distance of 30 km from Bokaro Steel City, the dam is known for its scenic beauty and surrounding greenery.
  • Hazaribagh National Park: Located at about 80 km from Bokaro Steel City, the park is known for its flora and fauna and offers several opportunities for wildlife spotting.
  • Ranchi: The capital city of Jharkhand is situated at a distance of 120 km from Bokaro Steel City and is well known for its waterfalls, temples, and scenic views.

Conclusion

Bokaro Steel City is a perfect blend of nature, history, and modern infrastructure that offers a great travel experience. From scenic beauty to adventure, from traditional foods to handicrafts, Bokaro Steel City has something for everyone. Follow our above guide, and you will have found everything you need to know about the city to plan your travel itinerary.
