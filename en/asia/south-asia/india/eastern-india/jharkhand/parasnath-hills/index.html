Introduction to Parasnath Hills

Parasnath Hills is a mountain range that is situated in Jharkhand, in Eastern India. It is known for its religious importance, as it is believed to be the place where 20 of the 24 Jain Tirthankaras achieved Nirvana. The mountain range is home to the highest peak in Jharkhand, which is known as Parasnath Hill. This pilgrimage site is also surrounded by beautiful forests, scenic landscapes, and an array of flora and fauna. So, whether you are a nature lover, a history buff, or a spiritual seeker, Parasnath Hills has something to offer for everyone.

Local Culture and Customs of Parasnath Hills

Parasnath Hills is an important pilgrimage site for the Jain community. It is important to dress modestly while visiting the temples and shrines.

Best Time to Visit Parasnath Hills

The best time to visit Parasnath Hills is between October and February when the weather is pleasant and ideal for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Parasnath Hills

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Carry a copy of your identification documents
  • Try local food and drinks

Don’t:

  • Litter in the natural surroundings
  • Pluck flowers or disturb wildlife
  • Dress inappropriately while visiting temples and shrines

Safety and Health

Safety in Parasnath Hills

While visiting Parasnath Hills, it is important to be careful while trekking and hiking, especially during the monsoon season when the trails can become slippery.

Health in Parasnath Hills

It is advisable to carry necessary medication and consult a doctor before embarking on a trip to Parasnath hills.
