Introduction to Ambikapur

Nestled in the foothills of the charming Surguja district, Ambikapur is a must-visit destination in Chhattisgarh. It is a perfect amalgamation of rich cultural heritage, serene natural beauty, and thrilling adventure spots. The picturesque landscapes, historic sites, and traditional art forms of Ambikapur are sure to leave every visitor spellbound.

List of Places to Visit in Ambikapur

If you plan to visit Ambikapur, you must add these must-see destinations to your itinerary.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Tattapani: Tattapani is a hot spring that holds significant religious value. It is believed that the holy water of Tattapani can absolve one of their sins. The hot water has its unique charm and is an ideal spot for a relaxing dip.
  • Bambleshwari Temple: Devotees, tourists, and architecture enthusiasts visit the ancient Bambleshwari temple located in Rajgiri hills. Bambleshwari Temple is one of the 52 Shaktipeeths in India and is dedicated to the goddess of power and strength.
  • Thinthini Patthar Caves: Thinti Patthar caves are a perfect blend of natural and man-made caves. These caves are home to a large number of bats and are a treat for history enthusiasts who come to this place in search of hidden treasures inside the caves.

Natural Attractions of Ambikapur: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Sarvodaya Park: Sarvodaya Park is a perfect place for travelers looking for a picnic spot surrounded by lush greenery. It has a beautiful lake, manicured lawns, a jogging track, and fun rides for kids. The park also serves as an excellent bird watching destination.
  • Semarsot Wildlife Sanctuary: If you are an adventure lover, the Semarsot wildlife sanctuary is the place to be. It is home to several wild species, and one can spot a large number of migratory birds in the winter season. You can also indulge in trekking, hiking, and camping activities in the sanctuary.
  • Tiger Point: If you are in search of some adrenaline rush, Tiger Point is the right destination for you. It is a cliff that overlooks the beautiful and serene landscapes of Ambikapur. This spot is a beautiful spot for a campsite, and sunrise views from here are breathtaking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ambikapur

Ambikapur is known for its scenic beauty and most extensive flora and fauna. The place has many trekking and biking routes that offer a once-in-a-lifetime experience. From rock-climbing, hiking to forest walks, there is something for everyone.

Here are a few breathtaking routes to explore:

  • Sarvodaya Park Trail: The Sarvodaya Park Trail is a walking trail that winds its way through dense and lush green forests. This trail provides an opportunity to enjoy the flora and fauna around Ambikapur.
  • Tiger Point Trek: The Tiger Point Trek involves rock climbing and reaching the cliff to enjoy the panoramic views of Ambikapur. This trek requires a good level of fitness and endurance as it involves a lot of climbing.
  • Semarsot Wildlife Sanctuary: Trek through Semarsot wildlife sanctuary is a walk through the dense forest and spot rare species of animals and birds.

Best Things to Do in Ambikapur: Activities and Tours

Apart from trekking, rock climbing, and exploring the city, here are a few other things to do in Ambikapur:

  • Cultural tours: Take a cultural tour to explore the traditional tribal art forms, local handicrafts and learn about the rich cultural heritage of Ambikapur.
  • Hot Spring Bath: Soak in the hot springs of Tattapani and rejuvenate yourself.
  • Wildlife Safari: Take a wildlife safari, and spot migratory birds and other animals in the forests around the city.
  • Visit to Waterfalls: Visit the Amritdhara waterfall and enjoy the cascading beauty of nature.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ambikapur

Day 1: Visit Tattapani Hot Springs in the morning and spend the evening trekking/ rock climbing at Tiger Point.

Day 2: Embark on a wildlife safari at the Semarsot Wildlife Sanctuary in the morning, and visit the Barbar Caves in the evening.

Day 3: Spend the day relaxing and exploring the Sarvodya park and enjoy the sunset at Bambleshwari temple.

Day 4: Go for a Kathikund trek and enjoy the scenic beauty of the forest trails.

Day 5: Spend the day exploring the Ambikapur city, visiting historic sites and enjoying local delicacies.

Day 6: Visit Amritdhara waterfalls and spend your day relaxing amid the natural beauty of this scenic spot.

Day 7: Relax and take a day trip to Ranchi city.

Food and Drink Scene of Ambikapur

Ambikapur is a paradise for foodies. It offers a plethora of delicacies that adds to your travel experience. Here are a few must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chilla: Chilla is a local delicacy made from rice flour and lentils, which is cooked on a hotplate to serve as dumplings.
  • Kohda Curry: Kohda is a rare jungle vegetable. Kohda curry is a traditional tribal recipe made from this vegetable that is usually eaten with rice or chapatti.
  • Balushahi: Balushahi is a sweet dish made with flour and deep-fried doughballs soaked in sugar syrup.
  • Poha: Poha is a popular breakfast option made with flattened rice, potato, onions, and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Mahua: Mahua is a local beverage made from the flowers of the Mahua tree. It has a sweet fragrance and a fruity taste.
  • Madua: Madua is a traditional alcoholic beverage made from fermented millet.

Where to Stay in Ambikapur: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ambikapur offers various accommodation options ranging from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxurious hotels. Here are a few options:

  • Hotel Grand Basant: A budget hotel located in the heart of the city.
  • Hotel Ambika: A mid-range hotel with elegant rooms situated in the center of the city.
  • Hotel Pankh Palace: A luxury hotel located close to the Ambikapur city bus stand.

What to Buy in Ambikapur: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Ambikapur is famous for its unique handicrafts and traditional artworks. Here are a few things that you can take back home:

  • Bell Metalware: Ambikapur is known for its bell metalware or Dokra artwork, which makes for an excellent souvenir.
  • Kosa Silk: Kosa silk is a well-known saree, and Ambikapur produces some of the finest quality silk woven sarees, perfect for a wedding outfit.
  • Handmade Jewelry and Pottery: Amber Pottery and handmade jewelry made by artisans is a unique souvenir to bring home.

Local Culture and Customs of Ambikapur

Ambikapur has a rich cultural heritage that has been preserved for many years. The local tribes have their arts, culture, and customs. Their traditional art forms and handicrafts are made using natural resources and carry immense value. Ambikapur celebrates various festivals throughout the year, and visitors are welcome to join in the festivities.

Best Time to Visit Ambikapur

The best time to visit Ambikapur is between October and February. The weather is pleasant during this time, and one can enjoy outdoor activities and scenic views comfortably.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ambikapur

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Dress appropriately while visiting religious places.
  • Keep the city clean and maintain its beauty.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter.
  • Do not deface or damage any historical monuments.
  • Do not pick up plants or flowers from wildlife sanctuaries or parks.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ambikapur

Ambikapur is a safe destination for tourists. However, it is recommended to take necessary precautions during traveling and avoid venturing out alone at night.

Health in Ambikapur

It is advisable to carry necessary medications and avoid consuming the food and water sold by street vendors. Don’t forget to take necessary vaccinations and a health insurance cover while traveling.

Getting to Ambikapur: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ambikapur

The Swami Vivekananda Airport in Raipur is the nearest airport to Ambikapur, which is about 345 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Ambikapur

Ambikapur has its own bus terminal located in the city. There are many regular bus services to Ambikapur from nearby cities and towns.

Other Options

One can also take a taxi or book a private car to reach Ambikapur.

How to Get Around in Ambikapur

The best way to explore the beautiful city of Ambikapur is by renting a bike or a scooter. One can also travel by autorickshaws or taxis, which are readily available across the city.

Where to Go Next from Ambikapur: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Raipur: Raipur is the capital of Chhattisgarh and is home to many nature and wildlife reserves, which are a must-visit.
  • Kanha National Park: Kanha National Park is a wildlife reserve and is famous for its tiger reserve and rare species of birds.
  • Amarkantak: Amarkantak is a pilgrimage site and is the source of three rivers- Narmada, Johila, and Sone.

Conclusion

Ambikapur is a mesmerizing city with its unique culture, heritage, and natural beauty. From breathtaking landscapes to historic sites, Ambikapur has it all. Visit this charming destination, explore the city, and carry memories to cherish for a lifetime.
