Introduction to Borigumma

Borigumma is a small town in Southern Odisha, located in the Rayagada district. The town is known for its beautiful natural vistas, ancient temples, and off-the-beaten-path experiences. Borigumma is an ideal destination for travelers looking to experience the authentic rural lifestyle of India.
