Introduction to Lachung

Nestled in the lap of Eastern Himalayas, Lachung is a charming village in North Sikkim that features stunning natural beauty and rich culture. The word “Lachung” literally translates to “small pass”, which refers to the location of the village on the banks of Lachung Chu River. The village is surrounded by magnificent mountains, flowing rivers, and lush greenery that make it an ideal destination for nature lovers.

List of Places to Visit in Lachung

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Lachung Monastery

Built in the year 1880, Lachung Monastery is one of the oldest monasteries in Sikkim. It is also known as Lachen Gompa and is a prominent cultural attraction in Lachung. The monastery is decorated with intricate designs and paintings that showcase the rich heritage and culture of Sikkim.

Singhik View Point

Located at a distance of 22 km from Lachung, Singhik View Point offers a breathtaking view of the entire Lachung Valley. It is also the place from where one can witness the mesmerizing sunrise over the snow-capped mountains.

Natural Attractions of Lachung: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Yumthang Valley

Situated at an altitude of 11,693 feet above sea level, Yumthang Valley is a paradise for nature lovers. The valley features a diverse range of flowers and is also known as the “Valley of Flowers”. It is an ideal place for those seeking adventure, as there are several trekking routes available in the vicinity.

Shingba Rhododendron Sanctuary

Located near Yumthang Valley, Shingba Rhododendron Sanctuary is another must-see attraction in Lachung. The sanctuary is home to diverse species of rhododendron flowers and is a popular destination among nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lachung

Lachung offers several trekking and biking routes that allow visitors to witness the beauty of the place up close. Some of the popular routes include the Singalila Trek, Goecha La Trek, and the Kanchenjunga Base Camp Trek. Biking enthusiasts can also explore the region by opting for the Lachung-Gangtok biking route, which is approximately 110 km long and offers picturesque views of the mountains and rivers.

Best Things to Do in Lachung: Activities and Tours

Some of the popular activities and tours that tourists can indulge in while in Lachung include:

  • River rafting in Teesta River
  • Yak safari in Yumthang Valley
  • Snowboarding and skiing in Yumthang Valley
  • Camping in Lachung Valley
  • Birdwatching in Shingba Rhododendron Sanctuary

Food and Drink Scene of Lachung

Must-Try Dishes

Lachung offers a diverse range of local delicacies that visitors must try. Some of the popular dishes include:

  • Thenthuk: a noodle soup made with vegetables and meat
  • Momos: steamed or fried dumplings filled with meat or vegetables
  • Phagshapa: pork cooked with radish and chilies
  • Sael Roti: a deep-friend rice bread that is a popular breakfast dish

Local Beverages

Visitors can also try out the local alcoholic beverage known as Chaang, which is made from fermented millet. Butter tea is another local beverage that is a must-try for tea lovers.

Where to Stay in Lachung: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Lachung offers several accommodation options for visitors, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxury resorts. Some of the popular places to stay in Lachung include:

  • Royal Lachung
  • Golden Valley Lachung
  • Yarlam Resort

What to Buy in Lachung: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors can buy several souvenirs and local products such as yak wool shawls, carpets, and blankets. Tea lovers can also take home the world-famous Darjeeling tea from the local markets in Lachung.

Local Culture and Customs of Lachung

Lachung is a culturally rich destination with a significant influence of Tibetan culture. The people of Lachung are warm and friendly, and their traditional attire and architecture reflect their rich heritage.

Best Time to Visit Lachung

The best time to visit Lachung is from October to June, when the weather is pleasant and favorable for outdoor activities. However, visitors should avoid the monsoon season from July to September, as heavy rainfall can cause landslides and other hazards in the region.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lachung

Do:

  • Always respect the local customs and culture while visiting Lachung.
  • Carry warm clothes and comfortable shoes for outdoor activities.
  • Carry enough cash, as there are limited ATMs in Lachung.

Don’t:

  • Litter or harm the natural surroundings while exploring the region.
  • Disrespect local customs or religious beliefs.
  • Take photographs of people and religious sites without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lachung

Lachung is a safe destination for tourists, but visitors are advised to take precautions while exploring the region. Travelling with licensed travel agents and avoiding unknown areas at night is recommended.

Health in Lachung

Visitors are advised to carry necessary medication and health insurance before visiting Lachung. The high altitude and harsh weather conditions can cause altitude sickness or other health issues, and visitors should consult their doctor before planning their trip.

Getting to Lachung: Transportation Options and Logistics

Getting to Lachung can be a bit challenging, as there are limited transportation options available. Visitors can opt for shared taxis or hire private cars from Gangtok or Bagdogra to reach Lachung. It takes approximately 6-7 hours to cover the 117 km distance between Gangtok and Lachung.

Airports Near Lachung

The nearest airport to Lachung is Bagdogra Airport, which is located at a distance of approximately 203 km.

Bus Stations Near Lachung

There are limited bus services available between Gangtok and Lachung. Private taxis are a preferred mode of transportation for visitors.

Other Options

Visitors can also opt for shared taxis or private cars to reach Lachung from other major cities such as Darjeeling, Kalimpong, and Siliguri.

How to Get Around in Lachung

Shared taxis are a common mode of transportation in Lachung, and visitors can also hire private cars or bikes to explore the region on their own. Guided tours and trekking expeditions are also popular among tourists.

Where to Go Next from Lachung: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can plan a visit to nearby attractions such as Lachen, Yumthang, and Gangtok after exploring Lachung. These destinations offer a diverse range of natural beauty, cultural attractions, and adventure activities, making them an ideal extension to a trip to Lachung.

Conclusion

Lachung is a hidden gem that offers a perfect blend of natural beauty, adventure, and cultural heritage. From trekking to skiing, from monasteries to local delicacies, Lachung has something to offer for everyone. A trip to Lachung is an ideal way to escape the mundane and experience the true essence of the Himalayas.
