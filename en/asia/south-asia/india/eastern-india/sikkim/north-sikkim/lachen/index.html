Introduction to Lachen

Lachen is a charming village located in North Sikkim, in the eastern Indian state of Sikkim. Nestled in the lap of the Himalayas, Lachen is known for its breathtaking natural beauty, cultural richness, and warm hospitality. The village is situated at an altitude of 8,838 feet above sea level and is surrounded by alpine forests, snow-clad mountains, and emerald green valleys. Lachen is a perfect destination for those seeking peace, tranquility, and adventure amidst nature.

List of Places to Visit in Lachen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Lachen Monastery: Also known as Ngodub Choling Monastery, it is one of the oldest and most significant monasteries in North Sikkim. The monastery is adorned with beautiful Buddhist art, paintings, and sculptures.
  • Thangu Valley: It is a high altitude valley located at a distance of 23 km from Lachen. The valley is renowned for its scenic beauty and is an excellent place to witness the traditional nomadic lifestyle of the locals.
  • Gurudongmar Lake: It is a pristine and sacred lake located at an altitude of 17,800 feet above sea level. The lake holds significant religious and cultural importance among the locals and is surrounded by snow-clad mountains and breathtaking views.

Natural Attractions of Lachen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Night Sky Observation: Due to its high altitude and remoteness, Lachen is an ideal location for stargazing and observing the Northern Lights. One can head to the nearby Gurudongmar Lake or Thangu Valley for the best viewing experience.
  • Trek to Green Lake: It is a strenuous trek that takes you through lush green forests, pristine lakes, and snow-clad mountains. The trek offers an unmatched view of the Kanchenjunga and Mt. Everest ranges.
  • Chopta Valley: It is a beautiful valley known for its vibrant rhododendron forests and alpine meadows. The valley is also home to some of the rarest species of wildlife, including musk deer, Himalayan tahr, and snow leopard.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lachen

Trekking Routes

  • Green Lake Trek: It is a 9-day trek that starts from Lachen and takes you through beautiful forests, lakes, glaciers, and mountains.
  • Tholung Trek: It is a 7-day trek that takes you through picturesque landscapes, dense forests, and pristine lakes.

Biking Routes

  • Lachen to Lachung: It is a scenic route that takes you through the beautiful valleys, lush green forests, and snow-capped mountains of North Sikkim.
  • Lachen to Gurudongmar Lake: It is a challenging and adventurous route that takes you through rugged terrain and offers unmatched views of the Himalayan ranges.

Best Things to Do in Lachen: Activities and Tours

  • Yak Safari: One of the best ways to experience the local culture and traditions is to go on a yak safari. The safaris take you through the pristine valleys and forests of Lachen while riding on the back of a gentle yak.
  • Bird Watching: Lachen is home to some of the most exotic and rarest species of birds. You can hire a local guide and explore the nearby forests and meadows for an unforgettable bird watching experience.
  • Village Tour: A village tour is an excellent way to learn about the local culture, customs, and traditions. You can interact with the locals, visit the traditional houses, and taste the local cuisine.

Food and Drink Scene of Lachen

Must-Try Dishes

  • Thukpa: A hearty noodle soup made with vegetables or meat.
  • Momos: Steamed dumplings filled with meat or vegetables.
  • Chhang: A fermented rice beer, a popular local drink.

Local Beverages

  • Butter Tea: A salty tea made with butter and yak milk.
  • Chang: A millet-based alcoholic beverage brewed by local farmers.

Where to Stay in Lachen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Apple Valley Inn: A cozy and comfortable hotel located in the heart of Lachen village.
  • Lachen View Point Homestay: A traditional homestay with warm hospitality, located on the outskirts of the village.
  • Delight Hotels Royal Lachen: A luxury hotel with stunning views of the Himalayas and world-class amenities.

What to Buy in Lachen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handicrafts: Lachen is famous for its traditional handicrafts, such as woolen shawls, carpets, and hand-woven baskets.
  • Tea: Sikkim is known for its premium quality tea, and you can buy a wide variety of tea blends in Lachen.
  • Local Spices: The locals of Lachen use a variety of locally grown spices in their cuisine, which can make great souvenirs to bring back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Lachen

The locals of Lachen are predominantly Buddhist and follow a tradition of hospitality and warmth towards visitors. It is essential to dress appropriately and respect the local traditions, customs, and culture. Taking photographs of the locals or inside the monasteries without permission is considered disrespectful.

Best Time to Visit Lachen

The best time to visit Lachen is between the months of October and May when the weather is pleasant and clear. The monsoon season (June-September) should be avoided as the region can experience heavy rainfall, which can make trekking and outdoor activities challenging.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lachen

Do:

  • Dress appropriately and modestly, especially while visiting the monasteries and other religious places.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Respect the local culture and traditions.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs of the locals or inside the monasteries without permission.
  • Litter or pollute the environment.
  • Damage any natural or cultural heritage sites.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lachen

Lachen is generally a safe destination, but visitors are advised to take appropriate precautions and be aware of their surroundings. It is recommended to avoid exploring the remote areas alone and hire a local guide for trekking and outdoor activities.

Health in Lachen

Visitors are advised to take necessary precautions against altitude sickness and carry adequate medication for any pre-existing conditions. It is also essential to stay hydrated and maintain proper hygiene while travelling.

Getting to Lachen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lachen

  • Bagdogra Airport: Located at a distance of 190 km from Lachen, it is the nearest airport and has regular flights to major Indian cities.

Bus Stations Near Lachen

  • Gangtok Bus Station: Regular buses and taxis are available from the capital city of Gangtok, which is located at a distance of 107 km from Lachen.

Other Options

  • Shared Taxis: Shared taxis are a popular and affordable mode of transportation in Lachen and nearby areas.
  • Private Taxis: Private taxis can be hired from Gangtok or Siliguri for a comfortable and hassle-free journey.

How to Get Around in Lachen

The best way to explore Lachen is on foot or by hiring a Jeep or SUV. Local guides can also be hired for trekking and outdoor activities.

Where to Go Next from Lachen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Lachung: A picturesque village located at a distance of 47 km from Lachen, known for its pristine valleys, waterfalls, and hot springs.
  • Yumthang Valley: A beautiful valley located at a distance of 50 km from Lachen, known for its vibrant rhododendron forests and hot springs.
  • Gangtok: The bustling capital city of Sikkim, known for its vibrant culture, gourmet food scene, and panoramic views of the Himalayas.

Conclusion

Lachen is a hidden gem in the heart of North Sikkim, offering a unique blend of natural beauty, culture, and adventure. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can plan your trip to Lachen and make the most of your stay. Whether you are looking for a peaceful retreat or an adrenaline-fueled adventure, Lachen has something for everyone. Don’t forget to pack your camera, hiking boots, and a sense of adventure for an unforgettable experience in Lachen.
