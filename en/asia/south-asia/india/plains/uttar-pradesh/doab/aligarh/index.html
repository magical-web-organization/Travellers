Introduction to Aligarh

Aligarh, a city located in the Doab region of Uttar Pradesh in India and is known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance. It is a perfect blend of the old and the new, with its ancient forts, monuments, museums, and modern establishments. The city is home to the renowned Aligarh Muslim University, which boasts a rich history of excellence and diversity.

List of Places to Visit in Aligarh

There are plenty of top-rated tourist attractions in Aligarh, from ancient historical sites and museums to stunning natural landscapes. Here are some of the must-visit places in Aligarh:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Aligarh Fort
  2. Sir Syed Academy Museum
  3. Aligarh Muslim University (AMU)
  4. Maulana Azad Library
  5. Teerthdham Mangalayatan
  6. Naqvi Park

Natural Attractions of Aligarh: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Shekha Jheel
  2. Aligarh Mahaveer Aquarium

Hiking/Biking Routes of Aligarh

For nature lovers and fitness enthusiasts, there are plenty of hiking and biking routes in Aligarh, among which the following are considered the best:

  1. AMU Road to Sasni Gate
  2. Aligarh Munshi Pulia to Dube Chauraha

Best Things to Do in Aligarh: Activities and Tours

  1. Visit the Aligarh Fort
  2. Explore Sir Syed Academy Museum
  3. Take a stroll through Teerthdham Mangalayatan
  4. Visit Shekha Jheel
  5. Attend various cultural events at AMU

Food and Drink Scene of Aligarh

Aligarh is famous for its unique cuisine, influenced by Mughal and Awadhi flavors. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages that you should not miss:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Aligarh Ki Biryani
  2. Korma and Kebabs
  3. Nihari

Local Beverages

  1. Thandai - a refreshing drink made with almonds, milk, and sugar
  2. Lassi - a yogurt-based drink

Local Culture and Customs of Aligarh

Aligarh is a diverse city comprising of people from various cultures and religions. The predominant religion in Aligarh is Islam. The city is known for its unique blend of Mughal and Awadhi cultures and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Aligarh

The best time to visit Aligarh is between November and February when the weather is pleasant and optimum for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Aligarh

Do:

  1. Respect the local customs and traditions.
  2. Dress modestly when visiting religious places.
  3. Try the local food.

Don’t:

  1. Engage in any form of political discussion.
  2. Take pictures of people without their permission.
  3. Over-tip at restaurants.

Safety and Health

Safety in Aligarh

Aligarh is a relatively safe city to visit, but it’s always advisable to take necessary precautions while traveling. Here are some safety tips to keep in mind:

  1. Try to avoid traveling alone at night.
  2. Beware of pickpockets and bag snatchers.
  3. Avoid discussing sensitive issues with strangers.

Health in Aligarh

Aligarh has a few good hospitals and healthcare centers, and It is always a good idea to carry necessary medication while you travel.

Getting to Aligarh: Transportation Options and Logistics

Aligarh is well-connected to other cities and towns across India by air, road, and rail.

Airports Near Aligarh

The nearest airport to Aligarh is the Indira Gandhi International Airport in Delhi, which is approximately 138 km away.

Bus Stations Near Aligarh

Aligarh has several bus stations that connect the city to major cities in Uttar Pradesh, including:

  1. UPSRTC bus station, Aligarh
  2. Aligarh Bus Stop

Other Options

You can also reach Aligarh by train as it is well-connected with major cities in India.
