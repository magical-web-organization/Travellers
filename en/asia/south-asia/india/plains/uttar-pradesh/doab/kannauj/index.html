Introduction to Kannauj

Kannauj is a small town situated in the Doab region of Uttar Pradesh, India. The town is known for its rich cultural heritage and historical significance. It is said to be one of the oldest settlements in India, boasting a rich history that dates back to ancient times. Kannauj is renowned for its perfumes and fragrances, which are exported all over the world. The town is also a popular pilgrimage spot and attracts thousands of devotees every year. Kannauj is an ideal destination for those who want to experience the authentic culture of Uttar Pradesh.

List of Places to Visit in Kannauj

Kannauj has a lot of attractions to offer visitors. From culturally-rich museums to natural scenic views, this town has something for everyone.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kannauj Museum: This museum houses various artifacts and historical items, including coins, pots, sculptures, and manuscripts, that depict the rich cultural heritage of the town.
  • Kannauj Fort: The fort is a must-visit attraction in Kannauj. It is said to have been built in the 10th century and is a prime example of ancient Indian architecture. The fort is currently under the supervision of the Archaeological Survey of India.
  • Kannauj Temple: Situated on the banks of the River Ganges, the Kannauj Temple is an ancient shrine that attracts thousands of devotees. The temple is known for its intricate carvings and beautiful architecture.

Natural Attractions of Kannauj: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Kannauj Waterfall: This waterfall is situated on the outskirts of the town and is a popular picnic spot. The natural beauty of the waterfall, coupled with the serene surroundings, makes it an ideal place for nature lovers.
  • Talgram Bird Sanctuary: The Talgram Bird Sanctuary is home to a variety of bird species, including kingfishers, herons, and cormorants. The sanctuary is a popular spot for bird watchers and nature enthusiasts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kannauj

Kannauj offers some great biking and hiking routes for adventure enthusiasts. The scenic beauty of the town, coupled with its rich culture and history, makes it an ideal destination for these activities.

  • Kannauj to Sarai: This hiking route covers a distance of around 10 km and takes you through some of the most scenic areas of Kannauj. The route passes through small villages and offers some great views of the countryside.
  • Kannauj to Sasni: This biking route covers a distance of around 20 km and takes you through some of the most picturesque areas of Kannauj. The route offers fantastic views of the River Ganges and takes you through small villages and fields.

Best Things to Do in Kannauj: Activities and Tours

Kannauj offers a variety of things to do for visitors. Whether you want to explore its historical monuments or indulge in some adventure activities, there is something for everyone in Kannauj.

  • Perfume Making Workshops: Kannauj is famous for its perfumes, and visitors can attend workshops to learn the art of perfume making. These workshops offer visitors an opportunity to interact with local craftsmen and learn about their trade.
  • Heritage Walks: Visitors can take heritage walks that cover some of the most significant historical monuments in Kannauj. These tours are conducted by local guides who provide visitors with insights into the rich cultural heritage of the town.

Food and Drink Scene of Kannauj

Kannauj offers some fantastic culinary experiences for visitors. The town is known for its delicious street food and sweets.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Peda: This sweet is made from milk and sugar and is a popular dish in Kannauj. It is usually served as a dessert.
  • Samosa: This is a popular snack in Kannauj and is made from potatoes and peas. It is served with chutney and is a must-try dish for visitors.

Local Beverages

  • Thandai: This is a refreshing drink that is made from milk, almonds, and a variety of spices. It is a popular drink during festivals and celebrations.

Where to Stay in Kannauj: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Kannauj offers a variety of accommodation options for visitors. From budget-friendly guesthouses to luxury hotels, there is something for everyone in Kannauj.

  • Hotel Royal Inn: This is a luxury hotel that offers modern amenities and comfortable accommodation.
  • Kannauj Guesthouse: This guesthouse offers budget-friendly accommodation and is located in the heart of the town.

What to Buy in Kannauj: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Kannauj is famous for its perfumes and fragrances, and visitors can buy these products from local vendors. The town also offers some fantastic handicrafts and textiles that are unique to the region.

Local Culture and Customs of Kannauj

Kannauj has a rich cultural heritage that is deeply rooted in Indian traditions. Visitors should be respectful of the local customs and traditions while in the town. It is customary to remove footwear before entering a place of worship, and visitors should dress modestly while visiting shrines and temples.

Best Time to Visit Kannauj

The best time to visit Kannauj is from October to March. The weather during this time is pleasant, making it ideal for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kannauj

Do:

  • Try the local street food and sweets
  • Attend a perfume-making workshop
  • Respect the local customs and traditions

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing or skimpy clothing while visiting shrines and temples
  • Litter or damage the natural surroundings while hiking or biking in the countryside

Safety and Health

Safety in Kannauj

Kannauj is a relatively safe town, and visitors should not encounter any major security concerns during their stay. However, visitors should be cautious of pickpocketing and petty theft in crowded markets and tourist areas.

Health in Kannauj

Visitors should make sure to stay hydrated during their visit to Kannauj, especially during the summer months. The town does not have many hospitals or medical facilities, so visitors should bring any required medication with them.

Getting to Kannauj: Transportation Options and Logistics

Kannauj can be reached by air, rail, and road.

Airports Near Kannauj

The nearest airport to Kannauj is the Amausi Airport in Lucknow, which is around 110 km away from the town.

Bus Stations Near Kannauj

Kannauj has a bus stand that is well-connected to various towns and cities in Uttar Pradesh.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire private taxis or rental cars to reach Kannauj from nearby towns and cities.

How to Get Around in Kannauj

Visitors can hire bicycles or motorbikes to explore the town. Local taxis and auto-rickshaws are also available for transportation within the town.

Where to Go Next from Kannauj: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Farrukhabad: This town is known for its rich historical heritage and is located around 40 km away from Kannauj.
  • Kanpur: Kanpur is a major city in Uttar Pradesh and is located around 90 km away from Kannauj. The city is known for its industrial heritage and is home to various historical monuments and attractions.

Conclusion

Kannauj is a small town that offers a unique cultural experience for visitors. The town’s historical significance, coupled with its scenic beauty, makes it an ideal destination for those who want to explore the authentic culture of Uttar Pradesh. Visitors can indulge in perfume-making workshops, go hiking or biking in the countryside, or try the local street food and sweets. With its rich heritage and diverse attractions, Kannauj is truly an offbeat gem of Uttar Pradesh.
