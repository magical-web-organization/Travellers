Introduction to Pathankot

Pathankot is a city located in the Majha region of Punjab, India. It is situated at the intersection of the three northern states of Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, and Jammu and Kashmir. The city is surrounded by majestic mountain ranges and is an ideal place for nature lovers and adventure seekers. It is surrounded by lush greenery and offers a perfect blend of history, culture, and nature. It has a rich historical and cultural heritage and is considered to be a significant trade and commercial center.

List of Places to Visit in Pathankot

Whether you’re looking for cultural experiences or natural scenery, Pathankot offers a diverse range of attractions.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Ranjit Sagar Dam: This dam is one of the largest hydroelectric power stations in India and is located on the Ravi River.
  • Shahpurkandi Fort: This historical fort was built in the 16th century and is situated on the banks of River Ravi.
  • Nurpur Fort: This historical fort was built in the 10th century and is known for its architectural heritage.
  • Kathgarh Temple: Famous for its architecture, Kathgarh temple is located in the nearby village, Kathgarh.

Natural Attractions of Pathankot: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mukteshwar Temple: Known for its panoramic view of the valley, Mukteshwar Temple is located on a hilltop.
  • Shahpurkandi Wildlife Sanctuary: Located on the banks of River Ravi, the wildlife sanctuary is home to a diverse range of fauna and flora.
  • Ranjit Sagar Lake: A popular picnic spot, Ranjit Sagar Lake is located at the foot of the Shivalik range.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pathankot

Pathankot offers several hiking and biking routes for adventure enthusiasts. Some of the popular ones include:

  • Dhruva Lake Trek: Dhruva Lake is located in Dalhousie, and the trek takes around 5 hours to complete.
  • Kangra Valley Trek: This trek takes you to the peaceful Kangra valley and takes around 3 days to complete.
  • Lahesh Cave Trek: The Lahesh Cave Trek starts from Mcleodganj and leads you to the snow-capped mountains of Dhauladhar ranges.

Best Things to Do in Pathankot: Activities and Tours

There is no shortage of activities and tours to do in Pathankot.

  • River Rafting: Try river rafting in the gushing waters of River Ravi and enjoy some of the best scenic views.
  • Paragliding: Paragliding in the Kangra Valley is an experience of a lifetime. You can enjoy the breathtaking view of the valley while gliding in the sky.
  • Explore the local markets: Pathankot has a busy and bustling market, offering a wide range of souvenirs and local products.

Food and Drink Scene of Pathankot

Pathankot offers a diverse range of dishes and local beverages that you must try.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chole Bhature: A staple dish, Chole Bhature is spicy chickpeas cooked with puri, bread made of wheat flour.
  • Amritsari Kulcha: A popular dish, Amritsari Kulcha is a spicy flatbread stuffed with potatoes and onion.
  • Butter Chicken: A mild curry made with tender chicken and a buttery tomato sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Lassi: A refreshing drink made from yogurt, sugar, and ice. It is a perfect drink for summers.
  • Kulhad Tea: Served in earthen cups, Kulhad tea is a unique experience and a must-try for tea lovers.

Where to Stay in Pathankot: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Pathankot offers a wide range of accommodation options to suit different budgets and preferences.

  • Luxury Hotels: Citrus Hotel, Lords Eco Inn, and Hotel Unite are some of the popular luxury stay options in Pathankot.
  • Budget Hotels: Hotel Venice, Hotel Sunrise, and Hotel Trisha are some of the budget-friendly stay options in Pathankot.
  • Hostels: Backpacker’s Nest Hostel and KVR Guest House are some of the popular hostels in Pathankot.

What to Buy in Pathankot: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Pathankot is famous for its woolen shawls, carpets, and pashmina shawls. You can also buy some souvenirs from the local market.

  • Shawls and Carpets: Woolen shawls, carpets, and pashmina shawls are popular souvenirs to buy in Pathankot.
  • Phulkari: Phulkari is a traditional embroidery pattern and is a popular souvenir among tourists.

Local Culture and Customs of Pathankot

Pathankot is a city with a rich cultural heritage, and its customs reflect the same.

  • Dress Code: The traditional attire of men is Kurta Pajama, while women wear Salwar Kameez or Saree.
  • Festivals: Baisakhi, Diwali, and Holi are some of the popular festivals celebrated in Pathankot.
  • Hospitality: Pathankot is known for its hospitality, and guests are treated with utmost respect and warmth.

Best Time to Visit Pathankot

The best time to visit Pathankot is from October to March. The weather during these months is pleasant, and you can enjoy outdoor activities and sightseeing without any hassle.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pathankot

Do:

  • Respect the local customs: While in Pathankot, it is essential to respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Carry a light jacket or sweater: Even during summers, the temperature can drop unexpectedly.
  • Try the local cuisine: Pathankot is famous for its cuisine, and you must try some of the local dishes.

Don’t:

  • Litter: Pathankot is a beautiful city, and you must not litter the surroundings.
  • Disrespect the local customs: It is crucial to respect the local customs and etiquette while in Pathankot.
  • Travel alone during late hours: It is not advisable to travel alone during late hours as the city can be unsafe for solo travelers.

Safety and Health

Safety in Pathankot

Pathankot is generally a safe city, but it is essential to take necessary precautions.

  • Avoid isolated areas: Avoid traveling to isolated areas or at odd hours.
  • Be vigilant: Be vigilant of your surroundings and keep an eye on your belongings.

Health in Pathankot

It is crucial to take precautions to ensure good health during your stay in Pathankot.

  • Drink bottled water: Drink only bottled water to avoid any risk of water-borne diseases.
  • Carry a first-aid kit: Carry essential medicines and a first-aid kit with you.

Getting to Pathankot: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Pathankot

  • Sri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport: The nearest airport, located in Amritsar, is around 121 km from Pathankot.

Bus Stations Near Pathankot

  • Pathankot Bus Stand: This is the main bus stand in Pathankot, and there are regular buses to neighboring cities and states.

Other Options

  • Train: Pathankot Railway station is well-connected to major cities of India.
  • Private Cabs: You can hire private cabs to travel to Pathankot from nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Pathankot

Pathankot has a well-connected infrastructure, and you can easily get around the city using various modes of transportation.

  • Auto-Rickshaws: Auto-rickshaws are the most popular mode of transportation in Pathankot.
  • Taxis: Taxis are easily available in Pathankot, and you can hire them for local sightseeing or a trip to nearby cities.
  • Buses: Buses are available for local transportation, and you can also take them for long-distance travel.

Where to Go Next from Pathankot: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Pathankot is surrounded by beautiful destinations, and there are several nearby destinations that you can explore.

  • Dalhousie: Located around 70 km from Pathankot, Dalhousie is a beautiful hill station known for its natural beauty and colonial architecture.
  • McLeodGanj: A cultural hub, McLeodGanj is situated around 65 km from Pathankot, and known for its beautiful monasteries and trekking trails.
  • Amritsar: Located at a distance of around 100 km from Pathankot, Amritsar is a vibrant city known for its rich cultural heritage and spirituality.

Conclusion

Pathankot is a city that offers an unforgettable experience of history, culture, and nature. From its beautiful natural scenery, rich cultural heritage, and delicious culinary scene, Pathankot has something to offer everyone. With this comprehensive guide, you can plan your trip to Pathankot and explore this beautiful city at your leisure.
