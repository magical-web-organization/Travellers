Introduction to Rajpura

Rajpura is a small town nestled in the Malwa region of Punjab. The town is famous for its rich cultural heritage, historical sites and breathtaking natural landscapes. Rajpura is located 35 km south of Chandigarh and is easily accessible by road, rail and air. The best time to visit Rajpura is between October and March, when the weather is pleasant and perfect for outdoors.

List of Places to Visit in Rajpura

Rajpura brims with historical landmarks, serene parks and natural wonders. Here are some of the must-visit attractions in Rajpura:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Kali Mata Temple
  • Gurudwara Nauwin Patshahi
  • DukhBhanjani Ber Tree

Natural Attractions of Rajpura: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Baradari Garden
  • Shimla Pahari
  • Jassian Park

Best Things to Do in Rajpura: Activities and Tours

What to Buy in Rajpura: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Rajpura is known for its vibrant textile and handicraft industry. You can buy souvenirs and local products that reflect the region’s rich culture and heritage. Some popular items to bring back home include:

  • Phulkari embroidered clothes
  • Juttis
  • Punjabi jholas and bags
  • Handicraft items like pottery and clay items

Local Culture and Customs of Rajpura

Punjab, and specifically Rajpura, is famous for its rich cultural heritage, vibrant music, and colorful festivals. Punjabi’s are known for their warm hospitality and love for food. The traditional Punjabi dance form, Bhangra, is famous all over the world. Punjabi women wear phulkari embroidered clothes and men wear colourful turbans.

Best Time to Visit Rajpura

The best time to visit Rajpura is between October and March, when the weather is pleasant and perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rajpura

Do:

  • Try local delicacies and drinks
  • Respect the local customs and traditions
  • Visit the historical landmarks and natural wonders

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothes in religious places
  • Indulge in smoking and drinking in public places
  • Disrespect the locals

Safety and Health

Safety in Rajpura

Rajpura is generally considered safe. But it is advised to take general precautions like locking your valuables, so as not to invite petty theft.

Health in Rajpura

Rajpura offers good health facilities, but it is advisable to take some basic medicines and a first aid kit.

Getting to Rajpura: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Rajpura

The nearest airport to Rajpura is Chandigarh International Airport, located 36 km away.

Bus Stations Near Rajpura

Rajpura has a bus station that is well-connected to nearby cities.

Other Options

You can also reach Rajpura by train. The town is well-connected to major cities via rail.

How to Get Around in Rajpura

Getting around Rajpura is convenient and easy, with several options like local buses, taxis, and rented cars.

Where to Go Next from Rajpura: Nearby Destinations and Excursions
