Introduction to Omkareshwar

Omkareshwar is a hidden gem in Madhya Pradesh, India. Located in Western Madhya Pradesh, it is a popular pilgrimage site for Hindus due to the presence of two ancient Lord Shiva temples, Omkareshwar and Mamleshwar. The town is located on an island in the Narmada river and offers a mix of historical and natural attractions.

This comprehensive travel guide will provide you with essential information on the must-visit places, the best things to do and see, hiking and biking routes, food and drink scene, where to stay, and travel tips to help you make the most of your trip to Omkareshwar.

Best Things to Do in Omkareshwar: Activities and Tours

Omkareshwar offers a range of activities and tours that cater to all kinds of travelers, from solo travelers to families. Here are some of the top things to do in Omkareshwar:

  • Attend Aarti at Omkareshwar Temple

  • Enjoy the breathtaking viewpoints of the town

Local Culture and Customs of Omkareshwar

The people of Omkareshwar are known for their hospitality and friendliness. As this is a pilgrimage site, it is important to be aware of the customs and traditions of the place. Please be respectful and dress appropriately when visiting temples and holy places.

Best Time to Visit Omkareshwar

The best time to visit Omkareshwar is from October to March when the weather is mild and pleasant. However, if you wish to avoid the crowds, it is advisable to visit during the monsoon season from June to September.

Do’s and Don’ts of Omkareshwar

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting Omkareshwar:

Do:

  • Cover your head while entering temples and holy places
  • Respect and follow the customs and traditions of the town
  • Dress modestly
  • Bargain while shopping at local markets

Don’t:

  • Drink alcohol or consume non-vegetarian food in the town
  • Litter in public places
  • Engage in disrespectful behavior towards religious sentiments

Safety and Health

Omkareshwar is relatively safe for travelers. However, here are some essential tips to keep in mind:

Safety in Omkareshwar

  • Avoid walking or traveling alone at night
  • Be careful of pickpockets in crowded areas
  • Keep valuables safely

Health in Omkareshwar

  • Drink only bottled water
  • Carry necessary medications and a first-aid kit
  • Be cautious of street food
