A Comprehensive Tour Guide for Bhiwani, Haryana

Bhiwani, a small town in the state of Haryana, is a fascinating destination for those who love to explore off-the-beaten-path places. Known for its rich cultural heritage, adventurous hiking & biking routes, and local delicacies, this town offers a unique experience to every traveler. This comprehensive tour guide for Bhiwani helps you plan your visit, whether you’re looking for outdoor adventures, historical sites, or culinary delights.

List of Places to Visit in Bhiwani

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Badhra Gurudwara: A popular pilgrimage site for Sikhs, it is believed that Guru Nanak Dev Ji visited this gurudwara.
  • Kharak temple: A must-visit temple in Bhiwani that is believed to be over 5000 years old, dedicated to Lord Shiva.
  • Bhiwani Fort: This historical fort was built during the reign of the Tughlaq dynasty and is a major attraction for history buffs.
  • Tosham Hill: Located around 30 km from Bhiwani, this hill offers stunning views of the Aravalli Range and is considered a sacred place by locals.

Natural Attractions of Bhiwani: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ch. Surinder Singh Stadium: Ideal for sports enthusiasts, this stadium hosts various state and national-level tournaments.
  • Jogi Wala Mandir: A serene temple located in the lap of nature, Jogi Wala Mandir offers a peaceful environment for meditation and introspection.
  • Khaparwas Wildlife Sanctuary: Spread over 45 sq km, this sanctuary is home to blackbuck, blue bull, and deer.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bhiwani

  • Tosham Hill Trek: A moderate trek that involves walking through dense forest trails and rocky terrains, passing through ancient temples.
  • Bhiwani-Hansi-Bhiwani bike route: A 75 km round trip route that passes through scenic countryside, small villages and farmlands.

Best Things to Do in Bhiwani: Activities and Tours

  • Explore the historic Bhiwani Fort
  • Take a dip in the natural hot water springs of Bhawani Khera
  • Enjoy a picnic at Khaparwas Wildlife Sanctuary

Food and Drink Scene of Bhiwani

Must-Try Dishes

  • Bajra Khichdi: A nutritious dish made from bajra, rice, and tuvar dal.
  • Kachri ki Sabzi: A spicy vegetable dish made from wild cucumbers.
  • Anjeer Halwa: A sweet dessert made from figs, sugar, and ghee.

Local Beverages

  • Chaach: A refreshing drink made from curd and water, seasoned with spices.
  • Sharbat: A sweet syrup made from fresh fruits like mango, rose, and pomegranate.

Where to Stay in Bhiwani: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hotel Grand Plaza: A budget-friendly option located in the heart of the town.
  • Hotel Oberoi: A luxury hotel with top-rated amenities.

What to Buy in Bhiwani: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Bajra: A type of millet grown in Haryana, which is used to make various dishes.
  • Handicrafts: Locally-made handicrafts like baskets, pottery, and sculptures are popular souvenirs.

Local Culture and Customs of Bhiwani

Bhiwani has a rich cultural heritage and is known for its hospitality. The majority of the people follow Hinduism and Sikhism. The women here wear traditional clothing like salwar-kameez and sarees, while men mostly wear kurta-pajama.

Best Time to Visit Bhiwani

The best time to visit Bhiwani is from October to March, when the weather is pleasant for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bhiwani

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local cuisine.
  • Bargain while shopping in local markets.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothes in temples and other religious places.
  • Litter or damage the natural surroundings.
  • Use plastic bags and other non-biodegradable materials.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bhiwani

Bhiwani is a relatively safe town for travelers, with low crime rates. However, it’s always recommended to take basic precautions like not carrying too much cash and keeping your belongings secure.

Health in Bhiwani

Bhiwani has adequate medical facilities including hospitals and clinics. It’s always recommended to carry necessary medication and take precautions like drinking only bottled water.

Getting to Bhiwani: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bhiwani

  • Indira Gandhi International Airport in Delhi is the nearest major airport, around 200 km away.

Bus Stations Near Bhiwani

  • Bhiwani Bus Stand is the main bus station in the town, and is well-connected to major cities in North India.

Other Options

  • Taxis and private cars can be easily arranged from Delhi and other towns nearby.

How to Get Around in Bhiwani

  • Buses and auto-rickshaws are the most common modes of transportation in Bhiwani.

Where to Go Next from Bhiwani: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Charkhi Dadri: A small town located around 60 km from Bhiwani, known for its ancient temples and serene environment.
  • Jind: A historic town located around 70 km from Bhiwani, known for its rich cultural heritage and royal palaces.

Conclusion

Bhiwani is a hidden gem in Haryana, perfect for those looking to explore the rich culture and natural beauty of the state. The town offers a mix of adventure, history, and culinary delights, making it an ideal destination for travelers of all types. With this comprehensive guide, plan your visit to Bhiwani and experience the best of what this town has to offer.
