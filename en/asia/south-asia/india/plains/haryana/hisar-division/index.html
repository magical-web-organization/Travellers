Located in northern India, Hisar Division is a charming destination that boasts a wide range of cultural and natural attractions. Introduction to Hisar Division

Hisar Division is a district in the state of Haryana in northern India. It is located in the Indo-Gangetic Plains and is surrounded by the Aravalli hills in the north and east. With a fascinating history and rich cultural heritage, Hisar Division attracts tourists from all over the world. The district has a rich legacy of architecture, arts, and crafts. It is also home to a range of wildlife with different species of flora and fauna.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hisar Division

Hisar Division is also a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts. With its breathtaking natural landscapes and scenery, it is a popular destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the top hiking and biking routes in Hisar Division:

  1. Hisar to Agroha Fort (20 km)
  2. Agroha to Tosham Mountain Trek (40 km)
  3. Hanuman Mandir to Badakhalsa Lake Trek (30 km)
  4. Jharia Talab to Balasar Trek(60 km)

Best Things to Do in Hisar Division: Activities and Tours

Local Culture and Customs of Hisar Division

Hisar Division is a land of rich heritage, and the locals are warm and hospitable. It is essential to respect their culture and customs while visiting. Some of the things to keep in mind include:

  1. Dress appropriately, especially when visiting religious sites
  2. Always remove your shoes before entering a temple
  3. Do not disrespect or damage local monuments.
  4. Do not take photographs of locals without their consent.

Best Time to Visit Hisar Division

The best time to visit Hisar Division is between November and February when the weather is mild, and the skies are clear. It is also during this time that the Lohari Ragho Wildlife Sanctuary is at its best.

Safety and Health

Safety in Hisar Division

Hisar Division is generally safe, and violent crime is rare. However, it is advisable to take standard precautions like avoiding deserted areas at night and taking care of your belongings when in crowded areas.

Health in Hisar Division

As with any trip, it is crucial to take care of your health while in Hisar Division. Make sure you drink bottled water and avoid drinking tap water. It is also wise to carry basic health supplies like insect repellent and sunscreen.
