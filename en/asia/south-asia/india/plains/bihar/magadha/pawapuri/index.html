Introduction to Pawapuri

Located in the plains of Bihar, in the ancient region of Magadha, Pawapuri is known as one of the holiest sites for Jains. The town is believed to be the place where Lord Mahavira, the twenty-fourth Tirthankara, attained his salvation or Moksha. Many Jains travel from around the world to visit this sacred site. But even for non-Jains, Pawapuri has a lot to offer, from historical places to natural wonders, making it an excellent destination for an offbeat getaway.

List of Places to Visit in Pawapuri

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Jalmandir

Jalmandir is one of the most crucial historical and religious sites in Pawapuri. It is a temple situated in the middle of a lake, surrounded by beautiful greenery. This temple is known for its exquisite architecture and intricate murals. In the past, only Jain monks were allowed inside the temple, but in the present day, it is open to everyone.

Maha Parinirvana Temple

The Maha Parinirvana Temple was built to commemorate the final resting place of Lord Mahavira. The temple has beautiful Buddhist-style architecture and is adorned with frescoes depicting Lord Mahavira’s life.

Samosharan Temple

The Samosharan Temple is another crucial temple in Pawapuri, and it is believed that Lord Mahavira gave his last sermon here. The temple has excellent architecture and beautiful carvings.

Bihar Sharif

Bihar Sharif, located 35km away from Pawapuri, has many important archaeological sites, including the Tomb of Malik Ibrahim Baya and Ashoka Inscriptions.

Natural Attractions of Pawapuri: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lotus Lake

Lotus Lake, which is adjacent to Jalmandir, is a beautiful and peaceful lake filled with lotus flowers. It offers stunning views and is a great spot for a family picnic or a romantic getaway.

Nalanda

Nalanda, located about 75kms away from Pawapuri, is an important site of historical and architectural importance. The Nalanda University ruins are a world heritage site, and visitors can explore the site and learn about ancient Indian history.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pawapuri

Pawapuri is a great spot for nature lovers, and there are several hiking and biking routes around the town that offer great sightseeing opportunities. One of the most popular hiking trails in Pawapuri is the Lotus Lake Walking Path.

Best Things to Do in Pawapuri: Activities and Tours

Jain food tasting experience

Visitors can have a unique culinary experience in Pawapuri by trying Jain cuisine. The town has many restaurants and street food stalls that offer delicious Jain food.

Boat Ride at Jalmandir

Taking a boat ride around Jalmandir is an incredibly peaceful and calming experience.

Week-Long Itinerary for Pawapuri

Day 1: Arrival in Pawapuri, visit Jalmandir and Lotus Lake Day 2: Visit Maha Parinirvana Temple and Samosharan Temple Day 3: Day trip to Bihar Sharif Day 4-5: Hiking/biking and exploring the town Day 6: Visit Nalanda Day 7: Departure from Pawapuri

Food and Drink Scene of Pawapuri

Must-Try Dishes

One of the most popular Jain dishes in Pawapuri is Khichdi, which is made with rice and lentils. Visitors should also try Kachori, a deep-fried snack filled with savory stuffing, and Dhuska, a pancake-like dish made from rice flour.

Local Beverages

A popular beverage among Jains is Sugarcane Juice, which is available at many street food stalls in Pawapuri. Another popular drink is Bael Sharbat, made from the pulp of the Bael fruit.

Where to Stay in Pawapuri: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Visitors can find several budget-friendly hotels, hostels, and guesthouses in Pawapuri. Some of the highly-rated hotels include Hotel Pawapuri, Hotel Sri Vihar, and Nalanda Regency.

What to Buy in Pawapuri: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors can purchase souvenirs such as Jain statues, traditional clothing, and handicrafts made from brass, copper, and silver. Local markets such as Nagar Parishad Market and Sadhu Market have several options for souvenirs and local products.

Local Culture and Customs of Pawapuri

Jainism is the central religion of Pawapuri, and visitors should respect the local customs and traditions. It is essential to dress modestly and remove shoes before entering temples or holy places.

Best Time to Visit Pawapuri

The ideal time to visit Pawapuri is in the winter months of November to February when the weather is cool and pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pawapuri

Do:

  • Dress modestly while visiting temples and holy sites
  • Try the Jain cuisine
  • Explore the natural beauty of Pawapuri

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local customs and traditions
  • Disturb the environment by littering
  • Wear shoes inside temples

Safety and Health

Safety in Pawapuri

Pawapuri is considered a safe destination for tourists, and visitors should take standard precautions to ensure their safety.

Health in Pawapuri

It is essential to drink only bottled water in Pawapuri to avoid water-borne diseases. Additionally, visitors should carry a basic medical kit, including medicines for stomach problems and insect bites.

Getting to Pawapuri: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Pawapuri

The nearest airport to Pawapuri is Jay Prakash Narayan International Airport in Patna, located around 63kms away.

Bus Stations Near Pawapuri

The nearest bus station is in the nearby town of Nalanda, located around 19kms away.

Other Options

Visitors can also reach Pawapuri by train as the town has a railway station.

How to Get Around in Pawapuri

Visitors can hire taxis or auto-rickshaws to get around Pawapuri. Bicycles and scooters are also available for rent.

Where to Go Next from Pawapuri: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Nalanda is an excellent destination to visit after Pawapuri, as it has several historical sites, including the World Heritage Site of the Nalanda University ruins.

Conclusion

Pawapuri may be a small town, but it has a rich history, culture, and natural beauty. By following our comprehensive travel guide, visitors can make the most of their trip to Pawapuri and experience the best of what this unique town has to offer.
