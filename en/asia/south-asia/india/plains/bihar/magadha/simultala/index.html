Introduction to Simultala

Simultala is located in Magadha, in the Indian state of Bihar. This small town is surrounded by green hills and dense forests, making it the perfect destination for outdoor enthusiasts. Simultala is known for its breathtaking scenery, fresh air, and peaceful atmosphere. Although it’s a lesser-known travel destination, it offers a unique cultural experience for those who visit.

List of Places to Visit in Simultala

If you’re planning a trip to Simultala, there are plenty of exciting places to visit. Here are some recommendations:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Simultala Palace: Built in the 19th century, this palace is a symbol of the town’s heritage. Visitors can explore the palace’s stunning architecture and learn about its fascinating history.
  • Simultala Hills: These hills offer a panoramic view of Simultala and the surrounding areas. The view is especially stunning at sunrise and sunset.
  • Hanspura Temple: This ancient temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and has been decorated with beautiful murals painted in traditional Bihari style.

Natural Attractions of Simultala: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Simultala Waterfall: This waterfall is located in the center of Simultala Forest and is surrounded by stunning scenery. Trekking to the waterfall is an unforgettable experience.
  • Simultala Forest: This forest is home to various wildlife species, including leopards, bears, and deer. It’s a perfect place to take a nature walk and breathe some fresh air.
  • Sita Kund: This is a natural spring, located near Simultala, known for its crystal-clear water and serene atmosphere.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Simultala

Simultala offers many opportunities for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Simultala to Hanspura: This is an easy hike that offers a scenic view of the town.
  • Simultala to Simultala Waterfall: This is a moderate to difficult hike that takes you through dense forest and rocky terrain.
  • Simultala to Deoghar: This is a challenging bike route that passes through some of the most scenic landscapes in the area.

Best Things to Do in Simultala: Activities and Tours

There is no shortage of things to do in Simultala. Here are some of the best activities and tours:

  • Nature Walks: Explore Simultala’s scenic sites on foot with a guided nature walk.
  • Bird Watching: Discover the diverse bird species in Simultala.
  • Heritage Walks: Take a serene stroll through Simultala’s historical sites and learn about its rich cultural heritage.
  • Adventure Sports: Experience the thrill of camping, rock climbing, river rafting, and more.

Food and Drink Scene of Simultala

If you’re a foodie, Simultala won’t disappoint. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Litti Chokha: This is a traditional Bihari dish made with roasted wheat balls stuffed with sattu, a mixture of roasted gram flour and spices, and served with mashed potatoes.
  • Dalpuri: This is a deep-fried bread made with wheat flour stuffed with a mixture of mashed lentils and spices.
  • Mutton Curry: Mutton curry is a popular dish in Simultala, made with tender mutton pieces cooked in a spicy gravy.

Local Beverages

  • Aam panna: This is a refreshing drink made with raw mangoes, sugar, and spices.
  • Thandai: This is a sweet drink made with milk and flavored with nuts and saffron.

What to Buy in Simultala: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Simultala is known for its handicrafts and local products. Here are some souvenirs you should consider buying:

  • Handicrafts: Handicrafts made with bamboo and wood are popular souvenirs in Simultala. Some of the popular handicrafts include baskets and toys.
  • Mithila Paintings: Mithila paintings are a traditional art form of Bihar and make for an excellent souvenir.
  • Sattu: Sattu, a roasted gram flour mixture, is a staple food in Bihar and makes for a unique souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Simultala

Simultala has a rich cultural heritage and unique customs. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Respect Religious Sites: Simultala has many religious sites, so dress appropriately and be respectful.
  • Acknowledge Elders: Elders are highly respected in Bihari culture, so make sure to address them respectfully.
  • Use Your Right Hand: It is considered impolite to use your left hand while eating or interacting with others in Bihari culture.

Best Time to Visit Simultala

The best time to visit Simultala is between October and March. The weather is pleasant, and the landscape is lush green during this time. Avoid visiting during monsoon season (June-September) as trekking and other outdoor activities can be challenging.

Do’s and Don’ts of Simultala

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Simultala:

Do:

  • Wear comfortable and modest clothing that covers your shoulders and knees.
  • Carry a sweater or jacket during winters as the weather can get chilly.
  • Respect local customs, traditions, and religious sites.

Don’t:

  • Drink tap water. Only drink bottled water.
  • Leave trash behind while trekking or hiking.
  • Engage in any disrespectful behavior or conversations that may offend local customs, culture, or religion.

Safety and Health

Safety in Simultala

Simultala is generally safe for tourists, but it’s always better to take precautions wherever you travel. Here are some safety tips:

  • Keep your belongings secure and never leave valuables unattended.
  • Avoid traveling alone at night and stick to well-lit areas.
  • Trek with a local guide or group.

Health in Simultala

Here are some health tips for visiting Simultala:

  • Drink only bottled water and avoid street food.
  • Apply insect repellant and dress in full-sleeved clothing to avoid mosquito bites.
  • Carry basic medication for common ailments.

Getting to Simultala: Transportation Options and Logistics

Simultala is well-connected by air, rail, and road.

Airports Near Simultala

The nearest airport is the Birsa Munda International Airport in Ranchi, which is 170 km away from Simultala. From the airport, you can hire a taxi or take a bus to reach Simultala.

Bus Stations Near Simultala

Simultala is well-connected to major cities of Bihar and Jharkhand through road networks. Buses are available from nearby cities like Ranchi, Hazaribagh, and Deoghar.

Other Options

The nearest railway station is Jasidih Junction, which is 40 km away from Simultala. From there, you can take a taxi or hire a car to reach Simultala.

How to Get Around in Simultala

The best way to explore Simultala is on foot or by bike. You can also hire a taxi or auto-rickshaw to travel around the town.

Where to Go Next from Simultala: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have some more time, you can visit these nearby destinations:

  • Deoghar: This is a popular pilgrimage spot and is located around 60 km away from Simultala.
  • Hazaribagh: Hazaribagh is home to some stunning waterfalls and is located approximately 80 km from Simultala.
  • Ranchi: Ranchi, the capital of Jharkhand, is known for its beautiful lakes and temples and is located around 170 km from Simultala.

Conclusion

Simultala is a hidden gem for those who are looking for an offbeat travel destination. The town offers a unique cultural experience, stunning scenery, and outdoor adventures that will leave you refreshed and rejuvenated. Plan your visit to Simultala today and immerse yourself in the beauty of this charming town.
