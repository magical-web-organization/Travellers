Khagrachhari is a culturally rich district in the Chittagong Division of Bangladesh. It’s a land of lush green hills and serene waterfalls that make it a perfect getaway for nature lovers. There are also historical sites, cultural centers, and outdoor adventures that attract visitors from around the world. In this tour guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and local food culture and customs.

Introduction to Khagrachhari

Khagrachhari is a district in the south-eastern part of Bangladesh, located in the Chittagong Division. It’s known for its natural beauty, cultural diversity, and friendly people. The district is surrounded by endless mountains, including the Chittagong Hill Tracts, which offer spectacular views and challenging hiking trails.

Local Culture and Customs of Khagrachhari

Khagrachhari is a diverse district with many ethnicities and cultures. The tribes here have their tradition; many people wear colorful clothing and have unique styles. So while traveling, be respectful of local customs and ask before taking photographs.

Best Time to Visit Khagrachhari

The best time to visit Khagrachhari is from October to February. The weather is mild this time with a clear blue sky. The summer months, from March to June, can be hot and humid. From July to September, it’s the monsoon season, and torrential rain can disrupt travel.

Do’s and Don’ts of Khagrachhari

Here are some Do’s and Don’ts to follow:

Do:

  • Dress appropriately and respectfully when visiting religious and cultural sites.
  • Carry cash as most traders may not accept cards.
  • Learn some basic Bengali to help communicate with locals.

Don’t:

  • Do not disrespect any religious sites.
  • Do not photograph people without asking for their permission.
  • Do not litter or damage nature sites.

Safety and Health

Khagrachhari is safe for tourists, but as with any place, it’s important to take precautions. Here are some safety and health tips to keep in mind:

Safety in Khagrachhari

  • Avoid traveling alone in isolated areas.
  • Always carry a mobile phone or some form of communication.
  • Be cautious when hiking in the hills and make sure to follow official routes.

Health in Khagrachhari

  • Carry your medications and first aid kit.
  • Drink clean water, and if not available, go for boiled or bottled water.
  • Wear appropriate clothing to avoid mosquito bites.

Getting to Khagrachhari: Transportation Options and Logistics

Khagrachhari is well-connected with the rest of the country. Here are the ways to reach Khagrachhari:

Airports Near Khagrachhari

The nearest airport is Chittagong International Airport, which is 104km away from the district.

Bus Stations Near Khagrachhari

The district has two bus terminals, Khagrachhari Bus Terminal and Dighinala Bus Terminal, with regular bus services to major cities in Bangladesh.

Other Options

You can also hire a car or motorcycle to reach Khagrachhari. The road trip from Chittagong to Khagrachhari is especially picturesque.

How to Get Around in Khagrachhari

The best way to get around in Khagrachhari is by renting a car, hiring a local guide, or taking a rickshaw. These options are cheap and convenient compared to the bus service.
