Introduction to Outer

Outer is a district located in Dhaka, the capital city of Bangladesh. It is a beautiful destination with a mix of natural and cultural attractions. The district has something to offer for everyone, from outdoor adventure enthusiasts to history buffs. In this tour guide, we will explore the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, the local food and drink scene, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Outer

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Ahsan Manzil

Ahsan Manzil is a beautiful palace located on the banks of the Buriganga River. It was the residence of the Nawab of Dhaka and is now a national museum. This pink-colored building is a fine example of the Indo-Saracenic style of architecture.

Lalbagh Fort

Lalbagh Fort is an incomplete Mughal palace complex that was built in the 17th century. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and features several beautiful structures such as the Diwan-i-Aam, the Mosque, and the Audience Hall.

Dhakeshwari National Temple

Dhakeshwari National Temple is a Hindu temple located in Old Dhaka. It is one of the oldest temples in Bangladesh and is believed to have been built in the 12th century. The temple is dedicated to the goddess Dhakeshwari, who is considered to be the protector of Dhaka.

Natural Attractions of Outer: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Baldha Garden

Baldha Garden is a botanical garden located in Wari, Dhaka. It covers an area of 3.15 acres and features a wide variety of plants and trees. The garden is popular among nature lovers and is an excellent place to relax and unwind.

Suhrawardy Udyan

Suhrawardy Udyan is a popular park located in the heart of Dhaka. It features several lakes, a rose garden, and a children’s playground. It is a great place to spend an afternoon with friends and family.

Dhaka Zoo

Dhaka Zoo is a large zoo located in Mirpur, Dhaka. It is home to over 2,000 animals, including tigers, lions, elephants, and giraffes. The zoo is a popular attraction for both locals and tourists and is a great place to spend a day with the family.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Outer

Lalmatia Canal

The Lalmatia Canal is a scenic waterway that runs through the heart of Dhaka. It is a popular location for early morning walks and bike rides. The canal is surrounded by lush greenery and is a great place to escape the city’s hustle and bustle.

Hatirjheel Lake

Hatirjheel Lake is a man-made lake located in the heart of Dhaka. It is surrounded by several parks and features a walking and cycling track. The lake is a popular spot among locals and tourists alike and is the perfect place to have a picnic or relax on a lazy afternoon.

Best Things to Do in Outer: Activities and Tours

Dhaka City Tour

A city tour of Dhaka is a great way to explore the city’s rich history and culture. The tour includes visits to several historical sites such as Lalbagh Fort and Ahsan Manzil. It also includes a visit to the bustling markets and bazaars of Old Dhaka.

Boat Ride on the Buriganga River

A boat ride on the Buriganga River is a must-do activity when visiting Outer. The river is the lifeline of Dhaka and provides a unique perspective of the city. The boat ride also includes a visit to the Sadarghat Launch Terminal, one of the busiest river ports in the world.

Food and Drink Scene of Outer

Must-Try Dishes

Biriyani

Biriyani is a popular rice dish in Bangladesh. It is made with spices, meat, and rice and is often served with a side of raita.

Pitha

Pitha is a type of cake made with rice flour and coconut. It is a popular dessert in Bangladesh and is often served during special occasions.

Local Beverages

Cha

Cha is a type of tea that is popular in Bangladesh. It is made with loose tea leaves, milk, and sugar and is often accompanied by biscuits or pitha.

Lassi

Lassi is a yogurt-based drink that is popular in South Asia. It is made with yogurt, water, and sugar and is often flavored with fruit.

Where to Stay in Outer: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Pan Pacific Sonargaon Hotel

The Pan Pacific Sonargaon Hotel is a luxury hotel located in the heart of Dhaka. It features several restaurants, a swimming pool, and a fitness center.

Hotel Victory

The Hotel Victory is a budget-friendly hotel located in the heart of Old Dhaka. It features several rooms with air-conditioning and en-suite bathrooms.

What to Buy in Outer: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Nakshi Kantha

Nakshi Kantha is a type of embroidered quilt that is popular in Bangladesh. It is made by hand and features intricate designs and patterns.

Jute Products

Jute is a plant fiber that is commonly grown in Bangladesh. It is used to make a wide variety of products, including bags, mats, and shoes.

Local Culture and Customs of Outer

The people of Bangladesh are known for their hospitality and warmth. It is common for guests to be offered tea or snacks when visiting someone’s home. Religion plays an important role in the daily lives of many Bangladeshis, with Islam being the predominant religion. Visitors are expected to respect local customs and dress modestly when visiting religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Outer

The best time to visit Outer is from November to February when the weather is cool and pleasant. The monsoon season, which lasts from June to September, can make it difficult to explore the outdoors and can cause flooding in some areas.

Do’s and Don’ts of Outer

Do:

  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Try the local food and drink

Don’t:

  • Take photographs of people without their permission
  • Wear shoes inside mosques and temples
  • Drink tap water

Safety and Health

Safety in Outer

Visitors to Outer should be aware of their surroundings and take precautions to avoid theft and scams. It is recommended to travel in groups and to avoid wandering alone at night. Petty crime is common, so it is essential to keep valuables out of sight and in a secure place.

Health in Outer

It is recommended to drink bottled water and to avoid consuming street food to avoid contracting a stomach bug. Visitors should also take precautions against mosquito-borne illnesses such as dengue fever and malaria.

Getting to Outer: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Outer

The nearest airport to Outer is Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport in Dhaka.

Bus Stations Near Outer

The main bus station in Dhaka is the Kamalapur Bus Station, which is located in the heart of the city.

Other Options

Taxis and ride-sharing services such as Uber and Pathao are available in Dhaka and can be used to travel around the city.

How to Get Around in Outer

The best way to get around Outer is by using taxis or ride-sharing services. Visitors can also rent a bike or scooter to explore the city’s outdoors.

Where to Go Next from Outer: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Cox’s Bazar

Cox’s Bazar is a popular beach destination located in southeastern Bangladesh. It is known for its long sandy beach and crystal-clear waters.

Sundarbans Mangrove Forest

The Sundarbans Mangrove Forest is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in southwestern Bangladesh. It is home to the Bengal tiger and several other species of wildlife.
