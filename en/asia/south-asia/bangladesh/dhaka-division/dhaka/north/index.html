North is a district of Dhaka Division located in the northern part of Bangladesh. The district is home to picturesque landscapes, natural beauty, and rich cultural heritage. From historical monuments to stunning natural views, North has a lot to offer to its visitors. In this travel guide, we will cover everything that you need to know to plan your trip to North.

Introduction to North

North is blessed with natural beauty that includes mountains, rivers, and forests. The district is well-known for its ancient ruins, archaeological sites, and museums that offer a glimpse into the history of this region. North is also an ideal destination for outdoor adventure enthusiasts with its scenic trails for hiking and biking.

Local Culture and Customs of North

North has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its art, music, and cuisine. The locals are friendly and welcoming, and visitors are expected to respect their traditions and customs. Some of the cultural customs in North include:

  • Greetings with a smile and a nod
  • Dressing conservatively in public places
  • Removing shoes before entering religious places
  • Using the right hand for eating and shaking hands

Best Time to Visit North

The best time to visit North is between October and March when the weather is cooler and more pleasant. The monsoon season lasts from June to September, so it is advisable to avoid visiting during this time as the roads may be difficult to navigate, and some attractions may be closed.

Do’s and Don’ts of North

Do:

  • Dress conservatively in public places
  • Be respectful of the local culture and customs
  • Try local dishes and drinks
  • Learn some basic phrases in Bengali

Don’t:

  • Touch someone’s head, as it is considered rude
  • Speak loudly in public places
  • Display affection in public places
  • Point at people or things

Safety and Health

Safety in North

North is considered a safe district. However, it is advisable to take the following precautions:

  • Avoid traveling alone at night
  • Keep valuable items hidden
  • Wear a mask and practice social distancing

Health in North

North has a well-established health care system. Visitors are advised to carry enough medication and get vaccinated before visiting the district.

Getting to North: Transportation Options and Logistics

North is accessible by air, road, and rail. The nearest airport is Shah Makhdum Airport in Rajshahi. The district is well-connected to major cities in Bangladesh by bus and train.

Airports Near North

  • Shah Makhdum Airport, Rajshahi

Bus Stations Near North

  • Joypurhat Bus Terminal
  • Panchbibi Bus Terminal
  • Bogra Bus Terminal

Other Options

  • Train: North Bengal Express, Subarna Express

How to Get Around in North

The best way to get around in North is by rickshaw or rented car. Buses and trains are also available but may not be as convenient or comfortable.
