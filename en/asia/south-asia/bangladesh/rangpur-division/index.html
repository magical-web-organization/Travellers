Introduction to Rangpur Division

Rangpur Division is a region in the north-western part of Bangladesh. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, scenic beauty, and adventure activities. The division is home to a number of ethnic communities, including Bengalis, Santals, and Garos. The region has a long and fascinating history, with evidence of human settlement dating back to the Neolithic Age.

List of Places to Visit in Rangpur Division

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Rangpur Division has many historical sites and museums that offer a glimpse into its rich past. Some of the top attractions are:

  • Tajhat Palace
  • Kantajew Temple
  • Paharpur Buddhist Monastery

Natural Attractions of Rangpur Division: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you like the outdoors, Rangpur Division has plenty of options. The region is home to a number of scenic spots, parks, and reserves. Some of the must-visit places are:

  • Nilsagar
  • Barendra Museum
  • Lalbagh Fort

Hiking/Biking Routes of Rangpur Division

For hikers and bikers, Rangpur Division offers scenic routes that take you through some of the most scenic areas of the region. Some of the best routes are:

  • Thakurgaon - Panchagarh Route
  • Rangpur - Dinajpur Route
  • Pirganj - Badarganj Route

Best Things to Do in Rangpur Division: Activities and Tours

Rangpur Division offers a range of activities and tours, some of which are:

  • Explore the forests and rivers of Rangpur Division on a boat cruise
  • Visit the local markets and sample local food and crafts
  • Take part in traditional dance and music performances.

Week-Long Itinerary for Rangpur Division

A week-long itinerary for Rangpur Division can include:

  • Day 1: Explore the historic landmarks of Rajshahi
  • Day 2: Visit the serene temples at Puthia, Natore
  • Day 3: Take a nature walk through the Lawachara National Park, Srimangal
  • Day 4: Visit the remanent of British Raj life at Madhabpur Lake, Habiganj
  • Day 5: Take in the scenic views at Umium Lake, Meghalaya
  • Day 6: Explore the indigenous culture and local market, Rangamati
  • Day 7: Visit a tea garden and experience tea processing, Sylhet

Food and Drink Scene of Rangpur Division

Must-Try Dishes

Rangpur Division is known for its delicious food. Some of the must-try local dishes are:

  • Kacchi Biryani
  • Roshogolla
  • Shondesh
  • Pitha

Local Beverages

Rangpur Division is famous for its tea plantations, so tea is the most popular beverage in the region. Other drinks to try are:

  • Lassi
  • Borhani

Where to Stay in Rangpur Division: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are many accommodation options in Rangpur Division, from budget hostels to luxury hotels. Some of the best places to stay are:

  • Hotel Polash

  • Hotel RDRS

What to Buy in Rangpur Division: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Rangpur Division is famous for its handicrafts, textiles, and traditional items. Here are some popular items to buy as souvenirs:

  • Nakshi Kantha
  • Gamchha
  • Handmade crafts
  • Traditional dresses

Local Culture and Customs of Rangpur Division

Rangpur Division’s culture is a mix of Bengali and indigenous customs. Respect for elders and hospitality are an important part of the culture.

Best Time to Visit Rangpur Division

The best time to visit Rangpur Division is between October and February, when the weather is cool and dry.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rangpur Division

Do:

  • Dress modestly and respectfully, particularly in religious sites and rural areas
  • Learn a few words of the local language
  • Respect local customs and cultures

Don’t:

  • Eat food from street vendors if you have an easily upset stomach
  • Disrespect religious sites or cultural monuments
  • Photograph people without seeking permission first

Safety and Health

Safety in Rangpur Division

Rangpur Division is a relatively safe region, but it is important to take precautions to ensure your safety. Some tips to remember:

  • Avoid walking alone at night
  • Carry your passport and valuables in a safe place
  • Take care when traveling by road

Health in Rangpur Division

It is advisable to take precautions for your health while traveling in Rangpur Division, particularly:

  • Drinking bottled water
  • Avoiding food from unhygienic outlets
  • Seeking medical attention in case of any illness

Getting to Rangpur Division: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Rangpur Division

The nearest airport to Rangpur Division is Saidpur Airport, which is around 22 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Rangpur Division

There are many bus services from Dhaka to Rangpur Division. Buses depart frequently and are the most popular mode of transport.

Other Options

Trains and private cars are an alternative way to reach Rangpur Division.

How to Get Around in Rangpur Division

There are several ways to get around Rangpur Division, including buses, taxis, and auto-rickshaws. Walking is also a great way to explore the region’s towns and cities.

Where to Go Next from Rangpur Division: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some popular destinations near Rangpur Division are:

  • Sylhet
  • Bandarban
  • Chittagong

Conclusion

Rangpur Division is a fascinating and beautiful region of Bangladesh, with plenty to see and do. Whether you’re interested in history, culture, nature, or adventure, Rangpur Division has something for everyone. Take your time exploring this diverse and interesting region and soak in its many rich experiences.
