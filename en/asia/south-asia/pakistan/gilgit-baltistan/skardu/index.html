Introduction to Skardu

Skardu, a picturesque town situated in the Gilgit-Baltistan region of Northern Pakistan, is a popular destination for tourists looking for rugged mountain landscapes and incredible outdoor adventures.

Bordered by the Himalayas and the Karakoram mountain range, Skardu is best known for its mountainous terrain, stunning glacier-fed lakes, and unmatched natural beauty.

This travel guide to Skardu will provide you with everything you need to know to plan your trip to this amazing destination.

List of Places to Visit in Skardu

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Skardu boasts a rich and diverse cultural heritage, with various historical places and museums that tell the stories of its past. Below are some of the most important cultural attractions in Skardu:

  • Kharpocho Fort: A historical fort constructed in the 16th century by Ali Sher Khan Anchan, the ruler of Skardu. The fort is located on a hill overlooking the town and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding region.

  • Mantokha Waterfall: A beautiful waterfall located near Kharpocho Fort. Visitors can hike around the waterfall and enjoy the sight of the falling water from various angles.

  • Skardu Museum: Skardu Museum is the perfect place for history buffs who want to learn more about the region’s culture and history. This museum displays archaeological artifacts and historical objects related to the Skardu region.

Natural Attractions of Skardu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Skardu is an adventure lover’s paradise, with a variety of activities and outdoor adventures to enjoy. Below are some of the most popular natural attractions in Skardu:

  • Kachura Lake: Kachura Lake is a serene and scenic lake surrounded by majestic mountains. Visitors can enjoy boating and camping near the lake and can explore the nearby areas on foot or on horseback.

  • Satpara Lake: Satpara Lake is another picturesque lake located near Skardu. Visitors can enjoy a leisurely stroll along the lake’s banks, go fishing, or take a boat ride.

  • Deosai Plains: Deosai Plains is a vast plateau that spreads over 3,000 square kilometers. It is home to a wide variety of flora and fauna, including the Himalayan brown bear. Visitors can camp, hike, and explore the beautiful landscape of Deosai Plains.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Skardu

Skardu is a popular destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. There are several scenic routes that visitors can choose from, catering to all levels of fitness and skill. Some of the best routes in Skardu are:

  • Basho Valley: Basho Valley is a beautiful trail that passes through the picturesque Basho village. The hike takes visitors through lush green fields, waterfalls, and rocky terrain.

  • Upper Kachura Lake Trek: This trek takes visitors to the upper reaches of the Kachura Lake, offering stunning views of the lake and the surrounding mountains.

  • Khaplu Valley Trek: Khaplu Valley Trek is a moderate trek that takes visitors through the beautiful Khaplu Valley. The trail offers breathtaking views of the valley and the surrounding mountains.

Best Things to Do in Skardu: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, Skardu offers other activities that visitors can enjoy. Here are some of the best activities to do in Skardu:

  • Shangrila Water Park: Shangrila Water Park is a water park that offers visitors a place to cool off on a hot day. Visitors can enjoy water rides, swimming, and other water activities.

  • Rock Climbing: Skardu’s mountainous terrain makes it an ideal location for rock climbing enthusiasts. Visitors can climb the rocky hills and mountains while enjoying beautiful views of the surrounding region.

  • Jeep Safari: A jeep safari is a popular activity in Skardu that takes visitors through the rugged terrain of the region. Visitors can explore the beautiful landscape, including the mountains, lakes, and valleys.

Food and Drink Scene of Skardu

Must-Try Dishes

Skardu has a diverse culinary scene that offers a range of dishes to visitors. Some of the must-try dishes in Skardu are:

  • Momo: Momo is a popular traditional Tibetan dumpling that is available in Skardu. It is made with a variety of fillings, including meat and vegetables.

  • Khambheer: Khambheer is a type of bread that is made with wheat flour and is a staple food in the region. It is often served with various vegetable dishes and meat dishes.

  • Butter Tea: Butter tea is a traditional tea made by boiling tea leaves with milk, butter, and salt. It is a popular drink in the region and is often served to guests.

Local Beverages

In Skardu, visitors can sample a variety of local beverages. Some of the most popular ones are:

  • Chang: Chang is a traditional alcoholic beverage made from fermented barley. It is often served during festivals and special occasions.

  • Green Tea: Green tea is a popular non-alcoholic beverage that is widely consumed in Skardu. It is often served with a variety of snacks and sweets.

Where to Stay in Skardu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Skardu offers a range of accommodation options for visitors, catering to all budgets and preferences. Some of the best places to stay in Skardu are:

  • Concordia Motel Skardu: Concordia Motel Skardu is a budget-friendly option that offers visitors stunning views of the surrounding mountains. The motel offers basic amenities such as Wi-Fi, room service, and laundry service.

  • Serena Shigar Fort: Serena Shigar Fort is a luxury hotel located in the Shigar Valley, about an hour’s drive from Skardu. The hotel is housed in an ancient fort that has been restored to its former glory and offers visitors a glimpse into the region’s rich history.

  • K2 View Inn: K2 View Inn is a mid-range hotel located on Airport Road in Skardu. The hotel offers comfortable rooms with amenities such as Wi-Fi, room service, and laundry service.

What to Buy in Skardu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Skardu is famous for its handicrafts, carpets, and textiles. Visitors can find a wide range of souvenirs and local products to bring back home. Some of the best items to buy in Skardu are:

  • Pashmina shawls: Skardu is famous for its Pashmina shawls, which are made from the fine wool of the Himalayan goats. These shawls are warm, soft, and lightweight, making them the perfect souvenir to bring back home.

  • Gemstones: Skardu is home to a variety of gemstones, including rubies, emeralds, and sapphires. Visitors can buy jewelry made from these gemstones or can purchase loose gemstones to take back home.

  • Handicrafts: Skardu is famous for its handicrafts, including wooden carvings, pottery, and woven textiles. These items make excellent souvenirs and are a great way to support local artisans.

Local Culture and Customs of Skardu

Skardu is a culturally diverse region, with people from different ethnic and linguistic backgrounds living in the area. The people of Skardu are known for their hospitality and friendliness. Visitors to Skardu should respect local customs and traditions, including dressing modestly and removing their shoes before entering homes or places of worship.

Best Time to Visit Skardu

The best time to visit Skardu is during the summer months, from June to August, when the weather is mild and pleasant. During this time, visitors can enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, biking, and camping without having to contend with harsh weather conditions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Skardu

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.

  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites and public places.

  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.

  • Learn a few phrases in the local language.

Don’t:

  • Smoke or consume alcohol in public places.

  • Take photographs of people without their permission.

  • Wear revealing clothing or shorts in public places.

Safety and Health

Safety in Skardu

Skardu is a relatively safe place for tourists. However, visitors should take precautions when exploring the region’s natural attractions. Some safety tips to follow are:

  • Hire a local guide when hiking or exploring natural attractions.

  • Carry enough food and water when hiking or camping.

  • Keep valuables such as passports, money, and cameras in a safe place.

Health in Skardu

Visitors to Skardu should take precautions to prevent illness. Some health tips to follow are:

  • Drink only bottled water or water that has been boiled or treated.

  • Use insect repellent to avoid mosquito bites.

  • Visit a doctor before traveling to ensure that all necessary vaccinations are up to date.

Getting to Skardu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Skardu is easily accessible by air and road. Below are some transportation options to consider when planning your trip to Skardu:

Airports Near Skardu

  • Skardu Airport: Skardu Airport is the main airport in the region, with flights available from Islamabad and other major cities in Pakistan.

Bus Stations Near Skardu

  • Gilgit Bus Station: Gilgit Bus Station is about 220 kilometers from Skardu and is the main bus station that connects Skardu to other cities in Pakistan.

  • Rawalpindi Bus Station: Rawalpindi Bus Station is about 630 kilometers from Skardu and offers bus services to Skardu.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive or hire a taxi to reach Skardu. The driving distance from Islamabad to Skardu is approximately 670 kilometers.

How to Get Around in Skardu

The best way to get around in Skardu is by jeep or minibus. Visitors can hire a jeep or minibus with a driver to explore the region’s natural attractions. Taxis and rickshaws are also available for short journeys and trips within the town.

Where to Go Next from Skardu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Skardu is a great starting point for exploring other destinations in the region. Some nearby destinations and excursions to consider are:

  • Gilgit: Gilgit is a bustling town located about 200 kilometers from Skardu. It is known for its lively bazaars, ancient fortresses, and scenic mountain views.

  • Hunza Valley: Hunza Valley is a popular tourist destination that is located about 200 kilometers from Skardu. Visitors can explore the quaint villages, ancient forts, and stunning valleys that make up this beautiful location.

Conclusion

Skardu is a beautiful destination that offers a range of activities and natural attractions to visitors. This comprehensive guide has provided you with all the information you need to plan your trip to Skardu, from the best places to visit to the most important cultural customs to follow.

Whether you’re a seasoned hiker or a cultural enthusiast, Skardu is sure to leave a lasting impression on you!
