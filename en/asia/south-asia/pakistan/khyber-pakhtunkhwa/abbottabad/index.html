Abbottabad is a beautiful city in Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Pakistan, known for its serene environment, breathtaking natural beauty, and historical landmarks. Being a popular tourist destination in Pakistan, Abbottabad has a lot to offer to its visitors. In this travel guide, we will explore the top destinations, activities, cuisine, and accommodation options in the city, along with important tips for safe travel and transportation options.

Introduction to Abbottabad

Abbottabad is situated at an altitude of around 4,120 feet above sea level, surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty. With a moderate climate throughout the year, Abbottabad is a perfect destination for both summer and winter vacations. The city has a rich cultural history and a blend of ancient and modern architecture.

Local Culture and Customs of Abbottabad

Abbottabad has a rich cultural heritage, and people are friendly and open-hearted. The most common language spoken in Abbottabad is Hindko, whereas Urdu and English are also widely spoken.

Best Time to Visit Abbottabad

The best time to visit Abbottabad is from March to May and from September to November when the weather is mild and manageable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Abbottabad

Do:

  1. Respect local customs and traditions.
  2. Wear modest clothing when visiting religious sites.
  3. Make sure to carry your important documents, including your passport and visa.

Don’t:

  1. Don’t take pictures of military installations.
  2. Avoid carrying drugs and other illegal substances.
  3. Don’t travel alone at night in isolated areas.

Safety and Health in Abbottabad

Abbottabad is a relatively safe city, with a low crime rate. However, it’s always better to be cautious and avoid traveling alone at night, especially to isolated areas. As for health, make sure to carry your first aid kit, prescription medicines, and insect repellent spray to avoid mosquito bites.

How to Get Around in Abbottabad

The most popular modes of transportation in Abbottabad are taxis and rented cars. However, if you are an adventure lover, trekking and hiking are the best options to explore the city’s beauty.
