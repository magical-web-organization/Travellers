A Comprehensive Guide to Murree - Best Places to Visit, Things to Do, and Where to Stay

Murree is a serene and popular hill station in the Northern Punjab province of Pakistan. Known for its scenic beauty, Murree is a favourite among locals and international tourists. If you’re planning a trip to this beautiful destination, this guide will help you prepare for your visit.

Introduction to Murree

Murree is situated in the Pir Panjal Range, in the Margalla Hills. It is a hill station with a moderate climate and attractions including scenic views, outdoor activities, and cultural landmarks. Murree has been a popular tourist destination for decades with visitors coming from all over Pakistan and neighboring countries.

List of Places to Visit in Murree

Murree has plenty of cultural and natural attractions. Here is a list of the best places to visit in Murree:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Patriata Chairlift: A popular tourist attraction offering breathtaking views of the mountainous landscape
  2. Murree Museum: A small yet fascinating museum showcasing the cultural and historical background of Murree
  3. Kashmir Point: A scenic hilltop resembling the scenic beauty of Kashmir, offering a view of the surrounding hills and mountains

Natural Attractions of Murree: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Ayubia National Park: A wildlife reserve featuring a variety of animals, birds and flora
  2. Dunga Gali Pine Line Track: A scenic hiking trail amidst the pine trees giving you a remarkable view of nature
  3. Nathiagali: A picturesque hill station known for its stunning views and walking trails

Hiking/Biking Routes of Murree

Murree is famous for its scenic trails, biking and hiking routes, and mountainous landscapes. There are several routes to choose from ranging from easy treks to more difficult mountaineering routes that will challenge your physical abilities. Here are the top recommended routes:

  1. Mushkpuri Peak Trek: An exciting trek that takes you to the second-highest peak of the Murree mountains
  2. Nathia Gali to Dunga Gali Trek: A popular hiking trail of intermediate difficulty that offers panoramic views
  3. Ghora Gali to Bara Gali Trek: An ideal trek for beginners offering a peaceful walk through lush greenery

Best Things to Do in Murree: Activities and Tours

  1. Indulge in traditional cuisine: Sample the delicious foods that the locals have been enjoying for generations
  2. Experience the local culture: Explore the lifestyle and traditions of the locals with a trip to any of the numerous markets and bazaars
  3. Adventure Sports: Engage yourselves in the sheer fun of rappelling, rock climbing, zip-lining or paragliding

Week-Long Itinerary for Murree

Here is a sample week-long itinerary for exploring Murree:

Day 1: Visit the local museums Day 2: Travel to Ayubia National Park Day 3: Trek to Mushkpuri Peak or take any of the scenic hiking trails Day 4: Take a trip to Nathia Gali Day 5: Go rock-climbing or paragliding Day 6: Visit the local markets and bazaars
Day 7: Explore the scenic corners of the city

Food and Drink Scene of Murree

Murree is famous for its traditional Pakistani cuisine. Here are some of the must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Karahi: A spicy curry made out of meat and tomatoes, seasoned with a blend of spices
  2. Chapli Kebab: A local specialty made of ground beef flavoured with a variety of herbs and spices
  3. Biryani: An aromatic rice dish prepared with meat, vegetables and spices

Local Beverages

  1. Kashmiri Chai: A traditional tea beverage with a unique taste
  2. Lassi: A refreshing drink made with yoghurt, milk and sugar
  3. Fresh Fruit Juices: A perfect drink for hot summer days

Where to Stay in Murree: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Murree has plenty of accommodation options ranging from luxury hotels to budget-friendly guesthouses. Here are the top recommended options:

  1. Pearl Continental Hotel Murree: A luxurious and elegant hotel situated in a tranquil location
  2. Lockwood Hotel: A stylish and comfortable hotel that offers excellent facilities and services
  3. Pine Hills Hotel: A budget-friendly option that offers basic amenities and is ideal for backpackers

What to Buy in Murree: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Murree has many souvenir shops that feature locally made products and handicrafts. Here are the best options:

  1. Wooden Crafts: Exquisite wooden carved crafts
  2. Shawls & Scarves: Lovely traditional shawls and scarves to carry home
  3. Jewellery: Unique and handmade jewellery made from local gems and stones

Local Culture and Customs of Murree

The locals of Murree are warm and inviting. Visitors are expected to be respectful of their religious and cultural beliefs. It is advisable to dress modestly and follow local customs and traditions.

Best Time to Visit Murree

The best time to visit Murree is from April to October. The weather is pleasant during this time of the year, and the natural beauty of the hills is at its peak.

Do’s and Don’ts of Murree

Here are some things to keep in mind while visiting Murree:

Do:

  1. Respect local customs and traditions
  2. Dress appropriately while visiting religious sites
  3. Carry appropriate clothing for the trips and hikes

Don’t:

  1. Litter or damage the natural environment
  2. Disrespectful towards local culture or language
  3. Bring banned substances or engage in illegal activities

Safety and Health

Safety in Murree

Murree is quite safe and secure for tourists. Visitors need to be mindful of their surroundings and take the necessary precautions in crowded areas.

Health in Murree

Murree has plenty of health centres and clinics that offer basic health services. Visitors need to carry their own medicine and medical supplies, including insect repellent and sunscreen.

Getting to Murree: Transportation Options and Logistics

Murree is well-connected to major cities in Pakistan. Here are the best ways to reach Murree:

Airports Near Murree

The closest airport to Murree is Islamabad International Airport. From the airport, visitors can hire a taxi or take a bus to Murree.

Bus Stations Near Murree

Rawalpindi Bus Station is located less than 1 hour away from Murree. Visitors can take a bus from the station to reach Murree.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a private car or taxi to get to Murree from Islamabad or Rawalpindi.

How to Get Around in Murree

The best way to explore Murree is on foot as the city is small and easily accessible. Alternatively, visitors can hire a local taxi or rent a bike to explore the city’s beautiful corners.

Where to Go Next from Murree: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some of the nearby destinations to visit from Murree:

  1. Abbottabad: A beautiful city located 2 hours away from Murree, known for its scenic beauty and historical landmarks
  2. Peshawar: A 3-hour drive from Murree, offering historical sites and cultural landmarks
  3. Lahore: A 5-hour drive away from Murree, Lahore offers a mix of modern and traditional attractions.

Conclusion

Murree offers a unique blend of natural beauty, scenic vistas, and cultural heritage. This guide should come in handy while planning your trip to Murree. Remember to respect local customs and traditions, carry appropriate clothing and supplies, and make the most of your time in this scenic hill station.
