Introduction to Tobelo

Tobelo is a small town located in the Maluku province of Indonesia, known for its natural beauty and cultural significance. This hidden gem is perfect for adventurous travelers seeking a less touristy destination with plenty of activities to keep them occupied.

The people of Tobelo are a mix of indigenous groups and migrants, creating a unique culture that is both welcoming and intriguing. Many of the locals still adhere to traditional practices and beliefs, making Tobelo a fascinating place to explore.

List of Places to Visit in Tobelo

When it comes to things to do in Tobelo, there is no shortage of options. Whether you’re interested in history, culture, or natural beauty, Tobelo has something to offer. As a traveler, you must explore the following:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Fort Tolukko: This 17th-century Portuguese fort is one of Tobelo’s most significant historical sites. It’s located on a hill overlooking the town and the surrounding ocean, providing stunning views for visitors.
  • Museum Siwalima: This museum displays a vast collection of artifacts from the Tobelo and North Halmahera regions. Here, you can learn about the local culture and history.
  • Tobelo Old Mosque: This 16th-century mosque is one of the oldest mosques in Indonesia. It’s still used for daily prayers, and visitors can explore the beautiful architecture and interior design.

Natural Attractions of Tobelo: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Kali Batu Putih Waterfall: Located in the village of Sidangoli, this waterfall is surrounded by lush rainforest and provides a refreshing spot for swimming.
  • Sorake Bay: This beautiful bay offers crystal-clear waters and is perfect for snorkeling and diving. It’s also an ideal spot to relax and watch the stunning sunset.
  • Gua Mamuya: This cave is famous for its impressive stalactites and stalagmites. Visitors can explore the cave system with a guide and learn about the local myths and legends associated with the area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tobelo

Tobelo is an outdoor lover’s paradise, with plenty of hiking and biking trails to explore. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • Mount Gamkonora: This active volcano is the highest point in North Maluku, and the trek to the summit is a challenging but rewarding experience. The hike offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Tobelo-Sidangoli Trail: This 10-kilometer trail takes you from Tobelo to the nearby village of Sidangoli. The route provides stunning views of the ocean and is ideal for both hiking and biking.

Best Things to Do in Tobelo: Activities and Tours

Apart from natural beauty and cultural attractions, Tobelo is also home to a variety of activities and tours that cater to different interests and preferences. Some of the best things to do in Tobelo include:

  • Island hopping: Tobelo is surrounded by several small islands that can be visited by boat. Each island has its unique charm, and visitors can snorkel, swim, and explore the beautiful beaches.
  • Traditional weaving workshops: Tobelo is famous for its traditional weaving methods, and visitors can visit local workshops to learn about the process and create their unique fabrics.
  • Bird watching tour: Tobelo’s rainforests are home to a variety of bird species, and visitors can take a guided tour to spot and learn about them.

Food and Drink Scene of Tobelo

Tobelo’s cuisine is influenced by its cultural diversity, and visitors can explore various traditional dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Nasi Kuning: This flavorful yellow rice dish is usually served with grilled chicken, fried tofu, and local vegetables.
  • Huhate: This spicy dish is made with sliced beef or chicken and mixed with chili sauce and grated coconut.
  • Ikan Tongkol: This grilled or fried tuna dish is served with chili sauce and lime.

Local Beverages

  • Saguer: This refreshing drink is made from fermented palm sap and is available in several flavors, including pineapple, vanilla, and mango.
  • Kopi Arab: This local coffee is usually served black and is known for its strong and rich flavor.

Where to Stay in Tobelo: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Tobelo offers several options for accommodation, ranging from budget-friendly guesthouses to high-end hotels. Some of the best places to stay include:

  • D’Paragon Hotel: This modern hotel offers comfortable rooms with all the necessary amenities and a rooftop restaurant with stunning views of the ocean.
  • Nirwana Beach Resort: This beachfront resort offers luxurious rooms and suites with private beach access, a swimming pool, and a restaurant serving both local and international cuisine.

What to Buy in Tobelo: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for unique souvenirs and local products to bring back home, Tobelo has plenty to offer. Some of the best products to buy include:

  • Traditional woven fabrics: Tobelo is famous for its traditional weaving techniques, and visitors can buy unique fabrics and textiles from local shops.
  • Spices and herbs: Tobelo is known for its delicious spices and herbs, including nutmeg, cloves, and cinnamon.
  • Handmade crafts: Local artisans create a variety of handmade crafts, including wooden carvings, pottery, and jewelry.

Local Culture and Customs of Tobelo

The people of Tobelo have a rich and diverse culture that is deeply ingrained in their way of life. Visitors should respect local customs and traditions, including:

  • Dress appropriately when visiting mosques and other religious sites.
  • Avoid pointing with your feet, as it is considered rude.
  • Seek permission before taking photos of local people.

Best Time to Visit Tobelo

The best time to visit Tobelo is between April and November when the dry weather makes it easy to explore the natural and cultural attractions. The rainy season from December to March can cause flooding and make some areas inaccessible.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tobelo

Do:

  • Learn a few words of Bahasa Indonesia, the local language, to communicate with locals.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Be adventurous and try local cuisine and activities.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing when visiting religious sites.
  • Take photos of locals without their permission.
  • Litter or damage the local environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tobelo

Tobelo is relatively safe, and violent crime is rare. However, visitors should take standard precautions, such as not carrying too much cash and not wandering alone at night.

Health in Tobelo

Visitors should be up to date on their routine vaccinations before traveling to Tobelo. Malaria is prevalent in some areas, and visitors should take appropriate precautions, such as using mosquito repellent and sleeping under mosquito nets.

Getting to Tobelo: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tobelo

Sultan Babullah Airport, located in Ternate, is the nearest airport to Tobelo. From there, visitors can take a taxi or bus to Tobelo, a journey that takes about four hours.

Popular airlines that fly to Ternate from Jakarta are Garuda Indonesia and Lion Air.

Bus Stations Near Tobelo

Tobelo’s main bus terminal connects it to neighboring towns and cities. However, bus travel can be uncomfortable and unreliable.

Other Options

Visitors can also take a ferry from Ternate to Tobelo, but the journey can be long and rough, especially during the rainy season.

How to Get Around in Tobelo

Motorbike rentals are a popular option for getting around Tobelo. Alternatively, visitors can hire a taxi or take a local bus, known as “angkot.”

Where to Go Next from Tobelo: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several nearby destinations and excursions that visitors can explore after Tobelo, including:

  • Ternate: Famous for its historic forts and stunning beaches.
  • Bacan Island: A paradise for beach lovers and snorkelers.
  • Sulawesi: A neighboring island known for its rich culture and natural beauty.

Conclusion

Tobelo’s rich culture and natural beauty make it a destination worth exploring. Whether you’re interested in history, adventure, or relaxation, Tobelo has something for everyone. With this comprehensive travel guide, you are ready to explore Tobelo and have an unforgettable experience.
