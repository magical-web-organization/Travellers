Tanjung Batu Travel Guide

Welcome to Tanjung Batu, a hidden gem located in Kundur, Riau Islands in Sumatra, Indonesia. Tanjung Batu is a breathtaking island that offers unique cultural attractions, natural landmarks, and active pursuits for adventure seekers. This travel guide will provide you with everything you need to plan your visit to this beautiful destination.

Introduction to Tanjung Batu

Tanjung Batu is an enchanting island with pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, lush tropical forests, towering cliffs, and serene seascapes. The island boasts of enigmatic historic artifacts, friendly locals, and a laid-back way of life.

The lush vegetation, rocky hills, and pristine beaches make Tanjung Batu a popular destination for tourists from all over the world. The island is a great place to relax, unwind, and escape the hustle and bustle of city life.

List of Places to Visit in Tanjung Batu

Tanjung Batu has a lot to offer visitors. From museums and historical places to outdoor adventures and scenic views, this destination has something for everyone.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re interested in exploring the rich history and culture of Tanjung Batu, then there are many museums and historical sites for you to visit. Tanjung Batu has a long and storied past that is reflected in its museums and historical sites.

  • The Benteng Keraton Tanjung Batu Fort - a fort with an exciting history that stands on a cliff overlooking the sea, is a must-visit.
  • Museum Sri Bundar - This museum features a grand collection of artifacts, from antique ceramics to captivating traditional clothing and batik art to the writing system of Riau.
  • Lighthouse of Tanjung Pinang - Climb the stairs of this historic lighthouse for a stunning panoramic view of the island.

Natural Attractions of Tanjung Batu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Tanjung Batu is perfect for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers. The island’s natural beauty is unparalleled, and you’ll find plenty of options for hiking, biking, and water sports.

  • Tanjung Batu Waterfall - If you love hiking, then the Tanjung Batu waterfall is a must-visit. The waterfall is situated in the forest area, and the trek to get there will be worth it once you arrive at the location.
  • Tanjung Batu Beach - Spend some time soaking up the sun and taking a dip in crystal-clear waters.
  • Bukit Senyum - This hill is the perfect trek for nature lovers, where the journey involves viewing along the way, bird watching, and taking pictures with the beautiful panoramic view that awaits you.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tanjung Batu

Trekking and cycling are popular activities in Tanjung Batu. There are many routes for both, and each of them is as remarkable as the next.

  • Lagoi Bay - A wonderful bike trail that takes you through forests, wetlands, and other excellent scenic locations.
  • Tanjung Batu Trail - This hiking trail is popular amongst visitors for its exciting journey through the forest that leads them to the waterfall mentioned above.

Best Things to Do in Tanjung Batu: Activities and Tours

In Tanjung Batu, there are plenty of wonderful things to do both on land and in the water. Here are some of our top picks to enjoy:

  • Scuba Diving - Explore the colorful underwater world of Tanjung Batu.
  • Snorkeling - Snorkeling is a fun way to explore the underwater world for those who are not yet ready to dive.
  • Island Hopping - Take a boat tour and visit nearby islands with exotic beaches and crystal clear water.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tanjung Batu

It’s easy to get caught up in all the fun things to do in Tanjung Batu, which is why we’ve put together a week-long itinerary to experience everything the island has to offer:

Day Activity
1 Visit Benteng Keraton Tanjung Batu Fort, Museums Sri Bundar, and have lunch at the beach.
2 Go snorkeling at the Tanjung Batu beach followed by hiking the Tanjung Batu trail.
3 Go diving at one of the diving spots and enjoy lunch on the boat.
4 Take a bike ride around the island and stop by Bukit Senyum to snap photos.
5 Take an island-hopping tour to nearby islands.
6 Visit the lighthouse of Tanjung Pinang and have a picnic on the beach.
7 Spend a day relaxing on the beach or just in your resort.

Food and Drink Scene of Tanjung Batu

If you’re a foodie, then Tanjung Batu will not disappoint. The island has a plethora of dishes that range from spicy, sweet, salty, and sour. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Gado-gado - A popular dish that consists of cooked vegetables with peanut sauce
  • Laksa - A spicy noodle soup
  • Sop Ikan - A fish soup dish that beats the blues of the cold days
  • Nasi Goreng - A traditional Indonesian fried rice dish
  • Ayam Penyet - Spicy and fragrant smashed chicken

Local Beverages

  • Teh Tarik - A frothy tea that is perfect to have with meals
  • Kweh - A sweet dessert made with tapioca and brown sugar

Where to Stay in Tanjung Batu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Tanjung Batu has a range of accommodation options for those who decide to stay on the island. From luxurious resorts to budget-friendly guesthouses, there is something for everyone.

  • Bintan Spa Villa Beach Resort - A resort that offers beautiful scenery and ultimate indulgence in nature, complete with a full-service spa offering traditional massages and body treatments.
  • Grand Lagoi Village - Located on the crystal-clear lagoon bay, this resort is a perfect spot to get a peaceful and comfortable stay

What to Buy in Tanjung Batu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don’t forget to take back some souvenirs from Tanjung Batu. The island offers exciting souvenirs that reflect the culture and history of the island, such as:

  • Batik cloths
  • Handcrafted souvenirs
  • Sarongs
  • Rare and delicious local spices.

Local Culture and Customs of Tanjung Batu

It’s essential to respect the local customs and cultures of Tanjung Batu during your visit. Modest dress is appreciated, especially when visiting religious or cultural sites.

Best Time to Visit Tanjung Batu

The best time to visit Tanjung Batu is from June to September. During this time, the weather is warm and dry making it ideal for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tanjung Batu

Do:

  • Dress modestly
  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Try the local cuisine

Don’t:

  • Litter the environment
  • Damage reefs or harm marine life
  • Go swimming without a life jacket or appropriate supervision

Safety and Health

It’s important to stay safe and healthy during your visit to Tanjung Batu.

Safety in Tanjung Batu

  • Always keep an eye on your belongings
  • Be aware of your surroundings
  • Don’t wander in unfamiliar areas

Health in Tanjung Batu

  • Drink plenty of water to avoid dehydration
  • Protect yourself from the sun
  • Bring insect repellent to protect against mosquito-borne diseases

Getting to Tanjung Batu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Getting to Tanjung Batu is easy. Here are some transportation options and logistics to consider:

Airports Near Tanjung Batu

  • Hang Nadim Airport (BTH) is the main airport serving Tanjung Batu.

Bus Stations Near Tanjung Batu

  • Telaga Punggur Bus Terminal is located in Kundur and connected to major destinations in Riau Islands

Other Options

  • A ferry ride from Batam Harbor can take you to Tanjung Batu.

How to Get Around in Tanjung Batu

Walking and cycling are popular ways to get around Tanjung Batu. You can also rent a bike or motorcycle to explore the island.

Where to Go Next from Tanjung Batu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Batam Island - Batam Island is an industrial island situated less than an hour away from Tanjung Batu.
  • Bintan Island - This island is just an hour away from Tanjung Batu, and it offers a range of fun-filled activities.

Conclusion

Tanjung Batu is a unique destination that offers plenty of things to do for everyone. Whether you’re a nature lover, history buff, or foodie, this island has something for you. Book your next vacation in Tanjung Batu and experience the beauty of Indonesia.
