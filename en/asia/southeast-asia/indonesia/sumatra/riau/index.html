Riau, a province located in the central region of Sumatra in Indonesia, has a blend of culture, history, nature, and cuisine that entices travelers from all over the world. It is famous for its remarkable landscapes, beautiful beaches, delicious food, and rich cultural heritage.

Plan your trip to Riau with our comprehensive travel guide featuring things to do, places to visit, where to stay, what to eat, and more.

Introduction to Riau

Riau is a province in Indonesia that offers a cultural and natural beauty experience that cannot be found anywhere else. The province has a diverse population, mostly consisting of Malays, Chinese, and indigenous tribes, resulting in a unique blend of cultures, customs, and traditions.

List of Places to Visit in Riau

There are many amazing places to visit in Riau. Here are some of the most popular:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Istana Siak
  • Riau Provincial Museum
  • Riau Islamic Centre Grand Mosque
  • Tengku Sulung Monument
  • Taman Budaya Riau

Natural Attractions of Riau: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Bukit Batu Nature Reserve
  • Pulau Jemur Island
  • Rawa Kucing Lake
  • Tesso Nilo National Park

Hiking/Biking Routes of Riau

Riau has many hiking and biking trails. Here are some of the best:

  • Mount Pulai
  • Rimba Alam Forest Park
  • Mount Sibayak
  • Mount Merbabu
  • Mount Sinabung

Best Things to Do in Riau: Activities and Tours

There are many things to do in Riau, from exploring historical sites to trekking through nature. Here are some of the best things to do in Riau:

  • Visit Riau Provincial Museum
  • Take a food tour of the city
  • Visit Pulau Jemur Island
  • Explore Taman Budaya Riau
  • Trek through Bukit Batu Nature Reserve

Food and Drink Scene of Riau

One of the main reasons to visit Indonesia is for its delicious cuisine - and Riau is no exception. Here are some must-try foods and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Ikan Bakar (Grilled Fish)
  • Rendang (Spicy Meat Curry)
  • Sambal (Spicy Chili Sauce)
  • Laksa (Noodle Soup)

Local Beverages

  • Teh Tarik (Pulled Tea)
  • Es Cendol (Iced Sweet Coconut Milk)
  • Kopi Luwak (Civet Coffee)

When in Riau, make sure to buy some of these local products:

Local Culture and Customs of Riau

The people of Riau have a unique blend of cultures, customs, and traditions. Knowing and respecting the local customs will add to your experience and make your trip more enjoyable.

Best Time to Visit Riau

The best time to visit Riau is between March to October when the weather is dry and pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Riau

Do:

  • Respect local culture and traditions
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites and villages
  • Carry small change and small denominations for shopping

Don’t:

  • Consume alcohol in public places
  • Smoke in public places
  • Disrespect cultural symbols and monuments

Safety and Health

Safety in Riau

Riau is relatively safe for tourists. However, it’s always recommended to keep your valuables safe and be wary of pickpockets and scams when travelling in crowded areas.

Health in Riau

Make sure you have all necessary vaccinations before travelling to Riau. The province has MOSQUITO BORNE DECEASE which is why it is always recommended to carry mosquito repellents and nets.

Getting to Riau: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Riau

  • Sultan Syarif Kasim II International Airport (Pekanbaru)

Bus Stations Near Riau

  • Pekanbaru is a major hub for inter-city and inter-province buses

Other Options

  • Get a taxi from nearby cities or hire a car

How to Get Around in Riau

The best way to get around in Riau is by renting a car or hiring a driver. You can also take a taxi or a motorcycle-taxi (Ojek).

It’s the perfect destination for adventure seekers, nature lovers, and cultural enthusiasts alike.
