Bandar Lampung is located in the province of Lampung, in the island of Sumatra, Indonesia. The city is blessed with a rich tapestry of cultural attractions, outdoor adventures, scenic views, and delicious cuisine. Whether you are a history buff, nature lover, thrill-seeker, or just looking to relax and unwind, Bandar Lampung has something to offer for everyone. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will explore the best places to visit, food and drink scene, local customs, safety and health, transportation options and logistics, and nearby destinations and excursions in Bandar Lampung.

List of Places to Visit in Bandar Lampung

Bandar Lampung offers a diverse array of attractions that cater to different interests and preferences. From museums and historical landmarks to outdoor adventures, the city has something for every type of traveler. Here are some of the top places to visit in Bandar Lampung:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum Lampung: This museum showcases the history, culture, and heritage of the Lampung people. You can learn about traditional costumes, handicrafts, musical instruments, and agricultural tools.
  • Krakatoa Monument: This monument honors the victims of the Krakatoa volcano eruption that occurred in 1883. The eruption was one of the deadliest natural disasters in history.
  • Bukit Randu Hotel: This colonial-style hotel was originally built in 1937 and features art deco architecture and elegant interiors. It has accommodated notable guests such as the former Indonesian President Sukarno.
  • Tugu Adipura: This monument is a symbol of Lampung’s commitment to preserving the environment and promoting sustainable development.

Natural Attractions of Bandar Lampung: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Way Kambas National Park: This national park is home to rare and endangered species such as Sumatran elephants, tigers, and rhinoceros. You can take a safari tour, watch elephant bathing, or go birdwatching.
  • Puncak Mas: This mountain peak offers a panoramic view of Bandar Lampung and the surrounding areas. You can reach the summit by hiking or taking a cable car ride.
  • Tanjung Setia Beach: This beach is a popular destination for surfers due to its consistent waves and pristine waters.
  • Teluk Kiluan Dolphin Bay: This bay is a natural habitat for dolphins and is known for its clear waters and stunning scenery.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bandar Lampung

Bandar Lampung has a wide range of hiking and biking trails for those who enjoy outdoor activities. Here are some of the most popular routes:

  • Way Kambas Trekking: This route takes you through tropical rainforests and grasslands, where you can encounter exotic wildlife such as elephants, tigers, and rhinos.
  • Tanjung Pesona Trail: This trail offers breathtaking views of the Lampung Bay and the surrounding mountains. It is suitable for both hiking and biking.
  • Sukadana Green Lane: This cycling route takes you along paddy fields, villages, and riverbanks, where you can experience the rural life of Lampung.

Best Things to Do in Bandar Lampung: Activities and Tours

Bandar Lampung offers a wide range of activities and tours for travelers of all ages and interests. Here are some of the best things to do in the city:

  • Elephant Safari: You can take a safari tour in Way Kambas National Park and watch elephants bathing and playing in their natural habitat.
  • Surfing: Tanjung Setia Beach offers consistent waves and is a popular destination for surfers. You can rent surfboards or take lessons from local instructors.
  • Culinary Tour: You can explore Bandar Lampung’s food scene and taste local dishes such as pempek (fishcakes), otak-otak (grilled fish cake), and martabak (sweet or savory pancake).
  • Island Hopping: You can take a boat tour and visit nearby islands such as Pulau Pahawang and Pulau Krakatoa.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bandar Lampung

If you have a week to spare, here is a suggested itinerary for exploring Bandar Lampung and its surrounding areas:

Food and Drink Scene of Bandar Lampung

Bandar Lampung has a diverse and delicious food scene that reflects its cultural heritage and regional flavors. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes:

  • Pempek: Fishcakes that originated from Palembang and are made from groundfish and tapioca flour.
  • Otak-Otak: Grilled fish cake wrapped in banana leaves and seasoned with spices and coconut milk.
  • Sate Bandeng: Grilled milkfish skewers served with a spicy peanut sauce.
  • Martabak: Sweet or savory pancake filled with meat, vegetables, or chocolate.

Local Beverages:

  • Kopi Lampung: Lampung’s signature coffee that is known for its bold and earthy flavor.
  • Wedang Jahe: Ginger tea that is brewed with palm sugar and served hot.
  • Es Kacang Merah: Red bean shaved ice dessert that is topped with evaporated milk and syrup.

Where to Stay in Bandar Lampung: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bandar Lampung offers a variety of accommodation options that cater to different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the top choices:

  • Novotel Lampung: A luxury hotel in the heart of the city with modern amenities and excellent service.
  • Capsule Inn: A budget-friendly hostel that offers capsule-style beds and shared facilities.
  • Ibis Budget Lampung: A mid-range hotel that offers clean and comfortable rooms and basic amenities.

What to Buy in Bandar Lampung: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Bandar Lampung is known for its handicrafts, textiles, and agricultural products. Here are some of the best souvenirs and local products to bring home:

  • Songket: A traditional Lampung textile that is woven with gold or silver threads.
  • Kain Tenun: Handwoven fabrics that feature intricate designs and patterns.
  • Coffee: Lampung produces some of the best coffee in Indonesia, and you can buy high-quality beans or ground coffee from the local markets.
  • Krakatoa Souvenirs: You can buy souvenirs such as T-shirts, keychains, and miniatures that feature the iconic Krakatoa volcano.

Local Culture and Customs of Bandar Lampung

Bandar Lampung is a multicultural city that is influenced by various ethnic groups, including Lampung, Javanese, Chinese, and Arabic. Here are some of the local customs and traditions:

  • Respect: Indonesians value respect and politeness, and you should always greet people with a smile and a polite gesture.
  • Religion: Lampungese people practice Islam as their main religion, and it is important to respect their customs and traditions.
  • Batik: Batik is a form of traditional Indonesian textile that is created using a wax-resistant dyeing technique. Lampung has its own unique batik patterns that reflect the region’s cultural heritage.
  • Kerapu Dance: Kerapu is a traditional Lampung dance that features graceful movements and music.

Best Time to Visit Bandar Lampung

The best time to visit Bandar Lampung is from May to September when the weather is dry and sunny. During these months, you can enjoy outdoor activities and scenic views without worrying about rain or humidity. However, if you prefer a more relaxed and peaceful environment, you can visit during the low season from October to April.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bandar Lampung

Here are some of the do’s and don’ts of Bandar Lampung to help you navigate the local customs and culture:

Do:

  • Dress modestly and respectfully, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Learn a few words in Indonesian, such as “terima kasih” (thank you) or “maaf” (excuse me) to show your respect for the local language.
  • Smile and greet people with a friendly gesture.
  • Try the local food and drinks.

Don’t:

  • Do not show public displays of affection, as this is considered impolite.
  • Do not wear shoes inside someone’s home or a mosque.
  • Do not touch someone’s head or use your left hand for eating or giving things.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bandar Lampung

Bandar Lampung is generally a safe city, but it is important to take precautions and be aware of your surroundings. Here are some safety tips:

  • Avoid flashing expensive items in public places.
  • Keep your valuables in a safe place and carry only what you need.
  • Use reputable transportation services and avoid walking alone at night.
  • Be cautious when swimming or participating in water activities.
  • Follow local rules and regulations, especially when it comes to wildlife conservation.

Health in Bandar Lampung

Bandar Lampung has several hospitals and clinics that offer reliable medical services. However, it is recommended to take precautionary measures and vaccinations before traveling to Indonesia. Here are some health tips:

  • Drink only bottled water or water that has been boiled or treated.
  • Use insect repellent and wear long-sleeved shirts and pants to avoid mosquito bites.
  • Seek medical attention if you experience stomach problems or fever.

Getting to Bandar Lampung: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bandar Lampung is accessible by air, land, and sea. Here are some transportation options and logistics:

Airports Near Bandar Lampung

  • Radin Inten II Airport: This is the main airport in Bandar Lampung and serves domestic flights from Jakarta, Bali, Surabaya, and other cities in Indonesia.

Bus Stations Near Bandar Lampung

  • Terminal Rajabasa: This is the main bus station in Bandar Lampung and serves buses from Jakarta, Palembang, Jambi, and other cities in Sumatra.

Other Options

  • Ferry: You can take a ferry from Merak to Bakauheni and then take a bus or rent a car to reach Bandar Lampung.

How to Get Around in Bandar Lampung

Bandar Lampung offers several transportation options for getting around the city and its surrounding areas. Here are some of the most common ones:

  • Bus: Public buses and minibusses are available for getting around Bandar Lampung. They are affordable but can be crowded and uncomfortable during peak hours.
  • Taxi and Grab: Taxis and ride-hailing services such as Grab are available in Bandar Lampung. They are convenient but can be more expensive than other options.
  • Car and Motorbike Rental: You can rent a car or motorbike from local providers or online booking platforms. This is a good option if you want to explore Bandar Lampung and its surroundings at your own pace.

Where to Go Next from Bandar Lampung: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bandar Lampung is a gateway to some of the most beautiful destinations in Sumatra. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions:

  • Krakatoa Island: You can take a boat tour to this iconic volcanic island and witness the stunning landscape and wildlife.
  • Bukit Barisan Selatan National Park: This park is home to rare and endangered species such as tigers, rhinos, and elephants. You can go hiking, camping, or wildlife spotting.
  • Palembang: This city is known for its rich history, diverse culinary scene, and authentic Palembangese culture. You can visit historical landmarks such as the Ampera Bridge and Al-Munawar Mosque.

Conclusion

Bandar Lampung is a hidden gem in Sumatra that offers a unique blend of culture, nature, and adventure. Whether you want to explore the city’s historical landmarks, hike its scenic trails, or taste its delicious cuisine, there is something for everyone in Bandar Lampung. We hope this comprehensive travel guide has given you a glimpse of what this beautiful city has to offer and has inspired you to plan your next adventure in Bandar Lampung. Happy travels!
