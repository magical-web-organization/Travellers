Introduction to Sumbawa Besar

Sumbawa Besar is a hidden gem located on the island of Sumbawa in Nusa Tenggara, Indonesia. Unlike its neighbor Bali, Sumbawa Besar remains unspoiled and rich in culture. It offers visitors an authentic experience of Indonesia, with its stunning landscapes, friendly locals, and unique customs and traditions.

List of Places to Visit in Sumbawa Besar

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re a history buff, you won’t want to miss these culturally important attractions in Sumbawa Besar:

  • Sultan Salahuddin Mosque: This beautiful mosque is a landmark in Sumbawa Besar and is famous for its intricate architecture and stunning courtyard.
  • Sumbawa Museum: Learn about the history and culture of Sumbawa at this fascinating museum.
  • Dalam Loka Palace: Once the home of the Sultan of Sumbawa, this palace is now open to visitors and showcases traditional architecture and décor.

Natural Attractions of Sumbawa Besar: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Outdoor enthusiasts can discover the natural beauty of Sumbawa Besar at the following attractions:

  • Kenawa Island: Accessible by boat, this small island boasts crystal-clear waters, white sands, and an unspoiled coral reef.
  • Bukit Batu Mandi: Trek up to the highest point on Sumbawa island for breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Watu Lanting Waterfall: A hidden gem located in the middle of a rainforest, this waterfall is a refreshing and scenic break from the heat.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Sumbawa Besar

For adventurous travelers, Sumbawa Besar offers a variety of hiking and biking trails:

  • Mount Tambora: At 2,851 meters, this volcano is the highest point in Sumbawa and is an excellent excursion for experienced hikers.
  • Pantai Menyelam Beach Trail: This easy and scenic trail takes you through coconut groves and along the stunning coastline of Pantai Menyelam Beach.
  • Lapangan Merdeka Park Trail: Bikers and joggers will enjoy this paved and shaded path that takes you through the heart of Sumbawa Besar.

Best Things to Do in Sumbawa Besar: Activities and Tours

Make the most of your visit to Sumbawa Besar with these experiences and tours:

  • Traditional Weaving Workshop: Learn about the art of weaving traditional textiles from skilled artisans in Sumbawa Besar.
  • Snorkeling and Diving: Explore the world-renowned underwater oasis around Sumbawa Besar, where you can see colorful corals and an abundance of marine life.
  • Sunrise Boat Tour: Experience the breathtaking sunrise from the middle of the ocean, surrounded by the tranquil waters of Sumbawa.

Food and Drink Scene of Sumbawa Besar

Must-Try Dishes

Sumbawa is known for its flavorful and spicy cuisine. Don’t leave without trying these dishes:

  • Ayam Taliwang: This traditional dish consists of grilled chicken marinated with a spicy blend of chili, garlic, and shrimp paste.
  • Sate Bulayak: These skewered beef or chicken are served with a flavorful sauce made with turmeric, chili, and coconut oil.
  • Plecing Kangkung: This dish features steamed water spinach topped with a spicy tomato sauce.

Local Beverages

Wash down your meal with these refreshing local beverages:

  • Kopi Sumbawa: This strong and unique coffee is made with beans grown in the highlands of Sumbawa.
  • Brem: This sweet rice wine is a popular drink in Sumbawa and is usually served during celebrations and festivals.

Where to Stay in Sumbawa Besar: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are plenty of accommodation options in Sumbawa Besar to suit all budgets and preferences:

  • Hotel Santika Sumbawa: A modern hotel with comfortable rooms and a central location.
  • Sunset Villa Sumbawa: This beachfront villa offers stunning views and a peaceful atmosphere.
  • Aloha Hotel Sumbawa: A budget-friendly option with clean and simple rooms.

What to Buy in Sumbawa Besar: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Take home a piece of Sumbawa with these unique souvenirs:

  • Traditional Textiles: Handwoven textiles made by skilled artisans in Sumbawa are an excellent souvenir and a testament to the island’s rich weaving tradition.
  • Bamboo Crafts: The craftsmen of Sumbawa are known for their intricate bamboo products, from baskets to furniture.
  • Sumbawa Coffee: Take home a bag of Sumbawa’s unique and delicious coffee beans.

Local Culture and Customs of Sumbawa Besar

Respect and appreciate the local customs and traditions of Sumbawa by following these tips:

  • Dress Conservatively: Sumbawa is a Muslim island, and it’s essential to dress respectfully in public places.
  • Remove Shoes Before Entering a Home: Take off your shoes before entering a Sumbawan home, as it’s considered impolite to wear shoes indoors.
  • Greet Locals with a Smile: The locals of Sumbawa are friendly and welcoming, so return the favor by greeting them with a smile and a friendly “hello.”

Best Time to Visit Sumbawa Besar

The best time to visit Sumbawa Besar is from April to October, during the dry season. The weather is warm and sunny, and the sea is calm, making it perfect for outdoor activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sumbawa Besar

Sumbawa Besar is relatively safe for tourists. However, it’s always important to take basic precautions to ensure your safety, such as keeping an eye on your belongings and avoiding walking alone at night along deserted streets.

Health in Sumbawa Besar

It’s essential to take precautions against tropical diseases such as dengue fever and malaria by taking preventative medication and using mosquito repellent. Make sure to drink bottled water and avoid street food that may not be hygienic.

Getting to Sumbawa Besar: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Sumbawa Besar

The nearest airport to Sumbawa Besar is Sultan Muhammad Kaharuddin III Airport (SWQ). It is located in Bima, approximately 3 hours away from Sumbawa Besar by car.

Bus Stations Near Sumbawa Besar

Sumbawa Besar is well-connected by bus to other cities in Nusa Tenguara. The main bus hub in Sumbawa Besar is located in the city center, with several companies offering daily trips to destinations such as Mataram and Denpasar.

Other Options

Ferry services are available from Lombok and Bali to Sumbawa Besar. The ferry terminal is located in Poto Tano, approximately 2 hours away from Sumbawa Besar by car.

How to Get Around in Sumbawa Besar

The best way to get around Sumbawa Besar is by hiring a car or motorbike. Taxis and ride-sharing services are also available, but they can be more expensive.

Where to Go Next from Sumbawa Besar: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Sumbawa Besar is an excellent base for exploring the surrounding areas of Sumbawa:

  • Lakey Beach: Famous for its world-class surfing, Lakey Beach is located approximately 2 hours away from Sumbawa Besar.
  • Wanokaka Beach: A hidden gem located approximately 3 hours away from Sumbawa Besar, Wanokaka Beach boasts crystal clear waters and pristine white sands.
  • Komodo Island: Located approximately one hour by flight from Sumbawa Besar, Komodo Island is a must-visit destination for nature lovers.

Conclusion

Sumbawa Besar is a unique and authentic destination in Indonesia, with a rich culture and beautiful landscapes waiting for you to explore. Use our comprehensive guide to plan your visit and make the most of your time in Sumbawa Besar.
