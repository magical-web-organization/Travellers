Introduction to Legian

Located in South Bali, Indonesia, Legian is a lively town that offers a perfect mix of natural beauty, adventure activities, and culture. The town is situated between Seminyak and Kuta, which are equally popular tourist destinations. Legian is known for its pristine beaches, delicious foods, outdoor adventures, and vibrant nightlife. The town is also an excellent place to explore fascinating museums and historical places.

List of Places to Visit in Legian

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • (1) Bali Bombing Memorial: Built-in memory of the victims of the 2002 Bali bombings, the Bali Bombing Memorial provides a moving tribute and educational experience for visitors.

  • (2) Arma Museum: The Arma Museum boasts impressive collections of Indonesian art, including traditional forms and modern exhibits, making it the perfect place to experience Indonesia’s rich artistic traditions.

Natural Attractions of Legian: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • (1) Legian Beach: With soft golden sand and serene waves, Legian Beach is the perfect place to relax and soak up the sun, and it is also an excellent spot for surfing.

  • (2) Bali Treetop Adventure Park: Bali Treetop Adventure Park offers hours of outdoor fun, including ziplining and tree-top obstacle courses set amidst the lush Bali forest.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Legian

  • (1) Mount Batur Sunrise Trek: This famous sunrise trek offers a stunning, picture-perfect sunrise view from the summit of Mt. Batur, one of Bali’s highest peaks.

  • (2) Ubud Bike Tour: Ubud Bike Tour is a guided cycling tour that takes you through rice paddies, charming villages, and local markets to explore the gorgeous countryside.

Best Things to Do in Legian: Activities and Tours

  • (1) Water Sports: Legian is the perfect place to enjoy watersports activities such as surfing, swimming, and windsurfing.

  • (2) Kintamani Volcano Tour: Kintamani Volcano Tour is a full-day guided tour, featuring visits to Balinese temples, the Tegalalang rice terrace, and Mt. Batur.

Food and Drink Scene of Legian

Must-Try Dishes

  • (1) Nasi Goreng: Nasi Goreng, or fried rice, is a staple dish in Indonesia and is a must-try dish in Legian.

  • (2) Satay: Satay (skewered and grilled meat) is a popular dish in Bali and is typically served with peanut sauce and rice.

Local Beverages

  • (1) Bintang Beer: Bintang Beer is a popular local beer in Bali and can be found in almost every bar or restaurant.

  • (2) Kopi Luwak: Considered the world’s most expensive coffee, Kopi Luwak is unique to Bali and is made using coffee beans eaten and fermented by civet cats.

Where to Stay in Legian: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • (1) Legian Beach Hotel: Legian Beach Hotel is perfect for families and offers excellent ocean views, lush tropical gardens, and a beautiful swimming pool.

  • (2) Anantara Vacation Club Legian Bali: This stunning resort offers guests the perfect blend of luxury, comfort, and convenience, with its gorgeous swimming pool, fitness center, and restaurant.

What to Buy in Legian: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • (1) Balinese Handicrafts: Balinese Handicrafts are a popular souvenir that encompasses everything from silver jewelry and woodcarvings to textiles and baskets.

  • (2) Kopi Luwak Coffee: Kopi Luwak Coffee is a unique and unusual souvenir and a perfect gift for coffee lovers.

Local Culture and Customs of Legian

The Balinese culture is based on a unique blend of Hinduism and animism. The people of Bali are known for their rich artistic and musical traditions, which range from intricate temple carvings and towering statues to elegant dance performances and gamelan music.

Best Time to Visit Legian

The best time to visit Legian is between May and September when the weather is warm and sunny, and rainfall is minimal. This is also the busiest time, so be sure to book your accommodations and excursions in advance.

Do’s and Don’ts of Legian

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Wear respectful clothing when visiting religious sites.
  • Learn a few basic phrases of the Indonesian language.

Don’t:

  • Engage in public displays of affection.
  • Disrespect religious customs or taboos.
  • Purchase goods made from endangered species.

Safety and Health

Safety in Legian

While Legian is a relatively safe place to visit, travelers should always take standard safety precautions, such as locking up valuables and using a safe, avoiding deserted areas after dark, and staying aware of their surroundings.

Health in Legian

Travelers to Legian should take appropriate precautions to avoid mosquito bites, as mosquito-borne illnesses such as dengue fever, malaria, and chikungunya can occur.

Getting to Legian: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Legian

  • (1) Ngurah Rai International Airport is the closest airport to Legian, and most international and domestic flights arrive there.

Bus Stations Near Legian

  • (1) Perama Bus Station: Located in Kuta, Perama Bus Station offers daily buses to most tourist destinations in Bali.

Other Options

  • (1) Taxi: Taxis are readily available throughout Legian and offer an affordable way to get around.

How to Get Around in Legian

Getting around in Legian is easy, with options such as private taxis, rental cars, and motorbikes. However, exploring on foot or by bicycle is also possible and is a great way to experience the town’s scenic views.

Where to Go Next From Legian: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • (1) Ubud: Ubud is a cultural and artistic hub, known for its lush rice paddies, art galleries, museums, and yoga retreats.

  • (2) Nusa Lembongan: Nusa Lembongan is a small island off Bali, famous for its crystal-clear waters, white sand beaches, and excellent dive sites.

Conclusion

Legian is a unique and exciting travel destination that offers something for everyone. With pristine beaches, outdoor adventures, cultural attractions, delicious food, and excellent accommodations, Legian is the perfect place to experience the best of Bali. Plan your visit today and create memories that will last a lifetime!
