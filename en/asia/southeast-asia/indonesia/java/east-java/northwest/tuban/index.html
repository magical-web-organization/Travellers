Introduction to Tuban

Tuban is a small city in East Java, located in the northwest part of the province. It is a relatively low-profile destination that has plenty to offer, including cultural and natural attractions, outdoor adventures, and delicious local food. Tuban is a great place to visit for those seeking a less touristy destination in Indonesia.

List of Places to Visit in Tuban

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Tuban has numerous culturally significant landmarks where visitors can explore the rich history and cultural heritage of the region. The Mustika Temple, Tuban Great Mosque, and Kwan Im Temple are some of the must-see attractions for spiritual and religious travelers. History buffs can head to the Tuban Museum, which showcases a variety of artifacts from the early 17th century.

Natural Attractions of Tuban: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Tuban offers many opportunities for outdoor activities and nature lovers. Do not miss the chance to explore the beautiful coastlines by enjoying the scenic views along Pancur Beach or Binangun Waterfall. A visit to the Candi Jawi or Banyu Anjlok hot springs are also popular activities.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tuban

Trekking, hiking, and biking are some of the exciting activities to do in Tuban. One of the best routes is the Songo- Songo hiking trail, which is an excellent spot for travelers to explore the lush green mountainside along with the beautiful sunrise and sunset views.

Best Things to Do in Tuban: Activities and Tours

For those seeking a hands-on experience, a pottery class at Sudimoro Village is a must-try. Visitors can also have an adventure safari experience in Baluran National Park, which offers a range of activities like snorkeling, bird watching, and jungle trekking.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tuban

Day 1: Explore cultural attractions in Tuban (Mustika Temple, Tuban Great Mosque, and Kwan Im Temple) Day 2: Enjoy a day trip to Binangun Waterfall or Pancur Beach. Day 3: Take a pottery class at Sudimoro Village. Day 4: Visit Banyu Anjlok hot springs. Day 5: Experience the Baluran National Park adventure safari. Day 6: Trekking in Songo-Songo Mountains Trail. Day 7: Explore Tuban Museum and souvenir shopping for local goods.

Food and Drink Scene of Tuban

Must-Try Dishes

Tuban has excellent dining choices with a range of delicious local food to savor. Some must-try dishes in the region include Nasi Tahu Campur, a mix of rice and vegetables served with tofu and peanut sauce, and Soto Gading, a flavorful soup made with beef or chicken.

Local Beverages

For a refreshing drink, try Es Cendol, a traditional Indonesian dessert made from coconut milk with pandan flavored jelly and palm sugar syrup. Another local beverage to try is Es Gempol Pleret, a refreshing cocktail made from a mixture of cucumber, kolang kaling, and syrup.

Where to Stay in Tuban: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Tuban provides an array of lodging options that cater to different budgets and interests. The best choices include the Bethsaida Cottage and Primebiz Hotel Tuban for luxury accommodations, among others. There are also affordable guesthouses and hostels available like the Rumah Lennah Guest House and Pondok Tamara Homestay.

What to Buy in Tuban: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Tuban is known for its traditional handicrafts, including Batik Tulis and Batik Cap. Also, check out Sudimoro Village that sells locally made pottery.

Local Culture and Customs of Tuban

Tuban has a rich cultural heritage with a blend of Javanese, Chinese, and Arabic heritage. One of Tuban’s lesser-known traditions is “Mocopat Syafaat,” a Javanese traditional music performance.

Best Time to Visit Tuban

The best time to visit Tuban is from June to September. Visitors can enjoy beautiful weather, and the probability of rain is low. During this time, travelers can also enjoy various outdoor activities, such as trekking or exploring the beautiful coastlines.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tuban

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Try local food and participate in local activities and festivals.
  • Wear appropriate clothing and dress modest when visiting religious sites or rural areas.

Don’t:

  • Drink tap water, always stick to bottled water.
  • Use your left hand to give or receive anything, as it is considered inappropriate in Indonesian culture.
  • Touch or sit on the head or point your feet at people or objects- It is considered impolite.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tuban

Tuban is generally safe, but like in any other tourist destination, visitors must exercise caution when traveling. Travelers should avoid unlit areas at night, wandering off the beaten path, and be aware of their surroundings.

Health in Tuban

Visitors should seek medical attention promptly if they experience any health issues while in Tuban. It is recommended that tourists obtain medical travel insurance before arriving in Indonesia.

Getting to Tuban: Transportation Options and Logistics

Tuban is connected by road and air transport to other Indonesian regions. There are direct flights to Madura-Sampang Airport and Juanda International Airport, Surabaya. Visitors can also use bus services, taxis or hire motorbikes to travel in Tuban.

Airports Near Tuban

The nearest airports are Madura-Sampang Airport and Juanda International Airport, Surabaya.

Bus Stations Near Tuban

The closest bus station is Tuban Bus Station, which is located in Tuban city center.

Other Options:

It is possible to hire a taxi or rent a motorbike to explore the city and other nearby destinations.

How to Get Around in Tuban

There are several transportation options in Tuban, including taxis, ride-hailing services, buses, and motorbike rentals. Visitors can also explore the area on foot or hire a private driver for more convenience.

Where to Go Next from Tuban: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Some nearby destinations that visitors can explore from Tuban include Surabaya City, Mount Bromo, Madura Island, and Wringinlawang Temple.

Conclusion

Tuban has a unique and charming collection of cultural and natural attractions. With so much to offer, the city can be explored by visitors at a leisurely pace. This comprehensive travel guide includes all the essential information needed to plan a perfect trip to Tuban. Visitors are sure to have an unforgettable experience that they will always cherish.
