Introduction to Pacet

Located in Surabaya Metropolitan Area, in East Java, Pacet is a picturesque mountain resort town enthralling travelers with its captivating landscape, cultural significance, and warm hospitality. The town is situated in the northern slopes of Mount Arjuno and is rich in beautiful natural attractions like hot springs, waterfalls, and beautiful flora and fauna. Pacet has a rich cultural history and is home to some beautiful temples, museums, and heritage buildings that tell the story of the town’s past.

List of Places to Visit in Pacet

Discover the beautiful places to visit in Pacet that make this place a must-visit for travelers.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Explore Pacet’s rich cultural heritage by visiting these beautiful Museums and Historical Places:

  • Tumpang Museum: Tumpang Museum is a small museum situated in the nearby town of Tumpang depicting the history of the Tumpang Kingdom. It has a beautiful collection of artifacts and relics from the past.
  • Ambarawa Colonial Heritage: Ambarawa Colonial Heritage is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Pacet, showcasing the beautiful colonial buildings of the area, and their unique architecture.

Natural Attractions of Pacet: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Discover the natural beauty of Pacet with these beautiful outdoor adventures and scenic views:

  • Mount Arjuno-Hiking: The beautiful Mount Arjuno is a must-visit for adventure seekers offering some excellent hiking and trekking trails to explore.
  • Cangar Hot Spring: The Cangar hot spring is one of the most popular tourist attractions in Pacet, inviting you to relax your body and soul amidst the stunning natural beauty.
  • Coban Pelangi Waterfall: Coban Pelangi with its stunning waterfalls offers breathtaking views and is perfect for nature lovers.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Pacet

Embark on a hiking or biking adventure in Pacet and explore the beautiful landscape, nature, and wildlife.

  • Mount Arjuno-Hiking Trail: A popular hiking trail, the Mount Arjuno trail offers some amazing views of the stunning landscapes and natural beauty of Pacet.
  • Bicycle Tour: Rent a bike and tour around the town for a scenic and relaxed adventure offering glimpses of the beautiful mountain scenery and traditional Indonesian village life.

Best Things to Do in Pacet: Activities and Tours

Discover the best things to do in Pacet with these exciting activities and tours:

  • Waterfall Tour: The waterfall tour offers an exciting opportunity to visit some of the most beautiful and scenic waterfalls in the area.
  • Hot Spring & Spa Experience: Get pampered and rejuvenate your body, soul, and mind with a hot spring and spa experience in Cangar.

Food and Drink Scene of Pacet

Discover Pacet’s local cuisine and beverage scene with these must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sate Klopo on Rasa Khas Pacet: A must-try dish that’s unique to Pacet, it is a noodle dish served with spicy meat sauce and shredded young coconut.
  • Bakso Tahu on Warung Gembira: A local version of meatball soup with a soft tofu filling, famous for its tender meatballs and flavor-rich broth.

Local Beverages

  • Jamu Herbal Drink: A traditional herbal drink made from various natural ingredients, believed to have medicinal properties, and is commonly consumed as a daily health tonic.
  • Wedang Jahe: A popular ginger tea that is sweet and aromatic with a strong ginger flavor.

Where to Stay in Pacet: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Discover the best places to stay in Pacet that offer a blend of comfort and luxury for a pleasant stay.

  • Pande Homestay: A beautiful and comfortable homestay situated in Jl. Dewa Baru offers a serene atmosphere and is perfect for budget travelers.
  • The Graha Kinasih Resort: A luxurious resort situated in the heart of the town, offering a range of modern amenities and facilities.

What to Buy in Pacet: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Shop for these beautiful souvenirs and local products to bring back home.

  • Keripik Tahu: A local snack made of tofu and deep-fried to enjoy.
  • Pottery: Handmade pottery products made by locals that showcase the traditional Indonesian way of life.

Local Culture and Customs of Pacet

Discover the local culture and customs while visiting Pacet, and learn about the traditional Indonesian way of life, which is captivating and enchanting.

Best Time to Visit Pacet

The best time to visit Pacet is between June and September when the monsoon season subsides, offering warm temperatures and sunny skies.

Do’s and Don’ts of Pacet

While visiting Pacet, follow these do’s and don’ts to respect the local culture and customs.

Do:

  • Dress modestly when visiting places of worship.
  • Respect the environment and avoid littering.

Don’t:

  • Do not touch or disturb wildlife and natural resources.
  • Do not disrespect the local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Ensure a safe and healthy trip to Pacet with these informative tips.

Safety in Pacet

  • Beware of pickpockets and bag snatchers.
  • Avoid dimly lit and secluded areas at night.

Health in Pacet

  • Drink safe and bottled water.
  • Pack a medical kit with essential medicines and first aid supplies.

Getting to Pacet: Transportation Options and Logistics

Discover the best ways to reach Pacet, East Java, and start your adventure.

Airports Near Pacet

  • Juanda International Airport-Located in Surabaya, Indonesia, is the nearest international airport to Pacet, located 50 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Pacet

  • Surabaya Bus Station- A major bus station located in the heart of Surabaya, from where you can take a direct bus to Pacet.

Other Options

  • Private car/taxi: Hire a car/taxi from the airport or bus station to reach Pacet conveniently.

How to Get Around in Pacet

Discover the best ways to get around Pacet.

  • Rent a Scooter or Bicycle: Renting a scooter or bicycle is the best way to get around Pacet, as public transportation is not very reliable.

Where to Go Next from Pacet: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Continue your adventure and explore the nearby destinations and excursions:

  • Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park: Known for its beautiful sunrise and surreal volcanic landscape.
  • Malang City: A beautiful ancient city with rich culture and heritage and popular for its art and cuisine.
