Introduction to Tulungagung

Tulungagung is a regency situated in Southern Mountainous, in East Java, in Java, in Indonesia, in Southeast Asia, in Asia. The region is known for its cultural heritage, lush nature, and exceptional natural attractions, including hills, waterfalls, and beaches. Tulungagung is perfect for both nature enthusiasts and cultural enthusiasts, and it is an excellent destination for travelers looking for a unique travel experience.

List of Places to Visit in Tulungagung

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Tulungagung has various culturally important attractions that offer a glimpse into the region’s history and culture. Visitors must visit the following museums and historical places.

  • Ngrowo Grand Mosque: The mosque was built in the 15th century and is a priceless cultural heritage of Tulungagung, located in Ngrowo village.
  • Brawijaya Museum: The museum displays tributes to the Indonesian Army, especially the Brawijaya Division, which was active from 1960 to 1999. They offer paintings, weapons, and other items from the division.
  • Kanoman Palace: The palace was built in 1758 and was used by the Kanoman Kingdom’s royal family. This palace is famous for its architecture and historical objects.

Natural Attractions of Tulungagung: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

When it comes to natural attractions, Tulungagung is second to none. The region is blessed with stunning natural landscapes that offer breathtaking views and exciting outdoor adventures. Below are some of the top natural attractions that you must visit while in Tulungagung:

  • Mount Wilis: The mountain is an exceptional natural wonder that draws visitors from all over the world. Visitors can hike and enjoy the stunning views from the peak of the mountain.
  • Beaches: Tulungagung has some of the best beaches in the region, including Selokambang beach, Ngampel Beach, and Popoh Beach, which are ideal for swimming and water sports activities.
  • Gondang Dam: Visitors can enjoy the serene beauty of Gondang Dam and take a boat ride to explore the area’s natural surroundings.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tulungagung

Tulungagung is a paradise for hiking and biking enthusiasts. The region has stunning natural landscapes that offer excellent hiking and biking trails. Here are some of the best hiking/biking routes of Tulungagung:

  • Mount Wilis: Hiking Mount Willis is a must-do activity for adventure enthusiasts. The mountain offers stunning views from the peak of the area, making it a great hiking destination.
  • Bendungan Pidekso: Visitors can enjoy the scenic views and take a biking tour around the Bendungan Pidekso area. The route is perfect for those who want to experience the beauty of nature and enjoy the fresh air.

Best Things to Do in Tulungagung: Activities and Tours

Tulungagung offers a range of activities and tours that cater to all types of travelers. Adventure enthusiasts can enjoy hiking, biking, and water sports activities, while cultural enthusiasts can explore the museums and historical places. Here are some of the best things to do in Tulungagung:

  • Visit the Ngrowo Grand Mosque: Visitors can explore the mosque’s unique architecture and stunning interiors and experience the Islamic culture of Tulungagung.
  • Go hiking at Mount Wilis: Hiking Mount Willis offers a unique experience that no other activity can match. Visitors can enjoy the refreshing air and stunning views from the mountain’s peak.
  • Beach hopping: Tulungagung has some of the best beaches in the region, and visitors can enjoy the pristine waters and try water sports activities such as snorkeling and diving.

Food and Drink Scene of Tulungagung

Tulungagung’s food and drink scene offers visitors a unique culinary experience. Visitors must try the local dishes and beverages while in Tulungagung.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sate Kambing: A famous dish of Tulungagung, made of tender goat meat that is marinated in various spices and grilled to perfection. It is served with peanut sauce and lontong or ketupat (compressed rice).
  • Sate Ayam: A dish made of grilled chicken with peanut sauce and lontong or ketupat.
  • Soto Tulungagung: A soup dish made of chicken broth with shredded chicken, vermicelli, and eggs.

Local Beverages

  • Jamu Kunyit Asam: A traditional ginger and turmeric drink believed to have excellent health benefits.
  • Wedang Jahe: A ginger tea commonly served during cold weather.

What to Buy in Tulungagung: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Visitors can purchase unique souvenirs and local products to bring home from Tulungagung. Here are some of the best items to buy:

  • Sate Kambing Sauce: Visitors can buy the sauce that makes sate kambing special.
  • Pineapple Cake: A famous snack of Tulungagung, made of pineapple jam and buttery pastry.

Local Culture and Customs of Tulungagung

Tulungagung has a rich cultural heritage that visitors can explore while in the region. The local culture and customs are heavily influenced by Javanese traditions, and visitors must respect them.

  • Beksan Lawung: A traditional dance of Tulungagung that is performed during a circumcision ceremony.
  • Reog Ponorogo: A traditional art form of Tulungagung that features a lion mask and a peacock mask.
  • Javanese Language: Visitors should learn some Javanese words to communicate with the locals and show respect for their culture and customs.

Best Time to Visit Tulungagung

The best time to visit Tulungagung is during the dry season, which runs from May to September. The weather is pleasant during this time, perfect for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tulungagung

Tulungagung is a relatively safe destination, and visitors need not worry about any significant safety concerns. However, visitors should take common-sense precautions, such as keeping their belongings close and avoiding unlit and busy areas during the night.

Health in Tulungagung

Visitors should drink bottled water and avoid tap water. While there is no significant health risk, they can take precautions against mosquito bites by using mosquito repellent and wearing long sleeves and pants.

Getting to Tulungagung: Transportation Options and Logistics

Tulungagung is accessible by air, bus, and private transport.

Airports Near Tulungagung

  • Juanda International Airport: The airport serves Surabaya, and visitors can take a taxi from the airport to Tulungagung.

Bus Stations Near Tulungagung

  • Tulungagung Bus Station: The station is located in the city center, and visitors can take a bus from various cities to Tulungagung.

Other Options

  • Private Transport: Visitors can hire private transport to take them to and from Tulungagung.

How to Get Around in Tulungagung

The easiest way to get around in Tulungagung is by renting a car or motorcycle. Visitors can also use taxis or public transport to get around the city, but it can be more challenging to explore the region without a private vehicle.

Where to Go Next from Tulungagung: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Tulungagung is located near various destinations and excursions that visitors can explore. Below are some of the top destinations:

  • Kediri: A cultural destination known for its ancient temples and museums.
  • Malang: A popular destination known for its beautiful landscapes and excellent culinary scene.
  • Mt. Bromo: A famous volcano known for its beautiful sunrise views.

Conclusion

Tulungagung is a must-visit destination in East Java, offering a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural heritage. Visitors can enjoy hiking, water sports activities, explore museums and historical places, and try delicious local dishes and beverages. Plan your dream trip to Tulungagung today!
