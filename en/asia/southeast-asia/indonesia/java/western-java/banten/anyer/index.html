Introduction to Anyer

Nestled in the western province of Banten, Anyer is a small coastal town in Indonesia. Known for its proximity to the Krakatau volcano and the Sunda Strait, Anyer offers visitors a fantastic blend of natural beauty, history, and culture. This town is perfect for travelers who wish to escape the hustle and bustle of larger Indonesian cities and immerse themselves in a tranquil environment.

Local Culture and Customs of Anyer

Anyer is a town that values local traditions and customs. Tapping on tables or any flat surface while eating is considered to be a sign of appreciation to the cook, while blowing one’s nose in public is considered impolite.

Best Time to Visit Anyer

The best time to visit Anyer is from May to September, when the town experiences a dry climate.

Do’s and Don’ts of Anyer

Do:

  • Respect local culture and customs
  • Dress modestly

Don’t:

  • Take photographs of people without their consent
  • Get involved in any illegal activities

Safety and Health

Safety in Anyer

While Anyer is generally considered a safe place, visitors should take the usual precautions such as keeping an eye on their belongings and not walking alone at night.

Health in Anyer

Visitors to Anyer should ensure they have all their necessary vaccinations up to date, including Hepatitis A and B. They should also avoid drinking tap water and purchasing food from street vendors.

Getting to Anyer: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Anyer

The nearest international airport to Anyer is Soekarno-Hatta International Airport, located in Jakarta. From there, visitors can take a taxi to Anyer.

Bus Stations Near Anyer

  • Terminal Pulo Gebang Jakarta
  • Terminal Kalideres Jakarta

Other Options

Visitors can also take a taxi or private car rental to reach Anyer from Jakarta.

How to Get Around in Anyer

The easiest way to get around Anyer is by taxi or motorbike, which can be rented locally. In addition, visitors can rent a car or bicycle to explore the town at their own pace.

Conclusion

Anyer is a hidden gem that offers visitors a delightful experience of Indonesian culture, history, and natural beauty. With a range of activities, accommodations, and attractions to choose from, it is a destination that should be added to every traveler’s Indonesia bucket list.
