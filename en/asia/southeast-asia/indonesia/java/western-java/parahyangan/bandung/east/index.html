Introduction to East

East is a region in the city of Bandung, located in Parahyangan, Western Java, Indonesia. It is a culturally significant and naturally beautiful area that attracts many visitors from around the world. The region is blessed with both historical and natural attractions that offer beautiful scenic views and outdoor adventures. In this travel guide, we will explore all the fascinating and must-see places in East Bandung, Indonesia.

List of Places to Visit in East

There are several places to visit in East, Bandung that will captivate your senses with their beauty and cultural significance. Here are some of the top attractions that you should add to your bucket list:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sri Baduga Museum - The museum has an extensive collection of Sundanese culture and history.
  • Geology Museum - The museum houses a diverse collection of minerals, rocks, and fossils from across Indonesia.
  • Gedung Merdeka - It is a historical building in the heart of Bandung that reflects the city’s colonial past.
  • Braga Street - The street is known for its modern cafes, antique shops, and boutiques that provide a glimpse of the city’s past.
  • Taman Film - It is a historical site that used to be a movie studio for Indonesia’s first-ever film production.

Natural Attractions of East: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Tangkuban Perahu Volcano - The volcano is located in the northern part of Bandung and offers breathtaking views of the crater and the surrounding areas.
  • Kawah Rengganis - It is a geothermal lake located in the Tangkuban Perahu area, famous for its natural hot springs.
  • Curug Cimahi Waterfall - This stunning waterfall is a favorite spot for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts who love to trek through the forest.
  • Sari Ater Hot Spring - The hot springs offer a beautiful and relaxing getaway for those looking to rejuvenate themselves in natural hot water.

Hiking/Biking Routes of East

East, Bandung offers several exciting trekking and biking paths that will take you through beautiful landscapes, valleys, and mountain trails while exploring the local culture and the surrounding nature. Here are some of the popular hikes and bike trails in the region:

  • Cikapundung River Trail - The river trail begins in Bandung’s city center and goes along a scenic route that offers a glimpse of urban life and its outskirts.
  • Bukit Moko - This hike leads you through stunning mountain valleys, lush green forests, and picturesque tea plantations.
  • Cikole Pine Forest Trail - Visitors can enjoy beautiful panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, blue skies, and fresh air while taking this scenic route.

Best Things to Do in East: Activities and Tours

Apart from the historical and natural attractions, East, Bandung also offers an array of activities and tours that you can enjoy. Here are some of the top activities and tours that you should not miss:

  • Sunda Kelapa Horse Riding - The horse riding tour takes you through charming and scenic villages, farms, and rice fields.
  • Sunda Flying Fox - Visitors can enjoy an adrenaline-pumping zip line ride through the scenic valley of Tangkuban Perahu.
  • Paragliding in Gunung Hawu - The activity offers spectacular views of the surrounding mountains and the beautiful Parahyangan landscape.

Week-Long Itinerary for East

A week-long itinerary for East can cover all the popular attractions and activities. Here is a sample itinerary that will help you explore the region to the fullest:

Day Activities
Day 1 Visit the Sri Baduga Museum, the Geology Museum, and Gedung Merdeka
Day 2 Visit Braga Street, Taman Film, and explore the city center
Day 3 Trek to Tangkuban Perahu Volcano and Kawah Rengganis
Day 4 Visit the Curug Cimahi Waterfall and explore the Sari Ater Hot Spring
Day 5 Take the Cikapundung River Trail and Bukit Moko hike
Day 6 Enjoy Sunda Kelapa Horse Riding and Sunda Flying Fox
Day 7 Go paragliding in Gunung Hawu and explore the local culture

Food and Drink Scene of East

East, Bandung, has a thriving food and drink scene that reflects the local culture and its diverse influences. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Nasi Goreng Kambing Kebon Jati - Fried rice with goat meat.
  • Sate Maranggi - The beef or lamb satay is a local specialty.
  • Batagor - Fried fish dumplings served with peanut sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Bandrek - A warm ginger drink with palm sugar and cinnamon.
  • Cendol - A dessert drink made with coconut milk, green rice flour jelly, and sugar.
  • Sindang Reret - A restaurant that serves traditional Sundanese cuisine.

Where to Stay in East: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

East, Bandung has a wide range of accommodations to suit different budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best options to consider:

  • Padma Hotel Bandung - A 5-star hotel that offers luxurious accommodation with stunning views of the valley.
  • Serela Waringin Hotel Bandung - A budget-friendly hotel that provides comfortable rooms with complimentary breakfast.
  • Sanghata Inn 2 - A guesthouse with affordable rates and comfortable rooms.

What to Buy in East: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

East, Bandung offers several unique souvenirs and local products that you can take back home as a memorable takeaway. Here are some of the popular items to consider:

  • Angklung - A traditional instrument made from bamboo.
  • Kain Batik - A cloth with unique patterns and colors that reflect the local culture.
  • Kacang Semarang - A local snack made with peanuts and sugar.

Local Culture and Customs of East

East, Bandung, is an area that takes great pride in its culture and customs. Visitors should respect the local customs and dress modestly when visiting religious sites and events.

Best Time to Visit East

The best time to visit East, Bandung, is between June to September when the weather is pleasant, and the skies are clear. However, visitors should expect crowds during the peak season, and prices can be higher.

Do’s and Don’ts of East

Do:

  • Learn some basic phrases in the local language.
  • Respect local customs and culture.
  • Try the local food and drinks.

Don’t:

  • Wear immodest clothing when visiting religious sites.
  • Disrespect the local culture and customs.
  • Litter and disrespect the natural beauty of the area.

Safety and Health

Safety in East

East, Bandung, is a relatively safe area for travelers. Visitors should practice common sense and take necessary precautions against pickpocketing and petty crimes.

Health in East

Visitors should check with their doctor before traveling to East, Bandung, and make sure they have the necessary vaccinations and medications to stay healthy.

Getting to East: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near East

The nearest airport to East, Bandung is Husein Sastranegara International Airport, located just outside the city.

Bus Stations Near East

There are several bus stations in Bandung that provide transportation to East, including Leuwi Panjang, Cicaheum, and Cicaheum Damri.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or hire a taxi to explore East, Bandung on their own.

How to Get Around in East

The best way to get around East, Bandung, is to rent a car or hire a taxi. Visitors can also use public transportation, including minibusses and angkots, to explore the area.

Where to Go Next from East: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

East, Bandung, is located in a region with several other nearby destinations and excursions. Some of the popular options include:

  • North Bandung - A region with several natural attractions and historic landmarks, including Tangkuban Perahu and Kawah Putih.
  • Garut - A district famous for its traditional crafts, delicious snacks, and nature attractions.
  • Lembang Street Market - A bustling market that offers a wide range of local products, including fresh fruits, vegetables, and snacks.
  • Ciwidey - A town famous for its coffee plantations, tea plantations, and natural hot springs.

Conclusion

East, Bandung, is a beautiful and fascinating area that offers a perfect combination of history, culture, and natural beauty. From stunning natural attractions to culturally significant museums and delicious local food, East, Bandung has something for everyone. We hope that this comprehensive tour guide will help you plan your visit to East, Bandung, and make it a memorable one.
