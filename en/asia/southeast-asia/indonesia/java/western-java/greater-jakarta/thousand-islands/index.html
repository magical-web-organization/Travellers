Introduction to Thousand Islands

Thousand Islands is a cluster of 105 islands located in the Java Sea, 45 km north of Jakarta, the capital city of Indonesia. The islands offer an escape from the bustling city life and an opportunity to explore the natural beauty and cultural significance of Indonesia. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take you on a journey to discover the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more in Thousand Islands.

Thousand Islands is home to an incredible array of natural wonders and outdoor activities. The stunning coral reefs and marine life attract divers and snorkelers from around the world. Best Time to Visit Thousand Islands

The best time to visit Thousand Islands is during the dry season, which runs from May to September. During this time, the weather is sunny and dry, making it perfect for outdoor activities and water sports.

Do’s and Don’ts of Thousand Islands

Do:

  2. Carry cash as credit cards are not widely accepted
  3. Try the local cuisine

Don’t:

  1. Swim in unauthorized areas
  2. Litter or damage the environment
  3. Disrespect local customs and traditions

Safety and Health

Safety in Thousand Islands

Thousand Islands is a safe destination for travelers, but it’s always important to take precautions to ensure your safety. Here are some safety tips:

  1. Avoid walking alone at night
  2. Keep valuable possessions out of sight
  3. Stay aware of your surroundings

Health in Thousand Islands

Thousand Islands has some health hazards that travelers should be aware of, such as malaria and dengue fever. It’s important to take precautions, such as using mosquito repellant and wearing long-sleeved clothing.

How to Get Around in Thousand Islands

The best way to get around Thousand Islands is by boat. Ferries and speedboats operate regularly between the islands, and private boat hire is also available.
