Introduction to Sukabumi

Sukabumi is a regency in the province of West Java, Indonesia. Located near the Bogor Raya highlands, this city is a paradise for nature enthusiasts. Sukabumi is surrounded by beautiful mountains, sweeping beaches, and dense forests. The regency is also the birthplace of former Indonesian president Soekarno, and numerous historical landmarks are dedicated to his legacy. This guide will take you on a journey to explore the best places to visit, things to do, local culture, and several other essential aspects of Sukabumi.

Food and Drink Scene of Sukabumi

Sukabumi’s culinary scene is a blend of traditional and modern Indonesian cuisine that satiates your taste buds. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages in Sukabumi:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Nasi Timbel (steamed rice wrapped in banana leaves)
  • Tahu Gejrot (fried tofu with a sweet and sour sauce)
  • Rawon (beef soup with black sauce)
  • Ayam Goreng Kalasan (fried chicken with a sweet and spicy sauce)

Local Beverages

  • Es Degan (iced young coconut juice)
  • Teh Poci (Indonesian herbal tea)
  • Wedang Jahe (ginger tea)

Local Culture and Customs of Sukabumi

Sukabumi’s culture is a blend of Sundanese and Javanese traditions. The local people are warm and friendly, and being respectful towards them and their culture is a must.

Best Time to Visit Sukabumi

The best time to visit Sukabumi is between July and October when the weather is dry and pleasant. The period from November to April has more rainfall, and it may affect your outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Sukabumi

Do:

  • Dress modestly while visiting temples or other religious sites
  • Respect the local culture and traditions
  • Carry enough cash while exploring the city as some establishments do not accept credit cards

Don’t:

  • Smoke in public places
  • Be disrespectful towards the local culture and customs
  • Litter the natural attractions and scenic locations

Safety and Health

Safety in Sukabumi

Sukabumi is generally safe for tourists, but it is recommended to exercise caution while exploring the city. Be aware of your surroundings and avoid carrying too much cash or valuables. It is also recommended to have proper travel insurance before embarking on your trip.

Health in Sukabumi

It is advised to stay hydrated throughout the trip and avoid tap water. Consult a doctor before visiting Sukabumi and carry necessary medicines, especially if you have medical conditions.

How to Get Around Sukabumi

Getting around Sukabumi is easy with various transportation options, including motorcycle taxi, bus, and car rental. However, it is recommended to hire a private car with a driver for your tours, as most tourist places are located far from the city center.
