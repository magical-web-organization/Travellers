Introduction to Soccsksargen

Soccsksargen is a region in Mindanao, Philippines, known for its diverse culture and stunning natural scenery. Its name is an acronym derived from its constituent provinces and cities - South Cotabato, Cotabato City, Sultan Kudarat, Sarangani, and General Santos City.

In this comprehensive guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit, must-see attractions, hiking trails, food and drink scene, where to stay, and more in Soccsksargen.

List of Places to Visit in Soccsksargen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

KCC Mall de Zamboanga

The KCC Mall de Zamboanga is one of the largest malls in Mindanao, offering a wide range of shops and restaurants. Its unique architecture, inspired by the Torogan houses of the Maranao people, makes it a must-visit attraction.

Sultan Kudarat Provincial Capitol

The Sultan Kudarat Provincial Capitol is a significant landmark in Soccsksargen, located in the capital town of Isulan. The building has two floors and a spacious balcony that provides a panoramic view of the town.

Natural Attractions of Soccsksargen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lake Sebu

Lake Sebu is a natural lake located in South Cotabato, known for its clear waters and stunning views. Visitors can go boating, zip-lining, and trekking around the lake, or learn about the local culture and traditions of the T’bolis who live in the area.

Mount Apo

Mount Apo is the highest mountain in the Philippines, rising 2,954 meters above sea level. It’s located on the border of Davao City and North Cotabato, and offers a wide range of hiking trails for both beginners and experienced hikers.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Soccsksargen

Soccsksargen has plenty of hiking and biking trails for outdoor enthusiasts. Here are some of the best ones:

Mount Matutum Trek

The Mount Matutum Trek is a challenging hike to the summit of Mount Matutum, located in South Cotabato. The hike takes approximately eight hours and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Sultan Peak Traverse

The Sultan Peak Traverse is a two-day hike that takes visitors from the picturesque Lake Holon to the peak of Sultan Peak. It’s a challenging hike, but the views at the top are well worth the effort.

Best Things to Do in Soccsksargen: Activities and Tours

Koronadal City Tour

Koronadal City is the capital of South Cotabato and offers an excellent introduction to the local culture and traditions of Soccsksargen. Visitors can explore the city’s landmarks, museums, and restaurants on a guided tour.

T’boli Cultural Tour

The T’boli Cultural Tour takes visitors to Lake Sebu, where they can learn about the T’boli tribe’s way of life and traditions. Visitors can see traditional weaving, try local delicacies, and learn about the tribe’s intricate and colorful beadwork.

Week-Long Itinerary for Soccsksargen

Here’s a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to Soccsksargen:

Day 1: Arrive in General Santos City and explore the city’s landmarks.

Day 2: Visit Lake Sebu and go boating, zip-lining, and trekking around the lake.

Day 3: Go on a Mount Matutum Trek.

Day 4: Take a Koronadal City tour and learn about the local culture and traditions.

Day 5: Embark on a T’boli Cultural Tour and experience the tribe’s way of life.

Day 6: Visit Mount Apo and go on a hiking trail.

Day 7: End the trip with some shopping and souvenir hunting at KCC Mall de Zamboanga.

Food and Drink Scene of Soccsksargen

Must-Try Dishes

Pinikpikan

Pinikpikan is a dish made with chicken that has been beaten before cooking. It’s a traditional dish of the Kalinga tribe, and the meat is said to be more tender and flavorful because of the process.

Satti

Satti is a popular dish in Mindanao, made with grilled meat skewers served with a spicy peanut sauce and a side of rice.

Local Beverages

Tubâ

Tubâ is a fermented drink made from coconut water. It’s popular throughout the Philippines and is a great way to cool off after a day of outdoor activities in Soccsksargen.

Where to Stay in Soccsksargen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Soccsksargen has plenty of accommodation options for travelers on any budget. Here are some of the best:

Greenleaf Hotel

The Greenleaf Hotel is a luxurious hotel in General Santos City, offering spacious rooms, an outdoor pool, and a fitness center.

Microtel Inn and Suites

The Microtel Inn and Suites is an affordable and comfortable option, located in the heart of General Santos City.

What to Buy in Soccsksargen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

T’nalak Cloth

T’nalak Cloth is a traditional cloth made by the T’boli tribe. It’s made from abaca fiber and is known for its intricate patterns and bright hues.

Heirloom Jewelry

The T’boli tribe is skilled in beadwork and jewelry-making, and visitors can find unique and exquisite pieces to bring back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Soccsksargen

Soccsksargen is home to diverse ethnic groups, each with its unique culture and traditions. Visitors are expected to respect these customs and traditions, such as dressing modestly and refraining from taking photos without permission.

Best Time to Visit Soccsksargen

The best time to visit Soccsksargen is from December to May when the weather is dry and pleasant, ideal for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Soccsksargen

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Take part in cultural tours and experiences.
  • Try local delicacies and buy unique souvenirs.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Take photos without permission.
  • Litter or disturb the natural environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Soccsksargen

While Soccsksargen may be considered a safe region, visitors should take the usual precautions such as not carrying too much cash and avoiding dark and secluded areas at night.

Health in Soccsksargen

Visitors should take the necessary precautions against tropical illnesses such as dengue fever and malaria by applying mosquito repellent and wearing protective clothing.

Getting to Soccsksargen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Soccsksargen

The General Santos International Airport is the region’s primary airport, offering flights to major cities in the Philippines.

Bus Stations Near Soccsksargen

Several bus companies offer regular trips to Soccsksargen from Manila and other neighboring cities.

Other Options

Visitors can also opt to rent a car or hire a private car service to explore Soccsksargen at their own pace.

How to Get Around in Soccsksargen

Jeepneys, buses, and tricycles are the primary means of public transportation within Soccsksargen. Visitors can also opt to rent a car or a motorcycle to explore the region.

Where to Go Next from Soccsksargen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

From Soccsksargen, visitors can easily travel to nearby destinations such as Davao City, Surigao del Sur, and Agusan del Norte, among others.

Conclusion

Soccsksargen is a fascinating region in Mindanao, offering diverse cultural experiences and breathtaking natural landscapes. From hiking to cultural tours, there’s something for everyone in Soccsksargen.
