Introduction to Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park is a protected area located in Northern Mindanao, Philippines. It is one of the country’s most important biodiversity hotspots, being home to unique flora and fauna, including several endemic species. The park covers over 47,000 hectares of land and is named after Mount Kitanglad, which is the fourth highest peak in the Philippines.

Visitors to Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park can expect to discover a range of outdoor adventures, from hiking and birdwatching to camping and cycling. The park also boasts culturally significant attractions and a vibrant local culture worth exploring.

List of Places to Visit in Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Manolo Fortich Heritage Museum - Located in Barangay Poblacion, Manolo Fortich, this museum houses a collection of artifacts and memorabilia that showcase the town’s history and culture.
  • Sumalsag Cave - This cave holds great archaeological significance as it is believed to have been inhabited by early human civilizations thousands of years ago.
  • Kaamulan Park - Located in Malaybalay City, this park hosts the annual Kaamulan Festival, which celebrates the customs and traditions of the indigenous peoples of Bukidnon.

Natural Attractions of Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Kitanglad - At 2,899 meters, Mount Kitanglad is the park’s highest peak and offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape. Hiking to the summit is a popular activity.
  • Agusan Marsh Wildlife Sanctuary - Located in the lower portions of the park, this sanctuary boasts a diverse range of flora and fauna, including several rare and endangered species, such as the Philippine crocodile.
  • Pulangi River - This scenic river runs through the heart of the park and is a popular spot for rafting and kayaking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

There are several hiking and biking routes in Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park, each offering a unique experience.

  • Mount Dulang-Dulang Traverse - This hike is considered one of the most challenging in the Philippines but offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.
  • Sumalsag Cave and Gantungan Falls Trail - This relatively easy hike takes you to Sumalsag Cave and Gantungan Falls, two of the park’s most picturesque spots.
  • Manan-aw Trail - This trail takes you through a dense forest and leads to a viewpoint with stunning panoramic views of the park.

Best Things to Do in Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park: Activities and Tours

  • Birdwatching - The park is home to over 250 bird species, including several endemic species. The best time for birdwatching is early morning and late afternoon.
  • Camping - Pitch a tent and spend a night or two in the park under the stars.
  • Mountain Biking - Take a guided tour or rent a bike and explore the park’s trails on two wheels.

Week-Long Itinerary for Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

Day 1: Arrival and check-in at your accommodation. Day 2: Hike to the summit of Mount Kitanglad. Day 3: Spend the day exploring the park’s waterfalls. Day 4: Take a guided tour of the park’s indigenous communities. Day 5: Go birdwatching in the morning and mountain biking in the afternoon. Day 6: Visit the Manolo Fortich Heritage Museum and nearby cultural attractions. Day 7: Departure.

Food and Drink Scene of Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sinina - A traditional dish made with grated cassava and coconut milk.
  • Kilawin - A dish made with raw fish, vinegar, spices, and coconut milk.
  • Pinikpikan - A dish made with chicken that has been beaten to death to tenderize the meat.

Local Beverages

  • Barako Coffee - A strong coffee made with Liberica beans, which are only grown in the Philippines.
  • Lambanog - A distilled spirit made from the sap of coconut trees.

Where to Stay in Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Mount Kitanglad Agro-Eco Farm and Forest Resort - A nature retreat that offers comfortable cottages and a range of outdoor activities, including birdwatching, hiking, and mountain biking.
  • Dahilayan Forest Park - A family-friendly resort that offers various attractions, including a zipline, giant swing, and a treetop adventure park.
  • Pinoy Backpacker’s Place - A budget-friendly hostel that offers comfortable dorm rooms and a relaxed, friendly atmosphere.

What to Buy in Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Hand-woven baskets - Made by indigenous communities in the park.
  • Local coffee - Buy a bag of Barako Coffee to take home.
  • Hand-carved wooden objects - Made by local artisans using sustainable materials.

Local Culture and Customs of Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

The park is home to several indigenous communities, including the Bukidnon, Talaandig, and Higaonon. Visitors should always show respect to the local culture and customs, including asking for permission before taking photographs and avoiding any behavior that may be considered disrespectful.

Best Time to Visit Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

The best time to visit the park is between December and May when the weather is drier and cooler. However, the park is open year-round, and visitors can expect to enjoy outdoor activities even during the rainy season.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Follow the park’s rules and regulations.
  • Dress appropriately when visiting cultural sites and villages.

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave trash behind.
  • Disturb the park’s flora and fauna.
  • Wander off the designated trails.

Safety and Health

Safety in Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

Visitors should be aware that the park’s trails can be challenging and require a certain level of fitness. It is always best to hike with a guide, especially if you are inexperienced. Visitors should also be cautious when swimming in rivers and waterfalls, as the currents can be strong. It is also a good idea to check weather conditions before embarking on any outdoor activities.

Health in Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

Visitors should always bring a sufficient supply of insect repellent, sunscreen, and any necessary medications. It is also advisable to bring a first aid kit with basic supplies such as band-aids and antiseptic.

Getting to Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

  • Laguindingan Airport - Located in Cagayan de Oro City, approximately 80 kilometers from the park.

Bus Stations Near Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

  • Cagayan de Oro City - Buses to Malaybalay City and Manolo Fortich depart regularly from Cagayan de Oro City.
  • Davao City - Buses to Malaybalay City and Manolo Fortich depart regularly from Davao City.

Other Options

Visitors can also drive to the park, and car rentals are available in nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park

The park is best explored on foot or by bike, and visitors can hire a guide or rent bikes from several local tour operators.

Where to Go Next from Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Cagayan de Oro City - The Gateway to Northern Mindanao, Cagayan de Oro City is a bustling city that boasts several attractions, including the whitewater rapids of the Cagayan de Oro River and the Macahambus Cave.
  • Camiguin Island - Also known as the “Island Born of Fire,” Camiguin is a volcanic island that boasts several natural attractions, including waterfalls, hot springs, and marine sanctuaries.
  • Siargao Island - Known for its world-famous surf breaks, Siargao Island is a popular destination for adventure-seekers and beach lovers.

Conclusion

Mount Kitanglad Range Natural Park is a hidden gem in the Philippines that is worth exploring. With its stunning natural beauty, vibrant local culture, and range of outdoor activities, it has everything you need for a memorable travel experience.
