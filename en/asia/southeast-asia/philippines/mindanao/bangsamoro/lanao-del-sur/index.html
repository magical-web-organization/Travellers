Introduction to Lanao del Sur

Lanao del Sur is a province located in the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Philippines. It is bordered by Lanao del Norte to the north, Bukidnon to the east, and Maguindanao to the south. The province is known for its rich cultural heritage, stunning natural scenery, and warm hospitality of its people.

List of Places to Visit in Lanao del Sur

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Ranao Museum

Ranao Museum is a cultural institution in Marawi City that showcases the traditional arts and crafts of the Maranao people. It offers a unique opportunity to see the intricate designs of native textiles, brassware, wood carvings, and musical instruments.

Mindanao State University Campus

The Mindanao State University (MSU) Campus is a sprawling educational institution that contains several important historical landmarks. These include the Masjid Dimaukom, one of the most beautiful mosques in the country, and the MSU Museum, which houses precious artifacts from the pre-colonial era.

Natural Attractions of Lanao del Sur: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Lake Lanao

Lake Lanao is the largest lake in Mindanao and the second-largest in the Philippines. It is a great destination for water sports enthusiasts, offering activities such as canoeing, kayaking, and fishing. The lake is also home to several species of migratory birds, making it an ideal spot for birdwatching.

Marawi City View Deck

The Marawi City View Deck provides a panoramic view of the city and the lake. It is a perfect spot to watch the sunrise or sunset and to appreciate the beauty of nature.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lanao del Sur

Mindanao State University (MSU) Eco-Park

The MSU Eco-Park is a nature reserve inside the university campus that offers several hiking trails for all levels of fitness. Visitors can enjoy the scenic views of Lake Lanao and the surrounding mountains while taking a leisurely walk or a challenging trek.

Panoramic Heights

Panoramic Heights is a popular destination for mountain biking enthusiasts. The hill offers a panoramic view of Marawi City and the lake. The road to Panoramic Heights is steep and challenging, making it an exciting adventure for experienced bikers.

Best Things to Do in Lanao del Sur: Activities and Tours

Marawi City Heritage Tour

The Marawi City Heritage Tour is a guided tour that takes visitors to the most important cultural and historical sites in the city. The tour includes visits to the Ranao Museum, the Mindanao State University Campus, and the Masjid Dimaukom.

Lake Lanao Adventure

The Lake Lanao Adventure is a unique tour that takes visitors on a boat ride around the lake. The tour includes a visit to the floating village of the Badjao people and a stopover at the historic Fort Pilar.

Food and Drink Scene of Lanao del Sur

Must-Try Dishes

Piaparan

Piaparan is a traditional Maranao dish made of either chicken or beef. It is cooked slowly in a mixture of spices and coconut milk until it is tender and flavorful.

Beef Rendang

Beef Rendang is another dish that originated from the Maranao people. It is a slow-cooked dish made with beef, coconut milk, and a variety of spices and herbs.

Local Beverages

Kapeng Barako

Kapeng Barako is a strong coffee that is popular in the region. It is made with locally grown coffee beans that are roasted to perfection, making it a favorite among coffee lovers.

Where to Stay in Lanao del Sur: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Marawi Resort Hotel

Marawi Resort Hotel is a 3-star hotel located in the heart of Marawi City. It offers comfortable rooms and a wide range of amenities such as an outdoor swimming pool, a restaurant, and a spa.

MSU Guesthouse

The MSU Guesthouse is a budget-friendly accommodation located inside the university campus. It offers basic amenities such as air-conditioned rooms and free Wi-Fi.

What to Buy in Lanao del Sur: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Maranao Brassware

Maranao Brassware is known for its intricate designs and unique patterns. Visitors can purchase brassware items such as gongs, kulintang, and brass vases.

Local Culture and Customs of Lanao del Sur

The Maranao people are known for their unique customs and traditions. They have a rich culture that centers on their ancient beliefs and practices such as the kulintang, a set of gongs that is used in both spiritual and social events.

Best Time to Visit Lanao del Sur

The best time to visit Lanao del Sur is during the dry season, which runs from December to May. The weather is sunny and pleasant during these months, making it ideal for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lanao del Sur

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions of the Maranao people.
  • Dress modestly when visiting historical and cultural sites.
  • Ask for permission before taking photos of people.

Don’t:

  • Engage in activities that may be considered disrespectful or offensive.
  • Carry or display excessive amounts of cash or valuables.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lanao del Sur

Lanao del Sur has been known to have security concerns in the past. Visitors are advised to stay updated on current events and to avoid traveling to areas with travel advisories. Traveling in groups and using reputable tour operators are recommended.

Health in Lanao del Sur

Visitors are advised to bring mosquito repellent and to take precautionary measures against waterborne illnesses. It is also important to have a travel insurance policy that covers medical emergencies.

Getting to Lanao del Sur: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lanao del Sur

The nearest airport to Lanao del Sur is the Laguindingan Airport in Cagayan de Oro City. From there, visitors can take a taxi or a van to Marawi City.

Bus Stations Near Lanao del Sur

Several bus companies operate daily trips between Cagayan de Oro City and Marawi City, with travel time taking about 3 hours.

Other Options

Taking a private car or hiring a van is another option for getting to Lanao del Sur. However, visitors are advised to check with their tour operator or hotel for reliable and safe transportation options.

How to Get Around in Lanao del Sur

Jeepneys and buses are the most common modes of transportation in Lanao del Sur. Tricycles and motorcycles are also available for short distances or for exploring local areas.

Where to Go Next from Lanao del Sur: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Iligan City

Iligan City is located 32 kilometers north of Marawi City. It is known as the City of Majestic Waterfalls and is home to several stunning waterfalls such as the Maria Cristina Falls and Tinago Falls.

Bukidnon Province

Bukidnon Province is located east of Lanao del Sur. It is a mountainous province known for its cool climate, scenic views, and cultural events.

Conclusion

Lanao del Sur is a province that offers visitors a unique opportunity to experience the rich culture and stunning natural scenery of the Bangsamoro Autonomous Region in Muslim Mindanao, Philippines. With its rich history, unique cuisine, beautiful landscapes, and warm hospitality, Lanao del Sur is definitely a destination worth exploring.
