Introduction to Surigao

Surigao is a city located in the Caraga region of Mindanao in the Philippines. It is known for its beautiful beaches, picturesque islands, and lush mountains. Surigao is a popular destination for travelers who are looking to experience the natural beauty of the Philippines.

List of Places to Visit in Surigao

Surigao has a variety of cultural and natural attractions that are worth exploring. Below are some of the must-see places in the city.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Surigao del Norte Provincial Museum
  • Surigao Cathedral
  • Mabua Pebble Beach
  • Hikdop Island

Natural Attractions of Surigao: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Sohoton Cove
  • Siargao Island
  • Laswitan Lagoon
  • Britania Islands

Hiking/Biking Routes of Surigao

Surigao also offers hiking and biking routes that are perfect for adventure enthusiasts. Below are some of the recommended routes in the city.

  • Mt. Bagarabon Peak
  • Mt. Mabua Trekking
  • Hinatuan Enchanted River Bike Tour

Best Things to Do in Surigao: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are many other activities and tours that are worth experiencing in Surigao. Below are some of the best things to do in the city.

  • Island hopping
  • Wakeboarding
  • Surfing
  • River tubing

Week-Long Itinerary for Surigao

Here is a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to Surigao.

Day Activity
1 Visit the Surigao del Norte Provincial Museum and go to the Mabua Pebble Beach
2 Explore Sohoton Cove and witness the beautiful lagoon and caves
3 Head to Siargao Island for some surfing and relaxed island vibes
4 Visit the stunning Laswitan Lagoon and see the cascading waterfalls
5 Go on a river tubing adventure in Hinatuan Enchanted River
6 Discover the Britania Islands and see the beautiful rock formations
7 Take a day off to relax and unwind in Surigao’s beaches

Local Culture and Customs of Surigao

Surigao has a rich history and a unique cultural identity. The locals are known for their hospitality and welcoming nature. Visitors are advised to respect the local culture and customs.

Best Time to Visit Surigao

The best time to visit Surigao is between December to February, when the weather is dry and the temperatures are cooler. March to May is also a good time to visit, but the temperatures can be high and humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Surigao

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Wear appropriate clothing when visiting religious or historical sites
  • Try the local delicacies and dishes

Don’t:

  • Litter or pollute the natural environments
  • Disrespect the locals or their culture
  • Wear revealing clothing in public areas

Safety and Health

Safety in Surigao

Surigao is generally safe for tourists, but like any other city, there are certain areas that may be unsafe. Travelers should take precautions when visiting unfamiliar areas and avoid traveling alone at night.

Health in Surigao

It is important to drink only bottled water and to avoid eating street food. Travelers should also make sure their vaccinations are up-to-date before visiting Surigao.

Getting to Surigao: Transportation Options and Logistics

Surigao can be reached through several transportation options including flights, buses, and ferries.

Airports Near Surigao

  • Surigao Airport (airport code: SUG)

Bus Stations Near Surigao

  • Surigao City Integrated Bus Terminal
  • Butuan Integrated Bus Terminal

Other Options

  • Ferries are available from Manila and Cebu to Surigao port.

How to Get Around in Surigao

The most convenient way to get around Surigao is by hiring a tricycle or motorcycle taxi (habal-habal) to explore the city’s attractions.

Where to Go Next from Surigao: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Travelers who want to explore nearby destinations can consider the following excursions.

  • Dinagat Islands
  • Camiguin Island
  • Cagayan De Oro City

Conclusion

Surigao is a city full of unique cultural and natural attractions. With its beautiful beaches, scenic views, and adventure activities, Surigao can offer an unforgettable and authentic travel experience.
