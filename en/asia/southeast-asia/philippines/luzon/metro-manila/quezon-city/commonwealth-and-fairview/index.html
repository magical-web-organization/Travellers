Introduction to Commonwealth and Fairview

Commonwealth and Fairview are two neighboring communities located in Quezon City, within the Metro Manila region of the Philippines. Commonwealth is known for its wide roads and significant thoroughfares, while Fairview is known for its vibrant local marketplaces. Both areas have plenty of attractions and activities for visitors to experience, making them popular destinations for tourists.

List of Places to Visit in Commonwealth and Fairview

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you are interested in exploring the rich history of the Philippines, Commonwealth and Fairview offer several interesting museums and historical places to visit. First on that list is the Quezon Memorial Circle, a popular landmark and park that honors the legacy of Manuel Quezon, the first president of the Philippine Commonwealth. Visitors can explore the museum dedicated to Quezon and learn more about his life and contributions to the country.

Other notable attractions include the Balara Filters Park, which was recognized as a National Historical Shrine by the National Historical Commission of the Philippines. The park features a filtration system that served as a source of clean water for over 70 years. The La Mesa Eco Park is another natural attraction to consider, where visitors can enjoy the lush environment and engage in fun outdoor activities.

Natural Attractions of Commonwealth and Fairview: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Surrounded by beautiful mountains and valleys, Commonwealth and Fairview also provide many opportunities for outdoor adventure and exploration for visitors of all ages. The La Mesa Watershed Reserve offers exciting activities such as zip-lining, trekking, kayaking, and cycling. Additionally, nature lovers can also visit the Botanical Gardens and the Eco Trail to explore the diversity of flora and fauna in the area. For those who love scenic views, the Quezon City Circle Observatory is a must-visit location, which offers a panoramic view of the surrounding areas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Commonwealth and Fairview

Commonwealth and Fairview also have several options for visitors who love to hike and bike. One notable outdoor destination is the Timog-La Mesa Forest Reserve, which features several trails for different skill levels. Visitors can also explore the Quezon City Park and Wildlife, which offers various outdoor experiences such as birdwatching, camping, and hiking.

Best Things to Do in Commonwealth and Fairview: Activities and Tours

In addition to outdoor activities, Commonwealth and Fairview have several other things to do, which cater to different tastes and interests. One of the top-rated activities is to take a walking tour of the Quezon Memorial Circle. Visitors can explore the vast space, learn more about the history of the Philippine Commonwealth and enjoy various local events.

Other exciting things to do include visiting the nearby shopping malls, which cater to different budgets and preferences. For example, the Fairview Terraces is a modern shopping destination featuring various shops, restaurants, and leisure activities. The Robinsons Place Novaliches is another shopping hub, featuring unique local and international brands and a wide array of dining options.

Week-Long Itinerary for Commonwealth and Fairview

Suppose you’re planning to spend a week in Commonwealth and Fairview. In that case, you can experience various activities and destinations in both areas. Here’s a sample itinerary to consider:

  • Day 1: Visit the Quezon Memorial Circle and explore the grounds and museums.
  • Day 2: Experience the La Mesa Watershed Reserve and enjoy outdoor activities such as zip-lining and cycling.
  • Day 3: Take a walking tour of the city and visit some of the famous landmarks and attractions.
  • Day 4: Explore the natural attractions and scenic views at the Botanical Gardens and Eco Trail.
  • Day 5: Shop and dine at the Fairview Terraces.
  • Day 6: Visit the Balara Filters Park and learn about the fascinating history of the filtration system.
  • Day 7: Enjoy a day trip to nearby destinations such as Antipolo or Rizal Province.

Food and Drink Scene of Commonwealth and Fairview

Must-Try Dishes

The food scene in Commonwealth and Fairview caters to different tastes, with local and international dishes to choose from. For local cuisine, one must-try dish is the lechon kawali, crispy fried pork belly that locals serve with rice and vinegar. The neighborhood also offers unique snacks such as kakanin, a range of rice-based cakes and pastries, and buko juice, a refreshing drink made from coconut water.

Local Beverages

In addition to the delicious food, Commonwealth and Fairview also have local beverages to try, such as the refreshing sago’t gulaman, a subtle flavoured drink made from brown sugar, tapioca pearls, and gelatin.

Local Culture and Customs of Commonwealth and Fairview

Before visiting Commonwealth and Fairview, it’s essential to know a bit about the local culture and customs. Best Time to Visit Commonwealth and Fairview

The best time to visit Commonwealth and Fairview is from November to April, which offers pleasant weather. The region’s rainy season is from May to October, making outdoor activities challenging during this time.
