Food and Drink Scene of Triangle and Scout Areas

The Triangle and Scout Areas have a rich food and drink scene, and there are plenty of local restaurants and street vendors offering a wide range of delicacies to try. Here are a few must-try dishes and local beverages to sample when you’re in the area:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Adobong Pusit (Squid Adobo)
  • Bistek Tagalog (Filipino Beef Steak)
  • Kare-Kare (peanut stew with oxtail, tripe and veggies)

Local Beverages

  • Sago’t Gulaman (a refreshing sweet drink made with brown sugar, water, and sago pearls)
  • Calamansi Juice (a citrus drink made with Calamansi, a small Filipino fruit that is similar to a lime)
