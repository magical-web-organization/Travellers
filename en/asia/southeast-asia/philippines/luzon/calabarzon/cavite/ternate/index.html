Introduction to Ternate

Ternate is a beautiful coastal town located in the province of Cavite, in the southern part of Luzon island, Philippines. The town is known for its stunning beaches, rich culture, and historical significance, making it a popular destination for both local and foreign tourists.

List of Places to Visit in Ternate

If you’re planning a trip to Ternate, here are some of the must-visit places for your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Ternate has a rich history that you can witness through its numerous historical sites and museums. Here are some of the top cultural attractions:

  • Ternate Beach Park. This park is located at the end of Ternate Boulevard and offers stunning views of the sea and the mountains.
  • Cavite Naval Base Historical Museum. This museum showcases the history and heritage of the Philippine Navy through its exhibits.
  • Agustinian Monastery Museum. Situated atop a hill, this building offers a breathtaking view of the whole town of Ternate. It also houses a small museum that exhibits antique artifacts.

Natural Attractions of Ternate: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Ternate is blessed with unspoiled natural wonders that you can explore for adventure or leisure. Here are some of the top natural attractions:

  • Mt. Palay-palay/Mataas na Gulod Protected Landscape. This protected area is perfect for hiking, camping, and bird watching. It has a diverse ecosystem that includes rainforests, waterfalls, rivers, and caves.
  • Ternate Marine Base. This secluded beach offers crystal-clear waters, coral reefs, and a picturesque scenery. You can also rent boats and kayaks here.
  • Balanghai Shrine. This ancient tree is believed to be more than 600 years old and is regarded as the “mother of all boats” by the locals.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ternate

Ternate is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, with plenty of trails for hiking and biking. Here are some of the top routes:

  • Mt. Palay-palay Traverse. This challenging trail covers a distance of 15 kilometers and takes around 7-8 hours to complete. The hike rewards you with stunning views of Cavite and Batangas.
  • Nasugbu-Ternate Loop. This loop covers a distance of 50 kilometers and takes around 5-6 hours to complete. The trail is ideal for intermediate to advanced level cyclists.

Best Things to Do in Ternate: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and sightseeing, Ternate also offers a variety of unique activities and tours. Here are some of the best things to do:

  • Island Hopping. Explore the nearby islands of Cavite and witness their unique beauty and culture.
  • Camping. Spend a night under the stars at Mt. Palay-palay/Mataas na Gulod Protected Landscape and immerse yourself in nature.
  • Snorkeling and Diving. Witness the vibrant and diverse marine life of Ternate’s waters through snorkeling or diving activities.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ternate

If you’re planning to spend a week in Ternate, here’s a sample itinerary:

Day Activity
Day 1 Explore Ternate Beach Park and stroll along the boulevard.
Day 2 Visit the Cavite Naval Base Historical Museum and Agustinian Monastery Museum.
Day 3 Go island hopping to Bataan and Corregidor.
Day 4 Hike to the peak of Mt. Palay-palay and camp overnight.
Day 5 Snorkel or dive at Ternate Marine Base.

Food and Drink Scene of Ternate

Ternate offers a diverse range of cuisine and local delicacies that you must try. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Adobong Pugo. This dish is made of quail meat cooked in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and bay leaves.
  • Lumpiang Ternate. This dish is similar to a spring roll, made up of vegetables, pork, and ground shrimp.
  • Labong Tamales. This dish is made of bamboo shoots mixed with rice flour, chili, and meat, wrapped in banana leaves and steamed.

Local Beverages

  • Barako Coffee. This is a strong coffee that originated from Batangas but is also readily available in Ternate.
  • Buko Juice. Fresh coconut juice is widely available in Ternate.

Where to Stay in Ternate: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ternate offers plenty of accommodation options ranging from budget-friendly to high-end hotels. Here are some of the top picks:

  • Bahay Ni Tuding Guesthouse. This guesthouse offers cozy rooms, a peaceful ambiance, and a delicious breakfast.
  • Canyon Cove Hotel and Spa. This hotel is located near the beach and features an outdoor pool, spa, and restaurant.
  • Microtel Inn and Suites. This budget-friendly hotel features clean and comfortable rooms, free Wi-Fi, and a restaurant.

What to Buy in Ternate: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Ternate offers various souvenirs and local products that you can take back home as a reminder of your trip. Here are some of the must-buy items:

  • Barako Coffee. This coffee has a strong flavor and comes in various blends.
  • Ternate Tablea. This chocolate is made from roasted cacao beans and comes in various shapes and sizes.
  • Cavite Adobo. This cooking sauce is made of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices and is the perfect souvenir for foodies.

Local Culture and Customs of Ternate

Ternate has a rich culture and traditions that are worth learning and experiencing. Here are some of the local customs:

  • Pagpapasalamat sa Mahal na Araw. This is a religious activity held every Holy Week where locals reenact the passion, death, and resurrection of Jesus Christ.
  • Santacruzan. This is a religious festival held every May where locals parade in costumes to commemorate the finding of the Holy Cross.
  • Karakol. This is a dance ritual held in honor of the town’s patron saint, St. Joseph the Worker.

Best Time to Visit Ternate

The best time to visit Ternate is from December to June when the weather is dry and sunny, making it ideal for outdoor activities. Avoid visiting during the typhoon season from July to November.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ternate

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Dress appropriately when visiting religious sites and cultural attractions.
  • Sample local cuisine and delicacies.

Don’t:

  • Litter in public places and beaches.
  • Take pictures without asking for permission.
  • Disrespect local beliefs and practices.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ternate

Ternate is generally safe for tourists, but it’s always advisable to take extra precautions such as:

  • Avoid wearing expensive jewelry and carrying too much cash.
  • Keep your valuables safe and secure.
  • Always be aware of your surroundings.

Health in Ternate

Make sure to bring your own medication and insect repellant, and to take precautions against mosquito-born diseases like dengue fever.

Getting to Ternate: Transportation Options and Logistics

Ternate is easily accessible from Manila and nearby provinces. Here are some of the transportation options:

Airports Near Ternate

  • Ninoy Aquino International Airport. This airport is located in Pasay City, and is the largest airport in the Philippines.

Bus Stations Near Ternate

  • Baclaran Bus Terminal. This bus terminal is located in Pasay City and offers buses that go to Ternate.
  • Cubao Bus Terminal. This bus terminal offers buses that go to Ternate.

Other Options

  • Private Car. You can also rent a car or hire a driver to take you to Ternate.

How to Get Around Ternate

Ternate is a small town, and the best way to explore it is by walking or renting a tricycle.

Where to Go Next from Ternate: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Ternate is located near several tourist destinations and is an ideal starting point for exploring Cavite and Batangas. Here are some of the top picks:

  • Tagaytay City. This city is known for its scenic views, cool climate, and iconic Taal Volcano.
  • Nasugbu, Batangas. This town offers various beach resorts and is a popular destination for watersports and diving.
  • Batangas City. This city is known for its rich history, cultural landmarks, and heritage sites.

Conclusion

Ternate offers a unique blend of natural wonders, cultural attractions, and historical significance that every traveler must experience. Whether you’re a nature lover, history enthusiast, or foodie, Ternate has something to offer for everyone. Plan your trip now and discover the beauty of Ternate, Cavite.
