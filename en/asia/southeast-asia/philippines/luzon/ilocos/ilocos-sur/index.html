Introduction to Ilocos Sur

Ilocos Sur, located in the Ilocos region of Luzon, Philippines, is a charming province with a rich history and culture. Known for its Spanish colonial architecture, beaches, and outdoor adventures, Ilocos Sur is a must-visit destination for travelers looking for an authentic experience. In this ultimate guide, we’ll take you through the top things to do, places to visit, and local culture of Ilocos Sur.

List of Places to Visit in Ilocos Sur

There is no shortage of attractions in Ilocos Sur. Here’s a list of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Calle Crisologo
  • Vigan Cathedral
  • Bantay Bell Tower
  • National Museum Ilocos Complex
  • Syquia Mansion Museum

Natural Attractions of Ilocos Sur: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Hidden Garden
  • Baluarte Zoo
  • Kapurpurawan Rock Formation
  • Paoay Lake
  • Cape Bojeador Lighthouse

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ilocos Sur

For outdoor enthusiasts, Ilocos Sur offers several hiking and biking routes:

  • Cordillera Mountain Range - One of the toughest hikes in the province, this range has breathtaking views.
  • Baluarte Bike Trail - A unique route that will take you through the Baluarte Zoo.
  • Aponit Cave - A natural cave with challenging trails and stunning rock formations.

Best Things to Do in Ilocos Sur: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of activities and tours to choose from in Ilocos Sur:

  • Kalesa Ride - Take a horse-drawn carriage ride through the picturesque streets of Vigan.
  • Beach Hopping - Visit the famous beaches of Maira-ira, Saud, and Blue Lagoon.
  • Heritage Tours - Discover the rich history and culture of Ilocos Sur through guided tours.

Week-Long Itinerary for Ilocos Sur

Here is a week-long itinerary to make the most of your time in Ilocos Sur:

Day Itinerary
1 Arrive in Vigan, explore Calle Crisologo
2 Visit National Museum Ilocos Complex and Syquia Mansion Museum
3 Head to Kapurpurawan Rock Formation, Hidden Garden, and the city of Laoag
4 Go beach hopping in Pagudpud
5 Take a tour of Bantay Bell Tower, Vigan Cathedral, and the Plaza Salcedo
6 Experience the Baluarte Zoo and Paoay Lake
7 Enjoy an outdoor adventure at Cordillera Mountain Range or Aponit Cave

Food and Drink Scene of Ilocos Sur

Food and drink in Ilocos Sur are a mix of Spanish and native cuisines.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Vigan Longganisa - a garlicky sausage made from ground pork.
  • Bagnet - crispy-fried pork belly that is a staple in Ilocano cuisine.
  • Empanada - a savory pastry filled with meat and vegetables.

Local Beverages

  • Basi - a sweet rice wine traditionally made in Ilocos Sur.
  • Sugarcane Juice - a refreshing drink made from freshly pressed sugarcane.

Where to Stay in Ilocos Sur: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ilocos Sur has a diverse range of accommodations, from budget hostels to luxury hotels. Some of the most popular options include:

  • Grandpa’s Inn
  • Villa Angela Heritage House

  • Playa Tropical Resort Hotel

What to Buy in Ilocos Sur: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don’t leave Ilocos Sur without buying these unique souvenirs:

  • Abel Iloco Cloth - a traditional cloth made from cotton and silk.
  • Burnay Pot - a handmade clay pot that is commonly used in cooking.
  • Sukang Iloko - a local vinegar made from sugarcane.

Local Culture and Customs of Ilocos Sur

The people of Ilocos Sur are proud of their culture and traditions. Some important customs to note include:

  • Saying “po” and “opo” when speaking to elders is a sign of respect.
  • The “Bagoong” or fish sauce is an essential component of Ilocano cuisine.
  • The “Horn” gesture, with the thumb and pinky finger, is used to celebrate.

Best Time to Visit Ilocos Sur

The best time to visit Ilocos Sur is from November to March when the weather is dry and cool. The temperature usually peaks in April and May, which can make outdoor activities uncomfortable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ilocos Sur

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Bring cash as not all establishments accept cards.

Don’t:

  • Litter in public spaces.
  • Be disrespectful to the locals and their beliefs.
  • Haggle too aggressively at the markets.

Safety and Health

Safety in Ilocos Sur

Ilocos Sur is generally a safe place for tourists. However, it is important to take precautions, such as:

  • Keep your valuables secure.
  • Avoid walking alone at night.
  • Use a trusted guide when exploring the outdoors.

Health in Ilocos Sur

The health situation in Ilocos Sur is generally good. However, travelers should be wary of health risks associated with tropical environments, such as water-borne diseases. It is advisable to drink bottled water and follow good hygiene practices.

Getting to Ilocos Sur: Transportation Options and Logistics

Ilocos Sur is easily accessible by various transportation options.

Airports Near Ilocos Sur

  • Laoag International Airport
  • San Fernando Airport

Bus Stations Near Ilocos Sur

  • Vigan Bus Station
  • Laoag Bus Station

Other Options

  • Private Car Rental - A car rental service is available in Ilocos Sur, which is ideal for exploring the province at your own pace.

How to Get Around in Ilocos Sur

The best way to get around in Ilocos Sur is by tricycle or jeepney. Tricycles are common in smaller towns and can be negotiated for a good price. Jeepneys are a common form of public transportation and are affordable.

Where to Go Next from Ilocos Sur: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have more time to explore, here are some nearby destinations and excursions to consider:

  • Vigan City in Ilocos Norte - A World Heritage Site famous for its Spanish colonial architecture.
  • La Union - Known for its surfing spots and beach resorts.
  • Baguio City - Known as the “Summer Capital of the Philippines,” it features cool weather and scenic views.

Conclusion

Ilocos Sur is a gem in the Philippines, offering visitors a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and warm hospitality. With this comprehensive guide, you’re ready to plan your trip to Ilocos Sur and make unforgettable memories.
