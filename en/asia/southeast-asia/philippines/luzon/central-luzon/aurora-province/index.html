Introduction to Aurora (Province)

Located in Central Luzon, Philippines, Aurora (Province) is a hidden gem waiting to be explored. Known for its stunning natural beauty, Aurora is often referred to as “The Gateway to Paradise”. From picturesque landscapes to outdoor activities, Aurora has everything to offer for a memorable vacation. Whether you’re an adventure-seeker or looking for a tranquil getaway, Aurora is the perfect destination for you.

List of Places to Visit in Aurora (Province)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Aurora (Province) has an abundance of cultural attractions that allow you to learn more about the region’s heritage and history. Here are some of the cultural sites worth visiting:

  • Baler Museum - A museum that showcases the history and art of Baler, the capital of Aurora.
  • Museo de Baler - This museum highlights the bravery and resilience of the Filipinos during the Siege of Baler.
  • Ditumabo Falls - A popular waterfall that features a natural pool that is perfect for swimming.

Natural Attractions of Aurora (Province): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Aurora (Province) is blessed with a diverse range of eco-tourism attractions, including waterfalls, caves, and pristine beaches. Below are some of the most stunning natural attractions to explore in Aurora:

  • Ditumabo Mother Falls - This majestic waterfall is one of the most beautiful natural attractions in Aurora. You can take a guided trek to reach the falls.
  • Cemento Beach - Located in the town of Dingalan, this tranquil beach is perfect for relaxation and unwinding.
  • Dicasalarin Cove - A secluded beach that is situated on the northeastern side of Baler. It boasts beautiful rock formations and pristine waters that are great for swimming.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Aurora (Province)

Aurora (Province) is a great place for outdoor activities. Its diverse terrain and scenic views make it perfect for hiking and biking enthusiasts. The following are some of the most popular routes for hiking and biking:

  • Mt. Aurora Trekking Trail - This trekking trail is best for those who want to enjoy the stunning views of the Pacific Ocean while hiking.
  • Dingalan White Beach Trail - A popular biking trail that offers breathtaking views of the coast.
  • Sierra Madre Mountain Range Trail - This trail is perfect for those who want to experience trekking through the dense forest and reach the summit that offers a beautiful view of Baler Bay.

Best Things to Do in Aurora (Province): Activities and Tours

From outdoor activities to cultural immersion, Aurora (Province) has something for everyone. Here are some of the best things to do in Aurora:

  • Surfing in Sabang Beach - Sabang Beach is a popular surfing spot in Aurora and is ideal for both experts and beginners.
  • White Water Rafting in Tibag and Bulalacao Rivers - For thrill-seekers, white water rafting is a must-do activity in Aurora.
  • Milka-Aurora Farm Tour - This farm tour offers visitors a chance to experience a traditional Filipino rural farm setup.
  • Historical Tour of Baler - Discover the rich history of Aurora by taking a historical tour of Baler, the capital of Aurora.

Food and Drink Scene of Aurora (Province)

Aurora (Province) has a diverse culinary scene that offers local dishes and beverages that reflect the region’s heritage. Below are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Pancit Baler - A delicious local dish made from noodle and seafood, it has a distinct flavor.
  • Inabraw - A vegetable soup made from meat and kamote leaves, a great comfort food during rainy days.
  • Chicharabao - Deep-fried buffalo meat that is crispy on the outside and flavourful on the inside.

Local Beverages

  • Barako Coffee - This strong and flavorful coffee is locally produced in Aurora and is a must-try beverage.
  • Papaitan - This soup is made from goat liver and bile, a Filipino dish believed to have medicinal properties.

Where to Stay in Aurora (Province): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Aurora (Province) has a variety of accommodations to suit every traveler’s needs and budget. There are high-end hotels, budget-friendly hostels, and cozy guesthouses available throughout Aurora. Some of the best places to stay in Aurora are:

  • Costa Pacifica Resort - A luxurious resort located on Sabang Beach which offers a wide range of activities and amenities for their guests.
  • Bayler View Hotel - A budget-friendly hotel located in the heart of Baler town offering comfortable stays without breaking your budget.
  • AB’s Beach House - A guesthouse situated on one of the most beautiful beaches of Aurora. The perfect place for travelers who value comfort, tranquility and luxury.

What to Buy in Aurora (Province): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Some of the must-buy souvenirs and local products in Aurora (Province) include:

  • Eco-Bags made with native “banig” (Matting) - These eco-bags are made from the matting material that boasts Aurora’s traditional weaving methods. A perfect souvenir that is both stylish and sustainable.
  • Fresh Catch - Aurora is known for its abundant seafood, and visitors should try to bring home some fresh fish or seafood.
  • Honey - Aurora produces some of the finest honey in the Philippines, make sure to bring some home as a delicious souvenir from your trip.

Local Culture and Customs of Aurora (Province)

Aurora (Province) has a rich cultural heritage, and visitors should respect the region’s customs and traditions. Some of the unique customs of Aurora include:

  • Bayanihan - This is a traditional practice in which locals come together to help one another in times of need.
  • Pasalubong - This is a Filipino custom of bringing home a souvenir or gift from a trip to share with friends and family.

Best Time to Visit Aurora (Province)

The best time to visit Aurora (Province) is from February to May, during the dry season, when the weather is pleasant and perfect for outdoor activities. The province boasts hot and humid weather throughout the year, but the monsoon season, lasting from June to January, may bring heavy rain and strong winds that may hinder travel arrangements.

Do’s and Don’ts of Aurora (Province)

Do:

  • Respect the locals and their customs.
  • Be mindful of your environment by practicing eco-tourism principles.
  • Bring suncream and other essentials, as the weather can be hot and humid.

Don’t:

  • Leave your trash behind. Always dispose of your garbage in designated areas.
  • Damage or remove marine life and corals while snorkeling or diving.
  • Wear revealing clothes in public places, as it can be considered disrespectful in the local culture.

Safety and Health

Safety in Aurora (Province)

Aurora (Province) is a safe place for travelers, but it is always essential to take precautions while traveling. Here are some safety tips for a worry-free journey:

  • Make sure to have travel insurance before embarking on your trip.
  • Always carry a photocopy of your passport and travel documents.
  • Avoid carrying large amounts of cash and valuable items.

Health in Aurora (Province)

Visitors are advised to be mindful of their health while traveling in Aurora (Province). We recommend the following health tips:

  • Bring mosquito repellent and protect yourself from mosquito bites to prevent vector-borne diseases.
  • Drink bottled water to avoid stomach illnesses.
  • If you feel unwell, contact a medical professional or seek medical attention.

Getting to Aurora (Province): Transportation Options and Logistics

Aurora (Province) is accessible by air, land and sea from popular cities in the Philippines. Below are some of the transportation options to reach Aurora:

Airports Near Aurora (Province)

  • Clark International Airport - Located in Pampanga, this airport is the closest airport to Aurora Province. Los Angeles, Seoul, Macau, Bangkok, and Hong Kong flights are available.

Bus Stations Near Aurora (Province)

  • Cubao Bus Terminal - For those coming from Metro Manila, the Cubao Bus Terminal offers direct trips to Baler.
  • Victory Liner bus - Victory Liners provide regular bus services from Manila, Baguio, and Cabanatuan to Baler.

Other Options

  • Private Car Rental - For those who want flexible travel arrangements, private car rental is available.
  • Van Rental - This option is suitable for those traveling in groups and offers practicality and affordability especially when visiting tourist spots with limited public transportation.

How to Get Around in Aurora (Province)

Aurora (Province) is relatively small in size, and transportation options are abundant to reach tourist sites. Visitors can choose from the following options to explore Aurora:

  • Tricycle - The popular mode of transportation within the town is by tricycle, a motorcycle with a sidecar. This is the go-to option for a quick and affordable trip around the town and to the nearest tourist attractions.
  • Habal-Habal - This is a motorcycle ride that offers more flexibility when it comes to route and destination.
  • Buses, Jeeps, and Vans - The most common mode of transportation for those traveling from town to town.

Where to Go Next from Aurora (Province): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Aurora (Province) has plenty of nearby destinations and excursions that offer a unique experience. Here are some of the best places to visit near Aurora:

  • San Rafael River Adventure - Located near Bulacan, this destination offers kayaking and river swimming.
  • Palanan - This town situated at the northernmost part of Isabela offers lush rainforests, marine reserves, and historic lighthouses.
  • Nueva Ecija - The rice granary of the Philippines, the province of Nueva Ecija, offers various eco-tourism attractions such as mountain climbing and cave explorations.

Conclusion

Aurora (Province) is a hidden treasure waiting to be discovered, offering rich cultural heritage, stunning landscapes, and outdoor activities. With various transportation options, accommodations, and unique experiences, Aurora is the perfect destination for those looking for a memorable vacation. Make sure to plan your trip well and experience the beauty and charm of Aurora (Province), Philippines.
