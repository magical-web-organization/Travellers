Introduction to Albay

Albay is a province located in Bicol Region, Luzon, Philippines. Known for its perfect cone-shaped Mayon Volcano - a majestic natural wonder that towers over the province and attracts visitors from around the globe.

Beyond the scenic beauty of the region, Albay has much to offer with its rich culture, history, outdoor attractions, food scene, and local experiences.

In this albey travel guide, we will cover all the essentials, including the best places to visit, local cuisines to try, outdoor adventures to explore, and much more.

So let’s dive in and begin our ultimate Albay tour!

  • Respect for the elderly and hospitality to guests are highly valued in Bicolano culture.

Best Time to Visit Albay

The best time to visit Albay is from November to February, when the climate is relatively cool and dry.

The summer months of March until May are generally hot with occasional thunderstorms. Meanwhile, the region experiences the occasional typhoon from June until October.

Do’s and Don’ts of Albay

Do:

  • Respect the culture and traditions of the locals
  • Bring light clothing suitable for warm and humid weather
  • Try the local delicacies

Don’t:

  • Litter and damage the natural attractions and historical sites
  • Wear skimpy clothing when visiting religious and cultural sites
  • Be disrespectful towards elders and locals

Safety and Health

Safety in Albay

Albay is generally a safe place to travel. However, petty crimes such as pickpocketing and bag-snatching can still occur. Always take appropriate precautions and avoid walking in dark or isolated areas at night.

Health in Albay

Necessary vaccinations should include Hepatitis A and B, typhoid, and tetanus. Make sure to bring bug spray as mosquitoes carrying dengue and malaria have been reported.

Getting to Albay: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Albay

Legazpi City Airport is the main gateway to Albay. The airport has regular flights to major cities in the Philippines such as Manila and Cebu.

Bus Stations Near Albay

Several bus companies operate in Albay with frequent trips from Legazpi City and other points around the region. Some of the popular ones include Cagsawa Travel and DLTB Co.

Other Options

You can also reach Albay via ferry from Masbate and Samar.

How to Get Around in Albay

Jeepneys, tricycles, and air-conditioned buses are the main modes of transportation in Albay.
