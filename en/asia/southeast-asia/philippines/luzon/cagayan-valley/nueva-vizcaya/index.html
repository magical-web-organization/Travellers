Introduction to Nueva Vizcaya

Nestled in the northeastern region of Luzon, Philippines, lies the landlocked province of Nueva Vizcaya. Known as the Gateway to the Cagayan Valley, Nueva Vizcaya boasts a diverse range of attractions, from cultural landmarks to natural wonders. Experience the province’s tightly-knit communities and rich customs, along with its lush landscapes and panoramic views. In this comprehensive travel guide, you’ll learn all about the various places to visit, activities to try, and local culture to experience in this hidden gem of Southeast Asia.

List of Places to Visit in Nueva Vizcaya

Whether you want to explore the cultural landmarks or partake in adventurous hikes, Nueva Vizcaya has something for everyone. Here are some of the top places to visit for a memorable trip:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Museum of Nueva Vizcaya - Learn about the province’s rich and diverse heritage at this informative museum.
  • Bangan Hill National Park - Visit the historic Bangan Hill, a site important in local folklore and history.
  • St. Dominic Cathedral - Marvel at the impressive architecture and intricate design of this 17th-century cathedral and fortress.

Natural Attractions of Nueva Vizcaya: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Pulag National Park - Trek to the summit of Mount Pulag, one of the highest peaks in the Philippines, to experience its picturesque views.
  • Sampaquita Falls - Take a dip in the cool waters of the Sampaquita Falls, surrounded by lush greenery and natural rock formations.
  • Imugan Falls - Enjoy a quick hike and a refreshing swim at the Imugan Falls, famous for its towering cascades and gorgeous scenic landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Nueva Vizcaya

If you’re a fan of outdoor adventures, then Nueva Vizcaya is the perfect destination. Drop by these beautiful hiking trails for a scenic experience:

  • Dalton Pass - Hike through the Dalton Passage on the way to Mount Pulag to see the diverse flora and fauna of the region.
  • Imugan Trail - Explore the forests and hills through this scenic trail, leading to the Imugan Falls.
  • Aritao Ridge Trail - Walk the Aritao Ridge Trail for stunning views of the Cagayan Valley and the Cordillera Mountains.

Best Things to Do in Nueva Vizcaya: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, here are some other activities worth adding to your itinerary:

  • Salakot Waterfalls Adventure - Hop on a 4x4 ride and trek through scenic forests to experience the enchanting Salakot Waterfalls.
  • Trek to Mount Palali - Experience an epic trek to Mount Palali Trail and see a panoramic view of Santa Fe, Aritao, and Nueva Vizcaya.
  • Agricultural Farm Tour - Visit local farms and witness traditional farming methods of Nueva Vizcaya up close.

Week-Long Itinerary for Nueva Vizcaya

Food and Drink Scene of Nueva Vizcaya

Nueva Vizcaya offers a unique blend of traditional and modern cuisine along with a variety of local beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and drinks of the region:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Binoltong - Sticky rice cooked in bamboo tubes, served as a dessert with latik.
  • Inanud-o Taguig - Wild boar meat soup cooked with local herbs and spices.
  • Kinuday - Smoked meat from the highlands, typically carabao or goat meat.

Local Beverages

  • Rice coffee - A caffeine-free alternative to regular coffee made of roasted rice, perfect for those sensitive to caffeine.
  • Basim - A fermented rice wine that has a sweet aroma and a tangy taste.
  • Kalabao Lamot - A refreshing local coconut alcoholic drink.

Where to Stay in Nueva Vizcaya: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Nueva Vizcaya offers a variety of accommodation options for all types of travellers. Here are some of the best options:

  • Bambang Country Lodge - This quaint hotel provides a comfortable stay with excellent views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Amancio’s Hotel and Restaurant - This budget-friendly hotel offers clean rooms and fast Wi-Fi, along with a hearty breakfast.
  • Dakli Eco-Lodge - A rustic, eco-friendly lodge immersed in the natural surroundings of the Imugan Falls.

What to Buy in Nueva Vizcaya: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Apart from memories, here are some souvenirs and local products you can bring back home:

  • Kakanin - Traditional Filipino desserts made from rice and coconut milk.
  • Handicrafts - Handmade accessories and decorations made of native materials such as abaca, rattan, and bamboo.
  • Coffee and Veggies - Take home some of the best locally sourced coffee and vegetables as souvenirs for your friends and family.

Local Culture and Customs of Nueva Vizcaya

Home to various indigenous communities, Nueva Vizcaya has a rich and diverse cultural heritage. Here are some customs worth experiencing:

  • Wedding Dance - A traditional dance performed by any couple who had been blessed with their first child.
  • Bayahihan - Filipino cultural practice exemplifying communal unity, work, and cooperation, giving a group spirit.
  • Banga Community Dance - Dance performed as a form of tribute to stonemasons.

Best Time to Visit Nueva Vizcaya

The best time to visit Nueva Vizcaya is during the dry season, from November to May. This is the most ideal time for outdoor activities as the weather is less humid and more comfortable. Avoid travelling during the rainy season, from June to October, as it can be quite difficult to move around due to flooded roads and unpredictable weather conditions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Nueva Vizcaya

To ensure a safe and enjoyable trip to Nueva Vizcaya, keep in mind these do’s and don’ts:

Do:

  • Respect local culture and traditions.
  • Keep surroundings clean and tidy.
  • Try to experience local cuisine and beverages.
  • Be respectful towards wildlife and natural resources.

Don’t:

  • Engage in disrespectful behaviour towards other people or their customs.
  • Litter in public places or natural areas.
  • Harm or interfere with wildlife.

Safety and Health

Here are some important safety and health tips for your Nueva Vizcaya travel:

Safety in Nueva Vizcaya

  • Be careful in your movements when hiking or trekking, especially in areas with steep terrain or uneven paths.
  • Ensure your accommodation has functioning smoke alarms and other safety features.
  • Keep your valuables locked up in a safe or secure hiding place to safeguard them from theft.

Health in Nueva Vizcaya

  • Bring insect repellent and sunscreen to protect against sunburn and insect bites.
  • Be sure to maintain proper hydration by drinking ample water during your outdoor excursions.
  • Pack any essential medication required for your health needs.

Getting to Nueva Vizcaya: Transportation Options and Logistics

There are various ways to reach Nueva Vizcaya, depending on your starting point. Here are some transportation options:

Airports Near Nueva Vizcaya

  • Cauayan Airport - A domestic airport, the nearest airport to Nueva Vizcaya.

Bus Stations Near Nueva Vizcaya

  • Victory Liner - A reliable bus service with routes throughout Northern and Central Luzon.
  • Five Star Bus - Another reputable bus service offering transport to the region.

Other Options

  • Private Car Rental - Rent a car for added convenience and flexibility.
  • Hired Transportation Services - From private vehicles to tricycles, avail of hired transportation services for shorter commutes within Nueva Vizcaya.

How to Get Around in Nueva Vizcaya

Once you arrive in Nueva Vizcaya, there are various ways to move around:

  • Tricycle - A popular mode of transport in Nueva Vizcaya, available for hire for short trips around the town.
  • Jeepney - A more cost-effective mode of transportation; however, the schedule and route of jeepneys may be less convenient.
  • Private Car Rental - Rent a vehicle for more flexibility and comfort in travelling.

Where to Go Next from Nueva Vizcaya: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several other destinations worth exploring near Nueva Vizcaya. Here are a few places to include as part of your trip:

  • Ifugao - Visit the famous Banaue Rice Terraces in Ifugao, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • Isabela - Explore the Northern Sierra Madre Natural Park and the Dinapigue Bay Tidal Flats in the province of Isabela.
  • Pangasinan - Discover the Hundred Islands National Park and the famous beaches of Bolinao in Pangasinan.

Conclusion

Nueva Vizcaya is a hidden gem among the numerous provinces of the Philippines with its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and adventurous activities. It provides a remarkable opportunity for travellers to explore and experience the diversity of the Cordillera region, its people and traditions. Use this comprehensive travel guide and enjoy a once-in-a-lifetime trip to this beautiful province of the Philippines.
