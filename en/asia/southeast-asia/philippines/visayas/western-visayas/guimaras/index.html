Introduction to Guimaras

Guimaras Island is a hidden gem located in Western Visayas, Philippines. It is known for its white sand beaches, clear blue waters, and sweet mangoes. The island offers a unique blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation, making it a perfect holiday destination for all types of travelers. In this guide, we will provide a detailed breakdown of the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and more!

List of Places to Visit in Guimaras

Guimaras Island is filled with various attractions that showcase its natural beauty, history, and culture. Here’s a list of the top places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Guisi Lighthouse and Ruins
  • Trappist Monastery
  • Navalas Church
  • Museo De Guimaras
  • Pitstop Adventure

Natural Attractions of Guimaras: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Alubihod Beach
  • Ave Maria Island
  • Guisi Beach
  • Los Angeles Beach
  • SEAFDEC Igang Marine Station and Research
  • Roca Encantada Museum

Hiking/Biking Routes of Guimaras

Guimaras Island is a great destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. The island has several trails that showcase its natural beauty. Here are some of the top hiking and biking routes:

  • Mango Plantation Trail (easy trail perfect for beginners)
  • Alubihod Bay Trail (moderate-level trail)
  • Mt. Carmel Peak Trail (advanced level trail)

Best Things to Do in Guimaras: Activities and Tours

Guimaras Island offers many exciting things to do and tours to embark on. Here are some of the best:

  • Island hopping tour (visit the various islands around Guimaras)
  • Mangrove tour (explore the mangrove forests of Guimaras)
  • Standup paddleboarding (enjoy the beautiful waters of Guimaras while getting a good workout)

Week-Long Itinerary for Guimaras

If you’re planning to visit Guimaras Island for a week, here’s a sample itinerary:

  • Day 1: Arrival and check-in at hotel followed by a visit to Guisi Lighthouse and Ruins
  • Day 2: Island hopping tour and beach hopping
  • Day 3: Hiking and biking the Mango Plantation Trail and Alubihod Bay Trail
  • Day 4: Mangrove tour in the morning and visit the Trappist Monastery in the afternoon
  • Day 5: Visit the Roca Encantada Museum and Los Angeles Beach
  • Day 6: Standup paddleboarding activity in the morning and visit the Pitstop Adventure in the afternoon
  • Day 7: Departure

Food and Drink Scene of Guimaras

Must-Try Dishes

  • Mango Pizza
  • Mango Shake
  • Sinigang sa Mangga

Local Beverages

Where to Stay in Guimaras: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Guimaras Island offers a variety of accommodations depending on your budget and preference. Here are some of the top hotels, hostels, and guesthouses:

  • Raymen Beach Resort
  • Kenyama Beach Resort

  • La Puerta Al Paraiso

What to Buy in Guimaras: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don’t forget to bring home some souvenirs and local products from Guimaras Island. Here are some of the top things to buy:

  • Guimaras Mangoes (fresh and dried)
  • Mango-infused products (e.g. Mango soap, Mango lotion)
  • Handwoven products (e.g. bags, hats, mats)

Local Culture and Customs of Guimaras

Guimaras Island is a mix of various cultures primarily influenced by the Ilonggo and Hiligaynon culture. When visiting, it is essential to show respect to the locals and follow their customs, such as the traditional “pawikan” (sea turtle) releasing ceremony.

Best Time to Visit Guimaras

The best time to visit Guimaras Island is during the dry season, which runs from November to April. During this time, the weather is perfect for outdoor activities, and the waters are calm. If you’re looking to attend festivals, the Manggahan Festival in May is a must-see.

Do’s and Don’ts of Guimaras

Here are some essential do’s and don’ts when visiting Guimaras Island:

Do:

  • Respect the locals and their customs
  • Try the local cuisine
  • Bring cash as most establishments do not accept credit cards
  • Apply sunscreen and wear protective gear when doing outdoor activities

Don’t:

  • Litter
  • Disturb the natural habitat of wildlife
  • Go on hiking or biking trails during rainy weather

Safety and Health

Safety in Guimaras

Guimaras Island is generally safe for tourists. However, it is essential to take standard safety precautions, such as:

  • Keep valuables safe and secure
  • Avoid carrying large sums of cash
  • Do not walk alone at night in the isolated areas

Health in Guimaras

When visiting Guimaras Island, it is recommended to:

  • Drink bottled or filtered water
  • Apply mosquito repellent to avoid mosquito-related illnesses such as Dengue, Chikungunya, and Zika

Getting to Guimaras: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Guimaras

The nearest airport to Guimaras Island is Iloilo International Airport. From the airport, take a taxi or bus to Parola Jetty Port in Iloilo City and take a boat to Guimaras Island.

Bus Stations Near Guimaras

Several bus companies operate in Iloilo City that has direct routes to Guimaras Island.

Other Options

You can also take a private or shared shuttle service to Guimaras Island from Iloilo City.

How to Get Around in Guimaras

Upon arrival at Guimaras Island, there are various modes of transportation to explore the island:

  • Tricycle (popular mode of transportation)
  • Motorcycles for rent (easily accessible and cheaper option)
  • Jeepney (most reliable public transportation)

Where to Go Next from Guimaras: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you’re looking to explore other areas near Guimaras Island, here are some options:

  • Boracay Island (famous for its white sand beaches)
  • Negros Island (known for its majestic mountain views)
  • Antique Province (known for its eco-tourism destinations)

Conclusion

Guimaras Island is a hidden gem in the Philippines that offers a unique blend of adventure, culture, and relaxation. With our comprehensive guide, you’re now equipped with everything you need to know to plan your trip to Guimaras Island. From the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay and more, you’re guaranteed to have an unforgettable experience.
