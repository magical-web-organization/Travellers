Introduction to Panglao Town

Panglao Town is a charming destination located on Panglao Island, in Bohol, the beautiful Philippines. Known for its scenic views, sandy beaches, and crystal clear waters, Panglao Town is a must-visit destination for anyone who likes outdoor activities, picturesque scenery, and local culture. The town offers a range of exciting experiences, from historical landmarks to diving spots that will take your breath away.

Whether you want to explore the rich history and culture of Panglao Town or go on an adrenaline-fueled adventure, there’s something for everyone. In this comprehensive guide, we’ve curated a selection of some of the top attractions, hiking and biking routes, food and drink scene, and local culture and customs in Panglao Town.

Local Culture and Customs of Panglao Town

The people of Panglao Town are friendly, warm, and welcoming. The town’s culture and customs reflect the Philippines’ rich history and diverse heritage. Be sure to respect the local customs and traditions during your visit.

Best Time to Visit Panglao Town

The best time to visit Panglao Town is from November to May when the weather is dry and pleasant.

Do’s and Don’ts of Panglao Town

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Keep the beaches clean and avoid littering
  • Bargain at the local markets
  • Bring sunscreen and sun protection

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing in public places
  • Touch or disturb the wildlife
  • Buy souvenirs made from endangered species
  • Share your personal information with strangers

Safety and Health

Safety is not a major concern in Panglao Town, but it’s always good to take precautions. Here are some safety and health tips to keep in mind during your visit:

Safety in Panglao Town

  • Always be aware of your surroundings and belongings
  • Use reputable tours and guides
  • Avoid walking alone at night
  • Follow the local traffic rules

Health in Panglao Town

  • Drink bottled water or bring a filter
  • Use mosquito repellant and/or mosquito nets
  • Bring any necessary medications for your trip

Getting to Panglao Town: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Panglao Town

Other Options

How to Get Around in Panglao Town

The best way to get around Panglao Town is by motorcycle or scooter. Other options include tricycles, taxis, and private cars. Note that traffic can be congested during peak tourist season.
