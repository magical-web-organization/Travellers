Introduction to Tagbilaran

Tagbilaran is a beautiful city located in Bohol, an island province in the Central Visayas region of the Philippines. It is the capital of Bohol and is known for its natural beauty, rich culture, and history. It is a popular destination for travelers who are looking for a mix of both adventure and relaxation.

In this guide, we will explore the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and local culture and customs of Tagbilaran.

List of Places to Visit in Tagbilaran

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Baclayon Church
    • This 16th-century church is one of the oldest in the Philippines and has been declared a National Cultural Treasure.
  • Blood Compact Shrine
    • This landmark commemorates the first treaty of friendship between the Spanish and the Filipinos.
  • Bohol National Museum
    • This museum features various exhibits that represent the history and culture of Bohol.
  • Tagbilaran City Tourist Port
    • This port is the gateway to the beautiful nearby islands such as Panglao Island, Balicasag Island, and Pamilacan Island.

Natural Attractions of Tagbilaran: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Danao Adventure Park
    • This eco-adventure park offers various outdoor activities such as zip-lining, rappelling, kayaking, and more.
  • Mag-Aso Falls
    • This stunning waterfall is located in Antequera town and is perfect for swimming and relaxing.
  • Hinagdanan Cave
    • This cave has a natural pool inside and is open for swimming and exploration.
  • Panglao Island
    • This island is known for its white sandy beaches, crystal-clear waters, and the beautiful coral reefs that are perfect for snorkeling and scuba diving.
  • Loboc River
    • This river is surrounded by lush tropical forests and offers a scenic and peaceful boat ride.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Tagbilaran

  • Chocolate Hills
    • This iconic attraction features more than a thousand hills that look like chocolate when they turn brown during the dry season.
  • Bilar Man-Made Forest
    • This forest serves as a natural habitat for numerous wildlife species and offers a scenic hiking and biking trail.
  • Dimiao Twin Falls
    • This beautiful attraction features two stunning waterfalls that are surrounded by lush tropical forests, and offers a challenging hiking trail for adventurous travelers.

Best Things to Do in Tagbilaran: Activities and Tours

  • Island Hopping Tour
    • This tour takes visitors to nearby islands like Panglao Island, Balicasag Island, and Pamilacan Island, where they can go snorkeling, diving, or just relax on the beach.
  • Dolphin Watching
    • This tour takes visitors to watch dolphins play in their natural habitat.
  • Countryside Tour
    • This tour takes visitors to various landmarks and attractions in the countryside such as the Chocolate Hills, Tarsier Sanctuary, and Bilar Man-Made Forest.

Week-Long Itinerary for Tagbilaran

Food and Drink Scene of Tagbilaran

Must-Try Dishes

  • Chicken Inasal
    • This dish is a popular grilled chicken recipe that is marinated in vinegar, coconut milk, and various spices.
  • Lechon
    • This dish is a famous Filipino dish that is roasted pig slow-cooked over an open flame.
  • Binignit
    • This dessert is made from a mix of sweet potatoes, taro, and tapioca pearls cooked in coconut milk and sugar.

Local Beverages

  • Tuba
    • This alcoholic drink is made from the sap of coconut trees and is a popular drink in the Philippines.
  • Bohol Bee Farm’s Honey Ice Cream
    • This smooth and creamy ice cream is made from locally sourced honey and is a must-try for any sweet tooth.

Where to Stay in Tagbilaran: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Belian Hotel
    • This hotel is located in the heart of Tagbilaran City and offers a comfortable stay.
  • Bohol Tropics Resort
    • This resort is located on a hillside in the city and offers stunning views of the city and the sea.
  • Soledad Suites
    • This hotel is located just minutes away from Tagbilaran City, making it an ideal location for travelers who want to be close to the city’s attractions.

What to Buy in Tagbilaran: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Peanut Kisses
    • These bite-sized cookies made from peanuts and sugar are a popular souvenir from Bohol.
  • Bohol Bee Farm’s Organic Products
    • Bohol is known for its organic products, and the Bohol Bee Farm offers a wide range of honey-based organic products such as honey soap and honey lip balm.

Local Culture and Customs of Tagbilaran

Tagbilaran is a predominantly Catholic city, and religious festivals are an integral part of the city’s culture and traditions. Some of the popular festivals include:

  • Sandugo Festival
    • This festival commemorates the historic blood compact between Rajah Sikatuna and the Spanish explorer Miguel Lopez de Legazpi.
  • Holy Week
    • This week is observed with religious processions and other events leading up to Easter Sunday.

Best Time to Visit Tagbilaran

The best time to visit Tagbilaran is between November and April when the weather is dry and sunny. Temperatures during this time of the year are comfortable, and there is less chance of rain.

Do’s and Don’ts of Tagbilaran

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
  • Carry a photocopy of your passport and other important documents with you.

Don’t:

  • Drink tap water.
  • Engage in any illegal activity.
  • Disrespect the local culture and customs.

Safety and Health

Safety in Tagbilaran

Tagbilaran is a relatively safe city, but travelers should take general precautions to ensure they stay safe while traveling.

  • Avoid carrying large amounts of cash or valuables.
  • Be aware of your surroundings and keep an eye on your belongings.

Health in Tagbilaran

Travelers to Tagbilaran should take the necessary precautions to prevent illness. Some tips include:

  • Drink bottled water.
  • Wash your hands frequently.
  • Get the necessary vaccinations before traveling.

Getting to Tagbilaran: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Tagbilaran

  • Tagbilaran City Airport
    • This airport is the primary airport serving Tagbilaran and is located only a few minutes away from the city center.

Bus Stations Near Tagbilaran

  • Bohol Integrated Transport Terminal
    • This bus terminal is the largest bus terminal in Tagbilaran, and buses from here travel to various destinations across the Philippines.

Other Options

  • Ferry
    • Tagbilaran is accessible by ferry from various locations across the Philippines.

How to Get Around in Tagbilaran

  • Tricycle
    • This is a popular mode of transportation in Tagbilaran. Tricycle fares are relatively inexpensive.
  • Taxi
    • Taxis are also available in Tagbilaran, but they are more expensive than tricycles.

Where to Go Next from Tagbilaran: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Bohol
    • Explore more of Bohol and visit attractions like the Chocolate Hills, Tarsier Sanctuary, and Alona Beach.
  • Cebu
    • Take a ferry to Cebu, the nearest major city to Tagbilaran, and visit attractions such as Magellan’s Cross and Basilica del Santo Nino.

Conclusion

Tagbilaran is a beautiful destination in the Philippines that offers a mix of natural beauty, rich culture, and history. Whether you are looking for outdoor adventures, cultural experiences, or just a relaxing vacation, Tagbilaran has something for everyone. With this comprehensive guide, you can plan your perfect trip to Tagbilaran and explore all that this amazing city has to offer.
