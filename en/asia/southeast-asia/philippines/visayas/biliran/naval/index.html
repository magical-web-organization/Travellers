Naval is a small town located in Biliran, a province in the Visayas region of the Philippines. This peaceful town has a rich cultural heritage and breathtaking natural attractions that make it a perfect destination for travelers seeking adventure, relaxation, and culture. In this travel guide, we’ll take you through the best places to visit, things to do, and where to stay in Naval.

Introduction to Naval

Naval is a town that boasts of natural attractions, such as pristine beaches, waterfalls, and mountain ranges, which make it an ideal destination for nature lovers. It is rich in culture and history, which is evident in its landmarks, museums, and religious festivals. Naval is a humble town that is dotted with small local businesses, making it a perfect place to experience authentic Filipino life.

There are numerous places to visit in Naval that offer a wide range of experiences for travelers.

Naval is also blessed with beautiful natural attractions that offer breathtaking views and opportunities for outdoor adventures. The following are some of the best natural attractions in Naval:

Food and Drink Scene of Naval

Naval is known for its fresh seafood, tropical fruits, and unique local delicacies. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in Naval:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kinilaw na Isda (raw fish salad)
  • Inun-unan (fish cooked in vinegar)
  • Binagol (sweet delicacy made of grated taro root)

Local Beverages

  • Bahalina (fermented beverage made from coconut or nipa palm sap)
  • Laksoy (coconut wine)

What to Buy in Naval: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Local Culture and Customs of Naval

Naval is a predominantly Catholic town, which is evident in its religious festivals and landmarks. The town’s locals are known for their hospitality and warmth, and visitors are expected to reciprocate by showing respect for the town’s culture and customs.

Best Time to Visit Naval

The best time to visit Naval is from November to May, as this is the dry season and the weather is ideal for outdoor activities and exploring the town’s natural attractions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Naval

Do:

  • Respect the town’s culture and customs.
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
  • Try the local delicacies and beverages.

Don’t:

  • Litter or damage the town’s natural attractions.
  • Disrespect local traditions and cultural practices.
  • Get too drunk in public or cause a disturbance.

Safety and Health

Safety in Naval

Naval is generally a safe town for travelers. However, visitors are advised to practice caution and avoid shady areas at night.

Health in Naval

There are several hospitals and clinics in Naval that offer medical services to locals and travelers. It is advisable to bring any necessary medication and obtain travel insurance before visiting Naval.

Getting to Naval: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Naval

The closest airport to Naval is the Daniel Z. Romualdez Airport in Tacloban City, which is about 3-4 hours away by bus or van.

Bus Stations Near Naval

There are several bus stations in Tacloban City that offer transportation services to Naval. The most popular bus stations are the Tacloban City Bus Terminal and the San Juanico Bridge Bus Terminal.

Other Options

Another transportation option is hiring a private van or car. Several rental companies in Tacloban City offer vehicle rental services.

How to Get Around in Naval

Transportation options in Naval include tricycles, jeepneys, and motorcycles. Visitors can also rent a bicycle to explore the town’s natural attractions up close.
