Introduction to Laoang

Laoang is a town in the province of Northern Samar on the island of Samar, Visayas region, in the eastern part of the Philippines. The town is known for its rich history, natural beauty, and cultural heritage.

If you’re planning a trip to Laoang, this travel guide will provide you with all the information you need to make the most of your visit.

List of Places to Visit in Laoang

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Laoang Church
  • Tambis Hill
  • Old Spanish Bridge
  • Caluwayan Palm Island Resort
  • Laoang Museum

Natural Attractions of Laoang: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Week-Long Itinerary for Laoang

Day Activities
1 Arrive in Laoang and settle in at your accommodation. Visit Laoang Church and Tambis Hill.
2 Hike to Pinipisakan Falls and swim in the natural pool.
3 Explore Sunog Cave and relax at Cangumbang Beach.
4 Visit Pagatpat Mangrove Forest and take a kayak tour.
5 Take an island-hopping tour and snorkel in the clear waters.
6 Visit Lulugayan Falls and try a buggy ride.
7 Souvenir shopping at the local market and depart from Laoang.

Local Culture and Customs of Laoang

  • The people of Laoang are friendly and hospitable.
  • Respect the local customs and dress modestly.
  • Try to learn a few words of the local language.

Best Time to Visit Laoang

The best time to visit Laoang is from November to February when the weather is dry and cool.

Do’s and Don’ts of Laoang

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local cuisine.
  • Take photos of the natural attractions.
  • Plan ahead for transportation.

Don’t:

  • Be disrespectful to locals or their customs.
  • Litter or damage the natural attractions.
  • Leave your valuables unattended.

Safety and Health

Safety in Laoang

Laoang is a safe town to visit, but take the usual precautions to avoid any untoward incidents.

Health in Laoang

Make sure to have a comprehensive medical insurance in case of any medical emergencies that may arise during your trip.

Getting to Laoang: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Laoang

  • Catarman Airport in Catarman, Northern Samar (33 km)

Bus Stations Near Laoang

  • Ceres Bus Terminal in Calbayog City, Samar (54 km)
  • Duptours Bus Terminal in Catbalogan City, Samar (102 km)

Other Options

  • Renting a car or motorcycle
  • Hiring a taxi or tricycle

How to Get Around in Laoang

The best way to get around Laoang is by tricycle or by renting a motorbike.
