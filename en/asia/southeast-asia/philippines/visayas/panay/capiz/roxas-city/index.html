Introduction to Roxas City

Roxas City is a charming and historic city located in the province of Capiz in the Western Visayas region of the Philippines. The city was named after Manuel Roxas, the first president of the Philippines after the country gained independence from the United States. Roxas City is a relatively small city compared to other major cities in the Philippines, but it is full of character and charm.

If you’re looking for an off-the-beaten-path destination to explore in the Philippines, Roxas City is the perfect place to visit. With its rich history, stunning natural scenery, and warm hospitality, it’s no wonder why Roxas City is becoming an increasingly popular destination for travelers.

List of Places to Visit in Roxas City

Whether you’re interested in history, culture, or the great outdoors, there is something for everyone in Roxas City. Here are some of the must-see places to visit:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Roxas City is rich in cultural heritage, and there are several museums and historical places to explore:

1. Panublion Museum

Panublion Museum is located in the heart of Roxas City, and it is an excellent place to learn about the history and culture of Capiz. The museum features exhibits on pre-Hispanic Capiz, Filipino customs and traditions, and the region’s natural resources.

2. Santa Monica Church

Santa Monica Church is one of the most famous landmarks in Roxas City and is a must-visit for anyone interested in history and architecture. The church is over 200 years old and is one of the oldest in the Philippines. It has stunning interiors and boasts of intricate details on the façade.

3. Conde de Guaubarra Monument

The Conde de Guaubarra Monument is a historical landmark located at the heart of Roxas City. The monument was erected in honor of Capiz’s first governor, Antonio Guaubarra, and serves as a reminder of the region’s rich history.

Natural Attractions of Roxas City: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

In addition to its cultural heritage, Roxas City is also blessed with natural beauty. Here are some of the must-visit natural attractions:

1. Baybay Beach

Baybay Beach is one of the most popular beaches in Roxas City, and it’s easy to see why. The beach boasts of white sands, crystal-clear waters that are perfect for swimming, snorkeling and other water-sports. It’s an excellent place to relax and enjoy the sun and the sea.

2. Suhoton Beach

Suhoton Beach is an idyllic and secluded beach on the outskirts of Roxas City. The beach features a long stretch of pristine sands and picturesque scenery that makes it ideal for a day trip. It’s also a perfect place for snorkeling and other activities.

3. Olotayan Island

Olotayan Island is a small and quiet island just a few minutes away from Roxas City. Despite its small size, the island is packed with natural beauty, including a lush vegetation canopy, white sand beaches and crystal clear waters that make it an excellent spot to unwind and relax.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Roxas City

If you’re the adventurous type, there are several hiking and biking routes in Roxas City that are worth exploring. Here are some of the best ones:

1. Mt. Kanlaon Natural Park

Mt. Kanlaon Natural Park is a protected area located in the western part of Roxas City. The park is home to numerous species of flora and fauna and boasts of picturesque hiking trails that are perfect for nature lovers.

2. Capiz Bike Trail

The Capiz Bike Trail is a 60-kilometer mountain bike trail that winds through the lush forests and countryside of Roxas City. The trail is perfect for mountain bikers of all levels and offers stunning views of the surrounding scenery.

Best Things to Do in Roxas City: Activities and Tours

In addition to its natural and cultural attractions, Roxas City is also home to several exciting activities and tours. Here are some of the best ones:

1. Island Hopping

Roxas City is surrounded by several islands, and island hopping is a popular activity among tourists. You can rent a boat or join a guided tour to discover the hidden gems of the islands around Roxas City.

2. Food Tours

Roxas City is a foodie’s paradise, with plenty of unique and delicious dishes to try. A food tour is an excellent way to explore the city’s vibrant food scene and discover the local dishes and flavors.

Food and Drink Scene of Roxas City

One of the best things about Roxas City is its food and drink scene, with plenty of mouth-watering dishes and refreshing drinks to try.

Must-Try Dishes

Here are some of the must-try dishes in Roxas City:

1. Seafood

Because of the city’s coastal location, seafood dishes are the highlight of any food tour in Roxas City. Some of the must-try dishes include grilled squid, oysters, and crab.

2. Kadyos

Kadyos is a local dish made with pigeon peas and pork. It is slow-cooked for hours to bring out the flavors, and it’s usually paired with rice and a side dish of vegetables.

3. Chicken Inasal

Chicken inasal is a tasty grilled chicken dish that is popular all over the Philippines. However, Roxas City has its own unique spin on this popular dish, using local herbs and spices that make it stand out.

Local Beverages

Beer and soft drinks are readily available in Roxas City, but for an authentic experience, you should try the local drinks.

1. Pinya Juice

Pinya juice is a refreshing drink made from fresh pineapples. It’s perfect for hot summer days and to cool down after exploring Roxas City’s attractions.

2. Coconut Juice

Coconut juice is a popular drink in the Philippines, and Roxas City is no exception. It’s best enjoyed fresh from the coconut, and it’s a great way to quench your thirst and rehydrate after a long day.

Where to Stay in Roxas City: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Roxas City has plenty of accommodation options to suit every budget and preference. Here are some of the best places to stay:

1. Urban Sands Iloilo

Located in the heart of the city, Urban Sands Iloilo is a stylish and modern hotel that offers affordable rates and excellent amenities. It is perfect for travelers who want to be in the center of the action.

2. Espacio Verde Resort

Espacio Verde Resort is a luxury resort located a few minutes away from Roxas City. The resort boasts of beautiful gardens and stunning views, making it an ideal place for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of the city.

3. Lagoon Resort

Lagoon Resort is a budget-friendly option that offers comfortable rooms and excellent amenities. It’s located near the city’s attractions, making it a convenient option for those who want to explore Roxas City.

What to Buy in Roxas City: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Roxas City is known for several unique products that you can bring home as souvenirs. Here are some of the best things to buy:

1. Capiz Shell Crafts

Capiz Shell Crafts are highly popular and are made from the local shells. You can find everything from small trinkets to large decorative pieces, with intricate designs and patterns.

2. Binakol Fabrics

Binakol fabric is a type of hand-loomed fabric made using traditional techniques. It’s used to create stunning clothes, bags, and accessories, making it an ideal souvenir to bring back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Roxas City

Roxas City is deeply rooted in tradition, and the locals take pride in their customs and cultural heritage. The traditional practices include:

1. Ati-Atihan Festival

The Ati-Atihan Festival is a vibrant and colorful celebration held in Roxas City every January. It’s a time of lively music, dance, and street parties, with tourists flocking from all over the world to witness the festivities.

2. Paraw Regatta Festival

The Paraw Regatta Festival celebrates the city’s seafaring heritage and is held every February. It’s a multi-day event that features boat races, cultural dances, and other exciting activities.

Best Time to Visit Roxas City

The best time to visit Roxas City is during the dry season, which runs from November to April. During this time, the weather is pleasant, and you can enjoy the city’s outdoor attractions without having to worry about rain.

Do’s and Don’ts of Roxas City

To make the most of your trip to Roxas City, here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local cuisine.
  • Carry a water bottle and sunscreen.
  • Wear comfortable shoes.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing when visiting religious sites.
  • Engage in any illegal activity.
  • Litter in public places.

Safety and Health

Roxas City is a safe and welcoming destination for tourists. However, it’s important to take the necessary precautions to ensure your safety and health.

Safety in Roxas City

  • Avoid carrying large amounts of cash when exploring the city.
  • Use only authorized transportation services.
  • Be aware of your surroundings in crowded areas.

Health in Roxas City

  • Drink only bottled water.
  • Be careful of the food you eat and stick to reputable restaurants.
  • Bring insect repellent, especially during the rainy season.

How to Get Around in Roxas City

The best way to get around Roxas City is by tricycle, which is a motorcycle with a sidecar. Tricycles are widely available and are an affordable and convenient way to travel around the city. You can also rent a car or a motorbike if you prefer to have more control over your transportation.

Conclusion

From its rich cultural heritage to its stunning natural scenery and vibrant food scene, there are plenty of reasons to visit Roxas City.
