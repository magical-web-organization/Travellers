Introduction to Bantayan Island

Bantayan Island is a paradise getaway located in the northern portion of Cebu Province, Visayas, Philippines. This island features white sandy beaches, crystal-clear water, and a tranquil atmosphere that attracts tourists from all over the world. Bantayan Island has a lot to offer in terms of culture, history, natural attractions, outdoor adventures, and tasty local cuisines.

In this comprehensive guide, we will give you a detailed overview of all the key information you need to know to plan your trip to Bantayan Island.

List of Places to Visit in Bantayan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Santa Fe Church – a beautiful, colonial-era church built in the 1800s that features stunning baroque architecture, bell tower, and altar. The church is known for its rich religious history and serves as a landmark of the island.
  • Bantayan Ancestral House – a popular museum that shows visitors how people lived during the Spanish colonial period in Bantayan. This house features antique furniture, paintings, sculptures, and other important historical artefacts.

Natural Attractions of Bantayan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Bantayan Beaches – Bantayan Island is famous for its pristine beaches, which are perfect spots for sunbathing, swimming, snorkelling, and more. The most visited beaches in Bantayan include Kota Beach, Alice Beach, and Santa Fe Beach.
  • Ogtong Cave – This underground cave is a popular tourist spot in Bantayan. You can take a dip in the cave’s freshwaters or explore the cave’s many fascinating features.
  • Silion Island – an unspoilt island paradise that is great for a day trip. It is home to a diverse range of marine life and coral reefs, making it a popular spot for snorkelling and diving.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bantayan

  • Bantayan Centre Trail – this is an easy, 3-km hike that extends from Bantayan Proper to Bantayan Centro. The trail is perfect for beginners and offers stunning scenic views of the island.

Best Things to Do in Bantayan: Activities and Tours

  • Island Hopping – A highly popular Bantayan Island activity that allows you to explore the different islands and enjoy different activities like coral reef snorkelling, swimming with whale sharks, and island hopping.
  • Beach Camping – A perfect activity to experience Bantayan Island’s natural beauty and tranquillity. You can enjoy a night at the beach with a campfire, a tasty barbecue, under the stars and under the moonlight.
  • Sunset Sailing – This is one of the best ways to watch the sunset on Bantayan Island. You can rent a boat and sail around the island while you enjoy the breathtaking sunset and the beautiful island scenery.

Food and Drink Scene of Bantayan

Must-Try Dishes

  • Tinolang Manok – A traditional Filipino chicken soup enjoyed with rice or noodles.
  • Puso – A steamed rice cake wrapped in a banana leaf and is a popular local delicacy.
  • Lechon – A famous roasted or grilled pig that is sliced and served with spicy vinegar.

Local Beverages

  • Tuba wine – locally made coconut wine which is fermented from coconut sap.
  • Buko Juice – Freshly squeezed coconut water that is both refreshing, hydrating, and delicious.

Where to Stay in Bantayan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bantayan Island offers a wide range of lodging options to suit different budgets, ranging from backpacker hostels to high-end resorts.

  • St. Bernard Beach Resort – a beachfront resort in Sta. Fe, offering peaceful and comfortable accommodation and luxury amenities including a restaurant, bar, and swimming pool.
  • Bantayan Cottages – one of the most favourite budget guesthouses on the island. It offers fully air-conditioned rooms with private bathrooms and a beautiful garden.

What to Buy in Bantayan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Bantayan Handicrafts – handmade crafts made from local materials, including handmade baskets, mats, bags, and hats.
  • Puso Handicrafts – Crafted from a combination of natural and recycled materials, Puso handicrafts offer hats, bags, and coasters that carry the distinct Bantayan culture.

Local Culture and Customs of Bantayan

Bantayan Island has retained its unique culture and customs as exemplified in its annual fiesta celebration. Locals wear traditional clothes and celebrate with street dancing and colourful parades.

Best Time to Visit Bantayan

The best time to visit and enjoy Bantayan’s popular beaches and other outdoor activities is from December to May. This period typically features warm and sunny weather with only a small chance of rainfall.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bantayan

Do:

  • Respect Bantayan’s culture and customs.
  • Use reef-friendly sunscreens since many natural attractions here depend on the balance of their ecosystems.
  • Take precautions to protect the island’s natural resources by practising sustainable tourism.

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave your trash while enjoying Bantayan’s outdoor activities and tourist destinations.
  • Damage natural resources while swimming or snorkelling in Bantayan’s pristine waters.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bantayan

Bantayan Island is a generally safe and secure tourist destination, be sure to keep your valuable belongings secure and locked away in your hotel or any destination you are visiting.

Health in Bantayan

Bantayan Island has a small medical facility to cater to ordinary health needs, but for severe cases or for chronic treatments, going to mainland Cebu for medical care is the better option.

Getting to Bantayan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bantayan

  • Mactan International Airport – a 3-hour drive that features major international airlines and national service providers, it is the most popular airport to reach Bantayan Island.

Bus Stations Near Bantayan

  • Cebu City North Bus Terminal – the major bus terminal located in Cebu, which allows easy access to any destination in the Visayas region through different bus service providers.

Other Options

  • Ferry Services – The ferry service is a popular option and has daily schedules that depart from Cebu City to Bantayan Island, which takes you across the ocean to Santa Fe Wharf in Bantayan Island.

How to Get Around in Bantayan

You can rent motorbikes, bicycles, or tricycles whenever you want to explore the island. Tricycles are the most common transport as it can easily fit tourists and locals for a maximum of three (3).

Where to Go Next from Bantayan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bantayan Island has several neighbouring locations that you should consider including in your itinerary. Some of the most popular spots nearby include:

  • Malapascua Island – Known as one of the best diving spots in the Philippines, this island is less than two hours away from Bantayan Island.
  • Cebu City – one of the biggest cities in the Philippines and a popular tourist destination that is just a couple of hours drive away from Bantayan Island.

Conclusion

Bantayan Island in the Philippines is an excellent holiday destination that offers beautiful beaches, lush greenery, and a laid-back atmosphere, suitable for relaxation and outdoor adventure. With this comprehensive travel guide, we hope that you now have all the information you need to plan a memorable trip to Bantayan Island.
