Santander (Cebu) is a town located in the province of Cebu, Philippines, prized for its rustic atmosphere and stunning seaside views. It is a must-visit destination for travelers who want to experience the charm of a typical Philippine town.

Introduction to Santander (Cebu)

Santander is a small town in the southwestern side of Cebu Island. The town is known for its beautiful beaches, including the famous Sumilon Island, which is just a few kilometers away from the town proper. The town is known for its Lambayok Festival which showcases the local fishing industry and the unique way of catching fish using bamboo rafts and hand lines.

List of Places to Visit in Santander (Cebu)

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

A trip to Santander is not complete without visiting the historical sites that the town has to offer. One of the most visited historical sites is the Liloan Lighthouse, built during the Spanish occupation and still stands today. The town’s Municipal Hall is a well-preserved building reflecting the municipality’s heritage and culture.

Natural Attractions of Santander (Cebu): Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Santander is a nature lover’s paradise. Here are the must-visit natural attractions in Santander:

  • Sumilon Island - A pristine island surrounded by crystal-clear water. The island is a paradise for snorkeling enthusiasts.
  • Boljoon Marine Sanctuary - This protected area has beautiful coral reefs, colorful schools of fish, and is perfect for diving and snorkeling.
  • Osmeña Peak - The highest peak in Cebu, it is a popular hiking destination to witness the sunrise or sunset.
  • Binalayan Hidden Falls - A hidden gem in Santander, this stunning waterfall is a must-visit spot for anyone wanting to escape the urban jungle.
  • The Cuartel Ruins - The ruins of this historic building is a significant location in the Philippine revolution and a perfect place for taking panoramic shots.

Best Things to Do in Santander (Cebu): Activities and Tours

Santander offers visitors a wide range of activities to experience, from island hopping, scuba diving, hiking, and more. Here are the top things to do in Santander:

  • Snorkeling and diving in Sumilon Island - With crystal clear waters, you can marvel at the beauty of the marine life in the area, including the famous thresher sharks.
  • Visit the whale watching destination of Oslob
  • Hike the Osmeña Peak - A 30-minute climb to the highest peak in Cebu for stunning sunrise or sunset views.
  • Enjoy Sabu Beach - A popular beach getaway, with powder-fine sand and pristine waters perfect for swimming and sunbathing.
  • Explore Boljoon Marine Sanctuary - Take a dive and discover the colorful marine life beneath the waters of Boljoon, or go kayaking around the area.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Santander (Cebu)

Santander’s outdoor activities include some of the best hiking and biking trails in Cebu. Here are some of the top scenic routes to take:

  • The Osmeña Peak Trail - Perfect for a good hike and a scenic sunrise or sunset view.
  • The Boljoon Trail - A route that allows bikers and hikers to enjoy a scenic view of Boljoon Marine Sanctuary
  • The Exploration Trail of Liloan - A scenic route that explores the beauty of Liloan town, with its rich history and panoramic views.

Week-Long Itinerary for Santander (Cebu)

For those who want to have a meaningful and exciting experience in Santander, here is a week-long itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival at Santander, check-in at your chosen accommodation Day 2: Explore Osmeña Peak and Sabu Beach Day 3: Island hopping and swimming in Sumilon Island Day 4: Biking and hiking in Liloan trail Day 5: Explore Boljoon Marine Sanctuary and Boljoon Church Day 6: Try the popular whale watching in Oslob Day 7: Depart from Santander

Food and Drink Scene of Santander (Cebu)

Hunger is not a problem in Santander with its delicious menus and refreshing drinks. Here are the must-try dishes and beverages of Santander:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lechon Cebu - The famous roasted pig of Cebu
  • Kinilaw (raw fish in vinegar and spices) - a popular appetizer in Cebu and a must-try for fish lovers.
  • Puso - This sticky rice is often served as an accompaniment for grilled fish or meat.
  • Guinamos - A local appetizer made of fermented shrimp paste and seasoned with garlic, lemon, and chili.

Local Beverages

  • Tuba - A refreshing coconut wine
  • Calamansi Juice - A freshly squeezed drink using a sweet-tart Philippine Lime
  • San Mig Light - A loved light beer in the Philippines

Where to Stay in Santander (Cebu): Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Santander offers a lot of accommodation options. Here are some of the top choices in Santander:

  • Bluewater Sumilon Island Resort - A luxurious resort on Sumilon Island
  • Maayo Beach Resorts - A family-friendly resort near Sabu Beach
  • Eden Resort - An eco-friendly resort on the beachfront of Barangay Liloan.

What to Buy in Santander (Cebu): Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Santander is known for its unique and locally made products. Souvenir items include woven baskets made from bamboo, beach shirts, and dresses with intricate embroidery, and dried fish.

Local Culture and Customs of Santander (Cebu)

Santander is a predominantly Catholic town, with traditional fiestas celebrated throughout the year. The Lambayok Festival is a significant event, where the local fishermen showcase their unique way of catching fish using bamboo rafts and hand lines.

Best Time to Visit Santander (Cebu)

The best time to visit Santander is from November to May when the weather is dry and sunny. The peak season is from December to January, so it would be best to book your accommodation early.

Do’s and Don’ts of Santander (Cebu)

Before visiting Santander, here are some tips to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Keep yourself hydrated at all times, as the weather can be humid.
  • Respect the locals and their culture, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Support the town’s local industries, such as fishing and weaving.

Don’t:

  • Litter on the streets or beaches.
  • Swim beyond the safety markers in Sumilon Island due to strong currents.
  • Disrespect the town’s cultural traditions and legacy.

Safety and Health

Safety in Santander (Cebu)

Santander is a relatively safe place to visit, with a low crime rate and friendly locals. Visitors are advised to take standard precautions when going out into the town at night.

Health in Santander (Cebu)

It is important to bring your own basic first aid kit. There are small clinics in Santander but it is highly advised to visit health centers in Cebu City for any emergency needs.

Getting to Santander (Cebu): Transportation Options and Logistics

Santander is accessible via land and sea transportation from Cebu City.

Airports Near Santander (Cebu)

Mactan-Cebu International Airport is the nearest airport to Santander, which is about a 3-hour drive away.

Bus Stations Near Santander (Cebu)

There are two major bus stations in Cebu City that have buses going to Santander: South Bus Terminal and Cebu South Coastal Road Terminal. The trip takes around 3 to 4 hours.

Other Options

Visitors can also opt for private transportation, rent-a-car, or a van to travel to Santander.

How to Get Around in Santander (Cebu)

Santander is a small town that can be explored by foot or tricycle. There are also jeepneys that go around the town and to neighboring towns.

Where to Go Next from Santander (Cebu): Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Santander’s proximity to neighboring towns and cities makes it an excellent jump-off point for exploring other destinations. Here some of the popular nearby destinations:

  • Cebu City, the nearest city in Cebu Island, is known for its rich history, distinctive culture, and exciting nightlife.
  • Oslob, a famous destination famous for its whale shark watching tours, located just an hour away from Santander.
  • Dumaguete City, one of the most popular cities in Negros Oriental also known for its university town atmosphere, artisanal food scene, and natural attractions.

Conclusion

Santander stands out among the many beautiful destinations in Cebu. With its breathtaking natural wonders, rich history, friendly locals, and unique culture, it is undoubtedly one of the must-visit destinations in the Philippines. Plan your trip to Santander now and be immersed in its beauty and charm.
