A Comprehensive Travel Guide to Koh Rung Sanloem

Koh Rung Sanloem is a paradise island located in Cardamom and Elephant Mountains, in Cambodia, in Southeast Asia, in Asia. Known for its pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters, lush forests, and vibrant marine life, this tropical gem is a popular destination among travelers who seek tranquility, adventure, and authentic local experiences.

Plan your trip to Koh Rung Sanloem with this comprehensive travel guide, featuring must-visit attractions, top activities, food & drink recommendations, transportation options, and much more!

Introduction to Koh Rung Sanloem

Koh Rung Sanloem is a small island located in the Gulf of Thailand, about 25 kilometers off the coast of Sihanoukville, Cambodia. The island is approximately 9 square kilometers in size, and its population consists of a few hundred locals and a growing number of tourists.

The island has two main villages, which are M’Pay Bay, located on the northern part of the island, and Saracen Bay, located on the southern part of the island. M’Pay Bay is a quiet fishing village with a few guesthouses, while Saracen Bay is the most popular tourist destination on the island, featuring a wide range of accommodation options, restaurants, bars, and activities.

List of Places to Visit in Koh Rung Sanloem

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

While Koh Rung Sanloem is predominantly a natural attraction, there are a few cultural and historical sites that are worth visiting, including:

  • Wat Koh Rung Sanloem: A traditional Buddhist temple located on the northern part of the island, surrounded by lush greenery and offering stunning panoramic views of the island and the sea.

Natural Attractions of Koh Rung Sanloem: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Koh Rung Sanloem is renowned for its natural beauty, and there are plenty of outdoor activities and scenic spots to explore, such as:

  • Lazy Beach: A secluded beach located on the southern part of the island, accessible through a short hike through the forest. The beach offers spectacular views, crystal-clear waters, and a tranquil atmosphere.
  • Saracen Bay Beach: The most popular beach on the island, featuring a long stretch of white sand and turquoise waters, surrounded by lush palm trees and beachside bars.
  • M’Pai Bay: A traditional Cambodian fishing village located on the northern part of the island, offering authentic local experiences, fresh seafood, and stunning sunsets.
  • Koh Rung Sanloem Lighthouse: Located on the southern part of the island, this historic lighthouse offers breathtaking views of the sea, the island, and the surrounding landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Koh Rung Sanloem

Koh Rung Sanloem is an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts, and there are plenty of hiking and biking trails to discover, such as:

  • Jungle Trekking: The island’s lush forests offer excellent opportunities for jungle trekking, with several hiking trails available, including the one leading to Lazy Beach.
  • Biking Around the Island: Rent a bike and explore the island on two wheels, along the scenic coastline, through rural villages, and into the heart of the forest.

Best Things to Do in Koh Rung Sanloem: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, there are plenty of other activities and tours to experience on Koh Rung Sanloem. Some of the must-try options are:

  • Snorkeling and Diving: The island is home to rich marine life, colorful coral reefs, and crystal-clear waters, making it a prime spot for snorkeling and diving adventures.
  • Kayaking and Paddleboarding: Rent a kayak or a paddleboard and explore the island’s hidden coves, mangrove forests, and stunning beaches.
  • Island-Hopping Tours: Discover the nearby islands of Koh Rung, Koh Ta Kiev, and Koh Thmei, offering pristine beaches, lush forests, and diverse wildlife.

Food and Drink Scene of Koh Rung Sanloem

Koh Rung Sanloem boasts a rich culinary scene, with a variety of restaurants, bars, and street food stalls offering both local and international cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Amok: A traditional Cambodian curry made with fish, coconut milk, and an array of spices.
  • Lort Cha: A stir-fried rice noodle dish with vegetables, eggs, and a tangy sauce.
  • Fresh Seafood: Koh Rung Sanloem is famous for its fresh crabs, prawns, and fish, which can be grilled, boiled, or served in a variety of styles.

Local Beverages

  • Angkor Beer: A popular Cambodian beer, available in most bars and restaurants on the island.
  • Coconut Water: A refreshing drink served in freshly cut coconuts, available on most beaches and street stalls.

Where to Stay in Koh Rung Sanloem: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are numerous accommodation options on Koh Rung Sanloem, ranging from basic backpacker hostels to luxurious beachfront resorts. Some of the popular choices are:

  • Saracen Bay Resort: A beautiful beachfront resort offering spacious rooms, an infinity pool, and panoramic views of the sea.
  • Mad Monkey Hostel: A popular backpacker hostel located on Saracen Bay, featuring dorms and private rooms, a vibrant bar, and regular social events.
  • Robinson Bungalows: A rustic bungalow complex located on Lazy Beach, offering simple rooms, stunning views, and a peaceful atmosphere.

What to Buy in Koh Rung Sanloem: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Koh Rung Sanloem has a limited shopping scene, and most of the souvenirs and local products can be found at small shops and street stalls on the beaches. Some of the recommended items to buy are:

  • Handmade Jewelry: Locally made bracelets, necklaces, and earrings, featuring colorful beads and natural stones.
  • Cambodian Scarves: Beautifully woven silk and cotton scarves, in various colors and patterns.
  • Fisherman Pants: Traditional Cambodian pants made of light cotton, available in bright colors and comfortable designs.

Local Culture and Customs of Koh Rung Sanloem

Koh Rung Sanloem is a predominantly Buddhist island, and visitors are advised to respect local customs and traditions, such as:

  • Dressing Modestly: When visiting temples or other cultural sites, it’s recommended to wear conservative clothing that covers the shoulders, chest, and knees.
  • Removing Shoes: It’s customary to remove shoes before entering temples, homes, and some businesses.
  • Respecting Monks: Monks are highly respected in Cambodian culture, and visitors should avoid touching or interrupting them.

Best Time to Visit Koh Rung Sanloem

The best time to visit Koh Rung Sanloem is during the dry season, which runs from November to May, when the weather is sunny, warm, and with limited rainfall. The peak season is from December to February, when the island gets crowded with tourists, and prices may be higher.

Safety and Health

Safety in Koh Rung Sanloem

Koh Rung Sanloem is a generally safe destination for travelers, but visitors are advised to take precautions, such as:

  • Avoid Walking Alone at Night
  • Keep Valuables Secure
  • Stay Hydrated and Protected from the Sun

Health in Koh Rung Sanloem

Visitors should be aware of the health risks in Koh Rung Sanloem, such as:

  • Mosquito-Borne Diseases: Dengue fever and Malaria are prevalent in some areas, and visitors should take precautions, such as using mosquito repellent and wearing long-sleeved clothing.
  • Food and Water Safety: To avoid food poisoning and other gastrointestinal illnesses, visitors should stick to bottled water and avoid street food.

Getting to Koh Rung Sanloem: Transportation Options and Logistics

Koh Rung Sanloem is accessible only by boat, and there are several options for transportation from Sihanoukville, such as:

Airports Near Koh Rung Sanloem

  • Sihanoukville International Airport (KOS): The nearest airport, with daily flights to and from Phnom Penh, Siem Reap, and other domestic and international destinations.

Bus Stations Near Koh Rung Sanloem

  • Sihanoukville Bus Station (Central): The main bus station in Sihanoukville, with regular services to Phnom Penh, Siem Reap, and other destinations in Cambodia.

Other Options

  • Private Taxi or Transfer: Visitors can arrange a private taxi or transfer from Sihanoukville to the pier, and then take a ferry or a speedboat to Koh Rung Sanloem.
  • Ferry or Speedboat: Several ferry and speedboat companies operate regular services from Sihanoukville to Koh Rung Sanloem, with various schedules, prices, and durations.

How to Get Around in Koh Rung Sanloem

The best way to get around Koh Rung Sanloem is on foot or by bicycle. There are no cars or motorbikes on the island, and the roads are mostly dirt tracks or footpaths. Alternatively, visitors can hire a tuk-tuk or a local taxi for longer distances or to transport luggage.

Where to Go Next from Koh Rung Sanloem: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Koh Rung Sanloem is a great base for exploring the nearby islands and destinations, such as:

  • Sihanoukville: The nearest mainland city, with a range of attractions, including beaches, markets, and restaurants.
  • Koh Rung: The sister island of Koh Rung Sanloem, located about 20 minutes by boat, offering similar attractions and experiences.
  • Koh Thmei: A pristine nature reserve located on the mainland, famous for its diverse birdlife, sea turtles, and saltwater crocodiles.

Conclusion

Koh Rung Sanloem is a true tropical paradise, offering pristine beaches, lush forests, vibrant marine life, and authentic local experiences. With this comprehensive travel guide, visitors can plan their itinerary, explore the island’s attractions, and immerse themselves in the culture and traditions of Cambodia.
