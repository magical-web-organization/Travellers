Introduction to Kawthaung

Kawthaung is a small town in Southeastern Myanmar that sits at the southernmost tip of the Tanintharyi Region. Perched along the serene Andaman Sea, this picturesque town is known for its vibrant culture, breathtaking natural attractions, and exciting adventures. Despite being a lesser-known destination, Kawthaung is a hidden gem that offers a unique taste of Myanmar’s diverse beauty.

List of Places to Visit in Kawthaung

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. U Nar Auk Monastery: This thousand-year-old monastery is a must-visit attraction, known for its stunning architectures, intricate wood carvings, and tranquil surroundings.

  2. King Bayinnaung’s Palace: Built-in the 16th century, King Bayinnaung’s Palace is a historical site that reflects Myanmar’s rich cultural heritage, as well as showcasing the magnificent sculpture works of ancient Myanmar craftsmen.

  3. Kawthaung Market: This bustling bazaar is a cultural hub where you can get a taste of authentic Myanmar life. Tourists can find everything from traditional Burmese handicrafts, fabrics, and souvenirs to fresh produce and local street food.

Natural Attractions of Kawthaung: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Victoria Cliff: Victoria Cliff is a magnificent limestone formation that overlooks the breathtaking view of the Andaman Sea. Tourists can trek up the cliff and enjoy the scenic views of the beaches and the lush greenery of the surrounding nature reserve.

  2. Maliwn Waterfall: This beautiful waterfall located deep in the jungle is a hidden gem and provides an unforgettable experience of the jungle trekking trails, and unique natural rock formations.

  3. Lagoon Bay: This secluded paradise is a must-visit beach, pristine turquoise waters, and stunning white sandy beach.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Kawthaung

  1. Base Camp for Mt. Victoria: Mt. Victoria is one of the highest mountains in Myanmar, and the base camp is an excellent site for trekking, hiking, and rock climbing. The scenic view of the mountain will take your breath away, and it’s a perfect spot for those seeking an adventure amidst natural beauty.

  2. Island Hopping: Kawthaung’s surrounding islands are a perfect destination for those who love outdoor activities and watersports. Visitors can hike around stunning islands and beaches, or snorkel in the turquoise waters of the Andaman Sea.

Best Things to Do in Kawthaung: Activities and Tours

  1. Snorkeling and Diving: Kawthaung’s coral reef is one of the most diverse and beautiful in Asia, making it an ideal destination for snorkeling and diving. The area offers a wealth of opportunities to explore the underwater world and encounter incredible marine life.

  2. River Safari: Take a riverboat safari through the wildlife-rich mangrove forests that line the banks of the Salween River, which is home to some of the most unique wildlife and bird species in Myanmar.

Week-Long Itinerary for Kawthaung

Day 1: Explore Historical Places Day 2: Scenic Mountain Hiking and Trekking Day 3: Jungle trekking and waterfall visit Day 4: Island hopping, beach activities, and water sports Day 5: River Safari and bird watching Day 6: Discover Kawthaung Market and local food vendors Day 7: Relax and enjoy the stunning white sandy beach of Lagoon Bay

Food and Drink Scene of Kawthaung

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Mohinga: This is Myanmar’s national dish and is a breakfast staple, consisting of a fragrant fish broth, noodles, and a mix of delicious herbs and spices.

  2. Tea-Leaf Salad: Tea-leaf salad is a traditional Burmese dish made with fermented tea leaves, onions, garlic, peanuts, sesame seeds, and dried shrimp.

Local Beverages

  1. Myanmar Beer: Myanmar Beer is the most popular beer in the country and is a refreshing drink that’s perfect for cooling off during hot weather.

  2. Burmese Wine: Myanmar is home to some of the finest grape varieties, making Burmese wine a must-try for wine enthusiasts.

Where to Stay in Kawthaung: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Victoria Cliff Hotel & Resort: This spectacular hotel has a fantastic location overlooking the Andaman Sea, with comfortable rooms, an outdoor pool, and fantastic dining options.

  2. Grand Andaman Hotel: This luxury hotel is a perfect base camp for travelers who want to explore the natural beauty of the area, with rooms featuring stunning ocean views and excellent food options.

What to Buy in Kawthaung: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Local handicrafts: Kawthaung’s handmade arts and crafts, including exquisite wood carvings, hand-woven fabrics, and silverware, are perfect for those interested in local handicrafts.

  2. Morinda Citrifolia: Morinda Citrifolia is a tropical fruit that’s commonly used in traditional Burmese medicine to boost immunity and health and can make a unique souvenir for those interested in traditional medicine.

Local Culture and Customs of Kawthaung

Myanmar is a country with a long and rich history and a deeply ingrained culture. Respect for customs and traditions is essential in Myanmar, including respecting religious sites and customs. Visitors are advised to dress modestly and remove shoes before entering temples and pagodas.

Best Time to Visit Kawthaung

Kawthaung can be an enjoyable destination at any time of the year, but the best time to visit is between November and March, when the weather is dry, and the temperature is cooler and more comfortable for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Kawthaung

Do:

  1. Engage with locals and learn about their culture and traditions.
  2. Carry adequate sun protection, insect repellent, and bottled water when you go on outdoor adventures.

Don’t:

  1. Wear shoes or hats inside temples and pagodas.
  2. Take pictures of monks without permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Kawthaung

Kawthaung is generally a safe and secure destination for tourists, but visitors are advised to stay alert and cautious while exploring the area, especially at night. Take basic precautions like not wandering alone in secluded areas, and only taking licensed taxis.

Health in Kawthaung

For international travelers, a yellow fever vaccination is recommended. Ensure to carry a medical kit and drink only bottled water.

Getting to Kawthaung: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Kawthaung

Kawthaung Airport (KAW) is the nearest airport to the city center, and it offers daily flights to Yangon, the capital city.

Bus Stations Near Kawthaung

The central bus station in Kawthaung is the main hub for long-distance buses to other cities and towns in Myanmar.

Other Options

Tourists can also get to Kawthaung by sea and travel on a ferry from Ranong, Thailand.

How to Get Around in Kawthaung

Visitors can travel around Kawthaung by using a taxi, local tuk-tuks, or hiring a motorbike to explore the surrounding areas more in-depth.

Where to Go Next from Kawthaung: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can head north to the charming town of Myeik or visit the beautiful islands of Phuket or Krabi in Thailand, only a short boat ride away.

Conclusion

Kawthaung is a charming town that offers visitors a unique experience of Myanmar’s diverse natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. From stunning cliffs, beautiful waterfalls, and pristine beaches, to exotic local delicacies and vibrant markets, there is something for everyone in Kawthaung. Use this guide to plan your next adventure to this hidden gem in Southeast Asia and get ready to explore the wonders of Myanmar.
