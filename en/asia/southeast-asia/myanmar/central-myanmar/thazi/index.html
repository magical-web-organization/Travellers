Thazi is a hidden gem in Central Myanmar, located in the Mandalay Division. Surrounded by misty mountains and expansive rice fields, Thazi offers stunning natural beauty, cultural attractions, and outdoor adventures. In this comprehensive travel guide, we will take you through the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, eat, and more while in Thazi.

Introduction to Thazi

Thazi is a peaceful town that offers a unique peek into the traditional daily life of Myanmar. It is best known for its charming hillside location, picturesque landscapes, and ancient buildings. Its quiet atmosphere and friendly people make it a great destination for those who want to escape the hustle and bustle of the major tourist spots in Myanmar.

List of Places to Visit in Thazi

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Shwe Let War Pagoda: This stunning golden pagoda is located on a hillside overlooking the Thazi town. It is a popular spot for locals and visitors alike to visit.
  • Shwe Kyaung: It is a beautiful wooden monastery with intricate carvings and a vibrant atmosphere. Make sure to visit during early morning meditation sessions.
  • Myin Ma Hti Cave: This religious site is home to ancient murals and Buddha images, located inside a cave. It is a must-visit for history buffs.

Natural Attractions of Thazi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Thazi Forest Reserve: It is a protected area that offers a jungle-like atmosphere. Visitors can go on a trek while enjoying the flora and fauna.
  • Thaung Ta Man Lake: The misty surrounding and serene atmosphere of the lake make for a perfect picnic spot for visitors seeking to unwind.
  • Danu Mountain Range: This sprawling mountain range is a haven for hikers and nature enthusiasts seeking adventure and breathtaking views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Thazi

  • Thazi to Kyauk Se: It is a moderate 40 km route that offers some stunning views of the surrounding area.
  • Thazi to Taunggyi: It is a challenging 80 km route that is ideal for experienced cyclists.
  • Thazi to Nyaung Shwe: This 110 km route passes through lush green tea plantations and ancient monasteries in the Inle Lake area.

Best Things to Do in Thazi: Activities and Tours

  • Hot Air Balloon Ride: Experience a bird’s eye view of the town and its surroundings from up above.
  • Horse Carriage Ride: Take in the town’s peaceful atmosphere while enjoying a comfortable ride in a traditional horse carriage.
  • Traditional Craftsmanship Tour: Observe local artisans display their skills in pottery, weaving, and woodcarving in the town’s workshops.

Week-Long Itinerary for Thazi

Day Itinerary
1 Visit Shwe Let War Pagoda and take a Horse Carriage Ride around Thazi town
2 Trek around Thazi Forest Reserve or visit Myin Ma Hti Cave
3 Relax by Thaung Ta Man Lake or opt for a Hot Air Balloon Ride
4 Visit Shwe Kyaung and take part in morning meditation sessions
5 Hike up to the Danu Mountain Range
6 Explore the nearby village life and traditional crafts
7 Biking from Thazi to Nyaung Shwe through the tea plantations and ancient monasteries

Food and Drink Scene of Thazi

Must-Try Dishes

  • Ohn No Khauk Swe: A commonly found traditional dish in Myanmar, it is a creamy coconut curry served with egg noodles, chicken, and various condiments.
  • Pa Zun Chin: It is a spicy bamboo shoot soup made with fish, vegetables, and a mix of traditional spices.
  • Myanmar Tea: A hot and refreshing tea that is a staple drink in the country.

Local Beverages

  • Shan Wine: Made from rice, it is a sweet and potent wine that is a hit with the locals.
  • Mandalay Beer: It is a classic beer in Myanmar that goes well with local dishes.
  • Laphet Yay: Made from fermented tea leaves, it is a local favorite with a zesty and tangy taste.

Where to Stay in Thazi: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Royal Parkview Hotel: A luxurious hotel that offers scenic views from the hotel rooms and a relaxing spa after an adventurous day.
  • Shwe Taung Tan Guest House: A budget-friendly guesthouse that offers clean and comfortable lodging for budget travelers.
  • Golden Sunrise Resort: A peaceful resort that offers traditional Myanmar-style villas.

What to Buy in Thazi: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Traditional Longyi: A versatile traditional skirt worn by both men and women in Myanmar made from soft cotton or silk.
  • Handwoven Baskets: Made by skilled artisans, these baskets come in various shapes and sizes and make for great souvenirs or practical buy.
  • Marionettes: Handcrafted puppets that depict various Myanmar culture and costume, making them a colorful and unique souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Thazi

  • Myanmar is a predominantly Buddhist country; therefore, visitors are expected to dress respectfully while visiting religious sites.
  • Remove footwear while entering place of worship, private homes, and offices.
  • It is common to greet locals with a “Mingalaba” or “Hello” when you meet them.

Best Time to Visit Thazi

Thazi’s climate is dominated by tropical wet/dry seasons. November to February is the ideal time to visit Thazi, as this season provides a perfect balance of sunshine and rain. The day and nighttime temperature are mild, making it great for outdoor adventures.

Do’s and Don’ts of Thazi

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and traditions.
  • Learn a few words of Myanmar to communicate with locals.
  • Try local cuisine to experience the unique flavors of Myanmar.

Don’t:

  • Do not litter, as it a significant offense in Myanmar.
  • Do not touch Buddha images or monks without permission.
  • Do not wear revealing clothing while visiting religious sites.

Safety and Health

Safety in Thazi

Thazi is a safe town, and tourists very rarely face any major safety issues. However, we advise travelers to be cautious while moving alone at night or carrying valuables to avoid any inconvenience successfully.

Health in Thazi

It is recommended to have vaccinations for Typhoid, Hepatitis A and B, and Japanese Encephalitis at least a month before visiting Myanmar. It is best to carry mosquito repellents to avoid getting bitten by them and contract mosquito-borne diseases like dengue, malaria, and Zika virus.

Getting to Thazi: Transportation Options and Logistics

Thazi is located in the Mandalay Division, and the nearest international airport is Mandalay airport. Below are the different transportation options to reach Thazi:

Airports Near Thazi

  • Mandalay International Airport (MDL): 139 km (3.5 hours on road)

Bus Stations Near Thazi

  • Mandalay Central Bus Station: 129 km (3 hours)
  • Taunggyi Bus Station: 73 km (2 hours)

Other Options

  • Taxis and private cars are easily available from the airport or nearby towns to reach Thazi.

How to Get Around in Thazi

Rental bikes, taxis, and horse carriages are the best options to get around Thazi. The town is relatively small, and most destinations can be covered on foot, allowing tourists to appreciate the local surroundings while exploring the town.

Where to Go Next from Thazi: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Inle Lake: Famous for its floating villages, stilt houses, and Bohemian vibe, it is a great destination for visitors to explore less touristy areas.
  • Popa National Park: Known for its stunning landscape and scenic views, this site is easily accessible from Thazi as it is located only 120 km away.
  • Bagan: The ancient capital of Myanmar boasts over 3000 temples and a rich cultural heritage. It is easily accessible from Thazi and is only 180 km away.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Thazi is a small town with plenty of hidden gems to discover. It offers a great blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and adventurous activities. We hope this comprehensive travel guide to Thazi has given you a good idea of what to expect from this awe-inspiring destination.
