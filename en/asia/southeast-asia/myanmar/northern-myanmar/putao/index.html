Introduction to Putao

Putao is a town located in the Northern part of Myanmar. It is well-known for its scenic beauty and adventurous outdoor activities. Putao is surrounded by mountains and forests, making it a perfect destination for nature lovers.

List of Places to Visit in Putao

Whether it is a historical monument or a natural wonder, Putao is filled with places to visit that cater to all tastes. Here are some of the most popular:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Putao Cultural Museum
  • Fort Hertz
  • Machanbaw Stupa
  • Kachin Baptist Church

Natural Attractions of Putao: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Phongan Razi
  • Malikha River
  • Naung Yah Village
  • Snow Leopard Trail

Hiking/Biking Routes of Putao

Putao is a hiker’s paradise with a myriad of hiking routes that will take you through ancient forests, across terraced rice fields, and up stunning mountain peaks. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • Mount Phongan Razi Loop
  • Namkhan River Trek
  • Ziyard River Trek
  • Rawanbargyi Trek

Best Things to Do in Putao: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking, there are multiple other exciting and engaging activities to immerse yourself in. Here are some of the top things to do in Putao:

  • Whitewater rafting on the Malikha River
  • Mountain biking through forest trails
  • Birdwatching in the Naung Yah village
  • Visiting Putao’s tea plantations

Week-Long Itinerary for Putao

Here’s a suggested itinerary for a week-long trip to Putao:

Day Activity
Day 1 Arrival in Putao, check-in at the hotel, and visit Machanbaw Stupa
Day 2 Visit the Putao Cultural Museum and Fort Hertz
Day 3 Trek along the Namkhan River
Day 4 Visit the tea plantations and experience the tea-making process
Day 5 Whitewater rafting on the Malikha River
Day 6 Trek along the Ziyard River
Day 7 Departure from Putao

Food and Drink Scene of Putao

Putao’s food scene is diverse and infused with flavor. The town is well-known for its unique cuisine, which combines Indian, Chinese, and Southeast Asian flavors. Here are some must-try dishes:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kachin-style fermented tea leaf salad
  • Kachin-style steamed fish
  • Shan-style chicken or pork curry

Local Beverages

  • Cane juice
  • Tea

Where to Stay in Putao: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Putao offers a range of accommodations, ranging from luxurious hotels to budget-friendly hostels. Here are some options:

  • Malikha Lodge
  • Putao Trekking House
  • Nam Lang Kharai Guesthouse
  • Little Bagan Guesthouse

What to Buy in Putao: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Putao has a vibrant marketplace offering an array of local products to bring home. Here are some souvenirs to consider buying:

  • Traditional Kachin fabrics and textiles
  • Handmade jewelry
  • Local tea

Local Culture and Customs of Putao

The predominant ethnic group in Putao is the Kachin, a hill tribe with a rich cultural history. The Kachin are known for their distinct music, dance, and dress. Visitors are encouraged to learn about their culture and customs, including their unique bamboo and wooden houses.

Best Time to Visit Putao

The best time to visit Putao is from October to March, which is the dry season and offers optimal outdoor activities. The weather is cool and crisp, and there will be fewer mosquitoes compared to the wet season, which runs from June to September.

Do’s and Don’ts of Putao

Do:

  • Dress modestly when visiting temples and cultural landmarks
  • Try the local food and beverages
  • Respect the locals and their customs

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local religious sentiments
  • Litter or harm the environment
  • Visit unauthorized areas or go on an unapproved trek

Safety and Health

Safety in Putao

Putao is a safe destination for visitors. However, it is essential to be aware of your surroundings and take necessary precautions when hiking through narrow and steep trails.

Health in Putao

It is advisable to take malaria prophylaxis before reaching Putao. Visitors must carry essential medications, including antibiotics and anti-diarrheal medicine, and consult their doctor before traveling.

Getting to Putao: Transportation Options and Logistics

Putao is an isolated town in northern Myanmar, and there are limited transportation options. Here are a few ways to get to Putao:

Airports Near Putao

  • Putao Airport

Bus Stations Near Putao

  • Buses leave from Yangon and Mandalay, but it can be a long and tiring journey.

Other Options

  • Private car hire is possible but expensive, given the distance and terrain.

How to Get Around in Putao

Putao is a small town that can be easily covered on foot or by bicycle. However, the best way to explore the natural attractions and go on treks is to hire a local guide or book a tour company.

Where to Go Next from Putao: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Putao is a gateway to some of the most unexplored and untouched regions of Myanmar. Here are some nearby destinations to consider:

  • Naungmung Town
  • Myitkyina City
  • Bhamo Town

Conclusion

Putao offers a perfect blend of adventure, culture, and nature. Whether it is hiking through the forests, exploring ancient culture, or trying the local tea, Putao is sure to leave a lasting impression on visitors.
