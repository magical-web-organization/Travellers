Introduction to Ang Thong National Marine Park

Ang Thong National Marine Park is a beautiful archipelago located in the central Gulf Coast region of Southern Thailand. Spread across an area of 42 islands covering 102 square km, the park boasts of breathtaking natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and abundant marine and wildlife. Visitors can experience beautiful beaches, crystal clear waters, scenic vistas, and diverse flora and fauna.

Local Culture and Customs of Ang Thong National Marine Park

Ang Thong National Marine Park has a rich cultural heritage, influenced by Buddhism and the indigenous Thai people. Visitors are expected to respect the local culture and customs, including dressing modestly, removing shoes before entering temples, and being mindful of their behavior in public places.

Best Time to Visit Ang Thong National Marine Park

The best time to visit Ang Thong National Marine Park is between December and April, during the dry season when the weather is pleasant and the sea is calm. However, the park can be visited throughout the year, as there is always something to see and do in every season.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ang Thong National Marine Park

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Protect the environment by not littering
  • Follow designated trails when hiking and trekking

Don’t:

  • Touch or harm any wildlife
  • Remove any natural resources from the park
  • Ignore safety guidelines when participating in water activities

Safety and Health

Ang Thong National Marine Park is a safe destination for visitors. However, it is recommended to take some basic precautions to ensure a safe and healthy trip:

Safety in Ang Thong National Marine Park

  • Wear appropriate safety gear when participating in adventure activities
  • Stay on designated trails when hiking and trekking
  • Be mindful of your surroundings and avoid isolated areas

Health in Ang Thong National Marine Park

  • Pack essential medicines and medical supplies
  • Stay hydrated and avoid contact with stagnant water
  • Protect yourself from mosquito bites

How to Get Around in Ang Thong National Marine Park

The best way to get around Ang Thong National Marine Park is by boat. Many tour operators offer boat tours that cover popular attractions and activities in the park. However, visitors can also explore the park on foot, bike, or kayak.
