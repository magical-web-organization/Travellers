Introduction to Khok Kloi

Khok Kloi is a small town located in the Northern Andaman Coast of Phang Nga Province, Thailand. It is situated near Phang Nga Bay, which is surrounded by beautiful limestone cliffs and dotted with small islands. Khok Kloi is known for its peaceful ambiance, low-key vibe, and historic treasures. Also, it is an excellent base for exploring the nearby destinations in the province such as Phuket, Krabi and Khao Lak.

List of Places to Visit in Khok Kloi

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you are interested in culture and history, there are plenty of places to visit in Khok Kloi.

1. Tha Nun Temple

Tha Nun Temple is a historic temple that is over 150 years old, situated on a hill in Khok Kloi. The temple is known for its unique architecture and interior decor. Visitors can explore the temple grounds, admire the Buddhist statues, and enjoy the panoramic view of the surrounding area.

2. Khao Chang Nam Cave

Khao Chang Nam Cave is a significant archeological site that dates back to the prehistoric era. It consists of a series of limestone caves, stalactites and stalagmites, and ancient rock paintings. There are guided tours available, or visitors can explore on their own.

Natural Attractions of Khok Kloi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Khok Kloi is surrounded by stunning natural beauty, and there are plenty of outdoor activities and scenic spots for visitors to explore.

1. Phang Nga Bay

Phang Nga Bay is undoubtedly one of the most stunning natural attractions in Khok Kloi. It is famous for its dramatic limestone cliffs, clear waters, and small islands. Visitors can take a boat tour or kayak around the bay and explore some of the islands and hidden caves.

2. Sapam Bay

Sapam Bay is a quiet beach that is perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing. It is surrounded by coconut trees and has a peaceful ambiance. Visitors can rent chairs or umbrellas, and there are several bars and restaurants nearby.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Khok Kloi

Khok Kloi is an excellent destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. There are several trails and routes available for visitors.

1. Ton Prai Waterfall

Ton Prai Waterfall is a picturesque waterfall situated in Khao Lak-Lam Ru National Park. Visitors can hike through the park, admire the lush tropical vegetation and spot some of the local wildlife.

2. Khao Yao Mountain

Khao Yao Mountain is a popular hiking spot that offers stunning panoramic views of the surrounding area. The hike can be quite challenging, but it’s worth it for the breathtaking views from the top.

Best Things to Do in Khok Kloi: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of things to do in Khok Kloi, and visitors can choose from a wide range of activities and tours.

1. Thai Cooking Class

Learn how to cook Thai food and immerse yourself in the local culinary culture. Several cooking classes are available in Khok Kloi, and visitors can learn how to make traditional Thai dishes.

2. Visit a Local Market

Experience the local way of life by visiting a nearby market. Khok Kloi has several markets where visitors can buy fresh produce, local snacks, and souvenirs.

Week-Long Itinerary for Khok Kloi

Here is a suggested week-long itinerary for visitors to Khok Kloi:

Day 1: Visit Tha Nun Temple and Khao Chang Nam Cave Day 2: Take a boat tour of Phang Nga Bay Day 3: Relax on Sapam Bay Day 4: Hike to Ton Prai Waterfall Day 5: Participate in a Thai cooking class Day 6: Visit a local market Day 7: Explore Khao Yao Mountain

Food and Drink Scene of Khok Kloi

The food and drink scene in Khok Kloi is a mix of traditional Thai cuisine and international flavors. Visitors can indulge in local delicacies and try some of the regional specialties.

Must-Try Dishes

1. Khao Tom Mud

Khao Tom Mud is a sticky rice dish that is stuffed with various ingredients such as mushroom, chicken, or shrimp. It is then wrapped in banana leaves and steamed.

2. Tom Yum Goong

Tom Yum Goong is a spicy and sour soup that is made with shrimp, lemongrass, lime juice, and chili.

Local Beverages

1. Thai Iced Tea

Thai Iced Tea is a sweet and creamy drink that is made with black tea, milk, and sugar.

2. Singha Beer

Singha Beer is one of the most popular beers in Thailand. It is a crisp and refreshing lager that pairs well with spicy food.

Where to Stay in Khok Kloi: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several accommodation options available in Khok Kloi, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxury resorts.

Budget-Friendly

1. Khao Lak Palm Beach Resort

Khao Lak Palm Beach Resort is a beachfront property that offers comfortable rooms at an affordable price.

2. Khao Lak Ace Family Suite

Khao Lak Ace Family Suite is a family-friendly hotel that features spacious rooms, a swimming pool, and a restaurant.

Luxury

1. Le Menara North Khao Lak Resort

Le Menara North Khao Lak Resort is a luxurious resort that features private villas, a swimming pool, a spa, and several dining options.

2. The Sarojin

The Sarojin is a luxury resort that is surrounded by lush tropical gardens. It features a swimming pool, a spa, and a private beach.

What to Buy in Khok Kloi: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

There are several local products and souvenirs that visitors can buy in Khok Kloi, such as:

1. Handmade Crafts

Khok Kloi has several shops that specialize in handmade crafts such as pottery, wood carvings, and textiles.

2. Coconut Products

Coconut products are a local specialty in Khok Kloi. Visitors can buy coconut oil, coconut sugar, or coconut-based snacks.

Local Culture and Customs of Khok Kloi

The local culture in Khok Kloi is a mix of Thai and Malay influences. Visitors should dress modestly when visiting temples or other religious sites. Also, it is customary to remove shoes before entering someone’s home or a temple.

Best Time to Visit Khok Kloi

The best time to visit Khok Kloi is between November and February when the weather is cooler and drier.

Do’s and Don’ts of Khok Kloi

Do:

  • Try the local food and drink
  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites
  • Learn some basic Thai phrases

Don’t:

  • Engage in public displays of affection
  • Disrespect the monarchy or Buddha images
  • Overstay your visa

Safety and Health

Safety in Khok Kloi

Khok Kloi is a relatively safe place for visitors. However, it’s always essential to take precautions such as locking your valuables in a safe, avoiding carrying too much cash, and staying aware of your surroundings.

Health in Khok Kloi

Visitors should drink only bottled or filtered water and should avoid eating street food that has not been properly cooked. Also, it’s a good idea to bring mosquito repellent to protect against mosquito-borne illnesses such as dengue fever.

How to Get Around in Khok Kloi

The best way to get around in Khok Kloi is by motorbike or bicycle. Visitors can rent these vehicles from local shops. Taxis and tuk-tuks are also available for hire.

Conclusion

Khok Kloi is an excellent destination for those who want to experience the local culture, natural beauty, and relaxed lifestyle of Southern Thailand. With its historical sites, outdoor activities, and excellent cuisine, there is no shortage of things to do and see in Khok Kloi. Plan a visit today and discover the charms of this hidden gem in Northern Andaman Coast!
