Introduction to Baan Ao Leuk

Baan Ao Leuk is a scenic village in Krabi Province, Thailand. Nestled in a lush valley and surrounded by limestone cliffs, this charming village offers visitors an escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, with stunning scenery and a laid-back atmosphere.

There is no shortage of cultural and natural attractions in Baan Ao Leuk, and visitors can enjoy a variety of outdoor activities, including hiking, cycling, and swimming in the natural pools.

List of Places to Visit in Baan Ao Leuk

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Baan Ao Leuk Museum: This small museum showcases the history and culture of the village, with exhibits on local traditions, customs, and ancient artifacts.

  • Wat Sa Krabi: This beautiful temple has a stunning architecture and intricate sculptures and carvings. Visitors can explore the temple grounds and learn about Buddhism and the local customs and traditions.

Natural Attractions of Baan Ao Leuk: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Ao Leuk Waterfall: This stunning waterfall is a must-visit attraction in Baan Ao Leuk. Visitors can swim in the natural pools, hike to the top of the waterfall for a panoramic view of the surrounding forests, and enjoy a picnic in the shade of the trees.

  • Than Bok Khorani National Park: This national park is home to a rich variety of flora and fauna, as well as limestone formations, caves, and waterfalls. Visitors can explore the park on foot or by bicycle, and enjoy a range of activities, including hiking, kayaking, and wildlife spotting.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Baan Ao Leuk

Cycling Routes

  • Baan Ao Leuk Loop: This 25-kilometer loop takes visitors through the picturesque countryside around Baan Ao Leuk, with stunning views of limestone cliffs, rice paddies, and coconut plantations.

  • Krabi Coastal Ride: This 50-kilometer route takes visitors along the beautiful coastline of Krabi Province, with stops at beaches, fishing villages, and local markets.

Best Things to Do in Baan Ao Leuk: Activities and Tours

  • Swim in the natural pools at Ao Leuk Waterfall
  • Cycle around the scenic countryside surrounding Baan Ao Leuk
  • Explore the history and culture of the village at Baan Ao Leuk Museum
  • Visit the beautiful Wat Sa Krabi temple
  • Kayak through the mangrove forests of Than Bok Khorani National Park

Food and Drink Scene of Baan Ao Leuk

Must-Try Dishes

  • Tom Kha Gai: This spicy chicken soup is made with coconut milk, lemongrass, galangal, and chili peppers.

  • Pad Thai: A popular Thai dish made with stir-fried rice noodles, eggs, peanuts, and a sweet and sour sauce.

  • Khao Soi: A traditional Northern Thai dish made with coconut milk and curry paste, served with egg noodles and a choice of meat.

Local Beverages

  • Singha Beer: A popular Thai beer with a smooth and refreshing flavor.

  • Thai Iced Tea: This sweet and creamy tea is made with black tea, condensed milk, and sugar.

  • Lao Khao: A Thai rice whiskey that is often used in traditional rituals and ceremonies.

Where to Stay in Baan Ao Leuk: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Baan Ao Leuk Resort: A luxury resort located in a picturesque setting overlooking the valley and the mountains.

  • Krabi Cinta House: A mid-range guesthouse with clean and comfortable rooms and a friendly atmosphere.

  • Baan Phu Mee: A budget-friendly hostel with a dormitory and private rooms, located in the heart of Baan Ao Leuk village.

What to Buy in Baan Ao Leuk: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Local handicrafts: Baan Ao Leuk is known for its traditional handicrafts, including woven baskets, hand-carved wooden sculptures, and pottery.

  • Coconut products: Thailand is famous for its coconuts, and Baan Ao Leuk is no exception. Visitors can purchase coconut oil, coconut sugar, and coconut milk products.

Local Culture and Customs of Baan Ao Leuk

  • Respect for the monarchy: Thailand is a monarchy, and visitors should show respect for the royal family and avoid any negative comments or behavior.

  • Remove shoes before entering houses and temples: It is customary in Thailand to remove shoes before entering homes, temples, and other places of worship.

  • Etiquette in public places: Visitors should dress modestly and behave respectfully in public places, avoiding loud or offensive language or behavior.

Best Time to Visit Baan Ao Leuk

The best time to visit Baan Ao Leuk is between November and February, when the weather is dry and cool, and humidity is low. The temperatures during these months are pleasant, with average highs ranging from 28-30°C. March to May can be very hot, with temperatures reaching up to 36°C. June to October is the rainy season, and visitors may experience heavy rainfall and flooding.

Safety and Health

Safety in Baan Ao Leuk

Baan Ao Leuk is a relatively safe destination, with low levels of crime and violence. However, visitors should take common sense precautions, such as keeping valuables secure and staying aware of their surroundings. Visitors should also take precautions when participating in outdoor activities, such as hiking or kayaking, and should only engage in these activities with a reputable, licensed provider.

Health in Baan Ao Leuk

Visitors to Baan Ao Leuk should take precautions to protect their health, including getting vaccinated before the trip and using mosquito repellent to prevent the spread of mosquito-borne diseases, such as dengue fever and malaria. Visitors should also drink only bottled or boiled water, avoid eating raw or undercooked food, and practice good hygiene by washing hands frequently.

Getting to Baan Ao Leuk: Transportation Options and Logistics

Baan Ao Leuk is located approximately 20 kilometers from Krabi Airport and can be reached by taxi or private transfer. Visitors can also take a bus from Krabi Town or Ao Nang to the village, with frequent departures throughout the day.

Airports Near Baan Ao Leuk

  • Krabi Airport: Located approximately 20 kilometers from Baan Ao Leuk, Krabi Airport is served by domestic and international airlines, with frequent flights to Bangkok, Chiang Mai, and other destinations in Thailand.

Bus Stations Near Baan Ao Leuk

  • Krabi Bus Terminal: Located in Krabi Town, approximately 30 kilometers from Baan Ao Leuk, Krabi Bus Terminal is the main bus station serving Krabi Province, with regular buses to Bangkok, Phuket, and other destinations in Thailand.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a car or motorbike to explore the area around Baan Ao Leuk independently. However, visitors should be aware that driving in Thailand can be challenging, with narrow roads and chaotic traffic.

How to Get Around in Baan Ao Leuk

The best way to get around in Baan Ao Leuk is by cycling or walking, as the village is relatively small and easy to navigate. Visitors can rent bicycles or hire a local guide to lead them on a cycling tour of the countryside. Alternatively, visitors can hire a taxi or private car to explore the area around Baan Ao Leuk independently.

Where to Go Next from Baan Ao Leuk: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Krabi Town: The capital of Krabi Province, Krabi Town is a charming, laid-back city with a rich history and culture, as well as a vibrant nightlife and dining scene.

  • Ao Nang: A popular beach destination, Ao Nang is known for its stunning beaches, offshore islands, and water sports activities, including snorkeling, kayaking, and scuba diving.

  • Railay Beach: Known for its stunning limestone cliffs and crystal-clear waters, Railay Beach is a popular destination for rock climbing, hiking, and swimming.

Conclusion

Baan Ao Leuk is a hidden gem in Southern Thailand, offering visitors an escape from the crowds and a chance to explore the rich history and culture of the region. Whether you are seeking adventure, relaxation, or simply a chance to soak up the local culture, Baan Ao Leuk has something for everyone. With its stunning scenery, friendly locals, and diverse attractions, this charming village is a must-visit destination in Thailand.
