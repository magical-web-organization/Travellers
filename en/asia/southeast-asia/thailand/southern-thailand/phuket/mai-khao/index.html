Introduction to Mai Khao

Mai Khao is a beautiful beach town located in the northwest coast of Phuket, Southern Thailand. Known for being a serene getaway spot, Mai Khao offers visitors a chance to relax and unwind.

The town has a rich history and culture, evident in its various museums and historical attractions. Mai Khao is also a popular spot for outdoor enthusiasts, thanks to its outdoor adventures and scenic views. And of course, don’t forget the stunning beaches!

In this guide, we’ll take you through everything you need to know about Mai Khao, from the best places to visit and things to do to where to eat, stay and shop.

List of Places to Visit in Mai Khao

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Mai Khao has plenty of culturally significant attractions to explore. Here are some must-visit places:

Phuket Trickeye Museum

The Phuket Trickeye Museum is an interactive museum that offers visitors a chance to embrace their creativity and take captivating pictures. The museum has various themed galleries that bring art and entertainment to life.

Wat Mai Khao

Wat Mai Khao is a Buddhist temple that has been in existence for over a century. It’s famous for its intricate design and white and gold exterior. Once inside, visitors can observe elaborate murals and sculptures that celebrate the Buddhist religion.

Natural Attractions of Mai Khao: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Mai Khao has some really incredible natural attractions that outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers will enjoy. Here are some of the best:

Sirinat National Park

Sirinat National Park is a protected area that boasts pristine beaches and is an excellent spot for birdwatching. The park is home to over 200 species of birds, including the rare spoon-billed sandpiper.

Mai Khao Beach

Undeniably the biggest attraction in Mai Khao is the beach. With its uninterrupted stretch of soft white sand and misty turquoise waters, Mai Khao is the perfect spot for sunbathing, swimming and beach games.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Mai Khao

Mai Khao is a great place to get active, with plenty of hiking and biking trails to explore. Here are some of the best routes:

Khao Phra Thaeo National Park

Khao Phra Thaeo National Park is home to some of the most challenging hiking trails in Thailand. The park’s forest is teeming with wildlife, including monkeys, deer and gibbons. Hiking trails here take visitors through dense forests, waterfalls and stunning lookout points.

Mai Khao Village

Mai Khao Village is a rural village that’s an excellent option for those looking for a leisurely bike ride. The village has traditional houses, local markets and rice fields, making it an excellent way to experience traditional Thai culture.

Best Things to Do in Mai Khao: Activities and Tours

There’s plenty to do in Mai Khao, and you won’t run out of options. Here are some of the best activities and tours to experience:

Day Trip to Phi Phi Islands

Phi Phi Island is a group of six islands known for their crystal clear waters and breathtaking rock formations. A day trip from Mai Khao will take you on speedboats that will stop at several spots to snorkel, swim, sunbathe and enjoy some delicious Thai cuisine.

Thai Cooking Class

Thai cuisine is world-renowned, and there’s no better place to learn the art of Thai cooking than Thailand. A Thai Cooking Class in Mai Khao will teach participants how to cook a variety of Thai dishes, including Pad Thai, Tom Yum soup, and green curry.

Week-Long Itinerary for Mai Khao

Here’s a week-long itinerary for making the most of your visit to Mai Khao:

Day 1 - Explore the Beach

Spend your first day soaking up the sun and relaxing on Mai Khao Beach.

Day 2 - Visit Sirinat National Park

Head to Sirinat National Park for some birdwatching and hiking

Day 3 - Take a Cooking Class

Take a Thai Cooking Class to learn about the local cuisine and enjoy your creations at the end of the day.

Day 4 - Explore Local Villages

Take a bike ride through Mai Khao Village and experience local culture.

Day 5 - Visit Museums

Visit some of the museums and historical sites in Mai Khao, such as the Phuket Trickeye Museum and Wat Mai Khao.

Day 6 - Day Trip to Phi Phi Island

Take a speedboat to explore the stunning Phi Phi Islands

Day 7 - Departure Day

Spend your last day in Mai Khao relaxing and soaking up the sun before your departure.

Food and Drink Scene of Mai Khao

There’s no shortage of delicious food and drink options in Mai Khao. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

Khao Man Gai

Khao Man Gai is a popular Thai dish of boiled chicken served on top of rice with a side of chicken broth.

Tom Yum Goong

Tom Yum Goong is a spicy and sour soup made with shrimp, lemongrass, ginger, lime leaves and chili peppers.

Local Beverages

Chang Beer

Chang Beer is the most popular Thai beer, known for its crisp, clean taste and low price.

Thai Iced Tea

Thai Iced Tea is a sweet and creamy tea made with condensed milk and spices.

Where to Stay in Mai Khao: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Mai Khao has several accommodation options to fit any budget. Here are some top choices in each category:

Top Hotels

JW Marriott Phuket Resort & Spa

The JW Marriott Phuket Resort & Spa is a luxury resort with world-class amenities, including six swimming pools, a private beach and a spa.

Anantara Mai Khao Phuket Villas

Anantara Mai Khao Phuket Villas is an elegant resort with spacious villas, private pools and lush gardens.

Top Hostels

Phuket Airport Place

Phuket Airport Place is a budget-friendly hostel within walking distance of Mai Khao Beach.

Phuket Airport Hostel

Phuket Airport Hostel is another affordable option, offering private rooms and dormitories.

What to Buy in Mai Khao: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

There are plenty of local products to bring home from Mai Khao. Here are some top choices:

Thai Silk Scarves

Thai silk scarves are made from high-quality silk and are available in a range of colors and designs.

Elephant Carvings

Thailand is famous for its elephant carvings, which are traditionally made from wood and come in a range of sizes and styles.

Local Culture and Customs of Mai Khao

Mai Khao and the surrounding region have a fascinating history and culture. Respectful behavior towards locals, their beliefs and customs is expected.

Best Time to Visit Mai Khao

The best time to visit Mai Khao is during the dry season, from December to April. This time offers the best weather for outdoor activities, sightseeing, and enjoying the beach.

Do’s and Don’ts of Mai Khao

Do:

  • Respect Thai culture and customs.
  • Dress conservatively when visiting temples and other religious sites.
  • Remove shoes when entering someone’s home or a temple.

Don’t:

  • Speak loudly or disrespectfully in public.
  • Display affection in public, especially in strictly conservative areas.
  • Disrespect the royal family, as this is a criminal offense in Thailand.

Safety and Health

Safety in Mai Khao

Mai Khao is known to be a safe destination for both locals and tourists alike. However, it is always advisable to practice common sense when traveling and take precautions.

Health in Mai Khao

Mai Khao is a safe destination from a health perspective. There are several hospitals and clinics in the area in case of any health concerns. However, travelers should pack mosquito repellent and keep up to date with their vaccinations.

Getting to Mai Khao: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Mai Khao

Phuket International Airport is the nearest airport to Mai Khao and receives daily flights from Bangkok and other major cities around the world.

Bus Stations Near Mai Khao

Phuket Town Bus Station is the closest bus station to Mai Khao, with regular buses from Bangkok.

Other Options

Taxis and rental cars are both available options for traveling around Mai Khao and the surrounding region.

How to Get Around in Mai Khao

The best way to get around Mai Khao is by renting a scooter, which is cheap and convenient. Taxis and tuk-tuks are also available, although they are more expensive than scooters.

Where to Go Next from Mai Khao: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Phuket is a great destination for those looking for more excitement, and there are plenty of nearby destinations to explore:

Phuket Town

Phuket Town is a historic town with Sino-Portuguese architecture and fantastic street food.

Patong Beach

Patong Beach is busiest tourist spot in Phuket, with lots of restaurants and bars and a lively nightlife scene.

Conclusion

Mai Khao is an excellent destination for those looking to relax, unwind and enjoy some time in Thailand. With stunning natural attractions, outdoor adventures, delicious food, and plenty of cultural landmarks, Mai Khao truly has something for everyone.
