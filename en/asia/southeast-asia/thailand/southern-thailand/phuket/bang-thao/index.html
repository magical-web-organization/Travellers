Introduction to Bang Thao

Bang Thao is a serene paradise located in the northwest of Phuket, Thailand. Also known as Laguna, it is a relatively quiet area compared to the bustling streets of Patong and Phuket City. It is ideal for travelers looking for a peaceful getaway with stunning beaches and natural attractions. Bang Thao is also known for its luxurious resorts and golf courses.

List of Places to Visit in Bang Thao

There are many fascinating places to visit in Bang Thao, and here are some recommendations:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Wat Phra Thong - This temple is around 20 kilometers north of Bang Thao and houses a golden Buddha statue. According to folklore, the statue was covered in plaster to hide its beauty during a Burmese invasion, and it was discovered later when a villager struck it with a hoe.

  2. Thalang National Museum - Located about 10 kilometers from Bang Thao, the museum showcases the history and culture of the Thalang district from ancient times to the present day.

Natural Attractions of Bang Thao: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Bang Thao Beach - This is a stunning three-kilometer-long white sand beach that offers fantastic views of the Andaman Sea. The waters here are ideal for swimming and many water activities like kayaking, parasailing, and jet skiing.

  2. Khao Phra Thaeo National Park - This is a vast natural reserve that covers 23 square kilometers and is home to various species of animals like gibbons, wild boars, and macaques. It is also home to the Ton Sai and Bang Pae waterfalls.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bang Thao

Bang Thao is an ideal place for those who love hiking and biking, with many routes offering breathtaking views of nature. Here are some recommended trail options:

  1. Bangtao Beach to Layan Beach Trail - This is a beautiful eight-kilometer trail that passes through jungle terrain with stunning views of the ocean.

  2. Ton Sai Waterfall Trail - This is a moderate three-kilometer trail that leads to the Ton Sai waterfall, perfect for a refreshing break during your hike.

Best Things to Do in Bang Thao: Activities and Tours

There are many activities and tours to explore in Bang Thao, and here are some recommendations:

  1. Snorkeling and Diving - The crystal clear waters of the Andaman Sea offer incredible opportunities to snorkel and dive, with many tour companies providing equipment and guides.

  2. Golfing - Bang Thao is home to world-class golf courses, and many resorts offer packages to play on these championship grade courses.

Week-Long Itinerary for Bang Thao

Here’s a sample itinerary for a week-long stay in Bang Thao:

Food and Drink Scene of Bang Thao

Bang Thao has a vibrant food and drink scene with many street stalls, restaurants, and cafes. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Tom Yam Goong - A spicy, sour soup made with shrimp, lemongrass, galangal, and chili.

  2. Pad Thai - A delicious stir-fried noodle dish made with tamarind sauce, vegetables, and your choice of protein, typically chicken or shrimp.

Local Beverages

  1. Chang Beer - A popular Thai beer that is refreshing and light.

  2. Thai Tea - A sweet and creamy cold drink made with black tea, condensed milk, and spices.

Where to Stay in Bang Thao: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bang Thao has a range of accommodation options, including budget-friendly guesthouses and luxury hotels.

Here are some recommendations:

  1. Outrigger Laguna Phuket Beach Resort - A luxurious beachfront resort with well-appointed rooms, private balconies, pool, and various facilities like a spa, fitness center, and multiple dining options.

  2. Tinidee Golf Resort Phuket - A charming resort situated near the Laguna golf course. The rooms are spacious and designed with traditional Thai decor, and it also has an outdoor pool and fitness center.

What to Buy in Bang Thao: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Bang Thao is known for its handicrafts, and here are some souvenirs and local products to bring home:

  1. Thai Silk Scarves - A colorful, luxurious Thai silk scarf that will be a perfect addition to your wardrobe.

  2. Handcrafted Soaps - Made with natural ingredients and essential oils, these soaps come in various scents and shapes and are perfect for gifting.

Local Culture and Customs of Bang Thao

Bang Thao is a predominantly Buddhist community, and it is essential to show respect for their religious beliefs and practices. When visiting temples, dress modestly by covering your knees and shoulders.

Best Time to Visit Bang Thao

The best time to visit Bang Thao is from November to April. This dry season has mild temperatures and less rainfall, making it ideal for outdoor activities and beach exploration.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bang Thao

Do:

  1. Respect local customs and traditions.
  2. Carry a copy of your passport for identification.
  3. Learn some basic Thai language phrases.

Don’t:

  1. Drink tap water.
  2. Engage in drugs or prostitution.
  3. Take Buddha images without permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bang Thao

Bang Thao is a safe destination, but it is always important to practice common sense measures like not leaving your valuables unattended on the beach and not walking alone at night.

Health in Bang Thao

Tourists are advised to drink bottled or purified water to avoid water-borne diseases. Mosquito-borne diseases like dengue fever are prevalent in Southeast Asia, so it is recommended to bring mosquito repellent.

Getting to Bang Thao: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Bang Thao

The nearest airport to Bang Thao is Phuket International Airport, which is around 20 kilometers away. It is well-connected to various cities in Asia, and there are several flights daily to Bangkok.

Bus Stations Near Bang Thao

The main bus station in Phuket City is about 20 kilometers south of Bang Thao. From there, regular buses and minivans are available to Bang Thao and other destinations on the island.

Other Options

Taxis, tuk-tuks, and private car hires are also available for going to Bang Thao from neighboring cities.

How to Get Around in Bang Thao

Walking, cycling, and motorbikes are the best ways to explore Bang Thao. Taxis and tuk-tuks are also readily available, but they tend to be expensive.

Where to Go Next from Bang Thao: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bang Thao is an excellent base to explore other exciting destinations in Phuket, like:

  1. Phuket Old Town - Just a 30-minute drive from Bang Thao, this town has a unique Sino-Portuguese architecture and a fascinating history.

  2. Patong Beach - A popular beach destination located about 20 kilometers south of Bang Thao with a lively nightlife and a wide range of shopping and dining options.

Conclusion

Bang Thao is a beautiful destination in Phuket that offers a perfect balance of tranquility, natural beauty, and luxury. It is a destination that has something for everyone, whether you’re a culture enthusiast, adventure seeker, foodie, or just looking for a place to unwind. Plan your next visit now and experience the paradise that is Bang Thao.
