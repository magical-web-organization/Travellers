Introduction to Karon

Karon, a small town on the west coast of Phuket, is a hidden gem that offers a perfect mix of natural beauty and cultural heritage. While it’s known for its pristine beaches, Karon has a lot more to offer including hiking trails, temples, local markets, and an exciting nightlife.

In this travel guide, we have compiled a list of the best places to visit, activities to do, and experiences to have in Karon. Whether you’re a solo traveler or with family and friends, Karon has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Karon

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Karon has several culturally significant attractions that offer a glimpse into its history and heritage.

Wat Suwan Khiri Khet

Also known as the Karon Temple, Wat Suwan Khiri Khet is a popular attraction among tourists and locals alike. The temple features a stunning Emerald Buddha statue and intricate carvings that give insights into Thailand’s rich cultural heritage.

Phuket Big Buddha

Located atop Nakkerd Hills, Phuket Big Buddha is a 45-metre-tall marble statue that offers panoramic views of the island. The statue, which took over a decade to build, is a symbol of peace, and the surrounding area features shrines, gardens, and stunning views of the island’s coastline.

Old Phuket Town

A short drive from Karon, Old Phuket Town offers a glimpse of the island’s colonial past. The town is dotted with Sino-Portuguese buildings, local markets, and museums.

Natural Attractions of Karon: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Karon boasts several natural attractions that are perfect for outdoor enthusiasts and nature lovers.

Karon Viewpoint

Karon Viewpoint, also known as Three Beaches Viewpoint, offers stunning views of Karon Beach, Kata Noi Beach, and Kata Yai Beach. The viewpoint is an ideal spot to capture some memorable photographs.

Promthep Cape

Promthep Cape, located at the southernmost point of Phuket, offers panoramic views of the Andaman Sea. The sunset over the ocean is a sight to behold, and the area also features a lighthouse and a scenic walking trail.

Karon Beach

Karon Beach, one of the largest and most popular beaches in Phuket, is the perfect place to relax and soak up some sun. The crystal-clear waters and soft white sands make it an ideal spot for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Karon

Karon is an ideal destination for outdoor enthusiasts and thrill-seekers. The town offers several hiking and biking trails that offer stunning views of the surrounding landscapes.

Khao Phra Thaeo National Park

Khao Phra Thaeo National Park, located near Karon, is home to several hiking and biking trails that wind through dense forests, waterfalls, and streams. The park is home to several rare and endangered species of flora and fauna, and a visit here offers a glimpse of Thailand’s biodiversity.

Jungle Xtrem Adventure Park

Jungle Xtrem Adventure Park offers an exciting outdoor adventure that includes ziplining, abseiling, and hiking through the jungle. The park’s team of trainers will ensure that visitors have a safe and enjoyable experience.

Best Things to Do in Karon: Activities and Tours

Karon offers visitors a plethora of activities and tours that cater to all types of travelers.

Elephant Sanctuary Tour

The Elephant Sanctuary Tour is an ethical and sustainable animal encounter that allows visitors to interact with rescued elephants in their natural habitat. The tour includes feeding, bathing, and learning about these gentle giants.

Thai Cooking Class

A Thai Cooking Class is an ideal way to learn about the local cuisine and culture. The class includes a visit to a local market to source ingredients, and a cooking lesson with a local chef.

Simon Cabaret Show

The Simon Cabaret Show is a world-famous cabaret show that features over 100 performers in extravagant costumes. The show is a mixture of traditional Thai and modern performances, and is a must-see for anyone visiting Karon.

Food and Drink Scene of Karon

Karon offers an array of dining options ranging from street food to fine dining.

Must-Try Dishes

Tom Yam Goong

Tom Yam Goong is a spicy shrimp soup that is famous all over Thailand. The soup is flavoured with lemongrass, chili paste, and lime juice, and is a must-try for anyone visiting Karon.

Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a stir-fried dish consisting of rice noodles, shrimp, eggs, and vegetables. This dish is a staple in Thai cuisine, and variations of Pad Thai can be found in every corner of Karon.

Local Beverages

Singha Beer

Singha Beer is a popular local beer and can be found in every bar and restaurant in Karon.

Mai Tai

Mai Tai is a fruity cocktail made with rum, lime juice, and sugar syrup. The cocktail is a perfect way to cool off after a day of sightseeing.

Where to Stay in Karon: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Karon offers a range of accommodation options that cater to all budgets and preferences.

Luxury Accommodation

Mövenpick Resort & Spa Karon Beach

Mövenpick Resort & Spa Karon Beach is a luxurious beachfront resort that offers stunning ocean views, lavish spa treatments, and a range of dining options.

Mid-Range Accommodation

Centara Grand Beach Resort Phuket

Centara Grand Beach Resort Phuket is a family-friendly resort that offers a range of activities and amenities, including a water park, a kids’ club, and several dining options.

Budget Accommodation

Kata Country House

Kata Country House is a budget-friendly guesthouse that offers comfortable rooms, an outdoor pool, and a convenient location close to Karon Beach.

What to Buy in Karon: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Karon offers several local products that make the perfect souvenirs and gifts for family and friends.

Thai Silk

Thai Silk is a luxurious fabric that is famous all over the world. Karon has several local silk shops where visitors can purchase scarves, clothing, and other products made from Thai Silk.

Handicrafts

Karon has several local markets that sell handmade crafts, including woodcarvings, jewellery, and pottery.

Local Culture and Customs of Karon

Thailand is a predominantly Buddhist country, and visitors should be respectful of the local customs and traditions. It’s important to dress conservatively when visiting temples and to remove shoes before entering a sacred space.

Best Time to Visit Karon

The best time to visit Karon is from November to April, when the weather is dry and cool. Peak tourist season is from December to February, so visitors should book accommodation in advance during this time.

Do’s and Don’ts of Karon

Do:

  • Try local cuisine
  • Dress conservatively when visiting temples
  • Be respectful of Thai culture and customs

Don’t:

  • Public displays of affection
  • Dress inappropriately
  • Disrespect monks or Buddha statues

Safety and Health

Safety in Karon

Karon is a safe destination for travelers. However, visitors should be cautious when driving scooters and be aware of potential scams and thefts.

Health in Karon

Visitors should be cautious of sunstroke and dehydration due to the hot and humid climate. It’s recommended to drink bottled water and wear sunscreen at all times.

Getting to Karon: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Karon

Phuket International Airport is the nearest airport to Karon and is approximately 50 minutes’ drive away.

Bus Stations Near Karon

Phuket Bus Terminal 2 is the nearest bus station to Karon and is approximately 30 minutes’ drive away.

Other Options

Visitors can also take a private transfer or taxi from the airport or Phuket Town to Karon.

How to Get Around in Karon

Karon is a small town, and visitors can easily walk to most attractions. However, visitors can also hire a scooter or take a tuk-tuk or taxi to get around.

Where to Go Next from Karon: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Karon is located on the west coast of Phuket and is close to several nearby destinations and excursions.

Phi Phi Island

Phi Phi Island is an idyllic destination that offers pristine beaches, coral reefs, and stunning views. Visitors can take a day trip to the island from Karon.

Phang Nga Bay

Phang Nga Bay is a serene and scenic destination that features towering limestone cliffs, emerald green waters, and hidden lagoons. Visitors can take a boat tour of the area from Karon.

Conclusion

Karon, Phuket, is a perfect destination for travelers seeking a mix of natural beauty and cultural heritage. From stunning beaches to hiking trails, Karon offers a range of activities and experiences that cater to all interests and budgets. With this comprehensive travel guide, you can plan your trip to Karon and make the most of your visit.
