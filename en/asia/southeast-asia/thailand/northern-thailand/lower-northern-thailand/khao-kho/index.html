Introduction to Khao Kho

Khao Kho is a charming mountain town located in the Lower Northern region of Thailand. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, amazing outdoor activities, and rich cultural heritage. The town is situated at an elevation of 1,143 meters above sea level, with temperatures usually hovering around 10 to 25 degrees Celsius.

If you’re looking for a peaceful and scenic destination away from the hustle and bustle of city life, then Khao Kho is the perfect place to be. With its lush forests, breathtaking landscapes, and a wide array of tourist attractions, you’ll never run out of things to explore and discover here.

List of Places to Visit in Khao Kho

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums, and Historical Places

  1. Kanchanapisek Pagoda - a golden pagoda located on top of Khao Kho Mountain with a panoramic view of the surrounding landscape.
  2. Wat Phra That Chon Kaew - a stunning temple with a high position overlooking the mountains and valleys.
  3. Khao Kho Memorial - a museum that showcases the history of Khao Kho and its military significance during the Vietnamese War.

Natural Attractions of Khao Kho: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Phu Thap Boek - the highest mountain in Petchabun Province, with a breathtaking view of misty mountains.
  2. Khao Kho Wind Farm - a massive wind farm located on a hilltop that offers an excellent view of the sunset.
  3. Khao Kho National Park - home to a dense forest, waterfalls, and a variety of wildlife like monkeys, bears, and deer.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Khao Kho

  1. Phu Hin Rong Kla National Park - offers fantastic hiking and trekking trails with stunning views of the countryside.
  2. Khao Kho Forest Park - perfect for nature lovers looking for a peaceful and serene environment.

Best Things to Do in Khao Kho: Activities and Tours

  1. Go on a Hot Air Balloon Ride - get a bird’s eye view of the picturesque landscape of Khao Kho.
  2. ATV Adventure - explore the stunning Khao Kho countryside and mountains on an all-terrain vehicle.
  3. Zipline Adventure - fly through the air and enjoy spectacular views of the mountains and forests.

Food and Drink Scene of Khao Kho

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Khao Soi - a northern Thai curry noodle soup with chicken or beef.
  2. Tom Yum - a spicy and sour soup made with shrimp or chicken.
  3. Som Tam - a spicy and tangy green papaya salad.

Local Beverages

  1. Thai Iced Tea - a sweet and refreshing beverage perfect for hot weather.
  2. Singha Beer - the most popular beer brand in Thailand.

Where to Stay in Khao Kho: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Sirinati Khao Kho National Park - a luxurious hotel located inside Khao Kho National Park.
  2. Happy Home Phu Ruea - a cozy and affordable guesthouse in the heart of town.
  3. Khao Kho Grand View Hotel - a stunning hotel with stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

What to Buy in Khao Kho: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Local fruits such as longan, tamarind, and pineapple.
  2. Handmade pottery and ceramic products.
  3. Traditional Thai clothing and handicrafts.

Local Culture and Customs of Khao Kho

The people of Khao Kho are known for their hospitality and respect for tradition and culture. Many of the locals are farmers and craftsmen who still practice age-old methods of agriculture and handicrafts.

When visiting temples and religious sites, it is essential to dress modestly and remove shoes before entering.

Best Time to Visit Khao Kho

The best time to visit Khao Kho is from November to February, when temperatures are cooler and the weather is pleasant. The months of March to May can be hot and humid, while the rainy season lasts from June to October.

Do’s and Don’ts of Khao Kho

Do:

  1. Respect the local culture and customs.
  2. Explore the natural beauty of Khao Kho by going on nature walks and hikes.
  3. Try out the local food and drinks.

Don’t:

  1. Disrespect temples and religious sites.
  2. Litter or damage natural environments.
  3. Engage in illegal activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Khao Kho

Khao Kho is generally safe for tourists, with a low crime rate and friendly locals. However, it’s always advisable to take necessary precautions and be aware of your surroundings.

Health in Khao Kho

The tap water in Khao Kho is not suitable for drinking, so it’s essential to stick to bottled water. Mosquitoes can be a problem in rural areas, so bring insect repellent to avoid bites.

Getting to Khao Kho: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Khao Kho

The nearest airport to Khao Kho is Phitsanulok International Airport, which is approximately 110 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Khao Kho

Khao Kho Bus Station is located in the center of town and offers daily bus services to various cities in Thailand.

Other Options

Taxis and private shuttle services are also available for transport to and from Khao Kho.

How to Get Around in Khao Kho

The best way to get around Khao Kho is by renting a motorbike or private car. Taxis and songthaews (shared taxis) are also available to travel around the town.

Where to Go Next from Khao Kho: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Phrae - a charming town with traditional teak houses and longan orchards.
  2. Chiang Mai - the cultural capital of Northern Thailand with stunning architecture, temples, and outdoor activities.
  3. Loei - a province of northeastern Thailand known for its beautiful mountains and historical sites.

Conclusion

Khao Kho is a hidden gem in Northern Thailand that offers visitors a unique travel experience filled with natural beauty, adventure, culture, and tradition. We hope this comprehensive guide helps you plan your next trip to Khao Kho with ease and excitement.
