Introduction to Doi Ang Khang

Doi Ang Khang is a mountainous region located in Chiang Mai Province, Northern Thailand, that offers exceptional natural beauty and unique cultural experiences. It is home to the Doi Ang Khang National Park, known for its stunning wildlife, scenic trails, and traditional villages.

Whether you are an outdoor enthusiast looking for adventure or a cultural explorer interested in learning about the local traditions, Doi Ang Khang caters to all. From exploring the natural wonders to experiencing the local cuisine, this guide will provide you with all the necessary information and tips to make your trip to Doi Ang Khang an unforgettable one.
