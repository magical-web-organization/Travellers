Introduction to Sattahip

Sattahip is a hidden gem located on the eastern coast of Thailand, away from the bustling cities and tourist hotspots. It is a quiet and laid-back town with pristine beaches, beautiful landscapes, and friendly locals. Sattahip is a part of the Chonburi province and is home to the country’s largest naval base. This town is perfect for travelers who want to unwind and escape the crowds. In this comprehensive guide to Sattahip, we will explore the best things to do, places to stay, and local culture and customs.

Local Culture and Customs of Sattahip

Sattahip is a small town, and the locals follow traditional Thai customs. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Dress modestly when visiting temples and other cultural sites.
  • Remove your shoes when entering someone’s home.
  • Show respect to elders and monks.

Best Time to Visit Sattahip

The best time to visit Sattahip is between November and February, when the weather is cool and dry. March to May can be very hot, while June to October is the rainy season, which could hamper outdoor activities.
