Introduction to Hat Lek

Hat Lek is a less well-known tourist destination located in Trat Province, Eastern Thailand, that offers visitors an authentic experience that showcases the country’s natural beauty and cultural offerings. The area attracts visitors who are seeking a low-key, relaxed vacation that includes lovely beaches, low-key places to stay, outdoor adventures, and opportunities to experience the local culture. The town is situated on the southeastern border of Thailand, near the Thai-Cambodian border crossing into Cambodia. Hat Lek happens to be a popular location for Cambodian travelers to visit Thailand.

List of Places to Visit in Hat Lek

Here is a list of the best places to visit in Hat Lek:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Wat Khao Saming Suttharam Temple: A beautiful temple located atop a scenic hill that offers stunning views of the Gulf of Thailand. The temple complex can be reached by hiking trails or by driving.

  2. The Khao Yai Da Waterfall: A popular destination offering picturesque views of the waterfall and attractive nature trails that visitors can explore.

  3. The Khao Hin Lek Fai Viewpoint: The viewpoint provides panoramic views of Trat Province and a glimpse of the Gulf of Thailand.

Natural Attractions of Hat Lek: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Hat Lek Beach: A peaceful and serene beach providing visitors the opportunity to swim or sunbathe and enjoy the sound of waves while enjoying the scenery.

  2. The Koh Kood Island: An idyllic island located nearby Hat Lek, with a backdrop of white sands and blue water, that offers plenty of water sports and other outdoor activities.

  3. The Laem Sok Pier: The gateway to Marine Park, which has a vast stretch of biodiversity that includes coral reefs, whale sharks, turtle conservation rests. This makes it a perfect tourist spot for those who enjoy aquatic life.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hat Lek

The adventurous visitors who are fond of outdoor activities will enjoy the various hiking and biking routes on offer in Hat Lek. It is recommended to hire a local guide to explore the trails, such as:

  1. The Khao Saming Mountain: offers a variety of hiking routes which present various challenges and provide breathtaking views of the surroundings.

  2. The Laem Ngop port: the perfect location for those who seek an unbreakable combination of cycling and a stunning view of nature.

Best Things to Do in Hat Lek: Activities and Tours

  1. Island Tour: Visitors can take a tour of the nearby islands and enjoy the local ambiance while witnessing the breathtaking views around.

  2. Sunset cruise: Witness the sunset and the view of the countryside on the evening cruise from Hat Lek is a must-do.

  3. Trat City Tour: Take a day trip to Trat city, it offers a perfect balance of history, culture, and modern-day lifestyle.

Week-Long Itinerary for Hat Lek

Day 1: Arrival and relaxation on Hat Lek Beach.

Day 2: Early morning trek to Wat Khao Saming Temple.

Day 3: Island tour where visitors can spend the whole day enjoying the island hopping.

Day 4: A day trip to the Laem Sok pier and Marine Park.

Day 5: Rent a scooter and explore the surrounding villages and countryside.

Day 6: A day trip to Trat city.

Day 7: Departure day.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Seafood

Local Beverages

  • Thai Iced Tea
  • Chang beer

Local Culture and Customs of Hat Lek

Temple visits require visitors to wear appropriate clothing that covers the shoulders and legs below the knees. Headgear is optional, but shoes should be removed before entering any temple buildings.

Best Time to Visit Hat Lek

The best time to visit Hat Lek is between November and February, when the weather is drier and cooler. It is recommended to avoid Thai holidays and monsoon season.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hat Lek

Do:

  • Respect the local religion and culture
  • Carry adequate cash as not many places accept credit cards.
  • Respect the local wildlife and fauna

Don’t:

  • Don’t litter in public spaces.
  • Avoid visiting non-tourist locations without guidance.

Safety and Health

Safety in Hat Lek

Hat Lek is a relatively safe destination for tourists. Visitors are advised to stay alert and aware of their surroundings when traveling alone at night.

Health in Hat Lek

Visitors are advised to carry an adequate amount of bottled drinking water and avoid consuming drinks with ice, as well as street food. It is recommended to have an emergency kit, that includes the basic medications, insect repellent, and sunscreen.

Getting to Hat Lek: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Hat Lek

The nearest airport to Hat Lek is Trat airport.

Bus Stations Near Hat Lek

Hat Lek has a direct bus from Bangkok’s Ekamai bus terminal.

Other Options

Visitors can hire a taxi or car to travel to Hat Lek from nearby cities.

How to Get Around in Hat Lek

Visitors can rent a scooter or bicycle to travel in Hat Lek. Taxis and Tuk-Tuk are also available in the area.

Conclusion

Hat Lek is an excellent destination for travelers looking for a less touristy vacation that offers excellent weather conditions and an abundance of natural beauty and outdoor activities. While it might not have the typical flashy tourist features, it provides visitors with a unique and authentic insight into Thai culture and lifestyle.
