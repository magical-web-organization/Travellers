Soc Trang is a vibrant city located in Southern Vietnam. This destination is perfect for travelers who are looking for a unique cultural experience, natural beauty, and outdoor adventure. In this guide, we will explore everything you need to know to plan your trip to Soc Trang, including the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, food and drink recommendations, local culture, safety, and transport options.

Introduction to Soc Trang

Soc Trang is a lively city in the Mekong Delta region of Vietnam. It is known for its unique blend of Khmer, Chinese, and Vietnamese cultures, as well as its picturesque landscapes, authentic food scene, and friendly locals. The city is surrounded by canals, rice fields, and fruit orchards, making it an excellent base for outdoor adventures and nature lovers.

List of Places to Visit in Soc Trang

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Khmer Pagodas - Soc Trang is known for its beautifully crafted Khmer-style pagodas, including Chua Doi (Bat Pagoda) and Chua Dat Set (Clay Pagoda). These pagodas are adorned with colorful statues, intricate carvings, and intricate architecture.

  2. Soc Trang Museum - This museum showcases the history and culture of the Khmer and Vietnamese people living in Soc Trang. You can learn about the region’s rich history, including its relationship with the Khmer Empire and the influence of Chinese traders.

Natural Attractions of Soc Trang: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. My Phuoc Islet - This island is situated on the Hau River and boasts picturesque landscapes and a range of outdoor activities. You can go cycling, kayaking, or simply take a stroll along the riverbanks.

  2. Ma Thien Lanh Valley - This stunning valley is located in Vinh Chau district. It is known for its breathtaking natural beauty, including lush greenery, beautiful waterfalls, and scenic hiking trails.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Soc Trang

  1. Vinh Chau - This district is located to the northwest of the Soc Trang province and is known for its scenic beauty. The area boasts vast rice fields and stunning sceneries. It is a popular spot for cycling and hiking enthusiasts.

  2. Tan Long Island - This island is situated on the Ham Luong River and is known for its picturesque landscapes and serene atmosphere. You can take a bike ride around the island and enjoy the stunning views.

Best Things to Do in Soc Trang: Activities and Tours

  1. Canal tour - You can take a tour of the canals which run through the city. You will get to see ancient pagodas, beautiful scenery, and even watch the locals fish and farm.

  2. Visit a honey farm - The region surrounding Soc Trang is known for its honey and beekeeping traditions. You can take a trip to a local honey farm to learn about the process of honey making and even taste some delicious honey.

Food and Drink Scene of Soc Trang

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Banh Xeo Nhech - This dish is a crispy pancake that is filled with shrimp, mung bean sprouts, and herbs. It is served with a savory dipping sauce.

  2. Seafood Hotpot - This hotpot is made with a variety of fresh seafood, such as prawns, squid, and fish. It is cooked with fragrant spices and herbs for a flavorful and hearty meal.

Local Beverages

  1. Sugarcane Juice - This is a refreshing and sweet juice that is made from freshly pressed sugarcane. It is a popular drink in Soc Trang and throughout Vietnam.

  2. Coffee with Condensed Milk - This is a traditional Vietnamese coffee that is sweetened with condensed milk. It is a must-try drink for coffee lovers.

Where to Stay in Soc Trang: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Green Hotel - This hotel is located in the heart of Soc Trang and offers comfortable and affordable accommodation. It features modern amenities and friendly staff.

  2. Mekong Homestay - This homestay is located in the nearby Vinh Chau district and provides a unique opportunity to experience the local culture and traditions. The homestay has cozy rooms and delicious home-cooked meals.

What to Buy in Soc Trang: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Khmer-style handicrafts - You can find a variety of handmade crafts, such as jewelry, textiles, and wooden carvings, that showcase the Khmer traditions.

  2. Local produce - Soc Trang is known for its fresh fruits and vegetables, such as mangoes and dragonfruit. You can buy these items at local markets and bring them home as a tasty souvenir.

Local Culture and Customs of Soc Trang

  1. Dress modestly - It is important to dress modestly when visiting local temples and pagodas.

  2. Remove your shoes - It is customary to remove your shoes before entering religious sites and some local homes.

Best Time to Visit Soc Trang

The best time to travel to Soc Trang is from November to April. During this time, the weather is dry, pleasant, and less humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Soc Trang

Do:

  1. Try the local food and beverages

  2. Learn about the local culture and traditions

Don’t:

  1. Disrespect local customs and traditions

  2. Wear revealing clothing when visiting religious sites

Safety and Health

Safety in Soc Trang

Soc Trang is a safe city, but it is always important to take precautions when traveling. You should be aware of your surroundings, keep your valuables safe, and be cautious when using public transportation.

Health in Soc Trang

It is always important to take precautions when traveling to a new destination. Before your trip, be sure to consult with your doctor and get the necessary vaccinations. It is also a good idea to bring insect repellent and sunscreen.

Getting to Soc Trang: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Soc Trang

The nearest airport to Soc Trang is Can Tho International Airport, which is located around 60 kilometers away. From there, you can take a bus or a taxi to reach the city.

Bus Stations Near Soc Trang

There are several bus stations in Soc Trang that connect to other destinations in Vietnam, such as Saigon, Can Tho, and Ha Tien.

Other Options

You can also travel to Soc Trang by boat or car. However, these options may not be as convenient as flying or taking a bus.

How to Get Around in Soc Trang

The best way to get around in Soc Trang is by motorbike or bicycle. You can also take a taxi or a bus to get to other destinations in the city.

Where to Go Next from Soc Trang: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Can Tho - This city is known for its floating markets, beautiful gardens, and cultural attractions. It is located around 50 kilometers from Soc Trang.

  2. Phu Quoc Island - This island is situated in the Gulf of Thailand and is known for its stunning beaches, natural beauty, and fresh seafood. It is also a popular spot for snorkeling and scuba diving.

Soc Trang is a vibrant and unique destination that offers something for every type of traveler. Whether you are interested in culture, adventure, or relaxation, you will find plenty to do and see in this beautiful city.
