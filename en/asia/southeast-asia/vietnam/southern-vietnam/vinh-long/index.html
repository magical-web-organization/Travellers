Introduction to Vinh Long

Vinh Long is a charming city located in Southern Vietnam, known for its scenic landscapes, rich cultural heritage, and mouth-watering cuisine. Situated in the Mekong Delta, the city is surrounded by lush green fields, fruit orchards, and small channels that make for an unforgettable experience. Vinh Long is also home to several museums, temples, and ancient houses that offer a glimpse into the traditional Vietnamese way of life.

List of Places to Visit in Vinh Long

Here are some of the top places to visit in Vinh Long:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Vinh Long Museum: Discover the city’s fascinating history, culture, and traditions through exhibits and artifacts at this informative museum.
  • Trung Vuong Temple: This ancient temple has a unique blend of Chinese and Vietnamese architectural designs and often attracts devotees from nearby towns.
  • Binh Thanh Ancient House: Visit this 19th-century house and learn about traditional Vietnamese architecture and the country’s history.

Natural Attractions of Vinh Long: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • An Binh Island: Hop on a boat and explore this picturesque island with abundant fruit orchards and floating markets.
  • Can Tho Canal: Take a leisurely boat ride along this serene canal and admire the scenic views of the countryside.
  • Dong Phu Island: This beautiful island is known for its stunning sunsets and is a popular spot for photography enthusiasts.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Vinh Long

If you’re looking for some adventure, Vinh Long offers excellent hiking and biking opportunities. Here are some popular routes:

  • Vinh Long – Dong Phu Island: This 30 km roundtrip from Vinh Long to Dong Phu Island is a perfect cycling route that will take you through the lush rice paddies, orchards, and scenic countryside of Vietnam.
  • An Binh Island – Vinh Sang Floating Market: Take a leisurely hike from the An Binh Island to the Vinh Sang Floating Market. The trail is dotted by numerous farms and orchards and is a delight to the senses.

Best Things to Do in Vinh Long: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best activities and tours in Vinh Long:

  • Cycling Tours: Rent a bicycle and explore the scenic countryside of Vinh Long at your own pace.
  • Boat Tours: Take a boat tour around Vinh Long and visit the floating markets and nearby islands.
  • Cooking Classes: Learn to cook authentic Vietnamese cuisine with locals and indulge in a gastronomic journey.

Week-Long Itinerary for Vinh Long

If you’re planning a week-long trip to Vinh Long, here is a suggested itinerary:

Food and Drink Scene of Vinh Long

Vietnamese food is famous around the world for its aromatic herbs, spice, and unique taste. Vinh Long is no exception. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Banh Mi: This famous Vietnamese sandwich made with bread, meats, herbs, and sauces is a must-try for any foodie.
  • Bun Ca: A delectable soup made with fish, rice vermicelli, vegetables, and a variety of herbs.
  • Goi Cuon: Otherwise known as Vietnamese spring rolls, these rolls are filled with fresh vegetables, herbs, cooked meat, or seafood and served with a dipping sauce.

Local Beverages

  • Tra Da: Otherwise known as iced tea, is a popular Vietnamese drink that is served cold.
  • Fresh Coconut Juice: Chilled coconut water served in the green shell, an ideal drink for hot afternoons.
  • Bia, Vietnamese beer, is a great way to unwind after a long day of exploring Vinh Long.

Where to Stay in Vinh Long: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Vinh Long offers a range of options for accommodation to suit every budget and preferences. Some of the best places to stay in Vinh Long include:

  • Mekong Riverside Homestay: A charming guesthouse overlooking the river that offers comfortable accommodation and home-cooked meals.
  • Vinh Long Hotel: A cozy hotel located in the city center that offers value for money.
  • An Binh Homestay: This homestay, located on An Binh Island, offers a more traditional experience and is set within a fruit orchard.

What to Buy in Vinh Long: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Vinh Long is known for its delicious food, traditional handicrafts, and beautiful produce. Here are some items you can bring home from your trip:

  • Dried Fruits: The Mekong Delta is known for its abundance of fresh and dried fruits, including jackfruit and mango.
  • Handicrafts: Vinh Long is known for its traditional pottery, weaving, and wooden boat making.
  • Rice Paper: A staple food in Vietnam, you can buy fresh rice paper made by local artisans in the Vinh Long markets.

Local Culture and Customs of Vinh Long

Vietnamese people are known for their hospitality, friendliness, and respect for their traditions and culture. In Vinh Long, people practice several customs such as the ancestor worship, Tet holiday, and Vietnam’s national day.

Best Time to Visit Vinh Long

Vinh Long can be visited throughout the year. However, the area experiences the rainy season from August to October, which may make travel slightly uncomfortable. The dry season from November to April is considered the best time to visit Vinh Long as the weather is cool, and everything is in full bloom.

Do’s and Don’ts of Vinh Long

Here are some things to keep in mind when traveling to Vinh Long:

Do:

  • Respect local customs and culture.
  • Try the local food and drinks.
  • Bargain at the markets.

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave trash behind.
  • Wear revealing clothing when visiting cultural or religious sites.
  • Take photographs of locals without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Vinh Long

Like any other place, it is always essential to keep valuables in a safe place and to be vigilant at all times. Vinh Long is generally considered a safe destination for travelers. However, it is always advisable to take the necessary precautions, such as avoiding dark and deserted areas.

Health in Vinh Long

It’s crucial to take necessary vaccinations such as tetanus, typhoid, and hepatitis A before traveling to Vinh Long. Also, it’s always advisable to drink bottled water to avoid any waterborne diseases.

Getting to Vinh Long: Transportation Options and Logistics

Vinh Long is well connected with the rest of Vietnam, which makes access to the city easy.

Airports Near Vinh Long

  • Tan Son Nhat International Airport: located in Ho Chi Minh City, is 130km away from Vinh Long.
  • Can Tho International Airport: located in Can Tho city, 30km away from Vinh Long.

Bus Stations Near Vinh Long

  • Mien Tay Bus Station: located in Ho Chi Minh City, is the main station for buses heading towards Vinh Long.
  • Can Tho Bus Station: located in Can Tho and is 30km from Vinh Long.

Other Options

Taxis, private cars, and motorbikes can also be hired from Ho Chi Minh City to Vinh Long.

How to Get Around in Vinh Long

Vinh Long has several transport options available for getting around. These include cyclos, motorbikes, and taxis. Visitors can also rent bicycles and explore the city on their own.

Where to Go Next from Vinh Long: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some of the nearby destinations and excursions that you can plan for:

  • Can Tho: A bustling city known for its floating markets, rich cultural heritage, and delicious food.
  • Ho Chi Minh City: A vibrant and cosmopolitan city known for its street food, shopping, and historical attraction.
  • Phu Quoc Island: A tropical paradise with white sandy beaches, clear blue waters, and coral reefs.

Conclusion

Vinh Long is undoubtedly one of the hidden gems of Southern Vietnam that offers an insight into traditional Vietnamese culture and heritage. From its delicious food, serene landscapes to vibrant culture, there is something for everyone in Vinh Long. By following this travel guide, visitors will experience a memorable adventure through Vinh Long’s charming countryside and discover its hidden treasures.
