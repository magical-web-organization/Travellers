Introduction to Long Xuyen

Long Xuyen is a city located in Southern Vietnam, in the Mekong Delta region. It is known for being the commercial and cultural center of An Giang Province, with a rich history and charming local culture.

The city offers a unique mix of modern city life and rustic countryside charm. It is a fascinating destination for tourists looking to experience authentic Vietnamese culture and explore the natural beauty of the Mekong Delta region.

In this travel guide, we will provide you with the ultimate itinerary for your trip to Long Xuyen, including the best places to visit, things to do, and local culture.

List of Places to Visit in Long Xuyen

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. The An Giang Museum - Learn about the rich history of An Giang Province, with exhibitions on the Cham culture, the Indian influence in the region, and the struggle for independence during the Vietnam War.
  2. Long Xuyen Floating Market - Visit this vibrant market on the Hau River to experience the hustle and bustle of local life and taste some of the best street food in the city.
  3. Chau Doc Temple - Located just outside Long Xuyen, this ancient temple is a must-visit for anyone interested in local Vietnamese culture and religion.

Natural Attractions of Long Xuyen: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Tra Su Cajuput Forest - This natural reserve is a must-visit for nature lovers, with its unique ecosystem of water, cajuput trees, and diverse wildlife. Take a boat ride to explore the lush greenery and watch the birds and fish.
  2. Sam Mountain - This scenic mountain is a popular spot for hiking and offers spectacular views of the Mekong Delta region. It is also home to several ancient temples and caves.

Hiking/Biking Routes in Long Xuyen

  1. Tra Su Cajuput Forest Hiking Trail - Follow the winding trails of the Tra Su Cajuput Forest to experience the unique ecosystem up close and personal.
  2. Sam Mountain Biking Trail - Challenge yourself on the steep slopes of Sam Mountain and enjoy the natural beauty of the Mekong Delta region.

Best Things to Do in Long Xuyen: Activities and Tours

  1. Long Xuyen Floating Market Tour - Take a boat ride on the Hau River and explore the vibrant Long Xuyen Floating Market, where you can purchase fresh produce, handicrafts, and souvenirs.
  2. Tra Su Cajuput Forest Tour - Discover the unique ecosystem of the Tra Su Cajuput Forest with a guided tour through the waterways and greenery.
  3. Sam Mountain Hike and Temple Tour - Hike up the scenic Sam Mountain and explore the ancient temples and caves.

Food and Drink Scene of Long Xuyen

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Banh Canh Cua - A hearty crab noodle soup that is a local favorite.
  2. Lau Mam - A spicy seafood hot pot that is perfect for sharing with friends and family.
  3. Com Tam - A delicious rice dish served with grilled pork and a fried egg.

Local Beverages

  1. Tra Atiso - A refreshing herbal tea made from the hibiscus flower.
  2. Ca Phe Sua Da - The Vietnamese version of iced coffee, made with sweetened condensed milk.

Where to Stay in Long Xuyen: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Victoria Nui Sam Lodge - A luxurious hotel located on the scenic Sam Mountain.
  2. Dong Xuyen Hotel - A budget-friendly option located in the city center.
  3. Long Xuyen Sao Mai Hotel - A mid-range hotel with comfortable rooms and friendly staff.

What to Buy in Long Xuyen: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Traditional Vietnamese Clothing - Bring home a piece of local culture with traditional clothing such as ao dai or conical hats.
  2. Local Handicrafts - Purchase handmade crafts such as woven baskets or embroidered textiles.

Local Culture and Customs of Long Xuyen

Long Xuyen is a predominantly Buddhist city, and visitors should be respectful of local customs and traditions. It is also important to dress modestly when visiting temples or other cultural attractions.

Best Time to Visit Long Xuyen

The best time to visit Long Xuyen is between December and April, when the weather is dry and cool. However, the city can be visited year-round with a bit of preparation for the hot and wet seasons.

Do’s and Don’ts of Long Xuyen

Do:

  1. Try the local cuisine and street food.
  2. Visit the cultural attractions such as the An Giang Museum and Long Xuyen Floating Market.
  3. Practice basic Vietnamese phrases to communicate with locals.

Don’t:

  1. Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  2. Raise your voice or act aggressively in public.
  3. Litter or harm the natural environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Long Xuyen

Long Xuyen is generally a safe city for tourists, but as with any destination, visitors should take basic precautions to protect themselves and their belongings. It is also important to be aware of traffic when walking or biking on the city streets.

Health in Long Xuyen

Visitors to Long Xuyen should be up to date on routine vaccinations and take precautions against mosquito-borne illnesses such as dengue fever and malaria.

Getting to Long Xuyen: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Long Xuyen

The nearest airport to Long Xuyen is the Can Tho International Airport, located approximately 65 kilometers away.

Bus Stations Near Long Xuyen

Long Xuyen has a centrally located bus station that services regional destinations.

Other Options

Taxis, ride-hailing services, and private car rentals are also available for transportation to and from Long Xuyen.

How to Get Around in Long Xuyen

The city center of Long Xuyen is easily walkable, but visitors can also rent bicycles or motorcycles to explore the surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Long Xuyen: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Chau Doc Town - A small, picturesque town located just outside Long Xuyen, known for its natural beauty and ancient temples.
  2. Can Tho City - A bustling metropolis located approximately 100 kilometers from Long Xuyen, known for its floating markets and unique Mekong Delta culture.

Conclusion

Long Xuyen is a truly unique destination that offers a blend of traditional Vietnamese culture, stunning natural scenery, and modern city life. With our comprehensive travel guide, you can plan the perfect trip to this charming city and explore all that it has to offer.
