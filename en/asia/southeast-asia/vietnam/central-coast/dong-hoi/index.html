Introduction to Dong Hoi

Nestled in the heart of Central Coast Vietnam, Dong Hoi is a small city that is slowly gaining popularity amongst travelers. The city boasts of scenic landscapes, rich culture, and history. Dong Hoi has something to offer to every type of traveler – from historically significant places to natural wonders, there’s plenty to explore in and around the city.

List of Places to Visit in Dong Hoi

Culturally Important Attractions

1. Quang Binh Provincial Museum

The Quang Binh Provincial Museum displays a diverse collection of archaeological artifacts, war memorials, and cultural relics of the region. It’s an excellent place to learn about the history and culture of Dong Hoi.

2. Hoang Phuc Pagoda

Hoang Phuc Pagoda is a revered Buddhist temple in Dong Hoi. It’s known for its intricate architecture, serene environment, and panoramic views of the city from the top of the hill.

3. Tam Toa Church

Built-in 1886, Tam Toa Church is a symbol of the French colonial era in Dong Hoi. Despite significant damage during the Vietnam War, the church has been restored and is an excellent example of Gothic and Roman architectures.

Natural Attractions of Dong Hoi

1. Phong Nha - Ke Bang National Park

Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park is home to some of the world’s most spectacular caves and karst formations. The national park offers outdoor adventure activities such as trekking, caving, and camping.

2. Nhat Le Beach

Nhat Le Beach is a serene and scenic beach located on the outskirts of Dong Hoi. The beach offers breathtaking views of the sunrise and sunset and has plenty of water activities for beach enthusiasts.

3. Dong Hoi Citadel

The Dong Hoi Citadel is a 900-year-old ancient fortress that played a crucial role in Vietnamese history. The citadel offers a glimpse of Vietnam’s feudal era and is an excellent example of Vietnamese architectural marvels.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dong Hoi

1. Chay River - Dark Cave

The Chay River - Dark Cave route is a popular trek amongst adventure enthusiasts. The trek involves traversing the Chay River on a boat, hiking through the dense forest and exploring the Dark Cave – an unassuming cave with an underground river and a mud bath.

2. Phong Nha - Ke Bang Trekking Trail

The Phong Nha - Ke Bang trekking trail is a moderate to challenging route that takes travelers through the dense jungle and limestone formations of the national park. The trek offers breathtaking views of the park and is a must-do for nature lovers.

Best Things to Do in Dong Hoi: Activities and Tours

1. Phong Nha - Ke Bang Day Trip

A day trip to the Phong Nha - Ke Bang national park is an experience of a lifetime. The tour includes activities such as caving, trekking, and zip-lining and is perfect for adventure enthusiasts.

2. Tam Toa Church Night Tour

A tour of the Tam Toa Church at night is an excellent way to experience the French colonial-era architecture of the church and its surroundings. The night tour includes a light show and a brief history of the church.

Food and Drink Scene of Dong Hoi

Must-Try Dishes

1. Banh Xeo

Banh Xeo is a crispy pancake-like dish made with rice flour and stuffed with meat, bean sprouts, and vegetables. The dish is served with sweet and sour dipping sauce and is a popular street food in Dong Hoi.

2. Banh Beo

Banh Beo is a steamed rice cake topped with minced shrimp and scallions. The dish is served with a sweet and savory dipping sauce and is a traditional Vietnamese dish.

Local Beverages

1. Vietnamese Coffee

Vietnamese coffee is a strong and rich coffee that is brewed with sweetened condensed milk and is famous all over Vietnam, including Dong Hoi.

2. Bia Hoi

Bia Hoi is a light and refreshing local beer that is made with rice and is served in small glasses. Bia Hoi is typically consumed in the evening and is a staple drink at local bars and restaurants.

Where to Stay in Dong Hoi: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Dong Hoi has plenty of accommodation options for every budget range. The city has several hotels, hostels, and guesthouses that cater to locals as well as tourists. The following are some of the top-rated accommodations in Dong Hoi:

1. Nam Long Plus Hotel

Nam Long Plus Hotel is a 4-star hotel located in the heart of Dong Hoi. The hotel offers comfortable and spacious rooms, a rooftop pool, and an onsite restaurant.

2. Nguyen Sinh Hotel

Nguyen Sinh Hotel is a budget-friendly option for travelers. The hotel offers clean and comfortable rooms, free wifi, and a great location close to Nhat Le Beach.

3. Oxalis Home

Oxalis Home is a cozy guesthouse located in the lush countryside of Dong Hoi. The guesthouse offers clean and comfortable rooms, a garden, and an onsite restaurant serving delicious Vietnamese cuisine.

What to Buy in Dong Hoi: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

1. Handicrafts

Dong Hoi is known for its locally produced handicrafts such as wooden carvings, lacquerware, and embroidery. Visitors can buy beautiful handcrafted souvenirs from local shops and markets.

2. Fish Sauce

Fish sauce is a staple ingredient in Vietnamese cuisine, and Dong Hoi is known for its locally produced fish sauce. Visitors can buy high-quality fish sauce from local markets and shops.

Local Culture and Customs of Dong Hoi

Dong Hoi is a city steeped in rich history and culture. The locals are warm and welcoming towards tourists and take pride in their cultural heritage. Visitors should dress modestly while visiting temples, pagodas, and other religious places. Learning a few basic phrases in Vietnamese can go a long way in connecting with locals.

Best Time to Visit Dong Hoi

The best time to visit Dong Hoi is between March and August when the weather is warm and dry. During this period, visitors can explore the city and its attractions with ease. Dong Hoi can get crowded during the peak season, so it’s best to book accommodation and tours in advance.

Do’s and Don’ts of Dong Hoi

Do:

  • Dress conservatively while visiting religious and cultural sites.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Bargain while shopping at local markets.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the culture and religion of the locals.
  • Take photographs without asking for permission.
  • Litter in public places.
  • Get involved in illegal activities.

Safety and Health

Safety in Dong Hoi

Dong Hoi is generally a safe city to visit. However, visitors should take precautions against petty theft and avoid carrying valuables while exploring the city. It’s best to travel with a trusted guide or group while exploring the wilderness and outdoor locations.

Health in Dong Hoi

Dong Hoi has several public and private medical facilities that provide quality healthcare services. Visitors should carry adequate medication and medical insurance while visiting Dong Hoi.

Getting to Dong Hoi: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Dong Hoi

Dong Hoi has its airport, the Dong Hoi Airport. The airport is well-connected to major cities in Vietnam, including Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh City.

Bus Stations Near Dong Hoi

Dong Hoi has a central bus station that connects the city with other destinations in Vietnam. Visitors can book tickets online or at the station.

Other Options

Visitors can hire private cars or bikes to explore the city and surrounding areas. Dong Hoi also has several taxi and ride-hail services that operate across the city.

How to Get Around Dong Hoi

Dong Hoi is a compact city, and most attractions are within walking distance. Visitors can also hire motorbikes or bicycles to explore the city at their own pace.

Where to Go Next from Dong Hoi: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Hue

Hue is a beautiful city located 160 km south of Dong Hoi and is famous for its historical landmarks, imperial palace, and natural landscapes.

Hoi An

Hoi An is a charming coastal town located 280 km south of Dong Hoi. The town is famous for its old-world charm, ancient architecture, and scenic beaches.

Conclusion

Dong Hoi is a hidden gem in Central Coast Vietnam that is slowly gaining popularity amongst travelers. The city offers a perfect blend of natural wonders, historical landmarks, and rich culture. Follow this comprehensive travel guide to plan your perfect trip to Dong Hoi!
