Introduction to Giao Xuân

Giao Xuân is a small town located in the Central Coast region of Vietnam. It is known for its pristine beaches, lush green forests, and rich cultural heritage. The town is home to several ancient temples, pagodas, and historical sites that offer a glimpse into Vietnam’s fascinating past. Whether you’re looking for a tranquil escape or an adventurous getaway, Giao Xuân has something for everyone.

List of Places to Visit in Giao Xuân

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Giao Xuân is steeped in history and culture. There are several museums and historical places that you must visit during your stay:

  • Hoa Lu Ancient Capital: This site was the capital city of Vietnam during the Dinh and Le Dynasties. It is home to two magnificent temples – Dinh Tien Hoang and Le Dai Hanh.

  • Phat Diem Cathedral: Built in 1892, this cathedral is an exquisite blend of Eastern and Western architecture. It’s known for its stone carvings and stained-glass windows.

  • Bich Dong Pagoda: Located in a cave, this pagoda is renowned for its beautiful scenery and peaceful ambiance.

Natural Attractions of Giao Xuân: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The natural beauty of Giao Xuân is awe-inspiring. There are several outdoor adventures and scenic views that you must explore:

  • Tam Coc: This area is often referred to as “Halong Bay on land” and is known for its stunning karst formations and scenic boat rides.

  • Trang An: This UNESCO World Heritage Site is home to several caves, mountains, and rivers. It’s a paradise for hikers and nature enthusiasts.

  • Van Long Nature Reserve: This wetland reserve is home to a diverse range of flora and fauna, including the rare Delacour’s langur.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Giao Xuân

Giao Xuân is the ideal destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. There are several routes that you can take to explore the beauty of the region:

  • Cuc Phuong National Park: This park is home to several trails that take you through dense forests and beautiful waterfalls.

  • Hoa Lu – Tam Coc: This route takes you through rice paddies, limestone mountains, and beautiful countryside.

  • Trang An – Bai Dinh: This route takes you through caves, temples, and stunning scenery.

Best Things to Do in Giao Xuân: Activities and Tours

There are several things to do in Giao Xuân that will make your trip truly unforgettable:

  • Take a boat ride in Tam Coc and Trang An
  • Explore the ancient temples and pagodas
  • Go on a hike or bike ride in the scenic countryside
  • Visit the local markets and sample the delicious street food
  • Join a cooking class and learn how to make Vietnamese dishes
  • Take a day trip to Ninh Binh or Ha Long Bay

Week-Long Itinerary for Giao Xuân

If you’re planning to spend a week in Giao Xuân, this itinerary will help you make the most of your trip:

Day 1: Arrive in Giao Xuân, relax and explore the town Day 2: Visit the Hoa Lu Ancient Capital and Phat Diem Cathedral Day 3: Take a boat ride in Tam Coc and explore the Bich Dong Pagoda Day 4: Hike or bike in Cuc Phuong National Park Day 5: Take a day trip to Ninh Binh or Ha Long Bay Day 6: Visit the Trang An UNESCO World Heritage Site Day 7: Explore the local markets and sample the delicious street food

Food and Drink Scene of Giao Xuân

Vietnam is known for its delicious cuisine, and Giao Xuân is no exception. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Banh Mi: A Vietnamese sandwich with grilled pork or chicken, vegetables, and mayonnaise.

  • Bun Cha: Grilled pork served with rice noodles, herbs, and dipping sauce.

  • Pho: Beef (or chicken) noodle soup, a staple in Vietnamese cuisine.

  • Com Chay: A delicious vegetarian dish made with broken rice, vegetables, and tofu.

Local Beverages

  • Ca Phe Sua Da: Iced coffee with condensed milk, perfect for hot Vietnamese weather.

  • Tra Da: Vietnamese iced tea, refreshing and sweet.

Where to Stay in Giao Xuân: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

There are several options for accommodations in Giao Xuân, spanning all budget ranges:

  • Luxury: Emeralda Resort Ninh Binh, Tam Coc Garden Resort

  • Mid-Range: Tam Coc Boutique Garden, Espoir Hotel Ninh Binh

  • Budget: Nam Hoa Hotel, Viet Nhat Hotel

What to Buy in Giao Xuân: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

The markets and shops of Giao Xuân are filled with unique and beautiful souvenirs and local products:

  • Handicrafts: Wooden carvings, embroidered fabrics, and hand-painted pottery

  • Traditional clothing: Ao dai (traditional Vietnamese dress) and conical hats

  • Local products: Rice wine, tea, and silk scarves

Local Culture and Customs of Giao Xuân

Vietnamese culture and customs are deeply ingrained in the daily life of Giao Xuân:

  • Respect: The Vietnamese are respectful and polite. Always use proper greetings and respectful language.

  • Dress Code: Dress conservatively when visiting temples and pagodas.

  • Family: Family is highly regarded in Vietnam, and elderly family members are treated with great respect.

Best Time to Visit Giao Xuân

The best time to visit Giao Xuân is from November to April when the weather is dry and sunny with temperatures ranging from the mid-60s to mid-80s.

Do’s and Don’ts of Giao Xuân

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Try the delicious street food.
  • Visit the ancient temples and pagodas.
  • Explore the scenic countryside.

Don’t:

  • Wear disrespectful clothing when visiting temples and pagodas.
  • Disrespect the elderly or families.

Safety and Health

Safety in Giao Xuân

Giao Xuân is considered to be a safe destination for travelers. However, it’s always best to take necessary precautions to avoid petty theft and scams.

Health in Giao Xuân

Vietnam is a tropical country, and certain health risks such as Dengue Fever and Malaria are present. It’s best to get vaccinated and take necessary precautions such as applying insect repellent and mosquito nets.

Getting to Giao Xuân: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Giao Xuân

The closest airport to Giao Xuân is Noi Bai International Airport in Hanoi, about 90 miles away. From there, you can take a taxi or a bus to Giao Xuân.

Bus Stations Near Giao Xuân

  • Ninh Binh: From here, you can take a taxi or a bus to Giao Xuân.

Other Options

  • Train: There are several trains from Hanoi to Ninh Binh. From Ninh Binh, you can take a taxi or bus to Giao Xuân.

How to Get Around in Giao Xuân

The best way to get around Giao Xuân is by motorbike, bicycle, or on foot. Motorbikes and bicycles are readily available for rent.

Where to Go Next from Giao Xuân: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have more time, there are several nearby destinations and excursions you can explore:

  • Ninh Binh: Just a short drive from Giao Xuân, Ninh Binh is home to several temples, pagodas, and natural wonders.

  • Ha Long Bay: This UNESCO World Heritage Site is known for its stunning limestone formations and beautiful boat rides.

Conclusion

Giao Xuân is a hidden gem in Vietnam that is waiting to be explored. With its rich history, beautiful natural scenery, and delicious cuisine, it’s the perfect destination for anyone looking for a unique and memorable travel experience. We hope this guide helps you plan your trip and make the most of your time in Giao Xuân. Happy travels!
