Da Lat Travel Guide - A Comprehensive Tour Guide to Da Lat, Vietnam

Da Lat, located in the central highlands of Vietnam, is a charming resort town that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, rich culture, and an opportunity to relax and unwind amidst the serene surroundings. With its pleasant year-round climate, lush green hills, and scenic views, Da Lat has often been referred to as the “City of Eternal Spring” or the “City of Flowers”.

In this comprehensive Da Lat travel guide, we’ll take you on a journey through the best things to do, sights to see, local culture, food & drink scene and much more. So, let’s start planning your trip to one of Vietnam’s most picturesque destinations.

List of Places to Visit in Da Lat

Da Lat is a city that has something for everyone: outdoor enthusiasts, history lovers, and culture seekers. Our list of must-visit places in Da Lat includes:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Crazy House: This unique architectural masterpiece is designed by a Vietnamese artist, Hang Nga, and is a must-see attraction in Da Lat. The house is covered in whimsical, fairy-tale-like decorations and feels like something out of a dream.

  • Da Lat Railway Station: Built in the early 20th century during the French Indochina era, the Da Lat Railway Station is a beautiful example of French colonial architecture.

  • Lam Dong Museum: The museum houses a vast collection of artifacts showcasing the history and culture of the Lam Dong province.

Natural Attractions of Da Lat: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Lang Biang Mountain: With its stunning panoramic views, Lang Biang Mountain is a haven for outdoor adventurers and nature lovers. The mountain can be hiked or biked, but the best experience is to hire a local guide to take you to the peak.

  • Pongour Waterfall: Located around 50km from Da Lat, the Pongour waterfall is one of the most beautiful waterfalls in Vietnam. It’s a perfect spot for a picnic or a refreshing swim on a hot day.

  • Xuan Huong Lake: The centerpiece of Da Lat city, Xuan Huong Lake is a popular destination for a romantic stroll, yoga, jogging or going for a cycle.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Da Lat

Da Lat offers plenty of opportunities for hiking and mountain biking enthusiasts. The city is surrounded by lush green hills and beautiful trails that lead to some of the best scenic views. Here are some of our top picks:

  • Lam Vien Square to Lang Biang Mountain: This trail is one of the most scenic routes in Da Lat, offering panoramic views of the city.

  • Ho Xuan Huong to Golden Valley: This trail starts from Xuan Huong Lake and takes you through pine forests and small karst hills.

  • Da Lat to Nha Trang: This trail offers an exhilarating downhill ride through stunning landscapes that will take you from Da Lat to the beautiful coastal city of Nha Trang.

Best Things to Do in Da Lat: Activities and Tours

Da Lat is an excellent destination for adventure seekers and those who want to explore the city’s unique culture. Here are some of the best things to do in Da Lat:

  • Canyoning: Join a tour and experience the thrill of rappelling down waterfalls, swimming and jumping into pools of water.

  • Motorbike Tours: Rent a motorbike and explore the outskirts of the city, the country-side, and the nearby attractions.

  • Night Market: Experience the hustle and bustle of the Da Lat Night Market, indulge in the local street food and immerse yourself in the culture and colour of Vietnam.

Food and Drink Scene of Da Lat

Vietnamese cuisine is known for its diverse flavours, and Da Lat is no exception. Your trip to Da Lat would be incomplete without a taste of local cuisine. Here are some of the must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Banh Can: A popular street food dish that consists of smallcakes made from rice flour and served with meat, vegetables, and fish sauce.

  • Banh Trang Nuong: A grilled, crispy rice paper topped with toppings such as egg, cheese, minced meat, or seafood.

  • Com Nieu: A traditional Vietnamese dish where rice is cooked in pottery over a fire, creating a crispy texture and unique flavour.

Local Beverages

  • Da Lat Wine: A unique wine produced using locally grown fruits, such as strawberries and mulberries.

  • Ca Phe Da: A traditional Vietnamese iced coffee, made using strong coffee brewed with sweetened condensed milk and served over ice.

  • Artichoke Tea: A herbal tea made from artichoke leaves that is believed to have many health benefits.

Where to Stay in Da Lat: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Da Lat offers several affordable, comfortable, and well-located accommodation options that cater to all budgets, including guest houses, hostels, and hotels. Here are some of our top recommendations:

  • Terrace Cafe and Hostel: Located in the city center near Xuan Huong Lake, Terrace Cafe, and Hostel is a great budget-friendly option for backpackers and solo travellers.

  • Swiss-Belresort Tuyen Lam Dalat: A luxurious resort that offers stunning lake views, excellent dining options, and a golf course.

  • Dalat Palace Heritage Hotel: A 5-star hotel that offers luxurious rooms, an in-house restaurant, and a convenient location in the heart of the city.

What to Buy in Da Lat: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Da Lat is known for its unique souvenirs and local products that make for excellent gifts. Here are some of the items to look out for:

  • Crazy House Replica: Miniature replicas of the Crazy House make for an interesting gift to take home.

  • Da Lat Coffee: A bag of locally produced Da Lat Coffee makes for an excellent souvenir for coffee enthusiasts.

  • **Embroidery: ** Da Lat is known for its intricately embroidered clothing, accessories and home decor.

Local Culture and Customs of Da Lat

Da Lat has a rich cultural heritage, and it’s essential to be respectful of local customs. Here are some things to be aware of:

  • Dress conservatively: Vietnamese culture, especially in small towns like Da Lat, is fairly conservative, and it is advisable to dress appropriately while visiting religious sites.

  • Remove shoes when entering religious sites: It’s customary to remove your shoes before entering temples or pagodas in Vietnam.

  • Respect elderly and authority: The Vietnamese culture puts a significant emphasis on respecting elderly people and figures of authority such as monks.

Best Time to Visit Da Lat

Da Lat has a relatively stable climate throughout the year, with temperatures ranging from mild to cool. However, different seasons offer different experiences. The best time to visit Da Lat is from December to March when the weather is pleasant, and the flowers are blooming.

Do’s and Don’ts of Da Lat

Here are some of the dos and don’ts to keep in mind while visiting Da Lat:

Do:

  • Be open-minded: Embrace the local culture and customs with an open mind.

  • Learn basic Vietnamese: Try to learn some basic Vietnamese phrases as it can go a long way in helping you communicate with locals.

  • Take the bus: Da Lat has an excellent bus transportation system, which is a cheap and convenient way to get around the city and nearby attractions.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect Buddhist temples: It’s important to be respectful when visiting religious sites in Da Lat.

  • Litter: Da Lat is a beautiful and serene city, and it’s important to not litter and keep the city clean.

  • Haggle aggressively: While haggling is a common practice in Vietnam, it’s important not to be too aggressive.

Safety and Health

Da Lat is a relatively safe city, but visitors should take necessary precautions while travelling. Here are some tips:

Safety in Da Lat

  • Stay vigilant: Keep an eye on your valuables and be aware of your surroundings while exploring the city.

  • Stay on the beaten path: Avoid wandering off alone during the night or going to areas that may be secluded.

Health in Da Lat

  • Food and Water: Be cautious of what you eat and drink, ensure that food is cooked and avoid drinking tap water.

  • Sun Protection: The sun can be strong during the day, and it’s important to protect yourself with hats and sunscreen.

Getting to Da Lat: Transportation Options and Logistics

Da Lat is well-connected by air, bus, and train. Here are the transportation options available:

Airports Near Da Lat

  • Lien Khuong International Airport: This airport is around 30km from Da Lat and is the primary airport for all domestic and international flights to Da Lat.

Bus Stations Near Da Lat

  • Da Lat Bus Station: Several bus companies operate from Da Lat Bus Station, offering services to nearby towns like Nha Trang and Ho Chi Minh City.

  • Phuong Trang Bus Station: This bus station offers services to several other destinations in Vietnam, including Hoi An and Danang.

Other Options

  • Train: Da Lat has a small train station that can be accessed from HCMC.

  • Private Cars and Taxis: Taxis are readily available in Da Lat and are a convenient way to explore the city and nearby attractions.

How to Get Around in Da Lat

Da Lat has several options to explore the city and its surroundings. These include:

  • Motorbike and bicycle rentals: Renting a motorbike or a bicycle is a popular way to get around the city and explore nearby attractions.

  • Taxis: Taxis are readily available in Da Lat and are a convenient way to get around the city.

  • Buses: Da Lat has several local bus routes that are cheap and an excellent way to explore the city.

Where to Go Next from Da Lat: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Da Lat is also a great base to explore nearby towns and destinations. Here are some nearby attractions that you can explore:

  • Mekong Delta: A popular destination for its floating markets, lush green rice paddies, and unique riverine communities.

  • Ho Chi Minh City: Known as Vietnam’s largest city, Ho Chi Minh City is a bustling metropolis that offers a mix of historical sites, culture and a vibrant nightlife.

  • Nha Trang: A coastal city known for its beaches, clear waters, and many activities such as snorkeling and scuba diving.

Conclusion

Da Lat is truly a hidden gem of Vietnam. It’s a city that offers a unique blend of natural beauty, culture, and serene surroundings. We hope this comprehensive Da Lat travel guide has helped you plan your trip to this beautiful city. Plan your trip today and get ready for an unforgettable experience!
