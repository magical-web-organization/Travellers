Introduction to Co To Island

Co To Island is a lovely island in Northern Vietnam, located in the waters of the Gulf of Tonkin. It is a paradise for beach lovers where you can enjoy the pristine beaches, crystal-clear waters and colorful coral reefs. The island’s natural beauty combined with a rich culture and unique local lifestyle make it a must-visit destination in Vietnam. Join us in this guide to discover the best of Co To Island!

List of Places to Visit in Co To Island

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

For those interested in history and culture, Co To Island offers several museums and temples to explore. Let’s take a look at some of the most popular ones.

Co To Island Museum

The Co To Island Museum is a great place to learn about the local culture and history. It showcases a vast collection of items that the islanders used in the past like traditional clothing, fishing equipment, agricultural tools, pottery, and more.

Hang Doi (Cave Temple)

Hang Doi is a famous temple located in a cave in Co To Island. The cave is 20 meters high, 60 meters deep and has three main chambers. The temple was built in the 18th century and is dedicated to Princess Ngoc Van, a legendary figure in Vietnamese history.

Natural Attractions of Co To Island: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Co To Island is a destination for outdoor enthusiasts looking to explore the island’s natural beauty. Here are some of the top outdoor attractions of Co To Island.

Van Chay Beach

Van Chay Beach is the most famous beach on Co To Island. It has a 3 kilometer-long coastline with soft white sand and crystal-clear water, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and relaxing under the sun.

Co To Lighthouse

At the peak of the Dat Do Mountain, located on the eastern side of Co To Island stands the Co To Lighthouse. It is the highest point here, offering a panoramic view of the island and the surrounding sea.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Co To Island

Co To Island offers breathtaking views that can be explored on foot or by bike. Here are some recommended routes.

  • Ha Van Trail: This 7-kilometer trail starting from Van Chay Beach leads you through thick forests, limestone mountains, and ends at the Co To Lighthouse.

  • Binh Khung Lan Trail: This trail will take you to scenic places such as Bong Beach, Co To Bungalow, and Lan Ha Bay. You can reach these places by renting a bike.

Best Things to Do in Co To Island: Activities and Tours

Co To Island offers various activities to keep you engaged and entertained. Here are some of the best activities to enjoy on Co To Island.

Snorkeling and Scuba Diving

The water in Co To Island is crystal clear, making it perfect for snorkeling and scuba diving. You can go on diving tours to explore the beautiful coral reefs and get an up-close look at the diverse marine life.

Kayaking and Boat Tours

If you want to explore the island’s coastline, you can rent a kayak or take a boat tour. It’s a great way to discover the hidden coves and secluded beaches of Co To Island.

Week-Long Itinerary for Co To Island

To make the most of your stay on Co To Island, here’s a suggested week-long itinerary you can follow:

Day Activity
Day 1-2 Relax and unwind on Van Chay Beach
Day 3 Explore the Co To Island Museum and Hang Doi
Day 4 Take a hike on the Ha Van Trail
Day 5 Go snorkeling or scuba diving near the coral reefs
Day 6 Kayak or take a boat tour to explore the island’s coast
Day 7 Watch the sunset from the Co To Lighthouse

Food and Drink Scene of Co To Island

Co To Island is not only famous for its natural beauty but also for its tasty local food and drinks.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Banh Cuon: A popular Vietnamese dish made of steamed rice cake, stuffed with minced pork and mushroom, and served with dipping sauce.

  • Cha Tom: A tasty shrimp cake made with crushed shrimp, pork fat, and mung bean noodles, seasoned with fish sauce, then grilled or fried.

  • Seafood Hotpot: A flavorful soup with a variety of seafood like fish, shrimp, crab, squid, and vegetables.

Local Beverages

  • Rice Wine: A traditional Vietnamese alcoholic drink made from fermented rice.

  • Ca Phe Trung: A Vietnamese coffee made with a duck egg, condensed milk and coffee powder, and served hot.

Where to Stay in Co To Island: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

From luxury hotels to budget-friendly guesthouses, Co To Island has a wide range of accommodation options to choose from.

Some of the best places to stay include:

  • Co To Island Resort & Spa: This luxury resort features modern amenities and a lush garden setting. It is a five-minute walk from Van Chay Beach.

  • Co To Cozy Hostel: A cozy and budget-friendly hostel located in the center of Co To Island. It offers basic facilities, clean rooms, and free breakfast.

  • Co To Honey House: This guesthouse is situated on the edge of the forest, near the beach. It offers a tranquil setting, modern amenities, and excellent hospitality.

What to Buy in Co To Island: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When visiting Co To Island, you can bring home some unique souvenirs and local products to remember your trip. Here are some suggestions:

  • Jewelry made with seashells and pearls
  • Artwork and paintings by local artists
  • Dried seafood and seaweed products like shrimp, squid, and Nori

Local Culture and Customs of Co To Island

Co To Island has a distinct culture and unique local lifestyle that visitors can witness and learn about. The local people on the island make their living mainly by fishing and agriculture. They celebrate traditional festivals throughout the year and guests are welcome to participate.

Best Time to Visit Co To Island

The best time to visit Co To Island is from March to October when the weather is mild and there is plenty of sunshine. Avoid visiting during the monsoon season (November to January) when the island experiences heavy rain and winds.

Do’s and Don’ts of Co To Island

Here are some important tips to follow:

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs
  • Bring sunscreen, a hat, and sunglasses
  • Keep the beaches and shores clean

Don’t:

  • Litter or pollute the environment
  • Touch or take items from coral reefs
  • Disrupt the natural habitat of the island’s wildlife

Safety and Health

Safety in Co To Island

Co To Island is a safe destination for travelers, with low crime rates. However, always be cautious, keep your valuables safe, and stay aware of your surroundings.

Health in Co To Island

Make sure to drink only bottled water and avoid consuming street food that is not cooked or washed properly. Bring insect repellent and wear protective clothing to prevent mosquito bites, as dengue fever is prevalent in some areas of Vietnam.

Getting to Co To Island: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Co To Island

The nearest airport to Co To Island is Van Don International Airport, located 45km from the island.

Bus Stations Near Co To Island

From Hanoi, you can take a bus to Cai Rong Port, then catch a ferry to reach Co To Island. The journey takes around 3-4 hours, depending on the weather and sea conditions.

Other Options

Alternatively, you can hire a private car or travel by motorbike to the Cai Rong Port. From there, you can take a ferry to Co To Island.

How to Get Around in Co To Island

To get around Co To Island, rent a bike or hire a motorbike. You can also take a taxi or rent a jeep with a driver to explore the island. Walking is another option if you plan to stay near Van Chay Beach.

Where to Go Next from Co To Island: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

If you have more time to spare, consider visiting these nearby destinations:

  • Ha Long Bay: A UNESCO World Heritage Site with beautiful limestone mountains and caves.

  • Mong Cai City: Known for its beaches, seafood, and border crossings to China.

  • Cua Ong Temple: A famous temple located in Quang Ninh Province, just 10km from Halong Bay.

Conclusion

Co To Island is one of the best island destinations in Vietnam, offering breathtaking natural scenery, cultural experiences, and outdoor activities. Use this travel guide to plan your trip and make your journey to Co To Island unforgettable.
