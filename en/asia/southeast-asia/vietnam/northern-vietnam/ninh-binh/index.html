Introduction to Ninh Binh

Ninh Binh, a province in Northern Vietnam, is known for its natural beauty. With stunning landscapes and ancient relics, this region is becoming popular among tourists visiting Vietnam. It’s located about 100 kilometers south of Hanoi, and it’s easily accessible by car or train.

List of Places to Visit in Ninh Binh

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Hoa Lu Ancient Capital
  • Bai Dinh Pagoda
  • Phat Diem Cathedral
  • Van Long Nature Reserve

Natural Attractions of Ninh Binh: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Tam Coc-Bich Dong
  • Hang Mua Peak
  • Trang An Scenic Landscape Complex
  • Cuc Phuong National Park

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ninh Binh

  • Van Long Nature Reserve
  • Tam Coc-Bich Dong
  • Cuc Phuong National Park
  • Trang An Scenic Landscape Complex

Best Things to Do in Ninh Binh: Activities and Tours

  • Boat trip on Trang An river
  • Rock climbing at Hang Mua Peak
  • Biking through the countryside
  • Kayaking at Tam Coc

Week-Long Itinerary for Ninh Binh

Day Activities
1 Visit Hoa Lu Ancient Capital, Bai Dinh Pagoda, and Phat Diem Cathedral
2 Explore Tam Coc-Bich Dong and take a boat trip on the river
3 Hike or bike to Hang Mua Peak and enjoy the sunrise/sunset
4 Visit Cuc Phuong National Park and go on a nature trek
5 Kayak at Tam Coc and explore more caves
6 Relax at a local spa and enjoy the hot springs
7 Visit Van Long Nature Reserve and take a boat tour

Food and Drink Scene of Ninh Binh

Must-Try Dishes

  • Ninh Binh goat meat
  • Cơm Cháy Hoành Thánh
  • Ninh Binh eel vermicelli
  • Sautéed morning glory with garlic

Local Beverages

  • Trà sen (lotus tea)
  • Nước Mía (sugarcane juice)
  • Mật ong nhà nghèo (poor man’s honey)

What to Buy in Ninh Binh: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Handmade souvenirs like silk, pottery, and vases
  • Traditional art like Dong Ho paintings and cinnamon paintings

Local Culture and Customs of Ninh Binh

  • Respect for the elders and ancestors
  • Modesty in clothing and behavior

Best Time to Visit Ninh Binh

The best time to visit Ninh Binh is from February to May or September to November, when the weather is cooler and more stable.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ninh Binh

Do:

  • Dress modestly when visiting temples and religious sites
  • Take off shoes before entering temples or people’s houses
  • Try the local dishes and drinks

Don’t:

  • Litter or pollute the environment
  • Haggling too much

Safety and Health

Safety in Ninh Binh

Ninh Binh is generally safe, but be careful of pickpocketing and other types of petty crime, especially in crowded areas.

Health in Ninh Binh

  • It’s recommended to drink bottled water and avoid street food.
  • If you need medical attention, seek professional help at a hospital or clinic.

Getting to Ninh Binh: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Ninh Binh

The nearest airports to Ninh Binh are Noi Bai International Airport in Hanoi and Cat Bi International Airport in Hai Phong.

Bus Stations Near Ninh Binh

Some bus companies that connect Hanoi to Ninh Binh are Hoang Long, The Sinh Tourist, and Kumho.

Other Options

You can also rent a car or book a private transfer from Hanoi.

How to Get Around in Ninh Binh

The most common mode of transportation in Ninh Binh is motorbikes, which can be rented from guesthouses and hotels. You can also hire a car or a taxi.

Where to Go Next from Ninh Binh: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Conclusion

Ninh Binh is a hidden gem in Northern Vietnam, offering a mix of natural beauty and cultural richness. This travel guide has introduced you to the best attractions, activities, food, and logistics of Ninh Binh. Don’t hesitate to begin planning your next adventure to this enchanting province.
