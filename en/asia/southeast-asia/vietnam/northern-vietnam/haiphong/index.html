Introduction to Haiphong

Located in northern Vietnam, Haiphong is a bustling port city that has long been an important trading hub in the country. With a rich history and culture, Haiphong offers visitors plenty of interesting places to visit, delicious local cuisine to try, and outdoor adventures to embark on. In this guide, we have compiled all the information you need to plan your trip to Haiphong and make the most of your vacation.

List of Places to Visit in Haiphong

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Hai Phong Opera House - A stunning piece of French colonial architecture, now used for cultural events and concerts.
  2. Haiphong Museum - Learn about the history and culture of Haiphong and its people.
  3. Du Hang Pagoda - A beautiful Buddhist temple that dates back to the 16th century.
  4. Hang Kenh Communal House - An ancient communal house that serves as a showcase for traditional architecture and art in Haiphong.

Natural Attractions of Haiphong: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Do Son Beach - A popular destination for swimming and sunbathing.
  2. Cat Ba Island - A beautiful island with hiking trails, a national park, and scenic beaches.
  3. Lan Ha Bay - A stunningly beautiful bay that is less crowded than its famous neighbor, Ha Long Bay.
  4. Bach Dang River - A scenic river that runs through the heart of Haiphong and is home to a lively night market.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Haiphong

  1. Du Hang Pagoda Trail - A short hike that takes visitors through beautiful scenery, including a lotus pond and a bamboo forest.
  2. Voi Mountain Trail - A more challenging hike that rewards visitors with panoramic views of the city and surrounding landscapes.
  3. Cat Ba Island Biking Trail - A 30-kilometer route that takes visitors through some of the most scenic spots on the island.

Best Things to Do in Haiphong: Activities and Tours

  1. Take a boat tour of Ha Long Bay.
  2. Visit the Du Hang Pagoda and take a scenic hike.
  3. Enjoy the local cuisine at a traditional restaurant.
  4. Go shopping at the Hang Khoai Night Market.
  5. Visit the Haiphong Opera House and enjoy a cultural performance.

Food and Drink Scene of Haiphong

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Banh Da Cua - A crab noodle dish that is a local specialty.
  2. Nem Hai San - A type of spring roll made with seafood and herbs.
  3. Ca Nuc Kho To - A braised fish dish that is both sweet and savory.
  4. La Vong Grilled Fish - A grilled fish dish that is famous in the region.

Local Beverages

  1. Bia Haiphong - A local beer that is refreshing and crisp.
  2. Cafe Sua Da - A Vietnamese iced coffee that is sweet and creamy.
  3. Nuoc Mia - A sweet and refreshing sugar cane juice.

Where to Stay in Haiphong: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. AVANI Hai Phong Harbour View Hotel - A luxurious hotel with stunning views of the harbor.
  2. Hoang Hai Hotel - A budget-friendly hotel that is centrally located.
  3. Nam Cuong Hotel - A well-appointed hotel with a fitness center and swimming pool.

What to Buy in Haiphong: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Lacquerware - Beautifully decorated bowls, plates, and other household items.
  2. Silk Products - High-quality silk scarves, clothing, and accessories.
  3. Handicrafts - Intricately carved wooden statues and other handmade items.

Local Culture and Customs of Haiphong

  1. Dress conservatively when visiting religious sites.
  2. Remove your shoes before entering someone’s home.
  3. Bargaining is common when shopping at markets and street vendors.

Best Time to Visit Haiphong

The best time to visit Haiphong is from October to December, when the weather is cooler and drier, making it perfect for outdoor activities and sightseeing.

Do’s and Don’ts of Haiphong

Do:

  1. Try the local food and drink.
  2. Dress appropriately for religious sites.
  3. Learn a few basic Vietnamese phrases.

Don’t:

  1. Share your personal information with strangers.
  2. Photograph people without their permission.
  3. Leave your valuables unattended in public places.

Safety and Health

Safety in Haiphong

Haiphong is generally safe for travelers, but it’s always wise to take precautions such as staying alert when traveling at night and keeping your valuables close to you.

Health in Haiphong

It’s advisable to make sure you have all the necessary vaccinations before traveling to Vietnam. Also, be sure to drink bottled water and practice good hygiene to avoid illnesses such as food poisoning.

Getting to Haiphong: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Haiphong

  1. Cat Bi International Airport - The main airport that serves Haiphong, with connections to major cities in Vietnam and other countries in Southeast Asia.

Bus Stations Near Haiphong

  1. Haiphong Bus Station - The main bus station in Haiphong, with regular services to other cities in Vietnam.

Other Options

  1. Train - The nearest train station is in Hanoi, which is about two hours away from Haiphong.

How to Get Around in Haiphong

The most convenient way to get around Haiphong is by taxi or motorbike taxi. However, if you’re feeling adventurous, you can also rent a bicycle or motorbike to explore the city at your leisure.

Where to Go Next from Haiphong: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Hanoi - The bustling capital city of Vietnam, known for its rich history and culture.
  2. Ha Long Bay - A breathtakingly beautiful UNESCO World Heritage Site, known for its iconic limestone karsts and emerald-colored waters.
  3. Ninh Binh - A picturesque province famous for its stunning natural beauty, including caves, waterfalls, and rice paddies.

Conclusion

Haiphong is a fascinating destination that is a great addition to any Vietnam travel itinerary. Whether you’re interested in history, culture, food, or outdoor adventures, Haiphong has something to offer everyone. With this comprehensive guide, you’ll be able to plan your trip to Haiphong with ease and make unforgettable memories of your Vietnam vacation.
