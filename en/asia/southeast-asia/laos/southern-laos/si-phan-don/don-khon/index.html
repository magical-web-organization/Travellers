Don Khon, a charming island in the Si Phan Don area of Southern Laos, is a perfect place to get away from the hustle and bustle of city life. The island is surrounded by the Mekong River, where visitors can enjoy water activities like swimming, kayaking, and fishing. Tourists can also immerse themselves in the local culture by checking out the many museums and historical sites on the island. Read on to discover everything you need to know before visiting Don Khon.

Introduction to Don Khon

Don Khon is a small island in Southern Laos known for its serene atmosphere, stunning views, and rich cultural history. Visitors can expect to find peaceful surroundings and a laid-back way of life, making it a popular destination for those looking to relax and connect with nature.

There are several things to do on the island, ranging from outdoor adventures to culturally significant sites, which makes it an ideal destination for backpackers and travelers looking for a unique experience.

List of Places to Visit in Don Khon

Here are some of the must-visit sites in Don Khon:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Wat Chomphet: A beautiful temple on the east side of the island. Visitors can explore the temple and enjoy the scenic views of the surrounding areas.
  • Liphi Waterfall: A popular site where visitors can go swimming or relax on the beach.
  • Old French Railway Bridge: A historic bridge that was built by the French in the early 1900s.
  • Don Khon Pier: A great place to watch the sunset and see the boats that come in and out of the harbor.

Natural Attractions of Don Khon: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • 4000 Islands: A collection of small islands in the Mekong River, where visitors can enjoy various water activities such as kayaking, swimming, and fishing.
  • Li Phi Falls: A breathtaking waterfall that is also known as the “Devil’s Corridor.”
  • Dolphin Watching: The Mekong River is home to the endangered Irrawaddy dolphins, and visitors can take a boat tour to see them.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Don Khon

Don Khon is a perfect place for outdoor enthusiasts who love to hike and bike. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • Don Khon Island Bike Tour: A four-hour bike tour that takes visitors around the island to explore the surroundings and see the beautiful views.
  • Pha Dai Trek: A three-hour trek that takes visitors through the jungle and across streams to reach a stunning viewpoint overlooking the Mekong River.

Best Things to Do in Don Khon: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of things to do in Don Khon, from exploring the natural wonders of the island to immersing yourself in the local culture. Here are some activities and tours to consider:

  • Kayaking Tour: A half-day guided tour that takes visitors around the island to explore the surroundings and see the beautiful views.
  • Village Tour: A chance to learn about the local culture and customs of Don Khon by visiting the nearby villages.
  • Sunset Boat Tour: A great way to experience the Mekong River while enjoying the beautiful sunset.

Food and Drink Scene of Don Khon

Don Khon Island has plenty of authentic local restaurants that visitors can visit. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Khao Niew Mamuang: A popular dessert made of sweet, sticky rice and mango.
  • Khao Soi: A spicy noodle soup that is a staple in Laos cuisine.
  • Lao Pho: A traditional soup made with beef, rice noodles, and herbs.

Local Beverages

  • Beer Lao: A popular beer in Laos that is refreshing and light.
  • Lao Lao: A potent rice whiskey that is a favorite among locals.
  • Coconut Water: A refreshing and healthy drink that can be found all over the island.

Where to Stay in Don Khon: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Don Khon Island has a range of accommodation options, including hotels, hostels, and guesthouses. Here are some popular choices:

  • Sala Don Khone Hotel: A luxury hotel with beautiful views of the Mekong River.
  • Little Eden Guesthouse: A budget-friendly guesthouse with clean and comfortable rooms.
  • Somphamit Guesthouse: A cozy guesthouse that is located near the Li Phi Falls.

What to Buy in Don Khon: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Don Khon Island is known for its handmade products and unique souvenirs, making it a great place to shop for gifts. Here are some of the top items to buy:

  • Hand-woven Fabrics: Buy a traditional Lao skirt or scarf made with locally sourced materials.
  • Handicrafts: Wooden carvings, pottery, and hand-woven baskets are some of the many handicrafts available on the island.
  • Silk Products: Visit the local silk weavers to purchase silk products like scarves and wall hangings.

Local Culture and Customs of Don Khon

Laos has a rich cultural history and unique customs that visitors should be aware of. Here are some things to keep in mind when visiting Don Khon:

  • Dress Modestly: As a sign of respect for the local customs, be sure to dress modestly and cover your shoulders and knees.
  • Remove Shoes: It is customary to remove your shoes before entering someone’s home or a temple.
  • Respect the Monks: When visiting temples, be respectful of the monks and their daily routines.

Best Time to Visit Don Khon

The best time to visit Don Khon is from November to February when the weather is dry and cool. The temperatures are comfortable, and the Mekong River is calm during this time, making it great for water-related activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Don Khon

Do:

  • Try the Local Cuisine: Lao cuisine is delicious and unique, so be sure to try some of the local dishes.
  • Be Respectful: As with any destination, be respectful of the local culture and customs.
  • Explore the Outdoors: Don Khon is known for its stunning views and natural beauty, so take some time to explore the island’s outdoor spaces.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the Monks: When visiting temples, always show respect to the monks and adhere to their daily routines.
  • Litter: Keep the island clean by disposing of waste properly.
  • Overuse Water: Water is a scarce resource on the island, so be sure to use it sparingly.

Safety and Health

When traveling to Don Khon, it’s important to take precautions to ensure your safety and health.

Safety in Don Khon

  • Be Mindful of Wildlife: Don Khon is home to various snakes and insects, so be cautious when exploring the outdoors.
  • Use Sunscreen: Protect your skin from the sun’s harmful rays by using a high SPF sunscreen.
  • Keep Valuables Safe: Don Khon is a safe place to travel, but it’s essential to keep your valuables safe at all times.

Health in Don Khon

  • Drink Bottled Water: Tap water is not potable on the island, so be sure to drink bottled water to avoid getting sick.
  • Bring Insect Repellent: Protect yourself from mosquito-borne illnesses by using insect repellent.
  • Get Vaccinated: Make sure you are up-to-date on your vaccinations before traveling to Laos.

Getting to Don Khon: Transportation Options and Logistics

Don Khon Island can only be accessed by boat, and there are several transportation options available. Here’s what you need to know:

Airports Near Don Khon

The nearest airport is Pakse International Airport, which is around four hours’ drive from Don Khon Island.

Bus Stations Near Don Khon

The closest bus station is in Ban Nakasang, which is located on the mainland. From there, visitors can take a boat to Don Khon Island.

Other Options

Private transfer services are also available from Pakse to Don Khon Island.

How to Get Around in Don Khon

The best way to get around the island is by bike. Visitors can rent bikes from their hotels or hostels for a small daily fee. Alternatively, visitors can also explore the island on foot or take a tuk-tuk or boat ride for a more leisurely pace.

Where to Go Next from Don Khon: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Don Khon Island is a perfect place to relax, and there are plenty of nearby destinations to explore before or after your stay. Here are some popular options:

  • Don Det: Another popular island in the Si Phan Don area with plenty of things to do and see.
  • Pakse: A charming city known for its French colonial architecture and beautiful temples.
  • Siem Reap (Cambodia): A popular destination known for its ancient temples, including the famous Angkor Wat.

Conclusion

Don Khon Island is a hidden gem in Southern Laos with plenty of natural beauty, cultural significance, and exciting activities to keep visitors entertained. With the above information, you are now well-equipped and knowledgeable about what to do, where to stay, what to eat and drink, and how to get around while exploring this stunning island.
