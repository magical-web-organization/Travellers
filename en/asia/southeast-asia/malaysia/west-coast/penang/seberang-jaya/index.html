Introduction to Seberang Jaya

Seberang Jaya is a town in Penang, Malaysia, located on the mainland of the state of Penang. It is connected to the island of Penang by the Penang Bridge and the ferry service from Butterworth. Seberang Jaya is considered as one of the fast-growing towns in the region and is home to various residential areas, commercial centers, and cultural sites.
