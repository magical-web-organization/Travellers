Terengganu is a state located on the east coast of Malaysia, in Southeast Asia. This beautiful state is known for its pristine beaches, crystal clear waters, rich culture, and natural beauty. If you are planning a trip to Terengganu, this comprehensive travel guide will help you discover the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, what to eat, and much more.

Introduction to Terengganu

Terengganu is one of the most beautiful states in Malaysia and is located on the east coast of the country. This state has a lot to offer visitors, including beautiful beaches, coral reefs, unique cultural experiences, and an abundance of natural beauty.

The state is also known for its rich history, which is reflected in the many museums and historical sites in the region. Terengganu is an ideal destination for those who love the outdoors, with hiking and biking routes, water sports, and some of the most beautiful scenery in Malaysia.

List of Places to Visit in Terengganu

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Terengganu State Museum - This is one of the largest museums in Malaysia and houses an impressive collection of artifacts that showcase the rich history and culture of Terengganu.
  2. Islamic Civilization Park - This park features a collection of Islamic structures, including a replica of the Masjid Al-Haram in Mecca and several other mosques and tombs.
  3. Tengku Tengah Zaharah Mosque - This mosque is one of the most beautiful in Malaysia. Its unique design is inspired by traditional Islamic architecture and incorporates modern elements.

Natural Attractions of Terengganu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Redang Island - This is one of the most popular islands in Malaysia and is known for its crystal-clear waters, white sandy beaches, and coral reefs.
  2. Kenyir Lake - This is the largest man-made lake in Southeast Asia and is home to an abundance of wildlife, including over 300 species of birds and several species of freshwater fish.
  3. Sekayu Waterfall - This waterfall is surrounded by lush rainforest and is a popular spot for swimming and picnicking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Terengganu

  1. Chemerong Waterfall - This is a challenging hike that offers stunning views of the waterfall and surrounding rainforest.
  2. Bukit Keluang - This scenic hill offers panoramic views of the South China Sea and is a popular spot for hiking and paragliding.
  3. Kenyir Elephant Conservation Village - This is a unique opportunity to hike through the rainforest and see elephants in their natural habitat.

Best Things to Do in Terengganu: Activities and Tours

  1. Snorkeling and Diving - Terengganu is home to some of the most beautiful coral reefs in Malaysia and offers excellent snorkeling and diving opportunities.
  2. Island Hopping - Explore some of the most beautiful islands around Terengganu, including Redang Island, Kapas Island, and Perhentian Island.
  3. River Cruises - Take a peaceful cruise down Terengganu’s rivers and enjoy the beautiful scenery and wildlife.

Week-Long Itinerary for Terengganu

Day Activities
1 Explore Terengganu State Museum and Tengku Tengah Zaharah Mosque

Food and Drink Scene of Terengganu

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Nasi Dagang - A traditional dish made from rice cooked with coconut milk and served with fish curry.
  2. Sata - A popular snack made from grilled fish wrapped in a banana leaf.
  3. Keropok Lekor - This is a local specialty made from fish paste and served with a spicy dipping sauce.

Local Beverages

  1. Teh Tarik - A sweet, creamy tea that is popular throughout Malaysia.
  2. Air Kelapa - Fresh coconut water served straight from the coconut.
  3. Barley Drink - A refreshing and cooling drink made from barley, served with a dash of lime.

Where to Stay in Terengganu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  1. Tanjong Jara Resort - Located on a secluded beach, this 5-star resort offers luxury accommodations and a range of activities.
  2. Setiu Wetlands Homestay - Experience local hospitality at this eco-tourism homestay that offers a range of activities, including bird watching and kayaking.
  3. Felda Residence Kuala Terengganu - This budget-friendly hotel is located in the heart of Kuala Terengganu and offers comfortable rooms at an affordable price.

What to Buy in Terengganu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  1. Batik Fabric - Terengganu is known for its vibrant batik fabrics, which make great souvenirs or gifts.
  2. Keropok Lekor - This local snack is also available to take home in packages.
  3. Traditional Handicrafts - Look for local handicrafts, such as hand-woven baskets or wooden carvings to bring home as mementos.

Local Culture and Customs of Terengganu

Terengganu has a unique and rich culture that is reflected in the food, art, and traditions of its people. Visitors should take the time to learn about the local customs and try to experience as much of the local culture as possible.

Best Time to Visit Terengganu

The best time to visit Terengganu is from March to September, when the weather is dry and sunny. However, the island destinations, such as Redang Island, are closed during the monsoon season from November to February.

Do’s and Don’ts of Terengganu

Do:

  1. Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
  2. Try local food and drinks, they are delicious and unique.
  3. Respect the local culture and traditions.

Don’t:

  1. Sunbathe topless or wear revealing clothing.
  2. Litter or damage the environment in any way.
  3. Touch or disturb wildlife.

Safety and Health

Safety in Terengganu

Terengganu is a safe state to visit, but visitors should take standard precautions to ensure their safety.

Health in Terengganu

Visitors should make sure to drink only bottled water and take precautions against mosquito-borne illnesses such as dengue fever.

Getting to Terengganu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Terengganu

Sultan Mahmud Airport is the closest airport to Terengganu and is located just outside Kuala Terengganu. This airport is served by several airlines, including Malaysia Airlines, AirAsia, and Firefly.

Bus Stations Near Terengganu

The main bus station in Terengganu is located in Kuala Terengganu and offers bus services to destinations throughout Malaysia.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a car or take a taxi to get around Terengganu.

How to Get Around in Terengganu

The best way to get around Terengganu is by car or taxi. Visitors can also hire a scooter or bicycle to explore the local area.

Where to Go Next from Terengganu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  1. Kuala Lumpur - Malaysia’s vibrant capital city is just a short flight from Terengganu and offers a range of cultural and entertainment experiences.
  2. Cameron Highlands - This beautiful region is known for its tea plantations and offers a cool climate and stunning scenery.
  3. Taman Negara National Park - This park is home to an abundance of wildlife, including elephants, tigers, and monkeys, and offers a range of outdoor activities.

Conclusion

Terengganu is a beautiful state that offers visitors a range of outdoor activities, cultural experiences, and natural beauty. By following this comprehensive travel guide, visitors can experience the best that Terengganu has to offer and make the most of their time in this stunning destination.
