Introduction to Lankaran

Nestled in the Talysh region of Azerbaijan, Lankaran is a coastal town that offers incredible natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and exciting outdoor activities. Located on the shores of the Caspian Sea, Lankaran is blessed with a humid subtropical climate that makes it a popular holiday destination all year round. In this comprehensive tour guide, we’ll take you through the best things to do, see, eat, and stay in Lankaran.

List of Places to Visit in Lankaran

From museums and historical places to scenic views and outdoor adventures, Lankaran has a lot to offer. Here is a list of the top places you should visit in Lankaran.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Lankaran Fortress: Built in the 18th century by the Persian Empire, the Lankaran Fortress is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The fortress has a unique architecture and offers a panoramic view of the city.
  • Lankaran Palace: Located in the heart of the city, the Lankaran Palace is a must-visit attraction for history lovers. The palace was built in the early 20th century and is an excellent example of Azerbaijani architecture.
  • Lankaran Historical Museum: If you want to learn more about the history of Lankaran, visit the Lankaran Historical Museum. The museum has a vast collection of artifacts, including pottery, coins, and manuscripts.

Natural Attractions of Lankaran: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Gizil-Agach State Reserve: Located 10 km from Lankaran, Gizil-Agach State Reserve is a protected area that offers stunning landscapes and a wide range of outdoor activities, including hiking, trekking, and bird watching.
  • Istisu Sanatorium: If you want to relax and rejuvenate, visit the Istisu Sanatorium. The sanatorium is famous for its hot springs, which are believed to have therapeutic properties.
  • Yanar Bulag: Yanar Bulag is a natural gas flame that burns continuously. Legend has it that the flame was lit by a boy who accidentally dropped a match while he was tending sheep.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lankaran

Lankaran is an excellent destination for outdoor enthusiasts. The town offers a variety of hiking and biking trails that cater to all levels of expertise. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Lankaran.

  • Lankaran-Divichi Trail: This 12 km trail takes you through the lush forests and stunning coastal views of Lankaran. The trail is relatively easy and suitable for hikers and bikers of all levels.
  • Gizil-Agach State Reserve Trail: This 20 km trail takes you through the protected forests of Gizil-Agach State Reserve. The trail is ideal for experienced hikers and bikers who want a challenge.
  • Yanar Bulag Trail: This 5 km trek takes you to the Yanar Bulag natural gas flame. The trail is relatively easy and suitable for hikers of all levels.

Best Things to Do in Lankaran: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, Lankaran offers many other activities and tours that are worth experiencing. Here are some of the best things to do in Lankaran.

  • Caspian Sea Cruise: A cruise on the Caspian Sea is a must-do activity in Lankaran. The cruise takes you to the nearby islands and offers stunning views of the sea.
  • Parasailing: Get a bird’s eye view of the Caspian Sea as you parasail over it. This exciting activity is suitable for all age groups.
  • Horseback Riding: Explore the beautiful countryside of Lankaran on horseback. This activity is perfect for anyone who wants to experience the Azerbaijani way of life.

Food and Drink Scene of Lankaran

No trip to Lankaran is complete without trying the local cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Lankaran Piti: A traditional Azerbaijani dish made with meat, potatoes, chickpeas, and herbs. The dish is cooked in clay pots and served with a side of bread.
  • Lavangi: A stuffed chicken dish that is a specialty of Lankaran. The chicken is stuffed with herbs, nuts, and dried fruits and then baked in a tandoor.
  • Kebab: Azerbaijani kebab is famous worldwide for its tender, juicy meat and unique blend of spices. Lankaran has many kebab shops that serve delicious kebabs.

Local Beverages

  • Azerbaijani Tea: Azerbaijani tea is a black tea that is brewed with saffron and served with sugar cubes. The tea is an important part of Azerbaijani culture and is often served during social gatherings.
  • Ayran: Ayran is a yogurt-based drink that is a popular beverage in Azerbaijan. The drink is refreshing and perfect for hot summer days.

Where to Stay in Lankaran: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Lankaran offers a range of accommodation options to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the best hotels, hostels, and guesthouses in Lankaran.

  • Lankaran Springs Wellness Resort: A luxury resort that offers stunning views of the Caspian Sea. The resort has an outdoor pool, spa, and fitness center.
  • Old Castle Hotel: Located in the heart of the city, Old Castle Hotel is a budget-friendly hotel that offers comfortable rooms and basic amenities.
  • Mardakan Hotel: A mid-range hotel that is popular with tourists. The hotel has a restaurant, bar, and outdoor pool.

What to Buy in Lankaran: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Lankaran is famous for its handicrafts, textiles, and local products. Here are some of the best things you can buy in Lankaran.

  • Lankaran Carpets: Lankaran is known for its unique carpets that are made using traditional weaving techniques. You can buy these carpets in the local markets.
  • Pomegranate Juice: Azerbaijan is famous for its pomegranates, and Lankaran is no exception. You can buy fresh pomegranate juice in the local markets.
  • Wooden Handicrafts: Lankaran has many talented woodworkers who make beautiful handicrafts such as carvings, decorations, and furniture.

Local Culture and Customs of Lankaran

Azerbaijan has a rich cultural heritage, and Lankaran is no exception. Here are some things you should know about the local culture and customs of Lankaran.

  • Hospitality: Azerbaijani people are known for their hospitality, and Lankaran is no exception. You’ll find that the locals are friendly, welcoming, and eager to show you their culture.
  • Clothing: Azerbaijan is a Muslim country, and modest clothing is recommended. However, tourists are free to wear their usual clothing, but it’s important to respect local customs.
  • Greetings: Azerbaijani people greet each other with a handshake and say “salam” (hello) or “sagol” (thank you).

Best Time to Visit Lankaran

Lankaran is a year-round destination, but the best time to visit is from March to May and from September to November. During these months, the weather is pleasant, and the crowds are relatively low.

Safety and Health

Lankaran is a safe destination for tourists, and the chances of encountering any serious problems are low. However, here are some tips on how to stay safe and healthy in Lankaran.

Safety in Lankaran

  • Be aware of your surroundings and avoid dark alleys and unlit areas.
  • Keep your valuables in a safe place and carry only what you need.
  • Be cautious when using public transportation.

Health in Lankaran

  • Drink bottled water to avoid getting sick.
  • Apply sunscreen and wear a hat to protect yourself from the sun.
  • Seek medical attention if you experience any symptoms of illness.

Getting to Lankaran: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lankaran

The nearest airport to Lankaran is the Lankaran International Airport, which is located approximately 8 km from the city center. The airport serves domestic and international flights.

Bus Stations Near Lankaran

Lankaran has two bus stations, the Old Bus Station and the New Bus Station. Both stations serve domestic and international destinations.

Other Options

You can also get to Lankaran by train, taxi, or private car. The train station is located approximately 5 km from the city center.

How to Get Around in Lankaran

The best way to get around Lankaran is by taxi, which is relatively cheap and easy to find. You can also rent a car or a bike to explore the town and its surroundings.

Where to Go Next from Lankaran: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Lankaran is an excellent starting point for exploring the Talysh region and the nearby regions. Here are some of the best destinations and excursions you can go on from Lankaran.

  • Astara: A picturesque town located on the border with Iran. Astara is famous for its natural beauty and cultural heritage.
  • Masalli: A town located in the southern part of Azerbaijan that offers breathtaking landscapes and exciting outdoor activities.
  • Baku: The capital city of Azerbaijan is just a few hours’ drive from Lankaran. Baku offers a great mix of modern and traditional attractions.

Conclusion

Lankaran is a hidden gem in Azerbaijan that offers stunning natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and exciting outdoor activities. We hope this comprehensive tour guide has given you all the information you need to plan your trip to Lankaran. Happy travels!
