Introduction to Beylaqan

Nestled in the southern region of Azerbaijan, Beylaqan offers a unique blend of history, culture, and outdoor adventure. Surrounded by stunning mountains and rolling hills, the city is known for its breathtaking landscape, lively bazaars, and friendly locals. Whether you’re a culture vulture or an outdoor enthusiast, there’s something for everyone in this charming city.

List of Places to Visit in Beylaqan

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The city is steeped in history, and its many museums and historical sites are a testament to Azerbaijan’s rich culture. Here are some must-see attractions:

  • Nizami House Museum: This museum is a great way to learn about the life and works of Azerbaijan’s most romantic poet, Nizami Ganjavi. It contains rare manuscripts and personal items that belonged to the poet.
  • Tovuz Historical and Ethnographic Museum: This museum showcases the customs and traditions of the Tovuz region. It has exhibits on traditional Azerbaijani clothes, carpets, and household items.
  • Qala Archaeological and Ethnographic Museum: This museum is set in a 17th-century castle and contains a wide range of archaeological artifacts, including pottery, coins, and jewelry.

Natural Attractions of Beylaqan: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Nature-lovers will be delighted by Beylaqan’s stunning natural scenery. Here are some of the city’s must-see outdoor attractions:

  • Bash Dashagil: This stunning waterfall is located within a scenic canyon and is a popular site for picnicking and hiking.
  • Qaragozal: This beautiful lake is surrounded by lush forests and is an ideal spot for fishing and boating.
  • Goygol Lake: Located just outside the city, this lake is known for its crystal clear waters and stunning mountain views.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Beylaqan

Beylaqan is home to some of Azerbaijan’s most picturesque hiking and biking trails. Here are some popular routes:

  • Turyanchay Trail: This trail winds through a scenic canyon and offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.
  • Leyli Peak: This challenging hike rewards visitors with stunning panoramic views of the entire region.
  • Bahmut-Tugay Trail: This trail takes hikers through dense woodland and past beautiful waterfalls.

Best Things to Do in Beylaqan: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are plenty of other activities to enjoy in Beylaqan. Whether you’re a culture vulture or an adrenaline junkie, there’s something for everyone. Here are some ideas:

  • Visit the Bazaars: Beylaqan is home to several lively bazaars where visitors can sample local cuisine, shop for souvenirs, and mingle with locals.
  • Take a Balloon Ride: Get a bird’s eye view of the stunning landscape by taking a hot air balloon ride over the city.
  • Visit a Vineyard: Azerbaijan is known for its wine, and a visit to one of Beylaqan’s vineyards is a great way to sample some of the region’s finest varieties.

Food and Drink Scene of Beylaqan

Azerbaijani cuisine is well-known for its bold flavors and hearty portions. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Plov: This classic Azerbaijani rice dish is typically made with lamb and is flavored with saffron, cumin, and other spices.
  • Dolma: These stuffed grape leaves are a staple of Azerbaijani cuisine and are typically filled with a mixture of rice, meat, and herbs.
  • Kebab: Azerbaijani kebabs are made with juicy marinated meat and are cooked over an open flame.

Local Beverages

  • Black tea: Azerbaijani tea is typically sweetened with sugar and is served in traditional glass cups.
  • Ayran: This refreshing yogurt drink is a popular choice on hot summer days.
  • Local wine: Azerbaijan is known for its wine production, and a visit to one of Beylaqan’s vineyards is a great way to sample some of the region’s finest varieties.

Where to Stay in Beylaqan: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Beylaqan has a range of accommodations to suit all budgets and tastes. Here are some options:

  • Beylaqan Hotel: This modern hotel offers comfortable rooms and a range of amenities, including a gym and spa.
  • Javahir Hotel: This budget-friendly hotel is centrally located and offers clean and comfortable rooms.
  • Local Guesthouses: For a more authentic experience, consider staying in a homestay or guesthouse run by a local family.

What to Buy in Beylaqan: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Beylaqan is known for its traditional crafts and homemade products. Here are some unique souvenirs to bring home:

  • Carpets: Azerbaijani carpets are renowned for their intricate designs and vibrant colors.
  • Pomegranate Wine: This sweet and flavorful wine is made from local pomegranates and makes for a great gift.
  • Traditional Clothes and Textiles: Beylaqan’s bazaars are a great place to find traditional Azerbaijani clothes, including silk scarves and embroidered jackets.

Local Culture and Customs of Beylaqan

Azerbaijani culture is deeply rooted in tradition and hospitality. Here are some customs to be aware of:

  • Shoes Off Inside: It is customary to remove your shoes before entering someone’s home.
  • Respectful Dress: Visitors should dress conservatively, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Hospitality: Azerbaijani hosts are known for their hospitality, and guests are often offered tea, sweets, or other treats.

Best Time to Visit Beylaqan

The best time to visit Beylaqan is between May and October when the weather is warm and sunny. However, visitors can enjoy Beylaqan’s natural beauty and cultural attractions all year round.

Do’s and Don’ts of Beylaqan

Do:

  • Respect Local Customs: Learn about local customs and practices, and be mindful of cultural differences.
  • Dress Conservatively: Dress modestly, especially when visiting mosques or other religious sites.
  • Try Local Cuisine: Azerbaijani cuisine is delicious and diverse, so be sure to sample some of the local dishes.

Don’t:

  • Photograph People Without Permission: Always ask permission before taking photos of people.
  • Drink Tap Water: Stick to bottled water to avoid illness.
  • Be Disrespectful: Avoid any actions or behaviors that may be perceived as disrespectful towards local customs or traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Beylaqan

Beylaqan is a safe and friendly city, but visitors should take normal precautions, such as staying in well-lit areas at night and avoiding carrying large amounts of cash.

Health in Beylaqan

Visitors should be up to date on routine vaccinations and should also consider getting vaccinated against Hepatitis A and B. It is also a good idea to carry any necessary medications with you.

Getting to Beylaqan: Transportation Options and Logistics

Getting to Beylaqan is relatively easy, with several transportation options available.

Airports Near Beylaqan

The nearest airport is the Ganja International Airport, which is located approximately 100 km from Beylaqan.

Bus Stations Near Beylaqan

There are several bus stations in Azerbaijan, including the Baku International Bus Terminal and the Ganja Bus Station, which offer regular services to Beylaqan.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a taxi or rent a car to travel to Beylaqan. However, it is important to ensure that the driver is licensed and that the car is in good condition.

How to Get Around in Beylaqan

Getting around Beylaqan is easy, with several transportation options available. Taxis are readily available, and visitors can also rent a car or hire a bicycle to explore the city.

Where to Go Next from Beylaqan: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are several nearby destinations and excursions that visitors can explore from Beylaqan, including the historic city of Ganja, the scenic Shahdag National Park, and the stunning Caspian Sea.

Conclusion

Beylaqan is a hidden gem in Southern Azerbaijan, offering visitors a unique blend of history, culture, and outdoor adventure. From its stunning natural scenery to its lively bazaars and friendly locals, the city has something to offer everyone. Whether you’re a culture vulture or an outdoor enthusiast, a trip to Beylaqan is sure to be an unforgettable experience.
