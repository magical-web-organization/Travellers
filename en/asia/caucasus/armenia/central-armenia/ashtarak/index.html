Introduction to Ashtarak

Ashtarak is a picturesque town located in the central part of Armenia, in the Caucasus region of Asia. The town is known for its scenic landscapes, important cultural sites, and outdoor adventures. Ashtarak is popular among tourists and locals alike due to its relaxing ambiance, hospitable people, and affordable prices. Let’s explore the best tourist attractions and other useful information that will guide you through your trip to Ashtarak.

List of Places to Visit in Ashtarak

Ashtarak offers travelers a range of options, from culturally important museums and historical places to natural attractions, outdoor adventures, and scenic views. Check out the list of top places to visit in Ashtarak.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Karmravor Church
  • Spitakavor Monastery
  • Ashtarak Old Bridge
  • Cathedral of Ashtarak
  • Artavazik Church
  • Surp Stepanos Church

Natural Attractions of Ashtarak: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Amberd Fortress
  • Sevanavank Monastery and Lake Sevan
  • Mount Aragats
  • Byurakan Astrophysical Observatory

Hiking/Biking Routes of Ashtarak

Ashtarak offers numerous hiking and biking trails, with some trails offering panoramic views of the town and the surrounding mountains. Some popular options include:

  • Hiking from Amberd Fortress to Lake Kari
  • Hiking to the Arkashen Waterfall
  • Biking along the Ashtarak-Geghard Highway

Best Things to Do in Ashtarak: Activities and Tours

  • Go on a cultural walking tour of Ashtarak and learn about its rich history
  • Take a wine tour and taste some of the best Armenian wines
  • Join a jeep tour and explore the scenic mountains and picturesque villages
  • Participate in an adventure sports tour and try activities such as paragliding or rock climbing

Week-Long Itinerary for Ashtarak

Day 1: Visit Karmravor Church, Spitakavor Monastery, and Ashtarak Old Bridge

Day 2: Visit Amberd Fortress and Lake Kari, go on a hiking trail

Day 3: Visit the Cathedral of Ashtarak, Artavazik Church, Surp Stepanos Church, and Byurakan Astrophysical Observatory

Day 4: Take a wine tour and visit local vineyards

Day 5: Visit Mount Aragats and go on a hiking trail

Day 6: Visit Sevanavank Monastery and Lake Sevan

Day 7: Participate in an adventure sports tour

Food and Drink Scene of Ashtarak

Armenian cuisine is well-known for its rich flavors and unique combinations of spices and herbs. Ashtarak offers many unforgettable culinary experiences. Some must-try dishes are:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Khorovats (Armenian barbecue)
  • Dolma (stuffed grape leaves)
  • Lavash (Armenian bread)
  • Harissa (traditional Armenian porridge)
  • Khashlama (meat stew)
  • Gata (a sweet pastry)

Local Beverages

  • Armenian wine
  • Ararat brandy
  • Tarkhun (Armenian soda)

Where to Stay in Ashtarak: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Ashtarak offers many accommodation options that suit all types of travelers’ budgets and preferences. There are many hotels, hostels, and guesthouses available in Ashtarak that offer comfortable accommodation at affordable prices.

What to Buy in Ashtarak: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Ashtarak offers various souvenirs and local products that make for unique gifts and souvenirs that will remind you of your trip. Some of the must-buy items include:

  • Armenian rugs
  • Armenian ceramics
  • Armenian wines and brandy
  • Handmade jewelry
  • Traditional Armenian costumes

Local Culture and Customs of Ashtarak

Armenians are known for their hospitality and warm nature. Some local customs that visitors should be aware of include:

  • Greeting people with a handshake is common
  • Table manners are appreciated
  • Respect for elders and family is important
  • Dress modestly when visiting churches or mosques

Best Time to Visit Ashtarak

Ashtarak has cool and dry winters and hot summers with clear blue skies. The best time to visit Ashtarak is from May to October when the weather is warm and pleasant. Ashtarak is beautiful all year round, and each season offers different experiences.

Do’s and Don’ts of Ashtarak

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions
  • Try local foods and drinks
  • Learn basic Armenian phrases
  • Make friends with locals
  • Bargain in bazaars and crafts markets

Don’t:

  • Be disrespectful to religious monuments
  • Show public displays of affection
  • Make negative comments about local customs and traditions
  • Overstay your visa

Safety and Health

Safety in Ashtarak

Ashtarak is generally a safe destination for travelers. However, it is advisable to take precautions such as:

  • Keep your valuables in a safe place
  • Avoid unlit and deserted areas at night
  • Follow the instructions of tour guides and travel professionals

Health in Ashtarak

Ashtarak has a good healthcare system with advanced medical facilities and well-trained professionals. Travelers are advised to have travel health insurance and carry a basic medical kit with them.

Getting to Ashtarak: Transportation Options and Logistics

Ashtarak is well-connected to major cities and towns of Armenia. Some transportation options available are:

Airports Near Ashtarak

  • Zvartnots International Airport
  • Shirak International Airport
  • Gumri International Airport

Bus Stations Near Ashtarak

  • Yerevan’s Central Bus Station
  • Gumri’s Bus Station

Other Options

  • Taxi services
  • Car rentals
  • Train services

How to Get Around in Ashtarak

Ashtarak can easily be explored on foot or by renting a bicycle. Taxis and buses are also available to travel longer distances.

Conclusion

Ashtarak is a hidden gem in Armenia waiting to be explored. This picturesque town offers a range of attractions, from cultural sites to natural landscapes and outdoor adventures.
