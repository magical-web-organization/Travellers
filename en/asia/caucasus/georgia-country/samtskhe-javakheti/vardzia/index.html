Introduction to Vardzia

If you’re an adventurer looking for a unique destination to explore, Vardzia is the place to be. It’s a rock-hewn city that was carved in the 12th century during the reign of Queen Tamar of Georgia. Vardzia is located in Samtskhe-Javakheti, in the southern part of Georgia, which is known for its magnificent natural scenery.

In this comprehensive tour guide, you’ll find information on the best things to do in Vardzia, including a list of places to visit, hiking routes, where to stay, what to eat and drink, and more.

Best Things to Do in Vardzia: Activities and Tours

  • Visit the Vardzia Monastery and explore its tunnels and secret passages.
  • Enjoy an outdoor adventure at Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park.
  • Relax and rejuvenate at one of the natural hot springs in Vardzia.
  • Take a tour of the Sapara Monastery and discover its fascinating history.
  • Visit the Rabati Castle and immerse yourself in Georgian culture.

Local Beverages

Local Culture and Customs of Vardzia

  • Georgian people are very friendly and hospitable
  • Respect Georgian Orthodox traditions
  • Dress modestly while visiting religious sites

Best Time to Visit Vardzia

The best time to visit Vardzia is from May to September, which is when the weather is pleasant for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Vardzia

Do:

  • Respect local traditions and customs
  • Try Georgian cuisine
  • Dress modestly while visiting religious sites

Don’t:

  • Be disrespectful toward Georgian culture
  • Take photos of religious sites without asking for permission
  • Enter religious sites without proper attire

Safety and Health

Safety in Vardzia

Vardzia is a safe place to visit. However, visitors are advised to take standard safety precautions like avoiding walking alone at night.

Health in Vardzia

It is recommended that visitors receive a tetanus shot before traveling to Vardzia.
