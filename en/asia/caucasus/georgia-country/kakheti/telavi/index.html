Telavi - The Ultimate Travel Guide

Telavi is a beautiful city located in the Kakheti region of Georgia, in the Caucasus Mountains of Asia. Telavi is rich in culture and history, and known for its stunning natural beauty. The city has many attractions for tourists, especially those looking for an adventure in the great outdoors. Below, we’ve put together a comprehensive travel guide to Telavi, to help you plan your perfect trip.

List of Places to Visit in Telavi

Telavi has a lot to offer for visitors. Whether you’re interested in exploring its history, culture or natural beauty, Telavi has it all. Here are some of the top places to visit during your stay in Telavi:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  1. Alaverdi Monastery - An ancient Georgian Orthodox Monastery located in the Alazani Valley, built in the 11th century.
  2. Tsinandali Palace - The birthplace of famous Georgian poet Alexander Chavchavadze, the palace is now a museum showcasing several exhibitions, including Georgian wines.
  3. King Erekle II Palace - The Palace is a restored royal residence of King Erekle II, which now houses a museum and showcases various Georgian cultural and art exhibitions.
  4. Telavi State Theater - Established in 1927, the theater is a great place for arts and culture lovers. It hosts various local and international plays and festivals throughout the year.

Natural Attractions of Telavi: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  1. Gremi Fortress - A well-preserved 16th-century castle located on a hill offering panoramic views of the surrounding Alazani Valley.
  2. Lopota Lake - A beautiful lake located near Telavi, offering fishing, boating, and swimming. Here, you can also enjoy horseback riding, hiking, and picnicking.
  3. Ikalto Monastery Complex - A historic monastery located in the Alazani Valley, offering stunning natural vistas and beautiful vineyards.
  4. Vashlovani National Park - Located just a short drive from Telavi, this beautiful national park is home to rare species of plants and animals, beautiful hiking trails and outstanding views of the Caucasus Mountains.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Telavi

Telavi is a perfect destination for hikers and bikers. The beautiful landscapes and captivating views make this region great for exploring on foot or by bike. Here are some of the best hiking and biking routes in Telavi:

  1. Kvareli Lake Trail - This beautiful 5 miles trail offers a challenging hike with gorgeous views of the valley and lake.
  2. Alazani Trail - It’s a popular 12-mile trail allowing visitors to experience the beautiful Alazani Valley.
  3. Gombori Pass Trail - A 15-mile trail located at nearly 2,000 meters above sea level, allowing hikers to explore the Caucasus Mountains.

Best Things to Do in Telavi: Activities and Tours

Apart from hiking and biking, Telavi offers various activities and tours for tourists. Here are some of the best things to do in Telavi:

  1. Wine Tasting - Telavi is considered as the wine capital of Georgia. You can visit various wineries in the city to experience the unique taste of Georgian wine
  2. Hot Air Balloon Rides - You can enjoy bird’s eye views of the beautiful Alazani Valley and picturesque Georgian landscapes with the very popular hot air balloon rides
  3. Sightseeing Tours - There are various sightseeing tours available in Telavi, such as city hop-on hop-off bus tours, walking tours, and private guided tours.
  4. Horse Riding - You can explore the beautiful natural surroundings of Telavi on horseback.

Week-Long Itinerary for Telavi

Here’s a week-long itinerary for Telavi:

Day Activities
Day 1 Arrive in Telavi, explore the city center, visit City Hall Square, and have a relaxing evening at one of the local bars.
Day 2 Visit Alaverdi Monastery, Gremi Fortress and have a wine-tasting tour at one of the local wineries
Day 3 Explore the beautiful landscapes of Lopota Lake, hike around the lake, fish or swim, and have a picnic lunch. In the evening, return to Telavi, head to the city’s historic center, and dine at one of the local restaurants.
Day 4 Visit Tsinandali Palace, the Alexander Chavchavadze Museum, and the King Erekle II Palace. Have dinner at one of the city’s famous restaurants.
Day 5 Go on a hot air balloon ride over the Alazani Valley. Afterward, go for a horseback riding tour.
Day 6 Explore the Ikalto Monastery Complex and spend the afternoon enjoying the wineries of Telavi.
Day 7 Take a day trip to Vashlovani National Park. In the evening, head back to Telavi for a farewell dinner at one of the city’s famous eateries.

Food and Drink Scene of Telavi

Telavi is famous for its wine and traditional Georgian cuisine. Georgia is known as the birthplace of wine, and Telavi is the wine capital of the country. Here’s what visitors must try during their stay:

Must-Try Dishes

  1. Khinkali - a delicious Georgian dumpling filled with juicy meat and spices.
  2. Khachapuri - a Georgian cheese bread that comes in various shapes and fillings.
  3. Lobio - A hearty bean stew made with onions, garlic, and herbs.
  4. Churchkhela - sweet and chewy candy made from nuts and grape juice, often compared to a fruit rollup.

Local Beverages

  1. Wine - Georgian wine is world-famous and the main component of most Georgian cuisine. Telavi is home to various wineries, and wine tasting is a must-do activity.
  2. Chacha - A Georgian brandy made from grapes, traditionally enjoyed as a digestive after a meal.

Where to Stay in Telavi: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Telavi offers various accommodation options to suit different budgets and preferences. Here are some popular choices:

  1. Chateau Mere - A combination of a guesthouse and winery, with beautifully furnished rooms, a great restaurant, and friendly service.
  2. Lopota Lake Resort and Spa - A luxury hotel located near Lopota Lake, offering a spa, water activities, horseback riding, and golf facilities.
  3. Old Telavi Hotel - A cozy and affordable hotel located in the city center, great for budget travelers.

What to Buy in Telavi: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Telavi offers various unique souvenirs and local products to bring back home as a memento of your trip. Here are some popular choices:

  1. Wine - Georgian wine is the perfect souvenir from Telavi. Buy wine from local wineries or attend a wine tasting session.
  2. Local Crafts - Georgian craftsmen are famous for their handmade products, such as decorative knives, pottery and handicrafts made from wood and ceramics.
  3. Churchkhela - The sweet candy prepared from nuts and grape juice is a unique souvenir to bring back home.

Local Culture and Customs of Telavi

Telavi is a conservative city, and visitors should respect the local customs and culture. Here are some key points to keep in mind:

  1. Dress conservatively, especially when visiting religious sites like churches and monasteries.
  2. Remove your shoes before entering someone’s home or a place of worship.
  3. Learn a few phrases of Georgian. It’s always appreciated by the locals.

Best Time to Visit Telavi

The best time to visit Telavi is during the summer months from June to August. The weather is warm and ideal for outdoor activities. Spring and autumn offer milder temperatures making it a pleasant time to hike or explore the city, while winter may be a bit colder; however, the views of the snowy Caucasus Mountains covering Telavi during wintertime are breathtakingly beautiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of Telavi

Here are a few do’s and don’ts to keep in mind when visiting Telavi:

Do:

  1. Take part in a wine-tasting tour.
  2. Respect the local customs and dress appropriately.
  3. Bring your hiking boots and explore the trails.

Don’t:

  1. Overdrink or get totally drunk in a public place.
  2. Disrespect any local customs or culture.
  3. Enter a church or a monastery wearing short or revealing clothes.

Safety and Health

Telavi is generally safe, but it’s advised to take some precautions while visiting the city.

Safety in Telavi

  1. Don’t leave your valuable items or phone in the car, especially while parked on the street.
  2. Don’t go to deserted alleys at night.
  3. Stick to well-lit areas in the dark.

Health in Telavi

  1. Book a medical insurance before you begin your trip
  2. Observe food hygiene precautions regarding any street foods.
  3. Drink bottled water and avoid tap water unless filtered.

Getting to Telavi: Transportation Options and Logistics

Telavi is a small city but accessible from other main cities in Georgia via road or air.

Airports Near Telavi

The nearest international airports to Telavi are in Tbilisi, the Tbilisi International Airport, and the Kutaisi International Airport, both of which are approximately 90 miles away.

Bus Stations Near Telavi

Tbilisi Central Bus Station is located in the capital city of Tbilisi and has frequent buses connecting Telavi to other cities in Georgia.

Other Options

There is no railway station in Telavi; however, the Georgian Railway provides railway transport services to different cities in Georgia.

How to Get Around in Telavi

Telavi is a small city and easy to navigate. Walking is the most popular mode of transportation, but visitors can also take taxis or rent cars or bikes to get around the city and explore its surrounding areas.

Where to Go Next from Telavi: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

There are plenty of destinations near Telavi that you can explore during your trip:

  1. Sighnaghi - Known as the “City of Love,” Sighnaghi is a beautiful village located in the Kakheti region, popular for its romantic atmosphere and stunning views.
  2. Kvareli Lake - Located just 10 kilometers away from Telavi, Kvareli Lake is a picturesque destination ideal for hiking, swimming, and relaxing.
  3. Tbilisi - Georgia’s capital city has plenty of cultural landmarks, museums, and vibrant nightlife, located just 100 kilometres away from Telavi.

Conclusion

Telavi is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to the Caucasus region. With a mix of history, culture, natural beauty, outdoor activities, food, and wine, Telavi has something to offer everyone. Whether you’re looking for adventure or relaxation, Telavi is the perfect destination for you!
