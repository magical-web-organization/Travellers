Introduction to Khulo

Situated in southwestern Georgia in the Caucasus region, Khulo is a charming small town that offers perfect opportunities for travelers to experience Georgian culture and stunning natural beauty. It’s close to the border with Turkey and surrounded by the lush green mountains of the Adjara region. Khulo is a great place to explore if you’re interested in hiking, outdoor activities, and experiencing a slower pace of life.

List of Places to Visit in Khulo

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums, and Historical Places

  • Khulo Museum of Local Lore - Located in the center of the town, this museum features exhibits showcasing the history and culture of Khulo and Adjara region as a whole. Visitors can view artifacts dating back to the Bronze Age and learn about the unique local customs and traditions.
  • Makhuntseti Waterfall - One of the most popular attractions in Adjara, Makhuntseti Waterfall is an impressive waterfall located just a few kilometers south of Khulo. It’s a perfect place for picnics and swimming.
  • Skhalta Monastery - Nestled in the mountains, this ancient monastery dates back to the 10th century and is considered one of the most important historical sites in Khulo.

Natural Attractions of Khulo: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Tago - Mount Tago, located close to the border with Turkey, is home to some of the most scenic views in the Caucasus region. Hiking to the summit is a popular adventure.
  • Kintrishi Protected Areas - This is the largest protected area of Adjara, encompassing over 30,000 hectares of natural beauty. Visitors can take a guided tour and explore the unique flora and fauna of the region.
  • Green Lake - This emerald green lake, located deep in the mountains, is a hidden gem that can be reached via a moderate hiking trail.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Khulo

Khulo is a hiker’s paradise, and there are several trails of varying levels of difficulty to explore:

  • Khulo-Khertvisi Trail - This 25-kilometer trail takes you from Khulo to the village of Khertvisi, passing through breathtaking natural scenery along the way. It’s a moderately challenging hike, with steep inclines and rocky terrain.
  • Kichkheti to Khulo Trail - This trail of around 15 km is an easy downhill hike that takes you through forests, wildflower meadows, and small rural settlements.

Best Things to Do in Khulo: Activities and Tours

  • Khulo Cable Car Ride - This attraction takes visitors on a thrilling ride above the mountains, offering stunning views of the town and the surrounding forests.
  • Horse-riding Tour - Horse-back riding is one of the best ways to explore the natural beauty of Khulo. Visitors can book a tour that takes them through scenic trails, with views of waterfalls and natural springs.
  • Paragliding - For thrill-seekers, paragliding is an adventurous activity that offers spectacular views of the mountains from above.

Food and Drink Scene of Khulo

Must-Try Dishes

  • Khachapuri - A traditional Georgian dish made with cheese-filled bread, Khachapuri is a must-try when in Khulo.
  • Chakhrakeli - This sweet dish is made with grape juice and walnuts, and it’s a popular dessert in Khulo.
  • Mchadi - Mchadi is a cornmeal flatbread that’s usually served with cheese and butter.

Local Beverages

  • ChaCha - This traditional Georgian brandy is made from grapes and is a popular after-dinner drink.
  • Ajaran Tea - Ajaran tea is a specialty tea that’s made with local herbs and honey.

Where to Stay in Khulo: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Guesthouse Small Mestia - This cozy guesthouse offers comfortable rooms and stunning views of the mountains.
  • Art Villa Khulo - Art Villa Khulo is a beautifully designed villa located in the heart of Khulo.
  • Sufara Guest House - Sufara Guest House offers comfortable rooms, with views of the surrounding mountains.

What to Buy in Khulo: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Honey - Khulo is known for its high-quality honey. Look for jars of honey from local honey-makers for a tasty souvenir.
  • Handicrafts - Traditional Georgian handicrafts, such as woven blankets and hand-carved wooden items, can be found in shops around Khulo.

Local Culture and Customs of Khulo

Khulo is a highly traditional town, and cultural customs are retained by the locals. Visitors should be respectful of local customs and dress modestly when visiting religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Khulo

The best time to visit Khulo is from late spring to early autumn (May - October) when the weather is mild, and the mountain trails are open for hiking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Khulo

Do:

  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Try local dishes and drinks.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs of people or religious sites without asking for permission.
  • Show disrespect towards local customs and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Khulo

Khulo is a safe town, with a low crime rate. Visitors should take usual precautions when travelling, such as keeping valuables close and staying aware of their surroundings.

Health in Khulo

Visitors should make sure their vaccines are up-to-date before traveling to Khulo. It’s also important to note that the town is located in a high-altitude area, and visitors should be aware of the risk of altitude sickness.

Getting to Khulo: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Khulo

The nearest airport to Khulo is Batumi International Airport, which is located approximately 100 km away.

Bus Stations Near Khulo

Bus services to Khulo are available from Batumi and other nearby cities. The bus journey takes around four hours.

Other Options

Taxis and rental cars are also available for transportation to and around Khulo.

How to Get Around in Khulo

The best way to explore Khulo is on foot. However, taxis and rental cars are also available.

Where to Go Next from Khulo: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Batumi - The bustling seaside city of Batumi is located approximately 100 km from Khulo and is a popular destination for travelers to Adjara.
  • Borjomi-Kharagauli National Park - This stunning national park is located approximately 120 km from Khulo and is ideal for outdoor activities such as hiking and camping.

Conclusion

If you’re looking for a destination that offers a mix of natural beauty, adventure activities, and cultural experiences, then Khulo is an excellent choice. With plenty to see and do, it’s the ideal location for travelers who want to get off the beaten path and explore the beauty of Georgia.
