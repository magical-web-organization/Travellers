Introduction to Dvabzu

Located in Southwestern Georgia, Dvabzu is a hidden gem off the beaten path, waiting to be explored. The town boasts a unique blend of history, culture, and natural beauty, making it a perfect destination for travelers seeking an authentic Georgian experience. A visit to Dvabzu promises stunning landscapes, a fascinating history, and delicious cuisine, all with a touch of Georgian hospitality.

List of Places to Visit in Dvabzu

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Dvabzu is steeped in history, and there are several museums and historical sites that visitors should not miss. The town’s crown jewel, the Sakrebulo building, is one of the most remarkable buildings in the historic region of Samtskhe-Javakheti. This impressive structure dates back to the early 1900s and once served as a government building. Today it houses a museum, showcasing the history and culture of the region.

Another must-visit historical site is the Akhaltsikhe Fortress, located just a short drive from Dvabzu. This impressive fortress played a vital role in Georgia’s history and consists of several different structures and areas worth exploring, including a mosque, a church, a palace, and a prison.

Natural Attractions of Dvabzu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The natural beauty of Dvabzu is undeniable, and there are several outdoor activities to enjoy that showcase the town’s natural wonders. The Paravani Lake Nature Reserve is a vast area with stunning views of Paravani Lake, volcanic mountains, pristine forests, and unique flora and fauna. The surrounding area provides an array of recreational activities including hiking, bird watching, and fishing

On a clear day, a hike up Mount Kazbek in the Caucasus Range promises unrestricted panoramic views of the surroundings. The mountain takes around eight hours to reach the summit, but the hike is worth it.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dvabzu

Dvabzu and its surrounding areas are perfect for those looking to hike or bike. There are several breathtaking routes to explore for hikers of all levels, from short hikes to extended overnight treks. One of the popular trails is the Beshumi to Dvabzu trail, which is approximately 40km long and takes two to three days to complete. The trail offers stunning views of the region’s natural beauty, including untouched forests, waterfalls, and rugged geological formations.

Best Things to Do in Dvabzu: Activities and Tours

In addition to hiking and biking, there are several other activities and tours worth exploring in Dvabzu. Indulge in the local spa culture with a visit to Tbilisi Sulphur Baths, a traditional bathhouse renowned for its healing hot springs. For something more adventurous, try hot air ballooning over the area’s stunning landscapes for a once-in-a-lifetime experience.

Week-Long Itinerary for Dvabzu

A week spent in Dvabzu allows ample time to explore the area’s historical and natural attractions. Day 1: Arrive in Dvabzu and check into local accommodation. Days 2-3: Visit historical landmarks such as the Sakrebulo building and Akhaltsikhe fortress. Day 4: Indulge in a local spa experience or hot air balloon ride. Day 5-7: Hike or bike through the stunning natural beauty of Paravani Lake Nature Reserve and Mount Kazbek.

Food and Drink Scene of Dvabzu

Must-Try Dishes

Georgian cuisine is world-famous for its rich flavors, spices, and unique combinations of ingredients. Adjarian Khachapuri, a cheesy pastry dish, is a must-try when visiting Dvabzu. Other local delights include Khinkali, a dumpling filled with spices and meat, and grilled Shashlik. Finish off your meal with a glass of Georgian wine, renowned for its quality and taste.

Local Beverages

Georgian brandy is another well-known alcoholic beverage. It is produced using traditional methods and quality ingredients, making it a popular choice among locals and visitors alike.

Where to Stay in Dvabzu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Dvabzu offers a range of accommodations to suit all budgets, from basic hostels to luxurious hotels. One popular accommodation option is a stay in a traditional Georgian family home, where visitors can experience Georgian hospitality and cuisine firsthand.

What to Buy in Dvabzu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

For souvenirs to bring back home, consider purchasing traditional Georgian handicrafts such as pottery, textiles, and handmade jewelry. Georgian wine, brandy, and honey also make for excellent gifts.

Local Culture and Customs of Dvabzu

Georgian culture is unique, with a rich history and customs that are still prevalent today. Visitors to Dvabzu should be aware of local customs, such as removing shoes before entering a home, and always showing respect for elders.

Best Time to Visit Dvabzu

The best time to visit Dvabzu is during the spring and summer months, from April to September, when the weather is mild and perfect for outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Dvabzu

Do:

  • Try local foods and drinks.
  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Carry cash as some restaurants and shops do not accept credit cards.

Don’t:

  • Wear shoes inside houses.
  • Be loud or disruptive, as it is considered impolite.
  • Deal with drugs, as this is illegal and can lead to serious consequences.

Safety and Health

Safety in Dvabzu

Dvabzu is a safe town for tourists, but visitors should still be cautious of pickpockets and scams in touristy areas.

Health in Dvabzu

To stay healthy during your visit to Dvabzu, it is essential to practice good hygiene and stay hydrated. Visitors should also be up to date with their routine vaccinations before traveling to Georgia.

Getting to Dvabzu: Transportation Options and Logistics

The nearest airport to Dvabzu is the Tbilisi International Airport, which is around a four-hour drive away. Visitors can also take a bus or hire a taxi to reach the town.

Airports Near Dvabzu

  • Tbilisi International Airport (218km away)

Bus Stations Near Dvabzu

  • Akhaltsikhe Bus Station (17km away)
  • Akhalkalaki Bus Station (53km away)

Other Options

  • Hire a taxi from the airport.

How to Get Around in Dvabzu

The best way to get around Dvabzu is by taxi or renting a car. Visitors can also choose to take guided tours or hire local guides for hiking and biking trips.

Where to Go Next from Dvabzu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Dvabzu is surrounded by several other popular Georgian destinations worth visiting, including Tbilisi, Kutaisi, and Batumi.

Conclusion

Dvabzu is a unique Georgian town that offers a wealth of natural beauty, culture, and history to its visitors. From its historical landmarks to its delicious food and stunning natural wonders, there is something for everyone in this hidden gem of Southwestern Georgia.
