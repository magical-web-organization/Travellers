Introduction to Bakhmaro

Nestled in the mountains of southwestern Georgia, Bakhmaro is a small town that offers travelers a unique and unforgettable experience. With a rich cultural history, stunning natural beauty, and a welcoming community, Bakhmaro is a hidden gem that should be on any traveler’s bucket list.

List of Places to Visit in Bakhmaro

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Bakhmaro is home to several museums and historical sites that offer visitors insights into the region’s rich cultural heritage.

The Bakhmaro Museum of Local Lore is a must-visit attraction, showcasing the town’s history and traditional way of life. Another important cultural attraction is the Bakhmaro Ethnographic Museum, which features exhibits on local crafts and customs.

For those interested in religious history, the Bakhmaro Monastery is a must-see attraction. Built in the 10th century, the monastery is home to several beautiful frescoes and offers visitors a peaceful and serene atmosphere.

Natural Attractions of Bakhmaro: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Bakhmaro is known for its stunning natural beauty and offers visitors a wide range of outdoor activities to enjoy.

One of the most popular activities in Bakhmaro is hiking. With several trails to choose from, hikers can explore the region’s beautiful forests, mountains, and waterfalls. Other popular outdoor activities include biking, horseback riding, and fishing.

For those who prefer a more relaxing pace, the village offers breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and forests that can be enjoyed from several scenic viewpoints.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bakhmaro

Bakhmaro offers several hiking and biking routes that cater to different fitness levels and interests. Some of the most popular routes include:

  • The Tskhratskaro Trail: This is a challenging hike that takes around 6 hours to complete. The route offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and forest, and hikers will come across several natural springs along the way.

  • Sazano Nature Trail: This is an easy to moderate hike that takes around 3 hours to complete. The trail offers views of the village and surrounding forests, as well as an impressive waterfall.

  • The Bakhmaro Bike Trail: This easy route takes around 2 hours to complete and is suitable for beginners. The trail offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys and passes through several traditional villages.

Best Things to Do in Bakhmaro: Activities and Tours

Bakhmaro offers a wide range of activities and tours that cater to different interests and preferences. Some of the best things to do in Bakhmaro include:

  • Take a guided tour of the Bakhmaro Museum of Local Lore and learn about the town’s rich history.

  • Go fishing in one of the many streams that run through the forests surrounding Bakhmaro.

  • Take a horseback riding tour to explore the region’s stunning natural beauty.

  • Taste local wine and cheese produced in the surrounding villages.

  • Go on a guided mountain bike tour to explore the region’s stunning mountain trails.

Food and Drink Scene of Bakhmaro

Must-Try Dishes

Bakhmaro is known for its traditional cuisine, with many dishes featuring locally sourced ingredients. Some must-try dishes include:

  • Lobio: A bean stew cooked with traditional herbs and spices.

  • Khachapuri: A Georgian cheese-filled bread that can be found all over the country.

  • Mtsvadi: Grilled meat skewers seasoned with traditional spices.

Local Beverages

Bakhmaro is known for its delicious wine and traditional beer, both of which are made using locally grown grapes and hops. Be sure to try them during your visit!

Where to Stay in Bakhmaro: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bakhmaro offers several accommodation options to suit different needs and budgets. Some of the most popular options include:

  • Hotel Time: A modern hotel with comfortable rooms and stunning views of the surrounding mountains.

  • Hostel Bakhmaro: A budget-friendly option that offers clean and comfortable rooms and a shared kitchen.

  • Guesthouse Tekla: A traditional guesthouse that offers comfortable rooms and a friendly atmosphere.

What to Buy in Bakhmaro: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Bakhmaro is known for its traditional crafts and products, many of which can be found at local markets and stores. Some popular souvenirs and local products include:

  • Handmade traditional clothing, such as woolen hats and socks.

  • Locally produced wine and cheese.

  • Handmade pottery.

Local Culture and Customs of Bakhmaro

Bakhmaro has a rich cultural history, and visitors are encouraged to respect local customs and traditions. Some important customs to keep in mind include:

  • Dress modestly when visiting religious and cultural sites.

  • Remove your shoes when entering homes and religious buildings.

  • Show respect for elders and those in positions of authority.

Best Time to Visit Bakhmaro

The best time to visit Bakhmaro is during the summer months, when the weather is warm and dry. The fall months are also a great time to visit, as the leaves change color and the surrounding forests and mountains are particularly beautiful.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bakhmaro

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.

  • Experience the local food and drink scene.

  • Explore the stunning natural beauty of the surrounding area.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local customs or traditions.

  • Be loud or disruptive in public areas.

  • Leave litter or damage the surrounding natural beauty.

Safety and Health

Safety in Bakhmaro

Bakhmaro is a safe destination for travelers, and violent crime is rare. However, visitors are advised to take common sense precautions, such as avoiding unlit areas at night and securing valuables in their accommodations.

Health in Bakhmaro

Travelers should be up to date on their routine vaccinations and may wish to consider getting vaccinated against hepatitis A and B, particularly if they plan to try locally produced food and drink.

Getting to Bakhmaro: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bakhmaro can be reached via several transportation options, including:

Airports Near Bakhmaro

  • Kutaisi International Airport: The closest international airport, located approximately 2 hours away.

  • Batumi International Airport: Located approximately 3 hours away.

Bus Stations Near Bakhmaro

  • Kutaisi Bus Station: Located approximately 2 hours away.

  • Batumi Bus Station: Located approximately 3 hours away.

Other Options

Some visitors may choose to rent a car or hire a private driver to reach Bakhmaro.

How to Get Around in Bakhmaro

The best way to get around Bakhmaro is on foot, as the town is small and easily walkable. However, those looking to explore the surrounding area may wish to rent a bike, car, or hire a private driver.

Where to Go Next from Bakhmaro: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bakhmaro is located within easy reach of several other destinations in Georgia. Some popular options include:

  • Batumi: A coastal city known for its beautiful beaches and vibrant nightlife.

  • Kutaisi: A historic city that serves as a gateway to some of Georgia’s most popular attractions, including the Prometheus Cave and the Gelati Monastery.

  • Tbilisi: The capital of Georgia, known for its vibrant culture, museums, and ancient architecture.

Conclusion

Bakhmaro is a hidden gem in southwestern Georgia, offering travelers a unique and unforgettable experience. From its rich cultural history to its stunning natural beauty, there is truly something for everyone in Bakhmaro. Be sure to add it to your travel bucket list!
