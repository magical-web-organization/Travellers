Introduction to Bukavu

Welcome to Bukavu, located in the Kivu province of the Democratic Republic of Congo. This bustling city is located in Central Africa, surrounded by the breathtaking beauty of Lake Kivu and the Mitumba Mountains. Bukavu is a city full of culture and history, welcoming tourists from all over the world. Whether you are looking for adventure or relaxation, Bukavu has something to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Bukavu

Looking for what to do in Bukavu? Here is a list of our top attractions:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Bukavu has a rich history and culture that you can discover through its museums and landmarks. Make sure to visit:

  • The Oral History Museum of the Bakavu People
  • Mulume Monastery
  • Kalambo Falls
  • Bukavu Museum of Education
  • Musée Régional de Kivu

Natural Attractions of Bukavu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Bukavu is a city surrounded by natural landscapes and has an abundance of outdoor adventures for every traveler. Here are some of the natural attractions you should not miss out on:

  • Mount Kahuzi
  • The Congo-Nile Trail
  • Ihusi Falls
  • The Itombwe Nature Reserve
  • The Odzala-Kokoua National Park

Hiking/Biking Routes of Bukavu

For adventurous travelers, Bukavu also offers many hiking and biking trails for a physical challenge and a closer view of these natural attractions. Check out:

  • Mount Kahuzi trek
  • The Virunga National Park bike trail
  • The Congo-Nile trail hiking route

Best Things to Do in Bukavu: Activities and Tours

Bukavu is full of fun things to enjoy, from traditional festivals, to exhilarating adventures. Here are some of the best things to do in Bukavu:

  • Enjoy world-class coffee in the local cafes
  • Take a boat tour of Lake Kivu
  • Visit the Virunga National Park and go gorilla trekking
  • Explore the forests and countryside on a nature walk
  • Visit the Kundelungu National Park

Week-Long Itinerary for Bukavu

If you’re planning an extended stay in Bukavu, here’s a week-long itinerary you can follow:

Day 1: Visit the Oral History Museum of the Bakavu People and explore the surrounding areas. Day 2: Do a gorilla trekking trip in the Virunga National Park. Day 3: Discover the great Itombwe Natural Reserve by taking a nature walk. Day 4: Go on a boat trip on Lake Kivu. Day 5: Do the Congo-Nile trail or the Mount Kahuzi trek, depending on your physical ability. Day 6: Visit the Kundelungu National Park. Day 7: Get a taste of the local cuisine before leaving for home.

Food and Drink Scene of Bukavu

Bukavu is famous for its cuisine and strong, vibrant coffee. Here are some local dishes and drinks that you should try:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kachumbali salad
  • Fufu with Ndibibi soup
  • Saka-Saka
  • Yambanengele soup

Local Beverages

  • Amani Coffee
  • Dibondo Tree Juice
  • Kivu Beer
  • Caprice des Anges Wine

Where to Stay in Bukavu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Bukavu offers a range of accommodation options suited for every budget, from guesthouses to luxury hotels. Here are some of the best places to stay in Bukavu:

  • Hotel Begonias
  • Panorama Hotel
  • Hotel Horizon
  • Hotel la joie

What to Buy in Bukavu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

For souvenir hunters, Bukavu has unique local products to purchase as a reminder of your visit:

  • Local coffee beans from Amani Coffee
  • Artisanal crafts such as jewelry, baskets, and sculptures
  • Handmade fabrics such as Kitenge and Pagne cloth
  • Paintings and sculptures from local artists at the Kivu Art Gallery

Local Culture and Customs of Bukavu

The people of Bukavu are welcoming and hospitable, and it’s best to observe their customs and traditions while visiting. Here are some important traditions and customs:

  • Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites
  • Respect the local culture and traditions
  • Greet people you meet with courtesy

Best Time to Visit Bukavu

Bukavu has a subtropical highland climate, with cool to moderate temperatures and occasional rainfall. The best time to visit Bukavu is between June and October, which is the dry season. It’s also the best time for outdoor activities like gorilla trekking and nature walks.

Do’s and Don’ts of Bukavu

To ensure a safe and enjoyable trip, here are some things you should and shouldn’t do while in Bukavu:

Do:

  • Dress modestly
  • Respect the local culture
  • Learn a few French or Swahili phrases

Don’t:

  • Don’t accept a ride from strangers
  • Don’t walk alone at night
  • Don’t carry large sums of cash or valuable items

Safety and Health

Bukavu is generally a safe city, but it’s still best to be extra cautious. Here are some important safety and health precautions to consider:

Safety in Bukavu

  • Avoid walking alone at night, especially in unfamiliar areas
  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially in crowded places
  • Use trusted taxi services with recommended drivers
  • Carry a copy of your passport, and keep the original in a safe place

Health in Bukavu

  • Take precautions against mosquito bites
  • Get vaccinated for yellow fever and other common tropical diseases
  • Use bottled water for drinking and brushing teeth
  • Seek medical attention if you notice any symptoms of illness

Getting to Bukavu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Bukavu is accessible by road, air, and water. Here are the options for getting to Bukavu:

Airports Near Bukavu

  • Kavumu Airport
  • Goma Airport
  • Kinshasa-N’Djili Airport

Bus Stations Near Bukavu

  • Nyabibwe Bus Station
  • Hawa Bus Station

Other Options

  • Taking a taxi or private car
  • Arranging transport through your accommodation provider

How to Get Around in Bukavu

Bukavu can easily be explored by taxi or by bike, which can be arranged at your accommodation or on the streets. Walking is also a good option and is a great way to explore the city in detail.

Where to Go Next from Bukavu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Bukavu is located in a region full of beautiful natural attractions and exciting cities to explore. Here are some nearby destinations to consider:

  • Rwanda
  • Burundi
  • The Goma City in the DRC

Conclusion

Bukavu is a beautiful destination full of cultural landmarks, natural wonders, and fun activities. With this comprehensive tour guide, you now have everything you need to plan your unforgettable trip to Bukavu. Don’t forget to book your accommodation, arrange transport, and follow all essential safety and health precautions before you go. Have a wonderful trip!
