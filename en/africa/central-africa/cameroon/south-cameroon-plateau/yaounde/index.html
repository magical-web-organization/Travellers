Introduction to Yaoundé

Yaoundé is the capital city of Cameroon, located in the South Cameroon Plateau. It is a bustling, vibrant city known for its rich heritage, diverse cultures, and scenic landscapes. The city was founded in the late 19th century by the German colonialists and later became the administrative capital after independence in 1960.

List of Places to Visit in Yaoundé

Yaoundé offers a plethora of attractions for tourists to explore. Here are some of the must-visit places in the city:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • National Museum of Cameroon: This museum is considered to be one of the best museums in Cameroon. It houses a wide range of historical and cultural artifacts that showcase the rich history of the country.

  • Palais des Sports: This indoor stadium is a landmark of Yaoundé, built-in 1981. It is considered an architectural masterpiece and also hosts concerts and exhibitions.

  • Basilique Marie-Reine-des-Apôtres: This Catholic basilica is an architectural marvel that attracts visitors from all over the world. It was built-in 1955 and is famous for its stained glass windows and mosaics.

Natural Attractions of Yaoundé: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Cameroon: This active volcano is one of the highest peaks in Africa, towering at a height of 4035 meters above sea level. It is a popular spot for hiking, trekking, and camping.

  • Lobéké National Park: This park is located in the southeast of Cameroon and is famous for its wildlife. It is home to rare species such as gorillas and elephants.

  • Mvog-Betsi Zoo: This zoo is situated in Yaoundé and offers a wide range of wildlife species for visitors to see. It is an excellent place for families to visit and learn about wildlife.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Yaoundé

Yaoundé has an extensive network of hiking and biking routes. Here are some of the popular routes for tourists:

  • Pic du Jerde: This is a popular hiking trail that leads to the highest peak in Yaoundé. The trail offers panoramic views of the city and is perfect for adventure seekers.

  • Lac de Ouest: This trail leads to the west of Yaoundé and offers scenic views of the lake. It is a popular spot for cycling and bird watching.

Best Things to Do in Yaoundé: Activities and Tours

Yaoundé offers a wide range of activities and tours for tourists. Here are some of the best things to do in the city:

  • City Tour: A city tour is an excellent way to explore the rich heritage of Yaoundé. It includes visits to historical landmarks, museums, and cultural centers.

  • Food Tour: Cameroon is famous for its diverse cuisine, and a food tour is an excellent way to experience the unique flavors of the country.

  • Wildlife Safari: Yaoundé is home to a wide range of wildlife species, and a safari is an excellent way to explore the natural beauty of the region.

Food and Drink Scene of Yaoundé

Cameroon is famous for its diverse and unique cuisine. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages in Yaoundé:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Ndole: This is a traditional dish made with nuts, vegetables, and fish or meat.

  • Poulet DG: This dish is made with chicken, plantains, and spices.

  • Suya: This is a popular street food made with grilled meat, spices, and onions.

Local Beverages

  • Palm wine: This is a traditional alcoholic beverage made from the sap of palm trees.

  • Ginger juice: This is a refreshing drink made with ginger, lemon, and sugar.

  • Bissap: This is a hibiscus tea that is sweetened with sugar or honey.

What to Buy in Yaoundé: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Yaoundé has an extensive market scene where tourists can purchase unique souvenirs and local products. Here are some of the best places to shop:

  • Mfoundi Market: This is the largest market in Yaoundé, and it offers a wide range of goods, from clothing to handicrafts.

  • Artisanal Center: This center is famous for its unique collection of handicrafts, including sculptures and carvings.

Local Culture and Customs of Yaoundé

Yaoundé is a culturally diverse city, and it is essential to respect the local customs and traditions. Here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Cameroonian culture values hospitality and respect.

  • When visiting a Cameroonian home, it is customary to bring a small gift or token of appreciation.

Best Time to Visit Yaoundé

The best time to visit Yaoundé is between December and April, which is the dry season. The weather is pleasant, and tourists can explore the city comfortably.

Do’s and Don’ts of Yaoundé

Safety and Health

Safety in Yaoundé

Yaoundé is a relatively safe city, but tourists should take precautions to avoid any inconvenience. Here are some safety tips:

  • Avoid traveling alone at night.

  • Keep your valuables safe and out of sight.

Health in Yaoundé

Tourists should take precautions regarding their health during their stay in Yaoundé. Here are some tips:

  • Drink only bottled or purified water.

  • Apply mosquito repellent and mosquito nets to avoid mosquito-borne diseases like Malaria.
