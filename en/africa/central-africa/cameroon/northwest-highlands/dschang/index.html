Introduction to Dschang

Located in the Northwest Highlands of Cameroon, Dschang is a stunning city known for its rich culture, beautiful mountainous scenery and hospitable people. If you’re looking for an off-the-beaten-path destination to explore, Dschang is certainly worth a visit. This guide highlights some of the best experiences and activities this city has to offer.

List of Places to Visit in Dschang

To help you make the most of your time in Dschang, we’ve compiled a list of the best places to visit. Here are some of the top attractions worth adding to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Dschang Museum - Discover the history and culture of Cameroon at this fascinating museum.

  • Bali Traditional Palace - The impressive Bali Palace is a must-visit for anyone interested in the history and customs of the Bali people.

  • The Bamoun Kingdom Palace - Explore this grand palace that marks the historical heritage of the Bamoun people.

Natural Attractions of Dschang: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Mount Oku - If you’re a nature lover, head to Mount Oku to experience the beautiful flora and fauna of Cameroon.

  • The Bafoussam waterfalls - Situated within the Bana Forest Reserve, these waterfalls offer spectacular views and photo opportunities.

  • The Massif des Doupkoue and its lakes - A beautiful series of mountains and lakes in the region that offer amazing views and scenic walks.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Dschang

Dschang’s natural surroundings make it an excellent destination for hiking or biking enthusiasts. Here are a few routes to consider:

  • Mount Oku Trek - This 3-day trek takes you to the summit of Mount Oku and through some stunning landscapes.

  • The Bafoussam Loop - A popular circuit of around 80 km that leads through picturesque villages, hills and forests.

  • The Kimbi Fungom National Park Hike - This remote national park offers a challenging hike that rewards you with stunning views of the surrounding landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Dschang: Activities and Tours

Whether you prefer adventure or relaxation, Dschang has something to offer everyone. Here are some popular activities and tours to consider:

  • Cultural Tours - Explore Dschang’s rich cultural heritage through guided tours of museums, palaces, and other historical landmarks.

  • Hiking and Biking Tours - Check out one of Dschang’s many natural attractions with a guided tour through Cameroon’s stunning landscapes.

  • Culinary Experiences - Learn about the traditional cuisine of Cameroon with a cooking class or local food tour.

Week-Long Itinerary for Dschang

Here’s a suggested itinerary for a week-long stay in Dschang:

Day Activity
Day 1 Visit Dschang Museum and the Bali Palace.
Day 2 Hike Mount Oku.
Day 3 Take a cultural tour to Bamoun Kingdom Palace.
Day 4 Explore the Bafoussam Waterfalls.
Day 5 Relax and enjoy the beautiful scenery of the Massif des Doupkoue.
Day 6 Go on a biking tour of the Bafoussam Loop.
Day 7 Try traditional Cameroon dishes or take a cooking class.

Food and Drink Scene of Dschang

Cameroonian cuisine is heavily influenced by the country’s West African roots. Here’s a list of must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Ndole Stew - A popular dish made from peanuts, spices, and bitter leaves.

  • Poulet DG - A hearty dish made with chicken, plantains, and spices.

  • Koki - A steamed dish made of black-eyed beans and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Palm Wine - A traditional African alcoholic beverage made from the sap of palm trees.

  • Hibiscus Juice - A popular refreshing drink made from the hibiscus flower and served either hot or cold.

  • Bissap - A deliciously sweet and tangy juice made from the Hibiscus flower, served ice-cold.

Where to Stay in Dschang: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Dschang has a variety of accommodation options to choose from, ranging from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Hotel Royal Palace - A great option for a luxury stay in Dschang.

  • Fountain d’Espoir - A budget-friendly guesthouse located in the heart of Dschang.

  • Les Jardins de Leontine - A charming boutique hotel located in a beautiful garden setting.

What to Buy in Dschang: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

If you’re looking for souvenirs to bring home, you’re in luck. Dschang has many local products and handicrafts worth picking up. Here are a few ideas:

  • Cameroonian Decorative Masks - These beautifully crafted masks are perfect for adding a touch of African style to any room.

  • Kente Cloth - A brightly-colored, patterned fabric that is traditionally woven and worn as a garment.

  • Cameroonian Coffee - Known for its rich flavor, Cameroon’s coffee is a popular local product.

Local Culture and Customs of Dschang

Like many cities in Cameroon, Dschang has a rich and diverse cultural heritage. It’s important to respect local customs while you’re here. Here are a few things to keep in mind:

  • Language - The official languages of Cameroon are French and English; however, many locals also speak one of the indigenous African languages.

  • Religion - Christianity and Islam are the two main religions practiced in Cameroon.

  • Dress Code - Cameroonians tend to dress conservatively, so it’s best to avoid wearing revealing or provocative clothing.

Best Time to Visit Dschang

The best time to visit Dschang is during the dry season between November and February when temperatures are moderate, and there’s little rainfall. However, Dschang is a year-round destination, so you can visit any time of the year.

Do’s and Don’ts of Dschang

Here are some things to keep in mind to ensure you have a safe and pleasant trip:

Do:

  • Respect Local Customs - Be respectful of local customs and traditions, dress modestly, and ask for permission before taking pictures.

  • Carry Cash - Cash is the most widely accepted form of payment in Cameroon, so be sure to carry enough money with you.

  • Stay Hydrated - Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated, especially if you are hiking or traveling during the day.

Don’t:

  • Be Out Alone at Night - Make sure to stay in well-lit areas and avoid walking alone at night, especially in unfamiliar areas.

  • Take Photos of Military or Police Buildings - It’s illegal to take pictures of military or police installations, so be respectful and avoid doing so.

  • Give Money to Begging Children - This can encourage children to continue begging and can put them in danger.

Safety and Health

While Dschang is a relatively safe destination, it’s always important to take precautions to ensure your safety and health.

Safety in Dschang

  • Avoid Demonstrations - Political tensions in Cameroon can often lead to demonstrations, so be sure to avoid large crowds or protests.

  • Use Licensed Taxis - When using taxis, make sure they are licensed and that the driver is reputable.

  • Be Aware of Pickpockets - Like most tourist destinations, Dschang has its share of pickpockets. Keep your belongings secure and stay aware of your surroundings.

Health in Dschang

  • Vaccinations - Make sure you are up-to-date on routine vaccinations and consider getting vaccinated for typhoid, hepatitis A and B, and yellow fever.

  • Malaria Prevention - Malaria is prevalent in Cameroon, so be sure to take appropriate measures, such as using mosquito nets and repellent.

  • Safe Food and Water - Avoid drinking tap water and be cautious when eating street food to avoid foodborne illnesses.

Getting to Dschang: Transportation Options and Logistics

There are several transportation options for getting to Dschang, depending on your location and travel preferences.

Airports Near Dschang

The closest international airports to Dschang are Douala International Airport and Yaounde Nsimalen International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Dschang

The main bus station in Dschang is the Dschang Bus Station, which is located in the city center.

Other Options

Private car rental and taxi services are also available in Dschang.

How to Get Around in Dschang

While taxis are readily available in Dschang, walking or cycling is often the best way to explore the city and its surroundings.

Where to Go Next from Dschang: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Once you’ve explored Dschang, there are several other destinations in Cameroon worth visiting. Here are a few suggestions:

  • Lobeke National Park - A stunning national park known for its rich biodiversity and rare animal species.

  • Bamenda - A vibrant city known for its bustling markets, lively nightlife, and stunning mountain views.

  • Yaounde - Cameroon’s capital city is home to several museums, cultural events, and government buildings worth exploring.

Conclusion

Dschang is a city of stunning natural beauty, cultural heritage, and friendly locals that make it an ideal destination for travel. With its awe-inspiring mountain ranges, cascading waterfalls, exotic cuisine, and abundant attractions, a trip to Dschang is sure to be a memorable experience.
