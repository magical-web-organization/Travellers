Introduction to Lagunes

Lagunes is a beautiful region located in Côte d’Ivoire, a country situated in West Africa. Home to the vibrant city of Abidjan, the area is famous for its cultural significance, natural beauty, and outdoor adventures. In this Lagunes travel guide, we explore the must-see attractions and hidden gems of the region, along with tips on where to stay, what to eat, and how to stay safe.

List of Places to Visit in Lagunes

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Lagunes is home to several museums and historical places that offer unique insights into the region’s culture and history. Some of the must-visit attractions include:

  • The National Museum of Abidjan - This museum houses a significant collection of West African art, including wood sculptures, textiles, masks, and more.

  • The Palais de la Culture - This cultural center is known for its concerts, shows, and art exhibitions.

  • The Basilica of Our Lady of Peace - This is the biggest church in Africa, and its architecture is a unique mix of modern and traditional styles.

Natural Attractions of Lagunes: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

If you love the great outdoors, Lagunes has numerous natural attractions to explore. Some of the popular places to visit include:

  • The Banco National Park - Covering an area of over 3,000 hectares, this park is known for its diverse flora and fauna, and it’s a great place for bird watching and hiking.

  • Le Jardin Botanique de Bingerville - This botanic garden is home to several plant species, including rare ones from around the world.

  • The Ebrié Lagoon - This lagoon is a stunning natural feature and provides a perfect venue for water sports like surfing, sailing, and canoeing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Lagunes

Lagunes offers several hiking and biking routes, which are suitable for trekking enthusiasts of all levels. Some of the popular ones are:

  • The Plateau to Cocody Loop - This route is perfect for bikers looking for a challenge. It covers a distance of about 20km and takes you through some of the most picturesque parts of the region.

  • The Banco National Park Trail - This trail runs through the Banco National Park and is ideal for hikers who love exploring nature.

Best Things to Do in Lagunes: Activities and Tours

There are many things to see and do in Lagunes that cater to every type of traveler. Some of the must-do tours and activities include:

  • Urban Adventure tour of Abidjan - This tour takes you through the bustling streets of Abidjan, visiting the famous landmarks and cultural centers of the city.

  • Traditional markets tour - This tour takes you through some of the local markets where you can sample the delicious food and interact with the friendly locals.

Food and Drink Scene of Lagunes

Lagunes has an exciting and diverse food scene that is sure to satisfy any food lover. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Foutou - A popular Ivorian dish made from yam or cassava, which is mashed together with a sauce of your choice.

  • Garba - A delicious street food snack made from ground peanuts, onions, and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Bissap - A refreshing drink made from hibiscus flowers, which is perfect for hot days.

  • Palm Wine - A traditional alcoholic beverage made from the sap of palm trees.

Where to Stay in Lagunes: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Lagunes offers a wide range of accommodation options from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. Here are some options you can consider:

  • Radisson Blu Hotel Abidjan Airport - A luxurious hotel located near the airport, offering excellent amenities and excellent services.

  • Ibis Abidjan Plateau - A budget-friendly option in the heart of Abidjan, offering modern amenities and a comfortable stay.

What to Buy in Lagunes: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Lagunes is known for its vibrant culture and artisanal products. Some of the local products you can take home as souvenirs include:

  • Ivorian Fabric - The region is known for its brightly colored fabrics, which make a great souvenir or gift for loved ones.

  • Wood Sculptures - Handcrafted wood sculptures are abundant in Lagunes, and you can find them at the local markets and street vendors.

Local Culture and Customs of Lagunes

Lagunes has a rich cultural heritage, with a diverse mix of West African and European influences. Some of the customs and traditions to note include:

  • Greetings - It is customary to greet someone before speaking to them. A simple “Bonjour” or “Comment ça va?” will suffice.

  • Attire - Respectful attire is expected in public spaces, especially when visiting mosques, churches, and other religious sites.

Best Time to Visit Lagunes

The best time to visit Lagunes is between November and February, when the weather is relatively cool and dry. However, you can visit the region any time throughout the year, as the area is blessed with a tropical climate.

Do’s and Don’ts of Lagunes

Here are some do’s and don’ts to remember when visiting Lagunes:

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.

  • Take necessary precautions if you’re traveling alone.

Don’t:

  • Don’t carry large amounts of cash with you.

  • Don’t wear expensive jewelry or accessories in public places.

Safety and Health

Safety in Lagunes

Lagunes is generally safe for visitors, but it’s always advisable to take precautions, such as:

  • Be vigilant and aware of your surroundings.

  • Avoid walking alone at night, especially in secluded areas.

Health in Lagunes

Travelers to Lagunes are advised to take necessary vaccinations and precautions before arriving. It is essential to take necessary medications with you, such as anti-malaria drugs.

Getting to Lagunes: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Lagunes

The primary airport serving Lagunes and the surrounding region is the Félix-Houphouët-Boigny International Airport, which is located near Abidjan.

Bus Stations Near Lagunes

Several bus stations connect the nearby regions to Abidjan, the main commercial hub of Côte d’Ivoire.

Other Options

You can also arrive in Lagunes by train or private car. However, it’s advisable to hire a licensed driver or arrange your transportation with a reputable tour company.

How to Get Around in Lagunes

Lagunes has several transportation options, including:

  • Taxis

  • Buses

  • Car rentals

Taxis are the best option for getting around Abidjan, while buses are ideal for traveling between cities.

Where to Go Next from Lagunes: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Lagunes is surrounded by several other cities and regions worth exploring, such as:

  • Yamoussoukro - The official capital of Côte d’Ivoire, known for its stunning basilica and palaces.

  • Korhogo - A city famous for its indigenous crafts and vibrant cultural scene.

Conclusion

Lagunes is a region full of surprises, offering visitors a diverse mix of culture, adventure, and natural beauty. Whether you’re an adventure seeker, a history buff, a food lover, or a culture vulture, Lagunes has something for every type of traveler. With this Lagunes travel guide, you’re all set to explore this unique and exciting destination.
