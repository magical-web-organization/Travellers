Introduction to LUFASI Nature Park

LUFASI (Lekki Urban Forest and Animal Sanctuary Initiative) Nature Park is a serene and picturesque park located in Lagos, Nigeria. This conservation center covers over 20 hectares of undulating savannah alongside the Lekki Lagoon.

The park opened to the public in 2016 and is committed to promoting environmental awareness, conservation, and education. The conservation center also serves as an animal sanctuary for endangered species, including tortoises, monkeys, and birds.

List of Places to Visit in LUFASI Nature Park

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

1. LUFASI Museum and Art Gallery

This museum showcases the cultural and historical heritage of Lagos and Nigeria through various artifacts, art, and photography. The gallery section of the museum features contemporary art exhibitions from local and international artists, making it a must-visit for lovers of art and culture.

2. Oniru Private Beach

Situated along the coast of Lagos, the Oniru Private Beach offers an idyllic space for snorkeling, sunbathing, or a cool dip in the water. The beach is perfect for families, groups of friends, or individuals seeking a relaxed atmosphere.

Natural Attractions of LUFASI Nature Park: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

1. Canopy Walkway

For those seeking an adrenaline rush, LUFASI offers a canopy walkway that swings among the treetops over 22m high. The canopy walkway also offers a stunning view of the park and the adjacent Lekki Lagoon.

2. Bird Watching Spot

With over 100 species of birds residing in the park, bird enthusiasts can explore the aviary life while hiking through various trails within the park. LUFASI offers bird watching tours with trained guides to help visitors spot different bird species.

Hiking/Biking Routes of LUFASI Nature Park

LUFASI Nature Park offers several routes for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Visitors can explore the park’s scenic beauty while hiking and biking through designated trails, including the following:

  1. The Savanah Trail
  2. The Forest Trail
  3. The Wetland Trail

Best Things to Do in LUFASI Nature Park: Activities and Tours

The park offers several activities and tours for visitors to engage in, including:

  1. Wildlife photography and tours
  2. Night camping and bonfire
  3. Conservation and environmental awareness classes
  4. Canoe rides

Day 1: Check-in and settle into selected accommodation. Day 2: Explore LUFASI Museum and Art Gallery. Day 3: Hike the Savannah Trail and enjoy some wildlife photography. Day 4: Take a canoe ride around Lekki Lagoon. Day 5: Enjoy the canopy walkway and bird watching tour. Day 6: Relax on Oniru Private Beach. Do’s and Don’ts of LUFASI Nature Park

Do:

  1. Follow the designated trails.
  2. Respect the park’s rules and regulations.
  3. Take a guided tour for a richer experience.
  4. Bring along sunscreen, insect repellent, and a hat.

Don’t:

  1. Litter or destroy the park’s natural habitats.
  2. Feed the animals.
  3. Venture off into restricted areas.
  4. Bring along pets to the park.

Getting to LUFASI Nature Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near LUFASI Nature Park

The closest airport to LUFASI Nature Park is the Murtala Muhammed International Airport, with a distance of about 45 km from the park.

Bus Stations Near LUFASI Nature Park

Major bus stations close to LUFASI Nature Park include CMS, Ojodu Berger, and Oshodi.

Other Options

Visitors can also get a taxi or ride-hailing service such as Uber or Bolt to get to LUFASI Nature Park.

How to Get Around in LUFASI Nature Park
