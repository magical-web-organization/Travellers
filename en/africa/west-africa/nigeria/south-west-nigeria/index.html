Introduction to South West Nigeria

South West Nigeria is located in the Western region of Africa and is home to some of Nigeria’s most popular cities such as Lagos, Ibadan, and Abeokuta. It is a region rich in history, culture, and natural beauty.

List of Places to Visit in South West Nigeria

If you’re planning a trip to South West Nigeria, these are the must-visit places:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • National Museum Lagos
  • Freedom Park Lagos
  • Olumo Rock Abeokuta
  • Mapo Hall Ibadan
  • Trans-Amusement Park Ibadan

Natural Attractions of South West Nigeria: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Idanre Hills
  • IITA Forest Reserve
  • Erin-Ijesha Waterfalls
  • Nike Art Gallery Lagos
  • Tarkwa Bay Beach Lagos

Hiking/Biking Routes of South West Nigeria

Best Things to Do in South West Nigeria: Activities and Tours

If you’re looking for things to do in South West Nigeria, here are some of the best activities and tours:

  • Lagos City Tour

  • Ibadan Cultural Tour

Week-Long Itinerary for South West Nigeria

Day 1: Visit the National Museum Lagos and Freedom Park Lagos. Day 2: Explore Obudu Cattle Ranch and go hiking or biking. Day 3: Visit Olumo Rock Abeokuta and Mapo Hall Ibadan. Day 4: Go on the Ibadan Cultural Tour. Day 5: Travel to Osogbo and visit the Osun-Osogbo Sacred Grove. Day 6: Climb Ado-Awaye Mountain and explore Arinta Waterfalls. Day 7: Relax on Tarkwa Bay Beach Lagos and visit Nike Art Gallery Lagos before departure.

Local Culture and Customs of South West Nigeria

South West Nigeria has a rich and diverse culture. Here are some things to be aware of:

  • Respect the elders.
  • Greet people when entering a room.
  • Remove your shoes before entering a home.
  • It’s customary to use your right hand to give or receive anything.

Best Time to Visit South West Nigeria

The best time to visit South West Nigeria is between November and March when the weather is cool and dry. Avoid visiting during the rainy season (April-October) when the weather can be unpredictable.

Do’s and Don’ts of South West Nigeria

Do:

  • Dress conservatively.
  • Ask for permission before taking photos.
  • Learn a few words in Yoruba.
  • Try the local food.

Don’t:

  • Touch someone’s head without permission.
  • Eat with your left hand.
  • Refuse hospitality.

Safety and Health

Safety in South West Nigeria

South West Nigeria is generally safe for travelers, but it’s always important to exercise caution. Here are some safety tips:

  • Avoid traveling alone at night.
  • Don’t carry large sums of money with you.
  • Keep your valuables safe.
  • Be aware of your surroundings at all times.

Health in South West Nigeria

It’s recommended that you get vaccinated before traveling to South West Nigeria. Here are some additional health tips:

  • Drink bottled water.
  • Use insect repellent.
  • Wash your hands regularly.
  • Seek medical attention if you feel unwell.
