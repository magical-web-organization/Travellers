Introduction to Owerri

Owerri is the capital city of Imo State in the southeastern region of Nigeria. It is home to several tourist attractions, including rich cultural and historical sites, amazing natural scenery, and vibrant local culture.

Whether you’re a solo traveler, group, or family visitor, Owerri has something for everyone looking for a unique vacation experience. This comprehensive travel guide will help you plan your trip to Owerri, with information on the best places to visit, things to do, local food and drinks, where to stay, safety tips, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in Owerri

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Some of the culturally significant attractions to visit in Owerri include:

1. Ahiajioku Center

The Ahiajoku Center is a cultural center dedicated to showcasing the rich cultural heritage of Igbo people, including dance performances, art, music, and literature.

2. Mbari Cultural and Art Center

The Mbari Cultural and Art Center was built in the 1960s to display bronze sculptures, carvings, and other forms of art from the Igbo tribe.

3. National Museum of Unity

The National Museum of Unity is a significant cultural center in Owerri that displays the diverse cultural heritage of Nigeria, from its pre-colonial history to the present day.

Natural Attractions of Owerri: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

There are many beautiful and scenic places to visit in Owerri, including:

1. Oguta Lake

Oguta Lake is one of the largest natural lakes in Nigeria and provides a serene environment for water sports activities such as boating, skiing, and fishing.

2. Nekede Zoo

The Nekede Zoo is home to different animal species, and visitors can observe the numerous species in natural habitats.

3. Ezeogba waterfall

The Ezeogba waterfall is a stunning natural waterfall located just outside the city of Owerri, providing beautiful scenery and a refreshing atmosphere.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Owerri

For outdoor enthusiasts, Owerri boasts various biking and hiking trails, including:

  • The Ngwa road trail is a 10-kilometer trail suitable for biking and hiking routes.
  • The Njaba River trail provides a stunning view of the Njaba River and surrounding scenery.

Best Things to Do in Owerri: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the exciting activities and tours visitors can engage in while in Owerri:

  • Enjoy the local nightlife by visiting a popular club such as Blue Ribbon or Villa Garden.
  • Participate in a traditional Igbo village tour, to learn about customs and culture, such as carving and weaving.
  • Attend a cultural festival, such as the New Yam Festival, which is celebrated in August of every year.

Local Culture and Customs of Owerri

The Igbo tribe is the dominant ethnic group in Owerri with rich cultural customs such as dancing, carving, and weaving. Visitors can experience the rich Igbo culture by attending the different festivals and cultural events held throughout the year.

Best Time to Visit Owerri

The best time to visit Owerri is during the dry season, from November to February.

Do’s and Don’ts of Owerri

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Learn common phrases in the local language.
  • Keep an open mind and be willing to explore the culture.

Don’t:

  • Take pictures of people and places without permission.
  • Disrespect local customs and traditions.
  • Wear revealing clothing in public.

Safety and Health

Safety in Owerri

While Owerri is relatively safe, visitors must take some precautions, such as:

  • Avoid walking alone at night.
  • Take official taxis or reputable transportation services.
  • Avoid carrying large sums of cash.

Health in Owerri

Visitors to Owerri must protect themselves against mosquito bites, which causes malaria. It is advisable to take prophylactic medication, wear long-sleeved clothing, and use insect repellent.

Getting to Owerri: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Owerri

The closest airport to Owerri is the Sam Mbakwe International Airport, which is a 30-minute drive to the city center.

Bus Stations Near Owerri

Visitors can also take a bus to Owerri from different parts of the country. Some of the popular bus stations include Wazobia and Peace Mass.

Other Options

Visitors can also choose to take a taxi, Uber, or car rental services to explore the city.

How to Get Around in Owerri
