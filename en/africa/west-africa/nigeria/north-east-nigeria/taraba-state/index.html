Introduction to Taraba State

Welcome to Taraba State! Located in the North East region of Nigeria, Taraba State is a land of natural beauty and cultural diversity. With breathtaking landscapes, vibrant culture, and welcoming people, Taraba State is a perfect destination for those who want to explore the unknown and beauty of unspoiled Nigeria.

Taraba State is home to a range of culturally significant attractions, natural wonders, hiking/biking routes, and various activities, offering something for everyone.

Best Time to Visit Taraba State

The best time to visit Taraba State is between November and February when the climate is cooler and drier.

Do’s and Don’ts of Taraba State

Here are some important considerations for tourists traveling to Taraba State:

Do:

  • Respect the cultural and religious practices of the locals.
  • Dress modestly when in public
  • Try the local cuisine

Don’t:

  • Drink tap water; stick to bottled water
  • Smoke in public areas
  • Take photos of people without their permission

Safety and Health

Safety in Taraba State

Taraba State is generally a safe place for tourists to visit. However, as with any other city or remote area, visitors should keep their guard up and stay aware of their surroundings.

Health in Taraba State

Visitors traveling to Taraba State should make sure they are up-to-date with their vaccinations and boosters. It is highly recommended to seek vaccinations as per an individual’s situations and not necessary for all. However, malaria infection is quite common in Nigeria; visitors should take malaria prophylaxis medication and carry insect repellent sprays and mosquito nets. Also, make sure to drink only bottled or purified water.
