Introduction to Obubra

Obubra is a town located in the Cross River State of Southern Nigeria. This town is surrounded by hills and mountains, making it one of the most scenic towns in Nigeria. Obubra is a popular destination for tourists who want to experience the rich culture of Nigeria, as it is home to several museums and historical places. In this travel guide, we will highlight the must-visit places in Obubra, things to do, best places to stay, food and drink scene, and much more.

List of Places to Visit in Obubra

There are several places to visit in Obubra. Below is a list of the most popular tourist attractions.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Obubra Museum: This museum is located in the town center and showcases the history and culture of the people of Obubra. Visitors can see artifacts and learn about the traditions and customs of the local people.
  • Alok Ikom Stone Monoliths: These monoliths are located in Alok Ikom and are believed to have been erected over 2000 years ago. They are a popular attraction for tourists because of their artistic and cultural significance.
  • Ediba Town: This is a traditional town located in Obubra. Visitors can see beautiful Ediba masks and learn about the history and lifestyle of the Ediba people.

Natural Attractions of Obubra: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Oban Hills: These hills offer beautiful views of the town and the surrounding areas. Visitors can go on a guided hike through the hills for stunning views and to explore the rich landscape.
  • Kwa Waterfalls: Located near the town, Kwa Waterfalls is a perfect place for nature lovers. This is a great place for swimming, taking a picnic, and enjoying the beautiful scenery.
  • Cross River National Park: This park is home to over 300 species of birds, animals, and plants. Visitors can go on a guided tour of the park and see wildlife such as gorillas, chimpanzees, baboons, and more.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Obubra

Obubra is a great place for outdoor enthusiasts. There are several hiking and biking routes that provide stunning views of the town and the surrounding hills.

  • Oban Hills Hiking Trail: This trail takes visitors through the Oban Hills and provides beautiful views of the landscape.
  • Obubra-Buruku Biking Trail: This trail takes visitors through some of the most scenic areas around Obubra. It offers an incredible biking experience that is perfect for those who love outdoor activities.

Best Things to Do in Obubra: Activities and Tours

  • Explore the Museums and Historical Places: Obubra has several museums and historical places that are worth exploring. Visitors can learn about the rich history and culture of the people of Obubra.
  • Visit the Kwa Waterfalls: This is a perfect spot for swimming and picnicking. Visitors can hike around the falls and appreciate the beautiful scenery.
  • Go on a Safari Tour: Visitors can take a guided tour of the Cross River National Park and see wildlife such as gorillas, chimpanzees, and baboons.

Food and Drink Scene of Obubra

The food and drink scene in Obubra is a perfect blend of local and international cuisine. Visitors can try several local dishes and beverages that are unique to the region.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Afia Efere: This is a soup made with pumpkin leaves and has a unique taste.
  • Ekpang Nkukwo: This is a traditional dish made with grated cocoyam and is usually served with fish or meat.
  • Ofe Nsala: This is a spicy soup made with catfish.

Local Beverages

  • Palm Wine: This is a traditional alcoholic drink made from the sap of palm trees. Visitors can try fresh palm wine at local bars.
  • Zobo: This is a delicious drink made from hibiscus petals and is served cold.

What to Buy in Obubra: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Obubra is known for its unique crafts and artifacts. Visitors can buy several souvenirs and local products that are perfect for bringing home. Some of the best things to buy in Obubra are:

  • Traditional Masks: These masks are made by local artisans and are very unique.
  • Handmade Baskets: These baskets are made from natural materials and are perfect for home decoration.
  • Jewelry: Visitors can find several shops that sell handmade jewelry made from beads and other materials.

Local Culture and Customs of Obubra

Obubra has a rich cultural heritage that is reflected in its art, food, and customs. The people of Obubra are friendly and welcoming, and visitors can expect a warm reception. It is important to show respect for local customs and traditions when visiting Obubra.

Best Time to Visit Obubra

The best time to visit Obubra is during the dry season, which runs from November to February. During this time, the weather is perfect for outdoor activities, and visitors can enjoy the scenery without worrying about rain.

Do’s and Don’ts of Obubra

Do:

  • Show respect for local customs and traditions.
  • Try local cuisine and immerse yourself in the culture.
  • Visit the museums and historical places in Obubra.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect local traditions and customs.
  • Venture out alone at night, especially as a solo traveler.
  • Take photographs of individuals without their permission.

Safety and Health

Safety in Obubra

Obubra is generally a safe place for tourists. However, visitors should take precautions like not leaving their belongings unattended and avoiding dark and isolated areas at night.

Health in Obubra

Visitors should take precautions against malaria by taking the necessary medication and using mosquito repellents. Visitors should also ensure that they have sufficient medical insurance coverage.

Getting to Obubra: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Obubra

The nearest airport to Obubra is Margaret Ekpo International Airport in Calabar. Visitors can take a taxi or bus from the airport to Obubra.

Bus Stations Near Obubra

Visitors can take a bus from Calabar to Obubra. There are several bus stations in Calabar, including Ohafia Park, which offers regular bus services to Obubra.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a private car to drive them to Obubra from Calabar.

How to Get Around in Obubra

The best way to get around Obubra is by taxi or hired car. Visitors can also rent a bike or motorbike to explore the town.

Where to Go Next from Obubra: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Calabar: This is a coastal town located near Obubra. Calabar is known for its rich history, museums, and the Calabar Carnival, which is held annually.
  • Obudu Mountain Resort: This resort is located in Nigeria’s highland region and offers beautiful scenery, hiking trails, and outdoor activities.

Conclusion

Obubra is a hidden gem in Nigeria, and visitors will be amazed by the beautiful scenery, rich culture, and friendly people.
