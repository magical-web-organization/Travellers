Introduction to Shai Hills Resource Reserve

Shai Hills Resource Reserve is a must-see destination located in the coastal plain of Ghana, West Africa. The reserve covers an area of about 52 square kilometers, consisting of rolling hills, lush forests, and savannah grasslands. This reserve is home to a wide range of wildlife, including baboons, antelopes, and several species of birds, making it the perfect destination for adventure and nature enthusiasts.

List of Places to Visit in Shai Hills Resource Reserve

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

If you’re interested in learning about the rich history and culture of the Ghanaian people, make sure to visit the following museums and historical places:

  • Shai Hills Resource Reserve Visitor Center: This is the main entrance to the reserve and provides visitors with useful information about the area’s flora and fauna, geology, and cultural heritage.
  • Shai Hills Wildlife Sanctuary: The sanctuary is home to a wide variety of wildlife species, including baboons, hyenas, antelopes, and several species of birds.
  • Shai Hills Military Cemetery: This is a war cemetery that honors the British and West African soldiers who died during World War II.

Natural Attractions of Shai Hills Resource Reserve: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

The reserve offers several options for outdoor activities and scenic views, including:

  • Hiking and Biking Trails: The reserve has several well-marked trails that lead through the rolling hills, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding landscapes.
  • Picnic Areas: The reserve has several designated picnic areas where you can enjoy a relaxing afternoon in the shade of the trees.
  • Shai Hills Quarry: This is a former limestone quarry that has been transformed into a beautiful lake where you can enjoy canoeing or fishing.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Shai Hills Resource Reserve

If you’re an outdoor enthusiast, one of the best ways to explore the reserve is through hiking or biking. The following are some of the best hiking and biking routes in the reserve:

  • Shai Hills Loop: This is a 10-kilometer loop trail that offers scenic views of the surrounding hills and wildlife.
  • Shai Hills Summit Trail: This is a steep but rewarding trail that leads to the highest point in the reserve, offering panoramic views of the surrounding landscapes.

Best Things to Do in Shai Hills Resource Reserve: Activities and Tours

There are plenty of things to do in Shai Hills Resource Reserve, including:

  • Game Drives: This is a great way to see the reserve’s wildlife up close and learn more about their habitats and behaviors.
  • Cultural Tours: Learn about the traditional way of life of the people who live near the reserve and their cultural practices.
  • Bird Watching: Shai Hills is home to over 175 bird species, making it an ideal destination for bird watching enthusiasts.

Week-Long Itinerary for Shai Hills Resource Reserve

If you’re planning to stay for a few days in Shai Hills Resource Reserve, the following itinerary will help you make the most of your visit:

Day 1: Arrival and Familiarization

  • Arrive at Accra International Airport and transfer to your accommodation in Shai Hills.
  • Visit the Shai Hills Resource Reserve Visitor Center to obtain information about the reserve and its attractions.
  • Go on an evening hike to explore the reserve’s landscape.

Day 2: Game Drive and Cultural Tour

  • Begin the day with a game drive in the reserve to see the wildlife.
  • Take a cultural tour of the nearby villages to learn about the traditional way of life of the people who live near the reserve.
  • Return to your accommodation for dinner.

Day 3: Bird Watching and Hiking

  • Begin the day with a bird watching excursion in the reserve.
  • After lunch, take a guided hiking tour of the hills to explore the natural beauty of the reserve.
  • Return to your accommodation for dinner.

Day 4: Canoeing and Fishing at Shai Hills Quarry

  • Spend the morning canoeing or fishing on the tranquil waters of the Shai Hills Quarry.
  • Enjoy a picnic lunch on the shores of the lake.
  • Return to your accommodation for dinner.

Day 5: Relaxation Day

  • Spend the day relaxing at your accommodation.
  • Enjoy a spa treatment or a swim in the pool.
  • Take a leisurely walk around the surrounding village.

Day 6: Summit Trail Hiking and Sunset Watch

  • Challenge yourself with a hiking excursion to the summit of Shai Hills.
  • Watch the sunset over the reserve from the summit.
  • Return to your accommodation for dinner.

Day 7: Departure

  • After breakfast, check out of your accommodation and transfer to Accra International Airport for departure.

Food and Drink Scene of Shai Hills Resource Reserve

Must-Try Dishes

When visiting Shai Hills Resource Reserve, you should try the following local dishes:

  • Jollof Rice: This is a popular Ghanaian dish that consists of rice cooked with a spicy tomato and onion sauce. It’s often served with chicken or beef.
  • Banku: This is a staple food in Ghana made from corn and cassava dough. It’s usually served with soup or stew.
  • Fufu: This is another staple Ghanaian food made from yam, plantain, or cassava. It’s often served with soup or stew.

Local Beverages

You should also try the following local beverages:

  • Palm Wine: This is a traditional alcoholic beverage made from the sap of palm trees.
  • Sobolo: This is a popular non-alcoholic beverage made from hibiscus flowers and sweetened with sugar.
  • Pito: This is a traditional alcoholic beverage made from fermented millet or sorghum.

  • Achimota Forest Hotel: This hotel is located in a peaceful forest reserve and offers comfortable rooms, a restaurant, and outdoor pool.
  • Nkunim Guesthouse: This is a budget-friendly option that offers basic but comfortable rooms and a restaurant.

What to Buy in Shai Hills Resource Reserve: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Local Culture and Customs of Shai Hills Resource Reserve

When visiting Shai Hills Resource Reserve, it’s important to respect the local culture and customs. Some things to keep in mind include:

  • Dress modestly, especially when visiting the local villages.
  • Do not take photos of people without their permission.
  • Always ask before touching any cultural artifacts or items.

Best Time to Visit Shai Hills Resource Reserve

The best time to visit Shai Hills Resource Reserve is during the dry season, which runs from November to February. During this time, the weather is cooler and drier, making it easier to explore the reserve’s attractions.

Do’s and Don’ts of Shai Hills Resource Reserve

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Keep the environment clean by disposing of your waste properly.
  • Stay on designated trails when hiking or biking.

Don’t:

  • Pick any plants or flowers.
  • Smoke or light fires in the reserve.

Safety and Health

Safety in Shai Hills Resource Reserve

Shai Hills Resource Reserve is generally a safe destination, but it’s important to take precautions to ensure your safety. Some things to keep in mind include:

  • Do not wander off the designated trails or areas.
  • Be aware of your surroundings, especially when hiking or biking.
  • Avoid carrying too much cash or valuables with you.

Health in Shai Hills Resource Reserve

When visiting Shai Hills Resource Reserve, it’s important to take the necessary health precautions. Some things to keep in mind include:

  • Drink bottled water only, as the tap water is not always safe for drinking.
  • Use insect repellent to avoid mosquito bites, which can cause malaria and other diseases.
  • Make sure to have all the necessary vaccinations before traveling to Ghana.
