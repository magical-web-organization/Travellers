Introduction to Robertsport

Nestled along the Atlantic Ocean in the Grand Cape Mount County of Liberia, is the picturesque town of Robertsport. Not only is Robertsport a beautiful, laid-back town, but it is also steeped in history and culture that dates back to pre-colonial times. With a range of activities and attractions, from surfing and hiking to museums and historical sites, Robertsport is a hidden gem that is waiting to be discovered.

List of Places to Visit in Robertsport

Here are some must-see places that you should include in your itinerary when visiting Robertsport:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Sierra Leonean- Liberian-American Cultural Center Museum: This museum provides an insight into the Sierra Leonean, Liberian and American cultural heritage of the area.
  • The Historical Buildings of Robertsport: Robertsport boasts a rich history dating back to pre-colonial times, and its architecture reflects this. Take a stroll through the town and appreciate the beautiful buildings that have been preserved.

Natural Attractions of Robertsport: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Cape Mount: The highest mountain in Liberia offers beautiful views of the surrounding lush green landscape.
  • Lake Piso: This is Liberia’s largest lake and is surrounded by beautiful beaches which are great for a picnic, swimming or just relaxing.
  • Robertsport Beach: It is perfect for surfing, swimming, and sunbathing. The beach also hosts different surf competitions throughout the year.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Robertsport

Robertsport features beautiful hiking and biking trails that are surrounded by natural beauty. These trails lead you through the lush green forests and mountains around the town. Here are some routes that you can take:

  • The Trail to the Summit of Cape Mount: This is a challenging hike that rewards you with stunning views of the surrounding areas. It takes approximately 6-7 hours to complete.
  • Robertsport to Lakpatala Wildlife Sanctuary Route: This is a scenic bike ride that takes you through rice paddies, pristine forests and local villages.

Best Things to Do in Robertsport: Activities and Tours

Here are some activities and tours that you can take during your visit to Robertsport:

  • Surfing lessons: Robertsport is a surfer’s paradise, and there are several surf schools in the area that offer lessons for all skill levels.
  • Kayaking on Lake Piso: Take a peaceful paddle on the serene waters of Lake Piso and enjoy the beautiful scenery.
  • Fishing Trip: Join a local guide for a fishing trip on the Atlantic Ocean and catch some of the area’s delicious fish.

Week-Long Itinerary for Robertsport

If you have a week to explore Robertsport, you can follow this itinerary:

Day 1: Arrival and check-in at your accommodation.

Day 2: Visit Sierra Leonean-Liberian-American Cultural Centre Museum and take a walking tour of the town.

Day 3: Hike to the summit of Cape Mount

Day 4: Take a surf lesson and experience the beautiful waves of the Atlantic Ocean.

Day 5: Enjoy a kayaking trip to Lake Piso.

Day 6: Take a day trip to a nearby village and visit their market.

Day 7: Departure from Robertsport.

Food and Drink Scene of Robertsport

Liberian cuisine has many influences, including African, European and American. Robertsport does not disappoint if you are looking for locally inspired food and drinks.

Must-Try Dishes

  • Cassava Leaf: a spicy stew of cassava leaves and meat.
  • Jollof Rice: a flavorful rice dish that is popular throughout coastal West Africa.

Local Beverages

  • Palm Wine: a beverage made from the sap of palm trees that is fermented and has an alcohol content of approximately 3-6%.
  • Dig Water: a popular local drink made from sugarcane.

Where to Stay in Robertsport: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Robertsport has a range of accommodation options to suit different budgets and preferences. Here are some suggestions:

  • Guesthouses: These are popular in Robertsport and offer a homely atmosphere while providing affordable rooms.
  • Hostels: This type of accommodation offers shared rooms and amenities for budget-conscious travellers.
  • Hotels: If you prefer a more luxurious stay, there are several hotels in Robertsport that offer excellent service and amenities.

What to Buy in Robertsport: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

There are plenty of unique and authentic items that you can buy to take back home from Robertsport. Try some of the local markets and shops to find:

  • Handcrafted wooden carvings depicting traditional African designs
  • Beautiful handmade textiles made by local weavers
  • Handmade and unique jewelry and accessories

Local Culture and Customs of Robertsport

The people of Robertsport are friendly and welcoming, and their culture is based on respect and community. Here are some things to keep in mind when visiting:

  • It is not advisable to wear shorts or revealing clothing in public.
  • It is essential to ask for permission before taking photos of people.
  • Liberia is a conservative country, so it is always respectful to behave in a manner appropriate to its culture.

Best Time to Visit Robertsport

The best time to visit Robertsport is from December to April during the dry season. These months tend to have less rainfall, and the weather is perfect for outdoor activities such as hiking, surfing, and kayaking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Robertsport

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Try the local food.
  • Engage with the local people, they are friendly and welcoming.

Don’t:

  • Don’t wear revealing clothing in public.
  • Don’t take photographs without asking for permission.
  • Don’t be disrespectful towards the local community.

Safety and Health

Safety in Robertsport

Robertsport is generally safe for tourists. However, it is still a good idea to exercise caution, especially at night. Ensure that you are informed about the areas you are visiting and always stay aware of your surroundings.

Health in Robertsport

Consult your doctor before travelling to Robertsport, and ensure that you have all the necessary vaccinations. When in Robertsport, always drink bottled water, and avoid consuming raw foods or uncooked vegetables.

Getting to Robertsport: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Robertsport

  • Roberts International Airport, Liberia (ROB): This is the nearest international airport to Robertsport.

Bus Stations Near Robertsport

  • Gbarnga Bus Station: This is a popular bus station that services many routes in Liberia, including Robertsport.

Other Options

  • Taxis: Taxis are readily available in most urban areas and can be a convenient option for visitors to Robertsport.

How to Get Around in Robertsport

The best way to explore Robertsport is by foot, as most of the town’s attractions are within walking distance. For longer distances outside the town, you can arrange for a taxi or car rental.

Where to Go Next from Robertsport: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions that you can take from Robertsport:

  • Monrovia: Liberia’s capital city, located about 3 hours’ drive from Robertsport.
  • Lake Piso Forest Reserve: A protected area of rainforest that is home to many wildlife species.

Conclusion

Robertsport is a beautiful and unique destination that offers a blend of history, culture, and outdoor adventure. Whether you are a history buff or an adrenaline junkie, this town has something for everyone. With our comprehensive guide, you can now explore Robertsport with confidence and create unforgettable memories.
