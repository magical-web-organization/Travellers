Introduction to Fish River Canyon Park

Fish River Canyon Park is one of the most popular tourist destinations in Namibia, located in the ǁKaras region in Southern Africa. The park covers more than 160 km, and it is home to one of the largest canyons in the world, the Fish River Canyon. The canyon is approximately 160 km long, 27 km wide, and 550 meters deep, and it offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Fish River Canyon Park

Fish River Canyon Park is known for its challenging hiking trails, the most famous being the Fish River Canyon Hiking Trail. This trail is approximately 85 km long and takes approximately 5 to 7 days to complete. Other popular hiking and biking routes in Fish River Canyon Park include:

  • The Klipspringer Trail
  • The Canyon Road
  • The Arid Eden Route

Getting to Fish River Canyon Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Fish River Canyon Park

The nearest airport to Fish River Canyon Park is Keetmanshoop Airport, approximately 160 km away. There are also several international airports in Windhoek, the capital of Namibia.

Bus Stations Near Fish River Canyon Park

There are several bus stations near Fish River Canyon Park, including the Keetmanshoop Bus Station and the Mariental Bus Station.

Other Options

The most popular way to get to Fish River Canyon Park is by car or tour bus. You can also arrange for private transfers or use taxi services.

How to Get Around in Fish River Canyon Park

The best way to get around Fish River Canyon Park is by car or tour bus. There are also several hiking and biking trails for those who prefer an active adventure.
