Introduction to Rundu

Rundu is a bustling town located in the Kavango region of northern Namibia. It’s situated on the banks of the Kavango River, which forms the border with Angola, and is known for its rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and warm hospitality. Here, travelers can experience an authentic African adventure, whether it’s visiting local museums and historical sites or exploring the picturesque countryside. With a wide range of activities, attractions, and accommodations available, Rundu is an ideal destination for all types of travelers.

List of Places to Visit in Rundu

Rundu offers a host of attractions and activities for visitors to enjoy. Here is a list of the top places to visit.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Mbunza Living Museum: A fascinating outdoor museum that showcases the traditional way of life of the Mbunza tribe, one of the major ethnic groups in the area.

  • Rundu Open Market: The bustling central market of Rundu sells fresh produce, handcrafted souvenirs, and other local goods.

  • Divundu Crater: An impressive meteorite impact site located just outside the town.

  • Mbambi Cultural Village: Another traditional village museum that offers an immersive experience of the indigenous cultures of the Kavango region.

Natural Attractions of Rundu: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Kavango River: A scenic river that offers spectacular views of the surrounding landscape and is perfect for fishing, canoeing and bird-watching.

  • Bwabwata National Park: Located in the eastern part of the Kavango region, this national park boasts an impressive array of wildlife, including elephants, lions, cheetahs, and more.

  • Popa Falls: Located just a few kilometers from Rundu, Popa Falls is a series of rapids and cascades on the Kavango River that offers breathtaking views and a serene atmosphere.

  • The Mahangu Game Reserve: This reserve is home to many rare and unique species of wildlife such as roan antelopes, wild dogs, and hyenas.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Rundu

Rundu is surrounded by beautiful natural areas that are perfect for hiking and biking. Here are some of the top routes to explore:

  • The Kavango River Trail: A scenic trail along the river that offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape and the chance to see local wildlife up close.

  • The Divundu Hiking Trail: This trail offers hikers an authentic experience of the Kavango region’s culture and history while exploring the natural beauty of the area.

  • The Bwabwata Eco Trail: A challenging trail for experienced hikers that takes visitors through the heart of the Bwabwata National Park, offering a chance to see a diverse array of wildlife and stunning views of the landscape.

Best Things to Do in Rundu: Activities and Tours

From outdoor adventures to cultural experiences, there is no shortage of things to do in Rundu. Here are some of the best activities and tours:

  • Visit the Mbunza Living Museum to learn about the local culture and history of the Kavango people.

  • Take a scenic drive along the banks of the Kavango River to see stunning views of the countryside and spot wildlife such as hippos and crocodiles.

  • Go bird-watching at the Kavango River, where visitors can spot a variety of bird species, including kingfishers and bee-eaters.

  • Enjoy a sunset river cruise on the Kavango River to see the stunning colors of the sunset and enjoy a relaxing evening on the water.

Week-Long Itinerary for Rundu

To make the most of your trip to Rundu, we recommend the following itinerary:

Day 1: Explore the town and its historic landmarks. Day 2: Visit the Mbunza Living Museum and learn about the local culture. Day 3: Take a scenic drive along the Kavango River to Popa Falls. Day 4: Hike in the Divundu Hiking Trail and explore the natural beauty of the area. Day 5: Visit the Bwabwata National Park to see wildlife and stunning views. Day 6: Enjoy a sunset river cruise on the Kavango River. Day 7: Experience the local food and drink scene in Rundu before departing.

Food and Drink Scene of Rundu

Rundu’s food and drink scene is a mix of traditional African cuisine and Western-influenced dishes. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Kapana: A popular local dish made with grilled beef or goat meat, served with a spicy dip.

  • Oshifima: A type of porridge made from mahangu flour, served with meat or vegetables.

  • Mopane Worms: A traditional delicacy made from caterpillars of the Mopane moth, which are dried and fried.

Local Beverages

  • Oshikundu: A traditional drink made from mahangu flour, water, and sugar, served cold.

  • Tafel Lager: A popular local beer known for its refreshing taste and crisp finish.

Where to Stay in Rundu: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Rundu offers a range of accommodations to suit all budgets and preferences. Here are some of the top options:

  • Nkwazi Lodge: A luxurious lodge located on the banks of the Kavango River, offering stunning views and top-notch amenities.

  • Hakusembe River Lodge: A charming lodge located in the heart of the Kavango region, offering comfortable rooms and delicious food.

  • Tambuti Lodge: A cozy lodge located just outside Rundu, surrounded by nature and offering a tranquil atmosphere.

What to Buy in Rundu: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Rundu is known for its vibrant local market, which sells a variety of handmade crafts, souvenirs, and local products. Here are some of the top items to bring home:

  • Handcrafted wooden sculptures and carvings, made by local artisans.

  • Traditional textiles, such as colorful fabrics and woven baskets.

  • Locally grown produce, such as fresh fruit and vegetables.

Local Culture and Customs of Rundu

Rundu is a culturally rich town with a strong sense of tradition and community. Visitors are encouraged to embrace the culture and customs of the local people, including respecting local customs, dressing modestly, and being courteous and polite.

Best Time to Visit Rundu

The best time to visit Rundu is during the dry season, which runs from May to October. During this time, temperatures are mild, and wildlife is more active, making it an ideal time for outdoor activities and safaris.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rundu

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.

  • Dress appropriately, especially when visiting cultural sites.

  • Try the local cuisine and drinks.

Don’t:

  • Worry too much about safety, but take precautions such as not walking alone at night in less crowded areas.

  • Litter, as it is frowned upon and considered disrespectful.

Safety and Health

Safety in Rundu

Rundu is generally a safe town, but visitors should take precautions, such as not walking alone at night in less crowded areas and taking care of valuables.

Health in Rundu

Travelers should be up to date with their vaccinations and take necessary precautions against common diseases such as Malaria. It is recommended to consult a doctor before traveling to Rundu.

Getting to Rundu: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Rundu

Rundu has its airport, the Rundu Airport, which connects it to the major cities of Namibia.

Bus Stations Near Rundu

Intercape and coach companies operate bus trips to Rundu frequently. There are bus stations in the town center.

Other Options

Renting a car is an excellent option for getting around Rundu and exploring the surrounding areas.

How to Get Around in Rundu

Taxis are readily available in Rundu, and they are safe and affordable. Car rentals are also a good option for exploring the town and its surroundings.

Where to Go Next from Rundu: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

From Rundu, visitors can explore the famous Caprivi Strip, a long and narrow band of land that stretches through Namibia, Botswana, and Zimbabwe, offering visitors breathtaking views and unique experiences.

Conclusion

Rundu is a gem of a destination in Namibia, offering a unique mix of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and warm hospitality. With this comprehensive tour guide, visitors can make the most of their trip, experience the best of what Rundu has to offer, and create memories that will last a lifetime.
