Introduction to Francistown

Francistown is a bustling city located in the southeast of Botswana, in Southern Africa. This city is famous for its rich cultural heritage, natural beauty, and a perfect blend of modern and traditional lifestyle. The city is home to a population of around 100,000 people and is a hub of trade, commerce, and culture in the region. Francistown is the perfect destination for travelers who want to explore the wilderness, learn about the history and culture of Botswana, and indulge in modern luxuries.

List of Places to Visit in Francistown

Here is a list of places to visit in Francistown to get an insight into its rich cultural heritage and natural beauty.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Supa Ngwao Museum: This museum displays the cultural and historical heritage of Botswana, including traditional crafts, artifacts, and photography.
  • Tachila Nature Reserve: It is the perfect place to explore the natural beauty and wildlife of the region.
  • White City: This is a historic shopping complex located in the center of Francistown, which offers a unique shopping experience.

Natural Attractions of Francistown: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Tswapong Hills: It is a beautiful mountain range located near Francistown, which offers excellent hiking and trekking opportunities.
  • Domboshaba Ruins: It is a significant archaeological site and offers a glimpse of the country’s rich cultural and historical heritage.
  • Matsiloje Hot Springs: This natural geothermal spring is a perfect place to relax and unwind.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Francistown

Francistown offers some of the best hiking and biking routes, which showcase the scenic beauty and wildlife of the region. Some popular routes include:

  • Tswapong Hills Trail: This is a 20km trail that traverses through the beautiful Tswapong Hills and offers stunning views.
  • Domboshaba Trail: This is a 15km trail that offers a glimpse of the cultural and historical heritage of the region.
  • Old Gerald Trail: This is a 30km trail that offers a thrilling biking experience and takes you through lush green forests and mountain ranges.

Best Things to Do in Francistown: Activities and Tours

Here are some of the best things to do in Francistown:

  • Take a cultural tour of the Supa Ngwao Museum and learn about Botswana’s rich cultural heritage.
  • Explore the natural beauty of Tswapong Hills and indulge in hiking and trekking.
  • Go on a safari at Tachila Nature Reserve and witness the wildlife of the region up close.
  • Take a hot spring dip at Matsiloje Hot Springs and relax amidst the serene surroundings.

Food and Drink Scene of Francistown

The food and drink scene of Francistown is a perfect reflection of the region’s cultural and historical heritage. Here are some of the must-try dishes and local beverages of Francistown:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Seswaa: This is a traditional dish made with meat, onions, and salt.
  • Pap: A staple food made with maize and water.
  • Vetkoek: A fried doughnut-like dish that is served with a savory or sweet filling.

Local Beverages

  • Chibuku: A traditional beer made from maize and sorghum.
  • Mampoer: A potent liquor made from fruit.

Where to Stay in Francistown: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Francistown offers a range of accommodation options, from budget-friendly hostels to luxury hotels. Here are some options:

  • Cresta Thapama Hotel: A luxury hotel located in the heart of Francistown with all modern amenities.
  • Woodlands Stop-Over Lodge: A budget-friendly accommodation option that offers comfortable rooms and a warm ambiance.
  • Diggers Inn: A guesthouse with a friendly atmosphere located in the city center.

What to Buy in Francistown: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some souvenirs and local products to bring home from Francistown:

  • Handcrafted baskets and jewelry.
  • Traditional pottery and wood carvings.
  • Local spices and tea blends.

Local Culture and Customs of Francistown

The culture and customs of Francistown are a perfect example of the region’s rich heritage. It is essential to respect the local customs and traditions while exploring the city. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Dress modestly while visiting places of worship or cultural attractions.
  • Greetings are an essential part of the culture in Botswana, and it is customary to shake hands while greeting someone.
  • Tipping is not expected but appreciated for excellent service.

Best Time to Visit Francistown

The best time to visit Francistown is from April to September when the weather is dry and mild. The summer months from October to March can be hot and humid.

Do’s and Don’ts of Francistown

Here are some do’s and don’ts to keep in mind while traveling in Francistown:

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.
  • Explore the city’s natural beauty and cultural heritage.
  • Try the local food and drinks.

Don’t:

  • Engage in public displays of affection.
  • Disrespect the local customs and traditions.
  • Take photographs of people without their consent.

Safety and Health

Safety in Francistown

While Francistown is generally a safe city, it is essential to take precautions to ensure a safe trip. Here are some tips:

  • Avoid walking alone at night.
  • Be aware of pickpockets and keep your belongings safe.
  • Take precautions while driving on the highways and be aware of animals on the road.

Health in Francistown

It is essential to take precautions while traveling in Francistown to prevent any health hazards. Here are some tips:

  • Carry mosquito repellent and wear long-sleeved clothing to prevent mosquito bites.
  • Drink bottled water and avoid tap water.
  • Carry a first aid kit with basic medications and supplies.

Getting to Francistown: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Francistown

The Francistown International Airport is the primary airport serving the city and is located around 3 km from the city center.

Bus Stations Near Francistown

Francistown is well-connected by bus, with several bus companies operating services to and from the city. Some of the popular bus stations are:

  • Francistown Bus Station
  • Bobonong Bus Station
  • Lerala Bus Station

Other Options

There are several car rental companies in Francistown, and it is an excellent option for travelers who want to explore the city at their pace.

How to Get Around in Francistown

The best way to get around in Francistown is by car rental or taxi. There are several taxi services available in the city, which can be hired for a fixed price or by the meter.

Where to Go Next from Francistown: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Francistown is an excellent starting point for exploring other destinations in Botswana. Here are some suggestions:

  • Gaborone: The capital city of Botswana, located around 2 hours from Francistown.
  • Kasane: A popular tourist destination, located around 4 hours from Francistown, and is known for its wildlife and natural beauty.

Conclusion

Francistown is a vibrant city with a rich cultural heritage and natural beauty. It is a perfect destination for travelers who want to explore the wilderness, learn about the history and culture of Botswana, and indulge in modern luxuries. With this comprehensive tour guide, you can plan a memorable trip to Francistown and experience the best the city has to offer.
