Introduction to Valley of 1000 Hills

Valley of 1000 Hills is a stunning destination that offers visitors an unforgettable experience. Located in South Coast, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa, the area boasts of breathtaking natural beauty that draws visitors from around the world. The area got its name from the thousand hills that surround it, all of which offer unforgettable views of lush green valleys and meandering rivers. Whether you are a history buff or a nature lover, there is something for everyone in this valley.

List of Places to Visit in Valley of 1000 Hills

When you visit Valley of 1000 Hills, there are many places to visit and things to do. Here is a comprehensive list of sights and attractions that you should not miss.

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Umgeni Steam Railway: This is a heritage railway that is operated by volunteers. It is a fun experience for both adults and children, offering a scenic ride through the valley on vintage locomotives.
  • The Mariannhill Monastery: This is a historic monastery founded in the late 19th century by Trappist monks. Visitors can learn about the history of the monastery and the monks’ way of life.
  • Durban Holocaust Centre: A thought-provoking museum dedicated to educating visitors about the Holocaust and what it means today.

Natural Attractions of Valley of 1000 Hills: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Kloof Gorge: This is a nature reserve that is ideal for hiking and nature walks. Visitors can enjoy walks through lush forests, bird watching, and breathtaking views of the valley.
  • Inanda Dam: A popular spot for fishing and water sports, the dam also has a scenic picnic area and braai facilities.
  • Shongweni Dam Nature Reserve: This is a beautiful reserve that offers breathtaking views of the river and the surrounding hills. Visitors can enjoy fishing, boating, and bird watching in this serene environment.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Valley of 1000 Hills

Valley of 1000 Hills is a great destination for hiking and cycling enthusiasts. Here are some of the best routes to explore:

  • Kranzkloof Nature Reserve: This is a challenging hike that is suitable for experienced hikers. The trail winds through dense forests, rocky cliffs, and beautiful waterfalls.
  • Giba Gorge MTB Park: This is a popular mountain bike trail that offers beautiful views of the valley. The trails are suitable for all levels of mountain bikers, from beginners to experts.
  • Shongweni Dam Nature Reserve: The reserve offers several hiking and cycling trails, with different levels of difficulty.

Best Things to Do in Valley of 1000 Hills: Activities and Tours

Valley of 1000 Hills has a wide range of activities and tours that will keep you entertained throughout your stay. Here are some of the top things to do:

  • Hot air balloon rides: Enjoy a breathtaking view of the valley from above.
  • Zulu cultural tours: Learn about Zulu culture and traditions, including traditional dances and songs.
  • Helicopter tours: Get a bird’s eye view of the valley on an exhilarating helicopter ride.
  • Wildlife safaris: Explore the local wildlife and nature reserves on a guided safari tour.

Food and Drink Scene of Valley of 1000 Hills

Valley of 1000 Hills offers a unique culinary experience that combines traditional Zulu dishes with contemporary cuisine. Here are some must-try dishes and local beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Amasi: A traditional Zulu dish made from fermented milk.
  • Bunny Chow: A popular street food made of a bread loaf filled with curry.
  • Boerewors: A type of sausage made from beef and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Umqombothi: A traditional African beer made from sorghum and maize.
  • Rooibos tea: A herbal tea grown in South Africa, it is known for its health benefits and delicious taste.

Where to Stay in Valley of 1000 Hills: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Valley of 1000 Hills has many accommodation options for all budgets. Whether you are looking for luxury hotels or budget-friendly hostels, there is something for everyone. Here are some top recommendations:

  • Ikhaya Lami Hostel: A budget-friendly hostel that offers clean and comfortable dormitory rooms.
  • The Hilton Durban: This luxurious hotel offers stunning views of the valley and is located close to major attractions.
  • Qambathi Mountain Lodge: For a unique experience, stay at this luxurious lodge located in the Drakensberg Mountains. It offers breathtaking views of the valley and has its own wildlife reserve.

What to Buy in Valley of 1000 Hills: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Valley of 1000 Hills offers a wide range of souvenirs and local products for you to take home as a memento of your trip. Here are some recommendations:

  • Zulu beadwork: Beautiful beaded jewelry and traditional Zulu artifacts.
  • Craft beer: Take home a taste of South Africa with some locally brewed craft beer.
  • Artwork: Buy some locally-made artwork, from paintings to sculptures and prints.

Local Culture and Customs of Valley of 1000 Hills

The Zulu people are the dominant culture in Valley of 1000 Hills. It is important to respect their customs and traditions while visiting the area. Here are some tips:

  • Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Remove your shoes when entering someone’s home.
  • Do not touch someone’s head without permission, as it is considered rude.

Best Time to Visit Valley of 1000 Hills

The best time to visit Valley of 1000 Hills is during the cooler months from May to September. The weather is mild, and there are fewer crowds. However, if you are a wildlife enthusiast, it is best to visit from June to August when the animals are more active.

Do’s and Don’ts of Valley of 1000 Hills

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Try the local cuisine and beverages.
  • Take a scenic drive through the valley.

Don’t:

  • Wear revealing clothing when visiting religious sites.
  • Take photographs of people without their permission.
  • Leave valuables unattended.

Safety and Health

Safety in Valley of 1000 Hills

Valley of 1000 Hills is a safe area to visit. However, it is always advisable to take precautions, such as avoiding isolated areas at night and not flashing valuables in public.

Health in Valley of 1000 Hills

There are no major health risks in Valley of 1000 Hills. However, it is advisable to take the necessary precautions, such as drinking bottled water and wearing insect repellent while outdoors.

Getting to Valley of 1000 Hills: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Valley of 1000 Hills

The closest airport to Valley of 1000 Hills is King Shaka International Airport in Durban, located approximately 50 km away.

Bus Stations Near Valley of 1000 Hills

The closest bus station is the Greyhound Bus Station in Durban, approximately 50 km from Valley of 1000 Hills.

Other Options

Car rental services are available from King Shaka International Airport and Durban.

How to Get Around in Valley of 1000 Hills

The best way to get around Valley of 1000 Hills is by car or taxi. However, there are also bicycle rentals and guided tours available for visitors who prefer not to drive.
