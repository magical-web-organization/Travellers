Introduction to Wartrail and New England

Wartrail and New England are located in the heart of Settler Country, in the Eastern Cape province of South Africa. These two small farming communities are nestled in the beautiful valleys and peaks of the Southern Drakensberg Mountains. Wartrail and New England are surrounded by stunning natural beauty that will take your breath away. This area is a must-visit for those who love adventure and outdoor activities like hiking, mountain biking, fly-fishing, skiing, and snowboarding.

Local Culture and Customs of Wartrail and New England

The culture in Wartrail and New England is heavily influenced by the Sotho and Xhosa tribes, who have lived in the area for centuries. Visitors can experience the vibrant cultural traditions and customs through local music, art, and traditional dances.

Best Time to Visit Wartrail and New England

The best time to visit Wartrail and New England is in the summer months between November to February. The weather is warm and perfect for outdoor activities like hiking, biking, and fishing.
