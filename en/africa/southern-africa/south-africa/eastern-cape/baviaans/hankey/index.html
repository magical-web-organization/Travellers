Introduction to Hankey

Hankey is an extraordinary tourist destination located in the heart of Eastern Cape’s Baviaans district, South Africa. The town’s unusual geographical location makes it an oasis for outdoor enthusiasts looking for an unparalleled blend of natural wonders and culturally significant attractions. Hankey’s charming atmosphere, unique history, and world-class scenic beauty have resulted in it being one of the most desirable places to visit in South Africa.

List of Places to Visit in Hankey

Here are some top tourist destinations in Hankey that you must add to your itinerary:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums, and Historical Places

  • The Cuyler Manor Museum: A historic building that tells the story of the region’s earliest settlers and their everyday life.

  • The Sentraal Suid Co-Operative: It is a cultural heritage site that is known for great architecture and designing.

  • The Hankey Cemetery: It is a place of historical significance where many of Hankey’s prominent inhabitants rest at peace.

Natural Attractions of Hankey: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • The Kouga Dam: The captivating man-made reservoir provides an ideal spot for swimming, fishing, and boating.

  • The Baviaanskloof Biosphere Reserve: The reserve is an outstanding natural wonder and a haven for bird-watchers, hikers, and nature lovers.

  • The Gamtoos River Valley: Is one of the most scenic areas in South Africa and ideal for diverse activities such as trail running, fishing, and picnicking.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Hankey

Hankey is a great destination for outdoor enthusiasts looking for an active adventure. The town offers numerous hiking and biking trails for travelers of all skill levels. Here are some popular trails that you must explore:

  • Kouga Wilderness Trail: An excellent choice for hikers looking for an exhilarating adventure in the two-day trail. It offers unmatched beauty and landscape views of the surrounding region.

  • The Dolphin Trail: One of the most scenic coastal hiking trails. It stretches over four days and covers 17 km of pure natural beauty.

  • Tsitsikamma Mountain Trail: It is a five-day hike for adventurers seeking breathtaking views of the ocean, the forests, and the wildlife.

Best Things to Do in Hankey: Activities and Tours

Besides hiking and biking, Hankey offers a wide range of activities and tours that you must add to your itinerary:

  • River Rafting: The Gamtoos River offers a fantastic rafting experience for adrenaline-seekers.

  • Bungee Jumping: One of the world’s highest bungee jumping spots, Bloukrans Bridge offers a thrilling experience.

  • Wildlife Safari and Vineyard Tour: An opportunity to experience the natural beauty of Hankey and its splendid vineyards.

Week-Long Itinerary for Hankey

Here’s an ideal itinerary for a week-long trip to Hankey:

Food and Drink Scene of Hankey

Hankey offers a variety of local culinary delights that are must-try dishes and beverages. Here are some popular options:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Bobotie: A traditional South African dish made with ground meat and spices.

  • Biltong: Dried, cured meat that is a popular snack throughout South Africa.

  • Koeksisters: A traditional Afrikaner pastry made with dough and covered in syrup.

Local Beverages

  • Amarula: A locally produced alcoholic drink made from the Marula fruit.

  • Rooibos Tea: A caffeine-free tea, famous for its unique taste and health benefits.

Where to Stay in Hankey: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Hankey offers various accommodation options that cater to all budgets and group sizes. Here are some of the best places to stay:

  • Bokmakierie Place: A stylish guesthouse located in Hankey town.

  • Gamtoos Bed & Breakfast: A charming B&B located in close proximity to the Gamtoos River.

  • Crows Nest: Located on the outskirts of Hankey, this accommodation place offers a unique glamping and camping experience.

What to Buy in Hankey: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Here are some popular products that you must buy as souvenirs or gifts from Hankey:

  • Beaded jewelry: Made by the locals, the jewelry is uniquely crafted with colorful beads.

  • Amarula Liquor: A locally brewed alcoholic beverage made from the Marula fruit.

  • Handmade crafts: You can buy handmade items made of wood, stones, and other materials.

Local Culture and Customs of Hankey

Hankey is a cultural blend of various influences, including European, African, and Indian. The locals are welcoming, friendly, and proud of their heritage. The town is known for its unique music, artworks, and poetry.

Best Time to Visit Hankey

The best time to visit Hankey is from May to September when the weather is mild and dry, making it a perfect time to enjoy outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Hankey

Here are some do’s and don’ts when traveling to Hankey:

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and traditions.

  • Follow the rules and regulations of the different sites and attractions.

  • Consider practicing sustainable tourism when exploring the town.

Don’t:

  • Don’t leave garbage lying around.

  • Don’t show disregard for the local population and their beliefs.

  • Don’t venture into areas that are off-limits.

Safety and Health

Hankey is a safe place for tourists to visit. However, like any other destination, you must take precautions to ensure your safety and good health.

Safety in Hankey

  • Avoid walking alone at night in isolated areas.

  • Keep an eye on your belongings when in public or tourist areas.

  • Use reliable taxi services when traveling long distances or at night.

Health in Hankey

  • Drink only bottled or boiled water.

  • Carry sunscreen, sunglasses, and hats to protect yourself from the harsh sun.

  • Carry OTC and prescription medication with you in case of any health emergencies.

Getting to Hankey: Transportation Options and Logistics

Here are some popular transportation options that you can use to reach Hankey:

Airports Near Hankey

The nearest airport to Hankey is the Port Elizabeth International Airport, which is located 80 km from the town. From the airport, you can hire a taxi, rent a car or take the bus to Hankey.

Bus Stations Near Hankey

The nearest bus stations are located in Port Elizabeth, and the town is accessible via intercity bus services.

Other Options

Several rental car companies operate in Port Elizabeth, and you can rent a car to reach Hankey.

How to Get Around in Hankey

Hankey is a small town, and it is easy to explore on foot. However, if you want to explore further, you can hire a car or take a taxi.

Where to Go Next from Hankey: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations and excursions that you can visit after exploring Hankey:

  • Port Elizabeth: A coastal city located 80 km from Hankey, known for its wide array of tourist attractions, including wildlife parks and beach activities.

  • Jeffreys Bay: Famous among surfers, it is a coastal town located 110 km from Hankey.

  • Addo Elephant National Park: An excellent destination located 135 km from Hankey, with a significant elephant population, among other wildlife.

Conclusion

Hankey is an incredible tourist destination that gives visitors the opportunity to enjoy South Africa’s natural wonders and cultural heritage. Make sure to add Hankey to your South African itinerary and explore the town’s rich history, outdoor activities, and local areas.
