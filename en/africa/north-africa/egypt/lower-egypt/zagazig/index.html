Introduction to Zagazig

Zagazig is a city located in Lower Egypt, known for its rich history and beautiful scenery. The city has become one of the most popular tourist destinations in Egypt, attracting visitors from all over the world.

List of Places to Visit in Zagazig

If you’re planning a trip to Zagazig, here are some must-visit places that should be at the top of your list:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Sharkeya Museum: A museum that showcases the history and culture of Sharkeya Governorate, including various artifacts from ancient tomb ruins.
  • Zagazig Cultural Palace: A cultural center that hosts various events and exhibitions, including art exhibits and live performances.
  • Saidi House Museum: A museum showcasing the lifestyle and culture of Saidi people, including their traditional clothes, crafts, and music.

Natural Attractions of Zagazig: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Nile River: Take a boat ride on the Nile and enjoy the beautiful scenery of the river.
  • Wadi El-Rayan: A nature reserve located 5 hours away from Zagazig that offers stunning waterfalls, lakes, and great birdwatching spots.
  • Mount Sinai: A mountain in the Sinai Peninsula, about 6 hours away from Zagazig, is believed to be where Moses received the Ten Commandments.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Zagazig

Zagazig offers several hiking and biking trails that take you through scenic landscapes and natural wonders. The most popular routes in Zagazig include:

  • El-Temsah: A scenic trek that offers stunning views of the Nile River and the surrounding mountains.
  • Jabal Al Hashir: A hiking trail that takes you through the beautiful mountains and offers stunning views of the city.
  • Kherbit El-Rowaniya: A mountain hike that takes you through the scenic nature reserve and offers the chance to spot various wildlife species.

Best Things to Do in Zagazig: Activities and Tours

  • Visit the Pharaoh’s Island: A historical site and a popular tourist attraction; it’s a small island with a castle and ancient ruins.
  • Take a stroll in Al-Azhar park: A large park in the middle of the city, perfect for a picnic or family outing.
  • Tour Sharkeya Governorate: Visit the different villages and towns within the governorate, each with its own unique cultural and historical landmarks.

Food and Drink Scene of Zagazig

Must-Try Dishes

  • Koshary: a popular Egyptian dish made of rice, lentils, chickpeas, tomatoes, and topped with onions.
  • Falafel: deep-fried balls made of chickpeas or fava beans.
  • Molokhia: a popular Egyptian soup made of molokhia leaves, chicken or meat, and spices.

Local Beverages

  • Hibiscus tea: a refreshing tea made from dried hibiscus flowers.
  • Karkadeh: another popular Egyptian tea made of hibiscus, sugar, and mint.

Where to Stay in Zagazig: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Zagazig offers a variety of accommodations to suit different preferences and budgets. Here are some of the best options:

  • Grand Zagazig Hotel: A 5-star hotel located in the heart of Zagazig, offering luxurious accommodations and excellent amenities.
  • Sakkara Inn Hotel: A budget-friendly hotel located near the city center, offering comfortable accommodations and friendly service.
  • Al-Tahrir Grand Hotel: A spacious hotel located near the city’s main attractions, offering a range of facilities and amenities.

What to Buy in Zagazig: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

When in Zagazig, make sure to get some souvenirs and local products to bring back home. Here are some of the best options:

  • Handmade crafts: Zagazig is known for its beautiful handmade crafts, including pottery, woven baskets, and handmade fabrics.
  • Local foods and spices: Bring home some of the local foods and spices, including Za’atar, the popular Arabic spice mix.
  • Jewelry: Check out the local jewelry stores for some unique and beautiful pieces.

Local Culture and Customs of Zagazig

In Zagazig, it’s essential to respect the local culture and customs. Here are some tips:

  • Dress modestly when visiting religious sites.
  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions.
  • Refrain from taking pictures of people unless you ask permission.

Best Time to Visit Zagazig

The best time to visit Zagazig is between October and April when the weather is mild and comfortable. The summer months, from June to August, can be extremely hot, making it difficult to enjoy outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Zagazig

Do:

  • Be respectful of local customs and traditions.
  • Dress appropriately in mosques and religious sites.
  • Take the time to learn some Arabic phrases.

Don’t:

  • Drink tap water.
  • Display affection in public.
  • Disrespect local culture and traditions.

Safety and Health

Safety in Zagazig

Zagazig is a relatively safe city for travelers, but it’s always best to take precautions. Here are some safety tips:

  • Avoid walking alone at night.
  • Make sure to keep your belongings safe.
  • Be cautious when using public transportation.

Health in Zagazig

Zagazig has several hospitals and clinics that offer medical assistance. However, it’s always best to take precautions when traveling. Here are some health tips:

  • Drink bottled water.
  • Protect yourself against the sun by wearing sunscreen.
  • Bring any necessary medication with you.

Getting to Zagazig: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Zagazig

Zagazig doesn’t have its airport, but the closest airport is Cairo International Airport, which is about an hour and a half away by car.

Bus Stations Near Zagazig

Zagazig has several bus stations that connect the city to other destinations in Egypt, including:

  • Zagazig Bus Station.
  • El-Tahrir Bus Station.
  • El-Salam Bus Station.

Other Transportation Options

Taxis and private cars offer transportation services within Zagazig and to other nearby destinations.

How to Get Around in Zagazig

Zagazig has several transportation options to help you get around the city, including:

  • Taxis: Taxis are widely available and a convenient way to get around the city.
  • Buses: The city has several bus routes that connect different parts of the city.
  • Tuk-tuks: Tuk-tuks are a popular mode of transportation for short distances.

Where to Go Next from Zagazig: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Here are some nearby destinations that you can consider visiting after your trip to Zagazig:

  • Cairo: The cosmopolitan capital of Egypt, known for its ancient history and modern architecture.
  • Alexandria: A coastal city that offers beautiful beaches, historical landmarks, and a vibrant nightlife.
  • Luxor: A city in Upper Egypt that’s home to several historic sites and temples.

Conclusion

Zagazig is a charming city that offers a mix of culture, history, and nature. Whether you’re interested in learning about the city’s past, exploring its natural wonders, or simply relaxing, Zagazig offers something for everyone.
