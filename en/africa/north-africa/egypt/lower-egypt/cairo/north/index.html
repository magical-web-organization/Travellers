Introduction to North

North is a beautiful district located in Cairo, Lower Egypt, Egypt. It is one of the most visited regions in Cairo, known for its rich history, cultural landmarks, and stunning natural scenery. Whether you’re a nature lover, outdoor enthusiast, or history buff, North has something for everyone. In this guide, we’ll take a closer look at the best places to visit, things to do, where to stay, and local culture of North.

List of Places to Visit in North

Here are the top places to visit in North:

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • The Egyptian Textile Museum: The museum showcases the rich history of textile arts in Egypt and features a variety of traditional textile artworks, including woven fabrics, carpets, and more.

  • The Coptic Museum: A must-visit museum for history enthusiasts, it displays a large collection of Coptic art and ancient artifacts, including manuscripts, textiles, and holy icons.

  • The Tell Basta Museum: The museum is dedicated to documenting the history and archaeological finds of Tell Basta, one of the most significant ancient sites in the area. It features a collection of pottery and other historic and artistic relics.

Natural Attractions of North: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Al-Alamein Beach: Located on the Mediterranean Sea coast, the beach offers a stunning view of the sea and its surroundings. Visitors can enjoy swimming in crystal clear water or taking long walks on the beach.

  • Rasheed Beach: This serene and picturesque beach is an excellent choice for families with children. Edged by palm trees and grand hotels, it is a fantastic spot for relaxing, swimming, and sunbathing.

  • Wadi El-Natrun Valley: A desert oasis located just 100 kilometers from Cairo, this picturesque valley sits among the hills surrounded by palm trees and is known for its beautiful monastery complexes.

Hiking/Biking Routes of North

North is an excellent destination for hiking and biking enthusiasts. Here are some of the best hiking/biking routes in North:

  • The Whales Valley: An excellent hiking and biking route that extends through the desert and along the shore, starting in El-Alamein and passing by the Whales Valley and the Roman ruins.

  • The Moses Mountain: This hiking trail starts in Wadi El Natrun and takes hikers on a journey through the valleys and mountains surrounding the monastery.

  • The Wadi Hitan Reserve: The reserve is a protected area in the middle of the Sahara desert, located near El Fayoum. It is a popular spot for hiking, biking, and camping due to its unique geological formations and diverse wildlife.

Best Things to Do in North: Activities and Tours

North offers a plethora of activities and tours for visitors. Here are some of the best things to do in North:

  • Sea Adventures: Visitors can enjoy a wide range of sea activities, including swimming, snorkeling, and diving in the crystal clear waters of the Mediterranean Sea.

  • Historical Tours: North is home to significant historical landmarks, including the story-rich WW2 memorials and ancient temples that make for a fascinating insight into the country’s past.

Week-Long Itinerary for North

Here is a suggested week-long itinerary for exploring North:

Day 1: Visit the Tell Basta Museum, then relax on Al-Alamein Beach

Day 2: Take a historical tour of the WW2 memorials

Day 3: Explore the Wadi El-Natrun Valley

Day 4: Go hiking in the Whales Valley

Day 5: Visit the Coptic Museum and the Egyptian Textile Museum

Day 6: Take a day trip to the Moses Mountain

Day 7: Indulge in some sea adventures at Rasheed Beach

Food and Drink Scene of North

North is known for its delicious cuisine and unique beverages. Here are some must-try dishes and beverages:

Must-Try Dishes

  • Koshary: A popular street food dish made with mixed lentils, rice, and pasta topped with a spicy tomato sauce.

  • Fiteer: A delicious pastry dish made with flaky layers of dough baked into crispy, golden layers, served with a variety of fillings.

Local Beverages

  • Sahlab: A hot and creamy beverage made with milk, cinnamon, sugar and topped with nuts.

  • Karkadeh: A refreshing and zesty red tea made from hibiscus.

Where to Stay in North: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

North offers a range of accommodation options, from luxury hotels to affordable hostels and guesthouses. Here are some of the top places to stay:

  • Four Seasons Hotel Alexandria: A luxurious hotel featuring premium suites, elegant restaurants, panoramic views, and an exceptional spa.

  • Hilton Alexandria King’s Ranch: An upscale hotel ideally located in the heart of the North, featuring elegant rooms, outdoor terraces, and beautiful gardens.

What to Buy in North: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

North is known for its handicrafts and local souvenirs. Here are some of the best products to bring home:

  • Hand-woven Carpets and Rugs: A unique and intricate representation of Egyptian textile art.

  • Papyrus Artworks: Beautiful artwork created by skilled artisans on the Egyptian papyrus plant.

Local Culture and Customs of North

Egypt has a rich cultural heritage, and North is no exception. Here are some essential cultural insights to keep in mind while visiting North:

  • Religion: North has a predominantly Muslim population, and visitors should be respectful of religious traditions and dress modestly.

  • Greeting: A typical local greeting involves shaking hands and then placing the right hand over the heart.

Best Time to Visit North

The best time to visit North is during the shoulder season, from March to May and September to November, when the weather is pleasant, and there are fewer crowds than during peak season.

Do’s and Don’ts of North

When visiting North, here are some important do’s and don’ts to keep in mind:

Do:

  • Respect Local Customs and Traditions: Dress modestly, especially when visiting religious sites.

  • Be Friendly and Polite: Egyptians are known for their warm hospitality, so visitors should strive to be friendly and engage with locals.

Don’t:

  • Be Disrespectful: Avoid showing disrespect towards the local culture and religion.

  • Drink Tap Water: Tap water in North is not safe for consumption, so visitors should stick to bottled water only.

Safety and Health

When traveling to North, it’s essential to keep safety and health in mind. Here are some useful tips to ensure a safe and healthy trip:

Safety in North

  • Be Aware of Your Surroundings: As with any tourist destination, visitors should be aware of their surroundings and keep an eye on their belongings.

  • Avoid Solo Night Walks: Visitors are advised to avoid solo night walks, especially in unfamiliar areas.

Health in North

  • Stay Hydrated: Drink plenty of bottled water to avoid dehydration, which is common in the hot and dry climate.

  • Observe Punctuality of Medicine: If you are on medication, be sure to observe the punctuality to maintain healthy balanced within your body while on holiday.

Getting to North: Transportation Options and Logistics

North is easily accessible via air, bus, or other means of transport. Here are some options:

Airports Near North

  • Cairo International Airport: The primary airport located about 25 kilometers from North.

Bus Stations Near North

  • Turgoman Bus Station: Located in central Cairo, from where visitors can easily take a bus or arrange a taxi to North.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire private cars, taxis or join tours that include transport.

How to Get Around in North

There are several ways to get around North, including taxis, rental cars, buses, and minibusses. Taxis are readily available and offer a convenient and affordable way to explore the region.

Where to Go Next from North: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Exploring the neighboring areas of North can be a great way to expand your cultural experience in Egypt. Here are some nearby destinations and excursions worth exploring:

  • Cairo: The capital city of Egypt, Cairo, is a must-visit destination for history and culture enthusiasts.

  • Giza Pyramids: A stunning landmark located just outside Cairo, featuring several ancient pyramids and temples.

Conclusion

North is a fantastic destination for anyone looking to explore the rich culture, history, and nature of Cairo. From stunning beaches to ancient monuments and delicious cuisine, there is something here for everyone. By following this comprehensive guide, you are sure to have an unforgettable experience in North.
