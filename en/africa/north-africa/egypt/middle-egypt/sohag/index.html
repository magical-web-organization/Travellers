Introduction to Sohag

Located in the Middle Egypt region, Sohag is a beautiful city known for its rich history, architecture, and natural beauty. This ancient city has been inhabited since 3200 BC, making it one of the oldest cities in Egypt. Today, Sohag is a popular tourist destination in Egypt that offers plenty of exciting experiences to visitors.

Local Culture and Customs of Sohag

Sohag is predominantly Muslim and adheres to the Islamic traditions, but the city also embraces a Christian minority. Visitors are advised to dress modestly while sightseeing, with women covering their heads, arms, and legs with loose-fitting clothing. In addition, it is important to respect the local customs and beliefs.

Best Time to Visit Sohag

The best time to visit Sohag is from October to April when the weather is mild, perfect for outdoor activities, and sightseeing.

Safety and Health

Safety in Sohag

Sohag is a relatively safe city with low crime rates. Tourists are advised to take caution when traveling after dark and avoid poorly lit areas. It is recommended to carry minimal cash and valuables while visiting public areas.

Health in Sohag

Visitors are advised to take precautionary measures against mosquito bites and to carry medications to prevent potential diseases. It is important to stay hydrated in the hot weather, wear sunscreen, and to take caution when swimming in the Nile River.
