Introduction to Cirque de Mafate

Cirque de Mafate is a volcanic caldera located in Réunion National Park, in Réunion, one of the East African Islands located in Africa. The caldera is surrounded by steep mountains and can only be accessed by hiking, making it a remote and authentic destination that offers a unique experience for adventure seekers and nature lovers.

List of Places to Visit in Cirque de Mafate

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • Maison Folio: A museum that showcases the history and lifestyle of the people of Cirque de Mafate.
  • Notre-Dame-des-Neiges church: A historic church that dates back to the 19th century.
  • Roche Plate village: A traditional village that showcases the unique way of life of the people of Cirque de Mafate.

Natural Attractions of Cirque de Mafate: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Piton des Neiges: The highest peak in Réunion Island, offering breathtaking views of Cirque de Mafate from its summit.
  • Col du Taïbit: A scenic viewpoint that offers panoramic views of Cirque de Mafate and the surrounding mountains.
  • Trou de Fer: A beautiful waterfall that can be accessed by hiking or by helicopter.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Cirque de Mafate

Cirque de Mafate offers some of the best hiking and biking routes that are suitable for both beginners and experienced hikers. Some popular routes include:

  • Sentier Scout: A 6 km trail that offers beautiful views of Roche Plate and the surrounding mountains.
  • Sentier des Tamarins: A 10 km trail that offers breathtaking views of the Indian Ocean and the mountains.
  • Sentier Ravenale: A 4 km trail that takes visitors through lush forests and scenic viewpoints.

Best Things to Do in Cirque de Mafate: Activities and Tours

  • Helicopter Tour: Get an aerial view of Cirque de Mafate and the surrounding mountains by taking a helicopter tour.
  • Canyoning: Explore the natural beauty of Cirque de Mafate by participating in a canyoning tour.
  • Paragliding: Get a bird’s eye view of Cirque de Mafate by taking a paragliding tour.

Week-Long Itinerary for Cirque de Mafate

Day 1: Arrive in Cirque de Mafate and check into a guesthouse.

Day 2: Hike Sentier Scout and visit Roche Plate village.

Day 3: Take a helicopter tour and visit Trou de Fer waterfall.

Day 4: Explore the natural beauty of Cirque de Mafate by participating in a canyoning tour.

Day 5: Hike Sentier des Tamarins and visit Notre-Dame-des-Neiges church.

Day 6: Paraglide above Cirque de Mafate and visit Maison Folio museum.

Day 7: Depart from Cirque de Mafate.

Food and Drink Scene of Cirque de Mafate

Must-Try Dishes

  • Rougaille saucisse: A Creole dish made with smoked sausage and spicy tomato sauce.
  • Cari poulet: A curry chicken dish that is served with rice and vegetables.
  • Chouchou gratin: A gratin made with chouchou (a local vegetable), cream, and cheese.

Local Beverages

  • Rhum arrangé: A traditional Réunionese rum that is infused with local fruits and spices.
  • Dodo beer: A local beer that is made by Brasserie de Bourbon.

Where to Stay in Cirque de Mafate: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Kelonia Gîtes: A guesthouse that offers comfortable accommodation and stunning views of the mountains.
  • La Nouvelle Auberge: A hostel that offers budget-friendly accommodation and a communal kitchen.
  • Les Gîtes de Roche Plate: A collection of guesthouses that offer cozy rooms and traditional Creole cuisine.

What to Buy in Cirque de Mafate: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Vanilla: Réunionese vanilla is known for its unique flavor and is a popular souvenir to bring home.
  • Embroidery: The women of Cirque de Mafate are known for their embroidery skills and sell beautiful embroidered items.
  • Local Rum: Rhum arrangé is a popular souvenir to bring home and can be found in local stores.

Local Culture and Customs of Cirque de Mafate

The people of Cirque de Mafate are known for their unique way of life and their connection to the land. They have preserved their traditional way of life and have a strong sense of community.

Best Time to Visit Cirque de Mafate

The best time to visit Cirque de Mafate is during the dry season, which runs from May to November. During this time, the weather is mild and the skies are clear, making it perfect for hiking and outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Cirque de Mafate

Do:

  • Respect the local culture and customs.
  • Take only pictures and leave only footprints.
  • Be prepared for changing weather conditions.

Don’t:

  • Pick flowers or disturb wildlife.
  • Litter or leave trash behind.
  • Walk off designated paths or trails.

Safety and Health

Safety in Cirque de Mafate

  • Always hike with a partner or a guide.
  • Check the weather forecast before hiking.
  • Stay on designated paths and trails.

Health in Cirque de Mafate

  • Bring plenty of water and snacks with you.
  • Wear sunscreen and insect repellent to protect yourself.
  • Be aware of altitude sickness if hiking to higher elevations.

Getting to Cirque de Mafate: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Cirque de Mafate

The closest airport is Roland Garros Airport in Saint-Denis, which is approximately 40 km from Cirque de Mafate.

Bus Stations Near Cirque de Mafate

There are no bus stations in Cirque de Mafate, but visitors can take a bus from Saint-Denis or Saint-Paul to the nearest village, and then hike to their accommodation.

Other Options

Visitors can also rent a car or take a taxi to the nearest village and then hike to their accommodation.

How to Get Around in Cirque de Mafate

The only way to get around Cirque de Mafate is by hiking or biking. Visitors can also take a helicopter tour to get an aerial view of the caldera.

Where to Go Next from Cirque de Mafate: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Piton de la Fournaise: A volcano that is located in the eastern part of Réunion Island.
  • Cirque de Salazie: Another caldera located in Réunion National Park that offers beautiful views and hiking trails.
  • La Roche Ecrite: A scenic viewpoint that offers panoramic views of the Indian Ocean and the surrounding mountains.

Conclusion

Cirque de Mafate is a remote and authentic destination that offers a unique experience for adventure seekers and nature lovers. With its breathtaking views, hiking and biking trails, and rich cultural heritage, Cirque de Mafate is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Réunion Island.
