Introduction to Piton de la Fournaise

Piton de la Fournaise is a stunning volcanic mountain located in Réunion National Park, in Réunion, in East African Islands, Africa. It’s considered one of the most active volcanoes, with regular eruptions that attract adventurers and nature lovers alike. This natural gem boasts rugged terrain, stunning vistas, and incredible biodiversity. Planning a trip to Piton de la Fournaise is an adventure that promises to be unforgettable. In this travel guide, we’ll take a closer look at some of the top things to do in Piton de la Fournaise, where to stay, what to eat, and more.

List of Places to Visit in Piton de la Fournaise

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

Museum Stella Matutina

Museum Stella Matutina is a fascinating place to learn about the island’s rich history and culture. Here, you can learn about the sugar cane industry, slavery, and the island’s colonial past. It’s also an excellent place to learn how the island’s economy changed over time.

Saint-Paul Market

When you’re looking for souvenirs, make sure to check out the Saint-Paul Market. It’s a bustling market known for its fresh fruits, vegetables, and spices. There are also a set of stalls that sell traditional handicrafts, including artisanal woodcarvings and textiles.

Natural Attractions of Piton de la Fournaise: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Piton de la Fournaise

To see Piton de la Fournaise up close, consider hiking to the summit. The trail is tough, so it takes some effort, but the view is worth it. Along the trail, you’ll see stunning views of the island, as well as smaller craters on the way to the top. Don’t forget to bring a few layers along with you—it can get quite cold at the summit.

La Nouvelle

La Nouvelle is a small community located on the mountainside of Piton de la Fournaise. If you’re looking for a truly unique experience, consider booking a bungalow or a guesthouse here. This area is also a popular spot for hiking, with well-trodden trails that lead you deep into the heart of the mountain.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Piton de la Fournaise

Valleys of Dreams

Valleys of Dreams is a popular hiking route that takes you through some of the most spectacular landscapes on the island. Along the way, you’ll see a rare bird, the Tuit-Tuit. The trail is well-trodden, and it’s suitable for novice hikers.

Trail du Piton des Neiges

The Trail du Piton des Neiges is an epic hiking trail. It’s the tallest peak on the island, and you can climb to the summit in a single day. The trail is well-trodden, but it’s challenging, so it’s not ideal for beginners. The reward for climbing to the top of the trail is an incredible view of the island.

Best Things to Do in Piton de la Fournaise: Activities and Tours

Helicopter Tours

Helicopter tours are a popular way to see Piton de la Fournaise from a unique perspective. You can take off from the coastal city of Saint-Denis and get a bird’s eye view of the island’s active volcano.

Scuba Diving

Scuba diving is another popular activity in Piton de la Fournaise. There are several dive sites around the island, ranging from shallow reefs to deeper, more challenging dive sites. Divers can expect to see a wide range of marine life in Piton de la Fournaise, including colorful fish and undulating sea grass.

Food and Drink Scene of Piton de la Fournaise

Must-Try Dishes

Curry

In Piton de la Fournaise, curry is a staple dish. It’s made of meat, vegetables, and a range of spices. Some popular curries in Réunion are made with chicken, beef, or fish.

Rougaïl

Rougaïl is a traditional dish made with pork and a tomato sauce. It’s served with rice and comes in many variations.

Local Beverages

The Paille en Queue

If you’re looking for something refreshing, try a Paille en Queue. It’s a non-alcoholic drink made with guava juice, coconut milk, and sugar. It’s perfect for hot weather and pairs well with spicy food.

Rhum Arrangé

Rhum Arrangé is a sweet and strong alcoholic drink popular in Piton de la Fournaise. It’s made by infusing fresh fruit with rum, and the result is a potent and flavorful drink.

Where to Stay in Piton de la Fournaise: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Villa Delisle Hotel & Spa

The Villa Delisle Hotel & Spa is a luxurious place to stay in Piton de la Fournaise. It’s located in Saint-Pierre, a coastal town that’s popular for its beaches and nightlife. The hotel offers an outdoor pool, a spa, and several restaurants and bars.

Auberge Belle Vue

Auberge Belle Vue is a charming guest house with stunning views of Piton de la Fournaise. The rooms are spacious and comfortable, and the staff is friendly and knowledgeable about the island’s attractions.

What to Buy in Piton de la Fournaise: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Vanilla

Vanilla is one of the most popular items to bring back from Piton de la Fournaise. It’s grown locally and has a unique and potent flavor compared to imported vanilla.

Handicrafts

Handicrafts are popular souvenirs on the island. You can find everything from wooden carvings to intricate lacework at the Saint-Paul Market.

Local Culture and Customs of Piton de la Fournaise

Piton de la Fournaise is a cultural melting pot. The island was settled by people from all over the world and has a rich history that reflects its diverse cultural influences. The official language is French, but Creole is widely spoken. The people of Piton de la Fournaise are warm and welcoming, and visitors are encouraged to learn about the island’s unique heritage.

Best Time to Visit Piton de la Fournaise

The best time to visit Piton de la Fournaise is during the dry season, which runs from May to November. During this time, the weather is warm and humid, and there’s plenty of sunshine. It’s an excellent time to explore the island’s attractions and enjoy outdoor activities.

Do’s and Don’ts of Piton de la Fournaise

Do:

  • Do bring sunscreen and bug spray.
  • Do be respectful of the island’s cultural heritage.
  • Do try local dishes and drinks.
  • Do explore the island’s natural beauty.

Don’t:

  • Don’t leave trash behind.
  • Don’t touch or disturb the island’s wildlife.
  • Don’t wander off-trail when hiking.

Safety and Health

Safety in Piton de la Fournaise

Piton de la Fournaise is a safe destination for tourists. However, travelers should be aware of street crime in the cities and take precautions to keep their belongings safe.

Health in Piton de la Fournaise

The island has a high standard of healthcare, but visitors should take precautions to stay healthy. It’s essential to stay hydrated, wear sunscreen, and take necessary vaccinations before traveling to Piton de la Fournaise.

Getting to Piton de la Fournaise: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Piton de la Fournaise

The main airport on the island is Roland Garros Airport, which is located in Saint-Denis. It’s an international airport with flights to cities around the world.

Bus Stations Near Piton de la Fournaise

There are several bus stations around the island that offer transportation services to visitors. The stations are located in major cities and towns, and the rates are affordable.

Other Options

Taxis and rental cars are also available on the island. It’s important to book in advance because these services are in high demand.

How to Get Around in Piton de la Fournaise

The best way to get around Piton de la Fournaise is by car or taxi. Visitors can rent cars at the airport, and taxis are available throughout the island. There are also bike rental shops in major towns that offer bicycles for hire.

Where to Go Next from Piton de la Fournaise: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Saint-Gilles-les-Bains

Saint-Gilles-les-Bains is a charming coastal town located on the west side of the island. It’s known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and excellent restaurant scene.

Cilaos

Cilaos is a small town located in the mountains of Piton de la Fournaise. It’s known for its hot springs, hiking trails, and scenic views. It’s an excellent destination for those looking for a peaceful retreat from the cities.

Conclusion

A trip to Piton de la Fournaise is an adventure in every sense of the word. From hiking to the summit of the volcano to scuba diving in its pristine waters, visitors will never run out of activities to enjoy. This travel guide highlights some of the top attractions, activities, and accommodations on the island. Whether you’re looking for a luxurious getaway or an immersive cultural experience, Piton de la Fournaise has something for everyone. Plan your trip today and discover the beauty of this East African Island destination.
