Introduction to Huye

Huye, located in southern Rwanda, is a beautiful city with a rich cultural heritage. It is often referred to as the intellectual center of the country due to the concentration of educational institutions in the area. Huye’s charming colonial architecture, vibrant cultural scene, and stunning natural beauty make it a must-visit destination in Rwanda.

List of Places to Visit in Huye

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

  • National Museum of Rwanda: This museum located in Huye is an informative and engaging introduction to Rwanda’s history, culture, and natural heritage. The museum has exhibits on traditional crafts, ancient history, and contemporary art. It also has an outdoor section that showcases different types of traditional Rwandan houses.
  • Ethnographic Museum: This museum houses an extensive collection of artifacts related to traditional Rwandan life, including traditional clothing, musical instruments, and pottery. The museum also has a beautiful botanical garden, making it an excellent spot for a picnic.
  • Nyanza Palace: The palace was once the residence of the Rwandan kings before colonialism. Today, visitors can explore the beautifully restored palace, which provides insight into Rwanda’s pre-colonial history.

Natural Attractions of Huye: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

  • Nyungwe National Park: This park located close to Huye is a must-visit destination for nature lovers. It is home to an impressive array of wildlife, including chimpanzees, colobus monkeys, and forest elephants. Visitors can also hike through the trails in the park to see waterfalls, lakes, and natural hot springs.
  • Urwibutso Rwa Kamegeri Natural Forest: This beautiful forest provides shade for locals and a serene environment for visitors. The area also hosts many bird species and can be a destination for birders.
  • Mt. Huye: This mountain located east of the city provides stunning views of the surrounding area. Visitors can hike up the mountain and take in the panoramic views from the top.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Huye

  • Trails in Nyungwe National Park: Nyungwe National Park has many trails for hiking enthusiasts. Visitors can take a leisurely stroll, or for experienced hikers, the trail to the Kamiranzovu waterfall.
  • Huye Mountain Bike Trails: These trails located in Cyahinda can offer a thrilling ride for experienced mountain bikers. The trails are challenging and provide stunning scenery.

Best Things to Do in Huye: Activities and Tours

  • Gorilla Trekking: Visitors can travel to Volcanoes National Park and spend a day trekking up the Virunga Mountains to encounter the endangered mountain gorillas.
  • Cultural Tour: Visitors can tour through the nearby villages to experience traditional Rwandan ways of life.
  • Coffee Tour: Rwanda is known for its high-quality coffee, and visitors can take a tour at a coffee plantation to learn about the process of growing and harvesting coffee.

Food and Drink Scene of Huye

Must-Try Dishes

  • Sambaza: A local fish that is typically fried and served with lemon and potatoes.
  • Akabenz: A hearty savory dish made with cassava, potatoes, beans, and vegetables.
  • Isombe: A dish made with cassava leaves, beans, and potatoes with roasted meat, typically enjoyed for lunch.

Local Beverages

  • Banana Beer: a fermented banana beverage that has a sour taste but is enjoyed by locals.
  • Agashya: a traditional Rwandan tea that is brewed by boiling water with ginger, cinnamon, and cardamom.

Where to Stay in Huye: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

  • Hotel Ibis: A three-star hotel located in the city center, with modern rooms and excellent amenities.
  • Eco-Hotel: A budget-friendly hotel located near Nyungwe National Park known for its eco-friendliness.
  • Akagera Guesthouse: A charming guesthouse with an excellent location and homely ambiance.

What to Buy in Huye: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

  • Imigongo paintings: These are traditional Rwandan paintings that use cow dung as their base material, which is then decorated with natural pigments to create intricate patterns.
  • Agaseke baskets: Made from locally sourced materials, these baskets are known for their intricate designs and bright colors.
  • Traditional fabrics: Visitors can purchase traditional Rwandan fabrics like Kitenge, ankara, or batik.

Local Culture and Customs of Huye

  • Rwandans are known for being hospitable, and visitors are expected to respect them in return.
  • When greeting someone, it is customary to say “Muraho” and shake hands.
  • Men should avoid taking photographs of women in traditional dress without permission.

Best Time to Visit Huye

The best time to visit Huye is during the dry season from June to September when the weather is mild, and visitors can enjoy outdoor activities like hiking and gorilla trekking.

Do’s and Don’ts of Huye

Do:

  • Respect the local customs and culture.
  • Try the traditional dishes and beverages.
  • Pack appropriate clothing for outdoor activities.

Don’t:

  • Disrespect the local culture and customs.
  • Take photographs without permission.
  • Litter or damage the natural environment.

Safety and Health

Safety in Huye

Huye is generally considered a safe city for visitors, and there has been low crime reported. Visitors should take typical safety measures like not leaving valuables in plain sight and avoiding walking in unlit areas at night.

Health in Huye

Visitors to Huye are advised to get vaccinations for diseases like yellow fever, hepatitis A, and typhoid. Malaria is present in the area, and visitors should take preventive measures like using mosquito-netted beddings and repellents.

Getting to Huye: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Huye

The closest airport to Huye is the Kigali International Airport, located about 128 kilometers away. From there, visitors can take a taxi or a bus to Huye.

Bus Stations Near Huye

Buses from Kigali depart every day from the Nyabugogo Bus Terminal and arrive at the Huye Bus Terminal.

Other Options

Visitors can also hire a private car or taxi from Kigali to Huye, or take a shared taxi.

How to Get Around in Huye

Visitors can opt for moto-taxis or taxi cabs to get around the city. Bicycles are also available for rent for those who want to explore the city at a leisurely pace.

Where to Go Next from Huye: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

  • Kigali: Rwanda’s capital city is just a few hours away and offers many cultural and historical sights for visitors.
  • Lake Kivu: a stunning freshwater lake located on the border between Rwanda and the Democratic Republic of Congo, known for its scenic views and tranquil atmosphere.

Conclusion

Huye may be one of the lesser-known destinations in Rwanda, but this charming city has something for everyone. With its blend of cultural and natural attractions, visitors will leave enchanted and longing to come back for more.
