Introduction to Vihiga

Nestled amidst the lush green hills of Western Kenya lies the serene county of Vihiga. Scenic views, outdoor adventures, and a rich cultural heritage make Vihiga an excellent destination for intrepid explorers. With its friendly locals and vibrant food scene, Vihiga is the perfect spot to experience the beauty of Kenya off the beaten path.

List of Places to Visit in Vihiga

Culturally Important Attractions: Museums and Historical Places

The region is known for its history and culture, and there are many museums and historical places to visit. The Vihiga Cultural Center showcases a collection of traditional artifacts, while the Bukura Agricultural College Museum’s interactive exhibits highlight the region’s agricultural heritage. The ancient rock paintings at Kakamega Forest National Reserve, which date back over 3,000 years, offer a glimpse into the region’s early human history.

Natural Attractions of Vihiga: Outdoor Adventures and Scenic Views

Vihiga is a haven for outdoor enthusiasts, boasting a diverse range of natural attractions such as:

  • The stunning Kakamega Forest National Reserve, home to over 400 species of birds, primates, and butterflies.
  • The Kaimosi Forest Nature Trail, perfect for hiking and bird watching.
  • The Chetambe Hills, which offer panoramic views of the surrounding countryside.

Hiking/Biking Routes of Vihiga

Vihiga offers a wide range of hiking and biking routes, ranging from easy to challenging. The Kakamega Forest Nature Trail is a relatively easy hike that takes visitors through lush vegetation and is perfect for birdwatchers. The Chetambe Hills and Ikonya Forest Reserve offer more challenging hikes that take visitors through rugged terrain and offer stunning views of the countryside.

Best Things to Do in Vihiga: Activities and Tours

Aside from hiking, biking and exploring nature trails, there is a variety of things to do in Vihiga. Visitors can enjoy a cultural performance at the Vihiga Cultural Centre, go shopping for souvenirs, visit the local markets, and enjoy the local cuisine. Guided tours of the local towns and villages are also available.

Week-Long Itinerary for Vihiga

Day 1: Arrive & check-in at Hotel in Vihiga Town

Day 2: Visit the Vihiga Cultural Centre and Bukura Agricultural College Museum. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant and in the night experience the local nightlife.

Day 3: Take a hike or bike ride through the Chetambe Hills or Ikonya Forest Reserve for a stunning panoramic view

Day 4: Visit the Kakamega Forest Reserve for bird watching and a picnic

Day 5: Visit the Kakamega Forest National Reserve for chimpanzee trekking and butterfly watching

Day 6: Experience Vihiga market and Buy local souvenirs

Day 7: Departure from Vihiga and on to the next adventure

Food and Drink Scene of Vihiga

Must-Try Dishes

Vihiga is famous for its wholesome and delicious meals. Some of the must-have dishes include:

  • The staple dish, Ugali (a stiff maize porridge, usually served with sautéed cabbage, kale, or spinach)
  • Chapati (fried flatbread, often served with stew or curry)
  • Sukuma Wiki (collard greens or kale, sautéed with onions and spices)

Local Beverages

Vihiga is famous for brewing traditional drinks such as chang’aa (a potent distilled spirit), Busaa (a locally brewed beer made from maize), and Mursik (a traditional fermented milk and tea blend).

Where to Stay in Vihiga: Hotels, Hostels, and Guesthouses

Vihiga offers a wide range of accommodation options, including budget-friendly guesthouses and hostels, mid-range hotels, and luxury lodges. Some of the highly-rated places to stay include the Soroptimist Hostel, the Kimilili Guest House, and the Serengeti Hotel.

What to Buy in Vihiga: Souvenirs and Local Products to Bring Home

Vihiga is known for its woven baskets, baskets made from maize husks, and a variety of handmade jewelry. Buying these souvenirs supports local artisans and their sustainable livelihoods.

Local Culture and Customs of Vihiga

Vihiga is a vibrant community, steeped in ancient cultural traditions, and visitors are expected to respect local customs. Women should dress modestly, and photographs should not be taken without permission. Visitors should demonstrate respectful behavior and take an interest in the local culture.

Best Time to Visit Vihiga

The best time to visit Vihiga is during the dry season, between June and September. During this time, the weather is mild and pleasant, and the wildlife is most active.

Do’s and Don’ts of Vihiga

Do:

  • Respect local customs and traditions.
  • Cover up shoulders and legs when visiting religious sites.
  • Take an interest in the local culture.

Don’t:

  • Take photographs without permission.
  • Wear revealing clothing in public areas.
  • Show disrespect to locals.

Safety and Health

Safety in Vihiga

As in most areas, visitors should take standard precautions regarding their health and safety. It is essential to keep an eye on your belongings in crowded spaces and avoid walking alone at night.

Health in Vihiga

Visitors should ensure that they have received all necessary vaccines, such as the yellow fever vaccine. It is also essential to bring along medication and mosquito repellent to avoid malaria.

Getting to Vihiga: Transportation Options and Logistics

Visitors can get to Vihiga by air or road. The nearest airport is Kisumu International Airport, which is about 45 minutes away from Vihiga by road. It is recommended to hire a taxi from the airport. The road network is relatively good, and visitors can travel by bus or alternatively by private hire taxis from major cities in Kenya like Nairobi, Kisumu, and Eldoret.

Airports Near Vihiga

  • Kisumu International Airport

Bus Stations Near Vihiga

  • Nairobi Bus station

Other Options

  • Self-drive option: Visitors can rent a vehicle to explore the region independently.

How to Get Around in Vihiga

The best way to get around Vihiga is by taxi, motorcycle, or bicycle. Visitors can hire taxis at affordable rates from any point in the town or hail motorcycle taxi from the street. Bicycles can be rented from local bike shops.

Where to Go Next from Vihiga: Nearby Destinations and Excursions

Visitors can explore the nearby destinations such as:

  • Kakamega town and Kakamega Forest Reserve
  • Kisumu city and Lake Victoria
  • Eldoret, a vibrant town rich in athletics history

Conclusion

Vihiga is a hidden gem in Kenya that offers an excellent opportunity to experience the beauty of the country off the beaten path. With its stunning natural landscapes and rich cultural heritage, Vihiga is a must-visit destination for intrepid travelers seeking to explore the vibrancy of Kenya.
