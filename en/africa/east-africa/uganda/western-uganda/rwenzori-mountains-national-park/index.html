Introduction to Rwenzori Mountains National Park

Rwenzori Mountains National Park is located in Western Uganda and is named after the Rwenzori Mountains, also known as the Mountains of the Moon. The park was established in 1991 and covers an area of 996 km². It is home to the third highest peak in Africa, Mount Stanley (5,109m), and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. The park is known for its diverse landscapes, including glaciers, lakes, waterfalls, and forests.

Best Time to Visit Rwenzori Mountains National Park

The best time to visit Rwenzori Mountains National Park is during the dry season, from December to February and June to August. The park is open year-round, but some trails may be closed during the rainy season.

Do’s and Don’ts of Rwenzori Mountains National Park

Do:

  • Follow park regulations and instructions from guides
  • Carry sufficient supplies, including water and medicines
  • Hire local guides and porters
  • Respect wildlife and its habitat
  • Take care of the environment

Don’t:

  • Litter or leave trash behind
  • Remove any natural or cultural artifacts
  • Start fires outside dedicated areas
  • Feed or disturb wildlife
  • Use single-use plastics

Safety and Health

Safety in Rwenzori Mountains National Park

Visitors are advised to take precautions and adhere to park regulations to prevent accidents or injuries. Safety guidelines include:

  • Hire experienced guides and porters
  • Carry emergency equipment, such as a first aid kit
  • Acclimatize properly before undertaking any strenuous activity
  • Stay on designated trails

Health in Rwenzori Mountains National Park

Visitors are advised to take precautions to prevent common ailments associated with high altitude and tropical environments. Recommendations include:

  • Vaccinations (yellow fever, hepatitis A and B, typhoid fever, among others)
  • Drinking boiled or bottled water
  • Using insect repellent and mosquito netting
  • Avoiding raw or undercooked food

Getting to Rwenzori Mountains National Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Rwenzori Mountains National Park

The nearest airport is Entebbe International Airport, located near the capital city of Kampala.

Bus Stations Near Rwenzori Mountains National Park

Buses and private taxis connect Kampala to Kasese, which is the closest town to the park.

Other Options

Private hire vehicles, including 4x4s and minivans, are available for hire.

How to Get Around in Rwenzori Mountains National Park

Hiking and trekking are the main ways to explore the park. Local guides and porters are available for hire. There are no motorized vehicles allowed in the park.
