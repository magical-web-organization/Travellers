Introduction to Banc d’Arguin National Park

Located in coastal Mauritania in Sahel, Africa, Banc d’Arguin National Park is a jewel in the country’s crown. Established in 1976, the park covers an area of 12,000 square kilometres, of which over 2,000 square kilometres are designated as a UNESCO World Heritage site. The park is home to diverse and abundant wildlife, including fish, birds, and mammals, and boasts some of Africa’s most stunning landscapes. In this comprehensive guide, we will explore the park’s natural and cultural attractions, outdoor adventures, food and drink scene, accommodations, local culture and customs, transportation, and logistics.

If you’re craving an outdoor adventure, you can go mountain biking or trekking in the park. Here are some of the popular routes:

Apart from hiking and mountain biking, you can also go on guided tours and enjoy other activities. Here are some of the best things to do in the park:

Local Culture and Customs of Banc d’Arguin National Park

The local culture and customs of Banc d’Arguin National Park are both diverse and fascinating. Here are some things to keep in mind:

  • Dress modestly
  • Respect the local customs and traditions
  • Learn basic Arabic or French to communicate with locals

Best Time to Visit Banc d’Arguin National Park

The best time to visit Banc d’Arguin National Park is from November to February, when the weather is mild and the bird migration is at its peak. The park is closed during the hot and humid summer months, from June to October.

Do’s and Don’ts of Banc d’Arguin National Park

Do:

  • Respect the natural environment
  • Travel with a guide or join a tour group
  • Learn about the culture and traditions of the locals

Don’t:

  • Damage or remove any objects from the park
  • Feed the wildlife
  • Wear revealing clothing

Safety and Health

Safety in Banc d’Arguin National Park

Banc d’Arguin National Park is a safe destination for tourists. However, make sure to take standard precautions such as keeping your valuables safe, drinking bottled water, and avoiding areas that may be dangerous.

Health in Banc d’Arguin National Park

Make sure to get vaccinated for yellow fever, hepatitis A and B, and typhoid before visiting Banc d’Arguin National Park. Also, take precautions against malaria, such as using insect repellent and sleeping under a mosquito net.

Getting to Banc d’Arguin National Park: Transportation Options and Logistics

Airports Near Banc d’Arguin National Park

If you’re flying to Banc d’Arguin National Park, the nearest airport is Nouakchott International Airport.

Bus Stations Near Banc d’Arguin National Park

There are no bus services to Banc d’Arguin National Park. However, you can take a taxi or a shared taxi from Nouakchott.

Other Options

You can also reach Banc d’Arguin National Park by car or by boat.

How to Get Around in Banc d’Arguin National Park

The best way to get around Banc d’Arguin National Park is on foot or by boat. If you’re taking a guided tour, your transportation will be arranged for you.
